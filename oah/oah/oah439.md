### Ahura'Mazda

CREATOR.

1\. DESCENDED by the Yi-ha light through mortals, and in the Vedan Gods
revealed from Zarathustra in Juian, Zend and Haizariyi, and thence into
Vede, and thence into Sanscrit, and by Brahma and by Buddha. These,
then, that follow, are the pillars of light of Great Jehovih, still
standing as the remnants of His beloved sons and daughters in the East.

2\. Airyana, the good, created things. Anra'mainyus, the evil, created
things. Gau, place of science in heaven. Maori, the second holy heaven.
Bakhdhi, third holy place in heaven. Haroyu, confederate republics in
heaven. Haetumat, emancipated heaven above the lower or bound heavens.
