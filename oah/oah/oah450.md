### OTE.

1\. PROCLAIM Him, O ye Andes and Cordilleras, stretching avast and on
high, ye monuments of the Almighty!

2\. Praise His majesty, O ye Himal Layas, and ye Akai Shine, ye
foot-prints of Jehovih!

3\. Praise His greatness, O ye Thia Shan, and ye Tapa Ling, that feed
the Pacific, the Daughter of Jehovih.

4\. Sing unto Him, O all ye mountains of the earth; proclaim His power,
which stood you up by the touch of his hand.
