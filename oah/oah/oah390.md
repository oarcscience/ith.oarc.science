Plate 49.--Cevorkum, roadway of solar phalanx. a, a, a, lines of
different currents; b, b, b, transverse currents. The crossing denote
the localities of the highest etherean light. The numbers with their
signatures, show the densities through which the great serpent passes
each cycle. The lines across the cevorkum denote a cycle of three
thousand years, but overdrawn one thousand times in order to be apparent
to the eye, i.e., one to 4,700,000.
