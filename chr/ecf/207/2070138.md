id="iv.iv.v.vi-p1.1"></span>Ep. XXVIII.</span>

<span id="iv.iv.v.vi-p2"></span>In visiting the mountain cities which
border on Pamphylia I fished up in the Mountains a sea Glaucus; I did
not drag the fish out of the depths with a net of flax, but I snared my
game with the love of a friend.  And having once taught my Glaucus to
travel by land, I sent him as the bearer of a letter to Your Goodness. 
Please receive him kindly, and honour him with the hospitality commended
in the Bible, not forgetting the vegetables.
