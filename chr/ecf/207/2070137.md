<span id="iv.iv.v.v-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="iv.iv.v.v-p1.1"></span>Ep. XXVII.</span>

<span id="iv.iv.v.v-p2"></span>You make a joke of it; but I know the
danger of an Ozizalean starving when he has taken most pains with his
husbandry.  There is only this praise to be given them, that even if
they die of hunger they smell sweet, and have a gorgeous funeral.  How
so?  Because they are covered with plenty of all sorts of flowers.
