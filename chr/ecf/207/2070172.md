<span id="iv.iv.ix.ii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="iv.iv.ix.ii-p1.1"></span>Ep. CV.</span>

<span id="iv.iv.ix.ii-p2"></span>The time is swift, the struggle great,
and my sickness severer, reducing me almost to immovability.  What is
left but to pray to God, and to supplicate your kindness, the one, that
<span id="page_478">p. 478</span> <span
id="iv.iv.ix.ii-Page_478"></span>He will incline your mind to gentler
counsels, the other that you will not roughly dismiss our intercession,
but will receive kindly the wretched Paulus, whom justice has brought
under your hands, perhaps in order that it may make you more illustrious
by the greatness of your kindness, and may commend our prayers (such as
they are) to your mercy.
