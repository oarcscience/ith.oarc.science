<span id="iv.iv.viii.vi-p2"></span>An Invitation.  You flee when I
pursue you:  perhaps in accordance with the laws of love, to make
yourself more valuable.  Come then, and fill up at last the loss I have
suffered by your long delay.  And if any home affairs detain you, you
shall leave us again, and so make yourself more precious as an object of
desire.
