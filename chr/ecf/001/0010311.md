## <span id="v.xix-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to Mary at Neapolis, Near Zarbus.

<span id="v.xix-p1"></span><span id="v.xix-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.xix-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theaphorus, to her
who has obtained mercy through the grace of the most high God the
Father, and Jesus Christ the Lord, who died for us, to Mary, my
daughter, most faithful, worthy of God, and bearing Christ \[in her
heart\], wishes abundance of happiness in God.*
