### <span id="ix.viii.i-p0.1"></span>I.

<span id="ix.viii.i-p1"></span><span class="upper"><span
id="ix.viii.i-p1.1"></span>I adjure</span> thee, who shalt transcribe
this book, <span id="fr_4796"></span>[4797](#fn_4796.md) by our Lord Jesus
Christ, and by His glorious appearing, when He comes to judge the living
and the dead, that thou compare what thou hast transcribed, and be
careful to set it right according to this copy from which thou hast
transcribed; also, that thou in like manner copy down this adjuration,
and insert it in the transcript.
