### <span id="viii.ix.viii-p0.1"></span>VIII.

<span id="viii.ix.viii-p1"></span>The unskilfulness of the teacher
proves destructive to his disciples, and the carelessness of the
disciples entails danger on the teacher, and especially should they owe
their negligence to his want of knowledge.
