### <span id="iv.ii.xiv-p0.1"></span>Chapter XIV.—Conclusion.

<span id="iv.ii.xiv-p1"></span>These things I have written to you by
Crescens, whom up to the present <span id="fr_416"></span>[417](#fn_416.md)
time I have recommended unto you, and do now recommend. For he has acted
blamelessly among us, and I believe also among you. Moreover, ye will
hold his sister in esteem when she comes to you. Be ye safe in the Lord
Jesus Christ. Grace be with you all. <span
id="fr_417"></span>[418](#fn_417.md) Amen.
