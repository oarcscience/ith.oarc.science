### <span id="v.xiv.viii-p0.1"></span>Chapter VIII.—Exhortations to holiness and good order.

<span id="v.xiv.viii-p1"></span>May I have joy of you in the Lord! Be ye
sober. <span id="v.xiv.viii-p1.1"></span>Lay aside, every one of you,
all malice and beast-like fury, evil-speaking, calumny, filthy speaking,
ribaldry, whispering, arrogance, drunkenness, lust, avarice, vainglory,
envy, and everything akin to these. “But put ye on the Lord Jesus
Christ, and make no provision for the flesh, to fulfil the lusts
thereof.” <span id="fr_1210"></span>[1211](#fn_1210.md) Ye presbyters, be
subject to the bishop; ye deacons, to the presbyters; and ye, the
people, to the presbyters and the deacons. Let my soul be for theirs who
preserve this good order; and may the Lord be with them continually!
