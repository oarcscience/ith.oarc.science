### <span id="vii.ii.vii-p0.1"></span>VII. <span id="fr_1759"></span>[1760](#fn_1759)

<span id="vii.ii.vii-p2"></span>*Papias thus speaks, word for word:* To
some of them \[angels\] He gave dominion over the arrangement of the
world, and He commissioned them to exercise their dominion well. *And he
says, immediately after this:* but it happened that their arrangement
came to nothing. <span id="fr_1760"></span>[1761](#fn_1760)
