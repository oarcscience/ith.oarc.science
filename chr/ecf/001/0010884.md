### <span id="ix.viii.ix-p0.1"></span>IX.

<span id="ix.viii.ix-p1"></span>Ever, <span
id="fr_4812"></span>[4813](#fn_4812.md) indeed, speaking well of the
deserving, but never ill of the undeserving, we also shall attain to the
glory and kingdom of God.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_4812"></span>[570:4813](0010884.htm#fr_4812.md) <span
id="ix.viii.ix-p1.1"></span>

<span id="ix.viii.ix-p2"></span> This fragment and the next three are
from the *Parallela* of John of Damascus. Frag. ix. x. xii. seem to be
quotations from the treatise of Irenæus on the resurrection. No. xi. is
extracted from his *Miscellaneous Dissertations*, a work mentioned by
Eusebius, <span class="Greek"><span id="ix.viii.ix-p2.1"></span>βιβλίον
τι διαλεξέων διαφόρων</span>.
