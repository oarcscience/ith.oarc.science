## <span id="iv.iv-p0.1"></span>The Encyclical Epistle of the Church at Smyrna Concerning the Martyrdom of the Holy Polycarp

<span id="iv.iv-p1"></span><span id="iv.iv-p1.1"></span><span
id="iv.iv-p1.2"></span><span class="upper"><span
id="iv.iv-p1.3"></span>The</span> Church of God which sojourns at
Smyrna, to the Church of God sojourning in Philomelium, <span
id="fr_419"></span>[420](#fn_419.md) and to all the congregations <span
id="fr_420"></span>[421](#fn_420.md) of the Holy and Catholic Church in
every place: Mercy, peace, and love from God the Father, and our Lord
Jesus Christ, be multiplied.
