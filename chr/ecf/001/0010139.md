### <span id="v.ii.xvi-p0.1"></span>Chapter XVI.—The fate of false teachers.

<span id="v.ii.xvi-p1"></span><span id="v.ii.xvi-p1.1"></span>Do not
err, my brethren. <span id="fr_595"></span>[596](#fn_595) Those that
corrupt families shall not inherit the kingdom of God. <span
id="fr_596"></span>[597](#fn_596) If, then, those who do this as
respects the flesh have suffered death, how much more shall this be the
case with any one who corrupts by wicked doctrine the faith of God, for
which Jesus Christ was crucified! Such an one becoming defiled \[in this
way\], shall go away into everlasting fire, and so shall every one that
hearkens unto him.

<span id="v.ii.xvi-p4"></span><span id="v.ii.xvi-p4.1"></span>Do not
err, my brethren. <span id="fr_597"></span>[598](#fn_597) Those that
corrupt families shall not inherit the kingdom of God. <span
id="fr_598"></span>[599](#fn_598) And if those that corrupt mere human
families are condemned to death, how much more shall those suffer
everlasting punishment who endeavour to corrupt the Church of Christ,
for which the Lord Jesus, the only-begotten Son of God, endured the
cross, and submitted to death! Whosoever, “being waxen fat,” <span
id="fr_599"></span>[600](#fn_599) and “become gross,” sets at nought His
doctrine, shall go into hell. <span id="v.ii.xvi-p7.2"></span>In like
manner, every one that has received from God the power of
distinguishing, and yet follows an unskilful shepherd, and receives a
false opinion for the truth, shall be punished. <span
id="v.ii.xvi-p7.3"></span>“What communion hath light with darkness? or
Christ with Belial? Or what portion hath he that believeth with an
infidel? or the temple of God with idols?” <span
id="fr_600"></span>[601](#fn_600) And in like manner say I, what
communion hath truth with falsehood? or righteousness with
unrighteousness? or true doctrine with that which is false?
