### <span id="viii.ix.xii-p0.1"></span>XII.

<span id="viii.ix.xii-p1"></span>As it is inherent in all bodies formed
by God to have a shadow, so it is fitting that God, who is just, should
render to those who choose what is good, and to those who prefer what is
evil, to every one according to his deserts.—*From the writings of*
<span class="upper"><span id="viii.ix.xii-p1.1"></span>John of
Damascus</span>.
