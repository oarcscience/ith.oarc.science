## <span id="v.xiv-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Tarsians

<span id="v.xiv-p1"></span><span id="v.xiv-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.xiv-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church which is at Tarsus, saved in Christ, worthy of praise, worthy of
remembrance, and worthy of love: Mercy and peace from God the Father,
and the Lord Jesus Christ, be ever multiplied.*
