### <span id="viii.ix.xvii-p0.1"></span>XVII.

<span id="viii.ix.xvii-p1"></span>As the good of the body is health, so
the good of the soul is knowledge, which is indeed a kind of health of
soul, by which a likeness to God is attained.—*From the writings of*
<span class="upper"><span id="viii.ix.xvii-p1.1"></span>John of
Damascus</span>.
