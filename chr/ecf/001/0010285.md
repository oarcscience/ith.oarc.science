### <span id="v.xvi.vii-p0.1"></span>Chapter VII.—Solemn charge to Hero, as future bishop of Antioch.

<span id="v.xvi.vii-p1"></span>Keep God in remembrance, and thou shalt
never sin. Be not double-minded <span
id="fr_1289"></span>[1290](#fn_1289) in thy prayers; for blessed is he
who doubteth not. For I believe in the Father of the Lord Jesus Christ,
and in His only-begotten Son, that God will show me, Hero, upon my
throne. Add speed, therefore, <span id="fr_1290"></span>[1291](#fn_1290)
to thy course. I charge thee before the God of the universe, and before
Christ, and in the presence of the Holy Spirit, and of the ministering
ranks \[of angels\], keep in safety that deposit which I and Christ have
committed to thee, and do not judge thyself unworthy of those things
which have been shown by God \[to me\] concerning thee. I hand over to
thee the Church of Antioch. I have commended you to Polycarp in the Lord
Jesus Christ.
