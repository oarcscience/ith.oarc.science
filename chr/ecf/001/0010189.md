### <span id="v.vi.ii-p0.1"></span>Chapter II.—Maintain union with the bishop.

<span id="v.vi.ii-p1"></span><span id="v.vi.ii-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.vi.ii-p1.2"></span>Wherefore, as children of light and truth, flee
from division and wicked <span id="page_80">p. 80</span> <span
id="v.vi.ii-Page_80"></span> doctrines; but where the shepherd is, there
do ye as sheep follow. For there are many wolves that appear worthy of
credit, who, by means of a pernicious pleasure, carry captive <span
id="fr_888"></span>[889](#fn_888) those that are running towards God;
but in your unity they shall have no place.

<span id="v.vi.ii-p3"></span>Wherefore, as children of light and truth,
avoid the dividing of your unity, and the wicked doctrine of the
heretics, from whom “a defiling influence has gone forth into all the
earth.” <span id="fr_889"></span>[890](#fn_889) <span
id="v.vi.ii-p4.2"></span>But where the shepherd is, there do ye as sheep
follow. For there are many wolves in sheep’s clothing, <span
id="fr_890"></span>[891](#fn_890) who, by means of a pernicious
pleasure, carry captive <span id="fr_891"></span>[892](#fn_891) those
that are running towards God; but in your unity they shall have no
place.
