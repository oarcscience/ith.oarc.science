## <span id="v.xi-p0.1"></span>The Second Epistle of Ignatius to the Ephesians <span id="fr_1145"></span>[1146](#fn_1145)

<span id="v.xi-p2"></span><span id="v.xi-p2.1"></span>*Ignatius, who is
\[also called\] Theophorus, to the Church which is blessed in the
greatness of God the Father, and perfected; to her who was
selected* <span id="fr_1146"></span>[1147](#fn_1146) *from eternity,
that she might be at all times for glory, which abideth, and is
unchangeable, and is perfected and chosen in the purpose of truth by the
will of the Father of Jesus Christ our God; to her who is worthy of
happiness; to her who is at Ephesus, in Jesus Christ, in joy which is
unblameable: \[wishes\] abundance of happiness.*
