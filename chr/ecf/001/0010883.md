### <span id="ix.viii.viii-p0.1"></span>VIII.

<span id="ix.viii.viii-p1"></span><span
id="ix.viii.viii-p1.1"></span>For <span
id="fr_4811"></span>[4812](#fn_4811.md) as the ark \[of the covenant\] was
gilded within and without with pure gold, so was also the body of Christ
pure and resplendent; for it was adorned within by the Word, and
shielded without by the Spirit, in order that from both \[materials\]
the splendour of the natures might be clearly shown forth.
