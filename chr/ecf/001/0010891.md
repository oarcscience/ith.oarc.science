### <span id="ix.viii.xvi-p0.1"></span>XVI.

<span id="ix.viii.xvi-p1"></span><span
id="ix.viii.xvi-p1.1"></span>Expounding again the law to that generation
which followed those who were slain in the wilderness, he published
Deuteronomy; not as giving to them a different law from that which had
been appointed for their fathers, but as recapitulating this latter, in
order that they, by hearing what had happened to their fathers, might
fear God with their whole heart.
