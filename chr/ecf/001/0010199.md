## <span id="v.vii-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Smyrnæans Shorter and Longer Versions.

<span id="v.vii-p1"></span><span id="v.vii-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.vii-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church of God the Father, and of the beloved Jesus Christ, which has
through mercy obtained every kind of gift, which is filled with faith
and love, and is deficient in no gift, most worthy of God, and adorned
with holiness:* <span id="fr_972"></span>[973](#fn_972.md) *the Church
which is at Smyrna, in Asia, wishes abundance of happiness, through the
immaculate Spirit and word of God.*

<span id="v.vii-p3"></span><span id="v.vii-p3.1"></span><span
id="v.vii-p3.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church of God the most high Father, and His beloved Son Jesus Christ,
which has through mercy obtained every kind of gift, which is filled
with faith and love, and is deficient in no gift, most worthy of God,
and adorned with holiness:* <span id="fr_973"></span>[974](#fn_973.md) *the
Church which is at Smyrna, in Asia, wishes abundance of happiness,
through the immaculate Spirit and word of God.*
