## <span id="v.iv-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Trallians Shorter and Longer Versions

<span id="v.iv-p1"></span><span id="v.iv-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.iv-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
holy Church which is at Tralles, in Asia, beloved of God, the Father of
Jesus Christ, elect, and worthy of God, possessing peace through the
flesh, and blood, and passion of Jesus Christ, who is our hope, through
our rising again to Him,* <span id="fr_727"></span>[728](#fn_727.md) *which
also I salute in its fulness,* <span id="fr_728"></span>[729](#fn_728.md)
*and in the apostolical character,* <span
id="fr_729"></span>[730](#fn_729.md) *and wish abundance of happiness.*

<span id="v.iv-p5"></span><span id="v.iv-p5.1"></span><span
id="v.iv-p5.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theaphorus, to the
holy Church which is at Tralles, beloved by God the Father, and Jesus
Christ, elect, and worthy of God, possessing peace through the flesh and
Spirit of Jesus Christ, who is our hope, in His passion by the cross and
death, and in His resurrection, which also I salute in its
fulness,* <span id="fr_730"></span>[731](#fn_730.md) *and in the
apostolical character,* <span id="fr_731"></span>[732](#fn_731.md) *and
wish abundance of happiness.*
