### <span id="ix.viii.x-p0.1"></span>X.

<span id="ix.viii.x-p1"></span>It is indeed proper to God, and befitting
His character, to show mercy and pity, and to bring salvation to His
creatures, even though they be brought under danger of destruction. “For
with Him,” says the Scripture, “is propitiation.” <span
id="fr_4813"></span>[4814](#fn_4813)
