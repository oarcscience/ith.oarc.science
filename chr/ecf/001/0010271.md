### <span id="v.xv.viii-p0.1"></span>Chapter VIII.—Exhortations to the presbyters and others.

<span id="v.xv.viii-p1"></span>Ye presbyters, “feed the flock which is
among you,” <span id="fr_1246"></span>[1247](#fn_1246) till God shall
show who is to hold the rule over you. For “I am now ready to be
offered,” <span id="fr_1247"></span>[1248](#fn_1247) that I “may win
Christ.” <span id="fr_1248"></span>[1249](#fn_1248) Let the deacons know
of what dignity they are, and let them study to be blameless, that they
may be the followers of Christ. Let the people be subject to the
presbyters and the deacons. Let the virgins know to whom they have
consecrated themselves.
