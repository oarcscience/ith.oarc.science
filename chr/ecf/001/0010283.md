### <span id="v.xvi.v-p0.1"></span>Chapter V.—Various relative duties.

<span id="v.xvi.v-p1"></span>Flee from haughtiness, “for the Lord
resisteth the proud.” <span id="fr_1283"></span>[1284](#fn_1283) Abhor
falsehood, for says \[the Scripture\], “Thou shalt destroy all them that
speak lies.” <span id="fr_1284"></span>[1285](#fn_1284) Guard against
envy, for its author is the devil, and his successor Cain, who envied
his brother, and out of envy committed murder. Exhort my sisters to love
God, and be content with their own husbands only. In like manner, exhort
my brethren also to be content with their own wives. Watch over the
virgins, as the precious treasures of Christ. Be long-suffering, <span
id="fr_1285"></span>[1286](#fn_1285) that thou mayest be great in
wisdom. Do not neglect the poor, in so far as thou art prosperous. For
“by alms and fidelity sins are purged away.” <span
id="fr_1286"></span>[1287](#fn_1286)
