### <span id="ix.viii.xv-p0.1"></span>XV.

<span id="ix.viii.xv-p1"></span><span
id="ix.viii.xv-p1.1"></span>When, <span
id="fr_4820"></span>[4821](#fn_4820) in times of old, Balaam spake these
things in parables, he was not acknowledged; and now, when Christ has
appeared and fulfilled them, He was not believed. Wherefore \[Balaam\],
foreseeing this, and wondering at it, exclaimed, “Alas! alas! who shall
live when God brings these things to pass?” <span
id="fr_4821"></span>[4822](#fn_4821)
