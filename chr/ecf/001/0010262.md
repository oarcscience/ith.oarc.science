### <span id="v.xiv.x-p0.1"></span>Chapter X.—Salutations.

<span id="v.xiv.x-p1"></span><span id="v.xiv.x-p1.1"></span>May I enjoy
your prayers! Pray ye that may attain to Jesus. I commend unto you the
Church which is at Antioch. The Churches of Philippi, <span
id="fr_1212"></span>[1213](#fn_1212.md) whence also I write to you, salute
you. Philo, your deacon, to whom also I give thanks as one who has
zealously ministered to me in all things, salutes you. Agathopus, the
deacon from Syria, who follows me in Christ, salutes you. “Salute ye one
another with a holy kiss.” <span id="fr_1213"></span>[1214](#fn_1213.md) I
salute you all, both male and female, who are in Christ. Fare ye well in
body, and soul, and in one Spirit; and do not ye forget me. The Lord be
with you!
