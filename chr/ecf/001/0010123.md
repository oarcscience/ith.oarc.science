## <span id="v.ii-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Ephesians Shorter and Longer Versions

<span id="v.ii-p1"></span><span id="v.ii-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.ii-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church which is at Ephesus, in Asia, deservedly most happy, being
blessed in the greatness and fulness of God the Father, and
predestinated before the beginning* <span
id="fr_484"></span>[485](#fn_484) *of time, that it should be always for
an enduring and unchangeable glory, being united* <span
id="fr_485"></span>[486](#fn_485) *and elected through the true passion
by the will of the Father, and Jesus Christ, our God: Abundant happiness
through Jesus Christ, and His undefiled grace.*

<span id="v.ii-p4"></span><span id="v.ii-p4.1"></span><span
id="v.ii-p4.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church which is at Ephesus, in Asia, deservedly most happy, being
blessed in the greatness and fulness of God the Father, and
predestinated before the beginning* <span
id="fr_486"></span>[487](#fn_486) *of time, that it should be always for
an enduring and unchangeable glory, being united* <span
id="fr_487"></span>[488](#fn_487) *and elected through the true passion
by the will of God the Father, and of our Lord Jesus Christ our Saviour:
Abundant happiness through Jesus Christ, and His undefiled joy.* <span
id="fr_488"></span>[489](#fn_488)
