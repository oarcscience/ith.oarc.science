## <span id="v.xv-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Antiochians

<span id="v.xv-p1"></span><span id="v.xv-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.xv-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church sojourning in Syria, which has obtained mercy from God, and been
elected by Christ, and which first* <span
id="fr_1214"></span>[1215](#fn_1214) *received the name Christ,
\[wishes\] happiness in God the Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.*
