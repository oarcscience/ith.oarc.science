### <span id="v.xvi.iii-p0.1"></span>Chapter III.—Exhortations as to ecclesiastical duties.

<span id="v.xvi.iii-p1"></span>“Honour widows that are widows
indeed.” <span id="fr_1277"></span>[1278](#fn_1277.md) Be the friend of
orphans; for God is “the Father of the fatherless, and the Judge of the
widows.” <span id="fr_1278"></span>[1279](#fn_1278.md) Do nothing without
the bishops; for they are priests, and thou a servant of the priests.
They baptize, offer sacrifice, <span
id="fr_1279"></span>[1280](#fn_1279.md) ordain, and lay on hands; but thou
ministerest to them, as the holy Stephen <span id="page_114">p.
114</span> <span id="v.xvi.iii-Page_114"></span> did at Jerusalem to
James and the presbyters. Do not neglect the sacred meetings <span
id="fr_1280"></span>[1281](#fn_1280.md) \[of the saints\]; inquire after
every one by name. “Let no man despise thy youth, but be thou an example
to the believers, both in word and conduct.” <span
id="fr_1281"></span>[1282](#fn_1281.md)
