<span id="xiii.ix-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xiii.ix-p1.1"></span>Session XVI.</span>

<span id="xiii.ix-p2"></span>(Labbe and Cossart, *Concilia*, Tom. VI.,
col. 1010.)

<span id="xiii.ix-p3"></span>\[*The Acclamations of the Fathers*.\]

<span id="xiii.ix-p4"></span>Many years to the Emperor!  Many years to
Constantine, our great Emperor!  Many years to the Orthodox King!  Many
years to our Emperor that maketh peace!  Many years to Constantine, a
second Martian!  Many years to Constantine, a new Theodosius!  Many
years to Constantine, a new Justinian!  Many years to the keeper of the
orthodox faith!  O Lord preserve the foundation of the Churches!  O Lord
preserve the keeper of the faith!

<span id="xiii.ix-p5"></span>Many years to Agatho, Pope of Rome!  Many
years to George, Patriarch of Constantinople!  Many years to Theophanus,
Patriarch of Antioch!  Many years to the orthodox council!  Many years
to the orthodox Senate!

<span id="xiii.ix-p6"></span>To Theodore of Pharan, the heretic,
anathema!  To Sergius, the heretic, anathema!  To Cyrus, the heretic,
anathema!  To Honorius, the heretic, anathema!  To Pyrrhus, the heretic,
anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p7"></span>To Paul the heretic, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p8"></span>To Peter the heretic, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p9"></span>To Macarius the heretic, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p10"></span>To Stephen the heretic, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p11"></span>To Polychronius the heretic, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p12"></span>To Apergius of Perga the heretic,
anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p13"></span>To all heretics, anathema!  To all who
side with heretics, anathema!

<span id="xiii.ix-p14"></span>May the faith of the Christians increase,
and long years to the orthodox and Ecumenical Council!
