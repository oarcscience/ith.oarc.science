<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p1.1"></span>Canon XXXIX.</span>

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p2.1"></span>It</span> is not lawful to feast
together with the heathen, and to be partakers of their godlessness.

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p3"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p3.1"></span>Notes.</span>

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p4"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p4.1"></span>Ancient Epitome of Canons XXXVII.,
XXXVIII, and XXXIX.</span>

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p5"></span>Thou shalt not keep feasts with
Hebrews or heretics, nor receive festival offerings from them.

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p6"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p6.1"></span>Balsamon.</span>

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p7"></span>Read canon lxx. and canon lxxj.
of the Holy Apostles, and Canon lx. <span
id="fr_196"></span>[197](#fn_196.md) of the Synod of Carthage.

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p9"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p9.1"></span>Aristenus.</span>

<span id="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p10"></span>Light hath no communion with
darkness.  Therefore no Christian should celebrate a feast with heretics
or Jews, neither should he receive anything connected with these feasts
such as azymes and the like.
