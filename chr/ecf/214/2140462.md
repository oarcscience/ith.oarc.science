<span id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span

<span id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span

<span id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p3"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p3.1"></span>Notes.</span>

<span id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p5"></span>*He that is illuminated is to recite*
(<span class="Greek"><span
id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p5.1"></span>ἀπαγγελλέτω</span> ) *the faith on the
fifth feria of the week.*

<span id="xiv.iii.lxxix-p6"></span>This is a renewal of canon xlvi. of
Laodicea.
