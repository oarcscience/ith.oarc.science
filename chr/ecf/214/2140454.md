<span id="xiv.iii.lxxi-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xiv.iii.lxxi-p1.1"></span>Canon LXX.</span>

<span id="xiv.iii.lxxi-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xiv.iii.lxxi-p2.1"></span>Women</span> are not permitted to speak at
the time of the Divine Liturgy; but, according to the word of Paul the
Apostle, “let them be silent.  For it is not permitted to them to speak,
but to be in subjection, as the law also saith.  But if they wish to
learn anything let them ask their own husbands at home.”
