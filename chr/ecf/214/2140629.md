<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p1.1"></span>Canon CV.  (Greek cviii.)</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p2"></span>Of those who do not communicate in
Africa and would go across seas.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p3"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p3.1"></span>Whoever</span> does not communicate in
Africa, and goes to communicate across seas, let him be cast out of the
clergy.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p4"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p4.1"></span>Notes.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p5"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p5.1"></span>Ancient Epitome of Canon CV.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p6"></span>Whoever is cut off from communion in
Africa, and goes to parts across seas that he may there communicate, is
to be cast out of the clergy.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p7"></span>This canon is Canon j. of Carthage,
<span class="sc"><span id="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p7.1"></span>a.d.</span> 407.
