<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p1.1"></span>Canon XLVI.  (Greek l.)</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p2"></span>Concerning the passions of the
martyrs.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p3"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p3.1"></span>The</span> passions of the Martyrs may
be read when their anniversary days are celebrated.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p4"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p4.1"></span>Note.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p5"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p5.1"></span>Ancient Epitome of Canon XLVI.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p6"></span>The passions of the martyrs are to
be read on their commemorations.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p7"></span>This canon is the last part of Canon
xxxvj. of the Synod of Hippo, <span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p7.1"></span>a.d.</span> 393.
