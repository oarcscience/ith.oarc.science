<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p1.1"></span>Canon XXIV.  (Greek xxvii.)</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p2"></span>That nothing be read in church besides
the Canonical Scripture.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p3"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p3.1"></span>Item</span>, that besides the Canonical
Scriptures nothing be read in church under the name of divine Scripture.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p4"></span>But the Canonical Scriptures are as
follows:

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p5"></span>Genesis.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p6"></span>Exodus.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p7"></span>Leviticus.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p8"></span>Numbers.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p9"></span>Deuteronomy.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p10"></span>Joshua the Son of Nun.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p11"></span>The Judges.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p12"></span>Ruth.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p13"></span>The Kings, iv. books.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p14"></span><span id="page_454">p. 454</span>
<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-Page_454"></span>The Chronicles, ij. books.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p15"></span>Job.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p16"></span>The Psalter.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p17"></span>The Five books of Solomon.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p18"></span>The Twelve Books of the Prophets.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p19"></span>Isaiah.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p20"></span>Jeremiah.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p21"></span>Ezechiel.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p22"></span>Daniel.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p23"></span>Tobit.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p24"></span>Judith.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p25"></span>Esther.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p26"></span>Ezra, ij. books.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p27"></span>Macchabees, ij. books.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p28"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p28.1"></span>The New Testament.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p29"></span>The Gospels, iv. books.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p30"></span>The Acts of the Apostles, j. book.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p31"></span>The Epistles of Paul, xiv.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p32"></span>The Epistles of Peter, the Apostle,
ij.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p33"></span>The Epistles of John the Apostle,
iij.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p34"></span>The Epistles of James the Apostle, j.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p35"></span>The Epistle of Jude the Apostle, j.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p36"></span>The Revelation of John, j. book.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p37"></span>Let this be sent to our brother and
fellow bishop, Boniface, and to the other bishops of those parts, that
they may confirm this canon, for these are the things which we have
received from our fathers to be read in church.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p38"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p38.1"></span>Notes.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p39"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p39.1"></span>Ancient Epitome of Canon XXIV.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p40"></span>Let nothing besides the canonical
Scriptures be read in church.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p41"></span>This is Canon xxxvj. of Hippo., 393. 
The last phrase allowing the reading of the “passions of the Martyrs” on
their Anniversaries is omitted from the African code.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p42"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p42.1"></span>Johnson.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p43"></span>These two books \[i.e. the two
Maccabees\] are mentioned only in Dionysius Exiguus’s copy.  See *Can.
Ap. ult., Can. Laod. ult*.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.xxv-p44"></span>“Boniface,” i.e., Bishop of Rome.
