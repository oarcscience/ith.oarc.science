<span id="xvii.xxvii-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xvii.xxvii-p1.1"></span>Of the Same to Agatho the Bishop.</span>

<span id="xvii.xxvii-p2"></span>Whereas Maximus has for ten years lived
in unlawful marriage, but pretends that it was through ignorance, and
that they are now parted by mutual consent, let them stand among the
catechumens, if it appear that they be in earnest.
