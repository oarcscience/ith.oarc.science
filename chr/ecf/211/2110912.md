#### <span id="iv.v.vii.xxi-p0.1"></span>Chapter XXI.

<span id="iv.v.vii.xxi-p1"></span>A question how if we obey the commands
of Christ we can fail of evangelical perfection.

<span id="iv.v.vii.xxi-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.v.vii.xxi-p2.1"></span>Germanus</span>: How can we blame one who
satisfies the command of the Gospel and not only does not retaliate, but
is actually prepared to have a double wrong offered to him?
