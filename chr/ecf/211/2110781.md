#### <span id="iv.iv.x.xi-p0.1"></span>Chapter XI.

<span id="iv.iv.x.xi-p1"></span>Of Supplications.

<span id="iv.iv.x.xi-p2"></span>“I <span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iv.x.xi-p2.1"></span>exhort</span> therefore first of all that
supplications be made.” Supplication is an imploring or petition
concerning sins, in which one who is sorry for his present or past deeds
asks for pardon.
