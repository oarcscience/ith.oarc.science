#### <span id="iv.v.viii.xiii-p0.1"></span>Chapter XIII.

<span id="iv.v.viii.xiii-p1"></span>Our answer as to the reason which
demanded an oath from us.

<span id="iv.v.viii.xiii-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.v.viii.xiii-p2.1"></span>Germanus</span>: The first reason, as we
said, was that we were afraid of vexing our Elders and resisting their
orders; the second was that we very foolishly believed that, if we had
learnt from you anything perfect or splendid to hear or look at, when we
returned to the monastery, we should be able to perform it.
