#### <span id="iv.iii.xi.xii-p0.1"></span>Chapter XII.

<span id="iv.iii.xi.xii-p1"></span>Several testimonies against
vainglory.

<span id="iv.iii.xi.xii-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iii.xi.xii-p2.1"></span>And</span> so the Apostle warns us: “Be
not desirous of vainglory.” <span id="fr_1011"></span>[1012](#fn_1011.md)
And the Lord, rebuking the Pharisees, says, “How can ye believe, who
receive glory from one another, and seek not the glory which comes from
God alone?” <span id="fr_1012"></span>[1013](#fn_1012.md) Of these too the
blessed David speaks with a threat: “For God hath scattered the bones of
them that please men.” <span id="fr_1013"></span>[1014](#fn_1013.md)
