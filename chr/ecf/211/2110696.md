#### <span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p0.1"></span>Chapter V.

<span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p1"></span>An objection, how God Himself can be
said to create evil.

<span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iv.vii.v-p2.1"></span>Germanus</span>: We often read in holy
Scripture that God has created evil or brought it upon men, as is this
passage: “There is none beside Me. I am the Lord, and there is none
else: I form the light and create darkness, I make peace, and create
evil.” <span id="fr_1378"></span>[1379](#fn_1378) And again: “Shall
there be evil in a city which the Lord hath not done?” <span
id="fr_1379"></span>[1380](#fn_1379)
