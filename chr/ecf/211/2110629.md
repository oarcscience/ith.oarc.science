#### <span id="iv.iv.iv.xi-p0.1"></span>Chapter XI.

<span id="iv.iv.iv.xi-p1"></span>A question on the free will of man and
the grace of God.

<span id="iv.iv.iv.xi-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iv.iv.xi-p2.1"></span>Germanus</span>: Where then is there room
for free will, and how is it ascribed to our efforts that we are worthy
of praise, if God both begins and ends everything in us which concerns
our salvation?
