#### <span id="iv.iii.ii.i-p0.1"></span>Chapter I.

<span id="iv.iii.ii.i-p1"></span>Of the Canonical System of the
Nocturnal Prayers and Psalms.

<span id="iv.iii.ii.i-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iii.ii.i-p2.1"></span>Girt</span>, therefore, with this twofold
girdle of which we have spoken, <span id="fr_671"></span>[672](#fn_671)
the soldier of Christ should next learn the system of the canonical
prayers and Psalms which was long ago arranged by the holy fathers in
the East. Of their character, however, and of the way in which we can
pray, as the Apostle directs, “without ceasing,” <span
id="fr_672"></span>[673](#fn_672) we shall treat, as the Lord may enable
us, in the proper place, when we begin to relate the Conferences of the
Elders.
