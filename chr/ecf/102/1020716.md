<span id="v.iv.xx-p1"></span><span class="c2"><span
id="v.iv.xx-p1.1"></span>Chapter 20.—The Resurrection to
Damnation.</span>

<span id="v.iv.xx-p2"></span>19.  Now he whose soul does not die to this
world and begin here to be conformed to the truth, falls when the body
dies into a more terrible death, and shall revive, not to change his
earthly for a heavenly habitation, but to endure the penalty of his sin.
