<span id="v.iv.xxi-p1"></span><span class="c2"><span
id="v.iv.xxi-p1.1"></span>Chapter 21.—Neither Body Nor Soul Extinguished
at Death.</span>

<span id="v.iv.xxi-p2"></span>And so faith clings to the assurance, and
we must believe that it is so in fact, that neither the human soul nor
the human body suffers complete extinction, but that the wicked rise
again to endure inconceivable punishment, and the good to receive
eternal life.
