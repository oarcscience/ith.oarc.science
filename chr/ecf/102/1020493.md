id="iv.XVII-p1.1"></span>Book XVII.</span>

<span id="iv.XVII-p2"></span> ————————————

<span id="iv.XVII-p3"></span>Argument—In this book the history of the
city of God is traced during the period of the kings and prophets from
Samuel to David, even to Christ; and the prophecies which are recorded
in the books of Kings, Psalms, and those of Solomon, are interpreted of
Christ and the church.
