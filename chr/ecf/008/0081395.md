id="viii.iii.ii.iii-p1.1"></span>II.</span>

<span id="viii.iii.ii.iii-p2"></span>On the Ordination of Presbyters and
Deacons.

<span id="viii.iii.ii.iii-p3"></span>Ordinations of presbyters and
Levites, moreover, solemnly perform on a suitable occasion, and in the
presence of many witnesses; and to <span id="page_612">p. 612</span>
<span id="viii.iii.ii.iii-Page_612"></span>this duty advance tried and
learned men, that ye may be greatly gladdened by their fellowship and
help.  Place the confidence of your hearts without ceasing on the
goodness of God, and declare these and the other divine words to
succeeding generations:  “For this is our God for ever and ever, and He
will guide us to eternity.” <span id="fr_2720"></span>[2721](#fn_2720.md)  
Given on the 7th November, in the consulship of the most illustrious
Saturninus and Gallicanus. <span id="fr_2721"></span>[2722](#fn_2721.md)
