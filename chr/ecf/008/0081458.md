id="ix.v.viii-p1.1"></span>VIII.</span>

<span id="ix.v.viii-p2"></span>*From the homily about the town of
Antioch.* <span id="fr_2955"></span>[2956](#fn_2955.md)

<span id="ix.v.viii-p4"></span>To Simon was allotted Rome, <span
id="fr_2956"></span>[2957](#fn_2956.md) and to John Ephesus; to Thomas
India, and to Addæus the country of the Assyrians. <span
id="fr_2957"></span>[2958](#fn_2957.md)   And, when they were sent each one
of them to the district which had been allotted to him, they devoted
themselves <span id="fr_2958"></span>[2959](#fn_2958.md) to bring the
*several* countries to discipleship.
