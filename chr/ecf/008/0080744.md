<span id="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p1.1"></span>Chapter LVII.—Teaching of Christ.</span>

<span id="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p2"></span>“But to those who are persuaded that
He is evil, as the Scriptures say, He said, ‘Call not <span
id="page_249">p. 249</span> <span id="vi.iv.vi.lvii-Page_249"></span>me
good, for One *only* is good.’ <span
id="fr_1015"></span>[1016](#fn_1015.md)   And again, ‘Be ye good and
merciful, as your Father in the heavens, who makes the sun rise on good
and evil men, and brings rain upon just and unjust.’ <span
id="fr_1016"></span>[1017](#fn_1016.md)   But to those who were misled to
imagine many gods, as the Scriptures say, He said, ‘Hear, O Israel; the
Lord your God is one Lord.’” <span id="fr_1017"></span>[1018](#fn_1017.md)
