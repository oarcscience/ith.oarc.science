<span id="vi.iv.vi.lvi-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vi.iv.vi.lvi-p1.1"></span>Chapter LVI.—Teaching of Christ.</span>

<span id="vi.iv.vi.lvi-p2"></span>“And to those who think that He is not
good, as the Scriptures say, He said, ‘From which of you shall his son
ask bread, and he will give him a stone; or shall ask a fish, and he
will give him a serpent?  If ye then, being evil, know to give good
gifts to your children, how much more shall your heavenly Father give
good things to those who ask Him, and to those who do His will!’ <span
id="fr_1012"></span>[1013](#fn_1012)   But to those who affirmed that He
was in the temple, He said, ‘Swear not by heaven, for it is God’s
throne; nor by the earth, for it is the footstool of His feet.’ <span
id="fr_1013"></span>[1014](#fn_1013)   And to those who supposed that
God is pleased with sacrifices, He said, ‘God wishes mercy, and not
sacrifices’ <span id="fr_1014"></span>[1015](#fn_1014) —the knowledge of
Himself, and not holocausts.
