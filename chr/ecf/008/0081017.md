<span id="vi.iv.xvi.xii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vi.iv.xvi.xii-p1.1"></span>Chapter XII.—The Difficulty
Solved.</span>

<span id="vi.iv.xvi.xii-p2"></span>And Peter said:  “Let not wickedness
have dominion over us, finding a pretext in Providence and your
affection for your mother; but rather abide this day in your fast, and I
shall join you in it, and tomorrow she will be baptized.  And, besides,
this hour of the day is not suitable for baptism.”  Then we all agreed
that it should be so.
