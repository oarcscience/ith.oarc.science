<span id="x.iii.li-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="x.iii.li-p1.1"></span>Chapter 51.—From the Apocalypse.</span>

<span id="x.iii.li-p2"></span>The Revelation of John likewise tells us
that in a new song these praises are offered to Christ: “Thou art worthy
to take the book, and to open the seals thereof: for Thou wast slain,
and hast redeemed us to God by Thy blood out of every kindred, and
tongue, and people, and nation.” <span id="fr_383"></span>[384](#fn_383)
