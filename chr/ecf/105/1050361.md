<span id="xiii.xxix"></span>

<span id="xiii.xxix-p1"></span> (25.) Hence the force of the statement:
“There was no injustice in my hands, but my prayer was pure.” <span
id="fr_1467"></span>[1468](#fn_1467.md) For the purity of his prayer arose
from this circumstance, that it was not improper for him to ask
forgiveness in prayer, when he really bestowed forgiveness himself.
