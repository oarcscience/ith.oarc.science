<span id="xiii.xi-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="xiii.xi-p1.1"></span>(7.) The Seventh Breviate.</span>

<span id="xiii.xi-p2"></span>VII. “The next question we shall have to
propose,” he says, “is, whether God wishes that man be without sin.
Beyond doubt God wishes it; and no doubt he has the ability. For who is
so foolhardy as to hesitate to believe that to be possible, which he has
no doubt about God’s wishing?” This is the answer. If God wished not
that man should be without sin, He would not have sent His Son without
sin, to heal men of their sins. This takes place in believers who are
being renewed day by day, <span id="fr_1375"></span>[1376](#fn_1375)
until their righteousness becomes perfect, like fully restored health.
