<span id="ix.ccciv-p1"></span> <span class="c18"><span
id="ix.ccciv-p1.1"></span>Letter CCCIII.</span>

<span id="ix.ccciv-p2"></span>To the Comes Privatarum.

<span id="ix.ccciv-p3"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="ix.ccciv-p3.1"></span>You</span> have, I think, been led to impose a
contribution of mares <span id="fr_3260"></span>[3261](#fn_3260.md) on
these people by false information on the part of the inhabitants.  What
is going on is quite unfair.  It cannot but be displeasing to your
excellency, and is distressing to me on account of my intimate connexion
with the victims of the wrong.  I have therefore lost no time in begging
your Lordship not to allow these promoters of iniquity to succeed in
their malevolence.
