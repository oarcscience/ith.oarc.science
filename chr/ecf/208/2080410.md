<span id="ix.cccxxxviii-p1"></span> <span class="c18"><span
id="ix.cccxxxviii-p1.1"></span>Letter CCCXLIII.</span>

<span id="ix.cccxxxviii-p2"></span>Libanius to Basil.

<span id="ix.cccxxxviii-p3"></span>IF these are the words of an
untrained tongue, what would you be if you would polish them?  On your
lips live fountains of words better than the flowing of springs.  I, on
the contrary, if I am not daily watered, am silent.
