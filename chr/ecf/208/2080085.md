<span id="ix.xiii-p3"></span>*To Olympius*. <span

<span id="ix.xiii-p5"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="ix.xiii-p5.1"></span>Before</span> you did write me a few words: 
now not even a few.  Your brevity will soon become silence.  Return to
your old ways, and do not let me have to scold you for your laconic
behaviour.  But I shall be glad even of a little letter in token of your
great love.  Only write to me.
