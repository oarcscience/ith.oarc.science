<span id="ix.clxxxiii-p1"></span> <span class="c18"><span
id="ix.clxxxiii-p1.1"></span>Letter CLXXXII. <span

<span id="ix.clxxxiii-p3"></span>*To the presbyters of Samosata*.

<span id="ix.clxxxiii-p4"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="ix.clxxxiii-p4.1"></span>Grieved</span> as I am at the desolation of
the Church, <span id="fr_2596"></span>[2597](#fn_2596.md) I none the less
congratulate you on having been brought so soon to this extreme limit of
your hard struggle.  God grant that you may pass through it with
patience, to the end that in return for your faithful stewardship, and
the noble constancy which you have shewn in Christ’s cause, you may
receive the great reward.
