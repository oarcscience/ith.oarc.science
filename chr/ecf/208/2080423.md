id="ix.cccli-p1.1"></span>Letter CCCLVI.</span>

<span id="ix.cccli-p2"></span>Basil to Libanius.

<span id="ix.cccli-p3"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="ix.cccli-p3.1"></span>I am</span> delighted at receiving what you
write, but when you ask me to reply, I am in a difficulty.  What could I
say in answer to so Attic a tongue, except that I confess, and confess
with joy, that I am a pupil of fishermen?
