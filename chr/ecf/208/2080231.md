<span id="ix.clix-p1"></span> <span class="c18"><span
id="ix.clix-p1.1"></span>Letter CLVIII. <span
id="fr_2516"></span>[2517](#fn_2516) </span>

<span id="ix.clix-p3"></span>*To Antiochus*.

<span id="ix.clix-p4"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="ix.clix-p4.1"></span>My</span> sins have prevented me from carrying
out the wish to meet you, which I have long entertained.  Let me
apologise by letter for my absence, and beseech you not to omit to
remember me in your prayers, that, if I live, I may be permitted to
enjoy your <span id="page_212">p. 212</span> <span
id="ix.clix-Page_212"></span>society.  If not, by the aid of your
prayers may I quit this world with good hope.  I commend to you our
brother the camel-master.
