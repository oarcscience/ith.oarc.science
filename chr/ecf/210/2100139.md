#### <span id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p0.1"></span>Chapter I.

<span id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p1"></span>The Spirit is the Lord and Power; and
in this is not inferior to the Father and the Son.

<span id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p2"></span>17. <span class="sc"><span
id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p2.1"></span>Above</span>, you read that “the Lord
blessed him, and the Spirit of the Lord began to go with him.” <span
id="fr_1026"></span>[1027](#fn_1026.md) Farther on it is said: “And the
Spirit of the Lord came upon him.” <span
id="fr_1027"></span>[1028](#fn_1027.md) Again he says: “If I be shaven, my
strength will depart from me.” <span
id="fr_1028"></span>[1029](#fn_1028.md) After he was shaven, see what the
Scripture says: “The Lord,” he says, “departed from him.” <span
id="fr_1029"></span>[1030](#fn_1029.md)

<span id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p7"></span>18. You see, then, that He Who went
with him, Himself departed from him. The Same is, then, the Lord, Who is
the Spirit of the Lord, that is, he called the Spirit of God, Lord, as
also the Apostle says: “The Lord is the Spirit, now where the Spirit of
the Lord is, there is liberty.” You find, then, the Holy Spirit called
the Lord; for the Holy Spirit and the Son are not one Person \[*unus*\]
but one Substance \[*unum*\].

<span id="iv.ii.iii.ii-p8"></span>19. In this place he used the word
Power, and implied the Spirit. For as the Father is Power, so, too, the
Son is Power, and the Holy Spirit is Power. Of the Son you have read
that Christ is “the Power of God and the Wisdom of God.” <span
id="fr_1030"></span>[1031](#fn_1030.md) We read, too, that the Father is
Power, as it is written: “Ye shall see the Son of Man sitting at the
right hand of the Power of God.” <span
id="fr_1031"></span>[1032](#fn_1031.md) He certainly named the Father
Power, at Whose right hand the Son sits, as you read: “The Lord said
unto My Lord, Sit Thou on My right hand.” <span
id="fr_1032"></span>[1033](#fn_1032.md) And the Lord Himself named the Holy
Spirit Power, when He said: “Ye shall receive Power when the Holy Spirit
cometh upon you.” <span id="fr_1033"></span>[1034](#fn_1033.md)
