<span id="iv.x.xl-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="iv.x.xl-p1.1"></span>Chapter 38.—Eternal Fire Torturing the Wicked,
Not Evil.</span>

<span id="iv.x.xl-p2"></span>For neither is eternal fire itself, which
is to torture the impious, an evil nature, since it has its measure, its
form and its order depraved by no iniquity; but it is an evil torture
for the damned, to whose sins it is due.  For neither is yonder light,
because it tortures the blear-eyed, an evil nature.
