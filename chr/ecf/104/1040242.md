<span id="v.iv.iii-p1"></span> <span class="c7"><span
id="v.iv.iii-p1.1"></span>Book I.</span>

<span id="v.iv.iii-p2"></span>He proves that baptism can be conferred
outside the Catholic communion by heretics or schismatics, but that it
ought not to be received from them; and that it is of no avail to any
while in a state of heresy or schism.
