<span id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-p1.1"></span>Chapter 86.—</span>188.  <span
class="c11"><span id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-p1.2"></span>Petilianus</span>
said:  "Is it then the case that God has ordered the massacre even of
schismatics? and if He were to issue such an order at all, you ought to
be slain by some barbarians and Scythians, not by Christians."

<span id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-p2"></span>189.  <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-p2.1"></span>Augustin</span> answered:  Let your
Circumcelliones remain quiet, and let me entreat you not to terrify us
about barbarians.  But as to whether we or you are schismatics, let the
question be put neither to you nor to me, but to Christ, that He may
show where His Church is to be found.  Read the gospel <span
id="page_575">p. 575</span> <span
id="v.v.iv.lxxxvi-Page_575"></span>then, and there you find the answer,
"In Jerusalem, and in all Judea, and in Samaria and even in the whole
earth." <span id="fr_2206"></span>[2207](#fn_2206)   If any one,
therefore, is not found within the Church, let not any further question
be put to him, but let him either be corrected or converted, or else,
being detected, let him not complain.
