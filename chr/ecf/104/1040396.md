<span id="v.iv.viii.xlii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.iv.viii.xlii-p1.1"></span>Chapter 42.—</span>81.  Adelphius of
Thasbalte <span id="fr_1742"></span>[1743](#fn_1742.md) said:  "It is
surely without cause that they find fault with the truth in false and
invidious terms, saying that we rebaptize, since the Church does not
rebaptize heretics, but baptizes them." <span
id="fr_1743"></span>[1744](#fn_1743.md)

<span id="v.iv.viii.xlii-p4"></span>82.  Truly enough it does not
rebaptize them, because it only baptizes those who were not baptized
before; and this earlier custom has only been confirmed in a later
Council by a more careful perfecting of the truth.
