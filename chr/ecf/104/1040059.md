<span id="iv.v.ii-p1"></span><span class="c7"><span
id="iv.v.ii-p1.1"></span>On the Morals of the Manichæans.</span>

<span id="iv.v.ii-p2"></span><span class="c2"><span
id="iv.v.ii-p2.1"></span>\[De Moribus Manichæorum.\] <span
class="c11"><span id="iv.v.ii-p2.2"></span>a.d.</span> 388.</span>

<span id="iv.v.ii-p3"></span>*Containing a particular refutation of the
doctrine of these heretics regarding the origin and nature of evil; an
exposure of their pretended symbolical customs of the mouth, of the
hands, and of the breast; and a condemnation of their superstitious
abstinence and unholy mysteries.  Lastly, some crimes brought to light
among the Manichæans are mentioned.*
