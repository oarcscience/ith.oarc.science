<span id="iv.x.xxxi-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="iv.x.xxxi-p1.1"></span>Chapter 29.—That God is Not Defiled by Our
Sins.</span>

<span id="iv.x.xxxi-p2"></span>And yet, though all things that He
established are in Him, those who sin do not defile Him, of whose wisdom
it is said:  "She touches all things by reason of her purity, and
nothing defiled assails her." <span id="fr_1109"></span>[1110](#fn_1109)
  For it behooves us to believe that as God is incorruptible and
unchangeable, so also is He consequently undefilable.
