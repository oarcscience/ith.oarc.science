##  IV.-A Point in the Same Disputation.<sup>[1](footnote/fn31)</sup>

------------

Did I not say before that you do not admit that the only-begotten Son,
who is from all eternity before every creature, was made substantially
existent<sup>[2](footnote/fn31.htm#P3188_814458)</sup> in the whole
person of the Saviour;<sup>[3](footnote/fn31.htm#P3189_814539)</sup>
that is to say, was united *with Him* according to substance?
