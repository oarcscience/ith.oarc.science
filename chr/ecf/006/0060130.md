##  General Note.

(Vexillas,-as they are called, p. 399.)

IT is very curious to note how certain ideas are inherited from the
earliest Fathers, and travel down, as here, to find a new expression in
a distant age. Here our author reflects Justin
Martyr,<sup>[16](footnote/fn63.htm#P6311_1930437.md)</sup> and the
*Labarum*<sup>[17](footnote/fn63.htm#P6312_1930491.md)</sup> itself is the
outcrop of what Justin wrote to Antoninus Pius.
