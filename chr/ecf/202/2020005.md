emendations needed in the edition of Socrates. The text of our historian
has not been as thoroughly and completely examined and corrected as
other writings. Valesius’ edition (Hussey) gives an account of a few
<span class="c13"><span id="ii.iii.i-p21.1"></span>mss</span>. examined
