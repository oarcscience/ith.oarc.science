<span id="v.iii.lxxiv-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.lxxiv-p2.1"></span>Archelaus</span>, <span
id="fr_2490"></span>[2491](#fn_2490) bishop of Mesopotamia, composed in
the Syriac language, a book of the discussion which he held with
Manichaeus, when he came from Persia. This book, which is translated
into Greek, is possessed by many.

<span id="v.iii.lxxiv-p4"></span>He flourished under the Emperor Probus,
who succeeded Aurelianus and Tacitus.
