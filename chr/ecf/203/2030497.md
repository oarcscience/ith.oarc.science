<span id="v.iii.cxvi-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.cxvi-p2.1"></span>Epiphanius</span>, <span
id="fr_2546"></span>[2547](#fn_2546.md) bishop of Salamina in Cyprus, wrote
books *Against all heresies* and many others which are eagerly read by
the learned, on account of their subject matter, and also by the plain
people, on account of their language. He is still living, and in his
extreme old age composes various brief works.
