<span id="v.iii.xli-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.xli-p2.1"></span>Miltiades <span
id="fr_2446"></span>[2447](#fn_2446.md) </span> of whom Rhodo gives an
account in the work which he wrote against Montanus, Prisca and
Maximilla, wrote a considerable volume against these same persons, and
other books *Against the nations and the Jews* and addressed an
*Apology* to the then ruling emperors. He flourished in the reign of
Marcus Antoninus and Commodus.
