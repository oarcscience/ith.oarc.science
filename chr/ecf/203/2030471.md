<span id="v.iii.xc-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.xc-p2.1"></span>Anthony <span
id="fr_2512"></span>[2513](#fn_2512) </span> the monk, whose life
Athanasius bishop of Alexandria wrote a long work upon, sent seven
letters in Coptic to various monasteries, letters truly apostolic in
idea and language, and which have been translated into Greek. The chief
of these is *To the Arsenoites.* He flourished during the reign of
Constantinus and his sons.
