id="fr_2540"></span>[2541](#fn_2540) </span> the African, bishop of
Milevis, <span id="fr_2541"></span>[2542](#fn_2541) during the reign of
the Emperors Valentinianus and Valens, wrote in behalf of the Catholic
party six books against the calumny of the Donatian party, in which he
asserts that the crime of the Donatists is falsely charged upon the
catholic party.
