id="fr_2659"></span>[2660](#fn_2659) </span> the deacon. composed out of
the traditions of the fathers a *Rule for monks,* which is accustomed to
be read in the monastery for the profit of the assembled monks. It is
written in condensed and clear language and covers the whole range of
monastic duties.
