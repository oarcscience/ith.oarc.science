<span id="iv.x.lv-p1"></span> *LV. Festal.*

<span id="iv.x.lv-p2"></span>We are much distressed, for we are gifted
with the nature not of rocks but of men, but the recollection of the
Lord’s Epiphany has been to me a very potent medicine; so at once I
write, according to the custom of the feast, and salute your
magnificence with a prayer that you may live in prosperity and repute.
