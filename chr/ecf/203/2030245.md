<span id="iv.x.lvi-p1"></span> *LVI. Festal.*

<span id="iv.x.lvi-p2"></span>My grief is now at its height and my mind
is seriously affected by it, but I have thought it right to fulfil the
custom of the feast, so now I take my pen to salute your reverence and
pay the debt of affection.
