<span id="vi.xi.iii.xxi-p1"></span> 17. Once more, in his letter to
Marcella he says: <span id="fr_2964"></span>[2965](#fn_2964.md)

<span id="vi.xi.iii.xxi-p3"></span>“Ambrose, who supplied the paper, the
money and the secretaries by the aid of which our Adamantius <span
id="fr_2965"></span>[2966](#fn_2965.md) and Chalcenterus <span
id="fr_2966"></span>[2967](#fn_2966.md) completed his innumerable books, in
a certain letter written to the same person from Athens, declares that
he never had a meal, when Origen was present, without something being
read, and that he never went to bed without having some brother read
aloud from the holy Scriptures. This he said he continued day and night,
so that prayer waited upon reading and reading upon prayer.”
