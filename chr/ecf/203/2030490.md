<span id="v.iii.cix-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.cix-p2.1"></span>Photinus</span>, <span
id="fr_2536"></span>[2537](#fn_2536.md) of Gallograecia, a disciple of
Marcellus, and ordained bishop of Sirmium, attempted to introduce the
Ebionite heresy, and afterwards having been expelled from the church by
the Emperor Valentinianus, wrote many volumes, among which the most
distinguished are *Against the nations,* and *To Valentinianus.*
