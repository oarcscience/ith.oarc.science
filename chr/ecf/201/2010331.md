<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p2"></span>Bishops of Rome.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p3"></span>(Dates taken from the table given by
Lipsius in his *<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p3.1"></span>Chronologie der

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p4"></span>Linus.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p5"></span>Pontianus, 5 years 2 months 7 days;
(July 21?), 230–Sept. 28, 235.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p6"></span>Anencletus.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p7"></span>Anteros, 1 month 12 days; Nov. 21,
235–Jan. 3, 236.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p8"></span>Clement.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p9"></span>Fabianus, 14 years 10 days; 236–Jan.
20, 250. Vacancy from Jan. 21, 250–March, 251.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p10"></span>Evarestus.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p11"></span>Cornelius, 2 years 3 months 10 days;
beginning of March, 251–middle of June, 253.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p12"></span>Alexander.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p13"></span>Lucius, 8 months 10 days; June
(25?), 253–March 5, 254.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p14"></span>Xystus I, for about ten years; died
between 124 and 126.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p15"></span>Stephanus, 3 years 2 months 21 days;
(May 12?), 254–Aug. 6, 258.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p16"></span>Telesphorus, 11 years; died between
135 and 137.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p17"></span>Xystus II., 11 months 12 (6?) days;
Aug. 24 (31?), 257–Aug. 6, 258.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p18"></span>Hyginus, 4 years; died between 139
and 141.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p19"></span>Dionysius, 9 years 5 months 2 days;
July 22, 259–Dec. 27, 268.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p20"></span>Pius, 15–16 years; died between 154
and 156.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p21"></span>Felix I., 5 years 11 months 25 days;
Jan. 5, 269–Dec. 30, 274.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p22"></span>Anicetus, 11–12 years; died in 166
or 167.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p23"></span>Eutychian, 8 years 11 months 3 days;
(Jan. 5?) 275–Dec. 8, 283.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p24"></span>Soter, 8–9 years; died in 174 or
175.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p25"></span>Caius, 12 years 4 months 6 days;
Dec. 17, 283–April 22, 296.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p26"></span>Eleutherus, 15 years; died in 189.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p27"></span>Marcellinus, 8 years 3 months 25

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p28"></span>Victor, 9–10 years; 189–198 or 199.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p29"></span>Marcellus, 1 year 7 months 21 days;
(May 24?), 307–Jan. 15, 309.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p30"></span>Zephyrinus, 18–19 years; 198 or
199–217 (Aug. 26?).

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p31"></span>Eusebius, 3 (4?) months 23 (16?)
days; April 23 (16?), 309–Aug. 17, 309. Vacancy until 310.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p32"></span>Callistus, 5 years; 217–Oct. 14,
222.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p33"></span>Miltiades, 3 years 6 months 8 days;
July 2, 310–Jan. 10 (11?), 314.

<span id="iii.xvii.xxiv-p34"></span>Urbanus, 8 years; 222–230 (May 19?).
