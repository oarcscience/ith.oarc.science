<span id="iv.vi.iv.xxxiv-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iv.xxxiv-p1.1"></span>Chapter XXXIV</span>.—*That he wrote to
Eusebius respecting Easter, and respecting Copies of the Holy
Scriptures.*

<span id="iv.vi.iv.xxxiv-p2"></span><span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iv.xxxiv-p2.1"></span>Ever</span> careful for the welfare of
the churches of God, the emperor addressed me personally in a letter on
the means of providing copies of the inspired oracles, and also on the
subject of the most holy feast of Easter. For I had myself dedicated to
him an exposition of the mystical import of that feast; and the manner
in which he honored me with a reply may be understood by any one who
reads the following letter.
