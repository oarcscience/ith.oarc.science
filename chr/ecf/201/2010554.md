<span id="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p1.1"></span>Chapter XXXV</span>.—*Description of the
Atrium and Porticos.*

<span id="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p2"></span><span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p2.1"></span>The</span> next object of his attention
was a space of ground of great extent, and open to the pure air of
heaven. This he adorned with a pavement of finely polished stone, and
enclosed it on three sides with porticos of great length.
