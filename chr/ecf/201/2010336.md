<span id="iii.xvii.xxix-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span

<span id="iii.xvii.xxix-p2"></span>The months of the Macedonian year, as
commonly employed in the time of Eusebius, corresponded exactly to the
Roman months, but the year began with the first of September. The names
of the months were as follows:—
