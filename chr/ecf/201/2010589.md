<span id="iv.vi.iv.iii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iv.iii-p1.1"></span>Chapter III</span>.—*Equalization of the
More Oppressive Taxes.*

<span id="iv.vi.iv.iii-p2"></span><span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.iv.iii-p2.1"></span>And</span> whereas some persons found
fault with the surveys of land which had been made under former
emperors, and complained that their property was unduly burdened; acting
in this case also on the principles of justice, he sent commissioners to
equalize the tribute, and to secure immunity to those who had made this
appeal.
