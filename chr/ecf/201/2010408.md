<span id="iv.vi.i.xxiii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.i.xxiii-p1.1"></span>Chapter XXIII</span>.—*A Brief Notice of
the Destruction of the Tyrants.*

<span id="iv.vi.i.xxiii-p2"></span><span class="c11"><span
id="iv.vi.i.xxiii-p2.1"></span>With</span> respect to the other princes,
who made war against the churches of God, I have not thought it fit in
the present work to give any account of their downfall, <span
id="fr_3099"></span>[3100](#fn_3099.md) nor to stain the memory of the good
by mentioning them in connection with those of an opposite character.
The knowledge of the facts themselves will of itself suffice for the
wholesome admonition of those who have witnessed or heard of the evils
which severally befell them.
