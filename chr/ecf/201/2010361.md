id="iv.iii.iii.i-p4.2"></span>

<span id="iv.iii.iii.i-p5"></span> It is curious that there should be no
critical edition of the collected works of so considerable a writer. A
large portion of his works are, to be sure, included in Migne’s
*Patrologia Latina,* vol. 84, Paris, 1844; but this *Opera Universa* is
neither wholly complete nor in any sense critical, and this seems to be
the only attempt at a collection. The works enumerated here are mostly
in the edition of Migne, but not all.
