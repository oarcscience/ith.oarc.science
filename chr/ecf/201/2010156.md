<span id="iii.x.xvi-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="iii.x.xvi-p1.1"></span>Chapter XV.—</span>*The Schism of Blastus at
Rome.* <span id="fr_1565"></span>[1566](#fn_1565)

<span id="iii.x.xvi-p3"></span><span class="c11"><span
id="iii.x.xvi-p3.1"></span>Others</span>, of whom Florinus <span
id="fr_1566"></span>[1567](#fn_1566) was chief, flourished at Rome. He
fell from the presbyterate of the Church, and Blastus was involved in a
similar fall. They also drew away many of the Church to their opinion,
each striving to introduce his own innovations in respect to the truth.
