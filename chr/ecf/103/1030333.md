<span id="iv.ii.lxxxv-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="iv.ii.lxxxv-p1.1"></span>Chapter 83.—The Man Who Despises the Mercy
of God is Guilty of the Sin Against the Holy Ghost.</span>

<span id="iv.ii.lxxxv-p2"></span>Now the man who, not believing that
sins are remitted in the Church, despises this great gift of God’s
mercy, and persists to the last day of his life in his obstinacy of
heart, is guilty of the unpardonable sin against the Holy Ghost, in whom
Christ forgives sins. <span id="fr_1265"></span>[1266](#fn_1265.md) But
this difficult question I have discussed as clearly as I could in a book
devoted exclusively to this one point.
