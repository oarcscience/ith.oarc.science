<span id="v.vi.i-p1"></span> *From the Retractations*, Book II. Chap.
60.

<span id="v.vi.i-p2"></span>“ Then <span
id="fr_2382"></span>[2383](#fn_2382.md) also I wrote a Book against Lying,
the occasion of which work was this. In order to discover the
Priscillianist heretics, who think it right to conceal their heresy not
only by denial and lies, but even by perjury, it seemed to certain
Catholics that they ought to pretended themselves Priscillianists, in
order that they might penetrate their lurking places. In prohibition of
which thing, I composed this book. It begins: *Multa mihi legenda
misisti*.”
