<span id="iv.i.xi-p2"></span> ————————————

<span id="iv.i.xi-p3"></span>That a kind of trinity exists in man, who
is the image of God, viz. the mind, and the knowledge wherewith the mind
knows itself, and the love wherewith it loves both itself and its own
knowledge; and these three are shown to be mutually equal, and of one
essence.
