<span id="xxv.iii.iii.xxvii-p1"></span> <span class="c10"><span
id="xxv.iii.iii.xxvii-p1.1"></span>From Letter XLII</span>.—*(For 370.)*

<span id="xxv.iii.iii.xxvii-p2"></span><span class="c10"><span
id="xxv.iii.iii.xxvii-p2.1"></span>For</span> we have been called,
brethren, and are now called together, by Wisdom, and according to the
Evangelical parable, to that great and heavenly Supper, and sufficient
for every creature; I mean, to the Passover,—to Christ, Who is
sacrificed; for ‘Christ our Passover is sacrificed.’ (*And afterwards:*)
They, therefore, that are thus prepared shall hear, ‘Enter into the joy
of thy Lord <span id="fr_4550"></span>[4551](#fn_4550) .’
