<span id="vi.I_1.V-p2"></span><span class="c2"><span
id="vi.I_1.V-p2.1"></span>Chapter V.—He Seeks Rest in God, and Pardon of
His Sins.</span>

<span id="vi.I_1.V-p3"></span>5. Oh! how shall I find rest in Thee? Who
will send Thee into my heart to inebriate it, so that I may forget my
woes, and embrace Thee my only good? What art Thou to me? Have
compassion on me, that I may speak. What am I to Thee that Thou
demandest my love, and unless I give it Thee art angry, and threatenest
me with great sorrows? Is it, then, a light sorrow not to love Thee?
Alas! alas! tell me of Thy compassion, O Lord my God, what Thou art to
me. “Say unto my soul, I am thy salvation.” <span
id="fr_137"></span>[138](#fn_137) So speak that I may hear. Behold,
Lord, the ears of my heart are before Thee; open Thou them, and “say
unto my soul, I am thy salvation.” When I hear, may I run and lay hold
on Thee. Hide not Thy face from me. Let me die, lest I die, if only I
may see Thy face. <span id="fr_138"></span>[139](#fn_138)

<span id="vi.I_1.V-p6"></span>6. Cramped is the dwelling of my soul; do
Thou expand it, that Thou mayest enter in. It is in ruins, restore Thou
it. There is that about it which must offend Thine eyes; I confess and
<span id="page_47">p. 47</span> <span id="vi.I_1.V-Page_47"></span>know
it, but who will cleanse it? or to whom shall I cry but to Thee? Cleanse
me from my secret sins, <span id="fr_139"></span>[140](#fn_139) O Lord,
and keep Thy servant from those of other men. I believe, and therefore
do I speak; <span id="fr_140"></span>[141](#fn_140) Lord, Thou knowest.
Have I not confessed my transgressions unto Thee, O my God; and Thou
hast put away the iniquity of my heart? <span
id="fr_141"></span>[142](#fn_141) I do not contend in judgment with
Thee, <span id="fr_142"></span>[143](#fn_142) who art the Truth; and I
would not deceive myself, lest my iniquity lie against itself. <span
id="fr_143"></span>[144](#fn_143) I do not, therefore, contend in
judgment with Thee, for “if Thou, Lord, shouldest mark iniquities, O
Lord, who shall stand?” <span id="fr_144"></span>[145](#fn_144)
