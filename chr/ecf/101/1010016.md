<span id="I_1-p2"></span>————————————

<span id="I_1-p3"></span>Commencing with the invocation of God, Augustin
relates in detail the beginning of his life, his infancy and boyhood, up
to his fifteenth year; at which age he acknowledges that he was more
inclined to all youthful pleasures and vices than to the study of
letters.
