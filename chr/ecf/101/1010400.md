<span id="vii.1.XCIV-p2"></span>(<span class="c9"><span

<span id="vii.1.XCIV-p3"></span>A letter to Augustin from Paulinus and
Therasia, the substance of which is sufficiently stated in the next
letter, which contains the reply of Augustin to his friend’s questions
concerning the present life, the nature of the bodies of the blessed in
the life to come, and the functions of the members of the body after the
resurrection.
