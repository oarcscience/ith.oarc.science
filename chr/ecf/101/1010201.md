id="vi.XI-p1.1"></span>Book XI.</span>

<span id="vi.XI-p2"></span>————————————

<span id="vi.XI-p3"></span>The design of his confessions being declared,
he seeks from God the knowledge of the Holy Scriptures, and begins to
expound the words of Genesis I. I, concerning the creation of the world.
The questions of rash disputers being refuted, “What did God before he
created the world?” That he might the better overcome his opponents, he
adds a copious disquisition concerning time.
