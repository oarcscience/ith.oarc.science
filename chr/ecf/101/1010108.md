id="vi.VII-p1.1"></span>Book VII.</span>

<span id="vi.VII-p2"></span>————————————

<span id="vi.VII-p3"></span>He recalls the beginning of his youth, i.e.
the thirty-first year of his age, in which very grave errors as to the
nature of God and the origin of evil being distinguished, and the Sacred
Books more accurately known, he at length arrives at a clear knowledge
of God, not yet rightly apprehending Jesus Christ.
