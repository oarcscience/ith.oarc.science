##  Epistle XXXVIII.<sup>[1](footnote/fn53.htm#P5369_1637308.md)</sup>

The Letter of Caldonius, Herculanus, and Others, on the Excommunication
of Felicissimus with His People.

Caldonius, with Herculanus and Victor, his colleagues, also with
Rogatianus and Numidicus,
presbyters.<sup>[2](footnote/fn53.htm#P5374_1637654.md)</sup> We have
rejected Felicissimus and Augendus from communion; also Repostus from
among the exiles, and Irene of the Blood-stained
ones;<sup>[3](footnote/fn53.htm#P5375_1637819.md)</sup> and Paula the
sempstress; which you ought to know from my subscription; also we have
rejected Sophronius and Soliassus
(budinarius),<sup>[4](footnote/fn53.htm#P5376_1638009.md)</sup> -himself
also one of the exiles.
