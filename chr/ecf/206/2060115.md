<span id="v.CIV-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.CIV-p1.1"></span>Letter CIV. From Augustine.</span>

<span id="v.CIV-p2"></span>In this letter Augustine (1) commends to
Jerome the deacon Cyprian, (2) explains how it is that his first letter
(Letter LVI.) has miscarried, and (3) urges Jerome to base his
scriptural labours not on the Hebrew text but on the version of the LXX.
The date of the letter is 403 <span class="c17"><span
id="v.CIV-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span> See Augustine, vol. i., Letter LXXI.
