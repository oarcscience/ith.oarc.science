<span id="v.CII-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.CII-p1.1"></span>Letter CII. To Augustine.</span>

<span id="v.CII-p2"></span>Jerome’s reply to the foregoing in which, it
has been said, friendship struggles with suspicion and resentment. He
warns Augustine not to provoke him, lest old as he is he may prove a
dangerous opponent; and encloses part of his reply to the apology of
Rufinus. Written in 402 <span class="c17"><span
id="v.CII-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span> See Augustine, vol. i., Letter XXXIX.
