<span id="v.CXXIX-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.CXXIX-p1.1"></span>Letter CXXIX. To Dardanus.</span>

<span id="v.CXXIX-p2"></span>In answer to a question put by Dardanus,
prefect of Gaul, Jerome writes concerning the Promised Land which he
identifies not with Canaan but with heaven. He then points out that the
present sufferings of the Jews are due altogether to the crime of which
they have been guilty in the crucifixion of Christ. The date of the
letter is 414 <span class="c17"><span
id="v.CXXIX-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span>
