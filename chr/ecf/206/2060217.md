<span id="vii.iv.vii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vii.iv.vii-p1.1"></span>Titus.</span>

<span id="vii.iv.vii-p2"></span>*Addressed to Paula and Eustochium,
<span class="c17"><span id="vii.iv.vii-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span> 387. The
Preface speaks of the rejection of the Epistle by Marcion and Basilides,
its acceptance by Tatius, but without assigning reasons. It ought,
Jerome says, to be of special interest to Paula and Eustochium, as being
written from Nicopolis, near Actium, where their property lay.*
