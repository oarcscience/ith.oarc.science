<span id="v.CI-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.CI-p1.1"></span>Letter CI. From Augustine.</span>

<span id="v.CI-p2"></span>A letter from Augustine in which he denies
that he has written a book against Jerome and sent it to Rome but
confesses that he has criticized him although without giving details.
Written in 402 <span class="c17"><span id="v.CI-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span>
This and the following letters are to be found in the First Volume of
the First Series of this Library. Letter LXVII.
