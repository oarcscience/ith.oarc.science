<span id="vii.iv.i-p1"></span> <span id="page_495">p. 495</span> <span
id="vii.iv.i-Page_495"></span><span class="c14"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p1.1"></span>The Commentaries.</span>

<span id="vii.iv.i-p2"></span>The extant commentaries by Jerome on the
books of Holy Scripture may be arranged thus, chronological sequence
being observed as far as possible:

<span id="vii.iv.i-p3"></span>A. New Testament:

<span id="vii.iv.i-p4"></span>The Epistles to Philemon, Galatians,
Ephesians, Titus. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p4.1"></span>a.d.</span> 387.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p5"></span>Origen on St. Luke. <span
class="c17"><span id="vii.iv.i-p5.1"></span>a.d.</span> 389.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p6"></span>St. Matthew. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p6.1"></span>a.d.</span> 398.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p7"></span>B. Old Testament:

<span id="vii.iv.i-p8"></span>Ecclesiastes. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p8.1"></span>a.d.</span> 388.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p9"></span>1. The Twelve Minor Prophets:

<span id="vii.iv.i-p10"></span>Nahum, Michah, Zephaniah, Haggai,
Habakkuk. <span class="c17"><span id="vii.iv.i-p10.1"></span>a.d.</span>
392.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p11"></span>Jonah. Begun three years after the
foregoing (Preface). Finished between <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p11.1"></span>a.d.</span> 395 and <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p11.2"></span>a.d.</span> 397.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p12"></span>Obadiah. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p12.1"></span>a.d.</span> 403.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p13"></span>Zechariah, Malachi, Hosea, Joel, Amos.
Finished by <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p13.1"></span>a.d.</span> 406.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p14"></span>2. The Four Greater Prophets:

<span id="vii.iv.i-p15"></span>Daniel. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p15.1"></span>a.d</span>. 407.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p16"></span>Isaiah. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p16.1"></span>a.d.</span> 408–410.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p17"></span>Ezekiel. <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p17.1"></span>a.d</span>. 410–414.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p18"></span>Jeremiah. Commenced after the death of
Eustochium in <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iv.i-p18.1"></span>a.d.</span> 418. The commentary on this book,
which stops short at chapter xxxii., was therefore written in <span
class="c17"><span id="vii.iv.i-p18.2"></span>a.d.</span> 419, the year
which intervened between Eustochium’s death and Jerome’s own.

<span id="vii.iv.i-p19"></span>We have thought it best to give the
Prefaces, as in those to the Vulgate, in the order of the books as they
stand in our Bible, not in the order in which they were written.
