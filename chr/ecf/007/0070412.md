#### <span id="viii.iii.iv-p0.1"></span>Chapter IV. <span id="fr_2408"></span>[2409](#fn_2408) —Various Precepts.

<span id="viii.iii.iv-p2"></span>1. My child, him that speaketh to thee
the word of God remember night and day; and thou shalt honour him as the
Lord; <span id="fr_2409"></span>[2410](#fn_2409) for <span
id="viii.iii.iv-p3.3"></span>in the place whence lordly rule is
uttered, <span id="fr_2410"></span>[2411](#fn_2410) there is the Lord.
2. And thou shalt seek out day by day the faces of the saints, in order
that thou mayest rest upon <span id="fr_2411"></span>[2412](#fn_2411)
their words. 3. Thou shalt not long for <span
id="fr_2412"></span>[2413](#fn_2412) division, but shalt bring those who
contend to peace. Thou shalt judge righteously, thou shalt not respect
persons in reproving for transgressions. 4. Thou shalt not be undecided
whether it shall be or no. <span id="fr_2413"></span>[2414](#fn_2413) 5.
Be not a stretcher forth of the hands to receive and a drawer of them
back to give. <span id="fr_2414"></span>[2415](#fn_2414) 6. If thou hast
<span id="viii.iii.iv-p8.5"></span>aught, through thy hands thou shalt
give ransom for thy sins. <span id="fr_2415"></span>[2416](#fn_2415) 7.
Thou shalt not hesitate to give, nor murmur when thou givest; for thou
shalt know who is the good repayer of the hire. 8. Thou shalt not turn
away from him that is in want, but thou shalt share all things with thy
brother, and shalt not say that they are thine own; for if ye are
partakers in that which is immortal, how much more in things which are
mortal? <span id="fr_2416"></span>[2417](#fn_2416) 9. Thou shalt not
remove thy hand from thy son or from thy daughter, but from <span
id="viii.iii.iv-p10.5"></span>their youth shalt teach <span
id="viii.iii.iv-p10.6"></span>them the fear of God. <span
id="fr_2417"></span>[2418](#fn_2417) 10. Thou shalt not enjoin aught in
thy bitterness upon thy bondman or maidservant, who hope in the same
God, lest ever they shall fear not God who is over both; <span
id="fr_2418"></span>[2419](#fn_2418) for he cometh not to call according
to the outward appearance, but unto them whom the Spirit hath prepared.
11. And ye bondmen shall be subject to your <span
id="fr_2419"></span>[2420](#fn_2419) masters as to a type of God, in
modesty and fear. <span id="fr_2420"></span>[2421](#fn_2420) 12. Thou
shalt hate all hypocrisy and everything which is not pleasing to the
Lord. 13. Do thou in no wise forsake the commandments of the Lord; but
thou shalt keep what thou hast received, neither adding <span
id="viii.iii.iv-p14.3"></span>thereto nor taking away <span
id="viii.iii.iv-p14.4"></span>therefrom. <span
id="fr_2421"></span>[2422](#fn_2421) 14. In the church <span
id="fr_2422"></span>[2423](#fn_2422) thou shalt acknowledge thy
transgressions, and thou shalt not come near for thy prayer <span
id="fr_2423"></span>[2424](#fn_2423) with an evil conscience. <span
id="fr_2424"></span>[2425](#fn_2424) This is the way of life. <span
id="fr_2425"></span>[2426](#fn_2425)
