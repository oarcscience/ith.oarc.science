#### <span id="viii.iii.viii-p0.1"></span>Chapter VIII. <span id="fr_2446"></span>[2447](#fn_2446) —Concerning Fasting and Prayer (the Lord’s Prayer.md).

<span id="viii.iii.viii-p2"></span>1. But let not your fasts be with the
hypocrites; <span id="fr_2447"></span>[2448](#fn_2447.md) for they fast on
the second and fifth day of the week; but do ye fast on the fourth <span
id="viii.iii.viii-p3.2"></span>day and the Preparation (Friday). <span
id="fr_2448"></span>[2449](#fn_2448.md) 2. Neither pray as the hypocrites;
but as the Lord commanded in His Gospel, <span
id="fr_2449"></span>[2450](#fn_2449.md) thus pray: Our Father who art in
heaven, hallowed be Thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done, as in
heaven, <span id="viii.iii.viii-p5.3"></span>so on earth. Give us to-day
our daily (needful) bread, <span id="fr_2450"></span>[2451](#fn_2450.md)
and forgive us our debt as we also forgive our debtors. And bring us not
into temptation, but deliver us from the evil <span
id="viii.iii.viii-p6.3"></span>one (or, evil); for Thine is the power
and the glory for ever. <span id="fr_2451"></span>[2452](#fn_2451.md) 3.
Thrice in the day thus pray. <span id="fr_2452"></span>[2453](#fn_2452.md)
