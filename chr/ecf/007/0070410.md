#### <span id="viii.iii.ii-p0.1"></span>Chapter II. <span id="fr_2388"></span>[2389](#fn_2388.md) —The Second Commandment: Gross Sin Forbidden.

<span id="viii.iii.ii-p2"></span>1. And the second commandment of the
Teaching; 2. Thou shalt not commit murder, thou shalt not commit
adultery, <span id="fr_2389"></span>[2390](#fn_2389.md) thou shalt not
commit pæderasty, <span id="fr_2390"></span>[2391](#fn_2390.md) thou shalt
not commit fornication, thou shalt not steal, <span
id="fr_2391"></span>[2392](#fn_2391.md) thou shalt not practice magic, thou
shalt not practice witchcraft, thou shalt not murder a child by abortion
nor kill that which is begotten. <span
id="fr_2392"></span>[2393](#fn_2392.md) Thou shalt not covet the things of
thy neighbour, <span id="fr_2393"></span>[2394](#fn_2393.md) 3. thou shalt
not forswear thyself, <span id="fr_2394"></span>[2395](#fn_2394.md) thou
shalt not bear false witness, <span id="fr_2395"></span>[2396](#fn_2395.md)
thou shalt not speak evil, thou shalt bear no grudge. <span
id="fr_2396"></span>[2397](#fn_2396.md) 4. Thou shalt not be double-minded
nor double-tongued; for to be double-tongued is a <span id="page_378">p.
378</span> <span id="viii.iii.ii-Page_378"></span> snare of death. <span
id="fr_2397"></span>[2398](#fn_2397.md) 5. Thy speech shall not be false,
nor empty, but fulfilled by deed. <span
id="fr_2398"></span>[2399](#fn_2398.md) 6. Thou shalt not be covetous, nor
rapacious, nor a hypocrite, nor evil disposed, nor haughty. Thou shalt
not take evil counsel against thy neighbour. <span
id="fr_2399"></span>[2400](#fn_2399.md) 7. Thou shalt not hate any man; but
some thou shalt reprove, <span id="fr_2400"></span>[2401](#fn_2400.md) and
concerning some thou shalt pray, and some thou shalt love more than thy
own life. <span id="fr_2401"></span>[2402](#fn_2401.md)
