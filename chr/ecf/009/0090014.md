<span id="iv.iii.v-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.v-p1.1"></span>Section V.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.v-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_375"></span>[376](#fn_375.md) Jesus answered and said unto him, Get
thee hence, Satan:  for it is written, Thou \[2\] shalt worship the Lord
thy God, and him alone shalt thou serve.   <span
id="fr_376"></span>[377](#fn_376.md) And when the \[3\] devil <span
id="fr_377"></span>[378](#fn_377.md) had completed all his temptations, he
departed from him for a season.   <span
id="fr_378"></span>[379](#fn_378.md) And behold, the angels drew near and
ministered unto him.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p7"></span>\[4, 5\]  <span
id="fr_379"></span>[380](#fn_379.md) And next day John was standing, and
two of his disciples;  <span id="fr_380"></span>[381](#fn_380.md) and he
saw Jesus as \[6\] he was walking, and said, Behold, the Lamb of God! 
 <span id="fr_381"></span>[382](#fn_381.md) And his two disciples heard
\[7\] him saying *this*, <span id="fr_382"></span>[383](#fn_382.md) and
they followed Jesus.   <span id="fr_383"></span>[384](#fn_383.md) And Jesus
turned and saw them coming after him, and said unto them, What seek ye? 
They said unto him, Our master, \[8\] where art thou staying?   <span
id="fr_384"></span>[385](#fn_384.md) And he said unto them, Come and see. 
And they came and saw his place, and abode with him that day:  and it
was about the tenth hour.  \[9\]  <span
id="fr_385"></span>[386](#fn_385.md) One of the two which heard from <span
id="fr_386"></span>[387](#fn_386.md) John, and followed Jesus, was Andrew
the \[10\] brother of Simon.   <span id="fr_387"></span>[388](#fn_387.md)
And he saw first Simon his brother, and said unto him, We have \[11\]
found the Messiah.   <span id="fr_388"></span>[389](#fn_388.md) And he
brought him unto Jesus.  And Jesus looked upon him and said, Thou art
Simon, son of Jonah:  thou shalt be called Cephas. <span
id="fr_389"></span>[390](#fn_389.md)

<span id="iv.iii.v-p19"></span>\[12\]  <span
id="fr_390"></span>[391](#fn_390.md) And on the next day Jesus desired to
go forth to Galilee, and he found Philip, \[13\] \[Arabic, p. 19\] and
said unto him, Follow me.   <span id="fr_391"></span>[392](#fn_391.md) Now
Philip was of Bethsaida, of the city \[14\] of Andrew and Simon.   <span
id="fr_392"></span>[393](#fn_392.md) And Philip found Nathanael, and said
unto him, He of whom Moses did write in the law and in the prophets, we
have found that \[15\] he is Jesus the son of Joseph of Nazareth. 
 <span id="fr_393"></span>[394](#fn_393.md) Nathanael said unto him, Is it
possible <span id="page_51">p. 51</span> <span
id="iv.iii.v-Page_51"></span>that there can be any good thing from
Nazareth?  Philip said unto him, Come and \[16\] see.   <span
id="fr_394"></span>[395](#fn_394.md) And Jesus saw Nathanael coming to him,
and said of him, This is indeed a <span
id="fr_395"></span>[396](#fn_395.md) \[17\] son of Israel in whom is no
guile.   <span id="fr_396"></span>[397](#fn_396.md) And Nathanael said unto
him, Whence knowest thou me?  Jesus said unto him, Before Philip called
thee, while thou wast under the \[18\] fig tree, I saw thee.   <span
id="fr_397"></span>[398](#fn_397.md) Nathanael answered and said unto him,
My Master, thou art \[19\] the Son of God; thou art the King of Israel. 
 <span id="fr_398"></span>[399](#fn_398.md) Jesus said unto him, Because I
said unto thee, I saw thee under the fig tree, hast thou believed? thou
shalt see what is \[20\] greater than this.   <span
id="fr_399"></span>[400](#fn_399.md) And he said unto him, Verily, verily,
I say unto you, Henceforth ye shall see the heavens opened, and the
angels of God ascending and descending upon the Son of man.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p30"></span>\[21\]  <span
id="fr_400"></span>[401](#fn_400.md) And Jesus returned in the power of the
Spirit to Galilee.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p32"></span>\[22\]  <span
id="fr_401"></span>[402](#fn_401.md) And on the third day there was a feast
in Cana, <span id="fr_402"></span>[403](#fn_402.md) a <span
id="fr_403"></span>[404](#fn_403.md) city of Galilee; and the \[23\] mother
of Jesus was there:   <span id="fr_404"></span>[405](#fn_404.md) and Jesus
also and his disciples were invited to the \[24\] feast.   <span
id="fr_405"></span>[406](#fn_405.md) And they lacked wine:  and his mother
said unto Jesus, They have no wine.  \[25\]  <span
id="fr_406"></span>[407](#fn_406.md) And Jesus said unto her, What have I
to do with thee, woman? hath not mine \[26\] hour come? <span
id="fr_407"></span>[408](#fn_407.md)    <span
id="fr_408"></span>[409](#fn_408.md) And his mother said unto the servants,
What he saith unto you, do.  \[27\]  <span
id="fr_409"></span>[410](#fn_409.md) And there were there six vessels of
stone, placed for the Jews’ purification, such as \[28\] \[Arabic, p.
20\] would contain two or three jars.   <span
id="fr_410"></span>[411](#fn_410.md) And Jesus said unto them, Fill the
vessels \[29\] with water.  And they filled them to the top.   <span
id="fr_411"></span>[412](#fn_411.md) He said unto them, Draw \[30\] out
now, and present to the ruler of the feast. And they did *so*.   <span
id="fr_412"></span>[413](#fn_412.md) And when the ruler of the company
tasted that water which had become wine, and knew not whence it was (but
the servants knew, because they filled up the water), the ruler of the
company called \[31\] the bridegroom,  <span
id="fr_413"></span>[414](#fn_413.md) and said unto him, Every man
presenteth first the good wine, and on intoxication he bringeth what is
poor; but thou hast kept the good wine until \[32\] now.   <span
id="fr_414"></span>[415](#fn_414.md) And this is the first sign <span
id="fr_415"></span>[416](#fn_415.md) which Jesus did in Cana of Galilee,
and manifested \[33\] his glory; and his disciples believed on him. 
 <span id="fr_416"></span>[417](#fn_416.md) And his fame spread in all the
country \[34\] which was around them.   <span
id="fr_417"></span>[418](#fn_417.md) And he taught in their synagogues, and
was glorified \[35\] by <span id="fr_418"></span>[419](#fn_418.md) every
man.   <span id="fr_419"></span>[420](#fn_419.md) And he came to Nazareth,
where he had been brought up, and entered, according to his custom, into
the synagogue on the sabbath day, and stood \[36\] up to read.   <span
id="fr_420"></span>[421](#fn_420.md) And he was given the book of Isaiah
the prophet.  And Jesus opened the book and found the place where it was
written,

<span id="iv.iii.v-p53"></span>\[37\]  <span
id="fr_421"></span>[422](#fn_421.md) The Spirit of the Lord is upon me,

<span id="iv.iii.v-p55"></span>And for this anointed he me, to preach
good tidings to the poor;

<span id="iv.iii.v-p56"></span>And he hath sent me to heal the
broken-hearted,

<span id="iv.iii.v-p57"></span>And to proclaim forgiveness to the
evil-doers, <span id="fr_422"></span>[423](#fn_422.md) and sight to the
blind,

<span id="iv.iii.v-p59"></span>And to bring the broken into
forgiveness, <span id="fr_423"></span>[424](#fn_423.md)

<span id="iv.iii.v-p61"></span>\[38\]  <span
id="fr_424"></span>[425](#fn_424.md) And to proclaim an acceptable year of
the Lord.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p63"></span>\[39\]  <span
id="fr_425"></span>[426](#fn_425.md) And he rolled up the book and gave it
to the servant, and went and sat down:  \[40\] and the eyes of all that
were in the synagogue were observing him.   <span
id="fr_426"></span>[427](#fn_426.md) And he began to say unto them, To-day
hath this scripture been fulfilled which ye have heard with \[41\] your
ears.   <span id="fr_427"></span>[428](#fn_427.md) And they all bare him
witness, and wondered at the words of grace which were proceeding from
his mouth.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p67"></span>\[42\] \[Arabic, p. 21\]  <span
id="fr_428"></span>[429](#fn_428.md) And from that time began Jesus to
proclaim the gospel of the kingdom \[43\] of God, and to say, Repent ye,
and believe in the gospel.   <span id="fr_429"></span>[430](#fn_429.md) The
time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of heaven hath come near.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p70"></span>\[44\]  <span
id="fr_430"></span>[431](#fn_430.md) And while he was walking on the shore
of the sea of Galilee, he saw two brethren, Simon who was called Cephas,
and Andrew his brother, casting their nets into \[45\] the sea; for they
were fishers.   <span id="fr_431"></span>[432](#fn_431.md) And Jesus said
unto them, Follow me, and I will \[46\] make you fishers of men.   <span
id="fr_432"></span>[433](#fn_432.md) And they immediately left their nets
there and followed \[47\] him.   <span id="fr_433"></span>[434](#fn_433.md)
And when he went on from thence, he saw other two brothers, James the
son of Zebedee, and John his brother, in the ship with Zebedee their
father, mending \[48\] their nets; and Jesus called them.   <span
id="fr_434"></span>[435](#fn_434.md) And they immediately forsook the ship
and their father Zebedee, and followed him.

<span id="iv.iii.v-p76"></span><span id="page_52">p. 52</span> <span
id="iv.iii.v-Page_52"></span>\[49\]  <span
id="fr_435"></span>[436](#fn_435.md) And when the multitude gathered unto
him to hear the word of God, while he \[50\] was standing on the shore
of the sea of Gennesaret,  <span id="fr_436"></span>[437](#fn_436.md) he
saw two boats standing beside the sea, while <span
id="fr_437"></span>[438](#fn_437.md) the two fishers which were gone out of
them were washing their \[51\] nets.   <span
id="fr_438"></span>[439](#fn_438.md) And one of them belonged to Simon
Cephas.  And Jesus went up and sat down in it, and commanded that they
should move away a little from the land into \[52\] the water.  And he
sat down and taught the multitudes from the boat.   <span
id="fr_439"></span>[440](#fn_439.md) And when he had left off his speaking,
he said unto Simon, Put out into the deep, and cast your \[53\] net for
a draught.   <span id="fr_440"></span>[441](#fn_440.md) And Simon answered
and said unto him, My Master, we toiled \[54\] all night and caught
nothing; now <span id="fr_441"></span>[442](#fn_441.md) at thy word I will
cast the net.   <span id="fr_442"></span>[443](#fn_442.md) And when they
did this, there were enclosed <span id="fr_443"></span>[444](#fn_443.md) a
great many fishes; and their net was on the \[55\] point of breaking. 
 <span id="fr_444"></span>[445](#fn_444.md) And they beckoned to their
comrades that were in the other boat, to come and help them.  And when
they came, they filled both boats, so that they were on the point of
sinking.
