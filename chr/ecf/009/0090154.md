<span id="xii.iv.lix-p1"></span> <span id="page_247">p. 247</span> <span
id="xii.iv.lix-Page_247"></span><span class="c2"><span
id="xii.iv.lix-p1.1"></span>Chapter LIX.—Warning Against Disobedience. 
Prayer.</span>

<span id="xii.iv.lix-p2"></span>If, however, any shall disobey the words
spoken by Him through us, let them know that they will involve
themselves in transgression and serious danger; but we shall be innocent
of this sin, and, instant in prayer and supplication, shall desire that
the Creator of all preserve unbroken the computed number of His elect in
the whole world through His beloved Son Jesus Christ, through whom He
called us from darkness to light, from ignorance to knowledge of the
glory of His name, our hope resting on Thy name which is primal cause of
every creature,—having opened the eyes of our heart to the knowledge of
Thee, who alone “dost rest highest among the highest, holy among the
holy,” <span id="fr_4314"></span>[4315](#fn_4314) who “layest low the
insolence of the haughty,” <span id="fr_4315"></span>[4316](#fn_4315)
who “destroyest the calculations of the heathen,” <span
id="fr_4316"></span>[4317](#fn_4316) who “settest the low on high and
bringest low the exalted;” <span id="fr_4317"></span>[4318](#fn_4317)
who “makest rich and makest poor,” <span
id="fr_4318"></span>[4319](#fn_4318) who “killest and makest to
live,” <span id="fr_4319"></span>[4320](#fn_4319) only Benefactor of
spirits and God of all flesh, <span id="fr_4320"></span>[4321](#fn_4320)
who beholdest the depths, the eye-witness of human works, the help of
those in danger, the Saviour of those in despair, the Creator and
Guardian of every spirit, who multipliest nations upon earth, and from
all madest choice of those who love Thee through Jesus Christ, Thy
beloved Son, through whom Thou didst instruct, sanctify, honour us.  We
would have Thee, Lord, to prove our help and succour.  Those of us in
affliction save, on the lowly take pity; the fallen raise; upon those in
need arise; the sick <span id="fr_4321"></span>[4322](#fn_4321) heal;
the wandering ones of Thy people turn; fill the hungry; redeem those of
us in bonds; raise up those that are weak; comfort the faint-hearted;
let all the nations know that Thou art God alone and Jesus Christ Thy
Son, and we are Thy people and the sheep of Thy pasture.
