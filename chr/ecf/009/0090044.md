<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xxxv-p1.1"></span>Section XXXV.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_2388"></span>[2389](#fn_2388.md) And on the great day, which is the
last of the feast, Jesus stood, crying out and \[2\] saying, If *any*
man is thirsty, let him come unto me, and drink.   <span
id="fr_2389"></span>[2390](#fn_2389.md) Every one that believeth in me, as
the scriptures said, there shall flow from his belly rivers of pure
\[3\] water.   <span id="fr_2390"></span>[2391](#fn_2390.md) He said that
referring to the Spirit, which those who believed in him were to
receive:  for the Spirit was not yet granted; and because Jesus had not
yet been \[4\] \[Arabic, p. 133\] glorified.   <span
id="fr_2391"></span>[2392](#fn_2391.md) And many of the multitude that
heard his words said, This is \[5\] in truth the prophet.   <span
id="fr_2392"></span>[2393](#fn_2392.md) And others said, This is the
Messiah.  But others \[6\] said, Can it be that the Messiah will come
from Galilee?   <span id="fr_2393"></span>[2394](#fn_2393.md) Hath not the
scripture said that from the seed of David, and from Bethlehem, the
village of David, the \[7\] Messiah cometh?   <span
id="fr_2394"></span>[2395](#fn_2394.md) And there occurred a dissension in
the multitude because of him.  \[8\]  <span
id="fr_2395"></span>[2396](#fn_2395.md) And some of them were wishing to
seize him; but no man laid a hand upon him.

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p11"></span>\[9\]  <span
id="fr_2396"></span>[2397](#fn_2396.md) And those officers came to the
chief priests and Pharisees:  and the priests said \[10\] unto them, Why
did ye not bring him?   <span id="fr_2397"></span>[2398](#fn_2397.md) The
officers said, Never spake man thus \[11\] as speaketh this man.   <span
id="fr_2398"></span>[2399](#fn_2398.md) The Pharisees said unto them,
Perhaps ye also have gone \[12, 13\] astray?   <span
id="fr_2399"></span>[2400](#fn_2399.md) Hath any of the rulers or the
Pharisees haply believed in him?  <span
id="fr_2400"></span>[2401](#fn_2400.md) except \[14\] this people which
knows not the law; they are accursed.   <span
id="fr_2401"></span>[2402](#fn_2401.md) Nicodemus, one of them, \[15\] he
that had come to Jesus by night,  <span
id="fr_2402"></span>[2403](#fn_2402.md) said unto them, Doth our law haply
condemn \[16\] a man, except it hear him first and know what he hath
done?   <span id="fr_2403"></span>[2404](#fn_2403.md) They answered and
said unto him, Art thou also haply from Galilee?  Search, and see that a
prophet riseth not from Galilee.

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p20"></span>\[17, 18\]  <span
id="fr_2404"></span>[2405](#fn_2404.md) And when the Pharisees assembled,
Jesus asked them, and said,  <span id="fr_2405"></span>[2406](#fn_2405.md)
What say ye of \[19\] the Messiah? whose son is he?  They said unto him,
The son of David.   <span id="fr_2406"></span>[2407](#fn_2406.md) He said
unto them, And how doth David in the Holy Spirit call him Lord? for he
said,

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p24"></span>\[20\]  <span
id="fr_2407"></span>[2408](#fn_2407.md) The Lord said unto my Lord,

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p26"></span>Sit on my right hand,

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p27"></span>That I may put thine enemies under thy
feet.

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p28"></span>\[21, 22\]  <span
id="fr_2408"></span>[2409](#fn_2408.md) If then David calleth him Lord, how
is he his son?   <span id="fr_2409"></span>[2410](#fn_2409.md) And no one
was able to answer him; and no man dared from that day again to ask him
of anything.

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p31"></span>\[23\]  <span
id="fr_2410"></span>[2411](#fn_2410.md) And Jesus addressed them again, and
said, I am the light of the world; and he that \[24\] followeth me shall
not walk in darkness, but shall find the light of life.   <span
id="fr_2411"></span>[2412](#fn_2411.md) The Pharisees \[Arabic, p. 134\]
said unto him, Thou bearest witness to thyself; thy witness is not
true.   <span id="fr_2412"></span>[2413](#fn_2412.md) Jesus \[25\] answered
and said unto them, If I bear witness to myself, my witness is true; for
I know whence I came, and whither I go; but ye know not whence I came,
or \[26, 27\] whither I go.   <span id="fr_2413"></span>[2414](#fn_2413.md)
And ye judge after the flesh; and I judge no man.   <span
id="fr_2414"></span>[2415](#fn_2414.md) And even if I judge, my judgement
is true; because I am not alone, but I and my Father which \[28, 29\]
sent me.   <span id="fr_2415"></span>[2416](#fn_2415.md) And in your law it
is written, that the witness of two men is true.   <span
id="fr_2416"></span>[2417](#fn_2416.md) I am he that beareth witness to
myself, and my Father which sent me beareth witness to \[30\] me. 
 <span id="fr_2417"></span>[2418](#fn_2417.md) They said unto him, Where is
thy Father?  Jesus answered and said unto them, Ye know not me, nor my
Father:  for did ye know me, ye would know my \[31\] Father.   <span
id="fr_2418"></span>[2419](#fn_2418.md) He said these sayings in the
treasury, where he was teaching in the \[32\] temple:   <span
id="fr_2419"></span>[2420](#fn_2419.md) and no man seized him; because his
hour had not yet come.  Jesus said unto them again, I go truly, and ye
shall seek me and not find me, and ye shall die \[33\] in your sins: 
and where I go, ye cannot come.   <span
id="fr_2420"></span>[2421](#fn_2420.md) The Jews said, Will he haply kill
\[34\] himself, that he saith, Where I go, ye cannot come?   <span
id="fr_2421"></span>[2422](#fn_2421.md) He said unto them, Ye are from
below; and I am from above:  ye are of this world; and I am not of this
\[35\] world.   <span id="fr_2422"></span>[2423](#fn_2422.md) I said unto
you, that ye shall die in your sins:  if ye believe not that I am \[36\]
he, ye shall die in your sins.   <span
id="fr_2423"></span>[2424](#fn_2423.md) The Jews said, And thou, who art
thou?  Jesus said <span id="page_98">p. 98</span> <span
id="iv.iii.xxxv-Page_98"></span>\[37\] unto them, If I should begin to
speak unto you,  <span id="fr_2424"></span>[2425](#fn_2424.md) I have
concerning you many words and judgement:  but he that sent me is true;
and I, what I heard from him is what \[38, 39\] I say in the world. 
 <span id="fr_2425"></span>[2426](#fn_2425.md) And they knew not that he
meant by that the Father.   <span id="fr_2426"></span>[2427](#fn_2426.md)
Jesus \[Arabic, p. 135\] said unto them again, When ye have lifted up
the Son of man, then ye shall know that I am he:  and I do nothing of
myself, but as my Father \[40\] taught me, so I speak.   <span
id="fr_2427"></span>[2428](#fn_2427.md) And he that sent me is with me; and
my Father hath not \[41\] left me alone; because I do what is pleasing
to him at all times.   <span id="fr_2428"></span>[2429](#fn_2428.md) And
while he was saying that, many believed in him.

<span id="iv.iii.xxxv-p51"></span>\[42\]  <span
id="fr_2429"></span>[2430](#fn_2429.md) And Jesus said to those Jews that
believed in him, If ye abide in my words, truly \[43\] ye are my
disciples;  <span id="fr_2430"></span>[2431](#fn_2430.md) and ye shall know
the truth, and the truth shall make you free.  \[44\]  <span
id="fr_2431"></span>[2432](#fn_2431.md) They said unto him, We are the seed
of Abraham, and have never served any man \[45\] in the way of slavery: 
how then sayest thou, Ye shall be free children?   <span
id="fr_2432"></span>[2433](#fn_2432.md) Jesus said unto them, Verily,
verily, I say unto you, Every one that doeth a sin is a slave of \[46\]
sin.   <span id="fr_2433"></span>[2434](#fn_2433.md) And the slave doth not
remain for ever in the house; but the son remaineth \[47, 48\] for
ever.   <span id="fr_2434"></span>[2435](#fn_2434.md) And if the Son set
you free, truly ye shall be free children.  I know that ye are the seed
of Abraham; but ye seek to slay me, because ye are unable for my \[49\]
word.   <span id="fr_2435"></span>[2436](#fn_2435.md) And what I saw with
my Father, I say:  and what ye saw with your father, \[50\] ye do. 
 <span id="fr_2436"></span>[2437](#fn_2436.md) They answered and said unto
him, Our father is Abraham.  Jesus said unto them, If ye were the
children of Abraham, ye would do the deeds of Abraham.  \[51\]  <span
id="fr_2437"></span>[2438](#fn_2437.md) Now, behold, ye seek to kill me, a
man that speak <span id="fr_2438"></span>[2439](#fn_2438.md) with you <span
id="fr_2439"></span>[2440](#fn_2439.md) the truth, that I \[52\] heard from
God:  this did Abraham not do.   <span
id="fr_2440"></span>[2441](#fn_2440.md) And ye do the deeds of your
father.  They said unto him, We were not *born* of fornication; <span
id="fr_2441"></span>[2442](#fn_2441.md) we have one Father, who is \[53\]
God.   <span id="fr_2442"></span>[2443](#fn_2442.md) Jesus said unto them,
If God were your Father, ye would love me:  I proceeded and came <span
id="fr_2443"></span>[2444](#fn_2443.md) from God; and it was not of my own
self that I came, <span id="fr_2444"></span>[2445](#fn_2444.md) but he sent
\[54\] \[Arabic, p. 136\] me.   <span
id="fr_2445"></span>[2446](#fn_2445.md) Why then do ye not know my word? 
Because ye cannot hear my word.  \[55\]  <span
id="fr_2446"></span>[2447](#fn_2446.md) Ye are from the father, the
devil, <span id="fr_2447"></span>[2448](#fn_2447.md) and the lust of your
father do ye desire to do, who from the beginning is a slayer of men,
and in the truth standeth not, because the truth is not in him.  And
when he speaketh untruth, he speaketh from \[56\] himself:  for he is a
liar, and the father of untruth.   <span
id="fr_2448"></span>[2449](#fn_2448.md) And I who speak the truth, ye
\[57\] believe me not.   <span id="fr_2449"></span>[2450](#fn_2449.md) Who
of you rebuketh me for a sin?  And if I speak the truth, ye \[58\] do
not believe me. <span id="fr_2450"></span>[2451](#fn_2450.md)    <span
id="fr_2451"></span>[2452](#fn_2451.md) Whosoever is of God heareth the
words of God:  therefore do \[59\] ye not hear, because ye are not of
God.   <span id="fr_2452"></span>[2453](#fn_2452.md) The Jews answered and
said unto him, \[60\] Did we not say well that thou art a Samaritan, and
hast demons?   <span id="fr_2453"></span>[2454](#fn_2453.md) Jesus said
unto them, As for me, I have not a devil; but my Father do I honour, and
ye dishonour \[61\] me.   <span id="fr_2454"></span>[2455](#fn_2454.md) I
seek not my glory:  here is one who seeketh and judgeth.
