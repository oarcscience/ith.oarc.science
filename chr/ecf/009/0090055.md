<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xlvi-p1.1"></span>Section XLVI.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_3164"></span>[3165](#fn_3164) Whosoever hath my commandments, and
keepeth them, he it is that loveth me:  and he that loveth me shall be
loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will \[2\] shew myself unto
him.   <span id="fr_3165"></span>[3166](#fn_3165) Judas (not Iscariot)
said unto him, My Lord, what is the \[3\] purpose of thy intention to
shew thyself to us, and not to the world?   <span
id="fr_3166"></span>[3167](#fn_3166) Jesus answered and said unto him,
Whosoever loveth me will keep my word:  and my Father will love him, and
to him will we come, and make our <span
id="fr_3167"></span>[3168](#fn_3167) abode with him.  \[4\]  <span
id="fr_3168"></span>[3169](#fn_3168) But he that loveth me not keepeth
not my word:  and this word that ye hear is not my word, but the
Father’s which sent me.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p8"></span>\[5, 6\]  <span
id="fr_3169"></span>[3170](#fn_3169) This have I spoken unto you, while
I was yet with you.   <span id="fr_3170"></span>[3171](#fn_3170) But the
Paraclete, the Holy Spirit, whom my Father will send in my name, he will
teach you everything, and \[7\] \[Arabic, p. 174\] he will bring to your
remembrance all that I say unto you.   <span
id="fr_3171"></span>[3172](#fn_3171) Peace I leave you; my peace I give
unto you:  and not as this world giveth, give I unto you.  \[8\]  <span
id="fr_3172"></span>[3173](#fn_3172) Let your heart not be
troubled, <span id="fr_3173"></span>[3174](#fn_3173) nor fearful.  Ye
heard that I said unto you, that I go away, and come unto you.  If <span
id="fr_3174"></span>[3175](#fn_3174) ye loved me, ye would rejoice, that
I go away to my \[9\] Father:  for my Father is greater than I.   <span
id="fr_3175"></span>[3176](#fn_3175) And now I say unto you before it
come \[10\] to pass, that, when it cometh to pass, ye may believe me. 
 <span id="fr_3176"></span>[3177](#fn_3176) Now I will not speak with
you much:  the Archon of the world will come, and he will have nothing
in \[11\] me:   <span id="fr_3177"></span>[3178](#fn_3177) but that the
world may know that I love my Father, and as my Father charged me, so I
do.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p18"></span>\[12\]  <span
id="fr_3178"></span>[3179](#fn_3178) And he said unto them, When I sent
you without purses, or wallets, and shoes, <span
id="fr_3179"></span>[3180](#fn_3179) \[13\] lacked ye perchance
anything?  They said unto him, Nothing.   <span
id="fr_3180"></span>[3181](#fn_3180) He said unto them, Henceforth,
whosoever hath a purse, let him take it, and likewise the wallet also: 
and whosoever hath not a sword, shall sell his garment, and buy for
himself a \[14\] sword.   <span id="fr_3181"></span>[3182](#fn_3181) I
say unto you, that this scripture also must be fulfilled in me, that I
should be reckoned <span id="fr_3182"></span>[3183](#fn_3182) with the
transgressors:  for all that is said of me is fulfilled in \[15\] me. 
 <span id="fr_3183"></span>[3184](#fn_3183) His disciples said unto him,
Our Lord, lo, here are two swords.  He said \[16\] unto them, They are
sufficient.   <span id="fr_3184"></span>[3185](#fn_3184) Arise, let us
go hence.  And they arose, and praised, and went forth, and went,
according to their custom, to the mount of Olives, he and his disciples.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p26"></span>\[17\]  <span
id="fr_3185"></span>[3186](#fn_3185) And he said unto them, I am the
true vine, and my Father is the husbandman.  \[18\]  <span
id="fr_3186"></span>[3187](#fn_3186) Every branch that produceth not
fruit in me, he taketh it:  and that which giveth fruit, \[19\] he
cleanseth it, that it may give much fruit.   <span
id="fr_3187"></span>[3188](#fn_3187) Ye are already clean because of the
word \[20\] that I have spoken unto you.   <span
id="fr_3188"></span>[3189](#fn_3188) Abide in me, and I in you.  And as
the branch of the \[Arabic, p. 175\] vine cannot produce fruit of
itself, if it be not abiding in the vine; so too ye \[21\] also, if ye
abide not in me.   <span id="fr_3189"></span>[3190](#fn_3189) I am the
vine, and ye are the branches:  He then that abideth in me, and I in
him, he giveth much fruit:  for without me ye cannot \[22\] do
anything.   <span id="fr_3190"></span>[3191](#fn_3190) And if a man
abide not in me, he is cast without, like a withered \[23\] branch; and
it is gathered, and cast <span id="fr_3191"></span>[3192](#fn_3191) into
the fire, that it may be burned.   <span
id="fr_3192"></span>[3193](#fn_3192) If ye abide in me, and my word
abide in you, everything that ye desire to ask shall be \[24\] *done*
unto you.   <span id="fr_3193"></span>[3194](#fn_3193) And herein is the
Father glorified, that ye may give much fruit; \[25\] and ye *shall* be
my disciples.   <span id="fr_3194"></span>[3195](#fn_3194) And as my
Father loved me, I loved you also:  \[26\] abide in my love.   <span
id="fr_3195"></span>[3196](#fn_3195) If ye keep my commands, ye shall
abide in my love; as I have \[27\] kept my Father’s commands, and abode
in his love.   <span id="fr_3196"></span>[3197](#fn_3196) I have spoken
that unto you, \[28\] that my joy <span
id="fr_3197"></span>[3198](#fn_3197) may be in you, and your joy <span
id="fr_3198"></span>[3199](#fn_3198) be fulfilled.   <span
id="fr_3199"></span>[3200](#fn_3199) This is my commandment, \[29\] that
ye love one another, as I loved you.   <span
id="fr_3200"></span>[3201](#fn_3200) And no love is greater than this,
namely, \[30\] that a man should give his life for his friends.   <span
id="fr_3201"></span>[3202](#fn_3201) Ye are my friends, if ye do all
that \[31\] I command you.   <span id="fr_3202"></span>[3203](#fn_3202)
I call you not now servants; for the servant knoweth not what <span
id="page_115">p. 115</span> <span id="iv.iii.xlvi-Page_115"></span>his
lord doeth:  my friends have I now called you; for everything that I
heard from \[32\] my Father I have made known unto you.   <span
id="fr_3203"></span>[3204](#fn_3203) Ye did not choose <span
id="fr_3204"></span>[3205](#fn_3204) me, but I chose I you, and
appointed you, that ye also should go and bear fruit, and *that* your
fruit should <span id="fr_3205"></span>[3206](#fn_3205) \[33\] abide;
and *that* all that ye shall ask my Father in my name, he may <span
id="fr_3206"></span>[3207](#fn_3206) give you.   <span
id="fr_3207"></span>[3208](#fn_3207) This \[34\] I command <span
id="fr_3208"></span>[3209](#fn_3208) you, that ye love one another. 
 <span id="fr_3209"></span>[3210](#fn_3209) And if the world hate you,
know that \[35\] before you it hated me.   <span
id="fr_3210"></span>[3211](#fn_3210) If then ye were of the world, the
world would love its own:  but ye are not of the world:  I chose you out
of the world:  therefore the world \[36\] \[Arabic, p. 176\] hateth
you.   <span id="fr_3211"></span>[3212](#fn_3211) Remember the word that
I said unto you, that no servant is greater than his lord.  And if they
persecuted <span id="fr_3212"></span>[3213](#fn_3212) me, you also will
they \[37\] persecute; <span id="fr_3213"></span>[3214](#fn_3213) and if
they kept my word, your word also will they keep.   <span
id="fr_3214"></span>[3215](#fn_3214) But all these things will they do
unto you for my name’s sake, for they have not known <span
id="fr_3215"></span>[3216](#fn_3215) him \[38\] that sent me.   <span
id="fr_3216"></span>[3217](#fn_3216) And if I had not come and spoken
unto them, they had not had sin:  \[39\] but now they have no excuse for
their sins.   <span id="fr_3217"></span>[3218](#fn_3217) Whosoever
hateth me, also hateth my \[40\] Father.   <span
id="fr_3218"></span>[3219](#fn_3218) And if I had not done the deeds
before them that no other man did, they would not have had sin:  but now
they have seen and hated me and my Father \[41\] also:  that the word
may be fulfilled that is written in their law,  <span
id="fr_3219"></span>[3220](#fn_3219) They hated me for \[42\] nothing. 
 <span id="fr_3220"></span>[3221](#fn_3220) But when the Paraclete is
come, whom I will send unto you from my Father, even the Spirit of
truth, which goeth forth from my Father, he shall bear witness of \[43\]
me:   <span id="fr_3221"></span>[3222](#fn_3221) and ye also bear
witness, because from the beginning ye *have been* with me.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvi-p64"></span>\[44, 45\]  <span
id="fr_3222"></span>[3223](#fn_3222) I have said that unto you, that ye
may not stumble. <span id="fr_3223"></span>[3224](#fn_3223)    <span
id="fr_3224"></span>[3225](#fn_3224) And they shall put you out of their
synagogues:  and there cometh an <span
id="fr_3225"></span>[3226](#fn_3225) hour when every one that killeth
\[46\] you shall think that he hath offered unto God an offering. 
 <span id="fr_3226"></span>[3227](#fn_3226) And they will do that,
\[47\] because they do not know me, nor my Father.   <span
id="fr_3227"></span>[3228](#fn_3227) I have said that unto you, so that
\[48\] when its time is come, ye may remember it, that I told you. 
 <span id="fr_3228"></span>[3229](#fn_3228) And this hitherto I said not
unto you, because I was with you.  But <span
id="fr_3229"></span>[3230](#fn_3229) now I go unto him that sent me; and
no \[49\] man of you asketh me whither I go.   <span
id="fr_3230"></span>[3231](#fn_3230) I have said that unto you now, and
grief hath \[50\] come and taken possession of your hearts    <span
id="fr_3231"></span>[3232](#fn_3231) But I say the truth unto you; It is
better <span id="fr_3232"></span>[3233](#fn_3232) for you that I go
away:  for if I go not away, the Paraclete will not come unto you;
\[51\] \[Arabic, p. 177\] but if I go away, I will send him unto you. 
 <span id="fr_3233"></span>[3234](#fn_3233) And when he cometh, he will
reprove the world for sin, and for righteousness, and for judgement: 
\[52, 53\] for sin,  <span id="fr_3234"></span>[3235](#fn_3234) because
they have not believed in me;  <span
id="fr_3235"></span>[3236](#fn_3235) and for righteousness, because I go
\[54\] to my Father;  <span id="fr_3236"></span>[3237](#fn_3236) and for
judgement, because <span id="fr_3237"></span>[3238](#fn_3237) the Archon
of this world hath been \[55\] judged.   <span
id="fr_3238"></span>[3239](#fn_3238) And further have I many things to
speak unto you, but ye cannot tarry <span
id="fr_3239"></span>[3240](#fn_3239) \[56\] now.   <span
id="fr_3240"></span>[3241](#fn_3240) Howbeit <span
id="fr_3241"></span>[3242](#fn_3241) when the Spirit of truth is come,
he will remind <span id="fr_3242"></span>[3243](#fn_3242) you of all the
truth:  he will say nothing from himself; but everything that he
heareth, that shall \[57\] he say:  and he shall make known unto you the
things that are to be.   <span id="fr_3243"></span>[3244](#fn_3243) And
he shall \[58\] glorify me; for from me shall he take and shew you. 
 <span id="fr_3244"></span>[3245](#fn_3244) All that my Father hath is
mine:  therefore said I unto you, that he taketh <span
id="fr_3245"></span>[3246](#fn_3245) of mine, and shall shew <span
id="fr_3246"></span>[3247](#fn_3246) you.
