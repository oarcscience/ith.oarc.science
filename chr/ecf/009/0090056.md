<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xlvii-p1.1"></span>Section XLVII.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_3247"></span>[3248](#fn_3247) A little *while*, and ye shall not
behold me; and a little *while* again, and ye shall \[2\] behold me;
because I go to the Father.   <span id="fr_3248"></span>[3249](#fn_3248)
His disciples therefore said one to another, What is this that he hath
said unto us, A little *while*, and ye shall not behold me; and a little
*while* again, and ye shall behold me:  and, I go to my \[3\] Father? 
 <span id="fr_3249"></span>[3250](#fn_3249) And they said, What is this
little *while* that he hath said?  We know not \[4\] what he speaketh. 
 <span id="fr_3250"></span>[3251](#fn_3250) And Jesus perceived that
they were seeking to ask him, and said unto them, Do ye inquire among
yourselves concerning this, that I said unto you, A little *while*, and
ye behold me not, and a little while again, and ye shall \[5\] behold
me?   <span id="fr_3251"></span>[3252](#fn_3251) Verily, verily, <span
id="fr_3252"></span>[3253](#fn_3252) I say unto you, that ye shall weep
and grieve, but the world shall rejoice:  and ye shall be sorrowful, but
your grief shall turn <span id="fr_3253"></span>[3254](#fn_3253) to joy.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-p10"></span><span id="page_116">p. 116</span>
<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-Page_116"></span>\[6\]  <span
id="fr_3254"></span>[3255](#fn_3254) For, a woman when the time is come
for her that she should bring forth, the arrival of the day of her
bringing forth distresseth her:  but whenever she hath brought forth a
son, she remembereth not her distress, for joy at the birth of a man
into the \[7\] world.   <span id="fr_3255"></span>[3256](#fn_3255) And
ye now also grieve:  but I shall see you, and your hearts shall rejoice,
\[8\] \[Arabic, p. 178\] and your joy no man taketh from you.   <span
id="fr_3256"></span>[3257](#fn_3256) And in that day ye shall ask me
nothing.  And verily, verily, <span id="fr_3257"></span>[3258](#fn_3257)
I say unto you, All that ye ask my Father in my name, he will give you. 
 <span id="fr_3258"></span>[3259](#fn_3258) Hitherto ye have asked
nothing \[9\] in my name:  ask, and ye shall receive, that your joy may
be complete.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-p16"></span>\[10\]  <span
id="fr_3259"></span>[3260](#fn_3259) I have spoken unto you now in
ænigmas: <span id="fr_3260"></span>[3261](#fn_3260)   but there will
come an hour when <span id="fr_3261"></span>[3262](#fn_3261) I shall not
speak to you in ænigmas, <span id="fr_3262"></span>[3263](#fn_3262) but
shall reveal unto you the Father plainly, \[11\] in that day when <span
id="fr_3263"></span>[3264](#fn_3263) ye shall ask in my name:   <span
id="fr_3264"></span>[3265](#fn_3264) and I say not unto you, that I
shall \[12\] entreat the Father for you;  <span
id="fr_3265"></span>[3266](#fn_3265) for the Father loveth you, because
ye have loved me, \[13\] and have believed that I came forth from my
Father.   <span id="fr_3266"></span>[3267](#fn_3266) I came forth from
my Father, and came into the world:  and I leave the world, and go unto
my Father.  \[14\]  <span id="fr_3267"></span>[3268](#fn_3267) His
disciples said unto him, Lo, thy speech is now plain, and thou hast not
said one \[15\] thing in an ænigma.   <span
id="fr_3268"></span>[3269](#fn_3268) Now, lo, we know that thou knowest
everything, and needest not that any man should ask thee:  and by this
we believe that thou camest forth \[16, 17\] from God.   <span
id="fr_3269"></span>[3270](#fn_3269) Jesus said unto them, Believe that
an hour cometh,  <span id="fr_3270"></span>[3271](#fn_3270) and lo, it
hath come, and ye shall be scattered, every one of you to his place, and
shall leave me \[18\] alone:  and yet I am not alone, because the Father
is with me.   <span id="fr_3271"></span>[3272](#fn_3271) This have I
said unto you, that in me ye may have peace.  And in the world trouble
shall overtake you:  but be of good courage; for I have overcome the
world.

<span id="iv.iii.xlvii-p30"></span>\[19\]  <span
id="fr_3272"></span>[3273](#fn_3272) This said Jesus, and lifted up his
eyes unto heaven, and said, My Father, the hour \[20\] is come; glorify
thy Son, that thy Son may glorify thee:   <span
id="fr_3273"></span>[3274](#fn_3273) as thou gavest him authority \[21\]
over all flesh, that all that thou hast given him, he might give
them <span id="fr_3274"></span>[3275](#fn_3274) eternal life.   <span
id="fr_3275"></span>[3276](#fn_3275) And this is eternal life, that they
should <span id="fr_3276"></span>[3277](#fn_3276) know that thou alone
art true God, and *that he* \[22\] \[Arabic, p. 179\] whom thou didst
send is Jesus the Messiah. <span id="fr_3277"></span>[3278](#fn_3277)  
 <span id="fr_3278"></span>[3279](#fn_3278) I glorified thee in the
earth, \[23\] and the work which thou gavest me to do I have
accomplished.   <span id="fr_3279"></span>[3280](#fn_3279) And now
glorify thou me, O Father, beside thee, with that glory which I had with
thee \[24\] before the world was.   <span
id="fr_3280"></span>[3281](#fn_3280) I made known thy name to the men
whom thou gavest me out of the world:  thine they were, and thou gavest
them to me; and they have kept \[25, 26\] thy word.   <span
id="fr_3281"></span>[3282](#fn_3281) Now they <span
id="fr_3282"></span>[3283](#fn_3282) know that all that thou hast given
me is from thee:   <span id="fr_3283"></span>[3284](#fn_3283) and the
sayings which thou gavest me I have given unto them; and they received
*them*, and knew of a truth that I came forth from thee, and believed
that thou didst send me.  \[27\]  <span
id="fr_3284"></span>[3285](#fn_3284) And I ask for their sake:  and my
asking is not for the world, but for those whom \[28\] thou hast given
me; for they are thine:   <span id="fr_3285"></span>[3286](#fn_3285) and
all that is mine is thine, and all that is \[29\] thine is mine:  and I
am glorified in them.   <span id="fr_3286"></span>[3287](#fn_3286) And
now I am not in the world, and they are in the world, and I come to
thee.  My <span id="fr_3287"></span>[3288](#fn_3287) holy Father, keep
them in thy \[30\] name which thou hast given unto me, that they may be
one, as we are.   <span id="fr_3288"></span>[3289](#fn_3288) When I was
with them in the world, I kept them in thy name:  and I kept those whom
thou gavest unto me:  and no man of them hath perished, but the son of
perdition; that \[31\] the scripture might be fulfilled.   <span
id="fr_3289"></span>[3290](#fn_3289) Now I come to thee:  and this I say
in the world, \[32\] that my joy may be complete in them.   <span
id="fr_3290"></span>[3291](#fn_3290) I have given them thy word; and the
world \[33\] hated them, because they were not of the world, as I was
not of the world.   <span id="fr_3291"></span>[3292](#fn_3291) And I ask
not this, that thou take them from the world, but that thou keep them
from the \[34, 35\] evil one.   <span
id="fr_3292"></span>[3293](#fn_3292) They were not of the world, as I
was not of the world.   <span id="fr_3293"></span>[3294](#fn_3293) O
Father, sanctify \[36\] them in thy truth:  for thy word is truth. 
 <span id="fr_3294"></span>[3295](#fn_3294) And as thou didst send me
into the world, I \[37\] \[Arabic, p. 180\] also send them into the
world.   <span id="fr_3295"></span>[3296](#fn_3295) And for their sake I
sanctify myself, that they \[38\] also may be sanctified in the truth. 
 <span id="fr_3296"></span>[3297](#fn_3296) Neither for these alone do I
ask, but for \[39\] the sake of them that believe in me through their
word;  <span id="fr_3297"></span>[3298](#fn_3297) that they may be all
one; <span id="page_117">p. 117</span> <span
id="iv.iii.xlvii-Page_117"></span>as thou art in me, and I in thee, and
so they also shall be one in us:  that the world \[40\] may believe that
thou didst send me.   <span id="fr_3298"></span>[3299](#fn_3298) And the
glory which thou hast given unto \[41\] me I have given unto them;
 <span id="fr_3299"></span>[3300](#fn_3299) that they may be one, as we
are one; I in them, and thou in me, that they may be perfect into <span
id="fr_3300"></span>[3301](#fn_3300) one; and *that* the world may know
that \[42\] thou didst send me, and that I <span
id="fr_3301"></span>[3302](#fn_3301) loved them, as thou lovedst me. 
 <span id="fr_3302"></span>[3303](#fn_3302) Father, and those whom thou
hast given me, I wish that, where I am, they may be with me also; that
they may behold my glory, which thou hast given me:  for thou lovedst me
before \[43\] the foundation <span id="fr_3303"></span>[3304](#fn_3303)
of the world.   <span id="fr_3304"></span>[3305](#fn_3304) My righteous
Father, <span id="fr_3305"></span>[3306](#fn_3305) and the world knew
thee not, \[44\] but I know thee; and they knew that thou didst send me;
and I made known unto them thy name, and will make it known to them;
 <span id="fr_3306"></span>[3307](#fn_3306) that the love *wherewith*
thou lovedst me may be in them, and I shall <span
id="fr_3307"></span>[3308](#fn_3307) be in them.
