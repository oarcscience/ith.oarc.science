<span id="iv.iii.xi-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xi-p1.1"></span>Section XI.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p2"></span>\[1\] \[Arabic, p. 42\]  <span
id="fr_813"></span>[814](#fn_813) And when Jesus finished these sayings,
the multitudes were astonished \[2\] at his teaching;  <span
id="fr_814"></span>[815](#fn_814) and that because he was teaching them
as one having authority, not as their scribes and the Pharisees.

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p5"></span>\[3\]  <span
id="fr_815"></span>[816](#fn_815) And when he descended from the
mountain, great multitudes followed him.

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p7"></span>\[4\]  <span
id="fr_816"></span>[817](#fn_816) And when Jesus entered Capernaum, the
servant of one of the chiefs was in an \[5\] evil case, and he was
precious to him, and he was at the point of death.   <span
id="fr_817"></span>[818](#fn_817) And he \[6\] heard of Jesus, and came
to him with the elders of the Jews;  <span
id="fr_818"></span>[819](#fn_818) and he besought him, and said, My
Lord, my boy is laid in the house paralysed, <span
id="fr_819"></span>[820](#fn_819) and he is suffering grievous \[7\]
torment.   <span id="fr_820"></span>[821](#fn_820) And the elders
urgently requested of him, and said, He is worthy that \[8\] this should
be done unto him:   <span id="fr_821"></span>[822](#fn_821) for he
loveth our people, and he also built the synagogue \[9, 10\] for us. 
 <span id="fr_822"></span>[823](#fn_822) Jesus said unto him, I will
come and heal him.   <span id="fr_823"></span>[824](#fn_823) That chief
answered and said, My Lord, I am not worthy that my roof should shade
thee; but it sufficeth \[11\] that thou speak a word, and my lad shall
be healed.   <span id="fr_824"></span>[825](#fn_824) And I also am a man
in obedience to authority, having under my hand soldiers: <span
id="fr_825"></span>[826](#fn_825)   and I say to this *one*, Go, and he
goeth; and to another, Come, and he cometh; and to my servant that he do
this, \[12\] and he doeth *it*.   <span
id="fr_826"></span>[827](#fn_826) And when Jesus heard that, he
marvelled at him, <span id="fr_827"></span>[828](#fn_827) and turned and
said unto the multitude that were coming with him,  <span
id="fr_828"></span>[829](#fn_828) Verily I say unto you, I have \[13\]
not found in Israel *the* like *of* this faith.   <span
id="fr_829"></span>[830](#fn_829) I say unto you, that many shall come
from the east and the west, and shall recline with Abraham and Isaac and
Jacob \[14\] \[Arabic, p. 43\] in the kingdom of heaven:   <span
id="fr_830"></span>[831](#fn_830) but the children of the kingdom shall
be cast <span id="page_61">p. 61</span> <span
id="iv.iii.xi-Page_61"></span>\[15\] forth into the outer darkness:  and
there shall be weeping and gnashing of teeth.   <span
id="fr_831"></span>[832](#fn_831) And Jesus said to that chief, Go thy
way; as thou hast believed, *so* shall it be unto thee.  \[16\]  <span
id="fr_832"></span>[833](#fn_832) And his lad was healed in that hour. 
And that chief returned to the house and found that sick servant healed.

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p25"></span>\[17\]  <span
id="fr_833"></span>[834](#fn_833) And the day after, he was going to a
city called Nain, and his disciples with him, \[18\] and a great
multitude.   <span id="fr_834"></span>[835](#fn_834) And when he was
come near the gate of the city, he saw a crowd <span
id="fr_835"></span>[836](#fn_835) accompanying one *that was* dead, the
only son of his mother; and his mother was a widow:  and there was with
her a great multitude of the people of the \[19\] city.   <span
id="fr_836"></span>[837](#fn_836) And when Jesus saw her, he had
compassion on her, and said unto her, Weep \[20\] not.   <span
id="fr_837"></span>[838](#fn_837) And he went and advanced to the bier,
and the bearers of it stood still; and \[21\] he said, Young man, I say
unto thee, Arise.   <span id="fr_838"></span>[839](#fn_838) And that
dead *man* sat up and began \[22\] to speak; and he gave him to his
mother.   <span id="fr_839"></span>[840](#fn_839) And fear came on all
the people:  and they praised God, and said, There hath risen among us a
great prophet:  and, God \[23\] hath had regard to his people.   <span
id="fr_840"></span>[841](#fn_840) And this news concerning him spread in
all Judæa, and in all the region which was about them.

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p34"></span>\[24\]  <span
id="fr_841"></span>[842](#fn_841) And when Jesus saw great multitudes
surrounding him, he commanded them to \[25\] depart to the other side. 
 <span id="fr_842"></span>[843](#fn_842) And while they were going in
the way, there came one of the scribes and said unto him, My Master, I
will follow thee whithersoever thou \[26\] goest.   <span
id="fr_843"></span>[844](#fn_843) Jesus said unto him, The foxes have
holes, and the birds of the heaven have \[27\] nests; but the Son of man
hath not a place in which to lay his head.   <span
id="fr_844"></span>[845](#fn_844) And he said unto another, Follow me. 
And he said unto him, My Lord, suffer me first to go and \[28\] bury my
father.   <span id="fr_845"></span>[846](#fn_845) Jesus said unto him,
Leave the dead to bury their dead; but thou, \[29\] follow me and preach
the kingdom of God.   <span id="fr_846"></span>[847](#fn_846) And
another said unto him, I will follow \[Arabic, p. 44\] thee, my Lord;
but first suffer me to go and salute my household and \[30\] come. 
 <span id="fr_847"></span>[848](#fn_847) Jesus said unto him, There is
no one who putteth his hand to the plough <span
id="fr_848"></span>[849](#fn_848) and looketh behind him, and *yet* is
fit for the kingdom of God.

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p43"></span>\[31\]  <span
id="fr_849"></span>[850](#fn_849) And he said to them on that day in the
evening, Let us go over to the other side \[32\] of the lake; and he
left <span id="fr_850"></span>[851](#fn_850) the multitudes.   <span
id="fr_851"></span>[852](#fn_851) And Jesus went up and sat in the ship,
\[33\] he and his disciples, and there were with them other ships. 
 <span id="fr_852"></span>[853](#fn_852) And there occurred on the sea a
great tempest <span id="fr_853"></span>[854](#fn_853) of whirlwind and
wind,  <span id="fr_854"></span>[855](#fn_854) and the ship was on the
point of \[34\] sinking from the greatness <span
id="fr_855"></span>[856](#fn_855) of the waves.   <span
id="fr_856"></span>[857](#fn_856) But Jesus was sleeping on a cushion in
the stern of the ship;  <span id="fr_857"></span>[858](#fn_857) and his
disciples came and awoke him, and said unto him, Our \[35\] Lord, save
us; lo, we perish.   <span id="fr_858"></span>[859](#fn_858) And he
rose, and rebuked the winds and the turbulence of the water, and said to
the sea, Be still, for thou art rebuked;  <span
id="fr_859"></span>[860](#fn_859) and the wind \[36\] was still, and
there was a great calm.   <span id="fr_860"></span>[861](#fn_860) And he
said unto them, Why are ye thus \[37\] afraid? and why have ye no
faith?   <span id="fr_861"></span>[862](#fn_861) And they feared
greatly. <span id="fr_862"></span>[863](#fn_862)   And they marvelled,
and said one to another, Who, think you, is this, who commandeth also
the wind and the waves and the sea, and they obey him?

<span id="iv.iii.xi-p58"></span>\[38\]  <span
id="fr_863"></span>[864](#fn_863) And they departed and came to the
country of the Gadarenes, which is on the \[39\] other side, opposite
the land of Galilee.   <span id="fr_864"></span>[865](#fn_864) And when
he went out of the ship to the land,  <span
id="fr_865"></span>[866](#fn_865) there met him from among the tombs a
man who had a devil for a long time, \[40\] and wore no clothes, neither
dwelt in a house, but among the tombs.   <span
id="fr_866"></span>[867](#fn_866) And no man was \[Arabic, p. 45\] able
to bind him with chains,  <span id="fr_867"></span>[868](#fn_867)
because any time that he was bound with chains \[41\] and fetters he cut
the chains and loosened the fetters;  <span
id="fr_868"></span>[869](#fn_868) and he was snatched <span
id="fr_869"></span>[870](#fn_869) \[42\] away of the devil into the
desert,  <span id="fr_870"></span>[871](#fn_870) and no man was able to
quiet him; and at all times, in the night and in the day, he would be
among the tombs and in the mountains;  <span
id="fr_871"></span>[872](#fn_871) and no man was able to pass by that
way;  <span id="fr_872"></span>[873](#fn_872) and he would cry out and
wound himself \[43\] with stones.   <span
id="fr_873"></span>[874](#fn_873) And when he saw Jesus at a distance,
he hastened and worshipped \[44\] him, and cried with a loud voice and
said,  <span id="fr_874"></span>[875](#fn_874) What have we to do with
thee, Jesus, \[45\] Son of the most high God?   <span
id="fr_875"></span>[876](#fn_875) I adjure thee by God, torment me not. 
And Jesus commanded the unclean spirit to come out of the man:  and he
had *suffered* <span id="fr_876"></span>[877](#fn_876) a long \[46\]
time since the time when he came into captivity to it.   <span
id="fr_877"></span>[878](#fn_877) And Jesus asked him, What is thy
name?  He said unto him, Legion; for there had entered into him many
\[47\] devils.   <span id="fr_878"></span>[879](#fn_878) And they
besought him that he would not command them to depart into \[48\] the
depths.   <span id="fr_879"></span>[880](#fn_879) And there was there a
herd of many swine, feeding in the mountain, <span id="page_62">p.
62</span> <span id="iv.iii.xi-Page_62"></span>and those devils besought
him to give them leave to enter the swine; and he gave \[49\] them
leave.   <span id="fr_880"></span>[881](#fn_880) And the devils went out
of the man and entered into the swine.   <span
id="fr_881"></span>[882](#fn_881) And that herd hastened to the summit
and fell down into the midst of the sea, about two \[50\] thousand, and
they were choked in the water.   <span id="fr_882"></span>[883](#fn_882)
And when the keepers saw what \[51\] happened, they fled, and told those
in the cities and villages.   <span id="fr_883"></span>[884](#fn_883)
And the people went out to see what had happened; and they came to
Jesus, and found the man whose \[Arabic, p. 46\] devils had gone out,
clothed, modest, <span id="fr_884"></span>[885](#fn_884) seated at the
feet of Jesus; and they \[52\] feared.   <span
id="fr_885"></span>[886](#fn_885) And they reported what they saw, and
how the man was healed who had a devil,  <span
id="fr_886"></span>[887](#fn_886) and concerning those swine also.
