<span id="xv.iii.i.xviii-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="xv.iii.i.xviii-p1.1"></span>18.  (3) of Substance.</span>

<span id="xv.iii.i.xviii-p2"></span>In the third place a beginning may
be that out of which a thing comes, the underlying matter from which
things are formed.  This, however, is the view of those who hold matter
itself to be uncreated, a view which we believers cannot share, since we
believe God to have made the things that are out of the things which are
not, as the <span id="page_307">p. 307</span> <span
id="xv.iii.i.xviii-Page_307"></span>mother of the seven martyrs in the
Maccabees teaches, <span id="fr_4537"></span>[4538](#fn_4537.md) and as the
angel of repentance in the Shepherd inculcated. <span
id="fr_4538"></span>[4539](#fn_4538.md)
