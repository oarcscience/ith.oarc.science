<span id="iv.iii.lv-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.lv-p1.1"></span>Section LV.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.lv-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_3847"></span>[3848](#fn_3847) But the eleven disciples went into
Galilee, to the mountain <span id="fr_3848"></span>[3849](#fn_3848)
where Jesus had \[2\] appointed them.   <span
id="fr_3849"></span>[3850](#fn_3849) And when they saw him, they
worshipped him:  but there were of \[3\] them who doubted.   <span
id="fr_3850"></span>[3851](#fn_3850) And while they sat there he
appeared to them again, and upbraided *them* for their lack of faith and
the hardness of their hearts, those that saw him when he was risen, and
believed not. <span id="fr_3851"></span>[3852](#fn_3851)

<span id="iv.iii.lv-p8"></span>\[4\] \[Arabic, p. 209\]  <span
id="fr_3852"></span>[3853](#fn_3852) Then said Jesus unto them, I have
been given all authority in heaven \[5\] and earth; and as my Father
hath sent me, so I also send you.   <span
id="fr_3853"></span>[3854](#fn_3853) Go now into \[6\] all the world,
and preach my gospel in all the creation;  <span
id="fr_3854"></span>[3855](#fn_3854) and teach <span
id="fr_3855"></span>[3856](#fn_3855) all the peoples, and \[7\] baptize
them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit;  <span
id="fr_3856"></span>[3857](#fn_3856) and teach them to keep all
whatsoever I commanded you:  and lo, I am with you all the days, unto
\[8\] the end of the world.   <span id="fr_3857"></span>[3858](#fn_3857)
For whosoever believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but \[9\]
whosoever believeth not shall be rejected.   <span
id="fr_3858"></span>[3859](#fn_3858) And the signs <span
id="fr_3859"></span>[3860](#fn_3859) which shall attend those that
believe in me are these:  that they shall cast out devils in my name;
and they \[10\] shall speak with new tongues;  <span
id="fr_3860"></span>[3861](#fn_3860) and they shall take *up* serpents,
and if they drink <span id="page_129">p. 129</span> <span
id="iv.iii.lv-Page_129"></span>deadly poison, <span
id="fr_3861"></span>[3862](#fn_3861) it shall not injure them; and they
shall lay their hands on the diseased, \[11\] and they shall be healed. 
 <span id="fr_3862"></span>[3863](#fn_3862) But ye, abide in the city of
Jerusalem, until ye be clothed with power from on high.

<span id="iv.iii.lv-p20"></span>\[12\]  <span
id="fr_3863"></span>[3864](#fn_3863) And our Lord Jesus, after speaking
to them, took them out to Bethany:  and he \[13\] lifted up his hands,
and blessed them.   <span id="fr_3864"></span>[3865](#fn_3864) And while
he blessed them, he was separated from them, and ascended into heaven,
and sat down at the right hand of God.  \[14, 15\]  <span
id="fr_3865"></span>[3866](#fn_3865) And they worshipped him, and
returned to Jerusalem with great joy:   <span
id="fr_3866"></span>[3867](#fn_3866) and at all times they were in the
temple, praising and blessing God.  Amen.

<span id="iv.iii.lv-p25"></span>\[16\]  <span
id="fr_3867"></span>[3868](#fn_3867) And from thence they went forth,
and preached in every place; and our Lord helped them, and confirmed
their sayings by the signs which they did. <span
id="fr_3868"></span>[3869](#fn_3868)

<span id="iv.iii.lv-p28"></span>\[17\]  <span
id="fr_3869"></span>[3870](#fn_3869) And here are also many other things
which Jesus did, which if they were written every one of them, not even
the world, according to my opinion, would contain the books which should
be written. <span id="fr_3870"></span>[3871](#fn_3870)
