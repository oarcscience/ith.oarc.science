<span id="xii.vi.xviii-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="xii.vi.xviii-p1.1"></span>Chapter XVIII.—The Author Sinful, Yet
Pursuing.</span>

<span id="xii.vi.xviii-p2"></span>And let us, then, be of the number of
those who give thanks, who have served God, and not of the ungodly who
are judged.  For I myself, though a sinner every whit and not yet
fleeing temptation but continuing in the midst of the tools of the
devil, study to follow after righteousness, that I may make, be it only
some, approach to it, fearing the judgment to come.
