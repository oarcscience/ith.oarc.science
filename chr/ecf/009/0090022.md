<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xiii-p1.1"></span>Section XIII.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_950"></span>[951](#fn_950.md) I am sending you as lambs among
wolves:  be ye now wise as serpents, and \[2\] harmless <span
id="fr_951"></span>[952](#fn_951.md) as doves.   <span
id="fr_952"></span>[953](#fn_952.md) Beware of men:  they shall deliver you
to the councils of the \[3\] magistrates, and scourge you in their
synagogues;  <span id="fr_953"></span>[954](#fn_953.md) and shall bring you
before governors and kings for my sake, for a testimony against them and
against the nations.  \[4\]  <span id="fr_954"></span>[955](#fn_954.md) And
when they deliver you up, be not <span id="fr_955"></span>[956](#fn_955.md)
anxious, nor consider beforehand, what ye \[5\] shall say; but ye shall
be given <span id="fr_956"></span>[957](#fn_956.md) in that hour what ye
ought to speak.   <span id="fr_957"></span>[958](#fn_957.md) Ye do not
\[6\] speak, but the Spirit of your Father speaketh in you.   <span
id="fr_958"></span>[959](#fn_958.md) The brother shall deliver up his
brother to death, and the father his son; and the sons shall rise
against their \[7\] parents, and put them to death.   <span
id="fr_959"></span>[960](#fn_959.md) And ye shall be hated of every man
because of \[8\] my name; but he that endureth unto the end of the
matter shall be saved. <span id="fr_960"></span>[961](#fn_960.md)    <span
id="fr_961"></span>[962](#fn_961.md) When they expel you from this city,
flee to another.  Verily I say unto you, Ye shall not finish all the
cities of the people of Israel, until the Son of man come.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p15"></span>\[9, 10\]  <span
id="fr_962"></span>[963](#fn_962.md) A disciple is not superior to his
lord, nor a servant to his master.   <span
id="fr_963"></span>[964](#fn_963.md) For it is enough then for the disciple
that he be as his lord, and the servant as his master.  If they have
called the master of the house Beelzebul, how much more the people <span
id="page_64">p. 64</span> <span id="iv.iii.xiii-Page_64"></span>\[11\]
of his house!   <span id="fr_964"></span>[965](#fn_964.md) Fear them not
therefore: <span id="fr_965"></span>[966](#fn_965.md)   for there is
nothing covered, that shall \[12\] \[Arabic, p. 51\] not be revealed;
nor hid, that shall not be disclosed and published.   <span
id="fr_966"></span>[967](#fn_966.md) What I say unto you in the darkness,
speak ye in the light; and what ye have told \[13\] secretly in the ears
in closets, let it be proclaimed on the housetops.   <span
id="fr_967"></span>[968](#fn_967.md) I say unto you now, my beloved, Be not
agitated at <span id="fr_968"></span>[969](#fn_968.md) those who kill the
body, but have no power to \[14\] kill the soul.  I will inform you whom
ye shall fear:  him <span id="fr_969"></span>[970](#fn_969.md) which is
able to destroy \[15\] soul and body in hell.   <span
id="fr_970"></span>[971](#fn_970.md) Yea, I say unto you, Be afraid of him
especially.  Are not two sparrows sold for a farthing in a bond? <span
id="fr_971"></span>[972](#fn_971.md) and one of them shall not fall on the
\[16\] ground without your Father.   <span
id="fr_972"></span>[973](#fn_972.md) But what concerns you:  even the hair
of your heads \[17, 18\] also is numbered.   <span
id="fr_973"></span>[974](#fn_973.md) Fear not therefore; ye are better than
many sparrows.   <span id="fr_974"></span>[975](#fn_974.md) Every man who
confesseth me now before men, I also will confess him before my Father
\[19\] which is in heaven;  <span id="fr_975"></span>[976](#fn_975.md) but
whosoever denieth me before men, I also will deny him before my Father
which is in heaven.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p30"></span>\[20\]  <span
id="fr_976"></span>[977](#fn_976.md) Think ye that I am come to cast peace
into the earth?  I came not to cast peace, \[21\] but to cast
dissension.   <span id="fr_977"></span>[978](#fn_977.md) Henceforth there
shall be five in one house, three of them \[22\] disagreeing with two,
and the two with the three.   <span id="fr_978"></span>[979](#fn_978.md)
The father shall become hostile to his son, and the son to his father;
and the mother to her daughter, and the daughter to her mother; and the
mother in law to her daughter in law, and the daughter \[23\] in law to
her mother in law:   <span id="fr_979"></span>[980](#fn_979.md) and a man’s
enemies shall be the people of his house.  \[24\]  <span
id="fr_980"></span>[981](#fn_980.md) Whosoever loveth father or mother
better than me is not worthy of me; and whosoever \[Arabic, p. 52\]
loveth son or daughter more than his love of me is not worthy of me. 
\[25\]  <span id="fr_981"></span>[982](#fn_981.md) And every one that doth
not take his cross and follow me is not worthy of \[26\] me.   <span
id="fr_982"></span>[983](#fn_982.md) Whosoever findeth his life <span
id="fr_983"></span>[984](#fn_983.md) shall lose it; and whosoever loseth
his life <span id="fr_984"></span>[985](#fn_984.md) for my sake shall find
it.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p40"></span>\[27\]  <span
id="fr_985"></span>[986](#fn_985.md) And whosoever receiveth you receiveth
me; and whosoever receiveth me receiveth \[28\] him that sent me. 
 <span id="fr_986"></span>[987](#fn_986.md) And whosoever receiveth a
prophet in the name of a prophet shall take <span
id="fr_987"></span>[988](#fn_987.md) a prophet’s reward; and whosoever
shall receive a righteous man \[29\] in the name of a righteous man
shall take <span id="fr_988"></span>[989](#fn_988.md) a righteous man’s
reward.   <span id="fr_989"></span>[990](#fn_989.md) And every one that
shall give to drink to one of these least ones a drink of water only, in
the name of a disciple, verily I say unto you, he shall not lose his
reward.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p46"></span>\[30\]  <span
id="fr_990"></span>[991](#fn_990.md) And when Jesus finished charging his
twelve disciples, he removed thence to \[31\] teach and preach in their
cities.   <span id="fr_991"></span>[992](#fn_991.md) And while they were
going in the way they entered into a certain village; and a woman named
Martha entertained him in her house.  \[32\]  <span
id="fr_992"></span>[993](#fn_992.md) And she had a sister named Mary, and
she came and sat at the feet of our Lord, \[33\] and heard his sayings. 
 <span id="fr_993"></span>[994](#fn_993.md) But Martha was disquieted by
much serving; and she came and said unto him, My Lord, givest thou no
heed that my sister left me alone to \[34\] serve? speak to her that she
help me.   <span id="fr_994"></span>[995](#fn_994.md) Jesus answered and
said unto her, Martha, \[35\] Martha, thou art solicitous and
impatient <span id="fr_995"></span>[996](#fn_995.md) on account of many
things:   <span id="fr_996"></span>[997](#fn_996.md) but what is sought is
one *thing*.  But Mary hath chosen for herself a good portion, and that
which shall not be taken from her.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p54"></span>\[36\]  <span
id="fr_997"></span>[998](#fn_997.md) And the apostles went forth, and
preached to the people that they might repent.  \[37\]  <span
id="fr_998"></span>[999](#fn_998.md) And they cast out many devils, and
anointed many sick with oil, and healed them.  \[38, 39\]  <span
id="fr_999"></span>[1000](#fn_999.md) And the disciples of John told
him <span id="fr_1000"></span>[1001](#fn_1000.md) of all these things. 
 <span id="fr_1001"></span>[1002](#fn_1001.md) And when John heard in
\[Arabic, p. 53\] the prison of the doings of the Messiah, he called two
of his disciples, and sent them to Jesus, and said, Art thou he that
cometh, or look we for \[40\] another?   <span
id="fr_1002"></span>[1003](#fn_1002.md) And they came to Jesus, and said
unto him, John the Baptist hath sent \[41\] us unto thee, and said, Art
thou he that cometh, or look we for another?   <span
id="fr_1003"></span>[1004](#fn_1003.md) And in that hour he cured many of
diseases, and of plagues of an evil spirit; and he gave sight \[42\] to
many blind.   <span id="fr_1004"></span>[1005](#fn_1004.md) Jesus answered
and said unto them, Go and tell John everything ye have seen and heard: 
the blind see, and the lame walk, and the lepers are cleansed, and the
blind <span id="fr_1005"></span>[1006](#fn_1005.md) hear, and the dead
rise, and the poor have the gospel preached to \[43\] them.   <span
id="fr_1006"></span>[1007](#fn_1006.md) And blessed is he who doubteth not
in me.

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p65"></span><span id="page_65">p. 65</span> <span
id="iv.iii.xiii-Page_65"></span>\[44\]  <span
id="fr_1007"></span>[1008](#fn_1007.md) And when John’s disciples departed,
Jesus began to say to the multitudes concerning John, What went ye out
into the wilderness to see? a reed shaken with the \[45\] winds?   <span
id="fr_1008"></span>[1009](#fn_1008.md) And if not, then what went ye out
to see? a man clothed in soft raiment?  Behold, they that are in
magnificent garments and in voluptuousness are in the abode \[46\] of
kings.   <span id="fr_1009"></span>[1010](#fn_1009.md) And if not, then
what went ye out to see? a prophet?  Yea, I say unto \[47\] you, and
more than a prophet.   <span id="fr_1010"></span>[1011](#fn_1010.md) This
is he of whom it is written,

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p70"></span>I am sending my messenger before thy
face

<span id="iv.iii.xiii-p71"></span>To prepare the way before thee.
