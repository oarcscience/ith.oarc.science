<span id="vi.v.lx-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="vi.v.lx-p1.1"></span>Chapter LIX.—Of the Occasion on Which the
Disciples Were Exceeding Sorry When He Spoke to Them of His Passion, as
It is Related in the Same Order by the Three Evangelists.</span>

<span id="vi.v.lx-p2"></span>117. Matthew continues thus: “And while
they abode in Galilee, Jesus said unto them, The Son of man shall be
betrayed into the hands of men; and they shall kill Him, and the third
day He shall rise again. And they were exceeding sorry.” <span
id="fr_1104"></span>[1105](#fn_1104.md) Mark and Luke record this passage
in the same order. <span id="fr_1105"></span>[1106](#fn_1105.md)
