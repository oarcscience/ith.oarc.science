<span id="vi.v-p1"></span><span class="c37"><span
id="vi.v-p1.1"></span>Book II.</span>

<span id="vi.v-p2"></span>In this book Augustin undertakes an orderly
examination of the Gospel according to Matthew, on to the narrative of
the Supper, and institutes a comparison between it and the other gospels
by Mark, Luke, and John, with the view of demonstrating a complete
harmony between the four evangelists throughout all these sections.
