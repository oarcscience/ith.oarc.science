<span id="vi.vi-p1"></span><span class="c37"><span
id="vi.vi-p1.1"></span>Book III.</span>

<span id="vi.vi-p2"></span>This book contains a demonstration of the
harmony of the evangelists from the accounts of the Supper on to the end
of the Gospel, the narratives given by the several writers being
collated, and the whole arranged in one orderly connection.
