### <span id="iv.XV-p1.1"></span>CHAPTER XV.

<span id="iv.XV-p2"></span>

<span id="iv.XV-p3"></span>He is greater than can be conceived.

<span id="iv.XV-p4"></span>

<span id="iv.XV-p5"></span>THEREFORE, O Lord, thou art not only that
than which a greater cannot be conceived, but thou art a being greater
than can be conceived. For, since it can be conceived that there is such
a being, if thou art not this very being, a greater than thou can be
conceived. But this is impossible.
