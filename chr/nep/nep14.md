THE INFANT'S GRACE BEFORE AND AFTER MEAT.

BLESS me, O Lord, and let my food strengthen me to serve thee, for Jesus
Christ's sake. Amen.  
I desire to thank God, who gives me food to eat every day of my life.
Amen.
