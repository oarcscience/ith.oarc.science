<span id="iii.viii-p1"></span>The Sacred Oblations

<span id="iii.viii-p2"></span><span id="iii.viii-p2.1"></span>[Leviticus
24](../../../bib/kjv/lev024.htm#000)<span id="iii.viii-p2.1"></span>

<span id="iii.viii-p3"></span><span id="iii.viii-p3.1"></span>[Leviticus
24:5-9](../../../bib/kjv/lev024.htm#005)<span id="iii.viii-p3.1"></span>

<span id="iii.viii-p4"></span>**5.** And thou shalt take fine flour, and
bake twelve cakes thereof: two tenth-deals shall be in one cake.

<span id="iii.viii-p5"></span>**5.** Accipies similam, et coques ex ea
duodecim placentas: duarum decimarum sit unaquseque placenta.

<span id="iii.viii-p6"></span>**6.** And thou shalt set them in two
rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before the LORD.

<span id="iii.viii-p7"></span>**6.** Et pones eas in duobus ordinibus:
seni ordines super mensam mundam coram Jehova.

<span id="iii.viii-p8"></span>**7.** And thou shalt put pure
frankincense upon *each* row, that it may be on the bread for a
memorial, *even* an offering made by fire unto the LORD.

<span id="iii.viii-p9"></span>**7.** Pones quoque super ordinem utrunque
thus purum, eritque pani in memoriale et incensum Jehovae.

<span id="iii.viii-p10"></span>**8.** Every sabbath he shall set it in
order before the LORD continually, *being taken* from the children of
Israel by an everlasting covenant.

<span id="iii.viii-p11"></span>**8.** Per singula sabbatha ordinabit
illos coram Jehova semper, a filiis Israel foedere sempiterno.

<span id="iii.viii-p12"></span>**9.** And it shall be Aaron’s and his
sons’; and they shall eat it in the holy place: for it *is* most holy
unto him of the offerings of the LORD made by fire by a perpetual
statute.

<span id="iii.viii-p13"></span>**9.** Et erit Aharonis et filiorum ejus,
qui comedent eum in loco sancto: quia sanctitatis sanctitatum est ei ex
oblationibus ignitis Jehovae statuto perpetuo.
