# CHAPTER XXXI<span id="page_65_fr_8"></span>[<sup><span class="small">8</span></sup>](#page_65_note_8).

OF JOSHUA THE SON OF NUN, AND BRIEF NOTICES OF THE YEARS OF THE JUDGES
AND THE KINGS OF THE CHILDREN OF ISRAEL.

   A<span class="small">FTER</span> Moses was dead, God said to Joshua
the son of Nun, 'Moses My servant is dead; now therefore arise, go over
this Jordan, thou and <span id="page_66">p. 66</span> all this people,
unto the land which I have sworn to their fathers to give them, Every
place upon which ye tread shall be yours<span
id="page_66_fr_1"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">1</span></sup>](#page_66_note_1).' So Joshua the son of
Nun gathered the people together, and passed over Jordan. Jordan was
divided on this side and on that, and the children of Israel passed over
as upon dry ground, even as their fathers passed through the sea of
Sôph, when they went forth from Egypt<span
id="page_66_fr_2"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">2</span></sup>](#page_66_note_2). And they took twelve
stones from the midst of Jordan, as a memorial for those after them<span
id="page_66_fr_3"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">3</span></sup>](#page_66_note_3). And they took Jericho,
and destroyed it<span id="page_66_fr_4"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">4</span></sup>](#page_66_note_4); and Joshua the son of
Nun slew thirty-one kings of the foreign nations, and divided the land
among them, and he brake their idols and images. These are the names of
the kings whom Joshua the son of Nun destroyed<span
id="page_66_fr_5"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">5</span></sup>](#page_66_note_5). The king of Jericho, the
king of Ai, the king of Jerusalem, the king of Hebron, the king of
Jarmuth, the king of Lachish, the king of Eglon, the king of Gezer, the
king of Debir, the king of Hormah, the king of Geder, the king of
Arad<span id="page_66_fr_6"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">6</span></sup>](#page_66_note_6), the king of Libnah, the
king of Adullam<span id="page_66_fr_7"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">7</span></sup>](#page_66_note_7), the king of
Makkedah<span id="page_66_fr_8"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">8</span></sup>](#page_66_note_8), the king of Bethel, the
king of Tappuah, the king of Hepher, the king of Aphek, the king of
Lashsharon<span id="page_66_fr_9"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">9</span></sup>](#page_66_note_9); the king of Madon, the
king of Hazor, the king of Shimron-meron<span
id="page_66_fr_10"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">10</span></sup>](#page_66_note_10), the king of Achshaph,
the king of Taanach, the king of Megiddo<span
id="page_66_fr_11"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">11</span></sup>](#page_66_note_11), the king of
Re<u>k</u>am (Kadesh), the king of Jokneam<span
id="page_66_fr_12"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">12</span></sup>](#page_66_note_12), the king of Dor and
Naphath-Dor, the king of Goiim<span
id="page_66_fr_13"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">13</span></sup>](#page_66_note_13), the king of
Tirzah<span id="page_66_fr_14"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">14</span></sup>](#page_66_note_14),

   And as we do not intend to write a complete history of the kings and
judges, but only to collect a few matters which may serve for the
consolation of the feeble in a time of despondency, behold we pass over
them with brief notices. If however any one seeks to know these
(things), let him read in the Tôrah and in the Bêth-Maut<u>e</u>bhê<span
id="page_66_fr_15"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">15</span></sup>](#page_66_note_15), whence he will
understand clearly. Moses ruled the people in the desert forty <span
id="page_67">p. 67</span> years<span
id="page_67_fr_1"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">1</span></sup>](#page_67_note_1). Joshua ruled the people
twenty-five years<span id="page_67_fr_2"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">2</span></sup>](#page_67_note_2). Judah was ruler of the
people forty-eight years<span id="page_67_fr_3"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">3</span></sup>](#page_67_note_3). Eglon king of Moab<span
id="page_67_fr_4"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">4</span></sup>](#page_67_note_4) oppressed the people
eighteen years. Ahôr (Ehûd) was ruler of the people eighty years<span
id="page_67_fr_5"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">5</span></sup>](#page_67_note_5). Nâbîn (Jabin) oppressed
Israel twenty years<span id="page_67_fr_6"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">6</span></sup>](#page_67_note_6). Deborah and Barak were
rulers of the people forty years<span
id="page_67_fr_7"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">7</span></sup>](#page_67_note_7). The Midianites oppressed
Israel seven years<span id="page_67_fr_8"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">8</span></sup>](#page_67_note_8). Gideon was ruler of the
people forty years<span id="page_67_fr_9"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">9</span></sup>](#page_67_note_9). He had seventy sons, who
rode with him upon seventy ass colts<span
id="page_67_fr_10"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">10</span></sup>](#page_67_note_10). Abimelech the son of
Gideon was ruler of the people sixty years<span
id="page_67_fr_11"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">11</span></sup>](#page_67_note_11). Tola the son of Puah
was ruler of the people twenty-three years<span
id="page_67_fr_12"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">12</span></sup>](#page_67_note_12). Jair was ruler of the
people twenty-two years<span id="page_67_fr_13"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">13</span></sup>](#page_67_note_13). The Philistines and
Ammonites oppressed the people eighteen years<span
id="page_67_fr_14"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">14</span></sup>](#page_67_note_14). Naphthah (Jephthah)
was ruler of the people six years<span
id="page_67_fr_15"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">15</span></sup>](#page_67_note_15). He vowed a vow to the
Lord and said, 'Whatsoever cometh forth to meet me from my house, I will
offer up as an offering to the Lord.' And his only daughter came forth,
and he offered her up as an offering to the Lord. Abîzan (Ibzan) was
ruler of the people seven years<span
id="page_67_fr_16"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">16</span></sup>](#page_67_note_16). He had thirty sons and
thirty daughters; he sent out the thirty daughters and brought in thirty
daughters-in-law. Elon was a ruler of the people ten years<span
id="page_67_fr_17"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">17</span></sup>](#page_67_note_17). Acrôn (Abdon) was
ruler of the people eight years<span
id="page_67_fr_18"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">18</span></sup>](#page_67_note_18). The <span
id="page_68">p. 68</span> Philistines oppressed Israel forty years<span
id="page_68_fr_1"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">1</span></sup>](#page_68_note_1). Samson was ruler of the
people twenty years<span id="page_68_fr_2"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">2</span></sup>](#page_68_note_2). He slew a thousand men
with the jawbone of a dead ass. Eli was ruler of the people forty
years<span id="page_68_fr_3"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">3</span></sup>](#page_68_note_3). From Eli, the ark was in
the house of Abinadab twenty years<span
id="page_68_fr_4"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">4</span></sup>](#page_68_note_4). Samuel was ruler of the
people thirty years<span id="page_68_fr_5"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">5</span></sup>](#page_68_note_5). Saul was ruler of the
people forty<span id="page_68_fr_6"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">6</span></sup>](#page_68_note_6) years.These years of the
Judges (*lit*. rulers) amount to six hundred and fifty-five<span
id="page_68_fr_7"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">7</span></sup>](#page_68_note_7). King David reigned forty
years<span id="page_68_fr_8"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">8</span></sup>](#page_68_note_8). Solomon reigned forty
years<span id="page_68_fr_9"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">9</span></sup>](#page_68_note_9). Rehoboam reigned
seventeen years<span id="page_68_fr_10"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">10</span></sup>](#page_68_note_10). Abijah reigned three
years<span id="page_68_fr_11"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">11</span></sup>](#page_68_note_11). Asa reigned forty-one
years<span id="page_68_fr_12"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">12</span></sup>](#page_68_note_12). Jehoshaphat reigned
twenty-five years<span id="page_68_fr_13"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">13</span></sup>](#page_68_note_13). Joram reigned eight
years<span id="page_68_fr_14"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">14</span></sup>](#page_68_note_14). Ahaziah reigned one
year<span id="page_68_fr_15"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">15</span></sup>](#page_68_note_15). Athaliah reigned six
years<span id="page_68_fr_16"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">16</span></sup>](#page_68_note_16). Joash reigned forty
years<span id="page_68_fr_17"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">17</span></sup>](#page_68_note_17). Amaziah reigned
twenty-three years<span id="page_68_fr_18"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">18</span></sup>](#page_68_note_18). Uzziah reigned
fifty-two years<span id="page_68_fr_19"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">19</span></sup>](#page_68_note_19). Jotham reigned sixteen
years<span id="page_68_fr_20"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">20</span></sup>](#page_68_note_20). Hezekiah reigned
twenty-nine years<span id="page_68_fr_21"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">21</span></sup>](#page_68_note_21). He prayed before God,
and fifteen years were added to his life; and he held back the sun and
the moon in their course. Manasseh reigned fifty-five years<span
id="page_68_fr_22"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">22</span></sup>](#page_68_note_22). He sawed Isaiah with a
wooden saw and killed him. Amon reigned two years<span
id="page_68_fr_23"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">23</span></sup>](#page_68_note_23). Josiah reigned
thirty-one years<span id="page_68_fr_24"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">24</span></sup>](#page_68_note_24). Jehoahaz reigned three
months<span id="page_68_fr_25"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">25</span></sup>](#page_68_note_25). Jehoiakim reigned
eleven years<span id="page_68_fr_26"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">26</span></sup>](#page_68_note_26). Jehoiachin reigned one
hundred <span id="page_69">p. 69</span> days<span
id="page_69_fr_1"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">1</span></sup>](#page_69_note_1). Zedekiah reigned seven
years<span id="page_69_fr_2"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">2</span></sup>](#page_69_note_2). These years of the kings
amount to four hundred and fifty-five years, six months, and ten
days<span id="page_69_fr_3"></span>[<sup><span
class="small">3</span></sup>](#page_69_note_3).
