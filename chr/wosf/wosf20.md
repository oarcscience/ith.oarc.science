### III.

### PRAYER TO OBTAIN DIVINE LOVE.

The authenticity of this prayer, accepted by the Quaracchi editors,
rests on the authority of St. Bernardine of Siena <span
id="fr_548"></span>[1](#fn_548) and Ubertino da Casale, <span
id="fr_549"></span>[2](#fn_549) both of whom are quoted in its behalf by
Wadding. The prayer is here translated according to the text given by
Ubertino in his *Arbor Vitae Crucifixae* composed on La Verna in
1305. <span id="fr_550"></span>[3](#fn_550) It follows:—

#### PRAYER.

I beseech Thee, O Lord, that the fiery and sweet strength of Thy love
may absorb my soul from all things that are under heaven, that I may die
for love of Thy love as Thou didst deign to die for love of my love.
