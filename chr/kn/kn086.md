### 86. *How Queen* <span class="small">MÂKĔDÂ</span> *made her son King*

   And on the third day <span class="small">MÂKĔDÂ</span> delivered over
to her son seventeen thousand and seven hundred chosen horses, which
were to watch the army of the enemy, and would again plunder the cities
of the enemy, and <span id="page_147">p. 147</span> seven thousand and
seven hundred mares that had borne foals, and one thousand female mules,
and seven hundred chosen mules, and apparel of honour, gold and silver
measured by the *gômôr*, and measured by the *ḳôr*, some six and some
seven, and she delivered over to her son everything that was his by law,
and all the throne of her kingdom.
