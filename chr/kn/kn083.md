### 83. *Concerning the King of the* <span class="small">ISHMAELITES</span>

   And therefore the children of <span class="small">ISHMAEL</span>
became kings over <span class="small">TEREB</span>, and over <span
class="small">ḲEBEṬ</span>, and over <span class="small">NÔBÂ</span>,
and <span class="small">SÔBA</span>, and <span
class="small">KUERGUE</span>, and <span class="small">KÎFÎ</span>, and
<span class="small">MÂKÂ</span>, and <span class="small">MÔRNÂ</span>,
and <span class="small">FÎNḲÂNÂ</span>, and <span
class="small">’ARSÎBÂNÂ</span>, and <span class="small">LÎBÂ</span>, and
<span class="small">MASE‛A</span>, for they were the seed of <span
class="small">SHEM</span>. And <span class="small">ISAAC</span> reigned
over <span class="small">JUDAH</span> and over <span

class="small">KÊṬÊWÔN</span>, and <span

Who when He pleaseth abrogateth their power; for unto Him belong power
and dominion over all created things for ever and ever. Amen.
