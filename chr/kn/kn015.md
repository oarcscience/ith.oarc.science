### 15. *Concerning* <span class="small">ISAAC</span> *and* <span class="small">JACOB</span>

   And <span class="small">ISAAC</span> his son became king, and he did
not transgress the commandment of God; and he was pure in his soul and
in his body, and he died in honour. And his son <span
class="small">JACOB</span> reigned, and he also did not transgress the
commandment of God, and his possessions became numerous, and his
children were many; and God blessed him and he died in honour.
