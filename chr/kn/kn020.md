### 20. *Concerning the Division of the Earth*

   From the middle of <span class="small">JERUSALEM</span>, and from the
north thereof to the south-east is the portion of the Emperor of <span
class="small">RÔM</span>; and from the middle of <span
class="small">JERUSALEM</span> from the north thereof to the south and
to <span class="small">WESTERN INDIA</span> is the portion of the
Emperor of <span class="small">ETHIOPIA</span>. For both of them are of
the seed of <span class="small">SHEM</span>, the son of <span
class="small">NOAH</span>, the seed of <span
class="small">ABRAHAM</span>, the seed of <span
class="small">DAVID</span>, the children of <span
class="small">SOLOMON</span>. For God gave the seed of <span
class="small">SHEM</span> glory because of the blessing of their father
<span class="small">NOAH</span>. The Emperor of <span
class="small">RÔM</span> is the son of <span
class="small">SOLOMON</span>, and the Emperor of <span
class="small">ETHIOPIA</span> is the firstborn and eldest son of <span
class="small">SOLOMON</span>.
