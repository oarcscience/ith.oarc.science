### CHAPTER VI

<span class="small">How Friar Juniper kept silence for six months</span>

FRIAR JUNIPER once resolved to keep silence for six months after this
manner. The first day for love of the Heavenly Father. The second day
for love of Jesus Christ His Son. The third for love of the Holy Ghost.
The fourth day for reverence of the most holy Virgin Mary; and so each
day, in succession, for love of some saint, he observed the six months
without speaking.
