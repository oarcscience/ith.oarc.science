### CHAPTER VIII

<span class="small">How a holy man, being in prayer, beheld the soul of
Friar Giles go to the life eternal</span>

A GOOD man, being in prayer, when Friar Giles passed from this life, saw
his soul go up to heaven, together with a multitude of other souls which

<span id="page_232">p. 232</span>

at that hour came forth from Purgatory; and he saw Jesus Christ come to
meet the soul of Friar Giles, and, with a multitude of Angels and all
those souls, ascend into the glory of Paradise, to a great sound of
sweet music.
