# Roe v. Wade

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is the text of the Roe v. Wade decision of the US Supreme Court,
which effectively legalized abortion in the United States. Of special
interest is the review of the legal status and attitudes toward
abortion, particularly religious, from antiquity on.
