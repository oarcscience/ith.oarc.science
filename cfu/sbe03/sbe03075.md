##### ODE 6. THE HÂO THIEN YÛ *KH*ĂNG MING.

##### APPROPRIATE TO A SACRIFICE TO KING *KH*ĂNG.

Khăng was the honorary title of Sung, the son and successor of king Wû,
B.C. 1115 to 1079.

Heaven made its determinate appointment, which our two sovereigns
received <span id="fr_361"></span>[2](#fn_349). King *Kh*ăng did not
dare to rest idly in it, But night and day enlarged

<span id="page_317">p. 317</span>

its foundations by his deep and silent virtue. How did he continue and
glorify (his heritage), Exerting all his heart, And so securing its
tranquillity!
