#### CHAPTER XIII

<span class="small">AMPLIFICATION OF "THE HIGHEST VIRTUE"</span>

<span class="smallcaps">Confucius</span> said: "When a ruler wishes to
teach his people to love their parents, he does not go to their family
every day to teach them. He teaches them by his showing reverence to all
old people. In the same manner he teaches his people to show respect to
their elders by doing so first; and to be loyal to their ruler by his
doing duty to his superiors first.

"The *Shih Ching* says, "The behaviour of the ruler is so good that he
is loved by the people as

<span id="page_28">p. 28</span>

their parent." A ruler could not have been so loved by his people had he
not possessed the highest virtue."
