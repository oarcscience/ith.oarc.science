### \[8\] SELF–ABANDONMENT

<span class="smallcaps">I sat</span> drinking and did not notice the
dusk,  
Till falling petals filled the folds of my dress.  
Drunken I rose and walked to the moonlit stream;  
The birds were gone, and men also few.
