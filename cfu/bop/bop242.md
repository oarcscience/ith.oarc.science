#### IX

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *T‘iao Chih Hua;* metaphorical. The writer laments his misery amidst
and in consequence of the general decay of the kingdom.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Deep yellow are
turned  
  The bignonia flowers; <span id="page_329">p. 329</span>  
And my wounded heart  
  Its sorrow outpours.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>The flowers are
all gone;  
  But green leaves are seen.  
Than this fate have known,  
  Better not to have been!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>In fish traps but
stars!  
  Ewes thin, with large head!  
While some may have food,  
  Most languish, ill fed.

</div>
