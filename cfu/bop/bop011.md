#### VIII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Fou I;* narrative. The song of the plantain gatherers.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>We gather and
gather the plantains;  
  Come gather them anyhow.  
Yes, gather and gather the plantains,  
  And here we have got them now.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>We gather and
gather the plantains;  
  Now off the ears we must tear.  
Yes, gather and gather the plantains,  
  And now the seeds are laid bare.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>We gather and
gather the plantains,  
  The seeds in our skirts are placed.  
Yes, gather and gather the plantains,  
  Ho! safe in the girdled waist.

</div>
