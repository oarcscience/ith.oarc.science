#### IV

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Chiao Liao;* allusive and metaphorical. Supposed to celebrate the
power and prosperity of Huanshu, and to predict the growth of his
family.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_1">1</span></span>                What clusters so rare  
                The pepper plants bear!  
A measure, luxuriant and large, they would fill.  
                As much without peer  
                Is this hero here!  
Sing hey for the shoots of the pepper plant still!

<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_2">2</span></span>                What clusters so rare  
                The pepper plants bear!  
Luxuriant and large, both your hands they would fill.  
                So generous and great  
                This chief of our state!  
And it's hey for the far-shooting pepper plant still!

</div>
