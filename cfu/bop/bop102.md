#### IV

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Tung Fang Chih Jih;* narrative. The licentious intercourse of the
people of Ch‘i.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>When the sun is in
the east,  
  That lovely girl I see. <span id="page_108">p. 108</span>  
In my chamber she appears;  
  There fronting me is she.  
She treads upon my footsteps,  
  And quickly comes to me.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>When the moon is
in the east,  
  That lovely girl I see.  
’Twixt door and screen she passes;  
  ’Twixt screen and door is she.  
She treads upon my footsteps,  
  And hastes away from me.

</div>
