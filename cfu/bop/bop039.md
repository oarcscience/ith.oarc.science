#### XI

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Shih Wei;* narrative. The officers of some state, who were refugees
and in distress in Wei, exhort their ruler to return with them.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>At this low ebb!
At this low ebb!  
  Why not, O prince, return to Li? <span id="page_39">p. 39</span>  
But for your sake, why bide we here,  
  Houseless beneath the dew to be?

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>At this low ebb!
At this low ebb!  
  Why not to Li go back again?  
But for your person, how should we  
  Here in the mire so long have lain?

</div>
