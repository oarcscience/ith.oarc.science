#### X

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Ch‘üan Yü;* narrative, Some parties, probably refugees, complain of
the diminished respect and attention paid to them.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>He lodged us in a
spacious house,  
  And plenteous was our fare.  
But now at every frugal meal  
  There's not a scrap to spare.  
Alas! alas that this good man  
  Could not go on as he began!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Four dishes on the
mat the grain  
  For every meal supplied.  
The change is great, from every meal  
  We rise unsatisfied.  
Alas! alas that this good man  
  Could not go on as he began!

</div>
