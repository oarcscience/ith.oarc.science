#### V

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Fa Ko;* metaphorical. While there is a proper and necessary way for
everything, man need not go far to find what it is.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>In hewing an ax
shaft, how must you act?  
  Another ax take, or you'll never succeed.  
In taking a wife, be sure ‘tis a fact,  
  That with no go-between you never can speed.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>In hewing an ax
shaft, hewing a shaft,  
  For a copy you have the ax in your hand.  
In choosing a wife, you follow the craft,  
  And forthwith on the mats the feast vessels stand.

</div>
