#### XI

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Lin Chih Chih;* allusive. Celebrating the goodness of the offspring
and descendants of King Wên.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>As the feet of the
*lin*, which avoid each living thing,  
So our prince's noble sons no harm to men will bring.  
*            They* are the *lin!* 

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>As the front of
the *lin*, never forward thrust in wrath, <span id="page_11">p.
11</span>  
So our prince's noble grandsons of love tread the path.  
*            They* are the *lin!* 

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>As the horn of the
*lin*, flesh-tipped, no wound to give,  
So our prince's noble kindred kindly with all live.  
*            They* are the *lin!* 

</div>
