#### VII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Lang Po;* allusive. The praise of the duke of Chou, more
distinguished through his trials.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Dewlap o’ergrown
and heavy tail  
  Th’ impatient wolf impede or trip.  
But see the duke, humble while tried!  
  Along his red shoes quiet slip.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Tail heavy and
dewlap o’ergrown,  
  Th’ impatient wolf trip or impede.  
But see the duke, humble while tried,  
  His fame unflawed by hasty deed!

</div>
