#### VII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Fang Yu Ch‘iao Ch‘ao;* allusive. A lady laments the alienation of
her lover by means of evil tongues.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>The magpies’ nests
are on the bank;  
On heights the lovely pea grows rank; <span id="page_156">p.
156</span>  
While withered my heart is and blank.  
  Who wiled my love away?

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>The temple path
its fine tiles shows;  
On heights the ribbon plant bright grows;  
While my breast heaves with sorrow's throes.  
  Who led his heart astray?

</div>
