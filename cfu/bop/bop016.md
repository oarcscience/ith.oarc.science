#### II

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Ts‘ai Fan;* narrative. The industry and reverence of a prince's
wife, assisting him in sacrificing.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Around the pools,
the islets o’er,  
  Fast she plucks white southernwood,  
To help the sacrificial store;  
  And for our prince does service good.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Where streams
among the valleys shine,  
  Of southernwoods she plucks the white;  
And brings it to the sacred shrine,  
  To aid our prince in solemn rite.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>In headdress high,
most reverent, she  
  The temple seeks at early dawn.  
The service o’er, the headdress see  
  To her own chamber slow withdrawn.

</div>
