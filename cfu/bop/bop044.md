#### XVI

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Pei Fêng;* metaphorical and narrative. Some one of Wei presses his
friends to leave the country with him at once, in consequence of the
prevailing oppression and misery.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>As when the north
winds keenly blow,.  
And all around fast falls the snow,  
The source of pain and suffering great,  
So now it is in Wei's poor state.  
  Let us join hands and haste away,  
    My friends and lovers all.  
  ’Tis not a time will brook delay;  
    Things for prompt action call.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>As when the north
winds whistle shrill,  
And drifting snows each hollow fill,  
The source of pain and suffering great,  
So now it is in Wei's poor state.  
  Let us join hands, and leave for aye,  
    My friends and lovers all,  
  ’Tis not a time will brook delay;  
    Things for prompt action call.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>We look for red,
and foxes meet;  
For black, and crows our vision greet.  
The creatures, both of omen bad,  
Well suit the state of Wei so sad. <span id="page_45">p. 45</span>  
  Let us join hands and mount our cars,  
    My friends and lovers all.  
  No time remains for wordy jars;  
    Things for prompt action call.

</div>
