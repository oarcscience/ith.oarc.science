#### VIII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Lu Ling;* narrative. The admiration in Ch‘i of hounds and hunters.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>*Ling-ling* go the
hounds; *ling-ling* the hounds go.  
Their master is kindly and good, as we know. <span id="page_112">p.
112</span>

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>With double rings
furnished, on go the hounds;  
In goodness and grace their master abounds.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>Three rings at
their necks, they rush to the chase;  
Their master is famous for power and for grace.

</div>
