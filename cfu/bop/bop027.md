#### XIII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Ho Pi Nung I*; allusive. The marriage of one of the royal
princesses.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Of flowers in
clusters large and gay,  
How bright the sparrow plum's display!  
In reverent harmony on glide  
The chariots of the royal bride.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>These flowers
their clusters large and gay,  
As of the peach or plum display.  
This grandchild of the peaceful king  
Joy to the marquis’ son shall bring.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>As threads of silk
together twine,  
To form the angler's faithful line;  
So may the union close abide  
Of gallant prince and royal bride!

</div>
