This page provides resources at Intangible Textual Heritage which
compare two or more religions. This page also indexes comparative
folklore and mythology, the study of the migration of story themes, and
similar topics. Right now we are still building out this index.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Graves</span> \[<span class="c_d">1860</span>\]   <span class="c_b">What
is the meaning of so many similar hero stories from around the world? An
early study of comparative religion.</span> </span>
