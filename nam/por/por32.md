#### SONG OF THE RAIN CHANT

**NAVAJO**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Far as man can see,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

From the Rain-Mount,  
Rain-Mount far away,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

O’er the corn,  
O’er the corn, tall corn,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

’Mid the lightnings,  
’Mid the lightnings zigzag,  
’Mid the lightnings flashing,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

’Mid the swallows,  
’Mid the swallows blue,  
Chirping glad together,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Through the pollen,  
Through the pollen blest,  
All in pollen hidden,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Far as man can see,  
    Comes the rain,  
    Comes the rain with me.

</div>
