### SPELL SONG

**KWAKIUTL**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

What of olden times,  
    shall I tell you of olden times  
    what of olden times  
    my grandchildren?  
You of olden times,  
    you of olden times,  
    a cloud,  
    lay on the mountains.
