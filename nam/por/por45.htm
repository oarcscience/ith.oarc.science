The Path on the Rainbow, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at Intangible Textual Heritage

p. 154

SPELL SONG

KWAKIUTL

What of olden times,
    shall I tell you of olden times
    what of olden times
    my grandchildren?
You of olden times,
    you of olden times,
    a cloud,
    lay on the mountains.
