### SACRED MIDÉ SONGS

**OJIBWA**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Drifting snow,  
    why do I sing?

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The Sun and Moon,  
Their bodies  
    shine over the world  
    unto me as unto you, my Midé  
    friend.

</div>
