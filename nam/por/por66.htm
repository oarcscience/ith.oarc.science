THE HEART'S FRIEND

SHOSHONE LOVE SONG

Fair is the white star of twilight,
And the sky clearer

p. 189

At the day's end;
But she is fairer, and she is dearer
She, my heart's friend!

Fair is the white star of twilight,
And the moon roving
To the sky's end;
But she is fairer, better worth loving,
She, my heart's friend.
