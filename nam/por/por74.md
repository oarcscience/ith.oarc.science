### BUFFALO DANCE

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

    Strike ye our land  
With curved horns!  
Now with cries  
Bending our bodies,

</div>

<span id="page_201">p. 201</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Breathe fire upon us;  
Now with feet  
Trampling the earth,  
Let your hoofs  
Thunder over us!  
Strike ye our land  
With curved horns!

</div>
