LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL

SMITHSONIAN INSTITUTION,          
BUREAU OF AMERICAN ETHNOLOGY,    
Washington, D. C., November 1, 1941.

Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a manuscript, entitled "Origin Myth of Acoma and Other Records," by Matthew W. Stirling, and to recommend that it be published as a bulletin of the Bureau of American Ethnology.

            Very respectfully yours,

M. W. STIRLING, Chief.

DR. C. G. ABBOT,

Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution.
