##### II

##### THAT MOUNTAIN FAR AWAY

My home over there, my home over there,  
My home over there, now I remember it!  
And when I see that mountain far away  
Why, then I weep. Alas! what can I do?  
What can I do? Alas! what can I do?  
My home over there, now I remember it.
