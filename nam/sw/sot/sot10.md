##### VI

##### LOST LOVE

At Su k’wa k’e there used to bloom a flower—  
That flower, that flower, whene’er I see it now  
Alas, so far away, why then I weep;  
That flower, that flower, whene’er I see it now,  
For yellow, fresh and full-blown once it bloomed.
