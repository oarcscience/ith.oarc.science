##### XLI

##### EAGLE SONG

On Leaf-Long Mountain  
Rabbit Old Woman, Old Man!  
Hereabouts they walk.  
They are my dear ones!  
*Ya’a héya, héya  
E’e ni ya, ni ya hé*.
