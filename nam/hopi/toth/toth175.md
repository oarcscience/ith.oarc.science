## 62.--THE COYOTE AND THE FROG.

Coyote goes to see his friend Frog. He is raided by dogs and jumps down
mesa, but is not killed. After he has drank much water, Frog suggests
that

<span id="page_304">p. 304</span>

they sit down. Coyote asks Frog to dance for him and Frog jumps into
deep water. He comes up with mouth wide open. He is pregnant. He tells
Coyote to draw him out. Coyote grasps him by arms and throws him on
ground. Frog bursts and tadpoles swarm around him. Frog dies. Coyote
starts off home. People see him and try to capture him. Rain and hail
storm comes up and Coyote's hole is filled with water. He tries to find
shelter, but hail stones are so heavy they kill him.
