## 32.--MÁSAUWUU AND THE HANO HUNTERS.

Háno go hunting rabbits in winter towards Sun shrine. They kill many,
and return still hunting. Másauwuu hunts during night and sleeps all
day, Háno follows cotton-tail, which jumps down just where Másauwuu is
sleeping. They follow, making great noise. Másauwuu jumps up quickly and
runs. Runs against rock several times and perforates his head and blood
streams down. He used to have white head, but now has bloody head. He
has much game, which Háno distribute, and proceed home.
