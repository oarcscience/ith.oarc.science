120\. I<span class="small">VIANGERSOOK TRAVELLED ALL AROUND THE</span>
C<span class="small">OAST OF</span> G<span
class="small">REENLAND</span>.—He started for the south, and having
passed Cape Farewell, he came on the eastward to some light-haired
people of European complexion; and lastly he returned through the Sound,
which was formerly open from east to west, near Ilulissat (Jakobshavn).
When approaching his home near Godthaab he lost his brother, who was
buried upon a small island, after them named Uviarniak (one who
travelled all around).
