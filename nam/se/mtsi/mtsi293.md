### 28. THE TURTLE <span id="fr_86"></span>[1](mtsi295.htm#fn_85.md)

A Turtle came out of the Water and began sunning himself on a log. While
he was doing so he looked up and saw that a rainstorm was coming. He
said to himself, "It is going to wet me." So he jumped into the water.
