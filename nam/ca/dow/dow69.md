### THE DEVIL OF SAN RAFAEL

*The Hookooeko of San Rafael say*:

*Yu'-ten me'-chah* the Evil One lives in the hills just north of San
Rafael; he travels about at night and sometimes comes and touches people
when they are asleep, to frighten them.
