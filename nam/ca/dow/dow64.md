### NOISE

*The Tuolumne Mewuk say*:

In the beginning all noise came from water--running water. \[Their word
for shouting is *Wah-kah-lah'-loo*, derived from *Wah-kah'-loo*,
river.\]

*The Northern Mewuk also say*:

All noise came in the beginning from running water; the echo originally
came from rapids or boisterous water.

*Other tribes say*:

Singing came from running water--the first song was sung by the creek.
