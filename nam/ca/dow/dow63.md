### THE EARTHQUAKE

*The Olayome of Putah Creek say*:

Under the earth is a great giant named *He-wow'-wah-tin*.

<span id="page_224">p. 224</span>

<span class="contnote">\[paragraph continues\]</span> When angry he
shakes the earth, causing *Yo'-wan-hew'-wah* the Earthquake.
