### ILHATAINA

In this myth lightning is "dug up," as in the preceding one. Electricity
is one of the earth's children.

The putting on of Gowila's skin by Ilhataina is one of the curious acts
frequent in Indian mythology. In the Aztec worship of Mexico, in
Montezuma's time, the sacrificing priest put on the skin of the victim
as far as the waist.

The wish of Ilhataina to get the old rabbit-skin robe is worthy of
attention.
