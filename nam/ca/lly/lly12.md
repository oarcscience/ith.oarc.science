### CATHEDRAL SPIRES

Next to Cathedral Rocks is one of the most striking rock formations in
the Valley, Cathedral Spires, called by the Indians Pu-see-na Chuck-ah,
or "mouse-proof storehouse." The double pinnacled towers are a real
architectural structure; two monoliths of granite climbing nearly
twenty-two hundred feet into the zenith, and buttressed at their base by
boulders of every conceivable
