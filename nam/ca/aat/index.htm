ACHOMAWI AND ATSUGEWI TALES.

ACHOMAWI MYTHS

by Roland B. Dixon

JAFL Vol. 22, no. 81, pp. 159-77 [1908] and JAFL Vol. 23, no. 85, pp. 283-7 [1909].

These two articles include myths of the Achomawi and Atsugewi, who lived in the north-eastern Sierra Nevada of California.
