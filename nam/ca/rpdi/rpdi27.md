EXPLANATION OF PLATE 24.

Ground Painting, made by Manuel Lachuso, an old man of Santa Ysabel. He
had forgotten the precise location of the milky way, sun, new moon, and
old moon, so they are omitted. He made no mention of toloache mortars.

1.—Atoloi, witch mountain on an island, identified by the informant with
Coronado island.

2.—Nyapukxaua, mountain where people were created.

3.—Wikaiyai, San Bernardino mountain.

4.—Axatu, Santa Catalina island.

5.—Awī, rattlesnake.

6.—Etcekur<span class="sc">l</span>k, wolf.

7.—Xatca, Pleiades.

8.—Namu<span class="sc">l</span>, bear.

9.—Nyimatai, panther.

10.—"Cross star".

11.—Sai<span class="it">r</span>, buzzard star.

12.—Xawitai, grass or blue garter snake.

13.—Xi<span class="sc">l</span>kaīr, red racer snake.

14.—Awiyuk, gopher snake.

15.—Watun, "shooting" constellation.

16.—Amu, mountain-sheep, three stars of Orion.

17.—Spitting hole, diameter about 8 inches.

18.—Horizon, forming the visible limits of the earth.

EXPLANATION OF PLATE 25.

Ground Painting made by Antonio Maces and Jo Waters, old men at Mesa
Grande.

1, 2, 3.—Kalmo and witūrt, toloache mortar and pestle.

4, 5.—Awī, rattlesnake.

6.—Xatatkū<span class="sc">r</span><span class="sc">l</span>, milky way.

7.—Amut kwaptcurpai, world's edge. There is some doubt as to the correct
color for this figure.

8.—Ha<span class="sc">l</span>yaxai, new moon.

9.—Ha<span class="sc">l</span>ya, full moon.

10.—Inyau, sun.

11.—Hatapa, coyote.

12.—Saī<span class="it">r</span>, buzzard star.

13.—Crow.

14.—Ha<span class="sc">l</span>turt, black spider.

15.—Katckurlk, wolf.

16.—Amu, mountain-sheep, Orion.

17.—Hatca, Pleiades.

18.—Awī-nī<span class="sc">l</span>, black snake.

19.—Awl-yuk, gopher snake.

20-23.—Mountains.
