### Origin of the Mink. <span id="fr_15"></span>[1](#fn_15.md)

Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started, and saw the Mink sharpening
(something). "What is that?"--"This will be for taking revenge." "Give
it to me, that I may see it!"--"This is nice. Turn your back to me."
Then he put it on his back. "Go start! You shall be the mink of later
man."
