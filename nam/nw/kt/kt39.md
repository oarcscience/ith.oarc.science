### Origin of the Raccoon. <span id="fr_16"></span>[2](#fn_16)

Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started, and saw Raccoon. He was
finishing what was going to be a spear. He put it over the fire and
marked rings around his spear. "This is pretty! Turn your back to me!"
Then he put it into his back. He pushed it into his back. Then he took
coal and painted with it across his eyes. Then Raccoon jumped away.
