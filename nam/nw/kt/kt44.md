### Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> meets Ô'?mâ~~l~~. <span id="fr_21"></span>[2](#fn_21.md)

Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started and saw Ô'?mâ~~l~~. Then
Ô'?mâ~~l~~ pointed at Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>. Then he pointed
at the forehead of Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>, and there was a hole
in the forehead of Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>. Then he took
revenge, and pointed from a long ways off. Truly, they were supernatural
beings. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> and Ô'?mâ~~l~~ were equal.
