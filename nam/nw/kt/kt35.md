### Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> meets Shaman. <span id="fr_12"></span>[1](#fn_12)

Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He saw Shaman, and he saw his
fire. The fire was in the woods. He saw his ring of red cedar-bark, and
he saw his wood-worms. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> was afraid, and
he went past behind him.
