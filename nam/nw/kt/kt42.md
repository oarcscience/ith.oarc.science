### Transformation of the Man with many Mouths. <span id="fr_19"></span>[2](#fn_19.md)

Then Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He saw people laughing.
Then he became afraid. Behold! he discovered Mouth-Body, who was this
way. Behold! he had many mouths. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>
started. Is that the way you are?" Then he said to him, "That is the way
we are."--"Don't be that way in later generations." Then he said, "Come
to me, that I may set you right, else you might continue this way in
later generations." Then (Mouth-Body) came

<span id="page_207">p. 207</span>

to him, and he stroked with his hand over his body, over his past
mouths, and he had only one mouth. Then (Mouth-Body) spoke. "You have
taken pity on me."
