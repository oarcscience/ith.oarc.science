### 17.

Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is <span
id="fr_23"></span>[2](#fn_19) ya'ta <u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;  
You \| only \| chief \| are of a high family  
Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta
<u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;  
You \| only \| chief \| are of a high family  
Gî L! (aya+) q!otgâ'ñdixan djâ'<u>g</u>adA'ñ<u>g</u>an<u>î</u>.  
For they \| used to be soliciting \| (they) tried to get the woman a
long time.  
Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta
<u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;  
You \| only \| chief \| are of a high family  
Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta
<u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;  
You \| only \| chief \| are of a high family

You alone are fit to be a chief woman,  
You alone are fit to be a chief woman.  
For (you) they begged a long time (to obtain you in marriage).  
You alone are fit to be a chief woman,  
You alone are fit to be a chief woman.
