### 63.

I hî'hîa.  
Gû'sgihao dâ kungiñâ'ñwañ?  
What \| you \| are crying for?  
Xâ'na-qa'~~l~~i gî gua dâ ku'ñgîñañ?  
Skidegate Inlet \| for \| ? \| you \| cry?  
<u>Î</u>'s<u>î</u>ñ sta dAñ kî'iñawasañ.  
Again \| from \| you \| will have news sent around about you.

I hî'hîa.  
What are you crying for?  
Do you cry for Skidegate Inlet?  
Again from there you will have news spread around about you (when your
husband puts up a house-pole).
