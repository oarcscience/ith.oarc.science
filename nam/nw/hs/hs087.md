### 85.

Gû'gus t!ao dAñ sû'kudjiwañ gia'ga t!a'gwa?  
What \| for \| you \| are crying \| things (clothing) \| for?  
WAsk!iên a'ña dAñ giA'nda kudjû'asañ.  
But \| yours \| you \| shall wear \| chief (or "dear").

For what do you cry, chief (or dear)? For clothing?  
You shall wear it, chief (or dear).
