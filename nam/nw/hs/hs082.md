### 80.

Â'gua nâ'nAñ Lga gut kû'~~l~~ga~~l~~guña'-i <u>g</u>An wa<u>g</u>A'n
sL!a'gA  
Here is \| her grand-mother's \| land \| upon \| walking about \| for \|
for it \| use (your) hands  
    kudjâ'wan. <span id="fr_84"></span>[2](#fn_78)  
    dear.

Use your hands, my dear, to walk about upon grandmother's land.
