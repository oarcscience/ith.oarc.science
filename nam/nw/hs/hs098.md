### 96.

Ha'k!un dAñ gudA'ns k!i<u>ê</u>n gu gAm qe'id LAk!ala <sup>?</sup>a dî  
Like that \| you \| thought \| although \| ? \| not \| tree \| shelter
of \| in  
dAñ q!a'odañ-ûdja.  
you \| seated me?

If you thought so (i. e., if you chose to die), why did you not seat me
in the shelter of a tree? <span id="fr_94"></span>[1](#fn_88.md)
