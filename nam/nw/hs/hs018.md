### 16.

Dî L!naxAn <u>g</u>ê'i~~l~~dañ hao a gûdâ'ñ<u>g</u>ani\[hê\].  
I \| like that \| became \| they used to wish.  
WAgaña'ñ ô la gêi~~l~~sgia'ñ hao ô.  
Like that \| she became \| soon.  
Â'haô dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î'</u>lsLa-i <span
id="fr_22"></span>[1](#fn_18) ya'ta dî gadAldjî'gañ  
You \| only \| chief \| only (or fit) \| you \| are (said of a high
family)  
hao ô+.

They used to wish that I should be like that.  
Like it she soon became.  
You are the only ones fit to be chiefs' daughters.
