90.

Qeda'o gu wa qeda'o gu wa, gada'-i li qêga'na.
War men | ? | killed 2 you, |war men | ? | killed dear daughter | I | bore you
Qeda'o gwa wa, qêda'o gwa wa.
War men | ? | killed you, | war men | ? | killed you.

Did warriors kill you, did warriors kill you, dear daughter that I bore?
Did warriors kill you, did warriors kill you?
