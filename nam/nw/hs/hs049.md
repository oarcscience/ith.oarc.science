### 47.

Hî hîyaihîya gwa-i kî'lsLa-i hao ahai'ya aya q!ai'g<u>î</u>ndalAñ  
              Island \| chief \| this \|              was coming  
    ~~l~~ gûdA'ñ hao îhî ahaiya  
    I thought, but  
K<u>î</u>lsLa'-is Lua'-i L!a q<u>ê</u>ng<u>î</u>ndala-i, hî hîyai
hîya.  
Chief's \| canoe, \| however, \| comes greatly.

I thought the island chief <span id="fr_55"></span>[1](#fn_50) was
coming,  
But the chief's canoe comes greatly. <span id="fr_56"></span>[2](#fn_51)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_50"></span>[34:1](hs049.htm#fr_55) The "island chief"
probably refers to Raven or another supernatural being.

<span id="fn_51"></span>[34:2](hs049.htm#fr_56) That is the canoe of
this infant.
