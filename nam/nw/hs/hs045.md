### Stasa'os qê'<u>g</u>awa-i.

### 43.

Wa'<u>L</u>u dî'nAñ gîda' kû<u>x</u>iâ'ñgwansî' <u>L</u>u g<u>î</u>ñxAn
sî'~~l~~<u>g</u>et a'ñ<u>g</u>a  
At that time \| my child \| youth \| goes around as a \| when \| for
nothing \| alone \| my own  
    ~~l~~a q!â'-ugwañ.  
    I sit -around.

When my child becomes a youth, I shall vainly sit around alone (for he
will go to live with his uncle).
