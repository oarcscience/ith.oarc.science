Right here \| for it \| crying \| sitting right here, \| chief?

Are you crying for it, chief?  
Are you crying for it, chief?  
Are you crying for your sisters, that you be seated up higher (on a pile
of blankets)?  
For your cousins, that there be people in front of you as numerous as if
people made sea-gulls cry, being obliged to step on them,--  
For those things are you crying here, chief?
