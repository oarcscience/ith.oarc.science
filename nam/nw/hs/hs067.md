### Qâ'-ia~~l~~ lâ'nas.

(Songs of Qâ-i l'nagâ'-i.)

### 65.

Qâ-i dî'txa+ ~~l~~ qî'ñgwañ qa'odî+ kî'lsLa-i ~~l~~îñ ~~l~~ qê'xa  
Sea-Lion (Town) \| behind \| I \| was looking around \| a while, \|
chief \| is going to be \| I \| found,  
    kA'n<u>x</u>ida-i+.  
    boy just big enough to walk.  
Aiyâ'ña, aiyâ'ña, kî'lsLa-igan.  
Take care, \| take care, \| my own chief!  
Aiyâ'ña qî'ñgêt<u>g</u>añ.  
Take care, \| my own master (or chief)!

While I was looking around behind Sea-Lion Town, I found the future
chief, a boy just big enough to walk.  
Take care, take care, my own chief!  
Take care, my own master!
