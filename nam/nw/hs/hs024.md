### Q!ô'na qê'<u>g</u>awa-i.

### 22.

Hao tcî'nAñ lanâ'<u>g</u>a gua dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a.  
Your grandfather's \| town \| ? \| you \| own.  
<u>G</u>a'<u>g</u>odiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga, <u>G</u>a'<u>g</u>odiya-i
gua s<u>g</u>â'naga.  
Lies \| large \| ? \| is it powerful, Lies \| large \| ? \| is it
powerful.  
Hao tcî'nAñ lanâ'<u>g</u>a gua dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a.  
Your grandfather's \| town \| ? \| you \| own.  
Qî'ngodiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga, Qî'ngodiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga.  
Lies down greatly \| ? \| is it powerful, Lies down greatly \| ? \| is
it powerful.  
Hao tcî'nAñ <u>x</u>a~~l~~ tc!â'anu<u>g</u>a dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a uyâ'te
gâ'djuyañ.  
Your grand-father's \| copper \| fire \| you \| own \| you only are fit
\| to sit \| greatly.

Do you own your grandfather's town ?  
Lying large, has it supernatural power? Lying large, has it supernatural
power'  
Do you own your grandfather's town?  
Lying greatly, has it supernatural power? Lying greatly, has it
supernatural power?  
You only are fit, sitting greatly, to own your grandfather's
copper-fire.
