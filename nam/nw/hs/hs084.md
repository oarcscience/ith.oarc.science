### 82.

A LAga-i yuku'ndjudasi î'ñgut s<u>g</u>â'nas û dalA'ñ
y<u>ê</u>'dada-dîgînî.  
This \| land is a point (Rose Spit) \| on \| supernatural beings \|
those \| you \| left.

Supernatural beings used to leave you on this point of land (i. e., Rose
Spit).
