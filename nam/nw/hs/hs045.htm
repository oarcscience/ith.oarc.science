Stasa'os qê'gawa-i.

43.

Wa'Lu dî'nAñ gîda' kûxiâ'ñgwansî' Lu gîñxAn sî'lget a'ñga
At that time | my child | youth | goes around as a | when | for nothing | alone | my own
    la q!â'-ugwañ.
    I sit -around.

When my child becomes a youth, I shall vainly sit around alone (for he will go to live with his uncle).
