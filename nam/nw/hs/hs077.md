### Lî'<u>ê</u>lAñ qê'awa-i.

### 75.

Êya hâ hiê', etc.  
Qâ'gai<u>g</u>aña nâ'<u>g</u>a L!ao Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s xê'gAndigi
wa'di kuñg<u>î</u>ña'ñ uga'-i.  
Uncles \| houses in \| but \| Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s \| makes a noise
(potlatch) \| for it \| (he or she) is crying.  
Wa'di kû'djiwa ê'ya hâ hîê!, etc.  
For it \| sits greatly.

Êya hâ hiê', etc.  
But he (or she) is crying for the noise Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s (now
reborn) makes in his uncles' houses (at the potlatch).  
For it he sits greatly.
