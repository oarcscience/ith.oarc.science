### Lî'<u>ê</u>lAñ kun l'nagâ'-i.

### 77.

Â'gua nâ'nAñ Lga gut dî'nañ ku-i-<u>ê</u>'ndalane.  
It was \| his grand-mother's \| land \| upon \| my child \| walks (a
proud word).  
Wa'<u>g</u>an st!a'<u>g</u>a kudja'oanê.  
For it \| his foot \| is dear.  
GAm ~~l~~a ku'ñg<u>î</u>ñañ Añ.  
Not \| you \| cry (excl.)!

My child walks proudly upon his grandmother's land.  
His dear foot is for that (i. e., to walk on it).  
Do not cry!
