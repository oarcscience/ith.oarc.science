### 28. <span id="fr_38"></span>[1](#fn_34)

Giä'<u>L</u>û Xâ'na qâ'~~l~~i, giä'<u>l</u>û Xâ'na qâ'~~l~~i,

Skidegate Inlet, Skidegate Inlet.  
When you burst with swelling,
