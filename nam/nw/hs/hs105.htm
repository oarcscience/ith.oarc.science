Battle-Song of Women.

103.

[Song by women during the absence of their husbands with a war-party.]

Sk!A'ga-o t!a'êt qô'nagAñ.
Sk!A'ga, | this one | killed | many people.

Sk!A'gao killed (and enslaved) many people.
