### 59.

L<u>î</u>'ñgua xAldâ'ñgAñ q!ô'~~l~~<u>g</u>et \[ahîyîa\]  
Why is it \| your slave \| close by,  
Hao dâ <u>î</u>lgîyâ'gAñAñ dâ sûkû'djiwañ?  
     You \| want something you cannot get, \| you \| say?  
DAñ qô'naga kûdjû'<u>g</u>eda qâñ ~~l~~ qê'<u>g</u>An.  
You \| are too foolish, \| my uncle I \| bore.

Why do you cry for something that you cannot get,  
Sitting close by your slave? <span id="fr_66"></span>[2](#fn_61)  
You are very foolish, uncle I bore.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_61"></span>[41:2](hs061.htm#fr_66) The mother calls herself
the child's "slave" in jest. An "uncle" is reborn in the child.
