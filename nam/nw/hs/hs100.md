### T!ô'~~l~~k!a g<u>î</u>tAnâ'-i.

### 98. <span id="fr_95"></span>[2](#fn_89.md)

LA'gas dAñ qä'ñ kû'gits <u>L</u>û agA'ñ ~~l~~ kû stAñ
g<u>î</u>'ndagiâ'ñaxAñ.  
Land \| you \| see, \| beloved one \| when \| yourself, \| dear (or
chief), \| two \| if you made in canoe.

If you had seen land, beloved, you would have saved yourself,
dear. <span id="fr_96"></span>[3](#fn_90.md)
