74.

Dja'+djâts L!aha'o lâ, dja'+djâts L!aha'o lâ.
Women are better (than men), women are better (than men).
Dja'+djâts L!a kîñgê'diasLa.
Women have more property.

My chiefs men of one's own family) | chiefs | you | are | where?

Women are better (than men), women are better (than men).
Women have more property.
Chiefs of my family, where are you?
