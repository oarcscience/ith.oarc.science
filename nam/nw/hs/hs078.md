### 76.

Gîsta L!ao da<u>g</u>a'sado sî'liya dâ skA'ndAñ kudjû'gi\[gê+\]  
Who \| but \| will own it \| after it \| you \| are crying \| are
sitting  
dô'nê a~~l~~djî'wai? <span id="fr_82"></span>[2](#fn_76)  
younger brother \| are sitting?

But do you sit crying over who will afterwards own it, younger brother
of good family?
