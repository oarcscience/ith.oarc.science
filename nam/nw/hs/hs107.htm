Song used by the ?ao sL!an l'nagâ'-i in making Peace.

105.

Xâ'La gwai'yê gô'lgal q!ê'aosgiên.
Haidas' | island | green | has become.

The island of the Haida has become green (i. e. the hats have appeared as when spring comes and the foliage turns green.)
