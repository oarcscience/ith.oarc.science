### 49.

Dâ gut gua <u>g</u>agwai'ya <u>g</u>agwai'ya; qa'-idjis qâs gû'stA  
You \| your mind made up \| ? \| to fall into (the cradle) \| to fall
into (the cradle) \| spruce \| top \| from  
<u>g</u>agwai'ya sq!aos qâs gû'stA <u>g</u>agwai'ya.  
to fall in; \| salmon-berry bush \| top \| from \| to fall in.

Did you make up your mind to fall (into the cradle), to fall in from the
top of a spruce-tree, to fall in from the top of a salmon-berry bush?
