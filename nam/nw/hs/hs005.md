### 3.

Ha'lA+ dî'+<u>g</u>a+ sk<u>î</u>'nxa<u>Lg</u>a'<u>g</u>o.  
Come \| for me \| all wake up.  
A~~l~~ qâ'+ñ<u>g</u>adigwâñ<u>g</u>a,  
I \| dreamed about,  
Ha ha gasî'n xega'n~~l~~îñ. <span id="fr_2"></span>[2](#fn_2)  
(Laughing) \| they are \| going to make a noise about him.

<span id="page_7">p. 7</span>

Come, wake up, and listen to me!  
I dreamed about it.  
Ha, ha! oh, what a noise they are going to make over him (at the
potlatch)!
