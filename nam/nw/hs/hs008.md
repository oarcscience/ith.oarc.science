### 6.

A+ya+ña'+ ayâ'ñê â'+yañô.  
Be careful, \| be careful, \| be careful.  
La hao îldja'oga+n.  
He \| is a nobleman.

<span id="page_9">p. 9</span>

I'+lgiañ wAga'ñ(añ) kû'+skî+twas sê+ Lû tcî'nañ qôniga'-i  
(Face) changed \| like it \| will be wherever your place is \| his
grand-father \| powerful  
    gî a la qea~~l~~dî+ga.  
    for \| he looks expectantly.  
Ayâ'ñô a o <u>î</u>ldja'oga+n.  
Be careful, this is a nobleman.

Be careful (of the child), be careful, be careful.  
This is going to be a great man.  
His face will be changed wherever he may be, when he  
looks for the coming of his powerful grandfather <span
id="fr_6"></span>[1](#fn_6) (and sees him.md).  
Be careful, this is going to be a great man.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_6"></span>[9:1](hs008.htm#fr_6.md) Skedans and his people
called Djê'basa, the Tsimshian chief at Kitkatla, "grand father," and
vice versa. After he has become a man, the child's face will look joyous
when he sees his Tsimshian friends approach.
