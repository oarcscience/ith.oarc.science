### 29.

DalA'ñ s<u>g</u>u'nxAn gua+ â'hao q<u>î</u>ndia'haowus  
You \| only \| ? \| here \| look about  
Tcî'nañ ~~l~~k!ia'n<u>g</u>a A'ñ<u>g</u>a dalA'n hao ha-iludâ'lL!xa
ûya'te  
Grandfather's (i.e., Raven peoples')\| timbers \| yours \| you \| got
all out of woods \| the only fit to do  
    gadA'ldîgwa'ñ.  
highly moving around.  
Uyatê' gâ'dAldiañ, uyatê' gâ'dAldiañ, kî'lsLa-i ya'ta  
Only \| fit \| highly moving around, \| only \| fit \| highly moving
around, \| chief \| fit to be  
    gâ'dAldiañ.  
    highly moving around.

<span id="page_22">p. 22</span>

Are you the only ones who sit looking about?  
You are the only ones fit to get all your grandfather's timbers <span
id="fr_40"></span>[1](#fn_36.md) out of the woods, chiefs highly moving
around.  
Highly moving around, highly moving around, chiefs highly moving around.
