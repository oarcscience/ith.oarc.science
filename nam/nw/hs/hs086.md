### 84.

Y<u>ê</u>n dAñ <u>î</u>'skudals <u>L</u>û gAm ~~l~~ dî'gu ku'ñgîñañAñ
hAñ.  
Truly \| you are chief (or "dear") if not you on my (imp.) (knees)
cry!  
Dî gwa ga lgaiqendigwA'ñus.  
I \| am not rich.

If you are truly a chief (reborn), do not cry upon my knees!  
I am not rich. <span id="fr_85"></span>[1](#fn_79)
