96.

Ha'k!un dAñ gudA'ns k!iên gu gAm qe'id LAk!ala ?a dî
Like that | you | thought | although | ? | not | tree | shelter of | in
dAñ q!a'odañ-ûdja.
you | seated me?

If you thought so (i. e., if you chose to die), why did you not seat me in the shelter of a tree? 1

_______________

Footnotes

59:1 The last part of this is metaphorical. It means, "Why did you die so suddenly?"
