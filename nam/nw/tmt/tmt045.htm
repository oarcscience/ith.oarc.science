One time Raven came to a place called Cold-town and said to the boys there, "Let us go shooting with bow and arrows." He took down his own canoe and they started out, but presently the canoe upset and the boys were all drowned. Then he said to them, "You will stay here." They are the îkAgâ'xe, sea birds whose voices can be heard at a long distance.
