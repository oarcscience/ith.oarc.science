# GLOSSARY

*Awéondágon*.--Name of a weed.

*Dádahwât*.--White beaver.

*Dadyoe<sup>n</sup>dzadáses*.--He who travels around the world (wolf).

*Dagwahgweoses*.--Long eyebrows.

*Dagwanoe<sup>n</sup>yent gowa*.--Whirlwind, cyclone.

*Daqsídes*.--Long foot (rabbit).

*De<sup>n</sup>de<sup>n</sup>dáne*.--Caterpillar.

*Déoneyont*.--Red hot.

*Dewaqso<sup>n</sup>thwûs*.--Flea.

*Dí´di*.--Blue jay.

*Digiá’go<sup>n</sup> gowa*.--Buffalo.

*Dí´sdes*.--Woodpecker.

*Djeonyaik*.--Robin.

*Djiäye<sup>n</sup>*.--Spider.

*Djiho<sup>n</sup>sūqgwen*.--Ants.

*Djisdáa*.--Grasshopper.

*Djisgan*.--Spirit, ghost.

*Djissáa*.--Fire

*Djoéaga*.--Coon.

*Djonkdjo<sup>n</sup>kwe<sup>n</sup>*.--Chickadee.

*Dóe<sup>n</sup>dzowes*.--Split the earth (earthquake).

*Dono<sup>n</sup>gáes*.--Long horns (snake).

*Dónyakdane*.--He who travels everywhere (inchworm).

*Dónyonda*.--Bald eagle.

*Doseno’daia*.--Flying squirrel.

*Dotgehondagane*.--Half red-headed (woodpecker).

*Dowisdowe*.--Tip-up (a bird).

*Doyadastethe*.--Bright body.

Dwâau<sup>n</sup>hdanege<sup>n</sup>.--Two feathers (rabbit).

*Dzainos gowa*.--Blue lizard, ancient of lizards.

*Dzodjógis*.--Blackbirds.

*Dzóega*.--Raccoon.

*Dzogéon*.--Little people (fairies).

*Dzohó’qwais*.--Chipmunk

*Dzotháwendo<sup>n</sup>*.--Humming bird.

*Gadjiqsa*.--Husk false face.

*Gai<sup>n</sup>so<sup>n</sup>he’*.--Daddy long legs.

*Gaísgen se*.--Ground-bird.

<span id="page_514">p. 514</span>

Ga<sup>n</sup>dewitha.--Morning star.

*Gane<sup>n</sup>itha*.--Corn beetle.

*Ganogeshegea*.--Sparrow.

*Gányage gowa*.--Diver (duck).

*Ganyâqde<sup>n</sup> hanówa*.--Mud-turtle.

*Ganyo gowa*.--Great game (white deer).

*Ganyuqdjidji*.--Yellowbird.

*Gáqga’*.--Raven.

*Gásyondetha*.--Lightning lion (meteor).

*Géha*.--Wind.

*Gendágahâdé<sup>n</sup>yatha*.--June-bug.

*Géno<sup>n</sup>skwa*.--Stone coat (ice and great cold).

*Gwíyee*.--Phebe bird.

*Hadäéonis*.--Net maker.

*Hadentheni*.--Speaker.

*Hadia’des*.--B lack-snake.

*Hadiqsado<sup>n</sup> geno<sup>n</sup>skwa ganyudai*.--Grave of frosts,
or the so-called stone coats.

*Hadjisgwâs*.--Mush eater.

*Hadjoqdja*.--Skin man.

*Hdjendonis*.--Woodmaker (a tree worm).

*Haienthwûs*.--Planter.

*Hane<sup>n</sup>yowa’ne*.--Big bones (mud turtle). A second name for
Mud-turtle, see *Ganyâqde<sup>n</sup> hanówa*.

*Hanigonge<sup>n</sup>datha*.--Definer.

*Hánisheono<sup>n</sup>*.--Muck-worm.

*Hanówa*.--Turtle.

*Haqgeeah*.--Ragged or shabby man.

*Hatdedases*.--Whirlwind maker.

*Hathóndes*.--The listener (mink).

*Háweniyo*.--Great Spirit.

*He<sup>n</sup>es*.--Panther.

*Heusdeóon*.--Rocks go to the water.

*Híno’*.--Thunder (personified).

*Híno’hoháwa<sup>n</sup>k*.--Thunder's son.

*Hodio<sup>n</sup>sko<sup>n</sup>*.--Trickster.

*Hóngâk*.--Wild (Canada) goose.

*Hoqua*.--The cry of frogs.

*Hótho*.--Cold -weather, winter.

*I’o<sup>n</sup>we*.--Wild duck.

*Ne vonoes*.--That is what we like.

*Nohnohsot*.--Heron.

*Nóndza’qgwe*.--Woodcock.

*No<sup>n</sup>gwatgwa*.--Fox.

*Nosgwais*.--Toad.

<span id="page_515">p. 515</span>

*Nyagwaihe gowa*.--Ancient of bears.

*Odjíe’da*.--Crawfish.

*Odzi’néowa*.--Wasp.

*Oge<sup>n</sup>hwan*.--Gnat.

*Okteondo<sup>n</sup>*.--Roots, rooted ones.

*Oneqsas*.--Mushroom eater (a bird).

*Oneo*.--Corn.

*Oñgwe*.--Man, Indian.

*Ongweias*.--Man eater.

*O<sup>n</sup>hdagwija*.--Good ear.

*Onóqgo<sup>n</sup>t gowa*.--Bumble bee.

*O’nowéhda*.--Angleworm.

*O<sup>n</sup>we<sup>n</sup>aunt*.--Blue snake.

*Onwi*.--Winged snake.

*O’ówa*.--Horned owl.

*Oshada*.--Dusty vapor.

*Óshonyúqda*.--Corn-worm

*Otgoé*.--Wampum.

*Otgon*.--Poison.

*Otgóndahe<sup>n</sup>*.--Red bellied (snake).

*Othägwe<sup>n</sup>da*.--Flint.

*Othägwe<sup>n</sup>donis*.--Flintmaker.

*Othaioni hoyáda*.--Wolf-marked.

*Othwénsawénhde*.--Small liver beside the large one.

*Otsoon*.--Turkey.

*Owéeyego<sup>n</sup>hdji*.--Old woman swan.

*Plétho*.--Thunder (the sound), a Delaware word.

*Pléthoak*.--Thunders, a Delaware word.

*Popkpéknos*.--Quail, a Delaware word.

*Poyeshao<sup>n</sup>*.--Orphan, a Delaware word.

*Sadzawíski*.--Thousand legs.

*Sehdo<sup>n</sup>hgwade*.--Wood-tick.

*Sgano<sup>n</sup>hses gowa*.--One of the Thunder family.

*Shagodiäqdane*.--The woman in the South.

*Shagodyoweg gowa*.--Great One who protects us; false face, god of the
air.

*Shagonoges*.--He torments them.

*Shagowenotha*.--The punisher.

*Shagóyádogédas*.--He drives them away.

*Shagoyagentha*.--He hides them.

*Sigweont*.--Rattlesnake.

*Skada’géa*.--In the mist (a bird).

*Skagedi*.--Half (of anything).

*Swe<sup>n</sup>gedaigea*.--Hawk, hen hawk.

*Téqdoo<sup>n</sup>huishĕ*.--Woodchuck leggings, the deceiver.

*Thago<sup>n</sup>hsówes*.--He splits his face.

*Tondayent*.--White rabbit.

*Tsodiqgwado<sup>n</sup>*.--Snake.

*Wadyo<sup>n</sup>yo<sup>n</sup>dyes*.--Wild duck people.

*Wáyo<sup>n</sup>*.--Rabbit.

*Wia’á*.--Call of phebe bird.

*Yenogeau<sup>n</sup>*.--Ear enter (barkworm).

Yeo<sup>n</sup>ogaa.--Shingled hair.

*Yeqsínye*.--Spinner.

*Yontq<sup>n</sup>wisas*.--Name of a song.
