SECTION 57

"'Now another message to tell your people.

"'You may ask three questions concerning three privileges when you go among your relatives at the ceremony of Nîsko'wûknî 1 and ask what one is fitted for them.

"'Who among you likes best to call upon the afflicted? Who among you loves to commune alone in the forests? Who among you is most anxious concerning religious conditions?''

So they asked him. Eniaiehuk.

Footnotes

53:1 February, the moon of the midwinter, the time of thanksgiving.
