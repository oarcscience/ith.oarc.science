### SECTION 30

"'Now another message to tell your people.

Four words the Creator has given for bringing happiness. They

<span id="page_41">p. 41</span>

are amusements devised in the heaven world, the Osto'wägo'wa, <span
id="fr_38"></span>[1](#fn_38.md) Gone:'owo<sup>n</sup>?,
Ado<sup>n</sup>'wê<sup>n</sup> and Ganäwê<sup>n</sup>'gowa.'"

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
