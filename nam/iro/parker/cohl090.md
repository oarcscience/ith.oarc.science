### SECTION 79

"Now another message.

"This will happen.

"You will sing three times and the third time you sing you will step
into oyâ?dedion'diade?, the other world. <span
id="fr_75"></span>[1](#fn_75.md) That you go there will be the earnest wish
of all who have heard your message."

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_75"></span>[60:1](cohl090.htm#fr_75.md) It was customary for
the friends and relatives to address the body of the dead and give
expression to one's desires, etc. The soul when it reached the
heaven-world would then tell the Great Ruler who would attend to the
wishes expressed.
