### SECTION 116

"Now that person spoke and said, 'I brought them with me to testify to
the truth that those of the lower world when they pass away come
hither.'

<span id="page_76">p. 76</span>

"Then spoke the daughter of Gaiän'twakâ, 'I send a message. It is this:
It grieves me to know that my brothers on the earth disagree with my
father. Bid them cease their disagreement.' So she said."

Eniaiehuk.
