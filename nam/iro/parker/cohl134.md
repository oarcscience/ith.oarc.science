### SECTION 123

"Now there will be another and his name will be the New Voice,
Hawênose?'.

"So now it was that Ganio\`dai'io\` was bidden the third time to sing
his song and this the messengers said would be the last.

"Now then he said, 'There is nothing to incumber me from fulfilling my
call."'

So said our head man. Eniaiehuk.
