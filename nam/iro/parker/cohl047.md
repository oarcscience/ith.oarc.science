### SECTION 36

"'Now another message to tell your people.

"'This will happen.

"'We have told you to watch.

<span id="page_43">p. 43</span>

"'The Honon'diont will go out in fours for game for the feasts. You may
think that they are fulfilling their duty to Gai'wiio\`.

"'The animals that fall must be thirty.

"'But this will happen when Gai'wiio\` is new. The Honon'diont will kill
twenty-nine and the twenty-ninth will be a cub bear. So there will not
be thirty.

"'So this will be done when Gai'wiio\` is new. It will be done at
Adekwe'o<sup>n</sup>ge, the Green Corn thanksgiving ceremony."'

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
