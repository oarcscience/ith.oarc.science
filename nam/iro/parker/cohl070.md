### SECTION 59

"'Now another message.

"'It is this: We, the messengers of our Creator, see strong drink used
during the season when corn is planted. Now let those

<span id="page_54">p. 54</span>

who use this evil drink know that it consumes the elements of life They
must repent.'"

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
