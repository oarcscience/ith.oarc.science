### SECTION 100

"Now they saw and then everyone knew that the house was very long and
extended far out of the eye's reach. Now this is true. When a certain
woman within saw the four and him drawing near she stretched out her
arms and cried for help. Then answered the four, 'It is beyond our power
to alter your condition now. Our work was with you on earth. Too late.'"

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
