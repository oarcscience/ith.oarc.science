SECTION 77

"Now another message.

"Now we think that when the end comes the earth will be destroyed by fire and not one upon it will escape for all the earth will be enveloped in flames and all those who refuse to believe in Gai'wiio` will be in it." 1

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.

Footnotes

59:1 Because Handsome Lake did not die in this manner some of his half believing followers at Onondaga repudiated his teaching.
