### SECTION 57

"'Now another message to tell your people.

"'You may ask three questions concerning three privileges when you go
among your relatives at the ceremony of Nîsko'wûknî <span
id="fr_64"></span>[1](#fn_64.md) and ask what one is fitted for them.

"'Who among you likes best to call upon the afflicted? Who among you
loves to commune alone in the forests? Who among you is most anxious
concerning religious conditions?''

So they asked him. Eniaiehuk.
