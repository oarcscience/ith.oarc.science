### SECTION 93

"So then they proceeded on their journey but had not gone far when they
stopped.

Then the messengers said, 'Watch,' and pointed to a certain spot toward
the setting sun.

"So he watched and saw a large object revolving. It was white and moving
slowly.

"Then said the four messengers, 'What did you see?'

"He answered, 'I saw a large object revolving. It was white and moving
slowly.'

"Then said the messengers, 'It is true. The thing is that which
regulates the air over the earth. It is that which we call the Odä'eo
(the veil over all). It is said that it would bring great calamity
should it revolve too fast. Should it turn faster it would injure
mankind. Now we are the regulators and watchers of the veil over all.'"

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
