### HYMN XVIII

#### A prayer for security and peace on all sides

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901801">1</span></span>Let those
who vex me from the eastern region, sinners, praise  
  Agni followed by the Vasus.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901802">2</span></span>Let those
who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Vāyu  
   compassed by the Air's mid region.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901803">3</span></span>Let those
who vex me from the southern quarter, sinners, sing  
   praise to Soma with the Rudras.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901804">4</span></span>Let those
who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Varuna  
   connected with Ādityas.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901805">5</span></span>Let those
who vex me from the western quarter, sinners, praise  
  Sūrya linked with Earth and Heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901806">6</span></span>Let those
who vex me from this quarter, sinners give praise to  
   plants' associates the Waters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901807">7</span></span>Let those
who from the north side vex me, sinners, praise  
  Visvakarman with the Seven Rishis.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901808">8</span></span>Let those
who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Indra  
   with the Marut host about him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901809">9</span></span>Let those
who vex me from the nadir, sinners, extol Prajāpati  
   of genial power.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901810">10</span></span>Let those
who vex me from the zenith, sinners, extol Brihaspati  
   with the Visve Devas.
