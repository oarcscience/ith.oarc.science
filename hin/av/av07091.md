### HYMN XCI

#### A Prayer for protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709101">1</span></span>May Indra
with his help, Lord of all treasures, be unto us a  
   careful protector.  
  Drive off our foes and give us peace and safety. May we be lords  
   of goodly store of heroes.
