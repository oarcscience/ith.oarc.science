### HYMN LIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005301">1</span></span>Who knows
what vital power he wins, drinking beside the  
   flowing juice?  
  This is the fair-cheeked God who, joying in the draught, breaks  
   down the castles in his strength.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005302">2</span></span>As a wild
elephant rushes on, this way and that way, mad with  
   heat.  
  None may restrain thee; yet come hither to the draught: thou  
   movest mighty in thy power.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005303">3</span></span>When he, the
mighty, ne'er o'erthrown, stedfast, made ready for  
   the fight.  
  When Indra, Bounteous Lord, lists to his praiser's call, he will  
   not stand aloof, but come.
