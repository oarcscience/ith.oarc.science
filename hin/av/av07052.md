### HYMN LII

#### A prayer for peace and concord

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0705201">1</span></span>Give us
agreement with our own, with strangers give us unity:  
  Do ye, O Asvins, in this place join us in sympathy and love.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0705202">2</span></span>May we agree
in mind, agree in purpose: let us not fight against  
   the heavenly spirit.  
  Around us rise no din of frequent slaughter, nor Indra's arrow  
   fly, for day is present!
