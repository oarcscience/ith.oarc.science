### HYMN LXXXIII

#### A prayer for deliverance from sin and other evils

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708301">1</span></span>Stablished
amid the waters is, King Varuna, thy golden home.  
  Thence let the Sovran who maintains the statutes loose all bind-  
   ing cords.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708302">2</span></span>Hence free
thou us, King Varuna, from each successive bond  
   and tie.  
  As we have cried, O Varuna! have said, The Waters, they are  
   kine, thence set us free, O Varuna.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708303">3</span></span>Loosen the
bonds, O Varuna, that hold us, loosen the bond.  
   above, between, and under.  
  So before Aditi may we be sinless under thy favouring auspices,  
  Āditya!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708304">4</span></span>Varuna, free
us from all snares that bind us, Varuna's bonds, the  
   upper and the lower.  
  Drive from us evil dream, drive off misfortune: then let us pass  
   into the world of virtue.
