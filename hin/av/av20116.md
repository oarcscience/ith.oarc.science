### HYMN CXVI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011601">1</span></span>Never may we
be cast aside and strangers, as it were to thee.  
  We, Thunder-wielding Indra, count ourselves as trees rejected  
   and unfit to burn.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011602">2</span></span>O Vritra
slayer, we were thought slow and unready for the fray:  
  Yet once in thy great bounty may we have delight, O Hero,  
   after praising thee.
