### HYMN XLII

#### A charm to reconcile estranged friends

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604201">1</span></span>I loose the
anger from thy heart as 'twere the bowstring from a  
   bow,  
  That we, one-minded now, may walk together as familiar  
   friends.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604202">2</span></span>Together let
us walk as friends: thy wrathful feeling I remove.  
  Beneath a heavy stone we cast thy wrath away and bury it.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604203">3</span></span>I trample on
thine anger thus, I tread it down with heel and toe:  
  So dost thou yield thee to my will, to speak no more rebelliously.
