### HYMN CI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010101">1</span></span>Agni we
choose, the messenger, the herald, master of all wealth,.  
  Well skilled in this our sacrifice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010102">2</span></span>With calls
they ever invocate Agni, Agni, Lord of the House,  
  Oblation-bearer, much-beloved.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010103">3</span></span>Bring the
Gods hither, Agni, born for him who strews the  
   sacred grass.  
  Thou art our herald, meet for praise.
