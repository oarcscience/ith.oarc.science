### HYMN XX

#### A prayer for prosperity and happiness

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702001">1</span></span>Anumati
approve to-day our sacrifice among the Gods!  
  May Agni bear mine offerings away for me the worshipper.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702002">2</span></span>Do thou,
Anumati! approve, and grant us health and happiness.  
  Accept the offered sacrifice, and, Goddess, give us progeny. <span
id="page_a279">p. a279</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702003">3</span></span>May he
approving in return accord us wealth inexhaustible with  
   store of children.  
  Never may we be subject to his anger, but rest in his benevo-  
   lence and mercy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702004">4</span></span>Thy name is
easy to invoke, good leader! approved, Anumati  
   and rich in bounty.  
  Source of all bonds! fill up therewith our worship, and, Blest  
  One! grant us wealth with goodly heroes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702005">5</span></span>Anumati hath
come to this our worship well-formed to give  
   good lands and valiant heroes:  
  For her kind care hath blessed us. God-protected, may she assist  
   the sacrifice we offer.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702006">6</span></span>Anumati
became this All, whatever standeth or walketh, every-  
   thing that moveth.  
  May we enjoy thy gracious love, O Goddess. Regard us, O Anu-  
   mati, with favour.
