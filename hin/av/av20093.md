### HYMN XCIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009301">1</span></span>May our
hymns give thee great delight. Display thy bounty,  
  Thunderer.  
  Drive off the enemies of prayer.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009302">2</span></span>Crush with
thy foot the niggard churls who bring no gifts.  
  Might art thou:  
  There is not one to equal thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009303">3</span></span>Thou art the
Lord of Soma pressed, Soma unpressed is also  
   thine,  
  Thou art the Sovran of the folk.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009304">4</span></span>Swaying
about the active Ones came nigh to Indra at his birth,  
  And shared his great heroic might.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009305">5</span></span>Based upon
strength and victory and power, O Indra is thy  
   birth.  
  Thou, Mighty One, art strong indeed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009306">6</span></span>Thou art the
Vritra-slayer, thou, Indra, hast spread the  
   firmament:  
  Thou hast with might upheld the heavens.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009307">7</span></span>Thou, Indra,
bearest in thine arms the lightning that accords  
   with thee,  
  Whetting thy thunderbolt with might.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009308">8</span></span>Thou, Indra,
art pre-eminent over all creatures in thy strength:  
  Thou hast pervaded every place.
