### HYMN LXI

#### A prayer for long life prosperity and final happiness in heaven

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906101">1</span></span>May my self
remain in my body: may I enjoy the full time of  
   life,  
  Rest thee pleasantly: pour forth abundance, purifying thyself in  
  Svarga.
