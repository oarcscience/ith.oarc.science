### HYMN CXI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011101">1</span></span>If, Indra,
thou drink Soma by Vishnu's or Trita Āptya's side,  
  Or with the Maruts take delight in flowing drops;  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011102">2</span></span>Or, Sakra,
if thou gladden thee afar or in the sea of air,  
  Rejoice thee in this juice of ours, in flowing drops.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011103">3</span></span>Or, Lord of
Heroes, if thou aid the worshipper who sheds the  
   juice,  
  Or him whose laud delights thee, and his flowing drops.
