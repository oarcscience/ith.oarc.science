### HYMN CXXXII

#### The same

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613201">1</span></span>The Philter,
burning with the pangs of yearning love, which  
  Gods have poured within the bosom of the floods,  
  That spell for thee I heat by Varuna's decree.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613202">2</span></span>The charm
which, burning with the pangs of love, the General  
  Gods have poured within the bosom of the floods,  
  That spell for thee I heat by Varuna's decree.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613203">3</span></span>The Philter,
burning with the pangs of longing, which Indrāni  
   hath effused within the waters' depth,  
  That spell for thee I heat by Varuna's decree.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613204">4</span></span>The charm,
aglow with longing, which Indra and Agni have  
   effused within the bosom of the floods,  
  That spell for thee I heat by Varuna's decree.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613205">5</span></span>The charm
aglow with longing which Mitra and Varuna have  
   poured within the bosom of the floods,  
  That spell for thee I heat by Varuna's decree.
