### HYMN LXVII

#### A priest's prayer to the Agnayo Dhishnyāh

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706701">1</span></span>May sense
return to me again, and spirit, return my Sacred  
  Power and my possessions!  
  Again let fires, aflame on lesser altars, each duly stationed, here  
   succeed and prosper.
