### HYMN LXXVIII

#### A charm for a prince's prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707801">1</span></span>I free thee
from the cord, I loose the bond, I loose the fastening.  
  Even here, perpetual, Agni, wax thou strong. <span id="page_a305">p.
a305</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707802">2</span></span>I with
celestial prayer appoint thee, Agni, maintainer of this  
   man in princely powers.  
  Here brightly shine for us with wealth: declare thou to Gods  
   this favoured giver of oblations.
