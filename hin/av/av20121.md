### HYMN CXXI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012101">1</span></span>Over the
three great distances, past the Five Peoples go thy way,  
  O Indra, noticing our voice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012102">2</span></span>Send forth
thy ray like Sūrya: let my songs attract thee hither-  
   ward.  
  Like waters gathering to the vale.
