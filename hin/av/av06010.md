### HYMN X

#### A thanksgiving for life, hearing, and sight

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601001">1</span></span>All hail for
hearing to the Earth, to Trees, to Agni, sovran  
  Lord!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601002">2</span></span>All hail for
breath to Air, for power to life to Vāyu, sovran  
  Lord!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601003">3</span></span>All hail for
vision to the Stars, to Heaven, to Sūrya, sovran  
  Lord!
