### HYMN CXI

#### A charm for insanity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611101">1</span></span>Unbind and
loose for me this man, O Agni, who bound and  
   well restrained is chattering folly. <span id="page_a252">p.
a252</span>  
  Afterward he will offer thee thy portion when he hath been  
   delivered from his madness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611102">2</span></span>Let Agni
gently soothe thy mind when fierce excitement troubles  
   it.  
  Well-skilled I make a medicine that thou no larger mayst be  
   mad.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611103">3</span></span>Insane
through sin against the Gods, or maddened by a demon's  
   power—  
  Well-skilled I make a medicine to free thee from insanity.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611104">4</span></span>May the
Apsarases release, Indra and Bhaga let thee go.  
  May all the Gods deliver thee that thou no longer mayst be  
   mad.
