### HYMN XVI

#### A prayer for prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701601">1</span></span>Increase
this man Brihaspati! Illume him, O Savitar, for high  
   and happy fortune.  
  Sharpen him thoroughly though already sharpened: with glad  
   acclaim let all the Gods receive him.
