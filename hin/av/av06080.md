### HYMN LXXX

#### A prayer for help and protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608001">1</span></span>He flieth in
the firmament observing all the things that be:  
  We with this offering will adore the greatness of the Heavenly  
  Hound. <span id="page_a237">p. a237</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608002">2</span></span>The three,
the Kālakānjas, set aloft in heaven as they were  
  Gods  
  All these I call to be our help and keep this man secure from  
   harm.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608003">3</span></span>In waters is
thy birth, in heaven thy station, thy majesty on  
   earth and in the ocean.  
  We with this offering will adore the greatness of the Heavenly  
  Hound.
