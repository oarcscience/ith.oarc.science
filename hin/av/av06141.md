### HYMN CXLI

#### A blessing on cattle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614101">1</span></span>Vayu
collected these: to find their sustenance be Tvashtar's  
   care:  
  May Indra bless and comfort them, and Rudra look that they  
   increase.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614102">2</span></span>Take thou
the iron axe and make a pair by marks upon their  
   ears.  
  This sign the Asvins have impressed: let these increase and  
   multiply.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614103">3</span></span>Even as Gods
and Asuras, even as mortal men have done,  
  Do ye, that these may multiply in thousands, Asvins! make the  
   mark.
