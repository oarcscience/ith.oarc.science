### HYMN CX

#### A prayer for success in battle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711001">1</span></span>Resistless,
Agni, Indra, smite his foemen for the worshipper,  
  For best foe-slayers are ye both.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711002">2</span></span>Agni I call,
and Indra, foe-destroyers, swift moving, heroes,  
  Gods who wield the thunder,  
  Through whom they won the light in the beginning, these who  
   have made all worlds their habitation.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711003">3</span></span>The God
Brihaspati hath won thy friendly favour with the cup.  
  With hymns, O Indra, enter us for the juice-pouring worshipper.
