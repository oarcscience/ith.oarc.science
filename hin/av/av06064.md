### HYMN LXIV

#### To promote unanimity in an assembly

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606401">1</span></span>Agree and be
united: let your minds be all of one accord,  
  Even as the Gods of ancient days, unanimous, await their share.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606402">2</span></span>The rede is
common, common the assembly, common the law,  
   so be their thoughts united.  
  I offer up your general oblation: together entertain one common  
   purpose.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606403">3</span></span>One and the
same be your resolve, be all your hearts in har-  
   mony:  
  One and the same be all your minds that all may happily con-  
   sent.
