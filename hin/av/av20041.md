### HYMN XLI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004101">1</span></span>With bones
of Dadhyach for his arms, Indra, resistless in attack,  
  Struck nine-and-ninety Vritras dead,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004102">2</span></span>He,
searching for the horse's head, removed among the moun-  
   tains, found  
  At Saryanāvān what he sought.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004103">3</span></span>Then verily
they recognized the essential form of Tvashtar's Bull.  
  Here in the mansion of the Moon.
