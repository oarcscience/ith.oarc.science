### HYMN XXXVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003801">1</span></span>Come, we
have pressed the juice for thee. O Indra, drink the  
  Soma here.  
  Sit thou on this my sacred grass.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003802">2</span></span>O Indra, let
thy long-maned Bays, yoked by prayer, bring thee  
   hitherward.  
  Give ear and listen to our prayers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003803">3</span></span>We,
Soma-bearing Brāhmans, call thee, Soma-drinker, with thy  
   friend,  
  We, Indra, bringing juice expressed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003804">4</span></span>Indra the
singers with high praise, Indra reciters with their  
   lauds,  
  Indra the choirs have glorified.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003805">5</span></span>Indra hath
ever close to him his two bay steeds and word-yoked:  
   car,  
  Indra, the golden, Thunder-armed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003806">6</span></span>Indra hath
raised the Sun aloft in heaven that he may see afar.  
  He burst the mountain for the kine.
