### HYMN LVII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005701">1</span></span>As a good
cow to him who milks, we call the doer of fair deeds.  
  To our assistance day by day.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005702">2</span></span>Come thou to
our libations, drink of Soma, Soma-drinker thou!  
  The rich One's rapture giveth kine.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005703">3</span></span>So may we be
acquainted with thine innermost benevolence:  
  Neglect us not, come hitherward.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005704">4</span></span>Drink for
our help the Soma bright, vigilant, and exceeding  
   strong,  
  O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005705">5</span></span>O Satakratu,
powers which thou mid the Five Races hast dis-  
   played,  
  These, Indra, do I claim of thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005706">6</span></span>Indra, great
glory hast thou gained. Win splendid fame which  
   none may mar.  
  We make thy might perpetual.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005707">7</span></span>Come to us
either from anear, or, Sakra, come from far away.  
  Indra, wherever be thy home, come thence, O Caster of the  
  Stone. <span id="page_311">p. 311</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005708">8</span></span>Verily
Indra, conquering all, driveth even mighty fear away.  
  For firm is he and swift to act.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005709">9</span></span>Indra be
gracious unto us: sin shall not reach us afterward,  
  And good shall be before us still.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005710">10</span></span>From all
the regions of the world let Indra send security.  
  The foe-subduer, swift to act.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005714">14</span></span>We compass
thee like waters, we whose grass in trimmed and  
  Soma pressed.  
  Here where the filter pours its stream thy worshippers round  
   thee, O Vritra-slayer, sit.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005715">15</span></span>Men, Vasu,
by the Soma with lauds call thee to the foremost  
   place.  
  When comest thou athirst unto the juice as home, O Indra, like  
   a bellowing bull?  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005716">16</span></span>Boldly,
bold Hero, bring us spoil in thousands for the Kanvas'  
   sake.  
  O active Maghavan, with eager prayer we crave the yellow-hued  
   with store of kine.
