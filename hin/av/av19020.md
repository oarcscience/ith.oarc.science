### HYMN XX

#### A prayer for protection from death and misfortune

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902001">1</span></span>May Soma,
Varuna the King, both Asvins, Yama and Pūshan  
   guard us well from Mrityu—  
  Death caused by men, which Indra-Agni, Dhātar, Brihaspati and  
  Savitar appointed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902002">2</span></span>All that the
World's Lord made, all that for creatures Prajāpati  
   and Mātarisvan fashioned,  
  All things within the quarters and their spaces, let all these be  
   my manifold defences.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902003">3</span></span>That which
the Gods bound on them when they battled for their  
   royal sway.  
  What Indra made his shield, may that protect us well on every  
   side,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902004">4</span></span>My shield is
Heaven and Earth, my shield is the bright Day, my  
   shield the Sun.  
  The Visva Devas made my shield. Let not misfortune fall on  
   me.
