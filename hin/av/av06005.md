### HYMN V

#### A prayer to Agni and Indra for the well being of a princely patron

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600501">1</span></span>Agni, adored
with sacred oil, lift up this man to high estate.  
  Endow him with full store of strength and make him rich in  
   progeny.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600502">2</span></span>Advance him,
Indra! Let him be ruler of all akin to him. <span id="page_a201">p.
a201</span>  
  Grant him sufficiency of wealth: guide him to life and length of  
   days.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600503">3</span></span>Prosper this
man, O Agni, in whose house we offer sacrifice.  
  May Soma bless him, and the God here present, Brāhmanaspati.
