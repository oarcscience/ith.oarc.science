### HYMN IX

#### A charm to secure wealth and felicity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600901">1</span></span>Ours is
superior place and ours is conquest: may I in all fights  
   tread down spite and malice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600902">2</span></span>This word
hath Agni, this hath Soma spoken. May Pūshan set  
   me in the world of virtue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600903">3</span></span>We have come
to the light of heaven; to the light of heaven  
   have we come: we have united with the light of Surya.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600904">4</span></span>Sacrifice is
fraught with wealth for the increase of prosperity. I  
   would win riches; fain would I be wealthy. Do thou bestow  
   wealth upon me.
