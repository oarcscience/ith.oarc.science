### HYMN LIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005903">3</span></span>His portion
is exceeding great, like a victorious soldier's spoil.  
  Him who is Indra, Lord of Bays, no foes subdue. He gives the  
  Soma-pourer strength.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005904">4</span></span>Make for the
holy Gods a hymn that is not mean, but well  
   arranged and fair in form.  
  Full many snares and bonds subdue not him who dwells with  
  Indra through his sacrifice.
