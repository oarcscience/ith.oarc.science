### HYMN IV

#### To the waters, for the prosperity of cattle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100401">1</span></span>Along their
paths the Mothers go, sisters of priestly  
   ministrants,  
  Blending their water with the mead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100402">2</span></span>May yonder
Waters near the Sun, or those wherewith the Sun is  
   joined,  
  Send forth this sacrifice of ours.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100403">3</span></span>I call the
Waters, Goddesses, hitherward where our cattle  
   drink:  
  The streams must share the sacrifice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100404">4</span></span>Amrit is in
the Waters, in the Waters balm.  
  Yea, through our praises of the Floods, O horses, be ye fleet and  
   strong, and, O ye kine, be full of strength.
