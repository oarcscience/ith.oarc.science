### HYMN XXXIII

#### A prayer to Agni for protection and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403301">1</span></span>Chasing our
pain with splendid light, O Agni, shine thou wealth  
   on us.  
  His lustre flash our pain away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403302">2</span></span>For goodly
fields, for pleasant homes, for wealth we sacrifice to  
   thee.  
  His lustre flash our pain away!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403303">3</span></span>Best praiser
of all these be he, and foremost be our noble chiefs.  
  His lustre flash our pain away!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403304">4</span></span>So that thy
worshipper and we, thine, Agni! in our sons may  
   live.  
  His lustre flash our pain away!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403305">5</span></span>As ever
conquering Agni's beams of splendour go to every side,  
  His lustre flash our pain away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403306">6</span></span>To every
side thy face is turned, thou art triumphant everywhere.  
  His lustre flash our pain away!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403307">7</span></span>O thou whose
face looks every way, bear off our foes as in a  
   ship.  
  His lustre flash our pain away!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0403308">8</span></span>As in a ship
across the flood, transport us to felicity. His lustre  
   flash our pain away
