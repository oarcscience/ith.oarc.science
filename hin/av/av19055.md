### HYMN LV

#### A hymn to Agni for protection and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905501">1</span></span>Bringing, as
'twere, with care unceasing fodder night after night  
   to feed this stabled Courser,  
  Joying in food and in the growth of riches, may we thy neigh-  
   bours, Agni, ne'er be injured.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905502">2</span></span>Here is
thine own desire for wealth: through this be gracious  
   unto us.  
  Joying in food and in the growth of riches, may we thy neigh-  
   bours, Agni, ne'er be injured.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905503">3</span></span>Each eve
that comes our household's Lord is Agni, showing his.  
   loving-kindness every morning.  
  Bestow upon us treasure after treasure: enkindling thee may we  
   increase thy body.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905504">4</span></span>Each morn
that comes our household's Lord is Agni, showing.  
   his loving-kindness every evening.  
  Vouchsafe us treasure after treasure: kindling thee may we  
   prosper through a hundred winters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905505">5</span></span>Never may I
come short of food to feed me.  
  Glory to Agni, Rudra, the consumer and the Lord of food!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905506">6</span></span>Protect my
company, protect its courteous members, courteous.  
  God! <span id="page_257">p. 257</span>  
  Only through thee, O much-invoked, may I be ever rich in kine.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905507">7</span></span>Only to thee
bringing our tribute, Agni, each day as fodder to a  
   stabled courser,  
  Joying in food and in the growth of riches, may we the neigh-  
   bours, Agni ne'er be injured.
