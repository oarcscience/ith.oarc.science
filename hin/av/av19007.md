### HYMN VII

#### A Prayer to the Lunar Mansions and other Powers for protection and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900701">1</span></span>The
brilliant lights shining in heaven together, which through  
   the world glide on with rapid motion.  
  And Days, and Firmament with songs I worship, seeking the  
  Twenty-eight-fold for its favour.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900702">2</span></span>Krittikās,
Rohinī be swift to hear me! Let Mrigasiras bless me,  
   help me Ārdrā!  
  Punarvasu and Sūnritā, fair Pushya, the Sun, Asleshās, Maghā  
   lead me onward!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900703">3</span></span>My bliss be
Svāti and benignant Chitrā, my right First Phalgunis <span
id="page_217">p. 217</span>  
   and present Hasta.  
  Rādhas, Visākhas, gracious Anurādhā, Jyeshthā and happy-  
   starred uninjured Mūla.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900704">4</span></span>Food shall
be earlier Ashādhas grant me; let those that follow  
   bring me strength and vigour;  
  With virtuous merit Abhijit endow me! Sravana and Sravishthās  
   make me prosper.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900705">5</span></span>Satabhishak
afford me ample freedom, and both the Proshtha-  
   padas guard me safely.  
  Revati and the Asvayujas bring me luck, and the Bharanis  
   abundant riches!
