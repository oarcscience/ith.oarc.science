### HYMN XXVII

#### A charm consigning an enemy to the serpents for punishment

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302701">1</span></span>Agni is
regent of the East, its warder is Asita, the Ādityas are  
   the arrows.  
  Worship to these the regents, these the warders, and to the  
   arrows, yea, to these be worship!  
  Within your jaws we lay the man who hateth us and whom we  
   hate.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302702">2</span></span>Indra is
regent of the South, its warder Tiraschirāji, and the  
   shafts the Fathers.  
  Worship to these the regents, these the warders, and to the  
   arrows, yea, to these be worship!  
  Within your jaws we lay the man who hateth us and whom we  
   hate.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302703">3</span></span>Of the West
region Varuna is ruler, Pridāku warder, Nourish-  
   ment the arrows.  
  Worship, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302704">4</span></span>Soma is
ruler of the Northern region, Svaja the warder, lightn-  
   ing's flash the arrows.  
  Worship, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302705">5</span></span>Vishnu is
ruler of the firm-set region, Kalmāshagriva warder,  
  Plants the arrows.  
  Worship, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302706">6</span></span>Brihaspati
controls the topmost region, Svitra is warder, and  
   the Rain the arrows.  
  Worship to these the regents, these the warders, and to the  
   arrows, yea, to these be worship,!  
  Within your jaws we lay the man who hateth us and whom we  
   hate.
