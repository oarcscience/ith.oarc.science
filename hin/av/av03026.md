### HYMN XXVI

#### A charm to win the favour of all serpents

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302601">1</span></span>Ye Gods who
dwell within this eastward region, entitled  
  Weapons, Agni forms your arrows.  
  Be kind and gracious unto us and bless us. To you be reverence,  
   to you be welcome!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302602">2</span></span>Ye Gods who
dwell within this southward region, entitled Eager,  
  Kāma forms your arrows.  
  Be kind, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302603">3</span></span>Ye Gods who
dwell within this westward region, whose name is  
  Radiant, Water forms your arrows.  
  Be kind, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302604">4</span></span>Ye Gods who
dwell within this northward region, whose name  
   is Piercers, Vāta forms your arrows.  
  Be kind, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302605">5</span></span>Ye Gods
whose home is in this firm-set region—Nilimpas is your  
   name—Plants are your arrow.  
  Be kind, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302606">6</span></span>Ye Gods
whose home is in this upmost region, Yearners by  
   name, Brihaspati forms your arrows.  
  Be kind and gracious unto us and bless us. To you be reverence,  
   to you be welcome!
