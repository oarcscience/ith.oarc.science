### HYMN XLI

#### A benediction on a newly elected king

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904101">1</span></span>Desiring
bliss, at first, light-finding Rishis began religious rite  
   and holy fervour.  
  Thence energy was born, and might, and kingship: so to this  
   man let gathered Gods incline them.
