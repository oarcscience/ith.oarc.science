### HYMN XI

#### An epithalamian charm to ensure the birth of a boy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601101">1</span></span>Asvattha on
the Sami-tree. There a male birth is certified.  
  There is the finding of a son: this bring we to the women-folk.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601102">2</span></span>The father
sows the genial seed, the woman tends and fosters it.  
  This is the finding of a son: thus hath Prajāpati declared.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601103">3</span></span>Prajāpati,
Anumati, Sinivāli have ordered it.  
  Elsewhere may he effect the birth of maids, but here prepare a  
   boy.
