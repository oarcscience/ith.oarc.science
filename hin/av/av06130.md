### HYMN CXXX

#### A woman's love-charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613001">1</span></span>This is the
Apsarases' love-spell, the conquering, resistless ones'.  
  Send the spell forth, ye Deities! Let him consume with love of  
   me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613002">2</span></span>I pray, may
he remember me, think of me, loving and beloved.  
  Send forth the spell, ye Deities! Let him consume with love  
   of me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613003">3</span></span>That he may
think of me, that I may never, never think of him,.  
  Send forth the spell, ye Deities! Let him consume with love  
   of me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613004">4</span></span>Madden him,
Maruts, madden him. Madden him, madden him,  
  O Air.  
  Madden him, Agni, madden him. Let him consume with love  
   of me.
