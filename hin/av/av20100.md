### HYMN C

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010001">1</span></span>Now have we,
Indra, Friend of Song, sent our great wishes  
   forth to thee.  
  Coming like floods that follow floods.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010002">2</span></span>As rivers
swell the ocean, so, Hero, our prayers increase thy  
   might,  
  Though of thyself, O Thunderer, waxing day by day.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010003">3</span></span>With holy
song they bind to the broad wide-yoked car the bay  
   steeds of the rapid God,  
  Bearers of India, yoked by prayer.
