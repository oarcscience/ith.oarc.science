### HYMN LVIII

#### A priest's prayer for power and glory

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605801">1</span></span>May Indra
Maghavan give me name and glory. May Heaven  
   and Earth, this couple, make me famous.  
  May Savitar the deity make me honoured. Here may the man  
   who gives the guerdon love me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605802">2</span></span>Indra from
Heaven and Earth receiveth glory among the plants  
   the Waters have their glory;  
  Even so may we be glorious'mid all the Universal Gods.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605803">3</span></span>Indra and
Agni were renowned, famous was Soma at his birth;  
  So too am I illustrious, most glorious of all that is.
