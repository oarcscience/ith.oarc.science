### HYMN CXXVII

#### A charm to banish various diseases

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612701">1</span></span>Of abscess,
of decline, of inflammation of the eyes. O Plant,  
  Of penetrating pain, thou Herb, let not a particle remain.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612702">2</span></span>Those nerves
of thine, Consumption! which stand closely hidden  
   in thy groin  
  I know the balm for that disease: the magic cure is Sipudru.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612703">3</span></span>We draw from
thee piercing pain that penetrates and racks thy  
   limbs,  
  That pierces ears, that pierces eyes, the abscess, and the heart's  
   disease.  
  Downward and far away from thee we banish that unknown.  
   decline.
