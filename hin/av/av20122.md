### HYMN CXXII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012201">1</span></span>With Indra
splendid feasts be ours enriched with ample spoil,  
   wherewith,  
  Wealthy in food, we may rejoice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012202">2</span></span>Like thee,
thyself, the singers' friend, thou movest as it were,  
   besought,  
  Bold One, the axle of the car.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012203">3</span></span>That,
Satakratu, thou to grace and please thy praisers, as it  
   were,  
  Stirrest the axle with thy strength.
