### HYMN XLVIII

#### Formulas to be used at the three daily libations

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604801">1</span></span>Thou art the
Hawk, Gāyatri's lord: I hold thee fast. Happily  
   bear me to the goal of this my sacrifice. All hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604802">2</span></span>Thou art the
Ribhu, lord of Jagatī: I hold thee fast. Happily  
   bear me to the goal of this my sacrifice. Al I hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604803">3</span></span>Thou art the
Bull, the Trishtup's lord: I hold thee fast. Happily  
   bear me to the goal of this my sacrifice. All hail!
