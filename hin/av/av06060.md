### HYMN LX

#### The wooing of a bride

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606001">1</span></span>With
forelock loosened o'er his brow here comes the wooer of  
   the bride, <span id="page_a228">p. a228</span>  
  Seeking a husband for this maid, a wife for this unmarried man.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606002">2</span></span>Wooer! this
girl hath toiled in vain, going to others' marriages.  
  Now to her wedding, verily, wooer! another maid shall come.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606003">3</span></span>Dhātar
upholds the spacious earth, upholds the sky, upholds the  
  Sun.  
  Dhātar bestow upon this maid a husband suited to her wish!
