### HYMN XII

#### A prayer for influence at deliberative and religious meetings

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701201">1</span></span>In concord
may Prajapati's two daughters, Gathering and As-  
   sembly, both protect me.  
  May every man I meet respect and aid me. Fair be my words,  
  O Fathers, at the meetings.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701202">2</span></span>We know thy
name, O Conference: thy name is interchange of  
   talk.  
  Let all the company who join the Conference agree with me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701203">3</span></span>Of these men
seated here I make the splendour and the lore  
   mine own.  
  Indra, make me conspicuous in all this gathered company.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701204">4</span></span>Whether your
thoughts are turned away, or bound and fastened  
   here or there,  
  We draw them hitherward again: let your mind firmly rest on  
   me.
