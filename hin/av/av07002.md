### HYMN II

#### Praise of Atharvan

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0700201">1</span></span>Invoke for
us, proclaim in sundry places, the kinsman of the  
  Gods, our sire Atharvan,  
  His mother's germ, his father's breath, the youthful, who with  
   his mind hath noticed this oblation.
