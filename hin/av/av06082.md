### HYMN LXXXII

#### A charm to win a bride

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608201">1</span></span>I call the
name of him who comes, hath come, and still draws-  
   nigh to us. <span id="page_a238">p. a238</span>  
  Foe-slaying Indra's name I love, the Vasus' friend with hundred  
   powers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608202">2</span></span>Thus Bhaga
spake to me: Let him bring thee a consort by the  
   path.  
  Whereon the Asvins brought the bride Sūryā the child of  
  Savitar.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608203">3</span></span>Great,
Indra. is that hook of thine, bestowing treasure, wrought  
   of gold:  
  Therewith, O Lord of Might, bestow a wife on me who long to  
   wed.
