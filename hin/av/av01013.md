### HYMN XIII

#### A prayer to Lightning, for happiness

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101301">1</span></span>Homage to
thee, the Lightning's flash, homage to thee, the  
  Thunder's roar!  
  Homage to thee, the Stone which thou hurlest against the  
   undevout!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101302">2</span></span>Homage to
thee, Child of the Flood whence thou collectest fer-  
   vent heat!  
  Be gracious to our bodies, give our children happiness and  
   joy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101303">3</span></span>Yea, homage
be to thee, O Offspring of the Flood! Homage we  
   pay to thee, the dart and fiery flame:  
  For well we know thy secret and sublimest home, where thou as  
   central point art buried in the sea.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101304">4</span></span>Thou, Arrow,
which the host of Gods created, making it strong  
   and mighty for the shooting,  
  Be gracious, lauded thus, to our assembly. To thee, that Arrow,.  
   be our homage, Goddess!
