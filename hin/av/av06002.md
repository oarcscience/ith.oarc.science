### HYMN II

#### In praise of Indra

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600201">1</span></span>For Indra,
ministering priests! run ye and press the Soma juice,  
  That he may hear his praiser's word, and this my call.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600202">2</span></span>Thou into
whom the drops find way as sap pours life into a  
   tree,  
  Drive off in thine abundant might our demon foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600203">3</span></span>For Indra,
thunder-armed, who drinks the Soma press the Soma  
   out:  
  He, youthful, conqueror, and Lord is praised by all.
