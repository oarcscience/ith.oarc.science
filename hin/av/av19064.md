### HYMN LXIV

#### A prayer to Agni for children, long life, and various blessings

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906401">1</span></span>For lofty
Jātavedas I have brought the fuel hither first.  
  May he who knoweth all bestow faith and intelligence on me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906402">2</span></span>With fuel
and with flaming wood we, Jātavedas, strengthen  
   thee;  
  So do thou strengthen us in turn with children and with store of  
   wealth.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906403">3</span></span>Whatever
even be the logs which, Agni, we lay down for thee,  
   propitious be it all to me: accept it, O most youthful God.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906404">4</span></span>Agni, these
logs are thine: with these be, fain to burn! a  
   flaming brand.  
  Vouchsafe us length of life and give us hope of immortality.
