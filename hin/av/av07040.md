### HYMN XL

#### A prayer for prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704001">1</span></span>We call
Sarasvān, under whose protection all cattle wander, to  
   preserve and aid us,  
  Him in whose ordinance abide the waters, to whose command  
   the Lord of Plenty listens.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704002">2</span></span>Abiding here
let us invoke Sarasvān, the seat of riches, glorious,  
   wealth-increaser,  
  Him who inclines and gives to him who worships, the rich pos-  
   sessor and the Lord of Fulness.
