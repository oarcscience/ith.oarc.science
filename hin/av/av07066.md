### HYMN LXVI

#### A prayer to Vāk

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706601">1</span></span>If it was in
the wind or air's mid-region, if it was in the trees or  
   in the bushes,  
  To meet whose utterance forth streamed the cattle, may that.  
  Celestial Power again approach us.
