### HYMN XXXVIII

#### A protective charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903801">1</span></span>Never
Consumption, never curse touches the man, Arundhatī!  
  Whom the delicious odour of the healing Bdellium penetrates  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903802">2</span></span>Consumptions
flee apart from it as from a wild beast fly the  
   deer.  
  If thou, O Bdellium, art produced from Sindhu or hast come  
   from sea,  
  The quality of both have I taken to keep this man unscathed.
