### HYMN XXXV

#### To Agni Vaisvānara

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603501">1</span></span>Forth from
the distance far away Vaisvānara come to succour  
   us! Agni approach our eulogies!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603502">2</span></span>Vaisvānara
with friendly thoughts hath come to this our sacrifice,  
  Agni who saves from woe, to lauds.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603503">3</span></span>Vaisvānara
hath formed the hymn and laud of the Angirases. To  
   these may he bring glorious right.
