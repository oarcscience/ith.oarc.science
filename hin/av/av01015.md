### HYMN XV

#### A prayer for the prosperity of an institutor of sacrifice

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101501">1</span></span>Let the
streams, flow together, let the winds and birds assembled  
   come.  
  Let this my sacrifice delight them always. I offer it with duly mixt  
   oblation.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101502">2</span></span>Come to my
call, Blent Offerings, come ye very nigh. And,  
   singers, do ye strengthen and increase this man.  
  Hither come every animal: with this man let all wealth abide.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101503">3</span></span>All river
founts that blend their streams for ever inexhaustible—  
  With all these confluent streams of mine we make abundant  
   riches flow.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101504">4</span></span>All streams
of melted butter, and all streams of water and of  
   milk  
  With all these confluent streams of mine we make abundant  
   riches flow.
