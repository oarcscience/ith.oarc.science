### HYMN LXIX

#### A prayer or charm for long life

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906901">1</span></span>Ye are
alive. I fain would live. I fain would live my complete  
   term of life.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906902">2</span></span>Ye live
dependent. I fain would live dependent. I fain would  
   live my complete term of life.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906903">3</span></span>Ye remain
alive. I fain would remain alive. I fain would live  
   my complete term of life.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906904">4</span></span>Ye are
life-givers. I fain would live. I fain would live my com-  
   plete term of life.
