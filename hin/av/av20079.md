### HYMN LXXIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2007901">1</span></span>O Indra,
give us wisdom as a sire gives wisdom to his sons.  
  Guide us, O Much-invoked, on this our foray: may we, living,  
   still enjoy the light.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2007902">2</span></span>Grant that
no mighty foes, unknown, malevolent, unhallowed,  
   tread us to the ground.  
  With thine assistance, Hero! may we pass through all the waters  
   that are rushing down.
