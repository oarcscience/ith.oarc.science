### HYMN CXXXI

#### A woman's love-charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613101">1</span></span>Down upon
thee, from head to foot, I draw the pangs of long-  
   ing love.  
  Send forth the charm, ye Deities! Let him consume with love  
   of me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613102">2</span></span>Assent to
this, O Heavenly Grace! Celestial Purpose, guide it  
   well!  
  Send forth the charm, ye Deities! Let him consume with love  
   of me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613103">3</span></span>If thou
shouldst run three leagues away, five leagues, a horse's  
   daily stage,  
  Thence thou shalt come to me again and be the father of our  
   sons.
