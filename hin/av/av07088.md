### HYMN LXXXVIII

#### A charm to cure a snake-bite

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708801">1</span></span>Depart! thou
art a foe, a foe. Poison with poison hast thou  
   mixt, yea, verily poison hast thou mixt.  
  Go to the serpent: strike him dead.
