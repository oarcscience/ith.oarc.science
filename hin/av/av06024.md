### HYMN XXIV

#### To the Rivers

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602401">1</span></span>Forth from
the Hills of Snow they stream, and meet in Sindhu  
   here or there.  
  To me the sacred Waters gave the balm that heals the heart's  
   disease.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602402">2</span></span>Whatever
rupture I have had that injured eyes or heels or toes.  
  All this the Waters, skilfullest physicians, shall make well again,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602403">3</span></span>All Rivers
who have Sindhu for your Lady, Sindhu for your  
  Queen,  
  Give us the balm that heals this ill: this boon let us enjoy from  
   you.
