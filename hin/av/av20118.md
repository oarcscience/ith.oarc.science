### HYMN CXVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011801">1</span></span>Indra with
all thy saving helps give us assistance, Lord of Power  
  For after thee we follow even as glorious bliss, thee, Hero,  
   finder-out of wealth.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011802">2</span></span>Increaser of
our steeds and multiplying kine, a golden well, O  
  God, art thou;  
  For no one may impair the gifts laid up in thee, Bring me what-  
   ever thing I ask.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011803">3</span></span>Indra for
worship of the Gods, Indra while sacrifice proceeds,  
  Indra as warriors in the battle-shock we call, Indra that we may  
   win the spoil.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011804">4</span></span>With might
hath Indra spread out heaven and earth, with power  
   hath Indra lighted up the Sun.  
  In Indra are all creatures closely held; in him meet the distilling  
  Soma drops.
