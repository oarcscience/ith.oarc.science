### HYMN LXIV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006401">1</span></span>Come unto
us, O Indra, dear, still conquering, unconcealable,  
  Vast as a mountain spread on all sides, Lord of heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006402">2</span></span>O truthful
Soma-drinker, thou art mightier than both the  
   worlds.  
  Thou strengthenest him who pours libation, Lord of heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006403">3</span></span>For thou
art, he, O Indra, who stormest all castles of the foe,  
  Slayer of Dasyus, man's supporter, Lord of heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006404">4</span></span>O
ministering priest, pour out of the sweet juice what gladdens  
   most.  
  So is the Hero praised who ever prospers us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006405">5</span></span>Indra whom
tawny coursers bear, praise such a thine,.  
   preeminent  
  None by his power or by his goodness hath attained  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006406">6</span></span>We seeking
glory, have invoked this Master of all power and'  
   might.  
  Who must be glorified by constant sacrifice.
