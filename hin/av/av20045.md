### HYMN XLV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004501">1</span></span>This is
thine own. Thou drawest near, as the dove turneth to his  
   mate.  
  Thou carest too for this our prayer.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004502">2</span></span>O Hero, Lord
of Bounties, praised in hymns, may power and  
   pleasantness  
  Be his who signs the laud to thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004503">3</span></span>Lord of a
Hundred Powers, stand up to lend us succour in this  
   fight:  
  In others too let us agree.
