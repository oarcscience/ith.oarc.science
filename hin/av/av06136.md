### HYMN CXXXVI

#### A charm to promote the growth of hair

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613601">1</span></span>Born from
the bosom of wide Earth the Goddess, godlike Plant,  
   art thou:  
  So we, Nitatnī! dig thee up to strengthen and fix fast the hair.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613602">2</span></span>Make the old
firm, make new hair spring, lengthen what has  
   already grown.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613603">3</span></span>Thy hair
where it is falling off, and with the roots is torn away,  
  I wet and sprinkle with the Plant, the remedy for all disease.
