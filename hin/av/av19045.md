### HYMN XLV

#### A curative and protective charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904501">1</span></span>As debt from
debt repay and send sorcery to the sorcerer's  
   house.  
  Split, Salve! the cruel villain's ribs whose evil eye bewitches us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904502">2</span></span>Whatever
evil dream we have, what'er befall our kine or home,  
  Be this that is salubrity, the evil-hearted's foe applied.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904503">3</span></span>Increasing
from the Waters' strength and vigour, sprung into life  
   from Agni Jātavedas,  
  Strong as four heroes, mountain born, this Ointment make for  
   thee quarters and mind-points auspicious!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904504">4</span></span>On thee is
laid the Chaturvira Ointment: let all the regions give  
   thee peace and safety.  
  Secure like precious Savitar thou standest: to thee let all these  
   regions bring their tribute.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904505">5</span></span>Make one thy
salve, thine amulet another, drink one, and with  
   another bathe thy body.  
  So let the Chaturvira keep us guarded from the four bonds of  
  Nirriti and Grāhi.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904506">6</span></span>May Agni
protect me with fire for inspiration and expiration,  
   for strength, for energy, for vigour, for weal and prosperity.  
  All Hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904507">7</span></span>May Indra
protect me with his Indra-power for inspiration, etc. <span
id="page_248">p. 248</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904508">8</span></span>May Soma
protect me with Soma-power, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904509">9</span></span>May Bhaga
with good fortune protect me, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904510">10</span></span>May the
Maruts protect me with their troops for inspiration  
   and expiration, for strength, for energy, for vigour, for weal  
   and prosperity. All Hail!
