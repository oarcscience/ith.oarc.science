### HYMN CXVI

#### A prayer for pardon of sin against mother, father, son, or brother

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611601">1</span></span>The wealth
which husbandmen aforetime, digging, like men who  
   find their food with knowledge, buried,  
  This to the King, Vivasvān's son, I offer, Sweet be our food and  
   fit for sacrificing!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611602">2</span></span>May he,
Vaivasvata, prepare our portion; May he whose share  
   is mead with mead besprinkle.  
  Our sin in hasty mood against our mother, or guilt whereby a  
   sire is wronged and angered.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611603">3</span></span>Whether this
sin into our heart hath entered regarding mother,  
   father, son or brother,  
  Auspicious be to us the zeal and spirit of all the fathers who are  
   here among us.
