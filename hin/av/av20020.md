### HYMN XX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002001">1</span></span>Drink thou
the Soma for our help, bright, vigilant, exceeding  
   strong,  
  O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002002">2</span></span>O Satakratu,
powers which thou mid the Five Races hast dis-  
   played  
  These, Indra, do I claim of thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002003">3</span></span>Indra, great
glory hast thou gained. Win splendid fame which  
   none may mar:  
  We make thy might perpetual.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002004">4</span></span>Come to us
either from anear, or, Sakra, come from far away. <span id="page_280">p.
280</span>  
  Indra, wherever be thy home, come to us thence, O Thunder-  
   armed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002005">5</span></span>Verily
Indra, conquering all, driveth even mighty fear away;  
  For firm is he and swift to act.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002006">6</span></span>Indra be
gracious unto us: sin shall not reach us afterward .  
  And good shall be before us still,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002007">7</span></span>From all the
regions of the world let Indra send security.  
  The foe-subduer, swift to act.
