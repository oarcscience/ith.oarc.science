### HYMN LII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005201">1</span></span>We compass
thee like waters, we whose grass is trimmed and  
  Soma pressed.  
  Here where the filter pours its stream thy worshipper round  
   thee, O Vritra-slayer, sit.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2005202">2</span></span>Men, Vasu!
by the Soma, with lauds call thee to the foremost  
   place.  
  When comest thou athirst unto the juice as home, O Indra, like  
   a bellowing bull?
