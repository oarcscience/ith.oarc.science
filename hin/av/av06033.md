### HYMN XXXIII

#### A prayer to Indra for riches

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603301">1</span></span>He who
controls this air and men who aid his strength, and  
   wood, and heaven, the lofty seat which Indra loves.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603302">2</span></span>The bold
whose overpowering might the boldest never hath  
   defied,— <span id="page_a215">p. a215</span>  
  As erst still, unassailable is Indra's wrath, and fame, and force.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603303">3</span></span>May he
bestow on us that wealth, far-spreading, bright with  
   yellow hue.  
  Indra is mightiest Lord among the folk.
