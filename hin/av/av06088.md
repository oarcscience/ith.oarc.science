### HYMN LXXXVIII

#### A benediction addressed to a newly elected King

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608801">1</span></span>Firm is the
sky, firm is the earth, and firm is all this living  
   world;  
  Firm are these mountains on their base, and stedfast is this King  
   of men.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608802">2</span></span>Stedfast may
Varuna the King, stedfast the God Brihaspati,  
  Stedfast may Indra stedfast, too, may Agni keep thy stedfast  
   reign.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608803">3</span></span>Firm, never
to be shaken, crush thy foemen, under thy feet lay  
   those who strive against thee.  
  One-minded, true to thee be all the regions: faithful to thee, the  
   firm, be this assembly!
