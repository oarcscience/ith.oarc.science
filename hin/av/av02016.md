### HYMN XVI

#### A prayer for general protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201601">1</span></span>Guard me
from death, Inhaling and Exhaling! All bliss to you!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201602">2</span></span>Guard me
from overhearing, Earth and Heaven! All hail to  
   you!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201603">3</span></span>Do thou, O
Sūrya, with thine eye protect me! All hail to  
   thee!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201604">4</span></span>Agni
Vaisvānara, with all Gods preserve me! All hail to thee!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201605">5</span></span>Preserve me
with all care. O All-Sustainer! All hail to thee!
