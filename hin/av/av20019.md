### HYMN XIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001901">1</span></span>O Indra, for
the strength that slays the foe and conquers in the  
   fight  
  We turn thee hitherward to us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001902">2</span></span>O Indra,
Lord of Hundred Powers, may those who praise thee  
   hitherward.  
  Direct thy spirit and thine eye.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001903">3</span></span>O Indra,
Lord of Hundred Powers, with all our songs we  
   invocate.  
  Thy names for triumph over foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001904">4</span></span>We strive
for glory through the powers immense of him whom  
   many praise,  
  Of Indra who supports mankind.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001905">5</span></span>For the
foe's slaughter I address Indra whom many invocate,  
  To win us booty in the wars.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001906">6</span></span>In battles
be victorious. We seek thee, Lord of Hundred Powers,  
  O Indra, that the foe may fall.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001907">7</span></span>In splendid
combats of the hosts, in glories where the fight is  
   won,  
  Indra, be victor over foes.
