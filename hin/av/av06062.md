### HYMN LXII

#### A prayer for purification and riches

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606201">1</span></span>Cleanse us
Vaisvānara with rays of splendour! With breath and  
   clouds let quickening Vāyu cleanse us. <span id="page_a229">p.
a229</span>  
  And, rich in milky rain, let Earth and Heaven, worshipful, holy,  
   cleanse us with their water.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606202">2</span></span>Lay hold on
Sūnritā whose forms and regions have fair smooth  
   backs, her who is all men's treasure.  
  Through her may we, in sacrificial banquets singing her glory, be  
   the lords of riches.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606203">3</span></span>For
splendour, seize on her whom all men worship, becoming  
   pure yourselves, and bright, and brilliant.  
  Here, through our prayer rejoicing in the banquet, long may we  
   look upon the Sun ascending.
