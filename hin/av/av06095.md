### HYMN XCV

#### A charm to remove disease

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609501">1</span></span>In the third
heaven above us stands the Asvattha tree, the seat  
   of Gods.  
  There the Gods gained the Kushtha plant, embodiment of end-  
   less life.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609502">2</span></span>There moved
through heaven a golden ship, a ship with cordage  
   wrought of gold.  
  There Gods obtained the Kushtha plant, the flower of immor-  
   tality.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609503">3</span></span>Thou art the
infant of the plants, the infant of the Snowy Hills:  
  The germ of every thing that is: free this my friend from his  
   disease.
