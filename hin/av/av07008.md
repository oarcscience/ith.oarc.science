### HYMN VIII

#### Godspeed to a departing traveller

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0700801">1</span></span>Go forward
on thy way from good to better: Brihaspati pre-  
   cede thy steps and guide thee! <span id="page_a274">p. a274</span>  
  Place this man here, within this earth's enclosure, afar from foes  
   with all his men about him.
