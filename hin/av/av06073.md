### HYMN LXXIII

#### A King's charm to conciliate his discontented kinsmen

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607301">1</span></span>Let Varuna
come hither, Soma, Agni, Brihaspati come hither  
   with the Vasus!  
  Unanimous, ye kinsmen, come united, come to the glory of this  
   mighty guardian.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607302">2</span></span>The
inclination which your hearts have harboured, the purpose  
   which hath occupied your spirits,  
  This I annul with sacrifice and butter. In me be your sweet  
   resting-place, O kinsmen.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607303">3</span></span>Stand even
here; forsake me not. Before us may Pūshan make  
   your path unfit to travel.  
  Vāstoshpati incessantly recall you! In me be your sweet resting-  
   place, O kinsmen!
