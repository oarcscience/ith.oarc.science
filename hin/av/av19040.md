### HYMN XL

#### A prayer for pardon of error in sacrifice, and for wisdom, strength, and life

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904001">1</span></span>For each
defect of mine in voice and spirit I have approached  
  One vehement and ardent.  
  With all the Deities, fully approving, Brihaspati supply the  
   want!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904002">2</span></span>Disturb ye
not our intellect, O Waters, nor the power of prayer\_  
  Glide on your way, strength-giving, invocated: may I be vigor-  
   ous and wise.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904003">3</span></span>Mar not our
consecrating rite, our intellect, or fervent zeal.  
  Gracious to us for lengthened life, propitious let the Mothers  
   be.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904004">4</span></span>Vouchsafe to
us, ye Asvins twain, such strength as, with atten-  
   dant light,  
  May through the darkness carry us.
