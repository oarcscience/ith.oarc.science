### HYMN VIII

#### An imprecation on an enemy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600801">1</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, whatever hath accrued to us, our  
  Right, our energy, our Devotion, our heavenly light, our <span
id="page_168">p. 168</span>  
   sacrifice, our domestic animals, our offspring, our men,—from  
   all share herein we exclude Such-an-one, descendant of Such-  
   an-one, son of Such-a-woman. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of Grāhi. Here I bind up his splendour, his energy, his  
   vital breath, his life, and cast him down beneath me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600802">2</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. (as in verse 1). Let him not be  
   freed from the noose of Nirriti, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600803">3</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of misery, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600804">4</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of disappearance, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600805">5</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of defeat, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600806">6</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of the sisters of the Gods, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600807">7</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of Brihaspati, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600808">8</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of Prajāpati, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600809">9</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of the Rishis, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600810">10</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, etc. Let him not be freed from the  
   noose of the Rishis' children, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600811">11</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Angirases, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600812">12</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Angirases, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600813">13</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Atharvans, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600814">14</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Atharvans, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600815">15</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Trees, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600816">16</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of  
  Shrubs, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600817">17</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Seasons, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600818">18</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Season-groups, etc. <span id="page_169">p. 169</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600819">19</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the  
  Months, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600820">20</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of the Half-  
   months, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600821">21</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of Day and  
  Night, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600822">22</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of continued  
  Day, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600823">23</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of Heaven  
   and Earth, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600824">24</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of Indra-  
  Agni, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600825">25</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of Mitra-  
  Varuna, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600826">26</span></span>Whatever,
etc. Let him not be freed from the noose of King  
  Varuna, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600827">27</span></span>Whatever we
have gained, whatever hath accrued to us, our  
  Right, our energy, our Devotion, our heavenly light, our  
   sacrifice, our domestic animals, our offspring, our men,—from  
   all share herein we exclude Such-as-one, descendant of Such-  
   an-one, son of Such-a-woman. Let him not be freed from the  
   fetter and noose of Death. Here I bind up his splendour, his  
   energy, his vital breath, his life, and cast him down beneath  
   me.
