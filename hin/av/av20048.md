### HYMN XLVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004801">1</span></span>The
swiftly-moving songs of praise pour on thee streams of vital  
   strength  
  As mother cows refresh the calf.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004802">2</span></span>Swift move
the bright ones while they blend the Milk with vital  
   vigour, as  
  A dame her infant with her heart.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004803">3</span></span>Fair hymns
bring glory to the Strong, and Indra-vigour; unto,  
   me  
  Fatness and milk and length of days.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004804">4</span></span>This
brindled Bull hath come and sat before the Mother in the  
   east,  
  Advancing to the Father Heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004805">5</span></span>As
expiration from breath she moves along the lucid spheres:  
  The Bull shines forth through all the sky.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004806">6</span></span>Song is
bestowed upon the Bird. It reigns supreme throughout  
   thirty realms  
  Throughout the days at break of morn.
