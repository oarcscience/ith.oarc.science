### HYMN XLII

#### A prayer for delivery from sin and sickness

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704201">1</span></span>Scatter and
drive away, Soma and Rudra, the sickness that hath  
   come within our dwelling, <span id="page_a287">p. a287</span>  
  Afar into the distance chase Destruction, and even from commit-  
   ted sin release us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704202">2</span></span>Lay on our
bodies, O ye twain, O Soma and Rudra, all those  
   balms that heal diseases.  
  Set free and draw away the sin committed, which we have still  
   inherent in our persons.
