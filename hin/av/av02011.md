### HYMN XI

#### Counter-charm, with an amulet, against an enemy's spell

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201101">1</span></span>Dart against
dart, destroyer of destruction, thou art the missile  
   sent to meet the missile. <span id="page_a44">p. a44</span>  
  Reach thy superior, thou; surpass thine equal.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201102">2</span></span>Sraktya art
thou, an Amulet art thou, a counter-charm of spells,  
  Reach thy superior, thou; surpass thine equal.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201103">3</span></span>Use spells
against the man we hate, against the man who hateth  
   us.  
  Reach thy superior, thou; surpass thine equal.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201104">4</span></span>A prince art
thou, giver of speech, thou art our bodies' strong  
   defence.  
  Reach thy superior, thou; surpass thine equal.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201105">5</span></span>Fulgent art
thou, and splendid, thou art heavenly lustre, thou  
   art light.  
  Reach thy superior, thou; surpass thine equal.
