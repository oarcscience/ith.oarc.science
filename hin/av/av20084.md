### HYMN LXXXIV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008401">1</span></span>O Indra
marvellously bright, come, these libations long for thee,  
  Thus by fine fingers purified.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008402">2</span></span>Urged by the
holy singer, sped by song, come, Indra, to the  
   prayers.  
  Of the libation-pouring priest.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008403">3</span></span>Approach, O
Indra, hasting thee, Lord of Bay Horses, to the  
   prayers:  
  Take pleasure in the juice we pour.
