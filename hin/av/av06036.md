### HYMN XXXVI

#### In praise of Agni Vaisvānara

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603601">1</span></span>Holy
Vaisvānara we seek, the Lord of light and endless life, the  
   burning One who fadeth not.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603602">2</span></span>He hath
directed all things; he sends forth the Seasons in his  
   might, furthering sacrifice's power.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603603">3</span></span>Agni Kāma in
other homes shines forth the sole imperial Lord  
   of all that is and is to be.
