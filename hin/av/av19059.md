### HYMN LIX

#### An expiatory hymn accompanying sacrifice

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905901">1</span></span>God among
mortals, Agni, thou art guard of holy Law, thou art  
  To be adored in sacred rites.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905902">2</span></span>When,
ignorant, we violate the statutes of you, O Deities, with  
   whom is knowledge,  
  Wise Agni shall correct our faults and failings, and Soma who  
   hath entered into Brāhmans.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1905903">3</span></span>To the Gods'
pathway have we come desiring to execute what  
   work we may accomplish.  
  Let Agni—for he knows—complete the worship. He is the  
  Priest: let him fix rites and seasons.
