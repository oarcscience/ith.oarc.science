### HYMN XV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501501">1</span></span>Of that
Vrātya.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501502">2</span></span>There are
seven vital airs, seven downward breaths, seven diff-  
   used breaths.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501503">3</span></span>His first
vital breath, called Upward, is this Agni.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501504">4</span></span>His second
vital breath, called Mature, is that Āditya.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501505">5</span></span>His third
vital breath, called Approached, is that Moon.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501506">6</span></span>His fourth
vital breath, called Pervading is this Pavamāna.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501507">7</span></span>His fifth
vital breath, called Source, are these Waters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501508">8</span></span>His sixth
vital breath, called Dear, are these domestic animals.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501509">9</span></span>His seventh
vital breath, called Unlimited, are these creatures.
