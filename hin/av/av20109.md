### HYMN CIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010901">1</span></span>The juice of
Soma thus diffused, sweet to the taste, the bright  
  Cows drink  
  Who for the sake of splendour close to mighty Indra's side  
   rejoice, good in their own supremacy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010902">2</span></span>Craving his
touch the dappled Kine mingle the Soma with their  
   milk. <span id="page_353">p. 353</span>  
  The milch-kine dear to Indra send forth his death-dealing thun-  
   derbolt, good in their own supremacy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010903">3</span></span>With
veneration, passing wise, honouring his victorious might,  
  They follow close his many laws to win them due preeminence,  
   good in their own supremacy.
