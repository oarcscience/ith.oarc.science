### HYMN XLIV

#### A charm to remove disease

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604401">1</span></span>Firm stood
the heaven, firm stood the earth, firm stood this  
   universal world.  
  Firm stood the trees that sleep erect: let this thy malady be still.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604402">2</span></span>Of all thy
hundred remedies, a thousand remedies combined.  
  This is the surest cure for flux, most excellent to heal disease.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604403">3</span></span>Thou art the
stream that Rudra pours, the closest kin of Amrita.  
  Thy name is called Vishānakā: thou sprangest from the Fathers'  
   root, removing illness caused by wind.
