### HYMN IX

#### A charm against rheumatism (vishkondha)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300901">1</span></span>Heaven is
the sire, the mother Earth, of Karsapha and Visapha.  
  As ye have brought them hither, Gods! so do ye move therm  
   hence away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300902">2</span></span>The bands
hold fast without a knot: this is the way that Manu-  
   used.  
  I make Vishkandha impotent as one emasculateth bulls.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300903">3</span></span>Then to a
tawny-coloured string the wise and skilful bind a  
   brush.  
  Let bandages make impotent the strong and active Kābava.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300904">4</span></span>Ye who move
active in your strength like Gods with Asuras'  
   magic powers,  
  Even as the monkey scorns the dogs, Bandages! scorn the  
  Kābava.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300905">5</span></span>Yea, I will
chide thee to thy shame, I will disgrace the Kābava. <span
id="page_a75">p. a75</span>  
  Under our impracations ye, like rapid cars, shall pass away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0300906">6</span></span>One and one
hundred over earth are the Vishkandhas spread  
   abroad.  
  Before these have they fetched thee forth. Vishkandha quelling  
  Amulet.
