### HYMN LI

#### A prayer for purification and forgiveness of sins

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605101">1</span></span>Cleansed by
the filter of the Wind comes Soma past all our  
   enemies, meet friend of Indra.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605102">2</span></span>May the
maternal Waters make us ready: cleanse us with fat-  
   ness they who cleanse with fatness!  
  The Goddesses bear off each blot and tarnish: I come forth  
   from the waters cleansed and stainless.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605103">3</span></span>O Varuna,
whatever the offence may be, the sin which men  
   commit against the heavenly folk— <span id="page_a224">p.
a224</span>  
  When, through our want of thought we violate thy laws, punish  
   us not, O God, for that iniquity.
