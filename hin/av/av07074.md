### HYMN LXXIV

#### A charm to cure pustules, sores, or scrofulous swellings (apachitas)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707401">1</span></span>Black is the
mother, we have heard, from whom the red-hued  
  Pustules sprang.  
  With the divine ascetic's root I pierce and penetrate them all.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707402">2</span></span>I pierce the
foremost one of these, I perforate the middlemost,  
  And here I cut the hindermost asunder like a lock of hair.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707403">3</span></span>With spell
that Tvashtar sent to us I have dispelled thy jealousy.  
  We mitigate and pacify the anger that thou feltest, Lord!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0707404">4</span></span>Lord of
religious rites, by law, anointed, shine thou forth here  
   for ever friendly-minded.  
  So may we all with children, Jātavedas! worship and humbly  
   wait on thee enkindled.
