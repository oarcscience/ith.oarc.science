### HYMN IV

#### A charm to ensure health and prosperity by wearing an amulet

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200401">1</span></span>For length
of life, for mighty joy, uninjured, ever showing  
   strength.  
  We wear Vishkandha's antidote, the Amulet of Jangida.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200402">2</span></span>Amulet of a
thousand powers, Jangida save us, all around.  
  From Jambha, and from Viara, Vishkandha, and tormenting  
   pain.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200403">3</span></span>This
overcomes Vishkandha, this chases the greedy fiends away:  
  May this our panacea, may Jangida save us from distress.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200404">4</span></span>With Jangida
that brings delight, Amulet given by the Gods,  
  We in the conflict overcome Vishkandha and all Rākshasas.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200405">5</span></span>May Cannabis
and Jangida preserve me from Vishkandha,—  
   that  
  Brought to us from the forest, this sprung from the saps of  
   husbandry.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200406">6</span></span>This Amulet
destroys the might of magic and malignity:  
  So may victorious Jangida prolong the years we have to live.
