### HYMN XXIII

#### A prose hymn of homage to various portions of the Atharva-vada classed according to the number of verses which their hymns contain

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902301">1</span></span>Hail to the
four-verse strophes of the Atharvanas!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902302">2</span></span>Hail to the
five-versed! <span id="page_230">p. 230</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902303">3</span></span>Hail to the
six-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902304">4</span></span>Hail to the
seven-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902305">5</span></span>Hail to the
eight-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902306">6</span></span>Hail to the
nine-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902307">7</span></span>Hail to the
ten-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902308">8</span></span>Hail to the
eleven-versed!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902309">9</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902310">10</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902311">11</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902312">12</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902313">13</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902314">14</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902315">15</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902316">16</span></span>Hail,

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902320">20</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902323">23</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902324">24</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902325">25</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902326">26</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902327">27</span></span>Hail to the

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902328">28</span></span>Hail to the
hymns for happiness!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902329">29</span></span>Hail to
Brahma!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902330">30</span></span>Collected
manly powers are topped by Brahma. Brahma at first  
   spread out the loftiest heaven.

   be this Brahma's rival?
