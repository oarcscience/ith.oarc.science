### HYMN IX

#### A man's love-charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600901">1</span></span>Desire my
body, love my feet, love thou mine eyes, and love my  
   legs.  
  Let both thine eyes and hair, fond girl! be dried and parched.  
   through love of me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600902">2</span></span>I make thee
hang upon mine arm, I make thee lie upon my  
   heart.  
  Thou yieldest to my wish, that thou mayst be submissive to my  
   will.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600903">3</span></span>May they
whose kisses are a bond, a love-charm laid within the  
   heart,  
  Mothers of butter, may the cows incline that maid to love of  
   me.
