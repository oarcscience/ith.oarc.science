### HYMN XIX

#### A prayer for purification

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601901">1</span></span>Let the Gods
purify me, let men purify me with a prayer.  
  Cleanse me all creatures that exist! may Pavamāna make me  
   pure.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601902">2</span></span>May Pavamāna
make me pure for wisdom and for power and  
   life, and unassailed security. <span id="page_a208">p. a208</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601903">3</span></span>God Savitar,
byboth of these, filter and pressing out this juice,  
   purify us that we may see.
