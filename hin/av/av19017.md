### HYMN XVII

#### A prayer for protection from dangers in all directions

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901701">1</span></span>Agni from
eastward guard me with the Vasus! To him I go, in  
   him I rest: this fort I seek for refuge.  
  May he protect me, may he be my guardian. I give my soul—All  
   hail! into his keeping.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901702">2</span></span>Vāyu with
Air protect me from this region. To him I go, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901703">3</span></span>May Soma
from the south, with Rudras, guard me. To him, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901704">4</span></span>Varuna with
Ādityas guard me from this region! To him etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901705">5</span></span>Sūrya with
Earth and Heaven from the western region guard me  
   well. To him, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901706">6</span></span>May Waters
joined with Plants protect me from this region. To  
   them I go, in them I rest: this fort I seek for refuge.  
  May they protect me, may they be my guardians. I give my  
   soul—All hail! —into their keeping.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901707">7</span></span>May
Visvakarman with the Seven Rishis be my protector from  
   the northern region. To him, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901708">8</span></span>May Indra,
Marut-girt, protect me from this region. To him,  
   etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901709">9</span></span>Prajāpati,
of generative power, with the Pratishlhā save me from  
   the nadir! To him, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901710">10</span></span>Brihaspati,
joined by the Visvedevas, protect me from the region  
   of the zenith! To him, I go, in him I rest; this fort I seek  
   for refuge.  
  May he protect me, may he be my guardian. I give my soul—  
  All hail!—into his keeping.
