### HYMN LXVI

#### A charm for the destruction and plunder of enemies

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606601">1</span></span>Handless be
every foeman who assaileth, they who with missiles  
   come to fight against us!  
  Dash them together with great slaughter, Indra! and let their  
   robber chief run pierced with arrows.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606602">2</span></span>Ye who run
hither bending bows, brandishing swords and cast-  
   ing darts.  
  Handless be ye, O enemies! Let Indra mangle you to-day.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606603">3</span></span>Handless be
these our enemies! We enervate their languid  
   limbs.  
  So let us part among ourselves, in hundreds, Indra! all their  
   wealth.
