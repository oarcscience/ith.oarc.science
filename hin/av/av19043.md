### HYMN XLIII

#### In praise of Brahma, Prayer, or Devotion

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904301">1</span></span>Whither men
versed in Brahma go, with fervour and the cleans-  
   ing rite,  
  Thither let Agni lead me, let Agni give me intelligence, All hail  
   to Agni!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904302">2</span></span>Whither
etc.  
  Thither let Vāyu lead me, let Vāyu vouchsafe me vital breath.  
  All hail to Vāyu!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904303">3</span></span>Whither,
etc.  
  Thither let Sūrya lead me, let Sūrya vouchsafe me power of  
   sight. All hail to Sürya  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904304">4</span></span>Whither,
etc.  
  Thither let Chandra lead me, let Chandra vouchsafe me intellect.  
  All hail to Chandra!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904305">5</span></span>Whither,
etc.  
  Thither let Soma lead me, let Soma vouchsafe me vital sap. All  
   hail to Soma!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904306">6</span></span>Whither,
etc. <span id="page_246">p. 246</span>  
  Thither let Indra lead me, let Indra bestow upon me power. All  
   hail to Indra!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904307">7</span></span>Whither,
etc.  
  Thither, let Waters lead me, let the Waters give me deathless  
   life. All hail to Waters!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1904308">8</span></span>Whither,
etc.  
  Thither let Brahma lead me, let Brahma give Brahma unto me.  
  All hail to Brahma!
