### HYMN LVI

#### A charm against snakes

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605601">1</span></span>Let not the
serpent slay us, O Gods, with our children and our  
   folk.  
  Let it not close the opened mouth nor open that which now is  
   closed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605602">2</span></span>Be worship
paid unto the black, worship to that with stripes  
   across!  
  To the brown viper reverence, reverence to the demon brood!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605603">3</span></span>I close
together fangs with fang, I close together jaws with jaw.  
  I close together tongue with tongue, I close together mouth  
   with mouth.
