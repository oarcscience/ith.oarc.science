### HYMN CXXXVIII

#### In praise of Indra

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013801">1</span></span>Indra, great
in his power and might and, like Parjanya, rich in  
   rain,  
  Is magnified by Vatsa's lauds,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013802">2</span></span>When the
priests, strengthening the Son of holy Law, present  
   their gifts,  
  Singers with Order's hymn of praise.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013803">3</span></span>Since Kanvas
with their lauds have made Indra complete the  
   sacrifice,  
  Words are their own appropriate arms.
