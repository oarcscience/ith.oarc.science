### HYMN LXXIV

#### A King's charm to secure the fidelity of his people

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607401">1</span></span>Close
gathered be your bodies: be your minds and vows in.  
   unison!  
  Here present Brāhmanaspati and Bhaga have assembled you.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607402">2</span></span>Let there be
union of your minds, let there be union of your  
   hearts:  
  All that is troubled in your lot with this I mend and harmonize.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607403">3</span></span>As, free
from jealousy, the strong Ādityas have been the Vasus'  
   and the Rudras' fellows.  
  So free from jealousy, Lord of Three Titles! cause thou these  
   people here to be one-minded.
