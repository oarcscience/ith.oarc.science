### HYMN LXXXIX

#### A man's love charm

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608901">1</span></span>This
strength that Soma hath bestowed, the head of her who  
   gladdeneth,—  
  With that which thence hath been produced we make thy spirit  
   sorrowful.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608902">2</span></span>We make thy
spirit sorrowful, we fill thy mind with pain and  
   grief.  
  As smoke accompanies the wind, so let thy fancy follow me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608903">3</span></span>May Varuna
and Mitra, may Sarasvati the Goddess,  
  May the centre of the earth, and both her limits bring thee close  
   to me.
