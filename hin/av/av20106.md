### HYMN CVI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010601">1</span></span>That lofty
energy of thine, thy strength and thine intelligence,  
  Thy thunderbolt for which we long, our wish makes keen.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010602">2</span></span>O Indra,
heaven and earth augment thy manly power and thy  
   renown,  
  The waters and the mountains stir and urge thee on. <span
id="page_350">p. 350</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010603">3</span></span>Vishnu, the
lofty Ruling Power, Varuna, Mitra sing thy praise:  
  In thee the Maruts' company hath great delight.
