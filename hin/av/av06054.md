### HYMN LIV

#### A benediction on a newly elected King

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605401">1</span></span>Win the love
of Indra that his friend may reach yet higher state.  
  Increase, as rain the grass, this man's dominion and his lofty  
   fame.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605402">2</span></span>Confirm the
princely power in him, Agni and Soma! grant him  
   wealth.  
  In all the circuit of his rule make him yet higher for your  
   friend.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0605403">3</span></span>The man who
shows us enmity, whether a stranger or akin,  
  Thou wilt give up entire to me who sacrifice and press the juice.
