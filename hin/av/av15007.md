### HYMN VII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1500701">1</span></span>He, having
become moving majesty, went to the ends of the  
   earth. He became the sea. <span id="page_155">p. 155</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1500702">2</span></span>Prajāpati
and Parameshthin and the Father and the Great Father  
   and the Waters and Faith, turned into rain, followed him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1500703">3</span></span>The Waters,
Faith, and rain approach him who possesses this  
   knowledge.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1500704">4</span></span>Faith, and
Sacrifice and the world, having become food and  
   nourishment, turned toward him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1500705">5</span></span>Faith
Sacrifice, the world, food and nourishment approach him  
   who possesses this knowledge.
