### HYMN LXVI

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006601">1</span></span>As Vyasva
did, praise Indra, praise the strong unfluctuating  
   guide.  
  Who gives the foe's possessions to the worshipper.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006602">2</span></span>Now, son of
Vyasva, praise thou him who to the tenth time still  
   is new.  
  The very wise, whom living men must glorify.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006603">3</span></span>Thou
knowest, Indra, thunder-armed, how to avoid destructive  
  Powers,  
  As one secure from pitfalls each succeeding day.
