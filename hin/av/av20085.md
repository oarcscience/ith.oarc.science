### HYMN LXXXV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008501">1</span></span>Glorify
naught besides, O friends; so shall no sorrow trouble  
   you.  
  Praise only mighty Indra when the juice is shed, and say your  
   lauds repeatedly:  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008502">2</span></span>Even him,
eternal, like a bull who rushes down, men's con-  
   queror, bounteous like a cow;  
  Him who is cause of both, of enmity and peace, to both sides  
   most munificent.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008503">3</span></span>Although
these men in sundry ways invoke thee to obtain thine  
   aid.  
  Be this our prayer, addressed, O Indra, unto thee, thine exalta-  
   tion every day.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008504">4</span></span>Those
skilled in song, O Maghavan, among these men o'ercome  
   with might the foeman's songs,  
  Come hither, bring us strength in many a varied form most near  
   that it may succour us.
