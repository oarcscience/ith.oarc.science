### HYMN XCVI

#### A prayer for deliverance from sin and sorrow

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609601">1</span></span>The many
plants of hundred shapes and forms that Soma rules  
   as King, <span id="page_a245">p. a245</span>  
  Commanded by Brihaspati, deliver us from grief and woe!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609602">2</span></span>Let them
release me from the curse and from the noose of  
  Varupa,  
  Free me from Yama's fetter, and from every sin against the  
  Gods!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609603">3</span></span>From every
fault in look, in word, in spirit that we, awake or  
   sleeping, have committed,  
  May Soma, with his godlike nature, cleanse us.
