### HYMN LXV

#### A sacrificial charm against enemies

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606501">1</span></span>The angry
spirit hath relaxed: loose are the arms that act with  
   mind.  
  Do thou, destroyer, overcome and drive these foemen's might  
   away, and then bring opulence to us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606502">2</span></span>The shaft
for handless fiends which, Gods! ye cast against the  
   handless ones,  
  With this, in shape of sacrifice, I rend the arms of enemies.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606503">3</span></span>Indra made
first for Asuras the shaft designed for handless foes:  
  Victorious shall my heroes be with Indra as their constant  
   friend.
