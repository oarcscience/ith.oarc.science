### HYMN IV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000401">1</span></span>Come unto us
who poured the juice, come hither to our eulogies.  
  Drink of the juice, O fair of face.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000402">2</span></span>I pour it
down within thee, so through all thy members let it  
   run.  
  Take with thy tongue the pleasant drink.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000403">3</span></span>Sweet to thy
body let it be, delicious be the savoury juice.  
  Sweet be the Soma to thy heart.
