### HYMN LXVIII

#### A prayer for children and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706801">1</span></span>Sarasvati,
in thy decrees, Goddess, in thy celestial laws,  
  Accept the offered sacrifice, and, Goddess, grant us progeny.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706802">2</span></span>Here is,
Sarasvati, thy fat libation, this sacrifice passing to the  
   mouth of Fathers.  
  These most auspicious offerings have ascended to thee: through,  
   these may we be full of sweetness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706803">3</span></span>Be kind and
most auspicious, be gracious to us, Sarasvati, May  
   we be ever in thy sight.
