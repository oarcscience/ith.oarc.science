### HYMN XXXIV

#### A prayer for freedom from sin and the overthrow of enemies

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0703401">1</span></span>Agni, drive
off my rivals born and living, repel those yet unborn,  
  O Jātavedas.  
  Cast down beneath my feet mine adversaries. In Aditi's regard  
   may we be sinless.
