### HYMN CVII

#### A charm to protect men and cattle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610701">1</span></span>Entrust me,
Visvajit, to Trāyamānā.  
  Guard, Trāyamānā, all our men, guard all our wealth of  
   quadrupeds.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610702">2</span></span>To Visvajit
entrust me, Trāyamānā.  
  O Visvajit, guard all our men, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610703">3</span></span>To Visvajit
entrust me, O Kalyāni.  
  Guard, O Kalyāni, all our men, etc. <span id="page_a250">p.
a250</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610704">4</span></span>To Sarvavid
entrust me, O Kalyāni.  
  O Sarvavid, guard all our men, guard all our wealth of  
   quadrupeds.
