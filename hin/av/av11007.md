### HYMN VII

#### A glorification of the Uchchhishta or Residue of Sacrifice

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100701">1</span></span>The Residue
of Sacrifice containeth name, and from, and world:  
  Indra and Agni and the whole universe are comprised therein.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100702">2</span></span>The Residue
of Sacrifice holdeth Earth, Heaven, and all that is:  
  The Residue of Sacrifice holdeth sea, waters, Moon, and Wind.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100703">3</span></span>Real,
non-real, both are there, Prajāpati, and Death, and  
   strength:  
  Thereon depend the worldly ones: in me are glory Dra and Vra.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100704">4</span></span>The firm,
the fast, the strong, the hard, Brahma, the All-creating  
   Ten.  
  Gods, as a wheel about the nave, are fixed all round the  
   Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100705">5</span></span>Verse, Song,
and Sacrificial Text, chanting, the prelude, and the  
   laud,  
  The hum is in the Residue, the tone, the murmur of the psalm.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100706">6</span></span>Within the
Residue, like babes unborn, the parts of sacrifice,  
  Aindrāgne Pāvamāna lie. Mahānāmnī, Mahavrata.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100707">7</span></span>The
Vājapeya, Royal Rite, the Agnishoma and its forms,  
  Hymns, joyfullest with living grass the Asvamedha, are therein,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100708">8</span></span>Dikshā and
Agnyādheya rite that sates the wish, with magic-  
   hymn,  
  Suspended rites, long sessions, are contained within the Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100709">9</span></span>Faith
fire-oblation, fervent zeal, service, and sacrificial cry,  
  Guerdon, good works and their reward, are stored within the  
  Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100710">10</span></span>Sacrifice
of one night, or two, Sadya1 kri, Ukthya, and Prakri,  
  Call, deep-toned summons are therein, fine parts, through lore,  
   of sacrifice,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100711">11</span></span>Sacrifice
of four nights, of five, of six nights, day and night  
   conjoined,  
  Shodai, seven-night sacrifice, all these sprang from the Residue,  
   these which the Immortal One contains. <span id="page_62">p.
62</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100712">12</span></span>Pratihāra
and Nidhanam, the Visvajit, the Abhijit,  
  The two Sāhnātirātrās and Twelve-day rite are stored therein.  
<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_1100713">13</span></span>Pleasantness, reverence, peace, and
power, strength, vigour,  
   immortality  
  All forward wishes are with love satisfied in the Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100714">14</span></span>Nine
several oceans, earths, and skies are set within, the Residue,  
  Bright shines the Sun therein, in me, the Residue, are Day and  
  Night.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100715">15</span></span>The Residue
the Father's sire, who bears this universe, supports  
  Vishūvān, Upahavya, and all worship offered secretly.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100716">16</span></span>The
Father's sire, the Residue, grandson of Spirit, primal Sire,  
  Lord of the universe, the Bull, dwells on the earth victorious.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100717">17</span></span>Right,
truth, dominion, fervent zeal, toil, duty, action, future,  
   past,  
  Valour; prosperity, and strength dwell in the Residue in  
   strength.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100718">18</span></span>Welfare,
resolve and energy, the six expanses, kingship, sway,  
  Prayer, and direction, and the year, oblation, planets, are there-  
   in;  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100719">19</span></span>And the
four Hotars, Apri hymns, the Nivids, and Four-  
   monthly rites,  
  Oblations, sacrifices, and animal offerings, and their forms.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100720">20</span></span>Months,
half-months, sections of the year, seasons are in the  
  Residue,  
  The waters resonant afar, the thunder, and the mighty noise.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100721">21</span></span>Pebbles,
sand, stones, and herbs, and plants, and grass are in  
   the Residue,  
  Closely embraced and laid therein are lightnings and the clouds  
   and rain.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100722">22</span></span>Gain,
acquisition, and success, fulness, complete prosperity.  
  Great gain and wealth, are laid, concealed and treasured, in the  
  Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100723">23</span></span>All things
that breathe the breath of life, all creatures that have  
   eyes to see,  
  All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the  
  Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100724">24</span></span>Verses, and
Songs, and magic hymns, Purāna, sacrificial text.  
  All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the  
  Residue. <span id="page_63">p. 63</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100725">25</span></span>Inbreath
and outbreath, eye and ear, decay and freedom from  
   decay,  
  All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the  
   Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100726">26</span></span>All
pleasures and enjoyments, all delights and rapturous ecsta-  
   sies,  
  All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the  
   Residue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100727">27</span></span>The
Deities, the Fathers, men, Gandharvas, and Apsarases.  
  6 All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the  
  Residue.
