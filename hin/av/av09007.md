### HYMN VII

#### A glorification of the typically bull and cow

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900701">1</span></span>Prajapati
and Parameshthin are the two horns, Indra is the  
   head, Agni the forehead, Yama the joint of the neck.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900702">2</span></span>King Soma is
the brain, Sky is the upper jaw, Earth is the  
   lower jaw.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900703">3</span></span>Lightning is
the tongue, the Maruts are the teeth, Revati is the  
   neck, the Krittikās are the shoulders, the Gharma s the  
   shoulder-bar.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900704">4</span></span>His universe
is Vāyu, Svarga is his world, Krishpadram is the  
   tendons and Vertebrae. <span id="page_a378">p. a378</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900705">5</span></span>The Syena
ceremony is the breast, Air is the region of the belly,.  
  Brihaspati is the hump, Brihatī the breast-bone and cartilages  
   of the ribs.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900706">6</span></span>The consorts
of the Gods are the ribs, the attendants are ribs.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900707">7</span></span>Mitra and
Varuna are the shoulder-blades. Tvashtar and Arya-  
   man the fore-arms, Mahādeva is the arms.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900708">8</span></span>Indrāni is
the hinder parts, Vāyu the tail, Pavamāna the hair.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900709">9</span></span>Priestly
rank and princely power are the hips, and strength is.  
   the thigh.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900710">10</span></span>Dhātar and
Savitar are the two knee-bones, the Gandharvas are  
   the legs the Apsarases are bits of the feet, Aditi is the hooves.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900711">11</span></span>Thought is
the heart, intelligence is the liver, law the pericar-  
   dium.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900712">12</span></span>Hunger is
the belly, refreshing drink is the rectum, mountains.  
   are the inward parts.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900713">13</span></span>Wrath is
the kidneys, anger the testes, offspring the generative  
   organ.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900714">14</span></span>The river
is the womb, the Lords of the Rain are the breasts,.  
   the thunder is the udder.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900715">15</span></span>The
All-embracing (Aditi) is the hide, the herbs are her hair,.  
   and the Lunar Mansions her form.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900716">16</span></span>The hosts
of Gods are her entrails, man are her bowels, and  
   demons her abdomen.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900717">17</span></span>Rākshasas
are the blood, the Other Folk are the contents of the  
  Stomach.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900718">18</span></span>The
rain-cloud is her fat, her resting-place her marrow.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900719">19</span></span>Sitting he
is Agni, when he hath stood up he is the Asvins.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900720">20</span></span>Standing
east-wards he is Indra, standing southwards, Yama.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900721">21</span></span>Standing
westwards he is Dhātar, standing northwards Savitar.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900722">22</span></span>When he
hath got his grass he is King Soma.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900723">23</span></span>He is Mitra
when he looks about him, and when he hath turned  
   round he is joy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900724">24</span></span>When he is
yoking he belongs to the All-Gods, when yoked he  
   is Prajāpati, when unyoked he is All.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900725">25</span></span>This verily
is omniform, wearing all forms, bovine-formed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0900726">26</span></span>Upon him
wait omniform beasts, wearing every shape, each one  
   who hath this knowledge.
