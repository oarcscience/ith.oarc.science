### HYMN XXXIX

#### A sacrificial charm for rain and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0703901">1</span></span>May he
establish in our home the master of riches, gladdening  
   with rain in season,  
  Mighty, strong-winged, celestial, dropping moisture, Bull of the  
    plants and embryo of waters.
