### HYMN XIV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001401">1</span></span>We call on
thee, O peerless One. We, seeking help, possessing  
   nothing firm ourselves,  
  Call on thee wonderful in fight.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001402">2</span></span>On thee for
aid in sacrifice. This youth of ours, the bold, the  
   mighty, hath gone forth.  
  We, therefore, we thy friends, Indra, have chosen thee, free-  
   giver, as our guardian God.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001403">3</span></span>Him who of
old hath brought to us this and that blessing, him  
  I magnify for you,  
  Even Indra, O my friends, for help:  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2001404">4</span></span>Borne by bay
steeds, the Lord of heroes, ruling men, for it is he  
   who takes delight. <span id="page_274">p. 274</span>  
  The Bounteous Lord bestows on us his worshippers hundreds of  
   cattle and of steeds.
