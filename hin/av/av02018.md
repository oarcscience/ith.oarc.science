### HYMN XVIII

#### A charm against enemies, goblins, and other evil creatures

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201801">1</span></span>Destruction
of the foe art thou, give me the scaring of my foes.  
  All hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201802">2</span></span>The rival's
ruiner art thou, give me to drive my rivals off. All  
   hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201803">3</span></span>Arāyis'
ruiner art thou, give me to drive Arāyis off. All hail! <span

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201804">4</span></span>Pisa-chas'
ruiner art thou, give me to drive Pisāchas off. All  
   hail!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201805">5</span></span>Sadānvās'
ruiner art thou, give me to drive Sadānvās off. All  
   hail!
