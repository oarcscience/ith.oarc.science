### HYMN XXIX

#### A charm for the destruction of enemies, continued from 28

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902901">1</span></span>Pierce thou
my rivals, Darbha, pierce the men who fain would  
   fight with me.  
  Pierce all who wish me evil, pierce the men who hate me,.  
  Amulet!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902902">2</span></span>Split thou
my rivals, Darbha, etc. (as in 1, with 'split' for  
  'pierce' throughout).  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902903">3</span></span>Check thou,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902904">4</span></span>Crush thou,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902905">5</span></span>Shake thou,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902906">6</span></span>Bruise thou,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902907">7</span></span>Burn thou,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902908">8</span></span>Consume,
etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902909">9</span></span>Slay thou my
rivals. Darbha, slay the men who fain would fight  
   with me.  
  Slay all who wish me evil, slay the men who hate me, Amulet.
