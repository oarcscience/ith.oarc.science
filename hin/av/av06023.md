### HYMN XXIII

#### To the Waters

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602301">1</span></span>Here flow
the restless ones, they flow unceasing through the day  
   and night,  
  Most excellently wise I call the Goddess Waters hitherward.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602302">2</span></span>Let the deft
Waters, summoned, give permission that we bear  
   them off,  
  And quickly set us on our way.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602303">3</span></span>Let all the
people celebrate the rite of Savitar the God.  
  Sweet unto us be Waters, Plants propitious!
