### HYMN XXVIII

#### A charm for the destruction of enemies

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902801">1</span></span>On thee I
bind this Amulet for lengthened life and brilliancy.  
  The rival-quelling Darbha grass that burns the spirit of a foe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902802">2</span></span>Burning the
spirit of the foe, vexing the heart of enemies.  
  Darbha, on every side, like heat, inflame all evil-hearted men.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902803">3</span></span>O Darbha,
burning round like heat, consuming foes, O Amulet,.  
  Like Indra rending Vala cleave mine adversaries' hearts in twain.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902804">4</span></span>Cleave
through. O Darbha, Amulet, my foes', mine adversaries'.  
   heart.  
  Rise thou and batter down their heads like growth that coverethl  
   the earth.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902805">5</span></span>Cleave thou
my rivals, Darbha, cleave the men who fain would,  
   fight with me.  
  Cleave all who wish me evil, cleave the men who hate me,.  
  Amulet!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902806">6</span></span>Wound thou,
my rivals, Darbha, etc. (as in 5, substituting.  
  'wound' for cleave.)  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902807">7</span></span>Tear thou my
rivals, Darbha, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902808">8</span></span>How thou my
rivals, Darbha, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902809">9</span></span>Carve thou
my rivals, Darbha, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1902810">10</span></span>Pierce thou
my rivals, Darbha, pierce the men who fain would  
   fight with me.  
  Pierce those who wish me evil, pierce the men who hate me,  
   Amulet!
