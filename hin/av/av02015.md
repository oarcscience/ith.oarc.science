### HYMN XV

#### A charm against fear

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201601">1</span></span>As Heaven
and Earth are not afraid, and never suffer loss or  
   harm,  
  Even so, my spirit, fear not thou.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201602">2</span></span>As Day and
Night are not afraid, nor ever suffer loss or harm,  
  Even so, my spirit, fear not thou.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201603">3</span></span>As Sun and
Moon are not afraid, nor ever suffer loss or harm.  
  Even so. my spirit, fear not thou.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201604">4</span></span>As
Brāhmanhood and Princely Power fear not, nor suffer loss,  
   or harm,  
  Even so, my spirit, fear not thou.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201605">5</span></span>As Truth and
Falsehood have no fear, nor ever suffer loss or  
   harm,  
  Even so, my spirit, fear not thou.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0201606">6</span></span>As What Hath
Been and What Shall Be fear not, nor suffer loss  
   or harm,  
  Even so, my spirit, fear not thou.
