# BOOK XX

### HYMN I

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000101">1</span></span>Thee, Indra,
we invoke, the Bull, what time the Soma hath been  
   pressed.  
  Drink of the sweetly-flavoured juice.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000102">2</span></span>The best of
guardian hath the man within whose dwelling-place  
   ye drink,  
  O Maruts, giants of the sky.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000103">3</span></span>Let us serve
Agni with our hyms, Sage who consumeth ox and  
   cow,  
  Who beareth Soma on his back.
