### HYMN LX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006001">1</span></span>For so thou
art the hero's Friend, a Warrior too art thou, and  
   strong:  
  So may thy heart be won to us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006002">2</span></span>So hath the
offering; wealthiest Lord, been paid by all the  
   worshippers:  
  So dwell thou, Indra, even with me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006003">3</span></span>Be not thou,
like a slothful priest, O Lord of wealth and spoil:  
   rejoice.  
  In the pressed Soma blent with milk.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006004">4</span></span>So also is
his excellence, great copious, rich in cattle, like  
  A ripe branch to the worshipper. <span id="page_313">p. 313</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006005">5</span></span>For verily
thy mighty powers, Indra, are saving helps at once  
  Unto a worshipper like me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006006">6</span></span>So are his
lovely gifts: let laud be said and praise to Indra sung.  
  That he may drink the Soma juice.
