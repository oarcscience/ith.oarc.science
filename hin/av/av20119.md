### HYMN CXIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011901">1</span></span>An ancient
praise-song hath been sung: to Indra have ye said  
   the prayer.  
  They have sung many a Brihatī of sacrifice, poured forth th,  
   worshipper's many thoughts.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011902">2</span></span>In zealous
haste the singers have sung forth a song distilling oil  
   and rich in sweets.  
  Riches have spread among us, and heroic strength; with us are  
   flowing Soma drops.
