### HYMN II

#### A charm to ensure success in gambling

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200201">1</span></span>Lord of the
World, divine Gandharva, only he should be  
   honoured in the Tribes and worshipped.  
  Fast with my spell, celestial God, I hold thee. Homage to thee!  
  Thy home is in the heavens.  
<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_0200202">2</span></span>Sky-reaching, like the Sun in brightness,
holy, he who averts  
   from us the Gods' displeasure.  
  Lord of the World, may the Gandharva bless us, the friendly  
  God who only must be worshipped.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200203">3</span></span>I came, I
met these faultless, blameless beings: among the  
  Apsarases was the Gandharva.  
  Their home is in the sea—so men have told me,—whence they  
   come quickly hitherward and vanish.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200204">4</span></span>Thou,
Cloudy! ye who follow the Gandharva Visvā-vasu, ye,  
  Starry! Lightning-Flasher!  
  You, O ye Goddesses, I truly worship.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200205">5</span></span>Haunters of
darkness, shrill in voice, dice-lovers, maddeners of  
   the mind  
  To these have I paid homage, the Gandharva's wives, Apsarases.
