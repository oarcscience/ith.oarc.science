### HYMN VI

#### A prayer to all Divinities and Sanctities for deliverance from distress

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100601">1</span></span>We call on
Agni, on the trees lords of the forest, herbs and.  
   plants,  
  Indra, Sūrya, Brihaspati: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100602">2</span></span>We call on
Vishnu, Bhaga, on Mitra and Varuna the King,  
  Ansa Vivasvān we address: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100603">3</span></span>We call on
Savitar the God, on Pūshan the establisher, <span id="page_59">p.
59</span>  
  Tvashtar the foremost we address: may they deliver us from  
   woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100604">4</span></span>Gandharvas
and Apsarases; the Asvins, Brāhmanaspati,  
  Aryaman, God, by name we call: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100605">5</span></span>This word of
ours to Day and Night, and to the Sun and Moon  
   we speak,  
  All the Ādityas we address: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100606">6</span></span>Vāta,
Parjanya we address, the Quarters, and the Firmament,  
  And all the Regions of the sky: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100607">7</span></span>From all
that brings a curse may Day and Night and Dawn  
   deliver me,  
  May Soma free me, God to whom they give the name of Chan-  
   dramās.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100608">8</span></span>All
creatures both of heaven and earth, wild beasts and sylvan  
   animals,  
  And winged birds of air we call: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100609">9</span></span>Bhava and
Sarva we address, and Rudra who is Lord of Beasts,  
  Their arrows which we feel and know: may they be ever kind to  
   us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100610">10</span></span>We speak to
Constellations, Heaven, to Earth, to Genii, and to  
  Hills,  
  To Seas, to Rivers, and to Lakes: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100611">11</span></span>Or the
Seven Rishis we address, Waters divine, Prajāpati,  
  Fathers with Yama at their head: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100612">12</span></span>Gods whose
abode is in the heaven and those who dwell in  
   middle air,  
  And Mighty ones who rest on earth: may they deliver us from.  
   woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100613">13</span></span>Ādityas,
Rudra, Vasus, Gods aloft in heaven, Atharvan's sons,  
  The sages, sons of Angiras: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100614">14</span></span>To
sacrifice, to worshipper, hymns, songs, and healing charms,  
   we speak,  
  To priestly acts and Yajus texts: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100615">15</span></span>To the five
kingdoms of the plants which Soma rules as Lord we  
   speak.  
  Darbha, hemp, barley, mighty power: may these deliver us from  
   woe,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100616">16</span></span>To demons
and fierce fiends we speak, to Holy Genii, Fathers,.  
  Snakes, <span id="page_60">p. 60</span>  
  And to the hundred deaths and one: may these deliver us from  
   woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100617">17</span></span>We speak to
Seasons, Season-Lords, to years and sections of the  
   year,  
  To Months, half-months, and years complete: may they deliver  
   us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100618">18</span></span>Come hither
from the south, ye Gods, rise and come forward  
   from the west.  
  Gathered together, all ye Gods, ye mighty Ones, from east and  
   and north: may they deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100619">19</span></span>This we
address to all the Gods, faithful, maintainers of the  
  Right,  
  With all their Consorts by their side: may they deliver us from  
   woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100620">20</span></span>We speak to
the collected Gods, faithful, maintainers of the  
  Right.  
  Present with their collective Dames: may these deliver us from  
   woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100621">21</span></span>The spirit,
yea, the spirits' Lord, ruler of spirits, we address.  
  Together let all spirits meet: may these deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100622">22</span></span>The five
Sky regions, Goddesses, and the twelve Seasons which  
   are Gods.  
  The teeth of the completed year, may these deliver us from woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1100623">23</span></span>The
deathless balm that Mātalī knows, purchased at a chariot's  
   price,  
  Indra effused into the floods. Waters, give us that healing  
   balm!
