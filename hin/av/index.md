This is the Ralph T.H. Griffith translation of the Atharvaveda. The
Atharvaveda is a Vedic-era collection of spells, prayers, charms, and
hymns. There are prayers to protect crops from lightning and drought,
charms against venomous serpents, love spells, healing spells, hundreds
of verses, some derived from the Rig veda, all very ancient.

This is the first time a complete translation of the Atharvaveda has
been posted on the Internet. The Maurice Bloomfield partial translation,
previously at this location, has been moved to [the sbe42
directory](../sbe42/index.md).
