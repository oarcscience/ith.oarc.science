### HYMN XXIX

#### A charm to avert misfortune foreshown by the coming of a dove and an owl

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602901">1</span></span>On these men
yonder fall the winged missile: the screeching of  
   the Owl is ineffective,  
  And that the Dove beside the fire hath settled. <span
id="page_a213">p. a213</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602902">2</span></span>Thine envoys
who came hither, O Destruction, sent or not sent  
   by thee unto our dwelling,  
  The Dove and Owl, effectless be their visit!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602903">3</span></span>Oft may it
fly to us to save our heroes from slaughter, oft perch  
   here to bring fair offspring,  
  Turn thee and send thy voice afar: cry to the region far away;  
  That I may see thee in the home of Yama reft of all thy power,  
   that I may see thee impotent.
