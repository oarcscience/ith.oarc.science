### HYMN XV

#### A charm for general prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0501501">1</span></span>Plant! I
have those who shall avert the threatened danger, ten  
   and one. <span id="page_a173">p. a173</span>  
  O sacred Plant, produced aright! make sweetness, sweet thy self,  
   for me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0501502">2</span></span>Twenty and
two, O Plant, have I who shall avert the threatened  
   ill.  
  O sacred Plant, produced aright! make sweetness, sweet thyself,  
   for me.
