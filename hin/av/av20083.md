### HYMN LXXXIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008301">1</span></span>O Indra,
grant a happy home, a triple refuge, triply strong.  
  Bestow a dwelling-place on the rich lords and me, and keep thy  
   dart afar from these.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008302">2</span></span>They who
with minds intent on spoil subdue the foe, boldly  
   attack and smite him down.  
  From these, O Indra, Bounteous Lord who lovest song, be  
   closest guardian of our lives.
