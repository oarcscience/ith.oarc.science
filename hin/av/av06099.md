### HYMN XCIX

#### A prayer for protection in battle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609901">1</span></span>Indra,
before affliction comes, I call thee from the wide expanse.  
  The mighty guardian, born alone, wearer of many names, I call.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609902">2</span></span>Whatever
deadly missile launched to-day flies forth to slaughter  
   us.  
  We take both arms of Indra to encompass us on every side.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609903">3</span></span>We draw
about us both the arms of Indra, our deliverer. May  
   they protect us thoroughly.  
  O Savitar, thou God, O royal Soma, make thou me pious-  
   minded for my welfare.
