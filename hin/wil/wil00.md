IN sending this book out into the world, I desire to record my thanks to
Mr. Romesh Chunder Dutt for his constant interest and encouragement, and
also to Prof. Patrick Geddes, who, by teaching me to understand a little
of Europe, indirectly gave me a method by which to read my Indian
experiences.
