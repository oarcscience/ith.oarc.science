# THIRTY MINOR UPANISHAḌS

###### TRANSLATED BY

## K. NĀRĀYAṆASVAMI AIYAR

###### Translator of

##### Laghu Yoga Vāsishtha, Vāsuḍevamanana

###### &c. &c. &c.

#### MADRAS

#### \[1914\]

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage by John
Bruno Hare, November 2009. This text is in the public domain in the US
because it was published prior to 1923.

TO

**THE ṚSHIS OF INDIA**

WHO BY TREADING THE PATH

OF

THE UPANISHAḌS HAVE PERFECTED THEMSELVES

AND

REALISED THE GOAL

THIS HUMBLE EFFORT

<span class="smallcaps">To spread Their Ancient Teachings in a Modern
Garb</span>

IS DEDICATED

BY ONE

WHO LOVES AND WORSHIPS THEM

<span class="smallcaps">And tries to walk in Their Footsteps</span>

<span class="smallcaps">As Their Humble and Devoted Disciple</span>
