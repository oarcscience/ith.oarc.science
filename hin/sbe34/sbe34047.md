10\. And on account of the topic under discussion.

That the highest Self only can be the eater referred to

<span id="page_118">p. 118</span>

is moreover evident from the passage (Ka. Up. I, 2, 18), ('The knowing
Self is not born, it dies not'), which shows that the highest Self is
the general topic. And to adhere to the general topic is the proper
proceeding. Further, the clause, 'Who then knows where he is,' shows
that the cognition is connected with difficulties; which circumstance
again points to the highest Self.
