45\. And on account of contradictions.

Moreover, manifold contradictions are met with in the Bhâgavata system,
with reference to the assumption of qualities and their bearers.
Eminence of knowledge and

<span id="page_443">p. 443</span>

ruling capacity, strength, valour, and glory are enumerated as
qualities, and then they are in some other place spoken of as Selfs,
holy Vâsudevas, and so on.--Moreover, we meet with passages
contradictory of the Veda. The following passage, for instance, blames
the Veda, 'Not having found the highest bliss in the Vedas *S*â*nd*ilya.
studied this *s*âstra.'--For this reason also the Bhâgavata doctrine
cannot be accepted.
