### SECOND ANUVÂKA.

1\. Om <span id="fr_199"></span>[1](#fn_199.md)! Let us explain *S*îkshâ,
the doctrine of pronunciation, viz. letter, accent, quantity, effort (in
the formation of letters), modulation, and union of letters (sandhi).
This is the lecture on *S*îkshâ.
