### FIFTH ANUVÂKA.

He perceived that understanding (vi*gñ*âna) was Brahman, for from
understanding these beings are born; by understanding, when born, they
live; into understanding they enter at their death.

Having perceived this, he went again to his father Varu*n*a,
saying--'Sir, teach me Brahman.' He said to him: 'Try to know Brahman by
penance, for penance is (the means of knowing) Brahman.'
