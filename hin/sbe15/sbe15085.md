### SEVENTH BRÂHMANA.

1\. They say that lightning is Brahman, because lightning (vidyut) is
called so from cutting off (vidânât) <span
id="fr_562"></span>[3](#fn_562.md). Whosoever knows this, that lightning is
Brahman, him (that Brahman) cuts off from evil, for lightning indeed is
Brahman.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_562"></span>[192:3](sbe15085.htm#fr_562.md) From do,
avakha*nd*ane, to cut; the lightning cutting through the darkness of the
clouds, as Brahman, when known, cuts through the darkness of ignorance.
