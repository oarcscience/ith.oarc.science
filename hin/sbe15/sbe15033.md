### TWELFTH ANUVÂKA.

1\. May Mitra be propitious to us, and Varu*n*a, Aryaman also, Indra,
B*ri*haspati, and the wide-striding Vish*n*u! Adoration to Brahman!
Adoration to thee, O Vâyu! Thou indeed art the visible Brahman. I
proclaimed thee alone as the visible Brahman.

(1-5) I proclaimed the right. I proclaimed the true. It protected me. It
protected the teacher. Yes, it protected me, it protected the teacher.
Om! Peace! peace! peace!
