### SIXTH BRÂHMA*N*A.

1\. That person, under the form of mind (manas), being light
indeed <span id="fr_561"></span>[2](#fn_561), is within the heart, small
like a grain of rice or barley. He is the ruler of all, the lord of
all--he rules all this, whatsoever exists.
