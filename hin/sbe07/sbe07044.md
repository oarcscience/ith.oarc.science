# XLII.

1\. Miscellaneous crimes are those which have not been mentioned before.

2\. Having committed one out of the number of miscellaneous crimes, a
prudent man should always perform a penance, by the advice of a
Brâhma*n*a, after the higher or less degree of his guilt has been
ascertained.
