VII, 59, 12.

bandhu-eshá: bandhu-eshé, when there was inquiry for their kindred, V,
52, 16.

babhrú, tawny: babhrú*h*, II, 33, 5; 9; babhráve, II, 33, 8; babhro, II,

barhá*n*â, weapon, bolt: barhá*n*â, I, 166, 6:6;

77, 3.

barhís, grass-pile, altar: barhí*h*, I, 85, 6; VII, 57, 2:1; 59, 6;
barhíshi, I, 85, 7; 86, 4; VII, 46, 4:1.

bahulá, manifold: bahulám, V, 55, 9.

bâhvó*h*, V, 57, 6.

bî´*g*a, seed: bî´*g*am, V, 53, 13.

budh, to awake: Sâya*n*a, bodhi = budhyasva, (II, 33, 15:2.)—prá

budhnyã, hidden: budhnyẫ, VII, 56, 14:1.
