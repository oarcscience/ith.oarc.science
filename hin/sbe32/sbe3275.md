khâd, to chew up: khâdatha, I, 64, 7:2.

khâdí, ring, quoit: khâdí*h*, I, 168, 3.—khâdáya*h*, I, 166, 9:2; V, 54,
11; VII, 56, 13; khâdíshu, V, 53, 4.—(I, 64, 10:2; II, 34, 2:1.)

khâdín, having rings: khâdína*h*, II, 34, 2:1.

khâ´di-hasta, adorned with rings on their hands, or, holding the quoits
in their hands: khâ´di-hastam, V, 58, 2; (I, 166, 9:2.)
