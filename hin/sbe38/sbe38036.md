35\. On account of its taking.

The quality of being an agent has to be attributed to the soul for that
reason also that in the same chapter treating of the soul the text
speaks of the soul taking its instruments, 'Having taken, through the
intelligence of the senses, intelligence,' and 'having taken the senses'
(B*ri*. Up. II. 1, 18; 17).
