10\. (The direct statement is) non-comprehensive.

The direct scriptural statement implied in 'What a man does with
knowledge' &c. does not refer to all knowledge, as it is connected with
the knowledge forming the subject-matter of the section. And the latter
is the knowledge of the udgîtha only, 'Let a man meditate on the
syllable Om (as) the udgîtha.'
