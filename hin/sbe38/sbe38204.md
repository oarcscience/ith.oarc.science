61\. With the (meditations on) members (of sacrificial acts) it is as
with their abodes.

Are those meditations--enjoined in the three Vedas--which rest on
members of sacrificial actions such as the

<span id="page_282">p. 282</span>

udgîtha to be superadded to each other, or may we proceed with regard to
them as we like?--To this doubt the Sûtra replies, 'it is according to
the abodes.' As the abiding-places of those meditations, viz. the Stotra
and so on, are combined (for the performance of the sacrifice), so those
meditations also. For a meditation is subject to what it rests on.
