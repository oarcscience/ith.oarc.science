13\. There being no specification (the rule does) not (specially apply
to him who knows).

In passages such as 'Performing works here let a man live' &c., which
state definite rules, there is no specification

<span id="page_294">p. 294</span>

of him who knows, since the definite rule is enjoined without any such
specification.
