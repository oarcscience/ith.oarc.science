28\. Or else like that of light to its substratum, both being fire.

Or else the relation of the two may be viewed as follows. Just as the
light of the sun and its substratum, i.e. the sun himself, are not
absolutely different--for they both consist of fire--and yet are spoken
of as different, so also the soul and the highest Self.
