48\. On account of his being all, however, there is winding up with the
householder.

The word 'however' is meant to lay stress on the householder's being
everything. For the performance of many works belonging to his own
â*s*rama, such as sacrifices and the like, which involve not a little
trouble, is enjoined on him by scripture; and at the same time the
duties of the other â*s*ramas--such as tenderness for all living
creatures, restraint of the senses and so on--are incumbent on him also
as far as circumstances allow. There is therefore nothing contradictory

<span id="page_325">p. 325</span>

in the *Kh*ândogya winding up with the householder.
