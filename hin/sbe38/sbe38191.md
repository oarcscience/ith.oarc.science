48\. And because (indicatory marks of that) are seen (in the text).

And that there are to be observed indicatory marks leading to the same
conclusion, has already been declared in Sûtra 44.--But, under Sûtra 45,
it was shown that indicatory marks unaided by other reasons cannot be
admitted as proving anything, and it was consequently determined that,
owing to the influence of the leading subject-matter, the Agnis form
part of the sacrificial action!--To this objection the next Sûtra
replies.
