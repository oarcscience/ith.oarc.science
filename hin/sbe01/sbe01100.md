### SIXTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'The altar is the earth, O Gautama; its fuel is the year itself, the
smoke the ether, the light the night, the coals the quarters, the sparks
the intermediate quarters.

2\. 'On that altar the Devas (prâ*n*as) offer rain. From that oblation
rises food (corn, &c.)
