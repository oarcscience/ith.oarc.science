### SEVENTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'The altar is man, O Gautama; its fuel speech itself, the smoke the
breath, the light the tongue, the coals the eye, the sparks the ear.

2\. 'On that altar the Devas (prâ*n*as) offer food. From that oblation
rises seed.
