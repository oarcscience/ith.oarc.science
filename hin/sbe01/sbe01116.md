### TWENTY-SECOND KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'And he who offers the fourth oblation, should offer it to Samâna
(on-breathing), saying Svâhâ. Then Samâna is satisfied,

2\. 'If Samâna is satisfied, the mind is satisfied, if the mind is
satisfied, Par*g*anya (god of rain) is satisfied, if Par*g*anya is
satisfied, lightning is satisfied, if lightning is satisfied, whatever
is under Par*g*anya and under lightning is satisfied. And through their

<span id="page_91">p. 91</span>

satisfaction he (the sacrificer or cater) himself is satisfied with
offspring, cattle, health, brightness, and Vedic splendour.
