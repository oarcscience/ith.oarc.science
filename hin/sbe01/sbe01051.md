### SEVENTEENTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. The hiṅkâra is the earth, the prastâva the sky, the udgîtha heaven,
the pratihâra the regions, the nidhana the sea. These are the *S*akvarî
Sâmans, as interwoven in the worlds <span
id="fr_148"></span>[1](#fn_147.md).

2\. He who thus knows the *S*akvarîs, as interwoven in the worlds,
becomes possessed of the worlds, he reaches the full life, he lives
long, becomes great with children and cattle, great by fame. His rule
is, 'Never complain of the worlds.'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_147"></span>[31:1](sbe01051.htm#fr_148.md) The *S*akvarîs are

said to rest on water.
