### FOURTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. Let a man meditate on the fivefold Sâman in all waters. When the
clouds gather, that is the hiṅkâra; when it rains, that is the prastâva
that which flows in the east <span id="fr_129"></span>[2](#fn_128.md), that
is the udgîtha that which flows in the west <span
id="fr_130"></span>[3](#fn_129.md), that is the pratihâra the sea is the
nidhana.

2\. He does not die in water <span id="fr_131"></span>[4](#fn_130.md), nay,
he is rich in

<span id="page_25">p. 25</span>

water who knowing this meditates on the fivefold Sâman as all waters.
