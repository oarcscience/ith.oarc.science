### SIXTEENTH KHA*ND*A <span id="fr_319"></span>[1](sbe01152.htm#fn_317.md).

1\. 'But in reality he is an ativâdin who declares the Highest Being to
be the True (Satya, τὸ ὄντως ὄν).'

'Sir, may I become an ativâdin by the True?'

'But we must desire to know the True.'

'Sir, I desire to know the True.'
