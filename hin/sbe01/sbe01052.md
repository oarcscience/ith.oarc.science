### EIGHTEENTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. The hiṅkâra is goats, the prastâva sheep, the udgîtha cows, the
pratihâra horses, the nidhana man. These are the Revatî Sâmans, as
interwoven in animals.

2\. He who thus knows these Revatîs, as interwoven in animals, becomes
rich in animals <span id="fr_149"></span>[2](#fn_148), he reaches the
full life, he lives long, becomes great with children and cattle, great
by fame. His rule is, 'Never complain of animals.'
