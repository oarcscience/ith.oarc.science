## VI, 25. Charm against scrofulous sores upon neck and shoulders.

1\. The five and fifty (sores) that gather together upon the nape of the
neck, from here they all shall pass away, as the pustules of the
(disease called) apakit!  
2. The seven and seventy (sores) that gather together upon the neck,
from here they all shall pass away, as the pustules of the (disease
called) apakit!  
3. The nine and ninety (sores) that gather together upon the shoulders,
from here they all shall pass away, as the pustules of the (disease
called) apakit!
