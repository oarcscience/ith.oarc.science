## VII, 13. Charm to deprive enemies of their strength.

1\. As the rising sun takes away the lustre of the stars, thus do I take
away the strength of both the women and the men that hate me.  
2. As many enemies as ye are, lookina out auainst me, as I come on--of
those that hate me do I take away the strenorth, as the sun takes away
the strength of persons asleep (while it rises).
