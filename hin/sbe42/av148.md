## VI, 99. Battle-charm of a king on the eve of battle.

1\. I call -upon thee, O Indra, from afar, upon thee for protection
against tribulation. I call the strong avenger that has many names, and
is of unequalled birth.  
2. Where the hostile weapon now rises against us,threatening to slay,
there do we place the two arms of Indra round about.  
3. The two arms of Indra, the protector, do we place round about us: let
him protect us! O god Savitar, and king Soma, render me of confident
mind, that I may prosper!
