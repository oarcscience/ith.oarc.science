## VI, 17. Charm to prevent miscarriage.

1\. As this great earth conceives the germs of the beings, thus shalt
thy embryo be-beld fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!  
2. As this great earth holds these trees, thus shall thy embryo be held
fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!  
3. As this great earth holds the mountains and the peaks, thus shall thy
embryo be held fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!  
4. As this great earth holds the animals scattered far, thus shall thy
embryo be held fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!
