### KHA*ND*A 23.

1\. Let him pound the roots of the plants kâkâtanî, ma*k*aka*k*âtanî,
ko*s*âtakî, of the egg-plant, and of the indigo plant, and besmear
(therewith) the place in which she is going to be confined, in order to
drive away the Rakshas.
