### KHA*ND*A 18.

1\. A student who is going to set out on a journey, speaks thus to his
teacher:

2 <span id="fr_201"></span>[2](#fn_200.md). 'Of inhalation and
exhalation'—(this he says) in a low voice; 'Om, I will dwell'—this
aloud.

3\. (The teacher) in a low voice (replies), 'To inhalation and
exhalation I, the wide-extended one, resort with thee. To the protecting
god I give thee in charge. God Savitar; this student belongs to thee; I
give him in charge to thee; protect him; do not forsake him.'

4\. 'Om, hail!' the teacher aloud. 'Hail!' the teacher aloud.

Here ends the Second Adhyâya.
