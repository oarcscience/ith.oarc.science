### KA*ND*IKÂ 4.

1 <span id="fr_374"></span>[4\_1](#fn_373). During the northern course
of the sun, in the time of the increasing moon, under an auspicious
Nakshatra the tonsure (of the child's head), the initiation (of a
Brahma*k*ârin), the cutting of the beard, and marriage (should be
celebrated).

2\. According to some (teachers), marriage (may be celebrated) at any
time.

3\. Before those (ceremonies) let him sacrifice four Â*g*ya oblations—

4\. With the three (verses), 'Agni, thou purifiest life' (Rig-veda I X,
66, 10 seq.), and with (the one verse), 'Pra*g*âpati, no other one than
thou' (Rig-Veda X, 121, 10).

5 <span id="fr_375"></span>[5](#fn_374). Or with the Vyâh*ri*tis.

6 <span id="fr_376"></span>[6](#fn_375). According to some (teachers),
the one and the other.

7 <span id="fr_377"></span>[7](#fn_376). No such(oblations), according
to some (teachers).

8\. At the marriage the fourth oblation with the verse, 'Thou (O Agni)
art Aryaman towards the girls' (Rig-veda V, 3, 2).
