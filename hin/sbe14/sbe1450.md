### PRA*S*NA I, ADHYÂYA 9, KA*ND*IKÂ 17.

1\. The Rathakâra (carpenter), the Ambash*th*a, the Sûta (charioteer),
the Ugra, the Mâgadha, the Âyogava, the Vai*n*a, the Kshatt*ri*, the
Pulkasa, the Kukku*t*a, the Vaidehaka, the *K*a*nd*âla, and so
forth, <span id="fr_982"></span>[1](#fn_982)

2\. Among these, sons of equal caste (spring) from women of equal caste.

3\. A Brâhma*n*a (begets) on a female of the Kshatriya caste a
Brâhma*n*a, on a female of the Vai*s*ya caste an Ambash*th*a, on a
female of the *S*ûdra caste a Nishâda, <span
id="fr_983"></span>[3](#fn_983)

4\. (According to) some a Pâra*s*ava.

<span id="page_198">p. 198</span>

5\. A Kshatriya (begets) on a female of the Vai*s*ya caste a Kshatriya,
on a female of the *S*ûdra caste an Ugra.

6\. A Vai*s*ya (begets) on a female of the *S*ûdra caste a Rathakâra.

7\. A *S*ûdra begets on a female of the Vai*s*ya caste a Mâgadha, on a
female of the Kshatriya caste a Kshatt*ri*, but on a female of the
Brâhma*n*a caste a *K*a*nd*âla. <span id="fr_984"></span>[7](#fn_984)

8\. A Vai*s*ya begets on a female of the Kshatriya caste an Âyogava, on
a female of the Brâhma*n*a caste a Sûta.

9\. If among these an Ambash*th*a (male) and an Ugra (female) unite,
(their son) will be born in the direct order of the castes
(Anuloma). <span id="fr_985"></span>[9](#fn_985)

10\. If a Kshatt*ri* (male) and a Vaidehaka (female) unite, (their son
will be) born against the order of the castes (Pratiloma).

11\. An Ugra (begets) on a female of the Kshatt*ri* caste a
*S*vapâka, <span id="fr_986"></span>[11](#fn_986)

12\. A Vaidehaka on a female of the Ambash*th*a caste a Vai*n*a,

13\. A Nishâda on a female of the *S*ûdra caste a Pulkasa, <span
id="fr_987"></span>[13](#fn_987)

14\. A *S*ûdra on a female of the Nishâda caste a Kukku*t*aka.

15\. The wise declare those sprung from an inter-mixture of the castes
to be Vrâtyas. <span id="fr_988"></span>[15](#fn_988)

<span id="page_199">p. 199</span>
