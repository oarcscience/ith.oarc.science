# THE GOSPEL

# OF

# RAMAKRISHNA

##### Revised by

## SWAMI ABHEDANANDA

##### From

### M.'s (Mahendra Nath Gupta) Original English Text

#### New York: The Vedanta Society

#### \[1907\]

Scanned, proofed, and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage, July
2009, by John Bruno Hare. This text is in the public domain in the US
because it was published prior to 1923.

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

*Niranjanam Nityam anantarupam,  
Bhaktânukampâ dhritavigraham vai;  
Ishâvatâram Paramesham Idyam,  
Tam Râmakrishnam Shirashâ Namâmah*.

</div>

Salutations to Bhagavân Srî Râmakrishna, the perfect Embodiment of the
Eternal Truth which manifests Itself in various forms to help mankind,
and the Incarnation of the Supreme Lord who is worshipped by all.

<span class="smallcaps">Hari Om Tat Sat.</span>
