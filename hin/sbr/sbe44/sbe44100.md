<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7">13:3:7</span></span>

### SEVENTH BRÂHMA*N*A.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:1">13:3:7:1</span></span>1.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Strengthful: wherever they worship
with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes strong.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:2">13:3:7:2</span></span>2.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Plenteous: wherever they worship
with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes plentiful.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:3">13:3:7:3</span></span>3.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Obtainment: wherever they worship
with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes obtained.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:4">13:3:7:4</span></span>4.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Distinction: wherever they worship
with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes distinct <span
id="fr_884"></span>[1](#fn_880.md).

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:5">13:3:7:5</span></span>5.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Severance: wherever they worship
with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes severed <span
id="fr_885"></span>[2](#fn_881.md).

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:6">13:3:7:6</span></span>6.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Food-abounding: wherever they
worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes abounding in
food.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:7">13:3:7:7</span></span>7.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Sapful:

<span id="page_345">p. 345</span>

wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes
rich in sap (or drink).

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:8">13:3:7:8</span></span>8.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Abounding in holiness: wherever
they worship with this sacrifice, the Brâhma*n*a is born as one rich in
holiness.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:9">13:3:7:9</span></span>9.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Excelling in hitting: wherever they
worship with this sacrifice, the Râ*g*anya is born as one excelling in
hitting (the mark).

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:10">13:3:7:10</span></span>10.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called the Long (wide) one: wherever they
worship with this sacrifice, a wide tract of forest-land will be
provided <span id="fr_886"></span>[1](#fn_882.md).

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:11">13:3:7:11</span></span>11.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Fitness: wherever they worship with
this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes fit and proper.

<span class="margnote"><span id="13:3:7:12">13:3:7:12</span></span>12.
Verily, this is the sacrifice called Support (foundation): wherever they
worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes supported (firmly
established).

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_880"></span>[344:1](sbe44100.htm#fr_884.md) Svakarmasu bhâgena
sthâpitam (? confined respectively to its own functions), comm.

<span id="fn_881"></span>[344:2](sbe44100.htm#fr_885.md) Harisvâmin
supplies 'akâryebhya*h*'--is kept away from what it is forbidden to do,
or from what is not one's business.

<span id="fn_882"></span>[345:1](sbe44100.htm#fr_886.md) That is, as would
seem, either as a protection from neighbouring countries, or as room for
spreading, and as pasture-land. Cf., however, [XIII, 2-4, 2,
4](sbe44084.htm#13:2:2:4.md), where, as in Ait. Br. III, 44; VI, 23, such a
belt of jungle is referred to as a source of danger to the inhabitants
of a country.
