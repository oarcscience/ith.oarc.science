### SECOND BRÂHMA*N*A.

<span class="margnote"><span id="12:1:2:1">12:1:2:1</span></span>1.
Verily, from out of faith the gods fashioned the initiation, from out of
Aditi the opening (sacrifice <span id="fr_424"></span>[2](#fn_424).md),
from out of Soma the buying (of Soma-plants), from out of Vish*n*u the
guest-offering, from out of the sun the Pravargya, from out of the
Svadhâ (the food of departed ancestors) the Upasads,

<span id="page_139">p. 139</span>

from out of Agni and Soma the day of fasting, and from out of this world
the opening Atirâtra <span id="fr_425"></span>[1](#fn_425.md).

<span class="margnote"><span id="12:1:2:2">12:1:2:2</span></span>2. From
out of the year (they fashioned) the *K*aturvi*ms*a day, from out of the
priesthood the Abhiplava (sha*d*aha), from out of the nobility the
P*ri*sh*th*ya (sha*d*aha) <span id="fr_426"></span>[2](#fn_426.md), from
out of Agni the Abhi*g*it, from out of the waters the Svarasâman days,
from out of the sun the Vishuvat,--the Svarasâman days have been
told;--from out of Indra the Vi*s*va*g*it,--the P*ri*sh*th*ya and
Abhiplava have been told;--from out of Mitra and Varu*n*a the Go and

<span id="page_140">p. 140</span>

<span class="contnote">\[paragraph continues\]</span> Âyus <span
id="fr_427"></span>[1](#fn_427.md), from out of the Vi*s*ve Devâ*h* the
Da*s*arâtra <span id="fr_428"></span>[2](#fn_428.md), from out of the
regions the P*ri*sh*th*ya-sha*d*aha of the Da*s*arâtra, from out of
these worlds the *Kh*andoma days.

<span class="margnote"><span id="12:1:2:3">12:1:2:3</span></span>3. From
out of the year (they fashioned) the tenth day, from out of Pra*g*âpati
the Mahâvrata, and from out of the world of heaven the Udayanîya
Atirâtra:--such was the birth of the Year; and, verily, whosoever thus
knows that birth of the Year becomes more (and more) glorious to (the
end of) it, he becomes possessed of a (new) body, he becomes the Year,
and, as the Year <span id="fr_429"></span>[3](#fn_429.md), he goes to the
gods.
