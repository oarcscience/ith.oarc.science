### LIX

I. 52.

khasm na cînhai bâwari

O MAN, if thou dost not know thine own Lord, whereof art thou so
proud?  
Put thy cleverness away: mere words shall never unite thee to Him.  
Do not deceive thyself with the witness of the Scriptures:  
Love is something other than this, and he who has sought it truly has
found it.
