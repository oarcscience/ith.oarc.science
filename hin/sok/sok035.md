### XXXIV

II\. 110.

mohi tohi lâgî kaise chu**t**e

HOW could the love between Thee and me sever?  
As the leaf of the lotus abides on the water: so thou art my Lord, and I
am Thy servant.  
As the night-bird Chakor gazes all night at the moon: so Thou art my
Lord and I am Thy servant.  
From the beginning until the ending of time, there is love between Thee
and me; and how shall such love be extinguished?  
Kabîr says: "As the river enters into the ocean, so my heart touches
Thee."
