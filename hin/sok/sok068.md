### LXVII

I. 9.

nâ jâne sâhab kaisâ hai

I DO not know what manner of God is mine.  
The Mullah cries aloud to Him: and why? Is your Lord deaf? The subtle
anklets that ring on the <span id="page_111">p. 111</span> feet of an
insect when it moves are heard of Him.  
Tell your beads, paint your forehead with the mark of your God, and wear
matted locks long and showy: but a deadly weapon is in your heart, and
how shall you have God?
