### IV

I. 58.

bâgo nâ jâ re nâ jâ

Do not go to the garden of flowers!  
O Friend! go not there;  
In your body is the garden of flowers.  
Take your seat on the thousand petals of the lotus, and there gaze on
the Infinite Beauty.
