### LVIII

I. 63.

pîle pyâlâ, ho matwâlâ

EMPTY the Cup! O be drunken!  
Drink the divine nectar of His Name!  
Kabîr says: "Listen to me, dear Sadhu! <span id="page_104">p.
104</span>  
From the sole of the foot to the crown of the head this mind is filled
with poison."
