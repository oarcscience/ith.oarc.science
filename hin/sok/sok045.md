### XLIV

I. 93. *gagan ma**t**h gaib nisân ga*d

e

THE Hidden Banner is planted in the temple of the sky; there the blue
canopy decked with the moon and set with bright jewels is spread.  
There the light of the sun and the moon is shining: still your mind to
silence before that splendour. <span id="page_92">p. 92</span>  
Kabîr says: "He who has drunk of this nectar, wanders like one who is
mad."
