### XXXVIII

I. 50.

bhram kâ tâlâ lagâ mahal re

THE lock of error shuts the gate, open it with the key of love: Thus, by
opening the door, thou shalt wake the Beloved. <span id="page_87">p.
87</span>  
Kabîr says: "O brother! do not pass by such good fortune as this."
