### XCIII

III\. 111.

ko**t**î<u>n</u> bhânu candra târâga

n

BENEATH the great umbrella of my King millions of suns and moons and
stars are shining!  
He is the Mind within my mind: He is the Eye within mine eye.  
Ah, could my mind and eyes be one! Could my love but reach to my Lover!
Could but the fiery heat of my heart be cooled!  
Kabîr says: "When you unite love with the Lover, then you have love's
perfection."
