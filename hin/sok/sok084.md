### LXXXIII

III\. 84.

jhî jhî jantar bâjai

THE harp gives forth murmurous music; and the dance goes on without
hands and feet.  
It is played without fingers, it is heard without ears: for He is the
ear, and He is the listener.  
The gate is locked, but within there is fragrance: and there the meeting
is seen of none.  
The wise shall understand it.
