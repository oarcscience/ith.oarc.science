13\. Sm*ri*ti also declares this.

Sm*ri*ti also declares that the soul of him who knows departs by means
of an artery of the head. 'Of those, one is situated above which pierces
the disc of the sun and passes beyond the world of Brahman; by way of
that the soul reaches the highest goal' (Yâ*gñ*. Sm*ri*. III,
167).--Here terminates the adhikara*n*a of 'up to the beginning of the
road.'
