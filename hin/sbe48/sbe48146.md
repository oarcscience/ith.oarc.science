4\. And on account of there being no statement of its being an object of
knowledge.

If the text meant the Non-evolved as understood by the Sâ<u>n</u>khyas
it would refer to it as something to be known; for the Sâ<u>n</u>khyas,
who hold the theory of Release resulting from the discriminative
knowledge of the Evolved, the

<span id="page_360">p. 360</span>

\[paragraph continues\] Non-evolved, and the soul, admit that all these
are objects of knowledge. Now our text does not refer to the Un-evolved
as an object of knowledge, and it cannot therefore be the Pradhâna
assumed by the Sâ<u>n</u>khyas.
