34\. On account of taking and the declaration as to its moving about.

The text beginning 'And as a great king,' &c., declares that 'the Self
taking the pranas moves about in its own body,

<span id="page_555">p. 555</span>

according to its pleasure' (B*ri*. Up. II, 1, 18), i.e. it teaches that
the Self is active in taking to itself the prâ*n*as and moving about in
the body.
