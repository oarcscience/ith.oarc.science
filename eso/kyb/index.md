This concise work on 'The Hermetic Philosophy' outlines seven universal
principles: Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm,
Cause and Effect, and Gender. Rather than the actual Kybalion, this is
purportedly an exegesis on that text. While this was probably one of the
books which William Walker Atikinson wrote for the Yogi Publication
Society under a pseudonym, it probably bears a closer read than most.
