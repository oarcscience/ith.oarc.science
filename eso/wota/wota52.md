### TLASKAN WORDS.

<span class="smallcaps">Akasa</span>. The great ocean of ether sweeping
in and through all things.

<span class="smallcaps">Lomkatos</span>. Omn taken; those who have
passed through the change called death.

<span class="smallcaps">Neontu</span>. One of the disciples of Zertoulem
most deeply loved by the Master.

<span class="smallcaps">Omn</span>. The Eternal; God.

<span class="smallcaps">Otmar</span>. Under clouds; not yet awakened to
the light of the spirit.

<span class="smallcaps">Sebas-thā-ontu</span>. Sebas, mountains; thā,
the sun; ontu, setting. "The Mountains of the Setting Sun."

<span class="smallcaps">Thā</span>. The sun.

<span class="smallcaps">Tlaskan</span>. The sacred race that peopled a
portion of Central America many thousands of years ago.

<span class="smallcaps">Tlaskanata</span>. The Land of the Sacred
Record; so called because the people were led to this land by a prophecy
in their sacred writings.
