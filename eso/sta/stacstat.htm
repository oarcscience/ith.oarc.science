THE SECRET TEACHINGS OF ALL AGES: COPYRIGHT STATUS.

 

There is more than sufficient evidence online to validate the claim that the work "The Secret Teachings of All Ages," published in 1928, is currently in the public domain. This is based on the legal requirement that a work published during the period 1923-1962 (inclusive) had to be renewed at the US Copyright Office in the 28th year. In practice, the renewals for books published in 1928 were published in 1955 through 1957.

 

If no renewal is found, the book's copyright has lapsed. We believe that this can be more than adequately demonstrated using online resources.

ADDITIONAL CLAIMS

 

There are few additional claims relating to this work in the LOCIS database at the US Copyright Office (https://www.copyright.gov/records/cohm.html). You can verify the information in this section by going to that URL and searching for 'Secret Teachings.'

 

It is important to understand that this database only goes back to 1978. Due to the 28 year rule, this means that it only has information on copyright renewal claims filed after 1950. Hence it cannot be used to search for renewals for a book from 1928 (for which see the additional online resources above).

The most important lines here are 'Claim Limits' (here highlighted in red). This is a copyright office term which means that the registration applies only to the material specified.

 

The first of these applies to some unspecified 'editorial revision' in 1962. We believe that this applies to a new title page and verso that was added to reprints of the work in 1962. The second applies only to a new preface, added in 1988.

 

Neither of these claims relates to the main body of the text. And neither of these items are included in the ith.oarc.science etext of the book.
