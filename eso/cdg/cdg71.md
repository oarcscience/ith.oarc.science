### 779 A.D. AGOBARD, 840 A.D.

### BISHOP OF LYONS.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_HHH">HHH</span></span>The record of
the date of the birth inscribed by his own hand is extant in Bede's
Martyrology, which is preserved at Rome. This book states that Agobard
was born in Spain and came to France when three years of age. At
thirty-seven he became Archbishop of Lyons, and was one of the most
celebrated and learned prelates of the ninth century.
