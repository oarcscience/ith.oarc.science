SEVEN ANCIENT PROPHECIES

OF

WORLD PEACE.

TRUTH IS JUSTICE

AND THE

MESSENGER OF PEACE

TO

MANKIND.

"The paternal self-begotten intellect disseminated in all things the bond of love, heavy with fire, that all things might remain loving for an infinite time ; that the connected series of things might intellectually remain in all the light of the Father ; and that the elements of the world might continue running in love." PROCLUS.
