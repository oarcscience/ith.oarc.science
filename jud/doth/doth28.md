### <span class="smallcaps">The Signs and Consequences of True Humility</span>

<span class="smallcaps">Humility</span> before the Creator obliges a man
to behave meekly and unselfishly in all his transactions with his
fellow-men, whether in matters of business or in any other relation of
life.

The truly humble man will mourn for all the mistakes made by other men,
and not triumph or rejoice over them.
