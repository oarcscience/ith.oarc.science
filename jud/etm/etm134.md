# XXVIII. TREATISE GITTIN.

### <span class="smallcaps">Introduction</span>.

<span class="smallcaps">Under</span> this title the laws relating to the
Get \[i.e. bill or letter of divorce, of which the word Gittin is the
plural term\] are specified, without which document no marriage can be
legally dissolved. The texts of the Holy Law on which the regulations of
this Treatise are founded, are contained in chapter xxiv. of
Deuteronomy.
