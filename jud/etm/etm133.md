# XXV. TREATISE NEDARIM.

\[Contains laws relating to vows made by females, which the father and
the husband have the power to annul, founded on Numbers xxx. 4–16.\]

# XXVI. TREATISE NAZIR.

\[Relates to vows of abstinence. The precepts are founded on Numbers vi.
1–21.\]

# XXVII. TREATISE SOOTAH.

\[Contains laws relating to the woman suspected of adultery, founded on
Numbers v. 11–31.\]
