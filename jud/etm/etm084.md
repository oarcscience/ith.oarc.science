# XIX. TREATISE ROSH HASHANAH,

###### OR, OF THE NEW YEAR.

### <span class="smallcaps">Introduction</span>.

<span class="smallcaps">In</span> this Treatise are enumerated various
periods of commencement of the year for different objects, during the
existence of the second Temple. The mode of determining the day of the
feast of new moon from the observation of witnesses, as also the manner
of examining them, and other matters incident thereunto, is related
next; and finally, this Treatise treats of the feast of new year, and
whatever is peculiar to it, such as the sounding of the cornet ‏שופר‎,
and the prayers and service of that festival.
