### (2) Who steals a Soul from Israel.

<span class="margnote">**M**.</span>He who steals a soul from Israel is
not guilty till he bring it into his own domain and make use of it, as
it is written: AND TREAT HIM AS A SLAVE AND SELL HIM. <span
id="fr_481"></span>[1](#fn_474.md) If a man steal his own son, R. Johanan,
the son of Beruka, <span id="fr_482"></span>[2](#fn_475.md) convicts, but
the majority acquit. If one steal a man who is half free and half a
slave, <span id="fr_483"></span>[3](#fn_476.md) R. Jehuda convicts, but the
majority acquit.

<span id="fn_476"></span>[137:3](tsa41.htm#fr_483.md) One who has paid his
