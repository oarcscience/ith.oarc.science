IV.--OFFENDERS LIABLE TO CAPITAL PUNISHMENT

A.--THOSE WHO ARE PUNISHABLE BY STONING

VII. 4. These are they who are to be stoned: the man who has criminal connexion with his

p. 96

M.mother, his father's wife, his daughter-in-law, a male, or a beast; the woman who has criminal connexion with a beast; a blasphemer; an idolater; he who offers one of his children to Moloch; the Ba‘al ‘Ob; the Yidd‘oni; 1 he who defiles the Sabbath; he who curses his father or mother; he who has criminal connexion with a betrothed damsel; the beguiler to idolatry; he who leads a town astray; the sorcerer; and the stubborn and rebellious son.
