# CHAPTER XX.

### CONCERNING THE BODY OF MICROPROSOPUS.

709\. THE masculine power is extended through Däath; and the Assemblies
and Conclaves are filled.

710\. It commenceth from the beginning of the skull, and it is extended
throughout the whole body, through the breast, and through the arms, and
through all the other parts.
