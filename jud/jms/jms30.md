<span id="an_9_21">21</span>. Grunwald, *MGJV*, V (1900), 83; *MJV*, XIX

could overlook the entire conflagration, and, while slowly reciting Nu.
11:2, pour with each syllable a drop of water into a pan of burning
coals.
