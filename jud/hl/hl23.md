# XXI.

The bathing season at (the hot baths of) Dimsis lasted twenty-one days.

Shabbath, fol. 147, col. 2.

A fowl hatches in twenty-one days, and the almond tree ripens its fruit
in twenty-one days.

Bechoroth, fol. 8, col. 1.
