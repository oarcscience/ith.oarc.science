### Tanakh (Hebrew Bible)

The Tanakh is the Hebrew Bible, the quintessential sacred text. The
first five books of this comprise the Torah (or Pentateuch), the core
sacred writings of the ancient Jews, traditionally written by Moses
under divine inspiration.

[Tanakh (Hebrew Bible)](../bib/tan/index.md).
