## \[SECT. CXXXIV.—EMPEROR KAN-AN.\]

His Augustness Hiro-kuni-oshi-take-*kana*-hi dwelt in the Palace of
Kanahashi at Magari, and ruled the Empire. This Heavenly Monarch had no
august children. His august grave is at the village of Takaya in Furuchi
in Kafuchi.
