## \[SECT. CLXXX.—EMPRESS SUI-KO.\]

Her Augustness Toyo-mike-kashiki-ya-hime dwelt at the Palace of
Wohorida, and ruled the Empire for thirty seven years. Her august
mausoleum, which had been on the mound of Ohonu, was afterwards removed
to the great sepulchre at Shinaga.
