### Footnotes

<span id="fn_2408"></span>[A1:1](kj188.htm#fr_2413.md) There are few
various readings of the text of these poems. Where any occur, the
translator has been guided by the decisions of Motowori and Moribe.
Occasionally these two authorities differ as to the division of the
words into lines, and Moribe in particular does not hesitate to propose
such emendations as seem to him necessary. The translator has in almost
all cases adhered to the traditional text, but gives in foot-notes such
emendations as appear worthy of notice. Moribe's division of the lines
being in almost every case preferable to Motowori's, it has however here
been generally adopted.

<span id="fn_2409"></span>[A3:2](kj188.htm#fr_2414.md) Motowori reads
*Magishi    Atane tsuki* as two lines.
