### 36

### FUKA-YABU KIYOWARA

### KIYOWARA NO FUKA-YABU

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

  Natsu no yo wa  
Mada yoi nagara  
  Akenuru wo  
Kumo no izuko ni  
Tsuki yadoruramu.

Too short the lovely summer night,  
  Too soon ’tis passed away;  
I watched to see behind which cloud  
  The moon would chance to stay,  
  And here's the dawn of day!

</div>

<span class="small">Nothing is known of this writer, except that he was
the father of the author of verse No. [42](hvj043.htm#page_42).</span>
