What does the future hold? This is one of the big questions we'd all
like answered. This page has links to resources at Intangible Textual
Heritage that relate to prophecy and divination.

### Prophecy

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Prophecies of
Nostradamus](../nos/index)</span>   by <span
class="c_a">Nostradamus</span>. <span class="c_b">The complete
predictions of Nostradamus both in English and Old French</span>, with a
biography of Nostradamus and some notes about the events of 9/11.
</span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[Nostradamus: The Man Who Saw
Through Time](../nos/mst/index)</span>   by <span class="c_a">Lee
McCann</span> \[<span class="c_d">1941</span>\].   <span class="c_b">A
biography and historical novel of Nostradamus</span>, with many
interpreted quatrains, written during World War II. </span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Oracles of
Nostradamus](../nos/oon/index)</span>   by <span class="c_a">Charles A.
Ward</span> \[<span class="c_d">1891</span>\].   <span class="c_b">The
complete text of one of the best books about Nostradamus ever
written.</span> </span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Sibylline
Oracles](../cla/sib/index)</span>  
Tr. by <span class="c_a">Milton S. Terry</span> \[<span
class="c_d">1899</span>\].   <span class="c_b">Read the mysterious
(purported) predictions of the Roman Sibylline Oracle.</span> </span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Book of
Revelation](../bib/kjv/rev)</span>   <span class="c_b">The most famous
Christian prophecy.</span> </span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Prophecies of
Paracelsus](pop/index)</span>   by <span class="c_a">Paracelsus</span>,
tr. by <span class="c_a">J.K</span> \[<span class="c_d">1915</span>\].  
<span class="c_b">The alchemist and esoteric philosopher issued a set of
32 predictions</span>, each illustrated by an enigmatic woodcut. </span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[Mother Shipton
Investigated](msi/index)</span>   by <span class="c_a">William H.
Harrison</span> \[<span class="c_d">1881</span>\]   <span
class="c_b">Prophecies attributed to Mother Shipton</span>, a legendary
English figure who lived over 500 years ago are startlingly accurate.
Were these true predictions or just simply a self-perpetuating hoax?
</span>

<span class="c_e"> <span class="c_t">[The Prophecies of the Brahan
Seer](../neu/celt/pbs/index)</span>   by <span class="c_a">Alexander
Mackenzie</span>, intro. by <span class="c_a">Andrew Lang</span> \[<span
class="c_d">1899</span>\]   <span class="c_b">The tale of the famed
Scottish clarivoyant and his amazing, and very specific,
predictions.</span> </span>
