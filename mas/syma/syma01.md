THE M.S.A. NATIONAL MASONIC LIBRARY

<span class="smallcaps">The</span> M. S. A. <span
class="smallcaps">National Masonic Library</span> presents, in a series
of volumes of uniform binding and competent craftsmanship, the best
results of Masonic research by masters of the Craft in America and
abroad. The <span class="smallcaps">Library</span> will cover every
aspect of Freemasonry, its ritual, its symbolism, its philosophy, its
past history and present activities and development. Representing all
recognized schools of Masonic thought, it will bring the best literature
of the Craft within reach of lodges and members.
