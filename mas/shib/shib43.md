### POPULAR ABBREVIATIONS.

#### SPECIALLY FOR RECORDERS OF COMMANDERIES.

A. C. (*Ante Christum*), Before Christ.

B. C., Before Christ.

A. D. (*Anno Domini*), Year of our Lord.

<span class="smallcaps">Æt</span>., Aged.

A. V., Authorized Version.

D. V. (*Deo Volente*), God willing.

G. M., Grand Master.

<span class="smallcaps">Ibid</span>. (*Ibidem*), In the same place.

<span class="smallcaps">Id</span>. (*Idem*), The same.

I. E. (*Id est*), That is.

I. H. S. (*Jesus Hominum Salvator*, or *In Hoc Salus*), Jesus Saviour of
men, or In Him is Salvation.

I. N. R. I. (*Iesus Nazarenus Rex Judæorum*), Jesus of Nazareth King of
the Jews.

K. T., Knight Templar.

K. M., Knight of Malta.

L. S. (*Locus Sigilli*), Place of the seal.

N. B. (*Nota Bene*), Take notice.

<span class="smallcaps">Nem. Con.</span> (*Nemine contradicente*),
Unanimously.

<span class="smallcaps">Ob</span>. (*Obiit*), Died.

<span class="smallcaps">Pro Tem</span>. (*Pro tempore*), For the time
being.

<span class="smallcaps">Prox</span>. (*Proximo*), Next.

Q. V. (*Quod vide*), Which see.

<span class="smallcaps">Sc</span>. (*Sculpsit*), Engraved.

<span class="smallcaps">Ult</span>. (*Ultimo*), Last.

V. (*Vide*), See.

<span class="smallcaps">Viz</span>. (*Videlicet*), Namely.

<span class="smallcaps">Etc</span>. (*Et cetera*), And so forth.
