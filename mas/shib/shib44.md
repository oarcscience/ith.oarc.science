### SOME WORDS NOT FULLY UNDERSTOOD

<span class="smallcaps">Acre</span>, *Ack´-er*, A city of Syria of
Crusade interest.

<span class="smallcaps">Acacia</span>, *Acay´-shi-a*, An evergreen,
thorny plant.

<span class="smallcaps">Adonai</span>, *Ay-do´-nah*, Lord, used where
Jehovah occurs.

<span class="smallcaps">Agnus Dei</span>, *Ag´-nus Dee´-i*, Lamb of God.

<span class="smallcaps">Ahasuerus</span>, *Ay-has-yu-ee´-rus*, King of
Persia.

<span class="smallcaps">Almoner</span>, *Al´-mo-ner*, One who
distributes alms.

<span class="smallcaps">Anno Ordinis</span>, *An´-no Or´-din-is*, Year
of the Order.

<span class="smallcaps">Artaxerxes</span>, *Ar-tax-erks´-eez*, King of
Persia.

<span class="smallcaps">Barabbas</span>, *Bah-rab´-bas*, One of the
Robbers.

<span class="smallcaps">Beauceant</span>, *Bo´-see-an*, Battle Flag of
Templars.

<span class="smallcaps">Belshazzar</span>, *Bel-shaz´-zar*, King of the
Chaldees.

<span class="smallcaps">Boaz</span>, *Boh´-az*, A Pillar of the Porch of
the Temple.

<span class="smallcaps">Chapeau</span>, *Shah´-po*, A hat.

<span class="smallcaps">Clandestine</span>, *Klan-des´-tin*, Hidden,
Fraudulent.

<span class="smallcaps">Consummatum Est</span>, *Kon-sum-mah´-tum est*,
It is finished.

<span class="smallcaps">Crux Ansata</span>, *Kroox An-sah´-ta*, Cross
with a handle.

<span class="smallcaps">Darius</span>, *Day-ri´-us*, King of Persia.

<span class="smallcaps">Dieu Le Veut</span>, *Dyuh la Voot*, God´s will.

<span class="smallcaps">Deus Vult</span>, *Dee´-us Vult*, God´s will.

<span class="smallcaps">Dimit</span>, *Dy-mit´*, To let go.

<span class="smallcaps">Eccosaise</span>, *Ay´-ko-zayze*, (Rite) Scotch.

<span class="smallcaps">Ecbatana</span>, *Ec-bat´-ah-nah*. Capital cf
the Medes.

<span class="smallcaps">Elu</span>, *Ayl´-yu*, Elect.

<span class="smallcaps">Emeritus</span>, *Ee´-mer-i-tus*, Out of active
service by honorable discharge.

<span class="smallcaps">Emmanuel</span>, *Em-man´-u-el*, (Immanuel) God
with us.

<span class="smallcaps">Frater</span>, *Fray´-ter*, or *Frah´-ter*,
Brother.

<span class="smallcaps">Fratres</span>, Brethren.

<span class="smallcaps">Gavel</span>, *Gah´-vel*, The mallet of a
Presiding Officer.

<span class="smallcaps">Gethsemane</span>, *Geth-sem´-a-nee*, Garden
where Jesus was arrested.

<span class="smallcaps">Golgotha</span>, *Gol´-go-thah*, A place of a
skull.

<span class="smallcaps">Heredom</span>, *Her´-ee-dom*, Holy House.

<span class="smallcaps">Houri</span>, *Hoo´-ree*, Black-eyed nymph.

<span class="smallcaps">Impostor</span>, *Im-pos´-tor*, One who
deceives.

<span class="smallcaps">Insignia</span>, *In-sig´-ni-ya*, A badge.

<span class="smallcaps">Jakin</span>, *Jah´-kin*, A pillar of the Porch
of the Temple. (*Yahkin*.)

<span class="smallcaps">Kabala</span>, *Kah´-bah-lah*, Jewish Theosophy.

<span class="smallcaps">Kadosh Kadoshim</span>, *Kay-doash´
Kay-doash´-im*, Holy of Holies.

<span class="smallcaps">Koran</span>, *Ko-ran´*, Mahometan sacred book.

<span id="page_145">p. 145</span>

<span class="smallcaps">Laus Deo</span>, *Laws Dee´-o*, Praise God.

<span class="smallcaps">Legend</span> *Lej´-end*, A story handed down
from ancient times.

<span class="smallcaps">Libanus</span>, *Ly-bah´-nus*, Lebanon.

<span class="smallcaps">Lux e Tenebris</span>, *Lux ee Ten´-e-bris*,
Light out of darkness.

<span class="smallcaps">Magdalene</span>, *Mag-day-lee´-nay*, She of New
Testament fame.

<span class="smallcaps">Magus</span>, *May´-jus*, Persian Wise Mau.

<span class="smallcaps">Magi</span>, *May´-ji*, Priest of Magian
religion.

<span class="smallcaps">Magian</span>, *May´-ji-an*, Believer in
religion of the Magi.

<span class="smallcaps">Maher-Shalal-Hash-Baz</span>,
*Mah´-her-shal´-al-hash-baz*, Make speed to the spoil, hasten the prey.

<span class="smallcaps">Melita</span>, *Mel´-i-ta*, Malta.

<span class="smallcaps">Nebuchadnezzar</span>, *Neb-uk-ad-nez´-zar*,
King of the Chaldeans.

<span class="smallcaps">Nebuzaradan</span>, *Neb-yu-zar-ah´-dan*,
General of Nebuchadnezzar.

<span class="smallcaps">Ne Varietur</span>, *Nee Var-i´-tur*, To prevent
change.

<span class="smallcaps">Omnific</span>, *Om-nif´-ik*, All creating.

<span class="smallcaps">Palestine</span>, *Pal´-es-tine*, The Holy Land.

<span class="smallcaps">Pro Deo et Patria</span>, *Pro Dee´-o et
Pay-tree´-a*, For God and my country.

<span class="smallcaps">Pax Vobiscum</span>, *Pax Voh-bis´-kum*, Peace
be with you.

<span class="smallcaps">Pentateuch</span>, *Pent´-ah-tuke*, Five books
of Moses.

<span class="smallcaps">Persepolis</span>, *Per-sep´-o-lis*, Chief city
of Persia.

<span class="smallcaps">Prelate</span>, *Prel´-ate*, Officer of a
Commandery,

<span class="smallcaps">Pursuivant</span>, *Pur´-swee-vant*, A
messenger.

<span class="smallcaps">Pythagoras</span>, *Py-thag´-oh-ras*, A
Philosopher of Greece.

<span class="smallcaps">Rose Croix</span>, *Roze Krwah*, Rose Cross.

<span class="smallcaps">Satrap</span>, *Sat´-rap*, A Persian Governor.

<span class="smallcaps">Shethar-Boznai</span>, *Shee´-thar-boz´-nay-i*,
Persian nobleman.

<span class="smallcaps">Shushan</span>, *Shoo´-shan*, A Persian Capital.

<span class="smallcaps">Tatnai</span>, *Tat´-nay-i*, A Persian Satrap,
or Governor of a province.

<span class="smallcaps">Tetragrammaton</span>, *Tet-ra gram´ mah-ton*,
The concealing number Four.

<span class="smallcaps">Tirshatha</span>, *Tur-shah´-thah*, Persian
Governor of Judea.

<span class="smallcaps">Turcopolier</span>, *Tur-ko-po´-ly-er*, An
ancient Officer of Knights of Malta.

<span class="smallcaps">Viva Voce</span>, *Vy´-vah Voh´-see*, By the
living voice.

<span class="smallcaps">Xerxes</span>, *Zerks´-eez*, King of Persia.

<span class="smallcaps">Zeradatha</span>, *Zer-e-dah´-thah*, Ancient
city of Judea.

<span class="smallcaps">Zerubbabel</span>, *Zer-ub´-bah-bel*, Judean
Prince, and Tirshatha.

<span class="smallcaps">Zoroaster</span>, *Zoh-roh-as´-ter*, Founder of
Magian religion.

<span class="smallcaps">Zarathrustra</span>, Persian name of Zoroaster.
