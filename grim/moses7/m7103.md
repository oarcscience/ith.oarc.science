### Instructions

The seals or General Characters of the Seven Great Princes must be
written upon virgin parchment with the blood of butterflies, at the time
of the full moon. The Seven Great Princes have among them some of the
legions of crown-spirits who were expelled from Heaven, according to
tradition.

Mundus ater cum illis  
    Me pactum dicit habere  
Sed me teque Deus  
    Te illo custodiat omnes.
