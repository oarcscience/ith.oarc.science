### CHAPTER IV. EGYPT

![](img/08701.jpg)

Conjuration of Three Angels

GEBRIL! MEACHUEL! NESANEL!--By the lamp of the threefold eternal light,
let N.N. appear before me.

Three calls with the voice and three with the horn.
