### THE SPIRIT APPEARS IN THE BURNING BUSH

![](img/07101.jpg)

The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:

BABA CUCI HIEBU ZIADHI ELENEHET NA VEAN VIE ACHYA SALNA

The spirit which appears here is God himself.
