### THE OTHER MAGICAL REQUISITES.

THE other magical requisites are: a sceptre, a sword, a mitre, a cap, a
long white robe of linen, and other garments for the purpose; <span
id="fr_31"></span>[1](#fn_31) also a girdle of lion's skin three inches
broad, with all the names written about it which he round the outmost
part of the Magical Circle. Also perfumes, and a chafing-dish of
charcoal kindled to put the fumes on, to smoke or perfume the place
appointed for action; also anointing oil to anoint thy temples and thine
eyes with; and fair water to wash thyself in. And in so doing, thou
shalt say as David said:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_31"></span>[55:1](lks15.htm#fr_31) In many Codices it is
written "a sceptre or sword, a mitre or cap." By the "other garments"
would be meant not only undergarments, but also mantles of different
colours.
