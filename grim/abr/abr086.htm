THE TWENTY-FIRST CHAPTER.

O transform oneself, and take different Faces and Forms.

(1) To appear old.

(2) To take on the appearance of an Old Woman.

(3) To appear young.

(4) To transform oneself into a Girl.

(5) To appear like a young Child.
