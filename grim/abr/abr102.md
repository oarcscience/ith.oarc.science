### THE TWENTY-NINTH CHAPTER.

<span id="img_tinit"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/tinit.jpg) |

O cause Armed Men to appear.

\(1\) To cause an Army to appear.

\(2\) Armed Men for one's defence.,

\(3\) To cause a Siege to appear.
