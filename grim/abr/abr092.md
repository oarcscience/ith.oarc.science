### THE TWENTY-FOURTH CHAPTER.

<span id="img_tinit"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/tinit.jpg) |

O discover any Thefts that hath occurred.

\(1\) Jewels stolen.

\(2\) Money.

\(3\) Worked Gold.

\(4\) Silver Workmanship.

\(5\) Effects, such as Furniture.

\(6\) Horses, and other Animals.
