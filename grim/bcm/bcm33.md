XV\. Gives immunity from the most ferocious animals; gives the means of
overcoming them; gives the knowledge of their language; and drives mad
animals away. The talisman should be of deep green satin embroidered
with gold. For the first three objects, say: HOCATOS, IMORAD, SURATER,
MARKILA. For the last: TRUMANTREM, RICONA, ESTUPIT, OXA.

XVI\. Gives discernment for the good or bad intentions of any person.
The talisman is of black satin embroidered with gold. It should be
placed upon the heart and the ring on the little finger of the right
hand. The words are: CROSTES, FURINOT, KATIPA, GARINOS.
