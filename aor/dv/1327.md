### 1327.

<span id="page_376">p. 376</span>

The cloth which is held in the hand in the current of a running stream,
in the waters of which the cloth leaves all its foulness and dirt, is
meant to signify this &c

By the thorn with inoculated good fruit is signified those natures which
of themselves were not disposed towards virtue, but by the aid of their
preceptors they have the repudation of it.
