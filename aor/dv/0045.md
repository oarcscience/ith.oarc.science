### 45.

1, The superficies is a limitation of the body. 2, and the limitation of
a body is no part of that body. 4, and the limitation of one body is
that which begins another. 3, that which is not part of any body is
nothing. Nothing is that which fills no space.

<span id="img_v102800"></span> ![](img/v102800.jpg)

If one single point placed in a circle may be the starting point of an
infinite number of lines, and the termination of an infinite number of
lines, there must be an infinite number of points separable from this
point, and these when reunited become one again; whence it follows that
the part may be equal to the whole.
