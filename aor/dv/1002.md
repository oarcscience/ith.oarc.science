### 1002.

From the wall of the Arno at \[the gate of\] la Giustizia to the bank of
the Arno at Sardigna where the walls are, to the mills, is 7400 braccia,
that is 2 miles and 1400 braccia and beyond the Arno is 5500 braccia.

 <span id="fr_200"></span>[534](#fn_200)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_200"></span>[228:534](1002.htm#fr_200) : 2. *Giustizia*. By
this the Porta della Giustizia seems to be meant; from the XVth to the
XVIth centuries it was also commonly known as Porta Guelfa, Porta San
Francesco del Renaio, Porta Nuova, and Porta Reale. It was close to the
Arno opposite to the Porta San Niccolo, which still exists.
