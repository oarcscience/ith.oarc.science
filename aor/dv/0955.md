### 955.

<span class="margnote"> The ebb and flow of the tide
(955-960).</span>Where there is a larger quantity of water, there is a
greater flow and ebb, but the contrary in narrow waters.

Look whether the sea is at its greatest flow when the moon is half way
over our hemisphere \[on the meridian\].
