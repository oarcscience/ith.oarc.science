### 631.

Since walnuts are enveloped in a thin rind, which partakes of the nature
of ..., if you do not remove it when you make the oil from them, this
skin tinges the oil, and when you work with it this skin separates from
the oil and rises to the surface of the painting, and this is what makes
it change.
