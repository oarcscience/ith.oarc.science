*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 852.

<span id="page_133">p. 133</span>

A remedy for scratches taught me by the Herald to the King of France. 4
ounces of virgin wax, 4 ounces of colophony, 2 ounces of incense. Keep
each thing separate; and melt the wax, and then put in the incense and
then the colophony, make a mixture of it and put it on the sore place.
