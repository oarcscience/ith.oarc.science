### 209.

Describe how it is that no object has its limitation in the mirror but
in the eye which sees it in the mirror. For if you look at your face in
the mirror, the part resembles the whole in as much as the part is
everywhere in the mirror, and the whole is in every part of the same
mirror; and the same is true of the whole image of any object placed
opposite to this mirror, &c
