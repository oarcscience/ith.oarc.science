*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 236.

### OF PAINTING.

When you represent in your work shadows which you can only discern with
difficulty, and of which you cannot distinguish the edges so that you
apprehend them confusedly, you must not make them sharp or definite lest
your work should have a wooden effect.
