### CAMELS.

The Bactrian have two humps; the Arabian one only. They are swift in
battle and most useful to carry burdens. This animal is extremely
observant of rule and measure, for it will not move if it has a greater
weight than it is used to, and if it is taken too far it does the same,
and suddenly stops and so the merchants are obliged to lodge there.
