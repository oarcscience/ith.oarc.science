### 640.

Sea water filtered through mud or clay, leaves all its saltness in it.
Woollen stuffs placed on board ship absorb fresh water. If sea water is
distilled under a retort it becomes of the first excellence and any one
who has a little stove in his kitchen can, with the same wood as he
cooks with, distil a great quantity of water if the retort is a large
one.
