*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 997.

<span id="page_220">p. 220</span>

The element of fire acts upon a wave of air in the same way as the air
does on water, or as water does on a mass of sand --that is earth; and
their motions are in the same proportions as those of the motors acting
upon them.
