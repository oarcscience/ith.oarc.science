### PERSPECTIVE.

You are asked, O Painter, why the figures you draw on a small scale
according to the laws of perspective do not appear--notwithstanding the
demonstration of distance--as large as real ones--their height being the
same as in those painted on the wall.

And why \[painted\] objects seen at a small distance appear larger than
the real ones?
