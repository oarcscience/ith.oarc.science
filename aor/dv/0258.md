*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 258.

<span id="page_138">p. 138</span>

Of several objects equal in size, brightness of background and length
that which has the flattest surface will look the largest. A bar of iron
equally thick throughout and of which half is red hot, affords an
example, for the red hot part looks thicker than the rest.
