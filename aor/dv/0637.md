### 637.

### VARNISH \[OR POWDER\].

Mercury with Jupiter and Venus,--a paste made of these must be corrected
by the mould (?) continuously, until Mercury separates itself entirely
from Jupiter and Venus.  <span id="fr_297"></span>[295](#fn_297)

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_297"></span>[322:295](0637.htm#fr_297) : Here, and in No.
641 *Mercurio* seems to mean quicksilver, *Giove* stands for iron,
*Venere* for copper and *Saturno* for lead.
