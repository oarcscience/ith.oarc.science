### 485.

### PRECEPTS FOR DRAWING.

The artist ought first to exercise his hand by copying drawings from the
hand of a good master. And having acquired that practice, under the
criticism of his master, he should next practise drawing objects in
relief of a good style, following the rules which will presently be
given.
