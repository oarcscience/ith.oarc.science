### 280.

### OF COLOURS.

Of several colours, all equally white, that will look whitest which is
against the darkest background. And black will look intensest against
the whitest background.

And red will look most vivid against the yellowest background; and the
same is the case with all colours when surrounded by their strongest
contrasts.
