### 1071.

<span id="page_250">p. 250</span>

At Bordeaux in Gascony the sea rises about 40 braccia before its ebb,
and the river there is filled with salt water for more than a hundred
and fifty miles; and the vessels which are repaired there rest high and
dry on a high hill above the sea at low tide.  <span
id="fr_254"></span>[588](#fn_254.md)
