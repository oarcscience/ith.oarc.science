### 727.

### POWDER FOR MEDALS.

<span class="margnote"> On the coining of medals (727. 728).</span>The
incombustible growth of soot on wicks reduced to powder, burnt tin and
all the metals, alum, isinglass, smoke from a brass forge, each
ingredient to be moistened, with aqua vitae or malmsey or strong malt
vinegar, white wine or distilled extract of turpentine, or oil; but
there should be little moisture, and cast in moulds.  <span
id="fr_15"></span>[349](#fn_15)
