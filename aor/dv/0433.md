### OF DARK LEAVES IN FRONT OF TRANSPARENT ONES.

When the leaves are interposed between the light and the eye, then that
which is nearest to the eye will be the darkest, and the most distant
will be the lightest, not being seen against the atmosphere; and this is
seen in the leaves which are away from the centre of the tree, that is
towards the light.
