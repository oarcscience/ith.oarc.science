### THIRD BOOK ON LIGHT AND SHADE.

<span class="margnote"> Definition of derived shadow (158.
159).</span>Derived shadow cannot exist without primary shadow. This is
proved by the first of this which says: Darkness is the total absence of
light, and shadow is an alleviation of darkness and of light, and it is
more or less dark or light in proportion as the darkness is modified by
the light.
