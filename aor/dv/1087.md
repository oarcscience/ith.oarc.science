### 1087.

<span id="page_260">p. 260</span>

<span class="margnote"> Libya.</span>Describe the mountains of shifting
deserts; that is to say the formation of waves of sand borne by the
wind, and of its mountains and hills, such as occur in Libya. Examples
may be seen on the wide sands of the Po and the Ticino, and other large
rivers.
