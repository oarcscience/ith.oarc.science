### 232.

<span class="margnote"> On indistinctness at great distances
(232-234).</span>Objects near to the eye will appear larger than those
at a distance.

Objects seen with two eyes will appear rounder than if they are seen
with only one.

Objects seen between light and shadow will show the most relief.
