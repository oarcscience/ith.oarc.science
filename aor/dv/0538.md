### 538.

### OF PLACING A FIGURE IN THE FOREGROUND OF A HISTORICAL PICTURE.

<span class="margnote"> The apparent size of figures in a picture
(538-539)</span>You must make the foremost figure in the picture less
than the size of nature in proportion to the number of braccia at which
you place it from the front line, and make the others in proportion by
the above rule.
