### 1461.

On the 23rd day of August, 12 lire from Pulisona. On the 14th of March
1494, Galeazzo came to live with me, agreeing to pay 5 lire a month for
his cost paying on the l4th day of each month.

His father gave me 2 Rhenish florins.

On the l4th of July, I had from Galeazze 2 Rhenish florins.
