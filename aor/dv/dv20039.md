### *XII.*

### *Architectural Designs.*

### *I. Plans for towns.*

*A. Sketches for laying out a new town with a double system of high-
level and low-level road-ways.*

*[Pl. LXXVII](pl077.htm#img_pl077), No.* 1 *(MS. B,* 15*<sup>b</sup>). A
general view of a town, with the roads outside it sloping up to the
high-level ways within.*

*[Pl. LXXVII](pl077.htm#img_pl077), No.* 3 *(MS. B,* 16*<sup>b</sup>.
see No.* 741*; and MS. B.* 15*<sup>b</sup>, see No.* 742*) gives a
partial view of the town, with its streets and houses, with explanatory
references.*

*[Pl. LXXVII](pl077.htm#img_pl077), No.* 2 *(MS. B, 15<sup>b</sup>; see
No. 743). View of a double staircaise with two opposite flights of
steps.*

*[Pl. LXXVIII](pl078.htm#img_pl078), Nos.* 2 *and* 3 *(MS. B,
37<sup>a</sup>). Sketches illustrating the connection of the two levels
of roads by means of steps. The lower galleries are lighted by openings
in the upper roadway.*

*B. Notes on removing houses (MS. Br. M.,* 270*<sup>b</sup>, see No.*
744*).*
