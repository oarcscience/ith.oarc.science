### 335.

<span class="margnote"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the
leg (335. 336).</span>*a b c* are equal to each other and to the space
from the armpit of the shoulder to the genitals and to the distance from
the tip of the fingers of the hand to the joint of the arm, and to the
half of the breast; and you must know that *c b* is the third part of
the height of a man from the shoulders to the ground; *d e f* are equal
to each other and equal to the greatest width of the shoulders.
