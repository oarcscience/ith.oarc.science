### THE SERPENT.

The serpent is a very large animal. When it sees a bird in the air it
draws in its breath so strongly that it draws the birds into its mouth
too. Marcus Regulus, the consul of the Roman army was attacked, with his
army, by such an animal and almost defeated. And this animal, being
killed by a catapult, measured 123 feet, that is 64 1/2 braccia and its
head was high above all the trees in a wood.
