### 273.

<span class="margnote"> The effect of colours in the camera obscura
(273-274).</span>The edges of a colour(ed object) transmitted through a
small hole are more conspicuous than the central portions.

The edges of the images, of whatever colour, which are transmitted
through a small aperture into a dark chamber will always be stronger
than the middle portions.
