### 1094.

The Egyptians, the Ethiopians, and the Arabs, in crossing the Nile with
camels, are accustomed to attach two bags on the sides of the camel's
bodies that is skins in the form shown underneath.

In these four meshes of the net the camels for baggage place their feet.
