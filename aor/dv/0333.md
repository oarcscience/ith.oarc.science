### 333.

<span class="margnote"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the
whole figure.</span>The cubit is one fourth of the height of a man and
is equal to the greatest width of the shoulders. From the joint of one
shoulder to the other is two faces and is equal to the distance from the
top of the breast to the navel.  <span id="fr_157"></span>[155](#fn_157)
From this point to the genitals is a face's length.
