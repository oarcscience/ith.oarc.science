### 615.

<span id="page_316">p. 316</span>

If you want to make foreshortened letters stretch the paper in a drawing
frame and then draw your letters and cut them out, and make the sunbeams
pass through the holes on to another stretched paper, and then fill up
the angles that are wanting.
