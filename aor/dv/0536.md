### 536.

### HOW HIGH THE POINT OF SIGHT SHOULD BE PLACED.

<span class="margnote"> On the choice of a position (536-537)</span>The
point of sight must be at the level of the eye of an ordinary man, and
the farthest limit of the plain where it touches the sky must be placed
at the level of that line where the earth and sky meet; excepting
mountains, which are independent of it.
