### 327.

The whole length of the foot will lie between the elbow and the wrist
and between the elbow and the inner angle of the arm

<span id="page_177">p. 177</span>

towards the breast when the arm is folded. The foot is as long as the
whole head of a man, that is from under the chin to the topmost part of
the head <span id="fr_145"></span>[143](#fn_145.md) in the way here
figured.
