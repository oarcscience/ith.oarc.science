### 1553.

Having become anxious.......... Bernardo di Simone, Silvestro di
Stefano, Bernardo di Jacopo, Francesco di Matteo Bonciani, Antonio di
Giovanni Ruberti, Antonio da Pistoia.... Antonio; He who has time and
waits for time, will lose his friends and his money.
