### 708.

### MEASUREMENT AND DIVISION OF A STATUE.

Divide the head into 12 degrees, and each degree divide into 12 points,
and each point into 12 minutes, and the minutes into minims and the
minims into semi minims.

Degree--point--minute--minim.
