*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 519.

That the light should fall upon a picture from one window only. This may
be seen in the case of objects in this form. If you want to represent a
round ball at a certain height you must make it oval in this shape, and
stand so far off as that by foreshortening it appears round.
