### 470.

### OF REPRESENTING WIND.

<span class="margnote"> The effect of wind on trees (470-473).</span>In
representing wind, besides the bending of the boughs and the reversing
of their leaves towards the quarter whence the wind comes, you should
also represent them amid clouds of fine dust mingled with the troubled
air.
