### II.

### FABLES.

### A FABLE.

<span class="margnote"> Fables on animals (1265-1270).</span>An oyster
being turned out together with other fish in the house of a fisherman
near the sea, he entreated a rat to take him to the sea. The rat
purposing to eat him bid him open; but as he bit him the oyster squeezed
his head and closed; and the cat came and killed him.
