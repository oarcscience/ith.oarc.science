### 1068.

A spring may be seen to rise in Sicily which at certain times of the
year throws out chesnut leaves in quantities; but in Sicily chesnuts do
not grow, hence it is evident that that spring must issue from some
abyss in Italy and then flow beneath the sea to break forth in Sicily.
