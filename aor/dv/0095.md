### 95.

<span id="page_58">p. 58</span>

Among objects of equal size that which is most remote from the eye will
look the smallest.  <span id="fr_49"></span>[47](#fn_49)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_49"></span>[58:47](0095.htm#fr_49) : This axiom,
sufficiently clear in itself, is in the original illustrated by a very
large diagram, constructed like that here reproduced under No. 108.

The same idea is repeated in C. A. I a; I a, stated as follows: *Infra
le cose d'equal grandeza quella si dimostra di minor figura che sara più
distante dall' ochio*.--
