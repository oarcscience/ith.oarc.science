### 812.

### ANATOMY.

<span class="margnote"> The divisions of the head (812.
813).</span>There are eleven elementary tissues:-- Cartilage, bones,
nerves, veins, arteries, fascia, ligament and sinews, skin, muscle and
fat.

### OF THE HEAD.

The divisions of the head are 10, viz. 5 external and 5 internal, the
external are the hair, skin, muscle, fascia and the skull; the internal
are the dura mater, the pia mater, \[which enclose\] the brain. The pia
mater and the dura mater come again underneath and enclose the brain;
then the rete mirabile, and the occipital bone, which supports the brain
from which the nerves spring.
