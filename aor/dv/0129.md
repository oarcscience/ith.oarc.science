*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 129.

### MEMORANDUM OF THINGS I REQUIRE TO HAVE GRANTED \[AS AXIOMS\] IN MY EXPLANATION OF PERSPECTIVE.

I ask to have this much granted me--to assert that every ray passing
through air of equal density throughout, travels in a straight line from
its cause to the object or place it falls upon.
