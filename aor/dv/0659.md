*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 659.

Though you may be able to tell or write the exact description of forms,
the painter can so depict them that they will appear alive, with the
shadow and light which show the expression of a face; which you cannot
accomplish with the pen though it can be achieved by the brush.
