### III.

### POLEMICS.--SPECULATION.

<span class="margnote"> Against Speculators (1205. 1206).</span>Oh!
speculators on things, boast not of knowing the things that nature
ordinarily brings about; but rejoice if you know the end of those things
which you yourself devise.
