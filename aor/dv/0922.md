*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 922.

First write of all water, in each of its motions; then describe all its
bottoms and their various materials, always referring to the
propositions concerning the said waters; and let the order be good, for
otherwise the work will be confused.

Describe all the forms taken by water from its greatest to its smallest
wave, and their causes.
