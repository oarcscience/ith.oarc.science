### 1343.

<span id="page_399">p. 399</span>

There are here, my Lord, many gentlemen who will undertake this expense
among them, if they are allowed to enjoy the use of admission to the
waters, the mills, and the passage of vessels and when it is sold to
them the price will be repaid to them by the canal of Martesana.
