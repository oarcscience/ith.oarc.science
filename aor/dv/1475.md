### 1475.

Archimedes gave the quadrature of a polygonal figure, but not of the
circle. Hence Archimedes never squared any figure with curved sides. He
squared the circle minus the smallest portion that the intellect can
conceive, that is the smallest point visible. <span
id="fr_518"></span>[818](#fn_518.md)
