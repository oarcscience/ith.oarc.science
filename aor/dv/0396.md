### 396.

<span id="page_206">p. 206</span>

If the plant n grows to the thickness shown at m, its branches will
correspond \[in thickness\] to the junction a b in consequence of the
growth inside as well as outside.

<span class="margnote"> The law of proportion in the growth of the
branches (396--402).</span>The branches of trees or plants have a twist
wherever a minor branch is given off; and this giving off the branch
forms a fork; this said fork occurs between two angles of which the
largest will be that which is on the side of the larger branch, and in
proportion, unless accident has spoilt it.
