*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 231.

The outlines of objects will be least clear when they are nearest to the
eye, and therefore remoter outlines will be clearer. Among objects which
are smaller than the pupil of the eye those will be less distinct which
are nearer to the eye.
