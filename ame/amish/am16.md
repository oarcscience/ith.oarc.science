### BOOKS ABOUT THE AMISH AND THE MENNONITES

• A number of books are to be had dealing with the Amish and the
Mennonites. Space will not allow listing of more than a few of the
titles, viz:

**Story of the Mennonites, The**. By C. Henry Smith. 8vo cloth (new
edition) Newton, Kansas 1950. (The story of the Mennonites from the days
of the Dutch Reformer, Menno Simons, to our present time; an important
book).

**Rosanna of the Amish**. By Joseph W. Yoder. 8vo cloth 319 pp
Huntingdon, Pa. (An intimate story of the Conservative Amish, by one
well-informed).

**Rosanna's Boys**. By Joseph W. Yoder. 8vo cloth 345 pp. (A follow-up
to **Rosanna of the Amish**).

**Story of the Pennsylvania Dutch**, The. Interesting lithographs in
color by C. H. DeWitt; text by Ann Hark. Quarto (Suitable to juvenile
and adult).

• Prices of the above titles, and many others on the Pennsylvania
Germans, may be had by writing for latest catalog. **The Aurand Press,
Harrisburg, Pa**.
