*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

TO HEAL BURNS.

Pound or press the juice of male fern, and put it on the burnt spots and
they will heal very fast. Better yet, however, if you smear the above
juice upon a rag, and put it on like a plaster.
