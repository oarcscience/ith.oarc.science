ANOTHER METHOD OF STOPPING FIRE.

Our dear Sarah journeyed through the land, having a fiery hot brand in
her hand. The fiery brand heats; the fiery brand sweats. Fiery brand,
stop your beat; fiery brand, stop your sweat.
