*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

FOR THE SWELLING OF CATTLE.

To Desh break no Flesh, but to Desh! While saying this, run your hand
along the back of the animal. + + +

NOTE.--The hand must be put upon the bare skin in all cases of using
sympathetic words.
