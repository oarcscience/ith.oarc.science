*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

FOR THE HOLLOW HORN IN COWS.

Bore a small hole in the hollow horn, milk the same cow, and squirt her
milk into the horn; this is the best cure. Use a syringe to squirt the
milk into the horn.
