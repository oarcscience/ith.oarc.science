<span id="page_15">p. 15</span>

A GOOD REMEDY FOR BAD WOUNDS AND BURNS.

The word of God, the milk of Jesus' mother, and Christ's blood, is for
all wounds and burnings good.

\+ + +

It is the safest way in all these cases to make the crosses with the
hand or thumb three times over the affected parts; that is to say, over
all those things to which the three crosses are attached.
