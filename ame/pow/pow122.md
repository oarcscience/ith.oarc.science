*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

A DIRECTION FOR A GYPSY SENTENCE, TO BE CARRIED ABOUT THE PERSON AS A
PROTECTION UNDER ALL CIRCUMSTANCES.

Like unto the prophet Jonas, as a type of Christ, who was guarded for
three days and three nights in the belly of a whale, thus shall the
Almighty God, as a Father, guard and protect me from all evil. J. J. J.
