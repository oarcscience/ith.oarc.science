*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

A REMEDY FOR EPILEPSY, PROVIDED THE SUBJECT HAD NEVER FALLEN INTO FIRE
OR WATER.

Write reversedly or backwards upon a piece of paper: "IT IS ALL OVER!"
This is to be written but once upon the paper; then put it in a
scarlet-red cloth, and then wrap it in a piece of unbleached linen, and
hang it around the neck on the first Friday of the new moon. The thread
with which it is tied must also be unbleached.
