TO CURE FITS OR CONVULSIONS.

You must go upon another person's land, and repeat the following words:
"I go before another court--I tie up my 77-fold fits." Then cut three
small twigs off any tree on the land; in each twig you must make a knot.
This must be done on a Friday morning before sunrise., in the decrease
of the moon unbeshrewdly. + + + Then over your body where you feel the
fits you make the

<span id="page_20">p. 20</span>

crosses. And thus they may be made in all cases where they are applied.
