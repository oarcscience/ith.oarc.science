TO PREVENT THE HESSIAN FLY FROM INJURING THE WHEAT.

Take pulverized charcoal, make ley of it, and soak the seed wheat in it;
take it out of the ley, and on every bushel of wheat sprinkle a quart of
urine; stir it well, then spread it out to dry.
