TO MAKE A WICK WHICH IS NEVER CONSUMED.

Take an ounce of asbestos and boil it in a quart of strong lye for two
hours; then pour off the lye and clarify what remains by pouring
rain-water on it three or four times, after which you can form a wick
from it which will never be consumed by the fire.
