Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend, by John George Hoffman, [1820], at Intangible Textual Heritage

TO CURE FITS OR CONVULSIONS.

You must go upon another person's land, and repeat the following words: "I go before another court--I tie up my 77-fold fits." Then cut three small twigs off any tree on the land; in each twig you must make a knot. This must be done on a Friday morning before sunrise., in the decrease of the moon unbeshrewdly. + + + Then over your body where you feel the fits you make the

p. 20

crosses. And thus they may be made in all cases where they are applied.
