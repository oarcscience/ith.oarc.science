WORDS TO BE SPOKEN WHILE MAKING DIVINATORY WANDS.

In making divinatory wands, they must be broken as before directed, and
while breaking and before using them, the following words must be
spoken:

Divining rod, do thou keep that power,  
Which God gave unto thee at the very first hour.
