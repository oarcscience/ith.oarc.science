AGAINST SWELLINGS.

"Three pure virgins went out on a journey to inspect a swelling and
sickness. The first one said, It is hoarse. The second said, It is not.
The third said, If it is not, then will our Lord Jesus Christ come."
This must be spoken in the name of the Holy Trinity.
