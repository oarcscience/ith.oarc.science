A VERY GOOD PLASTER.

Take wormwood, rue, medels, sheeprip-wort, pointy plantain, in equal
proportions, a larger proportion of bees'-wax and tallow, and some
spirits of turpentine; put it together in a pot, boil it well, and then
strain it, and you have a very good plaster.
