Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

ANOTHER WAY TO STOP BLEEDING, AND TO HEAL WOUNDS IN MAN AS WELL AS
ANIMALS.

On Christ's grave there grows three roses; the first is kind, the second
is valued among the rulers, and the third says: blood, thou must stop,
and wound, thou must heal. Everything prescribed for man in this book is
also applicable to animals.
