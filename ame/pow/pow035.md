TO MAKE A GOOD EYE-WATER.

Take four cents' worth of white vitriol, four cents' worth of prepared
spicewort (calamus root), four cents'

<span id="page_18">p. 18</span>

worth of cloves, a gill of good whiskey and a gill of water. Make the
calamus fine and mix all together; then use it after it has stood a few
hours.
