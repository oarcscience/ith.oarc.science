TO BANISH CONVULSIVE FEVERS.

Write the following letters on a piece of white paper, sew it on a piece
of linen or muslin, and bang it around the neck until the fever leaves
you:

<span id="page_17">p. 17</span>

A b a x a C a t a b a x  
A b a x a C a t a b a x  
A b a x a C a t a b a  
A b a x a C a t a b  
A b a x a C a t a  
A b a x a C a t  
A b a x a C a  
A b a x a C  
A b a x a  
A b a x  
A b a  
A b
