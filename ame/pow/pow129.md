AGAINST THE FEVER.

Pray early in the morning, and then turn your shirt around the left
sleeve, and say: Turn, thou, shirt, and thou, fever, do likewise turn.
(Do not forget to mention the name of the person having the fever.)
This, I tell thee, for thy repentance sake, in the name of God the
Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost. Amen. If you repeat this for three
successive mornings the fever will disappear.
