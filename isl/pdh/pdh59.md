## XVII

*Stanza 3*.—The allusion is to the expulsion of Adam from the Garden of
Eden.

*Stanza 4*.—Concerning the Last judgment, a beautiful tradition relates
that there are seven degrees of punishment, but eight of blessedness,
because God's mercy exceeds His justice.
