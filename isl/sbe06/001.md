# THE QUR'ÂN.

### THE OPENING CHAPTER.

### (I. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.

Praise belongs to God, the Lord of the worlds, the merciful, the
compassionate, the ruler of the day of judgment! Thee we serve and Thee
we ask for aid. \[5\] Guide us in the right path, the path of those Thou
art gracious to <span id="fr_39"></span>[1](#fn_39.md); not of those Thou
art wroth with; nor of those who err.
