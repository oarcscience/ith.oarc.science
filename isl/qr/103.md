### SURA CIII.--THE AFTERNOON \[XXVII.\]

MECCA.--3 Verses

In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful  
I SWEAR by the declining day!  
Verily, man's lot is cast amid destruction, <span
id="fr_71"></span>[1](#fn_71.md)  
Save those who believe and do the things which be right, and enjoin
truth and enjoin stedfastness on each other.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_71"></span>[1](103.htm#fr_71.md) Said to have been recited in
the Mosque shortly before his death by Muhammad. See Weil, p. 328.
