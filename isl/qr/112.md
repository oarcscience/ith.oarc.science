### SURA CXII.--THE UNITY \[X.\]

MECCA.--4 Verses

In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful  
SAY: He is God alone:  
God the eternal!  
He begetteth not, and He is not begotten;  
And there is none like unto Him.
