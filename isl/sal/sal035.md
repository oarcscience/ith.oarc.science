\(34\)

His supplication while prostrating

*In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful*

On the eve of Eid-ul-Fitr recite two Rakats Namaz. In first unit after

Surah Hamd recite this supplication 1000 times and in second unit after

Surah Hamd recite this supplication in prostration once.

O’ the possessor of Honour and O’ the doer of obligations O’ the one to

appoint Muhammad Mustafa (s.a.w.a.). Bless Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and

his progeny.
