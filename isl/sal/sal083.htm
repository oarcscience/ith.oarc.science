(82)

His supplication to be recited after praying

two rakats prayer in which Surah Yasin is

recited after Surah Hamd

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful

O Allah! O the one Who gets lost things returned, get the thing, which I

have lost, back to me.
