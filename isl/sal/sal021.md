\(20\)

Dua seeking forgiveness

*In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful*

O Allah forgive my sins of which You are aware and if I repeat forgive

me again. O Allah! I couldn't get an opportunity to fulfill the
covenants

against my evil deeds, forgive these sins also. O Allah, forgive the
sins

committed by the action of eyes, mistake of the lips, negligence of the

heart and by the movement of the tongue.
