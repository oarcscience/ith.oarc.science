# Sūra LXXIV.: Mudda<u>thth</u>ir, or One Wrapped Up.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_032.gif)

32. Kall<u>a</u> wa**a**lqamar**i**

32\. Nay, verily:  
By the Moon,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_033.gif)

33. Wa**a**llayli i<u>th</u> adbar**a**

33\. And by the Night  
As it retreateth,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_034.gif)

34. Wa**al**<u>ss</u>ub<u>h</u>i i<u>tha</u> asfar**a**

34\. And by the Dawn  
As it shineth forth,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_035.gif)

35. Innah<u>a</u> la-i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> alkubar**i**

35\. This is but one  
Of the mighty (portents),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_036.gif)

36. Na<u>th</u>eeran lilbashar**i**

36\. A warning to mankind,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_037.gif)

37. Liman sh<u>a</u>a minkum an yataqaddama aw yataakhkhar**a**

37\. To any of you that  
Chooses to press forward,  
Or to follow behind;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_038.gif)

38. Kullu nafsin bim<u>a</u> kasabat raheena**tun**

38\. Every soul will be (held)  
In pledge for its deeds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_039.gif)

39. Ill<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba alyameen**i**

39\. Except the Companions  
Of the Right Hand.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_040.gif)

40. Fee jann<u>a</u>tin yatas<u>a</u>aloon**a**

40\. (They will be) in Gardens  
(Of Delight): they will  
Question each other,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_041.gif)

41. AAani almujrimeen**a**

41\. And (ask) of the Sinners:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_042.gif)

42. M<u>a</u> salakakum fee saqar**a**

42\. "What led you  
Into Hell-Fire?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_043.gif)

43. Q<u>a</u>loo lam naku mina almu<u>s</u>alleen**a**

43\. They will say:  
"We were not of those  
Who prayed;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_044.gif)

44. Walam naku nu<u>t</u>AAimu almiskeen**a**

44\. "Nor were we of those  
Who fed the indigent;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_045.gif)

45. Wakunn<u>a</u> nakhoo<u>d</u>u maAAa alkh<u>a</u>-i<u>d</u>een**a**

45\. "But we used to talk  
Vanities with vain talkers;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_046.gif)

46. Wakunn<u>a</u> nuka<u>thth</u>ibu biyawmi a**l**ddeen**i**

46\. "And we used to deny  
The Day of Judgment,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_047.gif)

47. <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> alyaqeen**u**

47\. "Until there came to us  
(The Hour) that is certain."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_048.gif)

48. Fam<u>a</u> tanfaAAuhum shaf<u>a</u>AAatu
a**l**shsh<u>a</u>fiAAeen**a**

48\. Then will no intercession  
Of (any) intercessors  
Profit them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_049.gif)

49. Fam<u>a</u> lahum AAani a**l**tta<u>th</u>kirati
muAAri<u>d</u>een**a**

49\. When what is  
The matter with them  
That they turn away  
From admonition?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_050.gif)

50. Kaannahum <u>h</u>umurun mustanfira**tun**

50\. As if they were  
Affrighted asses,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_051.gif)

51. Farrat min qaswara**tin**

51\. Fleeing from a lion!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_052.gif)

52. Bal yureedu kullu imri-in minhum an yu/t<u>a</u>
<u>s</u>u<u>h</u>ufan munashshara**tan**

52\. Forsooth, each one of them  
Wants to be given  
Scrolls (of revelation) spread out!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_053.gif)

53. Kall<u>a</u> bal l<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>foona al-<u>a</u>khira**ta**

53\. By no means! But  
They fear not the Hereafter.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_054.gif)

54. Kall<u>a</u> innahu ta<u>th</u>kira**tun**

54\. Nay, this surely  
Is an admonition:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_055.gif)

55. Faman sh<u>a</u>a <u>th</u>akarah**u**

55\. Let any who will,  
Keep it in remembrance!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/074/074_056.gif)

56. Wam<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuroona ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a
All<u>a</u>hu huwa ahlu a**l**ttaqw<u>a</u> waahlu almaghfirat**i**

56\. But none will keep it  
In remembrance except  
As God wills: He  
Is the Lord of Righteousness,  
And the Lord of Forgiveness.
