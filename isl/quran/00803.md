# Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_020.gif)

20. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo
a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wal<u>a</u> tawallaw AAanhu
waantum tasmaAAoon**a**

20\. O ye who believe!  
Obey God and His Apostle,  
And turn not away from him  
When ye hear (him speak).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_021.gif)

21. Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka**a**lla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo
samiAAn<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon**a**

21\. Nor be like those who say,  
"We hear," but listen not:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_022.gif)

22. Inna sharra a**l**ddaw<u>a</u>bbi AAinda All<u>a</u>hi
a**l**<u>ss</u>ummu albukmu alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon**a**

22\. For the worst of beasts  
In the sight of God  
Are the deaf and the dumb,—  
Those who understand not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_023.gif)

23. Walaw AAalima All<u>a</u>hu feehim khayran laasmaAAahum walaw
asmaAAahum latawallaw wahum muAAri<u>d</u>oon**a**

23\. If God had found in them  
Any good, He would indeed  
Have made them listen:  
(As it is), if He had made them  
Listen, they would but have  
Turned back and declined (faith).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_024.gif)

24. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo istajeeboo
lill<u>a</u>hi wali**l**rrasooli i<u>tha</u> daAA<u>a</u>kum lim<u>a</u>
yu<u>h</u>yeekum wa**i**AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha ya<u>h</u>oolu bayna
almar-i waqalbihi waannahu ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon**a**

24\. O ye who believe!  
Give your response to God  
And His Apostle, when He  
Calleth you to that which  
Will give you life;  
And know that God  
Cometh in between a man  
And his heart, and that  
It is He to Whom  
Ye shall (all) be gathered.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_025.gif)

25. Wa**i**ttaqoo fitnatan l<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>eebanna alla<u>th</u>eena
*<u>th</u>*alamoo minkum kh<u>ass</u>atan wa**i**AAlamoo anna
All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b**i**

25\. And fear tumult or oppression,  
Which affecteth not in particular  
(Only) those of you who do wrong:  
And know that God  
Is strict in punishment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_026.gif)

26. Wa**o**<u>th</u>kuroo i<u>th</u> antum qaleelun
musta<u>d</u>AAafoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i takh<u>a</u>foona an
yatakha<u>tt</u>afakumu a**l**nn<u>a</u>su fa<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>kum
waayyadakum bina<u>s</u>rihi warazaqakum mina
a**l**<u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti laAAallakum tashkuroon**a**

26\. Call to mind when ye  
Were a small (band),  
Despised through the land,  
And afraid that men might  
Despoil and kidnap you;  
But He provided a safe asylum  
For you, strengthened you  
With His aid, and gave you  
Good things for sustenance:  
That ye might be grateful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_027.gif)

27. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u>
takhoonoo All<u>a</u>ha wa**al**rrasoola watakhoonoo
am<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>tikum waantum taAAlamoon**a**

27\. O ye that believe!  
Betray not the trust  
Of God and the Apostle,  
Nor misappropriate knowingly  
Things entrusted to you.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_028.gif)

28. Wa**i**AAlamoo annam<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lukum waawl<u>a</u>dukum
fitnatun waanna All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu ajrun AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**un**

28\. And know ye  
That your possessions  
And your progeny  
Are but a trial;  
And that it is God  
With whom lies  
Your highest reward.
