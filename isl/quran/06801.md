# Sūra LXVIII.: Qalam, or the Pen, or Nūn.

### Section 1

![](../gif/068/068_001.gif)

1. Noon wa**a**lqalami wam<u>a</u> yas<u>t</u>uroon**a**

1\. Nūn. By the Pen  
And by the (Record)  
Which (men) write,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_002.gif)

2. M<u>a</u> anta biniAAmati rabbika bimajnoon**in**

2\. Thou art not,  
By the grace of thy Lord,  
Mad or possessed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_003.gif)

3. Wa-inna laka laajran ghayra mamnoon**in**

3\. Nay, verily for thee  
Is a Reward unfailing:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_004.gif)

4. Wa-innaka laAAal<u>a</u> khuluqin AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**in**

4\. And thou (standest)  
On an exalted standard  
Of character.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_005.gif)

5. Fasatub<u>s</u>iru wayub<u>s</u>iroon**a**

5\. Soon wilt thou see,  
And they will see,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_006.gif)

6. Bi-ayyikumu almaftoon**u**

6\. Which of you is  
Afflicted with madness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_007.gif)

7. Inna rabbaka huwa aAAlamu biman <u>d</u>alla AAan sabeelihi wahuwa
aAAlamu bi**a**lmuhtadeen**a**

7\. Verily it is thy Lord  
That knoweth best,  
Which (among men)  
Hath strayed from His Path:  
And He knoweth best  
Those who receive  
(True) Guidance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_008.gif)

8. Fal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAi almuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

8\. So hearken not  
To those who  
Deny (the Truth),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_009.gif)

9. Waddoo law tudhinu fayudhinoon**a**

9\. Their desire is that  
Thou shouldst be pliant:  
So would they be pliant.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_010.gif)

10. Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAA kulla <u>h</u>all<u>a</u>fin maheen**in**

10\. Heed not the type  
Of despicable man,—  
Ready with oaths,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_011.gif)

11. Hamm<u>a</u>zin mashsh<u>a</u>-in binameem**in**

11\. A slanderer, going about  
With calumnies,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_012.gif)

12. Mann<u>a</u>AAin lilkhayri muAAtadin atheem**in**

12\. (Habitually) hindering (all) good,  
Transgressing beyond bounds,  
Deep in sin,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_013.gif)

13. AAutullin baAAda <u>tha</u>lika zaneem**in**

13\. Violent (and cruel),—  
With all that, base-born,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_014.gif)

14. An k<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u> m<u>a</u>lin wabaneen**a**

14\. Because he possesses  
Wealth and (numerous) sons.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_015.gif)

15. I<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u>
q<u>a</u>la as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen**a**

15\. When to him are rehearsed  
Our Signs,  
"Tales of the Ancients",  
He cries!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_016.gif)

16. Sanasimuhu AAal<u>a</u> alkhur<u>t</u>oom**i**

16\. Soon shall We brand  
(The beast) on the snout!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_017.gif)

17. Inn<u>a</u> balawn<u>a</u>hum kam<u>a</u> balawn<u>a</u>
a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba aljannati i<u>th</u> aqsamoo
laya<u>s</u>rimunnah<u>a</u> mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een**a**

17\. Verily We have tried them  
As We tried the People  
Of the Garden,  
When they resolved to gather  
The fruits of the (garden)  
In the morning,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_018.gif)

18. Wal<u>a</u> yastathnoon**a**

18\. But made no reservation,  
("If it be God's Will").

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_019.gif)

19. Fa<u>ta</u>fa AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-ifun min rabbika wahum
n<u>a</u>-imoon**a**

19\. Then there came  
On the (garden)  
A visitation from thy Lord,  
(Which swept away) all around,  
While they were asleep.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_020.gif)

20. Faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>at ka**al**<u>ss</u>areem**i**

20\. So the (garden) became,  
By the morning, like  
A dark and desolate spot,  
(Whose fruit had been gathered).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_021.gif)

21. Fatan<u>a</u>daw mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een**a**

21\. As the morning broke,  
They called out,  
One to another,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_022.gif)

22. Ani ighdoo AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arthikum in kuntum
<u>sa</u>rimeen**a**

22\. "Go ye to your tilth  
(Betimes) in the morning,  
If ye would gather  
The fruits."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_023.gif)

23. Fa**i**n<u>t</u>alaqoo wahum yatakh<u>a</u>fatoon**a**

23\. So they departed, conversing  
In secret low tones, (saying)—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_024.gif)

24. An l<u>a</u> yadkhulannah<u>a</u> alyawma AAalaykum miskeen**un**

24\. "Let not a single indigent  
Person break in upon you  
Into the (garden) this day."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_025.gif)

25. Waghadaw AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ardin q<u>a</u>direen**a**

25\. And they opened the morning,  
Strong in an (unjust) resolve.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_026.gif)

26. Falamm<u>a</u> raawh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u>
la<u>da</u>lloon**a**

26\. But when they saw  
The (garden), they said:  
"We have surely lost our way:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_027.gif)

27. Bal na<u>h</u>nu ma<u>h</u>roomoon**a**

27\. "Indeed we are shut out  
(Of the fruits of our labour)!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_028.gif)

28. Q<u>a</u>la awsa<u>t</u>uhum alam aqul lakum lawl<u>a</u>
tusabbi<u>h</u>oon**a**

28\. Said one of them,  
More just (than the rest):  
"Did I not say to you,  
"Why not glorify (God)?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_029.gif)

29. Q<u>a</u>loo sub<u>ha</u>na rabbin<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u>
*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

29\. They said: "Glory  
To our Lord! Verily we  
Have been doing wrong!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_030.gif)

30. Faaqbala baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in
yatal<u>a</u>wamoon**a**

30\. Then they turned, one  
Against another, in reproach.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_031.gif)

31. Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u>
<u>ta</u>gheen**a**

31\. They said: "Alas for us!  
We have indeed transgressed!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_032.gif)

32. AAas<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> an yubdilan<u>a</u> khayran minh<u>a</u>
inn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> rabbin<u>a</u> r<u>a</u>ghiboon**a**

32\. "It may be that our Lord  
Will give us in exchange  
A better (garden) than this:  
For we do turn to Him  
(In repentance)!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_033.gif)

33. Ka<u>tha</u>lika alAAa<u>tha</u>bu walaAAa<u>tha</u>bu
al-<u>a</u>khirati akbaru law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon**a**

33\. Such is the Punishment  
(In this life); but greater  
Is the Punishment  
In the Hereafter,—  
If only they knew!
