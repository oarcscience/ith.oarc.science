# Sūra CI.: Al-Qāri’a, or The Day of Noise and Clamour.

### Section 1

![](../gif/101/101_001.gif)

1. Alq<u>a</u>riAAa**tu**

1\. The (Day) of  
Noise and Clamour:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_002.gif)

2. M<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>riAAa**tu**

2\. What is the (Day)  
Of Noise and Clamour?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_003.gif)

3. Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>riAAa**tu**

3\. And what will explain  
To thee what the (Day)  
Of Noise and Clamour is?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_004.gif)

4. Yawma yakoonu a**l**nn<u>a</u>su ka**a**lfar<u>a</u>shi
almabthooth**i**

4\. (It is) a Day whereon  
Men will be like moths  
Scattered about,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_005.gif)

5. Watakoonu aljib<u>a</u>lu ka**a**lAAihni almanfoosh**i**

5\. And the mountains  
Will be like carded wool.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_006.gif)

6. Faamm<u>a</u> man thaqulat maw<u>a</u>zeenuh**u**

6\. Then, he whose  
Balance (of good deeds)  
Will be (found) heavy,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_007.gif)

7. Fahuwa fee AAeeshatin r<u>ad</u>iya**tin**

7\. Will be in a Life  
Of good pleasure and  
satisfaction.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_008.gif)

8. Waamm<u>a</u> man khaffat maw<u>a</u>zeenuh**u**

8\. But he whose  
Balance (of good deeds)  
Will be (found) light,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_009.gif)

9. Faommuhu h<u>a</u>wiya**tun**

9\. Will have his home  
In a (bottomless) Pit.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_010.gif)

10. Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> hiya**h**

10\. And what will explain  
To thee what this is?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/101/101_011.gif)

11. N<u>a</u>run <u>ha</u>miya**tun**

11\. (It is) a Fire  
Blazing fiercely!
