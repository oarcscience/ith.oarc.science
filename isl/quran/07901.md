# Sara LXXIX.: Nāzi’āt, or Those Who Tear Out.

### Section 1

![](../gif/079/079_001.gif)

1. Wa**al**nn<u>a</u>ziAA<u>a</u>ti gharq<u>a</u>**n**

1\. By the (angels)  
Who tear out  
(The souls of the wicked)  
With violence;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_002.gif)

2. Wa**al**nn<u>a</u>shi<u>ta</u>ti nash<u>ta</u>**n**

2\. By those who gently  
Draw out (the souls  
Of the blessed);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_003.gif)

3. Wa**al**ss<u>a</u>bi<u>ha</u>ti sab<u>ha</u>**n**

3\. And by those who glide  
Along (on errands of mercy),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_004.gif)

4. Fa**al**ss<u>a</u>biq<u>a</u>ti sabq<u>a</u>**n**

4\. Then press forward  
As in a race,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_005.gif)

5. Fa**a**lmudabbir<u>a</u>ti amr<u>a</u>**n**

5\. Then arrange to do  
(The Commands of their Lord),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_006.gif)

6. Yawma tarjufu a**l**rr<u>a</u>jifa**tu**

6\. One Day everything that  
Can be in commotion will  
Be in violent commotion,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_007.gif)

7. TatbaAAuh<u>a</u> a**l**rr<u>a</u>difa**tu**

7\. Followed by oft-repeated  
(Commotions):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_008.gif)

8. Quloobun yawma-i<u>th</u>in w<u>a</u>jifa**tun**

8\. Hearts that Day  
Will be in agitation;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_009.gif)

9. Ab<u>sa</u>ruh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>shiAAa**tun**

9\. Cast down will be  
(Their owners’) eyes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_010.gif)

10. Yaqooloona a-inn<u>a</u> lamardoodoona fee al<u>ha</u>fira**ti**

10\. They say (now): "What!  
Shall we indeed be  
Returned to (our) former state?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_011.gif)

11. A-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAi*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>man nakhira**tan**

11\. "What!—when we shall  
Have become rotten bones?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_012.gif)

12. Q<u>a</u>loo tilka i<u>th</u>an karratun kh<u>a</u>sira**tun**

12\. They say: "It would  
In that case, be  
A return with loss!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_013.gif)

13. Fa-innam<u>a</u> hiya zajratun w<u>ah</u>ida**tun**

13\. But verily, it will  
Be but a single  
(Compelling) Cry,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_014.gif)

14. Fa-i<u>tha</u> hum bi**al**ss<u>a</u>hira**ti**

14\. When, behold, they  
Will be in the (full)  
Awakening (to Judgment).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_015.gif)

15. Hal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu moos<u>a</u>

15\. Has the story  
Of Moses reached thee?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_016.gif)

16. I<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu bi**a**lw<u>a</u>di
almuqaddasi <u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>**n**

16\. Behold, thy Lord did call  
To him in the sacred valley  
Of Ṭuwā:—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_017.gif)

17. I<u>th</u>hab il<u>a</u> firAAawna innahu <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>

17\. "Go thou to Pharaoh,  
For he has indeed  
Transgressed all bounds:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_018.gif)

18. Faqul hal laka il<u>a</u> an tazakk<u>a</u>

18\. "And say to him,  
"Wouldst thou that thou  
Shouldst he purified  
(From sin)?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_019.gif)

19. Waahdiyaka il<u>a</u> rabbika fatakhsh<u>a</u>

19\. "And that I guide thee  
To thy Lord, so thou  
Shouldst fear Him?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_020.gif)

20. Faar<u>a</u>hu al-<u>a</u>yata alkubr<u>a</u>

20\. Then did (Moses) show him  
The Great Sign.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_021.gif)

21. Faka<u>thth</u>aba waAAa<u>sa</u>

21\. But (Pharaoh) rejected it  
And disobeyed (guidance);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_022.gif)

22. Thumma adbara yasAA<u>a</u>

22\. Further, he turned his back,  
Striving hard (against God).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_023.gif)

23. Fa<u>h</u>ashara fan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>

23\. Then he collected (his men)  
And made a proclamation,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_024.gif)

24. Faq<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> rabbukumu al-aAAl<u>a</u>

24\. Saying, "I am your Lord,  
Most High".

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_025.gif)

25. Faakha<u>th</u>ahu All<u>a</u>hu nak<u>a</u>la al-<u>a</u>khirati
wa**a**l-ool<u>a</u>

25\. But God did punish him,  
(And made an) example  
Of him,—in the Hereafter,  
As in this life.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/079/079_026.gif)

26. Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika laAAibratan liman yakhsh<u>a</u>

26\. Verily in this is  
An instructive warning  
For whosoever feareth (God).
