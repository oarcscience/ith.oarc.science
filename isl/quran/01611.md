# Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.

### Section 11

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_077.gif)

77. Walill<u>a</u>hi ghaybu a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> amru a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAati ill<u>a</u>
kalam<u>h</u>i alba<u>s</u>ari aw huwa aqrabu inna All<u>a</u>ha
AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer**un**

77\. To God belongeth the Mystery  
Of the heavens and the earth.  
And the Decision of the Hour  
(Of Judgment) is as  
The twinkling of an eye,  
Or even quicker:  
For God hath power  
Over all things.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_078.gif)

78. Wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu akhrajakum min bu<u>t</u>ooni ommah<u>a</u>tikum
l<u>a</u> taAAlamoona shay-an wajaAAala lakumu a**l**ssamAAa
wa**a**l-ab<u>sa</u>ra wa**a**l-af-idata laAAallakum tashkuroon**a**

78\. It is He Who brought you  
Forth from the wombs  
Of your mothers when  
Ye knew nothing; and He  
Gave you hearing and sight  
And intelligence and affections:  
That ye may give thanks  
(To God).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_079.gif)

79. Alam yaraw il<u>a</u> a**l**<u>tt</u>ayri musakhkhar<u>a</u>tin fee
jawwi a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> yumsikuhunna ill<u>a</u>
All<u>a</u>hu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin
yu/minoon**a**

79\. Do they not look at  
The birds, held poised  
In the midst of (the air  
And) the sky? Nothing  
Holds them up but (the power  
Of) God. Verily in this  
Are Signs for those who believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_080.gif)

80. Wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakum min buyootikum sakanan wajaAAala
lakum min juloodi al-anAA<u>a</u>mi buyootan tastakhiffoonah<u>a</u>
yawma *<u>th</u>*aAAnikum wayawma iq<u>a</u>matikum wamin
a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>fih<u>a</u> waawb<u>a</u>rih<u>a</u>
waashAA<u>a</u>rih<u>a</u> ath<u>a</u>than wamat<u>a</u>AAan il<u>a</u>
<u>h</u>een**in**

80\. It is God Who made your habitations  
Homes of rest and quiet  
For you; and made for you,  
Out of the skins of animals,  
(Tents for) dwellings, which  
Ye find so light (and handy)  
When ye travel and when  
Ye stop (in your travels);  
And out of their wool,  
And their soft fibres  
(Between wool and hair),  
And their hair, rich stuff  
And articles of convenience  
(To serve you) for a time.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_081.gif)

81. Wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakum mimm<u>a</u> khalaqa
*<u>th</u>*il<u>a</u>lan wajaAAala lakum mina aljib<u>a</u>li
akn<u>a</u>nan wajaAAala lakum sar<u>a</u>beela taqeekumu al<u>h</u>arra
wasar<u>a</u>beela taqeekum ba/sakum ka<u>tha</u>lika yutimmu niAAmatahu
AAalaykum laAAallakum tuslimoon**a**

81\. It is God Who made.  
Out of the things He created,  
Some things to give you shade;  
Of the hills He made some  
For your shelter; He made you  
Garments to protect you  
From heat, and coats of mail  
To protect you from  
Your (mutual) violence.  
Thus does He complete  
His favours on you, that  
Ye may bow to His Will  
(In Islām).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_082.gif)

82. Fa-in tawallaw fa-innam<u>a</u> AAalayka albal<u>a</u>ghu
almubeen**u**

82\. But if they turn away,  
Thy duty is only to preach  
The Clear Message.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_083.gif)

83. YaAArifoona niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi thumma yunkiroonah<u>a</u>
waaktharuhumu alk<u>a</u>firoon**a**

83\. They recognise the favours  
Of God; then they deny them;  
And most of them  
Are (creatures) ungrateful.
