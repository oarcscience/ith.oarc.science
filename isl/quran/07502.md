# Sūra LXXV.: Qiyāmat, or the Resurrection.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_031.gif)

31. Fal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>addaqa wal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>all<u>a</u>

31\. So he gave nothing  
In charity, nor  
Did he pray!—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_032.gif)

32. Wal<u>a</u>kin ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>

32\. But on the contrary,  
He rejected Truth  
And turned away!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_033.gif)

33. Thumma <u>th</u>ahaba il<u>a</u> ahlihi yatama<u>tta</u>

33\. Then did he stalk  
To his family  
In full conceit!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_034.gif)

34. Awl<u>a</u> laka faawl<u>a</u>

34\. Woe to thee,  
(O man!, yea, woe!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_035.gif)

35. Thumma awl<u>a</u> laka faawl<u>a</u>

35\. Again, woe to thee,  
(O man!), yea, woe!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_036.gif)

36. Aya<u>h</u>sabu al-ins<u>a</u>nu an yutraka sud<u>a</u>**n**

36\. Does Man think  
That he will be left  
Uncontrolled, (without purpose)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_037.gif)

37. Alam yaku nu<u>t</u>fatan min manayyin yumn<u>a</u>

37\. Was he not a drop  
Of sperm emitted  
(In lowly form)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_038.gif)

38. Thumma k<u>a</u>na AAalaqatan fakhalaqa fasaww<u>a</u>

38\. Then did he become  
A leech-like clot;  
Then did (God) make  
And fashion (him)  
In due proportion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_039.gif)

39. FajaAAala minhu a**l**zzawjayni a**l**<u>thth</u>akara
wa**a**l-onth<u>a</u>

39\. And of him He made  
Two sexes, male  
And female.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_040.gif)

40. Alaysa <u>tha</u>lika biq<u>a</u>dirin AAal<u>a</u> an
yu<u>h</u>yiya almawt<u>a</u>

40\. Has not He, (the same),  
The power to give life  
To the dead?
