# Sūra XIII.: Ra’d, or Thunder.

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/013/013_027.gif)

27. Wayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayhi
<u>a</u>yatun min rabbihi qul inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>d</u>illu man
yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee ilayhi man an<u>a</u>b**a**

27\. The Unbelievers say: "Why  
Is not a Sign sent down  
To him from his Lord?"  
Say: "Truly God leaveth,  
To stray, whom He will;  
But He guideth to Himself  
Those who turn to Him  
In penitence,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/013/013_028.gif)

28. Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wata<u>t</u>ma-innu quloobuhum
bi<u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi al<u>a</u> bi<u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi
ta<u>t</u>ma-innu alquloob**u**

28\. "Those who believe, and whose hearts  
Find satisfaction in the remembrance  
Of God: for without doubt  
In the remembrance of God  
Do hearts find satisfaction.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/013/013_029.gif)

29. Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo
a**l**<u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti <u>t</u>oob<u>a</u> lahum wa<u>h</u>usnu
ma<u>a</u>b**in**

29\. "For those who believe  
And work righteousness,  
Is (every) blessedness,  
And a beautiful place  
Of (final) return."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/013/013_030.gif)

30. Ka<u>tha</u>lika arsaln<u>a</u>ka fee ommatin qad khalat min
qablih<u>a</u> omamun litatluwa AAalayhimu alla<u>th</u>ee
aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka wahum yakfuroona
bi**al**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni qul huwa rabbee l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha
ill<u>a</u> huwa AAalayhi tawakkaltu wa-ilayhi mat<u>a</u>b**i**

30\. Thus have We sent thee  
Amongst a People before whom  
(Long since) have (other) Peoples  
(Gone and) passed away;  
In order that thou mightest  
Rehearse unto them what We  
Send down unto thee by inspiration;  
Yet do they reject (Him),  
The Most Gracious!  
Say: "He is my Lord!  
There is no god but He!  
On Him is my trust,  
And to Him do I turn!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/013/013_031.gif)

31. Walaw anna qur-<u>a</u>nan suyyirat bihi aljib<u>a</u>lu aw
qu<u>tt</u>iAAat bihi al-ar<u>d</u>u aw kullima bihi almawt<u>a</u> bal
lill<u>a</u>hi al-amru jameeAAan afalam yay-asi alla<u>th</u>eena
<u>a</u>manoo an law yash<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hu lahad<u>a</u>
a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa jameeAAan wal<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>lu alla<u>th</u>eena
kafaroo tu<u>s</u>eebuhum bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>anaAAoo q<u>a</u>riAAatun
aw ta<u>h</u>ullu qareeban min d<u>a</u>rihim <u>h</u>att<u>a</u>
ya/tiya waAAdu All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yukhlifu
almeeAA<u>a</u>d**a**

31\. If there were a Qur-ān  
With which mountains were moved,  
Or the earth were cloven asunder,  
Or the dead were made to speak,  
(This would be the one!)  
But, truly, the Command is  
With God in all things!'  
Do not the Believers know,  
That, had God (so) willed,  
He could have guided  
All mankind (to the Right)?  
But the Unbelievers,—never  
Will disaster cease to seize  
Them for their (ill) deeds,  
Or to settle close to their homes,  
Until the promise of God  
Come to pass, for, verily,  
God will not fail  
In His promise.
