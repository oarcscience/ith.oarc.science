# Sūra LV.: Raḥmān, or (God) Most Gracious.

### Section 1

![](../gif/055/055_001.gif)

1. A**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>n**u**

1\. (God) Most Gracious!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_002.gif)

2. AAallama alqur-<u>a</u>n**a**

2\. It is He Who has  
Taught the Qur-ān.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_003.gif)

3. Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>n**a**

3\. He has created man:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_004.gif)

4. AAallamahu albay<u>a</u>n**a**

4\. He has taught him speech  
(And Intelligence).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_005.gif)

5. A**l**shshamsu wa**a**lqamaru bi<u>h</u>usb<u>a</u>n**in**

5\. The sun and the moon  
Follow courses (exactly) computed;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_006.gif)

6. Wa**al**nnajmu wa**al**shshajaru yasjud<u>a</u>n**i**

6\. And the herbs and the trees—  
Both (alike) bow in adoration.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_007.gif)

7. Wa**al**ssam<u>a</u>a rafaAAah<u>a</u> wawa<u>d</u>aAAa
almeez<u>a</u>n**a**

7\. And the Firmament has He  
Raised high, and He has set up  
The Balance (of Justice),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_008.gif)

8. All<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>ghaw fee almeez<u>a</u>n**i**

8\. In order that ye may  
Not transgress (due) balance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_009.gif)

9. Waaqeemoo alwazna bi**a**lqis<u>t</u>i wal<u>a</u> tukhsiroo
almeez<u>a</u>n**a**

9\. So establish weight with justice  
And fall not short  
In the balance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_010.gif)

10. Wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>a wa<u>d</u>aAAah<u>a</u> lil-an<u>a</u>m**i**

10\. It is He Who has  
Spread out the earth  
For (His) creatures:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_011.gif)

11. Feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun wa**al**nnakhlu <u>tha</u>tu
al-akm<u>a</u>m**i**

11\. Therein is fruit  
And date-palms, producing  
Spathes (enclosing dates);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_012.gif)

12. Wa**a**l<u>h</u>abbu <u>th</u>oo alAAa<u>s</u>fi
wa**al**rray<u>ha</u>n**i**

12\. Also corn, with (its)  
Leaves and stalk for fodder,  
And sweet-smelling plants.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_013.gif)

13. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

13\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_014.gif)

14. Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>na min <u>s</u>al<u>sa</u>lin
ka**a**lfakhkh<u>a</u>r**i**

14\. He created man  
From sounding clay  
Like unto pottery,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_015.gif)

15. Wakhalaqa alj<u>a</u>nna min m<u>a</u>rijin min n<u>a</u>r**in**

15\. And He created Jinns  
From fire free of smoke:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_016.gif)

16. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

16\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_017.gif)

17. Rabbu almashriqayni warabbu almaghribayn**i**

17\. (He is) Lord  
Of the two Easts  
And Lord  
Of the two Wests:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_018.gif)

18. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

18\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_019.gif)

19. Maraja alba<u>h</u>rayni yaltaqiy<u>a</u>n**i**

19\. He has let free  
The two bodies  
Of flowing water,  
Meeting together:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_020.gif)

20. Baynahum<u>a</u> barzakhun l<u>a</u> yabghiy<u>a</u>n**i**

20\. Between them is a Barrier  
Which they do not transgress:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_021.gif)

21. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

21\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_022.gif)

22. Yakhruju minhum<u>a</u> allu/luo wa**a**lmarj<u>a</u>n**u**

22\. Out of them come  
Pearls and Coral:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_023.gif)

23. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>ni

23\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_024.gif)

24. Walahu aljaw<u>a</u>ri almunsha<u>a</u>tu fee alba<u>h</u>ri
ka**a**l-aAAl<u>a</u>m**i**

24\. And His are the Ships  
Sailing smoothly through the seas,  
Lofty as mountains:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_025.gif)

25. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

25\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?
