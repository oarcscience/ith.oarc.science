# Sūra LIII.: Najm, or the Star.

### Section 1

![](../gif/053/053_001.gif)

1. Wa**al**nnajmi i<u>tha</u> haw<u>a</u>

1\. By the Star  
When it goes down,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_002.gif)

2. M<u>a</u> <u>d</u>alla <u>sah</u>ibukum wam<u>a</u> ghaw<u>a</u>

2\. Your Companion is neither  
Astray nor being misled,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_003.gif)

3. Wam<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqu AAani alhaw<u>a</u>

3\. Nor does he say (aught)  
Of (his own) Desire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_004.gif)

4. In huwa ill<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>yun yoo<u>ha</u>

4\. It is no less than  
Inspiration sent down to him:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_005.gif)

5. AAallamahu shadeedu alquw<u>a</u>

5\. He was taught by one  
Mighty in Power,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_006.gif)

6. <u>Th</u>oo mirratin fa**i**staw<u>a</u>

6\. Endued with Wisdom:  
For he appeared  
(In stately form)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_007.gif)

7. Wahuwa bi**a**l-ofuqi al-aAAl<u>a</u>

7\. While he was in  
The highest part  
Of the horizon:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_008.gif)

8. Thumma dan<u>a</u> fatadall<u>a</u>

8\. Then he approached  
And came closer,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_009.gif)

9. Fak<u>a</u>na q<u>a</u>ba qawsayni aw adn<u>a</u>

9\. And was at a distance  
Of but two bow-lengths  
Or (even) nearer;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_010.gif)

10. Faaw<u>ha</u> il<u>a</u> AAabdihi m<u>a</u> aw<u>ha</u>

10\. So did (God) convey  
The inspiration to His Servant—  
(Conveyed) what He (meant)  
To convey.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_011.gif)

11. M<u>a</u> ka<u>th</u>aba alfu-<u>a</u>du m<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u>

11\. The (Prophet's) (mind and) heart  
In no way falsified  
That which he saw.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_012.gif)

12. Afatum<u>a</u>roonahu AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yar<u>a</u>

12\. Will ye then dispute  
With him concerning  
What he saw?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_013.gif)

13. Walaqad ra<u>a</u>hu nazlatan okhr<u>a</u>

13\. For indeed he saw him  
At a second descent,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_014.gif)

14. AAinda sidrati almuntah<u>a</u>

14\. Near the Lote-tree  
Beyond which none may pass:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_015.gif)

15. AAindah<u>a</u> jannatu alma/w<u>a</u>

15\. Near it is the Garden  
Of Abode.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_016.gif)

16. I<u>th</u> yaghsh<u>a</u> a**l**ssidrata m<u>a</u> yaghsh<u>a</u>

16\. Behold, the Lote-tree  
Was shrouded  
(In mystery unspeakable!)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_017.gif)

17. M<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>gha alba<u>s</u>aru wam<u>a</u>
<u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>

17\. (His) sight never swerved,  
Nor did it go wrong!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_018.gif)

18. Laqad ra<u>a</u> min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihi alkubr<u>a</u>

18\. For truly did he see,  
Of the Signs of his Lord,  
The Greatest!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_019.gif)

19. Afaraaytumu all<u>a</u>ta wa**a**lAAuzz<u>a</u>

19\. Have ye seen  
Lāt, and ‘Uzzā,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_020.gif)

20. Waman<u>a</u>ta a**l**thth<u>a</u>lithata al-okhr<u>a</u>

20\. And another,  
The third (goddess), Manāt?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_021.gif)

21. Alakumu a**l**<u>thth</u>akaru walahu al-onth<u>a</u>

21\. What! For you  
The male sex,  
And for Him, the female?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_022.gif)

22. Tilka i<u>th</u>an qismatun <u>d</u>eez<u>a</u>

22\. Behold, such would be  
Indeed a division  
Most unfair!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_023.gif)

23. In hiya ill<u>a</u> asm<u>a</u>on sammaytumooh<u>a</u> antum
wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> min
sul<u>ta</u>nin in yattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> a**l***<u>thth</u>*anna
wam<u>a</u> tahw<u>a</u> al-anfusu walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum min rabbihimu
alhud<u>a</u>

23\. These are nothing but names  
Which ye have devised,—  
Ye and your fathers,  
For which God has sent  
Down no authority (whatever).  
They follow nothing but  
Conjecture and what  
Their own souls desire!—  
Even though there has already  
Come to them Guidance  
From their Lord!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_024.gif)

24. Am lil-ins<u>a</u>ni m<u>a</u> tamann<u>a</u>

24\. Nay, shall man have (just)  
Anything he hankers after?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_025.gif)

25. Falill<u>a</u>hi al-<u>a</u>khiratu wa**a**l-ool<u>a</u>

25\. But it is to God  
That the End and  
The Beginning (of all things)  
Belong.
