# Sūra CVII.: Mā’ūn, or Neighbourly Needs.

### Section 1

![](../gif/107/107_001.gif)

1. Araayta alla<u>th</u>ee yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bi**al**ddeen**i**

1\. Seest thou one  
Who denies the Judgment  
(To come)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_002.gif)

2. Fa<u>tha</u>lika alla<u>th</u>ee yaduAAAAu alyateem**a**

2\. Then such is the (man)  
Who repulses the orphan  
(With harshness),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_003.gif)

3. Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>u<u>dd</u>u AAal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi
almiskeen**i**

3\. And encourages not  
The feeding of the indigent.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_004.gif)

4. Fawaylun lilmu<u>s</u>alleen**a**

4\. So woe to the worshippers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_005.gif)

5. Alla<u>th</u>eena hum AAan <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim s<u>a</u>hoon**a**

5\. Who are neglectful  
Of their Prayers,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_006.gif)

6. Alla<u>th</u>eena hum yur<u>a</u>oon**a**

6\. Those who (want but)  
To be seen (of men),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/107/107_007.gif)

7. WayamnaAAoona alm<u>a</u>AAoon**a**  

7\. But refuse (to supply)  
(Even) neighbourly needs.
