# Sūra XLVIII.: Fat-ḥ or Victory.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_011.gif)

11. Sayaqoolu laka almukhallafoona mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi
shaghalatn<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lun<u>a</u> waahloon<u>a</u>
fa**i**staghfir lan<u>a</u> yaqooloona bi-alsinatihim m<u>a</u> laysa
fee quloobihim qul faman yamliku lakum mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an in
ar<u>a</u>da bikum <u>d</u>arran aw ar<u>a</u>da bikum nafAAan bal
k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u>**n**

11\. The desert Arabs who  
Lagged behind will  
Say to thee:  
"We were engaged in  
(Looking after) our flocks  
And herds, and our families:  
Do thou then ask  
Forgiveness for us.  
They say with their tongues  
What is not in their hearts.  
Say: "Who then has  
Any power at all  
(To intervene) on your behalf  
With God, if His Will  
Is to give you some loss  
Or to give you some profit?  
But God is well acquainted  
With all that ye do.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_012.gif)

12. Bal *<u>th</u>*anantum an lan yanqaliba a**l**rrasoolu
wa**a**lmu/minoona il<u>a</u> ahleehim abadan wazuyyina <u>tha</u>lika
fee quloobikum wa*<u>th</u>*anantum *<u>th</u>*anna a**l**ssaw-i
wakuntum qawman boor<u>a</u>**n**

12\. "Nay, ye thought that  
The Apostle and the Believers  
Would never return to  
Their families; this seemed  
Pleasing in your hearts, and  
Ye conceived an evil thought,  
For ye are a people  
Lost (in wickedness)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_013.gif)

13. Waman lam yu/min bi**A**ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi fa-inn<u>a</u>
aAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena saAAeer<u>a</u>**n**

13\. And if any believe not  
In God and His Apostle,  
We have prepared,  
For those who reject God,  
A Blazing Fire!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_014.gif)

14. Walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i yaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu
man yash<u>a</u>o wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran
ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u>**n**

14\. To God belongs the dominion  
Of the heavens and the earth:  
He forgives whom He wills,  
And He punishes whom He  
Wills: but God is  
Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_015.gif)

15. Sayaqoolu almukhallafoona i<u>tha</u> in<u>t</u>alaqtum il<u>a</u>
magh<u>a</u>nima lita/khu<u>th</u>ooh<u>a</u> <u>th</u>aroon<u>a</u>
nattabiAAkum yureedoona an yubaddiloo kal<u>a</u>ma All<u>a</u>hi qul
lan tattabiAAoon<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>likum q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu min
qablu fasayaqooloona bal ta<u>h</u>sudoonan<u>a</u> bal k<u>a</u>noo
l<u>a</u> yafqahoona ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u>**n**

15\. Those who lagged behind  
(Will say), when ye (are  
Free to) march and take  
Booty (in war): "Permit us  
To follow you." They wish  
To change God's decree:  
Say: "Not thus  
Will ye follow us:  
God has already declared  
(This) beforehand": then they  
Will say, "But ye are  
Jealous of us." Nay,  
But little do they understand  
(Such things).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_016.gif)

16. Qul lilmukhallafeena mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi satudAAawna il<u>a</u>
qawmin olee ba/sin shadeedin tuq<u>a</u>tiloonahum aw yuslimoona fa-in
tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo yu/tikumu All<u>a</u>hu ajran <u>h</u>asanan wa-in
tatawallaw kam<u>a</u> tawallaytum min qablu yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibkum
AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u>**n**

16\. Say to the desert Arabs  
Who lagged behind: "Ye  
Shall be summoned (to fight)  
Against a people given to  
Vehement war: then shall ye  
Fight, or they shall submit.  
Then if ye show obedience,  
God will grant you  
A goodly reward, but if  
Ye turn back as ye  
Did before, He will punish  
You with a grievous Penalty."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/048/048_017.gif)

17. Laysa AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAm<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u>
AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAraji <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u>
almaree<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>arajun waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha
warasoolahu yudkhilhu jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u>
al-anh<u>a</u>ru waman yatawalla yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban
aleem<u>a</u>**n**

17\. No blame is there  
On the blind, nor is  
There blame on the lame,  
Nor on one ill (if he  
Joins not the war):  
But he that obeys God  
And His Apostle,—(God)  
Will admit him to Gardens  
Beneath which rivers flow;  
And he who turns back,  
(God) will punish him  
With a grievous Penalty.
