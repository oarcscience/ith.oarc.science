# Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.

### Section 9

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_060.gif)

60. Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> lifat<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u>
abra<u>h</u>u <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ablugha majmaAAa alba<u>h</u>rayni aw
am<u>d</u>iya <u>h</u>uqub<u>a</u>**n**

60\. Behold, Moses said  
To his attendant, "I will not  
Give up until I reach  
The junction of the two  
Seas or (until) I spend  
Years and years in travel."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_061.gif)

61. Falamm<u>a</u> balagh<u>a</u> majmaAAa baynihim<u>a</u>
nasiy<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ootahum<u>a</u> fa**i**ttakha<u>th</u>a sabeelahu
fee alba<u>h</u>ri sarab<u>a</u>**n**

61\. But when they reached  
The Junction, they forgot  
(About) their Fish, which took  
Its course through the sea  
(Straight) as in a tunnel.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_062.gif)

62. Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>waz<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la lifat<u>a</u>hu
<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ghad<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> laqad laqeen<u>a</u> min
safarin<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> na<u>s</u>ab<u>a</u>**n**

62\. When they had passed on  
(Some distance), Moses said  
To his attendant: "Bring us  
Our early meal; truly  
We have suffered much fatigue  
At this (stage of) our journey."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_063.gif)

63. Q<u>a</u>la araayta i<u>th</u> awayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>
a**l**<u>ss</u>akhrati fa-innee naseetu al<u>h</u>oota wam<u>a</u>
ans<u>a</u>neehu ill<u>a</u> a**l**shshay<u>ta</u>nu an a<u>th</u>kurahu
wa**i**ttakha<u>th</u>a sabeelahu fee alba<u>h</u>ri AAajab<u>a</u>**n**

63\. He replied: "Sawest thou  
(What happened) when we  
Betook ourselves to the rock?  
I did indeed forget  
(About) the Fish: none but  
Satan made me forget  
To tell (you) about it:  
It took its course through  
The sea in a marvellous way!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_064.gif)

64. Q<u>a</u>la <u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> nabghi
fa**i**rtadd<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim<u>a</u>
qa<u>s</u>a<u>sa</u>**n**

64\. Moses said: "That was what  
We were seeking after:"  
So they went back  
On their footsteps, following  
(The path they had come).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_065.gif)

65. Fawajad<u>a</u> AAabdan min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u>
<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu ra<u>h</u>matan min AAindin<u>a</u>
waAAallamn<u>a</u>hu min ladunn<u>a</u> AAilm<u>a</u>**n**

65\. So they found one  
Of Our servants,  
On whom We had bestowed  
Mercy from Ourselves  
And whom We had taught  
Knowledge from Our own  
Presence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_066.gif)

66. Q<u>a</u>la lahu moos<u>a</u> hal attabiAAuka AAal<u>a</u> an
tuAAallimani mimm<u>a</u> AAullimta rushd<u>a</u>**n**

66\. Moses said to him:  
"May I follow thee,  
On the footing that  
Thou teach me something  
Of the (Higher) Truth  
Which thou hast been taught?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_067.gif)

67. Q<u>a</u>la innaka lan tasta<u>t</u>eeAAa maAAiya
<u>s</u>abr<u>a</u>**n**

67\. (The other) said: "Verily  
Thou wilt not be able  
To have patience with me!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_068.gif)

68. Wakayfa ta<u>s</u>biru AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lam
tu<u>h</u>i<u>t</u> bihi khubr<u>a</u>**n**

68\. "And how canst thou  
Have patience about things  
About which thy understanding  
Is not complete?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_069.gif)

69. Q<u>a</u>la satajidunee in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu <u>sa</u>biran
wal<u>a</u> aAA<u>s</u>ee laka amr<u>a</u>**n**

69\. Moses said: "Thou wilt  
Find me, if God so will,  
(Truly) patient: nor shall I  
Disobey thee in aught."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_070.gif)

70. Q<u>a</u>la fa-ini ittabaAAtanee fal<u>a</u> tas-alnee AAan shay-in
<u>h</u>att<u>a</u> o<u>h</u>ditha laka minhu <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u>**n**

70\. The other said: "If then  
Thou wouldst follow me,  
Ask me no questions  
About anything until I  
Myself speak to thee  
Concerning it."
