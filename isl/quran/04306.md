# Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.

### Section 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_057.gif)

57. Walamm<u>a</u> <u>d</u>uriba ibnu maryama mathalan i<u>tha</u>
qawmuka minhu ya<u>s</u>iddoon**a**

57\. When (Jesus) the son  
Of Mary is held up  
As an example, behold,  
Thy people raise a clamour  
Thereat (in ridicule)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_058.gif)

58. Waq<u>a</u>loo a<u>a</u>lihatun<u>a</u> khayrun am huwa m<u>a</u>
<u>d</u>araboohu laka ill<u>a</u> jadalan bal hum qawmun
kha<u>s</u>imoon**a**

58\. And they say, "Are  
Our gods best, or he?"  
This they set forth  
To thee, only by way  
Of disputation: yea, they  
Are a contentious people.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_059.gif)

59. In huwa ill<u>a</u> AAabdun anAAamn<u>a</u> AAalayhi
wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu mathalan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eel**a**

59\. He was no more than  
A servant: We granted  
Our favour to him,  
And We made him  
An example to the Children  
Of Israel.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_060.gif)

60. Walaw nash<u>a</u>o lajaAAaln<u>a</u> minkum mal<u>a</u>-ikatan fee
al-ar<u>d</u>i yakhlufoon**a**

60\. And if it were Our Will,  
We could make angels  
From amongst you, succeeding  
Each other on the earth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_061.gif)

61. Wa-innahu laAAilmun li**l**ss<u>a</u>AAati fal<u>a</u> tamtarunna
bih<u>a</u> wa**i**ttabiAAooni h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un
mustaqeem**un**

61\. And (Jesus) shall be  
A Sign (for the coming  
Of) the Hour (of Judgment):  
Therefore have no doubt  
About the (Hour), but  
Follow ye Me: this  
Is a Straight Way.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_062.gif)

62. Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>uddannakumu a**l**shshay<u>ta</u>nu innahu
lakum AAaduwwun mubeen**un**

62\. Let not the Evil One  
Hinder you: for he is  
To you an enemy avowed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_063.gif)

63. Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a AAees<u>a</u> bi**a**lbayyin<u>a</u>ti
q<u>a</u>la qad ji/tukum bi**a**l<u>h</u>ikmati wali-obayyina lakum
baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee takhtalifoona feehi fa**i**ttaqoo
All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon**i**

63\. When Jesus came  
With Clear Signs, he said:  
"Now have I come  
To you with Wisdom,  
And in order to make  
Clear to you some  
Of the (points) on which  
Ye dispute: therefore fear God  
And obey me.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_064.gif)

64. Inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa rabbee warabbukum fa**o**AAbudoohu
h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem**un**

64\. "For God, He is my Lord  
And your Lord: so worship  
Ye Him: this is  
A Straight Way."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_065.gif)

65. Fa**i**khtalafa al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bu min baynihim fawaylun
lilla<u>th</u>eena *<u>th</u>*alamoo min AAa<u>tha</u>bi yawmin
aleem**in**

65\. But sects from among  
Themselves fell into disagreement:  
Then woe to the wrong-doers,  
From the Penalty  
Of a Grievous Day!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_066.gif)

66. Hal yan*<u>th</u>*uroona ill<u>a</u> a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAata an
ta/tiyahum baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon**a**

66\. Do they only wait  
For the Hour—that it  
Should come on them  
All of a sudden,  
While they perceive not?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_067.gif)

67. Al-akhill<u>a</u>o yawma-i<u>th</u>in baAA<u>d</u>uhum
libaAA<u>d</u>in AAaduwwun ill<u>a</u> almuttaqeen**a**

67\. Friends on that Day  
Will be foes, one  
To another,—except  
The Righteous.
