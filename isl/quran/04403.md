# Sūra XLIV.: Du<u>kh</u>ān, or Smoke (or Mist.)

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_043.gif)

43. Inna shajarata a**l**zzaqqoom**i**

43\. Verily the tree  
Of  Zaqqūm

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_044.gif)

44. <u>T</u>aAA<u>a</u>mu al-atheem**i**

44\. Will be the food  
Of the Sinful,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_045.gif)

45. Ka**a**lmuhli yaghlee fee albu<u>t</u>oon**i**

45\. Like molten brass;  
It will boil  
In their insides,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_046.gif)

46. Kaghalyi al<u>h</u>ameem**i**

46\. Like the boiling  
Of scalding water.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_047.gif)

47. Khu<u>th</u>oohu fa**i**AAtiloohu il<u>a</u> saw<u>a</u>-i
alja<u>h</u>eem**i**

47\. (A voice will cry:  
"Seize ye him  
And drag him  
Into the midst  
Of the Blazing Fire!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_048.gif)

48. Thumma <u>s</u>ubboo fawqa ra/sihi min AAa<u>tha</u>bi
al<u>h</u>ameem**i**

48\. "Then pour over his head  
The Penalty of Boiling Water

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_049.gif)

49. <u>Th</u>uq innaka anta alAAazeezu alkareem**u**

49\. "Taste thou (this)!  
Truly wast thou  
Mighty, full of honour!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_050.gif)

50. Inna h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum bihi tamtaroon**a**

50\. "Truly this is what  
Ye used to doubt!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_051.gif)

51. Inna almuttaqeena fee maq<u>a</u>min ameen**in**

51\. As to the Righteous  
(They will be) in  
A position of Security,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_052.gif)

52. Fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon**in**

52\. Among Gardens and Springs;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_053.gif)

53. Yalbasoona min sundusin wa-istabraqin mutaq<u>a</u>bileen**a**

53\. Dressed in fine silk  
And in rich brocade,  
They will face each other;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_054.gif)

54. Ka<u>tha</u>lika wazawwajn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>h</u>oorin AAeen**in**

54\. So; and We shall  
Join them to Companions  
With beautiful, big,  
And lustrous eyes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_055.gif)

55. YadAAoona feeh<u>a</u> bikulli f<u>a</u>kihatin <u>a</u>mineen**a**

55\. There can they call  
For every kind of fruit  
In peace and security;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_056.gif)

56. L<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>ooqoona feeh<u>a</u> almawta ill<u>a</u>
almawtata al-ool<u>a</u> wawaq<u>a</u>hum AAa<u>tha</u>ba
alja<u>h</u>eem**i**

56\. Nor will they there  
Taste Death, except the first  
Death; and He will preserve  
Them from the Penalty  
Of the Blazing Fire,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_057.gif)

57. Fa<u>d</u>lan min rabbika <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu
alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**u**

57\. As a Bounty from thy Lord!  
That will be  
The supreme achievement!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_058.gif)

58. Fa-innam<u>a</u> yassarn<u>a</u>hu bilis<u>a</u>nika laAAallahum
yata<u>th</u>akkaroon**a**

58\. Verily, We have made  
This (Qur-ān) easy,  
In thy tongue,  
In order that they  
May give heed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/044/044_059.gif)

59. Fa**i**rtaqib innahum murtaqiboon**a**

59\. So wait thou and watch;  
For they (too) are waiting.
