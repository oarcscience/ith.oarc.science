# Sūra CIV.: Humaza, or the Scandal-monger.

### Section 1

![](../gif/104/104_001.gif)

1. Waylun likulli humazatin lumaza**tin**

1\. Woe to every  
(Kind of) scandal-monger  
And backbiter,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_002.gif)

2. Alla<u>th</u>ee jamaAAa m<u>a</u>lan waAAaddadah**u**

2\. Who pileth up wealth  
And layeth it by,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_003.gif)

3. Ya<u>h</u>sabu anna m<u>a</u>lahu akhladah**u**

3\. Thinking that his wealth  
Would make him last  
For ever!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_004.gif)

4. Kall<u>a</u> layunba<u>th</u>anna fee al<u>h</u>u<u>t</u>ama**ti**

4\. By no means! He will  
Be sure to be thrown into  
That which Breaks to Pieces.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_005.gif)

5. Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>u<u>t</u>ama**tu**

5\. And what will explain  
To thee That which Breaks  
To Pieces?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_006.gif)

6. N<u>a</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi almooqada**tu**

6\. (It is) the Fire  
Of (the Wrath of) God  
Kindled (to a blaze),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_007.gif)

7. Allatee ta<u>tt</u>aliAAu AAal<u>a</u> al-af-ida**ti**

7\. The which doth mount  
(Right) to the Hearts:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_008.gif)

8. lnnah<u>a</u> AAalayhim mu/<u>s</u>ada**tun**

8\. It shall be made  
Into a vault over them,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/104/104_009.gif)

9. Fee AAamadin mumaddada**tin**  

9\. In columns outstretched.
