# Sūra LXXXVI.: Ṭāriq, or The Night-Visitant.

### Section 1

![](../gif/086/086_001.gif)

1. Wa**al**ssam<u>a</u>-i wa**al**<u>tta</u>riq**i**

1\. By the Sky  
And the Night-Visitant  
(Therein);—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_002.gif)

2. Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> a**l**<u>tta</u>riq**u**

2\. And what will explain to thee  
What the Night-Visitant is?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_003.gif)

3. A**l**nnajmu a**l**thth<u>a</u>qib**u**

3\. (It is) the Star  
Of piercing brightness;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_004.gif)

4. In kullu nafsin lamm<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u>
<u>ha</u>fi*<u>th</u>***un**

4\. There is no soul but has  
A protector over it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_005.gif)

5. Falyan*<u>th</u>*uri al-ins<u>a</u>nu mimma khuliq**a**

5\. Now let man but think  
From what he is created!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_006.gif)

6. Khuliqa min m<u>a</u>-in d<u>a</u>fiq**in**

6\. He is created from  
A drop emitted—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_007.gif)

7. Yakhruju min bayni a**l**<u>ss</u>ulbi wa**al**ttar<u>a</u>-ib**i**

7\. Proceeding from between  
The backbone and the ribs:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_008.gif)

8. Innahu AAal<u>a</u> rajAAihi laq<u>a</u>dir**un**

8\. Surely (God) is able  
To bring him back  
(To life)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_009.gif)

9. Yawma tubl<u>a</u> a**l**ssar<u>a</u>-ir**u**

9\. The Day that  
(All) things secret  
Will be tested,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_010.gif)

10. Fam<u>a</u> lahu min quwwatin wal<u>a</u> n<u>as</u>ir**in**

10\. (Man) will have  
No power,  
And no helper.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_011.gif)

11. Wa**al**ssam<u>a</u>-i <u>tha</u>ti a**l**rrajAA**i**

11\. By the Firmament  
Which returns (in its round),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_012.gif)

12. Wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i <u>tha</u>ti a**l**<u>ss</u>adAA**i**

12\. And by the Earth  
Which opens out  
(For the gushing of springs  
Or the sprouting of vegetation),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_013.gif)

13. Innahu laqawlun fa<u>s</u>l**un**

13\. Behold this is the Word  
That distinguishes (Good  
From Evil):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_014.gif)

14. Wam<u>a</u> huwa bi**a**lhazl**i**

14\. It is not a thing  
For amusement.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_015.gif)

15. Innahum yakeedoona kayd<u>a</u>**n**

15\. As for them, they  
Are but plotting a scheme,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_016.gif)

16. Waakeedu kayd<u>a</u>**n**

16\. And I am planning  
A scheme.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/086/086_017.gif)

17. Famahhili alk<u>a</u>fireena amhilhum ruwayd<u>a</u>**n**

17\. Therefore grant a delay  
To the unbelievers:  
Give respite to them  
Gently (for awhile).
