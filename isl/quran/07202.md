# Sūra LXXII.: Jinn, or the Spirits.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_020.gif)

20. Qul innam<u>a</u> adAAoo rabbee wal<u>a</u> oshriku bihi
a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

20\. Say: "I do  
No more than invoke  
My Lord, and I join not  
With Him any (false god)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_021.gif)

21. Qul innee l<u>a</u> amliku lakum <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u>
rashad<u>a</u>**n**

21\. Say: "It is not  
In my power to cause  
You harm, or to bring  
You to right conduct."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_022.gif)

22. Qul innee lan yujeeranee mina All<u>a</u>hi a<u>h</u>adun walan
ajida min doonihi multa<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

22\. Say: "No one can  
Deliver me from God  
(If I were to disobey Him),  
Nor should I find refuge  
Except in Him,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_023.gif)

23. Ill<u>a</u> bal<u>a</u>ghan mina All<u>a</u>hi
waris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tihi waman yaAA<u>s</u>i All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu
fa-inna lahu n<u>a</u>ra jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u>
abad<u>a</u>**n**

23\. "Unless I proclaim what  
I receive from God  
And His Messages:  
For any that disobey God  
And His Apostle,—for them  
Is Hell: they shall dwell  
Therein for ever."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_024.gif)

24. <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> raaw m<u>a</u> yooAAadoona
fasayaAAlamoona man a<u>d</u>AAafu n<u>as</u>iran waaqallu
AAadad<u>a</u>**n**

24\. At length, when they  
See (with their own eyes)  
That which they are promised,—  
Then will they know  
Who it is that is  
Weakest in (his) helper  
And least important  
In point of numbers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_025.gif)

25. Qul in adree aqareebun m<u>a</u> tooAAadoona am yajAAalu lahu rabbee
amad<u>a</u>**n**

25\. Say: "I know not whether  
The (Punishment) which ye  
Are promised is near,  
Or whether my Lord  
Will appoint for it  
A distant term.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_026.gif)

26. AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi fal<u>a</u> yu*<u>th</u>*hiru AAal<u>a</u>
ghaybihi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

26\. "He (alone) knows the Unseen,  
Nor does He make any one  
Acquainted with his Mysteries,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_027.gif)

27. Ill<u>a</u> mani irta<u>da</u> min rasoolin fa-innahu yasluku min
bayni yadayhi wamin khalfihi ra<u>s</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

27\. "Except an apostle  
Whom He has chosen:  
And then He makes  
A band of watchers  
March before him  
And behind him,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/072/072_028.gif)

28. LiyaAAlama an qad ablaghoo ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti rabbihim
waa<u>hat</u>a bim<u>a</u> ladayhim waa<u>hsa</u> kulla shay-in
AAadad<u>a</u>**n**

28\. "That he may know  
That they have (truly)  
Brought and delivered  
The Messages of their Lord:  
And He surrounds  
(All the mysteries) that are  
With them, and takes account  
Of every single thing."
