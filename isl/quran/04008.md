# Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.

### Section 8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_069.gif)

69. Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee
<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi ann<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>rafoon**a**

69\. Seest thou not those  
That dispute concerning  
The Signs of God?  
How are they turned away  
(From Reality)?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_070.gif)

70. Alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi**a**lkit<u>a</u>bi
wabim<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> bihi rusulan<u>a</u> fasawfa
yaAAlamoon**a**

70\. Those who reject the Book  
And the (revelations) with which  
We sent Our apostles:  
But soon shall they know,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_071.gif)

71. I<u>th</u>i al-aghl<u>a</u>lu fee aAAn<u>a</u>qihim
wa**al**ssal<u>a</u>silu yus<u>h</u>aboon**a**

71\. When the yokes (shall be)  
Round their necks,  
And the chains;  
They shall be dragged along—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_072.gif)

72. Fee al<u>h</u>ameemi thumma fee a**l**nn<u>a</u>ri yusjaroon**a**

72\. In the boiling fetid fluid  
Then in the Fire  
Shall they be burned;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_073.gif)

73. Thumma qeela lahum ayna m<u>a</u> kuntum tushrikoon**a**

73\. Then shall it be said  
To them: "Where are  
The (deities) to which  
Ye gave part-worship—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_074.gif)

74. Min dooni All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>loo <u>d</u>alloo AAann<u>a</u> bal
lam nakun nadAAoo min qablu shay-an ka<u>tha</u>lika yu<u>d</u>illu
All<u>a</u>hu alk<u>a</u>fireen**a**

74\. "In derogation of God?"  
They will reply: "They have  
Left us in the lurch:  
Nay, we invoked not,  
Of old, anything (that had  
Real existence)." Thus  
Does God leave  
The Unbelievers to stray.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_075.gif)

75. <u>Tha</u>likum bim<u>a</u> kuntum tafra<u>h</u>oona fee
al-ar<u>d</u>i bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi wabim<u>a</u> kuntum
tamra<u>h</u>oon**a**

75\. "That was because  
Ye were wont to rejoice  
On the earth in things  
Other than the Truth,  
And that ye were wont  
To be insolent.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_076.gif)

76. Odkhuloo abw<u>a</u>ba jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u>
fabi/sa mathw<u>a</u> almutakabbireen**a**

76\. "Enter ye the gates  
Of Hell, to dwell therein:  
And evil is (this) abode  
Of the arrogant!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_077.gif)

77. Fa**i**<u>s</u>bir inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun
fa-imm<u>a</u> nuriyannaka baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee naAAiduhum aw
natawaffayannaka fa-ilayn<u>a</u> yurjaAAoon**a**

77\. So persevere in patience;  
For the Promise of God  
Is true: and whether  
We show thee (in this life)  
Some part of what We  
Promise them,—or We  
Take thy soul (to Our Mercy)  
(Before that),—(in any case)  
It is to Us that  
They shall (all) return.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_078.gif)

78. Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> rusulan min qablika minhum man
qa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayka waminhum man lam
naq<u>s</u>u<u>s</u> AAalayka wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lirasoolin an
ya/tiya bi-<u>a</u>yatin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi
fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a amru All<u>a</u>hi qu<u>d</u>iya
bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi wakhasira hun<u>a</u>lika almub<u>t</u>iloon**a**

78\. We did aforetime send  
Apostles before thee: of them  
There are some whose story  
We have related to thee,  
And some whose story  
We have not related  
To thee. It was not  
(Possible) for any apostle  
To bring a Sign except  
By the leave of God:  
But when the Command  
Of God issued,  
The matter was decided  
In truth and justice,  
And there perished,  
There and then, those  
Who stood on Falsehoods.
