# Sūra LV.: Raḥmān, or (God) Most Gracious.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_046.gif)

46. Waliman kh<u>a</u>fa maq<u>a</u>ma rabbihi jannat<u>a</u>n**i**

46\. But for such as fear  
The time when they will  
Stand before (the Judgment Seat  
Of) their Lord,  
There will be two Gardens—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_047.gif)

47. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

47\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_048.gif)

48. <u>Th</u>aw<u>a</u>t<u>a</u> afn<u>a</u>n**in**

48\. Containing all kinds  
(Of trees and delights);—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_049.gif)

49. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

49\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_050.gif)

50. Feehim<u>a</u> AAayn<u>a</u>ni tajriy<u>a</u>n**i**

50\. In them (each) will be  
Two Springs flowing (free);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_051.gif)

51. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

51\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_052.gif)

52. Feehim<u>a</u> min kulli f<u>a</u>kihatin zawj<u>a</u>n**i**

52\. In them will be Fruits  
Of every kind, two and two.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_053.gif)

53. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

53\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_054.gif)

54. Muttaki-eena AAal<u>a</u> furushin ba<u>ta</u>-inuh<u>a</u> min
istabraqin wajan<u>a</u> aljannatayni d<u>a</u>n**in**

54\. They will recline on Carpets,  
Whose inner linings will be  
Of rich brocade: the Fruit  
Of the Gardens will be  
Near (and easy of reach).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_055.gif)

55. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

55\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_056.gif)

56. Feehinna q<u>as</u>ir<u>a</u>tu a**l**<u>tt</u>arfi lam
ya<u>t</u>mithhunna insun qablahum wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n**nun**

56\. In them will be (Maidens),  
Chaste, restraining their glances,  
Whom no man or Jinn  
Before them has touched;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_057.gif)

57. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

57\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_058.gif)

58. Kaannahunna aly<u>a</u>qootu wa**a**lmarj<u>a</u>n**u**

58\. Like unto rubies and coral.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_059.gif)

59. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

59\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_060.gif)

al-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>n**u**

60\. Is there any Reward  
For Good-other than Good?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_061.gif)

61. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

61\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_062.gif)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_063.gif)

63. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

63\. Then which of the favours

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_064.gif)

(From plentiful watering).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_065.gif)

65. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

65\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_066.gif)

66. Feehim<u>a</u> AAayn<u>a</u>ni na<u>dda</u>khat<u>a</u>n**i**

66\. In them (each) will be  
Two Springs pouring forth water  
In continuous abundance:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_067.gif)

67. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

67\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_068.gif)

68. Feehim<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun wanakhlun warumm<u>a</u>n**un**

68\. In them will be Fruits,  
And dates and pomegranates:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_069.gif)

69. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

69\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_070.gif)

70. Feehinna khayr<u>a</u>tun <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>n**un**

70\. In them will be  
Fair (Companions), good,  
beautiful;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_071.gif)

71. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

71\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_072.gif)

72. <u>H</u>oorun maq<u>s</u>oor<u>a</u>tun fee alkhiy<u>a</u>m**i**

72\. Companions restrained (as to  
Their glances), in (goodly)  
pavilions;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_073.gif)

73. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

73\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_074.gif)

74. Lam ya<u>t</u>mithhunna insun qablahum wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n**nun**

Before them has touched;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_075.gif)

75. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

75\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?—

![](../gif/055/055_076.gif)

76\. Reclining on green Cushions  
And rich Carpets of beauty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_077.gif)

77. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

77\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_078.gif)

78. Tab<u>a</u>raka ismu rabbika <u>th</u>ee aljal<u>a</u>li
wa**a**l-ikr<u>a</u>m**i**

78\. Blessed be the name  
Of thy Lord,  
Full of Majesty,  
Bounty and Honour.
