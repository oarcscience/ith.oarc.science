# Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_042.gif)

42. Qul man yaklaokum bi**a**llayli wa**al**nnah<u>a</u>ri mina
a**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni bal hum AAan <u>th</u>ikri rabbihim
muAAri<u>d</u>oon**a**

42\. Say, "Who can keep  
You safe by night and by day  
From (the Wrath of) (God)  
Most Gracious?" Yet they  
Turn away from the mention  
Of their Lord.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_043.gif)

43. Am lahum <u>a</u>lihatun tamnaAAuhum min doonin<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>
yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona na<u>s</u>ra anfusihim wal<u>a</u> hum
minn<u>a</u> yu<u>sh</u>aboon**a**

43\. Or have they gods that  
Can guard them from Us?  
They have no power to aid  
Themselves, nor can they  
Be defended from Us.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_044.gif)

44. Bal mattaAAn<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum
<u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>la AAalayhimu alAAumuru afal<u>a</u>
yarawna ann<u>a</u> na/tee al-ar<u>d</u>a nanqu<u>s</u>uh<u>a</u> min
a<u>t</u>r<u>a</u>fih<u>a</u> afahumu algh<u>a</u>liboon**a**

44\. Nay, We gave the good things  
Of this life to these men  
And their fathers until  
The period grew long for them;  
See they not that We  
Gradually reduce the land  
(In their control) from  
Its outlying borders? Is it  
Then they who will win?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_045.gif)

45. Qul innam<u>a</u> on<u>th</u>irukum bi**a**lwa<u>h</u>yi wal<u>a</u>
yasmaAAu a**l**<u>ss</u>ummu a**l**dduAA<u>a</u>a i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u>
yun<u>th</u>aroon**a**

45\. Say, "I do but warn you  
According to revelation":  
But the deaf will not hear  
The call, (even) when  
They are warned!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_046.gif)

46. Wala-in massat-hum naf<u>h</u>atun min AAa<u>tha</u>bi rabbika
layaqoolunna y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u>
*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

46\. If but a breath of the Wrath  
Of thy Lord do touch them,  
They will then say, "Woe  
To us! we did wrong indeed!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_047.gif)

47. Wana<u>d</u>aAAu almaw<u>a</u>zeena alqis<u>t</u>a liyawmi
alqiy<u>a</u>mati fal<u>a</u> tu*<u>th</u>*lamu nafsun shay-an wa-in
k<u>a</u>na mithq<u>a</u>la <u>h</u>abbatin min khardalin atayn<u>a</u>
bih<u>a</u> wakaf<u>a</u> bin<u>a</u> <u>ha</u>sibeen**a**

47\. We shall set up scales  
Of justice for the day  
Of Judgment, so that  
Not a soul will be dealt with  
Unjustly in the least.  
And if there be  
(No more than) the weight  
Of a mustard seed,  
We will bring it (to account):  
And enough are We  
To take account.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_048.gif)

48. Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roona
alfurq<u>a</u>na wa<u>d</u>iy<u>a</u>an wa<u>th</u>ikran
lilmuttaqeen**a**

48\. In the past We granted  
To Moses and Aaron  
The Criterion (for judgment),  
And a Light and a Message  
For those who would do right,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_049.gif)

49. Alla<u>th</u>eena yakhshawna rabbahum bi**a**lghaybi wahum mina
a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAati mushfiqoon**a**

49\. Those who fear their Lord  
In their most secret thoughts,  
And who hold the Hour  
(Of Judgment) in awe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_050.gif)

50. Wah<u>atha</u> <u>th</u>ikrun mub<u>a</u>rakun anzaln<u>a</u>hu
afaantum lahu munkiroon**a**

50\. And this is a blessed  
Message which We have  
Sent down: will ye then  
Reject it?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_051.gif)

51. Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema rushdahu min qablu
wakunn<u>a</u> bihi AA<u>a</u>limeen**a**

51\. We bestowed aforetime  
On Abraham his rectitude  
Of conduct, and well were we  
Acquainted with him.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_052.gif)

52. I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la li-abeehi waqawmihi m<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi
a**l**ttam<u>a</u>theelu allatee antum lah<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>kifoon**a**

52\. Behold! he said  
To his father and his people  
"What are these images,  
To which ye are  
(So assiduously) devoted?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_053.gif)

53. Q<u>a</u>loo wajadn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u>
AA<u>a</u>bideen**a**

53\. They said, "We found  
Our fathers worshipping them."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_054.gif)

54. Q<u>a</u>la laqad kuntum antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum fee
<u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen**in**

54\. He said, "Indeed ye  
Have been made in manifest  
Error—ye and your fathers."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_055.gif)

55. Q<u>a</u>loo aji/tan<u>a</u> bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi am anta mina
all<u>a</u>AAibeen**a**

55\. They said, "Have you  
Brought us the Truth,  
Or are you one  
Of those who jest?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_056.gif)

56. Q<u>a</u>la bal rabbukum rabbu a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>arahunna waan<u>a</u>
AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum mina a**l**shsh<u>a</u>hideen**a**

56\. He said, "Nay, your Lord  
Is the Lord of the heavens  
And the earth, He Who  
Created them (from nothing):  
And I am a witness  
To this (truth).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_057.gif)

57. Wata**A**ll<u>a</u>hi laakeedanna a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>makum baAAda an
tuwalloo mudbireen**a**

57\. "And by god, I have  
A plan for your idols—  
After ye go away  
And turn your backs"…

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_058.gif)

58. FajaAAalahum ju<u>thath</u>an ill<u>a</u> kabeeran lahum laAAallahum
ilayhi yarjiAAoon**a**

58\. So he broke them to pieces,  
(All) but the biggest of them,  
That they might turn  
(And address themselves) to it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_059.gif)

59. Q<u>a</u>loo man faAAala h<u>atha</u> bi-<u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u>
innahu lamina a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

59\. They said, "Who has  
Done this to our gods?  
He must indeed be  
Some man of impiety!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_060.gif)

60. Q<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> fatan ya<u>th</u>kuruhum yuq<u>a</u>lu
lahu ibr<u>a</u>heem**u**

60\. They said, "We heard  
A youth talk of them:  
He is called Abraham."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_061.gif)

61. Q<u>a</u>loo fa/too bihi AAal<u>a</u> aAAyuni a**l**nn<u>a</u>si
laAAallahum yashhadoon**a**

61\. They said, "Then bring him  
Before the eyes of the people,  
That they may bear witness:"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_062.gif)

62. Q<u>a</u>loo aanta faAAalta h<u>atha</u> bi-<u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u>
y<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem**u**

62\. They said, "Art thou  
The one that did this  
With our gods, O Abraham?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_063.gif)

63. Q<u>a</u>la bal faAAalahu kabeeruhum h<u>atha</u> fa**i**s-aloohum
in k<u>a</u>noo yan<u>t</u>iqoon**a**

63\. He said: "Nay, this  
Was done by —  
This is their biggest one!  
Ask them, if they  
Can speak intelligently!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_064.gif)

64. FarajaAAoo il<u>a</u> anfusihim faq<u>a</u>loo innakum antumu
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limoon**a**

64\. So they turned to themselves  
And said, "Surely ye  
Are the ones in the wrong!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_065.gif)

65. Thumma nukisoo AAal<u>a</u> ruoosihim laqad AAalimta m<u>a</u>
h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i yan<u>t</u>iqoon**a**

65\. Then were they confounded  
With shame: (they said),  
"Thou knowest full well that  
These (idols) do not speak!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_066.gif)

66. Q<u>a</u>la afataAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u>
l<u>a</u> yanfaAAukum shay-an wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urrukum

66\. (Abraham) said, "Do ye then  
Worship, besides God,  
Things that can neither  
Be of any good to you  
Nor do you harm?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_067.gif)

67. Offin lakum walim<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi
afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon**a**

67\. "Fie upon you, and upon  
The things that ye worship  
Besides God! Have ye  
No sense?"…

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_068.gif)

68. Q<u>a</u>loo <u>h</u>arriqoohu wa**o**n<u>s</u>uroo
<u>a</u>lihatakum in kuntum f<u>a</u>AAileen**a**

68\. They said, "Burn him  
And protect your gods,  
If ye do (anything at all)!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_069.gif)

69. Quln<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ru koonee bardan wasal<u>a</u>man
AAal<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heem**a**

69\. We said, "O Fire!  
Be thou cool,  
And (a means of) safety  
For Abraham!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_070.gif)

70. Waar<u>a</u>doo bihi kaydan fajaAAaln<u>a</u>humu al-akhsareen**a**

70\. Then they sought a stratagem  
Against him: but We  
Made them the ones  
That lost most!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_071.gif)

71. Wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waloo<u>t</u>an il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i
allatee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> lilAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

71\. But We delivered him  
And (his nephew) Lūt  
(And directed them) to the land  
Which We have blessed  
For the nations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_072.gif)

72. Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba n<u>a</u>filatan
wakullan jaAAaln<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>een**a**

72\. And We bestowed on him Isaac  
And, as an additional gift,  
(A grandson), Jacob, and We  
Made righteous men of every one  
(Of them).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_073.gif)

73. WajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum a-immatan yahdoona bi-amrin<u>a</u>
waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayhim fiAAla alkhayr<u>a</u>ti wa-iq<u>a</u>ma
a**l**<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa-eet<u>a</u>a a**l**zzak<u>a</u>ti
wak<u>a</u>noo lan<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bideen**a**

73\. And We made them  
Leaders, guiding (men) by  
Our Command, and We  
Sent them inspiration  
To do good deeds,  
To establish regular prayers,  
And to practise regular charity;  
And they constantly served  
Us (and Us only).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_074.gif)

74. Waloo<u>t</u>an <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>ukman waAAilman
wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu mina alqaryati allatee k<u>a</u>nat taAAmalu
alkhab<u>a</u>-itha innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawma saw-in
f<u>a</u>siqeen**a**

74\. And to Lūt, too,  
We gave Judgment and Knowledge,  
And We saved him  
From the town which practised  
Abominations: truly they were  
A people given to Evil,  
A rebellious people.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/021/021_075.gif)

75. Waadkhaln<u>a</u>hu fee ra<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> innahu mina
a**l**<u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een**a**

75\. And We admitted him  
To Our Mercy: for he  
Was one of the Righteous.
