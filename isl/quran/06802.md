# Sūra LXVIII.: Qalam, or the Pen, or Nūn.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_034.gif)

34. Inna lilmuttaqeena AAinda rabbihim jann<u>a</u>ti
a**l**nnaAAeem**i**

34\. Verily, for the Righteous,  
Are Gardens of Delight,  
In the Presence  
Of their Lord.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_035.gif)

35. AfanajAAalu almuslimeena ka**a**lmujrimeen**a**

35\. Shall We then treat  
The People of Faith  
Like the People of Sin?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_036.gif)

36. M<u>a</u> lakum kayfa ta<u>h</u>kumoon**a**

36\. What is the matter  
With you? How judge ye?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_037.gif)

37. Am lakum kit<u>a</u>bun feehi tadrusoon**a**

37\. Or have ye a Book  
Through which ye learn—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_038.gif)

38. Inna lakum feehi lam<u>a</u> takhayyaroon**a**

38\. That ye shall have,  
Through it whatever  
Ye choose?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_039.gif)

39. Am lakum aym<u>a</u>nun AAalayn<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>lighatun il<u>a</u>
yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati inna lakum lam<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>kumoon**a**

39\. Or have ye Covenants  
With Us on oath,  
Reaching to the Day  
Of Judgment, (providing)  
That ye shall have  
Whatever ye shall demand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_040.gif)

40. Salhum ayyuhum bi<u>tha</u>lika zaAAeem**un**

40\. Ask thou of them,  
Which of them will stand  
Surety for that!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_041.gif)

41. Am lahum shurak<u>a</u>o falya/too bishurak<u>a</u>-ihim in
k<u>a</u>noo <u>sa</u>diqeen**a**

41\. Or have they some  
"Partners" (in Godhead)?  
Then let them produce  
Their "partners",  
If they are truthful!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_042.gif)

42. Yawma yukshafu AAan s<u>a</u>qin wayudAAawna il<u>a</u>
a**l**ssujoodi fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoon**a**

42\. The Day that the Shin  
Shall be laid bare,  
And they shall be summoned  
To bow in adoration,  
But they shall not be able,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_043.gif)

43. Kh<u>a</u>shiAAatan ab<u>sa</u>ruhum tarhaquhum <u>th</u>illatun
waqad k<u>a</u>noo yudAAawna il<u>a</u> a**l**ssujoodi wahum
s<u>a</u>limoon**a**

43\. Their eyes will be  
Cast down,—ignominy will  
Cover them; seeing that  
They had been summoned  
Aforetime to bow in adoration,  
While they were whole,  
(And had refused).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_044.gif)

44. Fa<u>th</u>arnee waman yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bih<u>atha</u>
al<u>h</u>adeethi sanastadrijuhum min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u>
yaAAlamoon**a**

44\. Then leave Me alone  
With such as reject  
This Message: by degrees  
Shall We punish them  
From directions they perceive not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_045.gif)

45. Waomlee lahum inna kaydee mateen**un**

45\. A (long) respite will I  
Grant them: truly  
Powerful is My Plan.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_046.gif)

46. Am tas-aluhum ajran fahum min maghramin muthqaloon**a**

46\. Or is it that thou dost  
Ask them for a reward,  
So that they are burdened  
With a load of debt?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_047.gif)

47. Am AAindahumu alghaybu fahum yaktuboon**a**

47\. Or that the Unseen  
Is in their hands, so that  
They can write it down?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_048.gif)

48. Fa**i**<u>s</u>bir li<u>h</u>ukmi rabbika wal<u>a</u> takun
ka<u>sah</u>ibi al<u>h</u>ooti i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> wahuwa
mak*<u>th</u>*oom**un**

48\. So wait with patience  
For the Command  
Of thy Lord, and be not  
Like the Companion  
Of the Fish,—when he  
Cried out in agony.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_049.gif)

49. Lawl<u>a</u> an tad<u>a</u>rakahu niAAmatun min rabbihi
lanubi<u>th</u>a bi**a**lAAar<u>a</u>-i wahuwa ma<u>th</u>moom**un**

49\. Had not Grace  
From His Lord  
Reached him, he  
Would indeed have been  
Cast off on the naked  
Shore, in disgrace.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_050.gif)

50. Fa**i**jtab<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu fajaAAalahu mina
a**l**<u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een**a**

50\. Thus did his Lord  
Choose him and make him  
Of the company  
Of the Righteous.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_051.gif)

51. Wa-in yak<u>a</u>du alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo layuzliqoonaka
bi-ab<u>sa</u>rihim lamm<u>a</u> samiAAoo a**l**<u>thth</u>ikra
wayaqooloona innahu lamajnoon**un**

51\. And the Unbelievers  
Would almost trip thee up  
With their eyes when they  
Hear the Message; and they  
Say: "Surely he is possessed!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/068/068_052.gif)

52. Wam<u>a</u> huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun lilAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

52\. But it is nothing less  
Than a Message  
To all the worlds.
