# Sūra CVIII.: Kau<u>th</u>ar, or Abundance.

### Section 1

![](../gif/108/108_001.gif)

1. Inn<u>a</u> aAA<u>t</u>ayn<u>a</u>ka alkawthar**a**

1\. To thee have We  
Granted the Fount  
(of Abundance).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/108/108_002.gif)

2. Fa<u>s</u>alli lirabbika wa**i**n<u>h</u>ar

2\. Therefore to thy Lord  
Turn in Prayer  
And Sacrifice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/108/108_003.gif)

3. Inna sh<u>a</u>ni-aka huwa al-abtar**u**  

3\. For he who hateth thee,—  
He will be cut off  
(From Future Hope).
