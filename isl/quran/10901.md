# Sūra CIX: Kāfirūn, or Those who reject Faith.

### Section 1

![](../gif/109/109_001.gif)

1. Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>firoon**a**

1\. Say: O ye  
That reject Faith!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/109/109_002.gif)

2. L<u>a</u> aAAbudu m<u>a</u> taAAbudoon**a**

2\. I worship not that  
Which ye worship,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/109/109_003.gif)

3. Wal<u>a</u> antum AA<u>a</u>bidoona m<u>a</u> aAAbud**u**

3\. Nor will ye worship  
That which I worship.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/109/109_004.gif)

4. Wal<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bidun m<u>a</u> AAabadtum

4\. And I will not worship  
That which ye have been  
Wont to worship,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/109/109_005.gif)

5. Wal<u>a</u> antum AA<u>a</u>bidoona m<u>a</u> aAAbud**u**

5\. Nor will ye worship  
That which I worship.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/109/109_006.gif)

6. Lakum deenukum waliya deen**i**

6\. To you be your Way,  
And to me mine.
