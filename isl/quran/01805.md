# Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.

### Section 5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_032.gif)

32. Wa**i**<u>d</u>rib lahum mathalan rajulayni jaAAaln<u>a</u>
li-a<u>h</u>adihim<u>a</u> jannatayni min aAAn<u>a</u>bin
wa<u>h</u>afafn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> binakhlin wajaAAaln<u>a</u>
baynahum<u>a</u> zarAA<u>a</u>**n**

32\. Set forth to them  
The parable of two men:  
For one of them We provided  
Two gardens of grape-vines  
And surrounded them  
With date palms;  
In between the two  
We placed corn-fields.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_033.gif)

33. Kilt<u>a</u> aljannatayni <u>a</u>tat okulah<u>a</u> walam
ta*<u>th</u>*lim minhu shay-an wafajjarn<u>a</u>
khil<u>a</u>lahum<u>a</u> nahar<u>a</u>**n**

33\. Each of those gardens  
Brought forth its produce,  
And failed not in the least  
Therein: in the midst  
Of them We caused  
A river to flow.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_034.gif)

34. Wak<u>a</u>na lahu thamarun faq<u>a</u>la li<u>sah</u>ibihi wahuwa
yu<u>ha</u>wiruhu an<u>a</u> aktharu minka m<u>a</u>lan waaAAazzu
nafar<u>a</u>**n**

34\. (Abundant) was the produce  
This man had: he said  
To his companion, in the course  
Of a mutual argument:  
"More wealth have I  
Than you, and more honour  
And power in (my following  
Of) men."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_035.gif)

35. Wadakhala jannatahu wahuwa *<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>limun linafsihi
q<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> a*<u>th</u>*unnu an tabeeda h<u>ath</u>ihi
abad<u>a</u>**n**

35\. He went into his garden  
In a state (of mind)  
Unjust to his soul:  
He said, "I deem not  
That this will ever perish,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_036.gif)

36. Wam<u>a</u> a*<u>th</u>*unnu a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAata q<u>a</u>-imatan
wala-in rudidtu il<u>a</u> rabbee laajidanna khayran minh<u>a</u>
munqalab<u>a</u>**n**

36\. "Nor do I deem  
That the Hour (of Judgment)  
Will (ever) come:  
Even if I am brought back  
To my Lord, I shall  
Surely find (there)  
Something better in exchange."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_037.gif)

37. Q<u>a</u>la lahu <u>sah</u>ibuhu wahuwa yu<u>ha</u>wiruhu akafarta
bi**a**lla<u>th</u>ee khalaqaka min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma min
nu<u>t</u>fatin thumma saww<u>a</u>ka rajul<u>a</u>**n**

37\. His companion said to him,  
In the course of the argument  
With him: "Dost thou deny  
Him Who created thee  
Out of dust, then out of  
A sperm-drop, then fashioned  
Thee into a man?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_038.gif)

38. L<u>a</u>kinna huwa All<u>a</u>hu rabbee wal<u>a</u> oshriku
birabbee a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

38\. "But (I think) for my part  
That He is God,  
My Lord, and none shall I  
Associate with my Lord.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_039.gif)

39. Walawl<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> dakhalta jannataka qulta m<u>a</u>
sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> quwwata ill<u>a</u>
bi**A**ll<u>a</u>hi in tarani an<u>a</u> aqalla minka m<u>a</u>lan
wawalad<u>a</u>**n**

39\. "Why didst thou not,  
As thou wentest into  
Thy garden, say, "God's Will  
(Be done)! There is no power  
But with God!" If thou  
Dost see me less than  
Thee in wealth and sons,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_040.gif)

40. FaAAas<u>a</u> rabbee an yu/tiyani khayran min jannatika wayursila
AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>usb<u>a</u>nan mina a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i
fatu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>a <u>s</u>aAAeedan zalaq<u>a</u>**n**

40\. "It may be that my Lord  
Will give me something  
Better than thy garden,  
And that He will send  
On thy garden thunderbolts  
(By way of reckoning)  
From heaven, making it  
(But) slippery sand!—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_041.gif)

41. Aw yu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>a m<u>a</u>oh<u>a</u> ghawran falan
tasta<u>t</u>eeAAa lahu <u>t</u>alab<u>a</u>**n**

41\. "Or the water of the garden  
Will run off underground  
So that thou wilt never  
Be able to find it."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_042.gif)

42. Wao<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>a bithamarihi faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a yuqallibu
kaffayhi AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> anfaqa feeh<u>a</u> wahiya
kh<u>a</u>wiyatun AAal<u>a</u> AAurooshih<u>a</u> wayaqoolu y<u>a</u>
laytanee lam oshrik birabbee a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>**n**

42\. So his fruits (and enjoyment)  
Were encompassed (with ruin),  
And he remained twisting  
And turning his hands  
Over what he had spent  
On his property, which had  
(Now) tumbled to pieces  
To its very foundations,  
And he could only say,  
"Woe is me! Would I had  
Never ascribed partners  
To my Lord and Cherisher!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_043.gif)

43. Walam takun lahu fi-atun yan<u>s</u>uroonahu min dooni All<u>a</u>hi
wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na munta<u>s</u>ir<u>a</u>**n**

43\. Nor had he numbers  
To help him against God,  
Nor was he able  
To deliver himself.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_044.gif)

44. Hun<u>a</u>lika alwal<u>a</u>yatu lill<u>a</u>hi al<u>h</u>aqqi huwa
khayrun thaw<u>a</u>ban wakhayrun AAuqb<u>a</u>**n**

44\. There, the (only) protection comes  
From God, the True One.  
He is the Best to reward,  
And the Best to give success.
