# Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_023.gif)

23. Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi
faq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min
il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon**a**

23\. (Further, We sent a long line  
Of prophets for your instruction).  
We sent Noah to his people:  
He said, "O my people!  
Worship God! Ye have  
No other god but Him.  
Will ye not fear (Him)?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_024.gif)

24. Faq<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qawmihi
m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum yureedu an
yatafa<u>dd</u>ala AAalaykum walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu laanzala
mal<u>a</u>-ikatan m<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> bih<u>atha</u> fee
<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> al-awwaleen**a**

24\. The chiefs of the Unbelievers  
Among his people said:  
"He is no more than a man  
Like yourselves: his wish is  
To assert his superiority  
Over you: if God had wished  
(To send messengers),  
He could have sent down  
Angels: never did we hear  
Such a thing (as he says),  
Among our ancestors of old."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_025.gif)

25. In huwa ill<u>a</u> rajulun bihi jinnatun fatarabba<u>s</u>oo bihi
<u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een**in**

25\. (And some said): "He is  
Only a man possessed:  
Wait (and have patience)  
With him for a time."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_026.gif)

26. Q<u>a</u>la rabbi on<u>s</u>urnee bim<u>a</u>
ka<u>thth</u>aboon**i**

26\. (Noah) said: "O my Lord!  
Help me: for that they  
Accuse me of falsehood!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_027.gif)

27. Faaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayhi ani i<u>s</u>naAAi alfulka
bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> wawa<u>h</u>yin<u>a</u> fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a
amrun<u>a</u> waf<u>a</u>ra a**l**ttannooru fa**o**sluk feeh<u>a</u> min
kullin zawjayni ithnayni waahlaka ill<u>a</u> man sabaqa AAalayhi
alqawlu minhum wal<u>a</u> tukh<u>at</u>ibnee fee alla<u>th</u>eena
*<u>th</u>*alamoo innahum mughraqoon**a**

27\. So We inspired him  
(With this message): "Construct  
The Ark within Our sight  
And under Our guidance: then  
When comes Our command,  
And the fountains of the earth  
Gush forth, take thou on board  
Pairs of every species, male  
And female, and thy family—  
Except those of them  
Against whom the Word  
Has already gone forth:  
And address Me not  
In favour of the wrong-doers;  
For they shall be drowned  
(In the Flood).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_028.gif)

28. Fa-i<u>tha</u> istawayta anta waman maAAaka AAal<u>a</u> alfulki
faquli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee
najj<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> mina alqawmi
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

28\. And when thou hast embarked  
On the Ark—thou and those  
With thee,—say: "Praise be  
To God, Who has saved us  
From the people who do wrong."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_029.gif)

29. Waqul rabbi anzilnee munzalan mub<u>a</u>rakan waanta khayru
almunzileen**a**

29\. And say: "O my Lord!  
Enable me to disembark  
With Thy blessing: for Thou  
Art the Best to enable (us)  
To disembark."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_030.gif)

30. Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin wa-in kunn<u>a</u>
lamubtaleen**a**

30\. Verily in this there are  
Signs (for men to understand);  
(Thus) do We try (men)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_031.gif)

31. Thumma ansha/n<u>a</u> min baAAdihim qarnan <u>a</u>khareen**a**

31\. Then We raised after them  
Another generation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/023/023_032.gif)

32. Faarsaln<u>a</u> feehim rasoolan minhum ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha
m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu afal<u>a</u> tattaqoon**a**

32\. And We sent to them  
An apostle from among themselves,  
(Saying), "Worship God!  
Ye have no other god  
But Him. Will ye not  
Fear (Him)?"
