# Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.

### Section 15

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_111.gif)

111. Yawma ta/tee kullu nafsin tuj<u>a</u>dilu AAan nafsih<u>a</u>
watuwaff<u>a</u> kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> AAamilat wahum l<u>a</u>
yu*<u>th</u>*lamoon**a**

111\. One Day every soul  
Will come up struggling  
For itself, and every soul  
Will be recompensed (fully)  
For all its actions, and none  
Will be unjustly dealt with.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_112.gif)

112. Wa<u>d</u>araba All<u>a</u>hu mathalan qaryatan k<u>a</u>nat
<u>a</u>minatan mu<u>t</u>ma-innatan ya/teeh<u>a</u> rizquh<u>a</u>
raghadan min kulli mak<u>a</u>nin fakafarat bi-anAAumi All<u>a</u>hi
faa<u>tha</u>qah<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu lib<u>a</u>sa aljooAAi
wa**a**lkhawfi bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>s</u>naAAoon**a**

112\. God sets forth a Parable:  
A city enjoying security  
And quiet, abundantly supplied  
With sustenance from every place:  
Yet was it ungrateful  
For the favours of God:  
So God made it taste  
Of hunger and terror (in extremes)  
(Closing in on it) like a garment  
(From every side), because  
Of the (evil) which  
(Its people) wrought.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_113.gif)

113. Walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum rasoolun minhum faka<u>thth</u>aboohu
faakha<u>th</u>ahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu wahum
*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>limoon**a**

113\. And there came to them  
An Apostle from among themselves,  
But they falsely rejected him;  
So the Wrath seized them  
Even in the midst  
Of their iniquities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_114.gif)

114. Fakuloo mimm<u>a</u> razaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan
<u>t</u>ayyiban wa**o**shkuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi in kuntum
iyy<u>a</u>hu taAAbudoon**a**

114\. So eat of the sustenance  
Which God has provided  
For you, lawful and good;  
And be grateful for the favours  
Of God, if it is He  
Whom ye serve.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_115.gif)

115. Innam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama AAalaykumu almaytata wa**al**ddama
wala<u>h</u>ma alkhinzeeri wam<u>a</u> ohilla lighayri All<u>a</u>hi
bihi famani i<u>dt</u>urra ghayra b<u>a</u>ghin wal<u>a</u>
AA<u>a</u>din fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

115\. He has only forbidden you  
Dead meat, and blood,  
And the flesh of swine,  
And any (food) over which  
The name of other than God  
Has been invoked.  
But if one is forced by necessity,  
Without wilful disobedience,  
Nor transgressing due limits,—  
Then God is Oft-Forgiving,  
Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_116.gif)

116. Wal<u>a</u> taqooloo lim<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>ifu alsinatukumu
alka<u>th</u>iba h<u>atha</u> <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lun wah<u>atha</u>
<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mun litaftaroo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi
alka<u>th</u>iba inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaftaroona AAal<u>a</u>
All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>oon**a**

116\. But say not—for any false thing  
That your tongues may put forth,—  
"This is lawful, and this  
Is forbidden," so as to ascribe  
False things to God. For those  
Who ascribe false things  
To God, will never prosper.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_117.gif)

117. Mat<u>a</u>AAun qaleelun walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem**un**

117\. (In such falsehood)  
Is but a paltry profit;  
But they will have  
A most grievous Penalty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_118.gif)

118. WaAAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo
<u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> qa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayka
min qablu wam<u>a</u> *<u>th</u>*alamn<u>a</u>hum wal<u>a</u>kin
k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya*<u>th</u>*limoon**a**

118\. To the Jews We prohibited  
Such things as We have  
Mentioned to thee before:  
We did them no wrong,  
But they were used to  
Doing wrong to themselves.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/016/016_119.gif)

119. Thumma inna rabbaka lilla<u>th</u>eena AAamiloo a**l**ssoo-a
bijah<u>a</u>latin thumma t<u>a</u>boo min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika
waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>oo inna rabbaka min baAAdih<u>a</u> laghafoorun
ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

119\. But verily thy Lord,—  
To those who do wrong  
In ignorance, but who  
Thereafter repent and make amends,—  
Thy Lord, after all this,  
Is Oft-Forgiving, Most  
Merciful.
