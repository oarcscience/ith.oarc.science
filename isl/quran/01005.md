# Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.

### Section 5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_041.gif)

41. Wa-in ka<u>thth</u>abooka faqul lee AAamalee walakum AAamalukum
antum baree-oona mimm<u>a</u> aAAmalu waan<u>a</u> baree-on mimm<u>a</u>
taAAmaloon**a**

41\. If they charge thee  
With falsehood, say:  
"My work to me,  
And yours to you!  
Ye are free from responsibility  
For what I do, and I  
For what ye do!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_042.gif)

42. Waminhum man yastamiAAoona ilayka afaanta tusmiAAu
a**l**<u>ss</u>umma walaw k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon**a**

42\. Among them are some who  
(Pretend to) listen to thee:  
But canst thou make the deaf  
To hear,—even though  
They are without understanding?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_043.gif)

43. Waminhum man yan*<u>th</u>*uru ilayka afaanta tahdee alAAumya walaw
k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon**a**

43\. And among them are some  
Who look at thee:  
But canst thou guide  
The blind,—even though  
They will not see?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_044.gif)

44. Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> ya*<u>th</u>*limu a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa
shay-an wal<u>a</u>kinna a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa anfusahum
ya*<u>th</u>*limoon**a**

44\. Verily God will not deal  
Unjustly with man in aught:  
It is man that wrongs  
His own soul.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_045.gif)

45. Wayawma ya<u>h</u>shuruhum kaan lam yalbathoo ill<u>a</u>
s<u>a</u>AAatan mina a**l**nnah<u>a</u>ri yataAA<u>a</u>rafoona baynahum
qad khasira alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo biliq<u>a</u>-i
All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo muhtadeen**a**

45\. One day He will  
Gather them together:  
(It will be) as if  
They had tarried  
But an hour of a day:  
They will recognise each other:  
Assuredly those will be lost  
Who denied the meeting  
With God and refused  
To receive true guidance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_046.gif)

46. Wa-imm<u>a</u> nuriyannaka baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee naAAiduhum
aw natawaffayannaka fa-ilayn<u>a</u> marjiAAuhum thumma All<u>a</u>hu
shaheedun AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yafAAaloon**a**

46\. Whether We show thee  
(Realised in thy life-time)  
Some part of what We  
Promise them,—or We  
Take thy soul (to Our Mercy)  
(Before that),—in any case,  
To Us is their return:  
Ultimately God is witness.  
To all that they do.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_047.gif)

47. Walikulli ommatin rasoolun fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a rasooluhum
qu<u>d</u>iya baynahum bi**a**lqis<u>t</u>i wahum l<u>a</u>
yu*<u>th</u>*lamoon**a**

47\. To every people (was sent)  
An Apostle: when their Apostle  
Comes (before them), the matter  
Will be judged between them  
With justice, and they  
Will not be wronged.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_048.gif)

48. Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum
<u>sa</u>diqeen**a**

48\. They say: "When  
Will this promise  
Come to pass,  
If ye speak the truth?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_049.gif)

49. Qul l<u>a</u> amliku linafsee <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u> nafAAan
ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu likulli ommatin ajalun
i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluhum fal<u>a</u> yasta/khiroona
s<u>a</u>AAatan wal<u>a</u> yastaqdimoon**a**

49\. Say: "I have no power  
Over any harm or profit  
To myself except as God  
Willeth. To every People  
Is a term appointed:  
When their term is reached,  
Not an hour can they cause  
Delay, nor (an hour) can they  
Advance (it in anticipation)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_050.gif)

50. Qul araaytum in at<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>buhu bay<u>a</u>tan aw
nah<u>a</u>ran m<u>atha</u> yastaAAjilu minhu almujrimoon**a**

50\. Say: "Do ye see,  
If His punishment should come  
To you by night or by day,—  
What portion of it  
Would the Sinners  
Wish to hasten?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_051.gif)

51. Athumma i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> waqaAAa <u>a</u>mantum bihi
<u>a</u>l-<u>a</u>na waqad kuntum bihi tastaAAjiloon**a**

51\. "Would ye then believe in it  
At last, when it actually cometh  
To pass? (It will then be said:)  
'Ah! now? and ye wanted  
(Aforetime) to hasten it on!'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_052.gif)

52. Thumma qeela lilla<u>th</u>eena *<u>th</u>*alamoo <u>th</u>ooqoo
AAa<u>tha</u>ba alkhuldi hal tujzawna ill<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kuntum
taksiboon**a**

52\. "At length will be said  
To the wrong-doers: 'Taste ye  
The enduring punishment!  
Ye get but the recompense  
Of what ye earned!'"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_053.gif)

53. Wayastanbi-oonaka a<u>h</u>aqqun huwa qul ee warabbee innahu
la<u>h</u>aqqun wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeen**a**

53\. They seek to be informed  
By thee: "Is that true?"  
Say: "Aye! by my Lord!  
It is the very truth!  
And ye cannot frustrate it!"
