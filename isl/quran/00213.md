# Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.

### Section 13

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_104.gif)

104. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u>
taqooloo r<u>a</u>AAin<u>a</u> waqooloo on*<u>th</u>*urn<u>a</u>
wa**i**smaAAoo walilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem**un**

104\. ye of Faith!  
Say not (to the Apostle)  
Words of ambiguous import,  
But words of respect;  
And hearken (to him):  
To those without Faith  
Is a grievous punishment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_105.gif)

105. M<u>a</u> yawaddu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min ahli
alkit<u>a</u>bi wal<u>a</u> almushrikeena an yunazzala AAalaykum min
khayrin min rabbikum wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu yakhta<u>ss</u>u
bira<u>h</u>matihi man yash<u>a</u>o wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo
alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**i**

105\. It is never the wish  
Of those without Faith  
Among the People of the Book,  
Nor of the Pagans,  
That anything good  
Should come down to you  
From your Lord.  
But God will choose  
For His special Mercy  
Whom He will—for God is  
Lord of grace abounding.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_106.gif)

106. M<u>a</u> nansakh min <u>a</u>yatin aw nunsih<u>a</u> na/ti
bikhayrin minh<u>a</u> aw mithlih<u>a</u> alam taAAlam anna
All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer**un**

106\. None of Our revelations  
Do We abrogate  
Or cause to be forgotten,  
But We substitute  
Something better or similar:  
Knowest thou not that God  
Hath power over all things?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_107.gif)

107. Alam taAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha lahu mulku
a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> lakum min
dooni All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer**in**

107\. Knowest thou not  
That to God belongeth  
The dominion of the heavens  
And the earth?  
And besides Him ye have  
Neither patron nor helper.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_108.gif)

108. Am tureedoona an tas-aloo rasoolakum kam<u>a</u> su-ila
moos<u>a</u> min qablu waman yatabaddali alkufra bi**a**l-eem<u>a</u>ni
faqad <u>d</u>alla saw<u>a</u>a a**l**ssabeel**i**

108\. Would ye question  
Your Apostle as Moses's  
Was questioned of old?  
But whoever changeth  
From Faith to Unbelief,  
Hath strayed without doubt  
From the even way.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_109.gif)

109. Wadda katheerun min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi law yaruddoonakum min
baAAdi eem<u>a</u>nikum kuff<u>a</u>ran <u>h</u>asadan min AAindi
anfusihim min baAAdi m<u>a</u> tabayyana lahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu
fa**o**AAfoo wa**i**<u>s</u>fa<u>h</u>oo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiya
All<u>a</u>hu bi-amrihi inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in
qadeer**un**

109\. Quite a number of the People  
Of the Book wish they could  
Turn you (people) back  
To infidelity after ye have believed,  
From selfish envy,  
After the Truth hath become  
Manifest unto them:  
But forgive and overlook,  
Till God accomplish  
His purpose; for God  
Hath power over all things.'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_110.gif)

110. Waaqeemoo a**l**<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too
a**l**zzak<u>a</u>ta wam<u>a</u> tuqaddimoo li-anfusikum min khayrin
tajidoohu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u>
taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer**un**

110\. And be steadfast in prayer  
And regular in charity:  
And whatever good  
Ye send forth for your souls  
Before you, ye shall find it  
With God: for God sees  
Well all that ye do.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_111.gif)

111. Waq<u>a</u>loo lan yadkhula aljannata ill<u>a</u> man k<u>a</u>na
hoodan aw na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> tilka am<u>a</u>niyyuhum qul h<u>a</u>too
burh<u>a</u>nakum in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen**a**

111\. And they say: "None  
Shall enter Paradise unless  
He be a Jew or a Christian."  
Those are their (vain) desires.  
Say: "Produce your proof  
If ye are truthful."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_112.gif)

112. Bal<u>a</u> man aslama wajhahu lill<u>a</u>hi wahuwa
mu<u>h</u>sinun falahu ajruhu AAinda rabbihi wal<u>a</u> khawfun
AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon**a**

112\. Nay,—whoever submits  
His whole self to God  
And is a doer of good,  
He will get his reward  
With his Lord;  
On such shall be no fear,  
Nor shall they grieve.
