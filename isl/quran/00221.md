# Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.

### Section 21

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_168.gif)

168. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>su kuloo mimm<u>a</u> fee
al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan <u>t</u>ayyiban wal<u>a</u>
tattabiAAoo khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a**l**shshay<u>ta</u>ni innahu lakum
AAaduwwun mubeen**un**

168\. O ye people!  
Eat of what is on earth,  
Lawful and good;  
And do not follow  
The footsteps of the Evil One,  
For he is to you  
An avowed enemy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_169.gif)

169. Innam<u>a</u> ya/murukum bi**al**ssoo-i
wa**a**lfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i waan taqooloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi
m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon**a**

169\. For he commands you  
What is evil  
And shameful,  
And that ye should say  
Of God that of which  
Ye have no knowledge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_170.gif)

170. Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu ittabiAAoo m<u>a</u> anzala
All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>loo bal nattabiAAu m<u>a</u> alfayn<u>a</u>
AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> awa law k<u>a</u>na
<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ohum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloona shay-an wal<u>a</u>
yahtadoon**a**

170\. When it is said to them:  
"Follow what God hath revealed:"  
They say: "Nay! we shall follow  
The ways of our fathers."  
What! even though their fathers  
Were void of wisdom and guidance?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_171.gif)

171. Wamathalu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo kamathali alla<u>th</u>ee
yanAAiqu bim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasmaAAu ill<u>a</u> duAA<u>a</u>an
wanid<u>a</u>an <u>s</u>ummun bukmun AAumyun fahum l<u>a</u>
yaAAqiloon**a**

171\. The parable of those  
Who reject Faith is  
As if one were to shout  
Like a goat-herd, to things  
That listen to nothing  
But calls and cries:  
Deaf, dumb, and blind,  
They are void of wisdom.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_172.gif)

172. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo kuloo min
<u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum wa**o**shkuroo
lill<u>a</u>hi in kuntum iyy<u>a</u>hu taAAbudoon**a**

172\.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_173.gif)

173. Innam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama AAalaykumu almaytata wa**al**ddama
wala<u>h</u>ma alkhinzeeri wam<u>a</u> ohilla bihi lighayri
All<u>a</u>hi famani i<u>dt</u>urra ghayra b<u>a</u>ghin wal<u>a</u>
AA<u>a</u>din fal<u>a</u> ithma AAalayhi inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun
ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

173\. He hath only forbidden you  
Dead meat, and blood,  
And the flesh of swine,  
And that on which  
Any other name hath been invoked  
Besides that of God.  
But if one is forced by necessity,  
Without wilful disobedience,  
Nor transgressing due limits,—  
Then is he guiltless.  
For God is Oft-forgiving  
Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_174.gif)

174. Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaktumoona m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu
mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wayashtaroona bihi thamanan qaleelan ol<u>a</u>-ika
m<u>a</u> ya/kuloona fee bu<u>t</u>oonihim ill<u>a</u>
a**l**nn<u>a</u>ra wal<u>a</u> yukallimuhumu All<u>a</u>hu yawma
alqiy<u>a</u>mati wal<u>a</u> yuzakkeehim walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun
aleem**un**

174\. Those who conceal  
God's revelations in the Book,  
And purchase for them  
A miserable profit,—  
They swallow into themselves  
Naught but Fire;  
God will not address them  
On the Day of Resurrection,  
Nor purify them:  
Grievous will be  
Their Penalty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_175.gif)

175. Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena ishtarawoo
a**l**<u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lata bi**a**lhud<u>a</u>
wa**a**lAAa<u>tha</u>ba bi**a**lmaghfirati fam<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>barahum
AAal<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>r**i**

175\. They are the ones  
Who buy Error  
In place of Guidance  
And Torment in place  
Of Forgiveness.  
Ah! what boldness  
(They show) for the Fire!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/002/002_176.gif)

176. <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha nazzala alkit<u>a</u>ba
bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena ikhtalafoo fee
alkit<u>a</u>bi lafee shiq<u>a</u>qin baAAeed**in**

176\. (Their doom is) because  
God sent down the Book  
In truth but those who seek  
Causes of dispute in the Book  
Are in a schism  
Far (from the purpose).
