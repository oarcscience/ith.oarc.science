# Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.

### Section 8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_054.gif)

54. Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni
li**l**nn<u>a</u>si min kulli mathalin wak<u>a</u>na al-ins<u>a</u>nu
akthara shay-in jadal<u>a</u>**n**

54\. We have explained  
In detail in this Qur-ān,  
For the benefit of mankind,  
Every kind of similitude:  
But man is, in most things,  
Contentious.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_055.gif)

55. Wam<u>a</u> manaAAa a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa an yu/minoo i<u>th</u>
j<u>a</u>ahumu alhud<u>a</u> wayastaghfiroo rabbahum ill<u>a</u> an
ta/tiyahum sunnatu al-awwaleena aw ya/tiyahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu
qubul<u>a</u>**n**

55\. And what is there  
To keep back men  
From believing, now that  
Guidance has come to them,  
Nor from praying for forgiveness  
From their Lord, but that  
(They ask that) the ways  
Of the ancients be repeated  
With them, or the Wrath  
Be brought to them  
Face to face?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_056.gif)

56. Wam<u>a</u> nursilu almursaleena ill<u>a</u> mubashshireena
wamun<u>th</u>ireena wayuj<u>a</u>dilu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo
bi**a**lb<u>at</u>ili liyud<u>h</u>i<u>d</u>oo bihi al<u>h</u>aqqa
wa**i**ttakha<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee wam<u>a</u>
on<u>th</u>iroo huzuw<u>a</u>**n**

56\. We only send the apostles  
To give glad tidings  
And to give warnings  
But the Unbelievers dispute  
With vain argument, in order  
Therewith to weaken the truth,  
And they treat My Signs  
As a jest, as also the fact  
That they are warned!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_057.gif)

57. Waman a*<u>th</u>*lamu mimman <u>th</u>ukkira bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti
rabbihi faaAAra<u>d</u>a AAanh<u>a</u> wanasiya m<u>a</u> qaddamat
yad<u>a</u>hu inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim
akinnatan an yafqahoohu wafee <u>atha</u>nihim waqran wa-in tadAAuhum
il<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> falan yahtadoo i<u>th</u>an abad<u>a</u>**n**

57\. And who doth more wrong  
Than one who is reminded  
Of the Signs of his Lord,  
But turns away from them,  
Forgetting the (deeds) which his hands  
Have sent forth? Verily We  
Have set veils over their hearts  
Lest they should understand this,  
and over their ears, deafness.  
If thou callest them  
To guidance, even then  
Will they never accept guidance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_058.gif)

58. Warabbuka alghafooru <u>th</u>oo a**l**rra<u>h</u>mati law
yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>uhum bim<u>a</u> kasaboo laAAajjala lahumu
alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bal lahum mawAAidun lan yajidoo min doonihi
maw-il<u>a</u>**n**

58\. But your Lord is most forgiving,  
Full of Mercy. If he were  
To call them (at once) to account  
For what they have earned,  
then surely He would  
Have hastened their Punishment:  
But they have their appointed  
Time, beyond which they  
Will find no refuge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/018/018_059.gif)

59. Watilka alqur<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u>hum lamm<u>a</u>
*<u>th</u>*alamoo wajaAAaln<u>a</u> limahlikihim mawAAid<u>a</u>**n**

59\. Such were the populations  
We destroyed when they  
Committed iniquities; but  
We fixed an appointed time  
For their destruction.
