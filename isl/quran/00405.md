# Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.

### Section 5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_026.gif)

26. Yureedu All<u>a</u>hu liyubayyina lakum wayahdiyakum sunana
alla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum wayatooba AAalaykum wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu
AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem**un**

26\. God doth wish  
To make clear to you  
And to show you  
The ordinances of those  
Before you; and (He  
Doth wish to) turn to you  
(In Mercy): and God  
Is All-knowing, All-wise.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_027.gif)

27. Wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu yureedu an yatooba AAalaykum wayureedu
alla<u>th</u>eena yattabiAAoona a**l**shshahaw<u>a</u>ti an tameeloo
maylan AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**an**

27\. God doth wish  
To turn to you,  
But the wish of those  
Who follow their lusts  
Is that ye should turn  
Away (from Him),  
Far, far away.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_028.gif)

28. Yureedu All<u>a</u>hu an yukhaffifa AAankum wakhuliqa
al-ins<u>a</u>nu <u>d</u>aAAeef<u>a</u>**n**

28\. God doth wish  
To lighten your (difficulties);  
For man was created  
Weak (in flesh).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_029.gif)

29. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u>
ta/kuloo amw<u>a</u>lakum baynakum bi**a**lb<u>at</u>ili ill<u>a</u> an
takoona tij<u>a</u>ratan AAan tar<u>ad</u>in minkum wal<u>a</u> taqtuloo
anfusakum inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bikum
ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u>**n**

29\. O ye who believe! sot  
Eat not up your property  
Among yourselves in vanities:  
But let there be amongst you  
Traffic and trade  
By mutual good-will:  
Nor kill (or destroy)  
Yourselves: for verily  
God hath been to you  
Most Merciful!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_030.gif)

30. Waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika AAudw<u>a</u>nan wa*<u>th</u>*ulman
fasawfa nu<u>s</u>leehi n<u>a</u>ran wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika
AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<u>a</u>**n**

30\. If any do that  
In rancour and injustice,—  
Soon shall We cast them  
Into the Fire: and easy  
It is for God.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_031.gif)

31. In tajtaniboo kab<u>a</u>-ira m<u>a</u> tunhawna AAanhu nukaffir
AAankum sayyi-<u>a</u>tikum wanudkhilkum mudkhalan kareem<u>a</u>**n**

31\. If ye (but) eschew  
The most heinous  
Of the things  
Which ye are forbidden to do,  
We shall expel  
Out of you  
All the evil in you,  
And admit you to a Gate  
Of great honour.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_032.gif)

32. Wal<u>a</u> tatamannaw m<u>a</u> fa<u>dd</u>ala All<u>a</u>hu bihi
baAA<u>d</u>akum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in li**l**rrij<u>a</u>li
na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> iktasaboo wali**l**nnis<u>a</u>-i
na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> iktasabna wa**i**s-aloo All<u>a</u>ha min
fa<u>d</u>lihi inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bikulli shay-in
AAaleem<u>a</u>**n**

32\. And in no wise covet  
Those things in which God  
Hath bestowed His gifts  
More freely on some of you  
Than on others: to men  
Is allotted what they earn,  
And to women what they earn:  
But ask God of His bounty.  
For God hath full knowledge  
Of all things.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/004/004_033.gif)

33. Walikullin jaAAaln<u>a</u> maw<u>a</u>liya mimm<u>a</u> taraka
alw<u>a</u>lid<u>a</u>ni wa**a**l-aqraboona wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena
AAaqadat aym<u>a</u>nukum fa<u>a</u>toohum na<u>s</u>eebahum inna
All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in
shaheed<u>a</u>**n**

33\. To (benefit) every one,  
We have appointed  
Sharers and heirs  
To property left  
By parents and relatives.  
To those, also, to whom  
Your right hand was pledged,  
Give their due portion.  
For truly God is witness  
To all things.
