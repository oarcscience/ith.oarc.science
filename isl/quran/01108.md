# Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).

### Section 8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_084.gif)

84. Wa-il<u>a</u> madyana akh<u>a</u>hum shuAAayban q<u>a</u>la
y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin
ghayruhu wal<u>a</u> tanqu<u>s</u>oo almiky<u>a</u>la
wa**a**lmeez<u>a</u>na innee ar<u>a</u>kum bikhayrin wa-innee
akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin
mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>**in**

84\. To the Madyan people  
(We sent) Shu’aib, one  
Of their own brethren: he said:  
"O my people! worship God:  
Ye have no other god  
But Him. And give not  
Short measure or weight:  
I see you in prosperity,  
But I fear for you  
The Penalty of a Day  
That will compass (you) all round.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_085.gif)

85. Way<u>a</u> qawmi awfoo almiky<u>a</u>la wa**a**lmeez<u>a</u>na
bi**a**lqis<u>t</u>i wal<u>a</u> tabkhasoo a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa
ashy<u>a</u>ahum wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen**a**

85\. "And O my people! give  
Just measure and weight,  
Nor withhold from the people  
The things that are their due:  
Commit not evil in the land  
With intent to do mischief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_086.gif)

86. Baqiyyatu All<u>a</u>hi khayrun lakum in kuntum mu/mineena
wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AAalaykum bi<u>h</u>afee*<u>th</u>***in**

86\. "That which is left you  
By God is best for you,  
If ye (but) believed!  
But I am not set  
Over you to keep watch!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_087.gif)

87. Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> shuAAaybu a<u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tuka ta/muruka
an natruka m<u>a</u> yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> aw an nafAAala
fee amw<u>a</u>lin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> nash<u>a</u>o innaka laanta
al<u>h</u>aleemu a**l**rrasheed**u**

87\. They said: "O Shu’aib!  
Does thy (religion of) prayer  
Command thee that we  
Leave off the worship which  
Our fathers practised, or  
That we leave off doing  
What we like with our property?  
Truly, thou art the one  
That forbeareth with faults  
And is right-minded!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_088.gif)

88. Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi araaytum in kuntu AAal<u>a</u>
bayyinatin min rabbee warazaqanee minhu rizqan <u>h</u>asanan
wam<u>a</u> oreedu an okh<u>a</u>lifakum il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>
anh<u>a</u>kum AAanhu in oreedu ill<u>a</u> al-i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>a
m<u>a</u> ista<u>t</u>aAAtu wam<u>a</u> tawfeeqee ill<u>a</u>
bi**A**ll<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi tawakkaltu wa-ilayhi oneeb**u**

88\. He said: "O my people!  
See ye whether I hate  
A Clear (Sign) from my Lord,  
And He hath given me  
Sustenance (pure and) good  
As from Himself? I wish not,  
In opposition to you, to do  
That which I forbid you to do.  
I only desire (your) betterment  
To the best of my power;  
And my success (in my task)  
Can only come from God.  
In Him I trust,  
And unto Him I look.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_089.gif)

89. Way<u>a</u> qawmi l<u>a</u> yajrimannakum shiq<u>a</u>qee an
yu<u>s</u>eebakum mithlu m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>ba qawma noo<u>h</u>in aw
qawma hoodin aw qawma <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>in wam<u>a</u> qawmu
loo<u>t</u>in minkum bibaAAeed**in**

89\. "And O my people!  
Let not my dissent (from you)  
Cause you to sin,  
Lest ye suffer  
A fate similar to that  
Of the people of Noah  
Or of Hūd or of Sālib,  
Nor are the people of Lūt  
Far off from you!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_090.gif)

90. Wa**i**staghfiroo rabbakum thumma tooboo ilayhi inna rabbee
ra<u>h</u>eemun wadood**un**

90\. "But ask forgiveness  
Of your Lord, and turn  
Unto Him (in repentance):  
For my Lord is indeed  
Full of mercy and loving-kindness."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_091.gif)

91. Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> shuAAaybu m<u>a</u> nafqahu katheeran
mimm<u>a</u> taqoolu wa-inn<u>a</u> lanar<u>a</u>ka feen<u>a</u>
<u>d</u>aAAeefan walawl<u>a</u> rah<u>t</u>uka larajamn<u>a</u>ka
wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayn<u>a</u> biAAazeez**in**

91\. They said: "O Shu’aib!  
Much of what thou sayest  
We do not understand!  
In fact among us we see  
That thou hast no strength!  
Were it not for thy family,  
We should certainly  
Have stoned thee!  
For thou hast among us  
No great position!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_092.gif)

92. Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi arah<u>t</u>ee aAAazzu AAalaykum mina
All<u>a</u>hi wa**i**ttakha<u>th</u>tumoohu war<u>a</u>akum
*<u>th</u>*ihriyyan inna rabbee bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona
mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>**un**

92\. He said: "O my people!  
Is then my family  
Of more consideration with you  
Than God? For ye cast Him  
Away behind your backs  
(With contempt). But verily  
My Lord encompasseth  
On all sides  
All that ye do!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_093.gif)

93. Way<u>a</u> qawmi iAAmaloo AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natikum innee
AA<u>a</u>milun sawfa taAAlamoona man ya/teehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun
yukhzeehi waman huwa k<u>ath</u>ibun wa**i**rtaqiboo inne maAAakum
raqeeb**un**

93\. "And O my people!  
Do whatever ye can:  
I will do (my part):  
Soon will ye know  
Who it is on whom  
Descends the Penalty  
Of ignominy, and who  
Is a liar!  
And watch ye!  
For I too am watching  
With you!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_094.gif)

94. Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> najjayn<u>a</u> shuAAayban
wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu bira<u>h</u>matin
minn<u>a</u> waakha<u>th</u>ati alla<u>th</u>eena *<u>th</u>*alamoo
a**l**<u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee
diy<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen**a**

94\. When Our decree issued,  
We saved Shu’aib and those  
Who believed with him,  
By (special) Mercy from Ourselves  
But the (mighty) Blast did seize  
The wrong-doers, and they  
Lay prostrate in their homes  
By the morning,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_095.gif)

95. Kaan lam yaghnaw feeh<u>a</u> al<u>a</u> buAAdan limadyana
kam<u>a</u> baAAidat thamood**u**

95\. As if they had never  
Dwelt and flourished there!  
Ah! Behold! How the Madyan  
Were removed (from sight)  
As were removed the <u>Th</u>amūd!
