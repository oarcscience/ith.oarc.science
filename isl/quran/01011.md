# Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.

### Section 11

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_104.gif)

104. Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>su in kuntum fee
shakkin min deenee fal<u>a</u> aAAbudu alla<u>th</u>eena taAAbudoona min
dooni All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u>kin aAAbudu All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee
yatawaff<u>a</u>kum waomirtu an akoona mina almu/mineen**a**

104\. Say: "O ye men!  
If ye are in doubt  
As to my religion, (behold!)  
I worship not what ye  
Worship, other than God!  
But I worship God  
Who will take your souls  
(At death): I am commanded  
To be (in the ranks)  
Of the Believers,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_105.gif)

105. Waan aqim wajhaka li**l**ddeeni <u>h</u>aneefan wal<u>a</u>
takoonanna mina almushrikeen**a**

105\. "And further (thus), "set thy face  
Towards Religion with true piety,  
And never in any wise  
Be of the Unbelievers;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_106.gif)

106. Wal<u>a</u> tadAAu min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>
yanfaAAuka wal<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urruka fa-in faAAalta fa-innaka
i<u>th</u>an mina a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

106\. "Nor call on any,  
Other than God;—  
Such will neither profit thee  
Nor hurt thee: if thou dost,  
Behold! thou shalt certainly  
Be of those who do wrong."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_107.gif)

107. Wa-in yamsaska All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>d</u>urrin fal<u>a</u>
k<u>a</u>shifa lahu ill<u>a</u> huwa wa-in yuridka bikhayrin fal<u>a</u>
r<u>a</u>dda lifa<u>d</u>lihi yu<u>s</u>eebu bihi man yash<u>a</u>o min
AAib<u>a</u>dihi wahuwa alghafooru a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**u**

107\. If God do touch thee  
With hurt, there is none  
Can remove it but He:  
If He do design some benefit  
For thee, there is none  
Can keep back His favour:  
He causeth it to reach  
Whomsoever of His servants  
He pleaseth. And He is  
The Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_108.gif)

108. Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>su qad j<u>a</u>akumu
al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbikum famani ihtad<u>a</u> fa-innam<u>a</u>
yahtadee linafsihi waman <u>d</u>alla fa-innam<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>illu
AAalayh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AAalaykum biwakeel**in**

108\. Say: "O ye men! Now  
Truth hath reached you  
From your Lord! Those who receive  
Guidance, do so for the good  
Of their own souls; those  
Who stray, do so to their own loss:  
And I am not (set) over you  
To arrange your affairs."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/010/010_109.gif)

109. Wa**i**ttabiAA m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayka wa**i**<u>s</u>bir
<u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kuma All<u>a</u>hu wahuwa khayru
al<u>ha</u>kimeen**a**

109\. Follow thou the inspiration  
Sent unto thee, and be  
Patient and constant, till God  
Do decide: for He  
Is the Best to decide.
