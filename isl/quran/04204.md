# Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_030.gif)

30. Wam<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bakum min mu<u>s</u>eebatin fabim<u>a</u>
kasabat aydeekum wayaAAfoo AAan katheer**in**

30\. Whatever misfortune  
Happens to you, is because  
Of the things your hands  
Have wrought, and for many  
(Of them) He grants forgiveness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_031.gif)

31. Wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> lakum
min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer**in**

31\. Nor can ye frustrate (aught),  
(Fleeing) through the earth;  
Nor have ye, besides God,  
Any one to protect  
Or to help.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_032.gif)

32. Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi aljaw<u>a</u>ri fee alba<u>h</u>ri
ka**a**l-aAAl<u>a</u>m**i**

32\. And among His Signs  
Are the ships, smooth-running  
Through the ocean, (tall)  
As mountains.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_033.gif)

33. In yasha/ yuskini a**l**rree<u>h</u>a faya*<u>th</u>*lalna
raw<u>a</u>kida AAal<u>a</u> *<u>th</u>*ahrihi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika
la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin shakoor**in**

33\. If it be His Will,  
He can still the Wind:  
Then would they become  
Motionless on the back  
Of the (ocean). Verily  
In this are Signs  
For everyone who patiently  
Perseveres and is grateful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_034.gif)

34. Aw yoobiqhunna bim<u>a</u> kasaboo wayaAAfu AAan katheer**in**

34\. Or He can cause them  
To perish because of  
The (evil) which (the men)  
Have earned; but much  
Doth He forgive.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_035.gif)

35. WayaAAlama alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee
<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lahum min
ma<u>h</u>ee<u>s</u>**in**

35\. But let those know, who  
Dispute about Our Signs,  
That there is for them  
No way of escape.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_036.gif)

36. Fam<u>a</u> ooteetum min shay-in famat<u>a</u>AAu
al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a**l**dduny<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> AAinda
All<u>a</u>hi khayrun waabq<u>a</u> lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo
waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon**a**

36\. Whatever ye are given (here)  
Is (but) a convenience  
Of this Life: but that  
Which is with God  
Is better and more lasting:  
(It is) for those who believe  
And put their trust  
In their Lord;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_037.gif)

37. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena yajtaniboona kab<u>a</u>-ira al-ithmi
wa**a**lfaw<u>ah</u>isha wa-i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> gha<u>d</u>iboo hum
yaghfiroon**a**

37\. Those who avoid the greater  
Crimes and shameful deeds,  
And, when they are angry  
Even then forgive;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_038.gif)

38. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena istaj<u>a</u>boo lirabbihim waaq<u>a</u>moo
a**l**<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waamruhum shoor<u>a</u> baynahum
wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon**a**

38\. Those who hearken  
To their Lord, and establish  
Regular prayer; who (conduct)  
Their affairs by mutual  
Consultation;  
Who spend out of what  
We bestow on them  
For Sustenance;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_039.gif)

39. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> a<u>sa</u>bahumu albaghyu hum
yanta<u>s</u>iroon**a**

39\. And those who, when  
An oppressive wrong is inflicted  
On them, (are not cowed  
But) help and defend themselves.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_040.gif)

40. Wajaz<u>a</u>o sayyi-atin sayyi-atun mithluh<u>a</u> faman
AAaf<u>a</u> waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a faajruhu AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi
innahu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

40\. The recompense for an injury  
Is an injury equal thereto  
(In degree): but if a person  
Forgives and makes reconciliation,  
His reward is due  
From God: for (God)  
Loveth not those who  
Do wrong.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_041.gif)

41. Walamani inta<u>s</u>ara baAAda *<u>th</u>*ulmihi faol<u>a</u>-ika
m<u>a</u> AAalayhim min sabeel**in**

41\. But indeed if any do help  
And defend themselves  
After a wrong (done)  
To them, against such  
There is no cause  
Of blame.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_042.gif)

42. Innam<u>a</u> a**l**ssabeelu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena
ya*<u>th</u>*limoona a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa wayabghoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i
bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun
aleem**un**

42\. The blame is only  
Against those who oppress  
Men with wrong-doing  
And insolently transgress  
Beyond bounds through the land,  
Defying right and justice:  
For such there will be  
A Penalty grievous.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/042/042_043.gif)

43. Walaman <u>s</u>abara waghafara inna <u>tha</u>lika lamin AAazmi
al-omoor**i**

43\. But indeed if any  
Show patience and forgive,  
That would truly be  
An exercise of courageous will  
And resolution in the conduct  
Of affairs.
