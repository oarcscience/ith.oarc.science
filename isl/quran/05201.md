# Sūra LII.: Ṭūr, or the Mount.

### Section 1

![](../gif/052/052_001.gif)

1. Wa**al**<u>tt</u>oor**i**

1\. By the Mount (of Revelation);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_002.gif)

2. Wakit<u>a</u>bin mas<u>t</u>oor**in**

2\. By a Decree Inscribed

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_003.gif)

3. Fee raqqin manshoor**in**

3\. In a Scroll unfolded;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_004.gif)

4. Wa**a**lbayti almaAAmoor**i**

4\. By the much-frequented Fane;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_005.gif)

5. Wa**al**ssaqfi almarfooAA**i**

5\. By the Canopy Raised High;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_006.gif)

6. Wa**a**lba<u>h</u>ri almasjoor**i**

6\. And by the Ocean  
Filled with Swell;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_007.gif)

7. Inna AAa<u>tha</u>ba rabbika law<u>a</u>qiAA**un**

7\. Verily, the Doom of thy Lord  
Will indeed come to pass;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_008.gif)

8. M<u>a</u> lahu min d<u>a</u>fiAA**in**

8\. There is none  
Can avert it;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_009.gif)

9. Yawma tamooru a**l**ssam<u>a</u>o mawr<u>a</u>**n**

9\. On the Day when  
The firmament will be  
In dreadful commotion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_010.gif)

10. Wataseeru aljib<u>a</u>lu sayr<u>a</u>**n**

10\. And the mountains will fly  
Hither and thither.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_011.gif)

11. Fawaylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

11\. Then woe that Day  
To those that treat  
(Truth) as Falsehood;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_012.gif)

12. Alla<u>th</u>eena hum fee khaw<u>d</u>in yalAAaboon**a**

12\. That play (and paddle)  
In shallow trifles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_013.gif)

13. Yawma yudaAAAAoona il<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ri jahannama
daAAAA<u>a</u>**n**

13\. That Day shall they be  
Thrust down to the Fire  
Of Hell, irresistibly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_014.gif)

14. H<u>ath</u>ihi a**l**nn<u>a</u>ru allatee kuntum bih<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>iboon**a**

14\. "This", it will be said,  
"Is the Fire,—which ye  
Were wont to deny!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_015.gif)

15. Afasi<u>h</u>run h<u>atha</u> am antum l<u>a</u>
tub<u>s</u>iroon**a**

15\. "Is this then a fake,  
Or is it ye that  
Do not see?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_016.gif)

16. I<u>s</u>lawh<u>a</u> fa**i**<u>s</u>biroo aw l<u>a</u>
ta<u>s</u>biroo saw<u>a</u>on AAalaykum innam<u>a</u> tujzawna m<u>a</u>
kuntum taAAmaloon**a**

16\. "Burn ye therein:  
The same is it to you  
Whether ye bear it  
With patience, or not:  
Ye but receive the recompense  
Of your (own) deeds."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_017.gif)

17. Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin wanaAAeem**in**

17\. As to the Righteous,  
They will be in Gardens,  
And in Happiness,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_018.gif)

18. F<u>a</u>kiheena bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum
wawaq<u>a</u>hum rabbuhum AAa<u>tha</u>ba alja<u>h</u>eem**i**

18\. Enjoying the (Bliss) which  
Their Lord hath bestowed  
On them, and their Lord  
Shall deliver them from  
The Penalty of the Fire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_019.gif)

19. Kuloo wa**i**shraboo hanee-an bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon**a**

19\. (To them will be said:)  
"Eat and drink ye,  
With profit and health,  
Because of your (good) deeds."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_020.gif)

20. Muttaki-eena AAal<u>a</u> sururin ma<u>s</u>foofatin
wazawwajn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>h</u>oorin AAeen**in**

20\. They will recline (with ease)  
On Thrones (of dignity)  
Arranged in ranks;  
And We shall join them  
To Companions, with beautiful  
Big and lustrous eyes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_021.gif)

21. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa**i**ttabaAAat-hum
<u>th</u>urriyyatuhum bi-eem<u>a</u>nin al<u>h</u>aqn<u>a</u> bihim
<u>th</u>urriyyatahum wam<u>a</u> alatn<u>a</u>hum min AAamalihim min
shay-in kullu imri-in bim<u>a</u> kasaba raheen**un**

21\. And those who believe  
And whose families follow  
Them in Faith,— to them  
Shall We join their families:  
Nor shall We deprive them  
(Of the fruit) of aught  
Of their works:  
(Yet) is each individual  
In pledge for his deeds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_022.gif)

22. Waamdadn<u>a</u>hum bif<u>a</u>kihatin wala<u>h</u>min mimm<u>a</u>
yashtahoon**a**

22\. And We shall bestow  
On them, of fruit and meat,  
Anything they shall desire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_023.gif)

23. Yatan<u>a</u>zaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> ka/san l<u>a</u> laghwun
feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta/theem**un**

23\. They shall there exchange,  
One with another,  
A (loving) cup  
Free of frivolity,  
Free of all taint  
Of ill.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_024.gif)

24. Waya<u>t</u>oofu AAalayhim ghilm<u>a</u>nun lahum kaannahum lu/luon
maknoon**un**

24\. Round about them will serve,  
(Devoted) to them,  
Youths (handsome) as Pearls  
Well-guarded.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_025.gif)

25. Waaqbala baAA<u>d</u>uhum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in
yatas<u>a</u>aloon**a**

25\. They will advance  
To each other, engaging  
In mutual enquiry.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_026.gif)

26. Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> qablu fee ahlin<u>a</u>
mushfiqeen**a**

26\. They will say: "Aforetime,  
We were not without fear  
For the sake of our people.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_027.gif)

27. Famanna All<u>a</u>hu AAalayn<u>a</u> wawaq<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>
AAa<u>tha</u>ba a**l**ssamoom**i**

27\. "But God has been good  
To us, and has delivered us  
From the Penalty  
Of the Scorching Wind.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/052/052_028.gif)

28. Inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> min qablu nadAAoohu innahu huwa albarru
a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**u**

28\. "Truly, we did call  
Unto Him from of old:  
Truly it is He,  
The Beneficent, the Merciful!
