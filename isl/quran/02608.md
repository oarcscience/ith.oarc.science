# Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.

### Section 8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_141.gif)

141. Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu almursaleen**a**

141\. The <u>Th</u>amūd (people) rejected  
The apostles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_142.gif)

142. I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum akhoohum <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>un
al<u>a</u> tattaqoon**a**

142\. Behold, their brother Ṣāliḥ  
Said to them: "Will you not  
Fear (God)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_143.gif)

143. Innee lakum rasoolun ameen**un**

143\. "I am to you an apostle  
Worthy of all trust.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_144.gif)

144. Fa**i**ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon**i**

144\. "So fear God, and obey me.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_145.gif)

145. Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u>
AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

145\. "No reward do I ask  
Of you for it: my reward  
Is only from the Lord  
Of the Worlds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_146.gif)

146. Atutrakoona fee m<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mineen**a**

146\. "Will ye be left secure,  
In (the enjoyment of) all  
That ye have here?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_147.gif)

147. Fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon**in**

147\. "Gardens and Springs,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_148.gif)

148. WazurooAAin wanakhlin <u>t</u>alAAuh<u>a</u> ha<u>d</u>eem**un**

148\. "And corn-fields and date palms  
With spathes near breaking  
(With the weight of fruit)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_149.gif)

149. Watan<u>h</u>itoona mina aljib<u>a</u>li buyootan
f<u>a</u>riheen**a**

149\. "And ye carve houses  
Out of (rocky) mountains  
With great skill.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_150.gif)

150. Fa**i**ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon**i**

150\. "But fear God and obey me;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_151.gif)

151. Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo amra almusrifeen**a**

151\. "And follow not the bidding  
Of those who are extravagant,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_152.gif)

152. Alla<u>th</u>eena yufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u>
yu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oon**a**

152\. "Who make mischief in the land,  
And mend not (their ways)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_153.gif)

153. Q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> anta mina almusa<u>hh</u>areen**a**

153\. Whey said: "Thou art only  
One of those bewitched!'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_154.gif)

154. M<u>a</u> anta ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> fa/ti
bi-<u>a</u>yatin in kunta mina a**l**<u>ssa</u>diqeena

154\. "Thou art no more than  
A mortal like us:  
Then bring us a Sign,  
If thou tellest the truth!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_155.gif)

155. Q<u>a</u>la h<u>ath</u>ihi n<u>a</u>qatun lah<u>a</u> shirbun
walakum shirbu yawmin maAAloom**in**

155\. He said: "Here is  
A she-camel: she has  
A right of watering,  
And ye have a right  
Of watering, (severally)  
On a day appointed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_156.gif)

156. Wal<u>a</u> tamassooh<u>a</u> bisoo-in faya/khu<u>th</u>akum
AAa<u>tha</u>bu yawmin AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**in**

156\. "Touch her not with harm,  
Lest the Penalty  
Of a Great Day  
Seize you."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_157.gif)

157. FaAAaqarooh<u>a</u> faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo n<u>a</u>dimeen**a**

157\. But they ham-strung her:  
Then did they become  
Full of regrets.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_158.gif)

158. Faakha<u>th</u>ahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika
la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen**a**

158\. But the Penalty seized them.  
Verily in this is a Sign:  
But most of them  
Do not believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_159.gif)

159. Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**u**

159\. And verily thy Lord  
Is He, the Exalted in Might,  
Most Merciful.
