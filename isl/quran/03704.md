# Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_114.gif)

114. Walaqad manann<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>
wah<u>a</u>roon**a**

114\. Again, (of old,)  
We bestowed Our favour  
On Moses and Aaron,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_115.gif)

115. Wanajjayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> waqawmahum<u>a</u> mina alkarbi
alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**i**

115\. And We delivered them  
And their people from  
(Their) Great Calamity;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_116.gif)

116. Wana<u>s</u>arn<u>a</u>hum fak<u>a</u>noo humu
algh<u>a</u>libeen**a**

116\. And We helped them,  
So they overcame  
(their troubles);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_117.gif)

117. Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba almustabeen**a**

117\. And We gave them  
The Book which helps  
To make things clear;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_118.gif)

118. Wahadayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> a**l**<u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>a
almustaqeem**a**

118\. And We guided them  
To the Straight Way.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_119.gif)

119. Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhim<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>khireen**a**

119\. And We left (this blessing)  
For them among generations  
(To come) in later times:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_120.gif)

120. Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon**a**

120\. "Peace and salutation  
To Moses and Aaron!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_121.gif)

121. Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen**a**

121\. Thus indeed do We reward  
Those who do right.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_122.gif)

122. Innahum<u>a</u> min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen**a**

122\. For they were two  
Of Our believing Servants.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_123.gif)

123. Wa-inna ily<u>a</u>sa lamina almursaleen**a**

123\. So also was Elias  
Among those sent (by Us).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_124.gif)

124. I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi al<u>a</u> tattaqoon**a**

124\. Behold, he said  
To his people,  
"Will ye not fear (God)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_125.gif)

125. AtadAAoona baAAlan wata<u>th</u>aroona a<u>h</u>sana
alkh<u>a</u>liqeen**a**

125\. "Will ye call upon Baal  
And forsake the Best  
Of Creators,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_126.gif)

126. All<u>a</u>ha rabbakum warabba <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ikumu
al-awwaleen**a**

126\. "God, your Lord and Cherisher  
And the Lord and Cherisher  
Of your fathers of old?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_127.gif)

127. Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu fa-innahum lamu<u>hd</u>aroon**a**

127\. But they rejected him,  
And they will certainly  
Be called up (for punishment),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_128.gif)

128. Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een**a**

128\. Except the sincere and devoted  
Servants of God (among them).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_129.gif)

129. Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhi fee al-<u>a</u>khireen**a**

129\. And We left (this blessing)  
For him among generations  
(To come) in later times:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_130.gif)

130. Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> il y<u>a</u>seen**a**

130\. "Peace and salutation  
To such as Elias!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_131.gif)

131. Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen**a**

131\. Thus indeed do We reward  
Those who do right.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_132.gif)

132. Innahu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen**a**

132\. For he was one  
Of Our believing Servants.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_133.gif)

133. Wa-inna loo<u>t</u>an lamina almursaleen**a**

133\. So also was Lot  
Among those sent (by Us).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_134.gif)

134. I<u>th</u> najjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu ajmaAAeen**a**

134\. Behold, We delivered him  
And his adherents, all

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_135.gif)

135. Ill<u>a</u> AAajoozan fee algh<u>a</u>bireen**a**

135\. Except an old woman  
Who was among those  
Who lagged behind:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_136.gif)

136. Thumma dammarn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen**a**

136\. Then We destroyed  
The rest.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_137.gif)

137. Wa-innakum latamurroona AAalayhim mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een**a**

137\. Verily, ye pass  
By their (sites),  
By day—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/037/037_138.gif)

138. Wabi**a**llayli afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon**a**

138\. And by night:  
Will ye not understand?
