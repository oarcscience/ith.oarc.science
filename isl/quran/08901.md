# Sūra LXXXIX.: Fajr, or The Break of Day.

### Section 1

![](../gif/089/089_001.gif)

1. Wa**a**lfajr**i**

1\. By the Break of Day;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_002.gif)

2. Walay<u>a</u>lin AAashr**in**

2\. By the Nights twice five;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_003.gif)

3. Wa**al**shshafAAi wa**a**lwatr**i**

3\. By the Even  
And Odd (contrasted);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_004.gif)

4. Wa**a**llayli i<u>tha</u> yasr**i**

4\. And by the Night  
When it passeth away;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_005.gif)

5. Hal fee <u>tha</u>lika qasamun li<u>th</u>ee <u>h</u>ijr**in**

5\. Is there (not) in these  
An adjuration (or evidence)  
For those who understand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_006.gif)

6. Alam tara kayfa faAAala rabbuka biAA<u>a</u>d**in**

6\. Seest thou not  
How thy Lord dealt  
With the ‘Ād (people),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_007.gif)

7. Irama <u>tha</u>ti alAAim<u>a</u>d**i**

7\. Of the (city of) Iram,  
With lofty pillars,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_008.gif)

8. Allatee lam yukhlaq mithluh<u>a</u> fee albil<u>a</u>d**i**

8\. The like of which  
Were not produced  
In (all) the land?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_009.gif)

9. Wathamooda alla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>boo a**l**<u>ss</u>akhra
bi**a**lw<u>a</u>d**i**

9\. And with the <u>Th</u>amūd  
(People), who cut out  
(Huge) rocks in the-valley?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_010.gif)

10. WafirAAawna <u>th</u>ee al-awt<u>a</u>d**i**

10\. And with Pharaoh,  
Lord of Stakes?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_011.gif)

11. Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>t</u>aghaw fee albil<u>a</u>d**i**

11\. (All) these transgressed  
Beyond bounds in the lands.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_012.gif)

12. Faaktharoo feeh<u>a</u> alfas<u>a</u>d**a**

12\. And heaped therein  
Mischief (on mischief).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_013.gif)

13. Fa<u>s</u>abba AAalayhim rabbuka saw<u>t</u>a AAa<u>tha</u>b**in**

13\. Therefore did thy Lord  
Pour on them a scourge  
Of diverse chastisements:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_014.gif)

14. Inna rabbaka labi**a**lmir<u>sa</u>d**i**

14\. For thy Lord is  
(As a Guardian)  
On a watch-tower.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_015.gif)

15. Faamm<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> ibtal<u>a</u>hu
rabbuhu faakramahu wanaAAAAamahu fayaqoolu rabbee akraman**i**

15\. Now, as for man,  
When his Lord trieth him,  
Giving him honour and gifts,  
Then saith he, (puffed up),  
"My Lord hath honoured me"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_016.gif)

16. Waamm<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> ibtal<u>a</u>hu faqadara
AAalayhi rizqahu fayaqoolu rabbee ah<u>a</u>nan**i**

16\. But when he trieth him,  
Restricting his subsistence  
For him, then saith he  
(In despair), "My Lord  
Hath humiliated me I."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_017.gif)

17. Kall<u>a</u> bal l<u>a</u> tukrimoona alyateem**a**

17\. Nay, nay! But ye  
Honour not the orphans!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_018.gif)

18. Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>hadd</u>oona AAal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi
almiskeen**i**

18\. Nor do ye encourage  
One another  
To feed the poor!—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_019.gif)

19. Wata/kuloona a**l**ttur<u>a</u>tha aklan lamm<u>a</u>**n**

19\. And ye devour Inheritance—  
All with greed,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_020.gif)

20. Watu<u>h</u>ibboona alm<u>a</u>la <u>h</u>ubban jamm<u>a</u>**n**

20\. And ye love wealth  
With inordinate love!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_021.gif)

21. Kall<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> dukkati al-ar<u>d</u>u dakkan
dakk<u>a</u>**n**

21\. Nay! When the earth  
Is pounded to powder,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_022.gif)

22. Waj<u>a</u>a rabbuka wa**a**lmalaku <u>s</u>affan
<u>s</u>aff<u>a</u>**n**

22\. And thy Lord cometh,  
And His angels,  
Rank upon rank,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_023.gif)

23. Wajee-a yawma-i<u>th</u>in bijahannama yawma-i<u>th</u>in
yata<u>th</u>akkaru al-ins<u>a</u>nu waann<u>a</u> lahu
a**l**<u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u>

23\. And Hell, that Day,  
Is brought (face to face),—  
On that Day will man  
Remember, but how will  
That remembrance profit him?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_024.gif)

24. Yaqoolu y<u>a</u> laytanee qaddamtu li<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tee

24\. He will say: "Ah!  
Would that I had  
Sent forth (Good Deeds)  
For (this) my (Future) Life!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_025.gif)

25. Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu AAa<u>tha</u>bahu
a<u>h</u>ad**un**

25\. For, that Day,  
His Chastisement will be  
Such as none (else)  
Can inflict,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_026.gif)

26. Wal<u>a</u> yoothiqu wath<u>a</u>qahu a<u>h</u>ad**un**

26\. And His bonds  
Will be such as  
None (other) can bind.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_027.gif)

27. Y<u>a</u> ayyatuh<u>a</u> a**l**nnafsu almu<u>t</u>ma-inna**tu**

27\. (To the righteous soul  
Will be said:)  
"O (thou) soul,  
In (complete) rest  
And satisfaction!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_028.gif)

28. IrjiAAee il<u>a</u> rabbiki r<u>ad</u>iyatan mar<u>d</u>iyya**tan**

28\. "Come back thou  
To thy Lord,—  
Well pleased (thyself),  
And well-pleasing  
Unto Him!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_029.gif)

29. Fa**o**dkhulee fee AAib<u>a</u>dee

29\. "Enter thou, then,  
Among my Devotees!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/089/089_030.gif)

30. Wa**o**dkhulee jannatee  

30\. "Yea, enter thou  
My Heaven!
