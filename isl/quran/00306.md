# Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.

### Section 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_055.gif)

55. I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu y<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> innee
mutawaffeeka war<u>a</u>fiAAuka ilayya wamu<u>t</u>ahhiruka mina
alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waj<u>a</u>AAilu alla<u>th</u>eena
ittabaAAooka fawqa alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo il<u>a</u> yawmi
alqiy<u>a</u>mati thumma ilayya marjiAAukum faa<u>h</u>kumu baynakum
feem<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon**a**

55\. Behold! God said:  
"O Jesus! I will take thee  
And raise thee to Myself  
And clear thee (of the falsehoods)  
Of those who blaspheme;  
I will make those  
Who follow thee superior  
To those who reject faith,  
To the Day of Resurrection:  
Then shall ye all  
Return unto me,  
And I will judge  
Between you of the matters  
Wherein ye dispute.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_056.gif)

56. Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo faoAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum
AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan fee a**l**dduny<u>a</u>
wa**a**l-<u>a</u>khirati wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen**a**

56\. "As to those who reject faith,  
I will punish them  
With terrible agony  
In this world and in the Hereafter,  
Nor will they have  
Anyone to help.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_057.gif)

57. Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo
a**l**<u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fayuwaffeehim ojoorahum
wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

57\. "As to those who believe  
And work righteousness,  
God will pay them (in full)  
Their reward;  
But God loveth not  
Those who do wrong.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_058.gif)

58. <u>Tha</u>lika natloohu AAalayka mina al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti
wa**al**<u>thth</u>ikri al<u>h</u>akeem**i**

58\. "This is what we rehearse  
Unto thee of the Signs  
And the Message  
Of Wisdom."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_059.gif)

59. Inna mathala AAees<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi kamathali
<u>a</u>dama khalaqahu min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma q<u>a</u>la lahu kun
fayakoon**u**

59\. The similitude of Jesus  
Before God is as that of Adam;  
He created him from dust,  
Then said to him, "Be":  
And he was.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_060.gif)

60. Al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika fal<u>a</u> takun mina almumtareen**a**

60\. The Truth (comes)  
From God alone;  
So be not of those  
Who doubt.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_061.gif)

61. Faman <u>ha</u>jjaka feehi min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aka mina
alAAilmi faqul taAA<u>a</u>law nadAAu abn<u>a</u>an<u>a</u>
waabn<u>a</u>akum wanis<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> wanis<u>a</u>akum
waanfusan<u>a</u> waanfusakum thumma nabtahil fanajAAal laAAnata
All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>ath</u>ibeen**a**

61\. If any one disputes  
In this matter with thee,  
Now after (full) knowledge  
Hath come to thee,  
Say: "Come! let us  
Gather together,  
Our sons and your sons,  
Our women and your women,  
Ourselves and yourselves:  
Then let us earnestly pray,  
And invoke the curse  
Of God on those who lie!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_062.gif)

62. Inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa alqa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>u al<u>h</u>aqqu
wam<u>a</u> min il<u>a</u>hin ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wa-inna
All<u>a</u>ha lahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem**u**

62\. This is the true account:  
There is no god  
Except God;  
And God—He is indeed  
The Exalted in Power,  
The Wise.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_063.gif)

63. Fa-in tawallaw fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun
bi**a**lmufsideen**a**

63\. But if they turn back,  
God hath full knowledge  
Of those who do mischief.
