# Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.

### Section 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_067.gif)

67. Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo a-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u>
tur<u>a</u>ban wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> a-inn<u>a</u>
lamukhrajoon**a**

67\. The Unbelievers say: "What!  
When we become dust,  
We and our fathers,—shall we  
Really be raised (from the dead)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_068.gif)

68. Laqad wuAAidn<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> na<u>h</u>nu
wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> min qablu in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u>
as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleen**a**

68\. "It is true we were promised  
This,—we and our fathers  
Before (us): these are nothing  
But tales of the ancients."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_069.gif)

69. Qul seeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa**o**n*<u>th</u>*uroo kayfa
k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almujrimeen**a**

69\. Say: "Go ye through the earth  
And see what has been  
The end of those guilty  
(Of sin)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_070.gif)

70. Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> takun fee
<u>d</u>ayqin mimm<u>a</u> yamkuroon**a**

70\. But grieve not over them,  
Nor distress thyself  
Because of their plots.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_071.gif)

71. Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum
<u>sa</u>diqeen**a**

71\. They also say: "When will  
This promise (come to pass)?  
(Say) if ye are truthful."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_072.gif)

72. Qul AAas<u>a</u> an yakoona radifa lakum baAA<u>d</u>u
alla<u>th</u>ee tastaAAjiloon**a**

72\. Say: "It may be that  
Some of the events which  
Ye wish to hasten on  
May be (close) in your pursuit!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_073.gif)

73. Wa-inna rabbaka la<u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u>
a**l**nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yashkuroon**a**

73\. But verily thy Lord is  
Full of grace to mankind:  
Yet most of them are ungrateful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_074.gif)

74. Wa-inna rabbaka layaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tukinnu <u>s</u>udooruhum
wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoon**a**

74\. And verily thy Lord knoweth  
All that their hearts do hide,  
As well as all that  
They reveal.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_075.gif)

75. Wam<u>a</u> min gh<u>a</u>-ibatin fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin mubeen**in**

75\. Nor is there aught  
Of the Unseen, in heaven  
Or earth, but is (recorded)  
In a clear record.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_076.gif)

76. Inna h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na yaqu<u>ss</u>u AAal<u>a</u> banee
isr<u>a</u>-eela akthara alla<u>th</u>ee hum feehi yakhtalifoon**a**

76\. Verily this Qur-ān doth explain  
To the Children of Israel  
Most of the matters  
In which they disagree.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_077.gif)

77. Wa-innahu lahudan wara<u>h</u>matun lilmu/mineen**a**

77\. And it certainly is  
A Guide and a Mercy  
To those who believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_078.gif)

78. Inna rabbaka yaq<u>d</u>ee baynahum bi<u>h</u>ukmihi wahuwa
alAAazeezu alAAaleem**u**

78\. Verily thy Lord will decide  
Between them by His Decree:  
And He is Exalted in Might,  
All-Knowing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_079.gif)

79. Fatawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innaka AAal<u>a</u>
al<u>h</u>aqqi almubeen**i**

79\. So put thy trust in God:  
For thou art on (the Path  
Of) manifest Truth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_080.gif)

80. Innaka l<u>a</u> tusmiAAu almawt<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tusmiAAu
a**l**<u>ss</u>umma a**l**dduAA<u>a</u>a i<u>tha</u> wallaw
mudbireen**a**

80\. Truly thou canst not cause  
The Dead to listen, nor  
Canst thou cause the Deaf  
To hear the call,  
(Especially) when they  
Turn back in retreat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_081.gif)

81. Wam<u>a</u> anta bih<u>a</u>dee alAAumyi AAan
<u>d</u>al<u>a</u>latihim in tusmiAAu ill<u>a</u> man yu/minu
bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fahum muslimoon**a**

81\. Nor canst thou be a guide  
To the Blind, (to prevent them)  
From straying: only those  
Wilt thou get to listen  
Who believe in Our Signs,  
And they will bow in Islam.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/027/027_082.gif)

82. Wa-i<u>tha</u> waqaAAa alqawlu AAalayhim akhrajn<u>a</u> lahum
d<u>a</u>bbatan mina al-ar<u>d</u>i tukallimuhum anna a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa
k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yooqinoon**a**

82\. And when the Word is  
Fulfilled against them (the unjust),  
We shall produce from the earth  
A Beast to (face) them:  
He will speak to them,  
For that mankind did not  
Believe with assurance  
In Our Signs.
