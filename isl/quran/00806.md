# Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.

### Section 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_045.gif)

45. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u>
laqeetum fi-atan fa**o**thbutoo wa**o**<u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha
katheeran laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon**a**

45\. O ye who believe!  
When ye meet a force,  
Be firm, and call God  
In remembrance much (and often);  
That ye may prosper:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_046.gif)

46. Waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wal<u>a</u>
tan<u>a</u>zaAAoo fatafshaloo wata<u>th</u>haba ree<u>h</u>ukum
wa**i**<u>s</u>biroo inna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa
a**l**<u>ssa</u>bireen**a**

46\. And obey God and His Apostle;  
And fall into no disputes,  
Lest ye lose heart  
And your power depart;  
And be patient and persevering:  
For God is with those  
Who patiently persevere:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_047.gif)

47. Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka**a**lla<u>th</u>eena kharajoo min
diy<u>a</u>rihim ba<u>t</u>aran wari-<u>a</u>a a**l**nn<u>a</u>si
waya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu
bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>**un**

47\. And be not like those  
Who started from their homes  
Insolently and to be seen of men,  
And to hinder (men)  
From the path of God:  
For God compasseth round about  
All that they do.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/008/008_048.gif)

48. Wa-i<u>th</u> zayyana lahumu a**l**shshay<u>ta</u>nu
aAAm<u>a</u>lahum waq<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>liba lakumu alyawma
mina a**l**nn<u>a</u>si wa-innee j<u>a</u>run lakum falamm<u>a</u>
tar<u>a</u>ati alfi-at<u>a</u>ni naka<u>s</u>a AAal<u>a</u> AAaqibayhi
waq<u>a</u>la innee baree-on minkum innee ar<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>
tarawna innee akh<u>a</u>fu All<u>a</u>ha wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu shadeedu
alAAiq<u>a</u>b**i**

48\. Remember Satan made  
Their (sinful) acts seem  
Alluring to them, and said:  
"No one among men  
Can overcome you this day,  
While I am near to you":  
But when the two forces  
Came in sight of each other,  
He turned on his heels,  
And said: "Lo! I am clear  
Of you; to! I see  
What ye see not;  
Lo! I fear God; for God  
Is strict in punishment.
