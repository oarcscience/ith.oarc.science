# Sūra LI.: Zāriyāt, or the Winds That Scatter.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_047.gif)

47. Wa**al**ssam<u>a</u>a banayn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> bi-aydin
wa-inn<u>a</u> lamoosiAAoon**a**

47\. With power and skill  
Did We construct  
The Firmament:  
For it is We Who create  
The vastness of Space.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_048.gif)

48. Wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>a farashn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> faniAAma
alm<u>a</u>hidoon**a**

48\. And We have spread out  
The (spacious) earth:  
How excellently  
We do spread out!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_049.gif)

49. Wamin kulli shay-in khalaqn<u>a</u> zawjayni laAAallakum
ta<u>th</u>akkaroon**a**

49\. And of every thing  
We have created pairs  
That ye may receive  
Instruction.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_050.gif)

50. Fafirroo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innee lakum minhu na<u>th</u>eerun
mubeen**un**

50\. Hasten ye then (at once)  
To God: I am from Him  
A Warner to you,  
Clear and open!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_051.gif)

51. Wal<u>a</u> tajAAaloo maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han
<u>a</u>khara innee lakum minhu na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen**un**

51\. And make not another  
An object of worship  
With God:  
I am from Him  
A Warner to you,  
Clear and open!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_052.gif)

52. Ka<u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> at<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim
min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo s<u>ah</u>irun aw majnoon**un**

52\. Similarly, no apostle came  
To the Peoples before them,  
But they said (of him)  
In like manner,  
"A sorcerer, or  
One possessed"!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_053.gif)

53. Ataw<u>as</u>aw bihi bal hum qawmun <u>ta</u>ghoon**a**

53\. Is this the legacy  
They have transmitted,  
One to another  
Nay, they are themselves  
A people transgressing  
Beyond bounds!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_054.gif)

54. Fatawalla AAanhum fam<u>a</u> anta bimaloom**in**

54\. So turn away  
From them: not thine  
Is the blame.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_055.gif)

55. Wa<u>th</u>akkir fa-inna a**l**<u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u> tanfaAAu
almu/mineen**a**

55\. But teach (thy Message):  
For teaching benefits  
The Believers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_056.gif)

56. Wam<u>a</u> khalaqtu aljinna wa**a**l-insa ill<u>a</u>
liyaAAbudoon**i**

56\. I have only created  
Jinns and men, that  
They may serve Me.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_057.gif)

57. M<u>a</u> oreedu minhum min rizqin wam<u>a</u> oreedu an
yu<u>t</u>AAimoon**i**

57\. No Sustenance do I require  
Of them, nor do I  
Require that they should  
Feed Me.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_058.gif)

58. Inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa a**l**rrazz<u>a</u>qu <u>th</u>oo alquwwati
almateen**u**

58\. For God is He Who  
Gives (all) Sustenance,—  
Lord of Power,—  
Steadfast (for ever).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_059.gif)

59. Fa-inna lilla<u>th</u>eena *<u>th</u>*alamoo <u>th</u>anooban mithla
<u>th</u>anoobi a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bihim fal<u>a</u> yastaAAjiloon**a**

59\. For the wrong-doers,  
Their portion is like  
Unto the portion of their  
Fellows (of earlier generations):  
Then let them not ask Me  
To hasten (that portion)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/051/051_060.gif)

60. Fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min yawmihimu alla<u>th</u>ee
yooAAadoon**a**

60\. Woe, then, to the Unbelievers,  
On account of that Day  
Of theirs which they  
Have been promised!
