# Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.

### Section 7

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_068.gif)

68. Y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>di l<u>a</u> khawfun AAalaykumu alyawma
wal<u>a</u> antum ta<u>h</u>zanoon**a**

68\. My devotees  
No fear shall be  
On you that Day,  
Nor shall ye grieve,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_069.gif)

69. Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u>
wak<u>a</u>noo muslimeen**a**

69\. (Being) those who have believed  
In Our Signs and bowed  
(Their wills to Ours) in Islam.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_070.gif)

70. Odkhuloo aljannata antum waazw<u>a</u>jukum tu<u>h</u>baroon**a**

70\. Enter ye the Garden,  
Ye and your wives,  
In (beauty and) rejoicing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_071.gif)

71. Yu<u>ta</u>fu AAalayhim bi<u>s</u>i<u>ha</u>fin min <u>th</u>ahabin
waakw<u>a</u>bin wafeeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> tashtaheehi al-anfusu
watala<u>thth</u>u al-aAAyunu waantum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon**a**

71\. To them will be passed  
Round, dishes and goblets  
Of gold: there will be  
There all that the souls  
Could desire, all that  
The eyes could delight in:  
And ye shall abide  
Therein (for aye).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_072.gif)

72. Watilka aljannatu allatee oorithtumooh<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kuntum
taAAmaloon**a**

72\. Such will be the Garden  
Of which ye are made  
Heirs for your (good) deeds  
(In life).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_073.gif)

73. Lakum feeh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun katheeratun minh<u>a</u>
ta/kuloon**a**

73\. Ye shall have therein  
Abundance of fruit, from which  
Ye shall have satisfaction.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_074.gif)

74. Inna almujrimeena fee AAa<u>tha</u>bi jahannama
kh<u>a</u>lidoon**a**

74\. The Sinners will be  
In the Punishment of Hell,  
To dwell therein (for aye):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_075.gif)

75. L<u>a</u> yufattaru AAanhum wahum feehi mublisoon**a**

75\. Nowise will the (punishment)  
Be lightened for them,  
And in despair will they  
Be there overwhelmed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_076.gif)

76. Wam<u>a</u> *<u>th</u>*alamn<u>a</u>hum wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo
humu a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

76\. Nowise shall We  
Be unjust to them:  
But it is they who  
Have been unjust themselves.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_077.gif)

77. Wan<u>a</u>daw y<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>liku liyaq<u>d</u>i
AAalayn<u>a</u> rabbuka q<u>a</u>la innakum m<u>a</u>kithoon**a**

77\. They will cry: "O Mālik!  
Would that thy Lord  
Put an end to us!"  
He will say, "Nay, but  
Ye shall abide!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_078.gif)

78. Laqad ji/n<u>a</u>kum bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u>kinna
aktharakum lil<u>h</u>aqqi k<u>a</u>rihoon**a**

78\. Verily We have brought  
The Truth to you:  
But most of you  
Have a hatred for Truth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_079.gif)

79. Am abramoo amran fa-inn<u>a</u> mubrimoon**a**

79\. What! Have they settled  
Some Plan (among themselves)?  
But it is We Who  
Settle things.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_080.gif)

80. Am ya<u>h</u>saboona ann<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nasmaAAu sirrahum
wanajw<u>a</u>hum bal<u>a</u> warusulun<u>a</u> ladayhim yaktuboon**a**

80\. Or do they think  
That We hear not  
Their secrets and their  
Private counsels? Indeed  
(We do), and Our Messengers  
Are by them, to record.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_081.gif)

81. Qul in k<u>a</u>na li**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni waladun
faan<u>a</u> awwalu alAA<u>a</u>bideen**a**

81\. Say: If (God) Most Gracious  
Had a son, I would  
Be the first to worship."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_082.gif)

82. Sub<u>ha</u>na rabbi a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i rabbi alAAarshi AAamm<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ifoon**a**

82\. Glory to the Lord  
Of the heavens and the earth,  
The Lord of the Throne  
(Of Authority)! (He is  
Free) from the things  
They attribute (to Him)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_083.gif)

83. Fa<u>th</u>arhum yakhoo<u>d</u>oo wayalAAaboo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u>
yul<u>a</u>qoo yawmahumu alla<u>th</u>ee yooAAadoon**a**

83\. So leave them to babble  
And play (with vanities)  
Until they meet that Day,  
Of theirs, which they  
Have been promised.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_084.gif)

84. Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i il<u>a</u>hun wafee
al-ar<u>d</u>i il<u>a</u>hun wahuwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alAAaleem**u**

84\. It is He Who is God  
In heaven and God on earth;  
And He is Full  
Of Wisdom and Knowledge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_085.gif)

85. Watab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee lahu mulku
a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u>
baynahum<u>a</u> waAAindahu AAilmu a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAati wa-ilayhi
turjaAAoon**a**

85\. And blessed is He  
To Whom belongs the dominion  
Of the heavens and the earth,  
And all between them:  
With Him is the knowledge  
Of the Hour (of Judgment):  
And to Him shall ye  
Be brought back.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_086.gif)

86. Wal<u>a</u> yamliku alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona min doonihi
a**l**shshaf<u>a</u>AAata ill<u>a</u> man shahida bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi
wahum yaAAlamoon**a**

86\. And those whom they invoke  
Besides God have no power  
Of intercession;—only he  
Who bears witness to the Truth,  
And they know (him).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_087.gif)

87. Wala-in saaltahum man khalaqahum layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu
faann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon**a**

87\. If thou ask them, Who  
Created them, they will  
Certainly say, God: how  
Then are they deluded  
Away (from the Truth)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_088.gif)

88. Waqeelihi y<u>a</u> rabbi inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i qawmun
l<u>a</u> yu/minoon**a**

88\. (God has knowledge)  
Of the (Prophet's) cry,  
"O my Lord! Truly  
These are a people  
Who will not believe!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/043/043_089.gif)

89. Fa**i**<u>s</u>fa<u>h</u> AAanhum waqul sal<u>a</u>mun fasawfa
yaAAlamoon**a**

89\. But turn away from them,  
And say "Peace!"  
But soon shall they know!
