# Sūra XXXI.: Luqmān (the Wise).

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/031/031_031.gif)

31. Alam tara anna alfulka tajree fee alba<u>h</u>ri biniAAmati
All<u>a</u>hi liyuriyakum min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi inna fee
<u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin
shakoor**in**

31\. Seest thou not that  
The ships sail through  
The Ocean by the grace  
Of God?—that He may  
Show you of His Signs?  
Verily in this are Signs  
For all who constantly persevere  
And give thanks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/031/031_032.gif)

32. Wa-i<u>tha</u> ghashiyahum mawjun ka**al***<u>thth</u>*ulali
daAAawoo All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a**l**ddeena
falamm<u>a</u> najj<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> albarri faminhum
muqta<u>s</u>idun wam<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adu
bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> kullu khatt<u>a</u>rin
kafoor**in**

32\. When a wave covers them  
Like the canopy (of clouds),  
They call to God  
Offering Him sincere devotion.  
But when He has delivered them  
Safely to land, there are  
Among them those that halt  
Between (right and wrong).  
But none reject Our Signs  
Except only a perfidious  
Ungrateful (wretch)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/031/031_033.gif)

33. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>su ittaqoo rabbakum
wa**i**khshaw yawman l<u>a</u> yajzee w<u>a</u>lidun AAan waladihi
wal<u>a</u> mawloodun huwa j<u>a</u>zin AAan w<u>a</u>lidihi shay-an
inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun fal<u>a</u> taghurrannakumu
al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a**l**dduny<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> yaghurrannakum
bi**A**ll<u>a</u>hi algharoor**u**

33\. O mankind! do your duty  
To your Lord, and fear  
(The coming of) a Day  
When no father can avail  
Aught for his son, nor  
A son avail aught  
For his father.  
Verily, the promise of God  
Is true: let not then  
This present life deceive you,  
Nor let the Chief Deceiver  
Deceive you about God.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/031/031_034.gif)

34. Inna All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu AAilmu a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAati wayunazzilu
alghaytha wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>ha</u>mi wam<u>a</u> tadree
nafsun m<u>atha</u> taksibu ghadan wam<u>a</u> tadree nafsun bi-ayyi
ar<u>d</u>in tamootu inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun khabeer**un**

34.  
It is He Who sends down  
Rain, and He Who knows  
What is in the wombs.  
Nor does any one know  
What it is that he will  
Earn on the morrow:  
Nor does any one know  
In what land he is  
To die. Verily with God  
Is full knowledge and He  
Is acquainted (with all things)
