# Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_026.gif)

26. Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo l<u>a</u> tasmaAAoo
lih<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni wa**i**lghaw feehi laAAallakum
taghliboon**a**

26\. The Unbelievers say  
"Listen not to this Qur-ān,  
But talk at random  
In the midst  
Of its (reading), that ye  
May gain the upper hand!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_027.gif)

27. Falanu<u>th</u>eeqanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo AAa<u>tha</u>ban
shadeedan walanajziyannahum aswaa alla<u>th</u>ee k<u>a</u>noo
yaAAmaloon**a**

27\. But We will certainly  
Give the Unbelievers a taste  
Of a severe Penalty,  
And We will requite them  
For the worst of their deeds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_028.gif)

28. <u>Tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o aAAd<u>a</u>-i All<u>a</u>hi
a**l**nn<u>a</u>ru lahum feeh<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>ru alkhuldi jaz<u>a</u>an
bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u>
yaj<u>h</u>adoon**a**

28\. Such is the requital  
Of the enemies of God,—  
The Fire: therein will be  
For them the Eternal Home:  
A (fit) requital, for  
That they were wont  
To reject Our Signs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_029.gif)

29. Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo rabban<u>a</u> arin<u>a</u>
alla<u>th</u>ayni a<u>d</u>all<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> mina aljinni
wa**a**l-insi najAAalhum<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ta aqd<u>a</u>min<u>a</u>
liyakoon<u>a</u> mina al-asfaleen**a**

29\. And the Unbelievers will say:  
"Our Lord! Show us those,  
Among Jinns and men,  
Who misled us: we shall  
Crush them beneath our feet,  
So that they become  
The vilest (before all)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_030.gif)

30. Inna alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo rabbun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu
thumma istaq<u>a</u>moo tatanazzalu AAalayhimu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu
all<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>foo wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanoo waabshiroo
bi**a**ljannati allatee kuntum tooAAadoon**a**

30\. In the case of those  
Who say, "Our Lord  
Is God", and, further,  
Stand straight and steadfast,  
The angels descend on them.  
(From time to time):  
"Fear ye not!" (they suggest),  
"Nor grieve! But receive  
The Glad Tidings  
Of the Garden (of Bliss),  
The which ye were promised!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_031.gif)

31. Na<u>h</u>nu awliy<u>a</u>okum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti
a**l**dduny<u>a</u> wafee al-<u>a</u>khirati walakum feeh<u>a</u>
m<u>a</u> tashtahee anfusukum walakum feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>
taddaAAoon**a**

31\. "We are your protectors  
In this life and  
In the Hereafter:  
Therein shall ye have  
All that your souls  
Shall desire; therein  
Shall ye have all  
That ye ask for!—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/041/041_032.gif)

32. Nuzulan min ghafoorin ra<u>h</u>eem**in**

32\. "A hospitable gift from One  
Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful!"
