# Sūra LVI.: Wāqi‘a, or The Inevitable Event.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_039.gif)

39. Thullatun mina al-awwaleen**a**

39\. A (goodly) number  
From those of old,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_040.gif)

40. Wathullatun mina al-<u>a</u>khireen**a**

40\. And a (goodly) number  
From those of later times.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_041.gif)

41. Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a**l**shshim<u>a</u>li m<u>a</u>
a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a**l**shshim<u>a</u>l**i**

41\. The Companions of  
The Left Hand,—  
What will be  
The Companions of  
The Left Hand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_042.gif)

42. Fee samoomin wa<u>h</u>ameem**in**

42\. (They will be) in the midst  
Of a fierce Blast of Fire  
And in Boiling Water,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_043.gif)

43. Wa*<u>th</u>*illin min ya<u>h</u>moom**in**

43\. And in the shades  
Of Black Smoke:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_044.gif)

44. L<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>ridin wal<u>a</u> kareem**in**

44\. Nothing (will there be)  
To refresh, nor to please:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_045.gif)

45. Innahum k<u>a</u>noo qabla <u>tha</u>lika mutrafeen**a**

45\. For that they were wont  
To be indulged, before that,  
In wealth (and luxury),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_046.gif)

46. Wak<u>a</u>noo yu<u>s</u>irroona AAal<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>inthi
alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**i**

46\. And persisted obstinately  
In wickedness supreme!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_047.gif)

47. Wak<u>a</u>noo yaqooloona a-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u>
tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>man a-inn<u>a</u>
lamabAAoothoon**a**

47\. And they used to say,  
"What! when we die  
And become dust and bones,  
Shall we then indeed  
Be raised up again?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_048.gif)

48. Awa <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> al-awwaloon**a**

48\. "(We) and our fathers of old?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_049.gif)

49. Qul inna al-awwaleena wa**a**l-<u>a</u>khireen**a**

49\. Say: "Yea, those of old  
And those of later times,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_050.gif)

50. LamajmooAAoona il<u>a</u> meeq<u>a</u>ti yawmin maAAloom**in**

50\. "All will certainly be  
Gathered together for the meeting  
Appointed for a Day  
Well-known.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_051.gif)

51. Thumma innakum ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**<u>dda</u>lloona
almuka<u>thth</u>iboon**a**

51\. "Then will ye truly,—  
O ye that go wrong,  
And treat (Truth) as Falsehood!—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_052.gif)

52. La<u>a</u>kiloona min shajarin min zaqqoom**in**

52\. "Ye will surely taste  
Of the Tree of Zaqqūm.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_053.gif)

53. Fam<u>a</u>li-oona minh<u>a</u> albu<u>t</u>oon**a**

53\. "Then will ye fill  
Your insides therewith,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_054.gif)

54. Fash<u>a</u>riboona AAalayhi mina al<u>h</u>ameem**i**

54\. "And drink Boiling Water  
On top of it:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_055.gif)

55. Fash<u>a</u>riboona shurba alheem**i**

55\. "Indeed ye shall drink  
Like diseased camels  
Raging with thirst!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_056.gif)

56. H<u>atha</u> nuzuluhum yawma a**l**ddeen**i**

56\. Such will be their entertainment  
On the Day of Requital!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_057.gif)

57. Na<u>h</u>nu khalaqn<u>a</u>kum falawl<u>a</u>
tu<u>s</u>addiqoon**a**

57\. It is We Who have  
Created you: why will ye  
Not witness the Truth?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_058.gif)

58. Afaraaytum m<u>a</u> tumnoon**a**

58\. Do ye then see?—  
The (human Seed) that  
Ye throw out,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_059.gif)

59. Aantum takhluqoonahu am na<u>h</u>nu alkh<u>a</u>liqoon**a**

59\. Is it ye who create it,  
Or are We the Creators?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_060.gif)

60. Na<u>h</u>nu qaddarn<u>a</u> baynakumu almawta wam<u>a</u>
na<u>h</u>nu bimasbooqeen**a**

60\. We have decreed Death  
To be your common lot,  
And We are not  
To be frustrated

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_061.gif)

61. AAal<u>a</u> an nubaddila amth<u>a</u>lakum wanunshi-akum fee
m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon**a**

61\. From changing your Forms  
And creating you (again)  
In (Forms) that ye know not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_062.gif)

62. Walaqad AAalimtumu a**l**nnash-ata al-ool<u>a</u> falawl<u>a</u>
ta<u>th</u>akkaroon**a**

62\. And ye certainly know already  
The first form of creation:  
Why then do ye not  
Celebrate His praises?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_063.gif)

63. Afaraaytum m<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ruthoon**a**

63\. See ye the seed that  
Ye sow in the ground?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_064.gif)

64. Aantum tazraAAoonahu am na<u>h</u>nu a**l**zz<u>a</u>riAAoon**a**

64\. Is it ye that cause it  
To grow, or are We  
The Cause?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_065.gif)

65. Law nash<u>a</u>o lajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>u<u>ta</u>man
fa*<u>th</u>*altum tafakkahoon**a**

65\. Were it Our Will,  
We could crumble it  
To dry powder, and ye would  
Be left in wonderment,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_066.gif)

66. Inn<u>a</u> lamughramoon**a**

66\. (Saying), "We are indeed  
Left with debts (for nothing):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_067.gif)

67. Bal na<u>h</u>nu ma<u>h</u>roomoon**a**

67\. "Indeed are we shut out  
(Of the fruits of our labour)",

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_068.gif)

68. Afaraaytumu alm<u>a</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee tashraboon**a**

68\. See ye the water  
Which ye drink?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_069.gif)

69. Aantum anzaltumoohu mina almuzni am na<u>h</u>nu almunziloon**a**

69\. Do ye bring it Down  
(In rain) from the Cloud  
Or do We?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_070.gif)

70. Law nash<u>a</u>o jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu oj<u>a</u>jan falawl<u>a</u>
tashkuroon**a**

70\. Were it Our Will,  
We could make it  
Salt (and unpalatable):  
Then why do ye not  
Give thanks?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_071.gif)

71. Afaraaytumu a**l**nn<u>a</u>ra allatee tooroon**a**

71\. See ye the Fire  
Which ye kindle?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_072.gif)

72. Aantum ansha/tum shajaratah<u>a</u> am na<u>h</u>nu
almunshi-oon**a**

72\. Is it ye who grow  
The tree which feeds  
The fire, or do We  
Grow it?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_073.gif)

73. Na<u>h</u>nu jaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>kiratan
wamat<u>a</u>AAan lilmuqween**a**

73\. We have made it  
A memorial (of Our handiwork),  
And an article of comfort  
And convenience for  
The denizens of deserts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_074.gif)

74. Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi**i**smi rabbika alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**i**

74\. Then celebrate with praises,  
The name of thy Lord,  
The Supreme!
