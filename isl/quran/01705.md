# Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,

### Section 5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_041.gif)

41. Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni
liya<u>thth</u>akkaroo wam<u>a</u> yazeeduhum ill<u>a</u>
nufoor<u>a</u>**n**

41\. We have explained (things)  
In various (ways) in this Qur-ān,  
In order that they may receive  
Admonition, but it only increases  
Their flight (from the Truth)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_042.gif)

42. Qul law k<u>a</u>na maAAahu <u>a</u>lihatun kam<u>a</u> yaqooloona
i<u>th</u>an la**i**btaghaw il<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ee alAAarshi
sabeel<u>a</u>**n**

42\. Say: if there had been  
(Other) gods with Him,—  
As they say,—behold,  
They would certainly have  
Sought out a way  
To the Lord of the Throne!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_043.gif)

43. Sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yaqooloona
AAuluwwan kabeer<u>a</u>**n**

43\. Glory to Him! He is high  
Above all that they say!—  
Exalted and Great (beyond measure)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_044.gif)

44. Tusabbi<u>h</u>u lahu a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu a**l**ssabAAu
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>u waman feehinna wa-in min shay-in ill<u>a</u>
yusabbi<u>h</u>u bi<u>h</u>amdihi wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tafqahoona
tasbee<u>h</u>ahum innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>aleeman
ghafoor<u>a</u>**n**

44\. The seven heavens and the earth,  
And all beings therein,  
Declare His glory:  
There is not a thing  
But celebrates His praise;  
And yet ye understand not  
How they declare His glory!  
Verily He is Oft-Forbearing,  
Most Forgiving!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_045.gif)

45. Wa-i<u>tha</u> qara/ta alqur-<u>a</u>na jaAAaln<u>a</u> baynaka
wabayna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi**a**l-<u>a</u>khirati
<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ban mastoor<u>a</u>**n**

45\. When thou dost recite  
The Qur-ān, We put,  
Between thee and those who  
Believe not to the Hereafter,  
A veil invisible:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_046.gif)

46. WajaAAaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim akinnatan an yafqahoohu
wafee <u>atha</u>nihim waqran wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>akarta rabbaka fee
alqur-<u>a</u>ni wa<u>h</u>dahu wallaw AAal<u>a</u> adb<u>a</u>rihim
nufoor<u>a</u>**n**

46\. And We put coverings  
Over their hearts (and minds)  
Lest they should understand  
The Our-ān, and deafness  
Into their ears: when thou  
Dost commemorate thy Lord—  
And Him alone—in the Qur-ān,  
They turn on their backs,  
Fleeing (from the Truth).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_047.gif)

47. Na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yastamiAAoona bihi i<u>th</u>
yastamiAAoona ilayka wa-i<u>th</u> hum najw<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> yaqoolu
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limoona in tattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> rajulan
mas<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u>**n**

47\. We know best why it is  
They listen, when they listen  
To thee; and when they  
Meet in private conference,  
Behold, the wicked say,  
"Ye follow none other than  
A man bewitched!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_048.gif)

48. On*<u>th</u>*ur kayfa <u>d</u>araboo laka al-amth<u>a</u>la
fa<u>d</u>alloo fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona sabeel<u>a</u>**n**

48\. See what similes they strike  
For thee: but they have gone  
Astray, and never can they  
Find a way.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_049.gif)

49. Waq<u>a</u>loo a-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAi*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>man
waruf<u>a</u>tan a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoona khalqan
jadeed<u>a</u>**n**

49\. They say: "What!  
When we are reduced  
To bones and dust,  
Should we really be raised up  
(To be) a new creation?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_050.gif)

50. Qul koonoo <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan aw <u>h</u>adeed<u>a</u>**n**

50\. Say: "(Nay!) be ye  
Stones or iron,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_051.gif)

51. Aw khalqan mimm<u>a</u> yakburu fee <u>s</u>udoorikum fasayaqooloona
man yuAAeedun<u>a</u> quli alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>arakum awwala
marratin fasayunghi<u>d</u>oona ilayka ruoosahum wayaqooloona
mat<u>a</u> huwa qul AAas<u>a</u> an yakoona qareeb<u>a</u>**n**

51\. "Or created matter  
Which, in your minds,  
Is hardest (to be raised up),  
(Yet shall ye be raised up)!"  
Then will they say:  
"Who will cause us  
To return?" Say: "He  
Who created you first!"  
Then will they wag  
Their heads towards thee,  
And say, "When will  
That be?" Say, "May be  
It will be quite soon!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/017/017_052.gif)

52. Yawma yadAAookum fatastajeeboona bi<u>h</u>amdihi
wata*<u>th</u>*unnoona in labithtum ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u>**n**

52\. "It will be on a Day  
When He will call you,  
And ye will answer  
(His call) with (words  
Of) His praise, and ye  
Will think that ye tarried  
But a little while!
