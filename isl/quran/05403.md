# Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_041.gif)

41. Walaqad j<u>a</u>a <u>a</u>la firAAawna a**l**nnu<u>th</u>ur**u**

41\. To the People  
Of Pharaoh, too, aforetime,  
Came Warners (from God).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_042.gif)

42. Ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> kullih<u>a</u>
faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum akh<u>th</u>a AAazeezin muqtadir**in**

42\. The (people) rejected all  
Our Signs; but We  
Seized them with such Penalty  
(As comes) from One  
Exalted in Power,  
Able to carry out His Will.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_043.gif)

43. Akuff<u>a</u>rukum khayrun min ol<u>a</u>-ikum am lakum
bar<u>a</u>atun fee a**l**zzubur**i**

43\. Are your Unbelievers,  
(O Quraish), better than they?  
Or have ye an immunity  
In the Sacred Books?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_044.gif)

44. Am yaqooloona na<u>h</u>nu jameeAAun munta<u>s</u>ir**un**

44\. Or do they say:  
"We acting together  
Can defend ourselves"?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_045.gif)

45. Sayuhzamu aljamAAu wayuwalloona a**l**ddubur**a**

45\. Soon will their multitude  
Be put to flight,  
And they will show  
Their backs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_046.gif)

46. Bali a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAatu mawAAiduhum wa**al**ss<u>a</u>AAatu
adh<u>a</u> waamar**ru**

46\. Nay, the Hour (of Judgment)  
Is the time promised them  
(For their full recompense):  
And that Hour will be  
Most grievous and most bitter.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_047.gif)

47. Inna almujrimeena fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin wasuAAur**in**

47\. Truly those in sin  
Are the ones  
Straying in mind, and mad.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_048.gif)

48. Yawma yus<u>h</u>aboona fee a**l**nn<u>a</u>ri AAal<u>a</u>
wujoohihim <u>th</u>ooqoo massa saqar**a**

48\. The Day they will be  
Dragged through the Fire  
On their faces, (they  
Will hear:) "Taste ye  
The touch of Hell!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_049.gif)

49. Inn<u>a</u> kulla shay-in khalaqn<u>a</u>hu biqadar**in**

49\. Verily, all things  
Have We created  
In proportion and measure.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_050.gif)

50. Wam<u>a</u> amrun<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> w<u>ah</u>idatun
kalam<u>h</u>in bi**a**lba<u>s</u>ar**i**

50\. And Our Command  
Is but a single (Act),—  
Like the twinkling  
Of an eye.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_051.gif)

51. Walaqad ahlakn<u>a</u> ashy<u>a</u>AAakum fahal min muddakir**in**

51\. And (oft) in the past,  
Have We destroyed gangs  
Like unto you: then  
Is there any that  
Will receive admonition?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_052.gif)

52. Wakullu shay-in faAAaloohu fee a**l**zzubur**i**

52\. All that they do  
Is noted in (their)  
Books (of Deeds):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_053.gif)

53. Wakullu <u>s</u>agheerin wakabeerin musta<u>t</u>ar**un**

53\. Every matter, small and great,  
Is on record.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_054.gif)

54. Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin wanahar**in**

54\. As to the Righteous,  
They will be in the midst  
Of Gardens and Rivers,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_055.gif)

55. Fee maqAAadi <u>s</u>idqin AAinda maleekin muqtadir**in**

55\. In an Assembly of Truth,  
In the Presence of  
A Sovereign Omnipotent.
