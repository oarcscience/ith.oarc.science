# Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.

### Section 1

![](../gif/028/028_001.gif)

1. <u>Ta</u>-seen-meem

1\. Tā. Sīn. Mīm.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_002.gif)

2. Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi almubeen**i**

2\. These are Verses of the Book  
That makes (things) clear.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_003.gif)

3. Natloo AAalayka min naba-i moos<u>a</u> wafirAAawna
bi**a**l<u>h</u>aqqi liqawmin yu/minoon**a**

3\. We rehearse to thee some  
Of the story of Moses  
And Pharaoh in Truth,  
For people who believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_004.gif)

4. Inna firAAawna AAal<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wajaAAala
ahlah<u>a</u> shiyaAAan yasta<u>d</u>AAifu <u>ta</u>-ifatan minhum
yu<u>th</u>abbi<u>h</u>u abn<u>a</u>ahum wayasta<u>h</u>yee
nis<u>a</u>ahum innahu k<u>a</u>na mina almufsideen**a**

4\. Truly Pharaoh elated himself  
In the land and broke up  
Its people into sections,  
Depressing a small group  
Among them: their sons he slew,  
But he kept alive their females:  
For he was indeed  
A maker of mischief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_005.gif)

5. Wanureedu an namunna AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena
istu<u>d</u>AAifoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wanajAAalahum a-immatan
wanajAAalahumu alw<u>a</u>ritheen**a**

5\. And We wished to be  
Gracious to those who were  
Being depressed in the land,  
To make them leaders (in faith)  
And make them heirs,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_006.gif)

6. Wanumakkina lahum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wanuriya firAAawna
wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na wajunoodahum<u>a</u> minhum m<u>a</u>
k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>hth</u>aroon**a**

6\. To establish a firm place  
For them in the land,  
And to show Pharaoh, Haman,  
And their hosts, at their hands,  
The very things against which  
They were taking precautions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_007.gif)

7. Waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ommi moos<u>a</u> an
ar<u>d</u>iAAeehi fa-i<u>tha</u> khifti AAalayhi faalqeehi fee alyammi
wal<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>fee wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zanee inn<u>a</u>
r<u>a</u>ddoohu ilayki waj<u>a</u>AAiloohu mina almursaleen**a**

7\. So We sent this inspiration  
To the mother of Moses:  
"Suckle (thy child), but when  
Thou hast fears about him,  
Cast him into the river,  
But fear not nor grieve:  
For We shall restore him  
To thee, and We shall make  
Him one of Our apostles."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_008.gif)

8. Fa**i**ltaqa<u>t</u>ahu <u>a</u>lu firAAawna liyakoona lahum
AAaduwwan wa<u>h</u>azanan inna firAAawna wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na
wajunoodahum<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo kh<u>at</u>i-een**a**

8\. Then the people of Pharaoh  
Picked him up (from the river):  
(It was intended) that (Moses)  
Should be to them an adversary  
And a cause of sorrow:  
For Pharaoh and Haman  
And (all) their hosts were  
Men of sin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_009.gif)

9. Waq<u>a</u>lati imraatu firAAawna qurratu AAaynin lee walaka
l<u>a</u> taqtuloohu AAas<u>a</u> an yanfaAAan<u>a</u> aw
nattakhi<u>th</u>ahu waladan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon**a**

9\. The wife of Pharaoh said:  
"(Here is) a joy of the eye,'  
For me and for thee:  
Slay him not. It may be  
That he will be of use  
To us, or we may adopt  
Him as a son." And they  
Perceived not (what they  
Were doing)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_010.gif)

10. Waa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a fu-<u>a</u>du ommi moos<u>a</u>
f<u>a</u>righan in k<u>a</u>dat latubdee bihi lawl<u>a</u> an
raba<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qalbih<u>a</u> litakoona mina
almu/mineen**a**

10\. But there came to be  
A void in the heart  
Of the mother of Moses:  
She was going almost to  
Disclose his (case), had We  
Not strengthened her heart  
(With faith), so that she  
Might remain a (firm) believer.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_011.gif)

11. Waq<u>a</u>lat li-okhtihi qu<u>ss</u>eehi faba<u>s</u>urat bihi AAan
junubin wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon**a**

11\. And she said to the sister  
Of (Moses), "Follow him".  
So she (the sister) watched him  
In the character of a stranger.  
And they knew not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_012.gif)

12. Wa<u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> AAalayhi almar<u>ad</u>iAAa min qablu
faq<u>a</u>lat hal adullukum AAal<u>a</u> ahli baytin yakfuloonahu lakum
wahum lahu n<u>as</u>i<u>h</u>oon**a**

12\. And We ordained that he  
Refused suck at first, until  
(His sister came up  
And) said: "Shall I  
Point out to you the people  
Of a house that will nourish  
And bring him up for you'"  
And be sincerely attached  
To him?"...

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/028/028_013.gif)

13. Faradadn<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> ommihi kay taqarra AAaynuh<u>a</u>
wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zana walitaAAlama anna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi
<u>h</u>aqqun wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon**a**

13\. Thus did We restore him  
To his mother, that her eye  
Might be comforted, that she  
Might not grieve, and that  
She might know that the promise  
Of God is true: but  
Most of them do not understand.
