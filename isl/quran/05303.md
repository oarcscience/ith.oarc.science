# Sūra LIII.: Najm, or the Star.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_033.gif)

33. Afaraayta alla<u>th</u>ee tawall<u>a</u>

33\. Seest thou one  
Who turns back,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_034.gif)

34. WaaAA<u>ta</u> qaleelan waakd<u>a</u>

34\. Gives a little,  
Then hardens (his heart)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_035.gif)

35. aAAindahu AAilmu alghaybi fahuwa yar<u>a</u>

35\. What! Has he knowledge  
Of the unseen  
So that he can see?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_036.gif)

36. Am lam yunabba/ bim<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>u<u>h</u>ufi moos<u>a</u>

36\. Nay, is he not acquainted  
With what is in the books  
Of Moses—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_037.gif)

37. Wa-ibr<u>a</u>heema alla<u>th</u>ee waff<u>a</u>

37\. And of Abraham  
Who fulfilled his  
engagements;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_038.gif)

38. All<u>a</u> taziru w<u>a</u>ziratun wizra okhr<u>a</u>

38\. Namely, that no bearer  
Of burdens can bear  
The burden of another;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_039.gif)

39. Waan laysa lil-ins<u>a</u>ni ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> saAA<u>a</u>

39\. That man can have nothing  
But what he strives for;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_040.gif)

40. Waanna saAAyahu sawfa yur<u>a</u>

40\. That (the fruit of) his striving  
Will soon come in sight;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_041.gif)

41. Thumma yujz<u>a</u>hu aljaz<u>a</u>a al-awf<u>a</u>

41\. Then will he be rewarded  
With a reward complete;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_042.gif)

42. Waanna il<u>a</u> rabbika almuntah<u>a</u>

42\. That to thy Lord  
Is the final Goal;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_043.gif)

43. Waannahu huwa a<u>dh</u>aka waabk<u>a</u>

43\. That it is He Who  
Granteth Laughter and Tears;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_044.gif)

44. Waannahu huwa am<u>a</u>ta waa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>

44\. That it is He Who  
Granteth Death and Life;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_045.gif)

45. Waannahu khalaqa a**l**zzawjayni a**l**<u>thth</u>akara
wa**a**l-onth<u>a</u>

45\. That He did create  
In pairs,—male and female,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_046.gif)

46. Min nu<u>t</u>fatin i<u>tha</u> tumn<u>a</u>

46\. From a seed when lodged  
(In its place);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_047.gif)

47. Waanna AAalayhi a**l**nnash-ata al-okhr<u>a</u>

47\. That He hath promised  
A Second Creation  
(Raising of the Dead);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_048.gif)

48. Waannahu huwa aghn<u>a</u> waaqn<u>a</u>

48\. That it is He Who  
Giveth wealth and satisfaction;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_049.gif)

49. Waannahu huwa rabbu a**l**shshiAAr<u>a</u>

49\. That He is the Lord  
Of Sirius (the Mighty Star);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_050.gif)

50. Waannahu ahlaka AA<u>a</u>dan al-ool<u>a</u>

50\. And that it is He  
Who destroyed the (powerful)  
Ancient ‘Ād (people),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_051.gif)

51. Wathamooda fam<u>a</u> abq<u>a</u>

51\. And the <u>Th</u>amūd,  
Nor gave them a lease  
Of perpetual life.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_052.gif)

52. Waqawma noo<u>h</u>in min qablu innahum k<u>a</u>noo hum
a*<u>th</u>*lama waa<u>t</u>gh<u>a</u>

52\. And before them,  
The people of Noah,  
For that they were (all)  
Most unjust  
And most insolent transgressors,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_053.gif)

53. Wa**a**lmu/tafikata ahw<u>a</u>

53\. And He destroyed  
The Overthrown Cities  
(Of Sodom and Gomorrah),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_054.gif)

54. Faghashsh<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ghashsh<u>a</u>

54\. So that (ruins unknown)  
Have covered them up.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_055.gif)

55. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbika tatam<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>

55\. Then which of the gifts  
Of thy Lord, (O man,)  
Wilt thou dispute about?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_056.gif)

56. H<u>atha</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mina a**l**nnu<u>th</u>uri
al-ool<u>a</u>

56\. This is a Warner,  
Of the (series of) Warners  
Of old!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_057.gif)

57. Azifati al-<u>a</u>zifat**u**

57\. The (Judgment) ever approaching  
Draws nigh:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_058.gif)

58. Laysa lah<u>a</u> min dooni All<u>a</u>hi k<u>a</u>shifat**un**

58\. No (soul) but God  
Can lay it bare.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_059.gif)

59. Afamin h<u>atha</u> al<u>h</u>adeethi taAAjaboon**a**

59\. Do ye then wonder  
At this recital?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_060.gif)

60. Wata<u>dh</u>akoona wal<u>a</u> tabkoon**a**

60\. And will ye laugh  
And not weep,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_061.gif)

61. Waantum s<u>a</u>midoon**a**

61\. Wasting your time  
In vanities?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/053/053_062.gif)

62. Fa**o**sjudoo lill<u>a</u>hi wa**o**AAbudoo

62\. But fall ye down in prostration  
To God, and adore (Him)!
