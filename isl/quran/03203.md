# Sūra XXXII: Sajda, or Adoration.

### Section 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_023.gif)

23. Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba
fal<u>a</u> takun fee miryatin min liq<u>a</u>-ihi wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu
hudan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eel**a**

23\. We did indeed aforetime  
Give the Book to Moses:  
Be not then in doubt  
Of its reaching (thee):  
And We made it  
A guide to the Children  
Of Israel.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_024.gif)

24. WajaAAaln<u>a</u> minhum a-immatan yahdoona bi-amrin<u>a</u>
lamm<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo wak<u>a</u>noo
bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yooqinoon**a**

24\. And We appointed, from among  
Them, Leaders, giving guidance  
Under Our command, so long  
As they persevered with patience  
And continued to have faith  
In Our Signs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_025.gif)

25. Inna rabbaka huwa yaf<u>s</u>ilu baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati
feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon**a**

25\. Verily thy Lord will judge  
Between them on the Day  
Of Judgment, in the matters  
Wherein they differ  
(among themselves)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_026.gif)

26. Awa lam yahdi lahum kam ahlakn<u>a</u> min qablihim mina alqurooni
yamshoona fee mas<u>a</u>kinihim inna fee <u>tha</u>lika
la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin afal<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon**a**

26\. Does it not teach them  
A lesson, how many generations  
We destroyed before them,  
In whose dwellings they  
(Now) go to and fro?  
Verily in that are Signs:  
Do they not then listen?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_027.gif)

27. Awa lam yaraw ann<u>a</u> nasooqu alm<u>a</u>a il<u>a</u>
al-ar<u>d</u>i aljuruzi fanukhriju bihi zarAAan ta-kulu minhu
anAA<u>a</u>muhum waanfusuhum afal<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon**a**

27\. And do they not see  
That We do drive Rain  
To parched soil (bare  
Of herbage), and produce therewith  
Crops, providing food  
For their cattle and themselves?  
Have they not the vision?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_028.gif)

28. Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alfat<u>h</u>u in kuntum
<u>sa</u>diqeen**a**

28\. They say: "When will  
This Decision be, if ye  
Are telling the truth?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_029.gif)

29. Qul yawma alfat<u>h</u>i l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu alla<u>th</u>eena
kafaroo eem<u>a</u>nuhum wal<u>a</u> hum yun*<u>th</u>*aroon**a**

29\. Say: "On the Day  
Of Decision, no profit  
Will it he to Unbelievers  
If they (then) believe!  
Nor Will they he granted  
A respite."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/032/032_030.gif)

30. FaaAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum wa**i**nta*<u>th</u>*ir innahum
munta*<u>th</u>*iroon**a**

30\. So turn away from them,  
And wait: they too  
Are waiting.
