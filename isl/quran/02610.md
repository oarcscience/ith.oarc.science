# Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.

### Section 10

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_176.gif)

176. Ka<u>thth</u>aba a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aykati almursaleen**a**

176\. The Companions of the Wood  
Rejected the apostles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_177.gif)

177. I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum shuAAaybun al<u>a</u> tattaqoon**a**

177\. Behold, Shu‘aib said to them:  
Will ye not fear (God)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_178.gif)

178. Innee lakum rasoolun ameen**un**

178\. "I am to you an apostle  
Worthy of all trust.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_179.gif)

179. Fa**i**ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon**i**

179\. "So fear God and obey me.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_180.gif)

180. Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u>
AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

180\. "No reward do I ask  
Of you for it: my, reward  
Is only from the Lord  
Of the Worlds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_181.gif)

181. Awfoo alkayla wal<u>a</u> takoonoo mina almukhsireen**a**

181\. "Give just measure,''  
And cause no loss  
(To others by fraud).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_182.gif)

182. Wazinoo bi**a**lqis<u>ta</u>si almustaqeem**i**

182\. "And weigh with scales  
True and upright.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_183.gif)

183. Wal<u>a</u> tabkhasoo a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa ashy<u>a</u>ahum
wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen**a**

183\. "And withhold not things  
Justly due to men,  
Nor do evil in the land,  
Working mischief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_184.gif)

184. Wa**i**ttaqoo alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum wa**a**ljibillata
al-awwaleen**a**

184\. "And fear Him Who created  
You and (Who created)  
The generations before (you)"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_185.gif)

185. Q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> anta mina almusa<u>hh</u>areen**a**

185\. Whey said: "Thou art only  
One of those bewitched!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_186.gif)

186. Wam<u>a</u> anta ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> wa-in
na*<u>th</u>*unnuka lamina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen**a**

186\. "Thou art no more than  
A mortal like us,  
And indeed we think  
Thou art a liar!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_187.gif)

187. Faasqi<u>t</u> AAalayn<u>a</u> kisafan mina a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i in
kunta mina a**l**<u>ssa</u>diqeen**a**

187\. "Now cause a piece  
Of the sky to fall on us,  
If thou art truthful!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_188.gif)

188. Q<u>a</u>la rabbee aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon**a**

188\. He said: "My Lord  
Knows best what ye do."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_189.gif)

189. Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faakha<u>th</u>ahum AAa<u>tha</u>bu yawmi
a**l***<u>thth</u>*ullati innahu k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin
AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**in**

189\. But they rejected him.  
Then the punishment  
Of a day of overshadowing gloom  
Seized them, and that was  
The Penalty of a Great Day.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_190.gif)

190. Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na
aktharuhum mu/mineen**a**

190\. Verily in that is a Sign:  
But most of them  
Do not believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_191.gif)

191. Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**u**

191\. And verily thy Lord  
Is He, the Exalted in Might,  
Most Merciful.
