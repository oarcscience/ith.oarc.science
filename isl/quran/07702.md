# Sūra LXXVII.: Mursalāt, or Those Sent Forth.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_041.gif)

41. Inna almuttaqeena fee *<u>th</u>*il<u>a</u>lin waAAuyoon**in**

41\. As to the Righteous,  
They shall be amidst  
(Cool) shades and springs  
(Of water),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_042.gif)

42. Wafaw<u>a</u>kiha mimm<u>a</u> yashtahoon**a**

42\. And (they shall have)  
Fruits,—all they desire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_043.gif)

43. Kuloo wa**i**shraboo hanee-an bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon**a**

43\. "Eat ye and drink ye  
To your heart's content:  
For that ye worked  
(Righteousness).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_044.gif)

44. Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen**a**

44\. Thus do We certainly  
Reward the Doers of Good.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_045.gif)

45. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

45\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_046.gif)

46. Kuloo watamattaAAoo qaleelan innakum mujrimoon**a**

46\. (O ye Unjust!)  
Eat ye and enjoy yourselves  
(But) a little while,  
For that ye are Sinners.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_047.gif)

47. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

47\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_048.gif)

48. Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu irkaAAoo l<u>a</u> yarkaAAoon**a**

48\. And when it is said  
To them, "Prostrate yourselves!"  
They do not so.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_049.gif)

49. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

49\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_050.gif)

50. Fabi-ayyi <u>h</u>adeethin baAAdahu yu/minoon**a**

50\. Then what Message,  
After that,  
Will they believe in?
