# Sūra LXXXIV.: Inshiqāq, or The Rending Asunder.

### Section 1

![](../gif/084/084_001.gif)

1. I<u>tha</u> a**l**ssam<u>a</u>o inshaqqat

1\. When the Sky is  
Rent asunder,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_002.gif)

2. Waa<u>th</u>inat lirabbih<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>uqqat

2\. And hearkens to  
(The Command of) its Lord,—  
And it must needs  
(Do so);—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_003.gif)

3. Wa-i<u>tha</u> al-ar<u>d</u>u muddat

3\. And when the Earth  
Is flattened out,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_004.gif)

4. Waalqat m<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> watakhallat

4\. And casts forth  
What is within it  
And becomes (clean) empty,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_005.gif)

5. Waa<u>th</u>inat lirabbih<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>uqqat

5\. And hearkens to  
(The Command of) its Lord,—  
And it must needs  
(Do so);—(then will come  
Home the full Reality)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_006.gif)

6. Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu innaka k<u>a</u>di<u>h</u>un
il<u>a</u> rabbika kad<u>h</u>an famul<u>a</u>qeeh**i**

6\. O thou man!  
Verily thou art ever  
Toiling on towards thy Lord—  
Painfully toiling,—but thou  
Shalt meet Him.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_007.gif)

7. Faam<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu biyameenih**i**

7\. Then he who is given  
His Record in his  
Right hand,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_008.gif)

8. Fasawfa yu<u>ha</u>sabu <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>ban yaseer<u>a</u>**n**

8\. Soon will his account  
Be taken by an easy reckoning,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_009.gif)

9. Wayanqalibu il<u>a</u> ahlihi masroor<u>a</u>**n**

9\. And he will turn  
To his people, rejoicing!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_010.gif)

10. Waamm<u>a</u> man ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu war<u>a</u>a
*<u>th</u>*ahrih**i**

10\. But he who is given  
His Record behind his back,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_011.gif)

11. Fasawfa yadAAoo thuboor<u>a</u>**n**

11\. Soon will he cry  
For Perdition,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_012.gif)

12. Waya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> saAAeer<u>a</u>**n**

12\. And he will enter  
A Blazing Fire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_013.gif)

13. Innahu k<u>a</u>na fee ahlihi masroor<u>a</u>**n**

13\. Truly, did he go about  
Among his people, rejoicing!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_014.gif)

14. Innahu *<u>th</u>*anna an lan ya<u>h</u>oor**a**

14\. Truly, did he think  
That he would not  
Have to return (to Us)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_015.gif)

15. Bal<u>a</u> inna rabbahu k<u>a</u>na bihi ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u>**n**

15\. Nay, nay! for his Lord  
Was (ever) watchful of him!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_016.gif)

16. Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu bi**al**shshafaq**i**

16\. So I do call  
To witness the ruddy glow  
Of Sunset;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_017.gif)

17. Wa**a**llayli wam<u>a</u> wasaq**a**

17\. The Night and its Homing;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_018.gif)

18. Wa**a**lqamari i<u>tha</u> ittasaq**a**

18\. And the Moon  
In her Fulness:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_019.gif)

19. Latarkabunna <u>t</u>abaqan AAan <u>t</u>abaq**in**

19\. Ye shall surely travel  
From stage to stage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_020.gif)

20. Fam<u>a</u> lahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon**a**

20\. What then is the matter  
With them, that they  
Believe not?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_021.gif)

21. Wa-i<u>tha</u> quri-a AAalayhimu alqur-<u>a</u>nu l<u>a</u>
yasjudoon**a**

21\. And when the Qur-ān  
Is read to them, they  
Fall not prostrate,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_022.gif)

22. Bali alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yuka<u>thth</u>iboon**a**

22\. But on the contrary  
The Unbelievers reject (it).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_023.gif)

23. Wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yooAAoon**a**

23\. But God has full Knowledge  
Of what they secrete  
(In their breasts).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_024.gif)

24. Fabashshirhum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem**in**

24\. So announce to them  
A Penalty Grievous,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/084/084_025.gif)

25. Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo
a**l**<u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum ajrun ghayru mamnoon**in**

25\. Except to those who believe  
And work righteous deeds:  
For them is a Reward  
That will never fail.
