# Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_036.gif)

36. Waoo<u>h</u>iya il<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>in annahu lan yu/mina min
qawmika ill<u>a</u> man qad <u>a</u>mana fal<u>a</u> tabta-is
bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafAAaloon**a**

36\. It was revealed to Noah:  
"None of thy People will believe  
Except those who have believed  
Already! So grieve no longer  
Over their (evil) deeds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_037.gif)

37. Wa**i**<u>s</u>naAAi alfulka bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u>
wawa<u>h</u>yin<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tukh<u>at</u>ibnee fee
alla<u>th</u>eena *<u>th</u>*alamoo innahum mughraqoon**a**

37\. "But construct an Ark  
Under Our eyes and Our  
Inspiration, and address Me  
No (further) on behalf  
Of those who are in sin:  
For they are about to be  
Overwhelmed (in the Flood)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_038.gif)

38. Waya<u>s</u>naAAu alfulka wakullam<u>a</u> marra AAalayhi malaon min
qawmihi sakhiroo minhu q<u>a</u>la in taskharoo minn<u>a</u>
fa-inn<u>a</u> naskharu minkum kam<u>a</u> taskharoon**a**

38\. Forthwith he (starts)  
Constructing the Ark:  
Every time that the Chiefs  
Of his People passed by him,  
They threw ridicule on him.  
He said: "If ye ridicule  
Us now, we (in our turn)  
Can look down on you  
With ridicule likewise!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_039.gif)

39. Fasawfa taAAlamoona man ya/teehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun yukhzeehi
waya<u>h</u>illu AAalayhi AAa<u>tha</u>bun muqeem**un**

39\. "But soon will ye know  
Who it is on whom  
Will descend a Penalty  
That will cover them  
With shame,—on whom will be  
Unloosed a Penalty lasting:"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_040.gif)

40. <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u>
waf<u>a</u>ra a**l**ttannooru quln<u>a</u> i<u>h</u>mil feeh<u>a</u> min
kullin zawjayni ithnayni waahlaka ill<u>a</u> man sabaqa AAalayhi
alqawlu waman <u>a</u>mana wam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mana maAAahu ill<u>a</u>
qaleel**un**

40\. At length, behold!  
There came Our Command,  
And the fountains of the earth  
Gushed forth! We said:  
"Embark therein, of each kind  
Two, male and female,  
And your family—except  
Those against whom the Word  
Has already gone forth,—  
And the Believers."  
But only a few  
Believed with him.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_041.gif)

41. Waq<u>a</u>la irkaboo feeh<u>a</u> bismi All<u>a</u>hi
majr<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wamurs<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> inna rabbee laghafoorun
ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

41\. So he said: "Embark ye  
On the Ark,  
In the name of God,  
Whether it move  
Or be at rest!  
For my Lord is, be sure,  
Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_042.gif)

42. Wahiya tajree bihim fee mawjin ka**a**ljib<u>a</u>li
wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>unu ibnahu wak<u>a</u>na fee maAAzilin
y<u>a</u> bunayya irkab maAAan<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> takun maAAa
alk<u>a</u>fireen**a**

42\. So the Ark floated  
With them on the waves  
(Towering) like mountains,  
And Noah called out  
To his son, who had  
Separated himself (from the rest):  
"O my son! embark  
With us, and be not  
With the Unbelievers!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_043.gif)

43. Q<u>a</u>la sa<u>a</u>wee il<u>a</u> jabalin yaAA<u>s</u>imunee mina
alm<u>a</u>-i q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> AA<u>as</u>ima alyawma min amri
All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> man ra<u>h</u>ima wa<u>ha</u>la
baynahum<u>a</u> almawju fak<u>a</u>na mina almughraqeen**a**

43\. The son replied: "I will  
Betake myself to some mountain:  
It will save me from  
The water." Noah said:  
"This day nothing can save,  
From the Command of God,  
Any but those on whom  
He hath mercy!"—  
And the waves came  
Between them, and the son  
Was among those  
Overwhelmed in the Flood.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_044.gif)

44. Waqeela y<u>a</u> ar<u>d</u>u iblaAAee m<u>a</u>aki way<u>a</u>
sam<u>a</u>o aqliAAee waghee<u>d</u>a alm<u>a</u>o waqu<u>d</u>iya
al-amru wa**i**stawat AAal<u>a</u> aljoodiyyi waqeela buAAdan lilqawmi
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeen**a**

44\. Then the word went forth:  
"O earth! swallow up  
Thy water, and O sky!  
Withhold (thy rain)!"  
And the water abated,  
And the matter was ended.  
The Ark rested on Mount  
Jūdī, and the word  
Went forth: "Away  
With those who do wrong!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_045.gif)

45. Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>un rabbahu faq<u>a</u>la rabbi inna
ibnee min ahlee wa-inna waAAdaka al<u>h</u>aqqu waanta a<u>h</u>kamu
al<u>ha</u>kimeen**a**

45\. And Noah called upon  
His Lord, and said:  
"O my Lord! surely  
My son is of my family!  
And Thy promise is true,  
And Thou art  
The Justest of Judges!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_046.gif)

46. Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u innahu laysa min ahlika innahu
AAamalun ghayru <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>in fal<u>a</u> tas-alni m<u>a</u>
laysa laka bihi AAilmun innee aAAi*<u>th</u>*uka an takoona mina
alj<u>a</u>hileen**a**

46\. He said: "O Noah!  
He is not of thy family:  
For his conduct is unrighteous.  
So ask not of Me  
That of which thou  
Hast no knowledge!  
I give thee counsel, lest  
Thou act like the ignorant!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_047.gif)

47. Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee aAAoo<u>th</u>u bika an as-alaka m<u>a</u>
laysa lee bihi AAilmun wa-ill<u>a</u> taghfir lee watar<u>h</u>amnee
akun mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen**a**

47\. Noah said: "O my Lord!  
I do seek refuge with Thee,  
Lest I ask Thee for that  
Of which I have no knowledge.  
And unless Thou forgive me  
And have Mercy on me,  
I should indeed be lost!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_048.gif)

48. Qeela y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u ihbi<u>t</u> bisal<u>a</u>min
minn<u>a</u> wabarak<u>a</u>tin AAalayka waAAal<u>a</u> omamin mimman
maAAaka waomamun sanumattiAAuhum thumma yamassuhum minn<u>a</u>
AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem**un**

48\. The word came: "O Noah!  
Come down (from the Ark)  
With Peace from Us,  
And Blessing on thee  
And on some of the Peoples  
(Who will spring) from those  
With thee: but (there will be  
Other) Peoples to whom We  
Shall grant their pleasures  
(For a time), but in the end  
Will a grievous Penalty  
Reach them from Us."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/011/011_049.gif)

49. Tilka min anb<u>a</u>-i alghaybi noo<u>h</u>eeh<u>a</u> ilayka
m<u>a</u> kunta taAAlamuh<u>a</u> anta wal<u>a</u> qawmuka min qabli
h<u>atha</u> fa**i**<u>s</u>bir inna alAA<u>a</u>qibata
lilmuttaqeen**a**

49\. Such are some of the stories  
Of the Unseen, which We  
Have revealed unto thee:  
Before this, neither thou  
Nor thy People knew them.  
So persevere patiently:  
For the End is for those  
Who are righteous.
