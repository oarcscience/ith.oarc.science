# Sūra LXXI.: Nūḥ, or Noah.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_021.gif)

21. Q<u>a</u>la noo<u>h</u>un rabbi innahum AAa<u>s</u>awnee
wa**i**ttabaAAoo man lam yazidhu m<u>a</u>luhu wawaladuhu ill<u>a</u>
khas<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

21\. Noah said: "O my Lord!  
They have disobeyed me,  
But they follow (men)  
Whose wealth and children  
Give them no Increase  
But only Loss.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_022.gif)

22. Wamakaroo makran kubb<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

22\. "And they have devised  
A tremendous Plot.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_023.gif)

23. Waq<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>arunna <u>a</u>lihatakum
wal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>arunna waddan wal<u>a</u> suw<u>a</u>AAan
wal<u>a</u> yaghootha wayaAAooqa wanasr<u>a</u>**n**

23\. "And they have said  
(To each other),  
"Abandon not your gods:  
Abandon neither Wadd  
Nor Suwā‘, neither  
Yagūth nor Ya‘ūq,  
Nor Nasr’;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_024.gif)

24. Waqad a<u>d</u>alloo katheeran wal<u>a</u> tazidi
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeena ill<u>a</u>
<u>d</u>al<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>**n**

24\. "They have already  
Misled many; and  
Grant Thou no increase  
To the wrong-doers but in  
Straying (from their mark)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_025.gif)

25. Mimm<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>ee-<u>a</u>tihim oghriqoo faodkhiloo
n<u>a</u>ran falam yajidoo lahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi
an<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

25\. Because of their sins  
They were drowned  
(In the flood),  
And were made to enter  
The Fire (of Punishment):  
And they found  
In lieu of God  
None to help them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_026.gif)

26. Waq<u>a</u>la noo<u>h</u>un rabbi l<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>ar
AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i mina alk<u>a</u>fireena
dayy<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

26\. And Noah said:  
"O my Lord! Leave not  
Of the Unbelievers,  
A single one on earth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_027.gif)

27. Innaka in ta<u>th</u>arhum yu<u>d</u>illoo AAib<u>a</u>daka
wal<u>a</u> yalidoo ill<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>jiran
kaff<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

27\. "For, if Thou dost leave  
(Any of) them, they will  
But mislead Thy devotees,  
And they will breed none  
But wicked ungrateful ones.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/071/071_028.gif)

28. Rabbi ighfir lee waliw<u>a</u>lidayya waliman dakhala baytiya
mu/minan walilmu/mineena wa**a**lmu/min<u>a</u>ti wal<u>a</u> tazidi
a**l***<u>thth</u>*<u>a</u>limeena ill<u>a</u> tab<u>a</u>ran

28\. "O my Lord! Forgive me,  
My parents, all who  
Enter my house in Faith,  
And (all) believing men  
And believing women:  
And to the wrong-doers  
Grant Thou no increase  
But in Perdition!"
