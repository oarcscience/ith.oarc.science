# Sūra LXXVIII.: Nabaa, or The (Great) News.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_031.gif)

31. Inna lilmuttaqeena maf<u>a</u>z<u>a</u>**n**

31\. Verily for the Righteous  
There will be  
A fulfilment of  
(The Heart's) desires;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_032.gif)

32. <u>H</u>ad<u>a</u>-iqa waaAAn<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

32\. Gardens enclosed, and Grapevines;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_033.gif)

33. Wakaw<u>a</u>AAiba atr<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

33\. Companions of Equal age;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_034.gif)

34. Waka/san dih<u>a</u>q<u>a</u>**n**

34\. And a Cup full  
(To the brim).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_035.gif)

35. L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> laghwan wal<u>a</u>
ki<u>ththa</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

35\. No Vanity shall they hear  
Therein, nor Untruth;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_036.gif)

36. Jaz<u>a</u>an min rabbika AAa<u>ta</u>an
<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

36\. Recompense from thy Lord,  
A Gift, (amply) sufficient,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_037.gif)

37. Rabbi a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u>
baynahum<u>a</u> a**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni l<u>a</u> yamlikoona minhu
khi<u>ta</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

37\. (From the Lord  
Of the heavens  
And the earth, and all between,—  
(God) Most Gracious:  
None shall have power  
To argue with Him.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_038.gif)

38. Yawma yaqoomu a**l**rroo<u>h</u>u wa**a**lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu
<u>s</u>affan l<u>a</u> yatakallamoona ill<u>a</u> man a<u>th</u>ina
lahu a**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu waq<u>a</u>la
<u>s</u>aw<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

38\. The Day that  
The Spirit and the angels  
Will stand forth in ranks,  
None shall speak  
Except any who is  
Permitted by (God) Most Gracious,  
And he will say  
What is right.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_039.gif)

39. <u>Tha</u>lika alyawmu al<u>h</u>aqqu faman sh<u>a</u>a
ittakha<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u> rabbihi ma<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

39\. that day will be  
The sure Reality:  
Therefore, whoso will let him  
Take a (straight) Return  
To his Lord!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/078/078_040.gif)

40. Inn<u>a</u> an<u>th</u>arn<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>ban qareeban
yawma yan*<u>th</u>*uru almaro m<u>a</u> qaddamat yad<u>a</u>hu
wayaqoolu alk<u>a</u>firu y<u>a</u> laytanee kuntu
tur<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

40\. Verily, we have warned you  
Of a Penalty near,—  
The Day when man will  
See (the Deeds) which  
His hands have sent forth,  
And the Unbeliever will say,  
"Woe unto me! Would that  
I were mere dust!"
