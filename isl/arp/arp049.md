#### ON A MISER.

##### BY THE SAME.

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

HANG her—a thoughtless, wasteful fool,  
  She scatters corn where’er she goes!"  
Quoth Hassan, angry at his mule,  
  That dropped a dinner to the crows.
