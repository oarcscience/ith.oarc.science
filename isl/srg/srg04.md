# ACKNOWLEDGMENT

I wish to acknowledge my indebtedness to Professor E. H. Whinfield,
whose book, the standard translation of the *Gulshan i Rāz*, has been of
the greatest assistance to me in compiling this little volume. I am
grateful to Dr. R. A. Nicholson for kind permission to quote from his
works on Sūfiism, and to Captain L. Cranmer-Byng I offer my best thanks
for his kind interest in my work, and for many helpful hints and
suggestions.
