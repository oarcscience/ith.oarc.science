This short collection of Islamic wisdom literature was originally
published in the [Wisdom of the East](../../woe/index.md) Series. It
features gems from the Quran, the Hadith, and traditional proverbs and
aphorisms. This is principally meant as an enjoyable read; unfortunately
the sayings are unattributed, which makes it less useful as a scholarly
work.
