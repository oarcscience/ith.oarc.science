### THE CHAPTER OF MISBELIEVERS.

### (CIX. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.  
Say, 'O ye misbelievers! I do not serve what ye serve; nor will ye serve
what I serve; nor will I serve what ye serve; \[5\] nor will ye serve
what I serve;--ye have your religion, and I have my religion!'
