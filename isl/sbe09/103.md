### THE CHAPTER OF THE AFTERNOON <span id="fr_499"></span>[4](#fn_499).

### (CIII. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.

By the afternoon! verily, man is in loss! save

<span id="page_341">p. 341</span>

those who believe and do right, and bid each other be true, and bid each
other be patient.
