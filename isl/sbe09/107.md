### THE CHAPTER OF 'NECESSARIES.'

### (CVII. Place of origin doubtful.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.  
Hast thou considered him who calls the judgment a lie? He it is who
pushes the orphan away; and urges not (others) to feed the poor.  
But we to those who pray \[5\] and who are careless in their prayers,  
Who pretend and withhold necessaries <span
id="fr_503"></span>[2](#fn_503).
