### THE CHAPTER OF UNITY <span id="fr_508"></span>[2](#fn_508.md).

### (CXII. Place of origin doubtful.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.  
Say, 'He is God alone!  
God the Eternal!  
He begets not and is not begotten!  
Nor is there like unto Him any one!'
