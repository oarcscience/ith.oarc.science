p. 342

THE CHAPTER OF THE QURÂIS.

(CVI. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.
For the uniting of the Qurâis; uniting them for the caravan of winter and summer.
So let them serve the Lord of this house who feeds them against hunger and makes them safe against fear 1.
