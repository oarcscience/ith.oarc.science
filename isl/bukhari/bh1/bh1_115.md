### Hadith 1:115

*Narrated Um Salama:*

One night Allah's Apostle got up and said, "Subhan Allah! How many
afflictions have been descended tonight and how many treasures have been
disclosed! Go and wake the sleeping lady occupants of these dwellings
(his wives) up (for prayers). A well-dressed (soul) in this world may be
naked in the Hereafter. "
