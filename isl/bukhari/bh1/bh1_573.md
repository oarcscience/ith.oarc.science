### Hadith 1:571

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet said, "If anyone forgets a prayer he should pray that prayer
when he remembers it. There is no expiation except to pray the same."
Then he recited: "Establish prayer for My (i.e. Allah's) remembrance."
(20.14).
