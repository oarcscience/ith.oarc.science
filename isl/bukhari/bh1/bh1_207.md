### Hadith 1:207

*Narrated Ja'far bin 'Amr bin Umaiya:*

My father said, "I saw Allah's Apostle taking a piece of (cooked) mutton
from the shoulder region and then he was called for prayer. He put his
knife down and prayed without repeating ablution."
