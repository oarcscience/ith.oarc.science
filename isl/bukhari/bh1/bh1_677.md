### Hadith 1:675

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abi Qatada:*

My father said, "The Prophet said, 'When I stand for prayer, I intend to
prolong it but on hearing the cries of a child, I cut it short, as I
dislike to trouble the child's mother.' "
