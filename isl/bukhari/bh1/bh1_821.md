### Hadith 1:819

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

My grandmother Mulaika invited Allah's Apostle for a meal which she had
prepared specially for him. He ate some of it and said, "Get up. I shall
lead you in the prayer." I brought a mat that had become black owing to
excessive use and I sprinkled water on it. Allah's Apostle stood on it
and prayed two Rakat; and the orphan was with me (in the first row), and
the old lady stood behind us.
