### Hadith 1:649

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet did not come out for three days. The people stood for the
prayer and Abu Bakr went ahead to lead the prayer. (In the meantime) the
Prophet caught hold of the curtain and lifted it. When the face of the
Prophet appeared we had never seen a scene more pleasing than the face
of the Prophet as it appeared then. The Prophet beckoned to Abu Bakr to
lead the people in the prayer and then let the curtain fall. We did not
see him (again) till he died.
