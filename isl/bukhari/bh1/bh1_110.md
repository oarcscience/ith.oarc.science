### Hadith 1:110

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Name yourselves with my name (use my name) but do not
name yourselves with my Kunya name (i.e. Abu-l Qasim). And whoever sees
me in a dream then surely he has seen me for Satan cannot impersonate
me. And whoever tells a lie against me (intentionally), then (surely)
let him occupy his seat in Hell-fire."
