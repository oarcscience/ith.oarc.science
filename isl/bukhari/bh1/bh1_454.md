### Hadith 1:452

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

On the day of Al-Khandaq (battle of the Trench' the medial arm vein of
Sa'd bin Mu'ad was injured and the Prophet pitched a tent in the mosque
to look after him. There was another tent for Banu Ghaffar in the mosque
and the blood started flowing from Sa'd's tent to the tent of Bani
Ghaffar. They shouted, "O occupants of the tent! What is coming from you
to us?" They found that Sa'd' wound was bleeding profusely and Sa'd died
in his tent.
