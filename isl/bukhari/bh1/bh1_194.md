### Hadith 1:194

*Narrated Anas:*

It was the time for prayer, and those whose houses were near got up and
went to their people (to perform ablution), and there remained some
people (sitting). Then a painted stove pot (Mikhdab) containing water
was brought to Allah's Apostles The pot was small, not broad enough for
one to spread one's hand in; yet all the people performed ablution. (The
sub narrator said, "We asked Anas, 'How many persons were you?' Anas
replied 'We were eighty or more"). (It was one of the miracles of
Allah's Apostle).
