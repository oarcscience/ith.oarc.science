### Hadith 1:799

*Narrated Um Salama:*

Whenever Allah's Apostle finished his prayers with Taslim, the women
would get up and he would stay on for a while in his place before
getting up. Ibn Shihab said, "I think (and Allah knows better), that the
purpose of his stay was that the women might leave before the men who
had finished their prayer. "
