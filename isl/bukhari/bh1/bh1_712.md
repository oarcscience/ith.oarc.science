### Hadith 1:710

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet, Abu Bakr and 'Umar used to start the prayer with "Al-hamdu
lil-lahi Rabbil-'ala-min (All praises are for Allah the Lord of the
Worlds)."
