### Hadith 1:703

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

I saw that whenever Allah's Apostle stood for the prayer, he used to
raise both his hands up to the shoulders, and used to do the same on
saying the Takbir for bowing and on raising his head from it and used to
say, "Sami a-l-lahu Liman hamida". But he did not do that (i.e. raising
his hands) in prostrations.
