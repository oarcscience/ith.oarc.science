### Hadith 1:145

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Once the Prophet entered a lavatory and I placed water for his ablution.
He asked, "Who placed it?" He was informed accordingly and so he said,
"O Allah! Make him (Ibn 'Abbas) a learned scholar in religion (Islam)."
