### Hadith 1:747

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Say Amin" when the Imam says it and if the Amin of
any one of you coincides with that of the angels then all his past sins
will be forgiven." Ibn Shihab said, "Allah's Apostle used to Say "Amin."
