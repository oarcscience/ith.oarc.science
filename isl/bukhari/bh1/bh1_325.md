### Hadith 1:325

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

(the wife of the Prophet) I told Allah's Apostle that Safiya bint Huyai
had got her menses. He said, "She will probably delay us. Did she
perform Tawaf (Al-Ifada) with you?" We replied, "Yes." On that the
Prophet told her to depart.
