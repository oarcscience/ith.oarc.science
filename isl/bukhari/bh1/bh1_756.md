### Hadith 1:754

*Narrated 'Ikrima:*

I saw a person praying at Muqam-lbrahim (the place of Abraham by the
Ka'ba) and he was saying Takbir on every bowing, rising, standing and
sitting. I asked Ibn 'Abbas (about this prayer). He admonished me
saying: "Isn't that the prayer of the Prophet?"
