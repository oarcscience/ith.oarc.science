### Hadith 1:599

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Allah's Apostle used to pray two light Rakat before the morning
(compulsory) prayer after the day dawned and the Mu'adhdhin had finished
his Adhan. He then would lie on his right side till the Mu'adhdhin came
to pronounce the Iqama.
