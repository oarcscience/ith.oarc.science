### Hadith 1:36

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said: "Whoever establishes prayers during the nights of
Ramadan faithfully out of sincere faith and hoping to attain Allah's
rewards (not for showing off), all his past sins will be forgiven."
