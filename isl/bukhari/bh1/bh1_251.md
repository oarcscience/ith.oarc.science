### Hadith 1:251

*Narrated Abu Salama:*

'Aisha's brother and I went to 'Aisha and he asked her about the bath of
the Prophet. She brought a pot containing about a Sa' of water and took
a bath and poured it over her head and at what time there was a screen
between her and us.
