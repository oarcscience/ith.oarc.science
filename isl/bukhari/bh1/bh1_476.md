### Hadith 1:474

*Narrated 'Aun bin Abi Juhaifa:*

I heard my father saying, "The Prophet led us, and prayed a two-Rak'at
Zuhr prayer and then a two-Rak'at 'Asr prayer at Al-Batha' with an 'Anza
(planted) in front of him (as a Sutra) while women and donkeys were
passing in front of him (beyond that 'Anza)."
