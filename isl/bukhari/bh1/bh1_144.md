### Hadith 1:144

*Narrated Anas:*

Whenever the Prophet went to answer the call of nature, he used to say,
"Allah-umma inni a'udhu bika minal khubuthi wal khaba'ith i.e. O Allah,
I seek Refuge with You from all offensive and wicked things (evil deeds
and evil spirits)."
