### Hadith 1:492

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

the wife of the Prophet, "I used to sleep in front of Allah's Apostle
with my legs opposite his Qibla (facing him); and whenever he
prostrated, he pushed my feet and I withdrew them and whenever he stood,
I stretched them." 'Aisha added, "In those days there were no lamps in
the houses."
