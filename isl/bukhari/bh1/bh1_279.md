### Hadith 1:279

*Narrated Maimuna:*

I screened the Prophet while he was taking a bath of Janaba. He washed
his hands, poured water from his right hand over his left and washed his
private parts. Then he rubbed his hand over a wall or the earth, and
performed ablution similar to that for the prayer but did not wash his
feet. Then he poured water over his body, shifted from that place, and
washed his feet.
