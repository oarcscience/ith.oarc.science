### Hadith 1:564

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

By Allah, Who took away the Prophet. The Prophet never missed them (two
Rakat) after the 'Asr prayer till he met Allah and he did not meet Allah
till it became heavy for him to pray while standing so he used to offer
most of the prayers while sitting. (She meant the two Rakat after Asr)
He used to pray them in the house and never prayed them in the mosque
lest it might be hard for his followers and he loved what was easy for
them .
