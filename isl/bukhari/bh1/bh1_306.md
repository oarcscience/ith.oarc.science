### Hadith 1:306

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Once one of the wives of the Prophet did Itikaf along with him and she
was getting bleeding in between her periods. She used to see the blood
(from her private parts) and she would perhaps put a dish under her for
the blood. (The sub-narrator 'Ikrima added, 'Aisha once saw the liquid
of safflower and said, "It looks like what so and so used to have.")
