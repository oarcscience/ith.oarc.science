### Hadith 1:94

*Narrated Anas:*

Whenever the Prophet asked permission to enter, he knocked the door
thrice with greeting and whenever he spoke a sentence (said a thing) he
used to repeat it thrice. (See Hadith No. 261, Vol. 8).
