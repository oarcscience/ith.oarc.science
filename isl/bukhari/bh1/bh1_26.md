### Hadith 1:27

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Amr:*

A person asked Allah's Apostle . "What (sort of) deeds in or (what
qualities of) Islam are good?" He replied, "To feed (the poor) and greet
those whom you know and those whom you don't know."
