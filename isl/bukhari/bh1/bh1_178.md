### Hadith 1:178

*Narrated 'Ali:*

I used to get emotional urethral discharges frequently and felt shy to
ask Allah's Apostle about it. So I requested Al-Miqdad bin Al-Aswad to
ask (the Prophet ) about it. Al-Miqdad asked him and he replied, "On has
to perform ablution (after it)."
