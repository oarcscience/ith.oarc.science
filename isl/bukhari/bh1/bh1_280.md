### Hadith 1:280

*Narrated Um-Salama:*

(the mother of the believers) Um Sulaim, the wife of Abu Talha, came to
Allah's Apostle and said, "O Allah's Apostle! Verily Allah is not shy of
(telling you) the truth. Is it necessary for a woman to take a bath
after she has a wet dream (nocturnal sexual discharge)?" Allah's Apostle
replied, "Yes, if she notices a discharge."
