### Hadith 1:752

*Narrated Abu Salama:*

When Abu Huraira led us in prayer he used to say Takbir on each bowing
and rising. On the completion of the prayer he used to say, "My prayer
is more similar to the prayer of Allah's Apostle than that of anyone of
you."
