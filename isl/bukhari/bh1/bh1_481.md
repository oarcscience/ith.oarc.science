### Hadith 1:479

*Narrated Anas Ibn Malik:*

Whenever the Prophet went for answering the call of nature, I and
another boy used to go after him with a staff, a stick or an 'Anza and a
tumbler of water and when he finished from answering the call of nature
we would hand that tumbler of water to him.
