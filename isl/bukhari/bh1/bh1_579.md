### Hadith 1:577

*Narrated Anas:*

The people mentioned the fire and the bell (they suggested those as
signals to indicate the starting of prayers), and by that they mentioned
the Jews and the Christians. Then Bilal was ordered to pronounce Adhan
for the prayer by saying its wordings twice, and for the Iqama (the call
for the actual standing for the prayers in rows) by saying its wordings
once. (Iqama is pronounced when the people are ready for the prayer).
