### Hadith 1:658

*Narrated Al-Bara:*

(and he was not a liar) When Allah's Apostle said, "Sami a-l-lahu Liman
hamida " none of us bent his back (for prostrations) till the Prophet
prostrated and then we would prostrate after him.
