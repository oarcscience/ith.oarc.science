### Hadith 1:260

*Narrated Maimuna:*

The Prophet took the bath of Janaba. (sexual relation or wet dream). He
first cleaned his private parts with his hand, and then rubbed it(that
hand) on the wall (earth) and washed it. Then he performed ablution like
that for the prayer, and after the bath he washed his feet.
