### Hadith 1:337

*Narrated 'Abdur Rahman bin Abza:*

'Ammar said to 'Umar "I rolled myself in the dust and came to the
Prophet who said, 'Passing dusted hands over the face and the backs of
the hands is sufficient for you.' "
