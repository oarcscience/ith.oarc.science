### Hadith 1:54

*Narrated Jarir bin Abdullah:*

I gave the pledge of allegiance to Allah's Apostle for the following:

1\. offer prayers perfectly

2\. pay the Zakat (obligatory charity)

3\. and be sincere and true to every Muslim.
