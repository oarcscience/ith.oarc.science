### Hadith 1:272

*Narrated Hisham bin 'Urwa:*

(on the authority of his father) 'Aisha said, "Whenever Allah's Apostle
took the bath of Janaba, he cleaned his hands and performed ablution
like that for prayer and then took a bath and rubbed his hair, till he
felt that the whole skin of the head had become wet, then he would pour
water thrice and wash the rest of the body." 'Aisha further said, "I and
Allah's Apostle used to take a bath from a single water container, from
which we took water simultaneously."
