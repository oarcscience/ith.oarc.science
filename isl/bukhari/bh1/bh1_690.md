### Hadith 1:688

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Martyrs are those who die because of drowning,
plague, an abdominal disease, or of being buried alive by a falling
building." And then he added, "If the people knew the Reward for the
Zuhr prayer in its early time, they would race for it. If they knew the
reward for the 'Isha' and the Fajr prayers in congregation, they would
join them even if they had to crawl. If they knew the reward for the
first row, they would draw lots for it."
