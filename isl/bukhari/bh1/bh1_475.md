### Hadith 1:473

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

Whenever Allah's Apostle came out on 'Id day, he used to order that a
Harba (a short spear) to be planted in front of him (as a Sutra for his
prayer) and then he used to pray facing it with the people behind him
and used to do the same while on a journey. After the Prophet , this
practice was adopted by the Muslim rulers (who followed his traditions).
