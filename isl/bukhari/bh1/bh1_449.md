### Hadith 1:448

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

A black man or a black woman used to sweep the mosque and he or she
died. The Prophet asked about her (or him). He was told that she (or he)
had died. He said, "Why did you not inform me? Show me his grave (or her
grave)." So he went to her (his) grave and offered her (his) funeral
prayer."
