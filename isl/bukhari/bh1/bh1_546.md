### Hadith 1:544

*Narrated Ibn Shihab from 'Urwa:*

'Aisha said, "Once Allah's Apostle delayed the 'Isha' prayer till 'Umar
reminded him by saying, "The prayer!" The women and children have slept.
Then the Prophet came out and said, 'None amongst the dwellers of the
earth has been waiting for it (the prayer) except you." Urwa said,
"Nowhere except in Medina the prayer used to be offered (in those
days)." He further said, "The Prophet used to offer the 'Isha' prayer in
the period between the disappearance of the twilight and the end of the
first third of the night."
