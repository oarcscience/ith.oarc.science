### Hadith 1:608

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi Qatada:*

My father said, "While we were praying with the Prophet he heard the
noise of some people. After the prayer he said, 'What is the matter?'
They replied 'We were hurrying for the prayer.' He said, 'Do not make
haste for the prayer, and whenever you come for the prayer, you should
come with calmness, and pray whatever you get (with the people) and
complete the rest which you have missed."
