### Hadith 1:213

*Narrated 'Amr bin 'Amir:*

Anas said, "The Prophet used to perform ablution for every prayer." I
asked Anas, "What you used to do?' Anas replied, "We used to pray with
the same ablution until we break it with Hadath."
