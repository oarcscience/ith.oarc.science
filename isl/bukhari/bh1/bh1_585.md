### Hadith 1:583

*Narrated 'Abdul Rahman:*

Abu Sa'id Al-Khudri told my father, "I see you liking sheep and the
wilderness. So whenever you are with your sheep or in the wilderness and
you want to pronounce Adhan for the prayer raise your voice in doing so,
for whoever hears the Adhan, whether a human being, a jinn or any other
creature, will be a witness for you on the Day of Resurrection." Abu
Said added, "I heard it (this narration) from Allah's Apostle."
