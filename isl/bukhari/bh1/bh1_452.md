### Hadith 1:450

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

"The Prophet said, "Last night a big demon (afreet) from the Jinns came
to me and wanted to interrupt my prayers (or said something similar) but
Allah enabled me to overpower him. I wanted to fasten him to one of the
pillars of the mosque so that all of you could See him in the morning
but I remembered the statement of my brother Solomon (as stated in
Quran): My Lord! Forgive me and bestow on me a kingdom such as shall not
belong to anybody after me (38.35)." The sub narrator Rauh said, "He
(the demon) was dismissed humiliated."
