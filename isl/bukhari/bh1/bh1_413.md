### Hadith 1:412

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

Allah's Apostle ordered for a horse race; the trained horses were to run
from a place called Al-Hafya' to Thaniyat Al-Wada' and the horses which
were not trained were to run from Al-Thaniya to the Masjid (mosque of)
Bani Zuraiq. The sub narrator added: Ibn Umar was one of those who took
part in the race.
