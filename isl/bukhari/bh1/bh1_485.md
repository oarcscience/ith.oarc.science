### Hadith 1:483

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

The Prophet entered the Ka'ba along with Usama bin Zaid, 'Uthman bin
Talha and Bilal and remained there for a long time. When they came out,
I was the first man to enter the Ka'ba. I asked Bilal "Where did the
Prophet pray?" Bilal replied, "Between the two front Pillars."
