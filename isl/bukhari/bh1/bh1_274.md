### Hadith 1:274

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Once the call (Iqama) for the prayer was announced and the rows were
straightened. Allah's Apostle came out; and when he stood up at his
Musalla, he remembered that he was Junub. Then he ordered us to stay at
our places and went to take a bath and then returned with water dropping
from his head. He said, "Allahu-Akbar", and we all offered the prayer
with him.
