### Hadith 1:717

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet said, "What is wrong with those people who look towards the
sky during the prayer?" His talk grew stern while delivering this speech
and he said, "They should stop (looking towards the sky during the
prayer); otherwise their eye-sight would be taken away."
