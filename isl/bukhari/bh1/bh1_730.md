### Hadith 1:728

*Narrated Abu Ma'mar:*

I asked Khabbab bin Al-Art whether the Prophet used to recite the Qur'an
in the Zuhr and the 'Asr prayers. He replied in the affirmative. I said,
"How did you come to know that?" He replied, "From the movement of his
beard."
