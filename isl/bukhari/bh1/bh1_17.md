### Hadith 1:18

*Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri:*

Allah's Apostle said, "A time will come that the best property of a
Muslim will be sheep which he will take on the top of mountains and the
places of rainfall (valleys) so as to flee with his religion from
afflictions."
