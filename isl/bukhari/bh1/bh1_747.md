### Hadith 1:745

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi Qatada:*

My father said, "The Prophet used to recite Al-Fatiha along with another
Sura in the first two Rakat of the Zuhr and 'Asr prayers. A verse or so
was audible at times and he used to prolong the first Rak'a."
