### Hadith 1:828

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Had Allah's Apostle known what the women were doing, he would have
forbidden them from going to the mosque as the women of Bani Israel had
been forbidden. Yahya bin Said (a sub-narrator) asked 'Amra (another
sub-narrator), "Were the women of Bani Israel forbidden?" She replied
"Yes."
