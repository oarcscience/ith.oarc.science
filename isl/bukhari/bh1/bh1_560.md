### Hadith 1:558

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle forbade two kinds of sales, two kinds of dresses, and
two prayers. He forbade offering prayers after the Fajr prayer till the
rising of the sun and after the 'Asr prayer till its setting. He also
forbade "Ishtimal-Assama" and "al-Ihtiba" in one garment in such a way
that one's private parts are exposed towards the sky. He also forbade
the sales called "Munabadha" and "Mulamasa." (See Hadith No. 354 and 355
Vol. 3).
