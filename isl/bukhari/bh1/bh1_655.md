### Hadith 1:653

*Narrated Malik bin Huwairth:*

We went to the Prophet and we were all young men and stayed with him for
about twenty nights. The Prophet was very merciful. He said, "When you
return home, impart religious teachings to your families and tell them
to offer perfectly such and such a prayer at such and such a time and
such and such a prayer at such and such a time. And al the time of the
prayer one of you should pronounce the Adhan and the oldest of you
should lead the prayer."
