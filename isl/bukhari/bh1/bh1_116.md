### Hadith 1:116

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

Once the Prophet led us in the 'Isha' prayer during the last days of his
life and after finishing it (the prayer) (with Taslim) he said: "Do you
realize (the importance of) this night?" Nobody present on the surface
of the earth tonight will be living after the completion of one hundred
years from this night."
