### Hadith 1:82

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

Allah's Apostle said, "While I was sleeping, I saw that a cup full of
milk was brought to me and I drank my fill till I noticed (the milk) its
wetness coming out of my nails. Then I gave the remaining milk to 'Umar
Ibn Al-Khattab" The companions of the Prophet asked, "What have you
interpreted (about this dream)? "O Allah's Apostle ,!" he replied, "(It
is religious) knowledge."
