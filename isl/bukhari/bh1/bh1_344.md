### Hadith 1:344

*Narrated 'Imran bin Husain Al-Khuza'i:*

Allah's Apostle saw a person sitting aloof and not praying with the
people. He asked him, "O so and so! What prevented you from offering the
prayer with the people?" He replied, "O Allah's Apostle! I am Junub and
there is no water." The Prophet said, "Perform Tayammum with clean earth
and that will be sufficient for you."
