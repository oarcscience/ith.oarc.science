### Hadith 4:19

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The mother of Sad bin 'Ubada died in his absence. He said, "O Allah's
Apostle! My mother died in my absence; will it be of any benefit for her
if I give Sadaqa on her behalf?" The Prophet said, "Yes," Sad said, "I
make you a witness that I gave my garden called Al Makhraf in charity on
her behalf."
