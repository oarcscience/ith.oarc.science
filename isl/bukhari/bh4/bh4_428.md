### Hadith 4:424

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abbas:*

The Prophet said, "The sun and the moon are two signs amongst the Signs
of Allah. They do not eclipse because of someone's death or life. So, if
you see them (i.e. eclipse), celebrate the Praises of Allah (i.e.
pray)."
