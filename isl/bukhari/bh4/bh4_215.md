### Hadith 4:214

*Narrated 'Umar bin Al-Khattab:*

I gave a horse to be used in Allah's Cause, but later on I saw it being
sold. I asked the Prophet whether I could buy it. He said, "Don't buy it
and don't take back your gift of charity."
