### Hadith 4:77

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet used to say, "O Allah! I seek refuge with You from
helplessness, laziness, cowardice and feeble old age; I seek refuge with
You from afflictions of life and death and seek refuge with You from the
punishment in the grave."
