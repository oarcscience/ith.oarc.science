### Hadith 4:507

*Narrated Alqama:*

I went to Sham (and asked. "Who is here?"), The people said, "Abu
Ad-Darda." Abu Darda said, "Is the person whom Allah has protected
against Satan, (as Allah's Apostle said) amongst you". The sub-narrator,
Mughira said that the person who was given Allah's Refuge through the
tongue of the Prophet was 'Ammar (bin Yasir).
