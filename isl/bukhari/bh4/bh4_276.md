### Hadith 4:273

*Narrated Jarir:*

Allah's Apostle did not screen himself from me since my embracing Islam,
and whenever he saw me he would receive me with a smile. Once I told him
that I could not sit firm on horses. He stroke me on the chest with his
hand and said, "O Allah! Make him firm and make him a guiding and a
rightly-guided man."
