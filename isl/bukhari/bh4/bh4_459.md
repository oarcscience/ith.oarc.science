### Hadith 4:455

*Narrated Abu Ishaq-Ash-Shaibani:*

I asked Zir bin Hubaish regarding the Statement of Allah: "And was at a
distance Of but two bow-lengths Or (even) nearer; So did (Allah) convey
The Inspiration to His slave (Gabriel) and then he (Gabriel) Conveyed
(that to Muhammad). (53.9-10) On that, Zir said, "Ibn Mas'ud informed us
that the Prophet had seen Gabriel having 600 wings."
