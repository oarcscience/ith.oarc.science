### Hadith 4:632

*Narrated Mujahid:*

I asked Ibn 'Abbas, "Should we perform a prostration on reciting
Surat-Sad?" He recited (the Sura) including: 'And among his progeny,
David, Solomon..(up to)...so follow their guidance (6.84-91) And then he
said, "Your Prophet is amongst those people who have been ordered to
follow them (i.e. the preceding apostles).
