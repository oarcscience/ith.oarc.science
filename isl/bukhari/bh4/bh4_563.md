### Hadith 4:560

*Narrated Abdullah bin Zam'a:*

I heard the Prophet while referring to the person who had cut the legs
of the she-camel (of the Prophet Salih), saying, "The man who was
appointed for doing this job, was a man of honor and power in his nation
like Abu Zam'a,"
