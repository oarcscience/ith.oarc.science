### Hadith 4:443

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Allah's Apostle was the most generous of all the people, and he used to
be more generous in the month of Ramadan when Gabriel used to meet him.
Gabriel used to meet him every night in Ramadan to study the Holy Quran
carefully together. Allah's Apostle used to become more generous than
the fast wind when he met Gabriel
