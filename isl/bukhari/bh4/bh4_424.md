### Hadith 4:420

*Narrated Said bin Zaid bin Amr bin Nufail:*

That Arwa sued him before Marwan for a right, which she claimed, he had
deprived her of. On that Said said, "How should I deprive her of her
right? I testify that I heard Allah's Apostle saying, 'If anyone takes a
span of land unjustly, his neck will be encircled with it down seven
earths on the Day of Resurrection."
