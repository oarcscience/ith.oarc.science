### Hadith 4:170

*Narrated Anas:*

Abdur Rahman bin 'Auf and Az-Zubair complained to the Prophet, i.e.
about the lice (that caused itching) so he allowed them to wear silken
clothes. I saw them wearing such clothes in a holy battle.
