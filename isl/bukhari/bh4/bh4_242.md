### Hadith 4:240

*Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:*

On the day of the battle of the Trench, the Prophet wanted somebody from
amongst the people to volunteer to be a reconnoitre. Az-Zubair
volunteered. He demanded the same again and Az-Zubair volunteered again.
Then he repeated the same demand (thrice) and AzZubair volunteered once
more. The Prophet then said, " Every prophet has a disciple and my
disciple is Az-Zubair."
