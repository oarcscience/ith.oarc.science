### Hadith 4:472

*Narrated Al-Bara bin Azib:*

Allah's Apostle was given a silken garment, and its beauty and delicacy
astonished the people. On that, Allah's Apostle said, "No doubt, the
handkerchiefs of Sad bin Muadh in Paradise are better than this."
