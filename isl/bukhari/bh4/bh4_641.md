### Hadith 4:638

*Narrated 'Abdullah:*

When the Verse:-- 'Those who believe and mix not their belief with
wrong." was revealed, the companions of the Prophet said, "Who amongst
us has not mixed his belief with wrong?" Then Allah revealed: "Join none
in worship with Allah, Verily joining others in worship with Allah is a
great wrong indeed."
