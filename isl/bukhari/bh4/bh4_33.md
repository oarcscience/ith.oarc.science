### Hadith 4:34

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

Umar got some property in Khaibar and he came to the Prophet and
informed him about it. The Prophet said to him, "If you wish you can
give it in charity." So 'Umar gave it in charity (i.e. as an endowment)
the yield of which was to be used for the good of the poor, the needy,
the kinsmen and the guests.
