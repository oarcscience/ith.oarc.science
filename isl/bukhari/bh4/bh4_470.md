### Hadith 4:466

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Qais Al-Ashari:*

The Prophet said, "A tent (in Paradise) is like a hollow pearl which is
thirty miles in height and on every corner of the tent the believer
Imran in another narration, "(The tent is) sixty miles (in height)")
