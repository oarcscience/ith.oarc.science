### Hadith 4:438

*Narrated Aisha:*

Al Harith bin Hisham asked the Prophet, "How does the divine inspiration
come to you?" He replied, "In all these ways: The Angel sometimes comes
to me with a voice which resembles the sound of a ringing bell, and when
this state abandons me, I remember what the Angel has said, and this
type of Divine Inspiration is the hardest on me; and sometimes the Angel
comes to me in the shape of a man and talks to me, and I understand and
remember what he says."
