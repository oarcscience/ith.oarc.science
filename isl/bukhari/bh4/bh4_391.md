### Hadith 4:387

*Narrated Abu Humaid As-Saidi:*

We accompanied the Prophet in the Ghazwa of Tabuk and the king of 'Aila
presented a white mule and a cloak as a gift to the Prophet. And the
Prophet wrote to him a peace treaty allowing him to keep authority over
his country.
