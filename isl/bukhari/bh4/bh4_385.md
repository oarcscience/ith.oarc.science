### Hadith 4:381

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Mughaffal:*

While we were besieging the fort of Khaibar, a person threw a leather
container containing fat, and I ran to take it, but when I turned I saw
the Prophet (standing behind), so I felt embarrassed in front of him.
