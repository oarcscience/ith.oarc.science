### Hadith 4:426

*Narrated Abu Mas'ud:*

The Prophet said, "the sun and the moon do not eclipse because of the
death or life of someone, but they are two signs amongst the Signs of
Allah. So, if you see them, offer the Prayer (of eclipse)."
