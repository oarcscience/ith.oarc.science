### Hadith 4:42

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Allah's Apostle said, "There is no Hijra (i.e. migration) (from Mecca to
Medina) after the Conquest (of Mecca), but Jihad and good intention
remain; and if you are called (by the Muslim ruler) for fighting, go
forth immediately.
