### Hadith 4:306

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Al-Hasan bin 'All took a date from the dates of the Sadaqa and put it in
his mouth. The Prophet said (to him) in Persian, "Kakh, kakh! (i.e.
Don't you know that we do not eat the Sadaqa (i.e. what is given in
charity) (charity is the dirt of the people))."
