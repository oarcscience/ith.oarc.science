### Hadith 4:399

*Narrated ' Abdullah bin 'Abbas:*

That Abu Sufyan bin Harb Informed him that Heraclius called him and the
members of a caravan from Quraish who had gone to Sham as traders,
during the truce which Allah's Apostle had concluded with Abu Sufyan and
the Quraish infidels.
