### Hadith 4:37

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "My heirs will not inherit a Dinar or a Dirham
(i.e. money), for whatever I leave (excluding the adequate support of my
wives and the wages of my employees) is given in charity."
