### Hadith 4:150

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

While some Ethiopians were playing in the presence of the Prophet, 'Umar
came in, picked up a stone and hit them with it. On that the Prophet
said, "O 'Umar! Allow them (to play)." Ma'mar (the sub-narrator) added
that they were playing in the Mosque.
