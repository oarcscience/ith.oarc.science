### Hadith 4:311

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The Prophet said, on the day of the Conquest of Mecca, "There is no
migration (after the Conquest), but Jihad and good intentions, and when
you are called for Jihad, you should immediately respond to the call."
