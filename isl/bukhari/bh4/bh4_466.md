### Hadith 4:462

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

The Prophet said, "On the night of my Ascent to the Heaven, I saw Moses
who was a tall brown curly-haired man as if he was one of the men of
Shan'awa tribe, and I saw Jesus, a man of medium height and moderate
complexion inclined to the red and white colors and of lank hair. I also
saw Malik, the gate-keeper of the (Hell) Fire and Ad-Dajjal amongst the
signs which Allah showed me." (The Prophet then recited the Holy Verse):
"So be not you in doubt of meeting him' when you met Moses during the
night of Mi'raj over the heavens" (32.23)

Medina from Ad-Dajjal (who will not be able to enter the city of
Medina)."
