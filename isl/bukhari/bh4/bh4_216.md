### Hadith 4:215

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

'Umar gave a horse to be used in Allah's Cause, but later on he found it
being sold. So, he intended to buy it and asked Allah's Apostle who
said, "Don't buy it and don't take back your gift of charity."
