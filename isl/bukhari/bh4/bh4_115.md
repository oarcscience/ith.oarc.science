### Hadith 4:114

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

There was a feeling of fright in Medina, so the Prophet borrowed a horse
called Mandub belonging 'to Abu Talha and mounted it. (On his return),
he said, "I did not see anything of fright and I found this horse very
fast."
