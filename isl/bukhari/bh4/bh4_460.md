### Hadith 4:456

*Narrated Abdullah:*

Regarding the Verse: "Indeed he (Muhammad) did see. Of the Signs of his
Lord, The Greatest!" (53.18) That the Prophet had seen a green carpet
spread all over the horizon of the sky.
