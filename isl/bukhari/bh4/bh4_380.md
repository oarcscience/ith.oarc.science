### Hadith 4:376

*Narrated Jubair bin Mutim:*

That while he was with Allah's Apostle who was accompanied by the people
on their way back from Hunain, the bedouins started begging things of
Allah's Apostle so much so that they forced him to go under a Samura
tree where his loose outer garment was snatched away. On that, Allah's
Apostle stood up and said to them, "Return my garment to me. If I had as
many camels as these trees, I would have distributed them amongst you;
and you will not find me a miser or a liar or a coward."
