### Hadith 4:94

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Whoever spends two things in Allah's Cause, will be
called by all the gate-keepers of Paradise who will be saying, 'O
so-and-so! Come here.' " Abu Bakr said, "O Allah's Apostle! Such persons
will never be destroyed." The Prophet said, "I hope you will be one of
them."
