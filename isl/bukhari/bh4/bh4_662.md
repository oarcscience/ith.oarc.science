### Hadith 4:659

*Narrated Rabi bin Hirash:*

'Uqba bin 'Amr said to Hudhaifa, "Won't you relate to us of what you
have heard from Allah's Apostle ?" He said, "I heard him saying, "When
Al-Dajjal appears, he will have fire and water along with him. What the
people will consider as cold water, will be fire that will burn
(things). So, if anyone of you comes across this, he should fall in the
thing which will appear to him as fire, for in reality, it will be fresh
cold water." Hudhaifa added, "I also heard him saying, 'From among the
