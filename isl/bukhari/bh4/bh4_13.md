### Hadith 4:14

*Narrated Ibn Umar:*

I heard Allah's Apostle saying, "All of you are guardians and
responsible for your charges: the Ruler (i.e. Imam) is a guardian and
responsible for his subjects; and a man is a guardian of his family and
is responsible for his charges; and a lady is a guardian in the house of
her husband and is responsible for her charge; and a servant is a
guardian of the property of his master and is responsible for his
charge." I think he also said, "And a man is a guardian of the property
of his father."
