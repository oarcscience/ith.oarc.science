### Hadith 4:483

*Narrated Abu Jamra Ad-Dabi:*

I used to sit with Ibn 'Abbas in Mecca. Once I had a fever and he said
(to me), "Cool your fever with Zam-zam water, for Allah's Apostle said:
'It, (the Fever) is from the heat of the (Hell) Fire; so, cool it with
water (or Zam-zam water)."
