### Hadith 4:260

*Narrated Ikrima:*

Ali burnt some people and this news reached Ibn 'Abbas, who said, "Had I
been in his place I would not have burnt them, as the Prophet said,
'Don't punish (anybody) with Allah's Punishment.' No doubt, I would have
killed them, for the Prophet said, 'If somebody (a Muslim) discards his
religion, kill him.' "
