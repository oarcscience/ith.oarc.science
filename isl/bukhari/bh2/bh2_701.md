### Hadith 2:699

*Narrated Ibn Umar:*

Al Abbas bin Abdul-Muttalib asked the permission of Allahs Apostle to
let him stay in Mecca during the nights of Mina in order to provide the
pilgrims with water to drink, so the Prophet permitted him.
