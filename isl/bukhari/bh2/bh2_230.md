### Hadith 2:231

*Narrated Abdullah bin 'Amr bin Al-'As:*

Allah's Apostle told me, "The most beloved prayer to Allah is that of
David and the most beloved fasts to Allah are those of David. He used to
sleep for half of the night and then pray for one third of the night and
again sleep for its sixth part and used to fast on alternate days."
