### Hadith 2:251

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

A woman from the tribe of Bani Asad was sitting with me and Allah's
Apostle (p.b.u.h) came to my house and said, "Who is this?" I said,
"(She is) So and so. She does not sleep at night because she is engaged
in prayer." The Prophet said disapprovingly: Do (good) deeds which is
within your capacity as Allah never gets tired of giving rewards till
you get tired of doing good deeds."
