### Hadith 2:511

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

The Prophet went out for the 'Id prayer on the 'Id day and offered a two
Rakat prayer; and he neither offered a prayer before it or after it.
Then he went towards the women along with Bilal. He preached them and
ordered them to give in charity. And some (amongst the women) started
giving their fore-arm bangles and ear-rings.
