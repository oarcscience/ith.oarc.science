### Hadith 2:7

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

While 'Umar (bin Al-Khattab) was delivering the Khutba on a Friday, a
man entered (the mosque). 'Umar asked him, "What has detained you from
the prayer?" The man said, "It was only that when I heard the Adhan I
performed ablution (for the prayer)." On that 'Umar said, "Did you not
hear the Prophet saying: 'Anyone of you going out for the Jumua prayer
should take a bath'?".
