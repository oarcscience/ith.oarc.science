### Hadith 2:626

*Narrated Mujahid:*

I was in the company of Ibn Abbas and the people talked about Ad-Dajjal
and said, "Ad-Dajjal will come with the word Kafir (non-believer)
written in between his eyes." On that Ibn Abbas said, "I have not heard
this from the Prophet but I heard him saying, 'As if I saw Moses just
now entering the valley reciting Talbyia. ' "
