### Hadith 2:557

*Narrated Abu Huraira*

Allah's Apostle said, "The poor person is not the one who goes round the
people and ask them for a mouthful or two (of meals) or a date or two
but the poor is that who has not enough (money) to satisfy his needs and
whose condition is not known to others, that others may give him
something in charity, and who does not beg of people."
