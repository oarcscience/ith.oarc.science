### Hadith 2:212

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

Whenever the Prophet started a journey before noon, he used to delay the
Zuhr prayer till the time of 'Asr and then offer them together; and if
the sun declined (at noon) he used to offer the Zuhr prayer and then
ride (for the journey).
