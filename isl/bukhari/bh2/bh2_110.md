### Hadith 2:111

*Narrated 'Aisha :*

The Prophet (p.b.u.h) used to offer his night prayer while I was
sleeping across in his bed. Whenever he intended to offer the Witr
prayer, he used to wake me up and I would offer the Witr prayer too.
