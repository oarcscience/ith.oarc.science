### Hadith 2:549

*Narrated Abu Huraira :*

Allah's Apostle said, "By Him in Whose Hand my life is, it is better for
anyone of you to take a rope and cut the wood (from the forest) and
carry it over his back and sell it (as a means of earning his living)
rather than to ask a person for something and that person may give him
or not."
