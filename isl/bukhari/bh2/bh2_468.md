### Hadith 2:467

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Every child is born with a true faith of Islam (i.e.
to worship none but Allah Alone) and his parents convert him to Judaism
or Christianity or Magianism, as an animal delivers a perfect baby
animal. Do you find it mutilated?"
