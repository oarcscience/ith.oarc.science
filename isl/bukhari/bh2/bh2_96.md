### Hadith 2:97

*Narrated Um-'Atiya:*

We were ordered to go out (for 'Id) and also to take along with us the
menstruating women, mature girls and virgins staying in seclusion. (Ibn
'Aun said, "Or mature virgins staying in seclusion)." The menstruating
women could present themselves at the religious gathering and invocation
of Muslims but should keep away from their Musalla.
