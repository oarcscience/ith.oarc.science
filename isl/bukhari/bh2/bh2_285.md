### Hadith 2:284

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Dinar:*

Ibn 'Umar said, "The Prophet used to go to the Mosque of Quba every
Saturday (sometimes) walking and (sometimes) riding." 'Abdullah (Ibn
'Umar) used to do the same.
