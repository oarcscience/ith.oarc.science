### Hadith 2:219

*Narrated Aisha:*

(the mother of the faithful believers) I never saw Allah's Apostle
offering the night prayer while sitting except in his old age and then
he used to recite while sitting and whenever he wanted to bow he would
get up and recite thirty or forty verses (while standing) and then bow.
