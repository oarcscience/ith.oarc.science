### Hadith 2:723

*Narrated Um Al-Fadl bint Al Harith:*

On the day of 'Arafat, some people who were with me, differed about the
fasting of the Prophet (p.b.u.h) some said that he was fasting while
others said that he was not fasting. So I sent a bowl full of milk to
him while he was riding his camel, and he drank that milk.
