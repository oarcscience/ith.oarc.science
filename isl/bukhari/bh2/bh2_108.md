### Hadith 2:109

*Narrated Anas bin Sirin:*

I asked Ibn 'Umar, "What is your opinion about the two Rakat before the
Fajr (compulsory) prayer, as to prolonging the recitation in them?" He
said, "The Prophet (p.b.u.h) used to pray at night two Rakat followed by
two and so on, and end the prayer by one Raka Witr. He used to offer two
Rakat before the Fajr prayer immediately after the Adhan." (Hammad, the
sub-narrator said, "That meant (that he prayed) quickly.)"
