### Hadith 2:107

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar :*

The Prophet said, "Night prayer is offered as two Rakat followed by two
Rakat and so on, and if you want to finish it, pray only one Raka which
will be Witr for all the previous Rakat." Al-Qasim said, "Since we
attained the age of puberty we have seen some people offering a
three-Rakat prayer as Witr and all that is permissible. I hope there
will be no harm in it."
