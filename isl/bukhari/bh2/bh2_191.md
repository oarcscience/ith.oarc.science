### Hadith 2:192

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

The Prophet said, "A woman should not travel for more than three days
except with a Dhi-Mahram (i.e. a male with whom she cannot marry at all,
e.g. her brother, father, grandfather, etc.) or her own husband.)"
