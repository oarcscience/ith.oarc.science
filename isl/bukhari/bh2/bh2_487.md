### Hadith 2:486

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Whoever is made wealthy by Allah and does not pay
the Zakat of his wealth, then on the Day of Resurrection his wealth will
be made like a bald-headed poisonous male snake with two black spots
over the eyes. The snake will encircle his neck and bite his cheeks and
say, 'I am your wealth, I am your treasure.' " Then the Prophet recited
the holy verses:-- 'Let not those who withhold . . .' (to the end of the
verse). (3.180).
