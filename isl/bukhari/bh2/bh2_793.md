### Hadith 2:791

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

The Prophet was asked (as regards the ceremonies of Hajj) at Mina on the
Day of Nahr and he replied that there was no harm. Then a man said to
him, "I got my head shaved before slaughtering." He replied, "Slaughter
(now) and there is no harm in it." (Another) man said, "I did the Rami
(of the Jimar) after midday." The Prophet replied, "There was no harm in
it."
