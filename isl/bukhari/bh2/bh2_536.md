### Hadith 2:535

*Narrated Anas:*

Abu Bakr wrote to me what Allah had ordered His Apostle (about Zakat)
which goes: Neither an old nor a defected animal, nor a male-goat may be
taken as Zakat except if the Zakat collector wishes (to take it).
