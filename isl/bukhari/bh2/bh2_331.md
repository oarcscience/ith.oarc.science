### Hadith 2:329

*Narrated Abu Dhar:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Someone came to me from my Lord and gave me the
news (or good tidings) that if any of my followers dies worshipping none
(in any way) along with Allah, he will enter Paradise." I asked, "Even
if he committed illegal sexual intercourse (adultery) and theft?" He
replied, "Even if he committed illegal sexual intercourse (adultery) and
theft."
