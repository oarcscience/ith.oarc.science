### Hadith 2:195

*Narrated Anas bin Malik :*

offered four Rakat of Zuhr prayer with the Prophet (p.b.u.h) at Medina
and two Rakat at Dhul-Hulaifa. (i.e. shortened the 'Asr prayer).
