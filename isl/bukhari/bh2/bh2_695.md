### Hadith 2:693

*Narrated Ibn Umar:*

The Prophet reached Mecca, circumambulated the Kaba seven times and then
offered a two Rakat prayer behind Maqam ibrahim. Then he went towards
the Safa. Allah has said, "Verily, in Allah's Apostle you have a good
example."
