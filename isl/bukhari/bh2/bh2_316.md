### Hadith 2:314

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

People say that I narrate too many narrations of the Prophet; once I met
a man (during the life-time of the Prophet) and asked him, "Which Sura
did Allah's Apostle s recite yesterday in the 'Isha' prayer?" He said,
"I do not know." I said, "Did you not attend the prayer?" He said, "Yes,
(I did)." I said, "I know. He recited such and such Sura."
