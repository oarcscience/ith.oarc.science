### Hadith 2:509

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

I heard Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) while he was on the pulpit speaking
about charity, to abstain from asking others for some financial help and
about begging others, saying, "The upper hand is better than the lower
hand. The upper hand is that of the giver and the lower (hand) is that
of the beggar."
