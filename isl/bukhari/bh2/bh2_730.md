### Hadith 2:728

*Narrated Usama bin Zaid:*

As soon as the Prophet departed from 'Arafat, he went towards the
mountain pass, and there he answered the call of) the prayer is ahead of
you (i.e. at asked, "O Allah's Apostle! Will you offer the prayer here?"
He replied, "(The place of) the prayer is ahead of you (i.e. at
Al-Muzdalifa)."
