### Hadith 2:650

*Narrated Hisham:*

'Urwa said, "The Prophet entered Mecca in the year of the conquest of
Mecca from the side of Kada' which is at the higher part of Mecca."
'Urwa often entered from Kada' which was nearer of the two to his
dwelling place.
