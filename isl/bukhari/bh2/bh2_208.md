### Hadith 2:209

*Narrated Salim's father:*

The Prophet used to offer the Maghrib and Isha' prayers together
whenever he was in a hurry on a journey.

prayers together on journeys, and also used to offer the Maghrib and
'Isha' prayers together.

'Isha' prayers together on journeys.
