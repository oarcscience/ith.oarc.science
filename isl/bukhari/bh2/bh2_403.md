### Hadith 2:402

*Narrated Abu Sa'id Al-Khudri*

The Prophet said, "When a funeral is ready and the men carry the
deceased on their necks (shoulders), if it was pious then it will say,
'Present me quickly', and if it was not pious, then it will say, 'Woe to
it (me), where are they taking it (me)?' And its voice is heard by
everything except mankind and if he heard it he would fall unconscious."
