### Hadith 2:320

*Narrated Salama bin 'Alqama:*

I asked Muhammad (bin Sirin) whether Tashah-hud should be recited after
the two prostrations of Sahu. He replied, "It is not (mentioned) in Abu
Huraira's narration . "
