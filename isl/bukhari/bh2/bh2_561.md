### Hadith 2:560

*Narrated Salim bin 'Abdullah from his father:*

The Prophet said, "On a land irrigated by rain water or by natural water
channels or if the land is wet due to a near by water channel Ushr (i.e.
one-tenth) is compulsory (as Zakat); and on the land irrigated by the
well, half of an Ushr (i.e. one-twentieth) is compulsory (as Zakat on
the yield of the land)."
