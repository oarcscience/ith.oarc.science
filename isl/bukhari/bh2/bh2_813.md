### Hadith 2:811

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet offered the Zuhr, 'Asr, Maghrib and the 'Isha' prayers and
slept for a while at a place called Al-Mahassab and then rode to the
Ka'ba and performed Tawaf round it .
