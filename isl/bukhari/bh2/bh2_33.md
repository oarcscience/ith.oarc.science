### Hadith 2:34

*Narrated Ibn Juraij:*

I heard Nazi' saying, "Ibn Umar, said, 'The Prophet forbade that a man
should make another man to get up to sit in his place' ". I said to
Nafi', 'Is it for Jumua prayer only?' He replied, "For Jumua prayer and
any other (prayer)."
