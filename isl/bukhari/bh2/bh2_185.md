### Hadith 2:186

*Narrated Ibn Abbas :*

The Prophet once stayed for nineteen days and prayed shortened prayers.
So when we travel led (and stayed) for nineteen days, we used to shorten
the prayer but if we travelled (and stayed) for a longer period we used
to offer the full prayer.
