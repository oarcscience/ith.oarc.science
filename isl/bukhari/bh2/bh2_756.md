### Hadith 2:754

*Narrated Hafsa:*

I said, "O Allah's Apostle! What is wrong with the people, they have
finished their Ihram but you have not?" He said, "I matted my hair and I
have garlanded my Hadi, so I will not finish my Ihram till I finished my
Hajj ."
