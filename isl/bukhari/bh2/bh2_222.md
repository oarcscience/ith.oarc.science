### Hadith 2:223

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Allah's Apostle used to offer eleven Rakat and that was his prayer. He
used to prolong the prostration to such an extent that one could recite
fifty verses (of the Quran) before he would lift his head. He used to
pray two Rakat (Sunna) before the Fajr prayer and then used to lie down
on his right side till the call-maker came and informed him about the
prayer.
