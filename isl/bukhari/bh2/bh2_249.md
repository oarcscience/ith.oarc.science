### Hadith 2:250

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

At the time of the Fajr prayer the Prophet asked Bilal, "Tell me of the
best deed you did after embracing Islam, for I heard your footsteps in
front of me in Paradise." Bilal replied, "I did not do anything worth
mentioning except that whenever I performed ablution during the day or
night, I prayed after that ablution as much as was written for me."
