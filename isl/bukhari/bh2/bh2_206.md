### Hadith 2:207

*Narrated Ibn Abu Laila:*

Only Um Hani told us that she had seen the Prophet (p.b.u.h) offering
the Duha (forenoon prayer). She said, "On the day of the conquest of
Mecca, the Prophet took a bath in my house and offered eight Rakat. I
never saw him praying such a light prayer but he performed perfect had
told him that he had seen the Prophet (p.b.u.h) praying Nawafil at night
on the back of his Mount on a journey, facing whatever direction it
took.
