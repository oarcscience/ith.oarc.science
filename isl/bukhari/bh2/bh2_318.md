### Hadith 2:316

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Buhaina :*

Allah's Apostle got up after the second Raka of the Zuhr prayer without
sitting in between (the second and the third Rakat). When he finished
the prayer he performed two prostrations (of Sahu) and then finished the
prayer with Tasllm.
