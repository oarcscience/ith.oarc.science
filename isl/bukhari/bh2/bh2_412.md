### Hadith 2:411

*Narrated 'Amir:*

Ibn Abbas (who was at that time a boy) said, "Allah's Apostle came to a
grave and the people said, 'He or she was buried yesterday.' " Ibn Abbas
added, "We aligned behind the Prophet and he led the funeral prayer of
the deceased."
