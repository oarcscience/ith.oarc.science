### Hadith 2:566

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

Umar bin Al-Khattab gave a horse in charity in Allah's Cause and later
he saw it being sold in the market and intended to purchase it. Then he
went to the Prophet and asked his permission. The Prophet said, "Do not
take back what you have given in charity." For this reason, Ibn 'Umar
never purchased the things which he had given in charity, and in case he
had purchased something (unknowingly) he would give it in charity again.
