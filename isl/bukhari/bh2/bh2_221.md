### Hadith 2:222

*Narrated Salim's father:*

In the life-time of the Prophet whosoever saw a dream would narrate it
to Allah's Apostle. I had a wish of seeing a dream to narrate it to
Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) I was a grown up boy and used to sleep in the
Mosque in the life-time of the Prophet. I saw in the dream that two
angels caught hold of me and took me to the Fire which was built all
round like a built well and had two poles in it and the people in it
were known to me. I started saying, "I seek refuge with Allah from the
Fire." Then I met another angel who told me not to be afraid. I narrated
the dream to Hafsa who told it to Allah's Apostle. The Prophet said,
"Abdullah is a good man. I wish he prayed Tahajjud." After that
'Abdullah (i.e. Salim's father) used to sleep but a little at night.
