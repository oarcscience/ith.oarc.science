### Hadith 2:73

*Narrated Anas bin Malik,:*

Allah's Apostle never proceeded (for the prayer) on the Day of
'Id-ul-Fitr unless he had eaten some dates. Anas also narrated: The
Prophet used to eat odd number of dates.
