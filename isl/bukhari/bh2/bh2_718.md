### Hadith 2:716

*Narrated 'Abdul 'Aziz:*

I went out to Mina on the day of Tarwiya and met Anas going on a donkey.
I asked him, "Where did the Prophet offer the Zuhr prayer on this day?"
Anas replied, "See where your chiefs pray and pray similarly."
