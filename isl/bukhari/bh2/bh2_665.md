### Hadith 2:663

*Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri:*

The Prophet said "The people will continue performing the Hajj and 'Umra
to the Ka'ba even after the appearance of Gog and Magog."

The Hour (Day of Judgment) will not be established till the Hajj (to the
Ka'ba) is abandoned.
