### Hadith 2:256

*Narrated 'Aisha;*

Allah's Apostle offered the 'Isha' prayer (and then got up at the
Tahajjud time) and offered eight Rakat and then offered two Rakat while
sitting. He then offered two Rakat in between the Adhan and Iqama (of
the Fajr prayer) and he never missed them.
