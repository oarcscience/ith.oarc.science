### Hadith 2:304

*Narrated Ibn'Umar:*

The Prophet saw some sputum on the wall facing the Qibla of the mosque
and became furious with the people of the mosque and said, "During the
prayer, Allah is in front of everyone of you and so he should not spit
(or said, 'He should not expectorate')." Then he got down and scratched
the sputum with his hand. Ibn 'Umar said (after narrating), "If anyone
of you has to spit during the prayer, he should spit to his left."
