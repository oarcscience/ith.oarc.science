### Hadith 2:249

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

I did not see the Prophet reciting (the Quran) in the night prayer while
sitting except when he became old; when he used to recite while sitting,
and when thirty or forty verses remained from the Sura, he would get up
and recite them and then bow.
