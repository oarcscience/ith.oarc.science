### Hadith 2:487

*Narrated Abu Said:*

Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) said, "No Zakat is due on property mounting to
less than five Uqiyas (of silver), and no Zakat is due on less than five
camels, and there is no Zakat on less than five Wasqs." (A Wasqs equals
60 Sa's) & (1 Sa=3 K gms App.)
