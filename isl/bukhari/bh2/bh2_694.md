### Hadith 2:692

*Narrated Um Salama:*

(the wife of the Prophet) I informed Allah's Apostle (about my illness).
(Through other sub-narrators, Um Salama narrated that when Allah's
Apostle was at Mecca and had just decided to leave (Mecca) while she had
not yet done Tawaf of the Kaba (and after listening to her). The Prophet
said, "When the morning prayer is established, perform the Tawaf on your
camel while the people are in prayer." So she did the same and did not
offer the two Rakat of Tawaf until she came out of the Mosque.
