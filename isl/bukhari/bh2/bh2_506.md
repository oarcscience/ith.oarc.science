### Hadith 2:505

*Narrated Haritha bin Wahab Al-Khuza'i:*

I heard the Prophet (p.b.u.h) saying, "(O people!) Give in charity (for
Allah's cause) because a time will come when a person will carry his
object of charity from place to place (and he will not find any person
to take it) and any person whom he shall request to take it, I will
reply, 'If you had brought it yesterday I would have taken it, but today
I am not

in need of it."
