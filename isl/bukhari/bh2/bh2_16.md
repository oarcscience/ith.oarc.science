### Hadith 2:17

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The first Jumua prayer which was offered after a Jumua prayer offered at
the mosque of Allah's Apostle took place in the mosque of the tribe of
'Abdul Qais at Jawathi in Bahrain.
