### Hadith 2:322

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Buhaina Al-Asdi:*

(the ally of Bani 'Abdul Muttalib) Allah's Apostle stood up for the Zuhr
prayer and he should have sat (after the second Raka but he stood up for
the third Raka without sitting for Tashah-hud) and when he finished the
prayer he performed two prostrations and said Takbir on each prostration
while sitting, before ending (the prayer) with Taslim; and the people
too performed the two prostrations with him instead of the sitting he
forgot.
