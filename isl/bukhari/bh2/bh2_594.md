### Hadith 2:592

*Narrated Thumama bin 'Abdullah bin Anas:*

Anas performed the Hajj on a pack-saddle and he was not a miser. Anas
said, "Allah's Apostle

performed Hajj on a pack-saddle and the same Mount was carrying his
baggage too."
