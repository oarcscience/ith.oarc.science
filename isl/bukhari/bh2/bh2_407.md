### Hadith 2:406

*Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah :*

The Prophet said, "Today a pious man from Ethiopia (i.e. An Najashi) has
expired, come on to offer the funeral prayer." (Jabir said): We lined up
in rows and after that the Prophet led the prayer and we were in rows.
Jabir added, I was in the second row."
