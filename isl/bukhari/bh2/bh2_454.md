### Hadith 2:453

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

The Prophet said, "They now realize that what I used to tell them was
the truth. "And Allah said, 'Verily! You cannot make the dead to hear
(i.e. benefit them, and similarly the disbelievers) nor can you make the
deaf hear. (27.80).
