### Hadith 2:87

*Narrated Muhammad bin Abi Bakr Al-Thaqafi:*

While we were going from Mina to 'Arafat, I asked Anas bin Malik, about
Talbiya, "How did you use to say Talbiya in the company of the Prophet?"
Anas said: "People used to say Talbiya and their saying was not objected
to and they used to say Takbir and that was not objected to either. "
