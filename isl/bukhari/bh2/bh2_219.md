### Hadith 2:220

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

(the mother of the faithful believers) Allah's Apostle (in his last
days) used to pray sitting. He would recite while sitting, and when
thirty or forty verses remained from the recitation he would get up and
recite them while standing and then he would bow and prostrate. He used
to do the same in the second Raka. After finishing the Prayer he used to
look at me and if I was awake he would talk to me and if I was asleep,
he would lie down.
