### Hadith 2:696

*Narrated Abida bin Humaid:*

Abdul, Aziz bin Rufai Said, "I saw Abdullah bin Az-Zubair performing
Tawaf of the Kaba after the morning prayer then offering the two Rakat
prayer." Abdul Aziz added, "I saw Abdullah bin Az-Zubair offering a two
Rakat prayer after the Asr prayer." He informed me that Aisha told him
that the Prophet used to offer those two Rakat whenever he entered her
house."
