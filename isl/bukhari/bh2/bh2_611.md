### Hadith 2:609

*Narrated 'Umar:*

In the valley of Al-'Aqiq I heard Allah's Apostle saying, "To night a
messenger came to me from my Lord and asked me to pray in this blessed
valley and to assume Ihram for Hajj and 'Umra together. "
