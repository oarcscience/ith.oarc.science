### Hadith 2:568

*Narrated Abu Huraira :*

Al-Hasan bin 'Ali took a date from the dates given in charity and put it
in his mouth. The Prophet said, "Expel it from your mouth. Don't you
know that we do not eat a thing which is given in charity?"
