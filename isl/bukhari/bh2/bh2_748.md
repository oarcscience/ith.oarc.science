### Hadith 2:746

*Narrated 'Ubaidullah bin 'Abdullah:*

Ibn Abbas said, "Usama bin Zaid rode behind the Prophet from 'Arafat to
Al-Muzdalifa; and then from Al-Muzdalifa to Mina, Al-Fadl rode behind
him." He added, "Both of them (Usama and Al-Fadl) said, 'The Prophet was
constantly reciting Talbiya till he did Rami of the Jamarat-al-'Aqaba."
