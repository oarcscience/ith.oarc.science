### Hadith 2:686

*Narrated Um Salama:*

(the wife of the Prophet) I informed Allah's Apostle that I was ill. So
he said, "Perform the Tawaf while riding behind the people." I did so,
and at that time the Prophet was praying beside the Ka'ba and reciting
Surat-at-Tur.
