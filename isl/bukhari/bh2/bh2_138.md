### Hadith 2:139

*Narrated Abbas bin Tamim from his uncle who said,*

"The Prophet went out to the Musalla to offer the Istisqa' prayer, faced
the Qibla and offered a two-Rakat prayer and turned his cloak cloak on
his left side."
