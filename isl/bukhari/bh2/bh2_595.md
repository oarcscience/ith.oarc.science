### Hadith 2:593

*Narrated Al-Qasim bin Muhammad:*

'Aisha said, "O Allah's Apostle! You performed 'Umra but I did not." He
said, "O 'Abdur-Rahman! Go along with your sister and let her perform
'Umra from Tan'im." 'Abdur-Rahman made her ride over the pack-saddle of
a she-camel and she performed 'Umra.
