### Hadith 3:22

*Narrated Al-Aswad:*

Abdullah the slave of Asma bint Abu Bakr, told me that he used to hear
Asma', whenever she passed by Al-Hajun, saying, "May Allah bless His
Apostle Muhammad. Once we dismounted here with him, and at that time we
were traveling with light luggage; we had a few riding animals and a
little food ration. I, my sister, 'Aisha, Az-Zubair and such and such
persons performed 'Umra, and when we had passed our hands over the Ka'ba
(i.e. performed Tawaf round the Ka'ba and between As-Safa and Al-Marwa)
we finished our lhram. Later on we assumed Ihram for Hajj the same
evening."
