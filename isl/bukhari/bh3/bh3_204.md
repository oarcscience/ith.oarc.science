### Hadith 3:207

*Narrated Abu Aiyub from Juwairiya bint Al-Harith:*

The Prophet visited her (Juwairiya) on a Friday and she was fasting. He
asked her, "Did you fast yesterday?" She said, "No." He said, "Do you
intend to fast tomorrow?" She said, "No." He said, "Then break your
fast." Through another series of narrators, Abu Aiyub is reported to
have said, "He ordered her and she broke her fast."
