### Hadith 3:65

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

A man was crushed to death by his she-camel and was brought to Allah's
Apostle who said, "Give him a bath and shroud him, but do not cover his
head, and do not bring any perfume near to him, as he will be
resurrected reciting Talbiya."
