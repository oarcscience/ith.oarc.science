### Hadith 3:95

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "I was ordered to migrate to a town which will
swallow (conquer) other towns and is called Yathrib and that is Medina,
and it turns out (bad) persons as a furnace removes the impurities of
iron.
