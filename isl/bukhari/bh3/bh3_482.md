### Hadith 3:485

*Narrated Abdullah bin Umar:*

"Allah's Apostle gave the land of Khaibar to the Jews to work on and
cultivate and take half of its yield. Ibn 'Umar added, "The land used to
be rented for a certain portion (of its yield)." Nafi mentioned the
amount of the portion but I forgot it. Rafi' bin Khadij said, "The 'Umar
said: (The contract of Khaibar continued) till 'Umar evacuated the Jews
(from Khaibar).
