### Hadith 3:141

*Narrated Sahl bin Saud:*

When the following verses were revealed: 'Eat and drink until the white
thread appears to you, distinct from the black thread' and of dawn was
not revealed, some people who intended to fast, tied black and white
threads to their legs and went on eating till they differentiated
between the two. Allah then revealed the words, 'of dawn', and it became
clear that meant night and day.
