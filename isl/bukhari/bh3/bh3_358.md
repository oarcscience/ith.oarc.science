### Hadith 3:361

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Whoever buys a sheep which has been kept unmilked
for a long period, and milks it, can keep it if he is satisfied, and if
he is not satisfied, he can return it, but he should pay one Sa of dates
for the milk."
