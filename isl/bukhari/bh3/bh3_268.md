### Hadith 3:271

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet passed by a fallen date and said, "Were it not for my doubt
that this might have been given in charity, I would have eaten it." And
narrated Abu Huraira the Prophet said, "I found a date-fruit fallen on
my bed."
