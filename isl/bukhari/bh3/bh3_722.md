### Hadith 3:723

*Narrated Abu Musa Al-Ashari:*

The Prophet said, "He who has a slave-girl and teaches her good manners
and improves her education and then manumits and marries her, will get a
double reward; and any slave who observes Allah's right and his master's
right will get a double reward."
