### Hadith 3:78

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

A woman from the tribe of Khath'am came in the year (of ,Hajjat-ul-wada'
of the Prophet ) and said, "O Allah's Apostle! My father has come under
Allah's obligation of performing Hajj but he is a very old man and
cannot sit properly on his Mount. Will the obligation be fulfilled if I
perform Hajj on his behalf?" The Prophet replied in the affirmative.
