### Hadith 3:436

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

I heard the Prophet saying, "If a slave-girl of yours commits illegal
sexual intercourse and her illegal sexual intercourse is proved, she
should be lashed, and after that nobody should blame her, and if she
commits illegal sexual intercourse the second time, she should be lashed
and nobody should blame her after that, and if she does the offense for
the third time and her illegal sexual intercourse is proved, she should
be sold even for a hair rope."
