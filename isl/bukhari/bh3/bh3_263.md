### Hadith 3:266

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

'Ukaz, Majanna and Dhul-Majaz were market-places in the Pre-lslamic
period of ignorance. When Islam came, Muslims felt that marketing there
might be a sin. So, the Divine Inspiration came: "There is no harm for
you to seek the bounty of your Lord (in the seasons of Hajj)." (2.198)
Ibn 'Abbas recited the Verse in this way.
