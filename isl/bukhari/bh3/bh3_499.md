### Hadith 3:501

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet owed somebody a camel of a certain age. When he came to
demand it back, the Prophet said (to some people), "Give him (his due)."
When the people searched for a camel of that age, they found none, but
found a camel one year older. The Prophet said, "Give (it to) him." On
that, the man remarked, "You have given me my right in full. May Allah
give you in full." The Prophet said, "The best amongst you is the one
who pays the rights of others generously."
