### Hadith 3:82

*Narrated As-Sa'ib bin Yazid:*

(While in the company of my parents) I was made to perform Hajj with
Allah's Apostle and I was a seven-year-old boy then. (Fatch-Al-Bari,
p.443, Vol.4)
