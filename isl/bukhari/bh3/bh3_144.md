### Hadith 3:147

*Narrated Salama bin Al-Akwa:*

Once the Prophet ordered a person on 'Ashura' (the tenth of Muharram) to
announce, "Whoever has eaten, should not eat any more, but fast, and who
has not eaten should not eat, but complete his fast (till the end of the
day).
