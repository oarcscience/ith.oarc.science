### Hadith 3:647

*Narrated Usama bin Zaid:*

Once the Prophet stood at the top of one of the castles (or higher
buildings) of Medina and said, "Do you see what I see? No doubt I am
seeing the spots of afflictions amongst your houses as numerous as the
spots where rain-drops fall (during a heavy rain). (See Hadith No. 102)
