### Hadith 3:529

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

While the Prophet was passing the night at his place of rest in
Dhul-Hulaifa in the bottom of the valley (of Aqiq), he saw a dream and
it was said to him, "You are in a blessed valley." Musa said, "Salim let
our camels kneel at the place where 'Abdullah used to make his camel
kneel, seeking the place where Allah's Apostle used to take a rest,
which is situated below the mosque which is in the bottom of the valley;
it is midway between the mosque and the road."
