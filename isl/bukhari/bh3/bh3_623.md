### Hadith 3:624

*Narrated Anas:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Help your brother, whether he is an oppressor or
he is an oppressed one. People asked, "O Allah's Apostle! It is all
right to help him if he is oppressed, but how should we help him if he
is an oppressor?" The Prophet said, "By preventing him from oppressing
others."
