### Hadith 3:796

*Narrated Aiman:*

I went to 'Aisha and she was wearing a coarse dress costing five
Dirhams. 'Aisha said, "Look up and see my slave-girl who refuses to wear
it in the house though during the lifetime of Allah's Apostle I had a
similar dress which no woman desiring to appear elegant (before her
husband) failed to borrow from me."
