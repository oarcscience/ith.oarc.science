### Hadith 3:88

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet saw an old man walking, supported by his two sons, and asked
about him. The people informed him that he had vowed to go on foot (to
the Ka'ba). He said, "Allah is not in need of this old man's torturing
himself," and ordered him to ride.
