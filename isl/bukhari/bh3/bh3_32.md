### Hadith 3:33

*Narrated Nafi:*

When Abdullah bin Umar set out for Mecca intending to perform Umra, at
the time of afflictions, he said, "If I should be prevented from
reaching the Kaba, then I would do the same as Allah's Apostle did, so I
assume the lhram for Umra as Allah's Apostle assumed the Ihram for Umra
in the year of Hudaibiya."
