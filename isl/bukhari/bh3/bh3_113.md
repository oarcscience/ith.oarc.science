### Hadith 3:116

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

The Prophet observed the fast on the 10th of Muharram ('Ashura), and
ordered (Muslims) to fast on that day, but when the fasting of the month
of Ramadan was prescribed, the fasting of the 'Ashura' was abandoned.
'Abdullah did not use to fast on that day unless it coincided with his
routine fasting by chance.
