### Hadith 3:643

*Narrated Al-Araj:*

Abu Huraira said, "Allah's Apostle said, 'No-one should prevent his
neighbor from fixing a wooden peg in his wall." Abu Huraira said (to his
companions), "Why do I find you averse to it? By Allah, I certainly will
narrate it to you."
