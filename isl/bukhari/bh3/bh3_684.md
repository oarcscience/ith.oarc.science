### Hadith 3:685

*Narrated Anas:*

No doubt, the Prophet mortgaged his armor for barley grams. Once I took
barley bread with some dissolved fat on it to the Prophet and I heard
him saying, "The household of Muhammad did not possess except a Sa (of
food grain, barley, etc.) for both the morning and the evening meals
although they were nine houses."
