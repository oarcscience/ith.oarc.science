### Hadith 3:869

*Narrated Abdullah bin Kab bin Malik from Kab bin Malik:*

Abdullah bin Abu Hadrad Al-Aslami owed Kab bin Malik some money. One day
the latter met the former and demanded his right, and their voices grew
very loud. The Prophet passed by them and said, "O Ka'b," beckoning with
his hand as if intending to say, "Deduct half the debts." So, Kab took
half what the other owed him and remitted the other half.
