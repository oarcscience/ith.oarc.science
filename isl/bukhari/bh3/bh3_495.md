### Hadith 3:497

*Narrated 'Uqba bin Amir:*

that the Prophet had given him sheep to distribute among his companions
and a male kid was left (after the distribution). When he informed the
Prophet of it, he said (to him), "Offer it as a sacrifice on your
behalf."
