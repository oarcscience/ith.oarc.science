### Hadith 3:362

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "If a slave-girl commits illegal sexual intercourse
and it is proved beyond doubt, then her owner should lash her and should
not blame her after the legal punishment. And then if she repeats the
illegal sexual intercourse he should lash her again and should not blame
her after the legal punishment, and if she commits it a third time, then
he should sell her even for a hair rope."
