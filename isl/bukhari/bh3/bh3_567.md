### Hadith 3:568

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet allowed the sale of the dates of the 'Araya for ready dates
by estimating the former which should be estimated as less than five
Awsuq or five Awsuq. (Dawud, the sub-narrator is not sure as to the
right amount.)
