### Hadith 3:458

*Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:*

Allah's Apostle gave a verdict regarding Shuf'a in every undivided joint
thing (property). But if the limits are defined (or demarcated) or the
ways and streets are fixed, then there is no pre-emption.
