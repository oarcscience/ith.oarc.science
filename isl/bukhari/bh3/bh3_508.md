### Hadith 3:509

*Narrated 'Uqba bin Al-Harith:*

When An-Nuaman or his son was brought in a state of drunkenness, Allah's
Apostle ordered all those who were present in the house to beat him. I
was one of those who beat him. We beat him with shoes and palm-leaf
stalks.
