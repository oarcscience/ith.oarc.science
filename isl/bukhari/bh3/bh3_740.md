### Hadith 3:741

*Narrated Urwa:*

Aisha said to me, "O my nephew! We used to see the crescent, and then
the crescent and then the crescent in this way we saw three crescents in
two months and no fire (for cooking) used to be made in the houses of
Allah's Apostle. I said, "O my aunt! Then what use to sustain you?"
'Aisha said, "The two black things: dates and water, our neighbors from
Ansar had some Manarh and they used to present Allah's Apostle some of
their milk and he used to make us drink."
