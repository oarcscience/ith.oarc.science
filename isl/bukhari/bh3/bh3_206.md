### Hadith 3:209

*Narrated Um Al-Fadl bint Al-Harith:*

"While the people were with me on the day of 'Arafat they differed as to
whether the Prophet was fasting or not; some said that he was fasting
while others said that he was not fasting. So, I sent to him a bowl full
of milk while he was riding over his camel and he drank it."
