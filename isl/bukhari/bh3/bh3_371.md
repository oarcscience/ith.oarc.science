### Hadith 3:374

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Umar:*

Allah's Apostle said, "You should not try to cancel the purchases of one
another (to get a benefit thereof), and do not go ahead to meet the
caravan (for buying the goods) (but wait) till it reaches the market."
