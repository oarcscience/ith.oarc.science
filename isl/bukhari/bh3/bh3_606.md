### Hadith 3:607

*Narrated Khabbab:*

I was a blacksmith In the Pre-lslamic period of ignorance, and 'Asi bin
Wail owed me some money. I went to him to demand it, but he said to me,
"I will not pay you unless you reject faith in Muhammad." I replied, "By
Allah, I will never disbelieve Muhammad till Allah let you die and then
resurrect you." He said, "Then wait till I die and come to life again,
for then I will be given property and offspring and will pay your
right." So, thus revelation came: "Have you seen him who disbelieved in
Our signs and yet says, 'I will be given property and offspring?' "
(19.77)
