### Hadith 3:724

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "A pious slave gets a double reward." Abu Huraira
added: By Him in Whose Hands my soul is but for Jihad (i.e. holy
battles), Hajj, and my duty to serve my mother, I would have loved to
die as a slave.
