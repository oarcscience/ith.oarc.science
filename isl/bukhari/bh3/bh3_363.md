### Hadith 3:366

*Narrated Jarir:*

I have given a pledge of allegiance to Allah's Apostle for to testify
that None has the right to be worshipped but Allah, and Muhammad is His
Apostle, to offer prayers perfectly, to pay Zakat, to listen to and obey
(Allah's and His Prophet's orders), and to give good advice to every
Muslim.
