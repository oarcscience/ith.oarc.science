### Hadith 3:580

*Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:*

My father was martyred on the day (of the battle) of Uhud, and he was in
debt. His creditors demanded their rights persistently. I went to the
Prophet (and informed him about it). He told them to take the fruits of
my garden and exempt my father from the debts but they refused to do so.
So, the Prophet did not give them my garden and told me that he would
come to me the next morning. He came to us early in the morning and
wandered among the datepalms and invoked Allah to bless their fruits. I
then plucked the dates and paid the creditors, and there remained some
of the dates for us.
