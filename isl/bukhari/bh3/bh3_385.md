### Hadith 3:388

*Narrated Abdur-Rahman bin Abu Bakra:*

that his father said, "The Prophet forbade the selling of gold for gold
and silver for silver except if they are equivalent in weight, and
allowed us to sell gold for silver and vice versa as we wished."
