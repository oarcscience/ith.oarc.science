### Hadith 3:530

*Narrated 'Umar:*

While the Prophet was in Al-'Aqiq he said, "Someone (meaning Gabriel)
came to me from my Lord tonight (in my dream) and said, 'Offer the
prayer in this blessed valley and say (I intend to perform) Umra along
with Hajj (together).' "
