### Hadith 3:219

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Allah's Apostle ordered (the Muslims) to fast on the day of 'Ashura',
and when fasting in the month of Ramadan was prescribed, it became
optional for one to fast on that day ('Ashura') or not.
