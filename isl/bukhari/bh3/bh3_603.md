### Hadith 3:604

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle sent horsemen to Najd and they arrested and brought a
man called Thumama bin Uthal, the chief of Yamama, and they fastened him
to one of the pillars of the Mosque. When Allah's Apostle came up to
him; he asked, "What have you to say, O Thumama?" He replied, "I have
good news, O Muhammad!" Abu Huraira narrated the whole narration which
ended with the order of the Prophet "Release him!"
