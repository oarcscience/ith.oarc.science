### Hadith 3:9

*Narrated Abu Ishaq:*

I asked Masruq, 'Ata' and Mujahid (about the 'Umra of Allah's Apostle).
They said, "Allah's Apostle had performed 'Umra in Dhi-l-Qa'da before he
performed Hajj." I heard Al-Bara' bin 'Azib saying, "Allah's Apostle had
performed 'Umra in Dhi-l-Qa'da twice before he performed Hajj."
