### Hadith 3:681

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

The Prophet said, "Whoever manumits his share o a jointly possessed
slave, it is imperative on him to manumit the slave completely if he has
sufficient money to pay the rest of its price which is to be estimated
justly. He should pay his partners their shares and release him (the
freed one).
