### Hadith 3:51

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abbas:*

From As-Sa'b bin Jath-thama Al-Laithi that the latter presented an
onager to Allah's Apostle while he was at Al-Abwa' or at Waddan, and he
refused it. On noticing the signs of some unpleasant feeling of
disappointment on his (As-Sab's) face, the Prophet said to him, "I have
only returned it because I am Muhrim."
