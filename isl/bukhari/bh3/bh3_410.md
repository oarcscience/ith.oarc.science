### Hadith 3:413

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Hind, the mother of Mu'awiya said to Allah's Apostle, "Abu Sufyan (her
husband) is a miser. Am I allowed to take from his money secretly?" The
Prophet said to her, "You and your sons may take what is sufficient
reasonably and fairly."
