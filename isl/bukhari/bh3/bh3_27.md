### Hadith 3:28

*Narrated Humaid:*

Anas said, "Whenever Allah's Apostle returned from a journey, he, on
seeing the high places of Medina, would make his she-camel proceed
faster; and if it were another animal, even then he used to make it
proceed faster."

his love for Medina.
