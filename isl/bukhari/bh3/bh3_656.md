### Hadith 3:657

*Narrated Salama bin Al-Akwa:*

On the day of Khaibar the Prophet saw fires being lighted. He asked,
"Why are these fires being lighted?" The people replied that they were
cooking the meat of donkeys. He said, "Break the pots and throw away
their contents." The people said, "Shall we throw away their contents
and wash the pots (rather than break them)?" He said, "Wash them."
