### Hadith 3:140

*Narrated 'Adi bin Hatim:*

When the above verses were revealed: 'Until the white thread appears to
you, distinct from the black thread,' I took two (hair) strings, one
black and the other white, and kept them under my pillow and went on
looking at them throughout the night but could not make anything out of
it. So, the next morning I went to Allah's Apostle and told him the
whole story. He explained to me, "That verse means the darkness of the
night and the whiteness of the dawn."
