### Hadith 3:602

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "No doubt, I intended to order somebody to pronounce
the Iqama of the (compulsory congregational) prayer and then I would go
to the houses of those who do not attend the prayer and burn their
houses over them."
