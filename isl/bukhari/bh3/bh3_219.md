### Hadith 3:222

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The Prophet came to Medina and saw the Jews fasting on the day of
Ashura. He asked them about that. They replied, "This is a good day, the
day on which Allah rescued Bani Israel from their enemy. So, Moses
fasted this day." The Prophet said, "We have more claim over Moses than
you." So, the Prophet fasted on that day and ordered (the Muslims) to
fast (on that day).
