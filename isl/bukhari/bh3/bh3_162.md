### Hadith 3:165

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Allah's Apostle set out for Mecca in Ramadan and he fasted, and when he
reached Al-Kadid, he broke his fast and the people (with him) broke
their fast too. (Abu 'Abdullah said, "Al-Kadid is a land covered with
water between Usfan and Qudaid.")
