### Hadith 3:558

*Narrated As-Sab bin Jaththama:*

Allah's Apostle said, No Hima except for Allah and His Apostle. We have
been told that Allah's Apostle made a place called An-Naqi' as Hima, and
'Umar made Ash-Sharaf and Ar-Rabadha Hima (for grazing the animals of
Zakat).
