### Hadith 3:344

*Narrated Az-Zuhri from Malik bin Aus:*

that the latter said, "Who has change?" Talha said, "I (will have
change) when our store-keeper comes from the forest."

gold for silver is Riba, (usury), except if it is from hand to hand and
equal in amount, and wheat grain for wheat grain is usury except if it
is form hand to hand and equal in amount, and dates for dates is usury
except if it is from hand to hand and equal in amount, and barley for
barley is usury except if it is from hand to hand and equal in amount."
(See Riba-Fadl in the glossary).
