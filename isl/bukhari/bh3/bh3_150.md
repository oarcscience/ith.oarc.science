### Hadith 3:153

*Narrated Abu Bakr bin 'Abdur-Rahman:*

My father and I went to 'Aisha and she said, "I testify that Allah's
Apostle at times used to get up in the morning in a state of Janaba from
sexual intercourse, not from a wet dream and then he would fast that
day." Then he went to Um Salama and she also narrated a similar thing.
