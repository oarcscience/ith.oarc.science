### Hadith 3:492

*Narrated Salama bin Al-Akwa:*

A dead person was brought to the Prophet so that he might lead the
funeral prayer for him. He asked, "Is he in debt?" When the people
replied in the negative, he led the funeral prayer. Another dead person
was brought and he asked, "Is he in debt?" They said, "Yes." He (refused
to lead the prayer and) said, "Lead the prayer of your friend." Abu
Qatada said, "O Allah's Apostle! I undertake to pay his debt." Allah's
Apostle then led his funeral prayer.
