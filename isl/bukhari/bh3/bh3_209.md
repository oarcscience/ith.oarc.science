### Hadith 3:212

*Narrated Abu Sa'id:*

The Prophet forbade the fasting of 'Id-ul-Fitr and 'Id-ul-Adha (two
feast days) and also the wearing of As-Samma' (a single garment covering
the whole body), and sitting with one's leg drawn up while being wrapped
in one garment. He also forbade the prayers after the Fajr (morning) and
the 'Asr (afternoon) prayers.
