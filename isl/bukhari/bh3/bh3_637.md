### Hadith 3:638

*Narrated Um Salama:*

(the wife of the Prophet) Allah's Apostle heard some people quarreling
at the door of his dwelling. He came out and said, "I am only a human
being, and opponents come to me (to settle their problems); maybe
someone amongst you can present his case more eloquently than the other,
whereby I may consider him true and give a verdict in his favor. So, If
I give the right of a Muslim to another by mistake, then it is really a
portion of (Hell) Fire, he has the option to take or give up (before the
Day of Resurrection)."
