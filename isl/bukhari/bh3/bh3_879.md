### Hadith 3:880

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Ansar said to the Prophet, "Divide our date-palms between us and our
emigrant brothers." The Prophet said, "No." The Ansar said to the
emigrants, "You may do the labor (in our gardens) and we will share the
fruits with you." The emigrants said, "We hear and obey."
