### Hadith 3:706

*Narrated 'Umar bin Al-Khattab:*

The Prophet said, "The (reward of) deeds depend on intentions, and every
person will get the reward according to what he intends. So, whoever
migrated for Allah and His Apostle, then his migration will be for Allah
and His Apostle, and whoever migrated for worldly benefits or for
marrying a woman, then his migration will be for what he migrated for."
(See Hadith No. 1, Vol. 1)
