### Hadith 3:284

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

When Abu Bakr As-Siddiq was chosen Caliph, he said, "My people know that
my profession was not incapable of providing substance to my family. And
as I will be busy serving the Muslim nation, my family will eat from the
National Treasury of Muslims, and I will practise the profession of
serving the Muslims."
