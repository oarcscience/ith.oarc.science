### Hadith 3:714

*Narrated Anas:*

Some men of the Ansar asked for the permission of Allah's Apostle and
said, "Allow us to give up the ransom from our nephew Al-'Abbas. The
Prophet said (to them), "Do not leave (even) a Dirham (of his ransom)."
