### Hadith 3:868

*Narrated Aisha:*

Once Allah's Apostle heard the loud voices of some opponents quarreling
at the door. One of them was appealing to the other to deduct his debt
and asking him to be lenient but the other was saying, "By Allah I will
not do so." Allah's Apostle went out to them and said, "Who is the one
who was swearing by Allah that he would not do a favor?" That man said,
"I am that person, O Allah's Apostle! I will give my opponent whatever
he wishes."
