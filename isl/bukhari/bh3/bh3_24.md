### Hadith 3:25

*Narrated Ibn Umar:*

Whenever Allah's Apostle left for Mecca, he used to pray in the mosque
of Ash-Shajra, and when he returned (to Medina), he used to pray in the
middle of the valley of Dhul-Hulaifa and used to pass the night there
till morning.
