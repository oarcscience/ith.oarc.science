### Hadith 3:381

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

The Prophet forbade Muzabana; and Muzabana is the selling of fresh fruit
(without measuring it) for something by measure on the basis that if
that thing turns to be more than the fruit, the increase would be for
the seller of the fruit, and if it turns to be less, that would be of
his lot.

selling of the fruits on the trees after estimation (when they are
ripe).
