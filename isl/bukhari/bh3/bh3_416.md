### Hadith 3:419

*Narrated 'Abdur-Rahman bin Abu Bakr:*

We were with the Prophet when a tall pagan with long matted unkempt hair
came driving his sheep. The Prophet asked him, "Are those sheep for sale
or for gifts?" The pagan replied, "They are for sale." The Prophet
bought one sheep from him.
