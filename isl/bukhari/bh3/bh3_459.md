### Hadith 3:462

*Narrated Abu Musa:*

I went to the Prophet with two men from Ash-ari tribe. I said (to the
Prophet), "I do not know that they want employment." The Prophet said,
"No, we do not appoint for our jobs anybody who demands it earnestly."
