### Hadith 3:253

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

One of the wives of Allah's Apostle practiced Itikaf with him while she
ad bleeding in between her periods and she would see red (blood) or
yellowish traces, and sometimes we put a tray beneath her when she
offered the prayer.
