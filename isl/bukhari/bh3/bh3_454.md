### Hadith 3:457

*Narrated 'Abdullah:*

The people used to sell camels on the basis of Habal-al-Habala. The
Prophet forbade such sale. Nafi' explained Habalal-Habala by saying.
"The camel is to be delivered to the buyer after the she-camel gives
birth."
