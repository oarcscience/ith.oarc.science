### Hadith 3:339

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Zaid:*

The Prophet said, "The Prophet Abraham made Mecca a sanctuary, and asked
for Allah's blessing in it. I made Medina a sanctuary as Abraham made
Mecca a sanctuary and I asked for Allah's Blessing in its measures the
Mudd and the Sa as Abraham did for Mecca.
