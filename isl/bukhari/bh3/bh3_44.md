### Hadith 3:45

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Whoever performs Hajj to this House (Ka'ba) and
does not approach his wife for sexual relations nor commits sins (while
performing Hajj), he will come out as sinless as a newly-born child.
(Just delivered by his mother)."
