# 105. al-Fil: The Elephant

<span id="an_105_001">1</span> Hast thou not seen how thy Lord dealt
with the owners of the Elephant ?

<span id="an_105_002">2</span> Did He not bring their stratagem to
naught,

<span id="an_105_003">3</span> And send against them swarms of flying
creatures,

<span id="an_105_004">4</span> Which pelted them with stones of baked
clay,

<span id="an_105_005">5</span> And made them like green crops devoured
(by cattle) ?
