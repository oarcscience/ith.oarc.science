# 110. an-Nasr: The Help

<span id="an_110_001">1</span> When Allah's succour and the triumph
cometh

<span id="an_110_002">2</span> And thou seest mankind entering the
religion of Allah in troops,

<span id="an_110_003">3</span> Then hymn the praises of thy Lord, and
seek forgiveness of Him. Lo! He is ever ready to show mercy.
