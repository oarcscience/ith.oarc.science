# 111. al-Lahab: The Flame

<span id="an_111_001">1</span> The power of Abu Lahab will perish, and
he will perish.

<span id="an_111_002">2</span> His wealth and gains will not exempt him.

<span id="an_111_003">3</span> He will be plunged in flaming Fire,

<span id="an_111_004">4</span> And his wife, the wood-carrier,

<span id="an_111_005">5</span> Will have upon her neck a halter of
palm-fibre.
