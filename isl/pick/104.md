# 104. al-Humazah: The Slanderer

<span id="an_104_001">1</span> Woe unto every slandering traducer,

<span id="an_104_002">2</span> Who hath gathered wealth (of this world)
and arranged it.

<span id="an_104_003">3</span> He thinketh that his wealth will render
him immortal.

<span id="an_104_004">4</span> Nay, but verily he will be flung to the
Consuming One.

<span id="an_104_005">5</span> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the
Consuming One is!

<span id="an_104_006">6</span> (It is) the fire of Allah, kindled,

<span id="an_104_007">7</span> Which leapeth up over the hearts (of
men).

<span id="an_104_008">8</span> Lo! it is closed in on them

<span id="an_104_009">9</span> In outstretched columns.
