# 88. al-Ghashiya: The Overwhelming Calamity

<span id="an_088_001">1</span> Hath there come unto thee tidings of the
Overwhelming ?

<span id="an_088_002">2</span> On that day (many) faces will be
downcast,

<span id="an_088_003">3</span> Toiling, weary,

<span id="an_088_004">4</span> Scorched by burning fire,

<span id="an_088_005">5</span> Drinking from a boiling spring,

<span id="an_088_006">6</span> No food for them save bitter thorn-fruit

<span id="an_088_007">7</span> Which doth not nourish nor release from
hunger.

<span id="an_088_008">8</span> In that day other faces will be calm,

<span id="an_088_009">9</span> Glad for their effort past,

<span id="an_088_010">10</span> In a high Garden

<span id="an_088_011">11</span> Where they hear no idle speech,

<span id="an_088_012">12</span> Wherein is a gushing spring,

<span id="an_088_013">13</span> Wherein are couches raised

<span id="an_088_014">14</span> And goblets set at hand

<span id="an_088_015">15</span> And cushions ranged

<span id="an_088_016">16</span> And silken carpets spread.

<span id="an_088_017">17</span> Will they not regard the camels, how
they are created ?

<span id="an_088_018">18</span> And the heaven, how it is raised ?

<span id="an_088_019">19</span> And the hills, how they are set up ?

<span id="an_088_020">20</span> And the earth, how it is spread ?

<span id="an_088_021">21</span> Remind them, for thou art but a
remembrancer,

<span id="an_088_022">22</span> Thou art not at all a warder over them.

<span id="an_088_023">23</span> But whoso is averse and disbelieveth,

<span id="an_088_024">24</span> Allah will punish him with direst
punishment.

<span id="an_088_025">25</span> Lo! unto Us is their return

<span id="an_088_026">26</span> And Ours their reckoning.
