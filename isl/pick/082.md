# 82. al-Infitar: The Cleaving Asunder

<span id="an_082_001">1</span> When the heaven is cleft asunder,

<span id="an_082_002">2</span> When the planets are dispersed,

<span id="an_082_003">3</span> When the seas are poured forth,

<span id="an_082_004">4</span> And the sepulchres are overturned,

<span id="an_082_005">5</span> A soul will know what it hath sent before
(it) and what left behind.

<span id="an_082_006">6</span> O man! What hath made thee careless
concerning thy Lord, the Bountiful,

<span id="an_082_007">7</span> Who created thee, then fashioned, then
proportioned thee ?

<span id="an_082_008">8</span> Into whatsoever form He will, He casteth
thee.

<span id="an_082_009">9</span> Nay, but ye deny the Judgment.

<span id="an_082_010">10</span> Lo! there are above you guardians,

<span id="an_082_011">11</span> Generous and recording,

<span id="an_082_012">12</span> Who know (all) that ye do.

<span id="an_082_013">13</span> Lo! the righteous verily will be in
delight.

<span id="an_082_014">14</span> And lo! the wicked verily will be in
hell;

<span id="an_082_015">15</span> They will burn therein on the Day of
Judgment,

<span id="an_082_016">16</span> And will not be absent thence.

<span id="an_082_017">17</span> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the
Day of Judgment is!

<span id="an_082_018">18</span> Again, what will convey unto thee what
the Day of Judgment is!

<span id="an_082_019">19</span> A day on which no soul hath power at all
for any (other) soul. The (absolute) command on that day is Allah's.
