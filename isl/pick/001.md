# 1. al-Fatihah: The Opening

<span id="an_001_001">1</span> In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the
Merciful.

<span id="an_001_002">2</span> Praise be to Allah, Lord of the Worlds,

<span id="an_001_003">3</span> The Beneficent, the Merciful.

<span id="an_001_004">4</span> Master of the Day of Judgment,

<span id="an_001_005">5</span> Thee (alone) we worship; Thee (alone) we
ask for help.

<span id="an_001_006">6</span> Show us the straight path,

<span id="an_001_007">7</span> The path of those whom Thou hast
favoured; Not the (path) of those who earn Thine anger nor of those who
go astray.
