# 112. al-Ikhlas: The Unity

<span id="an_112_001">1</span> Say: He is Allah, the One!

<span id="an_112_002">2</span> Allah, the eternally Besought of all!

<span id="an_112_003">3</span> He begetteth not nor was begotten.

<span id="an_112_004">4</span> And there is none comparable unto Him.
