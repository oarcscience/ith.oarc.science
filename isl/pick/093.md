# 93. ad-Duha: The Early Hours

<span id="an_093_001">1</span> By the morning hours

<span id="an_093_002">2</span> And by the night when it is stillest,

<span id="an_093_003">3</span> Thy Lord hath not forsaken thee nor doth
He hate thee,

<span id="an_093_004">4</span> And verily the latter portion will be
better for thee than the former,

<span id="an_093_005">5</span> And verily thy Lord will give unto thee
so that thou wilt be content.

<span id="an_093_006">6</span> Did He not find thee an orphan and
protect (thee) ?

<span id="an_093_007">7</span> Did He not find thee wandering and direct
(thee) ?

<span id="an_093_008">8</span> Did He not find thee destitute and enrich
(thee) ?

<span id="an_093_009">9</span> Therefor the orphan oppress not,

<span id="an_093_010">10</span> Therefor the beggar drive not away,

<span id="an_093_011">11</span> Therefor of the bounty of thy Lord be
thy discourse.
