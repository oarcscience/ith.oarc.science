# 108. al-Kauthar: Abundance

<span id="an_108_001">1</span> Lo! We have given thee Abundance;

<span id="an_108_002">2</span> So pray unto thy Lord, and sacrifice.

<span id="an_108_003">3</span> Lo! it is thy insulter (and not thou) who
is without posterity.
