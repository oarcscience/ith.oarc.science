# 95. at-Tin: The Fig

<span id="an_095_001">1</span> By the fig and the olive,

<span id="an_095_002">2</span> By Mount Sinai,

<span id="an_095_003">3</span> And by this land made safe;

<span id="an_095_004">4</span> Surely We created man of the best stature

<span id="an_095_005">5</span> Then we reduced him to the lowest of the
low,

<span id="an_095_006">6</span> Save those who believe and do good works,
and theirs is a reward unfailing.

<span id="an_095_007">7</span> So who henceforth will give the lie to
thee about the judgment ?

<span id="an_095_008">8</span> Is not Allah the most conclusive of all
judges ?
