# 103. al-\`Asr: Time

<span id="an_103_001">1</span> By the declining day,

<span id="an_103_002">2</span> Lo! man is a state of loss,

<span id="an_103_003">3</span> Save those who believe and do good works,
and exhort one another to truth and exhort one another to endurance.
