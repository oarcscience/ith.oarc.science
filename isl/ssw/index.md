------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Persian poet Sadi, best known for his poetic collections the
[Bustan](../bus/index) and [Gulistan](../gulistan.txt), also wrote this
work, the Pand Namah, or Scroll of Wisdom. This is a small collection of
poems on moral themes. Translated by Arthur N. Wollaston, this entry in
the Wisdom of the East series is one of the best.
