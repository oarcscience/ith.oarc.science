### Sūra XCV.

### *Tīn,* or The Fig.

*In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.*

1\. By the Fig  
And the Olive,

2\. And the Mount  
Of Sinai,

3\. And this City  
Of security,—

<span id="page_1759">p. 1759</span>

4\. We have [indeed](errata.htm#9) created man  
In the best of moulds,

5\. Then do We abase him  
(To be) the lowest  
Of the low,—

6\. Except such as believe  
And do righteous deeds:  
For they shall have  
A reward unfailing.

7\. Then what can,  
After this, contradict thee  
As to the Judgment  
(To come)?

8\. Is not God  
The wisest of Judges?
