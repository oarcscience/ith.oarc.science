### Sūra CV.

### *Fīl,* or The Elephant.

*In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.*

1\. Seest thou not  
How thy Lord dealt  
With the Companions  
Of the Elephant?

2\. Did He not make  
Their treacherous plan  
Go astray?

3\. And He sent against them  
Flights of Birds,

4\. Striking them with stones  
Of baked clay.

5\. Then did He make them  
Like an empty field  
Of stalks and straw,  
(Of which the corn)  
Has been eaten up.
