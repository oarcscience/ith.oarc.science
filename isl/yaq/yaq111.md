### Sūra CXI.

### *Lahab,* or (the Father of) Flame.

*In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.*

1\. Perish the hands  
Of the Father of Flame!  
Perish he!

2\. No profit to him  
From all his wealth,  
And all his gains!

3\. Burnt soon will he be  
In a Fire  
Of blazing Flame!

4\. His wife shall carry  
The (crackling) wood—  
As fuel!—

5\. A twisted rope  
Of palm-leaf fibre  
Round her (own) neck!
