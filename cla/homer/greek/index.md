# The Iliad and Odyssey

## of Homer

### in Unicode Greek

------------------------------------------------------------------------

is cross-linked with the Samuel Butler translation of the
[Iliad](../ili/index) and [Odyssey](../ody/index). To view this, your
browser must support [Unicode](../../../unicode).
