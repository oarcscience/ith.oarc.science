99

Ἦῤ ἔτι παρθενίασ επιβάλλομαι; [transcription]

Do I still long for maidenhood?

Quoted by Apollonius to show the Aeolic form ἦρα [transcription] , the interrogative particle ἆρα [transcription] , and also as a specimen of metre.
