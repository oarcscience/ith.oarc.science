# <span id="BM80">80</span>

Δαύοισ ἀπάλασ ἐτάρασ ἐν στήθεσιν. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#103.md)

Sleep thou, in the bosom of thy sweetheart.

From the "Etymologicum Magnum." This fragment probably belongs among the
Epithalamia.
