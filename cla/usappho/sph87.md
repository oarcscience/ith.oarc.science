# <span id="BM86">86</span>

Ἀμφὶ δ᾽ ἄβροισ λασίοισ εὖ ϝε
πύκασσεν. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#110.md)

Pollux says that the line refers to finely woven linen.
