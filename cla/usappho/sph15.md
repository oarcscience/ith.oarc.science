# <span id="BM14">14</span>

ταῖσ καλαισ ὔμμιν \[τὸ\] νόημα τὦμον  
οἰ διάμειπτον. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#20.md)

To you, fair maidens, my mind does not change.

Quoted by Apollonius to illustrate the Aeolic form
ὔμμιν [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#21.md) .
