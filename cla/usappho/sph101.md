# <span id="BM100">100</span>

Χαίροισα ηύμφα, χαιρέτω δ᾽ ὀ
γάμβροσ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#128.md)

The bride \[comes\] rejoicing, let the bridegroom also rejoice.

From Hephaestion as a specimen of catalectic iambic.
