# <span id="BM23">23</span>

Οὔ τι μοι ὔμμεσ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#39.md)

You are nought to me.

Ασ θελετ᾽ ὔμμεσ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#40.md)

While you will.

These are quoted by Apollonius to show the Aeolic form
ὔμμεσ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#41.md)
