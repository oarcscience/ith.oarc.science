45

Μάλα δὴ κεκορημένασ Γόργωσ. [transcription]

Very weary of Gorgo.

Quoted by Choeroboscus about A.D. 600 to show the Aeolic genitive with -ωσ [transcription] . Gorgo is mentioned by Maximus Tyrius with Andromeda as being friends of Sappho.
