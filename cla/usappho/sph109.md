# <span id="BM108">108</span>

Μὴ κίνη χέραδασ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#137.md)

Stir not the pebbles.

Mary Barnard translates this: "If you're squeamish, don't prod the beach
rubble."

From the Scholiast on Apollonius Rhodius.
Χεράδεσ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#138.md) were little heaps of stone.
