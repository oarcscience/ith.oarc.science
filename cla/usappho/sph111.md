# 110

Ἠμιτύβιον σταλάσσον. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#141.md)

A napkin dripping.

From the Scholiast on the Plutus of Aristophanes to show the meaning of
ἠμιτύβιον [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#142.md) . This was a piece of
soft linen for wiping the hands.
