# <span id="BM12">12</span>

... Ὄττινασ γὰρ  
εὖ θέω κῆνοί με μάλιστα σίννονται  
... [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#18.md)

For thee to whom I do good, thou harmest me the most.

From the "Etymologicum Magnum," tenth century A.D.
