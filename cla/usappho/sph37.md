# <span id="BM36">36</span>

Ὤσ δὲ παῖσ πέδα μάτερα πεπτερύτωμαι. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#56.md)

So, like a child after its mother, I flutter.

From the "Etymologicum Magnum."
