# <span id="BM35">35</span>

Πσαύην δ᾽ οὐ δοκίμοιμ᾽ ὀράνω δύσι
πάχεσιν. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#55)

With my two arms, I do not aspire to to touch the sky.

Quoted by Herodian.
