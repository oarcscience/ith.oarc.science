# <span id="BM13">13</span>

Ἕγω δὲ κῆν᾽ ὄττω τισ ἔπαται. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#19)

But that which one desires I.

Quoted by Apollonius and in 1914 found to be part of the poem in the
"Oxyrhynchus Papyrus," No. 1231.
