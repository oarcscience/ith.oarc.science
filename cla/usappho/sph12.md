# <span id="BM11">11</span>

... Τάδε νῦν ἐταίραισ  
ταῖσ ἔμαισι τέρπνα κάλωσ ἀείσω. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#16)

This will I now sing skilfully to please my friends.

Athanaeus quotes this to show that there is not necessarily any reproach
in the word ἐταίραι [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#17) . Like many
others, the fragment is unfortunately too short for anything but a
literal translation. The breathing of the word in question in Attic
Greek would of course be rough.
