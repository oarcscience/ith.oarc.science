# <span id="BM99">99</span>

Ἦῤ ἔτι παρθενίασ επιβάλλομαι; [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#125.md)

Do I still long for maidenhood?

Quoted by Apollonius to show the Aeolic form
ἦρα [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#126.md) , the interrogative particle
ἆρα [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#127.md) , and also as a specimen of
metre.
