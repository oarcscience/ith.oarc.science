# <span id="BM122">122</span>

Athenaeus mentions Βάρωμοσ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#153) and
σάρβιτοσ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#154) , two stringed instruments
in use in the time of Sappho. Their exact character is not known. He
also gives the form Βάρμοσ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#155) for the
name of the former instrument.
