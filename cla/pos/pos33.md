### GOLD

<span class="smallcaps">Gold</span> is the son of Zeus,  
  Immortal, bright;  
Nor moth nor worm may eat it,  
  Nor rust tarnish.

So are the Muse's gifts  
  The offspring fair,  
That merit from high heaven  
  Youth eternal.
