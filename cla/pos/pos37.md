### THE DYE

<span class="smallcaps">From</span> Scythian wood they brew  
The dye whose yellow hue  
Turns gold the lovely hair  
Of Lesbians fair.

So, Zanthis, slave of mine,  
Shall dip the fleeces fine,  
And dye the robes I made  
A saffron shade.
