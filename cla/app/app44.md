### 49. TÎMAEUS OF ITALIAN LOCRI

<span class="smallcaps">Tîmaeus</span> Of <span
class="smallcaps">Italian Locri</span>.

Nothing is known of him except from Plato's dialogue. A treatise *On the
Soul of the Universe and Nature* ascribed to him is a forgery derived
from Plato's *Timaeus*.
