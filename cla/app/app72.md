### 77. BIÔN OF ABDÊRA

<span class="smallcaps">Biôn</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Abdêra</span> probably lived at the end of the fourth
century B.C.

A follower of Democritus. He wrote on meteorology, but none of his
writings survive.
