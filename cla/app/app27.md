### 32. MENESTÔR OF SYBARIS

<span class="smallcaps">Menestôr</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Sybaris</span> was in his prime about the middle of
the fifth century. He wrote on botany, but none of his writings has
survived. His opinions are quoted by Theophrastus.
