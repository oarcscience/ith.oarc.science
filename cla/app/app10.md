### 8. THEAGENES OF RHÊGIUM

<span class="smallcaps">Theagenes</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Rhêgium</span> lived in the time of Cambyses (529-522
B.C.).

He was the first scholar known to have applied the allegorical method of
interpretation to Homer. No writings survive.
