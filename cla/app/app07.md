### 5. PHÔCUS OF SAMOS

<span class="smallcaps">Phôcus</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Samos</span>: date unknown.

He was credited with a verse work on *Nautical Astronomy* sometimes
ascribed to Thales. Nothing is known of its contents.
