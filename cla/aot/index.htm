This page lists resources at this site about Apollonius of Tyana, the philosopher and reputed wonder-worker of the first century Roman world.

The Life of Apollonius of Tyana   by Philostratus, tr. F.C. Conybeare [1912]   Philostratus' life of Apollonius is our primary contemporary account.

The Epistles of Apollonius of Tyana   by Apollonius, tr. F.C. Conybeare [1912]   All attributed correspondence of Apollonius of Tyana.

The Treatise of Eusebius Against the Life of Apollonius by Philostratus   by Eusebius, tr. F.C. Conybeare [1912]   An early Christian reviews Philostratus and gives a thumbs-down.
