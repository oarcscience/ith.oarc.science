# 6

### Priapus

Cum loquor, una mihi peccatur littera; nam te  
   pe-dico semper blaesaque lingua mihi est.

Oft in my speech one letter is lost; for Predicate always  
*Pedicate* I pronounce. Reason--a trip of the tongue!

Whenever I speak, one word slips me; for, talking with a lisp, I always
say instead of *praedico, paedico!*\[2\]

\[1. Which he held in his hand.  
2. Instead of saying '*praedico*', meaning 'I warn you not to trespass',
he lisps and says 'paedico', meaning 'I am sodomising you'.\]
