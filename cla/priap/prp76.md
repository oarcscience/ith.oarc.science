# 76

### Priapus

Per medios ibit pueros mediasque puellas  
   mentula, barbatis non nisi summa petet.

Right through the middle of lads and of lasses a passage shall pierce  
This yard, yet shall it touch bearded ones only aloft.

Through the middles of lads and girls will my mentule make its way;
those bearded 'twill not attack save at the height.

\[1. The courtesan's coynte being jocosely termed an altar.\]
