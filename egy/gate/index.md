# The Book of Gates

## by E. A. Wallis Budge

#### \[1905\]

#### (original title) The short form of the Book of am-tuat and the Book of Gates

------------------------------------------------------------------------

The *Book of Gates* is an Ancient Egyptian cosmological treatise
describing the architecture and inhabitants of the Tuat, the underworld
which the boat of the Sun God, Ra, traverses during the night hours.
This is the second volume of the three volume Budge series which deals
with the books of the Underworld, the *Egyptian Heaven and Hell*. It
also includes a short summary of the Book of Am-Tuat, the longer version
of which comprises the [first volume](../bat/index).
