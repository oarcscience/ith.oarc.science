### THE ONE HUNDRED AND SEVENTH CEREMONY.

Two vessels of roasted Set grain, with the formula:--

"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, the
Glorious One, which shall turn back \[the fiends of Set\]."
