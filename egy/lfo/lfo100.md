### THE ONE HUNDRED AND FIRST CEREMONY.

Two vessels of Hebnent wine, with the formula:--

"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, and it
hath overthrown them \[i.e., the companions of Set\]."
