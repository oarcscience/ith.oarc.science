### THE NINETY-SIXTH CEREMONY.

Two vessels of wine of the North, with the formula:--

"Osiris Unas, \[there hath been presented unto thee that which filled
thy father, and\] <span id="fr_78"></span>[1](#fn_78.md) thy mouth hath
been opened thereby."
