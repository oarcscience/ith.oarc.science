LX. HOW THEY DENOTE CHILDREN PLOTTING AGAINST THEIR MOTHERS.

When they would denote children plotting against their mothers, they delineate a VIPER; for the viper is not

p. 124

brought forth in the [usual manner?], but disengages itself by gnawing through the belly of its mother.
