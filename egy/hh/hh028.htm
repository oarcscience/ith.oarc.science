XXVI. HOW AN OPENING.

 1

When they would denote an opening, they delineate a HARE, because this animal always has its eyes open.

Footnotes

48:1

Denotes "opening" according to Wilkinson—according to Sharpe it signifies "It is lawful." Qy. "Open or unprohibited."
