### XLII. WHAT THEY DESIGN BY DEPICTING A MULE.

<span id="img_11300"></span> ![](img/11300.jpg)

When they would symbolise *a barren woman*, they delineate a MULE; for
this animal is barren, quod matricem rectam non habet.
