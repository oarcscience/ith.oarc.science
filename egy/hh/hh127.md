### LIV. HOW A MAN FOND OF DANCING.

<span id="img_11900"></span> ![](img/11900.jpg)

When they would symbolise *a man fond of dancing and piping*, they
delineate a TURTLE DOVE; for it is taken by means of a pipe and dancing.
