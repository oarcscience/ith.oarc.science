X. HOW THEY SYMBOLIZE PERMANENCY AND SAFETY.

 1

The BONE OF A QUAIL when delineated symbolizes permanency and safety; because the bone of this animal is difficult to be affected.

I. The quail.

II. Signifies "Good." Sh. 625.
