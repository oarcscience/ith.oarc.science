LXVII. HOW A MAN THAT CONCEALS HIS OWN DEFECTS.

When they would symbolise a man that conceals his own defects, they depict AN APE 1 MAKING WATER; for when he makes water he conceals his urine.

p. 128

127:1 Pier. mentions a reading, αἴλουρον "a cat."
