LXXXVIII. HOW A MAN THAT AT IS PROVIDING HIS OWN TOMB.

When they would symbolise a man that is providing 

p. 140

his own tomb, they depict an ELEPHANT 1 BURYING ITS OWN TEETH; for when his teeth fall out, he takes them up and buries them.
