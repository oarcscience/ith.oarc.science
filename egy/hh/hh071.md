### LXIX. HOW SUNSET.

<span id="img_08600"></span> ![](img/08600.jpg) <span
id="fr_97"></span>[1](#fn_92)

To denote *sunset*, they represent A CROCODILE TENDING DOWNWARDS, for
this animal is self productive \[?\] and inclining downwards.
