### LIX. HOW A VERY BAD KING.

<span id="img_08000"></span> ![](img/08000.jpg) <span
id="fr_88"></span>[1](#fn_86.md)

To denote *a very bad* \[a very good? a very powerful?\] king, they
depict a SERPENT in the form of a circle, whose tail they place in his
mouth, and they write the name of the king in the middle of the coil,
intimating that the king governs the world. The serpent's name among the
Egyptians is Meisi.
