### XV. HOW WIND.

<span id="img_09901"></span> ![](img/09901.jpg)

A HAWK soaring on high towards the east, signifies the *winds;* \[the
spirit or soul?\] and again otherwise, a HAWK with its wings expanded in
the air symbolizes the wind, as having wings.
