### LXXXV. HOW A KING THAT FLEES FROM FOLLY AND INTEMPERANCE.

<span id="img_13800"></span> ![](img/13800.jpg)

When they would symbolise *a king that flees from folly and
intemperance*, they delineate AN ELEPHANT <span
id="fr_137"></span>[1](#fn_132) AND A RAM; for he flees at the sight of
a ram.
