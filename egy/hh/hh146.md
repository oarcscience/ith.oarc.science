### LXXIII. HOW A MAN ANNOYED BY HIS PRIVATE ENEMIES.

<span id="img_13100"></span> ![](img/13100.jpg)

When they would represent *a man annoyed* <span
id="fr_133"></span>[1](#fn_128.md) *by his private enemies, and extricating
himself with small loss*, they portray a WOLF WHICH HAS LOST THE
EXTREMITY OF HIS TAIL; for the wolf, when about to be hunted, shoots the
hairs and extremity of his tail.
