\[§ XLIII. The height of the Nile in flood at Elephantine is
twenty-eight cubits, at Mendes and Xoïs low Nile is seven cubits, and at
Memphis middle Nile is fourteen cubits; these figures are to be compared
with the twenty-eight days of the Moon's revolution, the seven-day phase
of the Moon, and the fourteen days' Moon, or full moon. Apis was
begotten by a ray of light from the Moon, and on the fourteenth day of
the month Phamenoth <span id="fr_344"></span>[1](leg66.htm#fn_342.md)
Osiris entered the Moon. Osiris is the power of the Moon, Isis the
productive faculty in it.\]
