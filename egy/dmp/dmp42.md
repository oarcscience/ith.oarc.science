### VERSO COL. X.

\(1\) Another talisman for the foot of the gouty man: (2) you write
these names on a strip (3) of silver or tin; you put it (4) on a
deer-skin, you bind it to the foot (5) of the man named, δέρμα ἐλάφιον,
with the two feet. (6) 'θεμβαφαθεμ (7) ουρεμβρενουτιπε (8) αιοχθου (9)
σεμμαραθεμμου (10) ναιοου. Let N. son of N. recover (11) from every pain
which is in his feet and two legs.' (12) You do it when the moon is in
the constellation of Leo.
