### VERSO COL. XXV.

\(1\) ...... (2) hawk's dung, salt, *asi* plant, (3) *bel*, pound
together, anoint (4) your phallus with it and lie with (5) the woman. If
it is dry, you (6) pound a little of it with wine, and you (7) anoint
your phallus with it (8) and you lie with the woman. Excellent (*bis*).
