### COL. XXII.

\(1\) Behold! (spell?) of the name of the Great-of-Five which they
pronounce to every spirit. There is none

<span id="page_144">p. 144</span> <span id="page_145">p. 145</span>

that is (2) stronger than it in the books. If you pronounce these charms
to any vessel, (3) then the gods depart not before you have questioned
them concerning every word and they have told you (4) the answer about
heaven, earth, and the underworld, a distant inquiry (?), water, (5)
(and) the fields. A charm which is in the power (?) of a man to
pronounce.
