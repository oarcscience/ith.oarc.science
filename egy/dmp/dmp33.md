# VERSO

### VERSO COL. I.

\(1\) Eyebrow of Ra: ὀφρὺς ἡλίου.(3) Eyebrow of the moon. ὀφρὺς
σελήνης.(3) These are some herbs.

\(4\) *Heliogonon*. (5) *Selenegonon*. (6) These are some herbs.

\(7\) Spurge, (8) which is that small herb that is in the gardens (9)
and which exudes milk.(10) If you put its milk on a man's skin, (11) it
causes a blister.
