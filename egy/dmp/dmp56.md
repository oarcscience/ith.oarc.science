### VERSO COL. XXIV.

\(1\) .... (2), ... on it, and you ...... (3) ..... of fine linen on it
(? him), these three names being written on it, (4) ....... with myrrh;
you light it and place it (5) ......... your head; you recite them to it
again nine times. (6) ....... the lamp; you do it at the time of the
third hour(?) of evening (7) \[and you\] lie down (?). Formula:
'Iobasaoumptho (8) \[Khrome (?) Lou\]khar let my eyes be opened (9) in
truth concerning any given matter which I am praying for here (10)
\[to-day, in\] truth without telling thee (*sic*) falsehood.'

\(11\) 'Iobasaoumptthokhromeloukhar, (12) let my eyes be opened in truth
concerning any given thing which I am praying (13) for here to-day.'
