# NOTE

THIS volume is the first of a series of three volumes which treat of the
Egyptian Heaven and Hell. It contains the complete hieroglyphic text of
the Book AM-TUAT, with translations, and reproductions of all the
illustrations. A series of Chapters dealing with the origin and contents
of Books of the Other World, with prefatory remarks, and a full index to
the whole work, will be found in the third volume.
