### Stelliones

<span class="smallcaps">The</span> supporters of the Ironmongers’
Company of London are two lizards. Bossewell describes beasts of similar
shape—"Stelliones" as he terms them, evidently in allusion to steel. He
says, "Stellio is a beaste like a lysard, having on his back spotts like
starres." <span id="fr_27"></span>[\*](#fn_27)

Stellione-serpent, a serpent with the head of a weasel, borne by the
name of Baume.
