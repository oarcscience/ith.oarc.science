### The Enfield

<span class="smallcaps">An</span> imaginary hybrid animal with *the head
of a fox, chest of a greyhound, talons of an eagle, and body of a lion;
the hind legs and tail of a wolf*. It occurs as the crest of some Irish
families of the name of Kelly.
