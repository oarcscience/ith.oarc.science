### Baphomet

<span class="smallcaps">A fictitious</span> creature having two heads,
male and female, the rest of the body female; said to be used as an idol
or symbol by the Templars in their mysterious rites. The word is a
corruption of Mahomet. Though mentioned in old works it does not now
appear in British heraldry.
