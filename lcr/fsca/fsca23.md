### The Lion-Dragon

is compounded of the forepart of a lion conjoined to the hinder part of
a dragon.

*Or, a lion-dragon gules armed, langued and crowned of the first*, is
the *Bretigni* family.

*Party per chevron gules and or, three lion-dragons ducally crowned and
countercharged*.—*Easton*.
