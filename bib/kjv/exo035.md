### King James Version: Exodus Chapter 35

<span id="001">1</span> And [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) gathered all
the [congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) together, and said unto them, These are
the words which the LORD hath commanded, that ye should do them.  

<span id="002">2</span> Six days shall work be done, but on the seventh
day there shall be to you an holy day, a
[sabbath](../ebd/ebd317.htm#003) of rest to the LORD: whosoever doeth
work therein shall be put to death.  

<span id="003">3</span> Ye shall kindle no fire throughout your
habitations upon the [sabbath](../ebd/ebd317.htm#003) day.  

<span id="004">4</span> And [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) spake unto
all the [congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), saying, This is the thing which the
LORD commanded, saying,  

<span id="005">5</span> Take ye from among you an
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) unto the LORD: whosoever is of a
willing heart, let him bring it, an [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of
the LORD; [gold](../ebd/ebd152.htm#001), and
[silver](../ebd/ebd344.htm#000), and [brass](../ebd/ebd064.htm#003),  

<span id="006">6</span> And [blue](../ebd/ebd061.htm#003), and purple,
and [scarlet](../ebd/ebd323.htm#005), and fine
[linen](../ebd/ebd229.htm#008), and goats' hair,  

<span id="007">7</span> And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins,
and [shittim](../ebd/ebd339.htm#009) [wood](../ebd/ebd383.htm#003),  

<span id="008">8</span> And oil for the light, and
[spices](../ebd/ebd349.htm#005) for anointing oil, and for the sweet
[incense](../ebd/ebd188.htm#002),  

<span id="009">9</span> And [onyx](../ebd/ebd279.htm#004) stones, and
stones to be set for the [ephod](../ebd/ebd122.htm#008), and for the
[breastplate](../ebd/ebd064.htm#007).  

<span id="010">10</span> And every [wise](../ebd/ebd382.htm#004) hearted
among you shall come, and make all that the LORD hath commanded;  

<span id="011">11</span> The [tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003), his
[tent](../ebd/ebd361.htm#009), and his covering, his
[taches](../ebd/ebd357.htm#001), and his boards, his bars, his pillars,
and his sockets,  

<span id="012">12</span> The [ark](../ebd/ebd031.htm#000), and the
staves thereof, with the [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) seat, and the
vail of the covering,  

<span id="013">13</span> The table, and his staves, and all his vessels,
and the [shewbread](../ebd/ebd336.htm#008),  

<span id="014">14</span> The [candlestick](../ebd/ebd071.htm#001) also
for the light, and his furniture, and his lamps, with the oil for the
light,  

<span id="015">15</span> And the [incense](../ebd/ebd188.htm#002)
[altar](../ebd/ebd018.htm#006), and his staves, and the anointing oil,
and the sweet [incense](../ebd/ebd188.htm#002), and the
[hanging](../ebd/ebd163.htm#001) for the door at the entering in of the
[tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003),  

<span id="016">16</span> The [altar](../ebd/ebd018.htm#006) of burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), with his brasen
[grate](../ebd/ebd154.htm#007), his staves, and all his vessels, the
[laver](../ebd/ebd224.htm#009) and his foot,  

<span id="017">17</span> The hangings of the court, his pillars, and
their sockets, and the [hanging](../ebd/ebd163.htm#001) for the door of
the court,  

<span id="018">18</span> The pins of the
[tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003), and the pins of the court, and
their cords,  

<span id="019">19</span> The cloths of service, to do service in the
holy place, the holy garments for [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001) the
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003), and the garments of his sons, to
[minister](../ebd/ebd255.htm#007) in the priest's office.  

<span id="020">20</span> And all the
[congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) departed from the presence of
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003).  

<span id="021">21</span> And they came, every one whose heart stirred
him up, and every one whom his [spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009) made
willing, and they brought the LORD's [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001)
to the work of the [tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003) of the
[congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004), and for all his service, and for
the holy garments.  

<span id="022">22</span> And they came, both men and women, as many as
were willing hearted, and brought bracelets, and
[earrings](../ebd/ebd110.htm#007), and rings, and tablets, all jewels of
[gold](../ebd/ebd152.htm#001): and every man that offered offered an
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of [gold](../ebd/ebd152.htm#001) unto
the LORD.  

<span id="023">23</span> And every man, with whom was found
[blue](../ebd/ebd061.htm#003), and purple, and
[scarlet](../ebd/ebd323.htm#005), and fine
[linen](../ebd/ebd229.htm#008), and goats' hair, and red skins of rams,
and badgers' skins, brought them.  

<span id="024">24</span> Every one that did offer an
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of [silver](../ebd/ebd344.htm#000) and
[brass](../ebd/ebd064.htm#003) brought the LORD's
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001): and every man, with whom was found
[shittim](../ebd/ebd339.htm#009) [wood](../ebd/ebd383.htm#003) for any
work of the service, brought it.  

<span id="025">25</span> And all the women that were
[wise](../ebd/ebd382.htm#004) hearted did spin with their hands, and
brought that which they had spun, both of [blue](../ebd/ebd061.htm#003),
and of purple, and of [scarlet](../ebd/ebd323.htm#005), and of fine
[linen](../ebd/ebd229.htm#008).  

<span id="026">26</span> And all the women whose heart stirred them up
in wisdom spun goats' hair.  

<span id="027">27</span> And the rulers brought
[onyx](../ebd/ebd279.htm#004) stones, and stones to be set, for the
[ephod](../ebd/ebd122.htm#008), and for the
[breastplate](../ebd/ebd064.htm#007);  

<span id="028">28</span> And spice, and oil for the light, and for the
anointing oil, and for the sweet [incense](../ebd/ebd188.htm#002).  

<span id="029">29</span> The children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000)
brought a willing [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) unto the LORD, every
man and woman, whose heart made them willing to bring for all manner of
work, which the LORD had commanded to be made by the hand of
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003).  

<span id="030">30</span> And [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) said unto
the children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), See, the LORD hath
called by name [Bezaleel](../ebd/ebd057.htm#009) the son of Uri, the son
of [Hur](../ebd/ebd184.htm#009), of the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004)
of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007);  

<span id="031">31</span> And he hath filled him with the
[spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009) of God, in wisdom, in understanding, and
in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship;  

<span id="032">32</span> And to devise curious works, to work in
[gold](../ebd/ebd152.htm#001), and in [silver](../ebd/ebd344.htm#000),
and in [brass](../ebd/ebd064.htm#003),  

<span id="033">33</span> And in the [cutting](../ebd/ebd095.htm#000) of
stones, to set them, and in carving of [wood](../ebd/ebd383.htm#003), to
make any manner of cunning work.  

<span id="034">34</span> And he hath put in his heart that he may teach,
both he, and [Aholiab](../ebd/ebd015.htm#006), the son of Ahisamach, of
the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of [Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009).  

<span id="035">35</span> Them hath he filled with wisdom of heart, to
work all manner of work, of the [engraver](../ebd/ebd121.htm#001), and
of the cunning workman, and of the embroiderer, in
[blue](../ebd/ebd061.htm#003), and in purple, in
[scarlet](../ebd/ebd323.htm#005), and in fine
[linen](../ebd/ebd229.htm#008), and of the weaver, even of them that do
any work, and of those that devise cunning work.
