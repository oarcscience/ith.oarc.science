### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 128

<span id="001">1</span> Blessed is every one that feareth the LORD; that
walketh in his ways.  

<span id="002">2</span> For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands:
happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.  

<span id="003">3</span> Thy wife shall be as a fruitful
[vine](../ebd/ebd377.htm#004) by the sides of thine house: thy children
like [olive](../ebd/ebd277.htm#009) plants round about thy table.  

<span id="004">4</span> Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that
feareth the LORD.  

<span id="005">5</span> The LORD shall [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008)
thee out of [Zion](../ebd/ebd394.htm#002): and thou shalt see the good
of [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005) all the days of thy life.  

<span id="006">6</span> Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children, and
peace upon [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).
