### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 70

<span id="001">1</span> MAKE HASTE, O GOD, TO DELIVER ME; MAKE HASTE TO
HELP ME, O LORD.  

<span id="002">2</span> Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek
after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that
desire my hurt.  

<span id="003">3</span> Let them be turned back for a reward of their
shame that say, [Aha](../ebd/ebd013.htm#001),
[aha](../ebd/ebd013.htm#001).  

<span id="004">4</span> Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad
in thee: and let such as love thy [salvation](../ebd/ebd320.htm#003) say
continually, Let God be magnified.  

<span id="005">5</span> But I am [poor](../ebd/ebd298.htm#006) and
needy: make haste unto me, O God: thou art my help and my deliverer; O
LORD, make no tarrying.
