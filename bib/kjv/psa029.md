### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 29

<span id="001">1</span> Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the
LORD [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006) and strength.  

<span id="002">2</span> Give unto the LORD the
[glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006) due unto his name;
[worship](../ebd/ebd384.htm#002) the LORD in the beauty of
[holiness](../ebd/ebd180.htm#006).  

<span id="003">3</span> The voice of the LORD is upon the waters: the
God of [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006) thundereth: the LORD is upon many
waters.  

<span id="004">4</span> The voice of the LORD is powerful; the voice of
the LORD is full of majesty.  

<span id="005">5</span> The voice of the LORD breaketh the cedars; yea,
the LORD breaketh the cedars of [Lebanon](../ebd/ebd226.htm#001).  

<span id="006">6</span> He maketh them also to skip like a
[calf](../ebd/ebd069.htm#004); [Lebanon](../ebd/ebd226.htm#001) and
[Sirion](../ebd/ebd345.htm#006) like a young
[unicorn](../ebd/ebd374.htm#007).  

<span id="007">7</span> The voice of the LORD divideth the flames of
fire.  

<span id="008">8</span> The voice of the LORD shaketh the
[wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005); the LORD shaketh the
[wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005) of
[Kadesh](../ebd/ebd215.htm#003).  

<span id="009">9</span> The voice of the LORD maketh the hinds to calve,
and discovereth the forests: and in his [temple](../ebd/ebd361.htm#004)
doth every one speak of his [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006).  

<span id="010">10</span> The LORD sitteth upon the
[flood](../ebd/ebd136.htm#000); yea, the LORD sitteth
[King](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) for ever.  

<span id="011">11</span> The LORD will give strength unto his people;
the LORD will [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008) his people with peace.
