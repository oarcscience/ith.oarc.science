### King James Version: Numbers Chapter 26

<span id="001">1</span> And it came to pass after the
[plague](../ebd/ebd297.htm#001), that the LORD spake unto
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and unto [Eleazar](../ebd/ebd115.htm#000)
the son of [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001) the
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003), saying,  

<span id="002">2</span> Take the sum of all the
[congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), from twenty years old and upward,
throughout their fathers' house, all that are able to go to war in
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).  

<span id="003">3</span> And [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and
[Eleazar](../ebd/ebd115.htm#000) the [priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003)
spake with them in the plains of [Moab](../ebd/ebd258.htm#005) by
[Jordan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#004) near [Jericho](../ebd/ebd203.htm#008),
saying,  

<span id="004">4</span> Take the sum of the people, from twenty years
old and upward; as the LORD commanded [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and
the children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), which went forth out of
the land of [Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009).  

<span id="005">5</span> [Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009), the eldest son
of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000): the children of
[Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009); Hanoch, of whom cometh the family of
the Hanochites: of [Pallu](../ebd/ebd283.htm#000), the family of the
Palluites:  

<span id="006">6</span> Of [Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007), the family
of the Hezronites: of [Carmi](../ebd/ebd072.htm#007), the family of the
Carmites.  

<span id="007">7</span> These are the families of the Reubenites: and
they that were numbered of them were forty and three thousand and
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) hundred and thirty.  

<span id="008">8</span> And the sons of [Pallu](../ebd/ebd283.htm#000);
[Eliab](../ebd/ebd115.htm#008).  

<span id="009">9</span> And the sons of [Eliab](../ebd/ebd115.htm#008);
[Nemuel](../ebd/ebd270.htm#004), and [Dathan](../ebd/ebd098.htm#002),
and [Abiram](../ebd/ebd004.htm#003). This is that
[Dathan](../ebd/ebd098.htm#002) and [Abiram](../ebd/ebd004.htm#003),
which were famous in the [congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004), who
strove against [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and against
[Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001) in the company of
[Korah](../ebd/ebd222.htm#003), when they strove against the LORD:  

<span id="010">10</span> And the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed
them up together with [Korah](../ebd/ebd222.htm#003), when that company
died, what time the fire devoured two hundred and fifty men: and they
became a sign.  

<span id="011">11</span> Notwithstanding the children of
[Korah](../ebd/ebd222.htm#003) died not.  

<span id="012">12</span> The sons of [Simeon](../ebd/ebd344.htm#002)
after their families: of [Nemuel](../ebd/ebd270.htm#004), the family of
the Nemuelites: of Jamin, the family of the Jaminites: of
[Jachin](../ebd/ebd194.htm#004), the family of the Jachinites:  

<span id="013">13</span> Of [Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001), the family
of the Zarhites: of Shaul, the family of the Shaulites.  

<span id="014">14</span> These are the families of the Simeonites,
twenty and two thousand and two hundred.  

<span id="015">15</span> The children of [Gad](../ebd/ebd140.htm#007)
after their families: of Zephon, the family of the Zephonites: of Haggi,
the family of the Haggites: of Shuni, the family of the Shunites:  

<span id="016">16</span> Of [Ozni](../ebd/ebd282.htm#001), the family of
the Oznites: of Eri, the family of the Erites:  

<span id="017">17</span> Of Arod, the family of the Arodites: of Areli,
the family of the Arelites.  

<span id="018">18</span> These are the families of the children of
[Gad](../ebd/ebd140.htm#007) according to those that were numbered of
them, forty thousand and five hundred.  

<span id="019">19</span> The sons of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) were
Er and [Onan](../ebd/ebd278.htm#008): and Er and
[Onan](../ebd/ebd278.htm#008) died in the land of
[Canaan](../ebd/ebd070.htm#005).  

<span id="020">20</span> And the sons of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007)
after their families were; of [Shelah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#000), the
family of the Shelanites: of [Pharez](../ebd/ebd292.htm#007), the family
of the Pharzites: of [Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001), the family of the
Zarhites.  

<span id="021">21</span> And the sons of [Pharez](../ebd/ebd292.htm#007)
were; of [Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007), the family of the Hezronites:
of [Hamul](../ebd/ebd161.htm#008), the family of the Hamulites.  

<span id="022">22</span> These are the families of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) according to those that were numbered of
them, threescore and sixteen thousand and five hundred.  

<span id="023">23</span> Of the sons of
[Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002) after their families: of
[Tola](../ebd/ebd369.htm#007), the family of the
[Tolaites](../ebd/ebd369.htm#009): of Pua, the family of the Punites:  

<span id="024">24</span> Of [Jashub](../ebd/ebd198.htm#001), the family
of the Jashubites: of [Shimron](../ebd/ebd338.htm#008), the family of
the Shimronites.  

<span id="025">25</span> These are the families of
[Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002) according to those that were numbered
of them, threescore and four thousand and three hundred.  

<span id="026">26</span> Of the sons of [Zebulun](../ebd/ebd389.htm#003)
after their families: of [Sered](../ebd/ebd328.htm#005), the family of
the Sardites: of [Elon](../ebd/ebd119.htm#000), the family of the
Elonites: of Jahleel, the family of the Jahleelites.  

<span id="027">27</span> These are the families of the Zebulunites
according to those that were numbered of them, threescore thousand and
five hundred.  

<span id="028">28</span> The sons of [Joseph](../ebd/ebd211.htm#005)
after their families were [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002) and
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000).  

<span id="029">29</span> Of the sons of
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002): of [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006),
the family of the Machirites: and [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) begat
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009): of [Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009) come
the family of the Gileadites.  

<span id="030">30</span> These are the sons of
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009): of Jeezer, the family of the
Jeezerites: of [Helek](../ebd/ebd172.htm#005), the family of the
Helekites:  

<span id="031">31</span> And of Asriel, the family of the Asrielites:
and of [Shechem](../ebd/ebd333.htm#003), the family of the
Shechemites:  

<span id="032">32</span> And of Shemida, the family of the Shemidaites:
and of [Hepher](../ebd/ebd174.htm#006), the family of the Hepherites.  

<span id="033">33</span> And [Zelophehad](../ebd/ebd390.htm#002) the son
of [Hepher](../ebd/ebd174.htm#006) had no sons, but daughters: and the
names of the daughters of [Zelophehad](../ebd/ebd390.htm#002) were
[Mahlah](../ebd/ebd238.htm#000), and [Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002),
[Hoglah](../ebd/ebd180.htm#003), Milcah, and Tirzah.  

<span id="034">34</span> These are the families of
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002), and those that were numbered of them,
fifty and two thousand and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) hundred.  

<span id="035">35</span> These are the sons of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) after their families: of Shuthelah, the
family of the Shuthalhites: of [Becher](../ebd/ebd049.htm#005), the
family of the Bachrites: of Tahan, the family of the Tahanites.  

<span id="036">36</span> And these are the sons of Shuthelah: of Eran,
the family of the Eranites.  

<span id="037">37</span> These are the families of the sons of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) according to those that were numbered
of them, thirty and two thousand and five hundred. These are the sons of
[Joseph](../ebd/ebd211.htm#005) after their families.  

<span id="038">38</span> The sons of [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008)
after their families: of [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006), the family of
the Belaites: of Ashbel, the family of the Ashbelites: of Ahiram, the
family of the Ahiramites:  

<span id="039">39</span> Of Shupham, the family of the Shuphamites: of
Hupham, the family of the Huphamites.  

<span id="040">40</span> And the sons of [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006)
were [Ard](../ebd/ebd029.htm#008) and [Naaman](../ebd/ebd263.htm#009):
of [Ard](../ebd/ebd029.htm#008), the family of the Ardites: and of
[Naaman](../ebd/ebd263.htm#009), the family of the Naamites.  

<span id="041">41</span> These are the sons of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008) after their families: and they that
were numbered of them were forty and five thousand and six hundred.  

<span id="042">42</span> These are the sons of
[Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009) after their families: of Shuham, the family
of the Shuhamites. These are the families of
[Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009) after their families.  

<span id="043">43</span> All the families of the Shuhamites, according
to those that were numbered of them, were threescore and four thousand
and four hundred.  

<span id="044">44</span> Of the children of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008) after their families: of Jimna, the
family of the Jimnites: of Jesui, the family of the Jesuites: of
[Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005), the family of the Beriites.  

<span id="045">45</span> Of the sons of [Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005):
of [Heber](../ebd/ebd170.htm#008), the family of the Heberites: of
Malchiel, the family of the Malchielites.  

<span id="046">46</span> And the name of the daughter of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008) was [Sarah](../ebd/ebd322.htm#003).  

<span id="047">47</span> These are the families of the sons of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008) according to those that were numbered of
them; who were fifty and three thousand and four hundred.  

<span id="048">48</span> Of the sons of
[Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006) after their families: of
[Jahzeel](../ebd/ebd195.htm#008), the family of the Jahzeelites: of
Guni, the family of the Gunites:  

<span id="049">49</span> Of Jezer, the family of the Jezerites: of
Shillem, the family of the Shillemites.  

<span id="050">50</span> These are the families of
[Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006) according to their families: and they
that were numbered of them were forty and five thousand and four
hundred.  

<span id="051">51</span> These were the numbered of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), six hundred thousand and a thousand
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) hundred and thirty.  

<span id="052">52</span> And the LORD spake unto
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003), saying,  

<span id="053">53</span> Unto these the land shall be divided for an
inheritance according to the number of names.  

<span id="054">54</span> To many thou shalt give the more inheritance,
and to few thou shalt give the less inheritance: to every one shall his
inheritance be given according to those that were numbered of him.  

<span id="055">55</span> Notwithstanding the land shall be divided by
lot: according to the names of the tribes of their fathers they shall
inherit.  

<span id="056">56</span> According to the lot shall the possession
thereof be divided between many and few.  

<span id="057">57</span> And these are they that were numbered of the
Levites after their families: of [Gershon](../ebd/ebd146.htm#009), the
family of the Gershonites: of [Kohath](../ebd/ebd222.htm#001), the
family of the [Kohathites](../ebd/ebd222.htm#002): of
[Merari](../ebd/ebd249.htm#000), the family of the
[Merarites](../ebd/ebd249.htm#001).  

<span id="058">58</span> These are the families of the Levites: the
family of the Libnites, the family of the Hebronites, the family of the
Mahlites, the family of the Mushites, the family of the Korathites. And
[Kohath](../ebd/ebd222.htm#001) begat [Amram](../ebd/ebd022.htm#001).  

<span id="059">59</span> And the name of Amram's wife was
[Jochebed](../ebd/ebd208.htm#004), the daughter of
[Levi](../ebd/ebd227.htm#006), whom her mother bare to
[Levi](../ebd/ebd227.htm#006) in [Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009): and she
bare unto [Amram](../ebd/ebd022.htm#001) [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001)
and [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003), and [Miriam](../ebd/ebd256.htm#003)
their sister.  

<span id="060">60</span> And unto [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001) was
born [Nadab](../ebd/ebd264.htm#008), and [Abihu](../ebd/ebd003.htm#004),
[Eleazar](../ebd/ebd115.htm#000), and
[Ithamar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#005).  

<span id="061">61</span> And [Nadab](../ebd/ebd264.htm#008) and
[Abihu](../ebd/ebd003.htm#004) died, when they offered strange fire
before the LORD.  

<span id="062">62</span> And those that were numbered of them were
twenty and three thousand, all males from a month old and upward: for
they were not numbered among the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), because there was no inheritance given
them among the children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).  

<span id="063">63</span> These are they that were numbered by
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and [Eleazar](../ebd/ebd115.htm#000) the
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003), who numbered the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) in the plains of
[Moab](../ebd/ebd258.htm#005) by [Jordan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#004) near
[Jericho](../ebd/ebd203.htm#008).  

<span id="064">64</span> But among these there was not a man of them
whom [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001)
the [priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003) numbered, when they numbered the
children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) in the
[wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005) of [Sinai](../ebd/ebd344.htm#009).  

<span id="065">65</span> For the LORD had said of them, They shall
surely die in the [wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005). And there was not
left a man of them, save [Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003) the son of
[Jephunneh](../ebd/ebd203.htm#004), and [Joshua](../ebd/ebd211.htm#006)
the son of [Nun](../ebd/ebd275.htm#006).
