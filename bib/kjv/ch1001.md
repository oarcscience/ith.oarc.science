### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 1

<span id="001">1</span> [Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007),
[Sheth](../ebd/ebd336.htm#004), Enosh,  

<span id="002">2</span> Kenan, [Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001),
Jered,  

<span id="003">3</span> [Henoch](../ebd/ebd174.htm#005),
[Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009), [Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004),  

<span id="004">4</span> [Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002),
[Shem](../ebd/ebd334.htm#002), [Ham](../ebd/ebd160.htm#003), and
[Japheth](../ebd/ebd197.htm#002).  

<span id="005">5</span> The sons of [Japheth](../ebd/ebd197.htm#002);
[Gomer](../ebd/ebd152.htm#006), and [Magog](../ebd/ebd236.htm#009), and
[Madai](../ebd/ebd235.htm#008), and [Javan](../ebd/ebd198.htm#005), and
[Tubal](../ebd/ebd373.htm#005), and [Meshech](../ebd/ebd250.htm#008),
and [Tiras](../ebd/ebd367.htm#008).  

<span id="006">6</span> And the sons of [Gomer](../ebd/ebd152.htm#006);
Ashchenaz, and [Riphath](../ebd/ebd313.htm#008), and
[Togarmah](../ebd/ebd369.htm#004).  

<span id="007">7</span> And the sons of [Javan](../ebd/ebd198.htm#005);
[Elishah](../ebd/ebd118.htm#000), and [Tarshish](../ebd/ebd359.htm#002),
[Kittim](../ebd/ebd221.htm#004), and [Dodanim](../ebd/ebd105.htm#004).  

<span id="008">8</span> The sons of [Ham](../ebd/ebd160.htm#003);
[Cush](../ebd/ebd094.htm#005), and [Mizraim](../ebd/ebd258.htm#002),
Put, and [Canaan](../ebd/ebd070.htm#005).  

<span id="009">9</span> And the sons of [Cush](../ebd/ebd094.htm#005);
[Seba](../ebd/ebd325.htm#004), and [Havilah](../ebd/ebd167.htm#009), and
Sabta, and [Raamah](../ebd/ebd304.htm#002), and
[Sabtecha](../ebd/ebd317.htm#008). And the sons of
[Raamah](../ebd/ebd304.htm#002); [Sheba](../ebd/ebd332.htm#006), and
[Dedan](../ebd/ebd100.htm#005).  

<span id="010">10</span> And [Cush](../ebd/ebd094.htm#005) begat
[Nimrod](../ebd/ebd273.htm#004): he began to be mighty upon the earth.  

<span id="011">11</span> And [Mizraim](../ebd/ebd258.htm#002) begat
[Ludim](../ebd/ebd233.htm#000), and [Anamim](../ebd/ebd022.htm#007), and
Lehabim, and [Naphtuhim](../ebd/ebd266.htm#009),  

<span id="012">12</span> And Pathrusim, and
[Casluhim](../ebd/ebd073.htm#005), (of whom came the
[Philistines](../ebd/ebd294.htm#002),) and Caphthorim.  

<span id="013">13</span> And [Canaan](../ebd/ebd070.htm#005) begat
[Zidon](../ebd/ebd393.htm#000) his firstborn, and
[Heth](../ebd/ebd177.htm#001),  

<span id="014">14</span> The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the
[Girgashite](../ebd/ebd149.htm#006),  

<span id="015">15</span> And the Hivite, and the
[Arkite](../ebd/ebd031.htm#001), and the
[Sinite](../ebd/ebd345.htm#002),  

<span id="016">16</span> And the Arvadite, and the
[Zemarite](../ebd/ebd390.htm#005), and the Hamathite.  

<span id="017">17</span> The sons of [Shem](../ebd/ebd334.htm#002);
[Elam](../ebd/ebd114.htm#003), and [Asshur](../ebd/ebd035.htm#002), and
[Arphaxad](../ebd/ebd032.htm#004), and [Lud](../ebd/ebd232.htm#009), and
[Aram](../ebd/ebd028.htm#001), and [Uz](../ebd/ebd375.htm#007), and
[Hul](../ebd/ebd184.htm#004), and Gether, and
[Meshech](../ebd/ebd250.htm#008).  

<span id="018">18</span> And [Arphaxad](../ebd/ebd032.htm#004) begat
[Shelah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#000), and [Shelah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#000)
begat [Eber](../ebd/ebd112.htm#001).  

<span id="019">19</span> And unto [Eber](../ebd/ebd112.htm#001) were
born two sons: the name of the one was [Peleg](../ebd/ebd288.htm#007);
because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name was
[Joktan](../ebd/ebd210.htm#004).  

<span id="020">20</span> And [Joktan](../ebd/ebd210.htm#004) begat
[Almodad](../ebd/ebd017.htm#009), and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and
Jerah,  

<span id="021">21</span> [Hadoram](../ebd/ebd158.htm#001) also, and
[Uzal](../ebd/ebd375.htm#009), and Diklah,  

<span id="022">22</span> And [Ebal](../ebd/ebd111.htm#007), and
[Abimael](../ebd/ebd003.htm#009), and [Sheba](../ebd/ebd332.htm#006),  

<span id="023">23</span> And [Ophir](../ebd/ebd279.htm#007), and
[Havilah](../ebd/ebd167.htm#009), and [Jobab](../ebd/ebd208.htm#003).
All these were the sons of [Joktan](../ebd/ebd210.htm#004).  

<span id="024">24</span> [Shem](../ebd/ebd334.htm#002),
[Arphaxad](../ebd/ebd032.htm#004), [Shelah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#000),  

<span id="025">25</span> [Eber](../ebd/ebd112.htm#001),
[Peleg](../ebd/ebd288.htm#007), Reu,  

<span id="026">26</span> [Serug](../ebd/ebd329.htm#001),
[Nahor](../ebd/ebd265.htm#006), [Terah](../ebd/ebd362.htm#001),  

<span id="027">27</span> [Abram](../ebd/ebd005.htm#004); the same is
[Abraham](../ebd/ebd005.htm#005).  

<span id="028">28</span> The sons of [Abraham](../ebd/ebd005.htm#005);
[Isaac](../ebd/ebd189.htm#006), and [Ishmael](../ebd/ebd190.htm#005).  

<span id="029">29</span> These are their generations: The firstborn of
[Ishmael](../ebd/ebd190.htm#005), [Nebaioth](../ebd/ebd268.htm#001);
then [Kedar](../ebd/ebd216.htm#003), and
[Adbeel](../ebd/ebd008.htm#003), and [Mibsam](../ebd/ebd252.htm#003),  

<span id="030">30</span> [Mishma](../ebd/ebd257.htm#000), and
[Dumah](../ebd/ebd109.htm#001), [Massa](../ebd/ebd243.htm#005),
[Hadad](../ebd/ebd157.htm#000), and [Tema](../ebd/ebd361.htm#000),  

<span id="031">31</span> [Jetur](../ebd/ebd206.htm#001),
[Naphish](../ebd/ebd266.htm#005), and [Kedemah](../ebd/ebd216.htm#004).
These are the sons of [Ishmael](../ebd/ebd190.htm#005).  

<span id="032">32</span> Now the sons of
[Keturah](../ebd/ebd218.htm#001), Abraham's
[concubine](../ebd/ebd087.htm#007): she bare
[Zimran](../ebd/ebd393.htm#008), and [Jokshan](../ebd/ebd210.htm#003),
and [Medan](../ebd/ebd245.htm#008), and [Midian](../ebd/ebd253.htm#007),
and [Ishbak](../ebd/ebd190.htm#001), and [Shuah](../ebd/ebd341.htm#006).
And the sons of [Jokshan](../ebd/ebd210.htm#003);
[Sheba](../ebd/ebd332.htm#006), and [Dedan](../ebd/ebd100.htm#005).  

<span id="033">33</span> And the sons of
[Midian](../ebd/ebd253.htm#007); [Ephah](../ebd/ebd122.htm#003), and
[Epher](../ebd/ebd122.htm#004), and [Henoch](../ebd/ebd174.htm#005), and
[Abida](../ebd/ebd002.htm#007), and Eldaah. All these are the sons of
[Keturah](../ebd/ebd218.htm#001).  

<span id="034">34</span> And [Abraham](../ebd/ebd005.htm#005) begat
[Isaac](../ebd/ebd189.htm#006). The sons of
[Isaac](../ebd/ebd189.htm#006); [Esau](../ebd/ebd124.htm#005) and
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).  

<span id="035">35</span> The sons of [Esau](../ebd/ebd124.htm#005);
[Eliphaz](../ebd/ebd117.htm#005), [Reuel](../ebd/ebd312.htm#001), and
[Jeush](../ebd/ebd206.htm#003), and [Jaalam](../ebd/ebd192.htm#006), and
[Korah](../ebd/ebd222.htm#003).  

<span id="036">36</span> The sons of [Eliphaz](../ebd/ebd117.htm#005);
[Teman](../ebd/ebd361.htm#001), and [Omar](../ebd/ebd278.htm#003),
Zephi, and Gatam, [Kenaz](../ebd/ebd217.htm#003), and Timna, and
[Amalek](../ebd/ebd018.htm#009).  

<span id="037">37</span> The sons of [Reuel](../ebd/ebd312.htm#001);
[Nahath](../ebd/ebd265.htm#004), [Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001),
[Shammah](../ebd/ebd331.htm#002), and [Mizzah](../ebd/ebd258.htm#003).  

<span id="038">38</span> And the sons of [Seir](../ebd/ebd326.htm#004);
[Lotan](../ebd/ebd232.htm#003), and [Shobal](../ebd/ebd340.htm#004), and
[Zibeon](../ebd/ebd392.htm#004), and [Anah](../ebd/ebd022.htm#004), and
Dishon, and Ezar, and [Dishan](../ebd/ebd104.htm#005).  

<span id="039">39</span> And the sons of [Lotan](../ebd/ebd232.htm#003);
Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan's sister.  

<span id="040">40</span> The sons of [Shobal](../ebd/ebd340.htm#004);
Alian, and Manahath, and [Ebal](../ebd/ebd111.htm#007), Shephi, and
Onam. And the sons of [Zibeon](../ebd/ebd392.htm#004); Aiah, and
[Anah](../ebd/ebd022.htm#004).  

<span id="041">41</span> The sons of [Anah](../ebd/ebd022.htm#004);
Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; [Amram](../ebd/ebd022.htm#001), and
Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.  

<span id="042">42</span> The sons of [Ezer](../ebd/ebd129.htm#004);
Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of
[Dishan](../ebd/ebd104.htm#005); [Uz](../ebd/ebd375.htm#007), and
[Aran](../ebd/ebd028.htm#004).  

<span id="043">43</span> Now these are the kings that reigned in the
land of [Edom](../ebd/ebd113.htm#001) before any
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) reigned over the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000); [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006) the son
of [Beor](../ebd/ebd052.htm#009): and the name of his city was
[Dinhabah](../ebd/ebd104.htm#000).  

<span id="044">44</span> And when [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006) was
dead, [Jobab](../ebd/ebd208.htm#003) the son of
[Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001) of [Bozrah](../ebd/ebd063.htm#009)
reigned in his stead.  

<span id="045">45</span> And when [Jobab](../ebd/ebd208.htm#003) was
dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.  

<span id="046">46</span> And when Husham was dead,
[Hadad](../ebd/ebd157.htm#000) the son of Bedad, which smote
[Midian](../ebd/ebd253.htm#007) in the field of
[Moab](../ebd/ebd258.htm#005), reigned in his stead: and the name of his
city was Avith.  

<span id="047">47</span> And when [Hadad](../ebd/ebd157.htm#000) was
dead, Samlah of [Masrekah](../ebd/ebd243.htm#004) reigned in his
stead.  

<span id="048">48</span> And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of
[Rehoboth](../ebd/ebd309.htm#008) by the river reigned in his stead.  

<span id="049">49</span> And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of
[Achbor](../ebd/ebd006.htm#007) reigned in his stead.  

<span id="050">50</span> And when Baalhanan was dead,
[Hadad](../ebd/ebd157.htm#000) reigned in his stead: and the name of his
city was Pai; and his wife's name was
[Mehetabel](../ebd/ebd246.htm#006), the daughter of Matred, the daughter
of [Mezahab](../ebd/ebd252.htm#000).  

<span id="051">51</span> [Hadad](../ebd/ebd157.htm#000) died also. And
the dukes of [Edom](../ebd/ebd113.htm#001) were;
[duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009) [Timnah](../ebd/ebd366.htm#005),
[duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009) Aliah, [duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Jetheth](../ebd/ebd205.htm#008),  

<span id="052">52</span> [Duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Aholibamah](../ebd/ebd015.htm#008), [duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Elah](../ebd/ebd114.htm#002), [duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009) Pinon,  

<span id="053">53</span> [Duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Kenaz](../ebd/ebd217.htm#003), [duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Teman](../ebd/ebd361.htm#001), [duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009)
[Mibzar](../ebd/ebd252.htm#004),  

<span id="054">54</span> [Duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009) Magdiel,
[duke](../ebd/ebd108.htm#009) [Iram](../ebd/ebd189.htm#002). These are
the dukes of [Edom](../ebd/ebd113.htm#001).
