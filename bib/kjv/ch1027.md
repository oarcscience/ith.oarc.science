### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 27

<span id="001">1</span> Now the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) after their number, to wit, the chief
fathers and captains of [thousands](../ebd/ebd364.htm#008) and hundreds,
and their officers that served the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) in any
matter of the [courses](../ebd/ebd091.htm#006), which came in and went
out month by month throughout all the months of the year, of every
course were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="002">2</span> Over the first course for the first month was
[Jashobeam](../ebd/ebd198.htm#000) the son of
[Zabdiel](../ebd/ebd385.htm#009): and in his course were twenty and four
thousand.  

<span id="003">3</span> Of the children of
[Perez](../ebd/ebd289.htm#009) was the chief of all the captains of the
[host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007) for the first month.  

<span id="004">4</span> And over the course of the second month was
[Dodai](../ebd/ebd105.htm#003) an [Ahohite](../ebd/ebd015.htm#004), and
of his course was [Mikloth](../ebd/ebd254.htm#005) also the ruler: in
his course likewise were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="005">5</span> The third captain of the
[host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007) for the third month was
[Benaiah](../ebd/ebd052.htm#003) the son of
[Jehoiada](../ebd/ebd200.htm#009), a chief
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003): and in his course were twenty and four
thousand.  

<span id="006">6</span> This is that [Benaiah](../ebd/ebd052.htm#003),
who was mighty among the thirty, and above the thirty: and in his course
was [Ammizabad](../ebd/ebd021.htm#001) his son.  

<span id="007">7</span> The fourth captain for the fourth month was
[Asahel](../ebd/ebd033.htm#001) the brother of
[Joab](../ebd/ebd207.htm#006), and [Zebadiah](../ebd/ebd388.htm#005) his
son after him: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="008">8</span> The fifth captain for the fifth month was
Shamhuth the [Izrahite](../ebd/ebd192.htm#002): and in his course were
twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="009">9</span> The sixth captain for the sixth month was
[Ira](../ebd/ebd189.htm#000) the son of Ikkesh the Tekoite: and in his
course were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="010">10</span> The seventh captain for the seventh month was
[Helez](../ebd/ebd172.htm#008) the Pelonite, of the children of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000): and in his course were twenty and four
thousand.  

<span id="011">11</span> The eighth captain for the eighth month was
[Sibbecai](../ebd/ebd342.htm#005) the Hushathite, of the Zarhites: and
in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="012">12</span> The ninth captain for the ninth month was
[Abiezer](../ebd/ebd003.htm#000) the Anetothite, of the Benjamites: and
in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

<span id="013">13</span> The tenth captain for the tenth month was
Maharai the Netophathite, of the Zarhites: and in his course were twenty
and four thousand.  

<span id="014">14</span> The eleventh captain for the eleventh month was
[Benaiah](../ebd/ebd052.htm#003) the
[Pirathonite](../ebd/ebd296.htm#003), of the children of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000): and in his course were twenty and four
thousand.  

<span id="015">15</span> The twelfth captain for the twelfth month was
[Heldai](../ebd/ebd172.htm#002) the Netophathite, of
[Othniel](../ebd/ebd281.htm#003): and in his course were twenty and four
thousand.  

<span id="016">16</span> Furthermore over the tribes of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000): the ruler of the Reubenites was
[Eliezer](../ebd/ebd116.htm#007) the son of
[Zichri](../ebd/ebd392.htm#007): of the Simeonites,
[Shephatiah](../ebd/ebd335.htm#005) the son of
[Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004):  

<span id="017">17</span> Of the Levites,
[Hashabiah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#001) the son of
[Kemuel](../ebd/ebd217.htm#001): of the
[Aaronites](../ebd/ebd000.htm#002), [Zadok](../ebd/ebd386.htm#008):  

<span id="018">18</span> Of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007),
[Elihu](../ebd/ebd116.htm#008), one of the brethren of
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004): of [Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002),
[Omri](../ebd/ebd278.htm#006) the son of
[Michael](../ebd/ebd252.htm#009):  

<span id="019">19</span> Of [Zebulun](../ebd/ebd389.htm#003),
[Ishmaiah](../ebd/ebd190.htm#006) the son of
[Obadiah](../ebd/ebd276.htm#001): of [Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006),
[Jerimoth](../ebd/ebd203.htm#009) the son of Azriel:  

<span id="020">20</span> Of the children of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000), [Hoshea](../ebd/ebd183.htm#006) the
son of [Azaziah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#007): of the half
[tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002),
[Joel](../ebd/ebd208.htm#005) the son of
[Pedaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#001):  

<span id="021">21</span> Of the half [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002) in [Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009),
[Iddo](../ebd/ebd186.htm#005) the son of
[Zechariah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#006): of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008), [Jaasiel](../ebd/ebd193.htm#000) the
son of [Abner](../ebd/ebd005.htm#002):  

<span id="022">22</span> Of [Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009), Azareel the
son of [Jeroham](../ebd/ebd204.htm#001). These were the princes of the
tribes of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).  

<span id="023">23</span> But [David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004) took not the
number of them from twenty years old and under: because the LORD had
said he would increase [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) like to the
[stars](../ebd/ebd350.htm#008) of the heavens.  

<span id="024">24</span> [Joab](../ebd/ebd207.htm#006) the son of
[Zeruiah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#009) began to number, but he finished not,
because there fell wrath for it against [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000);
neither was the number put in the account of the
[chronicles](../ebd/ebd082.htm#003) of [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004).  

<span id="025">25</span> And over the king's treasures was
[Azmaveth](../ebd/ebd038.htm#000) the son of
[Adiel](../ebd/ebd008.htm#008): and over the storehouses in the fields,
in the cities, and in the [villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003), and in the
castles, was [Jehonathan](../ebd/ebd201.htm#004) the son of
[Uzziah](../ebd/ebd376.htm#004):  

<span id="026">26</span> And over them that did the work of the field
for tillage of the ground was [Ezri](../ebd/ebd129.htm#009) the son of
Chelub:  

<span id="027">27</span> And over the vineyards was
[Shimei](../ebd/ebd338.htm#002) the [Ramathite](../ebd/ebd307.htm#000):
over the increase of the vineyards for the [wine](../ebd/ebd382.htm#000)
cellars was [Zabdi](../ebd/ebd385.htm#008) the
[Shiphmite](../ebd/ebd339.htm#004):  

<span id="028">28</span> And over the [olive](../ebd/ebd277.htm#009)
trees and the sycomore trees that were in the low plains was Baalhanan
the Gederite: and over the cellars of oil was
[Joash](../ebd/ebd208.htm#000):  

<span id="029">29</span> And over the herds that fed in
[Sharon](../ebd/ebd331.htm#009) was Shitrai the Sharonite: and over the
herds that were in the valleys was [Shaphat](../ebd/ebd331.htm#005) the
son of Adlai:  

<span id="030">30</span> Over the camels also was
[Obil](../ebd/ebd276.htm#007) the Ishmaelite: and over the asses was
[Jehdeiah](../ebd/ebd200.htm#002) the
[Meronothite](../ebd/ebd250.htm#004):  

<span id="031">31</span> And over the flocks was Jaziz the Hagerite. All
these were the rulers of the substance which was
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) David's.  

<span id="032">32</span> Also [Jonathan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#000) David's
uncle was a [counsellor](../ebd/ebd091.htm#005), a
[wise](../ebd/ebd382.htm#004) man, and a scribe: and
[Jehiel](../ebd/ebd200.htm#003) the son of Hachmoni was with the king's
sons:  

<span id="033">33</span> And [Ahithophel](../ebd/ebd015.htm#000) was the
king's [counsellor](../ebd/ebd091.htm#005): and
[Hushai](../ebd/ebd185.htm#003) the [Archite](../ebd/ebd029.htm#006) was
the king's companion:  

<span id="034">34</span> And after [Ahithophel](../ebd/ebd015.htm#000)
was [Jehoiada](../ebd/ebd200.htm#009) the son of
[Benaiah](../ebd/ebd052.htm#003), and [Abiathar](../ebd/ebd002.htm#005):
and the general of the king's army was [Joab](../ebd/ebd207.htm#006).
