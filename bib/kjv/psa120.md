### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 120

<span id="001">1</span> In my distress I cried unto the LORD, and he
heard me.  

<span id="002">2</span> Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, and
from a deceitful tongue.  

<span id="003">3</span> What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be
done unto thee, thou false tongue?  

<span id="004">4</span> Sharp [arrows](../ebd/ebd032.htm#005) of the
mighty, with coals of [juniper](../ebd/ebd214.htm#005).  

<span id="005">5</span> Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I
dwell in the tents of [Kedar](../ebd/ebd216.htm#003)!  

<span id="006">6</span> My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth
peace.  

<span id="007">7</span> I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for
war.
