### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 117

<span id="001">1</span> O praise the LORD, all ye nations: praise him,
all ye people.  

<span id="002">2</span> For his merciful kindness is great toward us:
and the truth of the LORD endureth for ever. Praise ye the LORD.
