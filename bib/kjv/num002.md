### King James Version: Numbers Chapter 2

<span id="001">1</span> And the LORD spake unto
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) and unto [Aaron](../ebd/ebd000.htm#001),
saying,  

<span id="002">2</span> Every man of the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) shall [pitch](../ebd/ebd296.htm#008) by
his own standard, with the [ensign](../ebd/ebd121.htm#008) of their
father's house: far off about the [tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003) of
the [congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) shall they
[pitch](../ebd/ebd296.htm#008).  

<span id="003">3</span> And on the [east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000) side
toward the rising of the [sun](../ebd/ebd353.htm#001) shall they of the
standard of the [camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) [pitch](../ebd/ebd296.htm#008) throughout
their armies: and [Nahshon](../ebd/ebd265.htm#007) the son of
[Amminadab](../ebd/ebd020.htm#008) shall be captain of the children of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007).  

<span id="004">4</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and those
that were numbered of them, were threescore and fourteen thousand and
six hundred.  

<span id="005">5</span> And those that do [pitch](../ebd/ebd296.htm#008)
next unto him shall be the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002): and
[Nethaneel](../ebd/ebd271.htm#003) the son of Zuar shall be captain of
the children of [Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002).  

<span id="006">6</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and those
that were numbered thereof, were fifty and four thousand and four
hundred.  

<span id="007">7</span> Then the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Zebulun](../ebd/ebd389.htm#003): and [Eliab](../ebd/ebd115.htm#008) the
son of [Helon](../ebd/ebd173.htm#005) shall be captain of the children
of [Zebulun](../ebd/ebd389.htm#003).  

<span id="008">8</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and those
that were numbered thereof, were fifty and
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) thousand and four hundred.  

<span id="009">9</span> All that were numbered in the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) were an
hundred thousand and fourscore thousand and six thousand and four
hundred, throughout their armies. These shall first set forth.  

<span id="010">10</span> On the south side shall be the standard of the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009)
according to their armies: and the captain of the children of
[Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009) shall be Elizur the son of Shedeur.  

<span id="011">11</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered thereof, were forty and six thousand and five
hundred.  

<span id="012">12</span> And those which [pitch](../ebd/ebd296.htm#008)
by him shall be the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Simeon](../ebd/ebd344.htm#002): and the captain of the children of
[Simeon](../ebd/ebd344.htm#002) shall be Shelumiel the son of
[Zurishaddai](../ebd/ebd396.htm#007).  

<span id="013">13</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were fifty and nine thousand and three
hundred.  

<span id="014">14</span> Then the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Gad](../ebd/ebd140.htm#007): and the captain of the sons of
[Gad](../ebd/ebd140.htm#007) shall be Eliasaph the son of
[Reuel](../ebd/ebd312.htm#001).  

<span id="015">15</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were forty and five thousand and six
hundred and fifty.  

<span id="016">16</span> All that were numbered in the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009) were an
hundred thousand and fifty and one thousand and four hundred and fifty,
throughout their armies. And they shall set forth in the second rank.  

<span id="017">17</span> Then the [tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003) of
the [congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) shall set forward with the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of the Levites in the midst of the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002): as they [encamp](../ebd/ebd120.htm#003),
so shall they set forward, every man in his place by their standards.  

<span id="018">18</span> On the west side shall be the standard of the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000)
according to their armies: and the captain of the sons of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) shall be
[Elishama](../ebd/ebd118.htm#001) the son of
[Ammihud](../ebd/ebd020.htm#007).  

<span id="019">19</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were forty thousand and five
hundred.  

<span id="020">20</span> And by him shall be the
[tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002): and
the captain of the children of [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002) shall
be [Gamaliel](../ebd/ebd142.htm#006) the son of
[Pedahzur](../ebd/ebd288.htm#000).  

<span id="021">21</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were thirty and two thousand and two
hundred.  

<span id="022">22</span> Then the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008): and the captain of the sons of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008) shall be
[Abidan](../ebd/ebd002.htm#008) the son of Gideoni.  

<span id="023">23</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were thirty and five thousand and four
hundred.  

<span id="024">24</span> All that were numbered of the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) were
an hundred thousand and eight thousand and an hundred, throughout their
armies. And they shall go forward in the third rank.  

<span id="025">25</span> The standard of the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009) shall be
on the north side by their armies: and the captain of the children of
[Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009) shall be [Ahiezer](../ebd/ebd013.htm#008)
the son of [Ammishaddai](../ebd/ebd021.htm#000).  

<span id="026">26</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were threescore and two thousand and
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) hundred.  

<span id="027">27</span> And those that [encamp](../ebd/ebd120.htm#003)
by him shall be the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008): and the captain of the children of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008) shall be [Pagiel](../ebd/ebd282.htm#005)
the son of Ocran.  

<span id="028">28</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were forty and one thousand and five
hundred.  

<span id="029">29</span> Then the [tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of
[Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006): and the captain of the children of
[Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006) shall be
[Ahira](../ebd/ebd014.htm#008) the son of Enan.  

<span id="030">30</span> And his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007), and
those that were numbered of them, were fifty and three thousand and four
hundred.  

<span id="031">31</span> All they that were numbered in the
[camp](../ebd/ebd070.htm#002) of [Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009) were an
hundred thousand and fifty and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) thousand
and six hundred. They shall go hindmost with their standards.  

<span id="032">32</span> These are those which were numbered of the
children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) by the house of their
fathers: all those that were numbered of the camps throughout their
hosts were six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and
fifty.  

<span id="033">33</span> But the Levites were not numbered among the
children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000); as the LORD commanded
[Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003).  

<span id="034">34</span> And the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) did according to all that the LORD
commanded [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003): so they pitched by their
standards, and so they set forward, every one after their families,
according to the house of their fathers.
