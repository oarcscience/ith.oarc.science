### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 138

<span id="001">1</span> I will praise thee with my whole heart: before
the gods will I sing praise unto thee.  

<span id="002">2</span> I will [worship](../ebd/ebd384.htm#002.md) toward
thy holy [temple](../ebd/ebd361.htm#004.md), and praise thy name for thy
lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above
all thy name.  

<span id="003">3</span> In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, and
strengthenedst me with strength in my soul.  

<span id="004">4</span> All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O
LORD, when they hear the words of thy mouth.  

<span id="005">5</span> Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the LORD:
for great is the [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006.md) of the LORD.  

<span id="006">6</span> Though the LORD be high, yet hath he respect
unto the lowly: but the proud he knoweth afar off.  

<span id="007">7</span> Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt
revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine
enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.  

<span id="008">8</span> The LORD will perfect that which concerneth me:
thy [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005.md), O LORD, endureth for ever: forsake
not the works of thine own hands.
