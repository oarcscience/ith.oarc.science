### King James Version: Nehemiah Chapter 3

<span id="001">1</span> Then [Eliashib](../ebd/ebd116.htm#003.md) the high
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003.md) rose up with his brethren the priests,
and they builded the [sheep](../ebd/ebd333.htm#005.md)
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md); they sanctified it, and set up the
[doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md) of it; even unto the tower of
[Meah](../ebd/ebd245.htm#001.md) they sanctified it, unto the tower of
[Hananeel](../ebd/ebd162.htm#002.md).  

<span id="002">2</span> And next unto him builded the men of
[Jericho](../ebd/ebd203.htm#008.md). And next to them builded
[Zaccur](../ebd/ebd386.htm#004.md) the son of Imri.  

<span id="003">3</span> But the [fish](../ebd/ebd134.htm#005.md)
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md) did the sons of Hassenaah build, who also
laid the beams thereof, and set up the [doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md)
thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.  

<span id="004">4</span> And next unto them repaired
[Meremoth](../ebd/ebd249.htm#008.md) the son of
[Urijah](../ebd/ebd375.htm#004.md), the son of
[Koz](../ebd/ebd222.htm#007.md). And next unto them repaired
[Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001.md) the son of
[Berechiah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#003.md), the son of Meshezabeel. And next
unto them repaired [Zadok](../ebd/ebd386.htm#008.md) the son of
[Baana](../ebd/ebd040.htm#006.md).  

<span id="005">5</span> And next unto them the Tekoites repaired; but
their nobles put not their necks to the work of their LORD.  

<span id="006">6</span> Moreover the old [gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md)
repaired [Jehoiada](../ebd/ebd200.htm#009.md) the son of Paseah, and
[Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001.md) the son of Besodeiah; they laid the
beams thereof, and set up the [doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md) thereof,
and the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.  

<span id="007">7</span> And next unto them repaired Melatiah the
Gibeonite, and [Jadon](../ebd/ebd195.htm#000.md) the
[Meronothite](../ebd/ebd250.htm#004.md), the men of
[Gibeon](../ebd/ebd148.htm#002), and of [Mizpah](../ebd/ebd258.htm#000.md),
unto the [throne](../ebd/ebd365.htm#001.md) of the governor on this side
the river.  

<span id="008">8</span> Next unto him repaired
[Uzziel](../ebd/ebd376.htm#005.md) the son of
[Harhaiah](../ebd/ebd164.htm#001.md), of the goldsmiths. Next unto him also
repaired [Hananiah](../ebd/ebd162.htm#004.md) the son of one of the
apothecaries, and they fortified [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005.md) unto
the broad wall.  

<span id="009">9</span> And next unto them repaired Rephaiah the son of
[Hur](../ebd/ebd184.htm#009.md), the ruler of the half part of
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005.md).  

<span id="010">10</span> And next unto them repaired
[Jedaiah](../ebd/ebd199.htm#006.md) the son of Harumaph, even over against
his house. And next unto him repaired [Hattush](../ebd/ebd167.htm#006.md)
the son of [Hashabniah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#002.md).  

<span id="011">11</span> Malchijah the son of
[Harim](../ebd/ebd164.htm#003), and [Hashub](../ebd/ebd166.htm#005.md) the
son of Pahathmoab, repaired the other piece, and the tower of the
furnaces.  

<span id="012">12</span> And next unto him repaired
[Shallum](../ebd/ebd330.htm#008.md) the son of Halohesh, the ruler of the
half part of [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005.md), he and his daughters.  

<span id="013">13</span> The [valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008.md)
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008) repaired [Hanun](../ebd/ebd163.htm#004.md),
and the inhabitants of [Zanoah](../ebd/ebd387.htm#004.md); they built it,
and set up the [doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md) thereof, the locks
thereof, and the bars thereof, and a thousand cubits on the wall unto
the [dung](../ebd/ebd109.htm#003) [gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md).  

<span id="014">14</span> But the [dung](../ebd/ebd109.htm#003.md)
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008) repaired [Malchiah](../ebd/ebd239.htm#001.md)
the son of [Rechab](../ebd/ebd308.htm#002.md), the ruler of part of
Bethhaccerem; he built it, and set up the [doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md)
thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.  

<span id="015">15</span> But the [gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md) of the
[fountain](../ebd/ebd138.htm#000.md) repaired Shallun the son of Colhozeh,
the ruler of part of [Mizpah](../ebd/ebd258.htm#000.md); he built it, and
covered it, and set up the [doors](../ebd/ebd105.htm#009.md) thereof, the
locks thereof, and the bars thereof, and the wall of the pool of Siloah
by the king's garden, and unto the stairs that go down from the city of
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004.md).  

<span id="016">16</span> After him repaired
[Nehemiah](../ebd/ebd269.htm#007.md) the son of Azbuk, the ruler of the
half part of [Bethzur](../ebd/ebd057.htm#004.md), unto the place over
against the sepulchres of [David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004.md), and to the
pool that was made, and unto the house of the mighty.  

<span id="017">17</span> After him repaired the Levites,
[Rehum](../ebd/ebd309.htm#009) the son of [Bani](../ebd/ebd043.htm#004.md).
Next unto him repaired [Hashabiah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#001.md), the ruler of
the half part of [Keilah](../ebd/ebd216.htm#009.md), in his part.  

<span id="018">18</span> After him repaired their brethren, Bavai the
son of [Henadad](../ebd/ebd174.htm#004.md), the ruler of the half part of
[Keilah](../ebd/ebd216.htm#009.md).  

<span id="019">19</span> And next to him repaired
[Ezer](../ebd/ebd129.htm#004.md) the son of
[Jeshua](../ebd/ebd205.htm#003.md), the ruler of
[Mizpah](../ebd/ebd258.htm#000.md), another piece over against the going up
to the [armoury](../ebd/ebd031.htm#008.md) at the turning of the wall.  

<span id="020">20</span> After him [Baruch](../ebd/ebd046.htm#001.md) the
son of [Zabbai](../ebd/ebd385.htm#006.md) earnestly repaired the other
piece, from the turning of the wall unto the door of the house of
[Eliashib](../ebd/ebd116.htm#003.md) the high
[priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003.md).  

<span id="021">21</span> After him repaired
[Meremoth](../ebd/ebd249.htm#008.md) the son of
[Urijah](../ebd/ebd375.htm#004) the son of [Koz](../ebd/ebd222.htm#007.md)
another piece, from the door of the house of
[Eliashib](../ebd/ebd116.htm#003.md) even to the end of the house of
[Eliashib](../ebd/ebd116.htm#003.md).  

<span id="022">22</span> And after him repaired the priests, the men of
the [plain](../ebd/ebd297.htm#002.md).  

<span id="023">23</span> After him repaired
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008) and [Hashub](../ebd/ebd166.htm#005.md)
over against their house. After him repaired
[Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005.md) the son of
[Maaseiah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#007.md) the son of
[Ananiah](../ebd/ebd023.htm#000.md) by his house.  

<span id="024">24</span> After him repaired Binnui the son of
[Henadad](../ebd/ebd174.htm#004.md) another piece, from the house of
[Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005.md) unto the turning of the wall, even unto
the corner.  

<span id="025">25</span> Palal the son of Uzai, over against the turning
of the wall, and the tower which lieth out from the king's high house,
that was by the court of the [prison](../ebd/ebd300.htm#006.md). After him
[Pedaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#001.md) the son of Parosh.  

<span id="026">26</span> Moreover the Nethinims dwelt in
[Ophel](../ebd/ebd279.htm#006.md), unto the place over against the water
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008) toward the [east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000.md),
and the tower that lieth out.  

<span id="027">27</span> After them the Tekoites repaired another piece,
over against the great tower that lieth out, even unto the wall of
[Ophel](../ebd/ebd279.htm#006.md).  

<span id="028">28</span> From above the horse
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md) repaired the priests, every one over
against his house.  

<span id="029">29</span> After them repaired
[Zadok](../ebd/ebd386.htm#008) the son of [Immer](../ebd/ebd187.htm#008.md)
over against his house. After him repaired also
[Shemaiah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#005.md) the son of
[Shechaniah](../ebd/ebd333.htm#002.md), the keeper of the
[east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000) [gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md).  

<span id="030">30</span> After him repaired
[Hananiah](../ebd/ebd162.htm#004.md) the son of
[Shelemiah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#001), and [Hanun](../ebd/ebd163.htm#004.md)
the sixth son of Zalaph, another piece. After him repaired
[Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001.md) the son of
[Berechiah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#003.md) over against his
[chamber](../ebd/ebd076.htm#003.md).  

<span id="031">31</span> After him repaired
[Malchiah](../ebd/ebd239.htm#001.md) the goldsmith's son unto the place of
the Nethinims, and of the merchants, over against the
[gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md) Miphkad, and to the going up of the
corner.  

<span id="032">32</span> And between the going up of the corner unto the
[sheep](../ebd/ebd333.htm#005) [gate](../ebd/ebd143.htm#008.md) repaired
the goldsmiths and the merchants.
