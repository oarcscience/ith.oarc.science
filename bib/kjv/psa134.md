### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 134

<span id="001">1</span> Behold, [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008.md) ye the
LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of
the LORD.  

<span id="002">2</span> Lift up your hands in the
[sanctuary](../ebd/ebd321.htm#005), and [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008.md)
the LORD.  

<span id="003">3</span> The LORD that made
[heaven](../ebd/ebd170.htm#006) and earth [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008.md)
thee out of [Zion](../ebd/ebd394.htm#002.md).
