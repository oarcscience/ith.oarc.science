### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 133

<span id="001">1</span> Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for
brethren to dwell together in unity!  

<span id="002">2</span> It is like the precious
[ointment](../ebd/ebd277.htm#007) upon the head, that ran down upon the
[beard](../ebd/ebd049.htm#000), even Aaron's
[beard](../ebd/ebd049.htm#000): that went down to the skirts of his
garments;  

<span id="003">3</span> As the [dew](../ebd/ebd102.htm#008) of
[Hermon](../ebd/ebd175.htm#006), and as the [dew](../ebd/ebd102.htm#008)
that descended upon the mountains of [Zion](../ebd/ebd394.htm#002): for
there the LORD commanded the blessing, even life for evermore.
