### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 121

<span id="001">1</span> I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from
whence cometh my help.  

<span id="002">2</span> My help cometh from the LORD, which made
[heaven](../ebd/ebd170.htm#006) and earth.  

<span id="003">3</span> He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that
keepeth thee will not slumber.  

<span id="004">4</span> Behold, he that keepeth
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) shall neither slumber nor sleep.  

<span id="005">5</span> The LORD is thy keeper: the LORD is thy shade
upon thy right hand.  

<span id="006">6</span> The [sun](../ebd/ebd353.htm#001) shall not smite
thee by day, nor the [moon](../ebd/ebd259.htm#004) by night.  

<span id="007">7</span> The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he
shall preserve thy soul.  

<span id="008">8</span> The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy
coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.
