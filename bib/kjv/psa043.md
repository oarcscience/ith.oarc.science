### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 43

<span id="001">1</span> [Judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005) me, O God, and
plead my cause against an ungodly nation: O deliver me from the
deceitful and unjust man.  

<span id="002">2</span> For thou art the God of my strength: why dost
thou cast me off? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the
enemy?  

<span id="003">3</span> O send out thy light and thy truth: let them
lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles.  

<span id="004">4</span> Then will I go unto the
[altar](../ebd/ebd018.htm#006) of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea,
upon the [harp](../ebd/ebd165.htm#001) will I praise thee, O God my
God.  

<span id="005">5</span> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art
thou disquieted within me? hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, who
is the health of my countenance, and my God.
