### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 150

<span id="001">1</span> Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his
[sanctuary](../ebd/ebd321.htm#005): praise him in the
[firmament](../ebd/ebd134.htm#000) of his power.  

<span id="002">2</span> Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him
according to his excellent greatness.  

<span id="003">3</span> Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise
him with the [psaltery](../ebd/ebd301.htm#006) and
[harp](../ebd/ebd165.htm#001).  

<span id="004">4</span> Praise him with the
[timbrel](../ebd/ebd366.htm#004) and [dance](../ebd/ebd097.htm#000):
praise him with stringed instruments and organs.  

<span id="005">5</span> Praise him upon the loud
[cymbals](../ebd/ebd095.htm#001): praise him upon the high sounding
[cymbals](../ebd/ebd095.htm#001).  

<span id="006">6</span> Let every thing that hath breath praise the
LORD. Praise ye the LORD.
