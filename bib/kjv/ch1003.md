### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 3

<span id="001">1</span> Now these were the sons of
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004), which were born unto him in
[Hebron](../ebd/ebd171.htm#004); the firstborn
[Amnon](../ebd/ebd021.htm#004), of [Ahinoam](../ebd/ebd014.htm#006) the
Jezreelitess; the second [Daniel](../ebd/ebd097.htm#001), of
[Abigail](../ebd/ebd003.htm#002) the Carmelitess:  

<span id="002">2</span> The third, [Absalom](../ebd/ebd005.htm#008) the
son of [Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004) the daughter of
[Talmai](../ebd/ebd358.htm#001) [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Geshur](../ebd/ebd147.htm#001): the fourth,
[Adonijah](../ebd/ebd009.htm#006) the son of
[Haggith](../ebd/ebd158.htm#009):  

<span id="003">3</span> The fifth, [Shephatiah](../ebd/ebd335.htm#005)
of [Abital](../ebd/ebd004.htm#008): the sixth, Ithream by
[Eglah](../ebd/ebd113.htm#006) his wife.  

<span id="004">4</span> These six were born unto him in
[Hebron](../ebd/ebd171.htm#004); and there he reigned
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) years and six months: and in
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005) he reigned thirty and three years.  

<span id="005">5</span> And these were born unto him in
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005); [Shimea](../ebd/ebd338.htm#000), and
[Shobab](../ebd/ebd340.htm#001), and [Nathan](../ebd/ebd267.htm#002),
and [Solomon](../ebd/ebd347.htm#007), four, of Bathshua the daughter of
[Ammiel](../ebd/ebd020.htm#006):  

<span id="006">6</span> [Ibhar](../ebd/ebd185.htm#008) also, and
[Elishama](../ebd/ebd118.htm#001), and
[Eliphelet](../ebd/ebd117.htm#007),  

<span id="007">7</span> And [Nogah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#008), and Nepheg,
and [Japhia](../ebd/ebd197.htm#003),  

<span id="008">8</span> And [Elishama](../ebd/ebd118.htm#001), and
[Eliada](../ebd/ebd115.htm#009), and [Eliphelet](../ebd/ebd117.htm#007),
nine.  

<span id="009">9</span> These were all the sons of
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004), beside the sons of the concubines, and
[Tamar](../ebd/ebd358.htm#003) their sister.  

<span id="010">10</span> And Solomon's son was
[Rehoboam](../ebd/ebd309.htm#007), [Abia](../ebd/ebd002.htm#002) his
son, [Asa](../ebd/ebd033.htm#000) his son,
[Jehoshaphat](../ebd/ebd201.htm#006) his son,  

<span id="011">11</span> [Joram](../ebd/ebd211.htm#003) his son,
[Ahaziah](../ebd/ebd013.htm#006) his son, [Joash](../ebd/ebd208.htm#000)
his son,  

<span id="012">12</span> [Amaziah](../ebd/ebd019.htm#007) his son,
[Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005) his son,
[Jotham](../ebd/ebd212.htm#000) his son,  

<span id="013">13</span> [Ahaz](../ebd/ebd013.htm#005) his son,
[Hezekiah](../ebd/ebd177.htm#003) his son,
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002) his son,  

<span id="014">14</span> [Amon](../ebd/ebd021.htm#005) his son,
[Josiah](../ebd/ebd211.htm#008) his son.  

<span id="015">15</span> And the sons of [Josiah](../ebd/ebd211.htm#008)
were, the firstborn [Johanan](../ebd/ebd208.htm#009), the second
[Jehoiakim](../ebd/ebd201.htm#001), the third
[Zedekiah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#008), the fourth
[Shallum](../ebd/ebd330.htm#008).  

<span id="016">16</span> And the sons of
[Jehoiakim](../ebd/ebd201.htm#001): Jeconiah his son,
[Zedekiah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#008) his son.  

<span id="017">17</span> And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir,
[Salathiel](../ebd/ebd318.htm#009) his son,  

<span id="018">18</span> Malchiram also, and
[Pedaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#001), and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and
[Nedabiah](../ebd/ebd269.htm#002).  

<span id="019">19</span> And the sons of
[Pedaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#001) were,
[Zerubbabel](../ebd/ebd391.htm#008), and
[Shimei](../ebd/ebd338.htm#002): and the sons of
[Zerubbabel](../ebd/ebd391.htm#008); [Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001),
and [Hananiah](../ebd/ebd162.htm#004), and Shelomith their sister:  

<span id="020">20</span> And [Hashubah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#006), and
[Ohel](../ebd/ebd277.htm#004), and [Berechiah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#003),
and [Hasadiah](../ebd/ebd165.htm#009), Jushabhesed, five.  

<span id="021">21</span> And the sons of
[Hananiah](../ebd/ebd162.htm#004); [Pelatiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#006),
and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of
[Obadiah](../ebd/ebd276.htm#001), the sons of
[Shechaniah](../ebd/ebd333.htm#002).  

<span id="022">22</span> And the sons of
[Shechaniah](../ebd/ebd333.htm#002); [Shemaiah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#005):
and the sons of [Shemaiah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#005);
[Hattush](../ebd/ebd167.htm#006), and Igeal, and
[Bariah](../ebd/ebd044.htm#009), and Neariah, and
[Shaphat](../ebd/ebd331.htm#005), six.  

<span id="023">23</span> And the sons of Neariah;
[Elioenai](../ebd/ebd117.htm#003), and
[Hezekiah](../ebd/ebd177.htm#003), and Azrikam, three.  

<span id="024">24</span> And the sons of
[Elioenai](../ebd/ebd117.htm#003) were, Hodaiah, and
[Eliashib](../ebd/ebd116.htm#003), and [Pelaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#005),
and [Akkub](../ebd/ebd016.htm#003), and
[Johanan](../ebd/ebd208.htm#009), and Dalaiah, and Anani,
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005).
