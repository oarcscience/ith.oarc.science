### King James Version: Hosea Chapter 3

<span id="001">1</span> Then said the LORD unto me, Go yet, love a woman
beloved of her friend, yet an adulteress, according to the love of the
LORD toward the children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), who look to
other gods, and love flagons of [wine](../ebd/ebd382.htm#000).  

<span id="002">2</span> So I bought her to me for fifteen
[pieces](../ebd/ebd295.htm#002) of [silver](../ebd/ebd344.htm#000), and
for an [homer](../ebd/ebd181.htm#000) of
[barley](../ebd/ebd045.htm#003), and an half
[homer](../ebd/ebd181.htm#000) of [barley](../ebd/ebd045.htm#003):  

<span id="003">3</span> And I said unto her, Thou shalt abide for me
many days; thou shalt not play the [harlot](../ebd/ebd164.htm#005), and
thou shalt not be for another man: so will I also be for thee.  

<span id="004">4</span> For the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) shall abide many days without a
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001), and without a
[prince](../ebd/ebd300.htm#004), and without a
[sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002), and without an image, and without an
[ephod](../ebd/ebd122.htm#008), and without
[teraphim](../ebd/ebd362.htm#002):  

<span id="005">5</span> Afterward shall the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) return, and seek the LORD their God, and
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004) their [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001); and
shall fear the LORD and his [goodness](../ebd/ebd153.htm#000) in the
latter days.
