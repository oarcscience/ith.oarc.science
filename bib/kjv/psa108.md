### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 108

<span id="001">1</span> O god, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give
praise, even with my [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006).  

<span id="002">2</span> Awake, [psaltery](../ebd/ebd301.htm#006) and
[harp](../ebd/ebd165.htm#001): I myself will awake early.  

<span id="003">3</span> I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people:
and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.  

<span id="004">4</span> For thy [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) is great
above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds.  

<span id="005">5</span> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and
thy [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006) above all the earth;  

<span id="006">6</span> That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy
right hand, and answer me.  

<span id="007">7</span> God hath spoken in his
[holiness](../ebd/ebd180.htm#006); I will rejoice, I will divide
[Shechem](../ebd/ebd333.htm#003), and mete out the
[valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008) of [Succoth](../ebd/ebd352.htm#008).  

<span id="008">8</span> [Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009) is mine;
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002) is mine;
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) also is the strength of mine head;
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) is my lawgiver;  

<span id="009">9</span> [Moab](../ebd/ebd258.htm#005) is my washpot;
over [Edom](../ebd/ebd113.htm#001) will I cast out my
[shoe](../ebd/ebd340.htm#009); over [Philistia](../ebd/ebd294.htm#001)
will I triumph.  

<span id="010">10</span> Who will bring me into the strong city? who
will lead me into [Edom](../ebd/ebd113.htm#001)?  

<span id="011">11</span> Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and
wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?  

<span id="012">12</span> Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help
of man.  

<span id="013">13</span> Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is
that shall tread down our enemies.
