### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 136

<span id="001">1</span> O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for
his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="002">2</span> O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="003">3</span> O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="004">4</span> To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="005">5</span> To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="006">6</span> To him that stretched out the earth above the
waters: for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="007">7</span> To him that made great lights: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="008">8</span> The [sun](../ebd/ebd353.htm#001) to rule by day:
for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="009">9</span> The [moon](../ebd/ebd259.htm#004) and
[stars](../ebd/ebd350.htm#008) to rule by night: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="010">10</span> To him that smote
[Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009) in their firstborn: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="011">11</span> And brought out [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000)
from among them: for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for
ever:  

<span id="012">12</span> With a strong hand, and with a stretched out
arm: for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="013">13</span> To him which divided the Red sea into parts:
for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="014">14</span> And made [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) to
pass through the midst of it: for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005)
endureth for ever:  

<span id="015">15</span> But overthrew [Pharaoh](../ebd/ebd292.htm#005)
and his [host](../ebd/ebd183.htm#007) in the Red sea: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="016">16</span> To him which led his people through the
[wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005): for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="017">17</span> To him which smote great kings: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="018">18</span> And slew famous kings: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="019">19</span> [Sihon](../ebd/ebd343.htm#002)
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of the [Amorites](../ebd/ebd021.htm#006):
for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="020">20</span> And [Og](../ebd/ebd277.htm#002) the
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of [Bashan](../ebd/ebd046.htm#003): for
his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="021">21</span> And gave their land for an heritage: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="022">22</span> Even an heritage unto
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) his servant: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="023">23</span> Who remembered us in our low estate: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever:  

<span id="024">24</span> And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) endureth for ever.  

<span id="025">25</span> Who giveth food to all
[flesh](../ebd/ebd135.htm#007): for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005)
endureth for ever.  

<span id="026">26</span> O give thanks unto the God of
[heaven](../ebd/ebd170.htm#006): for his [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005)
endureth for ever.
