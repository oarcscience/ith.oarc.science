### King James Version: Genesis Chapter 5

<span id="001">1</span> This is the book of the generations of
[Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007.md). In the day that God created man, in the
likeness of God made he him;  

<span id="002">2</span> Male and female created he them; and blessed
them, and called their name [Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007.md), in the day
when they were created.  

<span id="003">3</span> And [Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007.md) lived an
hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, and after
his image; and called his name [Seth](../ebd/ebd329.htm#003.md):  

<span id="004">4</span> And the days of [Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007.md)
after he had begotten [Seth](../ebd/ebd329.htm#003.md) were eight hundred
years: and he begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="005">5</span> And all the days that
[Adam](../ebd/ebd007.htm#007.md) lived were nine hundred and thirty years:
and he died.  

<span id="006">6</span> And [Seth](../ebd/ebd329.htm#003.md) lived an
hundred and five years, and begat [Enos](../ebd/ebd121.htm#005.md):  

<span id="007">7</span> And [Seth](../ebd/ebd329.htm#003.md) lived after he
begat [Enos](../ebd/ebd121.htm#005.md) eight hundred and
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005.md) years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="008">8</span> And all the days of
[Seth](../ebd/ebd329.htm#003.md) were nine hundred and twelve years: and he
died.  

<span id="009">9</span> And [Enos](../ebd/ebd121.htm#005.md) lived ninety
years, and begat [Cainan](../ebd/ebd068.htm#008.md):  

<span id="010">10</span> And [Enos](../ebd/ebd121.htm#005.md) lived after
he begat [Cainan](../ebd/ebd068.htm#008.md) eight hundred and fifteen
years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="011">11</span> And all the days of
[Enos](../ebd/ebd121.htm#005.md) were nine hundred and five years: and he
died.  

<span id="012">12</span> And [Cainan](../ebd/ebd068.htm#008.md) lived
seventy years and begat [Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001.md):  

<span id="013">13</span> And [Cainan](../ebd/ebd068.htm#008.md) lived after
he begat [Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001.md) eight hundred and forty
years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="014">14</span> And all the days of
[Cainan](../ebd/ebd068.htm#008.md) were nine hundred and ten years: and he
died.  

<span id="015">15</span> And [Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001.md) lived
sixty and five years, and begat [Jared](../ebd/ebd197.htm#005.md):  

<span id="016">16</span> And [Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001.md) lived
after he begat [Jared](../ebd/ebd197.htm#005.md) eight hundred and thirty
years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="017">17</span> And all the days of
[Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001.md) were eight hundred ninety and five
years: and he died.  

<span id="018">18</span> And [Jared](../ebd/ebd197.htm#005.md) lived an
hundred sixty and two years, and he begat
[Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md):  

<span id="019">19</span> And [Jared](../ebd/ebd197.htm#005.md) lived after
he begat [Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md) eight hundred years, and begat
sons and daughters:  

<span id="020">20</span> And all the days of
[Jared](../ebd/ebd197.htm#005.md) were nine hundred sixty and two years:
and he died.  

<span id="021">21</span> And [Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md) lived sixty
and five years, and begat [Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009.md):  

<span id="022">22</span> And [Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md) walked with
God after he begat [Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009.md) three hundred
years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="023">23</span> And all the days of
[Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md) were three hundred sixty and five
years:  

<span id="024">24</span> And [Enoch](../ebd/ebd121.htm#004.md) walked with
God: and he was not; for God took him.  

<span id="025">25</span> And [Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009.md) lived
an hundred eighty and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005.md) years, and begat
[Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004.md).  

<span id="026">26</span> And [Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009.md) lived
after he begat [Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004.md)
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005.md) hundred eighty and two years, and begat
sons and daughters:  

<span id="027">27</span> And all the days of
[Methuselah](../ebd/ebd251.htm#009.md) were nine hundred sixty and nine
years: and he died.  

<span id="028">28</span> And [Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004.md) lived an
hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:  

<span id="029">29</span> And he called his name
[Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002.md), saying, This same shall comfort us
concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which
the LORD hath cursed.  

<span id="030">30</span> And [Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004.md) lived after
he begat [Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002.md) five hundred ninety and five
years, and begat sons and daughters:  

<span id="031">31</span> And all the days of
[Lamech](../ebd/ebd223.htm#004) were [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005.md)
hundred seventy and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005.md) years: and he died.  

<span id="032">32</span> And [Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002.md) was five
hundred years old: and [Noah](../ebd/ebd274.htm#002.md) begat
[Shem](../ebd/ebd334.htm#002), [Ham](../ebd/ebd160.htm#003.md), and
[Japheth](../ebd/ebd197.htm#002.md).
