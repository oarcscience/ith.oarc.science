### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 82

<span id="001">1</span> God standeth in the
[congregation](../ebd/ebd088.htm#004) of the mighty; he judgeth among
the gods.  

<span id="002">2</span> How long will ye [judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005)
unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked?
[Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="003">3</span> Defend the [poor](../ebd/ebd298.htm#006) and
fatherless: do [justice](../ebd/ebd214.htm#007) to the afflicted and
needy.  

<span id="004">4</span> Deliver the [poor](../ebd/ebd298.htm#006) and
needy: rid them out of the hand of the wicked.  

<span id="005">5</span> They know not, neither will they understand;
they walk on in [darkness](../ebd/ebd097.htm#008): all the foundations
of the earth are out of course.  

<span id="006">6</span> I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are
children of the most High.  

<span id="007">7</span> But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of
the princes.  

<span id="008">8</span> Arise, O God, [judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005) the
earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations.
