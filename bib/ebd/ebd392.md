<span id="000">**Zetham**</span> Olive planter, a Levite ([Ch1
23:8](../kjv/ch1023.htm#008).md).

<span id="001">**Zethan**</span> A Benjamite ([Ch1
7:10](../kjv/ch1007.htm#010).md).

<span id="002">**Zia**</span> Fear, a Gadite ([Ch1
5:13](../kjv/ch1005.htm#013).md).

<span id="003">**Ziba**</span> Post; statue, "a servant of the house of
Saul" ([Sa2 9:2](../kjv/sa2009.htm#002).md), who informed David that
Mephibosheth, a son of Jonathan, was alive. He afterwards dealt
treacherously toward Mephibosheth, whom he slanderously misrepresented
to David.

<span id="004">**Zibeon**</span> Robber; or dyed. (1.) A Hivite ([Gen
36:2](../kjv/gen036.htm#002)). (2..md) A Horite, and son of Seir ([Gen
36:20](../kjv/gen036.htm#020).md).

<span id="005">**Zibia**</span> Gazelle, a Benjamite ([Ch1
8:9](../kjv/ch1008.htm#009).md).

<span id="006">**Zibiah**</span> The mother of King Joash ([Kg2
12:1](../kjv/kg2012.htm#001); [Ch2 24:1](../kjv/ch2024.htm#001).md).

<span id="007">**Zichri**</span> Remembered; illustrious. (1.) A
Benjamite chief ([Ch1 8:19](../kjv/ch1008.htm#019)). (2..md) Another of the
same tribe ([Ch1 8:23](../kjv/ch1008.htm#023).md).

<span id="008">**Ziddim**</span> Sides, a town of Naphtali ([Jos
19:35](../kjv/jos019.htm#035).md), has been identified with Kefr-Hattin,
the "village of the Hittites," about 5 miles west of Tiberias.

<span id="009">**Zidkijah**</span> The Lord is righteous, one who sealed
the covenant with Nehemiah ([Neh 10:1](../kjv/neh010.htm#001).md).
