<span id="000">**Hemath**</span> A Kenite ([Ch1
2:55](../kjv/ch1002.htm#055)), the father of the house of Rechab.

<span id="001">**Hemlock**</span> (1.) Heb. rosh ([Hos
10:4](../kjv/hos010.htm#004); rendered "gall" in [Deu
29:18](../kjv/deu029.htm#018); [Deu 32:32](../kjv/deu032.htm#032); [Psa
69:21](../kjv/psa069.htm#021); [Jer 9:15](../kjv/jer009.htm#015); [Jer
23:15](../kjv/jer023.htm#015); "poison," [Job
20:16](../kjv/job020.htm#016); "venom," [Deu
32:33](../kjv/deu032.htm#033)). "Rosh is the name of some poisonous
plant which grows quickly and luxuriantly; of a bitter taste, and
therefore coupled with wormwood ([Deu 29:18](../kjv/deu029.htm#018);
[Lam 3:19](../kjv/lam003.htm#019)). Hence it would seem to be not the
hemlock cicuta, nor the colocynth or wild gourd, nor lolium darnel, but
the poppy so called from its heads" (Gesenius, Lex.). (2.) Heb. la'anah
, generally rendered "wormwood" (q.v.), [Deu
29:18](../kjv/deu029.htm#018), Text 17; [Pro
5:4](../kjv/pro005.htm#004); [Jer 9:15](../kjv/jer009.htm#015); [Jer
23:15](../kjv/jer023.htm#015). Once it is rendered "hemlock" ([Amo
6:12](../kjv/amo006.htm#012); R.V., "wormwood"). This Hebrew word is
from a root meaning "to curse," hence the accursed.

<span id="002">**Hen**</span> Common in later times among the Jews in
Palestine ([Mat 23:37](../kjv/mat023.htm#037); [Luk
13:34](../kjv/luk013.htm#034)). It is noticeable that this familiar bird
is only mentioned in these passages in connection with our Lord's
lamentation over the impenitence of Jerusalem.

<span id="003">**Hena**</span> One of the cities of Mesopotamia
destroyed by sennacherib ([Kg2 18:34](../kjv/kg2018.htm#034); [Kg2
19:13](../kjv/kg2019.htm#013)). It is identified with the modern Anah,
lying on the right bank of the Euphrates, not far from Sepharvaim.

<span id="004">**Henadad**</span> Favour of Hadad, the name of a Levite
after the Captivity ([Ezr 3:9](../kjv/ezr003.htm#009)).

<span id="005">**Henoch**</span> See ENOCH.

<span id="006">**Hepher**</span> A well or stream. (1.) A royal city of
the Canaanites taken by Joshua ([Jos 12:17](../kjv/jos012.htm#017)).
(2.) The youngest son of Gilead ([Num 26:32](../kjv/num026.htm#032);
[Num 27:1](../kjv/num027.htm#001)). (3.) The second son of Asher ([Ch1
4:6](../kjv/ch1004.htm#006)). (4.) One of David's heroes ([Ch1
11:36](../kjv/ch1011.htm#036)).

<span id="007">**Hephzibah**</span> My delight is in her. (1.) The wife
of Hezekiah and mother of king Manasseh ([Kg2
21:1](../kjv/kg2021.htm#001)). (2.) A symbolical name of Zion, as
representing the Lord's favour toward her ([Isa
62:4](../kjv/isa062.htm#004)).

<span id="008">**Herb**</span> (1.) Heb. 'eseb , any green plant;
herbage ([Gen 1:11](../kjv/gen001.htm#011), [Gen
1:12](../kjv/gen001.htm#012), [Gen 1:29](../kjv/gen001.htm#029), [Gen
1:30](../kjv/gen001.htm#030); [Gen 2:5](../kjv/gen002.htm#005); [Gen
3:18](../kjv/gen003.htm#018), etc.); comprehending vegetables and all
green herbage ([Amo 7:1](../kjv/amo007.htm#001), [Amo
7:2](../kjv/amo007.htm#002)). (2.) Yarak , green; any green thing;
foliage of trees ([Kg2 19:26](../kjv/kg2019.htm#026); [Psa
37:2](../kjv/psa037.htm#002)); a plant; herb ([Deu
11:10](../kjv/deu011.htm#010)). (3.) Or, meaning "light" In [Isa
26:19](../kjv/isa026.htm#019) it means "green herbs;" in [Kg2
4:39](../kjv/kg2004.htm#039) probably the fruit of some plant. (4.)
Merorim , plural, "bitter herbs," eaten by the Israelites at the
Passover ([Exo 12:8](../kjv/exo012.htm#008); [Num
9:11](../kjv/num009.htm#011)). They were bitter plants of various sorts,
and referred symbolically to the oppression in Egypt.

<span id="009">**Herd**</span> [Gen 13:5](../kjv/gen013.htm#005); [Deu
7:14](../kjv/deu007.htm#014). (See CATTLE.)
