### CHAPTER XI.

1\. And in those days I will open the store chambers of blessing which
are in the heaven, so as to send them down ⌈upon the earth⌉ over the
work and labour of the children of men. 2. And truth and peace shall be
associated together throughout all the days of the world and throughout
all the generations **of men**.'
