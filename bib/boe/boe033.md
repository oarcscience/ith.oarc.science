### CHAPTER XXX.

1 .And beyond these, I went afar to the east, and I saw another place, a
valley (full) of water. 2. And ⌈therein there was⌉ a tree, the colour
(?) of fragrant trees such as the mastic. 3. And on the sides of those
valleys I saw fragrant cinnamon. And beyond these I proceeded to the
east.
