LXIV\. *Vision of the fallen Angels in the Place of Punishment*.

### CHAPTER LXIV.

1\. And other forms I saw hidden in that place. 2. I heard the voice of
the angel saying: 'These are the angels who descended to the earth, and
revealed what was hidden to the children of men and seduced the children
of men into committing sin.'
