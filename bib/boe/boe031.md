XXVIII-XXXIII. *Further Journey to the East*.

### CHAPTER XXVIII

1\. And thence I went ⌈⌈towards the east⌉⌉, into the midst ⌈⌈of the
mountain range of the desert⌉⌉,

<span id="page_53">p. 53</span>

and I saw a wilderness and it was solitary, full of trees **and
plant**s. 2. ⌈⌈And⌉⌉ water gushed forth from above. 3. Rushing like a
copious watercourse \[which flowed\] towards the north-west it caused
clouds and dew to ascend on every side.
