------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a partial Gothic translation of the Bible. This edition is
probably that made by the Bishop Ulfilas in the 4th century and
preserved in the Codex Argenteus. This was published in 1910 by William
Streitberg as Die Gotische Bible. This text is probably in the public
domain in the US because it presumably was published prior to 1923.
