Potawatomi Bible: Acts

1:1 NKIWSHITON ne'tum msInukIn, O Tiapinus, caye'k Cisus kaoshkawe'pwshItot, ipi e'ki kuknomake't.

1:2 Nash i e'kishkIwuk kaotapnukasot, shpumuk kakishminat okukie'kwe'wunIn shi PanakwsIt Cipam, ni nwakanacIn kaowe'napmacIn;

1:3 Nie'ni ke'iapI kawaptacIn wia’w e'pmatsIt kakish kwtukitot shi mce'sh we'we'nI te'pwe'tatwunIn e'kiwapmukot nie'waptuk tsokwun ipi e'kiactuk cake'ko Kshe'mIne'to otokumauwunuk:

1:4 Me'kwacI e'ie' maosomat okiwitmowan e'wipwa shi ocI macinIt Cinose'ne'muk e'wi pwitowat Osma kowawituk, win kakitot nin kaocI notake'ie'k.

1:5 Can we'we'nI ki kcItapie'nwe', mpik nIshcI kinwa kukcItapie'nkom, shi panakwsIt Cipam e'pwamshI mce'shkonkuk.

1:6 IcI kamaoce'tiwat okinatawawan otI kikItwuk Te'pe'nke'n ConI otI e'picak minI kuminasi Isne'iIn o kumauwun.

1:7 IcI otI okinan cosu wi kinwa e'wi ke'ntume'k pic nikan wapiamkuk win osma katot shi owishkIswunuk.

1:8 KuotItnanawa wishkIswun ipi panakwsIt Cipam kikishotIskoie'k, ipi nin kaocI witmake'm caye'k shi Cinose'ne'muk ipi caye'k Cwtie'uk, ipi shi Se'me'ne'ok ipi shi mamwI pnocak shotI kik.

1:9 Pic cI kakish iactuk notI ke'ko me'kwa e'ie' wapmawat kiwcI otapnIkaso shpumuk; ipi shi ankoton okipon wapmawan.

1:10 Me'kwacI e'ie' kanapwat ipI shpumuk; kanmIshianIt, pInI nish nInwun okiwicka pwutakwan kiwapshkianI kapiskumwacIn.

1:11 OtI cI ke'iapI okikowan, Ke'nIni tshI nInituk tacI we'cnipwoie'k shpumuk e'napie'k? OtI nasap Cisus, kaocI otapnImakoie'k shpumuk; iw nasap ke'shI piat kashu wapme'k shpumuk kashu shiat.

1:12 IcI Cinose'ne'muk kishu kiwe'k kiwcIpie'k e'pkotnianuk AnIpIt kishInkate' e'nume'kishkuk e'picak shi Cinose'ne'muk.

1:13 Pic cI kapitke'we't shpumuk kishie'k wikwamuk kaci iuwat Pitu, ipi Ce'ms, ipi can ipi E'ntnu, Pinip, ipi Tame's, Pe'toniu, ipi Me'to ‘Ce'ms kaokwIsmukot E'npiusIn, ipi Sayi mun Sinotus, ipi Cwte's, wikane'iIn Ce'ms.

1:14 KotI caye'k pIne' kimsone'ntumwuk akimimawat, ipi kimumatmok shi kiInkwe'n, ipi Me'ni kaokie'imat o Cisus ipi niw wikane'iIn

1:15 IwcI notI e'kishkiwuk Pitu KinipwI na‘iukwan ni ke'knomake'cuk (e'tshuwat cukI tInoswunwa kansoiwat nkotwaktshik, ipi nishwaptuk) otI cI kikIto;

1:16 NInituk, ipi nikane'ituk, otI kikItowIn taocI nomkuk e'wite'pwe'mkuk Te'pIt kaocI kItot ni PanakwsIncIn CipamIn e'kiiacimat nikan ni Cwte'sIn, win kapmInikansIt e'kiwtapnawat CisusIn.

1:17 Win kiwictkokcIkaso ipi o kiwtItnan anIt otI pmitake'wun

1:18 OtI nInI okikishpIne'ton ktukan iwtpu‘ ukoswun miashuwe'psiwun, ipi nikan kishI pmokocIn kitokce'shInce' na‘iukwan icI caye'k picnI kisaktakwte'nI.

1:19 Ipi otI okikuke'ntanawa, caye'k ki katacuk shi Cinose'ne'muk kikuke'ncIkate' i ktukan, e'shInkate'kw winwa kwiuk e'shumwat E'sIntime' e'tmuk i ktukan mskwI.

1:20 Osam onIpie'kate' shi msInukIn Nkumwunuk, Win e'tat kupe'pshukwsInI ipi cowIiI shi Ite'si, ipi i otukwapke'wun pe'kansInIt okinapshkamakon.

1:21 OtI cI ie'i kotI nInwuk kapmuwice'okonuk, pIne' Tope'nmukwiIkon Cisus e'ki pitike't, ipi e'kisakuk, shi e'cIiIikon.

1:22 E'we'pkcItapie'nwe't Can nash i e'kishkiwuk kaotapnmakoikon nkot okInapshkawan e'wi wicwitma ke'ikon e'ki iapsishnInIt.

1:23 Okiwe'napmawan cI Nish Cosip kishInkaso Pe'se'pe's ipi ke'iapI kashInkasot Custus, ipi Me'taye's.

1:24 IcI e'kinumawat, ipi otI kikItwuk; Kin Te'pe'nke'n, kin ke'ke'ntumwutwa, caye'k nInwuk ote'‘iwan, knomoshInak we'kwe'nshutuk kote' nkote'iI kaowe'napmItwa.

1:25 Win e'wi anIt pmitake't ipi NokantawIn win o Cwte's kaocI pmukocIk iw omaia tuwe'psiwun, win e'wishiat tpInowe' e'cIiIt.

1:26 IcI e'kitatiwat wincI Me'taye's kiw tapnuk aso ipi kitkokcIkaso ni mtatso shItnkot nwakanmIcIn.

2:1 Pic e'kishkuk PIntikast wikwtowun kaocItsak, caye'k kimsote'e'k kimaoce'‘tik mamwInkoci.

2:2 IcI ka‘w kiwcI pie'mkItnI notakwsIwun shpumuk ke'cwa e'ki cI wishkanmuk, ipi kimosh kIne'se'nI caye'k shi wikwamuk kacI cipitupwat.

2:3 Owaptanawa se'knInwaknIn ne'swakwianuk ke'cwa shkote', ipi nankot e'tshowat kite'nI wiwuwak.

2:4 Ipi caye'k okimoshkIne'shkakwan ni PankwsIt Cipam icI kiwe'pkakikitok pkan kinowe'k kashu minkowat OcipamIn.

2:5 Kite'kcI ipI CInose'ne'muk Cwiuk me'noshuwe'pse'cuk nInwuk e'tsoshumowat shotI kik kiwcIpie'k mtunmuk shpumuk.

2:6 IcI otI kanotmoat, pnoc kamaoce'ticutk kipie'maoke'tik? ipi kimamkate'ntumwut os am e'tshIt nInI okinotwawan e'kikitonIte'shumot.

2:7 Caye'kcI kimamkate'ntumwuk, ipi pkan okine'mawan otIcI kishItwuk, PInI coe'I kotI caye'k kakitocuk Ke'nIni nInwuk?

2:8 TInicI e'shnotwukon e'tshIt nInI, e'ki ie'i nowe'ikon kashu nikiiIkon.

2:9 Pe'tie'nuk, ipi Mitiuk, ine'mituk ipi ki e'tacuk shi MIsopote'miuk, ipi shi Cwtie'uk, Kpe'toshie'uk shi Pantusuk ipi E'shie'uuk,

2:10 Pnicie'uk ipi shi e'kiwuk Nipie'uk ipi ipI Sinie'nk ipi pwake'ntakwse'cuk kiw cupie'k Nom ipi Cwiuk ipi te'pwe' twacIk.

2:11 E'nitniuk, ipi E'ne'pie'niuk knotwamIn e'kikitowat e'shumie'kon o oimamkate'nta kwIwun Kshe'mIne'ton.

2:12 Ipi caye'k ki mamkate'ntumwuk ipi kikwinwune'ntumwuk otIcI kishItwuk, we'kwnicI otI we'cnom Kuk?

2:13 AnItcI okinaptotwawan kItwuk, kotIsI nInwuk Omoshkne'shkakwnawa wshkI shomnapo.

2:14 NIshcI Pitu akiwickapwutwan ni mtatso shItnkot okiknonan otI okinan; kinwa e'tshI nIniwie'k Cwtie'uk, ipi caye'k kinwa, e'taie'k Cinose'ne'muk otI kuke'ntmok, ipi psItumok notI nkikitoonIn,

2:15 Cowi kotI kiwIshkwe'pie'sik i e'ne'nme'k o samkIni nsotpukIn e'kwokishkiwuk.

2:16 OtIsi ie'i katuk nakanacimot Cowun;

2:17 OtI ke'nomkuk pic wishkwakishkiwuk kItwak Kshe'mIne'to NkIsikna ncipam kwIciukwan caye'k wias, ipi kokwIswak, ipi kitansu’wak knikanacimok, ipi ktoshInunwe'mwak kukwsaptumwuk ipi ktukwe'simawak oke'iapwatanawa iapwe'wunIn.

2:18 Ipi npumitakaimuk, ipi ntupa’nkwe'imuk nkIsiknumwak ncipamIn niw e'kishkiwuk icI knikanacimok.

2:19 Ipi nkuwaptuwe' mamkate'ntumwunIn shi shpumuk kishkok, ipi mataoshuwe'psuwun shotI kik namiukwan, mskwI, ipi shkote', ipi ke'c pkwe'ne'k.

2:20 Ipi kisIs kukiskupkoniawapkIse', ipi tpukisIs kumskwiwe' e'pwamshupiamkuk i kcIkishIk, ipi omiuk kukishkom Kshe'mIne'to.

2:21 KnomkItcI we'kwe'ntuk ke'wawitmowakwe'n otInoswun Kshe'mIne'to kuke'skona.

2:22 Isne'iuk e'tshI nIniwie'k psItmok notI kikitonIn Cisus Ne'sInI nInI kamnonwe'kot Kshe'mIne'to kamataototuk, ipi mamkate'ntumwunIn, ipi mataoshuwe'psuwunIn win Kshe'mIne'to katotuk shi wiwunuk me'kwe' nawe'nI kinwa ke'iapI kuke'ntanawa.

2:23 Win kapkItnukasot kane'nmat ipi nikan kashuke'nmat Kshe'mIne'to, kinwa kautapne'k ipi kmIconIciwan kiskakwawa ipi kinsawa.

2:24 Win Kshe'mIne'to kapsukwitmat kaiapskopitot, i wis ke'ntumwun shi upomuk, o samco otakshkumac konkosin shiw.

2:25 Osam Te'pIt otI kItwak e'ki iacimot, nikan nkiwapma Kshe'mIne'to pIne'’ e'nasmupian shiuwak nte'pniw nak, e'wi pwamatpe'ian.

2:26 IwcI ie'i nte'’ kimnowankosimkIt ipi nse'knInwakIn kinciwe'nmomkIt wusimI ke'iapI ntuwiasum kInwashmomkIt shi pkose'nmowunuk.

2:27 Osam kin, cokInkinasi ncipam shi namkumuk, coke'kin kIne'nmasi panakwsIt Nkot e'wiwaptuk mpIshknInwun.

2:28 KikInomowasha pmatse'ian kin kumoshkIne'sto’w papine'nmowun shi ktIshnakwsiwunuk.

2:29 NInituk ipi nikane'ituk nocima we'we'nI kuknonum e'witshumuk, o osma Te'pIt iw e'kinpot, ipi e'kinkokasot, ipi i ocipe'okumuk e'ci iIikon te'nI otI e'kishkiwuk.

2:30 Osam win e'iacimot ipi e'ke'ntuk Kshe'mIne'ton e'kie'tnamukot niw nicansun shiw otowiasmuk okaocI psukwitnan ni KnaystIn e'wi cipitupnIt shiw cipitupwunuk.

2:31 Win nikane'kiwaptuk okitshItomowan e'wiapsishnInIt ni KnaystIn nike', ocipamIn, cokinkIc kasin shi namkumuk coke'i otowiasum co kiwaptusin mpIshknInwun.

2:32 OtI Cisus Kshe'mIne'to kapsukwitnat ie'i kinan caye'k watmake'ikon.

2:33 Iw ie'i ote'pniwnanuk Kshe'mIne'ton kapsukwitnukot ipi kaminkoni osIn kawawitmakot ni panakwsIncIn CipamIn kanitawe't iw nkom waptume'k ipi nwatme'k.

2:34 Osam Te'pIt comshu shpumuk shiIsi nIshcI. win tpInwe' kIto, Te'pe'nke't okinan ni Te'pe'n mukwian, cipitupIn shotI nte'pniwnak.

2:35 Nash kiw shu ukwa me'iane'nmukwiIn e'wipIste'piIn.

2:36 OtI cI ie'i caye'k otu wikwamwa Isne'iIn we'we'nI okuke'ntana Kshe'mIne'to okiwshuan oie'o Cisus kinwa kaskakwoe'k ne'ne'ish Te'pe'nke't ipi Knayst.

2:37 Pic cI otI kanotmoat kiptukte'e'shke'k ipi otI okinawan ni PituIn, ipi ni anIt nwakanmIcIn NInituk ipi nikane'ituk we'kwnicI ke'totmukon.

2:38 OtI cI Pitu okinan kisate'ntmok, ipi kcItapie'nkasok e'tshIie'k shiw tInoswunuk Cisus Knayst e'wi we'pnakoie'k ni kmIiashuwe'psiwnawan icI kuminkom i mikwe'wun o panakwsIt Cipam.

2:39 Osam i wawitmake'wun, kuwawitmakom, ipi ki ktupInoce'imowak ipi ki caye'k pnoc e'icIk; ke'iapI tso Te'pe'nke't Kshe'mIne'tomnan ke'ntomakwe'n.

2:40 Ipi mce'sh pe'kankInkItwunIn, kiwitmake', ipi kikie'kwe', otI kikIto, kiskontIsok, shiw ki me'iashuwe'pse'cIk pmats cIk.

2:41 Pic cI i kanciwe'nmocuk e'kiwtapnumwawat, iw kikitowun, kikcItapie'nkaswuk icI i e'kishkiwuk, nsuk mtatswak knIpIc kitshik.

2:42 IcI kitotmok we'we'nI i nwakancuk ote'pwe'ie'ntumwunwa, ipi tpantuwun, ipi e'kipikupitowat pkwe'shkIn ipi e'numawat.

2:43 Ipi se'ksowun, okiwtuskonawa e'tshuwat cipamwan, ipi mce'sh mamkate'ntumwunIn, ipi mataototmonIn kitotmok ki nwakancuk.

2:44 Ipi caye'k ki te'pwe'ie'ntukuk mamwI kiik; otIpInoe'wsiwnawa mamawI okitupe'ne'ntanawa.

2:45 Ipi kitawe'k otIpnoe'usiwnawan, ipi otInkackInwan, icI e'ki swe'nmowawat, caye'k nInwun kashumne'snIt cak nInwun.

2:46 Ipi pIne'‘ e'tso kishkItnuk e'kimsone'ntumwuk shi kcI nume'okumkok ipi okipikupitonawa pkwe'shkIn, e'tsunuk wikwame'n ipi okimicnawa micum shi mnowankoswunuk, ipi kinkotnInI ote'‘iwa.

2:47 E'winwanawat Kshe'mIne'ton, ipi e'mnonwe'awat caye'k nIshInape'n, ipi Kshe'mIne'to okiianke'san ni te'pwe'ie'ntumnIcIn e'tsokiskItnuk; ki wake'skonkascuk.

3:1 IcI Pitu ipi Can, kiwice'twuk shpumuk kcI-nume'okumkok shaktso tpukIn e'pite'ntakwuk, pic wanumak.

3:2 IwcI ukot nInI e'konikIt co kinupmose'si kipmaocIkaso; okisawan e'tso kishkuk e'cI shkwate'mkate'nuk kcI-nume'okumuk e'shnukate'k Ke'cwunuk; e'wintotmowawat mikwe'wunIn nipatike'nIt shi kcI-nume'okumkok.

3:3 E'wapmatcI Pitu, ipi CanIn e'nmupitike'nut kcI-nume'okumkok okintotmowan mikwe'wun.

3:4 Pitu cI okikanapman, ipi Can kItwak, WapmIshnak;

3:5 IwcI okinaksowapman e'pkose'nmat ke'koe'wiminkot.

3:6 Pitu cI kIto; Lonia ipi osaw shonia, cointe'isin; nIshcI e'toian kuminIn, Otnoswunuk, Cisus Knayst, Ne'se'nIt nInI, psukwin, ipi pmose'n.

3:7 IwcI e'kiskInce'nat ote'pniciwnuk ipi okipsukwitnan iwcI papuk niwsIte'n ipi okate'n kiwishksimkItnI.

3:8 IwcI e'kipsukwit kinipwI, ipi kipmose', ipi e'nmowice'wat shi kcI-nume'okumkok, e'nmupmose't, ipi e'nmukwakwaskwse'ot, ipi e'uwinwanat Kshe'mIne'ton.

3:9 Ipi caye'k ki nIshInape'k oki wapmawan e'pmose'nIt, ipi e'uwinwananIt Kshe'mIne'ton.

3:10 Okikike'nmawancI e'ki cipitupnIt shi ke'c wuntInuk shkwate'muk kcI-nume'okumkok icI kimoshkne'shkakwnawa kimamkate'ntumwun ipi okimamkate'nmawan kashu shuwe'ntakwsInIt.

3:11 IwcI o pwanupmose't nInI kane'se'e't okimackonan PituIn ipi CanIn, caye'k ki cIshInape'k ki pItok e'ciInIt shkwate'muk e'shnukate'k Sanuman, otIshkwate'mwun kcImamkate'ntumwan.

3:12 IwcI pic Pitu kawaptuk, okiknonan ni nIshInape'n, Kinwa isne'iuk nInwuk, we'kw nicI we'cmamkate'ntume'k otI ipi we'kwni, we'ckanapmiak, ke'cwa ninan te'pinak nwishkIsiwnInan tanke', nmInoshuwe'psiw nInan kaocIkshkukIt otI nInI e'wipmose't.

3:13 Okshe'mIne'tomIn. E'pe'ne'e'm, ipi Aysik, ipi Ce'kap, ipi okshe'mIne'tomwan kosnanuk oki kcI pite'nman okwIsIm CisusIn kinwa kapkIte'n me'k, ipi kaian we'nme'k e'nasmupIt Payne't, pic kawishte'pwe't e'wipkItnat.

3:14 NIshcI kianwe'nmawa panakwsIt, Nkot ipi me'noshuwe'psIt, iwcI kintuwe'nmawa e'wipkIte'ntmakoie'k.

3:15 Okanshuwe't kintonawa okuma pmatsowun Kshe'mInato kapsukwItnuk shi npoonuk ninan iwe'cwitmake'iak

3:16 Ipi otInoswunuk, ote'pwe'ie'ntumwIn shiw otInoswunuk, kaocI oshe'It otI nInI e'wishkIsIt kowapmawa ipi kuke'ntanaw we'we'nI ote'pwe'ie'ntumwun shiw tInosunuk we'cminIt otI kwiuk we'cne'se'e't e'nasmupie'k kinwa caye'k.

3:17 Ipi nkom nikane'ituk, Nin kuke'ntan, ke'ko e'pwakuke'ntume'k iw, kaocItotme'k katotmowat ke'ki ktokmamwak.

3:18 NIshcI notI cIie'k Kshe'mIne'to nikan kashu knomowat ni caye'k NakanacmoncIn, ni KnaystIn e'wikwutuke'ntumnIt, iwsI kanomkItnuk.

3:19 Kisate'ntumok, ipi kwe'knoik tanakmIcshuwe'psiwnawan ktakasipie'makom, pic piamkuk e'wshkinakwutot e'nasmupIt Kshe'mIne'to.

3:20 Cisus KnaystIn, okupie'nokanan ke'isha kashupie'wawitmakoie'k.

3:21 Kishuk win, ke'otapnukot nash kiwcItsunuk cake'ko, win Kshe'mIne'to, kashI kikitot shiw toninuk caye'k panakwsIncIn Nakan acimoncIn nash e'kowe'pkiwuk

3:22 Mosis we'we'nI okinan nosnanIn Nakan acimot Kshe'mIne'to Se'pe'nmukwie'k, kupsukwit namakwa kikane'iwak ke'cwa nin, winsu kupsItwawa cake'ko, we'kwe'ntuk ke'koie'k.

3:23 KnomkItcI we'kwe'ntuk cipam ke'pwapsItwakwe'n ni nakanacimocIn win kupinatsI me'kwe' nIshnape'kI.

3:24 Ipi caye'k we'piacimocuk Se'mwInIn ipi ki ke'naktone'wacuk e'tshowat kakItwat winwake' okinikan iactanawa notI e'kishkiwuk.

3:25 KinwasI ktonicansumkowak Nakan acimocuk, ipi wawitmatwun, Kshe'mIne'to kawawitmowat kosnanIn okinan E'pIne'e'mIn, ipi knicansuk caye'k noe'twun shotI kik kIshwe'ntakwsik.

3:26 Kshe'mIne'to kapsukwitnat okwIsIn CisusIn, okinokanan kinwa ne'tum e'wishwe'nmukwie'k, e'wiwe'pnumakwie'k kmiashuwe'psiwnawan.

4:1 Me'kwe'cI e'ie'knonawat ni nIshInape'n me'matmoncIn ipi Kapte'n shi kcI-nume'okumkok ipi Se'cwsiuk, oki pie'naskakwan.

4:2 Kikisate'ntumwuk e'ki knomowanIt ni nIshInape'n, ipi e'kie'kwe'nIt, i e'wionIshkanIt, ni ne'poncIn shi CisusIn.

4:3 OkimshunawancI, ipi okikpakwawan nash wiapnInuk osam e'nmupkonianuk.

4:4 NIshcI mce'sh okite'pwe'takwan, kanotakwat nianuk mtatswak nInwuk kitshik kate'pwe'takwat.

4:5 KanomkukcI wiapuk kiw nakanse'cuk, ipi otukwe'simowan, ipi we'npie'makwcIn.

4:6 Ipi E'ne's ke'cmatmot, ipi Ke'Ipe's ipi Can, ipi E'nukse'ntu, ipi e'tshowat e'nwe'mawacIn ni ke'cmatmonIt kimaoce'tik shi Cinose'ne'muk.

4:7 IwcI na‘wiukwan kacipitupawat okinatowawan. We'kwnicI kshke'osuwun tanakI we'ni otInoswunuk otI kaocItotme'k.

4:8 IwcI Pitu e'moshkIne'shkakot, PanakwsIncIn CipamIn, otI okinan, kinwa nakane'nmukwie'k nIshInape'k, ipi e'kwe'simukwie'k ki isne'iun

4:9 KishpIn nkom e'kishkiwuk natakoik, we'cmnototwut, otI pwanu‘pmose't otI nInI, kaocI ne'se't?

4:10 Kuke'ntanawa kinwa caye'k, ipi caye'k nIshInape'k shi isne'iuk, shiw tInoswunuk Cisus Knayst Ne'se'ni nInI kaskakowe'k Kshe'mIne'to kapsukwitnat e'kinponIt, winsI otI nInI we'cnipwIt e'ne'se't e'nasmupie'k.

4:11 OtIsI ie'i sIn, kawe'pne'me'k we'ske'ie'k, iw sI ie'i ne'tmIsuk shi e'wikwe'ak.

4:12 Coke' pe'kanuk ke'skonwe'wun otItosin cowi pe'kanuk noswun namiukwun kishkok minasik ninwuk ke'ocI ke'skonkoikon.

4:13 IwcI kawaptumwawat i onikansiunawa ni PituIn ipi CanIn, e'ke'nmawat ke'ko e'pwake'ntumnIt okimamkate'nmawan ipi okinaktowe'nmawan e'kipmowice'wanIt ni CisusIn.

4:14 Ipi e'wapmawat, ni nInwun, kane'se'mIt e'wickapwItakwat coke'ko okinasiwan.

4:15 PicI kamace'nashkawawat shi e'tshI kikitowat okitshItanawa winwa.
