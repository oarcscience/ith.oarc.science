Kabyle Bible: 2 Peter

1:1 Nekk Semɛun Buṭrus, d aqeddac, d amceggeɛ n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, i wid akk yesɛan liman am nukni, s lḥeqq n Sidi Ṛebbi Illu-nneɣ akk-d umsellek nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:2 Ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a wen-d tețțunefkent s tugeț, s tmusni n Sidi Ṛebbi akk-d Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa.

1:3 Tazmert n Sidi Ṛebbi tefka yaɣ-ed ayen akk ilaqen i tudert d ṭṭaɛa ; yis i ɣ-d-isbeggen win akken i ɣ-ixtaṛen s ṛṛeḥma-s ț-țmanegt-is.

1:4 Yis-sent daɣen i ɣ-d-tețțunefkent lemɛahdat yesɛan azal d ameqqran, iwakken s tmanegt-agi-ines akk-d ṛṛeḥma-s, aț-țekkim di lecɣal n Sidi Ṛebbi, aț-țbeɛdem ɣef ṭṭmeɛ ițțawin ɣer lefsad n ddunit.

1:5 Ɣef ayagi, ǧehdet, ẓewṛet iwakken aț-țernum i liman-nwen tikli yeṣfan, i tikli yeṣfan tamusni ;

1:6 i tmusni rnut ḥekmet deg yiman nwen, ṣebṛet, i ṣṣbeṛ rnut ṭṭaɛa,

1:7 i ṭṭaɛa tagmaț, i tegmaț leḥmala.

1:8 Ma yella wannect-agi deg-wen s tugeț, ur tețțilim ara d imeɛdazen, yerna ur tețɣimim ara mbla tamusni n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:9 Ma d win ixuṣṣen deg wannect-agi, am uderɣal isferfuden, ur ițwali ara, ițțu akk ddnubat-is iɛeddan i s-isemmeḥ Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:10 Ay atmaten, ẓewṛet, sǧehdet iman-nwen deg wannect-agi imi d Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-d-issawlen, d nețța i kkun-ixtaṛen. Ma txedmem ayagi, ur tɣellim ara.

1:11 S wakka daɣen ara wen-teldi tewwurt iwakken aț-țkecmem ɣer tgeldit ițdumun n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, amsellek-nneɣ.

1:12 A wen-d-țɛawadeɣ dayem annect-agi meṛṛa ɣas akken tesnem tideț-agi yerna teṭṭfem deg-s.

1:13 Skud mazal-iyi ddreɣ, ilaq-iyi a kkun-nhuɣ iwakken ur tɣeflem ara.

1:14 Axaṭer ẓriɣ belli qṛib ad ṛuḥeɣ si ddunit-agi, am akken i yi-d-ixebbeṛ Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ;

1:15 daymi i xeddmeɣ ayen iwumi zemreɣ iwakken ɣas mmuteɣ, a d-temmektim ayen akk i wen slemdeɣ.

1:16 Mačči ț-țimucuha i wen-d newwi mi i wen-d-neḥka ɣef tezmert n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ț-țuɣalin-is, meɛna s wallen-nneɣ i nwala tamanegt-is.

1:17 Nella yid-es asm' akken i s-d isbeggen Baba Ṛebbi ccan ț-țezmert, asm' akken i d-inṭeq Bab n tmanegt s imeslayen-agi : Wagi d Mmi eɛzizen deg-s i gella lfeṛḥ-iw.

1:18 Sṣut-agi i d-ikkan seg igenni, nesla-yas s yiman-nneɣ mi nella yid-es deg udrar iqedsen,

1:19 yerna numen s wayen i ɣ-d xebbṛen lenbiya di tira iqedsen belli ț-țideț ; nenha-kkun aț-țerrem ddehn nwen ɣuṛ-sent, axaṭer ț-țaftilt iceɛlen di ṭṭlam alamma yuli wass, alamma ifeǧǧeǧ yitri n ṣṣbeḥ deg ulawen nwen.

1:20 Uqbel kullec, ẓret belli imeslayen akk i d-nnan lenbiya, yuran di tektabt iqedsen, ur d-kkin ara s ɣuṛ-sen ;

1:21 axaṭeṛ ulac ayen i d-xebbṛen s tmusni-nsen nutni, meɛna s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i d-nnan imeslayen i d-ikkan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:1 Si zik llan lenbiya n lekdeb ger lɣaci, akken daɣen ara d-kkren gar-awen wid ara kkun-iɣuṛṛen s uselmed-nsen. A d-skecmen tamusni-nsen n lekdeb d usexṛeb, ad nekṛen ula d amsellek i ten-id-icufɛen ; s wakka ara d-sseɣlin ɣef yiman-nsen tawaɣit ur bnin fell-as.

2:2 Ɣas akken ffɣen i webrid, aṭas ara ten-iḍefṛen, ɣef ddemma-nsen ara ițwargem webrid n tideț ;

2:3 a ten-yawi ṭṭmeɛ, a wen kellxen s imeslayen n lekdeb ; meɛna nnger-nsen ur ițɛeṭṭil ara, tawaɣit tețṛaǧu-ten.

2:4 Teẓram belli Sidi Ṛebbi ur iḥunn ara ɣef lmalayekkat yeffɣen i webrid, meɛna iḍeggeṛ-iten ɣer tesraft lqayen n ṭṭlam anda țwaqnent, țṛaǧunt lḥisab.

2:5 Ur iḥunn ara daɣen ɣef yemdanen n zzman aqdim asm' akken i sen-d iceggeɛ lḥemla n waman i lɣaci amcum ; issemneɛ seg-sen anagar Sidna Nuḥ ițbecciṛen lḥeqq n Sidi Ṛebbi, akk-d sebɛa n yemdanen yid-es.

2:6 Iɛuqeb timdinin n Sudum d Gumuṛ, issenger-itent, isxeḍ-itent s tmes, yerra-tent d iɣed ; ifka-d ayagi d lemtel i yemcumen iwakken ad ẓren d acu i ten-ițṛaǧun.

2:7 Issemneɛ anagar Luṭ yellan d aḥeqqi, yemmuɣbnen ɣef yir lecɣal n lɣaci amcum yellan di zzman-is.

2:8 Argaz-agi aḥeqqi ițɛicin gar-asen, itețț-it wul-is ɣef ayen i gsell d wayen țwalint wallen-is yal ass, ɣef yir tikli nsen d yir lecɣal-nsen.

2:9 S wakka Sidi Ṛebbi yeẓra amek ara isellek di teswiɛin iweɛṛen imdanen i gțeklen fell-as ; ma d imcumen a ten-yerr ɣer rrif i lɛiqab i d-iteddun ass n lḥisab.

2:10 Ad iɛaqeb widak-nni i gxeddmen lefsad, ițnadin anagar lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen, imi ur țțaken ara lqima i lhiba n Sidi Ṛebbi. Ur țqadaṛen ara, qebbḥen, ur uggaden ara ad regmen ula d lmalayekkat.

2:11 Ma d lmalayekkat yesɛan lqeḍra ț-țezmert sennig wid yesselmaden lekdeb, ur sen-țarrant ara rregmat zdat Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:12 Meɛna imdanen-agi țɛicin am lmal n lexla, lhan kan i ṣṣyada, ur țxemmimen ara, reggmen ayen ur ssinen ; ad ɣlin di tesraft n ddnubat nsen, ad mmuṛdsen akken ițmuṛdus lmal.

2:13 D lbaṭel zerɛen ara d-megren, țțafen lfeṛḥ-nsen mi xeddmen ɛinani lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen. Sčuṛen d lɛib akk-d lḥecma, yerna m' ara qqimen yid-wen ɣer lmakla, ur țsetḥin ara s lehduṛ-nsen d ukellex-nsen.

2:14 Sṭamaɛen anagar yir tilawin iwakken ad znun yid-sent ; ur ɛeggun ara di ddnubat i xeddmen, țɣuṛṛun imdanen ur neǧhid ara di liman, ččuṛen d ṭṭmeɛ, ț-țarwa n deɛwessu.

2:15 Sǧan abrid n lewqam, ɛeṛqen mi tebɛen abrid i gewwi Belɛam mmi-s n Biɛuṛ i gḥemmlen ad yerbeḥ idrimen s lexdeɛ.

2:16 Lameɛna iḥeccem-it Sidi Ṛebbi, mi d-issenṭeq ɣuṛ-es taɣyult tagugamt s ṣṣut n wemdan ; s wakka i t-iḥbes, iwakken ur ițkemmil ara di yir ccɣel-is.

2:21 Axiṛ-asen i yemdanen-agi lemmer ur ssinen ara abrid n lḥeqq, wala imi i t-ssnen uɣalen ṭṭaxṛen i lameṛ iqedsen i sen-d-ițțucegɛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:22 Yedṛa yid-sen am akken yella di lemtul : Ițțuɣal weqjun ɣer iriran-is ; qqaṛen daɣen : Ilef iwumi ssarden, yuɣal imiren kan ad ixnunes deg waluḍ.

3:1 Ay iḥbiben, ațah tebṛaț-iw tis snat i wen-d-uriɣ. Di snat yid-sent nudaɣ amek ara d-ssakiɣ di leɛqel nwen ixemmimen iṣfan s uselmed-agi i wen-d-smektaɣ.

3:2 Bɣiɣ a d temmektim imeslayen i d-nnan lenbiya iqedsen akk-d lameṛ n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ amsellek nneɣ, i wen-slemden yakan imceggɛen n Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:3 Tazwara, ilaq aț-țeẓrem belli deg ussan ineggura a d-kkren yemdanen ara iɛicen s lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen, yerna ad tmesxiṛen fell-awen ;

3:4 a wen-d-qqaṛen : anaɣ yenna-yawen a d-uɣaleɣ, ihi anda yella tura ? Lejdud-nneɣ ɛeddan mmuten, ulac ayen i gbeddlen seg wasmi i d-texleq ddunit.

3:5 Țțun belli di zzman aqdim asmi i d-ixleq Sidi Ṛebbi igenwan d lqaɛa ; s wawal-is i d-issufeɣ lqaɛa seg waman ;

3:6 S wawal-is daɣen i gessenger ddunit s lḥemla n waman.

3:7 Ula d igenwan d lqaɛa n tura s wawal-is daɣen i ten-ihegga iwakken a ten-issenger s tmes ass n lḥisab asm' ara iɛaqeb wid ur nḍuɛ ara Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:8 Ay iɛzizen ! Ɣuṛ-wat aț-țețțum ayagi : ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi alef n iseggasen am yiwen wass, yiwen wass am alef n iseggasen.

3:9 Sidi Ṛebbi ur ițɛeṭṭil ara ad ixdem ayen i d-yenna ; ɣas akken kra n yemdanen ɣilen iɛeṭṭel ; meɛna nețța d ṣṣbeṛ i wen-iṣebbeṛ iwakken ur inegger ula yiwen deg-wen, yebɣa a d-tuɣalem akk ɣer ubrid-is.

3:10 Ass n Sidi Ṛebbi a d-yas akken i d-ițțas umakar. Igenwan ad negren s yiwen zzhir d ameqqran, ayen yellan deg igenwan a t-tečč tmes, lqaɛa d wayen i ț-iɛemmṛen ad fnun.

3:11 Imi ayagi meṛṛa ad ifnu, acḥal i wen-ilaq aț-țḍuɛem Sidi Ṛebbi, aț-țeṣfum di tikli-nwen !

3:12 Aṛǧut, xedmet lmeǧhud-nwen iwakken a d-yeɛjel wass n Sidi Ṛebbi. Ass-nni i deg ara tekker tmes deg igenwan, i deg ara yefsi kra n wayen yellan deg-sen.

3:13 Meɛna nețṛaǧu am akken i ɣ-t-id-iɛuhed Sidi Ṛebbi : Igenwan d lqaɛa imaynuten ( ijdiden ), i deg ara izdeɣ lḥeqq.

3:14 Daymi ay iɛzizen, deg uṛaǧu-agi tețṛaǧum ḥeṛset iman-nwen iwakken a kkun-id-yaf di lehna, zeddigit, mbla lɛib.

3:15 Ḥṣut belli ṣṣbeṛ ameqqran n Sidi Ṛebbi, d leslak-nwen ! Am akken i wen-t-id-yura gma-tneɣ eɛzizen Bulus s tmusni i s-d-ițțunefken.

3:16 D ayen i wen-d-iqqaṛ daɣen di mkul tabṛaț i deg i d-immeslay ɣef temsalin-agi. Llan deg-sent imeslayen iweɛṛen i lefhama, daymi imdanen ur neǧhid ara di liman, ixuṣṣen di lefhama, sseɛwajen lmeɛna-nsen akken i sseɛwajen ula ț-țira nniḍen, ayagi xeddmen-t i nnger-nsen.

3:17 A wid eɛzizen, kunwi yeslan s wannect-agi meṛṛa, ḥadret ɣef yiman-nwen, ɣuṛ-wat a kkun-ɣuṛṛen yemcumen ur numin ara s Sidi Ṛebbi, a kkun-sbeɛden ɣef ubrid-is i deg teṭṭfem.

3:18 Lameɛna nnernit di tmusni d ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, Amsellek-nneɣ. I nețța tamanegt am ass-a am ussan i d-iteddun i dayem. Amin !
