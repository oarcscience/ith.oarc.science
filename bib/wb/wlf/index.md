------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a Wolof translation of the New Testament of the Bible. Wolof is
a West African language spoken in Senegal, the Gambia and Mauritania,
belonging to the Niger-Congo language family. The publication date and
copyright status of this text are unknown.
