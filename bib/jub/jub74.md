### The Kings of Edom (xxxviii. 15-24; cf. Gen. xxxvi. 31-39).

15\. And these are the kings that reigned in Edom before there reigned
any king over the children of Israel \[until this day\] in the land of
Edom. 16. And Bâlâq, <span id="fr_830"></span>[1](#fn_822) the son of
Beor, reigned in Edom, and the name of his city was Danâbâ. <span
id="fr_831"></span>[2](#fn_823) 17. And Bâlâq died, and Jobab, the son
of Zârâ of Bôsêr, <span id="fr_832"></span>[3](#fn_824) reigned in his
stead. 18. And Jobab died, and ’Asâm, <span
id="fr_833"></span>[4](#fn_825) of the land of Têmân, reigned in his
stead. 19. And ’Asâm died, and ’Adâth, <span
id="fr_834"></span>[5](#fn_826) the son of Barad, <span
id="fr_835"></span>[6](#fn_827) who slew Midian in the field of Moab,
reigned in his stead, and the name of his city was Avith. 20. And ’Adâth
died, and Salman, <span id="fr_836"></span>[7](#fn_828) from
’Amâsêqâ, <span id="fr_837"></span>[8](#fn_829) reigned in his stead.
21. And Salman died, and Saul of Râ’abôth <span
id="fr_838"></span>[9](#fn_830) (by the) river, reigned in his stead.
22. And Saul died, and Ba’êlûnân, <span id="fr_839"></span>[10](#fn_831)
the son of Achbor, reigned in his stead. 23. And Ba’êlûnân, the son of
Achbor, died, and ’Adâth <span id="fr_840"></span>[11](#fn_832) reigned
in his stead, and the name of his wife was Maiṭabîth, <span
id="fr_841"></span>[12](#fn_833) the daughter of Mâṭarat, <span
id="fr_842"></span>[13](#fn_834) the daughter of Mêtabêdzâ’ab. <span
id="fr_843"></span>[14](#fn_835) 24. These are the kings who reigned in
the land of Edom.
