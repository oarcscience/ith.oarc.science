### LXI.

<span class="small">*Judas Undertakes to Point Out Jesus*.</span>

THEN one of the twelve, called Judas Iscariot, went unto the chief
priests,

2 And said *unto them*, What will ye give me, and I will deliver him
unto you? And they covenanted with him for thirty pieces of silver.

<span id="page_145">p. 145</span>

3 And from that time he sought opportunity to betray him.
