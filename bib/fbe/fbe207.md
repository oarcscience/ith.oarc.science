### ODE 13.

A strange little Ode.

1 Behold! the Lord is our mirror: open the eyes and see them in Him: and
learn the manner of your face:  
2 And tell forth praise to His spirit: and wipe off the filth from your
face: and love His holiness and clothe yourselves therewith:  
3 And be without stain at all times before Him. Hallelujah.
