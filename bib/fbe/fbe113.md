### VI.

Of the dew and of the olive-oil, and various flowers.

THEY showed me the treasure-house of the dew, like oil of the olive, and
the appearance of its form, as of all the flowers of the earth; further
many angels guarding the treasure-houses of these *things*, *and* how
they are made to shut and open.
