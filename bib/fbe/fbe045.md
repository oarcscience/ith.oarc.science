### CHAP. XL.

The first Human hunger.

THEN the Word of God came to Adam, and said unto him, "O, Adam, why
hadst thou not this dread, neither this fasting, nor this care ere this?
And why hadst thou not this fear before thou didst transgress?

2 "But when thou camest to dwell in this strange land, thy animal body
could not be on earth without earthly food, to strengthen it and to
restore its powers."

3 And God withdrew His Word from Adam.
