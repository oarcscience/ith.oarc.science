### ODE 37.

An elementary Ode.

1 I stretched out my hands to my Lord: and to the Most I High I raised
my voice:  
2 And I spake with the lips of my heart; and He heard me, when my voice
reached I Him:  
3 His answer came to me, and gave me the fruits of my labours;  
4 And it gave me rest by the grace of the Lord. Hallelujah.
