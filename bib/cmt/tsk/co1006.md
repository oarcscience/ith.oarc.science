<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co1 6:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co1 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1006.htm#001), The Corinthians must not vex
their brethren, in going to law with them; [Co1
6:6](../../../bib/kjv/co1006.htm#006), especially under infidels; [Co1
6:9](../../../bib/kjv/co1006.htm#009), The unrighteous shall not inherit
the kingdom of God; [Co1 6:15](../../../bib/kjv/co1006.htm#015), Our
bodies are the members of Christ, and temples of the Holy Ghost. they
must not therefore be defiled.
