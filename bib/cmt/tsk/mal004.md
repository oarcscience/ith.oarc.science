### [Malachi Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#001.md)

[Malachi](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mal 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mal 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#001.md), God's judgment on the
wicked; [Mal 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#002.md), and his blessing on
the good; [Mal 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#004.md), He exhorts to the
study of the law; [Mal 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#005.md), and tells
of Elijah's coming and office.
