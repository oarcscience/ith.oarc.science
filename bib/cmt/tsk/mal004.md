<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mal 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mal 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#001), God's judgment on the
wicked; [Mal 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#002), and his blessing on
the good; [Mal 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#004), He exhorts to the
study of the law; [Mal 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#005), and tells
of Elijah's coming and office.
