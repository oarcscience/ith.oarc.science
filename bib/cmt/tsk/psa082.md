### [Psalms Chapter 82](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 82:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 82:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001.md), The psalmist, having
exhorted the judges, [Psa 82:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#005.md), and
reproved their negligence, [Psa 82:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#008.md),
prays God to judge.

Some refer this psalm to the time of David, and others to that of
Hezekiah; but it is more probable that it was composed when Jehoshaphat
reformed the courts throughout his kingdom. [Ch2
19:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#006.md), [Ch2
19:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#007.md)
