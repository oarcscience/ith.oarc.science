### [2 Chronicles Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 20:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#001), Jehoshaphat, invaded by
Moab, proclaims a fast; [Ch2 20:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#005), His
prayer; [Ch2 20:14](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#014), The prophecy of
Jahaziel; [Ch2 20:20](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#020), Jehoshaphat
exhorts the people, and sets singers to praise the Lord; [Ch2
20:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#022), The great overthrow of his
enemies; [Ch2 20:26](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#026), The people,
having blessed God at Berachah, return in triumph; [Ch2
20:31](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#031), Jehoshaphat's reign; [Ch2
20:35](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#035), His convoy of ships, according
to the prophecy of Eliezer, unhappily perish.
