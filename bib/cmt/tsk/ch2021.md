### [2 Chronicles Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 21:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 21:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#001.md), Jehoram, succeeding
Jehoshaphat, slays his brethren; [Ch2
21:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#005.md), His wicked reign; [Ch2
21:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#008.md), Edom and Libnah revolt; [Ch2
21:12](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#012.md), The prophecy of Elijah against
him in writing; [Ch2 21:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#016.md),
Philistines and Arabians oppress him; [Ch2
21:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#018.md), His incurable disease, infamous
death, and burial.
