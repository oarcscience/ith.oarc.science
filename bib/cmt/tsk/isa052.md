### [Isaiah Chapter 52](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#001)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 52:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 52:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#001), Christ persuades the church
to believe his free redemption, [Isa
52:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#007), to receive the ministers
thereof, [Isa 52:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#009), to joy in the
power thereof, [Isa 52:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#011). and to free
themselves from bondage; [Isa 52:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#013),
Christ's kingdom shall be exalted.
