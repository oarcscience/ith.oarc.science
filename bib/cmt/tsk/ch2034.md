### [2 Chronicles Chapter 34](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#001.md)

[2 Chronicles](../../../bib/kjv/ch2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 34:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 34:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#001.md), Josiah's good reign; [Ch2
34:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#003.md), He destroys idolatry; [Ch2
34:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#008.md), He takes order for the repair of
the temple; [Ch2 34:14](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#014.md), Hilkiah,
having found a book of the law, Josiah sends to Huldah to enquire of the
Lord; [Ch2 34:23](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#023.md), Huldah prophesies
the destruction of Jerusalem, but respite thereof in Josiah's time; [Ch2
34:29](../../../bib/kjv/ch2034.htm#029.md), Josiah, causing it to be read
in a solemn assembly, renews the covenant with God.
