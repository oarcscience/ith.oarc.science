### [Isaiah Chapter 31](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 31:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 31:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#001.md), The prophet shews the folly
and danger of trusting to Egypt, and forsaking God; [Isa
31:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#006.md), He exhorts to conversion; [Isa
31:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#008.md), He shews the fall of Assyria.
