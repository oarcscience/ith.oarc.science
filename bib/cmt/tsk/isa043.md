### [Isaiah Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 43:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#001.md), The Lord comforts the
church with his promises; [Isa 43:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#008.md),
He appeals to the people for witness of his omnipotency; [Isa
43:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#014.md), He foretells them the
destruction of Babylon, [Isa 43:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#018.md).
and his wonderful deliverance of his people; [Isa
43:22](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#022.md), He reproves the people as
inexcusable.
