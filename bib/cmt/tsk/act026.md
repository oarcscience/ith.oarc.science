<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 26:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 26:1](../../../bib/kjv/act026.htm#001), Paul, in the presence of
Agrippa, declares his life from his childhood; [Act
26:12](../../../bib/kjv/act026.htm#012), and how miraculously he was
converted, and called to his apostleship; [Act
26:24](../../../bib/kjv/act026.htm#024), Festus charges him with being
mad, whereunto he answers modestly; [Act
26:28](../../../bib/kjv/act026.htm#028), Agrippa is almost persuaded to
be a Christian; [Act 26:31](../../../bib/kjv/act026.htm#031), The whole
company pronounce him innocent.
