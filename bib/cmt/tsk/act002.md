Overview

[Act 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#001), The apostles, filled with
the Holy Ghost, and speaking divers languages, are admired by some, and
derided by others; [Act 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#014), whom
Peter disproves; [Act 2:37](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#037), he
baptizes a great number that were converted; [Act
2:41](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#041), who afterwards devoutly and
charitably converse together; the apostles working many miracles, and
God daily increasing his church.
