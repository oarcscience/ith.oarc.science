Jeremiah Chapter 24

Jeremiah

jer 24:0

Overview

Jer 24:1, Under the type of good and bad figs, Jer 24:4, he foreshews the restoration of them that were in captivity; Jer 24:8, and the desolation of Zedekiah and the rest.
