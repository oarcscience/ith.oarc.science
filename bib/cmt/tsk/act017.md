### [Acts Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 17:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#001), Paul preaches at
Thessalonica, where some believe, [Act
17:5](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#005), and others persecute him; [Act
17:10](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#010), He is sent to Berea, and
preaches there; [Act 17:13](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#013), Being
persecuted by Jews from Thessalonica, [Act
17:16](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#016). he comes to Athens, and
disputes and preaches the living God, to them unknown; [Act
17:32](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#032), whereby, though some mock, many
are converted unto Christ.
