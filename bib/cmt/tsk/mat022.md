[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 22:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#001.md), The parable of the marriage
of the king's son; [Mat 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#009.md), The
vocation of the Gentiles; [Mat 22:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#012.md),
The punishment of him that wanted the wedding garment; [Mat
22:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#015.md), Tribute ought to be paid to
Caesar; [Mat 22:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#023.md), Christ confutes
the Sadducees for the resurrection; [Mat
22:34](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#034.md), answers the lawyer, which is
the first and great commandment; [Mat
22:41](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#041.md), and puzzles the Pharisees by a
question about the Messias.
