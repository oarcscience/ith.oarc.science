### [Isaiah Chapter 39](../../../bib/kjv/isa039.htm#001)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 39:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 39:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa039.htm#001), Merodach-baladan, sending
to visit Hezekiah because of the wonder, has notice of his treasures;
[Isa 39:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa039.htm#003), Isaiah, understanding
thereof, foretells the Babylonian captivity.
