### [2 Corinthians Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#001), He declares how he has used
all sincerity and faithful diligence in preaching the gospel, [Co2
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#007), and how the troubles and
persecutions which he daily endured for the same did redound to the
praise of God's power, [Co2 4:12](../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#012). to
the benefit of the church, [Co2 4:16](../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#016).
and to the apostle's own eternal glory.
