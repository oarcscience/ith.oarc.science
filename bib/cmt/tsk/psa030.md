<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 30:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 30:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001.md), David praises God for his
deliverance; [Psa 30:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#004.md), He exhorts
others to praise him by example of God's dealings with him.

Or, "A psalm or song of David, at the dedication of the house;" by which
is supposed to be meant the place he built on the threshing floor of
Araunah, after the grievous plague which had nearly desolated the
kingdom. [Sa2 24:25](../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#025.md); [Ch1
21:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1021.htm#006.md)
