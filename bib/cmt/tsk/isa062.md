<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 62:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 62:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa062.htm#001), The fervent desire of the
prophet to confirm the church in God's promises; [Isa
62:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa062.htm#006), The office of the ministers
(unto which they are incited) in preaching the Gospel, [Isa
62:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa062.htm#010). and preparing the people
thereto.
