illustration of God's power; [Isa
64:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa064.htm#004), Celebrating God's mercy, it
makes confession of their natural corruptions; [Isa
64:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa064.htm#009), It complains of their
afflictions.
