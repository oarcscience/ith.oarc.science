feed their flocks; [Pe1 5:5](../../../bib/kjv/pe1005.htm#005), the
younger to obey; [Pe1 5:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe1005.htm#008), and all to
be sober, watchful, and constant in the faith; [Pe1
5:9](../../../bib/kjv/pe1005.htm#009), and to resist the cruel adversary
