merits, and hatred of idolatry, flees to God for preservation; [Psa
16:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa016.htm#005), He shews the hope of his
calling, of the resurrection, and life everlasting.

corresponding to the Greek αδηϚ, i.e., ο αιδηϚ τοπος, the invisible
place and the Hebrew sheol, from shaal, to ask, seek, the place and
state of those who are out of the way, and to be sought for.
