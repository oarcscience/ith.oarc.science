### [Matthew Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#001.md)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 24:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#001.md), Christ foretells the
destruction of the temple; [Mat 24:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#003.md),
what and how great calamities shall be before it; [Mat
24:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#029.md), the signs of his coming to
judgment; [Mat 24:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#036.md), And because that
day and hour are unknown, [Mat 24:42](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#042.md).
we ought to watch like good servants, expecting every moment our
Master's coming.
