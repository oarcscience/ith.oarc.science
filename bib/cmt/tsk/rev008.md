seventh seal, [Rev 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/rev008.htm#002), Seven angels

8:6](../../../bib/kjv/rev008.htm#006), Four of them sound their trumpets
and great plagues follow; [Rev 8:9](../../../bib/kjv/rev008.htm#009),

altar.
