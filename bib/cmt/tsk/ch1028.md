Overview

[Ch1 28:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1028.htm#001), David in a solemn assembly
having declared God's favour to him, and promise to his son Solomon,
exhorts them to fear God; [Ch1 28:9](../../../bib/kjv/ch1028.htm#009),
He encourages Solomon to build the temple; [Ch1
28:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1028.htm#011), He gives him patterns, gold and
silver, etc.
