### [2 Corinthians Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#001.md)

[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#001.md), He stirs them up to a
liberal contribution for the poor saints at Jerusalem, by the example of
the Macedonians; [Co2 8:7](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#007.md), by
commendation of their former forwardness; [Co2
8:9](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#009.md), by the example of Christ; [Co2
8:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#014.md), and by the spiritual profit that
shall redound to themselves thereby; [Co2
8:16](../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#016.md), commending to them the integrity
and willingness of Titus, and those other brethren, who upon his
request, exhortation, and commendation, were purposely come to them for
this business.
