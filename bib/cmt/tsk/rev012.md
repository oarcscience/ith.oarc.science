<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">rev 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Rev 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#001.md), A woman clothed with the
sun travails; [Rev 12:4](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#004.md), The great red
dragon stands before her, ready to devour her child; [Rev
12:6](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#006.md), when she is delivered she flees
into the wilderness; [Rev 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#007.md),
Michael and his angels fight with the dragon, and prevail; [Rev
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#013.md), The dragon, being cast down
into the earth, persecutes the woman.
