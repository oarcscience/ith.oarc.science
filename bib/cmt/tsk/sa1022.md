### [1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">sa1 22:0</span></span>

Overview

[Sa1 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#001), Companies resort unto David
at Adullam; [Sa1 22:3](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#003), At Mizpeh he
commends his parents unto the king of Moab; [Sa1
22:5](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#005), Admonished by Gad, he comes to
Hareth; [Sa1 22:6](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#006), Saul going to
pursue him, complains of his servants' unfaithfulness; [Sa1
22:9](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#009), Doeg accuses Ahimelech; [Sa1
22:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#011), Saul commands to kill the
priests; [Sa1 22:17](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#017), The footmen
refusing, Doeg executes it; [Sa1
22:20](../../../bib/kjv/sa1022.htm#020), Abiathar escaping, brings David
the news.
