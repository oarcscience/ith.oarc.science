### [4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 13](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#001.md)

[4 Kings (2 Kings)](../../../bib/kjv/kg2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">kg2 13:0</span></span>

Overview

[Kg2 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#001.md), Jehoahaz's wicked reign;
[Kg2 13:3](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#003.md), Jehoahaz, oppressed by
Hazael, is relieved by prayer; [Kg2
13:8](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#008.md), Joash succeeds him; [Kg2
13:10](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#010.md), His wicked reign; [Kg2
13:12](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#012.md), Jeroboam succeeds him; [Kg2
13:14](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#014.md), Elisha dying, prophesies to
Joash three victories over the Syrians; [Kg2
13:20](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#020.md), The Moabites invading the land,
Elisha's bones raise a dead man; [Kg2
13:22](../../../bib/kjv/kg2013.htm#022.md), Joash gets three victories over
Ben-hadad.
