### [Jeremiah Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#001.md), Jeremiah laments the Jews
for their manifold sins; [Jer 9:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#009.md), and
for their judgment; [Jer 9:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#012.md),
Disobedience is the cause of their bitter calamity; [Jer
9:17](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#017.md), He exhorts to mourn for their
destruction; [Jer 9:23](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#023.md), and to trust
not in themselves, but in God; [Jer
9:25](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#025.md), He threatens both Jews and
Gentiles.
