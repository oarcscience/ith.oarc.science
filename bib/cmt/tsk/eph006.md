Overview

[Eph 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#001.md), The duty of children towards
their parents; [Eph 6:5](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#005.md), of servants
towards their masters; [Eph 6:10](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#010.md), Our
life is a warfare, not only against flesh and blood, but also spiritual
enemies; [Eph 6:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#013.md), The complete
armour of a Christian; [Eph 6:18](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#018.md), and
how it ought to be used; [Eph 6:21](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#021.md),
Tychicus is commended.
