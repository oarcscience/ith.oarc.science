Overview

[Rom 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001.md), The Scripture shows the
difference between the righteousness of the law, and that of faith; [Rom
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#011.md), and that all, both Jew and
Gentile, that believe, shall not be confounded; [Rom
10:18](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#018.md), and that the Gentiles shall
receive the word and believe; [Rom
10:19](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#019.md), Israel was not ignorant of
these things.
