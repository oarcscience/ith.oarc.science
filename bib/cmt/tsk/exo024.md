### [Exodus Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#001.md)

[Exodus](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">exo 24:0</span></span>

Overview

[Exo 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#001.md), Moses is called up into the
mountain; [Exo 24:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#003.md), The people
promise obedience; [Exo 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#004.md), Moses
builds an altar, and twelve pillars; [Exo
24:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#006.md), He sprinkles the blood of the
covenant; [Exo 24:9](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#009.md), The glory of God
appears; [Exo 24:14](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#014.md), Aaron and Hur
have the charge of the people; [Exo
24:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#015.md), Moses goes into the mountain,
where he continues forty days and forty nights.
