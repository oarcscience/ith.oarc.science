### [Jeremiah Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 24:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#001.md), Under the type of good and
bad figs, [Jer 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#004.md), he foreshews the
restoration of them that were in captivity; [Jer
24:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#008.md), and the desolation of Zedekiah
and the rest.
