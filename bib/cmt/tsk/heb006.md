from the faith; [Heb 6:11](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#011), but to be
stedfast, [Heb 6:12](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#012). diligent, and
patient to wait upon God; [Heb 6:13](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#013),
because God is most sure in his promise.
