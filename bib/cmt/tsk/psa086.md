Overview

[Psa 86:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa086.htm#001), David strengthens his
prayer by the consciousness of his religion; [Psa
86:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa086.htm#005), by the goodness and power of
God; [Psa 86:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa086.htm#011), He desires the
continuance of former grace; [Psa
86:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa086.htm#014), Complaining of the proud, he
craves some token of God's goodness.

David. This Psalm is supposed to have been composed by David either when
persecuted by Saul, or driven from Jerusalem by Absalom. [Psa
102:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa102.htm#001), [Psa
142:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#001) \*titles
