Overview

[Sa2 23:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#001.md), David, in his last words,
professes his faith in God's promises to be beyond sense or experience;
[Sa2 23:6](../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#006.md), The different state of the
wicked; [Sa2 23:8](../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#008.md), A catalogue of
David's mighty men.

the readings in the parallel place in Chronicles, where it is also,
"three hundred," instead of "eight hundred."
