be constant in Christ; [Col 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#008), to
beware of philosophy, and vain traditions; [Col
2:18](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#018), worshipping of angels; [Col
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#020), and legal ceremonies, which are
ended in Christ.
