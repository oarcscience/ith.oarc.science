Overview

[Rev 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#001.md), An angel seals the servants
of God in their foreheads; [Rev 7:4](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#004.md),
The number of them that were sealed. of the tribes of Israel a certain
number; [Rev 7:9](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#009.md), Of all the other
nations an innumerable multitude, which stand before the throne, clad in
white robes, and palms in their hands; [Rev
7:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014.md), Their robes were washed in the
blood of the Lamb.
