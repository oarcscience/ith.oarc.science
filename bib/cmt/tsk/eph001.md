Overview

[Eph 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#001.md), After the salutation, [Eph
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#003.md), and thanksgiving for the
Ephesians, [Eph 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#004.md), he treats of our
election, [Eph 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#006.md), and adoption by
grace; [Eph 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#011.md), which is the true
and proper fountain of man's salvation; [Eph
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#013.md), And because the height of this
mystery cannot be easily attained unto, [Eph
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#016.md). he prays that they may come to
the full knowledge and possession thereof in Christ.
