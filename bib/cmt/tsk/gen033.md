Succoth: i.e. Booths, Succoth was on the east of Jordan, between the
brook Jabbok and that river, about 40 miles from Jerusalem, and
consequently near Penuel; where a city was afterwards built, which
Joshua assigned to the tribe of Gad. Jerome says, that Succoth was in
the district of Scythopolis; and the Jews inform us, that the name of
Darala was sometime after applied to it.
