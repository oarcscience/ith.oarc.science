mount; [Mat 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#003), Who are blessed; [Mat
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#013), the salt of the earth; [Mat
5:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#014), the light of the world; [Mat
5:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#017), He came to fulfil the law; [Mat
5:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#021), What it is to kill; [Mat
5:27](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#027), to commit adultery; [Mat
5:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#033), to swear; [Mat
5:38](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#038), He exhorts to suffer wrong, [Mat
5:43](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#043). to love our enemies; [Mat
5:48](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048), and to labour after perfection.
