### [3 Kings (1 Kings) Chapter 19](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#001.md)

[3 Kings (1 Kings)](../../../bib/kjv/kg1001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">kg1 19:0</span></span>

Overview

[Kg1 19:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#001.md), Elijah, threatened by
Jezebel, flees to Beer-sheba; [Kg1
19:4](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#004.md), In the wilderness, being weary
of his life, he is comforted by an angel; [Kg1
19:9](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#009.md), At Horeb God appears unto him,
sending him to anoint Hazael, Jehu, and Elisha; [Kg1
19:19](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#019.md), Elisha, taking leave of his
friends, follows Elijah.
