Jeremiah Chapter 43

Jeremiah

jer 43:0

Overview

Jer 43:1, Johanan, discrediting Jeremiah's prophecy, carries him and the rest into Egypt; Jer 43:8, Jeremiah prophesies by a type the conquest of Egypt by the Babylonians.
