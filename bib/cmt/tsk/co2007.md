Overview

[Co2 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2007.htm#001.md), He proceeds in exhorting
them to purity of life; [Co2 7:2](../../../bib/kjv/co2007.htm#002.md), and
to bear him like affection as he does to them; [Co2
7:3](../../../bib/kjv/co2007.htm#003.md), Whereof lest he might seem to
doubt, he declares what comfort he took in his afflictions by the report
which Titus gave of their godly sorrow, which his former epistle had
wrought in them; [Co2 7:13](../../../bib/kjv/co2007.htm#013.md), and of
their loving-kindness and obedience toward Titus, answerable to his
former boastings of them.
