<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">kg2 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Kg2 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#001.md), Jehoram's reign; [Kg2
3:4](../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#004.md), Mesha rebels; [Kg2
3:6](../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#006.md), Jehoram, with Jehoshaphat, and
the king of Edom, being distressed for want of water, by Elisha obtains
water, and promise of victory; [Kg2
3:21](../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#021.md), The Moabites, deceived by the
colour of the water, coming to spoil, are overcome; [Kg2
3:26](../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#026.md), The king of Moab, failing in his
attempt to break through to the king of Edom, sacrifices his son, and
raises the siege.
