### [Psalms Chapter 58](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 58:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 58:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#001.md), David reproves wicked
judges; [Psa 58:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#003.md), describes the
nature of the wicked; [Psa 58:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#006.md),
devotes them to God's judgments; [Psa
58:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#010.md), whereat the righteous shall
rejoice.

burst by incantation."

a snail: Shabbelool, in Chaldee tivlala, the snail, is probably so

slime emitted from its body, by which it appears to waste itself away in
its own motion; and in the same manner the wicked prove their own
destruction.
