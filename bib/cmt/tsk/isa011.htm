Isaiah Chapter 11

Isaiah

isa 11:0

Overview

Isa 11:1, The peaceable kingdom of the Branch out of the root of Jesse; Isa 11:10, The victorious restoration of Israel, and vocation of the Gentiles.

Isaiah 11:1

isa 11:1

And there shall: The prophet having described the destruction of the Assyrian army under that of a mighty forest, here takes occasion to represent the Great Person, who makes the subject of this chapter, as a slender twig, shooting out of the trunk of an old tree; which tender twig, though weak in appearance, should become fruitful and prosper.
