### [Ezekiel Chapter 16](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#001.md)

[Ezekiel](../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 16:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#001.md), Under the similitude of a
wretched infant is shewn the natural state of Jerusalem; [Eze
16:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#006.md), God's extraordinary love towards
her; [Eze 16:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#015.md), Her monstrous
whoredom; [Eze 16:35](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#035.md), Her grievous
judgment; [Eze 16:44](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#044.md), Her sin, equal
to her mother, and exceeding her sisters, Sodom and Samaria, calls for
judgments; [Eze 16:60](../../../bib/kjv/eze016.htm#060.md), Mercy is
promised her in the end.
