Overview

[Isa 46:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa046.htm#001.md), The idols of Babylon could
not save themselves; [Isa 46:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa046.htm#003.md), God
saves his people to the end; [Isa
46:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa046.htm#005.md), Idols are not comparable to God
for power, [Isa 46:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa046.htm#012.md). or present
salvation.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">isa 46:1</span></span>

Baal; and Nebo is interpreted by Castell and Norberg of Mercury; the two
principal idols of Babylon. When that city was taken by the Persians,
