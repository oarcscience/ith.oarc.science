Ezekiel Chapter 24

Ezekiel

eze 24:0

Overview

Eze 24:1, Under the parable of a boiling pot, Eze 24:6, is shewn the irrevocable destruction of Jerusalem; Eze 24:15, By the sign of Ezekiel not mourning for the death of his wife, Eze 24:19. is shewn the calamity of the Jews to be beyond all sorrow.
