A Prayer: David was twice in great peril in caves. on one occasion, in
the cave of Adullam, when he fled from Achish king of Gath; and on
another, in the cave of Engedi, where he had taken refuge from the
pursuit of Saul. It is not certain to which of these events this Psalm
refers; though probably to the former.
