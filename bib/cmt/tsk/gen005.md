[Genesis 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen005.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">gen 5:3</span></span>

am 130, bc 3874

hundred: The chronology differs in the Hebrew Text, the Samaritan, the
LXX, and Josephus. The LXX adds 100 years to each of the patriarchs
Adam, Seth, Enos, Cainan, Mahalaleel, and Enoch, before the birth of
their sons; while they take 20 from the age of Methuselah, and add 6 to
that of Lamech Thus the space from the creation to the deluge is made
2,242 years, according to the Vatican copy, but 2,262 by the
Alexandrine; and the sum total by Josephus is 2,265, by the Samaritan
1,307, and the Hebrew Text, 1,656. The sum total from the Deluge to the
70th year of Terah, according to these authorities, is, Heb. 292; Sam.
942; Sept. Vat. 1,172; Alex. 1,072, and Josephus 1,002.
