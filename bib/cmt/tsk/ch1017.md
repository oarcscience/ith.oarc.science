Overview

[Ch1 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1017.htm#001), Nathan first approving the
purpose of David, to build God a house, [Ch1
17:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch1017.htm#003), after by the word of God forbids
him; [Ch1 17:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1017.htm#011), He promises him
blessings and benefits in his seed; [Ch1
17:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1017.htm#016), David's prayer and
thanksgiving.
