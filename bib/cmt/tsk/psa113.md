[Psa 113:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa113.htm#001), An exhortation to praise
God for his excellency; [Psa 113:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa113.htm#006),
for his mercy.

[Psalms 113:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa113.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 113:1</span></span>

(Title), This and the following five Psalms form what is called by the
Hebrews the great Hallel, or praise; which was sung on their most solemn
festivals, and particularly after the celebration of the Passover. (See
[Mat 26:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#030). [Mar
14:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar014.htm#026).) This and the following were
probably composed after the return from the captivity.
