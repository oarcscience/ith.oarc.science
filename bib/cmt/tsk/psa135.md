Overview

[Psa 135:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#001.md), An exhortation to praise
God for his mercy; [Psa 135:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#005.md), for his
power; [Psa 135:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#008.md), for his judgments;
[Psa 135:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#015.md), The vanity of idols; [Psa
135:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#019.md), An exhortation to bless God.

[Psalms 135:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 135:1</span></span>

am 3000, bc 1004 (Title), Bp. Patrick supposes this to be the morning
hymn which the precentor called upon the Levites to sing at the opening
of the gates of the temple, as the foregoing was sung at the shutting in
the evening; but it is more probable that it was composed by Solomon, to
be sung at the dedication of the temple.
