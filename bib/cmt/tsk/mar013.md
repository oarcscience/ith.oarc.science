<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mar 13:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mar 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#001), Christ foretells the
destruction of the temple; [Mar 13:9](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#009),
the persecutions for the gospel; [Mar
13:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#010), that the gospel must be
preached to all nations; [Mar 13:14](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#014),
that great calamities shall happen to the Jews; [Mar
13:24](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#024), and the manner of his coming to
judgment; [Mar 13:32](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#032), the hour whereof
being known to none, every man is to watch and pray, that we be not
found unprovided, when he comes to each one particularly by death.
