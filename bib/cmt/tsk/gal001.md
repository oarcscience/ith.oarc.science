### [Galatians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Gal 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#001.md), He wonders that they have so
soon left him and the gospel; [Gal
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#008.md), and accurses those that preach
any other gospel than he did; [Gal
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#011.md), He learned the gospel not of
men, but of God; [Gal 1:14](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#014.md), and shows
what he was before his calling; [Gal
1:17](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#017.md), and what he did immediately
after it.
