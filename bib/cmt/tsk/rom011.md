Overview

[Rom 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#001), God has not cast off all
Israel; [Rom 11:7](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#007), Some were elected,
though the rest were hardened; [Rom
11:16](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#016), There is hope of their
conversion; [Rom 11:18](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#018), The Gentiles
may not exult over them; [Rom 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026),
for there is a promise of their salvation; [Rom
11:33](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#033), God's judgments are
unsearchable.
