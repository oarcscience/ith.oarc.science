Overview

[Co2 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#001), That in his assured hope of
immortal glory, [Co2 5:9](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#009), and in
expectation of it, and of the general judgment, he labours to keep a
good conscience; [Co2 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#012), not that
he may herein boast of himself, [Co2
5:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#014). but as one that, having received
life from Christ, endeavours to live as a new creature to Christ only,
[Co2 5:18](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#018). and by his ministry of
reconciliation, to reconcile others also in Christ to God.
