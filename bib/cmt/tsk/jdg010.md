### [Judges Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#001)

[Judges](../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jdg 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jdg 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#001), Tola judges Israel in
Shamir; [Jdg 10:3](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#003), Jair, whose thirty
sons had thirty cities; [Jdg 10:6](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#006), The
Philistines and Ammonites oppress Israel; [Jdg
10:10](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#010), In their misery God sends them
to their false gods; [Jdg 10:15](../../../bib/kjv/jdg010.htm#015), Upon
their repentance he pities them.

grieved: Heb. shortened, Not that there is any grief in God; he has
infinite joy and happiness in himself, which cannot be broken in upon by
either the sins or the miseries of his creatures. Not that there is any
change in God; for he is of one mind, and who can turn him? But his
goodness is his glory; by it he proclaims his name, and magnifies it;
and as he is pleased to put himself into the relation of a father to his
people, so he is pleased to represent his goodness to them by the
compassion of a father to his children; for as he is the Father of
lights, so is he the Father of mercies.
