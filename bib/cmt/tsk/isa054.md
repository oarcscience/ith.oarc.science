### [Isaiah Chapter 54](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#001)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 54:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 54:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#001), The prophet, for the
comfort of the Gentiles, prophesies the amplitude of their church; [Isa
54:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#004), their safety; [Isa
54:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#006), their certain deliverance out of
affliction; [Isa 54:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#011), their fair
edification; [Isa 54:15](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#015), and their
sure preservation.
