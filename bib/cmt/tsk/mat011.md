### [Matthew Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#001.md)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#001.md), John sends his disciples to
Christ; [Mat 11:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#007.md), Christ's testimony
concerning John; [Mat 11:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#016.md), The
perverse judgment of the people; [Mat
11:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#020.md), Christ upbraids Chorazin,
Bethsaida, and Capernaum; [Mat 11:25](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#025.md),
and praising his Father's wisdom in revealing the Gospel to the simple,
[Mat 11:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#028.md). he calls to him all such
as feel the burden of their sins.
