<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#001), Rehoboam raising an army to
subdue Israel, is forbidden by Shemaiah; [Ch2
11:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#005), He strengthens his kingdom with
forts and provisions; [Ch2 11:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#013), The
priests and Levites, and such as feared God, forsaken by Jeroboam,
strengthen the kingdom of Judah; [Ch2
11:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#018), The wives and children of
Rehoboam.
