Overview

[Psa 29:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa029.htm#001.md), David exhorts princes to
give glory to God; [Psa 29:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa029.htm#003.md), by
reason of his power; [Psa 29:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa029.htm#011.md), and
protection of his people.
