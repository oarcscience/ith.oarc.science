### [Luke Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001)

[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001), Christ teaches us to pray,
and that instantly; [Luk 11:11](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#011),
assuring us that God will give all good things to them that ask him;
[Luk 11:14](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#014), He, casting out a dumb
devil, rebukes the blasphemous Pharisees; [Luk
11:27](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#027), and shows who are blessed; [Luk
11:29](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#029), preaches to the people; [Luk
11:37](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#037), and reprehends the outward shew
of holiness in the Pharisees, scribes, and lawyers.
