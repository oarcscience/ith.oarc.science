<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 147:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 147:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#001.md), The prophet exhorts to
praise God for his care of the church; [Psa
147:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#004.md), his power and wisdom; [Psa
147:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#006.md), his mercy; [Psa
147:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#007.md), his providence; [Psa
147:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#012.md), to praise him for his
blessings upon the kingdom; [Psa
147:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#015.md), for his power over the
elements; [Psa 147:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#019.md), and for his
ordinances in the church.
