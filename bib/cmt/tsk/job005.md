Overview

[Job 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/job005.htm#001.md), Eliphaz shews that the end
of the wicked is misery; [Job 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/job005.htm#006.md),
that man is born to trouble; [Job 5:8](../../../bib/kjv/job005.htm#008.md),
that God is to be regarded in affliction; [Job
5:17](../../../bib/kjv/job005.htm#017.md), the happy end of God's
correction.
