### [Genesis Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

Overview

[Gen 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001), God creates heaven and
earth; [Gen 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#003), the light; [Gen
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#006), the firmament; [Gen
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#009), separates the dry land; [Gen
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#014), forms the sun, moon, and stars;
[Gen 1:20](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#020), fishes and fowls; [Gen
1:24](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#024), cattle, wild beasts, and
creeping things; [Gen 1:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#026), creates
man in his own image, blesses him; [Gen
1:29](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#029), grants the fruits of the earth
for food.
