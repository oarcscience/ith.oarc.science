Isaiah Chapter 31

Isaiah

isa 31:0

Overview

Isa 31:1, The prophet shews the folly and danger of trusting to Egypt, and forsaking God; Isa 31:6, He exhorts to conversion; Isa 31:8, He shews the fall of Assyria.
