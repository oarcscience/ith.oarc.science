### [Psalms Chapter 126](../../../bib/kjv/psa126.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 126:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 126:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa126.htm#001.md), The church, celebrating
her incredible return out of captivity, [Psa
126:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa126.htm#004.md), prays for, and prophesies the
good success thereof.

This Psalm evidently appears to have been composed in consequence of the
proclamation of Cyrus in favour of the Jews, giving them leave to return
to their own land, and rebuild their city and temple. [Psa
120:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#001.md), [Psa
121:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001.md), [Psa
122:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa122.htm#001.md), [Psa
123:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa123.htm#001.md), [Psa
