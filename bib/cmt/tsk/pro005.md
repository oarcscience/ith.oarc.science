[Proverbs](../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">pro 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Pro 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#001.md), Solomon exhorts to wisdom;
[Pro 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#003.md), He shews the mischief of
whoredom and riot; [Pro 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#015.md), He
exhorts to contentedness, liberality, and chastity; [Pro
5:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#022.md), The wicked are overtaken with
their own sins.
