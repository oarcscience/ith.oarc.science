### [Psalms Chapter 87](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 87:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 87:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001.md), The nature and glory of the
church; [Psa 87:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#004.md), The increase,
honour, and comfort of the members thereof.

It is highly probable that this Psalm was written by one of the
descendants of Korah on the return from the Babylonian captivity. It
seems to have been written in praise of Jerusalem; and, typically, of
the Christian church.
