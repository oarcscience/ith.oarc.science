### [Daniel Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#001.md)

[Daniel](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">dan 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Dan 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#001.md), Nebuchadnezzar, forgetting
his dream, requires it of the Chaldeans, by promises and threatenings;
[Dan 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#010.md), They acknowledging their
inability are judged to die; [Dan
2:14](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#014.md), Daniel obtaining some respite
finds the dream; [Dan 2:19](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#019.md), He blesses
God; [Dan 2:24](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#024.md), He staying the decree
is brought to the king; [Dan 2:31](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#031.md), The
dream; [Dan 2:36](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#036.md), The interpretation;
[Dan 2:46](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#046.md), Daniel's advancement.
