<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 14:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#001.md), Paul and Barnabas are
persecuted from Iconium; [Act 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#008.md), At
Lystra Paul heals a cripple, whereupon they are reputed as gods; [Act
14:19](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#019.md), Paul is stoned; [Act
14:21](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#021.md), They pass through divers
churches, confirming the disciples in faith and patience; [Act
14:26](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#026.md), Returning to Antioch, they
report what God had done with them.
