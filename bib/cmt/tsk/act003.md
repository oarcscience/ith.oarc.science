### [Acts Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#001), Peter preaching to the
people that came to see a lame man restored to his feet, [Act
3:12](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#012). professes the cure not to have
been wrought by his or John's own power or holiness, but by God, and his
son Jesus, and through faith in his name; [Act
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#013), withal reprehending them for
crucifying Jesus; [Act 3:17](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#017), which
because they did it through ignorance, and that thereby were fulfilled
God's determinate counsel, and the Scriptures, [Act
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#019). he exhorts them by repentance
and faith to seek remission of their sins, and salvation in the same
Jesus.
