Overview

[Psa 56:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#001.md), David, praying to God in
confidence of his word, complains of his enemies; [Psa
56:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#009.md), He professes his confidence in
God's word, and promises to praise him.

instrument.
