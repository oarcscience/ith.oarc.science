### [Job Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Job 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001.md), The holiness, riches, and
religious care of Job for his children; [Job
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#006.md), Satan, appearing before God, by
calumniation obtains leave to afflict Job; [Job
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#013.md), Understanding of the loss of his
children and goods, in his mourning he blesses God.
