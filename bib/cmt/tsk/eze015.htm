Ezekiel Chapter 15

Ezekiel

eze 15:0

Overview

Eze 15:1, By the unfitness of the vine branch for any work, Eze 15:6, is shewn the rejection of Jerusalem.

Ezekiel 15:2

eze 15:2

What: The vine is only noble and useful while producing fruit: for, when cut down, its wood is fit only for fuel. So Israel, having ceased to be fruitful, they are good for nothing, but, like a withered branch of a vine, to be burnt.
