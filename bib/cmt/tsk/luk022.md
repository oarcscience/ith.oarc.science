[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 22:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#001), The Jews conspire against
Christ; [Luk 22:3](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#003), Satan prepares
Judas to betray him; [Luk 22:7](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#007), The
apostles prepare the passover; [Luk
22:19](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#019), Christ institutes his holy
supper; [Luk 22:21](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#021), covertly foretells
of the traitor; [Luk 22:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#024), rebukes
the rest of his apostles from ambition; [Luk
22:31](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#031), assures Peter his faith should
not fail; [Luk 22:34](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#034), and yet he
should deny him thrice; [Luk 22:39](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#039), He
prays in the mount, and sweats blood; [Luk
22:47](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#047), is betrayed with a kiss; [Luk
22:50](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#050), he heals Malchus' ear; [Luk
22:54](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#054), he is thrice denied of Peter;
[Luk 22:63](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#063), shamefully abused; [Luk
22:66](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#066), and confesses himself to be the
Son of God.

Matthew and Mark; yet here it is said ετερος \[Strong's G2087\], another

female; and Matthew says, "she said to them that were there," and Mark,
"she began to say to them that stood by." So that the maid gave the
information to those around her, and some man charged Peter with it.
