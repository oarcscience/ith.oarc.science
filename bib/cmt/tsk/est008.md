8:3](../../../bib/kjv/est008.htm#003), Esther makes suit to reverse

India: The Hebrew word Hoddo, in Syriac, Hendoo, and in Arabic, Hind, is
rendered India by all the versions. India, or Hindostan, is a large
country of the south of Asia, extending from north to south about 2,400
miles, and from east to west 1,800, between 8 degrees and 35 degrees n
lat. and 68 degrees and 92 degrees e long.; being bounded on the west by
the Indus, east by the Birman empire and Thibet, north by the Indian
Caucasus, and south by the Indian Ocean. It is probable, however, that
all the country east of the Indus was anciently called India.

and with a great crown: Mordecai was now made the chief minister, or
vizier, instead of Haman; and was accordingly invested with the "royal
apparel," in conformity to the custom of the East. So we are informed,
in the History of the Revolt of Ali Bey, that on the election of a new
sheikh bellet, or chief of the country, in Egypt, the pasha who approves
of him invests him with a robe of valuable fur. Perhaps the crown was
one of the insignia of the office of vizier. Concerning the blue, fine
linen, and purple; see note on [Exo
25:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#004), and see note on [Exo
39:27](../../../bib/kjv/exo039.htm#027).
