### [Hosea Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001.md)

[Hosea](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hos 1:0</span></span>

Overview

[Hos 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001.md), Hosea, to shew God's
judgment for spiritual whoredom, takes Gomer, [Hos
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#004.md), and has by her Jezreel; [Hos
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#006.md), Loruhamah; [Hos
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#008.md), and Lo-ammi; [Hos
1:10](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#010.md), The restoration of Judah and
Israel under one head.
