Psalms Chapter 52

Psalms

psa 52:0

Overview

Psa 52:1, David, condemning the spitefulness of Doeg, prophesies his destruction; Psa 52:6, The righteous shall rejoice at it; Psa 52:8, David, upon his confidence in God's mercy, gives thanks.
