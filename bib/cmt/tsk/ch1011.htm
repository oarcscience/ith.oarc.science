1 Chronicles Chapter 11

1 Chronicles

ch1 11:0

Overview

Ch1 11:1, David by general consent is made king at Hebron; Ch1 11:4, He wins the castle of Zion from the Jebusites by Joab's valour; Ch1 11:10, A catalogue of David's mighty men.
