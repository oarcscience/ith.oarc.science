### [2 Peter Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#001.md)

[2 Peter](../../../bib/kjv/pe2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">pe2 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Pe2 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#001.md), He assures them of the
certainty of Christ's coming to judgment, against those scorners who
dispute against it; [Pe2 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#008.md), warning
the godly, for the long patience of God, to hasten their repentance;
[Pe2 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#010.md), He describes also the
manner how the world shall be destroyed; [Pe2
3:11](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#011.md), exhorting them, from the
expectation thereof, to all holiness of life; [Pe2
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#016.md), and again to think the patience
of God to tend to their salvation, as Paul wrote to them in his
epistles.
