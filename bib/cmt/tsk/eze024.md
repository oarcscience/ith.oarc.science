<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 24:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#001.md), Under the parable of a
boiling pot, [Eze 24:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#006.md), is shewn the
irrevocable destruction of Jerusalem; [Eze
24:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#015.md), By the sign of Ezekiel not
mourning for the death of his wife, [Eze
24:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze024.htm#019.md). is shewn the calamity of the
Jews to be beyond all sorrow.
