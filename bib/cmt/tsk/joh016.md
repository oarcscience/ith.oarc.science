Overview

[Joh 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#001.md), Christ comforts his
disciples by the promise of the Holy Ghost, and his ascension; [Joh
16:23](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#023.md), assures their prayers made in
his name to be acceptable; [Joh 16:33](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#033.md),
Peace in Christ, and in the world affliction.
