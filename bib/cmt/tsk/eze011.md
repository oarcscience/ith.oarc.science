Overview

[Eze 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#001), The presumption of the
princes; [Eze 11:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#004), Their sin and
judgment; [Eze 11:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#013), Ezekiel
complaining, God shews him his purpose in saving a remnant; [Eze
11:21](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#021), and punishing the wicked; [Eze
11:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#022), The glory of God leaves the
city; [Eze 11:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#024), Ezekiel is returned
to the captivity.
