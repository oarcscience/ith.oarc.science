Thou shalt not seethe a kid: The true sense of this passage seems to be
that assigned by Dr. Cudworth, from a manuscript comment of a Karaite
Jew. "It was a custom with the ancient heathens, when they had gathered
in all their fruits, to take a kid, and boil it in the dam's milk; and
