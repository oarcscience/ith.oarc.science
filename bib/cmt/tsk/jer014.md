14:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer014.htm#007), causes Jeremiah to pray; [Jer
14:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer014.htm#010), The Lord will not be intreated

for them.

[Jeremiah 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer014.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 14:1</span></span>

am 3399, bc 605

The word: This discourse is supposed to have been delivered after the
fourth year of Jehoiakim. The Hebrew batzaroth rendered dearth,
signifies restraint, that is, "when the heaven is shut up that there is
no rain;" which Houbigant thinks happened early in the reign of
Zedekiah.

the dearth: Heb. the words of the dearths, or restraints, [Jer
17:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#008)
