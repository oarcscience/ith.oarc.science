### [Psalms Chapter 70](../../../bib/kjv/psa070.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 70:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 70:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa070.htm#001.md), David solicits God to the
speedy destruction of the wicked, and preservation of the godly.

This Psalm is almost word for word the same as the five [Psa
40:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#013.md) of Psalm 40; and it is written
as a part of the succeeding Psalm in about 27 manuscripts. Both Psalms
evidently appear to have been written by David during the rebellion of
Absalom, and probably at the crisis when he heard of the sanguinary
counsel which Ahithophel had given respecting him; or, as some suppose,
when beyond Jordan. 2Sam. 17:1-21
