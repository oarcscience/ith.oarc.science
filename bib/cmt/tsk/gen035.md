Overview

[Gen 35:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#001), God commands Jacob to go to
Bethel; [Gen 35:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#002), He purges his house
of idols; [Gen 35:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#006), He builds an
altar at Bethel; [Gen 35:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#008), Deborah
dies at Allon-bachuth; [Gen 35:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#009), God
blesses Jacob at Bethel; [Gen 35:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#016),
Rachel travails of Benjamin, and dies in the way to Edar; [Gen
35:22](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#022), Reuben lies with Bilhah; [Gen
35:23](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#023), The sons of Jacob; [Gen
35:27](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#027), Jacob comes to Isaac at Hebron;
[Gen 35:28](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#028), The age, death, and burial
of Isaac.
