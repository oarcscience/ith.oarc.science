Overview

[Joh 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#001.md), Jesus reproves the ambition
and boldness of his kinsmen; [Joh
7:10](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#010.md), goes up from Galilee to the
feast of tabernacles; [Joh 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#014.md),
teaches in the temple; [Joh 7:40](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#040.md),
Divers opinions of him among the people; [Joh
7:45](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#045.md), The Pharisees are angry that
their officers took him not, and chide with Nicodemus for taking his
part.
