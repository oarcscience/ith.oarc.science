### [Hosea Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/hos003.htm#001)

[Hosea](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hos 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Hos 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos003.htm#001), The Lord's intended future
kindness to Israel, not withstanding their wickedness, illustrated by
the emblem of Hosea's conduct towards his adulterous wife; [Hos
3:4](../../../bib/kjv/hos003.htm#004), The desolation of Israel before
their restoration.
