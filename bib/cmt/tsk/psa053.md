Overview

[Psa 53:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#001), David describes the
corruption of a natural man; [Psa
53:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#004), He convinces the wicked by the
light of their own conscience; [Psa
53:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#006), He glories in the salvation of
God.
