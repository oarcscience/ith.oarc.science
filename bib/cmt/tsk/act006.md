### [Acts Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#001.md)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 6:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#001.md), The apostles, desirous to
have the poor regarded for their bodily sustenance, as also careful
themselves to dispense the word of God, the food of the soul, [Act
6:3](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#003.md), recommend, [Act
6:5](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#005.md), and with the church's consent
ordain seven chosen men to the office of deaconship; [Act
6:7](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#007.md), The word of God prevails, [Act
6:8](../../../bib/kjv/act006.htm#008.md), Stephen, full of faith and the
Holy Ghost, confuting those with whom he disputed, is brought before the
council, and falsely accused of blasphemy against the law and the
temple.
