[John](../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">joh 14:0</span></span>

Overview

[Joh 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#001.md), Christ comforts his
disciples with the hope of heaven; [Joh
14:5](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#005.md), professes himself the way, the
truth, and the life, and one with the Father; [Joh
14:13](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#013.md), assures their prayers to be
effectual; [Joh 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#015.md), requires
obedience; [Joh 14:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#016.md), promises the
Comforter; [Joh 14:27](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#027.md), and leaves his
peace with them.
