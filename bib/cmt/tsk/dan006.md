Overview

[Dan 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#001), Daniel is made chief of the
presidents; [Dan 6:4](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#004), They, conspiring
against him, obtain an idolatrous decree; [Dan
6:10](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#010), Daniel, accused of the breach
thereof, is cast into the lion's den; [Dan
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#018), Daniel is saved; [Dan
6:24](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#024), his adversaries devoured; [Dan
6:25](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#025), and God magnified by a decree.
