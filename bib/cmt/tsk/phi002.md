<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">phi 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Phi 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#001.md), Paul exhorts them to unity,
and to all humbleness of mind, by the example of Christ's humility and
exaltation; [Phi 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#012.md), to a careful
proceeding in the way of salvation, that they be as lights to the wicked
world, [Phi 2:16](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#016.md). and comforts to him
their apostle, who is now ready to be offered up to God; [Phi
2:19](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#019.md), He hopes to send Timothy to
them, and Epaphroditus also.
