Overview

[Kg1 18:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#001), In the extremity of famine
Elijah, sent to Ahab, meets good Obadiah; [Kg1
18:9](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#009), Obadiah brings Ahab to Elijah;
[Kg1 18:17](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#017), Elijah, reproving Ahab, by
fire from heaven convinces Baal's prophets; [Kg1
18:41](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#041), Elijah, by prayer obtaining
rain, follows Ahab to Jezreel.

mount Carmel: Mount Carmel is situated north of Dora and south of
Ptolemais or Acre, from which it is distant, according to Josephus, 120
stadia, or, according to Thevenot, 10 miles; one of its principal points
advancing considerably into the Mediterranean, and forming an elevated
promontory. It is described as a flattened cone, about 2,000 feet (some
say 1,500) in height, very rocky, its sides steep and rugged, and the
soil neither deep nor rich. Capt. Mangles says its is now quite barren,
though at the north-eastern foot of it there are some pretty
olive-grounds. [Kg1 18:42](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#042), [Kg1
