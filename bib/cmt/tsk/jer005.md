### [Jeremiah Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#001), The judgments of God upon
the Jews, for their perverseness; [Jer
5:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#007), for their adultery; [Jer
5:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#010), for their impiety; [Jer
5:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#015), for their worship of idols; [Jer
5:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#019), for their contempt of God; [Jer
5:25](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#025), and for their great corruption
in the civil state; [Jer 5:30](../../../bib/kjv/jer005.htm#030), and
ecclesiastical.
