### [Amos Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/amo008.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Amo 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/amo008.htm#001.md), By a basket of summer fruit
is shown the approach of Israel's end; [Amo
8:4](../../../bib/kjv/amo008.htm#004.md), Oppression is reproved; [Amo
8:11](../../../bib/kjv/amo008.htm#011.md), A famine of the word of God
threatened.
