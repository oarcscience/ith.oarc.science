<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 23:4</span></span>

the names: The kingdom of Israel, of which Samaria was the capital,
containing ten tribes, and occupying a larger extent of country than
that of Judah, is therefore called "her elder sister;" and Aholah, the
name given to her, implies that the whole religious establishment in
Israel was a human invention, a temple and service of their own, and not
of God's appointment. Aholibah, the name given to Judah, implies that
the worship established there was from God, and that His temple was
truly at Jerusalem.
