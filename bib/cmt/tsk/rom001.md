### [Romans Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#001)

Overview

[Rom 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#001), Paul commends his calling to
the Romans; [Rom 1:9](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#009), and his desire
to come to them; [Rom 1:16](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016), What his
gospel is; [Rom 1:18](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#018), God is angry
with sin; [Rom 1:21](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#021), What were the
sins of the gentiles.
