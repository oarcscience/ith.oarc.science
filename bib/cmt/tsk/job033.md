<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">job 33:0</span></span>

Overview

[Job 33:1](../../../bib/kjv/job033.htm#001), Elihu offers himself
instead of God to reason with Job; [Job
33:8](../../../bib/kjv/job033.htm#008), He excuses God from giving man
an account of his ways, by his greatness; [Job
33:14](../../../bib/kjv/job033.htm#014), God calls man to repentance by
visions, by afflictions, and by his ministry; [Job
33:31](../../../bib/kjv/job033.htm#031), He incites Job to attention.
