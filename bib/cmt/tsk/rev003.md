[Rev 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#001.md), The angel of the church of
Sardis is reproved; [Rev 3:3](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#003.md), exhorted
to repent, and threatened if he do not repent; [Rev
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#008.md), The angel of the church of

3:15](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#015.md), the angel of Laodicea rebuked
for being neither hot nor cold, [Rev
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#019.md). and admonished to be more
zealous; [Rev 3:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#020.md), Christ stands at
the door and knocks.
