Cabul: that is, Displeasing, or dirty. Josephus says that Cabul, in the
Phoenician language, signifies ουκ αρεσκον, displeasing; and that these
cities were situated in the neighbourhood of Tyre. Most commentators are
persuaded that the city Cabul in the tribe of Asher was one; and
probably from this Hiram took occasion to give this name to all the
other cities which Solomon had ceded to him. [Jos
19:27](../../../bib/kjv/jos019.htm#027)
