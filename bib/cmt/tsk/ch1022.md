### [1 Chronicles Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/ch1022.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch1 22:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch1 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1022.htm#001), David, foreknowing the
place of the temple, prepares abundance for the building of it; [Ch1
22:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1022.htm#006), He instructs Solomon in God's
promises, and his duty in building the temple; [Ch1
22:17](../../../bib/kjv/ch1022.htm#017), He charges the princes to
assist his son.

[1 Chronicles 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1022.htm#001)

place on which God designed that His house should be built; and perhaps
