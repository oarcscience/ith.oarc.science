16:10](../../../bib/kjv/co1016.htm#010), Commends Timothy; [Co1
16:13](../../../bib/kjv/co1016.htm#013), and after friendly admonitions,
[Co1 16:16](../../../bib/kjv/co1016.htm#016). concludes his epistle with
divers salutations.
