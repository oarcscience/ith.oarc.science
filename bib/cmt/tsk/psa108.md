<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 108:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 108:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa108.htm#001), David encourages himself
to praise God; [Psa 108:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa108.htm#005), He prays
for God's assistance according to his promise; [Psa
108:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa108.htm#011), His confidence in God's help.
