<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 16:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2016.htm#001.md), Asa, by the aid of the
Syrians, diverts Baasha from building Ramah; [Ch2
16:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch2016.htm#007.md), Being reproved thereof by
Hanani, he puts him in prison; [Ch2
16:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch2016.htm#011.md), Among his other acts in his
disease he seeks not to God, but to the physicians; [Ch2
16:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch2016.htm#013.md), His death and burial.
