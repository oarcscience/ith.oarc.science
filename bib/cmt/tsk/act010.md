### [Acts Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#001.md)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#001.md), Cornelius, a devout man,
being commanded by an angel, sends for Peter, [Act
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#011.md). who by a vision is taught not
to despise the Gentiles; [Act 10:17](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#017.md),
and is commanded by the Spirit to go with the messenger to Caesarea;
[Act 10:25](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#025.md), Cornelius shows the
occasion of his sending for him; [Act
10:34](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#034.md), As he preaches Christ to
Cornelius and his company, [Act 10:44](../../../bib/kjv/act010.htm#044.md).
the Holy Ghost falls on them, and they are baptized.
