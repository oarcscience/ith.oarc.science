### [Luke Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#001.md)

[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#001.md), Women minister unto Christ
of their substance; [Luk 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#004.md), Christ,
after he had preached from place to place, attended by his apostles,
propounds the parable of the sower, [Luk
8:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#016.md). and the candle; [Luk
8:19](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#019.md), declares who are his mother, and
brethren; [Luk 8:22](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#022.md), rebukes the
winds; [Luk 8:26](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#026.md), casts the legion of
devils out of the man into the herd of swine; [Luk
8:37](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#037.md), is rejected of the Gadarenes,;
[Luk 8:43](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#043.md), heals the woman of her
bloody issue; [Luk 8:49](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#049.md), and raises
from death Jairus's daughter.
