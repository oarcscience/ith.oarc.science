Overview

[Joh 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#001), Jesus washes the disciples'
feet, and exhorts them to humility and charity; [Joh
13:18](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#018), He foretells and discovers to
John by a token, that Judas should betray him; v.31, commands them to
love one another; v.36, and forewarns Peter of his denials.
