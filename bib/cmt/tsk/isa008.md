Overview

[Isa 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#001), In Maher-shalal-hash-baz, he
prophesies that Syria and Israel shall be subdued by Assyria; [Isa
8:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#005), Judah likewise for their
infidelity; [Isa 8:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#009), God's judgments
shall be irresistible; [Isa 8:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#011),
Comfort shall be to them that fear God; [Isa
8:19](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#019), Great afflictions to idolaters.
