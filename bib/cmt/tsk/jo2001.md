Overview

[Jo2 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#001), He exhorts a certain
honourable matron, with her children, to persevere in Christian love and
belief, [Jo2 1:8](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#008), lest they lose the
reward of their former profession; [Jo2
1:10](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#010), and to have nothing to do with
those seducers that bring not the true doctrine of Christ Jesus.
