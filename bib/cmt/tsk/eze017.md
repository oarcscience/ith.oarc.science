[Ezekiel](../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 17:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#001.md), Under the parable of two
eagles and a vine, [Eze 17:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#011.md). is
shewn God's judgment upon Jerusalem for revolting from Babylon to Egypt;
[Eze 17:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#022.md), God promises to plant the
cedar of the Gospel.
