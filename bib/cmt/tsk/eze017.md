Overview

[Eze 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#001), Under the parable of two
eagles and a vine, [Eze 17:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#011). is
shewn God's judgment upon Jerusalem for revolting from Babylon to Egypt;
[Eze 17:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#022), God promises to plant the
cedar of the Gospel.
