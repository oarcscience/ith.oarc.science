### [2 Thessalonians Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#001.md)

[2 Thessalonians](../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">th2 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Th2 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#001.md), Paul craves their prayers
for himself; [Th2 3:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#003.md), testifies what
confidence he has in them; [Th2 3:5](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#005.md),
makes request to God in their behalf; [Th2
3:6](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#006.md), gives them divers precepts,
especially to shun idleness, and ill company; [Th2
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#016.md), and then concludes with prayer
and salutation.
