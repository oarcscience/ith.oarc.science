Overview

[Gal 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#001), He shows when he went up
again to Jerusalem, and for what purpose; [Gal
2:3](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#003), and that Titus was not
circumcised; [Gal 2:11](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#011), and that he
resisted Peter, and told him the reason; [Gal
2:14](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#014), why he and others, being Jews,
do believe in Christ to be justified by faith, and not by works; [Gal
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#020), and that they live not in sin,
who are so justified.
