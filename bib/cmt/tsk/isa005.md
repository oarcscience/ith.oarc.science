Overview

[Isa 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#001), Under the parable of a
vineyard, God excuses his severe judgment; [Isa
5:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#008), His judgments upon covetousness;
[Isa 5:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#011), upon lasciviousness; [Isa
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#013), upon impiety; [Isa
5:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#020), and upon injustice; [Isa
5:26](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#026), the executioners of God's
judgments.
