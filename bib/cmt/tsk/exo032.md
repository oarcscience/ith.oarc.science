Overview

[Exo 32:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#001), The people, in the absence
of Moses, cause Aaron to make a calf; [Exo
32:7](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#007), God informs Moses, who
intercedes for Israel, and prevails; [Exo
32:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#015), Moses comes down with the
tables; [Exo 32:19](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#019), He breaks them;
[Exo 32:20](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#020), He destroys the calf; [Exo
32:22](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#022), Aaron's excuse for himself;
[Exo 32:25](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#025), Moses causes the idolaters
to be slain; [Exo 32:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#030), He prays for
the people.
