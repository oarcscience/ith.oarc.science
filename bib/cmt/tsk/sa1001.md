### [1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Sa1 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001.md), Elkanah, a Levite, having
two wives, worships yearly at Shiloh; [Sa1
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#004.md), He cherishes Hannah, though
barren, and provoked by Peninnah; [Sa1
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#009.md), Hannah in grief prays for a
child; [Sa1 1:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#012.md), Eli first rebuking
her, afterwards blesses her; [Sa1
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#019.md), Hannah, having born Samuel,
stays at home till he is weaned; [Sa1
1:24](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#024.md), She presents him, according to
her vow, to the Lord.
