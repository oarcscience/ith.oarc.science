[Hos 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos006.htm#001.md), Exhortations to repent and
hope in God; [Hos 6:4](../../../bib/kjv/hos006.htm#004.md), A lamentation
over those who had sinned after conviction; [Hos
6:5](../../../bib/kjv/hos006.htm#005.md), Reproofs of obstinate sinners,
and threatenings against them.
