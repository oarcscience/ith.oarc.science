### [Genesis Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 15:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#001), God encourages Abram, who
complains for want of an heir; [Gen
15:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#004), God promises him a son, and a
multiplying of his seed; [Gen 15:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#006),
Abram is justified by faith; [Gen
15:7](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#007), Canaan is promised again, and
confirmed by a sign, and a vision, prophetic of the condition of his
posterity till brought out of Egypt.
