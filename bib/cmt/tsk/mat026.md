<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 26:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 26:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#001.md), Christ foretells his own
death; [Mat 26:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#003.md), The rulers conspire
against him; [Mat 26:6](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#006.md), The woman
anoints his feet; [Mat 26:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#014.md), Judas
bargains to betray him; [Mat 26:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#017.md),
Christ eats the passover; [Mat 26:26](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#026.md),
institutes his holy supper; [Mat
26:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#030.md), foretells the desertion of his
disciples, and Peter's denial; [Mat
26:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#036.md), prays in the garden; [Mat
26:47](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#047.md), and being betrayed by a kiss,
[Mat 26:57](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#057.md). is carried to Caiaphas,
[Mat 26:69](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#069.md). and denied of Peter.
