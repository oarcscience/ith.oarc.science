### [Isaiah Chapter 51](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 51:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 51:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#001.md), An exhortation, after the
pattern of Abraham, to trust in Christ, [Isa
51:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#003.md), by reason of his comfortable
promises, [Isa 51:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#004.md), of his righteous
salvation, [Isa 51:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#007.md), and man's
mortality; [Isa 51:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#009.md), Christ by his
sanctified arm defends his from the fear of man; [Isa
51:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#017.md), He bewails the afflictions of
Jerusalem, [Isa 51:21](../../../bib/kjv/isa051.htm#021.md). and promises
deliverance.
