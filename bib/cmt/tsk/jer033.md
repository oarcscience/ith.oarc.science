### [Jeremiah Chapter 33](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#001)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 33:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 33:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#001), God promises to the
captivity a gracious return; [Jer
33:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#009), a joyful state; [Jer
33:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#012), a settled government; [Jer
33:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#015), Christ the branch of
righteousness; [Jer 33:17](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#017), a
continuance of kingdom and priesthood; [Jer
33:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#019), and a stability of a blessed
seed.
