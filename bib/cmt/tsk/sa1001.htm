1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 1

Overview

Sa1 1:1, Elkanah, a Levite, having two wives, worships yearly at Shiloh; Sa1 1:4, He cherishes Hannah, though barren, and provoked by Peninnah; Sa1 1:9, Hannah in grief prays for a child; Sa1 1:12, Eli first rebuking her, afterwards blesses her; Sa1 1:19, Hannah, having born Samuel, stays at home till he is weaned; Sa1 1:24, She presents him, according to her vow, to the Lord.

Ramathaimzophim: This ancient town, now called Ramla, is, according to Phocas, about thirty-six miles west of Jerusalem, and, according to modern travellers, about nine miles from Joppa and a league from Lydda, between which it is situated. It is built on a rising ground, on a rich plain, and contains about two thousand families. Sa1 1:19; Mat 27:57, Arimathea
