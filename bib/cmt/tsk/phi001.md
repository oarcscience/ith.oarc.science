### [Philippians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#001)

Overview

[Phi 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#001), Paul testifies his
thankfulness to God, and his love towards them, for the fruits of their
faith, and fellowship in his sufferings; [Phi
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#009), daily praying to him for their
increase in grace; [Phi 1:12](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#012), he shows
what good the faith of Christ had received by his troubles at Rome; [Phi
1:21](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#021), and how ready he is to glorify
Christ either by his life or death; [Phi
1:27](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#027), exhorting them to unity; [Phi
1:28](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#028), and to fortitude in persecution.
