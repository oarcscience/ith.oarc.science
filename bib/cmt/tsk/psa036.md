### [Psalms Chapter 36](../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#001)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 36:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 36:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#001), The grievous estate of the
wicked; [Psa 36:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#008), The excellency of
God's mercy; [Psa 36:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#010), David prays
for favour to God's children.

This Psalm is supposed by some to have been composed by David at the
beginning of Saul's persecutions; but Calmet and others, on good
grounds, are of opinion that it was written during the Babylonian
captivity.
