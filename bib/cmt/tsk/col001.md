### [Colossians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#001)

Overview

[Col 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#001), After salutation Paul thanks
God for their faith; [Col 1:7](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#007),
confirms the doctrine of Epaphras; [Col
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#009), prays further for their increase
in grace; [Col 1:14](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#014), describes the
true Christ; [Col 1:21](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#021), encourages
them to receive Jesus Christ, and commends his own ministry.
