Overview

[Lam 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#001), The prophet bewails his own
calamities; [Lam 3:22](../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#022), By the mercies
of God, he nourishes his hope; [Lam
3:37](../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#037), He acknowledges God's justice;
[Lam 3:55](../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#055), He prays for deliverance,
[Lam 3:64](../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#064). and vengeance on his
enemies.
