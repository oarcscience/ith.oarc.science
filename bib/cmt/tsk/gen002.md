seventh day God: The LXX, Syriac, and the Samaritan Text read the sixth
day, which is probably the true reading; as ו \[Strong's H2053\], which
stands for six, might easily be changed into ז, which denotes seven.

rested: Or, rather, ceased, as the Hebrew word is not opposed to
weariness, but to action; as the Divine Being can neither know fatigue,
nor stand in need of rest.
