### [Luke Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#001.md)

[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#001.md), Christ teaches the people
out of Peter's ship; [Luk 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#004.md), in a
miraculous taking of fishes, shows how he will make him and his partners
fishers of men; [Luk 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#012.md), cleanses
the leper; [Luk 5:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#016.md), prays in the
wilderness; [Luk 5:17](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#017.md), heals one sick
of the palsy; [Luk 5:27](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#027.md), calls Matthew
the Publican; [Luk 5:29](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#029.md), eats with
sinners, as being the physician of souls; [Luk
5:33](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#033.md), foretells the fastings and
afflictions of the apostles after his ascension; [Luk
5:36](../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#036.md), and illustrates the matter by
the parable of old bottles and worn garments.
