<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#001), Saul, going towards
Damascus, is stricken down to the earth, and led blind to Damascus; [Act
9:10](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#010), is called to the apostleship;
[Act 9:18](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#018), and is baptized by Ananias;
[Act 9:20](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#020), He preaches Christ boldly;
[Act 9:23](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#023), The Jews lay wait to kill
him; [Act 9:29](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#029), so do the Grecians,
but he escapes both; [Act 9:31](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#031), The
church having rest, Peter heals Aeneas of the palsy; [Act
9:36](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#036), and restores Tabitha to life.
