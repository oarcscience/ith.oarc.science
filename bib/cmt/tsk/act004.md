### [Acts Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#001.md)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#001.md), The rulers of the Jews,
offended with Peter's sermon, [Act
4:3](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#003.md), imprison him and John; [Act
4:5](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#005.md), After, upon examination Peter
boldly avouching the lame man to be healed by the name of Jesus, and
that by the same Jesus only we must be eternally saved, [Act
4:13](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#013.md). they command him and John to
preach no more in that name, adding also threatening; [Act
4:23](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#023.md), whereupon the church flees to
prayer; [Act 4:31](../../../bib/kjv/act004.htm#031.md), And God, by moving
the place where they were assembled, testifies that he heard their
prayer; confirming the church with the gift of the Holy Ghost, and with
mutual love and charity.
