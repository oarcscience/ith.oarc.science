### [Joel Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#001.md)

Overview

judgments of God, exhorts to observe them, v.8, and to mourn; v.14, He
prescribes a solemn fast to deprecate those judgments.

locusts are no sooner hatched than they collect themselves into compact

them, eating up everything that is green or juicy; not only the lesser
vegetables, but the vine likewise, the fig-tree, the pomegranate, the
