### [Isaiah Chapter 48](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#001)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 48:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 48:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#001), God, to convince the people
of their foreknown obstinancy, revealed his prophecies; [Isa
48:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#009), He saves them for his own sake;
[Isa 48:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#012), He exhorts them to
obedience, because of his power and providence; [Isa
48:16](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#016), He laments their backwardness;
[Isa 48:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#020), He powerfully delivers his
people out of Babylon.
