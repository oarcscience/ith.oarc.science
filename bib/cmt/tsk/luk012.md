### [Luke Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#001)

[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#001), Christ preaches to his
apostles to avoid hypocrisy, and fearfulness in publishing his doctrine;
[Luk 12:13](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#013), warns the people to beware
of covetousness, by the parable of the rich man who set up greater
barns; [Luk 12:22](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#022), We must not be over
careful of earthly things, [Luk 12:31](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#031).
but seek the kingdom of God; [Luk
12:33](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#033), give alms; [Luk
12:35](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#035), be ready at a knock to open to
our Lord whensoever he comes; [Luk
12:41](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#041), Christ's ministers are to see
to their charges, [Luk 12:49](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#049). and look
for persecution; [Luk 12:54](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#054), The
people must take this time of grace; [Luk
12:57](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#057), because it is a fearful thing
to die without reconciliation.
