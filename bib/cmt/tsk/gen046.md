Overview

[Gen 46:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#001.md), Jacob is comforted by God
at Beer-sheba; [Gen 46:5](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#005.md), Thence he
with his company goes into Egypt; [Gen
46:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#008.md), The number of his family that
went into Egypt; [Gen 46:28](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#028.md), Joseph
meets Jacob; [Gen 46:31](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#031.md), He instructs
his brethren how to answer Pharaoh.
