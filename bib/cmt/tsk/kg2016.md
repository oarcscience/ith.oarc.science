Overview

[Kg2 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2016.htm#001), Ahaz's wicked reign; [Kg2
16:5](../../../bib/kjv/kg2016.htm#005), Ahaz, assailed by Rezin and
Pekah, hires Tiglath-pileser against them; [Kg2
16:10](../../../bib/kjv/kg2016.htm#010), Ahaz, sending a pattern of an
altar from Damascus to Urijah, diverts the brazen altar to his own
devotions; [Kg2 16:17](../../../bib/kjv/kg2016.htm#017), He spoils the
temple; [Kg2 16:19](../../../bib/kjv/kg2016.htm#019), Hezekiah succeeds
him.

the covert: There are a great number of conjectures concerning this
covert; but it is probable that it was either, as Locke supposes, a sort
