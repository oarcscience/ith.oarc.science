Overview

[Mar 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#001.md), Christ heals the withered
hand, [Mar 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#010.md). and many other
infirmities; [Mar 3:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#011.md), rebukes the
unclean spirit; [Mar 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#013.md), chooses his
twelve apostles; [Mar 3:22](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#022.md), convinces
the blasphemy of casting out devils by Beelzebub; [Mar
3:31](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#031.md), and shows who are his brother,
sister, and mother.
