Overview

[Psa 64:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa064.htm#001.md), David prays for
deliverance, complaining of his enemies; [Psa
64:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa064.htm#007.md), He promises himself to see such
an evident destruction of his enemies, as the righteous shall rejoice at
it.
