<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jam 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jam 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/jam005.htm#001), Wicked rich men are to fear
God's vengeance; [Jam 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/jam005.htm#007), We ought to
be patient in afflictions, after the example of the prophets, and Job;
v.12, to forbear swearing; v.13, to pray in adversity, to sing in
prosperity; v.14, to acknowledge mutually our several faults, to pray
one for another; v.19, and to reduce a straying brother to the truth.
