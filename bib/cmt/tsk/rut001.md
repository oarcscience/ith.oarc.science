### [Ruth Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Rut 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#001.md), Elimelech, driven by famine
into Moab, dies there; [Rut 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#004.md),
Mahlon and Chilion, having married wives of Moab, die also; [Rut
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#006.md), Naomi, returning homeward, [Rut
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#008.md), dissuades her two
daughters-in-law from going with her; [Rut
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#014.md), Orpah leaves her, but Ruth with
great constancy accompanies her; [Rut
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#019.md), They two come to Beth-lehem,
where they are gladly received.
