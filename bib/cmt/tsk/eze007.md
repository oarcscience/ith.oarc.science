<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 7:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze007.htm#001.md), The final desolation of
Israel; [Eze 7:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze007.htm#016.md), The mournful
repentance of them that escape; [Eze
7:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze007.htm#020.md), The enemies defile the sanctuary
because of the Israelites' abominations; [Eze
7:23](../../../bib/kjv/eze007.htm#023.md), Under the type of a chain is
shewn the miserable captivity of all orders of men.

An end: This is similar to the [Eze
7:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze007.htm#002.md); but there is a paronomasia, or
play upon words, here, deserving of notice: ketz ba, ba hakketz,
