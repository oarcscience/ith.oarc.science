Overview

[Th1 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/th1002.htm#001.md), In what manner the gospel
was brought and preached to the Thessalonians, and in what sort also
they received it; [Th1 2:18](../../../bib/kjv/th1002.htm#018.md), A reason
is rendered both why Paul was so long absent from them, and also why he
was so desirous to see them.
