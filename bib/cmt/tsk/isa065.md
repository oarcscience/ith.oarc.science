Gentiles, and the rejection of the Jews, for their incredulity,
idolatry, and hypocrisy; [Isa 65:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#008), A
remnant shall be saved; [Isa 65:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#011),
Judgments on the wicked, and blessings on the godly; [Isa
65:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#017), The blessed state of the new
Jerusalem.
