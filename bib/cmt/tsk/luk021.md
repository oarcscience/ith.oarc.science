Overview

widow; [Luk 21:5](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#005), He foretells the
destruction of the temple, and of the city Jerusalem; [Luk
21:25](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#025), the signs also which shall be
before the last day; [Luk 21:34](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#034), He
exhorts them to be watchful.
