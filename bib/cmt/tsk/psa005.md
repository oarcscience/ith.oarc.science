Overview

[Psa 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001.md), David prays, and professes
his study in prayer; [Psa 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#004.md), God
favours not the wicked; [Psa 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#007.md),
David, professing his faith, prays to God to guide him; [Psa
5:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#010.md), to destroy his enemies; [Psa
5:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#011.md), and to preserve the godly.
