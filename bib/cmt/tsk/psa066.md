<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 66:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 66:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#001), David exhorts to praise
God; [Psa 66:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#005), to observe his great
works; [Psa 66:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#008), to bless him for his
gracious benefits; [Psa 66:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#012), He vows
for himself religious service to God; [Psa
66:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#016), He declares God's special
goodness to himself.
