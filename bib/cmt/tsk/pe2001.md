Overview

[Pe2 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe2001.htm#001), Confirming them in hope of
the increase of God's graces, [Pe2
1:5](../../../bib/kjv/pe2001.htm#005), he exhorts them, by faith, and
good works, to make their calling sure; [Pe2
1:12](../../../bib/kjv/pe2001.htm#012), whereof he is careful to remind
them, knowing that his death is at hand; [Pe2
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/pe2001.htm#016), and warns them to be constant in
the faith of Christ, who is the true Son of God, by the eyewitness of
the apostles beholding his majesty, and by the testimony of the Father,
and the prophets.
