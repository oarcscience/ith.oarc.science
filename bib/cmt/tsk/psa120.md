Overview

[Psa 120:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#001), David prays against Doeg;
[Psa 120:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#003), reproves his tongue; [Psa
120:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#005), complains of his necessary
conversation with the wicked.

Bp. Patrick and others suppose this Psalm to have been composed by
David, when the calumnies of Doeg and others forced him to flee his
country. [Psa 121:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001), [Psa
