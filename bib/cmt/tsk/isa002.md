Overview

[Isa 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#001.md), Isaiah prophesies the coming
of Christ's kingdom; [Isa 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#006.md),
Wickedness is the cause of God's forsaking; [Isa
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010.md), He exhorts to fear, because of
the powerful effects of God's majesty.
