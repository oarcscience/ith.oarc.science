### [Mark Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#001)

[Mark](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mar 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mar 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#001), Christ disputes with the
Pharisees touching divorcement: [Mar
10:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#013). blesses the children that are
brought unto him; [Mar 10:17](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#017), resolves
a rich man how he may inherit life everlasting; [Mar
10:23](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#023), tells his disciples of the
danger of riches; [Mar 10:28](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#028), promises
rewards to them that forsake anything for the gospel; [Mar
10:32](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#032), foretells his death and
resurrection; [Mar 10:35](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#035), bids the two
ambitious suitors to think rather of suffering with him; [Mar
10:46](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#046), and restores to Bartimaeus his
sight.
