### [Jeremiah Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 21:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 21:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#001.md), Zedekiah sends to Jeremiah
to enquire the event of Nebuchadnezzar's war; [Jer
21:3](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#003.md), Jeremiah foretells a hard seige
and miserable captivity; [Jer 21:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#008.md), He
counsels the people to fall to the Chaldeans; [Jer
21:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#011.md), and upbraids the King's house.

[Jeremiah 21:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 21:1</span></span>

am cir, 3415, bc cir, 589

The word: This discourse was delivered about the ninth year of the reign
of Zedekiah. This chapter, observes Dr. Blayney, contains the first of
those prophecies which were delivered by Jeremiah subsequent to the
revolt of Zedekiah, and the breaking out of the war thereupon; and which
are continued on to the taking of Jerusalem, related in [Jer
39:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001.md), in the following order. [Jer
21:1-14](../../../bib/kjv/jer021.htm#001.md); 34; 32; 33; 38; 39.
