Overview

[Sa2 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#001), Nathan, first approving the
purpose of David to build God a house, [Sa2
7:4](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#004), after by the word of God forbids
him; [Sa2 7:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#012), God promises him
benefits and blessings in his seed; [Sa2
7:18](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#018), David's prayer and thanksgiving.

read, with Houbigant, Drs. Waterland and A. Clarke, and others, שׁפטי,
shophtey, "judges;" which is the reading in the parallel passage. Indeed
there is but one letter of difference between them, and letters which
might be easily mistaken for each other; the apex under the upper stroke
of the פ, pay, being the only mark to distinguish it from the ב, baith.
Compare with [Sa2 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#011).
