### [Esther Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/est007.htm#001.md)

[Esther](../../../bib/kjv/est001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">est 7:0</span></span>

Overview

[Est 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/est007.htm#001.md), Esther, entertaining the
king and Haman, makes suit for her own life, and her people's; [Est
7:5](../../../bib/kjv/est007.htm#005.md), She accuses Haman; [Est
7:7](../../../bib/kjv/est007.htm#007.md), The king in his anger,
understanding of the gallows which Haman had made for Mordecai, causes
him to be hanged thereon.
