Overview

[Psa 35:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#001.md), David prays for his own
safety, and his enemies confusion; [Psa
35:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#011.md), He complains of their wrongful
dealing; [Psa 35:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#022.md), Thereby he
incites God against them.
