Matthew Chapter 11

Matthew

mat 11:0

Overview

Mat 11:1, John sends his disciples to Christ; Mat 11:7, Christ's testimony concerning John; Mat 11:16, The perverse judgment of the people; Mat 11:20, Christ upbraids Chorazin, Bethsaida, and Capernaum; Mat 11:25, and praising his Father's wisdom in revealing the Gospel to the simple, Mat 11:28. he calls to him all such as feel the burden of their sins.
