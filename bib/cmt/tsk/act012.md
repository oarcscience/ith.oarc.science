[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#001), King Herod persecutes the
Christians, kills James, and imprisons Peter; whom an angel delivers
upon the prayers of the church; [Act
12:20](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#020), Herod in his pride taking to
himself the honour due to God, is stricken by an angel, and dies
miserably; [Act 12:24](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#024), After his
death, the word of God prospers; [Act
12:25](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#025), Saul and Barnabas return to
Antioch.
