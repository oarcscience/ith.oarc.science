### [Acts Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 7:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#001), Stephen, permitted to answer
to the accusation of blasphemy, [Act
7:2](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#002), shows that Abraham worshipped God
rightly, and how God chose the fathers, [Act
7:20](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#020). before Moses was born, and
before the tabernacle and temple were built; [Act
7:37](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#037), that Moses himself witnessed of
Christ; [Act 7:44](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#044), and that all
outward ceremonies were ordained, according to the heavenly pattern, to
last but for a time; [Act 7:51](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#051),
reprehending their rebellion, and murdering of Christ, the Just One,
whom the prophets foretold should come into the world; [Act
7:54](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#054), Whereupon they stone him to
death, who commends his soul to Jesus, and humbly prays for them.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">act 7:16</span></span>

were: Of the two burying-places of the patriarchs, one was at Hebron,
the cave and field which Abraham purchased of Ephron the Hittite ([Gen
23:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen023.htm#016), etc.); the other in Sychem,
which Jacob (not Abraham) bought of the sons of Emmor ([Gen
33:19](../../../bib/kjv/gen033.htm#019)). To remove this glaring
discrepancy, Markland interprets παρα \[Strong's G3844\], from, as it
frequently signifies with a genitive, and renders, "And were carried
over to Sychem; and afterwards from among the descendants of Emmor, the
father, or son, of Sychem, they were laid in the sepulchre which Abraham
bought for a sum of money." This agrees with the account which Josephus
gives of the patriarchs; that they were carried out of Egypt, first to
Sychem, and then to Hebron, where they were buried. [Exo
13:19](../../../bib/kjv/exo013.htm#019); [Jos
24:32](../../../bib/kjv/jos024.htm#032)

the sepulchre: [Gen 33:9-20](../../../bib/kjv/gen033.htm#009), [Gen
35:19](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#019), [Gen
49:29-32](../../../bib/kjv/gen049.htm#029)
