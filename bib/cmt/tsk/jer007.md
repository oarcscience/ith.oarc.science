### [Jeremiah Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 7:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#001.md), Jeremiah is sent to call to
true repentance, to prevent the Jews' captivity; [Jer
7:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#008.md), He rejects their vain confidence,
[Jer 7:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#012.md). by the example of Shiloh;
[Jer 7:17](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#017.md), He threatens them for their
idolatry; [Jer 7:21](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#021.md), He rejects the
sacrifices of the disobedient; [Jer
7:29](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#029.md), He exhorts to mourn for their
abominations in Tophet; [Jer 7:32](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#032.md), and
the judgments for the same.
