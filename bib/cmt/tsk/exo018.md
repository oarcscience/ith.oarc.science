<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">exo 18:0</span></span>

Overview

[Exo 18:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#001), Jethro brings his wife and
two sons to Moses; [Exo 18:7](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#007), Moses
entertains him, and relates the goodness of the Lord; [Exo
18:9](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#009), Jethro rejoices, blesses God,
and offers sacrifice; [Exo 18:13](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#013), He
gives good counsel, which is accepted; [Exo
18:27](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#027), Jethro departs.
