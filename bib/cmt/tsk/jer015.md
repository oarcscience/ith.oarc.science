Overview

[Jer 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#001.md), The utter rejection and
manifold judgments of the Jews; [Jer
15:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#010.md), Jeremiah, complaining of their
spite, receives a promise for himself; [Jer
15:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#012.md), and a threatening for them;
[Jer 15:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#015.md), He prays; [Jer
15:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#019.md), and receives a gracious
promise.
