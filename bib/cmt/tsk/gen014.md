[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 14:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#001.md), The battle of four kings
against the king of Sodom and his allies; [Gen
14:12](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#012.md), Lot is taken prisoner; [Gen
14:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#014.md), Abram rescues him; [Gen
14:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#017.md), Melchizedek blesses Abram at
his return, who gives him tithes; [Gen
14:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#021.md), The rest of the spoil, his
partners having had their portions he restores to the king of Sodom.
