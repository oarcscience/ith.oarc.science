### [Mark Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001.md)

[Mark](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mar 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mar 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001.md), In a parable of the
vineyard let out to wicked husbandmen Christ foretells the reprobation
of the Jews, and the calling of the Gentiles; [Mar
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#013.md), He avoids the snare of the
Pharisees and Herodians about paying tribute to Caesar; [Mar
12:18](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#018.md), convicts the Sadducees of
error, who denied the resurrection; [Mar
12:28](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#028.md), resolves the scribe, who
questioned of the first commandment; [Mar
12:35](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035.md), refutes the opinion that the
scribes held of Christ; [Mar 12:38](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#038.md),
bidding the people to beware of their ambition and hypocrisy; [Mar
12:41](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#041.md), and commends the poor widow for
her two mites, above all.
