his death; [Jos 23:3](../../../bib/kjv/jos023.htm#003.md), by former
benefits; [Jos 23:5](../../../bib/kjv/jos023.htm#005.md), by promises; [Jos
23:11](../../../bib/kjv/jos023.htm#011.md), and by threatenings.

That ye come: Have no civil or social contacts with them, as these will
infallibly lead to spiritual affinities, in consequence of which, ye
will make honourable "mention of the name of their gods," "swear by
them" "and serve" them in their abominable rites; and "bow yourselves
unto them," as your creators and preservers. All this will follow by
simply coming among them. He who walks in the counsel of the ungodly,
will soon stand in the way of sinners, and sit in the seat of scorners.
