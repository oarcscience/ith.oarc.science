### [Genesis Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#001.md)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 17:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#001.md), God renews the covenant
with Abram, and changes his name to Abraham, in token of a greater
blessing; [Gen 17:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#009.md), Circumcision is
instituted; [Gen 17:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#015.md), Sarai's name
is changed to Sarah, and she is blessed; [Gen
17:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#017.md), Isaac is promised, and the time
of his birth fixed; [Gen 17:23](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#023.md),
Abraham and Ishmael are circumcised.
