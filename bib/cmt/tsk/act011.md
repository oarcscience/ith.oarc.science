[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#001.md), Peter, being accused for
going in to the Gentiles, [Act 11:5](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#005.md),
makes his defence; [Act 11:18](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#018.md), which
is accepted; [Act 11:19](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#019.md), The gospel
being spread in Phenice, and Cyprus, and Antioch, Barnabas is sent to
confirm them; [Act 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#026.md), The
disciples there are first called Christians; [Act
11:27](../../../bib/kjv/act011.htm#027.md), They send relief to the
brethren in Judea in time of famine.
