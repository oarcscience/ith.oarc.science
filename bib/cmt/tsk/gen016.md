### [Genesis Chapter 16](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#001.md)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 16:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#001.md), Sarai, being barren, gives
Hagar to Abram; [Gen 16:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#004.md), Hagar,
being afflicted for despising her mistress, runs away; [Gen
16:7](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#007.md), An angel commands her to return
and submit herself, promises her a numerous posterity, and shows their
character and condition; [Gen 16:13](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#013.md),
Hagar names the place, and returns to Sarai; [Gen
16:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#015.md), Ishmael is born; [Gen
16:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen016.htm#016.md), The age of Abram.
