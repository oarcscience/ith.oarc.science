### [Isaiah Chapter 66](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#001)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 66:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 66:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#001), The glorious God will be
served in humble sincerity; [Isa 66:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#005),
He comforts the humble by shewing the confusion of their enemies; [Isa
66:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#007), with the marvellous growth, [Isa
66:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#010). and the gracious benefits of
the church; [Isa 66:15](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#015), God's severe
judgments against the wicked; [Isa
66:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#018), The Gentiles shall have an holy
church; [Isa 66:24](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#024), and see the
damnation of the wicked.
