### [Acts Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

Overview

[Act 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001), Christ, preparing his
apostles to the beholding of his ascension, gathers them together unto
the mount Olivet, commands them to expect in Jerusalem the sending down
of the Holy Ghost, promises after a few days to send it, and ascends
into heaven in their sight; [Act 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#010),
After his ascension they are warned by two angels to depart, and to set
their minds upon his second coming; [Act
1:12](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#012), They accordingly return, and,
giving themselves to prayer, choose Matthias apostle in the place of
Judas.
