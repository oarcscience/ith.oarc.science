Overview

[Isa 56:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa056.htm#001.md), The prophet exhorts to
sanctification; [Isa 56:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa056.htm#003.md), He promises
it shall be general, without respect of persons; [Isa
56:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa056.htm#009.md), He inveighs against blind
watchmen.
