an answer, is shewn that he must wait by faith; [Hab
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/hab002.htm#005.md), The judgment upon the Chaldean

covetousness, [Hab 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/hab002.htm#012.md). for cruelty,
[Hab 2:15](../../../bib/kjv/hab002.htm#015.md). for drunkenness, [Hab
2:18](../../../bib/kjv/hab002.htm#018.md). and for idolatry.
