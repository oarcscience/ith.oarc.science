Exodus Chapter 18

Exodus

exo 18:0

Overview

Exo 18:1, Jethro brings his wife and two sons to Moses; Exo 18:7, Moses entertains him, and relates the goodness of the Lord; Exo 18:9, Jethro rejoices, blesses God, and offers sacrifice; Exo 18:13, He gives good counsel, which is accepted; Exo 18:27, Jethro departs.
