Overview

[Luk 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#001), Augustus taxes all the Roman
empire; [Luk 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#006), The nativity of
Christ; [Luk 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#008), An angel relates it
to the shepherds, and many sing praises to God for it; [Luk
2:15](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#015), The shepherds glorify God; [Luk
2:21](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#021), Christ is circumcised; [Luk
2:22](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#022), Mary purified; [Luk
2:25](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#025), Simeon and Anna prophesy of
Christ, [Luk 2:39](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#039). who increases in
wisdom, [Luk 2:41](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#041). questions in the
temple with the doctors, [Luk 2:51](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#051).
and is obedient to his parents.
