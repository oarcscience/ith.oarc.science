### [Genesis Chapter 19](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 19:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 19:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#001), Lot entertains two angels;
[Gen 19:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#004), The vicious Sodomites are
smitten with blindness; [Gen 19:12](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#012),
Lot is warned, and in vain warns his sons-in-law; [Gen
19:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#015), He is directed to flee with his
family to the mountains, but obtains leave to go into Zoar; [Gen
19:24](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#024), Sodom and Gomorrah are
destroyed; [Gen 19:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#026), Lot's wife
becomes a pillar of salt; [Gen 19:29](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#029),
Lot dwells in a cave; [Gen 19:31](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#031), The
incestuous origin of Moab and Ammon.

[Genesis 19:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen019.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">gen 19:1</span></span>

And there came two angels. Or, rather, "the two angels came," referring
to those mentioned in the preceding chapter, and there called "men." It
seems (from [Gen 18:22](../../../bib/kjv/gen018.htm#022)), that these
two angels were sent to Sodom, while the third, who was the Lord or
Jehovah, remained with Abraham.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">gen 19:11</span></span>

with blindness: The word sanverim, rendered "blindness," and which
occurs only here, and in [Kg2 6:18](../../../bib/kjv/kg2006.htm#018), is
supposed to denote dazzlings, deceptions, or confusions of sight from
excessive light; being derived by Schultens, who is followed by
Parkhurst, from the Arabic sana, to pour forth, diffuse, and nor, light.
Dr. Geddes, to the same purpose, thinks it is compounded of the Arabic
sana, which signifies a flash, and or, light. The Targums, in both
places where it occurs, render it by eruptions, or flashes of light, or
as Mercer, in Robertson, explains the Chaldee word, irradiations. [Kg2
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/kg2006.htm#018); [Act
13:11](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#011)
