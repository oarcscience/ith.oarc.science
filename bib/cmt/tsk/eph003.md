[Ephesians](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eph 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eph 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#001.md), The hidden mystery that the
Gentiles should be saved was made known to Paul by revelation; [Eph
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#008.md), and to him was that grace given,
that he should preach it; [Eph 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#013.md),
He desires them not to faint for his tribulation; [Eph
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#014.md), and prays that they may perceive
the great love of Christ toward them.
