### [2 Thessalonians Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#001.md)

[2 Thessalonians](../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">th2 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Th2 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#001.md), Paul urges them to continue
stedfast in the truth received; [Th2
2:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#003.md), shows that there shall be a
departure from the faith, [Th2 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#009.md),
and a discovery of Antichrist, before the day of the Lord come; [Th2
2:15](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#015.md), and thereupon repeats his former
exhortation, and prays for them.
