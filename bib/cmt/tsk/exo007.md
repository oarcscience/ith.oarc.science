Overview

[Exo 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#001), Moses and Aaron are
encouraged to go again to Pharaoh; [Exo
7:7](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#007), Their age; [Exo
7:8](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#008), Aaron's rod is turned into a
serpent; [Exo 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#011), The sorcerers do
the like; but their rods are swallowed up by Aaron's; [Exo
7:13](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#013), Pharaoh's heart is hardened;
[Exo 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#014), God's message to Pharaoh;
[Exo 7:19](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#019), The river is turned into
blood; and the consequent distress of the Egyptians.

the large quantities which on that account are drunk of it, will, I am
sure, find an energy in those words of Moses to Pharaoh, the Egyptians
shall loathe to drink of the water of the river, which he never did
