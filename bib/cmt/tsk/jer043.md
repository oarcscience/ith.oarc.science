[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 43:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#001), Johanan, discrediting
Jeremiah's prophecy, carries him and the rest into Egypt; [Jer
43:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#008), Jeremiah prophesies by a type
the conquest of Egypt by the Babylonians.

to deny that God had spoken what the prophet had declared, the constant
method of hypocrites and infidels, who pretend that they are not
satisfied of the truth of Divine revelation, when the true cause of
their unbelief is, that the commands of God contradict their lusts and
