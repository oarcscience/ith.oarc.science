### [Matthew Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#001.md)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 18:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 18:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#001.md), Christ warns his disciples
to be humble and harmless, [Mat 18:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#007.md),
to avoid offences, [Mat 18:10](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#010.md). and not
to despise the little ones; [Mat
18:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#015.md), teaches how we are to deal with
our brethren when they offend us, [Mat
18:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#021.md). and how oft to forgive them;
[Mat 18:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#023.md), which he sets forth by a
parable of the king that took account of his servants, [Mat
18:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#032.md). and punished him who shewed no
mercy to his fellow.
