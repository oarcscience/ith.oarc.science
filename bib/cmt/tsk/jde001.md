### [Jude Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Jde 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#001.md), He exhorts them to be
constant in the profession of the faith; [Jde
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#004.md), False teachers are crept in to
seduce them, for whose evil doctrine and manners horrible punishment is
prepared; [Jde 1:20](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#020.md), whereas the
godly, by the assistance of the Holy Spirit, and prayers to God, may
persevere, and grow in grace, and keep themselves, and recover others
out of the snares of those deceivers.
