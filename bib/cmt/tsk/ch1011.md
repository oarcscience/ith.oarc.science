Overview

[Ch1 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#001.md), David by general consent is
made king at Hebron; [Ch1 11:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#004.md), He
wins the castle of Zion from the Jebusites by Joab's valour; [Ch1
11:10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#010.md), A catalogue of David's mighty
men.
