take: There were essential defects in these sacrifices. 1st - They were
not of the same nature with those who sinned. 2nd - They were not of
sufficient value to make satisfaction for the affronts done to the
justice and government of God. 3rd - The beasts offered up under the law
could not consent to put themselves in the sinner's room and place. The
atoning sacrifice must be one capable of consenting, and must
voluntarily substitute himself in the sinner's stead: Christ did so.
