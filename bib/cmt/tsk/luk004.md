Overview

[Luk 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#001), The temptation and fasting
of Christ; [Luk 4:14](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#014), He begins to
preach; [Luk 4:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#016), The people of
Nazareth admire his gracious words, but being offended, seek to kill
him; [Luk 4:33](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#033), He cures one possessed
of a devil, [Luk 4:38](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#038). Peter's
mother-in-law, [Luk 4:40](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#040). and divers
other sick persons; [Luk 4:41](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#041), The
devils acknowledge Christ, and are reproved for it; [Luk
4:42](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#042), He preaches through the cities
of Galilee.
