Esau: The word Esau has been generally considered to imply made, formed,
or perfected; or perfect, robust, etc. But it appears to be a
dialectical variation of the Arabic atha, to be covered with hair;
whence athai, hairy, as no doubt the word Esau imports, in allusion to
the circumstance of his being covered with red hair or down at his
birth. [Gen 27:11](../../../bib/kjv/gen027.htm#011), [Gen
27:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen027.htm#016), [Gen
27:23](../../../bib/kjv/gen027.htm#023)
