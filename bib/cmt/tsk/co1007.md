Overview

[Co1 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#001), He treats of marriage; [Co1
7:4](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#004), shewing it to be a remedy against
fornication, [Co1 7:10](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#010). and that the
bond thereof ought not lightly to be dissolved; [Co1
7:20](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#020), Every man must be content with
his vocation; [Co1 7:25](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#025), Virginity
wherefore to be embraced; [Co1 7:35](../../../bib/kjv/co1007.htm#035),
and for what respects we may either marry, or abstain from marrying.
