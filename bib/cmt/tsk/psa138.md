### [Psalms Chapter 138](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 138:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 138:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#001.md), David praises God for the
truth of his word; [Psa 138:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#004.md), He
prophesies that the kings of the earth shall praise God; [Psa
138:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#007.md), He professes his confidence in
God.

Five manuscripts omit ledawid; and the LXX and Arabic prefix also the
names of Haggai and Zechariah; and it is probable that it was composed
to be sung at the dedication of the second temple.
