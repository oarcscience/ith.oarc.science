Jeremiah Chapter 11

Jeremiah

jer 11:0

Overview

Jer 11:1, Jeremiah proclaims God's covenant; Jer 11:8, rebukes the Jews' disobeying thereof; Jer 11:11, prophesies evils to come upon them; Jer 11:18, and upon the men of Anathoth, for conspiring to kill him.
