Overview

[Psa 129:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa129.htm#001.md), An exhortation to praise
God for saving Israel in their great afflictions; [Psa
129:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa129.htm#005.md), The haters of the church are
cursed.

This Psalm was most probably composed in consequence of the opposition
of the Samaritans. Ezra 4:1-23; [Psa
