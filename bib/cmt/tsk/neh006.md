craft, by rumours, and by hired prophecies, to terrify Nehemiah; [Neh
6:15](../../../bib/kjv/neh006.htm#015), The work is finished, to the
terror of the enemies; [Neh 6:17](../../../bib/kjv/neh006.htm#017),
