### [Mark Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#001.md)

[Mark](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mar 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mar 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#001.md), Christ feeds the people
miraculously; [Mar 8:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#010.md), refuses to
give a sign to the Pharisees; [Mar
8:14](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#014.md), admonishes his disciples to
beware of the leaven of the Pharisees, and of the leaven of Herod; [Mar
8:22](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#022.md), gives a blind man his sight;
[Mar 8:27](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#027.md), acknowledges that he is the
Christ who should suffer and rise again; [Mar
8:34](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#034.md), and exhorts to patience in
persecution for the profession of the gospel.
