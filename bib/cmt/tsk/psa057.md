Overview

[Psa 57:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#001), David in prayer fleeing
unto God, complains of his dangerous case; [Psa
57:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#007), He encourages himself to praise
God.

not. A golden Psalm. This Psalm is supposed to have been called al
tashcheth, or "destroy not," because David thus addressed one of his
followers when about to kill Saul in the cave; and michtam, or "golden,"
because written, or worthy to be written, in gold. [Psa
58:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#001), [Psa
59:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa059.htm#001) \*titles
