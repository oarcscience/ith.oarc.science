### [1 Timothy Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#001)

Overview

[Ti1 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#001), Timothy is put in mind of
the charge which was given unto him by Paul at his going to Macedonia;
[Ti1 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#005), Of the right use and end of
the law; [Ti1 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#011), Of Saint Paul's
calling to be an apostle; [Ti1 1:20](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#020),
and Hymenaeus and Alexander.
