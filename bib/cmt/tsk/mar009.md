### [Mark Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#001.md)

[Mark](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mar 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mar 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#001.md), Jesus is transfigured; [Mar
9:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#011.md), He instructs his disciples
concerning the coming of Elias; [Mar
9:14](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#014.md), casts forth a deaf and dumb
spirit; [Mar 9:30](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#030.md), foretells his death
and resurrection; [Mar 9:33](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#033.md), exhorts
his disciples to humility; [Mar 9:38](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#038.md),
bidding them not to prohibit such as are not against them, nor to give
offence to any of the faithful.
