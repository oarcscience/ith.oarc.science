Overview

[Jer 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#001), The captivity of Judah for
her sin; [Jer 17:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#005), Trust in man is
cursed; [Jer 17:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#007), in God is blessed;
[Jer 17:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#009), The deceitful heart cannot
deceive God; [Jer 17:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#012), The salvation
of God; [Jer 17:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#015), The prophet
complains of the mockers of his prophecy; [Jer
17:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#019), He is sent to renew the
covenant in hallowing the sabbath.
