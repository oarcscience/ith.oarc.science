Overview

[Pe1 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#001.md), He exhorts them from the
breach of charity; [Pe1 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#004.md), shewing
that Christ is the foundation whereupon they are built; [Pe1
2:11](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#011.md), He beseeches them also to
abstain from fleshly lusts; [Pe1 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#013.md),
to be obedient to magistrates; [Pe1
2:18](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#018.md), and teaches servants how to obey
their masters; [Pe1 2:20](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#020.md), patiently
suffering for well doing, after the example of Christ.
