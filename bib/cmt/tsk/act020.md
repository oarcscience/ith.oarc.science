### [Acts Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 20:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#001), Paul goes to Macedonia, and
thence to Troas; [Act 20:7](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#007), He
celebrates the Lord's supper, and preaches; [Act
20:9](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#009), Eutychus having fallen down dead
is raised to life; [Act 20:13](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#013), He
continues his travels; [Act 20:17](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#017), and
at Miletum he calls the elders together, tells them what shall befall to
himself, [Act 20:28](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#028). commits God's
flock to them, [Act 20:29](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#029). warns them
of false teachers, [Act 20:32](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#032).
commends them to God, [Act 20:36](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#036).
prays with them, and departs.
