### [Psalms Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 43:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md), David, praying to be
restored to the temple, promises to serve God joyfully; [Psa
43:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#005.md), He encourages his soul to trust
in God.

[Psalms 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 43:1</span></span>

am 2983, bc 1021 (Title), This Psalm is evidently a continuation of the
preceding, and had the same author; and they are written as one in
forty-six manuscripts The sameness of subject, similarity of
composition, and return of the same burden in both, are sufficient
evidence of this opinion.
