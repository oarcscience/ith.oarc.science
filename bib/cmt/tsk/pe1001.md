### [1 Peter Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Pe1 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#001.md), He blesses God for his
manifold spiritual graces; [Pe1 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#010.md),
shewing that the salvation in Christ is no news, but a thing prophesied
of old; [Pe1 1:13](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#013.md), and exhorts them
accordingly to a godly conversation, forasmuch as they are now born anew
by the word of God.
