<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">isa 11:1</span></span>

And there shall: The prophet having described the destruction of the
Assyrian army under that of a mighty forest, here takes occasion to
represent the Great Person, who makes the subject of this chapter, as a
slender twig, shooting out of the trunk of an old tree; which tender
twig, though weak in appearance, should become fruitful and prosper.
