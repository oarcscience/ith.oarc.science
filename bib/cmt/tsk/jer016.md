### [Jeremiah Chapter 16](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 16:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#001.md), The prophet, under the
types of abstaining from marriage, from houses of mourning and feasting,
foreshews the utter ruin of the Jews; [Jer
16:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#010.md), because they were worse than
their fathers; [Jer 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#014.md), Their
return from captivity shall be stranger than their deliverance out of
Egypt; [Jer 16:16](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#016.md), God will doubly
recompense their idolatry.
