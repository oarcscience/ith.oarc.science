<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 14:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#001.md), God answers idolaters
according to their own heart; [Eze
14:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#006.md), They are exhorted to repent, for
fear of judgments, by means of seduced prophets; [Eze
14:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#012.md), God's irrevocable sentence of
famine; [Eze 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#015.md), of noisome beasts;
[Eze 14:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#017.md), of the sword; [Eze
14:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#019.md), and of pestilence; [Eze
14:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#022.md), A remnant shall be reserved for
example of others.
