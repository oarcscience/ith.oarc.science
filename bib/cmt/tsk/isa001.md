### [Isaiah Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md)

Overview

[Isa 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md), Isaiah complains of Judah
for her rebellion; [Isa 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#005.md), He
laments her judgments; [Isa 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#010.md), He
upbraids their whole service; [Isa
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#016.md), He exhorts to repentance, with
promises and threatenings; [Isa 1:21](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#021.md),
Bewailing their wickedness, he denounces God's judgments; [Isa
1:25](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#025.md), He promises grace; [Isa
1:28](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#028.md), and threatens destruction to the
wicked.
