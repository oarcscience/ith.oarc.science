Overview

[Jam 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#001.md), We are to strive against
covetousness; [Jam 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#004.md), intemperance;
[Jam 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#005.md), pride; [Jam
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#011.md), detraction and rash judgment of
others; [Jam 4:13](../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#013.md), and not to be
confident in the good success of worldly business, but mindful ever of
the uncertainty of this life, to commit ourselves and all our affairs to
God's providence.
