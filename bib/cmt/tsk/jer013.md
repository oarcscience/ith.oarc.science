### [Jeremiah Chapter 13](../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#001)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 13:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#001), By the type of a linen
girdle, hidden at Euphrates, God prefigures the destruction of his
people; [Jer 13:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#012), Under the parable
of the bottles filled with wine he foretells their drunkenness in
misery; [Jer 13:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#015), He exhorts to
prevent their future judgments; [Jer
13:22](../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#022), He shews their abominations are
the cause thereof.
