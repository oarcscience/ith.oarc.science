Overview

[Isa 42:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001), The office of Christ,
graced with meekness and constancy; [Isa
42:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#005), God's promise unto him; [Isa
42:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#010), An exhortation to praise God
for his Gospel; [Isa 42:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#013), God will
manifest himself, and check idolatry; [Isa
42:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#018), He reproves the people of
incredulity.
