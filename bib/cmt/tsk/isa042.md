### [Isaiah Chapter 42](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 42:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 42:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001.md), The office of Christ,
graced with meekness and constancy; [Isa
42:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#005.md), God's promise unto him; [Isa
42:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#010.md), An exhortation to praise God
for his Gospel; [Isa 42:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#013.md), God will
manifest himself, and check idolatry; [Isa
42:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#018.md), He reproves the people of
incredulity.
