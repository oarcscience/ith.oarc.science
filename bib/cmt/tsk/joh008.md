### [John Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#001.md)

[John](../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">joh 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Joh 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#001.md), Christ delivers the woman
taken in adultery; [Joh 8:12](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#012.md), He
declares himself the light of the world, and justifies his doctrine;
[Joh 8:31](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#031.md), promises freedom to those
who believe; [Joh 8:33](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#033.md), answers the
Jews that boasted of Abraham; [Joh
8:48](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#048.md), answers their reviling, by
shewing his authority and dignity; [Joh
8:59](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#059.md), and conveys himself from their
cruelty.
