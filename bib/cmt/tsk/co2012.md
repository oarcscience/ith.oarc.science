### [2 Corinthians Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#001.md)

[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#001.md), For commending of his
apostleship, though he might glory of his wonderful revelations, [Co2
12:9](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#009.md), yet he rather chooses to glory
of his infirmities; [Co2 12:11](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#011.md),
blaming them for forcing him to this vain boasting; [Co2
12:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#014.md), He promises to come to them
again; but yet altogether in the affection of a father; [Co2
12:20](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#020.md), although he fears he shall to
his grief find many offenders, and public disorders there.
