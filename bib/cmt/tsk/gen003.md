Overview

[Gen 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#001.md), The serpent deceives Eve;
[Gen 3:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#006.md), Both she and Adam transgress
the divine command, and fall into sin and misery; [Gen
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#008.md), God arraigns them; [Gen
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#014.md), The serpent is cursed; [Gen
3:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#015.md), The promised seed; [Gen
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#016.md), The punishment of mankind; [Gen
3:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#021.md), Their first clothing; [Gen
3:22](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#022.md), Their expulsion from paradise.
