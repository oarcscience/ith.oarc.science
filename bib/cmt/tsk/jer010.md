Overview

God and idols; [Jer 10:17](../../../bib/kjv/jer010.htm#017), The prophet
exhorts to flee from the calamity to come; [Jer
10:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer010.htm#019), He laments the spoil of the
tabernacle by foolish pastors; [Jer
10:23](../../../bib/kjv/jer010.htm#023), He makes an humble
supplication.
