### [Psalms Chapter 123](../../../bib/kjv/psa123.htm#001.md)

The series then returns (Psalm 123) to their sorrows and resource.
Blessing is not fully come; but Jehovah is looked to in the heavens, but
as the God of Israel; the remnant say "our God" now. But they are filled
yet with the contempt of those that are at ease and of the proud.
