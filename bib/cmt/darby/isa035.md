### [Isaiah Chapter 35](../../../bib/kjv/isa035.htm#001.md)

Chapter 35 gives a picture of the blessing that succeeds the judgment,
the blessing even of the wilderness, which depends on that of Israel.
The redeemed of Jehovah shall go up with joy in full security to Zion,
and all mourning shall pass away for ever.
