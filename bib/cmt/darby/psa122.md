### [Psalms Chapter 122](../../../bib/kjv/psa122.htm#001.md)

Psalm 122 celebrates Jerusalem. The saint is glad to go there. The
tribes go there; the thrones of judgment, of the house of David, are
there. His brethren and companions and the house of Jehovah, the God of
Israel, their God, made his heart cling to it. It is a restoring of the
associations with Jerusalem, recalling the old and establishing the new
ones.
