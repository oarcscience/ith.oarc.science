### [Ezekiel Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#001)

Chapter 15 shews that the vine-utterly useless if it bore no fruit-was
fit only for fuel, and to be consumed. Thus should it be with the
inhabitants of Jerusalem-a striking picture of this destruction, and of
the condition of Jerusalem, which was worth nothing more.
