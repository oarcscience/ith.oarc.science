INTRODUCTION TO 2 CHRONICLES 5 & 6

The words with which chapter five begins are the same with [Kg1
7:51](../../../bib/kjv/kg1007.htm#051) and what is contained in that and
chapter six is much the same with [Kg1
8:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg1008.htm#001) on which see the notes; the
blessing of Solomon on the people of Israel, which is there, is here
omitted, and two verses are here added, much the same with [Psa
132:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa132.htm#008).
