### [1 Chronicles Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md)

INTRODUCTION TO 1 CHRONICLES 14

This chapter gives an account of the message of Hiram king of Tyre to
David, and of David's taking him more wives, with the names of his
children by them; and of two battles he fought with the Philistines, in
which he had the victory over them; the same are recorded in [Sa2
5:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011.md) see the notes there; See Gill on
[Sa2 5:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#012.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#013.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:14](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#014.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:15](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#015.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:16](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#016.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:17](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#017.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:18](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#018.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:19](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#019.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:20](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#020.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:21](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#021.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:22](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#022.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:23](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#023.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:24](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#024.md). See Gill on [Sa2
5:25](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#025.md), and the chapter is concluded
with observing, that the fame of David, on account of his victories, was
spread in all countries, and they were struck with a panic from the
Lord, and were in dread of him.
