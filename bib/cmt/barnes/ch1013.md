[1 Chronicles 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1013.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 13:1</span></span>

The captains ... - Such an organisation had probably been established
generally through the tribes prior to the time of David: but David seems
to have been the first to recognize in these officers of the host
representatives of the people, to consult them on public affairs, and to
give them a certain political position.
