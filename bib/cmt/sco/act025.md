### [Acts Chapter 25](../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#001)

[Acts 25:6](../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">act 25:6</span></span>

more than ten

Or, as some copies read, no more than eight or ten days.

[Acts 25:13](../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">act 25:13</span></span>

Agrippa

This ([Act 5:13](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#013)) was Herod Agrippa II,
son of the Herod Agrippa I of ([Act
12:1](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#001)); and great-grandson of Herod the
Great.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#001)).

Bernice, or Berenice, was the sister of Herod Agrippa II. ([Act
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#013)).
