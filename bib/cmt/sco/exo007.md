### [Exodus Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#001)

[Exodus 7:5](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">exo 7:5</span></span>

when

A prophetic sign also. The nations shall know Jehovah when He restores
and blesses Israel in the kingdom. ([Isa
2:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#001)); ([Isa
11:10-1214](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#010)); ([Isa
11:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001)); ([Isa
60:4-5](../../../bib/kjv/isa060.htm#004)); ([Eze
37:28](../../../bib/kjv/eze037.htm#028)).

[Exodus 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">exo 7:11</span></span>

like

Compare ([Ti2 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#008)); ([Exo
8:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo008.htm#018)).

Neither Satan nor his tools can create life: ([Rev
13:15](../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#015)) will be a "lying wonder": ([Th2
2:9](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#009)).

[Exodus 7:12](../../../bib/kjv/exo007.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">exo 7:12</span></span>

Aaron's rod

(Cf) ([Exo 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#002)) As Moses' rod was the
rod of power, the rod of the King ([Deu
33:4](../../../bib/kjv/deu033.htm#004)); ([Deu
33:5](../../../bib/kjv/deu033.htm#005)) so Aaron's was the rod of life,
the rod of the Priest. As here the serpents, symbols of Satan, who had
the power of death; ([Rev 12:9](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#009)); ([Heb
2:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#014)) are swallowed up, so in
resurrection death will be "swallowed up in victory"; ([Co1
15:54](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#054)); ([Num
17:8](../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#008)).
