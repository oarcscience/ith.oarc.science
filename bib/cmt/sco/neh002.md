### [Nehemiah Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#001.md)

[Nehemiah 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">neh 2:1</span></span>

Nisan

first monththat is, April.

[Nehemiah 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">neh 2:10</span></span>

Tobiah

Two Tobiahs are distinguished by many:

\(1\) "Tobiah the servant, the Ammonite," ([Neh
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#010).md); ([Neh
2:19](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#019).md); Heh 4:3,7 6:1,12,14.

\(2\) A Jew, unable to prove his genealogy. But the reference to the
latter ([Neh 7:62](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#062).md) indicates that he
was already dead. But one Tobiah, and he the Ammonite, is active in this
book.

[Nehemiah 2:19](../../../bib/kjv/neh002.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">neh 2:19</span></span>

laughed

The obstacle of ridicule.
