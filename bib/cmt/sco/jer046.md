### [Jeremiah Chapter 46](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 46:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 46:1</span></span>

Gentiles

A near and a far fulfilment of these prophecies against Gentile powers
are to be distinguished. In Chapter 46, the near vision is of a
Babylonian invasion of Egypt, but verses ([Jer
46:27](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#027)); ([Jer
46:28](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#028)) look forward to the judgment of
the nations.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 25:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#032)),

after Armageddon ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014));

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 19:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017))

and the deliverance of Israel ("Israel," ([Gen
12:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002)); ([Gen
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003)

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)).

([Jer 50:4-7](../../../bib/kjv/jer050.htm#004)) also looks forward to
the last days.
