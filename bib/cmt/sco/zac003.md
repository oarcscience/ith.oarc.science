### [Zechariah Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#001)

[Zechariah 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 3:1</span></span>

Joshua

The fifth vision discloses:

\(1\) The change from self-righteousness to the righteousness of God

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:21](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021)).

of which Paul's experience, ([Phi
3:1-9](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#001)) is the illustration, as it is
also the foreshadowing of the conversion of Israel.

\(2\) In type, the preparation of Israel for receiving Jehovah's
"BRANCH"

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002)).

The refusal of the Jews to abandon self-righteousness for the
righteousness of God blinded them to the presence of the BRANCH in their
midst at His first advent ([Rom
10:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001)); ([Rom
11:7](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#007)); ([Rom
11:8](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#008)).

Compare ([Zac 6:12-15](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#012)) which speaks of
the manifestation of the BRANCH in glory ([Zac
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#013)) as the Priest-King, when Israel
will receive Him.

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#006)).

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004)).
