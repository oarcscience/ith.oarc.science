### [2 John Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#001)

[2 John 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">jo2 1:5</span></span>

that we love one another

Law (of Christ), Summary:

The new "law of Christ" is the divine love, as wrought into the renewed
heart by the Holy Spirit ([Rom 5:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#005));
([Heb 10:16](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#016)) and outflowing in the
energy of the Spirit, unforced and spontaneous, toward the objects of
the divine love; ([Co2 5:14-20](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#014)); ([Th1
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/th1002.htm#007)); ([Th1
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/th1002.htm#008)). It is, therefore, " the law of
liberty"; ([Jam 1:25](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#025)); ([Jam
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#012)) in contrast with the external
law of Moses. Moses' law demands love, ([Lev
19:18](../../../bib/kjv/lev019.htm#018)); ([Deu
6:5](../../../bib/kjv/deu006.htm#005)); ([Luk
10:27](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#027)) Christ's law is love; ([Rom
5:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#005)); ([Jo1
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/jo1004.htm#007)); ([Jo1
4:19](../../../bib/kjv/jo1004.htm#019)); ([Jo1
4:20](../../../bib/kjv/jo1004.htm#020)) and so takes the place of the
external law by fulfilling it; ([Rom
13:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom013.htm#010)); ([Gal
5:14](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#014)). It is the "law written in the
heart" under the New Covenant.

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 8:8](../../../bib/kjv/heb008.htm#008)).
