[Genesis 32:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen032.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">gen 32:2</span></span>

[Genesis 32:28](../../../bib/kjv/gen032.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">gen 32:28</span></span>

shall be no more called Jacob

Both names are applied to the nation descended from Jacob. When used
characteristically "Jacob" is the name for the natural posterity of
Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob; "Israel" for the spiritual part of the
nation. See ([Isa 9:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#008)). The "word" was
sent to all the people, "Jacob," but it "lighted upon Israel," that is,
was comprehended by the spiritual part of the people. See "Israel" ([Gen
12:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002)); ([Gen
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)).
