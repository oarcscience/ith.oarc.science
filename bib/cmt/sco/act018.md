### [Acts Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#001)

[Acts 18:5](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">act 18:5</span></span>

pressed

Or, constrained by the Word.

Compare ([Co2 5:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#014)).

[Acts 18:7](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">act 18:7</span></span>

Justus

Titus Justus.

[Acts 18:17](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">act 18:17</span></span>

cared for

Contrast ([Joh 19:13-16](../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#013)); ([Act
24:26](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#026)); ([Act
24:27](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#027)).

[Acts 18:25](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">act 18:25</span></span>

instructed

Taught by word of mouth, or, hearsay, that is, not by revelation.

Compare ([Gal 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#011)); ([Gal
1:12](../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#012)).

The New Testament Scriptures were not then written.

[Acts 18:28](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">act 18:28</span></span>

that Jesus

Apollos' ministry seems to have gone no further; Jesus was the long
expected Messiah. Of Paul's doctrine of justification through the blood,
and sanctification through the Spirit, he seems at that time to have
known nothing.

See ([Act 19:3-6](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#003)).
