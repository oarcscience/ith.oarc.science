### [Malachi Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/mal002.htm#001)

[Malachi 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/mal002.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mal 2:2</span></span>

curse

Compare ([Deu 28:3-14](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#003)) and ([Deu
28:15-35](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#015)).

Israel's distinctive blessings should turn to curses.

[Malachi 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/mal002.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">mal 2:10</span></span>

Have we not all one

Compare ([Act 17:24-29](../../../bib/kjv/act017.htm#024)).

In both instances the reference is to creation, not the new birth.

[Malachi 2:15](../../../bib/kjv/mal002.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">mal 2:15</span></span>

spirit

Summary of the Old Testament doctrine of the Holy Spirit:

\(1\) The personality and Deity of the Holy Spirit appear from the
attributes ascribed to Him, and from His works.

\(2\) He is revealed as sharing the work of creation and therefore
omnipotent ([Gen 1:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#002)); ([Job
26:13](../../../bib/kjv/job026.htm#013)); ([Job
33:4](../../../bib/kjv/job033.htm#004)); ([Psa
104:30](../../../bib/kjv/psa104.htm#030)) as omnipresent ([Psa
139:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa139.htm#007)) as striving with men ([Gen
6:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen006.htm#003)) as enlightening ([Job
32:8](../../../bib/kjv/job032.htm#008)) enduing with constructive skill;
([Exo 28:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#003)); ([Exo
31:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#003)) giving physical strength ([Jdg
14:6](../../../bib/kjv/jdg014.htm#006)); ([Jdg
14:19](../../../bib/kjv/jdg014.htm#019)) executive ability and wisdom;
([Jdg 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/jdg003.htm#010)); ([Jdg
6:34](../../../bib/kjv/jdg006.htm#034)); ([Jdg
11:29](../../../bib/kjv/jdg011.htm#029)); ([Jdg
13:25](../../../bib/kjv/jdg013.htm#025)) enabling men to receive and
utter divine revelations; ([Num
11:25](../../../bib/kjv/num011.htm#025)); ([Sa2
23:2](../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#002)) and, generally, as empowering
the servants of God; ([Psa 51:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa051.htm#012));
([Joe 2:28](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028)); ([Mic
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/mic003.htm#008)); ([Zac
4:6](../../../bib/kjv/zac004.htm#006)).

\(3\) He is called holy ([Psa 51:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa051.htm#011))
good ([Psa 143:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#010)) the Spirit of
judgment and burning ([Isa 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#004)) of
Jehovah, of wisdom, understanding, counsel, might, good, knowledge, the
fear of the Lord ([Isa 11:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#002)) and of
grace and supplications ([Zac 12:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#010)).

\(4\) In the Old Testament the Spirit acts in free sovereignty, coming
upon men and even upon a dumb beast as He will, nor are the conditions
set forth (as in the New Testament) by complying with which any one may
receive the Spirit. The indwelling of every believer by the abiding
Spirit is a New Testament blessing consequent upon the death and
resurrection of Christ ([Joh 7:39](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#039));
([Joh 16:7](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#007)); ([Act
2:33](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#033)); ([Gal
3:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#001)).

\(5\) The Old Testament contains prediction of a future pouring out of
the Spirit upon Israel ([Exo 37:14](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#014));
([Exo 39:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo039.htm#029)) and upon "all flesh"
([Joe 2:28](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028)); ([Joe
2:29](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#029)). The expectation of Israel,
therefore, was twofold -- of the coming of Messiah-Immanuel, and of such
an effusion of the Spirit as the prophets described. See ([Mat
1:18](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#018)).
