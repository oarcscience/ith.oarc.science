(See Scofield) - ([Isa 59:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020).md).

[Isaiah 52:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">isa 52:14</span></span>

so marred

The literal rendering is terrible: "So marred from the form of man was
His aspect that His appearance was not that of a son of man" -- that is,
not human -- the effect of the brutalities described in ([Mat
26:67](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#067).md); ([Mat
26:68](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#068).md); ([Mat
27:27-30](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#027).md).
