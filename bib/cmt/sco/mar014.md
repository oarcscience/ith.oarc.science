[Mark 14:45](../../../bib/kjv/mar014.htm#045.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_045">mar 14:45</span></span>

Master

Never once in the Gospel record does Judas Iscariot call Jesus "Lord."
He was the first Arian amongst the professed followers of Jesus. No one
can in reality say that Jesus is Lord, but by the Holy Ghost, ([Co1
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#003).md) but it is possible to use the
term as an empty formality without believing the Lordship of Christ;
([Mat 7:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#021).md); ([Mat
25:11](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#011).md); ([Mat
25:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#012).md).
