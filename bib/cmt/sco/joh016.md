[John 16:12](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">joh 16:12</span></span>

I have yet

Christ's pre-authentication of the New Testament:

\(1\) he expressly declared that He would leave "many things" unrevealed
([Joh 16:12](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#012)).

\(2\) He promised that this revelation should be completed ("all
things") after the Spirit should come, and that such additional
revelation should include new prophecies ([Joh
16:12](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#012)).

\(3\) He chose certain persons to receive such additional revelations,
and to be His witnesses to them ([Mat
28:19](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#019)); ([Joh
15:27](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#027)); ([Joh
16:13](../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#013)); ([Act
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#008)); ([Act
9:15-17](../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#015)).

\(4\) he gave to their words when speaking for Him in the Spirit
precisely the same authority as His own ([Mat
10:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#014)); ([Mat
10:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#015)); ([Luk
10:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#016)); ([Joh
13:20](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#020)); ([Joh
17:20](../../../bib/kjv/joh017.htm#020)).

See, for example, ([Co1 14:37](../../../bib/kjv/co1014.htm#037)) and

"Inspiration,"; ([Exo 4:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#015)); ([Rev
22:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#019)).
