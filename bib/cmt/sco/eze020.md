<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 20:1</span></span>

fifth month

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">eze 20:37</span></span>

rod

from all nations (see "Israel," ([Isa
1:24-26](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#024).md) refs. Into the old wilderness
wanderings. ([Eze 20:35](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#035).md). The issue of
this judgment determines who of Israel in that day shall enter the land
for kingdom blessing. ([Psa 50:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa050.htm#001).md);
([Eze 20:33-44](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#033).md); ([Mal
3:2-5](../../../bib/kjv/mal003.htm#002).md); ([Mal
