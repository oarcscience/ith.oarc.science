### [Ezekiel Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 20:1</span></span>

fifth month

That is, August.

[Ezekiel 20:37](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#037)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">eze 20:37</span></span>

rod

from all nations (see "Israel," ([Isa
1:24-26](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#024)) refs. Into the old wilderness
wanderings. ([Eze 20:35](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#035)). The issue of
this judgment determines who of Israel in that day shall enter the land
for kingdom blessing. ([Psa 50:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa050.htm#001));
([Eze 20:33-44](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#033)); ([Mal
3:2-5](../../../bib/kjv/mal003.htm#002)); ([Mal
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mal004.htm#001)); ([Mal
