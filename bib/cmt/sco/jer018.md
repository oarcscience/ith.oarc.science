### [Jeremiah Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/jer018.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 18:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer018.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 18:1</span></span>

The word

Israel (the whole nation) a vessel marred in the Potter's hand, is the
key to this prophetic strain. But Jehovah will make "it again another
vessel" ([Jer 18:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer018.htm#004)).
