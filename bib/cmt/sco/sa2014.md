[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 14:24](../../../bib/kjv/sa2014.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">sa2 14:24</span></span>

saw not the king's face

Not so had God taught David to forgive. Legalists have thought Absalom's
wilfulness to have been due to over-indulgence on the part of David.
There is no such intimation in Scripture. Rather it would seem that had
David at this time taken Absalom into intimacy, the rebellion might have
been averted.
