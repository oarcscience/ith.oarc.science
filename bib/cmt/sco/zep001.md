### [Zephaniah Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#001)

[Zephaniah 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zep 1:4</span></span>

Chemarims

That is, idolatrous priests.

Compare ([Kg2 23:5](../../../bib/kjv/kg2023.htm#005)).

[Zephaniah 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">zep 1:5</span></span>

Malcham

An idol of the Ammonites, same as Molech, or Milcom.

[Zephaniah 1:7](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">zep 1:7</span></span>

for the day of the Lord

As in the other Prophets, the approaching invasion of Nebuchadnezzar is
treated as an adumbration of the true day of the Lord in which all
earth-judgments will culminate, to be followed by the restoration and
blessing of Israel and the nations in the kingdom.

See "Day of the Lord" ([Isa 2:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010));
([Rev 19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)).

"Israel"; ([Gen 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002)); ([Gen
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003)); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)).

Compare Joel 1, 2.

[Zephaniah 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zep 1:11</span></span>

Maktesh

Literally, The Mortar, a depression in Jerusalem where the bazaars were.
