### [2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#001)

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 24:9](../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">sa2 24:9</span></span>

in Israel

Compare ([Ch1 21:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch1021.htm#005)).

The total military strength of Israel (the northern kingdom) was
1,100,000, and of Judah 500,000. The numbers actually set in array were,
of Israel, 800,000; of Judah, 470,000.
