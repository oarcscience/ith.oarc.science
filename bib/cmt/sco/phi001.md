### [Philippians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#001.md)

[Philippians 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">phi 1:1</span></span>

which are at

Churches (local), Summary:

A local church is an assembly of professed believers on the Lord Jesus
Christ, living for the most part in one locality, who assemble
themselves together in His name for the breaking of bread, worship,
praise, prayer, testimony, the ministry of the word, discipline, and the
furtherance of the Gospel, ([Heb
10:25](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#025).md); ([Act
20:7](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#007).md); ([Co1
14:26](../../../bib/kjv/co1014.htm#026).md); ([Co1
5:4](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#004).md); ([Co1
5:5](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#005).md); ([Phi
4:14-18](../../../bib/kjv/phi004.htm#014).md); ([Th1
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#008).md); ([Act
13:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#001).md).

Such a church exists where two or three are thus gathered ([Mat
18:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#020).md). Every such local church has
Christ in the midst, is a temple of God, and indwelt by the Holy Spirit
([Co1 3:16](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#016).md); ([Co1
3:17](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#017).md). When perfected in organization
a local church consists of "saints, with the bishops elders and
deacons."
