### [Jeremiah Chapter 32](../../../bib/kjv/jer032.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah 32:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer032.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">jer 32:9</span></span>

bought

\(1\) A sign of Jeremiah's faith in his own predictions of the
restoration of Judah ([Jer 32:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer032.htm#015).md) for
the field was then occupied by the Babylonian army; and

\(2\) a sign to Judah of that coming restoration.
