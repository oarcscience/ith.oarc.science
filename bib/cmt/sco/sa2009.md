### [2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#001)

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">sa2 9:1</span></span>

kindness

A lovely picture of salvation by grace.

\(1\) What grace is -- kindness to a helpless one for another's sake,
([Sa2 1:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#001)); ([Jo1
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#012)).

\(2\) Grace gives the highest place, ([Sa2
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#011)). ([Eph
1:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#001)).

\(3\) Grace keeps the saved one, ([Sa2
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#013)); ([Joh
10:28](../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#028)); ([Joh
10:29](../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#029)).
