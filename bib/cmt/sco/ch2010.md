### [2 Chronicles Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#001)

[2 Chronicles 10:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch2 10:16</span></span>

Israel

"Israel," the ten tribes other than Judah and Benjamin, often called
"Israel" in distinction from Judah. The division of the kingdom marks an
epoch of great importance in the history of the nation. Henceforth it is
"a kingdom divided against itself." ([Mat
12:25](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#025)). The two kingdoms are to be
reunited in the future kingdom. ([Isa
11:10-13](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#010)); ([Jer
23:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer023.htm#005)); ([Jer
23:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer023.htm#006)); ([Eze
37:15-28](../../../bib/kjv/eze037.htm#015)). See "Kingdom"
