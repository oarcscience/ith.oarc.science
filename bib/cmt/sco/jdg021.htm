Judges Chapter 21

Judges 21:3

jdg 21:3

tribe

There is here no mourning for sin, no humbling because of national transgression, no return to Jehovah. Accordingly, no word from Jehovah comes to them. They act wholly in self-will.

Compare (Jdg 21:10); (Dan 9:3-13).
