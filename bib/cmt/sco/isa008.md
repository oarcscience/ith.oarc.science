### [Isaiah Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#001)

[Isaiah 8:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">isa 8:12</span></span>

confederacy

The reference is to the attempt to terrify Judah by the confederacy
between Syria and Samaria. ([Isa 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#001));
([Isa 7:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#002)).

[Isaiah 8:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">isa 8:18</span></span>

signs

The primary application here is to the two sons of Isaiah,
"Maher-shalal-hash-baz", means "haste ye, haste ye to the spoil," a
"sign" of the coming judgment of the captivity of Judah; "Shear-jashub",
means "a remnant shall return," a "sign" of the return of a remnant of
Judah at the end of the seventy years, ([Jer
25:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#011)); ([Jer
25:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#012)); ([Dan
9:2](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#002)). The larger and final reference
is to our Lord ([Heb 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#013)); ([Heb
2:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#014)).
