### [Job Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#001)

[Job 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">job 8:1</span></span>

Bildad

Bildad is a religious dogmatist of the superficial kind, whose dogmatism
rests upon tradition (for example) ([Job
8:8-10](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#008)) and upon proverbial wisdom and
approved pious phrases. These abound in all his discourses. His
platitudes are true enough, but then every one knows them. ([Job
9:1](../../../bib/kjv/job009.htm#001)); ([Job
9:2](../../../bib/kjv/job009.htm#002)); ([Job
13:2](../../../bib/kjv/job013.htm#002)) nor do they shed any light on
such a problem as Job's.
