### [Psalms Chapter 72](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 72:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 72:1</span></span>

Give the king

The Psalm as a whole forms a complete vision of Messiah's kingdom so far
as the Old Testament revelation extended. All David's prayers will find
their fruition in the kingdom ([Psa
72:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#020).md); ([Sa2
23:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#001).md).

Verse 1 ([Psa 72:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#001).md) refers to the
investiture of the King's Son with the kingdom, of which investiture the
formal description is given in ([Dan
7:13](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#013).md); ([Dan
7:14](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#014).md); ([Rev
5:5-10](../../../bib/kjv/rev005.htm#005).md); ([Psa
72:2-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#002).md); ([Psa
72:12-14](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#012).md) give the character of the
kingdom.

Compare ([Isa 11:3-9](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#003).md).

The emphatic word is righteousness. The sermon on the Mount describes
the kingdom of righteousness. Verses 8-11 ([Psa
72:8-11](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#008).md) speak of the universality of
the kingdom. Verse 16 ([Psa 72:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#016).md)
hints at the means by which universal blessing is to be brought in.
Converted Israel will be the "handful of corn" ([Amo
9:9](../../../bib/kjv/amo009.htm#009).md) as the King Himself in death and
resurrection was the single grain, the "corn of wheat" ([Joh
12:24](../../../bib/kjv/joh012.htm#024).md) "To the Jew first" is the order
alike of Church and kingdom. ([Rom
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016).md); ([Act
13:46](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#046).md); ([Act
15:16](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#016).md); ([Act
15:17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#017).md). It is through restored Israel
that the kingdom is to be extended over the earth. ([Zac
8:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#013).md); ([Zac
8:20-23](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#020).md).

See Psalm 89, next in order of the Messianic Psalms.
