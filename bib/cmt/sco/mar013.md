[Mark 13:8](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">mar 13:8</span></span>

birthpangs. Answering to the "seals." (Revelation 6) ([Rev

The death-agony of this age is the birth-agony of the next.

[Mark 13:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">mar 13:13</span></span>

end

Not the end of the believer's life, but the end of the great
tribulation.

saved

In the sense of ([Rev 13:8](../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008).md); ([Rev
20:4](../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#004).md).

[Mark 13:14](../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">mar 13:14</span></span>

when ye

See "Beast." ([Dan 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008).md); ([Rev
19:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020).md).

then let

Compare ([Luk 21:20-24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020).md); which is a
prophecy fulfilled in the destruction of Jerusalem, A.D. 70, when the
Christians escaped, and which foreshadowed the more terrible day here
described.

See "Great Tribulation"; ([Psa 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005).md);
([Rev 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Psa 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014).md).
