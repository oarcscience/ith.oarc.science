[Romans 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">rom 5:12</span></span>

have sinned

The first sin wrought the moral ruin of the race. The demonstration is
simple.

\(1\) Death is universal ([Rom 4:12](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#012).md);
([Rom 4:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#014).md), all die: sinless infants,
moral people, religious people, equally with the depraved. For a
universal effect there must be a universal cause; that cause is a state
of universal sin ([Rom 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#012).md).

\(2\) But this universal state must have had a cause. It did. The
consequence of Adam's sin was that "the many were made sinners" ([Rom
5:19](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#019).md) -- "By the offence of one
judgment came upon all men unto condemnation" ([Rom
5:18](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#018).md).

\(3\) Personal sins are not meant here. From Adam to Moses death reigned
([Rom 5:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#014).md), although, there being no
law, personal guilt was not imputed ([Rom
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#013).md). Accordingly, from ([Gen
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/gen004.htm#007).md) to ([Exo
29:14](../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#014).md) the sin-offering is not once
mentioned. Then, since physical death from Adam to Moses was not due to
the sinful acts of those who die ([Rom
5:13](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#013).md), it follows that it was due to a
universal sinful state, or nature, and that state is declared to be out
inheritance from Adam.

\(4\) the moral state of fallen man is described in Scripture ([Gen
6:5](../../../bib/kjv/gen006.htm#005).md); ([Kg1
8:46](../../../bib/kjv/kg1008.htm#046).md); ([Psa
14:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#001).md); ([Psa
39:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa039.htm#005).md); ([Jer
17:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#009).md); ([Mat

[Romans 5:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">rom 5:14</span></span>

Adam to Moses

Broadly, the contrast is:

Adam: sin, death;

Christ: righteousness, life.

Adam drew down into his ruin the old creation ([Rom
8:19-22](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#019).md) of which he was lord and
head. Christ brings into moral unity with God, and into eternal life,
the new creation of which he is Lord and Head. ([Eph
1:22](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#022).md); ([Eph
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#023).md). Even the animal and material
creation, cursed for man's sake, ([Gen
3:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#017).md) will be delivered by Christ.
([Isa 11:6-9](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#006).md); ([Rom
8:19-22](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#019).md).
