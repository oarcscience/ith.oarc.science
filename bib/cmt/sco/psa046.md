### [Psalms Chapter 46](../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#001)

Alamoth

Alamoth, "soprano," from almah, a virgin. Some have thought the alamoth,
"virgins," were a temple choir, singing antiphonally to the sheminith,
or male choir.

(See Scofield) - ([Psa 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa006.htm#001)).

But contr, see ([Ch1 15:20](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#020)).
