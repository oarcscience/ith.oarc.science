Ephesians 5:25

eph 5:25

even as Christ

Christ's love-work for the church is threefold: past, present, future:

(1) Past; For love, He gave Himself to redeem the church (Eph 5:25);

(2) Present; In love, He is sanctifying the church (Eph 5:26);

(3) Future; For the reward of His sacrifice and labour of love, He will present the church to Himself in flawless perfection, "one pearl of great price" (Eph 5:27); (Mat 13:46).

Ephesians 5:32

eph 5:32

bride

(Eph 5:30); (Eph 5:31) are quoted from (Gen 2:23); (Gen 2:24), and exclude the interpretation that the reference is to the church merely as the body of Christ.
