### [Exodus Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#001.md)

[Exodus 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">exo 24:4</span></span>

builded

Compare ([Exo 33:7-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo033.htm#007).md).

This arrangement for worship was temporarily called the "tabernacle."

[Exodus 24:11](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">exo 24:11</span></span>

eat and drink

Symbol of communion.

Compare ([Luk 22:15-18](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#015).md).

A blood-sprinkled people, see ([Heb
9:19-22](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#019).md) who had not yet broken the
law could thus commune with God. Never again was this repeated.

Compare ([Exo 24:9-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#009).md) with ([Lev
16:2](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#002).md) and ([Heb
9:6-8](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#006).md).

But compare, under grace, ([Eph 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#013).md);
([Heb 10:19](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#019).md); ([Heb
10:20](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#020).md).
