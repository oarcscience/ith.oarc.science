### [Isaiah Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/isa017.htm#001)

[Isaiah 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa017.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">isa 17:1</span></span>

burden of Damascus

As in the burden of Moab, there was doubtless a near fulfilment in
Sennacherib's approaching invasion, but 17:12-14 as evidently look
forward to the final invasion and battle. ("Armageddon," ([Rev
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 19:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017))

Compare ([Isa 10:26-34](../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#026)).
