### [Matthew Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#001.md)

[Matthew 22:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mat 22:2</span></span>

kingdom

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002).md).

[Matthew 22:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">mat 22:7</span></span>

burned

Fulfilled as to Jerusalem; A.D. 70. ([Luk
21:20-24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020).md).

[Matthew 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">mat 22:9</span></span>

go ye

The world-wide call. ([Mat 28:16-20](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#016).md);
([Rev 22:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#017).md).

[Matthew 22:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">mat 22:15</span></span>

Then went

In the different classes, ([Mat
23:15-40](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#015).md). Jesus meets representatives
of all Israel, Pharisees, Sadducees, Herodians.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007).md).

For them, silenced but unrepentant, no message is left but "woe." ([Mat
23:1-39](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#001).md).

[Matthew 22:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">mat 22:29</span></span>

ye do err

Or, ye deceive yourselves, etc. Jesus' answer gives the three
incapacities of the rationalist: self-deception, ([Rom
1:21-22](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#021).md); ignorance of the spiritual
content of Scripture, ([Act 13:27](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#027).md);
disbelief in the intervention of divine power. ([Pe2
3:5-9](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#005).md).

[Matthew 22:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#030.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">mat 22:30</span></span>

angels

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004).md).

[Matthew 22:35](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#035.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">mat 22:35</span></span>

lawyer

(Greek, "nomikos", "of the law"); occurs also ([Luk
7:30](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#030).md); ([Luk
10:25](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#025).md); ([Luk
11:45](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#045).md); ([Luk
11:46](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#046).md); ([Luk
11:52](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#052).md); ([Luk
14:3](../../../bib/kjv/luk014.htm#003).md); ([Tit
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#013).md). Except in the last instance,
"lawyer" is another name for "scribe."

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#004).md).

In ([Tit 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#013).md), the term has the
modern meaning.

[Matthew 22:41](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#041.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_041">mat 22:41</span></span>

asked them

Compare ([Mat 21:24](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#024).md); ([Joh
19:7](../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#007).md).

Jesus' question is not personal but doctrinal: "Whose son is the
Messiah?"

Compare ([Act 2:25-36](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#025).md); ([Rom
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003).md); ([Rom
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#004).md).
