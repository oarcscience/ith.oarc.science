### [Isaiah Chapter 65](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah 65:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">isa 65:17</span></span>

behold

Verse 17 ([Isa 65:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#017).md) looks beyond the
kingdom-age to the new heavens and the new earth (see refs. At
"create"), but verses 18-25 ([Isa
65:18-25](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#018).md) describe the kingdom-age
itself. Longevity is restored, but death, the "last enemy" ([Co1
15:26](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#026).md) is not destroyed till after
Satan's rebellion at the end of the thousand years. ([Rev
20:7-14](../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#007).md).
