### [Acts Chapter 27](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#001)

[Acts 27:1](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">act 27:1</span></span>

centurion

Commander of 100 soldiers.

[Acts 27:9](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">act 27:9</span></span>

fast

The fast was on the tenth day of the seventh month. ([Lev
23:27](../../../bib/kjv/lev023.htm#027)); ([Lev
23:29](../../../bib/kjv/lev023.htm#029)).

[Acts 27:17](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">act 27:17</span></span>

fall

Be cast upon the Syrtis.

[Acts 27:23](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">act 27:23</span></span>

the angel

An angel of the God whose I am, whom also I serve.

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004)).

[Acts 27:28](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">act 27:28</span></span>

fathoms

One fathom is between 6 and 7 feet.

[Acts 27:37](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#037)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">act 27:37</span></span>

two hundred

Some ancient authorities read, about threescore and sixteen souls.

[Acts 27:40](../../../bib/kjv/act027.htm#040)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_040">act 27:40</span></span>

taken up

Or, cut the anchors, they left them in the sea, etc.
