### [Matthew Chapter 23](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#001.md)

[Matthew 23:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mat 23:2</span></span>

sit in

Compare ([Ezr 7:6](../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#006).md); ([Ezr
7:25](../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#025).md); ([Ezr
7:26](../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#026).md).

Jesus' disciples were to honour the law, but not the hypocritical
teachers of it.

[Matthew 23:5](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">mat 23:5</span></span>

phylacteries

Passages of Scripture enclosed in a small case, bound upon arm or
forehead, ([Deu 6:8](../../../bib/kjv/deu006.htm#008).md).

[Matthew 23:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">mat 23:14</span></span>

Woe

The best manuscripts omit verse 14. ([Mat
23:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#014).md).

[Matthew 23:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">mat 23:15</span></span>

hell

Gehenna.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 5:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#022).md).

[Matthew 23:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">mat 23:16</span></span>

debtor

Or, bound; also ([Mat 23:18](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#018).md).
"guilty:"

[Matthew 23:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">mat 23:28</span></span>

iniquity

Literally, Lawlessness.

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023).md).

[Matthew 23:34](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#034.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">mat 23:34</span></span>

send unto you

The Jews' treatment of the apostles is proved, ([Mat
23:31-33](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#031).md).

[Matthew 23:35](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#035.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">mat 23:35</span></span>

Abel

Jesus' confirmation of ([Gen 4:8-10](../../../bib/kjv/gen004.htm#008).md).

Compare ([Heb 12:24](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#024).md).

[Matthew 23:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#036.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">mat 23:36</span></span>

come upon

See ([Rev 18:21-24](../../../bib/kjv/rev018.htm#021).md).

It is the way also of history; judgment falls upon one generation for
the sins of centuries. The pediction was fulfilled in the destruction of
Jerusalem, A.D. 70.

[Matthew 23:39](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#039.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_039">mat 23:39</span></span>

till

The three "untils" of Israel's blessing:

\(1\) Israel must say, "blessed is He" ([Mat
23:39](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#039).md); ([Rom
10:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003).md); ([Rom
10:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#004).md).

\(2\) Gentile world-power must run its course. ([Luk
21:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024).md); ([Dan
2:34](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034).md); ([Dan
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035).md).

\(3\) The elect number of Gentiles must be brought in. Then "the
Deliverer shall come out of Zion." etc. ([Rom
11:25-27](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025).md).
