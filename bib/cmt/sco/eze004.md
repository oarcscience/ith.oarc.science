### [Ezekiel Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/eze004.htm#001.md)

[Ezekiel 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze004.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 4:1</span></span>

take

The symbolic actions during the prophet's dumbness were testimonies to
the past wickedness and chastisement of the house of Israel (the whole
nation), and prophetic of a coming siege. They therefore intermediate
between the siege of ([Kg2 24:10-16](../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#010).md)
at which time Ezekiel was carried to Babylon, and the siege of ([Kg2
25:1-11](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#001).md) eleven years later.
