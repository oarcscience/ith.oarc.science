famine

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 12:10](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#010)).

[Genesis 43:34](../../../bib/kjv/gen043.htm#034)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">gen 43:34</span></span>

But Benjamin's mess

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 35:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#018)).

It is important to observe that Benjamin now becomes prominent. Joseph
is peculiarly the type of Christ in His first advent, rejection, death,
resurrection, and present exaltation among the Gentiles, but
unrecognized of Israel. As the greater Benjamin, "Son of sorrow," but
also "Son of my right hand," He is to be revealed in power in the
Kingdom ([Gen 1:26-28](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#026)).

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008)).

It is then, and not till then, that Israel is to be restored and
converted.

(See Scofield) - ([Deu 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003)).

Typically ([Gen 45:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen045.htm#001)); ([Gen
45:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen045.htm#002)) anticipates the revelation
prophetically described, ([Eze
20:33-36](../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#033)); ([Hos
2:14-23](../../../bib/kjv/hos002.htm#014)) at which time the Benjamin
type of Christ will be fulfilled.
