### [Leviticus Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#001.md)

go forth

As a type of Gospel salvation the points are:

1\. The leper does nothing ([Rom 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#005).md);
([Rom 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#005).md).

2\. the priest seeks the leper, not the leper the priest ([Luk
19:10](../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#010).md).

3\. "with-out shedding of blood is no remission" ([Heb
9:22](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#022).md).

4\. "and if Christ be not raised, your faith is vain" ([Co1
15:17](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#017).md).

[Leviticus 14:4](../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">lev 14:4</span></span>

birds

The bird slain, and the live bird, dipped in blood and released, present
the two aspects of salvation in ([Rom
4:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#025).md) "delivered for our offences, and
raised again for our justification."

[Leviticus 14:5](../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">lev 14:5</span></span>

vessel

The earthen vessel typifies the humanity of Christ, as the running water
typifies the Holy Spirit as the "Spirit of life" ([Rom
8:2](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002).md) "put to death in the flesh, but
quickened by the Spirit." ([Pe1 3:18](../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#018).md).
