[Hebrews 12:23](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">heb 12:23</span></span>

church

Church (true), Summary:

The true church, composed of the whole number of regenerate persons from
Pentecost to the first resurrection ([Co1
15:52](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#052)) united together and to Christ
by the baptism with the Holy Spirit ([Co1
12:12](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#012)); ([Co1
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#013)) is the body of Christ of which
He is the Head. ([Eph 1:22](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#022)); ([Eph
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#023)). As such, it is a holy temple
for the habitation of God through the Spirit ([Eph
2:21](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#021)); ([Eph
2:22](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#022)) is "one flesh" with Christ ([Eph
5:30](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#030)); ([Eph
5:31](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#031)) and espoused to Him as a chaste
virgin to one husband. ([Co2 11:2-4](../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#002)).

perfect

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 5:48](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048)).
