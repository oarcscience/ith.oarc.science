### [Daniel Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#001.md)

[Daniel 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">dan 1:6</span></span>

Daniel

deported 8 years before Ezekiel. ([Kg2
24:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#001).md); ([Kg2
24:2](../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#002).md); ([Ch2
36:5-7](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#005).md); ([Jer
25:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#001).md); ([Jer
52:12-30](../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#012).md).

[Daniel 1:7](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">dan 1:7</span></span>

Belteshazzar

That is, the king's leader, or attendant. ([Dan
2:26](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#026).md); ([Dan
4:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#008).md); ([Dan
4:9](../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#009).md); ([Dan
4:18](../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#018).md); ([Dan
4:19](../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#019).md); ([Dan
5:12](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#012).md). Identical in meaning with
Belshazzar.

[Daniel 1:21](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">dan 1:21</span></span>

continued

That is, to see the return of the remnant of Judah at the end of the 70
years, ([Jer 25:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#011).md); ([Jer
25:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#012).md); ([Jer
29:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#010).md). Daniel actually lived beyond
the first year of Cyrus. ([Dan 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#001).md).
