### [Genesis Chapter 49](../../../bib/kjv/gen049.htm#001.md)

[Genesis 49:28](../../../bib/kjv/gen049.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">gen 49:28</span></span>

blessed them

Jacob's life, ending in serenity and blessing, testifies to the power of
God to transform character. His spiritual progress has six notable
phases:

\(1\) the first exercise of faith, as shown in the purchase of the
birthright ([Gen 25:28-34](../../../bib/kjv/gen025.htm#028).md); ([Gen
27:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/gen027.htm#010).md).

\(2\) the vision at bethel ([Gen
28:10-19](../../../bib/kjv/gen028.htm#010).md).

\(3\) walking in the flesh ([Gen
29:1-31](../../../bib/kjv/gen029.htm#001).md); ([Gen
29:55](../../../bib/kjv/gen029.htm#055).md).

\(4\) the transforming experience ([Gen
32:24-31](../../../bib/kjv/gen032.htm#024).md).

\(5\) the return to Bethel: idols put away ([Gen
35:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#001).md).

\(6\) the walk of faith ([Gen
37:1-49](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#001).md); ([Gen
37:33](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#033).md).
