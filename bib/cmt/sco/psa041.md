Yea, mine own familiar friend

Psalm 41 is the Psalm of the betrayal of the Son of man, as Jesus
Himself taught. ([Joh 13:18](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#018).md); ([Joh
13:19](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#019).md). See Psalm 45, next in order of
the Messianic Psalms.

trusted
