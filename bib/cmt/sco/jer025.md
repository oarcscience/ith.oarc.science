### [Jeremiah Chapter 25](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 25:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">jer 25:11</span></span>

seventy years

Compare ([Lev 26:33-35](../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#033)); ([Ch2
36:21](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#021)); ([Dan
9:2](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#002))

The 70 years may be reckoned to begin with the first deportation of
Judah to Babylon. ([Kg2 24:10-15](../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#010)).
B.C. 604 according to the Assyrian Eponym Canon, or B.C. 606 according
to Ussher; or from the final deportation; ([Kg2
25:1-30](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#001)); ([Ch2
36:17-20](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#017)); ([Jer
39:8-10](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#008)). B.C. 586 (Assyr. Ep. Canon),
or B.C. 588 (Ussher). In the first case the 70 years extend to the
decree of Cyrus for the return ([Ezr
1:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/ezr001.htm#001)) B.C. (Assyr. Ep. Canon), or
B.C. 536 (Ussher). In the second case the 70 years terminate B.C. 516
(Assyr. Ep. Canon) with the completion of the temple. The latter is more
probable reckoning in the light of ([Dan
9:25](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#025)).

[Jeremiah 25:29](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">jer 25:29</span></span>

all the inhabitants

The scope of this great prophecy cannot be limited to the invasion of
Nebuchadnezzar. If Jehovah does not spare His own city, should the
Gentile nations imagine that there is no judgment for them? The prophecy
leaps to the very end of this age.

(See "Day of the Lord," ([Isa
2:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010)); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011))

"Armageddon,"; ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)).
