### [Zechariah Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#001.md)

[Zechariah 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 6:1</span></span>

four chariots

The interpretation of the tenth vision must be governed by the
authoritative declaration of ([Zac
6:5](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#005).md). That which is symbolized by the
four chariots with their horses is not the four world-empires of Daniel,
but "the four spirits of heaven which go forth from standing before the
Lord of all the earth" ([Zac 6:5](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#005).md).

These "spirits" are angels; ([Luk
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#019).md); ([Heb
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#014).md) and are most naturally
interpreted of the four angels of; ([Rev
7:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#001).md); ([Rev
9:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev009.htm#014).md); ([Rev
9:15](../../../bib/kjv/rev009.htm#015).md). These have also a ministry
earthward, and of like nature with the "spirits" of ([Zac
6:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#001).md). namely, judgment.

The symbol (chariots and horses) is in perfect harmony with this. Always
in Scripture symbolism they stand for the power of God earthward in
judgment. ([Jer 46:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#009).md); ([Jer
46:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#010).md); ([Joe
2:3-11](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#003).md); ([Nah
3:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/nah003.htm#001).md).

The vision, then, speaks of the Lord's judgments upon the Gentile
nations north and south in the day of the Lord; ([Isa
2:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010).md); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md).

[Zechariah 6:11](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zac 6:11</span></span>

crowns

Following the earth-judgments symbolized in the horsed chariots ([Zac
6:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#001).md) comes the manifestation of
Christ in His kingdom glory ([Zac
6:9-15](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#009).md).

This is the invariable prophetic order: first the judgments of the day
of the Lord; ([Isa 2:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010).md); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)) then the kingdom (cf.md); ([Psa
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005).md); ([Psa
2:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#006).md); ([Isa
3:24-26](../../../bib/kjv/isa003.htm#024).md); ([Isa
4:2-6](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002).md); ([Isa
10:33](../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#033).md); ([Isa
10:34](../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#034).md); ([Isa
11:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001).md); ([Rev
19:19-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#019).md); ([Rev
20:4-6](../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#004).md). This is set forth
symbolically by the crowning of Joshua, which was not a vision, but
actually done

Compare ([Isa 8:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#003).md); ([Isa
8:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#004).md); ([Eze
37:16-22](../../../bib/kjv/eze037.htm#016).md).

The fulfilment in the BRANCH will infinitely transcend the symbol. He
"shall bear the glory"; ([Zac 6:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#013).md);
([Mat 16:27](../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#027).md); ([Mat
24:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#030).md); ([Mat
25:31](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031).md) as the Priest-King on His own
throne ([Zac 6:12](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#012).md); ([Zac
6:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#013).md); ([Heb
7:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/heb007.htm#001).md).

Christ is now a Priest, but still in the holiest within the veil; ([Lev
16:15](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#015).md); ([Heb
9:11-14](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#011).md); ([Heb
9:24](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#024).md) and seated on the Father's
throne ([Rev 3:21](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#021).md). He has not yet
come out to take His own throne ([Heb
9:28](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#028).md). The crowns made for the
symbolical crowning of Joshua were to be laid up in the temple as a
memorial to keep alive this larger hope of Israel.
