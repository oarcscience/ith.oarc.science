[Psalms 103:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 103:12</span></span>

Removed our transgression from us

Three Hebrew words are translated, forgive, forgiven: kaphar, to cover;
nasa, to lift away; salach, to send away (cf). ([Lev
16:21](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#021).md); ([Lev
16:22](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#022).md) the fundamental Old Testament
idea of forgiveness being not the remission of penalty, but the
separation of the sinner from his sin.

([Psa 103:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#012).md) expresses this.
