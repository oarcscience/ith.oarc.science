[Luke 9:11](../../../bib/kjv/luk009.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">luk 9:11</span></span>

need of

See ([Luk 4:40](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#040)); ([Rom
5:20](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#020)).

Wherever there is need acknowledged the Lord is ready to meet it. Men
might have put the bodily need of healing first, since that is keenly
felt. Spiritual need is often the greatest where there is the least
consciousness of it.

Compare ([Rev 3:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#017)).
