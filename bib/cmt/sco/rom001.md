[Romans 1:16](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">rom 1:16</span></span>

salvation

The Hebrew, and (Greek, "sōtēria", meaning "safety", "preservation",
"healing", and "soundness"). Salvation is the great inclusive word of
the Gospel, gathering into itself all the redemptive acts and processes:
as justification, redemption, grace, propitiation, imputation,
forgiveness, sanctification, and glorification. Salvation is in three
tenses:

\(1\) The believer has been saved from the guilt and penalty of sin
([Luk 7:50](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#050)); ([Co1
1:18](../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#018)); ([Co2
2:15](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#015)); ([Eph
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#005)); ([Eph
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#008)); ([Ti2
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#009)) and is safe.

\(2\) the believer is being saved from the habit and dominion of sin
([Rom 6:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#014)); ([Phi
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#019)); ([Phi
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#012)); ([Phi
2:13](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#013)); ([Th2
2:13](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#013)); ([Rom
8:2](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002)); ([Gal
2:19](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#019)); ([Gal
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#020)); ([Co2
3:18](../../../bib/kjv/co2003.htm#018)).

\(3\) The believer is to be saved in the sense of entire conformity to
Christ. ([Rom 13:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom013.htm#011)); ([Heb
10:36](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#036)); ([Pe1
1:5](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#005)); ([Jo1
3:2](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#002)).

Salvation is by grace through faith, is a free gift, and wholly without
works; ([Rom 3:27](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#027)); ([Rom
3:28](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#028)); ([Rom
4:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#001)); ([Rom
6:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#023)); ([Eph
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#008)).

The divine order is:

first, salvation,

then, works; ([Eph 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#009)); ([Eph
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#010)); ([Tit
3:5-8](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#005)).
