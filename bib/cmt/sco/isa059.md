Redeemer

Redemption: Kinsman type, summary. The goel, or Kinsman-Redeemer, is a
beautiful type of Christ.

\(1\) The kinsman redemption was of persons, and an inheritance ([Lev
25:48](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#048)); ([Lev
25:25](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#025)); ([Gal
4:5](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#005)); ([Eph
1:7](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#007)); ([Eph
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#011)); ([Eph
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#014)).

\(2\) The Redeemer must be a kinsman ([Lev
25:48](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#048)); ([Lev
25:49](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#049)); ([Rut
3:12](../../../bib/kjv/rut003.htm#012)); ([Rut
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/rut003.htm#013)); ([Gal
4:4](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#004)); ([Heb
2:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#014)); ([Heb
2:15](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#015)).

\(3\) The Redeemer must be able to redeem ([Rut
4:4-6](../../../bib/kjv/rut004.htm#004)); ([Jer
50:34](../../../bib/kjv/jer050.htm#034)); ([Joh
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#011)); ([Joh
10:18](../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#018)).

\(4\) Redemption is effected by the goel paying the just demand in full
([Lev 25:27](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#027)); ([Pe1
1:18](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#018)); ([Pe1
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#019)); ([Gal
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#013)).

(See Scofield) - ([Exo 14:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#030)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:24](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024)).

come to Zion

The time when the "Redeemer shall come to Zion" is fixed, relatively, by
([Rom 11:23-29](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#023)) as following the
completion of the Gentile Church. That is also the order of the great
dispensational passage, ([Act
15:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)).

In both, the return of the Lord to Zion follows the outcalling of the
Church.
