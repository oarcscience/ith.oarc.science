<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pe2 3:18</span></span>

grace

Grace (imparted). Summary (see "Grace").

(See Scofield) - ([Joh 1:17](../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#017))

Grace is not only dispensationally a method of divine dealing in
salvation,

(See Scofield) - ([Joh 1:17](../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#017)),

but is also the method of God in the believer's life and service. As
saved, he is "not under the law, but under grace". ([Rom
6:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#014)). Having by grace brought the
believer into the highest conceivable position. ([Eph
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#006)). God ceaselessly works through
grace, to impart to, and perfect in him, corresponding graces. ([Joh
15:4](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#004)); ([Joh
15:5](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#005)); ([Gal
5:22](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#022)); ([Gal
5:23](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#023)).
