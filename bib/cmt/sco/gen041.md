### [Genesis Chapter 41](../../../bib/kjv/gen041.htm#001.md)

[Genesis 41:45](../../../bib/kjv/gen041.htm#045.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_045">gen 41:45</span></span>

Zaphnath-paaneah

Asenath, the Gentile bride espoused by Joseph the rejected one ([Joh
19:15](../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#015).md) type of the Church, called out
from the Gentiles to be the bride of Christ during the time of His
rejection by His brethren, Israel. ([Act
15:14](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014).md); ([Eph
5:31](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#031).md); ([Eph
5:32](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#032).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 37:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#002).md).

Zaphnath-paaneah Coptic, revealer of secret things.
