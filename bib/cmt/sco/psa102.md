### [Psalms Chapter 102](../../../bib/kjv/psa102.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 102:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa102.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 102:1</span></span>

Hear my prayer, O Lord

The references of ([Psa 102:25-27](../../../bib/kjv/psa102.htm#025).md) to
Christ ([Heb 1:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#010).md) assures us that
in the preceding verses of Psalm 102 we have, prophetically, the
exercises of His holy soul in the days of His humiliation and rejection.

See Psalm 110 ([Psa 110:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001).md), next in
order of the Messianic Psalms.

[Psalms 102:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa102.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 102:15</span></span>

heathen

fear

(See Scofield) - ([Psa 19:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#009).md).
