### [Isaiah Chapter 32](../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#001.md)

king

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 29:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa029.htm#003).md).

In chapters 32-35, the same blended meanings of near and far fulfilments
are found. The near view is still of Sennacherib's invasion, the far
view of the day of the Lord. ([Isa
2:10-22](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010).md); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md) and the kingdom blessing to
follow.
