### [Isaiah Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/isa006.htm#001)

[Isaiah 6:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa006.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">isa 6:2</span></span>

seraphims

Hebrew, Burners. The word occurs only here.

(See Scofield) - ([Eze 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#005)).

The Seraphim are, in many respects, in contrast with the Cherubim,
though both are expressive of the divine holiness, which demands that
the sinner shall have access to divine presence only through a sacrifice
which really vindicates the righteousness of God.

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:24](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#026))

and that the saint shall be cleansed before serving. ([Gen
3:22-24](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#022)) illustrates the first; ([Isa
6:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/isa006.htm#001)) the second. The Cherubim may be
said to have to do with the altar, the Seraphim with the laver.

(See Scofield) - ([Psa 51:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa051.htm#007)).

(See Scofield) - ([Joh 13:10](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#010)).

The Seraphim appear to be actual angelic beings.
