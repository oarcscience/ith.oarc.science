### [Hosea Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001.md)

[Hosea 1:9](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">hos 1:9</span></span>

my people

"My people" is an expression used in the Old Testament exclusively of
Israel the nation. It is never used of the patriarchs, Abraham, Isaac,
and Jacob. ([Mat 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#006).md).

[Hosea 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">hos 1:10</span></span>

Israel

"Israel" in Hosea means the ten tribes forming the northern kingdom as
distinguished from "Judah" (the tribes of Judah and Benjamin) forming
the southern kingdom which adhered to the Davidic family.

(See ([Kg1 12:1-21](../../../bib/kjv/kg1012.htm#001).md). The promise of
verse 10 ([Hos 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#010).md) awaits
fulfilment.

See "Israel"; ([Gen 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002).md); ([Gen
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003).md); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026).md).
