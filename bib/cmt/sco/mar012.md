### [Mark Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001)

[Mark 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mar 12:1</span></span>

vineyard

Israel.

([Isa 5:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#001)). Israel was not
fruitless, but brought forth only wild grapes.

Compare ([Joh 3:6](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#006)); ([Hos
10:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos010.htm#001)).

Contrast ([Hos 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/hos014.htm#008)).

[Mark 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mar 12:2</span></span>

sent to

([Mar 12:2-5](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002)); The prophets and John
the Baptist.

[Mark 12:6](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">mar 12:6</span></span>

son

Jesus Himself.

Compare ([Heb 1:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#001)).

[Mark 12:9](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">mar 12:9</span></span>

destroy

Fulfilled in the destruction of Jerusalem, A.D. 70.

Compare ([Luk 21:20-24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020)).

[Mark 12:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">mar 12:10</span></span>

The stone

See ([Psa 118:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#022)); ([Psa
118:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#023)).

Compare ([Pe1 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#008)).

[Mark 12:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">mar 12:11</span></span>

Lord's

Jehovah. Verses ([Mar 10:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#010)); ([Mar
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#011)); ([Psa
118:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#022)); ([Psa
118:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#023)).

[Mark 12:18](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">mar 12:18</span></span>

Sadducees

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007)).

Also ([Mar 12:22-23](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#022)).

[Mark 12:24](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">mar 12:24</span></span>

said unto them

See margin reference, (See Scofield) - ([Mat
22:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#029)).

[Mark 12:25](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">mar 12:25</span></span>

from the dead

Literally, from amongst;

Compare ([Phi 3:11](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#011)).

Here it is the first resurrection.

(See Scofield) - ([Co1 15:52](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#052)).

angels

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004)).

[Mark 12:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">mar 12:26</span></span>

read in the book

Jesus affirms the historic truth and inspiration of Exodus 3.

[Mark 12:34](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#034)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">mar 12:34</span></span>

far

That is, not far in knowledge. He knew the very law which utterly
condemns the best man -- its true office. ([Rom
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#019)); ([Rom
10:3-5](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003)); ([Gal
3:10](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#010)); ([Gal
3:22-24](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#022)).

[Mark 12:35](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">mar 12:35</span></span>

son of

That is, David's Son only.

Compare ([Rom 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003)); ([Rom
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#004)).

[Mark 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#036)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">mar 12:36</span></span>

For David

Inspiration. (Jesus affirms the inspiration and Davidic authorship of
Psa 110) ([Mat 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036)); ([Luk
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#003)); ([Exo
4:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#015)); ([Rev
22:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#019)).

The Lord

Jehovah.

my Lord

Adonai, ([Psa 110:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001)).
