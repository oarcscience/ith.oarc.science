### [Mark Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001.md)

[Mark 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mar 12:1</span></span>

vineyard

Israel.

([Isa 5:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#001).md). Israel was not
fruitless, but brought forth only wild grapes.

Compare ([Joh 3:6](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#006).md); ([Hos
10:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos010.htm#001).md).

Contrast ([Hos 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/hos014.htm#008).md).

[Mark 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mar 12:2</span></span>

sent to

([Mar 12:2-5](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002).md); The prophets and John
the Baptist.

[Mark 12:6](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">mar 12:6</span></span>

son

Jesus Himself.

Compare ([Heb 1:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#001).md).

[Mark 12:9](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">mar 12:9</span></span>

destroy

[Mark 12:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">mar 12:26</span></span>

read in the book

[Mark 12:34](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#034.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">mar 12:34</span></span>

far

That is, not far in knowledge. He knew the very law which utterly
condemns the best man -- its true office. ([Rom
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#019).md); ([Rom
10:3-5](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003).md); ([Gal
3:10](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#010).md); ([Gal
3:22-24](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#022).md).

[Mark 12:35](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">mar 12:35</span></span>

son of

That is, David's Son only.

Compare ([Rom 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003).md); ([Rom
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#004).md).

[Mark 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#036.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">mar 12:36</span></span>

For David

Inspiration. (Jesus affirms the inspiration and Davidic authorship of
Psa 110) ([Mat 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036).md); ([Luk
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#003).md); ([Exo
4:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#015).md); ([Rev
22:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#019).md).

The Lord

Jehovah.

my Lord

Adonai, ([Psa 110:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001).md).
