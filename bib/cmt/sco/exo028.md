### [Exodus Chapter 28](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#001)

[Exodus 28:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">exo 28:1</span></span>

Aaron

Type of Christ, our High Priest. Christ is a priest after the order of
Melchizedek, but He executes his priestly office after the pattern of
Aaron. Hebrew, 7. gives the order; Hebrew, 9, the pattern.

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 14:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#018)).

[Exodus 28:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">exo 28:2</span></span>

holy garments

Hebrew, "Qodesh", means "set apart" for God. Translated, "holy," ([Exo
28:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#002)); "consecrate," ([Exo
28:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#003)). Often translated, "sanctify."
See summary,

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 8:3](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#003)).

This is always the fundamental idea of a holy, consecrated, separated,
or sanctified person or thing -- something set apart for God. Infinite
confusion would have been spared the reader if "qodesh" had been
uniformly translated, "set apart."

[Exodus 28:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">exo 28:30</span></span>

Urim and Thummim

Urim and Thummim, meaning "lights and perfections." Some make these to
be simply a collective name for the stones of the breastplate, so that
the total effect of the twelve stones is to manifest the "lights and
perfections" of Him who is the antitype of the Aaronic high priest. Per
contra, ([Lev 8:8](../../../bib/kjv/lev008.htm#008)). This would seem to
be conclusive that "the Urim and Thummim" are additional to the stones
of the breastplate. In use the Urim and Thummim were connected, in some
way not clearly expressed, with the ascertainment of the divine will in
particular cases; ([Num 27:21](../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#021)); ([Deu
33:8](../../../bib/kjv/deu033.htm#008)); ([Sa1
28:6](../../../bib/kjv/sa1028.htm#006)); ([Ezr
2:63](../../../bib/kjv/ezr002.htm#063)).
