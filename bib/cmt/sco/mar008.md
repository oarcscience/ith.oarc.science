[Mark 8:23](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">mar 8:23</span></span>

And he took

Our Lord's action here is most significant, Having abandoned Bethsaida
to judgment ([Mat 11:12-24](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#012)). He would
neither heal in that village, nor permit further testimony to be borne
there ([Mar 8:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#026)). The probation of
Bethsaida as a community was ended, but He would still show mercy to
individuals.

Compare ([Rev 3:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#020)).

Christ is outside the door of that church, but "If any man hear My
voice," etc.
