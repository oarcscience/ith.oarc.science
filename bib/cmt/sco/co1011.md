### [1 Corinthians Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#001)

[1 Corinthians 11:2](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">co1 11:2</span></span>

ordinances

Things delivered; often translated, "traditions".

[1 Corinthians 11:3](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">co1 11:3</span></span>

woman

Compare ([Gen 3:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#016)).

The woman's veil, or head-covering, is a symbol of this subordination.

[1 Corinthians 11:10](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">co1 11:10</span></span>

power

Authority, that is, the sign of the husband's authority.

angels

That is, of the presence of the angels.

[1 Corinthians 11:27](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">co1 11:27</span></span>

unworthily

That is, in an unworthy manner.

Compare ([Co1 11:20-22](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#020)).

[1 Corinthians 11:29](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">co1 11:29</span></span>

damnation

Judgment, in the sense of ([Co1
11:32](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#032)).

[1 Corinthians 11:31](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">co1 11:31</span></span>

judge

Self-judgment is not so much the believer's moral condemnation of his
own ways or habits, or of himself, for allowing such ways. Self-judgment
avoids chastisement. If neglected, the Lord judges, and the result is
chastisement, but never condemnation ([Co1
11:32](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#032)); ([Sa2
7:14](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#014)); ([Sa2
7:15](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#015)); ([Sa2
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/sa2012.htm#013)); ([Sa2
12:14](../../../bib/kjv/sa2012.htm#014)); ([Co1
5:5](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#005)); ([Ti1
1:20](../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#020)); ([Heb
12:7](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#007)).
