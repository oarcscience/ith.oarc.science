### [2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#001)

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 7:15](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">sa2 7:15</span></span>

mercy

Verses 14 and 15 ([Sa2 7:14-15](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#014)) state
the principle of judgment within the family of God.

(See Scofield) - ([Co1 11:31](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#031)).

It is always remedial, not penal ([Heb
12:5-11](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#005)).

Judgment of the wicked is penal, not remedial.

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 7:16](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">sa2 7:16</span></span>

The Davidic Covenant

The Davidic Covenant ([Sa2 7:8-17](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#008)).

This covenant, upon which the glorious kingdom of Christ "of the seed of
David according to the flesh" is to be founded, secures:

\(1\) A Davidic "house"; that is, Posterity, family

\(2\) A "throne"; that is, Royal authority

\(3\) A kingdom; that is, Sphere of rule

\(4\) In perpetuity; "for ever"

\(5\) And this fourfold covenant has but one condition: disobedience in
the Davidic family is to be visited with chastisement; but not to the
abrogation of the covenant ([Sa2
7:15](../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#015)); ([Psa
89:20-37](../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#020)); ([Isa
24:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa024.htm#005)); ([Isa
54:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa054.htm#003)).

The chastisement fell; first in the division of the kingdom under
Rehoboam, and, finally, in the captivities. ([Kg2
25:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#001)). Since that time but one King
of the Davidic family has been crowned at Jerusalem and He was crowned
with thorns. But the Davidic Covenant confirmed to David by the oath of
Jehovah, and renewed to Mary by the angel Gabriel, is immutable ([Psa
89:30-37](../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#030)) and the Lord God will yet
give to that thorn-crowned One "the throne of his father David."; ([Luk
1:31-33](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#031)); ([Act
2:29-32](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#029)); ([Act
15:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)).
