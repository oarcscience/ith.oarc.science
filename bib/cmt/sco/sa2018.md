### [2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/sa2018.htm#001.md)

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 18:18](../../../bib/kjv/sa2018.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">sa2 18:18</span></span>

I have no son

The pillar mentioned must have been reared before the birth of sons to
Absalom.

Compare ([Sa2 14:27](../../../bib/kjv/sa2014.htm#027).md).

Another view is that his sons died in youth. They are not mentioned in
the genealogies.
