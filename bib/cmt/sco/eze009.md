### [Ezekiel Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 9:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 9:3</span></span>

was gone

It is noteworthy that to Ezekiel the priest was given the vision of the
glory of the Lord

\(1\) departing from the Cherubim to the threshold of the temple ([Eze
9:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#003)); ([Eze
10:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#004)).

\(2\) from the threshold ([Eze 10:18](../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#018)).

\(3\) from temple and city to the mountain on the East of Jerusalem
(Olivet, ([Eze 11:23](../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#023)) and

\(4\) returning to the millennial temple to abide. ([Eze
43:2-5](../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#002)).

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 9:4</span></span>
