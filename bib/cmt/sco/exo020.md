### [Exodus Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#001.md)

[Exodus 20:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">exo 20:4</span></span>

shalt not make

There is a threefold giving of the law. First, orally, in ([Exo
20:1-17](../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#001).md). This was pure law, with no
provision of priesthood and sacrifice for failure, and was accompanied
by the "judgments"; ([Exo 21:1-23](../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#001).md);
([Exo 21:13](../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#013).md); relating to the
relations of Hebrew with Hebrew; to which were added ([Exo
23:14-19](../../../bib/kjv/exo023.htm#014).md) directions for keeping three
annual feasts, and ([Exo 23:20-33](../../../bib/kjv/exo023.htm#020).md)
instructions for the conquest of Canaan. These words Moses communicated
to the people. ([Exo 24:3-8](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#003).md).
Immediately, in the persons of their elders, they were admitted to the
fellowship of God. ([Exo 24:9-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#009).md).

Second, Moses was then called up to receive the tables of stone. ([Exo
24:12-18](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#012).md). The story then divides.
Moses, in the mount, receives the gracious instructions concerning the
tabernacle, priesthood, and sacrifice (Exodus 25-31) Meantime (Exodus
32), the people, led by Aaron, break the first commandment. Moses,
returning, breaks the tables "written with the finger of God."; ([Exo
31:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#018).md); ([Exo
32:16-19](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#016).md).

Third, the second tables were made by Moses, and the law again written
by the hand of Jehovah ([Exo 34:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#001).md);
([Exo 34:28](../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#028).md); ([Exo
34:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#029).md); ([Deu
10:4](../../../bib/kjv/deu010.htm#004).md).
