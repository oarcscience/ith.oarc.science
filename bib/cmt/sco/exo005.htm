Exodus Chapter 5

Exodus 5:1

exo 5:1

Let my people

Compare (Exo 4:22); (Exo 4:23).

Possibly Moses and Aaron shrank from delivering the message concerning the firstborn.

Exodus 5:3

exo 5:3

three days

Compare (Mat 12:38-40).

By death and resurrection will God have his people separated from Egypt -- the world. (Rom 6:1-11); (Gal 6:14-15); (Heb 13:12); (Heb 13:13).
