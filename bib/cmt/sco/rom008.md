### [Romans Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#001.md)

[Romans 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">rom 8:1</span></span>

who walk

The statement ends with "Christ Jesus"; the last ten words are
interpolated.

[Romans 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">rom 8:2</span></span>

Spirit

Hitherto, in Romans, the Holy Spirit has been mentioned but once; ([Rom
5:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#005).md); in this chapter, He is mentioned
nineteen times. Redemption is by blood and by power.

(See Scofield) - ([Exo 14:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#030).md); ([Rom
3:21-5](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021).md); ([Rom
3:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#011).md);

speaks of the redemptive price; Romans 8 speaks of redemptive power.

[Romans 8:16](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">rom 8:16</span></span>

children

(Greek, "teknon", "one born," a "child" (and so in ([Rom
8:17](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#017).md); ([Rom
8:21](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#021).md); not, as in ([Rom
8:14](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#014)); "sons" (Greek, "huios".md).

See ([Gal 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#001).md); ([Gal
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#007).md) where babyhood and sonhood are
contrasted.

Also "Adoption"; ([Rom 8:15](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#015).md); ([Rom
8:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#023).md); ([Eph
1:5](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#005).md).
