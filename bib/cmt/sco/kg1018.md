### [3 Kings (1 Kings) Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#001)

[3 Kings (1 Kings) 18:3](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">kg1 18:3</span></span>

Obadiah

In such a time as the reign of Ahab and Jezebel a believer's true place
was by Elijah's side. Obadiah is a warning type of the men of God who
adhere to the world while still seeking to serve God. The secret of the
Lord, and the power of the Lord were with Elijah, the separated servant.

Compare ([Ti2 2:20-21](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#020)).
