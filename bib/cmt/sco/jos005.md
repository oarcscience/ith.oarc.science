### [Joshua Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/jos005.htm#001.md)

[Joshua 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/jos005.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jos 5:2</span></span>

circumcision

Circumcision is the "sign" of the Abrahamic Covenant ([Gen
17:7-14](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#007).md); ([Rom
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#011).md). "The reproach of Egypt" was
that, during the later years of the Egyptian bondage, this separating
sign had been neglected (Compare ([Exo
4:24-26](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#024).md) and this neglect had
continued during the wilderness wanderings. The New Testament analogue
is world conformity; the failure openly to take a believer's place with
Christ in death and resurrection. ([Rom
6:2-11](../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#002).md); ([Gal
6:14-16](../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#014).md). Spiritually it is mortifying
the deeds of the body through the Spirit. ([Rom
8:13](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#013).md); ([Gal
5:16](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#016).md); ([Gal
5:17](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#017).md); ([Col
2:11](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#011).md); ([Col
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#012).md); ([Col
3:5-10](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#005).md).

[Joshua 5:11](../../../bib/kjv/jos005.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">jos 5:11</span></span>

corn

The manna is a type of Christ in humiliation, known "after the flesh,"
giving his flesh that the believer might have life ([Joh
6:49-51](../../../bib/kjv/joh006.htm#049).md) while the "old corn of the
land" is Christ apprehended as risen, glorified, and seated in the
heavenlies. Occupation with Christ on earth, "crucified through
weakness," tends to a wilderness experience. An experience befitting the
believer's place in the heavenlies demands an apprehension of the power
of His resurrection; ([Co2 5:16](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#016).md);
([Co2 13:4](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#004).md); ([Phi
3:10](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#010).md); ([Eph
1:15-23](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#015).md). It is the contrast between
"milk" and "meat" in Paul's writings. ([Co1
3:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#001).md); ([Co1
3:2](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#002).md); ([Heb
5:12-14](../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#012).md); ([Heb
6:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#001).md).

[Joshua 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/jos005.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">jos 5:15</span></span>

holy

translated, "consecrated," ([Jos 6:19](../../../bib/kjv/jos006.htm#019).md)
in R.V. "holy."
