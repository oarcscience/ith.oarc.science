### [Exodus Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#001)

[Exodus 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">exo 2:2</span></span>

son

Moses, type of Christ the Deliverer ([Isa
61:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa061.htm#001)); ([Luk
4:18](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#018)); ([Co2
1:10](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#010)); ([Th1
1:10](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#010)).

\(1\) A divinely chosen deliverer ([Exo
3:7-10](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#007)); ([Act
7:25](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#025)); ([Joh
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016)).

\(2\) Rejected by Israel he turns to the Gentiles ([Exo
2:11-15](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#011)); ([Act
7:25](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#025)); ([Act
18:5-6](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#005)); ([Act
28:17-28](../../../bib/kjv/act028.htm#017)).

\(3\) During his rejection he gains a Gentile bride ([Exo
2:16-21](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#016)); ([Mat
12:14-21](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#014)); ([Co2
11:2](../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#002)); ([Eph
5:30-32](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#030)).

\(4\) Afterward he again appears as Israel's deliverer, and is accepted
([Exo 4:29-31](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#029)); ([Rom
11:14-26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#014)); ([Act
15:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)).

\(5\) Officially, Moses typifies Christ as Prophet ([Act
3:22](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#022)); ([Act
3:23](../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#023)). Advocate; ([Exo
32:31-35](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#031)); ([Jo1
2:1](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#001)); ([Jo1
2:2](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#002)); Intercessor; ([Exo
17:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#001)); ([Heb
7:25](../../../bib/kjv/heb007.htm#025)) and Leader, or King; ([Deu
33:4](../../../bib/kjv/deu033.htm#004)); ([Deu
33:5](../../../bib/kjv/deu033.htm#005)); ([Isa
55:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa055.htm#004)); ([Heb
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#010)) while, in relation to the house
of God, he is in contrast with Christ. Moses was faithful as a servant
over another's house; Christ as a Son over His own house ([Heb
3:5](../../../bib/kjv/heb003.htm#005)); ([Heb
3:6](../../../bib/kjv/heb003.htm#006)).

[Exodus 2:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">exo 2:18</span></span>

Reuel

Called Raguel. ([Num 10:29](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#029)).
