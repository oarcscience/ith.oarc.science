### [Numbers Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#001.md)

[Numbers 14:23](../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">num 14:23</span></span>

Surely

Kadesh-barnea is, by the unbelief of Israel there, and the divine
comment on that unbelief ([Num
14:22-38](../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#022).md); ([Deu
1:19-40](../../../bib/kjv/deu001.htm#019).md); ([Co1
10:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#001).md); ([Heb
3:12-19](../../../bib/kjv/heb003.htm#012).md) invested with immense
spiritual significance. The people had faith to sprinkle the blood of
atonement ([Exo 12:28](../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#028).md) and to come out
of Egypt (the world), but had not faith to enter their Canaan rest.
Therefore, though redeemed, they were a forty years' grief to Jehovah.
The spiritual application is made in ([Heb
6:3-11](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#003).md):

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 6:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb006.htm#004).md).
