[3 Kings (1 Kings) 15:14](../../../bib/kjv/kg1015.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">kg1 15:14</span></span>

high places

Compare ([Ch2 14:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#003)).

It appears that local sacrifices to Jehovah (though not according to the
divine order) were offered in the times of the kings upon "high places"
(cf) ([Sa1 9:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa1009.htm#012)). Apparently Asa's
mother had defiled one of these with an idol, ([Kg1
15:13](../../../bib/kjv/kg1015.htm#013)). Asa destroyed the idol and the
idolatrous (but not the Jehovistic) "high places." But see "high
places,"

(See Scofield) - ([Kg1 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/kg1003.htm#002)).
