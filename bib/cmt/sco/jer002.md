### [Jeremiah Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 2:1</span></span>

word of the Lord

The general character of the first message from Jehovah to Judah by
Jeremiah is threefold:

\(1\) He reminds Israel of the days of blessing and deliverance, for
example ([Jer 2:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#001)).

\(2\) He reproaches them with forsaking Him, for example ([Jer
2:13](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#013)).

\(3\) He accuses them of choosing other, and impotent, gods, for example
([Jer 2:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#010)); ([Jer
2:26-28](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#026)).

All these messages are to be thought of as inspired sermons, spoken to
the people and subsequently written.

Compare ([Jer 36:1-32](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#001)).
