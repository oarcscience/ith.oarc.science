Beth-el

Compare ([Kg1 12:25-33](../../../bib/kjv/kg1012.htm#025).md).

Jerusalem was of necessity divisive and schismatic. ([Deu
12:4-14](../../../bib/kjv/deu012.htm#004).md).

Compare ([Joh 4:21-24](../../../bib/kjv/joh004.htm#021).md); ([Mat
18:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#020).md); ([Heb
13:10-14](../../../bib/kjv/heb013.htm#010).md).
