[Genesis 7:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen007.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">gen 7:9</span></span>

two of every sort

Compare ([Gen 7:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen007.htm#002)).

In addition to two animals, etc, commanded ([Gen
6:19](../../../bib/kjv/gen006.htm#019)) to be preserved for future
increase ("they shall be male and female"), the further command was
given more than 100 years later to take of clean beasts, that is, beasts
acceptable for sacrifice, seven each. Exodus gives ten such beasts, or
but seventy in all. Modern ships carry hundreds of live beasts, with
their food, besides scores of human beings.
