### [Matthew Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#001)

[Matthew 15:5](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">mat 15:5</span></span>

gift

That is, dedicated to God. ([Mat
5:23-24](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#023)); ([Mar
7:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011)).

(See Scofield) - ([Mar 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011)).

[Matthew 15:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">mat 15:21</span></span>

departed

The Syrophenician woman.

For the first time, the rejected Son of David ministers to a Gentile. It
is a precursive fulfilment of ([Mat
12:18](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#018)). Addressed by a Gentile as Son
of David, He makes no reply, for a Gentile has no claim upon Him in that
character.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#002)); ([Eph
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#012)).

Addressing him as "Lord," she obtained an immediate answer. ([Rom
10:12-13](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#012)).
