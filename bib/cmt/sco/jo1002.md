### [1 John Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#001)

[1 John 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jo1 2:1</span></span>

advocate

Advocacy is that work of Jesus Christ for sinning saints which He
carries on with the Father whereby, because of the eternal efficacy of
His own sacrifice, He restores them to fellowship.

Compare ([Psa 23:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#003)).

(See Scofield) - ([Joh 13:10](../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#010)).

sin

Sin

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023)).

[1 John 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jo1 2:2</span></span>

propitiation

(Greek, "hilasmos", "that which propitiates").

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025)).

the sins

Omit words "the sins".

world

Greek, "kosmos", means "mankind".

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 4:8](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#008)).

[1 John 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">jo1 2:3</span></span>

commandments

John uses "commandments"

\(1\) in the general sense of the divine will, however revealed, "his
word"; ([Jo1 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#005)); and

\(2\) especially of the law of Christ. ([Gal
6:2](../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#002)); ([Jo2
1:5](../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#005)).

See also ([Joh 15:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#010)).

[1 John 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">jo1 2:5</span></span>

perfect

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 5:48](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048)).

[1 John 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">jo1 2:12</span></span>

sins

Sin

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023)).

[1 John 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">jo1 2:13</span></span>

little children

The little ones of the family.

(See Scofield) - ([Jo1 2:28](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#028)).

[1 John 2:15](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">jo1 2:15</span></span>

world

Greek, "kosmos", means "world-system". ([Jo1
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#013)); ([Joh
7:7](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 13:8](../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008)).

[1 John 2:19](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">jo1 2:19</span></span>

went out from us

"Went out from us," that is, doctrinally. Doubtless then, as now, the
deniers of the Son ([Jo1 2:22](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#022)); ([Jo1
2:23](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#023)) still called themselves
Christians.

Compare ([Ti2 1:15](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#015)).

[1 John 2:28](../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">jo1 2:28</span></span>

little children

The general term for all children.
