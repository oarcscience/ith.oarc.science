[1 Timothy 3:15](../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">ti1 3:15</span></span>

church

Church (visible), Summary:

The passages under this head ([Co1
10:32](../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#032)); ([Ti1
3:15](../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#015)) refer to that visible body of
professed believers called, collectively, "the Church," of which history
takes account as such, though it exists under many names and divisions
based upon differences in doctrine or in government.

Within, for the most part, this historical "Church" has existed the true
Church, "which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all
([Eph 1:22](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#022)); ([Eph
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#023));

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 12:23](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#023))

like the believing Remnant within Israel.

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 11:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005)).

The predicted future of the visible Church is apostasy ([Luk
18:8](../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008)); ([Ti2
3:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001)) of the true Church, glory;
([Mat 13:36-43](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#036)); ([Rom
8:18-23](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#018)); ([Th1
4:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014)).
