[Zephaniah 3:9](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zep 3:9</span></span>

then will I turn

In Zephaniah the conversion of "the peoples" is stated out of the usual
prophetic order, in which the blessing of Israel and the setting up of
the kingdom precedes the conversion of the Gentiles.

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#001).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008).md).

But the passage gives clear testimony as to when the conversion of the
nations will occur. It is after the smiting of the nations.

Compare ([Isa 11:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#009).md) with context;
([Dan 2:34](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034).md); ([Dan
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035).md); ([Psa
2:5-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005).md); ([Act
15:15-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#015).md); ([Rev
19:19-20](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#019).md); ([Rev
19:6](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#006).md).

[Zephaniah 3:15](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">zep 3:15</span></span>

the midst of thee

That this, and all like passages in the Prophets (see "Kingdom (Old
Testament)," ([Gen 1:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#026).md); ([Zac
12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008).md) cannot refer to anything which
occurred at the first coming of Christ is clear from the context. The
precise reverse was true.

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001).md).
