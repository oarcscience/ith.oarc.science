<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">rev 16:19</span></span>

Babylon

Summary:

The Times of the Gentiles is that long period beginning with the
Babylonian captivity of Judah, under Nebuchadnezzar, and to be brought
to an end by the destruction of Gentile world-power by the "stone cut
out without hands", ([Dan 2:34](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034).md); ([Dan
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035).md); ([Dan
2:44](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#044).md), that is, the coming of the Lord
in glory ([Rev 19:11](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md); ([Rev
19:21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#021).md) until which time Jerusalem is
politically subject to Gentile rule. ([Luk
21:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024).md).
