[Luke 18:8](../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">luk 18:8</span></span>

faith

The reference is not to personal faith, but to belief in the whole body
of revealed truth.

Compare ([Rom 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#005)); ([Co1
16:13](../../../bib/kjv/co1016.htm#013)); ([Co2
13:5](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#005)); ([Col
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#023)); ([Col
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#007)); ([Tit
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/tit001.htm#013)); ([Jde
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#003)).

See "Apostasy," above, in margin reference of ([Luk
18:8](../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008)).

[Luke 18:13](../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">luk 18:13</span></span>

merciful

Greek, "hilaskomai", used in the Septuagint and New Testament in
connection with the mercy-seat ([Exo
25:17](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017)); ([Exo
25:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#018)); ([Exo
25:21](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#021)); ([Heb
9:5](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#005)). As an instructed Jew, the
publican is thinking, not of mere mercy, but of the blood-sprinkled
mercy seat.

(See Scofield) - ([Lev 16:5](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#005)).

"Propitiation,"

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025)).

His prayer might be paraphrased, "Be toward me as thou are when thou
lookest upon the atoning blood." The Bible knows nothing of divine
forgiveness apart from sacrifice.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 26:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#028)).

merciful

That is, propitiated.

sinner

Sin

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023)).
