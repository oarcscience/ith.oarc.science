more than ten

Acts 25:13

act 25:13

Agrippa

This (Act 5:13) was Herod Agrippa II, son of the Herod Agrippa I of (Act 12:1); and great-grandson of Herod the Great.

(See Scofield) - (Mat 2:1).

Bernice, or Berenice, was the sister of Herod Agrippa II. (Act 5:13).
