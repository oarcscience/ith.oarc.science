### [Joshua Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#001.md)

[Joshua 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">jos 7:11</span></span>

Israel

The sin of Achan and its results teach the great truth of the oneness of
the people of God, ([Jos 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#011).md).
"Israel hath sinned." See in illustration; ([Co1
5:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#001).md); ([Co1
12:12-14](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#012).md); ([Co1
12:26](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#026).md). The whole cause of Christ is
injured by the sin, neglect, or unspirituality of one believer.
