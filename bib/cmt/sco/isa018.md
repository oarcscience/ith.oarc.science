### [Isaiah Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/isa018.htm#001)

[Isaiah 18:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa018.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">isa 18:2</span></span>

That sendeth ambassadors

The local reference is evidently to an embassy from Egypt, resulting in
the alliance denounced in Isaiah 30, 31, ([Jer
37:7-11](../../../bib/kjv/jer037.htm#007)).
