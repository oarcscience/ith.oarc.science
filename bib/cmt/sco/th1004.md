### [1 Thessalonians Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#001.md)

[1 Thessalonians 4:17](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">th1 4:17</span></span>

caught up

Not church saints only, but all bodies of the saved, of whatever
dispensation, are included in the first resurrection

(See Scofield) - ([Co1 15:52](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#052).md)

as here described, but it is peculiarly the "blessed hope" of the
Church.

Compare ([Mat 24:42](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#042).md); ([Mat
25:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#013).md); ([Luk
12:36-48](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#036).md); ([Act
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#011).md); ([Phi
3:20](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#020).md); ([Phi
3:21](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#021).md); ([Tit
2:11-13](../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#011).md).
