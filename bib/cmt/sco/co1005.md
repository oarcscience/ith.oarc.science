### [1 Corinthians Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#001)

[1 Corinthians 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">co1 5:2</span></span>

And ye are puffed up

What contempt this pours upon the divisions among the Corinthians:
"Apollonians," and "Paulinians," and "Cephasites," all alike indifferent
to this instance of gross sin!
