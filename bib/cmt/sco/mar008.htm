Mark 8:23

mar 8:23

And he took

Our Lord's action here is most significant, Having abandoned Bethsaida to judgment (Mat 11:12-24). He would neither heal in that village, nor permit further testimony to be borne there (Mar 8:26). The probation of Bethsaida as a community was ended, but He would still show mercy to individuals.

Compare (Rev 3:20).

Christ is outside the door of that church, but "If any man hear My voice," etc.
