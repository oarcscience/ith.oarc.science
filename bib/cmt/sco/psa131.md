### [Psalms Chapter 131](../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 131:1</span></span>

A Song of degrees

Literally, "of ascents." Perhaps chanted by the people as they went up
to Jerusalem to the feasts. See, for example ([Psa
112:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001).md); ([Psa
112:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002).md).
