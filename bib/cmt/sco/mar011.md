### [Mark Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#001.md)

[Mark 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mar 11:1</span></span>

Bethphage

Meaning, "house of unripe figs", (see ([Mar
11:12](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#012).md); ([Mar
11:20](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#020).md), probably so called after the
fig tree was cursed.

[Mark 11:10](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">mar 11:10</span></span>

kingdom

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002).md).

[Mark 11:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">mar 11:13</span></span>

leaves

Fig trees which have retained their leaves through the winter usually
have figs also. It was still too early for new leaves or fruit.

[Mark 11:19](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">mar 11:19</span></span>

when even

whenever, that is, every day when evening came.

[Mark 11:22](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">mar 11:22</span></span>

Have faith

Have the faith of God; that is, the faith which God gives.

Compare ([Co1 12:9](../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#009).md); ([Eph
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#008).md).

[Mark 11:23](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">mar 11:23</span></span>

whosoever

See ([Mat 17:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#020).md); ([Luk
11:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Luk 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001).md). ([Luk
17:6](../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#006).md); ([Joh
14:13](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#013).md); ([Joh
14:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#014).md).

[Mark 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">mar 11:26</span></span>

if ye

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 6:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#012).md).

Verse 26 ([Mar 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#026).md) is omitted from
the best manuscripts.
