[Proverbs 8:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">pro 8:22</span></span>

The Lord possessed me

That wisdom is more than the personification of an attribute of God, or
of the will of God as best for man, but is a distinct adumbration of
Christ, is sure to the devout mind. ([Pro
8:22-36](../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#022)); ([Joh
1:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#001)); ([Col
1:17](../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#017)) can refer to nothing less than
the Eternal Son of God.
