### [Malachi Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#001.md)

[Malachi 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">mal 1:4</span></span>

Edom

That is, Esau's descendants.

See ([Gen 25:30](../../../bib/kjv/gen025.htm#030).md).

[Malachi 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">mal 1:6</span></span>

if then I be a father

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 63:16](../../../bib/kjv/isa063.htm#016).md).

The relationship here is national, not personal ([Jer
3:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer003.htm#018).md); ([Jer
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer003.htm#019).md) here, apparently, the Jews were
calling Jehovah, "Father," but yielding Him no filial obedience.

See; ([Joh 8:37-39](../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#037).md); ([Rom
9:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#001).md).

[Malachi 1:10](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">mal 1:10</span></span>

Who is there

Or, I would that one among you would shut the doors of the temple that
no more vain fire should kindle on mine altar.

Compare ([Isa 1:11-15](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#011).md).

[Malachi 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">mal 1:11</span></span>

For from the raising

That is, so it would have been had Israel been true. ([Isa
45:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#005).md); ([Isa
45:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#006).md). So it shall be despite Israel's
failure.
