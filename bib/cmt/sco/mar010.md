### [Mark Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#001.md)

[Mark 10:13](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">mar 10:13</span></span>

young

little.

Compare ([Mat 5:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#016).md).

[Mark 10:16](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">mar 10:16</span></span>

blessed them

In Hebrew custom, a father's act.

Compare ([Gen 27:38](../../../bib/kjv/gen027.htm#038).md).

"He had no children that He might adopt all children." -- Bengel.

[Mark 10:18](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">mar 10:18</span></span>

Why callest

Believing Me to be but a human teacher, why callest thou Me "good," etc.

[Mark 10:24](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">mar 10:24</span></span>

astonished

Or, amazed, that is, as Jews: knowing that temporal prosperity was, to
the Jew as such, a token of divine favour. For example ([Deu
28:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#001).md).

[Mark 10:25](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">mar 10:25</span></span>

eye of

It has been thought the reference here was to a postern door set in a
gate of Jerusalem.

[Mark 10:28](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">mar 10:28</span></span>

Then Peter

Compare note 2, ([Mat 19:27-30](../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#027).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 19:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#028).md).

[Mark 10:33](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#033.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">mar 10:33</span></span>

Son of man

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 8:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#020).md).

[Mark 10:45](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#045.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_045">mar 10:45</span></span>

life

Or, soul.

Compare ([Isa 53:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa053.htm#010).md); ([Isa
53:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa053.htm#012).md).

(Greek, "psuchē", "the soul" or "the essential life", not as commonly,
Greek, "zoe," "the active life").

[Mark 10:46](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#046.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_046">mar 10:46</span></span>

And they

[Mark 10:51](../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#051.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_051">mar 10:51</span></span>

Lord

Greek, "Rabboni," "my Master", a term of reverent love.

Compare ([Joh 20:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#016).md).
