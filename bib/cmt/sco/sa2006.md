### [2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#001)

[2 Kings (2 Samuel) 6:3](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">sa2 6:3</span></span>

new cart

The story of David's new cart and its results is a striking illustration
of the spiritual truth that blessing does not follow even the best
intentions in the service of God except as that service is rendered in
God's way. It is a constant point of failure. God had given explicit
directions how the ark should be borne ([Num
4:1-15](../../../bib/kjv/num004.htm#001)) but David adopted a Philistine
expedient.

([Sa1 6:7](../../../bib/kjv/sa1006.htm#007)); ([Sa1
6:8](../../../bib/kjv/sa1006.htm#008)). The church is full of Philistine
ways of doing service to Christ.

Compare ([Co1 1:17-31](../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#017)); ([Co2
10:4-5](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#004)).

See, also, ([Ch1 15:2](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#002)).
