### [Numbers Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#001.md)

[Numbers 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#001.md)

first month

That is, April.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">num 20:5</span></span>

water

(See Scofield) - ([Num 20:8](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#008).md).

[Numbers 20:8](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">num 20:8</span></span>

speak ye unto the rock before their eyes

See ([Exo 17:5](../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#005).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Exo 17:5](../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#005).md).

The rock (Christ) ([Co1 10:4](../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#004).md) once
smitten, needs not to be smitten (crucified) again. Moses' act exalted
himself ([Num 20:10](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#010).md) and implied (in
type) that the one sacrifice was ineffectual, thus denying the eternal
efficacy of the blood; ([Heb 9:25](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#025).md);
([Heb 9:26](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#026).md); ([Heb
10:3](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#003).md); ([Heb
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#011).md); ([Heb
10:12](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#012).md). The abundant water (grace
reaching the need of the people, despite the error of their leader)
tells of refreshing and power through the Spirit.

[Numbers 20:28](../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">num 20:28</span></span>

died

The death of Aaron marks the end of the wanderings. Henceforth, Israel
marches or halts, but does not wander.
