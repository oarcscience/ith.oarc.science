### [Joel Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#001.md)

[Joel 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">joe 1:4</span></span>

palmerworm

The palmerworm, locust, etc, are thought to be different forms, at
different stages of development, of one insect. The essential fact is
that, according to the usual method of the Spirit in prophecy, some
local circumstance is shown to be of spiritual significance, and is made
the occasion of a far-reaching prophecy (for example) ([Isa
7:1-14](../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#001).md) where the Syrian invasion and
the unbelief of Ahaz give occasion to the great prophecy of verse 14
([Joe 1:14](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#014).md). Here in Joel a plague of
devouring insects is shown to have spiritual significance ([Joe
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#013).md); ([Joe
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#014).md) and is made the occasion of the
prophecy of the day of the Lord, not yet fulfilled.

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#012).md).

This is more developed in Joel 2, where the literal locusts are left
behind, and the future day of Jehovah fills the scene.

The whole picture is of the end-time of this present age, of the "times
of the Gentiles" ([Luk 21:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024).md); ([Rev
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014).md) of the battle of Armageddon;
([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014).md); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md) of the regathering of
Israel.

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026).md)

and of kingdom blessing. It is remarkable that Joel, coming at the very
beginning of written prophecy (B.C. 836), gives the fullest view of the
consummation of all written prophecy.

The order of events is:

\(1\) The invasion of Palestine from the north by Gentile world-powers
headed up under the Beast and false prophet ([Joe
2:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#001).md) "Armageddon,"

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014).md).

\(2\) the Lord's army and destruction of the invaders ([Joe
2:11](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#011).md); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md).

\(3\) the repentance of Judah in the land ([Joe
2:12-17](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#012).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Deu 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003).md).

\(4\) the answer of Jehovah ([Joe
2:18-27](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#018).md).

\(5\) the effusion of the Spirit in the (Jewish) "last days" ([Joe
2:28](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028).md); ([Joe
2:29](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#029).md).

\(6\) the return of the Lord in glory and the setting up of the kingdom
([Joe 2:30-32](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#030).md); ([Act
15:15-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#015).md) by the regathering of the
nation and judgment of the nations ([Joe
3:1-16](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#001).md).

\(7\) full and permanent kingdom blessing ([Joe
3:17-21](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#017).md); ([Zac
14:1-21](../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#001).md).

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 25:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#032).md).
