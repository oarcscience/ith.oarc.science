### [Zechariah Chapter 13](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#001.md)

And it shall come to pass

Zechariah 13 now returns to the subject of ([Zac
12:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#010).md). ([Zac
13:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#008).md); ([Zac
13:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009).md) refer to the sufferings of the
remnant; ([Isa 1:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#009).md); ([Rom
11:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005).md) preceding the great battle.
Zechariah 14 is a recapitulation of the whole matter. The order is:

\(1\) The gathering of the nation, ([Zac
13:2](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#002).md) (see "Armageddon," ([Rev
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)) (See Scofield.md) - ([Rev
19:11](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011).md).

\(2\) the deliverance, ([Zac 13:3](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#003).md);

\(3\) the return of Christ to the Mount of Olives, and the physical
change of the scene, ([Zac 13:4-8](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#004).md);

\(4\) the setting up of the kingdom, and full earthly blessing, ([Zac
13:9-21](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009).md).
