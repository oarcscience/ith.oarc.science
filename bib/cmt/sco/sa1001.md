### [1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001.md)

[1 Kings (1 Samuel) 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">sa1 1:3</span></span>

Lord of hosts

Jehovah (Lord) of Hosts, Hebrew, Jehovah Sabaoth. For the distinctive
meanings of Jehovah,

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#004).md).

Sabaoth means simply host or hosts, but with especial reference to
warfare or service. In use the two ideas are united; Jehovah is LORD of
(warrior) hosts. It is the name, therefore, of Jehovah in manifestation
of power. "The Lord of Hosts, He is the King of glory" ([Psa
24:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#010).md) and accordingly in the Old
Testament Scripture this name is revealed in the time of Israel's need.
It is never found in the Pentateuch, nor directly in Joshua or Judges,
and occurs but rarely in the Psalms; but Jeremiah, the prophet of
approaching national judgment, uses the name about eighty times. Haggai
in two chapters uses the name fourteen times, Zechariah in fourteen
chapters calls upon the Lord of hosts about fifty times. In Malachi the
name occurs about twenty five times. In the utmost extremity, the
Psalmist twice comforts his heart with the assurance "the Lord of hosts
is with us." ([Psa 46:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#007).md); ([Psa
46:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#011).md).

The meanings and uses of this name may thus be summarized:

\(1\) The "hosts" are heavenly. Primarily the angels are meant, but the
name gathers into itself the idea of all divine or heavenly power as
available for the need of God's people ([Gen
32:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen032.htm#001).md); ([Gen
32:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen032.htm#002).md); ([Isa
6:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/isa006.htm#001).md); ([Kg1
22:19](../../../bib/kjv/kg1022.htm#019).md); ([Luk
2:13-15](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#013).md).

\(2\) In use this is the distinctive name of Deity for Israel's help and
comfort in the time of her division and failure ([Kg1
18:15](../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#015).md); ([Kg1
19:14](../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#014).md); ([Isa
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#009).md); ([Isa
8:11-14](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#011).md); ([Isa
9:13-19](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#013).md); ([Isa
10:24-27](../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#024).md); ([Isa
31:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#004).md); ([Isa
31:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#005).md); ([Hag
2:4](../../../bib/kjv/hag002.htm#004).md); ([Mal
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/mal003.htm#016).md); ([Mal
3:17](../../../bib/kjv/mal003.htm#017).md); ([Jam
5:4](../../../bib/kjv/jam005.htm#004).md).
