### [Isaiah Chapter 45](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#001.md)

[Isaiah 45:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">isa 45:1</span></span>

anointed

The only instance where the word is applied to a Gentile. Nebuchadnezzar
is called the "servant" of Jehovah ([Jer
25:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#009).md); ([Jer
27:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer027.htm#006).md); ([Jer
43:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#010).md) This, with the designation "My
shepherd" ([Isa 44:28](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#028).md) also a
Messianic title, marks Cyrus as that startling exception, a Gentile type
of Christ. The points are:

\(1\) both are irresistible conquerors of Israel's enemies. ([Isa
45:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#001).md); ([Rev
19:19-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#019).md).

\(2\) both are restorers of the holy city ([Isa
44:28](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#028).md); ([Zac
14:1-11](../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#001).md).

\(3\) through both is the name of the one true God glorified ([Isa
45:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#006).md); ([Co1
15:28](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#028).md).

[Isaiah 45:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">isa 45:7</span></span>

create evil

Hebrew, "ra" translated "sorrow," "wretchedness," "adversity,"
"afflictions," "calamities," but never translated sin. God created evil
only in the sense that He made sorrow, wretchedness, etc, to be the sure
fruits of sin.
