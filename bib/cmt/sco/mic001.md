### [Micah Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001)

[Micah 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mic 1:1</span></span>

Therefore

In ([Mic 1:6-16](../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#006)), the Assyrian
invasion is described.

Compare ([Kg2 17:1-18](../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#001)).

This is the local circumstance which gives rise to the prophecy of the
greater invasion in the last days. ([Mic
4:9-13](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#009)) and of the Lord's deliverance
at Armageddon. ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)); ([Rev
19:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017)).
