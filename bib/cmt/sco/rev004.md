### [Revelation Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/rev004.htm#001.md)

[Revelation 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/rev004.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">rev 4:1</span></span>

Come up hither

This call seems clearly to indicate the fulfilment of ([Th1
4:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014).md). The word "church" does not
again occur in the Revelation till all is fulfilled.
