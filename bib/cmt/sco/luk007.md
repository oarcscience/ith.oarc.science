### [Luke Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#001)

[Luke 7:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">luk 7:24</span></span>

were departed

Having gently removed His servant's doubt, the Lord bears witness to him
before others: He knows when to reprove, and where, and when, to praise.

[Luke 7:34](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#034)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">luk 7:34</span></span>

sinners

Sin

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023)).

[Luke 7:37](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#037)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">luk 7:37</span></span>

sinner

That is, in the sense of unchaste.

[Luke 7:44](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#044)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_044">luk 7:44</span></span>

Simon

See ([Jam 2:14-26](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#014)).

When Jesus would justify the woman in the eyes of Simon, He points to
her works, for only through her works could Simon see the proof of her
faith; but when He would send the woman away in peace, He points to her
faith, not her works.

See ([Tit 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#014)); ([Tit
3:4-8](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#004)).

His own works can never be to the believer his own ground of assurance,
which must rest upon the work of Christ.

Compare ([Mat 7:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#022)); ([Mat
7:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#023)).

See "Assurance"; ([Isa 32:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#017)); ([Jde
1:1](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#001)).
