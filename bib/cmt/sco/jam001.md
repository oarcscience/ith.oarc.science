### [James Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#001)

[James 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#004)

Mature and complete.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 5:48](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048)).

[James 1:14](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">jam 1:14</span></span>

tempted

"Temptation" is used in two senses:

\(1\) Solicitation to evil, (for example, ([Gen
3:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#001)); ([Mat
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#001)); ([Co1
10:13](../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#013)); ([Co2
11:3](../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#003)); ([Co2
11:4](../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#004)); ([Jam
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#014)).

\(2\) Testing under trial, (for example, ([Gen
22:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001)); ([Luk
22:28](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#028)).

Compare ([Luk 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#002)).

Compare ([Mat 6:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#013)); (solicitation to
evil) and ([Pe1 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#006)); (testing under
trial).

[James 1:17](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">jam 1:17</span></span>

gift

Two words are used in the original for "gift":

the first meaning the act of giving;

the second meaning the thing given.
