Book Introduction - Micah

Micah, a contemporary of Isaiah, prophesied during the reigns of Jotham,
Ahaz, and Hezekiah over Judah, and of Pekahiah, Pekah, and Hoshea over
Israel [Kg2 15:23-30](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#023); [Kg2
17:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#001). He was a prophet in Judah [Jer
26:17-19](../../../bib/kjv/jer026.htm#017) but the book called by his
name chiefly concerns Samaria.

Micah falls into three prophetic strains, each beginning, "Hear":

1\. (Micah 1:1 - 2:13)

2\. (Micah 3:1 - 5:15)

3\. (Micah 6:1 - 7:20)

The events recorded Micah cover a period of 40 years (Ussher).
