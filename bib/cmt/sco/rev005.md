hidden from Daniel, that the kings and priests of the church-age are to
be associated with the "Son of Man," the "Lamb as it had been slain," in
His reign "on the earth". ([Rev 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/rev002.htm#009).md);
([Rev 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/rev002.htm#010).md).
