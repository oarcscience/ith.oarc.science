### [2 Chronicles Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#001)

[2 Chronicles 4:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">ch2 4:7</span></span>

Their form - The old form which God prescribed to Moses.

[2 Chronicles 4:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">ch2 4:8</span></span>

Ten tables - Whereon the shew - bread was set, [Ch2
4:19](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#019). Perhaps each of these had twelve
loaves on it. As the house was enlarged, so was the provision.

[2 Chronicles 4:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch2 4:16</span></span>

His father - He is so called because Solomon usually called him by that
name out of that great respect which he bare to him for his excellent
art and service which he did for him: it being usual to call great
artists and inventors of things by this name.

[2 Chronicles 4:20](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">ch2 4:20</span></span>

The manner - According to the prescription of God to Moses.

[2 Chronicles 4:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">ch2 4:22</span></span>

Of gold - In part; they were made of wood, but covered with golden
plates.
