### [Psalms Chapter 114](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 114:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 114:2</span></span>

Judah - Or Israel, one tribe being put for all. Judah he mentions as the
chief of all the tribes.

[Psalms 114:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 114:4</span></span>

The mountains - Horeb and Sinai, two tops of one mountain, and other
neighbouring mountains.

[Psalms 114:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 114:7</span></span>

Tremble - The mountains did more than what was fit at the appearance of
the great God.
