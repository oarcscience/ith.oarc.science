*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

### [Psalms Chapter 27](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#001)

[Psalms 27:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 27:2</span></span>

Light - My counsellor in all my difficulties, and my comforter and
deliverer in all my distresses. Strength - The supporter and preserver
of my life.

[Psalms 27:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 27:3</span></span>

In this - That God is my light.

[Psalms 27:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 27:4</span></span>

Dwell. &c. - Have opportunity of constant attendance upon God. To behold
- That there I may delight myself, in the contemplation of thy amiable
and glorious majesty, and of thy infinite wisdom, holiness, justice,
truth, and mercy.

[Psalms 27:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 27:5</span></span>

The secret - In his tabernacle, into which mine enemies cannot come. He
alludes to the ancient custom of offenders, who used to flee to the
tabernacle or altar. Rock - A place high and inaccessible.

[Psalms 27:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 27:9</span></span>

Away - From thy face or presence, or from the place of thy worship.

[Psalms 27:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 27:11</span></span>

Because of - That I may neither fall into their hands by my folly, nor
give them any occasion of triumphing over me.

[Psalms 27:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 27:13</span></span>

The living - David was thus earnestly desirous of this mercy in this
life, not because he placed his portion in these things; but because the
truth and glory of God, were highly concerned in making good the promise
of the kingdom to him.
