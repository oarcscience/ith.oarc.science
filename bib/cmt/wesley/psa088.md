### [Psalms Chapter 88](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#001)

[Psalms 88:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 88:4</span></span>

Counted - l am given up by my friends for a lost man.

[Psalms 88:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 88:5</span></span>

Free - Well nigh discharged from the warfare of the present life, and
entered as a member into the society of the dead. Whom - Thou seemest to
neglect and bury in oblivion.

[Psalms 88:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 88:7</span></span>

Waves - With they judgments, breaking in furiously upon me like the
waves of the sea.

[Psalms 88:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 88:10</span></span>

Wonders - In raising them to life. To praise thee - In this world?

[Psalms 88:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 88:12</span></span>

Forgetfulness - In the grave, where men are forgotten by their nearest
relations.

[Psalms 88:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 88:13</span></span>

Prevent - Come to thee before the dawning of the day, or the rising of
the sun.

[Psalms 88:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">psa 88:17</span></span>

Water - As the waters of the sea encompass him who is in the midst of
it.
