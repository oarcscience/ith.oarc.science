*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001)

[Psalms 12:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 12:4</span></span>

Prevail - By raising and spreading evil reports concerning him. Our own
- At our own disposal to speak what we please, who can control or
restrain us?

[Psalms 12:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 12:5</span></span>

Puffeth - From him that despises him, and hopes to destroy him with a
puff of breath.

[Psalms 12:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 12:6</span></span>

Pure - Without the least mixture of falsehood; and therefore shall
infallibly be fulfilled.

[Psalms 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 12:7</span></span>

Thou shalt keep them - Thy words or promises: these thou wilt observe
and keep, both now, and from this generation for ever.

[Psalms 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 12:8</span></span>

Walk - They fill all places, and go about boldly and securely.
