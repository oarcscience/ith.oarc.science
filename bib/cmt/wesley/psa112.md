*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 112](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001)

[Psalms 112:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 112:2</span></span>

Generation - The posterity.

[Psalms 112:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 112:3</span></span>

Righteousness - The fruit or reward of his righteousness, which is God's
blessing upon his estate.

[Psalms 112:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 112:4</span></span>

Darkness - In the troubles and calamities of life. He - The upright man.

[Psalms 112:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 112:5</span></span>

Lendeth - Gives freely to some, and lends to others according to the
variety of their conditions. Affairs - His domestick affairs. Discretion
- Not getting his estate unjustly, nor casting it away prodigally, nor
yet withholding it from such as need it.

[Psalms 112:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 112:6</span></span>

Moved - Though he may for a season be afflicted, yet he shall not be
eternally destroyed.

[Psalms 112:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 112:7</span></span>

Evil tidings - At the report of approaching calamities.

[Psalms 112:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 112:9</span></span>

Dispersed - His goods, freely and liberally. Righteousness - His
liberality, or the reward of it. Ever - What he gives is not lost, but
indeed is the only part of his estate, which will abide with him to all
eternity.

[Psalms 112:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 112:10</span></span>

The desire - Either of the misery of good men; or of his own constant
prosperity.
