*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

### [Lamentations Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#001)

[Lamentations 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">lam 5:3</span></span>

We - We are all of us without a king, (our common father) we are
deprived of thy fatherly protection, and many young children among us
are left without an earthly parent.

[Lamentations 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">lam 5:4</span></span>

Sold - Whereas at other times there was abundance of wood and water
throughout Judea.

[Lamentations 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">lam 5:6</span></span>

We - The ten tribes were all carried captives into Assyria, and many of
the kingdom of Judah fled into Egypt. Giving the hand may signify
labouring for them: or, yielding up themselves to their power.

[Lamentations 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">lam 5:7</span></span>

Their iniquities - The punishment of them.

[Lamentations 5:9](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">lam 5:9</span></span>

The sword - The enemies lay encamped in all the plains, so that they
could stir out no way but the sword of the Chaldeans was upon them.

[Lamentations 5:13](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">lam 5:13</span></span>

Fell - Not being able to stand under the burdens laid upon them.

[Lamentations 5:16](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">lam 5:16</span></span>

The crown - All our honour, splendor and dignity.

[Lamentations 5:19](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">lam 5:19</span></span>

Thy throne - Altho' for our sins thou sufferest our throne to be cast
down, yet thou art the same, thy power is not diminished, nor thy
goodness abated.

[Lamentations 5:21](../../../bib/kjv/lam005.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">lam 5:21</span></span>

Renew - Restore us to our former estate.
