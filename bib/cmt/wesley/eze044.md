### [Ezekiel Chapter 44](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 44:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">eze 44:2</span></span>

Shall not be opened - Shall not ordinarily stand open. No man - None of
the common people. The Lord - That glory which was the visible sign of
his presence.

[Ezekiel 44:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 44:3</span></span>

He - The king might sit before the Lord, others might not. Bread - That
part of the sacrifice, which was allowed to the offerer.

[Ezekiel 44:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 44:4</span></span>

He - Christ in the appearance of a man.

[Ezekiel 44:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 44:5</span></span>

The entering - The persons who may, and who may not enter. The sanctuary
- Taken here for the courts, rather than the house itself.

[Ezekiel 44:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">eze 44:6</span></span>

Let it suffice - Let the time you have spent on your sins suffice.

[Ezekiel 44:7](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">eze 44:7</span></span>

Bread - Either the meal - offering or first - fruits of corn and dough,
and the shew - bread. They - The whole nation of the Jews.

[Ezekiel 44:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">eze 44:8</span></span>

Have not kept - You have not observed the laws I gave you for the
keeping of my holy things, house, sacrifices, and worship. Have set -
You have substituted others in your rooms.

[Ezekiel 44:10](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">eze 44:10</span></span>

Are gone away - By their idolatry.

[Ezekiel 44:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">eze 44:11</span></span>

Ministers - Servants employed in the lowest work. Sanctuary - Not the
temple itself, but about the courts of it. Having charge - They shall be
porters to open and shut, and sweep, and go on errands. To minister - To
wait on the priests.

[Ezekiel 44:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">eze 44:12</span></span>

Iniquity - The punishment of it.

[Ezekiel 44:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">eze 44:13</span></span>

Shall bear their shame - They shall be dealt with according to their
abominations, and bear the punishment thereof.

[Ezekiel 44:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">eze 44:15</span></span>

That kept the charge - Were constant, zealous, and faithful in their
priestly office.

[Ezekiel 44:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">eze 44:16</span></span>

Into my sanctuary - Both to the altar, to the temple, and the high -
priest into the holy of holies. Come near - To set the shew - bread on,
and to take it off. To minister - To offer sacrifice at the altar, and
incense in the house. God will put marks of honour upon those who are
faithful to him in trying times, and will, employ those in his service,
who have kept close to it, when others drew back.

[Ezekiel 44:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">eze 44:17</span></span>

And within - In the temple.

[Ezekiel 44:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">eze 44:19</span></span>

Shall not sanctify - By the law, common things, touching holy things,
became consecrated, and no more fit for common use.

[Ezekiel 44:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">eze 44:20</span></span>

To grow long - Priding themselves in it, as Absalom. Shall only poll -
When the hair is grown, they shall cut the ends of their hair, and keep
it in moderate size.

[Ezekiel 44:21](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">eze 44:21</span></span>

Drink wine - Or any other strong liquor, when they go either to trim the
lamps or set the shew - bread in order, or to offer incense in the
temple, or when they go to the altar to offer a sacrifice, which stood
in the inner court.

[Ezekiel 44:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">eze 44:24</span></span>

Shall judge - Shall determine the controversy. Assemblies - Publick
congregations.

[Ezekiel 44:26](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">eze 44:26</span></span>

Cleansed - After for seven days he hath kept from the dead. They - The
priests, who are about the house of God, shall appoint seven days more
to this defiled person for his cleansing before he is admitted into the
sanctuary.

[Ezekiel 44:28](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">eze 44:28</span></span>

It - The sin - offering: but under this one, all other offerings are
couched. For an inheritance - Instead of lands and cities.

[Ezekiel 44:30](../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">eze 44:30</span></span>

And the first - So soon as the first - fruits are ripe in the field,
your vineyards, and olive yards. Every oblation - Whether free - will
offering, or prescribed. The first of your dough - 'Tis conceived this
was of every mass of dough they made, and of the first of the dough,
which every year they first made of the new corn, as by the custom of
the Jews at this day appears. That he - The priest may bless, and pray
for thee.
