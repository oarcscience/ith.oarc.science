### [Zephaniah Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#001.md)

[Zephaniah 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zep 2:1</span></span>

Gather yourselves - Call a solemn assembly, proclaim a fast. Not desired
- Or, not desirous. Unwilling to return, and unworthy to be received on
your return.

[Zephaniah 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">zep 2:2</span></span>

The decree - Before God's decree is put in execution. The day - Before
the day of your calamities. As the chaff - Carry you away as the wind
carries chaff away.

[Zephaniah 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">zep 2:3</span></span>

Seek - Fear, worship, depend on him alone. Ye meek - Ye humble ones.
Wrought his judgment - Obeyed his precepts. Seek righteousness -
Continue therein. Seek meekness - Patiently wait on the just and
merciful God. Hid - Under the wing of Divine Providence.

[Zephaniah 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zep 2:4</span></span>

For - It is time to seek God; for your neighbours, as well as you, shall
be destroyed. Gaza - A chief city of the Philistines. They - The
Babylonians. Shall drive - Into captivity. At the noon day - It shall be
taken by force at noon.

[Zephaniah 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">zep 2:5</span></span>

The inhabitants - All the Philistines. Cherethites - Or destroyers, men
that were stout, fierce, and terrible to their neighbours. O Canaan -
That part that the Philistines kept by force from the Jews.

[Zephaniah 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">zep 2:6</span></span>

For shepherds - Instead of cities full of rich citizens, there shall be
only cottages for shepherds.

[Zephaniah 2:7](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">zep 2:7</span></span>

The coast - The sea - coast, the land of the Philistines. The remnant -
That survive the captivity. Shall feed - Their flocks. In the houses -
In places where these formerly stood. They - Both shepherds and flocks.
Shall visit - In mercy.

[Zephaniah 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">zep 2:8</span></span>

I - God. Magnified themselves - Invading their frontiers.

[Zephaniah 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zep 2:9</span></span>

Of nettles - Not cultivated, but over - run with nettles. Salt - pits -
A dry, barren earth, fit only to dig salt out of. The residue - That
return out of Babylon. Possess them - Settle upon those parts of their
lands, that are fit for habitation.

[Zephaniah 2:11](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zep 2:11</span></span>

Famish - Take away all their sacrifices and drink - offerings. The gods
- Idols of those lands. From his place - Not only at Jerusalem, but
every where.

[Zephaniah 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">zep 2:12</span></span>

By my sword - The Chaldeans are called God's sword; because God employed
them.

[Zephaniah 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">zep 2:13</span></span>

He - God. The north - Assyria, which lay northward of Judea, and due
north from Babylon.

[Zephaniah 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">zep 2:14</span></span>

All the beasts - All sorts of beasts which are found in those countries.
The bittern - A bird that delights in desolate places.

[Zephaniah 2:15](../../../bib/kjv/zep002.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">zep 2:15</span></span>

This - So the prophet triumphs over her. There is none - None like me,
or that can contend with me.
