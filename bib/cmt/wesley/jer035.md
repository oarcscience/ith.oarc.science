### [Jeremiah Chapter 35](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#001.md)

[Jeremiah 35:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 35:1</span></span>

The word - This is another evidence that the prophecies of this book are
not left us in that order wherein they were delivered; for those which
we had in the two or three foregoing chapters being in the time of
Zedekiah, must needs be ten or eleven years after this.

[Jeremiah 35:2](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jer 35:2</span></span>

Rechabites - These Rechabites had their name from Rechab their father,
descended from Hemath, a Kenite, who is also called Hobab, [Jdg
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/jdg004.htm#011.md), or Jethro the father in law of
Moses.

[Jeremiah 35:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">jer 35:4</span></span>

Igdaliah - A prophet. The princes - The chamber were the princes were
wont to meet.

[Jeremiah 35:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">jer 35:6</span></span>

Jonadab - Jonadab of whom we read, [Kg2
10:15](../../../bib/kjv/kg2010.htm#015.md), was the father, that is the
progenitor of the Rechabites, at three hundred years distance.

[Jeremiah 35:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">jer 35:7</span></span>

That ye may live - Jonadab cautions his sons by a thrifty, sober,
laborious life, to which they had been bred, in keeping flocks, to avoid
any thing which might expose them to the envy or hatred of the people
amongst whom they were come to sojourn.

[Jeremiah 35:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">jer 35:10</span></span>

Tents - Moveable habitations, which they could with little labour remove
to feed their flocks.

[Jeremiah 35:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">jer 35:19</span></span>

For ever - For ever here signifies the ever of the Jewish state, or
church.
