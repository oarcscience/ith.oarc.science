### [Ezekiel Chapter 30](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#001.md)

[Ezekiel 30:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">eze 30:2</span></span>

Ye - Inhabitants of Egypt.

[Ezekiel 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 30:3</span></span>

A cloudy day - So times of trouble are called. Of the heathen - The time
when God will reckon with the Heathens.

[Ezekiel 30:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 30:4</span></span>

Ethiopia - The neighbour and ally to Egypt. Take away - Into miserable
captivity. Her foundations - Their government, laws, and strong holds.

[Ezekiel 30:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 30:5</span></span>

Lydia - Not the Asiatic, but the Africans placed between some part of
Cyrene and Egypt. The mingled people - The hired soldiers from all
parts, a confused mixture of nations. And Chub - The inhabitants of the
inmost Libya; perhaps they may be the Nubians at this day. The men - All
the allies of Egypt. With them - With the Egyptians.

[Ezekiel 30:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">eze 30:6</span></span>

Upheld - Those that favour and help her. The pride - The glory of all
her strength.

[Ezekiel 30:7](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">eze 30:7</span></span>

They - All those before mentioned.

[Ezekiel 30:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">eze 30:8</span></span>

Destroyed - The fire that consumes nations is of God's kindling: and
when he sets fire to a kingdom, all they that go about to quench the
fire, shall be consumed by it.

[Ezekiel 30:9](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">eze 30:9</span></span>

Messengers - Such as having escaped the sword, shall tell the news. From
me - By my permission and providence. In ship's - Messengers by ships
might carry the news to both the Ethiopian, Asian, and African, by the
Red - sea. As in the day - During the mighty havock made by the
Chaldeans. It - A like storm.

[Ezekiel 30:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">eze 30:11</span></span>

His people - His own subjects, not hired soldiers.

[Ezekiel 30:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">eze 30:12</span></span>

The rivers dry - Probably the Chaldeans diverted them, and so their
fortified towns wanted one great defence. Sell - Give it up entirely.

[Ezekiel 30:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">eze 30:13</span></span>

Noph - Memphis, now Grand Cairo, the chief city of the country. A prince
- Either an Egyptian born, or independent, and over all Egypt. A fear -
Consternation and cowardice.

[Ezekiel 30:14](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">eze 30:14</span></span>

In Zoan - Zoan shall be burnt down to ashes. In No - A great and
populous city situate on one of the mouths of the Nile.

[Ezekiel 30:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">eze 30:15</span></span>

Sin - Pelusium, which was the key of Egypt, and therefore always well
fortified, and strongly garrisoned.

[Ezekiel 30:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">eze 30:16</span></span>

Shall be rent - Her walls, and towers, and fortresses broken through by
the violence of engines, and by the assaults of the soldiers.

[Ezekiel 30:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">eze 30:17</span></span>

Young men - 'Tis probable these might be a body of valiant youths,
collected out of these ten cities. Aven - Bethshemesh, or Heliopolis, an
idolatrous city, in which was a stately temple of the sun: an hundred
and fifty furlongs, that is six miles and three quarters in compass.
Phibeseth - Bubastus, sometimes called Hoephestus, not far from Aven.

[Ezekiel 30:18](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">eze 30:18</span></span>

Tehaphnehes - A great and goodly city of Egypt; Tachapanes, Tachpanes,
Tahapanes, Tahpanes, Chanes, and Hanes, are names given it, and this
from a queen of Egypt of that name in Solomon's time. It stood not far
from Sin, or Pelusium. Darkened - A night shall come upon it. Break - I
shall break the kingdom of Egypt, that it no more oppress with yokes,
that is, burdens. Her daughters - Her towns and villages.

[Ezekiel 30:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">eze 30:20</span></span>

The eleventh year - Of Jeconiah's captivity, three months and two days
before Jerusalem was taken, about the time that the Egyptians attempted
to raise the siege of Jerusalem.

[Ezekiel 30:21](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">eze 30:21</span></span>

Have broken - Partly by the victory of the Chaldeans over Pharaoh -
necho, partly by the victory of the Cyreneans over Pharaoh - hophra. The
sword - None can heal the wounds that God gives but himself. They whom
he disables, cannot again hold the sword.

[Ezekiel 30:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">eze 30:22</span></span>

His arms - Both his arms. The strong - That part of his kingdom which
remains entire. Broken - That which was shattered before.

[Ezekiel 30:25](../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">eze 30:25</span></span>

Will strengthen - As judges on the bench like Pilate, so generals in the
field, like Nebuchadrezzar, have no power but what is given them from
above.
