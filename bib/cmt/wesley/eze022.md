### [Ezekiel Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 22:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">eze 22:2</span></span>

Judge - The question is doubled, to awaken the prophet more fully, and
to quicken him to his work.

[Ezekiel 22:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 22:3</span></span>

Her time - The time of ripeness in her sins, and of execution of
judgments on her. To defile - For this does more defile them, and
provoke God to wrath against them.

[Ezekiel 22:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 22:4</span></span>

Thy days - The days of thy sorrows, and punishment. Art come - Thou art
grown up to the eldest years in sin, beyond which thou art not to go.

[Ezekiel 22:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 22:5</span></span>

Much vexed - Afflicted, impoverished, and ruined.

[Ezekiel 22:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">eze 22:6</span></span>

Every one - Not one to be found of a more merciful temper. To their
power - According to their ability.

[Ezekiel 22:7](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">eze 22:7</span></span>

In thee - In Jerusalem.

[Ezekiel 22:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">eze 22:8</span></span>

Thou - O Jerusalem. Mine holy things - All mine institutions, temple,
sacrifices, feasts.

[Ezekiel 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">eze 22:9</span></span>

Carry tales - Informers, or persons that for money, give in false
witness against the innocent. They eat - Offer sacrifice on the
mountains and feast there, in honour of their idols.

[Ezekiel 22:10](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">eze 22:10</span></span>

Discovered - Defiled their fathers bed.

[Ezekiel 22:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">eze 22:13</span></span>

Smitten mine hand - In testimony of my abhorrence.

[Ezekiel 22:14](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">eze 22:14</span></span>

Endure - Withstand the evils that are coming, or bear them when come.

[Ezekiel 22:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">eze 22:16</span></span>

In thyself - Whereas I was thine inheritance so long as thou wert a
holy, obedient people; now be an inheritance to thyself, if thou canst.

[Ezekiel 22:18](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">eze 22:18</span></span>

Dross - Utterly degenerate, and base metal. The furnace - The
afflictions I have laid upon them have not bettered them. The dross -
While they loved mercy, did justly, walked humbly with their God, they
were as silver; now they are but dross.

[Ezekiel 22:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">eze 22:19</span></span>

Gather you - From all parts. I will, by a secret over - ruling
providence, bring you into Jerusalem, as into a furnace, where you may
be consumed.

[Ezekiel 22:23](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">eze 22:23</span></span>

Her - The land of Israel. Not cleansed - Though God's judgments have
been as violent floods; and as hottest fires. Nor rained upon - Yet
neither thy filth hath been carried away, nor thy dross melted out of
thee. Therefore thou shalt be deprived of the rain, that should cool thy
thirsty land.

[Ezekiel 22:25](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">eze 22:25</span></span>

A conspiracy - A contrivance, to speak all alike, smooth words, and give
out promises of peace and safety. Thereof - Of the land. The treasure -
As a reward of their lies. Made her - By persuading Zedekiah to hold out
the war, which filled Jerusalem with dead husbands, and forlorn widows.

[Ezekiel 22:26](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">eze 22:26</span></span>

My holy things - Sacrifices, and oblations. Put no difference - Neither
have they in their practice, differenced holy and profane, nor in their
teaching acquainted the people with the difference, nor in the exercise
of their authority, separated the profane from the holy, either persons,
or things. Hid their eyes - Despised, and would not see the holiness of
the sabbaths. Profaned - Contemned, dishonoured, disobeyed.

[Ezekiel 22:27](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">eze 22:27</span></span>

Destroy souls - Ruin families; cutting off the fathers, and
impoverishing the widow, and fatherless.

[Ezekiel 22:28](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">eze 22:28</span></span>

Daubed them - Flattered them, in their ways of sin. Untempered mortar -
With promises that like ill - tempered mortar, will deceive them, though
all seems at present smooth and safe.

[Ezekiel 22:30](../../../bib/kjv/eze022.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">eze 22:30</span></span>

I sought - God speaks after the manner of men. A man - Any one, among
princes, prophets, priests, or people, to repair the breach. And stand -
Interpose between a sinful people, and their offended God, and intreat
for mercy. But - All were corrupted.
