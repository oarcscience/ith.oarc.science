*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 80](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001)

[Psalms 80:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 80:1</span></span>

Joseph - The children of Joseph or Israel. The name of Joseph, the most
eminent of the patriarchs, is elsewhere put for all the tribes. Cherubim
- Which were by the mercy seat above the ark.

[Psalms 80:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 80:2</span></span>

Before Ephraim - Here is an allusion to the ancient situation of the
tabernacle in the wilderness, where these tribes were placed on the west
- side of the tabernacle, in which the ark was, which consequently was
before them.

[Psalms 80:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 80:3</span></span>

Turn us - To thy self.

[Psalms 80:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 80:9</span></span>

Preparedst - Thou didst root out the idolatrous nations. Deep root -
Thou gavest them a firm settlement.

[Psalms 80:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 80:10</span></span>

The hills - They filled not only the fruitful valleys, but even the
barren mountains.

[Psalms 80:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 80:11</span></span>

The river - They possessed the whole land, from the mid - land sea to
the river Euphrates.

[Psalms 80:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 80:12</span></span>

Hedges - Taken away thy protection.

[Psalms 80:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">psa 80:16</span></span>

They - Thy people, signified by the vine. So now he passes from the
metaphor to the thing designed by it.

[Psalms 80:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">psa 80:17</span></span>

Be - To protect and strengthen him. Right - hand - Benjamin signifies
the son of the right hand, a dearly beloved son, as Benjamin was to
Jacob. Son of man - The people of Israel, who are often spoken of as one
person, as God's son and first - born.

[Psalms 80:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">psa 80:18</span></span>

Go back - Revolt from thee to idolatry or wickedness. Quicken - Revive
and restore us to our tranquility.
