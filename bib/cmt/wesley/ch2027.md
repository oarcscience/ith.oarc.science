### [2 Chronicles Chapter 27](../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#001)

[2 Chronicles 27:2](../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">ch2 27:2</span></span>

He did - He did according to all that his father Uzziah did; except in
his miscarriages. We must not imitate those we have the greatest esteem
for, any farther than we do well; but their failings must be warnings to
us, to walk more circumspectly.
