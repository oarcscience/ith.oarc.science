*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 130](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 130:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 130:3</span></span>

Mark - Observe them accurately and punish them as they deserve. Stand -
At thy tribunal.

[Psalms 130:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 130:4</span></span>

Forgiveness - Thou art able and ready to forgive repenting sinners.
Feared - Not with a slavish, but with a childlike fear. This mercy of
thine is the foundation of all religion, without which men would
desperately proceed in their impious courses.

[Psalms 130:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 130:5</span></span>

I wait - That he would pardon my sins.

[Psalms 130:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 130:6</span></span>

They - Whether soldiers that keep the night - watches in an army, or the
priests or Levites who did so in the temple.

[Psalms 130:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 130:7</span></span>

Israel - Every true Israelite. Plenteous - Abundantly sufficient for all
persons who accept it upon God's terms.
