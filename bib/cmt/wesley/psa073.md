*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 73](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 73:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 73:1</span></span>

A clean heart - To all true Israelites, who love God, and serve him in
spirit and truth.

[Psalms 73:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 73:2</span></span>

My feet - My faith in God's providence, was almost overthrown.

[Psalms 73:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 73:4</span></span>

No bands - They are not dragged to death, by the sentence of the
magistrate, which they deserve.

[Psalms 73:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 73:5</span></span>

As other men - As good men frequently are.

[Psalms 73:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 73:8</span></span>

And speak - Boasting of their oppressions.

[Psalms 73:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 73:9</span></span>

Against - Against God, blaspheming his name, and deriding his
providence. Walketh - Using all manner of liberty, reproaching all sorts
of persons.

[Psalms 73:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 73:10</span></span>

Turn - To this wicked company. Waters - And partake of the same
prosperity with their leaders. God seems to give them a full cup of
consolation, as if he would wring out all his blessings upon them.

[Psalms 73:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 73:12</span></span>

Behold - These seem to be the words of the psalmist, summing up the
matter.

[Psalms 73:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 73:13</span></span>

In vain - Hence I was sometimes tempted, to think that religion was a
vain, unprofitable thing. True religion is here described by its two
principal parts, the cleansing of the heart, and the hands.

[Psalms 73:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 73:15</span></span>

Offend - By grieving, discouraging and tempting them to revolt from God.

[Psalms 73:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">psa 73:16</span></span>

To know - To find out the reason of this providence.

[Psalms 73:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">psa 73:17</span></span>

Until - 'Till I consulted the word of God. He alludes to the practice of
those times, which was, in difficult cases to resort to God's sanctuary,
and the oracle therein. Their end - There I learned that their
prosperity was short.

[Psalms 73:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">psa 73:19</span></span>

Terrors - With God's dreadful judgements unexpectedly seizing upon them.

[Psalms 73:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">psa 73:20</span></span>

They awake - Out of the pleasant dream of this vain life. Despise - Thou
shalt make them despicable both to themselves and to all others; raise
them to shame, and everlasting contempt. Image - All their felicity and
glory, which shall be evidently discerned to be, no real or substantial
thing, but a mere image or shadow.

[Psalms 73:21](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">psa 73:21</span></span>

Pricked - I was deeply wounded with disquieting thoughts.

[Psalms 73:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">psa 73:22</span></span>

Nevertheless - Although I gave thee just cause to cast me off, yet thou
didst continue thy care and kindness to me. Hast held - That my faith
might not fail.

[Psalms 73:27](../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#027.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">psa 73:27</span></span>

Go a whoring - Those who revolt from thee, to work wickedness; which is
called whoredom in scripture.
