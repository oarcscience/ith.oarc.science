*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Proverbs Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#001)

[Proverbs 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 4:1</span></span>

A father - Of me, who have paternal authority over you and affection for
you.

[Proverbs 4:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 4:3</span></span>

Tender - Young and tender in years, and tenderly educated. Only beloved
- Beloved above all the rest.

[Proverbs 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 4:4</span></span>

Said - The following verses, at least as far as the tenth verse, are the
words of David.

[Proverbs 4:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 4:7</span></span>

With all - Even with the price of all.

[Proverbs 4:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 4:8</span></span>

Exalt - Let her have thine highest esteem and affection.

[Proverbs 4:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 4:9</span></span>

Grace - A beautiful ornament, such as they used to put upon their heads.

[Proverbs 4:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 4:16</span></span>

For - They cannot sleep with quietness.

[Proverbs 4:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 4:17</span></span>

For - Wickedness is as pleasant to them as their bread.

[Proverbs 4:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 4:18</span></span>

But - Just men daily grow in knowledge, and grace, and consolation,
'till all be perfected and swallowed up in glory.

[Proverbs 4:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 4:19</span></span>

Darkness - Full of ignorance and error, of uncertainty and confusion, of
danger and misery.

[Proverbs 4:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 4:23</span></span>

Heart - Thy thoughts, will, and affections. For - From thence proceed
all the actions, as of the natural, so of the spiritual life, which lead
to eternal life.

[Proverbs 4:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 4:24</span></span>

Mouth - All sorts of sinful words.

[Proverbs 4:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 4:25</span></span>

Right on - Direct all thine actions to a right end, and keep thy mind
fixed upon that way which leads to it, and neither look or turn aside to
the right - hand or the left.

[Proverbs 4:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 4:26</span></span>

Ponder - Consider thine actions before thou dost them, and see that they
agree with the rule. And - Let thine actions be uniformly and constantly
good in spite of all temptations.
