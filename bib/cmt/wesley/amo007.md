### [Amos Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#001)

[Amos 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">amo 7:1</span></span>

Thus - This is the first of five prophetic representations of what was
coming upon this people. The latter growth - The shooting up of the
first growth being too luxuriant, they often eat it down with cattle;
but if the second growth were eat up, it marred the whole harvest. Mowed
- It is supposed the first mowing of the corn in the blade was for the
king's use; and after this the second springing grew up to the harvest.

[Amos 7:2](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">amo 7:2</span></span>

It came to pass - In the vision. By whom - How shall any of Jacob
escape, if thou dost cast him down?

[Amos 7:3](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">amo 7:3</span></span>

Repented - This is spoken after the manner of men.

[Amos 7:4](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">amo 7:4</span></span>

Shewed - In vision. Called - Commanded fire from heaven. A part - Of the
land too.

[Amos 7:7](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">amo 7:7</span></span>

By a plumb - line - Strongly and beautifully built.

[Amos 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">amo 7:8</span></span>

Set a plumb - line - I will exactly measure the whole ten tribes. Pass
by them - I will no more forbear, but will pull down all that is faulty.

[Amos 7:9](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">amo 7:9</span></span>

The high places - The temples on high mountains built to idols. Of Isaac
- The seed of Isaac.

[Amos 7:10](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">amo 7:10</span></span>

In the midst - Openly, and publickly, endeavouring to stir up Israel to
sedition or rebellion. The land - The people cannot bear all his harsh
predictions.

[Amos 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">amo 7:11</span></span>

Jeroboam shall die - He nowhere said so, but spake of his house
distinguished from his person, as [Amo
7:9](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#009).

[Amos 7:13](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">amo 7:13</span></span>

The king's court - To which therefore thou oughtest to shew more
respect, and not thus to affront the king in his own house.

[Amos 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">amo 7:14</span></span>

No prophet - Not originally, or by study, or by any human designation.
An herdman - By breeding and occupation I was and still am, an herdman.
Sycamore fruit - This fruit was good food for man, or cattle.

[Amos 7:16](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">amo 7:16</span></span>

The house of Isaac - The posterity of Isaac.

[Amos 7:17](../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">amo 7:17</span></span>

In a polluted land - Among the Heathen. Israel - The ten tribes.
