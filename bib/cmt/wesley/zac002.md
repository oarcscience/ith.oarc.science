*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Zechariah Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)

[Zechariah 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 2:1</span></span>

With a line - Ready and prepared to lay out the platform of Jerusalem.

[Zechariah 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">zac 2:2</span></span>

To measure - To take the exact dimensions of it, that it may answer
God's promise, and be capable to receive its inhabitants. Jerusalem -
The city which was to be built hereafter.

[Zechariah 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">zac 2:3</span></span>

The angel - Christ, who had so long talked with Zechariah. Went forth -
From the midst of the myrtle - trees.

[Zechariah 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zac 2:4</span></span>

And he said - Christ to that angel who came to meet him. Run - Hasten
and tell Zechariah. As towns - The suburbs of it shall be as towns
unwalled, for extent and for safety.

[Zechariah 2:5](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">zac 2:5</span></span>

The glory - My presence and favour shall make her glorious.

[Zechariah 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">zac 2:6</span></span>

Ho, ho - Ye sleepy Jews. Come forth - Come out from your prisons. Flee -
Make all the haste you can. From the land - Babylon, which lay north of
Canaan. For I have spread you abroad - As I executed my threats in
scattering you, so I will perform my promise, and gather you.

[Zechariah 2:7](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">zac 2:7</span></span>

Deliver thyself - Accept of thy deliverance.

[Zechariah 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">zac 2:8</span></span>

After the glory - After that he is become your glory, I am to avenge you
of your enemies.

[Zechariah 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zac 2:9</span></span>

Upon them - Against the nation that doth violence to my people. Unto
their servants - Unto the Jews, who were first spoiled by, and then made
servants to them. Hath sent me - To inform them of my father's will.

[Zechariah 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">zac 2:10</span></span>

I come - To execute judgments on thine adversaries, and to compleat thy
deliverance and salvation. I will dwell - This was fulfilled in part to
the Jews, but more fully to the gospel church.

[Zechariah 2:11](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zac 2:11</span></span>

Shall be joined - Shall worship the God of Israel. In that day - When
Christ shall come in the flesh, and take down the partition wall. Sent
me - The Messiah.

[Zechariah 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">zac 2:12</span></span>

Shall inherit - Claim, recover, possess, and delight in, as a man doth
in his paternal inheritance.

[Zechariah 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">zac 2:13</span></span>

Be silent - Reverence and adore God, and expect the accomplishment of
his word. All flesh - Both Jew and Gentile. He is raised up - God is on
this work already, and he will not sit down again, 'till he has
accomplished his whole work.
