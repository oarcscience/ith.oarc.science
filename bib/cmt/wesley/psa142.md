### [Psalms Chapter 142](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 142:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 142:3</span></span>

Knowest - So as to direct me to it. My path - What paths I should chuse
whereby I might escape.

[Psalms 142:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 142:4</span></span>

Right - hand - The place where the patron or assistant used to stand.

[Psalms 142:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 142:5</span></span>

Portion - Even in this life.

[Psalms 142:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 142:7</span></span>

Prison - Set me at liberty. Compass - Shall flock to me from all parts,
to rejoice and bless God with me and for me.
