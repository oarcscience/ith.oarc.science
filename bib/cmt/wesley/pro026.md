### [Proverbs Chapter 26](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 26:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">pro 26:2</span></span>

By flying - Secures itself from the fowler. Not come - Upon the innocent
person, but he shall escape from it like a bird.

[Proverbs 26:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 26:4</span></span>

According - So as to imitate his folly, by passionate or reproachful
speeches.

[Proverbs 26:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">pro 26:5</span></span>

According - So as his folly needs and requires, convincing him strongly,
reproving him sharply, and exposing him to just shame.

[Proverbs 26:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 26:6</span></span>

Cutteth off the feet - Of his messenger; bids one go that wants legs.
Drinketh - Drinking, in scripture, frequently denotes the plentiful
doing or receiving of any thing.

[Proverbs 26:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 26:7</span></span>

The legs - Heb. the legs of the lame are lifted up, in going, or in
dancing, which is done with great inequality and uncomeliness. So - No
less incident are wise and pious speeches from a foolish and ungodly
man.

[Proverbs 26:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 26:8</span></span>

Bindeth - Whereby he hinders his own design of throwing the stone out of
it. So - No less absurd is he that giveth to a fool that honour which he
is not capable of using aright.

[Proverbs 26:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 26:9</span></span>

A thorn - As a thorn is in a drunkard's hand, which he cannot manage
cautiously, but employs to his own and others hurt. So - As
unprofitable, and, by accident, hurtful to himself and others.

[Proverbs 26:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 26:10</span></span>

Rewardeth - Will certainly give that recompence which is deserved by
fools and transgressors, by such as sin either thro' ignorance, or
wilfully.

[Proverbs 26:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">pro 26:14</span></span>

Turneth - Moving hither and thither upon it, but not removing from its
place.

[Proverbs 26:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 26:16</span></span>

A reason - A satisfactory reason of ail their actions.

[Proverbs 26:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 26:17</span></span>

He - Who is going upon the way. With strife - In which he is not
concerned, nor any way obliged to meddle. That taketh - Exposes himself
to needless hazard.

[Proverbs 26:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 26:18</span></span>

And death - Any instruments of death.

[Proverbs 26:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 26:23</span></span>

Burning - With malice or hatred: A slanderous or evil tongue. Dross -
Such a tongue and heart are of no real worth, although sometimes they
make a shew of it, as dross does of silver.

[Proverbs 26:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 26:26</span></span>

Deceit - With false professions of love.

[Proverbs 26:27](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#027.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">pro 26:27</span></span>

Rolleth - Up the hill with design to do mischief to some person.
