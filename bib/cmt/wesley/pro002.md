*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Proverbs Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 2:1</span></span>

Hide - Lay them up in thy heart with care, as men do their choicest
treasures.

[Proverbs 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 2:3</span></span>

Criest - To God, the only giver of it.

[Proverbs 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 2:4</span></span>

Seek her - With unwearied diligence and earnest desire and patient
expectation.

[Proverbs 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 2:8</span></span>

Path of judgment - Righteous paths, judgment being here put for
righteousness. And keeping of paths may be put for keeping them in their
paths.

[Proverbs 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 2:9</span></span>

Then - When God in answer to thy desires hath given thee wisdom. Equity
- All the parts of thy duty to man, as well as the fear of God.

[Proverbs 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 2:10</span></span>

When - Thou dost truly love wisdom.

[Proverbs 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 2:13</span></span>

Darkness - Of sin.

[Proverbs 2:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">pro 2:15</span></span>

Crooked - Who swerve from the straight way of God's law, and have
windings and turnings, to escape conviction.

[Proverbs 2:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 2:16</span></span>

Strange woman - From the adulteress or whore.

[Proverbs 2:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 2:17</span></span>

Forsaketh - Her husband whom she took to be her guide and governor, in
her youth. The covenant - The marriage covenant: so called because God
is the author of that mutual obligation: and because God is called to be
the witness and judge of that solemn promise and covenant.

[Proverbs 2:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 2:18</span></span>

For - Conversation with her (which was most usual in her own house) is
the certain way to death, which it brings many ways, and undoubtedly,
without repentance, to God's wrath and the second death.

[Proverbs 2:20](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">pro 2:20</span></span>

That - This depends upon [Pro 2:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#011.md),
and is mentioned as another happy fruit of wisdom.
