### [Zephaniah Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#001)

[Zephaniah 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zep 3:1</span></span>

To her - Jerusalem.

[Zephaniah 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">zep 3:2</span></span>

The voice - Of God by his mercy and judgments crying aloud.

[Zephaniah 3:3](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">zep 3:3</span></span>

Her princes - Persons of principal place and authority. Lions - Which
hunt for prey, and are ever affrighting or devouring. Wolves -
Insatiable and cruel, like wolves of the evening, whetted with hunger.
Gnaw not the bones - They leave nothing but the bones to be eaten on the
morrow.

[Zephaniah 3:4](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zep 3:4</span></span>

Her prophet - So called, false prophets. Light - Unstable and
inconstant. Violence to the law - Wresting it by perverse
interpretation.

[Zephaniah 3:5](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">zep 3:5</span></span>

In the midst - Observing all. Not do iniquity - He will judge them
righteously. Every morning - Daily he discovers his displeasure against
the wicked. Faileth not - Lets no season slip to convince them, by
public and visible punishments. The unjust - But the wicked Jews proceed
without shame, and without fear.

[Zephaniah 3:6](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">zep 3:6</span></span>

The nations - Of old, the Canaanites, lastly the ten tribes, and later
yet, the Assyrians.

[Zephaniah 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">zep 3:7</span></span>

I said - I thought (speaking after the manner of men). Thou - O
Jerusalem. Fear me - For the many and great judgments executed upon
others. I punished them - In some measure.

[Zephaniah 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">zep 3:8</span></span>

Therefore - Since you will not be amended. Wait ye - Attend my
resolution. Until - Until I rise up to destroy first, and next to take
the spoil. Upon them - The incorrigible Jews. Devoured - Consumed as if
burnt up. My jealousy - That jealousy wherewith God is jealous for his
own glory.

[Zephaniah 3:9](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zep 3:9</span></span>

A pure language - I will give them a pure way of worshipping me, the
issue of a pure heart.

[Zephaniah 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">zep 3:10</span></span>

My dispersed - The praying remnant of the scattered Jews shall return to
their own land, and bring themselves an offering unto the Lord.

[Zephaniah 3:11](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zep 3:11</span></span>

Thy doings - Thy sins formerly committed. In thy pride - Proud
formalists. No more be haughty - Ye shall no more boast, because of the
city, or the temple.

[Zephaniah 3:12](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">zep 3:12</span></span>

Of thee - In Judea and Jerusalem.

[Zephaniah 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">zep 3:13</span></span>

Shall feed - Shall enjoy peace and plenty.

[Zephaniah 3:15](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">zep 3:15</span></span>

Taken away - Abolished, and put an end to the judgments thy sins brought
upon thee. Thine enemy - The Babylonian. Is in the midst - He is
returned to redeem and govern thee. Any more - While thy carriage is as
becomes my presence with thee, thou shalt neither fear, nor feel the
like evils.

[Zephaniah 3:18](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">zep 3:18</span></span>

Sorrowful - That mourn their distance from the solemn worship of God.
Who are of thee - Thy children. Reproach - The taunts of their enemies.

[Zephaniah 3:19](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">zep 3:19</span></span>

Undo - I will break their power and dissolve their kingdom. That halteth
- Who is in trouble and ready to fall. Driven out - Into remote
countries.

[Zephaniah 3:20](../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">zep 3:20</span></span>

A praise - So the universal church of the first - born will be, in the
great day. And then the Israel of God be made a name and a praise to all
eternity.
