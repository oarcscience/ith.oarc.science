*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 37](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001)

[Psalms 37:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 37:1</span></span>

Fret not - Because they prosper in their wicked enterprizes.

[Psalms 37:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 37:5</span></span>

Commit - All thy cares and business, and necessities, commend to God by
fervent prayer.

[Psalms 37:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 37:6</span></span>

Judgment - It shall be as visible to men, as the light of the sun, at
noon - day.

[Psalms 37:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 37:7</span></span>

Rest - Do not repine at his dealings, but quietly submit to his will,
and wait for his help.

[Psalms 37:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 37:8</span></span>

Fret not - Either against the sinner for his success; or against God. Do
evil - If grief arise in thee, take care that it do not transport thee
to sin.

[Psalms 37:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 37:9</span></span>

The earth - This for the most part was literally fulfilled in that state
of the church.

[Psalms 37:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 37:10</span></span>

Not be - He shall be dead and gone. Diligently - Industriously seeking
him. His place - His place and estate, and glory.

[Psalms 37:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 37:11</span></span>

But - Those who patiently bear God's afflicting hand, and meekly pass by
injuries. Peace - Partly of outward peace and prosperity, which God in
his due time will give them: but principally of inward peace, in the
sense of God's favour and the assurance of endless happiness.

[Psalms 37:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 37:13</span></span>

His day - The day appointed by God for his punishment or destruction.

[Psalms 37:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">psa 37:18</span></span>

Knoweth - Observes with care and affection. The days - All things which
befal them, their dangers and fears, and suffering. For ever - To them
and their seed for ever: and when they die their inheritance is not
lost, but exchanged for one infinitely better.

[Psalms 37:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">psa 37:20</span></span>

Fat - Which in an instant melts before the fire.

[Psalms 37:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">psa 37:23</span></span>

Established - So that he shall not fall into mischief.

[Psalms 37:24](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">psa 37:24</span></span>

Fall - Into trouble.

[Psalms 37:25](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">psa 37:25</span></span>

Forsaken - These temporal promises, were more express to the Jews in the
times of the Old Testament, than to Christians.

[Psalms 37:26](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">psa 37:26</span></span>

Blessed - Not only with spiritual, but with temporal blessings.

[Psalms 37:27](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">psa 37:27</span></span>

Dwell - Thou shalt dwell in the land, and afterwards in heaven.

[Psalms 37:30](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">psa 37:30</span></span>

The mouth - Having shewed, God's singular care over the righteous, he
proceeds to give a character of them. Judgment - Of God's judgment, word
or law.

[Psalms 37:31](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">psa 37:31</span></span>

Heart - His thoughts, meditations and affections are fixed upon it.
Slide - Slide, or swerve, from the rule, from God's law.

[Psalms 37:35](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">psa 37:35</span></span>

Bay - tree - Which is continually green and flourishing even in winter.

[Psalms 37:36](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#036)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">psa 37:36</span></span>

Yet - He was gone in an instant. But - There was no monument or
remainder of him left.

[Psalms 37:37](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#037)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">psa 37:37</span></span>

Peace - Though he may meet with troubles in his way, yet all shall end
well.

[Psalms 37:38](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#038)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_038">psa 37:38</span></span>

Together - All without exception.
