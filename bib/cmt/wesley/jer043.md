### [Jeremiah Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 43:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">jer 43:7</span></span>

Tahpanhes - It was at this time the place where the king of Egypt made
his residence.

[Jeremiah 43:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">jer 43:12</span></span>

Carry them - He shall carry away both the idols, and the inhabitants of
Egypt captive. With the land - With the spoils of the land of Egypt, he
shall clothe his army.

[Jeremiah 43:13](../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">jer 43:13</span></span>

Beth - shemesh - Beth - shemesh is the name of a city, which had its
name from a famous temple of the sun.
