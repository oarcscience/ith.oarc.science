*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 25](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 25:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 25:2</span></span>

Ashamed - Disappointed of my hope.

[Psalms 25:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 25:3</span></span>

Cause - Without any provocation of mine.

[Psalms 25:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 25:4</span></span>

Teach - Teach me my duty, and cause me to keep close to it,
notwithstanding all temptations.

[Psalms 25:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 25:8</span></span>

Upright - Holy and true, in all his declarations and offers of mercy to
sinners. Therefore - He will not be wanting to such poor sinners as I
am, but will guide them into the way of life and peace.

[Psalms 25:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 25:9</span></span>

The meek - Such as meekly submit themselves to God, and are desirous to
be directed and governed by him. Judgment - In the paths of judgment, in
the right way.

[Psalms 25:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 25:10</span></span>

Paths - All the dealings of God with them, yea even those that are
afflictive, are done in kindness and faithfulness to them.

[Psalms 25:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 25:11</span></span>

For - Or, though (as this particle is often rendered) it be great.
Possibly he speaks of his sin against Uriah and Bathsheba. Great - Or,
much or manifold. For the Hebrew word signifies both great and much.

[Psalms 25:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 25:12</span></span>

Chuse - Which God appointeth.

[Psalms 25:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 25:13</span></span>

At ease - Heb. in Good; in the possession and enjoyment of the true
good. The land - Canaan; which was given as an earnest of the whole
Covenant of Grace, and all its promises.

[Psalms 25:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">psa 25:14</span></span>

The secret - His love and favour, which is called his secret, [Job
29:4](../../../bib/kjv/job029.htm#004.md); [Pro
3:32](../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#032.md), because it is known to none but
him that enjoyeth it. Will shew - He will make them clearly to
understand it, both its duties and its blessings; neither of which
ungodly men rightly understand.

[Psalms 25:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 25:15</span></span>

Pluck - He will deliver me out of all my troubles.

[Psalms 25:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">psa 25:20</span></span>

Soul - My life.

[Psalms 25:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">psa 25:22</span></span>

Israel - If thou wilt not help me, yet spare thy people who suffer for
my sake, and in my sufferings.
