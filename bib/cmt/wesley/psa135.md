### [Psalms Chapter 135](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#001)

[Psalms 135:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 135:2</span></span>

Ye - Ye priest and Levites.

[Psalms 135:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 135:6</span></span>

Seas - In the visible seas, and in the invisible depths both of the
earth and of the waters.

[Psalms 135:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 135:7</span></span>

From - From all parts of the earth, from one end to another. Rain - An
eminent instance of his good providence.

[Psalms 135:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">psa 135:14</span></span>

Judge - Will in due time plead the cause of his people. Repent - He will
recall that severe sentence which for their sins he had passed upon
them.
