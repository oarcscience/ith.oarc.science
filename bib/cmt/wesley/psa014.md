### [Psalms Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#001)

[Psalms 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 14:1</span></span>

The fool - The wicked man. Good - That is, actions really good or
pleasing to God.

[Psalms 14:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 14:2</span></span>

Looked - God knoweth all things without any enquiry: but he speaks after
the manner of men. Upon - Upon the whole Israelitish nation, and upon
all mankind for he speaks of all except his people, and the righteous
ones, who are opposed to these, [Psa
14:4-5](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#004).

[Psalms 14:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 14:3</span></span>

Gone - From God, and from the rule which he hath given them. Filthy -
Loathsome and abominable to God.

[Psalms 14:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 14:4</span></span>

Bread - With as little remorse, and with as much greediness. Call not -
They are guilty not only of gross injustice towards men, but also of
horrid impiety and contempt of God.

[Psalms 14:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 14:5</span></span>

There - Upon the spot, where they practised these insolences, God struck
them with a panick fear. For - God is on their side, and therefore their
enemies have cause to tremble.

[Psalms 14:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 14:6</span></span>

Because - This was the ground of their contempt, that he lived by faith
in God's promise and providence.

[Psalms 14:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 14:7</span></span>

O that - These words immediately concern the deliverance of Israel out
of that sinful state, in which they now were; which having described, he
concludes, with a prayer to God to help them out of Zion, where the ark
then was, but principally they design the spiritual redemption and
salvation of all God's Israel by the Messiah. The captivity - His
captive people. The children of Jacob, as Aaron is named for his sons,
[Ch1 12:27](../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#027).
