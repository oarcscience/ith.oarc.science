### [Jeremiah Chapter 45](../../../bib/kjv/jer045.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 45:3](../../../bib/kjv/jer045.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">jer 45:3</span></span>

I find no rest - Upon Baruch's reading the prophecies both he and
Jeremiah were advised to hide themselves. This probably disturbed
Baruch, and made him lament his condition.

[Jeremiah 45:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer045.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">jer 45:5</span></span>

Seek them not - Dost thou expect what none is like to meet with who
feareth God? Upon all flesh - I am bringing calamities upon the whole
nation.
