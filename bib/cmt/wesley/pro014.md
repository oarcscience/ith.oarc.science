### [Proverbs Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 14:1</span></span>

Buildeth - Maintains and improves her family and estate. Her hands - By
her idleness or sin.

[Proverbs 14:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 14:3</span></span>

A rod - His insolent speeches as they are like a rod, offensive to
others, so, they make a rod for their own backs.

[Proverbs 14:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 14:4</span></span>

The crib - The stable is empty of food for cattle, and much more for
man. In the same sense cleanness of teeth is put for famine, [Amo
4:6](../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#006.md). The strength - By their labours,
or by diligence in husbandry, which then was principally managed by
oxen.

[Proverbs 14:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 14:6</span></span>

Findeth it not - Because he doth not seek it aright. That understandeth
- That is honest and pious.

[Proverbs 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 14:8</span></span>

His way - Consists not in vain speculations, but in a diligent study of
his duty, and of the way to eternal happiness. The folly - The wit of
ungodly men, which is really their folly, is employed only in finding
out ways of deceiving.

[Proverbs 14:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 14:9</span></span>

Favour - They find favour both with God and men.

[Proverbs 14:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 14:10</span></span>

Bitterness - The inward griefs and joys of mens hearts, are not known to
any but a man's self.

[Proverbs 14:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">pro 14:12</span></span>

Right - There are some evil courses which men may think to be lawful and
good. The end - The event shews that they were sinful and destructive.

[Proverbs 14:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 14:13</span></span>

In laughter - The outward signs of joy are often mixed with real sorrow.

[Proverbs 14:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">pro 14:14</span></span>

In heart - He who departs from God inwardly. Filled - With the fruit of
his ways, the punishment of his sins. Satisfied - From the pious temper
of his own heart, which cleaves to the Lord, he shall receive
unspeakable comfort.

[Proverbs 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">pro 14:15</span></span>

The simple - A foolish man. Looketh - He orders his dealings in the
world with due circumspection, not considering so much what other men
say as what he ought to do.

[Proverbs 14:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 14:16</span></span>

Feareth - Trembles at God's judgments when they are either inflicted or
threatened. Rageth - Frets against God; or is enraged against his
messengers. Confident - Secure and insensible of his danger.

[Proverbs 14:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 14:17</span></span>

Foolishly - His passion hurries him into foolish speeches and actions.
Wicked devices - One who suppresses his passion, but meditates revenge.
Hated - Both by God and men.

[Proverbs 14:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 14:18</span></span>

Inherit - They possess it as their inheritance, holding it fast, and
glorying in it. Knowledge - The saving knowledge of God and of their own
duty.

[Proverbs 14:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 14:19</span></span>

The evil - Good men shall have the pre - eminency over the wicked.

[Proverbs 14:20](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">pro 14:20</span></span>

Hated - Despised and abandoned.

[Proverbs 14:21](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">pro 14:21</span></span>

Despiseth - That does not pity and relieve the poor.

[Proverbs 14:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 14:23</span></span>

The talk - Idle talking will bring a man to poverty.

[Proverbs 14:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 14:24</span></span>

Riches - They are a singular advantage and ornament to them. But - As
for rich fools, their folly is not cured, but made worse and more
manifest by their riches.

[Proverbs 14:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 14:25</span></span>

Souls - Such as are innocent, from false accusations.

[Proverbs 14:27](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#027.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">pro 14:27</span></span>

To depart - To preserve men from destructive courses.

[Proverbs 14:29](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">pro 14:29</span></span>

Exalteth - Heb. lifteth up, like a banner, makes it known and visible to
all men.

[Proverbs 14:30](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#030.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">pro 14:30</span></span>

A sound heart - Free from envy and inordinate passions. Is life -
Procures and maintains the health and vigour of the body.

[Proverbs 14:31](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#031.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">pro 14:31</span></span>

His maker - Whose image the poor man bears, by whose providence he is
made poor, and who hath declared himself to be their protector and
avenger.

[Proverbs 14:32](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#032.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_032">pro 14:32</span></span>

Driven away - In his death, from God's favour and presence. Death - In
his greatest dangers and distresses, yea even in death itself.

[Proverbs 14:33](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#033.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">pro 14:33</span></span>

Resteth - Is laid up and hid there. In the midst - In the heart. Made
known - They will publish in all times and companies.

[Proverbs 14:35](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#035.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">pro 14:35</span></span>

Shame - Both to himself, by his foolish management of the king's
affairs, and to the king who made so foolish a choice of a servant.
