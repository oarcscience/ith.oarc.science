### [Psalms Chapter 125](../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 125:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 125:3</span></span>

The rod - Their power and authority. Not rest - Not continue long. The
lot - Upon the habitations and persons of good men. Lest - Lest they
should be driven to indirect courses to relieve themselves.

[Psalms 125:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 125:5</span></span>

Lead them - Unto sinful courses. Israel - Upon the true Israel of God.
