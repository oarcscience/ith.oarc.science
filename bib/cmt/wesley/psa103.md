### [Psalms Chapter 103](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 103:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 103:5</span></span>

The eagles - Which lives long in great strength and vigour.

[Psalms 103:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 103:11</span></span>

So great - So much above their deserts and expectations.

[Psalms 103:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">psa 103:14</span></span>

Knoweth - The weakness and mortality of our natures, and the frailty of
our condition, so that if he should let loose his hand upon us, we
should be irrecoverably destroyed.

[Psalms 103:21](../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">psa 103:21</span></span>

His hosts - A title often given to the angels, in regard of their vast
numbers, mighty power, unanimous concurrence, and exquisite order.
Ministers - This Hebrew word is commonly used of the highest and most
honourable sort of servants,
