### [1 Chronicles Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 6:10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">ch1 6:10</span></span>

The priest's office - So did all the rest, but it is implied that he did
it worthily, he filled his place, and valiantly discharged his office in
Uzziah's time. Solomon built - In Solomon's temple; so called to
distinguish it from the second temple which was built or in building
when these books were written.

[1 Chronicles 6:44](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#044.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_044">ch1 6:44</span></span>

Ethan - Called also Jeduthun, [Ch1
9:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#016.md), [Ch2
35:15](../../../bib/kjv/ch2035.htm#015.md), and in the titles of divers
psalms.

[1 Chronicles 6:54](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#054.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_054">ch1 6:54</span></span>

Castles - So called, not only because, walled and well guarded by the
country; but because they and their possessions were in a particular
manner the care of divine providence. As God was their portion, so God
was their protector. And a cottage will be a castle to those that abide
under the shadow of the Almighty. The lot - Or, the first lot.

[1 Chronicles 6:66](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#066.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_066">ch1 6:66</span></span>

Coasts - Or, of their borders, of their country contained within its
borders.
