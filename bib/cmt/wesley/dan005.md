### [Daniel Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#001)

[Daniel 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">dan 5:1</span></span>

Belshazzar - The grandson of Nebuchadnezzar. Made a great feast - After
the manner of the eastern kings who shewed their magnificence this way.
But this is prodigious that he should carouse when the city was
besieged, and ready to be taken by Darius the Mede.

[Daniel 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">dan 5:2</span></span>

To bring the vessels - Triumphing thereby over God and his people.

[Daniel 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">dan 5:4</span></span>

And praised the gods of gold - At the same time insulting the great God
of heaven and earth.

[Daniel 5:5](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">dan 5:5</span></span>

Came forth fingers - The likeness of a man's hand.

[Daniel 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">dan 5:6</span></span>

His knees smote - So soon can the terrors of God make the loftiest
cedars, the tyrants of the earth.

[Daniel 5:10](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">dan 5:10</span></span>

The queen came - The women in those courts had an apartment by
themselves, and this being the queen - mother, and aged, did not mingle
herself with the king's wives and concubines, yet she broke the rule in
coming in now, upon this solemn occasion.

[Daniel 5:24](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">dan 5:24</span></span>

From him - From that God whom thou hast despised.

[Daniel 5:26](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">dan 5:26</span></span>

MENE - MENE MENE, it is numbered, it is numbered; the words are doubled
for the greater confirmation. It relates to the number of the seventy
years for the overthrow of the Babylonish empire.

[Daniel 5:27](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">dan 5:27</span></span>

Art found wanting - There is no weight nor worth in thee; thou hast made
light of God, and the Lord makes light of thee.

[Daniel 5:28](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">dan 5:28</span></span>

PERES - Separated, divided, broken. Phars signifies two things, broken
off, and Persian; noting that, first, this kingdom was broken down from
Belshazzar. Secondly, that it was given to the Persians.

[Daniel 5:31](../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">dan 5:31</span></span>

Darius the Mede - This was he that with Cyrus besieged and took Babylon.
