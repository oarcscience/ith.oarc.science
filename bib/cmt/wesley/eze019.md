### [Ezekiel Chapter 19](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 19:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 19:1</span></span>

For the princes - Jehoahaz, Jehoiachim, Jehoiachin, and Zedekiah.

[Ezekiel 19:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">eze 19:2</span></span>

What - What resemblance shall I use to set out the nature, deportment,
and state of the mother of these princes? Thy - One of whom was upon the
throne at once, and therefore the prophet speaks to one at a time.
Mother - The land of Judea, and Jerusalem, the chief city of it, the
royal family of David. Lioness - Tho' chosen of God to execute justice;
yet they soon degenerated into the fierce and ravening nature of the
lioness. Lay down - Associated, and grew familiar with neighbour kings,
called here lions; fierce and bloody. Her whelps - Her sons, successors
to the crown. Young lions - Either foreign princes and kings, or some of
the fierce, unjust, tyrannizing princes at home.

[Ezekiel 19:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 19:3</span></span>

Brought up - Advanced, caused him to take the throne after the slaughter
of Josiah. One - Jehoahaz the second son of Josiah. Became - Soon shewed
his fierce, cruel, and bloody disposition.

[Ezekiel 19:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 19:4</span></span>

The nations - The Egyptians heard what he did.

[Ezekiel 19:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 19:5</span></span>

Made him - King, and infused the lion - like maxims into him.

[Ezekiel 19:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">eze 19:6</span></span>

He - Jehoiachim. Went up - He continued eleven years on the throne;
whereas Jehoahaz was taken as soon as he first ventured out. The lions -
Heathen kings, with whom he entered into leagues. He became - Fierce,
ravenous, unsatiable.

[Ezekiel 19:7](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">eze 19:7</span></span>

He knew - By taking them, he came to know their places, which are here
called, what he made them, desolate. Roaring - By the perpetual violent
threats of this cruel king.

[Ezekiel 19:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">eze 19:8</span></span>

The nations - Which were tributary to Nebuchadnezzar. Set against - By
order of the king of Babylon. The provinces - Which belonged to the
Babylonish kingdom.

[Ezekiel 19:10](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">eze 19:10</span></span>

Thy mother - O thou prince of Israel. By the waters - In a very fruitful
soil. Full of branches - Full of children; when Josiah died, he left
four behind him, beside other branches of the royal line.

[Ezekiel 19:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">eze 19:11</span></span>

Strong rods - Many excellent persons endowed with qualifications
befitting kings, that they might sway the scepter. Exalted - Above the
ordinary majesty of other kingdoms. Thick branches - This kingdom
equalled, if not excelled, the greatest neighbour - kingdoms, and her
kings exceeded all their neighbouring kings, in riches and power.

[Ezekiel 19:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">eze 19:12</span></span>

The east wind - God raised up the king of Babylon to pull up this sinful
kingdom. Dried up - Blasted all her fruit, deposed her king, captivated
him, his family, and the whole kingdom. Strong rods - All the choice
men.

[Ezekiel 19:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">eze 19:13</span></span>

She - A few of the branches of the last pruning. In the wilderness -
Tho' Babylon was in a very fruitful place, yet the cruelty of the
Babylonians, made it to the Jews as terrible as a wilderness.

[Ezekiel 19:14](../../../bib/kjv/eze019.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">eze 19:14</span></span>

Fire - The fire of rebellion, kindled by Zedekiah, who is of the blood -
royal. No strong rod - The regal dignity is ceased.
