*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 138](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#001)

[Psalms 138:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 138:1</span></span>

The gods - Before kings and princes.

[Psalms 138:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 138:2</span></span>

Temple - Where the ark was. He was not permitted to enter into it.
Magnified - For thou hast glorified thy word or promise unto me more
than any other of thy glorious perfections.

[Psalms 138:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 138:4</span></span>

The kings - A prophecy of the calling of the Gentiles. Hear - The gospel
preached among then.

[Psalms 138:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 138:5</span></span>

The ways - His wonderful counsel and gracious providences.

[Psalms 138:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 138:8</span></span>

Perfect - Will finish the great work of my deliverance. Forsake not -
Or, do not give over, the work of my salvation, which is thus far
advanced, not by any human help, but by thy power and providence.
