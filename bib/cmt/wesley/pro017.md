### [Proverbs Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 17:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 17:4</span></span>

False lips - To false accusations and calumnies, which give him occasion
and encouragement to do mischief.

[Proverbs 17:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 17:7</span></span>

Excellent speech - Discourse of high and excellent things.

[Proverbs 17:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 17:8</span></span>

A precious stone - Pleasant and acceptable; and withal dazzles his eyes.
Whithersoever - To whomsoever it is presented. But this, as also many of
her proverbs, are to be understood of most men, not of all.

[Proverbs 17:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 17:9</span></span>

Covereth - That conceals as far as he may, other mens faults.

[Proverbs 17:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">pro 17:11</span></span>

Rebellion - Against God. Messenger - Or, a cruel angel, the angel of
death, the devil, or some bloody men employed by God to avenge his
quarrel.

[Proverbs 17:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">pro 17:12</span></span>

Folly - In the heat of his lust or passion.

[Proverbs 17:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">pro 17:14</span></span>

Letteth out - By cutting the bank of a river. Leave off - Avoid the
occasions, and prevent the beginning of contention.

[Proverbs 17:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 17:16</span></span>

A price - Opportunities and abilities of getting it. No heart - Neither
discretion to discern the worth of wisdom, nor any sincere desire to get
it.

[Proverbs 17:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 17:17</span></span>

Was born - Was sent into the world for this, that he might comfort and
relieve his brother in adversity.

[Proverbs 17:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 17:18</span></span>

Friend - Debtor, for whom being his friend, he becomes surety.

[Proverbs 17:20](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">pro 17:20</span></span>

No good - Shall not get that advantage which he expects.

[Proverbs 17:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">pro 17:22</span></span>

Doth good - Even to the body; it contributes much to bodily health and
vigour. Drieth - Wastes the marrow of the bones, and the moisture and
strength of the body.

[Proverbs 17:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 17:24</span></span>

In the face - His wisdom appears even in his looks. The eyes - Minding
most, those things which are remotest from him, and least concern him.

[Proverbs 17:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro017.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 17:26</span></span>

To strike - Nor to smite magistrates, either with the hand or tongue,
for the execution of justice.
