*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 63](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 63:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 63:1</span></span>

Early - Heb. in the morning, Which implies the doing it with diligence
and speed. Thirsteth - For the enjoyment of thee in thy house and
ordinances. Flesh - The desire of my soul, is so vehement, that my very
body feels the effects of it. No water - In a land where I want the
refreshing waters of the sanctuary.

[Psalms 63:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 63:2</span></span>

To see - To enjoy. Power - The powerful and glorious effects of thy
gracious presence.

[Psalms 63:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 63:5</span></span>

Satisfied - When thou shalt fulfil my earnest desire of enjoying thee in
the sanctuary.

[Psalms 63:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 63:9</span></span>

Shall go - Into the grave.

[Psalms 63:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 63:10</span></span>

Foxes - Their carcases shall become a prey to wild and ravenous
creatures.

[Psalms 63:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa063.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 63:11</span></span>

The king - I who am already anointed king. Every one - That sweareth by
the name of God, in truth, and judgment, and righteousness. Every
sincere servant and worshipper of God. Shall glory - Shall rejoice in my
deliverance.
