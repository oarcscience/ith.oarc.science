### [2 Chronicles Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#001.md)

[2 Chronicles 10:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">ch2 10:4</span></span>

Grievous - It is probable, when Solomon had declined from God, that God
left him to himself to act thus impolitically.

[2 Chronicles 10:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">ch2 10:7</span></span>

If thou be kind, &c. - Moderate counsels are generally best. Gentleness
will do what violence will not do. Good words cost nothing but a little
self - denial, and yet they purchase great things.

[2 Chronicles 10:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2010.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch2 10:16</span></span>

See to thine own house - When public affairs are in a ferment, violent
proceedings do but make ill worse. Many have been driven to the mischief
they did not intend, by being too severely dealt with.
