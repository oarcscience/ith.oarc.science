### [Luke Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#001)

[Luke 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">luk 8:2</span></span>

Mary Magdalene - Or Mary of Magdala, a town in Galilee: probably the
person mentioned in the last chapter.

[Luke 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">luk 8:4</span></span>

[Mat 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#001); [Mar
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#001).

[Luke 8:15](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">luk 8:15</span></span>

Who - keep it - Not like the highway side: And bring forth fruit - Not
like the thorny ground: With perseverance - Not like the stony.

[Luke 8:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">luk 8:16</span></span>

No man having lighted a candle - As if ho had said, And let your good
fruit appear openly. [Mat 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#015); [Mar
4:21](../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#021); [Luk
11:33](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#033).

[Luke 8:17](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">luk 8:17</span></span>

For nothing is hid - Strive not to conceal it at all; for you can
conceal nothing long. [Mat 10:26](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#026); [Mar
4:22](../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#022); [Luk
12:2](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#002).

[Luke 8:18](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">luk 8:18</span></span>

The word commonly translated seemeth, wherever it occurs, does not
weaken, but greatly strengthens the sense. [Mat
13:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#012); [Mar
4:25](../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#025); [Luk
19:26](../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#026).

[Luke 8:19](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">luk 8:19</span></span>

[Mat 12:46](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#046); [Mar
3:31](../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#031).

[Luke 8:22](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">luk 8:22</span></span>

[Mat 8:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#023); [Mar
4:35](../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#035).

[Luke 8:26](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">luk 8:26</span></span>

[Mat 8:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#028); [Mar
5:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#001).

[Luke 8:29](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">luk 8:29</span></span>

For many times it had caught him - Therefore our compassionate Lord made
the more haste to cast him out.

[Luke 8:31](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">luk 8:31</span></span>

The abyss - That is, the bottomless pit.

[Luke 8:32](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#032)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_032">luk 8:32</span></span>

To enter into the swine - Not that they were any easier in the swine
than out of them. Had it been so, they would not so soon have dislodged
themselves, by destroying the herd.

[Luke 8:52](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#052)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_052">luk 8:52</span></span>

She is not dead but sleepeth - Her soul is not separated finally from
the body; and this short separation is rather to be called sleep than
death.
