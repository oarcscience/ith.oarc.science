*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

### [Jeremiah Chapter 36](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 36:2](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jer 36:2</span></span>

A roll - Parchments, which anciently were their books. All the words -
All the revelations he had from God for twenty - two years last past.
God would have them recorded, that there might be a memorial of them,
that so the truth of them might appear, when God should bring them to
pass; the time of which now drew near.

[Jeremiah 36:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">jer 36:6</span></span>

Upon the fasting day - It was undoubtedly, because of the concourse of
people which the prophet knew would that day be in the temple, that he
chose that day, when some would be present from all parts of Judah.

[Jeremiah 36:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">jer 36:10</span></span>

Then read - Most likely out of some window, or balcony, the people being
below, and hearing it.

[Jeremiah 36:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">jer 36:18</span></span>

He pronounced - This could not but add to the princes fear, they must
needs conceive that without a special influence of God, it had been
impossible, that Jeremiah should have called to mind all that he had
spoken at several times in so many years.

[Jeremiah 36:22](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">jer 36:22</span></span>

The ninth month - Answered to part of our November and December.

[Jeremiah 36:23](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">jer 36:23</span></span>

He - The king not having patience to hear above three or four columns,
or periods, cut it in pieces and burned it in the fire.

[Jeremiah 36:25](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">jer 36:25</span></span>

Elnathan - These princes seemed to have had a greater dread of God upon
their hearts than the rest.

[Jeremiah 36:26](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">jer 36:26</span></span>

Hid them - God by his providence kept them both out of their hands;
directing them to find such a place of recess as the kings messengers
could not find out.

[Jeremiah 36:30](../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">jer 36:30</span></span>

None to sit - That is, none that shall be king any considerable time.
Jeconiah his son was set up, but kept his throne but three months.
