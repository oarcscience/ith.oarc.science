### [Psalms Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 43:1</span></span>

Nation - So he calls the company of his enemies for their great numbers.
Man - Probably Achitophel or Absalom.

[Psalms 43:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 43:3</span></span>

Send out - That is, actually discover them. Truth - Thy favour, or the
light of thy countenance, and the truth of thy promises made to me; or
the true - light, the illumination of thy spirit, and the direction of
thy gracious providence, whereby I may be led in the right way, to thy
holy hill. Hill - Of Zion, the place of God's presence and worship.
