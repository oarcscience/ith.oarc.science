*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 26](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001)

[Psalms 26:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 26:1</span></span>

Trusted - I have committed my cause and affairs to thee.

[Psalms 26:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 26:3</span></span>

For - I dare appeal to thee, because thou knowest I have a deep sense of
thy loving - kindness, by which I have been led to love and obey thee.

[Psalms 26:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 26:4</span></span>

Sat - Continued with them. I have been so far from an imitation of their
wicked courses, that I have avoided their company. Vain - With false and
deceitful persons. Go in - Into their company.

[Psalms 26:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 26:6</span></span>

Compass - Approach to thine altar with my sacrifices: which I could not
do with any comfort, if I were conscious of those crimes, whereof mine
enemies accuse me.

[Psalms 26:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 26:8</span></span>

House - Thy sanctuary and worship. Honour - Thy glorious and gracious
presence.

[Psalms 26:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 26:9</span></span>

Gather not - Do not bind me up in the same bundle, or put me into the
same accursed condition with them.

[Psalms 26:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 26:12</span></span>

Standeth - I stand upon a sure and solid foundation, being under the
protection of God's promise, and his almighty and watchful providence.
Congregations - I will not only privately, but in the assemblies of thy
people celebrate thy praise.
