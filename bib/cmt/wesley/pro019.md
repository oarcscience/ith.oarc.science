*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

### [Proverbs Chapter 19](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 19:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">pro 19:2</span></span>

Hasteth - That rashly and headily rushes into actions.

[Proverbs 19:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 19:3</span></span>

Perverteth - Blasts his designs and enterprises. Fretteth - He ascribes
his unhappiness not to his own sin, but to God and his providence.

[Proverbs 19:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 19:6</span></span>

A friend - in profession.

[Proverbs 19:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 19:7</span></span>

Brethren - His nearest relations. Hate - Despise and shun him. Pursueth
- Earnestly imploring their pity.

[Proverbs 19:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 19:10</span></span>

Delight - To live in pleasure and outward glory, doth not become him,
nor suit with him; because prosperity corrupts even wise men, and makes
fools mad; and because it gives him more opportunity to discover his
folly, and to do mischief both to himself and others.

[Proverbs 19:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 19:13</span></span>

Dropping - Are like rain continually dropping upon an house.

[Proverbs 19:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">pro 19:15</span></span>

Casteth - Makes a man careless, and like one asleep in his business.

[Proverbs 19:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 19:16</span></span>

His ways - The commands of God.

[Proverbs 19:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 19:18</span></span>

While - Before custom in sin, and thy indulgence have made him
incorrigible. Let not - Forbear not to give him due and necessary
correction.

[Proverbs 19:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 19:19</span></span>

Great wrath - Of strong passions. Deliver - If thou forbear to punish
him. Do it - Thou must deliver him again and again.

[Proverbs 19:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">pro 19:22</span></span>

The desire - The hearty desire of being kind is accepted by God, and
should be owned by men as a real kindness. A poor man - Who is not able
to give what he desires to do. A liar - Than a rich man who feeds the
poor with fair promises, but doth not perform what he pretends.

[Proverbs 19:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 19:23</span></span>

Satisfied - Shall be fully contented with God's favour and blessing.

[Proverbs 19:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 19:24</span></span>

Bring it - To feed himself; he expects that the meat should drop into
his mouth.

[Proverbs 19:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 19:25</span></span>

Scorner - An obstinate and impudent transgressor. Simple - Who sin thro'
ignorance, being possibly drawn to it by the scorner's evil counsel or
example. Reprove - A verbal reproof will be more effectual for his
reformation, than the severest punishments will be to a scorner.

[Proverbs 19:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 19:26</span></span>

Wasteth - His father's estate. Chaseth - Causes her to avoid his
presence, and to go from the house where he is.

[Proverbs 19:28](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">pro 19:28</span></span>

Scorneth - Hath no reverence to the place of justice, nor to the
presence of God there. Devoureth - Utters it with greediness and
delight.

[Proverbs 19:29](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">pro 19:29</span></span>

Prepared - Although they be deferred for a time, yet they are treasured
up for them.
