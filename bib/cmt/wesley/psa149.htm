Psalms Chapter 149

Psalms 149:4

psa 149:4

The Lord - He rejoiceth over them to do them good. Beautify - Heb. adorn, make them amiable and honourable in the eyes of the world, who now hate and despise them. The meek - All true Israelites are such.

Psalms 149:5

psa 149:5

In glory - For the honour which God putteth upon them. Beds - By night as well as by day.

Psalms 149:7

psa 149:7

Vengeance - For all their cruelties and injuries towards God's people. This was literally accomplished by David upon the Philistines, Ammonites, Syrians and other neighbouring nations.

Psalms 149:9

psa 149:9

Written - Appointed and declared in the holy scripture.
