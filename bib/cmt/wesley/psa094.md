*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Psalms Chapter 94](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#001)

[Psalms 94:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 94:4</span></span>

Utter - Or pour forth freely, constantly, abundantly, as a fountain doth
waters (so this Hebrew word signifies.) Hard things - Insolent, and
intolerable words against thee and thy people.

[Psalms 94:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 94:7</span></span>

Of Jacob - He who takes that name to himself, but has no regard to his
people.

[Psalms 94:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 94:9</span></span>

Planted - The word is emphatical, signifying the excellent structure of
the ear, or the several organs belonging to the sense of hearing. Formed
- By which word he intimates the accurate and curious workmanship of the
eye.

[Psalms 94:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 94:10</span></span>

Know - Mens thoughts and words and actions.

[Psalms 94:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 94:11</span></span>

Thoughts - Yes, he knoweth all things, even the most secret things, as
the thoughts of men; and in particular your thoughts; much more your
practices.

[Psalms 94:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 94:12</span></span>

Blessed - Those afflictions which are accompanied with Divine
instructions are great and true blessings.

[Psalms 94:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 94:15</span></span>

But - God will declare himself to be a righteous judge, and will again
establish justice in the earth. Follow - They will all approve of it,
and imitate this justice of God in all their actions.

[Psalms 94:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">psa 94:16</span></span>

Rise - To defend and help me. I looked hither and thither, but none
appeared; God alone helped me.

[Psalms 94:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">psa 94:17</span></span>

Dwelt - In the place of silence, the grave.

[Psalms 94:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">psa 94:19</span></span>

Thoughts - While my heart was filled with various and perplexing
thoughts, as this Hebrew word signifies. Comforts - Thy promises, and
the remembrance of my former experience of thy care and kindness.

[Psalms 94:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">psa 94:20</span></span>

Shall - Wilt thou take part with the unrighteous powers of the world who
oppress thy people. A law - By virtue of those unrighteous decrees which
they have made.
