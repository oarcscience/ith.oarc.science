### [1 Chronicles Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 10:10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">ch1 10:10</span></span>

And they put his armour in the house of their gods, and fastened his
head in the temple of (a) Dagon.

\(a\) Which was the idol of the Philistines, and from the belly downward
had the form of a fish, and upward of a man.
