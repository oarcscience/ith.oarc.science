### [Proverbs Chapter 23](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 23:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 23:1</span></span>

When thou sittest to eat with a ruler, (a) consider diligently what
\[is\] before thee:

\(a\) Eat with sobriety.

[Proverbs 23:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">pro 23:2</span></span>

\(b\) And put a knife to thy throat, if thou \[art\] a man given to
appetite.

\(b\) Bridle your appetite, as if by force and violence.

[Proverbs 23:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 23:3</span></span>

Be not desirous of his dainties: (c) for they \[are\] deceitful food.

\(c\) For often the rich when they bid their inferiors to their tables,
it is not for the love they bear them, but for their own secret
purposes.

[Proverbs 23:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 23:4</span></span>

Labour not to be rich: cease from thy own (d) wisdom.

\(d\) Bestow not the gifts that God has given you, to get worldly
riches.

[Proverbs 23:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 23:6</span></span>

Eat thou not the bread of \[him that hath\] an (e) evil eye, neither
desire thou his delicacies:

\(e\) That is, covetous, as contrary a good eye is taken for liberal as
in ([Pro 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#009).md).

[Proverbs 23:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 23:8</span></span>

The (f) morsel \[which\] thou hast eaten shalt thou vomit up, and lose
thy sweet words.

\(f\) He will not cease till he has done you some harm, and his
flattering words will come to no use.

[Proverbs 23:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">pro 23:14</span></span>

Thou shalt beat him with the rod, and shalt deliver his soul from (g)
hell.

\(g\) That is, from destruction.

[Proverbs 23:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 23:18</span></span>

For surely there is an end; (h) and thy expectation shall not be cut
off.

\(h\) The prosperity of the wicked will not continue.

[Proverbs 23:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 23:19</span></span>

Hear thou, my son, and be wise, and guide thy heart in the (i) way.

\(i\) In the observation of God's commandments.

[Proverbs 23:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 23:23</span></span>

Buy (k) the truth, and sell \[it\] not; \[also\] wisdom, and
instruction, and understanding.

\(k\) Spare no cost for truths sake, neither depart from it for any
gain.

[Proverbs 23:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 23:26</span></span>

My son, give me (l) thy heart, and let thy eyes observe my ways.

\(l\) Give yourself wholly to wisdom.

[Proverbs 23:28](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">pro 23:28</span></span>

She also lieth in wait as \[for\] a prey, (m) and increaseth the
transgressors among men.

\(m\) She seduces many and causes them to offend God.

[Proverbs 23:30](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#030.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">pro 23:30</span></span>

They that tarry long at the wine; they that go (n) to seek mixed wine.

\(n\) Who by art make wine stronger and more pleasant.

[Proverbs 23:33](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#033.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">pro 23:33</span></span>

Thy (o) eyes shall behold strange women, and thy heart shall utter
perverse things.

\(o\) That is, drunkenness will bring you to whoredom.

[Proverbs 23:34](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#034.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">pro 23:34</span></span>

And thou shalt be as he that lieth down in the midst of the (p) sea, or
as he that lieth upon the top of a mast.

\(p\) In such great danger will you be.

[Proverbs 23:35](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#035.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">pro 23:35</span></span>

They have stricken me, \[shalt thou say, and\] I was not sick; they have
beaten me, \[and\] I felt \[it\] not: when shall I awake? I will (q)
seek it yet again.

\(q\) Though drunkenness makes them more insensible then beasts, yet
they can not refrain.
