### [Judges Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#001)

[Judges 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jdg 5:2</span></span>

Praise ye the LORD for the avenging of Israel, when the (a) people
willingly offered themselves.

\(a\) That is, the two tribes of Zebulun and Naphtali.

[Judges 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">jdg 5:6</span></span>

In the days of Shamgar the son of Anath, in the days of Jael, the
highways were (b) unoccupied, and the travellers walked through byways.

\(b\) For fear of the enemies.

[Judges 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">jdg 5:7</span></span>

\[The inhabitants of\] the villages ceased, they ceased in Israel, until
that I Deborah arose, that I arose a (c) mother in Israel.

\(c\) Miraculously stirred up by God to pity them and deliver them.

[Judges 5:8](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">jdg 5:8</span></span>

They chose new gods; then \[was\] war in the gates: was there a (d)
shield or spear seen among forty thousand in Israel?

\(d\) They had no heart to resist their enemies.

[Judges 5:10](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">jdg 5:10</span></span>

Speak, ye that ride on (e) white asses, ye that sit (f) in judgment, and
walk by the way.

\(e\) You governors.

\(f\) Or by Middin, as in danger of your enemies.

[Judges 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">jdg 5:12</span></span>

Awake, awake, Deborah: awake, awake, utter a song: arise, Barak, and
lead (h) thy captivity captive, thou son of Abinoam.

\(h\) That is, they who kept your people in captivity.

[Judges 5:14](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">jdg 5:14</span></span>

Out of Ephraim (i) \[was there\] a root of them against Amalek; after
thee, Benjamin, among thy people; out of Machir came down governors, and
out of Zebulun they that handle the pen of the writer.

\(i\) Joshua first fought against Amalek, and Saul destroyed him.

[Judges 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">jdg 5:15</span></span>

And the princes of Issachar \[were\] with Deborah; even (l) Issachar,
and also Barak: he was sent on foot into the valley. For the divisions
of Reuben \[there were\] great (m) thoughts of heart.

\(l\) Even the whole tribe.

\(m\) They marvelled that they did not cross the Jordan to help them.

[Judges 5:17](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">jdg 5:17</span></span>

\(n\) Gilead abode beyond Jordan: and why did Dan remain in ships? Asher
continued on the sea shore, and abode in his (o) breaches.

\(n\) She reproves all those who did not come to help their brethren in
their time of need.

\(o\) Either by hearing of the sea, or by mining,

[Judges 5:19](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">jdg 5:19</span></span>

The kings came \[and\] fought, then fought the kings of Canaan in
Taanach by the waters of Megiddo; they took no gain of (p) money.

\(p\) They won nothing, but lost all.

[Judges 5:21](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">jdg 5:21</span></span>

The river of Kishon (q) swept them away, that ancient river, the river
Kishon. O my soul, thou hast trodden down strength.

\(q\) As a broom does to the filth of the house.

[Judges 5:23](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">jdg 5:23</span></span>

Curse ye (r) Meroz, said the angel of the LORD, curse ye bitterly the
inhabitants thereof; because they came not to the help of the LORD, to
the help of the LORD against the mighty.

\(r\) It was a city near Tabor, where they fought.

[Judges 5:25](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">jdg 5:25</span></span>

He asked water, \[and\] she gave \[him\] milk; she brought forth (s)
butter in a lordly dish.

\(s\) Some read churned milk in a great cup.

[Judges 5:29](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">jdg 5:29</span></span>

Her wise ladies answered her, yea, (t) she returned answer to herself,

\(t\) That is, she comforted herself.

[Judges 5:30](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">jdg 5:30</span></span>

Have they not sped? have they \[not\] divided the prey; to every man a
damsel \[or\] two; to Sisera a prey of divers colours, a prey of divers
colours of needlework, of divers colours of needlework on both sides,
\[meet\] (u) for the necks of \[them that take\] the spoil?

\(u\) Because he was chief of the army.

[Judges 5:31](../../../bib/kjv/jdg005.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">jdg 5:31</span></span>

So let all thine enemies perish, O LORD: but \[let\] them that love him
\[be\] as the (x) sun when he goeth forth in his might. And the land had
rest forty years.

\(x\) Shall grow daily more and more in God's favour.
