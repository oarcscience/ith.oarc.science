*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Numbers Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#001)

[Numbers 10:2](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">num 10:2</span></span>

Make thee two trumpets of silver; of a (a) whole piece shalt thou make
them: that thou mayest use them for the calling of the assembly, and for
the journeying of the camps.

\(a\) Or, of work beaten out with the hammer.

[Numbers 10:5](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">num 10:5</span></span>

When ye blow an alarm, then the camps that lie on the (b) east parts
shall go forward.

\(b\) That is, the host of Judah and they that are under his ensign.

[Numbers 10:6](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">num 10:6</span></span>

When ye blow an alarm the second time, then the camps that lie on the
(c) south side shall take their journey: they shall blow an alarm for
their journeys.

\(c\) Meaning, the heart of Reuben.

[Numbers 10:8](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">num 10:8</span></span>

And the sons of Aaron, the priests, shall (d) blow with the trumpets;
and they shall be to you for an ordinance for ever throughout your
generations.

\(d\) So that only the priests must blow the trumpets, as long as the
priesthood lasted.

[Numbers 10:10](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">num 10:10</span></span>

Also in the day of your (e) gladness, and in your solemn days, and in
the beginnings of your months, ye shall blow with the trumpets over your
burnt offerings, and over the sacrifices of your peace offerings; that
they may be to you for a memorial before your God: I \[am\] the LORD
your God.

\(e\) When you rejoice that God has removed any plague.

[Numbers 10:13](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">num 10:13</span></span>

And they (f) first took their journey according to the commandment of
the LORD by the hand of Moses.

\(f\) From Sinai to Paran, ([Num
33:1](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#001)).

[Numbers 10:17](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">num 10:17</span></span>

And the tabernacle was taken down; and the sons of Gershon and the sons
of Merari set forward, bearing (g) the tabernacle.

\(g\) With all the belongings of it.

[Numbers 10:21](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">num 10:21</span></span>

And the Kohathites set forward, (h) bearing the sanctuary: and (i) \[the
other\] did set up the tabernacle against they came.

\(h\) Upon their shoulders.

\(i\) The Merarites and Gershonites.

[Numbers 10:25](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">num 10:25</span></span>

And the standard of the camp of the children of Dan set forward, \[which
was\] the (k) rereward of all the camps throughout their hosts: and over
his host \[was\] Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai.

\(k\) Leaving none behind, nor any of the former that fainted in the
way.

[Numbers 10:28](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">num 10:28</span></span>

\(l\) Thus \[were\] the journeyings of the children of Israel according
to their armies, when they set forward.

\(l\) This was the order of their host when they travelled.

[Numbers 10:29](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">num 10:29</span></span>

And Moses said unto (m) Hobab, the son of Raguel the Midianite, Moses'
father in law, We are journeying unto the place of which the LORD said,
I will give it you: come thou with us, and we will do thee good: for the
LORD hath spoken good concerning Israel.

\(m\) Some think that Reuel, Jethro, Hobab, and Keni were all one: Kimhi
says that Reuel was Jethro's father: so Hobab was Moses father-in-law,
see ([Exo 2:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#018), [Exo
3:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#001), [Exo
4:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#018), [Exo
18:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#001); [Jdg
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/jdg004.htm#011))

[Numbers 10:33](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">num 10:33</span></span>

And they departed from the (n) mount of the LORD three days' journey:
and the ark of the covenant of the LORD went before them in the three
days' journey, to search out a resting place for them.

\(n\) Mount Sinai, or Horeb.

[Numbers 10:35](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">num 10:35</span></span>

And it came to pass, when the ark set forward, that Moses said, (o) Rise
up, LORD, and let thine enemies be scattered; and let them that hate
thee flee before thee.

\(o\) Declare your might and power.
