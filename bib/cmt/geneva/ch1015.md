### [1 Chronicles Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 15:1</span></span>

And \[David\] made him houses in the (a) city of David, and prepared a
place for the ark of God, and pitched for it a tent.

\(a\) That was in Zion ([Sa2 5:7,9](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#007).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">ch1 15:3</span></span>

And David gathered all Israel together to Jerusalem, to bring up the (b)
ark of the LORD unto his place, which he had prepared for it.

\(b\) From the house of Obed Edom, ([Sa2
6:10](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#010.md), [Sa2
6:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#012).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">ch1 15:8</span></span>

Of the sons of (c) Elizaphan; Shemaiah the chief, and his brethren two
hundred:

\(c\) Who was the son of Uzziel, the fourth son of Kohath, ([Exo
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#018.md), [Exo
6:22](../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#022.md); [Num
3:30](../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#030).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:9](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">ch1 15:9</span></span>

Of the sons of (d) Hebron; Eliel the chief, and his brethren fourscore:

\(d\) The third son of Kohath, ([Exo
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#018).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:12](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">ch1 15:12</span></span>

And said unto them, Ye \[are\] the chief of the fathers of the Levites:
(e) sanctify yourselves, \[both\] ye and your brethren, that ye may
bring up the ark of the LORD God of Israel unto \[the place that\] I
have prepared for it.

\(e\) Prepare yourselves and be pure, abstain from all things by which
you might be polluted, and so not able to come to the tabernacle.

[1 Chronicles 15:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">ch1 15:13</span></span>

For because ye \[did it\] not at the first, the LORD our God made a
breach upon us, for that we sought him not after the due (f) order.

\(f\) According as he has appointed in the law.

[1 Chronicles 15:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch1 15:16</span></span>

And David spake to the chief of the Levites to appoint their brethren
\[to be\] the singers with (g) instruments of musick, psalteries and
harps and cymbals, sounding, by lifting up the voice with joy.

\(g\) These instruments and other ceremonies which they observed, were
instructions of their infancy, which continued to the coming of Christ.

[1 Chronicles 15:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">ch1 15:18</span></span>

And with them their brethren of the (h) second \[degree\], Zechariah,
Ben, and Jaaziel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Unni, Eliab, and
Benaiah, and Maaseiah, and Mattithiah, and Elipheleh, and Mikneiah, and
Obededom, and Jeiel, the porters.

\(h\) Which were inferior in dignity.

[1 Chronicles 15:20](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">ch1 15:20</span></span>

And Zechariah, and Aziel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Unni, and
Eliab, and Maaseiah, and Benaiah, with psalteries on (i) Alamoth;

\(i\) This was an instrument of music or a certain tune, to which they
sang psalms.

[1 Chronicles 15:21](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">ch1 15:21</span></span>

And Mattithiah, and Elipheleh, and Mikneiah, and Obededom, and Jeiel,
and Azaziah, with harps on the (k) Sheminith to excel.

\(k\) Which was the eighth tune, over which he that was most excellent
had charge.

[1 Chronicles 15:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">ch1 15:22</span></span>

And Chenaniah, chief of the Levites, (l) \[was\] for song: he instructed
about the song, because he \[was\] skilful.

\(l\) That is, to appoint Psalms and songs to them that sang.

[1 Chronicles 15:24](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">ch1 15:24</span></span>

And Shebaniah, and Jehoshaphat, and Nethaneel, and Amasai, and
Zechariah, and Benaiah, and Eliezer, the priests, did blow with the
trumpets before the ark of God: and Obededom and Jehiah \[were\]
doorkeepers (m) for the ark.

\(m\) With Berechiah and Elkanah, ([Ch1
15:23](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#023).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:26](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">ch1 15:26</span></span>

And it came to pass, when God (n) helped the Levites that bare the ark
of the covenant of the LORD, that they offered (o) seven bullocks and
seven rams.

\(n\) That is, gave them strength to execute their office.

\(o\) Besides the bullock and the fat beast which David offered at every
sixth pace, ([Sa2 6:13](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#013).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:27](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#027.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">ch1 15:27</span></span>

And David \[was\] clothed with a robe of fine linen, and all the Levites
that bare the ark, and the singers, and Chenaniah the master of the song
with the singers: David also \[had\] upon him an (p) ephod of linen.

\(p\) Read ([Sa2 6:14](../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#014).md).

[1 Chronicles 15:29](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">ch1 15:29</span></span>

And it came to pass, \[as\] the ark of the (q) covenant of the LORD came
to the city of David, that Michal the daughter of Saul looking out at a
window saw king David dancing and playing: and she despised him in her
heart.

\(q\) It was so called because it reminded the Israelites of the Lord's
covenant made with them.
