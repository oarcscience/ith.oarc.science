*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [1 Chronicles Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 9:1</span></span>

So all Israel were reckoned by genealogies; and, behold, they \[were\]
written in the book of the kings of Israel and Judah, \[who\] were (a)
carried away to Babylon for their transgression.

\(a\) Until now he has described their genealogies before they went into
captivity, and now he describes their history after their return.

[1 Chronicles 9:2](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">ch1 9:2</span></span>

Now the first inhabitants that \[dwelt\] in their possessions in their
cities \[were\], the Israelites, the priests, Levites, and the (b)
Nethinims.

\(b\) Meaning, the Gibeonites, who served in the temple, read ([Jos
9:23](../../../bib/kjv/jos009.htm#023).md).

[1 Chronicles 9:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">ch1 9:11</span></span>

And Azariah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok,
the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, the (c) ruler of the house of
God;

\(c\) That is, he was the high priest.

[1 Chronicles 9:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">ch1 9:13</span></span>

And their brethren, heads of the house of their fathers, a thousand and
seven hundred and threescore; very able men for the (d) work of the
service of the house of God.

\(d\) To serve in the temple, every one according to his office.

[1 Chronicles 9:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">ch1 9:18</span></span>

Who hitherto \[waited\] in the (e) king's gate eastward: they \[were\]
porters in the companies of the children of Levi.

\(e\) So called because the king came into the temple by it, and not the
common people.

[1 Chronicles 9:19](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">ch1 9:19</span></span>

And Shallum the son of Kore, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah, and
his brethren, of the house of his father, the Korahites, \[were\] over
the work of the service, keepers of the gates of the (f) tabernacle: and
their fathers, \[being\] over the host of the LORD, \[were\] keepers of
the entry.

\(f\) Their charge was that no one should enter those places, which were
only appointed for the priests to minister in.

[1 Chronicles 9:25](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">ch1 9:25</span></span>

And their brethren, \[which were\] in their villages, \[were\] to come
after (g) seven days from time to time with them.

\(g\) They served weekly, as in ([Exo
29:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#030).md).

[1 Chronicles 9:29](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">ch1 9:29</span></span>

\[Some\] of them also \[were\] appointed to oversee the vessels, and all
the instruments of the sanctuary, and the fine (h) flour, and the wine,
and the oil, and the frankincense, and the spices.

\(h\) Of which the meat offering was made, ([Lev
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/lev002.htm#008).md).

[1 Chronicles 9:33](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#033.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">ch1 9:33</span></span>

And these \[are\] the singers, chief of the fathers of the Levites,
\[who remaining\] in the chambers (i) \[were\] free: for they were
employed in \[that\] work day and night.

\(i\) But were continually occupied in singing praises to God.

[1 Chronicles 9:42](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#042.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_042">ch1 9:42</span></span>

And Ahaz begat (k) Jarah; and Jarah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and
Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza;

\(k\) Who was also called Jehoiada, ([Ch1
8:36](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#036).md).
