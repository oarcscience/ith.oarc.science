Exodus Chapter 37

Exodus 37:2

exo 37:2

And he overlaid it with pure gold within and without, and made a (a) crown of gold to it round about.

(a) Like battlements.

Exodus 37:8

exo 37:8

One cherub on the end on this side, and another cherub on the [other] end on that side: out (b) of the mercy seat made he the cherubims on the two ends thereof.

(b) Of the same material that the mercyseat was.

Exodus 37:24

exo 37:24

[Of] a (c) talent of pure gold made he it, and all the vessels thereof.

(c) See (Exo 25:39).
