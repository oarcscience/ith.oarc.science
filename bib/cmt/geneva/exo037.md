### [Exodus Chapter 37](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001.md)

[Exodus 37:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">exo 37:2</span></span>

And he overlaid it with pure gold within and without, and made a (a)
crown of gold to it round about.

\(a\) Like battlements.

[Exodus 37:8](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">exo 37:8</span></span>

One cherub on the end on this side, and another cherub on the \[other\]
end on that side: out (b) of the mercy seat made he the cherubims on the
two ends thereof.

\(b\) Of the same material that the mercyseat was.

[Exodus 37:24](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">exo 37:24</span></span>

\[Of\] a (c) talent of pure gold made he it, and all the vessels
thereof.

\(c\) See ([Exo 25:39](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#039).md).
