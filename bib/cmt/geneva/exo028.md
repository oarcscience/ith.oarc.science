### [Exodus Chapter 28](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#001.md)

[Exodus 28:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">exo 28:2</span></span>

And thou shalt make holy garments for Aaron thy brother for (a) glory
and for beauty.

\(a\) By which his office may be known to be glorious and excellent.

[Exodus 28:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">exo 28:3</span></span>

And thou shalt speak unto all \[that are\] wise hearted, whom I have
filled with the spirit of wisdom, that they may make Aaron's garments to
(b) consecrate him, that he may minister unto me in the priest's office.

\(b\) Which is to separate him from the rest.

[Exodus 28:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">exo 28:4</span></span>

And these \[are\] the garments which they shall make; a breastplate, and
an (c) ephod, and a robe, and a broidered coat, a mitre, and a girdle:
and they shall make holy garments for Aaron thy brother, and his sons,
that he may minister unto me in the priest's office.

\(c\) A short an straight coat without sleeves, put on top of his
garments to keep them close to him.

[Exodus 28:8](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">exo 28:8</span></span>

And the (d) curious girdle of the ephod, which \[is\] upon it, shall be
of the same, according to the work thereof; \[even of\] gold, \[of\]
blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen.

\(d\) Which went about his upmost coat.

[Exodus 28:10](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">exo 28:10</span></span>

Six of their names on one stone, and \[the other\] six names of the rest
on the other stone, according to (e) their birth.

\(e\) As they were in age, so should they be graven in order.

[Exodus 28:12](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">exo 28:12</span></span>

And thou shalt put the two stones upon the shoulders of the ephod
\[for\] stones of (f) memorial unto the children of Israel: and Aaron
shall bear their names before the LORD upon his two shoulders for a
memorial.

\(f\) That Aaron might remind the Israelites of God.

[Exodus 28:14](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">exo 28:14</span></span>

And two chains \[of\] pure gold (g) at the ends; \[of\] wreathen work
shalt thou make them, and fasten the wreathen chains to the ouches.

\(g\) Of the bosses.

[Exodus 28:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">exo 28:15</span></span>

And thou shalt make the breastplate of (h) judgment with cunning work;
after the work of the ephod thou shalt make it; \[of\] gold, \[of\]
blue, and \[of\] purple, and \[of\] scarlet, and \[of\] fine twined
linen, shalt thou make it.

\(h\) It was so called, because the high priest could not give sentence
in judgment without that on his breast.

[Exodus 28:16](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">exo 28:16</span></span>

\(i\) Foursquare it shall be \[being\] doubled; a span \[shall be\] the
length thereof, and a span \[shall be\] the breadth thereof.

\(i\) The description of the breastplate.

[Exodus 28:23](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">exo 28:23</span></span>

And thou shalt make upon the breastplate two rings of gold, and shalt
put the two rings on (k) the two ends of the breastplate.

\(k\) Which are upmost toward the shoulder.

[Exodus 28:26](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">exo 28:26</span></span>

And thou shalt make two rings of gold, and thou shalt put them upon the
(l) two ends of the breastplate in the border thereof, which \[is\] in
the side of the ephod inward.

\(l\) Which are beneath.

[Exodus 28:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">exo 28:29</span></span>

And Aaron shall bear the names of the children of Israel in the
breastplate of judgment upon his heart, when he goeth in unto the holy
\[place\], for a memorial before the LORD continually.

\(m\) Aaron will not enter into the holy place in his own name, but in
the name of all the children of Israel.

[Exodus 28:30](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#030.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">exo 28:30</span></span>

And thou shalt put in the breastplate of judgment the (n) Urim and the
Thummim; and they shall be upon Aaron's heart, when he goeth in before
the LORD: and Aaron shall bear the judgment of the children of Israel
upon his heart before the LORD continually.

\(n\) Urim signifies light, and thummim perfection: declaring that the
stones of the breastplate were most clear, and of perfect beauty: by
urim also is meant knowledge, and thummim holiness, showing what virtues
are required in the priests.

[Exodus 28:36](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#036.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">exo 28:36</span></span>

And thou shalt make a plate \[of\] pure gold, and grave upon it,
\[like\] the engravings of a signet, (o) HOLINESS TO THE LORD.

\(o\) Holiness belongs to the Lord: for he is most holy, and nothing
unholy may appear before him.

[Exodus 28:38](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#038.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_038">exo 28:38</span></span>

And it shall be upon Aaron's forehead, that Aaron may (p) bear the
iniquity of the holy things, which the children of Israel shall hallow
in all their holy gifts; and it shall be always upon his forehead, that
they may be accepted before the LORD.

\(p\) Their offerings could not be so perfect, but some fault would be
in them: which sin the high priest bore and pacified God.

[Exodus 28:41](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#041.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_041">exo 28:41</span></span>

And thou shalt put them upon Aaron thy brother, and his sons with him;
and shalt anoint them, and (q) consecrate them, and sanctify them, that
they may minister unto me in the priest's office.

\(q\) Or "fill their hands", by giving them things to offer and
therefore admit them to their office.

[Exodus 28:43](../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#043.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_043">exo 28:43</span></span>

And they shall be upon Aaron, and upon his sons, when they come in unto
the tabernacle of the congregation, or when they come near unto the
altar to minister in the holy \[place\]; that they (r) bear not
iniquity, and die: \[it shall be\] a statute for ever unto him and his
seed after him.

\(r\) In not hiding their nakedness.
