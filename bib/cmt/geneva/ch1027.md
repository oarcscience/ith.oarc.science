### [1 Chronicles Chapter 27](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#001)

[1 Chronicles 27:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 27:1</span></span>

Now the children of Israel after their number, \[to wit\], the chief
fathers and captains of thousands and hundreds, and their officers that
served the king in any matter of the courses, (a) which came in and went
out month by month throughout all the months of the year, of every
course \[were\] twenty and four thousand.

\(a\) Who executed their charge and office, which is meant by coming in
and going out.

[1 Chronicles 27:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">ch1 27:4</span></span>

And over the course of the second month \[was\] Dodai an Ahohite, and of
his course \[was\] Mikloth also the (b) ruler: in his course likewise
\[were\] twenty and four thousand.

\(b\) That is, Dodais lieutenant.

[1 Chronicles 27:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch1 27:16</span></span>

Furthermore (c) over the tribes of Israel: the ruler of the Reubenites
\[was\] Eliezer the son of Zichri: of the Simeonites, Shephatiah the son
of Maachah:

\(c\) Meaning besides those twelve captains.

[1 Chronicles 27:21](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">ch1 27:21</span></span>

Of the (d) half \[tribe\] of Manasseh in Gilead, Iddo the son of
Zechariah: of Benjamin, Jaasiel the son of Abner:

\(d\) Which is beyond Jordan in respect to Judah, also one captain was
over the Reubenites and the Gadites.

[1 Chronicles 27:24](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">ch1 27:24</span></span>

Joab the son of Zeruiah began to number, but he finished not, (e)
because there fell wrath for it against Israel; neither was the number
put in the account of the (f) chronicles of king David.

\(e\) The commandment of the king was abominable to Joab, ([Ch1
21:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1021.htm#006)).

\(f\) The Hebrews make both these books of Chronicles but one, and at
this verse make the middle of the book concerning the number of verses.

[1 Chronicles 27:32](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#032)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_032">ch1 27:32</span></span>

Also Jonathan David's uncle was a counsellor, a wise man, and a (g)
scribe: and Jehiel the son of Hachmoni \[was\] with the king's (h) sons:

\(g\) That is, a man learned in the word of God.

\(h\) To be their schoolmasters and teachers.

[1 Chronicles 27:34](../../../bib/kjv/ch1027.htm#034)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">ch1 27:34</span></span>

And (i) after Ahithophel \[was\] Jehoiada the son of Benaiah, and
Abiathar: and the general of the king's army \[was\] Joab.

\(i\) After Ahithophel hanged himself, ([Sa2
17:23](../../../bib/kjv/sa2017.htm#023)), Jehoiada was made counsellor.
