------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [2 Chronicles Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#001.md)

[2 Chronicles 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch2 11:1</span></span>

And when Rehoboam was come to Jerusalem, he gathered of the house of
Judah and (a) Benjamin an hundred and fourscore thousand chosen \[men\],
which were warriors, to fight against (b) Israel, that he might bring
the kingdom again to Rehoboam.

\(a\) That is, the half tribe of Benjamin for the other half went after
Jeroboam.

\(b\) Meaning the ten tribes who rebelled.

[2 Chronicles 11:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">ch2 11:5</span></span>

And Rehoboam dwelt in Jerusalem, and (c) built cities for defence in
Judah.

\(c\) Or, repaired them and made them strong to be more able to resist
Jeroboam.

[2 Chronicles 11:15](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">ch2 11:15</span></span>

And he ordained him priests for the high places, and for the (d) devils,
and for the calves which he had made.

\(d\) Meaning idols, ([Isa 44:15](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#015).md).

[2 Chronicles 11:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">ch2 11:16</span></span>

And after them out of all the tribes of Israel such as set their (e)
hearts to seek the LORD God of Israel came to Jerusalem, to sacrifice
unto the LORD God of their fathers.

\(e\) Who were zealous of true religion and feared God.

[2 Chronicles 11:17](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">ch2 11:17</span></span>

So they strengthened the kingdom of Judah, and made Rehoboam the son of
Solomon strong, three years: for three years they (f) walked in the way
of David and Solomon.

\(f\) As long as they feared God, and set forth his word, they
prospered.

[2 Chronicles 11:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">ch2 11:22</span></span>

And Rehoboam made (g) Abijah the son of Maachah the chief, \[to be\]
ruler among his brethren: for \[he thought\] to make him king.

\(g\) Called also Abijam, who reigned three years, ([Kg1
15:2](../../../bib/kjv/kg1015.htm#002).md).

[2 Chronicles 11:23](../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">ch2 11:23</span></span>

And he dealt wisely, and dispersed of all his children throughout all
the countries of Judah and Benjamin, unto every fenced city: and he gave
them victual in abundance. And he (h) desired many wives.

\(h\) He gave himself to have many wives.
