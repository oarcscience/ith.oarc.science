### [Song of Solomon (Canticles) Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#001)

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">sol 8:1</span></span>

O (a) that thou \[wert\] as my brother, that was nourished at the
breasts of my mother! \[when\] I should find thee outside, I would kiss
thee; yea, I should not be despised.

\(a\) The Church called of the Gentiles speaks thus to the Church of
Jerusalem.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:3](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">sol 8:3</span></span>

\(b\) His left hand \[should be\] under my head, and his right hand
should embrace me.

\(b\) Read ([Sol 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/sol002.htm#006)).

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">sol 8:4</span></span>

\(c\) I charge you, O daughters of Jerusalem, that ye stir not, nor
awake \[my\] love, until he please.

\(c\) Read ([Sol 3:5](../../../bib/kjv/sol003.htm#005)).

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:6](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">sol 8:6</span></span>

\(d\) Set me as a seal upon thy heart, as a seal upon thy arm: for love
\[is\] strong as death; jealousy \[is\] cruel as the grave: the coals of
it \[are\] coals of fire, \[which hath a\] most vehement flame.

\(d\) The spouse desires Christ to be joined in perpetual love with him.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:8](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">sol 8:8</span></span>

\(e\) We have a little sister, and she hath no breasts: what shall we do
for our sister in the day when she shall be spoken for?

\(e\) The Jewish Church speaks this of the Church of the Gentiles.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:9](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">sol 8:9</span></span>

\(f\) If she \[is\] a wall, we will build upon her a palace of silver:
and if she \[is\] a door, we will inclose her with boards of cedar.

\(f\) If she is sure and fast, she is fit for her husband to dwell in.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:10](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">sol 8:10</span></span>

\(g\) I \[am\] a wall, and my breasts like towers: then was I in his
eyes as one that found favour.

\(g\) The Church promises fidelity and constancy.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:11](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">sol 8:11</span></span>

\(h\) Solomon had a vineyard at Baalhamon; he let out the vineyard to
keepers; every one for the fruit of it was to bring a thousand
\[pieces\] of silver.

\(h\) This is the vineyard of the Lord hired out, ([Mat
21:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#033)).

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:13](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">sol 8:13</span></span>

Thou that dwellest in the (i) gardens, the companions hearken to thy
voice: cause me to hear \[it\].

\(i\) Christ dwells in his Church, whose voice the faithful hear.

[Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:14](../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">sol 8:14</span></span>

\(k\) Make haste, my beloved, and be thou like a roe or a young hart
upon the mountains of spices.

\(k\) The Church desires Christ that if he depart from them, yet he
would haste to help them in their troubles.
