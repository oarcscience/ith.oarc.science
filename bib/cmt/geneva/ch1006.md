*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual
Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [1 Chronicles Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 6:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">ch1 6:8</span></span>

And Ahitub begat (a) Zadok, and Zadok begat Ahimaaz,

\(a\) Who was high priest after Abiathar was deposed, according to the
prophecy of Eli the priest, ([Sa1
2:31](../../../bib/kjv/sa1002.htm#031.md), [Sa1
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/sa1002.htm#035).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">ch1 6:10</span></span>

And Johanan begat Azariah, (he \[it is\] that executed the (b) priest's
office in the temple that Solomon built in Jerusalem:)

\(b\) And valiantly resisted king Uzziah, who would have usurped the
priest's office ([Ch2 26:17-18](../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#017).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:15](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">ch1 6:15</span></span>

And (c) Jehozadak went \[into captivity\], when the LORD carried away
Judah and Jerusalem by the hand of Nebuchadnezzar.

\(c\) That is, he was led into captivity with his father Seraiah the
high priest, ([Kg2 25:18](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#018).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">ch1 6:22</span></span>

The sons of Kohath; (d) Amminadab his son, Korah his son, Assir his son,

\(d\) Who seems to be called Izhar, ([Exo
6:21](../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#021).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:28](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">ch1 6:28</span></span>

And the sons of Samuel; the firstborn (e) Vashni, and Abiah.

\(e\) Who is also called Joel, ([Sa1
8:2](../../../bib/kjv/sa1008.htm#002.md); [Ch1
6:33](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#033).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:31](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#031.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">ch1 6:31</span></span>

And these \[are they\] whom David set over the service of song in the
house of the LORD, after that the ark had (f) rest.

\(f\) After it was brought to the place where the temple would be built
and was no longer carried to and fro.

[1 Chronicles 6:39](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#039.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_039">ch1 6:39</span></span>

And his brother (h) Asaph, who stood on his right hand, \[even\] Asaph
the son of Berachiah, the son of Shimea,

\(h\) Meaning, the son of Heman, ([Ch1
6:33](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#033).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:48](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#048.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_048">ch1 6:48</span></span>

Their (i) brethren also the Levites \[were\] (k) appointed unto all
manner of service of the tabernacle of the house of God.

\(i\) The Levites are called the singers brothers because they came from
the same stock.

\(k\) Read ([Num 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/num004.htm#004).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:54](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#054.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_054">ch1 6:54</span></span>

Now these \[are\] their (l) dwelling places throughout their castles in
their coasts, of the sons of Aaron, of the families of the Kohathites:
for theirs was the (m) lot.

\(l\) Or, cities which were given to the Levites.

\(m\) They were first appointed, and prepared for.

[1 Chronicles 6:55](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#055.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_055">ch1 6:55</span></span>

And they gave them (n) Hebron in the land of Judah, and the suburbs
thereof round about it.

\(n\) Which was also called Kirjatharba, ([Gen
23:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen023.htm#002.md); [Jos
21:11](../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#011).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:57](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#057.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_057">ch1 6:57</span></span>

And to the sons of Aaron they gave the cities of Judah, \[namely\],
Hebron, \[the city\] of (o) refuge, and Libnah with her suburbs, and
Jattir, and Eshtemoa, with their suburbs,

\(o\) That he that had killed a man might flee to it for help till his
case was tried, ([Deu 19:2](../../../bib/kjv/deu019.htm#002).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:58](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#058.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_058">ch1 6:58</span></span>

And (p) Hilen with her suburbs, Debir with her suburbs,

\(p\) Which Joshua called Holon, ([Jos
15:51](../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#051.md), [Jos
21:15](../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#015).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:60](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#060.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_060">ch1 6:60</span></span>

And out of the tribe of Benjamin; Geba with her suburbs, and (q) Alemeth
with her suburbs, and Anathoth with her suburbs. All their cities
throughout their families \[were\] thirteen cities.

\(q\) Or, Almon ([Jos 21:18](../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#018).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:61](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#061.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_061">ch1 6:61</span></span>

And unto the sons of (r) Kohath, \[which were\] left of the family of
that tribe, \[were cities given\] out of the half tribe, \[namely, out
of\] the half \[tribe\] of Manasseh, by lot, ten cities.

\(r\) That is, they gave a portion to the Kohathites, who were the
remnant of the tribe of Levi, out of the half tribe of Manasseh and out
of Ephraim, ([Ch1 6:66](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#066).md).

[1 Chronicles 6:71](../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#071.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_071">ch1 6:71</span></span>

Unto the sons of (s) Gershom \[were given\] out of the family of the
half tribe of Manasseh, Golan in Bashan with her suburbs, and Ashtaroth
with her suburbs:

\(s\) Who in the first verse is also called Gershon.
