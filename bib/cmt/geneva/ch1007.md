------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [1 Chronicles Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#001)

[1 Chronicles 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 7:1</span></span>

Now the sons of Issachar \[were\], Tola, and Puah, (a) Jashub, and
Shimron, four.

\(a\) Who also is called Job, ([Gen
46:13](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#013)).

[1 Chronicles 7:2](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">ch1 7:2</span></span>

And the sons of Tola; Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and
Jibsam, and Shemuel, heads of their father's house, \[to wit\], of Tola:
\[they were\] valiant men of might in their generations; (b) whose
number \[was\] in the days of David two and twenty thousand and six
hundred.

\(b\) That is, their number was found to be this big when David counted
the people, ([Sa2 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#001)).

[1 Chronicles 7:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">ch1 7:3</span></span>

And the sons of Uzzi; Izrahiah: and the sons of Izrahiah; Michael, and
Obadiah, and Joel, Ishiah, (c) five: all of them chief men.

\(c\) Meaning, the four sons and the father.

[1 Chronicles 7:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">ch1 7:6</span></span>

\[The sons\] of Benjamin; Bela, and Becher, and (d) Jediael, (e) three.

\(d\) Also called Ashbel, ([Gen 46:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#021);
[Num 26:38](../../../bib/kjv/num026.htm#038)).

\(e\) Who were the chief: or else there were seven in all as it appears
in ([Gen 46:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#021)).

[1 Chronicles 7:12](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">ch1 7:12</span></span>

Shuppim also, and Huppim, the children of Ir, \[and\] Hushim, the sons
of (f) Aher.

\(f\) Meaning that he was not the son of Benjamin, but of Dan ([Gen
46:23](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#023)).

[1 Chronicles 7:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">ch1 7:13</span></span>

The sons of Naphtali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, (g) the
sons of Bilhah.

\(g\) These came from Dan and Naphtali, who were the sons of Bilhah,
([Gen 46:23-25](../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#023)).

[1 Chronicles 7:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">ch1 7:18</span></span>

And (h) his sister Hammoleketh bare Ishod, and Abiezer, and Mahalah.

\(h\) Meaning, the sister of Gilead.

[1 Chronicles 7:21](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">ch1 7:21</span></span>

And Zabad his son, and Shuthelah his son, and Ezer, and Elead, whom the
men of (i) Gath \[that were\] born in \[that\] land slew, because they
came down to take away their cattle.

\(i\) Which was one of the five principal cities of the Philistines and
who slew the Ephraimites.

[1 Chronicles 7:25](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">ch1 7:25</span></span>

And Rephah \[was\] his (k) son, also Resheph, and Telah his son, and
Tahan his son,

\(k\) That is, of Ephraim.
