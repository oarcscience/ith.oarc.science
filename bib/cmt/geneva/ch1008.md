### [1 Chronicles Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 8:1</span></span>

Now Benjamin (a) begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah
the third,

\(a\) He continues in the description of the tribe of Benjamin, because
his purpose is to set forth the genealogy of Saul.

[1 Chronicles 8:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">ch1 8:6</span></span>

And these \[are\] the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers
of the inhabitants of Geba, and (b) they removed them to Manahath:

\(b\) Meaning, the inhabitants of the city of Geba.

[1 Chronicles 8:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">ch1 8:7</span></span>

And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and (c) begat Uzza,
and Ahihud.

\(c\) That is, Ehud.

[1 Chronicles 8:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">ch1 8:8</span></span>

And Shaharaim begat \[children\] in the country of Moab, after he had
sent them (d) away; Hushim and Baara \[were\] his wives.

\(d\) After he had put away his two wives.

[1 Chronicles 8:28](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#028.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">ch1 8:28</span></span>

These \[were\] heads of the (e) fathers, by their generations, chief
\[men\]. These dwelt in Jerusalem.

\(e\) The chief of the tribe of Benjamin, who dwelt in Jerusalem.

[1 Chronicles 8:33](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#033.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">ch1 8:33</span></span>

And (f) Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan,
and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and (g) Eshbaal.

\(f\) Who in ([Sa1 9:2](../../../bib/kjv/sa1009.htm#002).md) is called
Abiel.

\(g\) He is likewise called Mephibosheth, ([Sa2
9:6](../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#006).md).

[1 Chronicles 8:34](../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#034.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">ch1 8:34</span></span>

And the son of Jonathan \[was\] (h) Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat
Micah.

\(h\) He is also called Mephibosheth, ([Sa2
9:6](../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#006).md).
