*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Numbers Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#001.md)

[Numbers 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">num 24:1</span></span>

And when Balaam saw that it pleased the LORD to bless Israel, he went
not, as at other times, to seek for enchantments, but he set his face
toward the (a) wilderness.

\(a\) Where the Israelites camped.

[Numbers 24:3](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">num 24:3</span></span>

And he took up his parable, and said, Balaam the son of Beor hath said,
and the man whose eyes (b) are open hath said:

\(b\) His eyes were shut up before in respect to the clear visions which
he saw after.

[Numbers 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">num 24:4</span></span>

He hath said, which heard the words of God, which saw the vision of the
Almighty, (c) falling \[into a trance\], but having his eyes open:

\(c\) Though he lay as in a sleep, yet the eyes of his mind were open.

[Numbers 24:7](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">num 24:7</span></span>

He shall pour the (d) water out of his buckets, and his seed \[shall
be\] in many waters, and his king shall be higher than (e) Agag, and his
kingdom shall be exalted.

\(d\) His prosperity and posterity will be very great.

\(e\) Which name was common to the kings of Amalek.

[Numbers 24:10](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">num 24:10</span></span>

And Balak's anger was kindled against Balaam, and he (f) smote his hands
together: and Balak said unto Balaam, I called thee to curse mine
enemies, and, behold, thou hast altogether blessed \[them\] these three
times.

\(f\) In token of anger.

[Numbers 24:11](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">num 24:11</span></span>

Therefore now flee thou to thy place: I thought to promote thee unto
great honour; but, lo, the (g) LORD hath kept thee back from honour.

\(g\) Thus the wicked burden God when they cannot carry out their wicked
enterprises.

[Numbers 24:14](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">num 24:14</span></span>

And now, behold, I go unto my people: come \[therefore, and\] I will (h)
advertise thee what this people shall do to thy people in the latter
days.

\(h\) He gave also wicked counsel to cause the Israelites to sin, that
God might forsake them, ([Num 31:16](../../../bib/kjv/num031.htm#016).md).

[Numbers 24:17](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">num 24:17</span></span>

I shall see him, but not now: I shall behold him, but not nigh: there
shall come a (i) Star out of Jacob, and a Sceptre shall rise out of
Israel, and shall smite the (k) corners of Moab, and destroy all the
children of (l) Sheth.

\(i\) Meaning Christ.

\(k\) That is, the princes.

\(l\) He shall subdue all that resist: for of Sheth came Noah, and of
Noah all the world.

[Numbers 24:19](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">num 24:19</span></span>

Out of Jacob shall come he that shall have dominion, and shall destroy
him that remaineth of the (m) city.

\(m\) Of the Edomites.

[Numbers 24:20](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">num 24:20</span></span>

And when he looked on Amalek, he took up his parable, and said, Amalek
\[was\] the (n) first of the nations; but his latter end \[shall be\]
that he perish for ever.

\(n\) The Amalekites first made war against Israel, ([Num
14:45](../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#045).md).

[Numbers 24:21](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">num 24:21</span></span>

And he looked on the Kenites, and took up his parable, and said, Strong
is thy dwellingplace, and thou (o) puttest thy nest in a rock.

\(o\) Make yourself as strong as you can.

[Numbers 24:23](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">num 24:23</span></span>

And he took up his parable, and said, Alas, (p) who shall live when God
doeth this!

\(p\) Some read, Oh who shall not perish when the enemy (that is,
Antichrist) shall set himself up as God?

[Numbers 24:24](../../../bib/kjv/num024.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">num 24:24</span></span>

And ships \[shall come\] from the coast of (q) Chittim, and shall
afflict Asshur, and shall afflict Eber, and (r) he also shall perish for
ever.

\(q\) The Grecians and Romans.

\(r\) Meaning, Eber, or the Jews for rebelling against God.
