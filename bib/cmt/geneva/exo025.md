*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Exodus Chapter 25](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#001.md)

[Exodus 25:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">exo 25:2</span></span>

\(a\) Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering:
of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my
offering.

\(a\) After the moral and judicial law he gives them the ceremonial law
that nothing should be left to man's invention.

[Exodus 25:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">exo 25:3</span></span>

And this \[is\] the offering which ye shall (b) take of them; gold, and
silver, and brass,

\(b\) For the building and use of the tabernacle.

[Exodus 25:5](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">exo 25:5</span></span>

And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins, and (c) shittim wood,

\(c\) Which is thought to be a kindred of Cedar, which will not rot.

[Exodus 25:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">exo 25:6</span></span>

Oil for the light, spices for (d) anointing oil, and for sweet incense,

\(d\) Ordained for the priest.

[Exodus 25:8](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">exo 25:8</span></span>

And let them make me a (e) sanctuary; that I may dwell among them.

\(e\) A place both to offer sacrifice, and to hear the law.

[Exodus 25:16](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">exo 25:16</span></span>

And thou shalt put into the ark the (f) testimony which I shall give
thee.

\(f\) The stone tables, the rod of Aaron and manna which were a
testimony of God's presence.

[Exodus 25:17](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">exo 25:17</span></span>

And thou shalt make a (g) mercy seat \[of\] pure gold: two cubits and a
half \[shall be\] the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth
thereof.

\(g\) There God appeared mercifully to them: and this was a figure of
Christ.

[Exodus 25:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#029.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">exo 25:29</span></span>

And thou shalt make the (h) dishes thereof, and spoons thereof, and
covers thereof, and bowls thereof, to cover withal: \[of\] pure gold
shalt thou make them.

\(h\) To set the bread upon.

[Exodus 25:31](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#031.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">exo 25:31</span></span>

And thou shalt make a candlestick \[of\] pure gold: \[of\] beaten (i)
work shall the candlestick be made: his shaft, and his branches, his
bowls, his knops, and his flowers, shall be of the same.

\(i\) It shall not be molten, but beaten out of the lump of gold with
the hammer.

[Exodus 25:39](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#039.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_039">exo 25:39</span></span>

\[Of\] a (k) talent of pure gold shall he make it, with all these
vessels.

\(k\) This was the talent weight of the temple, and weighed 120 pounds.
