*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual
Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Numbers Chapter 33](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#001.md)

[Numbers 33:1](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">num 33:1</span></span>

These \[are\] the (a) journeys of the children of Israel, which went
forth out of the land of Egypt with their armies under the hand of Moses
and Aaron.

\(a\) From which they departed, and where they came.

[Numbers 33:4](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">num 33:4</span></span>

For the Egyptians buried all \[their\] firstborn, which the LORD had
smitten among them: upon their (b) gods also the LORD executed
judgments.

\(b\) Either meaning their idols, or their men of authority.

[Numbers 33:7](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">num 33:7</span></span>

And they removed from Etham, and turned again unto (c) Pihahiroth, which
\[is\] before Baalzephon: and they pitched before Migdol.

\(c\) At the commandment of the Lord in ([Exo
14:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#002).md).

[Numbers 33:38](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#038.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_038">num 33:38</span></span>

And Aaron the priest went up into mount Hor at the commandment of the
LORD, and died there, in the fortieth year after the children of Israel
were come out of the land of Egypt, in the first \[day\] of the (d)
fifth month.

\(d\) Which the Hebrews call Ab, and contains part of July and part of
August.

[Numbers 33:52](../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#052.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_052">num 33:52</span></span>

Then ye shall drive out all the inhabitants of the land from before you,
and destroy all their (e) pictures, and destroy all their molten images,
and quite pluck down all their high places:

\(e\) Which were set up in their high places to worship.
