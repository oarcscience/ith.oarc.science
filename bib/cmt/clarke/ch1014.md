### [1 Chronicles Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles](../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch1 14:0</span></span>

Hiram sends artificers and materials to David, to build him a house,
[Ch1 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md), [Ch1
14:2](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#002.md). David's wives and children, [Ch1
14:3-7](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#003.md). He defeats the Philistines in
two battles: one in the valley of Rephaim, [Ch1
14:8-12](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#008.md); and the other at Gibeon and
Gazer, [Ch1 14:13-16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#013.md). His fame goes
out into all the surrounding nations, [Ch1
14:17](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#017.md).

[1 Chronicles 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 14:1</span></span>

Now Hiram king of Tyre - See the transactions of this chapter related
[Sa2 5:11-25](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011) (note.md).

[1 Chronicles 14:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">ch1 14:4</span></span>

These are the names of his children - In [Sa2
5:14-16](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#014) (note.md), eleven persons only
are mentioned in the Hebrew text, but the Septuagint has twenty-four,
here there are thirteen, and all the versions have the same number, with
certain varieties in the names. - See the notes there.

[1 Chronicles 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">ch1 14:8</span></span>

The Philistines went up to seek David - See on [Sa2
5:17](../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#017) (note.md).

[1 Chronicles 14:10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">ch1 14:10</span></span>

David inquired of God - "David consulted the Word of the Lord." - T.

[1 Chronicles 14:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">ch1 14:11</span></span>

Like the breaking forth of waters - "And David said, The Lord hath
broken the enemies of David like to the breaking of a potter's vessel
full of water." - T.

[1 Chronicles 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">ch1 14:15</span></span>

A sound of going - "When thou shalt hear the sound of the angels coming
to thy assistance, then go out to battle; for an angel is sent from the
presence of God, that he may render thy way prosperous." - T.

[1 Chronicles 14:17](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">ch1 14:17</span></span>

Into all lands - That is, all the surrounding or neighboring lands and
nations, for no others can possibly be intended.
