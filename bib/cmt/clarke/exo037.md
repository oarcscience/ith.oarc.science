### [Exodus Chapter 37](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001)

[Exodus](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">exo 37:0</span></span>

Bezaleel and Aholiab make the ark, [Exo
37:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001). The mercy-seat, [Exo
37:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#006). The two cherubim, [Exo
37:7-9](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#007). The table of the shew-bread,
and its vessels, [Exo 37:10-16](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#010). The
candlestick, [Exo 37:17-24](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#017). The golden
altar of incense, [Exo 37:25-28](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#025). The
holy anointing oil and perfume, [Exo
37:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#029).

[Exodus 37:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">exo 37:1</span></span>

And Bezaleel made the ark, etc. - For a description of the ark, see [Exo
25:10](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#010) (note), etc.

[Exodus 37:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">exo 37:6</span></span>

He made the mercy-seat - See this described [Exo
25:17](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017) (note).

[Exodus 37:10](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">exo 37:10</span></span>

He made the table - See [Exo 25:23](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#023).

[Exodus 37:16](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">exo 37:16</span></span>

He made the vessels - See all these particularly described in the notes
on [Exo 25:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#029) (note).

[Exodus 37:17](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">exo 37:17</span></span>

He made the candlestick - See this described in the note on [Exo
25:31](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#031) (note).

[Exodus 37:25](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">exo 37:25</span></span>

He made the incense altar - See this described [Exo
30:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#001) (note).

[Exodus 37:29](../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">exo 37:29</span></span>

He made the holy anointing oil - See this and the perfume, and the
materials out of which they were made, described at large in the notes
on [Exo 30:23-25](../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#023) and [Exo
30:34-38](../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#034). As this chapter also is a
repetition of what has been mentioned in preceding chapters, the reader
is desired to refer to them.
