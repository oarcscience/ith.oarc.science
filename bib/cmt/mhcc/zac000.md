### [Zechariah Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#001)

[Zechariah](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">zac 0:0</span></span>

This prophecy is suitable to all, as the scope is to reprove for sin,
and threaten God's judgments against the impenitent, and to encourage
those that feared God, with assurances of the mercy God had in store for
his church, and especially of the coming of the Messiah, and the setting
up his kingdom in the world.
