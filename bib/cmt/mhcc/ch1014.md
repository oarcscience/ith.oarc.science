### [1 Chronicles Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md)

[1 Chronicles](../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch1 14:0</span></span>

David's victories.

[1 Chronicles 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">ch1 14:1</span></span>

In this chapter we have an account of, 1. David's kingdom established.
2. His family built up. 3. His enemies defeated. This is repeated from 2
Samuel 5. Let the fame of David be looked upon as a type and figure of
the exalted honour of the Son of David.
