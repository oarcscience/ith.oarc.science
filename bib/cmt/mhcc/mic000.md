### [Micah Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001.md)

[Micah](../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mic 0:0</span></span>

Micah was raised up to support Isaiah, and to confirm his predictions,
while he invited to repentance, both by threatened judgments and
promised mercies. A very remarkable passage, [Mic
5:1-15](../../../bib/kjv/mic005.htm#001.md) contains a summary of
prophecies concerning the Messiah.
