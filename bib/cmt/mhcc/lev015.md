### [Leviticus Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/lev015.htm#001)

[Leviticus](../../../bib/kjv/lev001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">lev 15:0</span></span>

Laws concerning ceremonial uncleanness.

[Leviticus 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/lev015.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">lev 15:1</span></span>

We need not be curious in explaining these laws; but have reason to be
thankful that we need fear no defilement, except that of sin, nor need
ceremonial and burdensome purifications. These laws remind us that God
sees all things, even those which escape the notice of men. The great
gospel duties of faith and repentance are here signified, and the great
gospel privileges of the application of Christ's blood to our souls for
our justification, and his grace for our sanctification.
