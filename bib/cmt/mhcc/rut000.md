### [Ruth Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#001.md)

[Ruth](../../../bib/kjv/rut001.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">rut 0:0</span></span>

We find in this book excellent examples of faith, piety, patience,
humility, industry, and loving-kindness, in the common events of life.
Also we see the special care which God's providence take of our smallest
concerns, encouraging us to full trust therein. We may view this book as
a beautiful, because natural representation of human life; as a curious
detail of important facts; and as a part of the plan of redemption.
