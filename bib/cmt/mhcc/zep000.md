### [Zephaniah Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#001)

[Zephaniah](../../../bib/kjv/zep001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">zep 0:0</span></span>

Zephaniah excites to repentance, foretells the destruction of the
enemies of the Jews, and comforts the pious among them with promises of
future blessings, the restoration of their nation, and the prosperity of
the church in the latter days.
