### [Psalms Chapter 54](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#001)

[Psalms 54:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 54:1</span></span>

See on [Psa 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001), title; [Psa
32:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa032.htm#001), title; for the history, see [Sa1
23:19](../../../bib/kjv/sa1023.htm#019), [Sa1
23:29](../../../bib/kjv/sa1023.htm#029); 1Sa. 26:1-25. After an earnest
cry for help, the Psalmist promises praise in the assurance of a
hearing. ([Psa 54:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#001))

by thy name-- ([Psa 5:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#011)), specially,
power.

judge me--as in [Psa 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#008); [Psa
26:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001).

[Psalms 54:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 54:2</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001); [Psa
5:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001)).

[Psalms 54:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 54:3</span></span>

strangers--perhaps Ziphites.

oppressors--literally, "terrible ones" ([Isa
13:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#011); [Isa
25:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa025.htm#003)). Such were Saul and his army.

not set . . . them--acted as atheists, without God's fear (compare [Psa
16:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa016.htm#008)).

[Psalms 54:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 54:4</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 30:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#010)).

with them--on their side, and for me (compare [Psa
46:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#011)).

[Psalms 54:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 54:5</span></span>

He shall . . . evil--or, "Evil shall return on" ([Psa
7:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#016)) my enemies or watchers, that is,
to do me evil ([Psa 6:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa006.htm#007)).

in thy truth--Thy verified promise.

[Psalms 54:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 54:6</span></span>

I will freely, &c.--or, present a freewill offering ([Lev
7:16](../../../bib/kjv/lev007.htm#016); [Num
15:3](../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#003)).

[Psalms 54:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 54:7</span></span>

mine eye . . . desire--(compare [Psa
59:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa059.htm#010); [Psa
112:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#008)), expresses satisfaction in
beholding the overthrow of his enemies as those of God, without implying
any selfish or unholy feeling (compare [Psa
52:6-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa052.htm#006)).
