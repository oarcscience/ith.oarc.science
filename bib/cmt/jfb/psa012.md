### [Psalms Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 12:1</span></span>

On title, see Introduction and see on [Psa
6:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa006.htm#001.md). The Psalmist laments the decrease
of good men. The pride and deceit of the wicked provokes God's wrath,
whose promise to avenge the cause of pious sufferers will be verified
even amidst prevailing iniquity. ([Psa
12:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001).md)

the faithful--or literally, "faithfulness" ([Psa
31:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#023).md).

[Psalms 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 12:2</span></span>

The want of it is illustrated by the prevalence of deceit and
instability.

[Psalms 12:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 12:3</span></span>

Boasting ([Dan 7:25](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#025).md) is, like
flattery, a species of lying.

lips, and . . . tongue--for persons.

[Psalms 12:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 12:5</span></span>

The writer intimates his confidence by depicting God's actions (compare
[Psa 9:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#019.md); [Psa
10:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#012).md) as coming to save the poor at
whom the wicked sneer ([Psa 10:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#005).md).

[Psalms 12:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 12:6</span></span>

The words--literally, "saying of" ([Psa
12:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#005).md).

seven times--thoroughly ([Dan 3:19](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#019).md).

[Psalms 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 12:7</span></span>

them--(Margin.)

[Psalms 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 12:8</span></span>

The wicked roam undisturbed doing evil, when vileness and vile men are
exalted.
