### [Psalms Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 43:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 43:1</span></span>

Excepting the recurrence of the refrain, there is no good reason to
suppose this a part of the preceding, though the scope is the same. It
has always been placed separate. ([Psa
43:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001).md)

Judge--or, "vindicate" ([Psa 10:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#018).md).

plead, &c.-- ([Psa 35:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#001).md).

ungodly--neither in character or condition objects of God's favor
(compare [Psa 4:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#003).md).

[Psalms 43:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 43:2</span></span>

God of my strength--by covenant relation my stronghold ([Psa
18:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa018.htm#001).md).

cast me off--in scorn.

because--or, "in," that is, in such circumstances of oppression.

[Psalms 43:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 43:3</span></span>

light--as in [Psa 27:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#001.md).

truth--or, "faithfulness" ([Psa 25:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#005).md),
manifest it by fulfilling promises. Light and truth are personified as
messengers who will bring him to the privileged place of worship.

tabernacles--plural, in allusion to the various courts.

[Psalms 43:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 43:4</span></span>

the altar--as the chief place of worship. The mention of the harp
suggests the prominence of praise in his offering.
