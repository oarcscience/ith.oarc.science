### [Psalms Chapter 80](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 80:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 80:1</span></span>

Shoshannim--"Lilies" (see on [Psa
45:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa045.htm#001), title.md). Eduth--Testimony,
referring to the topic as a testimony of God to His people (compare [Psa
19:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#007).md). This Psalm probably relates to
the captivity of the ten tribes, as the former to that of Judah. Its
complaint is aggravated by the contrast of former prosperity, and the
prayer for relief occurs as a refrain through the Psalm. (Psa. 80:1-19)

Joseph--for Ephraim ([Ch1 7:20-29](../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#020.md);
[Psa 78:67](../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#067.md); [Rev
7:8](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#008).md), for Israel.

Shepherd--(Compare [Gen 49:24](../../../bib/kjv/gen049.htm#024).md).

leadest, &c.-- ([Psa 77:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#020).md).

dwellest . . . cherubim-- ([Exo
25:20](../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#020).md); the place of God's visible
glory, whence He communed with the people ([Heb
9:5](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#005).md).

shine forth--appear ([Psa 50:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa050.htm#002.md); [Psa
94:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#001).md).

[Psalms 80:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 80:2</span></span>

Before Ephraim, &c.--These tribes marched next the ark ([Num
2:18-24](../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#018).md). The name of Benjamin may be
introduced merely in allusion to that fact, and not because that tribe
was identified with Israel in the schism ([Kg1
12:16-21](../../../bib/kjv/kg1012.htm#016.md); compare also [Num
10:24](../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#024).md).

[Psalms 80:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 80:3</span></span>

Turn us--that is, from captivity.

thy face to shine-- ([Num 6:25](../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#025).md).

[Psalms 80:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 80:4</span></span>

be angry--(Compare Margin.)

[Psalms 80:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 80:5</span></span>

bread of tears--still an Eastern figure for affliction.

[Psalms 80:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 80:6</span></span>

strife--object or cause of ([Isa
9:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#011).md). On last clause compare [Psa
79:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#004.md); [Eze
36:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#004.md).

[Psalms 80:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 80:8</span></span>

brought--or, "plucked up," as by roots, to be replanted.

a vine-- ([Psa 78:47](../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#047).md). The figure
([Isa 16:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa016.htm#008).md) represents the flourishing
state of Israel, as predicted ([Gen
28:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen028.htm#014).md), and verified ([Kg1
4:20-25](../../../bib/kjv/kg1004.htm#020).md).

[Psalms 80:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 80:12</span></span>

hedges-- ([Isa 5:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#005).md).

[Psalms 80:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 80:13</span></span>

The boar--may represent the ravaging Assyrian and

the wild beast--other heathen.

[Psalms 80:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">psa 80:14</span></span>

visit this vine--favorably ([Psa 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa008.htm#004).md).

[Psalms 80:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 80:15</span></span>

And the vineyard--or, "And protect or guard what thy right hand," &c.

the branch--literally, "over the Son of man," preceding this phrase,
with "protect" or "watch."

for thyself--a tacit allusion to the plea for help; for

[Psalms 80:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">psa 80:16</span></span>

it--the "vine" or

they--the "people" are suffering from Thy displeasure.

[Psalms 80:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">psa 80:17</span></span>

thy hand . . . upon--that is, strengthen ([Ezr
7:6](../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#006.md); [Ezr
8:22](../../../bib/kjv/ezr008.htm#022).md).

man of . . . hand--may allude to Benjamin ([Gen
35:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen035.htm#018).md). The terms in the latter clause
correspond with those of [Psa 80:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#015.md),
from "and the branch," &c., literally, and confirm the exposition given
above.

[Psalms 80:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">psa 80:18</span></span>

We need quickening grace ([Psa 71:20](../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#020.md);
[Psa 119:25](../../../bib/kjv/psa119.htm#025).md) to persevere in Thy right
worship ([Gen 4:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen004.htm#026.md); [Rom
10:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#011).md).

[Psalms 80:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">psa 80:19</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 80:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#003.md), "O God"; [Psa
80:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#007), "O God of hosts".md).
