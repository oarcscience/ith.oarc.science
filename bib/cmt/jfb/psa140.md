### [Psalms Chapter 140](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#001)

[Psalms 140:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 140:1</span></span>

The style of this Psalm resembles those of David in the former part of
the book, presenting the usual complaint, prayer, and confident hope of
relief. ([Psa 140:1-13](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#001))

evil man--Which of David's enemies is meant is not important.

[Psalms 140:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 140:2</span></span>

This character of the wicked, and the devices planned against the pious,
correspond to [Psa 10:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#007); [Psa
31:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#013); [Psa
58:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#004), &c.

[Psalms 140:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 140:3</span></span>

sharpened . . . like a serpent--not like a serpent does, but they are
thus like a serpent in cunning and venom.

[Psalms 140:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 140:5</span></span>

snare \[and\] net--for threatening dangers (compare [Psa
38:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa038.htm#012); [Psa
57:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#006)).

[Psalms 140:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 140:6</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 5:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001); [Psa
16:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa016.htm#002)).

[Psalms 140:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 140:7</span></span>

day of battle--literally, "of armor," that is, when using it.

[Psalms 140:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 140:8</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 37:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#012); [Psa
66:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#007)).

lest they exalt themselves--or, they will be exalted if permitted to
prosper.

[Psalms 140:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 140:9</span></span>

Contrasts his head covered by God ([Psa
140:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#007)) with theirs, or (as "head" may
be used for "persons") with them, covered with the results of their
wicked deeds ([Psa 7:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#016)).

[Psalms 140:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 140:10</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 11:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#006); [Psa
120:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#004)).

cast into the fire; into deep pits--figures for utter destruction.

[Psalms 140:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 140:11</span></span>

an evil speaker--or, "slanderer" will not be tolerated ([Psa
101:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#007)). The last clause may be
translated: "an evil (man) He (God) shall hunt," &c.

[Psalms 140:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 140:12</span></span>

(Compare [Psa 9:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#004)).

[Psalms 140:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa140.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 140:13</span></span>

After all changes, the righteous shall have cause for praise. Such

shall dwell--shall sit securely, under God's protection ([Psa
21:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#006); [Psa
41:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa041.htm#012)).
