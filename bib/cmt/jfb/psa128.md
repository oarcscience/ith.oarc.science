### [Psalms Chapter 128](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#001)

[Psalms 128:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 128:1</span></span>

The temporal blessings of true piety. The eighth chapter of Zecariah is
a virtual commentary on this Psalm. Compare [Psa
128:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#003) with [Zac
8:5](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#005); and [Psa
128:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#002) with [Lev
26:16](../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#016); [Deu
28:33](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#033); [Zac
8:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#010); and [Psa
128:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#006) with [Zac
8:4](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#004). ([Psa
128:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#001))

(Compare [Psa 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)).

[Psalms 128:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 128:2</span></span>

For thou shalt eat--that is, It is a blessing to live on the fruits of
one's own industry.

[Psalms 128:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 128:3</span></span>

by the sides--or, "within" ([Psa
48:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#002)).

olive plants--are peculiarly luxuriant ([Psa
52:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa052.htm#008)).

[Psalms 128:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 128:5</span></span>

In temporal blessings the pious do not forget the richer blessings of
God's grace, which they shall ever enjoy.

[Psalms 128:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 128:6</span></span>

Long life crowns all other temporal favors. As [Psa
125:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#005), this Psalm closes with a prayer
for peace, with prosperity for God's people.
