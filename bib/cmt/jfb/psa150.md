### [Psalms Chapter 150](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 150:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 150:1</span></span>

This is a suitable doxology for the whole book, reciting the "place,
theme, mode, and extent of God's high praise." ([Psa
150:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#001).md)

in his sanctuary--on earth.

firmament of his power--which illustrates His power.

[Psalms 150:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 150:2</span></span>

mighty acts-- ([Psa 145:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#004).md).

excellent greatness--or, abundance of greatness.

[Psalms 150:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 150:3</span></span>

trumpet--used to call religious assemblies;

[Psalms 150:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 150:4</span></span>

organs--or pipe, a wind instrument, and the others were used in worship.

[Psalms 150:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 150:5</span></span>

cymbals--suited to loud praise ([Neh
12:27](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#027).md).

[Psalms 150:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 150:6</span></span>

LIVING VOICES SHALL TAKE UP THE FAILING SOUNDS OF DEAD INSTRUMENTS, AND
AS THEY CEASE ON EARTH, THOSE OF INTELLIGENT RANSOMED SPIRITS AND HOLY
ANGELS, AS WITH THE SOUND OF MIGHTY THUNDERS, WILL PROLONG ETERNALLY THE
PRAISE, SAYING: "ALLELUIA! SALVATION, AND GLORY, AND HONOR, AND POWER,
UNTO THE LORD OUR GOD;" "ALLELUIA! FOR THE LORD GOD OMNIPOTENT
REIGNETH." AMEN!
