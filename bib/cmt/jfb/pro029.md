### [Proverbs Chapter 29](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001.md)

[Proverbs 29:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 29:1</span></span>

(Pro. 29:1-27)

hardeneth . . . neck--obstinately refuses counsel ([Kg2
17:14](../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#014.md); [Neh
9:16](../../../bib/kjv/neh009.htm#016).md).

destroyed--literally, "shivered" or "utterly broken to pieces."

without remedy--literally, "without healing" or repairing.

[Proverbs 29:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">pro 29:2</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 11:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#010.md); [Pro
28:28](../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#028).md).

in authority--(Compare Margin), increased in power.

[Proverbs 29:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 29:3</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 4:6-7](../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#006.md); [Pro
10:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#001), &c..md).

[Proverbs 29:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 29:4</span></span>

by judgment--that is, righteous decisions, opposed to those procured by
gifts (compare [Pro 28:21](../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#021).md), by which
good government is perverted.

land--for nation.

[Proverbs 29:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">pro 29:5</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 26:28](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#028).md).

spreadeth . . . feet--By misleading him as to his real character, the
flatterer brings him to evil, prepared by himself or others.

[Proverbs 29:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 29:6</span></span>

In--or, "By"

the transgression--he is brought into difficulty ([Pro
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro012.htm#013).md), but the righteous go on
prospering, and so sing or rejoice.

[Proverbs 29:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 29:7</span></span>

considereth--literally, "knows," as [Psa
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#006.md).

the cause--that is, in courts of justice (compare [Pro
29:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#014).md). The voluntary neglect of it by
the wicked ([Pro 28:27](../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#027).md) occasions
oppression.

[Proverbs 29:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 29:8</span></span>

Scornful men--those who contemptuously disregard God's law.

bring--(Compare Margin), kindle strife.

turn away wrath--that is, "abate wrath."

[Proverbs 29:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 29:9</span></span>

contendeth--that is, in law.

whether . . . laugh--The fool, whether angry or good-humored, is
unsettled; or referring the words to the wise man, the sense is, that
all his efforts, severe or gentle, are unavailing to pacify the fool.

[Proverbs 29:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#010.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 29:10</span></span>

bloodthirsty--(Compare Margin), murderers ([Psa
5:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#006.md); [Psa
26:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#009).md).

hate, &c.-- ([Pro 1:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#011.md); [Gen
3:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#004).md).

seek . . . soul--that is, to preserve it.

[Proverbs 29:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#011.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">pro 29:11</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 12:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro012.htm#016.md); [Pro
16:32](../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#032).md).

mind--or, "spirit," for anger or any ill passion which the righteous
restrain.

[Proverbs 29:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#012.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">pro 29:12</span></span>

His servants imitate him.

[Proverbs 29:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#013.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 29:13</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 22:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#002).md).

deceitful man--literally, "man of vexations," an exactor.

the Lord . . . their eyes--sustains their lives ([Sa1
14:27](../../../bib/kjv/sa1014.htm#027.md); [Psa
13:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa013.htm#003).md); that is, both depend on Him,
and He will do justice.

[Proverbs 29:14](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#014.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">pro 29:14</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 20:28](../../../bib/kjv/pro020.htm#028.md); [Pro
25:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro025.htm#005).md). Such is the character of the
King of kings ([Psa 72:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#004.md), [Psa
72:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#012).md).

[Proverbs 29:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">pro 29:15</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 13:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#024.md); [Pro
23:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#013).md).

[Proverbs 29:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#016.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 29:16</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 29:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#002.md), [Pro
29:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#012.md); [Psa
12:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001).md).

shall see . . . fall--and triumph in it ([Psa
37:34-38](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#034.md); [Psa
58:10-11](../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#010).md).

[Proverbs 29:17](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#017.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">pro 29:17</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 29:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#003.md), [Pro
29:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015.md); [Pro
19:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#018).md).

give thee rest--peace and quiet (compare [Pro
29:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009).md).

[Proverbs 29:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#018.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 29:18</span></span>

no vision--instruction in God's truth, which was by prophets, through
visions ([Sa1 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1003.htm#001).md).

people perish--(Compare Margin), are deprived of moral restraints.

keepeth the law--has, and observes, instruction ([Pro
14:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#011.md), [Pro
14:34](../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#034.md); [Psa
19:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#011).md).

[Proverbs 29:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#019.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 29:19</span></span>

A servant--who lacks good principle.

corrected--or discovered.

will not answer--that is, will not obey.

[Proverbs 29:20](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#020.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">pro 29:20</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 21:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro021.htm#005).md).

hasty in . . . words?--implying self-conceit ([Pro
26:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#012).md).

[Proverbs 29:21](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#021.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">pro 29:21</span></span>

become his son--assume the place and privileges of one.

[Proverbs 29:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#022.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">pro 29:22</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 15:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#018).md). Such are
delighted by discord and violence.

[Proverbs 29:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#023.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 29:23</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 16:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#018.md); [Pro
18:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#012).md).

honour . . . spirit--or, "such shall lay hold on honor" ([Pro
11:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#016).md).

[Proverbs 29:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#024.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 29:24</span></span>

hateth . . . soul--(Compare [Pro
8:36](../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#036).md).

heareth cursing-- ([Lev 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/lev005.htm#001).md), risks
the punishment, rather than reveal truth.

[Proverbs 29:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#025.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 29:25</span></span>

The fear . . . snare--involves men in difficulty (compare [Pro
29:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#006).md).

shall be safe--(Compare Margin; [Pro
18:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#010).md).

[Proverbs 29:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#026.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 29:26</span></span>

(Compare Margin; [Psa 27:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#008).md). God alone
will and can do exact justice.

[Proverbs 29:27](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#027.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">pro 29:27</span></span>

(Compare [Pro 3:32](../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#032).md). On last clause,
compare [Pro 29:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#016.md); [Psa
37:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#012.md).
