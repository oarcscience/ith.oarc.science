### [Psalms Chapter 53](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 53:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 53:1</span></span>

Upon Mahalath--(See on [Psa 88:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#001.md),
title). Why this repetition of the fourteenth Psalm is given we do not
know. ([Psa 53:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#001).md)

with few verbal changes, correspond with [Psa
14:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#001.md).

[Psalms 53:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 53:5</span></span>

Instead of assurances of God's presence with the pious, and a complaint
of the wicked, [Psa 14:5-6](../../../bib/kjv/psa014.htm#005.md) portrays
the ruin of the latter, whose "bones" even "are scattered" (compare [Psa
141:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa141.htm#007).md), and who are put to shame as
contemptuously rejected of God.
