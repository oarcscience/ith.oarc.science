### [Psalms Chapter 82](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001)

[Psalms 82:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 82:1</span></span>

Before the great Judge, the judges of the earth are rebuked, exhorted,
and threatened. ([Psa 82:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001))

congregation--(Compare [Exo 12:3](../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#003); [Exo
16:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo016.htm#001)).

of the mighty--that is, of God, of His appointment.

the gods--or, "judges" ([Exo 21:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#006);
[Exo 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#009)), God's representatives.

[Psalms 82:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 82:2</span></span>

accept the persons--literally, "lift up the faces," that is, from
dejection, or admit to favor and communion, regardless of merit ([Lev
19:15](../../../bib/kjv/lev019.htm#015); [Pro
18:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#005)).

[Psalms 82:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 82:3</span></span>

So must good judges act ([Psa 10:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#014);
[Job 29:12](../../../bib/kjv/job029.htm#012)).

[Psalms 82:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 82:4</span></span>

poor and needy--(Compare [Psa 34:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa034.htm#010);
[Psa 41:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa041.htm#001)).

[Psalms 82:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 82:5</span></span>

By the wilful ignorance and negligence of judges, anarchy ensues ([Psa
11:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#003); [Psa
75:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa075.htm#003)).

out of course--(Compare Margin; [Psa
9:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#006); [Psa
62:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa062.htm#002)).

[Psalms 82:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 82:6</span></span>

Though God admitted their official dignity ([Joh
10:34](../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#034)), He reminds them of their
mortality.

[Psalms 82:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 82:7</span></span>

fall like, &c.--be cut off suddenly ([Psa
20:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#008); [Psa
91:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa091.htm#007)).

[Psalms 82:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 82:8</span></span>

As rightful sovereign of earth, God is invoked personally to correct the
evils of His representatives.
