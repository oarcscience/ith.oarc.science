### [Psalms Chapter 83](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#001)

[Psalms 83:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 83:1</span></span>

Of Asaph--(See on [Psa 74:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa074.htm#001), title).
The historical occasion is probably that of [Ch2
20:1-2](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#001) (compare [Psa
47:1-9](../../../bib/kjv/psa047.htm#001); [Psa
48:1-14](../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#001)). After a general petition,
the craft and rage of the combined enemies are described, God's former
dealings recited, and a like summary and speedy destruction on them is
invoked. (Psa. 83:1-18)

God addressed as indifferent (compare [Psa
35:22](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#022); [Psa
39:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa039.htm#012)).

be not still--literally, "not quiet," as opposed to action.

[Psalms 83:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 83:2</span></span>

thine enemies--as well as ours ([Psa
74:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa074.htm#023); [Isa
37:23](../../../bib/kjv/isa037.htm#023)).

[Psalms 83:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 83:3</span></span>

hidden ones--whom God specially protects ([Psa
27:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#005); [Psa
91:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa091.htm#001)).

[Psalms 83:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 83:4</span></span>

from being a nation--utter destruction ([Isa
7:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#008); [Isa
23:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa023.htm#001)).

Israel--here used for Judah, having been the common name.

[Psalms 83:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 83:5</span></span>

they have consulted--with heart, or cordially.

together--all alike.

[Psalms 83:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 83:6</span></span>

tabernacles--for people ([Psa 78:67](../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#067)).

they--all these united with the children of Lot, or Ammonites and
Moabites (compare [Ch2 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#001)).

[Psalms 83:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 83:9</span></span>

Compare the similar fate of these ([Ch2
20:23](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#023)) with that of the foes mentioned
in [Jdg 7:22](../../../bib/kjv/jdg007.htm#022), here referred to. They
destroyed one another (Jdg. 4:6-24; [Jdg
7:25](../../../bib/kjv/jdg007.htm#025)). Human remains form manure
(compare [Kg2 9:37](../../../bib/kjv/kg2009.htm#037); [Jer
9:22](../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#022)).

[Psalms 83:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 83:12</span></span>

The language of the invaders.

houses--literally, "residences," enclosures, as for flocks ([Psa
65:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa065.htm#012)).

of God--as the proprietors of the land ([Ch2
20:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#011); [Isa
14:25](../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#025)).

[Psalms 83:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 83:13</span></span>

like a wheel--or, whirling of any light thing ([Isa
17:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa017.htm#013)), as stubble or chaff ([Psa
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#004)).

[Psalms 83:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">psa 83:14</span></span>

Pursue them to an utter destruction.

[Psalms 83:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">psa 83:16</span></span>

that they may seek--or as [Psa 83:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#018),
supply "men," since [Psa 83:17-18](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#017)
amplify the sentiment of [Psa 83:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#016),
expressing more fully the measure of destruction, and the lesson of
God's being and perfections (compare [Ch2
20:29](../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#029)) taught to all men.
