### [Psalms Chapter 61](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#001.md)

[Psalms 61:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#001.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 61:1</span></span>

Neginah--or, Neginoth (see on [Psa
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001), title.md). Separated from his usual
spiritual privileges, perhaps by Absalom's rebellion, the Psalmist prays
for divine aid, and, in view of past mercies, with great confidence of
being heard. ([Psa 61:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#001).md)

From the end--that is, places remote from the sanctuary ([Deu
28:64](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#064).md).

[Psalms 61:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#002.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 61:2</span></span>

heart is overwhelmed--literally, "covered over with darkness," or,
"distress."

to the rock-- ([Psa 18:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa018.htm#002.md); [Psa
40:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#002).md).

higher than I--which otherwise I cannot ascend.

[Psalms 61:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#003.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 61:3</span></span>

shelter . . . and strong tower--repeat the same sentiment.

[Psalms 61:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#004.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 61:4</span></span>

I will abide--So I desire to do (compare [Psa
23:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#006).md).

trust in the covert, &c.--make my refuge, in the shadow (compare [Psa
17:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa017.htm#008.md); [Psa
36:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#007).md).

[Psalms 61:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#005.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 61:5</span></span>

the heritage--or, part in the spiritual blessings of Israel ([Psa
21:2-4](../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#002).md).

vows--implies prayers.

[Psalms 61:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#006.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 61:6</span></span>

the king--himself and his royal line ending in Christ. Mercy and truth
personified, as in [Psa 40:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#011.md); [Psa
57:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#003.md).

[Psalms 61:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#007.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 61:7</span></span>

abide before God--literally, "sit as a king in God's presence," under
His protection.

[Psalms 61:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#008.md)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 61:8</span></span>

Thus for new blessings will new vows of praise ever be paid.
