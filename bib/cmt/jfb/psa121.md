### [Psalms Chapter 121](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001)

[Psalms 121:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 121:1</span></span>

God's guardian care of His people celebrated. ([Psa
121:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001))

I will lift up mine eyes--expresses desire (compare [Psa
25:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#001)), mingled with expectation. The
last clause, read as a question, is answered,

[Psalms 121:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 121:2</span></span>

by avowing God to be the helper, of whose ability His creative power is
a pledge ([Psa 115:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa115.htm#015)), to which,

[Psalms 121:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 121:3</span></span>

His sleepless vigilance is added.

to be moved--(Compare [Psa 38:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa038.htm#016); [Psa
66:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#009)).

[Psalms 121:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 121:5</span></span>

upon thy right hand--a protector's place ([Psa
109:31](../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#031); [Psa
110:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#005)).

[Psalms 121:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 121:6</span></span>

God keeps His people at all times and in all perils.

nor the moon by night--poetically represents the dangers of the night,
over which the moon presides ([Gen
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#016)).

[Psalms 121:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 121:8</span></span>

thy going out, &c.--all thy ways ([Deu
28:19](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#019); [Psa
104:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa104.htm#023)).

evermore--includes a future state.
