### Isaiah 35

[KJV](../kjv/isa035.md)

1  The wilderness<sup>[4057](h4057.md)</sup> and the solitary
place<sup>[6723](h6723)</sup> shall be glad<sup>[7797](h7797.md)</sup> for
them; and the desert<sup>[6160](h6160.md)</sup> shall
rejoice,<sup>[1523](h1523)</sup> and blossom<sup>[6524](h6524.md)</sup> as
the rose.<sup>[2261](h2261.md)</sup>

2  It shall blossom abundantly,<sup>[6524](h6524.md)</sup>
<sup>[6524](h6524)</sup> and rejoice<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup>
even<sup>[637](h0637)</sup> with joy<sup>[1525](h1525.md)</sup> and
singing:<sup>[7442](h7442)</sup> the glory<sup>[3519](h3519.md)</sup> of
Lebanon<sup>[3844](h3844)</sup> shall be given<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
unto it, the excellency<sup>[1926](h1926.md)</sup> of
Carmel<sup>[3760](h3760)</sup> and Sharon,<sup>[8289](h8289.md)</sup>
they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> shall see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> the
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> and
the excellency<sup>[1926](h1926.md)</sup> of our
God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

3  Strengthen<sup>[2388](h2388.md)</sup> ye the
weak<sup>[7504](h7504)</sup> hands,<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> and
confirm<sup>[553](h0553)</sup> the feeble<sup>[3782](h3782.md)</sup>
knees.<sup>[1290](h1290.md)</sup>

4  Say<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> to them that are of a
fearful<sup>[4116](h4116)</sup> heart,<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> Be
strong,<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> fear<sup>[3372](h3372.md)</sup>
not:<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> behold,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> your
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> will come<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> with
vengeance,<sup>[5359](h5359)</sup> even God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> with
a recompense;<sup>[1576](h1576)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> will
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> and save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> you.

5  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> the eyes<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> of the
blind<sup>[5787](h5787)</sup> shall be opened,<sup>[6491](h6491.md)</sup>
and the ears<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> of the deaf<sup>[2795](h2795.md)</sup>
shall be unstopped.<sup>[6605](h6605.md)</sup>

6  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> shall the lame<sup>[6455](h6455.md)</sup>
man leap<sup>[1801](h1801)</sup> as a hart,<sup>[354](h0354.md)</sup> and
the tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> of the dumb<sup>[483](h0483.md)</sup>
sing:<sup>[7442](h7442)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> in the
wilderness<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> shall waters<sup>[4325](h4325.md)</sup>
break out,<sup>[1234](h1234)</sup> and streams<sup>[5158](h5158.md)</sup>
in the desert.<sup>[6160](h6160.md)</sup>

7  And the parched ground<sup>[8273](h8273.md)</sup> shall
become<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a pool,<sup>[98](h0098.md)</sup> and the
thirsty land<sup>[6774](h6774)</sup> springs<sup>[4002](h4002.md)</sup> of
water:<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> in the habitation<sup>[5116](h5116.md)</sup>
of dragons,<sup>[8565](h8565.md)</sup> where each
lay,<sup>[7258](h7258)</sup> shall be grass<sup>[2682](h2682.md)</sup> with
reeds<sup>[7070](h7070)</sup> and rushes.<sup>[1573](h1573.md)</sup>

8  And a highway<sup>[4547](h4547.md)</sup> shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> there,<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> and a
way,<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> and it shall be
called<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> The way<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> of
holiness;<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> the unclean<sup>[2931](h2931.md)</sup>
shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> pass over<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup> it;
but it<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> shall be for those: the wayfaring
men,<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> <sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> though
fools,<sup>[191](h0191)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
err<sup>[8582](h8582.md)</sup> therein.

9  No<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> lion<sup>[738](h0738.md)</sup> shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> there,<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup>
nor<sup>[1077](h1077)</sup> any ravenous<sup>[6530](h6530.md)</sup>
beast<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> shall go up<sup>[5927](h5927.md)</sup>
thereon, it shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> be
found<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> there;<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> but the
redeemed<sup>[1350](h1350)</sup> shall walk<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
there:

10  And the ransomed<sup>[6299](h6299.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall return,<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> and
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> to Zion<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> with
songs<sup>[7440](h7440)</sup> and everlasting<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>
joy<sup>[8342](h8342)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> their
heads:<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> they shall obtain<sup>[5381](h5381.md)</sup>
joy<sup>[8342](h8342)</sup> and gladness,<sup>[8057](h8057.md)</sup> and
sorrow<sup>[3015](h3015)</sup> and sighing<sup>[585](h0585.md)</sup> shall
flee away.<sup>[5127](h5127.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/isa035.md)

1 εὐφράνθητι ἔρημος διψῶσα ἀγαλλιάσθω ἔρημος καὶ ἀνθείτω ὡς κρίνον

2 καὶ ἐξανθήσει καὶ ἀγαλλιάσεται τὰ ἔρημα τοῦ Ιορδάνου καὶ ἡ δόξα τοῦ
Λιβάνου ἐδόθη αὐτῇ καὶ ἡ τιμὴ τοῦ Καρμήλου καὶ ὁ λαός μου ὄψεται τὴν
δόξαν κυρίου καὶ τὸ ὕψος τοῦ θεοῦ

3 ἰσχύσατε χεῖρες ἀνειμέναι καὶ γόνατα παραλελυμένα

4 παρακαλέσατε οἱ ὀλιγόψυχοι τῇ διανοίᾳ ἰσχύσατε μὴ φοβεῖσθε ἰδοὺ ὁ θεὸς
ἡμῶν κρίσιν ἀνταποδίδωσιν καὶ ἀνταποδώσει αὐτὸς ἥξει καὶ σώσει ἡμᾶς

5 τότε ἀνοιχθήσονται ὀφθαλμοὶ τυφλῶν καὶ ὦτα κωφῶν ἀκούσονται

6 τότε ἁλεῖται ὡς ἔλαφος ὁ χωλός καὶ τρανὴ ἔσται γλῶσσα μογιλάλων ὅτι
ἐρράγη ἐν τῇ ἐρήμῳ ὕδωρ καὶ φάραγξ ἐν γῇ διψώσῃ

7 καὶ ἡ ἄνυδρος ἔσται εἰς ἕλη καὶ εἰς τὴν διψῶσαν γῆν πηγὴ ὕδατος ἔσται
ἐκεῖ εὐφροσύνη ὀρνέων ἔπαυλις καλάμου καὶ ἕλη

8 ἐκεῖ ἔσται ὁδὸς καθαρὰ καὶ ὁδὸς ἁγία κληθήσεται καὶ οὐ μὴ παρέλθῃ ἐκεῖ
ἀκάθαρτος οὐδὲ ἔσται ἐκεῖ ὁδὸς ἀκάθαρτος οἱ δὲ διεσπαρμένοι πορεύσονται
ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς καὶ οὐ μὴ πλανηθῶσιν

9 καὶ οὐκ ἔσται ἐκεῖ λέων οὐδὲ τῶν θηρίων τῶν πονηρῶν οὐ μὴ ἀναβῇ ἐπ᾽
αὐτὴν οὐδὲ μὴ εὑρεθῇ ἐκεῖ ἀλλὰ πορεύσονται ἐν αὐτῇ λελυτρωμένοι

10 καὶ συνηγμένοι διὰ κύριον ἀποστραφήσονται καὶ ἥξουσιν εἰς Σιων μετ᾽
εὐφροσύνης καὶ εὐφροσύνη αἰώνιος ὑπὲρ κεφαλῆς αὐτῶν ἐπὶ γὰρ κεφαλῆς
αὐτῶν αἴνεσις καὶ ἀγαλλίαμα καὶ εὐφροσύνη καταλήμψεται αὐτούς ἀπέδρα
ὀδύνη καὶ λύπη καὶ στεναγμός

[Vul](../vul/isa035.md)

1 \[Lætabitur deserta et invia,  
et exsultabit solitudo,  
et florebit quasi lilium.  

2 Germinans germinabit, et exsultabit  
lætabunda et laudans:  
gloria Libani data est ei,  
decor Carmeli et Saron;  
ipsi videbunt gloriam Domini,  
et decorem Dei nostri.  

3 Confortate manus dissolutas,  
et genua debilia roborate.  

4 Dicite pusillanimis:  
Confortamini, et nolite timere:  
ecce Deus vester ultionem adducet retributionis;  
Deus ipse veniet, et salvabit vos.  

5 Tunc aperientur oculi cæcorum,  
et aures surdorum patebunt;  

6 tunc saliet sicut cervus claudus,  
et aperta erit lingua mutorum:  
quia scissæ sunt in deserto aquæ,  
et torrentes in solitudine;  

7 et quæ erat arida, erit in stagnum,  
et sitiens in fontes aquarum.  
In cubilibus, in quibus prius dracones habitabant,  
orietur viror calami et junci.  

8 Et erit ibi semita et via,  
et via sancta vocabitur:  
non transibit per eam pollutus,  
et hæc erit vobis directa via,  
ita ut stulti non errent per eam.  

9 Non erit ibi leo,  
et mala bestia non ascendet per eam,  
nec invenietur ibi;  
et ambulabunt qui liberati fuerint.  

10 Et redempti a Domino convertentur,  
et venient in Sion cum laude,  
et lætitia sempiterna super caput eorum:  
gaudium et lætitiam obtinebunt,  
et fugiet dolor et gemitus.\]
