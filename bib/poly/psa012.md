### Psalms 12

[KJV](../kjv/psa012.md)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> Sheminith,<sup>[8067](h8067.md)</sup> A
Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup>
Help,<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the godly man<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup>
ceaseth;<sup>[1584](h1584)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
faithful<sup>[539](h0539)</sup> fail<sup>[6461](h6461.md)</sup> from among
the children<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> of
men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

2  They speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> vanity<sup>[7723](h7723.md)</sup>
every one<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854.md)</sup> his
neighbor:<sup>[7453](h7453.md)</sup> with
flattering<sup>[2513](h2513)</sup> lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> and with
a double heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> <sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> do they
speak.<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>

3  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> shall cut
off<sup>[3772](h3772)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
flattering<sup>[2513](h2513)</sup> lips,<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> and the
tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> that speaketh<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
proud things:<sup>[1419](h1419.md)</sup>

4  Who<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> have said,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> With our
tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> will we prevail;<sup>[1396](h1396.md)</sup>
our lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> are our own:
who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> is lord<sup>[113](h0113.md)</sup> over us?

5  For the oppression<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7701](h7701.md)</sup>
of the poor,<sup>[6041](h6041.md)</sup> for the
sighing<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[603](h0603.md)</sup> of the
needy,<sup>[34](h0034)</sup> now<sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup> will I
arise,<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> saith<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> the
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> I will set<sup>[7896](h7896.md)</sup> him in
safety<sup>[3468](h3468.md)</sup> from him that
puffeth<sup>[6315](h6315.md)</sup> at him.

6  The words<sup>[565](h0565)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
are pure<sup>[2889](h2889)</sup> words:<sup>[565](h0565.md)</sup> as
silver<sup>[3701](h3701)</sup> tried<sup>[6884](h6884.md)</sup> in a
furnace<sup>[5948](h5948)</sup> of earth,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
purified<sup>[2212](h2212)</sup> seven<sup>[7659](h7659.md)</sup> times.

7  Thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> shalt keep<sup>[8104](h8104.md)</sup> them,
O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> thou shalt
preserve<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> them from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
this<sup>[2098](h2098)</sup> generation<sup>[1755](h1755.md)</sup>
forever.<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

8  The wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> walk<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> on
every side,<sup>[5439](h5439.md)</sup> when the
vilest<sup>[2149](h2149)</sup> men<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup> are
exalted.<sup>[7311](h7311.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa012.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 ἕως πότε κύριε ἐπιλήσῃ μου εἰς τέλος ἕως πότε ἀποστρέψεις τὸ πρόσωπόν
σου ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ

3 ἕως τίνος θήσομαι βουλὰς ἐν ψυχῇ μου ὀδύνας ἐν καρδίᾳ μου ἡμέρας ἕως
πότε ὑψωθήσεται ὁ ἐχθρός μου ἐπ᾽ ἐμέ

4 ἐπίβλεψον εἰσάκουσόν μου κύριε ὁ θεός μου φώτισον τοὺς ὀφθαλμούς μου
μήποτε ὑπνώσω εἰς θάνατον

5 μήποτε εἴπῃ ὁ ἐχθρός μου ἴσχυσα πρὸς αὐτόν οἱ θλίβοντές με
ἀγαλλιάσονται ἐὰν σαλευθῶ

6 ἐγὼ δὲ ἐπὶ τῷ ἐλέει σου ἤλπισα ἀγαλλιάσεται ἡ καρδία μου ἐπὶ τῷ
σωτηρίῳ σου ᾄσω τῷ κυρίῳ τῷ εὐεργετήσαντί με καὶ ψαλῶ τῷ ὀνόματι κυρίου
τοῦ ὑψίστου

[Vul](../vul/psa012.md)

1 In finem. Psalmus David. \[Usquequo, Domine, oblivisceris me in
finem?  
usquequo avertis faciem tuam a me?  

2 quamdiu ponam consilia in anima mea;  
dolorem in corde meo per diem?  

3 usquequo exaltabitur inimicus meus super me?  

4 Respice, et exaudi me, Domine Deus meus.  
Illumina oculos meos, ne umquam obdormiam in morte;  

5 nequando dicat inimicus meus: Prævalui adversus eum.  
Qui tribulant me exsultabunt si motus fuero;  

6 ego autem in misericordia tua speravi.  
Exsultabit cor meum in salutari tuo.  
Cantabo Domino qui bona tribuit mihi;  
et psallam nomini Domini altissimi.\]
