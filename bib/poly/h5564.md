H5564  
סמך  
סָמַך ‎ sâmak  
*saw-mak‘*  
A primitive root; to *prop* (literally or figuratively); reflexively to
*lean* upon or *take* *hold* of (in a favorable or unfavorable sense): -
bear up, establish, (up-) hold, lay, lean, lie hard, put, rest self, set
self, stand fast, stay (self), sustain.
