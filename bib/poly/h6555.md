פּרץ  
פָּרַץ ‎ pârats  
*paw-rats‘*  
A primitive root; to *break* out (in many applications, direct and
indirect, literally and figuratively): - X abroad, (make a) breach,
break (away, down, -er, forth, in, up), burst out come (spread) abroad,
compel, disperse, grow, increase, open, press, scatter, urge.
