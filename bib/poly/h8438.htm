תּלעת תּולעת תּולעה תּולע
תּּוֹלָע תּּוֹלֵעָה תּּוֹלַעַת תּּוֹלַעַת ‎ tôlâ‛ tôlê‛âh tôla‛ath tôla‛ath
to-law‘, to-lay-aw‘, (3,4) to-lah‘-ath
From 3216 a maggot (as voracious); specifically (often with ellipsis of 8144 the crimson grub, but used only (in this connection) of the color from it, and cloths dyed therewith: - crimson, scarlet, worm.
