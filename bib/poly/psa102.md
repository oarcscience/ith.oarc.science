### Psalms 102

[KJV](../kjv/psa102.md)

1  A Prayer<sup>[8605](h8605.md)</sup> of the
afflicted,<sup>[6041](h6041)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he is
overwhelmed,<sup>[5848](h5848.md)</sup> and poureth
out<sup>[8210](h8210)</sup> his complaint<sup>[7879](h7879.md)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
Hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> my prayer,<sup>[8605](h8605.md)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and let my cry<sup>[7775](h7775.md)</sup>
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thee.

2  Hide<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> thy
face<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me in the
day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> when I am in
trouble;<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> incline<sup>[5186](h5186.md)</sup> thine
ear<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me: in the
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> when I call<sup>[7121](h7121.md)</sup>
answer<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> me speedily.<sup>[4116](h4116.md)</sup>

3  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my days<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> are
consumed<sup>[3615](h3615)</sup> like smoke,<sup>[6227](h6227.md)</sup> and
my bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> are burned<sup>[2787](h2787.md)</sup>
as<sup>[3644](h3644)</sup> a hearth.<sup>[4168](h4168.md)</sup>

4  My heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> is smitten,<sup>[5221](h5221.md)</sup>
and withered<sup>[3001](h3001)</sup> like grass;<sup>[6212](h6212.md)</sup>
so<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> that I forget<sup>[7911](h7911.md)</sup> to
eat<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> my
bread.<sup>[3899](h3899.md)</sup>

5  By reason of the voice<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of my groaning<sup>[585](h0585.md)</sup> my
bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> cleave<sup>[1692](h1692.md)</sup> to my
skin.<sup>[1320](h1320.md)</sup>

6  I am like<sup>[1819](h1819)</sup> a pelican<sup>[6893](h6893.md)</sup>
of the wilderness:<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> I am<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
like an owl<sup>[3563](h3563.md)</sup> of the
desert.<sup>[2723](h2723.md)</sup>

7  I watch,<sup>[8245](h8245)</sup> and am<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> as a
sparrow<sup>[6833](h6833)</sup> alone<sup>[909](h0909.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the house top.<sup>[1406](h1406.md)</sup>

8  Mine enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> reproach<sup>[2778](h2778.md)</sup>
me all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the day;<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> and they
that are mad<sup>[1984](h1984.md)</sup> against me are
sworn<sup>[7650](h7650.md)</sup> against me.

9  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I have eaten<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup>
ashes<sup>[665](h0665)</sup> like bread,<sup>[3899](h3899.md)</sup> and
mingled<sup>[4537](h4537)</sup> my drink<sup>[8249](h8249.md)</sup> with
weeping,<sup>[1065](h1065.md)</sup>

10  Because<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> of thine
indignation<sup>[2195](h2195.md)</sup> and thy
wrath:<sup>[7110](h7110)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou hast
lifted me up,<sup>[5375](h5375.md)</sup> and cast me
down.<sup>[7993](h7993.md)</sup>

11  My days<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> are like a
shadow<sup>[6738](h6738)</sup> that declineth;<sup>[5186](h5186.md)</sup>
and I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am withered<sup>[3001](h3001.md)</sup> like
grass.<sup>[6212](h6212.md)</sup>

12  But thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
shalt endure<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> forever;<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>
and thy remembrance<sup>[2143](h2143.md)</sup> unto all
generations.<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> <sup>[1755](h1755.md)</sup>

13  Thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> shalt arise,<sup>[6965](h6965.md)</sup> and
have mercy upon<sup>[7355](h7355)</sup> Zion:<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the time<sup>[6256](h6256.md)</sup> to
favor<sup>[2603](h2603)</sup> her, yea,<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the set
time,<sup>[4150](h4150)</sup> is come.<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup>

14  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thy servants<sup>[5650](h5650.md)</sup>
take pleasure in<sup>[7521](h7521)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> her
stones,<sup>[68](h0068)</sup> and favor<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> the
dust<sup>[6083](h6083.md)</sup> thereof.

15  So the heathen<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> shall
fear<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> and
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the kings<sup>[4428](h4428.md)</sup> of the
earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> thy
glory.<sup>[3519](h3519.md)</sup>

16  When<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> shall
build up<sup>[1129](h1129)</sup> Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> he shall
appear<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> in his glory.<sup>[3519](h3519.md)</sup>

17  He will regard<sup>[6437](h6437)</sup> <sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> of the destitute,<sup>[6199](h6199.md)</sup>
and not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> despise<sup>[959](h0959.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> their prayer.<sup>[8605](h8605.md)</sup>

18  This<sup>[2063](h2063.md)</sup> shall be
written<sup>[3789](h3789.md)</sup> for the
generation<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> to come:<sup>[314](h0314.md)</sup> and
the people<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> which shall be
created<sup>[1254](h1254)</sup> shall praise<sup>[1984](h1984.md)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup>

19  For<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he hath looked
down<sup>[8259](h8259)</sup> from the height<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[4791](h4791)</sup> of his sanctuary;<sup>[6944](h6944.md)</sup> from
heaven<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup> did the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> behold<sup>[5027](h5027.md)</sup>
<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the earth;<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

20  To hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> the groaning<sup>[603](h0603.md)</sup>
of the prisoner;<sup>[615](h0615)</sup> to loose<sup>[6605](h6605.md)</sup>
those that are appointed<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> to
death;<sup>[8546](h8546.md)</sup>

21  To declare<sup>[5608](h5608)</sup> the name<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> in Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> and
his praise<sup>[8416](h8416)</sup> in Jerusalem;<sup>[3389](h3389.md)</sup>

22  When the people<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> are
gathered<sup>[6908](h6908)</sup> together,<sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup> and
the kingdoms,<sup>[4467](h4467)</sup> to serve<sup>[5647](h5647.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

23  He weakened<sup>[6031](h6031.md)</sup> my
strength<sup>[3581](h3581)</sup> in the way;<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> he
shortened<sup>[7114](h7114)</sup> my days.<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>

24  I said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> O my God,<sup>[410](h0410.md)</sup>
take<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> me not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> away in the
midst<sup>[2677](h2677)</sup> of my days:<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> thy
years<sup>[8141](h8141.md)</sup> are throughout all
generations.<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> <sup>[1755](h1755.md)</sup>

25  Of old<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> hast thou laid the
foundation<sup>[3245](h3245)</sup> of the earth:<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
and the heavens<sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup> are the
work<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of thy hands.<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

26  They<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> shall perish,<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup> but
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> shalt endure:<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup> yea,
all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> of them shall wax
old<sup>[1086](h1086)</sup> like a garment;<sup>[899](h0899.md)</sup> as a
vesture<sup>[3830](h3830.md)</sup> shalt thou
change<sup>[2498](h2498.md)</sup> them, and they shall be
changed:<sup>[2498](h2498.md)</sup>

27  But thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> art the
same,<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> and thy years<sup>[8141](h8141.md)</sup>
shall have no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> end.<sup>[8552](h8552.md)</sup>

28  The children<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> of thy
servants<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> shall continue,<sup>[7931](h7931.md)</sup>
and their seed<sup>[2233](h2233.md)</sup> shall be
established<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> before<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> thee.

[Sep](../sep/psa102.md)

1 τῷ Δαυιδ εὐλόγει ἡ ψυχή μου τὸν κύριον καί πάντα τὰ ἐντός μου τὸ ὄνομα
τὸ ἅγιον αὐτοῦ

2 εὐλόγει ἡ ψυχή μου τὸν κύριον καὶ μὴ ἐπιλανθάνου πάσας τὰς
ἀνταποδόσεις αὐτοῦ

3 τὸν εὐιλατεύοντα πάσαις ταῖς ἀνομίαις σου τὸν ἰώμενον πάσας τὰς νόσους
σου

4 τὸν λυτρούμενον ἐκ φθορᾶς τὴν ζωήν σου τὸν στεφανοῦντά σε ἐν ἐλέει καὶ
οἰκτιρμοῖς

5 τὸν ἐμπιπλῶντα ἐν ἀγαθοῖς τὴν ἐπιθυμίαν σου ἀνακαινισθήσεται ὡς ἀετοῦ
ἡ νεότης σου

6 ποιῶν ἐλεημοσύνας ὁ κύριος καὶ κρίμα πᾶσι τοῖς ἀδικουμένοις

7 ἐγνώρισεν τὰς ὁδοὺς αὐτοῦ τῷ Μωυσῇ τοῖς υἱοῖς Ισραηλ τὰ θελήματα αὐτοῦ

8 οἰκτίρμων καὶ ἐλεήμων ὁ κύριος μακρόθυμος καὶ πολυέλεος

9 οὐκ εἰς τέλος ὀργισθήσεται οὐδὲ εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα μηνιεῖ

10 οὐ κατὰ τὰς ἁμαρτίας ἡμῶν ἐποίησεν ἡμῖν οὐδὲ κατὰ τὰς ἀνομίας ἡμῶν
ἀνταπέδωκεν ἡμῖν

11 ὅτι κατὰ τὸ ὕψος τοῦ οὐρανοῦ ἀπὸ τῆς γῆς ἐκραταίωσεν κύριος τὸ ἔλεος
αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ τοὺς φοβουμένους αὐτόν

12 καθ᾽ ὅσον ἀπέχουσιν ἀνατολαὶ ἀπὸ δυσμῶν ἐμάκρυνεν ἀφ᾽ ἡμῶν τὰς
ἀνομίας ἡμῶν

13 καθὼς οἰκτίρει πατὴρ υἱούς οἰκτίρησεν κύριος τοὺς φοβουμένους αὐτόν

14 ὅτι αὐτὸς ἔγνω τὸ πλάσμα ἡμῶν μνήσθητι ὅτι χοῦς ἐσμεν

15 ἄνθρωπος ὡσεὶ χόρτος αἱ ἡμέραι αὐτοῦ ὡσεὶ ἄνθος τοῦ ἀγροῦ οὕτως
ἐξανθήσει

16 ὅτι πνεῦμα διῆλθεν ἐν αὐτῷ καὶ οὐχ ὑπάρξει καὶ οὐκ ἐπιγνώσεται ἔτι
τὸν τόπον αὐτοῦ

17 τὸ δὲ ἔλεος τοῦ κυρίου ἀπὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος καὶ ἕως τοῦ αἰῶνος ἐπὶ τοὺς
φοβουμένους αὐτόν καὶ ἡ δικαιοσύνη αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ υἱοὺς υἱῶν

18 τοῖς φυλάσσουσιν τὴν διαθήκην αὐτοῦ καὶ μεμνημένοις τῶν ἐντολῶν αὐτοῦ
τοῦ ποιῆσαι αὐτάς

19 κύριος ἐν τῷ οὐρανῷ ἡτοίμασεν τὸν θρόνον αὐτοῦ καὶ ἡ βασιλεία αὐτοῦ
πάντων δεσπόζει

20 εὐλογεῖτε τὸν κύριον πάντες οἱ ἄγγελοι αὐτοῦ δυνατοὶ ἰσχύι ποιοῦντες
τὸν λόγον αὐτοῦ τοῦ ἀκοῦσαι τῆς φωνῆς τῶν λόγων αὐτοῦ

21 εὐλογεῖτε τὸν κύριον πᾶσαι αἱ δυνάμεις αὐτοῦ λειτουργοὶ αὐτοῦ
ποιοῦντες τὸ θέλημα αὐτοῦ

22 εὐλογεῖτε τὸν κύριον πάντα τὰ ἔργα αὐτοῦ ἐν παντὶ τόπῳ τῆς δεσποτείας
αὐτοῦ εὐλόγει ἡ ψυχή μου τὸν κύριον

[Vul](../vul/psa102.md)

1 Ipsi David. \[Benedic, anima mea, Domino,  
et omnia quæ intra me sunt nomini sancto ejus.  

2 Benedic, anima mea, Domino,  
et noli oblivisci omnes retributiones ejus.  

3 Qui propitiatur omnibus iniquitatibus tuis;  
qui sanat omnes infirmitates tuas:  

4 qui redimit de interitu vitam tuam;  
qui coronat te in misericordia et miserationibus:  

5 qui replet in bonis desiderium tuum;  
renovabitur ut aquilæ juventus tua:  

6 faciens misericordias Dominus,  
et judicium omnibus injuriam patientibus.  

7 Notas fecit vias suas Moysi;  
filiis Israël voluntates suas.  

8 Miserator et misericors Dominus:  
longanimis, et multum misericors.  

9 Non in perpetuum irascetur,  
neque in æternum comminabitur.  

10 Non secundum peccata nostra fecit nobis,  
neque secundum iniquitates nostras retribuit nobis.  

11 Quoniam secundum altitudinem cæli a terra,  
corroboravit misericordiam suam super timentes se;  

12 quantum distat ortus ab occidente,  
longe fecit a nobis iniquitates nostras.  

13 Quomodo miseretur pater filiorum,  
misertus est Dominus timentibus se.  

14 Quoniam ipse cognovit figmentum nostrum;  
recordatus est quoniam pulvis sumus.  

15 Homo, sicut fœnum dies ejus;  
tamquam flos agri, sic efflorebit:  

16 quoniam spiritus pertransibit in illo, et non subsistet,  
et non cognoscet amplius locum suum.  

17 Misericordia autem Domini ab æterno,  
et usque in æternum super timentes eum.  
Et justitia illius in filios filiorum,  

18 his qui servant testamentum ejus,  
et memores sunt mandatorum ipsius ad faciendum ea.  

19 Dominus in cælo paravit sedem suam,  
et regnum ipsius omnibus dominabitur.  

20 Benedicite Domino, omnes angeli ejus:  
potentes virtute, facientes verbum illius,  
ad audiendam vocem sermonum ejus.  

21 Benedicite Domino, omnes virtutes ejus;  
ministri ejus, qui facitis voluntatem ejus.  

22 Benedicite Domino, omnia opera ejus:  
in omni loco dominationis ejus,  
benedic, anima mea, Domino.\]
