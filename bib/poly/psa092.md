### Psalms 92

[KJV](../kjv/psa092.md)

1  A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> or Song<sup>[7892](h7892.md)</sup> for
the sabbath<sup>[7676](h7676)</sup> day.<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> It is a
good<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup> thing to give
thanks<sup>[3034](h3034)</sup> unto the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
and to sing praises<sup>[2167](h2167.md)</sup> unto thy
name,<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> O most High:<sup>[5945](h5945.md)</sup>

2  To show forth<sup>[5046](h5046.md)</sup> thy
lovingkindness<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> in the
morning,<sup>[1242](h1242.md)</sup> and thy
faithfulness<sup>[530](h0530)</sup> every night,<sup>[3915](h3915.md)</sup>

3  Upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> an instrument of ten
strings,<sup>[6218](h6218)</sup> and upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
psaltery;<sup>[5035](h5035)</sup> upon the harp<sup>[3658](h3658.md)</sup>
with<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> a solemn sound.<sup>[1902](h1902.md)</sup>

4  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou, LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> hast
made me glad<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> through thy
work:<sup>[6467](h6467)</sup> I will triumph<sup>[7442](h7442.md)</sup> in
the works<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of thy hands.<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

5  O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> how<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup>
great<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup> are thy works!<sup>[4639](h4639.md)</sup> and
thy thoughts<sup>[4284](h4284)</sup> are very<sup>[3966](h3966.md)</sup>
deep.<sup>[6009](h6009.md)</sup>

6  A brutish<sup>[1198](h1198)</sup> man<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup>
knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> not;<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> doth a fool<sup>[3684](h3684.md)</sup>
understand<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
this.<sup>[2063](h2063.md)</sup>

7  When the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>
spring<sup>[6524](h6524)</sup> as<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> the
grass,<sup>[6212](h6212)</sup> and when all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of iniquity<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup> do
flourish;<sup>[6692](h6692.md)</sup> it is that they shall be
destroyed<sup>[8045](h8045)</sup> forever:<sup>[5703](h5703.md)</sup>
<sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup>

8  But thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> art
most high<sup>[4791](h4791)</sup> forevermore.<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

9  For,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> lo,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> thine
enemies,<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
for,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> lo,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> thine
enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> shall perish;<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the workers<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> of
iniquity<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup> shall be
scattered.<sup>[6504](h6504.md)</sup>

10  But my horn<sup>[7161](h7161.md)</sup> shalt thou
exalt<sup>[7311](h7311.md)</sup> like the horn of a
unicorn:<sup>[7214](h7214.md)</sup> I shall be
anointed<sup>[1101](h1101)</sup> with fresh<sup>[7488](h7488.md)</sup>
oil.<sup>[8081](h8081.md)</sup>

11  Mine eye<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> also shall
see<sup>[5027](h5027.md)</sup> my desire on mine
enemies,<sup>[7790](h7790)</sup> and mine ears<sup>[241](h0241.md)</sup>
shall hear<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> my desire of the
wicked<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> that rise up<sup>[6965](h6965.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me.

12  The righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> shall
flourish<sup>[6524](h6524.md)</sup> like the palm
tree:<sup>[8558](h8558)</sup> he shall grow<sup>[7685](h7685.md)</sup> like
a cedar<sup>[730](h0730)</sup> in Lebanon.<sup>[3844](h3844.md)</sup>

13  Those that be planted<sup>[8362](h8362.md)</sup> in the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> shall
flourish<sup>[6524](h6524)</sup> in the courts<sup>[2691](h2691.md)</sup>
of our God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

14  They shall still<sup>[5750](h5750.md)</sup> bring forth
fruit<sup>[5107](h5107)</sup> in old age;<sup>[7872](h7872.md)</sup> they
shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> fat<sup>[1879](h1879.md)</sup> and
flourishing;<sup>[7488](h7488.md)</sup>

15  To show<sup>[5046](h5046)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is upright:<sup>[3477](h3477.md)</sup> he is
my rock,<sup>[6697](h6697)</sup> and there is no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
unrighteousness<sup>[5766](h5766.md)</sup> in him.

[Sep](../sep/psa092.md)

1 εἰς τὴν ἡμέραν τοῦ προσαββάτου ὅτε κατῴκισται ἡ γῆ αἶνος ᾠδῆς τῷ Δαυιδ
ὁ κύριος ἐβασίλευσεν εὐπρέπειαν ἐνεδύσατο ἐνεδύσατο κύριος δύναμιν καὶ
περιεζώσατο καὶ γὰρ ἐστερέωσεν τὴν οἰκουμένην ἥτις οὐ σαλευθήσεται

2 ἕτοιμος ὁ θρόνος σου ἀπὸ τότε ἀπὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος σὺ εἶ

3 ἐπῆραν οἱ ποταμοί κύριε ἐπῆραν οἱ ποταμοὶ φωνὰς αὐτῶν

4 ἀπὸ φωνῶν ὑδάτων πολλῶν θαυμαστοὶ οἱ μετεωρισμοὶ τῆς θαλάσσης
θαυμαστὸς ἐν ὑψηλοῖς ὁ κύριος

5 τὰ μαρτύριά σου ἐπιστώθησαν σφόδρα τῷ οἴκῳ σου πρέπει ἁγίασμα κύριε
εἰς μακρότητα ἡμερῶν

‎7 ‏אִֽישׁ־בַּ֭עַר לֹ֣א יֵדָ֑ע וּ֝כְסִ֗יל לֹא־יָבִ֥ין אֶת־זֹֽאת׃

[Vul](../vul/psa092.md)

1 Laus cantici ipsi David, in die ante sabbatum, quando fundata est
terra. \[Dominus regnavit, decorem indutus est:  
indutus est Dominus fortitudinem, et præcinxit se.  
Etenim firmavit orbem terræ, qui non commovebitur.  

2 Parata sedes tua ex tunc;  
a sæculo tu es.  

3 Elevaverunt flumina, Domine,  
elevaverunt flumina vocem suam;  
elevaverunt flumina fluctus suos,  

4 a vocibus aquarum multarum.  
Mirabiles elationes maris;  
mirabilis in altis Dominus.  

5 Testimonia tua credibilia facta sunt nimis;  
domum tuam decet sanctitudo, Domine,  
in longitudinem dierum.\]
