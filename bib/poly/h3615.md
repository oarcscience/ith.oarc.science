H3615  
כּלה  
כָּלָה ‎ kâlâh  
*kaw-law‘*  
A primitive root; to *end*, whether intransitively (to *cease*, *be*
*finished*, *perish*) or transitively (to *complete*, *prepare*,
*consume*): - accomplish, cease, consume (away), determine, destroy
(utterly), be (when . . . were) done, (be an) end (of), expire, (cause
to) fail, faint, finish, fulfil, X fully, X have, leave (off), long,
bring to pass, wholly reap, make clean riddance, spend, quite take away,
waste.
