H6963  
קל קול  
קוֹל קוֹל ‎ qôl qôl  
*kole,* *kole*  
From an unused root meaning to *call* aloud; a *voice* or *sound: -* +
aloud, bleating, crackling, cry (+ out), fame, lightness, lowing, noise,
+ hold peace, \[pro-\] claim, proclamation, + sing, sound, + spark,
thunder (-ing), voice, + yell.
