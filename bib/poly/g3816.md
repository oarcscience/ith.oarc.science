G3816  
παῖς  
pais  
*paheece*  
Perhaps from [3817](g3817) a *boy* (as often *beaten* with impunity.md), or
(by analogy) a *girl*, and (generally) a *child*; specifically a *slave*
or *servant* (especially a *minister* to a king; and by eminence to
God): - child, maid (-en), (man) servant, son, young man.
