### Psalms 40

[KJV](../kjv/psa040)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329)</sup> A
Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> I
waited<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> patiently<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> for the
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and he inclined<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> me, and heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> my

2  He brought me up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> also out of a
horrible<sup>[7588](h7588)</sup> pit,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[953](h0953)</sup> out of the miry<sup>[3121](h3121)</sup>
clay,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2916](h2916)</sup> and
set<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> my feet<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> a rock,<sup>[5553](h5553)</sup> and

3  And he hath put<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> a new<sup>[2319](h2319)</sup>

4  Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> is that man<sup>[1397](h1397)</sup>

5  Many,<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> my
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> are thy wonderful
works<sup>[6381](h6381)</sup> which thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> hast
done,<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> and thy thoughts<sup>[4284](h4284)</sup>
which are to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> usward: they
cannot<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> be reckoned up in

6  Sacrifice<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup> and
offering<sup>[4503](h4503)</sup> thou didst not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
desire;<sup>[2654](h2654)</sup> mine ears<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> hast
thou opened:<sup>[3738](h3738)</sup> burnt
offering<sup>[5930](h5930)</sup> and sin
offering<sup>[2401](h2401)</sup> hast thou not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
required.<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup>

7  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I,
Lo,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> I come:<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> in the
volume<sup>[4039](h4039)</sup> of the book<sup>[5612](h5612)</sup> it is
written<sup>[3789](h3789)</sup> of<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me,

8  I delight<sup>[2654](h2654)</sup> to do<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> thy
will,<sup>[7522](h7522)</sup> O my God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> yea, thy
law<sup>[8451](h8451)</sup> is within<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup> my
heart.<sup>[4578](h4578)</sup>

9  I have preached<sup>[1319](h1319)</sup>
righteousness<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> in the
great<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> congregation:<sup>[6951](h6951)</sup>
lo,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> I have not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
refrained<sup>[3607](h3607)</sup> my lips,<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup>

10  I have not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> hid<sup>[3680](h3680)</sup> thy

heart;<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> I have declared<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>

salvation:<sup>[8668](h8668)</sup> I have not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>

11  Withhold<sup>[3607](h3607)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>

lovingkindness<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> and thy
truth<sup>[571](h0571)</sup> continually<sup>[8548](h8548)</sup>
preserve<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> me.

12  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> innumerable<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> <sup>[4557](h4557)</sup>
evils<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> have compassed me

not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> able<sup>[3201](h3201)</sup> to look

13  Be pleased,<sup>[7521](h7521)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
to deliver<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> me: O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
make haste<sup>[2363](h2363)</sup> to help<sup>[5833](h5833)</sup> me.

14  Let them be ashamed<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> and
confounded<sup>[2659](h2659)</sup> together<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> that
seek after<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> my soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> to
destroy<sup>[5595](h5595)</sup> it; let them be
driven<sup>[5472](h5472)</sup> backward<sup>[268](h0268)</sup> and put

15  Let them be desolate<sup>[8074](h8074)</sup>
for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> a reward<sup>[6118](h6118)</sup> of their
shame<sup>[1322](h1322)</sup> that say<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> unto me,

16  Let all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> those that
seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> thee rejoice<sup>[7797](h7797)</sup> and be

love<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> thy salvation<sup>[8668](h8668)</sup>
say<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> continually,<sup>[8548](h8548)</sup> The
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> be magnified.<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup>

17  But I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am poor<sup>[6041](h6041)</sup> and

[Sep](../sep/psa040)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 μακάριος ὁ συνίων ἐπὶ πτωχὸν καὶ πένητα ἐν ἡμέρᾳ πονηρᾷ ῥύσεται αὐτὸν
ὁ κύριος

3 κύριος διαφυλάξαι αὐτὸν καὶ ζήσαι αὐτὸν καὶ μακαρίσαι αὐτὸν ἐν τῇ γῇ
καὶ μὴ παραδῴη αὐτὸν εἰς χεῖρας ἐχθροῦ αὐτοῦ

4 κύριος βοηθήσαι αὐτῷ ἐπὶ κλίνης ὀδύνης αὐτοῦ ὅλην τὴν κοίτην αὐτοῦ
ἔστρεψας ἐν τῇ ἀρρωστίᾳ αὐτοῦ

5 ἐγὼ εἶπα κύριε ἐλέησόν με ἴασαι τὴν ψυχήν μου ὅτι ἥμαρτόν σοι

6 οἱ ἐχθροί μου εἶπαν κακά μοι πότε ἀποθανεῖται καὶ ἀπολεῖται τὸ ὄνομα
αὐτοῦ

7 καὶ εἰ εἰσεπορεύετο τοῦ ἰδεῖν μάτην ἐλάλει ἡ καρδία αὐτοῦ συνήγαγεν
ἀνομίαν ἑαυτῷ ἐξεπορεύετο ἔξω καὶ ἐλάλει

8 ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ ἐψιθύριζον πάντες οἱ ἐχθροί μου κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ
ἐλογίζοντο κακά μοι

9 λόγον παράνομον κατέθεντο κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ μὴ ὁ κοιμώμενος οὐχὶ προσθήσει τοῦ
ἀναστῆναι

10 καὶ γὰρ ὁ ἄνθρωπος τῆς εἰρήνης μου ἐφ᾽ ὃν ἤλπισα ὁ ἐσθίων ἄρτους μου
ἐμεγάλυνεν ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ πτερνισμόν

11 σὺ δέ κύριε ἐλέησόν με καὶ ἀνάστησόν με καὶ ἀνταποδώσω αὐτοῖς

12 ἐν τούτῳ ἔγνων ὅτι τεθέληκάς με ὅτι οὐ μὴ ἐπιχαρῇ ὁ ἐχθρός μου ἐπ᾽
ἐμέ

13 ἐμοῦ δὲ διὰ τὴν ἀκακίαν ἀντελάβου καὶ ἐβεβαίωσάς με ἐνώπιόν σου εἰς
τὸν αἰῶνα

14 εὐλογητὸς κύριος ὁ θεὸς Ισραηλ ἀπὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος καὶ εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα
γένοιτο γένοιτο

[Vul](../vul/psa040)

1 In finem. Psalmus ipsi David.

2 \[Beatus qui intelligit super egenum et pauperem:  
in die mala liberabit eum Dominus.  

3 Dominus conservet eum, et vivificet eum,  
et beatum faciat eum in terra,  
et non tradat eum in animam inimicorum ejus.  

4 Dominus opem ferat illi super lectum doloris ejus;  
universum stratum ejus versasti in infirmitate ejus.  

5 Ego dixi: Domine, miserere mei;  
sana animam meam, quia peccavi tibi.  

6 Inimici mei dixerunt mala mihi:  
Quando morietur, et peribit nomen ejus?  

7 Et si ingrediebatur ut videret, vana loquebatur;  
cor ejus congregavit iniquitatem sibi.  
Egrediebatur foras et loquebatur.  

8 In idipsum adversum me susurrabant omnes inimici mei;  
adversum me cogitabant mala mihi.  

9 Verbum iniquum constituerunt adversum me:  
Numquid qui dormit non adjiciet ut resurgat?  

10 Etenim homo pacis meæ in quo speravi,  
qui edebat panes meos,  
magnificavit super me supplantationem.  

11 Tu autem, Domine, miserere mei,  
et resuscita me; et retribuam eis.  

12 In hoc cognovi quoniam voluisti me,  
quoniam non gaudebit inimicus meus super me.  

13 Me autem propter innocentiam suscepisti;  
et confirmasti me in conspectu tuo in æternum.  

14 Benedictus Dominus Deus Israël  
a sæculo et usque in sæculum. Fiat, fiat.\]
