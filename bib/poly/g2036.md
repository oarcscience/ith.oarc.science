G2036  
ἔπω  
epō  
*ep‘-o*  
A primary verb (used only in the definite past tense, the others being
borrowed from [2046](g2046) [4483](g4483) and [5346](g5346.md) ; to *speak*
or *say* (by word or writting): - answer, bid, bring word, call,
command, grant, say (on), speak, tell. Compare [3004](g3004.md)
