H5265  
נסע  
נָסַע ‎ nâsa‛  
*naw-sah‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *pull* up, especially the tent pins, that
is, *start* on a journey: - cause to blow, bring, get, (make to) go
(away, forth, forward, onward, out), (take) journey, march, remove, set
aside (forward), X still, be on his (go their) way.
