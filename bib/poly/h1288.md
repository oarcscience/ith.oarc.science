H1288  
בּרך  
בָּרַך ‎ bârak  
*baw-rak‘*  
A primitive root; to *kneel*; by implication to *bless* God (as an act
of adoration), and (vice-versa) man (as a benefit); also (by euphemism)
to *curse* (God or the king, as treason): - X abundantly, X altogether,
X at all, blaspheme, bless, congratulate, curse, X greatly, X indeed,
kneel (down), praise, salute, X still, thank.
