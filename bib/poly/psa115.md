### Psalms 115

[KJV](../kjv/psa115.md)

1  Not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> unto us, O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> unto us, but<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> unto
thy name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> give<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
glory,<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> for<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> thy
mercy,<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> and for<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> thy
truth's<sup>[571](h0571.md)</sup> sake.

2  Wherefore<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> should the
heathen<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> say,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup>
Where<sup>[346](h0346)</sup> is now<sup>[4994](h4994.md)</sup> their
God?<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

3  But our God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> is in the
heavens:<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> he hath done<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
whatsoever<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> <sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> he hath
pleased.<sup>[2654](h2654.md)</sup>

4  Their idols<sup>[6091](h6091.md)</sup> are
silver<sup>[3701](h3701)</sup> and gold,<sup>[2091](h2091.md)</sup> the
work<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of men's<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>
hands.<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

5  They have mouths,<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> but they
speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
eyes<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> have they, but they
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

6  They have ears,<sup>[241](h0241.md)</sup> but they
hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
noses<sup>[639](h0639.md)</sup> have they, but they
smell<sup>[7306](h7306)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

7  They have hands,<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> but they
handle<sup>[4184](h4184)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
feet<sup>[7272](h7272.md)</sup> have they, but they
walk<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> speak<sup>[1897](h1897.md)</sup> they
through their throat.<sup>[1627](h1627.md)</sup>

8  They that make<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> them
are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like unto them;<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> so
is every one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup>
trusteth<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> in them.

9  O Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> trust<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> thou
in the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> is their
help<sup>[5828](h5828)</sup> and their shield.<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup>

10  O house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Aaron,<sup>[175](h0175.md)</sup>
trust<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> is their help<sup>[5828](h5828.md)</sup> and
their shield.<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup>

11  Ye that fear<sup>[3373](h3373.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> trust<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> in the
LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> is their
help<sup>[5828](h5828)</sup> and their shield.<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup>

12  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> hath been
mindful<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup> of us: he will
bless<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup> us; he will bless<sup>[1288](h1288.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of
Israel;<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> he will bless<sup>[1288](h1288.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of
Aaron.<sup>[175](h0175.md)</sup>

13  He will bless<sup>[1288](h1288.md)</sup> them that
fear<sup>[3373](h3373)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> both
small<sup>[6996](h6996)</sup> and<sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup>
great.<sup>[1419](h1419.md)</sup>

14  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> shall increase you more and
more,<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> you and your
children.<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup>

15  Ye<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> are blessed<sup>[1288](h1288.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> which made<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
heaven<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> and earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

16  The heaven,<sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup> even the
heavens,<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> are the LORD's:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
but the earth<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> hath he
given<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> to the children<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> of
men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

17  The dead<sup>[4191](h4191)</sup> praise<sup>[1984](h1984.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> any<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> that go
down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> into silence.<sup>[1745](h1745.md)</sup>

18  But we<sup>[587](h0587)</sup> will bless<sup>[1288](h1288.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup> from this time
forth<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup> and
forevermore.<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>
Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> the LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup>

3 τί ἀνταποδώσω τῷ κυρίῳ περὶ πάντων ὧν ἀνταπέδωκέν μοι

4 ποτήριον σωτηρίου λήμψομαι καὶ τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου ἐπικαλέσομαι

6 τίμιος ἐναντίον κυρίου ὁ θάνατος τῶν ὁσίων αὐτοῦ

7 ὦ κύριε ἐγὼ δοῦλος σός ἐγὼ δοῦλος σὸς καὶ υἱὸς τῆς παιδίσκης σου
διέρρηξας τοὺς δεσμούς μου

8 σοὶ θύσω θυσίαν αἰνέσεως

9 τὰς εὐχάς μου τῷ κυρίῳ ἀποδώσω ἐναντίον παντὸς τοῦ λαοῦ αὐτοῦ

10 ἐν αὐλαῖς οἴκου κυρίου ἐν μέσῳ σου Ιερουσαλημ

עַל־חַ֝סְדְּךָ֗ עַל־אֲמִתֶּֽךָ׃

‎2 ‏לָ֭מָּה יֹאמְר֣וּ הַגּוֹיִ֑ם אַיֵּה־נָ֝֗א אֱלֹהֵיהֶֽם׃

‎3 ‏וֵֽאלֹהֵ֥ינוּ בַשָּׁמָ֑יִם כֹּ֭ל אֲשֶׁר־חָפֵ֣ץ עָשָֽׂה׃

‎4 ‏עֲ‍ֽ֭צַבֵּיהֶם כֶּ֣סֶף וְזָהָ֑ב מַ֝עֲשֵׂ֗ה יְדֵ֣י אָדָֽם׃

‎5 ‏פֶּֽה־לָ֭הֶם וְלֹ֣א יְדַבֵּ֑רוּ עֵינַ֥יִם לָ֝הֶ֗ם וְלֹ֣א יִרְאֽוּ׃

‎6 ‏אָזְנַ֣יִם לָ֭הֶם וְלֹ֣א יִשְׁמָ֑עוּ אַ֥ף לָ֝הֶ֗ם וְלֹ֣א יְרִיחֽוּן׃

‎7 ‏יְדֵיהֶ֤ם׀ וְלֹ֬א יְמִישׁ֗וּן רַ֭גְלֵיהֶם וְלֹ֣א יְהַלֵּ֑כוּ
לֹֽא־יֶ֝הְגּ֗וּ בִּגְרוֹנָֽם׃

‎8 ‏כְּ֭מוֹהֶם יִהְי֣וּ עֹשֵׂיהֶ֑ם כֹּ֭ל אֲשֶׁר־בֹּטֵ֣חַ בָּהֶֽם׃

‎9 ‏יִ֭שְׂרָאֵל בְּטַ֣ח בַּיהוָ֑ה עֶזְרָ֖ם וּמָגִנָּ֣ם הֽוּא׃

‎10 ‏בֵּ֣ית אַ֭הֲרֹן בִּטְח֣וּ בַיהוָ֑ה עֶזְרָ֖ם וּמָגִנָּ֣ם הֽוּא׃

‎11 ‏יִרְאֵ֣י יְ֭הוָה בִּטְח֣וּ בַיהוָ֑ה עֶזְרָ֖ם וּמָגִנָּ֣ם הֽוּא׃

‎12 ‏יְהוָה֮ זְכָרָ֪נוּ יְבָ֫רֵ֥ךְ יְ֭בָרֵךְ אֶת־בֵּ֣ית יִשְׂרָאֵ֑ל
יְ֝בָרֵ֗ךְ אֶת־בֵּ֥ית אַהֲרֹֽן׃

‎13 ‏יְ֭בָרֵךְ יִרְאֵ֣י יְהוָ֑ה הַ֝קְּטַנִּ֗ים עִם־הַגְּדֹלִֽים׃

‎14 ‏יֹסֵ֣ף יְהוָ֣ה עֲלֵיכֶ֑ם עֲ֝לֵיכֶ֗ם וְעַל־בְּנֵיכֶֽם׃

‎15 ‏בְּרוּכִ֣ים אַ֭תֶּם לַיהוָ֑ה עֹ֝שֵׂ֗ה שָׁמַ֥יִם וָאָֽרֶץ׃

‎16 ‏הַשָּׁמַ֣יִם שָׁ֭מַיִם לַיהוָ֑ה וְ֝הָאָ֗רֶץ נָתַ֥ן לִבְנֵי־אָדָֽם׃

‎17 ‏לֹ֣א הַ֭מֵּתִים יְהַֽלְלוּ־יָ֑הּ וְ֝לֹ֗א כָּל־יֹרְדֵ֥י דוּמָֽה׃

‎18 ‏וַאֲנַ֤חְנוּ׀ נְבָ֘רֵ֤ךְ יָ֗הּ מֵֽעַתָּ֥ה וְעַד־עוֹלָ֗ם
הַֽלְלוּ־יָֽהּ׃

1 Alleluja. \[Credidi, propter quod locutus sum;  
ego autem humiliatus sum nimis.  

2 Ego dixi in excessu meo:  
Omnis homo mendax.  

3 Quid retribuam Domino  
pro omnibus quæ retribuit mihi?  

4 Calicem salutaris accipiam,  
et nomen Domini invocabo.  

5 Vota mea Domino reddam  
coram omni populo ejus.  

6 Pretiosa in conspectu Domini  
mors sanctorum ejus.  

7 O Domine, quia ego servus tuus;  
ego servus tuus, et filius ancillæ tuæ.  
Dirupisti vincula mea:  

8 tibi sacrificabo hostiam laudis,  
et nomen Domini invocabo.  

9 Vota mea Domino reddam  
in conspectu omnis populi ejus;  

10 in atriis domus Domini,  
in medio tui, Jerusalem.\]
