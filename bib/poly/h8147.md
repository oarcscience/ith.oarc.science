H8147  
שׁתּים שׁנים  
שְׁנַיִם שְׁתּתַּּיִם ‎ sh<sup>e</sup>nayim sh<sup>e</sup>ttayim  
*shen-ah‘-yim,* *shet-tah‘-yim*  
(The first form being dual of [8145](h8145.md) the second form being
feminine); *two*; also (as ordinal) *twofold: -* both, couple, double,
second, twain, + twelfth, + twelve, + twenty (sixscore) thousand, twice,
two.
