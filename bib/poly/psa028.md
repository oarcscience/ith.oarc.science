### Psalms 28

[KJV](../kjv/psa028)

1  A Psalm of David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> Unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup>
thee will I cry,<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
my rock;<sup>[6697](h6697)</sup> be not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
silent<sup>[2790](h2790)</sup> to<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> me:
lest,<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> if thou be silent<sup>[2790](h2790)</sup>
to<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> me, I become like<sup>[4911](h4911)</sup>
<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> them that go down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> into
the pit.<sup>[953](h0953)</sup>

2  Hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> the voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of my
supplications,<sup>[8469](h8469)</sup> when I
cry<sup>[7768](h7768)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thee, when I
lift up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> my hands<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup>
toward<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thy holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup>
oracle.<sup>[1687](h1687)</sup>

3  Draw<sup>[4900](h4900)</sup> me not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> away
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the wicked,<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> and
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of
iniquity,<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> which speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup>
peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> to<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> their
neighbors,<sup>[7453](h7453)</sup> but mischief<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>
is in their hearts.<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup>

4  Give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> them according to their
deeds,<sup>[6467](h6467)</sup> and according to the
wickedness<sup>[7455](h7455)</sup> of their
endeavors:<sup>[4611](h4611)</sup> give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> them
after the work<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of their
hands;<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> render<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> to them
their desert.<sup>[1576](h1576)</sup>

5  Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they regard<sup>[995](h0995)</sup>
<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> the
works<sup>[6468](h6468)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> nor
the operation<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of his
hands,<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> he shall destroy<sup>[2040](h2040)</sup>
them, and not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> build them
up.<sup>[1129](h1129)</sup>

6  Blessed<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup> be the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he hath heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup>
the voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of my
supplications.<sup>[8469](h8469)</sup>

7  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is my
strength<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> and my shield;<sup>[4043](h4043)</sup>
my heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> trusted<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in him,
and I am helped:<sup>[5826](h5826)</sup> therefore my
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> greatly rejoiceth;<sup>[5937](h5937)</sup>
and with my song<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> will I
praise<sup>[3034](h3034)</sup> him.

8  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is their
strength,<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> and he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> is the
saving<sup>[3444](h3444)</sup> strength<sup>[4581](h4581)</sup> of his
anointed.<sup>[4899](h4899)</sup>

9  Save<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> thy
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> and bless<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> thine inheritance:<sup>[5159](h5159)</sup>

up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> forever.<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa028)

1 ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ ἐξοδίου σκηνῆς ἐνέγκατε τῷ κυρίῳ υἱοὶ θεοῦ ἐνέγκατε τῷ
κυρίῳ υἱοὺς κριῶν ἐνέγκατε τῷ κυρίῳ δόξαν καὶ τιμήν

2 ἐνέγκατε τῷ κυρίῳ δόξαν ὀνόματι αὐτοῦ προσκυνήσατε τῷ κυρίῳ ἐν αὐλῇ
ἁγίᾳ αὐτοῦ

3 φωνὴ κυρίου ἐπὶ τῶν ὑδάτων ὁ θεὸς τῆς δόξης ἐβρόντησεν κύριος ἐπὶ
ὑδάτων πολλῶν

4 φωνὴ κυρίου ἐν ἰσχύι φωνὴ κυρίου ἐν μεγαλοπρεπείᾳ

5 φωνὴ κυρίου συντρίβοντος κέδρους καὶ συντρίψει κύριος τὰς κέδρους τοῦ
Λιβάνου

6 καὶ λεπτυνεῖ αὐτὰς ὡς τὸν μόσχον τὸν Λίβανον καὶ ὁ ἠγαπημένος ὡς υἱὸς
μονοκερώτων

7 φωνὴ κυρίου διακόπτοντος φλόγα πυρός

8 φωνὴ κυρίου συσσείοντος ἔρημον καὶ συσσείσει κύριος τὴν ἔρημον Καδης

9 φωνὴ κυρίου καταρτιζομένου ἐλάφους καὶ ἀποκαλύψει δρυμούς καὶ ἐν τῷ
ναῷ αὐτοῦ πᾶς τις λέγει δόξαν

10 κύριος τὸν κατακλυσμὸν κατοικιεῖ καὶ καθίεται κύριος βασιλεὺς εἰς τὸν
αἰῶνα

11 κύριος ἰσχὺν τῷ λαῷ αὐτοῦ δώσει κύριος εὐλογήσει τὸν λαὸν αὐτοῦ ἐν
εἰρήνῃ

[Vul](../vul/psa028)

1 Psalmus David, in consummatione tabernaculi. \[Afferte Domino, filii
Dei,  
afferte Domino filios arietum.  

2 Afferte Domino gloriam et honorem;  
afferte Domino gloriam nomini ejus;  
adorate Dominum in atrio sancto ejus.  

3 Vox Domini super aquas;  
Deus majestatis intonuit:  
Dominus super aquas multas.  

4 Vox Domini in virtute;  
vox Domini in magnificentia.  

5 Vox Domini confringentis cedros,  
et confringet Dominus cedros Libani:  

6 et comminuet eas, tamquam vitulum Libani,  
et dilectus quemadmodum filius unicornium.  

7 Vox Domini intercidentis flammam ignis;  

8 vox Domini concutientis desertum:  
et commovebit Dominus desertum Cades.  

9 Vox Domini præparantis cervos:  
et revelabit condensa,  
et in templo ejus omnes dicent gloriam.  

10 Dominus diluvium inhabitare facit,  
et sedebit Dominus rex in æternum.  

11 Dominus virtutem populo suo dabit;  
Dominus benedicet populo suo in pace.\]
