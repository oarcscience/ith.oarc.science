H1993  
המה  
הָמָה ‎ hâmâh  
*haw-maw‘*  
A primitive root (compare [1949](h1949) ; to *make* *a* *loud* *sound*
(like English “hum”); by implication to *be* *in* *great* *commotion* or
*tumult*, to *rage*, *war*, *moan*, *clamor: -* clamorous, concourse,
cry aloud, be disquieted, loud, mourn, be moved, make a noise, rage,
roar, sound, be troubled, make in tumult, tumultuous, be in an uproar.
