H4994  
נא  
נָא ‎ nâ‘  
*naw*  
A primitive particle of incitement and entreaty, which may usually be
rendered I *pray*, *now* or *then*; added mostly to verbs (in the
imperative or future), or to interjections, occasionally to an adverb or
conjugation: - I beseech (pray) thee (you), go to, now, oh.
