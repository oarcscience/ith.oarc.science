### Psalms 4

[KJV](../kjv/psa004)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329)</sup> on
Neginoth,<sup>[5058](h5058)</sup> A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> Hear<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> me when I
call,<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> O God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of my
righteousness:<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> thou hast
enlarged<sup>[7337](h7337)</sup> me when I was in
distress;<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> have mercy<sup>[2603](h2603)</sup>
upon me, and hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> my
prayer.<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup>

2  O ye sons<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of men,<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> how
long<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> will ye turn my
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> into shame?<sup>[3639](h3639)</sup> how
long will ye love<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> vanity,<sup>[7385](h7385)</sup>
and seek after<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> leasing?<sup>[3577](h3577)</sup>
Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

3  But know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath set apart<sup>[6395](h6395)</sup> him
that is godly<sup>[2623](h2623)</sup> for himself: the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> when I
call<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> him.

4  Stand in awe,<sup>[7264](h7264)</sup> and sin<sup>[2398](h2398)</sup>
not:<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> commune<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> with your own
heart<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> your
bed,<sup>[4904](h4904)</sup> and be still.<sup>[1826](h1826)</sup>
Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

5  Offer<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> the sacrifices<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup>
of righteousness,<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> and put your
trust<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

6  There be many<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> that
say,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> will
show<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> us any good?<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup>
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> lift thou up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> the
light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> of thy countenance<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> us.

7  Thou hast put<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
gladness<sup>[8057](h8057)</sup> in my heart,<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>
more than in the time<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6256](h6256)</sup>
that their corn<sup>[1715](h1715)</sup> and their
wine<sup>[8492](h8492)</sup> increased.<sup>[7231](h7231)</sup>

8  I will both<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> lay me
down<sup>[7901](h7901)</sup> in peace,<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> and
sleep:<sup>[3462](h3462)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
only<sup>[910](h0910)</sup> makest me dwell<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> in
safety.<sup>[983](h0983)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa004)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ἐν ψαλμοῖς ᾠδὴ τῷ Δαυιδ

2 ἐν τῷ ἐπικαλεῖσθαί με εἰσήκουσέν μου ὁ θεὸς τῆς δικαιοσύνης μου ἐν
θλίψει ἐπλάτυνάς μοι οἰκτίρησόν με καὶ εἰσάκουσον τῆς προσευχῆς μου

3 υἱοὶ ἀνθρώπων ἕως πότε βαρυκάρδιοι ἵνα τί ἀγαπᾶτε ματαιότητα καὶ
ζητεῖτε ψεῦδος διάψαλμα

4 καὶ γνῶτε ὅτι ἐθαυμάστωσεν κύριος τὸν ὅσιον αὐτοῦ κύριος εἰσακούσεταί
μου ἐν τῷ κεκραγέναι με πρὸς αὐτόν

5 ὀργίζεσθε καὶ μὴ ἁμαρτάνετε λέγετε ἐν ταῖς καρδίαις ὑμῶν καὶ ἐπὶ ταῖς
κοίταις ὑμῶν κατανύγητε διάψαλμα

6 θύσατε θυσίαν δικαιοσύνης καὶ ἐλπίσατε ἐπὶ κύριον

7 πολλοὶ λέγουσιν τίς δείξει ἡμῖν τὰ ἀγαθά ἐσημειώθη ἐφ᾽ ἡμᾶς τὸ φῶς τοῦ
προσώπου σου κύριε

8 ἔδωκας εὐφροσύνην εἰς τὴν καρδίαν μου ἀπὸ καιροῦ σίτου καὶ οἴνου καὶ
ἐλαίου αὐτῶν ἐπληθύνθησαν

9 ἐν εἰρήνῃ ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ κοιμηθήσομαι καὶ ὑπνώσω ὅτι σύ κύριε κατὰ μόνας
ἐπ᾽ ἐλπίδι κατῴκισάς με

[Vul](../vul/psa004)

1 In finem, in carminibus. Psalmus David.

2 \[Cum invocarem exaudivit me Deus justitiæ meæ,  
in tribulatione dilatasti mihi.  
Miserere mei, et exaudi orationem meam.  

3 Filii hominum, usquequo gravi corde?  
ut quid diligitis vanitatem, et quæritis mendacium?  

4 Et scitote quoniam mirificavit Dominus sanctum suum;  
Dominus exaudiet me cum clamavero ad eum.  

5 Irascimini, et nolite peccare;  
quæ dicitis in cordibus vestris, in cubilibus vestris compungimini.  

6 Sacrificate sacrificium justitiæ, et sperate in Domino.  
Multi dicunt: Quis ostendit nobis bona?  

7 Signatum est super nos lumen vultus tui, Domine:  
dedisti lætitiam in corde meo.  

8 A fructu frumenti, vini, et olei sui, multiplicati sunt.  

9 In pace in idipsum dormiam, et requiescam;  

10 quoniam tu, Domine, singulariter in spe constituisti me.\]
