H2778  
חרף  
חָרַף ‎ châraph  
*khaw-raf‘*  
A primitive root; to *pull* off, that is, (by implication) to *expose*
(as by *stripping*); specifically to *betroth* (as if a surrender);
figuratively to carp at, that is, *defame*; denominatively (from
[2779](h2779) to spend the *winter: -* betroth, blaspheme, defy,
jeopard, rail, reproach, upbraid.
