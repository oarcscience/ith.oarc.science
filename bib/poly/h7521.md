H7521  
רצה  
רָצָה ‎ râtsâh  
*raw-tsaw‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* *pleased* *with*; specifically to *satisfy* a
debt: - (be) accept (-able), accomplish, set affection, approve, consent
with, delight (self), enjoy, (be, have a) favour (-able), like, observe,
pardon, (be, have, take) please (-ure), reconcile self.
