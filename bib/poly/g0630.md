G630  
ἀπολύω  
apoluō  
*ap-ol-oo‘-o*  
From [575](g0575) and [3089](g3089) to *free* fully, that is,
(literally) *relieve*, *release*, *dismiss* (reflexively *depart*), or
(figuratively) *let* *die*, *pardon*, or (specifically) *divorce:* -
(let) depart, dismiss, divorce, forgive, let go, loose, put (send) away,
release, set at liberty.
