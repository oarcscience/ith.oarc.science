H6808  
צעה  
צָּעָה ‎ tsâ‛âh  
*tsaw-aw‘*  
A primitive root; to *tip* over (for the purpose of *spilling* or
*pouring* out), that is, (figuratively) *depopulate*; by implication to
*imprison* or *conquer*; (reflexively) to *lie* *down* (for coition): -
captive exile, travelling, (cause to) wander (-er).
