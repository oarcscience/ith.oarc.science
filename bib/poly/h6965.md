H6965  
קוּם  
קוּם ‎ qûm  
*koom*  
A primitive root; to *rise* (in various applications, literally,
figuratively, intensively and causatively): - abide, accomplish, X be
clearer, confirm, continue, decree, X be dim, endure, X enemy, enjoin,
get up, make good, help, hold, (help to) lift up (again), make, X but
newly, ordain, perform, pitch, raise (up), rear (up), remain, (a-) rise
(up) (again, against), rouse up, set (up), (e-) stablish, (make to)
stand (up), stir up, strengthen, succeed, (as-, make) sure (-ly), (be)
up (-hold, -rising).
