H4186  
משׁב מושׁב  
מוֹשָׁב מוֹשָׁב ‎ môshâb môshâb  
*mo-shawb‘,* *mo-shawb‘*  
From [3427](h3427) a *seat*; figuratively a *site*; abstractly a
*session*; by extension an *abode* (the place or the time); by
implication *population: -* assembly, dwell in, dwelling (-place),
wherein (that) dwelt (in), inhabited place, seat, sitting, situation,
sojourning.
