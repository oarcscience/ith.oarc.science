### Jonah 2

[KJV](../kjv/jon002)

1  Then Jonah<sup>[3124](h3124)</sup> prayed<sup>[6419](h6419)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> his
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> out of the fish's<sup>[1710](h1710)</sup>
belly,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4578](h4578)</sup>

2  And said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I cried<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> by
reason of mine affliction<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6869](h6869)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and he heard<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> me;
out of the belly<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[990](h0990)</sup> of
hell<sup>[7585](h7585)</sup> cried<sup>[7768](h7768)</sup> I, and thou
heardest<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> my voice.<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup>

3  For thou hadst cast<sup>[7993](h7993)</sup> me into the
deep,<sup>[4688](h4688)</sup> in the midst<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup> of
the seas;<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> and the floods<sup>[5104](h5104)</sup>
compassed me about:<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
thy billows<sup>[4867](h4867)</sup> and thy
waves<sup>[1530](h1530)</sup> passed<sup>[5674](h5674)</sup>
over<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me.

4  Then I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I am cast
out<sup>[1644](h1644)</sup> of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> thy sight;<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup>
yet<sup>[389](h0389)</sup> I will look<sup>[5027](h5027)</sup>
again<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup> toward<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thy
holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> temple.<sup>[1964](h1964)</sup>

5  The waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> compassed me
about,<sup>[661](h0661)</sup> even to<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the
soul:<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> the depth<sup>[8415](h8415)</sup> closed
me round about,<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> the
weeds<sup>[5488](h5488)</sup> were wrapped<sup>[2280](h2280)</sup> about
my head.<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup>

6  I went down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> to the
bottoms<sup>[7095](h7095)</sup> of the
mountains;<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> with
her bars<sup>[1280](h1280)</sup> was about<sup>[1157](h1157)</sup> me
forever:<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> yet hast thou brought
up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> my life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> from
corruption,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7845](h7845)</sup> O
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> my God.<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>

7  When my soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> fainted<sup>[5848](h5848)</sup>
within<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me I remembered<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and my
prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> came in<sup>[935](h0935)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thee, into<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thine
holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> temple.<sup>[1964](h1964)</sup>

8  They that observe<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup>
lying<sup>[7723](h7723)</sup> vanities<sup>[1892](h1892)</sup>
forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> their own mercy.<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup>

9  But I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> will sacrifice<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup>
unto thee with the voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of
thanksgiving;<sup>[8426](h8426)</sup> I will pay<sup>[7999](h7999)</sup>
that that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> I have vowed.<sup>[5087](h5087)</sup>
Salvation<sup>[3444](h3444)</sup> is of the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

10  And the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> spoke<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>
unto the fish,<sup>[1709](h1709)</sup> and it vomited
out<sup>[6958](h6958)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup>
Jonah<sup>[3124](h3124)</sup> upon<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
dry<sup>[3004](h3004)</sup> land.

[Sep](../sep/jon002)

1 καὶ προσέταξεν κύριος κήτει μεγάλῳ καταπιεῖν τὸν Ιωναν καὶ ἦν Ιωνας ἐν
τῇ κοιλίᾳ τοῦ κήτους τρεῖς ἡμέρας καὶ τρεῖς νύκτας

2 καὶ προσηύξατο Ιωνας πρὸς κύριον τὸν θεὸν αὐτοῦ ἐκ τῆς κοιλίας τοῦ
κήτους

3 καὶ εἶπεν ἐβόησα ἐν θλίψει μου πρὸς κύριον τὸν θεόν μου καὶ εἰσήκουσέν
μου ἐκ κοιλίας ᾅδου κραυγῆς μου ἤκουσας φωνῆς μου

4 ἀπέρριψάς με εἰς βάθη καρδίας θαλάσσης καὶ ποταμοί με ἐκύκλωσαν πάντες
οἱ μετεωρισμοί σου καὶ τὰ κύματά σου ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ διῆλθον

5 καὶ ἐγὼ εἶπα ἀπῶσμαι ἐξ ὀφθαλμῶν σου ἆρα προσθήσω τοῦ ἐπιβλέψαι πρὸς
τὸν ναὸν τὸν ἅγιόν σου

6 περιεχύθη ὕδωρ μοι ἕως ψυχῆς ἄβυσσος ἐκύκλωσέν με ἐσχάτη ἔδυ ἡ κεφαλή
μου εἰς σχισμὰς ὀρέων

7 κατέβην εἰς γῆν ἧς οἱ μοχλοὶ αὐτῆς κάτοχοι αἰώνιοι καὶ ἀναβήτω φθορὰ
ζωῆς μου κύριε ὁ θεός μου

8 ἐν τῷ ἐκλείπειν ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ τὴν ψυχήν μου τοῦ κυρίου ἐμνήσθην καὶ ἔλθοι
πρὸς σὲ ἡ προσευχή μου εἰς ναὸν ἅγιόν σου

9 φυλασσόμενοι μάταια καὶ ψευδῆ ἔλεος αὐτῶν ἐγκατέλιπον

10 ἐγὼ δὲ μετὰ φωνῆς αἰνέσεως καὶ ἐξομολογήσεως θύσω σοι ὅσα ηὐξάμην
ἀποδώσω σοι σωτηρίου τῷ κυρίῳ

11 καὶ προσετάγη τῷ κήτει καὶ ἐξέβαλεν τὸν Ιωναν ἐπὶ τὴν ξηράν

1 Et præparavit Dominus piscem grandem ut deglutiret Jonam: et erat
Jonas in ventre piscis tribus diebus et tribus noctibus.

2 Et oravit Jonas ad Dominum Deum suum de ventre piscis,

3 et dixit: \[Clamavi de tribulatione mea ad Dominum,  
et exaudivit me;  
de ventre inferi clamavi,  
et exaudisti vocem meam.  

4 Et projecisti me in profundum in corde maris,  
et flumen circumdedit me:  
omnes gurgites tui, et fluctus tui super me transierunt.  

5 Et ego dixi:  
Abjectus sum a conspectu oculorum tuorum;  
verumtamen rursus videbo templum sanctum tuum.  

6 Circumdederunt me aquæ usque ad animam:  
abyssus vallavit me,  
pelagus operuit caput meum.  

7 Ad extrema montium descendi;  
terræ vectes concluserunt me in æternum:  
et sublevabis de corruptione vitam meam, Domine Deus meus.  

8 Cum angustiaretur in me anima mea,  
Domini recordatus sum:  
ut veniat ad te oratio mea,  
ad templum sanctum tuum.  

9 Qui custodiunt vanitates frustra,  
misericordiam suam derelinquunt.  

10 Ego autem in voce laudis immolabo tibi:  
quæcumque vovi, reddam pro salute Domino.\]

11 Et dixit Dominus pisci, et evomuit Jonam in aridam.
