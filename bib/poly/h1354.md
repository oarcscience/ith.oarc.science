H1354  
גב  
גַּב ‎ gab  
*gab*  
From an unused root meaning to *hollow* or *curve*; the *back* (as
*rounded* (compare [1460](h1460) and [1479](h1479) ; by analogy the
*top* or *rim*, a *boss*, a *vault*, *arch* of eye, *bulwarks*, etc.: -
back, body, boss, eminent (higher) place, \[eye\] brows, nave, ring.
