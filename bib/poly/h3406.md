y<sup>e</sup>rêmôth  
*(1)* *yer-ee-mohth‘,* *(2,3)* *yer-ay-mohth‘*  
Feminine plural from [7311](h7311) *elevations*; *Jerimoth* or
*Jeremoth*, the name of twelve Israelites: - Jeremoth, Jerimoth, and
Ramoth \[from the margin\].
