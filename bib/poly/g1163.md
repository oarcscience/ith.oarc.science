G1163  
δεῖ  
dei  
*die,* *deh-on‘*  
Third person singular active present of [1210](g1210.md) also δεόν deon
which is neuter active participle of the same; both used impersonally;
*it* *is* (*was*, etc.) *necessary* (as *binding*): - behoved, be meet,
must (needs), (be) need (-ful), ought, should.
