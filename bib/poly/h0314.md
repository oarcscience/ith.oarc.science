H314  
אחרן אחרין  
אַחֲריוֹן אַחֲרוֹן ‎ ‘achăryôn ‘achărôn  
*akh-ar-one‘,* *akh-ar-one‘*  
From [309](h0309) *hinder*; generally *late* or *last*; specifically (as
facing the east) *western: -* after (-ward), to come, following, hind
(-er, -ermost, -most), last, latter, rereward, ut(ter)most.
