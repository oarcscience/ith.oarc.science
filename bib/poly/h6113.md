H6113  
עצר  
עָצַר ‎ ‛âtsar  
*aw-tsar‘*  
A primitive root; to *inclose*; by analogy to *hold* *back*; also to
*maintain*, *rule*, *assemble: -* X be able, close up, detain, fast,
keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, X reign, restrain,
retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self).
