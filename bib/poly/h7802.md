שׁושׁנּים עדוּת שׁוּשׁן עדוּת  
שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּת ‎ shûshan ‛êdûth shôshannı̂ym
‛êdûth  
*shoo-shan‘* *or* *sho-shan-neem‘* *(ay-dooth‘)*  
The second form being plural; from [7799](h7799) and [5715](h5715.md)
*lily* (or *trumpet*) *of* *assemblage*; *Shushan* *Eduth* or
*Shoshannim* *Eduth*, the title of a popular song: - Shoshannim-Eduth,
Shushan-eduth.
