### Psalms 86

[KJV](../kjv/psa086.md)

1  A Prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup>
Bow down<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> thine ear,<sup>[241](h0241.md)</sup> O
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hear<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup> me:
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> am
poor<sup>[6041](h6041)</sup> and needy.<sup>[34](h0034.md)</sup>

2  Preserve<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> my soul;<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> am
holy:<sup>[2623](h2623)</sup> O thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> my
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> thy
servant<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> that trusteth<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> in
thee.<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>

3  Be merciful<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> unto me, O
Lord:<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> I
cry<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thee
daily.<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> <sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>

4  Rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> the soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> of
thy servant:<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thee, O Lord,<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> do I
lift up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> my soul.<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou,<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup>
Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> art good,<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup> and ready
to forgive;<sup>[5546](h5546.md)</sup> and
plenteous<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> in mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> unto
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> them that call upon<sup>[7121](h7121.md)</sup>
thee.

6  Give ear,<sup>[238](h0238)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> unto
my prayer;<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> and attend<sup>[7181](h7181.md)</sup> to
the voice<sup>[6963](h6963.md)</sup> of my
supplications.<sup>[8469](h8469.md)</sup>

7  In the day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> of my
trouble<sup>[6869](h6869)</sup> I will call upon<sup>[7121](h7121.md)</sup>
thee: for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou wilt
answer<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup> me.

8  Among the gods<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> there is
none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> like unto thee,<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> O
Lord;<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> neither<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup> are there
any works like unto thy works.<sup>[4639](h4639.md)</sup>

9  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> nations<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup>
whom<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> thou hast made<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> shall
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> and worship<sup>[7812](h7812.md)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> thee, O Lord;<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> and
shall glorify<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> thy name.<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>

10  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> art
great,<sup>[1419](h1419)</sup> and doest<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
wondrous things:<sup>[6381](h6381)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> art
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> alone.<sup>[905](h0905.md)</sup>

11  Teach<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> me thy way,<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> O
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> I will walk<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> in thy
truth:<sup>[571](h0571)</sup> unite<sup>[3161](h3161.md)</sup> my
heart<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup> to fear<sup>[3372](h3372.md)</sup> thy
name.<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>

12  I will praise<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup> thee, O
Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> my God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> with
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> my heart:<sup>[3824](h3824.md)</sup> and I will
glorify<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> thy name<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>
forevermore.<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

13  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> great<sup>[1419](h1419.md)</sup> is thy
mercy<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> toward<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me: and
thou hast delivered<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> my
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> from the lowest<sup>[8482](h8482.md)</sup>
hell.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7585](h7585.md)</sup>

14  O God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> the proud<sup>[2086](h2086.md)</sup> are
risen<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me, and
the assemblies<sup>[5712](h5712.md)</sup> of
violent<sup>[6184](h6184.md)</sup> men have sought
after<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> my soul;<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> and have
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> set<sup>[7760](h7760.md)</sup> thee
before<sup>[5048](h5048.md)</sup> them.

15  But thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> O Lord,<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> art
a God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> full of compassion,<sup>[7349](h7349.md)</sup>
and gracious,<sup>[2587](h2587.md)</sup>
longsuffering,<sup>[750](h0750)</sup> <sup>[639](h0639.md)</sup> and
plenteous<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> in mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> and
truth.<sup>[571](h0571.md)</sup>

16  O turn<sup>[6437](h6437)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me, and
have mercy<sup>[2603](h2603)</sup> upon me; give<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
thy strength<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup> unto thy
servant,<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> and save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> the
son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of thine handmaid.<sup>[519](h0519.md)</sup>

17  Show<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> <sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup> me a
token<sup>[226](h0226)</sup> for good;<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup> that they
which hate<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup> me may see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup>
it, and be ashamed:<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou,<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup>
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hast helped<sup>[5826](h5826.md)</sup> me,
and comforted<sup>[5162](h5162.md)</sup> me.

[Sep](../sep/psa086.md)

1 τοῖς υἱοῖς Κορε ψαλμὸς ᾠδῆς οἱ θεμέλιοι αὐτοῦ ἐν τοῖς ὄρεσιν τοῖς
ἁγίοις

2 ἀγαπᾷ κύριος τὰς πύλας Σιων ὑπὲρ πάντα τὰ σκηνώματα Ιακωβ

3 δεδοξασμένα ἐλαλήθη περὶ σοῦ ἡ πόλις τοῦ θεοῦ διάψαλμα

4 μνησθήσομαι Ρααβ καὶ Βαβυλῶνος τοῖς γινώσκουσίν με καὶ ἰδοὺ ἀλλόφυλοι
καὶ Τύρος καὶ λαὸς Αἰθιόπων οὗτοι ἐγενήθησαν ἐκεῖ

5 μήτηρ Σιων ἐρεῖ ἄνθρωπος καὶ ἄνθρωπος ἐγενήθη ἐν αὐτῇ καὶ αὐτὸς
ἐθεμελίωσεν αὐτὴν ὁ ὕψιστος

6 κύριος διηγήσεται ἐν γραφῇ λαῶν καὶ ἀρχόντων τούτων τῶν γεγενημένων ἐν
αὐτῇ διάψαλμα

7 ὡς εὐφραινομένων πάντων ἡ κατοικία ἐν σοί

‎12 ‏אוֹדְךָ֤׀ אֲדֹנָ֣י אֱ֭לֹהַי בְּכָל־לְבָבִ֑י וַאֲכַבְּדָ֖ה שִׁמְךָ֣

[Vul](../vul/psa086.md)

1 Filiis Core. Psalmus cantici. \[Fundamenta ejus in montibus sanctis;  

2 diligit Dominus portas Sion super omnia tabernacula Jacob.  

3 Gloriosa dicta sunt de te, civitas Dei!  

4 Memor ero Rahab et Babylonis, scientium me;  
ecce alienigenæ, et Tyrus, et populus Æthiopum,  
hi fuerunt illic.  

5 Numquid Sion dicet: Homo et homo natus est in ea,  
et ipse fundavit eam Altissimus?  

6 Dominus narrabit in scripturis populorum et principum,  
horum qui fuerunt in ea.  

7 Sicut lætantium omnium  
habitatio est in te.\]
