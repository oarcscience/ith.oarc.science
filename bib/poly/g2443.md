G2443  
ἵνα  
hina  
*hin‘-ah*  
Probably from the same as the former part of [1438](g1438) (through the
*demonstrative* idea; compare [3588](g3588) ; in order *that* (denoting
the *purpose* or the *result*): - albeit, because, to the intent (that),
lest, so as, (so) that, (for) to. Compare [3363](g3363)
