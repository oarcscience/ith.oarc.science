H4578  
מעה  
מֵעֶה ‎ mê‛eh  
*may-aw‘*  
From an unused root probably meaning to *be* *soft*; used only in plural
the *intestines*, or (collectively) the *abdomen*, figuratively
*sympathy*; by implication a *vest*; by extension the *stomach*, the
*uterus* (or of men, the seat of generation), the *heart*
(figuratively): - belly, bowels, X heart, womb.
