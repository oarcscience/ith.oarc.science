### Psalms 114

[KJV](../kjv/psa114.md)

1  When Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> went out<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup>
of Egypt,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup> the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290.md)</sup> from a
people<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> of strange
language;<sup>[3937](h3937.md)</sup>

2  Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> was<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> his
sanctuary,<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> and Israel<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>
his dominion.<sup>[4475](h4475.md)</sup>

3  The sea<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> saw<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> it, and
fled:<sup>[5127](h5127)</sup> Jordan<sup>[3383](h3383.md)</sup> was
driven<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> back.<sup>[268](h0268.md)</sup>

4  The mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> skipped<sup>[7540](h7540.md)</sup>
like rams,<sup>[352](h0352.md)</sup> and the little
hills<sup>[1389](h1389)</sup> like lambs.<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup>
<sup>[6629](h6629.md)</sup>

5  What<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> ailed thee, O thou
sea,<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou
fleddest?<sup>[5127](h5127)</sup> thou Jordan,<sup>[3383](h3383.md)</sup>
that thou wast driven<sup>[5437](h5437.md)</sup>
back?<sup>[268](h0268.md)</sup>

6  Ye mountains,<sup>[2022](h2022.md)</sup> that ye
skipped<sup>[7540](h7540)</sup> like rams;<sup>[352](h0352.md)</sup> and ye
little hills,<sup>[1389](h1389.md)</sup> like
lambs?<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> <sup>[6629](h6629.md)</sup>

7  Tremble,<sup>[2342](h2342)</sup> thou earth,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
at the presence<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> of the
Lord,<sup>[113](h0113)</sup> at the presence<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of the God<sup>[433](h0433.md)</sup> of
Jacob;<sup>[3290](h3290.md)</sup>

8  Which turned<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup> the rock<sup>[6697](h6697.md)</sup>
into a standing<sup>[98](h0098)</sup> water,<sup>[4325](h4325.md)</sup> the
flint<sup>[2496](h2496)</sup> into a fountain<sup>[4599](h4599.md)</sup> of
waters.<sup>[4325](h4325.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa114.md)

1 αλληλουια ἠγάπησα ὅτι εἰσακούσεται κύριος τῆς φωνῆς τῆς δεήσεώς μου

2 ὅτι ἔκλινεν τὸ οὖς αὐτοῦ ἐμοί καὶ ἐν ταῖς ἡμέραις μου ἐπικαλέσομαι

3 περιέσχον με ὠδῖνες θανάτου κίνδυνοι ᾅδου εὕροσάν με θλῖψιν καὶ ὀδύνην
εὗρον

4 καὶ τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου ἐπεκαλεσάμην ὦ κύριε ῥῦσαι τὴν ψυχήν μου

5 ἐλεήμων ὁ κύριος καὶ δίκαιος καὶ ὁ θεὸς ἡμῶν ἐλεᾷ

6 φυλάσσων τὰ νήπια ὁ κύριος ἐταπεινώθην καὶ ἔσωσέν με

7 ἐπίστρεψον ἡ ψυχή μου εἰς τὴν ἀνάπαυσίν σου ὅτι κύριος εὐηργέτησέν σε

8 ὅτι ἐξείλατο τὴν ψυχήν μου ἐκ θανάτου τοὺς ὀφθαλμούς μου ἀπὸ δακρύων
καὶ τοὺς πόδας μου ἀπὸ ὀλισθήματος

9 εὐαρεστήσω ἐναντίον κυρίου ἐν χώρᾳ ζώντων

[Vul](../vul/psa114.md)

1 Alleluja. \[Dilexi, quoniam exaudiet Dominus  
vocem orationis meæ.  

2 Quia inclinavit aurem suam mihi,  
et in diebus meis invocabo.  

3 Circumdederunt me dolores mortis;  
et pericula inferni invenerunt me.  
Tribulationem et dolorem inveni,  

4 et nomen Domini invocavi:  
o Domine, libera animam meam.  

5 Misericors Dominus et justus,  
et Deus noster miseretur.  

6 Custodiens parvulos Dominus;  
humiliatus sum, et liberavit me.  

7 Convertere, anima mea, in requiem tuam,  
quia Dominus benefecit tibi:  

8 quia eripuit animam meam de morte,  
oculos meos a lacrimis,  
pedes meos a lapsu.  

9 Placebo Domino in regione vivorum.\]
