H6601  
פּתה  
פָּתָה ‎ pâthâh  
*paw-thaw‘*  
A primitive root; to *open*, that is, *be* (causatively *make*) *roomy*;
usually figuratively (in a mental or moral sense) to *be* (causatively
*make*) *simple* or (in a sinister way) *delude: -* allure, deceive,
enlarge, entice, flatter, persuade, silly (one).
