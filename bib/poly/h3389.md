H3389  
ירוּשׁלים ירוּשׁלם  
יְרוּשָׁלִַם יְרוּשָׁלַיִם ‎ y<sup>e</sup>rûshâlaim
y<sup>e</sup>rûshâlayim  
*yer-oo-shaw-lah‘-im,* *yer-oo-shaw-lah‘-yim*  
A dual (in allusion to its two main hills (the true pointing, at least
of the former reading, seems to be that of [3390](h3390) ); probably
from (the passive participle of) [3384](h3384) and [7999](h7999)
*founded* *peaceful*; *Jerushalaim* or *Jerushalem*, the capital city of
Palestine: - Jerusalem.
