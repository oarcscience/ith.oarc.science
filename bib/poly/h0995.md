H995  
בּין  
בִּין ‎ bı̂yn  
*bene*  
A primitive root; to *separate* mentally (or *distinguish*), that is,
(generally) *understand: -* attend, consider, be cunning, diligently,
direct, discern, eloquent, feel, inform, instruct, have intelligence,
know, look well to, mark, perceive, be prudent, regard, (can) skill
(-ful), teach, think, (cause, make to, get, give, have) understand
(-ing), view, (deal) wise (-ly, man).
