m<sup>e</sup>qômâh  
*(1,2)* *maw-kome‘,* *(3,4)* *mek-o-mah‘*  
From [6965](h6965.md) properly a *standing*, that is, a *spot*; but used
widely of a *locality* (generally or specifically); also (figuratively)
of a *condition* (of body or mind): - country, X home, X open, place,
room, space, X whither \[-soever\].
