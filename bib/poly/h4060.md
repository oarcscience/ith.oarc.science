H4060  
מדּה  
מִדָּה ‎ middâh  
*mid-daw‘*  
Feminine of [4055](h4055) properly *extension*, that is, height or
breadth; also a *measure* (including its standard); hence a *portion*
(as measured) or a *vestment*; specifically *tribute* (as measured): -
garment, measure (-ing, meteyard, piece, size, (great) stature, tribute,
wide.
