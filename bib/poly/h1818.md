H1818  
דּם  
דָּם ‎ dâm  
*dawm*  
From [1826](h1826) (compare [119](h0119) ; *blood* (as that which when
shed causes *death*) of man or an animal; by analogy the *juice* of the
grape; figuratively (especially in the plural) *bloodshed* (that is,
*drops* of blood): - blood (-y, -guiltiness, \[-thirsty\]), +
innocent.
