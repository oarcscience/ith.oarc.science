*(1,2)* *shem-o-neh‘,* *(3,4)* *shem-o-naw‘*  
Apparently from [8082](h8082.md) through the idea of *plumpness*; a
cardinal number, *eight* (as if a *surplus* above the “perfect” seven);
also (as ordinal) *eighth: -* eight (\[-een, -eenth\]), eighth.
