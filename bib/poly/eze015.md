### Ezekiel 15

[KJV](../kjv/eze015)

1  And the word<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> came<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> me, saying,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>

2  Son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of man,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup>
What<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> the
vine<sup>[1612](h1612)</sup> tree<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> more than
any<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
tree,<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> or than a branch<sup>[2156](h2156)</sup>
which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> among the
trees<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> of the forest?<sup>[3293](h3293)</sup>

3  Shall wood<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> be taken<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup>
thereof<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> to do<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> any
work?<sup>[4399](h4399)</sup> or<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> will men
take<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> a pin<sup>[3489](h3489)</sup>
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> it to hang<sup>[8518](h8518)</sup>
any<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> vessel<sup>[3627](h3627)</sup>
thereon?<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>

4  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> it is cast<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
into the fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> for fuel;<sup>[402](h0402)</sup>
the fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> devoureth<sup>[398](h0398)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> both<sup>[8147](h8147)</sup> the
ends<sup>[7098](h7098)</sup> of it, and the
midst<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup> of it is burned.<sup>[2787](h2787)</sup>
Is it meet<sup>[6743](h6743)</sup> for any work?<sup>[4399](h4399)</sup>

5  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> when it was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
whole,<sup>[8549](h8549)</sup> it was meet<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> for
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> work:<sup>[4399](h4399)</sup> how much
less<sup>[637](h0637)</sup> shall it be meet<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
yet<sup>[5750](h5750)</sup> for any work,<sup>[4399](h4399)</sup>
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> hath
devoured<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> it, and it is
burned?<sup>[2787](h2787)</sup>

6  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> thus<sup>[3541](h3541)</sup>
saith<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> the Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup>
GOD;<sup>[3069](h3069)</sup> As<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> the
vine<sup>[1612](h1612)</sup> tree<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> among the
trees<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> of the forest,<sup>[3293](h3293)</sup>
which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> I have given<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> to the
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> for fuel,<sup>[402](h0402)</sup>
so<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> will I give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the inhabitants<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> of
Jerusalem.<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup>

7  And I will set<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> my
face<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> against them; they shall go
out<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> from one fire,<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> and
another fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> shall devour<sup>[398](h0398)</sup>

that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> when I set<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> my face<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> against them.

8  And I will make<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> desolate,<sup>[8077](h8077)</sup>

GOD.<sup>[3069](h3069)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/eze015)

1 καὶ ἐγένετο λόγος κυρίου πρός με λέγων

2 καὶ σύ υἱὲ ἀνθρώπου τί ἂν γένοιτο τὸ ξύλον τῆς ἀμπέλου ἐκ πάντων τῶν
ξύλων τῶν κλημάτων τῶν ὄντων ἐν τοῖς ξύλοις τοῦ δρυμοῦ

3 εἰ λήμψονται ἐξ αὐτῆς ξύλον τοῦ ποιῆσαι εἰς ἐργασίαν εἰ λήμψονται ἐξ
αὐτῆς πάσσαλον τοῦ κρεμάσαι ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν πᾶν σκεῦος

4 πάρεξ πυρὶ δέδοται εἰς ἀνάλωσιν τὴν κατ᾽ ἐνιαυτὸν κάθαρσιν ἀπ᾽ αὐτῆς
ἀναλίσκει τὸ πῦρ καὶ ἐκλείπει εἰς τέλος μὴ χρήσιμον ἔσται εἰς ἐργασίαν

5 οὐδὲ ἔτι αὐτοῦ ὄντος ὁλοκλήρου οὐκ ἔσται εἰς ἐργασίαν μὴ ὅτι ἐὰν καὶ
πῦρ αὐτὸ ἀναλώσῃ εἰς τέλος εἰ ἔσται ἔτι εἰς ἐργασίαν

6 διὰ τοῦτο εἰπόν τάδε λέγει κύριος ὃν τρόπον τὸ ξύλον τῆς ἀμπέλου ἐν
τοῖς ξύλοις τοῦ δρυμοῦ ὃ δέδωκα αὐτὸ τῷ πυρὶ εἰς ἀνάλωσιν οὕτως δέδωκα
τοὺς κατοικοῦντας Ιερουσαλημ

7 καὶ δώσω τὸ πρόσωπόν μου ἐπ᾽ αὐτούς ἐκ τοῦ πυρὸς ἐξελεύσονται καὶ πῦρ
αὐτοὺς καταφάγεται καὶ ἐπιγνώσονται ὅτι ἐγὼ κύριος ἐν τῷ στηρίσαι με τὸ
πρόσωπόν μου ἐπ᾽ αὐτούς

8 καὶ δώσω τὴν γῆν εἰς ἀφανισμὸν ἀνθ᾽ ὧν παρέπεσον παραπτώματι λέγει
κύριος

[Vul](../vul/eze015)

1 Et factus est sermo Domini ad me, dicens:

2 \[Fili hominis, quid fiet de ligno vitis,  
ex omnibus lignis nemorum  
quæ sunt inter ligna silvarum?  

3 numquid tolletur de ea lignum ut fiat opus,  
aut fabricabitur de ea paxillus  
ut dependeat in eo quodcumque vas?  

4 Ecce igni datum est in escam:  
utramque partem ejus consumpsit ignis,  
et medietas ejus redacta est in favillam:  
numquid utile erit ad opus?  

5 Etiam cum esset integrum,  
non erat aptum ad opus:  
quanto magis cum illud ignis devoraverit et combusserit,  
nihil ex eo fiet operis?  

6 Propterea hæc dicit Dominus Deus:  
Quomodo lignum vitis inter ligna silvarum,  
quod dedi igni ad devorandum,  
sic tradam habitatores Jerusalem.  

7 Et ponam faciem meam in eos:  
de igne egredientur,  
et ignis consumet eos:  
et scietis quia ego Dominus,  
cum posuero faciem meam in eos,  

8 et dedero terram inviam et desolatam,  
eo quod prævaricatores extiterint,  
dicit Dominus Deus.\]
