H3996
מבוא
מָבוֹא ‎ mâbô‘
maw-bo‘
From 935 an entrance (the place or the act); specifically (with or without 8121 sunset or the west; also (adverbially with preposition) towards: - by which came, as cometh, in coming, as men enter into, entering, entrance into, entry, where goeth, going down, + westward. Compare 4126
