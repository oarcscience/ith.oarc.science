H1419
גּדל גּדול
גָּדוֹל גָּדוֹל ‎ gâdôl gâdôl
gaw-dole‘, gaw-dole‘
From 1431 great (in any sense); hence older; also insolent: - + aloud, elder (-est), + exceeding (-ly), + far, (man of) great (man, matter, thing, -er, -ness), high, long, loud, mighty, more, much, noble, proud thing, X sore, (´) very.
