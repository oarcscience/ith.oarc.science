H7138  
קרב קרוב  
קָרוֹב קָרוֹב ‎ qârôb qârôb  
*kaw-robe‘,* *kaw-robe‘*  
From [7126](h7126) *near* (in place, kindred or time.md): - allied,
approach, at hand, + any of kin, kinsfolk (-sman), (that is) near (of
kin), neighbour, (that is) next, (them that come) nigh (at hand), more
ready, short (-ly).
