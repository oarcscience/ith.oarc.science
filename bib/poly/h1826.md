H1826  
דּמם  
דָּמַם ‎ dâmam  
*daw-mam‘*  
A primitive root (compare [1724](h1724) [1820](h1820) ; to *be* *dumb*;
by implication to be astonished, to *stop*; also to *perish: -* cease,
be cut down (off), forbear, hold peace, quiet self, rest, be silent,
keep (put to) silence, be (stand), still, tarry, wait.
