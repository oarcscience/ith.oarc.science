### Psalms 112

[KJV](../kjv/psa112)

1  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> ye the LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup>
Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> is the man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> that
feareth<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> that delighteth<sup>[2654](h2654)</sup>
greatly<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> in his
commandments.<sup>[4687](h4687)</sup>

2  His seed<sup>[2233](h2233)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
mighty<sup>[1368](h1368)</sup> upon earth:<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> the
generation<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> of the
upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> shall be
blessed.<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup>

3  Wealth<sup>[1952](h1952)</sup> and riches<sup>[6239](h6239)</sup>
shall be in his house:<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> and his
righteousness<sup>[6666](h6666)</sup> endureth<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup>
forever.<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup>

4  Unto the upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> there
ariseth<sup>[2224](h2224)</sup> light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> in the
darkness:<sup>[2822](h2822)</sup> he is
gracious,<sup>[2587](h2587)</sup> and full of
compassion,<sup>[7349](h7349)</sup> and
righteous.<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup>

5  A good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> showeth
favor,<sup>[2603](h2603)</sup> and lendeth:<sup>[3867](h3867)</sup> he
will guide<sup>[3557](h3557)</sup> his affairs<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup>
with discretion.<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup>

6  Surely<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
be moved<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> forever:<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> in
everlasting<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> remembrance.<sup>[2143](h2143)</sup>

7  He shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be
afraid<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> of evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>
tidings:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8052](h8052)</sup> his
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> is fixed,<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup>
trusting<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

8  His heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> is
established,<sup>[5564](h5564)</sup> he shall
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be afraid,<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup>
until<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[834](h0834)</sup> he
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> his desire upon his
enemies.<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup>

9  He hath dispersed,<sup>[6340](h6340)</sup> he hath
given<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> to the poor;<sup>[34](h0034)</sup> his
righteousness<sup>[6666](h6666)</sup> endureth<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup>
forever;<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup> his horn<sup>[7161](h7161)</sup> shall
be exalted<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> with honor.<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup>

10  The wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> shall see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
it, and be grieved;<sup>[3707](h3707)</sup> he shall
gnash<sup>[2786](h2786)</sup> with his teeth,<sup>[8127](h8127)</sup>
and melt away:<sup>[4549](h4549)</sup> the
desire<sup>[8378](h8378)</sup> of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup>
shall perish.<sup>[6](h0006)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa112)

1 αλληλουια αἰνεῖτε παῖδες κύριον αἰνεῖτε τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου

2 εἴη τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου εὐλογημένον ἀπὸ τοῦ νῦν καὶ ἕως τοῦ αἰῶνος

3 ἀπὸ ἀνατολῶν ἡλίου μέχρι δυσμῶν αἰνεῖτε τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου

4 ὑψηλὸς ἐπὶ πάντα τὰ ἔθνη ὁ κύριος ἐπὶ τοὺς οὐρανοὺς ἡ δόξα αὐτοῦ

5 τίς ὡς κύριος ὁ θεὸς ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν ὑψηλοῖς κατοικῶν

6 καὶ τὰ ταπεινὰ ἐφορῶν ἐν τῷ οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐν τῇ γῇ

7 ὁ ἐγείρων ἀπὸ γῆς πτωχὸν καὶ ἀπὸ κοπρίας ἀνυψῶν πένητα

8 τοῦ καθίσαι αὐτὸν μετὰ ἀρχόντων μετὰ ἀρχόντων λαοῦ αὐτοῦ

9 ὁ κατοικίζων στεῖραν ἐν οἴκῳ μητέρα τέκνων εὐφραινομένην

[Vul](../vul/psa112)

1 Alleluja. \[Laudate, pueri, Dominum;  
laudate nomen Domini.  

2 Sit nomen Domini benedictum  
ex hoc nunc et usque in sæculum.  

3 A solis ortu usque ad occasum  
laudabile nomen Domini.  

4 Excelsus super omnes gentes Dominus,  
et super cælos gloria ejus.  

5 Quis sicut Dominus Deus noster, qui in altis habitat,  

6 et humilia respicit in cælo et in terra?  

7 Suscitans a terra inopem,  
et de stercore erigens pauperem:  

8 ut collocet eum cum principibus,  
cum principibus populi sui.  

9 Qui habitare facit sterilem in domo,  
matrem filiorum lætantem.\]
