סבב  
סָבַב ‎ sâbab  
*saw-bab‘*  
A primitive root; to *revolve*, *surround* or *border*; used in various
applications, literally and figuratively: - bring, cast, fetch, lead,
make, walk, X whirl, X round about, be about on every side, apply,
avoid, beset (about), besiege, bring again, carry (about), change, cause
to come about, X circuit, (fetch a) compass (about, round), drive,
environ, X on every side, beset (close, come, compass, go, stand) round
about, remove, return, set, sit down, turn (self) (about, aside, away,
back).
