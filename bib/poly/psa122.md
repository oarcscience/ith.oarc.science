### Psalms 122

[KJV](../kjv/psa122.md)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees<sup>[4609](h4609.md)</sup> of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> I was glad<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> when
they said<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> unto me, Let us
go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> into the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

2  Our feet<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> shall<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
stand<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup> within thy gates,<sup>[8179](h8179.md)</sup>
O Jerusalem.<sup>[3389](h3389.md)</sup>

3  Jerusalem<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> is built<sup>[1129](h1129.md)</sup> as
a city<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> that is compact<sup>[7945](h7945.md)</sup>
<sup>[2266](h2266)</sup> together:<sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup>

4  Whither<sup>[7945](h7945)</sup> <sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> the
tribes<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> go up,<sup>[5927](h5927.md)</sup> the
tribes<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup> unto
the testimony<sup>[5715](h5715)</sup> of Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>
to give thanks<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup> unto the
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> of the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> there<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> are
set<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> thrones<sup>[3678](h3678.md)</sup> of
judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> the thrones<sup>[3678](h3678.md)</sup> of
the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup>

6  Pray<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> for the peace<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> of
Jerusalem:<sup>[3389](h3389.md)</sup> they shall
prosper<sup>[7951](h7951)</sup> that love<sup>[157](h0157.md)</sup> thee.

7  Peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> within thy
walls,<sup>[2426](h2426)</sup> and prosperity<sup>[7962](h7962.md)</sup>
within thy palaces.<sup>[759](h0759.md)</sup>

8  For<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> my brethren<sup>[251](h0251.md)</sup> and
companions'<sup>[7453](h7453.md)</sup> sakes, I will
now<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> say,<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
Peace<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> be within thee.

9  Because of<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> our God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> I
will seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> thy good.<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa122.md)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν πρὸς σὲ ἦρα τοὺς ὀφθαλμούς μου τὸν κατοικοῦντα ἐν τῷ
οὐρανῷ

2 ἰδοὺ ὡς ὀφθαλμοὶ δούλων εἰς χεῖρας τῶν κυρίων αὐτῶν ὡς ὀφθαλμοὶ
παιδίσκης εἰς χεῖρας τῆς κυρίας αὐτῆς οὕτως οἱ ὀφθαλμοὶ ἡμῶν πρὸς κύριον
τὸν θεὸν ἡμῶν ἕως οὗ οἰκτιρήσαι ἡμᾶς

3 ἐλέησον ἡμᾶς κύριε ἐλέησον ἡμᾶς ὅτι ἐπὶ πολὺ ἐπλήσθημεν ἐξουδενώσεως

4 ἐπὶ πλεῖον ἐπλήσθη ἡ ψυχὴ ἡμῶν τὸ ὄνειδος τοῖς εὐθηνοῦσιν καὶ ἡ
ἐξουδένωσις τοῖς ὑπερηφάνοις

[Vul](../vul/psa122.md)

1 Canticum graduum. \[Ad te levavi oculos meos,  
qui habitas in cælis.  

2 Ecce sicut oculi servorum  
in manibus dominorum suorum;  
sicut oculi ancillæ  
in manibus dominæ suæ:  
ita oculi nostri ad Dominum Deum nostrum,  
donec misereatur nostri.  

3 Miserere nostri, Domine, miserere nostri,  
quia multum repleti sumus despectione;  

4 quia multum repleta est anima nostra  
opprobrium abundantibus, et despectio superbis.\]
