G3778  
οὗτος, οὗτοι, αὕτη, αὕται  
houtos houtoi hautē hautai  
*hoo‘-tos,* *hoo‘-toy,* *how‘-tay,* *how‘-tahee*  
Including the nominative masculine plural (second form), nominative
feminine signular (third form), and the nominate feminine plural,
(fourth form). From the article [3588](g3588) and [846](g0846.md) *the*
*he* (*she* or it), that is, *this* or *that* (often with the article
repeated): - he (it was that), hereof, it, she, such as, the same,
these, they, this (man, same, woman), which, who.
