G1097  
γινώσκω  
ginōskō  
*ghin-oce‘-ko*  
A prolonged form of a primary verb; to “know” (absolutely), in a great
variety of applications and with many implications (as shown at left,
with others not thus clearly expressed): - allow, be aware (of), feel,
(have) known (-ledge), perceive, be resolved, can speak, be sure,
understand.
