G3303  
μέν  
men  
*men*  
A primary particle; properly indicative of *affirmation* or *concession*
(*in* *fact*); usually followed by a *contrasted* clause with
[1161](g1161) (*this* one, the *former*, etc.: - even, indeed, so, some,
truly, verily. Often compounded with other particles in an *intensive*
or *asseverative* sense.
