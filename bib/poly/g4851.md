G4851  
συμφέρω  
sumpherō  
*soom-fer‘-o*  
From [4862](g4862) and [5342](g5342) (including its alternate.md); to
*bear* *together* (*contribute*), that is, (literally) to *collect*, or
(figuratively) to *conduce*; especially (neuter participle as noun)
*advantage:* - be better for, bring together, be expedient (for), be
good, (be) profit (-able for).
