G4334  
προσέρχομαι  
proserchomai  
*pros-er‘-khom-ahee*  
From [4314](g4314) and [2064](g2064) (including its alternate); to
*approach*, that is, (literally) *come* *near*, *visit*, or
(figuratively) *worship*, *assent* *to:* - (as soon as he) come (unto),
come thereunto, consent, draw near, go (near, to, unto).
