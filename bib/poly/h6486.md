H6486  
פּקדּה  
פְּקוּדָּה ‎ p<sup>e</sup>qûddâh  
*pek-ood-daw‘*  
Feminine passive participle of [6485](h6485.md) *visitation* (in many
senses, chiefly official): - account, (that have the) charge, custody,
that which . . . laid up, numbers, office (-r), ordering, oversight, +
prison, reckoning, visitation.
