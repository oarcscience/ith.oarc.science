H6584  
פּשׁט  
פָּשַׁט ‎ pâshaṭ  
*paw-shat‘*  
A primitive root; to *spread* out (that is, *deploy* in hostile array);
by analogy to *strip* (that is, *unclothe*, *plunder*, *flay*, etc.): -
fall upon, flay, invade, make an invasion, pull off, put off, make a
road, run upon, rush, set, spoil, spread selves (abroad), strip (off,
self).
