H8628  
תּקע  
תָּּקַע ‎ tâqa‛  
*taw-kah‘*  
A primitive root; to *clatter*, that is, *slap* (the hands together),
*clang* (an instrument); by analogy to *drive* (a nail or tent pin, a
dart, etc.); by implication to *become* *bondsman* (by handclasping): -
blow (\[a trumpet\]), cast, clap, fasten, pitch \[tent\], smite, sound,
strike, X suretiship, thrust.
