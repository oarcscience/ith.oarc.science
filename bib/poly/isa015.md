### Isaiah 15

[KJV](../kjv/isa015)

1  The burden<sup>[4853](h4853)</sup> of Moab.<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup>
Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in the night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup>
Ar<sup>[6144](h6144)</sup> of Moab<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> is laid
waste,<sup>[7703](h7703)</sup> and brought to
silence;<sup>[1820](h1820)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in the
night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> Kir<sup>[7024](h7024)</sup> of
Moab<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> is laid waste,<sup>[7703](h7703)</sup> and
brought to silence;<sup>[1820](h1820)</sup>

2  He is gone up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> to
Bajith,<sup>[1006](h1006)</sup> and to Dibon,<sup>[1769](h1769)</sup>
the high places,<sup>[1116](h1116)</sup> to
weep:<sup>[1065](h1065)</sup> Moab<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> shall
howl<sup>[3213](h3213)</sup> over<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
Nebo,<sup>[5015](h5015)</sup> and over<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
Medeba:<sup>[4311](h4311)</sup> on all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> their
heads<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> shall be baldness,<sup>[7144](h7144)</sup>
and every<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> beard<sup>[2206](h2206)</sup> cut
off.<sup>[1639](h1639)</sup>

3  In their streets<sup>[2351](h2351)</sup> they shall
gird<sup>[2296](h2296)</sup> themselves with
sackcloth:<sup>[8242](h8242)</sup> on<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the tops
of their houses,<sup>[1406](h1406)</sup> and in their
streets,<sup>[7339](h7339)</sup> every one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> shall
howl,<sup>[3213](h3213)</sup> weeping<sup>[1065](h1065)</sup>
abundantly.<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup>

4  And Heshbon<sup>[2809](h2809)</sup> shall
cry,<sup>[2199](h2199)</sup> and Elealeh:<sup>[500](h0500)</sup> their
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> shall be heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup>
even unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> Jahaz:<sup>[3096](h3096)</sup>
therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> the armed
soldiers<sup>[2502](h2502)</sup> of Moab<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> shall
cry out;<sup>[7321](h7321)</sup> his life<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> shall
be grievous<sup>[3415](h3415)</sup> unto him.

5  My heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> shall cry<sup>[2199](h2199)</sup>
out for Moab;<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> his
fugitives<sup>[1280](h1280)</sup> shall flee
unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> Zoar,<sup>[6820](h6820)</sup> a
heifer<sup>[5697](h5697)</sup> of three years
old:<sup>[7992](h7992)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> by the mounting
up<sup>[4608](h4608)</sup> of Luhith<sup>[3872](h3872)</sup> with
weeping<sup>[1065](h1065)</sup> shall they go it
up;<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in the
way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> of Horonaim<sup>[2773](h2773)</sup> they
shall raise up<sup>[5782](h5782)</sup> a cry<sup>[2201](h2201)</sup> of
destruction.<sup>[7667](h7667)</sup>

6  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> of
Nimrim<sup>[5249](h5249)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
desolate:<sup>[4923](h4923)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the
hay<sup>[2682](h2682)</sup> is withered away,<sup>[3001](h3001)</sup>
the grass<sup>[1877](h1877)</sup> faileth,<sup>[3615](h3615)</sup> there
is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> green
thing.<sup>[3418](h3418)</sup>

7  Therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> the
abundance<sup>[3502](h3502)</sup> they have
gotten,<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> and that which they have laid
up,<sup>[6486](h6486)</sup> shall they carry
away<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> to<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
brook<sup>[5158](h5158)</sup> of the willows.<sup>[6155](h6155)</sup>

8  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the cry<sup>[2201](h2201)</sup> is gone
round<sup>[5362](h5362)</sup> about <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
borders<sup>[1366](h1366)</sup> of Moab;<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> the
howling<sup>[3213](h3213)</sup> thereof unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
Eglaim,<sup>[97](h0097)</sup> and the howling<sup>[3213](h3213)</sup>
thereof unto Beer-elim.<sup>[879](h0879)</sup>

9  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> of
Dimon<sup>[1775](h1775)</sup> shall be full<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> of
blood:<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I will
bring<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> more<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> Dimon,<sup>[1775](h1775)</sup>
lions<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> upon him that
escapeth<sup>[6413](h6413)</sup> of Moab,<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> and
upon the remnant<sup>[7611](h7611)</sup> of the
land.<sup>[127](h0127)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/isa015)

1 τὸ ῥῆμα τὸ κατὰ τῆς Μωαβίτιδος νυκτὸς ἀπολεῖται ἡ Μωαβῖτις νυκτὸς γὰρ
ἀπολεῖται τὸ τεῖχος τῆς Μωαβίτιδος

2 λυπεῖσθε ἐφ᾽ ἑαυτοῖς ἀπολεῖται γὰρ καὶ Δηβων οὗ ὁ βωμὸς ὑμῶν ἐκεῖ
ἀναβήσεσθε κλαίειν ἐπὶ Ναβαυ τῆς Μωαβίτιδος ὀλολύζετε ἐπὶ πάσης κεφαλῆς
φαλάκρωμα πάντες βραχίονες κατατετμημένοι

3 ἐν ταῖς πλατείαις αὐτῆς περιζώσασθε σάκκους καὶ κόπτεσθε ἐπὶ τῶν
δωμάτων αὐτῆς καὶ ἐν ταῖς ῥύμαις αὐτῆς πάντες ὀλολύζετε μετὰ κλαυθμοῦ

4 ὅτι κέκραγεν Εσεβων καὶ Ελεαλη ἕως Ιασσα ἠκούσθη ἡ φωνὴ αὐτῶν διὰ
τοῦτο ἡ ὀσφὺς τῆς Μωαβίτιδος βοᾷ ἡ ψυχὴ αὐτῆς γνώσεται

5 ἡ καρδία τῆς Μωαβίτιδος βοᾷ ἐν αὐτῇ ἕως Σηγωρ δάμαλις γάρ ἐστιν
τριετής ἐπὶ δὲ τῆς ἀναβάσεως τῆς Λουιθ πρὸς σὲ κλαίοντες ἀναβήσονται τῇ
ὁδῷ Αρωνιιμ βοᾷ σύντριμμα καὶ σεισμός

6 τὸ ὕδωρ τῆς Νεμριμ ἔρημον ἔσται καὶ ὁ χόρτος αὐτῆς ἐκλείψει χόρτος γὰρ
χλωρὸς οὐκ ἔσται

7 μὴ καὶ οὕτως μέλλει σωθῆναι ἐπάξω γὰρ ἐπὶ τὴν φάραγγα Ἄραβας καὶ
λήμψονται αὐτήν

8 συνῆψεν γὰρ ἡ βοὴ τὸ ὅριον τῆς Μωαβίτιδος τῆς Αγαλλιμ καὶ ὀλολυγμὸς
αὐτῆς ἕως τοῦ φρέατος τοῦ Αιλιμ

9 τὸ δὲ ὕδωρ τὸ Ρεμμων πλησθήσεται αἵματος ἐπάξω γὰρ ἐπὶ Ρεμμων Ἄραβας
καὶ ἀρῶ τὸ σπέρμα Μωαβ καὶ Αριηλ καὶ τὸ κατάλοιπον Αδαμα

[Vul](../vul/isa015)

1 Onus Moab. \[Quia nocte vastata est  
Ar Moab, conticuit;  
quia nocte vastatus est  
murus Moab, conticuit.  

2 Ascendit domus,  
et Dibon ad excelsa,  
in planctum super Nabo;  
et super Medaba, Moab ululavit;  
in cunctis capitibus ejus calvitium,  
et omnis barba radetur.  

3 In triviis ejus accincti sunt sacco;  
super tecta ejus et in plateis ejus  
omnis ululatus descendit in fletum.  

4 Clamabit Hesebon et Eleale,  
usque Jasa audita est vox eorum;  
super hoc expediti Moab ululabunt,  
anima ejus ululabit sibi.  

5 Cor meum ad Moab clamabit;  
vectes ejus usque ad Segor,  
vitulam conternantem;  
per ascensum enim Luith  
flens ascendet,  
et in via Oronaim  
clamorem contritionis levabunt.  

6 Aquæ enim Nemrim desertæ erunt,  
quia aruit herba, defecit germen,  
viror omnis interiit.  

7 Secundum magnitudinem operis, et visitatio eorum:  
ad torrentem Salicum ducent eos.  

8 Quoniam circuivit clamor terminum Moab;  
usque ad Gallim ululatus ejus,  
et usque ad puteum Elim clamor ejus.  

9 Quia aquæ Dibon repletæ sunt sanguine;  
ponam enim super Dibon additamenta;  
his qui fugerint de Moab leonem,  
et reliquiis terræ.\]
