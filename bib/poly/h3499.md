H3499  
יתר  
יֶתֶר ‎ yether  
*yeh‘-ther*  
Properly an *overhanging*, that is, (by implication) an *excess*,
*superiority*, *remainder*; also a small *rope* (as hanging free): - +
abundant, cord, exceeding, excellency (-ent), what they leave, that hath
left, plentifully, remnant, residue, rest, string, with.
