*so-rake‘,* *so-rake‘,* *so-ray-kaw‘*  
(The third form is feminine); from [8319](h8319) in the sense of
*redness* (compare [8320](h8320) ; a *vine* stock (properly one yielding
*purple* grapes, the richest variety): - choice (-st, noble) wine.
