H3956  
לשׁנה לשׁן לשׁון  
לָשׁוֹן לָשׁוֹן לְשׁוֹנָה ‎ lâshôn lâshôn l<sup>e</sup>shônâh  
*law-shone‘,* *law-shone‘,* *lesh-o-naw‘*  
From [3960](h3960) the *tongue* (of man or animals.md), used literally (as
the instrument of licking, eating, or speech), and figuratively (speech,
an ingot, a fork of flame, a cove of water): - + babbler, bay, + evil
speaker, language, talker, tongue, wedge.
