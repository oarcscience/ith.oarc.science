H7919  
שׂכל  
שָׂכַל ‎ śâkal  
*saw-kal‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* (causeatively *make* or *act*) *circumspect*
and hence *intelligent: -* consider, expert, instruct, prosper, (deal)
prudent (-ly), (give) skill (-ful), have good success, teach, (have,
make to) understand (-ing), wisdom, (be, behave self, consider, make)
wise (-ly), guide wittingly.
