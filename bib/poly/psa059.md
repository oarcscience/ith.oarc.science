### Psalms 59

[KJV](../kjv/psa059.md)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup>
Al-taschith,<sup>[516](h0516)</sup> Michtam<sup>[4387](h4387.md)</sup> of
David;<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> when Saul<sup>[7586](h7586.md)</sup>
sent,<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> and they watched<sup>[8104](h8104.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> to
kill<sup>[4191](h4191)</sup> him. Deliver<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> me
from mine enemies,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[341](h0341.md)</sup> O my
God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> defend<sup>[7682](h7682.md)</sup> me from them
that rise up against<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6965](h6965.md)</sup>
me.

2  Deliver<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> me from the
workers<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> of
iniquity,<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> and save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> me
from bloody<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> men.<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup>

3  For,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> lo,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> they lie in
wait<sup>[693](h0693)</sup> for my soul:<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> the
mighty<sup>[5794](h5794)</sup> are gathered<sup>[1481](h1481.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me; not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> for my
transgression,<sup>[6588](h6588)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> for
my sin,<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup> O LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

4  They run<sup>[7323](h7323.md)</sup> and prepare
themselves<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> without<sup>[1097](h1097.md)</sup> my
fault:<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> awake<sup>[5782](h5782.md)</sup> to
help<sup>[7125](h7125)</sup> me, and behold.<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup>

5  Thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> therefore, O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of hosts,<sup>[6635](h6635.md)</sup> the
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>
awake<sup>[6974](h6974)</sup> to visit<sup>[6485](h6485.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the heathen:<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> be
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> merciful<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> to
any<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> wicked<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup>
transgressors.<sup>[898](h0898)</sup> Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542.md)</sup>

6  They return<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> at
evening:<sup>[6153](h6153.md)</sup> they make a
noise<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup> like a dog,<sup>[3611](h3611.md)</sup> and go
round about<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> the city.<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup>

7  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> they belch
out<sup>[5042](h5042)</sup> with their mouth:<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup>
swords<sup>[2719](h2719.md)</sup> are in their
lips:<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
who,<sup>[4310](h4310.md)</sup> say they, doth
hear?<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup>

8  But thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
shalt laugh<sup>[7832](h7832.md)</sup> at them; thou shalt have
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the heathen<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> in
derision.<sup>[3932](h3932.md)</sup>

9  Because of his strength<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup> will I
wait<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> upon<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thee:
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> is my
defense.<sup>[4869](h4869.md)</sup>

10  The God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of my mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup>
shall prevent<sup>[6923](h6923)</sup> me: God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>
shall let me see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> my desire upon mine
enemies.<sup>[8324](h8324.md)</sup>

11  Slay<sup>[2026](h2026)</sup> them not,<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup>
lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> my people<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
forget:<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> scatter<sup>[5128](h5128.md)</sup> them by
thy power;<sup>[2428](h2428.md)</sup> and bring them
down,<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> O Lord<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> our
shield.<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup>

12  For the sin<sup>[2403](h2403.md)</sup> of their
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> and the words<sup>[1697](h1697.md)</sup> of
their lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> let them even be
taken<sup>[3920](h3920)</sup> in their pride:<sup>[1347](h1347.md)</sup>
and for cursing<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[423](h0423.md)</sup> and
lying<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3585](h3585.md)</sup> which they
speak.<sup>[5608](h5608.md)</sup>

13  Consume<sup>[3615](h3615.md)</sup> them in
wrath,<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup> consume<sup>[3615](h3615.md)</sup> them,
that they may not<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup> be: and let them
know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> ruleth<sup>[4910](h4910.md)</sup> in
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> unto the ends<sup>[657](h0657.md)</sup> of
the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542.md)</sup>

14  And at evening<sup>[6153](h6153.md)</sup> let them
return;<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> and let them make a
noise<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup> like a dog,<sup>[3611](h3611.md)</sup> and go
round about<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> the city.<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup>

15  Let them<sup>[1992](h1992.md)</sup> wander up and
down<sup>[5128](h5128)</sup> for meat,<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> and
grudge<sup>[3885](h3885)</sup> if<sup>[518](h0518.md)</sup> they be
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> satisfied.<sup>[7646](h7646.md)</sup>

16  But I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> will sing<sup>[7891](h7891.md)</sup> of
thy power;<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup> yea, I will sing
aloud<sup>[7442](h7442)</sup> of thy mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> in
the morning:<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou
hast been<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> my defense<sup>[4869](h4869.md)</sup> and
refuge<sup>[4498](h4498)</sup> in the day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> of my
trouble.<sup>[6862](h6862.md)</sup>

17  Unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thee, O my
strength,<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> will I sing:<sup>[2167](h2167.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> is my
defense,<sup>[4869](h4869)</sup> and the God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> of
my mercy.<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa059.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος τοῖς ἀλλοιωθησομένοις ἔτι εἰς στηλογραφίαν τῷ Δαυιδ εἰς
διδαχήν

2 ὁπότε ἐνεπύρισεν τὴν Μεσοποταμίαν Συρίας καὶ τὴν Συρίαν Σωβα καὶ
ἐπέστρεψεν Ιωαβ καὶ ἐπάταξεν τὴν φάραγγα τῶν ἁλῶν δώδεκα χιλιάδας

3 ὁ θεός ἀπώσω ἡμᾶς καὶ καθεῖλες ἡμᾶς ὠργίσθης καὶ οἰκτίρησας ἡμᾶς

4 συνέσεισας τὴν γῆν καὶ συνετάραξας αὐτήν ἴασαι τὰ συντρίμματα αὐτῆς
ὅτι ἐσαλεύθη

5 ἔδειξας τῷ λαῷ σου σκληρά ἐπότισας ἡμᾶς οἶνον κατανύξεως

6 ἔδωκας τοῖς φοβουμένοις σε σημείωσιν τοῦ φυγεῖν ἀπὸ προσώπου τόξου
διάψαλμα

7 ὅπως ἂν ῥυσθῶσιν οἱ ἀγαπητοί σου σῶσον τῇ δεξιᾷ σου καὶ ἐπάκουσόν μου

8 ὁ θεὸς ἐλάλησεν ἐν τῷ ἁγίῳ αὐτοῦ ἀγαλλιάσομαι καὶ διαμεριῶ Σικιμα καὶ
τὴν κοιλάδα τῶν σκηνῶν διαμετρήσω

9 ἐμός ἐστιν Γαλααδ καὶ ἐμός ἐστιν Μανασση καὶ Εφραιμ κραταίωσις τῆς
κεφαλῆς μου Ιουδας βασιλεύς μου

10 Μωαβ λέβης τῆς ἐλπίδος μου ἐπὶ τὴν Ιδουμαίαν ἐκτενῶ τὸ ὑπόδημά μου
ἐμοὶ ἀλλόφυλοι ὑπετάγησαν

11 τίς ἀπάξει με εἰς πόλιν περιοχῆς τίς ὁδηγήσει με ἕως τῆς Ιδουμαίας

12 οὐχὶ σύ ὁ θεός ὁ ἀπωσάμενος ἡμᾶς καὶ οὐκ ἐξελεύσῃ ὁ θεός ἐν ταῖς
δυνάμεσιν ἡμῶν

13 δὸς ἡμῖν βοήθειαν ἐκ θλίψεως καὶ ματαία σωτηρία ἀνθρώπου

14 ἐν δὲ τῷ θεῷ ποιήσομεν δύναμιν καὶ αὐτὸς ἐξουδενώσει τοὺς θλίβοντας
ἡμᾶς

[Vul](../vul/psa059.md)

1 In finem, pro his qui immutabuntur, in tituli inscriptionem ipsi
David, in doctrinam,

2 cum succendit Mesopotamiam Syriæ et Sobal, et convertit Joab, et
percussit Idumæam in valle Salinarum duodecim millia.

3 \[Deus, repulisti nos, et destruxisti nos;  
iratus es, et misertus es nobis.  

4 Commovisti terram, et conturbasti eam;  
sana contritiones ejus, quia commota est.  

5 Ostendisti populo tuo dura;  
potasti nos vino compunctionis.  

6 Dedisti metuentibus te significationem,  
ut fugiant a facie arcus;  
ut liberentur dilecti tui.  

7 Salvum fac dextera tua, et exaudi me.  

8 Deus locutus est in sancto suo:  
lætabor, et partibor Sichimam;  
et convallem tabernaculorum metibor.  

9 Meus est Galaad, et meus est Manasses;  
et Ephraim fortitudo capitis mei.  
Juda rex meus;  

10 Moab olla spei meæ.  
In Idumæam extendam calceamentum meum:  
mihi alienigenæ subditi sunt.  

11 Quis deducet me in civitatem munitam?  
quis deducet me usque in Idumæam?  

12 nonne tu, Deus, qui repulisti nos?  
et non egredieris, Deus, in virtutibus nostris?  

13 Da nobis auxilium de tribulatione,  
quia vana salus hominis.  

14 In Deo faciemus virtutem;  
et ipse ad nihilum deducet tribulantes nos.\]
