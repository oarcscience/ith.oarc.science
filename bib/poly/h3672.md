H3672  
כּנּרת כּנּרות  
כִּנְּרוֹת כִּנֶּרֶת ‎ kinn<sup>e</sup>rôth kinnereth  
*kin-ner-oth‘,* *kin-neh‘-reth*  
Respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as [3658](h3658)
perhaps *harp* shaped; *Kinneroth* or *Kinnereth*, a place in Palestine:
- Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.
