H3588  
כּי  
כִּי ‎ kı̂y  
*kee*  
A primitive particle (the full form of the prepositional prefix)
indicating *causal* relations of all kinds, antecedent or consequent;
(by implication) very widely used as a relative conjugation or adverb;
often largely modified by other particles annexed: - and, + (forasmuch,
inasmuch, where-) as, assured \[-ly\], + but, certainly, doubtless, +
else, even, + except, for, how, (because, in, so, than) that, +
nevertheless, now, rightly, seeing, since, surely, then, therefore, +
(al-) though, + till, truly, + until, when, whether, while, who, yea,
yet,
