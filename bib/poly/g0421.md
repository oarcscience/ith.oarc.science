G421  
ἀνεξιχνίαστος  
anexichniastos  
*an-ex-ikh-nee‘-as-tos*  
From [1](g0001) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a
compound of [1537](g1537) and a derivative of [2487](g2487) *not*
*tracked* *out*, that is, (by implication) *untraceable:* - past finding
out, unsearchable.
