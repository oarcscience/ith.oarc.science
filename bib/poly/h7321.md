H7321  
רוּע  
רוַּע ‎ rûa‛  
*roo-ah‘*  
A primitive root; to *mar* (especially by breaking); figuratively to
*split* the ears (with sound), that is, *shout* (for alarm or joy): -
blow an alarm, cry (alarm, aloud, out), destroy, make a joyful noise,
smart, shout (for joy), sound an alarm, triumph.
