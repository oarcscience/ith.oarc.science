H657
אפס
אֶפֶס ‎ ‘ephes
eh‘-fes
From 656 cessation, that is, an end (especially of the earth); often used adverbially no further; also (like 6466 the ankle (in the dual), as being the extremity of the leg or foot: - ankle, but (only), end, howbeit, less than nothing, nevertheless (where), no, none (beside), not (any, -withstanding), thing of nought, save (-ing), there, uttermost part, want, without (cause).
