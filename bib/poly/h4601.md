H4601  
מעכת מעכה  
מַעֲכָה מַעֲכָת ‎ ma‛ăkâh ma‛ăkâth  
*mah-ak-aw‘,* *mah-ak-awth‘*  
From [4600](h4600) *depression*; *Maakah* (or *Maakath*), the name of a
place in Syria, also of a Mesopotamian, of three Israelites, and of four
Israelitesses and one Syrian woman: - Maachah, Maachathites. See also
[1038](h1038)
