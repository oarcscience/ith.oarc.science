H3689  
כּסל  
כֶּסֶל ‎ kesel  
*keh‘-sel*  
From [3688](h3688.md) properly *fatness*, that is, by implication
(literally) the *loin* (as the seat of the leaf *fat*) or (generally)
the *viscera*; also (figuratively) *silliness* or (in a good sense)
*trust: -* confidence, flank, folly, hope, loin.
