### Psalms 149

[KJV](../kjv/psa149.md)

1  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> ye the LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup>
Sing<sup>[7891](h7891)</sup> unto the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> a
new<sup>[2319](h2319)</sup> song,<sup>[7892](h7892.md)</sup> and his
praise<sup>[8416](h8416.md)</sup> in the
congregation<sup>[6951](h6951)</sup> of saints.<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup>

2  Let Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> in
him that made<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> him: let the
children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of Zion<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> be
joyful<sup>[1523](h1523)</sup> in their King.<sup>[4428](h4428.md)</sup>

3  Let them praise<sup>[1984](h1984.md)</sup> his
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> in the dance:<sup>[4234](h4234.md)</sup> let
them sing praises<sup>[2167](h2167.md)</sup> unto him with the
timbrel<sup>[8596](h8596)</sup> and harp.<sup>[3658](h3658.md)</sup>

4  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> taketh
pleasure<sup>[7521](h7521)</sup> in his people:<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
he will beautify<sup>[6286](h6286.md)</sup> the
meek<sup>[6035](h6035)</sup> with salvation.<sup>[3444](h3444.md)</sup>

5  Let the saints<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup> be
joyful<sup>[5937](h5937)</sup> in glory:<sup>[3519](h3519.md)</sup> let
them sing aloud<sup>[7442](h7442)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
their beds.<sup>[4904](h4904.md)</sup>

6  Let the high<sup>[7318](h7318.md)</sup> praises of
God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> be in their mouth,<sup>[1627](h1627.md)</sup>
and a twoedged<sup>[6374](h6374)</sup> sword<sup>[2719](h2719.md)</sup> in
their hand;<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

7  To execute<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> vengeance<sup>[5360](h5360.md)</sup>
upon the heathen,<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> and
punishments<sup>[8433](h8433.md)</sup> upon the
people;<sup>[3816](h3816.md)</sup>

8  To bind<sup>[631](h0631)</sup> their kings<sup>[4428](h4428.md)</sup>
with chains,<sup>[2131](h2131.md)</sup> and their
nobles<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> with fetters<sup>[3525](h3525.md)</sup> of
iron;<sup>[1270](h1270.md)</sup>

9  To execute<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> upon them the
judgment<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> written:<sup>[3789](h3789.md)</sup>
this<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> honor<sup>[1926](h1926.md)</sup> have
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> his saints.<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup>
Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> ye the LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa149.md)

1 αλληλουια ᾄσατε τῷ κυρίῳ ᾆσμα καινόν ἡ αἴνεσις αὐτοῦ ἐν ἐκκλησίᾳ ὁσίων

2 εὐφρανθήτω Ισραηλ ἐπὶ τῷ ποιήσαντι αὐτόν καὶ υἱοὶ Σιων ἀγαλλιάσθωσαν
ἐπὶ τῷ βασιλεῖ αὐτῶν

3 αἰνεσάτωσαν τὸ ὄνομα αὐτοῦ ἐν χορῷ ἐν τυμπάνῳ καὶ ψαλτηρίῳ ψαλάτωσαν
αὐτῷ

4 ὅτι εὐδοκεῖ κύριος ἐν λαῷ αὐτοῦ καὶ ὑψώσει πραεῖς ἐν σωτηρίᾳ

5 καυχήσονται ὅσιοι ἐν δόξῃ καὶ ἀγαλλιάσονται ἐπὶ τῶν κοιτῶν αὐτῶν

6 αἱ ὑψώσεις τοῦ θεοῦ ἐν τῷ λάρυγγι αὐτῶν καὶ ῥομφαῖαι δίστομοι ἐν ταῖς
χερσὶν αὐτῶν

7 τοῦ ποιῆσαι ἐκδίκησιν ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ἐλεγμοὺς ἐν τοῖς λαοῖς

8 τοῦ δῆσαι τοὺς βασιλεῖς αὐτῶν ἐν πέδαις καὶ τοὺς ἐνδόξους αὐτῶν ἐν
χειροπέδαις σιδηραῖς

9 τοῦ ποιῆσαι ἐν αὐτοῖς κρίμα ἔγγραπτον δόξα αὕτη ἐστὶν πᾶσι τοῖς ὁσίοις
αὐτοῦ

[Vul](../vul/psa149.md)

1 Alleluja. \[Cantate Domino canticum novum;  
laus ejus in ecclesia sanctorum.  

2 Lætetur Israël in eo qui fecit eum,  
et filii Sion exsultent in rege suo.  

3 Laudent nomen ejus in choro;  
in tympano et psalterio psallant ei.  

4 Quia beneplacitum est Domino in populo suo,  
et exaltabit mansuetos in salutem.  

5 Exsultabunt sancti in gloria;  
lætabuntur in cubilibus suis.  

6 Exaltationes Dei in gutture eorum,  
et gladii ancipites in manibus eorum:  

7 ad faciendam vindictam in nationibus,  
increpationes in populis;  

8 ad alligandos reges eorum in compedibus,  
et nobiles eorum in manicis ferreis;  

9 ut faciant in eis judicium conscriptum:  
gloria hæc est omnibus sanctis ejus.  
Alleluja.\]
