H2142
זכר
זָכַר ‎ zâkar
zaw-kar‘
A primitive root; properly to mark (so as to be recognized), that is, to remember; by implication to mention; also (as denominative from 2145 to be male: - X burn [incense], X earnestly, be male, (make) mention (of), be mindful, recount, record (-er), remember, make to be remembered, bring (call, come, keep, put) to (in) remembrance, X still, think on, X well.
