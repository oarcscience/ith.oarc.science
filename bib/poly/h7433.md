*raw-moth‘* *gil-awd‘*  
From the plural of [7413](h7413) and [1568](h1568) *heights* *of*
*Gilad*; *Ramoth* *Gilad*, a place East of the Jordan: - Ramoth-gilead,
Ramoth in Gilead. See also [7216](h7216)
