H622  
אסף  
אָסַף ‎ ‘âsaph  
*aw-saf‘*  
A primitive root; to *gather* for any purpose; hence to *receive*,
*take* *away*, that is, remove (destroy, leave behind, put up, restore,
etc.): - assemble, bring, consume, destroy, fetch, gather (in, together,
up again), X generally, get (him), lose, put all together, receive,
recover \[another from leprosy\], (be) rereward, X surely, take (away,
into, up), X utterly, withdraw.
