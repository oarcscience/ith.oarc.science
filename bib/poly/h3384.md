H3384  
ירא ירה  
יָרָה יָרָא ‎ yârâh yârâ‘  
*yaw-raw‘,* *yaw-raw‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *flow* as water (that is, to *rain*);
transitively to *lay* or *throw* (especially an arrow, that is, to
*shoot*); figuratively to *point* out (as if by *aiming* the finger), to
*teach: -* (+) archer, cast, direct, inform, instruct, lay, shew, shoot,
teach (-er, -ing), through.
