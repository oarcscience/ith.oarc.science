### Micah 3

[KJV](../kjv/mic003.md)

1  And I said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Hear,<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> I
pray you,<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> O heads<sup>[7218](h7218.md)</sup> of
Jacob,<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> and ye princes<sup>[7101](h7101.md)</sup> of
the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Israel;<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> Is
it not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> for you to know<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> judgment?<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup>

2  Who hate<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup> the good,<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup>
and love<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> the evil;<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup> who
pluck off<sup>[1497](h1497)</sup> their skin<sup>[5785](h5785.md)</sup>
from off<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> them, and
their flesh<sup>[7607](h7607)</sup> from off<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> their bones;<sup>[6106](h6106.md)</sup>

3  Who<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> also eat<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> the
flesh<sup>[7607](h7607)</sup> of my people,<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> and
flay<sup>[6584](h6584)</sup> their skin<sup>[5785](h5785.md)</sup> from
off<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> them; and they
break<sup>[6476](h6476)</sup> their bones,<sup>[6106](h6106.md)</sup> and
chop them in pieces,<sup>[6566](h6566)</sup> as<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup>
for the pot,<sup>[5518](h5518.md)</sup> and as
flesh<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup> within<sup>[8432](h8432.md)</sup> the
caldron.<sup>[7037](h7037.md)</sup>

4  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> shall they cry<sup>[2199](h2199.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> but he
will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> hear<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup> them: he
will even hide<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> his face<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> them at that<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup>
time,<sup>[6256](h6256)</sup> as<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> they have
behaved themselves ill<sup>[7489](h7489.md)</sup> in their
doings.<sup>[4611](h4611.md)</sup>

5  Thus<sup>[3541](h3541)</sup> saith<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> concerning<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
prophets<sup>[5030](h5030)</sup> that make <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> my
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> err,<sup>[8582](h8582.md)</sup> that
bite<sup>[5391](h5391)</sup> with their teeth,<sup>[8127](h8127.md)</sup>
and cry,<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> Peace;<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> and he
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> putteth<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> into<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> their
mouths,<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> they even
prepare<sup>[6942](h6942)</sup> war<sup>[4421](h4421.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him.

6  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> night<sup>[3915](h3915.md)</sup> shall
be unto you, that ye shall not have a vision;<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[2377](h2377)</sup> and it shall be dark<sup>[2821](h2821.md)</sup>
unto you, that ye shall not divine;<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[7080](h7080)</sup> and the sun<sup>[8121](h8121.md)</sup> shall go
down<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> over<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
prophets,<sup>[5030](h5030)</sup> and the day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>
shall be dark<sup>[6937](h6937)</sup> over<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> them.

7  Then shall the seers<sup>[2374](h2374.md)</sup> be
ashamed,<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> and the diviners<sup>[7080](h7080.md)</sup>
confounded:<sup>[2659](h2659.md)</sup> yea, they shall
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> cover<sup>[5844](h5844.md)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> their lips;<sup>[8222](h8222.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> there is no<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>
answer<sup>[4617](h4617)</sup> of God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

8  But truly<sup>[199](h0199)</sup> I<sup>[595](h0595.md)</sup> am
full<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> of power<sup>[3581](h3581.md)</sup>
by<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the spirit<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup> of the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and of judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup>
and of might,<sup>[1369](h1369)</sup> to declare<sup>[5046](h5046.md)</sup>
unto Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290.md)</sup> his
transgression,<sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup> and to
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> his sin.<sup>[2403](h2403.md)</sup>

9  Hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> this,<sup>[2063](h2063.md)</sup> I pray
you,<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> ye heads<sup>[7218](h7218.md)</sup> of the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Jacob,<sup>[3290](h3290.md)</sup> and
princes<sup>[7101](h7101)</sup> of the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> that abhor<sup>[8581](h8581.md)</sup>
judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> and pervert<sup>[6140](h6140.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> equity.<sup>[3477](h3477.md)</sup>

10  They build up<sup>[1129](h1129)</sup> Zion<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>
with blood,<sup>[1818](h1818.md)</sup> and
Jerusalem<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> with iniquity.<sup>[5766](h5766.md)</sup>

11  The heads<sup>[7218](h7218.md)</sup> thereof
judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> for reward,<sup>[7810](h7810.md)</sup> and
the priests<sup>[3548](h3548.md)</sup> thereof
teach<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> for hire,<sup>[4242](h4242.md)</sup> and the
prophets<sup>[5030](h5030)</sup> thereof divine<sup>[7080](h7080.md)</sup>
for money:<sup>[3701](h3701.md)</sup> yet will they
lean<sup>[8172](h8172)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and say,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> Is
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
among<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> us? none<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> can come<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> us.

12  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> shall Zion<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>
for your sake<sup>[1558](h1558)</sup> be plowed<sup>[2790](h2790.md)</sup>
as a field,<sup>[7704](h7704.md)</sup> and
Jerusalem<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> shall become<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
heaps,<sup>[5856](h5856)</sup> and the mountain<sup>[2022](h2022.md)</sup>
of the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> as the high
places<sup>[1116](h1116)</sup> of the forest.<sup>[3293](h3293.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/mic003.md)

1 καὶ ἐρεῖ ἀκούσατε δὴ ταῦτα αἱ ἀρχαὶ οἴκου Ιακωβ καὶ οἱ κατάλοιποι
οἴκου Ισραηλ οὐχ ὑμῖν ἐστιν τοῦ γνῶναι τὸ κρίμα

2 οἱ μισοῦντες τὰ καλὰ καὶ ζητοῦντες τὰ πονηρά ἁρπάζοντες τὰ δέρματα
αὐτῶν ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν καὶ τὰς σάρκας αὐτῶν ἀπὸ τῶν ὀστέων αὐτῶν

3 ὃν τρόπον κατέφαγον τὰς σάρκας τοῦ λαοῦ μου καὶ τὰ δέρματα αὐτῶν ἀπὸ
τῶν ὀστέων αὐτῶν ἐξέδειραν καὶ τὰ ὀστέα αὐτῶν συνέθλασαν καὶ ἐμέλισαν ὡς
σάρκας εἰς λέβητα καὶ ὡς κρέα εἰς χύτραν

4 οὕτως κεκράξονται πρὸς κύριον καὶ οὐκ εἰσακούσεται αὐτῶν καὶ
ἀποστρέψει τὸ πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν ἐν τῷ καιρῷ ἐκείνῳ ἀνθ᾽ ὧν
ἐπονηρεύσαντο ἐν τοῖς ἐπιτηδεύμασιν αὐτῶν ἐπ᾽ αὐτούς

5 τάδε λέγει κύριος ἐπὶ τοὺς προφήτας τοὺς πλανῶντας τὸν λαόν μου τοὺς
δάκνοντας ἐν τοῖς ὀδοῦσιν αὐτῶν καὶ κηρύσσοντας ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν εἰρήνην καὶ
οὐκ ἐδόθη εἰς τὸ στόμα αὐτῶν ἤγειραν ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν πόλεμον

6 διὰ τοῦτο νὺξ ὑμῖν ἔσται ἐξ ὁράσεως καὶ σκοτία ὑμῖν ἔσται ἐκ μαντείας
καὶ δύσεται ὁ ἥλιος ἐπὶ τοὺς προφήτας καὶ συσκοτάσει ἐπ᾽ αὐτοὺς ἡ ἡμέρα

7 καὶ καταισχυνθήσονται οἱ ὁρῶντες τὰ ἐνύπνια καὶ καταγελασθήσονται οἱ
μάντεις καὶ καταλαλήσουσιν κατ᾽ αὐτῶν πάντες αὐτοί διότι οὐκ ἔσται ὁ
εἰσακούων αὐτῶν

8 ἐὰν μὴ ἐγὼ ἐμπλήσω ἰσχὺν ἐν πνεύματι κυρίου καὶ κρίματος καὶ
δυναστείας τοῦ ἀπαγγεῖλαι τῷ Ιακωβ ἀσεβείας αὐτοῦ καὶ τῷ Ισραηλ ἁμαρτίας
αὐτοῦ

9 ἀκούσατε δὴ ταῦτα οἱ ἡγούμενοι οἴκου Ιακωβ καὶ οἱ κατάλοιποι οἴκου
Ισραηλ οἱ βδελυσσόμενοι κρίμα καὶ πάντα τὰ ὀρθὰ διαστρέφοντες

10 οἱ οἰκοδομοῦντες Σιων ἐν αἵμασιν καὶ Ιερουσαλημ ἐν ἀδικίαις

11 οἱ ἡγούμενοι αὐτῆς μετὰ δώρων ἔκρινον καὶ οἱ ἱερεῖς αὐτῆς μετὰ μισθοῦ
ἀπεκρίνοντο καὶ οἱ προφῆται αὐτῆς μετὰ ἀργυρίου ἐμαντεύοντο καὶ ἐπὶ τὸν
κύριον ἐπανεπαύοντο λέγοντες οὐχὶ κύριος ἐν ἡμῖν ἐστιν οὐ μὴ ἐπέλθῃ ἐφ᾽
ἡμᾶς κακά

12 διὰ τοῦτο δι᾽ ὑμᾶς Σιων ὡς ἀγρὸς ἀροτριαθήσεται καὶ Ιερουσαλημ ὡς
ὀπωροφυλάκιον ἔσται καὶ τὸ ὄρος τοῦ οἴκου ὡς ἄλσος δρυμοῦ

1 \[Et dixi: Audite, princeps Jacob,  
et duces domus Israël:  
numquid non vestrum est scire judicium,  

2 qui odio habetis bonum, et diligitis malum;  
qui violenter tollitis pelles eorum desuper eis,  
et carnem eorum desuper ossibus eorum;  

3 qui comederunt carnem populi mei,  
et pellem eorum desuper excoriaverunt,  
et ossa eorum confregerunt,  
et conciderunt sicut in lebete,  
et quasi carnem in medio ollæ?  

4 Tunc clamabunt ad Dominum, et non exaudiet eos,  
et abscondet faciem suam ab eis in tempore illo,  
sicut nequiter egerunt in adinventionibus suis.  

5 Hæc dicit Dominus super prophetas,  
qui seducunt populum meum:  
qui mordent dentibus suis,  
et prædicant pacem;  
et si quis non dederit in ore eorum quippiam,  
sanctificant super eum prælium.  

6 Propterea nox vobis pro visione erit,  
et tenebræ vobis pro divinatione;  
et occumbet sol super prophetas,  
et obtenebrabitur super eos dies.  

7 Et confundentur qui vident visiones,  
et confundentur divini;  
et operient omnes vultos suos,  
quia non est responsum Dei.  

8 Verumtamen ego repletus sum fortitudine spiritus Domini,  
judicio, et virtute,  
ut annuntiem Jacob scelus suum,  
et Israël peccatum suum.  

9 Audite hoc, principes domus Jacob,  
et judices domus Israël,  
qui abominamini judicium,  
et omnia recta pervertitis:  

10 qui ædificatis Sion in sanguinibus,  
et Jerusalem in iniquitate.  

11 Principes ejus in muneribus judicabant,  
et sacerdotes ejus in mercede docebant,  
et prophetæ ejus in pecunia divinabant:  
et super Dominum requiescebant, dicentes:  
Numquid non Dominus in medio nostrum?  
non venient super nos mala.  

12 Propter hoc, causa vestri, Sion quasi ager arabitur,  
et Jerusalem quasi acervus lapidum erit,  
et mons templi in excelsa silvarum.\]
