### Hosea 9

[KJV](../kjv/hos009.md)

1  Rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> not,<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> O
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> for<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>
joy,<sup>[1524](h1524)</sup> as other people:<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou hast gone a
whoring<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> thou hast
loved<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> a reward<sup>[868](h0868.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> every<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
corn<sup>[1715](h1715)</sup> floor.<sup>[1637](h1637.md)</sup>

2  The floor<sup>[1637](h1637.md)</sup> and the
winepress<sup>[3342](h3342)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
feed<sup>[7462](h7462.md)</sup> them, and the new
wine<sup>[8492](h8492)</sup> shall fail<sup>[3584](h3584.md)</sup> in her.

3  They shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> dwell<sup>[3427](h3427.md)</sup>
in the LORD's<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> land;<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> but
Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> shall return<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> to
Egypt,<sup>[4714](h4714)</sup> and they shall eat<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup>
unclean<sup>[2931](h2931.md)</sup> things in
Assyria.<sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup>

4  They shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> offer<sup>[5258](h5258.md)</sup>
wine<sup>[3196](h3196.md)</sup> offerings to the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> neither<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> shall they
be pleasing<sup>[6149](h6149.md)</sup> unto him: their
sacrifices<sup>[2077](h2077.md)</sup> shall be unto them as the
bread<sup>[3899](h3899)</sup> of mourners;<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that eat<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> thereof
shall be polluted:<sup>[2930](h2930)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
their bread<sup>[3899](h3899.md)</sup> for their
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> come
into<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

5  What<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> will ye do<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> in
the solemn<sup>[4150](h4150)</sup> day,<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> and in
the day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of the feast<sup>[2282](h2282.md)</sup> of
the LORD?<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

6  For,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> lo,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup> they are
gone<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> because of
destruction:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7701](h7701.md)</sup>
Egypt<sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup> shall gather them
up,<sup>[6908](h6908)</sup> Memphis<sup>[4644](h4644.md)</sup> shall
bury<sup>[6912](h6912)</sup> them: the pleasant<sup>[4261](h4261.md)</sup>
places for their silver,<sup>[3701](h3701.md)</sup>
nettles<sup>[7057](h7057)</sup> shall possess<sup>[3423](h3423.md)</sup>
them: thorns<sup>[2336](h2336.md)</sup> shall be in their
tabernacles.<sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup>

7  The days<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> of
visitation<sup>[6486](h6486)</sup> are come,<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> the
days<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of recompense<sup>[7966](h7966.md)</sup> are
come;<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> Israel<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> shall
know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> it: the prophet<sup>[5030](h5030.md)</sup> is
a fool,<sup>[191](h0191)</sup> the spiritual<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup>
man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> is mad,<sup>[7696](h7696.md)</sup>
for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the multitude<sup>[7230](h7230.md)</sup> of
thine iniquity,<sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup> and the
great<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> hatred.<sup>[4895](h4895.md)</sup>

8  The watchman<sup>[6822](h6822.md)</sup> of
Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> was with<sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup> my
God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> but the prophet<sup>[5030](h5030.md)</sup> is a
snare<sup>[6341](h6341)</sup> of a fowler<sup>[3352](h3352.md)</sup>
in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> his
ways,<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> and hatred<sup>[4895](h4895.md)</sup> in the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of his God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

9  They have deeply<sup>[6009](h6009.md)</sup>
corrupted<sup>[7843](h7843.md)</sup> themselves, as in the
days<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of Gibeah:<sup>[1390](h1390.md)</sup>
therefore he will remember<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup> their
iniquity,<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> he will visit<sup>[6485](h6485.md)</sup>
their sins.<sup>[2403](h2403.md)</sup>

10  I found<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> Israel<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> like
grapes<sup>[6025](h6025.md)</sup> in the
wilderness;<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> I saw<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> your
fathers<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> as the firstripe<sup>[1063](h1063.md)</sup> in
the fig tree<sup>[8384](h8384.md)</sup> at her first
time:<sup>[7225](h7225)</sup> but they<sup>[1992](h1992.md)</sup>
went<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> to Baal-peor,<sup>[1187](h1187.md)</sup> and
separated themselves<sup>[5144](h5144.md)</sup> unto that
shame;<sup>[1322](h1322.md)</sup> and their
abominations<sup>[8251](h8251)</sup> were<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
according as they loved.<sup>[157](h0157.md)</sup>

11  As for Ephraim,<sup>[669](h0669.md)</sup> their
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> shall fly away<sup>[5774](h5774.md)</sup>
like a bird,<sup>[5775](h5775.md)</sup> from the
birth,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3205](h3205.md)</sup> and from the
womb,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[990](h0990.md)</sup> and from the
conception.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2032](h2032.md)</sup>

12  Though<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> <sup>[518](h0518.md)</sup> they bring
up<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> their
children,<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> yet will I
bereave<sup>[7921](h7921.md)</sup> them, that there shall not be a
man<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup> left:
yea,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> woe<sup>[188](h0188.md)</sup>
also<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup> to them when I
depart<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> them!

13  Ephraim,<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> as<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> I
saw<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> Tyrus,<sup>[6865](h6865.md)</sup> is
planted<sup>[8362](h8362.md)</sup> in a pleasant
place:<sup>[5116](h5116)</sup> but Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669.md)</sup> shall
bring forth<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> his children<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the murderer.<sup>[2026](h2026.md)</sup>

14  Give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> them, O LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
what<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> wilt thou give?<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> them a miscarrying<sup>[7921](h7921.md)</sup>
womb<sup>[7358](h7358)</sup> and dry<sup>[6784](h6784.md)</sup>
breasts.<sup>[7699](h7699.md)</sup>

15  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> their wickedness<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup>
is in Gilgal:<sup>[1537](h1537)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
there<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> I hated<sup>[8130](h8130.md)</sup> them:
for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the wickedness<sup>[7455](h7455.md)</sup> of
their doings<sup>[4611](h4611.md)</sup> I will drive them
out<sup>[1644](h1644)</sup> of mine house,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> I will love<sup>[157](h0157.md)</sup> them
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> more:<sup>[3254](h3254.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> their princes<sup>[8269](h8269.md)</sup> are
revolters.<sup>[5637](h5637.md)</sup>

16  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> is smitten,<sup>[5221](h5221.md)</sup>
their root<sup>[8328](h8328)</sup> is dried up,<sup>[3001](h3001.md)</sup>
they shall bear<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> no<sup>[1077](h1077.md)</sup>
fruit:<sup>[6529](h6529)</sup> yea,<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup>
though<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they bring forth,<sup>[3205](h3205.md)</sup>
yet will I slay<sup>[4191](h4191.md)</sup> even the
beloved<sup>[4261](h4261.md)</sup> fruit of their
womb.<sup>[990](h0990.md)</sup>

17  My God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> will cast them
away,<sup>[3988](h3988)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> they did
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> hearken<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> unto him:
and they shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
wanderers<sup>[5074](h5074.md)</sup> among the
nations.<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/hos009.md)

1 μὴ χαῖρε Ισραηλ μηδὲ εὐφραίνου καθὼς οἱ λαοί διότι ἐπόρνευσας ἀπὸ τοῦ
θεοῦ σου ἠγάπησας δόματα ἐπὶ πάντα ἅλωνα σίτου

2 ἅλων καὶ ληνὸς οὐκ ἔγνω αὐτούς καὶ ὁ οἶνος ἐψεύσατο αὐτούς

3 οὐ κατῴκησαν ἐν τῇ γῇ τοῦ κυρίου κατῴκησεν Εφραιμ εἰς Αἴγυπτον καὶ ἐν
Ἀσσυρίοις ἀκάθαρτα φάγονται

4 οὐκ ἔσπεισαν τῷ κυρίῳ οἶνον καὶ οὐχ ἥδυναν αὐτῷ αἱ θυσίαι αὐτῶν ὡς
ἄρτος πένθους αὐτοῖς πάντες οἱ ἔσθοντες αὐτὰ μιανθήσονται διότι οἱ ἄρτοι
αὐτῶν ταῖς ψυχαῖς αὐτῶν οὐκ εἰσελεύσονται εἰς τὸν οἶκον κυρίου

5 τί ποιήσετε ἐν ἡμέρᾳ πανηγύρεως καὶ ἐν ἡμέρᾳ ἑορτῆς τοῦ κυρίου

6 διὰ τοῦτο ἰδοὺ πορεύσονται ἐκ ταλαιπωρίας Αἰγύπτου καὶ ἐκδέξεται
αὐτοὺς Μέμφις καὶ θάψει αὐτοὺς Μαχμας τὸ ἀργύριον αὐτῶν ὄλεθρος
κληρονομήσει ἄκανθαι ἐν τοῖς σκηνώμασιν αὐτῶν

7 ἥκασιν αἱ ἡμέραι τῆς ἐκδικήσεως ἥκασιν αἱ ἡμέραι τῆς ἀνταποδόσεώς σου
καὶ κακωθήσεται Ισραηλ ὥσπερ ὁ προφήτης ὁ παρεξεστηκώς ἄνθρωπος ὁ
πνευματοφόρος ὑπὸ τοῦ πλήθους τῶν ἀδικιῶν σου ἐπληθύνθη μανία σου

8 σκοπὸς Εφραιμ μετὰ θεοῦ προφήτης παγὶς σκολιὰ ἐπὶ πάσας τὰς ὁδοὺς
αὐτοῦ μανίαν ἐν οἴκῳ κυρίου κατέπηξαν

9 ἐφθάρησαν κατὰ τὰς ἡμέρας τοῦ βουνοῦ μνησθήσεται ἀδικίας αὐτοῦ
ἐκδικήσει ἁμαρτίας αὐτοῦ

10 ὡς σταφυλὴν ἐν ἐρήμῳ εὗρον τὸν Ισραηλ καὶ ὡς σκοπὸν ἐν συκῇ πρόιμον
εἶδον πατέρας αὐτῶν αὐτοὶ εἰσῆλθον πρὸς τὸν Βεελφεγωρ καὶ
ἀπηλλοτριώθησαν εἰς αἰσχύνην καὶ ἐγένοντο οἱ ἠγαπημένοι ὡς οἱ
ἐβδελυγμένοι

11 Εφραιμ ὡς ὄρνεον ἐξεπετάσθη αἱ δόξαι αὐτῶν ἐκ τόκων καὶ ὠδίνων καὶ
συλλήμψεων

12 διότι καὶ ἐὰν ἐκθρέψωσιν τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν ἀτεκνωθήσονται ἐξ ἀνθρώπων
διότι καὶ οὐαὶ αὐτοῖς ἐστιν σάρξ μου ἐξ αὐτῶν

13 Εφραιμ ὃν τρόπον εἶδον εἰς θήραν παρέστησαν τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν καὶ Εφραιμ
τοῦ ἐξαγαγεῖν εἰς ἀποκέντησιν τὰ τέκνα αὐτοῦ

14 δὸς αὐτοῖς κύριε τί δώσεις αὐτοῖς δὸς αὐτοῖς μήτραν ἀτεκνοῦσαν καὶ
μαστοὺς ξηρούς

15 πᾶσαι αἱ κακίαι αὐτῶν εἰς Γαλγαλ ὅτι ἐκεῖ αὐτοὺς ἐμίσησα διὰ τὰς
κακίας τῶν ἐπιτηδευμάτων αὐτῶν ἐκ τοῦ οἴκου μου ἐκβαλῶ αὐτούς οὐ μὴ
προσθήσω τοῦ ἀγαπῆσαι αὐτούς πάντες οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῶν ἀπειθοῦντες

16 ἐπόνεσεν Εφραιμ τὰς ῥίζας αὐτοῦ ἐξηράνθη καρπὸν οὐκέτι μὴ ἐνέγκῃ
διότι καὶ ἐὰν γεννήσωσιν ἀποκτενῶ τὰ ἐπιθυμήματα κοιλίας αὐτῶν

17 ἀπώσεται αὐτοὺς ὁ θεός ὅτι οὐκ εἰσήκουσαν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἔσονται πλανῆται
ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν

[Vul](../vul/hos009.md)

1 \[Noli lætari, Israël;  
noli exsultare sicut populi:  
quia fornicatus es a Deo tuo;  
dilexisti mercedem super omnes areas tritici.  

2 Area et torcular non pascet eos,  
et vinum mentietur eis:  

3 non habitabunt in terra Domini.  
Reversus est Ephraim in Ægyptum,  
et in Assyriis pollutum comedit.  

4 Non libabunt Domino vinum,  
et non placebunt ei.  
Sacrificia eorum quasi panis lugentium;  
omnes qui comedent eum, contaminabuntur:  
quia panis eorum animæ ipsorum:  
non intrabit in domum Domini.  

5 Quid facietis in die solemni,  
in die festivitatis Domini?  

6 Ecce enim profecti sunt a vastitate:  
Ægyptus congregabit eos;  
Memphis sepeliet eos:  
desiderabile argentum eorum urtica hæreditabit,  
lappa in tabernaculis eorum.  

7 Venerunt dies visitationis,  
venerunt dies retributionis.  
Scitote, Israël,  
stultum prophetam,  
insanum virum spiritualem,  
propter multitudinem iniquitatis tuæ,  
et multitudinem amentiæ.  

8 Speculator Ephraim cum Deo meo,  
propheta laqueus ruinæ factus est super omnes vias ejus;  
insania in domo Dei ejus.  

9 Profunde peccaverunt, sicut in diebus Gabaa.  
Recordabitur iniquitatis eorum,  
et visitabit peccata eorum.  

10 Quasi uvas in deserto inveni Israël,  
quasi prima poma ficulneæ in cacumine ejus vidi patres eorum:  
ipsi autem intraverunt ad Beelphegor,  
et abalienati sunt in confusionem,  
et facti sunt abominabiles sicut ea quæ dilexerunt.  

11 Ephraim quasi avis avolavit;  
gloria eorum a partu, et ab utero, et a conceptu.  

12 Quod etsi enutrierint filios suos,  
absque liberis eos faciam in hominibus;  
sed et væ eis cum recessero ab eis!  

13 Ephraim, ut vidi, Tyrus erat fundata in pulchritudine;  
et Ephraim educet ad interfectorem filios suos.  

14 Da eis, Domine. Quid dabis eis?  
da eis vulvam sine liberis, et ubera arentia.  

15 Omnes nequitiæ eorum in Galgal,  
quia ibi exosos habui eos.  
Propter malitiam adinventionum eorum,  
de domo mea ejiciam eos;  
non addam ut diligam eos:  
omnes principes eorum recedentes.  

16 Percussus est Ephraim;  
radix eorum exsiccata est:  
fructum nequaquam facient,  
quod etsi genuerint,  
interficiam amantissima uteri eorum.  

17 Abjiciet eos Deus meus, quia non audierunt eum,  
et erunt vagi in nationibus.\]
