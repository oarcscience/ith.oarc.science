### Psalms 53

[KJV](../kjv/psa053.md)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> Mahalath,<sup>[4257](h4257.md)</sup>
Maschil,<sup>[4905](h4905.md)</sup> A Psalm of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> The fool<sup>[5036](h5036.md)</sup> hath
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> in his heart,<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> There
is no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>
Corrupt<sup>[7843](h7843.md)</sup> are they, and have done
abominable<sup>[8581](h8581)</sup> iniquity:<sup>[5766](h5766.md)</sup>
there is none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> that doeth<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
good.<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup>

2  God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> looked down<sup>[8259](h8259.md)</sup> from
heaven<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the children<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> of
men,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> to see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> if there
were<sup>[3426](h3426.md)</sup> any that did
understand,<sup>[7919](h7919.md)</sup> that did
seek<sup>[1875](h1875)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

3  Every one<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> of them is gone
back:<sup>[5472](h5472.md)</sup> they are
altogether<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> become filthy;<sup>[444](h0444.md)</sup>
there is none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> that doeth<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
good,<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> no,<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>
not<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> one.<sup>[259](h0259.md)</sup>

4  Have the workers<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> of
iniquity<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
knowledge?<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> who eat up<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> my
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> as they eat<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup>
bread:<sup>[3899](h3899)</sup> they have not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
called upon<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

5  There<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> were they in great
fear,<sup>[6342](h6342)</sup> <sup>[6343](h6343.md)</sup> where
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> fear<sup>[6343](h6343.md)</sup>
was:<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> hath scattered<sup>[6340](h6340.md)</sup> the
bones<sup>[6106](h6106.md)</sup> of him that
encampeth<sup>[2583](h2583.md)</sup> against thee: thou hast put them to
shame,<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> hath despised<sup>[3988](h3988.md)</sup> them.

6  Oh that<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> <sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup> the
salvation<sup>[3444](h3444)</sup> of Israel<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> were
come out of Zion!<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> When
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> bringeth back<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> the
captivity<sup>[7622](h7622)</sup> of his people,<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> shall rejoice,<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup> and
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> shall be glad.<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa053.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ἐν ὕμνοις συνέσεως τῷ Δαυιδ

2 ἐν τῷ ἐλθεῖν τοὺς Ζιφαίους καὶ εἰπεῖν τῷ σαουλ οὐκ ἰδοὺ Δαυιδ
κέκρυπται παρ᾽ ἡμῖν

3 ὁ θεός ἐν τῷ ὀνόματί σου σῶσόν με καὶ ἐν τῇ δυνάμει σου κρῖνόν με

4 ὁ θεός εἰσάκουσον τῆς προσευχῆς μου ἐνώτισαι τὰ ῥήματα τοῦ στόματός
μου

5 ὅτι ἀλλότριοι ἐπανέστησαν ἐπ᾽ ἐμέ καὶ κραταιοὶ ἐζήτησαν τὴν ψυχήν μου
οὐ προέθεντο τὸν θεὸν ἐνώπιον αὐτῶν διάψαλμα

6 ἰδοὺ γὰρ ὁ θεὸς βοηθεῖ μοι καὶ ὁ κύριος ἀντιλήμπτωρ τῆς ψυχῆς μου

7 ἀποστρέψει τὰ κακὰ τοῖς ἐχθροῖς μου ἐν τῇ ἀληθείᾳ σου ἐξολέθρευσον
αὐτούς

8 ἑκουσίως θύσω σοι ἐξομολογήσομαι τῷ ὀνόματί σου κύριε ὅτι ἀγαθόν

9 ὅτι ἐκ πάσης θλίψεως ἐρρύσω με καὶ ἐν τοῖς ἐχθροῖς μου ἐπεῖδεν ὁ
ὀφθαλμός μου

[Vul](../vul/psa053.md)

1 In finem, in carminibus. Intellectus David,

2 cum venissent Ziphæi, et dixissent ad Saul: Nonne David absconditus
est apud nos?

3 \[Deus, in nomine tuo salvum me fac,  
et in virtute tua judica me.  

4 Deus, exaudi orationem meam;  
auribus percipe verba oris mei.  

5 Quoniam alieni insurrexerunt adversum me,  
et fortes quæsierunt animam meam,  
et non proposuerunt Deum ante conspectum suum.  

6 Ecce enim Deus adjuvat me,  
et Dominus susceptor est animæ meæ.  

7 Averte mala inimicis meis;  
et in veritate tua disperde illos.  

8 Voluntarie sacrificabo tibi,  
et confitebor nomini tuo, Domine, quoniam bonum est.  

9 Quoniam ex omni tribulatione eripuisti me,  
et super inimicos meos despexit oculus meus.\]
