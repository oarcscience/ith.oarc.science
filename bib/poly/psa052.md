### Psalms 52

[KJV](../kjv/psa052.md)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup>
Maschil,<sup>[4905](h4905.md)</sup> A Psalm of
David,<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> when Doeg<sup>[1673](h1673.md)</sup> the
Edomite<sup>[130](h0130)</sup> came<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> and
told<sup>[5046](h5046)</sup> Saul,<sup>[7586](h7586.md)</sup> and
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> unto him, David<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup> is
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> to<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Ahimelech.<sup>[288](h0288.md)</sup>
Why<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> boastest thou
thyself<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> in mischief,<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup> O
mighty man?<sup>[1368](h1368)</sup> the goodness<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup>
of God<sup>[410](h0410.md)</sup> endureth
continually.<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> <sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>

2  Thy tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> deviseth<sup>[2803](h2803.md)</sup>
mischiefs;<sup>[1942](h1942)</sup> like a sharp<sup>[3913](h3913.md)</sup>
razor,<sup>[8593](h8593)</sup> working<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
deceitfully.<sup>[7423](h7423.md)</sup>

3  Thou lovest<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> evil<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup> more
than good;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup> and
lying<sup>[8267](h8267.md)</sup> rather than to
speak<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
righteousness.<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542.md)</sup>

4  Thou lovest<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
devouring<sup>[1105](h1105)</sup> words,<sup>[1697](h1697.md)</sup> O thou
deceitful<sup>[4820](h4820)</sup> tongue.<sup>[3956](h3956.md)</sup>

5  God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> shall likewise<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup>
destroy<sup>[5422](h5422)</sup> thee forever,<sup>[5331](h5331.md)</sup> he
shall take thee away,<sup>[2846](h2846.md)</sup> and pluck thee
out<sup>[5255](h5255.md)</sup> of thy dwelling
place,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup> and
root<sup>[8327](h8327.md)</sup> thee out of the
land<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> of the
living.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542.md)</sup>

6  The righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> also shall
see,<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> and fear,<sup>[3372](h3372.md)</sup> and shall
laugh<sup>[7832](h7832)</sup> at<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him:

7  Lo,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> this is the man<sup>[1397](h1397.md)</sup>
that made<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> his strength;<sup>[4581](h4581.md)</sup> but
trusted<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in the abundance<sup>[7230](h7230.md)</sup>
of his riches,<sup>[6239](h6239.md)</sup> and
strengthened<sup>[5810](h5810.md)</sup> himself in his
wickedness.<sup>[1942](h1942.md)</sup>

8  But I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am like a green<sup>[7488](h7488.md)</sup>
olive tree<sup>[2132](h2132)</sup> in the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup>
of God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> I trust<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> in the
mercy<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> of God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>
forever<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> and ever.<sup>[5703](h5703.md)</sup>

9  I will praise<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup> thee
forever,<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou
hast done<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> it: and I will wait
on<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> thy name;<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> it is good<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup>
before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> thy saints.<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa052.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ μαελεθ συνέσεως τῷ Δαυιδ

2 εἶπεν ἄφρων ἐν καρδίᾳ αὐτοῦ οὐκ ἔστιν θεός διεφθάρησαν καὶ
ἐβδελύχθησαν ἐν ἀνομίαις οὐκ ἔστιν ποιῶν ἀγαθόν

3 ὁ θεὸς ἐκ τοῦ οὐρανοῦ διέκυψεν ἐπὶ τοὺς υἱοὺς τῶν ἀνθρώπων τοῦ ἰδεῖν
εἰ ἔστιν συνίων ἢ ἐκζητῶν τὸν θεόν

4 πάντες ἐξέκλιναν ἅμα ἠχρεώθησαν οὐκ ἔστιν ποιῶν ἀγαθόν οὐκ ἔστιν ἕως
ἑνός

5 οὐχὶ γνώσονται πάντες οἱ ἐργαζόμενοι τὴν ἀνομίαν οἱ ἔσθοντες τὸν λαόν
μου βρώσει ἄρτου τὸν θεὸν οὐκ ἐπεκαλέσαντο

6 ἐκεῖ φοβηθήσονται φόβον οὗ οὐκ ἦν φόβος ὅτι ὁ θεὸς διεσκόρπισεν ὀστᾶ
ἀνθρωπαρέσκων κατῃσχύνθησαν ὅτι ὁ θεὸς ἐξουδένωσεν αὐτούς

7 τίς δώσει ἐκ Σιων τὸ σωτήριον τοῦ Ισραηλ ἐν τῷ ἐπιστρέψαι κύριον τὴν
αἰχμαλωσίαν τοῦ λαοῦ αὐτοῦ ἀγαλλιάσεται Ιακωβ καὶ εὐφρανθήσεται Ισραηλ

[Vul](../vul/psa052.md)

1 In finem, pro Maëleth intelligentiæ David. \[Dixit insipiens in corde
suo: Non est Deus.  

2 Corrupti sunt, et abominabiles facti sunt in iniquitatibus;  
non est qui faciat bonum.  

3 Deus de cælo prospexit super filios hominum,  
ut videat si est intelligens, aut requirens Deum.  

4 Omnes declinaverunt; simul inutiles facti sunt:  
non est qui faciat bonum, non est usque ad unum.  

5 Nonne scient omnes qui operantur iniquitatem,  
qui devorant plebem meam ut cibum panis?  

6 Deum non invocaverunt;  
illic trepidaverunt timore, ubi non erat timor.  
Quoniam Deus dissipavit ossa eorum qui hominibus placent:  
confusi sunt, quoniam Deus sprevit eos.  

7 Quis dabit ex Sion salutare Israël?  
cum converterit Deus captivitatem plebis suæ,  
exsultabit Jacob, et lætabitur Israël.\]
