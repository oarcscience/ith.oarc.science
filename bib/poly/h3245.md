H3245  
יסד  
יָסַד ‎ yâsad  
*yaw-sad‘*  
A primitive root; to *set* (literally or figuratively); intensively to
*found*; reflexively to *sit* down together, that is, *settle*,
*consult: -* appoint, take counsel, establish, (lay the, lay for a)
found (-ation), instruct, lay, ordain, set, X sure.
