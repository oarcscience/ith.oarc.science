H5046  
נגד  
נָגַד ‎ nâgad  
*naw-gad‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *front*, that is, stand boldly out
opposite; by implication (causatively), to *manifest*; figuratively to
*announce* (always by word of mouth to one present); specifically to
*expose*, *predict*, *explain*, *praise: -* bewray, X certainly,
certify, declare (-ing), denounce, expound, X fully, messenger, plainly,
profess, rehearse, report, shew (forth), speak, X surely, tell, utter.
