H6148  
ערב  
עָרַב ‎ ‛ârab  
*aw-rab‘*  
A primitive root; to *braid*, that is, *intermix*; technically to
*traffic* (as if by barter); also to *give* or *be* *security* (as a
kind of exchange): - engage, (inter-) meddle (with), mingle (self),
mortgage, occupy, give pledges, be (-come, put in) surety, undertake.
