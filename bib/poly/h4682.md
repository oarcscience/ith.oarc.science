H4682  
מצּה  
מַצָּה ‎ matstsâh  
*mats-tsaw‘*  
From [4711](h4711) in the sense of *greedily* devouring for sweetness;
properly *sweetness*; concretely *sweet* (that is, not soured or
bittered with yeast); specifically an *unfermented* *cake* or loaf, or
(elliptically) the festival of *Passover* (because no leaven was then
used): - unleavened (bread, cake), without leaven.
