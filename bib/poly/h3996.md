H3996  
מבוא  
מָבוֹא ‎ mâbô‘  
*maw-bo‘*  
From [935](h0935) an *entrance* (the place or the act); specifically
(with or without [8121](h8121) *sunset* or the *west*; also (adverbially
with preposition) *towards*: - by which came, as cometh, in coming, as
men enter into, entering, entrance into, entry, where goeth, going down,
+ westward. Compare [4126](h4126)
