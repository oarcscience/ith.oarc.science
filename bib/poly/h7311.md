H7311  
רוּם  
רוּם ‎ rûm  
*room*  
A primitive root; to *be* *high* actively to *rise* or *raise* (in
various applications, literally or figuratively): - bring up, exalt
(self), extol, give, go up, haughty, heave (up), (be, lift up on, make
on, set up on, too) high (-er, one), hold up, levy, lift (-er) up, (be)
lofty, (X a-) loud, mount up, offer (up), + presumptuously, (be) promote
(-ion), proud, set up, tall (-er), take (away, off, up), breed worms.
