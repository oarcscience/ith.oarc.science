### Psalms 27

[KJV](../kjv/psa027)

1  A Psalm of David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> The
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is my light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> and my
salvation;<sup>[3468](h3468)</sup> whom<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> shall I fear?<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is the strength<sup>[4581](h4581)</sup> of
my life;<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> of whom<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> shall I be afraid?<sup>[6342](h6342)</sup>

2  When the wicked,<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> even mine
enemies<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> and my foes,<sup>[341](h0341)</sup>
came<sup>[7126](h7126)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me to eat
up<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> my
flesh,<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup> they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup>
stumbled<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup> and fell.<sup>[5307](h5307)</sup>

3  Though<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> a host<sup>[4264](h4264)</sup> should
encamp<sup>[2583](h2583)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me, my
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
fear:<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> though<sup>[518](h0518)</sup>
war<sup>[4421](h4421)</sup> should rise<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me, in this<sup>[2063](h2063)</sup> will
I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> be confident.<sup>[982](h0982)</sup>

4  One<sup>[259](h0259)</sup> thing have I
desired<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> that will I
seek after;<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> that I may
dwell<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> in the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of
the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the

behold<sup>[2372](h2372)</sup> the beauty<sup>[5278](h5278)</sup> of the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and to inquire<sup>[1239](h1239)</sup> in

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in the time<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of
trouble<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> he shall hide<sup>[6845](h6845)</sup> me
in his pavilion:<sup>[5520](h5520)</sup> in the
secret<sup>[5643](h5643)</sup> of his tabernacle<sup>[168](h0168)</sup>
shall he hide<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> me; he shall set me
up<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> upon a rock.<sup>[6697](h6697)</sup>

6  And now<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> shall mine
head<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> be lifted up<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup>
above<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> mine enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> round
about<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> me: therefore will I
offer<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> in his tabernacle<sup>[168](h0168)</sup>
sacrifices<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup> of joy;<sup>[8643](h8643)</sup> I
will sing,<sup>[7891](h7891)</sup> yea, I will sing
praises<sup>[2167](h2167)</sup> unto the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

7  Hear,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> when I
cry<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> with my voice:<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> have
mercy<sup>[2603](h2603)</sup> also upon me, and
answer<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> me.

8  When thou saidst, Seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> ye my
face;<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> my heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>

seek.<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup>

9  Hide<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> thy

not thy servant away<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> <sup>[408](h0408)</sup>

10  When<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my father<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> and my

the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will take me up.<sup>[622](h0622)</sup>

11  Teach<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> me thy way,<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and lead<sup>[5148](h5148)</sup> me in a
plain<sup>[4334](h4334)</sup> path,<sup>[734](h0734)</sup> because
of<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> mine enemies.<sup>[8324](h8324)</sup>

12  Deliver<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> me not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> over
unto the will<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> of mine
enemies:<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
false<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> witnesses<sup>[5707](h5707)</sup> are
risen up<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> against me, and such as breathe
out<sup>[3307](h3307)</sup> cruelty.<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup>

13  I had fainted, unless<sup>[3884](h3884)</sup> I had
believed<sup>[539](h0539)</sup> to see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> the
goodness<sup>[2898](h2898)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> in
the land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of the living.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>

14  Wait<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> on<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> be of good
courage,<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> and he shall
strengthen<sup>[553](h0553)</sup> thine heart:<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>
wait,<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> I say, on<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa027)

1 τοῦ Δαυιδ πρὸς σέ κύριε ἐκέκραξα ὁ θεός μου μὴ παρασιωπήσῃς ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ
μήποτε παρασιωπήσῃς ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ καὶ ὁμοιωθήσομαι τοῖς καταβαίνουσιν εἰς
λάκκον

2 εἰσάκουσον τῆς φωνῆς τῆς δεήσεώς μου ἐν τῷ δέεσθαί με πρὸς σέ ἐν τῷ με
αἴρειν χεῖράς μου πρὸς ναὸν ἅγιόν σου

3 μὴ συνελκύσῃς μετὰ ἁμαρτωλῶν τὴν ψυχήν μου καὶ μετὰ ἐργαζομένων
ἀδικίαν μὴ συναπολέσῃς με τῶν λαλούντων εἰρήνην μετὰ τῶν πλησίον αὐτῶν
κακὰ δὲ ἐν ταῖς καρδίαις αὐτῶν

4 δὸς αὐτοῖς κατὰ τὰ ἔργα αὐτῶν καὶ κατὰ τὴν πονηρίαν τῶν ἐπιτηδευμάτων
αὐτῶν κατὰ τὰ ἔργα τῶν χειρῶν αὐτῶν δὸς αὐτοῖς ἀπόδος τὸ ἀνταπόδομα
αὐτῶν αὐτοῖς

5 ὅτι οὐ συνῆκαν εἰς τὰ ἔργα κυρίου καὶ εἰς τὰ ἔργα τῶν χειρῶν αὐτοῦ
καθελεῖς αὐτοὺς καὶ οὐ μὴ οἰκοδομήσεις αὐτούς

6 εὐλογητὸς κύριος ὅτι εἰσήκουσεν τῆς φωνῆς τῆς δεήσεώς μου

7 κύριος βοηθός μου καὶ ὑπερασπιστής μου ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ ἤλπισεν ἡ καρδία μου
καὶ ἐβοηθήθην καὶ ἀνέθαλεν ἡ σάρξ μου καὶ ἐκ θελήματός μου
ἐξομολογήσομαι αὐτῷ

8 κύριος κραταίωμα τοῦ λαοῦ αὐτοῦ καὶ ὑπερασπιστὴς τῶν σωτηρίων τοῦ
χριστοῦ αὐτοῦ ἐστιν

9 σῶσον τὸν λαόν σου καὶ εὐλόγησον τὴν κληρονομίαν σου καὶ ποίμανον
αὐτοὺς καὶ ἔπαρον αὐτοὺς ἕως τοῦ αἰῶνος

[Vul](../vul/psa027)

1 Psalmus ipsi David. \[Ad te, Domine, clamabo; Deus meus, ne sileas a
me:  
nequando taceas a me, et assimilabor descendentibus in lacum.  

2 Exaudi, Domine, vocem deprecationis meæ dum oro ad te;  
dum extollo manus meas ad templum sanctum tuum.  

3 Ne simul trahas me cum peccatoribus,  
et cum operantibus iniquitatem ne perdas me;  
qui loquuntur pacem cum proximo suo,  
mala autem in cordibus eorum.  

4 Da illis secundum opera eorum,  
et secundum nequitiam adinventionum ipsorum.  
Secundum opera manuum eorum tribue illis;  
redde retributionem eorum ipsis.  

5 Quoniam non intellexerunt opera Domini,  
et in opera manuum ejus destrues illos,  
et non ædificabis eos.  

6 Benedictus Dominus,  
quoniam exaudivit vocem deprecationis meæ.  

7 Dominus adjutor meus et protector meus;  
in ipso speravit cor meum, et adjutus sum:  
et refloruit caro mea,  
et ex voluntate mea confitebor ei.  

8 Dominus fortitudo plebis suæ,  
et protector salvationum christi sui est.  

9 Salvum fac populum tuum, Domine, et benedic hæreditati tuæ;  
et rege eos, et extolle illos usque in æternum.\]
