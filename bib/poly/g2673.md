G2673  
καταργέω  
katargeō  
*kat-arg-eh‘-o*  
From [2596](g2596) and [691](g0691) to *be* (*render*) *entirely* *idle*
(*useless*), literally or figuratively: - abolish, cease, cumber,
deliver, destroy, do away, become (make) of no (none, without) effect,
fail, loose, bring (come) to nought, put away (down), vanish away, make
void.
