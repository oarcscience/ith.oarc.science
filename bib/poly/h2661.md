H2661  
חפרפּרה חפר  
חֲפוֹר חַפַרפֵּרָה ‎ chăphôr chapharpêrâh  
*khaf-ore‘,* *khaf-ar-pay-raw‘*  
From [2658](h2658) a *hole*; only in connection with [6512](h6512) which
ought rather to be joined as one word (shown as second form; by
reduplication from [2658](h2658) a *burrower*, that is, probably a
*rat*): - + mole.
