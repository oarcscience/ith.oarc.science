H256  
אחב אחאב  
אַחאָב אֶחָב ‎ ‘ach‘âb ‘echâb  
*akh-awb‘,* *ekh-awb‘*  
The second form used once (by contraction) in [Jer
29:22](jer029.htm#022); from [251](h0251) and [1](h0001) *brother* (that
is, *friend*) of (his) *father*; *Achab*, the name of a king of Israel
and of a prophet at Babylon: - Ahab.
