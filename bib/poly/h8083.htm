שְׁמוֹנֶה שְׁמוֹנֶה שְׁמוֹנָה שְׁמוֹנָה ‎ shemôneh shemôneh shemônâh shemônâh
(1,2) shem-o-neh‘, (3,4) shem-o-naw‘
Apparently from 8082 through the idea of plumpness; a cardinal number, eight (as if a surplus above the “perfect” seven); also (as ordinal) eighth: - eight ([-een, -eenth]), eighth.
