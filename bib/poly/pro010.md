### Proverbs 10

[KJV](../kjv/pro010.md)

1  The proverbs<sup>[4912](h4912.md)</sup> of
Solomon.<sup>[8010](h8010)</sup> A wise<sup>[2450](h2450.md)</sup>
son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> maketh a glad<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup>
father:<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> but a foolish<sup>[3684](h3684.md)</sup>
son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> is the heaviness<sup>[8424](h8424.md)</sup> of
his mother.<sup>[517](h0517.md)</sup>

2  Treasures<sup>[214](h0214.md)</sup> of
wickedness<sup>[7562](h7562)</sup> profit<sup>[3276](h3276.md)</sup>
nothing:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> but
righteousness<sup>[6666](h6666)</sup> delivereth<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup>
from death.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4194](h4194.md)</sup>

3  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
suffer the soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> of the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> to famish:<sup>[7456](h7456.md)</sup> but
he casteth away<sup>[1920](h1920.md)</sup> the
substance<sup>[1942](h1942)</sup> of the wicked.<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>

4  He becometh poor<sup>[7326](h7326.md)</sup> that
dealeth<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> with a slack<sup>[7423](h7423.md)</sup>
hand:<sup>[3709](h3709)</sup> but the hand<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of
the diligent<sup>[2742](h2742.md)</sup> maketh
rich.<sup>[6238](h6238.md)</sup>

5  He that gathereth<sup>[103](h0103.md)</sup> in
summer<sup>[7019](h7019)</sup> is a wise<sup>[7919](h7919.md)</sup>
son:<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> but he that
sleepeth<sup>[7290](h7290)</sup> in harvest<sup>[7105](h7105.md)</sup> is a
son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> that causeth shame.<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup>

6  Blessings<sup>[1293](h1293.md)</sup> are upon the
head<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> of the just:<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> but
violence<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> covereth<sup>[3680](h3680.md)</sup> the
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> of the wicked.<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>

7  The memory<sup>[2143](h2143.md)</sup> of the
just<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> is blessed:<sup>[1293](h1293.md)</sup> but the
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup> shall
rot.<sup>[7537](h7537.md)</sup>

8  The wise<sup>[2450](h2450)</sup> in heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>
will receive<sup>[3947](h3947.md)</sup>
commandments:<sup>[4687](h4687.md)</sup> but a
prating<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> fool<sup>[191](h0191.md)</sup> shall
fall.<sup>[3832](h3832.md)</sup>

9  He that walketh<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
uprightly<sup>[8537](h8537)</sup> walketh<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
surely:<sup>[983](h0983.md)</sup> but he that
perverteth<sup>[6140](h6140)</sup> his ways<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup>
shall be known.<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup>

10  He that winketh<sup>[7169](h7169.md)</sup> with the
eye<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> causeth<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
sorrow:<sup>[6094](h6094)</sup> but a prating<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup>
fool<sup>[191](h0191)</sup> shall fall.<sup>[3832](h3832.md)</sup>

11  The mouth<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> of a
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> man is a well<sup>[4726](h4726.md)</sup>
of life:<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> but violence<sup>[2555](h2555.md)</sup>
covereth<sup>[3680](h3680)</sup> the mouth<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> of
the wicked.<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>

12  Hatred<sup>[8135](h8135)</sup> stirreth up<sup>[5782](h5782.md)</sup>
strifes:<sup>[4090](h4090)</sup> but love<sup>[160](h0160.md)</sup>
covereth<sup>[3680](h3680)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> sins.<sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup>

13  In the lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> of him that hath
understanding<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> wisdom<sup>[2451](h2451.md)</sup> is
found:<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> but a rod<sup>[7626](h7626.md)</sup> is for
the back<sup>[1460](h1460.md)</sup> of him that is
void<sup>[2638](h2638)</sup> of understanding.<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>

14  Wise<sup>[2450](h2450)</sup> men lay up<sup>[6845](h6845.md)</sup>
knowledge:<sup>[1847](h1847)</sup> but the mouth<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup>
of the foolish<sup>[191](h0191)</sup> is near<sup>[7138](h7138.md)</sup>
destruction.<sup>[4288](h4288.md)</sup>

15  The rich man's<sup>[6223](h6223.md)</sup>
wealth<sup>[1952](h1952)</sup> is his strong<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup>
city:<sup>[7151](h7151)</sup> the destruction<sup>[4288](h4288.md)</sup> of
the poor<sup>[1800](h1800.md)</sup> is their
poverty.<sup>[7389](h7389.md)</sup>

16  The labor<sup>[6468](h6468.md)</sup> of the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> tendeth to
life:<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> the fruit<sup>[8393](h8393.md)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> to sin.<sup>[2403](h2403.md)</sup>

17  He is in the way<sup>[734](h0734.md)</sup> of
life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> that keepeth<sup>[8104](h8104.md)</sup>
instruction:<sup>[4148](h4148.md)</sup> but he that
refuseth<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> reproof<sup>[8433](h8433.md)</sup>
erreth.<sup>[8582](h8582.md)</sup>

18  He that hideth<sup>[3680](h3680.md)</sup>
hatred<sup>[8135](h8135)</sup> with lying<sup>[8267](h8267.md)</sup>
lips,<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> and he that
uttereth<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> a slander,<sup>[1681](h1681.md)</sup> is a
fool.<sup>[3684](h3684.md)</sup>

19  In the multitude<sup>[7230](h7230.md)</sup> of
words<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> there wanteth<sup>[2308](h2308.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> sin:<sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup> but he that
refraineth<sup>[2820](h2820)</sup> his lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> is
wise.<sup>[7919](h7919.md)</sup>

20  The tongue<sup>[3956](h3956.md)</sup> of the
just<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> is as choice<sup>[977](h0977.md)</sup>
silver:<sup>[3701](h3701)</sup> the heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> is little worth.<sup>[4592](h4592.md)</sup>

21  The lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> of the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> feed<sup>[7462](h7462.md)</sup>
many:<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> but fools<sup>[191](h0191.md)</sup>
die<sup>[4191](h4191)</sup> for want<sup>[2638](h2638.md)</sup> of
wisdom.<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>

22  The blessing<sup>[1293](h1293.md)</sup> of the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> it<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> maketh
rich,<sup>[6238](h6238)</sup> and he addeth<sup>[3254](h3254.md)</sup>
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> sorrow<sup>[6089](h6089.md)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup> it.

23  It is as sport<sup>[7814](h7814.md)</sup> to a
fool<sup>[3684](h3684)</sup> to do<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
mischief:<sup>[2154](h2154)</sup> but a man<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> of
understanding<sup>[8394](h8394.md)</sup> hath
wisdom.<sup>[2451](h2451.md)</sup>

24  The fear<sup>[4034](h4034.md)</sup> of the
wicked,<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> it<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> shall come
upon<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> him: but the desire<sup>[8378](h8378.md)</sup>
of the righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> shall be
granted.<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>

25  As the whirlwind<sup>[5492](h5492.md)</sup>
passeth,<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup> so is the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> no<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup> more: but the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> is an
everlasting<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> foundation.<sup>[3247](h3247.md)</sup>

26  As vinegar<sup>[2558](h2558.md)</sup> to the
teeth,<sup>[8127](h8127)</sup> and as smoke<sup>[6227](h6227.md)</sup> to
the eyes,<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> so<sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> is the
sluggard<sup>[6102](h6102.md)</sup> to them that
send<sup>[7971](h7971.md)</sup> him.

27  The fear<sup>[3374](h3374)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
prolongeth<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup> days:<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> but the
years<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>
shall be shortened.<sup>[7114](h7114.md)</sup>

28  The hope<sup>[8431](h8431.md)</sup> of the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> shall be
gladness:<sup>[8057](h8057.md)</sup> but the
expectation<sup>[8615](h8615.md)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> shall perish.<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup>

29  The way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
is strength<sup>[4581](h4581.md)</sup> to the
upright:<sup>[8537](h8537)</sup> but destruction<sup>[4288](h4288.md)</sup>
shall be to the workers<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> of
iniquity.<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup>

30  The righteous<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> shall
never<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> <sup>[1077](h1077.md)</sup> be
removed:<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> but the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>
shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> inhabit<sup>[7931](h7931.md)</sup> the
earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

31  The mouth<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> of the
just<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> bringeth forth<sup>[5107](h5107.md)</sup>
wisdom:<sup>[2451](h2451)</sup> but the froward<sup>[8419](h8419.md)</sup>
tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> shall be cut out.<sup>[3772](h3772.md)</sup>

32  The lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup> of the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> know<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> what is
acceptable:<sup>[7522](h7522.md)</sup> but the
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>
speaketh frowardness.<sup>[8419](h8419.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/pro010.md)

1 υἱὸς σοφὸς εὐφραίνει πατέρα υἱὸς δὲ ἄφρων λύπη τῇ μητρί

2 οὐκ ὠφελήσουσιν θησαυροὶ ἀνόμους δικαιοσύνη δὲ ῥύσεται ἐκ θανάτου

3 οὐ λιμοκτονήσει κύριος ψυχὴν δικαίαν ζωὴν δὲ ἀσεβῶν ἀνατρέψει

4 πενία ἄνδρα ταπεινοῖ χεῖρες δὲ ἀνδρείων πλουτίζουσιν  
υἱὸς πεπαιδευμένος σοφὸς ἔσται τῷ δὲ ἄφρονι διακόνῳ χρήσεται

5 διεσώθη ἀπὸ καύματος υἱὸς νοήμων ἀνεμόφθορος δὲ γίνεται ἐν ἀμήτῳ υἱὸς
παράνομος

6 εὐλογία κυρίου ἐπὶ κεφαλὴν δικαίου στόμα δὲ ἀσεβῶν καλύψει πένθος
ἄωρον

7 μνήμη δικαίων μετ᾽ ἐγκωμίων ὄνομα δὲ ἀσεβοῦς σβέννυται

8 σοφὸς καρδίᾳ δέξεται ἐντολάς ὁ δὲ ἄστεγος χείλεσιν σκολιάζων
ὑποσκελισθήσεται

9 ὃς πορεύεται ἁπλῶς πορεύεται πεποιθώς ὁ δὲ διαστρέφων τὰς ὁδοὺς αὐτοῦ
γνωσθήσεται

10 ὁ ἐννεύων ὀφθαλμοῖς μετὰ δόλου συνάγει ἀνδράσι λύπας ὁ δὲ ἐλέγχων
μετὰ παρρησίας εἰρηνοποιεῖ

11 πηγὴ ζωῆς ἐν χειρὶ δικαίου στόμα δὲ ἀσεβοῦς καλύψει ἀπώλεια

12 μῖσος ἐγείρει νεῖκος πάντας δὲ τοὺς μὴ φιλονεικοῦντας καλύπτει φιλία

13 ὃς ἐκ χειλέων προφέρει σοφίαν ῥάβδῳ τύπτει ἄνδρα ἀκάρδιον

14 σοφοὶ κρύψουσιν αἴσθησιν στόμα δὲ προπετοῦς ἐγγίζει συντριβῇ

15 κτῆσις πλουσίων πόλις ὀχυρά συντριβὴ δὲ ἀσεβῶν πενία

16 ἔργα δικαίων ζωὴν ποιεῖ καρποὶ δὲ ἀσεβῶν ἁμαρτίας

17 ὁδοὺς δικαίας ζωῆς φυλάσσει παιδεία παιδεία δὲ ἀνεξέλεγκτος πλανᾶται

18 καλύπτουσιν ἔχθραν χείλη δίκαια οἱ δὲ ἐκφέροντες λοιδορίας
ἀφρονέστατοί εἰσιν

19 ἐκ πολυλογίας οὐκ ἐκφεύξῃ ἁμαρτίαν φειδόμενος δὲ χειλέων νοήμων ἔσῃ

20 ἄργυρος πεπυρωμένος γλῶσσα δικαίου καρδία δὲ ἀσεβοῦς ἐκλείψει

21 χείλη δικαίων ἐπίσταται ὑψηλά οἱ δὲ ἄφρονες ἐν ἐνδείᾳ τελευτῶσιν

22 εὐλογία κυρίου ἐπὶ κεφαλὴν δικαίου αὕτη πλουτίζει καὶ οὐ μὴ προστεθῇ
αὐτῇ λύπη ἐν καρδίᾳ

23 ἐν γέλωτι ἄφρων πράσσει κακά ἡ δὲ σοφία ἀνδρὶ τίκτει φρόνησιν

24 ἐν ἀπωλείᾳ ἀσεβὴς περιφέρεται ἐπιθυμία δὲ δικαίου δεκτή

25 παραπορευομένης καταιγίδος ἀφανίζεται ἀσεβής δίκαιος δὲ ἐκκλίνας
σῴζεται εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα

26 ὥσπερ ὄμφαξ ὀδοῦσι βλαβερὸν καὶ καπνὸς ὄμμασιν οὕτως παρανομία τοῖς
χρωμένοις αὐτήν

27 φόβος κυρίου προστίθησιν ἡμέρας ἔτη δὲ ἀσεβῶν ὀλιγωθήσεται

28 ἐγχρονίζει δικαίοις εὐφροσύνη ἐλπὶς δὲ ἀσεβῶν ὄλλυται

29 ὀχύρωμα ὁσίου φόβος κυρίου συντριβὴ δὲ τοῖς ἐργαζομένοις κακά

30 δίκαιος τὸν αἰῶνα οὐκ ἐνδώσει ἀσεβεῖς δὲ οὐκ οἰκήσουσιν γῆν

31 στόμα δικαίου ἀποστάζει σοφίαν γλῶσσα δὲ ἀδίκου ἐξολεῖται

32 χείλη ἀνδρῶν δικαίων ἀποστάζει χάριτας στόμα δὲ ἀσεβῶν ἀποστρέφεται

‎21 ‏שִׂפְתֵ֣י צַ֭דִּיק יִרְע֣וּ רַבִּ֑ים וֶֽ֝אֱוִילִ֗ים בַּחֲסַר־לֵ֥ב
יָמֽוּתוּ׃

‎22 ‏בִּרְכַּ֣ת יְ֭הוָה הִ֣יא תַעֲשִׁ֑יר וְלֹֽא־יוֹסִ֖ף עֶ֣צֶב עִמָּֽהּ׃

‎23 ‏כִּשְׂח֣וֹק לִ֭כְסִיל עֲשׂ֣וֹת זִמָּ֑ה וְ֝חָכְמָ֗ה לְאִ֣ישׁ
תְּבוּנָֽה׃

‎24 ‏מְגוֹרַ֣ת רָ֭שָׁע הִ֣יא תְבוֹאֶ֑נּוּ וְתַאֲוַ֖ת צַדִּיקִ֣ים
יִתֵּֽן׃

‎25 ‏כַּעֲב֣וֹר ס֭וּפָה<sup>3</sup> וְאֵ֣ין רָשָׁ֑ע וְ֝צַדִּ֗יק יְס֣וֹד
עוֹלָֽם׃

‎26 ‏כַּחֹ֤מֶץ׀ לַשִּׁנַּ֗יִם וְכֶעָשָׁ֥ן לָעֵינָ֑יִם כֵּ֥ן הֶ֝עָצֵ֗ל
לְשֹׁלְחָֽיו׃

‎27 ‏יִרְאַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה תּוֹסִ֣יף יָמִ֑ים וּשְׁנ֖וֹת רְשָׁעִ֣ים
תִּקְצֹֽרְנָה׃

‎28 ‏תּוֹחֶ֣לֶת צַדִּיקִ֣ים שִׂמְחָ֑ה וְתִקְוַ֖ת רְשָׁעִ֣ים תֹּאבֵֽד׃

‎29 ‏מָע֣וֹז לַ֭תֹּם דֶּ֣רֶךְ יְהוָ֑ה וּ֝מְחִתָּ֗ה לְפֹ֣עֲלֵי אָֽוֶן׃

‎30 ‏צַדִּ֣יק לְעוֹלָ֣ם בַּל־יִמּ֑וֹט וּ֝רְשָׁעִ֗ים לֹ֣א
יִשְׁכְּנוּ־אָֽרֶץ׃

‎31 ‏פִּֽי־צַ֭דִּיק יָנ֣וּב חָכְמָ֑ה וּלְשׁ֥וֹן תַּ֝הְפֻּכ֗וֹת
תִּכָּרֵֽת׃

‎32 ‏שִׂפְתֵ֣י צַ֭דִּיק יֵדְע֣וּן רָצ֑וֹן וּפִ֥י רְ֝שָׁעִ֗ים
תַּהְפֻּכֽוֹת׃

[Vul](../vul/pro010.md)

1 \[Filius sapiens lætificat patrem,  
filius vero stultus mœstitia est matris suæ.  

2 Nil proderunt thesauri impietatis,  
justitia vero liberabit a morte.  

3 Non affliget Dominus fame animam justi,  
et insidias impiorum subvertet.  

4 Egestatem operata est manus remissa;  
manus autem fortium divitias parat.  
Qui nititur mendaciis, hic pascit ventos;  
idem autem ipse sequitur aves volantes.  

5 Qui congregat in messe, filius sapiens est;  
qui autem stertit æstate, filius confusionis.  

6 Benedictio Domini super caput justi;  
os autem impiorum operit iniquitas.  

7 Memoria justi cum laudibus,  
et nomen impiorum putrescet.  

8 Sapiens corde præcepta suscipit;  
stultus cæditur labiis.  

9 Qui ambulat simpliciter ambulat confidenter;  
qui autem depravat vias suas manifestus erit.  

10 Qui annuit oculo dabit dolorem;  
et stultus labiis verberabitur.\]  

11 \[Vena vitæ os justi,  
et os impiorum operit iniquitatem.  

12 Odium suscitat rixas,  
et universa delicta operit caritas.  

13 In labiis sapientis invenitur sapientia,  
et virga in dorso ejus qui indiget corde.  

14 Sapientes abscondunt scientiam;  
os autem stulti confusioni proximum est.  

15 Substantia divitis, urbs fortitudinis ejus;  
pavor pauperum egestas eorum.  

16 Opus justi ad vitam,  
fructus autem impii ad peccatum.  

17 Via vitæ custodienti disciplinam;  
qui autem increpationes relinquit, errat.  

18 Abscondunt odium labia mendacia;  
qui profert contumeliam, insipiens est.  

19 In multiloquio non deerit peccatum,  
qui autem moderatur labia sua prudentissimus est.  

20 Argentum electum lingua justi;  
cor autem impiorum pro nihilo.  

21 Labia justi erudiunt plurimos;  
qui autem indocti sunt in cordis egestate morientur.\]  

22 \[Benedictio Domini divites facit,  
nec sociabitur eis afflictio.  

23 Quasi per risum stultus operatur scelus,  
sapientia autem est viro prudentia.  

24 Quod timet impius veniet super eum;  
desiderium suum justus dabitur.  

25 Quasi tempestas transiens non erit impius;  
justus autem quasi fundamentum sempiternum.  

26 Sicut acetum dentibus, et fumus oculis,  
sic piger his qui miserunt eum.  

27 Timor Domini apponet dies,  
et anni impiorum breviabuntur.  

28 Exspectatio justorum lætitia,  
spes autem impiorum peribit.  

29 Fortitudo simplicis via Domini,  
et pavor his qui operantur malum.  

30 Justus in æternum non commovebitur,  
impii autem non habitabunt super terram.  

31 Os justi parturiet sapientiam;  
lingua pravorum peribit.  

32 Labia justi considerant placita,  
et os impiorum perversa.\]
