H905  
בּד  
בַּד ‎ bad  
*bad*  
From [909](h0909.md) properly *separation*; by implication a *part* of the
body, *branch* of a tree, *bar* for carrying; figuratively *chief* of a
city; especially (with prepositional prefix) as adverb, *apart*, *only*,
*besides: -* alone, apart, bar, besides, branch, by self, of each alike,
except, only, part, staff, strength.
