H7185  
קשׁה  
קָשָׁה ‎ qâshâh  
*kaw-shaw‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *be* *dense*, that is, tough or *severe*
(in various applications): - be cruel, be fiercer, make grievous, be
(\[ask a\], be in, have, seem, would) hard (-en, \[labour\], -ly,
thing), be sore, (be, make) stiff (-en, \[-necked\]).
