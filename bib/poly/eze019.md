### Ezekiel 19

[KJV](../kjv/eze019.md)

1  Moreover take thou up<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> <sup>[5375](h5375.md)</sup>
a lamentation<sup>[7015](h7015)</sup> for<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
princes<sup>[5387](h5387)</sup> of Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>

2  And say,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> What<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> is thy
mother?<sup>[517](h0517)</sup> A lioness:<sup>[3833](h3833.md)</sup> she
lay down<sup>[7257](h7257)</sup> among<sup>[996](h0996.md)</sup>
lions,<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> she nourished<sup>[7235](h7235.md)</sup> her
whelps<sup>[1482](h1482)</sup> among<sup>[8432](h8432.md)</sup> young
lions.<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup>

3  And she brought up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> one<sup>[259](h0259.md)</sup>
of her whelps:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1482](h1482.md)</sup> it
became<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a young lion,<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup> and
it learned<sup>[3925](h3925)</sup> to catch<sup>[2963](h2963.md)</sup> the
prey;<sup>[2964](h2964)</sup> it devoured<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup>
men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

4  The nations<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> also
heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> of<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> him; he was
taken<sup>[8610](h8610)</sup> in their pit,<sup>[7845](h7845.md)</sup> and
they brought<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> him with
chains<sup>[2397](h2397)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of Egypt.<sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup>

5  Now when she saw<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
she had waited,<sup>[3176](h3176.md)</sup> and her
hope<sup>[8615](h8615)</sup> was lost,<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup> then she
took<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> another<sup>[259](h0259.md)</sup> of her
whelps,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1482](h1482.md)</sup> and
made<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> him a young lion.<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup>

6  And he went up and down<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
among<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup> the lions,<sup>[738](h0738.md)</sup> he
became<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a young lion,<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup> and
learned<sup>[3925](h3925)</sup> to catch<sup>[2963](h2963.md)</sup> the
prey,<sup>[2964](h2964)</sup> and devoured<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup>
men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

7  And he knew<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> their desolate
palaces,<sup>[759](h0759.md)</sup> and he laid
waste<sup>[2717](h2717)</sup> their cities;<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup> and
the land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> was desolate,<sup>[3456](h3456.md)</sup>
and the fullness<sup>[4393](h4393.md)</sup> thereof, by the
noise<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6963](h6963.md)</sup> of his
roaring.<sup>[7581](h7581.md)</sup>

8  Then the nations<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> set<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him on every
side<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> from the provinces,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[4082](h4082)</sup> and spread<sup>[6566](h6566.md)</sup> their
net<sup>[7568](h7568)</sup> over<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him: he was
taken<sup>[8610](h8610)</sup> in their pit.<sup>[7845](h7845.md)</sup>

9  And they put<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup> him in
ward<sup>[5474](h5474)</sup> in chains,<sup>[2397](h2397.md)</sup> and
brought<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> him to<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> of Babylon:<sup>[894](h0894.md)</sup> they
brought<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> him into holds,<sup>[4679](h4679.md)</sup>
that<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> his voice<sup>[6963](h6963.md)</sup> should
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> more<sup>[5750](h5750.md)</sup> be
heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> upon<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> of Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>

10  Thy mother<sup>[517](h0517.md)</sup> is like a
vine<sup>[1612](h1612)</sup> in thy blood,<sup>[1818](h1818.md)</sup>
planted<sup>[8362](h8362)</sup> by<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
waters:<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> she was<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
fruitful<sup>[6509](h6509.md)</sup> and full of
branches<sup>[6058](h6058.md)</sup> by reason of
many<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> waters.<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[4325](h4325.md)</sup>

11  And she had<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> strong<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup>
rods<sup>[4294](h4294)</sup> for<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
scepters<sup>[7626](h7626.md)</sup> of them that bore
rule,<sup>[4910](h4910)</sup> and her stature<sup>[6967](h6967.md)</sup>
was exalted<sup>[1361](h1361)</sup> among<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
<sup>[996](h0996)</sup> the thick branches,<sup>[5688](h5688.md)</sup> and
she appeared<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> in her
height<sup>[1363](h1363.md)</sup> with the
multitude<sup>[7230](h7230.md)</sup> of her
branches.<sup>[1808](h1808.md)</sup>

12  But she was plucked up<sup>[5428](h5428.md)</sup> in
fury,<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup> she was cast down<sup>[7993](h7993.md)</sup>
to the ground,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and the
east<sup>[6921](h6921)</sup> wind<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup> dried
up<sup>[3001](h3001)</sup> her fruit:<sup>[6529](h6529.md)</sup> her
strong<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> rods<sup>[4294](h4294.md)</sup> were
broken<sup>[6561](h6561)</sup> and withered;<sup>[3001](h3001.md)</sup> the
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> consumed<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> them.

13  And now<sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup> she is
planted<sup>[8362](h8362.md)</sup> in the
wilderness,<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> in a dry<sup>[6723](h6723.md)</sup> and
thirsty<sup>[6772](h6772)</sup> ground.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

14  And fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> is gone out<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup>
of a rod<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4294](h4294.md)</sup> of her
branches,<sup>[905](h0905.md)</sup> which hath
devoured<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> her fruit,<sup>[6529](h6529.md)</sup> so
that she hath<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
strong<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> rod<sup>[4294](h4294.md)</sup> to be a
scepter<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> to rule.<sup>[4910](h4910.md)</sup>
This<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> is a lamentation,<sup>[7015](h7015.md)</sup>
and shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> for a
lamentation.<sup>[7015](h7015.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/eze019.md)

1 καὶ σὺ λαβὲ θρῆνον ἐπὶ τὸν ἄρχοντα τοῦ Ισραηλ

2 καὶ ἐρεῖς τί ἡ μήτηρ σου σκύμνος ἐν μέσῳ λεόντων ἐγενήθη ἐν μέσῳ
λεόντων ἐπλήθυνεν σκύμνους αὐτῆς

3 καὶ ἀπεπήδησεν εἷς τῶν σκύμνων αὐτῆς λέων ἐγένετο καὶ ἔμαθεν τοῦ
ἁρπάζειν ἁρπάγματα ἀνθρώπους ἔφαγεν

4 καὶ ἤκουσαν κατ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἔθνη ἐν τῇ διαφθορᾷ αὐτῶν συνελήμφθη καὶ ἤγαγον
αὐτὸν ἐν κημῷ εἰς γῆν Αἰγύπτου

5 καὶ εἶδεν ὅτι ἀπῶσται ἀπ᾽ αὐτῆς καὶ ἀπώλετο ἡ ὑπόστασις αὐτῆς καὶ
ἔλαβεν ἄλλον ἐκ τῶν σκύμνων αὐτῆς λέοντα ἔταξεν αὐτόν

6 καὶ ἀνεστρέφετο ἐν μέσῳ λεόντων λέων ἐγένετο καὶ ἔμαθεν ἁρπάζειν
ἁρπάγματα ἀνθρώπους ἔφαγεν

7 καὶ ἐνέμετο τῷ θράσει αὐτοῦ καὶ τὰς πόλεις αὐτῶν ἐξηρήμωσεν καὶ
ἠφάνισεν γῆν καὶ τὸ πλήρωμα αὐτῆς ἀπὸ φωνῆς ὠρύματος αὐτοῦ

8 καὶ ἔδωκαν ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν ἔθνη ἐκ χωρῶν κυκλόθεν καὶ ἐξεπέτασαν ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν
δίκτυα αὐτῶν ἐν διαφθορᾷ αὐτῶν συνελήμφθη

9 καὶ ἔθεντο αὐτὸν ἐν κημῷ καὶ ἐν γαλεάγρᾳ ἦλθεν πρὸς βασιλέα Βαβυλῶνος
καὶ εἰσήγαγεν αὐτὸν εἰς φυλακήν ὅπως μὴ ἀκουσθῇ ἡ φωνὴ αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ τὰ ὄρη
τοῦ Ισραηλ

10 ἡ μήτηρ σου ὡς ἄμπελος ὡς ἄνθος ἐν ῥόᾳ ἐν ὕδατι πεφυτευμένη ὁ καρπὸς
αὐτῆς καὶ ὁ βλαστὸς αὐτῆς ἐγένετο ἐξ ὕδατος πολλοῦ

11 καὶ ἐγένετο αὐτῇ ῥάβδος ἰσχύος ἐπὶ φυλὴν ἡγουμένων καὶ ὑψώθη τῷ
μεγέθει αὐτῆς ἐν μέσῳ στελεχῶν καὶ εἶδεν τὸ μέγεθος αὐτῆς ἐν πλήθει
κλημάτων αὐτῆς

12 καὶ κατεκλάσθη ἐν θυμῷ ἐπὶ γῆν ἐρρίφη καὶ ἄνεμος ὁ καύσων ἐξήρανεν τὰ
ἐκλεκτὰ αὐτῆς ἐξεδικήθη καὶ ἐξηράνθη ἡ ῥάβδος ἰσχύος αὐτῆς πῦρ ἀνήλωσεν
αὐτήν

13 καὶ νῦν πεφύτευκαν αὐτὴν ἐν τῇ ἐρήμῳ ἐν γῇ ἀνύδρῳ

14 καὶ ἐξῆλθεν πῦρ ἐκ ῥάβδου ἐκλεκτῶν αὐτῆς καὶ κατέφαγεν αὐτήν καὶ οὐκ
ἦν ἐν αὐτῇ ῥάβδος ἰσχύος φυλὴ εἰς παραβολὴν θρήνου ἐστὶν καὶ ἔσται εἰς
θρῆνον

1 Et tu assume planctum super principes Israël,

2 et dices: \[Quare mater tua leæna inter leones cubavit?  
in medio leunculorum enutrivit catulos suos?  

3 Et eduxit unum de leunculis suis,  
et leo factus est:  
et didicit capere prædam,  
hominemque comedere.  

4 Et audierunt de eo gentes:  
et non absque vulneribus suis ceperunt eum,  
et adduxerunt eum in catenis in terram Ægypti.  

5 Quæ cum vidisset quoniam infirmata est,  
et periit exspectatio ejus,  
tulit unum de leunculis suis;  
leonem constituit eum.  

6 Qui incedebat inter leones,  
et factus est leo:  
et didicit prædam capere,  
et homines devorare:  

7 didicit viduas facere,  
et civitates earum in desertum adducere:  
et desolata est terra et plenitudo ejus a voce rugitus illius.  

8 Et convenerunt adversus eum gentes undique de provinciis,  
et expanderunt super eum rete suum:  
in vulneribus earum captus est,  

9 et miserunt eum in caveam:  
in catenis adduxerunt eum ad regem Babylonis,  
miseruntque eum in carcerem,  
ne audiretur vox ejus ultra super montes Israël.  

10 Mater tua quasi vinea in sanguine tuo super aquam plantata est:  
fructus ejus et frondes ejus creverunt ex aquis multis.  

11 Et factæ sunt ei virgæ solidæ in sceptra dominantium,  
et exaltata est statura ejus inter frondes,  
et vidit altitudinem suam in multitudine palmitum suorum.  

12 Et evulsa est in ira, in terramque projecta,  
et ventus urens siccavit fructum ejus:  
marcuerunt et arefactæ sunt virgæ roboris ejus:  
ignis comedit eam.  

13 Et nunc transplantata est in desertum,  
in terra invia et sitienti.  

14 Et egressus est ignis de virga ramorum ejus,  
qui fructum ejus comedit:  
et non fuit in ea virga fortis,  
sceptrum dominantium.\] Planctus est, et erit in planctum.
