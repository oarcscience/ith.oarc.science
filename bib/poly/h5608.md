H5608  
ספר  
סָפַר ‎ sâphar  
*saw-far‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *score* with a mark as a tally or record,
that is, (by implication) to *inscribe*, and also to *enumerate*;
intensively to *recount*, that is, *celebrate: -* commune, (ac-) count,
declare, number, + penknife, reckon, scribe, shew forth, speak, talk,
tell (out), writer.
