H1931  
היא הוּא  
הוּא הִיא ‎ hû‘ hı̂y‘  
*hoo,* *he*  
The second form is the feminine beyond the Pentateuch; a primitive word,
the third person pronoun singular, *he* (*she* or *it*); only expressed
when emphatic or without a verb; also (intensively) *self*, or
(especially with the article) the *same*; sometimes (as demonstrative)
*this* or *that*; occasionally (instead of copula) *as* or *are: -* he,
as for her, him (-self), it, the same, she (herself), such, that (. . .
it), these, they, this, those, which (is), who.
