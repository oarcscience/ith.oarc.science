H8543  
תּמל תּמול  
תְּּמוֹל תְּּמוֹל ‎ t<sup>e</sup>môl t<sup>e</sup>môl  
*tem-ole‘,* *tem-ole‘*  
Probably for [865](h0865) properly *ago*, that is, a (short or long.md)
*time* *since*; especially *yesterday*, or (with [8032](h8032.md) *day*
*before* yesterday: - + before (-time), + these \[three\] days, +
heretofore, + time past, yesterday.
