H802  
נשׁים אשּׁה  
אִשָּׁה נָשִׁים ‎ ‘ishshâh nâshı̂ym  
*ish-shaw‘,* *naw-sheem‘*  
The first form is the feminine of [376](h0376) or [582](h0582.md) the
second form is an irregular plural; a *woman* (used in the same wide
sense as [582](h0582.md) .: - \[adulter\]ess, each, every, female, X many,
+ none, one, + together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.
