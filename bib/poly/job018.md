### Job 18

[KJV](../kjv/job018.md)

1  Then answered<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> Bildad<sup>[1085](h1085.md)</sup>
the Shuhite,<sup>[7747](h7747)</sup> and said,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup>

2  How long<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[575](h0575.md)</sup> will it be
ere ye make<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> an end<sup>[7078](h7078.md)</sup> of
words?<sup>[4405](h4405)</sup> mark,<sup>[995](h0995.md)</sup> and
afterwards<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> we will speak.<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>

3  Wherefore<sup>[4069](h4069.md)</sup> are we
counted<sup>[2803](h2803)</sup> as beasts,<sup>[929](h0929.md)</sup> and
reputed vile<sup>[2933](h2933.md)</sup> in your
sight?<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup>

4  He teareth<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> himself<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> in
his anger:<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> shall the earth<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
be forsaken<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> for<sup>[4616](h4616.md)</sup> thee?
and shall the rock<sup>[6697](h6697.md)</sup> be
removed<sup>[6275](h6275.md)</sup> out of his
place?<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4725](h4725.md)</sup>

5  Yea,<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> the light<sup>[216](h0216.md)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> shall be put out,<sup>[1846](h1846.md)</sup>
and the spark<sup>[7632](h7632)</sup> of his fire<sup>[784](h0784.md)</sup>
shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> shine.<sup>[5050](h5050.md)</sup>

6  The light<sup>[216](h0216.md)</sup> shall be
dark<sup>[2821](h2821)</sup> in his tabernacle,<sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup>
and his candle<sup>[5216](h5216.md)</sup> shall be put
out<sup>[1846](h1846)</sup> with<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him.

7  The steps<sup>[6806](h6806.md)</sup> of his
strength<sup>[202](h0202.md)</sup> shall be
straitened,<sup>[3334](h3334.md)</sup> and his own
counsel<sup>[6098](h6098.md)</sup> shall cast him
down.<sup>[7993](h7993.md)</sup>

8  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he is cast<sup>[7971](h7971.md)</sup> into a
net<sup>[7568](h7568)</sup> by his own feet,<sup>[7272](h7272.md)</sup> and
he walketh<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> a
snare.<sup>[7639](h7639.md)</sup>

9  The gin<sup>[6341](h6341)</sup> shall take<sup>[270](h0270.md)</sup> him
by the heel,<sup>[6119](h6119.md)</sup> and the
robber<sup>[6782](h6782)</sup> shall prevail<sup>[2388](h2388.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him.

10  The snare<sup>[2256](h2256)</sup> is laid<sup>[2934](h2934.md)</sup>
for him in the ground,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and a
trap<sup>[4434](h4434)</sup> for him in<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
way.<sup>[5410](h5410.md)</sup>

11  Terrors<sup>[1091](h1091.md)</sup> shall make him
afraid<sup>[1204](h1204)</sup> on every side,<sup>[5439](h5439.md)</sup>
and shall drive<sup>[6327](h6327.md)</sup> him to his
feet.<sup>[7272](h7272.md)</sup>

12  His strength<sup>[202](h0202)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
hunger-bitten,<sup>[7457](h7457.md)</sup> and
destruction<sup>[343](h0343.md)</sup> shall be
ready<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> at his side.<sup>[6763](h6763.md)</sup>

13  It shall devour<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> the
strength<sup>[905](h0905)</sup> of his skin:<sup>[5785](h5785.md)</sup>
even the firstborn<sup>[1060](h1060.md)</sup> of
death<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> shall devour<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> his
strength.<sup>[905](h0905.md)</sup>

14  His confidence<sup>[4009](h4009.md)</sup> shall be rooted
out<sup>[5423](h5423)</sup> of his tabernacle,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[168](h0168)</sup> and it shall bring<sup>[6805](h6805.md)</sup> him
to the king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> of terrors.<sup>[1091](h1091.md)</sup>

15  It shall dwell<sup>[7931](h7931.md)</sup> in his
tabernacle,<sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup> because it is
none<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1097](h1097.md)</sup> of his:
brimstone<sup>[1614](h1614.md)</sup> shall be
scattered<sup>[2219](h2219)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> his
habitation.<sup>[5116](h5116.md)</sup>

16  His roots<sup>[8328](h8328.md)</sup> shall be dried
up<sup>[3001](h3001)</sup> beneath,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup> and above<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[4605](h4605)</sup> shall his branch<sup>[7105](h7105.md)</sup> be cut
off.<sup>[5243](h5243.md)</sup>

17  His remembrance<sup>[2143](h2143.md)</sup> shall
perish<sup>[6](h0006)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> the
earth,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and he shall have
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> name<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>
in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the street.<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup>
<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup>

18  He shall be driven<sup>[1920](h1920.md)</sup> from
light<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[216](h0216.md)</sup>
into<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> darkness,<sup>[2822](h2822.md)</sup> and
chased<sup>[5074](h5074)</sup> out of the world.<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[8398](h8398.md)</sup>

19  He shall neither<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> have
son<sup>[5209](h5209)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
nephew<sup>[5220](h5220)</sup> among his people,<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
nor<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> any remaining<sup>[8300](h8300.md)</sup> in his
dwellings.<sup>[4033](h4033.md)</sup>

20  They that come after<sup>[314](h0314.md)</sup> him shall be
astonished<sup>[8074](h8074)</sup> at<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> his
day,<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> as they that went
before<sup>[6931](h6931)</sup> were frightened.<sup>[8178](h8178.md)</sup>

21  Surely<sup>[389](h0389)</sup> such<sup>[428](h0428.md)</sup> are the
dwellings<sup>[4908](h4908)</sup> of the wicked,<sup>[5767](h5767.md)</sup>
and this<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> is the place<sup>[4725](h4725.md)</sup> of
him that knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
God.<sup>[410](h0410.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/job018.md)

1 ὑπολαβὼν δὲ Βαλδαδ ὁ Σαυχίτης λέγει

2 μέχρι τίνος οὐ παύσῃ ἐπίσχες ἵνα καὶ αὐτοὶ λαλήσωμεν

3 διὰ τί ὥσπερ τετράποδα σεσιωπήκαμεν ἐναντίον σου

4 κέχρηταί σοι ὀργή τί γάρ ἐὰν σὺ ἀποθάνῃς ἀοίκητος ἡ ὑπ᾽ οὐρανόν ἢ
καταστραφήσεται ὄρη ἐκ θεμελίων

5 καὶ φῶς ἀσεβῶν σβεσθήσεται καὶ οὐκ ἀποβήσεται αὐτῶν ἡ φλόξ

6 τὸ φῶς αὐτοῦ σκότος ἐν διαίτῃ ὁ δὲ λύχνος ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ σβεσθήσεται

7 θηρεύσαισαν ἐλάχιστοι τὰ ὑπάρχοντα αὐτοῦ σφάλαι δὲ αὐτοῦ ἡ βουλή

8 ἐμβέβληται δὲ ὁ ποὺς αὐτοῦ ἐν παγίδι ἐν δικτύῳ ἑλιχθείη

9 ἔλθοισαν δὲ ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν παγίδες κατισχύσει ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν διψῶντας

10 κέκρυπται ἐν τῇ γῇ σχοινίον αὐτοῦ καὶ ἡ σύλλημψις αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ τρίβων

11 κύκλῳ ὀλέσαισαν αὐτὸν ὀδύναι πολλοὶ δὲ περὶ πόδας αὐτοῦ ἔλθοισαν ἐν
λιμῷ στενῷ

12 πτῶμα δὲ αὐτῷ ἡτοίμασται ἐξαίσιον

13 βρωθείησαν αὐτοῦ κλῶνες ποδῶν κατέδεται δὲ τὰ ὡραῖα αὐτοῦ θάνατος

14 ἐκραγείη δὲ ἐκ διαίτης αὐτοῦ ἴασις σχοίη δὲ αὐτὸν ἀνάγκη αἰτίᾳ
βασιλικῇ

15 κατασκηνώσει ἐν τῇ σκηνῇ αὐτοῦ ἐν νυκτὶ αὐτοῦ κατασπαρήσονται τὰ
εὐπρεπῆ αὐτοῦ θείῳ

16 ὑποκάτωθεν αἱ ῥίζαι αὐτοῦ ξηρανθήσονται καὶ ἐπάνωθεν ἐπιπεσεῖται
θερισμὸς αὐτοῦ

17 τὸ μνημόσυνον αὐτοῦ ἀπόλοιτο ἐκ γῆς καὶ ὑπάρχει ὄνομα αὐτῷ ἐπὶ
πρόσωπον ἐξωτέρω

18 ἀπώσειεν αὐτὸν ἐκ φωτὸς εἰς σκότος

19 οὐκ ἔσται ἐπίγνωστος ἐν λαῷ αὐτοῦ οὐδὲ σεσῳσμένος ἐν τῇ ὑπ᾽ οὐρανὸν ὁ
οἶκος αὐτοῦ ἀλλ᾽ ἐν τοῖς αὐτοῦ ζήσονται ἕτεροι

20 ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ ἐστέναξαν ἔσχατοι πρώτους δὲ ἔσχεν θαῦμα

21 οὗτοί εἰσιν οἶκοι ἀδίκων οὗτος δὲ ὁ τόπος τῶν μὴ εἰδότων τὸν κύριον

1 Respondens autem Baldad Suhites, dixit:

2 \[Usque ad quem finem verba jactabitis?  
intelligite prius, et sic loquamur.  

3 Quare reputati sumus ut jumenta,  
et sorduimus coram vobis?  

4 Qui perdis animam tuam in furore tuo,  
numquid propter te derelinquetur terra,  
et transferentur rupes de loco suo?  

5 Nonne lux impii extinguetur,  
nec splendebit flamma ignis ejus?  

6 Lux obtenebrescet in tabernaculo illius,  
et lucerna quæ super eum est extinguetur.  

7 Arctabuntur gressus virtutis ejus,  
et præcipitabit eum consilium suum.  

8 Immisit enim in rete pedes suos,  
et in maculis ejus ambulat.  

9 Tenebitur planta illius laqueo,  
et exardescet contra eum sitis.  

10 Abscondita est in terra pedica ejus,  
et decipula illius super semitam.  

11 Undique terrebunt eum formidines,  
et involvent pedes ejus.  

12 Attenuetur fame robur ejus,  
et inedia invadat costas illius.  

13 Devoret pulchritudinem cutis ejus;  
consumat brachia illius primogenita mors.  

14 Avellatur de tabernaculo suo fiducia ejus,  
et calcet super eum, quasi rex, interitus.  

15 Habitent in tabernaculo illius socii ejus qui non est;  
aspergatur in tabernaculo ejus sulphur.  

16 Deorsum radices ejus siccentur:  
sursum autem atteratur messis ejus.  

17 Memoria illius pereat de terra,  
et non celebretur nomen ejus in plateis.  

18 Expellet eum de luce in tenebras,  
et de orbe transferet eum.  

19 Non erit semen ejus, neque progenies in populo suo,  
nec ullæ reliquiæ in regionibus ejus.  

20 In die ejus stupebunt novissimi,  
et primos invadet horror.  

21 Hæc sunt ergo tabernacula iniqui,  
et iste locus ejus qui ignorat Deum.\]
