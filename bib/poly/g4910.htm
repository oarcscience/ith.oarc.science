G4910
συνευωχέω
suneuōcheō
soon-yoo-o-kheh‘-o
From 4862 and a derivative of a presumed compound of 2095 and a derivative of 2192 (meaning to be in good condition, that is, [by implication] to fare well, or feast); to entertain sumptuously in company with, that is, (middle or passive voice) to revel together: - feast with.
