H32  
אביחיל אביהיל  
אֲבִיהַיִל אֲבִיחַיִל ‎ ‘ăbı̂yhayil ‘ăbı̂ychayil  
*ab-ee-hah‘-yil,* *ab-ee-khah‘-yil*  
(More correct in its second form); from [1](h0001) and [2428](h2428)
*father* (that is, *possessor*) *of* *might*; *Abihail* or *Abichail*,
the name of three Israelites and two Israelitesses: - Abihail.
