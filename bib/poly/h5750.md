H5750  
עד עוד  
עוֹד עוֹד ‎ ‛ôd ‛ôd  
*ode,* *ode*  
From [5749](h5749.md) properly *iteration* or *continuance*; used only
adverbially (with or without preposition), *again*, *repeatedly*,
*still*, *more: -* again, X all life long, at all, besides, but, else,
further (-more), henceforth, (any) longer, (any) more (-over), X once,
since, (be) still, when, (good, the) while (having being), (as, because,
whether, while) yet (within).
