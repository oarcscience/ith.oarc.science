H7126  
קרב  
קָרַב ‎ qârab  
*kaw-rab‘*  
A primitive root; to *approach* (causatively *bring* *near*) for
whatever purpose: - (cause to) approach, (cause to) bring (forth, near),
(cause to) come (near, nigh), (cause to) draw near (nigh), go (near), be
at hand, join, be near, offer, present, produce, make ready, stand,
take.
