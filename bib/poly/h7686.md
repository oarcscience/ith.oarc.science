H7686  
שׁגה  
שָׁגָה ‎ shâgâh  
*shaw-gaw‘*  
A primitive root; to *stray* (causatively *mislead*), usually
(figuratively) to *mistake*, especially (morally) to *transgress*; by
extension (through the idea of intoxication) to *reel*, (figuratively)
*be* *enraptured: -* (cause to) go astray, deceive, err, be ravished,
sin through ignorance, (let, make to) wander.
