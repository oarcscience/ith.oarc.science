H5145  
נזר נזר  
נֶזֶר נֵזֶר ‎ nezer nêzer  
*neh‘-zer,* *nay‘-zer*  
From [5144](h5144) properly something *set* *apart*, that is,
(abstractly) *dedication* (of a priest or Nazirite); hence (concretely)
unshorn *locks*; also (by implication) a *chaplet* (especially of
royalty): - consecration, crown, hair, separation.
