G1437  
ἐάν  
ean  
*eh-an‘*  
From [1487](g1487) and [302](g0302.md) a *conditional* particle; *in*
*case* that, *provided*, etc.; often used in connection with other
particles to denote *indefiniteness* or *uncertainty*: - before, but,
except, (and) if, (if) so, (what-, whither-) soever, though, when
(-soever), whether (or), to whom, \[who-\] so (-ever). See
[3361](g3361.md)
