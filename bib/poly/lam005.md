### Lamentations 5

[KJV](../kjv/lam005.md)

1  Remember,<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
what<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> is come<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> upon us:
consider,<sup>[5027](h5027)</sup> and behold<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> our reproach.<sup>[2781](h2781.md)</sup>

2  Our inheritance<sup>[5159](h5159.md)</sup> is
turned<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup> to strangers,<sup>[2114](h2114.md)</sup> our
houses<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> to aliens.<sup>[5237](h5237.md)</sup>

3  We are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> orphans<sup>[3490](h3490.md)</sup> and
fatherless,<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> <sup>[1](h0001.md)</sup> our
mothers<sup>[517](h0517)</sup> are as widows.<sup>[490](h0490.md)</sup>

4  We have drunken<sup>[8354](h8354.md)</sup> our
water<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> for money;<sup>[3701](h3701.md)</sup> our
wood<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> is sold<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup>
<sup>[4242](h4242.md)</sup> unto us.

5  Our necks<sup>[6677](h6677)</sup> are under<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
persecution:<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> we labor,<sup>[3021](h3021.md)</sup>
and have no rest.<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> <sup>[5117](h5117.md)</sup>

6  We have given<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup> the
hand<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> to the Egyptians,<sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup>
and to the Assyrians,<sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup> to be
satisfied<sup>[7646](h7646)</sup> with bread.<sup>[3899](h3899.md)</sup>

7  Our fathers<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> have sinned,<sup>[2398](h2398.md)</sup>
and are not;<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> and we<sup>[587](h0587.md)</sup> have
borne<sup>[5445](h5445)</sup> their iniquities.<sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup>

8  Servants<sup>[5650](h5650.md)</sup> have ruled
over<sup>[4910](h4910)</sup> us: there is none<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>
that doth deliver<sup>[6561](h6561.md)</sup> us out of their
hand.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

9  We got<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> our bread<sup>[3899](h3899.md)</sup> with
the peril of our lives<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> because
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> the
sword<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> of the wilderness.<sup>[4057](h4057.md)</sup>

10  Our skin<sup>[5785](h5785)</sup> was black<sup>[3648](h3648.md)</sup>
like an oven<sup>[8574](h8574)</sup> because of<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the terrible<sup>[2152](h2152.md)</sup>
famine.<sup>[7458](h7458.md)</sup>

11  They ravished<sup>[6031](h6031.md)</sup> the
women<sup>[802](h0802)</sup> in Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup> and the
maids<sup>[1330](h1330)</sup> in the cities<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup> of
Judah.<sup>[3063](h3063.md)</sup>

12  Princes<sup>[8269](h8269.md)</sup> are hanged
up<sup>[8518](h8518)</sup> by their hand:<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> the
faces<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of elders<sup>[2205](h2205.md)</sup> were
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> honored.<sup>[1921](h1921.md)</sup>

13  They took<sup>[5375](h5375.md)</sup> the young
men<sup>[970](h0970)</sup> to grind,<sup>[2911](h2911.md)</sup> and the
children<sup>[5288](h5288)</sup> fell<sup>[3782](h3782.md)</sup> under the
wood.<sup>[6086](h6086.md)</sup>

14  The elders<sup>[2205](h2205.md)</sup> have
ceased<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> from the gate,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[8179](h8179)</sup> the young men<sup>[970](h0970.md)</sup> from their
music.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5058](h5058.md)</sup>

15  The joy<sup>[4885](h4885)</sup> of our heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>
is ceased;<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> our dance<sup>[4234](h4234.md)</sup> is
turned<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup> into mourning.<sup>[60](h0060.md)</sup>

16  The crown<sup>[5850](h5850)</sup> is fallen<sup>[5307](h5307.md)</sup>
from our head:<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> woe<sup>[188](h0188.md)</sup> unto
us, that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> we have sinned!<sup>[2398](h2398.md)</sup>

17  For<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> this<sup>[2088](h2088.md)</sup> our
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> is<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
faint;<sup>[1739](h1739)</sup> for<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
these<sup>[428](h0428)</sup> things our eyes<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> are
dim.<sup>[2821](h2821.md)</sup>

18  Because of<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
mountain<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> of Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>
which<sup>[7945](h7945)</sup> is desolate,<sup>[8074](h8074.md)</sup> the
foxes<sup>[7776](h7776)</sup> walk<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> upon it.

19  Thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
remainest<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> forever;<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup> thy
throne<sup>[3678](h3678)</sup> from generation<sup>[1755](h1755.md)</sup>
to generation.<sup>[1755](h1755.md)</sup>

20  Wherefore<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> dost thou
forget<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> us forever,<sup>[5331](h5331.md)</sup> and
forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> us so long<sup>[753](h0753.md)</sup>
time?<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>

21  Turn<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> thou us unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>
thee, O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> and we shall be
turned;<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> renew<sup>[2318](h2318.md)</sup> our
days<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> as of old.<sup>[6924](h6924.md)</sup>

22  But<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> <sup>[518](h0518.md)</sup> thou hast
utterly rejected<sup>[3988](h3988)</sup> <sup>[3988](h3988.md)</sup> us;
thou art very wroth<sup>[7107](h7107)</sup> <sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup>
<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> us.

[Sep](../sep/lam005.md)

1 μνήσθητι κύριε ὅ τι ἐγενήθη ἡμῖν ἐπίβλεψον καὶ ἰδὲ τὸν ὀνειδισμὸν ἡμῶν

2 κληρονομία ἡμῶν μετεστράφη ἀλλοτρίοις οἱ οἶκοι ἡμῶν ξένοις

3 ὀρφανοὶ ἐγενήθημεν οὐχ ὑπάρχει πατήρ μητέρες ἡμῶν ὡς αἱ χῆραι

4 ἐξ ἡμερῶν ἡμῶν ξύλα ἡμῶν ἐν ἀλλάγματι ἦλθεν

5 ἐπὶ τὸν τράχηλον ἡμῶν ἐδιώχθημεν ἐκοπιάσαμεν οὐκ ἀνεπαύθημεν

6 Αἴγυπτος ἔδωκεν χεῖρα Ασσουρ εἰς πλησμονὴν αὐτῶν

7 οἱ πατέρες ἡμῶν ἥμαρτον οὐχ ὑπάρχουσιν ἡμεῖς τὰ ἀνομήματα αὐτῶν
ὑπέσχομεν

8 δοῦλοι ἐκυρίευσαν ἡμῶν λυτρούμενος οὐκ ἔστιν ἐκ τῆς χειρὸς αὐτῶν

9 ἐν ταῖς ψυχαῖς ἡμῶν εἰσοίσομεν ἄρτον ἡμῶν ἀπὸ προσώπου ῥομφαίας τῆς
ἐρήμου

10 τὸ δέρμα ἡμῶν ὡς κλίβανος ἐπελειώθη συνεσπάσθησαν ἀπὸ προσώπου
καταιγίδων λιμοῦ

11 γυναῖκας ἐν Σιων ἐταπείνωσαν παρθένους ἐν πόλεσιν Ιουδα

12 ἄρχοντες ἐν χερσὶν αὐτῶν ἐκρεμάσθησαν πρεσβύτεροι οὐκ ἐδοξάσθησαν

13 ἐκλεκτοὶ κλαυθμὸν ἀνέλαβον καὶ νεανίσκοι ἐν ξύλῳ ἠσθένησαν

14 καὶ πρεσβῦται ἀπὸ πύλης κατέπαυσαν ἐκλεκτοὶ ἐκ ψαλμῶν αὐτῶν
κατέπαυσαν

15 κατέλυσεν χαρὰ καρδίας ἡμῶν ἐστράφη εἰς πένθος ὁ χορὸς ἡμῶν

16 ἔπεσεν ὁ στέφανος τῆς κεφαλῆς ἡμῶν οὐαὶ δὴ ἡμῖν ὅτι ἡμάρτομεν

17 περὶ τούτου ἐγενήθη ὀδυνηρὰ ἡ καρδία ἡμῶν περὶ τούτου ἐσκότασαν οἱ
ὀφθαλμοὶ ἡμῶν

18 ἐπ᾽ ὄρος Σιων ὅτι ἠφανίσθη ἀλώπεκες διῆλθον ἐν αὐτῇ

19 σὺ δέ κύριε εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα κατοικήσεις ὁ θρόνος σου εἰς γενεὰν καὶ
γενεάν

20 ἵνα τί εἰς νεῖκος ἐπιλήσῃ ἡμῶν καταλείψεις ἡμᾶς εἰς μακρότητα ἡμερῶν

21 ἐπίστρεψον ἡμᾶς κύριε πρὸς σέ καὶ ἐπιστραφησόμεθα καὶ ἀνακαίνισον
ἡμέρας ἡμῶν καθὼς ἔμπροσθεν

22 ὅτι ἀπωθούμενος ἀπώσω ἡμᾶς ὠργίσθης ἐφ᾽ ἡμᾶς ἕως σφόδρα

[Vul](../vul/lam005.md)

1 \[Recordare, Domine, quid acciderit nobis;  
intuere et respice opprobrium nostrum.  

2 Hæreditas nostra versa est ad alienos,  
domus nostræ ad extraneos.  

3 Pupilli facti sumus absque patre,  
matres nostræ quasi viduæ.  

4 Aquam nostram pecunia bibimus;  
ligna nostra pretio comparavimus.  

5 Cervicibus nostris minabamur,  
lassis non dabatur requies.  

6 Ægypto dedimus manum et Assyriis,  
ut saturaremur pane.  

7 Patres nostri peccaverunt, et non sunt:  
et nos iniquitates eorum portavimus.  

8 Servi dominati sunt nostri:  
non fuit qui redimeret de manu eorum.  

9 In animabus nostris afferebamus panem nobis,  
a facie gladii in deserto.  

10 Pellis nostra quasi clibanus exusta est,  
a facie tempestatum famis.  

11 Mulieres in Sion humiliaverunt,  
et virgines in civitatibus Juda.  

12 Principes manu suspensi sunt;  
facies senum non erubuerunt.  

13 Adolescentibus impudice abusi sunt,  
et pueri in ligno corruerunt.  

14 Senes defecerunt de portis,  
juvenes de choro psallentium.  

15 Defecit gaudium cordis nostri;  
versus est in luctum chorus noster.  

16 Cecidit corona capitis nostri:  
væ nobis, quia peccavimus!  

17 Propterea mœstum factum est cor nostrum;  
ideo contenebrati sunt oculi nostri,  

18 propter montem Sion quia disperiit;  
vulpes ambulaverunt in eo.  

19 Tu autem, Domine, in æternum permanebis,  
solium tuum in generationem et generationem.  

20 Quare in perpetuum oblivisceris nostri,  
derelinques nos in longitudine dierum?  

21 Converte nos, Domine, ad te, et convertemur;  
innova dies nostros, sicut a principio.  

22 Sed projiciens repulisti nos:  
iratus es contra nos vehementer.\]
