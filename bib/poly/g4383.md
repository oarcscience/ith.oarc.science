G4383  
πρόσωπον  
prosōpon  
*pros‘-o-pon*  
From [4314](g4314) and ὤψ ōps (the *visage*; from [3700](g3700) ; the
*front* (as being *towards* *view*), that is, the *countenance*,
*aspect*, *appearance*, *surface*; by implication *presence*, *person:*
- (outward) appearance, X before, countenance, face, fashion, (men’s)
person, presence.
