H2896  
טוב  
טּוֹב ‎ ṭôb  
*tobe*  
From [2895](h2895) *good* (as an adjective.md) in the widest sense; used
likewise as a noun, both in the masculine and the feminine, the singular
and the plural (*good*, a *good* or *good* thing, a *good* man or woman;
the *good*, *goods* or *good* things, *good* men or women), also as an
adverb (*well*): - beautiful, best, better, bountiful, cheerful, at
ease, X fair (word), (be in) favour, fine, glad, good (deed, -lier,
liest, -ly, -ness, -s), graciously, joyful, kindly, kindness, liketh
(best), loving, merry, X most, pleasant, + pleaseth, pleasure, precious,
prosperity, ready, sweet, wealth, welfare, (be) well (\[-favoured\]).
