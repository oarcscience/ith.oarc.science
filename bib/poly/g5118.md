G5118  
τοσοῦτος  
tosoutos  
*tos-oo‘-tos*  
From τόσος tosos (*so* *much*; apparently from [3588](g3588) and
[3739](g3739) and [3778](g3778) (including its variations); so *vast*
*as* *this*, that is, *such* (in quantity, amount, number or space): -
as large, so great (long, many, much), these many.
