### Psalms 50

[KJV](../kjv/psa050)

1  A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of Asaph.<sup>[623](h0623)</sup> The
mighty<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> even the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath spoken,<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> and
called<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> from the
rising<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4217](h4217)</sup> of the
sun<sup>[8121](h8121)</sup> unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the going
down<sup>[3996](h3996)</sup> thereof.

2  Out of Zion,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> the
perfection<sup>[4359](h4359)</sup> of beauty,<sup>[3308](h3308)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> hath shined.<sup>[3313](h3313)</sup>

3  Our God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> shall come,<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> and
shall not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> keep silence:<sup>[2790](h2790)</sup> a
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> shall devour<sup>[398](h0398)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> him, and it shall be
very<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> tempestuous<sup>[8175](h8175)</sup> round
about<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> him.

4  He shall call<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
heavens<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> from above,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5920](h5920)</sup> and to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
earth,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> that he may judge<sup>[1777](h1777)</sup>
his people.<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup>

5  Gather my saints together<sup>[622](h0622)</sup>
<sup>[2623](h2623)</sup> unto me; those that have
made<sup>[3772](h3772)</sup> a covenant<sup>[1285](h1285)</sup> with me
by<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> sacrifice.<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup>

6  And the heavens<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> shall
declare<sup>[5046](h5046)</sup> his
righteousness:<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> is judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup>
himself.<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

7  Hear,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> O my people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup>
and I will speak;<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> O
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> and I will
testify<sup>[5749](h5749)</sup> against thee: I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup>
am God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> even thy God.<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>

8  I will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> reprove<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup>
thee for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy sacrifices<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup>
or thy burnt offerings,<sup>[5930](h5930)</sup> to have been
continually<sup>[8548](h8548)</sup> before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> me.

9  I will take<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
bullock<sup>[6499](h6499)</sup> out of thy
house,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> nor he
goats<sup>[6260](h6260)</sup> out of thy folds.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4356](h4356)</sup>

10  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> every<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
beast<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> of the forest<sup>[3293](h3293)</sup> is
mine, and the cattle<sup>[929](h0929)</sup> upon a
thousand<sup>[505](h0505)</sup> hills.<sup>[2042](h2042)</sup>

11  I know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the
fowls<sup>[5775](h5775)</sup> of the mountains:<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup>
and the wild beasts<sup>[2123](h2123)</sup> of the
field<sup>[7704](h7704)</sup> are mine.<sup>[5978](h5978)</sup>

12  If<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> I were hungry,<sup>[7456](h7456)</sup> I
would not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> tell<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> thee:
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the world<sup>[8398](h8398)</sup> is mine,
and the fullness<sup>[4393](h4393)</sup> thereof.

13  Will I eat<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> the flesh<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup>
of bulls,<sup>[47](h0047)</sup> or drink<sup>[8354](h8354)</sup> the
blood<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> of goats?<sup>[6260](h6260)</sup>

14  Offer<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> unto God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>
thanksgiving;<sup>[8426](h8426)</sup> and pay<sup>[7999](h7999)</sup>
thy vows<sup>[5088](h5088)</sup> unto the most
High:<sup>[5945](h5945)</sup>

15  And call upon<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> me in the
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of trouble:<sup>[6869](h6869)</sup> I will
deliver<sup>[2502](h2502)</sup> thee, and thou shalt
glorify<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> me.

16  But unto the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> saith,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>
What<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> hast thou to do to
declare<sup>[5608](h5608)</sup> my statutes,<sup>[2706](h2706)</sup> or
that thou shouldest take<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> my
covenant<sup>[1285](h1285)</sup> in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy
mouth?<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>

17  Seeing thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> hatest<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup>
instruction,<sup>[4148](h4148)</sup> and castest<sup>[7993](h7993)</sup>
my words<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> behind<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> thee.

18  When<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> thou sawest<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> a
thief,<sup>[1590](h1590)</sup> then thou
consentedst<sup>[7521](h7521)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> him,
and hast been partaker<sup>[2506](h2506)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> adulterers.<sup>[5003](h5003)</sup>

19  Thou givest<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> thy
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> to evil,<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> and thy
tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> frameth<sup>[6775](h6775)</sup>
deceit.<sup>[4820](h4820)</sup>

20  Thou sittest<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> and
speakest<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> against thy
brother;<sup>[251](h0251)</sup> thou slanderest<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
<sup>[1848](h1848)</sup> thine own mother's<sup>[517](h0517)</sup>
son.<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup>

21  These<sup>[428](h0428)</sup> things hast thou
done,<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> and I kept
silence;<sup>[2790](h2790)</sup> thou thoughtest<sup>[1819](h1819)</sup>
that I was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> altogether<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
such a one as thyself:<sup>[3644](h3644)</sup> but I will
reprove<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup> thee, and set them in
order<sup>[6186](h6186)</sup> before thine eyes.<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup>

22  Now<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> consider<sup>[995](h0995)</sup>
this,<sup>[2063](h2063)</sup> ye that forget<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup>
God,<sup>[433](h0433)</sup> lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> I tear you in
pieces,<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> and there be none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup>
to deliver.<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup>

23  Whoso offereth<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup>
praise<sup>[8426](h8426)</sup> glorifieth<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> me:
and to him that ordereth<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> his
conversation<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> aright will I
show<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> the salvation<sup>[3468](h3468)</sup> of
God.<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa050)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 ἐν τῷ ἐλθεῖν πρὸς αὐτὸν Ναθαν τὸν προφήτην ἡνίκα εἰσῆλθεν πρὸς
Βηρσαβεε

3 ἐλέησόν με ὁ θεός κατὰ τὸ μέγα ἔλεός σου καὶ κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος τῶν
οἰκτιρμῶν σου ἐξάλειψον τὸ ἀνόμημά μου

4 ἐπὶ πλεῖον πλῦνόν με ἀπὸ τῆς ἀνομίας μου καὶ ἀπὸ τῆς ἁμαρτίας μου
καθάρισόν με

5 ὅτι τὴν ἀνομίαν μου ἐγὼ γινώσκω καὶ ἡ ἁμαρτία μου ἐνώπιόν μού ἐστιν
διὰ παντός

6 σοὶ μόνῳ ἥμαρτον καὶ τὸ πονηρὸν ἐνώπιόν σου ἐποίησα ὅπως ἂν δικαιωθῇς
ἐν τοῖς λόγοις σου καὶ νικήσῃς ἐν τῷ κρίνεσθαί σε

7 ἰδοὺ γὰρ ἐν ἀνομίαις συνελήμφθην καὶ ἐν ἁμαρτίαις ἐκίσσησέν με ἡ μήτηρ
μου

8 ἰδοὺ γὰρ ἀλήθειαν ἠγάπησας τὰ ἄδηλα καὶ τὰ κρύφια τῆς σοφίας σου
ἐδήλωσάς μοι

9 ῥαντιεῖς με ὑσσώπῳ καὶ καθαρισθήσομαι πλυνεῖς με καὶ ὑπὲρ χιόνα
λευκανθήσομαι

10 ἀκουτιεῖς με ἀγαλλίασιν καὶ εὐφροσύνην ἀγαλλιάσονται ὀστᾶ
τεταπεινωμένα

11 ἀπόστρεψον τὸ πρόσωπόν σου ἀπὸ τῶν ἁμαρτιῶν μου καὶ πάσας τὰς ἀνομίας
μου ἐξάλειψον

12 καρδίαν καθαρὰν κτίσον ἐν ἐμοί ὁ θεός καὶ πνεῦμα εὐθὲς ἐγκαίνισον ἐν
τοῖς ἐγκάτοις μου

13 μὴ ἀπορρίψῃς με ἀπὸ τοῦ προσώπου σου καὶ τὸ πνεῦμα τὸ ἅγιόν σου μὴ
ἀντανέλῃς ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ

14 ἀπόδος μοι τὴν ἀγαλλίασιν τοῦ σωτηρίου σου καὶ πνεύματι ἡγεμονικῷ
στήρισόν με

15 διδάξω ἀνόμους τὰς ὁδούς σου καὶ ἀσεβεῖς ἐπὶ σὲ ἐπιστρέψουσιν

16 ῥῦσαί με ἐξ αἱμάτων ὁ θεὸς ὁ θεὸς τῆς σωτηρίας μου ἀγαλλιάσεται ἡ
γλῶσσά μου τὴν δικαιοσύνην σου

17 κύριε τὰ χείλη μου ἀνοίξεις καὶ τὸ στόμα μου ἀναγγελεῖ τὴν αἴνεσίν
σου

18 ὅτι εἰ ἠθέλησας θυσίαν ἔδωκα ἄν ὁλοκαυτώματα οὐκ εὐδοκήσεις

19 θυσία τῷ θεῷ πνεῦμα συντετριμμένον καρδίαν συντετριμμένην καὶ
τεταπεινωμένην ὁ θεὸς οὐκ ἐξουθενώσει

20 ἀγάθυνον κύριε ἐν τῇ εὐδοκίᾳ σου τὴν Σιων καὶ οἰκοδομηθήτω τὰ τείχη
Ιερουσαλημ

21 τότε εὐδοκήσεις θυσίαν δικαιοσύνης ἀναφορὰν καὶ ὁλοκαυτώματα τότε
ἀνοίσουσιν ἐπὶ τὸ θυσιαστήριόν σου μόσχους

[Vul](../vul/psa050)

1 In finem. Psalmus David,

2 cum venit ad eum Nathan propheta, quando intravit ad Bethsabee.

3 \[Miserere mei, Deus, secundum magnam misericordiam tuam;  
et secundum multitudinem miserationum tuarum, dele iniquitatem meam.  

4 Amplius lava me ab iniquitate mea,  
et a peccato meo munda me.  

5 Quoniam iniquitatem meam ego cognosco,  
et peccatum meum contra me est semper.  

6 Tibi soli peccavi, et malum coram te feci;  
ut justificeris in sermonibus tuis,  
et vincas cum judicaris.  

7 Ecce enim in iniquitatibus conceptus sum,  
et in peccatis concepit me mater mea.  

8 Ecce enim veritatem dilexisti;  
incerta et occulta sapientiæ tuæ manifestasti mihi.  

9 Asperges me hyssopo, et mundabor;  
lavabis me, et super nivem dealbabor.  

10 Auditui meo dabis gaudium et lætitiam,  
et exsultabunt ossa humiliata.  

11 Averte faciem tuam a peccatis meis,  
et omnes iniquitates meas dele.  

12 Cor mundum crea in me, Deus,  
et spiritum rectum innova in visceribus meis.  

13 Ne projicias me a facie tua,  
et spiritum sanctum tuum ne auferas a me.  

14 Redde mihi lætitiam salutaris tui,  
et spiritu principali confirma me.  

15 Docebo iniquos vias tuas,  
et impii ad te convertentur.  

16 Libera me de sanguinibus, Deus, Deus salutis meæ,  
et exsultabit lingua mea justitiam tuam.  

17 Domine, labia mea aperies,  
et os meum annuntiabit laudem tuam.  

18 Quoniam si voluisses sacrificium, dedissem utique;  
holocaustis non delectaberis.  

19 Sacrificium Deo spiritus contribulatus;  
cor contritum et humiliatum, Deus, non despicies.  

20 Benigne fac, Domine, in bona voluntate tua Sion,  
ut ædificentur muri Jerusalem.  

21 Tunc acceptabis sacrificium justitiæ, oblationes et holocausta;  
tunc imponent super altare tuum vitulos.\]
