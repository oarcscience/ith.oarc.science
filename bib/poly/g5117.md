G5117  
τόπος  
topos  
*top‘-os*  
Apparently a primary word; a *spot* (generally in *space*, but limited
by occupancy; whereas [5561](g5561) is a larger but particular
*locality*), that is, *location* (as a position, home, tract, etc.);
figuratively *condition*, *opportunity*; specifically a *scabbard:* -
coast, licence, place, X plain, quarter, + rock, room, where.
