H4932  
משׁנה  
מִשׁנֶה ‎ mishneh  
*mish-neh‘*  
From [8138](h8138) properly a *repetition*, that is, a *duplicate*
(*copy* of a document), or a *double* (in amount); by implication a
*second* (in order, rank, age, quality or location): - college, copy,
double, fatlings, next, second (order), twice as much.
