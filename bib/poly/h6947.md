H6947  
קדשׁ בּרנע  
קָדֵשׁ בַּרנֵַע ‎ qâdêsh barnêa‛  
*kaw-dashe‘* *bar-nay‘-ah*  
From the same as [6946](h6946.md) and an otherwise unused word (apparently
compounded of a correspondent to [1251](h1251.md) and a derivative of
[5128](h5128) meaning *desert* *of* *a* *fugitive*; *Kadesh* *of* (the.md)
*Wilderness* *of* *Wandering*; *Kadesh-Barnea*, a place in the Desert: -
Kadesh-barnea.
