H3722  
כּפר  
כָּפַר ‎ kâphar  
*kaw-far‘*  
A primitive root; to *cover* (specifically with bitumen); figuratively
to *expiate* or *condone*, to *placate* or *cancel: -* appease, make
(an) atonement, cleanse, disannul, forgive, be merciful, pacify, pardon,
to pitch, purge (away), put off, (make) reconcile (-liation).
