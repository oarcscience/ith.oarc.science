### Psalms 141

[KJV](../kjv/psa141)

1  A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup>
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> I cry<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto thee:
make haste<sup>[2363](h2363)</sup> unto me; give
ear<sup>[238](h0238)</sup> unto my voice,<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> when I
cry<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto thee.

2  Let my prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> be set
forth<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> thee as
incense;<sup>[7004](h7004)</sup> and the lifting
up<sup>[4864](h4864)</sup> of my hands<sup>[3709](h3709)</sup> as the
evening<sup>[6153](h6153)</sup> sacrifice.<sup>[4503](h4503)</sup>

3  Set<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> a watch,<sup>[8108](h8108)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> before my mouth;<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>
keep<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
door<sup>[1817](h1817)</sup> of my lips.<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup>

4  Incline<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> my
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> to any evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>
thing,<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> to practice<sup>[5953](h5953)</sup>
wicked<sup>[7562](h7562)</sup> works<sup>[5949](h5949)</sup>
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> men<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> that
work<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> iniquity:<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> and let me
not<sup>[1077](h1077)</sup> eat<sup>[3898](h3898)</sup> of their
dainties.<sup>[4516](h4516)</sup>

5  Let the righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup>
smite<sup>[1986](h1986)</sup> me; it shall be a
kindness:<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> and let him
reprove<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup> me; it shall be an
excellent<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> oil,<sup>[8081](h8081)</sup> which
shall not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> break<sup>[5106](h5106)</sup> my
head:<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
yet<sup>[5750](h5750)</sup> my prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> also shall
be in their calamities.<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>

6  When their judges<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> are
overthrown<sup>[8058](h8058)</sup> in stony<sup>[5553](h5553)</sup>
places,<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> they shall hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup>
my words;<sup>[561](h0561)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they are
sweet.<sup>[5276](h5276)</sup>

7  Our bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> are
scattered<sup>[6340](h6340)</sup> at the grave's<sup>[7585](h7585)</sup>
mouth,<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> as when<sup>[3644](h3644)</sup> one
cutteth<sup>[6398](h6398)</sup> and cleaveth<sup>[1234](h1234)</sup>
wood upon the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

8  But<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> mine eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> are
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> thee, O GOD<sup>[3069](h3069)</sup> the
Lord:<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> in thee is my
trust;<sup>[2620](h2620)</sup> leave not my soul
destitute.<sup>[6168](h6168)</sup> <sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup>

9  Keep<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> me from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> the snares<sup>[6341](h6341)</sup> which they
have laid<sup>[3369](h3369)</sup> for me, and the
gins<sup>[4170](h4170)</sup> of the workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of
iniquity.<sup>[205](h0205)</sup>

10  Let the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> fall<sup>[5307](h5307)</sup>
into their own nets,<sup>[4364](h4364)</sup>
whilst<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> that I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup>
withal<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> escape.<sup>[5674](h5674)</sup>

1 συνέσεως τῷ Δαυιδ ἐν τῷ εἶναι αὐτὸν ἐν τῷ σπηλαίῳ προσευχή

2 φωνῇ μου πρὸς κύριον ἐκέκραξα φωνῇ μου πρὸς κύριον ἐδεήθην

3 ἐκχεῶ ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ τὴν δέησίν μου τὴν θλῖψίν μου ἐνώπιον αὐτοῦ
ἀπαγγελῶ

4 ἐν τῷ ἐκλείπειν ἐξ ἐμοῦ τὸ πνεῦμά μου καὶ σὺ ἔγνως τὰς τρίβους μου ἐν
ὁδῷ ταύτῃ ᾗ ἐπορευόμην ἔκρυψαν παγίδα μοι

5 κατενόουν εἰς τὰ δεξιὰ καὶ ἐπέβλεπον ὅτι οὐκ ἦν ὁ ἐπιγινώσκων με
ἀπώλετο φυγὴ ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ὁ ἐκζητῶν τὴν ψυχήν μου

6 ἐκέκραξα πρὸς σέ κύριε εἶπα σὺ εἶ ἡ ἐλπίς μου μερίς μου ἐν γῇ ζώντων

7 πρόσχες πρὸς τὴν δέησίν μου ὅτι ἐταπεινώθην σφόδρα ῥῦσαί με ἐκ τῶν
καταδιωκόντων με ὅτι ἐκραταιώθησαν ὑπὲρ ἐμέ

8 ἐξάγαγε ἐκ φυλακῆς τὴν ψυχήν μου τοῦ ἐξομολογήσασθαι τῷ ὀνόματί σου
κύριε ἐμὲ ὑπομενοῦσιν δίκαιοι ἕως οὗ ἀνταποδῷς μοι

[Vul](../vul/psa141)

1 Intellectus David, cum esset in spelunca, oratio.

2 \[Voce mea ad Dominum clamavi,  
voce mea ad Dominum deprecatus sum.  

3 Effundo in conspectu ejus orationem meam,  
et tribulationem meam ante ipsum pronuntio:  

4 in deficiendo ex me spiritum meum,  
et tu cognovisti semitas meas.  
In via hac qua ambulabam  
absconderunt laqueum mihi.  

5 Considerabam ad dexteram, et videbam,  
et non erat qui cognosceret me:  
periit fuga a me,  
et non est qui requirat animam meam.  

6 Clamavi ad te, Domine;  
dixi: Tu es spes mea,  
portio mea in terra viventium.  

7 Intende ad deprecationem meam,  
quia humiliatus sum nimis.  
Libera me a persequentibus me,  
quia confortati sunt super me.  

8 Educ de custodia animam meam  
ad confitendum nomini tuo;  
me exspectant justi donec retribuas mihi.\]
