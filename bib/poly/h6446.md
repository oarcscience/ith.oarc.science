H6446  
פּס  
פַּס ‎ pas  
*pas*  
From [6461](h6461) properly the *palm* (of the hand.md) or *sole* (of the
foot), (compare [6447](h6447) ; by implication (plural.md) a *long* *and*
*sleeved* tunic (perhaps simply a *wide* one; from the original sense of
the root, that is, of *many* *breadths*): - (divers) colours.
