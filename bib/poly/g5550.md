G5550  
χρόνος  
chronos  
*khron‘-os*  
Of uncertain derivation; a space of *time* (in genitive case, and thus
properly distinguished from [2540](g2540.md) which designates a *fixed* or
special occasion; and from [165](g0165.md) which denotes a particular
*period*) or *interval*; by extension an individual *opportunity*; by
implication *delay:* - + years old, season, space, (X often-) time (-s),
(a) while.
