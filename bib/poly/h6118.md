H6118  
עקב  
עֵקֶב ‎ ‛êqeb  
*ay‘-keb*  
From [6117](h6117) in the sense of [6119](h6119) a *heel*, that is,
(figuratively) the *last* of anything (used adverbially *for* *ever*);
also *result*, that is, *compensation*; and so (adverbially with
preposition or relatively) on *account* of: - X because, by, end, for,
if, reward.
