H5375  
נסה נשׂא  
נָשָׂא נָסָה ‎ nâśâ‘ nâsâh  
*naw-saw‘,* *naw-saw‘*  
A primitive root; to *lift*, in a great variety of applications,
literally and figuratively, absolutely and relatively: - accept,
advance, arise, (able to, \[armour\], suffer to) bear (-er, up), bring
(forth), burn, carry (away), cast, contain, desire, ease, exact, exalt
(self), extol, fetch, forgive, furnish, further, give, go on, help,
high, hold up, honourable (+ man), lade, lay, lift (self) up, lofty,
marry, magnify, X needs, obtain, pardon, raise (up), receive, regard,
respect, set (up), spare, stir up, + swear, take (away, up), X utterly,
wear, yield.
