H3782  
כּשׁל  
כָּשַׁל ‎ kâshal  
*kaw-shal‘*  
A primitive root; to *totter* or *waver* (through weakness of the legs,
especially the ankle); by implication to *falter*, *stumble*, faint or
fall: - bereave \[from the margin\], cast down, be decayed, (cause to)
fail, (cause, make to) fall (down, -ing), feeble, be (the) ruin (-ed,
of), (be) overthrown, (cause to) stumble, X utterly, be weak.
