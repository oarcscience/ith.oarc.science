### Isaiah 16

[KJV](../kjv/isa016.md)

1  Send<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> ye the lamb<sup>[3733](h3733.md)</sup> to
the ruler<sup>[4910](h4910)</sup> of the land<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
from Sela<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5554](h5554.md)</sup> to the
wilderness,<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
mount<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> of the daughter<sup>[1323](h1323.md)</sup> of
Zion.<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>

2  For it shall be,<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> that, as a
wandering<sup>[5074](h5074)</sup> bird<sup>[5775](h5775.md)</sup> cast
out<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> of the nest,<sup>[7064](h7064.md)</sup> so the
daughters<sup>[1323](h1323)</sup> of Moab<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup> shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> at the fords<sup>[4569](h4569.md)</sup> of
Arnon.<sup>[769](h0769.md)</sup>

3  Take<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> counsel,<sup>[6098](h6098.md)</sup>
execute<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> judgment;<sup>[6415](h6415.md)</sup>
make<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> thy shadow<sup>[6738](h6738.md)</sup> as the
night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> in the midst<sup>[8432](h8432.md)</sup> of
the noonday;<sup>[6672](h6672)</sup> hide<sup>[5641](h5641.md)</sup> the
outcasts;<sup>[5080](h5080)</sup> betray<sup>[1540](h1540.md)</sup>
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> him that wandereth.<sup>[5074](h5074.md)</sup>

4  Let mine outcasts<sup>[5080](h5080.md)</sup>
dwell<sup>[1481](h1481)</sup> with thee, Moab;<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup>
be<sup>[1933](h1933)</sup> thou a covert<sup>[5643](h5643.md)</sup> to them
from the face<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> of the
spoiler:<sup>[7703](h7703)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
extortioner<sup>[4160](h4160)</sup> is at an end,<sup>[656](h0656.md)</sup>
the spoiler<sup>[7701](h7701)</sup> ceaseth,<sup>[3615](h3615.md)</sup> the
oppressors<sup>[7429](h7429)</sup> are consumed<sup>[8552](h8552.md)</sup>
out of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> the land.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

5  And in mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> shall the
throne<sup>[3678](h3678)</sup> be established:<sup>[3559](h3559.md)</sup>
and he shall sit<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> it
in truth<sup>[571](h0571)</sup> in the tabernacle<sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup>
of David,<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> judging,<sup>[8199](h8199.md)</sup> and
seeking<sup>[1875](h1875)</sup> judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup> and
hasting<sup>[4106](h4106)</sup> righteousness.<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup>

6  We have heard<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> of the
pride<sup>[1347](h1347)</sup> of Moab;<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup> he is
very<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> proud:<sup>[1341](h1341.md)</sup> even of his
haughtiness,<sup>[1346](h1346.md)</sup> and his
pride,<sup>[1347](h1347)</sup> and his wrath:<sup>[5678](h5678.md)</sup>
but his lies<sup>[907](h0907)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> be
so.<sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup>

7  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> shall Moab<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup>
howl<sup>[3213](h3213)</sup> for Moab,<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup> every
one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> shall howl:<sup>[3213](h3213.md)</sup> for the
foundations<sup>[808](h0808.md)</sup> of
Kir-hareseth<sup>[7025](h7025.md)</sup> shall ye
mourn;<sup>[1897](h1897)</sup> surely<sup>[389](h0389.md)</sup> they are
stricken.<sup>[5218](h5218.md)</sup>

8  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the fields<sup>[7709](h7709.md)</sup> of
Heshbon<sup>[2809](h2809)</sup> languish,<sup>[535](h0535.md)</sup> and the
vine<sup>[1612](h1612)</sup> of Sibmah:<sup>[7643](h7643.md)</sup> the
lords<sup>[1167](h1167)</sup> of the heathen<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup>
have broken down<sup>[1986](h1986.md)</sup> the principal
plants<sup>[8291](h8291.md)</sup> thereof, they are
come<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> even unto<sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup>
Jazer,<sup>[3270](h3270)</sup> they wandered<sup>[8582](h8582.md)</sup>
through the wilderness:<sup>[4057](h4057.md)</sup> her
branches<sup>[7976](h7976.md)</sup> are stretched
out,<sup>[5203](h5203)</sup> they are gone over<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup>
the sea.<sup>[3220](h3220.md)</sup>

9  Therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> I will
bewail<sup>[1058](h1058)</sup> with the weeping<sup>[1065](h1065.md)</sup>
of Jazer<sup>[3270](h3270)</sup> the vine<sup>[1612](h1612.md)</sup> of
Sibmah:<sup>[7643](h7643)</sup> I will water<sup>[7301](h7301.md)</sup>
thee with my tears,<sup>[1832](h1832.md)</sup> O
Heshbon,<sup>[2809](h2809)</sup> and Elealeh:<sup>[500](h0500.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the shouting<sup>[1959](h1959.md)</sup>
for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy summer fruits<sup>[7019](h7019.md)</sup>
and for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy harvest<sup>[7105](h7105.md)</sup> is
fallen.<sup>[5307](h5307.md)</sup>

10  And gladness<sup>[8057](h8057.md)</sup> is taken
away,<sup>[622](h0622)</sup> and joy<sup>[1524](h1524.md)</sup> out
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> the plentiful field;<sup>[3759](h3759.md)</sup>
and in the vineyards<sup>[3754](h3754.md)</sup> there shall be
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> singing,<sup>[7442](h7442.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> shall there be
shouting:<sup>[7321](h7321)</sup> the treaders<sup>[1869](h1869.md)</sup>
shall tread<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> out no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
wine<sup>[3196](h3196)</sup> in their presses;<sup>[3342](h3342.md)</sup> I
have made their vintage shouting<sup>[1959](h1959.md)</sup> to
cease.<sup>[7673](h7673.md)</sup>

11  Wherefore<sup>[921](h0921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> my
bowels<sup>[4578](h4578)</sup> shall sound<sup>[1993](h1993.md)</sup> like
a harp<sup>[3658](h3658)</sup> for Moab,<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup> and
mine inward parts<sup>[7130](h7130.md)</sup> for
Kir-haresh.<sup>[7025](h7025.md)</sup>

12  And it shall come to pass,<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> it is seen<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> Moab<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup> is
weary<sup>[3811](h3811)</sup> on<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the high
place,<sup>[1116](h1116)</sup> that he shall come<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> his sanctuary<sup>[4720](h4720.md)</sup> to
pray;<sup>[6419](h6419)</sup> but he shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
prevail.<sup>[3201](h3201.md)</sup>

13  This<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> is the word<sup>[1697](h1697.md)</sup>
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> hath
spoken<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> concerning<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>
Moab<sup>[4124](h4124)</sup> since that time.<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[227](h0227.md)</sup>

14  But now<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
hath spoken,<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> saying,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup>
Within three<sup>[7969](h7969)</sup> years,<sup>[8141](h8141.md)</sup> as
the years<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> of a hireling,<sup>[7916](h7916.md)</sup>
and the glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> of Moab<sup>[4124](h4124.md)</sup>
shall be contemned,<sup>[7034](h7034.md)</sup> with
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that great<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup>
multitude;<sup>[1995](h1995.md)</sup> and the
remnant<sup>[7605](h7605.md)</sup> shall be very
small<sup>[4592](h4592)</sup> <sup>[4213](h4213.md)</sup> and

[Sep](../sep/isa016.md)

1 ἀποστελῶ ὡς ἑρπετὰ ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν μὴ πέτρα ἔρημός ἐστιν τὸ ὄρος Σιων

2 ἔσῃ γὰρ ὡς πετεινοῦ ἀνιπταμένου νεοσσὸς ἀφῃρημένος θύγατερ Μωαβ ἔπειτα
δέ Αρνων

3 πλείονα βουλεύου ποιεῖτε σκέπην πένθους αὐτῇ διὰ παντός ἐν μεσημβρινῇ
σκοτίᾳ φεύγουσιν ἐξέστησαν μὴ ἀπαχθῇς

4 παροικήσουσίν σοι οἱ φυγάδες Μωαβ ἔσονται σκέπη ὑμῖν ἀπὸ προσώπου
διώκοντος ὅτι ἤρθη ἡ συμμαχία σου καὶ ὁ ἄρχων ἀπώλετο ὁ καταπατῶν ἐπὶ
τῆς γῆς

5 καὶ διορθωθήσεται μετ᾽ ἐλέους θρόνος καὶ καθίεται ἐπ᾽ αὐτοῦ μετὰ
ἀληθείας ἐν σκηνῇ Δαυιδ κρίνων καὶ ἐκζητῶν κρίμα καὶ σπεύδων δικαιοσύνην

6 ἠκούσαμεν τὴν ὕβριν Μωαβ ὑβριστὴς σφόδρα τὴν ὑπερηφανίαν ἐξῆρας οὐχ
οὕτως ἡ μαντεία σου

7 οὐχ οὕτως ὀλολύξει Μωαβ ἐν γὰρ τῇ Μωαβίτιδι πάντες ὀλολύξουσιν τοῖς
κατοικοῦσιν Δεσεθ μελετήσεις καὶ οὐκ ἐντραπήσῃ

8 τὰ πεδία Εσεβων πενθήσει ἄμπελος Σεβαμα καταπίνοντες τὰ ἔθνη
καταπατήσατε τὰς ἀμπέλους αὐτῆς ἕως Ιαζηρ οὐ μὴ συνάψητε πλανήθητε τὴν
ἔρημον οἱ ἀπεσταλμένοι ἐγκατελείφθησαν διέβησαν γὰρ τὴν ἔρημον

9 διὰ τοῦτο κλαύσομαι ὡς τὸν κλαυθμὸν Ιαζηρ ἄμπελον Σεβαμα τὰ δένδρα σου
κατέβαλεν Εσεβων καὶ Ελεαλη ὅτι ἐπὶ τῷ θερισμῷ καὶ ἐπὶ τῷ τρυγήτῳ σου
καταπατήσω καὶ πάντα πεσοῦνται

10 καὶ ἀρθήσεται εὐφροσύνη καὶ ἀγαλλίαμα ἐκ τῶν ἀμπελώνων σου καὶ ἐν
τοῖς ἀμπελῶσίν σου οὐ μὴ εὐφρανθήσονται καὶ οὐ μὴ πατήσουσιν οἶνον εἰς
τὰ ὑπολήνια πέπαυται γάρ

11 διὰ τοῦτο ἡ κοιλία μου ἐπὶ Μωαβ ὡς κιθάρα ἠχήσει καὶ τὰ ἐντός μου
ὡσεὶ τεῖχος ὃ ἐνεκαίνισας

12 καὶ ἔσται εἰς τὸ ἐντραπῆναί σε ὅτι ἐκοπίασεν Μωαβ ἐπὶ τοῖς βωμοῖς καὶ
εἰσελεύσεται εἰς τὰ χειροποίητα αὐτῆς ὥστε προσεύξασθαι καὶ οὐ μὴ
δύνηται ἐξελέσθαι αὐτόν

13 τοῦτο τὸ ῥῆμα ὃ ἐλάλησεν κύριος ἐπὶ Μωαβ ὁπότε καὶ ἐλάλησεν

14 καὶ νῦν λέγω ἐν τρισὶν ἔτεσιν ἐτῶν μισθωτοῦ ἀτιμασθήσεται ἡ δόξα Μωαβ
ἐν παντὶ τῷ πλούτῳ τῷ πολλῷ καὶ καταλειφθήσεται ὀλιγοστὸς καὶ οὐκ
ἔντιμος

1 \[Emitte agnum, Domine, dominatorem terræ,  
de petra deserti  
ad montem filiæ Sion.  

2 Et erit: sicut avis fugiens,  
et pulli de nido avolantes,  
sic erunt filiæ Moab  
in transcensu Arnon.  

3 Ini consilium,  
coge concilium;  
pone quasi noctem umbram tuam  
in meridie;  
absconde fugientes, et vagos ne prodas.  

4 Habitabunt apud te profugi mei;  
Moab, esto latibulum eorum a facie vastatoris:  
finitus est enim pulvis, consummatus est miser,  
defecit qui conculcabat terram.  

5 Et præparabitur in misericordia solium,  
et sedebit super illud in veritate  
in tabernaculo David,  
judicans et quærens judicium,  
et velociter reddens quod justum est.  

6 Audivimus superbiam Moab:  
superbus est valde;  
superbia ejus, et arrogantia ejus, et indignatio ejus  
plus quam fortitudo ejus.  

7 Idcirco ululabit Moab ad Moab;  
universus ululabit:  
his qui lætantur super muros cocti lateris,  
loquimini plagas suas.  

8 Quoniam suburbana Hesebon deserta sunt,  
et vineam Sabama  
domini gentium exciderunt:  
flagella ejus usque ad Jazer pervenerunt,  
erraverunt in deserto;  
propagines ejus relictæ sunt,  
transierunt mare.  

9 Super hoc plorabo in fletu Jazer  
vineam Sabama;  
inebriabo de lacrima mea,  
Hesebon et Eleale,  
quoniam super vindemiam tuam et super messem tuam  
vox calcantium irruit.  

10 Et auferetur lætitia et exsultatio de Carmelo,  
et in vineis non exsultabit neque jubilabit.  
Vinum in torculari non calcabit qui calcare consueverat;  
vocem calcantium abstuli.  

11 Super hoc venter meus ad Moab  
quasi cithara sonabit,  
et viscera mea ad murum cocti lateris.  

12 Et erit: cum apparuerit quod laboravit Moab  
super excelsis suis,  
ingredietur ad sancta sua ut obsecret,  
et non valebit.\]

13 Hoc verbum quod locutus est Dominus ad Moab ex tunc.

14 Et nunc locutus est Dominus, dicens: In tribus annis, quasi anni
mercenarii, auferetur gloria Moab super omni populo multo, et
relinquetur parvus et modicus, nequaquam multus.
