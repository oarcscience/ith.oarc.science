H3808  
לה לוא לא  
לוֹא לוֹא לוֹה ‎ lô‘ lô‘ lôh  
*lo,* *lo,* *lo*  
*lo*; a primitive particle; *not* (the simple or abstract negation); by
implication *no*; often used with other particles: - X before, + or
else, ere, + except, ig \[-norant\], much, less, nay, neither, never, no
(\[-ne\], -r, \[-thing\]), (X as though . . . , \[can-\], for) not (out
of), of nought, otherwise, out of, + surely, + as truly as, + of a
truth, + verily, for want, + whether, without.
