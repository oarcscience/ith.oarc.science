H8441  
תּעבה תּועבה  
תּּוֹעֵבַה תּּוֹעֵבַה ‎ tô‛êbah tô‛êbah  
*to-ay-baw‘,* *to-ay-baw‘*  
Feminine active participle of [8581](h8581) properly something
*disgusting* (morally), that is, (as noun) an *abhorrence*; especially
*idolatry* or (concretely) an *idol: -* abominable (custom, thing),
abomination.
