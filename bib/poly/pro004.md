### Proverbs 4

[KJV](../kjv/pro004)

1  Hear,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> ye children,<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup>
the instruction<sup>[4148](h4148)</sup> of a
father,<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> and attend<sup>[7181](h7181)</sup> to
know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> understanding.<sup>[998](h0998)</sup>

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> you
good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> doctrine,<sup>[3948](h3948)</sup>
forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> ye not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> my
law.<sup>[8451](h8451)</sup>

3  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> my
father's<sup>[1](h0001)</sup> son,<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup>
tender<sup>[7390](h7390)</sup> and only<sup>[3173](h3173)</sup> beloved
in the sight<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of my
mother.<sup>[517](h0517)</sup>

4  He taught<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> me also, and
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> unto me, Let thine
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> retain<sup>[8551](h8551)</sup> my
words:<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> keep<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> my
commandments,<sup>[4687](h4687)</sup> and live.<sup>[2421](h2421)</sup>

5  Get<sup>[7069](h7069)</sup> wisdom,<sup>[2451](h2451)</sup>
get<sup>[7069](h7069)</sup> understanding:<sup>[998](h0998)</sup>
forget<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> it not;<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
neither<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> decline<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> from the
words<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[561](h0561)</sup> of my
mouth.<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>

6  Forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> her not,<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> and
she shall preserve<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> thee:
love<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> her, and she shall
keep<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> thee.

7  Wisdom<sup>[2451](h2451)</sup> is the principal
thing;<sup>[7225](h7225)</sup> therefore get<sup>[7069](h7069)</sup>
wisdom:<sup>[2451](h2451)</sup> and with all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> thy
getting<sup>[7075](h7075)</sup> get<sup>[7069](h7069)</sup>
understanding.<sup>[998](h0998)</sup>

8  Exalt<sup>[5549](h5549)</sup> her, and she shall
promote<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> thee: she shall bring thee to
honor,<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou dost
embrace<sup>[2263](h2263)</sup> her.

9  She shall give<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> to thine
head<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> an ornament<sup>[3880](h3880)</sup> of
grace:<sup>[2580](h2580)</sup> a crown<sup>[5850](h5850)</sup> of
glory<sup>[8597](h8597)</sup> shall she deliver<sup>[4042](h4042)</sup>
to thee.

10  Hear,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> O my son,<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> and
receive<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> my sayings;<sup>[561](h0561)</sup> and
the years<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> of thy life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>
shall be many.<sup>[7235](h7235)</sup>

11  I have taught<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> thee in the
way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> of wisdom;<sup>[2451](h2451)</sup> I have
led<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> thee in right<sup>[3476](h3476)</sup>
paths.<sup>[4570](h4570)</sup>

12  When thou goest,<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> thy
steps<sup>[6806](h6806)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be
straitened;<sup>[3334](h3334)</sup> and when<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> thou
runnest,<sup>[7323](h7323)</sup> thou shalt not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
stumble.<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup>

13  Take fast hold<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> of
instruction;<sup>[4148](h4148)</sup> let her not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
go:<sup>[7503](h7503)</sup> keep<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> her;
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> she<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> is thy
life.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>

14  Enter<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> into the
path<sup>[734](h0734)</sup> of the wicked,<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> and
go<sup>[833](h0833)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> in the
way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> of evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> men.

15  Avoid<sup>[6544](h6544)</sup> it, pass<sup>[5674](h5674)</sup>
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> by it, turn<sup>[7847](h7847)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> it, and pass
away.<sup>[5674](h5674)</sup>

16  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they sleep<sup>[3462](h3462)</sup>
not,<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> except<sup>[518](h0518)</sup>
<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> they have done
mischief;<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> and their
sleep<sup>[8142](h8142)</sup> is taken away,<sup>[1497](h1497)</sup>
unless<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> <sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> they cause some
to fall.<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup>

17  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they eat<sup>[3898](h3898)</sup> the
bread<sup>[3899](h3899)</sup> of wickedness,<sup>[7562](h7562)</sup> and
drink<sup>[8354](h8354)</sup> the wine<sup>[3196](h3196)</sup> of
violence.<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup>

18  But the path<sup>[734](h0734)</sup> of the
just<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> is as the shining<sup>[5051](h5051)</sup>
light,<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> that shineth<sup>[215](h0215)</sup> more
and more<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the
perfect<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> day.<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup>

19  The way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> is as darkness:<sup>[653](h0653)</sup>
they know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> at
what<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> they stumble.<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup>

20  My son,<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> attend<sup>[7181](h7181)</sup> to my
words;<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> incline<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> thine
ear<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> unto my sayings.<sup>[561](h0561)</sup>

21  Let them not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> depart<sup>[3868](h3868)</sup>
from thine eyes;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5869](h5869)</sup>
keep<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> them in the midst<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup>
of thine heart.<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup>

22  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> are
life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> unto those that
find<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> them, and health<sup>[4832](h4832)</sup> to
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> their flesh.<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup>

23  Keep<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> thy heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> with
all<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
diligence;<sup>[4929](h4929)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> out
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> it are the issues<sup>[8444](h8444)</sup> of
life.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>

24  Put away<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> thee a
froward<sup>[6143](h6143)</sup> mouth,<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> and
perverse<sup>[3891](h3891)</sup> lips<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> put
far<sup>[7368](h7368)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> thee.

25  Let thine eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> look<sup>[5027](h5027)</sup>
right on,<sup>[5227](h5227)</sup> and let thine
eyelids<sup>[6079](h6079)</sup> look straight<sup>[3474](h3474)</sup>
before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> thee.

26  Ponder<sup>[6424](h6424)</sup> the path<sup>[4570](h4570)</sup> of
thy feet,<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> and let all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
thy ways<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> be established.<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup>

27  Turn<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> to the right
hand<sup>[3225](h3225)</sup> nor to the left:<sup>[8040](h8040)</sup>
remove<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup> thy foot<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> from
evil.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/pro004)

1 ἀκούσατε παῖδες παιδείαν πατρὸς καὶ προσέχετε γνῶναι ἔννοιαν

2 δῶρον γὰρ ἀγαθὸν δωροῦμαι ὑμῖν τὸν ἐμὸν νόμον μὴ ἐγκαταλίπητε

3 υἱὸς γὰρ ἐγενόμην κἀγὼ πατρὶ ὑπήκοος καὶ ἀγαπώμενος ἐν προσώπῳ μητρός

4 οἳ ἔλεγον καὶ ἐδίδασκόν με ἐρειδέτω ὁ ἡμέτερος λόγος εἰς σὴν καρδίαν

5 φύλασσε ἐντολάς μὴ ἐπιλάθῃ μηδὲ παρίδῃς ῥῆσιν ἐμοῦ στόματος

6 μηδὲ ἐγκαταλίπῃς αὐτήν καὶ ἀνθέξεταί σου ἐράσθητι αὐτῆς καὶ τηρήσει σε

8 περιχαράκωσον αὐτήν καὶ ὑψώσει σε τίμησον αὐτήν ἵνα σε περιλάβῃ

9 ἵνα δῷ τῇ σῇ κεφαλῇ στέφανον χαρίτων στεφάνῳ δὲ τρυφῆς ὑπερασπίσῃ σου

10 ἄκουε υἱέ καὶ δέξαι ἐμοὺς λόγους καὶ πληθυνθήσεται ἔτη ζωῆς σου ἵνα
σοι γένωνται πολλαὶ ὁδοὶ βίου

11 ὁδοὺς γὰρ σοφίας διδάσκω σε ἐμβιβάζω δέ σε τροχιαῖς ὀρθαῖς

12 ἐὰν γὰρ πορεύῃ οὐ συγκλεισθήσεταί σου τὰ διαβήματα ἐὰν δὲ τρέχῃς οὐ
κοπιάσεις

13 ἐπιλαβοῦ ἐμῆς παιδείας μὴ ἀφῇς ἀλλὰ φύλαξον αὐτὴν σεαυτῷ εἰς ζωήν σου

14 ὁδοὺς ἀσεβῶν μὴ ἐπέλθῃς μηδὲ ζηλώσῃς ὁδοὺς παρανόμων

15 ἐν ᾧ ἂν τόπῳ στρατοπεδεύσωσιν μὴ ἐπέλθῃς ἐκεῖ ἔκκλινον δὲ ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν
καὶ παράλλαξον

16 οὐ γὰρ μὴ ὑπνώσωσιν ἐὰν μὴ κακοποιήσωσιν ἀφῄρηται ὁ ὕπνος αὐτῶν καὶ
οὐ κοιμῶνται

17 οἵδε γὰρ σιτοῦνται σῖτα ἀσεβείας οἴνῳ δὲ παρανόμῳ μεθύσκονται

18 αἱ δὲ ὁδοὶ τῶν δικαίων ὁμοίως φωτὶ λάμπουσιν προπορεύονται καὶ
φωτίζουσιν ἕως κατορθώσῃ ἡ ἡμέρα

19 αἱ δὲ ὁδοὶ τῶν ἀσεβῶν σκοτειναί οὐκ οἴδασιν πῶς προσκόπτουσιν

20 υἱέ ἐμῇ ῥήσει πρόσεχε τοῖς δὲ ἐμοῖς λόγοις παράβαλε σὸν οὖς

21 ὅπως μὴ ἐκλίπωσίν σε αἱ πηγαί σου φύλασσε αὐτὰς ἐν σῇ καρδίᾳ

22 ζωὴ γάρ ἐστιν τοῖς εὑρίσκουσιν αὐτὰς καὶ πάσῃ σαρκὶ ἴασις

23 πάσῃ φυλακῇ τήρει σὴν καρδίαν ἐκ γὰρ τούτων ἔξοδοι ζωῆς

24 περίελε σεαυτοῦ σκολιὸν στόμα καὶ ἄδικα χείλη μακρὰν ἀπὸ σοῦ ἄπωσαι

25 οἱ ὀφθαλμοί σου ὀρθὰ βλεπέτωσαν τὰ δὲ βλέφαρά σου νευέτω δίκαια

26 ὀρθὰς τροχιὰς ποίει σοῖς ποσὶν καὶ τὰς ὁδούς σου κατεύθυνε

27 μὴ ἐκκλίνῃς εἰς τὰ δεξιὰ μηδὲ εἰς τὰ ἀριστερά ἀπόστρεψον δὲ σὸν πόδα
ἀπὸ ὁδοῦ κακῆς  
ὁδοὺς γὰρ τὰς ἐκ δεξιῶν οἶδεν ὁ θεός διεστραμμέναι δέ εἰσιν αἱ ἐξ
ἀριστερῶν  
αὐτὸς δὲ ὀρθὰς ποιήσει τὰς τροχιάς σου τὰς δὲ πορείας σου ἐν εἰρήνῃ
προάξει

1 \[Audite, filii, disciplinam patris,  
et attendite ut sciatis prudentiam.  

2 Donum bonum tribuam vobis:  
legem meam ne derelinquatis.  

3 Nam et ego filius fui patris mei,  
tenellus et unigenitus coram matre mea.  

4 Et docebat me, atque dicebat:  
Suscipiat verba mea cor tuum;  
custodi præcepta mea, et vives.  

5 Posside sapientiam, posside prudentiam:  
ne obliviscaris, neque declines a verbis oris mei.  

6 Ne dimittas eam, et custodiet te:  
dilige eam, et conservabit te.  

7 Principium sapientiæ: posside sapientiam,  
et in omni possessione tua acquire prudentiam.  

8 Arripe illam, et exaltabit te;  
glorificaberis ab ea cum eam fueris amplexatus.  

9 Dabit capiti tuo augmenta gratiarum,  
et corona inclyta proteget te.\]  

10 \[Audi, fili mi, et suscipe verba mea,  
ut multiplicentur tibi anni vitæ.  

11 Viam sapientiæ monstrabo tibi;  
ducam te per semitas æquitatis:  

12 quas cum ingressus fueris, non arctabuntur gressus tui,  
et currens non habebis offendiculum.  

13 Tene disciplinam, ne dimittas eam;  
custodi illam, quia ipsa est vita tua.  

14 Ne delecteris in semitis impiorum,  
nec tibi placeat malorum via.  

15 Fuge ab ea, nec transeas per illam;  
declina, et desere eam.  

16 Non enim dormiunt nisi malefecerint,  
et rapitur somnus ab eis nisi supplantaverint.  

17 Comedunt panem impietatis,  
et vinum iniquitatis bibunt.  

18 Justorum autem semita quasi lux splendens procedit,  
et crescit usque ad perfectam diem.  

19 Via impiorum tenebrosa;  
nesciunt ubi corruant.  

20 Fili mi, ausculta sermones meos,  
et ad eloquia mea inclina aurem tuam.  

21 Ne recedant ab oculis tuis:  
custodi ea in medio cordis tui:  

22 vita enim sunt invenientibus ea,  
et universæ carni sanitas.  

23 Omni custodia serva cor tuum,  
quia ex ipso vita procedit.  

24 Remove a te os pravum,  
et detrahentia labia sint procul a te.  

25 Oculi tui recta videant,  
et palpebræ tuæ præcedant gressus tuos.  

26 Dirige semitam pedibus tuis,  
et omnes viæ tuæ stabilientur.  

27 Ne declines ad dexteram neque ad sinistram;  
averte pedem tuum a malo:  
vias enim quæ a dextris sunt novit Dominus:  
perversæ vero sunt quæ a sinistris sunt.  
Ipse autem rectos faciet cursus tuos,  
itinera autem tua in pace producet.\]
