H8040  
שׂמאל שׂמאול  
שְׂמוֹאל שְׂמאוֹל ‎ ś<sup>e</sup>mô‘l ś<sup>e</sup>m‘ôl  
*sem-ole‘,* *sem-ole‘*  
A primitive word (rather perhaps from the same as [8071](h8071.md) (by
insertion of the *‘aleph*) through the idea of *wrapping* up); properly
*dark* (as *enveloped*), that is, the *north*; hence (by orientation)
the *left* hand: - left (hand, side).
