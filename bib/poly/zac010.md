### Zechariah 10

[KJV](../kjv/zac010.md)

1  Ask<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> ye of the LORD<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> rain<sup>[4306](h4306.md)</sup> in the
time<sup>[6256](h6256)</sup> of the latter rain;<sup>[4456](h4456.md)</sup>
so the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall make<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
bright clouds,<sup>[2385](h2385)</sup> and give<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup>
them showers<sup>[1653](h1653)</sup> of rain,<sup>[4306](h4306.md)</sup> to
every one<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> grass<sup>[6212](h6212.md)</sup> in the
field.<sup>[7704](h7704.md)</sup>

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the idols<sup>[8655](h8655.md)</sup> have
spoken<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> vanity,<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup> and the
diviners<sup>[7080](h7080)</sup> have seen<sup>[2372](h2372.md)</sup> a
lie,<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> and have told<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
false<sup>[7723](h7723)</sup> dreams;<sup>[2472](h2472.md)</sup> they
comfort<sup>[5162](h5162)</sup> in vain:<sup>[1892](h1892.md)</sup>
therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> they went
their way<sup>[5265](h5265)</sup> as<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> a
flock,<sup>[6629](h6629.md)</sup> they were
troubled,<sup>[6031](h6031)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> there
was no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> shepherd.<sup>[7462](h7462.md)</sup>

3  Mine anger<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> was kindled<sup>[2734](h2734.md)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the shepherds,<sup>[7462](h7462.md)</sup>
and I punished<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
goats:<sup>[6260](h6260)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of hosts<sup>[6635](h6635.md)</sup> hath
visited<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> his
flock<sup>[5739](h5739)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Judah,<sup>[3063](h3063.md)</sup> and hath
made<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> them as his goodly<sup>[1935](h1935.md)</sup>
horse<sup>[5483](h5483)</sup> in the battle.<sup>[4421](h4421.md)</sup>

4  Out of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> him came forth<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup>
the corner,<sup>[6438](h6438)</sup> out of<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> him
the nail,<sup>[3489](h3489)</sup> out of<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> him the
battle<sup>[4421](h4421)</sup> bow,<sup>[7198](h7198.md)</sup> out
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> him every<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
oppressor<sup>[5065](h5065)</sup> together.<sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup>

5  And they shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> as
mighty<sup>[1368](h1368.md)</sup> men, which tread
down<sup>[947](h0947.md)</sup> their enemies in the
mire<sup>[2916](h2916)</sup> of the streets<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup> in
the battle:<sup>[4421](h4421.md)</sup> and they shall
fight,<sup>[3898](h3898)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is with<sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup> them, and
the riders<sup>[7392](h7392)</sup> on horses<sup>[5483](h5483.md)</sup>
shall be confounded.<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup>

6  And I will strengthen<sup>[1396](h1396)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Judah,<sup>[3063](h3063.md)</sup> and
I will save<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> the house<sup>[1004](h1004.md)</sup> of
Joseph,<sup>[3130](h3130.md)</sup> and I will bring them
again<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> to place them; for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
I have mercy upon<sup>[7355](h7355.md)</sup> them: and they shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as though<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> I had
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> cast them off:<sup>[2186](h2186.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> am the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> their God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> and will
hear<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup> them.

7  And they of Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669.md)</sup> shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like a mighty<sup>[1368](h1368.md)</sup> man,
and their heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> shall
rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> as through<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup>
wine:<sup>[3196](h3196.md)</sup> yea, their
children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> shall see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> it,
and be glad;<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> their heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>
shall rejoice<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup> in the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

8  I will hiss<sup>[8319](h8319.md)</sup> for them, and
gather<sup>[6908](h6908)</sup> them; for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> I have
redeemed<sup>[6299](h6299.md)</sup> them: and they shall
increase<sup>[7235](h7235)</sup> as<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> they have
increased.<sup>[7235](h7235.md)</sup>

9  And I will sow<sup>[2232](h2232.md)</sup> them among the
people:<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> and they shall
remember<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup> me in far
countries;<sup>[4801](h4801.md)</sup> and they shall
live<sup>[2421](h2421)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854.md)</sup> their
children,<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> and turn
again.<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup>

10  I will bring them again<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> also out of the
land<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> of
Egypt,<sup>[4714](h4714)</sup> and gather<sup>[6908](h6908.md)</sup> them
out of Assyria;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup> and I
will bring<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> them into<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of Gilead<sup>[1568](h1568.md)</sup> and
Lebanon;<sup>[3844](h3844.md)</sup> and place shall
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be found<sup>[4672](h4672.md)</sup> for them.

11  And he shall pass<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup> through the
sea<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> with affliction,<sup>[6869](h6869.md)</sup> and
shall smite<sup>[5221](h5221)</sup> the waves<sup>[1530](h1530.md)</sup> in
the sea,<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> and all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
deeps<sup>[4688](h4688)</sup> of the river<sup>[2975](h2975.md)</sup> shall
dry up:<sup>[3001](h3001)</sup> and the pride<sup>[1347](h1347.md)</sup> of
Assyria<sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup> shall be brought
down,<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> and the scepter<sup>[7626](h7626.md)</sup> of
Egypt<sup>[4714](h4714)</sup> shall depart away.<sup>[5493](h5493.md)</sup>

12  And I will strengthen<sup>[1396](h1396.md)</sup> them in the
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> and they shall walk up and
down<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> in his name,<sup>[8034](h8034.md)</sup>
saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/zac010.md)

1 αἰτεῖσθε ὑετὸν παρὰ κυρίου καθ᾽ ὥραν πρόιμον καὶ ὄψιμον κύριος
ἐποίησεν φαντασίας καὶ ὑετὸν χειμερινὸν δώσει αὐτοῖς ἑκάστῳ βοτάνην ἐν
ἀγρῷ

2 διότι οἱ ἀποφθεγγόμενοι ἐλάλησαν κόπους καὶ οἱ μάντεις ὁράσεις ψευδεῖς
καὶ τὰ ἐνύπνια ψευδῆ ἐλάλουν μάταια παρεκάλουν διὰ τοῦτο ἐξήρθησαν ὡς
πρόβατα καὶ ἐκακώθησαν διότι οὐκ ἦν ἴασις

3 ἐπὶ τοὺς ποιμένας παρωξύνθη ὁ θυμός μου καὶ ἐπὶ τοὺς ἀμνοὺς
ἐπισκέψομαι καὶ ἐπισκέψεται κύριος ὁ θεὸς ὁ παντοκράτωρ τὸ ποίμνιον
αὐτοῦ τὸν οἶκον Ιουδα καὶ τάξει αὐτοὺς ὡς ἵππον εὐπρεπῆ αὐτοῦ ἐν πολέμῳ

4 καὶ ἐξ αὐτοῦ ἐπέβλεψεν καὶ ἐξ αὐτοῦ ἔταξεν καὶ ἐξ αὐτοῦ τόξον ἐν θυμῷ
ἐξ αὐτοῦ ἐξελεύσεται πᾶς ὁ ἐξελαύνων ἐν τῷ αὐτῷ

5 καὶ ἔσονται ὡς μαχηταὶ πατοῦντες πηλὸν ἐν ταῖς ὁδοῖς ἐν πολέμῳ καὶ
παρατάξονται διότι κύριος μετ᾽ αὐτῶν καὶ καταισχυνθήσονται ἀναβάται
ἵππων

6 καὶ κατισχύσω τὸν οἶκον Ιουδα καὶ τὸν οἶκον Ιωσηφ σώσω καὶ κατοικιῶ
αὐτούς ὅτι ἠγάπησα αὐτούς καὶ ἔσονται ὃν τρόπον οὐκ ἀπεστρεψάμην αὐτούς
διότι ἐγὼ κύριος ὁ θεὸς αὐτῶν καὶ ἐπακούσομαι αὐτοῖς

7 καὶ ἔσονται ὡς μαχηταὶ τοῦ Εφραιμ καὶ χαρήσεται ἡ καρδία αὐτῶν ὡς ἐν
οἴνῳ καὶ τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν ὄψονται καὶ εὐφρανθήσονται καὶ χαρεῖται ἡ καρδία
αὐτῶν ἐπὶ τῷ κυρίῳ

8 σημανῶ αὐτοῖς καὶ εἰσδέξομαι αὐτούς διότι λυτρώσομαι αὐτούς καὶ
πληθυνθήσονται καθότι ἦσαν πολλοί

9 καὶ σπερῶ αὐτοὺς ἐν λαοῖς καὶ οἱ μακρὰν μνησθήσονταί μου ἐκθρέψουσιν
τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν καὶ ἐπιστρέψουσιν

10 καὶ ἐπιστρέψω αὐτοὺς ἐκ γῆς Αἰγύπτου καὶ ἐξ Ἀσσυρίων εἰσδέξομαι
αὐτοὺς καὶ εἰς τὴν Γαλααδῖτιν καὶ εἰς τὸν Λίβανον εἰσάξω αὐτούς καὶ οὐ
μὴ ὑπολειφθῇ ἐξ αὐτῶν οὐδὲ εἷς

11 καὶ διελεύσονται ἐν θαλάσσῃ στενῇ καὶ πατάξουσιν ἐν θαλάσσῃ κύματα
καὶ ξηρανθήσεται πάντα τὰ βάθη ποταμῶν καὶ ἀφαιρεθήσεται πᾶσα ὕβρις
Ἀσσυρίων καὶ σκῆπτρον Αἰγύπτου περιαιρεθήσεται

12 καὶ κατισχύσω αὐτοὺς ἐν κυρίῳ θεῷ αὐτῶν καὶ ἐν τῷ ὀνόματι αὐτοῦ
κατακαυχήσονται λέγει κύριος

[Vul](../vul/zac010.md)

1 \[Petite a Domino pluviam in tempore serotino,  
et Dominus faciet nives:  
et pluviam imbris dabit eis,  
singulis herbam in agro.  

2 Quia simulacra locuta sunt inutile,  
et divini viderunt mendacium:  
et somniatores locuti sunt frustra,  
vane consolabantur:  
idcirco abducti sunt quasi grex:  
affligentur, quia non est eis pastor.  

3 Super pastores iratus est furor meus,  
et super hircos visitabo:  
quia visitavit Dominus exercituum gregem suum, domum Juda,  
et posuit eos quasi equum gloriæ suæ in bello.  

4 Ex ipso angulus, ex ipso paxillus,  
ex ipso arcus prælii,  
ex ipso egredietur omnis exactor simul.  

5 Et erunt quasi fortes conculcantes lutum viarum in prælio,  
et bellabunt, quia Dominus cum eis:  
et confundentur ascensores equorum.  

6 Et confortabo domum Juda,  
et domum Joseph salvabo:  
et convertam eos, quia miserebor eorum:  
et erunt sicut fuerunt quando non projeceram eos:  
ego enim Dominus Deus eorum, et exaudiam eos.  

7 Et erunt quasi fortes Ephraim,  
et lætabitur cor eorum quasi a vino:  
et filii eorum videbunt, et lætabuntur,  
et exsultabit cor eorum in Domino.  

8 Sibilabo eis, et congregabo illos,  
quia redemi eos:  
et multiplicabo eos sicut ante fuerant multiplicati.  

9 Et seminabo eos in populis,  
et de longe recordabuntur mei:  
et vivent cum filiis suis, et revertentur.  

10 Et reducam eos de terra Ægypti,  
et de Assyriis congregabo eos,  
et ad terram Galaad et Libani adducam eos,  
et non invenietur eis locus:  

11 et transibit in maris freto,  
et percutiet in mari fluctus,  
et confundentur omnia profunda fluminis:  
et humiliabitur superbia Assur,  
et sceptrum Ægypti recedet.  

12 Confortabo eos in Domino,  
et in nomine ejus ambulabunt, dicit Dominus.\]
