H6367  
פּי החרת  
פִּי הַחִרוֹת ‎ pı̂y hachirôth  
*pee* *hah-khee-roth‘*  
From [6310](h6310) and the feminine plural of a noun (from the same root
as [2356](h2356) , with the article interposed; *mouth* *of* *the*
*gorges*; *Pi-ha-Chiroth*, a place in Egypt. (Found in [Num
14:19](num014.htm#019) without the *“pi”*.): - Pi-hahiroth. \[In Num.
[14](h0014) : [19](h0019) without Pi-.\]
