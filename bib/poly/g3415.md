G3415  
μνάομαι  
mnaomai  
*mnah‘-om-ahee*  
Middle voice of a derivative of [3306](g3306) or perhaps of the base of
[3145](g3145) (through the idea of *fixture* in the mind or of mental
*grasp*); to *bear* *in* *mind*, that is, *recollect*; by implication to
*reward* or *punish*: - be mindful, remember, come (have) in
remembrance. Compare [3403](g3403)
