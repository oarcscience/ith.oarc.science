### Psalms 6

[KJV](../kjv/psa006.md)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup> on
Neginoth<sup>[5058](h5058)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
Sheminith,<sup>[8067](h8067)</sup> A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210.md)</sup> of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
rebuke<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup> me not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> in thine
anger,<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> neither<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup>
chasten<sup>[3256](h3256.md)</sup> me in thy hot
displeasure.<sup>[2534](h2534.md)</sup>

2  Have mercy<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> upon me, O
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am weak:<sup>[536](h0536.md)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> heal<sup>[7495](h7495.md)</sup> me;
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my bones<sup>[6106](h6106.md)</sup> are
vexed.<sup>[926](h0926.md)</sup>

3  My soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> is also sore<sup>[3966](h3966.md)</sup>
vexed:<sup>[926](h0926)</sup> but thou,<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> how long?<sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup>
<sup>[4970](h4970.md)</sup>

4  Return,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
deliver<sup>[2502](h2502)</sup> my soul:<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> oh
save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> me for thy mercies'
sake.<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> <sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup>

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in death<sup>[4194](h4194.md)</sup> there is
no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> remembrance<sup>[2143](h2143.md)</sup> of thee:
in the grave<sup>[7585](h7585)</sup> who<sup>[4310](h4310.md)</sup> shall
give thee thanks?<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup>

6  I am weary<sup>[3021](h3021.md)</sup> with my
groaning;<sup>[585](h0585)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> make I my bed<sup>[4296](h4296.md)</sup> to
swim;<sup>[7811](h7811)</sup> I water<sup>[4529](h4529.md)</sup> my
couch<sup>[6210](h6210)</sup> with my tears.<sup>[1832](h1832.md)</sup>

7  Mine eye<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> is consumed<sup>[6244](h6244.md)</sup>
because of grief;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3708](h3708.md)</sup> it
waxeth old<sup>[6275](h6275.md)</sup> because of
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> mine enemies.<sup>[6887](h6887.md)</sup>

8  Depart<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me,
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> ye workers<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> of
iniquity;<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath heard<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> the
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of my weeping.<sup>[1065](h1065.md)</sup>

9  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath heard<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup>
my supplication;<sup>[8467](h8467.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will receive<sup>[3947](h3947.md)</sup> my
prayer.<sup>[8605](h8605.md)</sup>

10  Let all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> mine enemies<sup>[341](h0341.md)</sup>
be ashamed<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> and sore<sup>[3966](h3966.md)</sup>
vexed:<sup>[926](h0926)</sup> let them return<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup>
and be ashamed<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> suddenly.<sup>[7281](h7281.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa006.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ἐν ὕμνοις ὑπὲρ τῆς ὀγδόης ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 κύριε μὴ τῷ θυμῷ σου ἐλέγξῃς με μηδὲ τῇ ὀργῇ σου παιδεύσῃς με

3 ἐλέησόν με κύριε ὅτι ἀσθενής εἰμι ἴασαί με κύριε ὅτι ἐταράχθη τὰ ὀστᾶ
μου

4 καὶ ἡ ψυχή μου ἐταράχθη σφόδρα καὶ σύ κύριε ἕως πότε

5 ἐπίστρεψον κύριε ῥῦσαι τὴν ψυχήν μου σῶσόν με ἕνεκεν τοῦ ἐλέους σου

6 ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν ἐν τῷ θανάτῳ ὁ μνημονεύων σου ἐν δὲ τῷ ᾅδῃ τίς
ἐξομολογήσεταί σοι

7 ἐκοπίασα ἐν τῷ στεναγμῷ μου λούσω καθ᾽ ἑκάστην νύκτα τὴν κλίνην μου ἐν
δάκρυσίν μου τὴν στρωμνήν μου βρέξω

8 ἐταράχθη ἀπὸ θυμοῦ ὁ ὀφθαλμός μου ἐπαλαιώθην ἐν πᾶσιν τοῖς ἐχθροῖς μου

9 ἀπόστητε ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ πάντες οἱ ἐργαζόμενοι τὴν ἀνομίαν ὅτι εἰσήκουσεν
κύριος τῆς φωνῆς τοῦ κλαυθμοῦ μου

10 εἰσήκουσεν κύριος τῆς δεήσεώς μου κύριος τὴν προσευχήν μου
προσεδέξατο

11 αἰσχυνθείησαν καὶ ταραχθείησαν σφόδρα πάντες οἱ ἐχθροί μου
ἀποστραφείησαν καὶ καταισχυνθείησαν σφόδρα διὰ τάχους

[Vul](../vul/psa006.md)

1 In finem, in carminibus. Psalmus David. Pro octava.

2 \[Domine, ne in furore tuo arguas me,  
neque in ira tua corripias me.  

3 Miserere mei, Domine, quoniam infirmus sum;  
sana me, Domine, quoniam conturbata sunt ossa mea.  

4 Et anima mea turbata est valde;  
sed tu, Domine, usquequo?  

5 Convertere, Domine, et eripe animam meam;  
salvum me fac propter misericordiam tuam.  

6 Quoniam non est in morte qui memor sit tui;  
in inferno autem quis confitebitur tibi?  

7 Laboravi in gemitu meo;  
lavabo per singulas noctes lectum meum:  
lacrimis meis stratum meum rigabo.  

8 Turbatus est a furore oculus meus;  
inveteravi inter omnes inimicos meos.  

9 Discedite a me omnes qui operamini iniquitatem,  
quoniam exaudivit Dominus vocem fletus mei.  

10 Exaudivit Dominus deprecationem meam;  
Dominus orationem meam suscepit.  

11 Erubescant, et conturbentur vehementer, omnes inimici mei;  
convertantur, et erubescant valde velociter.\]
