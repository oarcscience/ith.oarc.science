H3205  
ילד  
יָלַד ‎ yâlad  
*yaw-lad‘*  
A primitive root; to *bear* young; causatively to *beget*; medically to
*act* *as* *midwife*; specifically to *show* *lineage: -* bear, beget,
birth (\[-day\]), born, (make to) bring forth (children, young), bring
up, calve, child, come, be delivered (of a child), time of delivery,
gender, hatch, labour, (do the office of a) midwife, declare pedigrees,
be the son of, (woman in, woman that) travail (-eth, -ing woman).
