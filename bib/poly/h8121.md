H8121  
שׁמשׁ  
שֶׁמֶשׁ ‎ shemesh  
*sheh‘-mesh*  
From an unused root meaning to be *brilliant*; the *sun*; by implication
the *east*; figuratively a *ray*, that is, (architecturally) a notched
*battlement*: - + east side (-ward), sun (\[rising\]), + west (-ward),
window. See also [1053](h1053.md)
