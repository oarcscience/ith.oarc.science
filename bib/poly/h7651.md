H7651  
שׁבעה שׁבע  
שֶׁבַע שִׁבעָה ‎ sheba‛ shib‛âh  
*sheh‘-bah,* *shib-aw‘*  
From [7650](h7650) a primitive cardinal number; *seven* (as the sacred
*full* one); also (adverbially) *seven* *times*; by implication a
*week*; by extension an *indefinite* number: - (+ by) seven (\[-fold\],
-s, \[-teen, -teenth\], -th, times). Compare [7658](h7658)
