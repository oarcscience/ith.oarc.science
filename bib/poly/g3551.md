G3551  
νόμος  
nomos  
*nom‘-os*  
From a primary word νέμω nemō (to *parcel* out, especially *food* or
*grazing* to animals); *law* (through the idea of prescriptive *usage*),
generally (*regulation*), specifically (of Moses \[including the
volume\]; also of the Gospel), or figuratively (a *principle*): - law.
