G3582  
ξέστης  
xestēs  
*xes‘-tace*  
As if from ξέω xeō (which properly means to *smooth*; by implication
\[of *friction*\] to *boil* or *heat*); a *vessel* (as *fashioned* or
for *cooking*), (or perhaps by corruption from the Latin *sextarius* ,
the *sixth* of a modius, that is, about a *pint*), that is,
(specifically) a *measure* for liquids or solids, (by analogy a
*pitcher*): - pot.
