H3966  
מאד  
מְאוֹד ‎ m<sup>e</sup>‘ôd  
*meh-ode‘*  
From the same as [181](h0181) properly *vehemence*, that is, (with or
without preposition) *vehemently*; by implication *wholly*, *speedily*,
etc. (often with other words as an intensive or superlative; especially
when repeated): - diligently, especially, exceeding (-ly), far, fast,
good, great (-ly), X louder and louder, might (-ily, -y), (so) much,
quickly, (so) sore, utterly, very (+ much, sore), well.
