H8548  
תּמיד  
תָּּמִידּ ‎ tâmı̂yd  
*taw-meed‘*  
From an unused root meaning to *stretch*; properly *continuance* (as
indefinite *extension*); but used only (attributively as adjective)
*constant* (or adverbially *constantly*); elliptically the *regular*
(daily) sacrifice: - alway (-s), continual (employment, -ly), daily,
(\[n-\]) ever (-more), perpetual.
