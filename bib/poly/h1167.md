H1167  
בּעל  
בַּעַל ‎ ba‛al  
*bah‘-al*  
From [1166](h1166) a *master*; hence a *husband*, or (figuratively)
*owner* (often used with another noun in modifications of this latter
sense: - + archer, + babbler, + bird, captain, chief man, + confederate,
+ have to do, + dreamer, those to whom it is due, + furious, those that
are given to it, great, + hairy, he that hath it, have, + horseman,
husband, lord, man, + married, master, person, + sworn, they of.
