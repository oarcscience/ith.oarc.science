H3671  
כּנף  
כָּנָף ‎ kânâph  
*kaw-nawf‘*  
From [3670](h3670.md) an *edge* or *extremity*; specifically (of a bird or
army) a *wing*, (of a garment or bed clothing) a *flap*, (of the earth)
a *quarter*, (of a building) a *pinnacle: -* + bird, border, corner,
end, feather \[-ed\], X flying, + (one an-) other, overspreading, X
quarters, skirt, X sort, uttermost part, wing (\[-ed\]).
