G4912  
συνέχω  
sunechō  
*soon-ekh‘-o*  
From [4862](g4862) and [2192](g2192) to *hold* *together*, that is, to
*compress* (the ears, with a crowd or siege) or *arrest* (a prisoner);
figuratively to *compel*, *perplex*, *afflict*, *preoccupy:* -
constrain, hold, keep in, press, lie sick of, stop, be in a strait,
straiten, be taken with, throng.
