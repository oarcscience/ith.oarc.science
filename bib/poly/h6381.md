H6381  
פּלא  
פָּלָא ‎ pâlâ‘  
*paw-law‘*  
A primitive root; properly perhaps to *separate*, that is, *distinguish*
(literally or figuratively); by implication to *be* (causatively *make*)
*great*, *difficult*, *wonderful: -* accomplish, (arise . . . too, be
too) hard, hidden, things too high, (be, do, do a, shew) marvelous (-ly,
-els, things, work), miracles, perform, separate, make singular, (be,
great, make) wonderful (-ers, -ly, things, works), wondrous (things,
works, -ly).
