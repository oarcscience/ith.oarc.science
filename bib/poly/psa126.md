### Psalms 126

[KJV](../kjv/psa126)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees.<sup>[4609](h4609)</sup>
When the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> turned
again<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
captivity<sup>[7870](h7870)</sup> of Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> we
were<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like them that
dream.<sup>[2492](h2492)</sup>

2  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> was our mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>
filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> with laughter,<sup>[7814](h7814)</sup>
and our tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> with
singing:<sup>[7440](h7440)</sup> then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup>
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> they among the
heathen,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath
done<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> great things<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup>
for<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> them.<sup>[428](h0428)</sup>

3  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath done<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
great things<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup> for<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> us;
whereof we are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> glad.<sup>[8056](h8056)</sup>

4  Turn again<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> our
captivity,<sup>[7622](h7622)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> as
the streams<sup>[650](h0650)</sup> in the south.<sup>[5045](h5045)</sup>

5  They that sow<sup>[2232](h2232)</sup> in
tears<sup>[1832](h1832)</sup> shall reap<sup>[7114](h7114)</sup> in
joy.<sup>[7440](h7440)</sup>

6  He that goeth forth<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> <sup>[1980](h1980)</sup>
and weepeth,<sup>[1058](h1058)</sup> bearing<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup>
precious<sup>[4901](h4901)</sup> seed,<sup>[2233](h2233)</sup> shall
doubtless come again<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> <sup>[935](h0935)</sup> with
rejoicing,<sup>[7440](h7440)</sup> bringing<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> his
sheaves<sup>[485](h0485)</sup> with him.

[Sep](../sep/psa126)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν τῷ Σαλωμων ἐὰν μὴ κύριος οἰκοδομήσῃ οἶκον εἰς μάτην
ἐκοπίασαν οἱ οἰκοδομοῦντες αὐτόν ἐὰν μὴ κύριος φυλάξῃ πόλιν εἰς μάτην
ἠγρύπνησεν ὁ φυλάσσων

2 εἰς μάτην ὑμῖν ἐστιν τοῦ ὀρθρίζειν ἐγείρεσθαι μετὰ τὸ καθῆσθαι οἱ
ἔσθοντες ἄρτον ὀδύνης ὅταν δῷ τοῖς ἀγαπητοῖς αὐτοῦ ὕπνον

3 ἰδοὺ ἡ κληρονομία κυρίου υἱοί ὁ μισθὸς τοῦ καρποῦ τῆς γαστρός

4 ὡσεὶ βέλη ἐν χειρὶ δυνατοῦ οὕτως οἱ υἱοὶ τῶν ἐκτετιναγμένων

5 μακάριος ἄνθρωπος ὃς πληρώσει τὴν ἐπιθυμίαν αὐτοῦ ἐξ αὐτῶν οὐ
καταισχυνθήσονται ὅταν λαλῶσι τοῖς ἐχθροῖς αὐτῶν ἐν πύλῃ

[Vul](../vul/psa126)

1 Canticum graduum Salomonis. \[Nisi Dominus ædificaverit domum,  
in vanum laboraverunt qui ædificant eam.  
Nisi Dominus custodierit civitatem,  
frustra vigilat qui custodit eam.  

2 Vanum est vobis ante lucem surgere:  
surgite postquam sederitis,  
qui manducatis panem doloris.  
Cum dederit dilectis suis somnum,  

3 ecce hæreditas Domini, filii;  
merces, fructus ventris.  

4 Sicut sagittæ in manu potentis,  
ita filii excussorum.  

5 Beatus vir qui implevit desiderium suum ex ipsis:  
non confundetur cum loquetur inimicis suis in porta.\]
