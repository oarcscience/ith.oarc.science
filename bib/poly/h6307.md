H6307  
פדּן ארם פּדּן  
פַּדָּן פַּדַּן אֲרָם ‎ paddân paddan ‘ărâm  
*pad-dawn‘,* *pad-dan‘* *ar-awm‘*  
From an unused root meaning to *extend*; a *plateau*; or the second form
which is from the same and [758](h0758.md) the *table* *land* *of* *Aram*;
*Paddan* or *Paddan-Aram*, a region of Syria: - Padan, Padan-aram.
