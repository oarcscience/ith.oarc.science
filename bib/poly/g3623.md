G3623  
οἰκονόμος  
oikonomos  
*oy-kon-om‘-os*  
From [3624](g3624) and the base of [3551](g3551.md) a *house* *distributor*
(that is, *manager*), or *overseer*, that is, an employee in that
capacity; by extension a fiscal *agent* (*treasurer*); figuratively a
*preacher* (of the Gospel): - chamberlain, governor, steward.
