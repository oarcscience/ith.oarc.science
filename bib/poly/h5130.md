H5130  
נוּף  
נוּף ‎ nûph  
*noof*  
A primitive root; to *quiver* (that is, *vibrate* up and down, or *rock*
to and fro); used in a great variety of applications (including
sprinkling, beckoning, rubbing, bastinadoing, sawing, waving, etc.): -
lift up, move, offer, perfume, send, shake, sift, strike, wave.
