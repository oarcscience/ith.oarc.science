H5060  
נגע  
נָגַע ‎ nâga‛  
*naw-gah‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *touch*, that is, *lay* *the* *hand*
*upon* (for any purpose; euphemistically, to *lie* *with* a woman); by
implication to *reach* (figuratively to *arrive*, *acquire*); violently,
to *strike* (punish, defeat, destroy, etc.): - beat, (X be able to)
bring (down), cast, come (nigh), draw near (nigh), get up, happen, join,
near, plague, reach (up), smite, strike, touch.
