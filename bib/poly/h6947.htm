H6947
קדשׁ בּרנע
קָדֵשׁ בַּרנֵַע ‎ qâdêsh barnêa‛
kaw-dashe‘ bar-nay‘-ah
From the same as 6946 and an otherwise unused word (apparently compounded of a correspondent to 1251 and a derivative of 5128 meaning desert of a fugitive; Kadesh of (the) Wilderness of Wandering; Kadesh-Barnea, a place in the Desert: - Kadesh-barnea.
