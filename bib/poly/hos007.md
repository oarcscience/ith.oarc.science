### Hosea 7

[KJV](../kjv/hos007.md)

1  When I would have healed<sup>[7495](h7495.md)</sup>
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> then the
iniquity<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> of Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669.md)</sup> was
discovered,<sup>[1540](h1540.md)</sup> and the
wickedness<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> of Samaria:<sup>[8111](h8111.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they commit<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup>
falsehood;<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> and the thief<sup>[1590](h1590.md)</sup>
cometh in,<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> and the troop of
robbers<sup>[1416](h1416)</sup> spoileth<sup>[6584](h6584.md)</sup>
without.<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup>

2  And they consider<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> not<sup>[1077](h1077.md)</sup>
in their hearts<sup>[3824](h3824.md)</sup> that I
remember<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> their
wickedness:<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> now<sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup> their

about;<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> they are<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> my face.<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup>

3  They make the king glad<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup>
<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> with their wickedness,<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup>
and the princes<sup>[8269](h8269.md)</sup> with their
lies.<sup>[3585](h3585.md)</sup>

4  They are all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
adulterers,<sup>[5003](h5003)</sup> as<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup> an
oven<sup>[8574](h8574)</sup> heated<sup>[1197](h1197.md)</sup>
by<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> the baker,<sup>[644](h0644.md)</sup> who
ceaseth<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> from raising<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[5782](h5782)</sup> after he hath kneaded<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3888](h3888)</sup> the dough,<sup>[1217](h1217.md)</sup>
until<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> it be leavened.<sup>[2556](h2556.md)</sup>

5  In the day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> of our
king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> the princes<sup>[8269](h8269.md)</sup> have
made him sick<sup>[2470](h2470.md)</sup> with
bottles<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup> of wine;<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>

hand<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854.md)</sup>
scorners.<sup>[3945](h3945.md)</sup>

6  For<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> they have made
ready<sup>[7126](h7126)</sup> their heart<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> like
an oven,<sup>[8574](h8574.md)</sup> while they lie in
wait:<sup>[693](h0693)</sup> their baker<sup>[644](h0644.md)</sup>
sleepeth<sup>[3463](h3463)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
night;<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> in the morning<sup>[1242](h1242.md)</sup>
it<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> burneth<sup>[1197](h1197.md)</sup> as a
flaming<sup>[3852](h3852)</sup> fire.<sup>[784](h0784.md)</sup>

7  They are all hot<sup>[2552](h2552)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> as
an oven,<sup>[8574](h8574.md)</sup> and have
devoured<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> their
judges;<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> their
kings<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> are fallen:<sup>[5307](h5307.md)</sup> there
is none<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup> among them that
calleth<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me.

8  Ephraim,<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> hath mixed
himself<sup>[1101](h1101.md)</sup> among the
people;<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669.md)</sup>
is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a cake<sup>[5692](h5692.md)</sup>
not<sup>[1097](h1097)</sup> turned.<sup>[2015](h2015.md)</sup>

9  Strangers<sup>[2114](h2114.md)</sup> have
devoured<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> his strength,<sup>[3581](h3581.md)</sup>
and he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> it
not:<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> yea,<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup> gray
hairs<sup>[7872](h7872)</sup> are here and there<sup>[2236](h2236.md)</sup>
upon him, yet he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup>
not.<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

10  And the pride<sup>[1347](h1347.md)</sup> of
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> testifieth<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup> to his
face:<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> and they do not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> to<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> their God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>
nor<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> seek<sup>[1245](h1245.md)</sup> him for
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> this.<sup>[2063](h2063.md)</sup>

11  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> also is<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> like
a silly<sup>[6601](h6601)</sup> dove<sup>[3123](h3123.md)</sup>
without<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> heart:<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> they
call<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> to Egypt,<sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup> they
go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> to Assyria.<sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup>

12  When<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> they shall go,<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> I
will spread<sup>[6566](h6566)</sup> my net<sup>[7568](h7568.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> them; I will bring them
down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> as the fowls<sup>[5775](h5775.md)</sup> of the
heaven;<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> I will chastise<sup>[3256](h3256.md)</sup>
them, as their congregation<sup>[5712](h5712.md)</sup> hath
heard.<sup>[8088](h8088.md)</sup>

13  Woe<sup>[188](h0188)</sup> unto them! for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
they have fled<sup>[5074](h5074)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me:
destruction<sup>[7701](h7701.md)</sup> unto them!
because<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> they have
transgressed<sup>[6586](h6586.md)</sup> against me: though
I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup> have redeemed<sup>[6299](h6299.md)</sup> them, yet
they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> have spoken<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
lies<sup>[3577](h3577)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me.

14  And they have not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
cried<sup>[2199](h2199)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me with their
heart,<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> they

themselves<sup>[1481](h1481)</sup> for<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>

15  Though I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> have bound<sup>[3256](h3256.md)</sup>
and strengthened<sup>[2388](h2388.md)</sup> their
arms,<sup>[2220](h2220.md)</sup> yet do they
imagine<sup>[2803](h2803)</sup> mischief<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup>

16  They return,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> but not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
to the most High:<sup>[5920](h5920.md)</sup> they
are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like a deceitful<sup>[7423](h7423.md)</sup>
bow:<sup>[7198](h7198)</sup> their princes<sup>[8269](h8269.md)</sup> shall

their derision<sup>[3933](h3933.md)</sup> in the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of Egypt.<sup>[4714](h4714.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/hos007.md)

1 ἐν τῷ ἰάσασθαί με τὸν Ισραηλ καὶ ἀποκαλυφθήσεται ἡ ἀδικία Εφραιμ καὶ ἡ
κακία Σαμαρείας ὅτι ἠργάσαντο ψευδῆ καὶ κλέπτης πρὸς αὐτὸν εἰσελεύσεται
ἐκδιδύσκων λῃστὴς ἐν τῇ ὁδῷ αὐτοῦ

2 ὅπως συνᾴδωσιν ὡς συνᾴδοντες τῇ καρδίᾳ αὐτῶν πάσας τὰς κακίας αὐτῶν
ἐμνήσθην νῦν ἐκύκλωσεν αὐτοὺς τὰ διαβούλια αὐτῶν ἀπέναντι τοῦ προσώπου
μου ἐγένοντο

3 ἐν ταῖς κακίαις αὐτῶν εὔφραναν βασιλεῖς καὶ ἐν τοῖς ψεύδεσιν αὐτῶν
ἄρχοντας

4 πάντες μοιχεύοντες ὡς κλίβανος καιόμενος εἰς πέψιν κατακαύματος ἀπὸ
τῆς φλογός ἀπὸ φυράσεως στέατος ἕως τοῦ ζυμωθῆναι αὐτό

5 αἱ ἡμέραι τῶν βασιλέων ὑμῶν ἤρξαντο οἱ ἄρχοντες θυμοῦσθαι ἐξ οἴνου
ἐξέτεινεν τὴν χεῖρα αὐτοῦ μετὰ λοιμῶν

6 διότι ἀνεκαύθησαν ὡς κλίβανος αἱ καρδίαι αὐτῶν ἐν τῷ καταράσσειν
αὐτούς ὅλην τὴν νύκτα ὕπνου Εφραιμ ἐνεπλήσθη πρωὶ ἐγενήθη ἀνεκαύθη ὡς
πυρὸς φέγγος

7 πάντες ἐθερμάνθησαν ὡς κλίβανος καὶ κατέφαγον τοὺς κριτὰς αὐτῶν πάντες
οἱ βασιλεῖς αὐτῶν ἔπεσαν οὐκ ἦν ὁ ἐπικαλούμενος ἐν αὐτοῖς πρός με

8 Εφραιμ ἐν τοῖς λαοῖς αὐτοῦ συνανεμείγνυτο Εφραιμ ἐγένετο ἐγκρυφίας οὐ
μεταστρεφόμενος

9 κατέφαγον ἀλλότριοι τὴν ἰσχὺν αὐτοῦ αὐτὸς δὲ οὐκ ἐπέγνω καὶ πολιαὶ
ἐξήνθησαν αὐτῷ καὶ αὐτὸς οὐκ ἔγνω

10 καὶ ταπεινωθήσεται ἡ ὕβρις Ισραηλ εἰς πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐκ
ἐπέστρεψαν πρὸς κύριον τὸν θεὸν αὐτῶν καὶ οὐκ ἐξεζήτησαν αὐτὸν ἐν πᾶσι
τούτοις

11 καὶ ἦν Εφραιμ ὡς περιστερὰ ἄνους οὐκ ἔχουσα καρδίαν Αἴγυπτον
ἐπεκαλεῖτο καὶ εἰς Ἀσσυρίους ἐπορεύθησαν

12 καθὼς ἂν πορεύωνται ἐπιβαλῶ ἐπ᾽ αὐτοὺς τὸ δίκτυόν μου καθὼς τὰ
πετεινὰ τοῦ οὐρανοῦ κατάξω αὐτούς παιδεύσω αὐτοὺς ἐν τῇ ἀκοῇ τῆς θλίψεως
αὐτῶν

13 οὐαὶ αὐτοῖς ὅτι ἀπεπήδησαν ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ δείλαιοί εἰσιν ὅτι ἠσέβησαν εἰς
ἐμέ ἐγὼ δὲ ἐλυτρωσάμην αὐτούς αὐτοὶ δὲ κατελάλησαν κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ ψεύδη

14 καὶ οὐκ ἐβόησαν πρός με αἱ καρδίαι αὐτῶν ἀλλ᾽ ἢ ὠλόλυζον ἐν ταῖς
κοίταις αὐτῶν ἐπὶ σίτῳ καὶ οἴνῳ κατετέμνοντο ἐπαιδεύθησαν ἐν ἐμοί

15 κἀγὼ κατίσχυσα τοὺς βραχίονας αὐτῶν καὶ εἰς ἐμὲ ἐλογίσαντο πονηρά

16 ἀπεστράφησαν εἰς οὐθέν ἐγένοντο ὡς τόξον ἐντεταμένον πεσοῦνται ἐν
ῥομφαίᾳ οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῶν δι᾽ ἀπαιδευσίαν γλώσσης αὐτῶν οὗτος ὁ
φαυλισμὸς αὐτῶν ἐν γῇ Αἰγύπτῳ

1 \[Cum sanare vellem Israël,  
revelata est iniquitas Ephraim,  
et malitia Samariæ,  
quia operati sunt mendacium;  
et fur ingressus est spolians,  
latrunculus foris.  

2 Et ne forte dicant in cordibus suis,  
omnem malitiam eorum me recordatum,  
nunc circumdederunt eos adinventiones suæ:  
coram facie mea factæ sunt.  

3 In malitia sua lætificaverunt regem,  
et in mendaciis suis principes.  

4 Omnes adulterantes,  
quasi clibanus succensus a coquente;  
quievit paululum civitas a commistione fermenti,  
donec fermentaretur totum.  

5 Dies regis nostri: cœperunt principes furere a vino;  
extendit manum suam cum illusoribus.  

6 Quia applicuerunt quasi clibanum cor suum,  
cum insidiaretur eis;  
tota nocte dormivit coquens eos:  
mane ipse succensus quasi ignis flammæ.  

7 Omnes calefacti sunt quasi clibanus,  
et devoraverunt judices suos:  
omnes reges eorum ceciderunt;  
non est qui clamat in eis ad me.  

8 Ephraim in populis ipse commiscebatur;  
Ephraim factus est subcinericius panis, qui non reversatur.  

9 Comederunt alieni robur ejus, et ipse nescivit;  
sed et cani effusi sunt in eo, et ipse ignoravit.  

10 Et humiliabitur superbia Israël in facie ejus;  
nec reversi sunt ad Dominum Deum suum,  
et non quæsierunt eum in omnibus his.  

11 Et factus est Ephraim quasi columba seducta non habens cor.  
Ægyptum invocabant; ad Assyrios abierunt.  

12 Et cum profecti fuerint,  
expandam super eos rete meum:  
quasi volucrem cæli detraham eos;  
cædam eos secundum auditionem cœtus eorum.  

13 Væ eis, quoniam recesserunt a me!  
vastabuntur, quia prævaricati sunt in me,  
et ego redemi eos,  
et ipsi locuti sunt contra me mendacia.  

14 Et non clamaverunt ad me in corde suo,  
sed ululabant in cubilibus suis:  
super triticum et vinum ruminabant;  
recesserunt a me.  

15 Et ego erudivi eos, et confortavi brachia eorum,  
et in me cogitaverunt malitiam.  

16 Reversi sunt ut essent absque jugo;  
facti sunt quasi arcus dolosus:  
cadent in gladio principes eorum, a furore linguæ suæ.  
Ista subsannatio eorum in terra Ægypti.\]
