H898  
בּגד  
בָּגַד ‎ bâgad  
*baw-gad‘*  
A primitive root; to *cover* (with a garment); figuratively to *act*
*covertly*; by implication to *pillage: -* deal deceitfully
(treacherously, unfaithfully), offend, transgress (-or), (depart),
treacherous (dealer, -ly, man), unfaithful (-ly, man), X very.
