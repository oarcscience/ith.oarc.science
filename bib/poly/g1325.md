G1325  
δίδωμι  
didōmi  
*did‘-o-mee*  
A prolonged form of a primary verb (which is used as an alternate in
most of the tenses); to *give* (used in a very wide application,
properly or by implication, literally or figuratively; greatly modified
by the connection): - adventure, bestow, bring forth, commit, deliver
(up), give, grant, hinder, make, minister, number, offer, have power,
put, receive, set, shew, smite (+ with the hand), strike (+ with the
palm of the hand), suffer, take, utter, yield.
