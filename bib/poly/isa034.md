### Isaiah 34

[KJV](../kjv/isa034)

1  Come near,<sup>[7126](h7126)</sup> ye
nations,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> to hear;<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> and
hearken,<sup>[7181](h7181)</sup> ye people:<sup>[3816](h3816)</sup> let

that is therein;<sup>[4393](h4393)</sup> the
world,<sup>[8398](h8398)</sup> and all things<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
that come forth<sup>[6631](h6631)</sup> of it.

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the indignation<sup>[7110](h7110)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> nations,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> and his
fury<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> their armies:<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> he
hath utterly destroyed<sup>[2763](h2763)</sup> them, he hath
delivered<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> them to the
slaughter.<sup>[2874](h2874)</sup>

3  Their slain<sup>[2491](h2491)</sup> also shall be cast
out,<sup>[7993](h7993)</sup> and their stink<sup>[889](h0889)</sup>
shall come up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> out of their
carcasses,<sup>[6297](h6297)</sup> and the

melted<sup>[4549](h4549)</sup> with their blood.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup>

4  And all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the host<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> of
heaven<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> shall be
dissolved,<sup>[4743](h4743)</sup> and the

falling<sup>[5034](h5034)</sup> fig from the fig

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my sword<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> shall be

down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>

people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> of my curse,<sup>[2764](h2764)</sup> to
judgment.<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup>

6  The sword<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
is filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> with blood,<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> it
is made fat<sup>[1878](h1878)</sup> with
fatness,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2459](h2459)</sup> and with the
blood<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> of

the fat<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2459](h2459)</sup> of the

sacrifice<sup>[2077](h2077)</sup> in Bozrah,<sup>[1224](h1224)</sup> and
a great<sup>[1419](h1419)</sup> slaughter<sup>[2874](h2874)</sup> in the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of Idumea.<sup>[123](h0123)</sup>

7  And the unicorns<sup>[7214](h7214)</sup> shall come
down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> them, and the
bullocks<sup>[6499](h6499)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the
bulls;<sup>[47](h0047)</sup> and their land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> shall
be soaked<sup>[7301](h7301)</sup> with blood,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup> and their dust<sup>[6083](h6083)</sup> made

<sup>[2459](h2459)</sup>

8  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> it is the day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of
the LORD's<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> vengeance,<sup>[5359](h5359)</sup>
and the year<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> of
recompenses<sup>[7966](h7966)</sup> for the
controversy<sup>[7379](h7379)</sup> of Zion.<sup>[6726](h6726)</sup>

9  And the streams<sup>[5158](h5158)</sup> thereof shall be
turned<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup> into pitch,<sup>[2203](h2203)</sup> and
the dust<sup>[6083](h6083)</sup> thereof into
brimstone,<sup>[1614](h1614)</sup> and the land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>
thereof shall become<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>

10  It shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be
quenched<sup>[3518](h3518)</sup> night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> nor

generation<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> it shall lie

ever.<sup>[5331](h5331)</sup>

11  But the cormorant<sup>[6893](h6893)</sup> and the
bittern<sup>[7090](h7090)</sup> shall possess<sup>[3423](h3423)</sup>
it; the owl<sup>[3244](h3244)</sup> also and the
raven<sup>[6158](h6158)</sup> shall dwell<sup>[7931](h7931)</sup> in it:
and he shall stretch out<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> it the line<sup>[6957](h6957)</sup> of
confusion,<sup>[8414](h8414)</sup> and the stones<sup>[68](h0068)</sup>
of emptiness.<sup>[922](h0922)</sup>

12  They shall call<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> the
nobles<sup>[2715](h2715)</sup> thereof to the
kingdom,<sup>[4410](h4410)</sup> but none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> shall
be there,<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> and all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> her
princes<sup>[8269](h8269)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
nothing.<sup>[657](h0657)</sup>

13  And thorns<sup>[5518](h5518)</sup> shall come
up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> in her palaces,<sup>[759](h0759)</sup>
nettles<sup>[7057](h7057)</sup> and brambles<sup>[2336](h2336)</sup> in
the fortresses<sup>[4013](h4013)</sup> thereof: and it shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a habitation<sup>[5116](h5116)</sup> of
dragons,<sup>[8565](h8565)</sup> and a court<sup>[2681](h2681)</sup> for
owls.<sup>[1323](h1323)</sup> <sup>[3284](h3284)</sup>

14  The wild beasts of the desert<sup>[6728](h6728)</sup> shall also
meet<sup>[6298](h6298)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the wild beasts
of the island,<sup>[338](h0338)</sup> and the
satyr<sup>[8163](h8163)</sup> shall cry<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup>
to<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> his fellow;<sup>[7453](h7453)</sup> the
screech owl<sup>[3917](h3917)</sup> also<sup>[389](h0389)</sup> shall
rest<sup>[7280](h7280)</sup> there,<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> and
find<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> for herself a place of
rest.<sup>[4494](h4494)</sup>

15  There<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> shall the great
owl<sup>[7091](h7091)</sup> make her nest,<sup>[7077](h7077)</sup> and
lay,<sup>[4422](h4422)</sup> and hatch,<sup>[1234](h1234)</sup> and
gather<sup>[1716](h1716)</sup> under her shadow:<sup>[6738](h6738)</sup>
there<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> shall the vultures<sup>[1772](h1772)</sup>
also<sup>[389](h0389)</sup> be gathered,<sup>[6908](h6908)</sup> every
one<sup>[802](h0802)</sup> with her mate.<sup>[7468](h7468)</sup>

16  Seek<sup>[1875](h1875)</sup> ye out of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the book<sup>[5612](h5612)</sup> of the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and read:<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup>
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> one<sup>[259](h0259)</sup> of
these<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2007](h2007)</sup> shall
fail,<sup>[5737](h5737)</sup> none<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
<sup>[802](h0802)</sup> shall want<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> her
mate:<sup>[7468](h7468)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my

commanded,<sup>[6680](h6680)</sup> and his
spirit<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> it<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> hath

17  And he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> hath cast<sup>[5307](h5307)</sup> the
lot<sup>[1486](h1486)</sup> for them, and his

unto them by line:<sup>[6957](h6957)</sup> they shall
possess<sup>[3423](h3423)</sup> it forever,<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> from generation<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> to

[Sep](../sep/isa034)

1 προσαγάγετε ἔθνη καὶ ἀκούσατε ἄρχοντες ἀκουσάτω ἡ γῆ καὶ οἱ ἐν αὐτῇ ἡ
οἰκουμένη καὶ ὁ λαὸς ὁ ἐν αὐτῇ

2 διότι θυμὸς κυρίου ἐπὶ πάντα τὰ ἔθνη καὶ ὀργὴ ἐπὶ τὸν ἀριθμὸν αὐτῶν
τοῦ ἀπολέσαι αὐτοὺς καὶ παραδοῦναι αὐτοὺς εἰς σφαγήν

3 οἱ δὲ τραυματίαι αὐτῶν ῥιφήσονται καὶ οἱ νεκροί καὶ ἀναβήσεται αὐτῶν ἡ
ὀσμή καὶ βραχήσεται τὰ ὄρη ἀπὸ τοῦ αἵματος αὐτῶν

4 καὶ ἑλιγήσεται ὁ οὐρανὸς ὡς βιβλίον καὶ πάντα τὰ ἄστρα πεσεῖται ὡς
φύλλα ἐξ ἀμπέλου καὶ ὡς πίπτει φύλλα ἀπὸ συκῆς

5 ἐμεθύσθη ἡ μάχαιρά μου ἐν τῷ οὐρανῷ ἰδοὺ ἐπὶ τὴν Ιδουμαίαν καταβήσεται
καὶ ἐπὶ τὸν λαὸν τῆς ἀπωλείας μετὰ κρίσεως

6 ἡ μάχαιρα κυρίου ἐνεπλήσθη αἵματος ἐπαχύνθη ἀπὸ στέατος ἀρνῶν καὶ ἀπὸ
στέατος τράγων καὶ κριῶν ὅτι θυσία κυρίῳ ἐν Βοσορ καὶ σφαγὴ μεγάλη ἐν τῇ
Ιδουμαίᾳ

7 καὶ συμπεσοῦνται οἱ ἁδροὶ μετ᾽ αὐτῶν καὶ οἱ κριοὶ καὶ οἱ ταῦροι καὶ
μεθυσθήσεται ἡ γῆ ἀπὸ τοῦ αἵματος καὶ ἀπὸ τοῦ στέατος αὐτῶν
ἐμπλησθήσεται

8 ἡμέρα γὰρ κρίσεως κυρίου καὶ ἐνιαυτὸς ἀνταποδόσεως κρίσεως Σιων

9 καὶ στραφήσονται αὐτῆς αἱ φάραγγες εἰς πίσσαν καὶ ἡ γῆ αὐτῆς εἰς θεῖον
καὶ ἔσται αὐτῆς ἡ γῆ καιομένη ὡς πίσσα

10 νυκτὸς καὶ ἡμέρας καὶ οὐ σβεσθήσεται εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα χρόνον καὶ
ἀναβήσεται ὁ καπνὸς αὐτῆς ἄνω εἰς γενεὰς ἐρημωθήσεται καὶ εἰς χρόνον
πολύν

11 καὶ κατοικήσουσιν ἐν αὐτῇ ὄρνεα καὶ ἐχῖνοι καὶ ἴβεις καὶ κόρακες καὶ
ἐπιβληθήσεται ἐπ᾽ αὐτὴν σπαρτίον γεωμετρίας ἐρήμου καὶ ὀνοκένταυροι
οἰκήσουσιν ἐν αὐτῇ

12 οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῆς οὐκ ἔσονται οἱ γὰρ βασιλεῖς αὐτῆς καὶ οἱ ἄρχοντες
αὐτῆς καὶ οἱ μεγιστᾶνες αὐτῆς ἔσονται εἰς ἀπώλειαν

13 καὶ ἀναφύσει εἰς τὰς πόλεις αὐτῶν ἀκάνθινα ξύλα καὶ εἰς τὰ ὀχυρώματα
αὐτῆς καὶ ἔσται ἔπαυλις σειρήνων καὶ αὐλὴ στρουθῶν

14 καὶ συναντήσουσιν δαιμόνια ὀνοκενταύροις καὶ βοήσουσιν ἕτερος πρὸς
τὸν ἕτερον ἐκεῖ ἀναπαύσονται ὀνοκένταυροι εὗρον γὰρ αὑτοῖς ἀνάπαυσιν

15 ἐκεῖ ἐνόσσευσεν ἐχῖνος καὶ ἔσωσεν ἡ γῆ τὰ παιδία αὐτῆς μετὰ ἀσφαλείας
ἐκεῖ ἔλαφοι συνήντησαν καὶ εἶδον τὰ πρόσωπα ἀλλήλων

16 ἀριθμῷ παρῆλθον καὶ μία αὐτῶν οὐκ ἀπώλετο ἑτέρα τὴν ἑτέραν οὐκ
ἐζήτησαν ὅτι κύριος ἐνετείλατο αὐτοῖς καὶ τὸ πνεῦμα αὐτοῦ συνήγαγεν
αὐτάς

17 καὶ αὐτὸς ἐπιβαλεῖ αὐτοῖς κλήρους καὶ ἡ χεὶρ αὐτοῦ διεμέρισεν
βόσκεσθαι εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα χρόνον κληρονομήσετε εἰς γενεὰς γενεῶν
ἀναπαύσονται ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς

[Vul](../vul/isa034)

1 \[Accedite, gentes, et audite;  
et populi, attendite:  
audiat terra, et plenitudo ejus;  
orbis, et omne germen ejus.  

2 Quia indignatio Domini super omnes gentes,  
et furor super universam militiam eorum:  
interfecit eos, et dedit eos in occisionem.  

3 Interfecti eorum projicientur,  
et de cadaveribus eorum ascendet fœtor;  
tabescent montes a sanguine eorum.  

4 Et tabescet omnis militia cælorum,  
et complicabuntur sicut liber cæli:  
et omnis militia eorum defluet,  
sicut defluit folium de vinea et de ficu.  

5 Quoniam inebriatus est in cælo gladius meus;  
ecce super Idumæam descendet,  
et super populum interfectionis meæ, ad judicium.  

6 Gladius Domini repletus est sanguine,  
incrassatus est adipe,  
de sanguine agnorum et hircorum,  
de sanguine medullatorum arietum:  
victima enim Domini in Bosra,  
et interfectio magna in terra Edom.  

7 Et descendent unicornes cum eis,  
et tauri cum potentibus;  
inebriabitur terra eorum sanguine,  
et humus eorum adipe pinguium.  

8 Quia dies ultionis Domini,  
annus retributionum judicii Sion.  

9 Et convertentur torrentes ejus in picem,  
et humus ejus in sulphur;  
et erit terra ejus in picem ardentem.  

10 Nocte et die non extinguetur,  
in sempiternum ascendet fumus ejus,  
a generatione in generationem desolabitur,  
in sæcula sæculorum non erit transiens per eam.  

11 Et possidebunt illam onocrotalus et ericius;  
ibis et corvus habitabunt in ea:  
et extendetur super eam mensura, ut redigatur ad nihilum,  
et perpendiculum in desolationem.  

12 Nobiles ejus non erunt ibi; regem potius invocabunt,  
et omnes principes ejus erunt in nihilum.  

13 Et orientur in domibus ejus spinæ et urticæ,  
et paliurus in munitionibus ejus;  
et erit cubile draconum,  
et pascua struthionum.  

14 Et occurrent dæmonia onocentauris,  
et pilosus clamabit alter ad alterum;  
ibi cubavit lamia,  
et invenit sibi requiem.  

15 Ibi habuit foveam ericius, et enutrivit catulos,  
et circumfodit, et fovit in umbra ejus;  
illuc congregati sunt milvi, alter ad alterum.  

16 Requirite diligenter in libro Domini, et legite:  
Unum ex eis non defuit,  
alter alterum non quæsivit;  
quia quod ex ore meo procedit, ille mandavit,  
et spiritus ejus ipse congregavit ea.  

17 Et ipse misit eis sortem,  
et manus ejus divisit eam illis in mensuram:  
usque in æternum possidebunt eam;  
in generationem et generationem habitabunt in ea.\]
