### Hosea 5

[KJV](../kjv/hos005)

1  Hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> ye this,<sup>[2063](h2063)</sup> O
priests;<sup>[3548](h3548)</sup> and hearken,<sup>[7181](h7181)</sup> ye
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Israel;<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> and
give ye ear,<sup>[238](h0238)</sup> O house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of
the king;<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
judgment<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> is toward you,
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> ye have been<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a
snare<sup>[6341](h6341)</sup> on Mizpah,<sup>[4709](h4709)</sup> and a
net<sup>[7568](h7568)</sup> spread<sup>[6566](h6566)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> Tabor.<sup>[8396](h8396)</sup>

2  And the revolters<sup>[7846](h7846)</sup> are
profound<sup>[6009](h6009)</sup> to make
slaughter,<sup>[7819](h7819)</sup> though I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> have
been a rebuker<sup>[4148](h4148)</sup> of them
all.<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>

3  I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup>
Ephraim,<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> and Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> is
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> hid<sup>[3582](h3582)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> me: for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
now,<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> O Ephraim,<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> thou
committest whoredom,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> and
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> is defiled.<sup>[2930](h2930)</sup>

4  They will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> frame<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
their doings<sup>[4611](h4611)</sup> to turn<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> their God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the spirit<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> of
whoredoms<sup>[2183](h2183)</sup> is in the
midst<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> of them, and they have
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> known<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

5  And the pride<sup>[1347](h1347)</sup> of
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> doth testify<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> to
his face:<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> therefore shall
Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> and Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup>
fall<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup> in their iniquity;<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>
Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> also<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> shall
fall<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> them.

6  They shall go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> with their
flocks<sup>[6629](h6629)</sup> and with their
herds<sup>[1241](h1241)</sup> to seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> but they shall
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> find<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> him; he hath
withdrawn<sup>[2502](h2502)</sup> himself from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
them.

7  They have dealt treacherously<sup>[898](h0898)</sup> against the
LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they have
begotten<sup>[3205](h3205)</sup> strange<sup>[2114](h2114)</sup>
children:<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> now<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> shall a
month<sup>[2320](h2320)</sup> devour<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> them
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> their portions.<sup>[2506](h2506)</sup>

8  Blow<sup>[8628](h8628)</sup> ye the cornet<sup>[7782](h7782)</sup> in
Gibeah,<sup>[1390](h1390)</sup> and the trumpet<sup>[2689](h2689)</sup>
in Ramah:<sup>[7414](h7414)</sup> cry aloud<sup>[7321](h7321)</sup> at
Beth-aven,<sup>[1007](h1007)</sup> after<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> thee, O
Benjamin.<sup>[1144](h1144)</sup>

9  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
desolate<sup>[8047](h8047)</sup> in the day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of
rebuke:<sup>[8433](h8433)</sup> among the tribes<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup>
of Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> have I made
known<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> that which shall surely
be.<sup>[539](h0539)</sup>

10  The princes<sup>[8269](h8269)</sup> of Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup>
were<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like them that
remove<sup>[5253](h5253)</sup> the bound:<sup>[1366](h1366)</sup>
therefore I will pour out<sup>[8210](h8210)</sup> my
wrath<sup>[5678](h5678)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> them like
water.<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup>

11  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> is oppressed<sup>[6231](h6231)</sup>
and broken<sup>[7533](h7533)</sup> in judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he willingly<sup>[2974](h2974)</sup>
walked<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> after<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> the
commandment.<sup>[6673](h6673)</sup>

12  Therefore will I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> be unto
Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> as a moth,<sup>[6211](h6211)</sup> and to
the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> as
rottenness.<sup>[7538](h7538)</sup>

13  When Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> saw<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> his sickness,<sup>[2483](h2483)</sup> and
Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> saw <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> his
wound,<sup>[4205](h4205)</sup> then went<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup>
Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the
Assyrian,<sup>[804](h0804)</sup> and sent<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup>
Jareb:<sup>[3377](h3377)</sup> yet could<sup>[3201](h3201)</sup>
he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
heal<sup>[7495](h7495)</sup> you, nor<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
cure<sup>[1455](h1455)</sup> <sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> you of your
wound.<sup>[4205](h4205)</sup>

14  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup> will be unto
Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> as a lion,<sup>[7826](h7826)</sup> and as
a young lion<sup>[3715](h3715)</sup> to the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Judah:<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup>
I,<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> even I,<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> will
tear<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> and go away;<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> I will
take away,<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> and none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> shall
rescue<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> him.

15  I will go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> and return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> my place,<sup>[4725](h4725)</sup>
till<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[834](h0834)</sup> they acknowledge
their offense,<sup>[816](h0816)</sup> and seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup>
my face:<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> in their
affliction<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> they will seek me
early.<sup>[7836](h7836)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/hos005)

1 ἀκούσατε ταῦτα οἱ ἱερεῖς καὶ προσέχετε οἶκος Ισραηλ καὶ ὁ οἶκος τοῦ
βασιλέως ἐνωτίζεσθε διότι πρὸς ὑμᾶς ἐστιν τὸ κρίμα ὅτι παγὶς ἐγενήθητε
τῇ σκοπιᾷ καὶ ὡς δίκτυον ἐκτεταμένον ἐπὶ τὸ ἰταβύριον

2 ὃ οἱ ἀγρεύοντες τὴν θήραν κατέπηξαν ἐγὼ δὲ παιδευτὴς ὑμῶν

3 ἐγὼ ἔγνων τὸν Εφραιμ καὶ Ισραηλ οὐκ ἄπεστιν ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ διότι νῦν
ἐξεπόρνευσεν Εφραιμ ἐμιάνθη Ισραηλ

4 οὐκ ἔδωκαν τὰ διαβούλια αὐτῶν τοῦ ἐπιστρέψαι πρὸς τὸν θεὸν αὐτῶν ὅτι
πνεῦμα πορνείας ἐν αὐτοῖς ἐστιν τὸν δὲ κύριον οὐκ ἐπέγνωσαν

5 καὶ ταπεινωθήσεται ἡ ὕβρις τοῦ Ισραηλ εἰς πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ καὶ Ισραηλ
καὶ Εφραιμ ἀσθενήσουσιν ἐν ταῖς ἀδικίαις αὐτῶν καὶ ἀσθενήσει καὶ Ιουδας
μετ᾽ αὐτῶν

6 μετὰ προβάτων καὶ μόσχων πορεύσονται τοῦ ἐκζητῆσαι τὸν κύριον καὶ οὐ
μὴ εὕρωσιν αὐτόν ὅτι ἐξέκλινεν ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν

7 ὅτι τὸν κύριον ἐγκατέλιπον ὅτι τέκνα ἀλλότρια ἐγεννήθησαν αὐτοῖς νῦν
καταφάγεται αὐτοὺς ἡ ἐρυσίβη καὶ τοὺς κλήρους αὐτῶν

8 σαλπίσατε σάλπιγγι ἐπὶ τοὺς βουνούς ἠχήσατε ἐπὶ τῶν ὑψηλῶν κηρύξατε ἐν
τῷ οἴκῳ Ων ἐξέστη Βενιαμιν

9 Εφραιμ εἰς ἀφανισμὸν ἐγένετο ἐν ἡμέραις ἐλέγχου ἐν ταῖς φυλαῖς τοῦ
Ισραηλ ἔδειξα πιστά

10 ἐγένοντο οἱ ἄρχοντες Ιουδα ὡς μετατιθέντες ὅρια ἐπ᾽ αὐτοὺς ἐκχεῶ ὡς
ὕδωρ τὸ ὅρμημά μου

11 κατεδυνάστευσεν Εφραιμ τὸν ἀντίδικον αὐτοῦ κατεπάτησεν κρίμα ὅτι
ἤρξατο πορεύεσθαι ὀπίσω τῶν ματαίων

12 καὶ ἐγὼ ὡς ταραχὴ τῷ Εφραιμ καὶ ὡς κέντρον τῷ οἴκῳ Ιουδα

13 καὶ εἶδεν Εφραιμ τὴν νόσον αὐτοῦ καὶ Ιουδας τὴν ὀδύνην αὐτοῦ καὶ
ἐπορεύθη Εφραιμ πρὸς Ἀσσυρίους καὶ ἀπέστειλεν πρέσβεις πρὸς βασιλέα
Ιαριμ καὶ αὐτὸς οὐκ ἠδυνάσθη ἰάσασθαι ὑμᾶς καὶ οὐ μὴ διαπαύσῃ ἐξ ὑμῶν
ὀδύνη

14 διότι ἐγώ εἰμι ὡς πανθὴρ τῷ Εφραιμ καὶ ὡς λέων τῷ οἴκῳ Ιουδα καὶ ἐγὼ
ἁρπῶμαι καὶ πορεύσομαι καὶ λήμψομαι καὶ οὐκ ἔσται ὁ ἐξαιρούμενος

15 πορεύσομαι καὶ ἐπιστρέψω εἰς τὸν τόπον μου ἕως οὗ ἀφανισθῶσιν καὶ
ἐπιζητήσουσιν τὸ πρόσωπόν μου ἐν θλίψει αὐτῶν ὀρθριοῦσι πρός με λέγοντες

[Vul](../vul/hos005)

1 \[Audite hoc, sacerdotes,  
et attendite, domus Israël,  
et domus regis, auscultate:  
quia vobis judicium est,  
quoniam laqueus facti estis speculationi,  
et rete expansum super Thabor.  

2 Et victimas declinastis in profundum;  
et ego eruditor omnium eorum.  

3 Ego scio Ephraim,  
et Israël non est absconditus a me:  
quia nunc fornicatus est Ephraim;  
contaminatus est Israël.  

4 Non dabunt cogitationes suas ut revertantur ad Deum suum,  
quia spiritus fornicationum in medio eorum,  
et Dominum non cognoverunt.  

5 Et respondebit arrogantia Israël in facie ejus,  
et Israël et Ephraim ruent in iniquitate sua:  
ruet etiam Judas cum eis.  

6 In gregibus suis et in armentis suis vadent ad quærendum Dominum,  
et non invenient:  
ablatus est ab eis.  

7 In Dominum prævaricati sunt,  
quia filios alienos genuerunt:  
nunc devorabit eos mensis, cum partibus suis.  

8 Clangite buccina in Gabaa,  
tuba in Rama;  
ululate in Bethaven,  
post tergum tuum, Benjamin.  

9 Ephraim in desolatione erit in die correptionis;  
in tribubus Israël ostendi fidem.  

10 Facti sunt principes Juda quasi assumentes terminum;  
super eos effundam quasi aquam iram meam.  

11 Calumniam patiens est Ephraim, fractus judicio,  
quoniam cœpit abire post sordes.  

12 Et ego quasi tinea Ephraim,  
et quasi putredo domui Juda.  

13 Et vidit Ephraim languorem suum,  
et Juda vinculum suum;  
et abiit Ephraim ad Assur,  
et misit ad regem ultorem:  
et ipse non poterit sanare vos,  
nec solvere poterit a vobis vinculum.  

14 Quoniam ego quasi leæna Ephraim,  
et quasi catulus leonis domui Juda.  
Ego, ego capiam, et vadam;  
tollam, et non est qui eruat.  

15 Vadens revertar ad locum meum,  
donec deficiatis, et quæratis faciem meam.\]
