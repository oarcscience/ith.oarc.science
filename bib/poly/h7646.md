H7646  
שׂבע שׂבע  
שָׂבַע שָׂבֵַע ‎ śâba‛ śâbêa‛  
*saw-bah‘,* *saw-bay‘-ah*  
A primitive root; to *sate*, that is, *fill* to satisfaction (literally
or figuratively): - have enough, fill (full, self, with), be (to the)
full (of), have plenty of, be satiate, satisfy (with), suffice, be weary
of.
