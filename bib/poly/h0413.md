H413  
אל אל  
אֵל אֶל ‎ ‘êl ‘el  
*ale,* *el*  
(Used only in the shortened constructive form (the second form)); a
primitive particle, properly denoting motion *towards*, but occasionally
used of a quiescent position, that is, *near*, *with* or *among*; often
in general, *to: -* about, according to, after, against, among, as for,
at, because (-fore, -side), both . . . and, by, concerning, for, from, X
hath, in (-to), near, (out) of, over, through,to (-ward), under, unto,
upon, whether, with(-in).
