G2680  
κατασκευάζω  
kataskeuazō  
*kat-ask-yoo-ad‘-zo*  
From [2596](g2596) and a derivative of [4632](g4632.md) to *prepare*
*thoroughly* (properly by external *equipment*; whereas [2090](g2090.md)
refers rather to internal *fitness*); by implication to *construct*,
*create:* - build, make, ordain, prepare.
