H833  
אשׁר אשׁר  
אָשַׁר אָשֵׁר ‎ ‘âshar ‘âshêr  
*aw-shar‘,* *aw-share‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* *straight* (used in the widest sense,
especially to *be* *level*, *right*, *happy*); figuratively to *go*
*forward*, *be* *honest*, *prosper: -* (call, be) bless (-ed, happy),
go, guide, lead, relieve.
