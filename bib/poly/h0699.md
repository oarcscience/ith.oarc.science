H699  
ארבּה  
אֲרֻבָּה ‎ ‘ărubbâh  
*ar-oob-baw‘*  
Feminine participle passive of [693](h0693) (as if for *lurking*); a
*lattice*; (by implication) a *window*, *dove* *cot* (because of the
pigeon holes), *chimney* (with its apertures for smoke), *sluice* (with
openings for water): - chimney, window.
