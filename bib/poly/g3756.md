G3756  
οὐ  
ou  
*oo*  
Also οὐκ ouk *ook* used before a vowel and οὐχ ouch *ookh* before an
aspirate. A primary word; the absolutely negative (compare [3361](g3361.md)
adverb; *no* or *not*: - + long, nay, neither, never, no (X man), none,
\[can-\] not, + nothing, + special, un (\[-worthy\]), when, + without, +
yet but. See also [3364](g3364) [3372](g3372.md)
