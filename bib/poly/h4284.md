H4284  
מחשׁבת מחשׁבה  
מַחֲשָׁבָה מַחֲשֶׁבֶת ‎ machăshâbâh machăshebeth  
*makh-ash-aw-baw‘,* *makh-ash-eh‘-beth*  
From [2803](h2803) a *contrivance*, that is, (concretely) a *texture*,
*machine*, or (abstractly) *intention*, *plan* (whether bad, a *plot*;
or good, *advice*): - cunning (work), curious work, device (-sed),
imagination, invented, means, purpose, thought.
