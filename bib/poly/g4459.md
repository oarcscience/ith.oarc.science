G4459  
πῶς  
pōs  
*poce*  
Adverb from the base of [4226](g4226) an interrogitive particle of
manner; *in* *what* *way?* (sometimes the question is indirect, *how?*);
also as exclamation, *how* much!: - how, after (by) what manner (means),
that. \[*Occasionally* *unexpressed* *in* *English*.\]
