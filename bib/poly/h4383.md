מכשׁל מכשׁול  
מִכשׁוֹל מִכשׁוֹל ‎ mikshôl mikshôl  
*mik-shole‘,* *mik-shole‘*  
Masculine from [3782](h3782) a *stumblingblock*, literally or
figuratively (*obstacle*, *enticement* (specifically an idol),
*scruple*): - caused to fall, offence, X \[no-\] thing offered, ruin,
stumbling-block.
