H1511
גּרזי גּזרי
גִּזרִי גִּרזִי ‎ gizrı̂y girzı̂y
ghiz-ree‘, gher-zee‘
The first form is patrial from 1507 a Gezerite (collectively) or inhabitant of Gezer; but the second form is better (as in the text) by transposition and is patrial of 1630 a Girzite (collectively) or member of a native tribe in Palestine: - Gezrites.
