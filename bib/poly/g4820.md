G4820  
συμβάλλω  
sumballō  
*soom-bal‘-lo*  
From [4862](g4862) and [906](g0906) to *combine*, that is, (in speaking.md)
to *converse*, *consult*, *dispute*, (mentally) to *consider*, (by
implication) to *aid*, (personally) to *join*, *attack:* - confer,
encounter, help, make, meet with, ponder.
