H5352  
נקה  
נָקָה ‎ nâqâh  
*naw-kaw‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* (or *make*) *clean* (literally or
figuratively); by implication (in an adverse sense) to *be* *bare*, that
is, *extirpated: -* acquit X at all, X altogether, be blameless,
cleanse, (be) clear (-ing), cut off, be desolate, be free, be (hold)
guiltless, be (hold) innocent, X by no means, be quit, be (leave)
unpunished, X utterly, X wholly.
