### Psalms 25

[KJV](../kjv/psa025.md)

1  A Psalm of David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> Unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>
thee, O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> do I lift
up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> my soul.<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>

2  O my God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> I trust<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup> in
thee: let me not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> be
ashamed,<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> let not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> mine
enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> triumph<sup>[5970](h5970.md)</sup> over me.

3  Yea,<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> let none<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> that wait<sup>[6960](h6960.md)</sup> on thee be
ashamed:<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup> let them be
ashamed<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> which transgress<sup>[898](h0898.md)</sup>
without cause.<sup>[7387](h7387.md)</sup>

4  Show<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> me thy ways,<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> O
LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> teach<sup>[3925](h3925.md)</sup> me thy
paths.<sup>[734](h0734.md)</sup>

5  Lead<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> me in thy truth,<sup>[571](h0571.md)</sup>
and teach<sup>[3925](h3925)</sup> me: for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art the God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> of my
salvation;<sup>[3468](h3468.md)</sup> on thee do I
wait<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
day.<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>

6  Remember,<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> thy
tender mercies<sup>[7356](h7356.md)</sup> and thy
lovingkindnesses;<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
they<sup>[1992](h1992.md)</sup> have been ever of
old.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

7  Remember<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> the
sins<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup> of my youth,<sup>[5271](h5271.md)</sup> nor my
transgressions:<sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup> according to thy
mercy<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> remember<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup>
thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> me for thy goodness'
sake,<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> <sup>[2898](h2898.md)</sup> O
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

8  Good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> and upright<sup>[3477](h3477.md)</sup> is
the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> therefore<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> will he teach<sup>[3384](h3384.md)</sup>
sinners<sup>[2400](h2400)</sup> in the way.<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup>

9  The meek<sup>[6035](h6035.md)</sup> will he
guide<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> in judgment:<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup> and
the meek<sup>[6035](h6035)</sup> will he teach<sup>[3925](h3925.md)</sup>
his way.<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup>

10  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the paths<sup>[734](h0734.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> are mercy<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> and
truth<sup>[571](h0571)</sup> unto such as keep<sup>[5341](h5341.md)</sup>
his covenant<sup>[1285](h1285.md)</sup> and his
testimonies.<sup>[5713](h5713.md)</sup>

11  For thy name's sake,<sup>[4616](h4616.md)</sup>
<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
pardon<sup>[5545](h5545)</sup> mine iniquity;<sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> it<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> is
great.<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup>

12  What<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> man<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> is he
that<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> feareth<sup>[3373](h3373.md)</sup> the
LORD?<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> him shall he teach<sup>[3384](h3384.md)</sup>
in the way<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> that he shall
choose.<sup>[977](h0977.md)</sup>

13  His soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> shall dwell<sup>[3885](h3885.md)</sup>
at ease;<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> and his seed<sup>[2233](h2233.md)</sup>
shall inherit<sup>[3423](h3423)</sup> the earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

14  The secret<sup>[5475](h5475.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> is with them that
fear<sup>[3373](h3373.md)</sup> him; and he will
show<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> them his covenant.<sup>[1285](h1285.md)</sup>

15  Mine eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> are ever<sup>[8548](h8548.md)</sup>
toward<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> shall pluck my
feet out<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> <sup>[7272](h7272.md)</sup> of the
net.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7568](h7568.md)</sup>

16  Turn<sup>[6437](h6437)</sup> thee unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me,
and have mercy<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> upon me;
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> am
desolate<sup>[3173](h3173)</sup> and afflicted.<sup>[6041](h6041.md)</sup>

17  The troubles<sup>[6869](h6869.md)</sup> of my
heart<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup> are enlarged:<sup>[7337](h7337.md)</sup> O
bring thou me out<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup> of my
distresses.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4691](h4691.md)</sup>

18  Look upon<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> mine
affliction<sup>[6040](h6040)</sup> and my pain;<sup>[5999](h5999.md)</sup>
and forgive<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> my
sins.<sup>[2403](h2403.md)</sup>

19  Consider<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> mine
enemies;<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> they are
many;<sup>[7231](h7231)</sup> and they hate<sup>[8130](h8130.md)</sup> me
with cruel<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> hatred.<sup>[8135](h8135.md)</sup>

20  O keep<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> my soul,<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> and
deliver<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> me: let me not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> be
ashamed;<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> I put my
trust<sup>[2620](h2620.md)</sup> in thee.

21  Let integrity<sup>[8537](h8537.md)</sup> and
uprightness<sup>[3476](h3476)</sup> preserve<sup>[5341](h5341.md)</sup> me;
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I wait on<sup>[6960](h6960.md)</sup> thee.

22  Redeem<sup>[6299](h6299)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> O God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> out of
all<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> his
troubles.<sup>[6869](h6869.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa025.md)

1 τοῦ Δαυιδ κρῖνόν με κύριε ὅτι ἐγὼ ἐν ἀκακίᾳ μου ἐπορεύθην καὶ ἐπὶ τῷ
κυρίῳ ἐλπίζων οὐ μὴ ἀσθενήσω

2 δοκίμασόν με κύριε καὶ πείρασόν με πύρωσον τοὺς νεφρούς μου καὶ τὴν
καρδίαν μου

3 ὅτι τὸ ἔλεός σου κατέναντι τῶν ὀφθαλμῶν μού ἐστιν καὶ εὐηρέστησα ἐν τῇ
ἀληθείᾳ σου

4 οὐκ ἐκάθισα μετὰ συνεδρίου ματαιότητος καὶ μετὰ παρανομούντων οὐ μὴ
εἰσέλθω

5 ἐμίσησα ἐκκλησίαν πονηρευομένων καὶ μετὰ ἀσεβῶν οὐ μὴ καθίσω

6 νίψομαι ἐν ἀθῴοις τὰς χεῖράς μου καὶ κυκλώσω τὸ θυσιαστήριόν σου κύριε

7 τοῦ ἀκοῦσαι φωνὴν αἰνέσεως καὶ διηγήσασθαι πάντα τὰ θαυμάσιά σου

8 κύριε ἠγάπησα εὐπρέπειαν οἴκου σου καὶ τόπον σκηνώματος δόξης σου

9 μὴ συναπολέσῃς μετὰ ἀσεβῶν τὴν ψυχήν μου καὶ μετὰ ἀνδρῶν αἱμάτων τὴν
ζωήν μου

10 ὧν ἐν χερσὶν ἀνομίαι ἡ δεξιὰ αὐτῶν ἐπλήσθη δώρων

11 ἐγὼ δὲ ἐν ἀκακίᾳ μου ἐπορεύθην λύτρωσαί με καὶ ἐλέησόν με

12 ὁ γὰρ πούς μου ἔστη ἐν εὐθύτητι ἐν ἐκκλησίαις εὐλογήσω σε κύριε

[Vul](../vul/psa025.md)

1 In finem. Psalmus David. \[Judica me, Domine, quoniam ego in
innocentia mea ingressus sum,  
et in Domino sperans non infirmabor.  

2 Proba me, Domine, et tenta me;  
ure renes meos et cor meum.  

3 Quoniam misericordia tua ante oculos meos est,  
et complacui in veritate tua.  

4 Non sedi cum concilio vanitatis,  
et cum iniqua gerentibus non introibo.  

5 Odivi ecclesiam malignantium,  
et cum impiis non sedebo.  

6 Lavabo inter innocentes manus meas,  
et circumdabo altare tuum, Domine:  

7 ut audiam vocem laudis,  
et enarrem universa mirabilia tua.  

8 Domine, dilexi decorem domus tuæ,  
et locum habitationis gloriæ tuæ.  

9 Ne perdas cum impiis, Deus, animam meam,  
et cum viris sanguinum vitam meam:  

10 in quorum manibus iniquitates sunt;  
dextera eorum repleta est muneribus.  

11 Ego autem in innocentia mea ingressus sum;  
redime me, et miserere mei.  

12 Pes meus stetit in directo;  
in ecclesiis benedicam te, Domine.\]
