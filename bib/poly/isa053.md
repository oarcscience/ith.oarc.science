### Isaiah 53

[KJV](../kjv/isa053.md)

1  Who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> hath believed<sup>[539](h0539.md)</sup> our
report?<sup>[8052](h8052)</sup> and to<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
whom<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> is the arm<sup>[2220](h2220.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> revealed?<sup>[1540](h1540.md)</sup>

2  For he shall grow up<sup>[5927](h5927.md)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> him as a tender
plant,<sup>[3126](h3126)</sup> and as a root<sup>[8328](h8328.md)</sup> out
of a dry<sup>[6723](h6723)</sup> ground:<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> he hath no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
form<sup>[8389](h8389)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
comeliness;<sup>[1926](h1926.md)</sup> and when we shall
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> him, there is no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
beauty<sup>[4758](h4758.md)</sup> that we should
desire<sup>[2530](h2530.md)</sup> him.

3  He is despised<sup>[959](h0959.md)</sup> and
rejected<sup>[2310](h2310)</sup> of men;<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> a
man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> of sorrows,<sup>[4341](h4341.md)</sup> and
acquainted<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> with grief:<sup>[2483](h2483.md)</sup>
and we hid<sup>[4564](h4564.md)</sup> as it were our
faces<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> him; he was
despised,<sup>[959](h0959)</sup> and we esteemed<sup>[2803](h2803.md)</sup>
him not.<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

4  Surely<sup>[403](h0403)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> hath
borne<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> our griefs,<sup>[2483](h2483.md)</sup> and
carried<sup>[5445](h5445)</sup> our sorrows:<sup>[4341](h4341.md)</sup> yet
we<sup>[587](h0587)</sup> did esteem<sup>[2803](h2803.md)</sup> him
stricken,<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> smitten<sup>[5221](h5221.md)</sup> of
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> and afflicted.<sup>[6031](h6031.md)</sup>

5  But he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> was wounded<sup>[2490](h2490.md)</sup>
for our transgressions,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup>
he was bruised<sup>[1792](h1792.md)</sup> for our
iniquities:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup> the
chastisement<sup>[4148](h4148.md)</sup> of our
peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> was upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him; and
with his stripes<sup>[2250](h2250.md)</sup> we are
healed.<sup>[7495](h7495.md)</sup>

6  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> we like sheep<sup>[6629](h6629.md)</sup>
have gone astray;<sup>[8582](h8582.md)</sup> we have
turned<sup>[6437](h6437)</sup> every one<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> to his
own way;<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> and the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
hath laid<sup>[6293](h6293)</sup> on him <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
iniquity<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> of us all.<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>

7  He was oppressed,<sup>[5065](h5065.md)</sup> and
he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> was afflicted,<sup>[6031](h6031.md)</sup> yet he
opened<sup>[6605](h6605)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> his
mouth:<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> he is brought<sup>[2986](h2986.md)</sup> as
a lamb<sup>[7716](h7716)</sup> to the slaughter,<sup>[2874](h2874.md)</sup>
and as a sheep<sup>[7353](h7353)</sup> before<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup>
her shearers<sup>[1494](h1494)</sup> is dumb,<sup>[481](h0481.md)</sup> so
he openeth<sup>[6605](h6605)</sup> not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> his
mouth.<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup>

8  He was taken<sup>[3947](h3947.md)</sup> from
prison<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6115](h6115.md)</sup> and from
judgment:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup> and
who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> shall declare<sup>[7878](h7878.md)</sup> his
generation?<sup>[1755](h1755)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he was
cut off<sup>[1504](h1504)</sup> out of the land<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of the living:<sup>[2416](h2416.md)</sup> for the
transgression<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup> of my
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> was he stricken.<sup>[5061](h5061.md)</sup>

9  And he made<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> his grave<sup>[6913](h6913.md)</sup>
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the wicked,<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup> and
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the rich<sup>[6223](h6223.md)</sup> in his
death;<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> because<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> he had
done<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
violence,<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> neither<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> was
any deceit<sup>[4820](h4820)</sup> in his mouth.<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup>

10  Yet it pleased<sup>[2654](h2654.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> to bruise<sup>[1792](h1792.md)</sup> him; he
hath put him to grief:<sup>[2470](h2470.md)</sup>
when<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> thou shalt make<sup>[7760](h7760.md)</sup> his
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> an offering for sin,<sup>[817](h0817.md)</sup>
he shall see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> his seed,<sup>[2233](h2233.md)</sup>
he shall prolong<sup>[748](h0748.md)</sup> his
days,<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> and the pleasure<sup>[2656](h2656.md)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> shall
prosper<sup>[6743](h6743)</sup> in his hand.<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

11  He shall see<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> of the
travail<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5999](h5999.md)</sup> of his
soul,<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> and shall be
satisfied:<sup>[7646](h7646.md)</sup> by his
knowledge<sup>[1847](h1847.md)</sup> shall my
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> servant<sup>[5650](h5650.md)</sup>
justify<sup>[6663](h6663)</sup> many;<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> for
he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> shall bear<sup>[5445](h5445.md)</sup> their
iniquities.<sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup>

12  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> will I
divide<sup>[2505](h2505.md)</sup> him a portion with the
great,<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> and he shall
divide<sup>[2505](h2505)</sup> the spoil<sup>[7998](h7998.md)</sup>
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the strong;<sup>[6099](h6099.md)</sup>
because<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup> <sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> he hath poured
out<sup>[6168](h6168)</sup> his soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> unto
death:<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> and he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> was
numbered<sup>[4487](h4487)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854.md)</sup> the
transgressors;<sup>[6586](h6586.md)</sup> and he
bore<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> the sin<sup>[2399](h2399.md)</sup> of
many,<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> and made
intercession<sup>[6293](h6293.md)</sup> for the
transgressors.<sup>[6586](h6586.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/isa053.md)

1 κύριε τίς ἐπίστευσεν τῇ ἀκοῇ ἡμῶν καὶ ὁ βραχίων κυρίου τίνι ἀπεκαλύφθη

2 ἀνηγγείλαμεν ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ ὡς παιδίον ὡς ῥίζα ἐν γῇ διψώσῃ οὐκ ἔστιν
εἶδος αὐτῷ οὐδὲ δόξα καὶ εἴδομεν αὐτόν καὶ οὐκ εἶχεν εἶδος οὐδὲ κάλλος

3 ἀλλὰ τὸ εἶδος αὐτοῦ ἄτιμον ἐκλεῖπον παρὰ πάντας ἀνθρώπους ἄνθρωπος ἐν
πληγῇ ὢν καὶ εἰδὼς φέρειν μαλακίαν ὅτι ἀπέστραπται τὸ πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ
ἠτιμάσθη καὶ οὐκ ἐλογίσθη

4 οὗτος τὰς ἁμαρτίας ἡμῶν φέρει καὶ περὶ ἡμῶν ὀδυνᾶται καὶ ἡμεῖς
ἐλογισάμεθα αὐτὸν εἶναι ἐν πόνῳ καὶ ἐν πληγῇ καὶ ἐν κακώσει

5 αὐτὸς δὲ ἐτραυματίσθη διὰ τὰς ἀνομίας ἡμῶν καὶ μεμαλάκισται διὰ τὰς
ἁμαρτίας ἡμῶν παιδεία εἰρήνης ἡμῶν ἐπ᾽ αὐτόν τῷ μώλωπι αὐτοῦ ἡμεῖς
ἰάθημεν

6 πάντες ὡς πρόβατα ἐπλανήθημεν ἄνθρωπος τῇ ὁδῷ αὐτοῦ ἐπλανήθη καὶ
κύριος παρέδωκεν αὐτὸν ταῖς ἁμαρτίαις ἡμῶν

7 καὶ αὐτὸς διὰ τὸ κεκακῶσθαι οὐκ ἀνοίγει τὸ στόμα ὡς πρόβατον ἐπὶ
σφαγὴν ἤχθη καὶ ὡς ἀμνὸς ἐναντίον τοῦ κείροντος αὐτὸν ἄφωνος οὕτως οὐκ
ἀνοίγει τὸ στόμα αὐτοῦ

8 ἐν τῇ ταπεινώσει ἡ κρίσις αὐτοῦ ἤρθη τὴν γενεὰν αὐτοῦ τίς διηγήσεται
ὅτι αἴρεται ἀπὸ τῆς γῆς ἡ ζωὴ αὐτοῦ ἀπὸ τῶν ἀνομιῶν τοῦ λαοῦ μου ἤχθη
εἰς θάνατον

9 καὶ δώσω τοὺς πονηροὺς ἀντὶ τῆς ταφῆς αὐτοῦ καὶ τοὺς πλουσίους ἀντὶ
τοῦ θανάτου αὐτοῦ ὅτι ἀνομίαν οὐκ ἐποίησεν οὐδὲ εὑρέθη δόλος ἐν τῷ
στόματι αὐτοῦ

10 καὶ κύριος βούλεται καθαρίσαι αὐτὸν τῆς πληγῆς ἐὰν δῶτε περὶ ἁμαρτίας
ἡ ψυχὴ ὑμῶν ὄψεται σπέρμα μακρόβιον καὶ βούλεται κύριος ἀφελεῖν

11 ἀπὸ τοῦ πόνου τῆς ψυχῆς αὐτοῦ δεῖξαι αὐτῷ φῶς καὶ πλάσαι τῇ συνέσει
δικαιῶσαι δίκαιον εὖ δουλεύοντα πολλοῖς καὶ τὰς ἁμαρτίας αὐτῶν αὐτὸς
ἀνοίσει

12 διὰ τοῦτο αὐτὸς κληρονομήσει πολλοὺς καὶ τῶν ἰσχυρῶν μεριεῖ σκῦλα
ἀνθ᾽ ὧν παρεδόθη εἰς θάνατον ἡ ψυχὴ αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐν τοῖς ἀνόμοις ἐλογίσθη
καὶ αὐτὸς ἁμαρτίας πολλῶν ἀνήνεγκεν καὶ διὰ τὰς ἁμαρτίας αὐτῶν παρεδόθη

[Vul](../vul/isa053.md)

1 \[Quis credidit auditui nostro?  
et brachium Domini cui revelatum est?  

2 Et ascendet sicut virgultum coram eo,  
et sicut radix de terra sitienti.  
Non est species ei, neque decor,  
et vidimus eum,  
et non erat aspectus, et desideravimus eum:  

3 despectum, et novissimum virorum,  
virum dolorum, et scientem infirmitatem,  
et quasi absconditus vultus ejus et despectus,  
unde nec reputavimus eum.  

4 Vere languores nostros ipse tulit,  
et dolores nostros ipse portavit;  
et nos putavimus eum quasi leprosum,  
et percussum a Deo, et humiliatum.  

5 Ipse autem vulneratus est propter iniquitates nostras;  
attritus est propter scelera nostra:  
disciplina pacis nostræ super eum,  
et livore ejus sanati sumus.  

6 Omnes nos quasi oves erravimus,  
unusquisque in viam suam declinavit:  
et posuit Dominus in eo  
iniquitatem omnium nostrum.  

7 Oblatus est quia ipse voluit,  
et non aperuit os suum;  
sicut ovis ad occisionem ducetur,  
et quasi agnus coram tondente se obmutescet,  
et non aperiet os suum.  

8 De angustia, et de judicio sublatus est.  
Generationem ejus quis enarrabit?  
quia abscissus est de terra viventium:  
propter scelus populi mei percussi eum.  

9 Et dabit impios pro sepultura,  
et divitem pro morte sua,  
eo quod iniquitatem non fecerit,  
neque dolus fuerit in ore ejus.  

10 Et Dominus voluit conterere eum in infirmitate.  
Si posuerit pro peccato animam suam,  
videbit semen longævum,  
et voluntas Domini in manu ejus dirigetur.  

11 Pro eo quod laboravit anima ejus,  
videbit et saturabitur.  
In scientia sua justificabit  
ipse justus servus meus multos,  
et iniquitates eorum ipse portabit.  

12 Ideo dispertiam ei plurimos,  
et fortium dividet spolia,  
pro eo quod tradidit in mortem animam suam,  
et cum sceleratis reputatus est,  
et ipse peccata multorum tulit,  
et pro transgressoribus rogavit.\]
