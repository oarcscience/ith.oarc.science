### Psalms 7

[KJV](../kjv/psa007)

1  Shiggaion<sup>[7692](h7692)</sup> of David,<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup>
which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> he sang<sup>[7891](h7891)</sup> unto the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> concerning<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
words<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> of Cush<sup>[3568](h3568)</sup> the
Benjamite.<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> <sup>[1145](h1145)</sup> O
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> my God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> in thee do I
put my trust:<sup>[2620](h2620)</sup> save<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> me
from all<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> them that
persecute<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> me, and
deliver<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> me:

2  Lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> he tear<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> my
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> like a lion,<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> rending
it in pieces,<sup>[6561](h6561)</sup> while there is
none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> to deliver.<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup>

3  O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> my God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>
if<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> I have done<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
this;<sup>[2063](h2063)</sup> if<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> there
be<sup>[3426](h3426)</sup> iniquity<sup>[5766](h5766)</sup> in my
hands;<sup>[3709](h3709)</sup>

4  If<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> I have rewarded<sup>[1580](h1580)</sup>
evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> unto him that was at
peace<sup>[7999](h7999)</sup> with me; (yea, I have

cause<sup>[7387](h7387)</sup> is mine enemy:)<sup>[6887](h6887)</sup>

5  Let the enemy<sup>[341](h0341)</sup>
persecute<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> my soul,<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> and
take<sup>[5381](h5381)</sup> it; yea, let him tread
down<sup>[7429](h7429)</sup> my life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> upon the
earth,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> and lay<sup>[7931](h7931)</sup> mine
honor<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> in the dust.<sup>[6083](h6083)</sup>
Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

6  Arise,<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> in
thine anger,<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> lift up
thyself<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> because of the
rage<sup>[5678](h5678)</sup> of mine enemies:<sup>[6887](h6887)</sup>
and awake<sup>[5782](h5782)</sup> for<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> me to the
judgment<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> that thou hast
commanded.<sup>[6680](h6680)</sup>

7  So shall the congregation<sup>[5712](h5712)</sup> of the
people<sup>[3816](h3816)</sup> compass thee
about:<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> for their sakes<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
therefore return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> thou on
high.<sup>[4791](h4791)</sup>

8  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall judge<sup>[1777](h1777)</sup>
the people:<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> me, O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> according to my
righteousness,<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> and according to mine
integrity<sup>[8537](h8537)</sup> that is in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me.

9  Oh<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> let the wickedness<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup>
of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> come to an
end;<sup>[1584](h1584)</sup> but establish<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> the
just:<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> for the righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> trieth<sup>[974](h0974)</sup> the
hearts<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> and reins.<sup>[3629](h3629)</sup>

10  My defense<sup>[4043](h4043)</sup> is of<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> which saveth<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> the
upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> in heart.<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>

11  God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> judgeth<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> the
righteous,<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> and God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> is
angry<sup>[2194](h2194)</sup> with the wicked
every<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> day.<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup>

12  If<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> he turn<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
not,<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> he will whet<sup>[3913](h3913)</sup> his
sword;<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> he hath bent<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> his
bow,<sup>[7198](h7198)</sup> and made it ready.<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup>

13  He hath also prepared<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> for him the
instruments<sup>[3627](h3627)</sup> of death;<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> he
ordaineth<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> his arrows<sup>[2671](h2671)</sup>
against the persecutors.<sup>[1814](h1814)</sup>

14  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> he
travaileth<sup>[2254](h2254)</sup> with iniquity,<sup>[205](h0205)</sup>
and hath conceived<sup>[2029](h2029)</sup>
mischief,<sup>[5999](h5999)</sup> and brought
forth<sup>[3205](h3205)</sup> falsehood.<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup>

15  He made<sup>[3738](h3738)</sup> a pit,<sup>[953](h0953)</sup> and
digged<sup>[2658](h2658)</sup> it, and is fallen<sup>[5307](h5307)</sup>
into the ditch<sup>[7845](h7845)</sup> which he
made.<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup>

16  His mischief<sup>[5999](h5999)</sup> shall
return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> upon his own
head,<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> and his violent
dealing<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> shall come down<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> his own pate.<sup>[6936](h6936)</sup>

17  I will praise<sup>[3034](h3034)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> according to his
righteousness:<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> and will sing
praise<sup>[2167](h2167)</sup> to the name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> of
the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> most high.<sup>[5945](h5945)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa007)

1 ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ ὃν ᾖσεν τῷ κυρίῳ ὑπὲρ τῶν λόγων Χουσι υἱοῦ Ιεμενι

2 κύριε ὁ θεός μου ἐπὶ σοὶ ἤλπισα σῶσόν με ἐκ πάντων τῶν διωκόντων με
καὶ ῥῦσαί με

3 μήποτε ἁρπάσῃ ὡς λέων τὴν ψυχήν μου μὴ ὄντος λυτρουμένου μηδὲ σῴζοντος

4 κύριε ὁ θεός μου εἰ ἐποίησα τοῦτο εἰ ἔστιν ἀδικία ἐν χερσίν μου

5 εἰ ἀνταπέδωκα τοῖς ἀνταποδιδοῦσίν μοι κακά ἀποπέσοιν ἄρα ἀπὸ τῶν
ἐχθρῶν μου κενός

6 καταδιώξαι ἄρα ὁ ἐχθρὸς τὴν ψυχήν μου καὶ καταλάβοι καὶ καταπατήσαι
εἰς γῆν τὴν ζωήν μου καὶ τὴν δόξαν μου εἰς χοῦν κατασκηνώσαι διάψαλμα

7 ἀνάστηθι κύριε ἐν ὀργῇ σου ὑψώθητι ἐν τοῖς πέρασι τῶν ἐχθρῶν μου
ἐξεγέρθητι κύριε ὁ θεός μου ἐν προστάγματι ᾧ ἐνετείλω

8 καὶ συναγωγὴ λαῶν κυκλώσει σε καὶ ὑπὲρ ταύτης εἰς ὕψος ἐπίστρεψον

9 κύριος κρινεῖ λαούς κρῖνόν με κύριε κατὰ τὴν δικαιοσύνην μου καὶ κατὰ
τὴν ἀκακίαν μου ἐπ᾽ ἐμοί

10 συντελεσθήτω δὴ πονηρία ἁμαρτωλῶν καὶ κατευθυνεῖς δίκαιον ἐτάζων
καρδίας καὶ νεφροὺς ὁ θεός

11 δικαία ἡ βοήθειά μου παρὰ τοῦ θεοῦ τοῦ σῴζοντος τοὺς εὐθεῖς τῇ καρδίᾳ

12 ὁ θεὸς κριτὴς δίκαιος καὶ ἰσχυρὸς καὶ μακρόθυμος μὴ ὀργὴν ἐπάγων καθ᾽
ἑκάστην ἡμέραν

13 ἐὰν μὴ ἐπιστραφῆτε τὴν ῥομφαίαν αὐτοῦ στιλβώσει τὸ τόξον αὐτοῦ
ἐνέτεινεν καὶ ἡτοίμασεν αὐτὸ

14 καὶ ἐν αὐτῷ ἡτοίμασεν σκεύη θανάτου τὰ βέλη αὐτοῦ τοῖς καιομένοις
ἐξειργάσατο

15 ἰδοὺ ὠδίνησεν ἀδικίαν συνέλαβεν πόνον καὶ ἔτεκεν ἀνομίαν

16 λάκκον ὤρυξεν καὶ ἀνέσκαψεν αὐτὸν καὶ ἐμπεσεῖται εἰς βόθρον ὃν
εἰργάσατο

17 ἐπιστρέψει ὁ πόνος αὐτοῦ εἰς κεφαλὴν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐπὶ κορυφὴν αὐτοῦ ἡ
ἀδικία αὐτοῦ καταβήσεται

18 ἐξομολογήσομαι κυρίῳ κατὰ τὴν δικαιοσύνην αὐτοῦ καὶ ψαλῶ τῷ ὀνόματι
κυρίου τοῦ ὑψίστου

[Vul](../vul/psa007)

1 Psalmus David, quem cantavit Domino pro verbis Chusi, filii Jemini.

2 \[Domine Deus meus, in te speravi;  
salvum me fac ex omnibus persequentibus me, et libera me:  

3 nequando rapiat ut leo animam meam,  
dum non est qui redimat, neque qui salvum faciat.  

4 Domine Deus meus, si feci istud,  
si est iniquitas in manibus meis,  

5 si reddidi retribuentibus mihi mala,  
decidam merito ab inimicis meis inanis.  

6 Persequatur inimicus animam meam, et comprehendat;  
et conculcet in terra vitam meam,  
et gloriam meam in pulverem deducat.  

7 Exsurge, Domine, in ira tua,  
et exaltare in finibus inimicorum meorum:  
et exsurge, Domine Deus meus, in præcepto quod mandasti,  

8 et synagoga populorum circumdabit te:  
et propter hanc in altum regredere:  

9 Dominus judicat populos.  
Judica me, Domine, secundum justitiam meam,  
et secundum innocentiam meam super me.  

10 Consumetur nequitia peccatorum, et diriges justum,  
scrutans corda et renes, Deus.  

11 Justum adjutorium meum a Domino,  
qui salvos facit rectos corde.  

12 Deus judex justus, fortis, et patiens;  
numquid irascitur per singulos dies?  

13 Nisi conversi fueritis, gladium suum vibrabit;  
arcum suum tetendit, et paravit illum.  

14 Et in eo paravit vasa mortis,  
sagittas suas ardentibus effecit.  

15 Ecce parturiit injustitiam;  
concepit dolorem, et peperit iniquitatem.  

16 Lacum aperuit, et effodit eum;  
et incidit in foveam quam fecit.  

17 Convertetur dolor ejus in caput ejus,  
et in verticem ipsius iniquitas ejus descendet.  

18 Confitebor Domino secundum justitiam ejus,  
et psallam nomini Domini altissimi.\]
