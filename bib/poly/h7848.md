H7848  
שׁטּים שׁטּה  
שִׁטָּה שִׁטִּים ‎ shiṭṭâh shiṭṭı̂ym  
*shit-taw‘,* *shit-teem‘*  
Feminine of a derivative (the second form being only in the plural,
meaning the *sticks* *of* *wood*) from the same as [7850](h7850) the
*acacia* (from its *scourging* thorns): - shittah, shittim. See also
[1029](h1029)
