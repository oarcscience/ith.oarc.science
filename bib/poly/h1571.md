H1571  
גּם  
גַּם ‎ gam  
*gam*  
By contraction from an unused root meaning to *gather*; properly
*assemblage*; used only adverbially *also*, *even*, *yea*, *though*;
often repeated as correlation *both*... *and: -* again, alike, also, (so
much) as (soon), both (so) . . . and, but, either . . . or, even, for
all, (in) likewise (manner), moreover, nay . . . neither, one, then
(-refore), though, what, with, yea.
