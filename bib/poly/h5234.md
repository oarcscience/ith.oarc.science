H5234  
נכר  
נָכַר ‎ nâkar  
*naw-kar‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *scrutinize*, that is, look intently at;
hence (with *recognition* implied), to *acknowledge*, *be* *acquainted*
*with*, *care* *for*, *respect*, *revere*, or (with *suspicion*
implied), to *disregard*, *ignore*, *be* *strange* toward, *reject*,
*resign*, *dissimulate* (as if ignorant or disowning): - acknowledge, X
could, deliver, discern, dissemble, estrange, feign self to be another,
know, take knowledge (notice), perceive, regard, (have) respect, behave
(make) self strange (-ly).
