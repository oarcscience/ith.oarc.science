H5158  
נחלה נחלה נחל  
נַחַל נַחלָה נַחֲלָה ‎ nachal nachlâh nachălâh  
*nakh‘-al,* *nakh‘-law,* *nakh-al-aw‘*  
From [5157](h5157.md) in its original sense; a *stream*, especially a
winter *torrent*; (by implication) a (narrow) *valley* (in which a brook
runs); also a *shaft* (of a mine): - brook, flood, river, stream,
valley.
