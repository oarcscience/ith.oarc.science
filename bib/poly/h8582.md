H8582  
תּעה  
תָּּעָה ‎ tâ‛âh  
*taw-aw‘*  
A primitive root; to *vacillate*, that is, *reel* or *stray* (literally
or figuratively); also causatively of both: - (cause to) go astray,
deceive, dissemble, (cause to, make to) err, pant, seduce, (make to)
stagger, (cause to) wander, be out of the way.
