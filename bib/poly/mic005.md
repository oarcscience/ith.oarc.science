### Micah 5

[KJV](../kjv/mic005.md)

1  Now<sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup> gather thyself in
troops,<sup>[1413](h1413)</sup> O daughter<sup>[1323](h1323.md)</sup> of
troops:<sup>[1416](h1416)</sup> he hath laid<sup>[7760](h7760.md)</sup>
siege<sup>[4692](h4692)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> us: they
shall smite<sup>[5221](h5221)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> of Israel<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> with a
rod<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
cheek.<sup>[3895](h3895.md)</sup>

2  But thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> Bethlehem<sup>[1035](h1035.md)</sup>
Ephratah,<sup>[672](h0672)</sup> though thou be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
little<sup>[6810](h6810.md)</sup> among the
thousands<sup>[505](h0505)</sup> of Judah,<sup>[3063](h3063.md)</sup> yet
out of<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> thee shall he come
forth<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup> unto me that is to
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> ruler<sup>[4910](h4910.md)</sup> in
Israel;<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup> whose goings
forth<sup>[4163](h4163.md)</sup> have been from of
old,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6924](h6924.md)</sup> from
everlasting.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

3  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> will he give them
up,<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> until<sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup> the
time<sup>[6256](h6256.md)</sup> that she which
travaileth<sup>[3205](h3205.md)</sup> hath brought
forth:<sup>[3205](h3205)</sup> then the remnant<sup>[3499](h3499.md)</sup>
of his brethren<sup>[251](h0251.md)</sup> shall
return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> unto<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478.md)</sup>

4  And he shall stand<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup> and
feed<sup>[7462](h7462)</sup> in the strength<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup> of
the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> in the majesty<sup>[1347](h1347.md)</sup>
of the name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
his God;<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> and they shall
abide:<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
now<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> shall he be great<sup>[1431](h1431.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the ends<sup>[657](h0657.md)</sup> of the
earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

5  And this<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> man shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
the peace,<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
Assyrian<sup>[804](h0804)</sup> shall come<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup> into
our land:<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> and when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he
shall tread<sup>[1869](h1869.md)</sup> in our
palaces,<sup>[759](h0759.md)</sup> then shall we
raise<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> him
seven<sup>[7651](h7651)</sup> shepherds,<sup>[7462](h7462.md)</sup> and
eight<sup>[8083](h8083)</sup> principal<sup>[5257](h5257.md)</sup>
men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

6  And they shall waste<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
the land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of Assyria<sup>[804](h0804.md)</sup> with
the sword,<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> and the land<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
of Nimrod<sup>[5248](h5248.md)</sup> in the
entrances<sup>[6607](h6607.md)</sup> thereof: thus shall he
deliver<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> us from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> the
Assyrian,<sup>[804](h0804)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he
cometh<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> into our land,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he treadeth<sup>[1869](h1869.md)</sup> within
our borders.<sup>[1366](h1366.md)</sup>

7  And the remnant<sup>[7611](h7611.md)</sup> of
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> in the
midst<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> of many<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup>
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> as a dew<sup>[2919](h2919.md)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[854](h0854.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> as the showers<sup>[7241](h7241.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the grass,<sup>[6212](h6212.md)</sup>
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> tarrieth<sup>[6960](h6960.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> for man,<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup>
nor<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> waiteth<sup>[3176](h3176.md)</sup> for the
sons<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of men.<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

8  And the remnant<sup>[7611](h7611.md)</sup> of
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> among the
Gentiles<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> in the midst<sup>[7130](h7130.md)</sup> of
many<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> people<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup> as a
lion<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> among the beasts<sup>[929](h0929.md)</sup> of
the forest,<sup>[3293](h3293.md)</sup> as a young
lion<sup>[3715](h3715)</sup> among the flocks<sup>[5739](h5739.md)</sup> of
sheep:<sup>[6629](h6629)</sup> who,<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup>
if<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> he go through,<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup> both
treadeth down,<sup>[7429](h7429.md)</sup> and teareth in
pieces,<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> and none<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup> can
deliver.<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup>

9  Thine hand<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> shall be lifted
up<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> thine
adversaries,<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> and all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
thine enemies<sup>[341](h0341.md)</sup> shall be cut
off.<sup>[3772](h3772.md)</sup>

10  And it shall come to pass<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> in
that<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> day,<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>
saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> that I
will cut off<sup>[3772](h3772)</sup> thy horses<sup>[5483](h5483.md)</sup>
out of the midst<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7130](h7130.md)</sup> of
thee, and I will destroy<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup> thy
chariots:<sup>[4818](h4818.md)</sup>

11  And I will cut off<sup>[3772](h3772.md)</sup> the
cities<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> of thy land,<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and
throw down<sup>[2040](h2040)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> thy
strongholds:<sup>[4013](h4013.md)</sup>

12  And I will cut off<sup>[3772](h3772.md)</sup>
witchcrafts<sup>[3785](h3785.md)</sup> out of thine
hand;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> and thou shalt
have<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> more
soothsayers:<sup>[6049](h6049.md)</sup>

13  Thy graven images<sup>[6456](h6456.md)</sup> also will I cut
off,<sup>[3772](h3772.md)</sup> and thy standing
images<sup>[4676](h4676)</sup> out of the midst<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[7130](h7130.md)</sup> of thee; and thou shalt
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> more<sup>[5750](h5750.md)</sup>
worship<sup>[7812](h7812)</sup> the work<sup>[4639](h4639.md)</sup> of
thine hands.<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

14  And I will pluck up<sup>[5428](h5428.md)</sup> thy
groves<sup>[842](h0842)</sup> out of the midst<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[7130](h7130.md)</sup> of thee: so will I
destroy<sup>[8045](h8045)</sup> thy cities.<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup>

15  And I will execute<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>
vengeance<sup>[5359](h5359)</sup> in anger<sup>[639](h0639.md)</sup> and
fury<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup> upon <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
heathen,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> such as<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> they
have not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> heard.<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/mic005.md)

1 καὶ σύ Βηθλεεμ οἶκος τοῦ Εφραθα ὀλιγοστὸς εἶ τοῦ εἶναι ἐν χιλιάσιν
Ιουδα ἐκ σοῦ μοι ἐξελεύσεται τοῦ εἶναι εἰς ἄρχοντα ἐν τῷ Ισραηλ καὶ αἱ
ἔξοδοι αὐτοῦ ἀπ᾽ ἀρχῆς ἐξ ἡμερῶν αἰῶνος

2 διὰ τοῦτο δώσει αὐτοὺς ἕως καιροῦ τικτούσης τέξεται καὶ οἱ ἐπίλοιποι
τῶν ἀδελφῶν αὐτῶν ἐπιστρέψουσιν ἐπὶ τοὺς υἱοὺς Ισραηλ

3 καὶ στήσεται καὶ ὄψεται καὶ ποιμανεῖ τὸ ποίμνιον αὐτοῦ ἐν ἰσχύι κυρίου
καὶ ἐν τῇ δόξῃ τοῦ ὀνόματος κυρίου τοῦ θεοῦ αὐτῶν ὑπάρξουσιν διότι νῦν
μεγαλυνθήσεται ἕως ἄκρων τῆς γῆς

4 καὶ ἔσται αὕτη εἰρήνη ὅταν Ἀσσύριος ἐπέλθῃ ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν ὑμῶν καὶ ὅταν
ἐπιβῇ ἐπὶ τὴν χώραν ὑμῶν καὶ ἐπεγερθήσονται ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν ἑπτὰ ποιμένες καὶ
ὀκτὼ δήγματα ἀνθρώπων

5 καὶ ποιμανοῦσιν τὸν Ασσουρ ἐν ῥομφαίᾳ καὶ τὴν γῆν τοῦ Νεβρωδ ἐν τῇ
τάφρῳ αὐτῆς καὶ ῥύσεται ἐκ τοῦ Ασσουρ ὅταν ἐπέλθῃ ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν ὑμῶν καὶ
ὅταν ἐπιβῇ ἐπὶ τὰ ὅρια ὑμῶν

6 καὶ ἔσται τὸ ὑπόλειμμα τοῦ Ιακωβ ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ἐν μέσῳ λαῶν πολλῶν
ὡς δρόσος παρὰ κυρίου πίπτουσα καὶ ὡς ἄρνες ἐπὶ ἄγρωστιν ὅπως μὴ συναχθῇ
μηδεὶς μηδὲ ὑποστῇ ἐν υἱοῖς ἀνθρώπων

7 καὶ ἔσται τὸ ὑπόλειμμα τοῦ Ιακωβ ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ἐν μέσῳ λαῶν πολλῶν
ὡς λέων ἐν κτήνεσιν ἐν τῷ δρυμῷ καὶ ὡς σκύμνος ἐν ποιμνίοις προβάτων ὃν
τρόπον ὅταν διέλθῃ καὶ διαστείλας ἁρπάσῃ καὶ μὴ ᾖ ὁ ἐξαιρούμενος

8 ὑψωθήσεται ἡ χείρ σου ἐπὶ τοὺς θλίβοντάς σε καὶ πάντες οἱ ἐχθροί σου
ἐξολεθρευθήσονται

9 καὶ ἔσται ἐν ἐκείνῃ τῇ ἡμέρᾳ λέγει κύριος ἐξολεθρεύσω τοὺς ἵππους σου
ἐκ μέσου σου καὶ ἀπολῶ τὰ ἅρματά σου

10 καὶ ἐξολεθρεύσω τὰς πόλεις τῆς γῆς σου καὶ ἐξαρῶ πάντα τὰ ὀχυρώματά
σου

11 καὶ ἐξαρῶ τὰ φάρμακά σου ἐκ τῶν χειρῶν σου καὶ ἀποφθεγγόμενοι οὐκ
ἔσονται ἐν σοί

12 καὶ ἐξολεθρεύσω τὰ γλυπτά σου καὶ τὰς στήλας σου ἐκ μέσου σου καὶ
οὐκέτι μὴ προσκυνήσῃς τοῖς ἔργοις τῶν χειρῶν σου

13 καὶ ἐκκόψω τὰ ἄλση σου ἐκ μέσου σου καὶ ἀφανιῶ τὰς πόλεις σου

14 καὶ ποιήσω ἐν ὀργῇ καὶ ἐν θυμῷ ἐκδίκησιν ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ἀνθ᾽ ὧν οὐκ
εἰσήκουσαν

[Vul](../vul/mic005.md)

1 \[Nunc vastaberis, filia latronis.  
Obsidionem posuerunt super nos:  
in virga percutient maxillam judicis Israël.\]  

2 \[Et tu, Bethlehem Ephrata,  
parvulus es in millibus Juda;  
ex te mihi egredietur qui sit dominator in Israël,  
et egressus ejus ab initio,  
a diebus æternitatis.  

3 Propter hoc dabit eos usque ad tempus in quo parturiens pariet,  
et reliquiæ fratrum ejus convertentur ad filios Israël.  

4 Et stabit, et pascet in fortitudine Domini,  
in sublimitate nominis Domini Dei sui:  
et convertentur,  
quia nunc magnificabitur usque ad terminos terræ.  

5 Et erit iste pax:  
cum venerit Assyrius in terram nostram,  
et quando calcaverit domibus nostris,  
et suscitabimus super eum septem pastores  
et octo primates homines;  

6 et pascent terram Assur in gladio,  
et terram Nemrod in lanceis ejus,  
et liberabit ab Assur cum venerit in terram nostram,  
et cum calcaverit in finibus nostris.  

7 Et erunt reliquiæ Jacob in medio populorum multorum  
quasi ros a Domino,  
et quasi stillæ super herbam,  
quæ non exspectat virum,  
et non præstolatur filios hominum.  

8 Et erunt reliquiæ Jacob in gentibus,  
in medio populorum multorum,  
quasi leo in jumentis silvarum,  
et quasi catulus leonis in gregibus pecorum,  
qui cum transierit, et conculcaverit, et ceperit,  
non est qui eruat.  

9 Exaltabitur manus tua super hostes tuos,  
et omnes inimici tui interibunt.  

10 Et erit in die illa, dicit Dominus:  
auferam equos tuos de medio tui,  
et disperdam quadrigas tuas.  

11 Et perdam civitates terræ tuæ,  
et destruam omnes munitiones tuas:  
et auferam maleficia de manu tua,  
et divinationes non erunt in te:  

12 et perire faciam sculptilia tua et statuas tuas de medio tui,  
et non adorabis ultra opera manuum tuarum:  

13 et evellam lucos tuos de medio tui,  
et conteram civitates tuas.  

14 Et faciam, in furore et in indignatione, ultionem  
in omnibus gentibus quæ non audierunt.\]
