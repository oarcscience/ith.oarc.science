H5048  
נגד  
נֶגֶד ‎ neged  
*neh‘-ghed*  
From [5046](h5046.md) a *front*, that is, part opposite; specifically a
*counterpart*, or mate; usually (adverbially, especially with
preposition) *over* *against* or *before: -* about, (over) against, X
aloof, X far (off), X from, over, presence, X other side, sight, X to
view.
