### Isaiah 6

[KJV](../kjv/isa006)

1  In the year<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> that king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup>
Uzziah<sup>[5818](h5818)</sup> died<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> I
saw<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> also <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> sitting<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> a throne,<sup>[3678](h3678)</sup>
high<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> and lifted up,<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> and
his train<sup>[7757](h7757)</sup> filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the temple.<sup>[1964](h1964)</sup>

2  Above<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4605](h4605)</sup> it
stood<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup> the seraphims:<sup>[8314](h8314)</sup>
each one<sup>[259](h0259)</sup> had six wings<sup>[8337](h8337)</sup>
<sup>[3671](h3671)</sup> <sup>[8337](h8337)</sup>
<sup>[3671](h3671)</sup> with twain<sup>[8147](h8147)</sup> he
covered<sup>[3680](h3680)</sup> his face,<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> and
with twain<sup>[8147](h8147)</sup> he covered<sup>[3680](h3680)</sup>
his feet,<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> and with twain<sup>[8147](h8147)</sup>
he did fly.<sup>[5774](h5774)</sup>

3  And one cried<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup>
<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> another,<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> and
said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Holy,<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup>
holy,<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup> holy,<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup> is the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of hosts:<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> the
whole<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> is
full<sup>[4393](h4393)</sup> of his glory.<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup>

4  And the posts<sup>[520](h0520)</sup> of the
door<sup>[5592](h5592)</sup> moved<sup>[5128](h5128)</sup> at the
voice<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of him that
cried,<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> and the house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> was
filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> with smoke.<sup>[6227](h6227)</sup>

5  Then said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I, Woe<sup>[188](h0188)</sup> is me!
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I am undone;<sup>[1820](h1820)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am a
man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> of unclean<sup>[2931](h2931)</sup>
lips,<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> and I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup>
dwell<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> in the midst<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup> of a
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> of unclean<sup>[2931](h2931)</sup>
lips:<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> mine
eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> have seen<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the King,<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of hosts.<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup>

6  Then flew<sup>[5774](h5774)</sup> one<sup>[259](h0259)</sup>
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> the seraphims<sup>[8314](h8314)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> me, having a live
coal<sup>[7531](h7531)</sup> in his hand,<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> which
he had taken<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> with the
tongs<sup>[4457](h4457)</sup> from off<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the altar:<sup>[4196](h4196)</sup>

7  And he laid<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> it upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
my mouth,<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> and said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>
Lo,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> this<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> hath
touched<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy
lips;<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> and thine iniquity<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>
is taken away,<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup> and thy
sin<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup> purged.<sup>[3722](h3722)</sup>

8  Also I heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of the Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup>
saying,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup>
Whom<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> shall I send,<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> and
who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> will go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> for us? Then
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I, Here am I;<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup>
send<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> me.

9  And he said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Go,<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> and
tell<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> this<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup>
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> Hear ye indeed,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup>
<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> but understand<sup>[995](h0995)</sup>
not;<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> and see ye indeed,<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> but perceive<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup>
not.<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>

10  Make the heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> of
this<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup>
fat,<sup>[8082](h8082)</sup> and make their ears<sup>[241](h0241)</sup>
heavy,<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> and shut<sup>[8173](h8173)</sup> their
eyes;<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> they
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> with their eyes,<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> and
hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> with their ears,<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> and
understand<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> with their
heart,<sup>[3824](h3824)</sup> and convert,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> and
be healed.<sup>[7495](h7495)</sup>

11  Then said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> I, Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> how
long?<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[4970](h4970)</sup> And he
answered,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Until<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> the cities<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> be
wasted<sup>[7582](h7582)</sup> without<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> inhabitant,<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> and the
houses<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> without<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> man,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> and the
land<sup>[127](h0127)</sup> be utterly<sup>[8077](h8077)</sup>
desolate,<sup>[7582](h7582)</sup>

12  And the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> have removed men far
away,<sup>[7368](h7368)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup>
<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> and there be a great<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup>
forsaking<sup>[5805](h5805)</sup> in the midst<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup>
of the land.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

13  But yet<sup>[5750](h5750)</sup> in it shall be a
tenth,<sup>[6224](h6224)</sup> and it shall
return,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> and shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup>
eaten:<sup>[1197](h1197)</sup> as a teil tree,<sup>[424](h0424)</sup>
and as an oak,<sup>[437](h0437)</sup> whose<sup>[834](h0834)</sup>
substance<sup>[4678](h4678)</sup> is in them, when they
cast<sup>[7995](h7995)</sup> their leaves: so the
holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> seed<sup>[2233](h2233)</sup> shall be the
substance<sup>[4678](h4678)</sup> thereof.

[Sep](../sep/isa006)

1 καὶ ἐγένετο τοῦ ἐνιαυτοῦ οὗ ἀπέθανεν Οζιας ὁ βασιλεύς εἶδον τὸν κύριον
καθήμενον ἐπὶ θρόνου ὑψηλοῦ καὶ ἐπηρμένου καὶ πλήρης ὁ οἶκος τῆς δόξης
αὐτοῦ

2 καὶ σεραφιν εἱστήκεισαν κύκλῳ αὐτοῦ ἓξ πτέρυγες τῷ ἑνὶ καὶ ἓξ πτέρυγες
τῷ ἑνί καὶ ταῖς μὲν δυσὶν κατεκάλυπτον τὸ πρόσωπον καὶ ταῖς δυσὶν
κατεκάλυπτον τοὺς πόδας καὶ ταῖς δυσὶν ἐπέταντο

3 καὶ ἐκέκραγον ἕτερος πρὸς τὸν ἕτερον καὶ ἔλεγον ἅγιος ἅγιος ἅγιος
κύριος σαβαωθ πλήρης πᾶσα ἡ γῆ τῆς δόξης αὐτοῦ

4 καὶ ἐπήρθη τὸ ὑπέρθυρον ἀπὸ τῆς φωνῆς ἧς ἐκέκραγον καὶ ὁ οἶκος ἐπλήσθη
καπνοῦ

5 καὶ εἶπα ὦ τάλας ἐγώ ὅτι κατανένυγμαι ὅτι ἄνθρωπος ὢν καὶ ἀκάθαρτα
χείλη ἔχων ἐν μέσῳ λαοῦ ἀκάθαρτα χείλη ἔχοντος ἐγὼ οἰκῶ καὶ τὸν βασιλέα
κύριον σαβαωθ εἶδον τοῖς ὀφθαλμοῖς μου

6 καὶ ἀπεστάλη πρός με ἓν τῶν σεραφιν καὶ ἐν τῇ χειρὶ εἶχεν ἄνθρακα ὃν
τῇ λαβίδι ἔλαβεν ἀπὸ τοῦ θυσιαστηρίου

7 καὶ ἥψατο τοῦ στόματός μου καὶ εἶπεν ἰδοὺ ἥψατο τοῦτο τῶν χειλέων σου
καὶ ἀφελεῖ τὰς ἀνομίας σου καὶ τὰς ἁμαρτίας σου περικαθαριεῖ

8 καὶ ἤκουσα τῆς φωνῆς κυρίου λέγοντος τίνα ἀποστείλω καὶ τίς πορεύσεται
πρὸς τὸν λαὸν τοῦτον καὶ εἶπα ἰδού εἰμι ἐγώ ἀπόστειλόν με

9 καὶ εἶπεν πορεύθητι καὶ εἰπὸν τῷ λαῷ τούτῳ ἀκοῇ ἀκούσετε καὶ οὐ μὴ
συνῆτε καὶ βλέποντες βλέψετε καὶ οὐ μὴ ἴδητε

10 ἐπαχύνθη γὰρ ἡ καρδία τοῦ λαοῦ τούτου καὶ τοῖς ὠσὶν αὐτῶν βαρέως
ἤκουσαν καὶ τοὺς ὀφθαλμοὺς αὐτῶν ἐκάμμυσαν μήποτε ἴδωσιν τοῖς ὀφθαλμοῖς
καὶ τοῖς ὠσὶν ἀκούσωσιν καὶ τῇ καρδίᾳ συνῶσιν καὶ ἐπιστρέψωσιν καὶ
ἰάσομαι αὐτούς

11 καὶ εἶπα ἕως πότε κύριε καὶ εἶπεν ἕως ἂν ἐρημωθῶσιν πόλεις παρὰ τὸ μὴ
κατοικεῖσθαι καὶ οἶκοι παρὰ τὸ μὴ εἶναι ἀνθρώπους καὶ ἡ γῆ
καταλειφθήσεται ἔρημος

12 καὶ μετὰ ταῦτα μακρυνεῖ ὁ θεὸς τοὺς ἀνθρώπους καὶ οἱ καταλειφθέντες
πληθυνθήσονται ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς

13 καὶ ἔτι ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς ἔστιν τὸ ἐπιδέκατον καὶ πάλιν ἔσται εἰς προνομὴν ὡς
τερέβινθος καὶ ὡς βάλανος ὅταν ἐκπέσῃ ἀπὸ τῆς θήκης αὐτῆς

[Vul](../vul/isa006)

1 In anno quo mortuus est rex Ozias, vidi Dominum sedentem super solium
excelsum et elevatum; et ea quæ sub ipso erant replebant templum.

2 Seraphim stabant super illud: sex alæ uni, et sex alæ alteri; duabus
velabant faciem ejus, et duabus velabant pedes ejus, et duabus volabant.

3 Et clamabant alter ad alterum, et dicebant: \[Sanctus, sanctus,
sanctus Dominus, Deus exercituum;  
plena est omnis terra gloria ejus.\]

4 Et commota sunt superliminaria cardinum a voce clamantis, et domus
repleta est fumo.

5 Et dixi: \[Væ mihi, quia tacui,  
quia vir pollutus labiis ego sum,  
et in medio populi polluta labia habentis ego habito,  
et regem Dominum exercituum vidi oculis meis.\]

6 Et volavit ad me unus de seraphim, et in manu ejus calculus, quem
forcipe tulerat de altari,

7 et tetigit os meum, et dixit: \[Ecce tetigit hoc labia tua,  
et auferetur iniquitas tua, et peccatum tuum mundabitur.\]

8 Et audivi vocem Domini dicentis: \[Quem mittam?  
et quis ibit nobis?\] Et dixi: \[Ecce ego, mitte me.\]

9 Et dixit: \[Vade, et dices populo huic:  
Audite audientes, et nolite intelligere;  
et videte visionem, et nolite cognoscere.  

10 Excæca cor populi hujus,  
et aures ejus aggrava,  
et oculos ejus claude:  
ne forte videat oculis suis,  
et auribus suis audiat,  
et corde suo intelligat,  
et convertatur, et sanem eum.\]

11 Et dixi: \[Usquequo, Domine?\] Et dixit: \[Donec desolentur civitates
absque habitatore,  
et domus sine homine,  
et terra relinquetur deserta.  

12 Et longe faciet Dominus homines,  
et multiplicabitur quæ derelicta fuerat in medio terræ.  

13 Et adhuc in ea decimatio,  
et convertetur, et erit in ostensionem  
sicut terebinthus, et sicut quercus quæ expandit ramos suos;  
semen sanctum erit id quod steterit in ea.\]
