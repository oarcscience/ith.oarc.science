### Psalms 1

[KJV](../kjv/psa001.md)

1  Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> is the man<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup>
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> walketh<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> in the counsel<sup>[6098](h6098.md)</sup> of
the ungodly,<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
standeth<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup> in the way<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> of
sinners,<sup>[2400](h2400)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
sitteth<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> in the seat<sup>[4186](h4186.md)</sup> of
the scornful.<sup>[3887](h3887.md)</sup>

2  But<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> <sup>[518](h0518.md)</sup> his
delight<sup>[2656](h2656)</sup> is in the law<sup>[8451](h8451.md)</sup> of
the LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and in his law<sup>[8451](h8451.md)</sup>
doth he meditate<sup>[1897](h1897)</sup> day<sup>[3119](h3119.md)</sup> and
night.<sup>[3915](h3915.md)</sup>

3  And he shall be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> like a
tree<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup> planted<sup>[8362](h8362.md)</sup>
by<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the rivers<sup>[6388](h6388.md)</sup> of
water,<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> that<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> bringeth
forth<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> his fruit<sup>[6529](h6529.md)</sup> in his
season;<sup>[6256](h6256)</sup> his leaf<sup>[5929](h5929.md)</sup> also
shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> wither;<sup>[5034](h5034.md)</sup> and
whatsoever<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> <sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> he
doeth<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> shall prosper.<sup>[6743](h6743.md)</sup>

4  The ungodly<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> are not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
so:<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> but<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> are like the chaff<sup>[4671](h4671.md)</sup>
which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> the wind<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup> driveth
away.<sup>[5086](h5086.md)</sup>

5  Therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651.md)</sup> the
ungodly<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
stand<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> in the judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup>
nor sinners<sup>[2400](h2400.md)</sup> in the
congregation<sup>[5712](h5712.md)</sup> of the
righteous.<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup>

6  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> the way<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> of the
righteous:<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> but the way<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup>
of the ungodly<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup> shall
perish.<sup>[6](h0006.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa001.md)

1 μακάριος ἀνήρ ὃς οὐκ ἐπορεύθη ἐν βουλῇ ἀσεβῶν καὶ ἐν ὁδῷ ἁμαρτωλῶν οὐκ
ἔστη καὶ ἐπὶ καθέδραν λοιμῶν οὐκ ἐκάθισεν

2 ἀλλ᾽ ἢ ἐν τῷ νόμῳ κυρίου τὸ θέλημα αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐν τῷ νόμῳ αὐτοῦ
μελετήσει ἡμέρας καὶ νυκτός

3 καὶ ἔσται ὡς τὸ ξύλον τὸ πεφυτευμένον παρὰ τὰς διεξόδους τῶν ὑδάτων ὃ
τὸν καρπὸν αὐτοῦ δώσει ἐν καιρῷ αὐτοῦ καὶ τὸ φύλλον αὐτοῦ οὐκ
ἀπορρυήσεται καὶ πάντα ὅσα ἂν ποιῇ κατευοδωθήσεται

4 οὐχ οὕτως οἱ ἀσεβεῖς οὐχ οὕτως ἀλλ᾽ ἢ ὡς ὁ χνοῦς ὃν ἐκριπτεῖ ὁ ἄνεμος
ἀπὸ προσώπου τῆς γῆς

5 διὰ τοῦτο οὐκ ἀναστήσονται ἀσεβεῖς ἐν κρίσει οὐδὲ ἁμαρτωλοὶ ἐν βουλῇ
δικαίων

6 ὅτι γινώσκει κύριος ὁδὸν δικαίων καὶ ὁδὸς ἀσεβῶν ἀπολεῖται

[Vul](../vul/psa001.md)

1 \[Beatus vir qui non abiit in consilio impiorum,  
et in via peccatorum non stetit,  
et in cathedra pestilentiæ non sedit;  

2 sed in lege Domini voluntas ejus,  
et in lege ejus meditabitur die ac nocte.  

3 Et erit tamquam lignum quod plantatum est secus decursus aquarum,  
quod fructum suum dabit in tempore suo:  
et folium ejus non defluet;  
et omnia quæcumque faciet prosperabuntur.  

4 Non sic impii, non sic;  
sed tamquam pulvis quem projicit ventus a facie terræ.  

5 Ideo non resurgent impii in judicio,  
neque peccatores in concilio justorum:  

6 quoniam novit Dominus viam justorum,  
et iter impiorum peribit.\]
