G3753  
ὅτε, ο ̔́τε, η ̔́τε, το τε  
hote ho te hē te tō te  
*hot‘-eh,* *ho,t‘-eh,* *hay‘-the,* *tot‘-eh*  
From [3739](g3739) and [5037](g5037) at *which* (thing) *too*, that is,
*when:* - after (that), as soon as, that, when, while. Second form also
feminine, third form also neuter and fourth form simply the article
[3588](g3588) followed by [5037](g5037) so written (in some editions) to
distinguish them from [3752](g3752) and [5119](g5119)
