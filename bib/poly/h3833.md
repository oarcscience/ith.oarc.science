H3833  
לבאות לבאים לביּא לביא  
לָבִיא לְבִיָא לְבָאִים לְבָאוֹת ‎ lâbı̂y‘ l<sup>e</sup>bı̂yâ‘
l<sup>e</sup>bâ‘ı̂ym l<sup>e</sup>bâ‘ôth  
*law-bee‘* *leb-ee-yaw‘* *leb-aw-eem‘* *(-oth‘)*  
From an unused root meaning to *roar*; a *lion* (properly a *lioness* as
the fiercer (although not a *roarer*; compare [738](h0738) .md): - (great,
old, stout) lion, lioness, young \[lion\].
