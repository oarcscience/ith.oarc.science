G2272  
ἡσύχιος  
hēsuchios  
*hay-soo‘-khee-os*  
A prolonged form of a compound probably of a derivative of the base of
[1476](g1476) and perhaps [2192](g2192.md) properly *keeping* one’s *seat*
(*sedentary*), that is, (by implication) *still* (*undisturbed*,
*undisturbing*): - peaceable, quiet.
