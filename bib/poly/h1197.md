H1197  
בּער  
בָּעַר ‎ bâ‛ar  
*baw-ar‘*  
A primitive root; to *kindle*, that is, *consume* (by fire or by
eating); also (as denominative from [1198](h1198) to *be* (*become*.md)
*brutish: -* be brutish, bring (put, take) away, burn, (cause to) eat
(up), feed, heat, kindle, set (\[on fire\]), waste.
