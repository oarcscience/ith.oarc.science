G303  
ἀνά  
ana  
*an-ah‘*  
A primary preposition and adverb; properly *up*; but (by extension) used
(distributively) *severally*, or (locally) *at* (etc.): - and, apiece,
by, each, every (man), in, through. In compounds (as a prefix) it often
means (by implication) *repetition*, *intensity*, *reversal*, etc.
