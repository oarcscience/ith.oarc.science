### Job 5

[KJV](../kjv/job005.md)

1  Call<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> now,<sup>[4994](h4994.md)</sup> if there
be<sup>[3426](h3426)</sup> any that will answer<sup>[6030](h6030.md)</sup>

and envy<sup>[7068](h7068)</sup> slayeth<sup>[4191](h4191.md)</sup> the

habitation.<sup>[5116](h5116.md)</sup>

4  His children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> are far<sup>[7368](h7368.md)</sup>

5  Whose<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> harvest<sup>[7105](h7105.md)</sup> the

6  Although<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> affliction<sup>[205](h0205.md)</sup>

8  I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> would seek<sup>[1875](h1875.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> God,<sup>[410](h0410.md)</sup> and
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> God<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> would I
commit<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> my cause:<sup>[1700](h1700.md)</sup>

9  Which doeth<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> great
things<sup>[1419](h1419)</sup> and unsearchable;<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>

without<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>
number:<sup>[4557](h4557.md)</sup>

10  Who giveth<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> rain<sup>[4306](h4306.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> the
earth,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> and sendeth<sup>[7971](h7971.md)</sup>
waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the fields:<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup>

11  To set up<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> on high<sup>[4791](h4791.md)</sup>
those that be low;<sup>[8217](h8217.md)</sup> that those which
mourn<sup>[6937](h6937)</sup> may be exalted<sup>[7682](h7682.md)</sup> to
safety.<sup>[3468](h3468.md)</sup>

12  He disappointeth<sup>[6565](h6565.md)</sup> the

cannot<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> perform<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup> their
enterprise.<sup>[8454](h8454.md)</sup>

13  He taketh<sup>[3920](h3920)</sup> the wise<sup>[2450](h2450.md)</sup>

counsel<sup>[6098](h6098)</sup> of the froward<sup>[6617](h6617.md)</sup>
is carried headlong.<sup>[4116](h4116.md)</sup>

14  They meet<sup>[6298](h6298.md)</sup> with
darkness<sup>[2822](h2822)</sup> in the daytime,<sup>[3119](h3119.md)</sup>
and grope<sup>[4959](h4959)</sup> in the noonday<sup>[6672](h6672.md)</sup>
as in the night.<sup>[3915](h3915.md)</sup>

15  But he saveth<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup> the poor<sup>[34](h0034.md)</sup>

their mouth,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> and from
the hand<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of the
mighty.<sup>[2389](h2389.md)</sup>

16  So the poor<sup>[1800](h1800)</sup> hath<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
hope,<sup>[8615](h8615)</sup> and iniquity<sup>[5766](h5766.md)</sup>
stoppeth<sup>[7092](h7092)</sup> her mouth.<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup>

17  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> happy<sup>[835](h0835.md)</sup> is the
man<sup>[582](h0582)</sup> whom God<sup>[433](h0433.md)</sup>
correcteth:<sup>[3198](h3198.md)</sup> therefore
despise<sup>[3988](h3988)</sup> not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> thou the
chastening<sup>[4148](h4148.md)</sup> of the
Almighty:<sup>[7706](h7706.md)</sup>

18  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> maketh
sore,<sup>[3510](h3510)</sup> and bindeth up:<sup>[2280](h2280.md)</sup> he
woundeth,<sup>[4272](h4272)</sup> and his hands<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>
make whole.<sup>[7495](h7495.md)</sup>

19  He shall deliver<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> thee in
six<sup>[8337](h8337)</sup> troubles:<sup>[6869](h6869.md)</sup> yea, in
seven<sup>[7651](h7651)</sup> there shall no<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> touch<sup>[5060](h5060.md)</sup> thee.

20  In famine<sup>[7458](h7458.md)</sup> he shall
redeem<sup>[6299](h6299)</sup> thee from death:<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[4194](h4194)</sup> and in war<sup>[4421](h4421.md)</sup> from the
power<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of the
sword.<sup>[2719](h2719.md)</sup>

21  Thou shalt be hid<sup>[2244](h2244.md)</sup> from the
scourge<sup>[7752](h7752)</sup> of the tongue:<sup>[3956](h3956.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> shalt thou be
afraid<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> of destruction<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[7701](h7701)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> it
cometh.<sup>[935](h0935.md)</sup>

22  At destruction<sup>[7701](h7701.md)</sup> and
famine<sup>[3720](h3720)</sup> thou shalt laugh:<sup>[7832](h7832.md)</sup>
neither<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> shalt thou be
afraid<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> of the beasts<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> of the earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

23  For<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> thou shalt be in
league<sup>[1285](h1285)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973.md)</sup> the
stones<sup>[68](h0068)</sup> of the field:<sup>[7704](h7704.md)</sup> and
the beasts<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> of the field<sup>[7704](h7704.md)</sup>
shall be at peace<sup>[7999](h7999.md)</sup> with thee.

24  And thou shalt know<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> that thy
tabernacle<sup>[168](h0168.md)</sup> shall be in
peace;<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> and thou shalt
visit<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> thy habitation,<sup>[5116](h5116.md)</sup>
and shalt not sin.<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> <sup>[2398](h2398.md)</sup>

25  Thou shalt know<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> also
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thy seed<sup>[2233](h2233.md)</sup> shall be
great,<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> and thine
offspring<sup>[6631](h6631)</sup> as the grass<sup>[6212](h6212.md)</sup>

26  Thou shalt come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> to<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thy

27  Lo<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> this,<sup>[2063](h2063.md)</sup> we have

know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> it for thy
good.

[Sep](../sep/job005.md)

1 ἐπικάλεσαι δέ εἴ τίς σοι ὑπακούσεται ἢ εἴ τινα ἀγγέλων ἁγίων ὄψῃ

2 καὶ γὰρ ἄφρονα ἀναιρεῖ ὀργή πεπλανημένον δὲ θανατοῖ ζῆλος

3 ἐγὼ δὲ ἑώρακα ἄφρονας ῥίζαν βάλλοντας ἀλλ᾽ εὐθέως ἐβρώθη αὐτῶν ἡ
δίαιτα

4 πόρρω γένοιντο οἱ υἱοὶ αὐτῶν ἀπὸ σωτηρίας κολαβρισθείησαν δὲ ἐπὶ
θύραις ἡσσόνων καὶ οὐκ ἔσται ὁ ἐξαιρούμενος

5 ἃ γὰρ ἐκεῖνοι συνήγαγον δίκαιοι ἔδονται αὐτοὶ δὲ ἐκ κακῶν οὐκ
ἐξαίρετοι ἔσονται ἐκσιφωνισθείη αὐτῶν ἡ ἰσχύς

6 οὐ γὰρ μὴ ἐξέλθῃ ἐκ τῆς γῆς κόπος οὐδὲ ἐξ ὀρέων ἀναβλαστήσει πόνος

7 ἀλλὰ ἄνθρωπος γεννᾶται κόπῳ νεοσσοὶ δὲ γυπὸς τὰ ὑψηλὰ πέτονται

8 οὐ μὴν δὲ ἀλλὰ ἐγὼ δεηθήσομαι κυρίου κύριον δὲ τὸν πάντων δεσπότην
ἐπικαλέσομαι

9 τὸν ποιοῦντα μεγάλα καὶ ἀνεξιχνίαστα ἔνδοξά τε καὶ ἐξαίσια ὧν οὐκ
ἔστιν ἀριθμός

10 τὸν διδόντα ὑετὸν ἐπὶ τὴν γῆν ἀποστέλλοντα ὕδωρ ἐπὶ τὴν ὑπ᾽ οὐρανόν

11 τὸν ποιοῦντα ταπεινοὺς εἰς ὕψος καὶ ἀπολωλότας ἐξεγείροντα

12 διαλλάσσοντα βουλὰς πανούργων καὶ οὐ μὴ ποιήσουσιν αἱ χεῖρες αὐτῶν
ἀληθές

13 ὁ καταλαμβάνων σοφοὺς ἐν τῇ φρονήσει βουλὴν δὲ πολυπλόκων ἐξέστησεν

14 ἡμέρας συναντήσεται αὐτοῖς σκότος τὸ δὲ μεσημβρινὸν ψηλαφήσαισαν ἴσα
νυκτί

15 ἀπόλοιντο δὲ ἐν πολέμῳ ἀδύνατος δὲ ἐξέλθοι ἐκ χειρὸς δυνάστου

16 εἴη δὲ ἀδυνάτῳ ἐλπίς ἀδίκου δὲ στόμα ἐμφραχθείη

17 μακάριος δὲ ἄνθρωπος ὃν ἤλεγξεν ὁ κύριος νουθέτημα δὲ παντοκράτορος
μὴ ἀπαναίνου

18 αὐτὸς γὰρ ἀλγεῖν ποιεῖ καὶ πάλιν ἀποκαθίστησιν ἔπαισεν καὶ αἱ χεῖρες
αὐτοῦ ἰάσαντο

19 ἑξάκις ἐξ ἀναγκῶν σε ἐξελεῖται ἐν δὲ τῷ ἑβδόμῳ οὐ μὴ ἅψηταί σου κακόν

20 ἐν λιμῷ ῥύσεταί σε ἐκ θανάτου ἐν πολέμῳ δὲ ἐκ χειρὸς σιδήρου λύσει σε

21 ἀπὸ μάστιγος γλώσσης σε κρύψει καὶ οὐ μὴ φοβηθῇς ἀπὸ κακῶν ἐρχομένων

22 ἀδίκων καὶ ἀνόμων καταγελάσῃ ἀπὸ δὲ θηρίων ἀγρίων οὐ μὴ φοβηθῇς

23 θῆρες γὰρ ἄγριοι εἰρηνεύσουσίν σοι

24 εἶτα γνώσῃ ὅτι εἰρηνεύσει σου ὁ οἶκος ἡ δὲ δίαιτα τῆς σκηνῆς σου οὐ
μὴ ἁμάρτῃ

25 γνώσῃ δὲ ὅτι πολὺ τὸ σπέρμα σου τὰ δὲ τέκνα σου ἔσται ὥσπερ τὸ
παμβότανον τοῦ ἀγροῦ

26 ἐλεύσῃ δὲ ἐν τάφῳ ὥσπερ σῖτος ὥριμος κατὰ καιρὸν θεριζόμενος ἢ ὥσπερ
θιμωνιὰ ἅλωνος καθ᾽ ὥραν συγκομισθεῖσα

27 ἰδοὺ ταῦτα οὕτως ἐξιχνιάσαμεν ταῦτά ἐστιν ἃ ἀκηκόαμεν σὺ δὲ γνῶθι
σεαυτῷ εἴ τι ἔπραξας

[Vul](../vul/job005.md)

1 \[Voca ergo, si est qui tibi respondeat,  
et ad aliquem sanctorum convertere.  

2 Vere stultum interficit iracundia,  
et parvulum occidit invidia.  

3 Ego vidi stultum firma radice,  
et maledixi pulchritudini ejus statim.  

4 Longe fient filii ejus a salute,  
et conterentur in porta,  
et non erit qui eruat.  

5 Cujus messem famelicus comedet,  
et ipsum rapiet armatus,  
et bibent sitientes divitias ejus.  

6 Nihil in terra sine causa fit,  
et de humo non oritur dolor.  

7 Homo nascitur ad laborem,  
et avis ad volatum.  

8 Quam ob rem ego deprecabor Dominum,  
et ad Deum ponam eloquium meum:  

9 qui facit magna et inscrutabilia,  
et mirabilia absque numero;  

10 qui dat pluviam super faciem terræ,  
et irrigat aquis universa;  

11 qui ponit humiles in sublime,  
et mœrentes erigit sospitate;  

12 qui dissipat cogitationes malignorum,  
ne possint implere manus eorum quod cœperant;  

13 qui apprehendit sapientes in astutia eorum,  
et consilium pravorum dissipat.  

14 Per diem incurrent tenebras,  
et quasi in nocte, sic palpabunt in meridie.  

15 Porro salvum faciet egenum a gladio oris eorum,  
et de manu violenti pauperem.  

16 Et erit egeno spes;  
iniquitas autem contrahet os suum.  

17 Beatus homo qui corripitur a Deo:  
increpationem ergo Domini ne reprobes:  

18 quia ipse vulnerat, et medetur;  
percutit, et manus ejus sanabunt.  

19 In sex tribulationibus liberabit te,  
et in septima non tangent te malum.  

20 In fame eruet te de morte,  
et in bello de manu gladii.  

21 A flagello linguæ absconderis,  
et non timebis calamitatem cum venerit.  

22 In vastitate et fame ridebis,  
et bestias terræ non formidabis.  

23 Sed cum lapidibus regionum pactum tuum,  
et bestiæ terræ pacificæ erunt tibi.  

24 Et scies quod pacem habeat tabernaculum tuum;  
et visitans speciem tuam, non peccabis.  

25 Scies quoque quoniam multiplex erit semen tuum,  
et progenies tua quasi herba terræ.  

26 Ingredieris in abundantia sepulchrum,  
sicut infertur acervus tritici in tempore suo.  

27 Ecce hoc, ut investigavimus, ita est:  
quod auditum, mente pertracta.\]
