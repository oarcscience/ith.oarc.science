H5162  
נחם  
נָחַם ‎ nâcham  
*naw-kham‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *sigh*, that is, *breathe* strongly; by
implication to *be* *sorry*, that is, (in a favorable sense) to *pity*,
*console* or (reflexively) *rue*; or (unfavorably) to *avenge*
(oneself): - comfort (self), ease \[one’s self\], repent (-er, -ing,
self).
