H3595  
כּיּר כּיּור  
כִּיוֹר כִּיוֹר ‎ kı̂yôr kı̂yôr  
*kee-yore‘,* *kee-yore‘*  
From the same as [3564](h3564) properly something *round* (as
*excavated* or *bored*), that is, a *chafing* *dish* for coals or a
*caldron* for cooking; hence (from similarity of form) a *washbowl*;
also (for the same reason) a *pulpit* or platform: - hearth, laver, pan,
scaffold.
