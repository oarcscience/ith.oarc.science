H7641  
שׁבּלת שׁבּל  
שִׁבּוֹל שִׁבּוֹלֶת ‎ shibbôl shibbôleth  
*shib-bole‘,* *shib-bo‘-leth*  
From the same as [7640](h7640) a *stream* (as *flowing*.md); also an *ear*
of grain (as *growing* out); by analogy a *branch*: - branch, channel,
ear (of corn), (\[water-\]) flood, Shibboleth. Compare [5451](h5451.md)
