H7136  
קרה  
קָרָה ‎ qârâh  
*kaw-raw‘*  
A primitive root; to *light* *upon* (chiefly by accident); causatively
to *bring* *about*; specifically to *impose* timbers (for roof or
floor): - appoint, lay (make) beams, befall, bring, come (to pass unto),
floor, \[hap\] was, happen (unto), meet, send good speed.
