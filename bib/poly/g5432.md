G5432  
φρουρέω  
phroureō  
*froo-reh‘-o*  
From a compound of [4253](g4253) and [3708](g3708.md) to *be* *a* *watcher*
*in* *advance*, that is, to *mount* *guard* as a sentinel (*post*
*spies* at gates); figuratively to *hem* *in*, *protect*: - keep (with a
garrison). Compare [5083](g5083.md)
