H4229  
מחה  
מָחָה ‎ mâchâh  
*maw-khaw‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *stroke* or *rub*; by implication to
*erase*; also to *smooth* (as if with oil), that is, *grease* or make
fat; also to *touch*, that is, reach to: - abolish, blot out, destroy,
full of marrow, put out, reach unto, X utterly, wipe (away, out).
