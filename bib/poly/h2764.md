H2764  
חרם חרם  
חֵרֶם חֶרֶם ‎ chêrem cherem  
*khay‘-rem,* *kheh‘-rem*  
From [2763](h2763) physically (as *shutting* *in*.md) a *net* (either
literally or figuratively); usually a *doomed* object; abstractly
*extermination: -* (ac-)curse (-d, -d thing), dedicated thing, things
which should have been utterly destroyed, (appointed to) utter
destruction, devoted (thing), net.
