H7251  
רבע  
רָבַע ‎ râba‛  
*raw-bah‘*  
A primitive root (rather identical with [7250](h7250.md) through the idea
of *sprawling* at all fours (or possibly the reverse is the order of
derivation); compare [702](h0702) ; properly to *be* *four* (sided.md);
used only as denominative of [7253](h7253) to *be* *quadrate: -* (four-.md)
square (-d).
