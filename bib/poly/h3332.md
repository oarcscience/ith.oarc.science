H3332  
יצק  
יָצַק ‎ yâtsaq  
*yaw-tsak‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *pour* out (transitively or
intransitively); by implication to *melt* or *cast* as metal; by
extension to *place* firmly, to *stiffen* or grow hard: - cast, cleave
fast, be (as) firm, grow, be hard, lay out, molten, overflow, pour
(out), run out, set down, stedfast.
