ῥέω  
rheō  
*hreh‘-o*  
For certain tenses of which a prolonged form ( ἐρέω ereō ) is used; and
both as alternate for [2036](g2036) perhaps akin (or identical) with
[4482](g4482) (through the idea of *pouring* forth); to *utter*, that
is, *speak* or *say*: - command, make, say, speak (of). Compare
[3004](g3004)
