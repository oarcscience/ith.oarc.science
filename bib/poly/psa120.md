### Psalms 120

[KJV](../kjv/psa120)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees.<sup>[4609](h4609)</sup> In
my distress<sup>[6869](h6869)</sup> I cried<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and he
heard<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> me.

2  Deliver<sup>[5337](h5337)</sup> my soul,<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> from lying<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup>
lips,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> and from a
deceitful<sup>[7423](h7423)</sup> tongue.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup>

3  What<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> shall be given<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup>
unto thee? or what<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> shall be
done<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup> unto thee, thou
false<sup>[7423](h7423)</sup> tongue?<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup>

4  Sharp<sup>[8150](h8150)</sup> arrows<sup>[2671](h2671)</sup> of the
mighty,<sup>[1368](h1368)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup>
coals<sup>[1513](h1513)</sup> of juniper.<sup>[7574](h7574)</sup>

5  Woe<sup>[190](h0190)</sup> is me, that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I
sojourn<sup>[1481](h1481)</sup> in Mesech,<sup>[4902](h4902)</sup> that
I dwell<sup>[7931](h7931)</sup> in<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the
tents<sup>[168](h0168)</sup> of Kedar!<sup>[6938](h6938)</sup>

6  My soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> hath long<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup>
dwelt<sup>[7931](h7931)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> him that
hateth<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup> peace.<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup>

7  I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am for peace:<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> but
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I speak,<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup>
they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> are for war.<sup>[4421](h4421)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa120)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν ἦρα τοὺς ὀφθαλμούς μου εἰς τὰ ὄρη πόθεν ἥξει ἡ
βοήθειά μου

2 ἡ βοήθειά μου παρὰ κυρίου τοῦ ποιήσαντος τὸν οὐρανὸν καὶ τὴν γῆν

3 μὴ δῷς εἰς σάλον τὸν πόδα σου μηδὲ νυστάξῃ ὁ φυλάσσων σε

4 ἰδοὺ οὐ νυστάξει οὐδὲ ὑπνώσει ὁ φυλάσσων τὸν Ισραηλ

5 κύριος φυλάξει σε κύριος σκέπη σου ἐπὶ χεῖρα δεξιάν σου

6 ἡμέρας ὁ ἥλιος οὐ συγκαύσει σε οὐδὲ ἡ σελήνη τὴν νύκτα

7 κύριος φυλάξει σε ἀπὸ παντὸς κακοῦ φυλάξει τὴν ψυχήν σου

8 κύριος φυλάξει τὴν εἴσοδόν σου καὶ τὴν ἔξοδόν σου ἀπὸ τοῦ νῦν καὶ ἕως
τοῦ αἰῶνος

[Vul](../vul/psa120)

1 Canticum graduum. \[Levavi oculos meos in montes,  
unde veniet auxilium mihi.  

2 Auxilium meum a Domino,  
qui fecit cælum et terram.  

3 Non det in commotionem pedem tuum,  
neque dormitet qui custodit te.  

4 Ecce non dormitabit neque dormiet  
qui custodit Israël.  

5 Dominus custodit te;  
Dominus protectio tua super manum dexteram tuam.  

6 Per diem sol non uret te,  
neque luna per noctem.  

7 Dominus custodit te ab omni malo;  
custodiat animam tuam Dominus.  

8 Dominus custodiat introitum tuum et exitum tuum,  
ex hoc nunc et usque in sæculum.\]
