H2254  
חבל  
חָבַל ‎ châbal  
*khaw-bal‘*  
A primitive root; to *wind* tightly (as a rope), that is, to *bind*;
specifically by a *pledge*; figuratively to *pervert*, *destroy*; also
to *writhe* in pain (especially of parturition): - X at all, band, bring
forth, (deal) corrupt (-ly) destroy, offend, lay to (take a) pledge,
spoil, travail, X very, withhold.
