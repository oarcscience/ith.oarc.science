H2710
חקק
חָקַק ‎ châqaq
khaw-kak‘
A primitive root; properly to hack, that is, engrave (Jdg 5:14, to be a scribe simply); by implication to enact (laws being cut in stone or metal tablets in primitive times) or (generally) prescribe: - appoint, decree, governor, grave, lawgiver, note, pourtray, print, set.
