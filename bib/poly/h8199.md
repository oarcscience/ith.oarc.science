H8199  
שׁפט  
שָׁפַט ‎ shâphaṭ  
*shaw-fat‘*  
A primitive root; to *judge*, that is, pronounce *sentence* (for or
against); by implication to *vindicate* or *punish*; by extension to
*govern*; passively to *litigate* (literally or figuratively): - +
avenge, X that condemn, contend, defend, execute (judgment), (be a)
judge (-ment), X needs, plead, reason, rule.
