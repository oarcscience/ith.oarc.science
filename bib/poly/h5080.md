H5080  
נדח  
נָדַח ‎ nâdach  
*naw-dakh‘*  
A primitive root; to *push* off; used in a great variety of
applications, literally and figuratively (to expel, mislead, strike,
inflict, etc.): - banish, bring, cast down (out), chase, compel, draw
away, drive (away, out, quite), fetch a stroke, force, go away, outcast,
thrust away (out), withdraw.
