מִקוֶה מִקוֵה מִקוֵא ‎ miqveh miqvêh miqvê‘
mik-veh‘, mik-vay‘, mik-vay‘
From 6960 something waited for, that is, confidence (objectively or subjectively); also a collection, that is, (of water) a pond, or (of men and horses) a caravan or drove: - abiding, gathering together, hope, linen yarn, plenty [of water], pool.
