G5591  
ψυχικός  
psuchikos  
*psoo-khee-kos‘*  
From [5590](g5590.md) *sensitive* that is, *animate* (in distinction on the
one hand from [4152](g4152.md) which is the higher or *renovated* nature;
and on the other from [5446](g5446.md) which is the lower or *bestial*
nature): - natural, sensual.
