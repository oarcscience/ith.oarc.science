H983  
בּטח  
בֶּטַח ‎ beṭach  
*beh‘-takh*  
From [982](h0982) properly a place of *refuge*; abstractly *safety*,
both the fact (*security*) and the feeling (*trust*); often (adverbially
with or without preposition) *safely: -* assurance, boldly, (without)
care (-less), confidence, hope, safe (-ly, -ty), secure, surely.
