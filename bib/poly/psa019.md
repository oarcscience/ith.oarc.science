### Psalms 19

[KJV](../kjv/psa019.md)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup> A
Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup> The
heavens<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> declare<sup>[5608](h5608.md)</sup> the
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> of God;<sup>[410](h0410.md)</sup> and the
firmament<sup>[7549](h7549)</sup> showeth<sup>[5046](h5046.md)</sup> his
handiwork.<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup>

2  Day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> unto day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup>
uttereth<sup>[5042](h5042)</sup> speech,<sup>[562](h0562.md)</sup> and
night<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup> unto night<sup>[3915](h3915.md)</sup>
showeth<sup>[2331](h2331)</sup> knowledge.<sup>[1847](h1847.md)</sup>

3  There is no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> speech<sup>[562](h0562.md)</sup>
nor<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> language,<sup>[1697](h1697.md)</sup> where their
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> is not<sup>[1097](h1097.md)</sup>
heard.<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup>

4  Their line<sup>[6957](h6957.md)</sup> is gone
out<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> through all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
earth,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> and their words<sup>[4405](h4405.md)</sup> to
the end<sup>[7097](h7097)</sup> of the world.<sup>[8398](h8398.md)</sup> In
them hath he set<sup>[7760](h7760.md)</sup> a
tabernacle<sup>[168](h0168)</sup> for the sun,<sup>[8121](h8121.md)</sup>

5  Which<sup>[1931](h1931.md)</sup> is as a
bridegroom<sup>[2860](h2860)</sup> coming out<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup> of
his chamber,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2646](h2646.md)</sup> and
rejoiceth<sup>[7797](h7797.md)</sup> as a strong
man<sup>[1368](h1368)</sup> to run<sup>[7323](h7323.md)</sup> a
race.<sup>[734](h0734.md)</sup>

6  His going forth<sup>[4161](h4161.md)</sup> is from the
end<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7097](h7097.md)</sup> of the
heaven,<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> and his circuit<sup>[8622](h8622.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the ends<sup>[7098](h7098.md)</sup> of it: and
there is nothing<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> hid<sup>[5641](h5641.md)</sup> from
the heat<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2535](h2535.md)</sup> thereof.

7  The law<sup>[8451](h8451)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
is perfect,<sup>[8549](h8549)</sup> converting<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup>
the soul:<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> the testimony<sup>[5715](h5715.md)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is sure,<sup>[539](h0539.md)</sup>
making wise<sup>[2449](h2449)</sup> the simple.<sup>[6612](h6612.md)</sup>

8  The statutes<sup>[6490](h6490.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> are right,<sup>[3477](h3477.md)</sup>
rejoicing<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> the heart:<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup> the
commandment<sup>[4687](h4687)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
is pure,<sup>[1249](h1249)</sup> enlightening<sup>[215](h0215.md)</sup> the
eyes.<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup>

9  The fear<sup>[3374](h3374)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
is clean,<sup>[2889](h2889)</sup> enduring<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup>
forever:<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup> the judgments<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup>
of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> are true<sup>[571](h0571.md)</sup> and
righteous<sup>[6663](h6663)</sup> altogether.<sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup>

10  More to be desired<sup>[2530](h2530.md)</sup> are they than
gold,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2091](h2091.md)</sup> yea, than
much<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> fine gold:<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[6337](h6337)</sup> sweeter<sup>[4966](h4966.md)</sup> also than
honey<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1706](h1706.md)</sup> and the
honeycomb.<sup>[5317](h5317)</sup> <sup>[6688](h6688.md)</sup>

11  Moreover<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup> by them is thy
servant<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> warned:<sup>[2094](h2094.md)</sup> and in
keeping<sup>[8104](h8104.md)</sup> of them there is
great<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> reward.<sup>[6118](h6118.md)</sup>

12  Who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> can understand<sup>[995](h0995.md)</sup>
his errors?<sup>[7691](h7691)</sup> cleanse<sup>[5352](h5352.md)</sup> thou
me from secret<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5641](h5641.md)</sup> faults.

13  Keep back<sup>[2820](h2820.md)</sup> thy
servant<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> also<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup> from
presumptuous<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2086](h2086.md)</sup> sins; let
them not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> have dominion<sup>[4910](h4910.md)</sup>
over me: then<sup>[227](h0227.md)</sup> shall I be
upright,<sup>[8552](h8552.md)</sup> and I shall be
innocent<sup>[5352](h5352)</sup> from the great<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup>
transgression.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6588](h6588.md)</sup>

14  Let the words<sup>[561](h0561.md)</sup> of my
mouth,<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> and the
meditation<sup>[1902](h1902)</sup> of my heart,<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> acceptable<sup>[7522](h7522.md)</sup> in thy
sight,<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> my
strength,<sup>[6697](h6697.md)</sup> and my
redeemer.<sup>[1350](h1350.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa019.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 ἐπακούσαι σου κύριος ἐν ἡμέρᾳ θλίψεως ὑπερασπίσαι σου τὸ ὄνομα τοῦ
θεοῦ Ιακωβ

3 ἐξαποστείλαι σοι βοήθειαν ἐξ ἁγίου καὶ ἐκ Σιων ἀντιλάβοιτό σου

4 μνησθείη πάσης θυσίας σου καὶ τὸ ὁλοκαύτωμά σου πιανάτω διάψαλμα

5 δῴη σοι κατὰ τὴν καρδίαν σου καὶ πᾶσαν τὴν βουλήν σου πληρώσαι

6 ἀγαλλιασόμεθα ἐν τῷ σωτηρίῳ σου καὶ ἐν ὀνόματι θεοῦ ἡμῶν
μεγαλυνθησόμεθα πληρώσαι κύριος πάντα τὰ αἰτήματά σου

7 νῦν ἔγνων ὅτι ἔσωσεν κύριος τὸν χριστὸν αὐτοῦ ἐπακούσεται αὐτοῦ ἐξ
οὐρανοῦ ἁγίου αὐτοῦ ἐν δυναστείαις ἡ σωτηρία τῆς δεξιᾶς αὐτοῦ

8 οὗτοι ἐν ἅρμασιν καὶ οὗτοι ἐν ἵπποις ἡμεῖς δὲ ἐν ὀνόματι κυρίου θεοῦ
ἡμῶν μεγαλυνθησόμεθα

9 αὐτοὶ συνεποδίσθησαν καὶ ἔπεσαν ἡμεῖς δὲ ἀνέστημεν καὶ ἀνωρθώθημεν

10 κύριε σῶσον τὸν βασιλέα σου καὶ ἐπάκουσον ἡμῶν ἐν ᾗ ἂν ἡμέρᾳ
ἐπικαλεσώμεθά σε

יְחַוֶּה־דָּֽעַת׃

‎5 ‏בְּכָל־הָאָ֨רֶץ׀ יָ֘צָ֤א קַוָּ֗ם וּבִקְצֵ֣ה תֵ֭בֵל מִלֵּיהֶ֑ם
לַ֝שֶּׁ֗מֶשׁ שָֽׂם־אֹ֥הֶל בָּהֶֽם׃

[Vul](../vul/psa019.md)

1 In finem. Psalmus David.

2 \[Exaudiat te Dominus in die tribulationis;  
protegat te nomen Dei Jacob.  

3 Mittat tibi auxilium de sancto,  
et de Sion tueatur te.  

4 Memor sit omnis sacrificii tui,  
et holocaustum tuum pingue fiat.  

5 Tribuat tibi secundum cor tuum,  
et omne consilium tuum confirmet.  

6 Lætabimur in salutari tuo;  
et in nomine Dei nostri magnificabimur.  

7 Impleat Dominus omnes petitiones tuas;  
nunc cognovi quoniam salvum fecit Dominus christum suum.  
Exaudiet illum de cælo sancto suo,  
in potentatibus salus dexteræ ejus.  

8 Hi in curribus, et hi in equis;  
nos autem in nomine Domini Dei nostri invocabimus.  

9 Ipsi obligati sunt, et ceciderunt;  
nos autem surreximus, et erecti sumus.  

10 Domine, salvum fac regem,  
et exaudi nos in die qua invocaverimus te.\]
