G5158  
τρόπος  
tropos  
*trop‘-os*  
From the same as [5157](g5157) a *turn*, that is, (by implication.md)
*mode* or *style* (especially with preposition or relatively prefixed as
adverb *like*); figuratively *deportment* or *character:* - (even) as,
conversation, \[+ like\] manner (+ by any) means, way.
