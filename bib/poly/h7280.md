H7280  
רגע  
רָגַע ‎ râga‛  
*raw-gah‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *toss* violently and suddenly (the sea
with waves, the skin with boils); figuratively (in a favorable manner)
to *settle*, that is, quiet; specifically to *wink* (from the motion of
the eye lids): - break, divide, find ease, be a moment, (cause, give,
make to) rest, make suddenly.
