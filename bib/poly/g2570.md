G2570  
καλός  
kalos  
*kal-os‘*  
Of uncertain affinity; properly *beautiful*, but chiefly (figuratively)
*good* (literally or morally), that is, *valuable* or *virtuous* (for
*appearance* or *use*, and thus distinguished from [18](g0018.md) which is
properly *intrinsic*): - X better, fair, good (-ly), honest, meet, well,
worthy.
