G2233  
ἡγέομαι  
hēgeomai  
*hayg-eh‘-om-ahee*  
Middle voice of a (presumed) strengthened form of [71](g0071) to *lead*,
that is, *command* (with official authority); figuratively to *deem*,
that is, *consider:* - account, (be) chief, count, esteem, governor,
judge, have the rule over, suppose, think.
