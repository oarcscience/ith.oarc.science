H4496  
מנחה מנוּחה  
מְנוּחָה מְנוּחָה ‎ m<sup>e</sup>nûchâh m<sup>e</sup>nûchâh  
*men-oo-khaw‘,* *men-oo-khaw‘*  
Feminine of [4495](h4495) *repose* or (adverbially) *peacefully*;
figuratively *consolation* (specifically *matrimony*); hence
(concretely) an *abode: -* comfortable, ease, quiet, rest (-ing place),
still.
