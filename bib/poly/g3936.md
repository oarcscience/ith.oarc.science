G3936  
παρίστημι, παριστάνω  
paristēmi paristanō  
*par-is‘-tay-mee,* *par-is-tan‘-o*  
From [3488](g3488) and [2476](g2476) to *stand* *beside*, that is,
(transitively) to *exhibit*, *proffer*, (specifically) *recommend*,
(figuratively) *substantiate*; or (intransitively) to *be* *at* *hand*
(or *ready*), *aid:* - assist, bring before, command, commend, give
presently, present, prove, provide, shew, stand (before, by, here, up,
with), yield.
