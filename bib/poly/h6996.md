H6996  
קטן קטן  
קָטָן קָטוֹן ‎ qâṭân qâṭôn  
*kaw-tawn‘,* *kaw-tone‘*  
From [6962](h6962) *abbreviated*, that is, *diminutive*, literally (in
quantity, size or number) or figuratively (in age or importance): -
least, less (-ser), little (one), small (-est, one, quantity, thing),
young (-er, -est).
