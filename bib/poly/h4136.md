H4136  
מל מואל מול מוּל  
מוּל מוֹל מוֹאל מוּל ‎ mûl môl mô‘l mûl  
*mool,* *mole,* *mole,* *mool*  
From [4135](h4135.md) properly *abrupt*, that is, a *precipice*; by
implication the *front*; used only adverbially (with prepositional
prefix) *opposite: -* (over) against, before, \[fore-\] front, from,
\[God-\] ward, toward, with.
