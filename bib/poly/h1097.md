H1097  
בּלי  
בְּלִי ‎ b<sup>e</sup>lı̂y  
*bel-ee‘*  
From [1086](h1086) properly *failure*, that is, *nothing* or
*destruction*; usually (with preposition) *without*, *not* *yet*,
*because* *not*, *as* *long* *as*, etc.: - corruption, ig\[norantly\],
for lack of, where no . . . is, so that no, none, not, un\[awares\],
without.
