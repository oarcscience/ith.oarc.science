H7925
שׁכם
שָׁכַם ‎ shâkam
shaw-kam‘
A primitive root; properly to incline (the shoulder to a burden); but used only as denominative from 7926 literally to load up (on the back of man or beast), that is, to start early in the morning: - (arise, be up, get [oneself] up, rise up) early (betimes), morning.
