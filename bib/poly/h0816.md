H816  
אשׁם אשׁם  
אָשַׁם אָשֵׁם ‎ ‘âsham ‘âshêm  
*aw-sham‘,* *aw-shame‘*  
A primitive root; *to* *be* *guilty*; by implication to *be* *punished*
or *perish: -* X certainly, be (-come, made) desolate, destroy, X
greatly, be (-come, found, hold) guilty, offend (acknowledge offence),
trespassive
