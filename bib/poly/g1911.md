G1911  
ἐπιβάλλω  
epiballō  
*ep-ee-bal‘-lo*  
From [1909](g1909) and [906](g0906) to *throw* *upon* (literally or
figuratively, transitively or reflexively; usually with more or less
force); specifically (with [1438](g1438) implied) to *reflect*;
impersonally to *belong* *to:* - beat into, cast (up-), on, fall, lay
(on), put (unto), stretch forth, think on.
