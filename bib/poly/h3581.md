H3581  
כּוח כּח  
כּוַֹח כּוַֹח ‎ kôach kôach  
*ko‘-akh,* *ko‘-akh*  
From an unused root meaning to *be* *firm*; *vigor*, literally (*force*,
in a good or a bad sense) or figuratively (*capacity*, *means*,
*produce*); also (from its hardiness) a large *lizard: -* ability, able,
chameleon, force, fruits, might, power (-ful), strength, substance,
wealth.
