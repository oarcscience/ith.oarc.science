H3887  
לוּץ  
לוּץ ‎ lûts  
*loots*  
A primitive root; properly to *make* *mouths* at, that is, to *scoff*;
hence (from the effort to pronounce a foreign language) to *interpret*,
or (generally) *intercede: -* ambassador, have in derision, interpreter,
make a mock, mocker, scorn (-er, -ful), teacher.
