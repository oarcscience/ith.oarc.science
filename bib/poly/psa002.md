### Psalms 2

[KJV](../kjv/psa002.md)

1  Why<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> do the heathen<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup>
rage,<sup>[7283](h7283)</sup> and the people<sup>[3816](h3816.md)</sup>
imagine<sup>[1897](h1897)</sup> a vain thing?<sup>[7385](h7385.md)</sup>

2  The kings<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> of the earth<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>
set themselves,<sup>[3320](h3320.md)</sup> and the
rulers<sup>[7336](h7336)</sup> take counsel<sup>[3245](h3245.md)</sup>
together,<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> his
anointed,<sup>[4899](h4899.md)</sup> saying,

3  Let us break their bands asunder,<sup>[5423](h5423.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> <sup>[4147](h4147.md)</sup> and cast
away<sup>[7993](h7993)</sup> their cords<sup>[5688](h5688.md)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> us.

4  He that sitteth<sup>[3427](h3427.md)</sup> in the
heavens<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> shall laugh:<sup>[7832](h7832.md)</sup> the
Lord<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> shall have them in
derision.<sup>[3932](h3932.md)</sup>

5  Then<sup>[227](h0227)</sup> shall he speak<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> them in his wrath,<sup>[639](h0639.md)</sup>
and vex<sup>[926](h0926.md)</sup> them in his sore
displeasure.<sup>[2740](h2740.md)</sup>

6  Yet have I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> set<sup>[5258](h5258.md)</sup> my
king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> my
holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> hill<sup>[2022](h2022.md)</sup> of
Zion.<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>

7  I will declare<sup>[5608](h5608)</sup> <sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
decree:<sup>[2706](h2706)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> hath
said<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> me,
Thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art my Son;<sup>[1121](h1121.md)</sup> this
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> have I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup>
begotten<sup>[3205](h3205.md)</sup> thee.

8  Ask<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> of<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me, and I
shall give<sup>[5414](h5414.md)</sup> thee the
heathen<sup>[1471](h1471.md)</sup> for thine
inheritance,<sup>[5159](h5159.md)</sup> and the uttermost
parts<sup>[657](h0657)</sup> of the earth<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> for thy
possession.<sup>[272](h0272.md)</sup>

9  Thou shalt break<sup>[7489](h7489.md)</sup> them with a
rod<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> of iron;<sup>[1270](h1270.md)</sup> thou shalt
dash them in pieces<sup>[5310](h5310.md)</sup> like a
potter's<sup>[3335](h3335)</sup> vessel.<sup>[3627](h3627.md)</sup>

10  Be wise<sup>[7919](h7919)</sup> now<sup>[6258](h6258.md)</sup>
therefore, O ye kings:<sup>[4428](h4428.md)</sup> be
instructed,<sup>[3256](h3256)</sup> ye judges<sup>[8199](h8199.md)</sup> of
the earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

11  Serve<sup>[5647](h5647)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> with fear,<sup>[3374](h3374.md)</sup> and
rejoice<sup>[1523](h1523)</sup> with trembling.<sup>[7461](h7461.md)</sup>

12  Kiss<sup>[5401](h5401)</sup> the Son,<sup>[1248](h1248.md)</sup>
lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> he be angry,<sup>[599](h0599.md)</sup> and ye
perish<sup>[6](h0006)</sup> from the way,<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup>
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> his wrath<sup>[639](h0639.md)</sup> is
kindled<sup>[1197](h1197)</sup> but a little.<sup>[4592](h4592.md)</sup>
Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> are all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> they that
put their trust<sup>[2620](h2620.md)</sup> in him.

[Sep](../sep/psa002.md)

1 ἵνα τί ἐφρύαξαν ἔθνη καὶ λαοὶ ἐμελέτησαν κενά

2 παρέστησαν οἱ βασιλεῖς τῆς γῆς καὶ οἱ ἄρχοντες συνήχθησαν ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ
κατὰ τοῦ κυρίου καὶ κατὰ τοῦ χριστοῦ αὐτοῦ διάψαλμα

3 διαρρήξωμεν τοὺς δεσμοὺς αὐτῶν καὶ ἀπορρίψωμεν ἀφ᾽ ἡμῶν τὸν ζυγὸν
αὐτῶν

4 ὁ κατοικῶν ἐν οὐρανοῖς ἐκγελάσεται αὐτούς καὶ ὁ κύριος ἐκμυκτηριεῖ
αὐτούς

5 τότε λαλήσει πρὸς αὐτοὺς ἐν ὀργῇ αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐν τῷ θυμῷ αὐτοῦ ταράξει
αὐτούς

6 ἐγὼ δὲ κατεστάθην βασιλεὺς ὑπ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ Σιων ὄρος τὸ ἅγιον αὐτοῦ

7 διαγγέλλων τὸ πρόσταγμα κυρίου κύριος εἶπεν πρός με υἱός μου εἶ σύ ἐγὼ
σήμερον γεγέννηκά σε

8 αἴτησαι παρ᾽ ἐμοῦ καὶ δώσω σοι ἔθνη τὴν κληρονομίαν σου καὶ τὴν
κατάσχεσίν σου τὰ πέρατα τῆς γῆς

9 ποιμανεῖς αὐτοὺς ἐν ῥάβδῳ σιδηρᾷ ὡς σκεῦος κεραμέως συντρίψεις αὐτούς

10 καὶ νῦν βασιλεῖς σύνετε παιδεύθητε πάντες οἱ κρίνοντες τὴν γῆν

11 δουλεύσατε τῷ κυρίῳ ἐν φόβῳ καὶ ἀγαλλιᾶσθε αὐτῷ ἐν τρόμῳ

12 δράξασθε παιδείας μήποτε ὀργισθῇ κύριος καὶ ἀπολεῖσθε ἐξ ὁδοῦ δικαίας
ὅταν ἐκκαυθῇ ἐν τάχει ὁ θυμὸς αὐτοῦ μακάριοι πάντες οἱ πεποιθότες ἐπ᾽
αὐτῷ

[Vul](../vul/psa002.md)

1 \[Quare fremuerunt gentes,  
et populi meditati sunt inania?  

2 Astiterunt reges terræ,  
et principes convenerunt in unum  
adversus Dominum, et adversus christum ejus.  

3 Dirumpamus vincula eorum,  
et projiciamus a nobis jugum ipsorum.  

4 Qui habitat in cælis irridebit eos,  
et Dominus subsannabit eos.  

5 Tunc loquetur ad eos in ira sua,  
et in furore suo conturbabit eos.  

6 Ego autem constitutus sum rex ab eo  
super Sion, montem sanctum ejus,  
prædicans præceptum ejus.  

7 Dominus dixit ad me: Filius meus es tu;  
ego hodie genui te.  

8 Postula a me, et dabo tibi gentes hæreditatem tuam,  
et possessionem tuam terminos terræ.  

9 Reges eos in virga ferrea,  
et tamquam vas figuli confringes eos.  

10 Et nunc, reges, intelligite;  
erudimini, qui judicatis terram.  

11 Servite Domino in timore,  
et exsultate ei cum tremore.  

12 Apprehendite disciplinam, nequando irascatur Dominus,  
et pereatis de via justa.  

13 Cum exarserit in brevi ira ejus,  
beati omnes qui confidunt in eo.\]
