### Psalms 97

[KJV](../kjv/psa097.md)

1  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> reigneth;<sup>[4427](h4427.md)</sup>
let the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> rejoice;<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup>
let the multitude<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> of
isles<sup>[339](h0339)</sup> be glad<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> thereof.

2  Clouds<sup>[6051](h6051)</sup> and darkness<sup>[6205](h6205.md)</sup>
are round about<sup>[5439](h5439.md)</sup> him:
righteousness<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup> and
judgment<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup> are the
habitation<sup>[4349](h4349.md)</sup> of his
throne.<sup>[3678](h3678.md)</sup>

3  A fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> goeth<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> him, and burneth
up<sup>[3857](h3857)</sup> his enemies<sup>[6862](h6862.md)</sup> round
about.<sup>[5439](h5439.md)</sup>

4  His lightnings<sup>[1300](h1300.md)</sup>
enlightened<sup>[215](h0215)</sup> the world:<sup>[8398](h8398.md)</sup>
the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> saw,<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> and
trembled.<sup>[2342](h2342.md)</sup>

5  The hills<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> melted<sup>[4549](h4549.md)</sup> like
wax<sup>[1749](h1749)</sup> at the presence<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> at the
presence<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> of the
Lord<sup>[113](h0113)</sup> of the whole<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
earth.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

6  The heavens<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> declare<sup>[5046](h5046.md)</sup>
his righteousness,<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup> and
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the people<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> his glory.<sup>[3519](h3519.md)</sup>

7  Confounded<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> be all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> they
that serve<sup>[5647](h5647.md)</sup> graven
images,<sup>[6459](h6459.md)</sup> that boast
themselves<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> of idols:<sup>[457](h0457.md)</sup>
worship<sup>[7812](h7812)</sup> him, all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> ye
gods.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

8  Zion<sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> heard,<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> and was
glad;<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> and the daughters<sup>[1323](h1323.md)</sup>
of Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> rejoiced<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup>
because of<sup>[4616](h4616.md)</sup> thy
judgments,<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> O LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

9  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou,<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup>
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> art high<sup>[5945](h5945.md)</sup>
above<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
earth:<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> thou art exalted<sup>[5927](h5927.md)</sup>
far<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> above<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> gods.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

10  Ye that love<sup>[157](h0157.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hate<sup>[8130](h8130.md)</sup>
evil:<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> he preserveth<sup>[8104](h8104.md)</sup> the
souls<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> of his saints;<sup>[2623](h2623.md)</sup> he
delivereth<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> them out of the
hand<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of the
wicked.<sup>[7563](h7563.md)</sup>

11  Light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> is sown<sup>[2232](h2232.md)</sup> for the
righteous,<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> and gladness<sup>[8057](h8057.md)</sup>
for the upright<sup>[3477](h3477.md)</sup> in
heart.<sup>[3820](h3820.md)</sup>

12  Rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> in the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
ye righteous;<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup> and give
thanks<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup> at the
remembrance<sup>[2143](h2143.md)</sup> of his
holiness.<sup>[6944](h6944.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa097.md)

1 ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ ᾄσατε τῷ κυρίῳ ᾆσμα καινόν ὅτι θαυμαστὰ ἐποίησεν
κύριος ἔσωσεν αὐτῷ ἡ δεξιὰ αὐτοῦ καὶ ὁ βραχίων ὁ ἅγιος αὐτοῦ

2 ἐγνώρισεν κύριος τὸ σωτήριον αὐτοῦ ἐναντίον τῶν ἐθνῶν ἀπεκάλυψεν τὴν
δικαιοσύνην αὐτοῦ

3 ἐμνήσθη τοῦ ἐλέους αὐτοῦ τῷ Ιακωβ καὶ τῆς ἀληθείας αὐτοῦ τῷ οἴκῳ
Ισραηλ εἴδοσαν πάντα τὰ πέρατα τῆς γῆς τὸ σωτήριον τοῦ θεοῦ ἡμῶν

4 ἀλαλάξατε τῷ θεῷ πᾶσα ἡ γῆ ᾄσατε καὶ ἀγαλλιᾶσθε καὶ ψάλατε

5 ψάλατε τῷ κυρίῳ ἐν κιθάρᾳ ἐν κιθάρᾳ καὶ φωνῇ ψαλμοῦ

6 ἐν σάλπιγξιν ἐλαταῖς καὶ φωνῇ σάλπιγγος κερατίνης ἀλαλάξατε ἐνώπιον
τοῦ βασιλέως κυρίου

7 σαλευθήτω ἡ θάλασσα καὶ τὸ πλήρωμα αὐτῆς ἡ οἰκουμένη καὶ οἱ
κατοικοῦντες ἐν αὐτῇ

8 ποταμοὶ κροτήσουσιν χειρὶ ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτό τὰ ὄρη ἀγαλλιάσονται

9 ὅτι ἥκει κρῖναι τὴν γῆν κρινεῖ τὴν οἰκουμένην ἐν δικαιοσύνῃ καὶ λαοὺς
ἐν εὐθύτητι

‎3 ‏אֵ֭שׁ לְפָנָ֣יו תֵּלֵ֑ךְ וּתְלַהֵ֖ט סָבִ֣יב צָרָֽיו׃

‎4 ‏הֵאִ֣ירוּ בְרָקָ֣יו תֵּבֵ֑ל רָאֲתָ֖ה וַתָּחֵ֣ל הָאָֽרֶץ׃

‎5 ‏הָרִ֗ים כַּדּוֹנַ֗ג נָ֭מַסּוּ מִלִּפְנֵ֣י יְהוָ֑ה מִ֝לִּפְנֵ֗י

quia mirabilia fecit.  
Salvavit sibi dextera ejus,  
et brachium sanctum ejus.  

2 Notum fecit Dominus salutare suum;  
in conspectu gentium revelavit justitiam suam.  

3 Recordatus est misericordiæ suæ,  
et veritatis suæ domui Israël.  
Viderunt omnes termini terræ  
salutare Dei nostri.  

4 Jubilate Deo, omnis terra;  
cantate, et exsultate, et psallite.  

5 Psallite Domino in cithara;  
in cithara et voce psalmi;  

6 in tubis ductilibus, et voce tubæ corneæ.  
Jubilate in conspectu regis Domini:  

7 moveatur mare, et plenitudo ejus;  
orbis terrarum, et qui habitant in eo.  

8 Flumina plaudent manu;  
simul montes exsultabunt

9 a conspectu Domini:  
quoniam venit judicare terram.  
Judicabit orbem terrarum in justitia,  
et populos in æquitate.\]
