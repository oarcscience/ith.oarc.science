G2647  
καταλύω  
kataluō  
*kat-al-oo‘-o*  
From [2596](g2596) and [3089](g3089) to *loosen* *down*
(*disintegrate*), that is, (by implication) to *demolish* (literally or
figuratively); specifically (compare [2646](g2646) to *halt* for the
night: - destroy, dissolve, be guest, lodge, come to nought, overthrow,
throw down.
