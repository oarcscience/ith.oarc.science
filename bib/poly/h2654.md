H2654  
חפץ  
חָפֵץ ‎ châphêts  
*khaw-fates‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *incline* to; by implication (literally
but rarely) to *bend*; figuratively to *be* *pleased* with, *desire: -*
X any at all, (have, take) delight, desire, favour, like, move, be
(well) pleased, have pleasure, will, would.
