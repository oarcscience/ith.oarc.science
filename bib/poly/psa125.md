### Psalms 125

[KJV](../kjv/psa125)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees.<sup>[4609](h4609)</sup>
They that trust<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall be as mount<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup>
Zion,<sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> which cannot<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be
removed,<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> but abideth<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup>
forever.<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup>

2  As the mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> are round
about<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> Jerusalem,<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> so the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is round about<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> his
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> from henceforth<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> even forever.<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup>

3  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the rod<sup>[7626](h7626)</sup> of the
wicked<sup>[7562](h7562)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
rest<sup>[5117](h5117)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
lot<sup>[1486](h1486)</sup> of the righteous;<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup>
lest<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> <sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> put forth<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup>
their hands<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> unto
iniquity.<sup>[5766](h5766)</sup>

4  Do good,<sup>[3190](h3190)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> unto
those that be good,<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> and to them that are
upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> in their hearts.<sup>[3826](h3826)</sup>

5  As for such as turn aside<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> unto their crooked
ways,<sup>[6128](h6128)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall
lead them forth<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> the
workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of iniquity:<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> but
peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> shall be upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa125)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν ἐν τῷ ἐπιστρέψαι κύριον τὴν αἰχμαλωσίαν Σιων
ἐγενήθημεν ὡς παρακεκλημένοι

2 τότε ἐπλήσθη χαρᾶς τὸ στόμα ἡμῶν καὶ ἡ γλῶσσα ἡμῶν ἀγαλλιάσεως τότε
ἐροῦσιν ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ἐμεγάλυνεν κύριος τοῦ ποιῆσαι μετ᾽ αὐτῶν

3 ἐμεγάλυνεν κύριος τοῦ ποιῆσαι μεθ᾽ ἡμῶν ἐγενήθημεν εὐφραινόμενοι

4 ἐπίστρεψον κύριε τὴν αἰχμαλωσίαν ἡμῶν ὡς χειμάρρους ἐν τῷ νότῳ

5 οἱ σπείροντες ἐν δάκρυσιν ἐν ἀγαλλιάσει θεριοῦσιν

6 πορευόμενοι ἐπορεύοντο καὶ ἔκλαιον αἴροντες τὰ σπέρματα αὐτῶν
ἐρχόμενοι δὲ ἥξουσιν ἐν ἀγαλλιάσει αἴροντες τὰ δράγματα αὐτῶν

[Vul](../vul/psa125)

1 Canticum graduum. \[In convertendo Dominus captivitatem Sion,  
facti sumus sicut consolati.  

2 Tunc repletum est gaudio os nostrum,  
et lingua nostra exsultatione.  
Tunc dicent inter gentes:  
Magnificavit Dominus facere cum eis.  

3 Magnificavit Dominus facere nobiscum;  
facti sumus lætantes.  

4 Converte, Domine, captivitatem nostram,  
sicut torrens in austro.  

5 Qui seminant in lacrimis,  
in exsultatione metent.  

6 Euntes ibant et flebant,  
mittentes semina sua.  
Venientes autem venient cum exsultatione,  
portantes manipulos suos.\]
