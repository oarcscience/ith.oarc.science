### Psalms 94

[KJV](../kjv/psa094)

1  O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> God,<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> to whom
vengeance<sup>[5360](h5360)</sup> belongeth; O
God,<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> to whom vengeance<sup>[5360](h5360)</sup>
belongeth, show thyself.<sup>[3313](h3313)</sup>

2  Lift up thyself,<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> thou
judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> of the earth:<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>
render<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> a reward<sup>[1576](h1576)</sup>
to<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the proud.<sup>[1343](h1343)</sup>

3  LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> how long<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[4970](h4970)</sup> shall the wicked,<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> how
long<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[4970](h4970)</sup> shall the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> triumph?<sup>[5937](h5937)</sup>

4  How long shall they utter<sup>[5042](h5042)</sup> and
speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> hard things?<sup>[6277](h6277)</sup> and
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of
iniquity<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> boast themselves?<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>

5  They break in pieces<sup>[1792](h1792)</sup> thy
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> and
afflict<sup>[6031](h6031)</sup> thine heritage.<sup>[5159](h5159)</sup>

6  They slay<sup>[2026](h2026)</sup> the widow<sup>[490](h0490)</sup>
and the stranger,<sup>[1616](h1616)</sup> and
murder<sup>[7523](h7523)</sup> the fatherless.<sup>[3490](h3490)</sup>

7  Yet they say,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> The LORD<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup>
shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> see,<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> shall the God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> regard<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> it.

8  Understand,<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> ye brutish<sup>[1197](h1197)</sup>
among the people:<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> and ye
fools,<sup>[3684](h3684)</sup> when<sup>[4970](h4970)</sup> will ye be
wise?<sup>[7919](h7919)</sup>

9  He that planted<sup>[5193](h5193)</sup> the
ear,<sup>[241](h0241)</sup> shall he not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
hear?<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> he that formed<sup>[3335](h3335)</sup> the
eye,<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> shall he not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
see?<sup>[5027](h5027)</sup>

10  He that chastiseth<sup>[3256](h3256)</sup> the
heathen,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> he
correct?<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup> he that
teacheth<sup>[3925](h3925)</sup> man<sup>[120](h0120)</sup>
knowledge,<sup>[1847](h1847)</sup> shall not he know?

11  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> knoweth<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> the
thoughts<sup>[4284](h4284)</sup> of man,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> are
vanity.<sup>[1892](h1892)</sup>

12  Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> is the man<sup>[1397](h1397)</sup>
whom<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> thou chastenest,<sup>[3256](h3256)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup> and teachest<sup>[3925](h3925)</sup> him
out of thy law;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8451](h8451)</sup>

13  That thou mayest give him rest<sup>[8252](h8252)</sup> from the
days<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of
adversity,<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> until<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the
pit<sup>[7845](h7845)</sup> be digged<sup>[3738](h3738)</sup> for the
wicked.<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup>

14  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> cast off<sup>[5203](h5203)</sup> his
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> will he
forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> his inheritance.<sup>[5159](h5159)</sup>

15  But<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> judgment<sup>[4941](h4941)</sup> shall
return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
righteousness:<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> and all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
the upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> in heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>
shall follow<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> it.

16  Who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> will rise up<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> for
me against<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the
evildoers?<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> or who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> will
stand up<sup>[3320](h3320)</sup> for me against<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup>
the workers<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup> of iniquity?<sup>[205](h0205)</sup>

17  Unless<sup>[3884](h3884)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> had
been my help,<sup>[5833](h5833)</sup> my soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup>
had almost<sup>[4592](h4592)</sup> dwelt<sup>[7931](h7931)</sup> in
silence.<sup>[1745](h1745)</sup>

18  When<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> I said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> My
foot<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> slippeth;<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> thy
mercy,<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> held me
up.<sup>[5582](h5582)</sup>

19  In the multitude<sup>[7230](h7230)</sup> of my
thoughts<sup>[8312](h8312)</sup> within<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> me thy
comforts<sup>[8575](h8575)</sup> delight<sup>[8173](h8173)</sup> my
soul.<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup>

20  Shall the throne<sup>[3678](h3678)</sup> of
iniquity<sup>[1942](h1942)</sup> have fellowship<sup>[2266](h2266)</sup>
with thee, which frameth<sup>[3335](h3335)</sup>
mischief<sup>[5999](h5999)</sup> by<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> a
law?<sup>[2706](h2706)</sup>

21  They gather themselves together<sup>[1413](h1413)</sup>
against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> of the
righteous,<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> and condemn<sup>[7561](h7561)</sup>
the innocent<sup>[5355](h5355)</sup> blood.<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup>

22  But the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> my
defense;<sup>[4869](h4869)</sup> and my God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> is
the rock<sup>[6697](h6697)</sup> of my refuge.<sup>[4268](h4268)</sup>

23  And he shall bring<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> them <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> their own
iniquity,<sup>[205](h0205)</sup> and shall cut them
off<sup>[6789](h6789)</sup> in their own
wickedness;<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> yea, the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> our God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> shall cut
them off.<sup>[6789](h6789)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa094)

1 αἶνος ᾠδῆς τῷ Δαυιδ δεῦτε ἀγαλλιασώμεθα τῷ κυρίῳ ἀλαλάξωμεν τῷ θεῷ τῷ
σωτῆρι ἡμῶν

2 προφθάσωμεν τὸ πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ ἐν ἐξομολογήσει καὶ ἐν ψαλμοῖς
ἀλαλάξωμεν αὐτῷ

3 ὅτι θεὸς μέγας κύριος καὶ βασιλεὺς μέγας ἐπὶ πάντας τοὺς θεούς

4 ὅτι ἐν τῇ χειρὶ αὐτοῦ τὰ πέρατα τῆς γῆς καὶ τὰ ὕψη τῶν ὀρέων αὐτοῦ
εἰσιν

5 ὅτι αὐτοῦ ἐστιν ἡ θάλασσα καὶ αὐτὸς ἐποίησεν αὐτήν καὶ τὴν ξηρὰν αἱ
χεῖρες αὐτοῦ ἔπλασαν

6 δεῦτε προσκυνήσωμεν καὶ προσπέσωμεν αὐτῷ καὶ κλαύσωμεν ἐναντίον κυρίου
τοῦ ποιήσαντος ἡμᾶς

7 ὅτι αὐτός ἐστιν ὁ θεὸς ἡμῶν καὶ ἡμεῖς λαὸς νομῆς αὐτοῦ καὶ πρόβατα
χειρὸς αὐτοῦ σήμερον ἐὰν τῆς φωνῆς αὐτοῦ ἀκούσητε

8 μὴ σκληρύνητε τὰς καρδίας ὑμῶν ὡς ἐν τῷ παραπικρασμῷ κατὰ τὴν ἡμέραν
τοῦ πειρασμοῦ ἐν τῇ ἐρήμῳ

9 οὗ ἐπείρασαν οἱ πατέρες ὑμῶν ἐδοκίμασαν καὶ εἴδοσαν τὰ ἔργα μου

10 τεσσαράκοντα ἔτη προσώχθισα τῇ γενεᾷ ἐκείνῃ καὶ εἶπα ἀεὶ πλανῶνται τῇ
καρδίᾳ καὶ αὐτοὶ οὐκ ἔγνωσαν τὰς ὁδούς μου

11 ὡς ὤμοσα ἐν τῇ ὀργῇ μου εἰ εἰσελεύσονται εἰς τὴν κατάπαυσίν μου

‎1 ‏אֵל־נְקָמ֥וֹת יְהוָ֑ה אֵ֖ל נְקָמ֣וֹת הוֹפִֽיַע׃

‎2 ‏הִ֭נָּשֵׂא שֹׁפֵ֣ט הָאָ֑רֶץ הָשֵׁ֥ב גְּ֝מ֗וּל עַל־גֵּאִֽים׃

‎3 ‏עַד־מָתַ֖י רְשָׁעִ֥ים׀ יְהוָ֑ה עַד־מָ֝תַ֗י רְשָׁעִ֥ים יַעֲלֹֽזוּ׃

‎4 ‏יַבִּ֣יעוּ יְדַבְּר֣וּ עָתָ֑ק יִֽ֝תְאַמְּר֗וּ כָּל־פֹּ֥עֲלֵי אָֽוֶן׃

‎5 ‏עַמְּךָ֣ יְהוָ֣ה יְדַכְּא֑וּ וְֽנַחֲלָתְךָ֥ יְעַנּֽוּ׃

‎6 ‏אַ֭לְמָנָה וְגֵ֣ר יַהֲרֹ֑גוּ וִֽיתוֹמִ֣ים יְרַצֵּֽחוּ׃

‎7 ‏וַ֭יֹּ֣אמְרוּ לֹ֣א יִרְאֶה־יָּ֑הּ וְלֹא־יָ֝בִ֗ין אֱלֹהֵ֥י יַעֲקֹֽב׃

‎14 ‏כִּ֤י׀ לֹא־יִטֹּ֣שׁ יְהוָ֣ה עַמּ֑וֹ וְ֝נַחֲלָת֗וֹ לֹ֣א יַעֲזֹֽב׃

‎15 ‏כִּֽי־עַד־צֶ֭דֶק יָשׁ֣וּב מִשְׁפָּ֑ט וְ֝אַחֲרָ֗יו

‎21 ‏יָ֭גוֹדּוּ עַל־נֶ֣פֶשׁ צַדִּ֑יק וְדָ֖ם נָקִ֣י יַרְשִֽׁיעוּ׃

1 Laus cantici ipsi David. \[Venite, exsultemus Domino;  
jubilemus Deo salutari nostro;  

2 præoccupemus faciem ejus in confessione,  
et in psalmis jubilemus ei:  

3 quoniam Deus magnus Dominus,  
et rex magnus super omnes deos.  

4 Quia in manu ejus sunt omnes fines terræ,  
et altitudines montium ipsius sunt;  

5 quoniam ipsius est mare, et ipse fecit illud,  
et siccam manus ejus formaverunt.  

6 Venite, adoremus, et procidamus,  
et ploremus ante Dominum qui fecit nos:  

7 quia ipse est Dominus Deus noster,  
et nos populus pascuæ ejus, et oves manus ejus.  

8 Hodie si vocem ejus audieritis,  
nolite obdurare corda vestra

9 sicut in irritatione,  
secundum diem tentationis in deserto,  
ubi tentaverunt me patres vestri:  
probaverunt me, et viderunt opera mea.  

10 Quadraginta annis offensus fui generationi illi,  
et dixi: Semper hi errant corde.  

11 Et isti non cognoverunt vias meas:  
ut juravi in ira mea:  
Si introibunt in requiem meam.\]
