H1768  
דּי  
דִּי ‎ dı̂y  
*dee*  
(Chaldee); apparently for [1668](h1668.md) *that*, used as relative,
conjugational, and especially (with preposition) in adverbial phrases;
also as a preposition *of: -* X as, but, for (-asmuch +), + now, of,
seeing, than, that, therefore, until, + what (-soever), when, which,
whom, whose.
