H3733  
כּר  
כַּר ‎ kar  
*kar*  
From [3769](h3769) in the sense of *plumpness*; a *ram* (as *full*
*grown* and *fat*), including a *battering* *ram* (as *butting*); hence
a *meadow* (as *for* *sheep*); also a *pad* or camel’s saddle (as
*puffed* *out*): - captain, furniture, lamb, (large) pasture, ram. See
also [1033](h1033) [3746](h3746)
