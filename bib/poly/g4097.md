G4097  
πιπράσκω, πράω  
pipraskō praō  
*pip-ras‘-ko,* *prah‘-o*  
The first is a reduplicated and prolonged form of the second (which
occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses); contracted from περάω
peraō (to *traverse*; from the base of [4008](g4008) ; to *traffic* (by
*travelling*), that is, *dispose* of as merchandise or into slavery
(literally or figuratively): - sell.
