G5429  
φρόνιμος  
phronimos  
*fron‘-ee-mos*  
From [5424](g5424) *thoughtful*, that is, *sagacious* or *discreet*
(implying a *cautious* character; while [4680](g4680) denotes
*practical* skill or acumen; and [4908](g4908) indicates rather
*intelligence* or mental acquirement); in a bad sense *conceited* (also
in the comparative): - wise (-r).
