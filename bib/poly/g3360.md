G3360  
μέχρι, μεχρίς  
mechri mechris  
*mekh‘-ree,* *mekh-ris‘*  
From [3372](g3372.md) as *far* *as*, that is, *up* *to* a certain point (as
preposition of extent \[denoting the *terminus*, whereas [891](g0891.md)
refers especially to the *space* of time or place intervening\] or
conjugation): - till, (un-) to, until.
