H6924  
קדמה קדם  
קֶדֶם קֵדמָה ‎ qedem qêdmâh  
*keh‘-dem,* *kayd‘-maw*  
From [6923](h6923.md) the *front*, of palce (absolutely the *fore* *part*,
relatively the *East*) or time (*antiquity*); often used adverbially
(*before*, *anciently*, *eastward*): - aforetime, ancient (time),
before, east (end, part, side, -ward), eternal, X ever (-lasting),
forward, old, past. Compare [6926](h6926.md)
