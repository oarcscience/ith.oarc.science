H7392  
רכב  
רָכַב ‎ râkab  
*raw-kab‘*  
A primitive root; to *ride* (on an animal or in a vehicle); causatively
to *place* *upon* (for riding or generally), to *despatch: -* bring (on
\[horse-\] back), carry, get \[oneself\] up, on \[horse-\] back, put,
(cause to, make to) ride (in a chariot, on, -r), set.
