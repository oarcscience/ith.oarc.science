H2498  
חלף  
חָלַף ‎ châlaph  
*khaw-laf‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *slide* by, that is, (by implication) to
*hasten* away, *pass* on, *spring* up, *pierce* or *change: -* abolish,
alter change, cut off, go on forward, grow up, be over, pass (away, on,
through), renew, sprout, strike through.
