### Psalms 8

[KJV](../kjv/psa008.md)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> Gittith,<sup>[1665](h1665.md)</sup> A
Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup> O
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> our Lord,<sup>[113](h0113.md)</sup>
how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> excellent<sup>[117](h0117.md)</sup> is thy
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> in all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
earth!<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> who<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> hast
set<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> thy glory<sup>[1935](h1935.md)</sup>
above<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the heavens.<sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup>

2  Out of the mouth<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> of
babes<sup>[5768](h5768)</sup> and sucklings<sup>[3243](h3243.md)</sup> hast
thou ordained<sup>[3245](h3245)</sup> strength<sup>[5797](h5797.md)</sup>
because of<sup>[4616](h4616.md)</sup> thine
enemies,<sup>[6887](h6887.md)</sup> that thou mightest
still<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> the enemy<sup>[341](h0341.md)</sup> and the
avenger.<sup>[5358](h5358.md)</sup>

3  When<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I consider<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> thy
heavens,<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> the work<sup>[4639](h4639.md)</sup> of thy
fingers,<sup>[676](h0676)</sup> the moon<sup>[3394](h3394.md)</sup> and the
stars,<sup>[3556](h3556)</sup> which<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> thou hast
ordained;<sup>[3559](h3559.md)</sup>

4  What<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> is man,<sup>[582](h0582.md)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou art mindful<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup> of
him? and the son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of man,<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou visitest<sup>[6485](h6485.md)</sup> him?

5  For thou hast made him a little lower<sup>[2637](h2637.md)</sup>
<sup>[4592](h4592)</sup> than the angels,<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> and hast crowned<sup>[5849](h5849.md)</sup> him
with glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> and honor.<sup>[1926](h1926.md)</sup>

6  Thou madest him to have dominion<sup>[4910](h4910.md)</sup> over the
works<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of thy hands;<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> thou
hast put<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> things
under<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup> his feet:<sup>[7272](h7272.md)</sup>

7  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> sheep<sup>[6792](h6792.md)</sup> and
oxen,<sup>[504](h0504)</sup> yea,<sup>[1571](h1571.md)</sup> and the
beasts<sup>[929](h0929)</sup> of the field;<sup>[7704](h7704.md)</sup>

8  The fowl<sup>[6833](h6833)</sup> of the air,<sup>[8064](h8064.md)</sup>
and the fish<sup>[1709](h1709)</sup> of the sea,<sup>[3220](h3220.md)</sup>
and whatsoever passeth through<sup>[5674](h5674.md)</sup> the
paths<sup>[734](h0734)</sup> of the seas.<sup>[3220](h3220.md)</sup>

9  O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> our Lord,<sup>[113](h0113.md)</sup>
how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> excellent<sup>[117](h0117.md)</sup> is thy
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> in all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
earth!<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa008.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ τῶν ληνῶν ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 κύριε ὁ κύριος ἡμῶν ὡς θαυμαστὸν τὸ ὄνομά σου ἐν πάσῃ τῇ γῇ ὅτι ἐπήρθη
ἡ μεγαλοπρέπειά σου ὑπεράνω τῶν οὐρανῶν

3 ἐκ στόματος νηπίων καὶ θηλαζόντων κατηρτίσω αἶνον ἕνεκα τῶν ἐχθρῶν σου
τοῦ καταλῦσαι ἐχθρὸν καὶ ἐκδικητήν

4 ὅτι ὄψομαι τοὺς οὐρανούς ἔργα τῶν δακτύλων σου σελήνην καὶ ἀστέρας ἃ
σὺ ἐθεμελίωσας

5 τί ἐστιν ἄνθρωπος ὅτι μιμνῄσκῃ αὐτοῦ ἢ υἱὸς ἀνθρώπου ὅτι ἐπισκέπτῃ
αὐτόν

6 ἠλάττωσας αὐτὸν βραχύ τι παρ᾽ ἀγγέλους δόξῃ καὶ τιμῇ ἐστεφάνωσας αὐτόν

7 καὶ κατέστησας αὐτὸν ἐπὶ τὰ ἔργα τῶν χειρῶν σου πάντα ὑπέταξας ὑποκάτω
τῶν ποδῶν αὐτοῦ

8 πρόβατα καὶ βόας πάσας ἔτι δὲ καὶ τὰ κτήνη τοῦ πεδίου

9 τὰ πετεινὰ τοῦ οὐρανοῦ καὶ τοὺς ἰχθύας τῆς θαλάσσης τὰ διαπορευόμενα
τρίβους θαλασσῶν

10 κύριε ὁ κύριος ἡμῶν ὡς θαυμαστὸν τὸ ὄνομά σου ἐν πάσῃ τῇ γῇ

[Vul](../vul/psa008.md)

1 In finem, pro torcularibus. Psalmus David.

2 \[Domine, Dominus noster,  
quam admirabile est nomen tuum in universa terra!  
quoniam elevata est magnificentia tua super cælos.  

3 Ex ore infantium et lactentium perfecisti laudem propter inimicos
tuos,  
ut destruas inimicum et ultorem.  

4 Quoniam videbo cælos tuos, opera digitorum tuorum,  
lunam et stellas quæ tu fundasti.  

5 Quid est homo, quod memor es ejus?  
aut filius hominis, quoniam visitas eum?  

6 Minuisti eum paulominus ab angelis;  
gloria et honore coronasti eum;  

7 et constituisti eum super opera manuum tuarum.  

8 Omnia subjecisti sub pedibus ejus,  
oves et boves universas,  
insuper et pecora campi,  

9 volucres cæli, et pisces maris  
qui perambulant semitas maris.  

10 Domine, Dominus noster,  
quam admirabile est nomen tuum in universa terra!\]
