G4811  
συκοφαντέω  
sukophanteō  
*soo-kof-an-teh‘-o*  
From a compound of [4810](g4810) and a derivative of [5316](g5316.md) to
*be* *a* *fig* *informer* (reporter of the law forbidding the
exportation of figs from Greece), “sycopant”, that is, (generally and by
extension) to *defraud* (*exact* unlawfully, *extort*): - accuse
falsely, take by false accusation.
