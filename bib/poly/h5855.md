H5855  
עטרות שׁופן  
עַטרוֹת שׁוֹפָן ‎ ‛aṭrôth shôphân  
*at-roth‘* *sho-fawn‘*  
From the same as [5852](h5852.md) and a name otherwise unused (being from
the same as [8226](h8226.md) meaning *hidden*; *crowns* *of* *Shophan*;
*Atroth-Shophan*, a place in Palestine: - Atroth, Shophan \[as if two
places\].
