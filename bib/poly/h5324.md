H5324  
נצב  
נָצַב ‎ nâtsab  
*naw-tsab‘*  
A primitive root; to *station*, in various applications (literally or
figuratively): - appointed, deputy, erect, establish, X Huzzah \[by
mistake for a proper name\], lay, officer, pillar, present, rear up, set
(over, up), settle, sharpen, stablish, (make to) stand (-ing, still, up,
upright), best state.
