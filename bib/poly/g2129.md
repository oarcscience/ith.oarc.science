G2129  
εὐλογία  
eulogia  
*yoo-log-ee‘-ah*  
From the same as [2127](g2127.md) *fine* *speaking*, that is, *elegance*
*of* *language*; *commendation* (“eulogy”), that is, (reverentially)
*adoration*; religiously, *benediction*; by implication *consecration*;
by extension *benefit* or *largess:* - blessing (a matter of) bounty (X
-tifully), fair speech.
