H4414  
מלח  
מָלַח ‎ mâlach  
*maw-lakh‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *rub* to pieces or pulverize;
intransitively to *disappear* as dust; also (as denominative from
[4417](h4417) to *salt* whether internally (to *season* with salt.md) or
externally (to *rub* with salt): - X at all, salt, season, temper
together, vanish away.
