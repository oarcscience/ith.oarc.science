רבה  
רָבָה ‎ râbâh  
*raw-baw‘*  
A primitive root; to *increase* (in whatever respect): - \[bring in\]
abundance (X -antly), + archer \[by mistake for [7232](h7232.md) , be in
authority, bring up, X continue, enlarge, excel, exceeding (-ly), be
full of, (be, make) great (-er, -ly), X -ness), grow up, heap, increase,
be long, (be, give, have, make, use) many (a time), (any, be, give, give
the, have) more (in number), (ask, be, be so, gather, over, take, yield)
much (greater, more), (make to) multiply, nourish, plenty (-eous), X
process \[of time\], sore, store, thoroughly, very.
