H7350  
רחק רחוק  
רָחוֹק רָחוֹק ‎ râchôq râchôq  
*raw-khoke‘,* *raw-khoke‘*  
From [7368](h7368.md) *remote*, literally of figuratively, of place or
time; specifically *precious*; often used adverbially (with
preposition): - (a-) far (abroad, off), long ago, of old, space, great
while to come.
