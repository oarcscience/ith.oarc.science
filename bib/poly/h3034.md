H3034  
ידה  
יָדָה ‎ yâdâh  
*yaw-daw‘*  
A primitive root; used only as denominative from [3027](h3027) literally
to *use* (that is, hold out) *the* *hand*; physically to *throw* (a
stone, an arrow) at or away; especially to *revere* or *worship* (with
extended hands); intensively to *bemoan* (by wringing the hands): - cast
(out), (make) confess (-ion), praise, shoot, (give) thank (-ful, -s,
-sgiving).
