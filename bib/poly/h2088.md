H2088  
זה  
זֶה ‎ zeh  
*zeh*  
A primitive word; the masculine demonstrative pronoun, *this* or *that*:
- he, X hence, X here, it (-self), X now, X of him, the one . . . the
other, X than the other, (X out of) the (self) same, such (an one) that,
these, this (hath, man), on this side . . . on that side, X thus, very,
which. Compare [2063](h2063) [2090](h2090) [2097](h2097) [2098](h2098)
