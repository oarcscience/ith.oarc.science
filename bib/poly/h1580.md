H1580  
גּמל  
גָּמַל ‎ gâmal  
*gaw-mal‘*  
A primitive root; to *treat* a person (well or ill), that is, *benefit*
or *requite*; by implication (of *toil*) to *ripen*, that is,
(specifically) to *wean: -* bestow on, deal bountifully, do (good),
recompense, requite, reward, ripen, + serve, wean, yield.
