### Psalms 35

[KJV](../kjv/psa035.md)

1  A Psalm of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup>
Plead<sup>[7378](h7378)</sup> my cause, O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
with<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> them that strive<sup>[3401](h3401.md)</sup>
with me: fight against<sup>[3898](h3898)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup>
them that fight against<sup>[3898](h3898.md)</sup> me.

2  Take hold<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> of shield<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup>
and buckler,<sup>[6793](h6793.md)</sup> and stand
up<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup> for mine help.<sup>[5833](h5833.md)</sup>

3  Draw out<sup>[7324](h7324.md)</sup> also the
spear,<sup>[2595](h2595)</sup> and stop<sup>[5462](h5462.md)</sup> the way
against<sup>[7125](h7125.md)</sup> them that
persecute<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> me: say<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> unto my
soul,<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> am thy
salvation.<sup>[3444](h3444.md)</sup>

4  Let them be confounded<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup> and put to
shame<sup>[3637](h3637)</sup> that seek after<sup>[1245](h1245.md)</sup> my
soul:<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> let them be turned<sup>[5472](h5472.md)</sup>
back<sup>[268](h0268.md)</sup> and brought to
confusion<sup>[2659](h2659)</sup> that devise<sup>[2803](h2803.md)</sup> my
hurt.<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup>

5  Let them be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as chaff<sup>[4671](h4671.md)</sup>
before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the wind:<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup> and let
the angel<sup>[4397](h4397)</sup> of the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
chase<sup>[1760](h1760.md)</sup> them.

6  Let their way<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup>
dark<sup>[2822](h2822)</sup> and slippery:<sup>[2519](h2519.md)</sup> and
let the angel<sup>[4397](h4397.md)</sup> of the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> persecute<sup>[7291](h7291.md)</sup> them.

7  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> without cause<sup>[2600](h2600.md)</sup>
have they hid<sup>[2934](h2934.md)</sup> for me their
net<sup>[7568](h7568)</sup> in a pit,<sup>[7845](h7845.md)</sup> which
without cause<sup>[2600](h2600.md)</sup> they have
digged<sup>[2658](h2658)</sup> for my soul.<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>

8  Let destruction<sup>[7722](h7722.md)</sup> come
upon<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> him at unawares;<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>
<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> and let his net<sup>[7568](h7568.md)</sup>
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> he hath hid<sup>[2934](h2934.md)</sup>
catch<sup>[3920](h3920.md)</sup> himself: into that very
destruction<sup>[7722](h7722.md)</sup> let him
fall.<sup>[5307](h5307.md)</sup>

9  And my soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> shall be
joyful<sup>[1523](h1523)</sup> in the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> it
shall rejoice<sup>[7797](h7797.md)</sup> in his
salvation.<sup>[3444](h3444.md)</sup>

10  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> my bones<sup>[6106](h6106.md)</sup> shall
say,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> is like unto thee,<sup>[3644](h3644.md)</sup>
which deliverest<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> the
poor<sup>[6041](h6041.md)</sup> from him that is too
strong<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2389](h2389.md)</sup>
for<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> him, yea, the poor<sup>[6041](h6041.md)</sup>
and the needy<sup>[34](h0034.md)</sup> from him that
spoileth<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1497](h1497.md)</sup> him?

11  False<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> witnesses<sup>[5707](h5707.md)</sup> did
rise up;<sup>[6965](h6965.md)</sup> they laid to my
charge<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> things that<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> I
knew<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> not.<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

12  They rewarded<sup>[7999](h7999.md)</sup> me
evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> for<sup>[8478](h8478.md)</sup>
good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> to the spoiling<sup>[7908](h7908.md)</sup> of
my soul.<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>

13  But as for me,<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup> when they were
sick,<sup>[2470](h2470)</sup> my clothing<sup>[3830](h3830.md)</sup> was
sackcloth:<sup>[8242](h8242)</sup> I humbled<sup>[6031](h6031.md)</sup> my
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> with fasting;<sup>[6685](h6685.md)</sup> and
my prayer<sup>[8605](h8605)</sup> returned<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup>
into<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> mine own bosom.<sup>[2436](h2436.md)</sup>

14  I behaved myself<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> as though he had been my
friend<sup>[7453](h7453)</sup> or brother:<sup>[251](h0251.md)</sup> I
bowed down<sup>[7817](h7817)</sup> heavily,<sup>[6937](h6937.md)</sup> as
one that mourneth<sup>[57](h0057.md)</sup> for his
mother.<sup>[517](h0517.md)</sup>

15  But in mine adversity<sup>[6761](h6761.md)</sup> they
rejoiced,<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> and gathered themselves
together:<sup>[622](h0622.md)</sup> yea, the
abjects<sup>[5222](h5222.md)</sup> gathered themselves
together<sup>[622](h0622)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me, and
I knew<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> it not;<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> they did
tear<sup>[7167](h7167)</sup> me, and ceased<sup>[1826](h1826.md)</sup>
not:<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup>

16  With hypocritical<sup>[2611](h2611.md)</sup>
mockers<sup>[3934](h3934)</sup> in feasts,<sup>[4580](h4580.md)</sup> they
gnashed<sup>[2786](h2786)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me with
their teeth.<sup>[8127](h8127.md)</sup>

17  Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> how long<sup>[4100](h4100.md)</sup> wilt
thou look on?<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> rescue<sup>[7725](h7725.md)</sup> my
soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> from their
destructions,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7722](h7722.md)</sup> my
darling<sup>[3173](h3173)</sup> from the lions.<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup>

18  I will give thee thanks<sup>[3034](h3034.md)</sup> in the
great<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> congregation:<sup>[6951](h6951.md)</sup> I
will praise<sup>[1984](h1984.md)</sup> thee among
much<sup>[6099](h6099)</sup> people.<sup>[5971](h5971.md)</sup>

19  Let not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup> them that are mine
enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> wrongfully<sup>[8267](h8267.md)</sup>
rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> over me: neither let them
wink<sup>[7169](h7169)</sup> with the eye<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> that
hate<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup> me without a cause.<sup>[2600](h2600.md)</sup>

20  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they speak<sup>[1696](h1696.md)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> peace:<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> but they
devise<sup>[2803](h2803)</sup> deceitful<sup>[4820](h4820.md)</sup>
matters<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> them
that are quiet<sup>[7282](h7282.md)</sup> in the
land.<sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup>

21  Yea, they opened<sup>[7337](h7337.md)</sup> their
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> wide against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me,
and said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Aha,<sup>[1889](h1889.md)</sup>
aha,<sup>[1889](h1889)</sup> our eye<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> hath
seen<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> it.

22  This thou hast seen,<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup> O
LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> keep not<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup>
silence:<sup>[2790](h2790)</sup> O Lord,<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup> be
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> far<sup>[7368](h7368.md)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me.

23  Stir up thyself,<sup>[5782](h5782.md)</sup> and
awake<sup>[6974](h6974)</sup> to my judgment,<sup>[4941](h4941.md)</sup>
even unto my cause,<sup>[7379](h7379.md)</sup> my
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> and my Lord.<sup>[136](h0136.md)</sup>

24  Judge<sup>[8199](h8199)</sup> me, O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> my
God,<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup> according to thy
righteousness;<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup> and let them
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055.md)</sup> over me.

25  Let them not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> say<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> in
their hearts,<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> Ah,<sup>[1889](h1889.md)</sup> so
would we have it:<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> let them
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> say,<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> We have swallowed
him up.<sup>[1104](h1104.md)</sup>

26  Let them be ashamed<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup> and brought to
confusion<sup>[2659](h2659)</sup> together<sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup> that
rejoice<sup>[8056](h8056)</sup> at mine hurt:<sup>[7451](h7451.md)</sup>
let them be clothed<sup>[3847](h3847.md)</sup> with
shame<sup>[1322](h1322)</sup> and dishonor<sup>[3639](h3639.md)</sup> that
magnify<sup>[1431](h1431.md)</sup> themselves
against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me.

27  Let them shout for joy,<sup>[7442](h7442.md)</sup> and be
glad,<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> that favor<sup>[2655](h2655.md)</sup> my
righteous cause:<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup> yea, let them
say<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> continually,<sup>[8548](h8548.md)</sup> Let the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> be magnified,<sup>[1431](h1431.md)</sup> which
hath pleasure<sup>[2655](h2655.md)</sup> in the
prosperity<sup>[7965](h7965.md)</sup> of his
servant.<sup>[5650](h5650.md)</sup>

28  And my tongue<sup>[3956](h3956.md)</sup> shall
speak<sup>[1897](h1897.md)</sup> of thy
righteousness<sup>[6664](h6664.md)</sup> and of thy
praise<sup>[8416](h8416)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> the
day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> long.

[Sep](../sep/psa035.md)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος τῷ δούλῳ κυρίου τῷ Δαυιδ

2 φησὶν ὁ παράνομος τοῦ ἁμαρτάνειν ἐν ἑαυτῷ οὐκ ἔστιν φόβος θεοῦ
ἀπέναντι τῶν ὀφθαλμῶν αὐτοῦ

3 ὅτι ἐδόλωσεν ἐνώπιον αὐτοῦ τοῦ εὑρεῖν τὴν ἀνομίαν αὐτοῦ καὶ μισῆσαι

4 τὰ ῥήματα τοῦ στόματος αὐτοῦ ἀνομία καὶ δόλος οὐκ ἐβουλήθη συνιέναι
τοῦ ἀγαθῦναι

5 ἀνομίαν διελογίσατο ἐπὶ τῆς κοίτης αὐτοῦ παρέστη πάσῃ ὁδῷ οὐκ ἀγαθῇ τῇ
δὲ κακίᾳ οὐ προσώχθισεν

6 κύριε ἐν τῷ οὐρανῷ τὸ ἔλεός σου καὶ ἡ ἀλήθειά σου ἕως τῶν νεφελῶν

7 ἡ δικαιοσύνη σου ὡσεὶ ὄρη θεοῦ τὰ κρίματά σου ἄβυσσος πολλή ἀνθρώπους
καὶ κτήνη σώσεις κύριε

8 ὡς ἐπλήθυνας τὸ ἔλεός σου ὁ θεός οἱ δὲ υἱοὶ τῶν ἀνθρώπων ἐν σκέπῃ τῶν
πτερύγων σου ἐλπιοῦσιν

9 μεθυσθήσονται ἀπὸ πιότητος τοῦ οἴκου σου καὶ τὸν χειμάρρουν τῆς τρυφῆς
σου ποτιεῖς αὐτούς

10 ὅτι παρὰ σοὶ πηγὴ ζωῆς ἐν τῷ φωτί σου ὀψόμεθα φῶς

11 παράτεινον τὸ ἔλεός σου τοῖς γινώσκουσίν σε καὶ τὴν δικαιοσύνην σου
τοῖς εὐθέσι τῇ καρδίᾳ

12 μὴ ἐλθέτω μοι ποὺς ὑπερηφανίας καὶ χεὶρ ἁμαρτωλῶν μὴ σαλεύσαι με

13 ἐκεῖ ἔπεσον οἱ ἐργαζόμενοι τὴν ἀνομίαν ἐξώσθησαν καὶ οὐ μὴ δύνωνται
στῆναι

[Vul](../vul/psa035.md)

1 In finem. Servo Domini ipsi David.

2 \[Dixit injustus ut delinquat in semetipso:  
non est timor Dei ante oculos ejus.  

3 Quoniam dolose egit in conspectu ejus,  
ut inveniatur iniquitas ejus ad odium.  

4 Verba oris ejus iniquitas, et dolus;  
noluit intelligere ut bene ageret.  

5 Iniquitatem meditatus est in cubili suo;  
astitit omni viæ non bonæ:  
malitiam autem non odivit.  

6 Domine, in cælo misericordia tua,  
et veritas tua usque ad nubes.  

7 Justitia tua sicut montes Dei;  
judicia tua abyssus multa.  
Homines et jumenta salvabis, Domine,  

8 quemadmodum multiplicasti misericordiam tuam, Deus.  
Filii autem hominum in tegmine alarum tuarum sperabunt.  

9 Inebriabuntur ab ubertate domus tuæ,  
et torrente voluptatis tuæ potabis eos:  

10 quoniam apud te est fons vitæ,  
et in lumine tuo videbimus lumen.  

11 Prætende misericordiam tuam scientibus te,  
et justitiam tuam his qui recto sunt corde.  

12 Non veniat mihi pes superbiæ,  
et manus peccatoris non moveat me.  

13 Ibi ceciderunt qui operantur iniquitatem;  
expulsi sunt, nec potuerunt stare.\]
