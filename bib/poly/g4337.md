G4337  
προσέχω  
prosechō  
*pros-ekh‘-o*  
From [4314](g4314) and [2192](g2192) (figuratively.md) to *hold* the mind
([3563](g3563) implied.md) *towards*, that is, *pay* *attention* *to*, *be*
*cautious* *about*, *apply* *oneself* *to*, *adhere* *to:* - (give)
attend (-ance, -ance at, -ance to, unto), beware, be given to, give
(take) heed (to, unto) have regard.
