H7789  
שׁוּר  
שׁוּר ‎ shûr  
*shoor*  
A primitive root (rather identical with [7788](h7788) through the idea
of *going* *round* for inspection); to *spy* out, that is, (generally)
*survey*, (for evil) *lurk* *for*, (for good) *care* *for: -* behold,
lay wait, look, observe, perceive, regard, see.
