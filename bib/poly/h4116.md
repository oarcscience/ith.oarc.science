H4116  
מהר  
מָהַר ‎ mâhar  
*maw-har‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *be* *liquid* or *flow* easily, that is,
(by implication); to *hurry* (in a good or bad sense); often used (with
another verb) adverbially *promptly: -* be carried headlong, fearful,
(cause to make, in, make) haste (-n, -ily, (be) hasty, (fetch, make
ready) X quickly, rash, X shortly, (be so) X soon, make speed, X
speedily, X straightway, X suddenly, swift.
