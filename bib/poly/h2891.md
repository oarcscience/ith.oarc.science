H2891  
טהר  
טָּהֵר ‎ ṭâhêr  
*taw-hare‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *be* *bright*; that is, (by implication)
to *be* *pure* (physically *sound*, *clear*, *unadulterated*;
Levitically *uncontaminated*; morally *innocent* or *holy*): - be (make,
make self, pronounce) clean, cleanse (self), purge, purify (-ier,
self).
