### Psalms 133

[KJV](../kjv/psa133.md)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees<sup>[4609](h4609.md)</sup> of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009.md)</sup>
how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> good<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup> and
how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> pleasant<sup>[5273](h5273.md)</sup> it is for
brethren<sup>[251](h0251)</sup> to dwell<sup>[3427](h3427.md)</sup>
together in unity!<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> <sup>[3162](h3162.md)</sup>

2  It is like the precious<sup>[2896](h2896.md)</sup>
ointment<sup>[8081](h8081)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
head,<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> that ran down<sup>[3381](h3381.md)</sup>
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the beard,<sup>[2206](h2206.md)</sup> even
Aaron's<sup>[175](h0175)</sup> beard:<sup>[2206](h2206.md)</sup> that went
down<sup>[7945](h7945)</sup> <sup>[3381](h3381.md)</sup>
to<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the skirts<sup>[6310](h6310.md)</sup> of his
garments;<sup>[4060](h4060.md)</sup>

3  As the dew<sup>[2919](h2919)</sup> of Hermon,<sup>[2768](h2768.md)</sup>
and as the dew that descended<sup>[7945](h7945.md)</sup>
<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> the
mountains<sup>[2042](h2042)</sup> of Zion:<sup>[6726](h6726.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> there<sup>[8033](h8033.md)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> commanded<sup>[6680](h6680.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> the blessing,<sup>[1293](h1293.md)</sup> even
life<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> forevermore.<sup>[5704](h5704.md)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa133.md)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν ἰδοὺ δὴ εὐλογεῖτε τὸν κύριον πάντες οἱ δοῦλοι κυρίου
οἱ ἑστῶτες ἐν οἴκῳ κυρίου ἐν αὐλαῖς οἴκου θεοῦ ἡμῶν

2 ἐν ταῖς νυξὶν ἐπάρατε τὰς χεῖρας ὑμῶν εἰς τὰ ἅγια καὶ εὐλογεῖτε τὸν
κύριον

3 εὐλογήσει σε κύριος ἐκ Σιων ὁ ποιήσας τὸν οὐρανὸν καὶ τὴν γῆν

[Vul](../vul/psa133.md)

1 Canticum graduum. \[Ecce nunc benedicite Dominum, omnes servi
Domini:  
qui statis in domo Domini,  
in atriis domus Dei nostri.  

2 In noctibus extollite manus vestras in sancta,  
et benedicite Dominum.  

3 Benedicat te Dominus ex Sion,  
qui fecit cælum et terram.\]
