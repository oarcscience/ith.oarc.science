G3936
παρίστημι, παριστάνω
paristēmi paristanō
par-is‘-tay-mee, par-is-tan‘-o
From 3488 and 2476 to stand beside, that is, (transitively) to exhibit, proffer, (specifically) recommend, (figuratively) substantiate; or (intransitively) to be at hand (or ready), aid: - assist, bring before, command, commend, give presently, present, prove, provide, shew, stand (before, by, here, up, with), yield.
