### Psalms 31

[KJV](../kjv/psa031.md)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329.md)</sup> A
Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David.<sup>[1732](h1732.md)</sup> In thee,
O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> do I put my
trust;<sup>[2620](h2620)</sup> let me never<sup>[408](h0408.md)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> be ashamed:<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup>
deliver<sup>[6403](h6403.md)</sup> me in thy
righteousness.<sup>[6666](h6666.md)</sup>

2  Bow down<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> thine ear<sup>[241](h0241.md)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> me; deliver<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> me
speedily:<sup>[4120](h4120)</sup> be<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> thou my
strong<sup>[4581](h4581)</sup> rock,<sup>[6697](h6697.md)</sup> for a
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of defense<sup>[4686](h4686.md)</sup> to
save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> me.

3  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> art my
rock<sup>[5553](h5553)</sup> and my fortress;<sup>[4686](h4686.md)</sup>
therefore for thy name's sake<sup>[4616](h4616.md)</sup>
<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> lead<sup>[5148](h5148.md)</sup> me, and
guide<sup>[5095](h5095.md)</sup> me.

4  Pull me out<sup>[3318](h3318.md)</sup> of the
net<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7568](h7568.md)</sup>
that<sup>[2098](h2098.md)</sup> they have laid
privily<sup>[2934](h2934)</sup> for me: for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup>
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art my strength.<sup>[4581](h4581.md)</sup>

5  Into thine hand<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> I
commit<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> my spirit:<sup>[7307](h7307.md)</sup> thou
hast redeemed<sup>[6299](h6299)</sup> me, O LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> of truth.<sup>[571](h0571.md)</sup>

6  I have hated<sup>[8130](h8130.md)</sup> them that
regard<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> lying<sup>[7723](h7723.md)</sup>
vanities:<sup>[1892](h1892)</sup> but I<sup>[589](h0589.md)</sup>
trust<sup>[982](h0982)</sup> in<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

7  I will be glad<sup>[1523](h1523.md)</sup> and
rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> in thy mercy:<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup>
for<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> thou hast considered<sup>[7200](h7200.md)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> my trouble;<sup>[6040](h6040.md)</sup> thou hast
known<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> my soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> in
adversities;<sup>[6869](h6869.md)</sup>

8  And hast not<sup>[3808](h3808.md)</sup> shut me
up<sup>[5462](h5462)</sup> into the hand<sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of the
enemy:<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> thou hast set<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup> my
feet<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> in a large room.<sup>[4800](h4800.md)</sup>

9  Have mercy<sup>[2603](h2603.md)</sup> upon me, O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> I am in
trouble:<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> mine eye<sup>[5869](h5869.md)</sup> is
consumed<sup>[6244](h6244)</sup> with grief,<sup>[3708](h3708.md)</sup>
yea, my soul<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup> and my
belly.<sup>[990](h0990.md)</sup>

10  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my life<sup>[2416](h2416.md)</sup> is
spent<sup>[3615](h3615)</sup> with grief,<sup>[3015](h3015.md)</sup> and my
years<sup>[8141](h8141)</sup> with sighing:<sup>[585](h0585.md)</sup> my
strength<sup>[3581](h3581)</sup> faileth<sup>[3782](h3782.md)</sup> because
of mine iniquity,<sup>[5771](h5771.md)</sup> and my
bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> are consumed.<sup>[6244](h6244.md)</sup>

11  I was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a reproach<sup>[2781](h2781.md)</sup>
among all<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> mine
enemies,<sup>[6887](h6887)</sup> but especially<sup>[3966](h3966.md)</sup>
among my neighbors,<sup>[7934](h7934.md)</sup> and a
fear<sup>[6343](h6343.md)</sup> to mine
acquaintance:<sup>[3045](h3045.md)</sup> they that did
see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> me without<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup>
fled<sup>[5074](h5074)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup> me.

12  I am forgotten<sup>[7911](h7911.md)</sup> as a dead
man<sup>[4191](h4191)</sup> out of mind:<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> I am<sup>[1961](h1961.md)</sup> like a
broken<sup>[6](h0006)</sup> vessel.<sup>[3627](h3627.md)</sup>

13  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I have heard<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup> the
slander<sup>[1681](h1681)</sup> of many:<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup>
fear<sup>[4032](h4032)</sup> was on every side:<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> while they took counsel<sup>[3245](h3245.md)</sup>
together<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> me,
they devised<sup>[2161](h2161.md)</sup> to take
away<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> my life.<sup>[5315](h5315.md)</sup>

14  But I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> trusted<sup>[982](h0982.md)</sup>
in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thee, O LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> I
said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Thou<sup>[859](h0859.md)</sup> art my
God.<sup>[430](h0430.md)</sup>

15  My times<sup>[6256](h6256.md)</sup> are in thy
hand:<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> deliver<sup>[5337](h5337.md)</sup> me from
the hand<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3027](h3027.md)</sup> of mine
enemies,<sup>[341](h0341.md)</sup> and from them that
persecute<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7291](h7291.md)</sup> me.

16  Make thy face<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> to
shine<sup>[215](h0215)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> thy
servant:<sup>[5650](h5650)</sup> save<sup>[3467](h3467.md)</sup> me for thy
mercies'<sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> sake.

17  Let me not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> be ashamed,<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup>
O LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> I have
called upon<sup>[7121](h7121.md)</sup> thee: let the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> be ashamed,<sup>[954](h0954.md)</sup> and
let them be silent<sup>[1826](h1826.md)</sup> in the
grave.<sup>[7585](h7585.md)</sup>

18  Let the lying<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> lips<sup>[8193](h8193.md)</sup>
be put to silence;<sup>[481](h0481.md)</sup> which
speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> grievous things<sup>[6277](h6277.md)</sup>
proudly<sup>[1346](h1346.md)</sup> and
contemptuously<sup>[937](h0937)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup>
the righteous.<sup>[6662](h6662.md)</sup>

19  Oh how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> great<sup>[7227](h7227.md)</sup> is thy
goodness,<sup>[2898](h2898)</sup> which<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup> thou hast
laid up<sup>[6845](h6845.md)</sup> for them that
fear<sup>[3373](h3373.md)</sup> thee; which thou hast
wrought<sup>[6466](h6466.md)</sup> for them that
trust<sup>[2620](h2620)</sup> in thee before<sup>[5048](h5048.md)</sup> the
sons<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of men!<sup>[120](h0120.md)</sup>

20  Thou shalt hide<sup>[5641](h5641.md)</sup> them in the
secret<sup>[5643](h5643)</sup> of thy presence<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup>
from the pride<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7407](h7407.md)</sup> of
man:<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> thou shalt keep them
secretly<sup>[6845](h6845)</sup> in a pavilion<sup>[5521](h5521.md)</sup>
from the strife<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[7379](h7379.md)</sup> of
tongues.<sup>[3956](h3956.md)</sup>

21  Blessed<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup> be the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> he hath showed me his marvelous
kindness<sup>[6381](h6381)</sup> <sup>[2617](h2617.md)</sup> in a
strong<sup>[4692](h4692)</sup> city.<sup>[5892](h5892.md)</sup>

22  For I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> said<sup>[559](h0559.md)</sup> in my
haste,<sup>[2648](h2648)</sup> I am cut off<sup>[1629](h1629.md)</sup> from
before<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5048](h5048.md)</sup> thine
eyes:<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> nevertheless<sup>[403](h0403.md)</sup> thou
heardest<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> the voice<sup>[6963](h6963.md)</sup> of my
supplications<sup>[8469](h8469.md)</sup> when I
cried<sup>[7768](h7768)</sup> unto<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup> thee.

23  O love<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup> ye his
saints:<sup>[2623](h2623)</sup> for the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>
preserveth<sup>[5341](h5341)</sup> the faithful,<sup>[539](h0539.md)</sup>
and plentifully<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3499](h3499.md)</sup>
rewardeth<sup>[7999](h7999)</sup> the proud<sup>[1346](h1346.md)</sup>
doer.<sup>[6213](h6213.md)</sup>

24  Be of good courage,<sup>[2388](h2388.md)</sup> and he shall
strengthen<sup>[553](h0553)</sup> your heart,<sup>[3824](h3824.md)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> ye that hope<sup>[3176](h3176.md)</sup> in the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa031.md)

1 τῷ Δαυιδ συνέσεως μακάριοι ὧν ἀφέθησαν αἱ ἀνομίαι καὶ ὧν ἐπεκαλύφθησαν
αἱ ἁμαρτίαι

2 μακάριος ἀνήρ οὗ οὐ μὴ λογίσηται κύριος ἁμαρτίαν οὐδὲ ἔστιν ἐν τῷ
στόματι αὐτοῦ δόλος

3 ὅτι ἐσίγησα ἐπαλαιώθη τὰ ὀστᾶ μου ἀπὸ τοῦ κράζειν με ὅλην τὴν ἡμέραν

4 ὅτι ἡμέρας καὶ νυκτὸς ἐβαρύνθη ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ ἡ χείρ σου ἐστράφην εἰς
ταλαιπωρίαν ἐν τῷ ἐμπαγῆναι ἄκανθαν διάψαλμα

5 τὴν ἁμαρτίαν μου ἐγνώρισα καὶ τὴν ἀνομίαν μου οὐκ ἐκάλυψα εἶπα
ἐξαγορεύσω κατ᾽ ἐμοῦ τὴν ἀνομίαν μου τῷ κυρίῳ καὶ σὺ ἀφῆκας τὴν ἀσέβειαν
τῆς ἁμαρτίας μου διάψαλμα

6 ὑπὲρ ταύτης προσεύξεται πᾶς ὅσιος πρὸς σὲ ἐν καιρῷ εὐθέτῳ πλὴν ἐν
κατακλυσμῷ ὑδάτων πολλῶν πρὸς αὐτὸν οὐκ ἐγγιοῦσιν

7 σύ μου εἶ καταφυγὴ ἀπὸ θλίψεως τῆς περιεχούσης με τὸ ἀγαλλίαμά μου
λύτρωσαί με ἀπὸ τῶν κυκλωσάντων με διάψαλμα

8 συνετιῶ σε καὶ συμβιβῶ σε ἐν ὁδῷ ταύτῃ ᾗ πορεύσῃ ἐπιστηριῶ ἐπὶ σὲ τοὺς
ὀφθαλμούς μου

9 μὴ γίνεσθε ὡς ἵππος καὶ ἡμίονος οἷς οὐκ ἔστιν σύνεσις ἐν χαλινῷ καὶ
κημῷ τὰς σιαγόνας αὐτῶν ἄγξαι τῶν μὴ ἐγγιζόντων πρὸς σέ

10 πολλαὶ αἱ μάστιγες τοῦ ἁμαρτωλοῦ τὸν δὲ ἐλπίζοντα ἐπὶ κύριον ἔλεος
κυκλώσει

11 εὐφράνθητε ἐπὶ κύριον καὶ ἀγαλλιᾶσθε δίκαιοι καὶ καυχᾶσθε πάντες οἱ
εὐθεῖς τῇ καρδίᾳ

‎25 ‏חִ֭זְקוּ וְיַאֲמֵ֣ץ לְבַבְכֶ֑ם כָּל־הַ֝מְיַחֲלִ֗ים לַיהוָֽה׃

[Vul](../vul/psa031.md)

1 Ipsi David intellectus. \[Beati quorum remissæ sunt iniquitates,  
et quorum tecta sunt peccata.  

2 Beatus vir cui non imputavit Dominus peccatum,  
nec est in spiritu ejus dolus.  

3 Quoniam tacui, inveteraverunt ossa mea,  
dum clamarem tota die.  

4 Quoniam die ac nocte gravata est super me manus tua,  
conversus sum in ærumna mea, dum configitur spina.  

5 Delictum meum cognitum tibi feci,  
et injustitiam meam non abscondi.  
Dixi: Confitebor adversum me injustitiam meam Domino;  
et tu remisisti impietatem peccati mei.  

6 Pro hac orabit ad te omnis sanctus  
in tempore opportuno.  
Verumtamen in diluvio aquarum multarum,  
ad eum non approximabunt.  

7 Tu es refugium meum a tribulatione quæ circumdedit me;  
exsultatio mea, erue me a circumdantibus me.  

8 Intellectum tibi dabo, et instruam te in via hac qua gradieris;  
firmabo super te oculos meos.  

9 Nolite fieri sicut equus et mulus,  
quibus non est intellectus.  
In camo et freno maxillas eorum constringe,  
qui non approximant ad te.  

10 Multa flagella peccatoris;  
sperantem autem in Domino misericordia circumdabit.  

11 Lætamini in Domino, et exsultate, justi;  
et gloriamini, omnes recti corde.\]
