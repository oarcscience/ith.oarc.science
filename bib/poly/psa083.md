### Psalms 83

silence,<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> <sup>[1824](h1824)</sup> O

up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> the head.<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup>

3  They have taken crafty<sup>[6191](h6191)</sup>
counsel<sup>[5475](h5475)</sup> against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> thy
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> and consulted<sup>[3289](h3289)</sup>

5  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they have consulted

6  The tabernacles<sup>[168](h0168)</sup> of
Edom,<sup>[123](h0123)</sup> and the

7  Gebal,<sup>[1381](h1381)</sup> and Ammon,<sup>[5983](h5983)</sup> and

with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the inhabitants<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> of
Tyre;<sup>[6865](h6865)</sup>

9  Do<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> unto them as unto the

10  Which perished<sup>[8045](h8045)</sup> at

11  Make<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> their nobles<sup>[5081](h5081)</sup>

12  Who<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Let us

13  O my God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> make<sup>[7896](h7896)</sup> them

wind.<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup>

14  As the fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> burneth<sup>[1197](h1197)</sup> a

15  So<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> persecute<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> them
with thy tempest,<sup>[5591](h5591)</sup> and make them
afraid<sup>[926](h0926)</sup> with thy storm.<sup>[5492](h5492)</sup>

16  Fill<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> their faces<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>
with shame;<sup>[7036](h7036)</sup> that they may
seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> thy name,<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> O

17  Let them be confounded<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> and
troubled<sup>[926](h0926)</sup> forever;<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup> yea, let them be put to
shame,<sup>[2659](h2659)</sup> and perish:<sup>[6](h0006)</sup>

18  That men may know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> whose
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> alone<sup>[905](h0905)</sup> is
JEHOVAH,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> art the most
high<sup>[5945](h5945)</sup> over<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

[Vul](../vul/psa083)

1 In finem, pro torcularibus filiis Core. Psalmus.

2 \[Quam dilecta tabernacula tua, Domine virtutum!  

3 Concupiscit, et deficit anima mea in atria Domini;  
cor meum et caro mea exsultaverunt in Deum vivum.  

4 Etenim passer invenit sibi domum,  
et turtur nidum sibi, ubi ponat pullos suos:  
altaria tua, Domine virtutum,  
rex meus, et Deus meus.  

5 Beati qui habitant in domo tua, Domine;  
in sæcula sæculorum laudabunt te.  

6 Beatus vir cujus est auxilium abs te:  
ascensiones in corde suo disposuit,  

7 in valle lacrimarum, in loco quem posuit.  

8 Etenim benedictionem dabit legislator;  
ibunt de virtute in virtutem:  
videbitur Deus deorum in Sion.  

9 Domine Deus virtutum, exaudi orationem meam;  
auribus percipe, Deus Jacob.  

10 Protector noster, aspice, Deus,  
et respice in faciem christi tui.  

11 Quia melior est dies una in atriis tuis super millia;  
elegi abjectus esse in domo Dei mei  
magis quam habitare in tabernaculis peccatorum.  

12 Quia misericordiam et veritatem diligit Deus:  
gratiam et gloriam dabit Dominus.  

13 Non privabit bonis eos qui ambulant in innocentia:  
Domine virtutum, beatus homo qui sperat in te.\]
