### Psalms 11

[KJV](../kjv/psa011)

1  To the chief Musician,<sup>[5329](h5329)</sup> A Psalm of
David.<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> In the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> put I
my trust:<sup>[2620](h2620)</sup> how<sup>[349](h0349)</sup>
say<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> ye to my soul,<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup>
Flee<sup>[5110](h5110)</sup> as a bird<sup>[6833](h6833)</sup> to your
mountain?<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup>

2  For,<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> lo,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> bend<sup>[1869](h1869)</sup> their
bow,<sup>[7198](h7198)</sup> they make ready<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup>
their arrow<sup>[2671](h2671)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
string,<sup>[3499](h3499)</sup> that they may
privily<sup>[1119](h1119)</sup> <sup>[652](h0652)</sup>
shoot<sup>[3384](h3384)</sup> at the upright<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup> in
heart.<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>

3  If<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the foundations<sup>[8356](h8356)</sup> be
destroyed,<sup>[2040](h2040)</sup> what<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> can the
righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> do?<sup>[6466](h6466)</sup>

4  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> is in his
holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> temple,<sup>[1964](h1964)</sup> the
LORD's<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> throne<sup>[3678](h3678)</sup> is in
heaven:<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> his eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup>
behold,<sup>[2372](h2372)</sup> his eyelids<sup>[6079](h6079)</sup>
try,<sup>[974](h0974)</sup> the children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of
men.<sup>[120](h0120)</sup>

5  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> trieth<sup>[974](h0974)</sup> the
righteous:<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup> but the
wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> and him that
loveth<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> violence<sup>[2555](h2555)</sup> his
soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> hateth.<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup>

6  Upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> he
shall rain<sup>[4305](h4305)</sup> snares,<sup>[6341](h6341)</sup>
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> and brimstone,<sup>[1614](h1614)</sup> and a
horrible<sup>[2152](h2152)</sup> tempest:<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> this
shall be the portion<sup>[4521](h4521)</sup> of their
cup.<sup>[3563](h3563)</sup>

7  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the righteous<sup>[6662](h6662)</sup>
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> loveth<sup>[157](h0157)</sup>
righteousness;<sup>[6666](h6666)</sup> his
countenance<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> doth behold<sup>[2372](h2372)</sup>
the upright.<sup>[3477](h3477)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa011)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ τῆς ὀγδόης ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 σῶσόν με κύριε ὅτι ἐκλέλοιπεν ὅσιος ὅτι ὠλιγώθησαν αἱ ἀλήθειαι ἀπὸ τῶν
υἱῶν τῶν ἀνθρώπων

3 μάταια ἐλάλησεν ἕκαστος πρὸς τὸν πλησίον αὐτοῦ χείλη δόλια ἐν καρδίᾳ
καὶ ἐν καρδίᾳ ἐλάλησαν

4 ἐξολεθρεύσαι κύριος πάντα τὰ χείλη τὰ δόλια καὶ γλῶσσαν μεγαλορήμονα

5 τοὺς εἰπόντας τὴν γλῶσσαν ἡμῶν μεγαλυνοῦμεν τὰ χείλη ἡμῶν παρ᾽ ἡμῶν
ἐστιν τίς ἡμῶν κύριός ἐστιν

6 ἀπὸ τῆς ταλαιπωρίας τῶν πτωχῶν καὶ ἀπὸ τοῦ στεναγμοῦ τῶν πενήτων νῦν
ἀναστήσομαι λέγει κύριος θήσομαι ἐν σωτηρίᾳ παρρησιάσομαι ἐν αὐτῷ

7 τὰ λόγια κυρίου λόγια ἁγνά ἀργύριον πεπυρωμένον δοκίμιον τῇ γῇ
κεκαθαρισμένον ἑπταπλασίως

8 σύ κύριε φυλάξεις ἡμᾶς καὶ διατηρήσεις ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τῆς γενεᾶς ταύτης καὶ
εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα

9 κύκλῳ οἱ ἀσεβεῖς περιπατοῦσιν κατὰ τὸ ὕψος σου ἐπολυώρησας τοὺς υἱοὺς
τῶν ἀνθρώπων

[Vul](../vul/psa011)

1 In finem, pro octava. Psalmus David.

2 \[Salvum me fac, Domine, quoniam defecit sanctus,  
quoniam diminutæ sunt veritates a filiis hominum.  

3 Vana locuti sunt unusquisque ad proximum suum;  
labia dolosa, in corde et corde locuti sunt.  

4 Disperdat Dominus universa labia dolosa,  
et linguam magniloquam.  

5 Qui dixerunt: Linguam nostram magnificabimus;  
labia nostra a nobis sunt.  
Quis noster dominus est?  

6 Propter miseriam inopum, et gemitum pauperum,  
nunc exsurgam, dicit Dominus.  
Ponam in salutari;  
fiducialiter agam in eo.  

7 Eloquia Domini, eloquia casta; argentum igne examinatum,  
probatum terræ, purgatum septuplum.  

8 Tu, Domine, servabis nos,  
et custodies nos a generatione hac in æternum.  

9 In circuitu impii ambulant:  
secundum altitudinem tuam multiplicasti filios hominum.\]
