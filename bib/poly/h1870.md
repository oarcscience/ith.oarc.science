H1870  
דּרך  
דֶּרֶך ‎ derek  
*deh‘-rek*  
From [1869](h1869) a *road* (as *trodden*.md); figuratively a *course* of
life or *mode* of action, often adverbially: - along, away, because of,
+ by, conversation, custom, \[east-\] ward, journey, manner, passenger,
through, toward, \[high-\] \[path-\] way \[-side\], whither
\[-soever\].
