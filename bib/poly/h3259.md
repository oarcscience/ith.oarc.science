H3259  
יעד  
יָעַד ‎ yâ‛ad  
*yaw-ad‘*  
A primitive root; to *fix* upon (by agreement or appointment); by
implication to *meet* (at a stated time), to *summon* (to trial), to
*direct* (in a certain quarter or position), to *engage* (for marriage):
- agree, (make an) appoint (-ment, a time), assemble (selves), betroth,
gather (selves, together), meet (together), set (a time).
