G4594  
σήμερον  
sēmeron  
*say‘-mer-on*  
Neuter (as adverb) of a presumed compound of the article [3588](g3588.md)
(“tau” changed to “sigma”) and [2250](g2250) on *the* (that is, *this*.md)
*day* (or *night* current or just passed); genitively *now* (that is, at
*present*, *hitherto*): - this (to-) day.
