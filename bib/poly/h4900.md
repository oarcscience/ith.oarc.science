H4900  
משׁך  
מָשַׁך ‎ mâshak  
*maw-shak‘*  
A primitive root; to *draw*, used in a great variety of applications
(including to *sow*, to *sound*, to *prolong*, to *develop*, to *march*,
to *remove*, to *delay*, to *be* *tall*, etc.): - draw (along, out),
continue, defer, extend, forbear, X give, handle, make (pro-, sound)
long, X sow, scatter, stretch out.
