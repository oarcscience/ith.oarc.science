G3779  
οὕτω  
houtō  
*hoo‘-to*  
Or, before a vowel, οὕτως houtōs *hoo‘-toce* . From [3778](g3778.md) *in*
*this* *way* (referring to what precedes or follows): - after that,
after (in) this manner, as, even (so), for all that, like (-wise), no
more, on this fashion (-wise), so (in like manner), thus, what.
