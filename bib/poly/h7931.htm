H7931
שׁכן
שָׁכַן ‎ shâkan
shaw-kan‘
A primitive root (apparently akin (by transmutation) to 7901 through the idea of lodging; compare 5531 and 7925 ; to reside or permanently stay (literally or figuratively): - abide, continue, (cause to, make to) dwell (-er), have habitation, inhabit, lay, place, (cause to) remain, rest, set (up).
