H3885  
לין לוּן  
לוּן לִין ‎ lûn lı̂yn  
*loon,* *leen*  
A primitive root; to *stop* (usually over night); by implication to
*stay* permanently; hence (in a bad sense) to be *obstinate* (especially
in words, to *complain*): - abide (all night), continue, dwell, endure,
grudge, be left, lie all night, (cause to) lodge (all night, in, -ing,
this night), (make to) murmur, remain, tarry (all night, that night).
