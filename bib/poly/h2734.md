H2734  
חרה  
חָרָה ‎ chârâh  
*khaw-raw‘*  
A primitive root (compare [2787](h2787) ; to *glow* or grow warm;
figuratively (usually) to *blaze* up, of anger, zeal, jealousy: - be
angry, burn, be displeased, X earnestly, fret self, grieve, be (wax)
hot, be incensed, kindle, X very, be wroth. See [8474](h8474)
