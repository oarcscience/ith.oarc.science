H3474  
ישׁר  
יָשַׁר ‎ yâshar  
*yaw-shar‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* *straight* or *even*; figuratively to *be*
(causatively to *make*) *right*, *pleasant*, *prosperous: -* direct,
fit, seem good (meet), + please (well), be (esteem, go) right (on),
bring (look, make, take the) straight (way), be upright (-ly).
