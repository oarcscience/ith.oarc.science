H7663  
שׁבר שׂבר  
שָׂבַר שָׁבַר ‎ śâbar shâbar  
*saw-bar‘,* *shaw-bar‘*  
The second form being used erroneously in [Neh 2:13](neh002.htm#013),
[Neh 2:15](neh002.htm#015); a primitive root; to *scrutinize*; by
implication (of *watching*) to *expect* (with hope and patience): -
hope, tarry, view, wait.
