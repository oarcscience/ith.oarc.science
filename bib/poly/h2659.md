H2659  
חפר  
חָפֵר ‎ châphêr  
*khaw-fare‘*  
A primitive root (perhaps rather the same as [2658](h2658) through the
idea of *detection*): to *blush*; figuratively to *be* *ashamed*,
*disappointed*; causatively to *shame*, *reproach: -* be ashamed, be
confounded, be brought to confusion (unto shame), come (be put to)
shame, bring reproach.
