H5401  
נשׁק  
נָשַׁק ‎ nâshaq  
*naw-shak‘*  
A primitive root (identical with [5400](h5400) through the idea of
*fastening* up; compare [2388](h2388) and [2836](h2836) ; to *kiss*,
literally or figuratively (*touch*); also (as a mode of *attachment*),
to *equip* with weapons: - armed (men), rule, kiss, that touched.
