### Psalms 130

[KJV](../kjv/psa130)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees.<sup>[4609](h4609)</sup>
Out of the depths<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4615](h4615)</sup> have
I cried<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> unto thee, O
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

2  Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> my
voice:<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> let thine ears<sup>[241](h0241)</sup>
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> attentive<sup>[7183](h7183)</sup> to the
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of my
supplications.<sup>[8469](h8469)</sup>

3  If<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> thou, LORD,<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup>
shouldest mark<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup>
iniquities,<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> O Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup>
who<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> shall stand?<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup>

4  But<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> there is
forgiveness<sup>[5547](h5547)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> thee,
that<sup>[4616](h4616)</sup> thou mayest be
feared.<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup>

5  I wait for<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
my soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> doth wait,<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> and
in his word<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> do I hope.<sup>[3176](h3176)</sup>

6  My soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> waiteth for the
Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> more than they that
watch<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> for the
morning:<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup> I say, more than they that
watch<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup> for the morning.<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup>

7  Let Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> hope<sup>[3176](h3176)</sup>
in<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> there is mercy,<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> and
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> him is plenteous<sup>[7235](h7235)</sup>
redemption.<sup>[6304](h6304)</sup>

8  And he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> shall redeem<sup>[6299](h6299)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> from
all<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> his
iniquities.<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>

[Vul](../vul/psa130)

1 Canticum graduum David. \[Domine, non est exaltatum cor meum,  
neque elati sunt oculi mei,  
neque ambulavi in magnis,  
neque in mirabilibus super me.  

2 Si non humiliter sentiebam,  
sed exaltavi animam meam:  
sicut ablactatus est super matre sua,  
ita retributio in anima mea.  

3 Speret Israël in Domino,  
ex hoc nunc et usque in sæculum.\]
