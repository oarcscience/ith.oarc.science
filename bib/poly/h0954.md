H954  
בּוּשׁ  
בּוּשׁ ‎ bûsh  
*boosh*  
A primitive root; properly to *pale*, that is, by implication to *be*
*ashamed*; also (by implication) to *be* *disappointed*, or *delayed: -*
(be, make, bring to, cause, put to, with, a-) shame (-d), be (put to)
confounded (-fusion), become dry, delay, be long.
