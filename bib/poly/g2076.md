G2076  
ἐστί  
esti  
*es-tee‘*  
Third person singular present indicative of [1510](g1510) he (she or it)
*is*; also (with neuter plural) they *are:* - are, be (-long), call, X
can \[-not\], come, consisteth, X dure for awhile, + follow, X have,
(that) is (to say), make, meaneth, X must needs, + profit, + remaineth,
+ wrestle.
