H8659  
תּרשׁישׁ  
תַּּרשִׁישׁ ‎ tarshı̂ysh  
*tar-sheesh‘*  
Probably the same as [8658](h8658) (as the region of the stone, or the
reverse); *Tarshish*, a place on the Mediterranean, hence the epithet of
a *merchant* vessel (as if for or from that port); also the name of a
Persian and of an Israelite: - Tarshish, Tharshish.
