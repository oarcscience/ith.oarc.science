H268  
אחר אחור  
אָחוֹר אָחוֹר ‎ ‘âchôr ‘âchôr  
*aw-khore‘,* *aw-khore‘*  
From [299](h0299) the *hinder* part; hence (adverbially.md) *behind*,
*backward*; also (as facing north) the *West: -* after (-ward), back
(part, -side, -ward), hereafter, (be-) hind (-er part), time to come,
without.
