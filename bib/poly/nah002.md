### Nahum 2

[KJV](../kjv/nah002.md)

1  He that dasheth in pieces<sup>[6327](h6327.md)</sup> is come
up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> before<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> thy
face:<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> keep<sup>[5341](h5341.md)</sup> the
munition,<sup>[4694](h4694)</sup> watch<sup>[6822](h6822.md)</sup> the
way,<sup>[1870](h1870.md)</sup> make thy loins
strong,<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> <sup>[4975](h4975.md)</sup>
fortify<sup>[553](h0553)</sup> thy power<sup>[3581](h3581.md)</sup>
mightily.<sup>[3966](h3966.md)</sup>

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> hath
turned away<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853.md)</sup> the
excellency<sup>[1347](h1347)</sup> of Jacob,<sup>[3290](h3290.md)</sup> as
the excellency<sup>[1347](h1347.md)</sup> of
Israel:<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588.md)</sup> the
emptiers<sup>[1238](h1238)</sup> have emptied<sup>[1238](h1238.md)</sup>
them out, and marred<sup>[7843](h7843.md)</sup> their vine
branches.<sup>[2156](h2156.md)</sup>

3  The shield<sup>[4043](h4043.md)</sup> of his mighty
men<sup>[1368](h1368)</sup> is made red,<sup>[119](h0119.md)</sup> the
valiant<sup>[2428](h2428)</sup> men<sup>[376](h0376.md)</sup> are in
scarlet:<sup>[8529](h8529)</sup> the chariots<sup>[7393](h7393.md)</sup>
shall be with flaming<sup>[784](h0784.md)</sup>
torches<sup>[6393](h6393)</sup> in the day<sup>[3117](h3117.md)</sup> of
his preparation,<sup>[3559](h3559.md)</sup> and the fir
trees<sup>[1265](h1265.md)</sup> shall be terribly
shaken.<sup>[7477](h7477.md)</sup>

4  The chariots<sup>[7393](h7393.md)</sup> shall
rage<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> in the streets,<sup>[2351](h2351.md)</sup>
they shall jostle one against another<sup>[8264](h8264.md)</sup> in the
broad ways:<sup>[7339](h7339.md)</sup> they shall
seem<sup>[4758](h4758)</sup> like torches,<sup>[3940](h3940.md)</sup> they
shall run<sup>[7323](h7323.md)</sup> like the
lightnings.<sup>[1300](h1300.md)</sup>

5  He shall recount<sup>[2142](h2142.md)</sup> his
worthies:<sup>[117](h0117.md)</sup> they shall
stumble<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup> in their walk;<sup>[1979](h1979.md)</sup>
they shall make haste<sup>[4116](h4116.md)</sup> to the
wall<sup>[2346](h2346.md)</sup> thereof, and the
defense<sup>[5526](h5526.md)</sup> shall be
prepared.<sup>[3559](h3559.md)</sup>

6  The gates<sup>[8179](h8179.md)</sup> of the
rivers<sup>[5104](h5104)</sup> shall be opened,<sup>[6605](h6605.md)</sup>
and the palace<sup>[1964](h1964.md)</sup> shall be
dissolved.<sup>[4127](h4127.md)</sup>

7  And Huzzab<sup>[5324](h5324.md)</sup> shall be led away
captive,<sup>[1540](h1540.md)</sup> she shall be brought
up,<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> and her maids<sup>[519](h0519.md)</sup> shall
lead<sup>[5090](h5090.md)</sup> her as with the
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of doves,<sup>[3123](h3123.md)</sup>
taboring<sup>[8608](h8608)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921.md)</sup> their
breasts.<sup>[3824](h3824.md)</sup>

8  But Nineveh<sup>[5210](h5210)</sup> is of old<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> like a pool<sup>[1295](h1295.md)</sup> of
water:<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> yet they<sup>[1992](h1992.md)</sup> shall
flee away.<sup>[5127](h5127)</sup> Stand,<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup>
stand,<sup>[5975](h5975.md)</sup> shall they cry; but
none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> shall look back.<sup>[6437](h6437.md)</sup>

9  Take ye the spoil<sup>[962](h0962.md)</sup> of
silver,<sup>[3701](h3701)</sup> take the spoil<sup>[962](h0962.md)</sup> of
gold:<sup>[2091](h2091)</sup> for there is none<sup>[369](h0369.md)</sup>
end<sup>[7097](h7097)</sup> of the store<sup>[8498](h8498.md)</sup> and
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> out of all<sup>[4480](h4480.md)</sup>
<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the pleasant<sup>[2532](h2532.md)</sup>
furniture.<sup>[3627](h3627.md)</sup>

10  She is empty,<sup>[950](h0950.md)</sup> and
void,<sup>[4003](h4003)</sup> and waste:<sup>[1110](h1110.md)</sup> and the
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> melteth,<sup>[4549](h4549.md)</sup> and the
knees<sup>[1290](h1290)</sup> smite together,<sup>[6375](h6375.md)</sup>
and much pain<sup>[2479](h2479)</sup> is in all<sup>[3605](h3605.md)</sup>
loins,<sup>[4975](h4975)</sup> and the faces<sup>[6440](h6440.md)</sup> of
them all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> gather<sup>[6908](h6908.md)</sup>
blackness.<sup>[6289](h6289.md)</sup>

11  Where<sup>[346](h0346)</sup> is the dwelling<sup>[4583](h4583.md)</sup>
of the lions,<sup>[738](h0738.md)</sup> and the feeding
place<sup>[4829](h4829.md)</sup> of the young
lions,<sup>[3715](h3715)</sup> where<sup>[834](h0834.md)</sup>
<sup>[8033](h8033)</sup> the lion,<sup>[738](h0738.md)</sup> even the old
lion,<sup>[3833](h3833)</sup> walked,<sup>[1980](h1980.md)</sup> and the
lion's<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> whelp,<sup>[1482](h1482.md)</sup> and
none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> made them afraid?<sup>[2729](h2729.md)</sup>

12  The lion<sup>[738](h0738.md)</sup> did tear in
pieces<sup>[2963](h2963)</sup> enough<sup>[1767](h1767.md)</sup> for his
whelps,<sup>[1484](h1484)</sup> and strangled<sup>[2614](h2614.md)</sup>
for his lionesses,<sup>[3833](h3833.md)</sup> and
filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> his holes<sup>[2356](h2356.md)</sup> with
prey,<sup>[2964](h2964)</sup> and his dens<sup>[4585](h4585.md)</sup> with
ravin.<sup>[2966](h2966.md)</sup>

13  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> I am against<sup>[413](h0413.md)</sup>
thee, saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068.md)</sup> of
hosts,<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> and I will burn<sup>[1197](h1197.md)</sup>
her chariots<sup>[7393](h7393.md)</sup> in the
smoke,<sup>[6227](h6227)</sup> and the sword<sup>[2719](h2719.md)</sup>
shall devour<sup>[398](h0398.md)</sup> thy young
lions:<sup>[3715](h3715.md)</sup> and I will cut
off<sup>[3772](h3772)</sup> thy prey<sup>[2964](h2964.md)</sup> from the
earth,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[776](h0776.md)</sup> and the
voice<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup> of thy messengers<sup>[4397](h4397.md)</sup>
shall no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> more<sup>[5750](h5750.md)</sup> be
heard.<sup>[8085](h8085.md)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/nah002.md)

1 ἰδοὺ ἐπὶ τὰ ὄρη οἱ πόδες εὐαγγελιζομένου καὶ ἀπαγγέλλοντος εἰρήνην
ἑόρταζε Ιουδα τὰς ἑορτάς σου ἀπόδος τὰς εὐχάς σου διότι οὐ μὴ
προσθήσωσιν ἔτι τοῦ διελθεῖν διὰ σοῦ εἰς παλαίωσιν συντετέλεσται ἐξῆρται

2 ἀνέβη ἐμφυσῶν εἰς πρόσωπόν σου ἐξαιρούμενος ἐκ θλίψεως σκόπευσον ὁδόν
κράτησον ὀσφύος ἄνδρισαι τῇ ἰσχύι σφόδρα

3 διότι ἀπέστρεψεν κύριος τὴν ὕβριν Ιακωβ καθὼς ὕβριν τοῦ Ισραηλ διότι
ἐκτινάσσοντες ἐξετίναξαν αὐτοὺς καὶ τὰ κλήματα αὐτῶν διέφθειραν

4 ὅπλα δυναστείας αὐτῶν ἐξ ἀνθρώπων ἄνδρας δυνατοὺς ἐμπαίζοντας ἐν πυρί
αἱ ἡνίαι τῶν ἁρμάτων αὐτῶν ἐν ἡμέρᾳ ἑτοιμασίας αὐτοῦ καὶ οἱ ἱππεῖς
θορυβηθήσονται

5 ἐν ταῖς ὁδοῖς καὶ συγχυθήσονται τὰ ἅρματα καὶ συμπλακήσονται ἐν ταῖς
πλατείαις ἡ ὅρασις αὐτῶν ὡς λαμπάδες πυρὸς καὶ ὡς ἀστραπαὶ διατρέχουσαι

6 καὶ μνησθήσονται οἱ μεγιστᾶνες αὐτῶν καὶ φεύξονται ἡμέρας καὶ
ἀσθενήσουσιν ἐν τῇ πορείᾳ αὐτῶν καὶ σπεύσουσιν ἐπὶ τὰ τείχη καὶ
ἑτοιμάσουσιν τὰς προφυλακὰς αὐτῶν

7 πύλαι τῶν ποταμῶν διηνοίχθησαν καὶ τὰ βασίλεια διέπεσεν

8 καὶ ἡ ὑπόστασις ἀπεκαλύφθη καὶ αὕτη ἀνέβαινεν καὶ αἱ δοῦλαι αὐτῆς
ἤγοντο καθὼς περιστεραὶ φθεγγόμεναι ἐν καρδίαις αὐτῶν

9 καὶ Νινευη ὡς κολυμβήθρα ὕδατος τὰ ὕδατα αὐτῆς καὶ αὐτοὶ φεύγοντες οὐκ
ἔστησαν καὶ οὐκ ἦν ὁ ἐπιβλέπων

10 διήρπαζον τὸ ἀργύριον διήρπαζον τὸ χρυσίον καὶ οὐκ ἦν πέρας τοῦ
κόσμου αὐτῆς βεβάρυνται ὑπὲρ πάντα τὰ σκεύη τὰ ἐπιθυμητὰ αὐτῆς

11 ἐκτιναγμὸς καὶ ἀνατιναγμὸς καὶ ἐκβρασμὸς καὶ καρδίας θραυσμὸς καὶ
ὑπόλυσις γονάτων καὶ ὠδῖνες ἐπὶ πᾶσαν ὀσφύν καὶ τὸ πρόσωπον πάντων ὡς
πρόσκαυμα χύτρας

12 ποῦ ἐστιν τὸ κατοικητήριον τῶν λεόντων καὶ ἡ νομὴ ἡ οὖσα τοῖς
σκύμνοις οὗ ἐπορεύθη λέων τοῦ εἰσελθεῖν ἐκεῖ σκύμνος λέοντος καὶ οὐκ ἦν
ὁ ἐκφοβῶν

13 λέων ἥρπασεν τὰ ἱκανὰ τοῖς σκύμνοις αὐτοῦ καὶ ἀπέπνιξεν τοῖς λέουσιν
αὐτοῦ καὶ ἔπλησεν θήρας νοσσιὰν αὐτοῦ καὶ τὸ κατοικητήριον αὐτοῦ ἁρπαγῆς

14 ἰδοὺ ἐγὼ ἐπὶ σέ λέγει κύριος παντοκράτωρ καὶ ἐκκαύσω ἐν καπνῷ πλῆθός
σου καὶ τοὺς λέοντάς σου καταφάγεται ῥομφαία καὶ ἐξολεθρεύσω ἐκ τῆς γῆς
τὴν θήραν σου καὶ οὐ μὴ ἀκουσθῇ οὐκέτι τὰ ἔργα σου

1 \[Ascendit qui dispergat coram te,  
qui custodiat obsidionem:  
contemplare viam, conforta lumbos,  
robora virtutem valde.  

2 Quia reddidit Dominus superbiam Jacob,  
sicut superbiam Israël;  
quia vastatores dissipaverunt eos,  
et propagines eorum corruperunt.  

3 Clypeus fortium ejus ignitus,  
viri exercitus in coccineis;  
igneæ habenæ currus in die præparationis ejus,  
et agitatores consopiti sunt.  

4 In itineribus conturbati sunt;  
quadrigæ collisæ sunt in plateis:  
aspectus eorum quasi lampades,  
quasi fulgura discurrentia.  

5 Recordabitur fortium suorum;  
ruent in itineribus suis:  
velociter ascendent muros ejus,  
et præparabitur umbraculum.  

6 Portæ fluviorum apertæ sunt,  
et templum ad solum dirutum.  

7 Et miles captivus abductus est,  
et ancillæ ejus minabantur gementes ut columbæ,  
murmurantes in cordibus suis.  

8 Et Ninive quasi piscina aquarum aquæ ejus;  
ipsi vero fugerunt. State, state!  
et non est qui revertatur.  

9 Diripite argentum, diripite aurum:  
et non est finis divitiarum ex omnibus vasis desiderabilibus.  

10 Dissipata est, et scissa, et dilacerata;  
et cor tabescens, et dissolutio geniculorum,  
et defectio in cunctis renibus,  
et facies omnium eorum sicut nigredo ollæ.  

11 Ubi est habitaculum leonum,  
et pascua catulorum leonum,  
ad quam ivit leo ut ingrederetur illuc catulus leonis,  
et non est qui exterreat?  

12 Leo cepit sufficienter catulis suis,  
et necavit leænis suis,  
et implevit præda speluncas suas,  
et cubile suum rapina.  

13 Ecce ego ad te, dicit Dominus exercituum,  
et succendam usque ad fumum quadrigas tuas,  
et leunculos tuos comedet gladius,  
et exterminabo de terra prædam tuam,  
et non audietur ultra vox nuntiorum tuorum.\]
