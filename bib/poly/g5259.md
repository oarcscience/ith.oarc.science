G5259  
ὑπό  
hupo  
*hoop-o‘*  
A primary preposition; *under*, that is, (with the genitive) of place
(*beneath*), or with verbs (the agency or means, *through*); (with the
accusative) of place (whither \[*underneath*\] or where \[*below*\]) or
time (when \[*at*\])*:* - among, by, from, in, of, under, with. In
compounds it retains the same genitive applications, especially of
*inferior* position or condition, and specifically *covertly* or
*moderately*.
