H376  
אישׁ  
אִישׁ ‎ ‘ı̂ysh  
*eesh*  
Contracted for [582](h0582) (or perhaps rather from an unused root
meaning to *be* *extant*); a *man* as an individual or a male person;
often used as an adjunct to a more definite term (and in such cases
frequently not expressed in translation.) : - also, another, any (man),
a certain, + champion, consent, each, every (one), fellow, \[foot-,
husband-\] man, (good-, great, mighty) man, he, high (degree), him (that
is), husband, man \[-kind\], + none, one, people, person, + steward,
what (man) soever, whoso (-ever), worthy. Compare [802](h0802)
