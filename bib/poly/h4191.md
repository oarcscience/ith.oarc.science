H4191  
מוּת  
מוּת ‎ mûth  
*mooth*  
A primitive root; to *die* (literally or figuratively); causatively to
*kill: -* X at all, X crying, (be) dead (body, man, one), (put to,
worthy of) death, destroy (-er), (cause to, be like to, must) die, kill,
necro \[-mancer\], X must needs, slay, X surely, X very suddenly, X in
\[no\] wise.
