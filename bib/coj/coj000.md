------------------------------------------------------------------------

THE

CHRONICLES OF JERAHMEEL;

OR,

The Hebrew Bible Historiale.

BEING

A COLLECTION OF APOCRYPHAL AND PSEUDO-EPIGRAPHICAL  
BOOKS DEALING WITH THE HISTORY OF THE WORLD FROM  
THE CREATION TO THE DEATH OF JUDAS MACCABEUS.

Translated for the first Time from an unique Manuscript in the Bodlean
Library,

BY

M. GASTER, Ph.D.

TOGETHER WITH

AN INTRODUCTION, CRITICAL NOTES, A FULL INDEX, AND FIVE FACSIMILES.

Oriental translation fund, London. Publications, new ser., v. 11.

\[1899\]

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage, December
2009, by John Bruno Hare. This text is in the public domain in the US
because it was published prior to 1923.
