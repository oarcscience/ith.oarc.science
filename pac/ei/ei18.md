### SALUTATION.

The form. of salutation is "Kohomai," literally interpreted, "Come to
me." This is always heartily expressed, and parties meeting often shout
out the kohomai while some distance apart. The greeting is varied by the
addition of a word of respect when addressed to a superior in rank, or a
stranger, and by a term of endearment, when to a child or to a relation.
