The Assyrian Calendar

 

 

CORRESPONDING MONTHS

1.

Ni’sannu (Nisan).

March—April.

2.

Aaru (Iyyar).

April—May.

3.

’Sivanu (Sivan)

May—June.

4.

Dûzu (Tammuz)

June—July.

5.

Abu (Ab)

July—August.

6.

Ululu (Elul)

August—September.

7.

Tasritu (Tisri)

September—October.

8.

Arakh-savna (Marchesvan) "the 8th month"

October—November.

9.

Ki’silivu (Chisleu).

November—December.

10.

Dhabitu (Tebet)

December—January.

11.

Sabadhu (Sebat)

January—February.

12.

Addaru (Adar)

February—March.

13.

Arakh-makhru (Ve-Adar), the intercalary month.
