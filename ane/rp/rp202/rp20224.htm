X. Inscription of Ghala-lamma

On the Fragment of a Statue 1

COLUMN I

1. [To the god …]Ra,
2. [the daughter of the goddess] Bau
3. [for his] mistress,
4. [for] the life
5. [of Dun]gi,
6. [the] puissant [prince],

COLUMN II

1. the king of Ur,
2. the king of Shumer and Accad,
3. Ghala-lamma,
4. the son of Lukâni,
5. the patesi
6. of Shirpurla.
