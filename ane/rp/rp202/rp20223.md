#### IX. <span class="smallcaps">Inscription of Nam-maghâni</span>

<span class="smallcaps">On a Stone from the Threshold of a
Door</span> <span id="fr_254"></span>[1](#fn_254)

1\. For the goddess <span class="smallcaps">Bau</span>,  
2. the good lady,  
3. the daughter of <span class="smallcaps">Anna</span>,  
4. the mistress of <span class="smallcaps">Uru-azagga</span>,  
5. his mistress,  
6. Nam-maghâni,  
7. the patesi  
8. of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla</span>,  
9. her powerful minister,  
10. as the stone of a threshold <span id="fr_255"></span>[2](#fn_255)  
11. has made this.
