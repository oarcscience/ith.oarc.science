#### X. <span class="smallcaps">Inscription of Ghala-lamma</span>

<span class="smallcaps">On the Fragment of a Statue</span> <span
id="fr_256"></span>[1](#fn_256.md)

<span class="small">COLUMN I</span>

1\. \[To the god …\]<span class="smallcaps">Ra</span>,  
2. \[the daughter of the goddess\] <span class="smallcaps">Bau</span>  
3. \[for his\] mistress,  
4. \[for\] the life  
5. \[of Dun\]gi,  
6. \[the\] puissant \[prince\],

<span class="small">COLUMN II</span>

1\. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Ur</span>,  
2. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Shumer</span> and <span
class="smallcaps">Accad</span>,  
3. Ghala-lamma,  
4. the son of Lukâni,  
5. the patesi  
6. of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla</span>.
