# A SONG SUNG BY THE STAR !GAUNU, AND ESPECIALLY BY BUSHMAN WOMEN.

Does the *\|\|garraken*<span class="small">\[1\]</span> flower open?  
The *\#ku-Yam*<span class="small">\[2\]</span> is the one which opens.  
Dost thou open?  
The *\#ku-Yam* is the one which opens.

\[1. The *\|\|garraken* are bulbs; the Bushmen dig them out.

2\. *Dimorphotheca annua*, a daisy-like flower, in bloom at Mowbray in
August, 1879.\]
