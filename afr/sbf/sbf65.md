# THE SHAPED RIB BONE.

A bone (it) is; a rib (it.) is; a Bushman is the one who makes it.<span
class="small">\[1\]</span> He works it; he shapes it with a knife.

"Kambro" is that which we eat (with) it.

<span class="small">\[1. He works two ribs, with a knife.\]</span>
