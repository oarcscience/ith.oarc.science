# 2. FURTHER CHANGES OF FORM.

The sun rose, and *\|Xue* was a *dui*;<span class="small">\[1\]</span>
and the sun set, and *\|Xue* was an Omuherero and lay down; and the sun
rose, and *\|Xue* was *\|Xue*, and went into another country and was a
*sha'o*<span class="small">\[2\]</span>; and the sun

\[1. The flower of the *dui* is light-coloured; its fruit is green;
another day, (when) its fruit has ripened, its fruit is red.

2\. The *shao* is a tall tree, like the *!kuni* (palm?).\]

set, and *\|Xue* was a Makoba, and lay down; and the sun rose and
*\|Xue* was a *\|naXane*.<span class="small">\[1\]</span>

<span class="small">\[1. (One kind of) *!naXane* lies upon the earth;
another (kind of) *!naXane* is a tree. The *!naXane* are numerous. The
fruit of the tree *!naXane* is yellowish. The fruit of the tree
*!naXane* is large; and the ground *!naXane* fruit is small, and
resembles the *\|kui* fruit, is red, is small, and abundant.\]</span>
