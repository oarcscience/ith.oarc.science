*NOTE:* 

### 76. The Greedy Child.

The idea of a water spirit who allows no one to cross a river without an
offering of food, seems to be common in West Africa; e. g. Dayrell,
107-114. Jekyll, 100-101, Dry River, has a Jamaica version of this
story. Compare also Tremearne, 209-210, 307-314.
