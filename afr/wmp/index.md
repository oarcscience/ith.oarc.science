This is an ethnography of the Iboibo, a Nigerian tribe. Written by a
pioneering English woman in the early 20th Century, this book focuses on
the ritual life of women. Despite the naïve colonialist attitude, it
presents a female perspective which was seldom seen in the ethnographic
literature of the period.
