Zoroastrianism, in spite of its small current number of adherents, has
played a huge role in the study of comparative religion. Not only was it
a cohort of the ancient Vedic Hinduism, but also had a huge influence on
the development of Judaism and Christianity.

This part of the site has complete online editions of the Sacred Books
of the East's Zoroastrian texts: the three volume Avesta series, as well
as the five volume Pahlavi series.
