### CHAPTER XI.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Is wisdom good, or
skill, or goodness <span id="fr_209"></span>[1](#fn_206.md)?'

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'Wisdom with which *there*
is no goodness, is not to be considered as wisdom; (5) and skill with
which *there* is no wisdom, is not to be considered as skill.'
