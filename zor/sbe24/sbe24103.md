### CHAPTER XV.

1\. The fifteenth subject is this, when *one* sees anything that is
welcome to the eyes, it is requisite to say 'in the name of the sacred
being.' 2. Because, if they do not say 'in the name of the sacred
being,' and an injury happens to that

<span id="page_277">p. 277</span>

thing, or a disaster occurs, *one* becomes a sinner; so far is notorious
(ma’hlûm).
