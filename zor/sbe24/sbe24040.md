### CHAPTER XXXIV.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: '*Has* the creator
Aûha*r*ma*zd* produced the creation of anything whatever for the worldly
*existence* <span id="fr_377"></span>[1](#fn_372.md), unto which Aharman is
not able to bring disturbance?'

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'To him who is a wise and
contented man it is *but* little possible to bring disturbance.'
