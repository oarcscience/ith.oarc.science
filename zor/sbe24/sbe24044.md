### CHAPTER XXXVIII.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Wherefore *is it*
when they do not allot the happiness

<span id="page_76">p. 76</span>

of the worldly *existence* according to worthiness, and they make the
soul a seizer upon the spiritual *existences* by worthiness of action?'

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'On account of the
compassion of Aûha*r*ma*zd*, the lord, as regards the creatures, he
allots all happiness alike among the good and alike among the bad. 5.
But when it does not always come upon them, *it is* on account of the
oppression of Aharman and the demons, and the extortion of those seven
planets <span id="fr_405"></span>[1](#fn_400).

6\. 'And they make *one* <span id="fr_406"></span>[2](#fn_401) a seizer
upon the spiritual *existences*, by worthiness of action, on this
account, because the wickedness of any <span
id="fr_407"></span>[3](#fn_402) one arises through the performance of
his own actions.'
