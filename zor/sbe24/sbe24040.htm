CHAPTER XXXIV.

1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Has the creator Aûharmazd produced the creation of anything whatever for the worldly existence 1, unto which Aharman is not able to bring disturbance?'

3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'To him who is a wise and contented man it is but little possible to bring disturbance.'

Footnotes

70:1 L19 omits these four words.
