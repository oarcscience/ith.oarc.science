CHAPTER LV 6.

1. The fifty-fifth subject is this, that when a Navazûd 7 ceremony is performed, and it happens that it is not possible to consecrate a sacred cake, it is necessary that one should eat bread with the Hôrmazd inward prayer; and, afterwards, he should

p. 317

complete the prayer just as when they complete it as regards the sacred cake, so that the sin may be less.
