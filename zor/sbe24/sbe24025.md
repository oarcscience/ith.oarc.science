### CHAPTER XIX.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Is living in fear
*and* falsehood worse, or death?'

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'To live in fear and
falsehood is worse than death. 5. Because every one's life is necessary
for the enjoyment *and* pleasure of the worldly *existence*, (6) and
when the enjoyment *and* pleasure of the worldly *existence* are not
his, and fear and even falsehood <span id="fr_273"></span>[3](#fn_269.md)
are with him, it is called worse than death.'
