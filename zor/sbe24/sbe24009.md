### CHAPTER III.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Is liberality good,
or truth <span id="fr_161"></span>[1](#fn_159), (3.md) or gratitude <span
id="fr_162"></span>[2](#fn_160), or wisdom, (4.md) or complete
mindfulness <span id="fr_163"></span>[3](#fn_161.md), or contentment?'

5\. The spirit of wisdom answered (6) thus: 'As to the soul *it is*
liberality, as to all the world *it is* truth, (7) unto the sacred
beings *it is* gratitude, as to a man's self <span
id="fr_164"></span>[4](#fn_162) *it is* wisdom, (8.md) as to all business
*it is* complete mindfulness, and as to the comfort of the body and the
vanquishing of Aharman and the demons contentment is good.'
