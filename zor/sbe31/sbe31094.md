### VISPARAD XVII (SP. XX) <span id="fr_1498"></span>[3](#fn_1481.md).

And we strive after the good thoughts, words, and deeds inculcated in
the Yasna Haptanghâiti. A blessing is the Right (called) the Best,
(there is) weal; (there is) weal for this (man) when toward
Righteousness Best (there is) right.
