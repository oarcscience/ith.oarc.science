### V.

1\. To Ahura Mazda, the radiant, the glorious, to the Bountiful
Immortals, to Force well-shaped and stately, to the Blow of Victory,
Ahura-given, to the Victorious Ascendency (which it secures), to the
path of pleasantness, to the good Zarenuma*n*t <span
id="fr_1597"></span>[1](#fn_1568.md), to the 'Glowing' Mountain made by
Mazda, and to all the Yazads! 2. We sacrifice to Ahura Mazda, the
radiant, the glorious, and to the Bountiful Immortals who rule aright,
who dispose (of all) aright, and to Force well-shaped and stately, and
to the Blow of Victory, and to the Ascendency of Victory, and to the
path of pleasantness, and to Zarenuma*n*t, the good, which Mazda
created, and to the 'Glowing' Mount, and to every saint.
