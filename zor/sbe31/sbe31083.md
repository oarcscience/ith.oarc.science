### VISPARAD VI (SP. VII) <span id="fr_1438"></span>[1](#fn_1425.md).

In accordance with the precept, with praise, and with the joyful
reception of grace, with Zaothras intelligently offered, with
sacrificial words correctly spoken, I call the good Amesha Spe*n*ta by
their names of beauty; yea, I worship the Bountiful Immortals by their
beautiful names, with the blessing of the ritual Order, with the longing
blessing of Righteousness the good.
