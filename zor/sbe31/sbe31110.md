### II.

1\. I confess myself a Mazda-worshipper—for the praise of Thraêtaona,
the Âthwyan. Let them declare it—Propitiation be to the Fravashi of
Thraêtaona, the Âthwyan, the saint. 2. We sacrifice to Thraêtaona, the
Âthwyan, the holy lord of the ritual order; and may we be free from the
dog Kuro <span id="fr_1591"></span>[3](#fn_1563.md), and the Tarewani <span
id="fr_1592"></span>[3](#fn_1563), and the Karpan, (we who are.md) of <span
id="fr_1593"></span>[4](#fn_1564.md) those who sacrifice in order. 3. (The
Ahuna

<span id="page_390">p. 390</span>

follows.) Sacrifice, homage, strength, and swiftness be to the Fravashi
of Thraêtaona, the saint. (The Ahem and Ahmâi raê*sk*a follow.)
