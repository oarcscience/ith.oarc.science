### VISPARAD IV (SP. V) <span id="fr_1435"></span>[4](#fn_1422.md).

1\. (Yea,) we sacrifice to the thoughts of the mind, and to the good
wisdom, and to the good and blessed

<span id="page_344">p. 344</span>

sanctity, and to the good religious knowledge, and to good health (of
soul and body). \[At their (several) seasons, and with the presence of
seasonable circumstances, they are hymned <span
id="fr_1436"></span>[1](#fn_1423.md).\] 2. Confession is to be made for the
Kine; we, Zarathu*s*trian Mazdayasnians, celebrate at the sacrificial
time for the Myazda-offering, at the time for the Ratufrîti, the prayer
for blessings, for the sacrificial worship, homage, propitiation, and
praise of the entire creation of the holy (and the clean).
