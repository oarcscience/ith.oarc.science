### VII. RA*D*Ö-DÂ*D*-AÎTAG NASK <span id="fr_1796"></span>[1](#fn_1747.md).

Dk. VIII, Chap. VIII, 4, probably refers to the passage containing the
statement thus mentioned in Sls. X, 29:—'In the Ra*d*ŏ-dâ*d*-aîtîh many
harsh things are said about the severe punishment of the unhelpful
*ones* (avi*g*î*d*âr-daî*s*nânŏ) <span id="fr_1797"></span>[2](#fn_1748.md)
in the spiritual *existence*.'
