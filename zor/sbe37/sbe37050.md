## DÎNKA*RD*.—BOOK IX.

### CHAPTER I <span id="fr_492"></span>[1](#fn_489).

1\. Satisfaction (shnôkhar) *to* the creator Aûha*r*ma*zd*, *and*
obeisance *to* the Ma*zd*a-worshipping religion.

2\. The ninth book (babâ) is about the Hâs *and* Fargar*d*s <span
id="fr_493"></span>[2](#fn_490) of the various Nasks; the object of
procuring the division of those portions which exist *being* owing to
the quantity of what is in each one of the Nasks; *also* an explanation
of a suitable selection <span id="fr_494"></span>[3](#fn_491) therefrom,
such as is an epitome (nisangag-1) of the abundant detail therein.
