### No. 101.

### PAROSATA-JĀTAKA.

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Far better than a hundred fools, though they  
Think hard a hundred years unceasingly,  
Is one who, hearing, straightway understands.

</div>

\[411\] This story is in all respects analogous to the
Parosahassa-Jātaka (No. 99), with the sole difference that 'think hard'
is read here.
