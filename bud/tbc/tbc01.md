### DEDICATION.

In token of respect and affection I dedicate to my counsellor and friend
of many years, Hikkaduwe Sumangala, Pradhâna Nâyaka Sthavira and High
Priest of Adam's Peak (Sripada) and the Western Province, the "Buddhist
Catechism," in its revised form.

H. S. OLCOTT.

ADYAR, 1903.
