### 15.

1\. When he had undergone that probation, and while he was liable to the
Mânatta, he committed an offence--the first Sa*m*ghâdisesa--which for
five days he concealed.

He told, &c. . . . \[all that happened, from chapter 1 3 downwards\].

They told, &c.

'Let then the Sa*m*gha, O Bhikkhus, for that he when he had (&c., as in
the first paragraph) throw Udâyi the Bhikkhu back to the commencement of

<span id="page_406">p. 406</span>

his probationary term, and impose upon him an inclusive probation (for
this and) for the former offence.

'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, \[here follow two kammavâ*k*âs, as in the last
chapter. <span id="fr_733"></span>[1](sbe17170.htm#fn_713.md)'
