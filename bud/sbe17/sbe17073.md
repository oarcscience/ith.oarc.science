### 6.

1\. At that time the Bhikkhus who had to receive the robes (presented),
after having received them, left them there (in the Vihâras) and went
away; the robes were spoilt.

They told this thing to the Blessed One.

'I prescribe, O Bhikkhus, that you appoint a Bhikkhu possessed of the
following five qualities, to lay by the robes (received): (a person) who
does not go in the evil course of lust, in the evil course of hatred, in
the evil course of delusion, in the evil course of fear, and who knows
what is laid by and what is not.

2\. 'And you ought, O Bhikkhus, to appoint (&c., see chap. 5, § 2).'
