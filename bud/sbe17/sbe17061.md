### 7.

'A Bhikkhu, after the Ka*th*ina ceremony has been held, takes a robe
with him and goes away, &c. <span id="fr_320"></span>[2](#fn_315)'

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

End of the Âdâya- ('Taking away') Bhâ*n*avâra.

 

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_315"></span>[162:2](sbe17061.htm#fr_320) The whole chapter
6 is repeated here three times, the first time replacing the words
'takes a robe' by 'takes a robe with him' (comp. chap. 3); the second
time replacing 'takes a robe' by 'takes a robe not ready' (comp. chap.
4); and the third time with these two modifications combined (comp.
chap. 5).
