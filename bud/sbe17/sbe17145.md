### 35.

'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, should it be carried out,' &c. \[Here follows the
Kammavâ*k*â for the re-vocation of a Kamma precisely as in chapter 8,
with the necessary alterations.\]

Here ends the seventh (Kamma), the Ukkhepaniya-kamma on not renouncing a
sinful doctrine.

Here ends the First Khandhaka, the Khandhaka on the Kammas.
