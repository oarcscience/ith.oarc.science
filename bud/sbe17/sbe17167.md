### 13.

1\. Now at that time the venerable Udâyi committed an offence--the first
Sa*m*ghâdisesa--and for half a month he concealed it.

\[The rest of this chapter is precisely the same as chapter 3, reading
'for half a month' instead of 'for one day.'\]
