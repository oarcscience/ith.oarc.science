### 19.

1\. When he had accomplished the Mânatta he told the Bhikkhus \[all that
happened, from chapter 13 downwards\].

<span id="page_408">p. 408</span>

They told this matter to the Blessed One.

'Let then the Sa*m*gha rehabilitate Udâyi the Bhikkhu.

'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, \[here follows the kammavâ*k*â as in chapter
5.\]'

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Here end the proceedings on the breach of the first Sa*m*ghâdisesa.
