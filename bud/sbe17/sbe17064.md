### 10.

'A Bhikkhu, after the Ka*th*ina ceremony has been held, goes away on
some business. And when he has got beyond the boundary, he conceives the
expectation of getting a robe (presented). And he devotes himself to the
realisation of his expectation, and he obtains, &c. <span
id="fr_326"></span>[2](#fn_321)'

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

End of the Kara*n*îya-do*l*asaka <span id="fr_327"></span>[3](#fn_322).
