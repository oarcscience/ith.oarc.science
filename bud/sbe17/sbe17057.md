### Footnotes

<span id="fn_305"></span>[160:1](sbe17057.htm#fr_310) This chapter is
word for word identical with chap. 2: only instead of 'takes' (âdâya)
read 'takes with him' (samâdâya). We cannot say what different meaning
these two words are intended to convey.

<span id="fn_306"></span>[160:2](sbe17057.htm#fr_311) That is, 'the
seven cases in which he takes a robe with him.'
