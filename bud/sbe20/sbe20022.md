### 7.

1\. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu, tormented by distaste (for
meditation, &c.), castrated himself <span
id="fr_159"></span>[3](#fn_154). They told this matter to the Blessed
One.

'When one thing wanted cutting off, O Bhikkhus, that foolish fellow has
cut off another! You are

<span id="page_78">p. 78</span>

not, O Bhikkhus, to castrate yourselves. Whosoever does so, shall be
guilty of a thulla*kk*aya.'
