### XVIII

### THE PALLIATION OF THE INFERIOR

When the great Tao is lost sight of, we still have the *idea* of
benevolence and righteousness. Prudence and wisdom come to mind when we
see great hypocrisy. When relatives are! unfriendly, we still have the
*teachings* of filial piety and paternal affection. When the state and
the clan are in confusion and disorder, we still have the ideals of
loyalty and faithfulness.
