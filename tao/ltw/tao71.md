### LXXI

### THE DISEASE OF KNOWLEDGE

To recognize one's ignorance of unknowable things is mental health, and
to be ignorant of knowable things is sickness. Only by grieving over
ignorance of knowable things are we in <span id="page_48">p. 48</span>
mental health. The wise man is wise because he understands his ignorance
and is grieved over it.
