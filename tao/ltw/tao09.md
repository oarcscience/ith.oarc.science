### IX

### MODERATION

Continuing to fill a pail after it is full the water will be wasted.
Continuing to grind an axe after it is sharp will soon wear it away.

Who can protect a public hall crowded with gold and jewels? The pride of
wealth and position brings about their own misfortune. To win <span
id="page_15">p. 15</span> true merit, to preserve just fame, the
personality must be retiring. This is the heavenly Tao.
