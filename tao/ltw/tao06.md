### VI

### THE INFINITUDE OF CREATIVE EFFORT

The Spirit of the perennial spring is said to be immortal, she is called
the Mysterious One. The Mysterious One is typical of the source of
heaven and earth. It is continually and endlessly issuing and without
effort.
