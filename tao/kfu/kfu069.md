No. 41.—Lan Ts‘ai-ho (\#) <span id="fr_15"></span>[\*](#fn_15.md) imitating
the Black Dragon shaking his Horns.—To cure pain of the entire body.

<span id="img_19500"></span> ![](img/19500.jpg)

<span id="page_196">p. 196</span>

<div style="font-size: 93%">

Sit upright, extend both feet, close firmly the two hands, and together
with the body direct them to the front, revolve the air in 24 mouthfuls,
place the feet on the ground, bend the head, let the two hands grasp the
toes of the two feet, and revolve the air as above.

The Soup to cause the Blood-vessels to circulate.

<div style="font-size: 93%">

*Prescription*.—Take of hsuen-hu-so, tang-kwei, jou-kwei, of each 1
ounce; powder, mix with wine, and take 3 or 4 mace. Add wine according
to each individual's wine capacity; when the pain ceases, cease the
medicine.
