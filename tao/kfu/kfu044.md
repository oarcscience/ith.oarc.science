No. 16.—Wei Po-yang's (\#) <span id="fr_8"></span>[\*](kfu045.htm#fn_8)
Method of beating the Wind.—To cure chronic paralysis.

<div style="font-size: 93%">

Sit upright, place the right fist against the right ribs, with the left
hand press the knee, extend and withdraw the feet, think, move the air
to the diseased part right and left each in 6 mouthfuls.

The Gold-producing Tiger-bones Powder.

<div style="font-size: 93%">

*Prescription*.—Take of tang-kwei, ch‘ïh-shao (\#), Paeonia albiflora
(the cultivated variety which bears red flowers), ch‘wen-hsü-twan (\#),
Dipsacus asper or Lamium album from Szechuan, pai-shu, kau-pên, tigers'
bones,—of each 1 ounce; wu-shao-shĕ-jou (\#) 5 mace. Powder, dose 2
mace, to be swallowed with tepid wine.
