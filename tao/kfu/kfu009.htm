5.—Third Month,—Solar Term "Pure Brightness."—Change hands right and left, like drawing the bow, each 7 × 8 times, knock the teeth, respire, taking in the outside pure air to displace the foul air from within, and swallow the saliva each 3 times. To cure the weakness and vicious air of the loins, kidneys, intestines, and stomach, painful deglutition, ears deaf .and painful, pain of the neck and inability to turn it, pain in the shoulder and unable to raise it, body bent and without strength, arm painful, and weakness of the loins.—See Figure, below. *
