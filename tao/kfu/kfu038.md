No. 10.—Ma Tan-yang's (\#) Method of using the strength of Fire for the
whole Body.—To cure the primordial air in a debilitated condition.

<div style="font-size: 93%">

Sit firmly cross-legged, first rub the two hands warm, then rub the
eyes; afterwards use them to sustain below the ribs on the two sides.
When the air advances, rouse it to go upwards, and move the air round in
12 mouthfuls.

<span id="page_163">p. 163</span>

The Ginseng Astragalus Soup.

<div style="font-size: 93%">

*Prescription*.—Take of ginseng, hwang-ch‘i (\#), Astragalus, pai-shu
(\#), tang-kwei, of each 2 mace; orange peel, fu-ling, liquorice, of
each 1 mace. Add ginger and jujubes, and boil in water.

The Figure is omitted.
