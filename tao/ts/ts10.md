### (Good and Evil Spirits.)

If a man's heart be awakened to the good, though the good be not yet
accomplished, good spirits verily are already following him. (1170-1184)

If a man's heart be awakened to evil, though evil be not yet
accomplished, evil spirits verily are already following him. (1185-1199)
