### (Quotations.<sup>\[64\]</sup>)

Those who have hitherto done evil deeds should henceforth mend and
repent. (1200-1209)

If evil be no longer practiced and good deeds done, and if in this way a
man continues and continues, he will surely obtain happiness

{p. 66}

and felicity. He will, indeed, so to speak, transform curses into
blessings. (1210-1230)
