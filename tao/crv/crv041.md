### 35. THE VIRTUE OF BENEVOLENCE.

<span id="img_tao35"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/tao35.jpg) |

1\. "Who holdeth fast to the great Form,  
Of him the world will come in quest:  
For there we never meet with harm,  
There we find shelter, comfort, rest."

2\. Music with dainties makes the passing stranger stop. But Reason,
when <span id="page_98">p. 98</span> coming from the mouth, how
tasteless is it! It has no flavor. When looked at, there is not enough
to be seen; when listened to, there is not enough to be heard. However,
when used, it is inexhaustible.
