### CHAPTER 64.

The last word here translated by "interfere" is in Chinese *wei*, "to
do" or "to act."

The terms "likely" and "unlikely" are literal translations of the
Chinese. Likely apparently means what is common or usual, and the
unlikely, what is unusual.
