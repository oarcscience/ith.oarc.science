### CHAPTER 54.

This chapter, like so many other passages, is directed against the
Confucianists who in their ethics insist on the ritual of ancestral
sacrifices. Lao-tze believes that wherever the Tao is observed, filial
piety and sacrificial celebrations will be spontaneous.
