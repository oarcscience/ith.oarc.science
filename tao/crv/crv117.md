### CHAPTER 45.

Literally the second quotation reads:

"Greatest straightness is like a curve,  
Greatest skill is like awkwardness,  
Greatest eloquence is like stammering."

The first line reminds us of modern geometry where the straight line may
be regarded as a curve of an infinitely small curvature. Cf. note on
Chapter 41.
