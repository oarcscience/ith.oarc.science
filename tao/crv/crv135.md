### CHAPTER 79.

The original reads, "The holy man keeps the left (*tso*) of contract"
and *tso*, "left," means the debit side. The right side of the contract
table contained the claims, *ch‘eh*, which in its original meaning
denotes "to go through" and then "that which can be enacted."
