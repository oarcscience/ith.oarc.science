Why include the works of Shakespeare at Intangible Textual Heritage? The
answer to this will be self-evident to some, and a mystery to others.

This site includes the works of several other dramatists, such as
[Aeschylus](../cla/aesch/index), [Sophocles](../cla/soph/index),
[Euripides](../cla/eurip/index), [Kalidasa](../hin/sha/index), the [Noh
Plays](../shi/npj/index) of Japan, and the Inca [Drama of
Ollantay](../nam/inca/oll/index). In common with Shakespeare, these
great works of world literature illuminate the main themes of this site,
as well as the human condition in general.
