116. Revelation to Joseph Smith, Spring Hill, Daviess County, Missouri, May 19, 1838.
History of the Church 3:35.

116:1 Spring Hill is named by the Lord Adam-ondi-Ahman, because, said he, it is the place where Adam shall come to visit his people, or the Ancient of Days shall sit, as spoken of by Daniel the prophet.
