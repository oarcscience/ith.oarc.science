TO CLOSE THE COMMANDERY.

__________________________

The Commander directs that the same precautionary steps be taken previous to the ceremony of Closing.

PRAYER.

The Prelate will lead in the Lord's Prayer, after which he will pronounce this, or some other Benediction:

"The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen."
