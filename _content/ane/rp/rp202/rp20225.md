#### XI. <span class="smallcaps">Inscriptions of Dungi, King of Ur</span>

No. 1.—<span class="smallcaps">Inscription On A Tablet</span> <span
id="fr_257"></span>[1](#fn_257)

1\. For the god <span class="smallcaps">Nin-girsu</span>,  
2. the powerful warrior  
3. of the god <span class="smallcaps">Ellilla</span>,  
4. for his king,  
5. Dungi  
6. the puissant prince,  
7. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Ur</span>, <span
id="fr_258"></span>[2](#fn_258)  
8. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Shumer</span> and <span
class="smallcaps">Accad</span>, <span id="fr_259"></span>[3](#fn_259)  
9. the temple of <span class="smallcaps">E-ninnû</span>  
10. his favourite temple  
11. has constructed.

No. 2.—<span class="smallcaps">Inscription on a Tablet</span> <span
id="fr_260"></span>[4](#fn_260)

1\. For the goddess <span class="smallcaps">Ninâ</span>,  
2. the lady of destinies (?),  
3. the lady of oracles (?),  
4. for his mistress,  
5. Dungi  
6. the puissant prince,  
7. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Ur</span>,  
8. the king of <span class="smallcaps">Shumer</span> and <span
class="smallcaps">Accad</span>,  
9. the temple of <span class="smallcaps">E-shish-shish-e-ma-ra</span>,  
10. her favourite temple,  
11. has constructed.
