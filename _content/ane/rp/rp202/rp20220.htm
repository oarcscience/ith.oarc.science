Inscriptions of Ur-Bau

No. 2.—On the Stone of a Threshold 1

1. For the god En-ki,
2. his king,
3. Ur-Bau,
4. the patesi
5. Of Shirpurla,
6. the offspring begotten
7. by the god Nin-âgal,
