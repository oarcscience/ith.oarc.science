#### IV. <span class="smallcaps">Inscription of Entena on a Buttress</span>

1\. To the goddess <span class="smallcaps">Gatumdug</span>,  
2. the mother of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla-ki</span>,  
3. Entena,  
4. the patesi  
5. of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla-ki</span>,  
6. who has built the temple of the goddess <span
class="smallcaps">Gatumdug</span>.  
7. His god  
8. is the god <span class="smallcaps">Dun-sir(?)-anna</span>.
