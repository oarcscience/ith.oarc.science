#### <span id="page_74">p. 74</span>

#### V. <span class="smallcaps">Inscription of En-anna-tumma on a Buttress</span> <span id="fr_155"></span>[1](#fn_154)

1\. For the god <span class="smallcaps">Nin-girsu</span>,  
2. the warrior of the god <span class="smallcaps">Ellilla</span>,  
3. En-anna-tumma,  
4. the patesi  
5. Of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla-ki</span>,  
6. the chosen of the heart  
7. of the goddess <span class="smallcaps">Ninâ</span>,  
8. the great patesi  
9. of the god <span class="smallcaps">Nin-girsu</span>,  
10. the son of Entena  
11. the patesi  
12. of <span class="smallcaps">Shirpurla-ki</span>.  
13. For the god <span class="smallcaps">Nin-girsu</span>  
14. his house of fruits  
15. he has restored.  
16. Of En-anna-tumma,  
17. who the house of fruits  
18. of the god <span class="smallcaps">Nin-girsu</span>  
19. has restored,  
20. his god  
21. is the god <span class="smallcaps">Dun-sir(?)-anna</span>.
