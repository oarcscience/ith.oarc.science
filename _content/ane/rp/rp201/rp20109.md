#### No. 4.—TRANSLATION OF THE BABYLONIAN CHRONICLE

*Obv*.—<span class="small">COLUMN I</span>

1\. \[In the 3d year of Nabonassar\] king of <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>

2\. \[Tiglath-pileser\] in <span class="smallcaps">Assyria</span> sat on
the throne.

3\. In the same year \[Tiglath-pileser\] descended into the country of
<span class="smallcaps">Accad</span>, and

4\. the cities of <span class="smallcaps">Rabbiku</span> and <span
class="smallcaps">Khamranu</span> he spoiled,

5\. and the gods of the city of <span class="smallcaps">Sapazza</span>
he carried away.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

6\. In the time of Nabu-natsir (Nabonassar) the town of <span
class="smallcaps">Borsippa</span>

7\. was separated from <span class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>. The
battle which Nabonassar

8\. fought against <span class="smallcaps">Borsippa</span> is not
described. <span id="fr_42"></span>[1](#fn_42)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

9\. In the 5th year of Nabu-natsir Umma(n)-nigas

10\. in <span class="smallcaps">Elam</span> sat upon the throne.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

11\. In (his) 14th year Nabu-natsir fell ill and died <span
id="fr_43"></span>[2](#fn_43) in his palace.

12\. For 14 years Nabu-natsir reigned over <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

13\. Nadinu <span id="fr_44"></span>[3](#fn_44) his son sat upon the
throne in <span class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

14\. In the second year Nadinu was slain in an insurrection.

15\. For two years Nadinu reigned over <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

16\. Suma-yukin <span id="fr_45"></span>[4](#fn_45) the governor, the

S. Yukin-zira … seized upon the throne.

20\. when he had descended into the country of <span
class="smallcaps">Accad</span>,

21\. destroyed <span class="smallcaps">Bit-Amukanu</span> and captured
Yukin-zira.

22\. For 3 years Yukin-zira reigned over <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

23\. Tiglath-pileser sat upon the throne in <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

class="smallcaps">Accad</span>

26\. and <span class="smallcaps">Assyria</span> had exercised. For two
years he reigned in <span class="smallcaps">Accad</span>.

27\. On the 25th day of the month Tebet Sulman-asarid (Shalmaneser) in
<span class="smallcaps">Assyria</span>

28\. sat upon the throne. He destroyed the city of <span
class="smallcaps">Sabarahin</span>. <span id="fr_47"></span>[2](#fn_47)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

29\. In (his) 5th year Sulman-asarid died in the month Tebet.

30\. For 5 years Sulman-asarid reigned over the countries of <span
class="smallcaps">Accad</span> and <span
class="smallcaps">Assyria</span>.

31\. On the 12th day of the month Tebet Sargon sat upon the throne in
<span class="smallcaps">Assyria</span>.

32\. In the month Nisan Merodach-baladan sat upon the throne in <span
class="smallcaps">Babylon</span>.

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

33\. In the 2d year of Merodach-baladan Umma(n)-nigas king of Elam
