IV. Inscription of Entena on a Buttress

1. To the goddess Gatumdug,
2. the mother of Shirpurla-ki,
3. Entena,
4. the patesi
5. of Shirpurla-ki,
6. who has built the temple of the goddess Gatumdug.
7. His god
8. is the god Dun-sir(?)-anna.
