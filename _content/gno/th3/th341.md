#### XI.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### ANONYMOUS

<span class="smallcaps">And</span> here we may conveniently append a
reference to the Dialogue of an ancient Christian writer on astrology—a
blend of Platonism, Astrology, and Christianity—entitled *Hermippus de
Astrologia Dialogus*, <span id="fr_660"></span>[1](#fn_660) from the
name of the chief speaker.

This writer was undoubtedly acquainted with our Corpus, for he quotes
(p. 9. 3) from *C. H.*, i. 5; (p. 21, 5) from *C. H.*, x. (xi.) 12; (p.
70, 17) from *C. H.*, x. (xi.) 6; in a general fashion (p. 24, 25) from
*C. H.*, xvi.; and phrases (p. 12, 21 and p. 14, 13) from *C. H.*,
xviii.
