<span id="page_xvi">p. xvi</span>

> <span style="font-size: small">Was he one or many, merging  
>     Name and fame in one,  
> Like a stream, to which, converging,  
>     Many streamlets run?</span>
>
>  . . . . . .
>
> <span style="font-size: small">Who shall call his dreams fallacious?  
>     Who has searched or sought  
> All the unexplored and spacious  
>     Universe of thought?  
>   
> Who in his own skill confiding,  
>     Shall with rule and line  
> Mark the border-land dividing  
>     Human and divine?  
>   
> Trismegistus! Three times greatest!  
>     How thy name sublime  
> Has descended to this latest  
>     Progeny of time! </span>

<span class="smallcaps">Longfellow</span>, *Hermes Trismegistus.* <span
id="fr_0"></span>[1](#fn_0)
