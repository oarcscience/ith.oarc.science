# KUNG-FU,

###### OR

### TAUIST MEDICAL GYMNASTICS.

##### BY

## JOHN DUDGEON, M.D., C.M.

pp. 67-291 in *The Beverages of the Chinese; Kung-fu; or, Tauist medical
gymnastics; the population of China; a modern Chinese anatomist; and a
chapter in Chinese surgery*.

#### Tientsin, Tientsin press

#### \[1895\]

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage by John
Bruno Hare, October 2008. This text is in the public domain in the US
because it was published prior to 1923.

This is an historical text which includes accounts of 19th century
Chinese medical practices. Please do not attempt any of the exercises or
treatments described in this document without consulting your physician
first.
