7.—*Fourth Month, Solar Term named "Beginning of Summer."*—Breath closed
and eyes shut, turn and change the hands, and press them on the knees
each 5 × 7 times, etc. To cure wind and dampness collected in the
ching-lo or net-work of small blood-vessels, arms and

<span id="page_131">p. 131</span>

axillæ swollen and painful, palms of the hands hot. This illustration
(see Figure below) corresponds with Amiot's No. 16, which is said to be
against the continual heat of the palms of the hands and soles of the
feet.
