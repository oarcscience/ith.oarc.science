### XXXIII

### THE VIRTUE (TEH) OF DISCRIMINATION

He who knows others is intelligent; he who understands himself is
enlightened; he who is able to conquer others has force, but he who is
able to control himself is mighty. He who appreciates contentment is
wealthy.

He who dares to act has nerve; if he can maintain <span id="page_28">p.
28</span> his position he will endure, but he, who dying does not
perish, is immortal.
