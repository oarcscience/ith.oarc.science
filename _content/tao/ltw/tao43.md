### XLIII

### THE FUNCTION OF THE UNIVERSAL

The most tender things of creation race over the hardest.

A non-material existence enters into the most impenetrable.

I therefore recognize an advantage in the doctrine of not doing (wu wei)
and not speaking. <span id="page_34">p. 34</span> But there are few in
the world who obtain the advantage of non-assertion (wu wei) and
silence.
