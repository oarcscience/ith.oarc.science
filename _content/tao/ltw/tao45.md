### XLV

### THE VIRTUE (TEH) OF GREATNESS

Extreme perfection seems imperfect, its function is not exhausted.
Extreme fullness appears empty, its function is not exercised.

Extreme straightness appears crooked; great skill, clumsy; great
eloquence, stammering. Motion conquers cold, quietude conquers heat. Not
greatness but purity and clearness are the world's standard.
