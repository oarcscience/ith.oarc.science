### XXXV

### THE VIRTUE (TEH) OF BENEVOLENCE

The world will go to him who grasps this Great Principle; they will seek
and not be injured, they will find contentment, peace and rest.

Music and dainties attract the passing people, while Tao's reality
seen-is insipid. Indeed it has no taste, when looked at there is not
enough seen to be prized, when listened for, it can scarcely be heard,
but, the use of it is inexhaustible.
