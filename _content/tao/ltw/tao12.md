### XII

### AVOIDING DESIRE

An excess of light blinds the human eye; an excess of noise ruins the
ear; an excess of condiments deadens the taste. The effect of too much
horse racing and hunting is bad, and the lure of hidden treasure tempts
one to do evil.

Therefore the wise man attends to the inner significance of things and
does not concern himself with outward appearances. Therefore he ignores
matter and seeks the spirit.
