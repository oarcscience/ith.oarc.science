### FORTY-THREE

The tenderest things of creation prevail over the hardest. Something
immaterial enters into the most impenetrable to preserve its unity. I,
therefore, recognise the advantages of the principle of *wu-wei* and
teach non-interference. There is no other doctrine

<span id="page_51">p. 51</span>

that is grander than the doctrine of TAO, and no other teaching that is
more universally potent than the teaching of *wu-wei*.
