### CHAPTER 25.

The word *shi*, "departing," may very well be understood in the sense of
dying.

The word *fan* means literally "return," denoting "coming back," and in
order

<span id="page_161">p. 161</span>

to imitate the terse Chinese text, the best translation for "having come
back" is "home." Lao-tze says: "Reason, the great distant beyond, is our
home."

Section 5 seems to be a gloss which slipped into the text. At any rate
the bracketed portion is too trivial to come from the hand of Lao-tze.
