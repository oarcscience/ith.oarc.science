### 4.

1\. Now at that time the *Kh*abbaggiya Bhikkhus used to wear woollen
cloth with long fleece to it <span id="fr_151"></span>[1](#fn_146).

The people murmured . . . (&c., down to) They told this matter to the
Blessed One.

'You are not, O Bhikkhus, to wear woollen cloth with long fleece to it.
Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a dukka*t*a.'
