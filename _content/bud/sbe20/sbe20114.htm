23.

Now at that time Bhikkhunîs dwelt in the forest, and men of abandoned life violated them.

p. 363

They told this matter to the Blessed One.

'A Bhikkhunî is not, O Bhikkhus, to adopt the forest life. Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a dukkata 1.'
