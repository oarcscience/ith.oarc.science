### 23.

Now at that time Bhikkhunîs dwelt in the forest, and men of abandoned
life violated them.

<span id="page_363">p. 363</span>

They told this matter to the Blessed One.

'A Bhikkhunî is not, O Bhikkhus, to adopt the forest life. Whosoever
does so, shall be guilty of a dukka*t*a <span
id="fr_947"></span>[1](#fn_931).'
