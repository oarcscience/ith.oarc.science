1. The Revelation of the True Doctrine.

In the one soul we may distinguish two aspects.

The one is the Soul as suchness (bhûtatathatâ), the other is the soul as birth-and-death (samsâra). Each in itself constitutes all things, and both are so closely interrelated that one cannot be separated from the other.
