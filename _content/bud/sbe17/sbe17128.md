### 17 <span id="fr_660"></span>[1](#fn_646).

1\. 'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, should the revocation be carried out. The
Bhikkhu, who has been subjected to the Pabbâ*g*aniya-kamma, should go
before the Sa*m*gha (&c., as before in chapter 8, §§ 1, 2, down to the
end).'

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Here ends the third (Kamma), the Pabbâ*g*aniya-k a mm a.
