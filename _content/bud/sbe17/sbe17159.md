### 5.

1\. When he had accomplished the Mânatta he told the Bhikkhus, saying,
'I committed (&c., as 4. 1, down to) for one day concealed. I asked, &c.
. . . The Sa*m*gha imposed a probation, &c. . . . When I passed through
that probation the Sa*m*gha imposed a Mânatta of &c. . . . for &c. . . .
I have accomplished that Mânatta. What now shall I do?'

They told that matter to the Blessed One.

'Let then the Sa*m*gha, O Bhikkhus, rehabilitate Udâyi the Bhikkhu.'

2, 3. \[Here follows the kammavâ*k*â as in chapter 2, §§ 2, 3, to the
end.\]
