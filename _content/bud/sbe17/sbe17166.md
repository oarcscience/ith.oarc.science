### 12.

1\. When he had accomplished that Mânatta he told the Bhikkhus \[all
that had happened\].

They told, &c.

'Let then the Sa*m*gha, O Bhikkhus, rehabilitate Udâyi the Bhikkhu.

'Now thus \[here follows the kammavâ*k*â as before\].
