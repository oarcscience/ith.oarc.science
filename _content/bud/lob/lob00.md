# THE LIFE

###### of

### BUDDHA

#### According to the Legends of Ancient India

###### By

## A. FERDINAND HEROLD

##### Translated from the French by

### PAUL C. BLUM

##### Decorations by Mac Hershberger

Original Title: La Vie du Bouddha, by André Ferdinand Hérold, Paris,
Édition d’Art, H. Piazza \[1922\], Translation by Paul C. Bloom, New
York, A. & C. Boni, \[1927\]. The copyright on the translation was not
renewed.

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage, March
2007 by John Bruno Hare. This text is in the public domain in the United
States because the original book it was translated prior to 1923, and
the copyright on the translation was not renewed in a timely fashion as
required by law at the time.
