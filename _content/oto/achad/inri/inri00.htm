I.N.R.I.

DE MYSTERIIS ROSÆ RUBEÆ ET AUREÆ CRUCIS

BY

ONE

Whose Number is

777

Wherein, under the form of an admonition to an Adeptus Minor of the R. R. et A. C., is disclosed the true Symbolism of the Rosy Cross for the enlightenment of those who are worthy of the same.

Frater Achad (Charles Robert Stansfeld Jones)

Chicago, Ill., Published for the Collegium ad Spiritum Sanctum by the New Æon Publishing Co.

[1924]
