108

Μὴ κίνη χέραδασ. [transcription]

Stir not the pebbles.

Mary Barnard translates this: "If you're squeamish, don't prod the beach rubble."

From the Scholiast on Apollonius Rhodius. Χεράδεσ [transcription] were little heaps of stone.
