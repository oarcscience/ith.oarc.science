97

Μελλίχιοσ δ᾽ ἐπ᾽ ἰμμέρτῳ κέχυται προσώποῳ. [transcription]

And a sweet expression spreads over her fair face.

From Hepaestion. Compare Catullus, "Mellitos oculos." and "Pulcher es neque te Venus negligit."
