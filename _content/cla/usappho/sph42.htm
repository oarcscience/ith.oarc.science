41

Ὄτα πάννυχοσ ἄσφι κατάγρει. [transcription]

When all night long [sleep] holds them.

Bergk suggest that the words ὄππατ᾽ ἄωροσ [transcription] may have preceeded these words. The fragment quoted by Apollonius, and its sense may be "when all night long sleep holds their eyes,"
