102

...Χαῖρε, νύμφα,
Χαῖρε, τίμιε γαμβε, πόλλα. [transcription]

Hail bride, and all hail! noble bridegroom.

Quoted by Servius about A.D. 390, and referred to by Pollux and Julian.
