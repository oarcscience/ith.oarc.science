# <span id="BM39">39</span>

Ἄτθι σοὶ δ ἔμεθέν μεν άπήχθετο  
φροντίσδεν, ἔπι δ᾽ Ἀνδρομέδαν πότῃ. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#59)

But to thee, Athis, the thought of me is hateful; thou fliest to
Andromeda.

Quoted by Hephaestion with the preceding, to which it does not appear
really to belong.
