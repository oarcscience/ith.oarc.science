74

Ασαροτέρασ οὔγδαμ᾽ ἐπ ὦ π᾽άννα σεθεν τύχοισα. [transcription]

One more scornful than thee, O Eranna, I have never found.

Quoted by Hephaestion. The reading is doubtful.
