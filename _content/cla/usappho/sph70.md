# <span id="BM69">69</span>

Ἄλλά τισ οὐκ ἔμμι παλιγκότων  
ὄργαν, ἀλλ᾽ ἀβάκην τὰν φρέν᾽ ἔχω. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#91)

I am not of a malign nature but have a calm temper.

Quoted in the "Etymologicum Magnum" to show the meaning of
ἀβάκησ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#92) , "innocent",
"unsophisticated."
