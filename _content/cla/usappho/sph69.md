# <span id="BM68">68</span>

Ἤρων ἐξεδίδαξ᾽ εκ Γυάρων τὰν
τανυσίδρομον. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#90)

Hero of Gyara, that swift runner, I taught.

Quoted by Choeroboscus to show an Aeolic form of the accusative.
