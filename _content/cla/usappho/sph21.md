# <span id="BM20">20</span>

... Παντοδάπαισ μεμιγμένα χροίαισιν. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#34)

Shot with innumerable hues.

Quoted by the Scholiast on Apollonius of Rhodes, i, 727. Sappho's
reference may be to the rainbow.
