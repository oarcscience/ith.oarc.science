# <span id="BM66">66</span>

Οὐδ᾽ ἴαν δοκίμοιμι προσίδοισαν φάοσ ἀλίω  
ἔσσεσθαι σοφίαν πάρθενον ἐισ οὐδένα πω χρόνον
τοιαύταν. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#88)

I think that no maiden shall ever see the sunlight, who shall have thy
wisdom.

No maiden, I think, more wise than thou  
Shall ever see the sun.

Quoted by Chrysippus, and may be part of the preceding poem.
