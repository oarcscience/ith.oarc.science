# <span id="BM41">41</span>

Ὄτα πάννυχοσ ἄσφι κατάγρει. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#61)

When all night long \[sleep\] holds them.

Bergk suggest that the words ὄππατ᾽
ἄωροσ [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#62) may have preceeded these
words. The fragment quoted by Apollonius, and its sense may be "when all
night long sleep holds their eyes,"
