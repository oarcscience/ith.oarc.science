# <span id="BM103">103</span>

Οὐ γὰρ ἦν ἀτέρα παῖσ, ὦ γάμβρε,
τοαύτα. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#131)

For, like her, O bridegroom, there was no other maiden.

From Dionysius of Halicarnassus.
