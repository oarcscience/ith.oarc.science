# <span id="BM9">9</span>

Αἴθ᾽ ἔγο χρυσοστέφαν᾽ Ἀφρόδιτα,  
τόνδε τὸν πάλον λαχόην. [\[transcription\]](xlatall.htm#13)

May I win this prize, O golden-crowned Aphrodite.

From Apollonius. Sappho invented many beautiful epithets to apply to
Aphrodite, and this fragment contains one of them.
