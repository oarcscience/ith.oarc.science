### THE LOVE OF SELENE

<span class="smallcaps">Across</span> the still sea's moonlit wave  
       Selene came  
Softly to seek the Latmian cave,  
       Her breast aflame

With secret passion's ruthless throe,  
       Her scruples done,  
And burning with desire to know  
       Endymion.
