### 78. BÔLUS OF MENDÊ

<span class="smallcaps">Bôlus</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Mendê</span> in the Nile Delta, lived in the third
century B.C.

He wrote works on *Potent Natural Products*, and on *Antipathetic and
Sympathetic Substances*, which were often ascribed to Democritus; these
were books of magic remedies. He also wrote other works of magical
doctrine, on alchemy and divination.
