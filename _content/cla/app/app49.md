### 54. PRÔRUS OF CYRENE, AMŶCLAS, CLEINIAS OF TARENTUM

<span class="smallcaps">Prôrus</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Cyrene</span>, <span class="smallcaps">Amŷclas</span>,
<span class="smallcaps">Cleinias</span> of <span
class="smallcaps">Tarentum</span>.

Latter-day Pythagoreans; forged writings in the name of Prôrus and
Cleinias were in circulation in later times, but no genuine writings
survive.
