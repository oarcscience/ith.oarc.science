# 10

### Priapus

Ne prendare, cave, prenso nec fuste nocebo,  
   saeva nec incurva vulnera falce dabo:  
traiectus conto sic extendere pedali,  
   ut culum rugam non habuisse putes.

'Ware of my catching! If caught, with rod I never will harm thee  
Nor to thee deal sore wound using my sickle that curves.  
Pierced with a foot-long pole thy skin shall be stretched in such
fashion  
Thou shalt be fain to believe ne'er had a wrinkle thine arse.

Take heed lest thou art caught. If I do seize thee, nor with my club
will I belabour thee, nor cruel wounds with the curved sickle will
inflict on thee. Thrust into by my twelve-inch I pole, thou shalt be so
stretched that thou wilt drink thy anus never had any wrinkles.
