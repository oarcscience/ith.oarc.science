The Origin and Significance of the Great Pyramid

by C. Staniland Wake

[1882]
Contents    Start Reading    Page Index    Text [Zipped]

In this classic of Pyramidology, C. Staniland Wake attempts to answer the questions: who actually built the pyramids, and why? He rejects the conventional theory that they were tombs, and explores astronomical and religious motivations for their construction instead.
