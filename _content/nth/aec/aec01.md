<td data-valign="top"><p>LOOK TO THIS DAY</p>
<p>Look to this day!<br />
For it is life, the very life of life.<br />
For yesterday is already a dream, and tomorrow is only a vision;<br />
But today, well lived, makes every yesterday<br />
A dream of happiness, and every tomorrow a vision of hope.<br />
Look well, therefore, to this day!<br />
Such is the salutation of the dawn!</p>
