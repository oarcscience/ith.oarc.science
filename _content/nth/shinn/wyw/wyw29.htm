Your Word is Your Wand, by Florence Scovel Shinn, [1928], at Intangible Textual Heritage

Nothing is too good
to be true

Nothing is too wonderful
to happen

Nothing is too good
to last

CONCLUSION

Choose the affirmation which appeals to you the most and wave it over the situation which confronts you.

It is your magic wand, for your word is God in action.

"It shall not return unto me void but shall accomplish that whereunto it is sent." (Isaiah 55-11.)

"But I say, have they not heard? Yes, verily, their sound went into all the earth and their words unto the end of the world." (Romans 10-18.)
