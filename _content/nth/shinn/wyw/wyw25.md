### ANIMALS

(*Dog: for example*)

———

#### AFFIRMATIONS

I deny any appearance of disorder. This dog is a perfect idea in Divine
Mind and now expresses God's perfect Idea of a perfect dog.

———

Infinite Intelligence illumines and directs this animal. It is a perfect
idea in Divine Mind and is always in its right place.
