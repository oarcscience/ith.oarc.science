Forty Modern Fables

by George Ade

[1901]

Contents    Start Reading

This is a set of 'fractured fables' written on the cusp of the 20th century. Written in breezy vernacular style, these tales are timeless, even if some of the slang and cultural references have passed into obscurity in the intervening century.
