This page indexes resources at Intangible Textual Heritage relating to the Christmas holiday.

Primary Texts

A Visit from St. Nicholas   by Clement C. Moore [1823]

A Christmas Carol   by Charles Dickens [1843]

Other books

Christmas in Ritual and Tradition   by Clement A. Miles [1912]   An in-depth study of the Pagan origins of the holiday.

Fifty Christmas Poems for Children   by Florence B. Hyett [1923]   Traditional Christmas songs.

Manners, Customs, and Observances: Their Origin and Significance   by Leopold Wagner [1894]   Includes information about Christmas traditions.

Festivals of Western Europe   by Dorothy Gladys Spicer [1958]   Includes information about on traditional European Christmas observances.
