El Castillo Interior (The Interior Castle) or Las Moradas (The Mansions)
was written by St. Teresa of Avila in 1577. According to contemporary
accounts, St. Teresa had a revelation from God of a crystal globe in the
shape of a castle containing seven mansions. This is St. Teresa's
interpretation of that vision, describing the journey of faith through
seven stages, ending with union with God.
