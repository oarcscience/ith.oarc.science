##  Note

\[On p. 43 *supra* I omitted to direct attention to the desirable
enlargement of<sup>[167](footnote/fn9.htm#P908_238641)</sup> by a
reference to Homer's Hymn of Mercury and its minute description of the
invention of the Lyre. The passage is given in Henry Nelson Coleridge's
*Introduction*, etc., p.202. The versified translation of Shelley is
inimitable; in *ottava rima*, but instinct with the *ethos* of the
original.\]
