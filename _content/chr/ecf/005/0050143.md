##  A Roman Council Celebrated Under Stephen.

A Divine and sacred provincial synod, gathered together at Rome by
Stephen, the blessed martyr and
*father*<sup>[1](footnote/fn105.htm#P10562_3333453)</sup> which
excommunicated those who in an African synod had, without reason,
conceded that they who came to the Catholic Church from any heresy
should be re-baptized.<sup>[2](footnote/fn105.htm#P10563_3333673)</sup>
