#### <span id="iv.iii.xi.v-p0.1"></span>Chapter V.

<span id="iv.iii.xi.v-p1"></span>A comparison which shows the nature of
vainglory.

<span id="iv.iii.xi.v-p2"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="iv.iii.xi.v-p2.1"></span>Our</span> elders admirably describe the
nature of this malady as like that of an onion, and of those bulbs which
when stripped of one covering you find to be sheathed in another; and as
often as you strip them, you find them still protected.
