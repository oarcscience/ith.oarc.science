<span id="vi.VIII-p2"></span>————————————

<span id="vi.VIII-p3"></span>He finally describes the thirty-second year
of his age, the most memorable of his whole life, in which, being
instructed by Simplicianus concerning the conversion of others, and the
manner of acting, he is, after a severe struggle, renewed in his whole
mind, and is converted unto God.
