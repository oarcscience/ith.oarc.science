<span id="v.iii.xciv-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.xciv-p2.1"></span>Triphylius</span>, <span
id="fr_2517"></span>[2518](#fn_2517) bishop of Ledra or
Leucotheon, <span id="fr_2518"></span>[2519](#fn_2518) in Cyprus, was
the most eloquent man of his age, and was distinguished during the reign
of Constantius. I have read his *Commentary on the Song of Songs.* He is
said to have written many other works, none of which have come to our
hand.
