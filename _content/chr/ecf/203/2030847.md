<span id="vi.xii.iii.xlv-p1"></span> 44. In the end of your letter you
say: “I hope that you love peace.” To this I will answer in a few words:
If you desire peace, lay down your arms. I can be at peace with one who
shews kindness; I do not fear one who threatens me. Let us be at one in
faith, and peace will follow immediately.
