<span id="v.iv.lxii-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iv.lxii-p2.1"></span>Victorinus, <span
id="fr_2678"></span>[2679](#fn_2678) </span> a rhetorician of
Marseilles, wrote to his son Etherius, a commentary *On Genesis,*
commenting, that is, from the beginning of the book to the death of the
patriarch Abraham, and published four <span
id="fr_2679"></span>[2680](#fn_2679) books in verse, words which have a
savour of piety indeed, but, in that he was a man busied with secular
literature and quite untrained in the Divine Scriptures, they are of
slight weight, so far as ideas are concerned.

<span id="v.iv.lxii-p5"></span>He died in the reign of Theodosius and
Valentinianus.
