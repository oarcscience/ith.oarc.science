<span id="v.iii.cxxxvi-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.cxxxvi-p2.1"></span>Sophronius</span>, <span
id="fr_2566"></span>[2567](#fn_2566) a man of superlative learning,
wrote while yet a lad, *In praise of Bethlehem* and recently a notable
volume, *On the overthrow of Serapis,* and also to Eustachius, *On
virginity,* and a *Life of Hilarion the monk.* He rendered short works
of mine into Greek in a very finished style, the *Psalter* also, and the
Prophets, which I translated from Hebrew into Latin.
