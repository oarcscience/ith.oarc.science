<span id="v.iii.xxviii-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.xxviii-p2.1"></span>Apollinaris</span>, <span
id="fr_2425"></span>[2426](#fn_2425) bishop of Hierapolis in Asia,
flourished in the reign of Marcus Antoninus Verus, to whom he addressed
a notable volume in behalf of the faith of the Christians. There are
extant also five other books of his *Against the Nations,* two *On
truth* and*Against the Cataphrygians* written at the time when Montanus
was making a beginning with Prisca and Maximilla.
