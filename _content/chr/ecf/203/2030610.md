id="v.iv.xci-p2.1"></span>Gennadius <span
id="fr_2732"></span>[2733](#fn_2732) </span> a Patriarch <span
id="fr_2733"></span>[2734](#fn_2733) of the church of Constantinople, a
man brilliant in speech and of strong genius, was so richly equipped by
his reading of the ancients that he was able to expound the prophet
Daniel entire commenting on every word.

<span id="v.iv.xci-p5"></span>He composed also many Homilies. He died
while the elder Leo was Emperor.
