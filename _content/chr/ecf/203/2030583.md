id="fr_2681"></span>[2682](#fn_2681) </span> the presbyter Jerome’s best
pupil, published a *Commentary on Job,* written in an unaffected style.
I have read his *Familiar letters,* exceedingly witty, exhorting the
endurance of poverty and sufferings. He died in the reign of Martianus
and Avitus.
