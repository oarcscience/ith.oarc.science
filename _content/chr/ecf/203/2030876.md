<span id="vi.xiii.xxviii"></span>

<span id="vi.xiii.xxviii-p1"></span> 27. Next it is written that “He
gave up the ghost.” <span id="fr_3343"></span>[3344](#fn_3343) This also
had been foretold, by the Prophet, who says, addressing the Father in
the Person of the Son, “Into Thy hands I commend My Spirit.” <span
id="fr_3344"></span>[3345](#fn_3344) He is related also to have been
buried, and a great stone laid at the door of the sepulchre. Hear what
the word of prophecy foretold by Jeremiah concerning this also, “They
have cut off my life in the pit, and have laid a stone upon Me.” <span
id="fr_3345"></span>[3346](#fn_3345) These words of the Prophet point
most plainly to His burial. Here are yet others, “The righteous hath
been taken away from beholding iniquity, and his place is in
peace.” <span id="fr_3346"></span>[3347](#fn_3346) And in another place,
“I will give the malignant for his burial;” <span
id="fr_3347"></span>[3348](#fn_3347) and yet once more, “He hath lain
down and slept as a lion, and as a lion’s whelp; who shall rouse Him
up?” <span id="fr_3348"></span>[3349](#fn_3348)
