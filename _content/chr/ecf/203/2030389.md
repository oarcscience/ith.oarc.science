<span id="v.iii.viii-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.viii-p2.1"></span>Barnabas <span
id="fr_2363"></span>[2364](#fn_2363) </span> the Cyprian, also called
Joseph the Levite, ordained apostle to the Gentiles with Paul, wrote one
*Epistle,* valuable for the edification of the church, which is reckoned
among the apocryphal writings. He afterwards separated from Paul on
account of John, a disciple also called Mark, <span
id="fr_2364"></span>[2365](#fn_2364) none the less exercised the work
laid upon him of preaching the Gospel.
