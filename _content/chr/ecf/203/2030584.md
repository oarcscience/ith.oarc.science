<span id="v.iv.lxv-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iv.lxv-p2.1"></span>Eucherius, <span
id="fr_2682"></span>[2683](#fn_2682) </span> bishop of the church at
Lyons, wrote to his relative Valerianus, *On contempt for the world and
worldly philosophy,* a single letter, written in a style which shows
sound learning and reasoning. He wrote also to his sons, Salonius and
Veranius, afterward bishops, a discussion *On certain obscure passages
of Holy Scriptures,* and besides, revising and condensing certain works
of Saint Cassianus, he compressed them into one volume, and wrote other
works suited to ecclesiastical or monastic pursuits. He died in the
reign of Valentinianus and Martianus.
