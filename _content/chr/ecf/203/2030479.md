<span id="v.iii.xcviii-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.xcviii-p2.1"></span>Eusebius</span>, <span
id="fr_2522"></span>[2523](#fn_2522) a native of Sardinia, at first a
lector at Rome and afterwards bishop of Vercelli, sent by the emperor
Constantius to Scythopolis, and afterwards to Cappadocia, on account of
his confession of the faith, returned to the church under the emperor
Julian and published the *Commentaries of Eusebius of Cæsarea on the
Psalms,* which he had translated from Greek into Latin, and died during
the reign of Valentian and Valens.
