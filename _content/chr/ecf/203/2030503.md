<span id="v.iii.cxxii-p2"></span><span class="c12"><span
id="v.iii.cxxii-p2.1"></span>Eunomius</span>, <span
id="fr_2552"></span>[2553](#fn_2552) bishop of Cyzicus and member of the
Arian party, fell into such open blasphemy in his heresy, as to proclaim
publicly what the others concealed. He is said to be still living in
Cappadocia, and to write much against the church. Replies to him have
been made by Apollinarius, Didymus, Basil of Cæsarea, Gregory Nazianzen,
and Gregory of Nyssa.
