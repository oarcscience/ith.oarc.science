edition by the monks of St. Maur, of 1705.

<span id="ii.iv-p54"></span>By far the best edition of the Epistolæ yet
attempted is that begun by Ewald, who died after editing pt. I., bks.
I.–IV.  The work is being continued on the same scale by L. M. Hartmann.
