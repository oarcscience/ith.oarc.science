### <span id="vii.ii.ii-p0.1"></span>II. <span id="fr_1739"></span>[1740](#fn_1739)

<span id="vii.ii.ii-p2"></span><span id="vii.ii.ii-p2.1"></span>\[The
early Christians\] *called those who practised a godly
guilelessness,* <span id="fr_1740"></span>[1741](#fn_1740) *children*,
\[as is stated by Papias in the first book of the Lord’s Expositions,
and by Clemens Alexandrinus in his *Pædagogue*.\]
