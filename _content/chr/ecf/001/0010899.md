### <span id="ix.viii.xxiv-p0.1"></span>XXIV.

<span id="ix.viii.xxiv-p1"></span><span
id="ix.viii.xxiv-p1.1"></span>“God is not as a man.” <span
id="fr_4835"></span>[4836](#fn_4835) He thus shows that all men are
indeed guilty of falsehood, inasmuch as they change from one thing to
another (<span class="Greek"><span
id="ix.viii.xxiv-p2.2"></span>μεταφερόμενοι</span>); but such is not the
case with God, for He always continues true, perfecting whatever He
wishes.
