### <span id="iv.ii.viii-p0.1"></span>Chapter VIII.—Persevere in hope and patience.

<span id="iv.ii.viii-p1"></span><span id="iv.ii.viii-p1.1"></span>Let us
then continually persevere in our hope, and the earnest of our
righteousness, which is Jesus Christ, “who bore our sins in His own body
on the tree,” <span id="fr_383"></span>[384](#fn_383) “who did no sin,
neither was guile found in His mouth,” <span
id="fr_384"></span>[385](#fn_384) but endured all things for us, that we
might live in Him. <span id="fr_385"></span>[386](#fn_385) <span
id="iv.ii.viii-p4.2"></span><span id="iv.ii.viii-p4.3"></span>Let us
then be imitators of His patience; and if we suffer <span
id="fr_386"></span>[387](#fn_386) for His name’s sake, let us glorify
Him. <span id="fr_387"></span>[388](#fn_387) For He has set us this
example <span id="fr_388"></span>[389](#fn_388) in Himself, and we have
believed that such is the case.
