### <span id="v.xiv.iv-p0.1"></span>Chapter IV.—Continuation.

<span id="v.xiv.iv-p1"></span>And \[know ye, moreover\], that He who was
born of a woman was the Son of God, and He that was crucified was “the
first-born of every creature,” <span
id="fr_1189"></span>[1190](#fn_1189) and God the Word, who also created
all things. For says the apostle, “There is one God, the Father, of whom
are all things; and <span id="page_108">p. 108</span> <span
id="v.xiv.iv-Page_108"></span> one Lord Jesus Christ, by whom are all
things.” <span id="fr_1190"></span>[1191](#fn_1190) And again, “For
there is one God, and one Mediator between God and man, the man Christ
Jesus;” <span id="fr_1191"></span>[1192](#fn_1191) and, “By Him were all
things created that are in heaven, and on earth, visible and invisible;
and He is before all things, and by Him all things consist.” <span
id="fr_1192"></span>[1193](#fn_1192)
