### <span id="ii.ii.xlvi-p0.1"></span>Chapter XLVI.—Let us cleave to the righteous: your strife is pernicious.

<span id="ii.ii.xlvi-p1"></span>Such examples, therefore, brethren, it
is right that we should follow; <span id="fr_200"></span>[201](#fn_200)
since it is written, “Cleave to the holy, for those that cleave to them
shall \[themselves\] be made holy.” <span
id="fr_201"></span>[202](#fn_201) And again, in another place, \[the
Scripture\] saith, “With a harmless man thou shalt prove <span
id="fr_202"></span>[203](#fn_202) thyself harmless, and with an elect
man thou shalt be elect, and with a perverse man thou shalt show <span
id="fr_203"></span>[204](#fn_203) thyself perverse.” <span
id="fr_204"></span>[205](#fn_204) Let us cleave, therefore, to the
innocent and righteous, since these are the elect of God. <span
id="ii.ii.xlvi-p6.2"></span><span id="ii.ii.xlvi-p6.3"></span><span
id="ii.ii.xlvi-p6.4"></span>Why are there strifes, and tumults, and
divisions, and schisms, and wars <span id="fr_205"></span>[206](#fn_205)
among you? Have we not \[all\] one God and one Christ? Is there not one
Spirit of grace poured out upon us? And have we not one calling in
Christ? <span id="fr_206"></span>[207](#fn_206) Why do we divide and
tear to pieces the members of Christ, and raise up strife against our
own body, and have reached such a height of madness as to forget that
“we are members one of another?” <span id="fr_207"></span>[208](#fn_207)
Remember the words of our Lord Jesus Christ, how <span
id="fr_208"></span>[209](#fn_208) He said, “Woe to that man \[by
whom <span id="fr_209"></span>[210](#fn_209) offences come\]! It were
better for him that he had never been born, than that he should cast a
stumbling-block before one of my elect. Yea, it were better for him that
a millstone should be <span id="page_18">p. 18</span> <span
id="ii.ii.xlvi-Page_18"></span> hung about \[his neck\], and he should
be sunk in the depths of the sea, than that he should cast a
stumbling-block before one of my little ones.” <span
id="fr_210"></span>[211](#fn_210) Your schism has subverted \[the faith
of\] many, has discouraged many, has given rise to doubt in many, and
has caused grief to us all. And still your sedition continueth.
