### <span id="v.xv.vi-p0.1"></span>Chapter VI.—Renewed cautions.

<span id="v.xv.vi-p1"></span>These things I write to you, thou new
olive-tree of Christ, not that I am aware you hold any such opinions,
but that I may put you on your guard, as a father does his children.
Beware, therefore, of those that hasten to work mischief, those “enemies
of the cross of Christ, whose end is destruction, whose glory is in
their shame.” <span id="fr_1239"></span>[1240](#fn_1239) Beware of those
“dumb dogs,” those trailing serpents, those scaly <span
id="fr_1240"></span>[1241](#fn_1240) dragons, those asps, and basilisks,
and scorpions. For these are subtle wolves, <span
id="fr_1241"></span>[1242](#fn_1241) and apes that mimic the appearance
of men.
