## <span id="v.iii-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Magnesians Shorter and Longer Versions

<span id="v.iii-p1"></span><span id="v.iii-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.iii-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
\[Church\] blessed in the grace of God the Father, in Jesus Christ our
Saviour, in whom I salute the Church which is at Magnesia, near the
Mæander, and wish it abundance of happiness in God the Father, and in
Jesus Christ.*

<span id="v.iii-p2"></span><span id="v.iii-p2.1"></span><span
id="v.iii-p2.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
\[Church\] blessed in the grace of God the Father, in Jesus Christ our
Saviour, in whom I salute the Church which is at Magnesia, near the
Mæander, and wish it abundance of happiness in God the Father, and in
Jesus Christ, our Lord, in whom may you have abundance of happiness.*
