The Epistle of Polycarp to the Philippians 337

Polycarp, and the presbyters 338 with him, to the Church of God sojourning at Philippi: Mercy to you, and peace from God Almighty, and from the Lord Jesus Christ, our Saviour, be multiplied.

Footnotes

33:337

The title of this Epistle in most of the mss. is, “The Epistle of St. Polycarp, Bishop of Smyrna, and holy martyr, to the Philippians.”

33:338

Or, “Polycarp, and those who with him are presbyters.”
