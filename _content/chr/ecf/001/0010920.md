### <span id="ix.viii.xlv-p0.1"></span>XLV.

<span id="ix.viii.xlv-p1"></span>“And <span
id="fr_4888"></span>[4889](#fn_4888) Balaam the son of Beor they slew
with the sword.” <span id="fr_4889"></span>[4890](#fn_4889) For,
speaking no longer by the Spirit of God, but setting up another law of
fornication contrary to the law of God, <span
id="fr_4890"></span>[4891](#fn_4890) this man shall no longer be
reckoned as a prophet, but as a soothsayer. For, as he did not continue
in the commandment of God, he received the just reward of his evil
devices.
