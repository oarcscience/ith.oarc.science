## <span id="v.xvii-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to the Philippians

<span id="v.xvii-p1"></span><span id="v.xvii-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.xvii-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the
Church of God which is at Philippi, which has obtained mercy in faith,
and patience, and love unfeigned: Mercy and peace from God the Father,
and the Lord Jesus Christ, “who is the Saviour of all men, specially of
them that believe.”* <span id="fr_1295"></span>[1296](#fn_1295)
