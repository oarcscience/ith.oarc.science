## <span id="v.xvi-p0.1"></span>The Epistle of Ignatius to Hero, a Deacon of Antioch

<span id="v.xvi-p1"></span><span id="v.xvi-p1.1"></span><span
id="v.xvi-p1.2"></span>*Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to
Hero, the deacon of Christ, and the servant of God, a man honoured by
God, and most dearly loved as well as esteemed, who carries Christ and
the Spirit within him, and who is mine own son in faith and love: Grace,
mercy, and peace from Almighty God, and from Christ Jesus our Lord, His
only-begotten Son, “who gave Himself for our sins, that He might deliver
us from the present evil world,”* <span
id="fr_1264"></span>[1265](#fn_1264) *and preserve us unto His heavenly
kingdom.*
