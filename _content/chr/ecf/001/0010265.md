### <span id="v.xv.ii-p0.1"></span>Chapter II.—The true doctrine respecting God and Christ.

<span id="v.xv.ii-p1"></span>For Moses, the faithful servant of God,
when he said, “The Lord thy God is one Lord,” <span
id="fr_1217"></span>[1218](#fn_1217) and thus proclaimed that there was
only one God, did yet forthwith confess also our Lord when he said, “The
Lord rained upon Sodom and Gomorrah fire and brimstone from the
Lord.” <span id="fr_1218"></span>[1219](#fn_1218) And again, “And
God <span id="fr_1219"></span>[1220](#fn_1219) said, Let Us make man
after our image: and so God made man, after the image of God made He
him.” <span id="fr_1220"></span>[1221](#fn_1220) And further “In the
image of God made He man.” <span id="fr_1221"></span>[1222](#fn_1221)
And that \[the Son of God\] was to be made man \[Moses shows when\] he
says, “A prophet shall the Lord raise up unto you of your brethren, like
unto me.” <span id="fr_1222"></span>[1223](#fn_1222)
