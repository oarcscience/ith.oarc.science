### <span id="ix.viii.xxv-p0.1"></span>XXV.

<span id="ix.viii.xxv-p1"></span><span id="ix.viii.xxv-p1.1"></span>“To
inflict vengeance from the Lord on Midian.” <span
id="fr_4836"></span>[4837](#fn_4836) For this man (Balaam), when he
speaks no longer in the Spirit of God, but contrary to God’s law, by
setting up a different law with regard to fornication, <span
id="fr_4837"></span>[4838](#fn_4837) is certainly not then to be counted
as a prophet, but as a soothsayer. For he who did not keep to the
commandment of God, received the just recompense of his own evil
devices. <span id="fr_4838"></span>[4839](#fn_4838)
