### <span id="ix.viii.xl-p0.1"></span>XL.

<span id="ix.viii.xl-p1"></span><span
id="ix.viii.xl-p1.1"></span>“And <span
id="fr_4879"></span>[4880](#fn_4879) he found the jaw-bone of an
ass.” <span id="fr_4880"></span>[4881](#fn_4880) It is to be observed
that, after \[Samson had committed\] fornication, the holy Scripture no
longer speaks of the things happily accomplished by him in connection
with the formula, “The Spirit of the Lord came upon him.” <span
id="fr_4881"></span>[4882](#fn_4881) For thus, according to the holy
apostle, the sin of fornication is perpetrated against the body, as
involving also sin against the temple of God. <span
id="fr_4882"></span>[4883](#fn_4882)
