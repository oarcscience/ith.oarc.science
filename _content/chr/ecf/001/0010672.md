### <span id="viii.ix.v-p0.1"></span>V.

<span id="viii.ix.v-p1"></span>And Justin of Neapolis, a man who was not
far separated from the apostles either in age or excellence, says that
that which is mortal is inherited, but that which is immortal inherits;
and that the flesh indeed dies, but the kingdom of heaven lives.—*From*
<span class="upper"><span id="viii.ix.v-p1.1"></span>Methodius</span>
*On the Resurrection, in Photius*.
