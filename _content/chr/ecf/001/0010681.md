### <span id="viii.ix.xiv-p0.1"></span>XIV.

<span id="viii.ix.xiv-p1"></span>Neither shall light ever be darkness as
long as light exists, nor shall the truth of the things pertaining to us
be controverted. For truth is that than which nothing is more powerful.
Every one who might speak the truth, and speaks it not, shall be judged
by God.—*Manuscript and works of* <span class="upper"><span
id="viii.ix.xiv-p1.1"></span>John of Damascus</span>.
