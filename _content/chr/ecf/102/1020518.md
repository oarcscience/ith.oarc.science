<span id="iv.XVIII-p2"></span> ————————————

<span id="iv.XVIII-p3"></span>Argument—Augustin traces the parallel
courses of the earthly and heavenly cities from the time of Abraham to
the end of the world; and alludes to the oracles regarding Christ, both
those uttered by the Sibyls, and those of the sacred prophets who wrote
after the foundation of Rome, Hosea, Amos, Isaiah, Micah, and their
successors.
