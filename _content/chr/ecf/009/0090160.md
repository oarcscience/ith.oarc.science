<span id="xii.iv.lxv-p1"></span> <span class="c2"><span
id="xii.iv.lxv-p1.1"></span>Chapter LXV.—The Corinthians are Exhorted
Speedily to Send Back Word that Peace Has Been Restored.  The
Benediction.</span>

<span id="xii.iv.lxv-p2"></span>Send back speedily to us in peace and
with joy these our messengers to you:  Claudius Ephebus and Valerius
Bito, with Fortunatus; that they may the sooner announce to us the peace
and harmony we so earnestly desire and long for \[among you\], and that
we may the more quickly rejoice over the good order re-established among
you.  The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you, and with all
everywhere that are the called of God through Him, by whom be to Him
glory, honour, power, majesty, and eternal dominion, <span
id="fr_4325"></span>[4326](#fn_4325) from everlasting to
everlasting. <span id="fr_4326"></span>[4327](#fn_4326)   Amen.
