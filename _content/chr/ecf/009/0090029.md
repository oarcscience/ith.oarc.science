<span id="iv.iii.xx-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xx-p1.1"></span>Section XX.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_1401"></span>[1402](#fn_1401) And Jesus knew within himself that
his disciples were murmuring because of \[2\] that, and he said unto
them, Doth this trouble you?   <span
id="fr_1402"></span>[1403](#fn_1402) *What* if ye should see the Son
\[3\] of man then ascend to the place where he was of old?   <span
id="fr_1403"></span>[1404](#fn_1403) It is the spirit that quickeneth,
and the body profiteth nothing:  the words <span
id="fr_1404"></span>[1405](#fn_1404) that I speak unto you are spirit
\[4\] and life.   <span id="fr_1405"></span>[1406](#fn_1405) But there
are some of you that do not believe.  And Jesus knew beforehand who they
were who should <span id="fr_1406"></span>[1407](#fn_1406) not believe,
and who it was that should betray \[5\] him.   <span
id="fr_1407"></span>[1408](#fn_1407) And he said unto them, Therefore I
said unto you, No man can come unto me, if that hath not been given him
by the Father.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p10"></span>\[6\] \[Arabic, p. 78\]  <span
id="fr_1408"></span>[1409](#fn_1408) And because of this word many of
his disciples turned back and walked \[7\] not with him.   <span
id="fr_1409"></span>[1410](#fn_1409) And Jesus said unto the twelve, Do
ye haply also wish to \[8\] go away?   <span
id="fr_1410"></span>[1411](#fn_1410) Simon Cephas answered and said, My
Lord, to whom shall we go? thou \[9\] hast the words of eternal life. 
 <span id="fr_1411"></span>[1412](#fn_1411) And we have believed and
known that thou art the \[10\] Messiah, the Son of the living God. 
 <span id="fr_1412"></span>[1413](#fn_1412) Jesus said unto them, Did
not I choose you, \[11\] ye company of the twelve, and of you one is a
devil?   <span id="fr_1413"></span>[1414](#fn_1413) He said that because
of Judas the son of Simon Iscariot; for he, being of the twelve, was
purposed to <span id="fr_1414"></span>[1415](#fn_1414) betray him.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p18"></span>\[12\]  <span
id="fr_1415"></span>[1416](#fn_1415) And while he was speaking, one of
the Pharisees came asking of him that he \[13\] would eat with him:  and
he went in, and reclined *to meat*.   <span
id="fr_1416"></span>[1417](#fn_1416) And that Pharisee, when \[14\] he
saw it, <span id="fr_1417"></span>[1418](#fn_1417) marvelled that he had
not first cleansed himself before his eating.   <span
id="fr_1418"></span>[1419](#fn_1418) Jesus said unto him, Now do ye
Pharisees wash the outside of the cup and the dish, and ye think that ye
are cleansed; but your inside is full of injustice and wickedness.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p23"></span>\[15, 16\]  <span
id="fr_1419"></span>[1420](#fn_1419) Ye of little mind, did not he that
made the outside make the inside?   <span
id="fr_1420"></span>[1421](#fn_1420) Now give what ye have <span
id="fr_1421"></span>[1422](#fn_1421) in alms, and everything *shall be*
clean unto you.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p27"></span>\[17, 18\]  <span
id="fr_1422"></span>[1423](#fn_1422) And there came to him Pharisees and
scribes, come from Jerusalem.   <span
id="fr_1423"></span>[1424](#fn_1423) And when they saw some of his
disciples eating bread while they had not washed their hands, \[19\]
they found fault.   <span id="fr_1424"></span>[1425](#fn_1424) For all
of the Jews and the Pharisees, if they wash not their \[20\] hands
thoroughly, eat not; for they held <span
id="fr_1425"></span>[1426](#fn_1425) to the ordinance <span
id="fr_1426"></span>[1427](#fn_1426) of the elders.   <span
id="fr_1427"></span>[1428](#fn_1427) And they ate not what was bought
from the market, except they washed it; and many other things did they
keep of what they had received, such as the washing of cups, and \[21\]
measures, and vessels of brass, and couches.   <span
id="fr_1428"></span>[1429](#fn_1428) And scribes <span
id="fr_1429"></span>[1430](#fn_1429) and Pharisees asked him, <span
id="page_75">p. 75</span> <span id="iv.iii.xx-Page_75"></span>\[Arabic,
p. 79\] Why do thy disciples not walk according to the ordinances of the
elders, but \[22\] eat bread without washing their hands?   <span
id="fr_1430"></span>[1431](#fn_1430) Jesus answered and said unto them,
Why do ye also overstep the command of God by reason of your ordinance? 
\[23\]  <span id="fr_1431"></span>[1432](#fn_1431) God said, Honour thy
father and thy mother; and, Whosoever revileth his father and \[24\] his
mother shall surely die.   <span id="fr_1432"></span>[1433](#fn_1432)
But ye say, If a man say to his father or to his mother, \[25\] What
thou receivest <span id="fr_1433"></span>[1434](#fn_1433) from me is an
offering,— <span id="fr_1434"></span>[1435](#fn_1434) and ye <span
id="fr_1435"></span>[1436](#fn_1435) suffer him not to do anything
\[26\] for his father or his mother;  <span
id="fr_1436"></span>[1437](#fn_1436) and ye <span
id="fr_1437"></span>[1438](#fn_1437) make void and reject the word of
God by reason of the ordinance that ye have ordained and commanded, such
as the washing \[27\] of cups and measures, and what resembles that ye
do much.   <span id="fr_1438"></span>[1439](#fn_1438) And ye forsook
\[28\] the command of God, and held to the ordinance of men.   <span
id="fr_1439"></span>[1440](#fn_1439) Do <span
id="fr_1440"></span>[1441](#fn_1440) ye well to wrong <span
id="fr_1441"></span>[1442](#fn_1441) \[29\] the command of God in order
that ye may establish your ordinance?   <span
id="fr_1442"></span>[1443](#fn_1442) Ye hypocrites, well did Isaiah the
prophet prophesy concerning you, and say,

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p49"></span>\[30\]  <span
id="fr_1443"></span>[1444](#fn_1443) This people honoureth me with
its <span id="fr_1444"></span>[1445](#fn_1444) lips;

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p52"></span>But their heart is very far from me.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p53"></span>\[31\]  <span
id="fr_1445"></span>[1446](#fn_1445) But in vain do they fear me,

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p55"></span>In that they teach the commands of men.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p56"></span>\[32\]  <span
id="fr_1446"></span>[1447](#fn_1446) And Jesus called all the multitude,
and said unto them, Hear me, all of you, and \[33\] understand:   <span
id="fr_1447"></span>[1448](#fn_1447) nothing without the man, which then
enters him, is able to defile him; \[34\] but what goeth out of him,
that it is which defileth the man.   <span
id="fr_1448"></span>[1449](#fn_1448) He that hath ears \[35\] that hear,
let him hear.   <span id="fr_1449"></span>[1450](#fn_1449) Then his
disciples drew near, and said unto him, Knowest \[36\] thou that the
Pharisees which heard this word were angry?   <span
id="fr_1450"></span>[1451](#fn_1450) He answered and said unto them,
Every plant which my Father which is in heaven planted not shall be
\[37\] \[Arabic, p. 80\] uprooted.   <span
id="fr_1451"></span>[1452](#fn_1451) Let them alone; for they are blind
leading blind.  And if the blind lead <span
id="fr_1452"></span>[1453](#fn_1452) the blind, both of them shall fall
into a hollow.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p64"></span>\[38\]  <span
id="fr_1453"></span>[1454](#fn_1453) And when Jesus entered the house
from the multitude, Simon Cephas asked him, \[39\] and said unto him, My
Lord, explain to us that parable.   <span
id="fr_1454"></span>[1455](#fn_1454) He said unto them, Do ye also thus
not understand?  Know ye not that everything that entereth into the
\[40\] man from without cannot defile him;  <span
id="fr_1455"></span>[1456](#fn_1455) because it entereth not into his
heart; it entereth into his stomach only, and thence is cast forth in
the cleansing which maketh \[41\] clean all the food? <span
id="fr_1456"></span>[1457](#fn_1456)    <span
id="fr_1457"></span>[1458](#fn_1457) The thing which goeth forth from
the mouth of the man proceedeth \[42\] from his heart, and it is that
which defileth the man.   <span id="fr_1458"></span>[1459](#fn_1458)
From within <span id="fr_1459"></span>[1460](#fn_1459) the \[43\] heart
of men proceed evil thoughts,  <span
id="fr_1460"></span>[1461](#fn_1460) fornication, adultery, theft, false
witness, murder, injustice, wickedness, deceit, stupidity, evil eye,
calumny, pride, foolishness:  \[44\]  <span
id="fr_1461"></span>[1462](#fn_1461) these evils all of them from within
proceed from the heart, and they are *the things* \[45\] which defile
the man:   <span id="fr_1462"></span>[1463](#fn_1462) but if a man eat
while he washeth not his hands, he is not defiled.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p75"></span>\[46\]  <span
id="fr_1463"></span>[1464](#fn_1463) And Jesus went out thence, and came
to the borders of Tyre and Sidon.  And he entered into a certain house,
and desired that no man should know it; <span
id="fr_1464"></span>[1465](#fn_1464) and \[47\] he could not be hid. 
 <span id="fr_1465"></span>[1466](#fn_1465) But straightway a
Canaanitish woman, whose daughter had an \[48, 49\] unclean spirit,
heard of him.   <span id="fr_1466"></span>[1467](#fn_1466) And that
woman was a Gentile of Emesa of Syria.   <span
id="fr_1467"></span>[1468](#fn_1467) And she came out after him, crying
out, and saying, Have mercy upon me, my Lord, *thou* \[50\] son of
David; for my daughter is seized in an evil way by Satan. <span
id="fr_1468"></span>[1469](#fn_1468)    <span
id="fr_1469"></span>[1470](#fn_1469) And he answered \[Arabic, p. 81\]
her not a word.  And his disciples came and besought him, and said, Send
\[51\] her away:  for she crieth after us.   <span
id="fr_1470"></span>[1471](#fn_1470) He answered and said unto them, I
was \[52\] not sent except to the sheep that are gone astray of the
house of Israel.   <span id="fr_1471"></span>[1472](#fn_1471) But she
came and worshipped him, and said, My Lord, help me, have mercy upon me.

<span id="iv.iii.xx-p85"></span>\[53\]  <span
id="fr_1472"></span>[1473](#fn_1472) Jesus said unto her, It is not
seemly that the children’s bread should be taken and \[54\] thrown to
the dogs.   <span id="fr_1473"></span>[1474](#fn_1473) But she said,
Yea, my Lord:  the dogs also eat of the crumbs \[55\] that fall from
their masters’ tables, and live.   <span
id="fr_1474"></span>[1475](#fn_1474) Then said Jesus unto her, O woman,
\[56\] great is thy faith:  it shall be unto thee as thou hast desired. 
 <span id="fr_1475"></span>[1476](#fn_1475) Go then *thy way;* and
\[57\] because of this word, the devil is gone out of thy daughter. 
 <span id="fr_1476"></span>[1477](#fn_1476) And her daughter was \[58\]
healed in that hour.   <span id="fr_1477"></span>[1478](#fn_1477) And
that woman went away to her house, and found her daughter laid upon the
bed, and the devil gone out of her.
