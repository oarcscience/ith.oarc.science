id="iv.iii.xlv-p1.1"></span>Section XLV.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p2"></span>\[1, 2\] \[Arabic, p. 170\]  <span
id="fr_3085"></span>[3086](#fn_3085) And one of his disciples was
sitting <span id="fr_3086"></span>[3087](#fn_3086) in his bosom, *he*
whom Jesus loved.   <span id="fr_3087"></span>[3088](#fn_3087) To him
Simon Cephas beckoned, that he should ask him who this *was*, concerning
\[3\] whom he spake.   <span id="fr_3088"></span>[3089](#fn_3088) And
that disciple leaned <span id="fr_3089"></span>[3090](#fn_3089) on
Jesus’ breast, and said unto him, \[4\] My Lord, who is this?   <span
id="fr_3090"></span>[3091](#fn_3090) Jesus answered and said, He to whom
I shall dip bread, and give it.  And Jesus dipped bread, and gave to
Judas, the son of Simon Iscariot.  \[5\]  <span
id="fr_3091"></span>[3092](#fn_3091) And after the bread, Satan entered
him.  And Jesus said unto him, What thou \[6\] desirest to do, hasten
the doing of it.   <span id="fr_3092"></span>[3093](#fn_3092) And no man
of them that sat knew why he \[7\] said this unto him.   <span
id="fr_3093"></span>[3094](#fn_3093) And some of them thought, because
Judas had the box, that he was bidding him buy what would be needed for
the feast; or, that he might pay \[8\] something to the poor.   <span
id="fr_3094"></span>[3095](#fn_3094) Judas the betrayer answered and
said, Can it be I, my \[9\] Master?  Jesus said unto him, Thou hast
said.   <span id="fr_3095"></span>[3096](#fn_3095) And Judas took the
bread straightway, and went forth without:  and it was still night.

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p14"></span>\[10\]  <span
id="fr_3096"></span>[3097](#fn_3096) And Jesus said, Now is the Son of
man being glorified, <span id="fr_3097"></span>[3098](#fn_3097) and God
is being glorified <span id="fr_3098"></span>[3099](#fn_3098) \[11\] in
him;  <span id="fr_3099"></span>[3100](#fn_3099) and if God is glorified
in him, God also will glorify him in him, and straightway will glorify
him.

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p19"></span>\[12\]  <span
id="fr_3100"></span>[3101](#fn_3100) And while they were eating, Jesus
took bread, and blessed, and divided;  <span
id="fr_3101"></span>[3102](#fn_3101) and he <span id="page_113">p.
113</span> <span id="iv.iii.xlv-Page_113"></span>\[13\] gave to his
disciples, and said unto them, Take and eat; this is my body.   <span
id="fr_3102"></span>[3103](#fn_3102) And he \[Arabic, p. 171\] took a
cup, and gave thanks, and blessed, and gave them,  <span
id="fr_3103"></span>[3104](#fn_3103) and said, Take \[14, 15\] and drink
of it, all of you.   <span id="fr_3104"></span>[3105](#fn_3104) And they
drank of it, all of them.   <span id="fr_3105"></span>[3106](#fn_3105)
And he said unto them,  <span id="fr_3106"></span>[3107](#fn_3106) This
is my blood, the new covenant, that is shed for many for the \[16\]
forgiveness of sins.   <span id="fr_3107"></span>[3108](#fn_3107) I say
unto you, I shall not drink henceforth of this, the juice of the vine,
until the day in which I drink <span
id="fr_3108"></span>[3109](#fn_3108) with you new *wine* in the kingdom
of \[17\] God.   <span id="fr_3109"></span>[3110](#fn_3109) And thus do
ye in remembrance of me.   <span id="fr_3110"></span>[3111](#fn_3110)
And Jesus said unto Simon, Simon, \[18\] behold, Satan asketh that he
may sift you like wheat:   <span id="fr_3111"></span>[3112](#fn_3111)
but I entreat <span id="fr_3112"></span>[3113](#fn_3112) for thee, that
thou lose not thy faith: <span id="fr_3113"></span>[3114](#fn_3113)  
and do thou, at some time, turn <span
id="fr_3114"></span>[3115](#fn_3114) and strengthen thy brethren.

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p35"></span>\[19\]  <span
id="fr_3115"></span>[3116](#fn_3115) My children, another little *while*
am I with you.  And ye shall seek me:  and as \[20\] I said unto the
Jews, Whither I go, ye cannot come; I say unto you now also.   <span
id="fr_3116"></span>[3117](#fn_3116) A new commandment I give you, that
ye may love one another; and as I have loved \[21\] you, so shall ye
also love one another.   <span id="fr_3117"></span>[3118](#fn_3117) By
this shall every man know that ye are \[22\] my disciples, if ye have
love one to another.   <span id="fr_3118"></span>[3119](#fn_3118) Simon
Cephas said unto him, Our Lord, whither goest thou?  Jesus answered and
said unto him, Whither I go, thou canst not now follow me; but later
thou shalt come.

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p40"></span>\[23\]  <span
id="fr_3119"></span>[3120](#fn_3119) Then said Jesus unto them, Ye all
shall desert <span id="fr_3120"></span>[3121](#fn_3120) me this
night: <span id="fr_3121"></span>[3122](#fn_3121)   it is written, I
\[24\] will smite the shepherd, and the sheep of the flock shall be
scattered.   <span id="fr_3122"></span>[3123](#fn_3122) But after my
\[25\] rising, I shall go before you into Galilee.   <span
id="fr_3123"></span>[3124](#fn_3123) Simon Cephas answered and said unto
\[26\] him, My Lord, if every man desert thee, I shall at no time desert
thee.   <span id="fr_3124"></span>[3125](#fn_3124) I am with thee ready
for imprisonment and for death.   <span
id="fr_3125"></span>[3126](#fn_3125) And my life will I give up for
thee.  \[27\] \[Arabic, p. 172\]  <span
id="fr_3126"></span>[3127](#fn_3126) Jesus said unto him, Wilt thou give
up thy life for me?   <span id="fr_3127"></span>[3128](#fn_3127) Verily,
verily, I say unto thee, Thou shalt to-day, during this night, before
the cock crow \[28\] twice, three times deny me, that thou knowest me
not.   <span id="fr_3128"></span>[3129](#fn_3128) But Cephas said the
more, <span id="fr_3129"></span>[3130](#fn_3129) Even if it lead
to <span id="fr_3130"></span>[3131](#fn_3130) death with thee, I shall
not deny thee, my Lord.  And in like manner said all the disciples also.

<span id="iv.iii.xlv-p53"></span>\[29\]  <span
id="fr_3131"></span>[3132](#fn_3131) Then Jesus said unto them, Let not
your hearts be troubled: <span id="fr_3132"></span>[3133](#fn_3132)  
believe in God, \[30\] and believe in me.   <span
id="fr_3133"></span>[3134](#fn_3133) The stations <span
id="fr_3134"></span>[3135](#fn_3134) in my Father’s house are many, else
I should \[31\] have told <span id="fr_3135"></span>[3136](#fn_3135)
you.  I <span id="fr_3136"></span>[3137](#fn_3136) go to prepare for you
a place.   <span id="fr_3137"></span>[3138](#fn_3137) And if I go *to*
prepare for you a place, I shall return again, and take you unto me: 
and so where I am, there ye \[32, 33\] shall be also.   <span
id="fr_3138"></span>[3139](#fn_3138) And the place that I go ye
know, <span id="fr_3139"></span>[3140](#fn_3139) and the way ye
know. <span id="fr_3140"></span>[3141](#fn_3140)    <span
id="fr_3141"></span>[3142](#fn_3141) Thomas said unto him, Our Lord, we
know not whither thou goest; and how is the way for \[34\] us to the
knowledge of that? <span id="fr_3142"></span>[3143](#fn_3142)    <span
id="fr_3143"></span>[3144](#fn_3143) Jesus said unto him, I am the way,
and the truth, \[35\] and the life:  and no man cometh unto my Father,
but through me.   <span id="fr_3144"></span>[3145](#fn_3144) And if ye
had known me, ye should have known my Father:  and from henceforth ye
know <span id="fr_3145"></span>[3146](#fn_3145) him, \[36\] and have
seen him.   <span id="fr_3146"></span>[3147](#fn_3146) Philip <span
id="fr_3147"></span>[3148](#fn_3147) said unto him, Our Lord, shew us
the Father, and it sufficeth \[37\] us.   <span
id="fr_3148"></span>[3149](#fn_3148) Jesus said unto him, Have I been
all this time with you, and dost thou not know <span
id="fr_3149"></span>[3150](#fn_3149) me, Philip? <span
id="fr_3150"></span>[3151](#fn_3150) whosoever hath seen me hath seen
the Father; how then sayest \[38\] thou, Shew us the Father?   <span
id="fr_3151"></span>[3152](#fn_3151) Believest thou not that I am in my
Father, and my Father in me? and the saying that I say, I say not of
myself:  but my Father who dwelleth in \[39\] me, he doeth these deeds. 
 <span id="fr_3152"></span>[3153](#fn_3152) Believe that I am in my
Father, and my Father in me:  \[40\] \[Arabic, p. 173\] or else believe
for the sake of the deeds.   <span id="fr_3153"></span>[3154](#fn_3153)
Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever believeth in me, the deeds
that I do shall he do also; and \[41\] more than that shall he do:  I go
unto the Father.   <span id="fr_3154"></span>[3155](#fn_3154) And what
ye shall ask in my \[42\] name, I shall do unto you, that the Father may
be glorified in his Son.   <span id="fr_3155"></span>[3156](#fn_3155)
And if ye \[43, 44\] ask me <span id="fr_3156"></span>[3157](#fn_3156)
in my name, I will do *it*.   <span id="fr_3157"></span>[3158](#fn_3157)
If ye love me, keep my commandments.   <span
id="fr_3158"></span>[3159](#fn_3158) And I will entreat of my Father,
and he will send unto you another Paraclete, that he \[45\] may be with
you for ever, *even* the Spirit of truth:   <span
id="fr_3159"></span>[3160](#fn_3159) whom the world cannot receive; for
it hath not seen him, nor known him:  but ye know him; for he hath
dwelt <span id="fr_3160"></span>[3161](#fn_3160) <span id="page_114">p.
114</span> <span id="iv.iii.xlv-Page_114"></span>\[46\] with you, and is
in you.   <span id="fr_3161"></span>[3162](#fn_3161) I will not leave
you orphans:  I will come unto you.  \[47\]  <span
id="fr_3162"></span>[3163](#fn_3162) Another little *while*, and the
world seeth me not; but ye see me that I live, and ye \[48\] shall live
also.   <span id="fr_3163"></span>[3164](#fn_3163) And in that day ye
shall know that I am in my Father, and ye in me, and I in you.
