<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xlii-p1.1"></span>Section XLII.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_2880"></span>[2881](#fn_2880) But when ye see Jerusalem with the
army compassing it about, then know that \[2\] its desolation is come
near.   <span id="fr_2881"></span>[2882](#fn_2881) Those then that are
in Judæa at that time shall flee to the mountain; and those that are
within her shall flee; and those that are in the \[3\] villages shall
not enter her.   <span id="fr_2882"></span>[2883](#fn_2882) For these
days are the days of vengeance, that all that \[4\] is written may be
fulfilled.   <span id="fr_2883"></span>[2884](#fn_2883) And when ye see
the unclean sign of desolation, <span
id="fr_2884"></span>[2885](#fn_2884) spoken of in Daniel the prophet,
standing in the pure place, he that readeth shall understand, \[5, 6\]
 <span id="fr_2885"></span>[2886](#fn_2885) and then he that is in Judæa
shall flee in to the mountain:   <span
id="fr_2886"></span>[2887](#fn_2886) and let him that is on the \[7\]
roof not go down, nor enter in to take anything from his house:   <span
id="fr_2887"></span>[2888](#fn_2887) and let him that is in \[8\]
\[Arabic, p. 159\] the field not turn behind him to take his garment. 
 <span id="fr_2888"></span>[2889](#fn_2888) Woe to them that are with
child and to them that give suck in those days! there shall be great
\[9\] distress in the land, and wrath against this nation.   <span
id="fr_2889"></span>[2890](#fn_2889) And they shall fall on the edge of
the sword, <span id="fr_2890"></span>[2891](#fn_2890) and shall be taken
captive to every land:  and Jerusalem shall be trodden down of the
nations, until the times of the nations be ended.

<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p14"></span>\[10\]  <span
id="fr_2891"></span>[2892](#fn_2891) Then if any man say unto you, The
Messiah is here; or, Lo, he is there; believe \[11\] him not:   <span
id="fr_2892"></span>[2893](#fn_2892) there shall rise then false
Messiahs and prophets of lying, and shall do signs and wonders, in order
that they may lead astray even the elect also, if they \[12\] be able. 
 <span id="fr_2893"></span>[2894](#fn_2893) But as for you, beware:  for
I have acquainted you with everything \[13\] beforehand.   <span
id="fr_2894"></span>[2895](#fn_2894) If then they say unto you, Lo, he
is in the desert; go not out, lest ye \[14\] be taken:  and if they say
unto you, Lo, he is in the chamber; believe not.   <span
id="fr_2895"></span>[2896](#fn_2895) And as the lightning appeareth from
the east, and is seen unto the west; so shall be the \[15\] coming of
the Son of man.   <span id="fr_2896"></span>[2897](#fn_2896) But first
he must suffer much and be rejected by this \[16\] generation. <span
id="fr_2897"></span>[2898](#fn_2897)    <span
id="fr_2898"></span>[2899](#fn_2898) Pray therefore that your flight be
not in winter, nor on a sabbath:  \[17\]  <span
id="fr_2899"></span>[2900](#fn_2899) there shall be then great
tribulation, <span id="fr_2900"></span>[2901](#fn_2900) the like of
which there hath not been from the \[18\] beginning of the world till
now, nor shall be.   <span id="fr_2901"></span>[2902](#fn_2901) And
except the Lord had shortened those days, no flesh would have lived: 
but because of the elect, whom he elected, \[19\] he shortened those
days.   <span id="fr_2902"></span>[2903](#fn_2902) And there shall be
signs in the sun and the moon and the stars; and upon the earth
affliction <span id="fr_2903"></span>[2904](#fn_2903) of the nations,
and rubbing of hands for the confusion <span
id="fr_2904"></span>[2905](#fn_2904) \[20\] \[Arabic, p. 160\] of the
noise of the sea, and an earthquake:   <span
id="fr_2905"></span>[2906](#fn_2905) the souls of men shall \[21\] go
forth from fear of that which is to come upon the earth.   <span
id="fr_2906"></span>[2907](#fn_2906) And in those days, straightway
after the distress of those days, the sun shall become dark, and the
moon shall not shew its light, and the stars shall fall from heaven, and
the powers \[22\] of heaven shall be convulsed:   <span
id="fr_2907"></span>[2908](#fn_2907) and then shall appear the sign of
the Son of man in heaven:  and at that time all the tribes of the earth
shall wail, and look unto the Son \[23\] of man coming on the clouds of
heaven with power and much glory.   <span
id="fr_2908"></span>[2909](#fn_2908) And he shall send his angels with
the great trumpet, and they shall gather his elect from the four \[24\]
winds, from one end of heaven to the other. <span
id="fr_2909"></span>[2910](#fn_2909)    <span
id="fr_2910"></span>[2911](#fn_2910) But when these things begin to be,
be of good cheer, and lift up your heads; for your salvation <span
id="fr_2911"></span>[2912](#fn_2911) is come near.

<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p36"></span>\[25\]  <span
id="fr_2912"></span>[2913](#fn_2912) Learn the example of the fig tree: 
when it letteth down its branches, <span
id="fr_2913"></span>[2914](#fn_2913) and putteth \[26\] forth its
leaves, ye know that the summer is come;  <span
id="fr_2914"></span>[2915](#fn_2914) so ye also, when ye see these
things begun to be, know ye that the kingdom of God hath arrived at the
\[27\] door.   <span id="fr_2915"></span>[2916](#fn_2915) Verily I say
unto you, This generation shall not pass away, until all these \[28\]
*things* shall be.   <span id="fr_2916"></span>[2917](#fn_2916) Heaven
and earth shall pass away, but my sayings shall not pass away.

<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p42"></span>\[29\]  <span
id="fr_2917"></span>[2918](#fn_2917) Take heed to yourselves, that your
hearts become not heavy with inordinate desire, <span
id="fr_2918"></span>[2919](#fn_2918) and drunkenness, and the care of
the world at any time, and that day come <span id="page_109">p.
109</span> <span id="iv.iii.xlii-Page_109"></span>\[30\] upon you
suddenly:   <span id="fr_2919"></span>[2920](#fn_2919) for it is as a
shock that shocks all the inhabitants that are on the \[31\] face of the
whole earth.   <span id="fr_2920"></span>[2921](#fn_2920) Watch at all
times, and pray, that ye may be worthy to escape \[Arabic, p. 161\] from
all the things that are to be, and that ye may stand before the Son of
\[32\] man.   <span id="fr_2921"></span>[2922](#fn_2921) Of that day and
of that hour hath no man learned, not even the angels \[33\] of heaven,
neither the Son, but the Father.   <span
id="fr_2922"></span>[2923](#fn_2922) See ye, and watch and pray:  for ye
know \[34\] not when that time *will be*.   <span
id="fr_2923"></span>[2924](#fn_2923) *It is* as a man, who journeyed,
and left his house, and gave his authority to his servants, and
appointed every man to his work, and \[35\] charged the porter to be
wakeful.   <span id="fr_2924"></span>[2925](#fn_2924) Be wakeful
then: <span id="fr_2925"></span>[2926](#fn_2925)   since ye know not
when the lord of the house cometh, in the evening, or in the middle of
the night, or when the \[36\] cock croweth, or in the morning;  <span
id="fr_2926"></span>[2927](#fn_2926) lest he come unexpectedly, and find
you sleeping.  \[37\]  <span id="fr_2927"></span>[2928](#fn_2927) The
thing that I say unto you, unto all of you do I say it, Be ye watchful.

<span id="iv.iii.xlii-p54"></span>\[38\]  <span
id="fr_2928"></span>[2929](#fn_2928) For as it was in the days of Noah,
so shall the coming of the Son of man be.  \[39\]  <span
id="fr_2929"></span>[2930](#fn_2929) As they were before the flood
eating and drinking, and taking wives, and giving \[40\] wives to men,
 <span id="fr_2930"></span>[2931](#fn_2930) until the day in which Noah
entered into the ark, and they perceived not till the flood came, and
took them all; so shall the coming of the Son of man \[41\] be.   <span
id="fr_2931"></span>[2932](#fn_2931) And as it was in the days of Lot;
they were eating and drinking, and selling \[42\] and buying, and
planting and building,  <span id="fr_2932"></span>[2933](#fn_2932) on
the day in which Lot went out from Sodom, and the Lord rained fire and
brimstone from heaven, and destroyed them \[43, 44\] all:   <span
id="fr_2933"></span>[2934](#fn_2933) so shall it be in the day in which
the Son of man is revealed. <span id="fr_2934"></span>[2935](#fn_2934)  
 <span id="fr_2935"></span>[2936](#fn_2935) And in that day, whosoever
is on the roof, and his garments <span
id="fr_2936"></span>[2937](#fn_2936) in the house, let him not go down
to \[45\] take them:  and he that is in the field shall not turn behind
him.   <span id="fr_2937"></span>[2938](#fn_2937) Remember Lot’s \[46\]
wife.   <span id="fr_2938"></span>[2939](#fn_2938) Whosoever shall
desire to save his life shall destroy it:  but whosoever shall \[47\]
destroy his life shall save it.   <span
id="fr_2939"></span>[2940](#fn_2939) Verily I say unto you, In that
night there shall be two on \[48\] \[Arabic, p. 162\] one bed; one shall
be taken, and another left.   <span id="fr_2940"></span>[2941](#fn_2940)
And two *women* shall be grinding \[49\] at one mill; one shall be
taken, and another left.   <span id="fr_2941"></span>[2942](#fn_2941)
And two shall be in the \[50\] field; one shall be taken, and another
left.   <span id="fr_2942"></span>[2943](#fn_2942) They answered and
said unto him, To what place, our Lord?  He said unto them, Where the
body is, there will the eagles \[51, 52\] gather.   <span
id="fr_2943"></span>[2944](#fn_2943) Be attentive now:  for ye know not
at what hour your Lord cometh.   <span
id="fr_2944"></span>[2945](#fn_2944) Know this:  if the master of the
house had known in what watch the thief would come, he would have been
attentive, and would not make it possible that his house should be
\[53\] broken through.   <span id="fr_2945"></span>[2946](#fn_2945)
Therefore be ye also ready:  for in the hour that ye think not the Son
of man cometh.
