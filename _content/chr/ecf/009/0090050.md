<span id="iv.iii.xli-p1"></span> <span class="c6"><span
id="iv.iii.xli-p1.1"></span>Section XLI.</span>

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p2"></span>\[1\]  <span
id="fr_2800"></span>[2801](#fn_2800) Therefore, behold, I, the wisdom of
God, am sending unto you prophets, and apostles, and wise men, and
scribes:  and some of them ye shall slay and crucify; and some of them
ye shall beat in your synagogues, and persecute <span
id="fr_2801"></span>[2802](#fn_2801) from city to \[2\] city:   <span
id="fr_2802"></span>[2803](#fn_2802) that there may come on you all the
blood of the righteous that hath been poured upon the ground <span
id="fr_2803"></span>[2804](#fn_2803) from the blood of Abel the pure to
the blood of Zachariah the son of Barachiah, whom ye slew between the
temple <span id="fr_2804"></span>[2805](#fn_2804) and the altar.  \[3\]
 <span id="fr_2805"></span>[2806](#fn_2805) Verily I say unto you, All
these *things* shall come upon this generation. <span
id="fr_2806"></span>[2807](#fn_2806)

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p10"></span>\[4\] \[Arabic, p. 155\]  <span
id="fr_2807"></span>[2808](#fn_2807) O Jerusalem, Jerusalem, slayer of
the prophets, and stoner of them that are sent unto her! how many times
did I wish to gather thy children, as \[5\] a hen gathereth her chickens
under her wings, and ye would not!   <span
id="fr_2808"></span>[2809](#fn_2808) Your house shall \[6\] be left over
you desolate.   <span id="fr_2809"></span>[2810](#fn_2809) Verily I say
unto you, Ye shall not see me henceforth, till ye shall say Blessed is
he that cometh in the name of the Lord.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p14"></span>\[7\]  <span
id="fr_2810"></span>[2811](#fn_2810) And many of the rulers also
believed on him; but because of the Pharisees they \[8\] were not
confessing *him*, lest they be put <span
id="fr_2811"></span>[2812](#fn_2811) out of the synagogue:   <span
id="fr_2812"></span>[2813](#fn_2812) and they loved \[9\] the praise of
men more than the praising of God.   <span
id="fr_2813"></span>[2814](#fn_2813) And Jesus cried and said, \[10\]
Whosoever believeth in me, believeth not in me, but in him that sent
me.   <span id="fr_2814"></span>[2815](#fn_2814) And \[11\] whosoever
seeth me hath seen him that sent me.   <span
id="fr_2815"></span>[2816](#fn_2815) I am come a light <span
id="fr_2816"></span>[2817](#fn_2816) into the \[12\] world, and so every
one that believeth in me abideth not in the darkness.   <span
id="fr_2817"></span>[2818](#fn_2817) And whosoever heareth my sayings,
and keepeth them not, I judge him not:  for I came \[13\] not to judge
the world, but to give the world life. <span
id="fr_2818"></span>[2819](#fn_2818)   <span
id="fr_2819"></span>[2820](#fn_2819) Whosoever wrongeth <span
id="fr_2820"></span>[2821](#fn_2820) me, and receiveth not my sayings,
there is one that judgeth him:  the word that I spake, it \[14\] shall
judge him at the last day.   <span id="fr_2821"></span>[2822](#fn_2821)
I from myself did not speak:  but the Father which sent me, he hath
given me commandment, <span id="fr_2822"></span>[2823](#fn_2822) what I
should say, and what I \[15\] should speak; and I know that his
commandment <span id="fr_2823"></span>[2824](#fn_2823) is eternal life. 
 <span id="fr_2824"></span>[2825](#fn_2824) The things that I say now,
as my Father hath said unto me, *even* so I say.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p30"></span>\[16\]  <span
id="fr_2825"></span>[2826](#fn_2825) And when he said that unto them,
the scribes and Pharisees began their evil-doing, being angry with
*him*, and finding fault with his sayings, and harassing <span
id="fr_2826"></span>[2827](#fn_2826) him \[17\] in many things;  <span
id="fr_2827"></span>[2828](#fn_2827) seeking to catch something from his
mouth, that they might be able to calumniate him.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p34"></span>\[18\]  <span
id="fr_2828"></span>[2829](#fn_2828) And when there gathered together
myriads of great multitudes, which almost trode <span id="page_107">p.
107</span> <span id="iv.iii.xli-Page_107"></span>\[Arabic, p. 156\] one
upon another, Jesus began to say unto his disciples, Preserve yourselves
\[19\] from the leaven of the Pharisees, which is hypocrisy.   <span
id="fr_2829"></span>[2830](#fn_2829) For there is nothing \[20\]
concealed, that shall not be revealed:  nor hid, that shall not be
known.   <span id="fr_2830"></span>[2831](#fn_2830) Everything that ye
have said in the darkness shall be heard in the light; and what ye have
spoken secretly in the ears in the inner chambers shall be proclaimed on
the roofs.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p38"></span>\[21, 22\]  <span
id="fr_2831"></span>[2832](#fn_2831) This said Jesus, and he went and
hid himself from them.   <span id="fr_2832"></span>[2833](#fn_2832) But
notwithstanding \[23\] his having done all these signs before them, they
believed not in him:   <span id="fr_2833"></span>[2834](#fn_2833) that
the word of Isaiah the prophet might be fulfilled, who said,

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p42"></span>My Lord, who is he that hath believed
to hear us?

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p43"></span>And the arm of the Lord, to whom hath
it appeared?

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p44"></span>\[24\]  <span
id="fr_2834"></span>[2835](#fn_2834) And for this reason it is not
possible for them to believe, because Isaiah also said,

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p46"></span>\[25\]  <span
id="fr_2835"></span>[2836](#fn_2835) They have blinded their eyes, and
made dark their heart;

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p48"></span>That they may not see with their eyes,
and understand with their heart,

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p49"></span>And turn,

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p50"></span>So that I should heal them.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p51"></span>\[26\]  <span
id="fr_2836"></span>[2837](#fn_2836) This said Isaiah when he saw his
glory, and spake of him.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p53"></span>\[27\]  <span
id="fr_2837"></span>[2838](#fn_2837) And when Jesus went out of the
temple, certain of his disciples came forward \[28\] to shew <span
id="fr_2838"></span>[2839](#fn_2838) him the buildings of the temple,
 <span id="fr_2839"></span>[2840](#fn_2839) and its beauty and
greatness, and the strength of the stones that were laid in it, and the
elegance of its building, and that \[29\] it was adorned with noble
stones and beautiful colours.   <span
id="fr_2840"></span>[2841](#fn_2840) Jesus answered and said \[30\] unto
them, See ye these great buildings?  <span
id="fr_2841"></span>[2842](#fn_2841) verily I say unto you, Days will
come, when there shall not be left here a stone upon another, that shall
not be cast down.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p59"></span>\[31\]  <span
id="fr_2842"></span>[2843](#fn_2842) And two days before <span
id="fr_2843"></span>[2844](#fn_2843) the passover of unleavened bread,
the chief priests and \[32\] the scribes sought how they might take him
by deceit, <span id="fr_2844"></span>[2845](#fn_2844) and kill him: 
 <span id="fr_2845"></span>[2846](#fn_2845) and they said, It shall not
be at the feast, lest the people be agitated.

<span id="iv.iii.xli-p64"></span>\[33\]  <span
id="fr_2846"></span>[2847](#fn_2846) And when Jesus sat on the mount of
Olives opposite the temple, his disciples, Simon Cephas and James and
John and Andrew, came forward unto him, and said unto him \[34\] between
themselves and him,  <span id="fr_2847"></span>[2848](#fn_2847) Teacher,
tell us when that shall be, and what is the sign \[35\] \[Arabic, p.
157\] of thy coming and the end of the world.   <span
id="fr_2848"></span>[2849](#fn_2848) Jesus answered and said unto them,
Days will come, when ye shall long to see one of the days of the Son of
\[36, 37\]  <span id="fr_2849"></span>[2850](#fn_2849) man, and shall
not behold.   <span id="fr_2850"></span>[2851](#fn_2850) Take heed lest
any man lead you astray.  Many shall \[38\] come in my name, and say, I
am the Messiah;  <span id="fr_2851"></span>[2852](#fn_2851) and they
shall say, The time is come \[39\] near, and shall lead many astray:  go
not therefore after them.   <span id="fr_2852"></span>[2853](#fn_2852)
And when ye hear of wars and tidings of insurrections, see *to it*,
be <span id="fr_2853"></span>[2854](#fn_2853) not agitated:  for these
*things* must \[40\] first be; only the end is not yet come.   <span
id="fr_2854"></span>[2855](#fn_2854) Nation shall rise against nation,
and kingdom \[41\] against kingdom:   <span
id="fr_2855"></span>[2856](#fn_2855) and great earthquakes shall be in
one place and another, and there shall be famines and deaths and
agitations:  and there shall be fear and terror and great signs
that <span id="fr_2856"></span>[2857](#fn_2856) shall appear from
heaven, and there shall be great \[42, 43\] storms    <span
id="fr_2857"></span>[2858](#fn_2857) All these *things* are the
beginning of travail.   <span id="fr_2858"></span>[2859](#fn_2858) But
before all of that, they shall lay hands upon you, and persecute you,
and deliver you unto the synagogues \[44\] and into prisons, and bring
you before kings and judges for my name’s sake.   <span
id="fr_2859"></span>[2860](#fn_2859) And \[45\] that shall be unto you
for a witness.   <span id="fr_2860"></span>[2861](#fn_2860) But first
must my gospel be preached unto all \[46\] nations.   <span
id="fr_2861"></span>[2862](#fn_2861) And when they bring you into the
synagogues before the rulers and the authorities, be not anxious
beforehand how ye shall answer for yourselves, or what ye \[47, 48\]
shall say:   <span id="fr_2862"></span>[2863](#fn_2862) because it is
not ye that speak, but the Holy Spirit.   <span
id="fr_2863"></span>[2864](#fn_2863) Lay it to your heart, not \[49\]
\[Arabic, p. 158\] to be anxious before the time what ye shall say: 
 <span id="fr_2864"></span>[2865](#fn_2864) and I shall <span
id="fr_2865"></span>[2866](#fn_2865) give you understanding and
wisdom, <span id="fr_2866"></span>[2867](#fn_2866) which all your
adversaries shall not be able to gainsay.  \[50\]  <span
id="fr_2867"></span>[2868](#fn_2867) And then shall they deliver you
unto constraint, and shall kill you:  and ye shall be \[51\] hated of
all nations because of my name.   <span
id="fr_2868"></span>[2869](#fn_2868) And then shall many go
astray, <span id="fr_2869"></span>[2870](#fn_2869) and they \[52\] shall
hate one another, and deliver one another unto death.   <span
id="fr_2870"></span>[2871](#fn_2870) And your parents, and your
brethren, and your kinsfolk, and your friends shall deliver you up, and
shall \[53, 54\] slay some of you.   <span
id="fr_2871"></span>[2872](#fn_2871) But a lock of hair from your heads
shall not perish.   <span id="fr_2872"></span>[2873](#fn_2872) And by
\[55\] your patience ye shall gain <span
id="fr_2873"></span>[2874](#fn_2873) your souls.   <span
id="fr_2874"></span>[2875](#fn_2874) And many *men*, <span
id="fr_2875"></span>[2876](#fn_2875) false prophets, shall arise, \[56\]
and lead many astray.   <span id="fr_2876"></span>[2877](#fn_2876) And
because of the abounding of iniquity, the love of many <span
id="page_108">p. 108</span> <span id="iv.iii.xli-Page_108"></span>\[57\]
shall wax cold.   <span id="fr_2877"></span>[2878](#fn_2877) But he that
endureth to the end, the same shall be saved.   <span
id="fr_2878"></span>[2879](#fn_2878) And \[58\] this, the <span
id="fr_2879"></span>[2880](#fn_2879) gospel of the kingdom, shall be
preached in all the world for a testimony to all nations; and then shall
come the end of all.
