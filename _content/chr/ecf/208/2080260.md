<span id="ix.clxxxviii-p1"></span> <span class="c18"><span
id="ix.clxxxviii-p1.1"></span>Letter CLXXXVII.</span>

<span id="ix.clxxxviii-p2"></span>*Antipater to Basil*.

<span id="ix.clxxxviii-p3"></span>“<span class="c14"><span
id="ix.clxxxviii-p3.1"></span>Twice</span> cabbage is death,” says the
unkind proverb.  I, however, though I have called for it often, shall
die once.  Yes:  even though I had never called for it at all!  If you
do die anyhow, don’t fear to eat a delicious relish, unjustly reviled by
the proverb!
