<span id="ii.iii.iv-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="ii.iii.iv-p1.1"></span>Translations.</span>

<span id="ii.iii.iv-p2"></span>1.  *Bright’s Leo on the Incarnation*,
London, 1862 (2nd edn. enlarged, 1886, in which the Tome is translated),
consists of xviii. sermons translated and the Tome in Latin, with many
valuable notes.

<span id="ii.iii.iv-p3"></span>2.  *Reithmayr’s Bibliothek*  (1869)
contains a German translation.

<span id="ii.iii.iv-p4"></span>3.  *Dr. Neale’s History of the
Alexandrian Patriarchate* embodies a translation of the Tome.

<span id="ii.iii.iv-p5"></span>4.  Dr. Heurtley published a version of
the Tome in 1886.
