<span id="ii.iv.cxxxi-p4"></span> *Œconomi*(stewards) were officers
appointed to manage the revenues of each diocese under the bishops’
direction, when the bishops and their archdeacons had enough to do
otherwise:  cf. Bingham, *Antiq*., Bk. III. chap. xii.
