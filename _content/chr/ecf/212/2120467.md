id="iii.v.v.xiii-p1.1"></span>Epistle XXV.</span>

<span id="iii.v.v.xiii-p2"></span>To Severus, Bishop.

<span id="iii.v.v.xiii-p3"></span>Gregory to Severus, Bishop of Ficulum.

<span id="iii.v.v.xiii-p4"></span>The report that has been sent to us
has informed us of the death of the bishop John <span
id="fr_1595"></span>[1596](#fn_1595) .  Wherefore we solemnly delegate
to thy Fraternity the work of the visitation of the bereaved Church: 
which work it becomes thee so to execute that no one may presume to
interfere with respect to the promotions of the clergy, the revenues,
ornaments, ministrations, or whatever else belongs to the patrimony of
the same Church.  *According to custom*.
