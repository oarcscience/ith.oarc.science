#### <span id="ix.viii.iv-p0.1"></span>Sec. IV.—Enumeration Ordained by Apostles.

##### <span id="ix.viii.iv-p0.2"></span>Who Were They that the Holy Apostles Sent and Ordained?

<span id="ix.viii.iv-p1"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="ix.viii.iv-p1.1"></span>XLVI</span>. Now concerning those bishops
which have been ordained in our lifetime, we let you know that they are
these:—<span id="ix.viii.iv-p1.2"></span>James the bishop of Jerusalem,
the brother of our Lord; <span id="fr_3534"></span>[3535](#fn_3534) upon
whose death the second was <span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.4"></span>Simeon the
son of <span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.5"></span>Cleopas; after whom the third
was <span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.6"></span>Judas the son of <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.7"></span>James. Of Cæsarea of Palestine, the first
was <span id="page_478">p. 478</span> <span
id="ix.viii.iv-Page_478"></span><span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.8"></span>Zacchæus, who was once a publican; after
whom was <span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.9"></span>Cornelius, and the third
<span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.10"></span>Theophilus. Of Antioch, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.11"></span>Euodius, ordained by me <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.12"></span>Peter; and <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.13"></span>Ignatius by <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.14"></span>Paul. Of Alexandria, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.15"></span>Annianus was the first, ordained by <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.16"></span>Mark the evangelist; the second <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.17"></span>Avilius by <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p2.18"></span>Luke, who was also an evangelist. Of the
church of Rome, <span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.19"></span>Linus the son of
<span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.20"></span>Claudia was the first, ordained by
<span id="ix.viii.iv-p2.21"></span>Paul; <span
id="fr_3535"></span>[3536](#fn_3535) and <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p3.2"></span>Clemens, after <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p3.3"></span>Linus’ death, the second, ordained by me
<span id="ix.viii.iv-p3.4"></span>Peter. <span
id="fr_3536"></span>[3537](#fn_3536) Of Ephesus, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.2"></span>Timotheus, ordained by <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.3"></span>Paul; and <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.4"></span>John, by me <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.5"></span>John. Of Smyrna, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.6"></span>Aristo the first; after whom <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.7"></span>Stratæas the son of <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p4.8"></span>Lois; <span
id="fr_3537"></span>[3538](#fn_3537) and the third <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.2"></span>Aristo. Of Pergamus, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.3"></span>Gaius. Of Philadelphia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.4"></span>Demetrius, by me. Of Cenchrea, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.5"></span>Lucius, by <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.6"></span>Paul. Of Crete, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.7"></span>Titus. Of Athens, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.8"></span>Dionysius. Of Tripoli in Phœnicia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.9"></span>Marathones. Of Laodicea in Phrygia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p5.10"></span>Archippus. <span
id="fr_3538"></span>[3539](#fn_3538) Of Colossæ, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p6.2"></span>Philemon. <span
id="fr_3539"></span>[3540](#fn_3539) Of Borea in Macedonia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p7.2"></span>Onesimus, once the servant of <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p7.3"></span>Philemon. <span
id="fr_3540"></span>[3541](#fn_3540) Of the churches of Galatia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p8.2"></span>Crescens. <span
id="fr_3541"></span>[3542](#fn_3541) Of the parishes of Asia, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p9.2"></span>Aquila and <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p9.3"></span>Nicetas. Of the church of Æginæ, <span
id="ix.viii.iv-p9.4"></span>Crispus. These are the bishops who are
entrusted by us with the parishes in the Lord; whose doctrine keep ye
always in mind, and observe our words. And may the Lord be with you now,
and to endless ages, as Himself said to us when He was about to be taken
up to His own God and Father. For says He, “Lo, I am with you all the
days, until the end of the world. Amen.” <span
id="fr_3542"></span>[3543](#fn_3542)
