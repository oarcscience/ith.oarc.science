#### <span id="viii.iii.xiv-p0.1"></span>Chapter XIV. <span id="fr_2489"></span>[2490](#fn_2489) —Christian Assembly on the Lord’s Day.

<span id="viii.iii.xiv-p2"></span>1. But every Lord’s day <span
id="fr_2490"></span>[2491](#fn_2490) do ye gather yourselves together,
and break bread, and give thanksgiving after having confessed your
transgressions, <span id="fr_2491"></span>[2492](#fn_2491) that your
sacrifice may be pure. <span id="fr_2492"></span>[2493](#fn_2492) 2. But
let no one that is at variance <span
id="fr_2493"></span>[2494](#fn_2493) with his fellow come together with
you, until they be reconciled, that your sacrifice may not be profaned.
3. For this is that which was spoken by the Lord: In every place and
time offer to me a pure sacrifice; <span
id="fr_2494"></span>[2495](#fn_2494) for I am a great King, saith the
Lord, and my name is wonderful among the nations. <span
id="fr_2495"></span>[2496](#fn_2495)
