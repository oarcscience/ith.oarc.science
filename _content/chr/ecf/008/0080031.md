<span id="v.iii.vi-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.iii.vi-p1.1"></span>Chapter VI.—Divinity of Virginity.</span>

<span id="v.iii.vi-p2"></span>The womb of a holy virgin <span
id="fr_337"></span>[338](#fn_337) carried our Lord Jesus Christ, the Son
of God; and the body <span id="page_57">p. 57</span> <span
id="v.iii.vi-Page_57"></span>which our Lord wore, and in which He
carried on the conflict in this world, He put on from a holy virgin. 
From this, therefore, understand the greatness and dignity of
virginity.  Dost thou wish to be a Christian?  Imitate Christ in
everything.  John, the ambassador, he who came before our Lord, he “than
whom there was not a greater among those born of women,” <span
id="fr_338"></span>[339](#fn_338) the holy messenger of our Lord, was a
virgin.  Imitate, therefore, the ambassador of our Lord, and be his
follower <span id="fr_339"></span>[340](#fn_339) in every thing.  *That*
John, again, who “reclined on the bosom of our Lord, and whom He greatly
loved,” <span id="fr_340"></span>[341](#fn_340) —he, too, was a holy
person. <span id="fr_341"></span>[342](#fn_341)   For it was not without
reason that our Lord loved him.  Paul, also, and Barnabas, and Timothy,
with all the others, “whose names are written in the book of
life,” <span id="fr_342"></span>[343](#fn_342) —these, I say, all
cherished and loved sanctity, <span id="fr_343"></span>[344](#fn_343)
and ran in the contest, and finished their course without blemish, as
imitators of Christ, and as sons of the living God.  Moreover, also,
Elijah and Elisha, and many other holy men, we find to have lived a
holy <span id="fr_344"></span>[345](#fn_344) and spotless life.  If,
therefore, thou desirest to be like these, imitate them with all thy
power.  For the Scripture has said, “The elders who are among you,
honour; and, seeing their manner of life and conduct, imitate their
faith.” <span id="fr_345"></span>[346](#fn_345)   And again it saith,
“Imitate me, my brethren, as I *imitate* Christ.” <span
id="fr_346"></span>[347](#fn_346)
