id="viii.viii.iii.iv-p1.1"></span>III.</span>

<span id="viii.viii.iii.iv-p2"></span>Of the arraigned.

<span id="viii.viii.iii.iv-p3"></span>A person arraigned ought to plead
his cause before his judge; and an arraigned person may refuse to speak,
if he choose so, before one who is not his own proper judge; and
indulgence (*induciæ*) should be granted to the arraigned as often as
they appeal.
