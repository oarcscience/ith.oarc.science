<span id="vi.iv.x.x-p2"></span>And the people were afraid, and promised
to do whatever he should command.  “Let none <span id="page_270">p.
270</span> <span id="vi.iv.x.x-Page_270"></span>of you, then,” said
Peter, “either hold conversation with these sorcerers, or have any thing
