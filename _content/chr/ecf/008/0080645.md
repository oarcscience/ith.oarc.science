<span id="vi.iv.v.xii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vi.iv.v.xii-p1.1"></span>Chapter XII.—Doctrine of the True
Prophet.</span>

<span id="vi.iv.v.xii-p2"></span>“Hence, O beloved Clement, if you would
know the things pertaining to God, you have to learn them from Him
alone, because He alone knows the truth.  For if any one else knows
any<span id="page_231">p. 231</span> <span
id="vi.iv.v.xii-Page_231"></span>thing, he has received it from Him or
from His disciples.  And this is His doctrine and true proclamation,
that there is one God, whose work the world is; who being altogether
righteous, shall certainly at some time render to every one according to
his deeds.
