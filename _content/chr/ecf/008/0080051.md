<span id="v.iv.xii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.iv.xii-p1.1"></span>Chapter XII.—Solomon’s Infatuation Through
Women.</span>

<span id="v.iv.xii-p2"></span>Hast thou not read the history of Solomon,
the son of David, the man to whom God gave wisdom, and knowledge, and
largeness of mind, <span id="fr_483"></span>[484](#fn_483) and riches,
and much glory, beyond all men?  Yet this same man, through women, came
to ruin, <span id="fr_484"></span>[485](#fn_484) and departed from the
Lord.
