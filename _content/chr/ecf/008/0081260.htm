Chapter 7.

And a woman 1827 cried out from a distance, and said:  I had an issue of blood, and I touched the hem of his garment, and the issue of blood which I had had for twelve years was stopped. 1828   The Jews say:  we have a law, that a woman’s evidence is not to be received. 1829
