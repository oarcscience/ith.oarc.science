<span id="v.v.iv.iv-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.iv-p1.1"></span>Chapter 4.—</span>8.  <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.iv-p1.2"></span>Petilianus</span> said:  "For he who receives
faith from the faithless, receives not faith but guilt."

<span id="v.v.iv.iv-p2"></span>9.  <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.iv-p2.1"></span>Augustin</span> answered:  Christ is not
faithless, from whom the faithful man receives not guilt but faith.  For
he believeth on Him that justifieth the ungodly, that his faith may be
counted for righteousness. <span id="fr_1968"></span>[1969](#fn_1968)
