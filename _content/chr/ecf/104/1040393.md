<span id="v.iv.viii.xxxix-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.iv.viii.xxxix-p1.1"></span>Chapter 39.—</span>75.  Eugenius of
Ammedera <span id="fr_1732"></span>[1733](#fn_1732) said:  "I too
pronounce this same judgment, that heretics should be baptized." <span
id="fr_1733"></span>[1734](#fn_1733)

<span id="v.iv.viii.xxxix-p4"></span>76.  To him we answer:  But this is
not the judgment which the Church pronounces, to which also God has now
revealed in a plenary Council the point in which ye were then still
otherwise minded, <span id="fr_1734"></span>[1735](#fn_1734) but because
saving charity was in you, ye remained in unity.
