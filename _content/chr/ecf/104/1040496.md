<span id="v.v.iv.ix-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.ix-p1.1"></span>Chapter 9.—</span>21.  <span
class="c11"><span id="v.v.iv.ix-p1.2"></span>Petilianus</span> said: 
"Hemmed in, therefore, by these offenses, you cannot be a true bishop."

<span id="v.v.iv.ix-p2"></span>22.  <span class="c11"><span
id="v.v.iv.ix-p2.1"></span>Augustin</span> answered:  By what offenses? 
What have you shown?  What have you proved?  And if you have proved
charges on the part of I know not whom, what has that to do with the
seed of Abraham, in which all the nations of the earth are blessed?
