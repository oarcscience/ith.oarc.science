<span id="v.iv.ix.xlii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.iv.ix.xlii-p1.1"></span>Chapter 42.—</span>82.  Victor of
Octavus <span id="fr_1866"></span>[1867](#fn_1866) said:  "As ye
yourselves also know, I have not been long appointed a bishop, and
therefore I waited for the counsel of my seniors.  This therefore I
express as my opinion, that whosoever comes from heresy should
undoubtedly be baptized." <span id="fr_1867"></span>[1868](#fn_1867)

<span id="v.iv.ix.xlii-p4"></span>83.  What, therefore, has been
answered to those for whom he waited, may be taken as the answer also to
himself.
