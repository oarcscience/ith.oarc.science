<span id="v.iv.ix.xxxii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.iv.ix.xxxii-p1.1"></span>Chapter 32.—</span>62.  Another Victor of
Assuras <span id="fr_1843"></span>[1844](#fn_1843) said:  "It is
written, that ‘there is one God and one Christ, one Church and one
baptism.’ <span id="fr_1844"></span>[1845](#fn_1844)   How then can any
one baptize in a place where there is not either God, or Christ, or the
Church?" <span id="fr_1845"></span>[1846](#fn_1845)

<span id="v.iv.ix.xxxii-p5"></span>63.  How can any one baptize either
in that sand, where the Church is not, seeing that it is on the rock;
nor God and Christ, seeing that there is not there the temple of God and
Christ?
