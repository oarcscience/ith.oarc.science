<span id="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p1"></span> <span class="c11"><span
id="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p1.1"></span>Chapter 28.—</span>54.  Another
Saturninus of Abitini <span id="fr_1834"></span>[1835](#fn_1834) said: 
"If Antichrist can give to any one the grace of Christ, then can
heretics also baptize, who are called Antichrists." <span
id="fr_1835"></span>[1836](#fn_1835)

<span id="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p4"></span>55.  What if another were to say, If
a murderer can give the grace of Christ, then can they also baptize that
hate their brethren who are called murderers?  For certainly he would
seem in a way to speak the truth, and yet they can baptize; in like
manner, therefore, can the heretics as well.
