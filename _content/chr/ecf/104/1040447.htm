Chapter 48.—94.  Junius of Neapolis 1890 said:  "I do not depart from the judgment which we once pronounced, that we should baptize heretics on their coming to the Church." 1891

95.  Since this man has adduced no argument nor proof from the Scriptures, he need not detain us long.
