<span id="vii.iii.v-p2"></span>*This Preface is almost wholly a
repetition of the arguments adduced in the Preface to Genesis. It is
addressed to Chromatius, bishop of Aquileia, who took great interest in

class="c17"><span id="vii.iii.v-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span> 395.*
