<span id="v.CXX-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.CXX-p1.1"></span>Letter CXX. To Hedibia. <span
id="fr_3112"></span>[3113](#fn_3112) </span>

<span id="v.CXX-p3"></span>At the request of Hedibia, a lady of Gaul
much interested in the study of scripture, Jerome deals with the
following twelve questions. It will be noticed that several of them
belong to the historical criticism of our own day.

<span id="v.CXX-p4"></span>(1) How can anyone be perfect? and How ought
a widow without children to live to God?

<span id="v.CXX-p5"></span>(2) What is the meaning of [Matt. xxvi.
29](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#029)<span id="v.CXX-p5.1"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p6"></span>(3) How are the discrepancies in the
evangelical narratives to be accounted for? How can [Matt. xxviii.
1](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#001)<span id="v.CXX-p6.1"></span> be
reconciled with [Mark 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/mar016.htm#001),
[2](../../../bib/kjv/mar016.htm#002)<span id="v.CXX-p6.2"></span>.

<span id="v.CXX-p7"></span>(4) How can [Matt. xxviii.
9](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#009)<span id="v.CXX-p7.1"></span>
(Saturday evening) be reconciled with [John xx.
1-18](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#001)<span id="v.CXX-p7.2"></span>
(Sunday morning)?

<span id="v.CXX-p8"></span>(5) How can [Matt. xxviii.
9](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#009)<span id="v.CXX-p8.1"></span> be
reconciled with [John xx. 17](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#017)<span
id="v.CXX-p8.2"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p9"></span>(6) How was it that, if there was a guard of
soldiers at the sepulchre, Peter and John were allowed to go in freely?
([Matt. 27:66](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#066), [John
20:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#001)<span id="v.CXX-p9.1"></span>.)

<span id="v.CXX-p10"></span>(7) How is the statement of Matthew and Mark
that the apostles were ordered to go into Galilee to see Jesus there to
be reconciled with that of Luke and John who make Him appear to them in
Jerusalem?

<span id="v.CXX-p11"></span>(8) What is the meaning of [Matt.
27:50](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#050),
[51](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#051)<span id="v.CXX-p11.1"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p12"></span>(9) How is the statement of [John xx.
22](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#022)<span id="v.CXX-p12.1"></span> that
Jesus breathed on his apostles the Holy Ghost to be reconciled with that
of Luke (<span id="v.CXX-p12.2"></span>Luke xxiv. 49: Acts i. 4) that He
would send it to them after His ascension?

<span id="v.CXX-p13"></span>(10) What is the meaning of the passage,
[Rom. ix. 14-29](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#014)<span
id="v.CXX-p13.1"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p14"></span>(11) What is the meaning of [2 Cor. ii.
16](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#016)<span id="v.CXX-p14.1"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p15"></span>(12) What is the meaning of [1 Thes. v.
23](../../../bib/kjv/th1005.htm#023)<span id="v.CXX-p15.1"></span>?

<span id="v.CXX-p16"></span>The date of the letter is 406 or 407 <span
class="c17"><span id="v.CXX-p16.1"></span>a.d.</span>
