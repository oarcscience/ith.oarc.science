<span id="vii.iii.xix-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="vii.iii.xix-p1.1"></span>Books of Solomon.</span>

<span id="vii.iii.xix-p2"></span>*This is addressed to Paula and
Eustochium. Jerome describes the numerous emendations he has had to make
in what was then the received Latin text, but says he has not found the
same necessity in dealing with Ecclesiasticus. He adds, “All I aim at is
to give you a revised edition of the Canonical Scriptures, and to employ
my Latin on what is certain rather than on what is doubtful.”*
