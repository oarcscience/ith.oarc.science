id="vii.iii.xiv-p1.1"></span>The Twelve Minor Prophets.</span>

<span id="vii.iii.xiv-p2"></span>*This Preface, dedicated to Paula and
Eustochium in <span class="c17"><span
id="vii.iii.xiv-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span> 392, contains nothing of

a single book.*
