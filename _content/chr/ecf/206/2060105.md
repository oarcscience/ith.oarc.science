<span id="v.XCIV-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="v.XCIV-p1.1"></span>Letter XCIV. From Dionysius to
Theophilus.</span>

<span id="v.XCIV-p2"></span>In this letter (translated into Latin by
Jerome) Dionysius, bishop of Lydda, praises Theophilus for his signal
victories over Origenism and urges him to continue his efforts against
that heresy. Written in 400 <span class="c17"><span
id="v.XCIV-p2.1"></span>a.d.</span>
