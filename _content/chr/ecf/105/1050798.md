id="xviii.ii-p6.1"></span>Then</span> follow four books which I wrote to
Boniface, bishop of the Roman Church, in opposition to two letters of
the Pelagians, because when they came into his hands he had sent them to
me, finding in them a calumnious mention of my name. This work commences
on this wise: “I had indeed known you by the praise of your renowned
fame.”
