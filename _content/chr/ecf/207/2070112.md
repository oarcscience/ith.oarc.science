<span id="iv.iv.ii.iii-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="iv.iv.ii.iii-p1.1"></span>Ep. LXXIII.</span>

<span id="iv.iv.ii.iii-p2"></span>As to the subject of your letter,
these are my sentiments.  I am not angry at being overlooked, but I am
glad when I am honoured.  The one is my own desert, the other is a proof
of your respect.  Pray for me.  Excuse this short letter, for anyhow,
though it is short, it is longer than silence.
