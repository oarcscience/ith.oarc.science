<span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p1"></span> <span class="c1"><span
id="iv.iv.vii.v-p1.1"></span>Ep. CXXIII.</span>

<span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p2"></span>(To excuse himself for postponing his
acceptance of an invitation.)

<span id="iv.iv.vii.v-p3"></span>I reverence your presence, and I
delight in your company; although otherwise I counsel<span
id="page_473">p. 473</span> <span id="iv.iv.vii.v-Page_473"></span>led
myself to remain at home and philosophize in quiet, for I found this of
all courses the most profitable for myself.  And since the winds are
still somewhat rough, and my infirmity has not yet left me, I beg you to
bear with me patiently for a little while, and to join me in my prayers
for health; and as soon as the fit season comes I will attend upon your
requests.
