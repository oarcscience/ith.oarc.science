<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p1"></span> <span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p1.1"></span>Canon CI.  (Greek civ. *bis*)</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p2"></span>Of making peace between the Churches
of Rome and Alexandria.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p3"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p3.1"></span>It</span> seemed good that a letter be
written to the holy Pope Innocent concerning the dissension between the
Churches of Rome and Alexandria, so that each Church might keep peace
with the other as the Lord commanded.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p4"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p4.1"></span>Notes.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p5"></span><span class="sc"><span
id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p5.1"></span>Ancient Epitome of Canon CI.</span>

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p6"></span>It seemed good to write to Innocent
that the Roman and Alexandrian churches might be at peace between
themselves.

<span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p7"></span>This canon is Canon vij. of Carthage,
<span class="sc"><span id="xv.iv.iv.cii-p7.1"></span>a.d.</span> 407.
