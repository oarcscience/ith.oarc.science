<span id="xvii.xxv-p2"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xvii.xxv-p2.1"></span>The Prosphonesus of Theophilus, Archbishop of
Alexandria, When the Holy Epiphanies Happened to Fall on a Sunday. <span

<span id="xvii.xxv-p4"></span><span class="c4"><span
id="xvii.xxv-p4.1"></span>Canon I.</span>

<span id="xvii.xxv-p5"></span>Because the fast of Epiphany chances to
fall on a Lord’s day, let us take a few dates, and so break our fast,
and honour the Lord’s day, and shew our dislike of heresy, and yet not
wholly neglect the fast which should be observed on this day; eating no
more till our evening assembly at three afternoon.
