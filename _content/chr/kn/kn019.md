### 19. *How this Book came to be found*

   And <span class="small">DĔMÂTĔYÔS</span> (the Patriarch <span
class="small">TIMOTHEUS</span> (?) who sat from 511 to 517), the
Archbishop of <span class="small">RÔM</span> (i.e., <span
class="small">CONSTANTINOPLE</span>, <span
class="small">BYZANTIUM</span>), said, "I have found in the Church of
\[Saint\] <span class="small">SOPHIA</span> among the books and the
royal treasures a manuscript \[which stated\] that the whole kingdom of
the world \[belonged\] to the Emperor of <span class="small">RÔM</span>
and the Emperor of <span class="small">ETHIOPIA</span>."
