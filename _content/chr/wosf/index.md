This short anthology of the writings of St. Francis includes a wide
sampling of his writings. Of general interest here is the transcendent
[Canticle of the Sun](wosf22.htm#canticle), a prayer which lucidly
describes a universe alive with joy, and praises 'Brother Sun...Sister
Moon...Mother Earth'.

Also available at this site is the [Little Flowers of St.
Francis](../lff/index).
