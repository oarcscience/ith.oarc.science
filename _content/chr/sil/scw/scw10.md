## *X LOVE*

<span id="page_172">p. 172</span>

 

229 (V. 242)  
THE HIGHEST GOOD

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td>What is the highest Good? Much talk hath been hereof<br />
And high debate: I swear the highest Good is Love.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

 

230 (V. 241)  
EVERYTHING IS SUBJECT TO LOVE

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td>Love is the Lord of All. Even the Trinity<br />
Hath been in thrall to Love from all eternity.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

 

231 (V. 243)  
THE NATURE OF GOD

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td>Love is God's nature. He can do naught else. Wouldst thou<br />
Be God, then likewise love in every instant's Now.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

 

232 (V. 292)  
BEAUTY COMETH FROM LOVE

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td>Beauty is born of Love alone.<br />
 The Countenance Divine<br />
Hath all its Loveliness from Love—<br />
 Else it would cease to shine.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">

</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

 

234 (V. 320)  
THE SHORTEST WAY TO GOD

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />

<tr class="odd">

Who takes the Path of Knowledge, long<br />
 Must tarry on the road.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<table data-align="center">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">

 Hath instant audience:<br />
Long in the antechamber wait<br />
 Wit and intelligence.<br />
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

242 (V. 108)

<table data-align="center">

<col style="width: 100%" />

</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
