THE CANON OF THE MASS

<div align="center">

|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |     |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <span class="smallcaps">Te</span> ígitur, clementíssime Pater, per Jesum Christum Fílium tuum Dóminum nostrum súpplices rogámus ac pétimus (*osculatur* *altare*) uti accépta hábeas, et benedícas (*jungit* *manus, deinde signat ter super oblata*), hæc ![](img/cross.jpg) dona, hæc ![](img/cross.jpg) múnera, hæc ![](img/cross.jpg) sancta sacrifícia illibáta (*extensis* *manibus prosequitur*): in primis quæ tibi offérimus pro Ecclésia tua sancta cathólica: quam pacificáre, custodíre, adunáre, et régere dignéris toto orbe terrárum, una cum famulo tuo Papa nostro *N*. et Antístite nostro *N*. et ómnibus orthodóxis, atque cathólicæ et apostólicæ fídei cultóribus. |     | <span class="smallcaps">Wherefore</span>, O most merciful Father, we humbly pray and beseech thee, through Jesus Christ thy Son, our Lord (*he kisses the altar*), that thou <span id="page_465">p. 465</span> wouldst vouchsafe to receive and bless (*he joins his hands together, and then makes the sign of the cross thrice over the offerings*) these ![](img/cross.jpg) gifts, these ![](img/cross.jpg) offerings, this ![](img/cross.jpg) holy and unblemished sacrifice (he extends his hands and continues), which in the first place we offer thee for thy holy Catholic Church, that it may please thee to grant her peace: as also to protect, unite, and govern her throughout the world, together with thy servant *N*., our Pope *N*., our bishop, as also all orthodox believers who keep the catholic and apostolic faith. |

</div>

</div>

*Holding the host between the first fingers and thumbs of both hands, he
says the words of consecration, silently with clearness and attention,
over the host, and at the same time over all the other hosts, if several
are to be consecrated*.

<div align="center">

|---------------------------|-----|----------------------|
| Hoc est enim Corpus meum. |     | For this is my Body. |

adores the consecrated host. He rises, shows it to the people, puts it
on the corporal, and again adores. Then, uncovering the chalice, he
says:* 

<div align="center">

|                                   |     |                                      |

over it with his right*.

<div align="center">

|                                                                                                 |     |                                                                                                      |

*He utters the words of consecration over the chalice silently,
attentively, carefully, and without pausing, holding it slightly
raised*.

<div align="center">

*As soon as the words of consecration have been said, he puts the
chalice on the corporal, and says silently:* 

<div align="center">
