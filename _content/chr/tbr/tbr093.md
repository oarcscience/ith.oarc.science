# The Seven New Things

### 1. THE NEW HEAVEN.

Rev. 21:1.

"And I saw a **NEW HEAVEN**."

As the word **HEAVEN** is here, and in Gen. 1:1, in the singular number,
it will clarify matters to limit this creative act to our own planet,
rather than the whole of the sidereal heavens, or the starry spaces of
the Universe. By a new **heaven** then we are to understand a new
atmosphere for the new earth."
