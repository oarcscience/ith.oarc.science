Notes on the Book of the Revelation

by John Nelson Darby

[1876] (2nd ed.)

This is John Nelson Darby's commentary on the Book of Revelation. This is the text of the second edition (the first edition was published in London in 1839); Material added to the second edition is apparently marked by square brackets in the text. Note that this book originally had no chapters; the internal divisions into 'parts' is arbitrary. These divisions were added by the redactor to assist low-bandwidth users of this site.
