<span id="v.i.i-p2"></span><span id="v.i.i-p2.1"></span>[Exodus
20:8-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#008)<span id="v.i.i-p2.1"></span>

<span id="v.i.i-p3"></span>**8.** Remember the sabbath day, to keep it
holy.

<span id="v.i.i-p4"></span>**8.** Recordare diei Sabbathi, ut
sanctifices eum:

<span id="v.i.i-p5"></span>**9.** Six days shalt thou labour, and do all
thy work:

<span id="v.i.i-p6"></span>**9.** Sex diebus operaberis et facies
universum opus tuum.

<span id="v.i.i-p7"></span>**10.** But the seventh day *is* the sabbath
of the LORD thy God: *in it* thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy
son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy
cattle, nor thy stranger that *is* within thy gates:

<span id="v.i.i-p8"></span>**10.** Dies autem septimus Sabbathum Jehovae
Dei tui est. Non facies ullum opus, tu, et filius tuus, et filia tua,
servus tuus, et ancilla tua, et inquilinus tuus qui est in portis tuis:

<span id="v.i.i-p9"></span>**11.** For *in* six days the LORD made
heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them *is,* and rested the
seventh day: wherefore the LORD blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed
it.

<span id="v.i.i-p10"></span>**11.** Quoniam sex diebus fecit Jehova
coelum et terram, mare et quaecunque in illis sunt, et quievit die
septimo: propterea benedixit Jehova diem sabbathi, et sanctificavit eum.
