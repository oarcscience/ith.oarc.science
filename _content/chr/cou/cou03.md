<span id="an_iv-p3"></span> 

<span id="an_iv-p4"></span>Here Beginneth the Prayer on the Prologue

<span id="an_iv-p5"></span> 

<span id="an_iv-p6"></span>GOD, unto whom all hearts be open, and unto
whom all will speaketh, and unto whom no privy thing is hid. I beseech
Thee so for to cleanse the intent of mine heart with the unspeakable
gift of Thy grace, that I may perfectly love Thee, and worthily praise
Thee. Amen.
