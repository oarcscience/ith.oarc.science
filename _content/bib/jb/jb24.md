### XX.

<span class="small">*Precepts*.</span>

AND he said unto them, Is a candle brought to be put under a bushel, or
under a bed? and not to be set on a candlestick?

1 For there is nothing hid, which shall not be manifested; neither was
any thing kept secret, but that it should come abroad.

2 If any man have ears to hear, let him hear.
