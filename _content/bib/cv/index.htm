This page indexes resources at this site presenting critical views of the Bible.

Works of Spinoza   Spinoza was one of the first to critically examine the legend that Moses wrote the Pentateuch.

Prolegomena to the History of Ancient Israel   by Julius Wellhausen [1885]   The foundation of modern critical theories of the Bible.

Pagan Christs: Studies in Comparative Hierology   by John M. Robertson [1911]   The dark secret at the heart of global myths of a Savior.

Self-Contradictions of the Bible   by William Henry Burr [1860]   Is the Bible internally self-consistent?

The World's Sixteen Crucified Saviors   by Kersey Graves [1860]   What is the meaning of so many similar hero stories from around the world?

India in Primitive Christianity   by Arthur Lillie [1908].   What are the links between Buddhism and early Christianity?

Absurdities of the Bible   by Clarence Darrow

The Age of Reason   by Thomas Paine [1794]

The Woman's Bible   by Elizabeth Cady Stanton [1895]
