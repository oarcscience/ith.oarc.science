H5595  
ספה  
סָפָה ‎ sâphâh  
*saw-faw‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *scrape* (literally to *shave*; but
usually figuratively) together (that is, to *accumulate* or *increase*)
or away (that is, to *scatter*, *remove* or *ruin*; intransitively to
*perish*): - add, augment, consume, destroy, heap, join, perish, put.
