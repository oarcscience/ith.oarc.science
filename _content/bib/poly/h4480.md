*min,* *min-nee‘,* *min-nay‘*  
For [4482](h4482) properly a *part* of; hence (prepositionally), *from*
or *out* *of* in many senses: - above, after, among, at, because of, by
(reason of), from (among), in, X neither, X nor, (out) of, over, since,
X then, through, X whether, with.
