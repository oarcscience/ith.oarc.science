G4053
περισσός
perissos
per-is-sos‘
From 4012 (in the sense of beyond); superabundant (in quantity) or superior (in quality); by implication excessive; adverb (with 1537 violently; neuter (as noun) preeminence: - exceeding abundantly above, more abundantly, advantage, exceedingly, very highly, beyond measure, more, superfluous, vehement [-ly].
