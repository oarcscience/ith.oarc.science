H1768
דּי
דִּי ‎ dı̂y
dee
(Chaldee); apparently for 1668 that, used as relative, conjugational, and especially (with preposition) in adverbial phrases; also as a preposition of: - X as, but, for (-asmuch +), + now, of, seeing, than, that, therefore, until, + what (-soever), when, which, whom, whose.
