H6999
קטר
קָטַר ‎ qâṭar
kaw-tar‘
A primitive root (rather identical with 7000 through the idea of fumigation in a close place and perhaps thus driving out the occupants); to smoke, that is, turn into fragrance by fire (especially as an act of worship): - burn (incense, sacrifice) (upon), (altar for) incense, kindle, offer (incense, a sacrifice).
