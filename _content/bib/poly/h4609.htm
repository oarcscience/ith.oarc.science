H4609
מעלה
מַעֲלָה ‎ ma‛ălâh
mah-al-aw‘
Feminine of 4608 elevation, that is, the act (literally a journey to a higher place, figuratively a thought arising), or (concretely) the condition (literally a step or grade mark, figuratively a superiority of station); specifically a climactic progression (in certain Psalms): - things that come up, (high) degree, deal, go up, stair, step, story.
