### Isaiah 64

[KJV](../kjv/isa064)

1  Oh that<sup>[3863](h3863)</sup> thou wouldest
rend<sup>[7167](h7167)</sup> the heavens,<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> that
thou wouldest come down,<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> that the
mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> might flow
down<sup>[2151](h2151)</sup> at thy presence,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>

2  As when the melting<sup>[2003](h2003)</sup>
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> burneth,<sup>[6919](h6919)</sup> the
fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> causeth the waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup>
to boil,<sup>[1158](h1158)</sup> to make thy
name<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup> known<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> to thine
adversaries,<sup>[6862](h6862)</sup> that the
nations<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> may tremble<sup>[7264](h7264)</sup> at
thy presence!<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>

3  When thou didst<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> terrible
things<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> which we looked not
for,<sup>[6960](h6960)</sup> <sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> thou camest
down,<sup>[3381](h3381)</sup> the mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup>
flowed down<sup>[2151](h2151)</sup> at thy
presence.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>

4  For since the beginning of the world<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> men have not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
heard,<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> nor<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> perceived by
the ear,<sup>[238](h0238)</sup> neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> hath the
eye<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> seen,<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> O
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> beside<sup>[2108](h2108)</sup> thee, what he
hath prepared<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> for him that
waiteth<sup>[2442](h2442)</sup> for him.

5  Thou meetest<sup>[6293](h6293)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> him that
rejoiceth<sup>[7797](h7797)</sup> and worketh<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
righteousness,<sup>[6664](h6664)</sup> those that
remember<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> thee in thy
ways:<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> behold,<sup>[2005](h2005)</sup>
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art wroth;<sup>[7107](h7107)</sup> for we
have sinned:<sup>[2398](h2398)</sup> in those is
continuance,<sup>[5769](h5769)</sup> and we shall be
saved.<sup>[3467](h3467)</sup>

6  But we are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> as an
unclean<sup>[2931](h2931)</sup> thing, and all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
our righteousnesses<sup>[6666](h6666)</sup> are as
filthy<sup>[5708](h5708)</sup> rags;<sup>[899](h0899)</sup> and we
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> do fade<sup>[5034](h5034)</sup> as a
leaf;<sup>[5929](h5929)</sup> and our
iniquities,<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> like the
wind,<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> have taken us
away.<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup>

7  And there is none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> that
calleth<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> upon thy name,<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup>
that stirreth up himself<sup>[5782](h5782)</sup> to take
hold<sup>[2388](h2388)</sup> of thee: for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou
hast hid<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> thy face<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> us, and hast
consumed<sup>[4127](h4127)</sup> us, because<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> of
our iniquities.<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>

8  But now,<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art our father;<sup>[1](h0001)</sup>
we<sup>[587](h0587)</sup> are the clay,<sup>[2563](h2563)</sup> and
thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> our potter;<sup>[3335](h3335)</sup> and we
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> are the work<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of thy
hand.<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup>

9  Be not wroth<sup>[7107](h7107)</sup> <sup>[408](h0408)</sup> very
sore,<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> neither<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
remember<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> iniquity<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>
forever:<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup> behold,<sup>[2005](h2005)</sup>
see,<sup>[5027](h5027)</sup> we beseech thee,<sup>[4994](h4994)</sup> we
are all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> thy people.<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup>

10  Thy holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> cities<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup>
are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a wilderness,<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup>
Zion<sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a
wilderness,<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> Jerusalem<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> a
desolation.<sup>[8077](h8077)</sup>

11  Our holy<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> and our
beautiful<sup>[8597](h8597)</sup> house,<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup>
where<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> our fathers<sup>[1](h0001)</sup>
praised<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> thee, is<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> burned
up<sup>[8316](h8316)</sup> with fire:<sup>[784](h0784)</sup> and
all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> our pleasant things<sup>[4261](h4261)</sup>
are<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> laid waste.<sup>[2723](h2723)</sup>

12  Wilt thou refrain thyself<sup>[662](h0662)</sup>
for<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> these<sup>[428](h0428)</sup> things, O
LORD?<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> wilt thou hold thy
peace,<sup>[2814](h2814)</sup> and afflict<sup>[6031](h6031)</sup> us
very sore?<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> <sup>[3966](h3966)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/isa064)

1 ὡς κηρὸς ἀπὸ πυρὸς τήκεται καὶ κατακαύσει πῦρ τοὺς ὑπεναντίους καὶ
φανερὸν ἔσται τὸ ὄνομα κυρίου ἐν τοῖς ὑπεναντίοις ἀπὸ προσώπου σου ἔθνη
ταραχθήσονται

2 ὅταν ποιῇς τὰ ἔνδοξα τρόμος λήμψεται ἀπὸ σοῦ ὄρη

3 ἀπὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος οὐκ ἠκούσαμεν οὐδὲ οἱ ὀφθαλμοὶ ἡμῶν εἶδον θεὸν πλὴν σοῦ
καὶ τὰ ἔργα σου ἃ ποιήσεις τοῖς ὑπομένουσιν ἔλεον

4 συναντήσεται γὰρ τοῖς ποιοῦσιν τὸ δίκαιον καὶ τῶν ὁδῶν σου
μνησθήσονται ἰδοὺ σὺ ὠργίσθης καὶ ἡμεῖς ἡμάρτομεν διὰ τοῦτο ἐπλανήθημεν

5 καὶ ἐγενήθημεν ὡς ἀκάθαρτοι πάντες ἡμεῖς ὡς ῥάκος ἀποκαθημένης πᾶσα ἡ
δικαιοσύνη ἡμῶν καὶ ἐξερρύημεν ὡς φύλλα διὰ τὰς ἀνομίας ἡμῶν οὕτως
ἄνεμος οἴσει ἡμᾶς

6 καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ὁ ἐπικαλούμενος τὸ ὄνομά σου καὶ ὁ μνησθεὶς ἀντιλαβέσθαι
σου ὅτι ἀπέστρεψας τὸ πρόσωπόν σου ἀφ᾽ ἡμῶν καὶ παρέδωκας ἡμᾶς διὰ τὰς
ἁμαρτίας ἡμῶν

7 καὶ νῦν κύριε πατὴρ ἡμῶν σύ ἡμεῖς δὲ πηλὸς ἔργον τῶν χειρῶν σου πάντες

8 μὴ ὀργίζου ἡμῖν σφόδρα καὶ μὴ ἐν καιρῷ μνησθῇς ἁμαρτιῶν ἡμῶν καὶ νῦν
ἐπίβλεψον ὅτι λαός σου πάντες ἡμεῖς

9 πόλις τοῦ ἁγίου σου ἐγενήθη ἔρημος Σιων ὡς ἔρημος ἐγενήθη Ιερουσαλημ
εἰς κατάραν

10 ὁ οἶκος τὸ ἅγιον ἡμῶν καὶ ἡ δόξα ἣν ηὐλόγησαν οἱ πατέρες ἡμῶν ἐγενήθη
πυρίκαυστος καὶ πάντα τὰ ἔνδοξα συνέπεσεν

11 καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσι τούτοις ἀνέσχου κύριε καὶ ἐσιώπησας καὶ ἐταπείνωσας ἡμᾶς
σφόδρα

1 \[Utinam dirumperes cælos, et descenderes;  
a facie tua montes defluerent;  

2 sicut exustio ignis tabescerent,  
aquæ arderent igni:  
ut notum fieret nomen tuum inimicis tuis;  
a facie tua gentes turbarentur.  

3 Cum feceris mirabilia, non sustinebimus;  
descendisti, et a facie tua montes defluxerunt.  

4 A sæculo non audierunt, neque auribus perceperunt;  
oculus non vidit, Deus, absque te,  
quæ præparasti exspectantibus te.  

5 Occurristi lætanti, et facienti justitiam;  
in viis tuis recordabuntur tui.  
Ecce tu iratus es, et peccavimus;  
in ipsis fuimus semper, et salvabimur.  

6 Et facti sumus ut immundus omnes nos,  
et quasi pannus menstruatæ universæ justitiæ nostræ;  
et cecidimus quasi folium universi,  
et iniquitates nostræ quasi ventus abstulerunt nos.  

7 Non est qui invocet nomen tuum;  
qui consurgat, et teneat te.  
Abscondisti faciem tuam a nobis,  
et allisisti nos in manu iniquitatis nostræ.  

8 Et nunc, Domine, pater noster es tu,  
nos vero lutum;  
et fictor noster tu,  
et opera manuum tuarum omnes nos.  

9 Ne irascaris, Domine, satis,  
et ne ultra memineris iniquitatis nostræ;  
ecce, respice, populus tuus omnes nos.  

10 Civitas Sancti tui facta est deserta,  
Sion deserta facta est,  
Jerusalem desolata est.  

11 Domus sanctificationis nostræ et gloriæ nostræ,  
ubi laudaverunt te patres nostri,  
facta est in exustionem ignis,  
et omnia desiderabilia nostra versa sunt in ruinas.  

12 Numquid super his continebis te, Domine;  
tacebis, et affliges nos vehementer?\]
