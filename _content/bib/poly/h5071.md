H5071  
נדבה  
נְדָבָה ‎ n<sup>e</sup>dâbâh  
*ned-aw-baw‘*  
From [5068](h5068) properly (abstractly) *spontaneity*, or (adjectively)
*spontaneous*; also (concretely) a *spontaneous* or (by inference, in
plural) *abundant* gift: - free (-will) offering, freely, plentiful,
voluntary (-ily, offering), willing (-ly, offering).
