G5093
τίμιος
timios
tim‘-ee-os
Including the comparative τίμιώτερος timiōteros and the superlative τίμιώτατος timiōtatos ; from 5092 valuable, that is, (objectively) costly, or (subjectively) honored, esteemed, or (figuratively) beloved: - dear, honourable, (more, most) precious, had in reputation.
