G846
αὐτός
autos
ow-tos‘
From the particle αὖ au (perhaps akin to the base of 109 through the idea of a baffling wind; backward); the reflexive pronoun self, used (alone or in the compound of 1438 of the third person, and (with the proper personal pronoun) of the other persons: - her, it (-self), one, the other, (mine) own, said, ([self-], the) same, ([him-, my-, thy-]) self, [your-] selves, she, that, their (-s), them ([-selves]), there [-at, -by, -in, -into, -of, -on, -with], they, (these) things, this (man), those, together, very, which. Compare 848
