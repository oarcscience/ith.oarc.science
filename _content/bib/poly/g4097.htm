πιπράσκω, πράω
pipraskō praō
pip-ras‘-ko, prah‘-o
The first is a reduplicated and prolonged form of the second (which occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses); contracted from περάω peraō (to traverse; from the base of 4008 ; to traffic (by travelling), that is, dispose of as merchandise or into slavery (literally or figuratively): - sell.
