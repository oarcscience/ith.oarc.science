H3467  
ישׁע  
יָשַׁע ‎ yâsha‛  
*yaw-shah‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *be* *open*, *wide* or *free*, that is,
(by implication) to *be* *safe*; causatively to *free* or *succor: -* X
at all, avenging, defend, deliver (-er), help, preserve, rescue, be
safe, bring (having) salvation, save (-iour), get victory.
