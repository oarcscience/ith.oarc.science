H4334
מישׁר מישׁור
מִישׁוֹר מִישׁוֹר ‎ mı̂yshôr mı̂yshôr
mee-shore‘, mee-shore‘
From 3474 a level, that is, a plain (often used (with the article prefixed) as a proper name of certain districts); figuratively concord; also straightness, that is, (figuratively) justice (sometimes adverbially justly): - equity, even place, plain, right (-eously), (made) straight, uprightness.
