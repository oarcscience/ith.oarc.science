H3847  
לבשׁ לבשׁ  
לָבַשׁ לָבֵשׁ ‎ lâbash lâbêsh  
*law-bash‘,* *law-bashe‘*  
A primitive root; properly *wrap* around, that is, (by implication) to
*put* *on* a garment or *clothe* (oneself, or another), literally or
figuratively: - (in) apparel, arm, array (self), clothe (self), come
upon, put (on, upon), wear.
