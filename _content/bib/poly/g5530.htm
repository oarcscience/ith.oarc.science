G5530
χράομαι
chraomai
khrah‘-om-ahee
Middle voice of a primary verb (perhaps rather from 5495 to handle); to furnish what is needed; (give an oracle, “graze” [touch slightly], light upon, etc.), that is, (by implication) to employ or (by extension) to act towards one in a given manner: - entreat, use. Compare 5531 5534
