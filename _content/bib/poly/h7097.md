H7097  
קצה קצה  
קָצֶה קֵצֶה ‎ qâtseh qêtseh  
*kaw-tseh‘,* *kay-tseh‘*  
The second form is negative only; from [7096](h7096) an *extremity*
(used in a great variety of applications and idioms; compare
[7093](h7093) : - X after, border, brim, brink, edge, end, \[in-\]
finite, frontier, outmost coast, quarter, shore, (out-) side, X some, ut
(-ter-) most (part).
