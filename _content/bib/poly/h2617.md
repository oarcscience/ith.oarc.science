H2617  
חסד  
חֵסֵד ‎ chêsêd  
*kheh‘-sed*  
From [2616](h2616) *kindness*; by implication (towards God) *piety*;
rarely (by opprobrium) *reproof*, or (subjectively) *beauty: -* favour,
good deed (-liness, -ness), kindly, (loving-) kindness, merciful
(kindness), mercy, pity, reproach, wicked thing.
