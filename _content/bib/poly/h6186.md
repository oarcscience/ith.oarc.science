H6186  
ערך  
עָרַך ‎ ‛ârak  
*aw-rak‘*  
A primitive root; to set in a *row*, that is, *arrange*, put in *order*
(in a very wide variety of applications): - put (set) (the battle, self)
in array, compare, direct, equal, esteem, estimate, expert \[in war\],
furnish, handle, join \[battle\], ordain, (lay, put, reckon up, set)
(in) order, prepare, tax, value.
