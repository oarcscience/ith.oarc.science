כּשׂדּימה כּשׂדּי
כַּשׂדִּי כַּשׂדִּימָה ‎ kaśdı̂y kaśdı̂ymâh
kas-dee‘, kas-dee‘-maw
(Occasionally shown as the second form with enclitic; meaning towards the Kasdites); patronymic from 3777 (only in the plural); a Kasdite, or descendant of Kesed; by implication a Chaldaean (as if so descended); also an astrologer (as if proverbial of that people): - into Chaldea), patronymicallyn. from 3777 (only in the plural); a Kasdite; or descendant of Kesed; by implication a Chaldan (as if so descended); also an astrologer (as if proverbial of that people): - Chaldeans, Chaldees, inhabitants of Chaldea.
