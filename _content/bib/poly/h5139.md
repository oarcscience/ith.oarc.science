H5139  
נזר נזיר  
נָזִיר נָזִר ‎ nâzı̂yr nâzir  
*naw-zeer‘,* *naw-zeer‘*  
From [5144](h5144) *separate*, that is, consecrated (as *prince*, a
*Nazirite*); hence (figuratively from the latter) an *unpruned* vine
(like an unshorn Nazirite). (The translation, *Nazarite*, is by a false
alliteration with Nazareth.): - Nazarite \[by a false alliteration with
Nazareth\], separate (-d), vine undressed.
