H5157  
נחל  
נָחַל ‎ nâchal  
*naw-khal‘*  
A primitive root; to *inherit* (as a (figurative) mode of descent), or
(generally) to *occupy*; causatively to *bequeath*, or (generally)
*distribute*, *instate: -* divide, have (\[inheritance\]), take as an
heritage, (cause to, give to, make to) inherit, (distribute for, divide
\[for, for an, by\], give for, have, leave for, take \[for\])
inheritance, (have in, cause to be made to) possess (-ion).
