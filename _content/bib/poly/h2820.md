H2820  
חשׂך  
חָשַׂך ‎ châśak  
*khaw-sak‘*  
A prim root; to *restrain* or (reflexively) *refrain*; by implication to
*refuse*, *spare*, *preserve*; also (by interchange with [2821](h2821)
to *observe: -* assuage, X darken, forbear, hinder, hold back, keep
(back), punish, refrain, reserve, spare, withhold.
