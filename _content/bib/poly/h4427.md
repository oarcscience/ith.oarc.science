H4427  
מלך  
מָלַך ‎ mâlak  
*maw-lak‘*  
A primitive root; to *reign*; inceptively to *ascend* *the* *throne*;
causatively to *induct* into royalty; hence (by implication) to *take*
*counsel: -* consult, X indeed, be (make, set a, set up) king, be (make)
queen, (begin to, make to) reign (-ing), rule, X surely.
