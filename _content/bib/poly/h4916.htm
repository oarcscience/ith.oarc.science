H4916
משׁלח משׁלח משׁלוח
מִשׁלוַֹח מִשׁלוַֹח מִשׁלָח ‎ mishlôach mishlôach mishlâch
(1,2) mish-lo‘-akh, mish-lawkh‘
From 7971 a sending out, that is, (abstractly) presentation (favorable), or seizure (unfavorable); also (concretely) a place of dismissal, or a business to be discharged: - to lay, to put, sending (forth), to set.
