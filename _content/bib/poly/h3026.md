H3026  
יגר שׂהדוּתא  
יְגַר שַׂהֲדוּתָא ‎ y<sup>e</sup>gar śahădûthâ‘  
*yegar‘* *sah-had-oo-thaw‘*  
(Chaldee); from a word derived from an unused root (meaning to *gather*)
and a derivative of a root corresponding to [7717](h7717) *heap* *of*
*the* *testimony*; *Jegar-Sahadutha*, a cairn East of the Jordan: -
Jegar-Sahadutha.
