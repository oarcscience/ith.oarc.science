H5331  
נצח נצח  
נֶצַח נֵצַח ‎ netsach nêtsach  
*neh‘-tsakh,* *nay‘-tsakh*  
From [5329](h5329) properly a *goal*, that is, the bright object at a
distance travelled towards; hence (figuratively), *splendor*, or
(subjectively) *truthfulness*, or (objectively) *confidence*; but
usually (adverbially), *continually* (that is, to the most distant point
of view): - alway (-s), constantly, end, (+ n-) ever (more), perpetual,
strength, victory.
