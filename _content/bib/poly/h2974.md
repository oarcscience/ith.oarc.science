H2974  
יאל  
יָאַל ‎ yâ‘al  
*yaw-al‘*  
A primitive root (probably rather the same as [2973](h2973) through the
idea of mental *weakness*); properly to *yield*, especially *assent*;
hence (positively) to *undertake* as an act of volition: - assay, begin,
be content, please, take upon, X willingly, would.
