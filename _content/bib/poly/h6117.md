H6117  
עקב  
עָקַב ‎ ‛âqab  
*aw-kab‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *swell* out or up; used only as
denominative from [6119](h6119) to *seize* *by* *the* *heel*;
figuratively to *circumvent* (as if *tripping* up the heels); also to
*restrain* (as if holding by the heel): - take by the heel, stay,
supplant, X utterly.
