H3254  
יסף  
יָסַף ‎ yâsaph  
*yaw-saf‘*  
A primitive root; to *add* or *augment* (often adverbially to *continue*
to do a thing): - add, X again, X any more, X cease, X come more, +
conceive again, continue, exceed, X further, X gather together, get
more, give moreover, X henceforth, increase (more and more), join, X
longer (bring, do, make, much, put), X (the, much, yet) more (and more),
proceed (further), prolong, put, be \[strong-\] er, X yet, yield.
