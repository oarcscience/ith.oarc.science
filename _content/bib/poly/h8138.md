H8138  
שׁנה  
שָׁנָה ‎ shânâh  
*shaw-naw‘*  
A primitive root; to *fold*, that is, *duplicate* (literally or
figuratively (); by implication to *transmute* (transitively or
intransitively): - do (speak, strike) again, alter, double, (be given
to) change, disguise, (be) diverse, pervert, prefer, repeat, return, do
the second time.
