H2942  
טעם  
טְּעֵם ‎ ṭ<sup>e</sup>‛êm  
*teh-ame‘*  
(Chaldee); from [2939](h2939) and equivalent to [2941](h2941) properly
*flavor*; figuratively *judgment* (both subjectively and objectively);
hence *account* (both subjectively and objectively): - + chancellor, +
command, commandment, decree, + regard, taste, wisdom.
