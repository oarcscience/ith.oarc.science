H6605  
פּתח  
פָּתַח ‎ pâthach  
*paw-thakh‘*  
A primitive root; to *open* wide (literally or figuratively);
specifically to *loosen*, *begin*, *plough*, *carve: -* appear, break
forth, draw (out), let go free, (en-) grave (-n), loose (self), (be,
beset) open (-ing), put off, ungird, unstop, have vent.
