H4864  
משׂאת  
מַשׂאֵת ‎ maś‘êth  
*mas-ayth‘*  
From [5375](h5375) properly (abstractly) a *raising* (as of the hands in
prayer), or *rising* (of flame); figuratively an *utterance*; concretely
a *beacon* (as *raised*); a *present* (as taken), *mess*, or *tribute*;
figuratively a *reproach* (as a burden): - burden, collection, sign of
fire, (great) flame, gift, lifting up, mess, oblation, reward.
