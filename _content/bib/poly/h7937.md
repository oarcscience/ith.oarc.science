H7937  
שׁכר  
שָׁכַר ‎ shâkar  
*shaw-kar‘*  
A primitive root; to *become* *tipsy*; in a qualified sense, to
*satiate* with a stimulating drink or (figuratively) influence.
(Superlative of [8248](h8248) ): - (be filled with) drink (abundantly),
(be, make) drunk (-en), be merry. \[Superlative of [8248](h8248) \]
