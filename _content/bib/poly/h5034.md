H5034  
נבל  
נָבֵל ‎ nâbêl  
*naw-bale‘*  
A primitive root; to *wilt*; generally to *fall* away, *fail*, *faint*;
figuratively to *be* *foolish* or (morally) *wicked*; causatively to
*despise*, *disgrace: -* disgrace, dishonour, lightly esteem, fade
(away, -ing), fall (down, -ling, off), do foolishly, come to nought, X
surely, make vile, wither.
