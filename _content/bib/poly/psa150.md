### Psalms 150

[KJV](../kjv/psa150)

1  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> ye the LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup>
Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> in his
sanctuary:<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup> praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him in
the firmament<sup>[7549](h7549)</sup> of his
power.<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup>

2  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him for his mighty
acts:<sup>[1369](h1369)</sup> praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him
according to his excellent<sup>[7230](h7230)</sup>
greatness.<sup>[1433](h1433)</sup>

3  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him with the
sound<sup>[8629](h8629)</sup> of the trumpet:<sup>[7782](h7782)</sup>
praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him with the
psaltery<sup>[5035](h5035)</sup> and harp.<sup>[3658](h3658)</sup>

4  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him with the
timbrel<sup>[8596](h8596)</sup> and dance:<sup>[4234](h4234)</sup>
praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him with stringed
instruments<sup>[4482](h4482)</sup> and organs.<sup>[5748](h5748)</sup>

5  Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him upon the
loud<sup>[8088](h8088)</sup> cymbals:<sup>[6767](h6767)</sup>
praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> him upon the high
sounding<sup>[8643](h8643)</sup> cymbals.<sup>[6767](h6767)</sup>

6  Let every<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> thing that hath
breath<sup>[5397](h5397)</sup> praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup> Praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> ye the
LORD.<sup>[3050](h3050)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa150)

1 αλληλουια αἰνεῖτε τὸν θεὸν ἐν τοῖς ἁγίοις αὐτοῦ αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν
στερεώματι δυνάμεως αὐτοῦ

2 αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐπὶ ταῖς δυναστείαις αὐτοῦ αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος
τῆς μεγαλωσύνης αὐτοῦ

3 αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν ἤχῳ σάλπιγγος αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν ψαλτηρίῳ καὶ κιθάρᾳ

4 αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν τυμπάνῳ καὶ χορῷ αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν χορδαῖς καὶ ὀργάνῳ

5 αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν κυμβάλοις εὐήχοις αἰνεῖτε αὐτὸν ἐν κυμβάλοις
ἀλαλαγμοῦ

6 πᾶσα πνοὴ αἰνεσάτω τὸν κύριον αλληλουια

[Vul](../vul/psa150)

1 Alleluja. \[Laudate Dominum in sanctis ejus;  
laudate eum in firmamento virtutis ejus.  

2 Laudate eum in virtutibus ejus;  
laudate eum secundum multitudinem magnitudinis ejus.  

3 Laudate eum in sono tubæ;  
laudate eum in psalterio et cithara.  

4 Laudate eum in tympano et choro;  
laudate eum in chordis et organo.  

5 Laudate eum in cymbalis benesonantibus;  
laudate eum in cymbalis jubilationis.  

6 Omnis spiritus laudet Dominum!  
Alleluja.\]
