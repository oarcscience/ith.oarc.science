צַּר צָּר ‎ tsar tsâr
tsar, tsawr
From 6887 narrow; (as a noun) a tight place (usually figuratively, that is, trouble); also a pebble (as in 6864 ; (transitively) an opponent (as crowding): - adversary, afflicted (-tion), anguish, close, distress, enemy, flint, foe, narrow, small, sorrow, strait, tribulation, trouble.
