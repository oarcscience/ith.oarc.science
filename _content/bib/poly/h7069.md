H7069  
קנה  
קָנָה ‎ qânâh  
*kaw-naw‘*  
A primitive root; to *erect*, that is, *create*; by extension to
*procure*, especially by purchase (causatively *sell*); by implication
to *own: -* attain, buy (-er), teach to keep cattle, get, provoke to
jealousy, possess (-or), purchase, recover, redeem, X surely, X
verily.
