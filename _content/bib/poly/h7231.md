H7231  
רבב  
רָבַב ‎ râbab  
*raw-bab‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *cast* together (compare [7241](h7241) ,
that is, *increase*, especially in number; also (as denominative from
[7233](h7233) to *multiply* *by* *the* *myriad: -* increase, be many
(-ifold), be more, multiply, ten thousands.
