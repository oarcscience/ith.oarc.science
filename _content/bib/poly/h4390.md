H4390  
מלא מלא  
מָלֵא מָלָא ‎ mâlê‘ mâlâ‘  
*maw-lay‘,* *maw-law‘*  
A primitive root, to *fill* or (intransitively) *be* *full* of, in a
wide application (literally and figuratively): - accomplish, confirm, +
consecrate, be at an end, be expired, be fenced, fill, fulfil, (be,
become, X draw, give in, go) fully (-ly, -ly set, tale), \[over-\] flow,
fulness, furnish, gather (selves, together), presume, replenish,
satisfy, set, space, take a \[hand-\] full, + have wholly.
