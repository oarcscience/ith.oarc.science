H1115
בּלתּי
בִּלתִּּי ‎ biltı̂y
bil-tee‘
Constructive feminine of 1086 (equivalent to 1097 ; properly a failure of, that is, (used only as a negative particle, usually with prepositional prefix) not, except, without, unless, besides, because not, until, etc.: - because un[satiable], beside, but, + continual, except, from, lest, neither, no more, none, not, nothing, save, that no, without.
