H8458  
תּחבּוּלה תּחבּלה  
תַּּחבּוּלָה תַּּחבּוּלָה ‎ tachbûlâh tachbûlâh  
*takh-boo-law‘,* *takh-boo-law‘*  
From [2254](h2254) as denominative from [2256](h2256) (only in plural)
properly *steerage* (as a management of *ropes*), that is,
(figuratively) *guidance* or (by implication) a *plan: -* good advice,
(wise) counsels.
