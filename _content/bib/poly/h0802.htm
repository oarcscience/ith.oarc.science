H802
נשׁים אשּׁה
אִשָּׁה נָשִׁים ‎ ‘ishshâh nâshı̂ym
ish-shaw‘, naw-sheem‘
The first form is the feminine of 376 or 582 the second form is an irregular plural; a woman (used in the same wide sense as 582 .: - [adulter]ess, each, every, female, X many, + none, one, + together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.
