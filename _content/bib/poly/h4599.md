H4599  
מעינה מעינו מעין  
מַעיָן מַעיְנוֹ מַעיָנָה ‎ ma‛yân ma‛y<sup>e</sup>nô ma‛yânâh  
*mah-yawn‘,* *mah-yen-o‘,* *mah-yaw-naw‘*  
From [5869](h5869) (as a denominative in the sense of a *spring*); a
*fountain* (also collectively), figuratively a *source* (of
satisfaction): - fountain, spring, well.
