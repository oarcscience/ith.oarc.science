H6565  
פּרר  
פָּרַר ‎ pârar  
*paw-rar‘*  
A primitive root; to *break* up (usually figuratively, that is, to
*violate*, *frustrate*): - X any ways, break (asunder), cast off, cause
to cease, X clean, defeat, disannul, disappoint, dissolve, divide, make
of none effect, fail, frustrate, bring (come) to nought, X utterly, make
void.
