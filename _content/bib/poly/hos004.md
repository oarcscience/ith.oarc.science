### Hosea 4

[KJV](../kjv/hos004)

1  Hear<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> the word<sup>[1697](h1697)</sup> of the
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> ye children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of
Israel:<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> hath a controversy<sup>[7379](h7379)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the inhabitants<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup> of
the land,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> there
is no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> truth,<sup>[571](h0571)</sup>
nor<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> mercy,<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup>
nor<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> knowledge<sup>[1847](h1847)</sup> of
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> in the land.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

2  By swearing,<sup>[422](h0422)</sup> and
lying,<sup>[3584](h3584)</sup> and killing,<sup>[7523](h7523)</sup> and
stealing,<sup>[1589](h1589)</sup> and committing
adultery,<sup>[5003](h5003)</sup> they break
out,<sup>[6555](h6555)</sup> and blood<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup>
toucheth<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> blood.<sup>[1818](h1818)</sup>

3  Therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> shall the
land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> mourn,<sup>[56](h0056)</sup> and every
one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that dwelleth<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup>
therein shall languish,<sup>[535](h0535)</sup> with the
beasts<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> of the field,<sup>[7704](h7704)</sup> and
with the fowls<sup>[5775](h5775)</sup> of
heaven;<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> yea, the fishes<sup>[1709](h1709)</sup>
of the sea<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> also<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> shall be
taken away.<sup>[622](h0622)</sup>

4  Yet<sup>[389](h0389)</sup> let no<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> strive,<sup>[7378](h7378)</sup>
nor<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> reprove<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup>
another:<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> for thy people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup>
are as they that strive<sup>[7378](h7378)</sup> with the
priest.<sup>[3548](h3548)</sup>

5  Therefore shalt thou fall<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup> in the
day,<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> and the prophet<sup>[5030](h5030)</sup>
also<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> shall fall<sup>[3782](h3782)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> thee in the night,<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup>
and I will destroy<sup>[1820](h1820)</sup> thy
mother.<sup>[517](h0517)</sup>

6  My people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> are
destroyed<sup>[1820](h1820)</sup> for lack<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[1097](h1097)</sup> of knowledge:<sup>[1847](h1847)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> hast
rejected<sup>[3988](h3988)</sup> knowledge,<sup>[1847](h1847)</sup> I
will also reject<sup>[3988](h3988)</sup> thee, that thou shalt be no
priest<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3547](h3547)</sup> to me: seeing
thou hast forgotten<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> the
law<sup>[8451](h8451)</sup> of thy God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>
I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> will also<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup>
forget<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> thy children.<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup>

7  As they were increased,<sup>[7231](h7231)</sup>
so<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> they sinned<sup>[2398](h2398)</sup> against
me: therefore will I change<sup>[4171](h4171)</sup> their
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> into shame.<sup>[7036](h7036)</sup>

8  They eat up<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> the sin<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup> of
my people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> and they set<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup>
their heart<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> on<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> their
iniquity.<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>

9  And there shall be,<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> like
people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> like priest:<sup>[3548](h3548)</sup> and
I will punish<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> them for
their ways,<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup> and reward<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
them their doings.<sup>[4611](h4611)</sup>

10  For they shall eat,<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> and
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> have enough:<sup>[7646](h7646)</sup> they
shall commit whoredom,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> and shall
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> increase:<sup>[6555](h6555)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they have left
off<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> to take heed<sup>[8104](h8104)</sup>
<sup>[853](h0853)</sup> to the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

11  Whoredom<sup>[2184](h2184)</sup> and wine<sup>[3196](h3196)</sup>
and new wine<sup>[8492](h8492)</sup> take away<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup>
the heart.<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>

12  My people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> ask
counsel<sup>[7592](h7592)</sup> at their stocks,<sup>[6086](h6086)</sup>
and their staff<sup>[4731](h4731)</sup>
declareth<sup>[5046](h5046)</sup> unto them: for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
the spirit<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> of whoredoms<sup>[2183](h2183)</sup>
hath caused them to err,<sup>[8582](h8582)</sup> and they have gone a
whoring<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> from under<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup> their God.<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>

13  They sacrifice<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
the tops<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> of the
mountains,<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> and burn
incense<sup>[6999](h6999)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the
hills,<sup>[1389](h1389)</sup> under<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup>
oaks<sup>[437](h0437)</sup> and poplars<sup>[3839](h3839)</sup> and
elms,<sup>[424](h0424)</sup> because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the
shadow<sup>[6738](h6738)</sup> thereof is good:<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup>
therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> your
daughters<sup>[1323](h1323)</sup> shall commit
whoredom,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> and your
spouses<sup>[3618](h3618)</sup> shall commit
adultery.<sup>[5003](h5003)</sup>

14  I will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> punish<sup>[6485](h6485)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> your daughters<sup>[1323](h1323)</sup>
when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they commit
whoredom,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> nor your
spouses<sup>[3618](h3618)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they commit
adultery:<sup>[5003](h5003)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
themselves<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> are separated<sup>[6504](h6504)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> whores,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> and they
sacrifice<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup>
harlots:<sup>[6948](h6948)</sup> therefore the
people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> that doth not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
understand<sup>[995](h0995)</sup> shall fall.<sup>[3832](h3832)</sup>

15  Though<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> thou,<sup>[859](h0859)</sup>
Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> play the harlot,<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup>
yet let not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup>
offend;<sup>[816](h0816)</sup> and come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup>
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> ye unto Gilgal,<sup>[1537](h1537)</sup>
neither<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> go ye up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> to
Beth-aven,<sup>[1007](h1007)</sup> nor<sup>[408](h0408)</sup>
swear,<sup>[7650](h7650)</sup> The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
liveth.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>

16  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> slideth
back<sup>[5637](h5637)</sup> as a backsliding<sup>[5637](h5637)</sup>
heifer:<sup>[6510](h6510)</sup> now<sup>[6258](h6258)</sup> the
LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> will feed<sup>[7462](h7462)</sup> them as a
lamb<sup>[3532](h3532)</sup> in a large place.<sup>[4800](h4800)</sup>

17  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> is joined<sup>[2266](h2266)</sup> to
idols:<sup>[6091](h6091)</sup> let him alone.<sup>[5117](h5117)</sup>

18  Their drink<sup>[5435](h5435)</sup> is sour:<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup>
they have committed whoredom continually:<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup>
<sup>[2181](h2181)</sup> her rulers<sup>[4043](h4043)</sup> with
shame<sup>[7036](h7036)</sup> do love,<sup>[157](h0157)</sup>
Give<sup>[3051](h3051)</sup> ye.

19  The wind<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> hath bound her
up<sup>[6887](h6887)</sup> <sup>[853](h0853)</sup> in her
wings,<sup>[3671](h3671)</sup> and they shall be
ashamed<sup>[954](h0954)</sup> because of their
sacrifices.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2077](h2077)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/hos004)

1 ἀκούσατε λόγον κυρίου υἱοὶ Ισραηλ διότι κρίσις τῷ κυρίῳ πρὸς τοὺς
κατοικοῦντας τὴν γῆν διότι οὐκ ἔστιν ἀλήθεια οὐδὲ ἔλεος οὐδὲ ἐπίγνωσις
θεοῦ ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς

2 ἀρὰ καὶ ψεῦδος καὶ φόνος καὶ κλοπὴ καὶ μοιχεία κέχυται ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς καὶ
αἵματα ἐφ᾽ αἵμασιν μίσγουσιν

3 διὰ τοῦτο πενθήσει ἡ γῆ καὶ σμικρυνθήσεται σὺν πᾶσιν τοῖς κατοικοῦσιν
αὐτήν σὺν τοῖς θηρίοις τοῦ ἀγροῦ καὶ σὺν τοῖς ἑρπετοῖς τῆς γῆς καὶ σὺν
τοῖς πετεινοῖς τοῦ οὐρανοῦ καὶ οἱ ἰχθύες τῆς θαλάσσης ἐκλείψουσιν

4 ὅπως μηδεὶς μήτε δικάζηται μήτε ἐλέγχῃ μηδείς ὁ δὲ λαός μου ὡς
ἀντιλεγόμενος ἱερεύς

5 καὶ ἀσθενήσεις ἡμέρας καὶ ἀσθενήσει καὶ προφήτης μετὰ σοῦ νυκτὶ
ὡμοίωσα τὴν μητέρα σου

6 ὡμοιώθη ὁ λαός μου ὡς οὐκ ἔχων γνῶσιν ὅτι σὺ ἐπίγνωσιν ἀπώσω κἀγὼ
ἀπώσομαι σὲ τοῦ μὴ ἱερατεύειν μοι καὶ ἐπελάθου νόμον θεοῦ σου κἀγὼ
ἐπιλήσομαι τέκνων σου

7 κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος αὐτῶν οὕτως ἥμαρτόν μοι τὴν δόξαν αὐτῶν εἰς ἀτιμίαν
θήσομαι

8 ἁμαρτίας λαοῦ μου φάγονται καὶ ἐν ταῖς ἀδικίαις αὐτῶν λήμψονται τὰς
ψυχὰς αὐτῶν

9 καὶ ἔσται καθὼς ὁ λαὸς οὕτως καὶ ὁ ἱερεύς καὶ ἐκδικήσω ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν τὰς
ὁδοὺς αὐτοῦ καὶ τὰ διαβούλια αὐτοῦ ἀνταποδώσω αὐτῷ

10 καὶ φάγονται καὶ οὐ μὴ ἐμπλησθῶσιν ἐπόρνευσαν καὶ οὐ μὴ κατευθύνωσιν
διότι τὸν κύριον ἐγκατέλιπον τοῦ φυλάξαι

11 πορνείαν καὶ οἶνον καὶ μέθυσμα ἐδέξατο καρδία λαοῦ μου

12 ἐν συμβόλοις ἐπηρώτων καὶ ἐν ῥάβδοις αὐτοῦ ἀπήγγελλον αὐτῷ πνεύματι
πορνείας ἐπλανήθησαν καὶ ἐξεπόρνευσαν ἀπὸ τοῦ θεοῦ αὐτῶν

13 ἐπὶ τὰς κορυφὰς τῶν ὀρέων ἐθυσίαζον καὶ ἐπὶ τοὺς βουνοὺς ἔθυον
ὑποκάτω δρυὸς καὶ λεύκης καὶ δένδρου συσκιάζοντος ὅτι καλὸν σκέπη διὰ
τοῦτο ἐκπορνεύσουσιν αἱ θυγατέρες ὑμῶν καὶ αἱ νύμφαι ὑμῶν μοιχεύσουσιν

14 καὶ οὐ μὴ ἐπισκέψωμαι ἐπὶ τὰς θυγατέρας ὑμῶν ὅταν πορνεύωσιν καὶ ἐπὶ
τὰς νύμφας ὑμῶν ὅταν μοιχεύωσιν διότι καὶ αὐτοὶ μετὰ τῶν πορνῶν
συνεφύροντο καὶ μετὰ τῶν τετελεσμένων ἔθυον καὶ ὁ λαὸς ὁ συνίων
συνεπλέκετο μετὰ πόρνης

15 σὺ δέ Ισραηλ μὴ ἀγνόει καὶ Ιουδα μὴ εἰσπορεύεσθε εἰς Γαλγαλα καὶ μὴ
ἀναβαίνετε εἰς τὸν οἶκον Ων καὶ μὴ ὀμνύετε ζῶντα κύριον

16 ὅτι ὡς δάμαλις παροιστρῶσα παροίστρησεν Ισραηλ νῦν νεμήσει αὐτοὺς
κύριος ὡς ἀμνὸν ἐν εὐρυχώρῳ

17 μέτοχος εἰδώλων Εφραιμ ἔθηκεν ἑαυτῷ σκάνδαλα

18 ᾑρέτισεν Χαναναίους πορνεύοντες ἐξεπόρνευσαν ἠγάπησαν ἀτιμίαν ἐκ
φρυάγματος αὐτῶν

19 συστροφὴ πνεύματος σὺ εἶ ἐν ταῖς πτέρυξιν αὐτῆς καὶ καταισχυνθήσονται
ἐκ τῶν θυσιαστηρίων αὐτῶν

[Vul](../vul/hos004)

1 \[Audite verbum Domini, filii Israël,  
quia judicium Domino cum habitatoribus terræ:  
non est enim veritas, et non est misericordia,  
et non est scientia Dei in terra.  

2 Maledictum, et mendacium,  
et homicidium, et furtum, et adulterium inundaverunt,  
et sanguis sanguinem tetigit.  

3 Propter hoc lugebit terra,  
et infirmabitur omnis qui habitat in ea,  
in bestia agri, et in volucre cæli;  
sed et pisces maris congregabuntur.  

4 Verumtamen unusquisque non judicet,  
et non arguatur vir:  
populus enim tuus sicut hi qui contradicunt sacerdoti.  

5 Et corrues hodie,  
et corruet etiam propheta tecum.  
Nocte tacere feci matrem tuam.  

6 Conticuit populus meus,  
eo quod non habuerit scientiam:  
quia tu scientiam repulisti,  
repellam te, ne sacerdotio fungaris mihi;  
et oblita es legis Dei tui,  
obliviscar filiorum tuorum et ego.  

7 Secundum multitudinem eorum sic peccaverunt mihi:  
gloriam eorum in ignominiam commutabo.  

8 Peccata populi mei comedent,  
et ad iniquitatem eorum sublevabunt animas eorum.  

9 Et erit sicut populus, sic sacerdos;  
et visitabo super eum vias ejus,  
et cogitationes ejus reddam ei.  

10 Et comedent, et non saturabuntur;  
fornicati sunt, et non cessaverunt:  
quoniam Dominum dereliquerunt in non custodiendo.  

11 Fornicatio, et vinum, et ebrietas auferunt cor.  

12 Populus meus in ligno suo interrogavit,  
et baculus ejus annuntiavit ei;  
spiritus enim fornicationum decepit eos,  
et fornicati sunt a Deo suo.  

13 Super capita montium sacrificabant,  
et super colles ascendebant thymiama;  
subtus quercum, et populum, et terebinthum,  
quia bona erat umbra ejus;  
ideo fornicabuntur filiæ vestræ,  
et sponsæ vestræ adulteræ erunt.  

14 Non visitabo super filias vestras cum fuerint fornicatæ,  
et super sponsas vestras cum adulteraverint,  
quoniam ipsi cum meretricibus conversabantur,  
et cum effeminatis sacrificabant;  
et populus non intelligens vapulabit.  

15 Si fornicaris tu, Israël,  
non delinquat saltem Juda;  
et nolite ingredi in Galgala,  
et ne ascenderitis in Bethaven,  
neque juraveritis: Vivit Dominus!  

16 Quoniam sicut vacca lasciviens declinavit Israël;  
nunc pascet eos Dominus, quasi agnum in latitudine.  

17 Particeps idolorum Ephraim: dimitte eum.  

18 Separatum est convivium eorum;  
fornicatione fornicati sunt:  
dilexerunt afferre ignominiam protectores ejus.  

19 Ligavit eum spiritus in alis suis,  
et confundentur a sacrificiis suis.\]
