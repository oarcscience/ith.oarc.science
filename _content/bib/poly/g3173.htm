G3173
μέγας
megas
meg‘-as
Including the prolonged forms, femine μεγάλη megalē , plural μέγάλοι megaloi , etc.; compare also 3176 3187 , big (literally or figuratively, in a very wide application): - (+ fear) exceedingly, great (-est), high, large, loud, mighty, + (be) sore (afraid), strong, X to years.
