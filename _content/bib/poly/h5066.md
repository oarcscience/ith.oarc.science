נגשׁ  
נָגַשׁ ‎ nâgash  
*naw-gash‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* or *come* (causatively *bring*) *near* (for
any purpose); euphemistically to *lie* *with* a woman; as an enemy, to
*attack*; religiously to *worship*; causatively to *present*;
figuratively to *adduce* an argument; by reversal, to *stand* *back: -*
(make to) approach (nigh), bring (forth, hither, near), (cause to) come
(higher, near, nigh), give place, go hard (up), (be, draw, go) near
(nigh), offer, overtake, present, put, stand.
