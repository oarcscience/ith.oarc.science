G3367
μηδείς, μηδεμία, μηδέν
mēdeis mēdemia mēden
may-dice‘, may -dem-ee‘-ah, may-den‘
The masculine, feminine irregular (second form) and neuter (third form) from 3361 and 1520 not even one (man, woman, thing): - any (man, thing), no (man), none, not (at all, any man, a whit), nothing, + without delay.
