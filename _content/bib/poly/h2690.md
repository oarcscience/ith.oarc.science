H2690  
חצרר חצצר חצר  
חָצַר חֲצוֹצֵר חֲצוֹרֵר ‎ châtsar chătsôtsêr chătsôrêr  
*khaw-tsar‘* *khast-o-tsare‘* *khats-o-rare‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *surround* with a stockade, and thus
*separate* from the open country; but used only in the reduplicated form
(the second and third forms; to *trumpet*, that is, blow on that
instrument): - blow, sound, trumpeter.
