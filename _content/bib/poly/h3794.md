H3794  
כּתּיּי כּתּי  
כִּתּתִּּי כִּתּתִּּיִי ‎ kittı̂y kittı̂yı̂y  
*kit-tee‘,* *kit-tee-ee‘*  
Patrial from an unused name denoting Cyprus (only in the plural); a
*Kittite* or Cypriote; hence an *islander* in general, that is, the
Greeks or Romans on the shores opposite Palestine: - Chittim, Kittim.
