H5144  
נזר  
נָזַר ‎ nâzar  
*naw-zar‘*  
A primitive root; to *hold* *aloof*, that is, (intransitively) *abstain*
(from food and drink, from impurity, and even from divine worship (that
is, *apostatize*)); specifically to *set* *apart* (to sacred purposes),
that is, *devote: -* consecrate, separate (-ing, self).
