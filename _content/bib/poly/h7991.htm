שָׁלִישׁ שָׁלוֹשׁ שָׁלוֹשׁ ‎ shâlı̂ysh shâlôsh shâlôsh
shaw-leesh‘, shaw-loshe‘, shaw-loshe‘
(The second form used in 1Ch 11:11, 1Ch 11:12, 1Ch 11:18; the third form used in 2Sa 23:13); from 7969 a triple, that is, (as a musical instrument) a triangle (or perhaps rather three stringed lute); also (as an indefinitely great quantity) a three fold measure (perhaps a treble ephah); also (as an officer) a general of the third rank (upward, that is, the highest): - captain, instrument of musick, (great) lord, (great) measure, prince, three [from the margin].
