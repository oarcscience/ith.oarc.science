H2790  
חרשׁ  
חָרַשׁ ‎ chârash  
*khaw-rash‘*  
A primitive root; to *scratch*, that is, (by implication) to *engrave*,
*plough*; hence (from the use of tools) to *fabricate* (of any
material); figuratively to *devise* (in a bad sense); hence (from the
idea of secrecy) to be *silent*, to *let* *alone*; hence (by
implication) to *be* *deaf* (as an accompaniment of dumbness): - X
altogether, cease, conceal, be deaf, devise, ear, graven, imagine, leave
off speaking, hold peace, plow (-er, -man), be quiet, rest, practise
secretly, keep silence, be silent, speak not a word, be still, hold
tongue, worker.
