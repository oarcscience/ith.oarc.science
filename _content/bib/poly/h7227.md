רַב ‎ rab  
*rab*  
By contraction from [7231](h7231) *abundant* (in quantity, size, age,
number, rank, quality): - (in) abound (-undance, -ant, -antly), captain,
elder, enough, exceedingly, full, great (-ly, man, one), increase, long
(enough, \[time\]), (do, have) many (-ifold, things, a time),
(\[ship-\]) master, mighty, more, (too, very) much, multiply (-tude),
officer, often \[-times\], plenteous, populous, prince, process \[of
time\], suffice (-ient).
