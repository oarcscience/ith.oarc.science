H6635  
צבאה צבא  
צָּבָא צֶּבָאָה ‎ tsâbâ‘ tsebâ‘âh  
*tsaw-baw‘,* *tseb-aw-aw‘*  
From [6633](h6633) a *mass* of persons (or figurative things),
especially regularly organized for war (an *army*); by implication a
*campaign*, literally or figuratively (specifically *hardship*,
*worship*): - appointed time, (+) army, (+) battle, company, host,
service, soldiers, waiting upon, war (-fare).
