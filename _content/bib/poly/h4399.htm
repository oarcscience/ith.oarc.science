H4399
מלאכה
מְלָאכָּה ‎ melâ‘kâh
mel-aw-kaw‘
From the same as 4397 properly deputyship, that is, ministry; generally employment (never servile) or work (abstractly or concretely); also property (as the result of labor): - business, + cattle, + industrious, occupation, (+ -pied), + officer, thing (made), use, (manner of) work ([-man], -manship).
