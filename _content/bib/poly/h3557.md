H3557  
כּוּל  
כּוּל ‎ kûl  
*kool*  
A primitive root; properly to *keep* *in*; hence to *measure*;
figuratively to *maintain* (in various senses): - (be able to, can)
abide, bear, comprehend, contain, feed, forbearing, guide, hold (-ing
in), nourish (-er), be present, make provision, receive, sustain,
provide sustenance (victuals).
