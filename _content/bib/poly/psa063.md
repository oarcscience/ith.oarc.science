### Psalms 63

[KJV](../kjv/psa063)

1  A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David,<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup>
when he was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> in the
wilderness<sup>[4057](h4057)</sup> of Judah.<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup> O
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> art my
God;<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> early will I seek<sup>[7836](h7836)</sup>
thee: my soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> thirsteth<sup>[6770](h6770)</sup>
for thee, my flesh<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup>
longeth<sup>[3642](h3642)</sup> for thee in a
dry<sup>[6723](h6723)</sup> and thirsty<sup>[5889](h5889)</sup>
land,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> where no<sup>[1097](h1097)</sup>
water<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> is;

2  To see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> thy power<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> and
thy glory,<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> so<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> as I have
seen<sup>[2372](h2372)</sup> thee in the
sanctuary.<sup>[6944](h6944)</sup>

3  Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thy
lovingkindness<sup>[2617](h2617)</sup> is better<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup>
than life,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> my
lips<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup> shall praise<sup>[7623](h7623)</sup> thee.

4  Thus<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> will I bless<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup>
thee while I live:<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> I will lift
up<sup>[5375](h5375)</sup> my hands<sup>[3709](h3709)</sup> in thy
name.<sup>[8034](h8034)</sup>

5  My soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> shall be
satisfied<sup>[7646](h7646)</sup> as<sup>[3644](h3644)</sup> with
marrow<sup>[2459](h2459)</sup> and fatness;<sup>[1880](h1880)</sup> and
my mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> shall praise<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup>
thee with joyful<sup>[7445](h7445)</sup> lips:<sup>[8193](h8193)</sup>

6  When<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> I remember<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> thee
upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> my bed,<sup>[3326](h3326)</sup> and
meditate<sup>[1897](h1897)</sup> on thee in the night
watches.<sup>[821](h0821)</sup>

7  Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou hast
been<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> my help,<sup>[5833](h5833)</sup> therefore
in the shadow<sup>[6738](h6738)</sup> of thy
wings<sup>[3671](h3671)</sup> will I rejoice.<sup>[7442](h7442)</sup>

8  My soul<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> followeth
hard<sup>[1692](h1692)</sup> after<sup>[310](h0310)</sup> thee: thy
right hand<sup>[3225](h3225)</sup> upholdeth<sup>[8551](h8551)</sup> me.

9  But those<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup> that seek<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup>
my soul,<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> to destroy<sup>[7722](h7722)</sup> it,
shall go<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> into the lower
parts<sup>[8482](h8482)</sup> of the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

10  They shall fall<sup>[5064](h5064)</sup> by<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> the sword:<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> they shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a portion<sup>[4521](h4521)</sup> for
foxes.<sup>[7776](h7776)</sup>

11  But the king<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup> shall
rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> in God;<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> every
one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that sweareth<sup>[7650](h7650)</sup> by him
shall glory:<sup>[1984](h1984)</sup> but<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the
mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup> of them that speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup>
lies<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> shall be stopped.<sup>[5534](h5534)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa063)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ

2 εἰσάκουσον ὁ θεός τῆς φωνῆς μου ἐν τῷ δέεσθαί με ἀπὸ φόβου ἐχθροῦ
ἐξελοῦ τὴν ψυχήν μου

3 ἐσκέπασάς με ἀπὸ συστροφῆς πονηρευομένων ἀπὸ πλήθους ἐργαζομένων τὴν
ἀνομίαν

4 οἵτινες ἠκόνησαν ὡς ῥομφαίαν τὰς γλώσσας αὐτῶν ἐνέτειναν τόξον αὐτῶν
πρᾶγμα πικρὸν

5 τοῦ κατατοξεῦσαι ἐν ἀποκρύφοις ἄμωμον ἐξάπινα κατατοξεύσουσιν αὐτὸν
καὶ οὐ φοβηθήσονται

6 ἐκραταίωσαν ἑαυτοῖς λόγον πονηρόν διηγήσαντο τοῦ κρύψαι παγίδας εἶπαν
τίς ὄψεται αὐτούς

7 ἐξηρεύνησαν ἀνομίας ἐξέλιπον ἐξερευνῶντες ἐξερευνήσει προσελεύσεται
ἄνθρωπος καὶ καρδία βαθεῖα

8 καὶ ὑψωθήσεται ὁ θεός βέλος νηπίων ἐγενήθησαν αἱ πληγαὶ αὐτῶν

9 καὶ ἐξησθένησαν ἐπ᾽ αὐτοὺς αἱ γλῶσσαι αὐτῶν ἐταράχθησαν πάντες οἱ
θεωροῦντες αὐτούς

10 καὶ ἐφοβήθη πᾶς ἄνθρωπος καὶ ἀνήγγειλαν τὰ ἔργα τοῦ θεοῦ καὶ τὰ
ποιήματα αὐτοῦ συνῆκαν

11 εὐφρανθήσεται δίκαιος ἐπὶ τῷ κυρίῳ καὶ ἐλπιεῖ ἐπ᾽ αὐτόν καὶ
ἐπαινεσθήσονται πάντες οἱ εὐθεῖς τῇ καρδίᾳ

[Vul](../vul/psa063)

1 In finem. Psalmus David.

2 \[Exaudi, Deus, orationem meam cum deprecor;  
a timore inimici eripe animam meam.  

3 Protexisti me a conventu malignantium,  
a multitudine operantium iniquitatem.  

4 Quia exacuerunt ut gladium linguas suas;  
intenderunt arcum rem amaram,  

5 ut sagittent in occultis immaculatum.  

6 Subito sagittabunt eum, et non timebunt;  
firmaverunt sibi sermonem nequam.  
Narraverunt ut absconderent laqueos;  
dixerunt: Quis videbit eos?  

7 Scrutati sunt iniquitates;  
defecerunt scrutantes scrutinio.  
Accedet homo ad cor altum,  

8 et exaltabitur Deus.  
Sagittæ parvulorum factæ sunt plagæ eorum,  

9 et infirmatæ sunt contra eos linguæ eorum.  
Conturbati sunt omnes qui videbant eos,  

10 et timuit omnis homo.  
Et annuntiaverunt opera Dei,  
et facta ejus intellexerunt.  

11 Lætabitur justus in Domino, et sperabit in eo,  
et laudabuntur omnes recti corde.\]
