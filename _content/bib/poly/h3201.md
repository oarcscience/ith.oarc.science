H3201  
יכול יכל  
יָכוֹל יָכוֹל ‎ yâkôl yâkôl  
*yaw-kole‘,* *yaw-kole‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* *able*, literally (*can*, *could*) or morally
(*may*, *might*): - be able, any at all (ways), attain, can (away with,
\[-not\]), could, endure, might, overcome, have power, prevail, still,
suffer.
