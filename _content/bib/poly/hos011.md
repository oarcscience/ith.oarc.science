### Hosea 11

[KJV](../kjv/hos011)

1  When<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> was a
child,<sup>[5288](h5288)</sup> then I loved<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> him,
and called<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> my son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> out of
Egypt.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4714](h4714)</sup>

2  As they called<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> them,
so<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> they went<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> them: they
sacrificed<sup>[2076](h2076)</sup> unto Baalim,<sup>[1168](h1168)</sup>
and burned incense<sup>[6999](h6999)</sup> to graven
images.<sup>[6456](h6456)</sup>

3  I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup> taught Ephraim also to
go,<sup>[8637](h8637)</sup> <sup>[669](h0669)</sup>
taking<sup>[3947](h3947)</sup> them by<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> their
arms;<sup>[2220](h2220)</sup> but they knew<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I
healed<sup>[7495](h7495)</sup> them.

4  I drew<sup>[4900](h4900)</sup> them with
cords<sup>[2256](h2256)</sup> of a man,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> with
bands<sup>[5688](h5688)</sup> of love:<sup>[160](h0160)</sup> and I
was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> to them as they that take
off<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> the yoke<sup>[5923](h5923)</sup>
on<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> their jaws,<sup>[3895](h3895)</sup> and I
laid<sup>[5186](h5186)</sup> meat<sup>[398](h0398)</sup>
unto<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> them.

5  He shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
into<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the land<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of
Egypt,<sup>[4714](h4714)</sup> but the Assyrian<sup>[804](h0804)</sup>
shall be his king,<sup>[4428](h4428)</sup>
because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> they refused<sup>[3985](h3985)</sup> to
return.<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>

6  And the sword<sup>[2719](h2719)</sup> shall
abide<sup>[2342](h2342)</sup> on his cities,<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> and
shall consume<sup>[3615](h3615)</sup> his
branches,<sup>[905](h0905)</sup> and devour<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> them,
because of their own counsels.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4156](h4156)</sup>

7  And my people<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> are
bent<sup>[8511](h8511)</sup> to backsliding<sup>[4878](h4878)</sup> from
me: though they called<sup>[7121](h7121)</sup> them
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the most High,<sup>[5920](h5920)</sup> none at
all<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> <sup>[3162](h3162)</sup> would
exalt<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> him.

8  How<sup>[349](h0349)</sup> shall I give thee
up,<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> Ephraim?<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> how shall I
deliver<sup>[4042](h4042)</sup> thee, Israel?<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>
how<sup>[349](h0349)</sup> shall I make<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> thee as
Admah?<sup>[126](h0126)</sup> how shall I set<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup>
thee as Zeboim?<sup>[6636](h6636)</sup> mine
heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> is turned<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup>
within<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me, my repentings<sup>[5150](h5150)</sup>
are kindled<sup>[3648](h3648)</sup> together.<sup>[3162](h3162)</sup>

9  I will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> execute<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
the fierceness<sup>[2740](h2740)</sup> of mine
anger,<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> I will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
return<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> to destroy<sup>[7843](h7843)</sup>
Ephraim:<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> for<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup> am God,<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> and
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> man;<sup>[376](h0376)</sup> the Holy
One<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup> in the midst<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> of
thee: and I will not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
enter<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> into the city.<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup>

10  They shall walk<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> after<sup>[310](h0310)</sup>
the LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> he shall roar<sup>[7580](h7580)</sup>
like a lion:<sup>[738](h0738)</sup> when<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
he<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> shall roar,<sup>[7580](h7580)</sup> then the
children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> shall tremble<sup>[2729](h2729)</sup>
from the west.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[3220](h3220)</sup>

11  They shall tremble<sup>[2729](h2729)</sup> as a
bird<sup>[6833](h6833)</sup> out of Egypt,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4714](h4714)</sup> and as a dove<sup>[3123](h3123)</sup> out of
the land<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[776](h0776)</sup> of
Assyria:<sup>[804](h0804)</sup> and I will place<sup>[3427](h3427)</sup>
them in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> their houses,<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup>
saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

12  Ephraim<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> compasseth me
about<sup>[5437](h5437)</sup> with lies,<sup>[3585](h3585)</sup> and the
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> of Israel<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> with
deceit:<sup>[4820](h4820)</sup> but Judah<sup>[3063](h3063)</sup>
yet<sup>[5750](h5750)</sup> ruleth<sup>[7300](h7300)</sup>
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> God,<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> and is
faithful<sup>[539](h0539)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> the
saints.<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/hos011)

1 διότι νήπιος Ισραηλ καὶ ἐγὼ ἠγάπησα αὐτὸν καὶ ἐξ Αἰγύπτου μετεκάλεσα
τὰ τέκνα αὐτοῦ

2 καθὼς μετεκάλεσα αὐτούς οὕτως ἀπῴχοντο ἐκ προσώπου μου αὐτοὶ τοῖς
Βααλιμ ἔθυον καὶ τοῖς γλυπτοῖς ἐθυμίων

3 καὶ ἐγὼ συνεπόδισα τὸν Εφραιμ ἀνέλαβον αὐτὸν ἐπὶ τὸν βραχίονά μου καὶ
οὐκ ἔγνωσαν ὅτι ἴαμαι αὐτούς

4 ἐν διαφθορᾷ ἀνθρώπων ἐξέτεινα αὐτοὺς ἐν δεσμοῖς ἀγαπήσεώς μου καὶ
ἔσομαι αὐτοῖς ὡς ῥαπίζων ἄνθρωπος ἐπὶ τὰς σιαγόνας αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐπιβλέψομαι
πρὸς αὐτόν δυνήσομαι αὐτῷ

5 κατῴκησεν Εφραιμ ἐν Αἰγύπτῳ καὶ Ασσουρ αὐτὸς βασιλεὺς αὐτοῦ ὅτι οὐκ
ἠθέλησεν ἐπιστρέψαι

6 καὶ ἠσθένησεν ῥομφαία ἐν ταῖς πόλεσιν αὐτοῦ καὶ κατέπαυσεν ἐν ταῖς
χερσὶν αὐτοῦ καὶ φάγονται ἐκ τῶν διαβουλίων αὐτῶν

7 καὶ ὁ λαὸς αὐτοῦ ἐπικρεμάμενος ἐκ τῆς κατοικίας αὐτοῦ καὶ ὁ θεὸς ἐπὶ
τὰ τίμια αὐτοῦ θυμωθήσεται καὶ οὐ μὴ ὑψώσῃ αὐτόν

8 τί σε διαθῶ Εφραιμ ὑπερασπιῶ σου Ισραηλ τί σε διαθῶ ὡς Αδαμα θήσομαί
σε καὶ ὡς Σεβωιμ μετεστράφη ἡ καρδία μου ἐν τῷ αὐτῷ συνεταράχθη ἡ
μεταμέλειά μου

9 οὐ μὴ ποιήσω κατὰ τὴν ὀργὴν τοῦ θυμοῦ μου οὐ μὴ ἐγκαταλίπω τοῦ
ἐξαλειφθῆναι τὸν Εφραιμ διότι θεὸς ἐγώ εἰμι καὶ οὐκ ἄνθρωπος ἐν σοὶ
ἅγιος καὶ οὐκ εἰσελεύσομαι εἰς πόλιν

10 ὀπίσω κυρίου πορεύσομαι ὡς λέων ἐρεύξεται ὅτι αὐτὸς ὠρύσεται καὶ
ἐκστήσονται τέκνα ὑδάτων

11 καὶ ἐκστήσονται ὡς ὄρνεον ἐξ Αἰγύπτου καὶ ὡς περιστερὰ ἐκ γῆς
Ἀσσυρίων καὶ ἀποκαταστήσω αὐτοὺς εἰς τοὺς οἴκους αὐτῶν λέγει κύριος

[Vul](../vul/hos011)

1 \[Sicut mane transiit, pertransiit rex Israël.  
Quia puer Israël, et dilexi eum;  
et ex Ægypto vocavi filium meum.  

2 Vocaverunt eos, sic abierunt a facie eorum;  
Baalim immolabant, et simulacris sacrificabant.  

3 Et ego quasi nutritius Ephraim:  
portabam eos in brachiis meis,  
et nescierunt quod curarem eos.  

4 In funiculis Adam traham eos,  
in vinculis caritatis;  
et ero eis quasi exaltans jugum super maxillas eorum,  
et declinavi ad eum ut vesceretur.  

5 Non revertetur in terram Ægypti, et Assur ipse rex ejus,  
quoniam noluerunt converti.  

6 Cœpit gladius in civitatibus ejus,  
et consumet electos ejus,  
et comedet capita eorum.  

7 Et populus meus pendebit ad reditum meum;  
jugum autem imponetur eis simul, quod non auferetur.  

8 Quomodo dabo te, Ephraim?  
protegam te, Israël?  
Quomodo dabo te sicut Adama,  
ponam te ut Seboim?  
Conversum est in me cor meum,  
pariter conturbata est pœnitudo mea.  

9 Non faciam furorem iræ meæ;  
non convertar ut disperdam Ephraim,  
quoniam Deus ego, et non homo;  
in medio tui sanctus,  
et non ingrediar civitatem.  

10 Post Dominum ambulabunt;  
quasi leo rugiet,  
quia ipse rugiet,  
et formidabunt filii maris.  

11 Et avolabunt quasi avis ex Ægypto,  
et quasi columba de terra Assyriorum:  
et collocabo eos in domibus suis, dicit Dominus.\]  

12 \[Circumdedit me in negatione Ephraim,  
et in dolo domus Israël;  
Judas autem testis descendit cum Deo,  
et cum sanctis fidelis.\]
