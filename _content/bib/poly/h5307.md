H5307  
נפל  
נָפַל ‎ nâphal  
*naw-fal‘*  
A primitive root; to *fall*, in a great variety of applications
(intransitively or causatively, literally or figuratively): - be
accepted, cast (down, self, \[lots\], out), cease, die, divide (by lot),
(let) fail, (cause to, let, make, ready to) fall (away, down, -en,
-ing), fell (-ing), fugitive, have \[inheritamce\], inferior, be judged
\[by mistake for [6419](h6419) , lay (along), (cause to) lie down, light
(down), be (X hast) lost, lying, overthrow, overwhelm, perish, present
(-ed, -ing), (make to) rot, slay, smite out, X surely, throw down.
