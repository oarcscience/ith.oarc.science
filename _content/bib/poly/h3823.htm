H3823
לבב
לָבַב ‎ lâbab
law-bab‘
A primitive root; properly to be enclosed (as if with fat); by implication (as denominative from 3824 to unheart, that is, (in a good sense) transport (with love), or (in a bad sense) stultify; also (as denominative from 3834 to make cakes: - make cakes, ravish, be wise.
