H7936  
סכר שׂכר  
שָׂכַר סָכַר ‎ śâkar sâkar  
*saw-kar‘,* *saw-kar‘*  
The second form by permutation and used in [Ezr 4:5](ezr004.htm#005); a
primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to [3739](h3739) through
the idea of temporary *purchase*; compare [7937](h7937) ; to *hire: -*
earn wages, hire (out self), reward, X surely.
