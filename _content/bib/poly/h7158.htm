H7158
קרית ספר קרית סנּה
קִריַת סַנָּה קִריַת סֵפֶר ‎ qiryath sannâh qiryath sêpher
keer-yath‘ san-naw‘, keer-yath‘ say‘-fer
From 7151 and a simpler feminine from the same as 5577 or (for the second form) 5612 city of branches, or of a book; Kirjath Sannah or Kirjath Sepher, a place in Palestine: - Kirjath-sannah, Kirjath-sepher.
