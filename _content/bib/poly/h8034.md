H8034  
שׁם  
שֵׁם ‎ shêm  
*shame*  
A primitive word (perhaps rather from [7760](h7760) through the idea of
definite and conspicuous *position*; compare [8064](h8064) ; an
*appellation*, as a mark or memorial of individuality; by implication
*honor*, *authority*, *character: -* + base, \[in-\] fame \[-ous\], name
(-d), renown, report.
