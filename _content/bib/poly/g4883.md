G4883  
συναρμολογέω  
sunarmologeō  
*soon-ar-mol-og-eh‘-o*  
From [4862](g4862) and a derivative of a compound of [719](g0719) and
[3004](g3004) (in its original sense of *laying*); to *render* *close*
*jointed* *together*, that is, *organize* *compactly:* - be fitly framed
(joined) together.
