זה
זֶה ‎ zeh
zeh
A primitive word; the masculine demonstrative pronoun, this or that: - he, X hence, X here, it (-self), X now, X of him, the one . . . the other, X than the other, (X out of) the (self) same, such (an one) that, these, this (hath, man), on this side . . . on that side, X thus, very, which.
