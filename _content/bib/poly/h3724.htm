H3724
כּפר
כּוֹפֶר ‎ kôpher
ko‘-fer
From 3722 properly a cover, that is, (literally) a village (as covered in); (specifically) bitumen (as used for coating), and the henna plant (as used for dyeing); figuratively a redemption price: - bribe, camphire, pitch, ransom, satisfaction, sum of money, village.
