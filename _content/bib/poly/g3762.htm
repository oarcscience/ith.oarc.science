G3762
οὐδείς
oudeis
oo-dice‘
Including the feminine οὐδεμία oudemia oo-dem-ee‘-ah and the neuter οὐδέν ouden oo-den‘ From 3761 and 1520 not even one (man, woman or thing), that is, none, nobody, nothing: - any (man), aught, man, neither any (thing), never (man), no (man), none (+ of these things), not (any, at all, -thing), nought.
