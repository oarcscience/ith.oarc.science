אשּׁר אשּׁוּר
אַשּׁוּר אַשּׁוּר ‎ ‘ashshûr ‘ashshûr
ash-shoor‘, ash-shoor‘
Apparently from 833 (in the sense of successful); Ashshur, the second son of Shem; also his descendants and the country occupied by them (that is, Assyria), its region and its empire: - Asshur, Assur, Assyria, Assyrians. See 838
