H5676  
עבר  
עֵבֶר ‎ ‛êber  
*ay‘-ber*  
From [5674](h5674) properly a region *across*; but used only adverbially
(with or without a preposition) on the *opposite* side (especially of
the Jordan; usually meaning the *east*): - X against, beyond, by, X
from, over, passage, quarter, (other, this) side, straight.
