G3741
ὅσιος
hosios
hos‘-ee-os
Of uncertain affinity; properly right (by intrinsic or divine character; thus distinguished from 1342 which refers rather to human statutes and relations; from 2413 which denotes formal consecration; and from 40 which relates to purity from defilement), that is, hallowed (pious, sacred, sure): - holy, mercy, shalt be.
