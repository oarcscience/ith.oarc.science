H7462  
רעה  
רָעָה ‎ râ‛âh  
*raw-aw‘*  
A primitive root; to *tend* a flock, that is, *pasture* it;
intransitively to *graze* (literally or figuratively); generally to
*rule*; by extension to *associate* with (as a friend): - X break,
companion, keep company with, devour, eat up, evil entreat, feed, use as
a friend, make friendship with, herdman, keep \[sheep\] (-er), pastor, +
shearing house, shepherd, wander, waste.
