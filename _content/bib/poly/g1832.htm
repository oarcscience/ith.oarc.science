G1832
ἔξεστι
exesti
ex‘-es-tee
Third person singular present indicative of a compound of 1537 and 1510 so also ἐξόν exon ; neuter present participle of the same (with or without some form of 1510 expressed); impersonally it is right (through the figurative idea of being out in public): - be lawful, let, X may (-est).
