H3547  
כּהן  
כָּהַן ‎ kâhan  
*kaw-han‘*  
A primitive root, apparently meaning to *mediate* in religious services;
but used only as denominative from [3548](h3548) to *officiate* as a
priest; figuratively to *put* *on* *regalia: -* deck, be (do the office
of a, execute the, minister in the) priest (‘s office).
