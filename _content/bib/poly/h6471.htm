פּעמה פּעם
פַּעַם פַּעֲמָה ‎ pa‛am pa‛ămâh
pah‘-am, pah-am-aw‘
From 6470 a stroke, literally or figuratively (in various applications): - anvil, corner, foot (-step), going, [hundred-] fold, X now, (this) + once, order, rank, step, + thrice, [often-], second, this, two) time (-s), twice, wheel.
