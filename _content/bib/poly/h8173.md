H8173  
שׁעע  
שָׁעַע ‎ shâ‛a‛  
*shaw-ah‘*  
A primitive root; (in a good acceptation) to *look* upon (with
complacency), that is, *fondle*, *please* or *amuse* (self); (in a bad
one) to *look* about (in dismay), that is, *stare*. (*cry* *out* is by
confusion with [7768](h7768) ): - cry (out) \[by confusion with
[7768](h7768) , dandle, delight (self), play, shut.
