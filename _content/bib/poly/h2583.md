H2583  
חנה  
חָנָה ‎ chânâh  
*khaw-naw‘*  
A primitive root (compare [2603](h2603) ; properly to *incline*; by
implication to *decline* (of the slanting rays of evening); specifically
to *pitch* a tent; generally to *encamp* (for abode or siege): - abide
(in tents), camp, dwell, encamp, grow to an end, lie, pitch (tent), rest
in tent.
