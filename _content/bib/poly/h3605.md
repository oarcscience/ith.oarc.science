H3605  
כּול כּל <sup>o</sup>  
כּוֹל כּוֹל ‎ kôl kôl  
*kole,* *kole*  
From [3634](h3634) properly the *whole*; hence *all*, *any* or *every*
(in the singular only, but often in a plural sense): - (in) all (manner,
\[ye\]), altogether, any (manner), enough, every (one, place, thing),
howsoever, as many as, \[no-\] thing, ought, whatsoever, (the) whole,
whoso (-ever).
