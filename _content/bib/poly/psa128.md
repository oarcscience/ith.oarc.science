### Psalms 128

[KJV](../kjv/psa128)

1  A Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> of degrees.<sup>[4609](h4609)</sup>
Blessed<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> is every one<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> that
feareth<sup>[3373](h3373)</sup> the LORD;<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> that
walketh<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> in his ways.<sup>[1870](h1870)</sup>

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou shalt eat<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> the
labor<sup>[3018](h3018)</sup> of thine hands:<sup>[3709](h3709)</sup>
happy<sup>[835](h0835)</sup> shalt thou be, and it shall be
well<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> with thee.

3  Thy wife<sup>[802](h0802)</sup> shall be as a
fruitful<sup>[6509](h6509)</sup> vine<sup>[1612](h1612)</sup> by the
sides<sup>[3411](h3411)</sup> of thine house:<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup>
thy children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> like olive<sup>[2132](h2132)</sup>
plants<sup>[8363](h8363)</sup> round about<sup>[5439](h5439)</sup> thy
table.<sup>[7979](h7979)</sup>

4  Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
thus<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> shall the man<sup>[1397](h1397)</sup> be
blessed<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup> that feareth<sup>[3373](h3373)</sup> the
LORD.<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>

5  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> shall bless<sup>[1288](h1288)</sup>
thee out of Zion:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[6726](h6726)</sup> and
thou shalt see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> the good<sup>[2898](h2898)</sup>
of Jerusalem<sup>[3389](h3389)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the
days<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of thy life.<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup>

6  Yea, thou shalt see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> thy
children's<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> children,<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> and
peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa128)

1 ᾠδὴ τῶν ἀναβαθμῶν πλεονάκις ἐπολέμησάν με ἐκ νεότητός μου εἰπάτω δὴ
Ισραηλ

2 πλεονάκις ἐπολέμησάν με ἐκ νεότητός μου καὶ γὰρ οὐκ ἠδυνήθησάν μοι

3 ἐπὶ τοῦ νώτου μου ἐτέκταινον οἱ ἁμαρτωλοί ἐμάκρυναν τὴν ἀνομίαν αὐτῶν

4 κύριος δίκαιος συνέκοψεν αὐχένας ἁμαρτωλῶν

5 αἰσχυνθήτωσαν καὶ ἀποστραφήτωσαν εἰς τὰ ὀπίσω πάντες οἱ μισοῦντες Σιων

6 γενηθήτωσαν ὡς χόρτος δωμάτων ὃς πρὸ τοῦ ἐκσπασθῆναι ἐξηράνθη

7 οὗ οὐκ ἐπλήρωσεν τὴν χεῖρα αὐτοῦ ὁ θερίζων καὶ τὸν κόλπον αὐτοῦ ὁ τὰ
δράγματα συλλέγων

8 καὶ οὐκ εἶπαν οἱ παράγοντες εὐλογία κυρίου ἐφ᾽ ὑμᾶς εὐλογήκαμεν ὑμᾶς
ἐν ὀνόματι κυρίου

[Vul](../vul/psa128)

1 Canticum graduum. \[Sæpe expugnaverunt me a juventute mea,  
dicat nunc Israël;  

2 sæpe expugnaverunt me a juventute mea:  
etenim non potuerunt mihi.  

3 Supra dorsum meum fabricaverunt peccatores;  
prolongaverunt iniquitatem suam.  

4 Dominus justus  
concidit cervices peccatorum.  

5 Confundantur, et convertantur retrorsum  
omnes qui oderunt Sion.  

6 Fiant sicut fœnum tectorum,  
quod priusquam evellatur exaruit:  

7 de quo non implevit manum suam qui metit,  
et sinum suum qui manipulos colligit.  

8 Et non dixerunt qui præteribant:  
Benedictio Domini super vos.  
Benediximus vobis in nomine Domini.\]
