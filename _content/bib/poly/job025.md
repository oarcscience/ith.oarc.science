### Job 25

[KJV](../kjv/job025)

1  Then answered<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> Bildad<sup>[1085](h1085)</sup>
the Shuhite,<sup>[7747](h7747)</sup> and said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>

2  Dominion<sup>[4910](h4910)</sup> and fear<sup>[6343](h6343)</sup> are
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> him, he maketh<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup>
peace<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup> in his high
places.<sup>[4791](h4791)</sup>

3  Is there<sup>[3426](h3426)</sup> any number<sup>[4557](h4557)</sup>
of his armies?<sup>[1416](h1416)</sup> and upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
whom<sup>[4310](h4310)</sup> doth not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> his
light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> arise?<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup>

4  How<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> then can man<sup>[582](h0582)</sup> be
justified<sup>[6663](h6663)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup>
God?<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> or how<sup>[4100](h4100)</sup> can he be
clean<sup>[2135](h2135)</sup> that is born<sup>[3205](h3205)</sup> of a
woman?<sup>[802](h0802)</sup>

5  Behold<sup>[2005](h2005)</sup> even<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> to the
moon,<sup>[3394](h3394)</sup> and it shineth<sup>[166](h0166)</sup>
not;<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> yea, the stars<sup>[3556](h3556)</sup> are
not pure<sup>[2141](h2141)</sup> <sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> in his
sight.<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup>

6  How much less<sup>[637](h0637)</sup> <sup>[3588](h3588)</sup>
man,<sup>[582](h0582)</sup> that is a worm?<sup>[7415](h7415)</sup> and
the son<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of man,<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> which is
a worm?<sup>[8438](h8438)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/job025)

1 ὑπολαβὼν δὲ Βαλδαδ ὁ Σαυχίτης λέγει

2 τί γὰρ προοίμιον ἢ φόβος παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ ὁ ποιῶν τὴν σύμπασαν ἐν ὑψίστῳ

3 μὴ γάρ τις ὑπολάβοι ὅτι ἔστιν παρέλκυσις πειραταῖς ἐπὶ τίνας δὲ οὐκ
ἐπελεύσεται ἔνεδρα παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ

4 πῶς γὰρ ἔσται δίκαιος βροτὸς ἔναντι κυρίου ἢ τίς ἂν ἀποκαθαρίσαι
ἑαυτὸν γεννητὸς γυναικός

5 εἰ σελήνῃ συντάσσει καὶ οὐκ ἐπιφαύσκει ἄστρα δὲ οὐ καθαρὰ ἐναντίον
αὐτοῦ

6 ἔα δέ ἄνθρωπος σαπρία καὶ υἱὸς ἀνθρώπου σκώληξ

[Vul](../vul/job025)

1 Respondens autem Baldad Suhites, dixit:

2 \[Potestas et terror apud eum est,  
qui facit concordiam in sublimibus suis.  

3 Numquid est numerus militum ejus?  
et super quem non surget lumen illius?  

4 Numquid justificari potest homo comparatus Deo?  
aut apparere mundus natus de muliere?  

5 Ecce luna etiam non splendet,  
et stellæ non sunt mundæ in conspectu ejus:  

6 quanto magis homo putredo,  
et filius hominis vermis?\]
