H4339
מישׁר
מֵישָׁר ‎ mêyshâr
may-shawr‘
From 3474 evenness, that is, (figuratively) prosperity or concord; also straightness, that is, (figuratively) rectitude (only in plural with singular sense; often adverbially): - agreement, aright, that are equal, equity, (things that are) right (-eously, things), sweetly, upright (-ly, -ness).
