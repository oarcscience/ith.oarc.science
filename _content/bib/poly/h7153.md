H7153  
קרית הארבּע קרית ארבּע  
קִריַת אַרבַּע קִריַת הָאַרבַּע ‎ qiryath ‘arba‛ qiryath hâ‘arba‛  
*(keer-yath‘)* *ar-bah‘,* *haw-ar-bah‘*  
The second form, used in [Neh 11:25](neh011.htm#025), has the article
interposed; from [7151](h7151) and [704](h0704) or [702](h0702) *city*
*of* *Arba*, or *city* *of* *the* *four* (giants); *Kirjath-Arba* or
*Kirjath-ha-Arba*, a place in Palestine: - Kirjath-arba.
