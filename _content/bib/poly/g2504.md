*kag-o‘,* *kam-oy‘,* *kam-eh‘*  
So also the dative (second form) and accusative (third form); from
[2532](g2532) and [1473](g1473) *and* (or *also*, *even*, etc.) I,
(*to*) *me:* - (and, even, even so, so) I (also, in like wise), both me,
me also.
