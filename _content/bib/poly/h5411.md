H5411  
נתוּן נתין <sup>o</sup>  
נָתִין נָתוּן ‎ nâthı̂yn nâthûn  
*naw-theen‘,* *naw-thoon‘*  
The second form is the proper form, as passive participle; from
[5414](h5414) one *given*, that is, (in the plural only) the *Nethinim*,
or Temple Servants (as *given* up to that duty): - Nethinims.
