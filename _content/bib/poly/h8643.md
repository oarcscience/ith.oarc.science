H8643  
תּרוּעה  
תְּּרוּעָה ‎ t<sup>e</sup>rû‛âh  
*ter-oo-aw‘*  
From [7321](h7321) *clamor*, that is, *acclamation* of joy or a *battle*
*cry*; especially *clangor* of trumpets, as an *alarum: -* alarm, blow
(-ing) (of, the) (trumpets), joy, jubile, loud noise, rejoicing, shout
(-ing), (high, joyful) sound (-ing).
