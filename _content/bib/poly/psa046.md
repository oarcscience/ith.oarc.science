### Psalms 46

[KJV](../kjv/psa046)

1  To the chief Musician<sup>[5329](h5329)</sup> for the
sons<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of Korah,<sup>[7141](h7141)</sup> A
Song<sup>[7892](h7892)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
Alamoth.<sup>[5961](h5961)</sup> God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> is our
refuge<sup>[4268](h4268)</sup> and strength,<sup>[5797](h5797)</sup> a
very<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> present<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup>
help<sup>[5833](h5833)</sup> in trouble.<sup>[6869](h6869)</sup>

2  Therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> will
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> we fear,<sup>[3372](h3372)</sup> though the
earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> be removed,<sup>[4171](h4171)</sup> and
though the mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> be
carried<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> into the midst<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>
of the sea;<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup>

3  Though the waters<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> thereof
roar<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup> and be troubled,<sup>[2560](h2560)</sup>
though the mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup>
shake<sup>[7493](h7493)</sup> with the swelling<sup>[1346](h1346)</sup>
thereof. Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

4  There is a river,<sup>[5104](h5104)</sup> the
streams<sup>[6388](h6388)</sup> whereof shall make
glad<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> the city<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> of
God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> the holy<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup> place of
the tabernacles<sup>[4908](h4908)</sup> of the most
High.<sup>[5945](h5945)</sup>

5  God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> is in the midst<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> of
her; she shall not<sup>[1077](h1077)</sup> be
moved:<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> shall
help<sup>[5826](h5826)</sup> her, and that right<sup>[6437](h6437)</sup>
early.<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup>

6  The heathen<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> raged,<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup>
the kingdoms<sup>[4467](h4467)</sup> were moved:<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup>
he uttered<sup>[5414](h5414)</sup> his voice,<sup>[6963](h6963)</sup>
the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> melted.<sup>[4127](h4127)</sup>

7  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of hosts<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> is
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> us; the God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> is our refuge.<sup>[4869](h4869)</sup>
Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

8  Come,<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> behold<sup>[2372](h2372)</sup> the
works<sup>[4659](h4659)</sup> of the LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
what<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> desolations<sup>[8047](h8047)</sup> he hath
made<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> in the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

9  He maketh wars<sup>[4421](h4421)</sup> to
cease<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> unto<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> the
end<sup>[7097](h7097)</sup> of the earth;<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> he
breaketh<sup>[7665](h7665)</sup> the bow,<sup>[7198](h7198)</sup> and
cutteth<sup>[7112](h7112)</sup> the spear<sup>[2595](h2595)</sup> in
sunder; he burneth<sup>[8313](h8313)</sup> the
chariot<sup>[5699](h5699)</sup> in the fire.<sup>[784](h0784)</sup>

10  Be still,<sup>[7503](h7503)</sup> and know<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup>
that<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[595](h0595)</sup> am
God:<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> I will be exalted<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup>
among the heathen,<sup>[1471](h1471)</sup> I will be
exalted<sup>[7311](h7311)</sup> in the earth.<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

11  The LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of hosts<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup> is
with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> us; the God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of
Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> is our refuge.<sup>[4869](h4869)</sup>
Selah.<sup>[5542](h5542)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa046)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ τῶν υἱῶν Κορε ψαλμός

2 πάντα τὰ ἔθνη κροτήσατε χεῖρας ἀλαλάξατε τῷ θεῷ ἐν φωνῇ ἀγαλλιάσεως

3 ὅτι κύριος ὕψιστος φοβερός βασιλεὺς μέγας ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν γῆν

4 ὑπέταξεν λαοὺς ἡμῖν καὶ ἔθνη ὑπὸ τοὺς πόδας ἡμῶν

5 ἐξελέξατο ἡμῖν τὴν κληρονομίαν αὐτοῦ τὴν καλλονὴν Ιακωβ ἣν ἠγάπησεν
διάψαλμα

6 ἀνέβη ὁ θεὸς ἐν ἀλαλαγμῷ κύριος ἐν φωνῇ σάλπιγγος

7 ψάλατε τῷ θεῷ ἡμῶν ψάλατε ψάλατε τῷ βασιλεῖ ἡμῶν ψάλατε

8 ὅτι βασιλεὺς πάσης τῆς γῆς ὁ θεός ψάλατε συνετῶς

9 ἐβασίλευσεν ὁ θεὸς ἐπὶ τὰ ἔθνη ὁ θεὸς κάθηται ἐπὶ θρόνου ἁγίου αὐτοῦ

10 ἄρχοντες λαῶν συνήχθησαν μετὰ τοῦ θεοῦ Αβρααμ ὅτι τοῦ θεοῦ οἱ
κραταιοὶ τῆς γῆς σφόδρα ἐπήρθησαν

1 In finem, pro filiis Core. Psalmus.

2 \[Omnes gentes, plaudite manibus;  
jubilate Deo in voce exsultationis:  

3 quoniam Dominus excelsus, terribilis,  
rex magnus super omnem terram.  

4 Subjecit populos nobis,  
et gentes sub pedibus nostris.  

5 Elegit nobis hæreditatem suam;  
speciem Jacob quam dilexit.  

6 Ascendit Deus in jubilo,  
et Dominus in voce tubæ.  

7 Psallite Deo nostro, psallite;  
psallite regi nostro, psallite:  

8 quoniam rex omnis terræ Deus,  
psallite sapienter.  

9 Regnabit Deus super gentes;  
Deus sedet super sedem sanctam suam.  

10 Principes populorum congregati sunt cum Deo Abraham,  
quoniam dii fortes terræ vehementer elevati sunt.\]
