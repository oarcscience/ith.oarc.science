G4766
στρώννυμι
strōnnumi
strone‘-noo-mee
Or a simpler form στρωννύω strōnnuō strone‘-noo‘-o , prolonged from a still simpler form στρόω stroō stro‘-o (used only as an alternate in certain tenses; probably akin to 4731 through the idea of positing); to “strew”, that is, spread (as a carpet or couch): - make bed, furnish, spread, strew.
