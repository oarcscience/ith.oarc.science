H1219
בּצר
בָּצַר ‎ bâtsar
baw-tsar‘
A primitive root; to clip off; specifically (as denominative from 1210 to gather grapes; also to be isolated (that is, inaccessible by height or fortification): - cut off, (de-) fenced, fortify, (grape) gather (-er), mighty things, restrain, strong, wall (up), withhold.
