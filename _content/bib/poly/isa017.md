### Isaiah 17

[KJV](../kjv/isa017)

1  The burden<sup>[4853](h4853)</sup> of
Damascus.<sup>[1834](h1834)</sup> Behold,<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup>
Damascus<sup>[1834](h1834)</sup> is taken away<sup>[5493](h5493)</sup>
from being a city,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> and
it shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> a ruinous<sup>[4654](h4654)</sup>
heap.<sup>[4596](h4596)</sup>

2  The cities<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup> of Aroer<sup>[6177](h6177)</sup>
are forsaken:<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> they shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> for flocks,<sup>[5739](h5739)</sup> which
shall lie down,<sup>[7257](h7257)</sup> and none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup>
shall make them afraid.<sup>[2729](h2729)</sup>

3  The fortress<sup>[4013](h4013)</sup> also shall
cease<sup>[7673](h7673)</sup> from Ephraim,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[669](h0669)</sup> and the kingdom<sup>[4467](h4467)</sup> from
Damascus,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[1834](h1834)</sup> and the
remnant<sup>[7605](h7605)</sup> of Syria:<sup>[758](h0758)</sup> they
shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as the glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> of
the children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of Israel,<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>
saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> of
hosts.<sup>[6635](h6635)</sup>

4  And in that<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> it
shall come to pass,<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> that the
glory<sup>[3519](h3519)</sup> of Jacob<sup>[3290](h3290)</sup> shall be
made thin,<sup>[1809](h1809)</sup> and the
fatness<sup>[4924](h4924)</sup> of his flesh<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup>
shall wax lean.<sup>[7329](h7329)</sup>

5  And it shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as when the harvest
man<sup>[7105](h7105)</sup> gathereth<sup>[622](h0622)</sup> the
corn,<sup>[7054](h7054)</sup> and reapeth<sup>[7114](h7114)</sup> the
ears<sup>[7641](h7641)</sup> with his arm;<sup>[2220](h2220)</sup> and
it shall be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as he that
gathereth<sup>[3950](h3950)</sup> ears<sup>[7641](h7641)</sup> in the
valley<sup>[6010](h6010)</sup> of Rephaim.<sup>[7497](h7497)</sup>

6  Yet gleaning grapes<sup>[5955](h5955)</sup> shall be
left<sup>[7604](h7604)</sup> in it, as the
shaking<sup>[5363](h5363)</sup> of an olive
tree,<sup>[2132](h2132)</sup> two<sup>[8147](h8147)</sup> or
three<sup>[7969](h7969)</sup> berries<sup>[1620](h1620)</sup> in the
top<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> of the uppermost
bough,<sup>[534](h0534)</sup> four<sup>[702](h0702)</sup> or
five<sup>[2568](h2568)</sup> in the outermost
fruitful<sup>[6509](h6509)</sup> branches<sup>[5585](h5585)</sup>
thereof, saith<sup>[5002](h5002)</sup> the LORD<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>

7  At that<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> shall a
man<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> look<sup>[8159](h8159)</sup>
to<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> his Maker,<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> and his
eyes<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> shall have respect<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the Holy One<sup>[6918](h6918)</sup> of
Israel.<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup>

8  And he shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> look<sup>[8159](h8159)</sup>
to<sup>[413](h0413)</sup> the altars,<sup>[4196](h4196)</sup> the
work<sup>[4639](h4639)</sup> of his hands,<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup>
neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> shall respect<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup>
that which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> his fingers<sup>[676](h0676)</sup>
have made,<sup>[6213](h6213)</sup> either the
groves,<sup>[842](h0842)</sup> or the images.<sup>[2553](h2553)</sup>

9  In that<sup>[1931](h1931)</sup> day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> shall his
strong<sup>[4581](h4581)</sup> cities<sup>[5892](h5892)</sup>
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as a forsaken<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup>
bough,<sup>[2793](h2793)</sup> and an uppermost
branch,<sup>[534](h0534)</sup> which<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> they
left<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> because<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of the children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> of
Israel:<sup>[3478](h3478)</sup> and there shall
be<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> desolation.<sup>[8077](h8077)</sup>

10  Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thou hast
forgotten<sup>[7911](h7911)</sup> the God<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> of thy
salvation,<sup>[3468](h3468)</sup> and hast not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
been mindful<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> of the rock<sup>[6697](h6697)</sup>
of thy strength,<sup>[4581](h4581)</sup>
therefore<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> <sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> shalt thou
plant<sup>[5193](h5193)</sup> pleasant<sup>[5282](h5282)</sup>
plants,<sup>[5194](h5194)</sup> and shalt set<sup>[2232](h2232)</sup> it
with strange<sup>[2114](h2114)</sup> slips:<sup>[2156](h2156)</sup>

11  In the day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> shalt thou make thy
plant<sup>[5194](h5194)</sup> to grow,<sup>[7735](h7735)</sup> and in
the morning<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup> shalt thou make thy
seed<sup>[2233](h2233)</sup> to flourish:<sup>[6524](h6524)</sup> but
the harvest<sup>[7105](h7105)</sup> shall be a
heap<sup>[5067](h5067)</sup> in the day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of
grief<sup>[2470](h2470)</sup> and of desperate<sup>[605](h0605)</sup>
sorrow.<sup>[3511](h3511)</sup>

12  Woe<sup>[1945](h1945)</sup> to the multitude<sup>[1995](h1995)</sup>
of many<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> people,<sup>[5971](h5971)</sup> which
make a noise<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup> like the
noise<sup>[1993](h1993)</sup> of the seas;<sup>[3220](h3220)</sup> and
to the rushing<sup>[7588](h7588)</sup> of
nations,<sup>[3816](h3816)</sup> that make a
rushing<sup>[7582](h7582)</sup> like the rushing<sup>[7588](h7588)</sup>
of mighty<sup>[3524](h3524)</sup> waters!<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup>

13  The nations<sup>[3816](h3816)</sup> shall
rush<sup>[7582](h7582)</sup> like the rushing<sup>[7588](h7588)</sup> of
many<sup>[7227](h7227)</sup> waters:<sup>[4325](h4325)</sup> but God
shall rebuke<sup>[1605](h1605)</sup> them, and they shall
flee<sup>[5127](h5127)</sup> far off,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[4801](h4801)</sup> and shall be chased<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> as
the chaff<sup>[4671](h4671)</sup> of the
mountains<sup>[2022](h2022)</sup> before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the
wind,<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> and like a rolling
thing<sup>[1534](h1534)</sup> before<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> the
whirlwind.<sup>[5492](h5492)</sup>

14  And behold<sup>[2009](h2009)</sup> at
eveningtide<sup>[6256](h6256)</sup> <sup>[6153](h6153)</sup>
trouble;<sup>[1091](h1091)</sup> and before<sup>[2962](h2962)</sup> the
morning<sup>[1242](h1242)</sup> he is not.<sup>[369](h0369)</sup>
This<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> is the portion<sup>[2506](h2506)</sup> of
them that spoil<sup>[8154](h8154)</sup> us, and the
lot<sup>[1486](h1486)</sup> of them that rob<sup>[962](h0962)</sup> us.

[Sep](../sep/isa017)

1 τὸ ῥῆμα τὸ κατὰ Δαμασκοῦ ἰδοὺ Δαμασκὸς ἀρθήσεται ἀπὸ πόλεων καὶ ἔσται
εἰς πτῶσιν

2 καταλελειμμένη εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα εἰς κοίτην ποιμνίων καὶ ἀνάπαυσιν καὶ οὐκ
ἔσται ὁ διώκων

3 καὶ οὐκέτι ἔσται ὀχυρὰ τοῦ καταφυγεῖν Εφραιμ καὶ οὐκέτι ἔσται βασιλεία
ἐν Δαμασκῷ καὶ τὸ λοιπὸν τῶν Σύρων ἀπολεῖται οὐ γὰρ σὺ βελτίων εἶ τῶν
υἱῶν Ισραηλ καὶ τῆς δόξης αὐτῶν τάδε λέγει κύριος σαβαωθ

4 ἔσται ἐν τῇ ἡμέρᾳ ἐκείνῃ ἔκλειψις τῆς δόξης Ιακωβ καὶ τὰ πίονα τῆς
δόξης αὐτοῦ σεισθήσεται

5 καὶ ἔσται ὃν τρόπον ἐάν τις συναγάγῃ ἀμητὸν ἑστηκότα καὶ σπέρμα
σταχύων ἐν τῷ βραχίονι αὐτοῦ ἀμήσῃ καὶ ἔσται ὃν τρόπον ἐάν τις συναγάγῃ
στάχυν ἐν φάραγγι στερεᾷ

6 καὶ καταλειφθῇ ἐν αὐτῇ καλάμη ἢ ὡς ῥῶγες ἐλαίας δύο ἢ τρεῖς ἐπ᾽ ἄκρου
μετεώρου ἢ τέσσαρες ἢ πέντε ἐπὶ τῶν κλάδων αὐτῶν καταλειφθῇ τάδε λέγει
κύριος ὁ θεὸς Ισραηλ

7 τῇ ἡμέρᾳ ἐκείνῃ πεποιθὼς ἔσται ἄνθρωπος ἐπὶ τῷ ποιήσαντι αὐτόν οἱ δὲ
ὀφθαλμοὶ αὐτοῦ εἰς τὸν ἅγιον τοῦ Ισραηλ ἐμβλέψονται

8 καὶ οὐ μὴ πεποιθότες ὦσιν ἐπὶ τοῖς βωμοῖς οὐδὲ ἐπὶ τοῖς ἔργοις τῶν
χειρῶν αὐτῶν ἃ ἐποίησαν οἱ δάκτυλοι αὐτῶν καὶ οὐκ ὄψονται τὰ δένδρα
αὐτῶν οὐδὲ τὰ βδελύγματα αὐτῶν

9 τῇ ἡμέρᾳ ἐκείνῃ ἔσονται αἱ πόλεις σου ἐγκαταλελειμμέναι ὃν τρόπον
ἐγκατέλιπον οἱ Αμορραῖοι καὶ οἱ Ευαῖοι ἀπὸ προσώπου τῶν υἱῶν Ισραηλ καὶ
ἔσονται ἔρημοι

10 διότι κατέλιπες τὸν θεὸν τὸν σωτῆρά σου καὶ κυρίου τοῦ βοηθοῦ σου οὐκ
ἐμνήσθης διὰ τοῦτο φυτεύσεις φύτευμα ἄπιστον καὶ σπέρμα ἄπιστον

11 τῇ δὲ ἡμέρᾳ ᾗ ἂν φυτεύσῃς πλανηθήσῃ τὸ δὲ πρωί ἐὰν σπείρῃς ἀνθήσει
εἰς ἀμητὸν ᾗ ἂν ἡμέρᾳ κληρώσῃ καὶ ὡς πατὴρ ἀνθρώπου κληρώσῃ τοῖς υἱοῖς
σου

12 οὐαὶ πλῆθος ἐθνῶν πολλῶν ὡς θάλασσα κυμαίνουσα οὕτως ταραχθήσεσθε καὶ
νῶτος ἐθνῶν πολλῶν ὡς ὕδωρ ἠχήσει

13 ὡς ὕδωρ πολὺ ἔθνη πολλά ὡς ὕδατος πολλοῦ βίᾳ καταφερομένου καὶ
ἀποσκορακιεῖ αὐτὸν καὶ πόρρω αὐτὸν διώξεται ὡς χνοῦν ἀχύρου λικμώντων
ἀπέναντι ἀνέμου καὶ ὡς κονιορτὸν τροχοῦ καταιγὶς φέρουσα

14 πρὸς ἑσπέραν ἔσται πένθος πρὶν ἢ πρωὶ καὶ οὐκ ἔσται αὕτη ἡ μερὶς τῶν
ὑμᾶς προνομευσάντων καὶ κληρονομία τοῖς ὑμᾶς κληρονομήσασιν

1 Onus Damasci. \[Ecce Damascus desinet esse civitas,  
et erit sicut acervus lapidum in ruina.  

2 Derelictæ civitates Aroër  
gregibus erunt,  
et requiescent ibi, et non erit qui exterreat.  

3 Et cessabit adjutorium ab Ephraim,  
et regnum a Damasco;  
et reliquiæ Syriæ  
sicut gloria filiorum Israël erunt,  
dicit Dominus exercituum.  

4 Et erit in die illa:  
attenuabitur gloria Jacob,  
et pinguedo carnis ejus marcescet.  

5 Et erit sicut congregans in messe quod restiterit,  
et brachium ejus spicas leget;  
et erit sicut quærens spicas  
in valle Raphaim.  

6 Et relinquetur in eo sicut racemus et sicut excussio oleæ  
duarum vel trium olivarum in summitate rami,  
sive quatuor aut quinque in cacuminibus ejus fructus ejus,  
dicit Dominus Deus Israël.  

7 In die illa inclinabitur homo ad  
factorem suum,  
et oculi ejus ad Sanctum Israël respicient;  

8 et non inclinabitur ad altaria quæ fecerunt manus ejus;  
et quæ operati sunt digiti ejus non respiciet  
lucos et delubra.  

9 In die illa erunt civitates fortitudinis ejus  
derelictæ sicut aratra, et segetes  
quæ derelictæ sunt a facie filiorum Israël;  
et eris deserta.  

10 Quia oblitus es Dei salvatoris tui,  
et fortis adjutoris tui non es recordata:  
propterea plantabis plantationem fidelem,  
et germen alienum seminabis;  

11 in die plantationis tuæ labrusca,  
et mane semen tuum florebit;  
ablata est messis in die hæreditatis,  
et dolebit graviter.  

12 Væ multitudini populorum multorum,  
ut multitudo maris sonantis;  
et tumultus turbarum,  
sicut sonitus aquarum multarum.  

13 Sonabunt populi sicut sonitus aquarum inundantium,  
et increpabit eum, et fugiet procul;  
et rapietur sicut pulvis montium a facie venti,  
et sicut turbo coram tempestate.  

14 In tempore vespere, et ecce turbatio;  
in matutino, et non subsistet.  
Hæc est pars eorum qui vastaverunt nos,  
et sors diripientium nos.\]
