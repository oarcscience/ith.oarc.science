H8085  
שׁמע  
שָׁמַע ‎ shâma‛  
*shaw-mah‘*  
A primitive root; to *hear* intelligently (often with implication of
attention, obedience, etc.; causatively to *tell*, etc.): - X
attentively, call (gather) together, X carefully, X certainly, consent,
consider, be content, declare, X diligently, discern, give ear, (cause
to, let, make to) hear (-ken, tell), X indeed, listen, make (a) noise,
(be) obedient, obey, perceive, (make a) proclaim (-ation), publish,
regard, report, shew (forth), (make a) sound, X surely, tell,
understand, whosoever \[heareth\], witness.
