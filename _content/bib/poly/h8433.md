H8433  
תּוכחת תּוכחה  
תּּוֹכֵחָה תּּוֹכַחַת ‎ tôkêchâh tôkachath  
*to-kay-khaw‘,* *to-kakh‘-ath*  
From [3198](h3198) *chastisement*; figuratively (by words) *correction*,
*refutation*, *proof* (even in defence): - argument, X chastened,
correction, reasoning, rebuke, reproof, X be (often) reproved.
