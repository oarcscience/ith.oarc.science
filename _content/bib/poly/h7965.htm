H7965
שׁלם שׁלום
שָׁלוֹם שָׁלוֹם ‎ shâlôm shâlôm
shaw-lome‘, shaw-lome‘
From 7999 safe, that is, (figuratively) well, happy, friendly; also (abstractly) welfare, that is, health, prosperity, peace: - X do, familiar, X fare, favour, + friend, X greet, (good) health, (X perfect, such as be at) peace (-able, -ably), prosper (-ity, -ous), rest, safe (-ly), salute, welfare, (X all is, be) well, X wholly.
