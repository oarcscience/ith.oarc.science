H3513  
כּבד כּבד  
כָּבַד כָּבֵד ‎ kâbad kâbêd  
*kaw-bad,* *kaw-bade‘*  
A primitive root; to *be* *heavy*, that is, in a bad sense
(*burdensome*, *severe*, *dull*) or in a good sense (*numerous*, *rich*,
*honorable*); causatively to *make* *weighty* (in the same two senses):
- abounding with, more grievously afflict, boast, be chargeable, X be
dim, glorify, be (make) glorious (things), glory, (very) great, be
grievous, harden, be (make) heavy, be heavier, lay heavily, (bring to,
come to, do, get, be had in) honour (self), (be) honourable (man), lade,
X more be laid, make self many, nobles, prevail, promote (to honour), be
rich, be (go) sore, stop.
