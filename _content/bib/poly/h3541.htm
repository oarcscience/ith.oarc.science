H3541
כּה
כּוֹה ‎ kôh
ko
From the prefix K and 1931 properly like this, that is, by implication (of manner) thus (or so); also (of place) here (or hither); or (of time) now: - also, here, + hitherto, like, on the other side, so (and much), such, on that manner, (on) this (manner, side, way, way and that way), + mean while, yonder.
