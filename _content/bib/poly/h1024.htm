בּית מרכּבות בּית המּרכּבות
בֵּיתּ הַמַּרכָּבוֹת בֵּיתּ מַרכָּבוֹת ‎ bêyth hammarkâbôth bêyth markâbôth
bayth ham-mar-kaw-both‘, mar-kaw-both‘
From 1004 and the plural of 4818 (with or without the article interposed); place of (the chariots; Beth-ham-Markaboth or Beth-Markaboth, a place in Palestine: - Beth-marcaboth.
