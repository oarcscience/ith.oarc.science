H5057  
נגד נגיד  
נָגִידּ נָגִד ‎ nâgı̂yd nâgid  
*naw-gheed‘,* *naw-gheed‘*  
From [5046](h5046) a *commander* (as occupying the *front*), civil,
military or religious; generally (abstract plural), *honorable* themes:
- captain, chief, excellent thing, (chief) governor, leader, noble,
prince, (chief) ruler.
