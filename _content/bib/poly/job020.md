### Job 20

[KJV](../kjv/job020)

1  Then answered<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> Zophar<sup>[6691](h6691)</sup>
the Naamathite,<sup>[5284](h5284)</sup> and said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>

2  Therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> do my
thoughts<sup>[5587](h5587)</sup> cause me to
answer,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> and for<sup>[5668](h5668)</sup> this I
make haste.<sup>[2363](h2363)</sup>

check<sup>[4148](h4148)</sup> of my reproach,<sup>[3639](h3639)</sup>
and the spirit<sup>[7307](h7307)</sup> of my
understanding<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[998](h0998)</sup> causeth me
to answer.<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup>

4  Knowest<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> thou not this<sup>[2063](h2063)</sup>
of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> old,<sup>[5703](h5703)</sup>
since<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> man<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> was
placed<sup>[7760](h7760)</sup> upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>
earth,<sup>[776](h0776)</sup>

5  That<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> the triumphing<sup>[7445](h7445)</sup>
of the wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> is short,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[7138](h7138)</sup> and the joy<sup>[8057](h8057)</sup> of the
hypocrite<sup>[2611](h2611)</sup> but for<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup> a
moment?<sup>[7281](h7281)</sup>

6  Though<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> his excellency<sup>[7863](h7863)</sup>
mount up<sup>[5927](h5927)</sup> to the heavens,<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup>
and his head<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup> reach<sup>[5060](h5060)</sup> unto
the clouds;<sup>[5645](h5645)</sup>

7  Yet he shall perish<sup>[6](h0006)</sup>
forever<sup>[5331](h5331)</sup> like his own
dung:<sup>[1561](h1561)</sup> they which have
seen<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> him shall say,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup>
Where<sup>[335](h0335)</sup> is he?

8  He shall fly away<sup>[5774](h5774)</sup> as a
dream,<sup>[2472](h2472)</sup> and shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> be
found:<sup>[4672](h4672)</sup> yea, he shall be chased
away<sup>[5074](h5074)</sup> as a vision<sup>[2384](h2384)</sup> of the
night.<sup>[3915](h3915)</sup>

9  The eye<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> also which
saw<sup>[7805](h7805)</sup> him shall see him no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
more;<sup>[3254](h3254)</sup> neither<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> shall his
place<sup>[4725](h4725)</sup> any more<sup>[5750](h5750)</sup>
behold<sup>[7789](h7789)</sup> him.

10  His children<sup>[1121](h1121)</sup> shall seek to
please<sup>[7521](h7521)</sup> the poor,<sup>[1800](h1800)</sup> and his
hands<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> shall restore<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup>
their goods.<sup>[202](h0202)</sup>

11  His bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> are full<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup>
of the sin of his youth,<sup>[5934](h5934)</sup> which shall lie
down<sup>[7901](h7901)</sup> with<sup>[5973](h5973)</sup> him
in<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> the dust.<sup>[6083](h6083)</sup>

12  Though<sup>[518](h0518)</sup> wickedness<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> be
sweet<sup>[4985](h4985)</sup> in his mouth,<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>
though he hide<sup>[3582](h3582)</sup> it under<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup>
his tongue;<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup>

13  Though he spare<sup>[2550](h2550)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> it,
and forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> it not;<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> but
keep it still<sup>[4513](h4513)</sup> within<sup>[8432](h8432)</sup> his
mouth:<sup>[2441](h2441)</sup>

14  Yet his meat<sup>[3899](h3899)</sup> in his
bowels<sup>[4578](h4578)</sup> is turned,<sup>[2015](h2015)</sup> it is
the gall<sup>[4846](h4846)</sup> of asps<sup>[6620](h6620)</sup>
within<sup>[7130](h7130)</sup> him.

15  He hath swallowed down<sup>[1104](h1104)</sup>
riches,<sup>[2428](h2428)</sup> and he shall vomit them up
again:<sup>[6958](h6958)</sup> God<sup>[410](h0410)</sup> shall cast
them out<sup>[3423](h3423)</sup> of<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> his
belly.<sup>[990](h0990)</sup>

16  He shall suck<sup>[3243](h3243)</sup> the
poison<sup>[7219](h7219)</sup> of asps:<sup>[6620](h6620)</sup> the
viper's<sup>[660](h0660)</sup> tongue<sup>[3956](h3956)</sup> shall
slay<sup>[2026](h2026)</sup> him.

17  He shall not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> see<sup>[7200](h7200)</sup> the
rivers,<sup>[6390](h6390)</sup> the floods,<sup>[5104](h5104)</sup> the
brooks<sup>[5158](h5158)</sup> of honey<sup>[1706](h1706)</sup> and
butter.<sup>[2529](h2529)</sup>

18  That which he labored for<sup>[3022](h3022)</sup> shall he
restore,<sup>[7725](h7725)</sup> and shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
swallow it down:<sup>[1104](h1104)</sup> according to his
substance<sup>[2428](h2428)</sup> shall the
restitution<sup>[8545](h8545)</sup> be, and he shall
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> rejoice<sup>[5965](h5965)</sup> therein.

19  Because<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he hath
oppressed<sup>[7533](h7533)</sup> and hath
forsaken<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> the poor;<sup>[1800](h1800)</sup>
because he hath violently taken away<sup>[1497](h1497)</sup> a
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> which he built<sup>[1129](h1129)</sup>
not;<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>

20  Surely<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> he shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
feel<sup>[3045](h3045)</sup> quietness<sup>[7961](h7961)</sup> in his
belly,<sup>[990](h0990)</sup> he shall not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
save<sup>[4422](h4422)</sup> of that which he
desired.<sup>[2530](h2530)</sup>

21  There shall none<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> of his
meat<sup>[400](h0400)</sup> be left;<sup>[8300](h8300)</sup>
therefore<sup>[3651](h3651)</sup> <sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> shall
no<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> man look<sup>[2342](h2342)</sup> for his
goods.<sup>[2898](h2898)</sup>

22  In the fullness<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> of his
sufficiency<sup>[5607](h5607)</sup> he shall be in
straits:<sup>[3334](h3334)</sup> every<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup>
hand<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> of the wicked<sup>[6001](h6001)</sup> shall
come<sup>[935](h0935)</sup> upon him.

23  When he<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> is about to
fill<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> his belly,<sup>[990](h0990)</sup> God shall
cast<sup>[7971](h7971)</sup> the fury<sup>[2740](h2740)</sup> of his
wrath<sup>[639](h0639)</sup> upon him, and shall
rain<sup>[4305](h4305)</sup> it upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> him while
he is eating.<sup>[3894](h3894)</sup>

24  He shall flee<sup>[1272](h1272)</sup> from the
iron<sup>[1270](h1270)</sup> weapon,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[5402](h5402)</sup> and the bow<sup>[7198](h7198)</sup> of
steel<sup>[5154](h5154)</sup> shall strike him
through.<sup>[2498](h2498)</sup>

25  It is drawn,<sup>[8025](h8025)</sup> and cometh
out<sup>[3318](h3318)</sup> of the body;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[1465](h1465)</sup> yea, the glittering
sword<sup>[1300](h1300)</sup> cometh<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup> out of his
gall:<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[4846](h4846)</sup>
terrors<sup>[367](h0367)</sup> are upon<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> him.

26  All<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> darkness<sup>[2822](h2822)</sup> shall
be hid<sup>[2934](h2934)</sup> in his secret
places:<sup>[6845](h6845)</sup> a fire<sup>[784](h0784)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> blown<sup>[5301](h5301)</sup> shall
consume<sup>[398](h0398)</sup> him; it shall go
ill<sup>[7489](h7489)</sup> with him that is
left<sup>[8300](h8300)</sup> in his tabernacle.<sup>[168](h0168)</sup>

27  The heaven<sup>[8064](h8064)</sup> shall
reveal<sup>[1540](h1540)</sup> his iniquity;<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> and
the earth<sup>[776](h0776)</sup> shall rise up<sup>[6965](h6965)</sup>
against him.

28  The increase<sup>[2981](h2981)</sup> of his
house<sup>[1004](h1004)</sup> shall depart,<sup>[1540](h1540)</sup> and
his goods shall flow away<sup>[5064](h5064)</sup> in the
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> of his wrath.<sup>[639](h0639)</sup>

29  This<sup>[2088](h2088)</sup> is the portion<sup>[2506](h2506)</sup>
of a wicked<sup>[7563](h7563)</sup> man<sup>[120](h0120)</sup> from
God,<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[430](h0430)</sup> and the
heritage<sup>[5159](h5159)</sup> appointed<sup>[561](h0561)</sup> unto
him by God.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[410](h0410)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/job020)

1 ὑπολαβὼν δὲ Σωφαρ ὁ Μιναῖος λέγει

2 οὐχ οὕτως ὑπελάμβανον ἀντερεῖν σε ταῦτα καὶ οὐχὶ συνίετε μᾶλλον ἢ καὶ
ἐγώ

3 παιδείαν ἐντροπῆς μου ἀκούσομαι καὶ πνεῦμα ἐκ τῆς συνέσεως ἀποκρίνεταί
μοι

4 μὴ ταῦτα ἔγνως ἀπὸ τοῦ ἔτι ἀφ᾽ οὗ ἐτέθη ἄνθρωπος ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς

5 εὐφροσύνη γὰρ ἀσεβῶν πτῶμα ἐξαίσιον χαρμονὴ δὲ παρανόμων ἀπώλεια

6 ἐὰν ἀναβῇ εἰς οὐρανὸν αὐτοῦ τὰ δῶρα ἡ δὲ θυσία αὐτοῦ νεφῶν ἅψηται

7 ὅταν γὰρ δοκῇ ἤδη κατεστηρίχθαι τότε εἰς τέλος ἀπολεῖται οἱ δὲ ἰδόντες
αὐτὸν ἐροῦσιν ποῦ ἐστιν

8 ὥσπερ ἐνύπνιον ἐκπετασθὲν οὐ μὴ εὑρεθῇ ἔπτη δὲ ὥσπερ φάσμα νυκτερινόν

9 ὀφθαλμὸς παρέβλεψεν καὶ οὐ προσθήσει καὶ οὐκέτι προσνοήσει αὐτὸν ὁ
τόπος αὐτοῦ

10 τοὺς υἱοὺς αὐτοῦ ὀλέσαισαν ἥττονες αἱ δὲ χεῖρες αὐτοῦ πυρσεύσαισαν
ὀδύνας

11 ὀστᾶ αὐτοῦ ἐνεπλήσθησαν νεότητος αὐτοῦ καὶ μετ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ χώματος
κοιμηθήσεται

12 ἐὰν γλυκανθῇ ἐν στόματι αὐτοῦ κακία κρύψει αὐτὴν ὑπὸ τὴν γλῶσσαν
αὐτοῦ

13 οὐ φείσεται αὐτῆς καὶ οὐκ ἐγκαταλείψει αὐτὴν καὶ συνέξει αὐτὴν ἐν
μέσῳ τοῦ λάρυγγος αὐτοῦ

14 καὶ οὐ μὴ δυνηθῇ βοηθῆσαι ἑαυτῷ χολὴ ἀσπίδος ἐν γαστρὶ αὐτοῦ

15 πλοῦτος ἀδίκως συναγόμενος ἐξεμεσθήσεται ἐξ οἰκίας αὐτοῦ ἐξελκύσει
αὐτὸν ἄγγελος

16 θυμὸν δὲ δρακόντων θηλάσειεν ἀνέλοι δὲ αὐτὸν γλῶσσα ὄφεως

17 μὴ ἴδοι ἄμελξιν νομάδων μηδὲ νομὰς μέλιτος καὶ βουτύρου

18 εἰς κενὰ καὶ μάταια ἐκοπίασεν πλοῦτον ἐξ οὗ οὐ γεύσεται ὥσπερ
στρίφνος ἀμάσητος ἀκατάποτος

19 πολλῶν γὰρ ἀδυνάτων οἴκους ἔθλασεν δίαιταν δὲ ἥρπασεν καὶ οὐκ ἔστησεν

20 οὐκ ἔστιν αὐτοῦ σωτηρία τοῖς ὑπάρχουσιν ἐν ἐπιθυμίᾳ αὐτοῦ οὐ
σωθήσεται

21 οὐκ ἔστιν ὑπόλειμμα τοῖς βρώμασιν αὐτοῦ διὰ τοῦτο οὐκ ἀνθήσει αὐτοῦ
τὰ ἀγαθά

22 ὅταν δὲ δοκῇ ἤδη πεπληρῶσθαι θλιβήσεται πᾶσα δὲ ἀνάγκη ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν
ἐπελεύσεται

23 εἴ πως πληρώσαι γαστέρα αὐτοῦ ἐπαποστείλαι ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν θυμὸν ὀργῆς
νίψαι ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν ὀδύνας

24 καὶ οὐ μὴ σωθῇ ἐκ χειρὸς σιδήρου τρώσαι αὐτὸν τόξον χάλκειον

25 διεξέλθοι δὲ διὰ σώματος αὐτοῦ βέλος ἀστραπαὶ δὲ ἐν διαίταις αὐτοῦ
περιπατήσαισαν ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ φόβοι

26 πᾶν δὲ σκότος αὐτῷ ὑπομείναι κατέδεται αὐτὸν πῦρ ἄκαυστον κακώσαι δὲ
αὐτοῦ ἐπήλυτος τὸν οἶκον

27 ἀνακαλύψαι δὲ αὐτοῦ ὁ οὐρανὸς τὰς ἀνομίας γῆ δὲ ἐπανασταίη αὐτῷ

28 ἑλκύσαι τὸν οἶκον αὐτοῦ ἀπώλεια εἰς τέλος ἡμέρα ὀργῆς ἐπέλθοι αὐτῷ

29 αὕτη ἡ μερὶς ἀνθρώπου ἀσεβοῦς παρὰ κυρίου καὶ κτῆμα ὑπαρχόντων αὐτῷ
παρὰ τοῦ ἐπισκόπου

[Vul](../vul/job020)

1 Respondens autem Sophar Naamathites, dixit:

2 \[Idcirco cogitationes meæ variæ succedunt sibi,  
et mens in diversa rapitur.  

3 Doctrinam qua me arguis audiam,  
et spiritus intelligentiæ meæ respondebit mihi.  

4 Hoc scio a principio,  
ex quo positus est homo super terram,  

5 quod laus impiorum brevis sit,  
et gaudium hypocritæ ad instar puncti.  

6 Si ascenderit usque ad cælum superbia ejus,  
et caput ejus nubes tetigerit,  

7 quasi sterquilinium in fine perdetur,  
et qui eum viderant, dicent: Ubi est?  

8 Velut somnium avolans non invenietur:  
transiet sicut visio nocturna.  

9 Oculus qui eum viderat non videbit,  
neque ultra intuebitur eum locus suus.  

10 Filii ejus atterentur egestate,  
et manus illius reddent ei dolorem suum.  

11 Ossa ejus implebuntur vitiis adolescentiæ ejus,  
et cum eo in pulvere dormient.  

12 Cum enim dulce fuerit in ore ejus malum,  
abscondet illud sub lingua sua.  

13 Parcet illi, et non derelinquet illud,  
et celabit in gutture suo.  

14 Panis ejus in utero illius  
vertetur in fel aspidum intrinsecus.  

15 Divitias quas devoravit evomet,  
et de ventre illius extrahet eas Deus.  

16 Caput aspidum suget,  
et occidet eum lingua viperæ.  

17 (Non videat rivulos fluminis,  
torrentes mellis et butyri.)  

18 Luet quæ fecit omnia, nec tamen consumetur:  
juxta multitudinem adinventionum suarum, sic et sustinebit.  

19 Quoniam confringens nudavit pauperes:  
domum rapuit, et non ædificavit eam.  

20 Nec est satiatus venter ejus:  
et cum habuerit quæ concupierat, possidere non poterit.  

21 Non remansit de cibo ejus,  
et propterea nihil permanebit de bonis ejus.  

22 Cum satiatus fuerit, arctabitur:  
æstuabit, et omnis dolor irruet super eum.  

23 Utinam impleatur venter ejus,  
ut emittat in eum iram furoris sui,  
et pluat super illum bellum suum.  

24 Fugiet arma ferrea,  
et irruet in arcum æreum.  

25 Eductus, et egrediens de vagina sua,  
et fulgurans in amaritudine sua:  
vadent et venient super eum horribiles.  

26 Omnes tenebræ absconditæ sunt in occultis ejus;  
devorabit eum ignis qui non succenditur:  
affligetur relictus in tabernaculo suo.  

27 Revelabunt cæli iniquitatem ejus,  
et terra consurget adversus eum.  

28 Apertum erit germen domus illius:  
detrahetur in die furoris Dei.  

29 Hæc est pars hominis impii a Deo,  
et hæreditas verborum ejus a Domino.\]
