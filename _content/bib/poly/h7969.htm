(1,2) shaw-loshe‘, (3,4) shel-o-shaw‘
The last two forms being masculine; a primitive number; three; occasionally (ordinal) third, or (multiplicative) thrice: - + fork, + often [-times], third, thir [-teen, -teenth], three, + thrice. Compare 7991
