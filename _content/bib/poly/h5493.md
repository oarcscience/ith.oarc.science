H5493  
שׂוּר סוּר  
סוּר שׂוּר ‎ sûr śûr  
*soor,* *soor*  
A primitive root; to *turn* off (literally or figuratively): - be
\[-head\], bring, call back, decline, depart, eschew, get \[you\], go
(aside), X grievous, lay away (by), leave undone, be past, pluck away,
put (away, down), rebel, remove (to and fro), revolt, X be sour, take
(away, off), turn (aside, away, in), withdraw, be without.
