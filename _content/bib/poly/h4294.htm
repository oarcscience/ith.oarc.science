H4294
מטּה מטּה
מַטֶּה מַטָּה ‎ maṭṭeh maṭṭâh
mat-teh‘, mat-taw‘
From 5186 a branch (as extending); figuratively a tribe; also a rod, whether for chastising (figuratively correction), ruling (a sceptre), throwing (a lance), or walking (a staff; figuratively a support of life, for example bread): - rod, staff, tribe.
