H2755
חראיון חרי־יונים
חֲרֵי ־ יוֹנִים חֲרָאיוֹן ‎ chărêy - yônı̂ym chărâ‘yôn
khar-ay‘-yo-neem‘, khar-aw-yone‘
From the plural of 2716 and the plural of 3123 excrements of doves (or perhaps rather the plural of a single word, the second form; of similar or uncertain derivation); probably a kind of vegetable: - doves’ dung.
