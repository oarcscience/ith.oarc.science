בּית מעון בּית בּעל מעון
בֵּיתּ בַּעַל מְעוֹן בֵּיתּ מְעוֹן ‎ bêyth ba‛al me‛ôn bêyth me‛ôn
bayth bah‘-al me-own‘, bayth me-own‘
From 1004 and 1168 and 4583 The first form meaning house of Baal of (the) habitation of (apparently by transposition). The second form meaning house of habitation of (Baal); Beth-Baal-Meon, a palce in Palestine: - Beth-baal-meon. Compare 1186 and 1194
