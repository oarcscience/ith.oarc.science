H7114  
קצר  
קָצַר ‎ qâtsar  
*kaw-tsar‘*  
A primitive root; to *dock* off, that is, *curtail* (transitively or
intransitively, literally or figuratively); especially to *harvest*
(grass or grain): - X at all, cut down, much discouraged, grieve,
harvestman, lothe, mourn, reap (-er), (be, wax) short (-en, -er),
straiten, trouble, vex.
