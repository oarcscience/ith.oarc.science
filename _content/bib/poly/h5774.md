H5774  
עוּף  
עוּף ‎ ‛ûph  
*oof*  
A primitive root; to *cover* (with wings or obscurity); hence (as
denominative from [5775](h5775) to *fly*; also (by implication of
dimness) to *faint* (from the darkness of swooning): - brandish, be
(wax) faint, flee away, fly (away - ), X set, shine forth, weary.
