G203
ἀκροβυστία
akrobustia
ak-rob-oos-tee‘-ah
From 206 and probably a modified form of πόσθη posthē (the penis or male sexual organ); the prepuce; by implication an uncircumcised (that is, gentile, figuratively unregenerate) state or person: - not circumcised, uncircumcised [with 2192 , uncircumcision.
