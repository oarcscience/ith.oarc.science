H4347  
מכּה מכּה  
מַכָּה מַכֶּה ‎ makkâh makkeh  
*mak-kaw‘,* *mak-keh‘*  
(Plural only) from [5221](h5221) a *blow* (in [2Ch
2:10](ch2002.htm#010), of the flail); by implication a *wound*;
figuratively *carnage*, also *pestilence: -* beaten, blow, plague,
slaughter, smote, X sore, stripe, stroke, wound (\[-ed\]).
