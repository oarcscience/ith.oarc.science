G591  
ἀποδίδωμι  
apodidōmi  
*ap-od-eed‘-o-mee*  
From [575](g0575) and [1325](g1325) to *give* *away*, that is, *up*,
*over*, *back*, etc. (in various applications): - deliver (again), give
(again), (re-) pay (-ment be made), perform, recompense, render,
requite, restore, reward, sell, yield,
