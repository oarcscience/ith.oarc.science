khiz-kee-yaw‘, khiz-kee-yaw‘-hoo, yekh-iz-kee-yaw‘, yekh-iz-kee-yaw‘-hoo
From 2388 and 3050 strengthened of Jah; Chizkijah, a king of Judah, also the name of two other Israelites: - Hezekiah, Hizkiah, Hizkijah. Compare 3169
