### Psalms 38

[KJV](../kjv/psa038)

1  A Psalm<sup>[4210](h4210)</sup> of David,<sup>[1732](h1732)</sup> to
bring to remembrance.<sup>[2142](h2142)</sup> O
LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> rebuke<sup>[3198](h3198)</sup> me
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> in thy wrath:<sup>[7110](h7110)</sup> neither
chasten<sup>[3256](h3256)</sup> me in thy hot
displeasure.<sup>[2534](h2534)</sup>

2  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> thine arrows<sup>[2671](h2671)</sup>
stick fast<sup>[5181](h5181)</sup> in me, and thy
hand<sup>[3027](h3027)</sup> presseth me sore.<sup>[5181](h5181)</sup>
<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup>

3  There is no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> soundness<sup>[4974](h4974)</sup>
in my flesh<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup> because<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of thine anger;<sup>[2195](h2195)</sup>
neither<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> is there any rest<sup>[7965](h7965)</sup>
in my bones<sup>[6106](h6106)</sup> because<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of my sin.<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup>

4  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> mine iniquities<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup>
are gone over<sup>[5674](h5674)</sup> mine head:<sup>[7218](h7218)</sup>
as a heavy<sup>[3515](h3515)</sup> burden<sup>[4853](h4853)</sup> they
are too heavy<sup>[3513](h3513)</sup> for<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> me.

5  My wounds<sup>[2250](h2250)</sup> stink<sup>[887](h0887)</sup> and
are corrupt<sup>[4743](h4743)</sup> because<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[6440](h6440)</sup> of my foolishness.<sup>[200](h0200)</sup>

6  I am troubled;<sup>[5753](h5753)</sup> I am bowed
down<sup>[7817](h7817)</sup> greatly;<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> I go<sup>[1980](h1980)</sup>
mourning<sup>[6937](h6937)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> long.

7  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> my loins<sup>[3689](h3689)</sup> are
filled<sup>[4390](h4390)</sup> with a loathsome<sup>[7033](h7033)</sup>
disease: and there is no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup>
soundness<sup>[4974](h4974)</sup> in my flesh.<sup>[1320](h1320)</sup>

8  I am feeble<sup>[6313](h6313)</sup> and sore<sup>[5704](h5704)</sup>
<sup>[3966](h3966)</sup> broken:<sup>[1794](h1794)</sup> I have
roared<sup>[7580](h7580)</sup> by reason of the
disquietness<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5100](h5100)</sup> of my
heart.<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup>

9  Lord,<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> my
desire<sup>[8378](h8378)</sup> is before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> thee;
and my groaning<sup>[585](h0585)</sup> is not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup>
hid<sup>[5641](h5641)</sup> from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> thee.

10  My heart<sup>[3820](h3820)</sup> panteth,<sup>[5503](h5503)</sup> my
strength<sup>[3581](h3581)</sup> faileth<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> me: as
for the light<sup>[216](h0216)</sup> of mine
eyes,<sup>[5869](h5869)</sup> it<sup>[1992](h1992)</sup>
also<sup>[1571](h1571)</sup> is gone<sup>[369](h0369)</sup>
from<sup>[854](h0854)</sup> me.

11  My lovers<sup>[157](h0157)</sup> and my
friends<sup>[7453](h7453)</sup> stand aloof<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup> from
my sore;<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> <sup>[5048](h5048)</sup>
<sup>[5061](h5061)</sup> and my kinsmen<sup>[7138](h7138)</sup>
stand<sup>[5975](h5975)</sup> afar off.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[7350](h7350)</sup>

12  They also that seek after<sup>[1245](h1245)</sup> my
life<sup>[5315](h5315)</sup> lay snares<sup>[5367](h5367)</sup> for me:
and they that seek<sup>[1875](h1875)</sup> my
hurt<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> speak<sup>[1696](h1696)</sup> mischievous
things,<sup>[1942](h1942)</sup> and imagine<sup>[1897](h1897)</sup>
deceits<sup>[4820](h4820)</sup> all<sup>[3605](h3605)</sup> the
day<sup>[3117](h3117)</sup> long.

13  But I,<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> as a deaf<sup>[2795](h2795)</sup> man,
heard<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup> not;<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> and I was as
a dumb man<sup>[483](h0483)</sup> that openeth<sup>[6605](h6605)</sup>
not<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> his mouth.<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>

14  Thus I was<sup>[1961](h1961)</sup> as a man<sup>[376](h0376)</sup>
that<sup>[834](h0834)</sup> heareth<sup>[8085](h8085)</sup>
not,<sup>[3808](h3808)</sup> and in whose mouth<sup>[6310](h6310)</sup>
are no<sup>[369](h0369)</sup> reproofs.<sup>[8433](h8433)</sup>

15  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> in thee, O LORD,<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup>
do I hope:<sup>[3176](h3176)</sup> thou<sup>[859](h0859)</sup> wilt
hear,<sup>[6030](h6030)</sup> O Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> my
God.<sup>[430](h0430)</sup>

16  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I said,<sup>[559](h0559)</sup> Hear me,
lest<sup>[6435](h6435)</sup> otherwise they should
rejoice<sup>[8055](h8055)</sup> over me: when my
foot<sup>[7272](h7272)</sup> slippeth,<sup>[4131](h4131)</sup> they
magnify<sup>[1431](h1431)</sup> themselves
against<sup>[5921](h5921)</sup> me.

17  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I<sup>[589](h0589)</sup> am
ready<sup>[3559](h3559)</sup> to halt,<sup>[6761](h6761)</sup> and my
sorrow<sup>[4341](h4341)</sup> is continually<sup>[8548](h8548)</sup>
before<sup>[5048](h5048)</sup> me.

18  For<sup>[3588](h3588)</sup> I will declare<sup>[5046](h5046)</sup>
mine iniquity;<sup>[5771](h5771)</sup> I will be
sorry<sup>[1672](h1672)</sup> for my sin.<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup>
<sup>[2403](h2403)</sup>

19  But mine enemies<sup>[341](h0341)</sup> are
lively,<sup>[2416](h2416)</sup> and they are
strong:<sup>[6105](h6105)</sup> and they that
hate<sup>[8130](h8130)</sup> me wrongfully<sup>[8267](h8267)</sup> are
multiplied.<sup>[7231](h7231)</sup>

20  They also that render<sup>[7999](h7999)</sup>
evil<sup>[7451](h7451)</sup> for<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup>
good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> are mine
adversaries;<sup>[7853](h7853)</sup> because<sup>[8478](h8478)</sup> I
follow<sup>[7291](h7291)</sup> the thing that
good<sup>[2896](h2896)</sup> is.

21  Forsake<sup>[5800](h5800)</sup> me not,<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> O
LORD:<sup>[3068](h3068)</sup> O my God,<sup>[430](h0430)</sup> be
not<sup>[408](h0408)</sup> far<sup>[7368](h7368)</sup>
from<sup>[4480](h4480)</sup> me.

22  Make haste<sup>[2363](h2363)</sup> to help<sup>[5833](h5833)</sup>
me, O Lord<sup>[136](h0136)</sup> my salvation.<sup>[8668](h8668)</sup>

[Sep](../sep/psa038)

1 εἰς τὸ τέλος τῷ Ιδιθουν ᾠδὴ τῷ Δαυιδ

2 εἶπα φυλάξω τὰς ὁδούς μου τοῦ μὴ ἁμαρτάνειν ἐν γλώσσῃ μου ἐθέμην τῷ
στόματί μου φυλακὴν ἐν τῷ συστῆναι τὸν ἁμαρτωλὸν ἐναντίον μου

3 ἐκωφώθην καὶ ἐταπεινώθην καὶ ἐσίγησα ἐξ ἀγαθῶν καὶ τὸ ἄλγημά μου
ἀνεκαινίσθη

4 ἐθερμάνθη ἡ καρδία μου ἐντός μου καὶ ἐν τῇ μελέτῃ μου ἐκκαυθήσεται πῦρ
ἐλάλησα ἐν γλώσσῃ μου

5 γνώρισόν μοι κύριε τὸ πέρας μου καὶ τὸν ἀριθμὸν τῶν ἡμερῶν μου τίς
ἐστιν ἵνα γνῶ τί ὑστερῶ ἐγώ

6 ἰδοὺ παλαιστὰς ἔθου τὰς ἡμέρας μου καὶ ἡ ὑπόστασίς μου ὡσεὶ οὐθὲν
ἐνώπιόν σου πλὴν τὰ σύμπαντα ματαιότης πᾶς ἄνθρωπος ζῶν διάψαλμα

7 μέντοιγε ἐν εἰκόνι διαπορεύεται ἄνθρωπος πλὴν μάτην ταράσσονται
θησαυρίζει καὶ οὐ γινώσκει τίνι συνάξει αὐτά

8 καὶ νῦν τίς ἡ ὑπομονή μου οὐχὶ ὁ κύριος καὶ ἡ ὑπόστασίς μου παρὰ σοῦ
ἐστιν

9 ἀπὸ πασῶν τῶν ἀνομιῶν μου ῥῦσαί με ὄνειδος ἄφρονι ἔδωκάς με

10 ἐκωφώθην καὶ οὐκ ἤνοιξα τὸ στόμα μου ὅτι σὺ εἶ ὁ ποιήσας με

11 ἀπόστησον ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ τὰς μάστιγάς σου ἀπὸ τῆς ἰσχύος τῆς χειρός σου ἐγὼ
ἐξέλιπον

12 ἐν ἐλεγμοῖς ὑπὲρ ἀνομίας ἐπαίδευσας ἄνθρωπον καὶ ἐξέτηξας ὡς ἀράχνην
τὴν ψυχὴν αὐτοῦ πλὴν μάτην ταράσσεται πᾶς ἄνθρωπος διάψαλμα

13 εἰσάκουσον τῆς προσευχῆς μου κύριε καὶ τῆς δεήσεώς μου ἐνώτισαι τῶν
δακρύων μου μὴ παρασιωπήσῃς ὅτι πάροικος ἐγώ εἰμι παρὰ σοὶ καὶ
παρεπίδημος καθὼς πάντες οἱ πατέρες μου

14 ἄνες μοι ἵνα ἀναψύξω πρὸ τοῦ με ἀπελθεῖν καὶ οὐκέτι μὴ ὑπάρξω

2 \[Dixi: Custodiam vias meas:  
ut non delinquam in lingua mea.  
Posui ori meo custodiam,  
cum consisteret peccator adversum me.  

3 Obmutui, et humiliatus sum, et silui a bonis;  
et dolor meus renovatus est.  

4 Concaluit cor meum intra me;  
et in meditatione mea exardescet ignis.  

5 Locutus sum in lingua mea:  
Notum fac mihi, Domine, finem meum,  
et numerum dierum meorum quis est,  
ut sciam quid desit mihi.

et substantia mea tamquam nihilum ante te.  
Verumtamen universa vanitas, omnis homo vivens.  

7 Verumtamen in imagine pertransit homo;  
sed et frustra conturbatur:  
thesaurizat, et ignorat cui congregabit ea.  

8 Et nunc quæ est exspectatio mea: nonne Dominus?  
et substantia mea apud te est.  

9 Ab omnibus iniquitatibus meis erue me:  
opprobrium insipienti dedisti me.  

10 Obmutui, et non aperui os meum,  
quoniam tu fecisti;  

11 amove a me plagas tuas.  

12 A fortitudine manus tuæ ego defeci in increpationibus:  
propter iniquitatem corripuisti hominem.  
Et tabescere fecisti sicut araneam animam ejus:  
verumtamen vane conturbatur omnis homo.  

13 Exaudi orationem meam, Domine, et deprecationem meam;  
auribus percipe lacrimas meas.  
Ne sileas, quoniam advena ego sum apud te,  
et peregrinus sicut omnes patres mei.  

14 Remitte mihi, ut refrigerer  
priusquam abeam et amplius non ero.\]
