H6903
קבל קבל
קְבֵל קֳבֵל ‎ qebêl qŏbêl
keb-ale‘, kob-ale‘
(Chaldee); corresponding to 6905 (adverbially) in front of; usually (with other particles) on account of, so as, since, hence: - + accounting to, + as, + because, before, + for this cause, + forasmuch as, + by this means, over against, by reason of, + that, + therefore, + though, + wherefore.
