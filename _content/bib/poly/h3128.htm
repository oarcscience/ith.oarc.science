H3128
יונת אלם רחקים
יוֹנַת אֵלֶם רְחוֹקִים ‎ yônath ‘êlem rechôqı̂ym
yo-nath‘ ay‘-lem rekh-o-keem‘
From 3123 and 482 and the plural of 7350 dove of (the) silence (that is, dumb Israel) of (that is, among) distances (that is, strangers); the title of a ditty (used for a name of its melody): - Jonath-elem-rechokim.
