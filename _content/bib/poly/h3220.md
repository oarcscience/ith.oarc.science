H3220  
ים  
יָם ‎ yâm  
*yawm*  
From an unused root meaning to *roar*; a *sea* (as breaking in *noisy*
surf) or large body of water; specifically (with the article) the
Mediterranean; sometimes a large *river*, or an artificial *basin*;
locally, the *west*, or (rarely) the *south: -* sea (X -faring man,
\[-shore\]), south, west (-ern, side, -ward).
