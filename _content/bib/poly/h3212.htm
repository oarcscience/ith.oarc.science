H3212
ילך
יָלַך ‎ yâlak
yaw-lak‘
A primitive root (compare 1980 ; to walk (literally or figuratively); causatively to carry (in various senses): - X again, away, bear, bring, carry (away), come (away), depart, flow, + follow (-ing), get (away, hence, him), (cause to, make) go (away, -ing, -ne, one’s way, out), grow, lead (forth), let down, march, prosper, + pursue, cause to run, spread, take away ([-journey]), vanish, (cause to) walk (-ing), wax, X be weak.
