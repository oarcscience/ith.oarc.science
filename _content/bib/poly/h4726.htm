H4726
מקר מקור
מָקוֹר מָקוֹר ‎ mâqôr mâqôr
maw-kore‘, maw-kore‘
From 6979 properly something dug, that is, a (generally) source (of water, even when naturally flowing; also of tears, blood (by euphemism of the female pudenda); figuratively of happiness, wisdom, progeny): - fountain, issue, spring, well (-spring).
