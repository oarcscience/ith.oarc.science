H8104  
שׁמר  
שָׁמַר ‎ shâmar  
*shaw-mar‘*  
A primitive root; properly to *hedge* about (as with thorns), that is,
*guard*; generally to *protect*, *attend* *to*, etc.: - beware, be
circumspect, take heed (to self), keep (-er, self), mark, look narrowly,
observe, preserve, regard, reserve, save (self), sure, (that lay) wait
(for), watch (-man).
