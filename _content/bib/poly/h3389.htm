H3389
ירוּשׁלים ירוּשׁלם
יְרוּשָׁלִַם יְרוּשָׁלַיִם ‎ yerûshâlaim yerûshâlayim
yer-oo-shaw-lah‘-im, yer-oo-shaw-lah‘-yim
A dual (in allusion to its two main hills (the true pointing, at least of the former reading, seems to be that of 3390 ); probably from (the passive participle of) 3384 and 7999 founded peaceful; Jerushalaim or Jerushalem, the capital city of Palestine: - Jerusalem.
