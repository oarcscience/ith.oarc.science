עשׁתּרת קרנים  
עַשׁתְּּרוֹת קַרנַיִם ‎ ‛asht<sup>e</sup>rôth qarnayim  
*ash-ter-oth‘* *kar-nah‘-yim*  
From [6252](h6252) and the dual of [7161](h7161) *Ashtaroth* *of* (the)
*double* *horns* (a symbol of the deity); *Ashteroth* *Karnaim*, a place
East of the Jordan: - Ashtoreth Karnaim.
