G2425
ἱκανός
hikanos
hik-an-os‘
From ἵκω hikō ( ἱκάνω or ἱκνέομαι ; akin to 2240 to arrive); competent (as if coming in season), that is, ample (in amount) or fit (in character): - able, + content, enough, good, great, large, long (while), many, meet, much, security, sore, sufficient, worthy.
