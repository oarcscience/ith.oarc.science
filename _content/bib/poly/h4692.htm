H4692
מצוּר מצור
מָצוֹר מָצוּר ‎ mâtsôr mâtsûr
maw-tsore‘, maw-tsoor‘
From 6696 something hemming in, that is, (objectively) a mound (of besiegers), (abstractly) a siege, (figuratively) distress; or (subjectively) a fastness: - besieged, bulwark, defence, fenced, fortress, siege, strong (hold), tower.
