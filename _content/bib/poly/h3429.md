H3429  
ישׁב בּשּׁבת  
יוֹשֵׁב בַּשֶּׁבֶת ‎ yôshêb bashshebeth  
*yo-shabe‘* *bash-sheh‘-beth*  
From the active participle of [3427](h3427) and [7674](h7674) with a
preposition and the article interposed; *sitting* *in* *the* *seat*;
*Josheb-bash-Shebeht*, an Israelite: - that sat in the seat.
