קִיר חֶרֶשׂ קִיר חֲרֶשֶׂת ‎ qı̂yr chereś qı̂yr chăreśeth  
*(keer)* *kheh‘-res,* *khar-eh‘-seth*  
From [7023](h7023) and [2789](h2789) *fortress* *of* *earthenware*;
*Kir-Cheres* or *Kir-Chares-eth*, a place in Moab: - Kir-haraseth,
Kir-hareseth, Kir-haresh, Kir-heres.
