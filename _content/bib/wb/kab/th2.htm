Kabyle Bible: 2 Thessalonians

1:1 Nekk Bulus, Silwan akk-d Timuti, i tejmaɛt n watmaten n temdint n Tiṣalunik yumnen s Sidi Ṛebbi d Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:2 Ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a wen-d țțunefkent s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:3 Ay atmaten eɛzizen, imi liman-nwen simmal yețnerni, simmal tețțemyeḥmalem wway gar-awen, eɛni mačči d lḥeqq ma nețḥemmid Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen am iḍ am ass ?

1:4 Newweḍ armi nețzuxxu yis-wen di yal tajmaɛt n watmaten, nețțawi-kkun d lemtel axaṭer ɣas akken tenneɛtabem tețwaqehṛem, tṣebrem, teṭṭfem di liman-nwen.

1:5 Sidi Ṛebbi d bab n lḥeqq, ma yella iɛemmed aț-țenneɛtabem akkagi, yebɣa a kkun-yerr d wid yuklalen tagelda-ines ; ț-țagelda-agi i ɣef tenneɛtabem.

1:6 Axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi yellan d Bab n lḥeqq, a d-yerr leqheṛ ɣef wid i kkun-ițqehhiṛen,

1:7 a kkun-iṣebber yid-nneɣ, kunwi yețwaqehṛen. Ayagi a d-idṛu asm'ara d-yuɣal Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, m'ara d-iṣubb seg igenwan nețța d lmalayekkat-is

1:8 di tlemmast n uḥeǧǧaǧu n tmes iwakken ad iɛaqeb wid iɛuṣan Sidi Ṛebbi, ur neqbil ara lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Sidna Ɛisa.

1:9 Lɛiqab ara ten-id-yasen, ț-țawaɣit ur nețfaka, Sidi Ṛebbi a ten-iḥeṛṛem ɣef udem-is, ur țwalin ara lɛaḍima-s.

1:10 Ayagi a d-idṛu asm'ara d-yas Sidna Ɛisa ; ass-nni wid yumnen yis ad tɛeǧǧben deg-s, a t-ḥemden m'ara t-walin di lɛaḍima-s ! Ula d kunwi aț-țilim gar-asen axaṭer tumnem s lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wen-nbecceṛ.

1:11 ?ef ayagi i ndeɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen am iḍ am ass, iwakken a kkun-yerr d wid yuklalen tudert i ɣer wen-d-issawel. Nessutur-as daɣen a wen-d-ifk tazmert, iwakken aț-țxedmem ayen akken tessaramem n wayen yelhan, yerna ad ibarek lecɣal txeddmem deg webrid n Sidna Ɛisa.

1:12 S wakka imdanen ad ḥemden isem-is ɣef ddemma n tikli-nwen yelhan, kunwi daɣen aț-țwaɛuzzem s ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi akk-d ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

2:1 ?ef wayen yeɛnan tuɣalin n Ssid nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ț-țemlilit-nneɣ yid-es, di leɛnaya-nwen ay atmaten :

2:2 ḥadret a kkun-iffeɣ leɛqel neɣ a wen-ikcem ukukru, ma yella win i wen-d-yennan belli ass n tuɣalin n Sidna Ɛisa yewweḍ-ed ; yezmer lḥal ad yili win i wen-t-id-yennan neɣ win i t-iwalan deg uweḥḥi, neɣ daɣen a wen-arun tabṛaț am akken d nukni i wen-țin-yuran.

2:3 Akken yebɣu yili lḥal ur țțaǧat yiwen a kkun-ikellex, axaṭer ass-nni ur d-ițaweḍ ara uqbel ussan n tijehli, uqbel a d-iban wemcum ijehlen i gețṛaǧu nnger.

2:4 Win akken ara yessimɣuṛen iman is sennig wayen meṛṛa iɛebbden yemdanen, ad yekcem ɣer lǧameɛ iqedsen n Ṛebbi ad yerr iman-is deg wemkan n Ṛebbi. f+

2:5 Ur tecfim ara belli xebbṛeɣ kkun id yakan ɣef ayagi asm'akken lliɣ yid-wen ?

2:6 Teẓram acu i t-iṭṭfen ur d-ițban ara tura alamma yewweḍ-ed lweqt-is !

2:7 Tazmert n cceṛ yellan d lbaḍna, tebda ccɣel-is, ilaq kan ayen i ț-iṭṭfen ar tura ad ițwakkes.

2:8 Imiren amcum-nni amejhul a d- ban, dɣa Sidna Ɛisa a t-iṣuḍ s nnefs-is a t-issenger, a t-imḥu s ufeǧǧeǧ n lɛaḍima-s asm'ara d-yuɣal.

2:9 Amcum-agi amejhul a d-yas s tezmert n Cciṭan, ad ixdem leɛǧubat d lbeṛhanat s wayes ara ikellex,

2:10 ad issexdem lekdeb d lbaṭel iwakken ad ikellex wid iḍaɛen, axaṭer ur qbilen ara leḥmala n tideț s wayes ara țwaselken.

2:11 Daymi Sidi Ṛebbi iceggeɛ-asen-d tazmert n ṭṭlam aț-țexdem deg-sen, iwakken ad amnen s wayen yellan d lekdeb.

2:12 S wakka wid akk ur numin ara s tideț, ad țțuɛaqben imi țțafen lfeṛḥ-nsen anagar di lbaṭel.

2:13 Ay atmaten ɛzizen ɣer Ṛebbi, ilaq-aɣ a nețḥemmid kull ass Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen, axaṭer ixtaṛ-ikkun d imezwura iwakken aț-țețțusellkem s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i kkun-iṣeffan s liman di tideț.

2:14 Sidi Ṛebbi issawel-awen-d aț țekkim di tmanegt n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, s lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ i wen-d newwi.

2:15 Ihi ay atmaten ḥerzet, ṭṭfet deg uselmed i wen-d-newwi, ama s yimi nneɣ ama s tebṛatin-nneɣ.

2:16 Nessutur di Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d baba-tneɣ Ṛebbi i ɣ-iḥemmlen, i ɣ-d-ifkan s ṛṛeḥma ines ṣṣbeṛ d usirem ițdumun,

2:17 ad țțuṣebbṛen wulawen-nwen, a wen-d-tețțunefk tezmert s wayes ara txeddmem anagar ayen yelhan ama di lecɣal-nwen ama deg imeslayen-nwen.

3:1 ?ef wayen nniḍen ay atmaten, dɛut Sidi Ṛebbi fell-aneɣ iwakken awal-is ad ițțubecceṛ, ad yaweḍ ɣer yal amkan, ad ițwaɛuzz am akken i t-tɛuzzem kunwi.

3:2 Dɛut daɣen iwakken a ɣ-isellek si ger ifassen n yemcumen i ɣ-ibɣan cceṛ, axaṭer llan wid ur nețțamen ara.

3:3 Lameɛna Sidi Ṛebbi d bab n lețkal, a kkun-isseǧhed, a kkun iḥader ɣef wemcum.

3:4 Nesɛa laman ɣuṛ-es fell-awen belli txeddmem yerna aț-țkemlem aț-țxedmem ayen i ɣef i kkun-nweṣṣa.

3:5 Ndeɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken ad iččaṛ ulawen-nwen s leḥmala-ines, iwakken aț-țesɛum ṣṣbeṛ n Lmasiḥ. f+

3:6 Ay atmaten a kkun-nenhu s yisem n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ : sbeɛdet iman-nwen ɣef atmaten imeɛdazen, ițɛicin akken bɣan, mačči am akken i wen-t-nesselmed.

3:7 Teẓram s yiman nwen amek i glaq a ɣ-tɛandem, axaṭer nukni ur nɛac ara deg ussexṛeb asmi nella yid-wen.

3:8 Ur neḍmiɛ ula deg yiwen aɣṛum-nneɣ, lameɛna nețɛețțib iman-nneɣ di lxedma iḍ d wass, iwakken ur nețțili ț-țaɛekkumt ula i yiwen.

3:9 Yerna lemmer nessuter-awen a ɣ-tɛiwnem, d lḥeqq fell-aɣ ; meɛna nexdem akkenni iwakken a wen-nili d lemtel.

3:10 Asmi nella gar-awen nweṣṣa kkun belli « win ur nebɣi ara ad ixdem ur ilaq ara ad yečč. »

3:11 Axaṭer nesla belli llan gar-awen imeɛdazen ur nebɣi ara ad xedmen meɛna ggaren iman-nsen di lecɣal i ten-ixḍan.

3:12 Imdanen-agi a ten-nweṣṣi s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ : ad xedmen akken ilaq iwakken a d-ḥeṛṛen aɣṛum nsen.

3:13 Ma d kunwi ay atmaten xedmet lxiṛ, ur ɛeggut ara !

3:14 Win ur neqbil ara ayen i d-nenna di tebṛaț-agi, jebdet iman-nwen fell-as, ur țeddut ara yid-es iwakken ad inneḥcam, ad issetḥi.

3:15 Ur t-ḥețțbet ara d aɛdaw, meɛna nhut-eț am yiwen seg watmaten-nwen.

3:16 Nessutur di Sidi Ṛebbi bab n talwit a wen-d-yefk lehna kull ass, di yal tagniț ! Sidi Ṛebbi ad yili yid-wen meṛṛa !

3:17 Nekk Bulus, uriɣ-awen sslam-agi s ufus-iw, ț-țagi i ț-țira-inu di yal tabṛaț i wen-ceggɛeɣ.

3:18 Ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ a d-ters fell-awen meṛṛa !
