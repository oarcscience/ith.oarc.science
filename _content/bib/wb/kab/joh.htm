Kabyle Bible: John

1:1 Di tazwara yella win yellan d Awal n Ṛebbi, yella akk-d Ṛebbi, d nețța i d Ṛebbi.

1:2 Si tazwara, yella akk-d Ṛebbi,

1:3 kullec yețwaxleq seg-s ; deg wayen meṛṛa yețwaxelqen, ulac ayen ur d-nekki ara s ɣuṛ-es.

1:4 Deg-s i tella tudert ; tudert-agi tella ț-țafat i yemdanen.

1:5 Tafat tețfeǧǧiǧ di ṭṭlam, yerna ṭṭlam ur ț-iqbil ara.

1:6 Yella yiwen wergaz yețwaceggeɛ ed s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, argaz-agi isem-is Yeḥya ;

1:7 yusa-d ad yili d inigi n tafat iwakken imdanen meṛṛa ad amnen s cchada-ines.

1:8 Mačči d nețța i ț-țafat, nețța yusa-d d inigi n tafat.

1:9 Tafat-agi ț-țafat n tideț, tusa-d ɣer ddunit iwakken aț-țecṛeq ɣef yal amdan.

1:10 Win yellan d Awal n Ṛebbi yella yakan si tazwara di ddunit, imi d nețța i ț-ixelqen lameɛna ddunit ur t- teɛqil ara.

1:11 Yusa-d ɣer lumma-s lameɛna nețțat ur t-teqbil ara.

1:12 Ma d wid i t-iqeblen yumnen yis, widak iɛuzz-iten, uɣalen d arraw n Ṛebbi,

1:13 mačči seg wemdan i d-lulen, mačči s lebɣi n tnefsit neɣ s lebɣi n wemdan, lameɛna d Ṛebbi i sen-yefkan tudert tajdiṭ.

1:14 Win akken yellan d Awal, yuɣal-ed d amdan, iɛac gar-aneɣ, yeččuṛ ț-țideț akk-d ṛṛeḥma. Nețweḥḥid di lqeḍra i d-yefka Baba Ṛebbi i Mmi-s awḥid.

1:15 Yeḥya iched-ed fell-as zdat lɣaci yeqqaṛ : D nețța ɣef i wen-d-nniɣ : win ara d-yasen deffir-iw yezwar-iyi-d axaṭer yella uqbel ad iliɣ.

1:16 Yesseṛwa-yaɣ s lxiṛat-is, lbaṛaka ɣef tayeḍ.

1:17 ccariɛa tusa-d s ufus n Sidna Musa, ma d ṛṛeḥma ț-țideț usant-ed s Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ

1:18 Yiwen ur yeẓri Sidi Ṛebbi, Mmi-s awḥid yellan ɣuṛ-es, d nețța i ɣ-t-id isbeggnen.

1:19 Atah wamek i geched Yeḥya asmi i d-ceggɛen ɣuṛes lḥekkam n wudayen si temdint n Lquds. Ceggɛen-d ɣuṛes tajmaɛt n lmu-qedmin akk-d yemṛabḍen n at Lewwi akken a t-steqsin, nnan-as : Anwa-k keččini ?

1:20 Yenna-yasen tideț mbla tuffra : Mačči d nekk i d Lmasiḥ !

1:21 Rnan steqsan-t : Anwa-k ihi ? D kečč i d nnbi Ilyas ? Yeḥya yenna-yasen : Xaṭi mačči d nekk. Nutni rnan nnan-as : D nnbi i telliḍ ? Nețța yerra-yasen : Xaṭi !

1:22 Rnan ḥeṛsen-t nnan-as : Anwa-k ihi ? Ilaq a nerr lexbaṛ i wid i ɣ-d-iceggɛen. D acu i teqqaṛeḍ ɣef yiman-ik ?

1:23 Yeḥya yenna-yasen : Nekk d win akken ɣef i d-yenna nnbi Iceɛya : ț-țaɣect tețɛeggiḍ deg unezṛuf : Heggit abrid i Sidi Ṛebbi !

1:24 Wid i d-yusan ɣuṛ-es i gellan di tejmaɛt n ifariziyen,

1:25 rnan steqsan-t nnan-as : Imi ur telliḍ ara d Lmasiḥ neɣ d nnbi Ilyas neɣ d nnbi nniḍen, iwacu ihi i tesseɣḍaseḍ lɣaci deg waman ?

1:26 Yeḥya yerra-yasen : Nekk sseɣḍaseɣ-kkun deg waman ; yella gar-awen yiwen ur t-tessinem ara,

1:27 usiɣ-ed uqbel-is, lameɛna ur uklaleɣ ara ad fsiɣ ulamma d lexyuḍ n warkasen-is.

1:28 Annect-agi meṛṛa yedṛa di taddart n Bitanya ɣer tama n cceṛq n wasif n Urdun anda i gesseɣḍas Yeḥya lɣaci.

1:29 Azekka-nni, Yeḥya iwala Sidna Ɛisa iteddu-d ɣuṛ-es, yenṭeq s ṣṣut ɛlayen : Atan Izimer n Ṛebbi, d win ițekksen ddnub n ddunit.

1:30 Fell-as i wen-d-nniɣ : « a d-yas yiwen, zwareɣ-t-id meɛna yella uqbel a d iliɣ.»

1:31 Nekk s yiman-iw ur ẓriɣ ara anwa-t meɛna usiɣ-ed ad sɣeḍseɣ deg waman akken a t-issinen wat Isṛail .

1:32 Atan wayen i d-yenna Yeḥya Aɣeṭṭas fell-as : Walaɣ Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen iṣubb-ed seg igenni s ṣṣifa n tetbirt yers-ed fell-as.

1:33 Nekk ur ẓriɣ ara belli d nețța, meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi i yi-d iceggɛen iwakken ad sɣeḍseɣ deg waman, yenna-yi-d : « Aț-țwaliḍ Ṛṛuḥ iṣubb-ed seg igenni, a d-yers ɣef yiwen wergaz, d nețța ara yesɣeḍsen imdanen s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen. »

1:34 Ayagi d ayen i ẓrant wallen-iw, daymi i d-cehdeɣ fell-as qqaṛeɣ : « Argaz-agi d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi.»

1:35 Azekka-nni, Yeḥya mazal-it dinna nețța d sin seg inelmaden-is.

1:36 Akken i gwala Sidna Ɛisa iɛedda-d, yenna-yasen : Atan izimer n Ṛebbi !

1:37 Mi slan i imeslayen-agi, inelmaden-nni tebɛen Sidna Ɛisa.

1:38 Sidna Ɛisa mi geẓra tebɛen-t-id, yezzi-d ɣuṛ-sen, yenna-yasen : D acu tebɣam ? Nutni nnan-as : Ṛabi ( yeɛni : a Sidi ), anda tzedɣeḍ ?

1:39 Yenna-yasen : Ddut yid-i aț-țeẓrem. Inelmaden-nni ddan yid-es, ẓran anda yezdeɣ. Aț-țili d ṛebɛa n tmeddit, qqimen yid-es armi yekfa wass.

1:40 Andriyus, gma-s n Semɛun Buṭrus d yiwen seg inelmaden-nni i gtebɛen Sidna Ɛisa m'akken i sen-d-yehdeṛ fell-as Yeḥya ;

1:41 iṛuḥ uqbel ɣer gma-s Semɛun, yenna-yas : Nufa Lmasiḥ ! Win i gextaṛ Ṛebbi.

1:42 Andriyus yewwi Semɛun ɣer Sidna Ɛisa. Akken i t-iwala Sidna Ɛisa, imuqel-it yenna-yas : Kečč i d Semɛun mmi-s n Yunes. Sya d asawen aț-tețțusemmiḍ Sifas yeɛni : Buṭrus.

1:43 Azekka-nni, Sidna Ɛisa yeqsed ad iṛuḥ ɣer tmurt n Jlili. Deg ubrid yemmuger-ed Filibus, yenna-yas : Ddu-d yid-i !

1:44 Filibus, d yiwen si taddart n Bitsayda, anda akken zedɣen Buṭrus d Andriyus.

1:45 Syenna Filibus yeẓra-d Natanahil, yenna-yas : Nufa win ɣef i d-yehdeṛ Sidna Musa di ccariɛa, win akken i ɣef d-xebbṛen lenbiya. D Ɛisa, mmi-s n Yusef n taddart n Naṣaret.

1:46 Natanahil yerra-yas : Amek, yezmer a d-iffeɣ wayen yelhan si taddart n Naṣaret ? Filibus yerra-yas : Eyya-d aț-țwaliḍ s wallen-ik !

1:47 Sidna Ɛisa mi gwala Natanahil iteddu-d ɣuṛ-es, yenna : Atah yiwen n wat Isṛail n tideț, ulac deg-s leɣdeṛ.

1:48 Natanahil yenna i Sidna Ɛisa : Anda i yi-tesneḍ ? Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Uqbel a k-d-yessiwel Filibus, mi telliḍ ddaw n tneqleț, walaɣ-k-in !

1:49 Dɣa Natanahil yenna-yas : A Sidi, kečč d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ! D agellid n wat Isṛail !

1:50 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Tumneḍ imi i k-d-nniɣ walaɣ k-in seddaw n tneqleț ; sya ɣer zdat aț-țeẓred ayen yugaren annect-a !

1:51 S tideț qqaṛeɣ-awen : aț-țwalim igenni yeldi, lmalayekkat n Sidi Ṛebbi țțalint țṣubbunt sennig Mmi-s n bunadem.

2:1 Mi ɛeddan sin wussan, tella-d tmeɣṛa di tmurt n Jlili di taddart Kana, tella dinna yemma-s n Sidna Ɛisa.

2:2 Ula d Sidna Ɛisa yețwaɛṛed nețța d inelmaden-is ɣer tmeɣṛa-nni.

2:3 Imi i ten-ixuṣṣ ccṛab, yemma-s n Sidna Ɛisa tusa-d ɣuṛ-es tenna-yas : Ifuk-asen ccṛab !

2:4 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : D nekk neɣ d kemm i teɛna temsalt-agi a tameṭṭut ? Taswiɛt-iw urɛad i d-tusi.

2:5 Dɣa yemma-s tenna i iqeddacen : Xedmet ayen akk ara wen-d yini !

2:6 Llant dinna sețța n tsebbalin ( icmuxen ) yețwaxedmen s wedɣaɣ, țțawint si tmanyin ar meyya uɛecrin n litrat, seg-sent i d-țɛemmiṛen wat Isṛail aman i luḍu.

2:7 Sidna Ɛisa yenna i iqeddacen nni : ?čaṛet ticbayliyin-agi d aman. ?čuṛen-tent armi d imi.

2:8 Dɣa yenna-asen : Ɛemmṛet-ed tura kra n waman seg-sent tawim-ten i bab n tmeɣṛa. Kkren xedmen akken i sen-d-yenna.

2:9 Win iḍebbṛen di leqdic n tmeɣṛa yeɛṛeḍ aman-nni i guɣalen d ccṛab. Nețța ur yeẓri ansi i d-yekka ccṛab-agi, ma d iqeddacen-is ẓran axaṭer d nutni i ten-id-yewwin. Iṛuḥ yessawel i yesli,

2:10 yenna-yas : Di tmeɣṛiwin, medden srusun-d uqbel ccṛab yelhan, mi ḥman inebgawen rennun-asen-d win ṛqiqen, kečč teǧǧiḍ ccṛab yelhan armi ț-țura !

2:11 D wagi i d lbeṛhan amezwaru i gexdem Sidna Ɛisa di taddart n Kana di tmurt n Jlili. S wakka i d-ibeggen tamanegt-is i inelmaden-is, dɣa umnen yis.

2:12 Deffir wayagi Sidna Ɛisa iṣubb ɣer temdint n Kafernaḥum. Tedda yid-es yemma-s, atmaten-is akk-d inelmaden-is ; qqimen dinna anagar kra n wussan.

2:13 Akken i d-yeqṛeb wass n Tfaska n izimer n leslak yellan d lɛid n wat Isṛail, Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer temdint n Lquds.

2:14 Yufa deg wefrag n lǧameɛ iqedsen wid yeznuzun izgaren, ulli d yetbiren, yufa daɣen dinna wid yețbeddilen idrimen ; rran lǧameɛ iqedsen d ssuq.

2:15 Ixdem-ed ajelkwaḍ ( acelliṭ ) s wemrar, yebda itellif-iten s nutni s wulli-nsen akk-d yezgaren-nsen, iqleb-asen ( ițți-yasen ) daɣen ṭṭwabel i wid yețbeddilen idrimen, iḍeggeṛ asen kullec ɣer lqaɛa, issufeɣ-iten meṛṛa seg ufrag n lǧameɛ iqedsen ,

2:16 yerna yenna i wid yeznuzun itbiren : Kkset syagi ssuq-nwen ! Wagi d axxam n Baba, ur t-țarrat ara d ssuq !

2:17 inelmaden-is mmektan-d ayen yuran di tektabt n ?abur : Leḥmala i sɛiɣ ɣef wexxam-ik am tmes itețțen ul-iw !

2:18 Kkren wudayen nnan-as : D acu n lbeṛhan ara ɣ-d-tbeggneḍ akken a neɛqel lḥekma s wayes i txeddmeḍ annect-agi ?

2:19 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yasen : Huddet lǧameɛ-agi iqedsen, a t-id-sbeddeɣ di tlata wussan.

2:20 At Isṛail nnan-as : Wid yebnan lǧameɛ-agi qqimen deg-s sețța uṛebɛin iseggasen, kečč tzemreḍ a s-tɛiwdeḍ lebni di tlata wussan !

2:21 Lameɛna « Lǧameɛ » i ɣef d-yehdeṛ Sidna Ɛisa d lǧețța-s.

2:22 Asmi i d-yeḥya Sidna Ɛisa si lmut ɣer tudert, inelmaden-is mmektan-d awal-agi i d-yenna, dɣa umnen s wayen yuran di tira iqedsen d wayen i d-yenna Sidna Ɛisa.

2:23 Mi gella Sidna Ɛisa di temdint n Lquds di lweqt n lɛid n Tfaska, aṭas i gumnen yis mi ẓran lbeṛhanat i gxeddem.

2:24 Lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa ur yesɛi ara deg-sen laman axaṭer yessen-iten akken ma llan.

2:25 Ur yeḥwaǧ ara a s-d-ḥkun ɣef yemdanen imi yessen ayen yellan deg wulawen-nsen.

3:1 Yella yiwen wergaz isem-is Nikudem i gellan si tejmaɛt n ifariziyen, d yiwen seg imeqqranen n wat Isṛail.

3:2 Iṛuḥ-ed deg iḍ ɣer Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : A Sidi neẓra belli d Sidi Ṛebbi i k-id-iceggɛen iwakken a ɣ-teslemdeḍ, axaṭer yiwen ur izmir ad yexdem lbeṛhanat i txeddmeḍ m'ur yelli ara Ṛebbi yid-es.

3:3 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : S tideț a k-d-iniɣ : yiwen ur izmir ad iẓer tagelda n Ṛebbi m'ur d-iɛawed ara talalit.

3:4 Nikudem yenna-yas : Amek i gezmer yiwen a d iɛiwed talalit ma yella d amɣaṛ ? Izmer ad yuɣal ɣer tɛebbuṭ n yemma-s a d iɛiwed talalit ?

3:5 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Nniɣ-ak s tideț : m'ur d-ilul ara yiwen seg waman akk-d Ṛṛuḥ, ur ițțizmir ara ad ikcem tagelda n Ṛebbi.

3:6 Win i d-ilulen seg wemdan ț-țaṛwiḥt n wemdan, win i d-ilulen s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen d Ṛṛuḥ.

3:7 Ur țțawham ara imi i k-d-nniɣ « ilaq a d-tɛiwdem talalit.»

3:8 Aḍu yețsuḍu ɣer wanda i s-yehwa, tselleḍ i ṣṣut-is lameɛna ur tẓerreḍ ara ansi d-yekka neɣ anda iteddu. Akka daɣen i gella ɣef kra n win i d-ilulen s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

3:9 Nikudem yenna-yas : Amek ara yedṛu wayagi ?

3:10 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Tesselmadeḍ ccariɛa i wat Isṛail ur tessineḍ ara annect-agi ?

3:11 A k- iniɣ tideț: neqqaṛ-ed ayen kan i nessen, nețcehhid-ed s wayen i nwala, lameɛna kunwi ur teqbilem ara cchada-nneɣ.

3:12 M'ur tuminem ara mi wen-d-hedṛeɣ ɣef temsalin n ddunit-agi amek ara tamnem s tid n igenwan ?

3:13 Yiwen ur yuli ɣer igenwan anagar Mmi-s n bunadem i d-iṣubben seg igenwan.

3:14 Akken i gɛelleq Musa azrem n nnḥas ɣef tgejdit deg unezṛuf, i glaq ad ițțuɛelleq daɣen Mmi-s n bunadem

3:15 iwakken kra n win yumnen yis, ad yesɛu tudert n dayem.

3:16 Sidi Ṛebbi iḥemmel imdanen n ddunit meṛṛa armi i d-yefka Mmi-s awḥid iwakken kra n win yumnen yis ur yețmețțat ara meɛna ad yesɛu tudert n dayem.

3:17 Sidi Ṛebbi ur d-iceggeɛ ara Mmi-s ɣer ddunit iwakken ad iḥaseb imdanen meɛna iwakken a ten-isellek.

3:18 Win yumnen yis ur yețțuḥaseb ara, ma d win ur numin ara atan yețțuḥaseb yakan, imi ur yumin ara s Mmi-s awḥid n Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:19 Atah lḥisab yellan fell-as : « tafat tusa-d ɣer ddunit meɛna imdanen ḥemmlen ṭṭlam axiṛ n tafat, axaṭer lecɣal-nsen diri-ten. »

3:20 Kra n win ixeddmen cceṛ, yekṛeh tafat yerna ur ițqeṛṛib ara ɣuṛ-es, ițțaggad a d-tessedheṛ yir lecɣal-is.

3:21 Ma d win iteddun di tideț yețțas-ed ɣer tafat akken a d-iban belli kra n wayen ixeddem, ixeddem it di lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:22 Syenna Sidna Ɛisa d inelmaden is ṛuḥen ɣer tmurt n Yahuda, qqimen dinna kra n wussan, sseɣḍasen lɣaci deg waman.

3:23 Yeḥya daɣen yella yesseɣḍas lɣaci di Ɛinun ț-țama n Salim imi qwan waman dinna. Aṭas lɣaci i d-ițasen ɣuṛ-es iwakken a ten-isseɣḍes.

3:24 Di lweqt-nni urɛad i skecmen Yeḥya ɣer lḥebs.

3:25 Yiwen wass kra seg inelmaden n Yeḥya hedṛen d yiwen wergaz n wat Isṛail ɣef luḍu.

3:26 Ṛuḥen ɣer Yeḥya, nnan-as : A Sidi, win yellan yid-ek mi telliḍ agemmaḍ i wasif n Urdun, win akken i ɣef d-tcehdeḍ, atan yesseɣḍas ula d nețța, lɣaci meṛṛa țṛuḥun ɣuṛ-es.

3:27 Yeḥya yerra-yasen : Yiwen ur izmir ad ixdem ccɣel nniḍen sennig wayen i s-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:28 Aql-ikkun s yiman-nwen d inigan, acḥal d abrid i wen-d nniɣ : mačči d nekk i d Lmasiḥ, țwaceggɛeɣ d kan uqbel-is .

3:29 Iwumi tewjed teslit ? Anaɣ i yesli ! Ma d aḥbib n yesli d win yețɣimin ɣer tama-s, ifeṛṛeḥ m'ara isel i ṣṣut n yesli. Ula d nekk tura lfeṛḥ-iw yennekmal.

3:30 Ilaq nețța ad yețțimɣuṛ nekk ad țțim?iyeɣ.

3:31 Win i d-yusan seg igenni yella nnig n wayen yellan meṛṛa, ma d win yellan n ddunit-agi iheddeṛ anagar ɣef wayen yellan di ddunit. Win i d-yusan seg igenni yella sennig-nsen meṛṛa.

3:32 Icehhed-ed ɣef wayen yeẓra d wayen yesla, lameɛna yiwen ur iqbil cchada-ines.

3:33 Win ara iqeblen cchada-ines, yetḥeqqeq belli Sidi Ṛebbi yeqqaṛ tideț.

3:34 S tideț amceggeɛ n Ṛebbi iheddeṛ imeslayen i s-d-yefka Ṛebbi, imi i t-yeččuṛ s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

3:35 Baba Ṛebbi iḥemmel Mmi-s, yerra kullec ger ifassen-is.

3:36 Win yumnen s Mmi-s yesɛa tudert ur nfennu ; ma d win ur numin ara s Mmi-s ur yețwali ara tudert, urrif n Ṛebbi yezga fell-as.

4:1 Ifariziyen slan s Sidna Ɛisa yesɛa aṭas n inelmaden yerna yesseɣḍas imdanen akteṛ n Yeḥya.

4:2 Lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa mačči d nețța s yiman-is i gesseɣḍasen lɣaci, ccɣel-agi yeǧǧa-t i inelmaden-is. Mi geẓra Sidna Ɛisa belli slan yis,

4:3 yeffeɣ si tmurt n at Yahuda. Iwakken ad yuɣal ɣer tmurt n Jlili,

4:4 ilaq-as ad iɛeddi ɣef tmurt n Samarya.

4:5 Yelḥeq ɣer taddart n Sicar iqeṛben akal i d-yeǧǧa Sidna Yeɛqub i mmi-s Yusef ;

4:6 dinna i gella lbir n Sidna Yeɛqub. Sidna Ɛisa yeqqim ɣef yiri n lbir akken ad yesteɛfu. Aț-țili d tnac n wass.

4:7 Yiwet n tmeṭṭut n Samarya tusa-d aț-țagem ; Sidna Ɛisa iluɛa-ț, yenna yas : A tameṭṭut, ma ulac aɣilif efk- yi-d ad sweɣ !

4:8 Yuɣ lḥal inelmaden-is ṛuḥen ɣer temdint a d-aɣen ayen ara ččen.

4:9 Tameṭṭut-nni tasamarit tenna-yas : Amek, kečč yellan n wat Isṛail tessutreḍ-iyi-d aț-țesweḍ i nekk yellan ț-țasamarit ? (axaṭer at Isṛail ur țemsaɛaden ara yerna ur țemxalaḍen ara d isamariyen).

4:10 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Lemmer teẓriḍ ayen i gebɣa Ṛebbi a m-t-id yefk akk-d win i m-d-yessutren aman, tili d kemm ara s-yessutren a m-d-yefk aț țesweḍ ; nețța, d aman yessidiren ara m-d-yefk.

4:11 Tameṭṭut-nni tenna-yas : A Sidi, ur tesɛiḍ ara s wacu ara d-tessaliḍ aman yerna lbir-agi lqay. Ansi ara k-d-kken waman-agi yessidiren ?

4:12 Tebɣiḍ eɛni a d-tiniḍ tɛeddaḍ jeddi-tneɣ Yeɛqub i ɣ-d yeǧǧan lbir-agi, i geswan seg-s nețța d warraw-is akk țqeḍɛiyin-is ?

4:15 Tameṭṭut-nni tenna-yas : A Sidi, efk-iyi-d seg waman-agi iwakken ur țțuɣaleɣ ara ad ffadeɣ neɣ a d-aseɣ ɣer lbir-agi ad agmeɣ.

4:23 Lameɛna iteddu-d lweqt yerna yewweḍ-ed, anda lmumnin n ṣṣeḥ ad ɛebbden Baba Ṛebbi s Ṛṛuḥ, s tideț, axaṭer d lmumnin-agi n ṣṣeḥ i gebɣa Baba Ṛebbi.

4:25 Tameṭṭut-nni tenna-yas : ?riɣ a d-yas win i gextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, win ițțusemman « Lmasiḥ ». Asm'ara d-yas a ɣ-d-yessefhem kra yellan.

4:26 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Lmasiḥ, d nekk s yiman-iw !

4:27 Imiren kan wwḍen-d inelmaden is. Wehmen imi i t-id-ufan yețmeslay ț-țmeṭṭut ; lameɛna yiwen deg-sen ur s-yenni : d acu i tebɣiḍ ɣuṛ-es, neɣ iwacu i tețmeslayeḍ yid-es ?

4:29 Eyyaw aț-țe?rem yiwen yenna-yi-d akk ayen xedmeɣ ! Ad yili d nețța i d Lmasiḥ !

4:30 Lɣaci ffɣen-d si taddart ṛuḥen ad ẓren Sidna Ɛisa.

4:31 Uqbel a d-yaweḍ lɣaci, inelmaden-is ḥeṛsen-t ad yečč, nnan-as : A Sidi ɛeddi-d aț-țeččeḍ !

4:32 Yenna-yasen : Sɛiɣ lqut ara ččeɣ meɛna kunwi ur teẓrim ara d acu-t.

4:33 Inelmaden qqaṛen wway gar asen : Ahat yella win i s-d-yewwin ad yečč.

4:34 Sidna Ɛisa yenna : Lqut-iw, d m'ara xedmeɣ lebɣi n win i yi-d-iceggɛen akken ad kemmleɣ ccɣel i ɣef i yi-iwekkel.

4:35 Kunwi teqqaṛem mazal ṛebɛa wagguren i tmegra ma d nekk a wen-d-iniɣ: ldit allen-nwen aț-țwalim igran weṛṛaɣ-it, wejden i tmegra !

4:36 Win imeggren yewwi lexlaṣ-is, ijemmeɛ lɣella i tudert n dayem. Akka lfeṛḥ yesdukkel win izerrɛen d win imeggren.

4:37 Axaṭer ț-țideț lemtel-agi i d-yeqqaṛen : « Yiwen izerreɛ wayeḍ imegger » !

4:38 Nekk ceggɛeɣ-kkun aț-țmegrem anda ur tezriɛem ara, anda i nɛețțaben wiyaḍ ; nutni nɛețțaben, kunwi tɣelltem lɣella n leɛtab-nsen.

4:39 Aṭas n isamariyen n taddart-nni i gumnen s Sidna Ɛisa mi d-tcehhed fell-as tmeṭṭut-nni s imeslayen-agi : «yenna-yi-d akk ayen i xedmeɣ !»

4:40 Mi wwḍen ɣuṛ-es isamariyen-nni, ḥellelen Sidna Ɛisa ad iqqim yid-sen. Dɣa yeqqim yid-sen sin wussan.

4:41 Aṭas nniḍen i gumnen yis mi slan i imeslayen-is.

4:42 Nnan-as i tmeṭṭut-nni : Tura numen yis mačči kan ɣef wayen i ɣ-d-tenniḍ fell-as, nesla yas s yiman-nneɣ, neẓra belli d nețța i d amcafeɛ n ddunit.

4:43 Mi ɛeddan sin wussan, Sidna Ɛisa iṛuḥ syenna ɣer tmurt n Jlili.

4:44 Yenna-yasen : « Ulac nnbi yesɛan leqdeṛ di tmurt-is. »

4:45 Mi gewweḍ ɣer tmurt n Jlili, lɣaci sṭreḥben yis s lfeṛḥ d ameqqran imi ula d nutni ḥedṛen i lɛid n Tfaska di temdint n Lquds, ẓran akk lbeṛhanat i gexdem dinna.

4:46 Yuɣal iɛedda ɣef taddart n Kana di tmurt n Jlili anda akken i gerra aman d ccṛab. Yella yiwen lḥakem n ddewla n temdint n Kafernaḥum yesɛa mmi-s yuḍen yenṭer.

4:47 Mi gesla s Sidna Ɛisa yuɣal-ed si tmurt n Yahuda ɣer tmurt n Jlili, iṛuḥ-ed ɣuṛ-es, iḥellel-it iwakken a d yas ad yesseḥlu mmi-s yețmețțaten.

4:48 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : M'ur twalam ara licaṛat d lbeṛhanat ur tețțamnem ara ?

4:49 Lḥakem n ddewla yenna-yas : A Sidi, di leɛnaya-k ɣiwel uqbel ad yemmet mmi !

4:50 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ṛuḥ, atan mmi-k yeḥla ! Argaz-nni yumen s wayen i s-d yenna Sidna Ɛisa, dɣa yuɣal

4:51 ɣer wexxam-is. Mi gteddu ɣer wexxam, mmugren-t-id iqeddacen-is nnan-as : Mmi-k yeḥla !

4:52 Yesteqsa-ten anta ssaɛa i deg yeḥla, rran-as-ed : Iḍelli ɣef lweḥda n tmeddit i t-teffeɣ tawla.

4:53 Baba-s n weqcic-nni yewwi-d s lexbaṛ belli di ssaɛa-nni i s-d-yenna Sidna Ɛisa « Mmi-k yeḥla » i geḥla mmi-s, seg imiren yumen nețța d wat wexxam-is meṛṛa.

4:54 D wagi i d lbeṛhan wis sin i gexdem Sidna Ɛisa di tmurt n Jlili mi d-yuɣal si tmurt n Yahuda.

5:1 Ɛeddan kra n wussan, Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer temdint n Lquds akken ad yeḥdeṛ i lɛid n wat Isṛail.

5:2 Di temdint-agi, zdat n tewwurt yețțusemman « tawwurt n wulli, »tella yiwet n temda n waman qqaṛen-as s tɛibṛanit : « Bitesda ». Zzin-as-d xemsa ifragen yesɛan ssqef.

5:3 Deg ifragen-agi aṭas n imuḍan i gḍelqen, iderɣalen, iɛibanen d wukrifen țṛaǧun akk ad ḥerrken waman.

5:4 Axaṭer tiswiɛin yețṣubbu-d yiwen n lmelk ɣer temda, yețḥerrik aman-nni ; win ara ikecmen d amezwaru ɣer temda m'ara ḥerrken waman ad yeḥlu si lehlak-is akken yebɣu yili.

5:5 Yella dinna yiwen wergaz tmanya utlatin iseggasen nețța di lehlak.

5:6 Sidna Ɛisa mi t-iwala yeḍleq, yeẓra belli aṭas aya segmi yuḍen, yenna-yas: Tebɣiḍ aț-țeḥluḍ ?

5:7 Amuḍin-nni yerra-yas-ed : A Sidi, ur sɛiɣ ara win ara yi gren ɣer temda m'ara ḥerken waman ; m'ara ɛeṛdeɣ ad kecmeɣ wayeḍ a yi-izwir.

5:8 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : Ekker fell-ak, ddem tagertilt-ik telḥuḍ !

5:9 Imiren kan argaz-nni yeḥla, yeddem tagertilt-is yebda tikli. Ayagi yedṛa deg wass n ssebt yellan d ass n westeɛfu.

5:10 Lecyux n wat Isṛail nnan-as i wergaz-nni yeḥlan : Ass-agi d ass n westeɛfu, d leḥṛam fell-ak aț-țebbibeḍ tagertilt-ik !

5:11 Nețța yerra-yasen : Win i yi-sseḥlan d nețța i yi-d yennan : « Ddem tagertilt-ik telḥuḍ. »

5:12 Steqsan-t nnan-as : Anwa i k-d-yennan akka ?

5:13 Lameɛna argaz-nni yeḥlan ur yeẓri ara anwa-t, axaṭer Sidna Ɛisa yekcem ger lɣaci ițemdeḥḥasen dinna.

5:14 Taswiɛt kan, Sidna Ɛisa yemlal-it-id deg wefrag n lǧameɛ iqedsen yenna yas : Aql-ik teḥliḍ, sya d asawen xḍu i ddnub iwakken ur ideṛṛu ara yid-ek akteṛ n wannect-nni !

5:15 Argaz-nni iṛuḥ ad ixebbeṛ lecyux n wat Isṛail belli d Sidna Ɛisa i t-yesseḥlan.

5:16 Lecyux n wat Isṛail bdan accetki ɣef Sidna Ɛisa imi i gexdem ayagi deg wass n westeɛfu.

5:17 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : Baba Ṛebbi ixeddem ar tura, nekk daɣen aql-i xeddmeɣ !

5:18 ?ef wawal-agi lecyux-nni ufan-as-ed sebba nniḍen, țqelliben amek ara t-nɣen, mačči kan imi ur iqudeṛ ara ass n westeɛfu meɛna imi gerra Ṛebbi d Baba-s yerna yesseɛdel iman-is d Ṛebbi.

5:19 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yasen : S tideț qqaṛeɣ-awen : Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ur ixeddem acemma s yiman is, ixeddem ala ayen yeẓra ixeddem-it Baba-s. Kra n wayen ixeddem Baba-s ixeddem-it Mmi-s daɣen.

5:20 Axaṭer Baba-s iḥemmel Mmi-s, yesbeggin-as-d lecɣal-is meṛṛa. Ad as-yernu tazmert s wacu ara yexdem ayen yugaren kra n wayen teẓram ar ass-a, aț-țwehmem di lecɣal-is.

5:21 Akken Baba Ṛebbi yeskkaray-ed lmegtin ițțarra-yasen tudert, akken daɣen Mmi-s yețțak tudert i win yebɣa.

5:22 Baba Ṛebbi ur iḥekkem ɣef yiwen, lameɛna yerra lḥekma ger ifassen n Mmi-s

5:23 iwakken imdanen meṛṛa ad qadṛen Mmi-s akken țqadaṛen Baba-s. Win ur nqudeṛ ara Mmi-s ur iqudeṛ ara Baba-s i t-id iceggɛen.

5:24 S tideț nniɣ-awen : « kra n win yesmeḥsisen i wawal-iw, yumnen s win i yi-d-iceggɛen yesɛa tudert n dayem yerna ulac fell-as lḥisab, lameɛna iɛedda yakan si lmut ɣer tudert.

5:25 S tideț nniɣ-awen : « iteddu-d lweqt yerna yewweḍ-ed anda lmegtin ad slen i ṣṣut n Mmi-s n Ṛebbi, yerna wid akk ara s-yeslen ad idiren.

5:26 Akken yesɛa Baba Ṛebbi tudert deg yiman-is i gefka daɣen i Mmi-s ad yesɛu tudert deg yiman-is.

5:27 Yerra ger ifassen-is lḥekma imi d Mmi-s n bunadem i gella.

5:28 Ur țțawhamet ara, axaṭer lweqt iteddu-d, wid akk yellan deg i?ekwan ad slen i ṣṣut n Mmi-s n bunadem,

5:29 imiren a d-kkren ; wid ixedmen lxiṛ a d-ḥyun ɣer tudert n dayem ma d wid ixedmen cceṛ a d-ḥyun iwakken ad țțuḥasben.

5:30 Ur xeddmeɣ acemma s lebɣi-w lameɛna ḥekkmeɣ s lebɣi n Baba Ṛebbi. Lḥekma-inu d lḥeqq axaṭer ur țqellibeɣ ara ad xedmeɣ lebɣi-w, lameɛna lebɣi n win i yi-d-iceggɛen. »

5:31 Ma yella d nekk kan i d icehden ɣef yiman-iw cchada-inu ur tețwaqbal ara.

5:32 Lameɛna sɛiɣ inigi nniḍen, yerna cchada-ines tṣeḥḥa.

5:33 Tceggɛem ɣer Yeḥya testeqsam-t, nețța icehhed-ed ɣef tideț.

5:34 Nekk ur țkileɣ ara ɣef cchada n wemdan, nniɣ-awen-d annect-agi akken aț-țețwasellkem.

5:35 Yeḥya yusa-d ț-țaftilt yețfeǧǧiǧen ger yemdanen, ma d kunwi tebɣam aț-țferḥem taswiɛt kan di tafat-is.

5:36 Tella fell-i cchada nniḍen yugaren tin n Yeḥya : d lecɣal i yi d-yefka Baba Ṛebbi a ten-xedmeɣ. Lecɣal-agi țcehhiden-d fell-i belli d Baba Ṛebbi i yi-d-iceggɛen.

5:37 Ula d Baba Ṛebbi i yi d-iceggɛen icehhed-ed fell-i, meɛna kunwi leɛmeṛ ur teslim i ṣṣut-is, ur teẓrim udem-is.

5:38 Awal-is ur yezdiɣ ara deg wulawen nwen imi ur tuminem ara s win i d-iceggeɛ.

5:39 Tețnadim di tektabin iqedsen tɣilem aț-țafem deg-sent tudert n dayem. Tiktabin-agi d nutenti i-d-icehden fell-i.

5:40 Lameɛna kunwi tugim a d-tasem ɣuṛ-i aț-țesɛum tudert.

5:41 Ur țqellibeɣ ara ad sɛuɣ cciɛa ɣer yemdanen,

5:42 ?riɣ belli deg wulawen-nwen ur tḥemmlem ara Ṛebbi.

5:43 Nekk usiɣ-ed s yisem n Baba Ṛebbi, kunwi ur iyi-teqbilem ara. Lemmer a d-yas wayeḍ a d-icehhed ɣef yiman-is a t-tqeblem !

5:44 Amek ara tesɛum liman ma yella tețnadim aț-țemyeɛǧabem wway gar awen wala aț-țɛeǧbem kan i Ṛebbi ?

5:45 ?adret aț-țɣilem d nekk ara iccetkin fell-awen zdat Baba Ṛebbi ! D Musa ara iccetkin fell-awen, d Musa-nni ɣef i tețțeklem.

5:46 Lemmer ț-țideț tumnem s Musa tili tumnem yis-i, axaṭer yura-d fell-i di tira iqedsen.

6:1 Syenna, Sidna Ɛisa yezger lebḥeṛ n Jlili ( iwumi qqaṛen daɣen lebḥeṛ n Tiberyas ).

6:2 Aṭas n yemdanen i geddan yid-es mi walan yesseḥlay imuḍan.

6:3 Daymi Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer wedrar ad innejmaɛ d inelmaden-is.

6:4 Lɛid n wat Isṛail iwumi qqaṛen Tafaska n izimer n leslak tqeṛb-ed.

6:5 Akken i gwala lɣaci iteddu-d ɣuṛ-es, Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq ɣer Filibus yenna-yas : Ansi ara d-naɣ aɣṛum i wannect agi n lɣaci ?

6:6 Yesteqsa-t kan iwakken ad i?er d acu ara s-d-yini, axaṭer nețța yeẓra yakan d acu ara yexdem.

6:7 Filibus yerra-yas : A Sidi ur d-keffunt ara mitin alef iwakken mkul yiwen deg-sen a t-id-iṣaḥ ciṭṭuḥ n weɣṛum.

6:13 Ayen i d-yegran si xemsa n teḥbulin-nni n weɣṛum n temẓin, jemɛen-t-id ččuṛen yis tnac n iḍellaɛen (tiquftin).

6:15 Sidna Ɛisa mi geẓra lɣaci bɣan a t-rren d agellid fell-asen, iḍeṛṛef iman-is, yuli ɣer wedrar.

6:17 Rekben taflukt ad zegren ɣer ugemmaḍ, ɣer Kafernaḥum. Yeɣli-d yakan ṭṭlam, Sidna Ɛisa urɛad yelḥiq ɣuṛ-sen.

6:27 Ur țḥebbiṛet ara ɣef yiɛebbaḍ-nwen, ḥebbṛet ɣef tudert yețdumun i dayem. Tudert-agi, d Mmi-s n bunadem ara wen-ț-id-yefken, Baba Ṛebbi yefka-yas tazmert imi i t-iɛellem s ṭṭabeɛ-ines.

6:28 Nnan-as : D acu ara nexdem ihi, d acu i gețṛaǧu Ṛebbi deg-nneɣ ?

6:29 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Sidi Ṛebbi yețṛaǧu deg-wen aț-țamnem s win i d-iceggeɛ !

6:30 Nutni nnan-as : Ihi acu n lbeṛhan ara ɣ-d-tessekneḍ akken a namen yis-ek ? D acu ara txedmeḍ ?

6:31 Lejdud-nneɣ ččan tamanna deg unezṛuf, akken yura di ccariɛa n Musa : Yefka-yasen ččan aɣṛum i d-yekkan seg igenni.

6:32 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : S tideț nniɣ-awen, mačči d Musa i wen-d-yefkan aɣṛum i d-yekkan seg igenni, lameɛna d Baba Ṛebbi ! D nețța i wen-d-yețțaken aɣṛum n ṣṣeḥ i d-itekken seg igenni.

6:33 Axaṭer aɣṛum i d-yekkan s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, d win i d-iṣubben seg igenni, d nețța i d-yețțaken tudert n ṣṣeḥ i yemdanen.

6:34 Nutni nnan-as : A Sidi țțak-aɣ-d mkul ass seg weɣṛum-agi.

6:35 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : D Nekk i d aɣṛum yețțaken tudert. Win i d-yusan ɣuṛ-i ur yețțuɣal ara ad illaẓ, win yumnen yis-i ur yețțuɣal ara ad iffad.

6:36 Nniɣ-awen-t-id yakan : twalam yerna tugim aț-țamnem !

6:37 Wid akk ara yi-d-yefk Baba Ṛebbi a d-asen ɣuṛ-i ; win ara d-yasen ɣuṛ-i ur t-țarraɣ ara ɣef tewwurt.

6:38 Ur d-ṣubbeɣ ara seg igenni akken ad xedmeɣ lebɣi-w, ṣubbeɣ-d ad xedmeɣ lebɣi nwin i yi-d-iceggɛen.

6:39 Win i yi-d iceggɛen ur yebɣi ara ad yeɛṛeq ula d yiwen seg wid i yi-d-yefka, lameɛna a ten-id-sseḥyuɣ si lmut deg wass aneggaru.

6:40 D wagi i d lebɣi n Baba Ṛebbi: « kra n win yețmuqulen ɣer Mmi-s, yumen yis, ad yesɛu tudert n dayem yerna a t-id-sseḥyuɣ si lmut ass aneggaru.»

6:41 Lɣaci bdan smermugen gar-asen ɣef Sidna Ɛisa imi d yenna: « d nekk i d aɣṛum i d yekkan seg igenni.»

6:42 Qqaṛen : « Mačči d Ɛisa mmi-s n Yusef wagi ? Nessen baba-s nessen yemma-s, amek i gezmer a d-yini tura : ṣubbeɣ-d seg igenni ? »

6:43 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Beṛkat asmermeg gar-awen !

6:44 Ulac win i gzemren a d-yas ɣuṛ-i ma yella mačči d Baba Ṛebbi i s-d yessawlen, yerna nekk a t-id-sseḥyuɣ si lmut ass aneggaru.

6:45 Akken yura deg idlisen n lenbiya : « Ad lemden akk s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi. » Kra n win isellen i wawal n Baba Ṛebbi, ilemden s ɣuṛ-es, a d-yas ɣuṛ-i.

6:46 Ulac win yeẓran Baba Ṛebbi ala win i d-yusan s ɣuṛ-es, nețța yeẓra Baba Ṛebbi.

6:47 S tideț nniɣ-awen : kra n win yumnen, yesɛa tudert n dayem,

6:48 axaṭer d nekk i d aɣṛum i d yețțaken tudert.

6:49 ?as akken lejdud nwen ččan tamanna deg unezṛuf, mmuten.

6:50 Atan weɣṛum i d-yekkan seg igenni : win ara yeččen seg-s ur yețmețțat ara.

6:51 D nekk i d aɣṛum n tudert i d yekkan seg igenni. Kra n win ara yeččen seg weɣṛum-agi ad yidir i dayem. Aɣṛum ara d-fkeɣ d lǧețța w, yis ara tɛic ddunit.

6:52 Mi slan wat Isṛail i wannect-agi bdan țemjadalen qqaṛen wway gar asen : Amek akka i gezmer a ɣ-d-yefk aksum-is a t-nečč ?

6:53 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen daɣen : S tideț a wen-d-iniɣ: m'ur teččim ara si lǧețța n Mmi-s n bunadem, m'ur teswim ara seg idammen-is ur tețțili ara deg-wen tudert.

6:54 Win ara yeččen si lǧețța-w, ara yeswen seg idammen-iw ad yesɛu tudert n dayem, yerna a t-id-sseḥyuɣ si lmut ass aneggaru.

6:55 Axaṭer lǧețța-w d lqut n ṣṣeḥ, idammen-iw ț-țissit n ṣṣeḥ.

6:56 Win ara yeččen si lǧețța-w, ara yeswen seg idammen-iw ad yili deg-i nekk ad iliɣ deg-s.

6:57 Baba Ṛebbi i yi-d-iceggɛen deg-s i tella tudert, d nețța i yi ssidiren. Akken daɣen win ara yeččen seg-i ad yidir yis-i.

6:58 D wagi i d aɣṛum i d-yekkan seg igenni. Mačči am weɣṛum-nni i ččan lejdud-nwen ; nutni mmuten, ma d win ara yeččen aɣṛum-agi ad yidir i dayem.

6:59 D imeslayen-agi i d-yenna Sidna Ɛisa asmi i gesselmed di lǧameɛ n wat Isṛail di temdint n Kafernaḥum.

6:60 Mi s-slan inelmaden-is, aṭas deg-sen nnan : Imeslayen-agi weɛṛen, anwa i gzemren a ten-yeqbel ?

6:61 Sidna Ɛisa, mi geẓra inelmaden is sgermuden wway gar-asen ɣef wayen i d-yenna, yenna-yasen : Iqṛeḥ-ikkun lḥal ?

6:62 D acu ara tinim ihi m'ara teẓrem Mmi-s n bunadem yuɣal ɣer wanda yella di tazwara ?

6:63 D Ṛṛuḥ i d-yețțaken tudert amdan ur yețțaweḍ ɣer wacemma ma yețkel ɣef yiman-is. Imeslayen-agi i wen-d-nniɣ, d wigi i d Ṛṛuḥ, d wigi i ț-țudert.

6:64 Lexsaṛa : llan kra gar awen ur numin ara ! Si tazwara Sidna Ɛisa yeẓra anwi deg-sen ur numin ara, yeẓra daɣen anwa ara t-ixedɛen.

6:65 Yenna-yasen daɣen : Daymi i wen-d-nniɣ : ulac win i gzemren a d-yas ɣuṛ-i ma yella mačči d Baba Ṛebbi i s-d-issawlen. »

6:66 Seg imiren, aṭas i t-yeǧǧan seg inelmaden-is , rewlen fell-as.

6:67 Sidna Ɛisa yezzi ɣer tnac inelmaden-is, yenna-yasen : Ur tebɣim ara aț-țṛuḥem ula d kunwi ?

6:68 Semɛun Buṭrus yerra-yas : A Sidi, anwa i ɣer ara nṛuḥ ? ?uṛ-ek i gella wawal n tudert.

6:69 Nukni nesɛa laman deg-k, yerna neẓra belli d kečč i d Imqeddes i d-iceggeɛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

6:70 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Mačči d nekk i kkun-ixtaṛen di tnac yid-wen ? ?as akken yiwen gar awen d Cciṭan .

6:71 Yenna-d annect-agi ɣef Yudas, mmi-s n Semɛun n taddart n Qeṛyut, yiwen ger tnac inelmaden-is ara t-ixedɛen.

7:1 Deffir wayagi, Sidna Ɛisa ikemmel abrid-is iteddu di tmurt n Jlili. Ur yebɣi ara ad iṛuḥ ɣer tmurt n Yahuda, axaṭer imeqqranen n wat Isṛail țqelliben fell-as iwakken a t nɣen.

7:2 Lɛid i deg xeddmen wat Isṛail tiɛecciwin iqeṛṛeb-ed.

7:3 Dɣa atmaten is nnan-as : Kker aț-țṛuḥeḍ ɣer tmurt n Yahuda akken inelmaden-ik yellan dinna ad ẓren ayen i txeddmeḍ.

7:4 Win yebɣan ad yețwassen, ur ilaq ara ad iffer wayen i gxeddem. Imi tzemreḍ i lecɣal imeqqranen, sbeggen-ed iman-ik a k-walin akk yemdanen.

7:5 Atmaten-is s yiman-nsen ur uminen ara yis.

7:6 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Ma yella ɣuṛ-wen iwweḍ-ed lweqt, nekk ɣuṛ-i mazal.

7:7 Medden ur sɛin ara sebba s wayes ara kkun keṛhen, keṛhen-iyi nekk imi d-sbeggneɣ yir lecɣal-nsen.

7:8 Kunwi ṛuḥet aț-țɛeggdem, ma d nekk ur tedduɣ ara, imi lweqt-iw urɛad i d-yusi.

7:9 Mi sen-d-yenna ayagi, yeǧǧa-ten ṛuḥen, nețța yeqqim di tmurt n Jlili.

7:10 Mi ṛuḥen watmaten-is ad ɛeggden, iṛuḥ ula d nețța mbla ma yesseɛlem yiwen.

7:11 Deg ussan n lɛid, imeqqranen n wat Isṛail țnadin fell-as, steqsayen anda yella.

7:12 Lɣaci i d-yusan ad ɛeggden, bdan țmeslayen fell-as. Kra deg-sen qqaṛen : D argaz n lxiṛ ! Wiyaḍ qqaṛen : Awah ! Yețkellix kan ɣef lɣaci !

7:13 Yiwen ur d-yehdiṛ fell-as ɛinani axaṭer uggaden imeqqranen n wat Isṛail.

7:14 Mi gneṣṣef lɛid n iɛecciwen, Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer lǧameɛ iqedsen, yebda yesselmad.

7:15 At Isṛail wehmen, qqaṛen : Ur yeɣṛi ara d lɛulama yerna yessen tira iqedsen, ansi i s-d-tekka tmusni-yagi ?

7:16 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : Ayen i sselmadeɣ mačči s ɣuṛ-i i d-yekka, yekka-d s ɣuṛ win i yi-d iceggɛen.

7:17 Win iqesden ad yexdem lebɣi n Ṛebbi ad yeɛqel ma yella wayen i sselmadeɣ yekka-d s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi neɣ d imeslayen-iw kan.

7:18 Win iheddṛen s yisem-is yețqellib ad yawi cciɛa i yiman-is. Ma d win yețqelliben a d-yawi cciɛa i win i t-id iceggɛen yeqqaṛ tideț, ulac deg-s lexdeɛ.

7:19 Nnbi Musa yeǧǧa-yawen-d ccariɛa, lameɛna ulac gar-awen win i ț-itebɛen ! Acuɣeṛ i tețqellibem a yi tenɣem ?

7:20 Lɣaci nnan-as : Anwa akka i gebɣan a k-ineɣ ? Waqila ikcem-ik lǧen !

7:21 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : ?ef wayen akka xedmeɣ i twehmem ?

7:22 Meyzet ɣef wayagi : nnbi Musa yeǧǧa-yawen-d lextana (ṭṭhaṛa), lɛadda-agi mačči s ɣuṛ-es i d-tekka meɛna s ɣuṛ lejdud-is. Atan tqeblem tessextanem deg wass n westeɛfu.

7:23 Ma yella tqeblem aț-țesxetnem i weqcic deg ass n westeɛfu, iwakken ur tețxalafem ara ccariɛa n Musa, acuɣeṛ terfam fell-i imi sseḥlaɣ amdan s lekmal-is deg wass n westeɛfu ?

7:24 Beṛkat ! Ur ḥekkmet ara s wayen kan i țwalint wallen-nwen, lemdet aț-țḥekmem s lḥeqq.

7:25 Kra n imezdaɣ n temdint n Lquds wehmen, qqaṛen : Mačči d win akken i țqelliben a t-nɣen ?

7:26 Atan iheddeṛ ɛinani zdat lɣaci, yiwen ur s-yenni acemma ! Ɛeqlen-t lɛulama-nneɣ d nețța i d Lmasiḥ ?

7:27 Argaz-agi neẓra ansi-t, lameɛna Lmasiḥ asm'ara d-yas yiwen ur iẓerr ansi ara d-yekk.

7:28 Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq s ṣṣut ɛlayen, slan-as-d akk deg wefrag n Lǧameɛ iqedsen, yenna : Tessnem-iyi yerna teẓram ansi i d-kkiɣ ! Ur d-usiɣ ara s lebɣi-w, lameɛna d Bab n lḥeqq i yi-d iceggɛen; kunwi ur t-tessinem ara.

7:29 Nekkini ssneɣ-t axaṭer s ɣuṛ-es i d-usiɣ yerna d nețța i yi-d-iceggɛen.

7:30 Bɣan a t-ṭṭfen imiren, meɛna yiwen ur yesris afus-is fell-as imi ass-is urɛad d-yewwiḍ.

7:31 Aṭas n lɣaci si terbaɛt-nni i gumnen yis, qqaṛen : M'ara d-yas Lmasiḥ, eɛni ad ixdem lbeṛhanat yugaren wigi ?

7:32 Ifariziyen wwin-d s lexbaṛ s wayen heddṛen lɣaci ɣef Sidna Ɛisa, dɣa lmuqedmin imeqqranen akk-d ifariziyen ceggɛen iɛessasen a t-id ṭṭfen.

7:33 Sidna Ɛisa yenna i lɣaci : Mazal-iyi kra n lweqt yid-wen, syin akkin ad uɣaleɣ ɣer win i yi-d iceggɛen.

7:34 Aț-țqellbem fell-i ur iyi-tețțafem ara imi ur tezmirem ara a d-tasem ɣer wanda ara yiliɣ.

7:35 At Isṛail qqaṛen wway gar-asen : Anda akka ara iṛuḥ armi ur nețțizmir ara a t-naf ? Eɛni ad iṛuḥ ɣer wat Isṛail ițwazerɛen ger leǧnas ? Neɣ ibɣa ad isselmed leǧnas nniḍen ?

7:36 D acu i gebɣa a d-yini mi d-iqqaṛ : « aț-țqellbem fell-i, ur iyi-tețțafem ara axaṭer ur tezmirem ara a d-tasem ɣer wanda ara yiliɣ.»

7:37 Ass aneggaru n lɛid, yellan d ass yesɛan aṭas n lqima ; Sidna Ɛisa ibedden ger lɣaci iɛeggeḍ, yenna : Win iffuden a d-yas ɣuṛ-i ad isew ; win yumnen yis-i a d-yas ad isew.

7:38 Akken yura di lkutub : D isaffen n waman ara d-ițfeggiḍen seg-s.

7:39 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-d ayagi ɣef Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ara yeččaṛen ulawen n wid yumnen yis. Di lweqt-nni, Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen mazal ur d-ițțunefk ara axaṭer Sidna Ɛisa werɛad yekcim tamanegt-is.

7:40 Ger lɣaci i s-d-yeslan, llan wid yeqqaṛen : Argaz-agi mbla ccekk d nețța i d nnbi-nni ara d-yasen !

7:41 Wiyaḍ qqaṛen : D nețța i d Lmasiḥ ! Ma d kra nniḍen qqaṛen : Yezmer Lmasiḥ a d-iffeɣ si tmurt n Jlili ?

7:42 Ur yuri ara di tira iqedsen : Lmasiḥ a d-yeffeɣ si dderya n Sidna Dawed, si taddart n Bitlḥem ?

7:47 Ifariziyen nnan-asen : Iseḥḥer-ikkun ula d kunwi ?

7:52 Nutni rran-as : Ula d kečč d ajlili ? Qelleb mliḥ di tira iqedsen aț-țafeḍ : « Ulac nnbi ara d-yekken si tmurt n Jlili.»

8:1 Sidna Ɛisa yuli s yiɣil uzemmur ;

8:3 Atnaya lɛulama n ccariɛa akk-d ifariziyen wwin-as-d yiwet n tmeṭṭut ṭṭfen-ț txeddem zzna. Sbedden-ț ger lɣaci akken a ț iwali Sidna Ɛisa.

8:4 Nnan-as : A Sidi, neṭṭef tameṭṭut-agi mi txeddem zzna.

8:5 Di ccariɛa, Sidna Musa yumeṛ-aɣ-d a neṛjem s yedɣaɣen tameṭṭut am tagi alamma temmut. I keččini d acu ara d-tiniḍ ?

8:7 Imi t-ḥeṛṣen s usteqsi, yekker yenna-yasen : Win deg-wen werǧin yednib a ț-yeṛjem d amezwaru !

8:21 Sidna Ɛisa yerna yenna-yasen : Atan ad ṛuḥeɣ, aț-țqellbem fell-i lameɛna aț-țemtem di ddnub-nwen, ur tezmirem ara a d-tasem ɣer wanda ara ṛuḥeɣ.

11:11 Mi d-yenna ayagi, yerna yenna yasen : Aḥbib-nneɣ Laɛẓar tewwi-t tnafa, ad ṛuḥeɣ a t-id-ssakiɣ.

11:12 ?ef wayagi inelmaden-is nnan as : A Sidi, ma ț-țaguni i geṭṭes ihi a d-yekker.

11:13 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-d ayagi ɣef lmut n Laɛẓar, nutni nwan ɣef tguni i d-yemmeslay.

11:14 Dɣa yenna-yasen ɛinani : Laɛẓar yemmut,

11:15 yerna feṛḥeɣ ɣef ddemma-nwen imi ur lliɣ ara dinna di teswiɛt-nni, axaṭer ayagi d ayen ara yernun di liman-nwen. Eyyaw tura a nṛuḥet ɣuṛ-es.

11:16 ?uma iwumi qqaṛen Akniw, yenna i inelmaden nniḍen : Eyyaw a neddut ula d nukkni iwakken a nemmet yid-es.

11:17 Mi qṛib ad yaweḍ ɣer dinna, Sidna Ɛisa yesla belli ṛebɛa wussan aya segmi i meḍlen Laɛẓar.

11:18 Ad ilin wazal n tlata alaf kilumitrat ger taddart n Bitanya ț-țemdint n Lquds.

11:19 Daymi aṭas n lɣaci i d-yusan ɣer Marṭa d Meryem a tent-ṣebbṛen ɣef lmut n gma-tsent.

11:20 Akken kan i tesla Marṭa belli Sidna Ɛisa iteddu-d ɣer taddart, tuzzel ɣuṛ-es a t-temmager. Ma d Meryem teqqim deg wexxam.

11:21 Marṭa tenna i Sidna Ɛisa : A Sidi, lemmer dagi i telliḍ, tili gma ur yemmut ara.

11:22 Lameɛna ẓriɣ ula ț-țura, ayen akk ara tessutreḍ i Ṛebbi a k-t-id-yeqbel.

11:23 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : Gma-m a d-yuɣal ɣer tudert.

11:24 Marṭa terra-yas : ?riɣ a d-yuɣal ɣer tudert ass aneggaru, ass n ḥeggu n lmegtin.

11:25 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : D nekk i d ḥeggu, d nekk i ț-țudert. Win ara yesɛun laman deg-i, ad yidir ɣas ma yemmut.

11:26 Yal amdan yeddren, yumnen yis-i, ur yețmețțat ara maḍi. Tumneḍ s wannect-agi ?

11:27 Marṭa tenna-yas : Umneɣ a Sidi, d kečč i d Lmasiḥ, d kečč i d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi, win akken ara d-yasen ɣer ddunit.

11:28 Syenna, Marṭa tṛuḥ tessawel i weltma-s Meryem tenna-yas s tuffra : Sidna Ɛisa yewweḍ-ed, yebɣa a kem-iẓer.

11:29 Mi tesla s lexbaṛ-agi, tekker Meryem tuzzel ɣer Sidna Ɛisa.

11:30 Yuɣ lḥal urɛad i d-yekcim ɣer taddart, mazal-it deg umkan-nni anda akken i t-temmuger Marṭa.

11:31 Wid yellan țṣebbiṛen deg-s deg wexxam, mi ț-walan tekker teffeɣ s tazzla, ḍefṛen-ț, nwan ɣer u?ekka i tṛuḥ iwakken aț-țețru ɣef gma-s.

11:32 Mi tewweḍ Meryem ɣer wemkan anda yella Sidna Ɛisa, akken kan i t-twala, teɣli ɣer iḍaṛṛen-is tenna : A Sidi, lemmer dagi i telliḍ tili gma ur yemmut ara.

11:33 Mi ț-iwala tețru nețțat d wid yellan yid-es, Sidna Ɛisa ijreḥ wul-is, tergagi tasa-s.

11:34 Yenna-yasen : Anda i t-tmeḍlem ? Nutni rran-as : Eyya-d aț-țwaliḍ a Sidi.

11:35 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yețru.

11:36 Mi t-walan yețru, nnan : Walit acḥal i t-iḥemmel !

11:37 Ma d kra deg-sen qqaṛen : Nețța i d-yerran iẓri i uderɣal, ur yezmir ara ad issemneɛ Laɛẓar si lmut !

11:38 Sidna Ɛisa tejreḥ tasa-s tikkelt tis snat. Mi wwḍen ɣer u?ekka yellan d lɣaṛ imedlen s yiwen wezṛu d ameqqran,

11:39 Sidna Ɛisa yenna : Kkset azṛu-yagi ! Marṭa weltma-s n lmegget-nni tenna : A Sidi, ṛebɛa wussan aya segmi yețwamḍel, ad yili yebda yețriḥ !

11:40 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ur am-d-nniɣ ara ma tumneḍ aț-țwaliḍ tamanegt n Ṛebbi ?

11:41 Kksen azṛu-nni, dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yerfed allen-is ɣer igenni yenna : Tanemmirt-ik a Baba Ṛebbi axaṭer tqebleḍ taẓallit-iw.

11:42 Nekkini ẓriɣ tqebbleḍ-iyi daymen, lameɛna nniɣ-ed akka ɣef ddemma n wid i yi-d-yezzin dagi iwakken ad amnen belli d kečč i yi-d-iceggɛen.

11:43 Imiren kan iɛeggeḍ s lǧehd yenna : A Laɛẓar, effeɣ-ed syenna !

11:44 Ataya lmegget-nni yeffeɣ-ed seg uẓekka, idaṛṛen-is d ifassen-is țțlen, udem-is iɣumm s lekfen. Sidna Ɛisa yenna i wid yellan dinna : Kkset-as lekfen, teǧǧem-t ad iṛuḥ !

11:45 Aṭas seg widak-nni i d-yeddan d Meryem umnen s Sidna Ɛisa mi walan ayen yexdem.

11:46 Kra deg-sen ṛuḥen ɣer ifariziyen ṣṣawḍen-asen lexbaṛ.

11:47 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen d ifariziyen nnejmaɛen deg wexxam n ccṛeɛ, nnan : D acu ara nexdem ? Argaz-agi yexdem aṭas n lbeṛhanat,

11:48 ma neǧǧa-t ad ikemmel akka, lɣaci akk ad amnen yis ; a d-asen iṛumaniyen a ɣ-hudden lǧameɛ-nneɣ ț-țmurt-nneɣ.

11:49 Kayef yellan aseggas-nni d lmuqeddem ameqqran fell-asen,

11:50 yenṭeq yenna-yasen : Ur teẓrim ara belli ad yemmet axiṛ yiwen ɣef lumma wala ad yenger wegdud-nneɣ ?

11:51 Ayagi mačči s ɣuṛ-es i d-yekka meɛna imi i gella d lmuqeddem ameqqran aseggas-nni. Daymi i s-d-iweḥḥa Sidi Ṛebbi belli ilaq ad immet Sidna Ɛisa ɣef wegdud.

11:52 Mačči kan ɣef wegdud-is ara yemmet meɛna akken ad isdukkel arraw n Ṛebbi i gemzerwaɛen di ddunit meṛṛa, a ten-yejmeɛ d yiwen wegdud.

11:53 Ass-nni dɣa i ɛewwlen ( qesden ) lmuqedmin imeqqranen ad nɣen Sidna Ɛisa.

11:54 Seg wass-nni, Sidna Ɛisa yejbed iman-is ɣef lɣaci. Iṛuḥ Syenna ɣer taddart n Ifṛahim i d-yezgan ț-țama unezṛuf, yesɛedda dinna kra n wussan nețța d inelmaden is.

11:55 Akken i d-iqeṛṛeb lɛid n Tfaska, aṭas n lɣaci i d-ițasen si mkul tamurt ɣer temdint n Lquds akken ad ssizedegen iman-nsen.

11:56 Lɣaci-nni țqelliben ɣef Sidna Ɛisa, țmesteqsayen deg ufrag n lǧameɛ, qqaṛen : D acu i twalam ? A d-yas ad iɛeggeḍ ?

11:57 Yuɣ lḥal lmuqedmin imeqqranen d ifariziyen nebhen-d nnan : Win yeẓran anda yella Ɛisa-nni, ilaq a d-yessiweḍ lexbaṛ i yiɛessasen akken a t-ṭṭfen.

12:1 Sețța wussan uqbel lɛid n Tfaska, Sidna Ɛisa iṛuḥ ɣer Bitani, taddart n Laɛẓar win akken i d-yesseḥya si lmut.

12:2 At wexxam n Laɛẓar heggan-as imensi. Marṭa tqeddec, Laɛẓar yeqqim ɣer imensi akk-d Sidna Ɛisa.

12:3 Meryem teddem-ed azgen n litra n leɛṭeṛ yeṣfan ɣlayen nezzeh, tesmar-it ɣef yiḍaṛṛen n Sidna Ɛisa, tesfeḍ-iten s ucebbub-is ; axxam meṛṛa yeččuṛ d rriḥa n leɛṭeṛ-nni.

12:4 Yudas n Qeṛyut, yiwen seg inelmaden-is, win akken ara t-ixedɛen inṭeq-ed yenna :

12:5 Acuɣeṛ ur nezzenz ara leɛṭeṛ agi ? Lemmer nezzenz-it tili yewwi-d azal n telt meyya twiztin n lfeṭṭa ara nefṛeq i igellilen.

12:6 Mi d-yenna annect-agi mačči d igellilen i t-iɣaḍen meɛna yebɣa ad yawi idrimen-nni, axaṭer d amakar i gella. D nețța i d amdebbeṛ ɣef yidrimen-nsen, yețțaker-iten.

12:7 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : Anfet-as ! Leɛṭeṛ-agi thegga-t i wass n temḍelt-iw.

12:8 Igellilen a ten-tesɛum daymen gar-awen ! Ma d nekk, ur iyi-tseɛɛum ara daymen yid-wen.

12:9 Yuɣ lḥal lɣaci slan belli Sidna Ɛisa di taddart n Bitanya i gella. Dɣa ț-țirebbaɛ i d-iṛuḥen ɣuṛ-es, mačči kan iwakken a t-ẓren nețța, meɛna bɣan ad ẓren Laɛẓar i d yesseḥya si lmut.

12:10 ?ef wannect-a, lmuqedmin imeqqranen qesden ad nɣen ula d Laɛẓar.

12:11 Axaṭer ɣef ddemma-ines aṭas n lɣaci i sen iwexxṛen uɣalen umnen s Sidna Ɛisa.

12:12 Azekka-nni, aṭas n lɣaci i d yusan ad ɛeggden di temdint n Lquds. Slan belli Sidna Ɛisa iteddu-d ɣer temdint.

12:13 Kksen-d tiseḍwa ( ticiṭwin ) n tezdayt, ṛuḥen a t-mmagren, țeddun țɛeggiḍen : ?usana ! Yețțubarek win i d-yusan s yisem n Sidi Ṛebbi ! Yețțubarek ugellid n wat Isṛail !

12:14 Sidna Ɛisa yufa yiwen weɣyul d amecṭuḥ, yerkeb fell-as akken yura di tira iqedsen :

12:15  Ur țțaggad a tamdint n Siyun, atan ugellid-im yusa-d, irkeb-ed ɣef weɣyul amecṭuḥ.

12:16 Imiren inelmaden is ur fhimen ara ayen ideṛṛun, lameɛna asmi i gekcem Sidna Ɛisa di tamanegt-is, mmektan-d belli ayen akk yuran fell-as yedṛa yerna yețwakemmel.

12:17 Wid akk i gḥedṛen akk-d Sidna Ɛisa asmi i gessawel i Laɛẓar a d-iffeɣ seg u?ekka mi i t-id-yesseḥya si lmut, cehden-d ɣef wayen ẓran ;

12:18 ɣef wannect-agi aṭas n lɣaci i d-yusan ɣuṛ-es imi slan s lbeṛhan i gexdem.

12:19 Imi walan akkenni, ifariziyen nnan wway gar-asen : Twalam tura : ur nezmir i wacemma, lɣaci akk ttabaɛen-t !

12:20 Ger widak-nni i d-yusan ad ɛeggden di temdint n Lquds, llan kra n iyunaniyen.

12:21 Iyunaniyen-agi ṛuḥen ɣer Filibus ajlili n taddart n Bitsayda, nnan-as : Nebɣa a nẓer Sidna Ɛisa !

12:22 Filibus ixebbeṛ Andriyus, dɣa ddukklen i sin a s-inin i Sidna Ɛisa.

12:23 Nețța yerra-yasen-d : Yewweḍ-ed lweqt i Mmi-s n bunadem ad ikcem di tmanegt-is.

12:24 S tideț, nniɣ-awen : aɛeqqa n yired yeɣlin deg wakal m'ur yemmut ara ad iqqim weḥd-es, lameɛna ma yemmut ad yefti, a d-yefk aṭas n iɛeqqayen.

12:25 Win yețḥadaren ɣef tudert-is ad as-tṛuḥ, ma d win ur nețḥebbiṛ ara fell-as di ddunit-agi a ț-yaf i tudert n dayem.

12:26 Win yebɣan ad iqdec fell-i, a yi-d-yetbeɛ. Anda bɣuɣ iliɣ, aqeddac-iw ad yili yid-i. Win iqeddcen fell-i, Ṛebbi a t-iɛuzz.

12:27 Atan tura ul-iw yetḥeyyeṛ ; d acu ara d-iniɣ ? A Baba sellek-iyi si teswiɛt-agi ? Nekk ẓriɣ ɣef teswiɛt agi i d-usiɣ iwakken a ț-qableɣ !

12:28 A Baba, sbeggen-ed tamanegt-ik ! Dɣa tenṭeq-ed yiwet n taɣect seg igenni, tenna-d : Sbeggneɣ-d yakan tamanegt-iw yerna mazal a ț-id-sbeggneɣ.

12:29 Wid yuɣ lḥal dinna slan i ṣṣut nni, ɣilen d ṛɛud. Wiyaḍ qqaṛen d lmalayekkat i s-d yemmeslayen.

12:30 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Mačči ɣuṛ-i i d-tenṭeq taɣect agi, tenṭeq-ed ɣuṛ-wen.

12:31 Tura ara yedṛu lḥisab n ddunit. Tura ara yețwaqceɛ umesbaṭli n ddunit-a.

12:32 Ma d nekk m'ara țwarefdeɣ sennig lqaɛa, a d-awiɣ imdanen meṛṛa ɣuṛ-i.

12:33 S imeslayen-agi, Sidna Ɛisa yebɣa a d-isbeggen amek ara yemmet.

12:34 Lɣaci nnan-as : Ccariɛa tenna-d belli Lmasiḥ ad yidir i dayem. Amek i d-tenniḍ « Mmi-s n bunadem ilaq ad yețwarfed sennig lqaɛa » ? Ini-yaɣ-d ihi anwa i d Mmi-s n bunadem ?

12:35 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Tafat mazal-iț gar-awen i kra n lweqt kan, lḥut skud tella uqbel a kkun-iṭṭef ṭṭlam, axaṭer win ileḥḥun di ṭṭlam ur yeẓri ara anda iteddu.

12:36 Skud tesɛam tafat, amnet yis akken aț-țuɣalem d arraw n tafat. Mi d-yenna imeslayen-agi, Sidna Ɛisa iḍeṛṛef iman-is anda ulac lɣaci.

12:37 ?as akken aṭas n lbeṛhanat i gexdem Sidna Ɛisa zdat-sen, nutni ugin ad amnen.

12:38 S wakka i gețwakemmel wayen i d-yenna nnbi Iceɛya : A Ṛebbi, anwa i gumnen s wayen i d-nbecceṛ, iwumi i d-tețwabeggen tezmert-ik ?

12:39 Acuɣeṛ ugin ad amnen ? D nnbi Iceɛya i d-yennan daɣen :

12:40  Ṛebbi yesderɣel-iten , yesɣeṛ ulawen-nsen, daymi allen-nsen ur țwalint ara, lefhama-nsen teqfel , ugin a d-uɣalen ɣer webrid, a kken a ten-sseḥluɣ.

12:41 Nnbi Iceɛya yenna-d ayagi ɣef Sidna Ɛisa mi gwala tamanegt-is.

12:42 Ula seg imeqqranen n wat Isṛail aṭas i gumnen yis ; lameɛna ɣef ddemma n ifariziyen, ur bɣin ara a d-sbeggnen liman-nsen, axaṭer uggaden a ten-sṭixxṛen si lǧameɛ.

12:43 ḥemmlen ad sɛun ccan ɣer yemdanen wala ɣer Ṛebbi.

12:44 Sidna Ɛisa yessaɛli taɣect-is yenna : Win yesɛan laman deg-i mačči yis-i kan i gumen, meɛna yumen ula s win i yi-d-iceggɛen.

12:45 Win i yi iwalan, iwala win i yi-d-iceggɛen.

12:46 Nekk usiɣ-ed ț-țafat ɣer ddunit, kra n win yumnen yis-i, ur yețɣimi ara di ṭṭlam.

12:47 Win yeslan i wawal-iw ur nexdim ara ayen i d-nniɣ mačči d nekk ara t-iḥasben, axaṭer ur d-usiɣ ara akken ad ḥasbeɣ ddunit, meɛna usiɣ-ed a ț-sellkeɣ.

12:48 Kra n win i yi-ḥeqṛen ur neḥsib ara imeslayen-iw, ițțuḥaseb yakan : d awal-agi i d-nniɣ ara t-iḥasben ass aneggaru.

12:49 Imeslayen-agi mačči s ɣuṛ-i i d-kkan : Baba i yi-d-iceggɛen, d nețța i yi-d-yefkan ayen i glaqen a t-id-iniɣ d wayen ara slemdeɣ.

12:50 Daymi nniɣ-awen : aselmed ɣef i yi-d-iweṣṣa Baba ț-țudert n dayem. Nekk sselmadeɣ kan ayen i yi-d-yenna Baba.

13:1 Uqbel lɛid n Tfaska n Izimer n leslak, Sidna Ɛisa yeẓra belli tewweḍ-ed teswiɛt i deg ara yeffeɣ si ddunit, akken ad iṛuḥ ɣer Baba-s. ?ef wannect-agi, isbeggen-ed leḥmala-ines i inelmaden-is yellan di ddunit, yerna yeǧǧa-yasen-d lmeɛna ț-țameqqrant.

13:2 Llan tețțen imensi n Tfaska, Cciṭan yekcem yakan deg wul n Yudas, mmi-s n Semɛun n Qeṛyut, iwelleh-it ad ixdeɛ Sidna Ɛisa, a t-yezzenz.

13:3 Sidna Ɛisa yeẓra belli Baba Ṛebbi yerra kullec ger ifassen-is, yeẓra s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi i d-yusa, ɣuṛ-es ara yuɣal.

13:4 Yekker ɣef yimensi, yekkes abeṛnus-is, yeddem-ed yiwen ubeḥnuq (ucettiḍ) yebges yis.

13:5 Syenna, yesmar-ed aman ɣer yiwet n tbaqit, yebda yessirid iḍaṛṛen n inelmaden-is, yesfeḍ-iten s ubeḥnuq-nni i gcudd ɣef wammas-is.

13:6 Mi gewweḍ ɣer Semɛun Buṭrus yenna-yas-d : A Sidi d kečč ara yi-ssirden iḍaṛṛen ?

13:7 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ayen xedmeɣ akka ur t-tfehhmeḍ ara tura, ɣer zdat ara t-tfehmeḍ.

13:8 Buṭrus yenna-yas : Ala ! D lmuḥal a yi-tessirdeḍ iḍaṛṛen-iw ! Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : M'ur k-ssardeɣ ara iḍaṛṛen-ik ur d-yeqqim wacemma i ɣ-icerken.

13:9 Semɛun Buṭrus yenna-yas : Ihi ur iyi-issirid ara iḍaṛṛen kan, ssired-iyi ula d ifassen d uqeṛṛuy-iw.

13:10 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : Win yessarden yakan d azedgan, anagar idaṛṛen-is i geḥwaǧ a ten yessired axaṭer zeddig meṛṛa. Kunwi aql-ikkun zeddigit lameɛna mačči akken ma tellam dagi.

13:11 Sidna Ɛisa yeẓra yakan win akken ara t-ixedɛen, daymi i genna i inelmaden-is : « kunwi ur zeddigit ara irkul. »

13:12 Mi sen-issared iḍaṛṛen-nsen, yerra abeṛnus-is, yuɣal ɣer wemkan is yenna-yasen : Tfehmem wayen akka i wen xedmeɣ neɣ ala ?

13:13 Tessawalem-iyi « a Sidi » ; tesɛam lḥeqq axaṭer ț-țideț !

13:14 Ma yella nekk yellan d Ssid-nwen ssardeɣ-awen iḍaṛṛen-nwen, ilaq awen aț-țețțemsiridem iḍaṛṛen wway gar-awen.

13:15 Fkiɣ-awen-d lemtel iwakken ula d kunwi aț-țxedmem akken i wen-xedmeɣ.

13:16 S tideț nniɣ awen : aqeddac ur yugar ara Ssid-is, amceggeɛ ur yugar ara win i t-id iceggɛen.

13:17 Amarezg-nwen ma tessnem ayagi, awi-d kan a t-txedmem.

13:18 Mačči fell-awen meṛṛa i d-nniɣ ayagi : ssneɣ widak i xtaṛeɣ. Lameɛna ayen yuran ad yețwakemmel : « Win yeččan aɣṛum yid-i, yuɣal-iyi-d d aɛdaw.»

13:19 Nniɣ-awen-t-id si tura uqbel a d-yaweḍ, iwakken asm'ara d-yedṛu aț-țamnem belli nekk «d Win yellan.»

13:20 A wen-iniɣ tideț : win iqeblen win ara d-ceggɛeɣ d nekk i geqbel, win ara yi-qeblen yeqbel win i yi-d-iceggɛen.

13:21 Segmi i d-yenna annect-agi, Sidna Ɛisa yetḥeyyeṛ, dɣa yenna ɛinani : A wen-d-iniɣ s tideț, yiwen deg-wen ad iyi-ixdeɛ.

13:22 Inelmaden-is tḥeyyṛen, qqimen țemyex?aṛen wway gar-asen. Kull yiwen yeqqaṛ deg wul-is : ?ef anwa i d-yenna imeslayen-agi ?

13:23 Yiwen deg-sen, anelmad-nni i gḥemmel Sidna Ɛisa, yeqqim ɣer lmakla ɣer tama-s.
