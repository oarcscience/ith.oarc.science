Kabyle Bible: 2 Timothy

1:1 Nekk Bulus aqeddac n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ s lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi, țwaceggɛeɣ ad beccṛeɣ tudert i ɣ-iwɛed Sidi Ṛebbi di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:2 A Timuti a mmi eɛzizen : leɛfu, ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a k-d-țțunefkent s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi baba-tneɣ akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ.

1:3 ?ḥemmideɣ Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣef qeddceɣ s wul yeṣfan akken qedcen fell-as lejdud-iw, deɛɛuɣ fell-ak am yiḍ am ass ;

1:4 m'ara d-mmektiɣ imeṭṭawen-ik, țḥiriɣ melmi ara k-ẓreɣ iwakken ad iliɣ di lfeṛḥ.

1:5 ?mektayeɣ-ed neyya-nni i tesɛiḍ di liman, akken i ț-tesɛa sețți-k Luiza akk-d yemma-k Anisa ; tḥeqqeqeɣ belli ar ass-a mazal-iț deg wul-ik.

1:6 Daymi bɣiɣ a k-weṣṣiɣ a d tessakiḍ tikci-nni i k-d-yețțunefken s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi asm'akken i sserseɣ ifassen-iw fell-ak.

1:7 Axaṭer mačči d Ṛṛuḥ n ukukru i ɣ-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi meɛna d Ṛṛuḥ yeččuṛen ț-țazmert d leḥmala akk-d leɛqel.

1:8 Ihi ur țsetḥi ara aț-țcehdeḍ ɣef Ssid-nneɣ, ur țsetḥi ara daɣen yis-i nekk yellan d ameḥbus ɣef ddemma-s. Meɛna eqbel leɛtab yid-i ɣef ddemma n lexbaṛ n lxiṛ, țkel ɣef tezmert i d-yețțak Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:9 D nețța i ɣ-isellken, i ɣ-d-issawlen ɣer tudert iqedsen, ayagi mačči ɣef ddemma n lecɣal-nneɣ meɛna d nețța i gebɣan s ṛṛeḥma-ines i ɣ-d yețțunefken s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ uqbel a d-texleq ddunit.

1:10 Ṛṛeḥma-agi tban-ed tura s tisin n wemsellek-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, i gɣelben lmut, ibeggen-ed tafat n tudert ur nețfaka s lexbaṛ n lxiṛ.

1:11 ?ef ddemma n lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ i d-țțucegɛeɣ d ṛṛasul iwakken ad beccṛeɣ, ad slemdeɣ.

1:12 Daymi i țenɛețțabeɣ tura, meɛna ur ssetḥaɣ ara axaṭer ẓriɣ anwa s wayes umneɣ, yerna țekleɣ fell-as yesɛa tazmert ad yeḥrez ayen akken i ɣef iyi-iwekkel alamma d ass n tuɣalin-is.

1:13 ?rez imeslayen iqedsen i tlemdeḍ s ɣuṛ-i, eddu yis-sen s liman d leḥmala i nesɛa di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:14 ?rez mliḥ deg ul-ik ayen akken i tlemḍeḍ, s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i gzedɣen deg-nneɣ.

1:15 Teẓriḍ belli wid akk yellan yid-i di tmurt n Asya ǧǧan-iyi weḥd-i, llan gar-asen Figilus d ?irmujinus.

1:16 Ad ibarek Sidi Ṛebbi axxam n gma-tneɣ Unisifur axaṭer daymen yețṣebbiṛ-iyi, ur yessetḥa ara yis-i imi di lḥebs i lliɣ ;

1:17 lameɛna akken kan i d-yusa ɣer temdint n Ṛuma inuda fell-i armi i yi-yufa.

1:18 Teẓriḍ acḥal i yi-iɛawen asmi lliɣ di temdint n Ifasus. A d-yessers Ṛebbi fell-as ṛṛeḥma-s deg ass n tuɣalin n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

2:1 Ihi kečč a mmi, sseǧhed iman-ik s ṛṛeḥma i gellan di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

2:2 Ayen tesliḍ s ɣuṛ-i zdat waṭas inigan, sseḥfeḍ-it i wid yeṭṭfen di liman di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ iwakken nutni daɣen ad izmiren a t-slemden i wiyaḍ.

2:3 Awi amur-ik n leɛtab am uɛsekṛi n Lmasiḥ.

2:4 Aɛsekṛi i gebɣan ad yeɛǧeb i lqebṭan-is ur ilaq ara ad ixemmem ɣef lecɣal n ddunit.

2:5 Ulac daɣen win ara irebḥen timzizla ma yella ur yuzzil ara akken i d-yenna lqanun.

2:6 Afellaḥ ițenɛețțaben, d nețța i gezwar lḥal ad iɣellet.

2:7 Efhem ayen akka i k-d-nniɣ axaṭer d Sidi Ṛebbi ara k-iɛawnen aț-țfehmeḍ kullec.

2:8 Mmekti-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i d-iḥyan si ger lmegtin, win akken i d-iffɣen seg uẓaṛ n Sidna Dawed akken i t-id-yenna lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ i ɣ ɣ ecciṛeɣ ;

2:9 lexbaṛ-agi i ɣef nneɛtabeɣ armi țwarzeɣ s snasel am akken d amcum i lliɣ, meɛna awal n Sidi Ṛebbi ur yețwacekkal ara.

2:10 Daymi ṣebṛeɣ i kullec ɣef ddemma n wid yețwaxtaṛen iwakken ula d nutni ad sɛun leslak i gellan di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d țmanegt n dayem.

2:11 Atah wayen yuklalen lețkal : ma nemmut yid-es, a nidir daɣen yid-es ;

2:12 ma yella neṭṭef di liman, a neḥkem yid-es ; ma yella nenkkeṛ-it ula d nețța a ɣ-yenkeṛ ;

2:13 ma yella nexdeɛ-it, nețța ur a ɣ-ixeddeɛ ara axaṭer ur yezmir ara ad yenkeṛ ayen i d-yenna.

2:14 Atah wayen ara sen-d-smektiḍ : nhu-ten zdat Ṛebbi ur țemčeqlalen ara ɣef yimeslayen ur nesɛi lmeɛna, yessufuɣen i webrid wid i sen ismeḥsisen.

2:15 Exdem lmeǧhud-ik zdat n Ṛebbi, aț-țiliḍ d aqeddac ibedden ɣer ccɣel-is, ur nețsetḥi ara deg wayen i gxeddem, i gețbecciṛen awal n Sidi Ṛebbi s lḥeqq.

2:16 Xḍu i yimeslayen ur nesɛi lmeɛna i gxulfen liman axaṭer wid i ten itebɛen simmal țzaden teffɣen i webrid n Ṛebbi.

2:17 Imeslayen-nsen am lehlak i teddun di lǧețța armi yečča-ț meṛṛa. Llan gar-asen ?imnayus d Filitus

2:18 i gwexxṛen ɣef webrid n tideț, qqaṛen belli ḥeggu n lmegtin dayen yedṛa-d, s wakka ssufuɣen aṭas n yemdanen seg webrid n liman.

2:19 Lameɛna d lsas i gessers Sidi Ṛebbi ara idumen : Ssid-nneɣ yeẓra wid yellan d ayla-s ; f+ yura daɣen win ara yinin nekk d ayla-s, ilaq ad yexḍu i wayen n diri. f+

2:20 Deg wexxam n imeṛkantiyen mačči d iqbucen n ddheb neɣ n lfeṭṭa kan i gellan meɛna llan daɣen wid n wesɣaṛ d wid n wakal ; wid n ddheb d lfeṭṭa țțaǧan-ten i leqdic n wussan n tmeɣṛa ma d wiyaḍ i leqdic n mkul ass.

2:21 Win ara yessizdgen iman-is yexḍa i lecɣal-agi n diri, ad yili am weqbuc-nni n ddheb neɣ n lfeṭṭa i sseqdacen deg wass n tmeɣṛa, yewjed ad yenfeɛ i wass m'ara t-yeḥwiǧ umɛellem-is.

2:22 Exḍu i ccehwat n temẓi i gețɣuṛṛun, kečč akk-d wid ideɛɛun ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi s wul yeṣfan, nadi ɣef lḥeqq, liman, leḥmala ț-țalwit,.

2:23 Ur ggar ara iman-ik di lketṛa n lehḍur ifeṛɣen ur nesɛi lmeɛna, xḍu-k seg-sen ; teẓriḍ belli țțawin-d anagar amennuɣ.

2:24 Ur s-ilaq ara i uqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi ad ițnaɣ. Ilaq ad yili d aḥnin ɣer medden, ad isselmed s ṣṣwab yerna ad isɛu ṣṣbeṛ ;

2:25 ilaq ad issefhem s leɛqel wid i t-yețxalafen, ahat Sidi Ṛebbi a ten-id-yerr ɣer webrid iwakken ad issinen tideț,

2:26 a d-ldint wallen-nsen a d-snesren iman-nsen si tifextin n Cciṭan i ten-yeṭṭfen ad xedmen lebɣi-s.

3:1 Ilaq aț-țeẓred belli ɣer taggara n ddunit a d-asen wussan iweɛṛen.

3:2 Imdanen ad țxemmimen ɣef yiman-nsen kan, ad ṭṭamaɛen deg idrimen, ad ssemɣuṛen iman-nsen, ad țzuxxun, ad reggmen di Ṛebbi, ad țɛaṣin imawlan-nsen, ad nekkṛen lxiṛ, ad tekksen sser ɣef wayen yesɛan lḥeṛma,

3:3 ur seɛɛun ṛṛeḥma ur seɛɛun leḥnana deg ulawen-nsen, ad skiddiben ɣef wiyaḍ, ur zemmren ara ad ḥekmen deg iman-nsen, ulawen nsen qquṛen, d iɛdawen n lxiṛ,

3:4 d ixeddaɛen, a ten-isderɣel zzux, ad ḥemmlen lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen wala lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi ;

3:5 ad tebɛen abrid n Sidi Ṛebbi s wawal kan meɛna ad nekkṛen tazmert yellan deg-s. Ihi xḍu-k i yemdanen am wigi.

3:6 Kra seg-sen keččmen ɣer kra n yexxamen, țkellixen tilawin ur nesɛi ara lḥeṛma i gețțawi zzhu n ddunit ;

3:7 țqellibent daymen ad fehment, lameɛna leɛmeṛ țțawḍent ɣer tmusni n tideț.

3:8 Am akken i xulfen Yanes d Yambris Sidna Musa, f+ akken daɣen ara xalfen tideț yemdanen-agi, aț-țeɛwej lefhama-nsen, ad ffɣen i webrid n liman.

3:9 Meɛna ur țțawḍen ɣer wacemma axaṭer imdanen meṛṛa a ten-ɛeqlen belli ffɣen i webrid am akken tedṛa d Yanes d Yambris.

3:10 Ma d kečč tettebɛeḍ akken ilaq ayen i k-slemdeɣ, tikli-inu, ixemmimen-iw, liman-iw, ṣṣbeṛ-iw, leḥmala-inu d ẓẓwara-inu,

3:11 leqheṛ i țwaqehṛeɣ akk-d leɛtab-iw. Teẓriḍ lemḥayen i gɛeddan fell-i di temdinin n Antyuc, n Ikunyum d Listra ? Anwa leqheṛ iwumi ur ṣbiṛeɣ ara ? Meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi isellek-iyi seg-sen meṛṛa.

3:12 Kra n win yebɣan ad iddu di liman n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ad ițțuqehheṛ.

3:13 Ma d imcumen ixeddaɛen, ad țkemmilen dayem di cceṛ-nsen, ad ɣuṛṛen wiyaḍ, ad rnun iman-nsen.

3:14 Ma d kečč eḥrez ayen i tlemdeḍ s wayes i tesqenɛeḍ ; teẓriḍ anwa i k-islemden.

3:15 Seg asmi telliḍ d ameẓyan i tessneḍ tira iqedsen ; yis-sent ara tesɛuḍ lefhama yețțawin ɣer leslak s liman di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:16 Yal tira iqedsen s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi i d-tekka yerna twulem i uselmed, i usefhem, i nehhu akk-d țṛebga deg webrid n lḥeqq,

3:17 iwakken yal aqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi ad yili d amdan ikemlen i gwulmen i yal ccɣel yelhan.

4:1 A k-n-ssutreɣ ɣef wudem n Ṛebbi d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ara iḥasben wid yeddren d wid yemmuten, i lmend n wass n tuɣalin-is ț-țgeldit-is.

4:2 Becceṛ awal n Sidi Ṛebbi ama di teswiɛt yelhan ama di teswiɛt iweɛṛen, ssefhem, lumm, enhu, sselmed s ṣṣbeṛ d leɛqel.

4:3 Axaṭer a d-yaweḍ lweqt anda kra n yemdanen ur zemmren ara ad slen i uselmed n tideț, lameɛna ad tebɛen lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen, a d-sbedden lecyux ara sen-d-ihedṛen ayen kan i sen-iɛeǧben ;

4:4 ad sɛuẓẓgen iman nsen ɣef tideț, ad rren ddehn-nsen ɣer tmucuha.

4:5 Ma d kečč ḥekkem deg iman-ik di kullec, ṣṣbeṛ i cedda d leɛtab, becceṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ , txedmeḍ lwaǧeb-ik n uqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi akken ilaq.

4:6 Ma d nekk yewweḍ-ed lweqt i deg ilaq ad sebbleɣ iman-iw, yewweḍ-ed lweqt i deg ara ṛuḥeɣ.

4:7 Kfiɣ ccɣel-iw yerna ṭṭfeɣ di liman ar tura, nnuɣeɣ imenɣi n tideț armi ț-țaggara.

4:8 Tura yețṛaǧu-yi lḥeqq n lxedma-w ara yi-d-yefk Bab n lḥeqq deg ass n lḥisab, yerna mačči i nekk kan, lameɛna i wid meṛṛa i t-iḥemmlen yețṛaǧun ass n tuɣalin-is.

4:9 Exdem lmeǧhud-ik iwakken ur tețɛeṭṭileḍ ara a d-taseḍ ɣuṛ-i.

4:10 Gma-tneɣ Dimas yeǧǧa-yi, yewwi-t zzhu n ddunit, iṛuḥ ɣer temdint n Tisalunik. Gma-tneɣ Krisis iṛuḥ ɣer tmurt n Galasya, ma d gma-tneɣ Tit iṛuḥ ɣer tmurt n Dalmasya.

4:11 Anagar gma-tneɣ Luqa i geqqimen yid-i. Awi-d yid-ek gma tneɣ Maṛqus iwakken ad iyi-iɛawen di lxedma-w.

4:12 Ma d gma-tneɣ Ticik atan ceggɛeɣ-t ɣer temdint n Ifasus.

4:13 M'ara d-taseḍ, awi-d yid-ek abeṛnus-nni i ǧǧiɣ deg wexxam n Karbus di temdint n Truwas, awi-d yid-ek daɣen idlisen, abeɛda wid yețwaxedmen s weglim.

4:14 Alixandru aḥeddad iḍuṛṛ-iyi, ixdem-iyi aṭas n cceṛ meɛna ayen yexdem a t-yaf ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:15 Ula d kečč ḥader iman-ik seg-s, axaṭer yețxaṣam-aɣ-d daymen deg wayen i nețbecciṛ.

4:16 Ass amezwaru i deg d-beddeɣ ɣer ccṛeɛ ur ufiɣ ula d yiwen ɣer tama-w, ǧǧan-iyi akk ; lameɛna a wer ten-iḥaseb Ṛebbi ɣef wayagi !

4:17 D Sidi Ṛebbi i yi-iɛawnen, i yi-isǧehden, iwakken lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ad ițțubecceṛ s lekmal-is i leǧnas nniḍen meṛṛa yerna isellek-iyi-d seg imi n yizmawen.

4:18 Sidi Ṛebbi mazal a yi-iḥader ɣef wayen akk n diri yerna ad iyi-isellek iwakken ad kecmeɣ tagelda-ines yellan deg igenwan. I nețța tamanegt si lǧil ɣer lǧil ! Amin !

4:19 Sellem ɣef Briska d Akilas akk-d țwacult n Unisifur.

4:20 Gma-tneɣ Irast yeqqim di temdint n Kurintus, ma d Trufim ǧǧiɣ-t di temdint n Milet mazal-it yehlek.

4:21 Exdem lmeǧhud-ik iwakken a d taseḍ uqbel ccetwa. Bulus, Buden, Linus, Kludya d watmaten akk nniḍen țsellimen-d fell-ak.

4:22 Sidi Ṛebbi ad yili yid-ek ! Ṛṛeḥma-s a d-ters fell-awen ! Amin.
