Kabyle Bible: 1 Thessalonians

1:1 Nekk Bulus, Silwan akk-d Timuti, i tejmaɛt n watmaten yellan di temdint n Tiṣalunik yumnen s Sidi Ṛebbi baba-tneɣ akk-d Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa lmasiḥ. Ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a d-ersent fell-awen.

1:2 Yal ass nețḥemmid Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen ; mkul m'ara nedɛu ɣuṛ-es, nețțader-ikkun-id di tẓallit-nneɣ.

1:3 Nețmektay-ikkun-id zdat Sidi Ṛebbi baba-tneɣ, ɣef wayen akk txeddmem seg wasmi tumnem s Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ; tqeblem aț-țenneɛtabem ɣef ddemma n yisem-is imi tḥemmlem-t yerna lețkal-nwen fell-as d ameqqran.

1:4 Ay atmaten eɛzizen ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi, neẓra belli d nețța i kkun-id ixtaṛen.

1:5 Lexbeṛ-agi n lxiṛ i wen-d newwi mačči d imeslayen kan, lameɛna s țțeḥqiq i wen-d-nbecceṛ lexbaṛ-agi n tideț, s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen. Teẓram amek i nedda gar-awen, asm'akken i nella ɣuṛ-wen ; nexdem ayagi iwakken a kkun-nɛiwen di tikli-nwen.

1:6 Ula d kunwi tuɣalem tețțeddum am nukni deg webrid n Ssid-nneɣ, imi di teswiɛt iweɛṛen i tqeblem awal-is s lfeṛḥ i d-ițțak Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

1:7 S wakka tuɣalem d lemtel i wid akk yumnen, i gellan di tmura n Masidunya akk-d Akaya.

1:8 Axaṭer s ɣuṛ-wen, lexbaṛ n lxiṛ iwweḍ mačči kan ɣer tmura-agi, meɛna slan s liman-nwen di Sidi Ṛebbi di mkul amkan. Daymi fiḥel a d-nemmeslay fell-awen.

1:9 Imdanen meṛṛa ḥekkun amek i tesṭerḥbem yis-nneɣ, amek i d-tuɣalem ɣer webrid n Sidi Ṛebbi, teǧǧam aɛbad n ssadaț, iwakken aț-țḍuɛem Sidi Ṛebbi n tideț, Sidi Ṛebbi yeddren.

1:10 Yerna tețṛaǧum a d-yuɣal Ssid nneɣ Ɛisa seg igenwan, Mmi-s-nni i d-isseḥya si ger lmegtin ; d nețța ara ɣ-isellken ass n lḥisab.

2:1 Ay atmaten, kunwi s yiman-nwen teẓram mačči mbla lmeɛna i d-nusa ɣuṛ-wen.

2:2 Teẓram daɣen nenneɛtab, nețwargem di temdint n Filibus, meɛna neṭṭef di liman-nneɣ di Sidi Ṛebbi, nkemmel abecceṛ n wawal-is gar-awen ɣas akken nețwaqehheṛ.

2:3 Lexbeṛ-agi n lxiṛ i wen-d newwi ur ibni ara ɣef lekdeb neɣ ɣef tḥila d lexdeɛ.

2:4 Sidi Ṛebbi ițkel fell-aɣ, daymi i ɣ-iceggeɛ iwakken a nbecceṛ awal-is. Axaṭer iẓra nețmeslay mačči iwakken a neɛǧeb i yemdanen, meɛna iwakken a s-neɛǧeb i nețța yessnen ulawen-nneɣ.

2:5 Teẓram belli leɛmeṛ ur nemmeslay s usqizzeb neɣ neḍmeɛ kra deg yiwen, atan Sidi Ṛebbi d inigi !

2:6 Leɛmeṛ ur nqelleb ad yali ccan-nneɣ ama ɣuṛ-wen ama ɣer wiyaḍ ; yerna lemmer nebɣi, nezmer a nḥettem iman-nneɣ fell-awen imi d imceggɛen n Lmasiḥ i nella !

2:7 Meɛna asmi nella gar-awen, neččuṛ d leḥnana am tyemmaț ițḥadaren ɣef warraw-is.

2:8 Imi tellam deg wulawen-nneɣ, mačči kan d lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi i nebɣa a wen-nbecceṛ, meɛna nezmer a nsebbel ula ț-țiṛwiḥin-nneɣ fell-awen, axaṭer teɛzizem fell-aɣ aṭas.

2:9 Ma tecfam ay atmaten ɣef wussan nni mi i wen-d-newwi lexbaṛ n lxiṛ, nenneɛtab, nxeddem iḍ d wass akken ur nețțili ț-țaɛkumt ula i yiwen deg-wen.

2:10 Ṛebbi yeẓra, kunwi teẓram belli tikli-nneɣ teṣfa, nedda s lḥeqq, yiwen ur aɣ-d-issukkes lɛib gar-awen kunwi s wid yumnen.

2:11 Teẓram daɣen nella i mkul yiwen deg-wen am ubabat ɣer warraw-is.

2:12 Nenha-kkun, nesseǧhed-ikkun, nḥellel-ikkun iwakken aț-țeddum akken yebɣa Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-d issawlen ɣer tgeldit-is d lɛaḍima-s.

2:13 S wakka nețḥemmid dayem Sidi Ṛebbi imi tqeblem awal-is i wen-d newwi. Tqeblem-t mačči am akken d awal n wemdan, meɛna tqeblem-t imi d awal n Sidi Ṛebbi. D awal-agi i gxeddmen deg-wen tura, kunwi s wid yumnen.

2:14 Ay atmaten, tedṛa yid-wen am tejmuyaɛ n imasiḥiyen yellan di tmurt n Yahuda : akken i kkun-qehṛen warraw n tmurt-nwen akken daɣen i ten-qehṛen wudayen.

2:15 D udayen-agi i genɣan Ssid nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d lenbiya, d nutni daɣen i ɣ-iqehṛen. Ɛuṣan Sidi Ṛebbi yerna d iɛdawen n yemdanen.

2:16 Rnan kemmlen di ddnubat-nsen, axaṭer ur ɣ-ǧǧin ara a nbecceṛ leslak n Sidi Ṛebbi i leǧnas nniḍen ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail. S wakka i d-yeɣli lɛiqab n Sidi Ṛebbi fell-asen.

2:17 Ma d nukni ay atmaten seg wasmi i d-nṛuḥ s ɣuṛ-wen, ɣas akken ur kkun-ẓerrent ara wallen, tellam deg wulawen-nneɣ ; nxaq fell-awen aṭas, daymi i neɛṛeḍ a d-nas iwakken a kkun-nẓer.

2:18 Acḥal n tikkal i nebɣa a nuɣal ɣuṛ-wen, nekk s yiman-iw, yiwet neɣ snat n tikkal i ɛerḍeɣ a n-ruḥeɣ, meɛna Cciṭan ur aɣ yeǧǧi ara, iqḍeɛ-aɣ abrid.

2:19 D kunwi i d sebba n usirem d lfeṛḥ-nneɣ ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi, yis-wen ara nzuxx zdat Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa asm'ara d-yuɣal.

2:20 Ț-țideț, d kunwi i d can nneɣ i d lfeṛḥ-nneɣ.

3:1 Imi ur nezmir ara a neṣbeṛ fell-awen nnig wannect-a, nufa-d yiwen ṛṛay : nukni a neqqim di temdint n Atinya,

3:2 ma d gma-tneɣ Timuti, aqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi, ițbecciṛen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ, a wen-t-in-nceggeɛ a kkun isseǧhed yerna a kkun-inhu di liman nwen,

3:3 iwakken yiwen ur ifeččel di teswiɛt-agi n uqehheṛ. Teẓram belli ayagi d ayen i ɣ-ițṛaǧun.

3:4 Asmi nella yid-wen nenna-yawen yakan belli a d-yas wass i deg ara nețwaqehheṛ. Atan tura twalam belli d ayen ideṛṛun.

3:5 Imi xaqeɣ fell-awen, ur zmireɣ ara ad ṣebṛeɣ, ceggɛeɣ-awen gma tneɣ Timuti iwakken a yi-d-yawi lexbaṛ ma mazal teṭṭfem di liman nwen ; axaṭer uggadeɣ a kkun iɣuṛṛ Cciṭan, aț-țețțum ayen akk i wen-nesselmed, uggadeɣ lxedma nneɣ aț-țuɣal d ulac.

3:6 Mi d-yuɣal Timuti s ɣuṛ-wen, yewwi-yaɣ-d lexbaṛ yelhan fell-awen : iḥka-yaɣ-d amek tḍuɛem Sidi Ṛebbi, d leḥmala yellan wway gar-awen ; yenna-yaɣ-d daɣen amek i ɣ-d țmektayem, ur aɣ-tețțum ara, tḥarem melmi ara ɣ-teẓrem am akken i nxaq fell-awen ula d nukni.

3:7 Ay atmaten, ɣas akka nella di lmeḥna d iɣeblan, theddnen wulawen nneɣ ɣef wayen yeɛnan liman-nwen.

3:8 Tura yuɣal-aɣ-d ṛṛuḥ imi neẓra teṭṭfem di Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:9 Lfeṛḥ-nneɣ d ameqqran zdat Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef ddemma-nwen. Akken nebɣu neḥmed-it fell-awen drus.

3:10 Nețḥellil Sidi Ṛebbi am iḍ am ass, a ɣ-d-yefk tagniț i deg ara kkun-nẓer, iwakken a wen-nkemmel aselmed n wayen i kkun-ixuṣṣen di liman-nwen.

3:11 Nessutur Sidi Ṛebbi baba-tneɣ d Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa a ɣ-d-ldin iberdan iwakken a n-nas ɣuṛ-wen.

3:12 Ad isnerni Ṛebbi leḥmala i tesɛam wway gar-awen, aț-țḥemmlem daɣen imdanen meṛṛa am akken i kkun nḥemmel nukni.

3:13 Ad isseǧhed Ṛebbi ulawen nwen, iwakken aț-țeṣfum, aț-țilim mbla lɛib zdat Sidi Ṛebbi baba-tneɣ i wass i deg ara d-yuɣal Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ nețța d lmalayekkat.

4:1 Ay atmaten, tlemdem s ɣuṛ-nneɣ amek ara teddum iwakken aț-țɛeǧbem i Sidi Ṛebbi ; a kkun nenhu s yisem n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ kemmlet ɣer zdat iwakken aț țimɣuṛem.

4:2 Ay atmaten teẓram belli lewṣayat i wen-nesselmed, kkant-ed s ɣuṛ Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

4:3 D ṣṣfa n wulawen-nwen i gebɣa Sidi Ṛebbi ; yebɣa aț-țḥadrem iman nwen si lefsad,

4:4 yal yiwen deg-wen ur ițțak ara lebɣi i tnefsit-is deg wayen icemten ; ad iddu s ṣṣfa d lḥeṛma,

4:5 ur ițmenni ara ayen icemten am akken xeddmen imejhal ur nessin ara Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:6 Deg wannect-agi ilaq yiwen ur ițkellix gma-s di tlufa yeɛnan idrimen. Acḥal n tikkal i kkun-nweṣṣa iwakken aț-țḥadrem iman-nwen axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi ad yerr țțar akken i wen-t-id-nenna !

4:7 Sidi Ṛebbi ur aɣ-d-inni ara a neddu di lefsad meɛna iweṣṣa-yaɣ-d a neddu s ṣṣfa.

4:8 S wakka win ur neqbil ara lewṣayat-agi, mačči d amdan i gɛuṣa, meɛna iɛuṣa Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-d-ițțaken Ṛṛuḥ-is iqedsen.

4:9 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan tagmaț, fiḥel ma nemmeslay-awen-d fell-as, imi temyeḥmalem wway gar-awen akken i tlemdem s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:10 Akken daɣen i tḥemmlem atmaten yellan di tmurt n Masidunya meṛṛa. Di leɛnaya nwen ay atmaten, kemmlet deg webrid-agi.

4:11 Nadit aț-țɛicem di talwit, ur țɛassat ara d acu i xeddmen wiyaḍ. Xedmet s ifassen-nwen, ur ṭṭamaɛet ara aɣṛum-nwen deg wiyaḍ, akken i kkun-nweṣṣa yakan

4:12 iwakken a kkun-qadṛen medden ɣef ddemma n tikli nwen yeṣfan, yerna ur tețțilim ț-țaɛkumt ula i yiwen.

4:13 Ay atmaten, ur nebɣi ara a kkun-neǧǧ di ccekk ɣef wayen yeɛnan wid yemmuten, iwakken ur tețțilim ara di leḥzen am wid ur nesɛi ara asirem n leslak n Sidna Ɛisa.

4:14 Axaṭer akken numen belli Sidna Ɛisa yemmut yuɣal yeḥya-d, numen daɣen belli Sidi Ṛebbi a d-isseḥyu wid yemmuten, imi umnen s Sidna Ɛisa.

4:15 Atah wayen ara wen-d-nini akken i t-id-isselmed Ssid-nneɣ : ma yella mazal-aɣ nedder asm'ara d-yuɣal, ur nețțili ara d imezwura ɣef wid yemmuten.

4:16 Axaṭer m'ara d-iffeɣ lameṛ, m'ara d-tenṭeq taɣect n lmelk akk-d ṣṣut n lbuq n Sidi Ṛebbi, Sidna Ɛisa s yiman-is a d-iṣubb seg igenni, imiren widak-nni yemmuten, llan umnen yis, a d-ḥyun d imezwura si ger lmegtin.

4:17 Ma d nukni ma yella mazal-aɣ nedder, a nețwarfed yid-sen ɣef wusigna, a nemmager Sidna Ɛisa deg igenwan, iwakken a nili yid-es i dayem.

4:18 Ihi mseǧhadet, mṣebbaṛet wway gar-awen s yimeslayen-agi.

5:1 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan ussan akk d lweqt, fiḥel ma nura-yawen fell-asen.

5:2 Teẓram s yiman-nwen belli ass-nni, Sidna Ɛisa a d-yuɣal am umakar deg iḍ.

5:3 M'ara yinin yemdanen : « aql-aɣ di lehna ț-țalwit, » ass-nni ur țfiqen ara alamma yeɣli-d fell-asen nnger, am lewjaɛ m'ara ṭṭfen tameṭṭut yellan s tadist, yernu yiwen ur imenneɛ.

5:4 Ma d kunwi ay atmaten, ur tellim ara di ṭṭlam iwakken a d-iɣli wass-nni fell-awen am umakar ;

5:5 axaṭer kunwi d arraw n tafat, tețțeddum deg wass ; nukni ur nelli ara d arraw n ṭṭlam iteddun deg yiḍ.

5:6 Ihi ur ilaq ara a neɣfel am wiyaḍ, meɛna ilaq a nɛass iman-nneɣ yerna a nɛiwez.

5:7 Wid yegganen gganen deg yiḍ, wid iṣekkren țṣekkiren deg yiḍ.

5:8 Ma d nukni yellan d arraw n tafat, a nɛass ɣef yiman-nneɣ : a nesɛu liman d leḥmala am llebsa n wuzzal ițlusu uɛeskṛi, a nesɛu asirem n leslak, am tcacit n wuzzal ɣef wuqeṛṛuy.

5:9 Axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi ur aɣ-ixtaṛ ara iwakken a nețwaɛaqeb, meɛna yebɣa a nesɛu leslak s Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ

5:10 i gemmuten fell-aɣ, iwakken ama nedder ama nemmut a nili yid-es.

5:11 S wakka ihi, țemseǧhadet, țemṣebbaṛet wway gar-awen akken i txeddmem yakan.

5:12 Di leɛnaya-nwen ay atmaten, qadṛet wid iqeddcen gar-awen, axaṭer s yisem n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i kkun nehhun, i kkun-sselmaden.

5:13 Ɛuzzet țen tḥemmlem-ten ɣef ddemma n lxedma-nsen, ɛicet di lehna wway gar-awen.

5:14 Di leɛnaya-nwen ay atmaten : nhut wid iteddun s yir tikli, ṣebbṛet wid iwumi ɣlin ifadden, refdet wid ifeclen, swesɛet ulawen-nwen ɣuṛ-sen meṛṛa.

5:15 Ḥadret iman-nwen ur țțarat ara țțaṛ, qellbet ɣef wayen yelhan ama gar-awen ama ɣer imdanen meṛṛa.

5:16 Ilit dayem di lfeṛḥ,

5:17 yal ass deɛɛut ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi,

5:18 ḥemmdet-eț ɣef kullec. D ayagi i gețṛaǧu Sidi Ṛebbi deg-wen, di tikli-nwen akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

5:19 Ur snusut ara tafat n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ;

5:20 ur ḥeqqṛet ara ayen i d-ițweḥḥi Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i yemdanen.

5:21 Meɛna meyzet, qeblet ayen yelhan,

5:22 ḥadret iman-nwen ɣef wayen n diri.

5:23 Sidi Ṛebbi bab n talwit, ad iṣeffi ṛṛuḥ-nwen, lǧețțat nwen ț-țeṛwiḥin-nwen, iwakken aț țeqqimem d izedganen i wass i deg ara d-yuɣal Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

5:24 Win i kkun-ixtaṛen, deg-s laman, d nețța ara kkun-iṣṣiwden ɣer wannect agi.

5:25 Ay atmaten, deɛɛut ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-aneɣ.

5:26 Sṣiwḍet sslam-iw i watmaten meṛṛa.

5:27 Di leɛnaya-nwen, s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ, sseɣṛet tabṛaț-agi i watmaten meṛṛa.

5:28 Ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ a d-ters fell-awen.
