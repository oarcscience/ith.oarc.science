4:18 Wiyaḍ țemcabin ɣer wakal yeččuṛen d isennanen, sellen i wawal n Ṛebbi,

4:19 meɛna iɣeblan, ṭṭmeɛ, lerbaḥ akk-d zzhu n ddunit-agi, skuffuren ( țkemmimen ) awal-nni, ur d-yețțawi ara lfayda.

4:20 Wiyaḍ cban akal yelhan anda teɣli zzerriɛa, sellen i wawal n Ṛebbi, qebblen-t, țțaken-d lfayda. Mkul aɛeqqa yeggar-ed wa tlatin, wa sețțin, wa meyya.

4:21 Yenna-yasen daɣen : Eɛni yella win ara iceɛlen taftilt iwakken a ț-yerr seddaw lḥila neɣ seddaw n wusu ? Mačči iwakken a ț-issers s ufella n lmeṣbeḥ ?

4:22 Ulac ayen yeffren ur d-nețban, ulac ayen iɣummen ur d-tețbeggin tafat.

4:23 Smeḥset-ed mliḥ, ma tesɛam imeẓẓuɣen isellen !

4:24 Yerna yenna-yasen : ?adret ɣef wayen tsellem ! Axaṭer akken i tețțektilim i wiyaḍ ara wen-d-ițwaktil i kunwi, yerna a wen-d ițwaktil s zzyada.

4:25 Axaṭer win yesɛan a s-rnun, ma d win ur nesɛi ara a s-yețwakkes ula d ayen yesɛa.

4:26 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen daɣen : Tageldit n Sidi Ṛebbi tețțemcabi ɣer yiwen ufellaḥ i gzerɛen iger.

4:27 Ama yeṭṭes ama ur yeṭṭis, am yiḍ am ass, zzerriɛa tețțemɣay tgemmu, ur yeẓri amek.

4:28 Akal yețțak-ed s yiman-is lɣella. Tazwara d imɣi ad yuɣal ț-țigdert, taggara tigdert-nni aț-țeččaṛ d iɛeqqayen.

4:29 Mi wwan yirden, afellaḥ-nni a d-yas a ten-yemger, axaṭer yewweḍ ed lweqt n tmegra.

4:30 Yenna daɣen : ?er wacu i nezmer a nmettel tageldit n Ṛebbi, neɣ anwa lemtel ara d-nawi fell-as ?

4:31 Ițemcabi ɣer uɛeqqa n uxerḍel. M'ara t-nezreɛ, d nețța i d aɛeqqa amecṭuḥ meṛṛa ger zzerriɛat n ddunit.

4:32 Mi gețwazreɛ, igemmu yețțali alamma yekka-d sennig akk yemɣan n tebḥirt, yeggar ed ifurkawen imeqqranen, ula d igṭaṭ ( ifṛax ) n igenni, țruḥun-d ad ddarin ( ddurqen ) seddaw tili-ines.

4:33 S waṭas n lemtul am wigi i sen ițbecciṛ awal n Ṛebbi, akken i tella lefhama n mkul yiwen.

4:34 Ițmeslay-asen anagar s lemtul, meɛna m'ara yili akk-d inelmaden-is, yessefham-asen kullec.

4:35 Ass-nni kan mi geɣli yiṭij, Sidna Ɛisa yenna i inelmaden-is : Eyyaw a nzegret agummaḍ n lebḥeṛ.

4:36 Mi gerra lɣaci-nni, iṛuḥ yid-sen di teflukt i deg yella. Ddant yid-es kra teflukin nniḍen.

4:37 Tekker-ed yiwet n tbuciḍant, tessalay-ed lemwaji ɣer teflukt tețțacaṛ-iț.

4:38 Sidna Ɛisa yella yeṭṭes di teflukt deffir n inelmaden-is, yessers aqeṛṛuy-is ɣef tsumta. Sakin-t-id nnan-as : A Sidi, eɛni ur ak-tewqiɛ ara ma nemmut ?

4:39 Sidna Ɛisa yekker-ed iɛeggeḍ ɣef waḍu, yenna i lebḥeṛ : Ssers iman-ik ! Ssusem ! Yers waḍu-nni, dɣa tuɣal-ed talwit.

4:40 Yenna i inelmaden-is : Acuɣer i tuggadem akka ? Mazal ur tuminem ara ?

4:41 Meɛna ikcem-iten akk lxuf d ameqqran, qqaṛen wway gar-asen : Anwa-t wagi iwumi țțaɣen awal ula d aḍu d lebḥeṛ ?

5:1 Usan-d seg ugemmaḍ n lebḥeṛ ɣer tmurt n ijiṛaziyen.

5:2 Akken kan i d-yers Sidna Ɛisa si teflukt, ataya yiwen wergaz yezdeɣ uṛuḥani, yeffeɣ-ed si tjebbant iteddu-d ɣuṛ-es.

5:3 Yezdeɣ ger iẓekwan, yiwen ur izmir a t-yarez ula s ssnesla

5:4 axaṭer acḥal d abrid i t-qqnen s ixelxalen n wuzzal deg iḍaṛṛen, s ssnasel deg ifassen, meɛna yesseɣṛas-iten akk, daymi ur yezmir yiwen a t-iṭṭef.

5:5 Iḍ d wass nețța deg uneẓruf, yețɛeggiḍ, ijerreḥ iman-is s iblaḍen.

5:6 Mi gwala Sidna Ɛisa si lebɛid, yuzzel-ed yeɣli ɣef tgecrar zdat-es.

5:7 Iɛeggeḍ s lǧehd : D acu i tebɣiḍ ɣuṛ-i a Ɛisa, a mmi-s n Ṛebbi ɛlayen ? Di leɛnaya-k, ɣef wudem n Ṛebbi ur iyi-țɛețțib ara !

5:8 Axaṭer Sidna Ɛisa yeqqaṛ-as : Effeɣ seg umdan-agi ay aṛuḥani !

5:9 Yesteqsa-t yenna-yas : Isem-ik ? Yerra-yas-d : Isem-iw tarbaɛt, axaṭer deg waṭas yid-nneɣ i nella.

5:10 Dɣa țḥellilen deg-s rennun iwakken ur ten-yessufuɣ ara si tmurt-nni.

5:11 Yuɣ lḥal tella dinna yiwet n tqeḍɛit n yilfan kessen ;

5:12 ḥellelent nnan-as : Di leɛnaya-k, ǧǧaɣ a nekcem deg yilfan-agi .

5:13 Iserreḥ-asen, dɣa ffɣen seg wergaz-nni, kecmen deg ilfan-nni, imiren grarben seg wedrar ɣer lebḥeṛ, llan deg wazal n walfin yid-sen, ɣeṛqen meṛṛa.

5:14 Wid ikessen ilfan-nni rewlen, ssawḍen lexbaṛ ɣer temdint akk-d tudrin dɣa lɣaci ṛuḥen-d ad walin ayen yedṛan.

5:15 Mi d-wwḍen ɣer Sidna Ɛisa, walan win akken tezdeɣ terbaɛt n iṛuḥaniyen yeqqim, yelsa llebsa, yuɣal-ed ɣer leɛqel-is ; imiren ikcem-iten lxuf.

5:16 Wid i gḥeḍren ḥekkun-d i wiyaḍ ayen yedṛan d win yețwazedɣen akk-d țqeḍɛit-nni n yilfan.

5:17 Imiren bdan țḥellilen Sidna Ɛisa ad iffeɣ si tmurt-nsen.

5:18 Akken iteddu Sidna Ɛisa ad yali ɣef teflukt, win akken yellan yețwazdeɣ yețḥellil deg-s yeqqaṛ-as : Di leɛnaya-k eǧǧ-iyi ad dduɣ yid-ek.

5:19 Lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa yugi, yenna-yas : Uɣal ɣer wexxam-ik d leḥbab-ik, teḥkuḍ-asen ayen i k-ixdem Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:20 Iṛuḥ, yebda yețberriḥ di ɛecṛa n temdinin-nni ayen akk i s-yexdem Sidna Ɛisa. Kra win i s-yeslan yetɛeǧǧeb.

5:21 Sidna Ɛisa yuɣal s teflukt ɣer ugemmaḍ n lebḥeṛ. Innejmaɛ-ed daɣen lɣaci ɣuṛ-es mi gella rrif n lebḥeṛ.

5:22 Yiwen seg lecyux n lǧameɛ, isem-is Jayṛus, akken i gwala Sidna Ɛisa, yeɣli ɣer iḍaṛṛen-is,

5:23 ițḥellil deg-s irennu, yeqqaṛ-as : A Sidi, di leɛnaya-k ! Yelli tețmețțat, ddu-d yid-i ssers ifassen-ik fell-as iwakken aț-țeslek, ur tețmețțat ara !

5:24 Sidna Ɛisa iṛuḥ yid-es. Aṭas n lɣaci i t-itebɛen, ḥerṣen-t si mkul tama.

5:25 Tella dinna yiwet n tmeṭṭut tehlek, tnac iseggasen nețțat tețțazal d idammen.

5:26 Tenɛețțab aṭas ger ifassen n ṭṭeba, texṣeṛ ayen akk i tesɛa, meɛna ur teḥli ara, terna tenṭer akteṛ.

5:27 Mi tesla heddṛen ɣef Sidna Ɛisa , tṛuḥ-ed deffir-es ger lɣaci, tennul ajellab-is.

5:28 Axaṭer teqqaṛ deg iman-is : ma nnuleɣ ulamma d icuḍaḍ n ubeṛnus-is, ad ḥluɣ.

5:29 Imiren kan ḥebsen idammen-nni, tḥulfa ( tḥuss ) i yiman-is teḥla seg waṭan-is.

5:30 Di teswiɛt-nni Sidna Ɛisa iḥuss i yiwet n tezmert teffeɣ seg-s. Yezzi ɣer lɣaci-nni yenna : Anwa i d-innulen ajellab-iw ?

5:31 Inelmaden-is rran-as : Aql-ik ger lɣaci ḥeṛsen-k-id si mkul tama, teqqaṛeḍ anwa i yi-d innulen ?

5:32 Nețța yețmuqul di lɣaci i s-d yezzin iwakken ad iwali tameṭṭut-nni.

5:33 Tameṭṭut-nni, mi twala ayen yedṛan yid-es texleɛ, tețțergigi ; tṛuḥ-ed teɣli ɣer iḍaṛṛen n Sidna Ɛisa tenna-yas akk tideț.

5:34 Yenna-yas : A yell-i, d liman-im i kkem-yesseḥlan ; ṛuḥ di lehna, aql-ikkem teṣfiḍ si lehlak-im.

5:35 Mazal-it yețmeslay, a ten-aya usan-d kra yergazen seg wexxam n Jayṛus ccix-nni n lǧameɛ, nnan-as : Yelli-k temmut, ur țɛețțib ara Sidna Ɛisa.

5:36 Lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa mi gesla i imeslayen-nni, yenna i ccix-nni : Ur țțaggad ara, amen kan !

5:37 Ur yeǧǧi yiwen ad yeddu yid-es anagar Buṭrus, Yeɛqub akk-d gma-s Yuḥenna.

5:38 Mi wwḍen ɣer wexxam n ccix-nni n lǧameɛ, Sidna Ɛisa yufa lɣaci yenhewwal, țrun, țmeǧǧiden.

5:39 Yekcem ɣer wexxam yenna-yasen : Iwacu lehwel d imeṭṭawen-agi ? Taqcict ur temmut ara, teṭṭes kan.

5:40 Stehzayen fell-as. Dɣa yessufeɣ iten akk, yewwi imawlan n teqcict-nni akk-d tlata-nni inelmaden, yekcem ɣer texxamt anda tella teqcict-nni ;

5:41 yeṭṭef afus n teqcict-nni yenna-yas : Talita, qumi ! (Yeɛni : « A taqcict, ekker ! »)

5:42 Imiren kan, tekker-ed teqcict-nni tebda tleḥḥu. Fell-as tnac iseggasen. Wid yellan dinna xelɛen tɛeǧǧben deg wayen walan.

5:43 Sidna Ɛisa iweṣṣa-ten ur țɛawaden i yiwen ayen yedṛan ; dɣa yenna-yasen : Fket-as aț-țečč.

6:1 Sidna Ɛisa iṛuḥ syenna ɣer tmurt-is, ddan yid-es inelmaden is.

6:2 Akken i d-yewweḍ wass n westeɛfu, yebda yesselmad di lǧameɛ. Aṭas i gwehmen seg wid i s-isellen, qqaṛen : Ansi i s-d-yekka wayagi ? D acu-ț lefhama-yagi i s-d ițțunefken ? Ansi i s-d-tekka tezmert s wayes ixeddem lbeṛhanat-agi meṛṛa ?

6:3 Mačči d wagi i d aneǧǧaṛ-nni, mmi-s n Meryem, gma-s n Yeɛqub, n Yuses, n Yuda akk-d Semɛun ? Anaɣ yessetma-s gar-aneɣ i țɛicint ? Ayagi yella-yasen d ugur daymi ur uminen ara yis.

6:4 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Anagar di tmurt-is ger imawlan is d wat wexxam-is i gețwaḥqeṛ nnbi.

6:5 Ur yexdim dinna ula d yiwen n lbeṛhan, anagar kra imuḍan i ɣef yessers ifassen-is yesseḥla-ten.

6:6 Yewhem deg-sen imi ugin ad amnen. Dɣa ileḥḥu, yesselmad di tudrin iqeṛben tamdint n Naṣaret.

6:7 Sidna Ɛisa yessawel i tnac-nni inelmaden, iceggeɛ-iten sin sin, yefka yasen tazmert ad ssufɣen iṛuḥaniyen.

6:8 Iweṣṣa-ten, yenna-yasen : Ur țțawit yid-wen acemma i webrid, ama d aɣṛum, ama d agrab ama d aṣurdi deg waggus, anagar aɛekkaz deg ufus.

6:9 Elset irkasen, meɛna ur țțawit ara yid-wen aqenduṛ wis sin.

6:10 Yenna-yasen daɣen : Axxam anda stṛeḥben yis-wen qqimet ɣuṛ-sen alamma tṛuḥem.

6:11 Ma yella wanda ur sṭerḥben ara yis-wen, ur bɣin ara a wen-slen, ṛuḥet syenna, zwit aɣebbaṛ n warkasen-nwen, ddnub i yirawen-nsen.

6:12 Ṛuḥen țbecciṛen i yemdanen iwakken a d-uɣalen ɣer webrid.

6:13 Ssufuɣen aṭas n leǧnun, dehhinen s zzit aṭas n imuḍan, sseḥlayen-ten.

6:14 Yuɣal yisem n Sidna Ɛisa mechuṛ di tmurt meṛṛa, yewweḍ lexbaṛ-is ɣer ugellid Hiṛudus. Kra qqaṛen : D Yeḥya aɣeṭṭas i d-iḥyan si ger lmegtin, daymi i gezmer ad ixdem lbeṛhanat.

6:15 Wiyaḍ qqaṛen : D nnbi Ilyas. Wiyaḍ daɣen qqaṛen-as : D nnbi am lenbiya nniḍen.

6:16 Meɛna mi gesla Hiṛudus s wannect-agi, yeqqaṛ : D Yehya-nni iwumi kkseɣ aqqeṛṛuy, i d-iḥyan !

6:17 Axaṭer d Hiṛudus s yiman-is i gerran Yeḥya ɣer lḥebs yeqqen-it s ssnasel, mi t-ilum imi guɣ Hiṛudyad yellan ț-țameṭṭut n gma-s Filbas.

6:18 Yeḥya aɣeṭṭas yella yeqqaṛ-as : D leḥṛam fell-ak aț-țaɣeḍ tameṭṭut n gma-k !

6:19 Daymi i t-tekṛeh Hiṛudyad, yerna tețqellib amek ara t-tneɣ lameɛna ur s-tufi ara abrid ;

6:20 axaṭer Hiṛudus yețqadaṛ Yeḥya imi i t-iẓra d argaz yeṣfan, d aḥeqqi, daymi i gețḥunnu fell-as. Iḥemmel ad ismeḥses i wawal-is, meɛna m'ara s-isel iɛewweq.

6:21 Ațaya teɣli-yas-d sebba i Hiṛudyad... Ass n lɛid n tlalit-is, Hiṛudus iɛreḍ-ed ɣer imensi imeqqranen n tgelda-s, imeqqranen n lɛeskeṛ akk-d imdebbṛen n tmurt n Jlili.

6:22 Tekcem-ed yelli-s n Hiṛudyad tecḍeḥ, dɣa teɛǧeb-as i Hiṛudus d inebgawen-is. Agellid yenna i teqcict-nni : Ssuter-iyi-d ayen tebɣiḍ a m-t-id fkeɣ.

6:23 Yerna yeggul-as : Ssuter-ed ayen tebɣiḍ a m-t-id fkeɣ, ɣas d azgen di tgelda-w.

6:24 Teffeɣ tenna i yemma-s : D acu ara s-ssutreɣ ? Terra-yas : Ssuter-as aqeṛṛuy n Yeḥya.

6:25 Tekcem ɣer ugellid tenna-yas : Efk-iyi-d tura kan aqeṛṛuy n Yeḥya deg uḍebsi !

6:26 Agellid-nni yeḥzen mačči d kra, lameɛna imi s-yeggul zdat inebgawen is, ur yezmir ara ad iḥnet.

6:27 Iceggeɛ imiren kan yiwen uɛessas, yumeṛ-it a s-d-yawi aqeṛṛuy n Yeḥya aɣeṭṭas. Aɛessas-nni iṛuḥ ɣer lḥebs, ikkes-as aqeṛṛuy i Yeḥya.

6:28 Yewwi-t-id deg uḍebsi, yefka-t i teqcict-nni, ma d nețțat tewwi-t i yemma-s.

6:29 Mi slan s wayagi, inelmaden n Yeḥya usan-d wwin lǧețța-s meḍlen-ț.

6:30 Uɣalen-d inelmaden, nnejmaɛen ɣer Sidna Ɛisa, ḥkan-as ayen akk xedmen d wayen slemden.

6:31 Imi aṭas n lɣaci i gețṛuḥun țțuɣalen-d, ur sɛin ara lweqt ad ččen, dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Eyyaw a nṛuḥet ɣer wemkan iḍeṛfen iwakken aț-țesteɛfum.

6:32 Rekben di teflukt, ṛuḥen ad stuṛṛfen iman-nsen.

6:33 Aṭas i ten-iwalan mi ṛuḥen, ɛeqlen-ten, imiren uzzlen-d ɣef wuḍar si mkul tamdint. Zwaren-ten ɣer wanda akken i teddun.

6:34 Mi d-yers si teflukt, iwala ayendin n lɣaci ; dɣa ɣaḍen-t imi cban taqeḍɛit n wulli ur nesɛi ameksa. Yebda yesselmad-iten deg waṭas n lecɣal.

6:35 Mi qṛib ad yeɣli wass, usan-d inelmaden-is nnan-as : Amkan-agi yexla yerna iṛuḥ lḥal,

6:36 serreḥ i lɣaci ad ṛuḥen ɣer leɛzayeb ț-țudrin i d-iqeṛben iwakken a d-aɣen ayen ara ččen.

6:37 Yerra-yasen : Fket-asen kunwi ayen ara ččen ! Nutni rran-as : Eɛni tebɣiḍ a nṛuḥ a d-naɣ aɣṛum s wazal n mitin alef iwakken a nessečč annect-agi n lɣaci ?

6:38 Dɣa yenna-yasen : Acḥal n teḥbulin n weɣṛum i tesɛam ? Ṛuḥet aț-țwalim . Mi d-ẓran, nnan-as : ?uṛ-nneɣ xemsa n teḥbulin n weɣṛum d sin iḥewtiwen (iselman ).

6:39 Imiren, Sidna Ɛisa yenna i inelmaden-is ad sɣimen lɣaci ț-țirebbaɛ ɣef leḥcic.

6:40 Qqimen ț-țirebbaɛ n meyya akk d xemsin.

6:41 Yeddem xemsa n teḥbulin-nni n weɣṛum d sin iḥewtiwen, yerfed allen-is ɣer igenni yeḥmed Ṛebbi. Yebḍa aɣṛum-nni, yefka-t i inelmaden a t-feṛqen i lɣaci. Ifṛeq-asen ula d sin-nni iḥewtiwen.

6:42 ?čan akk armi ṛwan,

6:43 jemɛen-d tnac iḍellaɛen n weɣṛum akk-d lḥut i d-yegran.

6:44 Wid yeččan si lqut-nni llan di xemsa alef yid-sen.

6:45 Imiren kan, iḥettem ɣef yinelmaden-is ad zegren s teflukt agummaḍ i lebḥeṛ, ad zwiren ɣer temdint n Bitsayda, ma d nețța ad iqqim ad yerr lɣaci.

6:46 Mi gemfaṛaq yid-sen, yuli ɣer wedrar iwakken ad iẓẓall.

6:47 Mi d-tewweḍ tmeddit, taflukt nni tella di tlemmast n lebḥeṛ, ma d Sidna Ɛisa yeqqim weḥd-es di lberr.

6:48 Iwala ṛwan lḥif mi kkaten imeqdafen, axaṭer aḍu yețțara-ten ɣer deffir. Mi qṛib ad yali wass iṛuḥ ɣuṛ-sen, iteddu ɣef waman, yebɣa a sen-iɛeddi.

6:49 Mi t-walan ileḥḥu ɣef waman, nwan-t d lexyal, bdan țɛeggiḍen,

6:50 xelɛen axaṭer țwalin-t akk. Imiren iluɛa-ten yenna-yasen : Ur xellɛet ara, d nekk ; ur țțaggadet ara !

6:51 Akken kan yekcem ɣer teflukt ɣuṛ-sen, yers waḍu-nni.

6:52 Inelmaden dehcen nezzeh, axaṭer ur fhimen ara lbeṛhan-nni n weɣṛum, imi ulawen nsen qquṛen.

6:53 Kemmlen abrid-nsen, ffɣen ɣer tmurt n Jiniṣaret, rsen dinna.

6:54 Mi d-ṣubben si teflukt, lɣaci yeɛqel Sidna Ɛisa imiren kan,

7:3 Axaṭer ifariziyen akk-d wat Isṛail ur tețțen ara uqbel ad ssirden ifassen-nsen, imi ṭṭfen di lɛadda n lejdud-nsen.

7:12 S wakka tserrḥem-as ur yețɛawan ara imawlan-is.

7:16 Ma tesɛam imeẓẓuɣen isellen, slet-ed !

7:22 zzna, yir ccehwat, cceṛ, tiḥila, lexdeɛ, tismin, lekfeṛ, zzux, d wayen akk yellan d lehbala.

7:25 ?-țideț, akken kan yewweḍ, ațaya yiwet n tmeṭṭut yesɛan yelli-s izdeɣ-iț uṛuḥani ; tesla s Sidna Ɛisa dɣa tusa-d teɣli ɣer idaṛṛen-is.

7:28 Terra-yas : Ih a Sidi, meɛna iqjan imecṭaḥ ddaw ṭṭabla, tețțen tifețțitin iɣellin i warrac.

7:29 Imiren yenna-yas : ?ef wawal-agi i d-tenniḍ, ṛuḥ, yelli-m yeffeɣ-iț uṛuḥani.

7:30 Mi tewweḍ ɣer wexxam-is, tufa yelli-s teḍleq ɣef wussu, yeffeɣ-iț uṛuḥani.

7:31 Sidna Ɛisa iṛuḥ si lǧiha n temdint n ?ur, iɛedda-d ɣef temdint n Sidun, yezger tamurt n ɛecṛa-nni n temdinin i deg llant ɛecṛa temdinin-nni, yewweḍ ɣer lebḥeṛ n Jlili.

8:1 Deg wussan-nni, innejmaɛ-ed daɣen waṭas n lɣaci, ur sɛin ara ayen ara ččen. Sidna Ɛisa yessawel i inelmaden-is, yenna-yasen :

8:2 Tejreḥ tasa-w ɣef lɣaci-agi, tlata wussan aya i qqimen yid-i, yerna ulac ayen ara ččen.

8:3 Ma yella rriɣ-ten ansi i d-kkan i laẓ, ad nezfen deg webrid axaṭer kra deg-sen usan-d si lebɛid.

8:4 Inelmaden-is rran-as-d : Ansi ara ɣ-d-yekk weɣṛum ara ten-yesseṛwun deg wemkan-agi yexlan ?

8:5 Sidna Ɛisa yesteqsa-ten : Acḥal n teḥbulin n weɣṛum i tesɛam ? Rran-as-ed : ?uṛ-nneɣ sebɛa teḥbulin !

8:6 Yenna i lɣaci ad qqimen ɣer lqaɛa. Dɣa yeddem-ed sebɛa teḥbulin-nni n weɣṛum, yeḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi, yebḍa-tent, yefka-tent i inelmaden-is feṛqen-tent i lɣaci.

8:7 Sɛan daɣen kra iselman ( iḥewtiwen ) imecṭaḥ. Yeḥmed daɣen Sidi Ṛebbi, yenna-yasen a ten-feṛqen i lɣaci.

8:8 ?čan armi ṛwan. Rnan sagren-d azal n sebɛa iqecwalen.

8:9 Llan deg wazal n ṛebɛa alaf n yemdanen. Mi fukken lmakla, iserreḥ-asen ad uɣalen ɣer ixxamen-nsen.

8:10 Imiren kan yuli ɣer teflukt nețța d inelmaden-is, ṛuḥen ɣer leǧwahi n Dalmanuta.

8:11 Ṛuḥen-d ifariziyen, bdan țmeslayen d Sidna Ɛisa iwakken a t-jeṛṛben, ssutren-as ad ixdem lbeṛhan ara d-iṣubben seg igenni.

8:12 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-d nnehta, yenna : Acuɣer i yi-d-ssuturen lbeṛhan yemdanen n lǧil-agi ? S tideț aql-i nniɣ-awen : d lmuḥal a s-d-ițunefk lbeṛhan i lǧil-agi !

8:13 Dɣa yeǧǧa-ten, yerkeb daɣen di teflukt iwakken ad izger agummaḍ.

8:15 Sidna Ɛisa iweṣṣa-ten, yenna-yasen : ?adret iman-nwen ɣef temtunt ( iɣes ) n ifariziyen akk-d win n Hiṛudus.

8:16 Inelmaden qqaṛen wway gar asen : «Yenna-yaɣ-d ayagi imi ur d newwi ara aɣṛum.»

8:17 Mi geẓra ayen țxemmimen, yenna-yasen : Acuɣer tețxemimem ɣef weɣṛum ? Urɛad tfaqem ? Mazal ur tefhimem ara ?

8:18  Eɛni qquṛen wulawen-nwen ? Tesɛam allen ur tețwalim ara, tesɛam imeẓẓuɣen ur tsellem ara ?

8:19 Ur tecfim ara mi bḍiɣ xemsa teḥbulin-nni n weɣṛum i xemsa alaf n yergazen, acḥal n iḍellaɛen n tsigar i tjemɛem ? Rran-as : Tnac iḍellaɛen.

8:20 I wasmi bḍiɣ sebɛa teḥbulin n weɣṛum i ṛebɛa alaf n yemdanen, acḥal n tqecwalin yeččuṛen ț-țisigar i d-tjemɛem ? Nnan-as : Sebɛa n tqecwalin.

8:21 Dɣa yenna-yasen : Werɛad tefhimem ihi ?

8:22 Ewwḍen ɣer temdint n Bitsayda. Wwin-as-d yiwen uderɣal, ḥellelen-t iwakken a t-yennal.

8:23 Sidna Ɛisa yeṭṭef afus i uderɣal-nni, yewwi-t beṛṛa n taddart. Yessers imetman ɣef wallen n uderɣal-nni, yessers ifassen is fell-as yesteqsa-t : Yella kra tețwaliḍ ?

8:25 Sidna Ɛisa iɛawed issers-as ifassen ɣef wallen, mi gemmuqel uderɣal-nni, yuɣal-it-id yeẓri, ițwali akken ilaq.

8:26 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa iceggeɛ-it ɣer wexxam-is, yenna-yas : Ur keččem ara ɣer taddart.

8:28 Rran-as-d : Kra deg-sen qqaṛen d Yeḥya aɣeṭṭas i telliḍ, wiyaḍ qqaṛen d Sidna Ilyas, ma d wiyaḍ ḥesben-k d yiwen si lenbiya.

8:29 Yerna yesteqsa-ten : I kunwi, d acu-iyi ɣuṛ-wen ? Buṭrus yerra-yas : Keččini d Lmasiḥ. ( yeɛni win yextaṛ Ṛebbi )

8:32 Yenna-yasen-d ayagi ɛinani. Buṭrus ijbed-it weḥd-es, ilumm fell-as.

8:33 Sidna Ɛisa yezzi, imuqel inelmaden-is, yenna i Buṭrus : Wexxeṛ akkin fell-i a Cciṭan ! Ixemmimen-ik mačči n Ṛebbi, meɛna n yemdanen !

9:1 Sidna Ɛisa yenna daɣen : -- S tideț a wen-d-iniɣ, kra seg wid yellan dagi, ur țmețțaten ara alamma walan tagelda n Ṛebbi tusa-d s tmanegt tameqqrant.

12:20 Llan ihi sebɛa watmaten : amezwaru yezweǧ yuɣal yemmut ur d-yeǧǧi ara dderya.

12:21 Wis sin yuɣ taǧǧalt-nni, yemmut ula d nețța mbla ma yeǧǧa-d dderya. Akken daɣen i tedṛa d wis tlata,

12:22 ula d yiwen si sebɛa-nni i gzewǧen yid-es ur d-yeǧǧi dderya. ?er taggara, ula ț-țameṭṭut-nni temmut.

12:23 Asmi ara d-ḥyun lmegtin, anwa si sebɛa watmaten-nni ara ț-yesɛun ț-țameṭṭut-is imi i ț-uɣen di sebɛa yid-sen ?

12:24 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yasen : Tɣelṭem ! Ur tefhimem tira iqedsen, ur tessinem tazmert n Ṛebbi.

12:25 Axaṭer ass n ḥeggu n lmegtin, irgazen ț-țlawin ur țemyezwaǧen ara, lameɛna ad ilin am lmalayekkat deg igenwan.

12:26 Ma d ayen yeɛnan ḥeggu n lmegtin, leɛmeṛ ur teɣṛim di tektabt n Musa, mi t-id-iluɛa Sidi Ṛebbi zdat udarnu ireqqen ? Yenna-yas-d : Nekk d Illu n Ibṛahim, d Illu n Isḥaq, d Illu n Yeɛqub.

12:27 Ihi Ṛebbi mačči d Illu n lmegtin i gella, lameɛna d Illu n wid yeddren. S tideț, atan tɣelṭem aṭas.

12:28 Yiwen n umusnaw n ccariɛa mi geẓra Sidna Ɛisa ijaweb akken ilaq, iqeṛṛeb ɣuṛ-es yenna-yas : Anwa i d lameṛ ameqqran di ccariɛa ?

12:29 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Atah lameṛ ameqqran : ?ess-ed ay agdud n Isṛail ! Sidi Ṛebbi-nneɣ d yiwen.

12:30 Ilaq aț-țḥemmleḍ Sidi Ṛebbi s wul-ik, s teṛwiḥt-ik akk, s wayen yellan di tezmert-ik akk-d lɛeqliya-k meṛṛa .

12:31 Atah wis sin : Ilaq aț-țḥemleḍ lɣiṛ-ik am yiman-ik. Ulac lameṛ yugaren wigi.

12:32 Lɛalem-nni yenna-yas : ?-țideț a Sidi ! Ayen i d-tenniḍ d ṣṣeḥ ; Ṛebbi d yiwen ulac wayeḍ.

12:33 Yerna ma nḥemmel-it s wul-nneɣ, s lɛeqliya-nneɣ, akk-d wayen yellan di țezmert-nneɣ meṛṛa, ma nḥemmel daɣen lɣiṛ-nneɣ am yiman-nneɣ, annect-agi axiṛ n iseflawen akk-d lewɛadi.

12:34 Sidna Ɛisa mi t-iwala ijaweb-ed s tmusni, yenna-yas : Ur tebɛideḍ ara ɣef tgelda n Sidi Ṛebbi. Ulac win i s-d-yernan asteqsi nniḍen.

12:35 Mi gella Sidna Ɛisa yesselmad di lǧameɛ iqedsen yenna : Amek armi imusnawen n ccariɛa qqaṛen-d belli Lmasiḥ d mmi-s n Sidna Dawed ?

12:36 S Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i d-yenna Dawed s yiman-is : Sidi Ṛebbi yenna-d i Sidi : Qqim ɣer tama-w tayeffust alamma rriɣ iɛdawen-ik seddaw iḍaṛṛen-ik.

12:37 Dawed s yiman-is issawel-as « a Sidi », amek ihi yezmer ad yili d mmi-s ? Aṭas n lɣaci i s-yesmeḥsisen s lfeṛḥ.

12:38 Sidna Ɛisa yesselmad lɣaci yeqqaṛ-asen : ?adret daɣen iman-nwen ɣef yimusnawen n ccariɛa i gḥemmlen ad ḥewwsen s ijellaben iɣezfanen, ḥemmlen daɣen ad selmen fell-asen medden di leswaq ;

12:39 țextiṛin imukan imezwura di leǧwameɛ akk-d imukan lɛali di tmeɣṛiwin, lameɛna

12:40 tețțen ayla n tuǧǧal ; ssiɣzifen taẓallit iwakken a ten-id-walin yemdanen. Lɛiqab-nsen ad yili d ameqqran !

12:41 Sidna Ɛisa yella yeqqim deg umkan iqublen asenduq i deg srusun lewɛadi, yețmuqul amek srusuyen lɣaci lewɛadi-nsen. Aṭas n imeṛkantiyen yeggaren xiṛella n yedrimen.

12:42 Tusa-d yiwet n taǧǧalt tigellilt tessers sin iṣurdiyen yesɛan azal n ṛebɛa twiztin n lfeṭṭa.

12:43 Sidna Ɛisa yessawel i inelmaden-is, yenna yasen : A wen-d-iniɣ tideț : lweɛda i tessers taǧǧalt-agi taẓawalit, tugar akk lewɛadi i ssersen wiyaḍ.

12:44 Axaṭer wiyaḍ seddqen seg wayen sɛan d zzyada, ma d nețțat tseddeq ciṭṭuh-nni s wacu ara tɛic.

13:1 Mi d-iffeɣ Sidna Ɛisa si lǧameɛ iqedsen, yiwen seg inelmaden-is yenna-yas : A Sidi, muqel acḥal yecbeḥ lebni-agi ! Acḥal cebḥen yedɣaɣen-is !

13:2 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Deg wayagi irkul i tețwaliḍ, ur d-yețɣimi yiwen wedɣaɣ ɣef wayeḍ ; kullec ad ihudd.

13:3 Sidna Ɛisa yeqqim deg iɣil uzemmur iqublen lǧameɛ iqedsen. Buṭrus, Yeɛqub, Yuḥenna akk-d Andriyus i gellan yid-es, steqsan-t :

13:4 Ini-yaɣ-d melmi ara d-yedṛu wayagi ; d acu ara ɣ-d-isbeggnen lweqt i deg ara yedṛu wannect-a.

13:5 Imiren, Sidna Ɛisa yebda yețweṣṣi ten : ?uṛ-wat win ara kkun-ikellxen !

13:6 Axaṭer aṭas ara d-yasen s yisem-iw, a wen-d-inin : « D nekk i d Lmasiḥ » ad kellxen i waṭas n lɣaci.

13:7 M'ara teslem s tegrawliwin i d-ideṛṛun di mkul amkan akk-d țid i d-iteddun, ur țțaggadet ara axaṭer ilaq a d-yedṛu wannect-nni, lameɛna mačči ț-țaggara n ddunit i d-yewḍen.

13:8  Agdud a d-yekker ɣer wayeḍ, tageldit aț-țennaɣ ț-țayeḍ, a d-ilint zzelzlat di kra imukan, a d-yili laẓ ; akkagi ara d-yebdu leɛtab di ddunit.

13:9 ?adret ɣef yiman-nwen ; atan a kkun-caṛɛen zdat lɛulama n ccariɛa, a kkun-wten di leǧwameɛ. ?ef ddemma-w, a kkun-sbedden zdat wid i gḥekmen tamurt akk-d igelliden iwakken aț-țilim d inigan-iw zdat-nsen.

13:10 Lameɛna di tazwara ilaq lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ad ițwabecceṛ uqbel i leǧnas meṛṛa.

13:11 M'ara kkun-ẓẓuɣṛen a kkun awin ɣer ccṛeɛ, ur xeddmet ara anezgum ɣef wayen ara tinim ; init-ed kan ayen ara wen-d-yasen di teswiɛt-nni, axaṭer mačči d kunwi ara imeslayen lameɛna d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

13:12 Yiwen ad yefk gma-s ɣer lmut, wayeḍ ad yefk mmi-s ; dderya a d-kkren ɣer imawlan-nsen a ten-ssiwḍen ɣer lmut.

13:13 A kkun-keṛhen irkul ɣef ddemma n yisem-iw. Lameɛna win ara yeṭṭfen alamma ț-țaggara ad ițwasellek.

13:14 Ass m'ara twalim lmuṣiba tameqqrant tella deg umkan anda ur ilaq ara aț-țili, « win ara yeɣṛen ayagi ilaq ad imeyyez ! » ass-nni wid ara yilin di tmurt n Yahuda ad rewlen ɣer idurar ;

13:15 win ara yilin s ufella n ssqef, ur ikeččem ara ɣer wexxam-is iwakken a d-iddem leḥwayeǧ-is.

13:16 Akken daɣen win ara yilin di lexla, ur yețțuɣal ara ɣer deffir iwakken a d-yawi abeṛnus-is.

13:17 A tawaɣit n tilawin ara yilin s tadist akk-d țid ara yeṣṣuṭuḍen deg wussan-nni.

13:18 Dɛut ɣer Ṛebbi iwakken ur d-ideṛṛu ara wannect-nni di ccetwa,

13:19 axaṭer deg wussan-nni, ad yili yiwen n leɛtab ur neẓri seg wasmi i d-texleq ddunit ar ass-a, yerna d ayen ur nețțuɣal a d-yedṛu.

13:20 Lemmer ur yessenqes ara Sidi Ṛebbi kra seg wussan-nni, yiwen ur yezmir ad imneɛ. Meɛna issenqes seg-sen ɣef ddemma n wid yextaṛ.

13:21 Di lweqt-nni ma yenna-yawen walebɛaḍ : « Lmasiḥ atah dagi » neɣ «atan dihin», ur t-țțamnet ara.

13:22 Axaṭer a d-asen wid ara yerren iman-nsen d Lmasiḥ neɣ d lenbiya, ad xeddmen lbeṛhanat akk-d licaṛat, iwakken ad kellxen ma zemren ula d wid yextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

13:23 Atah nniɣ-awen-d annect-agi uqbel a d-yeḍru. --

13:24  M'ara ɛeddin wussan n twaɣit-nni, tafat n yiṭij aț-țenqes, aggur ur d-yețțak ara tiziri-ines,

13:25  itran daɣen a d-ɣlin seg igenni, t inezmarin n igenwan ad rgagint .

13:26 Imiren, ad anwali Mmi-s n bunadem yusa-d s ufella n usigna s tezmert tameqqrant d lɛaḍima.

13:27 A d-iceggeɛ lmalayekkat-is ɣer yal amkan n ddunit, a d-snejmaɛen seg yixfawen n ddunit wid i gextaṛ meṛṛa.

13:28 Meyyzet ihi ɣef lemtel n tmeɣṛust : mi lqaqit isegman-is, fsan, fkan-d iferrawen, teẓram iqeṛṛeb-ed unebdu.

13:29 Akken ula d kunwi, m'ara twalim ayagi meṛṛa yewweḍ-ed, ḥṣut belli yusa-d lweqt, Mmi-s n bunadem atan ɣer tewwurt.

13:30 A wen-d-iniɣ tideț : lǧil-agi ur ițɛeddi ara alamma yedṛa-d wayagi meṛṛa.

13:31 Igenni d lqaɛa ad fnun ma d imeslayen-iw ur fennun ara.

13:32 ?ef wayen yeɛnan ass akk-d lweqt-nni yiwen ur ten-yessin, ama d lmalayekkat deg igenwan ama d Mmi-s n bunadem, anagar Baba Ṛebbi i ten-isnen.

13:33 Ur gganet ara, ɛasset iman-nwen imi ur teẓrim ara melmi ara d-yaweḍ lweqt-nni.

13:34 Ad yili wass-nni am yiwen wergaz ara yinigen ɣer lebɛid, ad iwekkel axxam-is i iqeddacen-is, yal yiwen s ccɣel-is, imiren ad iweṣṣi aɛessas n tewwurt ad iɛiwez.

13:35 Ɛiwzet ihi, axaṭer ur teẓrim ara melmi ara d-yas bab n wexxam, ama tameddit neɣ țnaṣfa n yiḍ, m'ara yeskkuɛ uyaziḍ neɣ taṣebḥit.

13:36 ?adret a kkun-id-yaf teṭṭsem imi ur teẓrim ara melmi ara d-yas.

13:37 Ayen akka i wen-d-nniɣ, nniɣ t-id i mkul yiwen deg-wen : ɛiwzet !

14:1 Qqimen-d sin wussan i Tfaska n izimer n leslak i deg xeddmen aɣṛum mbla tamtunt ( iɣes ). Lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɛulama, țnadin s tḥila, sebba s wayes ara ḥebbsen Sidna Ɛisa iwakken a t-nɣen.

14:2 Qqaṛen : Ur nezmir ara a t-nessekcem ɣer lḥebs deg wass n lɛid, neɣ m'ulac lɣaci ad sekkren ccwal.

14:3 Sidna Ɛisa yella di taddart n Bitanya, deg wexxam n Semɛun ihelken lbeṛs ; mi qqimen ad ččen, ațaya yiwet n tmeṭṭut tekcem-ed, tewwi-d yid-es taqbuct ițwaxedmen s wezṛu amellal, teččuṛ d leɛṭeṛ yeṣfan ɣlayen. Teṛẓa taqbuct-nni, tesmar-iț ɣef wuqeṛṛuy n Sidna Ɛisa.

14:4 Kra deg-sen zeɛfen nnan : Acuɣer i tessexṣeṛ leɛṭeṛ-agi ?

14:5 Lemmer i t-nezzenz, a d-yawi akteṛ n telt meyya twiztin n lfeṭṭa ara nefṛeq i igellilen ! Dɣa rfan ɣef tmeṭṭut-nni.

14:6 Sidna Ɛisa yenna : Anfet-as acimi i ț-tesnuɣnayem ? Ayen akka i yi-texdem d ayen yelhan !

14:7 Igellilen ad ilin daymen gar-awen, tzemrem a ten-tɛiwnem melmi i wen yehwa ; ma d nekkini ur țɣimiɣ ara daymen yid-wen.

14:8 Ayen tezmer a t-texdem texdem it : tedhen lǧețța-w thegga-ț i temḍelt.

14:9 A wen-d-iniɣ tideț : di mkul amkan n ddunit anda ara yețțubecceṛ lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ, a d-țmektayen tameṭṭut-agi d wayen akka texdem.

14:10 Yudas n Qeṛyut yiwen si tnac inelmaden, iṛuḥ ɣer imeqqranen n lmuqedmin, yemsefham yid-sen amek ara sen yezzenz Sidna Ɛisa.

14:11 Feṛḥen aṭas mi s-slan, ɛuhden-t a s-fken idrimen. Imiren Yudas ițqellib tagniț i deg ara sen-t-yezzenz.

14:12 Ass amenzu n Tfaska n weɣṛum mbla tamtunt ( iɣes ), i deg zellun izimer n leslak, inelmaden nnan i Sidna Ɛisa : Anda tebɣiḍ a nheggi imensi n Tfaska ?

14:13 Iceggeɛ sin seg inelmaden-is, yenna-yasen : Ṛuḥet ɣer temdint, a d-temlilem d yiwen wergaz yewwi-d tacmuxt n waman, tebɛet-eț ɣer wanda ara yekcem,

14:14 init i bab n wexxam-nni : Ssid-nneɣ yenna-yak : « anda-ț texxamt i deg ara ččeɣ imensi n Tfaska n izimer nekk d inelmaden-iw ? »

14:15 A kkun-yessali ɣer yiwet n tɣuṛfeț tameqqrant iwesɛen, kullec yella deg-s. Dinna ara theggim imensi n Tfaska.

14:16 Inelmaden ṛuḥen, mi wwḍen ɣer temdint, ufan kullec akken i sen-t yenna. Dɣa heggan imensi n Tfaska.

14:17 Mi d-teɣli tmeddit, Sidna Ɛisa yusa-d akk-d tnac-nni inelmaden.

14:18 Mi qqimen ad ččen, Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : A wen-iniɣ tideț : yiwen seg-wen itețțen yid-i, ad iyi-ixdeɛ.

14:19 Yeɣli-d leḥzen fell-asen, bdan steqsayen-t yiwen yiwen qqaṛen-as : A Sidi, neɣ mačči d nekk ?

14:20 Yenna-yasen : D yiwen seg-wen, yessasnen yid-i talqimt n weɣṛum deg uḍebsi.

14:21 ?-țideț, Mmi-s n bunadem ad iṛuḥ akken yețwakteb fell-as di tira iqedsen ; meɛna a nnger n wergaz-nni ara t-ixedɛen. Axiṛ-as lemmer ur d-ilul ara ɣer ddunit !

14:22 Mi bdan tețțen, Sidna Ɛisa yeddem-d aɣṛum, yeḥmed Ṛebbi, yebḍa-t, ifṛeq-it i inelmaden-is, yenna yasen : ?čet-eț, ayagi d-lǧețța-w.

14:23 Yeddem-ed daɣen lkas n ccṛab, yeḥmed Ṛebbi, yefka-yasen, swan meṛṛa seg-s.

14:24 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Ayagi d idammen n leɛqed amaynut, d idammen-iw ara yazlen ɣef waṭas n yemdanen.

14:25 S tideț nniɣ-awen : ur țuɣaleɣ ara ad sweɣ si lfakya n tẓurin, alamma d ass i deg ara sweɣ ccṛab ajdid di tgelda n Ṛebbi.

14:26 Mi cnan isefra n ?abur, ulin ɣer yiɣil n uzemmur.

14:27 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : A kkun-yewwet akk wugur akken yețwakteb : Ad ewwteɣ ameksa, ulli ad rewlent ɣer mkul lǧiha .

14:28 Yerna-yasen-d : Meɛna m'ara d-ḥyuɣ si ger lmegtin a wen-zwireɣ ɣer tmurt n Jlili.

14:29 Buṭrus yenna-yas : ?as ad ccḍen akk, nekkini ur țeccḍeɣ ara.

14:30 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : A k-d-iniɣ tideț : iḍ-agi uqbel ad yeskkuɛ uyaziḍ snat n tikkal, kečč a i-tnekṛeḍ tlata iberdan.

14:31 Meɛna Buṭrus yenna-yas daɣen : Ad qebleɣ ad dduɣ yid-ek ula ɣer lmut wala a k-nekkṛeɣ. Inelmaden nniḍen nnan-d meṛṛa akken.

14:32 Mi wwḍen ɣer tmazirt n Jitsimani, Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen i inelmaden-is : Qqimet dagi, ma d nekk ad ṛuḥeɣ ad ẓalleɣ.

14:33 Yewwi yid-es Buṭrus, Yeɛqub akk-d Yuḥenna. Yetḥeyyeṛ, tekcem-it tugdi.

14:34 Yenna-yasen : ?usseɣ s leḥzen n lmut ; qqimet dagi, ɛiwzet !

14:35 Issebɛed iman-is fell-asen, iḍeggeṛ iman-is ɣer lqaɛa, yedɛa ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi ma yella wamek ara yessebɛed fell-as taswiɛt-nni n leɛtab.

14:36 Yeqqaṛ-as : A baba, a baba Ṛebbi ! Kečč tzemreḍ i kullec ; ssebɛed fell-i lkas-agi n leɛtab. Lameɛna d lebɣi-k kan ara yedṛun mačči d lebɣi-w.

14:37 Yuɣal-ed ɣer inelmaden-is, yufa-ten-id ṭṭsen. Yenna i Buṭrus : A Semɛun, teṭṭseḍ ? Ur tezmireḍ ara aț-țɛiwzeḍ ula d ssaɛa ?

14:38 Ɛiwzet tẓallem, iwakken ur tɣellim ara deg ujeṛṛeb. Ṛṛuḥ yeǧhed yerna yebɣa, meɛna lǧețța ur tezmir ara, tulwa.

14:39 Issebɛed iman-is daɣen, iɛawed i tẓallit-nni.

14:40 Mi d-yuɣal yufa-ten daɣen ṭsen, iɣleb-iten nuddam. Ur ẓrin d acu ara s-d-erren.

14:41 Yuɣal-ed tikkelt tis tlata yenna yasen : Mazal-ikkun teṭṭsem, testeɛfam ! Dayen tura, tewweḍ-ed teswiɛt-nni ! Mmi-s n bunadem ițusellem ger ifassen n imednuben.

14:42 Kkret fell awen a nṛuḥet ! Win i yi-izzenzen yewweḍ-ed.

14:43 Mazal-it iheddeṛ, mi d-yewweḍ Yudas, yiwen si tnac-nni. Ddan-d yid-es aṭas n lɣaci s iɛekkzan d ijenwiyen, ceggɛen-ten-id lɛulama n ccariɛa, imeqqranen n lmuqedmin akk-d imeqqranen n wegdud.

14:44 Win akken i gzenzen Sidna Ɛisa yemsefham yid-sen, yenna-yasen : Win i ɣef ara selmeɣ, d nețța ; ṭṭfet-eț tefkem-t i iɛessasen a t-awin.

14:45 Akken kan i d-yewweḍ, iqeṛṛeb ed ɣer Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : A Sidi ! Dɣa isellem fell-as.

14:46 Imiren lɣaci mmɣen ɣef Sidna Ɛisa, ṭṭfen-t.

14:47 Yiwen seg wid yellan dinna ijbed-ed ajenwi, yewwet aqeddac n lmuqeddem ameqqran, igzem-as ameẓẓuɣ.

14:48 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Teffɣem-d ɣuṛ-i s ijenwiyen d iɛekzan am akken d amcum i lliɣ ?

14:49 Mkul ass lliɣ yid-wen sselmadeɣ di lǧameɛ, yiwen ur yessers afus-is fell-i. Meɛna ayagi yedṛa-d, iwakken ad nnekmalent tira iqedsen.

14:50 Imiren ǧǧan-t akk inelmaden-is, rewlen.

14:51 Yiwen ilemẓi yelsan anagar timelḥeft itbeɛ-it. Emmɣen fell-as a t-ṭṭfen,

14:52 meɛna iserreḥ i tmelḥeft-nni, yerwel akken d aɛeryan.

14:53 Wwin Sidna Ɛisa ɣer wexxam n lmuqeddem ameqqran, anda nnejmaɛen imeqqranen n lmuqedmin, lɛulama n ccariɛa akk-d imeqqranen n wegdud.

14:54 Buṭrus itbeɛ-it s lebɛid, armi d azniq n wexxam n lmuqeddem ameqqran. Yeqqim ger iɛessasen, yesseḥmuy ɣer tmes.

14:55 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen akk-d tejmaɛt n ccṛeɛ, țnadin cchada iṣṣeḥan s wacu ara ḥekmen fell-as s lmut, lameɛna ur ufin ara.

14:56 Aṭas i gcehden fell-as s ẓẓur, lameɛna cchadat-nsen ur ṣṣeḥant ara.

15:1 ?ṣbeḥ zik, lmuqedmin imeqqranen ruḥen mcawaṛen akk-d imeqqranen n wegdud, lɛulama n ccariɛa akk dusqamu n ccṛeɛ. Urzen Sidna Ɛisa, wwin-t sellmen-t i Bilaṭus.

15:2 Bilaṭus yesteqsa-t yenna yas : D kečč i d agellid n wat Isṛail ? Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Tenniḍ-t-id.

15:3 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen ccetkan fell-as ɣef waṭas n temsalin.

15:4 Bilaṭus yerna yesteqsa-t : Ur d-tețțaraḍ ara awal ? Ur tesliḍ ara ayen akk s wayes i d-țcetkin fell-ak ?

15:8 Lɣaci ulin ɣer Bilaṭus iwakken a s-ssutren ayen yennum ixeddem-asen-t.

15:9 Bilaṭus yerra-yasen-d : Tebɣam a wen-d-serrḥeɣ i ugellid n wat Isṛail ?

15:10 Axaṭer ifaq belli s tismin i s-t-id-wwin lmuqedmin imeqqranen.

15:11 Lameɛna lmuqedmin imeqqranen sḥeṛcen lɣaci iwakken ad inin i Bilaṭus: « d Barabas iwumi ara d-tserrḥeḍ. »

15:12 Bilaṭus yenna-yasen daɣen : D acu tebɣam ad xedmeɣ s win iwumi teqqaṛem agellid n wat Isṛail ?

15:13 ?ɛeggiḍen-as : ?emmeṛ-it ɣef wumidag !

15:14 Bilaṭus yenna-yasen : D acu i gexdem n diri ? Rnan țɛeggiḍen akteṛ : ?emmeṛ-it ɣef wumidag !

15:15 Imi i gebɣa Bilaṭus ad yeddu di lebɣi i lɣaci, iserreḥ-ed i Barabas, ma d Sidna Ɛisa yefka lameṛ a t-ewten s ujelkkaḍ, syenna a t-semmṛen ɣef wumidag.

15:16 Lɛeskeṛ wwin Sidna Ɛisa ɣer wefrag n lbeṛj, ssawlen-d i terbaɛt nsen.

15:20 Mi stehzan fell-as, kksen-as abeṛnus-nni azeggaɣ, rran-as lqecc-is, imiren wwin-t ɣer beṛṛa iwakken a t-ṣemmṛen ɣef lluḥ.

15:21 Iɛedda-d syenna yiwen umsebrid i d-yuɣalen si lexla, isem-is Semɛun n Qiṛwan, baba-s n Alexandrus akk-d Rufus ; ḥeṛsen-t lɛeskeṛ iwakken ad ibbib amidag n Sidna Ɛisa.

15:29 Kra n wid yețɛeddayen syenna reggmen-t, țhuzzun iqeṛṛay-nsen, qqaṛen-as : Hey ! a win yețhuddun lǧameɛ iqedsen, ibennu-t di tlata wussan,
