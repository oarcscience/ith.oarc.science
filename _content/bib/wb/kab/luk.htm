Kabyle Bible: Luke

1:1 Imi aṭas i gebdan tira ɣef wayen yedṛan gar-aneɣ,

1:2 akken i ɣ-t-id ɛawden wid yellan d inigan si tazwara, iwalan kullec s wallen-nsen ; uɣalen d iqeddacen n wawal n Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:3 Steqsaɣ ula d nekk akken ilaq ɣef wayen akk yedṛan seg wass amezwaru, ufiɣ d ayen yelhan a k-t-id aruɣ a Tawfilus,

1:4 iwakken aț-țissineḍ tideț ɣef wayen i tesliḍ meṛṛa.

1:5 Di zzman n Hiṛudus, agellid n tmurt n Yahuda, yella yiwen n lmuqeddem si tejmaɛt n Abya, isem-is Zakarya. Tameṭṭut-is Ilicaba, seg izuṛan n Haṛun.

1:6 D iḥeqqiyen i llan i sin, ḥerzen yerna ḍuɛen awal n Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:7 Ur sɛin ara dderya, meqqṛit i sin di leɛmeṛ yerna Ilicaba ț-țiɛiqeṛt.

1:8 Mi d-tewweḍ nnuba n leqdic n wedrum n Zakarya di lǧameɛ iqedsen

1:9 akken i tella di lɛadda n lmuqedmin, gren tasɣaṛt, tṣaḥ-ed Zakarya iwakken ad ikcem ɣer wemkan iqedsen ad isseṛɣ lebxuṛ.

1:10 Mi gella isseṛɣay lebxuṛ di lǧameɛ, lɣaci meṛṛa yeqqimen di beṛṛa deɛɛun ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:11 Iḍheṛ-as-d yiwen lmelk n Sidi Ṛebbi, ibedd ɣer tama tayeffust n udekkan-nni anda sseṛɣayen lebxuṛ.

1:12 Mi t-iwala Zakarya, yedhec ikcem-it lxuf.

1:13 Lmelk-nni yenna yas -ed : Ur țțagad ara a Zakarya, ayen i tessutreḍ ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi yețwaqbel. Tameṭṭut-ik Ilicaba ad a k-d-tesɛu aqcic, a s-tsemmiḍ Yeḥya.

1:14 Aț-țfeṛḥeḍ yis, a k-yili d sebba n sseɛd ; imdanen meṛṛa ad feṛḥen s tlalit-is.

1:15 Ad yesɛu ccan d ameqqran ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi, a d-iččaṛ d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen si tɛebbuṭ n yemma-s, ur itess ccṛab, ur itess ayen nniḍen isekkṛen.

1:16 A d-yerr aṭas n wat Isṛail ɣer webrid n Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:17 Ad ilḥu s leɛnaya n Sidi Ṛebbi, s ṛṛuḥ ț-țezmert n nnbi Ilyas. Ad issemlil imawlan d warraw-nsen, a d-yerr wid ijehlen ɣer webrid n iḥeqqiyen, akken ad iheggi i Sidi Ṛebbi agdud ara t-iḍuɛen.

1:18 Zakarya yenna i lmelk : Amek ara ɛeqleɣ belli ayen akka i d-tenniḍ ț-țideț ? Nekk d amɣaṛ, ula ț-țameṭṭut-iw meqqṛet di leɛmeṛ !

1:19 Lmelk-nni yerra-yas-d : Nekk d Jebrayil, d aqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi ; usiɣ-ed s ɣuṛ-es iwakken a k-d-awiɣ lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ.

1:20 Lameɛna imi ur tumineḍ ara s yimeslayen-iw atan aț-țeggugmeḍ, ur d-ițeffeɣ ara wawal seg yimi-k alamma d asmi ara d-yedṛu wannect-agi. Meɛna ḥṣu belli ayen akka i k-d-nniɣ ad yedṛu deg wass-is.

1:21 Lɣaci-nni yețṛaǧun Zakarya deg ufrag, wehmen acuɣeṛ iɛeṭṭel deg umkan iqedsen n lǧameɛ.

1:22 Mi d-iffeɣ, yeggugem, ur izmir ara a d-yessufeɣ awal ; lɣaci-nni fehmen belli iweḥḥa-yas-ed kra Sidi Ṛebbi. Yebda ițwehhi-yasen-d s ifassen-is, yeqqim d agugam.

1:23 Mi kfan wussan n lxedma-s di lǧameɛ, Zakarya yuɣal ɣer wexxam-is.

1:24 Ɛeddan kra n wussan, tameṭṭut-is Ilicaba terfed tadist ; dɣa tesseḥjeb iman-is xemsa wagguren, teqqaṛ :

1:25 Atan Sidi Ṛebbi yerra-d ddehn-is ɣuṛ-i, ikkes-iyi lɛib zdat yemdanen.

1:26 Aggur wis sețța, Sidi Ṛebbi iceggeɛ-d lmelk Jebrayil ɣer taddart n Naṣaret, di tmurt n Jlili.

1:27 Iceggeɛ-it-id ɣer yiwet n tlemẓit i gexḍeb yiwen wergaz isem-is Yusef, yellan seg izuṛan n Sidna Dawed. Tilemẓit-agi isem-is Meryem.

1:28 Ikcem lmelk ɣuṛ-es yenna-yas : Sslam fell-am a tin iburek Sidi Ṛebbi ! Sidi Ṛebbi yella yid-em.

1:29 Meryem tedhec, tenna : D acu i d lmeɛna n imeslayen agi ?

1:30 Lmelk yenna-yas : Ur țțaggad ara a Meryem ! S ṛṛeḥma-s tameqqrant, Sidi Ṛebbi yextaṛ-ikem.

1:31 Atan aț-țrefdeḍ tadist, a d-tesɛuḍ aqcic, semmi-yas Ɛisa, (yeɛni : « Amsellek »)

1:32 Ad yesɛu ccan d ameqqran, ad ițțusemmi Mmi-s n Sidi Ṛebbi ɛlayen. Sidi Ṛebbi a t-yerr d agellid akken i t-yella Sidna Dawed, yiwen si lejdud-is.

1:33 Ad iḥkem ɣef tarwa n Yeɛqub i dayem, tageldit-is ur tfennu ara.

1:34 Meryem tenna i lmelk-nni : Amek ara d-idṛu yid-i wayagi nekk ur nezwiǧ ara ?

1:35 Lmelk yerra-yas : Atan a d-yers fell-am Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ; tazmert n Sidi Ṛebbi ɛlayen a kem-tɣumm am tili, daymi aqcic-agi imqeddes ara d-ilalen ad ițțsemmi « Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ».

1:36 Ilicaba i m-ițțilin, ula d nețțat ațan s tadist a d-tesɛu aqcic ɣas akken meqqṛet di leɛmeṛ. Tin akken iwumi semman tiɛiqeṛt, ațan deg waggur wis sețța,

1:37 imi ulac wayen iwumi ur izmir Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:38 Meryem tenna : Aql-i ț-țaqeddact n Sidi Ṛebbi, ad idṛu yid-i wayen i d-tenniḍ. Imiren Lmelk-nni yeǧǧa-ț, iṛuḥ.

1:39 Deg wussan-nni, Meryem tekker tṛuḥ s lemɣawla ɣer yiwet n taddart yellan deg idurar n tmurt n Yahuda.

1:40 Tekcem ɣer wexxam n Zakarya, tsellem ɣef Ilicaba.

1:41 Akken i tesla Ilicaba i sslam n Meryem, yefrawes llufan yellan di tɛebbuṭ-is ; imiren teččuṛ d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen,

1:42 tɛeggeḍ tenna : A Meryem, tețțubarkeḍ ger tilawin meṛṛa, ițțubarek daɣen win yellan di tɛebbuṭ-im.

1:43 D acu-yi, iwakken a d-terzef ɣuṛ-i yemma-s n Ssid-iw ?

1:44 Akken kan i sliɣ i sslam-im, llufan yellan di tɛebbuṭ-iw yefrawes s lfeṛḥ.

1:45 ?-țaseɛdit kemm yumnen s wayen i m-d-yenna Sidi Ṛebbi !

1:46 Meryem tenna : Taṛwiḥt-iw teḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi,

1:47 ul-iw yeččuṛ d lfeṛḥ imi d nețța i d amsellek-iw,

1:48 axaṭer iwala-yi-d nekk taqeddact-is tameɣbunt ur nesɛi azal. Sya d asawen si lǧil ɣer lǧil a yi-qqaṛen : « ț-țaseɛdit »,

1:49 imi Sidi Ṛebbi Bab n tezmert, yexdem yis-i ayen issewhamen. Isem-is d imqeddes.

1:50  Ṛṛeḥma-s tețdum si lǧil ɣer lǧil ɣef wid akk i t-iḍuɛen.

1:51 Isken-ed tazmert n yiɣil-is, isseɛṛeq iberdan i wid yețzuxxun ;

1:52  yessers-ed imeqqranen seg imukan eɛlayen, yerfed wid yețwaḥeqṛen ;

1:53  yesseṛwa-yasen leṛẓaq i wid yelluẓen, ma d imeṛkantiyen yerra-ten ifassen d ilmawen.

1:54  Isellek tarwa n wat Isṛail yellan d iqeddacen-is, ur ițțu ara Ṛṛeḥma-s,

1:55 i gewɛed i Sidna Ibṛahim d warraw-is i dayem akken i t-yenna i lejdud-nneɣ.

1:56 Meryem teqqim ɣer Ilicaba azal n tlata wagguren d wamek i tuɣal ɣer taddart-is.

1:57 Yewweḍ-ed lweqt i deg ara d-tarew Ilicaba, tesɛa-d aqcic.

1:58 Imawlan-is d lǧiran-is feṛḥen aṭas mi slan s ṛṛehma i d-issers fell-as Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:59 Mi wwḍen tmanya n wussan ɣef weqcic-nni, usan-d a s-sḍehṛen, bɣan a s-semmin s yisem n baba-s : Zakarya.

1:60 Dɣa tenṭeq-ed yemma-s tenna-yasen : Xaṭi ! A s-nsemmi Yeḥya !

1:61 Nnan-as : Ula d yiwen deg wedrum-nwen ur yesɛi isem-agi.

1:62 Steqsan baba-s s uwehhi amek yebɣa ad isemmi i mmi-s.

1:63 Issuter asen-d talwiḥt, yura-yasen-d deg-s : « Yeḥya i d isem-is ». Dɣa wehmen meṛṛa.

1:64 Imiren kan iserreḥ yiles-is, yebda iheddeṛ, yețḥemmid Ṛebbi ițcekkiṛ-it.

1:65 Imezdaɣ n lǧiha-nni meṛṛa wehmen, tɛeǧǧben. Deg idurar n tmurt n Yahuda țmeslayen akk ɣef wayen yedṛan.

1:66 Wid akk yeslan imeslayen-nni, ḥerzen-ten deg wulawen-nsen, qqaṛen : d acu ara d-iffeɣ weqcic-agi ? Axaṭer s tideț afus n Sidi Ṛebbi yella fell-as.

1:67 Zakarya, baba-s n weqcic-nni, iččuṛ d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, icar-ed s imeslayen-agi :

1:68 Ad ițțubarek Sidi Ṛebbi, Illu n wat Isṛail imi d-yerra ddehn-is ɣer wegdud-is iwakken a t-isellek.

1:69 Yefka-yaɣ-d yiwen wemsellek d ameqqran seg izuṛan n Sidna Dawed aqeddac-is,

1:70 am akken i t-id-ixebbeṛ Sidi Ṛebbi si zzman iɛeddan, seg yimi n lenbiya iqedsen :

1:71 Ad yili d amsellek ara ɣ-imenɛen seg yiɛdawen-nneɣ, si ger ifassen n wid akk i ɣ-ikeṛhen.

1:72 Isbeggen ṛṛeḥma-s i lejdud-nneɣ, yemmekta-d lemɛahda-s iqedsen

1:73 i gefka i jeddi-tneɣ Sidna Ibṛahim.

1:74 M'ara nețțusellek seg ufus n yeɛdawen-nneɣ, a neɛbed Sidi Ṛebbi mbla lxuf,

1:75 s lḥeqq, s ṭṭaɛa d wannuz deg wussan n ddunit-nneɣ meṛṛa.

1:76 Ma d kečč a mmi, aț-țețțusemmiḍ d nnbi n Sidi Ṛebbi eɛlayen axaṭer aț-țedduḍ zdat-es iwakken aț-țheggiḍ iberdan-is,

1:77 aț-țesfehmeḍ agdud-is ɣef leslak n Sidi Ṛebbi s leɛfu n ddnubat-nsen.

1:78 Axaṭer Ṛebbi-nneɣ d aḥnin, s Ṛṛeḥma-ines a d-icṛeq fell-aɣ tafat-is am tin n yiṭij seg yigenwan.

1:79  Tafat-is a d-tecṛeq ɣef wid yellan di ṭṭlam n lmut, ad aɣ-yawi deg webrid n lehna.

1:80 Aqcic-nni ițțimɣuṛ yețnerni di leɛqel. Iɛac deg unezṛuf armi d ass i deg i d-ibeggen iman-is i wat Isṛail.

2:1 Deg ussan-nni, yeffeɣ-ed yiwen n lqanun s ɣuṛ Qayṣeṛ ameqqran ; yefka lameṛ ad țțujerrden imezdaɣ meṛṛa yellan di tmura-ines.

2:2 Ajerred-agi amezwaru yedṛa asmi yella Kirinyus d lḥakem n tmurt n Surya.

2:3 Imdanen meṛṛa ṛuḥen ad țțujerden mkul yiwen di taddart n lejdud-is.

2:4 Yusef yellan seg izuṛan n Sidna Dawed, yuli ula d nețța si taddart n Naṣaret n tmurt n Jlili ɣer Bitelḥem, taddart n lejdud-is yellan di tmurt n Yahuda.

2:5 Iṛuḥ ad ițțujerred nețța ț-țxedibt-is Meryem yellan s tadist.

2:6 Mi ten-yuɣ lḥal dinna, yewweḍ-d lweqt i deg ara terbu ( tarew ) Meryem.

2:7 Tesɛa-d mmi-s amenzu, tețțel-it, tessegen-it di lmedwed, imi ur ufin ara amkan deg wexxam n yemsafren.

2:8 Di tmurt-nni, llan imeksawen ițnusun di lexlawi, țɛassan lmal-nsen deg yiḍ.

2:9 Ataya lmelk n Sidi Ṛebbi iḍheṛ-asen-d, tamanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi tecceɛceɛ tezzi-yasen-d, ikcem-iten lxuf d ameqqran.

2:10 Lmelk yenna-yasen : Ur țțagadet ! Aql-i wwiɣ-awen-d lexbaṛ lxiṛ ara isfeṛḥen wugdud meṛṛa.

2:11 Ass-agi di taddart n Sidna Dawed, ilul-awen-d yiwen wemsellek, d nețța i d Lmasiḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi !

2:12 Atan wamek ara t-tɛeqlem : aț-țafem llufan yețțel, yeṭṭes di lmedwed.

2:13 Imiren kan, ț-țirebbaɛ n lmalayekkat i d-yernan ɣer lmelk nni, cennunt țḥemmident Sidi Ṛebbi, qqaṛent :

2:14 Lḥemd i Sidi Ṛebbi deg imukan eɛlayen, lehna di ddunit ɣef yemdanen eɛzizen fell-as.

2:15 Akken uɣalent lmalayekkat-nni ɣer yigenni, imeksawen nnan wway gar-asen : Kkret a nṛuḥet ɣer Bitelḥem, a nẓer ayen i gedṛan, d wayen akka s wayes i ɣ-d-ixebbeṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:16 Imiren kan ṛuḥen, ufan dinna Meryem d Yusef akk-d llufan-nni yeṭṭes di lmedwed.

2:17 Mi walan aqcic-nni, ḥkan-d wayen i sen-d-yenna lmelk fell-as.

2:18 Wid akk yeslan s wayen i d-ḥkan imeksawen-nni, dehcen.

2:19 Meryem teḥrez imeslayen-nni deg wul-is, tețxemmim fell-asen.

2:20 Mi uɣalen imeksawen-nni, țḥemmiden țcekkiṛen Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef wayen slan d wayen ẓran, imi ufan kullec akken i sen-d-ițțuxebbeṛ.

2:21 Yewweḍ-ed wass wis tmanya i deg ara s-sḍehṛen i weqcic-nni ; semman-as Ɛisa, isem-nni i s-yefka lmelk uqbel ad yili di tɛebbuṭ n yemma-s.

2:22 Asmi wwḍen ṛebɛin wussan ɣef tlalit n weqcic-nni, Yusef d Meryem wwin-t ɣer temdint n Lquds iwakken ad ițțuqeddem i Sidi Ṛebbi,

2:23 akken yura di ccariɛa n Sidna Musa : yal aqcic amenzu, ilaq ad ițțuqeddem i Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:24 Yerna ṛuḥen iwakken ad fken d asfel snat n tmilliwin neɣ sin yetbiren imeẓyanen akken yella di ccariɛa n Sidna Musa.

2:25 Di temdint n Lquds yella yiwen wergaz isem-is Semɛun. Argaz-agi d aḥeqqi, iḍuɛ Sidi Ṛebbi, yețṛaǧu amsellek n wat Isṛail, Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yețțili yid-es.

2:26 Iweḥḥa-yas-ed belli ur ițmețțat ara alamma yeẓra Lmasiḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:27 Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi iwelleh Semɛun ad iṛuḥ ɣer lǧameɛ iqedsen.Atnaya kecmen-d Yusef d Meryem, wwin-d llufan-nni iwakken ad xedmen wayen i d-tenna ccariɛa n Musa fell-as.

2:28 Semɛun yeṭṭef aqcic-nni ger ifassen-is, iḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi yenna :

2:29 Tura a Sidi Ṛebbi ɣas ad mmteɣ aql-i di lehna imi amsellek i ɣ-tweɛdeḍ,

2:30 yewweḍ-ed yerna walaɣ-t s wallen-iw :

2:31 win akken i theggaḍ i leǧnas meṛṛa,

2:32 d nețța ara yessalin ccan n wat Isṛail agdud-ik, ara yilin ț-țafat ara d-icceɛceɛen ula ɣef leǧnas nniḍen.

2:33 Baba-s d yemma-s n weqcic-nni tɛeǧǧben ɣef wayen i d-qqaṛen fell-as.

2:34 Semɛun iburek-iten, yenna i Meryem, yemma-s n weqcic-nni : Atan weqcic-agi ad yili i kra seg wat Isṛail d sebba n tuccḍa, ma d wiyaḍ a ten-id-yerr ɣer webrid. ?ef ddemma-s ara yili lxilaf ger yemdanen ;

2:35 s wakka ara d-banen ixemmimen n diri deg wulawen, ma d kemm aț-țejreḥ tasa-m, am akken tețwet s ujenwi.

2:36 Tella daɣen yiwet n tmeṭṭut isem-is ?anna, meqqṛet di leɛmeṛ, ula d nețțat si lenbiya, d yelli-s n Fanwil n wedrum n Azir. Seg wasmi i tedda ț-țislit, tɛac d wergaz is sebɛa n yiseggasen

2:37 dɣa teǧǧel. Di leɛmeṛ-is ṛebɛa utmanyin n yiseggasen. Am yiḍ am ass ur teṭṭixiṛ si lǧameɛ iqedsen, tɛebbed Sidi Ṛebbi, tețțuẓum, tețẓalla.

2:38 Ula d nețțat tewweḍ-ed di teswiɛt-nni, teḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi, theddeṛ ɣef weqcic-nni i wid akk yețṛaǧun leslak n temdint n Lquds.

2:39 Mi fukken imawlan n Ɛisa ayen akk i d-tenna ccariɛa n Sidi Ṛebbi, uɣalen ɣer taddart-nsen Naṣaret yellan di tmurt n Jlili.

2:40 Aqcic-nni yețțimɣuṛ yețnerni di tmusni, lbaṛaka n Sidi Ṛebbi tella fell-as.

2:41 Imawlan n Sidna Ɛisa țṛuḥun mkul aseggas ɣer temdint n Lquds iwakken ad sɛeddin Tafaska n izimer n leslak.

2:42 Asmi yesɛa Ɛisa tnac yiseggasen, yedda akk-d imawlan-is ɣer temdint n Lquds, akken tella di lɛadda n lɛid.

2:43 Mi tɛedda Tafaska, lɣaci țțuɣalen ɣer yexxamen-nsen, ma d Ɛisa yeqqim di temdint Lquds, imawlan-is ur d-wwin ara s lexbaṛ,

2:44 ɣilen yedda-d ț-țerbaɛt nniḍen. Lḥan ass kamel dɣa bdan țqelliben fell-as. Steqsan wid akk i sen-yețțilin d wid meṛṛa ssnen,

2:45 lameɛna ur t-ufin ara. Uɣalen ɣer temdint n Lquds iwakken ad qellben fell-as.

2:46 Ass wis tlata, ufan-t di lǧameɛ iqedsen yeqqim ger lecyux, ismeḥsis asen, isteqsay-iten.

2:47 Wid akk i s isellen wehmen di lefhama-s d wayen i d-ițarra i yesteqsiyen-nsen.

2:48 Mi t-walan imawlan-is, qqimen wehmen, dɣa tenna-yas yemma-s : A mmi, acuɣeṛ i ɣ-txedmeḍ akka ? Nekk d baba-k neṛwa aḥebbeṛ, acḥal i nqelleb fell-ak !

2:49 Nețța yenna-yasen : Acuɣeṛ i tețqellibem fell-i ? Eɛni ur teẓrim ara ilaq-iyi ad xedmeɣ lecɣal n baba ?

2:50 Lameɛna nutni ur fhimen ara ayen i sen-d-yenna.

2:51 Ɛisa yedda d imawlan-is ɣer temdint n Naṣaret, yețțaɣ-asen awal. Yemma-s teḥrez annect-agi meṛṛa deg wul-is.

2:52 Ɛisa ițțimɣuṛ, yetɛeqqil ; eɛziz ɣer Ṛebbi, eɛziz ɣer yemdanen.

3:1 Deg wuseggas wis xemseṭṭac n tgeldit n Qayṣer Tibeṛyus, llan ṛebɛa lḥekkam di tmurt n Falisṭin. Bunṭus Bilaṭus d lḥakem n tmurt n Yahuda, Hiṛudus Antifas yeḥkem ɣef tmurt n Jlili, Filbas gma-s n Hiṛudus, ɣef tmura n Iturya akk-d Tṛaxunit, ma d Lisanyas yeḥkem ɣef tmurt n Abilan.

3:2 Di lweqt-nni, ?ennan akk-d Qayif llan d lmuqedmin imeqqranen. Awal n Sidi Ṛebbi ițwaxebbeṛ-as-ed i Yeḥya, mmi-s n Zakarya, deg unezṛuf.

4:1 Mi d- yuɣal Sidna Ɛisa seg wasif n Urdun, Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yewwi-t ɣer unezṛuf.

4:2 Ṛebɛin wussan ur yečči, Cciṭan yețjeṛṛib deg-s. Mi ɛeddan wussan-nni, yuɣal illuẓ.

4:3 Cciṭan yenna-yas : Ma d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi i telliḍ, ini-yas i wedɣaɣ-agi a d-yuɣal d aɣṛum.

4:4 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Yura di tektabt iqedsen : amdan ur yețɛic ara s weɣṛum kan meɛna s mkul awal i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:5 Yuɣal Cciṭan yewwi-t ɣer yiwen wemkan eɛlayen, deg yiwet n teswiɛt yesken-as-ed akk tigeldiwin n ddunit,

4:6 yenna-yas : Ad a k-d-fkeɣ lḥekma d ccan n ddewlat-agi meṛṛa, axaṭer țțunefkent iyi-d. Zemreɣ a tent-fkeɣ i win i yi-ihwan ;

4:7 ma tseǧǧdeḍ zdat-i ad uɣalent akk d ayla-k.

4:8 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Yura : Anagar Sidi Ṛebbi Illu-inek ara tɛebdeḍ, i nețța kan iwumi ara tseǧǧdeḍ.

4:9 Cciṭan yewwi-t daɣen ɣer temdint n Lquds, yessers-it ɣef yixef n lǧameɛ iqedsen, yenna-yas : Ma d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi i telliḍ, ḍeggeṛ iman-ik d akessar,

4:10 axaṭer yura : Ad yefk lameṛ i lmalayekkat-is iwakken ad ḥarbent fell-ak,

4:11 yerna a k-awint ger ifassen-nsent, ad ḥadrent iḍaṛṛen-ik ɣef wedɣaɣ.

4:12 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Yura daɣen : Ur țjeṛṛib ara Sidi Ṛebbi, Illu-inek.

4:13 Mi i t-ijeṛṛeb s waṭas n tḥila ur s-yufi ara abrid, Iblis yeṭṭaxeṛ fell-as alamma ț-țikkelt nniḍen.

4:14 Sidna Ɛisa yuɣal ɣer tmurt n Jlili yeččuṛ ț-țazmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, slan yis di mkul tama n tmurt-agi.

4:15 Ikeččem ɣer leǧwameɛ n wat Isṛail yesselmad, imdanen meṛṛa țcekkiṛen-t.

4:16 Iṛuḥ ɣer taddart n Naṣaret anda yețțuṛebba ; akken innum, ikcem ɣer lǧameɛ deg wass n westeɛfu.

4:17 Mi gekker a d-iɣeṛ, fkan-as-d taktabt n nnbi Iceɛya, yeldi-ț, yufa amkan anda yura :

4:18  Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi yers-ed fell-i, axaṭer yextaṛ-iyi iwakken ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i yimeɣban, ad beccṛeɣ i yimeḥbas tilelli, iwakken a d-rreɣ iẓri i yiderɣalen , a d kkseɣ lḥif ɣef wid ițwaḍelmen,

4:19  ad beccṛeɣ ɣef wuseggas i deg ara d-issers Sidi Ṛebbi ṛṛeḥma ines .

4:20 Ijmeɛ taktabt-nni, yerra-ț i uqeddac, imiren yeqqim. Wid akk yellan di lǧameɛ ččan-t s wallen.

4:21 Dɣa yenna-yasen : Ayen i wen-d-ɣṛiɣ akka, atan yedṛa-d gar-awen ass-agi !

4:22 Wehmen atas, tɛeǧǧben deg yimeslayen i d-ițeffɣen seg yimi-s, qqaṛen : Amek ? Mačči d mmi-s n Yusef wagi ?

4:23 Dɣa yenna-yasen : ?riɣ belli a yi-d-tinim lemtel-agi : « A ṭṭbib sseḥlu iman-ik,» neɣ « xdem dagi di tmurt-ik ayen i nesla txedmeḍ-t di temdint n Kafernaḥum.»

4:24 Yenna-yasen daɣen : S tideț a wen-iniɣ : ulac nnbi yețwaqeblen di tmurt-is.

4:25 A wen-iniɣ tideț : Di zzman n nnbi Ilyas, tlata yiseggasen d wezgen ur d-tewwit lehwa dɣa yeɣli-d laẓ d ameqqran ɣef tmurt meṛṛa. Deg wussan-nni, aṭas n tuǧǧal i gellan di tmurt n wat Isṛail.

4:26 ?as akken, Sidi Ṛebbi ur iceggeɛ ara nnbi Ilyas ɣer yiwet deg-sent, anagar ɣ er yiwet n taǧǧalt yellan di taddart n ?urfat di tmurt n Sidun .

4:27 Llan daɣen aṭas n wid ihelken lbeṛs di tmurt n wat Isṛail di zzman n nnbi Ilyaceɛ , meɛna ula d yiwen deg-sen ur iḥli anagar Neɛman n tmurt n Surya.

4:28 Mi slan imeslayen-agi, lɣaci meṛṛa yellan di lǧameɛ yenɣa-ten lɣecc.

4:29 Kkren ɣuṛ-es, ssufɣen-t si taddart, ssawḍen-t armi d ixef n wedrar i ɣef tebna taddart-nsen iwakken a t-ssegrirben d akessar ;

4:30 lameɛna nețța iɛedda gar-asen, iṛuḥ.

4:31 Sidna Ɛisa iṣubb ɣer tamdint n Kafernaḥum, illan n at Jlili, anda yesselmad lɣaci deg wass n westeɛfu.

4:32 Imdanen wehmen aṭas deg wuselmed-ines axaṭer imeslayen-is sɛan tissas.

4:33 Di lǧameɛ, yella yiwen wergaz izdeɣ-it uṛuḥani, iɛeggeḍ s lǧehd n taɣect-is :

4:34 D acu i tebɣiḍ ɣuṛ-nneɣ a Ɛisa Anaṣari ? Tusiḍ-ed iwakken ad aɣ tesnegreḍ ? ?riɣ d acu-k , kečč d Imqeddes n Ṛebbi !

4:35 Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq ɣuṛ-es yenna-yas : Ssusem ! Effeɣ seg wergaz-agi ! Aṛuḥani iḍeggeṛ argaz-nni ɣer lqaɛa di tlemmast-nsen, dɣa iffeɣ seg-s mbla ma iḍuṛṛ-it.

4:36 Lɣaci ikcem-iten akk lxuf, qqaṛen wway gar-asen : D acu-ten yimeslayen-agi ? Yesɛa tazmert s wayes iḥekkem ula ɣef leǧnun ! Akken kan ara sen-yefk lameṛ ad ffɣen !

4:37 Di yal tama n tmurt slan s lexbaṛ n Sidna Ɛisa.

4:38 Iffeɣ-ed si lǧameɛ, yerra ɣer wexxam n Semɛun. Yuɣ lḥal taḍeggalt n Semɛun tuɣ-iț tawla ț-țameqqrant, ḥellelen Sidna Ɛisa iwakken a ț-yesseḥlu.

4:39 Yekna ɣuṛ-es, yumeṛ i tawla aț-țeffeɣ seg-s, dɣa teffeɣ-iț ; imiren kan tekker teqdec-asen-d.

4:40 Tameddit mi geɣli yiṭij, kra n win yesɛan amuḍin n mkul aṭan, yewwi-yas-t-id. Mkul win i ɣef yessers afus-is yeḥla ! Yesseḥla-ten meṛṛa !

4:41 Llan daɣen leǧnun i d-ițeffɣen seg waṭas n yemdanen, țɛeggiḍen qqaṛen : « Kečč d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ! » Lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa ițɛeggiḍ fell- asen, ur ten-ițțaga ara a d-hedṛen, axaṭer nutni ẓran d nețța i d Lmasiḥ.

4:42 Mi guli wass, iffeɣ si temdint, yerra ɣer yiwen wemkan yexlan. Aṭas n lɣaci ṛuḥen țqelliben fell-as. Mi t-ufan, ḥellelen-t iwakken ad yeqqim ɣuṛ-sen, ur ițṛuḥu ara.

4:43 Lameɛna nețța yenna-yasen : Ilaq-iyi ad ṛuḥeɣ ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n tgeldit n Ṛebbi i temdinin nniḍen ! ?ef wayagi i d țțuceggɛeɣ !

5:1 Yiwen wass, Sidna Ɛisa yella ɣef rrif n lebḥeṛ n Jiniṣaret. Lɣaci meṛṛa zzin-as-d, mčuččaɛen-d akken ad slen i wawal n Ṛebbi.

5:2 Iwala snat n teflukin rrif n lebḥeṛ, iḥewwaten rsen-d iwakken ad ssirden icebbaken nsen.

5:3 Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer yiwet si teflukin-nni, tin yellan n Semɛun, yenna-yas ad ibɛed acemma ɣef rrif. Yeqqim , yebda yesselmad-ed lɣaci syenna.

5:4 Mi gfukk aselmed yenna i Semɛun : Sbeɛdet taflukt ɣer wemkan lqayen, tḍeggṛem icebbaken-nwen aț-țṣeggdem.

5:5 Semɛun yenna-yas : A Sidi, iḍ kamel nukni d aṣeggeḍ ur d-neṭṭif acemma ; lameɛna ɣef wawal-ik a nexdem akken i d-tenniḍ, a nḍeggeṛ icebbaken.

5:11 Imiren rran-d tiflukin-nsen ɣer rrif, ǧǧan kullec dinna, ddan d Sidna Ɛisa.

5:15 Simmal ițwassen Sidna Ɛisa, simmal țnejmaɛen-d ɣuṛ-es lɣaci iwakken ad slen i wawal-is yerna a ten-isseḥlu si lehlakat-nsen ;

5:19 Imi aṭas n lɣaci i gellan, ur ufin ara ansi ara t-sɛeddin ; ulin ɣef ssqef, kksen kra n iqermuden, ṣubben-t-id s wusu-ines ɣer tlemmast n lɣaci, zdat Sidna Ɛisa.

5:20 Mi gwala liman-nsen, yenna i umuḍin-nni : Ay argaz, țwaɛeffan-ak ddnubat ik !

5:21 Lɛulama akk-d ifariziyen bdan țxemmimen qqaṛen : Amek yezmer a d-yini imeslayen agi ? Wagi d lekfeṛ ! Anwa i gzemren ad iɛfu ddnubat anagar Sidi Ṛebbi ?

5:22 Sidna Ɛisa yeẓran ayen i țxemmimen, yenna-yasen : Acuɣeṛ tețxemmimem akka deg ulawen-nwen ?

5:23 D acu i gsehlen a s-t-iniɣ ? « ?waɛeffan-ak ddnubat-ik» neɣ « ekker telḥuḍ ? »

5:24 Ilaq-awen aț-țeẓrem belli Mmi-s n bunadem yesɛa tazmert ad yeɛfu ddnubat di ddunit-agi ! Yezzi ɣer wukrif-nni, yenna-yas : Kker ddem usu-inek tuɣaleḍ ɣer wexxam-ik !

5:25 Imiren kan ukrif-nni yekker zdat-sen, yeddem usu i deg yeḍleq, yuɣal ɣer wexxam-is, ițḥemmid Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:26 Wehmen meṛṛa lɣaci, țḥemmiden Sidi Ṛebbi, ikcem-iten lxuf qqaṛen : Ass-agi neẓra ayen werǧin neẓri !

5:27 Mi geffeɣ Sidna Ɛisa, iwala yiwen umekkas isem-is Lewwi yeqqim di texxamt anda țxelliṣen tabzert ( leɣṛama ), iqeṛṛeb ɣuṛ-es yenna-yas : Ddu yid-i.

5:29 Lewwi yeɛṛeḍ Sidna Ɛisa ɣer wexxam-is, ihegga-yas imensi. Aṭas n yimekkasen d yemdanen nniḍen i geqqimen yid-sen ad ččen.

5:30 Ifariziyen d lɛulama n ukabar-nsen iɣaḍ-iten lḥal, nnan i yinelmaden is : Acuɣeṛ i tețțem, tessem akk-d imekkasen imakaren d imednuben ?

5:31 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yasen : Mačči d wid iṣeḥḥan i geḥwaǧen ṭṭbib, i t-iḥwaǧen d imuḍan.

5:32 Ur d-usiɣ ara ad ssiwleɣ i iḥeqqiyen, usiɣ-ed ɣer yimednuben iwakken a d-uɣalen ɣer webrid.

5:33 Nnan-as daɣen : Inelmaden n Yeḥya d wid n ifariziyen țțuẓummen acḥal d abrid yerna țẓallan, ma d inelmaden-ik tețțen tessen.

5:34 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : Eɛni tzemrem a sen-tinim i yinebgawen n yesli ad uẓummen ass n tmeɣṛa ? Ur tezmirem ara !

8:7 Amur nniḍen yeɣli daɣen ger isennanen , mi d-temɣi zzerriɛa mɣin-d yid-es isennanen, ɣummen-ț.

8:8 Amur nniḍen daɣen yeɣli deg wakal lɛali ; mi d-mɣin, mkul aɛeqqa yefka-d meyya. Mi d-yenna ayagi, iɛeggeḍ yenna : Win yesɛan imeẓẓuɣen isel-ed !

8:9 Inelmaden-is ssutren-as d acu i d lmeɛna n lemtel-agi.

8:10 Yerra-yasen : Kunwi yețțunefk-awen-d aț țissinem lbaḍna n tgeldit n Ṛebbi, ma d wiyaḍ ițțunefk-asen-d a ț-issinen s lemtul kan iwakken ɣas ad ẓren s wallen-nsen ur țwalin ara, ɣas ma slan ur fehhmen ara.

8:11 Atah lmeɛna n lemtel-agi : zzerriɛa d awal n Ṛebbi.

8:12 Llan kra n yemdanen țemcabin ɣer webrid-nni anda teɣli zzerriɛa, m'ara slen i wawal, Cciṭan a d-yas a t-iqleɛ seg ulawen-nsen iwakken ur țțamnen ur țwasellaken.

8:13 Wiyaḍ țemcabin ɣer wakal yeččuṛen d idɣaɣen. M'ara slen i wawal a t-qeblen s lfeṛḥ lameɛna ur t-țaǧǧan ara ad iger izuṛan ; imiren kan ad amnen, m'ara d-yas lweqt n ujeṛṛeb țțuɣalen ɣer deffir.

8:14 Ayen yeɣlin ger isennanen, d wid isellen i wawal meɛna m'ara d-imɣi ur igemmu ara deg-sen axaṭer țțaǧan anezgum d rrbeḥ akk-d zzhu n ddunit ad iɣumm awal-nni.

8:15 Ma d ayen yețwazerɛen deg wakal lɛali, d wid isellen i wawal s wul yeṣfan d neyya ḥerzen-t yerna țțaǧan-d lxiṛ s ṣṣbeṛ-nsen.

8:16 Yiwen ur iceɛɛel taftilt iwakken a ț-iɣum s lḥila neɣ a ț-issedreg; lameɛna a ț-issers ɣef lmeṣbeḥ iwakken kra n win ara d-ikecmen ad iwali tafat.

8:17 Kra n wayen yeffren a d-iban, kra n wayen yellan di ṭṭlam a t-id-tesban tafat.

8:18 Ɛasset iman-nwen ihi ɣef wayen i tsellem, axaṭer win i gḥerzen ayen yesɛa a s-nernu, ma d win ur nḥerrez ara a s-ițwakkes ula d ayen i gɣil yesɛa-t.

8:19 Yemma-s d watmaten n Sidna Ɛisa usan-d a t-ẓren, meɛna ur zmiren ara ad qeṛṛben ɣuṛ-es imi aṭas n lɣaci i gellan dinna.

8:20 Ssawḍen-as lexbaṛ nnan-as : A Sidi, yemma-k d watmaten-ik atnan di beṛṛa, bɣan a k-ẓren.

8:21 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yasen : Yemma d watmaten-iw d widak ismeḥsisen i wawal n Ṛebbi yerna ttabaɛen-t.

8:22 Yiwen wass, Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer yiwet n teflukt nețța d yinelmaden-is. Yenna-yasen : Eyyaw a nzegret ɣer tama nniḍen n lebḥeṛ n Jlili. Dɣa ṛuḥen.

8:23 Mi teddun ɣef waman, Sidna Ɛisa yeṭṭes. Yekker-ed waḍu iǧehden di lebḥeṛ, taflukt tebda tețțačaṛ d aman, ikcem-iten lxuf ameqqran.

8:24 Inelmaden qeṛṛben ɣuṛ-es, sekkren-t-id nnan-as : A Sidi, a Sidi, atan a neɣṛeq. Yekker-ed, dɣa yumeṛ i waḍu d lemwaji ad ḥebsen, imiren kan ters-ed talwit.

8:25 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Anida-t liman-nwen ? Tɛeǧǧben, țergigin, qqaṛen wway gar-asen : Anwa-t wagi ? Izmer ad yefk lameṛ i waḍu d lebḥeṛ yerna țțaɣen as awal !

8:26 Wwḍen ɣer tama nniḍen n lebḥeṛ, ɣer tmurt n ijiṛaziyen iqublen tamurt n Jlili.

8:27 Mi d-yers Sidna Ɛisa, ataya yusa-d ɣuṛ-es yiwen wergaz n taddart nni, melken-t waṭas n leǧnun ; acḥal ur yelsi llebsa, ur yezdiɣ axxam, yețɛici di tmeqbeṛt ger iẓekwan.

8:28 Mi gwala Sidna Ɛisa, yeɣli ɣer iḍaṛṛen-is iɛeggeḍ yenna : D acu i tebɣiḍ ɣuṛ-i a Ɛisa Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ɛlayen ? Di leɛnaya-k ur iyi-țɛețțib ara.

8:29 Yenna-yas-d akka axaṭer Sidna Ɛisa yefka lameṛ i uṛuḥani i t-izedɣen ad iffeɣ seg-s. Acḥal d abrid i s-cudden ifassen d iḍaṛṛen s ssnasel, nețța isseɣṛas-itent yerna aṛuḥani-nni yezzuɣuṛ-it ɣer yimukan yexlan.

8:30 Sidna Ɛisa isteqsa-t yenna-yas : Isem-ik ? yerra-yas : Tarbaɛt. Yenna-yas-d akka axaṭer aṭas n leǧnun i t-izedɣen.

8:31 ?ḥellilen Sidna Ɛisa ur ten-ițceggiɛ ara ɣer yimukan n ṭṭlam.

8:32 Tella dinna yiwet n tqeḍɛit tameqqrant n yilfan kessen deg yiwet n tiɣilt. Leǧnun-nni țḥellilen Sidna Ɛisa a ten-yeǧǧ ad kecmen deg yilfan-nni ; Sidna Ɛisa iserreḥ-asen.

8:33 Imiren ffɣen seg wergaz-nni, kecmen deg yilfan, grarben seg ixef n tiɣilt ɣer lebḥeṛ, ɣeṛqen.

8:34 Imeksawen-nsen mi walan ayen yedṛan uzzlen ɣer taddart d lexlawi ad ssiwḍen lexbaṛ.

8:35 Usan-d lɣaci ɣer Sidna Ɛisa, ufan-d argaz-nni i seg d-ffɣen leǧnun yeqqim ɣer iḍaṛṛen-is, yuɣal-ed ɣer leɛqel-is, yelsa llebsa. Wehmen akk, tekcem-iten tugdi.

8:36 Wid iḥedṛen i wayen yedṛan, ḥkan amek yețwasellek umeǧnun-nni.

8:37 Imezdaɣ n tmurt uggaden, dɣa ssutren i Sidna Ɛisa ad ibɛed fell-asen. Sidna Ɛisa yuli ɣer teflukt iwakken ad iṛuḥ.

8:38 Argaz-nni i seg d-ffɣen leǧnun, issuter a t-yeǧǧ ad yeddu yid-es, meɛna Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas :

8:39 Uɣal ɣer wexxam-ik, teḥkuḍ ayen akk i k-ixdem Ṛebbi. Argaz-nni iṛuḥ, ikcem ɣer taddart, yeḥka meṛṛa wayen i s-ixdem Sidna Ɛisa.

8:40 Mi d-yuɣal Sidna Ɛisa, lɣaci meṛṛa i t-ițṛaǧun mmugren-t-id.

8:41 Yewweḍ-ed yiwen wergaz isem-is Jayrus, d ameqqran n lǧameɛ, yeɣli ɣer iḍaṛṛen n Sidna Ɛisa, yețḥellil deg-s iwakken ad iddu ɣer wexxam is

8:42 axaṭer yesɛa yelli-s tnac yiseggasen di leɛmeṛ-is, tețmețțat yerna anagar nețțat i gesɛa. Akken ițeddu Sidna Ɛisa ɣer dinna, ḥeṛsen-t-id lɣaci si mkul lǧiha.

8:43 Tella dinna yiwet n tmeṭṭut ihelken, tnac iseggasen nețțat tessaq d idammen. Texṣeṛ ayen akk i tesɛa ɣer ṭṭebba, yiwen ur ț-isseḥla.

8:44 Tekka-d deffir Sidna Ɛisa, teḍleq afus-is, tennul iri n ubeṛnus-is, imiren kan teḥla ḥebsen idammen-nni.

8:45 Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq yenna : Anwa akka i yi-d-innulen ? Imi ssusmen meṛṛa, Buṭrus yenna yas : A Sidi, lɣaci yezzi-yak-d yerna iḥṛes-ik-id si mkul tama, kečč teqqaṛeḍ anwa i k-id-innulen !

8:46 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Yella yiwen i yi-d-innulen axaṭer ḥusseɣ i kra n tezmert teffeɣ seg-i.

8:47 Tameṭṭut-nni imi twala ulamek ara teffer, tusa-d tețțergigi teɣli ɣer yiḍaṛṛen n Sidna Ɛisa, teḥka-d zdat lɣaci meṛṛa acuɣeṛ i t-tennul d wamek teḥla imiren kan si lehlak-is.

8:48 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : A yell-i, imi tumneḍ yis-i teḥliḍ ! Ṛuḥ di talwit.

8:49 Mazal-it iheddeṛ, ataya yiwen wergaz yusa-d seg wexxam n Jayṛus, yewwi-yas-d lexbaṛ-agi : Yelli-k temmut ! Ur țɛețțib ara Sidna Ɛisa.

8:50 Sidna Ɛisa mi s-yesla i wergaz-nni, yenna i Jayṛus : Ur țțagad ara ! Amen kan, yelli-k aț-țeslek.

8:51 Mi gewweḍ ɣer wexxam, ur yeǧǧi yiwen ad ikcem yid-es anagar Buṭrus, Yuḥenna, Yeɛqub akk-d baba-s d yemma-s n teqcict-nni.

8:52 Wid yellan dinna țrun akk, țɣerriden ( țmeǧǧiden ) fell-as. Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Beṛka-kkun imeṭṭawen ! taqcict ur temmut ara ! D iḍes i teṭṭes.

8:53 Nutni ugin a s-d-ḥessen imi ẓran taqcict temmut.

8:54 Sidna Ɛisa yeṭṭef-iț-id seg wufus yenna-yas s taɣect ɛlayen : A taqcict, ekker !

8:55 Imiren kan yuɣal-iț-id ṛṛuḥ, tekker-ed. Sidna Ɛisa yumeṛ a s-fken ayen ara tečč.

8:56 Imawlan-is wehmen. Sidna Ɛisa iweṣṣa-ten ur țɛawaden i yiwen ayen yedṛan.

9:1 Sidna Ɛisa isnejmaɛ-ed tnac yinelmaden-is, yefka-yasen tazmert d lḥekma s wacu ara ssufɣen leǧnun, ara sseḥlun imuḍan.

9:2 Iceggeɛ-iten ad beccṛen tageldit n Ṛebbi, yerna ad sseḥlun imuḍan.

9:3 Yenna-yasen : Ur țțawit acemma yid-wen d aɛwin, ur țțawit aɛekkaz neɣ agrab (aqwrab), ur țțawit aɣṛum neɣ idrimen, ur țțawit ara daɣen sin yiqendyaṛ.

9:4 Kra n win ara yestṛeḥben yis wen, qqimet deg wexxam-is alamma tṛuḥem.

9:5 Ma d anda ur stṛeḥben ara yis-wen, m'ara teffɣem si taddart-nni, zwit ula d aɣebbaṛ-is seg yiḍaṛṛen nwen, iwakken aț-țcehdem fell-asen belli d nutni i kkun-yugin.

9:6 Ṛuḥen, țeddun si taddart ɣer tayeḍ, țbecciṛen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ, sseḥlayen imuḍan di mkul amkan.

9:7 Hiṛudus lḥakem n tmurt n Jlili, yeɛweq mi gesla s wayen yedṛan meṛṛa, axaṭer kra n yemdanen qqaṛen : « d Yeḥya i d-iḥyan si ger lmegtin ! »

9:8 Wiyaḍ qqaṛen : « d Sidna Ilyas i d-yuɣalen ! » Neɣ : « ahat d yiwen si lenbiya n zik i d-iḥyan ! »

9:9 Lameɛna Hiṛudus yeqqaṛ : « Yeḥya kkseɣ-as aqeṛṛuy ! Anwa-t ihi wagi i ɣef selleɣ annect-agi ? » Dɣa ițqellib ad iẓer Sidna Ɛisa.

9:10 Mi d-uɣalen ṛṛusul n Sidna Ɛisa, bdan ḥekkun-as ayen akk i xedmen. Yewwi-ten yid-es ɣer yiwen wemkan, ɣer tama n taddart n Bitsayda akken ad qqimen weḥḥed-sen.

9:11 Mi slan anda yella lɣaci tebɛen-t. Nețța yesteṛḥeb yis-sen, yebda iheddeṛ-asen ɣef tgeldit n Ṛebbi, yesseḥla daɣen wid ihelken.

9:12 Akken qṛib ad yeɣli yiṭij, tnac-nni n ṛṛusul usan-d ɣer Sidna Ɛisa nnan-as : Serreḥ i lɣaci ad ṛuḥen ɣer temdinin ț-țudrin iqeṛben iwakken ad afen ayen ara ččen d wanda ara ṭsen, axaṭer aql-aɣ deg wemkan yexlan.

9:13 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Fket-asen kunwi s yiman-nwen ayen ara ččen. Erran-as : ?uṛ-nneɣ anagar xemsa teḥbulin n weɣṛum d sin yiselman. Neɣ tebɣiḍ a nṛuḥ nukni a d-naɣ ayen ara ččen lɣaci-agi ?

9:14 Llan dinna azal n xemsa alaf n yergazen. Sidna Ɛisa yenna i yinelmaden-is : Feṛqet lɣaci ț-țirebbaɛ n xemsin xemsin, tesɣimem-ten.

9:15 Ṛuḥen sɣimen-ten akken i sen-d yenna.

9:16 Sidna Ɛisa yerfed allen-is ɣer yigenni, yeḥmed Ṛebbi icekkeṛ-it, yeddem-ed xemsa-nni teḥbulin n weɣṛum d sin-nni iselman yebḍa-ten, yefka-ten i inelmaden-is a ten- feṛqen i lɣaci.

9:17 ?čan akk armi ṛwan, yerna ččuṛen tnac yiḍellaɛen s wayen i d-yugran.

9:18 Yiwen wass Sidna Ɛisa yeqqim weḥd-es yețẓalla. Inelmaden-is qeṛṛben ɣuṛ-es dɣa isteqsa-ten yenna-yasen : D acu-yi ɣer lɣaci ?

9:19 Rran-as : Kra qqaṛen d Yeḥya aɣeṭṭas ; wiyaḍ d Sidna Ilyas i d-yuɣalen ; ma d kra nniḍen qqaṛen d yiwen si lenbiya n zik i d-iḥyan si ger lmegtin.

9:20 Yenna-yasen : I kunwi, d acu-yi ɣuṛ-wen ? Yenṭeq Buṭrus yenna-yas : D kečč i d Lmasiḥ n Ṛebbi.

9:21 Sidna Ɛisa iweṣṣa-ten ur qqaṛen i yiwen belli d nețța i d Lmasiḥ.

9:22 Yenna-yasen daɣen : Ilaq Mmi-s n bunadem ad yeɛteb aṭas. A t-nekkṛen lecyux d lmuqedmin imeqqranen akk-d lɛulama n ccariɛa yerna a t-nɣen, lameɛna ass wis tlata a d-iḥyu si ger lmegtin.

9:23 Yenna daɣen i lɣaci : Win yebɣan ad iddu yid-i ur ilaq ara ad ixemmem ɣef tudert-is ; ilaq ad iqbel ad yenɛețțab yal ass ɣef ddemma n yisem-iw yerna ad yetbeɛ abrid-iw.

9:24 Axaṭer win yebɣan ad isellek tudert-is a s-tṛuḥ. Ma d win iwumi ara tṛuḥ ɣef ddemma-w ad yuɣal a ț-yaf.

9:25 D acu i d lfayda n wemdan ara irebḥen ddunit meṛṛa ma yesṛuḥ tudert-is neɣ issenger iman-is ?

9:26 Atan win issetḥan yis-i neɣ s yimeslayen iw, ad issetḥi yis Mmi-s n bunadem m'ara d-yas di tmanegt-is ț-țmanegt n Baba Ṛebbi akk-d lmalayekkat iqedsen.

9:27 A wen-d-iniɣ tideț, llan gar-awen wid ur nețmețțat ara alamma walan Tageldit n Ṛebbi.

9:28 Ɛeddan azal n tmanya wussan segmi i d-yenna imeslayen-agi. Sidna Ɛisa yewwi yid-es Buṭrus, Yuḥenna d Yeɛqub, yuli ɣer wedrar ad iẓẓall.

9:29 Mi gella ideɛɛu yenbeddal wudem-is, llebsa-s tuɣal ț-țamellalt tețfeǧǧiǧ am yiṭij.

9:30 Taswiɛt kan bedden-d ɣuṛ-es sin yergazen heddṛen yid-es : d Sidna Musa akk-d Sidna Ilyas.

9:31 Banen-ed di tafat tameqqrant, țmeslayen yid-es ɣef lmut-is ara yedṛun di temdint n Lquds.

9:32 Buṭrus d yimdukkal-is yewwi ten yiḍes. Mi d-endekwalen, walan tamanegt n Sidna Ɛisa akk-d wid yellan yid-es.

9:33 Mi kkren ad ṛuḥen sin-nni yergazen, yenṭeq Buṭrus ɣer Sidna Ɛisa yenna : A Sidi, acḥal yelha m'akka nella dagi ! Ma tebɣiḍ a nesbedd tlata yiqiḍunen, yiwen i kečč, yiwen i Sidna Musa wayeḍ i Sidna Ilyas. Yenna-d akka, axaṭer ur yeẓri d acu i d-iqqaṛ !

9:34 Mazal-it iheddeṛ mi ten-id-tɣumm yiwet n tagut. Buṭrus d imdukkal-is ikcem-iten lxuf d ameqqran.

9:35 Iffeɣ-ed yiwen n ṣṣut si tagut-nni yenna-d : Wagi d Mmi, d nețța i xtaṛeɣ, smeḥsiset-as.

9:36 Mi geḥbes ṣṣut-nni, walan anagar Sidna Ɛisa. Deg ussan-nni, inelmaden ur ḥkin i yiwen ayen ẓran.

9:37 Azekka-nni mi d-ṣubben seg wedrar, aṭas n lɣaci i d-immugren Sidna Ɛisa.

9:38 Atah yiwen wergaz iɛeggeḍ-ed si ger lɣaci yenna : A Sidi, di leɛnaya-k muqel-ed ɣer mmi, ala nețța i sɛiɣ !

9:39 Ikcem-it uṛuḥani, tiswiɛin m'ara t-ițhuccu, yețɛeggiḍ alamma ffɣent-ed tkufta seg imi-s, ur as-ițserriḥ ara alamma yerẓa-t akk.

9:40 ?elleleɣ inelmaden-ik a t-ssufɣen seg-s, lameɛna ur zmiren ara.

9:41 Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq yenna : A lǧil ijehlen iɛewjen, ar melmi ara yiliɣ yid-wen, ara wen-sebṛeɣ ? Awi-d mmi-k ɣer dagi !

9:42 Akken i d-iqeṛṛeb weqcic-nni, aṛuḥani isseglallez-it di lqaɛa armi i d-țeffɣent tkufta seg yimi-s. Sidna Ɛisa yefka lameṛ i uṛuḥani-nni iwakken a t-iffeɣ. Aqcic-nni yeḥla, Sidna Ɛisa yerra-t i baba-s.

9:43 Lɣaci meṛṛa wehmen di tezmert n Sidi Ṛebbi. Imi tɛeǧǧben akk s wayen i gxeddem, Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq ɣer yinelmaden-is yenna :

9:44 ?esset-ed mliḥ i wayen ara wen-d-iniɣ tura : « Mmi-s n bunadem ad ițțusellem ger ifassen n yemdanen. »

9:45 Inelmaden-is ur fhimen ara imeslayen-agi, lmeɛna-ines tețwaffer fell-asen iwakken ur t-fehmen ara yerna kukran a t-steqsin.

9:46 Inelmaden țmeslayen wway gar-asen, țmesteqsayen anwa i d ameqqran deg-sen.

9:47 Sidna Ɛisa yeẓran ayen țxemmimen deg ulawen-nsen, ikker yeddem-ed yiwen weqcic isbedd-it zdat-es,

9:48 yenna-yasen : Win iqeblen aqcic am wagi ɣef ddemma n yisem-iw d nekk i geqbel, win i yi-qeblen, yeqbel win i yi-d iceggɛen. Win yessimẓiyen iman-is gar-awen d nețța i d ameqqran.

9:49 Yuḥenna yenna i Sidna Ɛisa : A Sidi, neẓra yiwen wergaz yessuffuɣ leǧnun s yisem-ik, nebɣa a s-neqḍeɛ imi ur k-itbiɛ ara am nukni.

9:50 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ur s-țțagit ara ! Win ur wen-d neffiɣ ara d aɛdaw, yid-wen i gella !

9:51 Mi qṛib a d-awḍen wussan i deg ara yețwarfed ɣer igenwan, Sidna Ɛisa yeqsed ad iṛuḥ ɣer temdint n Lquds.

9:52 Yessezwer imceggɛen zdat-es iwakken a s-heggin ayen ilaqen. Kecmen ɣer yiwet n taddart n yisamariyen,

9:53 lameɛna ugin ad stṛeḥben yis imi slan ɣer temdint n Lquds i gteddu.

9:54 Yeɛqub d Yuḥenna mi ẓran ayagi, nnan : A Sidi, tebɣiḍ a nefk lameṛ i tmes n yigenni a d-teɣli fell-asen a ten tessenger ?

9:55 Sidna Ɛisa yezzi ɣuṛ-sen, iɛeggeḍ fell-asen yenna : Acuɣeṛ i tețxemmimem akka ?

9:56 Mmi-s n bunadem ur d-yusi ara ad issenger imdanen lameɛna yusa-d a ten-isellek. Dɣa ṛuḥen ɣer taddart nniḍen.

9:57 Mi teddun deg webrid, yiwen wergaz yenna i Sidna Ɛisa : A Sidi, anda teddiḍ ad dduɣ !

9:58 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Uccanen ɣuṛ-sen lɣiṛan, ifṛax n yigenni sɛan leɛcuc, ma d Mmi-s n bunadem ur yesɛi ara anda ara yessers aqeṛṛuy-is.

9:59 Dɣa yenna i yiwen nniḍen : Eyya-d aț-țedduḍ yid-i. Yerra-yas : A Sidi, serreḥ-iyi uqbel ad ṛuḥeɣ ad meḍleɣ baba.

9:60 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : Eǧǧ wid yemmuten ad meḍlen lmegtin-nsen, ma d kečč eyya-d aț-țbecṛeḍ tageldit n Ṛebbi.

10:1 Syenna, Sidna Ɛisa yextaṛ-ed tnin usebɛin yinelmaden nniḍen, iceggeɛ-iten sin sin ad ṛuḥen ɣer mkul taddart d mkul amkan anda ilaq ad iṛuḥ ula d nețța.

10:31 Iɛedda-d syenna yiwen lmuqeddem n ccariɛa ; mi t-iwala iwwet iɛedda si tama nniḍen.

10:32 Iɛedda-d daɣen seg webrid-nni yiwen wemṛabeḍ n at Lewwi, iwala-t, ikemmel abrid-is iṛuḥ.

10:35 Azekka-nni yefka sin yiṣurdiyen i bab n wexxam-nni, yenna-yas : « Bedd ɣuṛ-es, ayen ara tṣeṛṛfeḍ n zzyada fell-as, a k-t-id-kemmleɣ asm'ara d-uɣaleɣ. »

12:13 Yiwen wergaz inṭeq-ed si ger n lɣaci yenna i Sidna Ɛisa : A Sidi, ini-yas i gma ad ibḍu yid-i lweṛt i d-iǧǧa baba-tneɣ.

12:14 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ay argaz, anwa i yi-sbedden iwakken ad bḍuɣ gar-awen ?

12:15 Dɣa yenna i lɣaci : ?adret iman-nwen ɣef ṭṭmeɛ imi tudert n wemdan ur d-tekki ara seg wayen yesɛa ɣas akken di rrbeḥ ameqqran i gella.

12:16 Imiren yewwi-yasen-d lemtel agi : Yiwen umeṛkanti, yesɛa tamurt tefka-d aṭas n lɣella.

12:17 Ixemmem deg iman-is, yenna : « amek ara xedmeɣ imi ur sɛiɣ ara amkan anda ara jemɛeɣ leṛẓaq-iw ?

12:18 Ad huddeɣ ikuffan-iw, ad bnuɣ wiyaḍ d imeqqranen iwakken ad jemɛeɣ irden-iw d leṛẓaq-iw meṛṛa.

12:19 Imiren ad iniɣ i yiman-iw : « atan tesxezneḍ leṛẓaq i waṭas n iseggasen ; steɛfu tura, ečč, sew tezhuḍ. »

12:20 Lameɛna Sidi Ṛebbi yenna yas-d : « a win iɣeflen ! Iḍ-agi ad a k-ddmeɣ ṛṛuḥ-ik ! Ayen meṛṛa i tjemɛeḍ, iwumi ara d-yeqqim ? »

12:21 Akka i tdeṛṛu d win ijemɛen leṛẓaq i yiman-is, wala ad ixdem ɣef laxeṛt-is, ad yili d ameṛkanti ɣer Ṛebbi.

12:22 Sidna Ɛisa yenna daɣen i yinelmaden-is : ?ef wayagi i wen-d-qqaṛeɣ ur țḥebbiṛet ara ɣef tudert-nwen, ɣef wayen ara teččem d wayen ara telsem.

12:23 Axaṭer tudert-nwen tugar lqut, lǧețța-nwen tugar llebsa.

12:24 Walit tigerfiwin , ur zerrɛent ur meggrent , ur sɛint akufi ur sɛint acbayli meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi ițɛeyyic itent. Acḥal teswam akteṛ n yefṛax n igenni !

12:25 Anwa deg-wen i gzemren s uḥebbeṛ ad yessiɣzef leɛmeṛ-is ula s yiwen n wass ?

12:26 Imi ur tezmirem ara i tɣawsiwin timecṭuḥin, acuɣeṛ i tețḥebbiṛem ɣef wayen nniḍen ?

12:27 Walit amek gemmun ijeǧǧigen n lexla ! Ur țellmen ur ẓeṭṭen ! Atan qqaṛeɣ-awen : ula d agellid Sliman s yiman-is di ccan-is, ur yelsi am yiwen seg yijeǧǧigen-agi.

12:28 Ma yella Sidi Ṛebbi yeslusu akka leḥcic yellan ass-agi di lexla, azekka ad iṛeɣ di tmes, amek ur kkun-islusu ara kunwi, ay imdanen ixuṣṣen di liman !

12:29 Ur țḥebbiṛet ara ihi ! Ur țqellibet ara ɣef wayen ara teččem d wayen ara teswem,

12:30 axaṭer d imdanen ur nețțamen ara s Ṛebbi i gețqelliben ɣef wayagi meṛṛa. Baba-twen yellan deg yigenwan yeẓra ayen teḥwaǧem.

12:31 Qellbet axiṛ ɣef tgeldit n Ṛebbi, ayen nniḍen meṛṛa d nețța ara wen-t-id ifken.

12:32 Ur țțagad ara a taqeḍɛit illan tamecṭuḥt, axaṭer iɛǧeb-as i Baba Ṛebbi a wen-d-yefk tageldit-is.

12:33 Zzenzet ayen tesɛam, tsedqem-t. Heggit i yiman-nwen deg yigenwan agerruj ur nkeffu, ur nrekku, dinna ulac imakaren ara t-yakren, ulac ibeɛɛac ara t-iččen.

12:34 Axaṭer anda yella ugerruj-ik, dinna ara yili wul-ik.

12:35 Heggit iman-nwen, ɛiwzet, beggset ɣef yiman-nwen tceɛlem tiftilin-nwen !

12:36 Ilit am wid yețṛaǧun amɛellem nsen ara d-yuɣalen si tmeɣṛa ; akken kan ara d-yesqeṛbeb a s-ldin tawwurt.

12:37 Amarezg n yiqeddacen-agi ara d-yaf wemɛellem-nsen ɛawzen. A wen-d iniɣ tideț, ad ibeddel llebsa, a ten isɣim ɣer ṭṭabla iwakken a sen-yefk ad ččen.

12:38 Ma yewweḍ-ed di țnaṣfa n yiḍ neɣ akteṛ, amarezg-nsen ma yufa-ten-id mazal țṛaǧun-t !

12:39 Fehmet ayagi : lemmer yeẓra bab n wexxam lweqt i deg ara d-yas umakar, tili a t-iɛass ur t-ițțaǧa ara ad ikcem axxam-is.

12:40 Heggit iman-nwen ula d kunwi imi di lweqt i ɣef ur tebnim (tedmim) ara a d-yas Mmi-s n bunadem.

12:41 Buṭrus inṭeq yenna-yas : A Sidi, i nukkni iwumi d-tenniḍ lemtel-agi neɣ i lɣaci meṛṛa ?

12:42 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Anwa i d lewkil iḥeṛcen i ɣef yella lețkal, ara iwekkel bab n wexxam ɣef wayla-s, iwakken ad ifṛeq i yiqeddacen amur-nsen n yirden di lweqt ilaqen ?

12:43 Amarezg n wuqeddac agi ara d-yaf bab-is ibedd ɣer cceɣl-is !

12:44 A wen-d-iniɣ tideț, a t iwekkel ɣef wayen akk yesɛa.

12:45 Lameɛna aqeddac-nni ma yeqqaṛ deg wul-is : « amɛellem-iw iɛeṭṭel ur d yuɣal ara» ! Yebda iḥeqqeṛ iqeddacen ț-țqeddacin, yekkat-iten, iteț itess alamma yeskkeṛ.

12:46 A d-yas umɛellem-is deg wass i ɣef ur yebni ara, di lweqt ur yessin ara, a t-iɛaqeb s lmut akken țțuɛaqben wid iɛuṣan Ṛebbi.

12:47 Aqeddac yessnen lebɣi n bab-is yerna ur t-ixdim ara, ad yečč tiɣṛit tameqqrant,

12:48 ma d win ur nessin ara lebɣi n bab-is, ixdem ayen yuklalen tiɣṛit, ad yečč tiɣṛit tamecṭuḥt. Win iwumi nefka aṭas a s-nessuter aṭas, ma d win i ɣef nețkel aṭas a neṛǧu deg-s akteṛ.

12:49 Usiɣ-ed a d-awiɣ yiwet n tmes ɣer ddunit ! Acḥal ɣeṣbeɣ melmi ara tecɛel !

12:50 Yella yiwen n weɣḍas i yi-țṛaǧun... D leɛtab ameqqran yerna ɣeṣbeɣ melmi ara d-yas wass-nni !

12:51 Tɣilem d lehna i d-wwiɣ ɣer ddunit ? Xaṭi, d beṭṭu i d-wwiɣ !

12:52 Sya ɣer zdat ma llan xemsa deg yiwen wexxam, ad bḍun ; tlata a d-kkren ɣer sin, sin a d-kkren ɣer tlata.

12:53 Ababat a d-innaɣ d mmi-s, mmi-s a d-yekker ɣer baba-s ; tayemmaț aț-țennaɣ d yelli-s, yelli-s akk-d yemma-s ; tamɣaṛt aț-țennaɣ ț-țeslit-is, tislit akk-d temɣaṛt-is.

12:54 Sidna Ɛisa yenna daɣen i lɣaci : M'ara twalim asigna yekka-d si lɣeṛb, teqqaṛem atan a d-tewwet lehwa ; d ayen i gdeṛṛun.

12:55 M'ara d-iṣuḍ waḍu seg usammer teqqaṛem ad iḥmu lḥal ; d ayen i d-ideṛṛun daɣen.

12:56 Ay at sin wudmawen ! Imi tessnem aț-țɛeqlem lḥal n yigenni d lqaɛa, amek ur tezmirem ara aț-țɛeqlem zzman-agi ?

12:57 Acuɣeṛ ur tețmeyyizem ara s yiman-nwen ɣef wayen yellan d lḥeqq ?

12:58 M'ara k-yessiweḍ wexṣim-ik ɣer ccṛeɛ, eɛṛeḍ a ț-tefruḍ yid-es uqbel aț-țawḍem ɣer dinna, neɣ m'ulac a k-yawi ɣer lḥakem, lḥakem a k-yefk i uɛessas, aɛessas a k-iḍeggeṛ ɣer lḥebs.

12:59 A k-d-iniɣ : ur d-tețțeffɣeḍ ara syenna alamma txellṣeḍ aṣurdi aneggaru n ṭlaba-inek.

13:1 Di lweqt-nni, kra n yemdanen usan-d a s-ḥkun i Sidna Ɛisa ɣef kra n yergazen n tmurt n Jlili i genɣa Bilaṭus, d wamek issexleḍ dammen-nsen d idammen n iseflawen nsen.

13:2 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : Tɣilem belli imezdaɣ-agi n Jlili ițțuɛetben akka d imednuben akteṛ n wiyaḍ ?

13:3 Xaṭi, meɛna a wen-d-iniɣ : m'ur tbeddlem ara tikli aț-țemtem ula d kunwi.

13:4 Neɣ am tmenṭac-nni yergazen yemmuten mi d-yeɣli fell-asen ṣṣuṛ n Silwi, tɣilem d imednuben akteṛ n imezdaɣ nniḍen n temdint n Lquds ?

13:5 Xaṭi, meɛna nniɣ-awen : m'ur tbeddlem ara tikli aț-țemtem ula d kunwi.

13:6 Yewwi-yasen-d lemtel-agi : Yiwen wergaz yesɛa taneqleț teẓẓa deg yiger n tẓurin. Yusa-d iwakken a d-ikkes tibexsisin lameɛna ur yufi ara.

13:7 Yenna i uxeddam n yiger-nni : tlata n yiseggasen aya nekk țțaseɣ ed iwakken a d-kkseɣ lexṛif si tneqleț agi meɛna ur d-țțafeɣ ara, gzem-iț ihi ! Acuɣeṛ ara teṭṭef akal mbla nnfeɛ ?

13:8 Axeddam-nni yerra-yas : a Sidi, eǧǧ-iț aț-țernu aseggas-a ; aț-țneqceɣ, a s-rreɣ leɣbaṛ,

13:9 sya d asawen ma tefka-d lfakya eǧǧ-iț, m'ulac qeleɛ -iț.

13:10 Sidna Ɛisa yesselmad deg yiwen n lǧameɛ deg wass n westeɛfu.

13:11 Tella dinna yiwet n tmeṭṭut ihelken tmenṭac yiseggasen aya ; d yiwen uṛuḥani i ț-ikerfen ur tezmir ara aț-țesbedd lqedd-is.

13:12 Mi ț-iwala Sidna Ɛisa, iluɛa-ț yenna-yas : A tameṭṭut, teḥliḍ seg wekraf-im.

13:13 Issers ifassen-is fell-as , imiren kan tesbedd lqedd-is, teḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi.

13:14 Ccix-nni n lǧameɛ iɣaḍ-it lḥal imi i ț-isseḥla Sidna Ɛisa deg wass n westeɛfu, yenna i lɣaci : Llan sețța wussan i deg ilaq a nexdem ; aset-ed ihi deg ussan-nni iwakken a kkun-isseḥlu mačči deg wass n westeɛfu !

13:15 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Ay at sin wudmawen ! Anwa deg-wen ur d-nețserriḥ i wezger-ines neɣ i weɣyul-is iwakken a t-yawi a d-isew ɣas deg wass n westeɛfu ?

13:16 I tmeṭṭut-agi yellan si dderya n Ibṛahim, i gekref Cciṭan tmenṭac n yiseggasen aya, eɛni ur ilaq ara a s-nekkes akraf ɣas akka d ass n westeɛfu ?

13:17 Mi d-yenna imeslayen-agi, ixṣimen-is meṛṛa nneḥcamen ma d lɣaci feṛḥen s leɛǧayeb i gxeddem.

13:18 Yenna daɣen : ?er wacu i tețțemcabi tgeldit n Ṛebbi ? ?er wacu ara ț-metleɣ ?

13:19 Tețțemcabi ɣer uɛeqqa amecṭuḥ n zerriɛa n uxerḍel i geẓẓa yiwen wergaz di tebḥirt-is. Mi d-imɣi yuɣal d ttejṛa, ifṛax n igenni bnan leɛcuc deg ifurkawen-is.

13:20 Yenna daɣen : ?er wacu i zemreɣ ad metleɣ tageldit n Ṛebbi ?

13:21 Tețțemcabi ɣer ciṭṭuḥ yiɣes n temtunt i d-teddem tmeṭṭut, tger-it di tlata lkilat n uwren dɣa teǧǧa-t armi yuli meṛṛa.

13:22 Deg webrid-is ɣer temdint n Lquds, Sidna Ɛisa ițɛedday ɣef temdinin ț-țudrin, isselmad.

13:23 Isteqsa-t yiwen yenna-yas : A Sidi, drus n yemdanen ara yețțuselken ?

13:24 Yenna-yasen : Qellbet amek ara tkecmem si tewwurt iḍeyqen ; a wen-d-iniɣ : aṭas ara yebɣun ad kecmen lameɛna ur țțizmiren ara.

13:25 M'ara d-yekker bab n wexxam ad isekkeṛ tawwurt, wid i d-yufa lḥal di beṛṛa a d-bdun asqeṛbeb ad qqaṛen : « A Sidi ! A Sidi ! Ldi-yaɣ tawwurt» ! Nețța a wen-d-yerr : ur kkun-ssineɣ ur ẓriɣ ansi i d-tekkam !?

13:26 Kunwi ad as-tinim : « anaɣ nečča neswa yid-ek, teslemdeḍ deg yizenqan nneɣ ! »

13:27 Nețța a wen-d-yerr : Nniɣ-awen ur kkun-ssineɣ ara, beɛdet akkin fell-i a kunwi ixeddmen cceṛ !

13:28 Dinna ara yilin yimeṭṭawen d nndama tameqqrant m'ara twalim Ibṛahim, Isḥaq, Yeɛqub akk-d lenbiya di tgelda n yigenwan, ma d kunwi aț-țeqqimem di beṛṛa.

13:29 A d-asen si cceṛq d lɣeṛb, seg umalu d usammer, ad ṭṭfen imukan di tmeɣṛa n tgelda n igenwan.

13:30 Imiren ad ilin ineggura ara yuɣalen d imezwura, am akken daɣen ara yilin imezwura ara yuɣalen d ineggura.

13:31 Di teswiɛt-nni, kra n ifariziyen usan-d ɣer Sidna Ɛisa nnan-as : Ṛuḥ tixxeṛ syagi, atan Hiṛudus ițqellib a k-ineɣ.

13:32 Yenna-yasen : Ṛuḥet init-as i wuccen-agi : ass-a d uzekka ad ssufɣeɣ leǧnun, ad sseḥluɣ imuḍan , ass wis tlata ad fakeɣ cceɣl-iw.

13:33 Lameɛna ilaq ad kemmleɣ tikli-inu ass-a, azekka d sellazekka, axaṭer ur s-ilaq ara i nnbi ad immet beṛṛa n temdint n Lquds.

13:34 A tamdint n Lquds ! A tamdint n Lquds ineqqen lenbiya, iṛeǧǧmen wid i m-d-ițwaceggɛen s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi ! Acḥal d abrid i ɛerḍeɣ a d-jemɛeɣ arraw-im am tyaziṭ i d-ijemɛen ifṛax-is seddaw wafriwen-is, lameɛna ur tebɣim ara.

13:35 Atan ihi, axxam-nwen ad ixlu yerna nniɣ-awen, ur iyi-tẓerrem ara alamma d asmi ara tinim : Ad ițțubarek win i d-yusan s yisem n Sidi Ṛebbi.

14:1 Yiwen wass yellan d ass n westeɛfu, Sidna Ɛisa illa yețwaɛreḍ ɣer wexxam n yiwen seg yimeqqranen n yifariziyen iwakken ad yefteṛ. Wid yellan dinna țɛassan-t d acu ara yexdem.

14:2 Yella dinna yiwen wergaz yesɛan lehlak n ubezzug.

14:3 Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq ɣer lɛulama n ccariɛa d yifariziyen yenna-yasen : D acu twalam ? D leḥlal win ara isseḥlun deg wass n westeɛfu neɣ ala ?

14:4 Imi yiwen deg-sen ur d-yenṭiq, Sidna Ɛisa iqeṛṛeb ɣer umuḍin-nni, yesseḥla-t, iserreḥ-as ad iṛuḥ.

14:5 Dɣa yenna i wiyaḍ : Anwa deg-wen ma yeɣli-yas mmi-s neɣ wezger-ines ɣer lbir, ur t-id-ițekkes ara ɣas ma d ass n westeɛfu ?

14:6 Yiwen ur izmir a s-d-yerr awal.

14:7 Mi gwala wid yețwaɛerḍen bdan țextiṛin imukan imezwura, Sidna Ɛisa yewwi-yasen-d lemtel-agi yenna yasen :

14:8 M'ara k-id-yeɛreḍ yiwen ɣer tmeɣṛa, ɣurek ur țțaṭṭaf ara amkan amezwaru ! Axaṭer yezmer ad yili yiwen ger inebgawen yesɛan leqdeṛ akteṛ-ik ,

14:9 a d-yas win i kkun-iɛerḍen i sin a k-yini : « eǧǧ-as amkan-nni i wagi ! » Imiren aț-țenneḥcameḍ, aț-țṛuḥeḍ aț-țeṭṭfeḍ amkan aneggaru.

14:10 Lameɛna m'ara tețwaɛeṛḍeḍ, ṭṭef amkan aneggaru iwakken m'ara d-yas win i k-id-iɛerḍen, a k-yini : « ay aḥbib ɛeddi-d ɣer zdat ! » Imiren aț-țesɛuḍ leqdeṛ zdat inebgawen meṛṛa.

14:11 Axaṭer kra n win yessalayen iman-is a t-id-yessers Ṛebbi , ma d win yesrusuyen iman-is a t-yerfed.

14:12 Yenna daɣen i win i t-id iɛeṛḍen : M'ara tebɣuḍ aț-țseččeḍ, ur d-ɛeṛṛeḍ ara imdukkal-ik neɣ atmaten ik, ur d-ɛeṛṛeḍ ara imawlan-ik neɣ lǧiran-ik imeṛkantiyen ; m'ulac a k ɛerḍen ula d nutni iwakken a k-d-rren lxiṛ-ik.

14:13 Lameɛna m'ara txedmeḍ tameɣṛa, ɛṛeḍ-ed igellilen, ineɛyuba, iquḍaren d yiderɣalen.

14:14 Ili-k di lfeṛḥ imi ur zmiren ara a k-d-rren lxiṛ-ik, axaṭer lxiṛ-ik a k-id-yuɣal asm'ara d-ḥyun wid yellan d iḥeqqiyen ɣer Ṛebbi.

14:15 Mi gesla i imeslayen-agi, yiwen seg inebgawen inṭeq-ed yenna i Sidna Ɛisa : Amasseɛd n win ara yeččen tiremt di tgelda n yigenwan!

14:16 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Yiwen wergaz ixdem imensi, iɛreḍ-ed aṭas lɣaci.

14:17 Lweqt n imensi, iceggeɛ aqeddac-is iwakken ad yini i inebgawen : aset-ed ! Kullec iwjed !

14:18 Akken ma llan ufan-d sebba. Amezwaru yenna-yas : « aql-i uɣeɣ iger, ilaq ad ṛuḥeɣ a t-id-ẓreɣ , di leɛnaya-k semmeḥ-iyi ».

14:19 Wayeḍ yenna-yas : « aql-i uɣeɣ-d xemsa n tyugiwin n yezgaren bɣiɣ ad ṛuḥeɣ a tent-ɛerḍeɣ, di leɛnaya-k semmeḥ iyi ».

14:20 Wayeḍ daɣen yenna-yas : « tura kan i zewǧeɣ, ulamek ara d aseɣ ».

14:21 Mi d-yuɣal uqeddac ɣer umɛellem-is, yeḥka-yas ayen akk i s-d-nnan. Bab n wexxam yerfa, yenna i uqeddac-is : « azzel, ṛuḥ ɣer yiberdan d yizenqan n temdint, tɛeṛḍeḍ-ed iẓawaliyen, ineɛyuba, iderɣalen d iquḍaren ».

14:22 Mi d-yuɣal uqeddac-nni yenna-yas : a Sidi, ayen i yi-d-tenniḍ xedmeɣ-t yerna mazal llan imukan.

14:23 Amɛellem yenna i uqeddac is : « effeɣ ɣer iberdan n tudrin d lexlawi ; wid akk ara tafeḍ, ḥṛeṣ-iten a d asen iwakken ad iččaṛ wexxam-iw. »

14:24 Atan nniɣ-awen : ula d yiwen seg wid-nni yețwaɛerḍen d imezwura ur iɛerreḍ imensi-inu.

14:25 Aṭas n lɣaci i geddan d Sidna Ɛisa. Yezzi ɣuṛ-sen yenna-yasen :

14:26 Kra n win ara d-yasen ɣuṛ-i, ma iḥemmel baba-s d yemma-s neɣ tameṭṭut-is d warraw-is neɣ ayetma-s d yessetma-s neɣ iḥemmel iman-is akteṛ-iw, ur izmir ara ad yili d anelmad-iw.

14:27 Kra n win ur neqbil ara ad yeɛteb ɣef ddemma n yisem-iw yerna ur iyi-d-itbiɛ ara, ur izmir ara ad yili d anelmad-iw.

14:28 Anwa deg-wen ma yebɣa ad ibnu lbeṛǧ, ur yețɣimi ara ad ixdem uqbel leḥsab-is, iwakken ad iẓer ma yezmer a t-ifak ?

14:29 Axaṭer ma yebda lsas yeǧǧa-t akken, kra n wid ara t-iẓren ad stehzin fell-as ad inin :

14:30 argaz-agi yebda lsas ur izmir ad ikemmel lebni.

14:31 Neɣ anwa agellid ara yekkren ad innaɣ d ugellid nniḍen, ur ixeddem ara uqbel leḥsab iwakken ad iẓer ma yezmer ad iqabel s ɛecṛa alaf iɛsekṛiwen win i d-iteddun ɣuṛ-es s ɛecrin alef iɛsekriwen ?

20:46 ?adret iman-nwen ɣef lɛulama n ccariɛa i gḥemmlen ad țḥewwisen s ijellaben iɣezfanen, ḥemmlen ad țsellimen fell-asen yemdanen di leswaq, ad ṭṭfen imukan imezwura di leǧwameɛ d imukan lɛali di tmeɣṛa.

21:1 Sidna Ɛisa yerfed allen-is, iwala imeṛkantiyen srusun lewɛadi deg usenduq n lǧameɛ.

21:2 Ațaya yiwet n taǧǧalt taẓawalit tger sin iṣurdiyen ɣer usenduq-nni,

21:3 dɣa yenna : A wen-iniɣ tideț : lweɛda n taǧǧalt-agi taẓawalit tugar akk tiyaḍ ;

21:4 axaṭer wiyaḍ fkan seg wayen sɛan d zzyada ma d nețțat tefka ciṭṭuḥ-nni s wacu ara tɛic.

21:5 Kra n yemdanen țmeslayen ɣef lǧameɛ iqedsen qqaṛen wway gar-asen : Acḥal yecbeḥ lebni n lǧameɛ-agi akk-d țɣawsiwin i s-d-yețțunefken d lweɛda ! Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen :

21:6 Deg wayen akka i tețwalim, a d-awḍen wussan anda kullec ad ihudd, ur d-yețɣimi wedɣaɣ ɣef wayeḍ.

21:7 Steqsan-t nnan-as : A Sidi, melmi ara d-yedṛu wannect-a ? Anta licaṛa ara d isbegnen belli qṛib a d-yedṛu waya ?

21:8 Yenna-yasen : ?uṛ-wat a kkun-kellxen, axaṭer aṭas ara d-yasen s yisem-iw a d inin : d nekk i d Lmasiḥ ! Neɣ a wen-d-inin : lweqt yewweḍ-ed ! Ur ten-țțamnet ara !

21:9 M'ara teslem heddṛen ɣef tegrawliwin d ṭradat ur țțagadet ara, axaṭer ilaq a d-yedṛu uqbel wayagi, lameɛna mačči imiren i ț-țaggara n ddunit.

21:10 Yenna-yasen daɣen : A d-yekker wegdud ɣer wayeḍ, a d-tekker tagelda ɣer tayeḍ.

21:11 A d-tilint zzelzlat timeqqranin, a d-ɣlin di yal lehlakat i gderrɛen ( i gneṭṭḍen) akk-d laẓ, a d-tilint leɛǧubat yessexlaɛen akk-d licaṛat timeqqranin deg igenni.

21:12 Lameɛna uqbel a d-yedṛu wannect-a, kunwi s inelmaden-iw a kkun-ṭṭfen, a kkun qehṛen, ad ḥekmen fell-awen di leǧwameɛ, a kkun-ḍeggṛen ɣer leḥbus, a kkun-sbedden zdat igelliden d lḥekkam ɣef ddemma n yisem-iw.

21:13 A d-yedṛu wannect-a yid-wen iwakken aț-țilim d inigan-iw zdat-sen.

21:14 Ur țḥebbiṛet ara amek ara tsellkem iman-nwen,

21:15 axaṭer a wen-d-fkeɣ imeslayen d leɛqel s wacu ara tqablem iɛdawen nwen, ur țizmiren ara ad bedden zdat-wen neɣ a kkun-xalfen.

21:16 Ula d imawlan-nwen akk-d atmaten-nwen d imdukkal-nwen a kkun-zzenzen yerna ad nɣen aṭas seg-wen.

21:17 A kkun-keṛhen yemdanen meṛṛa ɣef ddemma-w,

21:18 lameɛna yiwen wenẓad n uqeṛṛuy-nwen ur yețṛuḥu.

21:19 ?ebṛet, axaṭer s ṣṣbeṛ-nwen ara tsellkem tiṛwiḥin-nwen.

21:20 M'ara teẓrem tamdint n Lquds zzin-as-d lɛeskeṛ n yeɛdawen-is, ḥṣut belli nnger-is yewweḍ-ed.

21:21 Ass-nni ilaq wid ara yilin di temdint n Lquds ad ffɣen, wid ara yilin di lexla ur keččmen ara ɣer temdint, wid ara yilin di tmurt n Yahuda ad rewlen ɣer idurar.

21:22 Axaṭer ussan-nni d ussan n twaɣit deg wacu ara yedṛu meṛṛa wayen yuran.

21:23 A nnger n tilawin ara yilin s tadist neɣ tid ara yessuṭuḍen deg ussan-nni axaṭer a d-teɣli cedda ț-țameqqrant ɣef tmurt, a d-yeɣli wurrif n Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef wegdud,

21:24 imezdaɣ-is ad mmten s ujenwi, a ten-awin d imeḥbas ɣer tmura nniḍen. Tamdint n Lquds a ț-ṛekḍen leǧnas ur nețțamen ara s Sidi Ṛebbi, alamma yețwakemmel lweqt i sen-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi, imiren ad yezzi ɣef leǧnas-nni, a ten-iɛaqeb.

21:25 A d-tbanent licaṛat deg iṭij, deg waggur d yitran. Di ddunit, imdanen meṛṛa a ten-iffeɣ leɛqel, ur ẓerren ara d acu ara xedmen m'ara slen i ṣṣut n lemwaji d lebḥuṛ.

21:26 Ad ilin wid ara yemten si lxuf m'ara walin lmuṣibat ara d-yeɣlin ɣef ddunit ; igenwan d kra yellan deg-sen ad rgagin.

21:27 Imiren imdanen ad walin Mmi-s n bunadem iteddu-d ɣef wusigna s tezmert d lɛaḍima tameqqrant.

21:28 M'ara yebdu wannect-agi iḍeṛṛu, sbeddet lqedd-nwen trefdem iqeṛṛay-nwen axaṭer leslak-nwen yewweḍ-ed.

21:29 Yewwi-yasen-d lemtel-agi : Walit taneqleț neɣ ttjuṛ nniḍen !

21:30 M'ara bdunt fessunt, teẓram qṛib a d-yaweḍ unebdu.

21:31 Kunwi daɣen m'ara twalim yewweḍ-ed wannect-agi ḥṣut belli tageldit n Ṛebbi teqṛeb-ed.

21:32 A wen-iniɣ tideț: lǧil-agi ur yețmețțat ara alamma yedṛa wannect-agi meṛṛa.

21:33 Igenni d lqaɛa ad fnun ma d awal-iw ur ifennu ara.

21:34 Ɛasset ɣef yiman-nwen, ḥadret a kkun-iɣuṛṛ zzhu n ddunit ; ɛasset iman-nwen ɣef tissit n ccṛab d iɣeblan, neɣ m'ulac a d-yeɣli fell awen wass-nni ur tebnim fell-as.

21:35 Ass-nni a d-yeɣli am ucebbak ɣef yemdanen n ddunit meṛṛa.

21:36 Ɛiwzet ihi, deɛɛut dayem ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi a wen-d-yefk tazmert ara kkun-imenɛen seg wayen i d-iteddun, s wayes ara tbeddem zdat Mmi-s n bunadem.

21:37 Deg wass, Sidna Ɛisa yesselmad di lǧameɛ iqedsen ma deg iḍ, yețṛuḥu ad yens deg yiwen yiɣil ițțusemman : «Iɣil n uzemmur ».

21:38 ?ṣbeḥ zik, lɣaci meṛṛa țṛuḥun ɣuṛ-es ɣer lǧameɛ iqedsen iwakken ad slen i wawal-is.

22:1 Lɛid i deg xedmen aɣṛum mbla iɣes n temtunt ițțusemman « Tafaska n izimer n leslak » tqeṛb-ed.

22:2 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɛulama n ccariɛa țqelliben amek ara nɣen Sidna Ɛisa lameɛna uggaden lɣaci.

22:3 Yudas n Qeṛyut yiwen si tnac-nni n inelmadenis, ikcem-it Cciṭan.

22:4 Iṛuḥ ad yemcawaṛ d lmuqedmin imeqqranen akk-d lḥekkam iɛessasen n lǧameɛ iqedsen, iwakken a sen izzenz Sidna Ɛisa.

22:5 Nutni feṛḥen yerna ɛuhden-t a s-fken idrimen.

22:6 Yudas yefra awal yid-sen syenna yebda yețqellib amek ara sen-t-yezzenz s tuffra ɣef lɣaci.

22:7 Yewweḍ-ed wass n Tfaska n weɣṛum mbla iɣes n tamtunt i deg ilaq ad zlun izimer n leslak.

22:8 Sidna Ɛisa iceggeɛ Buṭrus d Yuḥenna, yenna-yasen : Ṛuḥet a ɣ-theggim imensi n Tfaska.

22:9 Nnan-as : Anda tebɣiḍ a t-nheggi a Sidi ?

22:10 Yerra-yasen : M'ara tkecmem ɣer temdint, a d-temmagrem yiwen wergaz yerfed tacmuxt n waman ; tebɛet-eț ɣer wexxam anda ara yekcem.

22:11 Init i bab n wexxam-nni : yeqqaṛ-ak-d Ssid nneɣ, anda ara nečč imensi n Tfaska nekk d inelmaden-iw ?

22:12 A wen-d-isken yiwet n tɣuṛfeț tameqqrant iwejden, dinna ara theggim imensi n Tfaska.

22:13 Mi ṛuḥen, ufan kullec akken i sen-t-id-yenna, dɣa heggan imensi n Tfaska.

22:14 Mi d-yewweḍ lweqt n imensi, Sidna Ɛisa yeqqim ad yečč nețța d Ṛṛusul-is,

22:15 yenna-yasen : Acḥal i mennaɣ ad ččeɣ imensi n Tfaska-agi yid-wen uqbel ad nneɛtabeɣ !

22:16 A wen-d-iniɣ : atan ur țțuɣaleɣ ara maḍi a t-ččeɣ alamma tețwakemmel lmeɛna-ines di tgelda n Sidi Ṛebbi.

22:17 Yeddem-d tabuqalt n waman n tẓurin, yeḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi yenna : Axet tabuqalt-agi, feṛqet-eț wway gar-awen,

22:18 a wen-d-iniɣ : sya d asawen ur țțuɣaleɣ ara ad sweɣ seg waman-agi n tẓurin alamma tewweḍ ed tgeldit n Sidi Ṛebbi.

22:19 Yeddem-ed daɣen aɣṛum, iḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi, yebḍa-t yefka-yasen-t, yenna : Aɣṛum-agi d lǧețța-w ara fkeɣ fell-awen, feṛqet-eț gar-awen. Sya d asawen xeddmet akkagi akken ad iyi-d-tețmektayem.

22:20 Mi gfukk imensi, yeddem-ed tabuqalt n waman n tẓurin, yefka yasen-ț yenna : Tabuqalt-agi, d leɛqed ajdid s idammen-iw ara yazzlen fell-awen.

22:21 Ma d win ara yi-zzenzen atan yeqqim-ed yid-i ɣer lmakla.

22:22 Mmi-s n bunadem ad immet akken yura fell-as di tira iqedsen, lameɛna a nnger n wemdan agi ara t-izzenzen !

22:23 Ṛṛusul bdan țmesteqsayen wway gar-asen anwa-t wagi ara t-ixedɛen.

22:24 Inelmaden bdan țemjadalen wway gar-asen iwakken ad ẓren anwa deg-sen ara ițwaḥesben d ameqqran.

22:25 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : Imeqqranen n ddunit ḥekmen s ddreɛ ɣef yigduden-nsen yerna țțusemman d at lxiṛ.

22:26 Kunwi xḍut i wannect-agi. Ameqqran deg-wen ad yili am umeẓyan, win iḥekmen ad yili am win iqeddcen.

22:27 Anwa i d ameqqran , d amɛelem yeqqimen ad yečč neɣ d win i s iqeddcen ? Yak d win yeqqimen ad yečč ! Ma d nekk, aql-i gar-awen am win iqeddcen.

22:28 Kunwi aql-ikkun tṣebṛem yid-i di lmeḥnat-iw.

22:29 Akken i yi-d-ihegga Baba tagelda, ula d nekk a wen-ț-id heggiɣ i kunwi

22:30 iwakken aț-țeččem aț-țeswem yid-i di tgelda-inu. Aț-țeqqimem ɣef yikursiyen n lḥekma, aț-țḥekmem ɣef tnac leɛṛac n wat Isṛail.

22:31 Sidna Ɛisa yenna : A Semɛun sel-iyi-d ! Cciṭan yessuter a kkun-isserwet am yirden,

22:32 lameɛna dɛiɣ ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-ak akken ur tfeččleḍ ara di liman. Ma d kečč m'ara d-tuɣaleḍ ɣuṛ-i, sseǧhed atmaten-ik.

22:33 Buṭrus yenna-yas : Aql-i heggaɣ iman-iw ad kecmeɣ yid-ek ɣer lḥebs, ad dduɣ yid-ek ula ɣer lmut.

22:34 Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : A k-d-iniɣ a Buṭrus , ass-a uqbel ad iskkuɛ uyaziḍ ad iyi-tnekṛeḍ tlata iberdan, aț-ținiḍ ur iyi-tessineḍ ara !

22:35 Yenna-yasen daɣen : M'akken i kkun-ceggɛeɣ mbla idrimen, mbla agrab, mbla arkasen, yella kra i kkun-ixuṣṣen ? Rran-as : Ur aɣ-ixuṣṣ wacemma !

22:36 Yenna-yasen : Ihi tura win yesɛan idrimen neɣ agrab yawi-ten, win ur nesɛi ara ajenwi ad izzenz abeṛnus-is iwakken a d-yaɣ yiwen.

22:37 Axaṭer qqaṛeɣ awen : Ilaq ad yedṛu yid-i wayen yuran. « Ițțuneḥsab seg yembaṣiyen, » yerna ayen yuran fell-i, atan yewweḍ ed.

22:38 Inelmaden-is nnan-as : A Sidi , atnan sin ijenwiyen ! Yenna-yasen : D ayen tura, beṛka !

22:39 Akken yuɣ tannumi, Sidna Ɛisa yeffeɣ, iṛuḥ ɣer yiɣil n uzemmur ; inelmaden-is tebɛen-t.

22:40 Mi gewweḍ ɣer wemkan-nni, yenna-yasen : Dɛut ɣer Ṛebbi akken ur tɣellim ara deg ujeṛṛeb.

22:41 Dɣa yessebɛed iman-is fell-asen azal n kra n lmitrat, yeqqim ɣef tgecrar ideɛɛu i Ṛebbi yeqqaṛ :

22:42 A Baba, ma tebɣiḍ ssebɛed fell-i leɛtab-agi i yi-țṛaǧun ; lameɛna d lebɣi-k ara yedṛun mačči d lebɣi inu.

22:43 Yiwen n lmelk n Sidi Ṛebbi iḍheṛ-as-d a t-yesseǧhed.

22:44 Iḍaq wul-is, ikețțeṛ di tẓallit armi tuɣal tidi-ines d idammen yețqiṭṭiren ɣer lqaɛa.

22:45 Mi gekfa, yuɣal-ed ɣer inelmaden-is, yufa-ten-id yewwi-ten yiḍes axaṭer yeɣli-d fell-asen leḥzen ameqqran ;

22:46 dɣa yenna-yasen : Acuɣeṛ i tegnem ? Kkret, dɛut ɣer Ṛebbi akken ur tɣellim ara deg ujeṛṛeb.

22:47 Mazal-it iheddeṛ, atnaya wwḍen d lɣaci. Yudas, yiwen si tnac-nni inelmaden-is yezwar-ed zdat-sen, iqeṛṛeb ɣer Sidna Ɛisa ad isellem fell-as.

22:48 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yas : A Yudas, s sslam ara txedɛeḍ Mmi-s n bunadem !

22:49 Wid yellan d Sidna Ɛisa, mi walan ayen ara yedṛun nnan-as : A Sidi, a newwet s ijenwiyen ?

22:50 Yiwen deg-sen yewwet aqeddac n lmuqeddem ameqqran, igzem-as ameẓẓuɣ ayeffus.

22:51 Sidna Ɛisa yenna yasen : ?ebset ! Dayen tura ! Yessers afus-is ɣef wemkan n umeẓẓuɣ n uqeddac-nni, yerra-yas-t akken yella.

22:52 Dɣa yezzi ɣer wid i d-yusan a t-ḥebsen, ɣer lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɛuqal akk-d lḥekkam n iɛessasen n lǧameɛ iqedsen yenna yasen : Tusam-d ɣuṛ-i s ijenwiyen d iɛekzan am akken d amakar i lliɣ.

22:53 Mkul ass țțiliɣ yid-wen di lǧameɛ iqedsen yiwen ur d-yerfid afus-is ɣuṛ-i. Lameɛna taswiɛt-agi ț-țaswiɛt nwen, ț-țaswiɛt n tezmert n ṭṭlam.

22:54 Mi ṭṭfen Sidna Ɛisa, wwin-t ɣer wexxam n lmuqeddem ameqqran. Buṭrus itbeɛ-iten s lebɛid.

22:55 Ceɛlen times di tlemmast n wefrag, qqimen. Buṭrus yella yid-sen.

22:56 Yiwet n tqeddact twala-t-id yeqqim yeẓẓiẓin, tmuqel-it tenna : Argaz-agi daɣen yella yid-es.

22:57 Lameɛna Buṭrus yenkeṛ, yenna : Ur t-ssineɣ ara a tameṭṭut !

22:58 Cwiṭ akka, iwala-t-id yiwen nniḍen, yenna : Kečč daɣen d yiwen seg-sen. Buṭrus yerra-yas, yenna : Ur lliɣ ara seg-sen ay argaz !

22:59 Iɛedda wazal n ssaɛa, yenṭeq-ed yiwen nniḍen yenna : Mbla ccekk argaz-agi yella yid-es axaṭer d ajlili.

22:60 Buṭrus yerra-yas : Ur ẓriɣ ara d acu i d-teqqaṛeḍ ay argaz ! Werɛad yekfi ameslay... yeskkuɛ uyaziḍ.

22:61 Sidna Ɛisa yezzi ɣuṛ-es, immuqel-it. Dɣa Buṭrus yemmekta-d wayen i s-d-yenna Sidna Ɛisa : « Ass-agi, uqbel ad iskkuɛ uyaziḍ ad iyi-tnekṛeḍ tlata iberdan.»

22:62 Yeffeɣ ɣer beṛṛa iṭṭerḍeq s imeṭṭawen axaṭer yendem.

22:63 Irgazen-nni i gɛussen Sidna Ɛisa, stehzayen fell-as kkaten-t.

22:64 ?ɣummun-as udem-is, rnan qqaṛen-as « eɛqel ihi anwa i k-id yewten ».

22:65 Rnan regmen-t s mkul rregmat yellan.

22:66 Mi guli wass, tajmaɛt n lɛuqal n wegdud, lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɛulama n ccariɛa nnejmaɛen. Wwin-d Sidna Ɛisa ɣer usqamu n ccṛeɛ.

22:67 Steqsan-t nnan-as : Ma d kečč i d Lmasiḥ, ini yaɣ t-id. Yerra-yasen : Atan ma nniɣ-awen-t-id ur iyi tețțamnem ara,

22:68 ma steqsaɣ-kkun-id ur iyi-d-țarram ara.

22:69 Lameɛna a wen-d-iniɣ, sya ɣer zdat, Mmi-s n bunadem ad yeḥkem ɣer tama tayeffust n Sidi Ṛebbi Bab n tezmert.

22:70 Kkren-d akken ma llan nnan : Ihi kečč d Mmi-s n Ṛebbi ? Nețța yerra-yasen : Tennam-t-id, d nekk !

22:71 Imiren ɛeggḍen nnan : Dayen ur neḥwaǧ ara inigan, yenna-t-id s yimi-s, nesla-yas s imeẓẓuɣen-nneɣ.

23:1 Tajmaɛt meṛṛa tekker, wwin Sidna Ɛisa ɣer lḥakem Bilaṭus.

23:2 Bdan țcetkin fell-as, qqaṛen : -- Nufa argaz-agi yeskker ccwal ger lɣaci-nneɣ, yeqqaṛ-asen : « ur țxelliṣet ara leɣṛama i Qayṣer », yeqqaṛ daɣen : « d nețța i d Lmasiḥ, i d agellid ».

23:3 Bilaṭus isteqsa-t s imeslayen-agi : D kečč i d agellid n wat Isṛail ? Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : Tenniḍ-t-id.

23:4 Bilaṭus yenna i lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɣaci : Ur s-d-ssukkseɣ acemma i wergaz-agi.

23:5 Nutni țkemmilen qqaṛen : Yeskker ccwal ger lɣaci s wayen yesselmad ; yebda-d si tmurt n Jlili, yuɣal ɣer tmurt n Yahuda meṛṛa, tura yewweḍ-ed ɣer dagi.

23:6 Bilaṭus, mi gesla bedren-d tamurt n Jlili, yesteqsa ɣef Sidna Ɛisa ma d ajlili i gella.

23:7 Mi geẓra belli Sidna Ɛisa ajlili, n tmurt i ɣef yeḥkem Hiṛudus, iceggeɛ-as-t i Hiṛudus yellan ula d nețța di temdint n Lquds ussan-nni.

23:8 Hiṛudus ifṛeḥ aṭas mi geẓra Sidna Ɛisa, axaṭer acḥal segmi yebɣa a t-iẓer ɣef ddemma n wayen yesla fell-as. Yețmenni a t-iwali yexdem lbeṛhan,

23:9 isteqsa-t aṭas, lameɛna Sidna Ɛisa ur as-d-yerri acemma.

23:10 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen akk-d lɛulama n ccariɛa yellan dinna țcetkayen fell-as s lɣecc.

23:11 Hiṛudus d lɛeskeṛ-is ḥeqṛen-t, stehzayen fell as, selsen-as yiwen ubeṛnus ifazen, imiren rran-t i Bilaṭus.

23:12 Seg wass-nni, Bilaṭus d Hiṛudus uɣalen d imdukkal nutni yellan zik d iɛdawen.

23:13 Imiren Bilaṭus yesnejmaɛ-ed lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lɛulama akk-d lɣaci,

23:14 yenna-yasen : Tewwim-iyi-d argaz-agi, tennam d yeskker ccwal ger lɣaci ; atan steqsaɣ-t zdat-wen ur ufiɣ ara deg-s ayen akka s wayes i d-teccetkam fell as.

23:15 Ula d Hiṛudus ur s-d yessukkes acemma imi i ɣ-t-id-yerra. Ihi argaz-agi ur yexdim ara ayen yuklalen lmut.

23:16 A s-fkeɣ tiɣṛit imiren a s-serrḥeɣ.

23:17 Mkul Tafaska n izimer n leslak Bilaṭus yețserriḥ-ed i yiwen umeḥbus.

23:18 Lɣaci meṛṛa țɛeggiḍen qqaṛen : Enɣ-it , tserḥeḍ-ed i Barabas !

23:19 Barabas-agi yețwaḥbes ɣef ccwal i gexdem di temdint-nni akk ț-țemgeṛṭ i genɣa.

23:20 Bilaṭus yebɣan a d-iserreḥ i Sidna Ɛisa ihḍeṛ-ed tikkelt nniḍen i lɣaci,

23:21 lameɛna nutni țɛeggiḍen qqaṛen : Semmeṛ-it, semmeṛ-it ɣef wumidag.

23:22 Bilaṭus inṭeq-ed tikkelt tis tlata, yenna-yasen : D acu n cceṛ i gexdem ? Ur ufiɣ ara deg-s ayen yuklalen lmut. Atan ihi a s-fkeɣ tiɣṛit, imiren a s-serrḥeɣ.

23:23 Lameɛna nutni ḥeṛsen-t s leɛyaḍ qqaṛen : Semmeṛ-it ɣef lluḥ ! ?elben-t s leɛyaḍ-nsen

23:24 dɣa Bilaṭus yeqbel ayen i s-ssutren.

23:25 Iserreḥ-as-d i umeḥbus-nni issekren ccwal yerna yenɣa tamgeṛṭ ; ma d Sidna Ɛisa yefka-yasen-t ad xedmen yis ayen bɣan.

23:26 Mi wwin Sidna Ɛisa, mmugren-d yiwen wergaz n temdint n Qiṛwan ițțusemman Semɛun, yuɣal-ed si lexla ; ḥettmen fell-as ad ibbib amidag-nni, ad iddu deffir Sidna Ɛisa.

23:27 Aṭas n lɣaci i d-itebɛen deffir nsen, llant gar-asen tilawin kkatent deg idmaren-nsent, țrunt fell-as.

23:28 Sidna Ɛisa yezzi-d ɣuṛ-sent yenna-yasent : A yessi-s n temdint n Lquds ur țrumt ara fell-i, țrumt ɣef yiman-nkunt d warraw-nkunt,

23:29 axaṭer atan a d-asen wussan i deg ara yinin : « ț-țiseɛdiyin tid ur nezmir ara a d-sɛunt dderya, ț-țiseɛdiyin tid ur d-nuriw ara, ur nessuṭeḍ ara.»

23:30 Imiren imdanen ad bdun ad qqaṛen : ay idurar ɣlit-ed fell-aɣ, a tiɣaltin ffremt-aɣ.

23:31 Axaṭer ma xedmen annect-a i wesɣaṛ akken d azegzaw amek ara tedṛu d uquṛan ?

23:32 Wwin daɣen sin n yemcumen iwakken a ten-semmṛen akk-d Sidna Ɛisa.

23:33 Mi wwḍen ɣer yiwen wemkan ițțusemman « Iɣil Uqeṛṛuy», semmṛen Sidna Ɛisa ɣef lluḥ, nețța d sin-nni yembaṣiyen, yiwen ɣer tama-s tayeffust wayeḍ ɣer tama-s tazelmaṭ.

23:34 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yenṭeq-ed yenna : A Baba eɛfu-yasen imi ur ẓrin ara d acu i xeddmen. Iɛsekṛiwen-nni gren tasɣaṛt ad feṛqen gar-asen llebsa-ines.

23:35 Lɣaci i gbedden dinna țfeṛṛiǧen ; lmuqedmin țmesxiṛen fell-as qqaṛen : Isellek wiyaḍ, ad isellek iman-is tura ma yella ț-țideț d Lmasiḥ i gextaṛ Ṛebbi.

23:36 Iɛsekṛiwen daɣen țmesxiṛen fell as, qeṛṛben ɣuṛ-es bɣan a s-sswen lxell,

23:37 qqaṛen-as : Ma d agellid n wat Isṛail i telliḍ, sellek iman-ik.

23:38 Ddmen-d daɣen yiwet n telwiḥt, semmṛen-ț sennig uqeṛṛuy-is, uran deg-s : « wagi d agellid n wat Isṛail ».

23:39 Yiwen seg seg imbaṣiyen-nni ițțusemmṛen ɣef lluḥ, ireggem Sidna Ɛisa, yeqqaṛ-as : Ma d Lmasiḥ i telliḍ sellek iman ik tsellkeḍ-aɣ ula d nukkni.

23:40 Lameɛna wayeḍ ilumm-it yenna yas : Ur tuggadeḍ ara Ṛebbi, kečč ițțusemmṛen am nețța ?

23:41 Nukni ma ḥekmen fell-aɣ d lḥeqq axaṭer nuklal lmut s wayen i nexdem ; lameɛna nețța ur yexdim acemma n wayen n diri.

23:42 Yezzi ɣer Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yas : A Ɛisa, mmekti-yi-d m'ara d-tas lḥekma n tgeldit-ik.

23:43 Sidna Ɛisa yerra-yas : A k-iniɣ tideț, ass-agi aț-țiliḍ yid-i di lǧennet.

23:44 Aț-țili d leǧwahi n tnac, yeɣli-d ṭṭlam ɣef tmurt meṛṛa armi d țlata n tmeddit.

23:45 Leḥjab yellan deg umkan iqedsen di lǧameɛ, icerreg di tlemmast.

23:46 Sidna Ɛisa iɛeggeḍ s ṣṣut ɛlayen : A Baba Ṛebbi, atan ṛṛuḥ-iw ger ifassen-ik. Mi genna imeslayen-agi, yeffeɣ-it ṛṛuḥ.

23:47 Lqebṭan mi geẓra ayen yedṛan yețweḥḥid Ṛebbi yenna : ?-țideț, amdan-agi d aḥeqqi.

23:48 Lɣaci akk i gḥedṛen dinna, mi walan ayen yedṛan ḥeznen, uɣalen syenna kkaten deg idmaren-nsen.

23:49 Wid akk iḥemmlen Sidna Ɛisa qqimen deg lebɛid țmuqulen ayen yedṛan, llant gar-asen tilawin i d yeddan yid-es si tmurt n Jlili.

23:50 Yella yiwen wergaz ger lmuqedmin imeqqranen n usqamu n ccṛeɛ, isem-is Yusef, n taddart n Arimati n at Yahuda. Ur iqbil ṛṛay-nsen, ur iqbil lecɣal-nsen, d argaz n lɛali yerna d aḥeqqi, yețṛaǧu tageldit n Ṛebbi.

23:52 Iṛuḥ ɣer Bilaṭus yessuter-as lǧețța n Sidna Ɛisa.

23:53 Iṣubb-it-id seg umidag, yețțel-it di lekfen, yessers-it deg uẓekka yeɣzan deg wezṛu anda ur yemḍil ula d yiwen.

23:54 Ass-nni d ass n lǧemɛa i deg țheggin lɣaci iman-nsen i wass n ssebt yellan d ass n westeɛfu.

23:55 Tilawin-nni i d-yeddan d Sidna Ɛisa si tmurt n Jlili, ddant d Yusef ɣer uẓekka, walant amek i ssersen lǧețța-s.

23:56 Syenna uɣalent ɣer yexxamen nsent iwakken ad heggint leɛṭeṛ d wayen xeddmen i lmegtin, i lǧețța n Sidna Ɛisa. Azekka-nni n ssebt, steɛfant akken i d-tenna ccariɛa.

24:1 Ass n lḥedd ṣṣbeḥ zik, tilawin nni ṛuḥent ɣer uẓekka, wwint yid-sent leɛṭeṛ i d-heggant.

24:2 Ufant azṛu-nni iqeflen aẓekka, yegrareb akkin.

24:3 Mi kecment ɣer daxel, ur ufint ara lǧețța n Sidna Ɛisa ;

24:4 qqiment wehment. Atnaya ḍehṛen-asent-id sin yergazen s llebsa yețfeǧǧiǧen.

24:5 Ikcem itent lxuf, brant i wallen nsent ɣer lqaɛa ; irgazen-nni nnan-asent : Acuɣeṛ tețqellibemt deg uẓekka win yeddren ?

24:6 Ulac-it dagi, yeḥya-d si ger lmegtin. Ur tecfimt ara ɣef wayen i wen-d-yenna di tmurt n Jlili ?

24:7 « Ilaq Mmi-s n bunadem ad ițwasellem ger ifassen n yemcumen, ad ițusemmeṛ ɣef wumidag, ass wis tlata a d-iḥyu si ger lmegtin. »

24:8 Dɣa mmektant-ed imeslayen n Sidna Ɛisa.

24:9 Fɣent seg uẓekka iwakken ad ssiwḍent lexbaṛ ɣef wayen yedṛan, i ḥdac-nni yinelmaden d wid yellan yid-sen.

24:10 Llant gar-asent Meryem-nni tamagdalit, Yunna, Meryem yemma-s n Yeɛqub akk-d tidak nniḍen yeddan yid-sent ; ḥkant kullec i inelmaden.

24:11 Lameɛna ayen i sen-ḥkant iban asen-d am tmacahuț, ur tent-uminen ara.

24:12 Dɣa Buṭrus yekker yuzzel ɣer uẓekka. Mi gewweḍ, imuqel ɣer daxel, iwala anagar lekfen di lqaɛa. Yuɣal syenna, iwhem deg wayen yedṛan.

24:13 Ass-nni, sin inelmaden teddun ɣer yiwet n taddart isem-is Amawes, taddart-agi tebɛed ɣef temdint n Lquds azal n snat sswayeɛ n tikli.

24:14 ?eddun țmeslayen wway gar-asen ɣef wayen akk yedṛan.

24:15 Akken llan țmeslayen, Sidna Ɛisa iqeṛṛeb ɣuṛ-sen, yedda yid-sen.

24:16 Lameɛna nutni ur t-eɛqilen ara.

24:17 Yenna-yasen : ?ef wacu i tețmeslayem akka deg webrid ? D acu i d yesseɣlin fell-awen leḥzen ?

24:18 Yiwen deg-sen, isem-is Klufas, yenna-yas : Eɛni anagar kečč ur neẓri ara ayen yedṛan ussan-agi di temdint n Lquds ?

24:19 Nețța yenna-yasen : D acu yedṛan akka ? Rran-as : Ayen yedṛan d Ɛisa anaṣari nnbi-nni, yesɛan tazmert tameqqrant deg wayen ixeddem d wayen i d-iqqaṛ zdat Ṛebbi akk-d yemdanen.

24:20 Lmuqedmin imeqqranen d lḥekkam-nneɣ sellmen-t ɣer ifassen n iṛumaniyen, ḥekmen fell-as s lmut, semmṛen-t ɣef wumidag.

24:21 Nukni neḍmeɛ d nețța ara isellken wat Isṛail ; ass-agi d ass wis tlata segmi yedṛa wannect-a.

24:22 ?-țideț, kra si tilawin-nneɣ swehment-aɣ ! Mi ṛuḥent ṣṣbeḥ zik ɣer uẓekka

24:23 ur ufint ara lǧețța-s. Uɣalent-ed nnant-aɣ-ed belli ḍehṛen asent-ed snat n lmalayekkat i sent id-yennan « atan yedder. »

24:24 Kra seg-neɣ ṛuḥen ɣer uẓekka ufan kullec akken i t-id-nnant tilawin nni ; lameɛna nețța ur t-ẓrin ara.

24:25 Dɣa Sidna Ɛisa yenna-yasen : A wid iɣeflen ! Acimi tețɛeṭṭilem aț-țamnem s wayen i d-nnan lenbiya ?

24:26 Eɛni ur ilaq ara ad yenɛețțab Lmasiḥ akka uqbel ad yekcem di tmanegt-is ?

24:27 Dɣa yessefhem-asen ayen meṛṛa yuran fell-as di tira iqedsen ; yebda-d si tektabin n Sidna Musa, armi ț-țid n lenbiya meṛṛa.

24:28 Mi qṛib ad awḍen ɣer taddart i ɣer teddun, Sidna Ɛisa yerra iman-is am akken yebɣa ad ikemmel abrid-is.

24:29 Lameɛna nutni ḥellelen-t nnan-as : Qqim yid-nneɣ, atan ț-țameddit, qṛib a d-yeɣli yiḍ. Sidna Ɛisa ikcem ad yeqqim yid-sen.

24:30 Mi qqimen ɣer imensi, yeddem ed aɣṛum, yeḥmed Ṛebbi, yebḍa-t, ifṛeq-asen-t.

24:31 Dɣa ldint-ed wallen nsen, ɛeqlen-t, imiren kan yețwaṛfeɛ gar-asen.

24:32 Bdan qqaṛen wway gar-asen : Ur nḥuss ara i kra iṛeqqen deg wulawen-nneɣ mi ɣ-d-iheddeṛ deg ubrid yerna yessefham-aɣ-d ayen yellan di tira iqedsen ?

24:33 Imiren kan, kkren-d uɣalen ɣer temdint n Lquds. Ufan ḥdac-nni inelmaden akk-d imdukkal-nsen nnejmaɛen.

24:34 Nnan-asen-d : ?-țideț Sidna Ɛisa iḥya-d, yesban ed iman-is i Semɛun.

24:35 Nutni daɣen bdan ḥekkun-asen ayen yedṛan yid-sen deg webrid d wamek i t-ɛeqlen mi sen-ifṛeq aɣṛum.

24:36 Mazal-iten țmeslayen, Sidna Ɛisa s yiman-is ibedd-ed gar-asen. Yenna yasen : Sslam fell-awen.

24:37 Ikcem-iten akk lxuf, ɣilen d lexyal i d-ibedden ɣuṛ-sen.

24:38 Nețța yenna-yasen : Acuɣeṛ i tuggadem ? D acu-t ccekk-agi deg ulawen-nwen ?

24:39 Walit ifassen-iw akk-d iḍaṛṛen iw, d nekk s yiman-iw ! Nnalet-iyi-d, walit ! Lexyal ur yesɛi aksum ur yesɛi iɣsan, akken tețwalim sɛiɣ-ten !

24:40 Mi sen-yenna ayagi, yesken-asen ifassen-is d iḍaṛṛen-is.

24:41 Wehmen, ččuṛen wulawen-nsen d lfeṛḥ armi ur zmiren ara ad amnen ayen țwalin, dɣa yenna-yasen : Yella dagi wayen ara ččeɣ ?

24:42 Fkan-as-d aftat n lḥut ikenfen.

24:43 Iddem-it-id, yečča-t zdat-sen.

24:44 Yenna yasen : Atah wayen i wen-d-nniɣ mi lliɣ yid-wen : « ilaq ad yedṛu wayen akk yuran fell-i di ccariɛa n Musa, d lenbiya akk-d ?abur ».

24:45 Dɣa yeldi-yasen leɛqel iwakken ad fehmen tira iqedsen,

24:46 yenna yasen : Atah wayen yuran tira iqedsen: « ilaq Lmasiḥ ad yeɛteb, a d-iḥyu ass wis tlata si ger lmegtin,

24:47 țțuba d leɛfu n ddnubat ad țwabeccṛen s yisem-is i leǧnas meṛṛa, a d-bdun si temdint n Lquds ».

24:48 Aql-ikkun d inigan-iw.

24:49 Atan a d-ceggɛeɣ fell-awen wayen i wen yewɛed Baba Ṛebbi. Qqimet di temdint-agi alamma ters-ed fell-awen tezmert n Ṛebbi ara d-yasen seg igenni.

24:50 Sidna Ɛisa yewwi inelmaden-is armi d leǧwahi n taddart n Bitani. Yerfed ifassen-is, iburek-iten.

24:51 Di teswiɛt-nni yemfaṛaq yid-sen, yețwarfed ɣer igenni,

24:52 ma d nutni ɣlin ɣef tgecrar, țḥemmiden-t ; imiren uɣalen ɣer temdint n Lquds s lfeṛḥ.

24:53 Yal ass țțilin deg wufrag n lǧameɛ iqedsen, țḥemmiden Sidi Ṛebbi.
