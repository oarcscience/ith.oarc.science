Kabyle Bible: 1 Peter

1:1 Nekk Buṭrus, amceggeɛ n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, i yeɣṛiben ițɛicin di tmurt n Galasya, n Kafadusya, n Asya, n Bitinya akk-d wid yellan di tmurt n tqenṭert.

1:2 Akken i t-iqsed Baba Ṛebbi, tețwaxtaṛem si tazwara s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen iwakken aț-țḍuɛem Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, a kkun-iṣfu s idammen-is yuzzlen. Ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a wen-d tețțunefkent s tugeț.

1:3 Ad ițțubarek Ṛebbi, Baba-s n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i ɣ-d-ifkan s ṛṛeḥma-ines tudert tajḍiṭ s ḥeggu n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ si ger lmegtin, s wakka i nesɛa asirem ameqqran,

1:4 iwakken a newṛet ayen ur nrekku, ur nețțames, ur nfennu. D annect-agi i kkun-ițṛaǧun deg igenwan kunwi yumnen yis.

1:5 Sidi Ṛebbi ițḥadar fell-awen s tezmert-is alamma iban-ed leslak nwen di taggara.

1:6 D ayagi i kkun-issefṛaḥen ɣas akken tura sseḥzanent-kkun teswiɛin agi deg ilaq a d-tɛeddim seg ujeṛṛeb.

1:7 D uguren-agi ara d-isbeggnen lǧehd n liman-nwen i gesɛan azal akteṛ n ddheb ; ɣas akken ddheb-agi ifennu, ilaq-as ad iɛeddi di tmes iwakken ad iṣfu. Akken daɣen ula d liman-nwen ilaq ad ițțujeṛṛeb ; s wakka a wen-yili d sebba n ccan, n ucekkeṛ, akk-d tmanegt asm'ara d-yuɣal Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:8 Tḥemmlem-t mbla ma twalam-t, tumnem yis ɣas akken ar tura ur t-teẓrim ara. Daymi lfeṛḥ-nwen d ameqqran armi ur tezmirem ara a t-id-tesfehmem s imeslayen.

1:9 Axaṭeṛ lfayda n liman-nwen, d leslak n teṛwiḥin-nwen.

1:10  Lenbiya caren-d ɣef wayen yeɛnan leslak i wen-d-ihegga Sidi Ṛebbi. Meyzen, qellben a d-afen lawan d wamek ara d-idṛu wayen i d-ixebbeṛ Ṛṛuḥ n Lmasiḥ yellan deg-sen ; axaṭer d Ṛṛuḥ-agi n Lmasiḥ i d-ixebbṛen ɣef wayen ara inneɛtab Lmasiḥ akk-d tmanegt ara d-yasen deffir leɛtab-agi.

1:12 Ițțubeggen-asen-d belli mačči i nutni, meɛna i kunwi i d-xebbṛen ayagi. D ayen i wen-d-xebbṛen tura wid ițbecciṛen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ, s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i d-ițțucegɛen seg igenni. Ula d lmalayekkat bɣant ad walint annect-agi.

1:13 Daymi, sǧehdet leɛqel-nwen, ur ɣefflet ara, ɛasset iman-nwen, sɛut asirem iṣeḥḥan di leslak ara wen d-ițțunefken asm' ara d-yuɣal Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:14 Ilit am arrac ițțaɣen awal, ur xeddmet ara ayen akk i txeddmem asm' akken werɛad i kkun-tekcim tmusni,

1:15 meɛna ilit teṣfam di tikli-nwen am akken iṣfa Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-d issawlen.

1:16 Imi yura : Ilit teṣfam axaṭer nekk ṣfiɣ.

1:17 Ma tnedhem ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi am akken d Baba-twen, sbeggnet-ed di tudert-nwen belli tḍuɛem-t s leqdeṛ ameqqran, deg wussan i wen-d iqqimen di ddunit-agi ; nețța ur nxeddem lxilaf ger yemdanen, yețḥasaben yal yiwen s wakken llan lecɣal-is.

1:18 Teẓram belli mačči s idrimen neɣ s ddheb neɣ s lfeṭṭa ur nețdum ara i tețwacafɛem si yir tikli i wen-d-ǧǧan lejdud-nwen,

1:19 meɛna s idammen ɣlayen n Lmasiḥ, i gefkan iman-is d asfel am izimer ur nesɛi lɛib, ur nesɛi ccama.

1:20 Ihegga-t-id Sidi Ṛebbi uqbel a d-texleq ddunit, meɛna armi d ussan-agi ineggura i t-id-isbeggen ɣef ddemma-nwen.

1:21 Imi yis i tesɛam liman ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi i t-id-isseḥyan si ger lmegtin, i s-ifkan tamanegt tameqqrant, iwakken lsas n usirem-nwen akk-d liman-nwen ad ilin di Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:22 Imi tessazdgem ulawen-nwen, teddam di tideț, akken aț-țḥemmlem seg wul atmaten-nwen, myeḥmalet ihi wway gar-awen s wul yeṣfan

1:23 axaṭer tɛawdem-d talalit ɣer tudert tajḍiṭ. Talalit-agi mačči seg wemdan ițmețțaten i d-tekka, meɛna seg wawal n tudert ițdumun n Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:24 Akken yella di tira iqedsen : Amdan am leḥcic, + ccan-is meṛṛa am ujeǧǧig n lexla,+ leḥcic ițɣaṛ, ajeǧǧig iɣelli,+

1:25 ma d awal n Sidi Ṛebbi+ ițdumu i dayem+ . Awal-agi, d awal n lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wen-d-ițțubecṛen.

2:1 Kkset seg ulawen-nwen cceṛ, tiḥila, tismin ; ur seɛɛut ara sin wudmawen, ur țmeslayet ara ayen n diri ɣef wiyaḍ.

2:2 Am llufan illuẓen ayefki n yemma-s, nadit ula d kunwi aț-țeṛwum awal n Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken aț-țimɣuṛem, aț-tețțuselkem,

2:3 ma yella tjeṛbem acḥal yelha Sidi Ṛebbi !

2:4 Qeṛṛbet ɣuṛ-es nețța yellan d-azṛu nni yeddren i ḍeggṛen yemdanen, meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi ixtaṛ-it, axaṭer azal-is meqqeṛ.

2:5 Ula d kunwi yellan am izṛa yeddren, dduklet iwakken ad ibedd wexxam i deg ara izdeɣ Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi. Aț-țilim ț-țajmaɛt n lmuqedmin ara iqeddmen s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, iseflawen iqedsen ara iɛeǧben i Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:6 Axaṭer atan wayen yuran di tira iqedsen : Xtaṛeɣ-ed azṛu+ yesɛan azal d ameqqran+ , sserseɣ-t deg udrar n Siyun,+ d nețța i geṭṭfen lebni,+ win ara yamnen yis ur ineddem ara.+

2:7 Amarezg-nwen kunwi yumnen yis. Ma d wid ur numin ara : Adɣaɣ-nni ḍeggṛen wid ibennun,+ d nețța i guɣalen d azṛu alemmas,+ Win yeṭṭfen lebni. +

2:8 D wagi i d azṛu + i guɣalen d ugur i yemdanen, + d azṛu i ten-isseɣlayen+ . Imi ugin ad amnen s wawal n Sidi Ṛebbi, ayen i ten-ițṛaǧun d ugur-agi ara ten-iɣeḍlen.

2:9 Kunwi d arraw n laṣel ițwaxtaṛen, d lmuqedmin n ugellid, d lǧens iṣfan, d agdud i d-ițwacefɛen. Tețwaxtaṛem iwakken aț-țbecṛem lecɣal yelhan n Sidi Ṛebbi, i kkun-id issufɣen si ṭṭlam yewwi-kkun-id ɣer tafat-is isteɛǧiben.

2:10 Zik-nni ur tewwiḍem ara ula d lǧens, ma ț-țura tuɣalem d agdud n Sidi Ṛebbi ; tellam zik-nni ur tesɛim ara ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi ma ț-țura tețțunefk-awen-d.

2:11 A wid eɛzizen ! Kunwi yellan d ibeṛṛaniyen, d imsebriden di ddunit a kkun-nhuɣ aț-țettixṛem i lebɣi n tnefsit i gețnaɣen d ṛṛuḥ-nwen.

2:12 Sɛut tikli ilhan ger wid ur nessin ara Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken ɣas a d-skidben fell-awen, neɣ xedmen awen cceṛ ; m' ara walin lecɣal-nwen ilhan ad ḥemden Sidi Ṛebbi asm' ara d-yerzu ɣuṛ-sen.

2:13 Ɣef ddemma n Sidi Ṛebbi qadṛet wid iḥekmen. Qadṛet agellid imi d nețța i gḥekmen,

2:14 qadṛet ineɣlafen-is imi d nețța i ten-isbedden iwakken ad ɛaqben imcumen, ad cekkṛen wid i gțeddun s lḥeqq.

2:15 Xedmet ayen yelhan iwakken aț-țerrem takmamt i imejhal ur nefhim ; d wagi i d lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:16 Ilit d ilelliyen, meɛna ur ssexdamet ara tilelli-nwen iwakken aț-țɣummem cceṛ i txeddmem, ilit d iqeddacen n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:17 Qadṛet imdanen meṛṛa ; ḥemmlet atmaten nwen, ḍuɛet Sidi Ṛebbi, qadṛet agellid.

2:18 Ay iqeddacen ! Ilaq aț-qadṛem imɛellmen nwen, aɣet-asen awal ama i wid ilhan ḥninen, ama i wid iweɛṛen.

2:19 D ayen ilhan i win iṣebbṛen m' ara iɛeddi fell-as lbaṭel ilmend n ṭṭaɛa n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:20 Ma tețwaɛaqbem imi txedmem kra n diri, ma tṣebṛem i wannect-agi, anwa ara kkun-icekkṛen ? Meɛna ma iɛedda fell-awen lbaṭel, mi txedmem ayen yelhan, tṣebṛem, d ayen yelhan ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:21 Ɣer wannect-agi i wen-d-issawel Sidi Ṛebbi, axaṭer Lmasiḥ s yiman-is inneɛtab fell-awen, iǧǧa-yawen-d lemtel iwakken aț țeddum di lateṛ-is.

2:22 Nețța ur nednib, leɛmeṛ ur d-iffiɣ lekdeb seg imi-s.

2:23 Nețța ițwaregmen ur d-yerri rregmat, qehṛen-t ur d-irfid afus-is ɣer yiwen ; lameɛna yerra kullec ger ifassen n win i gḥekmen s lḥeqq.

2:24 Nețța i gɛebban ddnubat-nneɣ, ițwasemmeṛ ɣef wumidag iwakken a nili am wid yemmuten ɣef wayen yeɛnan ddnub axaṭer ddnub ur isɛi ara tazmert ɣef wid immuten. S wakka, tura ur nețɛici ara di ddnub meɛna di lḥeqq, akken yura : Di leǧruḥ-is i tufam ḥellu. +

2:25 Zik tellam am ulli iɛeṛqen, ma ț-țura, tuɣalem ɣer umeksa ițɛassan ɣef teṛwiḥin-nwen.

3:1 Kunemti daɣen a tilawin ḍuɛemt irgazen-nkunt, iwakken ɣas ma llan kra n yergazen ur uminen ara s wawal n Sidi Ṛebbi, s tikli ilhan n tilawin-nsen,

3:2 ad amnen mbla imeslayen m' ara walin tikli-nsent teṣfa, tesɛa leqdeṛ.

3:3 Ur țzewwiqemt ara iman-nkunt s ufella kan, ama s ddheb, neɣ s llebsa ifazen, neɣ s wemcaḍ n ucekkuɛ nkunt,

3:4 meɛna ccbaḥa-nkunt ilaq aț-țili deg wulawen-nkunt ; axaṭer ayen yesɛan azal ameqqran ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi : ț-țezdeg n wul, lehna d leḥnana, imi ayagi d ayen ițdumun.

3:5 Akka i țcebbiḥent iman-nsent tilawin iḍuɛen Sidi Ṛebbi, yețqadaṛen irgazen-nsent ;

3:6 am Saṛa iḍuɛen argaz-is Ibṛahim, tessawal-as « A Sidi. » Aț-țuɣalemt d yessi-s ma txeddmemt ayen yelhan mbla tugdi.

3:7 Ma d kunwi ay irgazen, ḥadret tilawin-nwen imi teẓram ur ǧhident ara am kunwi. Ilaq a tent-tqadṛem axaṭer nutenti daɣen ad weṛtent yid-wen ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi ; s wakka ur d-ițili ara ẓẓeṛb i tẓallit-nwen.

3:8 Ihi sɛut akk yiwet n lɛeqliya, myeḥmalet wway gar-awen. Sɛut tagmaț, iḥninet, ddut s wannuz.

3:9 Ur țțarat ara țțaṛ, ur țarrat ara rregmat i wid i kkun-iregmen ; meɛna ddɛut s lxiṛ i wiyaḍ axaṭer d annect-agi i gețṛaǧu deg-wen Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken aț-țweṛtem lbaṛaka-ines.

3:10  Win iḥemmlen tudert, + ibɣan ad yili d aseɛdi deg ussan-is :+ + + Ad iḥader iles-is ɣef wayen n diri+ d yimi-s seg yimeslayen ițɣuṛṛun+ .

3:11 Ad issebɛed iman-is ɣef cceṛ, + ad ixeddem ayen yelhan,+ ad ițnadi amek ara yili dayem di talwit,+ +

3:12 axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi ṛeṣṣant wallen-is ɣef yiḥeqqiyen, + + isell-ed i tiɣṛi-nsen ; + meɛna ițeffer udem-is + ɣef wid ixeddmen cceṛ.

3:13 Anwa ara wen-ibɣun cceṛ ma yella tḥemmlem aț-țxedmem anagar ayen yelhan ?

3:14 Yerna ma yella tețwaḍelmem ɣef lḥeqq, feṛḥet ! Ur țțagadet ara imdanen, ur tḥeyyiṛet ara.

3:15 Ɛuzzet deg ulawen-nwen Lmasiḥ yellan d Ssid-nwen. Ilit dayem twejdem aț-țǧawbem s leɛqel d leqdeṛ i kra n wid i kkun-id-isteqsan ɣef wusirem nwen di Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken ad ithedden wul-nwen ;

3:16 imiren wid i d-iskiddiben ɣef tikli-nwen ilhan deg ubrid n Lmasiḥ, ad nneḥcamen.

3:17 Axiṛ aț-țenṭaṛṛem ma txedmem ayen yelhan di lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi, wala aț-țenṭaṛṛem ma txedmem ayen n diri.

3:18 Lmasiḥ s yiman-is immut ; s lmut-agi-ines i gefra tamsalt n ddnub n yemdanen. Nețța yellan d aḥeqqi, immut ɣef ddemma n imednuben meṛṛa iwakken a kkun-yawi ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi. Nɣan lgețța-s meɛna yuɣal-ed ɣer tudert s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

3:19 S tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i gṛuḥ ad ibecceṛ i leṛwaḥ ițwaḥebsen,

3:20 yugin ad amnen, asm' akken i ten-ițṛaǧu Sidi Ṛebbi s ṣṣbeṛ akken a d-uɣalen ɣer webrid-is ; asmi i gexdem Sidna Nuḥ lbabuṛ i ɣer kecmen anagar tmanya yemdanen i gmenɛen si lḥemla n waman.

3:21 Aman-agi, d lemtel n weɣḍas i kkun-ițselliken ass-a ula d kunwi. Aɣḍas-agi n waman mačči d ayen i gessizdigen lǧețța n wemdan, lameɛna ițbeggin-ed belli nefka tudert-nneɣ i Sidi Ṛebbi s wul yeṣfan. Ayagi yezmer a d-yedṛu i lmend n ḥeggu n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ

3:22 i gulin ɣer igenni, yeṭṭef amkan n lḥekma ɣer tama tayeffust n Sidi Ṛebbi, sennig n lmalayekkat, sennig n tezmar d lḥekmat meṛṛa.

4:1 Am akken inneɛtab Lmasiḥ di lǧețța-s, kunwi daɣen ilaq-awen aț-țheggim iman-nwen ; axaṭer win i genneɛtaben di lǧețța-s iṭṭaxeṛ ɣef ddnub ;

4:2 iwakken ussan i s-d-iqqimen di tudert-is, ad iddu s lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi, mačči akken ibɣa nețța.

4:3 Beṛka-kkun tura ! Tellam tețțeddum d wid ijehlen, ur nessin Sidi Ṛebbi : tellam d isekṛanen, tețțeddum deg webrid n lefsad d zzhu, tɛebdem lemqamat, ulawen nwen ččuṛen d ṭṭmeɛ.

4:4 Tura wehmen imi teṭṭaxṛem fell-asen ur tețțeddum ara deg webrid nsen i gețțawin ɣer nnger, daymi i kkaten deg-wen.

4:5 Lameɛna a ten-iḥaseb Sidi Ṛebbi, nețța yețḥasaben wid iddren d wid yemmuten.

4:6 S wakka, lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ițțubecceṛ ula i wid yemmuten iwakken ɣas țțuḥasben am imdanen n ddunit meṛṛa, ad idiren s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen akken ibɣa Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:7 Taggara n ddunit tețțeddu-d ! Ɣef wannect-a, sɛut leɛqel, ɛasset ɣef yiman-nwen iwakken aț-țizmirem aț-țeẓẓallem.

4:8 Lḥaǧa tamezwarut, myeḥmalet wway gar-awen seg wul, axaṭer leḥmala tețsamaḥ aṭas n ddnubat.

4:9 Țmesṭerḥabet wway gar-awen s wul iṣfan.

4:10 Mkul yiwen deg-wen d acu n tikci i s-d-ifka Sidi Ṛebbi, ihi sxedmet-eț iwakken ad sfaydin wiyaḍ seg-s.

4:11 Win i wumi i d-yețțunefk ad ihḍeṛ, ilaq ad ihḍeṛ s imeslayen i s-d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi. Akken daɣen ma yella wayeḍ ițțunefk-as-ed ad iqdec, ad iqdec ɣef wiyaḍ s tezmert i s-d-ifka Sidi Ṛebbi. Iwakken di yal ccɣel, ad ițwaḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi s yisem n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, bab n tezmert ț-țmanegt si lǧil ɣer lǧil. Amin !

4:12 A wid eɛzizen ! Ur wehhmet ara ɣef wujeṛṛeb i d-iɣellin fell-awen am tmes, ur xellɛet ara, axaṭer ayagi ilaq a d-idṛu.

4:13 Lameɛna feṛḥet imi i tețțekkim di leɛtab n Lmasiḥ, axaṭer asm' ara d-tban tmanegt-is, aț-țfeṛḥem yid-es lfeṛḥ d ameqqran.

4:14 Amarezg-nwen ma yella iɛedda fell-awen lbaṭel imi tumnem s Lmasiḥ ! Axaṭer Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen iččuṛen ț-țamanegt, Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi yella deg-wen !

4:15 Ḥadret a d-yili gar-awen win ara ițwaqehṛen axaṭer yenɣa tamgeṛṭ neɣ yuker, neɣ iger iman-is di lecɣal n wiyaḍ ;

4:16 lameɛna ma yella ițwaqheṛ imi yumen s Lmasiḥ, ur ilaq ara ad inneḥcam ; ilaq ad iḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef yisem-agi.

4:17 Yewweḍ-ed lweqt n lḥisab n Sidi Ṛebbi ; yerna ad ibdu seg wat wexxam-is. Ihi ma yella seg-neɣ ara ibdu lḥisab, amek ara tili taggara n wid ur numin ara s lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi ?

4:18 Ma yella iwɛeṛ-as i uḥeqqi iwakken ad ițwasellek, amek ara tedṛu ihi d wemcum ur numin ara s Sidi Ṛebbi ?

4:19 Ma yella s lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi i genneɛtab yiwen ; ilaq ad yerr kullec ger ifassen n win i t-id-ixelqen, ad ițkel fell-as, ad ikemmel di lecɣal-is ilhan.

5:1 Ad nhuɣ tura imeqqranen di liman yellan gar-awen. Nekk yellan d ameqqran am nutni, nekk iḥedṛen i leɛtab n Lmasiḥ, ițekkan di tmanegt-is ara d-ibanen ; a ten weṣṣiɣ :

5:2 Ḥadret wid i wen-d-iwekkel Sidi Ṛebbi akken ițḥadar umeksa ɣef qeḍɛit-is ; lhit ihi yid-sen s lebɣi-nwen, mačči sennig n wulawen-nwen, xedmet ayagi ɣef wudem n Sidi Ṛebbi mačči s ṭṭmeɛ.

5:3 Ur ssimɣuṛet ara iman-nwen ɣef wid i wen-d-iwekkel Sidi Ṛebbi, meɛna ilit d lemtel ara tebɛen.

5:4 Imiren asm' ara d-iban win yellan sennig imeqqranen meṛṛa, a wen d-tețțunefk tɛeṣṣabt n tmanegt ur nfennu.

5:5 Ula d kunwi daɣen ay ilmeẓyen, qadṛet imeqqranen di liman ! Ddut s leqdeṛ d wannuz, axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi ur iqebbel ara wid ițzuxxun, meɛna ițțak ṛṛeḥma-s i wid ițțanzen.

5:6 Anzet ihi seddaw ufus iǧehden n Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken a kkun-irfed di teswiɛt ilaqen.

5:7 Rret akk iɣeblan-nwen ger ifassen-is, axaṭer ițḥebbiṛ fell-awen.

5:8 Ḥadret, Ɛasset iman-nwen axaṭer aɛdaw-nwen d Cciṭan, ițezzi iṛeɛɛed am yizem, ițqellib anwa ara issebleɛ.

5:9 Ur s-țaǧǧat ara abrid, sɛut liman iǧehden ; axaṭer teẓram belli atmaten nwen di ddunit meṛṛa, akka i tdeṛṛu yid-sen am kunwi.

5:10 M'ara tenneɛtabem kra n wussan, Sidi Ṛebbi Bab n mkul ṛṛeḥma i kkun-ixtaṛen s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ iwakken aț-țilim di tmanegt-is ițdumun, a kkun-iheggi, a kkun iseggem, a kkun-isseǧhed, iwakken ur tɣellim ara.

5:11 I nețța lḥekma si lǧil ɣer lǧil. Amin !

5:12 S ufus n gma-tneɣ eɛzizen Silwan i ɣef nețkel, i wen-d-uriɣ kra n imeslayen-agi iwakken a kkun-nhuɣ yerna ad cehdeɣ belli ț-țagi i d ṛṛeḥma n tideț n Sidi Ṛebbi s wayes tumnem.

5:13 Tajmaɛt n watmaten yumnen s Lmasiḥ, yellan di temdint n Babilun țsellimen-d fell-awen ; ula d mmi Maṛqus ițsellim fell-awen.

5:14 Msalamet wway gar-awen am atmaten ; talwit a d-ters fell-awen kunwi yumnen s Lmasiḥ.
