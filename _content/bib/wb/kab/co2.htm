Kabyle Bible: 2 Corinthians

1:1 Nekk Bulus amceggeɛ n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ s lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi, akk-d gma-tneɣ Timuti, i watmaten n tejmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi yellan di temdint n Kurintus akk-d wid meṛṛa i gextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi di mkul amkan n tmurt n Akaya.

1:2 Ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a wen-d țțunefkent s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi Baba tneɣ akk-d Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:3 Ad ițțubarek Sidi Ṛebbi, Baba-s n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ yellan di yal taswiɛt d Bab n leḥnana d ṣṣbeṛ.

1:4 Ițṣebbiṛ-aɣ di lemḥayen-nneɣ iwakken s ṣṣbeṛ i ɣ-d-ifka, a nizmir ula d nukni a nṣebbeṛ wid yellan di lemḥayen.

1:5 Axaṭer am akken i d-țeɣlayent fell-aɣ lemḥayen ɣef ddemma n Lmasiḥ, akken daɣen i ɣ-d-ițțas ṣṣbeṛ s ɣuṛ-es.

1:6 Nukni ma nenɛețțab di lmeḥnat, nțenɛețțab iwakken aț-țețțusebbṛem yerna aț-țețțuselkem. Ma nețțuṣebbeṛ nukni, aț-țețțuṣebbṛem ula d kunwi iwakken aț-țesɛum ṣṣbeṛ di lemḥayen i deg tețɛeddayem am nukni.

1:7 Ihi ɣef wayen i kkun-yeɛnan nețkel, nekkes anezgum, axaṭer neẓra akken i tețțekkim di lemḥayen-nneɣ, ara tețțekkim daɣen di ṣṣbeṛ i ɣ-d ițțunefken.

1:8 Nebɣa aț-țeẓrem ay atmaten lmeḥna i nesɛedda di tmurt n Asya; ɣlint-ed fell-aɣ lemḥayen iwumi ur nezmir, neɛteb armi nɣil a nemmet.

1:9 Nețwali deg iman-nneɣ belli ḥekmen fell-aɣ s lmut, iwakken ur nețkal ara ɣef yiman-nneɣ lameɛna a nețkel ɣef Ṛebbi i d-iḥeggun lmegtin.

1:10 D nețța i ɣ-d-isellken si lmut am tagi yerna nețkel belli mazal a ɣ-isellek sya ɣer zdat.

1:11 Ula d kunwi tețɛawanem-aɣ m'ara tdeɛɛum ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-aɣ. S wakka, ɣef ddemma n tẓallit n waṭas n watmaten, a ɣ-d-țțunefkent lbaṛakat s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ; imiren aṭas ara iḥemden Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef ddemma nneɣ.

1:12 Ațah sebba s wayes i nefṛeḥ s yiman-nneɣ yerna ulawen-nneɣ thennan : nelḥa d yemdanen meṛṛa s wul yeṣfan d neyya, abeɛda yid wen kunwi. Ayagi yusa-yaɣ-d s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, axaṭer mačči s lɛeqliya n yemdanen i nțeddu meɛna d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣ-ițwellihen.

1:13  Ayen i wen-nura di tebṛatin- nneɣ, nenna-t-id s wul yeṣfan d neyya, ulac deg-sent lmeɛna nniḍen anagar ayen i teɣṛam d wayen i tfehmem deg-sent. Ssarameɣ aț-țfehmem ayagi akken ilaq am akken i tebdam tfehmem-t, yeɛni ass n tuɣalin n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ a nili d sebba n lfeṛḥ-nwen, kunwi aț-țilim d sebba n lfeṛḥ-nneɣ.

1:15 Imi tḥeqqeqeɣ belli akka i gella lḥal, ssarmeɣ a n-ɛeddiɣ di tazwara ɣuṛ-wen iwakken aț-țfeṛḥem snat n tikkal.

1:16 Qesdeɣ ad ɛeddiɣ fell-awen m'ara ṛuḥeɣ ɣer tmurt n Masidunya, syinna a d-uɣaleɣ ɣuṛ-wen iwakken a yi-tɛiwnem ad kemmleɣ abrid-iw ɣer tmurt n Yahuda.

1:17 Mi qesdeɣ ad xedmeɣ annect-agi, eɛni ur xemmemeɣ ara ? Neɣ m'ara qesdeɣ ad xedmeɣ kra, ma nniɣ-ed anɛam neɣ ala uɣaleɣ beddleɣ ṛṛay, eɛni d lebɣi-inu i țnadiɣ ?

1:18 Ṛebbi yeẓra belli ayen i wen-d-neqqaṛ mačči d anɛam akk-d ala ɣef yiwet n tikkelt ;

1:19 axaṭer Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Mmi-s n Ṛebbi i nbecceṛ gar-awen nekk d watmaten Silwan akk-d Timuti, ur d-yusi ara iwakken ad yili d anɛam akk-d ala, meɛna anagar anɛam i gellan deg-s.

1:20 Axaṭer yis i d-iqqaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi anɛam i wayen meṛṛa i gewɛed. Ihi s yisem n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i d-neqqaṛ daɣen amin i Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken a t-neḥmed a t-ncekkeṛ.

1:21 D Sidi Ṛebbi s yiman-is i ɣ isǧehden yid-wen di tikli-nneɣ akk-d Lmasiḥ, d nețța i ɣ-ixtaṛen.

1:22 Yessers fell-aɣ ṭṭabeɛ-ines yerna yefka-yaɣ-d deg ulawen-nneɣ Ṛṛuḥ-is iqedsen yellan d aɛeṛbun n lbaṛakat i ɣ-iwɛed.

1:23 Atan Sidi Ṛebbi yeẓra belli ț-țideț i d-qqaṛeɣ : mi qesdeɣ ur țțuɣaleɣ ara ɣer temdint n Kurintus, d aḥader i bɣiɣ a kkun-ḥadreɣ.

1:24 Ur nețnadi ara a wen-d-nesken ayen s wayes ara tamnem axaṭer neẓra tǧehdem di liman, lameɛna nebɣa a nexdem yid-wen iwakken a ndukkel di lfeṛḥ.

2:1 Ihi qesdeɣ ur n-țuɣaleɣ ara ɣuṛ wen, imi ur bɣiɣ ara a kkun sḥezneɣ daɣen.

2:2 Axaṭer ma sḥezneɣ kkun, anwa ara yi-isfeṛḥen ma yella mačči d kunwi ?

2:3 Uggadeɣ a yi-isḥeznen kra seg wid i glaq a yi-isfeṛḥen, daymi i wen-d-zzewreɣ tabṛaț-nni, axaṭer ẓriɣ ma yella feṛḥeɣ aț-țfeṛḥem ula d kunwi.

2:4 Uriɣ-awen tabṛaț-nni s imeṭṭawen d leḥzen ameqqran akk-d wul yetqelqen, mačči iwakken aț țḥeznem lameɛna iwakken aț-țeẓrem acḥal i kkun-ḥemmleɣ.

2:5 Ma yella gar-awen win yellan d sebba n leḥzen, mačči d nekk i gesseḥzen lameɛna d kunwi meṛṛa i gesseḥzen, yeɛni bɣiɣ a d-iniɣ amur ameqqran deg-wen.

2:6 Amdan-agi tlumm-it meṛṛa tejmaɛt, beṛka-t !

2:7 Tura, ilaq-awen kan a s-tsamḥem yerna a t-tesǧehdem, neɣ m'ulac a d iɣli fell-as leḥzen d ameqqran ara t-isfeclen.

2:8 Ihi a kkun-nhuɣ a s-d-beggnem belli tḥemmlem-t.

2:9 Uriɣ-awen ayagi iwakken ad ẓreɣ ma tețțaɣem awal di kullec.

2:10 Win iwumi ara tsamḥem, a s-samḥeɣ ula d nekk ; ma yella wayen i ɣef ara samḥeɣ i yiwen, ɣef ddemma-nwen i s-sumḥeɣ zdat Lmasiḥ ;

2:11 s wakka ur nețțaǧa ara Cciṭan a ɣ-iɣleb, axaṭer nessen lmeqṣud-is.

2:12 Mi wwḍeɣ ɣer temdint n Truwas iwakken ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ, Sidi Ṛebbi yeldi-yi-d tiwwura iwakken ad beccṛeɣ.

2:13 Lameɛna ur yethenna ara wul-iw imi ur ufiɣ ara dinna gma-tneɣ Tit ; daymi ǧǧiɣ atmaten n temdint n Truwas, imiren ṛuḥeɣ ɣer tmurt n Masidunya.

2:14 A neḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi a t-ncekkeṛ imi s Lmasiḥ i nɣelleb kullec yerna yerra-yaɣ d iqeddacen-is iwakken a nbecceṛ Lmasiḥ di mkul amkan am rriḥa taẓiḍant.

2:15 ?er Sidi Ṛebbi, d nukni i d rriḥa taẓiḍant n Lmasiḥ ger wid ițwasellken yellan deg webrid n Sidna Ɛisa akk-d wid iḍaɛen.

2:16 I wid iḍaɛen, d ṛṛiḥa n lmut i d-ițțaken lmut ; i wid yellan deg webrid n leslak, d rriḥa n tudert i d ițțaken tudert. Anwa ihi i gzemren ad ixdem ccɣel am wagi ?

2:17 Nukni ur nețḥerrif ara awal n Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken a netjaṛ yis am akken xeddmen waṭas n yemdanen, lameɛna zdat Sidi Ṛebbi, ț-țideț i d-neqqaṛ mi nețbecciṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ i ɣ-d-yekkan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:1 D acekkeṛ i nebɣa a d-ncekkeṛ iman-nneɣ mi d-nenna imeslayen agi ? Eɛni neḥwaǧ ula d nukni a wen-nawi tibṛatin n lewṣaya neɣ a tent-id-nawi s ɣuṛ-wen am akken xeddmen kra n yemdanen ?

3:2 Tabṛaț-nneɣ, d kunwi s yiman nwen ; ț-țabṛaț yuran deg ulawen nneɣ, tețwassen yerna ɣṛan-ț akk yemdanen.

3:3 Iban belli tabṛaț-agi yura-ț Lmasiḥ s yiman-is mi wen-d nbecceṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ; mačči s lmidad i ț-yura lameɛna s Ṛṛuḥ n Ṛebbi yeddren, mačči ɣef teblaḍin n wezṛu i ț-ijerred lameɛna deg ulawen-nwen.

3:4 Lețkal-agi i nesɛa deg wayen i d-nenna, yekka-d s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, s Lmasiḥ.

3:5 Ur nezmir ara a d-nini belli d nukni i gxeddmen ccɣel-agi s tezmert-nneɣ lameɛna d Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣ-d-ițțaken tazmert deg wayen akk i nxeddem.

3:6 D nețța i ɣ-d-ifkan tazmert s wayes i nuɣal d iqeddacen n leɛqed ajdid . Leɛqed-agi ur d-yekki ara seg wayen yuran di ccariɛa lameɛna s ɣuṛ Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen. Axaṭer ccariɛa tețțawi ɣer lmut, ma d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yețțak-ed tudert.

3:7 Ccariɛa yețwajerden ɣef teblaḍin n wezṛu tesban-ed tamanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi deg udem n Sidna Musa, yețfeǧǧiǧ wudem-is almi ur zmiren ara wat Isṛail ad muqlen ɣuṛ-es ; ɣas akken, afeǧǧeǧ-nni ur ițdum ara.

3:8 Ma yella ccariɛa i gețțawin ɣer lmut tban-ed s lɛaḍima am tin, acḥal ihi ara tili meqqṛet tmanegt i d-yewwi Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i d-ițakken tudert !

3:9 Ma yella leqdic ɣef ccariɛa yețțawin ɣer lmut yesɛa ccan, acḥal meqqeṛ akteṛ ccan n leqdic ɣef Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i gețțarran imdanen d iḥeqqiyen.

3:10 Nezmer a d-nini : anda ara d tban tmanegt icceɛceɛen zik-nni zdat tin n tura ? Tuɣal d ulac !

3:11 Ma yella ayen ur nețdum ara yesɛa ccan, acḥal ihi i t-yugar di ccan wayen yețdumun.

3:12 ?ef ddemma n wusirem-agi, i nesɛa lețkal d ameqqran ;

3:13 nukni ur nelli ara am Sidna Musa yețɣummun udem-is iwakken ur țwalin ara wat Isṛail lɛaḍima-nni ur nețdum ara.

3:14 Ar ass-a qquṛen iqeṛṛay-nsen, gguman ad fehmen ; imi leḥjab-nni ur yekkis ara m'ara d-ɣṛen di tira iqedsen n leɛqed aqdim, axaṭer leḥdjab-agi itekkes anagar s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:15 Ar ass-a, leḥjab-agi ițɣummu ulawen-nsen m'ara qqaṛen ccariɛa n Musa ;

3:16 meɛna m'ara d-yuɣal wul n wemdan ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi, leḥjab-nni itekkes ;

3:17 axaṭer Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, d Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi. Anda yella Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi, tella tlelli.

3:18 Nukni ur nesɛi ara leḥjab ɣef wudmawen-nneɣ, tamanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi tețban-ed deg-nneɣ am akken di lemri, Sidi Ṛebbi yețbeddil-aɣ dayem iwakken a necbu ɣuṛ-es s tmanegt yețnernin.

4:1 Ihi, ur ilaq ara a nefcel imi s Ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣ-d ițțunefk leqdic-agi ɣef Lmasiḥ.

4:2 Ur neqbil ara ayen yellan d lɛib d wayen yețwaxedmen s tuffra, ur nteddu s tḥila, ur nețḥerrif awal n Ṛebbi. Meɛna nețbeggin-ed awal n Sidi Ṛebbi s tideț, iwakken imdanen meṛṛa ad slen i wawal n Ṛebbi, a t-fehmen akken yella.

4:3 Ma mazal lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wen nbecceṛ ur ițwafhem ara, lmeɛna-ines tețwaffer kan ɣef wid iḍaɛen,

4:4 ur numin ara s Ṛebbi ; axaṭer Cciṭan yerran iman is d ṛebbi n ddunit-agi , yesderɣel ixemmimen nsen, daymi ur walan ara tafat yellan di lexbaṛ n lxiṛ yeɛnan tamanegt n Lmasiḥ, nețța yellan d ṣṣifa n Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:5 Axaṭer mačči ɣef yiman-nneɣ i nețbecciṛ, lameɛna nețbecciṛ ɣef Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ ; ma d nukni, nuɣal d iqeddacen-nwen ɣef ddemma n Sidna Ɛisa.

4:6 Ṛebbi i d-yennan : Tafat aț-țecceɛceɛ di ṭṭlam, d nețța daɣen i d-ifkan tafat tfeǧǧeǧ deg ulawen-nneɣ iwakken a ɣ-d ibeggen tamanegt-is yețfeǧǧiǧen ɣef wudem n Lmasiḥ.

4:7 Nukni yețțawin deg iman-nneɣ agerruj-agi, necba ticmuxin n wakal, iwakken tazmert-agi tameqqrant a d-tban belli tusa-d s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi mačči s ɣuṛ-nneɣ.

4:8 Nețwaḥṛes si mkul tama meɛna ur nefcil ara ; nella di teswiɛt n ṭṭiq meɛna ur neɣli ara ;

4:9 nețwaqheṛ meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi ur aɣ-yeǧǧi ara ; nețwarkeḍ meɛna ur aɣ-nɣin ara.

4:10 Nețțawi daymen di lǧețțat-nneɣ lmut n Sidna Ɛisa iwakken a d-tban daɣen deg-nneɣ tudert-is.

4:11 Nukni yeddren, nețqabal daymen lmut ɣef ddemma n Sidna Ɛisa, iwakken a d-tban tudert n Sidna Ɛisa di lǧețțat nneɣ ifennun.

4:12 S wakka lmut txeddem deg-nneɣ, ma ț-țudert txeddem deg-wen.

4:13 Am akken yura di tira iqedsen : umneɣ, daymi i d-hedṛeɣ ; nukni daɣen nesɛa liman-agi, daymi i d nheddeṛ.

4:14 Axaṭer neẓra belli Sidi Ṛebbi i d-isseḥyan Sidna Ɛisa si ger lmegtin, a ɣ-d-isseḥyu ula d nukni akk-d Sidna Ɛisa, iwakken a ɣ issemlil yid-wen m'ara nuɣal ɣuṛ-es.

4:15 Ayen akk ideṛṛun yid-nneɣ, ideṛṛu ɣef ddemma-nwen, iwakken ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi aț-țaweḍ ɣer waṭas n yemdanen ; s wakka aṭas ara iketṛen di leḥmada n Sidi Ṛebbi, a t-cekkṛen ɣef tmanegt-is.

4:16 Daymi ur ilaq ara a nefcel ; axaṭer ɣas akken lǧețța-nneɣ tfennu seg wass ɣer wayeḍ, ṛṛuḥ yellan deg-nneɣ yețnerni seg wass ɣer wayeḍ.

4:17 Leɛtab-agi i nesɛedday tura xfif yerna ur ițdum ara ; ițheggi-yaɣ-ed tamanegt tameqqrant ur nfennu.

4:18 Axaṭer nukni ur nețnadi ara ɣef wayen țwalint wallen, lameɛna nețnadi ɣef leḥwayeǧ ur țwalint ara ; axaṭer leḥwayeǧ i țwalint wallen fennunt, ma d ayen ur țwalint ara, yețdumu i dayem.

5:1 Lǧețțat i nesɛa di ddunit-agi am iqiḍunen ara yefnun, meɛna neẓra belli Sidi Ṛebbi ihegga-yaɣ deg igenwan tinezduɣin n dayem, mačči d ayen yexdem ufus n wemdan, meɛna d ayen yexdem nețța s yiman-is.

5:2 Aql-aɣ nenɛețțab zdaxel uqiḍun agi, nețṛaǧu s lḥir melmi ara naweḍ ɣer tnezduɣin n dayem yellan deg igenwan ;

5:3 lameɛna ilaq a nili nhegga iman-nneɣ di ddunit-agi, mačči alamma tusa-d lmut a ɣ-tawi.

5:4 Skud mazal-aɣ deg uqiḍun n ddunit-agi, yeɛni lǧețțat-nneɣ, nețnazaɛ am akken nbub taɛekkumt ẓẓayen ; mačči d lmut i nebɣa a nemmet, lameɛna nessaram a nels lǧețța n igenwan iwakken ad yețwakkes seg-neɣ wayen ifennun.

5:5 D Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣ-iheggan ɣer tudert-agi, ifka-yaɣ-d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen d aɛeṛbun n lbaṛakat ara ɣ-d ițțunefken.

5:6 S wakka nesɛa daymen lețkal deg wulawen-nneɣ. Neẓra belli skud mazal-aɣ di lǧețța n ddunit-agi, aql-aɣ mazal nebɛed ɣef Sidi Ṛebbi ;

5:7 axaṭer tura aql-aɣ nțeddu s liman, werɛad nwala s wallen.

5:8 S tideț nesɛa lețkal deg wulawen nneɣ, lameɛna a wi-yufan a neffeɣ si ddunit-agi iwakken a nili ɣer tama n Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:9 Ama nella di ddunit-agi ama neffeɣ seg-s, nețnadi kan a neɛǧeb i Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:10 Axaṭer a nɛeddi meṛṛa di ccṛeɛ n Lmasiḥ, iwakken mkul yiwen ad ițțuḥaseb ɣef lxiṛ neɣ cceṛ i gexdem asmi yella di ddunit.

5:11 Neẓra d acu i d lmeɛna n tugdi n Sidi Ṛebbi, daymi i nețnadi a d nehdu wiyaḍ. Sidi Ṛebbi yessen ulawen-nneɣ akken ilaq, ssarameɣ ula d kunwi tessnem-aɣ, teẓram ayen yellan deg ulawen-nneɣ.

5:12 Mačči d zzux i nebɣa a nzux s yiman-nneɣ ɣuṛ-wen, nebɣa kan a nili d sebba n lfeṛḥ-nwen. S wakka aț-țissinem amek ara terrem awal i wid ixedmen ccan i wayen i d ițbanen s ufella kan, mačči i wayen yellan deg wulawen.

5:13 Ma yella llant teswiɛin i deg nɛedda tilas deg uselmed-nneɣ, nɛedda tilas iwakken a d-tban tmanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi ; m'ara netɛqqel, i nnfeɛ-nwen i netɛeqqel.

5:14 Nețwaḥṛes s leḥmala n Lmasiḥ yeččuṛen ulawen-nneɣ, axaṭer neẓra imi yemmut yiwen wemdan ɣef yemdanen meṛṛa, ihi mmuten meṛṛa.

5:15 Yemmut ɣef medden meṛṛa, iwakken wid yeddren ur țɛicin ara i yiman nsen, lameɛna ad ɛicen i win yemmuten yerna yeḥya-d ɣef ddemma-nsen.

5:16 Sya d asawen ur nețwali yiwen s lɛeqliya n yemdanen ; ɣas akken zik-nni uqbel a namen nella nețwali Lmasiḥ s lɛeqliya n yemdanen, tura mačči akenni i t-nețwali.

5:17 Ihi win iteddun deg webrid n Lmasiḥ, iɛawed ed talalit, yuɣal d amdan ajdid : ayen yellan d aqdim iɛedda, kullec yuɣal d ajdid.

5:18 Ayagi meṛṛa yekka-d s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi i gessemṣalaḥen imdanen yid-es s Lmasiḥ, yerna iweṣṣa-yaɣ-ed a nbecceṛ amṣaleḥ-agi i yemdanen meṛṛa n ddunit,

5:19 axaṭer s Lmasiḥ i gessemṣaleḥ Sidi Ṛebbi imdanen yid-es mbla ma imuqel ɣer ddnubat-nsen yerna iweṣṣa-yaɣ a nbecceṛ amṣaleḥ-agi.

5:20 Ihi nukni d imceggɛen n Lmasiḥ, am akken d Ṛebbi s yiman-is i wen-d-issawalen yis nneɣ : Nețḥellil-ikkun s yisem n Lmasiḥ aț-țemṣalaḥem akk-d Ṛebbi.

6:1 Imi d iqeddacen n Sidi Ṛebbi i nella, bɣiɣ a kkun-nenhu ur tețḍeggiɛem ara ṛṛeḥma i wen-d ițțunefken s ɣuṛ-es.

6:2 Axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi yenna-d : Di lweqt ilaqen, qebleɣ-ed taẓallit-ik, di lweqt n leslak, sellkeɣ-k. Ihi tura, atan yewweḍ-ed lweqt ilaqen, yewweḍ-ed wass n leslak.

6:3 Di yal lḥaǧa, ur nebɣi ara a nili d ugur ula i yiwen deg-wen iwakken ur d-tețțekksem ara kra n diri di lxedma-nneɣ,

6:4 meɛna nețbeggin-ed belli d iqeddacen n Sidi Ṛebbi i nella deg wayen akk nxeddem : nṣebbeṛ i teswiɛin n ceddat, i lḥif, i tugdi,

6:5 i tiytiwin ; neṣbeṛ mi nella di leḥbus, di teswiɛin n ccwal, leɛtab, aɛawez akk-d laẓ ;

6:6 nedda s wul yeṣfan, s tmusni, s ṣṣbeṛ, s leḥnana, s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, s leḥmala n ṣṣeḥ,

6:7 s wawal n tideț, s tezmert n Sidi Ṛebbi, s leslaḥ n lḥeqq s wayes nețnaɣ yerna nețdafaɛ ɣef yiman-nneɣ ;

6:8 ama di teswiɛin n ccan neɣ n lɛaṛ, ama mi heddṛen fell-aɣ medden s lxiṛ neɣ s cceṛ. Nețwaḥseb d ikeddaben ɣas akken ț-țideț i d neqqaṛ,

6:9 nețwankeṛ, ɣas akken nețwassen ɣer yemdanen meṛṛa ; ḥesben-aɣ nemmut, nukni nedder ; țqehhiṛen-aɣ lameɛna ur nemmut ara ;

6:10 sseɣlayen-d fell-aɣ leḥzen, lameɛna nețțili daymen di lfeṛḥ. ?esben-aɣ d igellilen, lameɛna nesserbaḥ aṭas n yemdanen, ɣilen ur nesɛi acemma, lameɛna nukni ur aɣ-ixuṣṣ ula d kra.

6:11 Ay imezdaɣ n temdint n Kurintus, nemmeslay-awen-d, neldi-yawen mliḥ ulawen-nneɣ,

6:12 ur ten-neɣliq ara ɣuṛ wen, d kunwi i gɣelqen ulawen-nwen ɣuṛ-nneɣ.

6:13 ?meslayeɣ-awen-d am akken d arraw-iw i tellam : xedmet am nukni, swesɛet ulawen-nwen ula d kunwi !

6:14 Ur cerrket ara d wid ur nețțamen ara s Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken aț-țxedmem am nutni. Eɛni lḥeqq d lbaṭel, zemren ad cerken ? Tafat akk-d ṭṭlam, zemren ad ddukklen ?

6:15 Amek ! Yezmer Lmasiḥ ad imsefham akk-d Cciṭan ? Acu n tdukli yellan ger win yețțamnen s Ṛebbi d win ur nețțamen ara ?

6:16 D acu icerken lǧameɛ iqedsen n Sidi Ṛebbi akk-d lmeṣnuɛat ? Axaṭer d nukni i d lǧameɛ iqedsen n Ṛebbi yeddren. Sidi Ṛebbi yenna-t-id di tira iqedsen : Ad zedɣeɣ yerna ad lḥuɣ gar-asen, A d iliɣ d Illu-nsen, ad ilin d agdud iw

6:17 ?ef wannect-agi i d-yenna Sidi Ṛebbi : Mfaṛaqet d yemdanen-agi, beɛdet fell-asen ; ur țnalet ara ayen iḥeṛmen, nekk ad sṭerḥbeɣ yis-wen ;

7:1 A wid eɛzizen, imi i ɣ-d țțunefkent lemɛahdat-agi, ilaq a nessizdeg iman-nneɣ seg wayen akk issenǧasen lǧețța-nneɣ akk-d wulawen-nneɣ iwakken a nkemmel tezdeg n tikli-nneɣ akk-d Sidi Ṛebbi, s ṭṭaɛa d wannuz.

7:2 Qeblet-aɣ deg wulawen-nwen ! Ur neḍlim yiwen, ur nexdim cceṛ ula i yiwen, ur nečči lḥeqq n yiwen.

7:3 Ur d-nniɣ ara annect-agi iwakken a kkun-sḍelmeɣ ; axaṭer nniɣ-awen t-id yakan : ama nedder ama newweḍ ɣer lmut, tellam deg wulawen-nneɣ .

7:4 Lețkal-iw fell-awen meqqeṛ, țzuxxuɣ aṭas yis-wen ; yethedden wul-iw yerna yeččuṛ d lfeṛḥ ɣas akken nesɛedda aṭas n leɛtab.

7:5 Seg wasmi i d-nekcem tamurt n Masidunya ur nesteɛfa, nețwaḥṛes si mkul tama : ccwal di beṛṛa, tugdi deg ulawen-nneɣ.

7:6 Lameɛna Sidi Ṛebbi yețṣebbiṛen wid yețwaḥeqṛen, iṣebbeṛ aɣ s gma-tneɣ Titus mi d-yewweḍ.

7:7 Mačči kan mi d-yewweḍ gma tneɣ Titus i nețțuṣebbeṛ, lameɛna nețțuṣebbeṛ daɣen s ṣṣbeṛ i d-yewwi s ɣuṛ-wen. Yeḥka-yaɣ-d acḥal tețmennim a yi-teẓrem, acḥal tḥeznem d wamek tebɣam a yi-tɛiwnem seg ul. ?ef wayagi i gzad lfeṛḥ-iw.

7:8 ?riɣ belli sḥezneɣ-kkun s tebṛaț iw tamezwarut, lameɛna ur ndimeɣ ara ; ɣas akken ndemmeɣ, ndemmeɣ kan mi ẓriɣ sḥezneɣ-kkun kra n lweqt.

7:9 Tura aql-i feṛḥeɣ, mačči imi i kkun-sḥezneɣ, meɛna imi s leḥzen-agi tettubem yerna tbeddlem tikli. Axaṭer leḥzen-nni i d-yeɣlin fell awen yusa-d s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, s wakka ur d-yekki wacemma n diri s ɣuṛ-nneɣ.

7:10 Leḥzen i d-itekken s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ițbeddil tikli n wemdan, yețțawi ɣer leslak yerna ur nțendemmay ara deg-s ; ma d leḥzen i d-itekken si ddunit, yețțawi ɣer lmut.

7:11 Leḥzen-agi-nwen yeɛǧeb aṭas Sidi Ṛebbi, walit acḥal i gexdem deg-wen : acḥal i tenneḥcamem d wamek i tḍelbem ssmaḥ ! Acḥal tendemmem, tekcem-ikkun tugdi n Sidi Ṛebbi ; acḥal tcedham a yi teẓrem yerna tebɣam a yi tɛiwnem ; amek daɣen tɛuqbem wid ixeddmen cceṛ ! Deg wayagi meṛṛa tesbeggnem-d belli teṣfam di taluft-agi.

7:12 Ihi ma uriɣ-awen-d, mačči ɣef ddemma n win iḍelmen neɣ win yețwaḍelmen, lameɛna iwakken a d-iban gar-awen zdat Sidi Ṛebbi, acḥal meqqeṛ leḥmala-nwen ɣuṛ-nneɣ.

7:13 ?ef wannect-agi i theddnen wulawen-nneɣ. Sennig ṣṣbeṛ-agi, lfeṛḥ-nneɣ yennerna mi nwala acḥal yefṛeḥ yis-wen gma-tneɣ Titus, axaṭer tekksem-as anezgum.

7:14 ?ideț zuxxeɣ yis-wen zdat-es, lameɛna ur nneḥcameɣ ara, axaṭer anagar tideț i wen-d-nheddeṛ yal ass imi ayen akken s wayes nzuxx yis-wen zdat gma-tneɣ Titus, iban-ed belli ț-țideț ;

7:15 lemḥibba-s ɣuṛ-wen tețnerni mkul m'ara d-immekti amek i tesṭerḥbem yis s leqdeṛ d wannuz.

7:16 Feṛḥeɣ imi zemreɣ ad țekleɣ fell-awen di kullec.

8:1 Nebɣa a kkun-nesseɛlem ay atmaten ɣef wayen yeɛnan ṛṛeḥma i d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi i tejmuyaɛ n imasiḥiyen yellan di tmurt n Masidunya ;

8:2 țwajeṛben s waṭas n lmeḥnat, lameɛna lfeṛḥ-nsen yețnerni. ?as akken d igellilen i llan yerna xuṣṣen aṭas, seddqen mbla cceḥḥa ;

8:3 fkan-d ayen iwumi zemren ; aql-iyi d inigi, zemreɣ a d-iniɣ fkan-d nnig n tezmert-nsen yerna xedmen ayagi s lebɣi-nsen.

8:4 Ssutren-aɣ-d s uḥellel iwakken a sen-nexdem lemziya a d-fken ula d nutni amur-nsen n ssadaqa-agi i njemmeɛ iwakken a ț-țnawi i watmaten n tmurt n Yahuda yellan di lexṣas.

8:5 Zwaren deg iman-nsen mi fkan tudert-nsen i Ṛebbi, imiren s lebɣi n Sidi Ṛebbi, rnan qeddcen fell-aɣ seg ul ; ayagi sennig wayen nessaram !

8:6 S wakka ihi, nweṣṣa gma-tneɣ Titus ad ikemmel lxedma-nni i gebda ɣuṛ-wen ɣef wayen yeɛnan ssadaqa agi.

8:7 Am akken i tẓewṛem di kullec, ama di liman neɣ deg wawal, ama di tmusni neɣ di lemḥibba i tesɛam ɣuṛ-nneɣ, ẓewṛet daɣen di temsalt yeɛnan ssadaqa.

8:8 Mačči d lameṛ i bɣiɣ a wen-d fkeɣ ; ḥkiɣ-awen amek i zewṛen wiyaḍ, iwakken a kkun-jeṛṛbeɣ ma tesɛam leḥmala n tideț neɣ ala.

8:9 Tessnem ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ yellan d Bab n lerbaḥ n ddunit meṛṛa, yuɣal d igellil ɣef ddemma-nwen iwakken a kkun isserbeḥ s tigellelt-ines.

8:10 A wen-d-fkeɣ ṛṛay-iw deg wayagi axaṭer d ayen i wen-ilhan : aseggas iɛeddan, d kunwi i d imezwura i gebɣan aț-țɛiwnem atmaten n tmurt n Yahuda yellan di lexṣaṣ, d kunwi daɣen i d imezwura i gebdan tjemmɛem-d ssadaqa-agi ;

8:11 ihi tura kemmlet ccɣel-agi i tebdam, s wayen iwumi tzemrem ; am akken i t-tebdam seg ul, kemmlet-eț s wul.

8:12 Sidi Ṛebbi iqebbel ssadaqa-nneɣ, lameɛna s wakken tella tezmert-nneɣ, mačči sennig wayen nezmer.

8:13 Ur nebɣi ara a d-nini xlut ixxamen-nwen iwakken aț-țɛiwnem wiyaḍ, lameɛna iwakken aț-țemɛadalem ;

8:14 ayen tesɛam d zzyada ad yenfeɛ wid ixuṣṣen tura, iwakken asm'ara txuṣṣem kunwi, a kkun-ɛiwnen s wayen sɛan d zzyada ; s wakka ihi aț-țemɛadalem,

8:15 akken yura di tira iqedsen : Win yesɛan aṭas ur yesɛi ara zzyada, ma d win yesɛan cwiṭ ur t-ixuṣṣ kra .

8:17 Yeqbel mi t-in-nceggeɛ, yerna uqbel a s-nini yella iqsed a n-iruḥ ɣuṛ wen s lfeṛḥ d ameqqran.

8:18 A nceggeɛ yid-es yiwen seg watmaten-nneɣ i cekkṛent akk tejmuyaɛ n watmaten ɣef lxedma-ines deg ubecceṛ n lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ;

8:19 ur yețwaxtaṛ ara kan d aṛfiq, lameɛna ț-țijmuyaɛ n watmaten i t-ixtaṛen iwakken a ɣ-iɛiwen di ccɣel agi yeɛnan ssadaqa ɣef ddemma n tmanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi, s wakka daɣen a d-nbeggen acḥal nebɣa a nɛiwen wiyaḍ.

8:20 Nețḥadar iman-nneɣ iwakken ur heddṛen ara fell-aɣ lɣaci ɣef wayen yeɛnan aḍebbeṛ i nețḍebbiṛ di ssadaqa agi, axaṭer aṭas n yedrimen i d nejmeɛ ;

8:21 nețqellib ayen yelhan ama zdat Sidi Ṛebbi ama zdat yemdanen.

8:22 Nceggeɛ yid-sen gma-tneɣ agi i njeṛṛeb acḥal d abrid di yal taswiɛt, nwala amek yebɣa ad iqdec seg ul ; tikkelt-agi ẓẓwaṛa-ines di leqdic ɣef Sidi Ṛebbi tban-ed akteṛ, axaṭer yețkel fell-awen.

8:23 ?ef wayen yeɛnan gma-tneɣ Titus, d arfiq-iw yerna ixeddem yid-i ɣef ddemma-nwen, ma d atmaten nniḍen, d imceggɛen n tejmuyaɛ n watmaten ; qeddcen iwakken a d tban tmanegt n Lmasiḥ.

8:24 Sbeggnet-ed ihi belli tḥemmlem ten s tideț iwakken tijmuyaɛ n watmaten ad ẓrent belli nesɛa lḥeqq imi nețzuxxu yis-wen.

9:1 ?ef wayen yeɛnan lemɛawna n watmaten, fiḥel ma uriɣ awen-d.

9:2 ?riɣ acḥal tebɣam aț țqedcem, daymi i țzuxxuɣ yis-wen zdat n tejmuyaɛ n Masidunya qqaṛeɣ asen : atmaten n tmurt n Akaya heggan iman-nsen ad ɛiwnen seg useggas iɛeddan. ?ẓwaṛa-nwen di leqdic tessaki aṭas deg-sen.

9:3 Ceggɛeɣ atmaten-agi iwakken ad iban belli ț-țideț, tuklalem a nzux yis-wen yerna theggam iman-nwen am akken i t-id-nniɣ.

9:4 Ma yella ddan yid-i kra seg watmaten n Masidunya ilaq a kkun-in-afen theggam iman nwen iwakken zzux-nni i țzuxxuɣ yis wen ur iyi-țțuɣal ara d lḥecma, bɣiɣ a d-iniɣ d lḥecma nwen kunwi !

9:5 ?ef ddemma n wannect-agi i wen-ceggɛeɣ kra seg watmaten a n-zwiren ɣuṛ-wen iwakken a d jemɛen ssadaqa-nni i ɣ-tweɛdem. S wakka m'ara n-awḍeɣ ad afeɣ theggam kullec yerna a d-iban belli ayen i tseddqem tefkam-t-id seg ul, ur kkun-iḥettem yiwen.

9:6 Ilaq aț-țeẓrem belli win i gzerɛen cwiṭ a d-imger cwiṭ ; ma d win izerɛen aṭas, a d-imger aṭas.

9:7 Mkul yiwen ilaq ad iseddeq akken yenwa deg ul-is, mačči s uḥettem neɣ s nndama axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi iḥemmel win ițseddiqen s lfeṛḥ.

9:8 Sidi Ṛebbi yesɛa tazmert a kkun id yeṛzeq s lbaṛakat n mkul ṣṣenf, ur kkun-ițxaṣṣa wacemma deg wayen akk teḥwaǧem yerna aț-țesɛum s zzyada iwakken aț-țizmirem aț-țseddqem.

9:9 Axaṭer yura di tira iqedsen : Yețțak-ed lbaṛakat-is mbla cceḥḥa, irẓeq-ed imeɣban, ṛ ṛeḥma-ines tețdumu i dayem .

9:10  Win i d-yețțaken i ufellaḥ zzerriɛa akk-d weɣṛum ara yečč , a wen-d-yefk ula i kunwi s lketṛa yerna ad issefti lbaṛakat n wayen tețseddiqem .

9:11 Sidi Ṛebbi ad issefti leṛzaq-nwen, s wakka aț-țizmirem aț-țseddqem mbla cceḥḥa ; imiren aṭas ara iḥemden Sidi Ṛebbi a t-cekkṛen ɣef wayen i sen-tseddqem.

9:12 Axaṭer ssadaqa-agi, ur tețțili ara kan d lemɛawna i watmaten, lameɛna iwakken a d-ilin daɣen waṭas n yemdanen ara iḥemden Sidi Ṛebbi.

9:13 Imi i d-tbeggnem belli tqeddcem ɣef lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ yerna tfeṛṛqem ayen akk tesɛam akk-d watmaten nniḍen, țḥemmiden Sidi Ṛebbi țcekkiṛen-t ɣef liman d ṭṭaɛa nwen.

9:14 Deɛɛun ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen ɣef ddemma n ṛṛeḥma tameqqrant i wen-ițțunefken s ɣuṛ-es, axaṭer ḥemmlen-kkun.

10:2 Di leɛnaya-nwen, ur iyi-ḥeṛṛset ara ad iwɛiṛeɣ m'ara iliɣ yid-wen ! Axaṭer lewɛaṛa-inu a ț-id-sbeggneɣ s lețkal yerna mbla lḥecma ɣer wid i ɣ-iḥesben nțeddu s lɛeqliya n ddunit.

10:3 ?-țideț d imdanen i nella, lameɛna ur nețnaɣ ara s leslaḥ s wayes țnaɣen yemdanen ;

10:10 Axaṭer llan wid yeqqaṛen : tibṛatin n Bulus qeṛṛḥent, sɛant tissas lameɛna m'ara yili gar-aneɣ ur yesɛi lhiba ur yesɛi tissas di lehduṛ-is.

10:18 Axaṭer mačči d win yețzuxxun s yiman-is i gețwaqeblen lameɛna d win yextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

11:1 Ah ! A lukan aț-țqeblem kra n timmuhbelt si lǧiha-w ! Lameɛna qeblet-eț kan !

11:2 Imi i kkun-ḥemmleɣ, țțasmeɣ fell-awen s tismin i d-yekkan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ; axaṭer xeḍbeɣ-kkun i yiwen n yesli kan : d Lmasiḥ ; iwakken a kkun-qeddmeɣ ɣuṛ-es am tlemẓit leɛmeṛ nezwiǧ leɛmeṛ.

11:3 Uggadeɣ a wen-sxeṛben ixemmimen nwen, aț-țettixṛem i neyya d ṣṣfa n wul-nwen di Lmasiḥ, aț-țedṛu yid-wen am ?ewwa i gexdeɛ wezrem s tḥila-ines.

11:4 Amek armi tḥessem i wid i wen-ibeccṛen Ɛisa nniḍen ur nelli d win akken i wen-nbecceṛ nukni ; amek tqeblem ṛṛuḥ iqedsen nniḍen ur nelli ara d win s wayes i tesṭerḥbem di tazwara neɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ nniḍen ixulfen win akken i wen-nbecceṛ ?

11:5 Akken walaɣ, ṛṛusul-nni i tḥesbem d imeqqranen ur iyi-yifen deg wacemma.

11:6 ?as akken ur ssineɣ ara ad mmeslayeɣ, lameɛna ssneɣ Sidi Ṛebbi akken ilaq yerna nbeggen awen-t-id acḥal n tikkal di mkul lḥaǧa !

11:7 Asmi i wen-beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ, eɛni ḍelmeɣ mi ssanzeɣ iman-iw iwakken a kkun ssaɛliɣ kunwi ?

11:8 Ɛerraɣ tijmuyaɛ nniḍen imi i yi-d-țceggiɛen idrimen iwakken ad qedceɣ fell-awen.

11:9 Asmi lliɣ ɣuṛ-wen, ɣas akken xuṣṣeɣ ur lliɣ ț-țaɛkumt ɣef yiwen, axaṭer atmaten i d-yusan si tmurt n Masidunya fkan-iyi-d ayen akk i ḥwaǧeɣ. Di yal lḥaǧa, ḥudreɣ iman-iw iwakken ur țțiliɣ ara ț-țaɛekkumt fell-awen, yerna mazal ad ḥadreɣ iman-iw.

11:10 S tideț n Lmasiḥ yellan deg-i, a d-iniɣ : yiwen ur iyi-tekkes sebba agi n zzux i sɛiɣ di tmura n Akaya.

11:11 Acuɣeṛ ? Eɛni ur teɛzizem ara fell-i ? Atan Sidi Ṛebbi yezṛa !

11:12 Ayen akka xeddmeɣ, mazal a t-xedmeɣ iwakken ur sen-țaǧǧaɣ ara abrid i wid yebɣan ad zuxxen belli xeddmen am nukni.

11:13 Imdanen-agi, d ṛṛusul n lekdeb, d ixeddaɛen, țțaran iman-nsen d imceggɛen n Lmasiḥ.

11:14 Annect-a mačči d ayen issewhamen, imi ula d Cciṭan yezmer ad yerr iman-is d lmelk yețfeǧǧiǧen am tafat.

11:15 Ihi daɣen, mačči d ayen yessewhamen ma yella iqeddacen-is țțaran iman-nsen d iqeddacen n webrid n lḥeqq ; lameɛna taggara-nsen aț-țili akken llan lecɣal nsen.

11:16 A t-id-ɛiwdeɣ, ɣuṛ-wat a yi tḥesbem d amehbul ; neɣ qeblet-iyi am akken d amehbul iwakken ula d nekk ad zuxxeɣ cwiṭ.

11:17 Ayen akka ara d-iniɣ, mačči s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi i d-yekka lameɛna a d-hedṛeɣ s timmuhbelt, yerna țekleɣ axaṭer sɛiɣ ayen s wayes ara zuxxeɣ.

11:18 Imi aṭas i gețzuxxun s lɛeqliya n ddunit-agi, ula d nekk ad zuxxeɣ.

11:19 Axaṭer tṣebṛem i yimehbal kunwi yesɛan leɛqel !

11:20 ?as akken ḥesben-kkun am aklan, ččan lḥeqq-nwen, ɛerran-kkun, bbehdlen-kkun, ḥeqṛen-kkun, meɛna tṣebṛem-asen.

11:21 Ssetḥaɣ a t-id-iniɣ meɛna a t-id iniɣ : nukni ur nezmir ara a nexdem akka, meɛna s timmuhbelt zemreɣ a d-iniɣ : ma yella win yesɛan sebba s wayes ara izuxx, ula d nekk ad zuxxeɣ :

11:22 d iɛebṛaniyen i llan ? Ula d nekk d aɛibṛani ; seg wat Isṛail i llan ? Ula d nekk n wat Isṛail ; ț-țarwa n Sidna Ibṛahim ? Ula d nekk si tarwa-s ;

11:23 d iqeddacen n Lmasiḥ i llan ? ?ef wannect-agi daɣen a d hedṛeɣ am umdan yeffeɣ leɛqel : nekk ugareɣ-ten s waṭas, xedmeɣ akteṛ-nsen, kecmeɣ leḥbus acḥal n tikkal, țewwteɣ akteṛ-nsen, acḥal d abrid i wwḍeɣ ɣer lmut !

11:24 Xemsa yiberdan i ččiɣ tiyita s ɣuṛ wudayen ; mkul tikkelt s ṛebɛin tiyita ɣiṛ yiwet ;

11:25 tlata iberdan i yi-wten iṛumaniyen s ujelkaḍ ; yiwen webrid qṛib i yi-nɣan s weṛjam ; ɣeṛqeɣ tlata iberdan di lebḥeṛ, sɛeddaɣ yiwen wass d yiwen yiḍ deg waman.

11:26 Mmugreɣ-ed acḥal n wuguren m'ara tsafaṛeɣ : isaffen iweɛṛen, imakaren deg iberdan, lxuf di temdinin, deg imukan yexlan, m'ara zegreɣ i lebḥuṛ ; lxuf ger wat n tmurt-iw akk-d wid ur numin ara s Ṛebbi, lxuf ger watmaten n lekdeb.

11:27 Xedmeɣ yerna nneɛtabeɣ aṭas, acḥal d abrid țțuẓumeɣ, zgiɣ deg uɛawez, di laẓ akk-d fad, deg usemmiḍ akk-d ɛerru.

11:28 Mbla ma nniɣ-ed ayen nniḍen : aḥebbeṛ yal ass ɣef tejmuyaɛ n watmaten,

11:29 anwa i gfeclen di liman ur iyi-issefcel ara ? Anwa i geɣlin di ddnub ur tecɛil deg-i tmes ?

11:30 Ma ilaq ad zuxxeɣ, ad zuxxeɣ s wayen i d-ițbegginen belli ur sɛiɣ ara tazmert.

11:31 Sidi Ṛebbi yellan d baba-s n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, yețțubarken i dayem, yeẓra belli ur skiddibeɣ ara !

11:32 Di temdint n Dimecq, ameqqran n ineɣlafen n ugellid Ariṭas yesbedd taɛessast ɣef tewwurt n temdint iwakken a yi-ṭṭfen,

11:33 lameɛna ṣubben iyi-d si ṭṭaq, zdaxel uḍellaɛ, lḥiḍ lḥiḍ ; s wakka i d-menɛeɣ seg ifassen-is.

12:1 Eɛni ilaq ad zuxxeɣ ? Ayagi ur infiɛ ara ! Lameɛna a d-ḥkuɣ ɣef kra n leḥwayeǧ i yi-d-iweḥḥa Sidi Ṛebbi.

12:2 Ssneɣ yiwen wergaz yumnen s Lmasiḥ, yețwarfed alamma d igenni wis tlata ṛbeɛṭac iseggasen aya ; ur ẓriɣ ma yella yețwarfed nețța s yiman is neɣ d aweḥḥi kan i s-d-ițțuweḥḥa, anagar Sidi Ṛebbi i geẓran.

12:3  ?riɣ belli argaz-agi yețwarfed ɣer lǧennet, meɛna ur ẓriɣ ma yella yețwarfed nețța s yiman-is neɣ d aweḥḥi kan i s-d-ițțuweḥḥa, anagar Sidi Ṛebbi i geẓran ; yesla i yimeslayen iwumi ur yezmir yiwen a ten-id-yini, axaṭer ur ilaq i wemdan a ten-id-iɛawed.

12:5 Argaz am wagi zemreɣ ad zuxxeɣ yis, ma d nekk ur sɛiɣ ara s wayes ara zuxxeɣ anagar s lqella n lǧehd-iw.

12:6 Lemmer bɣiɣ ad zuxxeɣ, ur țțiliɣ ara d amehbul axaṭer anagar tideț ara d-iniɣ ; lameɛna ad ssusmeɣ, imi ur bɣiɣ ara imdanen a yi-ssalin ccan sennig n wayen țwalin xeddmeɣ-t neɣ ayen sellen qqaṛeɣ t-id.

12:7 Yenta-yi yiwen usennan s wayes i yi-țmeḥḥin Cciṭan iwakken ur ssimɣuṛeɣ ara iman-iw yerna ur țzuxxuɣ ara s uweḥḥi-nni yesteɛǧiben i yi-d-ibeggen Sidi Ṛebbi.

12:8 Tlata iberdan i dɛiɣ ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken ad issebɛed fell-i lmeḥna-agi,

12:9 lameɛna yenna-yi-d : « steqneɛ kan s ṛṛeḥma-inu, axaṭer m'ara tfecleḍ imiren i d-tețban deg-k tezmert-iw ». Ihi bɣiɣ ad zuxxeɣ axiṛ s lqella n tezmert-iw iwakken tazmert n Lmasiḥ aț-țezdeɣ deg-i.

12:10 ?ef wannect-agi, feṛḥeɣ m'ara fecleɣ, m'ara iliɣ di lḥeṛs, di leqheṛ, di leḥzen, di ceddat neɣ m'ara yi-regmen ɣef ddemma n Lmasiḥ, axaṭer m'ara fecleɣ imiren i ǧehdeɣ.

12:11 D kunwi i yi-ḥeṛsen armi heddṛeɣ am umehbul ; d kunwi i glaqen a yi-tcekkṛem, axaṭer ɣas akken ur swiɣ acemma, ur iyi-ifen deg wacemma wid iḥețțben iman-nsen d ṛṛusul.

12:12 Ayen akk i d-ițbegginen belli d ṛṛasul i lliɣ, yedṛa-d gar-awen : s ṣṣbeṛ ameqqran, s tezmert, s beṛhanat d licaṛat.

12:13 D acu i xedmeɣ i tejmuyaɛ nniḍen ur wen-t-xdimeɣ ara i kunwi, anagar imi ugiɣ ad iliɣ ț-țaɛkumt fell-awen ? Semmḥet-iyi ɣef wannect agi !

12:14 Aql-iyi țedduɣ a n-aseɣ ɣuṛ wen tikkelt tis tlata, yerna ur țțiliɣ ara ț-țaɛekkumt fell-awen. Mačči d idrimen-nwen i bɣiɣ lameɛna d kunwi ; axaṭer mačči d dderya i glaqen ad jemɛen i lwaldin-nsen, lameɛna d lwaldin i glaq ad jemɛen i dderya-nsen.

12:15 Ma d nekkini, zemreɣ ad ṣeṛṛfeɣ fell-awen seg ul ayen sɛiɣ, ad rnuɣ ad sebbleɣ ula ț-țudert-iw ɣef ddemma-nwen. Eɛni leḥmala-nwen ɣuṛ-i aț-țenqes imi i kkun-ḥemmleɣ aṭas ?

12:16 ?-țideț ur a wen-lliɣ ara ț țaɛekkumt, lameɛna kra deg-wen qqaṛen, imi ḥeṛceɣ, ṭṭfeɣ-kkun s tḥila !

12:17 Eɛni kellxeɣ-awen mi wen-ceggɛeɣ kra seg watmaten ?

12:18 Asmi ḥelleleɣ Tit a n-yas ɣuṛ-wen akk-d gma-tneɣ nniḍen, yella wayen i d-istenfeɛ seg-wen Titus ? Eɛni ur neddi ara nekk yid-es s yiwet n lɛeqliya akk d yiwet n tikli ?

12:19 Acḥal aya kunwi tɣilem nețnadi amek ara ndafeɛ ɣef yiman-nneɣ zdat-nwen. Lameɛna zdat Sidi Ṛebbi, nukni nheddeṛ-awen-d s lebɣi n Lmasiḥ. Annect-agi meṛṛa a wid eɛzizen fell-aɣ, nxeddem-it iwakken aț-țennernim di liman.

12:20 Axaṭer uggadeɣ m'ara n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen, ur kkun-țțafeɣ ara akken bɣiɣ, kunwi daɣen ur iyi-tețțafem ara akken tenwam ; uggadeɣ a n-afeɣ gar-awen amennuɣ, tismin, zzɛaf, amḥezwer, zzux, ccwal, aqeṛṛeḍ, wid yekkaten deg wiyaḍ.

12:21 Uggadeɣ m'ara n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen, ad ssetḥiɣ yis-wen zdat Sidi Ṛebbi ; uggadeɣ ad țruɣ ɣef waṭas deg-wen iɛacen zik-nni di ddnub yerna ar ass-a ur tuben, ur rrin aḍar ɣef lefsed-nsen, ɣef yir tikli-nsen akk-d ticmatin i xeddmen.

13:1 Atan a n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen tikkelt tis tlata, mkul taluft aț-țefru s sin neɣ tlata inigan akken yura di tira iqedsen.

13:2 Asmi i n-usiɣ ɣuṛ-wen tikkelt tis snat nniɣ-awen-t-id, tura daɣen ɣas ur lliɣ ara yid-wen, a t-id-ɛiwdeɣ ama i wid-nni yeɣlin di ddnub deg wayen iɛeddan, ama i wiyaḍ : asm'ara n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen ur țqileɣ ula d yiwen,

13:3 imi tebɣam aț-țetḥeqqeqem belli d Lmasiḥ i d-iheddṛen seg-i ; nețța ur nelli ara d win ifeclen ɣuṛ-wen lameɛna isbeggen-ed tazmert-is gar awen.

13:4 ?-țideț yețwaṣleb ɣef ddemma n wefcal-ines, lameɛna yedder s tezmert n Sidi Ṛebbi ; ula d nukni nefcel imi nella di Lmasiḥ lameɛna a nidir yid-es s tezmert n Sidi Ṛebbi di leqdic-nneɣ gar-awen.

13:5 Jeṛṛbet tmeyyzem deg iman-nwen iwakken aț-țwalim ma tețțeddum s liman neɣ ala ; eɛni ur teɛqilem ara belli Ɛisa Lmasiḥ yella deg-wen ? Neɣ ahat tfeclem mi tețțujeṛbem ?

13:6 Lameɛna ssarameɣ aț-țestɛeṛfem belli nukni nbeggen-ed liman-nneɣ di teswiɛt n ujeṛṛeb !

13:7 Ndeɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken ur txeddmem ara cceṛ, mačči iwakken a d-nbeggen belli nesɛa lḥeqq, lameɛna nebɣa kan aț-țxedmem lxiṛ, ɣas ma nban-ed belli nefcel deg ujeṛṛeb.

13:8 Axaṭer ur nesɛi ara tazmert iwakken a nxalef tideț, lameɛna nesɛa kan tazmert s wayes ara neqdec ɣef tideț.

13:9 Nfeṛṛeḥ s wefcal-nneɣ ma yella kunwi tǧehdem ; m'ara ndeɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen, nessaram aț-țennernim di liman.

13:10 Atah iwacu i wen-țaruɣ akkagi m'ara beɛdeɣ fell-awen, iwakken asm'ara iliɣ gar-awen, ur țiwɛiṛeɣ ara ɣuṛ-wen s tezmert i yi-d-ifka Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken a kkun-sǧehdeɣ mačči iwakken a kkun-sseɣliɣ.

13:11 Tura ay atmaten ilit di lfeṛḥ, ssimɣuṛet liman-nwen, nadit amek ara tawḍem ɣer lekmal, mseǧhadet wway gar-awen, msefhamet, ɛicet di lehna, imiren Sidi Ṛebbi Bab n talwit akk-d leḥmala ad yili yid-wen.

13:12 ?emsalamet wway gar-awen s sslam n tegmaț. Atmaten meṛṛa țsellimen fell-awen.

13:13 Ṛṛeḥma n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, leḥmala n Sidi Ṛebbi ț-țdukli i d-ițțak Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ad ilin yid-wen meṛṛa !
