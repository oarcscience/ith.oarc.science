Kabyle Bible: Galatians

1:1 Nekk Bulus, yellan d ṛṛasul, mačči s ufus neɣ s lebɣi n yemdanen, lameɛna s lebɣi n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d Baba Ṛebbi i t-id isseḥyan si ger lmegtin.

1:2 Uriɣ tabṛaț-agi nekk d watmaten meṛṛa yellan yid-i, i tejmuyaɛ n watmaten n tmurt n Galasya :

1:3 ṛṛeḥma d lehna a wen-d-țțunefkent s ɣuṛ Baba-tneɣ Ṛebbi akk-d Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:4 Lmasiḥ isebbel iman-is ɣef ddnubat-nneɣ, iwakken a ɣ-isellek si yir lǧil-agi, s lebɣi n Baba-tneɣ Ṛebbi.

1:5 I nețța tamanegt si lǧil ɣer lǧil ! Amin !

1:6 Wehmeɣ imi twexxṛem s lemɣawla ɣef win i wen-d-issawlen s ṛṛeḥma n Lmasiḥ, iwakken aț-țḍefṛem lexbaṛ n lxiṛ nniḍen.

1:7 Ur wen-d-nniɣ ara akka am akken yella lexbaṛ n lxiṛ nniḍen , lameɛna qqaṛeɣ-awen-d akka, axaṭer llan kra lɣaci i kkun-icewwlen yerna bɣan ad sɛewjen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ yeɛnan Lmasiḥ.

1:8 ?as a lukan d nukni, neɣ d lmelk ara d-yasen seg igenni a wen-d ibbecceṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ yemxalafen ɣef win i wen-d-nbecceṛ si tazwara, atan ad yețwanɛel !

1:9 Nenna-yawen-t-id si zik, a s-d ɛiwdeɣ tura : yețwanɛel win ara wen-d ibeccṛen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ yemxalafen ɣef win i tqeblem !

1:10 Ihi tura, eɛni d lemziya n yemdanen i țnadiɣ ? Xaṭi ! D lemziya n Sidi Ṛebbi i țnadiɣ. Neɣ eɛni țnadiɣ ad ɛeǧbeɣ i yemdanen ? Lemmer i mazal țnadiɣ ad ɛeǧbeɣ i yemdanen, tili ur țțilliɣ ara d aqeddac n Lmasiḥ.

1:11 A wen-d-iniɣ ay atmaten belli lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ i wen-d-beccṛeɣ mačči s ɣuṛ wemdan i d-yekka ;

1:12 axaṭer, nekk s yiman-iw mačči d amdan i yi-t-id-issawḍen neɣ i yi t-islemden, lameɛna d Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i yi-t-id-iweḥḥan.

1:13 Teslam ɣef tikli-inu n zik asmi lliɣ ttabaɛeɣ ddin n wat Isṛail ; teslam amek i qehṛeɣ tajmaɛt n imasiḥiyen d wamek bɣiɣ a ten-snegreɣ.

1:14 Asmi lliɣ ḍefṛeɣ ddin d leɛwayed n wat Isṛail, ifeɣ akk tizyiwin-iw akk-d wid n laṣel-iw, axaṭer ṭṭfeɣ di leɛwayed n lejdud-iw akken ilaq.

1:15 Lameɛna s ṛṛeḥma-s, Sidi Ṛebbi ixtaṛ-iyi-d uqbel a d-laleɣ yerna yessawel-iyi-d iwakken ad qedceɣ fell-as.

1:16 Asmi i yi-d-ibeggen Sidi Ṛebbi Mmi-s iwakken ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i t-iɛnan ger wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail, ur cawṛeɣ ula d yiwen ;

1:17 yerna ur uliɣ ara ɣer temdint Lquds ɣer wid yellan d ṛṛusul uqbel-iw, lameɛna ṛuḥeɣ ɣer tmurt n waɛṛaben ; syenna uɣaleɣ-ed daɣen ɣer temdint n Dimacq.

1:18 Mi ɛeddan tlata iseggasen, uliɣ ɣer temdint Lquds iwakken ad issineɣ Buṭrus, qqimeɣ ɣuṛ-es xemseṭṭac wussan.

1:19 Lameɛna ur ẓriɣ ula d yiwen nniḍen si ṛṛusul, anagar Yeɛqub, gma-s n Ssid-nneɣ.

1:20 Ayen i wen-d-uriɣ akka ț-țideț, zdat Ṛebbi ur skiddibeɣ ara.

1:21 Syenna, ṛuḥeɣ ɣer tmura n Surya d Silisya ;

1:22 kra wayen kkiɣ dinna, imasiḥiyen n tejmuyaɛ n tmurt n Yahuda leɛmeṛ ẓrin udem-iw.

1:23 Slan kan qqaṛen fell-i : win akken yellan ițqehhiṛ deg-nneɣ, atan tura ițbecciṛ liman i gebɣa zik-nni a t-issenger.

1:24 Dɣa țḥemmiden Ṛebbi ɣef ddemma-w.

2:1 Mi ɛeddan ṛbeɛṭac iseggasen, ɛawdeɣ uliɣ ɣer temdint n Lquds akk-d Barnabas, wwiɣ daɣen yid-i gma-tneɣ Tit.

2:2 Uliɣ, axaṭer d Sidi Ṛebbi i yi-t-id yumṛen deg uweḥḥi. Sfehmeɣ-asen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i țbecciṛeɣ ger wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail ; mliɣ-asen-t weḥḥed-sen i yimeqqranen n tejmaɛt iwakken ayen xedmeɣ armi d ass-a ur ițṛuḥ ara baṭel.

2:3 Tit, aṛfiq-iw yellan d ayunani, yiwen ur iḥettem fell-as ad iḍheṛ,

2:4 ɣas akken llan yir watmaten i d-ikecmen gar-aneɣ s tuffra, bɣan a t-ḥeṛsen ad iḍheṛ. Kecmen-d gar-aneɣ iwakken ad walin tilelli i nesɛa di tikli-nneɣ akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, bɣan a ɣ-rren d aklan-nsen.

2:5 Ula d yiwet n tikkelt ur nekni zdat-sen, iwakken tideț yellan di lexbaṛ n lxiṛ aț-țeqqim gar-awen.

2:6 Ma d wid yesɛan lqima gar-awen, [ ur iyi-tewqiɛ ara lmeɛna d acu i llan zik, axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi ur ixeddem ara lxilaf ger yemdanen ] ; wid-nni yesɛan lqima ur ḥețțmen ara fell-i ad xedmeɣ ayen nniḍen.

2:7 Lameɛna walan belli Sidi Ṛebbi iwekkel-iyi iwakken ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail, am akken i gceggeɛ Buṭrus ad ibecceṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wat Isṛail.

2:8 Axaṭer, Sidi Ṛebbi i gerran Buṭrus d ṛṛasul n wid yellan n wat Isṛail, yerra-yi ula d nekk d ṛṛasul n wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail.

2:9 Yeɛqub, Buṭrus akk-d Yuḥenna yețwaḥesben d imeqqranen n tejmaɛt, setɛeṛfen belli d Sidi Ṛebbi i yi-d ifkan ccɣel-agi, dɣa selmen fell-aneɣ afus deg ufus iwakken a ɣ-d-beggnen belli qeblen ayagi. Imiren nemsefham : nutni ad ṛuḥen ad beccṛen i wat Isṛail, ma d nekk akk-d Barnabas, a nṛuḥ a nbecceṛ i wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail ;

2:10 acu kan, weṣṣan-aɣ iwakken ur ntețțu ara igellilen n tejmuyaɛ-nsen yellan di temdint n Lquds, ayagi ur stehzaɣ ara deg-s, xedmeɣ-t seg-wul.

2:11 Asmi i d-yusa Buṭrus ɣer temdint n Antyuc, qubleɣ-t ɣer wudem-is zdat lɣaci, axaṭer yeɣleḍ.

2:12 Uqbel a d-asen kra n yemdanen i d-iceggeɛ Yeɛqub, yella itețț akk-d watmaten ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail, lameɛna akken kan i d-wwḍen, iḥbes lmakla yid-sen iwexxeṛ fell-asen, axaṭer yuggad wid iṭṭfen di lɛaddat n wat Isṛail.

2:13 Atmaten nniḍen n wat Isṛail xedmen am nețța, wexxṛen ula d nutni, yuɣal armi ula d Barnabas iɛuned-iten.

2:14 Mi walaɣ ur teddun ara akken ilaq am akken i d-yeqqaṛ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ , nniɣ i Buṭrus zdat lɣaci meṛṛa : ma yella kečč yellan seg wat Isṛail tețɛiceḍ am wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail, amek ara tḥeṛṣeḍ wid ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail ad ɛicen am wat Isṛail ?

2:15 Nukni ur nelli ara d imednuben am leǧnas nniḍen, laṣel-nneɣ seg wat Isṛail.

2:16 Lameɛna neẓra belli amdan yețțuɣal d aḥeqqi zdat Sidi Ṛebbi s liman-ines di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, mačči imi gxeddem ayen i d-teqqaṛ ccariɛa ; daymi ula d nukni numen s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, iwakken a nuɣal d iḥeqqiyen zdat Sidi Ṛebbi s liman di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, mačči imi nxeddem ayen i d-teqqaṛ ccariɛa n Musa. Axaṭer ula d yiwen ur izmir ad yuɣal d aḥeqqi zdat Ṛebbi imi i gettabaɛ ccariɛa n Musa.

2:17 Nukni nețnadi a nuɣal d iḥeqqiyen s liman di Lmasiḥ ; ma nufa iman-nneɣ d imednuben ula d nukni am leǧnas nniḍen, eɛni d Lmasiḥ i ɣ-ițțawin ɣer ddnub ? Xaṭi !

2:18 Axaṭer ma uɣaleɣ ɣer ccariɛa nni i ǧǧiɣ, ihi rriɣ iman-iw d amɛaṣi ;

2:19 ɣef wayen yeɛnan ccariɛa aql-iyi mmuteɣ, yerna d nețțat i yi-wwin ɣer lmut ; ayagi yedṛa-d iwakken ad sɛuɣ tudert di Sidi Ṛebbi ; aql-iyi țwasemmṛeɣ ɣef wumidag akk-d Lmasiḥ.

2:20 Tura mačči d nekk i gețțidiren, d Lmasiḥ i gețțidiren deg-i ; axaṭer tudert-iw di ddunit, țɛiceɣ-ț s liman di Mmi-s n Sidi Ṛebbi i yi-ḥemmlen yerna isebbel iman-is fell-i.

2:21 Ur sseɣlayeɣ ara lqima n ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi, axaṭer ma nezmer a nuɣal d iḥeqqiyen zdat Ṛebbi s ccariɛa n Musa, ihi Lmasiḥ yemmut ɣef wulac !

3:1 Ay at Galasya ur nessin leṣlaḥ nsen ! Anwa i kkun-iseḥḥren kunwi iwumi i d-nessefhem akken ilaq lmut n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ɣef wumidag ?

3:2 ?ef wayagi kan i bɣiɣ a kkun steqsiɣ : amek i wen-d-ițțunefk Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, imi i txeddmem ayen i d-teqqaṛ ccariɛa neɣ imi teslam yerna tumnem s lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ ?

3:3 Eɛni ixuṣṣ-ikkun leɛqel ? Amek, di tazwara tețțeklem ɣef Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, tura tebɣam aț-țețțeklem ɣef yiman-nwen ?

3:4 Eɛni ayen akk i tenɛețțabem iṛuḥ baṭel ? Ur ḥsiɣ ara iṛuḥ baṭel !

3:5 Win i wen-d-ifkan Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, ixeddmen lbeṛhanat gar-awen, eɛni ixeddem-iten imi i tettabaɛem ccariɛa neɣ imi tumnem s lexbaṛ n lxiṛ iwumi teslam ?

3:6 Akken yura : Ibṛahim yumen s Ṛebbi, yețkel fell-as, daymi i t-iḥseb Sidi Ṛebbi d aḥeqqi.

3:7 Ilaq aț-țfehmem ihi, wid iteddun s liman, d nutni i d arraw n tideț n Sidna Ibṛahim.

3:8 Si zik i d-ixebbeṛ Sidi Ṛebbi di tira iqedsen belli ad yerr leǧnas ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail d iḥeqqiyen ɣef ddemma n liman nsen, mi genna i Sidna Ibṛahim lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ : Leǧnas meṛṛa n ddunit ad țțubarken yis-ek ;

3:9 ihi kra n win yumnen s Ṛebbi akken yumen yis Ibṛahim, ad ițțubarek am nețța.

3:10 Axaṭer wid yettabaɛen ccariɛa iwakken ad țwaqeblen ɣer Ṛebbi, deɛwessu tezga fell-asen imi yura : ad ițwanɛel kra n win yettabaɛen ccariɛa, ur nxeddem ara ayen akk yuran deg-s.

3:11 Ula d yiwen ur izmir ad yuɣal d aḥeqqi zdat Ṛebbi s ccariɛa, ayagi d ayen ibanen imi yura : Aḥeqqi ad yidir s liman.

3:12 Abrid n ccariɛa d webrid n liman mxalafen, imi yura : d win ara ixedmen ayen akk i d-teqqaṛ ccariɛa ara yesɛun tudert n dayem.

3:13 Lmasiḥ icufeɛ-aɣ-d si deɛwessu n ccariɛa, d nețța i gețwaneɛlen deg umkan-nneɣ ; axaṭer yura : ițwanɛel kra n win ițwaɛelqen ɣer ttejṛa.

3:14 Ayagi yedṛa-d iwakken s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, leǧnas ur nelli ara n wat Isṛail ad weṛten lbaṛaka i gefka Sidi Ṛebbi i Ibṛahim ; s liman ara ɣ-d ițțunefk Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i ɣ-iwɛed Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:15 Ay atmaten, a wen-d-fkeɣ lemtel yellan di lɛaddat-nneɣ : m'ara yexdem yiwen leɛqed akken ilaq, yiwen ur izmir a t-inkeṛ neɣ a s-yernu kra.

3:16 Ihi, Sidi Ṛebbi yefka lemɛahda i Ibṛahim akk-d win ara d-ilalen seg-s, ( ur d-yenni ara : i wid ara d-ilalen seg-s am akken aṭas i gellan ). Di tira iqedsen yura : i win ara d-ilalen seg-k ; win i ɣef yura wayagi, d Lmasiḥ.

3:17 Atah wayen bɣiɣ a d-iniɣ : di tazwara Sidi Ṛebbi yesbedd leɛqed akken ilaq, ccariɛa i d-yusan ṛebɛa meyya utlatin iseggasen deffir-es, ur tezmir ara aț-țemḥu leɛqed-agi, neɣ m'ulac aț-țeɣli lqima n lemɛahda n Sidi Ṛebbi.

3:18 Lemmer si ccariɛa ara newṛet leslak, acuɣeṛ lemɛahda ? Axaṭer s lemɛahda-agi i d-ibeggen Sidi Ṛebbi ṛṛeḥma-s i Ibṛahim.

3:19 Acuɣeṛ ihi i d-tețțunefk ccariɛa ? Tețțunefk-ed iwakken a d-tesbeggen belli nɛuṣa lumuṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi, alamma yusa-d win akken ara d ilalen si dderya n Ibṛahim iwumi d tețțunefk lemɛahda. D lmalayekkat i d-yewwin ccariɛa ɣer ufus n Sidna Musa iwakken a ț-id-issiweḍ i yemdanen.

3:20 Sidi Ṛebbi iceggeɛ-ed amdan iwakken a d-yessiweḍ ccariɛa i wiyaḍ, meɛna ur iḥwaǧ ara amdan iwakken ad isbedd leɛqed yid-sen.

3:21 Eɛni ccariɛa txulef lemɛahdat n Sidi Ṛebbi ? Xaṭi ! Lemmer ccariɛa tezmer a d-tefk tudert i wemdan, tili yezmer wemdan ad yuɣal d aḥeqqi zdat Ṛebbi s ccariɛa.

3:22 Lameɛna tira iqedsen nnant-ed : ddunit meṛṛa tella seddaw tezmert n ddnub ; iwacu ? Iwakken lemɛahda n Sidi Ṛebbi aț-țețțunefk i yemdanen meṛṛa ɣef ddemma n liman-nsen di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:23 Uqbel a d-yas lweqt i deg ara namen s Lmasiḥ, nella seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa, armi d ass i deg i d-yedheṛ webrid n liman.

3:24 S wakka, nețțuṛebba-d s ccariɛa armi d asmi d-yusa Lmasiḥ iwakken a ɣ-yerr d iḥeqqiyen zdat Ṛebbi s liman.

3:25 Tura yusa-d lweqt i deg ara namen s Lmasiḥ, ifuk fell-aneɣ leḥkum n ccariɛa.

3:26 Axaṭer tuɣalem akk d arraw n Sidi Ṛebbi s liman di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:27 Kunwi akk yețwaɣeḍsen s yisem n Lmasiḥ, tețɛicim tudert tajḍiṭ yellan di Lmasiḥ.

3:28 Tura ulac lxilaf ger wat Isṛail d iyunaniyen, ger waklan d iḥeṛṛiyen, ger yergazen ț-țilawin, axaṭer kunwi akk tuɣalem d yiwen di Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:29 Yerna ma tellam d ayla n Lmasiḥ, aql-ikkun si dderya n Ibṛahim, wid ara iweṛten akken i t-id-iwɛed Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:1 Atah wayen i bɣiɣ a t-id-iniɣ : qqaṛeɣ-awen skud win ara iweṛten mazal-it meẓẓi, ur yemxalaf ara nețța d win yellan d akli, ɣas akken d nețța ara yuɣalen d bab n kullec ;

4:2 lameɛna ilaq-as ad yaɣ awal i wid ițelhayen yid-es, iseddayen lecɣal-is, alamma yessaweḍ leɛmeṛ akken i d-iweṣṣa baba-s.

4:3 Akken daɣen ula d nukni asmi nella meẓẓiyit, nɛac seddaw leḥkum n leqwanen n ddunit,

4:4 meɛna asmi i d-yewweḍ lweqt i ɣ-iweɛɛed Sidi Ṛebbi, iceggeɛ-ed Mmi-s i d-ilulen si tmeṭṭut, iɛac seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa,

4:5 iwakken a d-ifdu wid yellan seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa, iwakken a nuɣal d arraw-is.

4:6 Tura imi nuɣal d arraw n Sidi Ṛebbi, ifka-yaɣ-d ɣer wulawen-nneɣ Ṛṛuḥ n Mmi-s s wayes i nessawal i Ṛebbi : « Abba » ! Yeɛni : a Baba !

4:7 S wakka, ikkes fell-awen uzaglu n tkelwa, tuɣalem seg warraw-is ; ma tellam seg warraw-is, d kunwi ara iweṛten ayen i wen-iwɛed Sidi Ṛebbi s ṛṛeḥma-ines.

4:8 Zik-nni ur tessinem ara Ṛebbi yerna tețɛebbidem lemqamat d ssadaț deg umkan n Ṛebbi.

4:9 Lameɛna tura mi tessnem Ṛebbi, yerna nețța iḥseb ikkun d arraw-is, amek armi tebɣam aț-țuɣalem ɣer lɛaddat n zik ur nesɛi lmeɛna iwakken aț-țilim seddaw uzaglu-nsent ?

4:10 Tețțakem lqima i leɛyudat n kra wussan, kra wagguren, kra lewqat d kra yiseggasen !

4:11 Uggadeɣ ayen i nɛețțabeɣ akk fell-awen iṛuḥ baṭel.

4:12 Ay atmaten di leɛnaya-nwen, uɣalet am nekkini, imi ula d nekk lliɣ am kunwi. Ur iyi-texdimem acemma n diri.

4:13 Ur tecfim ara belli d lehlak i yi-ṭṭfen ɣuṛ-wen mi wen-beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ass amezwaru.

4:14 ?as akken lehlak-iw yeɛfen, ur teɛyim deg-i, ur iyi-teḥqiṛem, lameɛna telham yis-i am akken d lmelk n Sidi Ṛebbi neɣ d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ s yiman-is i lliɣ.

4:15 Anda-t lfeṛḥ-nni-nwen n zik ? Acu i gedṛan yid-wen ? Zemreɣ ad cehdeɣ fell-awen belli, lemmer d ayen iwumi tzemrem a t-txedmem, tili tekksem-d allen-nwen iwakken a yi-tent-id-tefkem.

4:16 Eɛni uɣaleɣ awen tura d aɛdaw imi i wen-d qqaṛeɣ tideț ?

4:17 Imdanen-agi țbegginen-awen-d belli ḥemmlen-kkun, lameɛna nutni ččuṛen ț-țiḥila ; bɣan kan a kkun sbeɛden fell-i iwakken a ten-tettebɛem nutni.

4:18 Yelha ma ixeddem yiwen lxiṛ, mačči kan m'ara iliɣ gar-awen lameɛna di mkul lweqt.

4:19 Ay arraw-iw, aql-i nɛețțabeɣ tura daɣen ɣef ddemma-nwen am tmeṭṭut yeṭṭfen addud, alamma iban-ed Lmasiḥ deg-wen.

4:20 Acḥal mennaɣ ad iliɣ gar-awen di teswiɛt-agi i wakken a wen-d mmeslayeɣ akken nniḍen, axaṭer aql-iyi tḥeyyṛeɣ ɣef ddemma-nwen.

4:21 Init-iyi-d, kunwi yebɣan aț-țilim seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa, eɛni ur teslim ara i wayen i d-teqqaṛ ccariɛa ?

4:22 Atah wayen i d-teqqaṛ : Sidna Ibṛahim yesɛa sin warraw-is, yiwen seg Haǧiṛa yellan ț-țaklit, wayeḍ seg Saṛa yellan ț-țaḥeṛṛit.

4:23 Lameɛna mmi-s n taklit-nni, ilul-ed s lebɣi n wemdan, ma d mmi-s n tḥeṛṛit-nni, ilul-ed ɣef ddemma n lemɛahda n Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:24 Taqsiṭ-agi tesɛa lmeɛna nniḍen : snat n tilawin-agi țusemmant d sin leɛqud : yiwen n leɛqed d win n wedrar n Sinay i d-ițaǧǧan aklan, yeɛni : d Haǧiṛa.

4:25 Haǧiṛa, d adrar n Sinay yellan di tmurt n waɛṛaben, tețmettil-ed tamdint n Lquds n tura, i gellan ț-țaklit s nețțat s warraw-is.

4:26 Lameɛna tamdint n Lquds yellan deg igenwan ț-țaḥeṛṛit, d nețțat i d yemma-tneɣ.

4:27 Axaṭer yura : Efṛeḥ kemm a tiɛiqeṛt ur nețțarew ara ! Ɛeggeḍ s lfeṛḥ kemm ur nessin lewjaɛ n tarrawt ! Axaṭer tameṭṭut yețwaḥeqṛen, aț-țes ɛu dderya akteṛ n tin yesɛan argaz-is .

4:28 Ma d kunwi ay atmaten, d arraw n lemɛahda i tellam am Isḥaq.

4:29 Zik-nni, win i d-ilulen s lebɣi n wemdan ițqehhiṛ win i d-ilulen s lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ n Ṛebbi, akka i gella lḥal ar ass-a.

4:30 Lameɛna d acu yuran di tira iqedsen ? Enfu taklit d mmi-s ;axaṭer mmi-s n taklit ur ilaq ara ad iwṛet akk-d mmi-s n tmeṭṭut taḥeṛṛit .

4:31 Akka ay atmaten, ur nelli ara d arraw n taklit lameɛna d arraw n tmeṭṭut taḥeṛṛit.

5:1 Ɛisa Lmasiḥ isellek-aɣ iwakken a nesɛu tilleli n tideț. Ihi ḥerzet tilelli-agi, ḥadret aț-țuɣalem d aklan !

5:2 ?esset-iyi-d ! Nekk Bulus, nniɣ awen-d : ma yella tḍehṛem, Lmasiḥ ur kkun-infiɛ ara.

5:3 Yerna ad ɛawdeɣ a d-iniɣ i yal argaz i gḍehṛen, belli ilzem ad ixdem ayen akk i d-tenna ccariɛa.

5:4 Kunwi yețnadin aț-țuɣalem d iḥeqqiyen zdat Ṛebbi s ccariɛa, ur d-yeqqim wacemma gar-awen d Lmasiḥ, tețwaḥeṛmem si ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:5 Ma d nukni, nessaram a ɣ-yerr Sidi Ṛebbi d iḥeqqiyen zdat-es ; ayagi nețṛaǧu-t s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen akk-d liman.

5:6 Axaṭer, di tikli-nneɣ akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, neḍheṛ neɣ ur neḍhiṛ ara, ayagi ur yesɛi ara azal ; ayen yesɛan azal, d liman i d-țbegginen lecɣal i nxeddem s leḥmala.

5:7 Di tazwara tewwim abrid ilaqen, anwa i kkun-issufɣen i webrid n tideț ?

5:8 Ṛṛay-agi ur d-yekki ara s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-d-issawlen.

5:9 Akken qqaṛen : « Ciṭṭuḥ n temtunt ( iɣes n temtunt ) yessalay akk arukti.»

5:10 Lameɛna Sidi Ṛebbi ihedden ul-iw fell-awen, țekleɣ belli ur tețxemmimem ara akken nniḍen. Lameɛna win i kkun-icewwlen, akken ibɣu yili, a t-iɛaqeb Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:11 Lemmer mazal nehhuɣ ar tura ɣef ṭṭhaṛa, iwacu i yi-mazal țwaqehṛeɣ ? Tili lexbaṛ i nețbecciṛ ɣef Lmasiḥ ițwasemmṛen ɣef wumidag, ur yețțili d ugur ula i yiwen.

5:12 Wid-nni i kkun-icewwlen ɣef ṭṭhaṛa ad ṛuḥen ihi ad ɛegben iman-nsen.

5:13 Ay atmaten, Sidi Ṛebbi ifka yawen tilelli, ur d-țafet ara sebba s wayes ara txedmem s tlelli-agi lebɣi n tnefsit-nwen ; lameɛna beggnet-ed lmaḥibba yellan gar-awen.

5:14 Axaṭer lameṛ i gesdukklen meṛṛa ccariɛa d wagi : ḥemmel lɣiṛ-ik am yiman-ik .

5:15 Ma yella tețțemkerracem am lewḥuc yerna wa itețț wa, ḥadret iman-nwen neɣ m'ulac yiwen ad issenger wayeḍ.

5:16 Ddut ihi s lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, iwakken ur txeddmem ara lebɣi n tnefsit-nwen.

5:17 Axaṭer lebɣi n tnefsit ixulef lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, akken daɣen lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ixulef lebɣi n tnefsit ; d iɛdawen wway gar-asen, daymi ur tezmirem ara aț-țxedmem akken tebɣam.

5:18 Lameɛna ma yella tleḥḥum s lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, atan ihi ur tellim ara seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa.

5:19 Axaṭer, lefɛayel n tnefsit țbanen : zzna, leḥṛam, lefsad,

5:20 zzyaṛa n lemqamat, ssḥur, lkeṛh, ccwal, tismin, zzɛaf, wid yebɣan a d-kken sennig wiyaḍ, assexṛeb, wid ifeṛqen ț-țirebbaɛ,

5:21 ṭṭmeɛ, ssekṛan, at iɛebbaḍ ur nesɛi lqaɛ d wayen akk ițemcabin ɣer wayagi meṛṛa. Ɛeggneɣ-awen-d am akken i wen-d-nniɣ yakan : wid i gxeddmen lecɣal-agi ur seɛɛun ara amkan di tgelda n Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:22 Lameɛna atan wayen i d-itekken si Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen : leḥmala, lfeṛḥ, lehna, ṣṣbeṛ, lehhu, leḥnana, liman,

5:23 ul yeṣfan, aɣlab n tnefsit ; eɛni ccariɛa tugi annect-agi ?

5:24 Wid yellan n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, semmṛen ɣef wumidag lebɣi n tnefsit-nsen, s ccehwat-is d umenni ines.

5:25 Imi d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i ɣ-d ifkan tudert, ilaq-aɣ ihi a neddu di lebɣi ines.

5:26 Ur ilaq a nessimɣuṛ iman nneɣ, ur ilaq a nețnaɣ neɣ a nețțemyasam wway-gar-aneɣ.

6:1 Ay atmaten, kunwi ileḥḥun s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, ma teṭṭfem yiwen yeɣleḍ, erret-ț-id ɣer webrid s leḥnana ; ḥadret ɣef yiman-nwen, axaṭer tzemrem aț-țeɣlim ula d kunwi deg ujeṛṛeb.

6:2 ?emɛawanet deg unerfud n tɛekkmin nwen, s wakka ara tḍuɛem ayen i d-tenna ccariɛa n Lmasiḥ.

6:3 Ma yeḥseb yiwen iman-is yesɛa lqima nețța ur yeswi kra, ɣef yiman-is i gețkellix.

6:4 Ilaq mkul yiwen ad imeyyez tikli-ines, imiren ma yufa ayen s wayes ara izux, ad izux i yiman-is kan mačči alamma imettel iman-is ɣer wiyaḍ.

6:5 Axaṭer mkul yiwen ad yerfed taɛkumt-is weḥd-es.

6:6 Win iwumi sselmaden awal n Ṛebbi ilaq ad yeǧǧ amur deg wayen akk yesɛa i win i t-isselmaden.

6:7 ?adret aț-țɣelṭem : yiwen ur yezmir ad istehzi ɣef Ṛebbi ; ayen yezreɛ wemdan, a t-id-imger.

6:8 Win yettabaɛen lebɣi n tnefsit-is, a t-tessiweḍ ɣer lmut ; ma d win iteddun s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen ad isɛu tudert n dayem.

6:9 Ilaq a nxeddem lxiṛ mbla ɛeyyu ; axaṭer m'ur nefcil ara, a d-nemger m'ara d-yaweḍ lweqt.

6:10 Ihi, skud yella wamek, a nexdem lxiṛ i yemdanen meṛṛa, abeɛda atmaten di liman !

6:11 Walit tira-agi : s ufus-iw i wen tent-id-uriɣ.

6:12 Wid meṛṛa i gebɣan ad ḥețțmen fell-awen ṭṭhaṛa, bɣan a d-sbeggnen iman-nsen zdat imdanen belli ṭṭfen di leɛwayed, iwakken ur țwaqehhaṛen ara ɣef ddemma n umidag n Lmasiḥ.

6:13 Widak-agi iḍehṛen, nutni s yiman nsen ur ttabaɛen ara ccariɛa ; lameɛna bɣan aț-țḍehṛem iwakken ad zuxxen imi ḥettmen fell-awen ṭṭhaṛa-agi.

6:14 Ma d nekk ur bɣiɣ ad zuxxeɣ s wacemma, anagar s lmut n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ɣef wumidag ; axaṭer s lmut n Lmasiḥ ɣef wumidag uɣaleɣ ur ḥsibeɣ ara ddunit-agi, akken daɣen ddunit ur iyi-teḥsib ara ula d nekk.

6:15 Axaṭer ayen yesɛan azal, mačči d ṭṭhaṛa, lameɛna ț-țalalit tajḍiṭ i d-itekken s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

6:16 I wid akk itebɛen abrid-agi , a sen-iniɣ: lehna d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi a wen-d țțunefkent i kunwi d wegdud n Sidi Ṛebbi meṛṛa !

6:17 Sya ɣer zdat, ur ḥwaǧeɣ yiwen a yi-isseḥzen ; axaṭer ccwami yellan di lǧețța-w țbegginent-ed belli d aqeddac n Sidna Ɛisa i lliɣ.

6:18 Ay atmaten, ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ aț-țili yid-wen meṛṛa ! Amin !
