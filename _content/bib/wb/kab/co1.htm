Kabyle Bible: 1 Corinthians

1:1 Nekk Bulus, iwumi i d-issawel Sidi Ṛebbi s lebɣi-ines iwakken ad iliɣ d amceggeɛ n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d gma-tneɣ Sustin,

1:2 i tejmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi i gellan di temdint n Kurintus, i wid i d-yextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ad ilin d imqedsen, d wid akk yellan di mkul amkan ineddhen s yisem n Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, i gellan d Ssid-nsen d Ssid nneɣ :

1:3 ṛṛeḥma ț-țalwit a wen-d țțunefkent s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi Baba-tneɣ akk-d Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:4 Zgiɣ țḥemmideɣ Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen ɣef ṛṛeḥma i wen-d-yefka s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ,

1:5 axaṭer yis i kkun-id yeṛzeq s mkul lxiṛ, ama s wawal-is ama s tmusni-ines,

1:6 imi cchada ɣef Lmasiḥ tṛeṣṣa nezzeh deg-wen.

1:7 Daymi ur kkun-ixuṣṣ wacemma n lxiṛ s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, kunwi yețṛaǧun ass i deg ara d-yuɣal Ssid-nneɣ.

1:8 A kkun isseǧhed alamma ț-țaggara iwakken a d-tbanem mbla lɛib ass n tuɣalin n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

1:9 Sidi Ṛebbi yețțaṭaf deg wawal-is ; d nețța i wen-d-issawlen iwakken aț-țɛicem s tdukli akk-d Mmi-s, Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ.

1:10 Ay atmaten, a kkun-nhuɣ s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ : msefhamet wway gar-awen, ur ilaq ara a d-yili beṭṭu gar-awen, ddukklet, sɛut yiwen uxemmem d yiwen n ṛṛay.

1:11 Axaṭer ay atmaten, irgazen n wexxam n Kluwi ṣṣawḍen-iyi-d lexbaṛ belli ițțili-d umennuɣ gar-awen.

1:12 Ayen bɣiɣ a d-iniɣ : sliɣ belli llan gar-awen wid yeqqaṛen :« nekk ddiɣ d Bulus », ma d wayeḍ yeqqaṛ : « nekk țekkiɣ d Abulus », wayeḍ daɣen : « nekk ddiɣ d Buṭrus », llan daɣen wid yeqqaṛen : « nekk țekkiɣ di Lmasiḥ ».

1:13 Amek akka, tɣilem Lmasiḥ yebḍa ? Eɛni d Bulus i gețwasemmṛen fell-awen neɣ s yisem n Bulus i tețwaɣeḍsem ?

1:14 Ad ḥemmdeɣ Ṛebbi imi ur sɣeḍseɣ ula d yiwen deg-wen anagar Krisbus d Gayus.

1:15 Akka, yiwen deg-wen ur izmir a d-yini belli yețwaɣḍes s yisem-iw.

1:16 Tella daɣen twacult n Stifanas, d nekk i ț-isɣeḍsen meɛna ur ḥsiɣ ara sɣeḍseɣ yiwen nniḍen dinna.

1:17 Lmasiḥ ur iyi-d-iceggeɛ ara iwakken ad sɣeḍseɣ meɛna iceggeɛ iyi-d ad beccṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ; mačči s yimeslayen i d-yekkan si lefhama n wemdan iwakken ur tekkseɣ ara lqima i lmut n Lmasiḥ ɣef wumidag.

1:18 Ț-țideț, abecceṛ n lmut n Lmasiḥ ɣef wumidag ț-țidderwect i wid iḍaɛen ; meɛna ɣuṛ-nneɣ nukni yellan deg webrid n leslak, ț-țazmert n Ṛebbi.

1:19 Atan wayen i d-nnant tira iqedsen : Ad snegreɣ tamusni n imusnawen, ad rreɣ d ula+ c lefhama n wid ifehmen+ .

1:20 Anda-t uɛeqli ? Anda-t umusnaw ? Anda-t uḥṛic ara d-isfehmen ayen ideṛṛun di lǧil-agi ? Ur d-isbeggen ara Sidi Ṛebbi belli tamusni n ddunit-agi d lehbala ?

1:21 Imi at ddunit s lefhama-nsen ur ɛqilen ara tamusni n Sidi Ṛebbi, nețța yebɣa ad isellek wid yumnen yis s lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ i ḥesben wiyaḍ d lehbala.

1:22 At Isṛail ssuturen lbeṛhanat, iyunaniyen țnadin tamusni ;

1:23 ma d nukni nețbecciṛ Lmasiḥ, win akken yețțusemmṛen ɣef wumidag, yellan d țbehdila i wat Isṛail, d lehbala ɣer iyunaniyen.

1:24 Meɛna wid iwumi i d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi, ama d at Isṛail ama d iyunaniyen, umnen belli d nețța i d Lmasiḥ, i ț-țazmert n Ṛebbi akk-d tmusni-ines.

1:25 Axaṭer ayen akken ḥesben yemdanen d lehbala n Sidi Ṛebbi, ț-țamusni iṣeḥḥan akteṛ n tmusni n yemdanen, ayen ḥesben daɣen d feccal n Sidi Ṛebbi, yeɣleb lǧehd n yemdanen.

1:26 Walit ay atmaten, kunwi iwumi d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi : ur llin gar-awen aṭas n wid yesɛan tamusni n ddunit, ur llin aṭas n wid yesɛan imukan ɛlayen, ur llin daɣen aṭas inaṣliyen yesɛan lqima tameqqrant ;

1:27 meɛna Sidi Ṛebbi yextaṛ ayen yellan d lehbala ɣer wemdan iwakken ad isneḥcam imusnawen, yextaṛ ayen ur nesɛi tazmert di ddunit iwakken ad isneḥcam wid iǧehden.

1:28 Sidi Ṛebbi yextaṛ ayen ur nesɛi azal, yețwaḥeqṛen di ddunit akk-d wayen yețwaḥesben d ulac, iwakken ad isseɣli lqima n wayen yesɛan azal ɣer yemdanen.

1:29 Akka, yiwen ur izmir ad izuxx zdat Sidi Ṛebbi.

1:30 Imi d Sidi Ṛebbi i kkun-isdukklen d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, d nețța i ɣ-yuɣalen d lefhama i d yekkan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi. Yis i ɣ yerra Sidi Ṛebbi d iḥeqqiyen, iṣeffa tikli-nneɣ, yefda-yaɣ-d si ddnub.

1:31 Am akken yura di tira iqedsen : Win yebɣan ad izuxx, ad izuxx s lecɣal n Ṛebbi.

2:1 Daymi ay atmaten mi n-ṛuḥeɣ ɣuṛ wen iwakken a wen mmeslayeɣ ɣef lbaḍna n Sidi Ṛebbi, ur a wen mmeslayeɣ ara s yimeslayen yeččuṛen d zzux neɣ s tmusni yessewhamen.

2:2 Axaṭer ur nudaɣ ara a d mmeslayeɣ gar-awen ɣef wayen nniḍen anagar ɣef Ɛisa Lmasiḥ ; Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i gețwaṣemmṛen ɣef wumidag.

2:3 Ula d nekk asmi lliɣ ɣuṛ-wen, lliɣ fecleɣ, kukraɣ yerna tekcem-iyi tugdi.

2:4 Ayen i wen-slemdeɣ d wayen i wen-beccṛeɣ ur yebni ara ɣef tmusni n wemdan, lameɛna d ayen yebnan ɣef tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

2:5 Akka, liman-nwen ur yețrus ara ɣef tmusni n yemdanen, meɛna ɣef tezmert n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:6 Nesselmad tamusni-agi i wid yesɛan liman iǧehden ; tamusni-agi, mačči ț-țamusni n ddunit-agi neɣ ț-țin n tezmar n igelliden n ddunit-agi iteddun ɣer nnger.

2:7 Nețbecciṛ tamusni n Sidi Ṛebbi yellan d lbaḍna yeffren ɣef yemdanen, i ghegga si zik i lɛezz-nneɣ.

2:8 Ula d yiwen deg igelliden n zzman-agi ur ț-yessin, lemmer ssnen-ț tili ur semmṛen ara Bab n tmanegt ɣef wumidag.

2:9 Am akken i t-id-nnant tira iqedsen : Ayen ur twala tiṭ, ayen ur yesli umeẓẓuɣ+ , ayen ɣef ur ixemmem ara wemdan,+ d ayen i ghegga Sidi Ṛebbi i wid i t-iḥemmlen+ .

2:10 Sidi Ṛebbi ibeggen-aɣ-t-id i nukni s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen. Axaṭer Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yezmer ad iẓer ulamma d lecɣal yeffren n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:11 Anwa i geẓran ayen yellan deg wul n wemdan ? Anagar ṛṛuḥ-ines i geẓran ayen yellan deg wul-is. Yiwen ur yessin daɣen ixemmimen n Ṛebbi anagar Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi.

2:12 Nukni mačči d ṛṛuḥ n ddunit i neqbel, meɛna d Ṛṛuḥ i d-yekkan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, iwakken a nissin ayen akk i ɣ-d-yefka s ṛṛeḥma-ines.

2:13 Nukni ur nețmeslay ara s yimeslayen i tesselmad tmusni n wemdan, meɛna nețmeslay s yimeslayen i ɣ-isselmad Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi. Akka i nessefham tideț n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i wid yesɛan Ṛṛuḥ-agi.

2:14 Amdan ițeddun s lɛeqliya n ddunit ur iqebbel ara ayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi imi ɣuṛ-es ayagi d lehbala ; ur yezmir ara a t-yissin axaṭer s Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi i nezmer a t-nefhem.

2:15 Amdan yesɛan Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen n Sidi Ṛebbi yezmer ad ifhem kullec, ma d nețța, yiwen ur izmir a t-iḥaseb.

2:16 Akken yura di tira iqedsen : Anwa i gessnen ixemmimen n+ Sidi Ṛebbi,+ anwa i gzemren a t-yenhu + ? Ma d nukni, nesɛa axemmem n Lmasiḥ.

3:1 Ay atmaten, ur ufiɣ ara amek ara wen-mmeslayeɣ am akken țmeslayeɣ i yemdanen yesɛan Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, meɛna mmeslayeɣ-awen am yemdanen n ddunit, am wid yellan d arrac deg ubrid n Lmasiḥ.

3:2 Fkiɣ-awen ayefki mačči d aɣṛum axaṭer aɣṛum ur as-tezmirem ara, yerna ar tura ur as-tezmirem ara, mazal-ikkun tețɛicim s lebɣi n tnefsit nwen.

3:3 Imi mazal gar-awen tismin d umceččew, tbeggnem-d belli mazal ikkun tețɛicim s lɛeqliya-nwen yerna tleḥḥum am yemdanen n ddunit.

3:4 M'ara yini yiwen deg-wen : « nekk ddiɣ d Bulus », wayeḍ ad yini : « Nekk țekkiɣ d Abulus » ; eɛni ayagi mačči d lɛeqliya n ddunit ?

3:5 D acu-t Abulus, d acu-t Bulus ? D iqeddacen kan i kkun-id-yewwin aț-țamnem s Lmasiḥ ! Yal yiwen deg-nneɣ ixeddem ayen i s-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi a t-yexdem.

3:6 Nekk ẓẓiɣ imɣi, Abulus issew-it, meɛna d Sidi Ṛebbi i t-issegman.

3:7 Ihi mačči d win ițeẓẓun neɣ d win yesswayen i gesɛan azal meɛna d Sidi Ṛebbi yessegmuyen.

3:8 Win ițeẓẓun d win yesswayen ɛedlen ; mkul yiwen a s-yerr Sidi Ṛebbi s wakken yuklal di lxedma-s.

3:9 Axaṭer aql-aɣ neddukkel d iqeddacen ɣef lecɣal n Sidi Ṛebbi. Kunwi d iger i gxeddem Sidi Ṛebbi, d axxam i gbennu.

3:10 S ṛṛeḥma i yi-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi, am umusnaw n lebni, nekk sserseɣ lsas, wayeḍ yebna fell-as ; meɛna yal yiwen ad iḥader amek ara yebnu.

3:11 Yiwen ur izmir ad issers lsas nniḍen deg wemkan n win yersen yakan : Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

3:12 Ma yella win yebnan ɣef lsas-agi ama s ddheb, ama s lfeṭṭa, ama s yeẓra ɣlayen, ama s wesɣaṛ neɣ s wusaɣuṛ d walim,

3:13 mkul yiwen a d-iban ccɣel-is ass n lḥisab, axaṭer mkul ccɣel ad iɛeddi di tmes ; ț-țimes ara d-ibeggnen lecɣal n yal yiwen ma seḥḥan neɣ ur ṣeḥḥan ara.

3:14 Win yebnan ɣef lsas, ur t-tečči ara tmes wayen akken yebna, a t-id-yaweḍ lxiṛ ameqqran ;

3:15 ma d win iwumi tečča tmes wayen yexdem, a s-iṛuḥ lxiṛ-is ; ma d nețța ad ițțusellek, am win izegren times imneɛ.

3:16 Ur teẓrim ara belli kunwi d lǧameɛ n Sidi Ṛebbi i tellam ? Ur teẓrim ara belli Ṛṛuḥ-is yezdeɣ deg-wen ?

3:17 Ihi win yesseɣlin axxam n Sidi Ṛebbi, Sidi Ṛebbi a t-isseɣli axaṭer axxam n Sidi Ṛebbi d imqeddes, d kunwi i d axxam-is.

3:18 Ḥadret ad yili win ara iɣuṛṛen iman-is ! Ma yella gar-awen win iḥesben iman-is d aɛeqli di ddunit-a, ilaq ad yuɣal d amehbul iwakken ad yili d aɛeqli.

3:19 Axaṭer tamusni n ddunit-agi, ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi d lehbala. Tira iqedsen nnant-ed : Sidi Ṛebbi yețțeṭṭef imusnawen di tḥila-nsen.

3:20 Yura daɣen : Sidi Ṛebbi yessen ixemmimen n imusnawen, yeẓra belli d ixemmimen ur nesɛi azal.

3:21 Ḥadret ad yili win ara izuxxen s yemdanen, axaṭer kullec d ayla nwen ;

3:22 Bulus, Abulus neɣ Buṭrus, ddunit, tudert, lmut, lweqt-a d lweqt i d-iteddun, kullec d ayla-nwen ;

3:23 kunwi d ayla n Lmasiḥ, Lmasiḥ d ayla n Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:1 Ilaq a ɣ-ḥesben yemdanen belli d iqeddacen kan n Lmasiḥ i nella, d wid i gwekkel Sidi Ṛebbi i wessefhem n lbaḍna-ines.

4:2 Acu i nețṛaǧu deg win i nwekkel ɣef lḥaǧa ? Nețṛaǧu a nețkel fell-as !

4:3 Nekk, ur iyi-d-tewqiɛ ara ma yella tesḍelmem-iyi kunwi neɣ ma isseḍlem-iyi ccṛeɛ n yemdanen ; nekk daɣen ur sḍelmeɣ ara iman-iw,

4:4 ɣas akken ur iyi-isseḍlem ara wul-iw, mačči d ayagi ara d-isbeggnen belli d aḥeqqi i lliɣ, axaṭer d Sidi Ṛebbi ara yi-ḥasben.

4:5 Daymi ur țḥasabet ara uqbel lweqt, uqbel tuɣalin n Ssid-nneɣ ; d nețța ara d-isbeggnen s tafat-is ayen yeffren di ṭṭlam, ara d-ibeggnen daɣen ayen yeffren deg wulawen. Imiren yal yiwen ad isɛu ccan yuklal ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

4:6 Ay atmaten, ayen akka i d-nniɣ fell-i akk-d Abulus nniɣ-t-id iwakken aț-țfehmem lmeɛna n lemtel-agi : « Ur țɛeddit ara akkin i tilas n wayen yuran » ; ilaq yiwen ur ixeddem lxilaf ger yemdanen, iwakken ad izuxx s yiwen deg-sen.

4:7 Ihi anwa i k-yessulin ccan sennig wiyaḍ ? Acu ur k-d-nekki ara s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi deg wayen akk tesɛiḍ ? Imi d nețța i k-d-yefkan, acuɣeṛ i tețcekkiṛeḍ iman-ik am akken mačči s ɣuṛ-es i k-d-yekka ?

4:8 Atan teṛwam yakan, tuɣalem d imeṛkantiyen ! Tuɣalem d imḍebbṛen mbla nukni, acḥal bɣiɣ lemmer ț-țideț tzemrem i yiman-nwen iwakken a nḍebbeṛ s tdukli !

4:9 Nukni yellan d imceggɛen n Lmasiḥ, yefka-yaɣ Ṛebbi imukan ineggura ; aql-aɣ am wid ibuṣan, am wid i tețɛassa lmut, tfeṛṛeǧ deg-nneɣ ddunit meṛṛa, am lmalayekkat, am yemdanen.

4:10 Nukni d imehbal ɣef ddemma n Lmasiḥ, ma d kunwi d iɛeqliyen di tikli-nwen akk-d Lmasiḥ ! Nukni nefcel, kunwi tesɛam tazmert ; nukni nețwaḥqeṛ ma d kunwi tesɛam ccan !

4:11 Ar tura, aql-aɣ neqqim i laẓ d fad, neɛra, nețwet, nețmenṭar si tama ɣer tayeḍ ;

4:12 nețɛețțib iman nneɣ a nexdem iwakken a d-nawi aɣṛum-nneɣ s ifassen-nneɣ. M'ara ɣ-reggmen nukni nețbarak-iten, m'ara ɣ-țqehhiṛen nukni nețțawi-yasen,

4:13 m'ara d-inin ayen n diri fell-aneɣ nenehhu-ten. Ar tura ḥesben-aɣ am iḍumman n ddunit, am ineggura n yemdanen.

4:14 Ur wen-d-uriɣ ara ayagi iwakken a kkun-sneḥcameɣ, meɛna iwakken a kkun-sfehmeɣ am arraw iw i ḥemmleɣ.

4:15 Ɣas ma llan ɛecṛa alaf yemdanen i kkun isselmaden deg webrid n Lmasiḥ, yiwen n baba-twen kan i tesɛam di Lmasiḥ, imi d nekk i kkun-id-yewwin ɣer webrid n Lmasiḥ s Lexbaṛ n lxiṛ.

4:16 A kkun-weṣṣiɣ ihi aț-țeddum am akken ddiɣ nekkini.

4:17 Ɣef wayagi i wen-ceggɛeɣ Timuti, mmi eɛzizen di Lmasiḥ, iḍuɛen Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken a wen-d-yesmekti amek i leḥḥuɣ deg webrid n Lmasiḥ, akken i t-sselmadeɣ di mkul tajmaɛt n watmaten.

4:18 Kra deg-wen ikcem-iten zzux, ɣilen ur n-țṛuḥuɣ ara a kkun-ẓreɣ ?

4:19 Meɛna ma yebɣa Sidi Ṛebbi, qṛib a n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen. Ad ẓreɣ widak-agi yețzuxxun, mačči d imeslayen-nsen kan iwumi ara ḥesseɣ meɛna ad waliɣ ayen iwumi izemren.

4:20 Axaṭer tazmert n tgeldit n Sidi Ṛebbi mačči d imeslayen kan ara ț-id-isbeggnen, meɛna d lecɣal.

4:21 D acu i tebɣam ? A n-aseɣ ɣuṛ wen s uɛekkaz, neɣ s wul yeččuṛen d leḥmala akk-d leḥnana ?

5:1 Di mkul amkan nesla yedṛa leḥṛam d ameqqran gar-awen, d ayen ur nețțaf ara ulamma ger leǧnas nniḍen, tessawḍem armi yiwen deg-wen yețɛic di leḥṛam akk ț-țmeṭṭut n baba-s !

5:2 Yerna mazal-ikkun tețzuxxum ! Ilaq-awen aț-țilim di leḥzen, argaz i gxeddmen annect-agi ilaq a t tessufɣem si gar-awen ;

5:3 ma d nekk, ɣas beɛdeɣ fell-awen leɛqel-iw yella yid-wen ; atan sḍelmeɣ win ixedmen lɛaṛ-agi am akken lliɣ yid-wen.

5:4 S yisem n Sidna Ɛisa, s tezmert is, nnejmaɛet aql-i yid-wen :

5:5 ssufɣet akkin argaz am wagi, sellmet-eț i tezmert n Cciṭan ad issenger lǧețța-s iwakken ṛṛuḥ-is ad ițwasellek ass n tuɣalin n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa.

5:6 A wen-d-iniɣ : zzux-agi-nwen ur imɛin ara ! Eɛni ur tessinem ara lemtel i d-yeqqaṛen belli cwiṭ n yiɣes n temtunt yessalay arukti meṛṛa ?

5:7 Ssizdeget iman-nwen, kkset si gar-awen tamtunt ( iɣes ) taqdimt n ddnub iwakken aț-țilim d arukti ajdid yeṣfan. Ț-țideț d arukti ajdid yeṣfan i tellam, axaṭer Tafaska-nneɣ tebda, imi Lmasiḥ yellan d izimer nneɣ n leslak yefka iman-is d asfel.

5:8 Tura ihi a nkemmel lɛid weɣṛum mbla tamtunt ( iɣes ), mačči s temtunt-nni ixesṛen yellan d ddnub akk-d cceṛ, meɛna s weɣṛum ur nesɛi tamtunt ( iɣes ) yellan d ṣṣfa akk-d tideț.

5:9 Di tebṛaț-nni i wen-n-uriɣ, nniɣ awen-d ur țeddut ara d wid yețɛicin di leḥṛam.

5:10 Ur wen-d-nniɣ ara annect-agi ɣef wat ddunit yețɛicin di leḥṛam, yeččuṛen d ṭṭmeɛ, d lexdeɛ ț-țukeṛda, yețɛebbiden lmeṣnuɛat am Ṛebbi, neɣ m'ulac ilaq-awen aț-țeffɣem si ddunit-agi.

5:11 Meɛna nniɣ-awen-d : ur țemxalaḍet ara d win yețțarran iman-is d amasiḥi yili nețța yețɛici di leḥṛam, yeččuṛ d ṭṭmeɛ, d lexdeɛ, iheddeṛ ɣef wiyaḍ, iɛebbed lmeṣnuɛat, d asekṛan, d amakar. Ur țɣimit ara d wergaz am agi ulamma ɣer lqut.

5:12 Acu ara ɣ-yawin a nḥaseb wid n beṛṛa ? Eɛni mačči d wid yellan gar-awen i glaq aț-țḥasbem ?

5:13 Wid n beṛṛa d Ṛebbi ara ten-iḥasben. Meɛna akken yura di tira iqedsen : Stixṛet amcum si gar-awen.

6:1 Ma yella yiwen deg-wen yebɣa ad iccetki ɣef gma-s, amek ara iṛuḥ a t-icaṛeɛ zdat wid ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ, mačči zdat wid yumnen am nețța ?

6:2 Eɛni ur teẓrim ara d wid yumnen s Lmasiḥ ara iḥasben at ddunit ? Ma yella ihi d kunwi ara iḥasben at ddunit, amek ur tețțizmirem ara aț-țefrum timsalin timecṭuḥin ?

6:3 Ur teẓrim ara belli a nḥaseb lmalayekkat ? Anda ara d-banent temsalin n ddunit-agi !

6:4 Ma d kunwi m'ara yili lxilaf gar-awen, tețṛuḥum ɣer wid ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ iwakken ad frun tilufa-nwen !

6:5 Ur tețneḥcamem ara ! Eɛni ulac ula d yiwen n wergaz ifehmen gar awen, i gzemren ad ifru timsalin ger watmaten ?

6:6 Meɛna kunwi atan wa yețcetki ɣef wa, yerna ɣer wid ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ !

6:7 Ihi amennuɣ i d-yețțilin gar awen, ițbeggin-ed lɛib-nwen. Acuɣeṛ ur tṣebbṛem ara axiṛ ad iɛeddi fell-awen lbaṭel, acuɣeṛ ur tețțaǧǧam ara iman-nwen aț-țețțuɛerrim ?

6:8 Meɛna d kunwi i d imesbaṭliyen, tețɛerrim wiyaḍ ɣas akken d atmaten-nwen !

6:9 Eɛni ur teẓrim ara belli wid ixeddmen lbaṭel ur keččmen ara ɣer tgelda n Sidi Ṛebbi ? Ur țɣuṛṛut ara iman-nwen axaṭer kra n win yețɛicin di leḥṛam neɣ wid iɛebbden lmeṣnuɛat, wid ixeddmen zzna, at leɛṛuṛ,

6:10 wid yețțakren, wid itețțen ayla n medden, wid ireggmen, ixeddaɛen akk-d isekṛanen, ur keččmen ara tagelda n Sidi Ṛebbi.

6:11 Akka i llan yakan kra deg-wen uqbel ad amnen. Meɛna ddnub-nwen yurad, tețwaxtaṛem iwakken aț-țilim d ayla n Sidi Ṛebbi, tuɣalem d iḥeqqiyen zdat Ṛebbi s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ akk-d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen n Sidi Ṛebbi, Illu-nneɣ.

6:12 Kra deg-wen qqaṛen : « Ɣuṛ-i kullec d leḥlal », meɛna mačči d kullec i kkun-inefɛen ; zemreɣ a d-iniɣ : kullec d leḥlal ɣuṛ-i, meɛna ur țțaǧaɣ ara lḥaǧa a yi-temlek.

6:13 Lqut yella-d i wɛebbuḍ, aɛebbuḍ yella-d i lqut ; a d-yas wass i deg ara ten-yessenger Sidi Ṛebbi i sin. Meɛna lǧețța ur d-tețwaxleq ara i yir tikli, tețwaxleq-ed iwakken aț-țqeddec ɣef Sidi Ṛebbi i ț-isedduyen.

6:14 Sidi Ṛebbi i d-yessekren Sidna Ɛisa si ger lmegtin, a ɣ-d-isseḥyu ula d nukni s tezmert-is.

6:15 Ur teẓrim ara belli lǧețțat-nwen d lemfaṣel n Lmasiḥ ? Eɛni zemreɣ a d-ddmeɣ lemfaṣel n Lmasiḥ iwakken a ten-erreɣ d lemfaṣel n tmeṭṭut ixeddmen asekkak yeɛni zzna ? Ur zmireɣ ara !

6:16 Ur teẓrim ara belli win ara yeznun nețța akk-d yir tameṭṭut ad yuɣal yid-es d yiwen ? Akken yura di tira iqedsen : Di sin ad uɣalen d yiwet n lǧețța.

6:17 Akka daɣen win ara iḥemmlen Lmasiḥ ad yili yid-es s yiwen n Ṛṛuḥ.

6:18 Xḍut i yir tikli ! Kra n ddnub i gezmer a t-ixdem wemdan ițɣimi beṛṛa n lǧețța-s, ma d win ara ixedmen zzna yessewsex lǧețța-s.

6:19 Ur teẓrim ara daɣen belli Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yezdeɣ di lǧețțat-nwen ? Ṛṛuḥ-nni i wen-d-ițțunefken s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ? Ur teẓrim ara belli ur tețțalasem ara deg yiman-nwen ?

6:20 Axaṭer yella win i kkun-id-ifdan s wazal d ameqqran, ɣlayen. Ɛuzzet ihi Sidi Ṛebbi di lǧețțat-nwen.

7:1 A nɛeddit tura ɣer isteqsiyen i yi-d-tefkam di tebṛaț-nwen teqqaṛem : « D ayen yelhan i wergaz m'ur yezwiǧ ara ».

7:2 Ț-țideț, meɛna iwakken ur d-yețțili ara leḥṛam, axiṛ-as i wergaz ad yesɛu tameṭṭut-is, axiṛ-as i tmeṭṭut aț-țesɛu argaz-is.

7:3 Argaz ilaq ad ixdem lebɣi i tmeṭṭut-is, tameṭṭut daɣen ilaq aț-țexdem lebɣi i wergaz-is.

7:4 Tameṭṭut ur tezmir ara aț-țexdem ayen tebɣa s lǧețța-s, axaṭer lǧețța-s d ayla n wergaz-is ; argaz daɣen ur yezmir ara ad yexdem ayen i gebɣa s lǧețța-s, axaṭer lǧețța-s d ayla n tmeṭṭut-is.

7:5 Ur țemyagit ara gar-awen anagar ma yella temsefhamem iwakken aț-țeǧǧem kra n wussan i tẓallit ; imiren ddukklet daɣen ; ḥadret a wen-yaf Cciṭan abrid a kkun-iɣuṛṛ ma yella ur tezmirem ara aț-țeṭṭfem iman-nwen.

7:6 Ayagi mačči d lameṛ i wen-fkiɣ, meɛna d aweṣṣi kan i kkun-weṣṣaɣ.

7:7 Lemmer ufiɣ medden akk ad ilin am nekk, meɛna mkul yiwen d acu i s-d-ifka Sidi Ṛebbi, wa yefka-yas-d aya, wayeḍ yefka-yas-d ayen nniḍen.

7:8 I wid ur nezwiǧ ara akk-d tuǧǧal a sen-iniɣ : yelha-yasen ma qqimen am nekk.

7:9 Meɛna m'ur zmiren ara ad ṭṭfen iman-nsen, ad zewǧen axiṛ. Axiṛ-asen ad zewǧen wala aț-țecɛel tmes deg wulawen-nsen.

7:10 A d-fkeɣ lameṛ i imasiḥiyen izewǧen ; lameṛ-agi mačči s ɣuṛ-i i d-yekka meɛna s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi : tameṭṭut izewǧen ur ilaq ara aț-țeǧǧ argaz-is :

7:11 ma yella teǧǧa-t, ur s-ilaq ara aț-țɛawed zzwaǧ. Neɣ ma tebɣa, tezmer aț-țemṣalaḥ yid-es aț-țuɣal ɣuṛ-es. Argaz daɣen ur s-ilaq ara ad yebru i tmeṭṭut-is.

7:12 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan wiyaḍ atan wayen zemreɣ a d-iniɣ ( ayagi s ɣuṛ-i mačči s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ) : ma yella yiwen seg watmaten yesɛa tameṭṭut ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ yili teqbel aț-țeqqim d wergaz-is, ur ilaq ara a s-yebru ;

7:13 akken daɣen, tameṭṭut tamasiḥit yesɛan argaz ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ yili yeqbel ad iɛic yid-es, ur ilaq ara aț-țerwel fell-as.

7:14 Axaṭer argaz ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ yella seddaw leɛnaya n Sidi Ṛebbi s tmeṭṭut-is yumnen ; daɣen tameṭṭut ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ, tella seddaw leɛnaya n Sidi Ṛebbi s wergaz-is yumnen ; neɣ m'ulac dderya-nsen ad țwaḥesben d dderya n leḥṛam nutni yellan d dderya n leḥlal.

7:15 Ma yella win ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ yebɣa ad imfaṛaq akk-d tmeṭṭut-is i gumnen, neɣ tameṭṭut ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ tebɣa aț-țemfaṛaq akk-d wergaz-is i gumnen, zemren ad mfaṛaqen ; argaz-nni neɣ tameṭṭut-nni yellan d imasiḥiyen ur d-yeqqim wayen i ten-icerken, axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi yessawel aɣ-d iwakken a nɛic di lehna.

7:16 Yerna amek i teẓriḍ kemm a tameṭṭut tamasiḥit belli tzemṛeḍ aț-țselkeḍ argaz-im ? Neɣ amek i teẓriḍ kečč ay argaz amasiḥi belli tzemreḍ aț-țselkeḍ tameṭṭut-ik ?

7:17 Ilaq yal yiwen ad yelḥu s wayen i s-d-yefka Sidi Ṛebbi, yerna ad yeqqim di lḥala i deg yella asmi i s-d-yessawel. Akka i țweṣṣiɣ tijmuyaɛ n watmaten meṛṛa.

7:18 Ma yella walebɛaḍ yuɣ-it lḥal yexten mi s-d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi, ilaq ad yeqqim akken yella ; ma yella walebɛaḍ ur yextin ara asmi i s-d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi, ur ilaq ara ad yexten.

7:19 Yexten walebɛaḍ neɣ ur yextin ara ulac deg-s ; ayen yesɛan azal d ṭṭaɛa n lumuṛat n Sidi Ṛebbi.

7:20 Ilaq yal yiwen ad iqqim akken i t-id-yufa lḥal asmi i s-d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi.

7:21 D akli i telliḍ asmi i k-d yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi ? Ur țxemmim ara, meɛna ma tusa-yak-ed teswiɛt i deg tzemreḍ aț-țesɛuḍ tilelli, faṛes-iț.

7:22 Axaṭer akli iwumi d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi yuɣal d win yesɛan tilelli ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi ; daɣen amdan aḥuṛṛi iwumi d-yessawel Sidi Ṛebbi yuɣal d akli n Lmasiḥ.

7:23 Yella win ixelṣen fell-awen iwakken aț-țilim d iḥuṛṛiyen, ihi ur țțuɣalet ara d aklan n yemdanen.

7:24 Ihi ay atmaten, ilaq yal yiwen ad yeqqim zdat Ṛebbi di lḥala i deg yella asmi s-d-yessawel.

7:25 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan wid ur nezwiǧ ara, Sidi Ṛebbi ur d-yenni kra fell-asen, meɛna a wen-d-fkeɣ ṛṛay-iw, nekk yerra Sidi Ṛebbi s ṛṛeḥma-ines tameqqrant d argaz yuklalen lețkal.

7:26 Ɣuṛ-i d ayen yelhan i wergaz ma yeqqim akken yella ɣef ddemma n lweqt-agi iweɛṛen.

7:27 Ma txeḍbeḍ tameṭṭut, ur țnadi ara a ț-teǧǧeḍ ; m'ur tezwiǧeḍ ara, ur țnadi ara aț-țzewǧeḍ.

7:28 Tura ma yella ilemẓi neɣ tilemẓit zewǧen, ur wwin ara ddnub ; meɛna wid ara izewǧen ad ṛwun lḥif di tudert-nsen ; nekk mačči d ayen i wen-mennaɣ.

7:29 Atan wayen ara d-iniɣ ay atmaten : lweqt ițțazal, drus i d iqqimen, wid izewǧen ilaq ad ilin am akken ur zwiǧen ara,

7:30 wid yețrun ad ilin am akken ur țrun ara, wid ifeṛḥen am akken ur fṛiḥen ara, wid yețțaɣen am akken ur sɛin ara.

7:31 Wid yesɛan nnfeɛ di ddunit-agi am akken ur t-sɛin ara axaṭer ddunit-agi ur tețdum ara.

7:32 Ur bɣiɣ ara aț-țesɛum iɣeblan. Win ur nezwiǧ ara ițxemmim i lecɣal n Sidi Ṛebbi, yețnadi kan ad yeɛǧeb Sidi Ṛebbi ;

7:33 meɛna win izewǧen yețxemmim ɣef lecɣal n ddunit, yețnadi ad yeɛǧeb i tmeṭṭut-is ; ul-is yebḍa ɣef sin.

7:34 Akken daɣen tameṭṭut ur nezwiǧ ara akk-d țlemẓit ; țxemmiment kan ɣef lecɣal n Sidi Ṛebbi iwakken ad ilint ț-țiqeddacin-ines s lǧețțat-nsent d ṛṛuḥ-nsent ; meɛna tin izewǧen tețxemmim kan ɣef lecɣal n ddunit d wamek ara teɛǧeb i wergaz-is.

7:35 Ur wen-d-nniɣ ara ayagi iwakken a kkun-kellxeɣ meɛna nniɣ awen-t-id i nnfeɛ-nwen axaṭer bɣiɣ aț-țeddum s tikli yelhan, yeṣfan iwakken aț-țeṭṭfem deg webrid n Lmasiḥ mačči deg wayen nniḍen.

7:36 Ma yella yiwen deg-wen ixḍeb tilemẓit, iḥar ur yezmir ara ad yeṛǧu ibɣa ad yezweǧ, mačči d ddnub ma yella yezweǧ.

7:37 Ma d win iqesden s lebɣi-ines ur izeggej ara, ma yella yeẓra iman-is yezmer ad iṣbeṛ, d ayen yelhan i gexdem m'ur yezwiǧ ara.

7:38 Ihi win izewǧen d ayen yelhan i gexdem, win ur nezwiǧ ara d ayen yelhan nezzeh.

7:39 Tameṭṭut izewǧen ur tezmir ara aț-țeǧǧ argaz-is skud mazal-it yedder, ma yella yemmut, tezmer aț-țɛiwed zzwaǧ d win tebɣa, acu kan ilaq ad yili d win yumnen s Lmasiḥ am nețțat.

7:40 Meɛna ɣef wakken walaɣ ma teqqim akken tella axiṛ-as, akken ara tesɛu lehna. Wagi d ṛṛay-iw nekk, ẓriɣ ula d nekk sɛiɣ Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:1 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan aksum n iseflawen i zellun i ssadaț, ț-țideț nesɛa akk tamusni, d acu kan tamusni tețțawi-d zzux i wemdan ma d leḥmala tesnernay deg-nneɣ laman ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:2 Win i gɣilen yessen kra, urɛad yessin akken i glaq ad yissin.

8:3 Meɛna win iḥemmlen Sidi Ṛebbi, Sidi Ṛebbi yessen-it.

8:4 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan učči n weksum n iseflawen immezlen i lmeṣnuɛat, neẓra belli lmeṣnuɛat-agi ț-țikellax di ddunit axaṭer anagar yiwen n Ṛebbi i gellan.

8:5 Ɣas qqaṛen aṭas n iṛebbiten i gellan ama deg igenni ama di lqaɛa, ( axaṭer ț-țideț ! Ɣuṛ-sen aṭas n iṛebbiten akk-d ssadaț i gellan)

8:6 meɛna ɣuṛ-nneɣ nukni yiwen kan n Ṛebbi i gellan, d baba-tneɣ Ṛebbi i d-ixelqen kullec, ula d nukni d ayla-s. Anagar yiwen n Ssid i gellan : d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, yis i d-yella kullec, yis daɣen i nedder ula d nukni.

8:7 Aṭas ur nessin ara tideț-agi. Kra deg-sen i guɣen tannumi tețțen asfel n ssadaț, tura m'ara tețțen aksum-nni mazal ḥesben-t belli ț-țideț d asfel i ssadaț, neyya-nsen tenquqel, ḥussen am akken denben.

8:8 Mačči d ayen yețmaččan ara ɣ-iqeṛṛben ɣer Ṛebbi. Ma nečča ur nerbiḥ acemma, m'ur nečči ara, ur aɣ-iṛuḥ wacemma.

8:9 Ihi, ɛasset iman-nwen, ɣuṛ-wat tilelli-agi i tesɛam aț-țili d ugur n uɣelluy i wid ur neǧhid ara di liman.

8:10 Axaṭer ma iwala-k-id walebɛaḍ ur neǧhid ara di liman, teqqimeḍ aț-țeččeḍ di lemqam kečč yessnen, amek tebɣiḍ ur yețṛuḥu ara ula d nețța ad yečč seg iseflawen-agi yemmezlen i ssadaț ?

8:11 S wakka ihi, gma-k-agi ur neǧhid ara di liman, i ɣef yemmut Lmasiḥ , ɣef ddemma n tmusni-nni inek i geɣli.

8:12 Imi tețțawim ddnub ɣef watmaten-nwen, tessexṛabem neyya-nsen, tdenbem ɣer Lmasiḥ.

8:13 Ihi ma yella ayen ara ččeɣ izmer ad isseɣli gma di ddnub, ur țțuɣaleɣ ara maḍi ad ččeɣ lmakla-nni iwakken ur iɣelli ara.

9:1 Eɛni ur zmireɣ ara ad xedmeɣ akken bɣiɣ ? Mačči d amceggeɛ n Lmasiḥ i lliɣ ? Ur ẓriɣ ara Sidna Ɛisa s wallen-iw ? Mačči d nekk i kkun-id-yewwin ɣer webrid n Lmasiḥ ?

9:2 Ɣas wiyaḍ ur iyi-ḥsiben ara d amceggeɛ n Lmasiḥ, kunwi ur tezmirem ara a yi-tnekkṛem axaṭer ț-țikli-nwen akk-d Lmasiḥ i d țbut belli d Lmasiḥ i yi-d-iceggɛen.

9:3 Atan wamek țțaraɣ i wid i yi-sseḍlamen :

9:4 Eɛni ur aɣ-d-iṣaḥ ara a nečč, a nsew nukni s imceggɛen n Lmasiḥ ?

9:5 Ur sɛiɣ ara lḥeqq ad aɣeɣ tameṭṭut yumnen aț-țețțeddu yid-i am akken xeddmen imceggɛen nniḍen n Lmasiḥ d watmaten n Ssid-nneɣ akk-d Buṭrus ?

9:6 Neɣ d nekk kan akk-d Barnabas i glaqen a nexdem iwakken a d-nawi aɣṛum nneɣ ?

9:7 Yella wass i deg teslam belli aɛeskṛi ilaq ad ixelleṣ m'ara d-isɛeddi lɛeskeṛ ? Anwa ara yeẓẓun tara ur itețț ara si tẓuṛin-is ? Ulac ameksa ara yeksen taqeḍɛit n lmal ur itess ara seg uyefki-ines !

9:8 Tɣilem ayen akka i d-nniɣ d lɛaddat kan n yemdanen ? Ula d ccariɛa n Musa tenna-t-id !

9:9 Axaṭer yura deg-s : Ur țțarat ara takmamt i wezger isserwaten nneɛma. Eɛni ɣef yezgaren i gḥebbeṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ?

9:10 Mačči ɣef ddemma-nneɣ i d-yenna akka ? Ț-țideț, d nukni iwumi i d-yenna imeslayen-agi ! Ilaq win ikerrzen akk-d win yesserwaten ad ssirmen ad sɛun amur di lɣella nsen.

9:11 Ma yella nezreɛ gar-awen lbaṛakat n Sidi Ṛebbi, d acu yellan ma nemger-ed s ɣuṛ-wen tamɛict nneɣ ?

9:12 Ma twalam d lḥeqq ad meggren wiyaḍ s ɣuṛ-wen, eɛni mačči d nukni i gezwaren ? Meɛna nukni ur d-newwi acemma s ɣuṛ-wen, neṣbeṛ i kullec iwakken ur d-yețțili ara wugur i lexbaṛ n lxiṛ n Lmasiḥ.

9:13 Ur teẓrim ara belli wid ixeddmen di lǧameɛ țțawin-d lqut-nsen si lǧameɛ ? Ur teẓrim ara daɣen lmuqeddem izellun i Ṛebbi iseflawen i d-țțawin medden, yețțawi amur-is seg-sen ?

9:14 Akka daɣen i d-yenna Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef wid yețbecciṛen lexbaṛ n lxiṛ : di lexbaṛ n lxiṛ ara afen tamɛict-nsen.

9:15 Nekk ur d-wwiɣ acemma s ɣuṛ wen, yerna ur d-uriɣ ara tabṛaț-agi iwakken a d-ssutreɣ lḥeqq-iw. Axiṛ-iyi lmut wala a yi-tețwakkes sebba-agi s wayes zemreɣ ad zuxxeɣ !

9:16 Ur țzuxxuɣ ara s yiman-iw imi țbecciṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ, axaṭer țwaḥeṛseɣ ad beccṛeɣ. A nnger-iw ma yella ur beccṛeɣ ara lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ.

9:17 Lemmer xedmeɣ ayagi s lebɣi w sɛiɣ lḥeqq ad ssutreɣ lexlaṣ, meɛna d Ṛebbi i gḥettmen fell-i ccɣel-agi.

9:18 Ihi, d acu i d lexlaṣ-iw ? D lfeṛḥ i seɛɛuɣ m'ara țbecciṛeɣ lexbaṛ n lxiṛ mbla lexlaṣ, mbla ma ssutreɣ lḥeqq-iw.

9:19 Ɣas akken ur iyi-ițțalas yiwen kra ; rriɣ iman-iw d aqeddac n mkul yiwen iwakken a d-rebḥeɣ aṭas n yemdanen ɣer webrid n Lmasiḥ.

9:20 Ɣer wat Isṛail rriɣ iman-iw am nutni iwakken a ten-id-rebḥeɣ ; ɣer wid yettabaɛen ccariɛa n Musa tebɛeɣ-ț am nutni iwakken a ten id rebḥeɣ, ɣas akken nekk ur lliɣ ara seddaw leḥkum n ccariɛa.

9:21 Akka daɣen, m'ara iliɣ d wid ur nesɛi ara ccariɛa, țțaraɣ iman-iw am akken ur sɛiɣ ara ccariɛa iwakken a ten-id-rebḥeɣ, ɣas akken nekk sɛiɣ ccariɛa n Ṛebbi imi d Lmasiḥ i d ccariɛa-inu.

9:22 Akk-d wid ur neǧhid ara deg webrid n Ṛebbi, uɣaleɣ am nutni, iwakken a ten-id-rebḥeɣ. Ddiɣ di lebɣi i mkul yiwen iwakken a d-sellkeɣ ulamma kra deg-sen.

9:23 Ayagi akk xedmeɣ-t ɣef ddemma n lexbaṛ n lxiṛ iwakken ad sɛuɣ yid-sen amur.

9:24 Di temzizla, teẓram belli deg wid akk yețțazalen deg wannar, yiwen kan ara irebḥen. Azzlet ihi iwakken aț-țrebḥem.

9:25 Wid akk yețțazzalen, m'ara heggin iman-nsen i tazzla, țɛețțiben iman-nsen ; nutni țenɛețțaben iwakken a d-rebḥen cciɛa ur nețdum ara ; lameɛna nukni, nețɛețțib iman-nneɣ iwakken a nerbeḥ cciɛa ara idumen.

9:26 Nekk ur țțazzaleɣ ara am win ur nesɛi ara lmeqsud, ur kkateɣ ara deg ubeḥri m'ara țnaɣeɣ ;

9:27 meɛna țɛețțibeɣ lǧețța-inu, țɛassaɣ ɣef yiman-iw, axaṭer uggadeɣ nekk i gbeccṛen i wiyaḍ ad ɣliɣ, ad uɣaleɣ ɣer deffir.

10:2 Țwaɣeḍsen meṛṛa di tagut-nni akk-d lebḥeṛ mi ddan d Sidna Musa,

10:3 ččan meṛṛa seg yiwen lqut n Sidi Ṛebbi,

10:4 swan meṛṛa si lɛinseṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi i d-iteffɣen seg wezṛu iteddun yid-sen ; azṛu-agi, d Lmasiḥ.

10:5 Ɣas akken, aṭas seg-sen ur neɛǧib ara i Sidi Ṛebbi ; daymi i ten-tesseɣli lmut yiwen yiwen mi teddun deg unezṛuf.

10:6 Ayen akken yedṛan yid-sen yeqqim-ed d lemtel i nukni, iwakken ur neṭṭamaɛ ara ayen n diri am akken i t-ṭṭamaɛen nutni.

10:7 Ur țțilit ara am wid iɛebbden ssadaț akken xeddmen kra seg-sen, axaṭer am akken yura di tira iqedsen : Yeqqim wegdud ad yečč ad isew, mi gfukk yekker ad yezhu, ad yecḍeḥ.

10:8 Ur ilaq ara daɣen a nxeddem leɛṛuṛ akken xeddmen kra seg-sen ; daymi deg yiwen n wass, lmut tečča tlata uɛecrin alef deg-sen.

10:9 Ur ilaq ara a njeṛṛeb Sidi Ṛebbi akken i xedmen kra seg-sen, dɣa qqsen-ten izerman mmuten.

10:10 Ur ilaq ara daɣen aț-țesmermugem ɣef wiyaḍ akken i smermugen kra deg-sen armi i ten-issenger lmelk n lmut.

10:11 Timsalin-agi akk i sen-yedṛan, qqiment-ed d lemtel, uran-tent di tektabin, iwakken a nelmed nukni ițɛicin di taggara n lweqt.

10:12 Ihi win iḥesben iman-is ibedd, ad iɛass iman-is ur iɣelli ara !

10:13 Ayen akk s wayes i tețwajeṛbem, d ayen iwumi yezmer wemdan. Sidi Ṛebbi d bab n lḥeqq, ur yețɛemmid ara aț-țenɛețțabem sennig n tezmert nwen ; lameɛna m'ara tețwajeṛbem, a wen-d-yefk tazmert s wayes ara tṣebṛem d wamek ara teffɣem si tegniț n leɛtab.

10:14 A wid eɛzizen fell-i, xḍut i weɛbad n ssadaț.

10:15 Țmeslayeɣ awen-d am akken țmeslayen i wid ifehmen ; meyyzet kunwi s yiman nwen ɣef wayen i d-qqaṛeɣ.

10:16 Taqbuct n lbaṛaka i ɣef nețḥemmid Sidi Ṛebbi, eɛni ur aɣ-tecrik ara d idammen n Lmasiḥ ? Neɣ aɣṛum i nbeṭṭu, ur aɣ-yecrik ara d lǧețța n Lmasiḥ ?

10:17 Imi yiwen n weɣṛum kan i gellan, ula d nukni nuɣal d yiwet n lǧețța ɣas akken deg waṭas yid-nneɣ i nella, axaṭer ntețț meṛṛa seg yiwen n weɣṛum.

10:18 Walit at Isṛail : wid itețțen seg weksum n iseflawen s weɛbad-nsen, eɛni ur criken ara d wemkan-nni anda i zellun iseflawen ?

10:19 Eɛni bɣiɣ a d-iniɣ belli asfel nni i zellun i ssadaț neɣ ssadaț-nni i țɛebbiden, sɛan lqima ? Xaṭi !

10:20 Meɛna ayen akka i zellun, zellun-t i leǧnun mačči i Ṛebbi ; nekk ur bɣiɣ ara aț-țcerkem akk-d leǧnun.

10:21 Ur tezmirem ara aț-țeswem si teqbuct n Sidi Ṛebbi ma yella tețțessem si țeqbuct n leǧnun ; ur tezmirem ara daɣen aț-țeččem seg weɣṛum n Sidi Ṛebbi ma yella tețțețțem seg weɣṛum n leǧnun.

10:22 Eɛni nebɣa a nejreḥ ul n Sidi Ṛebbi a d-neskker tismin-is ? Tɣilem tazmert-nneɣ teɣleb tazmert-is ?

10:23 Llan wid i d-iqqaṛen : kullec d leḥlal fell-aɣ ; ț-țideț, lameɛna ur aɣ-yenfiɛ ara kullec. Kullec yețțusemmeḥ-aɣ a t-nexdem, meɛna mačči d kullec i ɣ-yesnernayen di liman.

10:24 Ur țnadit ara ɣef nnfeɛ-nwen kan kunwi, meɛna nadit ɣef nnfeɛ n wiyaḍ.

10:25 Tzemrem aț-țeččem kra n wayen i gețnuzun di ssuq mbla asteqsi, mbla ma tḥebbṛem,

10:26 axaṭer ddunit d wayen akk yellan deg-s d ayla n Sidi Ṛebbi.

10:27 Ma yeɛṛeḍ-ikkun walebɛad ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ, ma tṛuḥem, ččet s neyya mbla asteqsi seg wayen akk ara wen-d-issers.

10:28 Lameɛna ma yella win i wen-d yennan : ayagi yețțunefk d asfel i ssadaț, ur tețțet ara imi i kkun-id ixebbeṛ iwakken ad yethedden wul nwen.

10:29 Mačči ɣef neyya-nwen i d-nniɣ akka, lameɛna ɣef neyya n win i kkun-iɛerḍen. Ihi, acimi ara ǧǧeɣ wayeḍ ad idebbeṛ deg ixemmimen-iw ?

10:30 Ma yella ḥemdeɣ Sidi Ṛebbi ɣef wayen i tețțeɣ, acimi ara ǧǧeɣ iman-iw ad wwten deg-i ɣef lqut i ɣef ḥemdeɣ Sidi Ṛebbi ?

10:31 Ama teččam neɣ teswam, neɣ kra n wayen ara txedmem, xedmet-eț i tmanegt n Sidi Ṛebbi.

10:32 Ur țțilit ara d sebba n uɣelluy ama i wat Isṛail, ama i iyunaniyen, ama i tejmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi,

10:33 am akken xeddmeɣ nekk, țnadiɣ a wen-ɛeǧbeɣ meṛṛa di kullec, ur țqellibeɣ ara ɣef nnfeɛ-inu, meɛna țqellibeɣ ɣef nnfeɛ n umur ameqqran deg-wen iwakken ad țwaselken.

11:1 Ɛandet-iyi am akken țɛanadeɣ Lmasiḥ.

11:2 A kkun-ibarek Ṛebbi imi i yi-d-tețmektayem di yal taswiɛt yerna teṭṭafaṛem lewṣayat-iw akken i wen-tent-slemdeɣ.

11:3 Meɛna, tura bɣiɣ aț-țissinem ayagi : Lmasiḥ d aqeṛṛuy ɣef yemdanen meṛṛa, argaz d aqeṛṛuy ɣef tmeṭṭut-is, ma d Ṛebbi d aqeṛṛuy ɣef Lmasiḥ.

11:4 Ihi argaz ara yesburren ɣef wuqeṛṛuy-is m'ara ideɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi neɣ m'ara d-yețxebbiṛ ayen i s-d-yusan s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, yekkes lḥeṛma ɣef Ssid-is.

11:5 Lameɛna tameṭṭut ara ideɛɛun ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi neɣ ara yețxebbiṛen s wayen i s-d-yețțasen s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi aqeṛṛuy-is ɛeryan, tekkes lḥeṛma ɣef wergaz-is, am akken d aqeṛṛuy-is i tseṭṭel.

11:6 Axaṭer ma yella tameṭṭut ur tesburr ara, aț-țegzem kan ihi acebbub-is am wergaz ; lameɛna ma yella d lɛib i tmeṭṭut aț-țegzem neɣ aț-țṣeṭṭel acebbub-is, ilaq ihi aț-țɣum aqeṛṛuy-is.

11:7 Argaz ur ilaq ara ad yesburr i wqeṛṛuy-is axaṭer d nețța i gmetlen Sidi Ṛebbi d lɛaḍima-ines ; ma ț-țameṭṭut d nețțat i d lḥeṛma n wergaz.

11:8 Axaṭer mačči d argaz i d- yețwaxelqen si tmeṭṭut meɛna ț- țameṭṭut i d-ițwaxelqen seg wergaz.

11:9 Daɣen argaz ur d-yețwaxleq ara i tmeṭṭut, lameɛna ț-țameṭṭut i d yețwaxelqen i wergaz.

11:10 Daymi ɣef ddemma n lmalayekkat, tameṭṭut ilaq aț-țesbur i uqeṛṛuy-is iwakken a d-tbeggen belli seddaw lḥekma n wergaz-is i tella.

11:11 Ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi, ur d-tețțili tmeṭṭut mbla argaz, ur d-yețțili wergaz mbla tameṭṭut.

11:12 Axaṭer akken i d-tețwaxleq tmeṭṭut seg wergaz, akken daɣen i d-yețlal wergaz si tmeṭṭut, ayagi akk s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi i d-yekka.

11:13 Meyzet kunwi s yiman-nwen : eɛni yelha-yas i tmeṭṭut aț-țdeɛɛu ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi mbla ma tesburr ɣef wuqeṛṛuy-is ?

11:14 Ula d lɛadda n yemdanen tesselmed awen belli d lɛib i wergaz ma iṛebba acebbub-is.

11:15 Lameɛna d ccan ameqqran i tmeṭṭut ma tṛebba acebbub-is axaṭer yețțunefk-as-d iwakken a s-yili d asburru.

11:16 Ma yella win yebɣan ad iɣanen ɣef wannect-agi, ilaq ad iẓer belli nukni ur nesɛi ara tannumi-agi, ur telli ara daɣen di tejmuyaɛ n Sidi Ṛebbi.

11:17 A nɛeddi tura ɣer wayen nniḍen. Ɣef wayen yeɛnan tijmuyaɛ-nwen ur kkun-țcekkiṛeɣ ara, axaṭer ayen i deg ilaq aț-țeddum ɣer zdat, kunwi tețțuɣalem ɣer deffir.

11:18 Sliɣ belli m'ara tețnejmaɛem yețțili lxilaf gar-awen yerna umneɣ belli tella kra n tideț deg wayagi.

11:19 Ilaq a d-yili lxilaf-agi gar-awen iwakken a d-banen wid yeṭṭfen di liman seg wul.

11:20 M'ara tedduklem ɣer imensi n usmekti, ur txeddmem ara akken yebɣa Sidi Ṛebbi !

11:21 Axaṭer wa yețḥiri ad yečč lqut-is, wa mazal-it yelluẓ wayeḍ yeṛwa, yeskkeṛ.

11:22 Amek ! Ur tesɛim ara ixxamen nwen iwakken aț-țeččem aț-țeswem ? Neɣ tebɣam aț-țesseɣlim lqima n tejmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi, aț-țesneḥcamem wid ur nesɛi acemma ? D acu ara wen-d-iniɣ, a kkun cekkṛeɣ ? Ur kkun-țcekkiṛeɣ ara ɣef wayagi !

11:23 D ayen i yi-d-ițțunefken s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi i wen-slemdeɣ nekkini. Deg iḍ-nni i deg yețțuzenz Sidna Ɛisa, yeddem-ed aɣṛum,

11:24 yeḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi yebḍa-t yenna : « Wagi d lgețța-w yețțunefken fell awen, xeddmet ayagi iwakken a yi-d țmektayem. »

11:25 Daɣen, mi fukken učči, yeddem ed taqbuct yenna : « Taqbuct-agi d lemɛahda tajḍiṭ s idammen-iw ; xeddmet ayagi iwakken a yi-d-tețmektayem. »

11:26 Axaṭer kra n tikkelt i deg ara teččem seg weɣṛum-agi, i deg ara teswem si teqbuct agi, tețxebbiṛem s lmut n Lmasiḥ alamma d ass i deg ara d-yuɣal.

11:27 Daymi win ara yeččen seg weɣṛum-agi, ara yeswen si teqbuct-agi n usmekti n Lmasiḥ, m'ur sen-yefki ara lqima, yewwi ddnub ɣer yiri-s ɣef ddemma n lǧețța akk-d idammen n Lmasiḥ.

11:28 Ihi mkul yiwen ad ixemmem deg yiman-is uqbel ad yečč seg weɣṛum-nni neɣ ad isew si teqbuct-nni.

11:29 Axaṭer ma yella yiwen deg-wen yečča neɣ yeswa tiremt n usmekti n Lmasiḥ, mbla ma yefka lqima i lǧețța n Lmasiḥ, yewwi-d lɛiqab i yiman-is.

11:30 Ɣef wayagi i llan gar-awen aṭas n wid ihelken akk-d ineɛyuba, yerna aṭas deg-sen i gemmuten.

11:31 Lemmer nețḥadar ɣef yiman nneɣ, tili ur d-nețțawi ara lɛiqab n Sidi Ṛebbi fell-aneɣ.

11:32 S wakka m'ara ɣ-iɛaqeb Sidi Ṛebbi, yețṛebbi-yaɣ iwakken ur aɣ yețḥasab ara d wat n ddunit.

11:33 Ihi ay atmaten, asmi ara tennejmaɛem iwakken aț-țeččem, myeṛǧut wway gar-awen.

11:34 Ma llan wid yelluẓen, ad ččen uqbel deg wexxam-nsen iwakken ur d-tesseɣlayem ara lɛiqab n Sidi Ṛebbi fell-awen m'ara tennejmaɛem. Timsalin nniḍen a tent-fruɣ m'ara n-aseɣ.

12:1 Ay atmaten, ur bɣiɣ ara aț-țeqqimem mbla tamusni ɣef wayen yeɛnan ayen i d-yețțak Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

12:2 Teẓram belli asm'akken ur tuminem ara s Lmasiḥ, țțawwin kkun wiyaḍ tɛebbdem lmeṣnuɛat ur neneṭṭeq ara.

12:3 Tura ilaq aț-țeẓrem belli yiwen ur izmir a d-yini : « ad yețwanɛel Ɛisa Lmasiḥ » ma yella s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i gețmeslay. Daɣen yiwen ur izmir ad iched belli Ɛisa Lmasiḥ d Ssid-is, ma yella mačči s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i t-id-yenna.

12:4 Llan aṭas n leṣnaf n tukciwin n Ṛebbi, lameɛna yiwen n Ṛṛuḥ kan i gellan ;

12:5 aṭas n leṣnaf n leqdic i gellan meɛna yiwen n Ssid-nneɣ kan i nesɛa ;

12:6 aṭas n leṣnaf n lecɣal i gellan, meɛna Ṛebbi d yiwen, d nețța i ten-isedduyen akk deg-nneɣ meṛṛa.

12:7 Yal yiwen tețțunefk-as-d tukci n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i nnfeɛ-nneɣ meṛṛa.

12:8 Ihi Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yefka-d i yiwen ad immeslay s ṣṣwab d lefhama, yefka-d i wayeḍ ad immeslay s tmusni.

12:9 Ṛṛuḥ-agi yefka daɣen i wayeḍ liman iǧehden, i wayeḍ tazmert s wayes ara yesseḥlu imuḍan ;

12:10 yefka daɣen i wayeḍ tazmert ad ixdem lbeṛhanat, ma d wayeḍ ad ițxebbiṛ s wayen i s-d-yețțassen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ; wayeḍ daɣen yefka-yas-ed tazmert s wayes ara yeɛqel ayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi d wayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ leṛwaḥ n diri ; wayeḍ yețțunefk-as-d ad immeslay timeslayin ur nețwassen ara ; wayeḍ daɣen yețțunefk-as-d a d-yessefhem timeslayin-agi ur nețwassen ara.

12:11 Ayagi akk itekk-ed seg yiwen n Ṛṛuḥ. Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen yețțak-ed ayagi meṛṛa akken yebɣa, i yal yiwen deg nneɣ.

12:12 Lǧețța n wemdan d yiwet, ɣas akken tesɛa aṭas n lemfaṣel. Lemfaṣel-agi ɣas deg waṭas yid-sen, d yiwet n lǧețța i llan ; akken daɣen i gella Lmasiḥ.

12:13 Axaṭer s yiwen n Ṛṛuḥ kan i nețwaɣḍes meṛṛa iwakken a nili d yiwet n lǧețța ; ama d at Isṛail neɣ d iyunaniyen, ama d aklan neɣ d iḥeṛṛiyen, seg yiwen n Ṛṛuḥ i neswa meṛṛa.

12:14 Lǧețța ur tebni ara ɣef yiwen n lmefṣel, lameɛna tebna ɣef waṭas n lemfaṣel.

12:15 Ma yenna-d uḍar : nekk mačči d afus ur țekkiɣ ara di lǧețța, eɛni imi ur yelli ara d afus ur ițekka ara ula d nețța di lǧețța ?

12:16 Ma yella yenna-d umeẓẓuɣ : nekk mačči ț-țiṭ, ur țekkiɣ ara di lǧețța, eɛni ț-țideț m'ur yelli ara ț-țiṭ ur ițekka ara ula d nețța di lǧețța ?

12:17 Ma yella lǧețța meṛṛa ț-țiṭ, amek ara tili tmesliwt ? Neɣ ma yella d ameẓẓuɣ kan i tella, amek ara yili wesriḥi ?

12:18 Sidi Ṛebbi yebna mkul lmefṣel di lǧețța akken i s-yehwa.

12:19 Ma yella lǧețța meṛṛa d yiwen n lmefṣel, anda-ț ihi lǧețța ?

12:20 S wakka ihi aṭas n lemfaṣel i gellan, meɛna lǧețța yiwet.

12:21 Tiṭ ur tezmir ara aț-țini i ufus : ur k-uḥwaǧeɣ ara ! Aqeṛṛuy ur yezmir ara ad yini i uḍaṛ : ur k-uḥwaǧeɣ ara.

12:22 Lemfaṣel-nni n lǧețța i d-yețbanen d imecṭaḥ, nefɛen aṭas.

12:23 Lemfaṣel n lǧețța iwumi ur nefki ara lqima, d nutni i nețḥadar akteṛ n wiyaḍ ; ihi d lemfaṣel ur nezmir ara a d-nebder i nețḥadar aṭas,

12:24 ma d wid yesɛan lqima, ur uḥwaǧen ara lemḥadra. Sidi Ṛebbi yebna lǧețța akken ara izegged lqima i lemfaṣel ixuṣṣen,

12:25 iwakken ur d-yețțili ara lxilaf ger lemfaṣel, meɛna ad țemḥadaren wway gar-asen.

12:26 Ma yella yiwen n lmefṣel yețwaqṛeḥ, wiyaḍ meṛṛa ad țwaqeṛḥen yid-es ; ma yella yețțunefk-as ccan i wayeḍ, wiyaḍ akk ad feṛḥen yid-es.

12:27 Kunwi meṛṛa d lǧețța n Lmasiḥ, yal yiwen deg-wen d lmefṣel di lǧețța-s, mkul yiwen deg umkan-is.

12:28 Di tejmaɛt n Lmasiḥ, Sidi Ṛebbi yesbedd uqbel ṛṛusul, deffir-nsen wid i d-yețxebbiṛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, rnan-d wid yesselmaden, rnan-d daɣen wid yesɛan tazmert ad xedmen lbeṛhanat, wid yesɛan tazmert ad sseḥlun imuḍan, wid ara iɛiwnen wiyaḍ, wid ara yilin d imdebbṛen akk-d wid ara yemmeslayen timeslayin ur nețwassen ara.

12:29 Eɛni țțuceggɛen-d meṛṛa d ṛṛusul ? Neɣ țxebbiṛen-d akk s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ? Sselmaden meṛṛa ? Xeddmen meṛṛa lbeṛhanat ?

12:30 Eɛni sɛan meṛṛa tazmert ad sseḥlun imuḍan, neɣ ad mmeslayen meṛṛa timeslayin ur nețwassen ara ? Neɣ zemren akk a d-sfehmen timeslayin-agi ?

12:31 Ihi a wen-d-iniɣ : nadit axiṛ ɣef tikciwin-nni inefɛen aṭas. A wen d-mleɣ tura abrid yifen akk iberdan.

13:1 Ɣas zemreɣ ad mmeslayeɣ timeslayin n ddunit meṛṛa, ad rnuɣ tid n lmalayekkat, m'ur sɛiɣ ara leḥmala n tideț, ad iliɣ am ṭṭbel yeddendunen neɣ nnaqus yeṭṭenṭunen.

13:2 Ɣas ma yețțunefk-iyi-d ad țxebbiṛeɣ s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, ad fehmeɣ kra yellan d lbaḍna, ad sɛuɣ tamusni di mkul lḥaǧa, ɣas ad sɛuɣ liman s wayes i zemreɣ ad iniɣ i wedrar qleɛ iman-ik syagi tṛuḥeḍ ɣer wemkan ihina, m'ur sɛiɣ ara leḥmala ɣer wiyaḍ, nekk d ulac.

13:3 Ɣas daɣen ad feṛqeɣ ayen akk sɛiɣ i wid yelluẓen, ad rnuɣ ad sebbleɣ tudert-iw alamma d lmut ɣef ddemma n wiyaḍ, m'ur sɛiɣ ara leḥmala deg ul-iw, ayagi ur iyi-infiɛ ara.

13:4 Leḥmala n tideț tețțawi-d ṣṣbeṛ, leḥmala tețɛawan, leḥmala ur tesɛi ara tismin, ur tesɛi ara zzux, win yesɛan leḥmala deg ul-is ur yețcekkiṛ ara iman-is,

13:5 Win yesɛan leḥmala n tideț ur ițnadi ara ad ixdem cceṛ, ur yețnadi ara ɣef nnfeɛ-ines, ur izeɛɛef ur yețțaṭṭaf cceḥnat ;

13:6 Win yesɛan leḥmala ur ifeṛṛeḥ ara s lbaṭel meɛna ifeṛṛeḥ s lḥeqq;

13:7 leḥmala tețsamaḥ kullec, tețțamen, tessaram tsebbeṛ i kullec.

13:8 Axebbeṛ n wayen i d-yețțasen s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi ad ifak, timeslayin ur nețwassen ara ad ḥebsent, tamusni aț-țefnu, ma d leḥmala n tideț werǧin aț-țfak ;

13:9 axaṭer tamusni-nneɣ txuṣṣ, ayen nețbecciṛ s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi ur innekmal ara,

13:10 meɛna m'ara d-yaweḍ wayen innekmalen, ayen ixuṣṣen meṛṛa ad imḥu.

13:11 Asmi lliɣ d aqcic, țmeslayeɣ am weqcic, țxemmimeɣ, țmeyyizeɣ slɛeqliya n weqcic ; asmi uɣaleɣ d argaz, ǧǧiɣ lɛeqliya n weqcic.

13:12 Ass-agi, nețwali am akken di lemri yumsen, meɛna imiren a nuɣal a nwali akken ilaq ; ass-agi tamusni inu mazal txuṣṣ lameɛna ad uɣaleɣ ad issineɣ akken i yi-issen Sidi Ṛebbi.

13:13 Tura ihi tlata n leḥwayeǧ-agi i gesɛan lqima : liman, asirem d leḥmala ; lameɛna di tlata-agi, d lehmala i ț-țameqqrant.

14:1 Qbel kullec nadit aț-țesɛum leḥmala n tideț. Nadit daɣen ɣef tukciwin n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, lameɛna nadit axiṛ a d-țxebbiṛem s wayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi.

14:2 Win yețmeslayen tutlayin ur nețwassen ara, mačči i yemdanen i gețmeslay lameɛna i Ṛebbi, imi yiwen ur ifehhem acu i d-yeqqaṛ ; axaṭer s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i d-iqqaṛ ayen ur nezmir a t-nefhem.

14:3 Ma d win i d-ițxebbiṛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, ițmeslay-ed i yemdanen, yesnernay liman-nsen, inehhu-ten, yețṣebbiṛ-iten.

14:4 Win yețmeslayen tutlayin ur nețwassen ara, yesseǧhad iman-is di liman, ma d win i d-ițxebbiṛen s wayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, yesseǧhad tajmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi meṛṛa.

14:5 Bɣiɣ aț-țemmeslayem meṛṛa s tutlayin ur nețwassen ara, lameɛna bɣiɣ axiṛ ma tețxebbiṛem s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi. Win yețxebbiṛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi yenfeɛ akteṛ n win yețmeslayen tutlayin-agi, anagar ma yella izmer a d-yessefhem ayen i d-yenna, iwakken tajmaɛt meṛṛa aț-țestenfeɛ.

14:6 Tura ay atmaten, lemmer a n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen a wen-d mmeslayeɣ s tutlayt ur nețwassen ara, deg wacu ara kkun-nefɛeɣ ? A kkun-nefɛeɣ ma wwiɣ-awen-d ayen i yi-d-ițțuweḥḥan d wayen i yi-d-yețțuxebṛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, neɣ țamusni akk-d uselmed !

14:7 A d-nefk lemtel n dduzan n lmusiqa am tjewwaqt d snitra : ma yella ur yemxalaf ara ṣṣut-nsent, amek ara neɛqel ṣṣut n tjewaqt neɣ win n snitra ?

14:8 Neɣ daɣen m'ur neɛqil ara mliḥ ṣṣut n lbuq s wayes i țheggin imnayen iman-nsen ɣer imenɣi, anwa ara iheggin iman-is ɣer umenɣi ?

14:9 Kunwi daɣen, ma yella tneṭqem-d s tutlayt ur nețwassen ara, amek ara nefhem d acu i d-teqqaṛem ? Am akken tețmeslayem i waḍu !

14:10 Llant aṭas n tutlayin yemxalafen di ddunit, lameɛna sɛant akk lmeɛna.

14:11 Ma yella ur fhimeɣ ara tameslayt n walebɛaḍ, ad iliɣ d abeṛṛani ar ɣuṛ-es, nețța daɣen ad yili ɣuṛ-i d abeṛṛani.

14:12 Kunwi daɣen, imi tețnadim aț-țesɛum tukciwin i d-yețțak Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, nadit axiṛ aț-țesɛum ayen ara inefɛen tajmaɛt n Sidi Ṛebbi.

14:13 Daymi, win yețmeslayen tutlayt ur netwassen ara ilaq ad issuter i Ṛebbi a s-d-yefk ad yessefhem ayen i d-iqqaṛ.

14:14 Axaṭer ma yella deɛɛuɣ s tutlayt ur nețwassen ara, d ṛṛuḥ-iw i gdeɛɛun ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi mačči d lefhama-inu.

14:15 D acu ara xedmeɣ ihi ? Ad dɛuɣ s ṛṛuḥ, lameɛna ad dɛuɣ daɣen s lefhama-inu ; ad cnuɣ s ṛṛuḥ, ad cnuɣ daɣen s lefhama-inu.

14:16 Axaṭer ma yella tḥemdeḍ Ṛebbi s ṛṛuḥ-ik kan s tutlayt ur nețwassen ara, win yeqqimen gar-awen, ur nessin ara, amek ara d-yini « amin » i yimeslayen-ik, m'ur yefhim ara d acu i d-teqqaṛeḍ ?

14:17 Ɣas yimeslayen-ik s wayes i tḥemdeḍ Ṛebbi gerrzen aṭas, ur nfiɛen ara win ur nefhim ara d acu i d-teqqaṛeḍ.

14:18 Ad ḥemdeɣ Ṛebbi imi țmeslayeɣ tutlayin ur nețwassen ara akteṛ-nwen meṛṛa.

14:19 Meɛna di tejmaɛt, axiṛ-iyi a d-iniɣ xemsa yimeslayen yețwafehmen iwakken ad lemden wiyaḍ, wala ɛecṛa alaf imeslayen s tutlayt ur nețwassen ara.

14:20 Ay atmaten, ur țțilit ara am arrac m'ara tmeyyzem ɣef kra, lameɛna sɛut lɛeqliya n imeqqranen ; ma ɣef wayen yeɛnan cceṛ, ilit am arrac.

14:21 Atan wayen yuran di tira : Sidi Ṛebbi yenna-d : A d-mmeslayeɣ i wegdud-agi+ seg imawen n ibeṛṛaniyen + s tutlayin nniḍen+ , meɛna ur iyi-d-smeḥsisen ara.+ +

14:22 Ihi tutlayin-agi ur nețwassen ara, d licaṛa i wid ur numin ara mačči i wid yumnen ; ma d win i d ițxebbiṛen s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi, d licaṛa i wid yumnen mačči i wid ur numin ara.

14:23 Ma yella nnejmaɛen imasiḥiyen meṛṛa deg yiwen wemkan, bdan țḥemmiden akk Sidi Ṛebbi s tutlayin ur nețwassen ara, ma kecmen-d yemdanen ur nessin ara ayagi neɣ wid ur numin ara s Lmasiḥ, eɛni ur d-qqaṛen ara tedrewcem ?

14:24 Meɛna ma yella țxebbiṛen-d akk s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, ma ikcem-ed win ur numin ara neɣ win ur nessin ara, a d-yawi s lexbaṛ belli d amednub i gella ɣef ddemma n wayen yesla, ad iḥuss deg iman-is belli ad ițțuḥaseb ;

14:25 ayen yeffren deg ul-is a d-iban, dɣa ad yeɣli ɣer lqaɛa ɣef wudem, ad yeɛbed Sidi Ṛebbi ad yini : ț-țideț Ṛebbi gar-awen i gella !

14:26 D acu ihi i glaqen ay atmaten ? M'ara tennejmaɛem akken tellam : wa a d-yawi ccna, wayeḍ d aselmed, wayeḍ d aweḥḥi, wayeḍ timeslayin ur nețwassen ara, wayeḍ a tent-id issefhem ; kullec ad yețwaxdem i wesnerni n tejmaɛt.

14:27 Ma llan wid yețmeslayen timeslayin ur nețwassen ara, sin neɣ tlata a d-mmeslayen meɛna yal yiwen s nnuba-s, yerna ilaq ad yili win ara d-yesfehmen ayen i d-qqaṛen.

14:28 Ma ulac win ara d-yesfehmen, ad ssusmen axiṛ di tejmaɛt, ad mmeslayen deg iman-nsen kan akk-d Sidi Ṛebbi.

14:29 Ma llan wid i d-ițxebbiṛen s wayen i d-ițțasen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, sin neɣ tlata a d-mmeslayen, wiyaḍ ad meyzen ɣef wayen i d-nnan.

14:30 Ma yella gar-awen win iwumi i d-iweḥḥa Sidi Ṛebbi kra, ilaq ad iɛass alamma yessusem win akken yellan yețmeslay.

14:31 Tzemrem akk a d tețxebbiṛem s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, meɛna wa deffir wa, iwakken atmaten meṛṛa ad lemden, ad innerni liman-nsen.

14:32 Win i d-ițxebbiṛen s ɣuṛ Sidi Ṛebbi yeḥkem deg wayen i d-iqqaṛ,

14:33 axaṭer Sidi Ṛebbi mačči d Ṛebbi n ussexṛeb, meɛna d win iḥemmlen lehna.

14:34 Am akken xeddmen di tejmuyaɛ n wid iḍuɛen Sidi Ṛebbi, ilaq tilawin ad ssusment di tejmaɛt, ur ilaq ara a d-grent iman-nsent ; meɛna ad qadṛent tajmaɛt, ad aɣent awal akken i t-id-tenna ccariɛa.

14:35 Ma yella bɣant ad steqsint ɣef wayen ur fhiment ara, ad steqsint irgazen-nsent deg wexxam ; axaṭer ur yessefk ara i tmeṭṭut a d-ger iman-is di tejmaɛt.

14:36 Eɛni s ɣuṛ-wen i d-yekka wawal n Ṛebbi ? Neɣ ɣuṛ-wen kan i gella ?

14:37 Ma yella yiwen deg-wen iwala iman-is yețweḥḥi-yas-d Sidi Ṛebbi neɣ yețxebbiṛ-ed s ɣuṛ-es, ilaq ad iɛqel belli ayen akka i wen-d-uriɣ d lameṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi ;

14:38 meɛna ma yella inkeṛ-it, iban belli ur yețwaqbel ara ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi.

14:39 Akka ihi ay atmaten, ssirmet a d-tețxebbiṛem s ɣuṛ Ṛebbi. Ur qeṭṭɛet ara i win yebɣan ad immeslay s tutlayin ur nețwassen ara,

14:40 acu kan xḍut i ussexṛeb, xedmet kullec s țțawil.

15:1 Ay atmaten, bɣiɣ a wen-d smektiɣ tura lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i wen-d-ssawḍeɣ, win akken i tqeblem yerna tḥerzem-t ;

15:2 s Lexbaṛ agi n lxiṛ i tețwasellkem ma yella tettebɛem-t akken i wen-t-id-nniɣ, neɣ m'ulac liman-nwen am akken d aḍu.

15:3 Slemdeɣ-awen di tazwara ayen i lemdeɣ nekkini : Lmasiḥ yemmut ɣef ddnubat-nneɣ akken yura ;

15:4 yețwamḍel, yuɣal-ed ɣer tudert ass wis tlata, akken i t-id-nnant tira iqedsen.

15:5 Iwala-t Buṭrus, walan-t daɣen ṛṛusul di tnac yid-sen.

15:6 Rnan walan-t daɣen akteṛ n xemsmeyya n watmaten deg yiwet n teswiɛt ; aṭas deg-sen mazal-iten ddren ar tura, ma d kra deg-sen mmuten.

15:7 Yerna iwala-t Yeɛqub akk-d ṛṛusul meṛṛa.

15:8 Mi t-walan akk, walaɣ-t idheṛ-iyi-d daɣen i nekkini yellan d ulac.

15:9 Nekk, yellan d aneggaru akk di ṛṛusul, ur uklaleɣ ara ad țțusemmiɣ d ṛṛasul axaṭer lliɣ țqehhiṛeɣ tajmaɛt n warraw n Ṛebbi.

15:10 S ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi i wwḍeɣ ɣer wayen akka i lliɣ tura ; ṛṛeḥma i yi-d-ifka ur tṛuḥ ara deg ulac, lameɛna uɣaleɣ xedmeɣ akteṛ-nsen meṛṛa, mačči s yiman-iw, meɛna s ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi yellan yid-i.

15:11 S wakka ihi, ama d nekk ama d nutni, atah wayen i nețbecciṛ yerna d ayen s wayes i tumnem.

15:12 Ma neqqaṛ belli Lmasiḥ yeḥya-d si ger lmegtin, amek ihi kra deg-wen qqaṛen ulac ḥeggu n lmegtin ?

15:13 Ma yella ulac ḥeggu n lmegtin, ihi Lmasiḥ daɣen ur d-iḥyi ara.

15:14 Ma yella ihi Lmasiḥ ur d-iḥyi ara, ayen i nețbecciṛ d aḍu, ayen s wayes tumnem daɣen d aḍu.

15:15 Lemmer ț-țideț lmegtin ur d ḥeggun ara, neskaddeb ɣef Sidi Ṛebbi nukni i d-icehden belli yesseḥya-d Lmasiḥ si ger lmegtin. Lemmer ur t-id-isseḥyi ara, ihi lmegtin ur d-ḥeggun ara ;

15:16 axaṭer ma yella lmegtin ur d-ḥeggun ara, Lmasiḥ daɣen ur d-iḥyi ara.

15:17 Ma yella Lmasiḥ ur d-iḥyi ara, ihi liman-nwen am akken d aḍu, mazal-ikkun di ddnub-nwen,

15:18 ula d imasiḥiyen yemmuten negren, liman-nsen iṛuḥ d ulac.

15:19 Ma yella asirem-nneɣ di Lmasiḥ di ddunit-agi kan i gella, d nukni i gețɣiḍen akteṛ n yemdanen n ddunit meṛṛa.

15:20 Meɛna Lmasiḥ iḥya-d si lmut, d nețța i d amezwaru i d-iḥyan ɣef wid akk yemmuten ;

15:21 axaṭer, am akken i d-tekcem lmut ɣer ddunit s yiwen wergaz, s yiwen wergaz daɣen i d-yusa ḥeggu n lmegtin.

15:22 Am akken i weṛten meṛṛa yemdanen lmut imi d-kkan s ɣuṛ Adam, akken daɣen wid akk yumnen s Lmasiḥ a d-ḥyun si ger lmegtin imi umnen yis,

15:23 ḥeggu-agi ilaq a d-idṛu di lweqt-is : Lmasiḥ iḥya-d d amezwaru, ass n tuɣalin-is a d-ḥyun meṛṛa wid yellan d ayla-s.

15:24 Imiren a d-taweḍ taggara, Lmasiḥ ad issenger tizmar, lḥekmat d wid iḥekmen ; ad yerr tagelda i Sidi Ṛebbi Baba-tneɣ.

15:25 Axaṭer ilaq Lmasiḥ ad yeḥkem alamma yerra iɛdawen-is seddaw iḍaṛṛen-is.

15:26 Aɛdaw aneggaru ara yessenger, d lmut.

15:27 Axaṭer am akken yura : Sidi Ṛebbi yerra kullec seddaw yiḍaṛṛen-is+ . Meɛna mi gura belli kullec yuɣal seddaw yiḍaṛṛen-is, iban belli Win yerran kullec seddaw yidaṛṛen-is ur ițekki ara.

15:28 M'ara yuɣal kullec ger ifassen n Lmasiḥ, imiren nețța yellan d mmi-s, ad yuɣal seddaw n lḥekma n win i s-d-yefkan kullec ; iwakken Sidi Ṛebbi ad yeḥkem ɣef kullec.

15:29 Kra n yemdanen țnadin ad țwaɣedṣen ɣas lukan ad awḍen ɣer lmut,f+ lemmer ț-țideț lmegtin ur d-ḥeggun ara, acuɣeṛ ihi i țnadin ad țwaɣedṣen ?

15:30 I nukni, acimi i nețqabal lmut kull ass ?

15:31 Ay atmaten, yal ass țwaliɣ lmut ger wallen-iw ; s tideț i wen-d-nniɣ ayagi axaṭer tellam d sebba n zzux-iw imi tedduklem d Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

15:32 Ma yella ɣef ddemma n ccan n yemdanen i nnuɣeɣ d lewḥuc di temdint n Ifasus, acu n lfayda i sɛiɣ ? Ihi m'ur d-ḥeggun ara lmegtin : a nṛuḥ kan a nečč, a nsew axaṭer azekka a nemmet akken i t-id-iqqaṛ yiwen n lemtel.

15:33 Ḥadret aț-țețwakellxem, axaṭer tikli d yemcumen tessufuɣ i webrid.

15:34 Uɣalet-ed ɣer leɛqel nwen, xḍut i yir abrid ; axaṭer kra deg-wen țeddun am akken ur ssinen ara Ṛebbi ; ayagi nniɣ-t-id iwakken aț-țenneḥcamem.

15:35 Meɛna alebɛaḍ a d-yini : « amek ara d-ḥyun lmegtin ? Amek ara tili lǧețța s wayes ara d-uɣalen ? »

15:36 A win ur nefhim ! Ayen ara tzerɛeḍ m'ur yemmut ara deg wakal, ur d-ițamɣay ara.

15:37 Ayen akken i tzerɛeḍ mačči d ayen i d-yemɣin meɛna d aɛeqqa kan n yired neɣ n zerriɛa nniḍen.

15:38 Imiren Sidi Ṛebbi a s-yefk lǧețța akken yebɣa, yal zzerriɛa a s-yefk ṣṣifa i s-ilaqen.

15:39 Seg wayen akk yețțidiren ur sɛin ara irkul yiwet n ṣṣifa ; ṣṣifa n wemdan weḥd-es, tin n lmal weḥd-es, tin n yefṛax weḥd-es, tin n iselman daɣen weḥd-es.

15:40 Llant daɣen ṣṣifat n igenwan akk-d ṣṣifat n ddunit, yal yiwet s tafat-is ;

15:41 tafat n yiṭij weḥd-es, tafat n waggur weḥd-es, tafat n yitran daɣen weḥd-es ; ula seg itri ɣer wayeḍ temxalaf tafat-nsen.

15:42 Akka ara tedṛu asm'ara d-ḥyun lmegtin. Lǧețța m'ara tekcem akal, trekku ; meɛna m'ara d-teḥyu ur tețțuɣal ara aț-țerku.

15:44 Tekcem ɣer wakal s yiwet n ṣṣifa, a d-teḥyu s ṣṣifa nniḍen ; akken tella lǧețța n wemdan, tella daɣen lǧețța n Ṛṛuḥ.

15:46 Mačči d amdan n ṛṛuḥ i d-yusan d amezwaru, meɛna d amdan n ddunit, amdan n ṛṛuḥ yerna-d deffir-es.

15:47 Amdan amezwaru yekka-d seg wakal, d amdan n ddunit ; amdan wis sin yusa-d seg igenni.

15:48 Akken yella wemdan n ddunit, ara yilin yemdanen n ddunit ; daɣen akken yella wemdan n igenwan ara yilin yemdanen n igenwan.

15:49 Akken i ncuba ɣer wemdan n ddunit ara ncabi ɣer wemdan n igenwan.

15:51 A wen-d-iniɣ yiwet n lbaḍna : ur nețɛedday ara akk si lmut meɛna lǧețțat-nneɣ ad nbeddalent.

15:52 Deg yiwet n teswiɛt, qqen tiṭ-ik teldiḍ-ț, m'ara d-inṭeq lbuq aneggaru, axaṭer lbuq a d-inṭeq, lmegtin a d-ḥyun ur țțuɣalen ara ad mmten, ma d wid i d-yufa lḥal mazal-iten ddren, ad beddlen ṣṣifa.

15:53 Ilaq lǧețța-yagi irekkun aț-țels ayen ur nrekku ara, lǧețța ara yemten aț-țels ayen ur nețmețțat ara.

15:54 Asm'ara tels lǧețța-agi irekkun ayen ur nrekku, asmi ara tels lǧețța-agi ara yemten ayen ur nețmețțat, ad yedṛu wawal-nni yuran di tira iqedsen : Tazmert n Sidi Ṛebbi teɣleb lmut, tessebleɛ-iț. +

15:55 A lmut, anida-ț tezmert-im ? A lmut, anda-ț tsiqest-im + ?

15:56 Tisiqest n lmut, d ddnub ; ma d ddnub, d ccariɛa i d-isbeggnen tazmert-is.

15:57 A neḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi i ɣ-d-yefkan tazmert-is s Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ !

15:58 S wakka ay atmaten eɛzizen, ǧehdet ur ɣellit ara. Țnernit kul ass di lecɣal n Ssid-nneɣ, ẓret belli leɛtab-nwen ɣef ddemma n Ssid-nneɣ ur yețțili ara mbla lfayda.

16:1 Ɣef wayen yeɛnan ajmaɛ n yedrimen i watmaten n temdint n Lquds yellan di lexṣaṣ, xedmet akken i weṣṣaɣ ad xedmen di tejmuyaɛ n tmurt n Galasya.

16:2 Mkul ass amezwaru n dduṛt, ilaq yal yiwen deg-wen ad yerr di rrif ayen iwumi yezmer n yedrimen, ur țṛaǧut ara a n-awḍeɣ d wamek ara ten-id jemɛem.

16:3 M'ara n-awḍeɣ, ad ceggɛeɣ wid ara d-textiṛem, a sen-fkeɣ tibṛatin, ad ṛuḥen ad awin ayen akken i d-tjemɛem ɣer temdint n Lquds.

16:4 Ma yella twalam ilaq ad ṛuḥeɣ nekk s yiman-iw, ad ddun yid-i.

16:5 Ad zegreɣ qbel tamurt n Masidunya d wamek ara n-aseɣ ɣuṛ-wen, axaṭer ilaq-iyi a n-ɛeddiɣ syenna.

16:6 Ad qqimeɣ kra n wussan, neɣ ahat ad sɛeddiɣ ccetwa ɣuṛ-wen, s wakka aț-țzemrem a yi-tɛiwnem iwakken ad kemleɣ abrid-iw.

16:7 Tikkelt-agi, ur bɣiɣ ara a n-ɛeddiɣ d abrid kan, meɛna ssarameɣ ad qqimeɣ kra n lweqt ɣuṛ-wen, ma yebɣa Sidi Ṛebbi.

16:8 Tura, aql-i ad qqimeɣ dagi di temdint n Ifasus, alamma d ass n lɛid n wass wis xemsin.

16:9 Axaṭer ldint iyi-d tewwura iwakken ad xedmeɣ yiwen ccɣel d ameqqran, ɣas akken aṭas n yeɛdawen i gellan.

16:10 M'ara n-iɛeddi ɣuṛ-wen gma tneɣ Timuti, lhit-ed yid-es a s-ikkes ukukru axaṭer d aqeddac n Sidi Ṛebbi i gella am nekk.

16:11 Ḥadret ad yili win ara t-iḥeqṛen. Ɛiwnet-eț ad ikemmel abrid-is iwakken a d-yaweḍ ɣuṛ-i di lehna ; aql-aɣ nețṛaǧu-t nekk d watmaten.

16:12 Ma d gma-tneɣ Abulus, nhiɣ-t aṭas iwakken a n-iddu d watmaten nniḍen ara n-yuɣalen ɣuṛ-wen, meɛna ur yebɣi ara a n-iṛuḥ tura ; a n-yas m'ara s-iserreḥ lḥal.

16:13 Ɛasset, ṭṭfet di liman, ilit d irgazen, ǧehdet.

16:14 Xedmet kullec s leḥmala.

16:15 Tessnem Stifanas ț-țwacult-is, teẓram belli di tmurt n Akaya d nutni i gumnen d imezwura, yerna uɣalen d iqeddacen ɣef wegdud n Sidi Ṛebbi. Ad ssutreɣ deg-wen ay atmaten,

16:16 aț-țaɣem awal i yergazen am wigi, aț-țqadṛem wid akk yețțekkin di leqdic yid-sen.

16:17 Aql-i feṛḥeɣ mi d-usan Stifanas, Furtunatus akk-d Axaykus ; feṛḥeɣ yis-sen am akken d kunwi.

16:18 axaṭer hednen ul-iw d wulawen nwen. Ilaq a nissin a nefk lqima i yergazen am wigi.

16:19 Tijmuyaɛ n tmura n Asya țselliment-ed fell-awen. Akilas akk-d Brisila d wid yețnejmaɛen deg wexxam-nsen țsellimen-d fell-awen s yisem n Ssid-nneɣ.

16:20 Atmaten akk yellan dagi țsellimen-d fell-awen. Msalamet wway gar-awen s sslam n tegmaț.

16:21 Nekk Bulus, s ufus-iw i d-uriɣ imeslayen-agi n sslam.
