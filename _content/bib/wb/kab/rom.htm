2:3 I kečč i gețḥasaben wid ixeddmen lecɣal-agi, ma txeddmeḍ am nutni, tɣileḍ aț-țmenɛeḍ i lḥisab n Sidi Ṛebbi ?

2:4 Eɛni tḥeqṛeḍ leḥnana d ṣṣbeṛ i gesɛa Sidi Ṛebbi mbla ceḥḥa ? Ur teẓriḍ ara belli iṣebber-ak s leḥnana-s iwakken aț-țbeddleḍ tikli ?

2:5 Lameɛna kečč yesɣaṛayen ul-ik tugiḍ aț-țbeddleḍ tikli, tețheggiḍ i yiman-ik lɛiqab ameqqran i wass n lḥisab, ass i deg ara d-ibin wurrif akk-d lḥeqq n Sidi Ṛebbi

2:6  ara yerren i mkul yiwen ayen yexdem .

2:7 ad yefk tudert n dayem i wid ixeddmen lxiṛ s ṣṣbeṛ, yețnadin ɣef lɛezz d lḥeṛma akk-d tudert n dayem.

2:8 Ma d wid yellan d imcumen, ikeṛhen tideț, ixeddmen lbaṭel, a d-isseɣli fell-asen urrif ț-țwaɣit ;

2:9 a d-isseɣli daɣen ccedda ț-țwaɣit ɣef yal amdan ixeddmen cceṛ, di tazwara ɣef wat Isṛail, a d-ḍefṛen leǧnas nniḍen.

2:10 Meɛna a d-ițțunefk lɛezz d lḥeṛma akk-d lehna i kra n win ixeddmen lxiṛ, i wat Isṛail uqbel, a d-ḍefṛen leǧnas nniḍen.

2:19 kečč i gɣilen tzemreḍ aț-țeṭṭfeḍ afus i iderɣalen, aț-țiliḍ ț-țafat n wid yellan di ṭṭlam ;

2:21 Tesselmadeḍ wiyaḍ, meɛna ur tesselmadeḍ ara iman-ik ;

2:26 Win ur neḍhiṛ ara, itebɛen ccariɛa, eɛni ur ițwaḥsab ara ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi am akken yeḍheṛ ?

2:27 Nețta ur neḍhiṛ ara di lǧețța-s, ixeddmen ayen i d-tenna ccariɛa, izmer a k-iḥaseb kečč iḍehṛen, yesnen ccariɛa meɛna ur txeddmeḍ ara ayen i d-teqqaṛ.

2:28 Win yellan s țideț n wat Isṛail, mačči s uqemmuc kan, neɣ s ṭṭhaṛa i d-ițbanen deg weglim-ines ;

4:17 Am akken yura di Tawṛat n Sidna Musa : Sbeddeɣ-k iwakken aț-țiliḍ d jedd n waṭas n leǧnas. Yuɣal d jeddi-tneɣ zdat Ṛebbi s wayes i gumen, i d-iḥeggun lmegtin, i d-ixelqen ayen ur nelli ara.

4:18 ?as akken ur d-iqqim usirem, Sidna Ibṛahim yuṛǧa s lețkal d ameqqran armi yuɣal d jedd n waṭas n yegduden akken i s-yenna Sidi Ṛebbi : Akka ara tili dderya n dderya-k.

4:19 Liman n Sidna Ibṛahim ur yenqis ara ɣas akken qṛib ad issiweḍ meyya iseggasen, iwala iman-is dayen ur yezmir ara ad isɛu dderya, yerna tameṭṭut-is Saṛa teǧǧa-ț dderya.

4:20 Yețkel ɣef Sidi Ṛebbi, ur t-yekcim ara ccekk ɣef wayen i s-yewɛed. D liman yesɛa i t-isǧehden, s wakka i gețḥemmid Sidi Ṛebbi Bab n tezmert.

4:21 Yețkel belli Sidi Ṛebbi yezmer a s-d-yefk ayen i s-yewɛed.

4:22 ?ef wannect-agi i t-yeḥseb Sidi Ṛebbi d aḥeqqi.

4:23 Mačči fell-as kan i gura di tira iqedsen : Yețwaḥseb d aḥeqqi,

4:24 lameɛna fell-aɣ ula d nukni, imi numen s win i d-isseḥyan Ɛisa Ssid nneɣ si ger lmegtin.

4:25 Yețțunefk ad immet ɣef ddemma n ddnubat nneɣ, isseḥya-t-id Sidi Ṛebbi si ger lmegtin iwakken a nuɣal d iḥeqqiyen.

5:1 Tura imi nuɣal d iḥeqqiyen s liman, ters-ed lehna gar-aɣ d Ṛebbi s Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

5:2 Yis daɣen i nufa abrid s liman ɣer ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi i deg nella tura. Lfeṛḥ-nneɣ, d asirem nesɛa belli a nțekki di lɛaḍima n Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:3 Ula di lmeḥnat, nețțili di lfeṛḥ axaṭer neẓra lmeḥna tețțawi-d ṣṣbeṛ,

5:4 ṣṣbeṛ isseǧhad deg ujeṛṛeb, lǧehd deg ujeṛṛeb ițțawi-d asirem.

5:5 Asirem-agi ulac deg-s lexdeɛ, axaṭer leḥmala n Sidi Ṛebbi teẓẓa deg ulawen-nneɣ s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i ɣ-d-yețțunefken ;

5:6 asm'akken i ɣ-iɣleb ddnub ur s-nezmir ara, Lmasiḥ yemmut ɣef ddemma n imednuben di lweqt ilaqen.

5:7 Iwɛeṛ i wemdan ad iqbel ad immet ɣef ddemma n uḥeqqi ; ahat ad yili win ara iqeblen ad immet ɣef ddemma n wemdan yelhan ;

5:8 ma d Lmasiḥ, nețța yemmut fell-aɣ m'akken nella d imednuben ; s wakka i ɣ-d-ibeggen Sidi Ṛebbi leḥmala-ines.

5:9 Imi nuɣal tura d iḥeqqiyen ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi s idammen n Lmasiḥ yuzzlen fell-aɣ, yis daɣen ara nețțusellek seg urrif n Sidi Ṛebbi.

5:10 Asm'akken nella d iɛdawen-is, yefka-d Mmi-s iwakken s lmut-is a ɣ-issemṣaleḥ yid-es. Tura imi nemṣalaḥ d Sidi Ṛebbi, a nețțusellek daɣen s tudert n Mmi-s.

5:11 Mačči d ayagi kan, lameɛna tura lfeṛḥ-nneɣ atan di Sidi Ṛebbi, s Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ i ɣ-issemṣalaḥen yid-es.

5:12 S wakka ihi, ddnub ikcem-ed ɣer ddunit s yiwen wemdan, amdan agi d Adem ; ddnub-agi yewwi-d lmut, lmut tețțawi akk imdanen, axaṭer imdanen meṛṛa denben.

5:13 Uqbel a d-yefk Sidi Ṛebbi ccariɛa i Musa, ddnub yella yakan di ddunit ; meɛna imdanen ur țțuḥasben ara s ccariɛa imi urɛad i d-tusi.

5:14 ?as akka, si zzman n Adem armi d zzman n Sidna Musa lmut teḥkem ula ɣef yemdanen ur nɛuṣa ara Ṛebbi am akken i t-iɛuṣa Adem, nețța yellan d lemtel n win ara d-yasen.

5:15 Ur nezmir ara a nmettel ddnub n Adem ɣer lemziya d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi ; Adem iɛuṣa Sidi Ṛebbi yiwet n tikkelt yewwi-d lmut i yemdanen meṛṛa, acḥal ihi meqqṛet ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi i d-yețțunefken i yemdanen s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

5:16 Ṛṛeḥma-agi n Sidi Ṛebbi mačči am ddnub n yiwen wemdan. Axaṭer yiwen n ddnub yewwi-d lḥisab d lɛiqab, ma d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi, tețțekkes lḥisab d lɛiqab ɣef waṭas n ddnubat.

5:17 Ma yella lmut teḥkem ɣef yemdanen meṛṛa s ddnub n yiwen wemdan, ihi wid iwumi d-tețțunefk ṛṛeḥma d lḥeqq, ad ḥekmen yerna ad idiren s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

5:18 S wakka, ddnub n yiwen wemdan yesseɣli-d lɛiqab ɣef yemdanen meṛṛa, akken daɣen lḥeqq i gexdem yiwen wemdan, yewwi-d leslak i gessidiren imdanen meṛṛa.

5:19 Ihi am akken ɛeṣṣu n yiwen wemdan, yerra aṭas n yemdanen d imednuben, akken daɣen ṭṭaɛa n yiwen wemdan terra aṭas n yemdanen d iḥeqqiyen.

5:20 Ccariɛa tger-ed iman-is iwakken a d-tbeggen acḥal meqqeṛ ddnub, lameɛna anda yekteṛ ddnub, ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi meqqṛet akteṛ.

5:21 S wakka ddnub yeḥkem s lmut, ma d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi teḥkem s lḥeqq, tețțawi ɣer tudert n dayem s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ.

6:1 Acu ara d-nini ihi ? A nkemmel a nețɛici di ddnub iwakken ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi aț-țimɣuṛ ?

6:2 Ala ! Nukni i gemmuten ɣef ddemma n ddnub, amek ara nkemmel a nețɛici deg-s ?

6:3 Neɣ ur teẓrim ara belli nukni akk i gețwaɣeḍsen iwakken a neddukel d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, nețwaɣḍeṣ yid-es di lmut-is ?

6:4 S weɣḍas, nețwamḍel yid-es iwakken, nukni daɣen a d-neḥyu akken i d-yeḥya Lmasiḥ si ger lmegtin s tezmert n Ṛebbi, a nekcem di tudert tajḍiṭ.

6:5 Axaṭer ma necrek yid-es di lmut-is, a necrek daɣen yid-es di ḥeggu-ines.

6:6 Ilaq-aɣ a nẓer belli amdan-nni i nella zik, yemmut, ițwasemmeṛ akk-d Lmasiḥ iwakken aț-țemmet lǧețța yeččuṛen d ddnub, ur nețțili ara d aklan n ddnub.

6:7 Axaṭer win yemmuten islek si ddnub,

6:8 imi nemmut akk-d Lmasiḥ, numen daɣen belli a nidir yid-es.

6:9 Neẓra belli Lmasiḥ seg wasmi i d-yeḥya si ger lmegtin, ur yețțuɣal ara ad immet, lmut ur tezmir ara aț-țeḥkem fell-as.

6:10 Axaṭer taluft n ddnub yefra-ț ɣef yiwet n tikkelt i dayem, s lmut-is. Tura atan d lḥey, idder i Sidi Ṛebbi.

6:11 Ula d kunwi ḥesbet iman-nwen am akken temmutem ɣef wayen yeɛnan ddnub. Ɛicet i Ṛebbi di tdukkli akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

6:12 Tura ur țțaǧat ara ddnub ad iḥkem fell-awen, ur xeddmet ara ccehwat n tnefsit-nwen am zik.

6:13 Ur țțaǧat ara lemfaṣel n lǧețțat-nwen ad ɣlint di ddnub, iwakken ad ixdem yis-sen cceṛ ; lameɛna qeddmet iman-nwen i Ṛebbi am win i d-iḥyan si lmut iwakken aț-țqeddcem fell-as a ț-țxeddmem anagar lxiṛ.

6:14 Ddnub ur yețțuɣal ara ad iḥkem fell-awen axaṭer ur tellim ara seddaw n ccariɛa lameɛna seddaw n ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi.

6:15 Acu ara d-nini ihi ? A neǧǧ ddnub ad idebbeṛ fell-aɣ tura imi nella seddaw n ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi mačči seddaw n ccariɛa ? A ɣ-imneɛ Ṛebbi deg wannect-agi !

6:16 Ur teẓrim ara belli ma tuɣalem d aklan n yiwen iwakken ad idebbeṛ fell-awen, ilaq a s-taɣem awal ama d ddnub yețțawin ɣer lmut neɣ d ṭṭaɛa n Ṛebbi yețțawin ɣer tudert taḥeqqit.

6:17 Lameɛna a neḥmed Ṛebbi imi zik tellam d aklan n ddnub, ma tura tḍuɛem s wulawen-nwen awal i tlemdem.

6:18 Tețțusellkem-d si ddnub tuɣalem d aklan n lḥeqq.

6:19 Tura a wen-d-hedṛeɣ s lɛeqliya nwen iwakken aț-țfehmem axaṭer annect-agi yewɛeṛ i lefhama. Am akken tsellmem iman-nwen zik d aklan i lɛaṛ iwakken aț-țxedmem lɛaṛ, tura sellmet iman-nwen d aklan i lḥeqq d ṣṣwab iwakken aț-țeṣfu tudert-nwen.

6:20 Asmi tellam d aklan n ddnub texḍam i lḥeqq.

6:21 Acu i trebḥem s lecɣal-nni s wayes tessetḥam tura, lecɣal-nni i gețțawin ɣer lmut.

6:22 Tura tețwaselkem si ddnub, tuɣalem d iqeddacen n Ṛebbi, lfayda nwen ț-țikli deg webrid yeṣfan akk-d tudert n dayem.

6:23 Ddnub yețțawi ɣer lmut, ma d ṛṛeḥma n Sidi Ṛebbi tewwi-yaɣ-d tudert n dayem di tikli-nneɣ akk-d Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ.

7:1 Ay atmaten ur teẓrim ara belli ccariɛa teḥkem ɣef wemdan skud mazal-it idder ? Heddṛeɣ-ed akka i wid yessnen ccariɛa.

7:2 A wen-d-fkeɣ yiwen n lemtel : tameṭṭut tețwarez ɣer wergaz-is s ccariɛa skud mazal-it yedder, meɛna ma yemmut wergaz-is tserreḥ-as ccariɛa ma tebɣa aț-țɛiwed zzwaǧ ;

7:3 ma tuɣ wayeḍ skud mazal argaz-is idder, yețțusemma tezna ; meɛna ma yemmut wergaz-is tserreḥ-as ccariɛa aț-țɛiwed zzwaǧ, ayagi ur yețțusemma ara d leḥṛam.

7:4 Ula d kunwi ay atmaten, temmutem ɣef wayen yeɛnan ccariɛa s lmut n Lmasiḥ, akken aț-țeṭṭfem deg win i d-yeḥyan si ger lmegtin iwakken aț-țxedmem ayen i s-iɛeǧben i Sidi Ṛebbi.

7:5 Axaṭer d ccariɛa i d-isbeggnen amek i nțeddu s lebɣi n tnefsit-nneɣ d ccehwat-nneɣ n diri ; d nețțat daɣen i d-isbeggnen amek yexdem ddnub-agi deg-nneɣ, armi i ɣ-yeṣṣaweḍ ɣer lmut.

7:6 Lameɛna tura nețțusellek si lḥekma n ccariɛa imi nețțusemma nemmut ɣef wayen yeɛnan ccariɛa nni i ɣ-yurzen, s wakka i nezmer tura a nḍuɛ Sidi Ṛebbi s lɛeqliya tajḍiṭ n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, mačči am zik s lɛeqliya-nni taqdimt mi nella seddaw n ccariɛa.

7:7 Acu ara d-nini ihi, eɛni ccariɛa deg-s ddnub ? Xaṭi ! Lameɛna lemmer ulac ccariɛa tili ur țissineɣ ara d acu i d ddnub. Axaṭer lemmer ur d-tenni ara ccariɛa : ur ṭṭamaɛ ara , tilli ur ẓriɣ ara belli ṭṭmeɛ d leḥṛam ?

7:8 Ddnub ifuṛes tagniț s lameṛ n ccariɛa ; yesnulfa-d deg-i mkul ṣṣenf n ṭṭmeɛ n diri ; axaṭer mbla ccariɛa ur d-ițban ara d acu i d ddnub.

7:9 Zik, asmi ulac ccariɛa lliɣ țɛiciɣ s nneya, mi d-iffeɣ lameṛ n ccariɛa, ddnub yuki-d deg-i, yeṣṣaweḍ-iyi ɣer lmut.

7:10 S wakka, lameṛ i glaqen ad iyi-ssiweḍ ɣer tudert, yewwi-yi ɣer lmut

7:11 axaṭer ddnub yufa abrid, s lameṛ-agi n ccariɛa dɣa ixdeɛ-iyi, yewwi-yi ɣer lmut.

7:12 Ihi, ccariɛa s yiman-is teṣfa, lumuṛat yellan deg-s ṣfan, lhan yerna d lḥeqq.

7:13 Eɛni yuɣal-iyi wayen yelhan d sebba n lmut ? Xaṭi ! D ddnub i yi-ṣṣawḍen ɣer lmut s wayen yelhan, iwakken s ccariɛa, a d-tban acḥal meqqṛet tezmert n ddnub.

7:14 Neẓra belli ccariɛa n Musa tețțunefk-ed s Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi. Ma d nekk, d amdan kan i lliɣ yerna imlek-iyi ddnub.

7:15 Ur ẓriɣ ara acu i xeddmeɣ, ayen bɣiɣ a t-xedmeɣ ur t-xeddmeɣ ara lameɛna d ayen akken keṛheɣ i xeddmeɣ.

7:16 Ma yella ayen i xeddmeɣ d ayen akken ur bɣiɣ ara a t-xedmeɣ, tḥeqqeqeɣ belli ccariɛa telha.

7:17 Tura ihi mačči d nekk i gxeddmen ayagi, meɛna d ddnub izedɣen deg-i.

7:18 Axaṭer ẓriɣ belli ayen yelhan ur yezdiɣ ara deg-i nekk yellan d amdan. ?as akken yebɣa wul-iw ad ixdem lxiṛ, nekk ur zmireɣ ara a t-xedmeɣ,

7:19 axaṭer ur xeddmeɣ ara lxiṛ i bɣiɣ, meɛna d cceṛ ur bɣiɣ ara i xeddmeɣ ;

7:20 ma yella xeddmeɣ ayen ur bɣiɣ ara ihi mačči d nekk i t-ixeddmen meɛna d ddnub-nni izedɣen deg-i.

7:21 Ihi atan wayen fehmeɣ deg yiman-iw : mkul m'ara bɣuɣ ad xedmeɣ lxiṛ ad afeɣ d cceṛ kan iwumi zemreɣ.

7:22 Deg ul-iw ḥemmleɣ ccariɛa n Sidi Ṛebbi,

7:23 meɛna țțafeɣ deg yiman-iw yiwet n ṭṭbiɛa yețnaɣen d ccariɛa n Ṛebbi i gḥemmel wul-iw ; ṭṭbiɛa-yagi terra-yi seddaw lḥekma n ddnub yellan deg-i.

7:24 A nnger-iw ! Anwa ara yi sellken si ṭṭbiɛa-agi n wemdan i yi-țțawin ɣer lmut ?

7:25 A neḥmed Sidi Ṛebbi imi s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ i nețțusellek ! S wakka ihi, s lefhama-inu, ḍuɛeɣ ccariɛa n Sidi Ṛebbi, meɛna s ṭṭbiɛa n wemdan yellan deg-i, d akli n ddnub i lliɣ.

8:1 Ihi ass n lḥisab ulac lɛiqab ɣef wid yumnen s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

8:2 Axaṭer tazmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, tin i d-ifkan tudert i wid yumnen s Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, tsellek-iyi-d si tezmert n ddnub akk-d lmut.

8:3 Ccariɛa n Musa ur tezmir ara aț-țeɣleb ddnub axaṭer ṭṭbiɛa-nneɣ tekkes-as tazmert. Ayen akken ur tezmir ara ccariɛa n Musa a t texdem, d ayen i gexdem Sidi Ṛebbi mi d-iceggeɛ Mmi-s s ṣṣifa n wemdan, i d-yusan s lǧețța am tin n yemdanen yellan meṛṛa d imednuben, iwakken ad yefk iman-is d asfel s wayes ara yeḥkem ɣef ddnub i ten-izedɣen.

8:4 S wakka ihi ara yețwakkemmel deg-nneɣ wayen i d-tenna ccariɛa n Musa, nukni yețɛicin s tezmert n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen mačči s ṭṭbiɛa-nneɣ.

8:5 Wid yețɛicin s ṭṭbiɛa n wemdan țqelliben kan ɣef wayen yeɛnan lebɣi n wemdan, ma d wid ițɛicin s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen țqelliben ɣef wayen yeɛnan lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen.

8:6 Wid ițɛicin s ṭṭbiɛa n wemdan țeddun ɣer lmut, ma d wid ițɛicin s lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen țeddun ɣer tudert d lehna.

8:7 Axaṭer wid ittabaɛen lebɣi n wemdan uɣalen d iɛdawen n Sidi Ṛebbi imi ur ḍuɛen ara ccariɛa-ines, axaṭer ṭṭbiɛa n wemdan ur tezmir ara aț-țḍuɛ ccariɛa n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:8 Wid temlek ṭṭbiɛa-nsen ulamek ara ɛeǧben i Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:9 Ma d kunwi mačči s ṭṭbiɛa n wemdan i tețțeddum lameɛna tețțeddum s lebɣi n Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen axaṭer Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi izdeɣ deg-wen. Win deg ur yezdiɣ ara Ṛṛuḥ n Lmasiḥ ur yelli ara d ayla-s.

8:10 Ma yella Ṛṛuḥ n Lmasiḥ deg-wen, ɣas akken lǧețța-nwen aț-țemmet ɣef ddemma n ddnub, ṛṛuḥ-nwen ad yidir ɣef ddemma n lḥeqq n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:11 Ma yezdeɣ deg-wen Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi i d-yesseḥyan Sidna Ɛisa si ger lmegtin, win i d-yesseḥyan Sidna Ɛisa si ger lmegtin, yezmer daɣen s Ṛṛuḥ-is izedɣen deg-wen, a d-yerr tudert i lǧețța-nwen ara yemten.

8:12 S wakka ihi ay atmaten lebɣi n ṭṭbiɛa-nneɣ ur yeṭebbiṛ ara fell-aɣ tura iwakken a nexdem lebɣi-ines.

8:13 Ma yella tɛacem s lebɣi n ṭṭbiɛa nwen atan aț-țemtem, lameɛna ma tɣelbem lebɣi n ṭṭbiɛa-nwen s Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, aț-țidirem.

8:14 Axaṭer wid ițeddun s Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi, d nutni i d arraw n Ṛebbi.

8:15 Ṛṛuḥ i wen-d-ițțunefken mačči d Ṛṛuḥ ara kkun-irren d aklan iwakken aț-țɛicem di tugdi, meɛna d Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen i kkun-irran d arraw n Ṛebbi, yis i nezmer a nessiwel i Sidi Ṛebbi : « A baba-tneɣ ! »

8:16 D Ṛṛuḥ n Sidi Ṛebbi s yiman-is i d-iḥeqqeqen deg wulawen-nneɣ belli nuɣal d arraw n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:17 Ihi ma yella d arraw-is i nella, d nukni daɣen ara iweṛten : a newṛet ɣer Sidi Ṛebbi nukni akk-d Lmasiḥ, axaṭer ma nenɛețțab yid-es, a nili daɣen yid-es di lɛaḍima-ines.

8:18 Ihi leɛtab i nesɛedday tura, ur nezmir ara a t-nmettel ɣer lɛaḍima ara d-ibeggen Sidi Ṛebbi deg-nneɣ.

8:19 Taxelqit meṛṛa tḥar melmi ara d-banen warraw n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:20 Axaṭer taxelqit teɣli seddaw leḥkum n lbaṭel, mačči s lebɣi-ines, lameɛna s lebɣi n win i ț-yesseɣlin.

8:21 Meɛna taxelqit-agi tessaram aț-țețwasellek si twaɣit i ț-isfesden, iwakken aț-țekki di lɛaḍima n tlelli n warraw n Sidi Ṛebbi.

8:22 Neẓra tura belli taxelqit meṛṛa tenɛețțab, tețnazaɛ am tmeṭṭut yeṭṭfen addud.

8:23 Mačči ț-țaxelqit kan, meɛna ula d nukni yesɛan Ṛṛuḥ iqedsen, yellan d amur amezwaru n tikciwin i ɣ-d-iwɛed Sidi Ṛebbi, nețnazaɛ deg iman-nneɣ, nețṛaǧu a nban s tideț d arraw n Ṛebbi i nella m'ara icafeɛ deg-nneɣ s lekmal.

8:24 Axaṭer leslak nneɣ d ayen i nessaram ; ma yella nesɛa ayen i nessaram, ayagi mačči d asirem : anwa ara yessirmen ayen yesɛa ?

8:25 Meɛna ma nessaram ayen ur nesɛi, nețṛaǧu-t s ṣṣbeṛ.

13:1 Mkul amdan, ilaq ad iqadeṛ imdebbṛen i gḥekkmen tamurt i deg yețɛici, axaṭer d Sidi Ṛebbi i d-iǧǧan lḥekma di ddunit, d nețța daɣen i gebɣan aț-țuɣal lḥekma-agi ɣer yifassen n yemdanen.

13:2 S wakka ihi, win ara ixalfen lḥekma i gesbedd Sidi Ṛebbi, d lameṛ n Sidi Ṛebbi i gɛuṣa, d lɛiqab kan ara d-isseɣli ɣef yiman-is.

15:1 Nukni s wid iǧehden di liman, ilaq a neṣbeṛ i wid ur neǧhid ara, ur nețnadi ara ɣef wayen kan i ɣ-iɛeǧben i nukni.

16:1 A kkun-weṣṣiɣ ɣef weltma tneɣ Fibya, nețțat illan ț-țaqeddact n Sidi Ṛebbi di tejmaɛt n temdint n Senkriya.

16:2 Sṭerḥbet yis s leqdeṛ, s yisem n Sidna Ɛisa, akken i glaq aț-țesṭeṛḥebem s watmaten. Ɛiwnet-eț deg wayen akk i teḥwaǧ, axaṭer aṭas n watmaten i tɛawen ; ula d nekk tɛawen-iyi.

16:3 ?ṣiwḍet sslam i gma-tneɣ Akilas akk-d Briska, wid akken iqeddcen yid-i ɣef Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ,

16:4 wid akken i gqeblen ad sebblen tudert-nsen fell-i ; mačči d nekkini kan i ten icekkṛen meɛna ula ț-țijmuyaɛ n imasiḥiyen n mkul amkan cekkṛent ten.

16:5 ?sellimet daɣen ɣef wid yețnejmaɛen deg wexxam-nsen, țsellimet ɣef gma-tneɣ Ibantus aḥbib eɛzizen fell-i, i gellan d amenzu yumnen s Lmasiḥ di tmurt n Asya.

16:6 ?sellimet ɣef Meryem i genneɛtaben aṭas fell-awen.

16:7 ?sellimet ɣef Andrunikus akk-d Yunya i yi-ițțilin yerna llan yid-i di lḥebs, wid eɛzizen ger ṛṛusul, nutni yumnen s Sidna Ɛisa uqbel ad amneɣ nekkini.

16:8 ?sellimet ɣef Ambliyatus gma tneɣ, win eɛzizen fell-i di Lmasiḥ.

16:9 ?sellimet ɣef gma-tneɣ Urban, win iqeddcen yid-nneɣ ɣef Lmasiḥ ; țsellimet daɣen ɣef Stakyis aḥbib-iw.

16:10 ?sellimet ɣef Abalis, win i nwala amek i geṭṭef di Lmasiḥ. ?sellimet ɣef wat wexxam n gma tneɣ Aristubul.

16:11 ?sellimet ɣef Hiṛudiɛun win i yi-ițțilin, țsellimet ɣef wat wexxam n Narsisus, wid yumnen s Lmasiḥ.

16:12 ?sellimet ɣef tyessetmatin Trifina akk-d Trifusa i genneɛtaben ɣef lecɣal n Ṛebbi, țsellimet ɣef Bersidya eɛzizen, tin yenneɛtaben aṭas ɣef Lecɣal n Lmasiḥ.

16:13 ?sellimet ɣef Rufus win yextaṛ Sidi Ṛebbi akk-d yemma-s i yi iḥesben am mmi-s.

16:14 ?sellimet ɣef Asinkritus, Flegun, Hermis, Batrubas, Hermas akk-d watmaten nniḍen yellan yid-sen.

16:15 ?sellimet ɣef Filulugus akk-d Julya, Niryus akk-d weltma-s, Ulimbas akk-d wid yumnen s Lmasiḥ yellan yid-sen.

16:16 Msalamet wway-gar-awen s sslam yeṣfan. Tijmuyaɛ akk n Lmasiḥ țselliment-ed fell-awen.

16:17 A kkun-weṣṣiɣ ay atmaten : ḥadret iman-nwen ɣef wid i d-ițțawin beṭṭu yerna skarayen ccwal, bɣan a wen-sɛeṛqen ayen i tlemdem, sbeɛdet iman-nwen fell-asen.

16:18 Axaṭer imdanen am wigi ur qeddcen ara ɣef Lmasiḥ Ssid-nneɣ meɛna ɣef yiɛebbaḍ-nsen kan i qeddcen ; s ukellex akk-d imeslayen ẓiden, țseḥḥiren ulawen n wid yesɛan neyya.

16:19 Ma d kunwi, medden meṛṛa ẓran tḍuɛem Sidi Ṛebbi, d ayen i yi-sfeṛḥen. ?menniɣ aț-țxedmem ayen yelhan s ṣṣwab, ur s-țaǧǧat ara abrid i cceṛ.

16:20 Sidi Ṛebbi yellan d Bab n lehna, ur yețɛeṭṭil ara ad yemḥeq Cciṭan yerna a t-yerr seddaw iḍaṛṛen nwen. Ṛṛeḥma n Ɛisa Ssid-nneɣ aț-țili yid-wen !

16:21 Gma-tneɣ Timuti win iqeddcen yid-i akk-d Lusyus, Yazun, Susibatrus i yi-ițțilin țsellimen-d fell-awen.

16:22 Nekk Tertius i guran tabṛaț-agi țsellimeɣ daɣen fell-awen s yisem n Ɛisa Lmasiḥ.

16:23 Yețsellim-ed fell-awen Gayus win i ɣer țțiliɣ anda țnejmaɛen daɣen watmaten meṛṛa. Erastus, win yeṭṭfen asenduq n yedrimen n temdint akk-d gma-tneɣ Kartus țsellimen-d fell-awen.

16:24 Ṛṛeḥma n Ssid-nneɣ Ɛisa Lmasiḥ aț-țili yid-wen meṛṛa ! Amin !

16:25 Yețțubarek Sidi Ṛebbi yesɛan tazmert a ɣ-yesseǧhed di liman, akken i t-țbecciṛeɣ di lexbaṛ n lxiṛ i d yewwi Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, iwakken a d-isban lbaḍna n Sidi Ṛebbi yețwaffren seg wasmi tebda ddunit,

16:26 meɛna sḍehṛent-ț-id lenbiya s wayen uran akken i sen-t-id-yumeṛ Sidi Ṛebbi, Illu n dayem. Tura leǧnas meṛṛa slan i lexbaṛ-agi n lxiṛ iwakken a ten-id-yawi ɣer ṭṭaɛa-ines s liman.

16:27 Lɛaḍima i Sidi Ṛebbi awḥid, Bab n tmusni d leɛqel, s Sidna Ɛisa Lmasiḥ si lǧil ɣer lǧil ! Amin !
