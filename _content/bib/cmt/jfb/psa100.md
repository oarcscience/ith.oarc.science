### [Psalms Chapter 100](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#001)

[Psalms 100:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 100:1</span></span>

As closing this series (see on [Psa
93:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#001)), this Psalm is a general call on
all the earth to render exalted praise to God, the creator, preserver,
and benefactor of men. ([Psa 100:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#001))

With thankful praise, unite service as the subjects of a king ([Psa
2:11-12](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#011)).

[Psalms 100:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 100:3</span></span>

To the obligations of a creature and subject is added that of a
beneficiary ([Psa 95:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa095.htm#007)).

[Psalms 100:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 100:4</span></span>

Join joyfully in His public worship. The terms are, of course,
figurative (compare [Psa 84:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#002); [Psa
92:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#013); [Isa
66:23](../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#023)).

Enter--or, "Come with solemnity" ([Psa
95:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa095.htm#006)).

[Psalms 100:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa100.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 100:5</span></span>

The reason: God's eternal mercy and truth ([Psa
25:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#008); [Psa
89:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#007)).
