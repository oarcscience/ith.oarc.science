### [Psalms Chapter 134](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001)

[Psalms 134:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 134:2</span></span>

1-2. The pilgrim bands arriving at the sanctuary call on the priests,
who ([Psa 134:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001))

Lift up your hands--(Compare [Psa
28:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa028.htm#002)).

[Psalms 134:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 134:3</span></span>
