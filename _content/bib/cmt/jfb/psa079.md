### [Psalms Chapter 79](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#001)

[Psalms 79:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 79:1</span></span>

This Psalm, like the seventy-fourth, probably depicts the desolations of
the Chaldeans ([Jer 52:12-24](../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#012)). It
comprises the usual complaint, prayer, and promised thanks for relief.
([Psa 79:1-13](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#001))

(Compare [Psa 74:2-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa074.htm#002)).

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 79:2</span></span>

16:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#004)).

[Psalms 79:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 79:5</span></span>

How long-- ([Psa 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa013.htm#001)).

be angry-- ([Psa 74:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/psa074.htm#001)).

jealousy burn-- ([Deu 29:20](../../../bib/kjv/deu029.htm#020)).

[Psalms 79:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 79:6</span></span>

(Compare [Jer 10:25](../../../bib/kjv/jer010.htm#025)). Though we
deserve much, do not the heathen deserve more for their violence to us
([Jer 51:3-5](../../../bib/kjv/jer051.htm#003); [Zac
1:14](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#014))? The singular denotes the chief
power, and the use of the plural indicates the combined confederates.

called upon--or, "by"

thy name--proclaimed Thy attributes and professed allegiance ([Isa
12:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa012.htm#004); [Act
2:21](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#021)).

[Psalms 79:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 79:8</span></span>

former iniquities--literally, "iniquities of former times."

prevent us--literally, "meet us," as in [Psa
21:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#003).

[Psalms 79:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 79:9</span></span>

for . . . glory of thy name \[and for\] thy name's sake--both mean for
illustrating Thy attributes, faithfulness, power, &c.

purge . . . sins--literally, "provide atonement for us." Deliverance
from sin and suffering, for their good and God's glory, often
distinguish the prayers of Old Testament saints (compare [Eph
1:7](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#007)).

[Psalms 79:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 79:10</span></span>

This ground of pleading often used ([Exo
32:12](../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#012); [Num
14:13-16](../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#013)).

blood . . . shed-- ([Psa 79:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#003)).

[Psalms 79:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 79:11</span></span>

prisoner--the whole captive people.

power--literally, "arm" ([Psa 10:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#015)).

[Psalms 79:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 79:12</span></span>

into their bosom--The lap or folds of the dress is used by Eastern
people for receiving articles. The figure denotes retaliation (compare
[Isa 65:6-7](../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#006)). They reproached God as
well as His people.

[Psalms 79:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa079.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 79:13</span></span>

sheep . . . pasture--(Compare [Psa
74:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa074.htm#001); [Psa
78:70](../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#070)).
