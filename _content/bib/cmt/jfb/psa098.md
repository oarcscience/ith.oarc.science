### [Psalms Chapter 98](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#001)

[Psalms 98:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 98:1</span></span>

In view of the wonders of grace and righteousness displayed in God's
salvation, the whole creation is invited to unite in praise. ([Psa
98:1-9](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#001))

gotten . . . victory--literally, "made salvation," enabled Him to save
His people.

right hand, and . . . arm--denote power.

holy arm--or, "arm of holiness," the power of His united moral
perfections ([Psa 22:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa022.htm#003); [Psa
32:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa032.htm#011)).

[Psalms 98:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 98:2</span></span>

salvation--the result of His righteousness ([Psa
7:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#017); [Psa
31:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#001)), and both are publicly
displayed.

[Psalms 98:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 98:3</span></span>

The union of mercy and truth ([Psa
57:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#003); [Psa
85:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa085.htm#010)) secure the blessings of the
promise ([Gen 12:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003); [Gen
18:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen018.htm#018)) to all the world ([Isa
52:10](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#010)).

[Psalms 98:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa098.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 98:4</span></span>

make a loud noise--or, "burst forth" ([Isa
14:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#007); [Isa
44:23](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#023)).

before . . . King--hail Him as your sovereign; and while, with every aid
to demonstrate zeal and joy, intelligent creatures are invited to
praise, as in [Psa 96:11-13](../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#011), inanimate
nature is also summoned to honor Him who triumphs and rules in
righteousness and equity.
