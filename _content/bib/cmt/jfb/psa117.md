### [Psalms Chapter 117](../../../bib/kjv/psa117.htm#001)

[Psalms 117:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa117.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 117:2</span></span>

This may be regarded as a doxology, suitable to be appended to any Psalm
of similar character, and prophetical of the prevalence of God's grace
in the world, in which aspect Paul quotes it ([Rom
15:11](../../../bib/kjv/rom015.htm#011); compare [Psa
47:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa047.htm#002); [Psa
66:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa066.htm#008)). ([Psa
117:1-2](../../../bib/kjv/psa117.htm#001))

is great toward us--literally, "prevailed over" or "protected us."
