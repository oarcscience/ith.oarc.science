### [Psalms Chapter 112](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001)

[Psalms 112:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 112:1</span></span>

This Psalm may be regarded as an exposition of [Psa
111:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#010), presenting the happiness of
those who fear and obey God, and contrasting the fate of the ungodly.
([Psa 112:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001))

True fear produces obedience and this happiness.

[Psalms 112:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 112:2</span></span>

Temporal blessings follow the service of God, exceptions occurring only
as they are seen by God to be inconsistent with those spiritual
blessings which are better.

[Psalms 112:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 112:4</span></span>

light--figurative for relief ([Psa
27:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#001); [Psa
97:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#011)).

the upright--are like God ([Luk 6:36](../../../bib/kjv/luk006.htm#036);
[Psa 111:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#004)).

[Psalms 112:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 112:5</span></span>

Generosity, sound judgment in business, and confidence in God, form a
character which preserves from fear of evil and ensures success against
enemies. While a man thus truly pious is liberal, he increases in
substance.

[Psalms 112:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 112:6</span></span>

not be moved--(compare [Psa 13:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa013.htm#004); [Psa
15:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#005)).

[Psalms 112:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 112:8</span></span>

heart is established--or, firm in right principles.

see his desire-- ([Psa 50:23](../../../bib/kjv/psa050.htm#023); [Psa
54:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#007)).

[Psalms 112:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">psa 112:10</span></span>

Disappointed in their malevolent wishes by the prosperity of the pious,
the wicked are punished by the working of their evil passions, and come
to naught.
