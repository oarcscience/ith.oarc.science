### [Psalms Chapter 67](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001)

[Psalms 67:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 67:1</span></span>

A prayer that, by God's blessing on His people, His salvation and praise
may be extended over the earth. ([Psa
67:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001))

cause his face to shine--show us favor ([Num
6:24-25](../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#024); [Psa
31:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#016)).

[Psalms 67:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 67:2</span></span>

thy way--of gracious dealing ([Isa
55:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa055.htm#008)), as explained by--

saving health--or literally, "salvation."

[Psalms 67:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 67:3</span></span>

Thanks will be rendered for the blessings of His wise and holy
government (compare [Isa 2:3-4](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#003); [Isa
11:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#004)).

[Psalms 67:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 67:6</span></span>

The blessings of a fruitful harvest are mentioned as types of greater
and spiritual blessings, under which all nations shall fear and love
God.
