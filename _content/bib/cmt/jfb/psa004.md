### [Psalms Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001)

[Psalms 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 4:1</span></span>

On Neginoth, that is, stringed instruments, as the kind of musical
accompaniment. On other parts of title, see Introduction., The
historical occasion was probably the same as that of the foregoing \[see
on [Psa 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#001)\]. The writer, praying for
further relief, admonishes his enemies of the vanity of attacking God's
servant, exhorts them to repentance, and avows his confidence and peace
in God's favor. ([Psa 4:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#001))

Hear--as in [Psa 3:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#004).

God of my righteousness--or, "my righteous God, as my holy hill" ([Psa
2:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#006)), who will act towards me on
righteous principles.

thou hast enlarged--expresses relief afforded in opposition to
"distress," which is expressed by a word denoting straits or pressure.
Past favor is a ground of hope for the future.

[Psalms 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 4:2</span></span>

sons of men--men of note or prominence (compare [Ch2
21:9](../../../bib/kjv/ch2021.htm#009)).

turn my glory--or, "royal dignity."

into shame--or, "reproach."

vanity--a foolish and hopeless enterprise ([Psa
2:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#001)).

leasing--a lie.

[Psalms 4:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 4:3</span></span>

godly--an object as well as subject of divine favor (compare [Psa
105:14-15](../../../bib/kjv/psa105.htm#014)).

[Psalms 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 4:4</span></span>

Stand in awe-- ([Eph 4:26](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#026)), from
Septuagint, "be angry." Both clauses are qualified by "not."

[Psalms 4:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 4:5</span></span>

Not only repent, but manifest penitence by sacrifices or righteousness
or righteous sacrifices, &c.

[Psalms 4:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 4:6</span></span>

Contrast true with vain confidence.

light of thy countenance upon us--figure for favor ([Num
6:26](../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#026); [Psa
44:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#003); [Psa
81:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#016)).

[Psalms 4:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 4:7</span></span>

corn and wine--literally, "new corn and wine."

increased--an abundant harvest giving great joy ([Isa
9:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#003)).

[Psalms 4:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa004.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 4:8</span></span>

both lay me down, &c.--or, will lie down at once, and sleep in sure
confidence and quiet repose ([Psa
3:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#005)).
