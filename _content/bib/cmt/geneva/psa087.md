### [Psalms Chapter 87](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001)

[Psalms 87:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 87:1</span></span>

"A Psalm \[or\] Song for the sons of Korah." His (a) foundation \[is\]
in the holy mountains.

\(a\) God chose that place among the hills to establish Jerusalem and
his temple.

[Psalms 87:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 87:3</span></span>

\(b\) Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah.

\(b\) Though your glorious estate does not yet appear, wait with
patience and God will accomplish his promise.

[Psalms 87:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 87:4</span></span>

I will make mention of (c) Rahab and Babylon to them that know me:
behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; (d) this \[man\] was born
there.

\(c\) That is, Egypt and these other countries will come to the
knowledge of God.

\(d\) It will be said of him who is regenerate and come to the Church,
that he is as one who was born in the Church.

[Psalms 87:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 87:5</span></span>

And of Zion it shall be said, (e) This and that man was born in her: and
the highest himself shall establish her.

\(e\) Out of all quarters they will come to the Church, and be counted
as citizens.

[Psalms 87:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">psa 87:6</span></span>

The LORD shall count, when he (f) writeth up the people, \[that\] this
\[man\] was born there. Selah.

\(f\) When he calls them by his word into the Church, whom he had
elected and written in the book.

[Psalms 87:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 87:7</span></span>

As well the singers as the players on instruments \[shall be there\]:
all my (g) springs \[are\] in thee.

\(g\) The prophet sets his whole affections and comfort in the church.
