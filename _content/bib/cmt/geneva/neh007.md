*The Geneva Bible Translation Notes*, \[1599\], at Intangible Textual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Nehemiah Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#001)

[Nehemiah 7:3](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">neh 7:3</span></span>

And I said unto them, Let not the gates of Jerusalem be opened until the
sun be hot; and while (a) they stand by, let them shut the doors, and
bar \[them\]: and appoint watches of the inhabitants of Jerusalem, every
one in his watch, and every one \[to be\] over against his house.

\(a\) That is, they who are mentioned, ([Neh
7:2](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#002)).

[Nehemiah 7:6](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">neh 7:6</span></span>

These \[are\] the (b) children of the province, that went up out of the
captivity, of those that had been carried away, whom Nebuchadnezzar the
king of Babylon had carried away, and came again to Jerusalem and to
Judah, every one unto his city;

\(b\) That is, the inhabitants of Judah.

[Nehemiah 7:7](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">neh 7:7</span></span>

Who came with Zerubbabel, Jeshua, Nehemiah, (c) Azariah, Raamiah,
Nahamani, Mordecai, Bilshan, Mispereth, Bigvai, Nehum, Baanah. The
number, \[I say\], of the men of the people of Israel \[was this\];

\(c\) Azariah in Ezra is called Seraiah, and Raamiah Reelaiah ([Ezr
2:2](../../../bib/kjv/ezr002.htm#002)).

[Nehemiah 7:25](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">neh 7:25</span></span>

The (d) children of Gibeon, ninety and five.

\(d\) That is, the inhabitants of Gibeon.

[Nehemiah 7:33](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">neh 7:33</span></span>

The men (e) of the other Nebo, fifty and two.

\(e\) For there were two cities with this name.

[Nehemiah 7:46](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#046)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_046">neh 7:46</span></span>

The (f) Nethinims: the children of Ziha, the children of Hashupha, the
children of Tabbaoth,

\(f\) Read ([Ezr 2:43](../../../bib/kjv/ezr002.htm#043)).

[Nehemiah 7:65](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#065)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_065">neh 7:65</span></span>

And (g) the Tirshatha said unto them, that they should not eat of the
most holy things, till there stood \[up\] a priest with Urim and
Thummim.

\(g\) Meaning, Nehemiah: for Tirshatha in the Chaldee tongue means a
butler.

[Nehemiah 7:70](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#070)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_070">neh 7:70</span></span>

And some of the chief of the fathers gave unto the work. The Tirshatha
gave to the treasure a thousand (h) drams of gold, fifty basons, five
hundred and thirty priests' garments.

\(h\) Read ([Ezr 2:69](../../../bib/kjv/ezr002.htm#069)).

[Nehemiah 7:73](../../../bib/kjv/neh007.htm#073)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_073">neh 7:73</span></span>

So the priests, and the Levites, and the porters, and the singers, and
\[some\] of the people, and the Nethinims, and all Israel, dwelt in
their cities; and when the (i) seventh month came, the children of
Israel \[were\] in their cities.

\(i\) Which contained part of September and part of October.
