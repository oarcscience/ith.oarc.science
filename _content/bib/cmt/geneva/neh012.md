### [Nehemiah Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#001)

[Nehemiah 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">neh 12:1</span></span>

Now these \[are\] the priests and the Levites that (a) went up with
Zerubbabel the son of Shealtiel, and Jeshua: Seraiah, Jeremiah, Ezra,

\(a\) From Babylon to Jerusalem.

[Nehemiah 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">neh 12:7</span></span>

Sallu, Amok, Hilkiah, Jedaiah. These \[were\] the (b) chief of the
priests and of their brethren in the days of Jeshua.

\(b\) Next in dignity to the high priests and who were of the stock of
Aaron.

[Nehemiah 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">neh 12:8</span></span>

Moreover the Levites: Jeshua, Binnui, Kadmiel, Sherebiah, Judah, \[and\]
Mattaniah, (c) \[which was\] over the thanksgiving, he and his brethren.

\(c\) Had charge of them who sang the psalms.

[Nehemiah 12:9](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">neh 12:9</span></span>

Also Bakbukiah and Unni, their brethren, \[were\] over against them in
the (d) watches.

\(d\) They kept the wards and watches according to their turns, ([Ch2
23:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch2023.htm#006)).

[Nehemiah 12:12](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">neh 12:12</span></span>

And in the days of Joiakim were priests, the chief of the fathers: of
(e) Seraiah, Meraiah; of Jeremiah, Hananiah;

\(e\) That is, next to Seraiah or rather of the order, who was called
after the name of Seraiah.

[Nehemiah 12:17](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">neh 12:17</span></span>

Of (f) Abijah, Zichri; of Miniamin, of Moadiah, Piltai;

\(f\) Of which was Zachariah, John the Baptist's father.

[Nehemiah 12:24](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">neh 12:24</span></span>

And the chief of the Levites: Hashabiah, Sherebiah, and Jeshua the son
of Kadmiel, with their brethren over against them, to praise \[and\] to
give thanks, according to the commandment of David the man of God, ward
over (g) against ward.

\(g\) That is, one after another and every one in his course.

[Nehemiah 12:28](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">neh 12:28</span></span>

And the sons of the singers gathered themselves together, both out of
the plain country round about Jerusalem, and from the villages of (h)
Netophathi;

\(h\) Who were a certain family and had their possessions in the fields,
([Ch1 2:54](../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#054)).

[Nehemiah 12:31](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">neh 12:31</span></span>

Then (i) I brought up the princes of Judah upon the wall, and appointed
two great \[companies of them that gave\] thanks, \[whereof one\] went
on the right hand upon the wall toward the dung gate:

\(i\) Meaning, Nehemiah.

[Nehemiah 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#036)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_036">neh 12:36</span></span>

And (k) his brethren, Shemaiah, and Azarael, Milalai, Gilalai, Maai,
Nethaneel, and Judah, Hanani, with the musical instruments of David the
man of God, and Ezra the scribe before them.

\(k\) That is, the brass throne of Zaccur.

[Nehemiah 12:37](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#037)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_037">neh 12:37</span></span>

And at the fountain gate, which was over against them, they went up by
the (l) stairs of the city of David, at the going up of the wall, above
the house of David, even unto the water gate eastward.

\(l\) Which was going up to the mount Zion, which is called the city of
David.

[Nehemiah 12:44](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#044)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_044">neh 12:44</span></span>

And at that time were some appointed (m) over the chambers for the
treasures, for the offerings, for the firstfruits, and for the tithes,
to gather into them out of the fields of the cities the portions of the
law for the priests and Levites: for Judah rejoiced for the priests and
for the Levites that waited.

\(m\) Which were chambers appointed by Hezekiah to put in the tither,
and such things, ([Ch2 31:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch2031.htm#011)) and now
were repaired again for the same use.

[Nehemiah 12:47](../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#047)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_047">neh 12:47</span></span>

And all Israel in the days of Zerubbabel, and in the days of Nehemiah,
gave the portions of the singers and the porters, every day his portion:
and they sanctified \[holy things\] unto the Levites; and the Levites
(n) sanctified \[them\] unto the children of Aaron.

\(n\) That is, the tenth part of the tithes.
