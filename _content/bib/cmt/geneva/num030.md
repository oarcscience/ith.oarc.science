### [Numbers Chapter 30](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#001)

[Numbers 30:1](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">num 30:1</span></span>

And Moses spake unto the heads of the tribes (a) concerning the children
of Israel, saying, This \[is\] the thing which the LORD hath commanded.

\(a\) Because they might declare them to the Israelites.

[Numbers 30:4](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">num 30:4</span></span>

And her father hear her vow, and her bond wherewith she hath bound her
soul, and her father shall hold his (b) peace at her: then all her vows
shall stand, and every bond wherewith she hath bound her soul shall
stand.

\(b\) For in so doing he approves her.

[Numbers 30:5](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">num 30:5</span></span>

But if her (c) father disallow her in the day that he heareth; not any
of her vows, or of her bonds wherewith she hath bound her soul, shall
stand: and the LORD shall forgive her, because her father disallowed
her.

\(c\) By not approving or consenting to her vow.

[Numbers 30:6](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">num 30:6</span></span>

And if she had at all an husband, when she vowed, or (d) uttered ought
out of her lips, wherewith she bound her soul;

\(d\) Either by oath, or solemn promise.

[Numbers 30:8](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">num 30:8</span></span>

But if her husband disallowed her on the day that he heard \[it\]; then
he shall make her vow which she vowed, and that which she uttered with
her lips, wherewith she bound her (e) soul, of none effect: and the LORD
shall forgive her.

\(e\) For she is in subjection to her husband, and can perform nothing
without his consent.

[Numbers 30:9](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">num 30:9</span></span>

But every vow of a widow, and of her that is divorced, wherewith they
have bound their souls, shall stand (f) against her.

\(f\) For they are not under the authority of the man.

[Numbers 30:10](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">num 30:10</span></span>

And if she vowed in her husband's (h) house, or bound her soul by a bond
with an oath;

\(h\) Her husband being alive.

[Numbers 30:13](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">num 30:13</span></span>

Every vow, and every binding oath to (h) afflict the soul, her husband
may establish it, or her husband may make it void.

\(h\) To mortify herself by abstinence or other bodily exercise.

[Numbers 30:14](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">num 30:14</span></span>

But if her husband altogether hold his peace at her from (i) day to day;
then he establisheth all her vows, or all her bonds, which \[are\] upon
her: he confirmeth them, because he held his peace at her in the day
that he heard \[them\].

\(i\) And warn her not the same day that he hears it, as in ([Num
30:8](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#008)).

[Numbers 30:15](../../../bib/kjv/num030.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">num 30:15</span></span>

But if he shall any ways (k) make them void after that he hath heard
\[them\]; then he shall bear her iniquity.

\(k\) Not the same day he heard them, but some day after, the sin will
be imputed to him and not to her.
