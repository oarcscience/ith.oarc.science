### [Numbers Chapter 32](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#001)

[Numbers 32:1](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">num 32:1</span></span>

Now the children of (a) Reuben and the children of Gad had a very great
multitude of cattle: and when they saw the land of Jazer, and the land
of (b) Gilead, that, behold, the place \[was\] a place for cattle;

\(a\) Reuben came from Leah, and Gad from Zilpah her handmaid.

\(b\) Which was named for the heap of stones that Jacob made as a sign
of the covenant between him and Laban in ([Gen
31:47](../../../bib/kjv/gen031.htm#047)).

[Numbers 32:13](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">num 32:13</span></span>

And the LORD'S anger was kindled against Israel, and he made them wander
in the wilderness forty years, until all the generation, that had done
(c) evil in the sight of the LORD, was consumed.

\(c\) Because they complained and would not believe their report, which
told the truth concerning the land.

[Numbers 32:15](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">num 32:15</span></span>

For if ye turn away from after him, he will yet again leave them in the
wilderness; and (d) ye shall destroy all this people.

\(d\) By your occasion.

[Numbers 32:17](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">num 32:17</span></span>

But we ourselves will go ready armed before the children of Israel,
until we have brought them unto their (e) place: and our little ones
shall dwell in the fenced cities because of the inhabitants of the land.

\(e\) In the land of Canaan.

[Numbers 32:20](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">num 32:20</span></span>

And Moses said unto them, If ye will do this thing, if ye will go (f)
armed before the LORD to war,

\(f\) Before the Ark of the Lord.

[Numbers 32:21](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">num 32:21</span></span>

And will go all of you armed over Jordan before the LORD, until he hath
driven out his (g) enemies from before him,

\(g\) That is, the inhabitants of the land.

[Numbers 32:22](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">num 32:22</span></span>

And the land be subdued before the LORD: then afterward ye shall return,
and be guiltless before the LORD, and before Israel; and this land shall
be your possession (h) before the LORD.

\(h\) The Lord will grant you this land which you request, ([Jos
1:15](../../../bib/kjv/jos001.htm#015)).

[Numbers 32:23](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">num 32:23</span></span>

But if ye will not do so, behold, ye have sinned against the LORD: and
be sure your sin (i) will find you out.

\(i\) You shall assuredly be punished for your sin.

[Numbers 32:28](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">num 32:28</span></span>

So concerning them Moses (k) commanded Eleazar the priest, and Joshua
the son of Nun, and the chief fathers of the tribes of the children of
Israel:

\(k\) Moses gave charge that his promise made to the Reubenites, and
other, would be performed after his death so that they would not break
theirs.

[Numbers 32:31](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">num 32:31</span></span>

And the children of Gad and the children of Reuben answered, saying, As
the (l) LORD hath said unto thy servants, so will we do.

\(l\) This is attributed to the Lord, which his messenger speaks.

[Numbers 32:33](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">num 32:33</span></span>

And Moses gave unto them, \[even\] to the children of Gad, and to the
children of Reuben, and unto half the tribe of Manasseh the son of
Joseph, the kingdom of Sihon king of the (m) Amorites, and the kingdom
of Og king of Bashan, the land, with the cities thereof in the coasts,
\[even\] the cities of the country round about.

\(m\) The Amorites dwelled on both sides of Jordan: but here he makes
mention of them that dwelt on this side: ([Jos
10:12](../../../bib/kjv/jos010.htm#012)) he speaks of them that
inhabited beyond Jordan.

[Numbers 32:41](../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#041)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_041">num 32:41</span></span>

And Jair the son of Manasseh went and took the small towns thereof, and
called them (n) Havothjair.

\(n\) That is the villages of Jair.
