### [Ezekiel Chapter 34](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 34:2](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">eze 34:2</span></span>

The shepherds - The rulers of the people kings, magistrates, and
princes; as also priests, and prophets. Of Israel - The two tribes, and
the few out of the ten that adhere to the house of David. That feed -
Contrive their own ease, advantage, and honour.

[Ezekiel 34:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 34:3</span></span>

Ye kill - You contrive methods, to take first the life, and next the
estate of the well - fed, the rich and wealthy. But - You take care to
lead, protect, provide for, and watch over them.

[Ezekiel 34:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 34:4</span></span>

The diseased - The weak and languishing. Bound up - Oppressors in the
state, or church, broke many then, but these shepherds bound them not
up.

[Ezekiel 34:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 34:5</span></span>

No shepherd - No vigilant, faithful shepherd. Became meat - Were made a
prey of, and devoured by all their neighbours.

[Ezekiel 34:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">eze 34:12</span></span>

In the cloudy and dark day - In the time of general distress.

[Ezekiel 34:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">eze 34:16</span></span>

The fat - The powerful and rich. I will feed - I will judge and punish
them.

[Ezekiel 34:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">eze 34:17</span></span>

I judge - Between men and men, between the smaller and weaker, and the
greater and stronger, as their different state requires I will do. The
rams - Rulers, who also shalt be dealt with according to their
behaviour.

[Ezekiel 34:18](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">eze 34:18</span></span>

But ye must tread down - You great ones, eat the fat, and sweet; and
what you cannot eat, you waste and spoil. The deep waters - Which are
sufficient for all.

[Ezekiel 34:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">eze 34:20</span></span>

I will judge - I will vindicate the poor. The fat cattle - The rich. The
lean - The poor.

[Ezekiel 34:23](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">eze 34:23</span></span>

One shepherd - Christ, the great good, chief, only shepherd, that laid
down his life for his sheep. My servant David - The seed of David, the
beloved one, who was typified by David, and is in other places called by
his name, as [Jer 30:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#009); [Eze
37:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze037.htm#024); [Isa
37:35](../../../bib/kjv/isa037.htm#035); [Hos
3:5](../../../bib/kjv/hos003.htm#005). He shall feed - Do all the office
of a good and faithful shepherd, and that for ever.

[Ezekiel 34:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">eze 34:24</span></span>

My servant - Christ was in this great work his fathers servant, [Isa
42:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001).

[Ezekiel 34:25](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">eze 34:25</span></span>

A covenant - A covenant of promises, which contain, and shall bring
peace, that is all good.

[Ezekiel 34:26](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">eze 34:26</span></span>

Them - My returned captives, The places - All the country. My hill -
Jerusalem.

[Ezekiel 34:29](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">eze 34:29</span></span>

A plant - The Messiah. The shame - The reproach.

[Ezekiel 34:30](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">eze 34:30</span></span>

Their God - By covenant, from their forefathers. Am with them - Present
with them, and reconciled to them.
