Psalms Chapter 142

Psalms 142:3

psa 142:3

Knowest - So as to direct me to it. My path - What paths I should chuse whereby I might escape.

Psalms 142:4

psa 142:4

Right - hand - The place where the patron or assistant used to stand.

Psalms 142:5

psa 142:5

Portion - Even in this life.

Psalms 142:7

psa 142:7

Prison - Set me at liberty. Compass - Shall flock to me from all parts, to rejoice and bless God with me and for me.
