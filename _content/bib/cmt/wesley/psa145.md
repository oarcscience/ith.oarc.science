### [Psalms Chapter 145](../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#001)

All - All that look up to him for help.

[Psalms 145:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">psa 145:15</span></span>

All - Of all living creatures. Wait - Expect their supplies wholly from
thy bounty. Expectation is here figuratively ascribed to brute
creatures.

[Psalms 145:18](../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">psa 145:18</span></span>

Nigh - To answer their prayers. In truth - With an upright heart.
