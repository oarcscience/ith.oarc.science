### [Zechariah Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#001)

[Zechariah 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">zac 8:2</span></span>

Jealousy - With great care that she should not, as formerly, sin against
my love, and her own welfare, and with a great desire to do her good,
and to rescue her from her enemies. Fury - With heat of anger against
her enemies.

[Zechariah 8:3](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">zac 8:3</span></span>

Shall be called a city of truth - Her citizens shall love the truth and
speak it, shall worship me in truth of heart, as well as in the true
manner prescribed to them.

[Zechariah 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zac 8:4</span></span>

Old men - Formerly war, or famine or pestilence, and wasting diseases,
cut off men and women before they came to old age.

[Zechariah 8:6](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">zac 8:6</span></span>

Marvellous - These things may seem strange to this people.

[Zechariah 8:7](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">zac 8:7</span></span>

The east - country - Persia and Media, which lay east from Jerusalem,
and were now masters of Babylon.

[Zechariah 8:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">zac 8:8</span></span>

In truth and in righteousness - This signifies both God's part, and
their part; on God's part truth, on their's righteousness, obedience to
God's righteous law.

[Zechariah 8:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zac 8:9</span></span>

The prophets - Haggai and Zechariah.

[Zechariah 8:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">zac 8:10</span></span>

Before these days - For eighteen years together. No hire - No profit by
the labour of man or beast, no sowing or planting. Affliction -
Distress, and want, through the barrenness, which attended all their
labour.

[Zechariah 8:11](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zac 8:11</span></span>

I will not be - That is, I will not deal with them as in former days.

[Zechariah 8:14](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">zac 8:14</span></span>

I repented not - I did not fail to do it.

[Zechariah 8:15](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">zac 8:15</span></span>

So - So with like steadiness of mind I have purposed to do well to you.

[Zechariah 8:16](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">zac 8:16</span></span>

Judgment of truth - True judgment. Peace - That may restore, and settle
peace among you. Gates - The places of judicature, where the judges sat.

[Zechariah 8:19](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">zac 8:19</span></span>

Thus saith the Lord - This verse is a final decision of the case:
provided they do these things required, [Zac
8:16-17](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#016), then shall the fasting cease,
and turn into joyful feasts. The fourth month - Wherein the city was
taken by the Babylonians. The fifth - In which the temple was burnt. The
seventh - Wherein Gedaliah was killed. The tenth - On the tenth day
whereof the king of Babylon's army sat down before the city.

[Zechariah 8:20](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">zac 8:20</span></span>

People - Multitudes.

[Zechariah 8:21](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">zac 8:21</span></span>

I will go also - The invited, shall with as much zeal embrace the
motion, as others made it.

[Zechariah 8:22](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">zac 8:22</span></span>

In Jerusalem - Literally understood, you have the first fruits of them
mentioned, [Act 2:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#010). Mystically,
Jerusalem is the church of Christ. To pray - To perform all gospel -
worship.

[Zechariah 8:23](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">zac 8:23</span></span>

Ten men - That is, many men. All languages - No nation is any longer
excluded. A Jew - To whom the gospel was first preached. We have heard -
And now see, and are assured. That God - The true God, the only true
God, whom to know is life eternal.
