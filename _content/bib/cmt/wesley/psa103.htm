Psalms Chapter 103

Psalms 103:5

psa 103:5

The eagles - Which lives long in great strength and vigour.

Psalms 103:11

psa 103:11

So great - So much above their deserts and expectations.

Psalms 103:14

psa 103:14

Knoweth - The weakness and mortality of our natures, and the frailty of our condition, so that if he should let loose his hand upon us, we should be irrecoverably destroyed.

Psalms 103:21

psa 103:21

His hosts - A title often given to the angels, in regard of their vast numbers, mighty power, unanimous concurrence, and exquisite order. Ministers - This Hebrew word is commonly used of the highest and most honourable sort of servants,
