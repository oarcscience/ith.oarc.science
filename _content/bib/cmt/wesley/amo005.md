*Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible*, by John Wesley, \[1754-65\], at

### [Amos Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#001)

[Amos 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">amo 5:2</span></span>

The virgin - So she was, when first espoused to God. Upon her land -
Broken to pieces upon her own land, and so left as a broken vessel.

[Amos 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">amo 5:3</span></span>

By a thousand - That sent out one thousand soldiers. An hundred - Shall
lose nine parts of them.

[Amos 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">amo 5:4</span></span>

Ye shall live - It shall be well with you.

[Amos 5:5](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">amo 5:5</span></span>

Seek not - Consult not, worship not the idol at Bethel, Gilgal, or
Beersheba.

[Amos 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">amo 5:6</span></span>

The house of Joseph - The kingdom of the ten tribes, the chief whereof
was Ephraim, the son of Joseph. In Beth - el - If once this fire breaks
out, all your idols in Beth - el shall not be able to quench it.

[Amos 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">amo 5:7</span></span>

Ye - Rulers and judges. Judgment - The righteous sentence of the law. To
wormwood - Proverbially understood; bitterness, injustice and
oppression. Leave off - Make to cease in your courts of judicature.

[Amos 5:8](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">amo 5:8</span></span>

The seven stars - A constellation, whose rising about September was
usually accompanied with sweet showers. Orion - Which arising about
November brings usually cold, rains and frosts intermixt very seasonable
for the earth. The shadow of the earth - The greatest adversity into as
great prosperity. Dark with might - Changes prosperity into adversity.
That calleth - Commands the vapour to ascend, which he turns into rain;
and then pours from the clouds to make the earth fruitful.

[Amos 5:9](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">amo 5:9</span></span>

The strong - The mighty, victorious and insolent. Shall come - Shall
rally and form a siege against their besiegers.

[Amos 5:10](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">amo 5:10</span></span>

They - The judges and people. In the gate - Where judges sat, and where
the prophets many times delivered their message.

[Amos 5:11](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">amo 5:11</span></span>

Your treading - You utterly oppress the helpless. Ye take - Ye extort
from the poor great quantities of wheat, on which he should live.

[Amos 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">amo 5:12</span></span>

In the gate - In their courts of justice.

[Amos 5:13](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">amo 5:13</span></span>

Shall keep silence - Be forced to it. Evil - Both for the sinfulness of
it, and for the troubles, wars, and captivity now at hand.

[Amos 5:14](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">amo 5:14</span></span>

With you - To bless and save you yet.

[Amos 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">amo 5:15</span></span>

The evil - All evil among the people, and yourselves. Love - Commend,
encourage, defend: let your heart be toward good things, and good men.
Remnant - What the invasions of enemies, or the civil wars have spared,
and left in Samaria and Israel.

[Amos 5:16](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">amo 5:16</span></span>

Therefore - The prophet foreseeing their obstinacy, proceeds to denounce
judgment against them. The husbandman - This sort of men are little used
to such ceremonies of mourning, but now such also shall be called upon;
leave your toil, betake yourselves to publick mourning.

[Amos 5:17](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">amo 5:17</span></span>

Vineyards - In these places were usually the greatest joy. Pass through
- To punish all every where.

[Amos 5:18](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">amo 5:18</span></span>

That desire - Scoffingly, not believing any such day would come. To what
end - What do you think to get by it? Is darkness - All adversity, black
and doleful. Not light - No joy, or comfort an it.

[Amos 5:19](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">amo 5:19</span></span>

And a bear - You may escape one, but shall fall in another calamity.
Into the house - At home you may hope for safety, but there other kind
of mischief shall meet you.

[Amos 5:21](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">amo 5:21</span></span>

I hate - Impure and unholy as they are. Will not smell - A savour, of
rest or delight, I will not accept and be pleased with.

[Amos 5:23](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">amo 5:23</span></span>

Thy songs - Used in their sacrifices, and solemn feasts; herein they
imitated the temple - worship, but all was unpleasing to the Lord. Will
not hear - Not with delight and acceptance. Thy viols - This one kind of
musical instrument is put for all the rest.

[Amos 5:24](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">amo 5:24</span></span>

Let judgment - Let justice be administered constantly. Righteousness -
Equity. Stream - Bearing down all that opposes it.

[Amos 5:25](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">amo 5:25</span></span>

Have ye - Their fathers and they, tho' at so great a distance of time,
are one people, and so the prophet considers them. Unto me - Was it to
me, or to your idols, that you offered, even in the wilderness?

[Amos 5:26](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">amo 5:26</span></span>

Ye have borne - Ye carried along with you in the wilderness; the shrine,
or canopy in which the image was placed. Moloch - The great idol of the
Ammonites. Chiun - Another idol.

[Amos 5:27](../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">amo 5:27</span></span>

Therefore - For all your idolatry and other sins, in which you have
obstinately continued.
