### [Proverbs Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#001)

[Proverbs 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 11:1</span></span>

A false balance - The use of all false weights and measures in commerce.

[Proverbs 11:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 11:8</span></span>

Cometh - Into trouble.

[Proverbs 11:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 11:9</span></span>

Delivered - From the infection of the hypocrite's evil counsel.

[Proverbs 11:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">pro 11:11</span></span>

The blessing - Wherewith they bless the city, by their sincere prayers,
and wise and wholesome counsels. The mouth - By their curses, and oaths,
and blasphemies, and wicked counsels.

[Proverbs 11:12](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">pro 11:12</span></span>

Despiseth - Which he shews by contemptuous or reproachful expressions.
Holdeth peace - Forbears all such expressions, and silently and
patiently bears those reproaches.

[Proverbs 11:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 11:13</span></span>

Secrets - Such things as were committed to his trust with a charge of
secrecy.

[Proverbs 11:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 11:16</span></span>

Gracious woman - A woman endued with the grace of God. Retaineth - Or,
holdeth fast her honour, or reputation.

[Proverbs 11:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 11:18</span></span>

Worketh - A work which will deceive his expectation. Soweth - That
worketh it with constancy, and diligence.

[Proverbs 11:21](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">pro 11:21</span></span>

Though hand join - Though they are fortified against God's judgments by
a numerous issue and kindred, and friends, and by mutual and strong
combinations. The seed - Not only their persons, but their children.

[Proverbs 11:22](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">pro 11:22</span></span>

So is a fair woman without discretion -

"Of beauty vain, of virtue void, What art thou in the sight of God? A
slave to every base desire, A creature wallowing in the mire. Go, gaudy
pageant of a day, Thy folly, with thy face display: Set all thy charms
and graces out, And shew - the Jewel in thy snout!"

[Proverbs 11:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 11:23</span></span>

The desire - The desires and expectations of the righteous shall end in
their happiness, but the desires and expectations of the wicked men
shall be disappointed, and end in the wrath of God.

[Proverbs 11:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 11:24</span></span>

Scattereth - That gives liberally. Increaseth - Through God's blessing
upon his estate. Poverty - By God's providence secretly blasting his
estate.

[Proverbs 11:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 11:25</span></span>

Fat - Shall be enriched both with temporal and spiritual blessings.

[Proverbs 11:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 11:26</span></span>

With - holdeth corn - In a time of scarcity. Selleth - Upon reasonable
terms.

[Proverbs 11:27](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">pro 11:27</span></span>

Seeketh - To do good to all men. Favour - With God and men.

[Proverbs 11:29](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">pro 11:29</span></span>

Troubleth - He who brings trouble upon himself and children, either by
prodigality, or by restless endeavours to heap up riches. Wind - Shall
be as unable to keep what he gets as a man is to hold the wind in his
hand.

[Proverbs 11:30](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">pro 11:30</span></span>

The fruit - His discourses and his whole conversation, is like the fruit
of the tree of life. Winneth - That gains souls to God.

[Proverbs 11:31](../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">pro 11:31</span></span>

Recompensed - Punished for his sins.
