### [Psalms Chapter 134](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001)

[Psalms 134:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 134:1</span></span>

Servant - Peculiarly so called, priests and Levites. Night - Not only by
day, but also by night, when their watch was more necessary. Stand -
Serve or minister.

[Psalms 134:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 134:3</span></span>

Thee - Thee whosoever thou art who dost faithfully perform the duty here
commanded.
