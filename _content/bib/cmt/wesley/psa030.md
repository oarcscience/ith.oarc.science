### [Psalms Chapter 30](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001)

[Psalms 30:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 30:5</span></span>

Cometh - Speedily and in due season.

[Psalms 30:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">psa 30:7</span></span>

Mountain - My kingdom: kingdoms are usually called mountains in
prophetical writings.

[Psalms 30:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 30:9</span></span>

Profit - What wilt thou gain by it? The dust - Shall they that are dead
celebrate thy goodness in the land of the living? Or, shall my dust
praise thee?

[Psalms 30:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">psa 30:11</span></span>

Sackcloth - Given me occasion to put off that sackcloth, which they used
to wear in times of mourning, [Est
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#001); [Psa
35:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#013); [Isa
32:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#011); [Joe
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#013). Girded - With joy, as with a
garment, surrounding me on every side.

[Psalms 30:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">psa 30:12</span></span>

My glory - My tongue.
