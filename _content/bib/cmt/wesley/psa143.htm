Psalms Chapter 143

Psalms 143:2

psa 143:2

Justified - Upon terms of strict justice.

Psalms 143:3

psa 143:3

For - This is not a reason of what he last said, but an argument to enforce his petition delivered, Psa 143:1. Soul - My life; nothing less will satisfy him. Dead - I am in as hopeless a condition in the eye of man, as those that have lain long in the grave.

Psalms 143:5

psa 143:5

The days - What thou hast done for thy servants in former times.

Psalms 143:6

psa 143:6

As land - Thirsteth for rain.

Psalms 143:8

psa 143:8

Morning - Seasonably and speedily.
