### [Psalms Chapter 101](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#001)

[Psalms 101:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 101:1</span></span>

I will sing - I will praise thee, O Lord, for thy mercy and justice,
which thou hast so eminently discovered in the government of the world,
and of thy people; and I will make it my care to imitate thee herein.

[Psalms 101:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">psa 101:2</span></span>

I will - I will manage all my affairs with wisdom and integrity. When -
God is often said in scripture to come to men, when he fulfills a
promise to them. House - In my court and family, as well as in my public
administrations.

[Psalms 101:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">psa 101:4</span></span>

Depart - Shall be turned out of my court. Know - Not own nor
countenance.

[Psalms 101:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa101.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">psa 101:8</span></span>

Early - Speedily; as soon as I am seated in the throne.
