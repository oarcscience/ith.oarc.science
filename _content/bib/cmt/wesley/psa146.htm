Psalms Chapter 146

Psalms 146:4

psa 146:4

That day - As soon as ever he is dead. Thoughts - All his designs and endeavours either for himself or for others.

Psalms 146:6

psa 146:6

For ever - Both because he liveth for ever to fulfil his promises, and because he is eternally faithful.
