### [Ezekiel Chapter 25](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 25:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 25:3</span></span>

Aha - When thou shouldest have pitied, thou didst proudly insult over my
people.

[Ezekiel 25:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">eze 25:4</span></span>

The men of the east - The Arabians, associates of Nebuchadnezzar, who
recompensed their service, with giving them this country when it was
conquered, as it was five years after the desolation of Jerusalem.

[Ezekiel 25:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">eze 25:5</span></span>

Rabbah - The royal city, called since Philadelphia from the king of
Egypt who built it. The Ammonites - The land they dwelt in.

[Ezekiel 25:7](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">eze 25:7</span></span>

Know - Thus God will bring those that were strangers to him into an
acquaintance with him, and it will be a blessed effect of their
calamities. How much better is it, to be poor and know God, than to be
rich, and ignorant of him?

[Ezekiel 25:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">eze 25:8</span></span>

Seir - The seed of Esau, the Edomites. Seir was the mountain where they
first planted themselves. Is like - Are no more a select people than
others.

[Ezekiel 25:9](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">eze 25:9</span></span>

The side - That part of his country which was best fortified.
Bethjeshimoth - An ancient city; it was a fortress toward the desert,
which watched lest any should make an inroad on the country.

[Ezekiel 25:10](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">eze 25:10</span></span>

With the Ammonites - As I have given Ammon, so I will with them give
Moab to the Chaldeans, who will give it to the Arabians.

[Ezekiel 25:13](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">eze 25:13</span></span>

Teman - A country in the southern coast of Edom. Dedan - Adjoining to
Edom.

[Ezekiel 25:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">eze 25:15</span></span>

It - Israel.

[Ezekiel 25:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">eze 25:16</span></span>

The Cherethim - The bowmen, the strength of Philistia. The remnant - Who
had escaped the sword of Samuel, David, Hezekiah, and of Psammetichus
king of Egypt.
