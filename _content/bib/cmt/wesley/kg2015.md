### [4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#001)

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">kg2 15:1</span></span>

To reign - Solely and fully to exercise his regal power.

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:5](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">kg2 15:5</span></span>

A leper - The cause whereof see [Ch2
26:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#016).

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:8](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">kg2 15:8</span></span>

Six months - After the throne had been vacant several years, thro' the
dissentions that were in the kingdom.

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:13](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">kg2 15:13</span></span>

Full moon - That dominion seldom lasts long, which is founded in blood
and falsehood.

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:30](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">kg2 15:30</span></span>

Twentieth year - The meaning is, that he began his reign in the
twentieth year after the beginning of Jotham's reign; or, which is the
same thing, in the fourth year of Ahaz, son of Jotham.

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:33](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">kg2 15:33</span></span>

To reign - Alone: for he had reigned before this, as his father's
deputy.

[4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:35](../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">kg2 15:35</span></span>

Gate - Not of the temple, but of one of the courts of the temple,
probably that which led to the king's palace.
