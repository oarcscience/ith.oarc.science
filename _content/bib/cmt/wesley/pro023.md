### [Proverbs Chapter 23](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001)

[Proverbs 23:1](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">pro 23:1</span></span>

Ruler - With a great man.

[Proverbs 23:2](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">pro 23:2</span></span>

Put a knife - Restrain thine appetite, as if a man stood with a knife at
thy throat.

[Proverbs 23:3](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">pro 23:3</span></span>

Deceitful - They do not yield thee that satisfaction which thou didst
expect from them.

[Proverbs 23:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 23:4</span></span>

Thine own wisdom - From worldly wisdom, which persuades men to use all
possible means to get riches.

[Proverbs 23:5](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">pro 23:5</span></span>

Set thine eyes - Look upon it with earnestness and desire. Is not -
Which has no solid and settled being. Eagle - Swiftly, strongly, and
irrecoverably.

[Proverbs 23:6](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">pro 23:6</span></span>

Evil eye - Of the envious or covetous man.

[Proverbs 23:7](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">pro 23:7</span></span>

So is he - You are not to judge of him by his words, but by the constant
temper of his mind.

[Proverbs 23:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 23:8</span></span>

Vomit up - When thou perceivest his churlish disposition, his meat will
be loathsome to thee. Sweet words - Thy pleasant discourse wherewith
thou didst design to delight and profit him.

[Proverbs 23:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 23:9</span></span>

Of a fool - Cast not away good counsels upon incorrigible sinners.

[Proverbs 23:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 23:10</span></span>

Enter not - To possess their lands.

[Proverbs 23:11](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">pro 23:11</span></span>

Redeemer - Heb. their near kinsman, to whom it belongs to avenge their
wrongs, and to recover and maintain their rights.

[Proverbs 23:13](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">pro 23:13</span></span>

Shall not die - It is a likely way to prevent his destruction.

[Proverbs 23:16](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">pro 23:16</span></span>

My reins - I shall rejoice with all my soul.

[Proverbs 23:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 23:18</span></span>

An end - An expected and happy end for such as fear God.

[Proverbs 23:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 23:19</span></span>

Guide - Order the whole course of thine affections and actions. In the
way - In God's way.

[Proverbs 23:23](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">pro 23:23</span></span>

Buy - Purchase it upon any terms, spare no pains or cost. Truth - The
true and saving knowledge of God's will. Sell it not - Do not forsake it
for any worldly advantage.

[Proverbs 23:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 23:26</span></span>

Give me - Solomon here speaks in God's name.

[Proverbs 23:30](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">pro 23:30</span></span>

Mixt wine - Either mixed with water, or with other ingredients to make
it strong and delicious.

[Proverbs 23:31](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#031)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_031">pro 23:31</span></span>

Red - Which was the colour of the best wines in that country. Aright -
When it sparkles, and seems to smile upon a man.

[Proverbs 23:34](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#034)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_034">pro 23:34</span></span>

Lieth down - To sleep. Sea - In a ship in the midst of the sea.

[Proverbs 23:35](../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">pro 23:35</span></span>

Sick - I was not sensible of it. Again - At present my condition
requires sleep to settle myself, and when I am composed, I purpose to
return to my former course.
