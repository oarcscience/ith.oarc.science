### [Psalms Chapter 53](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#001)

[Psalms 53:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa053.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">psa 53:5</span></span>

Scattered - Hath not only broken their bones, their strength, and force,
but also dispersed them hither and thither, so as there is no hope of a
restoration. Thee - Against my people. Thou - Thou oh Jerusalem, which
they besiege.
