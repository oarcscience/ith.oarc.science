Psalms Chapter 30

Psalms 30:5

psa 30:5

Cometh - Speedily and in due season.

Psalms 30:7

psa 30:7

Mountain - My kingdom: kingdoms are usually called mountains in prophetical writings.

Psalms 30:9

psa 30:9

Profit - What wilt thou gain by it? The dust - Shall they that are dead celebrate thy goodness in the land of the living? Or, shall my dust praise thee?

Psalms 30:11

psa 30:11

Sackcloth - Given me occasion to put off that sackcloth, which they used to wear in times of mourning, Est 4:1; Psa 35:13; Isa 32:11; Joe 1:13. Girded - With joy, as with a garment, surrounding me on every side.

Psalms 30:12

psa 30:12

My glory - My tongue.
