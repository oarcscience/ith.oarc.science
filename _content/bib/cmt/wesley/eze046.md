### [Ezekiel Chapter 46](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 46:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">eze 46:3</span></span>

In the sabbaths - Both weekly and other holy days, which are called
sabbaths.

[Ezekiel 46:9](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">eze 46:9</span></span>

Go forth over against it - Perhaps, only to prevent all jostling and
confusion.

[Ezekiel 46:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">eze 46:17</span></span>

His inheritance - Whatever lands of the prince are given to servants,
shall at the year of Jubilee revert to the sons of the prince. For them
- And to theirs after them.

[Ezekiel 46:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">eze 46:20</span></span>

The outer court - Where the people were.

[Ezekiel 46:21](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">eze 46:21</span></span>

A court - A smaller court made up on the outer sides with the walls of
the greater square, and on the inside made with two walls, the one forty
cubits long, the other thirty cubits broad.

[Ezekiel 46:23](../../../bib/kjv/eze046.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">eze 46:23</span></span>

A row of building - A range of building on the inside of the walls of
the lesser courts. Four - Four courts in the four corners.
