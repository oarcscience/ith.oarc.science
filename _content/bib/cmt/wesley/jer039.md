### [Jeremiah Chapter 39](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 39:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 39:1</span></span>

Tenth month - This month answers to part of our December and January.

[Jeremiah 39:3](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">jer 39:3</span></span>

The middle gate - The city was encompassed with two walls, before they
came to the wall of the temple; the gate in the inner wall is supposed
to have been that which is called the middle gate. They would not at
first venture farther, 'till they might without hazarding their persons.

[Jeremiah 39:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">jer 39:5</span></span>

Riblah - Riblah was upon the borders of Canaan. Zedekiah was a tributary
to the king of Babylon, and so subject to his power, having made a
covenant with him, and secured his allegiance by his oath to him.

[Jeremiah 39:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">jer 39:12</span></span>

Take him - It is probable, that Nebuchadnezzar had been informed,
Jeremiah had constantly told the king, that the Chaldeans should take
the city, and as steadily persuaded both the king and princes to
surrender it to them.

[Jeremiah 39:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">jer 39:15</span></span>

Now the word - These four verses mention a matter that happened before
the things mentioned in the foregoing verses.
