Psalms Chapter 121

Psalms 121:1

psa 121:1

Hills - To Sion and Moriah, which are called the holy mountains.

Psalms 121:5

psa 121:5

Shade - To keep thee from the burning heat of the sun.

Psalms 121:6

psa 121:6

Smite - With excessive heat. Moon - With that cold and moisture which come into the air by it. Intemperate heats and colds are the springs of many diseases.
