### [Proverbs Chapter 29](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001)

[Proverbs 29:4](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">pro 29:4</span></span>

Judgment - By the free and impartial exercise of justice. Gifts -
Bribes.

[Proverbs 29:8](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">pro 29:8</span></span>

Wrath - The wrath of God or of men, who were enraged against it.

[Proverbs 29:9](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">pro 29:9</span></span>

Whether - Whether he, the wise man, deal sharply with him, or mildly,
there is no rest, no end or fruit of the debate.

[Proverbs 29:10](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">pro 29:10</span></span>

Seek - To preserve it.

[Proverbs 29:15](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">pro 29:15</span></span>

Left - Suffered to follow his own will without restraint and chastening.

[Proverbs 29:18](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">pro 29:18</span></span>

No vision - No prophecy; no publick preaching of God's word.

[Proverbs 29:19](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">pro 29:19</span></span>

A servant - Who is so not only in condition, but also in the temper of
his mind, disingenuous, and stubborn. Words - By mere words. Tho' -
Though he know thy mind, and his own duty.

[Proverbs 29:20](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#020)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_020">pro 29:20</span></span>

Hasty - Who is rash and heady in the management of his affairs.

[Proverbs 29:21](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#021)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">pro 29:21</span></span>

Delicately - Allowing him too much freedom, and familiarity. A son -
Will find him at last grow insolent, and forgetful of his servile
condition.

[Proverbs 29:24](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">pro 29:24</span></span>

Bewrayeth it not - Which he is bound to do for the publick good.

[Proverbs 29:25](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">pro 29:25</span></span>

A snare - Is an occasion of many sins.

[Proverbs 29:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">pro 29:26</span></span>

Judgment - The decision of his cause, and the success of all his
endeavours.
