### [Haggai Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001)

[Haggai](../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hag 0:0</span></span>

After the return from captivity, Haggai was sent to encourage the people
to rebuild the temple, and to reprove their neglect. To encourage their
undertaking, the people are assured that the glory of the second temple
shall far exceed that of the first, by the appearing therein of Christ,
the Desire of all nations.
