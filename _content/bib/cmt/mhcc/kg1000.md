### [3 Kings (1 Kings) Introduction](../../../bib/kjv/kg1001.htm#001)

[3 Kings (1 Kings)](../../../bib/kjv/kg1001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">kg1 0:0</span></span>

The history now before us accounts for the affairs of the kingdoms of
Judah and Israel, yet with special regard to the kingdom of God among
them; for it is a sacred history. It is earlier as to time, teaches much
more, and is more interesting than any common histories.
