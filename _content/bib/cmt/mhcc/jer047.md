### [Jeremiah Chapter 47](../../../bib/kjv/jer047.htm#001)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 47:0</span></span>

The calamities of the Philistines.

[Jeremiah 47:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer047.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 47:1</span></span>

The Philistines had always been enemies to Israel; but the Chaldean army
shall overflow their land like a deluge. Those whom God will spoil, must
be spoiled. For when the Lord intends to destroy the wicked, he will cut
off every helper. So deplorable are the desolations of war, that the
blessings of peace are most desirable. But we must submit to His
appointments who ordains all in perfect wisdom and justice.
