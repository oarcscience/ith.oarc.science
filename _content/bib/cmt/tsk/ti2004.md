### [2 Timothy Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ti2 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ti2 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#001), He exhorts him to do his
duty with all care and diligence; [Ti2
4:6](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#006), certifies him of the nearness of
his death; [Ti2 4:9](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#009), wills him to come
speedily unto him, and to bring Marcus with him, and certain things
which he wrote for; [Ti2 4:14](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#014), warns
him to beware of Alexander the smith; [Ti2
4:16](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#016), informs him what had befallen
him at his first answering; [Ti2 4:19](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#019),
and soon after he concludes.
