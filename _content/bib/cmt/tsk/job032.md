Overview

[Job 32:1](../../../bib/kjv/job032.htm#001), Elihu is angry with Job and
his three friends; [Job 32:6](../../../bib/kjv/job032.htm#006), Because
wisdom comes not from age, he excuses the boldness of his youth; [Job
32:11](../../../bib/kjv/job032.htm#011), He reproves them for not
satisfying Job; [Job 32:16](../../../bib/kjv/job032.htm#016), His zeal
to speak.
