his study in prayer; [Psa 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#004), God
favours not the wicked; [Psa 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#007),

5:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#010), to destroy his enemies; [Psa
5:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#011), and to preserve the godly.
