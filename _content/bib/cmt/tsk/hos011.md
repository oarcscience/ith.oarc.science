<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hos 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Hos 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos011.htm#001), The ingratitude of Israel
unto God for his benefits; [Hos 11:5](../../../bib/kjv/hos011.htm#005),
His judgment; [Hos 11:8](../../../bib/kjv/hos011.htm#008), God's mercy
toward them; [Hos 11:12](../../../bib/kjv/hos011.htm#012), Israel's
falsehood and Judah's fidelity.
