### [2 Corinthians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001)

Overview

[Co2 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001), Paul salutes the
Corinthians; [Co2 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#003), he encourages
them against troubles, by the comforts and deliverances which God had
given him, as in all his afflictions, [Co2
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#008), so particularly in his late
danger in Asia; [Co2 1:12](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#012), And calling
both his own conscience and theirs to witness of his sincere manner of
preaching the immutable truth of the gospel, [Co2
1:15](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#015). he excuses his not coming to
them, as proceeding not of lightness, but of his lenity towards them.
