### [Genesis Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 17:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#001), God renews the covenant
with Abram, and changes his name to Abraham, in token of a greater
blessing; [Gen 17:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#009), Circumcision is
instituted; [Gen 17:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#015), Sarai's name
is changed to Sarah, and she is blessed; [Gen
17:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#017), Isaac is promised, and the time
of his birth fixed; [Gen 17:23](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#023),
Abraham and Ishmael are circumcised.
