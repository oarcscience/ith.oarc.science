Overview

[Isa 31:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#001), The prophet shews the folly
and danger of trusting to Egypt, and forsaking God; [Isa
31:6](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#006), He exhorts to conversion; [Isa
31:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#008), He shews the fall of Assyria.
