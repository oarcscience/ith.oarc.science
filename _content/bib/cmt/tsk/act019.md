Overview

[Act 19:1](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#001), The Holy Ghost is given by
Paul's hands; [Act 19:8](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#008), The Jews
blaspheme his doctrine, which is confirmed by miracles; [Act
19:13](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#013), The Jewish exorcists, [Act
19:16](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#016). are beaten by the devil; [Act
19:19](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#019), Conjuring books are burnt; [Act
19:21](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#021), Demetrius, for love of gain,
raises an uproar against Paul; [Act
19:35](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#035), which is appeased by the
townclerk.
