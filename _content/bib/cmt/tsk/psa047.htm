Psalms Chapter 47

Psalms

psa 47:0

Overview

Psa 47:1, The nations are exhorted cheerfully to entertain the kingdom of Christ.

This Psalm is supposed to have been composed by Solomon on the removal of the ark into the temple, 2Chr. 7.
