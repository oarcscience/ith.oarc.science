Overview

[Joh 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#001), Christ turns water into
wine; [Joh 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#012), departs into
Capernaum, [Joh 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#013). and to
Jerusalem, [Joh 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#014). where he purges
the temple of buyers and sellers; [Joh
2:18](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#018), He foretells his death and
resurrection; [Joh 2:23](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#023), Many believe
because of his miracles, but he will not trust himself with them.
