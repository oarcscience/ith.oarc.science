<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 48:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 48:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#001), Joseph with his sons visits
his sick father; [Gen 48:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#002), Jacob
strengthens himself to bless them; [Gen
48:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#003), He repeats God's promise; [Gen
48:5](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#005), He takes Ephraim and Manasseh as
his own sons; [Gen 48:7](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#007), He tells
Joseph of his mother's grave; [Gen
48:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#008), He blesses Ephraim and Manasseh;
[Gen 48:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#017), He prefers the younger
before the elder; [Gen 48:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen048.htm#021), He
prophesies their return to Canaan.
