### [Jeremiah Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001), Jeremiah proclaims God's
covenant; [Jer 11:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#008), rebukes the Jews'
disobeying thereof; [Jer 11:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#011),
prophesies evils to come upon them; [Jer
11:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#018), and upon the men of Anathoth,
for conspiring to kill him.
