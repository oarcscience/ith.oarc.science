Overview

[Psa 146:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa146.htm#001), The Psalmist vows
perpetual praises to God; [Psa 146:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa146.htm#003),
He exhorts not to trust in man; [Psa
146:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa146.htm#005), God, for his power, justice,
mercy, and kingdom, is only worthy to be trusted.
