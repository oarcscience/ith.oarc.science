### [Acts Chapter 18](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 18:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 18:1](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#001), Paul labours with his
hands, and preaches at Corinth to the Gentiles; [Act
18:9](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#009), The Lord encourages him in a
vision; [Act 18:12](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#012), He is accused
before Gallio the deputy, but is dismissed; [Act
18:18](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#018), Afterwards passing from city to
city, he strengthens the disciples; [Act
18:24](../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#024), Apollos, being more perfectly
instructed by Aquila and Priscilla, preaches Christ with great efficacy.
