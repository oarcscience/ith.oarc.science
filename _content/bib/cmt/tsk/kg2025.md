[4 Kings (2 Kings)](../../../bib/kjv/kg2001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">kg2 25:0</span></span>

Overview

[Kg2 25:1](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#001), Jerusalem is besieged; [Kg2
25:4](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#004), Zedekiah taken, his sons slain,
his eyes put out; [Kg2 25:8](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#008),
Nebuzar-adan defaces the city, carries the remnant, except a few poor
labourers, into captivity; [Kg2 25:13](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#013),
and spoils and carries away the treasures; [Kg2
25:18](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#018), The nobles are slain at Riblah;
[Kg2 25:22](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#022), Gedaliah, who was over
them that remained, being slain, the rest flee into Egypt; [Kg2
25:27](../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#027), Evil-merodach advances
Jehoiachin in his court.
