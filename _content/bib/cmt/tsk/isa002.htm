Isaiah Chapter 2

Isaiah

isa 2:0

Overview

Isa 2:1, Isaiah prophesies the coming of Christ's kingdom; Isa 2:6, Wickedness is the cause of God's forsaking; Isa 2:10, He exhorts to fear, because of the powerful effects of God's majesty.
