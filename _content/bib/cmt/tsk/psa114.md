Overview

[Psa 114:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#001), The miracles wrought by
God, when he brought his people out of Egypt, are a just ground of
fearing him.

[Psalms 114:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 114:1</span></span>

(Title), This short, and apparently imperfect Psalm, for elegance and
sublimity, yields to few in the whole book. The composition of it is
inexpressibly beautiful, and in the highest style of poetry.
