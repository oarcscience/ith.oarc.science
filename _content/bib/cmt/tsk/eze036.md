Overview

[Eze 36:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#001), The land of Israel is
comforted, both by destruction of the heathen, who spitefully used it,
[Eze 36:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#008), and by the blessings of God
promised unto it; [Eze 36:16](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#016), Israel
was rejected for their sin, [Eze
36:21](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#021). and shall be restored without
their desert; [Eze 36:25](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#025), The
blessings of Christ's kingdom.
