<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 15:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2015.htm#001), Asa, with Judah and many of
Israel, moved by the prophecy of Azariah the son of Oded, make a solemn
covenant with God; [Ch2 15:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch2015.htm#016), He puts
down Maachah his mother for her idolatry; [Ch2
15:18](../../../bib/kjv/ch2015.htm#018), He brings dedicated things into
the house of God, and enjoys a long peace.
