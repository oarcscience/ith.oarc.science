### [Matthew Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001)

Overview

[Mat 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001), The genealogy of Christ from
Abraham to Joseph; [Mat 1:18](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#018), He is
miraculously conceived of the Holy Ghost by the Virgin Mary, when she
was espoused to Joseph; [Mat 1:19](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#019), The
angel satisfies the doubts of Joseph, and declares the names and office
of Christ: Jesus is born.
