<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#001), Christ, fasting forty days,
is tempted of the devil and ministered unto by angels; [Mat
4:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#012), He dwells in Capernaum; [Mat
4:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#017), begins to preach; [Mat
4:18](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#018), calls Peter and Andrew, [Mat
4:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#021). James and John; v.23, teaches
and heals all the diseased.
