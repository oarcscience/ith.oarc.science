Overview

[Mat 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#001), Christ sends out his twelve
apostles, enabling them with power to do miracles; [Mat
10:5](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#005), giving them their charge,
teaches them; [Mat 10:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#016), comforts
them against persecutions; [Mat 10:40](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#040),
and promises a blessing to those that receive them.
