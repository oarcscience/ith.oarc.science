Overview

[Jer 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#001), God having shewed his former
kindness, expostulates with the Jews on their causeless and unexampled
revolt; [Jer 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#014), They are the causes
of their own calamities; [Jer 2:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#018),
The sins and idolatries of Judah; [Jer
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/jer002.htm#035), Her confidence is rejected.
