Overview

people for their confidence in Egypt, [Isa
30:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#008), and contempt of God's word; [Isa
30:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#018), God's mercies towards his
church; [Isa 30:27](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#027), God's wrath and
the people's joy, in the destruction of Assyria.
