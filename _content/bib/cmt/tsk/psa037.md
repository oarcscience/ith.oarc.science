Overview

[Psa 37:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001), David persuades to patience
and confidence in God, by the different estate of the godly and the
wicked.

[Psalms 37:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 37:1</span></span>

(Title), This is the third alphabetical Psalm. It seems to have been
intended as an instructive and consoling ode for the captives in
Babylon, who might feel themselves severely tempted when they saw those
idolaters in prosperity, and themselves in adversity.
