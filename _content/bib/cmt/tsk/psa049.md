### [Psalms Chapter 49](../../../bib/kjv/psa049.htm#001)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 49:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 49:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa049.htm#001), An earnest persuasion to
build the faith of resurrection, not on worldly power, but on God; [Psa
49:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa049.htm#016), Worldly prosperity is not to be
admired.

This Psalm was probably written by one of the descendants of the sons of
Korah, during the Babylonian captivity.
