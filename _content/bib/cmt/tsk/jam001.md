### [James Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#001)

Overview

[Jam 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#001), We are to rejoice under the
cross; [Jam 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#005), to ask patience of
God; [Jam 1:13](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#013), and in our trials not
to impute our weakness, or sins, to him, [Jam
1:19](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#019). but rather to hearken to the
word, to meditate on it, and to do thereafter; [Jam
1:26](../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#026), Otherwise men may seem, but
never be, truly religious.
