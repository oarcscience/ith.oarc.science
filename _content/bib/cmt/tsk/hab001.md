<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hab 1:0</span></span>

Overview

[Hab 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/hab001.htm#001), Unto Habakkuk, complaining
of the iniquity of the land, [Hab 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/hab001.htm#005),
is shewn the fearful vengeance by the Chaldeans; [Hab
1:12](../../../bib/kjv/hab001.htm#012), He complains that vengeance
should be executed by them who are far worse.
