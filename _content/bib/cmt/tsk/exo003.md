Overview

[Exo 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#001), Moses keeps Jethro's flock;
[Exo 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#002), God appears to him in a
burning bush; [Exo 3:9](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#009), He sends him
to deliver Israel; [Exo 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#013), The name
of God; [Exo 3:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#015), His message to
Israel, and Pharaoh, whose opposition is foretold; [Exo
3:20](../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#020), He is assured of Israel's
deliverance.

this is my name for ever: The name here referred to is that which
immediately precedes, יהוה, \[Strong's H3068\], Yehowah, which we
translate "Lord", the name by which God had been known from the creation
of the world ([Gen 2:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#002)), and by which
he is known to the present day. יהוה, Yehowah, from הוה, hawah, to be,
subsist, signifies "He who is", or "subsists", i.e., eminently and in a
manner superior to all other beings; and is essentially the same with
אהיה, eheyeh, "I AM", in the preceding verse. [Psa
72:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#017), [Psa
72:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#019), [Psa
