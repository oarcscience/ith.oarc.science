<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#001), The wise men from the east
enquire after Christ; [Mat 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#003), at
which Herod is alarmed; [Mat 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#009), They
are directed by a star to Bethlehem, worship him, and offer their
presents; [Mat 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#013), Joseph flees into
Egypt with Jesus and his mother; [Mat
2:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#016), Herod slays the children; [Mat
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#020), himself dies; [Mat
2:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#023), Christ is brought back again
into Galilee to Nazareth.
