Overview

[Phi 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/phi004.htm#001), From particular admonitions,
[Phi 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/phi004.htm#004), he proceeds to general
exhortations, [Phi 4:10](../../../bib/kjv/phi004.htm#010). shewing how
he rejoiced at their liberality towards him lying in prison, not so much
for the supply of his own wants as for the grace of God in them; [Phi
4:19](../../../bib/kjv/phi004.htm#019), And so he concludes with prayer
and salutations.
