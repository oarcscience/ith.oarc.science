<span class="margnote"><span id="an_021">rev 18:21</span></span>

a mighty: This was to represent the violence of her fall, and that she
should never rise again; which is further illustrated by varied emphatic
expressions taken from the ancient prophets. But Rome is still standing
and flourishing, and honoured by many nations as the metropolis of the
