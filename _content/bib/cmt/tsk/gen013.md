### [Genesis Chapter 13](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 13:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#001), Abram and Lot return with
great riches out of Egypt; [Gen 13:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#006),
Strife arises between Abram's herdsmen and those of Lot; [Gen
13:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#008), Abram meekly refers it to Lot to
choose his part of the country, [Gen
13:10](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#010), and Lot goes to Sodom; [Gen
13:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#014), God renews the promise to
Abram; [Gen 13:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#018), He removes to
Hebron, and there builds an altar.
