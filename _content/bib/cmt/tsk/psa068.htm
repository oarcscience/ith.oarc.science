Psalms Chapter 68

Psalms

psa 68:0

Overview

Psa 68:1, A prayer at the removing of the ark; Psa 68:4, An exhortation to praise God for his mercies; Psa 68:7, for his care of the church; Psa 68:19, for his great works.
