Overview

[Th1 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#001), He exhorts them to go
forward in all manner of godliness; [Th1
4:6](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#006), to live holily and justly; [Th1
4:9](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#009), to love one another; [Th1
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#011), and quietly to follow their own
business; [Th1 4:13](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#013), and last of all,
to sorrow moderately for the dead; [Th1
4:17](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#017), And unto this last exhortation
is annexed a brief description of the resurrection, and second coming of
Christ to judgment.
