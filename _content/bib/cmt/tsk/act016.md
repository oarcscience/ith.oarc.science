Overview

[Act 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#001), Paul having circumcised
Timothy, [Act 16:7](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#007), and being called
by the Spirit from one country to another, [Act
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#014). converts Lydia, [Act
16:16](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#016). and casts out a spirit of
divination; [Act 16:19](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#019), for which
cause he and Silas are whipped and imprisoned; [Act
16:25](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#025), The prison doors are opened;
[Act 16:31](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#031), The jailor is converted,
[Act 16:35](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#035). and they are delivered.
