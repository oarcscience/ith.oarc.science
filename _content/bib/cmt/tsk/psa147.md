### [Psalms Chapter 147](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#001)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 147:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 147:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#001), The prophet exhorts to
praise God for his care of the church; [Psa
147:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#004), his power and wisdom; [Psa
147:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#006), his mercy; [Psa
147:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#007), his providence; [Psa
147:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#012), to praise him for his
blessings upon the kingdom; [Psa
147:15](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#015), for his power over the
elements; [Psa 147:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa147.htm#019), and for his
ordinances in the church.
