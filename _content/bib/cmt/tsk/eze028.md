prince of Tyrus for his sacrilegious pride; [Eze
28:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze028.htm#011), A lamentation of his great
glory corrupted by sin; [Eze 28:20](../../../bib/kjv/eze028.htm#020),
The judgment of Zidon; [Eze 28:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze028.htm#024), The
restoration of Israel.
