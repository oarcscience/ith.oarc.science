Overview

[Joh 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#001), Christ comforts his
disciples with the hope of heaven; [Joh
14:5](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#005), professes himself the way, the
truth, and the life, and one with the Father; [Joh
14:13](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#013), assures their prayers to be
effectual; [Joh 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#015), requires
obedience; [Joh 14:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#016), promises the
Comforter; [Joh 14:27](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#027), and leaves his
peace with them.
