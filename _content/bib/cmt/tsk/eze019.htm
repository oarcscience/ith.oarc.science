Ezekiel Chapter 19

Ezekiel

eze 19:0

Overview

Eze 19:1, A lamentation for the princes of Israel, under the parable of lion's whelps taken in a pit; Eze 19:10, and for Jerusalem, under the parable of a wasted vine.
