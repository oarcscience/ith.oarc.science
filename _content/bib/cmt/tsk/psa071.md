faith, and experience of God's favour, prays both for himself, and
against the enemies of his soul; [Psa
71:14](../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#014), He promises constancy; [Psa
71:17](../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#017), He prays for perseverance; [Psa
71:19](../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#019), He praises God, and promises to
do it cheerfully.
