manifold judgments of the Jews; [Jer
15:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#010), Jeremiah, complaining of their
spite, receives a promise for himself; [Jer
15:12](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#012), and a threatening for them;
[Jer 15:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#015), He prays; [Jer
15:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#019), and receives a gracious
promise.
