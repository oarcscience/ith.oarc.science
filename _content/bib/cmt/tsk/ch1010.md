### [1 Chronicles Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#001)

[1 Chronicles](../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch1 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch1 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#001), Saul's overthrow and death;
[Ch1 10:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#008), The Philistines triumph
over Saul; [Ch1 10:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#011), The kindness of
Jabesh-gilead towards Saul and his sons; [Ch1
10:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#013), Saul's sin, for which the
kingdom was translated from him to David.

with him in the battle; and his family received such a blow, that it
never recovered itself again. For though Ishbosheth reigned over a part
of the country, yet it was not in any splendour. This history seems to
be repeated here as an introduction to that of the kingdom of David.
