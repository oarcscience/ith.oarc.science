Overview

[Gal 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#001), He wills them to stand in
their liberty, [Gal 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#003), and not to
observe circumcision; [Gal 5:13](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#013), but
rather love, which is the sum of the law; [Gal
5:19](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#019), He reckons up the works of the
flesh, [Gal 5:22](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#022). and the fruits of
the Spirit, [Gal 5:25](../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#025). and exhorts to
walk in the Spirit.
