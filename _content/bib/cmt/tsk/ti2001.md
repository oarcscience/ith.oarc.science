Overview

[Ti2 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#001), Paul's love to Timothy, and
the unfeigned faith which was in Timothy himself, his mother, and
grandmother; [Ti2 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#006), He is exhorted
to stir up the gift of God which was in him; [Ti2
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#008), to be stedfast and patient in
persecution; [Ti2 1:13](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#013), and to persist
in the form and truth of that doctrine which he had learned of him; [Ti2
1:15](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#015), Phygellus and Hermogenes, and
such like, are noted, and Onesiphorus is highly commended.
