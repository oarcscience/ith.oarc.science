### [Matthew Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#001)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#001), Christ reproves the
blindness of the Pharisees concerning the breach of the sabbath, [Mat
12:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#003), by scripture, [Mat
12:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#009), by reason, [Mat
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#013). and by a miracle; [Mat
12:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#022), He heals a man possessed that
was blind and dumb; [Mat 12:24](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#024), and
confuting the absurd charge of casting out devils by Beelzebub, he shows
that blasphemy against the Holy Ghost shall never be forgiven; [Mat
12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036), Account shall be made of idle
words; [Mat 12:38](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#038), He rebukes the
unfaithful, who seek after a sign, [Mat
12:46](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#046). and shows who is his brother,
sister, and mother.
