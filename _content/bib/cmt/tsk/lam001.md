Overview

[Lam 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#001), The miseries of Jerusalem
and of the Jews pathetically lamented, with confessions of their sins;
[Lam 1:12](../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#012), The attention and
compassion of beholders demanded to this unprecedented case; [Lam
1:18](../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#018), The justice of God acknowledged,
and his mercy supplicated, with prayers against insulting foes.
