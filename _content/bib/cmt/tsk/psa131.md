### [Psalms Chapter 131](../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#001)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 131:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 131:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#001), David, professing his
humility, [Psa 131:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#003), exhorts Israel
to trust in God.

Some think that this Psalm was composed by David when accused by Saul
and his courtiers that he affected the crown; though others refer it to
the time of the captivity; and consider it as containing a fair account
of the manner in which the captives behaved themselves. [Psa
122:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa122.htm#001), [Psa
124:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa124.htm#001), [Psa
133:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa133.htm#001) \*titles
