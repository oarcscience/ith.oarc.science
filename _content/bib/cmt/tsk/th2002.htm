2 Thessalonians Chapter 2

2 Thessalonians

th2 2:0

Overview

Th2 2:1, Paul urges them to continue stedfast in the truth received; Th2 2:3, shows that there shall be a departure from the faith, Th2 2:9, and a discovery of Antichrist, before the day of the Lord come; Th2 2:15, and thereupon repeats his former exhortation, and prays for them.
