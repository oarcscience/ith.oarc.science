Overview

[Eph 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#001), He exhorts to unity; [Eph
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#007), and declares that God therefore
gives divers gifts unto men; [Eph
4:11](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#011), that his church might be
edified, [Eph 4:16](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#016). and grow up in
Christ; [Eph 4:18](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#018), He calls them from
the impurity of the Gentiles; [Eph
4:24](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#024), to put on the new man; [Eph
4:25](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#025), to cast off lying; [Eph
4:29](../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#029), and corrupt communication.
