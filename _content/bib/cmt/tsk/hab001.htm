Habakkuk Chapter 1

Habakkuk

hab 1:0

Overview

Hab 1:1, Unto Habakkuk, complaining of the iniquity of the land, Hab 1:5, is shewn the fearful vengeance by the Chaldeans; Hab 1:12, He complains that vengeance should be executed by them who are far worse.
