<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 6:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#001), The enemies sent against
Judah, [Jer 6:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#004), encourage themselves;
[Jer 6:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#006), God sets them on work
because of their sins; [Jer 6:9](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#009), The
prophet laments the judgments of God because of their sins; [Jer
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#018), He proclaims God's wrath; [Jer
6:26](../../../bib/kjv/jer006.htm#026), He calls the people to mourn for
the judgment on their sins.
