### [Genesis Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001), God blesses Noah and his
sons, and grants them flesh for food; [Gen
9:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#004), Blood and murder are forbidden;
[Gen 9:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#008), God's covenant, of which the
rainbow was constituted a pledge; [Gen
9:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#018), Noah's family replenish the
world; [Gen 9:20](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#020), Noah plants a
vineyard; [Gen 9:21](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#021), Is drunken, and
mocked by his son; [Gen 9:25](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#025), Curses
Canaan; [Gen 9:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#026), Blesses Shem; [Gen
9:27](../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#027), Prays for Japheth, and dies.

enlarge: or, persuade

Japheth: Japheth denotes enlargement, and how wonderfully have his
boundaries been enlarged; for not only Europe, but Asia Minor, part of
Armenia, Iberia, the whole of the vast regions of Asia north of Taurus,
and probably America, fell to the share of his posterity.

he shall dwell: These words may mean either that God or that Japheth
shall dwell in the tents of Shem. In either sense the prophecy has been
literally fulfilled.
