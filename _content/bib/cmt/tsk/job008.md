[Job](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">job 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Job 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#001), Bildad shews God's justice
in dealing with men according to their works; [Job
8:8](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#008), He alleges antiquity to prove the
certain destruction of the hypocrite; [Job
8:20](../../../bib/kjv/job008.htm#020), He applies God's just dealing to
Job.
