### [Matthew Chapter 20](../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#001)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 20:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#001), Christ, by the similitude
of the labourers in the vineyard, shows that God is debtor unto no man;
[Mat 20:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#017), foretells his passion;
[Mat 20:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#020), by answering the mother of
Zebedee's children, teaches his disciples to be lowly; [Mat
20:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#029), and gives two blind men their
sight.
