Overview

[Gen 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001), Abraham is tempted to offer
Isaac; [Gen 22:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#003), He gives proof of
his faith and obedience; [Gen 22:11](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#011),
The angel prevents him; [Gen 22:13](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#013),
Isaac is exchanged for a ram; [Gen
22:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#014), The place is called
Jehovah-jireh; [Gen 22:15](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#015), Abraham is
again blessed; [Gen 22:20](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#020), The
generations of Nahor unto Rebekah.
