### [Genesis Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#001), God calls Abram, and
blesses him with a promise of Christ; [Gen
12:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#004), He departs with Lot from Haran,
and comes to Canaan; [Gen 12:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#006), He
journeys through Canaan, [Gen 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#007),
which is promised to him in a vision; [Gen
12:10](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#010), He is driven by famine into
Egypt; [Gen 12:11](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#011), Fear makes him
feign his wife to be his sister; [Gen
12:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#014), Pharaoh, having taken her from
him, by plagues is compelled to restore her; [Gen
12:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#018), He reproves Abram, whom he
dismisses.

Canaan: So called from Canaan the son of Ham, lies between the
Mediterranean sea on the west, the wilderness of Paran, Idumea, and
Egypt on the south, the mountains of Arabia on the east, and the
mountains of Lebanon and Phoenicia on the north. Its length, from Dan to
Beersheba, is about 200 miles, and its breadth, from the Mediterranean
sea to its eastern borders, about 90.
