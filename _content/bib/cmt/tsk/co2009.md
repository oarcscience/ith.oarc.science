[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001)

Overview

[Co2 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#001), He yields the reason why,
though he knew their forwardness, yet he sent Titus and his brethren
beforehand; [Co2 9:6](../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#006), And he proceeds
in stirring them up to a bountiful alms, as being but a kind of sowing
of seed, [Co2 9:10](../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#010). which shall return
a great increase to them, [Co2 9:13](../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#013).
and occasion a great sacrifice of thanksgivings unto God.
