[Romans](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">rom 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Rom 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#001), Being justified by faith, we
have peace with God; [Rom 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#002), and joy
in our hope; [Rom 5:8](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#008), that since we
were reconciled by his blood, when we were enemies; [Rom
5:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#010), we shall much more be saved,
being reconciled; [Rom 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#012), As sin
and death came by Adam; [Rom 5:17](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#017), so
much more righteousness and life by Jesus Christ; [Rom
5:20](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#020), Where sin abounded, grace did
superabound.
