Overview

[Psa 55:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa055.htm#001), David in his prayer
complains of his fearful case; [Psa
55:9](../../../bib/kjv/psa055.htm#009), He prays against his enemies, of
whose wickedness and treachery he complains; [Psa
55:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa055.htm#016), He comforts himself in God's
preservation of him, and confusion of his enemies.
