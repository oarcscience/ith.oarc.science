<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eze 6:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eze 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#001), The judgment of Israel for
their idolatry; [Eze 6:8](../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#008), A remnant
shall be blessed; [Eze 6:11](../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#011), The
faithful are exhorted to lament their abominations and calamities.

[Ezekiel 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 6:1</span></span>

And the word: This is a new prophecy, and was probably given after the
430 days of his lying on his left and right side were accomplished. By
Israel here Judea is simply meant; not the ten tribes, who had long
before been carried captive. [Eze 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#001)
