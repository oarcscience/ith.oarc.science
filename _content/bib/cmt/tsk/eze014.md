Overview

[Eze 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#001), God answers idolaters
according to their own heart; [Eze
14:6](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#006), They are exhorted to repent, for
fear of judgments, by means of seduced prophets; [Eze
14:12](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#012), God's irrevocable sentence of
famine; [Eze 14:15](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#015), of noisome beasts;
[Eze 14:17](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#017), of the sword; [Eze
14:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#019), and of pestilence; [Eze
14:22](../../../bib/kjv/eze014.htm#022), A remnant shall be reserved for
example of others.
