Overview

[Col 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#001), He shows where we should
seek Christ; [Col 3:5](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#005), He exhorts to
mortification; [Col 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#010), to put off
the old man, and put on Christ; [Col
3:12](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#012), exhorting to charity, humility,
and other several duties.
