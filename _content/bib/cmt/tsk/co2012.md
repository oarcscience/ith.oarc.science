Overview

[Co2 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#001), For commending of his
apostleship, though he might glory of his wonderful revelations, [Co2
12:9](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#009), yet he rather chooses to glory
of his infirmities; [Co2 12:11](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#011),
blaming them for forcing him to this vain boasting; [Co2
12:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#014), He promises to come to them
again; but yet altogether in the affection of a father; [Co2
12:20](../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#020), although he fears he shall to
his grief find many offenders, and public disorders there.
