Overview

[Isa 57:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa057.htm#001), The blessed death of the
righteous; [Isa 57:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa057.htm#003), God reproves the
Jews for their whorish idolatry; [Isa
57:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa057.htm#013), He gives promises of mercy to
the penitent.
