### [Psalms Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#001)

[Psalms](../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#001), David praises God for
executing judgment; [Psa 9:11](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#011), He
incites others to praise him; [Psa
9:13](../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#013), He prays that he may have cause
to praise him.

Probably, "the death of the champion:" so the Chaldee has, "A Psalm of
David, to be sung concerning the death of the man who went out between
(mibbeyney) the camps;" evidently considering labben, of the same import
as bainayim, "a middle-man or champion," as Goliath is termed, [Sa1
17:4](../../../bib/kjv/sa1017.htm#004), concerning whose defeat this
psalm is generally supposed to have been composed.
