Overview

[Ch2 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#001), Asa succeeding, destroys
idolatry; [Ch2 14:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#006), Having peace, he
strengthens his kingdom with forts and armies; [Ch2
14:9](../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#009), Calling on God, he overthrows
Zerah, and spoils the Ethiopians.
