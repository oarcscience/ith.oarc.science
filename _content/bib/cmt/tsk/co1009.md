Overview

[Co1 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#001), He shows his liberty; [Co1
9:7](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#007), and that the minister ought to
live by the Gospel; [Co1 9:15](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#015), yet
that himself has of his own accord abstained, [Co1
9:18](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#018). to be either chargeable unto
them, [Co1 9:22](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#022). or offensive unto
any, in matters indifferent; [Co1
9:24](../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#024), Our life is like unto a race.
