Psalms Chapter 21

Psalms

psa 21:0

Overview

Psa 21:1, A thanksgiving for victory; Psa 21:7, with confidence of further success.

This is the people's επινικιον, or song of triumph, after the victory for which they prayed in the former Psalm.
