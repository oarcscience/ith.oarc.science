### [Job Chapter 26](../../../bib/kjv/job026.htm#001)

[Job](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">job 26:0</span></span>

Overview

[Job 26:1](../../../bib/kjv/job026.htm#001), Job, reproving the
uncharitable spirit of Bildad, [Job
26:5](../../../bib/kjv/job026.htm#005), acknowledges the power of God to
be infinite and unsearchable.

[Job 26:2](../../../bib/kjv/job026.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">job 26:2</span></span>

and therefore Job ironically admires the assistance which Bildad had
