Overview

[Th1 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#001), The Thessalonians are given
to understand both how mindful of them Saint Paul was at all time in
thanksgiving, and prayer; [Th1 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#005),
and also how well he was persuaded of the truth and sincerity of their
faith and conversion to God.
