48:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer048.htm#007), for their pride; [Jer
48:11](../../../bib/kjv/jer048.htm#011), for their security; [Jer

Moab: This prophecy against Moab, as well as the following ones
concerning Ammon, Edom, and the neighbouring countries, seem to have
been fulfilled during the long siege of Tyre by Nebuchadnezzar. Josephus
