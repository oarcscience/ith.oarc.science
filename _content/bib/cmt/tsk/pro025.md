24:26](../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#026); [Ecc
12:10](../../../bib/kjv/ecc012.htm#010); [Isa
50:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa050.htm#004)

in baskets (maskeeyoth, in Arabic, shakeekat), of silver." A word spoken
with propriety, opportunely, and suitably to the occasion, is as much in
its place, and as conspicuously beautiful, as the golden fruit which
appears through the apertures of an exquisitely wrought silver basket.
