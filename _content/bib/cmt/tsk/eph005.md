### [Ephesians Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#001)

[Ephesians](../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">eph 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Eph 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#001), After general exhortations
to love; [Eph 5:3](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#003), to flee
fornication; [Eph 5:4](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#004), and all
uncleanness; [Eph 5:7](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#007), not to converse
with the wicked; [Eph 5:15](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#015), to walk
warily; [Eph 5:18](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#018), and to be filled
with the Spirit; [Eph 5:22](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#022), he
descends to the particular duties, how wives ought to obey their
husbands; [Eph 5:25](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#025), and husbands
ought to love their wives, [Eph 5:32](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#032).
even as Christ does his church.
