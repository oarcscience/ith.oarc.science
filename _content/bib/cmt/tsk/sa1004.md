### [1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#001)

[1 Kings (1 Samuel)](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">sa1 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Sa1 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#001), The Israelites are overcome
by the Philistines at Ebenezer; [Sa1
4:3](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#003), They fetch the ark unto the
terror of the Philistines; [Sa1 4:10](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#010),
They are smitten again, the ark taken, and Hophni and Phinehas are
slain; [Sa1 4:12](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#012), Eli at the news,
falling backward, breaks his neck; [Sa1
4:19](../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#019), Phinehas's wife, discouraged in
her travail with I-chabod, dies.
