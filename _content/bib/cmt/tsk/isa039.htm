Isaiah Chapter 39

Isaiah

isa 39:0

Overview

Isa 39:1, Merodach-baladan, sending to visit Hezekiah because of the wonder, has notice of his treasures; Isa 39:3, Isaiah, understanding thereof, foretells the Babylonian captivity.
