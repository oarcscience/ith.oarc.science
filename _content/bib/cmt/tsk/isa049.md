<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">isa 49:0</span></span>

Overview

[Isa 49:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#001), Christ being sent to the
Jews, complains of them; [Isa 49:5](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#005), He
is sent to the Gentiles with gracious promises; [Isa
49:13](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#013), God's love is perpetual to his
church; [Isa 49:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#018), The ample
restoration of the church; [Isa 49:24](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#024),
The powerful deliverance out of captivity.
