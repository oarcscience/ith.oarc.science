### [2 Corinthians Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#001)

[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#001), Having shown the reason why
he came not to them, [Co2 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#006), he
requires them to forgive and to comfort that excommunicated person, [Co2
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#010). even as himself also upon his
true repentance had forgiven him; [Co2
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#012), declaring withal why he departed
from Troas to Macedonia, [Co2 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2002.htm#014).
and the happy success which God gave to his preaching in all places.
