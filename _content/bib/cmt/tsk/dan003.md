### [Daniel Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001)

[Daniel](../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">dan 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Dan 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001), Nebuchadnezzar dedicates a
golden image in Dura; [Dan 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#008),
Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego are accused for not worshipping the
image; [Dan 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#013), They being
threatened, make a good confession; [Dan
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#019), They are cast into the furnace,
[Dan 3:24](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#024). from which God delivers
them; [Dan 3:28](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#028), Nebuchadnezzar seeing
the miracle blesses God, and advances them.

3:13](../../../bib/kjv/est003.htm#013).

gunjvar.

the counsellors: Dethavraya counsellors, judges, from dath, in Persian
dad, law, and var, possessor or guardian.

the sheriffs: Tiphtaya probably the same as the Arabic Mufti or head
officer of law.
