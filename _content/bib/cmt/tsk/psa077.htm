Psalms 77:10

psa 77:10

This is: etc. Or, as Dr. Waterland renders, "This my affliction is a change of the right hand of the Most High," i.e., it proceeds from a change of God's conduct towards me. De Dieu renders, Precari, hoc meum est; mutare dextram Altissimì "To pray, this is my business. to change the right hand of the Most High." I can do nothing else than pray: God is the Ruler of events.

Mr. N. M. Berlin translates, Dolere meum hoc est: mutare est dextre Altissimi. "To grieve is my portion. to change (my condition) belongs to the right hand of the Most High." Psa 31:22, Psa 73:22, Psa 116:11; Job 42:3; Lam 3:18-23; Mar 9:24
