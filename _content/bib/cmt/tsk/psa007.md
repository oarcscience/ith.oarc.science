Overview

[Psa 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#001), David prays against the
malice of his enemies, professing his innocency; [Psa
7:10](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#010), By faith he sees his defence,
and the destruction of his enemies.

Shiggaion probably denotes a mournful song or, elegy, from the Arabic
shaga, to be anxious, sorrowful. [Hab
3:1](../../../bib/kjv/hab003.htm#001)

[Psalms 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa007.htm#001)

words: or, business, 2Sam. 16:1-23

Cush: Cush signifies black, an epithet, in all languages, when applied
to the mind, expressive of moral turpitude; and therefore probably here
applied to Shimei, denoting that he was a calumniator and villain.
