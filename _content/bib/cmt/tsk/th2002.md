Overview

[Th2 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#001), Paul urges them to continue
stedfast in the truth received; [Th2
2:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#003), shows that there shall be a
departure from the faith, [Th2 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#009),
and a discovery of Antichrist, before the day of the Lord come; [Th2
2:15](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#015), and thereupon repeats his former
exhortation, and prays for them.
