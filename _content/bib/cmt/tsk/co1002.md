Overview

[Co1 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1002.htm#001), He declares that his
preaching, though it bring not excellency of speech, or of human wisdom,
yet consists in the power of God; [Co1
2:6](../../../bib/kjv/co1002.htm#006), and so far excels the wisdom of
this world, and human sense, that the natural man cannot understand it.
