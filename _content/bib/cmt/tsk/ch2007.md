Overview

[Ch2 7:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2007.htm#001), God having given testimony
to Solomon's prayer by fire from heaven, and glory in the temple, the
people worship him; [Ch2 7:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch2007.htm#004),
Solomon's solemn sacrifice; [Ch2 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/ch2007.htm#008),
Solomon having kept the feast of tabernacles, and the feast of
dedication of the altar, dismisses the people; [Ch2
7:12](../../../bib/kjv/ch2007.htm#012), God appearing to Solomon, gives
him promises upon condition.
