Overview

[Ch1 20:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1020.htm#001), Rabbah is besieged by Joab,
spoiled by David, and the people thereof tortured; [Ch1
20:4](../../../bib/kjv/ch1020.htm#004), Three giants are slain in three
several overthrows of the Philistines.

and cut: Instead of wyyasar, "and he cut," the parallel passage is
wyyasem, "and he put them;" which is also the reading here of seven
manuscripts collated by Dr. Kennicott. Sawing asunder, etc., of human
beings, have no more place in the text, than they had in David's conduct
towards the Ammonites.
