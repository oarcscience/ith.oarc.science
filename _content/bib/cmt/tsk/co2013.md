Overview

[Co2 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#001), He threatens severity, and
the power of his apostleship, against obstinate sinners; [Co2
13:5](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#005), And, advising them to a trial of
their faith, [Co2 13:7](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#007), and to a
reformation of their sins before his coming, [Co2
13:11](../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#011). he concludes his epistle with a
general exhortation and a prayer.
