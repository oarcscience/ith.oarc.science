[Psalms 40:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 40:1</span></span>

am 2970, bc 1034 (Title), This psalm is supposed to have been composed
by David about the same time, and on the same occasion, as the two
preceding; with this difference, that here he magnifies God for have
obtained the mercy which he sought there. It also contains a remarkable
prophecy of the incarnation and sacrifice of Jesus Christ.
