Overview

[Gal 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#001), We were under the law till
Christ came, as the heir is under the guardian till he be of age; [Gal
4:5](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#005), But Christ freed us from the law;
[Gal 4:7](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#007), therefore we are servants no
longer to it; [Gal 4:14](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#014), He remembers
their good will to him, and his to them; [Gal
4:22](../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#022), and shows that we are the sons
of Abraham by the freewoman.
