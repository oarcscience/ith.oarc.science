### [2 Corinthians Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#001)

[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 6:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#001), That he has approved himself
a faithful minister of Christ by his exhortations, [Co2
6:3](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#003), and by integrity of life, [Co2
6:4](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#004), and by patient enduring all kinds
of affliction and disgrace for the gospel; [Co2
6:10](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#010), Of which he speaks the more
boldly amongst them because his heart is open to them, [Co2
6:13](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#013). and he expects the like
affection from them again; [Co2 6:14](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#014),
exhorting them to flee the society and pollutions of idolaters, as being
themselves temples of the living God.
