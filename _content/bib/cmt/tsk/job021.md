Overview

judgment of man he has reason to be grieved; [Job
21:7](../../../bib/kjv/job021.htm#007), Sometimes the wicked prosper,
though they despise God; [Job 21:16](../../../bib/kjv/job021.htm#016),
Sometimes their destruction is manifest; [Job
21:21](../../../bib/kjv/job021.htm#021), The happy and unhappy are alike
in death; [Job 21:27](../../../bib/kjv/job021.htm#027), The judgment of
the wicked is in another world.
