Overview

[Joh 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#001), Christ teaches Nicodemus the
necessity of regeneration, [Joh 3:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#014).
of faith in his death, [Joh 3:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016). the
great love of God towards the world, [Joh
3:18](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#018). and the condemnation for
unbelief; [Joh 3:22](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#022), Jesus baptizes in
Judea; [Joh 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#023), The baptism,
witness, and doctrine of John concerning Christ.
