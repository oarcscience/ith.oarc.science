Overview

[Act 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#001), Paul and Barnabas are
persecuted from Iconium; [Act 14:8](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#008), At
Lystra Paul heals a cripple, whereupon they are reputed as gods; [Act
14:19](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#019), Paul is stoned; [Act
14:21](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#021), They pass through divers
churches, confirming the disciples in faith and patience; [Act
14:26](../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#026), Returning to Antioch, they
report what God had done with them.
