stories: or, spheres, Heb. ascensions, Maaloth "upper chambers," which

reference here to the various systems which God has created in
illimitable space, transcending each other as the planets do in our
system.

denote the vault of heaven, or atmosphere, which God "hath founded, or
established, upon, or over, al the earth," and into which "he calleth
the waters of the sea, and poureth them out upon the face of the earth."
