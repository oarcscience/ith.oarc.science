### [Matthew Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#001)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 11:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#001), John sends his disciples to
Christ; [Mat 11:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#007), Christ's testimony
concerning John; [Mat 11:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#016), The
perverse judgment of the people; [Mat
11:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#020), Christ upbraids Chorazin,
Bethsaida, and Capernaum; [Mat 11:25](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#025),
and praising his Father's wisdom in revealing the Gospel to the simple,
[Mat 11:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#028). he calls to him all such
as feel the burden of their sins.
