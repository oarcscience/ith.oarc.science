### [Matthew Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#001)

[Matthew](../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">mat 9:0</span></span>

Overview

[Mat 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#001), Christ cures one sick of the
palsy; [Mat 9:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#009), calls Matthew from
the receipt of custom; [Mat 9:10](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#010), eats
with publicans and sinners; [Mat 9:14](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#014),
defends his disciples for not fasting; [Mat
9:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#020), cures the bloody issue; [Mat
9:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#023), raises from death Jairus'
daughter; [Mat 9:27](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#027), gives sight to
two blind men; [Mat 9:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#032), heals a dumb
man possessed of a devil; [Mat 9:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#036),
and has compassion on the multitude.
