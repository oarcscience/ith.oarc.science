Overview

[Th2 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#001), Paul craves their prayers
for himself; [Th2 3:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#003), testifies what
confidence he has in them; [Th2 3:5](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#005),
makes request to God in their behalf; [Th2
3:6](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#006), gives them divers precepts,
especially to shun idleness, and ill company; [Th2
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/th2003.htm#016), and then concludes with prayer
and salutation.
