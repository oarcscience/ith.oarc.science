Genesis Chapter 22

Genesis

gen 22:0

Overview

Gen 22:1, Abraham is tempted to offer Isaac; Gen 22:3, He gives proof of his faith and obedience; Gen 22:11, The angel prevents him; Gen 22:13, Isaac is exchanged for a ram; Gen 22:14, The place is called Jehovah-jireh; Gen 22:15, Abraham is again blessed; Gen 22:20, The generations of Nahor unto Rebekah.
