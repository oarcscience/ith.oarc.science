Psalms Chapter 17

Psalms

psa 17:0

Overview

Psa 17:1, David, in confidence of his integrity, craves defence of God against his enemies; Psa 17:10, He shews their pride, craft, and eagerness; Psa 17:13, He prays against them in confidence of his hope.
