Overview

[Tit 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#001), Titus is yet further
directed by Paul, both concerning the things that he should teach and
not teach; [Tit 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#010), He is to reject
obstinate heretics; [Tit 3:12](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#012), He
appoints him time and place wherein he should come unto him.
