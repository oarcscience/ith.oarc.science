gourd: or, palmcrist, Heb. Kikajon, קיקיון \[Strong's H7021\], probably
the palma Christi, called kiki or kouki by the Egyptians, and Elkherod
by the Arabs, from which caster oil is extracted. It is as large as the
olive tree, has leaves like those of a vine, sometimes as broad as the
brim of a hat, and is of very quick growth.
