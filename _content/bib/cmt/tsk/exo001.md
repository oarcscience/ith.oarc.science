### [Exodus Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#001)

Overview

[Exo 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#001), The children of Israel,
after Joseph's death, increase; [Exo
1:8](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#008), The more they are oppressed by a
new king, the more they multiply; [Exo
1:15](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#015), The godliness of the midwives,
in saving the men children alive; [Exo
1:22](../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#022), Pharaoh commands the male
children to be cast into the river.
