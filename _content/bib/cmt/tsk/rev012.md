Overview

[Rev 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#001), A woman clothed with the
sun travails; [Rev 12:4](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#004), The great red
dragon stands before her, ready to devour her child; [Rev
12:6](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#006), when she is delivered she flees
into the wilderness; [Rev 12:7](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#007),
Michael and his angels fight with the dragon, and prevail; [Rev
12:13](../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#013), The dragon, being cast down
into the earth, persecutes the woman.
