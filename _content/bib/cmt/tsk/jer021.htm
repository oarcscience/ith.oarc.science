Jeremiah Chapter 21

Jeremiah

jer 21:0

Overview

Jer 21:1, Zedekiah sends to Jeremiah to enquire the event of Nebuchadnezzar's war; Jer 21:3, Jeremiah foretells a hard seige and miserable captivity; Jer 21:8, He counsels the people to fall to the Chaldeans; Jer 21:11, and upbraids the King's house.

Jeremiah 21:1

jer 21:1

am cir, 3415, bc cir, 589

The word: This discourse was delivered about the ninth year of the reign of Zedekiah. This chapter, observes Dr. Blayney, contains the first of those prophecies which were delivered by Jeremiah subsequent to the revolt of Zedekiah, and the breaking out of the war thereupon; and which are continued on to the taking of Jerusalem, related in Jer 39:1, in the following order.
