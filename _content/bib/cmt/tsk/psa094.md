<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">psa 94:0</span></span>

Overview

[Psa 94:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#001), The prophet, calling for
justice, complains of tyranny and impiety; [Psa
94:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#008), He teaches God's providence;
[Psa 94:12](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#012), He shews the blessedness
of affliction; [Psa 94:16](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#016), God is the
defender of the afflicted.

[Psalms 94:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 94:1</span></span>

am 3416, bc 588 (Title), Dr. Delaney supposes that this Psalm was
written by David on occasion of his war with the Ammonites, in
consequence of the indignities shewn to his messengers; but it is more
probable that it was written to bewail the destruction of Jerusalem and
the temple.
