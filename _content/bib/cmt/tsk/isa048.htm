Isaiah Chapter 48

Isaiah

isa 48:0

Overview

Isa 48:1, God, to convince the people of their foreknown obstinancy, revealed his prophecies; Isa 48:9, He saves them for his own sake; Isa 48:12, He exhorts them to obedience, because of his power and providence; Isa 48:16, He laments their backwardness; Isa 48:20, He powerfully delivers his people out of Babylon.
