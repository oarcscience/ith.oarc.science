God, [Job 23:6](../../../bib/kjv/job023.htm#006), in confidence of his

23:11](../../../bib/kjv/job023.htm#011), Job's innocency; [Job
23:13](../../../bib/kjv/job023.htm#013), God's decree is immutable.
