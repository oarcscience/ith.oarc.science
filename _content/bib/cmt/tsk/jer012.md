### [Jeremiah Chapter 12](../../../bib/kjv/jer012.htm#001)

[Jeremiah](../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jer 12:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jer 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer012.htm#001), Jeremiah, complaining of
the wicked's prosperity, by faith sees their ruin; [Jer
12:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer012.htm#005), God admonishes him of his
brethren's treachery against him; [Jer
12:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer012.htm#007), and laments his heritage; [Jer
12:14](../../../bib/kjv/jer012.htm#014), He promises to the penitent
return from captivity.

(people) to me as a fierce hyena? Is there a wild beast all around upon
her?" i.e., the land of Canaan. The hyena is a kind of wolf, a little
bigger than a mastiff; colour grey, streaked with black. it is of a
solitary and savage disposition.
