Overview

[Psa 124:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa124.htm#001), The church blesses God for
a miraculous deliverance.

It is uncertain what the particular deliverance was which is celebrated
in this Psalm. Some refer it to the deliverance of Hezekiah from
Sennacherib; and others to the return from the Babylonian captivity;
while Dr. A. Clarke refers it to that of the Jews from the massacre
intended by Haman. [Psa 120:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#001), [Psa
