### [Hosea Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/hos004.htm#001)

[Hosea](../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">hos 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Hos 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/hos004.htm#001), God denounces judgments on
Israel, for their aggravated impieties and iniquities; [Hos
4:12](../../../bib/kjv/hos004.htm#012), He exposes the ignorance and
wickedness of the priests, and profligacy of the people, he will leave
their wives and daughters to commit lewdness, without present
punishment; [Hos 4:15](../../../bib/kjv/hos004.htm#015), He warns Judah,
not to imitate Israel's crimes, which are still further reproved.
