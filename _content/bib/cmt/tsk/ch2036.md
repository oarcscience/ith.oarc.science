<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">ch2 36:0</span></span>

Overview

[Ch2 36:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#001), Jehoahaz succeeding, is
deposed by Pharaoh, and carried into Egypt; [Ch2
36:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#005), Jehoiakim reigning ill, is
carried bound into Babylon; [Ch2 36:9](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#009),
Jehoiachin succeeding, reigns ill, and is brought into Babylon; [Ch2
36:11](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#011), Zedekiah succeeding, reigns
ill, despises the prophets, and rebels against Nebuchadnezzar; [Ch2
36:14](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#014), Jerusalem, for the sins of the
priests and the people, is wholly destroyed; [Ch2
36:22](../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#022), The proclamation of Cyrus.
