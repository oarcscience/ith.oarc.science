Overview

[Eph 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#001), The hidden mystery that the
Gentiles should be saved was made known to Paul by revelation; [Eph
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#008), and to him was that grace given,
that he should preach it; [Eph 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#013),
He desires them not to faint for his tribulation; [Eph
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#014), and prays that they may perceive
the great love of Christ toward them.
