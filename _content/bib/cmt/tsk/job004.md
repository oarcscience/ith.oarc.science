### [Job Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/job004.htm#001)

[Job](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">job 4:0</span></span>

Overview

[Job 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/job004.htm#001), Eliphaz reproves Job for
want of religion; [Job 4:7](../../../bib/kjv/job004.htm#007), He teaches
God's judgments to be not for the righteous, but for the wicked; [Job
4:12](../../../bib/kjv/job004.htm#012), His fearful vision to humble the
excellency of creatures before God.
