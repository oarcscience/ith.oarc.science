<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">psa 80:13</span></span>

The boar: This wild boar, chazir, is the parent stock of our domestic
hog. He is much smaller, but stronger, and more undaunted, colour, an
iron grey inclining to black; snout, longer than that of the common
breed. ears comparatively short; tusks, very formidable; and habits,
fierce and savage. He is particularly destructive to corn-fields and
vineyards. 2Kings 18:1-19:37, 24:1-25:30; 2Chr. 32:1-33, 36:1-23; [Jer
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#007), [Jer
39:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001); [Jer
51:34](../../../bib/kjv/jer051.htm#034), [Jer
52:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#007), [Jer
52:12-14](../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#012)
