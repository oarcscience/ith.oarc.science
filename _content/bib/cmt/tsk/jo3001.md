<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">jo3 1:0</span></span>

Overview

[Jo3 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#001), He commends Gaius for his
piety, [Jo3 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#005), and hospitality, [Jo3
1:7](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#007), to true preachers; [Jo3
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#009), complaining of the unkind dealing
of ambitious Diotrephes on the contrary side; [Jo3
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#011), whose evil example is not to be
followed; [Jo3 1:12](../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#012), and gives special
testimony to the good report of Demetrius.
