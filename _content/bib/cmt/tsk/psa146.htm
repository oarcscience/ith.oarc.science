Psalms Chapter 146

Psalms

psa 146:0

Overview

Psa 146:1, The Psalmist vows perpetual praises to God; Psa 146:3, He exhorts not to trust in man; Psa 146:5, God, for his power, justice, mercy, and kingdom, is only worthy to be trusted.
