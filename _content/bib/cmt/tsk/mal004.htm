Malachi Chapter 4

Malachi

mal 4:0

Overview

Mal 4:1, God's judgment on the wicked; Mal 4:2, and his blessing on the good; Mal 4:4, He exhorts to the study of the law; Mal 4:5, and tells of Elijah's coming and office.
