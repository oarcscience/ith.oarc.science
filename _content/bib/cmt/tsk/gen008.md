### [Genesis Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#001)

[Genesis](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">gen 8:0</span></span>

Overview

[Gen 8:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#001), God remembers Noah, and
assuages the waters; [Gen 8:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#004), The ark
rests on Ararat; [Gen 8:6](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#006), Noah sends
forth a raven and then a dove; [Gen
8:13](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#013), Noah, being commanded, goes
forth from the ark; [Gen 8:20](../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#020), He
builds an altar, and offers sacrifice, which God accepts, and promises
to curse the earth no more.
