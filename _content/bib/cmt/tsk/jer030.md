return of the Jews; [Jer 30:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#004), After

30:10](../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#010), He comforts Jacob; [Jer
30:18](../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#018), Their return shall be gracious;
[Jer 30:23](../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#023), Wrath shall fall on the
wicked.
