### [1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 31](../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001)

[1 Kings (1 Samuel)](../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">sa1 31:0</span></span>

Overview

[Sa1 31:1](../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001), Saul, having lost his army,
and his sons slain, he and his armour-bearer kill themselves; [Sa1
31:7](../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#007), The Philistines possess the
forsaken towns of the Israelites; [Sa1
31:8](../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#008), They triumph over the dead
carcases; [Sa1 31:11](../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#011), They of
Jabesh-gilead recovering the dead bodies by night, burn them at Jabesh,
and mournfully bury their bones.
