Overview

[Pe1 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#001), He teaches the duty of wives
and husbands to each other; [Pe1 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#008),
exhorting all men to unity and love; [Pe1
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#014), and to suffer persecution; [Pe1
3:19](../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#019), He declares also the benefits of
Christ toward the old world.
