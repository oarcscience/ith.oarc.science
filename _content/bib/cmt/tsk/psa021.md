Overview

[Psa 21:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#001), A thanksgiving for victory;
[Psa 21:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#007), with confidence of further
success.

This is the people's επινικιον, or song of triumph, after the victory
for which they prayed in the former Psalm.
