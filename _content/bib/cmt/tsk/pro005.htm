Proverbs

pro 5:0

Overview

Pro 5:1, Solomon exhorts to wisdom; Pro 5:3, He shews the mischief of whoredom and riot; Pro 5:15, He exhorts to contentedness, liberality, and chastity; Pro 5:22, The wicked are overtaken with their own sins.
