Overview

[Job 17:1](../../../bib/kjv/job017.htm#001), Job appeals from men to
God; [Job 17:6](../../../bib/kjv/job017.htm#006), The unmerciful dealing
of men with the afflicted may astonish, but not discourage the
righteous; [Job 17:11](../../../bib/kjv/job017.htm#011), His hope is not
in life, but in death.
