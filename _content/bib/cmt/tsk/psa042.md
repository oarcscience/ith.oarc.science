Korah." It is generally supposed to have been written by David when
driven from Jerusalem and beyond Jordan, by Absalom's rebellion.

waterspouts: A water-spout is a large tube formed of clouds by means of
the electric fluid, the base being uppermost, and the point let down
perpendicularly form the clouds. It has a particular kind of circular
motion at the point; and, being hollow within, attracts vast quantities
of water, which it frequently pours down in torrents upon the earth.
These spouts are frequent on the coast of Syria; and no doubt the
Psalmist had often seen them, and the ravages which they made.
