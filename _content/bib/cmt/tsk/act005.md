### [Acts Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#001)

[Acts](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">act 5:0</span></span>

Overview

[Act 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#001), After that Ananias and
Sapphira his wife for their hypocrisy, at Peter's rebuke had fallen down
dead; [Act 5:12](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#012), and that the rest of
the apostles had wrought many miracles; [Act
5:14](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#014), to the increase of the faith;
[Act 5:17](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#017), the apostles are again
imprisoned; [Act 5:19](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#019), but delivered
by an angel bidding them preach openly to all; [Act
5:21](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#021), when, after their teaching
accordingly in the temple, [Act 5:29](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#029).
and before the council, [Act 5:33](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#033).
they are in danger to be killed; but through the advice of Gamaliel, a
great counsellor among the Jews, they are kept alive, and are only
beaten; [Act 5:41](../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#041), for which they
glorify God, and cease no day from preaching.
