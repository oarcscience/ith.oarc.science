<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">th1 3:0</span></span>

Overview

[Th1 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/th1003.htm#001), Saint Paul testifies his
great love to the Thessalonians, partly by sending Timothy unto them to
strengthen and comfort them; partly by rejoicing in their well-doing;
[Th1 3:10](../../../bib/kjv/th1003.htm#010), and partly by praying for
them, and desiring a safe coming unto them.
