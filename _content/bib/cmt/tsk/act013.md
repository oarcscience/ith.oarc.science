Overview

[Act 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#001), Paul and Barnabas are
chosen to go to the Gentiles; [Act
13:6](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#006), Of Sergius Paulus, and Elymas
the sorcerer; [Act 13:13](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#013), Paul
preaches at Antioch that Jesus is Christ; [Act
13:42](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#042), The Gentiles believe; [Act
13:44](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#044), but the Jews gainsay and
blaspheme, whereupon they turn to the Gentiles, of whom many believe;
[Act 13:50](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#050), The Jews raise a
persecution against Paul and Barnabas, who go to Iconium.
