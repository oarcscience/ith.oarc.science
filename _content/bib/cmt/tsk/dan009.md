Overview

[Dan 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#001), Daniel, considering the time
of the captivity, [Dan 9:3](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#003), makes
confession of sins, [Dan 9:16](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#016). and
prays for the restoration of Jerusalem; [Dan
9:20](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#020), Gabriel informs him of the
seventy weeks.
