### [Luke Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#001)

[Luke](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">luk 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Luk 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#001), Christ sends out at once
seventy disciples to work miracles, and to preach; [Luk
10:13](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#013), pronounces a woe against
certain cities; [Luk 10:17](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#017), The
seventy return with joy; he shows them wherein to rejoice, and thanks
his Father for his grace; [Luk 10:23](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#023),
magnifies the happy estate of his church; [Luk
10:25](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#025), teaches the lawyer how to
attain eternal life, and to take every one for his neighbour that needs
his mercy; [Luk 10:38](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#038), reprehends
Martha, and commends Mary her sister.
