### [2 Corinthians Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#001)

[2 Corinthians](../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">co2 10:0</span></span>

Overview

[Co2 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#001), Against the false apostles,
who disgraced the weakness of his person and bodily presence, he shows
the spiritual might and authority with which he was armed against all
adverse powers; [Co2 10:7](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#007), assuring
them that at his coming he will be found as mighty in word as he is now
in writing, being absent; [Co2 10:12](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#012),
and withal taxing them for reaching out themselves beyond their compass,
and vaunting themselves of other men's labours.
