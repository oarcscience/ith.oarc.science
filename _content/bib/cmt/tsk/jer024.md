Overview

[Jer 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#001), Under the type of good and
bad figs, [Jer 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#004), he foreshews the
restoration of them that were in captivity; [Jer
24:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#008), and the desolation of Zedekiah
and the rest.
