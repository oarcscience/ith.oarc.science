Overview

[Pe2 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#001), He foretells them of false
teachers, shewing the impiety and punishment both of them and their
followers; [Pe2 2:7](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#007), from which the
godly shall be delivered, as Lot was out of Sodom; [Pe2
2:10](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#010), and more fully describes the
manners of those profane and blasphemous seducers, whereby they may be
the better known, and avoided.
