Overview

[Co1 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#001), He reproves them, because
in holy assemblies, [Co1 11:4](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#004), their

uncovered; [Co1 11:17](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#017), and because
generally their meetings were not for the better, but for the worse;
[Co1 11:21](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#021), as, namely, in profaning
with their own feast the Lord's supper; [Co1
11:25](../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#025), Lastly, he calls them to the
first institution thereof.
