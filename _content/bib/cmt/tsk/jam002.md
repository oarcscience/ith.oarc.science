the law has broken the yoke, dissolved the covenant, and exposed the law
to contempt; and so has he done who has only broken one precept;" they
also taught, "that he who observed any principal command was equal to
him who kept the whole law," and gave for an example the forsaking of
idolatry. To correct this false doctrine was the object James had in
view.
