[Job](../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_000">job 2:0</span></span>

Overview

[Job 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/job002.htm#001), Satan appearing again before
God, obtains further leave to tempt Job; [Job
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/job002.htm#007), He smites him with sore boils;
[Job 2:9](../../../bib/kjv/job002.htm#009), Job reproves his wife, who
moved him to curse God; [Job 2:11](../../../bib/kjv/job002.htm#011), His
three friends console with him in silence.
