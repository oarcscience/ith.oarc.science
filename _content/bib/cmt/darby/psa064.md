### [Psalms Chapter 64](../../../bib/kjv/psa064.htm#001)

Psalm 64 chiefly speaks of the unceasing crafty hatred of the enemy and
cries to God: God will shoot at them suddenly. The result of this
judgment will be that all shall fear and declare the work of God, for
they shall wisely consider of His doing. Then (for judgment is now come)
the righteous shall be glad in Jehovah, for His covenant name is now
taken, the judgment having removed the power of evil. The upright in
heart glory. Thus judgment introduces the millennium.
