### [Psalms Chapter 127](../../../bib/kjv/psa127.htm#001)

These full blessings thus sought, the building the house, the keeping
the city, the desired abundance of children, are all (Psalm 127)
Jehovah's doing and gift, or man labors and watches in vain. The
blessing is distinctly Jewish.
