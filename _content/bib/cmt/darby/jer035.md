### [Jeremiah Chapter 35](../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#001)

The obedience of the Rechabites is set forth in order to shew out more
clearly the sin of Judah-disobedient in spite of the remonstrances and
the patience of God. God does not forget the obedience that glorifies
His name. The family of the Rechabites shall never fail.
