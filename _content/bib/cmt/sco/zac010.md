### [Zechariah Chapter 10](../../../bib/kjv/zac010.htm#001)

[Zechariah 10:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac010.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 10:1</span></span>

later rain

Compare ([Hos 6:3](../../../bib/kjv/hos006.htm#003)); ([Joe
2:23-32](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#023)); ([Zac
12:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#010)).

There is both a physical and spiritual meaning: Rain as of old will be
restored to Palestine, but, also, there will be a mighty effusion of the
Spirit upon restored Israel.

[Zechariah 10:4](../../../bib/kjv/zac010.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">zac 10:4</span></span>

came

The tense is future: "From him Judah shall be the cornerstone ([Exo
17:6](../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#006)).

(See Scofield) - ([Pe1 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#008)).

from him the nail ([Isa 22:23](../../../bib/kjv/isa022.htm#023)); ([Isa
22:24](../../../bib/kjv/isa022.htm#024)) from him the battle-bow," etc.
The whole scene is of the events which group about the deliverance of
the Jews in Palestine in the time of the northern invasion under the
"Beast"; ([Dan 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008)); ([Rev
19:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020)) and "Armageddon,"; ([Rev
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)); ([Rev
19:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017)).

The final deliverance is wholly effected by the return of the Lord ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)); but previously He
strengthens the hard-pressed Israelites; ([Mic
4:13](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#013)); ([Zac
9:13-15](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#013)); ([Zac
10:5-7](../../../bib/kjv/zac010.htm#005)); ([Zac
12:2-6](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#002)); ([Zac
14:14](../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#014)).

That there may have been a precursive fulfilment in the Maccabean
victories can neither be affirmed nor denied from Scripture.
