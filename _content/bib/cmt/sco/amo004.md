### [Amos Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#001)

[Amos 4:4](../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">amo 4:4</span></span>

Beth-el

Compare ([Kg1 12:25-33](../../../bib/kjv/kg1012.htm#025)).

Any altar at Beth-el, after the establishment of Jehovah's worship at
Jerusalem was of necessity divisive and schismatic. ([Deu
12:4-14](../../../bib/kjv/deu012.htm#004)).

Compare ([Joh 4:21-24](../../../bib/kjv/joh004.htm#021)); ([Mat
18:20](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#020)); ([Heb
13:10-14](../../../bib/kjv/heb013.htm#010)).
