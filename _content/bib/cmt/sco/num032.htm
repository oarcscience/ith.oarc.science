Numbers Chapter 32

Numbers 32:1

num 32:1

The Reubenites

The Reubenites, Gadites, and half-tribe of Manasseh, who chose their inheritance just outside the land, are types of world-borderers -- carnal Christians. What their descendants were when Messiah came is seen in (Mar 5:1-17).
