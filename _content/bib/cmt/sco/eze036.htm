Ezekiel Chapter 36

Ezekiel 36:1

eze 36:1

Chapter 36)

A beautiful order is discernible in this and the succeeding prophecies:

(1) Restoration of the land (Eze 36:1-15).

(2) of the people (Eze 36:16-37); (Eze 36:28).

(3) judgment on Israel's enemies. (Eze 38:1-39); (Eze 38:24). Afterward follows that which concerns the worship of Jehovah that He may dwell amongst His people.
