### [Exodus Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/exo015.htm#001)

[Exodus 15:13](../../../bib/kjv/exo015.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">exo 15:13</span></span>

redeemed

Hebrew, "goel," Redemption (Kinsman type). ([Isa
59:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020)).

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 59:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020)).

[Exodus 15:25](../../../bib/kjv/exo015.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">exo 15:25</span></span>

when he had cast into the waters

These bitter waters were in the very path of the Lord's leading, and
stand for the trials of God's people, which are educatory and not
punitive. The "tree" is the cross ([Gal
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#013)) which became sweet to Christ as
the expression of the Father's will ([Joh
18:11](../../../bib/kjv/joh018.htm#011)). When our Marahs are so taken
we cast the "tree" into the waters. ([Rom
5:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#003)); ([Rom
5:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#004)).
