[Nehemiah 9:14](../../../bib/kjv/neh009.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">neh 9:14</span></span>

madest known unto them

This important passage fixes beyond all cavil the time when the sabbath,
God's rest ([Gen 2:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#001)) was given to
man.

Compare ([Exo 20:9-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#009)).

f In ([Exo 31:13-17](../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#013)) the sabbath is
invested with the character of a sign between Jehovah and Israel.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#001)).
