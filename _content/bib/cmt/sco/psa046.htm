Psalms Chapter 46

Alamoth

Alamoth, "soprano," from almah, a virgin. Some have thought the alamoth, "virgins," were a temple choir, singing antiphonally to the sheminith, or male choir.

(See Scofield) - (Psa 6:1).

But contr, see (Ch1 15:20).
