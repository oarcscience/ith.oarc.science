1 Corinthians 6:11

justified

Justification. (Gal 2:16); (Luk 18:14); (Rom 3:28).

1 Corinthians 6:15

co1 6:15

shall I

Paul does not invoke the authority of the seventh commandment, but appeals to the believer's sacredness as a member of Christ.
