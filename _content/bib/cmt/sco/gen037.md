### [Genesis Chapter 37](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#001)

[Genesis 37:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">gen 37:2</span></span>

Joseph

While it is nowhere asserted that Joseph was a type of Christ, the
analogies are too numerous to be accidental. They are:

\(1\) both were especial objects of a father's love ([Gen
37:3](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#003)); ([Mat
3:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#017)); ([Joh
3:35](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#035)); ([Joh
5:20](../../../bib/kjv/joh005.htm#020)).

\(2\) both were hated by their brethren ([Gen
37:4](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#004)); ([Joh
15:25](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#025)).

\(3\) the superior claims of both were rejected by their brethren ([Gen
37:8](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#008)); ([Mat
21:37-39](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#037)); ([Joh
15:24](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#024)); ([Joh
15:25](../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#025)).

\(4\) the brethren of both conspired against them to slay them ([Gen
37:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#018)); ([Mat
26:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#003)); ([Mat
26:4](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#004)).

\(5\) Joseph was, in intent and figure, slain by his brethren, as was
Christ ([Gen 37:24](../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#024)); ([Mat
27:35-37](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#035)).

\(6\) each became a blessing among the Gentiles, and gained a Gentile
bride ([Gen 41:1-45](../../../bib/kjv/gen041.htm#001)); ([Act
15:14](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)); ([Eph
5:25-32](../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#025)).

\(7\) as Joseph reconciled his brethren to himself, and afterward
exalted them, so will it be with Christ and His Jewish brethren ([Gen
45:1-15](../../../bib/kjv/gen045.htm#001)); ([Deu
30:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001)); ([Hos
2:14-18](../../../bib/kjv/hos002.htm#014)); ([Rom
11:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#001)); ([Rom
11:15](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#015)); ([Rom
11:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025)); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)).
