### [Mark Chapter 7](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#001)

[Mark 7:5](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">mar 7:5</span></span>

tradition

That is, The so-called "oral law" alleged to have been handed down from
Moses; really a traditional interpretation of the written law.

See margin reference, (See Scofield) - ([Mar
7:7](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#007)).

[Mark 7:7](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">mar 7:7</span></span>

for doctrines

as authoritative the precepts of men.

Compare ([Mar 7:5](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#005)).

See "Pharisees" ([Mat 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007)).

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007)).

Compare ([Col 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#008)); ([Col
2:16](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#016)); ([Col
2:18](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#018)); ([Col
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#020)); ([Col
2:23](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#023)).

[Mark 7:11](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">mar 7:11</span></span>

a gift

Or, I have dedicated to God that which would relieve your need; ([Mar
7:12](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#012)). No longer do you permit him to
use it for his father or mother.

Compare ([Mat 15:5](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#005)); ([Mat
15:6](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#006)).

[Mark 7:19](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">mar 7:19</span></span>

Because it entereth

Because it does not enter into the heart of him, but into the bowels is
passed -- purifying all the food.

[Mark 7:26](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">mar 7:26</span></span>

Greek

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 15:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#021)).

[Mark 7:28](../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">mar 7:28</span></span>

And she

She, however, answered, saying, "True, Lord! And yet the little dogs
under the table eat from the children's crumbs." ([Rom
11:24](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#024)); ([Eph
2:11-22](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#011)).
