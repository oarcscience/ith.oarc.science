### [Psalms Chapter 19](../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">psa 19:9</span></span>

fear of the Lord

The "fear of the Lord," a phrase of the Old Testament piety, meaning
reverential trust, with hatred of evil.
