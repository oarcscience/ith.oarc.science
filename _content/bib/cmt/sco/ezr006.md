### [Ezra Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/ezr006.htm#001)

[Ezra 6:6](../../../bib/kjv/ezr006.htm#006)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_006">ezr 6:6</span></span>

your companions

Chaldean, "their societies."

[Ezra 6:14](../../../bib/kjv/ezr006.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">ezr 6:14</span></span>

finished it

The worship of Jehovah was thus re-established in Jerusalem, but the
theocracy was not restored. The remnant which returned from the
Babylonian captivity lived in the land by Gentile sufferance, though
doubtless by the providential care of Jehovah, till Messiah came, and
was crucified by soldiers of the fourth Gentile world-empire Rome, ([Dan
2:40](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#040)); ([Dan
7:7](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#007)). Soon after (A.D. 70) Rome
destroyed the city and temple. See "Times of the Gentiles"; ([Luk
21:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024)); ([Rev
16:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#019)).

[Ezra 6:15](../../../bib/kjv/ezr006.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">ezr 6:15</span></span>

Adar

twelfth monththat is, March.

[Ezra 6:19](../../../bib/kjv/ezr006.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">ezr 6:19</span></span>

first month

That is, April.
