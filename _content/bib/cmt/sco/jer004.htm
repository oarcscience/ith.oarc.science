Jeremiah Chapter 4

Jeremiah 4:23

jer 4:23

without form and void

Compare (Gen 1:2).

"Without form and void" describes the condition of the earth as the result of judgment; (Jer 4:24-26); (Isa 24:1) which overthrew the primal order of (Gen 1:1).
