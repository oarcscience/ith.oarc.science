### [Matthew Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#001)

[Matthew 22:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">mat 22:2</span></span>

kingdom

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002)).

[Matthew 22:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">mat 22:7</span></span>

burned

Fulfilled as to Jerusalem; A.D. 70. ([Luk
21:20-24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020)).

[Matthew 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">mat 22:9</span></span>

go ye

The world-wide call. ([Mat 28:16-20](../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#016));
([Rev 22:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#017)).

[Matthew 22:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">mat 22:15</span></span>

Then went

In the different classes, ([Mat
23:15-40](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#015)). Jesus meets representatives
of all Israel, Pharisees, Sadducees, Herodians.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007)).

For them, silenced but unrepentant, no message is left but "woe." ([Mat
23:1-39](../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#001)).

[Matthew 22:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#029)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_029">mat 22:29</span></span>

ye do err

Or, ye deceive yourselves, etc. Jesus' answer gives the three
incapacities of the rationalist: self-deception, ([Rom
1:21-22](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#021)); ignorance of the spiritual
content of Scripture, ([Act 13:27](../../../bib/kjv/act013.htm#027));
disbelief in the intervention of divine power. ([Pe2
3:5-9](../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#005)).

[Matthew 22:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#030)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_030">mat 22:30</span></span>

angels

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 1:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004)).

[Matthew 22:35](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#035)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_035">mat 22:35</span></span>

lawyer

(Greek, "nomikos", "of the law"); occurs also ([Luk
7:30](../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#030)); ([Luk
10:25](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#025)); ([Luk
11:45](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#045)); ([Luk
11:46](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#046)); ([Luk
11:52](../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#052)); ([Luk
14:3](../../../bib/kjv/luk014.htm#003)); ([Tit
3:13](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#013)). Except in the last instance,
"lawyer" is another name for "scribe."

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#004)).

In ([Tit 3:13](../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#013)), the term has the
modern meaning.

[Matthew 22:41](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#041)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_041">mat 22:41</span></span>

asked them

Compare ([Mat 21:24](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#024)); ([Joh
19:7](../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#007)).

Jesus' question is not personal but doctrinal: "Whose son is the
Messiah?"

Compare ([Act 2:25-36](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#025)); ([Rom
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003)); ([Rom
1:4](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#004)).
