[Isaiah 30:27](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">isa 30:27</span></span>

Behold

The imagery of ([Isa 30:27](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#027)); ([Isa
30:28](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#028)) is cumulative. Judah is making
an alliance with Egypt when she might be in league with Him whose
judgment upon the world-powers will be like a terrible thunder-tempest
(v.27), turning streams into torrents neck-deep (v. 28, f.c.); who will
sift the nations in their own sieve of vanity (or "destruction"), and
put His bridle into the jaws of the peoples.

[Isaiah 30:33](../../../bib/kjv/isa030.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">isa 30:33</span></span>

Tophet

See ([Jer 7:31](../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#031)); ([Jer
19:6](../../../bib/kjv/jer019.htm#006)); ([Kg2
23:10](../../../bib/kjv/kg2023.htm#010)).

Historically a place in the valley of Hinnom where human sacrifices were
offered. The word means "place of fire." The symbolic reference is to
the lake of fire and the doom of the Beast. ([Rev
19:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020)).
