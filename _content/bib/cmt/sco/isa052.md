### [Isaiah Chapter 52](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#001)

[Isaiah 52:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">isa 52:3</span></span>

redeemed

Hebrew, "goel," Redemption (Kinsman type).

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 59:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020)).

[Isaiah 52:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">isa 52:12</span></span>

For ye shall

Contrast ([Exo 12:33](../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#033)); ([Exo
12:39](../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#039)).

[Isaiah 52:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">isa 52:14</span></span>

so marred

The literal rendering is terrible: "So marred from the form of man was
His aspect that His appearance was not that of a son of man" -- that is,
not human -- the effect of the brutalities described in ([Mat
26:67](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#067)); ([Mat
26:68](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#068)); ([Mat
27:27-30](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#027)).
