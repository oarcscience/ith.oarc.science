Joshua Chapter 6

Joshua 6:5

jos 6:5

And it shall come to pass

The central truth here is that spiritual victories are won by means and upon principles utterly foolish and inadequate in the view of human wisdom. (Co1 1:17-29); (Co2 10:3-5).
