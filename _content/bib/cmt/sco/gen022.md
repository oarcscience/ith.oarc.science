### [Genesis Chapter 22](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001)

[Genesis 22:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">gen 22:1</span></span>

And it came to pass

The spiritual experience of Abraham was marked by four great crises,
each of which involved a surrender of something naturally most dear.
These were:

\(1\) Country and kindred ([Gen 12:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#001));
([Mat 10:34-39](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#034)); ([Co2
6:14-18](../../../bib/kjv/co2006.htm#014)).

\(2\) His nephew, Lot; especially dear to Abraham by nature, as a
possible heir and as a fellow believer ([Pe2
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#007)); ([Pe2
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#008)); ([Gen
13:1-18](../../../bib/kjv/gen013.htm#001)). The completeness of
Abraham's separation from one who, though a believer, was a "vessel unto
dishonour," is shown by; ([Gen
15:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#001)); ([Ti2
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#020)); ([Ti2
2:21](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#021)); ([Act
15:36-40](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#036)).

\(3\) His own plan about Ishmael ([Gen
17:17](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#017)); ([Gen
17:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen017.htm#018)); ([Ch1
13:1-14](../../../bib/kjv/ch1013.htm#001)); ([Ch1
15:1-2](../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#001)).

\(4\) Isaac, "thy son, thine only son Isaac, whom thou lovest" ([Gen
22:1-19](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001)); ([Heb
11:17](../../../bib/kjv/heb011.htm#017)); ([Heb
11:18](../../../bib/kjv/heb011.htm#018)).

[Genesis 22:9](../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">gen 22:9</span></span>

laid

The typical lessons here are:

\(1\) Isaac, type of Christ "obedient unto death" ([Phi
2:5-8](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#005)).

\(2\) Abraham, type of the Father, who "spared not His own son, but
delivered Him up for us all" ([Joh
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016)); ([Rom
8:32](../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#032)).

\(3\) the ram, type of substitution -- Christ offered as a
burnt-offering in our stead, ([Heb
10:5-10](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#005)).

\(4\) Compare resurrection ([Heb
11:17-19](../../../bib/kjv/heb011.htm#017)); ([Jam
2:21-23](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#021)).
