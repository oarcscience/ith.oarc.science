### [Isaiah Chapter 41](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#001)

[Isaiah 41:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">isa 41:2</span></span>

the righteous man from the east

The reference here seems to be to Cyrus, whose victories and rapid
growth in power are here ascribed to the providence of God. ([Isa
41:5-7](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#005)) describe the effect upon the
nations of the rise of the Persian power. They heartened each other, and
made ([Isa 41:7](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#007)) new idols. At ([Isa
41:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#008)), the prophet addresses Israel.
Since it was their God who raised up Cyrus, they should expect good, not
evil, from him ([Isa 41:8-20](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#008)). ([Isa
41:21-24](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#021)) form a contemptuous
challenge to the idols in whom the nations are trusting.

[Isaiah 41:8](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">isa 41:8</span></span>

servant

Three servants of Jehovah are mentioned in Isaiah:

\(1\) David ([Isa 37:35](../../../bib/kjv/isa037.htm#035)).

\(2\) Israel the nation ([Isa
41:8-16](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#008)); ([Isa
43:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#001)); ([Isa
44:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#001)); ([Isa
44:21](../../../bib/kjv/isa044.htm#021)); ([Isa
45:4](../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#004)); ([Isa
48:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa048.htm#020)).

\(3\) Messiah ([Isa 42:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001)); Isaiah
49, entire chapter, but note especially ([Isa
49:5-7](../../../bib/kjv/isa049.htm#005)); where the Servant Christ
restores the servant nation; ([Isa
50:4-6](../../../bib/kjv/isa050.htm#004)); ([Isa
52:13-15](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#013)); ([Isa
52:53](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#053)); ([Isa
52:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#001)). Israel the nation was a
faithless servant, but restored and converted will yet thresh mountains.
Against the Servant Christ no charge of unfaithfulness or failure is
brought.

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 42:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#001)).

[Isaiah 41:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">isa 41:14</span></span>

redeemed

Hebrew, "goel," Redemption (Kinsman type).

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 59:20](../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020)).
