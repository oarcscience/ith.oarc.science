### [Exodus Chapter 38](../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#001)

[Exodus 38:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">exo 38:1</span></span>

shittim

That is, acacia.

[Exodus 38:27](../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">exo 38:27</span></span>

silver

Silver thus receives its symbolic meaning -- redemption. The sockets
were made of the redemption money of the children of Israel.

Compare ([Exo 26:19](../../../bib/kjv/exo026.htm#019)); ([Exo
30:13-16](../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#013)); ([Num
3:44-51](../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#044)).

sockets

(See Scofield) - ([Exo 26:19](../../../bib/kjv/exo026.htm#019)).
