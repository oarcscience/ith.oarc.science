1 Corinthians Chapter 11

1 Corinthians 11:2

co1 11:2

ordinances

Things delivered; often translated, "traditions".

1 Corinthians 11:3

co1 11:3

woman

Compare (Gen 3:16).

The woman's veil, or head-covering, is a symbol of this subordination.

1 Corinthians 11:10

co1 11:10

power

Authority, that is, the sign of the husband's authority.

angels

That is, of the presence of the angels.

1 Corinthians 11:27

co1 11:27

unworthily

That is, in an unworthy manner.

Compare (Co1 11:20-22).

1 Corinthians 11:29

co1 11:29

damnation

Judgment, in the sense of (Co1 11:32).

1 Corinthians 11:31

co1 11:31

judge

Self-judgment is not so much the believer's moral condemnation of his own ways or habits, or of himself, for allowing such ways. Self-judgment avoids chastisement. If neglected, the Lord judges, and the result is chastisement, but never condemnation (Co1 11:32); (Sa2 7:14); (Sa2 7:15); (Sa2 12:13); (Sa2 12:14); (Co1 5:5); (Ti1 1:20); (Heb 12:7).
