### [Psalms Chapter 39](../../../bib/kjv/psa039.htm#001)

[Psalms 39:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa039.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 39:1</span></span>

Jeduthun

Jeduthun, a Levite, chief singer and instructor. See ([Ch1
9:16](../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#016)); ([Ch1
16:38](../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#038)); ([Ch1
16:41](../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#041)); ([Ch1
16:42](../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#042)); ([Ch1
25:1](../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#001)); ([Ch1
25:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#003)); ([Ch1
25:6](../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#006)); ([Ch2
5:12](../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#012)); ([Ch2
35:15](../../../bib/kjv/ch2035.htm#015)); ([Neh
11:17](../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#017)). He is mentioned in Psalms 39,
62, 77. Jeduthun was first called Ethan.
