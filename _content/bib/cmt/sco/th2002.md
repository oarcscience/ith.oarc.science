[2 Thessalonians 2:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">th2 2:3</span></span>

for that day

The order of events is:

\(1\) The working of the mystery of lawlessness under divine restraint
which had already begun in the apostle's time ([Th2
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007)).

\(2\) the apostasy of the professing church ([Th2
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#003)); ([Luk
18:8](../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008)); ([Ti2
3:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001)).

\(3\) the removal of that which restrains the mystery of lawlessness
([Th2 2:6](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#006)); ([Th2
2:7](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007)). The restrainer is a person --
"he," and since a "mystery" always implies a supernatural element,

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 13:11](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#011)),

this Person can be none other than the Holy Spirit in the church, to be
"taken out of the way"; ([Th2 2:7](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007));
([Th1 4:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014)).

\(4\) the manifestation of the lawless one ([Th2
2:8-10](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#008)); ([Dan
7:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008)); ([Dan
9:27](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#027)); ([Mat
24:15](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#015)); ([Rev
13:2-10](../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#002)).

\(5\) the coming of Christ in glory and the destruction of the lawless
one ([Th2 2:8](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#008)); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)).

\(6\) the day of Jehovah ([Th2
2:9-12](../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#009)); ([Isa
2:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#012)).
