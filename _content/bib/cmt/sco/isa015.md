### [Isaiah Chapter 15](../../../bib/kjv/isa015.htm#001)

[Isaiah 15:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa015.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">isa 15:1</span></span>

Moab

This "burden" had a precursive fulfilment in Sennacherib's invasion,
B.C. 704, three years after the prediction ([Isa
16:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa016.htm#014)) but the words have a breadth of
meaning which includes also the final world-battle. (See Scofield) -
([Rev 19:17](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017)), ([Isa
16:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/isa016.htm#001)) which is a continuation of
this "burden," shows the "tabernacle of David" set up, the next event in
order after the destruction of the Beast and his armies.

Compare the order in; ([Isa 10:28-34](../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#028));
([Isa 11:1-10](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001)); ([Act
15:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)); ([Rev
19:17-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017)); ([Rev
20:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#001)).

burden

See note 1;

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#001)).
