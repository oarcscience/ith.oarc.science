### [1 John Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#001)

[1 John 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jo1 3:1</span></span>

world

Greek, "kosmos", means "mankind".

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 4:8](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#008)).

[1 John 3:4](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">jo1 3:4</span></span>

committeth sin

Whosoever practiseth sin practiseth also lawlessness; and sin is
lawlessness.

sin

Sin

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:23](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023)).

[1 John 3:7](../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">jo1 3:7</span></span>

righteousness

"Righteousness" here, and in the passages having marginal references to
this, means the righteous life which is the result of salvation through
Christ. The righteous man under law became righteous by doing
righteously; under grace he does righteously because he has been made
righteous ([Rom 3:22](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#022)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 10:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003)).
