### [1 Thessalonians Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#001)

[1 Thessalonians 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">th1 1:3</span></span>

work of faith

Literally, operative faith, and laborious love, and hope-filled
patience.

[1 Thessalonians 1:9](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">th1 1:9</span></span>

how ye turned

The tenses of the believer's life here indicated are logical and give
the true order. They occur also in ([Th1
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#003)).

The "work of faith" is to "turn to God from idols";

(compare ([Joh 6:28](../../../bib/kjv/joh006.htm#028)); ([Joh
6:29](../../../bib/kjv/joh006.htm#029)).

The "labour of love" is to "serve the living and true God"; and

The "patience of hope" is to "wait for his Son from heaven".

(compare ([Mat 24:42](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#042)); ([Mat
25:13](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#013)); ([Luk
12:36-48](../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#036)); ([Act
1:11](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#011)); ([Phi
3:20](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#020)); ([Phi
3:21](../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#021)).

Paul repeats this threefold sequence in ([Tit
2:11-13](../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#011)).
