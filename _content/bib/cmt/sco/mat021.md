### [Matthew Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#001)

[Matthew 21:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mat 21:1</span></span>

nigh unto Jerusalem

([Mat 21:1-9](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#001)); ([Zac
9:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#009)); ([Zac
14:4-9](../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#004)). The two advents are in
striking contrast.

[Matthew 21:4](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">mat 21:4</span></span>

fulfilled

The king's final and official offer of Himself according to ([Zac
9:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#009)). Acclaimed by an unthinking
multitude whose real belief is expressed in ([Mat
21:11](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#011)); but with no welcome from the
official representatives of the nation, He was soon to hear the
multitude shout: "Crucify him."

[Matthew 21:12](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">mat 21:12</span></span>

cast

See ([Luk 19:45](../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#045)); ([Mar
11:15-18](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#015)).

Compare ([Joh 2:13-25](../../../bib/kjv/joh002.htm#013)) which
introduced, as this cleansing closed, the offer of Christ to Israel as
King.

[Matthew 21:17](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">mat 21:17</span></span>

Bethany

See ([Joh 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#001)); ([Joh
11:2](../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#002)); ([Luk
10:39-42](../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#039)).

Compare ([Mar 11:1-11](../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#001)); ([Luk
19:29-35](../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#029)); ([Joh
12:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/joh012.htm#001)).

With no other place is the human Christ so tenderly associated, while it
also was the place of manifestation of His divine power. ([Joh
11:43](../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#043)); ([Joh
11:44](../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#044)).

[Matthew 21:19](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">mat 21:19</span></span>

fig tree

Literally, a solitary fig tree. ([Luk
13:6-9](../../../bib/kjv/luk013.htm#006)). The withered fig tree is a
parabolic miracle concerning Israel. ([Luk
13:6-9](../../../bib/kjv/luk013.htm#006)).

Compare ([Mat 24:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#032)); ([Mat
24:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#033));

a prophecy that Israel shall again bud.

[Matthew 21:43](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#043)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_043">mat 21:43</span></span>

kingdom of God

Note that Matthew here as in ([Mat
21:31](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#031)) uses the larger word, kingdom
of God:

Compare (See Scofield) - ([Mat 6:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#033)).

The kingdom of heaven;

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002)).

(See Scofield) - ([Co1 15:24](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024)).

will yet be set up. Meantime, the kingdom of God and his righteousness
is taken from Israel nationally and given to the Gentiles. ([Rom
9:30-33](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#030)).

you

That is, national Israel, the barren vine. ([Mat
21:33-41](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#033)); ([Isa

[Matthew 21:44](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#044)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_044">mat 21:44</span></span>

stone shall be broken

Christ, as the "Stone", is revealed in a threefold way:

\(1\) To Israel Christ, coming not as a splendid monarch but in the form
of a servant, is a stumbling stone and rock of offence. ([Isa
8:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#014)); ([Isa
8:15](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#015)); ([Rom
9:32](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#032)); ([Rom
9:33](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#033)); ([Co1
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#023)); ([Pe1
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#008)).

\(2\) to the church, Christ is the foundation stone and the head of the
corner ([Co1 3:11](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#011)); ([Eph
2:20-22](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#020)); ([Pe1
2:4](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#004)); ([Pe1
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#005)).

\(3\) to the Gentile world-powers

(see "Gentiles," ([Luk 21:24](../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024)); ([Rev
16:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#019))

He is to be the smiting-stone of destruction ([Dan
2:34](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034)). Israel stumbled over Christ;
the church is built upon Christ; Gentile world-dominion will be broken
by Christ.

See "Armageddon" ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)); ([Rev
19:19](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#019)).

And whosoever

Or, Whosoever falls on this stone shall be crushed togetherthat is, the
Jews ([Isa 8:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#014)); ([Rom
9:32](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#032)); ([Rom
9:33](../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#033)); ([Co1
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#023))\] but on whomsoever it may fall,
he will be scattered as dust (Greek, "likmaō", "winnowed"), that is, the
Gentile nations. ([Dan 2:34](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034)); ([Dan
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035)); ([Dan
2:45](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#045)).

(See Scofield) - ([Dan 2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035)).
