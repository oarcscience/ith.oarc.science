[Hebrews 2:10](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">heb 2:10</span></span>

captain

Leader, or, originator, that is, one who initiates and carries through.
translated, "author" in ([Heb 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#002)).

[Hebrews 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#013)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_013">heb 2:13</span></span>

I will put

Septuagint. ([Isa 8:17](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#017)).

God

Jehovah. Septuagint. ([Isa 8:18](../../../bib/kjv/isa008.htm#018)).

[Hebrews 2:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#014)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_014">heb 2:14</span></span>

took part

The word translated, "took part" is not the same as the translated,
"partakers," but implies taking part in something outside one's self.

[Hebrews 2:16](../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#016)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_016">heb 2:16</span></span>

he took

Not of angels doth he take hold, but he taketh hold of.

Compare Septuagint ([Isa 41:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa041.htm#009)).

reconciliation

(Greek, "hilaskomai", "propitiation").
