Revelation 5:7

rev 5:7

came

Compare (Dan 7:13); (Dan 7:14).

The two visions are identical; the Revelation adding that which was hidden from Daniel, that the kings and priests of the church-age are to be associated with the "Son of Man," the "Lamb as it had been slain," in His reign "on the earth". (Rev 2:9); (Rev 2:10).
