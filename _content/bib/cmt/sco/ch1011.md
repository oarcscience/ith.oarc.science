### [1 Chronicles Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#001)

[1 Chronicles 11:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">ch1 11:5</span></span>

castle of Zion

Hebrew, castle.

\(1\) Zion, the ancient Jebusite stronghold, is the southwest eminence
in Jerusalem, called in Scripture the city of David, and associated with
the Davidic royalty both historically and prophetically ([Ch1
11:7](../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#007)); ([Psa
2:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#006)); ([Isa
2:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#003)). The word is often used of the
whole city of Jerusalem considered as the city of God ([Psa
48:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#002)); ([Psa
48:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#003)) especially in passages referring
to the future kingdom-age; ([Isa
1:27](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#027)); ([Isa
2:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#003)); ([Isa
4:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#001)); ([Joe
3:16](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#016)); ([Zac
1:16](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#016)); ([Zac
1:17](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#017)); ([Zac
8:3-8](../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#003)); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)). In ([Heb
12:22](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#022)) the word is used symbolically
of heaven.

\(2\) In ([Deu 4:48](../../../bib/kjv/deu004.htm#048)) the name is given
to a projection or peak of Mount Hermon.
