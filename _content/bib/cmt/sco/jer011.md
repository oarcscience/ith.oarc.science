### [Jeremiah Chapter 11](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 11:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 11:1</span></span>

The word

This, like the other messages, is made up of rebuke, exhortation, and
warning, but in this instance these are based upon the violation of the
Palestinian Covenant ([Deu 28:1-30](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#001));
([Deu 28:9](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#009)).

(See Scofield) - ([Deu 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003)).

The Assyrian and Babylonian captivities of Israel and of Judah were the
execution of the warning, ([Deu
28:63-68](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#063)).

[Jeremiah 11:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#015)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_015">jer 11:15</span></span>

holy flesh

See ([Hag 2:12](../../../bib/kjv/hag002.htm#012)) that is, to what
purpose the "holy flesh" of sacrifices? Its efficacy is "passed from
thee" who rejoicest in evil.

Compare ([Isa 1:13-15](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#013)).
