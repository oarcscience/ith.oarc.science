### [Joel Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#001)

[Joel 3:9](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">joe 3:9</span></span>

Prepare war

Verses 9-14 ([Joe 3:9-14](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009)) refer to
Armageddon; verses 15, 16 ([Joe
3:15-16](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#015)) are parallel with ([Joe
2:30-32](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#030)). From verses 9 to 16 ([Joe
3:9-16](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009)), we have a resume of ([Joe
2:9-32](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#009)).
