### [James Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#001)

[James 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jam 2:1</span></span>

the Lord of glory

The Glory, that is, in the sense of ([Heb
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#003)) as taking the place of the
shekinah.

with respect

In the presence of Christ the Glory, earthly distinctions disappear.
