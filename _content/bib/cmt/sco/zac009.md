### [Zechariah Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#001)

[Zechariah 9:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 9:1</span></span>

burden

(See Scofield) - ([Isa 13:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#001)), note 1.

[Zechariah 9:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">zac 9:8</span></span>

because of him

There seems to be a reference here to the advance and return of
Alexander ([Zac 9:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#013)) after the battle
of Issus, who subdued the cities mentioned in ([Zac
9:1-6](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#001)); and afterward returned to
Greece without harming Jerusalem. But the greater meaning converges on
the yet future last days

(See Scofield) - ([Act 2:17](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#017))

as the last clause of verse ([Zac 9:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#008))
shows, for many oppressors have passed through Jerusalem since the days
of Alexander.

[Zechariah 9:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">zac 9:9</span></span>

behold, thy King

The events following this manifestation of Christ as King are recorded
in the Gospels. The real faith of the multitude who cried, "Hosanna" is
given in ([Mat 21:11](../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#011)) and so little
was Jesus deceived by His apparent reception as King, that He wept over
Jerusalem and announced its impending destruction (fulfilled A.D. 70;
([Luk 19:38-44](../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#038)). The same multitude
soon cried, "Crucify Him."

[Zechariah 9:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">zac 9:10</span></span>

And I will cut off

Having introduced the King in ([Zac
9:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#009)); ([Zac
9:10](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#010)) and the verses which follow look
forward to the end-time and kingdom. Except in verse 9 ([Zac
9:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#009)), this present age is not seen in
Zechariah.

[Zechariah 9:11](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">zac 9:11</span></span>

prisoners

Compare ([Isa 24:17-23](../../../bib/kjv/isa024.htm#017)); ([Zac
9:21](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#021)); ([Zac
9:23](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#023)) fix the time as the day of the
LORD. ([Rev 19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)).

[Zechariah 9:12](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">zac 9:12</span></span>

strong hold

See context, ([Zac 9:14](../../../bib/kjv/zac009.htm#014)). Also, ([Jer
16:19](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#019)).
