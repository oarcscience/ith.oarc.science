2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 9

2 Kings (2 Samuel) 9:1

sa2 9:1

kindness

A lovely picture of salvation by grace.

(1) What grace is -- kindness to a helpless one for another's sake, (Sa2 1:1-3); (Jo1 2:12).

(2) Grace gives the highest place, (Sa2 1:11). (Eph 1:1-6).

(3) Grace keeps the saved one, (Sa2 1:13); (Joh 10:28); (Joh 10:29).
