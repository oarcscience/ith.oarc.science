Micah 7:7

mic 7:7

Therefore

(Mic 7:7-20) is, primarily, the confession and intercession of the prophet, who identifies himself with Israel.

Compare (Dan 9:3-19).

Intercession was a test of the prophetic office; (Jer 27:18); (Gen 20:7) But Micah's prayer voices also the heart exercise of the remnant in the last days. Such is prophecy, an intermingling of the near and the far.

Compare (Psa 22:1); (Mat 27:46).
