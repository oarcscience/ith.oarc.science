Isaiah Chapter 17

Isaiah 17:1

isa 17:1

burden of Damascus

As in the burden of Moab, there was doubtless a near fulfilment in Sennacherib's approaching invasion, but 17:12-14 as evidently look forward to the final invasion and battle. ("Armageddon," (Rev 16:14).
