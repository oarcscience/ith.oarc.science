### [Matthew Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#001)

[Matthew 6:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mat 6:1</span></span>

may be seen of men, but that must not be the motive.

forgiven. ([Mat 18:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#032)); ([Mat
26:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#028)).

[Matthew 6:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#033)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_033">mat 6:33</span></span>

kingdom of God

The kingdom of God is to be distinguished from the kingdom of heaven

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002)),

in five respects:

\(1\) The kingdom of God is universal, including all moral intelligences
willingly subject to the will of God, whether angels, the Church, or
saints of past or future dispensations ([Luk
13:28-29](../../../bib/kjv/luk013.htm#028)); ([Heb
12:22-23](../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#022)) while the kingdom of heaven
is Messianic, mediatorial, and Davidic, and has for its object the
establishment of the kingdom of God in the earth

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002)). ([Co1
15:24-25](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024)).

\(2\) The kingdom of God is entered only by the new birth ([Joh
3:3](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#003)); ([Joh
3:5-7](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#005)) the kingdom of heaven, during
this age, is the sphere of a profession which may be real or false.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 13:3](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#003)); ([Mat
25:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#001); [Mat
25:11-12](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#011)).

\(3\) Since the kingdom of heaven is the earthly sphere of the universal
kingdom of God, the two have almost all things in common. For this
reason many parables and other teachings are spoken of the kingdom of
heaven in Matthew, and of the kingdom of God in Mark and Luke. It is the
omissions which are significant. The parables of the wheat and tares,
and of the net ([Mat 13:24-30](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#024)); ([Mat
13:36-43](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#036)); ([Mat
13:47-50](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#047)) are not spoken of the
kingdom of God. In that kingdom there are neither tares nor bad fish.
But the parable of the leaven ([Mat
13:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#033)) is spoken of the kingdom of God
also, for, alas, even the true doctrines of the kingdom are leavened
with the errors of which the Pharisees, Sadducees, and the Herodians
were the representatives.

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 13:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#033)), margin
reference).

\(4\) The kingdom of God "comes not with outward show" ([Luk
17:20](../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#020)) but is chiefly that which is
inward and spiritual ([Rom 14:17](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#017))
while the kingdom of heaven is organic, and is to be manifested in glory
on the earth.

(See "Kingdom (Old Testament)," ([Zac
12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008)); note; (New Testament), ([Luk
1:31-33](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#031)); ([Co1
15:24](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024)); note; ([Mat
17:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#002)); note.)

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008)), ([Luk
1:31-33](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#031)).

(See Scofield) - ([Co1 15:24](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024)).

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 17:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#002)).

\(5\) The kingdom of heaven merges into the kingdom of God when Christ,
having put all enemies under his feet, "shall have delivered up the
kingdom to God, even the Father" ([Co1
15:24-28](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024)).

(See Scofield) - ([Mat 3:2](../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002)).
