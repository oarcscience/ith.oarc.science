[Matthew 7:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">mat 7:22</span></span>

Devils

devils, lit demons. To the reality and personality of demons the New
Testament scriptures bear abundant testimony. As to their origin nothing
is clearly revealed, but they are not to be confounded with the angels
mentioned in ([Pe2 2:4](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#004)); ([Jde
1:6](../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#006)).

Summary:

Demons are spirits ([Mat 12:43](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#043)); ([Mat
12:45](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#045)) are Satan's emissaries; ([Mat
12:26-27](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#026)); ([Mat
25:41](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#041)) and so numerous as to make
Satan's power practically ubiquitous. ([Mar
5:9](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#009)).

They are capable of entering and controlling both men and beasts ([Mar
5:8](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#008)); ([Mar
5:11-13](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#011)) and earnestly seek
embodiment, without which, apparently, they are powerless for evil.
([Mat 12:43-44](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#043)); ([Mar
5:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#010)).

Demon influence and demon possession are discriminated in the New
Testament instances of the latter are; ([Mat
4:24](../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#024)); ([Mat
8:16](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#016)); ([Mat
8:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#028)); ([Mat
8:33](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#033)); ([Mat
9:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#032)); ([Mat
12:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#022)); ([Mar
1:32](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#032)); ([Mar
5:15-16](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#015)); ([Mar
5:18](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#018)); ([Luk
8:35](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#035)); ([Act
8:7](../../../bib/kjv/act008.htm#007)); ([Act
16:16](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#016)).

They are unclean, sullen, violent, and malicious; ([Mat
8:28](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#028)); ([Mat
9:23](../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#023)); ([Mat
10:1](../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#001)); ([Mat
12:43](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#043)); ([Mar
1:23](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#023)); ([Mar
5:3-5](../../../bib/kjv/mar005.htm#003)); ([Mar
9:17](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#017)); ([Mar
9:20](../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#020)); ([Luk
6:18](../../../bib/kjv/luk006.htm#018)); ([Luk
9:39](../../../bib/kjv/luk009.htm#039)).

They know Jesus Christ as Most High God, and recognize His supreme
authority; ([Mat 8:31-32](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#031)); ([Mar
1:24](../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#024)); ([Act
19:15](../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#015)); ([Jam
2:19](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#019)).

They know their eternal fate to be one of torment; ([Mat
8:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#029)); ([Luk
8:31](../../../bib/kjv/luk008.htm#031)).

They inflict physical maladies; ([Mat
12:22](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#022)); ([Mat
17:15-18](../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#015)); ([Luk
13:16](../../../bib/kjv/luk013.htm#016)), but mental disease is to be
distinguished from the disorder of mind due to demonical control.

Demon influence may manifest itself in religion asceticism and formalism
([Ti1 4:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/ti1004.htm#001)) degenerating into
uncleanness ([Pe2 2:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#010)).

The sign of demon influence in religion is departing from the faith,
that is, the body of revealed truth in the Scriptures. ([Ti1
4:1](../../../bib/kjv/ti1004.htm#001)).

The demons maintain especially a conflict with believers who would be
spiritual. ([Eph 6:12](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#012)); ([Ti1
4:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/ti1004.htm#001)).

All unbelievers are open to demon possession ([Eph
2:2](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#002)). The believer's resources, prayer
and bodily control ([Mat 17:21](../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#021)) "the
whole armour of God" ([Eph 6:13-18](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#013)).

Exorcism in the name of Jesus Christ ([Act
16:18](../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#018)) was practised for demon
possession.

One of the awful features of the apocalyptic judgments in which this age
will end is an irruption of demons out the abyss. ([Rev
9:1-11](../../../bib/kjv/rev009.htm#001)).
