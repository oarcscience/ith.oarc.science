### [Zechariah Chapter 2](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)

[Zechariah 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">zac 2:1</span></span>

again

As in ([Zac 1:8-11](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#008)); the "man" of
([Zac 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)); ([Zac
2:1](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001)) is "the angel that talked with
me" of ([Zac 1:3](../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#003)). The measuring-line
(or reed) is used by Ezekiel ([Eze
40:3](../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#003)); ([Eze
40:5](../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#005)) as a symbol of preparation for
rebuilding the city and temple in the kingdom-age. Here also it has that
meaning, as the context ([Zac 2:4-13](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#004))
shows. The subject of the vision is the restoration of nation and city.
In no sense has this prophecy been fulfilled. The order is:

\(1\) The Lord in glory in Jerusalem, ([Zac
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#005)).

Compare ([Mat 24:29](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#029)); ([Mat
24:30](../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#030))

\(2\) the restoration of Israel, ([Zac
2:6](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#006));

\(3\) the judgment of Jehovah upon the nations, ([Zac
2:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#008)); "after the glory" ([Mat
25:31](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031)); ([Mat
25:32](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#032)).

\(4\) the full blessing of the earth in the kingdom, ([Zac
2:10-13](../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#010));

See "Kingdom (Old Testament)" ([Gen
1:26](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#026)).

(See Scofield) - ([Zac 12:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008)).

"Israel,"; ([Gen 12:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002)); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)).
