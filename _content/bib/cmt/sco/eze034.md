### [Ezekiel Chapter 34](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 34:28](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#028)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">eze 34:28</span></span>

prey to the heathen

The whole passage ([Eze 34:23-30](../../../bib/kjv/eze034.htm#023))
speaks of a restoration yet future, for the remnant which returned after
the 70 years, and their posterity, were continually under the Gentile
yoke, until, in A.D. 70, they were finally driven from the land into a
dispersion which still continues.
