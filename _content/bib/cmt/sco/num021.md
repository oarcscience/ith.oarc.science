### [Numbers Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#001)

[Numbers 21:3](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">num 21:3</span></span>

Hormah

That is, utter destruction.

[Numbers 21:9](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#009)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_009">num 21:9</span></span>

serpent

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 3:14](../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#014)).

The serpent is a symbol of sin judged; brass speaks of the divine
judgment, as in the brazen altar

(See Scofield) - ([Exo 27:1](../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#001))",

note (2) and self-judgment, as in the laver of brass. The brazen serpent
is a type of Christ "made sin for us"; ([Joh
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#014)); ([Joh
3:15](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#015)); ([Co2
5:21](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#021)) in bearing our judgment.
Historically, the moment is indicated in the cry: "My God, My God, why
hast Thou forsaken Me?" ([Mat 27:46](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#046)).

[Numbers 21:17](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">num 21:17</span></span>

Then Israel

The spiritual order here is beautiful:

\(1\) atonement ([Num 21:8](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#008)); ([Num
21:9](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#009)); ([Joh
3:14](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#014)); ([Joh
3:15](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#015)).

\(2\) water, symbol of the Spirit bestowed ([Num
21:16](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#016)); ([Joh
7:37-39](../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#037)).

\(3\) joy ([Num 21:17](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#017)); ([Num
21:18](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#018)); ([Rom
14:17](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#017)).

\(4\) power ([Num 21:21-24](../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#021)).
