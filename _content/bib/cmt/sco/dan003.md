### [Daniel Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001)

[Daniel 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">dan 3:1</span></span>

an image of gold

The attempt of this great king of Babylon to unify the religions of his
empire by self-deification will be repeated by the beast, the last head
of the Gentile world-dominion ([Rev
13:11-15](../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#011)).

See note on "Beast, the"

(See Scofield) - ([Dan 7:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 19:20](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020)).

It has repeatedly characterized Gentile authority in the earth, for
example; ([Dan 6:7](../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#007)); ([Act
12:22](../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#022)) and the later Roman emperors.

[Daniel 3:8](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">dan 3:8</span></span>

Chaldeans

Compare the conduct of Daniel, ([Dan
2:24](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#024)).

[Daniel 3:17](../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">dan 3:17</span></span>

and he will deliver us

The three Jews, faithful to God while the nation of Israel far from
their land bear no testimony, are a fit type of the Jewish remnant in
the last days ([Isa 1:9](../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#009)); ([Rom
11:5](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005)) who will be faithful in the
furnace of the great tribulation; ([Psa
2:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005)); ([Rev
7:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014)).
