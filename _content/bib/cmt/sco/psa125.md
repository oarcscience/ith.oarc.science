### [Psalms Chapter 125](../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#001)

A Song of degrees

Literally, "of ascents." Perhaps chanted by the people as they went up
to Jerusalem to the feasts. See, for example ([Psa
112:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001)); ([Psa
112:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002)).
