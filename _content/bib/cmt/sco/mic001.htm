Micah Chapter 1

Micah 1:1

mic 1:1

Therefore

In (Mic 1:6-16), the Assyrian invasion is described.

Compare (Kg2 17:1-18).

This is the local circumstance which gives rise to the prophecy of the greater invasion in the last days. (Mic 4:9-13) and of the Lord's deliverance at Armageddon. (Rev 16:14); (Rev 19:17).
