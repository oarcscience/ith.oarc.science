### [Jeremiah Chapter 39](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 39:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 39:1</span></span>

tenth month.

[Jeremiah 39:2](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">jer 39:2</span></span>

fourth month

That is, July.

[Jeremiah 39:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#007)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_007">jer 39:7</span></span>

Babylon

Here began the "times of the Gentiles," the mark of which is that
Jerusalem is "trodden down of the Gentiles," that is, Under Gentile
overlordship. This has been true from the time of Nebuchadnezzar to this
day.

See "Times of the Gentiles"
