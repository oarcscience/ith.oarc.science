### [Romans Chapter 14](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#001)

[Romans 14:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">rom 14:1</span></span>

to doubtful

For decisions of doubts, that is, doubts about meats, etc. The church
has no authority to decide questions of personal liberty in things not
expressly forbidden in Scripture. ([Rom
14:2-6](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#002)).
