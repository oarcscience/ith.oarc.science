### [Jeremiah Chapter 16](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 16:1](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">jer 16:1</span></span>

saying

The sign of the unmarried prophet is interpreted by the context. The
whole social life of Judah was about to be disrupted and cease from the
land. But note the promises of verses ([Jer
16:14-16](../../../bib/kjv/jer016.htm#014)); ([Jer
17:7](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#007)); ([Jer
17:8](../../../bib/kjv/jer017.htm#008)).
