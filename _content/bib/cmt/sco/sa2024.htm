2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 24

2 Kings (2 Samuel) 24:9

sa2 24:9

in Israel

Compare (Ch1 21:5).

The total military strength of Israel (the northern kingdom) was 1,100,000, and of Judah 500,000. The numbers actually set in array were, of Israel, 800,000; of Judah, 470,000.
