suffer

The believer's resources in a day of general declension and apostasy
are:

\(1\) Faith ([Ti2 1:5](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#005)).

\(2\) the Spirit ([Ti2 1:6](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#006)); ([Ti2
1:7](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#007)).

\(3\) the word of God ([Ti2 1:13](../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#013));
([Ti2 3:1-17](../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001)); ([Ti2
4:3](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#003)); ([Ti2
4:4](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#004)).

\(4\) the grace of Christ ([Ti2 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#001)).

\(5\) separation from vessels unto dishonour ([Ti2
2:4](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#004)); ([Ti2
2:20](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#020)); ([Ti2
2:21](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#021)).

\(6\) the Lord's sure reward ([Ti2
4:7](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#007)); ([Ti2
4:8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#008)).

\(7\) the Lord's faithfulness and power ([Ti2
2:13](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#013)); ([Ti2
2:19](../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#019)).
