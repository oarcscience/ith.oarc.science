### [Nehemiah Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#001)

[Nehemiah 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">neh 8:2</span></span>

seventh month

That is, October; ([Neh 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#002)); ([Neh
8:14](../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#014)).

[Nehemiah 8:17](../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#017)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_017">neh 8:17</span></span>

for since the days of Jeshua

It is not meant that there had not been some formal observance of the
feast of tabernacles (cf) ([Ch2 8:13](../../../bib/kjv/ch2008.htm#013));
([Eze 3:4](../../../bib/kjv/eze003.htm#004)), but that the people had
not dwelt in booths since Joshua's days.
