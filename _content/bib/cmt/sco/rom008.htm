rom 8:1

who walk

The statement ends with "Christ Jesus"; the last ten words are interpolated.

Romans 8:2

rom 8:2

Spirit

Hitherto, in Romans, the Holy Spirit has been mentioned but once; (Rom 5:5); in this chapter, He is mentioned nineteen times. Redemption is by blood and by power.

(See Scofield) - (Exo 14:30); (Rom 3:21-5); (Rom 3:11);

speaks of the redemptive price; Romans 8 speaks of redemptive power.
