Philemon Chapter 1

Philemon 1:18

plm 1:18

account

(Plm 1:17); (Plm 1:18) perfectly illustrate imputation:

"Receive him as myself" -- reckon to him my merit;

"If he hath wronged thee or oweth thee ought, put that on mine account" -- reckon to me his demerit.

See "Imputation," (Lev 25:50).
