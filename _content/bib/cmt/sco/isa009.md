### [Isaiah Chapter 9](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#001)

[Isaiah 9:3](../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">isa 9:3</span></span>

not

Omit, not. Isaiah complains that despite the wickedness of the northern
kingdom her afflictions are light, her prosperity great.

as "throne of the Caesars," and as little admits of "spiritualizing."

1:33](../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#033)).
