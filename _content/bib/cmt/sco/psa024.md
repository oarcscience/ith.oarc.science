### [Psalms Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#001)

[Psalms 24:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">psa 24:3</span></span>

Who shall

The order is:

\(1\) the declaration of title, "the earth is the Lord's" ([Psa
24:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#001)); ([Psa
24:2](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#002)).

\(2\) Who shall rule the earth? ([Psa
24:3-6](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#003)). It is a question of
worthiness, and no one is worthy but the Lamb.

Compare ([Dan 7:13](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#013)); ([Dan
7:14](../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#014)); ([Rev
5:3-10](../../../bib/kjv/rev005.htm#003)); ([Mat
25:31](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031)).

\(3\) The King of glory takes the throne of earth ([Psa
24:7-10](../../../bib/kjv/psa024.htm#007)).

See Psalm 40, next in order of the Messianic Psalms.
