### [Numbers Chapter 17](../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#001)

[Numbers 17:8](../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#008)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_008">num 17:8</span></span>

rod of Aaron

Aaron's rod that budded: Type of Christ in resurrection, owned of God as
High Priest. Aaron's priesthood had been questioned in the rebellion of
Korah, so God Himself will confirm it ([Num
17:5](../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#005)). Each of the tribe-heads brought
a perfectly dead rod; God put life into Aaron's only. So all the authors
of religions have died, Christ among them, but only Christ was raised
from the dead, and exalted to be a high priest. ([Heb
4:14](../../../bib/kjv/heb004.htm#014)); ([Heb
5:4-10](../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#004)).
