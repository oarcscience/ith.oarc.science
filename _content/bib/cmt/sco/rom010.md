[Romans 10:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">rom 10:3</span></span>

righteousness

The word "righteousness" here, and in the passages having marginal
references to this, means "legal", or "self-righteousness"; the futile
effort of man to work out under law a character which God can approve.

(See Scofield) - ([Rev 19:8](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#008)).

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:21](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021)).

[Romans 10:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">rom 10:10</span></span>

righteousness

Righteousness here, and in the passages which refer to ([Rom
10:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#010)); means that "righteousness of
God" which is judicially reckoned to all who believe on the Lord Jesus
Christ; believers are the "righteous".

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 3:21](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021)).
