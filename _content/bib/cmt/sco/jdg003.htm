Judges 3:7

jdg 3:7

Groves

Groves, like high places, have been associated with idolatrous worship from time immemorial. The Hebrew, asherah, translated, grove means also the idol enshrined there. (Deu 16:21). This idol seems often to have been a sacred tree, the figure of which is constantly found on Assyrian monuments.
