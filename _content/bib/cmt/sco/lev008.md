### [Leviticus Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/lev008.htm#001)

[Leviticus 8:2](../../../bib/kjv/lev008.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">lev 8:2</span></span>

Aaron

The priests did not consecrate themselves, all was done by another, in
this instance Moses, acting for Jehovah. The priests simply presented
their bodies in the sense of ([Rom
12:1](../../../bib/kjv/rom012.htm#001)).

[Leviticus 8:12](../../../bib/kjv/lev008.htm#012)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_012">lev 8:12</span></span>

poured of the anointing oil

Two important distinctions are made in the case of the high priest, thus
confirming his typical relation to Christ the anti-type:

1\. Aaron is anointed before the sacrifices are slain, while in the case
of the priests the application of blood precedes the anointing. Christ
the sinless One required no preparation for receiving the anointing oil,
symbol of the Holy Spirit;

2\. upon the high priest only was the anointing oil poured. "God giveth
not the Spirit by measure unto him" ([Joh
3:34](../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#034)). "Thy God hath anointed Thee
with the oil of gladness above Thy fellows." ([Heb
1:9](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#009)).
