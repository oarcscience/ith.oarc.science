### [Isaiah Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#001)

[Isaiah 4:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_002">isa 4:2</span></span>

branch

A name of Christ, used in fourfold way:

\(1\) "The Branch of Jehovah" ([Isa
4:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002)), that is, the "Immanuel"
character of Christ ([Isa 7:14](../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#014)) to be
fully manifested to restored and converted Israel after His return in
divine glory ([Mat 25:31](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031)).

\(2\) the "Branch of David" ([Isa
11:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001)); ([Jer
23:5](../../../bib/kjv/jer023.htm#005)); ([Jer
33:15](../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#015)) that is, the Messiah, "of the
seed of David according to the flesh" ([Rom
1:3](../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003)) revealed in His earthly glory as
King of kings, and Lord of lords;

\(3\) Jehovah's "Servant, the Branch" ([Zac
3:8](../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#008)) Messiah's humiliation and
obedience unto death according to; ([Isa
52:13-15](../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#013)); ([Isa
53:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/isa053.htm#001)); ([Phi
2:5-8](../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#005)).

\(4\) the "man whose name is the Branch" ([Zac
6:12](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#012)); ([Zac
6:13](../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#013)) that is His character as Son of
man, the "last Adam," the "second Man" ([Co1
15:45-47](../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#045)) reigning, as Priest-King,
over the earth in the dominion given to and lost by the first Adam.
Matthew is the Gospel of the "Branch of David"; Mark of "Jehovah's
Servant, the Branch"; Luke of "the man whose name is the Branch"; John
of "the Branch of Jehovah."
