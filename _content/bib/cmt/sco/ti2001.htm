2 Timothy 1:12

ti2 1:12

suffer

The believer's resources in a day of general declension and apostasy are:

(1) Faith (Ti2 1:5).

(2) the Spirit (Ti2 1:6); (Ti2 1:7).

(3) the word of God (Ti2 1:13); (Ti2 3:1-17); (Ti2 4:3); (Ti2 4:4).

(4) the grace of Christ (Ti2 2:1).

(5) separation from vessels unto dishonour (Ti2 2:4); (Ti2 2:20); (Ti2 2:21).

(6) the Lord's sure reward (Ti2 4:7); (Ti2 4:8).

(7) the Lord's faithfulness and power (Ti2 2:13); (Ti2 2:19).
