### [Deuteronomy Chapter 30](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001)

[Deuteronomy 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">deu 30:3</span></span>

turn thy captivity

The Palestinian Covenant gives the conditions under which Israel entered
the land of promise. It is important to see that the nation has never as
yet taken the land under the unconditional Abrahamic Covenant, nor has
it ever possessed the whole land (Compare ([Gen
15:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#018)); ([Num
34:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#001)).

The Palestinian Covenant is in seven parts.

\(1\) Dispersion for disobedience, ([Deu
30:1](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001)); ([Deu
28:63-68](../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#063)).

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 15:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#018))

\(2\) The future repentance of Israel while in the dispersion, ([Deu
30:2](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#002)).

\(3\) The return of the Lord, ([Deu
30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003)); ([Amo
9:9-14](../../../bib/kjv/amo009.htm#009)); ([Act
15:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014)).

\(4\) Restoration to the land, ([Deu
30:5](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#005)); ([Isa
11:11](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#011)); ([Isa
11:12](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#012)); ([Jer
23:3-8](../../../bib/kjv/jer023.htm#003)); ([Eze
37:21-25](../../../bib/kjv/eze037.htm#021)).

\(5\) National conversion, ([Deu
30:6](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#006)); ([Rom
11:26](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026)); ([Rom
11:27](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#027)); ([Hos
2:14-16](../../../bib/kjv/hos002.htm#014)).

\(6\) The judgment of Israel's oppressors, ([Deu
30:7](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#007)); ([Isa
14:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#001)); ([Isa
14:2](../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#002)); ([Joe
3:1-8](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#001)); ([Mat
25:31-46](../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031)).

\(7\) National prosperity, ([Deu
30:9](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#009)); ([Amo
9:11-14](../../../bib/kjv/amo009.htm#011)).
