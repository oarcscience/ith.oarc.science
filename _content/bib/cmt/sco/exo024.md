### [Exodus Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#001)

[Exodus 24:4](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">exo 24:4</span></span>

builded

Compare ([Exo 33:7-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo033.htm#007)).

This arrangement for worship was temporarily called the "tabernacle."

[Exodus 24:11](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#011)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_011">exo 24:11</span></span>

eat and drink

Symbol of communion.

Compare ([Luk 22:15-18](../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#015)).

A blood-sprinkled people, see ([Heb
9:19-22](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#019)) who had not yet broken the
law could thus commune with God. Never again was this repeated.

Compare ([Exo 24:9-11](../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#009)) with ([Lev
16:2](../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#002)) and ([Heb
9:6-8](../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#006)).

But compare, under grace, ([Eph 2:13](../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#013));
([Heb 10:19](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#019)); ([Heb
10:20](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#020)).
