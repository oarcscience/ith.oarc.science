### [Ezekiel Chapter 36](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 36:1](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">eze 36:1</span></span>

Chapter 36)

A beautiful order is discernible in this and the succeeding prophecies:

\(1\) Restoration of the land ([Eze
36:1-15](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#001)).

\(2\) of the people ([Eze 36:16-37](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#016));
([Eze 36:28](../../../bib/kjv/eze036.htm#028)).

\(3\) judgment on Israel's enemies. ([Eze
38:1-39](../../../bib/kjv/eze038.htm#001)); ([Eze
38:24](../../../bib/kjv/eze038.htm#024)). Afterward follows that which
concerns the worship of Jehovah that He may dwell amongst His people.
