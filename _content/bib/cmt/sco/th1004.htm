1 Thessalonians Chapter 4

1 Thessalonians 4:17

th1 4:17

caught up

Not church saints only, but all bodies of the saved, of whatever dispensation, are included in the first resurrection

(See Scofield) - (Co1 15:52)

as here described, but it is peculiarly the "blessed hope" of the Church.

Compare (Mat 24:42); (Mat 25:13); (Luk 12:36-48); (Act 1:11); (Phi 3:20); (Phi 3:21); (Tit 2:11-13).
