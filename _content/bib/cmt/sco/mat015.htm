Matthew 15:21

mat 15:21

departed

The Syrophenician woman.

For the first time, the rejected Son of David ministers to a Gentile. It is a precursive fulfilment of (Mat 12:18). Addressed by a Gentile as Son of David, He makes no reply, for a Gentile has no claim upon Him in that character.

(See Scofield) - (Mat 2:2); (Eph 2:12).

Addressing him as "Lord," she obtained an immediate answer. (Rom 10:12-13).
