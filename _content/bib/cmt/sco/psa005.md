### [Psalms Chapter 5](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001)

[Psalms 5:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 5:1</span></span>

Nehiloth

Nehiloth is not a musical instrument, but means "inheritance," and
indicates the character of the Psalm. The righteous are the Lord's
inheritance.
