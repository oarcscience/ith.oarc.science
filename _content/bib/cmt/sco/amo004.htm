amo 4:4

Beth-el

Compare (Kg1 12:25-33).

Any altar at Beth-el, after the establishment of Jehovah's worship at Jerusalem was of necessity divisive and schismatic. (Deu 12:4-14).

Compare (Joh 4:21-24); (Mat 18:20); (Heb 13:10-14).
