### [Psalms Chapter 69](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#001)

[Psalms 69:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 69:1</span></span>

Shoshannim

(See Scofield) - ([Psa 45:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa045.htm#001)).

Save me, O God

The New Testament quotations from, and references to, this Psalm
indicate in what way it adumbrates Christ. It is the psalm of His
humiliation and rejection ([Psa 69:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#004));
([Psa 69:7](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#007)); ([Psa
69:8](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#008)); ([Psa
69:10-12](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#010)). ([Psa
69:14-20](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#014)) may well describe the
exercises of His holy soul in Gethsemane ([Mat
26:36-45](../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#036)) while ([Psa
69:21](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#021)) is a direct reference to the
cross; ([Mat 27:34](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#034)); ([Mat
27:48](../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#048)); ([Joh
19:28](../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#028)). The imprecatory verses ([Psa
69:22-28](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#022)) are connected ([Rom
11:9](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#009)); ([Rom
11:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#010)) with the present judicial
blindness of Israel, ([Psa 69:25](../../../bib/kjv/psa069.htm#025))
having special reference to Judas. ([Act
1:20](../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#020)) who is thus made typical of his
generation, which shared his guilt.

See Psalm 72, next in order of the Messianic Psalms.
