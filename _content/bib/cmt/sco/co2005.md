[2 Corinthians 5:10](../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">co2 5:10</span></span>

For we must all appear

The judgment of the believer's works, not sins, is in question here.
These have been atoned for, and are "remembered no more forever" ([Heb
10:17](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#017)) but every work must come into
judgment, ([Mat 12:36](../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036)); ([Rom
14:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#010)); ([Gal
6:7](../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#007)); ([Eph
6:8](../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#008)); ([Col
3:24](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#024)); ([Col
3:25](../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#025)). The result is "reward" or
"loss" (of the reward), "but he himself shall be saved". ([Co1
3:11-15](../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#011)).

This judgment occurs at the return of Christ ([Mat
16:27](../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#027)); ([Luk
14:14](../../../bib/kjv/luk014.htm#014)); ([Co1
4:5](../../../bib/kjv/co1004.htm#005)); ([Ti2
4:8](../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#008)); ([Rev
22:12](../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#012)).
