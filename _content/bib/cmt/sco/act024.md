### [Acts Chapter 24](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#001)

[Acts 24:5](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">act 24:5</span></span>

world

(Greek, "oikoumenē", means "inhabited earth").

(See Scofield) - ([Luk 2:1](../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#001)).

[Acts 24:10](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#010)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_010">act 24:10</span></span>

the governor

Felix made procurator over Judaea, A.D. 53.

[Acts 24:22](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#022)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_022">act 24:22</span></span>

of that way

Concerning the Way.

See ([Joh 14:6](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#006)).

[Acts 24:25](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#025)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_025">act 24:25</span></span>

righteousness

(See Scofield) - ([Rom 10:10](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#010)).

trembled

Becoming afraid; (Greek, "emphobos", "afraid").

[Acts 24:27](../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">act 24:27</span></span>

But after

But when two years were fulfilled, Felix was succeeded by Porcius
Festus; and desiring to gain favour with the Jews, Felix left Paul in
bonds.
