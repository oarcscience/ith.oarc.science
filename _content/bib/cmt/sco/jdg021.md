### [Judges Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/jdg021.htm#001)

[Judges 21:3](../../../bib/kjv/jdg021.htm#003)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_003">jdg 21:3</span></span>

tribe

There is here no mourning for sin, no humbling because of national
transgression, no return to Jehovah. Accordingly, no word from Jehovah
comes to them. They act wholly in self-will.

Compare ([Jdg 21:10](../../../bib/kjv/jdg021.htm#010)); ([Dan
9:3-13](../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#003)).
