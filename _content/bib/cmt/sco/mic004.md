### [Micah Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#001)

[Micah 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">mic 4:1</span></span>

mountain

General predictions concerning the kingdom. In Scripture a mountain is
the symbol of a great earth power ([Dan
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035)); hills, of smaller power. The
prediction asserts

\(1\) the ultimate establishment of the kingdom, with Jerusalem for the
capital ([Mic 4:1](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#001));

\(2\) the universality of the future kingdom ([Mic
4:2](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#002));

\(3\) its character -- peace ([Mic
4:3](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#003));

\(4\) its effect -- prosperity ([Mic
4:4](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#004)); ([Mic
4:4](../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#004)).

Compare ([Isa 2:1-5](../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#001)); ([Isa
11:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001))

it shall be exalted

See "remnant"

(See Scofield) - ([Jer 15:21](../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#021)).
