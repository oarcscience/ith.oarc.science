1 Timothy 1:20

ti1 1:20

Hymenaeus and Alexander

It is significant as bearing upon the seriousness of all false teaching, and particularly as related to resurrection, that Paul calls it blasphemy to teach that "the resurrection is past already". (Ti2 2:17); (Ti2 2:18).
