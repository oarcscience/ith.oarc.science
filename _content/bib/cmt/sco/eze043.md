### [Ezekiel Chapter 43](../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#001)

[Ezekiel 43:19](../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#019)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_019">eze 43:19</span></span>

and thou shalt

doubtless these offerings will be memorial, looking back to the cross,
as the offerings under the old covenant were anticipatory, looking
forward to the cross. In neither case have animal sacrifices power to
put away sin. ([Heb 10:4](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#004)); ([Rom
3:25](../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025)).
