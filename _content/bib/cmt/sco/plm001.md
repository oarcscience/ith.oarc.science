### [Philemon Chapter 1](../../../bib/kjv/plm001.htm#001)

[Philemon 1:18](../../../bib/kjv/plm001.htm#018)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_018">plm 1:18</span></span>

account

([Plm 1:17](../../../bib/kjv/plm001.htm#017)); ([Plm
1:18](../../../bib/kjv/plm001.htm#018)) perfectly illustrate imputation:

"Receive him as myself" -- reckon to him my merit;

"If he hath wronged thee or oweth thee ought, put that on mine account"
-- reckon to me his demerit.

See "Imputation," ([Lev 25:50](../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#050)).

(See Scofield) - ([Jam 2:23](../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#023)).
