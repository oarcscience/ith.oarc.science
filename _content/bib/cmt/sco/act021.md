### [Acts Chapter 21](../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#001)

[Acts 21:4](../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#004)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_004">act 21:4</span></span>

go up to

Literally, set foot in. Not, as in ([Act
20:23](../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#023)) a warning of danger, but now an
imperative command.

See ([Act 22:17](../../../bib/kjv/act022.htm#017)); ([Act
22:18](../../../bib/kjv/act022.htm#018)).

[Acts 21:23](../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">act 21:23</span></span>

vow

Probably according to ([Num 6:1-7](../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#001)).

Compare ([Col 2:14-17](../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#014)).

[Acts 21:24](../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#024)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_024">act 21:24</span></span>

charges with them

Literally, spend something on them.

walkest orderly

Literally, art keeping in the ranks, guarding the law.

Compare ([Rom 10:1-12](../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001)).
