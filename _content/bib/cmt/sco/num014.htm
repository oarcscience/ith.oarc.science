Numbers Chapter 14

Numbers 14:23

num 14:23

Surely

Kadesh-barnea is, by the unbelief of Israel there, and the divine comment on that unbelief (Num 14:22-38); (Deu 1:19-40); (Co1 10:1-5); (Heb 3:12-19) invested with immense spiritual significance. The people had faith to sprinkle the blood of atonement (Exo 12:28) and to come out of Egypt (the world), but had not faith to enter their Canaan rest. Therefore, though redeemed, they were a forty years' grief to Jehovah. The spiritual application is made in (Heb 6:3-11):
