### [Psalms Chapter 110](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001)

[Psalms 110:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">psa 110:1</span></span>

The Lord said unto my Lord

The importance of Psalm 110 is attested by the remarkable prominence
given to it in the New Testament .

\(1\) It affirms the deity of Jesus, thus answering those who deny the
full divine meaning of his New Testament Title of "Lord." ([Mat
22:41-45](../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#041)); ([Mar
12:35-37](../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035)); ([Luk
20:41-44](../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#041)); ([Act
2:34](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#034)); ([Act
2:35](../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#035)); ([Heb
1:13](../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#013)); ([Heb
10:12](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#012)); ([Heb
10:13](../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#013)).

\(2\) This Psalm announces the eternal priesthood of Messiah -- one of
the most important statements of Scripture ([Psa
110:4](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#004)).

(See Scofield) - ([Gen 14:18](../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#018)).

(See Scofield) - ([Heb 5:6](../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#006)); ([Heb
7:1-28](../../../bib/kjv/heb007.htm#001)); ([Ti1
2:5-6](../../../bib/kjv/ti1002.htm#005)); ([Joh
14:6](../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#006)).

\(3\) Historically, the Psalm begins with the ascension of Christ ([Psa
110:1](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001)); ([Joh
20:17](../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#017)); ([Act
7:56](../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#056)); ([Rev
3:21](../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#021)).

\(4\) Prophetically, the Psalm looks on

\(a\) to the time when Christ will appear as the Rod of Jehovah's
strength, the Deliverer out of Zion. ([Rom
11:25-27](../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025)) and the conversion of
Israel; ([Psa 110:3](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#003)); ([Joe
2:27](../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#027)); ([Zac
13:9](../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009)); ([Deu
30:1-9](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001)

(See Scofield) - ([Deu 30:3](../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003)),

and

\(b\) to the judgment upon the Gentile powers which precedes the setting
up of the kingdom ([Psa 110:5](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#005)); ([Psa
110:6](../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#006)); ([Joe
3:9-17](../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009)); ([Zac
14:1-4](../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#001)); ([Rev
19:11-21](../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011)).

See "Armageddon" ([Rev 16:14](../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014)).
