### [Jeremiah Chapter 4](../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#001)

[Jeremiah 4:23](../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#023)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_023">jer 4:23</span></span>

without form and void

Compare ([Gen 1:2](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#002)).

"Without form and void" describes the condition of the earth as the
result of judgment; ([Jer 4:24-26](../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#024));
([Isa 24:1](../../../bib/kjv/isa024.htm#001)) which overthrew the primal
order of ([Gen 1:1](../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001)).

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_028">jer 4:28</span></span>
