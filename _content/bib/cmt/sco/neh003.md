### [Nehemiah Chapter 3](../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#001)

[Nehemiah 3:1](../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#001)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_001">neh 3:1</span></span>

sheep

The sheep for sacrifice were brought in here.

(See Scofield) - ([Joh 5:2](../../../bib/kjv/joh005.htm#002)).

tower

The towers appear to have been on either side the sheep gate.

[Nehemiah 3:26](../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#026)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_026">neh 3:26</span></span>

Nethinims

That is, Dedicated (persons), probably descendants of the Gibeonites
([Sa2 21:1-3](../../../bib/kjv/sa2021.htm#001)) devoted to the service
of the Levites. But see ([Jos
9:17-21](../../../bib/kjv/jos009.htm#017)).

[Nehemiah 3:27](../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#027)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_027">neh 3:27</span></span>

Ophel

translated, "tower," ([Kg2 5:24](../../../bib/kjv/kg2005.htm#024));
([Ch2 27:3](../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#003)); ([Ch2
33:14](../../../bib/kjv/ch2033.htm#014)). Perhaps part of the fort
called Millo, ([Kg1 9:15](../../../bib/kjv/kg1009.htm#015)); ([Ch2
32:5](../../../bib/kjv/ch2032.htm#005)).
