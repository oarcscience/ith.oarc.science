(1) disobedient, (Jon 1:1-11);

(2) afflicted, (Jon 1:12-17);

(3) praying (Jon 2:1-9(;

(4) delivered, (Jon 2:10);

(5) recommissioned, (Jon 3:1-3);

(6) powerful, (Jon 3:4-9);

(7) perplexed and fainting but not forsaken, (Jon 4:1-11).
