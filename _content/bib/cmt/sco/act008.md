### [Acts Chapter 8](../../../bib/kjv/act008.htm#001)

[Acts 8:5](../../../bib/kjv/act008.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">act 8:5</span></span>

city of

now begins to go out to "all nations." Compare ([Luk
24:47](../../../bib/kjv/luk024.htm#047)).
