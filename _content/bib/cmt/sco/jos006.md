### [Joshua Chapter 6](../../../bib/kjv/jos006.htm#001)

[Joshua 6:5](../../../bib/kjv/jos006.htm#005)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_005">jos 6:5</span></span>

And it shall come to pass

The central truth here is that spiritual victories are won by means and
upon principles utterly foolish and inadequate in the view of human
wisdom. ([Co1 1:17-29](../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#017)); ([Co2
10:3-5](../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#003)).
