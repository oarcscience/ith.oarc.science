### XXXVII.

Here God summons an angel.

AND the Lord called up one of the older angels, terrible and menacing,
and placed him

<span id="page_95">p. 95</span>

by me, in appearance white as snow, and his hands like ice, having the
appearance of great frost, and he froze my face, because I could not
endure the terror of the Lord, just as it is not possible to endure a
stove's fire and the sun's heat, and the frost of the air.

2 And the Lord said to me: 'Enoch, if thy face be not frozen here, no
man will be able to behold thy face.'
