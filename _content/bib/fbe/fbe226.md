### ODE 32.

Joy and light.

1 To the blessed there is joy from their hearts, and light from Him that
dwells in them:  
2 And words from the Truth, who was self-originate: for He is
strengthened by the holy power of the Most High: and He is unperturbed
for ever and ever. Hallelujah.
