### LIV.

Enoch instructs his sons, that they should hand the books to others
also.

AT that time, not understanding this let these books which I have given
you be for an inheritance of your peace.

2 Hand them to all who want them, and instruct them, that they may see
the Lord's very great and marvellous works.
