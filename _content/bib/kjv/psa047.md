### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 47

<span id="001">1</span> O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God
with the voice of triumph.  

<span id="002">2</span> For the LORD most high is terrible; he is a
great [King](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) over all the earth.  

<span id="003">3</span> He shall subdue the people under us, and the
nations under our feet.  

<span id="004">4</span> He shall choose our inheritance for us, the
excellency of [Jacob](../ebd/ebd194.htm#007) whom he loved.
[Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="005">5</span> God is gone up with a shout, the LORD with the
sound of a trumpet.  

<span id="006">6</span> Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises
unto our [King](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001), sing praises.  

<span id="007">7</span> For God is the [King](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.  

<span id="008">8</span> God reigneth over the
[heathen](../ebd/ebd170.htm#005): God sitteth upon the
[throne](../ebd/ebd365.htm#001) of his
[holiness](../ebd/ebd180.htm#006).  

<span id="009">9</span> The princes of the people are gathered together,
even the people of the God of [Abraham](../ebd/ebd005.htm#005): for the
shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted.
