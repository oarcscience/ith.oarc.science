### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 51

<span id="001">1</span> Have [mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) upon me, O
God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of
thy tender mercies [blot](../ebd/ebd061.htm#002) out my
transgressions.  

<span id="002">2</span> Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and
cleanse me from my [sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006).  

<span id="003">3</span> For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) is ever before me.  

<span id="004">4</span> Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done
this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou
speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.  

<span id="005">5</span> Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) did my mother conceive me.  

<span id="006">6</span> Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts:
and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.  

<span id="007">7</span> Purge me with [hyssop](../ebd/ebd185.htm#007),
and I shall be [clean](../ebd/ebd084.htm#005): wash me, and I shall be
whiter than [snow](../ebd/ebd347.htm#000).  

<span id="008">8</span> Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones
which thou hast broken may rejoice.  

<span id="009">9</span> Hide thy face from my sins, and
[blot](../ebd/ebd061.htm#002) out all mine iniquities.  

<span id="010">10</span> Create in me a [clean](../ebd/ebd084.htm#005)
heart, O God; and renew a right [spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009) within
me.  

<span id="011">11</span> Cast me not away from thy presence; and take
not thy holy [spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009) from me.  

<span id="012">12</span> Restore unto me the joy of thy
[salvation](../ebd/ebd320.htm#003); and uphold me with thy free
[spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009).  

<span id="013">13</span> Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and
sinners shall be converted unto thee.  

<span id="014">14</span> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou
God of my [salvation](../ebd/ebd320.htm#003): and my tongue shall sing
aloud of thy [righteousness](../ebd/ebd313.htm#004).  

<span id="015">15</span> O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall
shew forth thy praise.  

<span id="016">16</span> For thou desirest not
[sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002); else would I give it: thou
delightest not in burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="017">17</span> The sacrifices of God are a broken
[spirit](../ebd/ebd349.htm#009): a broken and a contrite heart, O God,
thou wilt not despise.  

<span id="018">18</span> Do good in thy good pleasure unto
[Zion](../ebd/ebd394.htm#002): build thou the walls of
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005).  

<span id="019">19</span> Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices
of [righteousness](../ebd/ebd313.htm#004), with burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and whole burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001): then shall they offer bullocks upon
thine [altar](../ebd/ebd018.htm#006).
