### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 61

<span id="001">1</span> Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my
[prayer](../ebd/ebd300.htm#000).  

<span id="002">2</span> From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee,
when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the
[rock](../ebd/ebd315.htm#001) that is higher than I.  

<span id="003">3</span> For thou hast been a shelter for me, and a
strong tower from the enemy.  

<span id="004">4</span> I will abide in thy
[tabernacle](../ebd/ebd356.htm#003) for ever: I will trust in the covert
of thy wings. [Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="005">5</span> For thou, O God, hast heard my
[vows](../ebd/ebd378.htm#001): thou hast given me the heritage of those
that fear thy name.  

<span id="006">6</span> Thou wilt prolong the king's life: and his years
as many generations.  

<span id="007">7</span> He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) and truth, which may preserve him.  

<span id="008">8</span> So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever,
that I may daily perform my [vows](../ebd/ebd378.htm#001).
