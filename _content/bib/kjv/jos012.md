### King James Version: Joshua Chapter 12

<span id="001">1</span> Now these are the kings of the land, which the
children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) smote, and possessed their
land on the other side [Jordan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#004) toward the rising
of the [sun](../ebd/ebd353.htm#001), from the river
[Arnon](../ebd/ebd032.htm#000) unto [mount](../ebd/ebd260.htm#007)
[Hermon](../ebd/ebd175.htm#006), and all the
[plain](../ebd/ebd297.htm#002) on the [east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000):  

<span id="002">2</span> [Sihon](../ebd/ebd343.htm#002)
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of the [Amorites](../ebd/ebd021.htm#006),
who dwelt in [Heshbon](../ebd/ebd176.htm#009), and ruled from
[Aroer](../ebd/ebd032.htm#001), which is upon the bank of the river
[Arnon](../ebd/ebd032.htm#000), and from the middle of the river, and
from half [Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009), even unto the river
[Jabbok](../ebd/ebd193.htm#006), which is the border of the children of
[Ammon](../ebd/ebd021.htm#002);  

<span id="003">3</span> And from the [plain](../ebd/ebd297.htm#002) to
the sea of Chinneroth on the [east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000), and unto the
sea of the [plain](../ebd/ebd297.htm#002), even the
[salt](../ebd/ebd319.htm#008) sea on the [east](../ebd/ebd111.htm#000),
the way to Bethjeshimoth; and from the south, under Ashdothpisgah:  

<span id="004">4</span> And the coast of [Og](../ebd/ebd277.htm#002)
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of [Bashan](../ebd/ebd046.htm#003), which
was of the remnant of the [giants](../ebd/ebd147.htm#006), that dwelt at
[Ashtaroth](../ebd/ebd034.htm#004) and at
[Edrei](../ebd/ebd113.htm#002),  

<span id="005">5</span> And reigned in [mount](../ebd/ebd260.htm#007)
[Hermon](../ebd/ebd175.htm#006), and in [Salcah](../ebd/ebd319.htm#000),
and in all [Bashan](../ebd/ebd046.htm#003), unto the border of the
[Geshurites](../ebd/ebd147.htm#002) and the Maachathites, and half
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009), the border of
[Sihon](../ebd/ebd343.htm#002) [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Heshbon](../ebd/ebd176.htm#009).  

<span id="006">6</span> Them did [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) the
servant of the LORD and the children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000)
smite: and [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) the servant of the LORD gave
it for a possession unto the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half
[tribe](../ebd/ebd372.htm#004) of [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002).  

<span id="007">7</span> And these are the kings of the country which
[Joshua](../ebd/ebd211.htm#006) and the children of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) smote on this side
[Jordan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#004) on the west, from Baalgad in the
[valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008) of [Lebanon](../ebd/ebd226.htm#001) even
unto the [mount](../ebd/ebd260.htm#007) [Halak](../ebd/ebd159.htm#006),
that goeth up to [Seir](../ebd/ebd326.htm#004); which
[Joshua](../ebd/ebd211.htm#006) gave unto the tribes of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) for a possession according to their
divisions;  

<span id="008">8</span> In the mountains, and in the valleys, and in the
plains, and in the springs, and in the
[wilderness](../ebd/ebd381.htm#005), and in the south country; the
[Hittites](../ebd/ebd179.htm#006), the
[Amorites](../ebd/ebd021.htm#006), and the
[Canaanites](../ebd/ebd070.htm#007), the
[Perizzites](../ebd/ebd290.htm#006), the
[Hivites](../ebd/ebd179.htm#007), and the
[Jebusites](../ebd/ebd199.htm#004):  

<span id="009">9</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Jericho](../ebd/ebd203.htm#008), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Ai](../ebd/ebd015.htm#009), which is beside
[Bethel](../ebd/ebd055.htm#006), one;  

<span id="010">10</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005), one; the
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of [Hebron](../ebd/ebd171.htm#004), one;  

<span id="011">11</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Jarmuth](../ebd/ebd197.htm#007), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Lachish](../ebd/ebd222.htm#009), one;  

<span id="012">12</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Eglon](../ebd/ebd113.htm#008), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Gezer](../ebd/ebd147.htm#004), one;  

<span id="013">13</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Debir](../ebd/ebd099.htm#007), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Geder](../ebd/ebd145.htm#000), one;  

<span id="014">14</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Hormah](../ebd/ebd182.htm#002), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Arad](../ebd/ebd028.htm#000), one;  

<span id="015">15</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Libnah](../ebd/ebd228.htm#004), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Adullam](../ebd/ebd010.htm#007), one;  

<span id="016">16</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Makkedah](../ebd/ebd238.htm#006), one; the
[king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of [Bethel](../ebd/ebd055.htm#006), one;  

<span id="017">17</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Tappuah](../ebd/ebd358.htm#008), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Hepher](../ebd/ebd174.htm#006), one;  

<span id="018">18</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of Aphek,
one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of Lasharon, one;  

<span id="019">19</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Madon](../ebd/ebd236.htm#003), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Hazor](../ebd/ebd169.htm#006), one;  

<span id="020">20</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
Shimronmeron, one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Achshaph](../ebd/ebd007.htm#002), one;  

<span id="021">21</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Taanach](../ebd/ebd355.htm#005), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Megiddo](../ebd/ebd246.htm#004), one;  

<span id="022">22</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Kedesh](../ebd/ebd216.htm#006), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001)
of [Jokneam](../ebd/ebd210.htm#002) of [Carmel](../ebd/ebd072.htm#006),
one;  

<span id="023">23</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Dor](../ebd/ebd106.htm#003) in the coast of
[Dor](../ebd/ebd106.htm#003), one; the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
the nations of [Gilgal](../ebd/ebd149.htm#001), one;  

<span id="024">24</span> The [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of Tirzah,
one: all the kings thirty and one.
