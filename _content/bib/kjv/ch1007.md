### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 7

<span id="001">1</span> Now the sons of
[Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002) were, [Tola](../ebd/ebd369.htm#007),
and [Puah](../ebd/ebd301.htm#008), [Jashub](../ebd/ebd198.htm#001), and
[Shimrom](../ebd/ebd338.htm#007), four.  

<span id="002">2</span> And the sons of [Tola](../ebd/ebd369.htm#007);
[Uzzi](../ebd/ebd376.htm#003), and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and
Jibsam, and [Shemuel](../ebd/ebd335.htm#000), heads of their father's
house, to wit, of [Tola](../ebd/ebd369.htm#007): they were valiant men
of might in their generations; whose number was in the days of
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004) two and twenty thousand and six
hundred.  

<span id="003">3</span> And the sons of [Uzzi](../ebd/ebd376.htm#003);
Izrahiah: and the sons of Izrahiah; [Michael](../ebd/ebd252.htm#009),
and [Obadiah](../ebd/ebd276.htm#001), and [Joel](../ebd/ebd208.htm#005),
Ishiah, five: all of them chief men.  

<span id="004">4</span> And with them, by their generations, after the
house of their fathers, were [bands](../ebd/ebd043.htm#003) of soldiers
for war, six and thirty thousand men: for they had many wives and
sons.  

<span id="005">5</span> And their brethren among all the families of
[Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002) were valiant men of might, reckoned in
all by their genealogies fourscore and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005)
thousand.  

<span id="006">6</span> The sons of [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008);
[Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006), and [Becher](../ebd/ebd049.htm#005), and
[Jediael](../ebd/ebd199.htm#007), three.  

<span id="007">7</span> And the sons of [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006);
Ezbon, and [Uzzi](../ebd/ebd376.htm#003), and
[Uzziel](../ebd/ebd376.htm#005), and [Jerimoth](../ebd/ebd203.htm#009),
and Iri, five; heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of
valour; and were reckoned by their genealogies twenty and two thousand
and thirty and four.  

<span id="008">8</span> And the sons of [Becher](../ebd/ebd049.htm#005);
[Zemira](../ebd/ebd390.htm#006), and [Joash](../ebd/ebd208.htm#000), and
[Eliezer](../ebd/ebd116.htm#007), and [Elioenai](../ebd/ebd117.htm#003),
and [Omri](../ebd/ebd278.htm#006), and
[Jerimoth](../ebd/ebd203.htm#009), and Abiah, and
[Anathoth](../ebd/ebd023.htm#004), and Alameth. All these are the sons
of [Becher](../ebd/ebd049.htm#005).  

<span id="009">9</span> And the number of them, after their genealogy by
their generations, heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of
valour, was twenty thousand and two hundred.  

<span id="010">10</span> The sons also of
[Jediael](../ebd/ebd199.htm#007); Bilhan: and the sons of Bilhan;
[Jeush](../ebd/ebd206.htm#003), and [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008),
and [Ehud](../ebd/ebd114.htm#000), and
[Chenaanah](../ebd/ebd078.htm#008), and [Zethan](../ebd/ebd392.htm#001),
and [Tharshish](../ebd/ebd363.htm#003), and Ahishahar.  

<span id="011">11</span> All these the sons of
[Jediael](../ebd/ebd199.htm#007), by the heads of their fathers, mighty
men of valour, were seventeen thousand and two hundred soldiers, fit to
go out for war and battle.  

<span id="012">12</span> Shuppim also, and Huppim, the children of Ir,
and Hushim, the sons of Aher.  

<span id="013">13</span> The sons of [Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006);
Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and [Shallum](../ebd/ebd330.htm#008), the
sons of [Bilhah](../ebd/ebd058.htm#008).  

<span id="014">14</span> The sons of [Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002);
Ashriel, whom she bare: (but his [concubine](../ebd/ebd087.htm#007) the
Aramitess bare [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) the father of
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009):  

<span id="015">15</span> And [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) took to
wife the sister of Huppim and Shuppim, whose sister's name was
[Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004);) and the name of the second was
[Zelophehad](../ebd/ebd390.htm#002): and
[Zelophehad](../ebd/ebd390.htm#002) had daughters.  

<span id="016">16</span> And [Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004) the wife
of [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) bare a son, and she called his name
Peresh; and the name of his brother was
[Sheresh](../ebd/ebd335.htm#008); and his sons were Ulam and Rakem.  

<span id="017">17</span> And the sons of Ulam;
[Bedan](../ebd/ebd049.htm#007). These were the sons of
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009), the son of
[Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006), the son of
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002).  

<span id="018">18</span> And his sister
[Hammoleketh](../ebd/ebd161.htm#001) bare Ishod, and
[Abiezer](../ebd/ebd003.htm#000), and Mahalah.  

<span id="019">19</span> And the sons of Shemidah were, Ahian, and
[Shechem](../ebd/ebd333.htm#003), and Likhi, and Aniam.  

<span id="020">20</span> And the sons of
[Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000); Shuthelah, and
[Bered](../ebd/ebd053.htm#004) his son, and Tahath his son, and Eladah
his son, and Tahath his son,  

<span id="021">21</span> And [Zabad](../ebd/ebd385.htm#005) his son, and
Shuthelah his son, and [Ezer](../ebd/ebd129.htm#004), and Elead, whom
the men of [Gath](../ebd/ebd143.htm#009) that were born in that land
slew, because they came down to take away their
[cattle](../ebd/ebd074.htm#002).  

<span id="022">22</span> And [Ephraim](../ebd/ebd123.htm#000) their
father mourned many days, and his brethren came to comfort him.  

<span id="023">23</span> And when he went in to his wife, she conceived,
and bare a son, and he called his name [Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005),
because it went evil with his house.  

<span id="024">24</span> (And his daughter was Sherah, who built
Bethhoron the nether, and the upper, and Uzzensherah.)  

<span id="025">25</span> And Rephah was his son, also Resheph, and Telah
his son, and Tahan his son.  

<span id="026">26</span> Laadan his son,
[Ammihud](../ebd/ebd020.htm#007) his son,
[Elishama](../ebd/ebd118.htm#001) his son.  

<span id="027">27</span> Non his son, Jehoshuah his son.  

<span id="028">28</span> And their possessions and habitations were,
[Bethel](../ebd/ebd055.htm#006) and the towns thereof, and eastward
[Naaran](../ebd/ebd264.htm#003), and [westward](../ebd/ebd380.htm#008)
[Gezer](../ebd/ebd147.htm#004), with the towns thereof;
[Shechem](../ebd/ebd333.htm#003) also and the towns thereof, unto
[Gaza](../ebd/ebd144.htm#003) and the towns thereof:  

<span id="029">29</span> And by the borders of the children of
[Manasseh](../ebd/ebd240.htm#002), Bethshean and her towns,
[Taanach](../ebd/ebd355.htm#005) and her towns,
[Megiddo](../ebd/ebd246.htm#004) and her towns,
[Dor](../ebd/ebd106.htm#003) and her towns. In these dwelt the children
of [Joseph](../ebd/ebd211.htm#005) the son of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000).  

<span id="030">30</span> The sons of [Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008);
Imnah, and Isuah, and Ishuai, and [Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005), and
[Serah](../ebd/ebd328.htm#002) their sister.  

<span id="031">31</span> And the sons of
[Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005); [Heber](../ebd/ebd170.htm#008), and
Malchiel, who is the father of Birzavith.  

<span id="032">32</span> And [Heber](../ebd/ebd170.htm#008) begat
Japhlet, and [Shomer](../ebd/ebd341.htm#000), and Hotham, and
[Shua](../ebd/ebd341.htm#005) their sister.  

<span id="033">33</span> And the sons of Japhlet;
[Pasach](../ebd/ebd286.htm#000), and Bimhal, and Ashvath. These are the
children of Japhlet.  

<span id="034">34</span> And the sons of Shamer; Ahi, and Rohgah,
Jehubbah, and [Aram](../ebd/ebd028.htm#001).  

<span id="035">35</span> And the sons of his brother
[Helem](../ebd/ebd172.htm#006); [Zophah](../ebd/ebd395.htm#009), and
Imna, and Shelesh, and Amal.  

<span id="036">36</span> The sons of [Zophah](../ebd/ebd395.htm#009);
Suah, and [Harnepher](../ebd/ebd164.htm#006), and Shual, and Beri, and
Imrah,  

<span id="037">37</span> [Bezer](../ebd/ebd058.htm#001), and Hod, and
Shamma, and Shilshah, and Ithran, and Beera.  

<span id="038">38</span> And the sons of
[Jether](../ebd/ebd205.htm#007); [Jephunneh](../ebd/ebd203.htm#004), and
Pispah, and Ara.  

<span id="039">39</span> And the sons of Ulla; Arah, and Haniel, and
Rezia.  

<span id="040">40</span> All these were the children of
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008), heads of their father's house, choice
and mighty men of valour, chief of the princes. And the number
throughout the genealogy of them that were apt to the war and to battle
was twenty and six thousand men.
