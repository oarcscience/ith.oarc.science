### King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 20

<span id="001">1</span> In the year that [Tartan](../ebd/ebd359.htm#005)
came unto [Ashdod](../ebd/ebd033.htm#006), (when
[Sargon](../ebd/ebd322.htm#009) the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Assyria](../ebd/ebd035.htm#005) sent him,) and fought against
[Ashdod](../ebd/ebd033.htm#006), and took it;  

<span id="002">2</span> At the same time spake the LORD by
[Isaiah](../ebd/ebd189.htm#007) the son of
[Amoz](../ebd/ebd021.htm#008), saying, Go and loose the
[sackcloth](../ebd/ebd318.htm#001) from off thy loins, and put off thy
[shoe](../ebd/ebd340.htm#009) from thy foot. And he did so, walking
[naked](../ebd/ebd266.htm#003) and [barefoot](../ebd/ebd044.htm#008).  

<span id="003">3</span> And the LORD said, Like as my servant
[Isaiah](../ebd/ebd189.htm#007) hath walked
[naked](../ebd/ebd266.htm#003) and [barefoot](../ebd/ebd044.htm#008)
three years for a sign and wonder upon [Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009)
and upon [Ethiopia](../ebd/ebd126.htm#005);  

<span id="004">4</span> So shall the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Assyria](../ebd/ebd035.htm#005) lead away the Egyptians prisoners, and
the Ethiopians captives, young and old, [naked](../ebd/ebd266.htm#003)
and [barefoot](../ebd/ebd044.htm#008), even with their buttocks
uncovered, to the shame of [Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009).  

<span id="005">5</span> And they shall be afraid and ashamed of
[Ethiopia](../ebd/ebd126.htm#005) their expectation, and of
[Egypt](../ebd/ebd113.htm#009) their [glory](../ebd/ebd150.htm#006).  

<span id="006">6</span> And the inhabitant of this isle shall say in
that day, Behold, such is our expectation, whither we flee for help to
be delivered from the [king](../ebd/ebd219.htm#001) of
[Assyria](../ebd/ebd035.htm#005): and how shall we escape?
