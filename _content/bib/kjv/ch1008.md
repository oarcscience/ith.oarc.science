### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 8

<span id="001">1</span> Now [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008) begat
[Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006) his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and
Aharah the third,  

<span id="002">2</span> Nohah the fourth, and
[Rapha](../ebd/ebd307.htm#006) the fifth.  

<span id="003">3</span> And the sons of [Bela](../ebd/ebd051.htm#006)
were, [Addar](../ebd/ebd008.htm#004), and [Gera](../ebd/ebd146.htm#003),
and [Abihud](../ebd/ebd003.htm#005),  

<span id="004">4</span> And [Abishua](../ebd/ebd004.htm#006), and
[Naaman](../ebd/ebd263.htm#009), and [Ahoah](../ebd/ebd015.htm#003),  

<span id="005">5</span> And [Gera](../ebd/ebd146.htm#003), and
Shephuphan, and Huram.  

<span id="006">6</span> And these are the sons of
[Ehud](../ebd/ebd114.htm#000): these are the heads of the fathers of the
inhabitants of [Geba](../ebd/ebd144.htm#004), and they removed them to
Manahath:  

<span id="007">7</span> And [Naaman](../ebd/ebd263.htm#009), and Ahiah,
and [Gera](../ebd/ebd146.htm#003), he removed them, and begat
[Uzza](../ebd/ebd376.htm#000), and [Ahihud](../ebd/ebd013.htm#009).  

<span id="008">8</span> And Shaharaim begat children in the country of
[Moab](../ebd/ebd258.htm#005), after he had sent them away; Hushim and
Baara were his wives.  

<span id="009">9</span> And he begat of Hodesh his wife,
[Jobab](../ebd/ebd208.htm#003), and [Zibia](../ebd/ebd392.htm#005), and
[Mesha](../ebd/ebd250.htm#006), and Malcham,  

<span id="010">10</span> And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were
his sons, heads of the fathers.  

<span id="011">11</span> And of Hushim he begat
[Abitub](../ebd/ebd004.htm#009), and Elpaal.  

<span id="012">12</span> The sons of Elpaal;
[Eber](../ebd/ebd112.htm#001), and [Misham](../ebd/ebd256.htm#008), and
Shamed, who built [Ono](../ebd/ebd279.htm#002), and Lod, with the towns
thereof:  

<span id="013">13</span> [Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005) also, and
[Shema](../ebd/ebd334.htm#003), who were heads of the fathers of the
inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of
[Gath](../ebd/ebd143.htm#009):  

<span id="014">14</span> And [Ahio](../ebd/ebd014.htm#007), Shashak, and
Jeremoth,  

<span id="015">15</span> And [Zebadiah](../ebd/ebd388.htm#005), and
[Arad](../ebd/ebd028.htm#000), and Ader,  

<span id="016">16</span> And [Michael](../ebd/ebd252.htm#009), and
Ispah, and Joha, the sons of [Beriah](../ebd/ebd053.htm#005);  

<span id="017">17</span> And [Zebadiah](../ebd/ebd388.htm#005), and
[Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001), and Hezeki, and
[Heber](../ebd/ebd170.htm#008),  

<span id="018">18</span> Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and
[Jobab](../ebd/ebd208.htm#003), the sons of Elpaal;  

<span id="019">19</span> And [Jakim](../ebd/ebd196.htm#004), and
[Zichri](../ebd/ebd392.htm#007), and [Zabdi](../ebd/ebd385.htm#008),  

<span id="020">20</span> And Elienai, and
[Zilthai](../ebd/ebd393.htm#006), and [Eliel](../ebd/ebd116.htm#006),  

<span id="021">21</span> And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and
[Shimrath](../ebd/ebd338.htm#005), the sons of
[Shimhi](../ebd/ebd338.htm#004);  

<span id="022">22</span> And Ishpan, and [Heber](../ebd/ebd170.htm#008),
and [Eliel](../ebd/ebd116.htm#006),  

<span id="023">23</span> And [Abdon](../ebd/ebd001.htm#002), and
[Zichri](../ebd/ebd392.htm#007), and [Hanan](../ebd/ebd162.htm#001),  

<span id="024">24</span> And [Hananiah](../ebd/ebd162.htm#004), and
[Elam](../ebd/ebd114.htm#003), and Antothijah,  

<span id="025">25</span> And [Iphedeiah](../ebd/ebd188.htm#009), and
[Penuel](../ebd/ebd289.htm#005), the sons of Shashak;  

<span id="026">26</span> And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and
[Athaliah](../ebd/ebd036.htm#002),  

<span id="027">27</span> And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and
[Zichri](../ebd/ebd392.htm#007), the sons of
[Jeroham](../ebd/ebd204.htm#001).  

<span id="028">28</span> These were heads of the fathers, by their
generations, chief men. These dwelt in
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005).  

<span id="029">29</span> And at [Gibeon](../ebd/ebd148.htm#002) dwelt
the father of [Gibeon](../ebd/ebd148.htm#002); whose wife's name was
[Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004):  

<span id="030">30</span> And his firstborn son
[Abdon](../ebd/ebd001.htm#002), and [Zur](../ebd/ebd396.htm#005), and
[Kish](../ebd/ebd220.htm#007), and [Baal](../ebd/ebd038.htm#004), and
[Nadab](../ebd/ebd264.htm#008),  

<span id="031">31</span> And [Gedor](../ebd/ebd145.htm#003), and
[Ahio](../ebd/ebd014.htm#007), and [Zacher](../ebd/ebd386.htm#007).  

<span id="032">32</span> And [Mikloth](../ebd/ebd254.htm#005) begat
[Shimeah](../ebd/ebd338.htm#001). And these also dwelt with their
brethren in [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005), over against them.  

<span id="033">33</span> And [Ner](../ebd/ebd270.htm#007) begat
[Kish](../ebd/ebd220.htm#007), and [Kish](../ebd/ebd220.htm#007) begat
[Saul](../ebd/ebd323.htm#002), and [Saul](../ebd/ebd323.htm#002) begat
[Jonathan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#000), and Malchishua, and
[Abinadab](../ebd/ebd004.htm#001), and
[Eshbaal](../ebd/ebd124.htm#009).  

<span id="034">34</span> And the son of
[Jonathan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#000) was Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat
[Micah](../ebd/ebd252.htm#005).  

<span id="035">35</span> And the sons of [Micah](../ebd/ebd252.htm#005)
were, Pithon, and [Melech](../ebd/ebd247.htm#005), and Tarea, and
[Ahaz](../ebd/ebd013.htm#005).  

<span id="036">36</span> And [Ahaz](../ebd/ebd013.htm#005) begat
Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat [Alemeth](../ebd/ebd016.htm#008), and
[Azmaveth](../ebd/ebd038.htm#000), and [Zimri](../ebd/ebd393.htm#009);
and [Zimri](../ebd/ebd393.htm#009) begat
[Moza](../ebd/ebd261.htm#007),  

<span id="037">37</span> And [Moza](../ebd/ebd261.htm#007) begat Binea:
[Rapha](../ebd/ebd307.htm#006) was his son, Eleasah his son,
[Azel](../ebd/ebd037.htm#009) his son:  

<span id="038">38</span> And [Azel](../ebd/ebd037.htm#009) had six sons,
whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and
[Ishmael](../ebd/ebd190.htm#005), and Sheariah, and
[Obadiah](../ebd/ebd276.htm#001), and [Hanan](../ebd/ebd162.htm#001).
All these were the sons of [Azel](../ebd/ebd037.htm#009).  

<span id="039">39</span> And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam
his firstborn, Jehush the second, and [Eliphelet](../ebd/ebd117.htm#007)
the third.  

<span id="040">40</span> And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour,
archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All
these are of the sons of [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008).
