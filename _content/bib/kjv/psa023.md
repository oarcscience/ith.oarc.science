### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 23

<span id="001">1</span> The LORD is my
[shepherd](../ebd/ebd335.htm#006); I shall not want.  

<span id="002">2</span> He maketh me to lie down in green pastures: he
leadeth me beside the still waters.  

<span id="003">3</span> He restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the paths
of [righteousness](../ebd/ebd313.htm#004) for his name's sake.  

<span id="004">4</span> Yea, though I walk through the
[valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008) of the [shadow](../ebd/ebd330.htm#002)
of death, I will fear no evil: for thou art with me; thy rod and thy
staff they comfort me.  

<span id="005">5</span> Thou preparest a table before me in the presence
of mine enemies: thou anointest my head with oil; my
[cup](../ebd/ebd094.htm#000) runneth over.  

<span id="006">6</span> Surely [goodness](../ebd/ebd153.htm#000) and
[mercy](../ebd/ebd249.htm#005) shall follow me all the days of my life:
and I will dwell in the house of the LORD for ever.
