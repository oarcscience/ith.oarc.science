### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 110

<span id="001">1</span> The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right
hand, until I make thine enemies thy
[footstool](../ebd/ebd136.htm#009).  

<span id="002">2</span> The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out
of [Zion](../ebd/ebd394.htm#002): rule thou in the midst of thine
enemies.  

<span id="003">3</span> Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy
power, in the beauties of [holiness](../ebd/ebd180.htm#006) from the
womb of the morning: thou hast the [dew](../ebd/ebd102.htm#008) of thy
youth.  

<span id="004">4</span> The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou
art a [priest](../ebd/ebd300.htm#003) for ever after the order of
[Melchizedek](../ebd/ebd247.htm#003).  

<span id="005">5</span> The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through
kings in the day of his wrath.  

<span id="006">6</span> He shall [judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005) among
the [heathen](../ebd/ebd170.htm#005), he shall fill the places with the
dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.  

<span id="007">7</span> He shall [drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) of the
[brook](../ebd/ebd065.htm#006) in the way: therefore shall he lift up
the head.
