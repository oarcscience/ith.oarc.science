### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 67

<span id="001">1</span> God be merciful unto us, and
[bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008) us; and cause his face to shine upon us;
[Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="002">2</span> That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving
health among all nations.  

<span id="003">3</span> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the
people praise thee.  

<span id="004">4</span> O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for
thou shalt [judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005) the people righteously, and
govern the nations upon earth. [Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="005">5</span> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the
people praise thee.  

<span id="006">6</span> Then shall the earth yield her increase; and
God, even our own God, shall [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008) us.  

<span id="007">7</span> God shall [bless](../ebd/ebd060.htm#008) us; and
all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
