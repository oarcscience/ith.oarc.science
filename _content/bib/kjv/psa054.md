### King James Version: Psalms Chapter 54

<span id="001">1</span> Save me, O God, by thy name, and
[judge](../ebd/ebd213.htm#005) me by thy strength.  

<span id="002">2</span> Hear my [prayer](../ebd/ebd300.htm#000), O God;
give [ear](../ebd/ebd110.htm#004) to the words of my mouth.  

<span id="003">3</span> For strangers are risen up against me, and
oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them.
[Selah](../ebd/ebd326.htm#008).  

<span id="004">4</span> Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with
them that uphold my soul.  

<span id="005">5</span> He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them
off in thy truth.  

<span id="006">6</span> I will freely [sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002)
unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good.  

<span id="007">7</span> For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and
mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies.
