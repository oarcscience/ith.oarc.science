### King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 2

<span id="001">1</span> These are the sons of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000); [Reuben](../ebd/ebd311.htm#009),
[Simeon](../ebd/ebd344.htm#002), [Levi](../ebd/ebd227.htm#006), and
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), [Issachar](../ebd/ebd191.htm#002), and
[Zebulun](../ebd/ebd389.htm#003),  

<span id="002">2</span> [Dan](../ebd/ebd096.htm#009),
[Joseph](../ebd/ebd211.htm#005), and [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008),
[Naphtali](../ebd/ebd266.htm#006), [Gad](../ebd/ebd140.htm#007), and
[Asher](../ebd/ebd033.htm#008).  

<span id="003">3</span> The sons of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007); Er,
and [Onan](../ebd/ebd278.htm#008), and [Shelah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#000):
which three were born unto him of the daughter of
[Shua](../ebd/ebd341.htm#005) the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), was evil in the sight of the LORD; and
he slew him.  

<span id="004">4</span> And [Tamar](../ebd/ebd358.htm#003) his daughter
in law bore him [Pharez](../ebd/ebd292.htm#007) and
[Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001). All the sons of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) were five.  

<span id="005">5</span> The sons of [Pharez](../ebd/ebd292.htm#007);
[Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007), and [Hamul](../ebd/ebd161.htm#008).  

<span id="006">6</span> And the sons of [Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001);
[Zimri](../ebd/ebd393.htm#009), and [Ethan](../ebd/ebd126.htm#002), and
[Heman](../ebd/ebd173.htm#009), and [Calcol](../ebd/ebd069.htm#002), and
Dara: five of them in all.  

<span id="007">7</span> And the sons of [Carmi](../ebd/ebd072.htm#007);
Achar, the troubler of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), who transgressed
in the thing accursed.  

<span id="008">8</span> And the sons of [Ethan](../ebd/ebd126.htm#002);
[Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005).  

<span id="009">9</span> The sons also of
[Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007), that were born unto him;
[Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005), and [Ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003),
and Chelubai.  

<span id="010">10</span> And [Ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003) begat
[Amminadab](../ebd/ebd020.htm#008); and
[Amminadab](../ebd/ebd020.htm#008) begat
[Nahshon](../ebd/ebd265.htm#007), [prince](../ebd/ebd300.htm#004) of the
children of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007);  

<span id="011">11</span> And [Nahshon](../ebd/ebd265.htm#007) begat
Salma, and Salma begat [Boaz](../ebd/ebd061.htm#006),  

<span id="012">12</span> And [Boaz](../ebd/ebd061.htm#006) begat
[Obed](../ebd/ebd276.htm#004), and [Obed](../ebd/ebd276.htm#004) begat
[Jesse](../ebd/ebd205.htm#005),  

<span id="013">13</span> And [Jesse](../ebd/ebd205.htm#005) begat his
firstborn [Eliab](../ebd/ebd115.htm#008), and
[Abinadab](../ebd/ebd004.htm#001) the second, and Shimma the third,  

<span id="014">14</span> [Nethaneel](../ebd/ebd271.htm#003) the fourth,
Raddai the fifth,  

<span id="015">15</span> [Ozem](../ebd/ebd281.htm#009) the sixth,
[David](../ebd/ebd098.htm#004) the seventh:  

<span id="016">16</span> Whose sisters were
[Zeruiah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#009), and [Abigail](../ebd/ebd003.htm#002).
And the sons of [Zeruiah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#009);
[Abishai](../ebd/ebd004.htm#005), and [Joab](../ebd/ebd207.htm#006), and
[Asahel](../ebd/ebd033.htm#001), three.  

<span id="017">17</span> And [Abigail](../ebd/ebd003.htm#002) bare
[Amasa](../ebd/ebd019.htm#003): and the father of
[Amasa](../ebd/ebd019.htm#003) was [Jether](../ebd/ebd205.htm#007) the
Ishmeelite.  

<span id="018">18</span> And [Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003) the son of
[Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007) begat children of
[Azubah](../ebd/ebd038.htm#002) his wife, and of Jerioth: her sons are
these; [Jesher](../ebd/ebd205.htm#001), and
[Shobab](../ebd/ebd340.htm#001), and [Ardon](../ebd/ebd029.htm#009).  

<span id="019">19</span> And when [Azubah](../ebd/ebd038.htm#002) was
dead, [Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003) took unto him Ephrath, which bare
him [Hur](../ebd/ebd184.htm#009).  

<span id="020">20</span> And [Hur](../ebd/ebd184.htm#009) begat Uri, and
Uri begat [Bezaleel](../ebd/ebd057.htm#009).  

<span id="021">21</span> And afterward [Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007)
went in to the daughter of [Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) the father of
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009), whom he married when he was threescore
years old; and she bare him [Segub](../ebd/ebd326.htm#003).  

<span id="022">22</span> And [Segub](../ebd/ebd326.htm#003) begat
[Jair](../ebd/ebd196.htm#001), who had three and twenty cities in the
land of [Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009).  

<span id="023">23</span> And he took [Geshur](../ebd/ebd147.htm#001),
and [Aram](../ebd/ebd028.htm#001), with the towns of
[Jair](../ebd/ebd196.htm#001), from them, with
[Kenath](../ebd/ebd217.htm#002), and the towns thereof, even threescore
cities. All these belonged to the sons of
[Machir](../ebd/ebd235.htm#006) the father of
[Gilead](../ebd/ebd148.htm#009).  

<span id="024">24</span> And after that [Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007)
was dead in Calebephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the
father of Tekoa.  

<span id="025">25</span> And the sons of
[Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005) the firstborn of
[Hezron](../ebd/ebd177.htm#007) were, [Ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003) the
firstborn, and Bunah, and [Oren](../ebd/ebd280.htm#003), and
[Ozem](../ebd/ebd281.htm#009), and [Ahijah](../ebd/ebd014.htm#000).  

<span id="026">26</span> [Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005) had also
another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.  

<span id="027">27</span> And the sons of [Ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003)
the firstborn of [Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005) were, Maaz, and
Jamin, and Eker.  

<span id="028">28</span> And the sons of Onam were, Shammai, and Jada.
And the sons of Shammai; [Nadab](../ebd/ebd264.htm#008) and
[Abishur](../ebd/ebd004.htm#007).  

<span id="029">29</span> And the name of the wife of
[Abishur](../ebd/ebd004.htm#007) was [Abihail](../ebd/ebd003.htm#003),
and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.  

<span id="030">30</span> And the sons of [Nadab](../ebd/ebd264.htm#008);
Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children.  

<span id="031">31</span> And the sons of Appaim;
[Ishi](../ebd/ebd190.htm#004). And the sons of
[Ishi](../ebd/ebd190.htm#004); Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan;
Ahlai.  

<span id="032">32</span> And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai;
[Jether](../ebd/ebd205.htm#007), and [Jonathan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#000):
and [Jether](../ebd/ebd205.htm#007) died without children.  

<span id="033">33</span> And the sons of
[Jonathan](../ebd/ebd211.htm#000); [Peleth](../ebd/ebd288.htm#009), and
[Zaza](../ebd/ebd388.htm#002). These were the sons of
[Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005).  

<span id="034">34</span> Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And
Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.  

<span id="035">35</span> And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his
servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.  

<span id="036">36</span> And Attai begat
[Nathan](../ebd/ebd267.htm#002), and [Nathan](../ebd/ebd267.htm#002)
begat [Zabad](../ebd/ebd385.htm#005),  

<span id="037">37</span> And [Zabad](../ebd/ebd385.htm#005) begat
Ephlal, and Ephlal begat [Obed](../ebd/ebd276.htm#004),  

<span id="038">38</span> And [Obed](../ebd/ebd276.htm#004) begat
[Jehu](../ebd/ebd202.htm#007), and [Jehu](../ebd/ebd202.htm#007) begat
[Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005),  

<span id="039">39</span> And [Azariah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#005) begat
[Helez](../ebd/ebd172.htm#008), and [Helez](../ebd/ebd172.htm#008) begat
Eleasah,  

<span id="040">40</span> And Eleasah begat Sisamai, and Sisamai begat
[Shallum](../ebd/ebd330.htm#008),  

<span id="041">41</span> And [Shallum](../ebd/ebd330.htm#008) begat
Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat [Elishama](../ebd/ebd118.htm#001).  

<span id="042">42</span> Now the sons of [Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003)
the brother of [Jerahmeel](../ebd/ebd203.htm#005) were,
[Mesha](../ebd/ebd250.htm#006) his firstborn, which was the father of
[Ziph](../ebd/ebd394.htm#004); and the sons of
[Mareshah](../ebd/ebd241.htm#009) the father of
[Hebron](../ebd/ebd171.htm#004).  

<span id="043">43</span> And the sons of
[Hebron](../ebd/ebd171.htm#004); [Korah](../ebd/ebd222.htm#003), and
[Tappuah](../ebd/ebd358.htm#008), and [Rekem](../ebd/ebd310.htm#002),
and [Shema](../ebd/ebd334.htm#003).  

<span id="044">44</span> And [Shema](../ebd/ebd334.htm#003) begat
[Raham](../ebd/ebd305.htm#007), the father of Jorkoam: and
[Rekem](../ebd/ebd310.htm#002) begat Shammai.  

<span id="045">45</span> And the son of Shammai was
[Maon](../ebd/ebd241.htm#001): and [Maon](../ebd/ebd241.htm#001) was the
father of [Bethzur](../ebd/ebd057.htm#004).  

<span id="046">46</span> And [Ephah](../ebd/ebd122.htm#003), Caleb's
[concubine](../ebd/ebd087.htm#007), bare [Haran](../ebd/ebd163.htm#007),
and [Moza](../ebd/ebd261.htm#007), and Gazez: and
[Haran](../ebd/ebd163.htm#007) begat Gazez.  

<span id="047">47</span> And the sons of
[Jahdai](../ebd/ebd195.htm#007); Regem, and
[Jotham](../ebd/ebd212.htm#000), and Gesham, and
[Pelet](../ebd/ebd288.htm#008), and [Ephah](../ebd/ebd122.htm#003), and
Shaaph.  

<span id="048">48</span> [Maachah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#004), Caleb's
[concubine](../ebd/ebd087.htm#007), bare Sheber, and Tirhanah.  

<span id="049">49</span> She bare also Shaaph the father of
[Madmannah](../ebd/ebd235.htm#009), [Sheva](../ebd/ebd336.htm#007) the
father of Machbenah, and the father of Gibea: and the daughter of
[Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003) was Achsa.  

<span id="050">50</span> These were the sons of
[Caleb](../ebd/ebd069.htm#003) the son of [Hur](../ebd/ebd184.htm#009),
the firstborn of [Ephratah](../ebd/ebd123.htm#006);
[Shobal](../ebd/ebd340.htm#004) the father of Kirjathjearim.  

<span id="051">51</span> Salma the father of
[Bethlehem](../ebd/ebd056.htm#005), Hareph the father of Bethgader.  

<span id="052">52</span> And [Shobal](../ebd/ebd340.htm#004) the father
of Kirjathjearim had sons; Haroeh, and half of the Manahethites.  

<span id="053">53</span> And the families of Kirjathjearim; the
Ithrites, and the Puhites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of
them came the Zareathites, and the Eshtaulites,  

<span id="054">54</span> The sons of Salma;
[Bethlehem](../ebd/ebd056.htm#005), and the Netophathites,
[Ataroth](../ebd/ebd036.htm#000), the house of
[Joab](../ebd/ebd207.htm#006), and half of the Manahethites, the
Zorites.  

<span id="055">55</span> And the families of the
[scribes](../ebd/ebd324.htm#003) which dwelt at
[Jabez](../ebd/ebd193.htm#009); the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, and
Suchathites. These are the [Kenites](../ebd/ebd217.htm#004) that came of
[Hemath](../ebd/ebd174.htm#000), the father of the house of
[Rechab](../ebd/ebd308.htm#002).
