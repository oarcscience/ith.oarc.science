### King James Version: Nehemiah Chapter 11

<span id="001">1</span> And the rulers of the people dwelt at
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005): the rest of the people also cast
lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005)
the holy city, and nine parts to dwell in other cities.  

<span id="002">2</span> And the people blessed all the men, that
willingly offered themselves to dwell at
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005).  

<span id="003">3</span> Now these are the chief of the province that
dwelt in [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005): but in the cities of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) dwelt every one in his possession in
their cities, to wit, [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), the priests, and
the Levites, and the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon's
servants.  

<span id="004">4</span> And at [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005) dwelt
certain of the children of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), and of the
children of [Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008). Of the children of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007); Athaiah the son of
[Uzziah](../ebd/ebd376.htm#004), the son of
[Zechariah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#006), the son of
[Amariah](../ebd/ebd019.htm#002), the son of
[Shephatiah](../ebd/ebd335.htm#005), the son of
[Mahalaleel](../ebd/ebd237.htm#001), of the children of
[Perez](../ebd/ebd289.htm#009);  

<span id="005">5</span> And [Maaseiah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#007) the son of
[Baruch](../ebd/ebd046.htm#001), the son of Colhozeh, the son of
Hazaiah, the son of Adaiah, the son of [Joiarib](../ebd/ebd209.htm#008),
the son of [Zechariah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#006), the son of Shiloni.  

<span id="006">6</span> All the sons of [Perez](../ebd/ebd289.htm#009)
that dwelt at [Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005) were four hundred
threescore and eight valiant men.  

<span id="007">7</span> And these are the sons of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008); [Sallu](../ebd/ebd319.htm#004) the
son of [Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001), the son of Joed, the son of
[Pedaiah](../ebd/ebd288.htm#001), the son of Kolaiah, the son of
[Maaseiah](../ebd/ebd234.htm#007), the son of Ithiel, the son of
Jesaiah.  

<span id="008">8</span> And after him Gabbai,
[Sallai](../ebd/ebd319.htm#003), nine hundred twenty and eight.  

<span id="009">9</span> And [Joel](../ebd/ebd208.htm#005) the son of
[Zichri](../ebd/ebd392.htm#007) was their overseer: and
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) the son of Senuah was second over the
city.  

<span id="010">10</span> Of the priests:
[Jedaiah](../ebd/ebd199.htm#006) the son of
[Joiarib](../ebd/ebd209.htm#008), [Jachin](../ebd/ebd194.htm#004).  

<span id="011">11</span> [Seraiah](../ebd/ebd328.htm#003) the son of
[Hilkiah](../ebd/ebd178.htm#006), the son of
[Meshullam](../ebd/ebd251.htm#001), the son of
[Zadok](../ebd/ebd386.htm#008), the son of
[Meraioth](../ebd/ebd248.htm#009), the son of
[Ahitub](../ebd/ebd015.htm#001), was the ruler of the house of God.  

<span id="012">12</span> And their brethren that did the work of the
house were eight hundred twenty and two: and Adaiah the son of
[Jeroham](../ebd/ebd204.htm#001), the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi,
the son of [Zechariah](../ebd/ebd389.htm#006), the son of
[Pashur](../ebd/ebd286.htm#002), the son of
[Malchiah](../ebd/ebd239.htm#001).  

<span id="013">13</span> And his brethren, chief of the fathers, two
hundred forty and two: and [Amashai](../ebd/ebd019.htm#005) the son of
Azareel, the son of Ahasai, the son of
[Meshillemoth](../ebd/ebd251.htm#000), the son of
[Immer](../ebd/ebd187.htm#008),  

<span id="014">14</span> And their brethren, mighty men of valour, an
hundred twenty and eight: and their overseer was
[Zabdiel](../ebd/ebd385.htm#009), the son of one of the great men.  

<span id="015">15</span> Also of the Levites:
[Shemaiah](../ebd/ebd334.htm#005) the son of
[Hashub](../ebd/ebd166.htm#005), the son of Azrikam, the son of
[Hashabiah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#001), the son of Bunni;  

<span id="016">16</span> And [Shabbethai](../ebd/ebd330.htm#000) and
[Jozabad](../ebd/ebd212.htm#002), of the chief of the Levites, had the
oversight of the outward business of the house of God.  

<span id="017">17</span> And [Mattaniah](../ebd/ebd243.htm#009) the son
of [Micha](../ebd/ebd252.htm#008), the son of
[Zabdi](../ebd/ebd385.htm#008), the son of
[Asaph](../ebd/ebd033.htm#002), was the principal to begin the
thanksgiving in [prayer](../ebd/ebd300.htm#000): and Bakbukiah the
second among his brethren, and [Abda](../ebd/ebd000.htm#008) the son of
[Shammua](../ebd/ebd331.htm#003), the son of Galal, the son of
[Jeduthun](../ebd/ebd199.htm#009).  

<span id="018">18</span> All the Levites in the holy city were two
hundred fourscore and four.  

<span id="019">19</span> Moreover the porters,
[Akkub](../ebd/ebd016.htm#003), [Talmon](../ebd/ebd358.htm#002), and
their brethren that kept the gates, were an hundred seventy and two.  

<span id="020">20</span> And the residue of
[Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000), of the priests, and the Levites, were
in all the cities of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), every one in his
inheritance.  

<span id="021">21</span> But the Nethinims dwelt in
[Ophel](../ebd/ebd279.htm#006): and [Ziha](../ebd/ebd393.htm#002) and
Gispa were over the Nethinims.  

<span id="022">22</span> The overseer also of the Levites at
[Jerusalem](../ebd/ebd204.htm#005) was [Uzzi](../ebd/ebd376.htm#003) the
son of [Bani](../ebd/ebd043.htm#004), the son of
[Hashabiah](../ebd/ebd166.htm#001), the son of
[Mattaniah](../ebd/ebd243.htm#009), the son of
[Micha](../ebd/ebd252.htm#008). Of the sons of
[Asaph](../ebd/ebd033.htm#002), the singers were over the business of
the house of God.  

<span id="023">23</span> For it was the king's commandment concerning
them, that a certain portion should be for the singers, due for every
day.  

<span id="024">24</span> And [Pethahiah](../ebd/ebd291.htm#005) the son
of Meshezabeel, of the children of [Zerah](../ebd/ebd391.htm#001) the
son of [Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), was at the king's hand in all
matters concerning the people.  

<span id="025">25</span> And for the [villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003),
with their fields, some of the children of
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007) dwelt at Kirjatharba, and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof, and at
[Dibon](../ebd/ebd103.htm#005), and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof, and at Jekabzeel, and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof,  

<span id="026">26</span> And at [Jeshua](../ebd/ebd205.htm#003), and at
[Moladah](../ebd/ebd258.htm#008), and at Bethphelet,  

<span id="027">27</span> And at Hazarshual, and at
[Beersheba](../ebd/ebd050.htm#008), and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof,  

<span id="028">28</span> And at [Ziklag](../ebd/ebd393.htm#003), and at
[Mekonah](../ebd/ebd247.htm#001), and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof,  

<span id="029">29</span> And at Enrimmon, and at Zareah, and at
[Jarmuth](../ebd/ebd197.htm#007),  

<span id="030">30</span> [Zanoah](../ebd/ebd387.htm#004),
[Adullam](../ebd/ebd010.htm#007), and in their
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003), at [Lachish](../ebd/ebd222.htm#009),
and the fields thereof, at [Azekah](../ebd/ebd037.htm#008), and in the
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003) thereof. And they dwelt from
[Beersheba](../ebd/ebd050.htm#008) unto the
[valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008) of [Hinnom](../ebd/ebd179.htm#002).  

<span id="031">31</span> The children also of
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008) from [Geba](../ebd/ebd144.htm#004)
dwelt at [Michmash](../ebd/ebd253.htm#002), and Aija, and
[Bethel](../ebd/ebd055.htm#006), and in their
[villages](../ebd/ebd377.htm#003).  

<span id="032">32</span> And at [Anathoth](../ebd/ebd023.htm#004),
[Nob](../ebd/ebd274.htm#003), [Ananiah](../ebd/ebd023.htm#000),  

<span id="033">33</span> [Hazor](../ebd/ebd169.htm#006),
[Ramah](../ebd/ebd306.htm#005), [Gittaim](../ebd/ebd149.htm#008),  

<span id="034">34</span> [Hadid](../ebd/ebd157.htm#009),
[Zeboim](../ebd/ebd388.htm#009), [Neballat](../ebd/ebd268.htm#002),  

<span id="035">35</span> Lod, and [Ono](../ebd/ebd279.htm#002), the
[valley](../ebd/ebd376.htm#008) of craftsmen.  

<span id="036">36</span> And of the Levites were divisions in
[Judah](../ebd/ebd212.htm#007), and in
[Benjamin](../ebd/ebd052.htm#008).
