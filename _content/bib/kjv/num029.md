### King James Version: Numbers Chapter 29

<span id="001">1</span> And in the seventh month, on the first day of
the month, ye shall have an holy [convocation](../ebd/ebd089.htm#003);
ye shall do no servile work: it is a day of blowing the
[trumpets](../ebd/ebd373.htm#001) unto you.  

<span id="002">2</span> And ye shall offer a burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) for a sweet savour unto the LORD; one
young [bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), one
[ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003), and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) lambs
of the first year without [blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="003">3</span> And their meat [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001)
shall be of [flour](../ebd/ebd136.htm#001) mingled with oil, three tenth
deals for a [bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), and two tenth deals for a
[ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003),  

<span id="004">4</span> And one tenth deal for one
[lamb](../ebd/ebd223.htm#003), throughout the
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) lambs:  

<span id="005">5</span> And one kid of the goats for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), to make
an [atonement](../ebd/ebd036.htm#004) for you:  

<span id="006">6</span> Beside the burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of the month, and his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and the daily burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings, according unto their manner,
for a sweet savour, a [sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002) made by fire
unto the LORD.  

<span id="007">7</span> And ye shall have on the tenth day of this
seventh month an holy [convocation](../ebd/ebd089.htm#003); and ye shall
afflict your souls: ye shall not do any work therein:  

<span id="008">8</span> But ye shall offer a burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) unto the LORD for a sweet savour; one
young [bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), one
[ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003), and [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) lambs
of the first year; they shall be unto you without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="009">9</span> And their meat [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001)
shall be of [flour](../ebd/ebd136.htm#001) mingled with oil, three tenth
deals to a [bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), and two tenth deals to one
[ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003),  

<span id="010">10</span> A several tenth deal for one
[lamb](../ebd/ebd223.htm#003), throughout the
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) lambs:  

<span id="011">11</span> One kid of the goats for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the [sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of
[atonement](../ebd/ebd036.htm#004), and the continual burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and the meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) of it, and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings.  

<span id="012">12</span> And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month
ye shall have an holy [convocation](../ebd/ebd089.htm#003); ye shall do
no servile work, and ye shall keep a [feast](../ebd/ebd132.htm#000) unto
the LORD [seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) days:  

<span id="013">13</span> And ye shall offer a burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), a [sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002)
made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD; thirteen young bullocks,
two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year; they shall be without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="014">14</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) shall be of
[flour](../ebd/ebd136.htm#001) mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto
every [bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003) of the thirteen bullocks, two
tenth deals to each [ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003) of the two rams,  

<span id="015">15</span> And a several tenth deal to each
[lamb](../ebd/ebd223.htm#003) of the fourteen lambs:  

<span id="016">16</span> And one kid of the goats for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="017">17</span> And on the second day ye shall offer twelve
young bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs of the first year without
spot:  

<span id="018">18</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for the rams,
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="019">19</span> And one kid of the goats for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and the meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) thereof, and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings.  

<span id="020">20</span> And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams,
fourteen lambs of the first year without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007);  

<span id="021">21</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for the rams,
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="022">22</span> And one [goat](../ebd/ebd151.htm#001) for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="023">23</span> And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams,
and fourteen lambs of the first year without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="024">24</span> Their meat [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001)
and their [drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for
the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after
the manner:  

<span id="025">25</span> And one kid of the goats for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="026">26</span> And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams,
and fourteen lambs of the first year without spot:  

<span id="027">27</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for the rams,
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="028">28</span> And one [goat](../ebd/ebd151.htm#001) for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="029">29</span> And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams,
and fourteen lambs of the first year without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="030">30</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for the rams,
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="031">31</span> And one [goat](../ebd/ebd151.htm#001) for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="032">32</span> And on the seventh day
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of
the first year without [blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="033">33</span> And their meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001) and their
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the bullocks, for the rams,
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="034">34</span> And one [goat](../ebd/ebd151.htm#001) for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="035">35</span> On the eighth day ye shall have a solemn
assembly: ye shall do no servile work therein:  

<span id="036">36</span> But ye shall offer a burnt
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), a [sacrifice](../ebd/ebd318.htm#002)
made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: one
[bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), one [ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003),
[seven](../ebd/ebd329.htm#005) lambs of the first year without
[blemish](../ebd/ebd060.htm#007):  

<span id="037">37</span> Their meat [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001)
and their [drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings for the
[bullock](../ebd/ebd066.htm#003), for the [ram](../ebd/ebd306.htm#003),
and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the
manner:  

<span id="038">38</span> And one [goat](../ebd/ebd151.htm#001) for a
[sin](../ebd/ebd344.htm#006) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001); beside
the continual burnt [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his meat
[offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001), and his
[drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) [offering](../ebd/ebd277.htm#001).  

<span id="039">39</span> These things ye shall do unto the LORD in your
set feasts, beside your [vows](../ebd/ebd378.htm#001), and your freewill
offerings, for your burnt offerings, and for your meat offerings, and
for your [drink](../ebd/ebd107.htm#009) offerings, and for your peace
offerings.  

<span id="040">40</span> And [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003) told the
children of [Israel](../ebd/ebd191.htm#000) according to all that the
LORD commanded [Moses](../ebd/ebd260.htm#003).
