### CHAPTER XXIX.

1\. And thence I went to another place in the desert, and approached to
the east of this mountain range. 2. And ⌈⌈there⌉⌉ I saw **aromatic**
trees exhaling the fragrance of frankincense and myrrh, and the trees
also were similar to the almond tree.
