### GENESIS CHAPTER 5

<span id="an_gen:5:1">1</span> This is the book of the generations of
Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made He
him; <span id="an_gen:5:2">2</span> male and female created He them, and
blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were
created. <span id="an_gen:5:3">3</span> And Adam lived a hundred and
thirty years, and begot a son in his own likeness, after his image; and
called his name Seth. <span id="an_gen:5:4">4</span> And the days of
Adam after he begot Seth were eight hundred years; and he begot sons and
daughters. <span id="an_gen:5:5">5</span> And all the days that Adam
lived were nine hundred and thirty years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:6">6</span> And Seth lived a hundred and five years,
and begot Enosh. <span id="an_gen:5:7">7</span> And Seth lived after he
begot Enosh eight hundred and seven years, and begot sons and daughters.
<span id="an_gen:5:8">8</span> And all the days of Seth were nine
hundred and twelve years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:9">9</span> And Enosh lived ninety years, and begot
Kenan. <span id="an_gen:5:10">10</span> And Enosh lived after he begot
Kenan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begot sons and daughters.
<span id="an_gen:5:11">11</span> And all the days of Enosh were nine
hundred and five years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:12">12</span> And Kenan lived seventy years, and
begot Mahalalel. <span id="an_gen:5:13">13</span> And Kenan lived after
he begot Mahalalel eight hundred and forty years, and begot sons and
daughters. <span id="an_gen:5:14">14</span> And all the days of Kenan
were nine hundred and ten years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:15">15</span> And Mahalalel lived sixty and five
years, and begot Jared. <span id="an_gen:5:16">16</span> And Mahalalel
lived after he begot Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begot
sons and daughters. <span id="an_gen:5:17">17</span> And all the days of
Mahalalel were eight hundred ninety and five years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:18">18</span> And Jared lived a hundred sixty and two
years, and begot Enoch. <span id="an_gen:5:19">19</span> And Jared lived
after he begot Enoch <span id="page_8">p. 8</span> eight hundred years,
and begot sons and daughters. <span id="an_gen:5:20">20</span> And all
the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:21">21</span> And Enoch lived sixty and five years,
and begot Methuselah. <span id="an_gen:5:22">22</span> And Enoch walked
with God after he begot Methuselah three hundred years, and begot sons
and daughters. <span id="an_gen:5:23">23</span> And all the days of
Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years. <span
id="an_gen:5:24">24</span> And Enoch walked with God, and he was not;
for God took him.

<span id="an_gen:5:25">25</span> And Methuselah lived a hundred eighty
and seven years, and begot Lamech. <span id="an_gen:5:26">26</span> And
Methuselah lived after he begot Lamech seven hundred eighty and two
years, and begot sons and daughters. <span
id="an_gen:5:27">27</span> And all the days of Methuselah were nine
hundred sixty and nine years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:28">28</span> And Lamech lived a hundred eighty and
two years, and begot a son. <span id="an_gen:5:29">29</span> And he
called his name Noah, saying: 'This same shall  <span
id="fr_14"></span>[a](#fn_13) comfort us in our work and in the toil of
our hands, which cometh from the ground which the LORD hath cursed.'
<span id="an_gen:5:30">30</span> And Lamech lived after he begot Noah
five hundred ninety and five years, and begot sons and daughters. <span
id="an_gen:5:31">31</span> And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred
seventy and seven years; and he died.

<span id="an_gen:5:32">32</span> And Noah was five hundred years old;
and Noah begot Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
