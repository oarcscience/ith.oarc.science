###### THE

# Lost Books

###### OF THE

# BIBLE

##### BEING ALL THE GOSPELS, EPISTLES, AND OTHER PIECES NOW EXTANT ATTRIBUTED IN THE FIRST FOUR CENTURIES

###### TO

## JESUS CHRIST

### HIS APOSTLES AND THEIR COMPANIONS

##### NOT INCLUDED, BY ITS COMPILERS, IN THE AUTHORIZED NEW TESTAMENT; AND, THE RECENTLY DISCOVERED SYRIAC MSS. OF PILATE'S LETTERS TO TIBERIUS, ETC.

### TRANSLATED FROM THE ORIGINAL TONGUES

##### ILLUSTRATED FROM ANCIENT PAINTINGS AND MISSALS

#### New York: Alpha House

#### \[1926\]

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage, August
2009, by John Bruno Hare. This text is in the public domain in the US
because its copyright was not renewed in a timely fashion.

*"Christ was the joyous boy of the fields. We are not permitted to think
that the shadows of Calvary darkened His pathway as a youth, and the
Apocryphal Books of the New Testament show a great deal of the early
life of Christ not to be found in the four Evangelists."*

—<span class="smallcaps">Dr. Talmage</span>
