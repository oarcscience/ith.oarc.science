### CHAPTER XXX.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: Which of any living
*existence* (zîvêndag-1) is the worse? 3. And in wisdom who is the more
unforeseeing <span id="fr_362"></span>[2](#fn_357)?'

4\. The spirit of wisdom answered (5) thus: 'A life of him is the worse,
who lives in fear and falsehood <span id="fr_363"></span>[3](#fn_358).
6. And in wisdom he is the more unforeseeing, who does not provide
for <span id="fr_364"></span>[4](#fn_359) the spiritual *existence*, and
attends to the worldly *one*.'
