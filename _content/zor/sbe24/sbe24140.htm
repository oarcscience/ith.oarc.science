CHAPTER LII.

1. The fifty-second subject is this, that it is incumbent on every one that, every year when the month Fravardîn comes on, he is to provide a sacred cake on the day Khurdâd 1, and whatever they are able to bring to hand, a little of everything, they are to place by that sacred cake, and to consecrate it with the dedicatory formula Ayaranãmka 2, so that

p. 315

the affairs of that year may be better through that day's provision and the entry and coming in of guests.

2. For it is declared in revelation 1, that, every year, when the day Khurdâd of the month Fravardîn comes on, they allot a daily provision for mankind, and whatever one wishes to pass to the lot of mankind in that year they write down that day. 3. Therefore, when they shall consecrate this sacred cake, the archangel Khurdâd 2 is making intercession for that person.
