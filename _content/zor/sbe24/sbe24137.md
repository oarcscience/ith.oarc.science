### CHAPTER XLIX <span id="fr_1345"></span>[6](#fn_1334).

1\. The forty-ninth subject is this, that, when *one*

<span id="page_312">p. 312</span>

gathers up a fire, it is requisite to leave *it for* a time, so that the
ash-bed (bûm) of the fire *may* become cold; afterwards, *one is* to
take *it* up and carry *it* to the precinct of fire. 2. It is not proper
that they carry the ash-bed (zamîn) of a hot fire to the precinct of
fire; so far is notorious.
