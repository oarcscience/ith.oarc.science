### CHAPTER XLIII.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'How is it possible to
make Aûha*r*ma*zd*, the archangels, and the fragrant, well-pleasing
heaven more fully for oneself? 3. And how is it possible to make
Aharman, the wicked, and the demons confounded, and to escape from hell,
the depreciated <span id="fr_443"></span>[2](#fn_438) and dark?'

4\. The spirit of wisdom answered (5) thus: 'To make Aûha*r*ma*zd*, the
lord, and the archangels, and

<span id="page_84">p. 84</span>

the fragrant, well-pleasing heaven for oneself, and <span
id="fr_444"></span>[1](#fn_439) Aharman, the wicked, and the. demons
confounded, and to escape from hell, the dark and depreciated, are
possible thus: (6) that is, when they make the spirit of wisdom a
protection for the back (pû*s*tîkpânakîh), (7) and wear the spirit of
contentment on the body, like arms and armour and valour, (8) and make
the spirit of truth <span id="fr_445"></span>[2](#fn_440) a shield, (9)
the spirit of thankfulness a club, (To) the spirit of complete
mindfulness a bow, (11) and the spirit of liberality an arrow; (12) and
they make the spirit of moderation like a spear, (13) the spirit of
perseverance a gauntlet, and they put forth the spirit of destiny as a
protection <span id="fr_446"></span>[3](#fn_441). 14. In this manner it
is possible to come to heaven and the sight of the sacred beings, and to
escape from Aharman, the wicked, and hell, the depreciated.'
