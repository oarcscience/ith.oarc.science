### CHAPTER XXIX.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'What is it necessary
to keep with more regard and more protection?'

<span id="page_67">p. 67</span>

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'It is necessary to keep a
young serving-boy (rasîk) <span id="fr_361"></span>[1](#fn_356), a wife,
a beast *of burden*, and a fire with more protection and more regard.'
