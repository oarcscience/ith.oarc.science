### CHAPTER XLIV.

1\. The forty-fourth subject is this, that it is not desirable *for*
those of the good religion, so far as they are able to manage *it*, to
put a bare foot upon the ground <span id="fr_1329"></span>[3](#fn_1318),
because it is a sin, *and* injury <span
id="fr_1330"></span>[4](#fn_1319) occurs to Spendârma*d* <span
id="fr_1331"></span>[5](#fn_1320), the archangel. 2. And they call that
the sin of running *about* uncovered.
