CHAPTER XLIV.

1. The forty-fourth subject is this, that it is not desirable for those of the good religion, so far as they are able to manage it, to put a bare foot upon the ground 3, because it is a sin, and injury 4 occurs to Spendârmad 5, the archangel. 2. And they call that the sin of running about uncovered.
