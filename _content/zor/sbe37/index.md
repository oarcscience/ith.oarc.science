This is part IV of the Sacred Books of the East translation of the
Pahlavi Texts of Zoroastrianism. There is a legend that after Alexander
the Great conquered Persia, a huge amount of Zoroastrian literature
disappeared. In this volume West presents what is known about a number
of fragmentary Zoroastrian texts, some only known by name, including
summaries, digests, and stray quotes from other books.
