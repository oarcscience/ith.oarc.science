### CHAPTER XXV.

*Ganabâ-sar-ni*g*a*d *Nask.*

1\. The second section is the Ar’*g*istân ('*value code*'), particulars
about the value of small consumption of animate, and also that of
inanimate, property; *with* the desirableness of information thereon,
each separately. 2. The value of not destroying a righteous man even for
a decree and justice, *and* of atonement for injuring the
existence <span id="fr_235"></span>[2](#fn_234) of the fire of
Varahrân <span id="fr_236"></span>[3](#fn_235).
