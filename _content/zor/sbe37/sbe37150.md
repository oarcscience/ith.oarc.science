### OBSERVATIONS.

The references are to the pages of the introduction and extant
fragments, and to the books, chapters, and sections of the translations;
the chapters being denoted by the larger ciphers. The letters ch. stand
for chapter, com. for commentator, Dk. for Dinka*rd*, Dv. for
Dîn-vi*g*irgard, Fr. for Fragments, Int. for Introduction, lun. man. for
lunar mansion, m. for mountain, meas. for measure, n for foot-note, Riv.
for Rivâyat, and Zs. for Selections of Zâ*d*-sparam.
