### CHAPTER XXVI.

*Var*s*tmânsar Nask*.

1\. The third fargar*d*, A she m-vohû <span
id="fr_770"></span>[2](#fn_765), is about

<span id="page_233">p. 233</span>

admonition as to the praising of righteousness, which is itself the
production of true awe of Aûha*r*ma*zd*, the perfection of existences,
the better state of prayers <span id="fr_771"></span>[1](#fn_766), and
the greatest assemblage of righteousness, good breeding, humility
(avŏpatagîh), awe of the spiritual *existence*, extreme joyfulness, and
comfort and enlightenment of soul. 2. *Also* the equipment (pa*d*mûkîh)
of him who is practising as a high-priest is righteousness and the
maintenance of the worship *and* obeisance for the spirit of
righteousness.

3\. Of righteousness perfect is the excellence.
