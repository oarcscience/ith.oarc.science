CHAPTER XXV.

Ganabâ-sar-nigad Nask.

1. The second section is the Ar’gistân ('value code'), particulars about the value of small consumption of animate, and also that of inanimate, property; with the desirableness of information thereon, each separately. 2. The value of not destroying a righteous man even for a decree and justice, and of atonement for injuring the existence 2 of the fire of Varahrân 3.

Footnotes

86:3 The sacred Bahirâm fire.
