CHAPTER XXXVI.

Varstmânsar Nask.

1. The thirteenth fargard, Ustavaiti 4, is about the great reward of him who, through virtuous procedure, may occasion the benefit of a man 5 and of the religion of righteousness also. 2. This, too, that the maintenance of righteousness 6 is through the practice of it.

3. About the tokens of a righteous man—that is, the evidence of him—and his reverence for duty and good works; also his imperceptible perversion (kastârîh)—that is, not a single sin is manifest
