### VI <span id="fr_1598"></span>[2](#fn_1569).

Propitiation be to the created body of the Kine of blessed endowment,
and to the Kine's soul (so, if there is one cow presented <span
id="fr_1599"></span>[3](#fn_1570)). Propitiation be to the body and soul
of you two (so, if there are two <span
id="fr_1600"></span>[3](#fn_1570)).—To your body and soul (if there are
three, or the entire herd <span id="fr_1601"></span>[3](#fn_1570)). (The
Ahuna follows.)
