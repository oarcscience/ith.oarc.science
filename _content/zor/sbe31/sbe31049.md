### YASNA XXVII.

#### PRELUDE TO THE CHIEF RECITAL OF THE AHUNA-VAIRYA.

1\. This is to render <span id="fr_1180"></span>[1](#fn_1171) Him who is
of all the greatest, our lord <span id="fr_1181"></span>[2](#fn_1172)
and master <span id="fr_1182"></span>[3](#fn_1173) (even) Ahura Mazda.
And this to smite <span id="fr_1183"></span>[4](#fn_1174) the wicked
Angra Mainyu, and to smite Aêshma of the bloody spear, and the Mazainya
Daêvas, and to smite all the wicked Varenya Daêvas.

2\. And this is to further Ahura Mazda, the resplendent, the glorious,
to further the Bountiful Immortals, and the influences of the star
Ti*s*trya, the resplendent, the glorious, (and) to the furtherance of
the holy man, and of all the (bountiful and) holy creatures of the
Bounteous Spirit.

3-5 <span id="fr_1184"></span>[5](#fn_1175). 6. The Haomas are crushed,
O Mazda, Khshathra, and Asha, O ye Lords! Good is Sraosha who
accompanies the sacrifice with the great glory <span
id="fr_1185"></span>[6](#fn_1176), and may he be present affording
strenuous help.

7\. We are offering saving acts of wisdom and of worship with the sacred
gift of the Ahuna-vairya intoned with sanctity, and of the two mortars
here

<span id="page_281">p. 281</span>

brought forward <span id="fr_1186"></span>[1](#fn_1177) with holy act,
and with that of the correctly uttered words likewise; and therefore may
they be to us the more saving in their wise significance

8-12 <span id="fr_1187"></span>[2](#fn_1178). 13. As the Ahû is
excellent, so is the Ratu (one who rules) from (his) sanctity, a creator
of mental goodness, and of life's actions done for Mazda; and the
Kingdom (is) for Ahura, which to the poor may offer a nurturer. 14.
(What is Your Kingdom, Your riches; how may I be Your own in my actions,
to nourish Your poor, O Mazda? Beyond; yea, beyond all we declare You,
far from Daêvas and Khrafstra-accursed mortals <span
id="fr_1188"></span>[3](#fn_1179)!)

15\. We worship the Ahuna-vairya. We worship Asha Vahi*s*ta, the most
beautiful <span id="fr_1189"></span>[4](#fn_1180), the Bountiful
Immortal <span id="fr_1190"></span>[5](#fn_1181).
