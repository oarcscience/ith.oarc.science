### VISPARAD XVIII (SP. XXI) <span id="fr_1499"></span>[4](#fn_1482).

1\. We worship Ahura Mazda with the u*s*ta <span
id="fr_1500"></span>[5](#fn_1483). And we worship the Amesha Spe*n*ta
with the u*s*ta, and the holy man, the saint. And we worship the prior
world of the holy (and of the clean) with an u*s*ta, and the state of
weal and salvation for the holy man (the saint). 2. And we worship that
life-long state of blessedness (for the holy) which is the evil man's
calamity <span id="fr_1501"></span>[6](#fn_1484); yea, we worship his
eternal <span id="fr_1502"></span>[7](#fn_1485) salvation, and with the
salvation prayer. And we sacrifice to every saint who

<span id="page_360">p. 360</span>

exists, who is now coming into existence, and who shall exist in future.
