### YASNA XIV (SP. XV).

#### DEDICATIONS.

1\. I will come to You, O Ye Bountiful Immortals! as a praiser and a
priest, and an invoker and sacrificer, as a memorising reciter and a
chanter, for Your sacrifice and homage, which are to be offered to You,
the Bountiful Immortals, and for our dedication and sanctification;
(yea, for ours) who are the holy prophets (destined to benefit the
saints).

2\. And to You, O Ye Bountiful Immortals <span
id="fr_1070"></span>[2a](#fn_1062)! would I dedicate the flesh of my
very body <span id="fr_1071"></span>[3](#fn_1063), and all the blessings
of a prospered life <span id="fr_1072"></span>[4](#fn_1064).

3\. In this Zaothra with this Baresman, I desire to

<span id="page_254">p. 254</span>

approach the holy Yazads with my praise <span
id="fr_1073"></span>[1](#fn_1065), and all the holy lords of the ritual
order at their times, Hâvani at his time, and Sâvanghi and Vîsya at
their times. 4. I confess myself a Mazdayasnian, and of Zarathu*s*tra's
order <span id="fr_1074"></span>[2](#fn_1066).

5\. The Zaotar speaks: As an Ahû (revered and) chosen, the Zaotar (?)
speaks forth to me (?).

The Ratu speaks: As an Ahû (revered and) to be chosen, the Zaotar speaks
forth to me.

The Zaotar: So let the Ratu from his Righteousness, holy and learned,
speak forth!
