### CHAPTER LV.

1\. *As to* the fifty-fourth question *and* reply, that *which* you ask
is thus: What is the occupation and capacity (giriftârîh) of the person
*that has* to preserve those who are in their three nights'
*trials* <span id="fr_693"></span>[2](#fn_690), *and* who is he?

2\. The reply is this, that it is said a husband (gab*r*â) is
indispensable for preservation through the three nights' *trials* which
shall be *for* a privileged wife, a father *for* those of a child, *and*
a master *for* those of a servant.
