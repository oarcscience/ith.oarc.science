------------------------------------------------------------------------

FOR GAINING A LAWFUL SUIT.

It reads, if anyone has to settle any just claim by way of a law suit
let him take some of the largest kind of sage and write the name of the
twelve apostles on the leaves, and put them in his shoes before entering
the courthouse, and he shall certainly gain the suit.
