HOW TO BANISH THE FEVER.

Write the following words upon a paper and wrap it up in knot-grass,
(**breiten megrich**,) and then tie it upon the body of the person who
has the fever:

Potmat sineat,  
Potmat sineat,  
Potmat sineat.
