*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

TO STOP PAINS OR SMARTING IN A WOUND.

Cut three small twigs from a tree--each to be cut off in one cut--rub
one end of each twig in the wound, and wrap them separately in a piece
of white paper, and put them in a warm and dry place.
