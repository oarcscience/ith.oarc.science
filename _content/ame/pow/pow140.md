A BENEDICTION AGAINST WORMS.

Peter and Jesus went out upon the fields; they ploughed three furrows,
and ploughed up three worms. The one was white, the other was black, and
the third one was red. Now all the worms are dead, in the name + + +
Repeat these words three times.
