Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend, by John George Hoffman, [1820], at Intangible Textual Heritage

A REMEDY TO RELIEVE PAIN.

Take a rag which was tied over a wound for the first time. and put it in water together with some copperas;

p. 19

but do not venture to stir the copperas until you are certain of the pain having left you.
