*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Another Remedy for Hysterics and for Colds.--This must be attended to
every evening, that is, whenever you pull off your shoes and stockings,
run your finger in between all the toes and smell it. This will
certainly effect a cure.
