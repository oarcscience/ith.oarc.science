Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

TO CURE ANY EXCRESCENCE OR WEN ON A HORSE.

Take any bone which you accidentally find, for you

<span id="page_26">p. 26</span>

dare not be looking for it, and rub the wen of the horse with it, always
bearing in mind that it must be done in the decreasing moon, and the wen
will certainly disappear. The bone, however, must be replaced as it was
lying before.
