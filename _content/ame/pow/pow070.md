HOW TO PREPARE A GOOD EYE-WATER.

Take one ounce of white vitriol and one ounce of sugar of lead, dissolve
them in oil of rosemary, and put it in a quart bottle, which you fill up
with rose-water. Bathe the eyes with it night and morning.
