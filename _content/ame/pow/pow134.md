A BENEDICTION FOR ALL PURPOSES.

Jesus, I will arise; Jesus, do thou accompany me; Jesus, do thou lock my
heart into thine, and let my body and my soul be commended unto thee.
The Lord is crucified. May God guard my senses that evil spirits may

<span id="page_48">p. 48</span>

not overcome me, in the name of God the Father, Son, and the Holy Ghost.
Amen.
