Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

TO REMOVE A WEN DURING THE CRESCENT MOON.

Look over the wen, directly towards the moon, and say "Whatever grows,
does grow; and whatever diminishes, does diminish." This must be said
three times in the same breath.
