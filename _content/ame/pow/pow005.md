*Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend*, by John George Hoffman, \[1820\], at
Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

A certain Remedy to stop Bleeding, which cures, no matter how far a
person be away, if only his first name is rightly pronounced while using
it:

    Jesus Christ dearest blood!  
    That stoppeth the pain and stoppeth the blood.  
In this help you \[*first name*\] God the Father, God the Son, God the
Holy Ghost. Amen.
