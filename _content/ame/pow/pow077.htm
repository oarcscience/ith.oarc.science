Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend, by John George Hoffman, [1820], at Intangible Textual Heritage

A VERY GOOD REMEDY TO CURE SORES.

Boil the bulbs (roots) of the white lily in cream, and

p. 28

put it on the sore in the form of a plaster. Southernwort has the same effect.
