UNITED AIRLINES FLIGHT 93

from Newark to San Francisco. Hijackers crashed this plane, which would have targeted the Capitol or White House, in rural southwest Pennsylvania, after passengers were able to take back the passenger compartment and started to break into the cockpit. 7 crew and 33 passengers perished.
