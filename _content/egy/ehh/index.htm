The Egyptian Heaven and Hell

by E. A. Wallis Budge

[1905]

(original title) The Contents of the Books of the Other World Described and Compared

This is the third volume in Budges' treatment of the Ancient Egyptian Books of the underworld, the Tuat. Budge, who among dozens of other books wrote the Cooks' Tour Guide to Egypt, collates the information from the Book of the Am-Tuat and the Book of Gates as a travel guide.
