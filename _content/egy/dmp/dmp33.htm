VERSO COL. I.

(1) Eyebrow of Ra: ὀφρὺς ἡλίου.(3) Eyebrow of the moon. ὀφρὺς σελήνης.(3) These are some herbs.

(4) Heliogonon. (5) Selenegonon. (6) These are some herbs.
