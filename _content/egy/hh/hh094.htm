XXI. How Anything of Long Duration.

 1 2

A STAG shoots its horns every year, and when depicted, signifies anything of long duration.

Footnotes

102:1 XXI. Several inverted stags’ heads alternating with hieroglyphics, are delineated in the judgment scene, where they seem to have some connexion with duration, perhaps eternity. See Pl. 3. and B. I. c. 69.
