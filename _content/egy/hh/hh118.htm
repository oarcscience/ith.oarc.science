XLV. HOW A WOMAN WHO MISCARRIES.

When they would symbolise a woman who miscarries, they depict a MARE KICKING A WOLF; for not only by

p. 115

kicking a wolf does a mare miscarry, but it immediately miscarries if it should merely tread on the footstep of a wolf.
