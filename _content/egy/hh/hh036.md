### XXXIV. HOW A SOUL CONTINUING A LONG TIME HERE.

<span id="img_05400"></span> ![](img/05400.jpg) <span
id="fr_58"></span>[1](#fn_56)

When they would denote a *soul continuing a long time here*, <span
id="fr_59"></span>[2](#fn_57) or an *inundation*, they depict the PHŒNIX
the bird: <span id="fr_60"></span>[3](#fn_58) and they denote the *soul*
by it, because this is the longest lived of all creatures in the world;
and an *inundation*, because the Phœnix is a symbol of the sun, than
which nothing is greater in the universe. For the sun passes over all
and

<span id="page_55">p. 55</span>

scrutinises all, hence he is called . . . . . Polys <span
id="fr_61"></span>[1](#fn_59) (much).
