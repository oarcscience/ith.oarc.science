XCI. How A MAN DECEIVED BY FLATTERY.

When they would symbolise a man deceived by flattery, they represent A STAG AND A MAN PLAYING ON A PIPE; for she is caught while

p. 142

listening to the sweet breathings of the singers as she stands entranced in pleasure.
