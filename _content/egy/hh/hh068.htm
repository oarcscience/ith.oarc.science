LXVI. HOW A MONTH. 2

 2

To represent a month, they depict as before explained the figure of the MOON when it has attained the age of eight and twenty days of equal lengths, each day containing twenty-four

p. 85

hours, for during these it is apparent, and in the remaining two it is in a state of evanescence.
