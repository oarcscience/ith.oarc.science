XXXVII. WHAT BY A HOG. 1

When they would symbolise a filthy man, they depict a HOG; from such being the nature of the hog.

Footnotes

110:1

XXXVII. There is an example of a hog in a judgment scene in the tombs of the kings, where, according to Champollion, it denotes the metampsychosis of a glutton condemned by Osiris into a hog: Bonomi. See Pl. 3.
