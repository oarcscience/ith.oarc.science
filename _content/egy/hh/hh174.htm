CI. HOW A MAN WHO IS IMPUDENT AND QUICK-SIGHTED.

When they would symbolise a man who is impudent 

p. 148

and quicksighted, they depict A FROG; for it has no blood except in the eyes alone, and they call those who have blood in those parts impudent: and hence the poet sings, "Drunkard with eyes of dog and heart of stag."
