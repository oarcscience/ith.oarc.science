XXX. WHAT ONE LINE BENT OVER ANOTHER SIGNIFIES.

 3

A straight line with a curved line above it signifies TEN PLANE LINES. 4

Footnotes

106:3

xxx. The plane lines denote units, the curved 10's, the coils 100's, and the other 1000's.
