### LXXXII. HOW A WOMAN THAT HAS BROUGHT FORTH ONCE.

<span id="img_13601"></span> ![](img/13601.jpg)

When they would symbolise *a woman that has brought forth once*, they
depict A LIONESS; for she never conceives twice.
