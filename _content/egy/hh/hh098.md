### XXV. HOW DEATH.

<span id="img_10302"></span> ![](img/10302.jpg)

A NIGHT RAVEN signifies *death;* <span id="fr_112"></span>[2](#fn_107)
for it suddenly pounces

<span id="page_104">p. 104</span>

upon the young of the crows by night, as death suddenly overtakes men.

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_107"></span>[103:2](hh098.htm#fr_112) Qy. Erebus? The
Hebrew word ‏ערב‎, EREB, signifies either <span id="page_104">p.
104</span> Erebus darkness, or the Nightraven, and it appears by
Damascius, that the Sidonians substituted this bird as an emblem of
Erebus.—An. Frag. p. 319.
