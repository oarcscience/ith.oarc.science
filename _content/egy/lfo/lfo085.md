### THE EIGHTY-SIXTH CEREMONY.

Two baskets of Nepat grain, with the formula:--

"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus, which hath been counted <span
id="fr_76"></span>[1](#fn_76) (or reckoned up), hath been presented unto
thee."
