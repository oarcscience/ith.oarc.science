### THE TWENTY-FIFTH CEREMONY.

He next offered a black vessel containing a *hent* measure of
beer, <span id="fr_63"></span>[1](#fn_63) while the Kher heb said.--

<span id="img_08400"></span> ![](img/08400.jpg)  
The Sem priest presenting a stone vessel of beer.

"Unas, there hath been presented unto thee that which hath been pressed
out and cometh forth from Osiris."
