### THE ONE HUNDRED AND SIXTH CEREMONY.

Two vessels of roasted Set grain, with the formula:--

"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus, the Glorious One, which shall turn back
\[the fiends of Set\], hath been presented unto thee."
