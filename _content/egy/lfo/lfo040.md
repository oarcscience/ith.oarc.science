### THE FORTY-FIRST CEREMONY.

The offering of two vessels of beer in the *usekh* hall <span
id="fr_72"></span>[1](#fn_72) followed, and the Kher heb said:--

<span id="img_10300"></span> ![](img/10300.jpg)  
The Sem priest presenting two vessels of beer.

"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, and he is
content therewith."
