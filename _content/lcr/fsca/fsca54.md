### Bagwyn

<span class="smallcaps">A fabulous</span> beast like the heraldic
antelope, but having the tail of a horse, and long horns of a goat
curved backwards. The dexter supporter of the arms of Carey, Lord
Hundson, in Westminster Abbey, is a Bagwyn.
