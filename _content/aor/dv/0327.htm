327.

The whole length of the foot will lie between the elbow and the wrist and between the elbow and the inner angle of the arm

p. 177

towards the breast when the arm is folded. The foot is as long as the whole head of a man, that is from under the chin to the topmost part of the head 143 in the way here figured.
