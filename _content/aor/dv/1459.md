### 1459.

On the last day but one of September;

Thursday the 27th day of September Maestro Tommaso came back and worked
for himself until the last day but one of February. On the 18th day of
March, 1493, Giulio, a German, came to live with me,--Lucia, Piero,
Leonardo.

On the 6th day of October.
