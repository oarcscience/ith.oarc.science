### 1384.

<span id="page_423">p. 423</span>

On the 16th day of July.

Caterina came on 16th day of July, 1493.

Messer Mariolo's Morel the Florentin, has a big horse with a fine neck
and a beautiful head.

The white stallion belonging to the falconer has fine hind quarters; it
is behind the Comasina Gate.

The big horse of Cermonino, of Signor Giulio.  <span
id="fr_408"></span>[714](#fn_408)
