### 1032.

The lake of Pusiano flows into the lake of Segrino  <span
id="fr_221"></span>[555](#fn_221) and of Annone and of Sala. The lake of
Annone is 22 braccia higher at the surface of its water than the surface
of the water of the lake of Lecco, and the lake of Pusiano is 20 braccia
higher than the lake of Annone, which added to the afore said 22 braccia
make 42 braccia and this is the greatest height of the surface of the
lake of Pusiano above the surface of the lake of Lecco.

 <span id="fr_222"></span>[556](#fn_222)
