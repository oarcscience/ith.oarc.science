*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 579.

### PRECEPTS IN PAINTING.

Let you sketches of historical pictures be swift and the working out of
the limbs not be carried too far, but limited to the position of the
limbs, which you can afterwards finish as you please and at your
leisure.
