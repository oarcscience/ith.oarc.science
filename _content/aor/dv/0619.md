*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 619.

Use black in the shadow, and in the lights white, yellow, green,
vermilion and lake. Medium shadows; take the shadow as above and mix it
with the flesh tints just alluded to, adding to it a little yellow and a
little green and occasionally some lake; for the shadows take green and
lake for the middle shades.
