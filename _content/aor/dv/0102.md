*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 102.

### I GIVE THE DEGREES OF THE OBJECTS SEEN BY THE EYE AS THE MUSICIAN DOES THE NOTES HEARD BY THE EAR.

Although the objects seen by the eye do, in fact, touch each other as
they recede, I will nevertheless found my rule on spaces of 20

<span id="page_61">p. 61</span>

braccia each; as a musician does with notes, which, though they can be
carried on one into the next, he divides into degrees from note to note
calling them 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th; and has affixed a name to each
degree in raising or lowering the voice.
