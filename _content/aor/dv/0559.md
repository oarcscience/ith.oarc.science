### 559.

### OF SURROUNDING BODIES BY VARIOUS FORMS OF SHADOW.

Take care that the shadows cast upon the surface of the bodies by
different objects must undulate according to the various curves of the
limbs which cast the shadows, and of the objects on which they are cast.
