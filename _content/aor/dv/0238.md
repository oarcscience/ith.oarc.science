*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 238.

### OF LIGHT AND SHADE.

You who draw from nature, look (carefully) at the extent, the degree,
and the form of the lights and shadows on each muscle; and in their
position lengthwise observe towards which muscle the axis of the central
line is directed.
