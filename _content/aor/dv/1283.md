### 1283.

An artizan often going to visit a great gentleman without any definite
purpose, the gentleman asked him what he did this for. The other said
that he came there to have a pleasure which his lordship could not have;

<span id="page_349">p. 349</span>

since to him it was a satisfaction to see men greater than himself, as
is the way with the populace; while the gentleman could only see men of
less consequence than himself; and so lords and great men were deprived
of that pleasure.
