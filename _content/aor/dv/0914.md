### 914.

<span id="page_171">p. 171</span>

<span class="margnote"> Observations on the stars.</span>To see the real
nature of the planets open the covering and note at the base  <span
id="fr_156"></span>[490](#fn_156) one single planet, and the reflected
movement of this base will show the nature of the said planet; but
arrange that the base may face only one at the time.
