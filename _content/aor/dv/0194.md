### 194.

### OF SHADOWS WHICH NEVER COME TO AN END.

The greater the difference between a light and the body lighted by it,
the light being the larger, the more vague will be the outlines of the
shadow of that object.

<span id="page_109">p. 109</span>

The derived shadow will be most confused towards the edges of its
interception by a plane, where it is remotest from the body casting it.
