### 337.

<span class="margnote"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the
foot.</span>The torso *a b* in its thinnest part measures a foot; and
from *a* to *b* is 2 feet, which makes two squares to the seat--its
thinnest part goes 3 times into the length, thus making 3 squares.
