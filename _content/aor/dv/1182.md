*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 1182.

Blind ignorance misleads us thus and delights with the results of
lascivious joys.

Because it does not know the true light.  
Because it does not know what is the true light.

Vain splendour takes from us the power of being .... behold! for its
vain splendour we go into the fire, thus blind ignorance does mislead
us. That is, blind ignorance so misleads us that ...

O! wretched mortals, open your eyes.
