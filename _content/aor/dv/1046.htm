1046.

In Romagna, the realm of all stupidity, vehicles with four wheels are used, of which ○ the two in front are small and two high ones are behind; an arrangement which is very unfavourable to the motion, because on the fore wheels more weight is laid than on those behind, as I showed in the first of the 5th on "Elements".
