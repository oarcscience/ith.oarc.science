SIXTH BOOK ON LIGHT AND SHADE.

PERSPECTIVE.

The effect of rays passing through holes (213. 214).If you transmit the rays of the sun through a hole in the shape of a star you will see a beautiful effect of perspective in the spot where the sun's rays fall.
