### 1385.

### OF THE INSTRUMENT.

Any one who spends one ducat may take the instrument; and he will not
pay more than half a ducat as a premium to the inventor of the
instrument and one grosso to the workman every year. I do not want
sub-officials.  <span id="fr_409"></span>[715](#fn_409)
