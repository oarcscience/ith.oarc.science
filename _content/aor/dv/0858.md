*The Da Vinci Notebooks at Intangible Textual Heritage*

### 858.

The earth is not in the centre of the Sun's orbit nor at the centre of
the universe, but in the centre of its companion elements, and united
with them. And any one standing on the moon, when it and the sun are
both beneath us, would see this our earth and the element of water

<span id="page_138">p. 138</span>

upon it just as we see the moon, and the earth would light it as it
lights us.
