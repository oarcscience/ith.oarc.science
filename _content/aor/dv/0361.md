### 361.

### OF \[CHANGE OF\] ATTITUDE.

The pit of the throat is over the feet, and by throwing one arm forward
the pit of the throat is thrown off that foot. And if the leg is thrown
forward the pit of the throat is thrown forward; and. so it varies in
every attitude.
