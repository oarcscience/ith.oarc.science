### 621.

<span id="page_318">p. 318</span>

### A FINE YELLOW.

Dissolve realgar with one part of orpiment, with aqua fortis.

### WHITE.

Put the white into an earthen pot, and lay it no thicker than a string,
and let it stand in the sun undisturbed for 2 days; and in the morning
when the sun has dried off the night dews.
