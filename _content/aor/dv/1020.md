### 1020.

### OF THE FORCE OF THE VACUUM FORMED IN A MOMENT.

I saw, at Milan, a thunderbolt fall on the tower della Credenza on its
Northern side, and it descended with a slow motion down that side, and
then at once parted from that tower and carried with it and tore away
from that wall a space of 3 braccia wide and two deep; and this wall was
4 braccia thick and was built of thin and small old bricks; and this was
dragged out by the vacuum which the flame of the thunderbolt had caused,
&c
