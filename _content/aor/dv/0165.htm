165.

Any shadow cast by a body in light and shade is of the same nature and character as that which is inseparable from the body. The centre of the length of a shadow always corresponds to that of the luminous body  72 . It is inevitable that every shadow must have its centre in a line with the centre of the light.

On the shape of derived shadows (166-174).
