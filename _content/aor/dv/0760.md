### 760.

<span class="margnote"> On the proportions of a courtyard.</span>In the
courtyard the walls must be half the height of its width, that is if the
court be 40 braccia, the house must be 20 high as regards the walls of
the said courtyard; and this courtyard must be half as wide as the whole
front.
