# NEWES FROM SCOTLAND

declaring the damnable life

and death of

DOCTOR FIAN, a notable SORCERER

\(1591\)

{p. 2}

NOTE

THE ORIGINAL of this text is in the Bodleian Library (Douce F. 210). The
text (*not the Preface*) is reproduced line for line and page for page.
Pages in the original are not numbered except by signatures. No
corrections have been made.

G. B. H.
