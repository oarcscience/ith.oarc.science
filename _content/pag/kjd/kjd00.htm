from the series BODLEY HEAD QUARTOS published by John Lane, The Bodley Head Ltd., London between 1922 and 1926

{Scanned at Intangible Textual Heritage, November 2001. The pagination is that of the Bodley Head edition. Word breaks between pages and typographical errors have been preserved. The letter 'o tilde' is written o~. The original 16th Century spelling has been retained throughout.--jbh.}

NOTE

THE ORIGINAL of this text is in the Bodleian Library (Douce. I. 230, which, however, lacks pp. 9 and 10; these have been taken from 'Wood B21'). The text (not the Preface) is reprinted line for line and page for page; pages are numbered as in the original. The few corrections which I have made are noted on p. 82.

G. B. H.
