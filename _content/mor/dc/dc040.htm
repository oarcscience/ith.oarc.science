40. Revelation to Joseph Smith and Sidney Rigdon, Fayette, New York, January 1831.
History of the Church 1:145.

40:1 Behold, verily I say unto you, that the heart of my servant James Covill was right before me, for he covenanted with me that he would obey my word.

40:2 And he received the word with gladness, but straightway Satan tempted him; and the fear of persecution and the cares of the world caused him to reject the word.

40:3 Wherefore he broke my covenant, and it remaineth with me to do with him as seemeth me good. Amen.
