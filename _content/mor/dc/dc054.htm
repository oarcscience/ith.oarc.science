54. Revelation through Joseph Smith to Newel Knight, Kirtland, Ohio, June 1831.
History of the Church 1:180-81.

54:1 Behold, thus saith the Lord, even Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, even he who was crucified for the sins of the world—

54:2 Behold, verily, verily, I say unto you, my servant Newel Knight, you shall stand fast in the office whereunto I have appointed you.

54:3 And if your brethren desire to escape their enemies, let them repent of all their sins, and become truly humble before me and contrite.

54:4 And as the covenant which they made unto me has been broken, even so it has become void and of none effect.

54:5 And wo to him by whom this offense cometh, for it had been better for him that he had been drowned in the depth of the sea.

54:6 But blessed are they who have kept the covenant and observed the commandment, for they shall obtain mercy.

54:7 Wherefore, go to now and flee the land, lest your enemies come upon you; and take your journey, and appoint whom you will to be your leader, and to pay moneys for you.

54:8 And thus you shall take your journey into the regions westward, unto the land of Missouri, unto the borders of the Lamanites.

54:9 And after you have done journeying, behold, I say unto you, seek ye a living like unto men, until I prepare a place for you.

54:10 And again, be patient in tribulation until I come; and, behold, I come quickly, and my reward is with me, and they who have sought me early shall find rest to their souls. Even so. Amen.
