THE CHRISTMAS CHILD

LITTLE one, who straight hast come
Down the heavenly stair,
Tell us all about your home,
And the father there."

"He is such a one as I
Like as like can be.
Do his will, and, by and by,
Home and him you'll see."

--GEORGE MACDONALD
