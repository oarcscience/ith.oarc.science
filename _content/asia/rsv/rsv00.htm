This volume was prepared by the Chaplain Corps Planning Group, Chaplains Division, Bureau of Naval Personnel. It is one of a series of materials supportive of the Personal Response Project, a systematic effort in intercultural attitude improvement which in Southeast Asia involves an understanding of the indigenous religions and cultural value systems.
