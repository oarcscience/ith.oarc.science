#### TRANSLATIONS OF EARLY DOCUMENTS

A *Series of texts important for the study of Christian origins, by
various authors*

UNDER THE JOINT EDITORSHIP OF

The Rev. W. O. E. OESTERLEY, D.D.

AND

The Rev. CANON G. H. BOX, M.A.

THE object of the Series is to provide short, cheap, and handy
text-books for students, either working by themselves or in classes. The
aim is to furnish in translations important texts unencumbered by
commentary or elaborate notes, which can be had in larger works.
