### 13. Who curses His Parents.

8*b*. He who curses his father or mother <span
id="fr_324"></span>[2](#fn_321) is not guilty until he curse them by the
Name; if he curse them under a pseudonym <span
id="fr_325"></span>[3](#fn_322) R. Meir would hold him guilty, but the
majority innocent.
