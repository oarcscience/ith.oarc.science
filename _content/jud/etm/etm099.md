# XXII. TREATISE MOED KATAN

###### OR, THE MIDDLE DAYS OF FESTIVALS.

### <span class="smallcaps">Introduction</span>.

<span class="smallcaps">This</span> Treatise contains regulations for
the better observance of the minor or middle days of the Passover and
Tabernacles, pointing out which kinds of work may be performed on these
middle days, and which are prohibited thereon. It also establishes
certain rules for mourners.
