### <span class="smallcaps">The Hall Marks of the Meek</span>

<span class="smallcaps">First</span> among the signs by which the meek
are known is that they forgive all injuries and subdue their

<span id="page_38">p. 38</span>

anger against those that treat them with contempt, even when they have
the opportunity of avenging or resenting what has been done to them.

The second is, that when misfortunes come to them their endurance
triumphs over their fear and grief, and they willingly submit to the
decree of God, and own that His judgments are righteous.
