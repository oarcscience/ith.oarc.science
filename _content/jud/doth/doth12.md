### <span class="smallcaps">Endless Virtues Spring from those of the Heart</span>

<span class="smallcaps">These</span> inner virtues of the mind and
heart, unlike the precepts that the body can fulfil, are not only very
numerous themselves, but form the inexhaustible source of innumerable
virtues and obligations.
