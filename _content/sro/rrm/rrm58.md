THE ROUND TABLE OF KING ARTHUR.  
From the Original, preserved in the Court-House of the Castle at
Winchester.  
''SANGREALE''--Or ''HOLY GRAIL.''  

 

Tradition, that Judas Iscariot left the Table at the words of the
Saviour--"What thou doest. do quickly!" and had *no portion* in the Last
Rite. (Refer below.)

"After the sop, Satan entered into him. Then said Jesus unto him, 'That
thou doest, do quickly!' Now no man at the table knew for what intent He
spoke this unto him.

"He" (Judas) "then having received the sop, WENT IMMEDIATELY OUT. AND IT
WAS NIGHT." S. JOHN, Chap. xiii., vers. 27, 28, 30.
