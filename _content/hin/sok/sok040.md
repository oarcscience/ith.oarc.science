### XXXIX

I. 59.

sâdho, y**a**h tan **t**hâ**t**h ta<u>n</u>vure k

a

O FRIEND! this body is His lyre; He tightens its strings, and draws from
it the melody of Brahma.  
If the strings snap and the keys slacken, then to dust must this
instrument of dust return:  
Kabîr says: "None but Brahma can evoke its melodies."
