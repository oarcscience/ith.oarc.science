### L

I. 126.

muralî bajat akha**nd** sadâye

THE flute of the Infinite is played without ceasing, and its sound is
love:  
When love renounces all limits, it reaches truth.  
How widely the fragrance spreads! It has no end, nothing stands in its
way.  
The form of this melody is bright like <span id="page_97">p. 97</span> a
million suns: incomparably sounds the vina, the vina of the notes of
truth.
