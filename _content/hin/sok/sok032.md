### XXXI

II\. 100.

nis\` din sâlai ghâw

A SORE pain troubles me day and night, and I cannot sleep;  
I long for the meeting with my Beloved, and my father's house gives me
pleasure no more. <span id="page_80">p. 80</span>  
The gates of the sky are opened, the temple is revealed:  
I meet my husband, and leave at His feet the offering of my body and my
mind.
