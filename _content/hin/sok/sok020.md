### XIX

II\. 20.

paramâtam guru nika**t** virâjat

n

O MY heart! the Supreme Spirit, the great Master, is near you: wake, oh
wake!  
Run to the feet of your Beloved: for <span id="page_69">p. 69</span>  
your Lord stands near to your head.  
You have slept for unnumbered ages; this morning will you not wake?
