### XXVIII

II\. 85.

nirgu**n** âge sargu**n** nâcai

BEFORE the Unconditioned, the Conditioned dances: "Thou and I are one!"
this trumpet proclaims. <span id="page_77">p. 77</span>  
The Guru comes, and bows down before the disciple:  
This is the greatest of wonders.
