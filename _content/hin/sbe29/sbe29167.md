### KA*ND*IKÂ 13.

1 <span id="fr_732"></span>[1](#fn_731). If she does not conceive, he
should, after having fasted, under (the Nakshatra) Pushya, lay down (in
his house) the root of a white-blooming Si*m*hî plant, and on the fourth
day, after (his wife) has bathed, he should in the night-time crush it
in water and insert it into her right nostril with (the verse), 'This
herb is protecting, overcoming, and powerful. May I, the son of this
great (mother), obtain the name of a father!'
