### KA*ND*IKÂ 16.

1 <span id="fr_444"></span>[1](#fn_443). In the sixth month the
Annaprâ*s*ana (i.e. the first feeding with solid food).

2\. Goat's flesh, if he is desirous of nourishment,

3\. Flesh of partridge, if desirous of holy lustre,

4\. Boiled rice with ghee, if desirous of splendour:

5\. (Such) food, mixed with curds, honey and ghee he should give (to the
child) to eat with (the verse), 'Lord of food, give us food painless and
strong;

<span id="page_184">p. 184</span>

bring forward the giver; bestow power on us, on men and animals.'

6\. The rite only (without the Mantra) for a girl.
