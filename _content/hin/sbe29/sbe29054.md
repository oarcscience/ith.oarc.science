### KHA*ND*A 6.

1\. One who has not set up the (sacred *S*rauta) fires, when setting out
on a journey, looks at his house.

2\. (He murmurs the text,) 'Do ye both, Mitra and Varu*n*a, protect this
house for me; unscathed, undisturbed, may Pûshan guard it till our
return;'

3\. And murmurs (the verse), 'Upon the path we have entered' (Rig-veda
VI, 51, 16).
