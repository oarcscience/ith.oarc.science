### KHA*ND*A 4.

1\. Now if a half-monthly sacrifice has not been performed, one or the
other of them, then a mess of rice (is to be offered as an expiation),

2\. With (the words), 'To Agni Vai*s*vânara svâhâ! To Agni Tantumat
svâhâ!'

3\. In the case of an intermission of the (morning or evening)
oblations—

4\. (He shall make expiatory oblations), in the evening with (the
formula), 'Enlightener of the darkness, adoration! Svâhâ!'

5\. In the morning with (the formula), 'Enlightener of the morning,
adoration! Svâhâ!'

6 <span id="fr_305"></span>[6](#fn_304). After he has sacrificed as many
oblations as there had been sacrifices (left out), the sacrifice (itself
goes on) as (stated) above.
