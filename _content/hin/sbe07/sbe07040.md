# XXXVIII.

1\. Causing (bodily) pain to a Brâhma*n*a;

2\. Smelling at things which ought not to be smelt (such as excrements),
or at spirituous liquor;

3\. Dishonest dealing;

4\. Sexual connection with cattle;

5\. And (sexual connection) with a man (or unnatural intercourse with a
woman):

6\. Such are the crimes effecting loss of caste.

7\. He who has knowingly committed one of the acts effecting loss of
caste shall perform the Sântapana<sup>\[1\]</sup> penance; he who has
done so unawares shall perform the Prâ*g*âpatya<sup>\[1\]</sup> penance.
