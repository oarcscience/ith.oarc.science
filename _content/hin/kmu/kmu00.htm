Six Chakras of Yoga with Petals and Deity Symbols

SYMBOLICAL

Six Chakras of Yoga with Petals and Deity
——Symbols——

1. The Earth Chakra is of Yellow color, has four Petals, a Beast, Two Deities and Kundalini.

2. The Water Chakra is White, has Six Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.

4. The Air Chakra is Black, has Twelve Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.

5. The Ether Chakra is like Sea Water, has Sixteen Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.

6. The Mental Chakra has Two Petals and a Deity.

Sahasrara is Above All and is called, the One Thousand Petalled Lotus.
