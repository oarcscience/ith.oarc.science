### CHAP. XXIII.

Future kings of Magadhá: descendants of Vrihadratha.

I WILL now relate to you the descendants of Vrihadratha, who will be the
kings of Magadhá. There have been several powerful princes of this
dynasty, of whom the most celebrated was Jarásandha; his son was
Sahadeva; his son is Somápi <span id="fr_1331"></span>[1](#fn_1331); his
son will be Śrutavat <span id="fr_1332"></span>[2](#fn_1332); his son
will be Ayutáyus <span id="fr_1333"></span>[3](#fn_1333); his son will
be Niramitra <span id="fr_1334"></span>[4](#fn_1334); his son will be
Sukshatra <span id="fr_1335"></span>[5](#fn_1335); his son will be
Vrihatkarman <span id="fr_1336"></span>[6](#fn_1336); his son will be
Senajit <span id="fr_1337"></span>[7](#fn_1337); his son will be
Śrutanjaya <span id="fr_1338"></span>[8](#fn_1338); his son will be
Vipra <span id="fr_1339"></span>[9](#fn_1339); his son will be
Śuchi <span id="fr_1340"></span>[10](#fn_1340); his son will be
Kshemya <span id="fr_1341"></span>[11](#fn_1341); his son will be
Suvrata <span id="fr_1342"></span>[12](#fn_1342); his son will be
Dharma <span id="fr_1343"></span>[13](#fn_1343); his son will be
Suśuma <span id="fr_1344"></span>[14](#fn_1344); his son will be
Drid́hasena <span id="fr_1345"></span>[15](#fn_1345); his son will be
Sumati <span id="fr_1346"></span>[16](#fn_1346); his son will be
Suvala <span id="fr_1347"></span>[17](#fn_1347); his son will be
Suníta <span id="fr_1348"></span>[18](#fn_1348); his son will be
Satyajit <span id="fr_1349"></span>[19](#fn_1349); his son will be
Viśwajit <span id="fr_1350"></span>[20](#fn_1350); his son will be
Ripunjaya <span id="fr_1351"></span>[21](#fn_1351). These are the
Várhadrathas, who will reign for a thousand years <span
id="fr_1352"></span>[22](#fn_1352).

text also in stating that 1000 years had elapsed from the great war, at
the death of the last Várhadratha prince; and this is more worthy of
credit than the details, which are obviously imperfect.
