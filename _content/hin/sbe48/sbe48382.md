38\. And on account of scriptural declaration.

That he bestows all rewards--whether in the form of enjoyment or
Release--Scripture also declares 'This indeed is the great, the unborn
Self, the eater of food, the giver of

<span id="page_626">p. 626</span>

wealth' (B*ri*. Up. IV, 4, 24); and 'For he alone causes delight'
(Taitt. Up. II, 7).--Next a primâ facie view is stated.
