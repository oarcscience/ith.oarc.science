12\. Earth (from water).

Earth originates from water; for so the texts declare 'From water earth'
(Taitt Up. II. 1, 1). 'It (water) sent forth food' (*Kh*. Up. VI, 2, 3).
But how can the word 'food' denote earth?--To this the next Sûtra
replies.
