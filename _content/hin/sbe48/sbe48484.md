37\. Sm*ri*ti also states this.

Sm*ri*ti also declares that men not belonging to an â*s*rama grow in
knowledge through prayer and the like. 'Through prayer also a Brâhma*n*a
may become perfect. May he perform other works or not, one who befriends
all creatures is called a Brâhma*n*a' (Manu Sm*ri*. II, 17).
