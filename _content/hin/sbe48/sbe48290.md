44\. Moreover it is so stated in Sm*ri*ti.

Sm*ri*ti moreover declares the individual soul to be a part of the
highest Person, 'An eternal part of myself becomes

<span id="page_563">p. 563</span>

the individual soul (*g*îva) in the world of life' (Bha. Gî. XV, 7). For
this reason also the soul must be held to be a part of Brahman.

But if the soul is a part of Brahman, all the imperfections of the soul
are Brahman's also! To this objection the next Sûtra replies.
