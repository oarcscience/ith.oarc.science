10\. And on account of Sm*ri*ti.

This follows from Sm*ri*ti also, which declares 'when the pralaya has
come and the end of the Highest, they all together with Brahman enter
the highest place.'--For all these reasons Bâdari holds that the troop
of the conducting deities, beginning with Light, leads the souls of
those only who meditate on the effected Brahman, i e. Hira*n*yagarbha.
