### EIGHTH ANUVÂKA.

1\. Om means Brahman. 2. Om means all this. 3. Om means obedience. When
they have been told, 'Om, speak,' they speak. 4. After Om they sing
Sâmans. 5. After Om they recite hymns. 6. After Om the Adhvaryu gives
the response. 7. After Om the Brahman-priest gives orders. 8. After Om
he (the sacrificer) allows the performance of the Agnihotra. 9. When a
Brâhma*n*a is going to begin his lecture, he says, 10. 'Om, may I
acquire Brahman (the Veda).' He thus acquires the Veda.
