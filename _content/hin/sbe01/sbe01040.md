### SIXTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. Let a man meditate on the fivefold Sâman in animals. The hiṅkâra is
goats, the prastâva sheep, the udgîtha cows, the pratihâra horses, the
nidhana man.

2\. Animals belong to him, nay, he is rich in animals who knowing this
meditates on the fivefold Sâman as animals.
