### SEVENTEENTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'When one understands the True, then one declares the True. One who
does not understand it, does not declare the True <span
id="fr_320"></span>[2](sbe01152.htm#fn_318). Only he who understands it,
declares the True. This understanding, however, we must desire to
understand.'

'Sir, I desire to understand it.'
