### TWENTY-FIRST KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'When one performs all sacred duties <span
id="fr_321"></span>[1](#fn_319), then one attends really on a tutor. One
who does not perform his duties, does not really attend on a tutor. Only
he who performs his duties, attends on his tutor. This performance of
duties, however, we must desire to understand.'

'Sir, I desire to understand it.'
