### EIGHTH KHA*ND*A.

1\. Next for the sevenfold Sâman. Let a man meditate on the sevenfold
Sâman in speech. Whenever there is in speech the syllable huṅ <span
id="fr_133"></span>[1](#fn_132), that is hiṅkâra, pra is the prastâva, â
is the âdi, the first, i.e. Om,

2\. Ud is the udgîtha, pra the pratihâra, upa the upadrava, ni the
nidhana.

3\. Speech yields the milk, which is the milk of speech itself, to him
who knowing this meditates on the sevenfold Sâman in speech. He becomes
rich in food, and able to eat food.
