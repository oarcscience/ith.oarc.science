### TWENTY-SECOND KHA*ND*A.

1\. 'When one obtains bliss (in oneself), then one performs duties. One
who does not obtain bliss, does not perform duties. Only he who obtains
bliss, performs duties. This bliss, however, we must desire to
understand.'

'Sir, I desire to understand it.'
