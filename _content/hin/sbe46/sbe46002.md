### INTRODUCTION.

In preparing this volume, which contains the greater part of the Agni
hymns of the Rig-veda, namely, those of the Ma*nd*alas I–V, the
translator enjoyed the high advantage of Professor Max Müller's
assistance, in the way stated in the Introduction to the first volume of
Vedic Hymns, Sacred Books of the East, vol. xxxii, p. xxvii.

H. O.

     KIEL:  
<span class="small">November, 1895.</span>
