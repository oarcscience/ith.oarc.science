THIRTY MINOR UPANISHAḌS

TRANSLATED BY

K. NĀRĀYAṆASVAMI AIYAR

Translator of
Laghu Yoga Vāsishtha, Vāsuḍevamanana
&c. &c. &c.

MADRAS

[1914]

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage by John Bruno Hare, November 2009. This text is in the public domain in the US because it was published prior to 1923.

TO

THE ṚSHIS OF INDIA

WHO BY TREADING THE PATH

OF

THE UPANISHAḌS HAVE PERFECTED THEMSELVES

AND

REALISED THE GOAL

THIS HUMBLE EFFORT

To spread Their Ancient Teachings in a Modern Garb

IS DEDICATED

BY ONE

WHO LOVES AND WORSHIPS THEM

And tries to walk in Their Footsteps

As Their Humble and Devoted Disciple
