### CORRECTIONS.

Introduction, page [xii](sbe4102.htm#page_xii), line 33. Read,--the day
preceding the Soma-day, the animal offering to Agni-Soma being indeed a
constant feature of that day's proceedings at every Soma-sacrifice;
whilst the slaughter of the special victim, or victims, of the
respective sacrifice takes place during the morning service, &c.

P. [5](sbe4103.htm#page_5), last line of text. Read,--therefor.

P. [6](sbe4104.htm#page_6), note [2](sbe4104.htm#fn_37), l. 3.
P*ri*sh*th*ya sha*d*aha, see Introduction, [p.
xxi](sbe4102.htm#page_xxi).

P. [8](sbe4104.htm#page_8), last line of notes
\*([1](sbe4104.htm#fn_39))\*. For 'II, 665' read 'II, 663, in a
different tune again.'

P. [9](sbe4104.htm#page_9), l. 5 of notes \*([1](sbe4104.htm#fn_40))\*.
Read,--II, 720-22.

P. [34](sbe4108.htm#page_34), l. 21. Read,--B*ri*haspati consecration.

P. [41](sbe4109.htm#page_41), l. 14. For 'offering' read 'offspring.'

P. [104](sbe4120.htm#page_104), l. 2. For 'truth' read 'law;' (cf. [VI,
7, 3, 11](sbe4151.htm#6:7:3:11).)

P. [146](sbe4128.htm#page_146), l. 23. For 'become' read 'became.'
