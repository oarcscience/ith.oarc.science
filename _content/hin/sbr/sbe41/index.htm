The Satapatha Brahmana, Part III

Sacred Books of the East, Vol. 41

translated by Julius Eggeling

[1894]

Contents    Start Reading

This is part III of the Sacred Books of the East Satapatha Brahamana translation, containing Books V, VI and VII. This portion is notable for the description of the extended Vedic creation myth. This includes a mention of a 'cosmic egg,' a key element of other widely dispersed creation myths.
