### HYMN LVIII. Soma Pavamana.

1\. SWIFT runs this giver of delight, even the stream of flowing
juice:  
Swift runs this giver of delight.  
2 The Morning knows all precious things, the Goddess knows her grace to
man:  
Swift runs this giver of delight.  
3 We have accepted thousands from Dhvasra's and Puruṣanti's hands:  
Swift runs this giver of delight.  
4 From whom we have accepted thus thousands and three times ten
beside:  
Swift runs this giver of delight.
