### HYMN CXIV. Soma Pavamana.

1\. THE man who waIketh as the Laws of Indu Pavamana bid,—  
Men call him rich in children, him, O Soma, who hath met thy thought.
Flow, Indu, flow for Indra's sake.  
2 Kasyapa, Ṛṣi, lifting up thy voice with hymn-composers' lauds,  
Pav reverence to King Soma born the Sovran Ruler of the plants. Flow,
Indu, flow for Indra's sake.  
3 Seven regions have their several Suns; the ministering priests are
seven;  
Seven are the Āditya Deities,—with these, O Soma, guard thou us. Flow,
Indu, flow for Indra's sake.  
4 Guard us with this oblation which, King Soma, hath been dressed for
thee.
