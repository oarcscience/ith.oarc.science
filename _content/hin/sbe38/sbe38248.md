39\. Better than this is the other (state of belonging to an â*s*rama),
on account of the indicatory marks.

'Than this,' i.e. 'than standing between,' a better means of knowledge
it is to stand within one of the â*s*ramas, since this is confirmed by
*S*ruti and Sm*ri*ti. For scripture supplies an indicatory mark in the
passage, 'On that path goes whoever knows Brahman and who has done holy

<span id="page_317">p. 317</span>

works (as prescribed for the â*s*ramas) and obtained splendour' (B*ri*.
Up. IV, 4, 9); and Sm*ri*ti in the passage, 'Let a Brâhma*n*a stay not
one day even outside the â*s*rama; having stayed outside for a year he
goes to utter ruin.'
