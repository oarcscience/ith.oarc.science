11\. And on account of Sm*ri*ti.

Sm*ri*ti also agrees with this view; cp. the following passage, 'When
the pralaya has come and the end of the highest (i.e. Hira*n*yagarbha),
then they all, together with Brahman, with purified minds enter the
highest place.'--The final conclusion (siddhânta) therefore is that the
going of the souls, of which scripture speaks, has for its goal the
effected Brahman.--But what is the primâ facie view, with regard to
which this final conclusion has been established in Sutras 7-11?--This
required primâ facie view is now set forth in the following Sûtras.
