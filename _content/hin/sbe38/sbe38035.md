34\. And on account of (the text) teaching its wandering about.

The quality of being an agent has to be attributed to the soul for that
reason also, that, in a chapter treating of the soul, the text declares
it to wander about in the state of sleep, 'The immortal one goes
wherever he likes' (B*ri*. Up. IV, 3, 12);

<span id="page_50">p. 50</span>

and again, 'He moves about, according to his pleasure, within his own
body' (B*ri*. Up. II, 1, 18).
