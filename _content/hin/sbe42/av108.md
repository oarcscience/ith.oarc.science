## VI, 81. A bracelet as an amulet to ensure conception.

1\. A holder art thou, holdest both hands, drivest off the Rakshas. An
acquirer of offspring and wealth this bracelet hath become!  
2. O bracelet, open up the womb, that the embryo be put (into it)! Do
thou, O limit (-setting bracelet), furnish a son, bring him here (A
gamaya), thou that comest here (Agame)!  
3. The bracelet that Aditi wore, when she desired a son.Tvashtar shall
fasten upon this woman, intending that she shall beget a son.
