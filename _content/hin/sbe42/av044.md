## VI, 16. Charm against ophthalmia.

1\. O âbayu, (and even if) thou art not âbayu, strong is thy juice, O
âbayu! We eat a gruel, compounded of thee.  
2. Vihalha is thy father's name, Madâvatî thy mother's name. Thou art
verily not such, as to have consumed thy own self.  
3. O Tauvilikâ, do be quiet! This howling one has become quiet. O brown
one, and brown-eared one, go away! Go out, O âla!  
4. Alasâlâ thou art first, silâñgalâlâ thou art the next, nîlâgalasâlâ
(thou art third?)!
