### HYMN CXXXV

#### A priest's fulmination against an enemy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613501">1</span></span>Whate'er I
eat I turn to strength, and thus I grasp the Thunder-  
   bolt,  
  Rending the shoulders of that man as Indra shattered Vritra's  
   neck.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613502">2</span></span>I drink
together what I drink, even as the sea that swallows all.  
  Drinking the life-breath of that man, we drink that man and  
   swallow him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0613503">3</span></span>Whate'er I
eat I swallow up, even as the sea that swallows all.  
  Swallowing that man's vital breath, we swallow him completely  
   up.
