### HYMN XVII

#### A charm to ensure conception

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601701">1</span></span>Even as this
mighty Earth conceived the germ of all the things  
   that be,  
  So may the germ of life be laid in thee that thou mayst bear a  
   son.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601702">2</span></span>Even as this
mighty Earth hath borne and bears the stately  
   forest trees,  
  So may the germ of life be borne in thee that thou mayst bear a  
   son.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601703">3</span></span>Even as this
mighty Earth hath borne and bears the mountains  
   and the hills,  
  So may the germ of life be borne in thee that thou mayst bear  
   a son. <span id="page_a207">p. a207</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601704">4</span></span>Even as this
mighty Earth supports the moving world that  
   dwells thereon,  
  So may the germ of life be borne in thee that thou mayst bear a  
   son.
