### HYMN XLIX

#### A prayer for children and booty

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704901">1</span></span>May the
Gods' Consorts aid us of their own free will, help us  
   to offspring and the winning of the spoil.  
  May Goddesses who quickly listen shelter us, both those on  
   earth and they within the waters' realm.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704902">2</span></span>May the
Dames, wives of Gods, enjoy our presents, Rāt, Asvini  
  Indrāni and Agnāyi;  
  May Rodasi and Varunāni hear us, and Goddesses come at the  
   matrons' season.
