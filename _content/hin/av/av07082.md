### HYMN LXXXII

#### In praise of Agni

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708201">1</span></span>Sing with
fair laud the combat for the cattle. Bestow upon us  
   excellent possessions.  
  Lead to the Gods the sacrifice we offer: let streams of oil flow  
   pure and full of sweetness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708202">2</span></span>Agni I first
appropriate with power, with splendour, and with  
   might.  
  I give myself children and lengthened life, with Hail! take Agni  
   to myself.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708203">3</span></span>Even here do
thou, O Agni, stablish wealth: let not oppressors  
   injure thee by thinking of thee first.  
  Light be thy task of ruling, Agni, with, thy power: may he who  
   worships thee wax strong, invincible.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708204">4</span></span>Agni hath
looked upon the spring of Morning, looked on the  
   days, the earliest Jātavedas.  
  So, following the gleams of Morning, Sūrya hath entered heaven  
   and earth as his possession.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708205">5</span></span>Agni hath
looked upon the spring of Mornings, looked on the  
   days, the earliest Jātavedas.  
  So he in countless places hath extended, full against heaven and  
   earth, the beams of Sūrya. <span id="page_a308">p. a308</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708206">6</span></span>Butter to
thee in heaven thy home, O Agni! Manu this day hath  
   kindled thee with butter.  
  Let the Celestial Daughters bring thee butter: Let cows pour  
   butter forth for thee, O Agni.
