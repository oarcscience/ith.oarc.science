### HYMN LXVIII

#### A charm to accompany the shaving of the beard

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606801">1</span></span>Savitar hath
come hither with the razor: come thou, O Vāyu,  
   with the heated water.  
  One-minded let Ādityas, Rudras, Vasus moisten the hair: shave  
   ye who know King Soma.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606802">2</span></span>Let Aditi
shave the beard, and let the Waters bathe it with their  
   strength: <span id="page_a232">p. a232</span>  
  Prajāpati restore his health for sight and days of lengthened life!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0606803">3</span></span>The razor
used by Savitar, for shaving, who knoweth Varuna  
   and royal Soma,  
  Even with this shave ye this man, O Brāhman. Let him be rich  
   in horses, kine, and children.
