### HYMN VIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000801">1</span></span>Drink as of
old, and let the draught delight thee: hear thou my  
   prayer and let our songs exalt thee.  
  Make the Sun visible, make food abundant: slaughter the foes,.  
   pierce through and free the cattle.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000802">2</span></span>Come to us;
they have called thee Soma-lover. Here is the  
   pressed juice: drink thereof for rapture.  
  Widely-capacious, pour it down within thee, and invocated hear  
   us like a father.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000803">3</span></span>Full is his
chalice. Blessing! Like a pourer I have filled up the  
   vessel for his drinking.  
  Presented on the right, dear Soma juices have brought us Indra,.  
   to rejoice him, hither.
