### HYMN CVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010801">1</span></span>O Indra,
bring great strength to us, bring valour, Satakratu, thou  
   most active, bring.  
  A hero conquering in war.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010802">2</span></span>For,
gracious Satakratu, thou hast ever been a mother and a  
   sire to us,  
  So now for bliss we pray to thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010803">3</span></span>To thee,
Strong, Much-invoked who showest forth thy strength,  
  O Satakratu, do I speak:  
  So grant thou us heroic might.
