### HYMN LXIX

#### A prayer for prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706901">1</span></span>May the wind
kindly breathe on us, may the Sun warm us.  
   pleasantly.  
  May days pass happily for us, may night draw near delightfully,  
   may dawn break joyfully for us!
