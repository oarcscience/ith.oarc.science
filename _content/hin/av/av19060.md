### HYMN LX

#### A prayer for perfect bodily and mental health and vigour

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906001">1</span></span>May I have
voice in my mouth, breath in my nostrils, sight in  
   mine eyes, hearing in mine ears, hair that hath not turned  
   gray, teeth free from yellowness, and much strength in mine  
   arms.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1906002">2</span></span>May I have
power in my thighs, swiftness in my legs, stedfast-  
   ness in my feet. May all my members be uninjured and my  
   soul unimpaired.
