### HYMN XCVIII

#### Anointing the sacred grass

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709801">1</span></span>Balmed is
the Grass with butter and libation, with Indra.  
   gracious Lord, and with the Maruts.  
  Hail! let the sacrifice go forth anointed to Indra with the Gods  
   and Visve Devas.
