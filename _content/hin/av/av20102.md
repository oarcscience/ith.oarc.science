### HYMN CII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010201">1</span></span>Meet to be
lauded and adored, showing in beauty through the=  
   dark, <span id="page_348">p. 348</span>  
  Agni the Bull is kindled well,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010202">2</span></span>Agni is
kindled as a Bull, like a horse bearer of the Gods;  
  Men with oblations worship him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010203">3</span></span>Thee will we
kindle as a Bull, we who are bulls ourselves, O  
  Bull,  
  Thee, Agni, shining mightily.
