### HYMN XXIV

#### A prayer for riches

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0702401">1</span></span>What
treasure hath been dug for us by Indra, by Agni, Visve-  
   devas, tuneful Maruts,  
  On us may Savitar whose laws are faithful, Prajāpati, and  
  Heavenly Grace bestow it.
