### HYMN XCII

#### A Prayer for protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709201">1</span></span>May this
rich Indra as our good protector keep even far away  
   the men who hate us.  
  May we enjoy his favour, his the holy: may we enjoy his  
   blessed loving-kindness.
