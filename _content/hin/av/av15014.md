### HYMN XIV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501401">1</span></span>He when he
went away to the eastern region, went away having  
   become the Marut host, and having made Mind an eater of  
   food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with Mind as  
   food-eater. <span id="page_159">p. 159</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501402">2</span></span>He, when he
went away to the southern region, went away  
   having become Indra, and having made Strength an eater of  
   food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with strength as  
   food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501403">3</span></span>He, when he
went away to the western region, went away having  
   become King Varuna, and having made the Waters eaters of  
   food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with the Waters  
   as food-eaters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501404">4</span></span>He, when he
went away to the northern region, went away  
   having become King Soma and having made the Seven Rishis'  
   oblation an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats  
   food with oblation as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501405">5</span></span>He, when he
went away to the stedfast region, went away having  
   become Vishnu and having made Virāj an eater of food. He  
   who hath this knowledge eats food with Virāj as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501406">6</span></span>He, when he
went away to animals, went away having become  
  Rudra and having made herbs eaters of food. He who hath  
   this knowledge eats food with herbs as food-eaters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501407">7</span></span>He, when he
went away to the Fathers, went away having be-  
   come King Yama and having made the exclamation Svadhā  
   an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with  
   the exclamation Svadhā as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501408">8</span></span>He, when he
went away to men, went away having become Agni  
   and having made the exclamation Svāhā an eater of food. He  
   who hath this knowledge eats food with the exclamation  
  Svahā as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501409">9</span></span>He, when he
went away to the upper region, went away having  
   become Brihaspati and having made the exclamation Vashat  
   an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with  
   the exclamation Vashat as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501410">10</span></span>He, when he
went away to the Gods, went away having become  
  Isana and having made Passion an eater of food. He who hath  
   this knowledge eats food with Passion as food-eater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501411">11</span></span>He, when he
went away to creatures, went away having become  
  Prajāpati and having made vital breath an eater of food. He who  
   hath this knowledge eats food with vital breath as foodeater.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501412">12</span></span>He, when he
went away to all the intermediate spaces, went  
   away having become Parameshthin and having made Devotion  
   an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with  
  Devotion as food-eater.
