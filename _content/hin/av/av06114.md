### HYMN CXIV

#### A prayer for pardon of faults and errors in sacrificing

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611401">1</span></span>Whatever
God-provoking wrong we priests have done, O  
  Deities.  
  Therefrom do ye deliver us, Ādityas! by the right of Law.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611402">2</span></span>Here set us
free, O holy ones, Ādityas, by the right of Law.  
  When striving, bringing sacrifice, we failed to offer it aright.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611403">3</span></span>With ladle
full of fatness we, worshippers, pouring holy oil,  
  Striving, have failed, O all ye Gods, against our will, to offer it.
