### HYMN XCVIII

#### Praise of Indra

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609801">1</span></span>Indra be
victor, never to be vanquished, to reign among the  
  Kings as sovran ruler! <span id="page_a246">p. a246</span>  
  Here be thou meet for praise and supplication, to be revered  
   and waited on and worshipped.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609802">2</span></span>Thou fain
for glory, an imperial ruler, hast won dominion over  
   men, O Indra,  
  Of these celestial tribes be thou the sovran: long-lasting be thy  
   sway and undecaying!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609803">3</span></span>Thou
governest the north and eastern regions, Indra! fiend-  
   slayer! thou destroycst foemen.  
  Thou hast won all, far as the rivers wander. Bull, called to  
   help, on our right hand thou goest.
