### HYMN XXXIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003901">1</span></span>For you,
from every side, we call Indra away from other men:  
  Ours, and none others,’ let him be.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003902">2</span></span>In Soma's
ecstasy Indra spread the firmament and realms of  
   light.  
  When he cleft Vala limb from limb.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003903">3</span></span>Showing the
hidden cows he draye them forth for the Angirases,  
  And Vala he cast headlong down.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003904">4</span></span>By Indra
were the luminous realms of heaven established and  
   secured,  
  Firm and immovable from their place.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2003900">0</span></span>5. Indra,
thy laud moves quickly like a joyous wave of waters:  
   bright.  
  Have shone the drops that gladden thee.
