### HYMN XXXIV

#### To Agni for protection from enemies

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603401">1</span></span>Send forth
thy voice to Agni, to the manly hero of our homes,  
  So may he bear us past our foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603402">2</span></span>That Agni
who with sharpened flame of fire consumes the  
  Rākshasas,  
  So may he bear us past our foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603403">3</span></span>He who from
distance far remote shineth across the tracts of  
   land,  
  May he transport us past our foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603404">4</span></span>He who
beholds all creatures, who observes them with a careful  
   eye,  
  May he transport us past our foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603405">5</span></span>That
brilliant Agni who was born beyond this region of the air,  
  May he transport us past our foes!
