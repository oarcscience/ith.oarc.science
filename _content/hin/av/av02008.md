### HYMN VIII

#### A charm against hereditary disease (kshetriya)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200801">1</span></span>Twin Stars
of happy omen, named Releasers, have gone up.  
  May they  
  Loose, of inherited disease, the uppermost and lowest bond.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200802">2</span></span>Vanish this
Night, extinct in Dawn! Let those who weave their  
   spells depart.  
  So let the plague-destroying Plant remove inherited disease.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200803">3</span></span>With straw
of barley tawny-brown in colour with its silvery ears,  
   with stalk and stem of Sesamum-  
  So let the plague-destroying Plant remove inherited disease.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200804">4</span></span>Let homage
to thy ploughs be paid, our homage to the pole and  
   yokes.  
  So let the plague-destroying Plant remove inherited disease.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0200805">5</span></span>Homage to
men with blinking eyes, homage to those who hear  
   and act! To the Field's Lord be homage paid.  
  So let the plague-destroying Plant remove inherited disease.
