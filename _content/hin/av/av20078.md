### HYMN LXXVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2007801">1</span></span>Sing this,
what time the juice is pressed, to him your Hero  
   much-invoked,  
  To please him as a mighty, Bull.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2007802">2</span></span>He,
excellent, withholdeth not his gift of power and wealth in  
   kine,  
  When he hath listened to our songs.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2007803">3</span></span>May he with
might disclose for us the cows' stall, whosesoe'er  
   it be,  
  To which the Dasyu-slayer goes.
