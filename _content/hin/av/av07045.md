### HYMN XLV

#### A charm against jealousy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704501">1</span></span>Brought
hitherward from Sindhu, from a folk of every mingled  
   race,  
  Fetched from afar, thou art I deem, a balm that cureth.  
   jealousy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704502">2</span></span>As one with
water quencheth fire, so calm this lover's  
   jealousy,  
  Like heat of fire that burneth here, or flame that rageth through  
   the wood.
