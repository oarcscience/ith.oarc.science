### HYMN LXXXIII

#### A charm against sores and pustules (apachitas)

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608301">1</span></span>Hence, Sores
and Pustules, fly away even as the eagle from his  
   home.  
  Let Sūrya bring a remedy, the Moon shine forth and banish  
   you.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608302">2</span></span>One bright
with variegated tints, one white, one black, a couple  
   red:—  
  The'names of all have I declared. Begone, and injure not our  
   men.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608303">3</span></span>Hence,
childless, shall the Pustule flee, grand-daughter of the  
   dusky one.  
  The Boil shall fly away from us, the morbid growth shall vanish  
   hence.  
  Taste, happy in thy mind, thine own oblation, as I with Svāhā  
   with my heart present it.
