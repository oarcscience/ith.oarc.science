### HYMN LXXXII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008201">1</span></span>If I, O
Indra, were the lord of riches ample as thine own,  
  I should support the singer, God who scatterest wealth! and!  
   not abandon him to woe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008202">2</span></span>Each day
would I enrich the man who sang my praise, in what-  
   soever place he were.  
  No kinship is there better, Maghavan, than thine: a father even)  
   is no more.
