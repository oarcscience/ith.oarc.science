### HYMN III

#### A charm to secure power and long life

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600301">1</span></span>I am the
head of riches. Fain would I be the head of mine  
   equals.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600302">2</span></span>Let not Ruja
and Vena desert me. Let not the Head and the  
  Preserver forsake me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600303">3</span></span>Let not the
Boiler and the Cup fail tme: let not the Supporter  
   and the Sustainer abandon me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600304">4</span></span>Let not
Unyoking and the Moist-fellied car desert me: let not  
   the Sender of Moisture and Matarisvan forsake me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600305">5</span></span>Brihaspati
is my soul, he who is called the Friend of man, dear  
   to my heart.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1600306">6</span></span>My heart is
free from sorrow; spacious is my dwelling-place. I  
   am the sea in capacity.
