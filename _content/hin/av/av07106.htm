HYMN CVI

A prayer for pardon of sin

1Each thoughtless ill that we have done, O Agni, all error in our
   conduct, Jātavedas!
  Therefrom do thou, O sapient God, preserve us. May we thy
   friends, for bliss, have life eternal.
