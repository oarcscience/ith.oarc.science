### HYMN CV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010501">1</span></span>Thou in the
battles, Indra, art subduer of all hostile bands.  
  Father art thou, all-conquering, cancelling the curse, thou victor  
   of the vanquisher.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010502">2</span></span>The earth,
and heaven cling close to thy victorious might, as sire  
   and mother to their child.  
  When thou attackest Vritra all the hostile bands shrink and  
   faint, Indra at thy wrath.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010503">3</span></span>Bring to
your aid the Eternal One, who shoots and none may  
   shoot at him,  
  Inciter, swift, victorious, best of charioteers, Tugrya's unvan-  
   quished strengthener;
