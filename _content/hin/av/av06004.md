### HYMN IV

#### A hymn to various deities for protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600401">1</span></span>May
Tvashtar, Brāhmanaspati, Parjanya hear my holy prayer.  
  May Aditi with all her sons, the brothers, guard us, invincible,  
   protecting power.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600402">2</span></span>May Ansa,
Bhaga, Varuna, and Mitra, Aryaman, Aditi, and  
  Maruts guard us.  
  May we be freed from that oppressor's hatred. May he keep  
   off that foeman who is near us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0600403">3</span></span>May both the
Asvins further our devotion. With ceaseless care  
   deliver us, Wide-Ranger! O Father Heaven, keep from us all  
   misfortunes.
