### HYMN XLI

#### A prayer for prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704101">1</span></span>Observing
men, and viewing home, the Falcon hath cleft his swift  
   way over wastes and waters.  
  May he, with Indra for a friend, auspicious, traversing all air's  
   lower realms, come hither.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0704102">2</span></span>The heavenly
Falcon, viewing men, well-pinioned, strength-giver,  
   hundred-footed, hundred-nested,  
  Shall give us treasure which was taken from us. May it be rich  
   in food among our Fathers.
