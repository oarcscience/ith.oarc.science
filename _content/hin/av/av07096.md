### HYMN XCVI

#### An incantation against an undetected thief

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709601">1</span></span>The kine are
resting in the stall, home to her nest hath flown  
   the bird,  
  The hills are firmly rooted: I have fixed the kidneys in their  
   place.
