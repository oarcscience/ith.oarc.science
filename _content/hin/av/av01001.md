# BOOK I

### HYMN I

#### A prayer to Vāchaspati for divine illumination and help.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100101">1</span></span>Now may
Vāchaspati assign to me the strength and powers of  
  Those  
  Who, wearing every shape and form, the triple seven, are  
   wandering round.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100102">2</span></span>Come thou
again, Vāchaspati, come with divine intelligence.  
  Vasoshpati, repose thou here. In me be Knowledge, yea, in me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100103">3</span></span>Here, even
here, spread sheltering arms like the two bow-ends  
   strained with cord.  
  This let Vāchaspati confirm. In me be Knowledge, yea, in me.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100104">4</span></span>Vāchaspati
hath been invoked: may he invite us in reply.  
  May we adhere to Sacred Lore. Never may I be reft thereof.
