### HYMN CIV

#### A prayer for prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0710401">1</span></span>Who will
prepare the dappled Cow, good milker, ne'er without  
   calf, whom Varuna gave Atharvan,  
  And, joying in Brihaspati's alliance, arrange according to his will  
   her body?
