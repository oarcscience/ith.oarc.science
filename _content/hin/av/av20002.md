### HYMN II

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000201">1</span></span>Let the
Maruts drink Trishtups from the Potar's cup, according  
   to the season Soma from heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000202">2</span></span>Let Agni
from the Kindler's cup drink Trishtups, according to  
   the season Soma from heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000203">3</span></span>Let Indra
the Brāhman from the Brāhman's cup drink Trishtups,  
   according to the season Soma from heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000204">4</span></span>Let the God,
Granter of Wealth, from the Potar's cup drink  
  Trishtups, according to the season Soma from heaven.
