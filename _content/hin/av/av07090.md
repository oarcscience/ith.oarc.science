### HYMN XC

#### A charm against a rival in love

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709001">1</span></span>Tear thou
asunder, as of old, like tangles of a creeping plant.  
  Demolish thou the Dāsa's might.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709002">2</span></span>May we with
Indra's help divide the gathered treasure of the  
   foe.  
  I, by the law of Varuna, bring down thy pride and wantonness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0709003">3</span></span>Ut virga
abeat et feminis innocua fiat, (virga) membri humidi,  
   membri quod verberat penetratque, id quod tentum est laxa,  
   id quod sursum tentum est deorsum tende.
