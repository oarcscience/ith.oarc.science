### HYMN XL

#### A prayer for peace and security

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604001">1</span></span>Here may we
dwell, O Heaven and Earth, in safety. May Savitar  
   and Soma send us safety.  
  Our safety be the wide air: ours be safety through the oblation  
   of the Seven Rishis. <span id="page_a218">p. a218</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604002">2</span></span>May the Four
Quarters give this hamlet power: Savitar favour  
   us and make us happy!  
  May Indra make us free from foes and danger: may wrath of  
  Kings be turned to other places.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0604003">3</span></span>Make thou us
free from enemies both from below and from  
   above.  
  O Indra, give us perfect peace, peace from behind and from be-  
   fore.
