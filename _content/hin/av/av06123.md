### HYMN CXXIII

#### A prayer for happiness in heaven

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612301">1</span></span>Ye who are
present, unto you I offer this treasure brought to  
   us by Jātavedas  
  Happily will the sacrificer follow: do ye acknowledge him in  
   highest heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612302">2</span></span>Do ye
acknowledge him in highest heaven: ye know the world  
   here present in assembly.  
  In peace will he who sacrifices follow: show him the joy which  
   comes from pious actions.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612303">3</span></span>Gods are the
Fathers, and the Fathers Gods. I am the very man  
  I am.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612304">4</span></span>I cook, I
give, I offer up oblation. From what I gave let me  
   not be disparted.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612305">5</span></span>O King, take
thou thy stand in heaven, there also let that gift  
   be placed. <span id="page_a259">p. a259</span>  
  Recognize, King, the gift which we have given, and be gracious,  
  God!
