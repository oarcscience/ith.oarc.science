### HYMN XXXI

#### To Sūrya the Sun-God

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603101">1</span></span>This spotted
Bull hath come and sat before his mother in the  
   east.  
  Advancing to his father Heaven. <span id="page_a214">p. a214</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603102">2</span></span>As
expiration from his breath his radiance penetrates within.  
  The Bull shines out through all the sky.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603103">3</span></span>He rules
supreme through thirty realms—One winged with song  
   hath made him mount  
  Throughout the days at break of morn.
