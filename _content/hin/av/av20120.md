### HYMN CXX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012101">1</span></span>Though,
Indra, thou art called by men eastward and westward,  
   north and south, <span id="page_357">p. 357</span>  
  Thou chiefly art with Anava and Turvasa, brave Champion!  
   urged by men to come.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011802">2</span></span>Or, Indra,
when with Ruma, Rusama, Syāvaka, and Kripa thou  
   rejoicest thee,  
  Still do the Kanvas bring praises, with their prayers, O Indra,  
   draw thee hither: come.
