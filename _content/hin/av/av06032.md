### HYMN XXXII

#### A charm against fiends and goblins

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603201">1</span></span>With butter,
in his hall v4here fire is burning, perform that sacri-  
   fice which quells the goblins.  
  Burn from afar against the demons Agni! Afflict not in thy fury  
   us who praise thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603202">2</span></span>Let Rudra
break your necks, O ye Pisāchas, and split your ribs  
   asunder, Yātudhānas!  
  Your herb of universal power with Yama hath allied itself.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0603203">3</span></span>Here,
Mitra-Varuna! may we dwell safely: with splendour drive  
   the greedy demons backward,  
  Let them not find a surety or a refuge, but torn away go down  
   to Death together.
