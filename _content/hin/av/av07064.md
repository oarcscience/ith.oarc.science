### HYMN LXIV

#### A charm to avert an evil omen

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706401">1</span></span>From all
that woe and trouble may the Waters save and rescue  
   me,  
  Whate'er the Raven, black of hue, flying out hither ward, hath  
   dropped.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0706402">2</span></span>May Agni
Gārhapatya save and set me free from all this guilt.  
  Which the black Raven with thy mouth, O Nirriti, hath wiped  
   away.
