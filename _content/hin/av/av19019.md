### HYMN XIX

#### A hymn recommending the protection of various Gods

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901901">1</span></span>Mitra arose
with Earth. To that fort I lead you: enter it,  
   occupy it. Let it give you protection and defence.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901902">2</span></span>Vāyu arose
with Air. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901903">3</span></span>Sūrya arose
with Heaven. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901904">4</span></span>Chandramās
arose with the Constellation. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901905">5</span></span>Sacrifice
arose with Priestly Fees. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901906">6</span></span>Ocean arose
with the Rivers. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901907">7</span></span>Brahma arose
with the Brahmachārīs. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901908">8</span></span>Indra arose
with Might. To that fort, etc. <span id="page_228">p. 228</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901909">9</span></span>The Gods
arose with Immortality. To that fort, etc.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1901910">10</span></span>Prajāpati
arose with Creatures. To that fort I lead you: enter  
   it, occupy it give you protection and defence.
