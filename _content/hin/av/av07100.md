### HYMN C

#### A charm against nightmare

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0710001">1</span></span>I turn away
from evil dream, from dream of sin, from indigence.  
  I make the prayer mine inmost friend. Hence! torturing.  
   dreamy phantasies!
