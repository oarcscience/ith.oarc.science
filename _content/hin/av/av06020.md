### HYMN XX

#### A charm against fever

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602001">1</span></span>He goes away
as 'twere from this fierce burning fire, inebriated  
   and lamenting he departs.  
  Let him, the lawless, seek another and not us. Worship be paid  
   to Fever armed with fiery heat.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602002">2</span></span>To Rudra and
to Fever be our worship paid: worship be paid  
   to Varuna the splendid King!  
  Worship to Dyaus, to Earth, worship be paid to Plants!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0602003">3</span></span>Thou who,
aglow with heat, makest all bodies green, to thee,  
   red, brown, I bow, the Fever of the wood.
