### HYMN XXVII

#### An Apri or Propitiatory hymn

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502701">1</span></span>Uplifted be
this sacrificer's fuel: lofty and brilliant be the flames  
   of Agni!  
  Splendidly bright, fair-faced, with all his offspring, Tanūnapāt  
   the Asura, many-handed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502702">2</span></span>God among
Gods, the God bedews the paths with fatness and'  
   with mead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502703">3</span></span>With store
of mead to sacrifice comes Agni, comes Narāsansa  
  Agni, friendly-minded, comes Savitar, righteous God who  
   brings all blessings.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502704">4</span></span>Hither he
comes with power and fatness also, the luminous,.  
   implored with adoration. <span id="page_a190">p. a190</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502705">5</span></span>At holy
rites and offerings Agni loveth the scoops: let this man  
   worship Agni's greatness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502706">6</span></span>He is the
furtherer at glad oblations: there stood the Vasus and  
   the treasure-givers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502707">7</span></span>Ever the
Doors divine, and all protect this worshipper's holy  
   work.  
<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_0502708">8</span></span>Far-reaching, ruling by the Law of
Agni,  
  May Dawn and Night, the holy, speeding near us, aid this our  
   sacrificial ceremony.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502709">9</span></span>Celestial
Hotars, with the tongues of Agni praise and extol our  
   lofty ceremony, so that our sacrifice be well conducted!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502710">10</span></span>Three
Goddesses upon this grass, be seated, Idā, Sarasvati,  
  Mahi, and Bhārati adored with praise.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502711">11</span></span>This our
nutritious genial flow, God Tvashtar! and growth of  
   wealth, pour down on this man's kindred.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502712">12</span></span>Vanaspati,
rejoicing, of thyself send God-ward! Let Agni, Im-  
   molator, sweeten our libation.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502713">13</span></span>Pay
sacrifice to Indra, Jātavedas Agni, with Hail! Let all the  
  Gods accept the gifts we offer.
