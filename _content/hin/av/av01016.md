### HYMN XVI

#### A prayer and charm against demons

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101601">1</span></span>May potent
Agni who destroys the demons bless and shelter us.  
  From greedy fiends who rise in troops at night-time when the  
   moon is dark.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101602">2</span></span>Varuna's
benison hath blessed the lead, and Agni strengthens it.  
  Indra hath given me the lead: this verily repels the fiends.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101603">3</span></span>This
overcomes Vishkandha, -this drives the voracious fiends  
   away:  
  By means of this have I, o'erthrown all the Pisāchi's demon  
   brood.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101604">4</span></span>If thou
destroy a cow of ours, a human being, or a steed,  
  We pierce thee with this piece of lead so that thou mayst not slay  
   our men.
