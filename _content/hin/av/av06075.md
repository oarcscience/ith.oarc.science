### HYMN LXXV

#### A charm to effect the removal of an enemy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607501">1</span></span>Forth from
his dwelling drive that man, the foeman who  
   assaileth us:  
  Through the Expellent sacrifice hath Indra rent and mangled  
   him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607502">2</span></span>Indra,
Foe-Slayer, drive him forth into the distance most  
   remote,  
  Whence never more shall be return in all the years that are to  
   come.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0607503">3</span></span>To the three
distances, beyond mankind's Five Races, let him go,  
  Beyond the three skies let him go, whence he shall never come-  
   again  
  In all the years that are to be, long as the Sun is in the heaven.
