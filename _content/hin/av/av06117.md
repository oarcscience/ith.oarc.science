### HYMN CXVII

#### A prayer for freedom from debt

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611701">1</span></span>That which I
eat, a debt which still is owing, the tribute due to  
  Yama, which supports me, <span id="page_a255">p. a255</span>  
  Thereby may I be free from debt, O Agni. Thou knowest how  
   to rend all bonds asunder.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611702">2</span></span>Still
dwelling here we give again this present; we send it forth,  
   the living from the living.  
  Throwing away the grain whence I have eaten, thereby shall I  
   be free from debt, O Agni.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0611703">3</span></span>May we be
free in this world and that yonder, in the third  
   world may we be unindebted.  
  May we, debt-free, abide in all the pathways, in all the worlds  
   which Gods and Fathers visit.
