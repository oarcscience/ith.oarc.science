### HYMN LIV

#### A charm to obtain knowledge of the Veda

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0705401">1</span></span>We worship
holy Verse and Song, by which they carry out their  
   acts,  
  Shining in order's seat these twain present the sacrifice to Gods.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0705402">2</span></span>As I have
asked about Verse, Song, Sacrifice, strength, force,.  
  Yajus-text,  
  So never let this lore that I have sought forsake me, Lord of  
  Might!
