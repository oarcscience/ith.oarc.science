### HYMN LXXXVII

#### A benediction addressed to a newly elected King

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608701">1</span></span>Here art
thou: I have chosen thee. Stand stedfast and immov-  
   able.  
  Let all the clans desire thee: let not thy kingdom fall away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608702">2</span></span>Be even
here: fall not away: be like a mountain unremoved.  
  Stand stedfast here like Indra's self, and hold the kingship in  
   the grasp.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0608703">3</span></span>This man
hath Indra stablished, made secure by constant sacri-  
   fice.  
  Soma, and Brāhmanaspati here present bless and comfort him!
