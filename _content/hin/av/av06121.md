### HYMN CXXI

#### A prayer for happiness in heaven

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612101">1</span></span>Spreading
them out, untie the snares that hold us, Varuna's  
   bonds, the upper and the lower.  
  Drive from us evil dream, drive off misfortune; then let us go in-  
   to the world of virtue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612102">2</span></span>If thou art
bound with cord or tied to timber, fixt in the earth,  
   or by a word imprisoned,  
  Our Agni Gārhapatya here shall free thee, and lead thee up into  
   the world of virtue.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612103">3</span></span>The two
auspicious stars whose name is called Releasers have  
   gone up.  
  Send Amrit hither, let it come freeing the captive from his  
   bonds!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0612104">4</span></span>Open
thyself, make room: from bonds thou shalt release the  
   prisoner.  
  Freed, like an infant newly born, dwell in all pathways where  
   thou wilt.
