### HYMN XVII

#### A prayer for wealth and children

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701701">1</span></span>May the
Ordainer give us wealth, Lord, ruler of the world of  
   life: with full hand may he give to us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701702">2</span></span>May Dhātar
grant the worshipper henceforth imperishable life.  
  May we obtain the favour of the God who giveth every boon.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701703">3</span></span>To him may
Dhātar grant all kinds of blessings who, craving  
   children, serves him in his dwelling.  
  Him may the Gods invest with life eternal, yea, all the Gods and  
  Aditi accordant.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701704">4</span></span>May this our
gift please Savitar, Rāti, Dhātar, Prajāpati, and  
  Agni Lord of Treasures.  
  May Tvashtar, Vishnu, blessing him with children, give store ot  
   riches to the sacrificer.
