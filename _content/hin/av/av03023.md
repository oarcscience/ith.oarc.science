### HYMN XXIII

#### A charm to remove a woman's sterility, and to assure the birth of boys

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302301">1</span></span>From thee we
banish and expel the cause of thy sterility.  
  This in another place we lay apart from thee and far removed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302302">2</span></span>As arrow to
the quiver, so let a male embryo enter thee.  
  Then from thy side be born a babe, a ten-month child, thy hero  
   son.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302303">3</span></span>Bring forth
a male, bring forth a son. Another male shall follow  
   him.  
  The mother shalt thou be of sons born and hereafter to be  
   born.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302304">4</span></span>With that
auspicious general flow wherewith steers propagate  
   their kind,  
  Do thou obtain thyself a son: be thou a fruitfu! mother-cow.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302305">5</span></span>I give thee
power to bear a child: within, thee pass the germ of  
   life!  
  Obtain a son, O woman, who shall be a blessing unto thee. Be  
   thou a blessing unto him.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0302306">6</span></span>May those
celestial herbs whose sire was Heaven, the Earth their  
   mother, and their root the ocean.  
  May those celestial healing Plants assist thee to obtain a son.
