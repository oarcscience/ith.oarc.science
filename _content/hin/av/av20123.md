### HYMN CXXIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012301">1</span></span>This is the
Godhead, this the might of Sūrya: he hath with-  
   drawn what spread o'er work unfinished.  
  When he hath loosed his horses from their station, straight over  
   all night spreadeth out her garment.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2012302">2</span></span>In the sky's
lap the Sun this form assumeth for Mitra and for  
  Vāruna to look on.  
  His bay steeds well maintain his power eternal, at one time  
   bright and darksome at another.
