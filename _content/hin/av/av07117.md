### HYMN CXVII

#### A charm to ensure prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711701">1</span></span>Come hither,
Indra, with bay steeds, joyous, with tails like pea-  
   cock plumes.  
  Let none impede thy way as fowlers stay the bird: pass o'er  
   them as o'er desert lands.
