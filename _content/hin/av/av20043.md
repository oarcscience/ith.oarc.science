### HYMN XLIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004301">1</span></span>Drive all
our enemies away, smite down the foes who press  
   around,  
  And bring the wealth for which we long;  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004302">2</span></span>O Indra,
that which is concealed in firm strong place  
   precipitous:  
  Bring us the wealth for which we long:  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004303">3</span></span>Great riches
which the world of men shall recognize as sent by  
   thee:  
  Bring us the wealth for which we long,
