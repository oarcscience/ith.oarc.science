### HYMN LXXXIX

#### A prayer for purification and prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708901">1</span></span>The heavenly
Waters have I ranged: we have been sated with  
   their dew. <span id="page_a311">p. a311</span>  
  Here, Agni, bearing milk, am I. Endow me with the gift of  
   strength.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708902">2</span></span>Endow me
with the gift of strength, with children, and a length-  
   ened life.  
  May the Gods mark this prayer of mine, may Indra with the  
  Rishis mark.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708903">3</span></span>Ye Waters,
wash away this stain and whatsoever taint be here,  
  Each sinful wrong that I have done and every harmless curse  
   of mine.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708904">4</span></span>Thou art the
wood, may I succeed! fuel, may I be glorified!  
   splendour, give splendour unto me.
