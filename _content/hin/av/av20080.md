### HYMN LXXX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008001">1</span></span>Bring us, O
Indra, name and fame, enriching, mightiest,  
   excellent,  
  Wherewith, O wondrous God, fair-cheeked and thunder-armed,  
   thou hast filled full this earth and heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2008002">2</span></span>We call on
thee, O King, mighty among the Gods, ruler of men,  
   to succour us,  
  All that is weak in us, excellent God, make firm: make our foes  
   easy to subdue.
