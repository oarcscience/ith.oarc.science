### HYMN XXXIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203301">1</span></span>From both
thy nostrils, from both eyes, from both thine ears,  
   and from thy chin,  
  Forth from thy brain and tongue I root Consumption seated in  
   thy head.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203302">2</span></span>Forth from
the neck and from the nape, from dorsal vertebrae  
   and spine.  
  From arms and shoulder-blades I root Consumption seated in  
   thine arms.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203303">3</span></span>Forth from
thy heart and from thy lungs, from thy gall-bladder  
   and thy sides,  
  From kidneys, spleen and liver thy Consumption we eradicate.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203304">4</span></span>From bowels
and intestines, from the rectum and the belly, I  
  Extirpate thy Consumption, from flanks, navel and mesentery.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203305">5</span></span>Forth from
thy thighs and from thy knees, heels and the fore-  
   parts of thy feet.  
  Forth from thy loins and hips I draw Consumption setted in  
   thy loins.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203306">6</span></span>Forth from
thy marrows and thy bones, forth from thy tendons  
   and thy veins  
  I banish thy Consumption, from thy hands, thy fingers, and thy  
   nails.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0203307">7</span></span>In every
member, every hair, in every joint wherein it lies,  
  We with the exorcising spell of Kasyapa drive far away Con-  
   sumption settled in thy skin.
