### HYMN C

#### A charm against poison

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610001">1</span></span>The Gods and
Sūrya gave the gift, the Earth and Heaven best-  
   owed the boon.  
  The three Sarasvatis in full accord bestowed the antidote.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610002">2</span></span>That water,
Upajīkās! which Gods poured for you on thirsty land,  
  With that same water sent by Gods, drive ye away this poison  
   here. <span id="page_a247">p. a247</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0609803">3</span></span>The daughter
of the Asuras art thou, and sister of the Gods.  
  Thou who hast sprung from heaven and earth hast robbed the  
   poison of its power.
