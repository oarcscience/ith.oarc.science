### HYMN XXXVII

#### A charm to secure long life and dominion to a prince

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903701">1</span></span>To me hath
come this word given by Agni, fame, force and  
   might, and strength, and life, and lustre.  
  May Agni too bestow on me three-times a hundred manly  
   powers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903702">2</span></span>For mighty
strength, for action, I receive thee, for manly power,  
   to last a hundred autumns. <span id="page_242">p. 242</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903703">3</span></span>For
conquering strength and energy and vigour  
  I fasten thee for chieftainship, for bearing royal dominion  
   through a hundred autumns.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903704">4</span></span>With Seasons
and with Season-groups, for vigour and extended  
   life.  
  With splendour of the perfect year we fasten thee about the  
   neck.
