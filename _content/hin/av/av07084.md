### HYMN LXXXIV

#### A prayer for protection

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708401">1</span></span>Holder of
sway, shine here refulgent, Agni! invincible immortal  
  Jātavedas.  
  With succours friendly to mankind, auspicious, driving away all  
   maladies, guard our dwelling.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708402">2</span></span>Thou, Indra,
lord and leader of the people, wast born for lovely  
   strength and high dominion.  
  Thou dravest off the folk who were unfriendly, and madest for  
   the Gods wide room and freedom.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0708403">3</span></span>Like a dread
wild beast roaming on the mountain, may he.  
   approach us from the farthest distance.  
  Whetting thy bolt and thy sharp blade, O Indra, crush down our  
   foes and scatter those who hate us.
