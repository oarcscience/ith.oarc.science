### HYMN XXIX

#### A charm to secure the supremacy of a dethroned King

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102901">1</span></span>With that
victorious Amulet which strengthened Indra's power-  
   and might <span id="page_a27">p. a27</span>  
  Do thou, O Brāhmanaspati, increase our strength for kingly  
   sway.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102902">2</span></span>Subduing
those who rival us, subduing all malignities,  
  Withstand the man who menaces, and him who seeks to injure-  
   us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102903">3</span></span>Soma and
Savitar the God have strengthened and exalted thee:  
  All elements have aided thee, to make thee general conqueror.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102904">4</span></span>Slayer of
rivals, vanquisher, may that victorious Amulet  
  Be bound on me for regal sway and conquest of mine enemies.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102905">5</span></span>Yon Sun hath
mounted up on high, and this my word hath  
   mounted up  
  That I may smite my foes and be slayer of rivals, rivalless.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102906">6</span></span>Destroyer of
my rivals, strong, victorious, with royal sway,  
  May I be ruler of these men, and King and sovran of the folk.
