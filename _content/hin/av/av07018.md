### HYMN XVIII

#### A prayer for rain

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701801">1</span></span>Burst open,
Prithvi, and cleave asunder this celestial cloud.  
  Untie, O Dhātar—for thou canst—the bottle of the breast of  
   heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0701802">2</span></span>Let not the
Sun's heat burn, nor cold destroy her. Let Earth  
   with all her quickening drops burst open.  
  Even for her the waters flow, and fatness: where Soma is even  
   there is bliss for ever.
