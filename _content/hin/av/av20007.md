### HYMN VII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000701">1</span></span>Sūrya, thou
mountest up to meet the Hero famous for his  
   wealth,  
  Who hurls the bolt and works for man:  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000702">2</span></span>Him who with
might of both his arms brake nine-and ninety  
   castles down,  
  Slew Vritra and smote Ahi dead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000703">3</span></span>This Indra
is our gracious Friend. He sends us in a full broad  
   stream  
  Riches in horses, kine, and corn.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000704">4</span></span>Indra, whom
many laud, accept the strength-conferring Soma  
   juice.  
  Quaff, pour down drink that satisfies.
