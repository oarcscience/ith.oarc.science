### HYMN CIX

#### A charm to heal punctured wounds

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610901">1</span></span>The Berry
heals the missile's rent, it heals the deeply-piercing  
   wound.  
  The Gods prepared and fashioned it. This hath sufficient power  
   for life.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610902">2</span></span>When from
their origin they came, the Berries spake among  
   themselves:  
  The man whom we shall find alive shall never suffer injury. <span
id="page_a251">p. a251</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610903">3</span></span>Asuras
buried thee in earth: the Gods again uplifted thee.  
  Healer of sickness caused by wounds and healer of the missile's  
   rent.
