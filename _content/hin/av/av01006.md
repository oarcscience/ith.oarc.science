### HYMN VI

#### To the waters, for health and wealth

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100601">1</span></span>The Waters
be to us for drink, Goddesses, for our aid and  
   bliss: <span id="page_a7">p. a7</span>  
  Let them stream health and wealth to us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100602">2</span></span>Within the
Waters—Soma thus hath told me—dwell all balms  
   that heal,  
  And Agni, he who blesseth all.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100603">3</span></span>O Waters,
teem with medicine to keep my body safe from harm,  
  So that I long may see the Sun.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100604">4</span></span>The Waters
bless us, all that rise in desert lands or marshy  
   pools!  
  Bless us the Waters dug from earth, bless us the Waters brought  
   in jars, bless us the Waters of the Rains!
