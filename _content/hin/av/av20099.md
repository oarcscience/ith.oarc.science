### HYMN XCIX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009901">1</span></span>Men with
their lauds are urging thee, Indra, to drink the Soma  
   first.  
  The Ribhus in accord have lifted up their voice and Rudras.  
   sung thee as the First.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2009902">2</span></span>Indra
increased his manly strength at sacrifice, in the wild  
   rapture of this juice;  
  And living men to-day, even as of old, sing forth their praises to  
   his majesty.
