### HYMN V

#### To the waters, for strength and power

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100501">1</span></span>Ye, Waters,
truly bring us bliss: so help ye us to strength and  
   power <span id="page_a6">p. a6</span>  
  That we may look on great delight.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100502">2</span></span>Here grant
to us a share of dew, that most auspicious dew of  
   yours,  
  Like mothers in their longing love.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100503">3</span></span>For you we
fain would go to him to whose abode ye send us  
   forth,  
  And, Waters, give us procreant strength.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0100504">4</span></span>I pray the
Floods to send us balm, those who bear rule o'er  
   precious things,  
  And have supreme control of men.
