### HYMN XVIII

#### A charm to avert evil spirits of misfortune and to secure prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101801">1</span></span>We drive
away the Spotted Hag, Misfortune, and Malignity:  
  All blessings to our children then! We chase Malignity  
   away.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101802">2</span></span>Let Savitar,
Mitra, Varuna, and Aryaman drive away Stinginess  
   from both the hands and feet:  
  May Favour, granting us her bounties, drive her off. The Gods  
   created Favour for our happiness.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0101803">3</span></span>Each fearful
sign upon thy body, in thyself, each inauspicious  
   mark seen in thy hair, thy face,  
  All this we drive away and banish with our speech. May Savitar  
   the God graciously further thee. <span id="page_a18">p. a18</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span
id="an_0101804">4</span></span>Antelope-foot, and Bullock-tooth,
Cow-terrifier, Vapour-form,  
  The Licker, and the Spotted Hag, all these we drive away from  
   us.
