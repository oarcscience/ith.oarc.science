### HYMN XXXI

#### A prayer for protection and general prosperity

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0103101">1</span></span>Here will we
serve with sacrifice the great Controllers of the  
   world,  
  The four immortal Warders who protect the regions of the sky.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0103102">2</span></span>Ye,
Guardians of the regions, Gods who keep the quarters of  
   the heavens,  
  Rescue and free us from the bonds of Nirriti, from grief and  
   woe!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0103103">3</span></span>I, free from
stiffness, serve thee with oblation, not lame I sacri-  
   fice with oil and fatness.  
  Let the strong Warder God, who keeps the regions bring to us  
   hither safety and well-being.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0103104">4</span></span>Well be it
with our mother and our father, well be it with our  
   cows, and beasts, and people.  
  Ours be all happy fortune, grace, and favour. Long, very long  
   may we behold the sunlight.
