### HYMN IX

#### A prayer to Heaven and Earth for protection and assistance

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500901">1</span></span>All hail to
Heaven!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500902">2</span></span>All hail to
Earth!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500903">3</span></span>All hail to
Air!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500904">4</span></span>All hail to
Air!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500905">5</span></span>All hail to
Heaven!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500906">6</span></span>All hail to
Eartht!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500907">7</span></span>Mine eye is
Sīirya and my breath is Vāta, Air is my soul and  
  Prithivī my body.  
  I verily who never have been conquered give up my life toe  
  Heaven and Earth for keeping. <span id="page_a165">p. a165</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0500908">8</span></span>Exalt my
life, my strength, my deed and action; increase my  
   understanding and my vigour.  
  Be ye my powerful keepers, watch and guard me, ye mistresses  
   of life and life's creators! Dwell ye within me, and forbear  
   to harm me.
