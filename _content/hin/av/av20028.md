### HYMN XXVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002801">1</span></span>In Soma's
ecstasy Indra spread the firmament and realms of  
   light.  
  When he cleft Vala limb from limb.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002802">2</span></span>Showing the
hidden he draye forth the cows for the Angirases,  
  And Vala he cast headlong down.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002803">3</span></span>By Indra
were the luminous realms of heaven established and  
   secured.  
  Firm and immovable in their place.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2002804">4</span></span>Indra, thy
laud moves quickly like a joyous wave of water-floods.  
  Bright shine the drops that gladden thee.
