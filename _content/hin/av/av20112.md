### HYMN CXII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011201">1</span></span>Whatever,
Vritra-slayer! thou Sūrya, hast risen upon to-day,  
  That, Indra, all is in thy power.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011202">2</span></span>When, Mighty
One, Lord of the Brave, thou thinkest, I shall  
   never die,  
  That thought of thine is true indeed.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2011203">3</span></span>Thou, Indra,
goest unto all Soma libations shed for thee,  
  Both far away and near at hand.
