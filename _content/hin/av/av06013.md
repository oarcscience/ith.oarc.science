### HYMN XIII

#### Homage to death

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601301">1</span></span>Worship to
weapons of the Gods! worship to weapons of the  
  Kings!  
  Then worship to the people's arms! worship, O Death, be paid  
   to thee!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601302">2</span></span>Let worship
be to thy defence and to thine accusation paid.  
  Death! be this worship paid to thy good-will and thy malevo-  
   lence!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601303">3</span></span>Worship to
thy physicians, to thy sorcerers be worship paid!  
  Death! let this reverence be done unto thy Brāhmans and thy  
   roots.
