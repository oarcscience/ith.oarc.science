### HYMN XXIII

#### A charm against leprosy

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102301">1</span></span>O Plant,
thou sprangest up at night, dusky, dark-coloured,  
   black in hue! <span id="page_a22">p. a22</span>  
  So, Rajani, re-colour thou these ashy spots, this leprosy.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102302">2</span></span>Expel the
leprosy, remove from him the spots and ashy hue:  
  Let thine own colour come to thee; drive far away the specks of  
   white.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102303">3</span></span>Dark is the
place of thy repose, dark is the place thou dwellest  
   in:  
  Dusky and dark, O Plant, art thou: remove from him each  
   speck and spot.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0102304">4</span></span>I with my
spell have chased away the pallid sign of leprosy,  
  Caused by infection, on the skin, sprung from the body, from the  
   bones.
