### HYMN CXLI

#### A blessing on cattle

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614201">1</span></span>Spring high,
O Barley, and become much through thine own  
   magnificence: <span id="page_a269">p. a269</span>  
  Burst all the vessels; let the bolt from heaven forbear to strike  
   thee down.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614202">2</span></span>As we invite
and call to thee, Barley, a God who heareth us,  
  Raise thyself up like heaven on high and be exhaustless as the  
   sea.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614203">3</span></span>Exhaustless
let thine out-turns be, exhaustless be thy gathered  
   heaps,  
  Exhaustless be thy givers, and exhaustless those who eat of  
   thee.
