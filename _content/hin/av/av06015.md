### HYMN XV

#### A charm for power and preeminence

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601501">1</span></span>Most
excellent of all the plants art thou: thy vassals are the  
   trees.  
  Let him be subject to our power, the man who seeks to injure  
   us.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601502">2</span></span>Whoever
seeks to injure us, with kinsmen or no kin to aid,  
  May I be uppermost of all, even as this Plant is queen of trees.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0601503">3</span></span>As Soma hath
been made the best of all oblations 'mid the  
   plants,  
  So, as Talāsā is the queen of trees, may I be chief of all.
