### HYMN V

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000501">1</span></span>Like women,
let this Soma juice invested with its raiment, glide..  
  Most active Indra, close to thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000502">2</span></span>Mighty in
bulk, strong-necked, stout-armed in the wild rapture.  
   of the juice <span id="page_267">p. 267</span>  
  Doth Indra smite the foemen dead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000503">3</span></span>Indra,
advance, go forward thou who by thy might art Lord of  
   all.  
  Slay, Vritra-slayer slay thy foes.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000504">4</span></span>Long be thy
grasping-hook wherewith thou givest treasure unto-  
   him  
  Who pours the juice and worships thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000505">5</span></span>Here, Indra,
is thy Soma draught, made pure upon the sacred.  
   grass.  
  Run hither, come and drink thereof.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000506">6</span></span>Famed for
thy radiance, worshipped well! this juice is shed for  
   thy delight:  
  Thou art invoked, Ākhandala!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2000507">7</span></span>To
Kundapāyya, grandson's son, grandson of Sringavrish, to  
   thee,  
  To him have I addressed my thought.
