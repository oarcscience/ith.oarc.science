### HYMN CVII

#### A charm against Cough

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0710701">1</span></span>The seven
bright beams of Surya bring the waters downward  
   from the sky,  
  The streams of ocean: these have made the sting that pained  
   thee drop away.
