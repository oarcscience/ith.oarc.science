### HYMN XVIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501801">1</span></span>Of that
Vrātya.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501802">2</span></span>The right
eye is the Sun and the left eye is the Moon.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501803">3</span></span>His right
ear is Agni and his left ear is Pavamāna.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501804">4</span></span>Day and
Night are his nostrils. Diti and Aditi are his head and  
   skull.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1501805">5</span></span>By day the
Vrātya is turned westward, by night he is turned  
   eastward. Worship to the Vrātya!
