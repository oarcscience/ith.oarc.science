### HYMN CXXX

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013101">1, 2</span></span>Who
carried off these stores of milk? Who took the dark  
   cow's milk away?  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013103">3, 4</span></span>Who took
away the white cow's milk t Who took the black  
   cow's milk away?  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013105">5, 6</span></span> Question
this man, Where do I ask? Where, whom that  
   knoweth do I ask?  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013107">7, 8</span></span>Not to
the belly comes the grain. The patient ones are  
   angry now.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013109">9, 10</span></span>Undecked
with gems, and decked with gems: deity rivalling  
   the Sun.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013111">11, 12</span></span>Dapple,
Harinikā, and Bay ran forward to the liberal gifts.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013113">13, 14</span></span>When
the horn's blast hath sounded forth let not our friend  
   discover thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013115">15, 16</span></span>Hither
to the cow's son they come. Libation hath rejoiced  
   the God.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013117">17, 18</span></span>Then
cried they. Here he is, and, Here; again the cry was,  
  Here is he.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2013119">19, 20</span></span>Then
not defective be our steeds! A splinter so diminutive!
