### HYMN XLII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004201">1</span></span>From Indra
have I measured an eight-footed and nine-cornered.  
   song, <span id="page_302">p. 302</span>  
  Delicate, faithful to the Law.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004202">2</span></span>Indra, both
worlds complained to thee when uttering thy fearful  
   roar,  
  What time thou smotest Dasyus dead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2004203">3</span></span>Arising in
thy might thy jaws thou shookest, Indra, having  
   quaffed  
  The Soma poured into the bowls.
