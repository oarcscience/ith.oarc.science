### HYMN LXV

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006501">1</span></span>Come, sing
we praise to Indra, friends! the Hero who deserves  
   the laud,  
  Him who with none to aid o'ercomes all tribes of men. <span
id="page_316">p. 316</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006502">2</span></span>To him who
wins the kine, who keeps no cattle back, celestial  
  God,  
  Speak wondrous speech more sweet than butter and than mead.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2006503">3</span></span>Whose hero
powers are measureless, whose bounty ne'er may be  
   surpassed,  
  Whose liberality, like light, is over all.
