### HYMN V

#### A prayer for riches

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1900501">4</span></span>King of the
living world and men is Indra, of all in varied form  
   that earth containeth.  
  Thence to the worshipper he giveth riches: may he enrich even  
   us when we have praised him.
