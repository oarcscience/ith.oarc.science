### HYMN CIII

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010301">1</span></span>Solicit with
your hymns, for aid, Agni the God with piercing  
   flame,  
  For riches famous Agni, Purmilha ‘and ye men, Agni to light  
   our dwelling well.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010302">2</span></span>Agni, come
hither with thy fires: we choose thee as our Hotai-  
   priest.  
  Let the extended ladle full of oil balm thee, best priest, to sit on  
   sacred grass.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2010303">3</span></span>For unto
thee, O Angiras, O Son of Strength, move ladles in the  
   sacrifice,  
  To Agni, Child of Force, whose locks drop oil, we seek, fore-  
   most in sacrificial rites.
