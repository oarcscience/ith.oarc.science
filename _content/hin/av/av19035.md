### HYMN XXXV

#### A similar charm addressed to the same

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903501">1</span></span>While their
lips uttered Indra's name the Rishis gave us Jangida..  
  Which in the earliest time Gods made a remedy, Vishkandha's-  
   cure.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903502">2</span></span>So may this
Jangida guard us, even as a treasurer guards wealth,.  
  Even this which Gods and Brāhmans made a malice-quelling sure  
   defence.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903503">3</span></span>Hard-hearted
men, the cruel eye, the sinner who hath come to-  
   us,  
  Destroy thou these with watchful care, O thou who hast a.  
   thousand eyes. Thou, Jangida, art my defence.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903504">4</span></span>Guard me
from earth and guard me from the heavens, guard  
   me from middle air, from plants protect me.  
  Protect me from the present and the future. From every region  
  Jangida preserve us!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1903505">5</span></span>All
sorcerers made by the Gods, all that arise from mortal men,.  
  These, one and all, let Jangida, healer of all, make impotent.
