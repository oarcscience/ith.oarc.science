### HYMN CXIV

#### A woman's incantation against a rival

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711401">1</span></span>I have
extracted from thy sides, I have extracted from thy heart,  
  I have extracted from thy face the strength and splendour that  
   were thine.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711402">2</span></span>Let pain and
suffering pass away, let cares and curses vanish.  
   hence.  
  Let Agni slay the fiendish hags, Soma kill bags who trouble us.
