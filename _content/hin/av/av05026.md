### HYMN XXVI

#### A hymn of invitation to the gods

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502601">1</span></span>In sacrifice
for you may sapient Agni—All hail!—use Yajus  
   texts and fuel.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502602">2</span></span>May Savitar
the God—All hail!—foreknowing, chief in this  
   sacrifice, employ them.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502603">3</span></span>In this
great rite—All hail!—may sapient Indra use lauds,  
   rejoicings, well-yoked coursers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502604">4</span></span>Bring
Praishas in the rite—All hail!—and Nivids, learned, con-  
   nected, with the Consorts.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502605">5</span></span>As a dame
brings her son—All hail! O Maruts, connected, in  
   the rite bring measures.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502606">6</span></span>Here Aditi
is come—All hail!—preparing the rite with grass and  
   lustral waters.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502607">7</span></span>Let Vishnu
in this rite in varied manner—All hail! use well-  
   yoked steeds, his fervours.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502608">8</span></span>Let Tvashtar
in this rite in varied manner—All hail!—use forms,  
   his well-yoked coursers. <span id="page_a189">p. a189</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502609">9</span></span>Let Bhaga in
this rite use prayers, foreknowing—All hail! for  
   this use well-yoked coursers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502610">10</span></span>Let Soma in
this rite in varied manner—All hail!—use milk-  
   streams, well-yoked coursers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502611">11</span></span>Let Indra
in this rite in varied manner—All hail!—use powers,.  
   his well-yoked coursers.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502612">12</span></span>Hitherward
come ye with the prayer, O Asvins, exalting sacrifice  
   with cry of Vashat!  
  Brihaspati!—All hail!—with prayer come hither. Here is the  
   rite, here heaven for him who worships.
