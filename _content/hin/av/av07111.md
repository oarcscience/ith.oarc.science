### HYMN CXI

#### A prayer for offspring

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711101">1</span></span>Belly of
Indra art thou, Soma-holder! the very soul of Gods  
   and human beings.  
  Here be the sire of offspring, thine here present! Here be they  
   glad in thee who now are elsewhere.
