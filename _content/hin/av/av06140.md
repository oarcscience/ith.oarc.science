### HYMN CXL

#### A blessing on a child's first two teeth

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614001">1</span></span>Two tigers
have grown up who long to eat the mother and the  
   sire:  
  Soothe, Brāhmanaspati, and thou, O Jātavedas, both these  
   teeth.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614002">2</span></span>Let rice and
barley be your food, eat also beans and sesamum.  
  This is the share allotted you, to be your portion, ye two Teeth.  
  Harm not your mother and your sire.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0614003">3</span></span>Both fellow
teeth have been invoked, gentle and bringing happi-  
   ness.  
  Else whither let the fierceness of your nature turn away, O  
  Teeth! Harm not your mother or your sire.
