### HYMN CXVI

#### A charm against Fever

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711601">1</span></span>Homage to
him the burning one, shaker, exciter, violent!  
  Homage to him the cold who acts according to his ancient will!  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0711602">2</span></span>May he, the
lawless one, who comes alternate or two following  
   days, pass over and possess the frog.
