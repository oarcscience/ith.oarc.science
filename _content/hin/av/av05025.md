### HYMN XXV

#### A charm to facilitate conception

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502501">1</span></span>Let the man,
sower of the germ, lay, as a feather on a shaft.  
  Limb drawn from limb, whate'er is culled from cloud and from  
   the womb of heaven.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502502">2</span></span>Even as this
broad earth received the germ of all the things that  
   be,  
  Thus within thee I lay the germ. I call thee, Earth, to strengthen  
   it.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502503">3</span></span>O Sinivāli,
set the germ, set thou the germ, Sarasvati! In thee  
   let both the Asvins, crowned with lotuses, bestow the germ.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502504">4</span></span>Let
Mitra-Varuna and God Brihaspati lay the germ in thee.  
  Indra and Agni lay the germ, Dhātar bestow the germ in thee.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502505">5</span></span>Let Vishnu
form and mould the womb, let Tvashtar duly shape  
   the forms,  
  Prajāpati infuse the stream, and Dhātar lay for thee the germ.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502506">6</span></span>Drink thou
the procreative draught well-known to Varuna the  
  King,  
  Known to divine Sarasvati, and Indra slayer of the foe.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502507">7</span></span>Thou art the
germ of plants and herbs, thou art the germ of  
   forest trees,  
  The germ of each existing thing, so here, O Agni, lay the germ.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502508">8</span></span>Rise up, put
forth thy manly strength, and lay thy germ within  
   the womb.  
  A bull art thou with vigorous strength: for progeny we bring  
   thee near.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502509">9</span></span>Prepare
thee, Bārhatsāmā, let the germ be laid within thy side.  
  The Soma-drinking Gods have given a son to thee, thy son and  
   mine.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502510">10</span></span>O Dhātar,
thou Disposer, lay within the body of this dame.  
  A male germ with the noblest form, for her, in the tenth month,  
   to bear.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502511">11</span></span>Tvashtar,
celestial artist, lay within the body of this dame.  
  A male germ with the noblest form for her in the tenth month  
   to bear. <span id="page_a188">p. a188</span>  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502512">12</span></span>Savitar,
vivifier, lay within the body of this dame A male germ  
   with the noblest form for her in the tenth month to bear.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0502513">13</span></span>O Lord of
Life, Prajāpati, within this woman's body lay  
  A male germ with the noblest form for her in the tenth month  
   to bear.
