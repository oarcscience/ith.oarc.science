### HYMN CI

#### A charm to promote virile vigour

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610101">1</span></span>Taurum age,
palpita, incresce et teipsum extende: per totum  
   membrum increscat penis: hoc tu caede feminam.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610102">2</span></span>Quo debilem
stimulant, quo aegrum excitant (homines), hoc, O  
  Brahmanaspatis, hujus penem in arcus modum extende.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_0610103">3</span></span>Velut nervum
in arcu ego tuum fascinum extendo. Aggredere  
  (mulierem) semper indefessus velut cervus damam.
