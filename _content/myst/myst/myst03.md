<span id="an_iii-p1"></span>“What the world, which truly knows
*nothing,* calls ‘mysticism’ is the science of *ultimates, . . .* the
science of self-evident Reality, which cannot be ‘reasoned about,’
because it is the object of pure reason or perception. The Babe sucking
its mother’s breast, and the Lover returning, after twenty years’
separation, to his home and food in the same bosom, are the types and
princes of Mystics.”
