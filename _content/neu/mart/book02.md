[BOOK III CHAPTER I](mart046) <span class="small">How King Arthur took a
wife, and wedded Guenever, daughter to Leodegrance, King of the Land of
Cameliard, with whom he had the Round Table.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER II](mart047) <span class="small">How the Knights of
the Round Table were ordained and their sieges blessed by the Bishop of
Canterbury.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER III](mart048) <span class="small">How a poor man
riding upon a lean mare desired King Arthur to make his son
knight.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER IV](mart049) <span class="small">How Sir Tor was known
for son of King Pellinore, and how Gawaine was made knight.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER V](mart050) <span class="small">How at feast of the
wedding of King Arthur to Guenever, a white hart came into the hall, and
thirty couple hounds, and how a brachet pinched the hart which was taken
away.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER VI](mart051) <span class="small">How Sir Gawaine rode
for to fetch again the hart, and how two brethren fought each against
other for the hart.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER VII](mart052) <span class="small">How the hart was
chased into a castle and there slain, and how Sir Gawaine slew a
lady.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER VIII](mart053) <span class="small">How four knights
fought against Gawaine and Gaheris, and how they were overcome, and
their lives saved at request of four ladies.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER IX](mart054) <span class="small">How Sir Tor rode
after the knight with the brachet, and of his adventure by the
way.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER X](mart055) <span class="small">How Sir Tor found the
brachet with a lady, and how a knight assailed him for the said
brachet.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER XI](mart056) <span class="small">How Sir Tor overcame
the knight, and how he lost his head at the request of a lady.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER XII](mart057) <span class="small">How King Pellinore
rode after the lady and the knight that led her away, and how a lady
desired help of him, and how he fought with two knights for that lady,
of whom he slew the one at the first stroke.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER XIII](mart058) <span class="small">How King Pellinore
gat the lady and brought her to Camelot to the court of King
Arthur.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER XIV](mart059) <span class="small">How on the way he
heard two knights, as he lay by night in a valley, and of their
adventures.</span>  
[BOOK III CHAPTER XV](mart060) <span class="small">How when he was come
to Camelot he was sworn upon a book to tell the truth of his
quest.</span>
