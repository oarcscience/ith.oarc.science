BELLERIAN

SAMUEL BUTLER of Shrewsbury, in his "Ancient and Modern Geography," p. 112, says, "Ocrinum was the Lizard, and Bolerium the Land's End." Ainsworth, Latin Dictionary, 4th edition, has "Balerium --Burien in Cornwall." It is really in the parish of Sennen.
