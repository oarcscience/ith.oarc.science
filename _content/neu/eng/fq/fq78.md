## The Mutabilitie Cantos

TWO CANTOS  
OF  
*MVTABILITIE:*

Which, both for Forme and Matter, appeare to be  
parcell of some following Booke of the  
*FAERIE QUEENE,*

('.')

VNDER THE LEGEND

OF

*Constancie.*

Neuer before imprinted.
