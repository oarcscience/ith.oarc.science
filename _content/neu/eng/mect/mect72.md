#### The Shorter Poems

### Womanly Noblesse

<span id="an_1"></span> So hath myn herte caught in remembraunce  
<span id="an_2"></span> Your beaute hoole and stidefast governaunce,  
<span id="an_3"></span> Your vertues al and yowre hie noblesse,  
<span id="an_4"></span> That you to serve is set al my plesaunce.  
<span id="an_5"></span> So wel me liketh your womanly contenaunce,  
<span id="an_6"></span> Your fresshe fetures and your comlynesse,  
<span id="an_7"></span> That whiles I live myn hert to his maystresse  
<span id="an_8"></span> You hath ful chose in trewe perseveraunce  
<span id="an_9"></span> Never to chaunge, for no maner distresse.  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_10">10</span></span> And sith I shal
do \[you\] this observaunce,  
<span id="an_11"></span> Al my lif withouten displesaunce  
<span id="an_12"></span> You for to serve with al my besynesse,  
<span id="an_13"></span> And have me somwhat in your souvenaunce.  
<span id="an_14"></span> My woful herte suffreth greet duresse,  
<span id="an_15"></span> And \[loke\] how humbly with al symplesse  
<span id="an_16"></span> My wil I conforme to your ordynaunce,  
<span id="an_17"></span> As you best list, my peynes for to redresse.  
<span id="an_18"></span> Considryng eke how I hange in balaunce  
<span id="an_19"></span> In your service, such, lo, is my chaunce,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_20">20</span></span> Abidyng grace,
whan that your gentilnesse  
<span id="an_21"></span> Of my grete wo liste do alleggeaunce,  
<span id="an_22"></span> And with your pite me som wise avaunce  
<span id="an_23"></span> In ful rebatyng of myn hevynesse;  
<span id="an_24"></span> And thynketh by resoun that wommanly noblesse  
<span id="an_25"></span> Shuld nat desire for to do the outrance  
<span id="an_26"></span> Ther as she fyndeth non unbuxumnesse.  
<span id="an_27"></span> Auctour of norture, lady of plesaunce,  
<span id="an_28"></span> Soveraigne of beautee, floure of wommanhede,  
<span id="an_29"></span> Take ye non hede unto myn ignoraunce,  
<span class="margnote"><span id="an_30">30</span></span> But this
receyveth of your goodlihede,  
<span id="an_31"></span> Thynkyng that I have caught in remembraunce,  
<span id="an_32"></span> Your beaute hole, your stidefast governaunce.
