Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 32

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Fiat misericordia tua, domine, super nos, quemadmodum sperauimus in te.
Wese þin mildheortnys,         mihtig drihten,
wel ofer us,         swa we wenað on ðe.
