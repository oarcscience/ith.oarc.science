# Bede's Death Song: West Saxon Version

Verse Early Saxon

  
For þam nedfere      næni wyrþeþ  
þances snotera,      þonne him þearf sy  
to gehicgenne      ær his heonengange  
hwæt his gaste      godes oþþe yfeles  

5  
æfter deaþe heonon      demed weorþe.
