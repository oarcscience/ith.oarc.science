Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 79

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Domine deus uirtutum, conuerte nos et ostende faciem tuam, et salui erimus.
Gehweorf us, mægna god,         and us milde æteow
þinne andwlitan;         ealle we beoð hale.
