# Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 53

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

  
*Deus, in momine tuo saluum me fac, et in uirtute tua libera me.*  
On þinum þam halgan naman,         gedo me halne, god;  
alys me fram laðum         þurh þin leofe mægen.
