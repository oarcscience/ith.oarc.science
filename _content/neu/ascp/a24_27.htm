Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 27

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Saluum fac populum tuum, domine, et benedic hereditati tuæ, et rege eos et extolle illos usque in aeternum.
Hal do þin folc,         halig drihten,
and ðin yrfe eac         eal gebletsa;
rece þu heo swylce         and on riht ahefe,
þæt hi on worulde         wynnum lifigen.
