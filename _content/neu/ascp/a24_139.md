# Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 139

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

  
*Eripe me, domine, ab homine malo; a uiro iniquo libera me.*  
Genere me wið niþe         on naman þinum,  
fram yfelum men,         ece drihten.
