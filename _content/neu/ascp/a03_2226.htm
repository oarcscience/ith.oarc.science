Riddle 26

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Mec feonda sum      feore besnyþede,
woruldstrenga binom,      wætte siþþan,

hy beoð þy gesundran      ond þy sigefæstran,

tilra ond getreowra,      þa hyra tyr ond ead

25
