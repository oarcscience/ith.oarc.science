<td data-valign="top"><p>MHICHEIL mhil nan steud geala,<br />
Choisinn cios air Dragon fala,<br />
Ghaol Dia ’s pian Mhic Muire,<br />
Sgaoil do sgiath oirnn, dion sinn uile,<br />
     Sgaoil do sgiath oirnn, dion sinn uile.</p>
<p>Mhoire ghradhach! Mhathair Uain ghil,<br />
Cobhair oirnne ghlan Oigh na h-uaisleachd,<br />
Bhride bhuaidheach, bhuachaille nan treud,<br />
Cum ar cuallach, cuartaich sinn le cheil,<br />
     Cum ar cuallach, cuartaich sinn le cheil.</p>
<p>A Chaluim-chille, chairdeil, chaoimh,<br />
An ainm Athar, is Mic, is Spiorad Naoimh,<br />
Trid na Trithinn, trid na Triaid<br />
Comaraig sinn fein, gleidh ar triall,<br />
     Comaraig sinn fein, gleidh ar triall.</p>
<p>Athair! a Mhic! a Spioraid Naoimh!<br />
Biodh an Trithinn leinn a la ’s a dh’ oidhche,<br />
’S air machair loim no air roinn nam beann<br />
Bidh an Trithinn leinn ’s bidh a lamh mu ’r ceann,<br />
     Bidh an Trithinn leinn ’s bidh a lamh mu ’r ceann!</p>
<p>IASGAIREAN BHARRAIDH--<br />
Athair! a Mhic! a Spioraid Naoimh!<br />
Bi-sa, Thrithinn, leinn a la ’s a dh’ oidhche,<br />
’S air chul nan tonn no air thaobh nam beann<br />
Bidh ar Mathair leinn ’s bidh a lamh fo ’r ceann,<br />
’S air chul nan tonn no air thaobh nam beann<br />
     Bidh ar Mathair leinn ’s bidh a lamh fo ’r ceann!</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>VALIANT Michael of the white steeds,<br />
Who subdued the Dragon of blood,<br />
For love of God, for pains of Mary's Son,<br />
Spread thy wing over us, shield us all,<br />
     Spread thy wing over us, shield us all.</p>
<p>Mary beloved! Mother of the White Lamb,<br />
Shield, oh shield us, pure Virgin of nobleness,<br />
And Bride the beauteous, shepherdess of the flocks.<br />
Safeguard thou our cattle, surround us together,<br />
     Safeguard thou our cattle, surround us together.</p>
<p>And Columba, beneficent, benign,<br />
In name of Father, and of Son, and of Spirit Holy,<br />
Through the Three-in-One, through the Trinity,<br />
Encompass thou ourselves, shield our procession,<br />
     Encompass thou ourselves, shield our procession.</p>
<p>O Father! O Son! O Spirit Holy!<br />
Be the Triune with us day and night,<br />
On the machair plain or on the mountain ridge<br />
Be the Triune with us and His arm around our head,<br />
     Be the Triune with us and His arm around our head.</p>
<p>BARRA FISHERMEN--<br />
O Father! O Son! O Spirit Holy!<br />
Be thou, Three-One, with us day and night,<br />
And on the back of the wave as on the mountain side<br />
Our Mother shall be with us with her arm under our head.<br />
And on the back of the wave as on the mountain side<br />
     Our Mother shall be with us with her arm under our head.</p></td>
