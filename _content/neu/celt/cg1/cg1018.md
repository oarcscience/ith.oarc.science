<span class="small">THE old man from whom this piece was taken down said
that in his boyhood innumerable hymns and fragments of hymns of this
nature were common throughout the isles of <span id="page_43">p.
43</span> Barra. When strangers began to come in they derided the old
people and their old lore and their old ways, and the younger
generations neglected the ways of their fathers, alike the questionably
and the unquestionably good.</span>
