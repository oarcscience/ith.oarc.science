<td data-valign="top"><p><span id="img_02200"></span> <img src="img/02200.jpg" alt="B" /></p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>BUIDHEACHAS duit, Athair Naomha na Gloir,<br />
Athair chaomha bhith-bheo, bhith-threin,<br />
Thaobh gach foghair, gach fabhair, gach foir,<br />
Tha thu bairigeadh oirnne ’n ar feum;<br />
Ge b’e freasdal thig oirnn mar do chlann,<br />
’N ar cuibhrionn, ’n ar crann, ’n ar ceum,<br />
Tabhair ’na chuideachd dhuinn soirbhis do laimh<br />
Agus suilbhireachd saibhir do bheuil.</p>
<p>Ta sinn ciontach is truaillidh, a Dhe,<br />
Ann an Spiorad, an cre, is an corp,<br />
Ann an smuain, am focal, am beus,<br />
Tha sinn cruaidh ’na do leirsinn ’s an olc.<br />
Cuir-sa tabhachd do ghraidh dhuinn an ceill.<br />
Bi leum thairis thar sleibhtean ar lochd,<br />
Is nigh sinn am fior-fhuil na reit<br />
Mar chanach an t-sleibh, mar leuig an loch.</p>
<p>An slighe chorraich choitchinn ar gairm,<br />
Biodh i soirbh no doirbh do ar feoil,<br />
Biodh i soilleir no doilleir ri seirm,<br />
Do threorachadh foirfe biodh oirnn.<br />
Bi ’n ad sgeith dhuinn bho chuilbh an fhir-cheilg,<br />
Bho’n chreach-cheilgneach ta le dheilg air ar toir,<br />
Is anns gach run gheobh ar curam r’a dheilbh,<br />
Bi-sa fein air ar failm is aig ar sgod.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THANKS be to Thee, Holy Father of Glory,<br />
Father kind, ever-loving, ever-powerful,<br />
Because of all the abundance, favour, and deliverance<br />
That Thou bestowest upon us in our need.<br />
Whatever providence befalls us as thy children,<br />
In our portion, in our lot, in our path,<br />
Give to us with it the rich gifts of Thine hand<br />
And the joyous blessing of Thy mouth.</p>
<p>We are guilty and polluted, O God,<br />
In spirit, in heart, and in flesh,<br />
In thought, in word, in act,<br />
We are hard in Thy sight in sin.<br />
Put Thou forth to us the power of Thy love,<br />
Be thou leaping over the mountains of our transgressions,<br />
And wash us in the true blood of conciliation,<br />
Like the down of the mountain, like the lily of the lake.</p>
<p>In the steep common path of our calling,<br />
Be it easy or uneasy to our flesh,<br />
Be it bright or dark for us to follow,<br />
Thine own perfect guidance be upon us.<br />
Be Thou a shield to us from the wiles of the deceiver,<br />
From the arch-destroyer with his arrows pursuing us,<br />
And in each secret thought our minds get to weave,<br />
Be Thou Thyself on our helm and at our sheet.</p></td>

<td data-valign="top"><p>Ged bhiodh madruich is gadruich gar sgaradh bho’n chro,<br />
Biodh Aoghar crodha na gloir air ar sgath.<br />
Ge be cuis no cion-fath no cion-sgeoil<br />
Bhios gu leireadh no leoin thoir ’n ar dail,<br />
No bheir fianuis ’n ar n-aghaidh fa-dheoidh,<br />
Taobh thall abhuinn mhor an dubh-sgail,<br />
O duabharaich thusa sin oirnn,<br />
Is as ar cridhe dean fhogradh gu brath.</p>
<p>Nis dh’ an Athair a chruthaich gach creubh,<br />
Nis dh’ an Mhac a phaigh eirig a shloigh,<br />
Nis dh’ an Spiorad an Comhfhurtair treun:--<br />
Bi d’ ar dion is d’ ar seun bho gach leon,<br />
Bi mu thus is mu dheireadh ar reis,<br />
Bi toir dhuinn a bhi seinn ann an gloir,<br />
Ann an sith, ann am fois, ann an reit,<br />
Far nach silear an deur, far nach eugar ni ’s mo.<br />
    Far nach silear an deur, far nach eugar ni ’s mo.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>Though dogs and thieves would reive us from the fold,<br />
Be Thou the valiant Shepherd of glory near us.<br />
Whatever matter or cause or propensity,<br />
That would bring to us grief, or pains, or wounds,<br />
Or that would bear witness against us at the last,<br />
On the other side of the great river of dark shadows,<br />
Oh! do Thou obscure it from our eyes,<br />
And from our hearts drive it for ever.</p>
<p>Now to the Father who created each creature,<br />
Now to the Son who paid ransom for His people,<br />
Now to the Holy Spirit, Comforter of might:--<br />
Shield and sain us from every wound;<br />
Be about the beginning and end of our race,<br />
Be giving us to sing in glory,<br />
In peace, in rest, in reconciliation,<br />
Where no tear shall be shed, where death comes no more.<br />
Where no tear shall be shed, where death comes no more.</p></td>
