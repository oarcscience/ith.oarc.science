<td data-valign="top"><p>TA mise laighe nochd<br />
Le Athair, le Mac,<br />
Le Spiorad na firinn,<br />
Ta ’m dhion o gach lochd.</p>
<p>Cha laigh mi le olc,<br />
Cha laigh olc liom,<br />
Ach laighidh mi le Dia,<br />
Is laighidh Dia liom.</p>
<p>Dia agus Criosd agus Spiorad naomh,<br />
Is crois nan naodh aingeal fionn,<br />
Da m’ dhion mar Thri is mar Aon,<br />
Bho chlar mhullach m’aodainn gu faobhar mo bhonn.</p>
<p>A Righ na greine agus na gloire,<br />
Ios a Mhic na h-Oighe cubhra,<br />
Gleidh-sa sinn a glinn nan diar,<br />
Is a taigh nan diamha dubhra,<br />
     Gleidh sinn a glinn nan diar,<br />
     Is a taigh nan diamha dubhra.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I AM lying down to-night,<br />
With Father, with Son,<br />
With the Spirit of Truth,<br />
Who shield me from harm.</p>
<p>I will not lie with evil,<br />
Nor shall evil lie with me,<br />
But I will lie down with God,<br />
And God will lie down with me.</p>
<p>God and Christ and Spirit Holy,<br />
And the cross of the nine white angels,<br />
Be protecting me as Three and as One,<br />
From the top tablet of my face to the soles of my feet.</p>
<p>Thou King of the sun and of glory,<br />
Thou Jesu, Son of the Virgin fragrant,<br />
Keep Thou us from the glen of tears,<br />
And from the house of grief and gloom,<br />
     Keep us from the glen of tears,<br />
     From the house of grief and gloom.</p></td>
