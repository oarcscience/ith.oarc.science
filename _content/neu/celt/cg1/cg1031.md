<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>O JESU! to-night,<br />
Thou Shepherd of the poor,<br />
Thou sinless person<br />
Who didst suffer full sore,<br />
By ban of the wicked,<br />
And wast crucified.</p>
<p>Save me from evil,<br />
Save me from harm,<br />
Save Thou my body,<br />
Sanctify me to-night,<br />
O Jesu! to-night,<br />
Nor leave me.</p>
<p>Endow me with strength,<br />
Thou Herdsman of might.<br />
Guide me aright,<br />
Guide me in Thy strength,<br />
O Jesu! in Thy strength<br />
Preserve me.</p></td>
