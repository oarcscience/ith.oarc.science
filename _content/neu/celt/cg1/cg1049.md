<td data-valign="top"><p>CO dha dhiolas mi cios<br />
An ainm Mhicheil o’n aird?<br />
Thugam deachamh dhe m’ ni,<br />
Dh’ an Diobarach Aigh.</p>
<p>Air sgath na chunna mi,<br />
Do shith is d’a bhaigh,<br />
Tog m’ anam riut, a Mhic De,<br />
Na treig mi gu brath.</p>
<p>Cuimhnich orm anns an t-sliabh,<br />
Fo do sgiath dean-sa mo sgail;<br />
Charra na firinn na dibir mi’n cian<br />
B’e mo mhiann bhi gu siorruidh na d’ dhail.</p>
<p>Tabhair domh trusgan bainnse,<br />
Biodh ainghlean a cainnt rium ’s gach cas,<br />
Biodh ostail naomha da m’ dhion,<br />
Moire mhin is thus, Iosa nan gras,<br />
     Moire mhin is thus, Iosa nan gras.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>To whom shall I offer oblation<br />
In name of Michael on high?<br />
I will give tithe of my means<br />
To the forsaken illustrious One.</p>
<p>Because of all that I have seen,<br />
Of His peace and of His mercy,<br />
Lift Thou my soul to Thee, O Son of God,<br />
Nor leave me ever.</p>
<p>Remember me in the mountain,<br />
Under Thy wing shield Thou me;<br />
Rock of truth, do not forsake me,<br />
My wish it were ever to be near Thee.</p>
<p>Give to me the wedding garment,<br />
Be angels conversing with me in every need,<br />
Be the holy apostles protecting me,<br />
The fair Mary and Thou, Jesu of grace,<br />
     The fair Mary and Thou, Jesu of grace.</p></td>
