<td data-valign="top"><p>CUIRIDH mi an Di seo romham,<br />
Mar a dh’ orduich Righ an domhan,<br />
Bride ’g an gleidheadh, ’g an coimhead, ’s ’g an comhnadh,<br />
Air bheann, air ghleann, air chomhnard,<br />
     Bride ’g an gleidheadh, ’g an coimhead, ’s ’g an comhnadh,<br />
     Air bheann, air ghleann, air chomhnard.</p>
<p>Eirich, a Bhride mhin-gheal,<br />
Glac do lion, do chir, agus t’ fholt,<br />
Bho rinn thu daibh eolas amhra,<br />
’G an cumail bho chall is bho lochd,<br />
     Bho rinn thu daibh colas amhra,<br />
     ’G an cumail bho chall is bho lochd.</p>
<p>Bho chreag, bho chathan, bho allt,<br />
Bho chadha cam, bho mhille sluic,<br />
Bho shaighde reang nam ban seanga sith,<br />
Bho chridhe mhi-ruin, bho shuil an uilc,<br />
     Bho shaighde reang nam ban seanga sith,<br />
     Bho chridhe mhi-ruin, bho shuil an uilc.</p>
<p>Mhoire Mhathair, cuallaich an t-al gu leir,<br />
Bhride nam basa mine, dion domh mo spreidh,<br />
Chaluim chaoimh, a naoimh nan ioma buadh,<br />
Comraig dhomh crodh an ail, bairig dhomh buar,<br />
     Chaluim chaoimh, a naoimh nan ioma buadh,<br />
     Comraig dhomh crodh an ail, bairig dhomh buar.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL place this flock before me,<br />
As was ordained of the King of the world,<br />
Bride to keep them, to watch them, to tend them.<br />
On ben, on glen, on plain,<br />
     Bride to keep them, to watch them, to tend them,<br />
     On ben, on glen, on plain.</p>
<p>Arise, thou Bride the gentle, the fair,<br />
Take thou thy lint, thy comb, and thy hair,<br />
Since thou to them madest the noble charm,<br />
To keep them from straying, to save them from harm,<br />
     Since thou to them madest the noble charm,<br />
     To keep them from straying, to save them from harm.</p>
<p>From rocks, from drifts, from streams,<br />
From crooked passes, from destructive pits,<br />
From the straight arrows of the slender ban-shee,<br />
From the heart of envy, from the eye of evil,<br />
     From the straight arrows of the slender ban-shee,<br />
     From the heart of envy, from the eye of evil.</p>
<p>Mary Mother, tend thou the offspring all,<br />
Bride of the fair palms, guard thou my flocks,<br />
Kindly Columba, thou saint of many powers,<br />
Encompass thou the breeding cows, bestow on me herds,<br />
     Kindly Columba, thou saint of many powers,<br />
     Encompass thou the breeding cows, bestow on me herds.</p></td>
