<td data-valign="top"><p>LAIGHIM sios an nochd mar is coir<br />
An cluanas Chriosda Mac Oigh nan cleachd,<br />
An cluanas Athair aigh na gloir,<br />
An cluanas Spioraid foir nam feart.</p>
<p>Laighim sios an nochd le Dia,<br />
Is laighidh Dia an nochd a sios liom,<br />
Cha laigh mi sios an nochd le olc, ’s cha dean<br />
Ole no fhiamh laighe liom.</p>
<p>Laighim sios an nochd le Spiorad Naomh,<br />
Is laighidh Spiorad Naomh an nochd a sios liom,<br />
Laighim sios le Teoiridh mo chaoimh,<br />
Is laighidh Teoiridh mo chaoimh a sios liom.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I AM lying down to-night as beseems<br />
In the fellowship of Christ, son of the Virgin of ringlets.<br />
In the fellowship of the gracious Father of glory,<br />
In the fellowship of the Spirit of powerful aid.</p>
<p>I am lying down to-night with God,<br />
And God to-night will lie down with me,<br />
I will not lie down to-night with sin, nor shall<br />
Sin nor sin's shadow lie down with me.</p>
<p>I am lying down to-night with the Holy Spirit,<br />
And the Holy Spirit this night will lie down with me,<br />
I will lie down this night with the Three of my love,<br />
And the Three of my love will lie down with me.</p></td>
