<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL pluck the gracious 'mothan,'<br />
Plant most precious in the field,<br />
That mine be the holiness of the seven priests,<br />
And the eloquence that is within them.</p>
<p>  *       *       *       *<br />
  *       *       *       *<br />

While the 'mothan' is mine.</p></td>
