<td data-valign="top"><p><span id="img_02800"></span> <img src="img/02800.jpg" alt="C" /></p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>CUIRIM an seun air do chom,<br />
Agus air do shealbhachd,<br />
Seun Dhe nan dul<br />
Chum do thearmaid.</p>
<p>An seun a chuir Bride nan ni<br />
Mu mhuineal min Dhornghil,<br />
An seun a chuir Moire mu Mac,<br />
Eadar bonn agus broghaid,<br />
Eadar cioch agus glun,<br />
Eadar cul agus broth,<br />
Eadar braigh agus bonn,<br />
Eadar suil agus folt.</p>
<p>Cliar Mhicheil air do thaobh,<br />
Sgiath Mhicheil air do shlinnean,<br />
Ni bheil eadar neamh is lar<br />
Na bheir buaidh air Righ nan gras.</p>
<p>Cha reub lainn thu,<br />
Cha mhill muir thu,<br />
Cha teum mnaoi thu,<br />
Cha treann duin thu.</p>
<p>Brat Chriosda fein umad,<br />
Sgath Chriosda fein tharad,<br />
Bho mhullach do chinn<br />
Gu buinn do chas.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I PLACE the charm on thy body,<br />
And on thy prosperity,<br />
The charm of the God of life<br />
For thy protection.</p>
<p>The charm that Bride of the kine<br />
Put round the fair neck of Dornghil,<br />
The charm that Mary put about her Son,<br />
Between sole and throat,<br />
Between pap and knee,<br />
Between back and breast,<br />
Between chest and sole,<br />
Between eye and hair.</p>
<p>The host of Michael on thy side,<br />
The shield of Michael on thy shoulder,<br />
There is not between heaven and earth<br />
That can overcome the King of grace.</p>
<p>No spear shall rive thee,<br />
No sea shall drown thee,<br />
No woman shall wile thee,<br />
No man shall wound thee.</p>
<p>The mantle of Christ Himself about thee,<br />
The shadow of Christ Himself above thee,<br />
From the crown of thy head<br />
To the soles of thy feet.</p></td>

<td data-valign="top"><p>Ta seun De ort a nis,<br />
Cha teid gu brath ort ailis.</p>
<p>Theid thu mach an ainm do Righ,<br />
Thig thu steach an ainm do Phriomh,<br />
Is le Dia nan dul thu nis gu h-uilidh,<br />
Agus leis na Cumhachdan comhla.</p>
<p>Cuirim an seun seo moch Di-luain,<br />
An ceum cruaidh, druiseach, droigheach,<br />
Falbh a mach ’s an seun mu d’ chom,<br />
Is na biodh bonn eagail ort.</p>
<p>Diridh tu cirein nan stuc,<br />
Dionar tu a thaobh do chuil,<br />
Is tu an eala chiuin ’s a bhlar,<br />
Cumhnar tu am measg nan ar,<br />
Seasaidh tu troimh choig ceud,<br />
Is bidh t’eircirich an sas.</p>
<p>     Seun De umad!<br />
     Feun De tharad!</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>The charm of God is on thee now,<br />
Thou shalt never know disgrace.</p>
<p>Thou shalt go forth in name of thy King,<br />
Thou shalt come in in name of thy Chief,<br />
To the God of life thou now belongest wholly,<br />
And to all the Powers together.</p>
<p>I place this charm early on Monday,<br />
In passage hard, brambly, thorny,<br />
Go thou out and the charm about thy body,<br />
And be not the least fear upon thee.</p>
<p>Thou shalt ascend the crest of the hill,<br />
Protected thou shalt be behind thee,<br />
Thou art the calm swan in battle,<br />
Preserved thou shalt be amidst the slaughter,<br />
Stand thou canst against five hundred,<br />
And thine oppressors shall be seized.</p>
<p>     The charm of God about thee!<br />
     The arm of God above thee!</p></td>
