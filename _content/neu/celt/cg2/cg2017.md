<td data-valign="top"><p>CUIRIM sian a bheatha bhuan,<br />
Mu ’r crodh luath, leathann, lan,<br />
An creagan air an laigh an spreidh,<br />
     Gun eirich iad beo slan.</p>
<p>A nuas le buaidh ’s le beannachd,<br />
A suas le luaths ’s le leannachd,<br />
Gun ghnu, gun tnu, gun fharmad,<br />
Gun suil bhig, gun suil mhoir,<br />
     Gun suil choig an dearmaid.</p>
<p>Sughaidh mise seo, sughadh feith farmaid<br />
Air ceannard an tighe ’s air teaghlaich a bhaile,<br />
Gun eirich gach droch-bhuil, ’s gach droch-bhuaidh<br />
     Bu dhualta dhuibh-se dhaibh-san.</p>
<p>          Ma mhallaich teanga duibh,<br />
          Bheannaich cridhe duibh;<br />
          Ma ghonaich suil duibh,<br />
          Shonaich run duibh.</p>
<p>Tionndanam is teanndanam,<br />
Culionn cruaidh is creanndagaich<br />
Air an caoire boirionn ’s air an laoighe firionn,<br />
     Fad nan naodh ’s nan naodh fichead bliadhna.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL place the charm of the lasting life,<br />
Upon your cattle active, broad, and full,<br />
The knoll upon which the herds shall lie down,<br />
     That they may rise from it whole and well.</p>
<p>Down with success, and with blessing,<br />
Up with activity and following,<br />
Without envy, without malice, without ill-will,<br />
Without small eye, without large eye,<br />
     Without the five eyes of neglect.</p>
<p>I will suck this, the sucking of envious vein<br />
On the head of the house, and the townland families,<br />
That every evil trait, and every evil tendency<br />
     Inherent in you shall cleave to them.</p>
<p>          If tongue cursed you,<br />
          A heart blessed you;<br />
          If eye blighted you,<br />
          A wish prospered you.</p>
<p>A hurly-burlying, a topsy-turvying,<br />
A hard hollying and a wan withering<br />
To their female sheep and to their male calves,<br />
     For the nine and the nine score years.</p></td>
