<td data-valign="top"><p>A RUADH ghaothar, atar, aogail,<br />
Fag an taobh agus an tac sin,<br />
Sin an carr ’s an lar,<br />
     Agus fag a chioch.</p>
<p>Seall, a Chriosd, a bhean<br />
Agus a cioch air at,<br />
Seall fein i, Mhuire,<br />
     ’S tu rug am Mac.</p>
<p>A ruadh ghaothar, aogar, iota,<br />
Fag a chioch agus am bac,<br />
Agus sin a mach,<br />
     Slan gu robh dh’ an chich,<br />
          Crion gu robh dh’ an at.</p>
<p>Teich a bhradag ruadh,<br />
Teich gu luath a bhradag,<br />
At a bha ’s a chich,<br />
     Fag a charr ’s a chioch,<br />
          Agus sin a mach.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THOU rose windy, swelling, deadly,<br />
Leave that part and spot,<br />
There is the udder in the ground,<br />
     And leave the breast.</p>
<p>See, Christ, the woman<br />
And her breast swollen,<br />
See her thyself, Mary,<br />
     It was thou didst bear the Son.</p>
<p>Thou rose windy, deadly, thirsty,<br />
Leave the breast and the spot,<br />
And take thyself off;<br />
     Healed be the breast,<br />
          Withered be the swelling.</p>
<p>Flee thieving red one,<br />
Flee quickly thieving one,<br />
Swelling that was in the breast,<br />
     Leave the udder and the breast,<br />
          And flee hence.</p></td>
