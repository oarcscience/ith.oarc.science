### THE FOUR QUARTERS OF THE YEAR.

1\. Winter. 2. Spring. 3. Summer. 4. Autumn.

Or thus:--

1\. Winter. 2. Spring. 3. Summer.--White grasses.

And the four points of the sun are thus:--

1\. The point of roughness. 2. The point of regeneration. 3. The point
of summer. 4. The point of reaping time. <span
id="fr_415"></span>[2](#fn_411)

Also vulgarly, thus:--

1\. Summer. 2. Winter. 3. Spring.
