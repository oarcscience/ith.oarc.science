### THE MATERIALS.

The five materials of every existence and life: calas; fluidity;
air; <span id="fr_349"></span>[2](#fn_347) sun; <span
id="fr_350"></span>[3](#fn_348) and nwyvre. <span
id="fr_351"></span>[4](#fn_349)

<span id="page_384">p. 384</span> <span id="page_385">p. 385</span>

They are thus variously arranged:--

Calas, fluidity, and nwyvre; <span id="fr_352"></span>[1](#fn_350) and
the uvel unites with the nwyvre, the air unites with the uvel, the
water, or fluidity, unites with the air, and the calas unites with
fluidity.
