# BARDDAS;

###### OR, A COLLECTION OF ORIGINAL DOCUMENTS, ILLUSTRATIVE OF THE THEOLOGY, WISDOM, AND USAGES OF

### The Bardo-Druidic System

###### OF THE ISLE OF BRITAIN.

###### BY

## THE REV. J. WILLIAMS AB ITHEL, M. A.,
