### GOD.

God is three things, <span id="fr_217"></span>[1](#fn_216) and cannot be
otherwise: coeval with all time; co-entire with all essence; and
co-local with all mental purpose. Could what is called God be otherwise,
it would not be God, since it could be surpassed, and no one is God that
can be surpassed. He is also co-sentient with all animation.
