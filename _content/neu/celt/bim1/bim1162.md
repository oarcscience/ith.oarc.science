### THE DIVISIONS OF THE YEAR.

Some of the ancient Bards divide the year as follows:--Harvest; Winter;
Spring; and the beginning of Summer. <span
id="fr_416"></span>[1](#fn_412)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Calangauaf garw hin,

The calends of winter, rough is the weather,  
Unlike to the *beginning of summer*.--Ll. Hen.
