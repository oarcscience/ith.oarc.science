### THE BIRDS OF RHIANON <span id="fr_129"></span>[1](bim1049.htm#fn_128)

The Birds of Rhianon sang until the Angels of heaven came to listen to
them; and it was from their songs that were first obtained vocal song
and instrumental music; vocal song being that which is sung by the lips
to melody and harp.

Evan Evans.
