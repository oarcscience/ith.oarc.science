### THE SEVEN MATERIALS OF MAN.

1\. Earth, from which is the body;

2\. Water, from which are the blood and humour;

3\. Sun, from which are the heat and light;

4\. Air, from which are the breath and motion;

5\. Nwyvre, from which are the feeling and affection;

6\. The Holy Ghost, from Whom are the reason and understanding;

7\. God, from Whom is life everlasting.

The Blue Bard of the Chair has said it.
