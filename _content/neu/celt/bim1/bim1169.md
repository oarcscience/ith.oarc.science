### THE DIVISIONS OF THE DAY.

The four parts of the day: morning, that is, the first six hours;
vapourlessness, the second six hours; noon, the third six hours; and the
fourth, evanescence, <span id="fr_421"></span>[1](#fn_417) reaching till
the morning.
