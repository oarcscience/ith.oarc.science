## Popular Tales

# of the West Highlands

### by J. F. Campbell

#### Volume I

#### \[1890\]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Contents](#contents)    [Start Reading](pt100)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is the first of four volumes of Campbell's collection of Scottish
folklore. This volume has many stories which have been anthologized
elsewhere, as well as the extensive introduction which not only serves
to introduce Scottish folklore, but as a prolegomena to the study of
oral folklore in general.

--John Bruno Hare, March 26th, 2004
