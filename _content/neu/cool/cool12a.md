12a. The Death Of Forgemen

And the bull's cowherd would not allow them to carry off the Brown Bull
of Cualnge, so that they urged on the bull, beating shafts on shields,
till they drove him into a narrow gap, and the herd trampled the
cowherd's body thirty feet into the ground, so that they made fragments
and shreds of his body. Forgemen was his name. This then is the Death of
Forgemen on the Cattle-prey of Cualnge.
