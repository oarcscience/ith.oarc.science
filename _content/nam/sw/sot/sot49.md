##### XLIV

##### WHEN THE CORN MOTHERS ARE FED

*The Bear Priest sings and throws meal on the altar*  
    Now and here  
    My gods I give you  
    This corn meal  
    And thank you.

*The People join and throw meal on the altar*  
    Now and here,  
    Our old women Gods,  
    Take this!  
    Long life give us  
    And our children!
