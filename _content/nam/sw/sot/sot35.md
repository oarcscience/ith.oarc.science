##### XXXI

##### RAIN MAGIC SONG

Ready we stand in San Juan town,  
Oh, our Corn Maidens and our Corn Youths!  
Oh, our Corn Mothers and our Corn Fathers!

Now we bring you misty water  
And throw it different ways,  
To the north, the west, the south, the east  
To heaven above and the drinking earth below!

Then likewise throw you misty water  
Toward San Juan!  
Oh, many that you are, pour water  
Over our Corn Maidens’ ears!  
On our Wheat Maidens  
Thence throw you misty water,  
All round about us here!

On Green Earth Woman's back  
Now thrives our flesh and breath,  
Now grows our strength of arm and leg,  
Now takes form our children's food!
