##### XII

##### RAGS ARE ROYAL RAIMENT . . . . .

"That dirty one, that ragged one."  
Thus do they deride us two.

But you, you have good fortune;  
Yellow clothes, pink clothes, blue clothes.  
These are the only kinds you wear!

But you not mind to be a mule!
