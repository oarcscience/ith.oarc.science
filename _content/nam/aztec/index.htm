This index lists resources at Intangible Textual Heritage relating to Aztec religion, folklore and spiritual practices.

Rig Veda Americanus (Aztec Hymns)   by Daniel G. Brinton, Brinton's Library of Aboriginal Literature number VIII. Philadelphia, [1890] This relatively short document is one of the few public domain translations of an Aztec religous manuscript.
