## 77.--THE COYOTE AND THE KÓKONTU-MAIDENS.

Coyote sees Kókontu grinding corn and singing. They talk with him and
then come out and go to steep bluff. Here they play, running up and
jumping down large tree and bluff. Coyote joins them and climbs tree. He
jumps down and lands forcibly on ground, killing himself. Kókontu laugh
at him and go home leaving him dead.
