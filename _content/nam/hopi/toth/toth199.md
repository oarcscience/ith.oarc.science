## 86.--A BET BETWEEN THE CÓOYOKO AND THE FOX.

Fox comes along as Cóoyoko uncle is sitting on bluff. He is waiting to
watch sun rise. He tells Fox to come and sit near him. Says they will
have contest to see on whose song sun will rise, looser to be killed
with knife. Cóoyoko sings song first. Fox follows. Cóoyoko sings again
and sun is just about to loom up. Fox repeats his song and while singing
sun looms up. Fox wins contest and he cuts Cóoyoko's throat with knife.
