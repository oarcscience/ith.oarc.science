MIK UM WESS,
THE INDIAN PUCK, OR ROBIN GOODFELLOW.
From a scraping on birch bark by Tomah Josephs, Indian Governor at Peter Dana's Point, Maine. The Mik um wess always wears a red cap like the Norse Goblin.
