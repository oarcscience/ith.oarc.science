### SONGS FROM THE GREAT PLAINS

#### HUNTING SONGS

**DAKOTA**

**I**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice;  
Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice;  
The northern buffalo I've killed, and I lift up my voice;  
Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice.

</div>

**II**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The saddle bind:  
Children dear.  
For half a day,  
I will kill.

</div>
