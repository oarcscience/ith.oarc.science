#### THE HEART'S FRIEND

**SHOSHONE LOVE SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Fair is the white star of twilight,  
And the sky clearer

</div>

<span id="page_189">p. 189</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

At the day's end;  
But she is fairer, and she is dearer  
She, my heart's friend!

Fair is the white star of twilight,  
And the moon roving  
To the sky's end;  
But she is fairer, better worth loving,  
She, my heart's friend.

</div>
