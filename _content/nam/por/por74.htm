BUFFALO DANCE

    Strike ye our land
With curved horns!
Now with cries
Bending our bodies,

p. 201

Breathe fire upon us;
Now with feet
Trampling the earth,
Let your hoofs
Thunder over us!
Strike ye our land
With curved horns!
