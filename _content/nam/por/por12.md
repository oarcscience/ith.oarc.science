### SONGS OF THE CHIPPEWA

**HEALING SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

They are in close consultation  
    with their heads together  
    Wenabojo  
    and his grandmother.

</div>

**LOVE-CHARM**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

What are you saying to me?  
I am arrayed like the roses  
    and beautiful as they.

</div>

<span id="page_14">p. 14</span>

**I AM WALKING**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Toward calm and shady places  
    I am walking  
    on the earth.

</div>

**THEY THINK ME UNWORTHY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

They think me unworthy  
    my Midé brethren  
    but look and see  
    the length of my wigwam.

</div>

**THE SOUND IS FADING AWAY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The sound is fading away  
It is of five sounds  
    freedom  
The sound is fading away  
It is of five sounds.

</div>

**DOCTOR'S SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I am singing and dreaming in my poor way  
    over the earth  
    I who will again disembark  
    upon earth.

</div>

**SONG OF THE TREES**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The wind  
    only  
    I am afraid of.

</div>

<span id="page_15">p. 15</span>

**SONG OF THE THUNDERS**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Sometimes  
    I go about pitying  
    myself  
    while I am carried by the wind  
    across the sky.

</div>

**THE APPROACH OF THE STORM**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

From the half  
    of the sky  
    that which lives there  
    is coming, and makes a noise.

</div>

**I AM AFRAID OF THE OWL**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Very much  
    also  
    I  
    of the owl  
    am afraid  
    whenever I am sitting alone  
        in the wigwam.

</div>

**I AM AS BRAVE AS OTHER MEN**

Men who are brave and heroic  
    as you esteem them to be  
Like them  
    I also  
    consider myself to be.

<span id="page_16">p. 16</span>

**MY MUSIC REACHES TO THE SKY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

My music  
         reaches  
        to the sky.

</div>

**FAREWELL TO THE WARRIORS**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Come  
    it is time for you to depart  
We are going on a long journey.

</div>

**SONG OF THE MAN WHO WAS TO BE HUNG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The thunders will take me home,  
    whenever I mind to go home,  
    my friends,  
    and the wind  
    it will take me home, too.

</div>

**FRIENDLY SONG**

**I**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Over there  
    in the sky  
    they have taken pity on me.

</div>

**II**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The sky  
    loves to hear me.

</div>

**GAMING SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I will go home  
    if I am beaten  
    after more articles  
    to wager.

</div>

<span id="page_17">p. 17</span>

**WAR SONGS**

At Co’goben's  
    village  
    they are weeping  
    the men

At Co’goben's  
    village  
    they are wailing  
    the women.

I feel no fear  
    when the Great River man  
    death  
    speaks of.

**THE MAN WHO STAYED HOME**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Although  
    Jinwábe  
    considers himself  
    a man  
    his wife  
    certainly  
    takes all his attention.

</div>

**ARROW SONG**

Scarlet  
    is its head

**SONG OF THE BUFFALO**

Strike ye  
    our land  
    with curved horns

<span id="page_18">p. 18</span>

**SONG OF THE MINISINOWUCK**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

It is uncertain what will happen  
    to the one from whom I fly  
It is uncertain what will happen  
    to the one from whom I rise.

</div>

**DRUM SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I make them dance  
    those brave men  
those brave men  
    every one of them

</div>

**SONG OF WOMAN-OF-THE-RED-SKY, WHO WENT WITH HER HUSBAND, SINGING INTO
BATTLE**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

At that time  
    if I had been a man  
    truly  
    a man  
    I would have seized.

</div>

**DEATH SONG OF NAMEBINES**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The odor of death  
I discern the odor of death  
    in the front of my body

</div>

**THE SIOUX WOMEN GATHER UP THEIR WOUNDED**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The Sioux women  
    pass to and fro wailing  
    as they gather up  
    their wounded men  
The voice of their weeping comes back  
    to us.

</div>

<span id="page_19">p. 19</span>

**THEY ARE PLAYING A GAME**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The noise of passing feet  
    on the prairie  
They are playing a game as they come  
    those men.

</div>

**SCALP SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I wonder  
    if she is humiliated  
    the Sioux woman  
    that I cut off her head.

</div>

**SONG OF REJOICING**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

It shall be  
    that I rejoice  
O, my son  
    your elder brother  
    you have brought back  
O, my son  
    it shall be  
    that I rejoice  
O, my son.

</div>

**DEATH SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Is there anyone who  
    would weep for me?  
My wife  
    would weep for me.

</div>

SONG OF THE BUTTERFLY

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

In the coming heat  
    of the day  
    I stood there.

</div>

<span id="page_20">p. 20</span>

**MAPLE SUGAR**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Maple sugar  
    is the only thing  
    that satisfies me.

</div>

**A SONG OF SPRING**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

As my eyes  
    search  
    the prairie  
    I feel the summer in the spring.

</div>

**CARRIED AROUND THE SKY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

As the wind is carrying me  
    around the sky.

</div>

**THE SKY WILL RESOUND**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

It will resound finely  
    the sky  
    when I come making a noise.

</div>

**AN OVERHANGING CLOUD**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

An overhanging  
    cloud  
    repeats my words with pleasing sound.

</div>

**HEAPS OF CLOUDS**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Great heaps  
    of clouds  
    in the direction I am looking.

</div>

<span id="page_21">p. 21</span>

**THE NOISE OF THE VILLAGE**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Whenever I pause  
    the noise  
    of the village.

</div>

**MIDÉ BURIAL SONG**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Neniwá  
    let us stand  
    and you shall see  
    my body  
    as I desire.

</div>

#### LOVE SONGS

**MY LOVE HAS DEPARTED**

**I**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

A loon  
    I thought it was  
    but it was  
    my love's  
    splashing oar.

</div>

**II**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

To Sault Ste. Marie  
    he has departed  
My love has gone on before me,  
Never again  
    can I see him.

</div>

**THE GENEROUS ONE**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Why should  
    I, even I

</div>

<span id="page_22">p. 22</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

be jealous  
because of that bad boy?

</div>

**RECOVERY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Do not weep  
I am not going to die.

</div>

**LOVE-HURT**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Although he said it  
    still  
I am filled with longing  
when I think of him.

</div>

**GLAD-PARTING**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Come  
    I am going away  
I pray you  
    let me go  
I will soon return  
Do not  
    weep for me  
Behold  
    we will be very glad  
    to meet each other  
    when I return  
Do not  
    weep for me.

</div>

**INVITATION**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

My sweetheart  
    a long time  
    I have been waiting for you

</div>

<span id="page_23">p. 23</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

to come over  
    where I am.

</div>

**YOU DESIRE VAINLY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

You desire vainly  
    that I seek you  
    the reason is  
    I come  
    to see your younger sister.

</div>

**HE IS GONE**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I might grieve  
I am sad  
    that he has gone  
    my lover.

</div>

**SONG OF AN AMBITIOUS MOTHER**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I am asking for  
    Bugac's  
    daughter  
My big  
    brass kettle  
    he is giving.

</div>

**NONSENSE SONG OF THE GAME OF SILENCE**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

(If you speak or laugh you are defeated)  
It is hanging  
    in the edge of the sunshine  
It is a pig I see  
    with its double (cloven) hoofs  
It is a very fat pig.  
The people who live in a hollow tree  
    are fighting

</div>

<span id="page_24">p. 24</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

They are fighting bloodily  
He is rich  
He will carry a pack toward the great water.  
*(The rabbit speaks)*  
At the end of the point of land  
I eat the bark off the tree  
I see the track of a lynx  
I don't care, I can get away from him  
It is a jumping trail  
sep!

</div>

**WORK STEADILY**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Be very careful  
    to work steadily  
I am afraid they will take you away from me.

</div>

**I HAVE FOUND MY LOVER**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Oh  
    I am thinking  
Oh  
    I am thinking  
    I have found .  
    my lover  
Oh  
    I think it is so.

</div>

#### MIDÉ SONGS

**THESE REPRESENT THE EXPRESSION OF RELIGIOUS IDEAS**

**I**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Is it that  
    which my voice resembles?

</div>

<span id="page_25">p. 25</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Even metal  
    the sounding of my voice?

</div>

**II**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

From beneath the high hill  
    my voice echoes forth.

</div>

**III**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The strength of metal  
    has entered into my arrow point.  
A spirit  
    I could kill.  
The strength of iron  
    has entered into my arrow point.

</div>

**IV**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Beautiful as a star hanging in the sky  
    is our Midé lodge.

</div>

**V**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I have gained such spirit-power  
    that I can tame it in my hand  
It is true  
    even our white shell  
    I can tame it in my hand.

</div>

**VI**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Do not speak ill of the Midé  
    my Midé brethren.  
Wherever you may be  
    do not speak ill of a woman  
    my Midé brethren.

</div>

**VII**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

We may live by it always  
    my Midé brethren  
It is spiritual  
    the inspiration we receive.

</div>

<span id="page_26">p. 26</span>

**VIII**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

In the middle of the sea  
    in the lengthy room of the sea  
    there I am standing.

</div>

**IX**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

I that hasten around  
I shoot at a man and he falls in a trance  
Then I feel with my hand  
    to see if he is still alive.

</div>

**X**

My Midé brother  
    is searched  
In his heart is found  
    that which I seek to remove  
    a white shell.

**XI**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Into thy body  
    I shoot  
    the spirit

</div>

**XII**

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

What is this  
    I promise you?  
The skies shall be bright and clear for you  
This is what I promise you.

</div>
