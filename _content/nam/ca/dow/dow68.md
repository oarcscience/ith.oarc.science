### PIGMIES AND WATER PEOPLE

*The Hookooeko of Nicasio and Tomales Bay say*:

*Se'-kah* the Little Folk dwell in thick places in the dark redwood
forest, where no people live. They are very small. Sometimes they make
people crazy.

*Le'-wah ke'-lak* the Water People live in the ocean, in a roundhouse
under the water; sometimes they come up and show themselves.
