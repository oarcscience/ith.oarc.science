# Beliefs Concerning Natural Phenomena

### THUNDER

*The Northern Mewuk near South Fork Cosumnes River say*:

*Tim'-mel-le* the Thunder is, or is like, *Ti'-e-te* the Valley Bluejay.
He lives down below \[west\] in the San Joaquin Valley, where the clouds
are. Sometimes he becomes angry and makes a great rumbling noise; this
noise is *Tim'-mel-le* the Thunder. <span id="fr_26"></span>[28](#fn_26)
