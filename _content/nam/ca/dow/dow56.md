### A HOLE IN THE NOSE SAVES TURNING INTO A FISH

*The Southern Mewuk of the Mariposa region say*:

If a person dies without a hole in his nose he will turn into a fish,
but if the nose is perforated

<span id="page_219">p. 219</span>

for the *kun-no-wah* <span id="fr_23"></span>[25](#fn_23) he will not
turn into a fish.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_23"></span>[219:25](dow56.htm#fr_23) The *kun-no-wah* is a
short white rod of shell or stone worn in the nose by both men and women
of this tribe.
