### KI'-KI'-AH THE MOUNTAIN BLUEJAY

*The Middle Mewuk of Stanislaus River region say*:

*Ki'-ki'-ah*, the Crested Bluejay of the mountains, plants acorns so
that oak trees come up almost everywhere.

\[Several other tribes mention the same habit which, by the way, is
hardly a myth.\]
