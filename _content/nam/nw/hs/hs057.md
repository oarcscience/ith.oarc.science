### Yâ'k<sup>u</sup> gîtîna'-i.

### 55.

(Words in Tlingit)

Ya'naxê, ya'naxê; â'hao; ya'naxê, ya'naxê. <span
id="fr_61"></span>[2](#fn_56)  
Gadjî'djûs dûqa~~l~~ dAsgî <u>x</u>ûk<sup>u</sup> yana.  
            his dog  
WAgakîda kAda'ostêdja.

<span id="page_39">p. 39</span>

Haida Equivalent.

Xa'as xA'nhao qê'gas wa'<u>g</u>a A'ñga k!û'<u>g</u>agA'ñga:  
Dogs \| even \| when they have pups \| to them \| theirs \| love:  
Wa'a~~l~~hao A'ñ<u>g</u>a ga dî k!ûga'gAñ.  
That is why \| mine \| I \| love.

Even dogs love their offspring:  
So I love mine.
