12.

K!ûstî'ñ gwalî'ñasi, k!ûstî'ñ gwalî'ñasi, wâ'ga qâ'dji la
Two if there were, two if there were, their heads I
    dagaga'olîñasi.
    would keep.

If there were two (boys), if there were two, I would keep their heads.
