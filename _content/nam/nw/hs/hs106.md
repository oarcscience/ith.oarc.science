### Song used by All Families in making Peace

### (Ga lâ' s?a'lAña-i).

### 104.

Yê~~l~~ dî tâda's <u>L</u>û q!e'nañ dî u'nsAtsgaiya'ndô.  
Raven \| me \| ate \| if \| myself \| I \| would not know.  
Ao L<u>î</u>sînôt dAga'ñ ~~l~~ sû'g.  
Now \| first time \| for myself \| I \| am singing.

If Raven had eaten me, <span id="fr_101"></span>[1](#fn_95) I would not
know myself.  
Now for the first time I am singing to myself.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_95"></span>[62:1](hs106.htm#fr_101) "Being eaten by Raven"
seems to mean being killed in war.
