### 100. <span id="fr_97"></span>[1](#fn_91)

DalA'ñ gu k<u>î</u>'lsta-us dî L!ao gAm kî'lstastA dî gudA'ñhAñgAn.  
Your \| ? \| voices are tired, \| I \| but \| not \| have a tired voice
\| I \| want to.

Your voices are tired, but I do not want to have a tired voice (i. e., I
do not want to cease wailing).
