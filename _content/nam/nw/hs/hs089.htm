Skî'daoqao.

87. 1

DAñ tcîn lk!iä'nao dAn dA qä'tcû kû'udAñ.
Your | grand-father | by stick (cedar trunk used for canoe) | you | for | is looking | chief's son (or "dear").
GA'il?Añ kû'sLîgAl.
To yourself | go straight up.

Your grandfather's canoe is looking for you, dear.
Go straight up to it.
