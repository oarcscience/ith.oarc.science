### 20.

L!a s<u>g</u>A'nxAn L! <u>g</u>êidAñ sû'ga. L!a s<u>g</u>A'nxAn hao
<u>g</u>êidAñ  
Those \| only \| are that way, \| they \| say. Those \| only \| are that
way,  
    L! sû'ga.  
    they say.  
GAm iL! La geitgâ'ñañ L! sû'ga.  
Not \| (with) us \| however \| it was that way \| they \| say.

They alone belong to a high family, they say. They alone belong to a
high family, they say.  
But it is not that way with us, they say.
