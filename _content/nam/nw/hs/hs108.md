### Songs of Derision.

\[Songs of this kind were largely in Tsimshian. The following is a
translation of one of them, used in making fun of a poor man who
pretended that he was a chief.\]

### 106.

Laugh at the chief! for, although he is a chief, he has no rattle in his
hand.
