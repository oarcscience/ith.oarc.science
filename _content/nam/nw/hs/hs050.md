### 48.

Ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i ga'ña (four times).  
Be careful of \| chief \| my own.  
Dja Lan ~~l~~a q!ô'gu<u>g</u>a<u>g</u>o.  
Say, \| stop \| biting.  
DAñ si-û' djigu'ldA<u>x</u>wañ gîda'-i.

Be careful of my chief.  
Stop biting!  
Your mouth might become crooked, common mosquitoes. <span

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_52"></span>[35:1](hs050.htm#fr_57) The singer is probably

towards the rich by common people, who are called "mosquitoes."
