### 45.

DAñ s<u>g</u>u'nxA'n gua djâ'ada kudjû'diawîs,  
You \| are not the only one \| woman \| we who belong to a low family,  
DAñ s<u>g</u>u'nxA'n gua djâ'ada kudjû'diawîs,  
You \| are not the only one \| woman \| we who belong to a low family,  
Djia'djats hao qoa'nga,  
Women \| are plenty,  
Djia'djats hao qoan kuha'oga.  
Women \| plenty \| belonging to a low class.

You are not the only woman of our low-class family,  
You are not the only woman of our low-class family.  
There are plenty of women,  
There are plenty of low-class women. <span id="fr_54"></span>[1](#fn_49)
