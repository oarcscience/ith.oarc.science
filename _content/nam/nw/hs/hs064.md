### 62.

Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya (many times).  
Dâ <u>î</u>s<u>î</u>'ñ gut lâ'<u>g</u>aasañ ~~L~~<u>g</u>ai-û' l'nagâ'-i
qâñ ~~l~~ qê'gAn.  
You \| too \| will be happy there \| Skidegate \| town, \| my uncle I \|
bore.  
Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya (many times).

Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya, etc.  
You will again be happy there in Skidegate town, <span
id="fr_70"></span>[3](#fn_65) uncle I bore.  
Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya, etc.
