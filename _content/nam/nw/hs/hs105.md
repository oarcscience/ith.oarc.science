### Battle-Song of Women.

### 103.

\[Song by women during the absence of their husbands with a war-party.\]

Sk!A'<u>g</u>a-o t!a'êt qô'nagAñ.  
Sk!A'<u>g</u>a, \| this one \| killed \| many people.

Sk!A'<u>g</u>ao killed (and enslaved) many people.
