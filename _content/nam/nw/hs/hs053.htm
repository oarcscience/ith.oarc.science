51.

Ayâ'ña î'ldjao yaña xî'lsîs gañâ'ñ gu'tgei dalA'ñ xî'ldjî
Be careful, | noble men | mine, | leaves | like to one another | you are going
    qê'ndaldiasga.
    to grow.
Ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i gâ'ña, ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i gâ'ña.
Be careful, | chief | mine! | Be careful, | chief | mine!

Be careful, my noble sons! you will grow to one another like leaves.
Be careful, my own chief! Be careful, my own chief!
