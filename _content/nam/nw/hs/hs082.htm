80.

Â'gua nâ'nAñ Lga gut kû'lgalguña'-i gAn wagA'n sL!a'gA
Here is | her grand-mother's | land | upon | walking about | for | for it | use (your) hands
    kudjâ'wan. 2
    dear.

Use your hands, my dear, to walk about upon grandmother's land.

Footnotes

52:2 Kudjâ'wan is also a "high word" for "to sit."
