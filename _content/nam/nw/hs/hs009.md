### 7.

A+yañê'+ a'da gua ê'dji tcî'na-i.  
Be careful \| you \| ? \| is \| grandfather.  
A+yañê'+ a'da gua ê'dji NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas.  
Be careful \| you \| ? \| is \| grandfather.  
A+yañê'+ a'qwês nAñ k<u>î</u>tnâ'ñugîn. <span
id="fr_7"></span>[2](#fn_7)  
Be careful \| this sky \| one \| touched.

Be careful. Is this you, grandfather? <span
id="fr_8"></span>[3](#fn_8)  
Be careful. Is this you, NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas? <span
id="fr_9"></span>[3](#fn_8)

### Footnotes

<span id="fn_7"></span>[9:2](hs009.htm#fr_7) Equivalent to
ugA'ñg<u>î</u>n.

<span id="fn_8"></span>[9:3](hs009.htm#fr_10) The mother refers to
ancestors, one of whom is possibly being reborn in her child. "The one
that touched the sky" is Many-Ledges (T!ês qoa'naiya), a cliff back of
Skedans inhabited by a supernatural being.
