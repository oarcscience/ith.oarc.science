### Skî'daoqao.

### 87. <span id="fr_86"></span>[1](#fn_80)

DAñ tcîn ~~l~~k!iä'nao dAn dA qä'tcû kû'udAñ.  
Your \| grand-father \| by stick (cedar trunk used for canoe) \| you \|
for \| is looking \| chief's son (or "dear").  
<u>G</u>A'i~~l~~<sup>?</sup>Añ kû's<u>Lî</u>gA~~l~~.  
To yourself \| go straight up.

Your grandfather's canoe is looking for you, dear.  
Go straight up to it.
