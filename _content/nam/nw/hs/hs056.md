### 54.

Gûgu's gi la g<u>î</u>tg<u>î</u>ñâ'+ñ?  
What \| for \| he (or she) \| is crying like a noble's son
\[g<u>î</u>t\]?  
Gûgu's gi la ga~~l~~ga~~l~~wa'ñ?  
What \| for \| he \| moves around?  
Atcî'na-i nâ'ga-i gî la kung<u>î</u>ña'ñ <span
id="fr_60"></span>[1](#fn_55) <u>g</u>a~~l~~ga~~l~~wa's ahî'gua, etc.  
Grandfather \| the house \| for \| he \| is crying \| moves about as he
is seated.

Why does he cry as a noble cries (i.e., softly)?  
Why does he move around as he sits?  
He moves around and cries for grandfather's house.
