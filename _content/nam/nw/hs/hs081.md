### 79.

Tcî'nAñ lû'<u>g</u>a giû'gulaga, tcî'nAñ lû'<u>g</u>a giû'gulaga.  
Grand-father's \| wave \| listens for, \| grand-father's \| wave \|
listens for.  
StA L!ao tcînA'ñ sî'<u>g</u>a gut kû'djûgia<u>g</u>andalanê.  
After it \| but \| grand-father's \| sea \| upon \| goes along stopping
often on the way (upon the water).

<span id="page_52">p. 52</span>

(He) listens for grandfather's <span id="fr_83"></span>[1](#fn_77) wave,
he listens for grandfather's wave;  
But afterwards he goes along upon grandfather's sea, stopping every now
and then on the way.
