76.

Gîsta L!ao daga'sado sî'liya dâ skA'ndAñ kudjû'gi[gê+]
Who | but | will own it | after it | you | are crying | are sitting
dô'nê aldjî'wai? 2
younger brother | are sitting?

But do you sit crying over who will afterwards own it, younger brother of good family?
