### 51.

Ayâ'ña î'ldjao yaña <u>x</u>î'lsîs gañâ'ñ gu't<u>g</u>ei dalA'ñ
<u>xî</u>'ldj<u>î</u>  
Be careful, \| noble men \| mine, \| leaves \| like to one another \|
you are going  
    q<u>ê</u>'ndaldiasga.  
    to grow.  
Ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i <u>g</u>â'ña, ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i <u>g</u>â'ña.  
Be careful, \| chief \| mine! \| Be careful, \| chief \| mine!

Be careful, my noble sons! you will grow to one another like leaves.  
Be careful, my own chief! Be careful, my own chief!
