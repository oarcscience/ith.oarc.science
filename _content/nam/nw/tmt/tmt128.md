### 103. THE WAR IN THE SPRUCE CANOE <span id="fr_156"></span>[a](#fn_156)

People went to war from Chilkat in a spruce canoe. They drifted down on
the people below, and they came to Dî'<u>g</u>âtiyA. Then they defeated
the Stikine people completely, and afterward they made peace together.
