### Origin of the Perch. <span id="fr_22"></span>[3](#fn_22)

Then he saw a perch. "Don't come near me on the water. You are
secular." <span id="fr_23"></span>[4](#fn_23)--"I am really the greatest
thrower of all, <span id="fr_24"></span>[5](#fn_24) Ô'lalahahë'", great
friend!" Thus he said to him. "You are a great shaman," said
Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> to him. "Hê'hehehe! you only say that I
sing my sacred shaman's song. I feel only glad on the water because it
is calm."--"Come, friend, and let us meet," said
Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> to him. The perch came ashore, and his
cedar-bark head-ring

<span id="page_209">p. 209</span>

was dragging along on the water. Then Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>
took him and put him into the water. He became a perch.
