### Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> makes a House for Only-One. <span id="fr_7"></span>[2](#fn_7)

Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> saw much dirt. He took it and made a
house. He finished the house. Then he took water and blew it on the
house. Then the house made of dirt became large. It was the house of
Only-One. He took cedar-sticks and put them up outside the house, and
Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> took many men and put them inside as
posts. <span id="fr_8"></span>[3](#fn_8)
