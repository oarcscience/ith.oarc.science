Q!â'nêqi?laxu meets Shaman. 1

Q!â'nêqi?laxu started. He saw Shaman, and he saw his fire. The fire was in the woods. He saw his ring of red cedar-bark, and he saw his wood-worms. Q!â'nêqi?laxu was afraid, and he went past behind him.
