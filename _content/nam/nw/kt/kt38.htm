Origin of the Mink. 1

Q!â'nêqi?laxu started, and saw the Mink sharpening (something). "What is that?"--"This will be for taking revenge." "Give it to me, that I may see it!"--"This is nice. Turn your back to me." Then he put it on his back. "Go start! You shall be the mink of later man."
