Q!â'nêqi?laxu meets Oldest-One-in-the-World. 1

Q!â'nêqi?laxu started and came to Oldest-One-in-the-World. Oldest-One-in -the- World went down to the beach and stood on the beach in the sea. "I [you] am going to die here," said Oldest-One-in-the-World. Then he was turned into stone by Q!â'nêqi?laxu, who was afraid of LExyâ'lik*ilayô.
