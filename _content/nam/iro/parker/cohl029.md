### SECTION 18

"'Now another message.

"'Tell your people that ofttimes when a woman hears that a child is born
and goes to see it, she returns and says in many houses where she stops
that its mother's husband is not its father. Now we say that it is
exceedingly wrong to speak such evil of children. The Creator formed the
children as they are; therefore, let the people stop their evil
sayings.'"

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
