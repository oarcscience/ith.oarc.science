### SECTION 70

"Now another message.

"Now we say that you must tell your friends and relatives that there
will be a time when all the earth will withhold its sustaining foods.
Then will come the end of the world and those who refuse to believe in
Gai'wiio\` will suffer great hardships."

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
