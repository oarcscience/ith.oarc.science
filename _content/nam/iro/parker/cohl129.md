### SECTION 118

"So they took up their journey again and after a ways the four
messengers said, 'We have arrived at the point where you must return.
Here there is a house prepared for your eternal abode but should you now
enter a room you could never go back to the earth-world.'"

So they said. Eniaiehuk.
