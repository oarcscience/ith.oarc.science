### SECTION 62

"'Now another message.

"'Now there are some who have boasted that they could drink all the
strong drink in the world. Now we, the messengers, say that they who
thus idly boast will never live to accomplish what they boast. White men
will ever distil the evil liquor.'" <span id="fr_67"></span>[2](#fn_67)

So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.
