### GENERATION SEAL

![](img/05700.jpg)

This Generation Seal, also known as Moloch familiarum or Ammonitarium
Ministering Spirits, makes its spirits obedient in all services. At the
time of Citation, it must be written on parchment and held in the right
hand, but it must not be read.
