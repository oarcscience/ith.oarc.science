CHAPTER VI. THREE SIGNS WITH GRASSHOPPERS AND DARKNESS

Conjuration

ARDUSI! DALUSI!--Grasshoppers, darkness, arise in our service.

These are the plagues which the Cuthians often employed in their exorcisms for punishment.
