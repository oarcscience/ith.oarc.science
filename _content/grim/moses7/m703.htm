THE SECOND TABLE OF THE SPIRITS OF FIRE

Conjuration

Aha, I conjure Thee (Tetragrammaton) Aha by Eheye, * by Ihros, Eheye, by Agla Aysch, Jehovah, conjure I Thee, that thou appear unto me.
