TALISMAN VIII.  
<span id="img_12200"></span> [![](tn/generic.jpg)  
Click to view](img/12200.jpg)  
*The characters should be graven on the outer side of the Ring*.

 

VIII\. Gives invisibility, even to the eyes of genii, so that God alone
shall witness the actions of the possessor. It is accompanied by the
power of penetrating everywhere and passing through brick walls. The
magical words are BENATIR, CARARKAU, DEDOS, ETINARMI. For each operation
the ring must be placed upon a different finger of the right hand. The
talisman is of yellow satin embroidered with black silk.
