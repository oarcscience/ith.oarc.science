THE NINTH CHAPTER.

O transform Animals into Men, and Men into Animals; etc.

(2) Into Stags or Deer.

(3) Into Elephants.

(4) Into Wild Boars.

(5) Into Dogs.

(6) Into Wolves.

(7) Animals into Stones.
