### THE TENTH CHAPTER.

<span id="img_tinit"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/tinit.jpg) |

O hinder any Necromantic or Magical Operations from taking effect,
except those of the Qabalah and of this Sacred Magic.

\(1\) To undo any Magic soever.

\(2\) To heal the bewitched.

\(3\) To make Magical Storms cease.

\(4\) To discover any Magic.

\(5\) To hinder Sorcerers from Operating.
