### THE SEVENTEENTH CHAPTER.

<span id="img_tinit"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/tinit.jpg) |

O fly in the Air and travel any whither.

\(1\) In a black Cloud.

\(2\) In a white Cloud.

\(3\) In the form of an Eagle.

(5) <span id="fr_230"></span>[1](#fn_230) In the form of a Crow (or
Raven).

\(4\) In the form of a Vulture.
