### THE FOURTH CHAPTER.

<span id="img_finit"></span>

|                    |
|--------------------|
| ![](img/finit.jpg) |

OR divers Visions.

\(1\) For Mirrors of Glass and Crystal.

\(2\) In Caverns and Subterranean Places.

\(3\) In the Air.

\(4\) In Rings and Circlets.

\(5\) In Wax.

\(6\) In Fire.

\(7\) In the Moon.

\(8\) In the Water.

\(9\) In the Hand.
