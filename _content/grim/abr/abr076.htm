THE SIXTEENTH CHAPTER.

O find and take possession of all kinds of Treasures, provided that they be not at all (Magically) guarded.

(1) For Treasure of Silver (or Silver Money).

(2) For Gold Money.

(3) For a great Treasure.

(4) For a small Treasure.

(5) For an unguarded Treasure.

(6) For Copper Money.

(7) For Gold in Ingots.

(8) For Silver in Ingots.

(9) For jewels.

(16) For Emeralds.

(17) For worked Gold.

(18) For Silver Plate.

(19) For Statues.
