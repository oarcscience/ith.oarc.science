THE TWENTY-SIXTH CHAPTER.

O open every Kind of Lock, without a Key, and without making any noise.

(1) To open Doors.

(2) To open Padlocks.

(3) To open Larders (or Charnel-Houses).

(4) To open Strong-boxes (or Caskets).

(5) To open Prisons.
