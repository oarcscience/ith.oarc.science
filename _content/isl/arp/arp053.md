### EPIGRAM

###### ON

#### EBN NAPHTA-WAH.

##### BY MOHAMMED BEN ZEID ALMOTAKALAM.

<span class="small">\[IN order to understand Ben Zeid's Charade. we must
remark that, in Arabic, *Naphta* signifies a combustible not very much
unlike our gunpowder, and that *Wah* is an exclamation of
sorrow.\]</span>

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

BY the former with ruin and death we are curst;  
  In the latter we grieve for the ills of the first;  
And as for the whole where together they meet,  
  It's a drunkard, a liar, a thief, and a cheat.

</div>
