# Sūra LV.: Raḥmān, or (God) Most Gracious.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_026.gif)

26. Kullu man AAalayh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>n**in**

26\. All that is on earth  
Will perish:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_027.gif)

27. Wayabq<u>a</u> wajhu rabbika <u>th</u>oo aljal<u>a</u>li
wa**a**l-ikr<u>a</u>m**i**

27\. But will abide (for ever)  
The Face of thy Lord,—  
Full of Majesty,  
Bounty and Honour.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_028.gif)

28. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

28\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_029.gif)

29. Yas-aluhu man fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i
kulla yawmin huwa fee sha/n**in**

29\. Of Him seeks (its need)  
Every creature in the heavens  
And on earth:  
Every day in (new) Splendour  
Doth He (shine)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_030.gif)

30. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

30\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_031.gif)

31. Sanafrughu lakum ayyuh<u>a</u> a**l**ththaqal<u>a</u>n**i**

31\. Soon shall We  
Settle your affairs,  
O both ye worlds!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_032.gif)

32. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

32\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_033.gif)

33. Y<u>a</u> maAAshara aljinni wa**a**l-insi ini ista<u>t</u>aAAtum an
tanfu<u>th</u>oo min aq<u>ta</u>ri a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wa**a**l-ar<u>d</u>i fa**o**nfu<u>th</u>oo l<u>a</u> tanfu<u>th</u>oona
ill<u>a</u> bisul<u>ta</u>n**in**

33\. O ye assembly of Jinns,  
And men! If it be  
Ye can pass beyond  
The zones of the heavens  
And the earth, pass ye!  
Not without authority  
Shall ye be able to pass!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_034.gif)

34. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

34\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_035.gif)

35. Yursalu AAalaykum<u>a</u> shuw<u>a</u>*<u>th</u>*un min n<u>a</u>rin
wanu<u>ha</u>sun fal<u>a</u> tanta<u>s</u>ir<u>a</u>n**i**

35\. On you will be sent  
(O ye evil ones twain!)  
A flame of fire (to burn)  
And a smoke (to choke):  
No defence will ye have:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_036.gif)

36. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

36\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_037.gif)

37. Fa-i<u>tha</u> inshaqqati a**l**ssam<u>a</u>o fak<u>a</u>nat
wardatan ka**al**ddih<u>a</u>n**i**

37\. When the sky is rent  
Asunder, and it becomes red  
Like ointment:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_038.gif)

38. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

38\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_039.gif)

39. Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> yus-alu AAan <u>th</u>anbihi insun
wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n**nun**

39\. On that Day  
No question will be asked  
Of man or Jinn  
As to his sin,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_040.gif)

40. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

40\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_041.gif)

41. YuAArafu almujrimoona biseem<u>a</u>hum fayu/kha<u>th</u>u
bi**al**nnaw<u>as</u>ee wa**a**l-aqd<u>a</u>m**i**

41\. (For) the sinners will be  
Known by their Marks:  
And they will be seized  
By their forelocks and  
Their feet.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_042.gif)

42. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

42\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_043.gif)

43. H<u>ath</u>ihi jahannamu allatee yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bih<u>a</u>
almujrimoon**a**

43\. This is the Hell which  
The Sinners deny:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_044.gif)

44. Ya<u>t</u>oofoona baynah<u>a</u> wabayna <u>h</u>ameemin
<u>a</u>n**in**

44\. In its midst  
And in the midst  
Of boiling hot water  
Will they wander round!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/055/055_045.gif)

45. Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u>
tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n**i**

45\. Then which of the favours  
Of your Lord will ye deny?
