# Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.

### Section 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_035.gif)

35. Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu rabbi ijAAal h<u>atha</u>
albalada <u>a</u>minan wa**o**jnubnee wabaniyya an naAAbuda
al-a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>m**a**

35\. Remember Abraham said:  
"O my Lord! make this city  
One of peace and security:  
And preserve me and my sons  
From worshipping idols.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_036.gif)

36. Rabbi innahunna a<u>d</u>lalna katheeran mina a**l**nn<u>a</u>si
faman tabiAAanee fa-innahu minnee waman AAa<u>sa</u>nee fa-innaka
ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

36\. "O my Lord! they have indeed  
Led astray many among mankind;  
He then who follows my (ways)  
Is of me, and he that  
Disobeys me,—but Thou  
Art indeed Oft-Forgiving,  
Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_037.gif)

37. Rabban<u>a</u> innee askantu min <u>th</u>urriyyatee biw<u>a</u>din
ghayri <u>th</u>ee zarAAin AAinda baytika almu<u>h</u>arrami
rabban<u>a</u> liyuqeemoo a**l**<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta fa**i**jAAal
af-idatan mina a**l**nn<u>a</u>si tahwee ilayhim wa**o**rzuqhum mina
a**l**ththamar<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum yashkuroon**a**

37\. "O our Lord! I have made  
Some of my offspring to dwell  
In a valley without cultivation,  
By Thy Sacred House;  
In order, O our Lord, that they  
May establish regular Prayer:  
So till the hearts of some  
Among men with love towards them,  
And feed them with Fruits:  
So that they may give thanks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_038.gif)

38. Rabban<u>a</u> innaka taAAlamu m<u>a</u> nukhfee wam<u>a</u>
nuAAlinu wam<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi min
shay-in fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>/-**i**

38\. "O our Lord! truly Thou  
Dost know what we conceal  
And what we reveal:  
For nothing whatever is hidden  
From God, whether on earth  
Or in heaven.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_039.gif)

39. Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee wahaba lee
AAal<u>a</u> alkibari ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa inna rabbee
lasameeAAu a**l**dduAA<u>a</u>/-**i**

39\. "Praise be to God, Who hath  
Granted unto me in old age  
Ismā’īl and Isaac: for truly  
My Lord is He, the Hearer  
Of Prayer!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_040.gif)

40. Rabbi ijAAalnee muqeema a**l**<u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wamin
<u>th</u>urriyyatee rabban<u>a</u> wtaqabbal duAA<u>a</u>/-**i**

40\. "O my Lord! make me  
One who establishes regular Prayer,  
And also (raise such)  
Among my offspring  
O our Lord!  
And accept Thou my Prayer.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/014/014_041.gif)

41. Rabban<u>a</u> ighfir lee waliw<u>a</u>lidayya walilmu/mineena yawma
yaqoomu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b**u**

41\. "O our Lord! cover (us)  
With Thy Forgiveness—me,  
My parents, and (all) Believers,  
On the Day that the Reckoning  
Will be established!
