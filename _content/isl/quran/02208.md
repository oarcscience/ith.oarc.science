# Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.

### Section 8

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_058.gif)

58. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>jaroo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi
thumma qutiloo aw m<u>a</u>too layarzuqannahumu All<u>a</u>hu rizqan
<u>h</u>asanan wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lahuwa khayru
a**l**rr<u>a</u>ziqeen**a**

58\. Those who leave their homes  
In the cause of God,  
And are then slain or die,  
On them will God bestow verily  
A goodly Provision:  
Truly God is He Who  
Bestows the best Provision.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_059.gif)

59. Layudkhilannahum mudkhalan yar<u>d</u>awnahu wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha
laAAaleemun <u>h</u>aleem**un**

59\. Verily He will admit then  
To a place with which  
They shall be well pleased:  
For God is All-Knowing,  
Most Forbearing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_060.gif)

60. <u>Tha</u>lika waman AA<u>a</u>qaba bimithli m<u>a</u> AAooqiba bihi
thumma bughiya AAalayhi layan<u>s</u>urannahu All<u>a</u>hu inna
All<u>a</u>ha laAAafuwwun ghafoor**un**

60\. That (is so). And if one  
Has retaliated to no greater  
Extent than the injury he received,  
And is again set upon  
Inordinately, God will help  
Him: for God is One  
That blots out (sins)  
And forgives (again and again).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_061.gif)

61. <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha yooliju allayla fee
a**l**nnah<u>a</u>ri wayooliju a**l**nnah<u>a</u>ra fee allayli waanna
All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun ba<u>s</u>eer**un**

61\. What is because God merges  
Night into Day, and He  
Merges Day into Night, and  
Verily it is God Who hears  
And sees (all things).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_062.gif)

62. <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu waanna
m<u>a</u> yadAAoona min doonihi huwa alb<u>at</u>ilu waanna
All<u>a</u>ha huwa alAAaliyyu alkabeer**u**

62\. That is because God—He  
Is the Reality; and those  
Besides Him whom they invoke,—  
They are but vain Falsehood:  
Verily God is He, Most High,  
Most Great.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_063.gif)

63. Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha anzala mina a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i
m<u>a</u>an fatu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>u al-ar<u>d</u>u mukh<u>d</u>arratan
inna All<u>a</u>ha la<u>t</u>eefun khabeer**un**

63\. Seest thou not that God  
Sends down rain from the sky,  
And forthwith the earth  
Becomes clothed with green?  
For God is He Who understands  
The finest mysteries, and  
Is well-acquainted (with them).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/022/022_064.gif)

64. Lahu m<u>a</u> fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee
al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lahuwa alghaniyyu
al<u>h</u>ameed**u**

64\. To Him belongs all that is  
In the heavens and on earth:  
For verily God,—He is  
Free of all wants,  
Worthy of all praise.
