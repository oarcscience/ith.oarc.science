# Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.

### Section 13

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_121.gif)

121. Wa-i<u>th</u> ghadawta min ahlika tubawwi-o almu/mineena
maq<u>a</u>AAida lilqit<u>a</u>li wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun
AAaleem**un**

121\. Remember that morning  
Thou didst leave  
Thy household (early)  
To post the Faithful  
At their stations for battle:  
And God heareth  
And knoweth all things:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_122.gif)

122. I<u>th</u> hammat <u>ta</u>-ifat<u>a</u>ni minkum an
tafshal<u>a</u> wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu waliyyuhum<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u>
All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon**a**

122\. Rememer two of your parties  
Meditated cowardice;  
But God was their protector,  
And in God should the Faithful  
(Ever) put their trust.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_123.gif)

123. Walaqad na<u>s</u>arakumu All<u>a</u>hu bibadrin waantum
a<u>th</u>illatun fa**i**ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum
tashkuroon**a**

123\. God had helped you  
At Badr, when ye were  
A contemptible little force;  
Then fear God; thus  
May ye show your gratitude.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_124.gif)

124. I<u>th</u> taqoolu lilmu/mineena alan yakfiyakum an yumiddakum
rabbukum bithal<u>a</u>thati <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>fin mina
almal<u>a</u>-ikati munzaleen**a**

124\. Remember thou saidst  
To the Faithful: "Is it not enough  
For you that God should help you  
With three thousand angels  
(Specially) sent down?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_125.gif)

125. Bal<u>a</u> in ta<u>s</u>biroo watattaqoo waya/tookum min fawrihim
h<u>atha</u> yumdidkum rabbukum bikhamsati <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>fin mina
almal<u>a</u>-ikati musawwimeen**a**

125\. "Yea,—if ye remain firm,  
And act aright, even if  
The enemy should rush here  
On you in hot haste,  
Your Lord would help you  
With five thousand angels  
Making a terrific onslaught."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_126.gif)

126. Wam<u>a</u> jaAAalahu All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> bushr<u>a</u> lakum
walita<u>t</u>ma-inna quloobukum bihi wam<u>a</u> a**l**nna<u>s</u>ru
ill<u>a</u> min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>akeem**i**

126\. God made it but a message  
Of hope for you, and an assurance  
To your hearts: (in any case)  
There is no help  
Except from God,  
The Exalted, the Wise:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_127.gif)

127. Liyaq<u>t</u>aAAa <u>t</u>arafan mina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo aw
yakbitahum fayanqaliboo kh<u>a</u>-ibeen**a**

127\. That He might cut off  
A fringe of the Unbelievers  
Or expose them to infamy,  
And they should then  
Be turned back,  
Frustrated of their purpose.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_128.gif)

128. Laysa laka mina al-amri shay-on aw yatooba AAalayhim aw
yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum fa-innahum *<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>limoon**a**

128\. Not for thee, (but for God),  
Is the decision:  
Whether He turn in mercy  
To them, or punish them;  
For they are indeed wrong-doers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/003/003_129.gif)

129. Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a**l**ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti
wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i yaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o
wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wa**A**ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun
ra<u>h</u>eem**un**

129\. To God belongeth all  
That is in the heavens  
And on earth.  
He forgiveth whom He pleaseth  
And punisheth whom He pleaseth  
But God is Oft-Forgiving,  
Most Merciful.
