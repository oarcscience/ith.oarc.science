# Sūra XCIV.: Inshirāḥ, or The Expansion.

### Section 1

![](../gif/094/094_001.gif)

1. Alam nashra<u>h</u> laka <u>s</u>adrak**a**

1\. Have We not  
Expanded thee thy breast?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_002.gif)

2. Wawa<u>d</u>aAAn<u>a</u> AAanka wizrak**a**

2\. And removed from thee  
Thy burden

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_003.gif)

3. Alla<u>th</u>ee anqa<u>d</u>a *<u>th</u>*ahrak**a**

3\. The which did gall  
Thy back?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_004.gif)

4. WarafaAAn<u>a</u> laka <u>th</u>ikrak**a**

4\. And raised high the esteem  
(In which) thou (art held)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_005.gif)

5. Fa-inna maAAa alAAusri yusr<u>a</u>**n**

5\. So, verily,  
With every difficulty,  
There is relief:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_006.gif)

6. Inna maAAa alAAusri yusr<u>a</u>**n**

6\. Verily, with every difficulty  
There is relief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_007.gif)

7. Fa-i<u>tha</u> faraghta fa**i**n<u>s</u>ab

7\. Therefore, when thou art  
Free (from thine immediate task),  
Still labour hard,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/094/094_008.gif)

8. Wa-il<u>a</u> rabbika fa**i**rghab  

8\. And to thy Lord  
Turn (all) thy attention.
