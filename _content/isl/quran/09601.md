# Sūra XCVI.: Iqraa, or Read! or Proclaim!

### Section 1

![](../gif/096/096_001.gif)

1. Iqra/ bi-ismi rabbika alla<u>th</u>ee khalaq**a**

1\. Proclaim! (or Read!)  
In the name  
Of thy Lord and Cherisher,  
Who created—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_002.gif)

2. Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>na min AAalaq**in**

2\. Created man, out of  
A (mere) clot  
Of congealed blood:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_003.gif)

3. Iqra/ warabbuka al-akram**u**

3\. Proclaim! And thy Lord  
Is Most Bountiful,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_004.gif)

4. Alla<u>th</u>ee AAallama bi**a**lqalam**i**

4\. He Who taught  
(The use of) the Pen,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_005.gif)

5. AAallama al-ins<u>a</u>na m<u>a</u> lam yaAAlam

5\. Taught man that  
Which he knew not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_006.gif)

6. Kall<u>a</u> inna al-ins<u>a</u>na laya<u>t</u>gh<u>a</u>

6\. Nay, but man doth  
Transgress all bounds,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_007.gif)

7. An ra<u>a</u>hu istaghn<u>a</u>

7\. In that he looketh  
Upon himself as self-sufficient.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_008.gif)

8. Inna il<u>a</u> rabbika a**l**rrujAA<u>a</u>

8\. Verily, to thy Lord  
Is the return (of all).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_009.gif)

9. Araayta alla<u>th</u>ee yanh<u>a</u>

9\. Seest thou one  
Who forbids—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_010.gif)

10. AAabdan i<u>tha</u> <u>s</u>all<u>a</u>

10\. A votary when he  
(Turns) to pray?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_011.gif)

11. Araayta in k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u>

11\. Seest thou if  
He is on (the road  
Of) Guidance?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_012.gif)

12. Aw amara bi**al**ttaqw<u>a</u>

12\. Or enjoins Righteousness?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_013.gif)

13. Araayta in ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>

13\. Seest thou if he  
Denies (Truth) and turns away?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_014.gif)

14. Alam yaAAlam bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha yar<u>a</u>

14\. Knoweth he not  
That God doth see?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_015.gif)

15. Kall<u>a</u> la-in lam yantahi lanasfaAAan
bi**al**nn<u>as</u>iya**ti**

15\. Let him beware! If he  
Desist not, We will  
Drag him by the forelock,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_016.gif)

16. N<u>as</u>iyatin k<u>ath</u>ibatin kh<u>at</u>i-a**tin**

16\. A lying, sinful forelock!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_017.gif)

17. FalyadAAu n<u>a</u>diyah**u**

17\. Then, let him call  
(For help) to his council  
(Of comrades):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_018.gif)

18. SanadAAu a**l**zzab<u>a</u>niya**ta**

18\. We will call  
On the angels of punishment  
(To deal with him)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/096/096_019.gif)

19. Kall<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAhu wa**o**sjud wa**i**qtarib  

19\. Nay, heed him not:  
But bow down in adoration,  
And bring thyself  
The closer (to God)!
