# Sūra I.: Fātiḥa, or the Opening Chapter.

### Section 1

![](../gif/001/001_001.gif)

1. Bismi All<u>a</u>hi a**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni
a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**i**

1\. In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_002.gif)

2. Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

2\. Praise be to God,  
The Cherisher and Sustainer of the Worlds;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_003.gif)

3. A**l**rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**i**

3\. Most Gracious, Most Merciful;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_004.gif)

4. M<u>a</u>liki yawmi a**l**ddeen**i**

4\. Master of the Day of Judgment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_005.gif)

5. Iyy<u>a</u>ka naAAbudu wa-iyy<u>a</u>ka nastaAAeen**u**

5\. Thee do we worship,  
And Thine aid we seek.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_006.gif)

6. Ihdin<u>a</u> a**l**<u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>a almustaqeem**a**

6\. Show us the straight way,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/001/001_007.gif)

7. <u>S</u>ir<u>at</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena anAAamta AAalayhim ghayri
almagh<u>d</u>oobi AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> a**l**<u>dda</u>lleen**a**

7\. The way of those on whom  
Thou hast bestowed Thy Grace,  
Those whose (portion)  
Is not wrath,  
And who go not astray.
