# Sūra LXXV.: Qiyāmat, or the Resurrection.

### Section 1

![](../gif/075/075_001.gif)

1. L<u>a</u> oqsimu biyawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mat**i**

1\. I do call to witness  
The Resurrection Day;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_002.gif)

2. Wal<u>a</u> oqsimu bi**al**nnafsi allaww<u>a</u>mat**i**

2\. And I do call to witness  
The self-reproaching spirit:  
(Eschew Evil).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_003.gif)

3. Aya<u>h</u>sabu al-ins<u>a</u>nu allan najmaAAa
AAi*<u>th</u>*<u>a</u>mah**u**

3\. Does man think that We  
Cannot assemble his bones?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_004.gif)

4. Bal<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>direena AAal<u>a</u> an nusawwiya
ban<u>a</u>nah**u**

4\. Nay, We are able to put  
Together in perfect order  
The very tips of his fingers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_005.gif)

5. Bal yureedu al-ins<u>a</u>nu liyafjura am<u>a</u>mah**u**

5\. But man wishes to do  
Wrong (even) in the time  
In front of him.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_006.gif)

6. Yas-alu ayy<u>a</u>na yawmu alqiy<u>a</u>ma**ti**

6\. He questions: "When  
Is the Day of Resurrection?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_007.gif)

7. Fa-i<u>tha</u> bariqa alba<u>s</u>ar**u**

7\. At length, when  
The Sight is dazed,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_008.gif)

8. Wakhasafa alqamar**u**

8\. And the moon is  
Buried in darkness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_009.gif)

9. WajumiAAa a**l**shshamsu wa**a**lqamar**u**

9\. And the sun and moon  
Are joined together,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_010.gif)

10. Yaqoolu al-ins<u>a</u>nu yawma-i<u>th</u>in ayna almafar**ru**

10\. That Day will Man say:  
"Where is the refuge?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_011.gif)

11. Kall<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> wazar**a**

11\. By no means!  
No place of safety!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_012.gif)

12. Il<u>a</u> rabbika yawma-i<u>th</u>in almustaqar**ru**

12\. Before thy Lord (alone),  
That Day will be  
The place of rest.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_013.gif)

13. Yunabbao al-ins<u>a</u>nu yawma-i<u>th</u>in bim<u>a</u> qaddama
waakhkhar**a**

13\. That Day will Man  
Be told (all) that he  
Put forward, and all  
That he put back.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_014.gif)

14. Bali al-ins<u>a</u>nu AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi ba<u>s</u>eera**tun**

14\. Nay, man will be  
Evidence against himself,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_015.gif)

15. Walaw alq<u>a</u> maAA<u>ath</u>eerah**u**

15\. Even though he were  
To put up his excuses.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_016.gif)

16. L<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>arrik bihi lis<u>a</u>naka litaAAjala bih**i**

16\. Move not thy tongue  
Concerning the (Qur-ān)  
To make haste therewith,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_017.gif)

17. Inna AAalayn<u>a</u> jamAAahu waqur-<u>a</u>nah**u**

17\. It is for Us to collect it  
And to promulgate it:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_018.gif)

18. Fa-i<u>tha</u> qara/n<u>a</u>hu fa**i**ttabiAA qur-<u>a</u>nah**u**

18\. But when We have  
Promulgated it, follow thou  
Its recital (as promulgated):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_019.gif)

19. Thumma inna AAalayn<u>a</u> bay<u>a</u>nah**u**

19\. Nay more, it is  
For us to explain it  
(And make it clear):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_020.gif)

20. Kall<u>a</u> bal tu<u>h</u>ibboona alAA<u>a</u>jila**ta**

20\. Nay, (ye men!)  
But ye love  
The fleeting life,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_021.gif)

21. Wata<u>th</u>aroona al-<u>a</u>khira**ta**

21\. And leave alone  
The Hereafter.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_022.gif)

22. Wujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in n<u>ad</u>ira**tun**

22\. Some faces, that Day,  
Will beam (in brightness  
And beauty);—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_023.gif)

23. Il<u>a</u> rabbih<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>*<u>th</u>*ira**tun**

23\. Looking towards their Lord;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_024.gif)

24. Wawujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in b<u>a</u>sira**tun**

24\. And some faces, that Day,  
Will be sad and dismal,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_025.gif)

25. Ta*<u>th</u>*unnu an yufAAala bih<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>qira**tun**

25\. In the thought that some  
Back-breaking calamity was about  
To be indicted on them;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_026.gif)

26. Kall<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balaghati a**l**ttar<u>a</u>qiy**a**

26\. Yea, when (the soul)  
Reaches to the collar-bone  
(In its exit),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_027.gif)

27. Waqeela man r<u>a</u>q**in**

27\. And there will be a cry,  
"Who is a magician  
(To restore him)?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_028.gif)

28. Wa*<u>th</u>*anna annahu alfir<u>a</u>q**u**

28\. And he will conclude  
That it was (the Time)  
Of Parting;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_029.gif)

29. Wa**i**ltaffati a**l**ss<u>a</u>qu bi**a**lss<u>a</u>q**i**

29\. And one leg will be  
Joined with another:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/075/075_030.gif)

30. Il<u>a</u> rabbika yawma-i<u>th</u>in almas<u>a</u>q**u**

30\. That Day the Drive  
Will be (all) to thy Lord!
