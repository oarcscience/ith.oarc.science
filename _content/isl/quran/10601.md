# Sūra CVI.: Quraish or The Quraish, (Custodians of the Ka’ba).

### Section 1

![](../gif/106/106_001.gif)

1. Li-eel<u>a</u>fi quraysh**in**

1\. For the covenants  
(Of security and safeguard  
Enjoyed) by the Quraish,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/106/106_002.gif)

2. Eel<u>a</u>fihim ri<u>h</u>lata a**l**shshit<u>a</u>-i
wa**al**<u>ss</u>ayf**i**

2\. Their covenants (covering) journeys  
By winter and summer,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/106/106_003.gif)

3. FalyaAAbudoo rabba h<u>atha</u> albayt**i**

3\. Let them adore the Lord  
Of this House

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/106/106_004.gif)

4. Alla<u>th</u>ee a<u>t</u>AAamahum min jooAAin wa<u>a</u>manahum min
khawf**in**  

4\. Who provides them  
With food against hunger,  
And with security  
Against fear (of danger).
