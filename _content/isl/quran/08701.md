# Sūra LXXXVII.: A’lā, or The Most High.

### Section 1

![](../gif/087/087_001.gif)

1. Sabbi<u>h</u>i isma rabbika al-aAAl<u>a</u>

1\. Glorify the name  
Of' thy Guardian-Lord  
Most High,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_002.gif)

2. Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa fasaww<u>a</u>

2\. Who hath created,  
And further, given  
Order and proportion;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_003.gif)

3. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>ee qaddara fahad<u>a</u>

3\. Who hath ordained laws.  
And granted guidance;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_004.gif)

4. Wa**a**lla<u>th</u>ee akhraja almarAA<u>a</u>

4\. And Who bringeth out  
The (green and luscious) pasture,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_005.gif)

5. FajaAAalahu ghuth<u>a</u>an a<u>h</u>w<u>a</u>

5\. And then doth make it  
(But) swarthy stubble.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_006.gif)

6. Sanuqri-oka fal<u>a</u> tans<u>a</u>

6\. By degrees shall We  
Teach thee to declare  
(The Message), so thou  
Shalt not forget,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_007.gif)

7. Ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu innahu yaAAlamu
aljahra wam<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u>

7\. Except as God wills:  
For He knoweth  
What is manifest  
And what is hidden.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_008.gif)

8. Wanuyassiruka lilyusr<u>a</u>

8\. And We will make it  
Easy for thee (to follow)  
The simple (Path).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_009.gif)

9. Fa<u>th</u>akkir in nafaAAati a**l**<u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u>

9\. Therefore give admonition  
In case the admonition  
Profits (the hearer).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_010.gif)

10. Saya<u>thth</u>akkaru man yakhsh<u>a</u>

10\. The admonition will be received  
By those who fear (God):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_011.gif)

11. Wayatajannabuh<u>a</u> al-ashq<u>a</u>

11\. But it will be avoided  
By those most unfortunate ones,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_012.gif)

12. Alla<u>th</u>ee ya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> a**l**nn<u>a</u>ra
alkubr<u>a</u>

12\. Who will enter  
The Great Fire,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_013.gif)

13. Thumma l<u>a</u> yamootu feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>
ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>

13\. In which they will then  
Neither die nor live.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_014.gif)

14. Qad afla<u>h</u>a man tazakk<u>a</u>

14\. But those will prosper  
Who purify themselves,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_015.gif)

15. Wa<u>th</u>akara isma rabbihi fa<u>s</u>all<u>a</u>

15\. And glorify the name  
Of their Guardian-Lord,  
And (lift their hearts)  
In Prayer.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_016.gif)

16. Bal tu/thiroona al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a**l**dduny<u>a</u>

16\. Nay (behold), ye prefer  
The life of this world;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_017.gif)

17. Wa**a**l-<u>a</u>khiratu khayrun waabq<u>a</u>

17\. But the Hereafter  
Is better and more enduring.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_018.gif)

18. Inna h<u>atha</u> lafee a**l**<u>ss</u>u<u>h</u>ufi al-ool<u>a</u>

18\. And this is  
In the Books  
Of the earliest (Revelations),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/087/087_019.gif)

19. <u>S</u>u<u>h</u>ufi ibr<u>a</u>heema wamoos<u>a</u>

19\. The Books of  
Abraham and Moses.
