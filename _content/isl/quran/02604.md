# Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.

### Section 4

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_053.gif)

53. Faarsala firAAawnu fee almad<u>a</u>-ini <u>ha</u>shireen**a**

53\. By inspiration We told Moses:  
"Travel by night with  
My servants; for surely  
Ye shall be pursued."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_054.gif)

54. Inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i lashir<u>th</u>imatun qaleeloon**a**

54\. Then Pharaoh sent heralds  
To (all) the Cities,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_055.gif)

55. Wa-innahum lan<u>a</u> lagh<u>a</u>-i*<u>th</u>*oon**a**

55\. (Saying): "These (Israelites)  
Are but a small band,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_056.gif)

56. Wa-inn<u>a</u> lajameeAAun <u>hath</u>iroon**a**

56\. "And they are raging  
Furiously against us;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_057.gif)

57. Faakhrajn<u>a</u>hum min jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon**in**

57\. "But we are a multitude  
Amply fore-warned."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_058.gif)

58. Wakunoozin wamaq<u>a</u>min kareem**in**

58\. So We expelled them  
From gardens, springs,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_059.gif)

59. Ka<u>tha</u>lika waawrathn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> banee
isr<u>a</u>-eel**a**

59\. Treasures, and every kind  
Of honourable position;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_060.gif)

60. FaatbaAAoohum mushriqeen**a**

60\. Thus it was, but  
We made the Children  
Of Israel inheritors  
Of such things.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_061.gif)

61. Falamm<u>a</u> tar<u>a</u>a aljamAA<u>a</u>ni q<u>a</u>la
a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu moos<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> lamudrakoon**a**

61\. So they pursued them  
At sunrise.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_062.gif)

62. Q<u>a</u>la kall<u>a</u> inna maAAiya rabbee sayahdeen**i**

62\. And when the two bodies  
Saw each other, the people  
Of Moses said: "We are  
Sure to be overtaken."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_063.gif)

63. Faaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> ani i<u>d</u>rib
biAAa<u>sa</u>ka alba<u>h</u>ra fa**i**nfalaqa fak<u>a</u>na kullu
firqin ka**al**<u>tt</u>awdi alAAa*<u>th</u>*eem**i**

63\. (Moses) said: "By no means!  
My Lord is with me!  
Soon will He guide me!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_064.gif)

64. Waazlafn<u>a</u> thamma al-<u>a</u>khareen**a**

64\. Then We told Moses  
By inspiration: "Strike  
The sea with thy rod."  
So it divided, and each  
Separate part became  
Like the huge, firm mass  
Of a mountain.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_065.gif)

65. Waanjayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> waman maAAahu ajmaAAeen**a**

65\. And We made the other  
Party approach thither.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_066.gif)

66. Thumma aghraqn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen**a**

66\. We delivered Moses and all  
Who were with him;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_067.gif)

67. Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na
aktharuhum mu/mineen**a**

67\. But We drowned the others.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_068.gif)

68. Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a**l**rra<u>h</u>eem**u**

68\. Verily in this is a Sign:  
But most of them  
Do not believe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/026/026_069.gif)

69. Wa**o**tlu AAalayhim nabaa ibr<u>a</u>heem**a**

69\. And verily thy Lord  
Is He, the Exalted in Might,  
Most Merciful.
