# Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights

### Section 14

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_109.gif)

109. Q<u>a</u>la almalao min qawmi firAAawna inna h<u>atha</u>
las<u>ah</u>irun AAaleem**un**

109\. Said the Chiefs of the people  
Of Pharaoh: "This is indeed  
A sorcerer well-versed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_110.gif)

110. Yureedu an yukhrijakum min ar<u>d</u>ikum fam<u>atha</u>
ta/muroon**a**

110\. "His plan is to get you out  
Of your land: then  
What is it ye counsel?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_111.gif)

111. Q<u>a</u>loo arjih waakh<u>a</u>hu waarsil fee almad<u>a</u>-ini
<u>ha</u>shireen**a**

111\. They said: "Keep him  
And his brother in suspense  
(For a while); and send  
To the cities men to collect—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_112.gif)

112. Ya/tooka bikulli s<u>ah</u>irin AAaleem**in**

112\. And bring up to thee  
All (our) sorcerers well-versed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_113.gif)

113. Waj<u>a</u>a a**l**ssa<u>h</u>aratu firAAawna q<u>a</u>loo inna
lan<u>a</u> laajran in kunn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu algh<u>a</u>libeen**a**

113\. So there came  
The sorcerers to Pharaoh:  
They said, "Of course  
We shall have a (suitable)  
Reward if we win!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_114.gif)

114. Q<u>a</u>la naAAam wa-innakum lamina almuqarrabeen**a**

114\. He said: "Yea, (and more),—  
For ye shall in that case  
Be (raised to posts)  
Nearest (to my person)."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_115.gif)

115. Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> imm<u>a</u> an tulqiya
wa-imm<u>a</u> an nakoona na<u>h</u>nu almulqeen**a**

115\. They said: "O Moses!  
Wilt thou throw (first),  
Or shall we have  
The (first) throw?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_116.gif)

116. Q<u>a</u>la alqoo falamm<u>a</u> alqaw sa<u>h</u>aroo aAAyuna
a**l**nn<u>a</u>si wa**i**starhaboohum waj<u>a</u>oo bisi<u>h</u>rin
AAa*<u>th</u>*eem**in**

116\. Said Moses: "Throw ye (first)."  
So when they threw,  
They bewitched the eyes  
Of the people, and struck  
Terror into them: for they  
Showed a great (feat of) magic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_117.gif)

117. Waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> an alqi
AAa<u>sa</u>ka fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya talqafu m<u>a</u> ya/fikoon**a**

117\. We put it into Moses's mind  
By inspiration: "Throw (now)  
Thy rod": and behold!  
It swallows up straightway  
All the falsehoods  
Which they fake!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_118.gif)

118. FawaqaAAa al<u>h</u>aqqu waba<u>t</u>ala m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo
yaAAmaloon**a**

118\. Thus truth was confirmed.  
And all that they did  
Was made of no effect.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_119.gif)

119. Faghuliboo hun<u>a</u>lika wa**i**nqalaboo <u>sa</u>ghireen**a**

119\. So the (great ones) were vanquished  
There and then, and were  
Made to look small.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_120.gif)

120. Waolqiya a**l**ssa<u>h</u>aratu s<u>a</u>jideen**a**

120\. But the sorcerers fell down  
Prostrate in adoration,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_121.gif)

121. Q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> birabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen**a**

121\. Saying: "We believe  
In the Lord of the Worlds,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_122.gif)

122. Rabbi moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon**a**

122\. "The Lord of Moses and Aaron.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_123.gif)

123. Q<u>a</u>la firAAawnu <u>a</u>mantum bihi qabla an <u>ath</u>ana
lakum inna h<u>atha</u> lamakrun makartumoohu fee almadeenati
litukhrijoo minh<u>a</u> ahlah<u>a</u> fasawfa taAAlamoon**a**

123\. Said Pharaoh: "Believe ye  
In Him before I give  
You permission? Surely  
This is a trick which ye  
Have planned in the City  
To drive out its people:  
But soon shall ye know  
(The consequences).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_124.gif)

124. Laoqa<u>t</u>iAAanna aydiyakum waarjulakum min khil<u>a</u>fin
thumma lao<u>s</u>allibannakum ajmaAAeen**a**

124\. "Be sure I will cut off  
Your hands and your feet  
On opposite sides, and I  
Will cause you all  
To die on the cross."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_125.gif)

125. Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> rabbin<u>a</u> munqaliboon**a**

125\. They said: "For us,  
We are but sent back  
Unto our Lord:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/007/007_126.gif)

126. Wam<u>a</u> tanqimu minn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an
<u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbin<u>a</u> lamm<u>a</u>
j<u>a</u>atn<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> afrigh AAalayn<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abran
watawaffan<u>a</u> muslimeen**a**

126\. "But thou dost wreak  
Thy vengeance on us  
Simply because we believed  
In the Signs of our Lord  
When they reached us!  
Our Lord! pour out on us  
Patience and constancy, and take  
Our souls unto Thee  
As Muslims (who bow  
To Thy Will)!
