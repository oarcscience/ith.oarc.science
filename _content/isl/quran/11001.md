# Sūra CX.: Naṣr, or Help.

### Section 1

![](../gif/110/110_001.gif)

1. I<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a na<u>s</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi
wa**a**lfat<u>h</u>**u**

1\. When comes the Help  
Of God, and Victory,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/110/110_002.gif)

2. Waraayta a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa yadkhuloona fee deeni All<u>a</u>hi
afw<u>a</u>j<u>a</u>**n**

2\. And thou dost see  
The People enter God's Religion  
In crowds,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/110/110_003.gif)

3. Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika wa**i**staghfirhu innahu
k<u>a</u>na taww<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**  

3\. Celebrate the Praises  
Of thy Lord, and pray  
For His Forgiveness:  
For He is Oft-Returning  
(In Grace and Mercy).
