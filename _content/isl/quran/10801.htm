Sūra CVIII.: Kauthar, or Abundance.

Section 1

1. Inna aAAtaynaka alkawthara

1. To thee have We
Granted the Fount
(of Abundance).

2. Fasalli lirabbika wainhar

2. Therefore to thy Lord
Turn in Prayer
And Sacrifice.

3. Inna shani-aka huwa al-abtaru  

3. For he who hateth thee,—
He will be cut off
(From Future Hope).
