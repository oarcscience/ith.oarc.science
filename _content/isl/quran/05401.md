# Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.

### Section 1

![](../gif/054/054_001.gif)

1. Iqtarabati a**l**ss<u>a</u>AAatu wa**i**nshaqqa alqamar**u**

1\. The Hour (of Judgment)  
Is nigh, and the moon  
Is cleft asunder.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_002.gif)

2. Wa-in yaraw <u>a</u>yatan yuAAri<u>d</u>oo wayaqooloo si<u>h</u>run
mustamirr**un**

2\. But if they see  
A Sign, they turn away,  
And say, "This is  
(But) transient magic."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_003.gif)

3. Waka<u>thth</u>aboo wa**i**ttabaAAoo ahw<u>a</u>ahum wakullu amrin
mustaqirr**un**

3\. They reject (the warning)  
And follow their (own) lusts  
But every matter has  
Its appointed time.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_004.gif)

4. Walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum mina al-anb<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> feehi
muzdajar**un**

4\. There have already come  
To them Recitals wherein  
There is (enough) to check (them),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_005.gif)

5. <u>H</u>ikmatun b<u>a</u>lighatun fam<u>a</u> tughnee
a**l**nnu<u>th</u>ur**u**

5\. Mature wisdom;—but  
(The preaching of) Warners  
Profits them not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_006.gif)

6. Fatawalla AAanhum yawma yadAAu a**l**dd<u>a</u>AAi il<u>a</u> shay-in
nukur**in**

6\. Therefore, (O Prophet,)  
Turn away from them.  
The Day that the Caller  
Will call (them)  
To a terrible affair,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_007.gif)

7. KhushshaAAan ab<u>sa</u>ruhum yakhrujoona mina al-ajd<u>a</u>thi
kaannahum jar<u>a</u>dun muntashir**un**

7\. They will come forth,  
Their eyes humbled—  
From (their) graves, (torpid)  
Like locusts scattered abroad,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_008.gif)

8. Muh<u>t</u>iAAeena il<u>a</u> a**l**dd<u>a</u>AAi yaqoolu
alk<u>a</u>firoona h<u>atha</u> yawmun AAasir**un**

8\. Hastening, with eyes transfixed,  
Towards the Caller!—  
"Hard is this Day!",  
The Unbelievers will say.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_009.gif)

9. Ka<u>thth</u>abat qablahum qawmu noo<u>h</u>in faka<u>thth</u>aboo
AAabdan<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>loo majnoonun wa**i**zdujir**a**

9\. Before them the People  
Of Noah rejected (their apostle):  
They rejected Our servant,  
And said, "Here is  
One possessed!", and he  
Was driven out.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_010.gif)

10. FadaAA<u>a</u> rabbahu annee maghloobun fa**i**nta<u>s</u>ir

10\. Then he called on his Lord:  
"I am one overcome:  
Do Thou then help (me)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_011.gif)

11. Fafata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> abw<u>a</u>ba a**l**ssam<u>a</u>-i
bim<u>a</u>-in munhamir**in**

11\. So We opened the gates  
Of heaven, with water  
Pouring forth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_012.gif)

12. Wafajjarn<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>a AAuyoonan fa**i**ltaq<u>a</u>
alm<u>a</u>o AAal<u>a</u> amrin qad qudir**a**

12\. And We caused the earth  
To gush forth with springs.  
So the waters met (and rose)  
To the extent decreed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_013.gif)

13. Wa<u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>ti alw<u>ah</u>in
wadusur**in**

13\. But We bore him  
On an (Ark) made of  
Broad planks and caulked  
With palm-fibre:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_014.gif)

14. Tajree bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>an liman k<u>a</u>na
kufir**a**

14\. She floats under our eyes  
(And care): a recompense  
To one who had been  
Rejected (with scorn)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_015.gif)

15. Walaqad tarakn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan fahal min
muddakir**in**

15\. And We have left  
This as a Sign  
(For all time): then  
Is there any that will  
Receive admonition?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_016.gif)

16. Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

16\. But how (terrible) was  
My Penalty and My Warning?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_017.gif)

17. Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li**l**<u>thth</u>ikri
fahal min muddakir**in**

17\. And We have indeed  
Made the Qur-ān easy  
To understand and remember:  
Then is there any that  
Will receive admonition?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_018.gif)

18. Ka<u>thth</u>abat AA<u>a</u>dun fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee
wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

18\. The ‘Ād (people) (too)  
Rejected (Truth): then  
How terrible was  
My Penalty and My Warning?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_019.gif)

19. Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim ree<u>h</u>an
<u>s</u>ar<u>s</u>aran fee yawmi na<u>h</u>sin mustamirr**in**

19\. For We sent against them  
A furious wind, on a Day  
Of violent Disaster,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_020.gif)

20. TanziAAu a**l**nn<u>a</u>sa kaannahum aAAj<u>a</u>zu nakhlin
munqaAAir**in**

20\. Plucking out men as if  
They were roots of palm-trees  
Torn up (from the ground).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_021.gif)

21. Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

21\. Yea, how (terrible) was  
My Penalty and My Warning!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_022.gif)

22. Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li**l**<u>thth</u>ikri
fahal min muddakir**in**

22\. But We have indeed  
Made the Qur-ān easy  
To understand and remember:  
Then is there any that  
Will receive admonition?
