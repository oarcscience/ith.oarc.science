# Sūra C.: ’Adiyāt, or Those that run.

### Section 1

![](../gif/100/100_001.gif)

1. Wa**a**lAA<u>a</u>diy<u>a</u>ti <u>d</u>ab<u>ha</u>**n**

1\. By the (Steeds)  
That run, with panting (breath),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_002.gif)

2. Fa**a**lmooriy<u>a</u>ti qad<u>ha</u>**n**

2\. And strike sparks of fire,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_003.gif)

3. Fa**a**lmugheer<u>a</u>ti <u>s</u>ub<u>ha</u>**n**

3\. And push home the charge  
In the morning,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_004.gif)

4. Faatharna bihi naqAA<u>a</u>**n**

4\. And raise the dust  
In clouds the while,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_005.gif)

5. Fawasa<u>t</u>na bihi jamAA<u>a</u>**n**

5\. And penetrate forthwith  
Into the midst (of the foe)  
En masse;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_006.gif)

6. Inna al-ins<u>a</u>na lirabbihi lakanood**un**

6\. Truly Man is,  
To his Lord,  
Ungrateful;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_007.gif)

7. Wa-innahu AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika lashaheed**un**

7\. And to that (fact)  
He bears witness  
(By his deeds);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_008.gif)

8. Wa-innahu li<u>h</u>ubbi alkhayri lashadeed**un**

8\. And violent is he  
In his love of wealth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_009.gif)

9. Afal<u>a</u> yaAAlamu i<u>tha</u> buAAthira m<u>a</u> fee
alquboor**i**

9\. Does he not know,—  
When that which is  
In the graves is  
Scattered abroad

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_010.gif)

10. Wa<u>h</u>u<u>ss</u>ila m<u>a</u> fee a**l**<u>ss</u>udoor**i**

10\. And that which is  
(Locked up) in (human) breasts  
Is made manifest—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/100/100_011.gif)

11. Inna rabbahum bihim yawma-i<u>th</u>in lakhabeer**un**  

11\. That their Lord had been  
Well-acquainted with them,  
(Even to) that Day?
