# Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.

### Section 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_023.gif)

23. Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu bi**al**nnu<u>th</u>ur**i**

23\. The Thamūd (also)  
Rejected (their) Warners.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_024.gif)

24. Faq<u>a</u>loo abasharan minn<u>a</u> w<u>ah</u>idan nattabiAAuhu
inn<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin wasuAAur**in**

24\. For they said: "What!  
A man! a solitary one  
From among ourselves!  
Shall we follow such a one?  
Truly should we then be  
Straying in mind, and mad!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_025.gif)

25. Aolqiya a**l**<u>thth</u>ikru AAalayhi min baynin<u>a</u> bal huwa
ka<u>ththa</u>bun ashir**un**

25\. "Is it that the Message  
Is sent to him,  
Of all people amongst us?  
Nay, he is a liar,  
An insolent one!"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_026.gif)

26. SayaAAlamoona ghadan mani alka<u>ththa</u>bu al-ashir**u**

26\. Ah! they will know  
On the morrow, which is  
The liar, the insolent one!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_027.gif)

27. Inn<u>a</u> mursiloo a**l**nn<u>a</u>qati fitnatan lahum
fa**i**rtaqibhum wa**i**<u>st</u>abir

27\. For We will send  
The she-camel  
By way of trial for them.  
So watch them, (O Ṣāliḥ),  
And possess thyself in patience!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_028.gif)

28. Wanabbi/hum anna alm<u>a</u>a qismatun baynahum kullu shirbin
mu<u>h</u>ta<u>d</u>ar**un**

28\. And tell them that  
The water is to be  
Divided between them:  
Each one's right to drink  
Being brought forward  
(By suitable turns).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_029.gif)

29. Fan<u>a</u>daw <u>sah</u>ibahum fataAA<u>ata</u> faAAaqar**a**

29\. But they called  
To their companion,  
And he took a sword  
In hand, and hamstrung (her).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_030.gif)

30. Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

30\. Ah! how (terrible) was  
My Penalty and My Warning!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_031.gif)

31. Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan
w<u>ah</u>idatan fak<u>a</u>noo kahasheemi
almu<u>h</u>ta*<u>th</u>*ir**i**

31\. For We sent against them  
A single Mighty Blast,  
And they became  
Like the dry stubble used  
By one who pens cattle.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_032.gif)

32. Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li**l**<u>thth</u>ikri
fahal min muddakir**in**

32\. And we have indeed  
Made the Qur-ān easy  
To understand and remember:  
Then is there any that  
Will receive admonition?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_033.gif)

33. Ka<u>thth</u>abat qawmu loo<u>t</u>in bi**al**nnu<u>th</u>ur**i**

33\. The People of Lūṭ  
Rejected (his) Warning.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_034.gif)

34. Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>has</u>iban ill<u>a</u>
<u>a</u>la loo<u>t</u>in najjayn<u>a</u>hum bisa<u>h</u>ar**in**

34\. We sent against them  
A violent tornado  
With showers of stones,  
(Which destroyed them), except  
Lūṭ's household: them We  
Delivered by early Dawn,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_035.gif)

35. NiAAmatan min AAindin<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee man
shakar**a**

35\. As a Grace from Us:  
Thus do We reward  
Those who give thanks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_036.gif)

36. Walaqad an<u>th</u>arahum ba<u>t</u>shatan<u>a</u> fatam<u>a</u>raw
bi**al**nnu<u>th</u>ur**i**

36\. And (Lūṭ) did warn them  
Of Our Punishment, but  
They disputed about the Warning.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_037.gif)

37. Walaqad r<u>a</u>wadoohu AAan <u>d</u>ayfihi fa<u>t</u>amasn<u>a</u>
aAAyunahum fa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

37\. And they even sought  
To snatch away his guests  
From him, but We blinded  
Their eyes. (They heard:)  
"Now taste ye My Wrath  
And My Warning."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_038.gif)

38. Walaqad <u>s</u>abba<u>h</u>ahum bukratan AAa<u>tha</u>bun
mustaqirr**un**

38\. Early on the morrow  
An abiding Punishment  
Seized them:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_039.gif)

39. Fa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur**i**

39\. "So taste ye My Wrath  
And My Warning."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/054/054_040.gif)

40. Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li**l**<u>thth</u>ikri
fahal min muddakir**in**

40\. And We have indeed  
Made the Qur-ān easy  
To understand and remember:  
Then is there any that  
Will receive admonition?
