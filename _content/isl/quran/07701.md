# Sūra LXXVII.: Mursalāt, or Those Sent Forth.

### Section 1

![](../gif/077/077_001.gif)

1. Wa**a**lmursal<u>a</u>ti AAurf<u>a</u>**n**

1\. By the (Winds) Sent Forth  
One after another  
(To man's profit);

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_002.gif)

2. Fa**a**lAA<u>as</u>if<u>a</u>ti AAa<u>s</u>f<u>a</u>**n**

2\. Which then blow violently  
In tempestuous Gusts,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_003.gif)

3. Wa**al**nn<u>a</u>shir<u>a</u>ti nashr<u>a</u>**n**

3\. And scatter (things)  
Far and wide;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_004.gif)

4. Fa**a**lf<u>a</u>riq<u>a</u>ti farq<u>a</u>**n**

4\. Then separate them,  
One from another,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_005.gif)

5. Fa**a**lmulqiy<u>a</u>ti <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u>**n**

5\. Then spread abroad  
A Message,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_006.gif)

6. AAu<u>th</u>ran aw nu<u>th</u>ra**n**

6\. Whether of Justification  
Or of Warning;—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_007.gif)

7. Innam<u>a</u> tooAAadoona law<u>a</u>qiAA**un**

7\. Assuredly, what ye are  
Promised must come to pass.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_008.gif)

8. Fa-i<u>tha</u> a**l**nnujoomu <u>t</u>umisat

8\. Then when the stars  
Become dim;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_009.gif)

9. Wa-i<u>tha</u> a**l**ssam<u>a</u>o furijat

9\. When the heaven  
Is cleft asunder;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_010.gif)

10. Wa-i<u>tha</u> aljib<u>a</u>lu nusifat

10\. When the mountains are  
Scattered (to the winds) as dust;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_011.gif)

11. Wa-i<u>tha</u> a**l**rrusulu oqqitat

11\. And when the apostles  
Are (all) appointed a time  
(To collect);—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_012.gif)

12. Li-ayyi yawmin ojjilat

12\. For what Day are these  
(Portents) deferred?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_013.gif)

13. Liyawmi alfa<u>s</u>l**i**

13\. For the Day of Sorting out.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_014.gif)

14. Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> yawmu alfa<u>s</u>l**i**

14\. And what will explain  
To thee what is  
The Day of Sorting out?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_015.gif)

15. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

15\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_016.gif)

16. Alam nuhliki al-awwaleen**a**

16\. Did We not destroy  
The men of old  
(For their evil)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_017.gif)

17. Thumma nutbiAAuhumu al-<u>a</u>khireen**a**

17\. So shall We make  
Later (generations)  
Follow them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_018.gif)

18. Ka<u>tha</u>lika nafAAalu bi**a**lmujrimeen**a**

18\. Thus do We deal  
With men of sin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_019.gif)

19. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

19\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_020.gif)

20. Alam nakhluqkum min m<u>a</u>-in maheen**in**

20\. Have We not created  
You from a fluid  
(Held) despicable?—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_021.gif)

21. FajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu fee qar<u>a</u>rin makeen**in**

21\. The which We placed  
In a place of rest,  
Firmly fixed,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_022.gif)

22. Il<u>a</u> qadarin maAAloom**in**

22\. For a period (of gestation),  
Determined (according to need)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_023.gif)

23. Faqadarn<u>a</u> faniAAma alq<u>a</u>diroon**a**

23\. For We do determine  
(According to need); for We  
Are the Best to determine (things).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_024.gif)

24. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

24\. Ah woe, that Day!  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_025.gif)

25. Alam najAAali al-ar<u>d</u>a kif<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>**n**

25\. Have We not made  
The earth (as a place)  
To draw together

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_026.gif)

26. A<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>an waamw<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>**n**

26\. The living and the dead,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_027.gif)

27. WajaAAaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya
sh<u>a</u>mikh<u>a</u>tin waasqayn<u>a</u>kum m<u>a</u>an
fur<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>**n**

27\. And made therein  
Mountains standing firm,  
Lofty (in stature);  
And provided for you  
Water sweet (and wholesome)?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_028.gif)

28. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

28\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_029.gif)

29. In<u>t</u>aliqoo il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum bihi
tuka<u>thth</u>iboon**a**

29\. (It will be said:)  
"Depart ye to that  
Which ye used to reject  
As false!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_030.gif)

30. In<u>t</u>aliqoo il<u>a</u> *<u>th</u>*illin <u>th</u>ee
thal<u>a</u>thi shuAAab**in**

30\. "Depart ye to a Shadow  
(Of smoke ascending)  
In three columns,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_031.gif)

31. L<u>a</u> *<u>th</u>*aleelin wal<u>a</u> yughnee mina allahab**i**

31\. "(Which yields) no shade  
Of coolness, and is  
Of no use against  
The fierce Blaze.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_032.gif)

32. Innah<u>a</u> tarmee bishararin ka**a**lqa<u>s</u>r**i**

32\. "Indeed it throws about  
Sparks (huge) as Forts,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_033.gif)

33. Kaannahu jim<u>a</u>latun <u>s</u>ufr**un**

33\. "As if there were  
(A string of) yellow camels  
(Marching swiftly).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_034.gif)

34. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

34\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_035.gif)

35. H<u>atha</u> yawmu l<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqoon**a**

35\. That will be a Day  
When they shall not  
Be able to speak,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_036.gif)

36. Wal<u>a</u> yu/<u>th</u>anu lahum fayaAAta<u>th</u>iroon**a**

36\. Nor will it be  
Open to them  
To put forth pleas.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_037.gif)

37. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

37\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_038.gif)

38. H<u>atha</u> yawmu alfa<u>s</u>li jamaAAn<u>a</u>kum
wa**a**l-awwaleen**a**

38\. That will be a Day  
Of Sorting out! We shall  
Gather you together  
And those before (you)!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_039.gif)

39. Fa-in k<u>a</u>na lakum kaydun fakeedoon**i**

39\. Now, if ye have  
A trick (or plot),  
Use it against Me!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/077/077_040.gif)

40. Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen**a**

40\. Ah woe, that Day,  
To the Rejecters of Truth!
