# Sūra LVI.: Wāqi‘a, or The Inevitable Event.

### Section 1

![](../gif/056/056_001.gif)

1. I<u>tha</u> waqaAAati alw<u>a</u>qiAAat**u**

1\. When the Event Inevitable  
Cometh to pass,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_002.gif)

2. Laysa liwaqAAatih<u>a</u> k<u>ath</u>iba**tun**

2\. Then will no (soul)  
Entertain falsehood  
Concerning its coming.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_003.gif)

3. Kh<u>a</u>fi<u>d</u>atun r<u>a</u>fiAAa**tun**

3\. (Many) will it bring low;  
(Many) will it exalt;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_004.gif)

4. I<u>tha</u> rujjati al-ar<u>d</u>u rajj<u>a</u>**n**

4\. When the earth shall be  
Shaken to its depths,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_005.gif)

5. Wabussati aljib<u>a</u>lu bass<u>a</u>**n**

5\. And the mountains shall  
Be crumbled to atoms,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_006.gif)

6. Fak<u>a</u>nat hab<u>a</u>an munbathth<u>a</u>**n**

6\. Becoming dust scattered abroad,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_007.gif)

7. Wakuntum azw<u>a</u>jan thal<u>a</u>tha**tan**

7\. And ye shall be sorted out  
Into three classes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_008.gif)

8. Faa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almaymanati m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu
almaymanat**i**

8\. Then (there will be)  
The Companions of  
The Right Hand;—  
What will be  
The Companions of  
The Right Hand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_009.gif)

9. Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almash-amati m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu
almash-amat**i**

9\. And the Companions of  
The Left Hand,—  
What will be  
The Companions of  
The Left Hand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_010.gif)

10. Wa**al**ss<u>a</u>biqoona a**l**ss<u>a</u>biqoon**a**

10\. And those Foremost  
(In Faith) will be  
Foremost (in the Hereafter).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_011.gif)

11. Ol<u>a</u>-ika almuqarraboon**a**

11\. These will be  
Those Nearest to God:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_012.gif)

12. Fee jann<u>a</u>ti a**l**nnaAAeem**i**

12\. In Gardens of Bliss:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_013.gif)

13. Thullatun mina al-awwaleen**a**

13\. A number of people  
From those of old,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_014.gif)

14. Waqaleelun mina al-<u>a</u>khireena

14\. And a few from those  
Of later times.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_015.gif)

15. AAal<u>a</u> sururin maw<u>d</u>oona**tin**

15\. (They will be) on Thrones  
Encrusted (with gold  
And precious stones),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_016.gif)

16. Muttaki-eena AAalayh<u>a</u> mutaq<u>a</u>bileen**a**

16\. Reclining on them,  
Facing each other.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_017.gif)

17. Ya<u>t</u>oofu AAalayhim wild<u>a</u>nun mukhalladoon**a**

17\. Round about them will (serve)  
Youths of perpetual (freshness),

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_018.gif)

18. Bi-akw<u>a</u>bin waab<u>a</u>reeqa waka/sin min maAAeen**in**

18\. With goblets, (shining) beakers,  
And cups (filled) out of  
Clear-flowing fountains:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_019.gif)

19. L<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>addaAAoona AAanh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>
yunzifoon**a**

19\. No after-ache will they  
Receive therefrom, nor will they  
Suffer intoxication:

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_020.gif)

20. Waf<u>a</u>kihatin mimm<u>a</u> yatakhayyaroon**a**

20\. And with fruits,  
Any that they may select;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_021.gif)

21. Wala<u>h</u>mi <u>t</u>ayrin mimm<u>a</u> yashtahoon**a**

21\. And the flesh of fowls,  
Any that they may desire.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_022.gif)

22. Wa<u>h</u>oorun AAeen**un**

22\. And (there will be) Companions  
With beautiful, big,  
And lustrous eyes,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_023.gif)

23. Kaamth<u>a</u>li allu/lui almaknoon**i**

23\. Like unto Pearls  
Well-guarded.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_024.gif)

24. Jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon**a**

24\. A Reward for the Deeds  
Of their past (Life).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_025.gif)

25. L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> laghwan wal<u>a</u>
ta/theem<u>a</u>**n**

25\. No frivolity will they  
Hear therein, nor any  
Taint of ill,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_026.gif)

26. Ill<u>a</u> qeelan sal<u>a</u>man sal<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>**n**

26\. Only the saying,  
"Peace! Peace".

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_027.gif)

27. Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alyameeni m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu
alyameen**i**

27\. The Companions of  
The Right Hand,—  
What will be  
The Companions of  
The Right Hand?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_028.gif)

28. Fee sidrin makh<u>d</u>ood**in**

28\. (They will be) among  
Lote-trees without thorns,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_029.gif)

29. Wa<u>t</u>al<u>h</u>in man<u>d</u>ood**in**

29\. Among Ṭalḥ trees  
With flowers (or fruits)  
Piled one above another,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_030.gif)

30. Wa*<u>th</u>*illin mamdood**in**

30\. In shade long-extended,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_031.gif)

31. Wam<u>a</u>-in maskoob**in**

31\. By water flowing constantly,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_032.gif)

32. Waf<u>a</u>kihatin katheera**tin**

32\. And fruit in abundance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_033.gif)

33. L<u>a</u> maq<u>t</u>ooAAatin wal<u>a</u> mamnooAAa**tin**

33\. Whose season is not limited,  
Nor (supply) forbidden,

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_034.gif)

34. Wafurushin marfooAAa**tin**

34\. And on Thrones (of Dignity),  
Raised high.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_035.gif)

35. Inn<u>a</u> ansha/n<u>a</u>hunna insh<u>aa</u>**n**

35\. We have created  
(their Companions)  
Of special creation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_036.gif)

36. FajaAAaln<u>a</u>hunna abk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>**n**

36\. And made them  
Virgin-pure (and undefiled),—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_037.gif)

37. AAuruban atr<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>**n**

37\. Beloved (by nature),  
Equal in age,—

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/056/056_038.gif)

38. Li-a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alyameen**i**

38\. For the Companions  
Of the Right Hand.
