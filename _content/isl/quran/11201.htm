Sūra CXII.: Ikhlāṣ, or Purity (of Faith).

Section 1

1. Qul huwa Allahu ahadun

1. Say: He is God,
The One and Only;

2. Allahu alssamadu

2. God, the Eternal, Absolute;

3. Lam yalid walam yooladu

3. He begetteth not,
Nor is He begotten;

4. Walam yakun lahu kufuwan ahadun  

4. And there is none
Like unto Him.
