# Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.

### Section 9

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_079.gif)

79. All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-anAA<u>a</u>ma
litarkaboo minh<u>a</u> waminh<u>a</u> ta/kuloon**a**

79\. It is God who made  
Cattle for you, that ye  
May use some for riding  
And some for food;

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_080.gif)

80. Walakum feeh<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>fiAAu walitablughoo AAalayh<u>a</u>
<u>ha</u>jatan fee <u>s</u>udoorikum waAAalayh<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u>
alfulki tu<u>h</u>maloon**a**

80\. And there are (other) advantages  
In them for you (besides);  
That ye may through them  
Attain to any need  
(There may be) in your hearts;  
And on them and on ships  
Ye are carried.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_081.gif)

81. Wayureekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi faayya <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti
All<u>a</u>hi tunkiroon**a**

81\. And He shows you (always)  
His Signs: then which  
Of the Signs of God  
Will ye deny?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_082.gif)

82. Afalam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan*<u>th</u>*uroo kayfa
k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim k<u>a</u>noo
akthara minhum waashadda quwwatan wa<u>a</u>th<u>a</u>ran fee
al-ar<u>d</u>i fam<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo
yaksiboon**a**

82\. Do they not travel through  
The earth and see what  
Was the End of those  
Before them? They were  
More numerous than these  
And superior in strength  
And in the traces  
(They have left) in the land:  
Yet all that they accomplished  
Was of no profit to them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_083.gif)

83. Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi**a**lbayyin<u>a</u>ti
fari<u>h</u>oo bim<u>a</u> AAindahum mina alAAilmi wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim
m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon**a**

83\. For when their apostles  
Came to them  
With Clear Signs, they exulted  
In such knowledge (and skill)  
As they had; but  
That very (Wrath) at which  
They were wont to scoff  
Hemmed them in.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_084.gif)

84. Falamm<u>a</u> raaw ba/san<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u>
bi**A**ll<u>a</u>hi wa<u>h</u>dahu wakafarn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u>
kunn<u>a</u> bihi mushrikeen**a**

84\. But when they saw  
Our Punishment, they said:  
"We believe in God,—  
The One God—and we  
Reject the partners we used  
To join with Him."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](../gif/040/040_085.gif)

85. Falam yaku yanfaAAuhum eem<u>a</u>nuhum lamm<u>a</u> raaw
ba/san<u>a</u> sunnata All<u>a</u>hi allatee qad khalat fee
AAib<u>a</u>dihi wakhasira hun<u>a</u>lika alk<u>a</u>firoon**a**

85\. But their professing the Faith  
When they (actually) saw  
Our Punishment was not going  
To profit them.  
(Such has been) God's way  
Of dealing with His servants  
(From the most ancient times).  
And even thus did  
The rejecters of God  
Perish (utterly)!
