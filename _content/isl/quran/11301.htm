Sūra CXIII: Falaq, or The Dawn.

Section 1

1. Qul aAAoothu birabbi alfalaqi

1. Say: I seek refuge
With the Lord of the Dawn,

2. Min sharri ma khalaqa

2. From the mischief
Of created things;

3. Wamin sharri ghasiqin itha waqaba

3. From the mischief
Of Darkness as it overspreads;

4. Wamin sharri alnnaffathati fee alAAuqadi

4. From the mischief
Of those who practise
Secret Arts:

5. Wamin sharri hasidin itha hasada

5. And from the mischief
Of the envious one
As he practises envy.
