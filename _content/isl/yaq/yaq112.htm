Sūra CXII.

Ikhlāṣ, or Purity (of Faith).

In the name of God, Most Gracious,
Most Merciful.

1. Say: He is God,
The One and Only;

2. God, the Eternal, Absolute;

3. He begetteth not,
Nor is He begotten;

4. And there is none
Like unto Him.
