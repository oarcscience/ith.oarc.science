### Sūra CXIII

### *Falaq,* or The Dawn.

*In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.*

1\. Say: I seek refuge  
With the Lord of the Dawn,

2\. From the mischief  
Of created things;

3\. From the mischief  
Of Darkness as it overspreads;

4\. From the mischief  
Of those who practise  
Secret Arts:

5\. And from the mischief  
Of the envious one  
As he practises envy.
