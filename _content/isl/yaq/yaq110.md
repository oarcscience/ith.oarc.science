### Sūra CX.

### *Naṣr,* or Help.

*In the name of God, Most Gracious,  
Most Merciful.*

1\. When comes the Help  
Of God, and Victory,

2\. And thou dost see  
The People enter God's Religion  
In crowds,

3\. Celebrate the Praises  
Of thy Lord, and pray  
For His Forgiveness:  
For He is Oft-Returning  
(In Grace and Mercy).
