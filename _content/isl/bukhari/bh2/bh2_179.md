### Hadith 2:180

*Narrated Abu Salma:*

I saw Abu Huraira reciting Idha-Sama' un-Shaqqat and he prostrated
during its recitation. I asked Abu Huraira, "Didn't I see you
prostrating?" Abu Huraira said, "Had I not seen the Prophet prostrating,
I would not have prostrated."
