### Hadith 2:817

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet offered the Zuhr, 'Asr, Maghrib and 'Isha' prayers and slept
for a while at a place called Al-Mahassab and then he rode towards the
Ka'ba and performed Tawaf (al-Wada').
