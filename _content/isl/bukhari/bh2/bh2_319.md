### Hadith 2:317

*Narrated*

Narrated' Abdullah:

Once Allah's Apostle offered five Rakat in the Zuhr prayer, and somebody
asked him whether there was some increase in the prayer. Allah's Apostle
said, "What is that?" He said, "You have offered five Rakat." So Allah's
Apostle performed two prostrations of Sahu after Taslim.
