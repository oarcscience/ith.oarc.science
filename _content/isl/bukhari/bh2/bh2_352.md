### Hadith 2:351

*Narrated Ibn Sirin:*

Um 'Atiyya (an Ansari woman who gave the pledge of allegiance to the
Prophet ) came to Basra to visit her son, but she could not find him.
She narrated to us, "The Prophet came to us while we were giving bath to
his (dead) daughter, he said: 'Wash her three times, five times or more,
if you think it necessary, with water and Sidr, and last of all put
camphor, and when you finish, notify me.' " Um 'Atiyya added, "After
finishing, we informed him and he gave us his waist sheet and told us to
shroud her in it and did not say more than that."
