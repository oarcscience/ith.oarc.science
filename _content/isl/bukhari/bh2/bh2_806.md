### Hadith 2:804

*Narrated 'Abdur-Rahman bin Yazid:*

When 'Abdullah, reached the big Jamra (i.e. Jamrat-ul-Aqaba) he kept the
Ka'ba on the left side and Mina on his right side and threw seven
pebbles (at the Jamra) and said, "The one on whom Surat-al-Baqara was
revealed (i.e. the Prophet) had done the Rami similarly."
