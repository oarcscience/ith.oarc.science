### Hadith 2:281

*Narrated Quza'a:*

I heard Abu Said saying four words. He said, "I heard the Prophet
(saying the following narrative)." He had participated in twelve holy
battles with the Prophet.

except for three Mosques i.e. Al-Masjid-AI-Haram, the Mosque of Allah's
Apostle , and the Mosque of Al-Aqsa, (Mosque of Jerusalem)."
