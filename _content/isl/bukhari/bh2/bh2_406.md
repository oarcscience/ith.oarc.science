### Hadith 2:405

*Narrated Ash-Shaibani:*

Ash Sha'bi said, "I was informed by a man who had seen the Prophet going
to a grave that was separate from the other graves and he aligned the
people in rows and said four Takbir." I said, "O Abu 'Amr! who narrated
(that) to you"? He said, "Ibn Abbas. "
