### Hadith 2:378

*Narrated Al-Mughira:*

I heard the Prophet saying, "Ascribing false things to me is not like
ascribing false things to anyone else. Whosoever tells a lie against me
intentionally then surely let him occupy his seat in Hell-Fire." I heard
the Prophet saying, "The deceased who is wailed over is tortured for
that wailing."
