### Hadith 2:358

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

A man fell from his Mount and died while he was with the Prophet at
'Arafat. The Prophet said, "Wash him with water and Sidr and shroud him
in two pieces of cloth and neither perfume him nor cover his head, for
he will be resurrected on the Day of Resurrection saying, 'Labbaik'."
