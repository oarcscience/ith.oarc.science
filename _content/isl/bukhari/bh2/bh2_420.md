### Hadith 2:419

*Narrated Talha bin 'Abdullah bin 'Auf:*

I offered the funeral prayer behind Ibn Abbas and he recited Al-Fatiha
and said, "You should know that it (i.e. recitation of Al-Fatiha) is the
tradition of the Prophet Muhammad.
