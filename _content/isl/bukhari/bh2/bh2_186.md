### Hadith 2:187

*Narrated Yahya bin Ishaq:*

I heard Anas saying, "We travelled with the Prophet from Medina to Mecca
and offered two Rakat (for every prayer) till we returned to Medina." I
said, "Did you stay for a while in Mecca?" He replied, "We stayed in
Mecca for ten days."
