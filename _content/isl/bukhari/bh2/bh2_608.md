### Hadith 2:606

*Narrated Ibn Umar:*

When these two towns (Basra and Kufa) were captured, the people went to
'Umar and said, "O the Chief of the faithful believers! The Prophet
fixed Qarn as the Miqat for the people of Najd, it is beyond our way and
it is difficult for us to pass through it." He said, "Take as your Miqat
a place situated opposite to Qarn on your usual way. So, he fixed
Dhatu-Irq (as their Miqat)."
