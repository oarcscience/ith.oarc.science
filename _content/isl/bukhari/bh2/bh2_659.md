### Hadith 2:657

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

On the Day of the Conquest of Mecca, Allah's Apostle said, "Allah has
made this town a sanctuary. Its thorny bushes should not be cut, its
game should not be chased, and its fallen things should not be picked up
except by one who would announce it publicly."
