### Hadith 2:691

*Narrated Ibn Abbas:*

The Prophet arrived at Mecca and performed Tawaf of the Kaba and Sa'i
between Safa and Marwa, but he did not go near the Kaba after his Tawaf
till he returned from Arafat.
