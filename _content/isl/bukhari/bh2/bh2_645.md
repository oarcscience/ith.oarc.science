### Hadith 2:643

*Narrated Nafi:*

On reaching the sanctuary of Mecca, Ibn 'Umar used to stop, reciting
Talbiya and then he would pass the night at Dhi-Tuwa and then offer the
Fajr prayer and take a bath. He used to say that the Prophet used to do
the same.
