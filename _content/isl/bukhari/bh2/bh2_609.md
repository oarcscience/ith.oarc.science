### Hadith 2:607

*Narrated Nafi :*

'Abdullah bin 'Umar' said, "Allah's Apostle made his camel sit (i.e. he
dismounted) at Al-Batha' in Dhul-Hulaifa and offered the prayer."
'Abdullah bin 'Umar used to do the same.
