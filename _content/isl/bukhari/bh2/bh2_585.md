### Hadith 2:583

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar*

The Prophet ordered (Muslims) to give one Sa' of dates or one Sa' of
barley as Zakat-ul-Fitr. The people rewarded two Mudds of wheat as equal
to that.
