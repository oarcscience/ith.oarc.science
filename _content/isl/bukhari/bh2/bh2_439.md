### Hadith 2:438

*Narrated Anas:*

A young Jewish boy used to serve the Prophet and he became sick. So the
Prophet went to visit him. He sat near his head and asked him to embrace
Islam. The boy looked at his father, who was sitting there; the latter
told him to obey Abu-l-Qasim and the boy embraced Islam. The Prophet
came out saying: "Praises be to Allah Who saved the boy from the
Hell-fire."
