### Hadith 2:546

*Narrated Zainab,:*

(the daughter of Um Salama) My mother said, "O Allah's Apostle! Shall I
receive a reward if I spend for the sustenance of Abu Salama's
offspring, and in fact they are also my sons?" The Prophet replied,
"Spend on them and you will get a reward for what you spend on them."
