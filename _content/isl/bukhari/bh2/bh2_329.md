### Hadith 2:327

*Narrated Asma':*

I went to 'Aisha and she was standing praying and the people, too, were
standing (praying). So I said, "What is the matter with the people?" She
beckoned with her head towards the sky.

I said, "(Is there) a sign?" She nodded intending to say, "Yes."
