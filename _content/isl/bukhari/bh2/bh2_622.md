### Hadith 2:620

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet offered four Rakat of the Zuhr prayer in Medina and two
Rakat of the 'Asr prayer in Dhul-Hulaifa and I heard them (the
companions of the Prophet) reciting Talbiya together loudly to the
extent of shouting.
