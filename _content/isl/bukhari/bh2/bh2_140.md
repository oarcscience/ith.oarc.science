### Hadith 2:141

*Narrated Anas bin Malik*

The Prophet never raised his hands for any invocation except for that of
Istisqa' and he used to raise them so much that the whiteness of his
armpits became visible. (Note: It may be that Anas did not see the
Prophet raising his hands, but it is narrated that the Prophet used to
raise his hands for invocations other than Istisqa. See Hadith No. 807 &
808 and also see Hadith No. 612, Vol. 5).
