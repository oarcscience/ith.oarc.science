### Hadith 2:176

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Masud:*

The Prophet recited Surat-an-Najm (53) and prostrated while reciting it
and all the people prostrated and a man amongst the people took a
handful of stones or earth and raised it to his face and said, "This is
sufficient for me. Later on I saw him killed as a Nobel ever. "
