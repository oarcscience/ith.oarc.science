### Hadith 2:295

*Narrated Abu Huraira :*

The Prophet said, "The saying 'Sub Han Allah' is for men and clapping is
for women." (If something happens in the prayer, the men can invite the
attention of the Imam by saying "Sub Han Allah". And women, by clapping
their hands).
