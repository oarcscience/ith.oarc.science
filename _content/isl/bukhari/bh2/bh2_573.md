### Hadith 2:572

*Narrated Anas:*

Some meat was presented to the Prophet (p.b.u.h) and it had been given
to Barira (the freed slave-girl of Aisha) in charity. He said, "This
meat is a thing of charity for Barira but it is a gift for us."
