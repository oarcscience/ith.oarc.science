### Hadith 2:54

*Narrated Anas:*

While the Prophet was delivering the Khutba on a Friday, a man stood up
and said, "O, Allah's Apostle! The livestock and the sheep are dying, so
pray to Allah for rain." So he (the Prophet) raised both his hands and
invoked Allah (for it).
