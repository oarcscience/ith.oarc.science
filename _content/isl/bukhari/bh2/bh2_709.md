### Hadith 2:707

*Narrated Nafi':*

Ibn 'Umar said, "When Allah's Apostle performed the first Tawaf he did
Ramal in the first three rounds and then walked in the remaining four
rounds (of Tawaf of the Ka'ba), where as in performing Tawaf between
Safa and Marwa he used to run in the midst of the rain-water passage," I
asked Nafi', "Did 'Abdullah (bin 'Umar) use to walk steadily on reaching
the Yemenite Corner?" He replied, "No, unless people were crowded at the
Corner; otherwise he would not leave it without touching it."
