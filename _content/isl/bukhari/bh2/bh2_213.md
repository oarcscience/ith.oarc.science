### Hadith 2:214

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Allah's Apostle prayed in his house while sitting during his illness and
the people prayed behind him standing and he pointed to them to sit
down. When he had finished the prayer, he said, "The Imam is to be
followed and so when he bows you should bow; and when he lifts his head
you should also do the same."
