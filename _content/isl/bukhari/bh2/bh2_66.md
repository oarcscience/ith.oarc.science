### Hadith 2:67

*Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:*

On the day of the Khandaq Umar came, cursing the disbelievers of Quraish
and said, "O Allah's Apostle! I have not offered the 'Asr prayer and the
sun has set." The Prophet replied, "By Allah! I too, have not offered
the prayer yet. "The Prophet then went to Buthan, performed ablution and
performed the 'Asr prayer after the sun had set and then offered the
Maghrib prayer after it."
