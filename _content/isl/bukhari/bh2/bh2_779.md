### Hadith 2:777

*Narrated Ibn Juraij:*

'Ata' said, "I heard Jabir bin 'Abdullah saying, 'We never ate the meat
of the Budn for more than three days of Mina. Later, the Prophet gave us
permission by saying: 'Eat and take (meat) with you. So we ate (some)
and took (some) with us.' " I asked 'Ata', "Did Jabir say (that they
went on eating the meat) till they reached Medina?" 'Ata' replied, "No."
