### Hadith 3:702

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

That he used to give his verdict regarding the male or female slaves
owned by more than one master, one of whom may manumit his share of the
slave. Ibn 'Umar used to say in such a case, "The manumitted should
manumit the slave completely if he has sufficient money to pay the rest
of the price of that slave (which is to be justly estimated) and the
other share-holders are to take the price of their shares and the slave
is freed (released from slavery)." Ibn 'Umar narrated this verdict from
the Prophet.
