### Hadith 3:46

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "Whoever performs Hajj to this Ka'ba and does not
approach his wife for sexual relations nor commit sins (while performing
Hajj), he will come out as sinless as a new-born child, (just delivered
by his mother)."
