### Hadith 3:474

*Narrated Tawus:*

Ibn 'Abbas said, "The Prophet forbade the meeting of caravans (on the
way) and ordained that no townsman is permitted to sell things on behalf
of a bedouin." I asked Ibn 'Abbas, "What is the meaning of his saying,
'No townsman is permitted to sell things on behalf of a bedouin.' " He
replied, "He should not work as a broker for him."
