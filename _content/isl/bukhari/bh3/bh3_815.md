### Hadith 3:816

*Narrated 'Urwa bin Az-Zubair:*

A woman committed theft in the Ghazwa of the Conquest (of Mecca) and she
was taken to the Prophet who ordered her hand to be cut off. 'Aisha
said, "Her repentance was perfect and she was married (later) and used
to come to me (after that) and I would present her needs to Allah's
Apostle."
