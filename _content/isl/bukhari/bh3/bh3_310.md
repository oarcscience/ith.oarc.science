### Hadith 3:313

*Narrated Abu Qatada:*

We set out with Allah's Apostle in the year of Hunain, (the Prophet gave
me an armor). I sold that armor and bought a garden in the region of the
tribe of Bani Salama and that was the first property I got after
embracing Islam.
