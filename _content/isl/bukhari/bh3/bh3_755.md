### Hadith 3:756

*Narrated 'Azra bin Thabit Al-Ansari:*

When I went to Thumama bin 'Abdullah, he gave me some perfume and said
that Anas would not reject the gifts of perfume. Anas said: The Prophet
used not to reject the gifts of perfume.
