### Hadith 3:795

*Narrated Anas:*

Once the people of Medina were frightened, so the Prophet borrowed a
horse from Abu Talha called Al-Mandub, and rode it. When he came back he
said, "We have not seen anything (to be afraid of), but the horse was
very fast (having an energy as inexhaustible as the water of the sea)."
