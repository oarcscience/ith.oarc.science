### Hadith 3:410

*Narrated Humaid:*

Anas said, "The Prophet forbade the selling of dates till they were
almost ripe." We asked Anas, "What does 'almost ripe' mean?" He replied,
"They get red and yellow. The Prophet added, 'If Allah destroyed the
fruits present on the trees, what right would the seller have to take
the money of his brother (somebody else)?' "
