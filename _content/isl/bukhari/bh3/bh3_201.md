### Hadith 3:204

*Narrated Mutarrif from 'Imran Ibn Husain:*

That the Prophet asked him (Imran) or asked a man and Imran was
listening, "O Abu so-and-so! Have you fasted the last days of this
month?" (The narrator thought that he said, "the month of Ramadan"). The
man replied, "No, O Allah's Apostle!" The Prophet said to him, "When you
finish your fasting (of Ramadan) fast two days (in Shawwal)." Through
another series of narrators 'Imran said, "The Prophet said, '(Have you
fasted) the last days of Sha'ban?"
