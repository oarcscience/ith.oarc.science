### Hadith 3:32

*Narrated Zaid bin Aslam from his father:*

I was with Ibn 'Umar on the way to Mecca, and he got the news that
Safiya bint Abu Ubaid was seriously ill. So, he hastened his pace, and
when the twilight disappeared, he dismounted and offered the Maghrib and
'Isha' prayers together. Then he said, "I saw that whenever the Prophet
had to hasten when traveling, he would delay the Maghrib prayer and join
them together (i.e. offer the Maghrib and the Isha prayers together)."
