### Hadith 3:650

*Narrated Jabir:*

The Prophet entered the Mosque, and I too went there after tying the
camel at the pavement of the Mosque. I said (to the Prophet ), "This is
your camel." He came out and started examining the camel and said, "Both
the camel and its price are for you."
