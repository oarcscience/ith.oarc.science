### Hadith 3:515

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Whoever keeps a dog, one Qirat of the reward of
his good deeds is deducted daily, unless the dog is used for guarding a
farm or cattle." Abu Huraira (in another narration) said from the
Prophet, "unless it is used for guarding sheep or farms, or for dog for
guarding cattle or for hunting."
