### Hadith 3:507

*Narrated 'Amr:*

Concerning the Waqf of 'Umar: It was not sinful of the trustee (of the
Waqf) to eat or provide his friends from it, provided the trustee had no
intention of collecting fortune (for himself). Ibn 'Umar was the manager
of the trust of 'Umar and he used to give presents from it to those with
whom he used to stay at Mecca.
