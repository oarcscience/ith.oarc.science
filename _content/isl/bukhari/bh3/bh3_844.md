### Hadith 3:845

*Narrated Um Salama:*

Once Allah's Apostle said, "You people present your cases to me and some
of you may be more eloquent and persuasive in presenting their argument.
So, if I give some one's right to another (wrongly) because of the
latter's (tricky) presentation of the case, I am really giving him a
piece of fire; so he should not take it."
