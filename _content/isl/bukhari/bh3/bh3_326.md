### Hadith 3:329

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Allah's Apostle said, "An army will invade the Ka'ba and when the
invaders reach Al-Baida', all the ground will sink and swallow the whole
army." I said, "O Allah's Apostle! How will they sink into the ground
while amongst them will be their markets (the people who worked in
business and not invaders) and the people not belonging to them?" The
Prophet replied, "all of those people will sink but they will be
resurrected and judged according to their intentions."
