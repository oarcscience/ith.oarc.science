### Hadith 3:259

*Narrated Ibn 'Umar:*

that 'Umar had vowed in the Pre-lslamic period to perform Itikaf in
Al-Masjid-al-Haram. (A sub-narrator thinks that 'Umar vowed to perform
Itikaf for one night.) Allah's Apostle said to 'Umar, "Fulfill your
vow."
