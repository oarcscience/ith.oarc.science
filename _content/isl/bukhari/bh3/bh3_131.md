### Hadith 3:134

*Narrated Um Salama:*

The Prophet vowed to keep aloof from his wives for a period of one
month, and after the completion of 29 days he went either in the morning
or in the afternoon to his wives. Someone said to him "You vowed that
you would not go to your wives for one month." He replied, "The month is
of 29 days."
