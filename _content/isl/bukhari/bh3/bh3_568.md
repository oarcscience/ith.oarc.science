### Hadith 3:569

*Narrated Rafi 'bin Khadij and Sahl bin Al Hathma:*

Allah's Apostle forbade the sale of Muzabana, i.e. selling of fruits for
fruits, except in the case of 'Araya; he allowed the owners of 'Araya
such kind of sale.
