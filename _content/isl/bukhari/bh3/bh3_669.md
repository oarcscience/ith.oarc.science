### Hadith 3:670

*Narrated Jabala:*

"While at Medina we were struck with famine. Ibn Az-Zubair used to
provide us with dates as our food. Ibn 'Umar used to pass by us and say,
"Don't eat two dates together at a time as the Prophet has forbidden
eating two dates together at a time (in a gathering) unless one takes
the permission of one's companion brother."
