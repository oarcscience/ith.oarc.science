### Hadith 3:593

*Narrated 'Abdullah:*

I heard a man reciting a verse (of the Holy Qur'an) but I had heard the
Prophet reciting it differently. So, I caught hold of the man by the
hand and took him to Allah's Apostle who said, "Both of you are right."
Shu'ba, the sub-narrator said, "I think he said to them, "Don't differ,
for the nations before you differed and perished (because of their
differences). "
