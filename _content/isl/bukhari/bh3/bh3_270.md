### Hadith 3:273

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

Some people said, "O Allah's Apostle! Meat is brought to us by some
people and we are not sure whether the name of Allah has been mentioned
on it or not (at the time of slaughtering the animals)." Allah's Apostle
said (to them), "Mention the name of Allah and eat it."
