### Hadith 3:432

*Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri:*

that while he was sitting with Allah's Apostle he said, "O Allah's
Apostle! We get female captives as our share of booty, and we are
interested in their prices, what is your opinion about coitus interrupt
us?" The Prophet said, "Do you really do that? It is better for you not
to do it. No soul that which Allah has destined to exist, but will
surely come into existence.
