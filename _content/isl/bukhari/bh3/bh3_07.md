### Hadith 3:8

*Narrated Hammam:*

The Prophet performed four 'Umra (three) in Dhi-l-Qa'da except the (one)
'Umra which he performed with his Hajj: His 'Umra from Al-hudaibiya, and
the one of the following year, and the one from Al-Jr'rana where he
distributed the booty (of the battle) of Hunain, and another 'Umra with
his Hajj.
