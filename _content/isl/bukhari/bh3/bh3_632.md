### Hadith 3:633

*Narrated Abu Salama:*

That there was a dispute between him and some people (about a piece of
land). When he told 'Aisha about it, she said, "O Abu Salama! Avoid
taking the land unjustly, for the Prophet said, 'Whoever usurps even one
span of the land of somebody, his neck will be encircled with it down
the seven earths."
