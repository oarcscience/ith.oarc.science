### Hadith 3:311

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

'Ukaz, Majanna and Dhul-Majaz were markets in the Pre-lslamic period.
When the people embraced Islam they considered it a sin to trade there.
So, the following Holy Verse came:-- 'There is no harm for you if you
seek of the bounty of your Lord (Allah) in the Hajj season." (2.198) Ibn
'Abbas recited it like this.
