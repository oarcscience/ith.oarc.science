### Hadith 3:749

*Narrated Said bin Jubair:*

Ibn Abbas said: Um Hufaid, Ibn 'Abbas's aunt sent some dried yogurt
(butter free), ghee (butter) and a mastigar to the Prophet as a gift.
The Prophet ate the dried yogurt and butter but left the mastigar
because he disliked it. Ibn 'Abbas said, "The mastigar was eaten at the
table of Allah's Apostle and if it had been illegal to eat, it could not
have been eaten at the table of Allah's Apostle."
