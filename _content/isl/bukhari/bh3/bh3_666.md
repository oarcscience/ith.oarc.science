### Hadith 3:667

*Narrated Anas:*

that Abu Bakr As-Siddiq wrote to him the law of Zakat which was made
obligatory by Allah's Apostle. He wrote: 'Partners possessing joint
property (sheep) have to pay its Zakat equally.
