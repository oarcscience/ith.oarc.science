### Hadith 3:789

*Narrated Asma' bint Abu Bakr:*

My mother came to me during the lifetime of Allah's Apostle and she was
a pagan. I said to Allah's Apostle (seeking his verdict), "My mother has
come to me and she desires to receive a reward from me, shall I keep
good relations with her?" The Prophet said, "Yes, keep good relation
with her. "
