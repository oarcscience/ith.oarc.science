### Hadith 3:314

*Narrated Abu Musa:*

Allah's Apostle said, "The example of a good companion (who sits with
you) in comparison with a bad one, is I like that of the musk seller and
the blacksmith's bellows (or furnace); from the first you would either
buy musk or enjoy its good smell while the bellows would either burn
your clothes or your house, or you get a bad nasty smell thereof."
