### Hadith 3:666

*Narrated Abu Musa:*

The Prophet said, "When the people of Ash'ari tribe ran short of food
during the holy battles, or the food of their families in Medina ran
short, they would collect all their remaining food in one sheet and then
distribute it among themselves equally by measuring it with a bowl. So,
these people are from me, and I am from them."
