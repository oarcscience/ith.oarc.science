### Hadith 3:68

*Narrated Abdullah:*

Allah's Apostle was asked what sort of clothes a Muhrim should wear. He
replied, "He should not wear a shirt, turbans, trousers, a hooded cloak,
or a dress perfumed with saffron or Wars; and if slippers are not
available he can wear Khuffs but he should cut them so that they reach
below the ankles.
