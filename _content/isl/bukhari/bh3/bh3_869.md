### Hadith 3:870

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "There is a Sadaqa to be given for every joint of
the human body; and for every day on which the sun rises there is a
reward of a Sadaqa (i.e. charitable gift) for the one who establishes
justice among people."
