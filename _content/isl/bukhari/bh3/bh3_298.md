### Hadith 3:301

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abu Aufa:*

A man displayed some goods in the market and swore by Allah that he had
been offered so much for that, that which was not offered, and he said
so, so as to cheat a Muslim. On that occasion the following Verse was
revealed: "Verily! Those who purchase a small gain at the cost of
Allah's covenant and their oaths (They shall have no portion in the
Hereafter ..etc.)' (3.77)
