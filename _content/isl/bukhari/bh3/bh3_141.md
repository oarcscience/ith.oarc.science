### Hadith 3:144

*Narrated Anas:*

Zaid bin Thabit said, "We took the Suhur with the Prophet . Then he
stood for the prayer." I asked, "What was the interval between the Suhur
and the Adhan?" He replied, "The interval was sufficient to recite fifty
verses of the Quran."
