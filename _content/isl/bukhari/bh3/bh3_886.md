### Hadith 3:887

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle forbade (1) the meeting of the caravan (of goods) on the
way, (2) and that a residing person buys for a bedouin, (3) and that a
woman stipulates the divorce of the wife of the would-be husband, (4)
and that a man tries to cause the cancellation of a bargain concluded by
another. He also forbade An-Najsh (see Hadith 824) and that one
withholds the milk in the udder of the animal so that he may deceive
people on selling it.
