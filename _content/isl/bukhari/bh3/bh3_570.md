### Hadith 3:571

*Narrated Al-Amash:*

When we were with Ibrahim, we talked about mortgaging in deals of Salam.
Ibrahim narrated from Aswad that 'Aisha had said, "The Prophet bought
some foodstuff on credit from a Jew and mortgaged an iron armor to him."
