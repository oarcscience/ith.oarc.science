### Hadith 3:130

*Narrated Abdullah bin Umar:*

Allah's Apostle mentioned Ramadan and said, "Do not fast unless you see
the crescent (of Ramadan), and do not give up fasting till you see the
crescent (of Shawwal), but if the sky is overcast (if you cannot see
it), then act on estimation (i.e. count Sha'ban as 30 days)."
