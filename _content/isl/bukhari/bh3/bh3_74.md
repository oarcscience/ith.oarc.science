### Hadith 3:76

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

A man was in the company of the Prophet and his she-camel crushed his
neck while he was in a state of Ihram and he died Allah's Apostle said,
"Wash him with water and Sidr and shroud him in his two garments;
neither perfume him nor cover his head, for he will be resurrected on
the Day of Resurrection, reciting Talbiya."
