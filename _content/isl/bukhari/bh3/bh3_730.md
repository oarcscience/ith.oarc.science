### Hadith 3:731

*Narrated Abu Huraira and Zaid bin Khalid:*

The Prophet said, "If a slave-girl (Ama) commits illegal sexual
intercourse, scourge her; if she does it again, scourge her again; if
she repeats it, scourge her again." The narrator added that on the third
or the fourth offence, the Prophet said, "Sell her even for a hair
rope."
