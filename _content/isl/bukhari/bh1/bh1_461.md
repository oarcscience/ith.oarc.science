### Hadith 1:459

*Narrated Al-Sa'ib bin Yazid:*

I was standing in the mosque and somebody threw a gravel at me. I looked
and found that he was 'Umar bin Al-Khattab. He said to me, "Fetch those
two men to me." When I did, he said to them, "Who are you? (Or) where do
you come from?" They replied, "We are from Ta'if." 'Umar said, "Were you
from this city (Medina) I would have punished you for raising your
voices in the mosque of Allah's Apostle
