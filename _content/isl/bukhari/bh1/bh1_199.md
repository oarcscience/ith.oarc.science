### Hadith 1:199

*Narrated Thabit:*

Anas said, "The Prophet asked for water and a tumbler with a broad base
and no so deep, containing a small quantity of water, was brought to him
whereby he put his fingers in it." Anas further said, ' noticed the
water springing out from amongst his fingers." Anas added, ' estimated
that the people who performed ablution with it numbered between seventy
to eighty."
