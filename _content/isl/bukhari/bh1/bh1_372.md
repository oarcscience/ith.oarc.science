### Hadith 1:372

*Narrated 'Uqba bin 'Amir:*

The Prophet was given a silken Farruj as a present. He wore it while
praying. When he had finished his prayer, he took it off violently as if
with a strong aversion to it and said, "It is not the dress of
Allah-fearing pious people."
