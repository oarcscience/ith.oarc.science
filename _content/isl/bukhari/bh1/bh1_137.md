### Hadith 1:137

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "The prayer of a person who does ,Hadath (passes,
urine, stool or wind) is not accepted till he performs (repeats) the
ablution." A person from Hadaramout asked Abu Huraira, "What is
'Hadath'?" Abu Huraira replied, " 'Hadath' means the passing of wind
from the anus."
