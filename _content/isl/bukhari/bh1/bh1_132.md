### Hadith 1:132

*Narrated Um Salama:*

Um-Sulaim came to Allah's Apostle and said, "Verily, Allah is not shy of
(telling you) the truth. Is it necessary for a woman to take a bath
after she has a wet dream (nocturnal sexual discharge?) The Prophet
replied, "Yes, if she notices a discharge." Um Salama, then covered her
face and asked, "O Allah's Apostle! Does a woman get a discharge?" He
replied, "Yes, let your right hand be in dust (An Arabic expression you
say to a person when you contradict his statement meaning "you will not
achieve goodness"), and that is why the son resembles his mother."
