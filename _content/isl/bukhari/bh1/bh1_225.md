### Hadith 1:225

*Narrated Hudhaifa':*

The Prophet and I walked till we reached the dumps of some people. He
stood, as any one of you stands, behind a wall and urinated. I went
away, but he beckoned me to come. So I approached him and stood near his
back till he finished.
