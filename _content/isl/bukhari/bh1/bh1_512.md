### Hadith 1:510

*Narrated Abu Huraira and 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

Allah's Apostle said, "If it is very hot, then pray the Zuhr prayer when
it becomes (a bit) cooler, as the severity of the heat is from the
raging of the Hell-fire."
