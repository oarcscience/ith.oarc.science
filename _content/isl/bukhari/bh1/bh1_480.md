### Hadith 1:478

*Narrated 'Aun bin Abi Juhaifa:*

that he had heard his father saying, "Allah's Apostle came to us at
mid-day and water was brought for his ablution. He performed ablution
and led us in Zuhr and 'Asr prayers with an 'Anza planted in front of
him (as a Sutra), while women and donkeys were passing beyond it."
