### Hadith 1:643

*Narrated Ja'far bin 'Amr bin Umaiya:*

My father said, "I saw Allah's Apostle eating a piece of meat from the
shoulder of a sheep and he was called for the prayer. He stood up, put
down the knife and prayed but did not perform ablutilon.''
