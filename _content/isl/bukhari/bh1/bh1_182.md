### Hadith 1:182

*Narrated Al-Mughira bin Shu'ba:*

I was in the company of Allah's Apostle on one of the journeys and he
went out to answer the call of nature (and after he finished) I poured
water and he performed ablution; he washed his face, forearms and passed
his wet hand over his head and over the two Khuff, (leather socks).
