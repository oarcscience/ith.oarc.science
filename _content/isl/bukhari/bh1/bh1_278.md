### Hadith 1:278

*Narrated Um Hani bint Abi Talib:*

I went to Allah's Apostle in the year of the conquest of Mecca and found
him taking a bath while Fatima was screening him. The Prophet asked,
"Who is it?" I replied, "I am Um-Hani."
