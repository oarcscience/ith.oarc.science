### Hadith 1:725

*Narrated Jabir bin Samura:*

Sa'd said, "I used to pray with them a prayer similar to that of Allah's
Apostle (the prayer of Zuhr and 'Asr) reducing nothing from them. I used
to prolong the first two Rakat and shorten the last two Rak'at." 'Umar
said to Sa'd "This was what we thought about you."
