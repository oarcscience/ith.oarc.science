### Hadith 1:363

*Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri*

Allah's Apostle forbade Ishtimal-As-Samma' (wrapping one's body with a
garment so that one cannot raise its end or take one's hand out of it).
He also forbade Al-Ihtiba' (sitting on buttocks with knees close to
abdomen and feet apart with the hands circling the knees) while wrapping
oneself with a single garment, without having a part of it over the
private parts.
