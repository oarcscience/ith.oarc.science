### Hadith 1:134

*Narrated 'Ali:*

I used to get the emotional urethral discharge frequently so I requested
Al-Miqdad to ask the Prophet about it. Al-Miqdad asked him and he
replied, "One has to perform ablution (after it)." (See Hadith No. 269).
