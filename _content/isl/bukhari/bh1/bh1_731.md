### Hadith 1:729

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi Qatada:*

My father said, "The Prophet used to recite Al-Fatiha along with another
Sura in the first two Rakat of the Zuhr and the 'Asr prayers and at
times a t verse or so was audible to us."
