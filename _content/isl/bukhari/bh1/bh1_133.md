### Hadith 1:133

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

Once Allah's Apostle said, "Amongst the trees there is a tree, the
leaves of which do not fall and is like a Muslim, tell me the name of
that tree." Everybody started thinking about the trees of the desert
areas and I thought of the date-palm tree but felt shy (to answer). The
others asked, "O Allah's Apostle! inform us of it." He replied, "it is
the date-palm tree." I told my father what had come to my mind and on
that he said, "Had you said it I would have preferred it to such and
such a thing that I might possess."
