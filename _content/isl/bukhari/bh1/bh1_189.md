### Hadith 1:189

*Narrated As-Sa'ib bin Yazid:*

My aunt took me to the Prophet and said, "O Allah's Apostle! This son of
my sister has got a disease in his legs." So he passed his hands on my
head and prayed for Allah's blessings for me; then he performed ablution
and I drank from the remaining water. I stood behind him and saw the
seal of Prophethood between his shoulders, and it was like the
"Zir-al-Hijla" (means the button of a small tent, but some said 'egg of
a partridge.' etc.)
