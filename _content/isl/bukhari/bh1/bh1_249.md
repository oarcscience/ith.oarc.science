### Hadith 1:249

*Narrated Maimuna:*

(the wife of the Prophet) Allah's Apostle performed ablution like that
for the prayer but did not wash his feet. He washed off the discharge
from his private parts and then poured water over his body. He withdrew
his feet from that place (the place where he took the bath) and then
washed them. And that was his way of taking the bath of Janaba.
