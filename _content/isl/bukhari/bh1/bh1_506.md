### Hadith 1:504

*Narrated Ibn Mas'ud:*

A man kissed a woman (unlawfully) and then went to the Prophet and
informed him. Allah revealed:

And offer prayers perfectly At the two ends of the day And in some hours
of the night (i.e. the five compulsory prayers). Verily! good deeds
remove (annul) the evil deeds (small sins) (11.114). The man asked
Allah's Apostle, "Is it for me?" He said, "It is for all my followers."
