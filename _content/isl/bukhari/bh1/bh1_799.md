### Hadith 1:797

*Narrated 'Abdullah:*

When we prayed with the Prophet we used to say, "Peace be on Allah from
His slaves and peace be on so and so." The Prophet said, "Don't say
As-Salam be on Allah, for He Himself is As-Salam, but say, 'At-tahiyatu
lil-lahi was-salawatu wat-taiyibatu. As-salamu 'Alaika aiyuhan-Nabiyu
warahmatu-l-lahi wa barakatuhu. As-salamu 'alaina wa 'ala
ibadillahis-salihin. (If you say this then it will be for all the slaves
in heaven or between heaven and earth). Ashhadu an la-ilaha illallahu wa
ashhadu anna Muhammadan 'Abduhu wa Rasuluhu.' Then select the invocation
you like best and recite it." (See Hadith No. 794, 795 & 796).
