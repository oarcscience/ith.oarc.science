### Hadith 1:813

*Narrated 'Ata':*

I heard Jabir bin 'Abdullah saying, "The Prophet said, 'Whoever eats
(from) this plant (he meant garlic) should keep away from our mosque." I
said, "What does he mean by that?" He replied, "I think he means only
raw garlic."
