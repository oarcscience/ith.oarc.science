### Hadith 1:423

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abbas:*

The sun eclipsed and Allah's Apostle offered the eclipse prayer and
said, "I have been shown the Hellfire (now) and I never saw a worse and
horrible sight than the sight I have seen today."
