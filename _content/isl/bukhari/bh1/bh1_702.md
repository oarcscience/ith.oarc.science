### Hadith 1:700

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

Allah's Apostle fell from a horse and got injured so he led the prayer
sitting and we also prayed sitting. When he completed the prayer he
said, "The Imam is to be followed; if he says Takbir then say Takbir,
bow if he bows; raise your heads when he raises his head, when he says,
'Sami' a-l-lahu Liman hamida say, 'Rabbana laka-l-hamd', and prostrate
when he prostrates."
