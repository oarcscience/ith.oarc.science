### Hadith 1:683

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet prayed two Rakat of Zuhr prayer (instead of four) and he was
told that he had prayed two Rakat only. Then he prayed two more Rakat
and finished them with the Taslim followed by two prostrations.
