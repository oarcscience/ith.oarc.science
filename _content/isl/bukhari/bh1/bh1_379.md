### Hadith 1:379

*Narrated Abu Salama:*

'Aisha the wife of the Prophet said, "I used to sleep in front o Allah's
Apostle and my legs were opposite his Qibla and in prostration he pushed
my legs and I withdrew then and when he stood, I stretched them.' 'Aisha
added, "In those days the houses were without lights."
