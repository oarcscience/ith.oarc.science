### Hadith 1:205

*Narrated 'Urwa bin Al-Mughira:*

My father said, "Once I was in the company of the Prophet on a journey
and I dashed to take off his Khuffs. He ordered me to leave them as he
had put them after performing ablution. So he passed wet hands or them.
