### Hadith 1:716

*Narrated Anas bin Malik:*

The Prophet led us in prayer and then went up to the pulpit and beckoned
with both hands towards the Qibla of the mosque and then said, "When I
started leading you in prayer, I saw the display of Paradise and Hell on
the wall of the mosque (facing the Qibla). I never saw good and bad as I
have seen today." He repeated the last statement thrice.
