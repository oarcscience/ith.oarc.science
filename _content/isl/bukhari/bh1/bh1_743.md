### Hadith 1:741

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The Prophet recited aloud in the prayers in which he was ordered to do
so and quietly in the prayers in which he was ordered to do so. "And
your Lord is not forgetful." "Verily there was a good example for you in
the ways of the Prophet."
