### Hadith 1:324

*Narrated 'Aisha:*

(the wife of the Prophet) Um Habiba got bleeding in between the periods
for seven years. She asked Allah's Apostle about it. He ordered her to
take a bath (after the termination of actual periods) and added that it
was (from) a blood vessel. So she used to take a bath for every prayer.
