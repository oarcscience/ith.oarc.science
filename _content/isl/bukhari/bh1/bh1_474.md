### Hadith 1:472

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Once I came riding a she-ass when I had just attained the age of
puberty. Allah's Apostle was offering the prayer at Mina with no wall in
front of him and I passed in front of some of the row. There I
dismounted and let my she-ass loose to graze and entered the row and
nobody objected to me about it.
