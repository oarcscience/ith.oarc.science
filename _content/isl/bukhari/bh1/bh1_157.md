### Hadith 1:157

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

I followed the Prophet while he was going out to answer the call of
nature. He used not to look this way or that. So, when I approached near
him he said to me, "Fetch for me some stones for ' cleaning the privates
parts (or said something similar), and do not bring a bone or a piece of
dung." So I brought the stones in the corner of my garment and placed
them by his side and I then went away from him. When he finished (from
answering the call of nature) he used, them .
