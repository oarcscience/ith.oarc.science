### Hadith 1:76

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

Once I came riding a she-ass and had (just) attained the age of puberty.
Allah's Apostle was offering the prayer at Mina. There was no wall in
front of him and I passed in front of some of the row while they were
offering their prayers. There I let the she-ass loose to graze and
entered the row, and nobody objected to it.
