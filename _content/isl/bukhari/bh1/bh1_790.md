### Hadith 1:788

*Narrated Said bin Al-Harith:*

Abu Said led us in the prayer and said the Takbir aloud on arising from
the prostration, and on prostrating, on rising again, and on getting up
from the second Rak'a. Abu Said said, "I saw the Prophet doing the
same."
