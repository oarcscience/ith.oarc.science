### Hadith 1:507

*Narrated Ghailan:*

Anas said, "I do not find (now-a-days) things as they were (practiced)
at the time of the Prophet." Somebody said "The prayer (is as it was.)"
Anas said, "Have you not done in the prayer what you have done?

him weeping and asked him why he was weeping. He replied, "I do not know
anything which I used to know during the life-time of Allah's Apostle
except this prayer which is being lost (not offered as it should be)."
