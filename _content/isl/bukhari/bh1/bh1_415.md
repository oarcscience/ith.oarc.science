### Hadith 1:414

*Narrated Anas:*

I found the Prophet in the mosque along with some people. He said to me,
"Did Abu Talha send you?" I said, "Yes". He said, "For a meal?" I said,
"Yes." Then he said to his companions, "Get up." They set out and I was
ahead of them.
