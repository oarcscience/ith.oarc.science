### Hadith 1:9

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Amr:*

The Prophet said, "A Muslim is the one who avoids harming Muslims with
his tongue and hands. And a Muhajir (emigrant) is the one who gives up
(abandons) all what Allah has forbidden."
