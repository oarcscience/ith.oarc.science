### Hadith 1:23

*Narrated 'Abdullah (bin 'Umar):*

Once Allah's Apostle passed by an Ansari (man) who was admonishing to
his brother regarding Haya'. On that Allah's Apostle said, "Leave him as
Haya' is a part of faith." (See Hadith No. 8)
