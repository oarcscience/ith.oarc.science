### Hadith 1:827

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi Qatada Al-Ansari:*

My father said, "Allah's Apostle said, "Whenever I stand for prayer, I
want to prolong it but on hearing the cries of a child, I would shorten
it as I dislike to put its mother in trouble."
