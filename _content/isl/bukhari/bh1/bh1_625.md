### Hadith 1:623

*Narrated Abu Musa:*

The Prophet said, "The people who get tremendous reward for the prayer
are those who are farthest away (from the mosque) and then those who are
next farthest and so on. Similarly one who waits to pray with the Imam
has greater reward than one who prays and goes to bed. "
