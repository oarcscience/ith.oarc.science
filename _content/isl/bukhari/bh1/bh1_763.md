### Hadith 1:761

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

When the Prophet said, "Sami' a-l-lahu Liman hamida," (Allah heard those
who sent praises to Him), he would say, "Rabbana wa-laka-l-hamd." On
bowing and raising his head from it the Prophet used to say Takbir. He
also used to say Takbir on rising after the two prostrations. (See
Hadith No. 656).
