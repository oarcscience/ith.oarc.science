### Hadith 1:789

*Narrated Mutarrif:*

'Imran and I prayed behind 'Ali bin Abi Talib and he said Takbir on
prostrating, on rising and on getting up after the two Rakat (i.e. after
the second Rak'a). When the prayer was finished, 'Imran took me by the
hand and said, "He ('Ali) has prayed the prayer of Muhammad" (or said,
"He made us remember the prayer of Muhammad)."
