### Hadith 1:776

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The Prophet said, "I have been ordered to prostrate on seven bones i.e.
on the forehead along with the tip of the nose and the Prophet pointed
towards his nose, both hands, both knees and the toes of both feet and
not to gather the clothes or the hair."
