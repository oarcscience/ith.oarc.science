### Hadith 1:604

*Narrated Malik:*

We came to the Prophet and stayed with him for twenty days and nights.
We were all young and of about the same age. The Prophet was very kind
and merciful. When he realized our longing for our families, he asked
about our homes and the people there and we told him. Then he asked us
to go back to our families and stay with them and teach them (the
religion) and to order them to do good things. He also mentioned some
other things which I have (remembered or) forgotten. The Prophet then
added, "Pray as you have seen me praying and when it is the time for the
prayer one of you should pronounce the Adhan and the oldest of you
should lead the prayer.
