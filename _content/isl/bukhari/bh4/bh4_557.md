### Hadith 4:554

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "Shall I not tell you about the Dajjal a story of
which no prophet told his nation? The Dajjall is one-eyed and will bring
with him what will resemble Hell and Paradise, and what he will call
Paradise will be actually Hell; so I warn you (against him) as Noah
warned his nation against him."
