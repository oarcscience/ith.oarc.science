### Hadith 4:423

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

The Prophet said, "The sun and the moon do not eclipse because of
someone's death or wife (i.e. birth), but they are two signs amongst the
Signs of Allah. So, if you see them (i.e. eclipse) offer the Prayer (of
eclipse)."
