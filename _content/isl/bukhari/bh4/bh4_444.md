### Hadith 4:440

*Narrated Abu Salama:*

'Aisha said that the Prophet said to her "O 'Aisha' This is Gabriel and
he sends his (greetings) salutations to you." 'Aisha said, "Salutations
(Greetings) to him, and Allah's Mercy and Blessings be on him," and
addressing the Prophet she said, "You see what I don't see."
