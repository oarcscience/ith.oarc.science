### Hadith 4:493

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

The Prophet said, "If anyone of you, when having sexual relation with
his wife, say: 'In the name of Allah. O Allah! Protect us from Satan and
prevent Satan from approaching our offspring you are going to give us,'
and if he begets a child (as a result of that relation) Satan will not
harm it."
