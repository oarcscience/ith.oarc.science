### Hadith 4:433

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

The Prophet said, "On every Friday the angels take heir stand at every
gate of the mosques to write the names of the people chronologically
(i.e. according to the time of their arrival for the Friday prayer and
when the Imam sits (on the pulpit) they fold up their scrolls and get
ready to listen to the sermon."
