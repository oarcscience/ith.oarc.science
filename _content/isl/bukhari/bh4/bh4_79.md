### Hadith 4:79

*Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:*

On the day of the Conquest (of Mecca) the Prophet said, "There is no
emigration after the Conquest but Jihad and intentions. When you are
called (by the Muslim ruler) for fighting, go forth immediately." (See
Hadith No. 42)
