### Hadith 4:498

*Narrated Ubai bin Kab:*

That he heard Allah's Apostle saying, "(The prophet) Moses said to his
attendant, "Bring us our early meal' (18.62). The latter said, 'Did you
remember when we betook ourselves to the rock? I indeed forgot the fish
and none but Satan made me forget to remember it." (18.63) Moses did not
feel tired till he had crossed the place which Allah ordered him to go
to."
