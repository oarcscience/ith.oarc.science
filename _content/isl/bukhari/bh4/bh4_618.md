### Hadith 4:615

*Narrated Abu Huraira:*

Allah's Apostle said, "It was said to Bani Israel, Enter the gate (of
the town) with humility (prostrating yourselves) and saying:
"Repentance", but they changed the word and entered the town crawling on
their buttocks and saying: "A wheat grain in the hair."
