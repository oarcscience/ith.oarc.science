### Hadith 4:93

*Narrated Abu Said:*

I heard the Prophet saying, "Indeed, anyone who fasts for one day for
Allah's Pleasure, Allah will keep his face away from the (Hell) fire for
(a distance covered by a journey of) seventy years."
