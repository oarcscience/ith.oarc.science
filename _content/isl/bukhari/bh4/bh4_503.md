### Hadith 4:499

*Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:*

I saw Allah's Apostle pointing towards the east saying, "Lo! Afflictions
will verily emerge hence; afflictions will verily emerge hence where the
(side of the head of) Satan appears."
