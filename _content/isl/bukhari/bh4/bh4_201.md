### Hadith 4:200

*Narrated Anas:*

The Prophet offered a four-Rak'at Zuhr prayer at Medina and then offered
a two Rak'at 'Asr prayer at Dhul-Hulaifa and I heard the companions of
the Prophet reciting Talbiya aloud (for Hajj and 'Umra) altogether.
