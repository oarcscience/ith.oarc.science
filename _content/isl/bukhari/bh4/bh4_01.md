### Hadith 4:2

*Narrated Amr bin Al-Harith:*

(The brother of the wife of Allah's Apostle. Juwaira bint Al-Harith)
When Allah's Apostle died, he did not leave any Dirham or Dinar (i.e.
money), a slave or a slave woman or anything else except his white mule,
his arms and a piece of land which he had given in charity .
