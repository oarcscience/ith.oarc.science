### Hadith 4:542

*Narrated Sufyan bin Abi Zuhair Ash-Shani:*

That he heard Allah's Apostle saying, "If somebody keeps a dog that is
neither used for farm work nor for guarding the livestock, he will lose
one Qirat (of the reward) of his good deeds everyday."
