(47)

His supplication for those having moles

It should be recited till the last seven days of the month.

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful

And the parable of an evil word is as an evil tree pulled up from the

earth’s surface; it has no stability. And the mountains shall be made to

crumble with (an awful) crumbling. So that they shall be as scattered

dust.
