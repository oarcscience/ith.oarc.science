\(97\)

His supplication while washing his left hand

*In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful*

O Allah! Do not give the scroll of my deeds in this hand, nor tie it
around

my neck. And by Your sake I seek refuge of the dangerous fire of hell.
