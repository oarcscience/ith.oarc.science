(39)

His supplication for cure from dropsy

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful

In the name of Allah and by Allah, I seek refuge from the Honoured name

of Allah, by the great name and with its help. I seek refuge from all the

blood supplying veins and from all the heat of every fire.
