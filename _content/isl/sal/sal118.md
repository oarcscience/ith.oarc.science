\(117\)

His supplication after waking

*In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful*

Sufficient is my Lord from all the people, That is sufficient for me,
one

Who is sufficient. He is sufficient since the time of my existence.
Allah is

sufficient and the best caretaker.
