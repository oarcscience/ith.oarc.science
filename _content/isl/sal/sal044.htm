(43)

His supplication for piles

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful

O the generous! O the possessor of greatness! O the Merciful! O the

one Who is near! O the one Who hears our supplication! O the Creator!

O the generous! Bless Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and his progeny and send

Your bounties upon me and cure the pain in me.
