### THE CHAPTER OF THE QURÂI*S*.

### (CVI. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.  
For the uniting of the Qurâi*s*; uniting them for the caravan of winter
and summer.  
So let them serve the Lord of this house who feeds them against hunger
and makes them safe against fear <span id="fr_502"></span>[1](#fn_502).
