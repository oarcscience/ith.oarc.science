### SURA CXIV.--MEN \[VII.\]

MECCA OR MEDINA.--6 Verses

In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful  
SAY: I betake me for refuge to the Lord of MEN,  
The King of men,  
The God of men,  
Against the mischief of the stealthily withdrawing whisperer, <span
id="fr_40"></span>[1](#fn_40)  
Who whispereth in man's breast--  
Against djinn and men.
