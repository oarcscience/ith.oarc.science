# 99. al-Zilzal: The Shaking

<span id="an_099_001">1</span> When Earth is shaken with her (final)
earthquake

<span id="an_099_002">2</span> And Earth yieldeth up her burdens,

<span id="an_099_003">3</span> And man saith: What aileth her ?

<span id="an_099_004">4</span> That day she will relate her chronicles,

<span id="an_099_005">5</span> Because thy Lord inspireth her.

<span id="an_099_006">6</span> That day mankind will issue forth in
scattered groups to be shown their deeds.

<span id="an_099_007">7</span> And whoso doeth good an atom's weight
will see it then,

<span id="an_099_008">8</span> And whoso doeth ill an atom's weight will
see it then.
