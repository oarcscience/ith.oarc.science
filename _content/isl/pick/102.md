# 102. at-Takathur: Worldly Gain

<span id="an_102_001">1</span> Rivalry in worldly increase distracteth
you

<span id="an_102_002">2</span> Until ye come to the graves.

<span id="an_102_003">3</span> Nay, but ye will come to know!

<span id="an_102_004">4</span> Nay, but ye will come to know!

<span id="an_102_005">5</span> Nay, would that ye knew (now) with a sure
knowledge!

<span id="an_102_006">6</span> For ye will behold hell-fire.

<span id="an_102_007">7</span> Aye, ye will behold it with sure vision.

<span id="an_102_008">8</span> Then, on that day, ye will be asked
concerning pleasure.
