# 106. al-Quraysh: The Quraish

<span id="an_106_001">1</span> For the taming of Qureysh.

<span id="an_106_002">2</span> For their taming (We cause) the caravans
to set forth in winter and summer.

<span id="an_106_003">3</span> So let them worship the Lord of this
House,

<span id="an_106_004">4</span> Who hath fed them against hunger and hath
made them safe from fear.
