# 109. al-Kafirun: The Unbelievers

<span id="an_109_001">1</span> Say: O disbelievers!

<span id="an_109_002">2</span> I worship not that which ye worship;

<span id="an_109_003">3</span> Nor worship ye that which I worship.

<span id="an_109_004">4</span> And I shall not worship that which ye
worship.

<span id="an_109_005">5</span> Nor will ye worship that which I worship.

<span id="an_109_006">6</span> Unto you your religion, and unto me my
religion.
