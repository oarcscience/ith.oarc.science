# 94. al-Inshirah: The Expansion

<span id="an_094_001">1</span> Have We not caused thy bosom to dilate,

<span id="an_094_002">2</span> And eased thee of the burden

<span id="an_094_003">3</span> Which weighed down thy back;

<span id="an_094_004">4</span> And exalted thy fame ?

<span id="an_094_005">5</span> But lo! with hardship goeth ease,

<span id="an_094_006">6</span> Lo! with hardship goeth ease;

<span id="an_094_007">7</span> So when thou art relieved, still toil

<span id="an_094_008">8</span> And strive to please thy Lord.
