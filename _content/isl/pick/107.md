# 107. al-Ma\`un: The Daily Necessaries

<span id="an_107_001">1</span> Hast thou observed him who belieth
religion ?

<span id="an_107_002">2</span> That is he who repelleth the orphan,

<span id="an_107_003">3</span> And urgeth not the feeding of the needy.

<span id="an_107_004">4</span> Ah, woe unto worshippers

<span id="an_107_005">5</span> Who are heedless of their prayer;

<span id="an_107_006">6</span> Who would be seen (at worship)

<span id="an_107_007">7</span> Yet refuse small kindnesses!
