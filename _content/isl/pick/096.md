# 96. al-\`Alaq: The Clot

<span id="an_096_001">1</span> Read: In the name of thy Lord Who
createth,

<span id="an_096_002">2</span> Createth man from a clot.

<span id="an_096_003">3</span> Read: And thy Lord is the Most Bounteous,

<span id="an_096_004">4</span> Who teacheth by the pen,

<span id="an_096_005">5</span> Teacheth man that which he knew not.

<span id="an_096_006">6</span> Nay, but verily man is rebellious

<span id="an_096_007">7</span> That he thinketh himself independent!

<span id="an_096_008">8</span> Lo! unto thy Lord is the return.

<span id="an_096_009">9</span> Hast thou seen him who dissuadeth

<span id="an_096_010">10</span> A slave when he prayeth ?

<span id="an_096_011">11</span> Hast thou seen if he relieth on the
guidance (of Allah)

<span id="an_096_012">12</span> Or enjoineth piety ?

<span id="an_096_013">13</span> Hast thou seen if he denieth (Allah's
guidance) and is froward ?

<span id="an_096_014">14</span> Is he then unaware that Allah seeth ?

<span id="an_096_015">15</span> Nay, but if he cease not We will seize
him by the forelock -

<span id="an_096_016">16</span> The lying, sinful forelock -

<span id="an_096_017">17</span> Then let him call upon his henchmen!

<span id="an_096_018">18</span> We will call the guards of hell.

<span id="an_096_019">19</span> Nay, Obey not thou him. But prostrate
thyself, and draw near (unto Allah).
