BUSHIDO

The Soul of Japan
An Exposition of Japanese Thought

by Inazo Nitobe

[1905]
Contents    Start Reading    Page Index    Text [Zipped]

This is Inazo Nitobe's account of the Samurai code, Bushido. This was a far-reaching philosophy of life which governed every action of the warrior caste. An understanding of Bushido is crucial if you want to understand Japanese culture at a deep level.
