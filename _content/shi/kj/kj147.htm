[SECT. CXI.—EMPEROR IN-GIYŌ (PART IV.—HIS AGE AND PLACE OF BURIAL).]

The Heavenly Sovereign's august years were seventy-eight. His august mausoleum is at Naga-ye near Wega in Kafuchi. 1a
