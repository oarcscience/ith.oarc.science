### \[SECT. CLXXIX.—EMPEROR SU-JUN.\]

The Heavenly Sovereign Hatsuse-be-no-waka-sazaki dwelt at the Palace of
Shibabaki at Kurahashi, and ruled the Empire for four years. His august
mausoleum is on the mound of Kurahashi.
