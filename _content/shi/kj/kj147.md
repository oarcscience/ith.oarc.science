## \[SECT. CXI.—EMPEROR IN-GIYŌ (PART IV.—HIS AGE AND PLACE OF BURIAL).\]

The Heavenly Sovereign's august years were seventy-eight. His august
mausoleum is at Naga-ye near Wega in Kafuchi. <span
id="fr_2217"></span>[1a](#fn_2212)
