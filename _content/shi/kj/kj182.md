## \[SECT. CLXXV.—EMPEROR SEN-KUWA.\]

His Augustness Take-wo-hiro-kuni-oshi-tate dwelt in the Palace of
Ihorinu at Hinokuma, and ruled the Empire.

<span id="page_425">p. 425</span>

<span class="contnote">\[paragraph continues\]</span> The august
children born to this Heavenly Sovereign by his wife Her Augustness
Tachi-bana-no-naka-tsu-hime, the august daughter of the Heavenly
Sovereign Ohoke, were: Her Augustness Ishi-hime; next Her Augustness
Wo-ishi-hime; next King Kura-no-waka-ye. The august children born to him
by his next wife, Kafuchi-no-waku-go-hime, were: King Honoho; next King
Weha. The august children of this Heavenly \[340\] Sovereign numbered
altogether five (<span class="small">three Kings and two Queens</span>).
So King Honoho (<span class="small">was the ancestor of the Dukes
Shihida.</span>) Prince Weha (<span class="small">was the ancestor of
the Dukes of Wina and of the Dukes of Tajihi.</span>)
