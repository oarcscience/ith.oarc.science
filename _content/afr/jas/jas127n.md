*NOTE:* 

### 127. Big-head, Big-belly and Little-foot.

The story is very common in Jamaica. See Grimm 18, The Straw, the Coal
and the Bean, Bolte u. Polívka 1:135--137, and compare Parsons, Andros
Island, 147.
