#### III

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Chu;* narrative. A bride describes her first meeting with her
bridegroom.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>He waited ’tween
the gate and screen,  
With ear-plug strings of white silk seen.  
The plugs themselves had *hua* stones’ sheen.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>He waited in the
court and stood,  
With ear-plug strings of silk green-hued.  
The plugs themselves were *yung* stones good.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>There in the hall
he stood and stayed.  
Of yellow silk his plug strings made.  
The plugs of fine *ying* gems displayed.

</div>
