#### VII

The *Ho Kuang;* narrative. Other things more difficult to overcome than
distance may keep one from a place.

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>They tell me that
the Ho is wide;—  
  With a few reeds I could cross through.  
They tell me Sung is distant far;—  
  Rising on tiptoe Sung I view. <span id="page_71">p. 71</span>

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>They tell me that
the Ho is wide;—  
  A little boat it will not bear.  
They tell me Sung is distant far;—  
  Ere morning fades I might be there.

</div>
