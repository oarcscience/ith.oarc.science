#### VIII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Ts‘ai Ko;* narrative. A lady longs for the society of the object of
her affection.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>He's there, the
dolichos among!  
Only one day from sight away,—  
To me it seems as three months long!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>He's there, among
the southernwood!  
Only one day from sight away,—  
It seems three seasons' solitude! <span id="page_83">p. 83</span>

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>Among the mugwort
he appears!  
Only one day from sight away,—  
To me it seems as three fully years!

</div>
