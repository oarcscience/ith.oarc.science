#### VII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Kao Ch‘iu;* narrative. The people of some great officer complain of
his hard treatment of them, while they declare their loyalty.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>You, of the
leopard's cuff and lambkin's fur,  
  To us have been a governor unkind;  
But we look back on many an ancestor,  
  And stay, when we another chief might find.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Unsympathizing,
violent, and rough  
  With us poor folks from day to day are you.  
Man of the lambkin fur and leopard cuff,  
  What keeps us here but hearts that still beat true?

</div>
