#### V

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The Chung Ssŭ; metaphorical. The fruitfulness of the locust; supposed to
celebrate Tai Ssŭ’s freedom from jealousy.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Ye locusts, wingèd
tribes,  
  Gather in concord fine;  
Well your descendants may  
  In numerous bright hosts shine!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Ye locusts, wingèd
tribes,  
  Your wings in flight resound;  
Well your descendants may  
  In endless lines be found!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>Ye locusts, wingèd
tribes,  
  Together cluster strong;  
Well your descendants may  
  In swarms forever throng!

</div>
