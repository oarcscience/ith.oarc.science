#### IX

The *Yu Hu;* metaphorical. A woman expresses her desire for a husband.

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Lonely,
suspicious, is that fox,  
  At that dame cross the Ch‘i.  
Like him that man, for whom I'm sad!  
  No lower dress has he.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Lonely,
suspicious, is that fox,  
  At that deep ford of Ch‘i.  
Like him that man, for whom I'm sad!  
  No girdle sash has he!

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>Lonely,
suspicious, is that fox,  
  There, on the bank of Ch‘i.  
Like him that man, for whom I'm sad!  
  No clothes at all has he!

</div>
