#### V

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Shih Mou Chih Chien;* narrative. The straits of the peasantry of
Wei.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Among their ten
acres of mulberry trees,
