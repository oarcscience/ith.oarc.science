#### X

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Ch‘iu Chung Yu Ma;* narrative. A woman longs for the presence of
her lovers, who, she thinks, are detained from her by another woman.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Where the hemp
grows on the mound,  
  There some one keeps Tsŭ-chieh;  
  There some one keeps Tsŭ-chieh:—  
Why comes not *he* to *me* with a bound?

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>On the mound where
grows the wheat,  
  There some one keeps Tzŭ-kuo;  
  There some one keeps Tzŭ-kuo:—  
Why comes not *he* with *me* here to eat?

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>On the mound plum
trees have place.  
  There some one keeps those youths;  
  There some one keeps those youths:—  
O for their *chiu*-stones girdle to grace!

</div>
