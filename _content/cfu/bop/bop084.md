#### VII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Tsun Ta Lu;* narrative. Old friendship should not be hastily broken
off.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Along the great
highway,  
  I hold you by the cuff.  
O spurn me not, I pray,  
  Nor break old friendship off. <span id="page_93">p. 93</span>

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>Along the highway
worn,  
  I hold your hand in mine.  
Do not as vile me scorn;  
  Your love I can't resign.

</div>
