#### VII

<div style="font-size: 90%">

The *Kao Yang;* narrative. The easy dignity of the officers at some
court.

</div>

<div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_1">1</span></span>Arrayed in skins
of lamb or sheep,  
  With five silk braidings all of white,  
From court they go, to take their meal,  
  All self-possessed, with spirits light.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_2">2</span></span>How on their skins
of lamb or sheep  
  The five seams wrought with white silk show!  
With easy steps, and self-possessed,  
  From court, to take their meal, they go.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_3">3</span></span>Upon their skins
of lamb or sheep  
  Shines the white silk the seams to link.  
With easy steps and self-possessed,  
  They go from court to eat and drink.

</div>
