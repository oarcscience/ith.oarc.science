### AT THE KUANG-LI PAVILION

Red-skirted ladies, robed for fairyland, all have flown,  
But my heart to the wail of their long reed-pipes lilts on:  
Their clarion songs ’mid the wandering clouds were blown,  
The tiny-waisted, dreamily-dancing girls are gone.
