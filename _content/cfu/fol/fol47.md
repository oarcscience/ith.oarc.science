### ON THE RIVER AT HUI-CH‘UNG

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

Beyond the twilight grove some sprays  
Of peach-bloom charm the lingering days.  
In spring, when first the waters warm,  
The wild duck on the river swarm.  
When artemisia lights the land  
Young reeds break through the dappled sand.

</div>
