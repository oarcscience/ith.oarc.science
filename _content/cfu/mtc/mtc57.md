### \[56\] TO A TALKATIVE GUEST

#### (A.D. *836*)

<span class="smallcaps">The</span> town visitor's easy talk flows in an
endless stream;  
The country host's quiet thoughts ramble timidly on.  
"I beg you, Sir, do not tell me about things at Ch‘ang-an;  
For you entered just when my harp was tuned and lying  
    balanced on my knees."
