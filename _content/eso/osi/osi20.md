### CHAPTER VIII.

### A TEXT FROM THE VEDAS.

Nothing is commenced or ended. Everything is changed or transformed.
Life and death are only modes of transformation which rule the vital
molecule, from the plant up to Brahma himself. (Atharva-Veda.)
