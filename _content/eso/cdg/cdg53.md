### CASSIODORUS RENIUS OR REYNA,

### 1520-30 A.D.--1594 A.D.

<span class="margnote"><span id="an_PP">PP</span></span>Was born at
Seville in Spain. His chief work was pp a scholarly translation of the
Old and New Testaments

<span id="page_293">p. 293</span>

into Spanish. His defence of Magdalen of the Cross occurs in a rare
treatise on the fourth chapter of St. Matthew, "de periculis piorum
ministrorum verbi in tempore cavendis," published with his "Evangelium
Joannis" in 1573 at Frankfurt, where he lived for some years prior to
his death.
