CASSIODORUS RENIUS OR REYNA,

1520-30 A.D.--1594 A.D.

PPWas born at Seville in Spain. His chief work was pp a scholarly translation of the Old and New Testaments

p. 293

into Spanish. His defence of Magdalen of the Cross occurs in a rare treatise on the fourth chapter of St. Matthew, "de periculis piorum ministrorum verbi in tempore cavendis," published with his "Evangelium Joannis" in 1573 at Frankfurt, where he lived for some years prior to his death.
