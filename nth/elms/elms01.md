*The Edinburgh Lectures on Mental Science*, by Thomas Troward, \[1909\],
at Intangible Textual Heritage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### FOREWORD.

This book contains the substance of a course of lectures recently given
by the writer in the Queen Street Hall, Edinburgh. Its purpose is to
indicate the *Natural Principles* governing the relation between Mental
Action and Material Conditions, and thus to afford the student an
intelligible starting-point for the practical study of the subject.

T.T.

<span class="small">March, 1904.</span>
