THE KEY TO LIFE

In this great universe of earth and sky
Strange forces make us mortals do strange things.
Humanity, doped or drugged by laws of man,
Wanders unsatisfied through time and space
Not knowing of the deep magnetic forces
Waiting entrance to the mind.
Oh, human soul, if you will pause
And take the key within your reach
And open up the cupboard of your mind,
There, upon the shelf of common sense,
You will find such great content
That man and mate would never want for more.
