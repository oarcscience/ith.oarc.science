### HEART DISEASE

(*Correspondence—Fear, Anger, etc*.)

———

#### AFFIRMATION

My heart is a perfect idea in Divine Mind and is now in its right place,
doing its right work.

It is a happy heart, a fearless heart and a loving heart.

The Light of the Christ streams through every cell and I give thanks for
my radiant health.
