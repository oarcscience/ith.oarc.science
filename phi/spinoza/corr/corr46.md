### LETTER XLVIII.

Written by a physician, Lambert de Velthuysen, to Isaac Orobio, and
forwarded by the latter to Spinoza. It contains a detailed attack on the
Tractatus Theologico-Politicus. Its tenor may be sufficiently seen from
Spinoza's reply. (Written at Utrecht, January 24th, 1671.) Velthuysen
afterwards became more friendly to Spinoza, as appears from Letter LXXV.
