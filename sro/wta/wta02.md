### PREFACE

THE following account of a psychic experience has been gathered from
notes handed to me by a friend, a writer of considerable repute. Whether
the adventures told therein are to be regarded as a dream, or an actual
experience on the astral plane, I must leave to the reader to judge.

F. H.
