Zanoni introduced the concept of the wandering, eternal adepts into
popular culture, with this tale of tragic love. Bulwer Lytton also wrote
the fantasy [Vril, The Power of the Coming Race](../../atl/vril/index),
a prototype for fictions of lost civilizations to come. Zanoni had a
huge influence on Theosophists and other 19th century occult groups.
