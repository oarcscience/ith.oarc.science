
# Sacred Time
<title>Intangible Textual Heritage: Sacred Time</title>


This page has links to resources at Intangible Textual Heritage related
to time: chronology of sacred texts and religion, and information about
calendrical systems used by major religions.

The above image is a whole-sky map of fluctuations in cosmic background
radiation, basically the closest thing to a photograph of the big bang
that we have.
