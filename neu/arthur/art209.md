## TROSTAN MADE THIS

### by

## ELLA YOUNG

  
  
The hawk in the cliff of Ben Edair  
Knows that I am stricken.  
  
The otter knows  
In the pool by the hurdles.  
  
Love has a short blossoming-  
But the dead remember it.
