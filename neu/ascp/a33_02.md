# Bede's Death Song: The Hague Version

Verse Early Saxon

  
Fore ðaem nedfere      nenig wiorðeð  
ðonosnottorra      ðon him ðearf riae  
to ymbhycgenne      aer his hinionge  
hwet his gaste      godes oððe yfles  

5  
ester deaðdege      doemed wiorðe.
