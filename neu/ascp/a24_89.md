# Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 89

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

  
*Conuertere, domine, aliquantulum, et deprecabilis esto super seruos
tuos.*  
Gehweorf us hwæthwygu,         halig drihten;  
wes ðinum scealcum         wel eaðbene.  
*Respice in seruos tuos et in opera tua, domine, et dirige filios
eorum.*  
Geseoh þine scealcas         swæsum eagum,  
and on þin agen weorc,         ece drihten,  

5  
and heora bearn gerece         bliðum mode.  
*Et **sit** splendor domini dei nostri super nos, et opera manuum
nostrarum dirige super nos, et opus manuum nostrarum dirige.*  
Wese us beorhtnys ofer         bliðan drihtnes,  
ures þæs godan godes         georne ofer ealle;  
gerece ure handgeweorc         heah ofer usic.
