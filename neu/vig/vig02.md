<span id="_Toc29619146">Dedication</span>

 

To Iceland

 

  
Hail, Isle! with mist and snowstorms girt around,  
Where fire and earthquake rend the shatter’d ground--  
Here once o’er furthest ocean’s icy path  
The Northmen fled a tyrant monarch’s wrath:  
Here, cheer’d by song and story, dwelt they free,  
And held unscath’d their laws and liberty.
