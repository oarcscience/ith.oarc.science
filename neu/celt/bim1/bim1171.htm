YEARS OF THE SUN AND MOON.

The years of the sun and moon are as follows:--The year of the sun, 366 days. The year of the moon, 354 days.

p. 424 p. 425

The year of memorial and computation, 364 days.

Twenty-nine years of the sun are thirty years of the moon.

In another place, thus:--

Thirty-one years of the moon make thirty years of the sun.

From the Book of Brith y Coed.
