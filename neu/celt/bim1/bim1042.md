### VARIATIONS OF LETTERS.

<span id="img_08131"></span> ![](img/08131.jpg) turned on its side, with
its base to the right, <span id="img_08132"></span> ![](img/08132.jpg),
stood for E; when turned thus, <span id="img_08133"></span>
![](img/08133.jpg), it stood for V, which being doubled thus, <span
id="img_08134"></span> ![](img/08134.jpg), stood for O; and from <span
id="img_08135"></span> ![](img/08135.jpg), were produced <span
id="img_08136"></span> ![](img/08136.jpg) and <span
id="img_08137"></span> ![](img/08137.jpg). And in this way the letters
were varied from nine to twelve, and sixteen, and eighteen, and twenty,
and twenty-four; and from thence to thirty-eight letters, that is to
say, forty save two.
