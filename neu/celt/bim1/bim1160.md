### THE DIVISIONS OF THE YEAR.

From the same Book.

The three divisions of the year:--

<div style="margin-left: 32px">

The time of summer from Cyntevin to the calend of October;  
Winter from the calend of October to the calend of February;  
Spring from the calend of February to Cyntevin.

</div>
