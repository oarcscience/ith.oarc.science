### THE ELEMENTS.

Manred, the original form of all the materials, or all the constituents,
that is, the elements, of which the first four of the five were dead,
namely, calas, <span id="fr_339"></span>[1](#fn_337.md) fluidity, breath,
and fire, until God agitated them by uttering His Name, when instantly
they became alive in one triumphant song, and manifested their
condition.
