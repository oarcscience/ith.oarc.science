<td data-valign="top"><p>LA domh ’s mi dol dh’ an Roimh,<br />
Thachair orm Calum-cille, Peadail, agus Pol,<br />
Is e comhradh a bh’ aca ’s a thachair bhi ’n am beul,<br />
Laoigh bheura, bhoirionn, bhailgionn,<br />
Mar thubhradh anns an dailgionn,<br />
Air an laraich seo gu ceann la ’s bliadhna,<br />
A uchd Dia nan dul is nan uile bhuadh,<br />
Triath nan triath ’s nan Cumhachdan siorruidh shuas.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>A DAY as I was going to Rome,<br />
I forgathered with Columba, Peter, and Paul,<br />
The talk that they had and that happened in their mouths,<br />
Was loud-lunged, white-bellied, female calves,<br />
As was spoken in the prophecy,<br />
On this foundation for a year and a day,<br />
Through the bosom of the God of life and all the hosts,<br />
Chief of chiefs and of the everlasting Powers above.</p></td>
