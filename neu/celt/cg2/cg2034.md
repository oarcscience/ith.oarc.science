<td data-valign="top"><p>OB a chuir Moire mhor-gheal<br />
Gu Bride mhin-gheal,<br />
Air muir, air tir, air li, ’s rachd fharmaid,<br />
Air fiacail coin-ghiorr, ’s air siadha coin-ghearr.</p>
<p>Ge be co leag ort an t-suil,<br />
Gum much i air fein,<br />
Gum much i air a thur,<br />
Gum much i air a spreidh.</p>
<p>Clomhadh mis an t-suil,<br />
Somhadh mis an t-suil,<br />
Tri teanga tur nan iomlan,<br />
Am feithean a chridhe,<br />
     An eibhlean imileig.</p>
<p>        A uchd Athar,<br />
        A uchd Mic,<br />
        A uchd Spioraid Naoimh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THE spell the great white Mary sent<br />
To Bride the lovely fair,<br />
For sea, for land, for water, and for withering glance,<br />
For teeth of wolf, for testicle of wolf.</p>
<p>Whoso laid on thee the eye,<br />
May it oppress himself,<br />
May it oppress his house,<br />
May it oppress his flocks.</p>
<p>Let me subdue the eye,<br />
Let me avert the eye,<br />
The three complete tongues of fullness,<br />
In the arteries of the heart,<br />
     In the vitals of the navel.</p>
<p>        From the bosom of Father,<br />
        From the bosom of Son,<br />
        From the bosom of Holy Spirit.</p></td>
