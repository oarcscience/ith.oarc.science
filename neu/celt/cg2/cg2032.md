<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I PLACE this spell to mine eye,<br />
As the Ring of life ordained,<br />
Spell of Peter, spell of Paul,<br />
Spell of James, spell of John,<br />
Spell of Columba benign,<br />
Spell of Patrick, chief of saints,<br />
Spell of Bride, tranquil of the kine,<br />
Spell of Mary, lovely of the joys,<br />
Spell of cows, spell of herds,<br />
Spell of sheep, spell of flocks,<br />
Spell of greatness, spell of means,<br />
Spell of joy, spell of peace,<br />
Spell of war, spell of the brave,<br />
The third best spell under the sun,<br />
The powerful spell of the Three Powers,<br />
     Father, Son, Spirit everlasting.</p></td>
