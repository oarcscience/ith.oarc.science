<td data-valign="top"><p>TRATH bha Ti nan dul fo choill,<br />
Agus daoibhidh air a dheigh,<br />
De thuirt daolaire na doill,<br />
     Ris an daol ’s an dealan-de?</p>
<p>'Am facas seach an diugh no ’n raoir,<br />
Mac mo ghaoil-sa--Mac De?'<br />
'Chunnas, chunnas,' os an daol,<br />
     'Mac na saorsa seach an de.'</p>
<p>'Cearr! cearr! cearr thu fhe,'<br />
Os an cearr-dubhan feach;<br />
'A bhliadhna mhor chon an de<br />
     Chaidh Mac De seach.'</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>WHEN the Being of glory was in retreat,<br />
And wicked men in pursuit of Him,<br />
What said the groveller of blindness,<br />
     To the beetle and the butterfly?</p>
<p>'Saw ye passing to-day or yestreen,<br />
The Son of my love--the Son of God?'<br />
'We saw, we saw,' said the black beetle,<br />
     'The Son of freedom pass yesterday.'</p>
<p>'Wrong! wrong! wrong art thou,'<br />
Said the sacred beetle earthy;<br />
'A big year it was yestreen<br />
     Since the Son of God passed.'</p></td>
