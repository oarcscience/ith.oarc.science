<td data-valign="top"><p>PETER and Paul were passing by,<br />
While a hind in the path was bearing a fawn;<br />
'A hind is bearing there,' said Peter;<br />
'I see it is so,' said Paul.</p>
<p>'As her foliage falls from the tree,<br />
So may her placenta fall to the ground,<br />
In name of the Father of love and of the Son of grace,<br />
And of the Spirit of loving wisdom;<br />
     Father of love and Son of grace,<br />
     And Spirit of loving wisdom.'</p></td>
