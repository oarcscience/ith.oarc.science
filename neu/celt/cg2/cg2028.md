<td data-valign="top"><p><span id="img_05800"></span> <img src="img/05800.jpg" alt="P" /></p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>      PEADAIR is Seumas is Eoin,<br />
      Triuir is binne beuis an gloir,<br />
      Dh’ eirich a dheanamh na h-eoir,<br />
      Romh mhor dhorus na Cathrach,<br />
            Ri glun deas De a Mhic.</p>
<p>      Air na feara fur-shuileach,<br />
      Air na bana bur-shuileach,<br />
      Air na siocharra seanga sith,<br />
            Air na saighde siubhlach sibheideach.</p>
<p>      Dithis a rinn dut dibhidh sul,<br />
      Fear agus bean le nimh agus tnu,<br />
      Triuir a chuirim an urra riu,<br />
            Athair, agus Mac, agus Spiorad Numh.</p>
<p>Ceithir ghalara fichead an aorabh duine ’s bruid,<br />
Dia d’ an sgrid, Dia d’ an sgroid, Dia d’ an sgruid,<br />
A t’ fhuil, a t’ fheoil a d’ chnamha cubhra caoin,<br />
O’n la’n duigh ’s gach la thig, gun tig la crich do shaoghail.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>      PETER and James and John,<br />
      The three of sweetest virtues in glory,<br />
      Who arose to make the charm,<br />
      Before the great door of the City,<br />
            By the right knee of God the Son.</p>
<p>      Against the keen-eyed men,<br />
      Against the peering-eyed women,<br />
      Against the slim, slender, fairy-darts,<br />
            Against the swift arrows of furies.</p>
<p>      Two made to thee the withered eye,<br />
      Man and woman with venom and envy,<br />
      Three whom I will set against them,<br />
            Father, Son, and Spirit Holy.</p>
<p>Four and twenty diseases in the constitution of man and beast,<br />
God scrape them, God search them, God cleanse them,<br />
From out thy blood, from out thy flesh, from out thy fragrant bones,<br />
From this day and each day that comes, till thy day on earth be done.</p></td>
