<span class="small">THIS verse, the only verse of the poem he could
remember, was obtained from John Kane, a native of Ireland. John Kane
had many traditional stories of Saint Columba showing that he 'being
dead yet speaketh.' These stories were <span id="page_169">p. 169</span>
vivid and graphic, the probable and improbable, possible and impossible,
blending and diffusing throughout.</span>
