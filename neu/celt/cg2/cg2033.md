<td data-valign="top"><p>THE spell fair-white,<br />
Sent of Mary Virgin,<br />
To the daughter of Dorail,<br />
Of the golden-yellow hair,<br />
Hither on main-land,<br />
Hither on coast-land,<br />
Hither on lake-land,<br />
Hither on ocean,<br />
To thwart eye,<br />
To thwart net,<br />
To thwart envy,<br />
To thwart hate.<br />
