<td data-valign="top"><p>BUAINIDH mi an torranan,<br />
Le toradh mara ’s tir,<br />
Lus nan agh ’s nan sonas e,<br />
Lus a bhainne mhi.</p>
<p>Mar a dh’ orduich Righ nan righ,<br />
Brigh a chur an cich ’s an carr,<br />
’S mar a dh’ orduich Ti nan dul,<br />
Sugh a chur an uth ’s an ar,<br />
Le bliochd, le blachd, le bladh,<br />
Le cobhan, le omhan, ’s le ais,<br />
Le laoigh bhoirionn, bhreac,<br />
Gun laoigh fhirionn ac,<br />
Le al, le agh, le toradh,<br />
Le gradh, le baigh, le sonadh,</p>
<p>Gun fear mi-run,<br />
Gun bhean mi-shul,<br />
Gun ghnu, gun tnu, gun toirinn,<br />
Gun mhaghan masach,<br />
Gun chu fasaich,<br />
Gun scan foirinn<br />
Dh’ fhaighinn greim air a chugain<br />
     Anns an teid seo,<br />
Torranan nan sionn,<br />
Toradh ga chur ann,<br />
Le al, le agh, le sonas.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL pluck the figwort,<br />
With the fruitage of sea and land,<br />
The plant of joy and gladness,<br />
The plant of rich milk.</p>
<p>As the King of kings ordained,<br />
To put milk in pap and gland,<br />
As the Being of life ordained,<br />
To place substance in udder and kidney,<br />
With milk, with milkiness, with butter milk,<br />
With produce, with whisked whey, with milk-product,<br />
With speckled female calves,<br />
Without male calves,<br />
With progeny, with joy, with fruitage,<br />
With love, with charity, with bounty,</p>
<p>Without man of evil wish,<br />
Without woman of evil eye,<br />
Without malice, without envy, without 'toirinn,'<br />
Without hipped bear,<br />
Without wilderness dog,<br />
Without 'scan foirinn,'<br />
Obtaining hold of the rich dainty<br />
     Into which this shall go.<br />
Figwort of bright lights,<br />
Fruitage to place therein,<br />
With fruit, with grace, with joyance.</p></td>
