<td data-valign="top"><p>CUIREAM tan a steach<br />
Air bhearn nan speach,<br />
Air ghuth mairbh,<br />
Air ghuth tairbh,<br />
Air ghuth dair,<br />
Air ghuth na ba ceire<br />
Cionnara, ceannara, cairr,<br />
Clach mhor bhun sgonnaig<br />
Gun faothachadh, gun lomadh,<br />
Na taodaiche tromaidh<br />
Bhi slaodadh ri dronnaig bhur tairr,<br />
Gon tig latha geal am mair.</p>
<p>An t-Athair, am Mac, an Spiorad Naomh,<br />
D’ar caomhnadh, d’ar comhnadh, ’s d’ar tilleadh,<br />
Gun comhlaich mise no mo dhuine sibh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I DRIVE the kine within<br />
The gateway of the herds,<br />
On voice of the dead,<br />
On voice of bull,<br />
On voice of pairing,<br />
On voice of grayling cow<br />
White-headed, strong-headed, of udder.<br />
Be the big stone of the base of the couple<br />
Without ceasing, without decreasing,<br />
As a full-weighted tether<br />
Trailing from the hunch of your rump,<br />
Till bright daylight comes in to-morrow.</p>
<p>The Father, the Son, the Holy Spirit,<br />
Save you, and shield you, and tend you,<br />
Till I or mine shall meet you again.</p></td>
