<td data-valign="top"><p>CHUALA mi chuthag ’s gun bhiadh am bhroinn,<br />
Chuala mi am fearan am barr a chroinn,<br />
Chuala mi ’n suaircean shuas anns a choill,<br />
’S chuala mi nualla cumhachag na h-oidhche.</p>
<p>Chunna mi ’n t-uan ’s a chula rium,<br />
Chunna mi ’n t-seiliche air lic luim,<br />
Chunna mi ’n searrach le thulachain rium,<br />
Chunna mi an clachran air gharadh tuill,<br />
An eunarag ’s mi ’m shuidhe cruinn,<br />
’S dh’ aithnich mi fhe’ nach teidheadh<br />
     A bhliadhna lion.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I HEARD the cuckoo with no food in my stomach,<br />
I heard the stock-dove on the top of the tree,<br />
I heard the sweet singer in the copse beyond,<br />
And I heard the screech of the owl of the night.</p>
<p>I saw the lamb with his back to me,<br />
I saw the snail on the bare flag-stone,<br />
I saw the foal with his rump to me,<br />
I saw the wheatear on a dyke of holes,<br />
I saw the snipe while sitting bent,<br />
And I foresaw that the year would not<br />
     Go well with me.</p></td>
