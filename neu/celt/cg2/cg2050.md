<td data-valign="top"><p>ACHLASAIN Chaluim-chille,<br />
Gun sireadh, gun iarraidh,<br />
Achlasain Chaluim-chille,<br />
Fo m’ righe gu siorruidh!</p>
<p>Air shealbh dhaona,<br />
Air shealbh mhaona,<br />
Air shealbh mhianna,<br />
Air shealbh chaora,<br />
Air shealbh mhaosa,<br />
Air shealbh iana,<br />
Air shealbh raona,<br />
Air shealbh mhaora,<br />
Air shealbh iasga,<br />
Air shealbh bhliochd is bhuar,<br />
Air shealbh shliochd is shluagh,<br />
Air shealbh bhlar is bhuadh,<br />
Air tir, air lir, air cuan,<br />
Trid an Tri ta shuas,<br />
Trid an Tri ta nuns,<br />
Trid an Tri ta buan,<br />
Achlasain Chaluim-chille,<br />
Ta mis a nis da d’ bhuain,<br />
     Ta mis a nis da d’ bhuain.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>PLANTLET of Columba,<br />
Without seeking, without searching,<br />
Plantlet of Columba,<br />
Under my arm for ever!</p>
<p>For luck of men,<br />
For luck of means,<br />
For luck of wish (?),<br />
For luck of sheep,<br />
For luck of goats,<br />
For luck of birds,<br />
For luck of fields,<br />
For luck of shell-fish,<br />
For luck of fish,<br />
For luck of produce and kine,<br />
For luck of progeny and people,<br />
For luck of battle and victory,<br />
On land, on sea, on ocean,<br />
Through the Three on high,<br />
Through the Three a-nigh,<br />
Through the Three eternal,<br />
Plantlet of Columba,<br />
I cull thee now,<br />
     I cull thee now.</p></td>
