<td data-valign="top"><p>PLUCK will I the little red-stalk of surety,<br />
The lint the lovely Bride drew through her palm,<br />
For success of health, for success of friendship,<br />
    For success of joyousness,<br />
For overcoming of evil mind, for overcoming of evil eye,<br />
    For overcoming of bewitchment,<br />
For overcoming of evil deed, for overcoming of evil conduct,<br />
    For overcoming of malediction,<br />
For overcoming of evil news, for overcoming of evil words,<br />
    For success of blissfulness--<br />
    For success of blissfulness.</p></td>
