     Air a ghalar a ta buan;<br />

     Air a ghalar a ta garg.</p>
<p>Mar a ruitheas abhuinn fhuar,<br />
     Mar a mheileas muileann luath,<br />
Fhir a dh’orduich tir is muir,<br />
     Casg air fhuil, ruith air fhual.</p>
<p>An ainm Athar, agus Mic,<br />
An ainm Spioraid Naoimh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I HAVE a charm for the gravel disease,<br />
     For the disease that is perverse<br />
I have a charm for the red disease,<br />
     For the disease that is irritating.</p>
<p>As runs a river cold,<br />
     As grinds a rapid mill,<br />
Thou who didst ordain land and sea,<br />
     Cease the blood and let flow the urine.</p>
<p>In name of Father, and of Son,<br />
     In name of Holy Spirit.</p></td>
