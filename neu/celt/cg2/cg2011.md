Maduinn mhoch,<br />
Le caraid each;<br />
Bhris each a chas,<br />
Le uinich och,<br />
Bha sid mu seach,<br />
Chuir i cnamh ri cnamh,<br />
Chuir i feoil ri feoil,<br />

<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>BRIDE went out<br />
In the morning early,<br />
With a pair of horses;<br />
One broke his leg,<br />
With much ado,<br />
That was apart,<br />
She put bone to bone,<br />
She put flesh to flesh,<br />
She put sinew to sinew,<br />
She put vein to vein;<br />
As she healed that<br />
May I heal this.</p></td>
