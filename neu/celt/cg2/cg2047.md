<td data-valign="top"><p>BUAINIDH mi an earr reidh,<br />
Gum bu treuinide mo bhas,<br />
Gum bu bhlathaide mo bheuil,<br />
Gum bu ceumaide mo chas;<br />
Gum bu h-eilean mi air muir,<br />

     Cha leon duine mi.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL pluck the yarrow fair,<br />
That more brave shall be my hand,<br />
That more warm shall be my lips,<br />
That more swift shall be my foot;<br />
May I an island be at sea,<br />
May I a rock be on land,<br />
That I can afflict any man,<br />
     No man can afflict me.</p></td>
