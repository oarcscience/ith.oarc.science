<td data-valign="top"><p>TA mi cur m’ anama ’s mo chorp<br />
Air do chomaraig a nochd, a Dhe,<br />
Air do chomaraig, Iosa Criosda,<br />
Air do chomaraig, a Spioraid na firinne reidh,<br />
     An Triuir a sheasadh mo chuis,<br />
     Is nach cuireadh an cul rium fein.</p>
<p>Thus, Athair, tha caomh agus ceart,<br />
Thus, a Mhic, thug air peacadh buaidh,<br />
Thus, a Spioraid Naoimhe nam feart,<br />
Da mo ghleidheadh an nochd o thruaigh;<br />
     An Triuir a dheanadh mo cheart<br />
     Mo ghleidheadh an nochd ’s gach uair.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I AM placing my soul and my body<br />
On Thy sanctuary this night, O God,<br />
On Thy sanctuary, O Jesus Christ,<br />
On Thy sanctuary, O Spirit of perfect truth,<br />
     The Three who would defend my cause,<br />
     Nor turn Their backs upon me.</p>
<p>Thou, Father, who art kind and just,<br />
Thou, Son, who didst overcome death,<br />
Thou, Holy Spirit of power,<br />
Be keeping me this night from harm;<br />
     The Three who would justify me<br />
     Keeping me this night and always.</p></td>
