<td data-valign="top"><p>LUIGHIM sios an nochd<br />
Le Muire min ’s le Mac,<br />
Le Micheal finn-gheal,<br />
’S le Bride fo brat.</p>
<p>Luighim sios le Dia,<br />
Is luighidh Dia lium,<br />
Cha luigh mi sios le Briain.<br />
’S cha luigh Briain lium.</p>
<p>A Dhe nam bochd,<br />
Fòir orm an nochd,<br />
Na treig mi tort,<br />
A t’ ionndastaigh.</p>
<p>Aig meid nan lot<br />
A reub mi ort,<br />
Cha leir ’omh nochd<br />
An cunntachadh.</p>
<p>A Righ na fola firinnich,<br />
Na dichuimhn mi ’na d’ thuinneachadh,<br />
Na tagair mi ’s ’na mi cheartan,<br />
Na dibir mi ’na d’ chruinneachadh.<br />
     O ’na d’ chruinneachadh!</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I LIE down to-night<br />
With fair Mary and with her Son,<br />
With pure-white Michael,<br />
And with Bride beneath her mantle.</p>
<p>I lie down with God,<br />
And God will lie down with me,<br />
I will not lie down with Satan,<br />
Nor shall Satan lie down with me.</p>
<p>O God of the poor,<br />
Help me this night,<br />
Omit me not entirely<br />
From Thy treasure-house.</p>
<p>For the many wounds<br />
That I inflicted on Thee,<br />
I cannot this night<br />
Enumerate them.</p>
<p>Thou King of the blood of truth,<br />
Do not forget me in Thy dwelling-place,<br />
Do not exact from me for my transgressions,<br />
Do not omit me in Thine ingathering.<br />
     In Thine ingathering.</p></td>
