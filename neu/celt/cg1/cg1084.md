<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THOU Michael the victorious,<br />
I make my circuit under thy shield,<br />
Thou Michael of the white steed,<br />
And of the bright brilliant blades,<br />
Conqueror of the dragon,<br />
Be thou at my back,<br />
Thou ranger of the heavens,<br />
Thou warrior of the King of all,<br />
     O Michael the victorious,<br />
     My pride and my guide,<br />
     O Michael the victorious,<br />
     The glory of mine eye.</p></td>

<td data-valign="top"><p>Deanam an cuarta<br />
An cluanas mo naomh,<br />
Air machair, air cluan domh,<br />
Air fuar-bheanna fraoch;<br />
Ged shiubhlam an cuan<br />
’S an cruaidh cruinne-ce<br />
Cha deifir domh gu sior<br />
’S mi fo dhidionn do sgeith;<br />
     A Mhicheal nam buadh,<br />
     M’ ailleagan ere,<br />
     A Mhicheil nam buadh,<br />
     Buachaille De.</p>
<p>Tri Naomh na Gloire<br />
Bhith ’n comhnuidh rium reidh,<br />
Ri n’ eachraidh, ri m’ lochraidh,<br />
Ri cioba cloimh an treud.<br />
Am barr ta fas air raona<br />
No caonachadh an raoid,<br />
Air machair no air mointeach,<br />
An toit, an torr, no an cruach.<br />
     Gach ni tha’n aird no’n iosal,<br />
     Gach insridh agus buar,<br />
     ’S le Trithinn naomh na gloire,<br />
     Agus Micheal corr nam buadh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I make my circuit<br />
In the fellowship of my saint,<br />
On the machair, on the meadow,<br />
On the cold heathery hill;<br />
Though I should travel ocean<br />
And the hard globe of the world<br />
No harm can e’er befall me<br />
’Neath the shelter of thy shield;<br />
     O Michael the victorious,<br />
     Jewel of my heart,<br />
     O Michael the victorious,<br />
     God's shepherd thou art.</p>
<p>Be the sacred Three of Glory<br />
Aye at peace with me,<br />
With my horses, with my cattle,<br />
With my woolly sheep in flocks.<br />
With the crops growing in the field<br />
Or ripening in the sheaf,<br />
On the machair, on the moor,<br />
In cole, in heap, or stack.<br />
     Every thing on high or low,<br />
     Every furnishing and flock,<br />
     Belong to the holy Triune of glory,<br />
     And to Michael the victorious.</p></td>
