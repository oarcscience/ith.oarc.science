<span class="small">THIS hymn was sung by a pilgrim in setting out on
his pilgrimage. The family and friends joined the traveller <span
id="page_317">p. 317</span> in singing the hymn and starting the
journey, from which too frequently, for various causes, he never
returned.</span>
