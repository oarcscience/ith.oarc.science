<td data-valign="top"><p>GACH min tha fo m’ chleibh,<br />
Theid am measgadh le cheil,<br />
An ainm Mhic De,<br />
     Thug fas daibh.</p>
<p>Bainn is uibheann is im,<br />
Sochair mhath ar cuid fhin,<br />
Cha bhi gainne ’n ar tir,<br />
     No ’n ar fardaich,</p>
<p>An ainm Mhicheil mo luaidh,<br />
Dh’ f hag againn a bhuaidh,<br />
Le beannachd an Uain,<br />
     ’S a Mhathar.</p>
<p>Umhlaich sinn aig do stol,<br />
Biodh do chumraig fein oirnn,<br />
Cum uainn fuath, fath, foirn,<br />
     Agus gleidh sinn.</p>
<p>Coisrig toradh ar tir,<br />
Bairig sonas is sith,<br />
An ainm an Athar an Righ,<br />
     ’S nan tri ostal gradhach.</p>
<p>Bearnan bride, creamh min,<br />
Lus-mor, glasrach is slim,<br />
Na tri ghroigeanan-cinn,<br />
     Is lus Mairi.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>EACH meal beneath my roof, [wattle<br />
They will all be mixed together,<br />
In name of God the Son,<br />
     Who gave them growth.</p>
<p>Milk, and eggs, and butter,<br />
The good produce of our own flock,<br />
There shall be no dearth in our land,<br />
     Nor in our dwelling.</p>
<p>In name of Michael of my love,<br />
Who bequeathed to us the power,<br />
With the blessing of the Lamb,<br />
     And of His Mother.</p>
<p>Humble us at thy footstool,<br />
Be thine own sanctuary around us,<br />
Ward from us spectre, sprite, oppression,<br />
     And preserve us.</p>
<p>Consecrate the produce of our land,<br />
Bestow prosperity and peace,<br />
In name of the Father the King,<br />
     And of the three beloved apostles.</p>
<p>Dandelion, smooth garlic,<br />
Foxglove, woad, and butterwort,<br />
The three carle-doddies,<br />
     And marigold.</p></td>

     And madder.</p>
<p>I will put water on them all,<br />
In precious name of the Son of God,<br />
In name of Mary the generous,<br />
     And of Patrick.</p>
<p>When we shall sit down<br />
To take our food,<br />
I will sprinkle in the name of God<br />
     On the children.</p></td>
