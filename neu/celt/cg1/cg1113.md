<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>MAY Mary the mild keep the sheep,<br />
May Bride the calm keep the sheep,<br />
May Columba keep the sheep,<br />
May Maolruba keep the sheep,<br />
May Carmac keep the sheep,<br />
From the fox and the wolf.</p>
<p>May Oran keep the kine,<br />
May Modan keep the kine,<br />
May Dorman keep the kine,<br />
May Moluag keep the kine,<br />
May Maolruan keep the kine,<br />
On soft land and hard land.</p>
<p>May the Spirit of peace preserve the flocks,<br />
May the Son of Mary Virgin preserve the flocks,<br />
May the God of glory preserve the flocks,<br />
May the Three preserve the flocks,<br />
From wounding and from death-loss,<br />
    From wounding and from death-loss.</p></td>
