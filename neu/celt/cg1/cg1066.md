<td data-valign="top"><p>OIDHCHE sin a dhealraich an reult,<br />
Rugadh Buachaille nan treud,<br />
Le Oigh nan ceudaibh beus,<br />
     Moire Mhathar.</p>
<p>An Trianaid shiorruidh r’a taobh,<br />
Ann am frasach fuar, faoin.<br />
Thig ’s thoir deachamh de d’ mhaoin,<br />
     Dh’ an t-Slan-Fhear.</p>
<p>An cobhrach, ciochrach, caomh,<br />
Gun aon dachaidh fo ’n t-saoghal,<br />
Am Fogaran naomha, maoth,<br />
     ’Manul!</p>
<p>A thri ainglibh nam buadh,<br />
Thigibh, thigibh a nuas;<br />
Do Chriosd an t-sluaigh<br />
     Thugaibh failte.</p>
<p>Pogaibh a bhasa,<br />
Tioramaichibh a chasa<br />
Le falt bhur cinn;<br />
’S O! Thi na cruinne,<br />
’S Iosa, Mhicheil, Mhuire,<br />
     Na fagaibh sinn.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THAT night the star shone<br />
Was born the Shepherd of the Flock,<br />
Of the Virgin of the hundred charms;<br />
     The Mary Mother.</p>
<p>The Trinity eternal by her side,<br />
In the manger cold and lowly.<br />
Come and give tithes of thy means<br />
     To the Healing Man.</p>
<p>The foam-white breastling beloved,<br />
Without one home in the world,<br />
The tender holy Babe forth driven,<br />
     Immanuel!</p>
<p>Ye three angels of power,<br />
Come ye, come ye down;<br />
To the Christ of the people<br />
     Give ye salutation.</p>
<p>Kiss ye His hands,<br />
Dry ye His feet<br />
With the hair of your heads;<br />
And O! Thou world-pervading God,<br />
And Ye, Jesu, Michael, Mary,<br />
     Do not Ye forsake us.</p></td>
