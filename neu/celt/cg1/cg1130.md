<td data-valign="top"><p>DHE, Athair uile-chumhachdaich, chaoimh,<br />
Ios a Mhic nan deur agus na caoidh,<br />
Le d’ chomh-chomhnadh, O! a Spioraid Naoimh.</p>
<p>Thrithinn bhi-bheo, bhi-mhoir, bhi-bhuain,<br />
Thug Clann Israil tri na Muir Ruaidh,<br />
Is Ionah gu fonn a bronn miol-mhor a’ chuain,</p>
<p>Thug Pol agus a chomhlain ’s an long,<br />
A doruinn na mara, a dolais nan tonn,<br />
A stoirm a bha mor, a doinne bha trom.</p>
<p>Duair bhruchd an tuil air Muir Ghailili,<br />
    *       *       *       *       *<br />
    *       *       *       *       *</p>
<p>Shun agus saor agus naomhaich sinne,<br />
Bi-sa, Righ nan dul, air ar stiuir ad shuidhe,<br />
’S treoirich an sith sinn gu ceann-crich ar n-uidhe.</p>
<p>Le gaotha caona, caomha, coistre, cubhr,<br />
Gun fhaobhadh, gun fhionnsadh, gun fhabhsadh,<br />
Nach deanadh gniamh fabhtach dhuinn.</p>
<p>Iarramaid gach sian a Dhe,<br />
A reir do rian ’s do bhriathra fein.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>GOD the Father all-powerful, benign,<br />
Jesu the Son of tears and of sorrow,<br />
With thy co-assistance, O! Holy Spirit.</p>
<p>The Three-One, ever-living, ever-mighty, everlasting,<br />
Who brought the Children of Israel through the Red Sea,<br />
And Jonah to land from the belly of the great creature of the ocean,</p>
<p>Who brought Paul and his companions in the ship,<br />
From the torment of the sea, from the dolour of the waves,<br />
From the gale that was great, from the storm that was heavy.</p>
<p>When the storm poured on the Sea of Galilee,<br />
    *       *       *       *       *<br />
    *       *       *       *       *</p>
<p>Sain us and shield and sanctify us,<br />
Be Thou, King of the elements, seated at our helm,<br />
And lead us in peace to the end of our journey.</p>
<p>With winds mild, kindly, benign, pleasant.<br />
Without swirl, without whirl, without eddy,<br />
That would do no harmful deed to us.</p>
<p>We ask all things of Thee, O God,<br />
According to Thine own will and word.</p></td>
