      Ho, hail! blessed the King!<br />
      Ho, hi! let there be joy!</p>
<p>Prosperity be upon this dwelling,<br />
On all that ye have heard and seen,<br />
On the bare bright floor flags,<br />
      On the shapely standing stone staves,<br />
      Hail King! hail King! blessed is He! blessed is He!</p>
<p>Bless this house and all that it contains,<br />
From rafter and stone and beam;<br />
Deliver it to God from pall to cover,<br />
      Be the healing of men therein,<br />
      Hail King! hail King! blessed is He! blessed is He!</p>
<p>Be ye in lasting possession of the house,<br />
Be ye healthy about the hearth,<br />
Many be the ties and stakes in the homestead,<br />
      People dwelling on this foundation,<br />
      Hail King! hail King! blessed is He! blessed is He!</p></td>
