<td data-valign="top"><p>TAING dhut Iosda Criosda,<br />
Thug mis a nios o ’n oidhche ’n raoir<br />
Chon solas soillse an la ’n diugh,<br />
Chon sonas siorruidh a chosnadh dha m’ anam,<br />
An cion na fal a dhoirt thu dhomh.</p>
<p>Cliu dhut fein a Dhe gu brath,<br />
An sgath gach agh a bhairig thu orm--<br />
Mo bhiadh, mo bhriathar, mo ghniomh, mo chail,<br />
*        *        *        *        *        *</p>
<p>’S tha mi griosad ort<br />
Mo dhion bho’n olc,<br />
Mo dhion bho lochd,<br />
Mo shian an nochd<br />
’S mi iosal bochd,<br />
O Dhia nam bochd!<br />
O Chriosd nan lot!<br />
Thoir ciall dhomh ’n cois do ghrais.</p>
<p>Gun coraich an Ti Naomha mi,<br />
Gun comhnaich air muir ’s air tir mi,<br />
’S gun treoraich o ir gu ir mi<br />
Chon sith na Cathair Shiorruiche,<br />
       Sith na Cathair Shiorruiche.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THANKS be to Thee, Jesus Christ,<br />
Who brought’st me up from last night,<br />
To the gladsome light of this day,<br />
To win everlasting life for my soul,<br />
Through the blood Thou didst shed for me.</p>
<p>Praise be to Thee, O God, for ever,<br />
For the blessings Thou didst bestow on me--<br />
My food, my speech, my work, my health,<br />
*        *        *        *        *        *</p>
<p>And I beseech Thee<br />
To shield me from sin,<br />
To shield me from ill,<br />
To sain me this night,<br />
And I low and poor,<br />
O God of the poor!<br />
O Christ of the wounds!<br />
Give me wisdom along with Thy grace.</p>
<p>May the Holy One claim me,<br />
And protect me on sea and on land,<br />
And lead me on from step to step,<br />
To the peace of the Everlasting City,<br />
       The peace of the Everlasting City!</p></td>
