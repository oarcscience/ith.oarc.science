<td data-valign="top"><p>SMALAIDH mis an tula<br />
Mar a smaladh Muire;<br />
Comraig Bhride ’s Mhuire,<br />
Air an tula ’s air an lar,<br />
     ’S air an fhardaich uile.</p>
<p>Co siud air liana mach?<br />
Muire ghrian-gheal ’s a Mac,<br />
Bial Dia dh’ iarradh, aingheal Dia labhradh;<br />
Ainghle geallaidh faire an teallaidh,<br />
     Gu’n tig latha geal gu beallaidh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I WILL smoor the hearth<br />
As Mary would smoor;<br />
The encompassment of Bride and of Mary,<br />
On the fire and on the floor,<br />
     And on the household all.</p>
<p>Who is on the lawn without?<br />
Fairest Mary and her Son,<br />
The mouth of God ordained, the angel of God spoke;<br />
Angels of promise watching the hearth,<br />
     Till white day comes to the fire.</p></td>
