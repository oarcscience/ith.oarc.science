<td data-valign="top"><p>THI tha chomhnadh nan ard,<br />
Tiuirich duinn do bheannachd aigh,<br />
Iomchair leinn air bharr an t-sal,<br />
Iomchair sinn gu cala tamh,<br />
Beannaich ar sgioba agus bat,<br />
Beannaich gach acair agus ramh,<br />
Gach stadh is tarruinn agus rac,<br />
Ar siuil-mhora ri crainn ard<br />
Cum a Righ nan dul ’n an ait<br />
Run ’s gu ’n till sinn dachaidh slan;<br />
Suidhidh mi fain air an stiuir,<br />
Is e Mac De a bheir domh iuil,<br />
Mar a thug e Chalum ciuin,<br />
’N am dha stadh a chur ri siuil.</p>
<p>Mhuire, Bhride, Mhicheil, Phail,<br />
Pheadair, Ghabriel, Eoin a ghraidh,<br />
Doirtibh oirnn an driuchd o’n aird,<br />
Bheireadh oirnn ’s a chreideamh fas,<br />
Daingnibh sinn ’s a Charraig Ail,<br />
Anns gach reachd a dhealbhas gradh,<br />
Run ’s gu ’n ruig sinn tir an aigh,<br />
Am hi sith is seirc is baigh<br />
Air an nochdadh duinn tre ghras;<br />
Chaoidh chan fhaigh a chnoimh ’n ar dail,<br />
Bithidh sinn tearuint ann gu brath,<br />
Cha bhi sinn an geimhlibh bais,<br />
Ge do tha sinn do shiol Adh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>O THOU who pervadest the heights,<br />
Imprint on us Thy gracious blessing,<br />
Carry us over the surface of the sea,<br />
Carry us safely to a haven of peace,<br />
Bless our boatmen and our boat,<br />
Bless our anchors and our oars,<br />
Each stay and halyard and traveller,<br />
Our mainsails to our tall masts<br />
Keep, O King of the elements, in their place<br />
That we may return home in peace;<br />
I myself will sit down at the helm,<br />
It is God's own Son who will give me guidance,<br />
As He gave to Columba the mild<br />
What time he set stay to sails.</p>
<p>Mary, Bride, Michael, Paul,<br />
Peter, Gabriel, John of love,<br />
Pour ye down from above the dew<br />
That would make our faith to grow,<br />
Establish ye us in the Rock of rocks,<br />
In every law that love exhibits,<br />
That we may reach the land of glory,<br />
Where peace and love and mercy reign,<br />
All vouchsafed to us through grace;<br />
Never shall the canker worm get near us,<br />
We shall there be safe for ever,<br />
We shall not be in the bonds of death<br />
Though we are of the seed of Adam.</p></td>

<td data-valign="top"><p>La Fheill Michell, La Fheill Mairt,<br />
La Fheill Andrais, bann na baigh,<br />
La Fheill Bride, la mo luaidh,<br />
Tilg an nimhir sios an chuan,<br />
Feuch an dean e slugadh suas;<br />
La Fheill Paruig, la nam buadh,<br />
Sorchair oirnn an stoirm o thuath,<br />
Casg a fraoch, maol a gruam,<br />
Diochd a gairge, marbh a fuachd.</p>
<p>La nan Tri Righrean shuas,<br />
Ciuinich dhuinne barr nan stuadh,<br />
La Bealltain thoir an driuchd,<br />
La Fheill Sheathain thoir an ciuin,<br />
La Fheill Moire mar nan char,<br />
Seachainn oirnn an stoirm o ’n iar,<br />
Gach la ’s oidhche, gach stoirm is fiamh,<br />
Bi thusa leinn, a Thriath nan triath,<br />
Bi fein duinn ad chairt-iuil,<br />
Biodh do lamh air failm ar stiuir,<br />
Do lamh fein, a Dhe nan dul,<br />
Moch is anamoch mar is iul,<br />
     Moch is anamoch mar is iul.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>On the Feast Day of Michael, the Feast Day of Martin,<br />
The Feast Day of Andrew, band of mercy,<br />
The Feast Day of Bride, day of my choice,<br />
Cast ye the serpent into the ocean,<br />
So that the sea may swallow her up;<br />
On the Feast Day of Patrick, day of power,<br />
Reveal to us the storm from the north,<br />
Quell its wrath and blunt its fury,<br />
Lessen its fierceness, kill its cold.</p>
<p>On the Day of the Three Kings on high,<br />
Subdue to us the crest of the waves,<br />
On Beltane Day give us the dew,<br />
On John's Day the gentle wind,<br />
The Day of Mary the great of fame,<br />
Ward off us the storm from the west;<br />
Each day and night, storm and calm,<br />
Be Thou with us, O Chief of chiefs,<br />
Be Thou Thyself to us a compass-chart,<br />
Be Thine hand on the helm of our rudder,<br />
Thine own hand, Thou God of the elements,<br />
Early and late as is becoming,<br />
     Early and late as is becoming.</p></td>
