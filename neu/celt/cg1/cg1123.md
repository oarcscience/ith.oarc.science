<p>Biolair uaine ga buain fo<br />

<td data-valign="top"><p>WELL can I say my rune,<br />
Descending with the glen;<br />
    One rune,<br />
    Two runes,<br />
    Three runes,<br />
    Four runes,<br />
    Five runes,<br />
    Six runes.<br />
    Seven runes,<br />

<p>May the man of this clothing never be wounded,<br />
May torn he never be;<br />
What time he goes into battle or combat,<br />
May the sanctuary shield of the Lord be his.<br />
What time he goes into battle or combat,<br />
May the sanctuary shield of the Lord be his.</p>
<p>This is not second clothing and it is not thigged,<br />
Nor is it the right of sacristan or of priest.</p>
<p>Cresses green culled beneath a stone,<br />
And given to a woman in secret.<br />
The shank of the deer in the head of the herring,<br />
