<td data-valign="top"><p>AN ainm na Trianailt, mar aon,<br />
Ann am briathar, an gniomh ’s an smaon,<br />
Ta mi ’g ionn mo lamha fein,<br />
Ann an sionn ’s an sian nan speur.</p>
<p>A dubhradh nach till mi ri m’ bheo<br />
Gun iasgach, gun ianach ni ’s mo,<br />
Gun seing, gun sithinn nuas a beinn,<br />
Gun sul, gun saill, a muigh a coill.</p>
<p>O Mhoire mhaoth-gheal, chaomh-gheal, ghradh-gheal,<br />
Seachainn orm s’ am bradan tarra-gheal marbh air sala,<br />
Lach le h-alach nam b’e b’aill leat,<br />
Nead ri beul an uisge far nach traigh e.</p>
<p>An liath-chearc air bharr nan stuc,<br />
Is coileach-dubh an tuchain truim,<br />
An deigh laighe luth na greine,<br />
Seachainn, o seachainn orm fein an eisdeachd.</p>
<p>O Mhoire, mhathair chubhr mo Righ,<br />
Crun-sa mi le crun do shith,<br />
Cuir do bhrat rioghach oir dha m’ dhion,<br />
Is comhnuich mi le comhnadh Chriosd,<br />
     Comhnuich mi le comhnadh Chriosd.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>IN name of the Holy Three-fold as one,<br />
In word, in deed, and in thought,<br />
I am bathing mine own hands,<br />
In the light and in the elements of the sky.</p>
<p>Vowing that I shall never return in my life,<br />
Without fishing, without fowling either,<br />
Without game, without venison down from the hill,<br />
Without fat, without blubber from out the copse.</p>
<p>O Mary tender-fair, gentle-fair, loving-fair,<br />
Avoid thou to me the silvery salmon dead on the salt sea,<br />
A duck with her brood an it please thee to show me,<br />
A nest by the edge of the water where it does not dry.</p>
<p>The grey-hen on the crown of the knoll,<br />
The black-cock of the hoarse croon,<br />
After the strength of the sun has gone down,<br />
Avoid, oh, avoid thou to me the hearing of them.</p>
<p>O Mary, fragrant mother of my King,<br />
Crown thou me with the crown of thy peace,<br />
Place thine own regal robe of gold to protect me,<br />
And save me with the saving of Christ,<br />
     Save me with the saving of Christ.</p></td>
