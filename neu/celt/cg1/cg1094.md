<td data-valign="top"><p>THA mi smaladh an teine,<br />
Mar a smaladh Mac Moire;<br />
Gu mu slan dh’ an taigh ’s dh’ an teine,<br />
     Gu mu slan dh’ an chuideachd uile.</p>
<p>Co siud shios air an lar?<br />
Eoin agus Peadail agus Pal.<br />
Co air am bheil an fhaire nochd?<br />
     Air Moire mhin-gheal ’s air a Mac.</p>
<p>Beul De a thubhradh,<br />
Aingheal De a labhradh,<br />
Aingheal an dorus an taighe,<br />
D’ar comhnadh ’s d’ar gleidheadh<br />
     Gu ’n tig la geal am maireach.</p>
<p>O! ainghlean Aon Naomha Dhe<br />
Da mo chaimhleachadh fein a nochd,<br />
O! ainghlean Aon Unga Dhe,<br />
Da mo chaim bho bheud ’s bho lochd,<br />
     Da mo chaim bho bheud a nochd.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I AM smooring the fire<br />
As the Son of Mary would smoor;<br />
Blest be the house, blest be the fire,<br />
     Blest be the people all.</p>
<p>Who are those down on the floor?<br />
John and Peter and Paul.<br />
On whom is the vigil to-night?<br />
     On the fair gentle Mary and on her Son.</p>
<p>The mouth of God said,<br />
The angel of God spake,<br />
An angel in the door of the house,<br />
To guard and to keep us all<br />
     Till comes daylight to-morrow.</p>
<p>Oh! may the angels of the Holy One of God<br />
Environ me all this night,<br />
Oh! may the angels of the Anointed One of God<br />
Encompass me from harm and from evil,<br />
     Oh! encompass me from harm this night.</p>
