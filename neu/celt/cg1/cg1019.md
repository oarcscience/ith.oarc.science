<td data-valign="top"><p>FEUCH Sorchar nan reul<br />
Air corbha nan neul,<br />
Agus ceolradh nan speur<br />
     Ri luaidh dha.</p>
<p>Tighinn le caithrim a nuas<br />
Bho an Athair tha shuas,<br />
Clar agus farcha nan duan<br />
     Ri seirm dha.</p>
<p>Chriosd, a chomairc mo ruin<br />
Corn nach togainn do chliu!<br />
Ainglich is naomhaich chiuil<br />
     Ri luaidh dhut.</p>
<p>A Mhic Mhoire nam buadh,<br />
Is fire finne-ghile snuadh,<br />
Liom bu shon a bhi an cluan<br />
     Do shaoibhreis.</p>
<p>A Chriosda mo chaoimhe,<br />
A Chriosda Chro-naoimhe,<br />
Bithim gach la agus oidhche<br />
     Ri luaidh ort.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>BEHOLD the Lightener of the stars<br />
On the crests of the clouds,<br />
And the choralists of the sky<br />
     Lauding Him.</p>
<p>Coming down with acclaim<br />
From the Father above,<br />
Harp and lyre of song<br />
     Sounding to Him.</p>
<p>Christ, Thou refuge of my love,<br />
Why should not I raise Thy fame!<br />
Angels and saints melodious<br />
     Singing to Thee.</p>
<p>Thou Son of the Mary of graces,<br />
Of exceeding white purity of beauty,<br />
Joy were it to me to be in the fields<br />
     Of Thy riches.</p>
<p>O Christ my beloved,<br />
O Christ of the Holy Blood,<br />
By day and by night<br />
     I praise Thee.</p></td>
