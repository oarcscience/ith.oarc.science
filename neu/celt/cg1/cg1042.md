<td data-valign="top"><p>AINGHIL Dhe, a fhuair mo churam,<br />
Bho Athair cubhraidh na trocaireachd,<br />
Cuartachadh caon na Cro-Naoimhe<br />
A dheanamh air mo choich-anam a nochd,<br />
     O air mo choich-anam a nochd.</p>
<p>Fuadaich uam gach cuar is cunnart,<br />
Cuartaich mi air cuan na corach,<br />
Iarram thu dheanamh solus ur romham,<br />
O ainghil aoibh-ghil, air an oidhche nochd,<br />
     O ainghil aoibh-ghil, air an oidhche nochd.</p>
<p>Bi fein a d’ reuil-iuil os mo chionn,<br />
Sorchair orm gach foirche is fonn,<br />
Stiuir mo bharc air bharr an liuinn,<br />
Chon cala tamh an samhchair thonn,<br />
     Chon cala tamh an samhchair thonn.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THOU angel of God who hast charge of me<br />
From the fragrant Father of mercifulness,<br />
The gentle encompassing of the Sacred Heart<br />
To make round my soul-shrine this night,<br />
     Oh, round my soul-shrine this night.</p>
<p>Ward from me every distress and danger,<br />
Encompass my course over the ocean of truth,<br />
I pray thee, place thy pure light before me,<br />
O bright beauteous angel on this very night,<br />
     Bright beauteous angel on this very night.</p>
<p>Be Thyself the guiding star above me,<br />
Illume Thou to me every reef and shoal,<br />
Pilot my barque on the crest of the wave,<br />
To the restful haven of the waveless sea,<br />
     Oh, the restful haven of the waveless sea.</p></td>
