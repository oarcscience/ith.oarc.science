<td data-valign="top"><p>LAIGHIM sios an nochd,<br />
Le Moire mhin is le Mac,<br />
Le Mathair mo Righ,<br />
Tha da m’ dhion o gach lochd.</p>
<p>Cha laigh mi leis an olc,<br />
Cha laigh an t’ olc liom,<br />
Ach laighidh mi le Dia,<br />
Is laighidh Dia liom.</p>
<p>Dia agus Moire agus Micheal caon,<br />
Agus crois nan naodh aingeal fionn<br />
Da m’ dhion mar Thri is mar Aon,<br />
Bho chlar m’ aodainn gu faobhar mo bhuinn.</p>
<p>Guidheam Peadail, guidheam Pol,<br />
Guidheam Moir Oigh, guidheam am Mac,<br />
Guidheam an da Ostal dochaidh deug<br />
Mo ghleidheadh bho bheud ’s bho lochd;<br />
     O gun mi a dhol eug a nochd,<br />
     Gun mi a dhol eug a nochd!</p>
<p>A Dhia, agus a Mhoire na glorach,<br />
Ios, a Mhic na h-Oighe cubhraidh,<br />
Siantaibh sinn bho phiantaibh siorruidh,<br />
’S bho theine diantaidh dubhraidh,<br />
     Sinn bho phiantaidh siorruidh,<br />
     ’S bho theine diantaidh dubhraidh.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I AM lying down to-night,<br />
With Mary mild and with her Son,<br />
With the Mother of my King,<br />
Who is shielding me from harm.</p>
<p>I will not lie down with evil,<br />
Nor shall evil lie down with me,<br />
But I will lie down with God,<br />
And God will lie down with me.</p>
<p>God and Mary and Michael kindly<br />
And the cross of the nine angels fair,<br />
Be shielding me as Three and as One,<br />
From the brow of my face to the edge of my soles.</p>
<p>I beseech Peter, I beseech Paul,<br />
I beseech Mary, I beseech the Son,<br />
I beseech the trustful Apostles twelve<br />
To preserve me from hurt and harm;<br />
     O from dying to-night,<br />
     From dying to-night!</p>
<p>O God! O Mary of Glory!<br />
O Jesu! Son of the Virgin fragrant,<br />
Sain Ye us from the pains everlasting,<br />
And from the fire fierce and murky,<br />
     From the pains everlasting,<br />
     And from the fire fierce and murky!</p></td>
