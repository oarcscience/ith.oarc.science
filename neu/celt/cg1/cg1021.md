<td data-valign="top"><p>AINGHIL Dhe a fhuair mo churam<br />
Bho Athair cumh na trocaireachd,<br />
Ciobaireachd caon cro nan naomh<br />
Dheanamh dha mo thaobh a nochd;</p>
<p>Fuad uam gach buar is cunnart<br />
Cuart mi air cuan na dobhachd,<br />
Anns a chunglait, chaimleit, chumhan,<br />
Cum mo churach fein an comhnuidh.</p>
<p>Bi ’na do lasair leith romham,<br />
Bi ’na do reuil iuil tharam,<br />
Bi ’na do ro reidh fotham,<br />
Is ’na do chiobair caomh mo dheoghann,<br />
An diugh, an nochd agus gu suthann.</p>
<p>Tha mi sgith is mi air m’ aineol,<br />
Treoraich mi do thir nan aingheal;<br />
Liom is tim a bhi dol dachaidh<br />
Do chuirt Chriosd, do shith nam flathas.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>THOU angel of God who hast charge of me<br />
From the dear Father of mercifulness,<br />
The shepherding kind of the fold of the saints<br />
To make round about me this night;</p>
<p>Drive from me every temptation and danger,<br />
Surround me on the sea of unrighteousness,<br />
And in the narrows, crooks, and straits,<br />
Keep thou my coracle, keep it always.</p>
<p>Be thou a bright flame before me,<br />
Be thou a guiding star above me,<br />
Be thou a smooth path below me,<br />
And be a kindly shepherd behind me,<br />
To-day, to-night, and for ever.</p>
<p>I am tired and I a stranger,<br />
Lead thou me to the land of angels;<br />
For me it is time to go home<br />
To the court of Christ, to the peace of heaven.</p></td>
