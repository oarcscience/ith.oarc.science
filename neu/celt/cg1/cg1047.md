<td data-valign="top"><p>DHE, beannaich an ce ’s na bheil ann,<br />
Dhe, beannaich mo cheile is mo chlann,<br />
Dhe, beannaich an re a ta ’na m’ cheann,<br />
Is beannaich, a Dhe, laimhseachadh mo laimh;<br />
An am domh eirigh ’s a mhaduinn mhoich,<br />
Is laighe air leabaidh anamoich,<br />
     Beannaich m’ eirigh ’s a mhaduinn mhoich,<br />
     Is mo laighe air leabaidh anamoich.</p>
<p>Dhe, teasruig an teach ’s an t-ardrach,<br />
Dhe, coistrig a chlann mhathrach,<br />
Dhe, cuartaich an spreidh ’s an t-alach;<br />
Bi-sa fein na’n deigh ’s da’n taladh,<br />
Duair dhireas ni ri frith ’s ri fruan,<br />
Duair shineas mi a sios an suan,<br />
     Duair dhireas ni ri frith ’s ri fruan,<br />
     Duair shineas mi an sith gu suan.</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>GOD, bless the world and all that is therein.<br />
God, bless my spouse and my children,<br />
God, bless the eye that is in my head,<br />
And bless, O God, the handling of my hand;<br />
What time I rise in the morning early,<br />
What time I lie down late in bed,<br />
     Bless my rising in the morning early,<br />
     And my lying down late in bed.</p>
<p>God, protect the house, and the household,<br />
God, consecrate the children of the motherhood,<br />
God, encompass the flocks and the young;<br />
Be Thou after them and tending them,<br />
What time the flocks ascend hill and wold,<br />
What time I lie down to sleep,<br />
     What time the flocks ascend hill and wold,<br />
     What time I lie down in peace to sleep.</p></td>
