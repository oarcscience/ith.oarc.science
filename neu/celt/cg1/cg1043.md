<td data-valign="top"><p>LAIGHIM am leabaidh,<br />
Mar a laighinn ’s an uaigh,<br />
Do ruighe ri m’ mhuineal,<br />
     Mhic Mhuire nam buadh.</p>
<p>Bidh ainghlean da m’ fhaire<br />
’S mi am laighe an suain,<br />
’S bidh ainghlean da m’ chaithris<br />
     ’S mi ’n cadal na h-uaigh.</p>
<p>Bidh Uiril ri m’ chasan,<br />
Bidh Airil ri m’ chul,<br />
Bidh Gabrail ri m’ bhathais,<br />
     ’S bidh Rafal ri m’ thubh.</p>
<p>Bidh Micheal le m’ anam<br />
Sgiath dhaingean mo ruin!<br />
’S bidh an Leighe Mac Moire,<br />
Cur na seile ri m’ shuil,<br />
     ’S bidh an Leighe Mac Moire,<br />
     Cur na seile ri m’ shuil!</p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p> </p></td>
<td data-valign="top"><p>I LIE in my bed<br />
As I would lie in the grave,<br />
Thine arm beneath my neck,<br />
     Thou Son of Mary victorious.</p>
<p>Angels shall watch me<br />
And I lying in slumber,<br />
And angels shall guard me<br />
     In the sleep of the grave.</p>
<p><a href="errata.htm#0">Uriel</a> shall be at my feet,<br />
Ariel shall be at my back,<br />
Gabriel shall be at my head,<br />
     And Raphael shall be at my side.</p>
<p>Michael shall be with my soul,<br />
The strong shield of my love!<br />
And the Physician Son of Mary<br />
Shall put the salve to mine eye,<br />
     The Physician Son of Mary<br />
     Shall put the salve to mine eye!</p></td>
