### THE ISLE OF PABAIDH. <span id="fr_53"></span>[2](#fn_53)

THERE came a woman of peace (a fairy) the way of the house of a man in
the island of Pabaidh, and

<span id="page_122">p. 122</span>

she had the hunger of motherhood on her. He gave her food, and that went
well with her. She stayed that night. When she went away she said to
him, "I am making a desire that none of the people of this island may go
in childbed after this." None of these people, and none others that
would make their dwelling in the island, ever departed in childbed from
that time.
