The

Feuds of the Clans

BY THE

Rev. ALEXANDER MACGREGOR, M.A.

TOGETHER WITH

THE HISTORY

OF THE

FEUDS AND CONFLICTS

AMONG THE CLANS

IN THE NORTHERN PARTS OF SCOTLAND

AND IN THE WESTERN ISLES,

FROM THE YEAR MXXXI UNTO MDCXIX

First published in 1764 "from a MS. wrote in the reign of King James
VI."

STIRLING:

ENEAS MACKAY, 43 MURRAY PLACE

Printed at the Sentinel Press, Stirling.

Scanned, proofed and formatted at Intangible Textual Heritage, June
