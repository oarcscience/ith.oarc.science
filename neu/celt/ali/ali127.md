For a Sore Breast

> To be said in Irish, while a piece of butter is rubbed over the
> breast--

> "O Son, see how swelled is the breast of the woman! O, you that bore a
> Son, look at it yourself! O Mary! O King of Heaven, let this woman be
> healed! AMEN."
