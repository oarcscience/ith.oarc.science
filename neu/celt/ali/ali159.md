An Elixir of Potency

(From a Manuscript of Date 1770)

> Two ounces of cochineal, one ounce of gentian root, two drachms of
> saffron, two drachms of snakeroot, two drachms of salt of wormwood,
> and the rind of ten oranges, The whole to be steeped in a quart of
> brandy, and kept for use.
