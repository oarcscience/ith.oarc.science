# GIANTS

WHEN the pagan gods of Ireland--the *Tuath-De-Danān*--robbed of worship
and offerings, grew smaller and smaller in the popular imagination,
until they turned into the fairies, the pagan heroes grew bigger and
bigger, until they turned into the giants.
