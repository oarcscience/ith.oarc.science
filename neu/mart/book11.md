[BOOK XII CHAPTER I](mart340) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot in
his madness took a sword and fought with a knight, and leapt in a
bed.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER II](mart341) <span class="small">How Sir Lancelot was
carried in an horse litter, and how Sir Launcelot rescued Sir Bliant,
his host.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER III](mart342) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot
fought against a boar and slew him, and how he was hurt, and brought
unto an hermitage.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER IV](mart343) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot was
known by Dame Elaine, and was borne into a chamber and after healed by
the Sangreal.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER V](mart344) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot,
after that he was whole and had his mind, he was ashamed, and how that
Elaine desired a castle for him.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER VI](mart345) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot
came into the joyous Isle, and there he named himself Le Chevaler Mal
Fet.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER VII](mart346) <span class="small">Of a great
tourneying in the Joyous Isle, and how Sir Pervivale and Sir Ector came
thither, and Sir Percivale fought with him.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER VIlI](mart347) <span class="small">How each of them
knew other, and of their great courtesy, and how his brother Sir Ector
came unto him, and of their joy.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER IX](mart348) <span class="small">How Sir Bors and Sir
Lionel came to King Brandegore, and how Sir Bors took his son Helin le
Blank, and of Sir Launcelot.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER X](mart349) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot with
Sir Percivale and Sir Ector came to the court, and of the great joy of
him.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER XI](mart350) <span class="small">How La Beale Isoud
counselled Sir Tristram to go unto the court, to the great feast of
Pentecost.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER XII](mart351) <span class="small">How Sir Tristram
departed unarmed and met with Sir Palomides, and how they smote each
other, and how Sir Palomides forbare him.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER XIII](mart352) <span class="small">How that Sir
Tristram gat him harness of a knight which was hurt, and how he
overthrew Sir Palomides.</span>  
[BOOK XII CHAPTER XIV](mart353) <span class="small">How Sir Tristram and
Sir Palomides fought long together, and after accorded, and how Sir
Tristram made him to be christened.</span>
