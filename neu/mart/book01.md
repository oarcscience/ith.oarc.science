[BOOK II CHAPTER I](mart027.md) <span class="small">Of a damosel which came
girt with a sword for to find a man of such virtue to draw it out of the
scabbard.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER II](mart028.md) <span class="small">How Balin, arrayed
like a poor knight, pulled out the sword, which afterward was the cause
of his death.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER III](mart029.md) <span class="small">How the Lady of the
Lake demanded the knight's head that had won the sword, or the maiden's
head.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER IV](mart030.md) <span class="small">How Merlin told the
adventure of this damosel.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER V](mart031.md) <span class="small">How Balin was pursued
by Sir Lanceor, knight of Ireland, and how he jousted and slew
him.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER VI](mart032.md) <span class="small">How a damosel, which
was love to Lanceor, slew herself for love, and how Balin met with his
brother Balan.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER VII](mart033.md) <span class="small">How a dwarf reproved
Balin for the death of Lanceor, and how King Mark of Cornwall found
them, and made a tomb over them.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER VIII](mart034.md) <span class="small">How Merlin
prophesied that two the best knights of the world should fight there,
which were Sir Lancelot and Sir Tristram.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER IX](mart035.md) <span class="small">How Balin and his
brother, by the counsel of Merlin, took King Rience and brought him to
King Arthur.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER X](mart036.md) <span class="small">How King Arthur had a
battle against Nero and King Lot of Orkney, and how King Lot was
deceived by Merlin, and how twelve kings were slain.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XI](mart037.md) <span class="small">Of the interment of
twelve kings, and of the prophecy of Merlin, and how Balin should give
the dolorous stroke.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XII](mart038.md) <span class="small">How a sorrowful
knight came before Arthur, and how Balin fetched him, and how that
knight was slain by a knight invisible.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XIII](mart039.md) <span class="small">How Balin and the
damosel met with a knight which was in likewise slain, and how the
damosel bled for the custom of a castle.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XIV](mart040.md) <span class="small">How Balin met with
that knight named Garlon at a feast, and there he slew him, to have his
blood to heal therewith the son of his host.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XV](mart041.md) <span class="small">How Balin fought with
King Pellam, and how his sword brake, and how he gat a spear wherewith
he smote the dolorous stroke.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XVI](mart042.md) <span class="small">How Balin was
delivered by Merlin, and saved a knight that would have slain himself
for love.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XVII](mart043.md) <span class="small">How that knight slew
his love and a knight lying by her, and after, how he slew himself with
his own sword, and how Balin rode toward a castle where he lost his
life.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XVIII](mart044.md) <span class="small">How Balin met with
his brother Balan, and how each of them slew other unknown, till they
were wounded to death.</span>  
[BOOK II CHAPTER XIX](mart045.md) <span class="small">How Merlin buried
them both in one tomb, and of Balin's sword.</span>
