[BOOK XVI CHAPTER I](mart390) <span class="small">How Sir Gawaine was
nigh weary of the quest of the Sangreal, and of his marvellous
dream.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER II](mart391) <span class="small">Of the advision of
Sir Ector, and how he jousted with Sir Uwaine les Avoutres, his sworn
brother.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER III](mart392) <span class="small">How Sir Gawaine and
Sir Ector came to an hermitage to be confessed, and how they told to the
hermit their advisions.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER IV](mart393) <span class="small">How the hermit
expounded their advision.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER V](mart394) <span class="small">Of the good counsel
that the hermit gave to them.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER VI](mart395) <span class="small">How Sir Bors met with
an hermit, and how he was confessed to him, and of his penance enjoined
to him.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER VII](mart396) <span class="small">How Sir Bors was
lodged with a lady, and how he took upon him for to fight against a
champion for her land.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER VIII](mart397) <span class="small">Of an advision
which Sir Bors had that night, and how he fought and overcame his
adversary.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER IX](mart398) <span class="small">How the lady was
returned to her lands by the battle of Sir Bors, and of his departing,
and how he met Sir Lionel taken and beaten with thorns, and also of a
maid which should have been devoured.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER X](mart399) <span class="small">How Sir Bors left to
rescue his brother, and rescued the damosel; and how it was told him
that Lionel was dead.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XI](mart400) <span class="small">How Sir Bors told his
dream to a priest, which he had dreamed, and of the counsel that the
priest gave to him.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XII](mart401) <span class="small">How the devil in a
woman's likeness would have had Sir Bors to have lain by her, and how by
God's grace he escaped.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XIII](mart402) <span class="small">Of the holy
communication of an Abbot to Sir Bors, and how the Abbot counselled
him.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XIV](mart403) <span class="small">How Sir Bors met
with his brother Sir Lionel, and how Sir Lionel would have slain Sir
Bors.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XV](mart404) <span class="small">How Sir Colgrevance
fought against Sir Lionel for to save Sir Bors, and how the hermit was
slain.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XVI](mart405) <span class="small">How Sir Lionel slew
Sir Colgrevance, and how after he would have slain Sir Bors.</span>  
[BOOK XVI CHAPTER XVII](mart406) <span class="small">How there came a
voice which charged Sir Bors to touch him not, and of a cloud that came
between them.</span>
