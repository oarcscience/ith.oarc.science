[BOOK XIII CHAPTER I](mart354) <span class="small">How at the vigil of
the Feast of Pentecost entered into the hall before King Arthur a
damosel, and desired Sir Launcelot for to come and dub a knight, and how
he went with her.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER II](mart355) <span class="small">How the letters were
found written in the Siege Perilous and of the marvellous adventure of
the sword in a stone.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER III](mart356) <span class="small">How Sir Gawaine
assayed to draw out the sword, and how an old man brought in
Galahad.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER IV](mart357) <span class="small">How the old man
brought Galahad to the Siege Perilous and set him therein, and how all
the knights marvelled.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER V](mart358) <span class="small">How King Arthur
shewed the stone hoving on the water to Galahad, and how he drew out the
sword.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER VI](mart359) <span class="small">How King Arthur had
all the knights together for to joust in the meadow beside Camelot or
they departed.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER VII](mart360) <span class="small">How the queen
desired to see Galahad; and how after, all the knights were replenished
with the Holy Sangreal, and how they avowed the enquest of the
same.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER VIII](mart361) <span class="small">How great sorrow
was made of the king and the queen and ladies for the departing of the
knights, and how they departed.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER IX](mart362) <span class="small">How Galahad gat him
a shield, and how they sped that presumed to take down the said
shield.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER X](mart363) <span class="small">How Galahad departed
with the shield, and how King Evelake had received the shield of Joseph
of Aramathie.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XI](mart364) <span class="small">How Joseph made a
cross on the white shield with his blood, and how Galahad was by a monk
brought to a tomb.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XII](mart365) <span class="small">Of the marvel that
Sir Galahad saw and heard in the tomb, and how he made Melias
knight.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XIII](mart366) <span class="small">Of the adventure
that Melias had, and how Galahad revenged him, and how Melias was
carried into an abbey.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XIV](mart367) <span class="small">How Sir Galahad
departed, and how he was commanded to go to the Castle of Maidens to
destroy the wicked custom.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XV](mart368) <span class="small">How Sir Galahad
fought with the knights of the castle, and destroyed the wicked
custom.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XVI](mart369) <span class="small">How Sir Gawaine
came to the abbey for to follow Galahad, and how he was shriven to a
hermit.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XVII](mart370) <span class="small">How Sir Galahad
met with Sir Launcelot and Sir Percivale, and smote them down, and
departed from them.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XVIII](mart371) <span class="small">How Sir
Launcelot, half sleeping and half waking, saw a sick man borne in a
litter, and how he was healed with the Sangreal.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XIX](mart372) <span class="small">How a voice spake
to Sir Launcelot, and how he found his horse and his helm borne away,
and after went afoot.</span>  
[BOOK XIII CHAPTER XX](mart373) <span class="small">How Sir Launcelot
was shriven, and what sorrow he made and of the good ensamples which
were shewed him.</span>
