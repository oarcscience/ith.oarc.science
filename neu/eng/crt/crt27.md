# PART III

# The Madness of Sir Tristram

HERE followeth the story of how Sir Tristram was driven out of Cornwall
and of how he went mad because of his troubles. Likewise it shall be
told how he performed several very wonderful adventures whilst he was in
that state, and of how he was brought back into his senses again.
