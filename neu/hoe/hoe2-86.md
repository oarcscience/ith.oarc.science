## CHARM AGAINST SNAKE-BITE.<span id="page_298_fr_1"></span>[1](#page_298_note_1.md)

<table data-align="center" data-cellpadding="10">
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />

<td><span class="small">T<span class="small">HOU</span> beneath the bridge, the smooth wood<br />
Under juniper the rough wood,<br />
Thou the arrow in the willows,<br />
O thou challenged gold-adorned one,<br />
Earthy-coloured, liver-coloured,<br />
Rainy-hued and hazel-coloured,<br />
Firebrand hued and cherry-coloured,<br />
Do not thou in secret bite me,<br />
Nor attack me unsuspecting,<br />
Do not bite me when I heed not.<br />
