# SECTION IV

### *FAMILIAR STORIES OF NORTHERN EUROPE*

U<span class="small">NDER</span> this heading we include variants of
well-known but not cosmopolitan tales, some of which are of considerable
interest. Among them is a variant of “Melusina,” close in some points,
but presenting many features of difference.
