# SECTION XII

### *UNNATURAL BROTHERS*

T<span class="small">HE</span> story of the wicked rich brother who
oppresses the poor one is not unknown in Esthonia. There is a hideous
story of such a pair, relating how when the poor brother died his widow
begged grave-clothing from the wife of the rich one. When the rich
brother returned, be scolded his wife, and rushed off, cursing and
swearing, to strip the body of his dead brother, even in his coffin,
crying, “That’s mine! that’s mine!” But when be would have laid the
naked corpse back in the coffin, it clung round his neck, and be was
compelled to carry it about with him for the rest of his life.
