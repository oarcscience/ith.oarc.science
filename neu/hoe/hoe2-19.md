## THE THREE WISHES.

T<span class="small">HIS</span> well-known story appears in one of its
commonest forms in the tale of “Loppi and Lappi” (Kreutzwald), a
quarrelsome couple who are granted three wishes by a fairy. At
supper-time the wife wishes for a sausage, which is wished on and off
her nose, and the couple remain as poor as before.
