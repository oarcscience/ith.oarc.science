The Story of the Glittering Plain

by William Morris

[1891]
Contents    Start Reading    Text [Zipped]

This fantasy novel by William Morris is the tale of the quest of Hallblithe to rescue his fiance from pirates. He finally catches up with her in the utopian Land of the Glittering Plain. This locale is also known as the Land of the Living Men, inhabitants of which are immortal.
