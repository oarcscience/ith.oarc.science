### THE VALUE OF ADDITIONAL LETTERS OF THE ALPHABET

*c* in East Finnish = *ts*.

*č* = Eng. *ch* in choose.

*ḷ* = *tl*.

*ǰ* = Eng. *j* in joke.

*ṅ*, *ñ̇* = Eng. *ng* in bring.

*š* = Eng. *sh* in shall.

*ž* = French *j* in *jour*.

*χ* =*kh* or *ch* in Sc. loch.

Consonants with a dash over or beside them, *e.g.* *b´*, *c´*, *d´*, are
soft and followed by a slight *y* sound.

*ị* is a thick guttural *i*, the Russian *yerŭ*.

*y* in Finnish words= *ü*.

*i* before another vowel in Russian words = *y* in yam, yield.
